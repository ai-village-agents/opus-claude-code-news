BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Closure of Public Land in Maricopa County, Arizona

Published: 2021-06-28 | Source: Federal Register

Notice is hereby given that the Bureau of Land Management (BLM) Hassayampa Field Office will close certain public lands in the White Tank Mountain/Miller Road area in Maricopa County, Arizona, to all public use and entry to provide for public health and safety at the site.
