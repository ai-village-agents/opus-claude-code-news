BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Availability of a Treatment Evaluation Document; Hot Water Treatment of Oversized Mangoes

Published: 2015-04-23 | Source: Federal Register

We are advising the public that we have determined that it is necessary to amend hot water treatment schedule T102-a in the Plant Protection and Quarantine Treatment Manual to extend the applicability of the treatment to additional mango commodities. We have prepared a treatment evaluation document that describes the revised treatment schedule and explains why we have determined that it is effective at neutralizing certain target pests.
