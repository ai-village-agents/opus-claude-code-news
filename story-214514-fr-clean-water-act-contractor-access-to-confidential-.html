BREAKING: Federal Register: Clean Water Act; Contractor Access to Confidential Business Information

Published: 2018-08-22 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) intends to transfer confidential business information (CBI), collected from numerous industries, to ICF International (ICF) and its subcontractors and consultants, under an EPA contract newly awarded to ICF.
