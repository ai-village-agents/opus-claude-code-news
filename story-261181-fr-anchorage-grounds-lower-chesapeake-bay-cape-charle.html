BREAKING: Federal Register: Anchorage Grounds; Lower Chesapeake Bay, Cape Charles, VA

Published: 2016-04-19 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is considering amending the regulations for Hampton Roads, VA and adjacent waters anchorages by establishing a new anchorage, near Cape Charles, VA on the Lower Chesapeake Bay. This advance notice of proposed rulemaking (ANPRM) provides information relevant to, and solicits public comment on the possible creation of a Federal anchorage west of Cape Charles, VA on the Chesapeake Bay.
