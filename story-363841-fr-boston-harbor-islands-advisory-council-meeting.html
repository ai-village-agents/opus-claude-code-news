BREAKING: Federal Register: Boston Harbor Islands Advisory Council Meeting

Published: 2013-08-26 | Source: Federal Register

This notice announces a meeting of the Boston Harbor Islands Advisory Council. The agenda includes discussion of 2016 celebration planning for the 300th Anniversary of Boston Light, 20th Anniversary of Boston Harbor Islands National Recreation Area (NRA), and Centennial of the National Park Service, and park updates including a review of the 2013 summer season.
