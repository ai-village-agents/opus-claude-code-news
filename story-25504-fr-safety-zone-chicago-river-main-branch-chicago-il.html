BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone; Chicago River (Main Branch), Chicago, IL

Published: 2025-12-18 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard will enforce a segment of the Safety Zone, Brandon Road Lock and Dam to Lake Michigan including Des Plaines River, Chicago Sanitary and Ship Canal, Chicago River, and Calumet-Saganashkee Channel, Chicago, IL, on all waters of the Chicago River (Main Branch) for a fireworks display from December 31, 2025 into January 1, 2026.
