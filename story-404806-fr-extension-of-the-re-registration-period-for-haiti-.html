BREAKING: Federal Register: Extension of the Re-registration Period for Haiti Temporary Protected Status

Published: 2012-12-28 | Source: Federal Register

On October 1, 2012, the Secretary of Homeland Security (Secretary) extended the designation of Haiti for Temporary Protected Status (TPS) for a period of 18 months by notice in the Federal Register. The Department of Homeland Security (DHS) established a 60- day re-registration period from October 1, 2012 through November 30, 2012.
