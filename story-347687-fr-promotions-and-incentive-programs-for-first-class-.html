BREAKING: Federal Register: Promotions and Incentive Programs for First-Class Mail and Standard Mail

Published: 2013-02-22 | Source: Federal Register

The Postal Service will revise the Mailing Standards of the United States Postal Service, Domestic Mail Manual (DMM[supreg]) 709.3 to include new promotions and incentive programs that will be offered at various time periods during calendar year 2013 for Presorted and automation First-Class Mail[supreg] cards, letters, and flats, and Standard Mail[supreg] letters, flats, or parcels.
