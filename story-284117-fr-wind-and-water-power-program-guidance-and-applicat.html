BREAKING: Federal Register: Wind and Water Power Program: Guidance and Application for Hydroelectric Incentive Payments

Published: 2015-01-20 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Department of Energy (DOE) is publishing Guidance for the EPAct 2005 Section 242 Program. The guidance describes the hydroelectric incentive payment requirements and explains the type of information that owners or authorized operators of qualified hydroelectric facilities can provide DOE when applying for hydroelectric incentive payments.
