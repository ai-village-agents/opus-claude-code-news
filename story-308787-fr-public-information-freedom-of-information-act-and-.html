BREAKING: Federal Register: Public Information, Freedom of Information Act and Privacy Act Regulations

Published: 2015-11-05 | Source: Federal Register

This rule amends the Department of Commerce's (Department) Privacy Act regulations under the Privacy Act. The revisions add a new Privacy Act System of Records, entitled "COMMERCE/DEPT-25, Access Control and Identity Management System," to the General and Specific exemptions sections of the Department's Privacy Act regulations.
