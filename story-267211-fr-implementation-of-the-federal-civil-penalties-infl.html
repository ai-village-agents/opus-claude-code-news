BREAKING: Federal Register: Implementation of the Federal Civil Penalties Inflation Adjustment Act Improvements Act of 2015

Published: 2016-06-27 | Source: Federal Register

The National Science Foundation (NSF or Foundation) is adjusting the maximum civil monetary penalties that may be imposed for violations of the Antarctic Conservation Act of 1978 (ACA), to reflect the requirements of the Federal Civil Penalties Inflation Adjustment Act Improvements Act of 2015 (the 2015 Act).
