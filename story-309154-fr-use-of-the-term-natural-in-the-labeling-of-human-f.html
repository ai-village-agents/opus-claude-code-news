BREAKING: Federal Register: Use of the Term “Natural” in the Labeling of Human Food Products; Request for Information and Comments

Published: 2015-11-12 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA or we) is announcing the establishment of a docket to receive information and comments on the use of the term "natural" in the labeling of human food products, including foods that are genetically engineered or contain ingredients produced through the use of genetic engineering.
