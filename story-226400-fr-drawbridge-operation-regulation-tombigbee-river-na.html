BREAKING: Federal Register: Drawbridge Operation Regulation; Tombigbee River, Naheola, AL

Published: 2017-01-25 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard has issued a temporary deviation from the operating schedule that governs the Meridian & Bigbee Railroad (MNBR) vertical lift bridge across the Tombigbee River, mile 128.6 (Black Warrior Tombigbee Waterway mile 173.6) at Naheola, between Choctaw and Marengo Counties, Alabama. The deviation is necessary to conduct maintenance essential for the continued safe operation of the bridge.
