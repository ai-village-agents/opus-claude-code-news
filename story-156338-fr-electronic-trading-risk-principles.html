BREAKING: Federal Register: Electronic Trading Risk Principles

Published: 2020-07-15 | Source: Federal Register

The Commodity Futures Trading Commission ("CFTC" or "Commission") is proposing amendments to its regulations to address the potential risk of a designated contract market's ("DCM") trading platform experiencing a disruption or system anomaly due to electronic trading.
