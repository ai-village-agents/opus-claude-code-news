BREAKING: Federal Register: Air Plan Revision; California; Placer County Air Pollution Control District; New Source Review

Published: 2026-01-06 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is taking final action to approve a permitting rule submitted as a revision to the Placer County Air Pollution Control District (PCAPCD or "District") portion of the California state implementation plan (SIP). This revision concerns the District's Nonattainment New Source Review permitting program for new and modified major stationary sources of air pollution in nonattainment areas under part D of title I of the Clean Air Act (CAA or "Act").
