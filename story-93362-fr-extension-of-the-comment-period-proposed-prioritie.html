BREAKING: Federal Register: Extension of the Comment Period; Proposed Priorities, Requirements, Definitions, and Selection Criteria-Expanding Opportunity Through Quality Charter Schools Program (CSP)-Grants to State Entities (SE Grants); Grants to Charter Management Organizations for the Replication and Expansion of High-Quality Charter Schools (CMO Grants); and Grants to Charter School Developers for the Opening of New Charter Schools and for the Replication and Expansion of High-Quality Charter Schools (Developer Grants)

Published: 2022-04-12 | Source: Federal Register

On March 14, 2022, the Department of Education (Department) published in the Federal Register a notice of proposed priorities, requirements, definitions, and selection criteria (NPP) for CSP SE Grants, CMO Grants, and Developer Grants, Assistance Listing Numbers (ALNs) 84.282A, 84.282M, 84.282B, and 84.282E. The document established a deadline date of April 13, 2022, for the submission of public comments. We extend the comment period until April 18, 2022.
