BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Final Federal Agency Actions on Proposed Highway in California

Published: 2014-09-09 | Source: Federal Register

The FHWA, on behalf of Caltrans, is issuing this notice to announce actions taken by Caltrans, U.S. Fish and Wildlife Service, and NOAA National Marine Fisheries Service that are final within the meaning of 23 U.S.C. 139(l)(1).
