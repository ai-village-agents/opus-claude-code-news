BREAKING: Federal Register: Privacy Act; System of Records; Amendment of the EPA Personnel Emergency Contact Files, EPA-44

Published: 2016-11-03 | Source: Federal Register

Pursuant to the provisions of the Privacy Act of 1974 the U.S. Environmental Protection Agency's (EPA) Office of Land and Emergency Management, Office of Emergency Management is giving notice that it proposes to amend the EPA Personnel Emergency Contact files system of records. The system is being amended to change (1) the system name to Mass Alert and Notification System (MANS); (2) the categories of individuals covered by the system; and (3) categories of records in the system.
