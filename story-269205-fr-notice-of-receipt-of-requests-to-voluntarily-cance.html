BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Receipt of Requests To Voluntarily Cancel Certain Pesticide Registrations

Published: 2016-07-21 | Source: Federal Register

In accordance with the Federal Insecticide, Fungicide, and Rodenticide Act (FIFRA), EPA is issuing a notice of receipt of request by registrants to voluntarily cancel certain pesticide registrations and amend one pesticide registration. The amendment request would delete dodecylguanidine hydrochloride (DGH) use in disposable diapers.
