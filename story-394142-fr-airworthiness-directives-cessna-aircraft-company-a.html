BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Cessna Aircraft Company Airplanes

Published: 2012-08-20 | Source: Federal Register

We propose to adopt a new airworthiness directive (AD) for certain Cessna Aircraft Company Models 172R and 172S airplanes. This proposed AD was prompted by reports of chafed fuel return line assemblies, which were caused by the fuel return line assembly rubbing against the right steering tube assembly during full rudder pedal actuation.
