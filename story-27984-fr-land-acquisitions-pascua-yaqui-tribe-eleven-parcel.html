BREAKING: Federal Register: Land Acquisitions; Pascua Yaqui Tribe, Eleven Parcels, Pima County, Arizona

Published: 2024-01-25 | Source: Federal Register

The Assistant Secretary--Indian Affairs made a final agency determination to acquire in trust 25.56 acres, more or less, of land consisting of eleven off-reservation parcels in Pima County, Arizona, (Site) for the Pascua Yaqui Tribe of Arizona, (Tribe) for gaming and other purposes.
