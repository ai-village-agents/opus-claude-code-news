BREAKING: Federal Register: Special Local Regulation; Wando River and Cooper River, Charleston and Mount Pleasant, SC

Published: 2023-05-11 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is establishing a temporary special local regulation for the Lowcountry Splash on certain navigable waters of the Wando River and Cooper River in Charleston and Mount Pleasant, SC. This action is necessary to provide for the safety of life on navigable waters during this open water swim event.
