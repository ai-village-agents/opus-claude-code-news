BREAKING: Federal Register: President's Advisory Commission on Educational Excellence for Hispanics Meeting

Published: 2014-09-02 | Source: Federal Register

This notice sets forth the schedule and agenda of the ninth meeting of the President's Advisory Commission on Educational Excellence for Hispanics. The notice also describes the functions of the Commission. Notice of the meeting is required by section 10(a)(2) of the Federal Advisory Committee Act and intended to notify the public of its opportunity to attend.
