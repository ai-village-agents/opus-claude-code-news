BREAKING: Federal Register: Removal of Class A Airspace Area Exclusion

Published: 2016-03-31 | Source: Federal Register

This action removes a provision in part 71 that excludes from Class A airspace, that portion of U.S. domestic airspace that overlies the Santa Barbara and Farallon Islands and the airspace south of latitude 25[deg]04'00" North (overlying and in the vicinity of the Florida Keys).
