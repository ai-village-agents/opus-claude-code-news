BREAKING: Federal Register: Guidance for Conducting Technical Analyses for Low-Level Radioactive Waste Disposal

Published: 2015-03-26 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Nuclear Regulatory Commission (NRC) is issuing for public comment a draft NUREG, NUREG-2175, "Guidance for Conducting Technical Analyses for 10 CFR part 61."
