BREAKING: Federal Register: National Advisory Committee on Institutional Quality and Integrity (NACIQI)

Published: 2014-05-07 | Source: Federal Register

This meeting notice is an update to the previous notices published in the Federal Register on February 25, 2014 (79 FR 10508) and on March 31, 2014 (79 FR 18021). This notice identifies a revision to the agenda and invites written third party comment on the question "What issues are critical to consider in advancing quality assurance in higher education?"
