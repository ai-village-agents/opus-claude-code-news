BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Pratt & Whitney Canada Corp. Engines

Published: 2023-11-09 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA proposes to adopt a new airworthiness directive (AD) for all Pratt & Whitney Canada Corp. (P&WC) Model PW307A and PW307D engines. This proposed AD was prompted by a root cause analysis of an event involving an uncontained failure of a high-pressure turbine (HPT) 1st-stage disk that resulted in high-energy debris penetrating the engine cowling and an aborted takeoff.
