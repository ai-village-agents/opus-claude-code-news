BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Intent To License Government-Owned Inventions; Intent To License on a Partially-Exclusive Basis

The U.S. Army Edgewood Chemical Biological Center intends to license these inventions on a partially-exclusive basis to Biodetech LLC, a Maryland corporation with principal offices at 2224 Choate Rd., Fallston, MD 21047.
