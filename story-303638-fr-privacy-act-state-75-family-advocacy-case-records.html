BREAKING: Federal Register: Privacy Act; STATE-75, Family Advocacy Case Records

Published: 2015-09-09 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of State is giving notice that certain portions of the Family Advocacy Case Records, STATE -75, system of records are proposed to be exempt from one or more provisions of the Privacy Act of 1974.
