BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone; Tug CHAMPION and Deck Barge MM-142 Operating in the Straits of Mackinac

Published: 2019-05-22 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is establishing a temporary safety zone in the Captain of the Port, Sault Sainte Marie zone. This temporary safety zone is necessary to protect the public, contractors, and the contractor's equipment from potential hazards associated with drilling, coring and surveying by persons conducting cable replacement and subsurface investigations in the Straits of Mackinac.
