BREAKING: Federal Register: Channel Lineup Requirements-Modernization of Media Regulation Initiative

Published: 2018-05-01 | Source: Federal Register

In this document, the Federal Communications Commission (Commission) proposes to eliminate the requirement that cable operators maintain at their local office a current listing of the cable television channels that each cable system delivers to its subscribers. In addition, the Commission invites comment on whether we should also eliminate the requirement that certain cable operators make their channel lineup available via their online public inspection file.
