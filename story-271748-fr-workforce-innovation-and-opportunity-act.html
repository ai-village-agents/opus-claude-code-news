BREAKING: Federal Register: Workforce Innovation and Opportunity Act

Published: 2016-08-19 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Labor (DOL or the Department) issues this Final Rule to implement titles I and III of the Workforce Innovation and Opportunity Act (WIOA). Through these regulations, the Department reforms and modernizes our nation's workforce development system.
