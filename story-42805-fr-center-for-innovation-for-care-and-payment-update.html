BREAKING: Federal Register: Center for Innovation for Care and Payment Update

Published: 2024-07-29 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Veterans Affairs (VA) amends its medical regulations governing the Center for Innovation for Care and Payment. This final rule is making several technical revisions to revise the organizational alignment of the Center for Innovation for Care and Payment and to revise where evaluation results are posted for public review for the pilot programs.
