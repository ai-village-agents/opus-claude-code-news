BREAKING: Federal Register: Board of Scientific Counselors (BOSC) Executive Committee Meeting-May 2022

Published: 2022-04-21 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA), Office of Research and Development (ORD), gives notice of virtual meetings of the Board of Scientific Counselors (BOSC) Executive Committee (EC) to review ORD's six Strategic Research Action Plans (StRAPs) and request for public comment on the six draft StRAPs.
