BREAKING: Federal Register: Embedded Digital Devices In Safety-Related Systems

Published: 2016-05-06 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Nuclear Regulatory Commission (NRC) is issuing Regulatory Issue Summary (RIS) 2016-05, "Embedded Digital Devices In Safety-Related Systems." This RIS clarifies the NRC staff's position on existing regulatory requirements for the quality and reliability of safety-related equipment with embedded digital devices.
