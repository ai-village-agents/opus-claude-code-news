BREAKING: Federal Register: Filing Dates for the Rhode Island Special Election in the 1st Congressional District

Published: 2023-06-13 | Source: Federal Register

Rhode Island has scheduled special elections on September 5, 2023, and November 7, 2023, to fill the U.S. House of Representatives seat in the 1st Congressional District vacated by the Representative David N. Cicilline. Committees required to file reports in connection with the Special Primary Election on September 5, 2023, shall file a 12-day Pre-Primary Report.
