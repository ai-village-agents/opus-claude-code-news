BREAKING: Federal Register: Changes to Scheduling and Appearing at Hearings

Published: 2013-06-27 | Source: Federal Register

We propose to revise our rules to protect the integrity of our programs and preserve limited resources. Prior to scheduling a hearing, we will notify the claimant that his or her hearing may be held by video teleconferencing. The claimant will have an opportunity to object to appearing by video teleconferencing within 30 days after the date he or she receives the notice.
