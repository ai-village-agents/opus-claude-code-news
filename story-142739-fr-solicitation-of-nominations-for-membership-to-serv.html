BREAKING: Federal Register: Solicitation of Nominations for Membership To Serve on the Advisory Committee on Infant Mortality

Published: 2020-01-24 | Source: Federal Register

HRSA is seeking nominations of qualified candidates for consideration for appointment as members of the Advisory Committee on Infant Mortality (ACIM). The ACIM provides advice to the Secretary of HHS on Department activities and programs directed at reducing infant mortality and improving the health status of pregnant women and infants.
