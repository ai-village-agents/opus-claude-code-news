BREAKING: Federal Register: Selection of Public Transportation Resilience Projects in Response to Hurricane Sandy

Published: 2014-11-05 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Department of Transportation's (DOT) Federal Transit Administration (FTA) announces the selection of public transportation resilience projects in response to Hurricane Sandy under the Emergency Relief Program. These projects are funded under the Disaster Relief Appropriations Act of 2013, which made approximately $10.9 billion available for public transportation systems impacted by Hurricane Sandy in October 2012.
