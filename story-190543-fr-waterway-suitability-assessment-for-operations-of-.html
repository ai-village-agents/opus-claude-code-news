BREAKING: Federal Register: Waterway Suitability Assessment for Operations of Liquefied Hazardous Gas Terminal; Nederland, TX

Published: 2019-10-07 | Source: Federal Register

We are requesting your comments on a Letter of Intent and Preliminary Waterway Suitability Assessment we received from Sunoco Partners Marketing & Terminals to expand their existing liquefied hazardous gas (LHG) operations by increasing the number of liquefied propane and butane ship visits from approximately 120 per year to 432 ship visits per year.
