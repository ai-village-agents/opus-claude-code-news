BREAKING: Federal Register: Modification of Class D and E Airspace; Twin Falls, ID

Published: 2013-07-05 | Source: Federal Register

This action modifies Class E airspace at Twin Falls Joslin Field-Magic Valley Regional Airport, Twin Falls, ID, to accommodate aircraft using the Navigation (RNAV) Global Positioning System (GPS) and the Instrument Landing System (ILS) or Localizer (LOC) standard instrument approach procedures at the airport.
