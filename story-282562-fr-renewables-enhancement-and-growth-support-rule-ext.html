BREAKING: Federal Register: Renewables Enhancement and Growth Support Rule; Extension of Comment Period

Published: 2016-12-27 | Source: Federal Register

On November 16, 2016, the Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) proposed the Renewables Enhancement and Growth Support (REGS) rule. The proposal specified that the public comment period would end on January 17, 2017, 60 days after publication in the Federal Register.
