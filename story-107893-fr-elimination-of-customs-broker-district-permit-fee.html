BREAKING: Federal Register: Elimination of Customs Broker District Permit Fee

Published: 2022-10-18 | Source: Federal Register

This document amends the U.S. Customs and Border Protection (CBP) regulations to eliminate customs broker district permit fees. Concurrently with this final rule, CBP is publishing a final rule to, among other things, eliminate customs broker districts (see "Modernization of the Customs Broker Regulations" RIN 1651-AB16).
