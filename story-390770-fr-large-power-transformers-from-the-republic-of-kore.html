BREAKING: Federal Register: Large Power Transformers From the Republic of Korea: Final Determination of Sales at Less Than Fair Value

Published: 2012-07-11 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Commerce (the Department) has determined that imports of large power transformers from the Republic of Korea (Korea) are being, or are likely to be, sold in the United States at less than fair value (LTFV), as provided in section 735 of the Tariff Act of 1930, as amended (the Act). The estimated margins of sales at LTFV are listed in the "Continuation of Suspension of Liquidation" section of this notice.
