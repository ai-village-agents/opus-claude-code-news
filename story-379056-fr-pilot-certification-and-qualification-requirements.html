BREAKING: Federal Register: Pilot Certification and Qualification Requirements for Air Carrier Operations

Published: 2012-02-29 | Source: Federal Register

This action would create new certification requirements for pilots in air carrier operations. The proposal would require a second in command (first officer) in part 121 operations to hold an airline transport pilot (ATP) certificate and a type rating for the aircraft to be flown. The FAA proposes to allow pilots with an aviation degree or military pilot experience to obtain an ATP certificate with restricted privileges with fewer than 1,500 hours total time as a pilot.
