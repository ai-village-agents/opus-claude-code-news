BREAKING: Federal Register: Special Technical Committee on Civil Disturbance Unit Personal Protective Equipment

Published: 2017-07-05 | Source: Federal Register

The National Institute of Justice (NIJ) is seeking qualified individuals to serve on a Special Technical Committee (STC) on Civil Disturbance Unit (CDU) Personal Protective Equipment (PPE). The purpose of the STC will be to oversee the development of performance standards for CDU PPE that meet the needs of U.S. law enforcement.
