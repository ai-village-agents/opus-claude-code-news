BREAKING: Federal Register: Excess Uranium Management: Secretarial Determination of No Adverse Impact on the Domestic Uranium Mining, Conversion, and Enrichment Industries

Published: 2015-10-27 | Source: Federal Register

On August 2, 2015, the Secretary of Energy issued a determination ("Secretarial Determination") covering the sale or transfer of high-assay low enriched uranium for medical isotope development projects.
