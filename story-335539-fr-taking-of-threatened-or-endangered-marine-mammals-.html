BREAKING: Federal Register: Taking of Threatened or Endangered Marine Mammals Incidental to Commercial Fishing Operations

Published: 2014-09-24 | Source: Federal Register

NMFS is announcing an extension to the public comment period for the amended permit to authorize the incidental, but not intentional, take of two stocks of marine mammals listed as threatened or endangered under the Endangered Species Act (ESA), under the Marine Mammal Protection Act (MMPA), by the California (CA) thresher shark/ swordfish drift gillnet fishery (>14 in mesh) and the Washington (WA)/ Oregon (OR)/CA sablefish pot fishery.
