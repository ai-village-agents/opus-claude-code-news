BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Inventory Completion: Columbia University, Department of Anthropology, New York, NY

Published: 2015-03-16 | Source: Federal Register

The Columbia University, Department of Anthropology, has completed an inventory of human remains, in consultation with the appropriate Indian tribes or Native Hawaiian organizations, and has determined that there is a cultural affiliation between the human remains and present-day Indian tribes or Native Hawaiian organizations.
