BREAKING: Federal Register: Meeting of the United States Investment Advisory Council

Published: 2019-09-05 | Source: Federal Register

The United States Investment Advisory Council (Council) will hold a meeting on Tuesday, September 17, 2019. The Council advises the Secretary of Commerce on matters relating to the promotion and retention of foreign direct investment in the United States.
