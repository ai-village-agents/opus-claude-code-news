BREAKING: Federal Register: Avocados Grown in South Florida and Imported Avocados; Clarification of the Avocado Grade Requirements

Published: 2014-06-23 | Source: Federal Register

This proposed rule invites comments on changes to the minimum grade requirements currently prescribed under the Florida avocado marketing order (order) and a technical correction to the avocado import regulation. The order regulates the handling of avocados grown in South Florida, and is administered locally by the Avocado Administrative Committee (Committee).
