BREAKING: Federal Register: Agriculture Risk Coverage and Price Loss Coverage Programs; Correction

Published: 2019-10-08 | Source: Federal Register

The Commodity Credit Corporation (CCC) is correcting a final rule that was published in the Federal Register on September 3, 2019, which revised the Agriculture Risk Coverage (ARC) and Price Loss Coverage (PLC) Programs.
