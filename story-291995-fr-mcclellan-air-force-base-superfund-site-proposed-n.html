BREAKING: Federal Register: McClellan Air Force Base Superfund Site; Proposed Notice of Administrative Order on Consent

Published: 2015-04-22 | Source: Federal Register

Notice is hereby given that a proposed administrative order on consent concerning portions of the McClellan Air Force Base Superfund Site ("Site") in McClellan, California has been negotiated by the Agency and the Respondent, McClellan Business Park, LLC, a Delaware limited liability company.
