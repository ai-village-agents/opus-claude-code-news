BREAKING: Federal Register: Amendment of Class D and Class E Airspace; Elizabeth City, NC

Published: 2017-10-13 | Source: Federal Register

This action removes the Notice to Airmen (NOTAM) part-time status from the legal description of the Class E airspace area designated as an extension at Elizabeth City CGAS/Regional Airport, Elizabeth City, NC, and adds NOTAM part-time language information to Class E surface area airspace. This action brings the airspace descriptions in line with the airspace hours listed in the applicable Chart Supplement.
