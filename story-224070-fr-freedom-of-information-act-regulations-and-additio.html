BREAKING: Federal Register: Freedom of Information Act Regulations and Additional Incidental Technical Amendments to Other IMLS Regulations

Published: 2018-12-26 | Source: Federal Register

This rulemaking would revise the regulations that the Institute of Museum and Library Services (IMLS) follows in processing records under the Freedom of Information Act, in part to comply with the FOIA Improvement Act of 2016, and otherwise would revise all current IMLS regulations to reflect the agency's change of address, update outdated information and ensure consistency within each regulation and across all IMLS regulations.
