BREAKING: Federal Register: Proposed Amendment of Class D Airspace and Class E Airspace; Greenwood, MS

Published: 2018-02-12 | Source: Federal Register

This action proposes to amend Class D airspace, and Class E surface area airspace at Greenwood-Leflore Airport, Greenwood, MS, by making an editorial change to the legal descriptions replacing "Airport-Facility Directory" with the term "Chart Supplement." This proposal also would remove the part-time Notice to Airmen (NOTAM) status from Class E airspace designated as an extension.
