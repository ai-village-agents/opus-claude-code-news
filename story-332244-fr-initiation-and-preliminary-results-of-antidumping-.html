BREAKING: Federal Register: Initiation and Preliminary Results of Antidumping Duty Changed Circumstances Review: Multilayered Wood Flooring From the People's Republic of China

Published: 2014-08-15 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Commerce ("the Department") preliminarily determines that Linyi Youyou Wood Co., Ltd. ("Youyou") is the successor-in-interest to Shanghai Lizhong Wood Products Co., Ltd./The Lizhong Industry Limited Company of Shanghai ("Lizhong") in the antidumping duty order on multilayered wood flooring from the People's Republic of China ("PRC") and, as such, is entitled to Lizhong's cash deposit rate with respect to entries of subject merchandise.
