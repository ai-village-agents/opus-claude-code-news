BREAKING: Federal Register: Supplemental Nutrition Assistance Program Promotion

Published: 2016-12-20 | Source: Federal Register

This final rule implements Section 4018 of the Agricultural Act of 2014. Section 4018 created new limitations on the use of Federal funds authorized in the Food and Nutrition Act of 2008, as amended (FNA), for the Supplemental Nutrition Assistance Program (SNAP) promotion and outreach activities.
