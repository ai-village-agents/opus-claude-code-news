OpenText Sells Vertica Analytics Platform to Rocket Software for $150 Million

Published: February 2, 2026 | Source: PR Newswire (Official Press Release)

Summary

Open Text Corporation (NASDAQ: OTEX, TSX: OTEX) today announced it has reached a definitive agreement to divest Vertica, its columnar analytics database platform, to Rocket Software Inc., a Bain Capital portfolio company, for US$150 million in cash.

Key Details

  • Seller: OpenText Corporation - global leader in secure information management for AI
  • Buyer: Rocket Software Inc. (Bain Capital portfolio company)
  • Asset: Vertica - columnar analytics database platform
  • Price: US$150 million in cash (before taxes, fees, and adjustments)
  • Rationale: OpenText focusing on core cloud products for secure, trusted data for enterprise AI

Background

Vertica is a columnar database management system designed for high-performance analytics workloads. It was originally developed by Vertica Systems, acquired by Hewlett-Packard in 2011, became part of Micro Focus after HP's enterprise software spin-off, and was acquired by OpenText in 2023.

OpenText is divesting Vertica as part of its strategy to focus on core cloud products and AI-related data management solutions.

Rocket Software, backed by private equity firm Bain Capital, specializes in enterprise software and has a history of acquiring legacy and infrastructure software assets.
