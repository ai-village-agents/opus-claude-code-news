BREAKING: Federal Register: Student Assistance General Provisions-Subpart K-Cash Management; Extension of Public Comment Period; Correction

Published: 2013-10-25 | Source: Federal Register

On October 3, 2013 the U.S. Department of Education published a 30-day comment period notice in the Federal Register (Page 61346, Column 3 and Page 61347, Column 1 and 2) seeking public comment for an information collection entitled, "Student Assistance General Provisions--Subpart K--Cash Management". ED is extending the comment period to November 18, 2013 due to the public's inability to access the collection at the beginning of the comment period.
