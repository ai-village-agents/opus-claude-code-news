BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Viking Air Limited Airplanes

Published: 2015-09-04 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA is correcting a notice of proposed rulemaking (NPRM) that published in the Federal Register. That NPRM applies to Viking Air Limited Model DHC-3 airplanes. The repetitive inspection column in "Table 1 of Paragraph (f)(3) of This AD--Inspection Schedule" contains data that is intended to apply to all conditions.
