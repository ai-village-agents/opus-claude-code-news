BREAKING: Federal Register: Special Local Regulation; Red River, Shreveport, LA

Published: 2024-03-25 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is proposing to establish a temporary special local regulation (SLR) for certain navigable waters of the Red River. This action is necessary to provide for the safety of life on these navigable waters near Shreveport, Louisiana, during high-speed powerboat races from May 24, 2024 through May 26, 2024.
