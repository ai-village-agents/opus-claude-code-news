BREAKING: Federal Register: Importation of Fresh Pomegranate Fruit From Turkey Into the Continental United States

Published: 2017-06-21 | Source: Federal Register

We are proposing to amend the fruits and vegetables regulations to allow the importation of commercial consignments into the continental United States of fresh pomegranate fruit from Turkey.
