BREAKING: Federal Register: Exelon Generation Company, LLC., Nine Mile Point Nuclear Station, Units 1 and 2

Published: 2014-10-27 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Nuclear Regulatory Commission (NRC) is considering issuance of an amendment to Facility Operating License Nos. DPR-63 and DPF-69, issued to Exelon Generation Company, (the licensee) LLC., for operation of the Nine Mile Point Nuclear Station, Units 1 and 2, located in Oswego County, New York.
