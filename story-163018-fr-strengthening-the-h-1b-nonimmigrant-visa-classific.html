BREAKING: Federal Register: Strengthening the H-1B Nonimmigrant Visa Classification Program

Published: 2020-10-08 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Homeland Security (DHS or the Department), is amending certain DHS regulations governing the H-1B nonimmigrant visa program. Specifically, DHS is: Revising the regulatory definition of and standards for a "specialty occupation" to better align with the statutory definition of the term; adding definitions for "worksite" and "third-party worksite"; revising the definition of "United States employer"; clarifying how U.S. Citizenship and Immigration Services (USCIS) will determine ...
