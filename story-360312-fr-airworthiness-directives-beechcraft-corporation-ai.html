BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Beechcraft Corporation Airplanes

Published: 2013-07-17 | Source: Federal Register

We propose to supersede airworthiness directive (AD) 2011-27- 51 that applies to certain Beechcraft Corporation Models 1900, 1900C, and 1900D airplanes. AD 2011-27-51 currently requires inspecting the elevator bob-weight and attaching linkage for correct installation and for damage or deformation to the weight and/or weight bracket with corrective action as necessary.
