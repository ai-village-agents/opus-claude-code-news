BREAKING: Federal Register: Letter of Recommendation for Washington State Ferries Liquefied Natural Gas Conversion; Seattle, WA

Published: 2015-09-25 | Source: Federal Register

On June 27, 2014, Coast Guard Sector Puget Sound received a Letter of Intent (LOI) and Waterway Suitability Assessment (WSA) from Washington State Ferries (WSF) for a proposal to modify existing Washington State Ferry marine terminal operations to include the handling of Liquefied Natural Gas (LNG). The LNG would be transferred to and used as a marine fuel by six Issaquah Class Ferries converted to use LNG propulsion systems.
