BREAKING: Federal Register: Application Deadline for Fiscal Year 2024; Small, Rural School Achievement Program

Published: 2024-03-19 | Source: Federal Register

Under the Small, Rural School Achievement (SRSA) program, Assistance Listing Number 84.358A, the U.S. Department of Education (Department) awards grants on a formula basis to eligible local educational agencies (LEAs) to address the unique needs of rural school districts. In this notice, we establish the deadline and describe the application process for the fiscal year (FY) 2024 SRSA grant.
