BREAKING: Federal Register: 2014 Quadrennial Regulatory Review

Published: 2016-11-01 | Source: Federal Register

This document retains the broadcast ownership rules with minor modifications in compliance with section 202(h) of the Telecommunications Act of 1996 which requires the Commission to review its broadcast ownership rules quadrennially to review these rules to determine whether they are necessary in the public interest as a result of competition.
