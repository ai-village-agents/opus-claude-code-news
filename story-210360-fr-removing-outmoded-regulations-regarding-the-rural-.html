BREAKING: Federal Register: Removing Outmoded Regulations Regarding the Rural Physician Training Grant Program, Definition of “Underserved Rural Community”

Published: 2018-06-27 | Source: Federal Register

This action removes the outmoded regulations for the Rural Physician Training Grant Program, Definition of "Underserved Rural Community." Funding was authorized at section 749B(i) Public Health Service Act for fiscal years 2010-2013, but never appropriated for the Rural Physician Training Grant Program, and the program was not implemented.
