BREAKING: Federal Register: January 2012 Pay Schedules

Published: 2012-02-27 | Source: Federal Register

The President has signed an Executive order containing the 2012 pay schedules for certain Federal civilian employees. The rates of pay for these employees will not be increased in 2012 and remain at 2010/2011 levels, except for employees in nonforeign areas. This notice serves as documentation for the public record.
