BREAKING: Federal Register: Special Local Regulation; Chesapeake Bay, Between Sandy Point and Kent Island, MD

Published: 2019-04-01 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is establishing temporary special local regulations for certain navigable waters of the Chesapeake Bay. This action is necessary to provide for the safety of life on these waters located between Sandy Point, Anne Arundel County, MD, and Kent Island, Queen Anne's County, MD, on June 1, 2019, during a paddling event.
