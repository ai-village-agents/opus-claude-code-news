BREAKING: Federal Register: Importer of Controlled Substances Registration: STEPAN COMPANY

Published: 2014-06-17 | Source: Federal Register

Stepan Company applied to be registered as an importer of a basic class of a narcotic controlled substance. The DEA grants Stepan Company registration as an importer of this controlled substance.
