BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Proposed Withdrawal Extension and Opportunity for Public Meeting; OR

Published: 2012-10-31 | Source: Federal Register

The Assistant Secretary of the Interior for Policy, Management and Budget proposes to extend the duration of Public Land Order (PLO) No. 6963 for an additional 20-year term. PLO No. 6963 withdrew 257.60 acres of public land from settlement, sale, location, or entry under the general land laws, including the United States mining laws, but not from leasing under the mineral leasing laws, to protect the natural values of the Florence Sand Dunes (FSD).
