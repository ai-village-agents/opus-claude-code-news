BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Intended Repatriation: Missouri Historical Society, St. Louis, MO

Published: 2025-12-17 | Source: Federal Register

In accordance with the Native American Graves Protection and Repatriation Act (NAGPRA), the Missouri Historical Society (MHS) intends to repatriate certain cultural items that meet the definition of objects of cultural patrimony and that have a cultural affiliation with the Indian Tribes or Native Hawaiian organizations in this notice.
