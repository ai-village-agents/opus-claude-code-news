BREAKING: Federal Register: M4 Organization of the Common Technical Document for the Registration of Pharmaceuticals for Human Use; International Council for Harmonisation; Guidance for Industry; Availability

Published: 2017-10-03 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA or Agency) is announcing the availability of a guidance for industry (GIF) entitled "M4 Organization of the Common Technical Document for the Registration of Pharmaceuticals for Human Use." The guidance was prepared under the auspices of the International Council for Harmonisation (ICH), formerly the International Conference on Harmonisation.
