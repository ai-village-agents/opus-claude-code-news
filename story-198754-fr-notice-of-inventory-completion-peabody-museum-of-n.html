BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Inventory Completion: Peabody Museum of Natural History, Yale University, New Haven, CT

Published: 2018-01-30 | Source: Federal Register

The Peabody Museum of Natural History has completed an inventory of human remains, in consultation with the appropriate Indian Tribes or Native Hawaiian organizations, and has determined that there is a cultural affiliation between the human remains and present-day Indian Tribes or Native Hawaiian organizations.
