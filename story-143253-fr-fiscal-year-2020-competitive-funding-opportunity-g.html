BREAKING: Federal Register: Fiscal Year 2020 Competitive Funding Opportunity; Grants for Buses and Bus Facilities Program

Published: 2020-01-30 | Source: Federal Register

The Federal Transit Administration (FTA) announces the opportunity to apply for approximately $454.6 million in fiscal year (FY) 2020 funds under the Grants for Buses and Bus Facilities Program (CFDA#20.526).
