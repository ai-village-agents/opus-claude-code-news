BREAKING: Federal Register: Availability of Seats for National Marine Sanctuary Advisory Councils

Published: 2014-02-14 | Source: Federal Register

ONMS is seeking applications for vacant seats for 7 of its 13 national marine sanctuary advisory councils (advisory councils).
