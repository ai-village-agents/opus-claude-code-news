BREAKING: Federal Register: Privacy Act of 1974; Systems of Records

Published: 2025-05-21 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Small Business Administration (SBA) proposes a modified system of records, Personnel Security Files (SBA 24), to update its inventory of records systems subject to the Privacy Act of 1974, as amended. Publication of this notice complies with the Privacy Act and the Office of Management and Budget (OMB) Circular A-108 and Circular A-130.
