BREAKING: Federal Register: Proposed Prospective Purchaser Agreement for the PCC Validation Site in Pontiac, Oakland County, Michigan

Published: 2014-01-23 | Source: Federal Register

In accordance with the Prospective Purchaser Agreement, notice is hereby given of a proposed administrative settlement concerning the PCC Validation Site in Pontiac, Oakland County, Michigan with the following settling party: M1 Concourse, LLC. The settlement requires the Settling Party to execute and record a Declaration of Restrictive Covenant; provide access to the Property and exercise due care with respect to existing contamination.
