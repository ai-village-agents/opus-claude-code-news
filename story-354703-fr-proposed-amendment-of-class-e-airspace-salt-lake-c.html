BREAKING: Federal Register: Proposed Amendment of Class E Airspace; Salt Lake City, UT

Published: 2013-05-13 | Source: Federal Register

This action proposes to modify Class E airspace at Salt Lake City International Airport, Salt Lake City, UT. Controlled airspace is necessary to accommodate aircraft using Area Navigation (RNAV) Global Positioning System (GPS) and Instrument Landing System (ILS) or Localizer (LOC) standard instrument approach procedures at the airport.
