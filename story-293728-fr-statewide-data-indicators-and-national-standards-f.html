BREAKING: Federal Register: Statewide Data Indicators and National Standards for Child and Family Services Reviews

Published: 2015-05-13 | Source: Federal Register

On October 10, 2014, the Administration of Children and Families (ACF) published a document in the Federal Register (79 FR 61241). The document provided CB's final plan to replace the statewide data indicators used to determine a state's substantial conformity with titles IV-B and IV-E of the Social Security Act through the Child and Family Services Reviews (CFSRs).
