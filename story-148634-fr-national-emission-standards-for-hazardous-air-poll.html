BREAKING: Federal Register: National Emission Standards for Hazardous Air Pollutants: Phosphoric Acid Manufacturing

Published: 2020-04-07 | Source: Federal Register

This action proposes to amend the National Emission Standards for Hazardous Air Pollutants (NESHAP) for the Phosphoric Acid Manufacturing source category. The proposed amendment is in response to a petition for rulemaking by an industry stakeholder on the mercury emission limit based on the maximum achievable control technology (MACT) floor for existing sources set in a rule that was finalized on August 19, 2015 ("2015 Rule").
