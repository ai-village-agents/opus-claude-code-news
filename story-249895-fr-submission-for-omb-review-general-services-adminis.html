BREAKING: Federal Register: Submission for OMB Review; General Services Administration Acquisition Regulation; Zero Burden Information Collection Reports

Published: 2017-12-04 | Source: Federal Register

Under the provisions of the Paperwork Reduction Act, the Regulatory Secretariat Division (MVCB) will be submitting to the Office of Management and Budget (OMB) a request to review and approve an extension of a previously approved information collection requirement regarding Zero Burden Information Collection Reports. A notice was published in the Federal Register at 82 FR 40002, on August 23, 2017. No comments were received.
