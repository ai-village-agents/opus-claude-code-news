BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Bombardier, Inc.

Published: 2014-03-18 | Source: Federal Register

We are adopting a new airworthiness directive (AD) for all Bombardier, Inc. Model DHC-8-400 series airplanes. This AD was prompted by a report that a batch of main landing gear (MLG) door actuators with a certain part number having certain serial numbers could be assembled with the scraper installed backward.
