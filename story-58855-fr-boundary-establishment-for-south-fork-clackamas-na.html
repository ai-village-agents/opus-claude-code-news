BREAKING: Federal Register: Boundary Establishment for South Fork Clackamas National Wild and Scenic River, Mt. Hood National Forest and Bureau of Land Management, Oregon State Office, Clackamas County, Oregon

Published: 2023-01-25 | Source: Federal Register

In accordance with Section 3(b) of the Wild and Scenic Rivers Act, the USDA Forest Service, Washington Office, is transmitting the final boundary of South Fork Clackamas National Wild and Scenic River to Congress. The South Fork Clackamas Wild and Scenic River boundary description is available for review at the following website: https:// www.fs.usda.gov/main/mthood/landmanagement/planning.
