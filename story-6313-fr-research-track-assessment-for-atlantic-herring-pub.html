BREAKING: Federal Register: Research Track Assessment for Atlantic Herring; Public Meeting

Published: 2025-03-03 | Source: Federal Register

NMFS will convene the Research Track Assessment Peer Review Meeting for the purpose of reviewing the Atlantic herring stock. The Research Track Assessment Peer Review is a formal scientific peer- review process for evaluating and presenting stock assessment results to managers for fish stocks in the offshore U.S. waters of the northwest Atlantic.
