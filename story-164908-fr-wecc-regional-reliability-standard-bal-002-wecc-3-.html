BREAKING: Federal Register: WECC Regional Reliability Standard BAL-002-WECC-3 (Contingency Reserve)

Published: 2020-10-30 | Source: Federal Register

The Federal Energy Regulatory Commission (Commission) proposes to approve regional Reliability Standard BAL-002-WECC-3 (Contingency Reserve) submitted jointly by the North American Electric Reliability Corporation (NERC), the Commission-certified Electric Reliability Organization, and the Western Electricity Coordinating Council (WECC). In addition, the Commission proposes to direct NERC and WECC to submit an informational filing.
