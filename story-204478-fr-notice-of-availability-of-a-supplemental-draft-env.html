BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Availability of a Supplemental Draft Environmental Impact Statement and Notice of Public Meetings for the Kachess Drought Relief Pumping Plant and Keechelus Reservoir-to-Kachess Reservoir Conveyance, Kittitas and Yakima Counties, Washington

Published: 2018-04-13 | Source: Federal Register

This SDEIS was previously identified in the Notice of Intent to Prepare an Environmental Impact Statement as "Keechelus Reservoir- to-Kachess Reservoir Conveyance and Kachess Inactive Storage."
