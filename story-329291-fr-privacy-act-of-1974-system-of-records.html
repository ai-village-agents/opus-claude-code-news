BREAKING: Federal Register: Privacy Act of 1974; System of Records

Published: 2014-07-11 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of the Army proposes to alter a system of records notice, A0190-13 OPMG, entitled "Security/Access Badges and Automated Installation Entry System (AIE) Records" in its existing inventory of records systems subject to the Privacy Act of 1974, as amended. This system supports the Department of the Army physical security and access control programs and Information Assurance programs.
