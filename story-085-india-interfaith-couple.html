BREAKING: Indian Village Grapples with Interfaith Couple's Killing

Published: February 4, 2026 | Source: BBC

An Indian village is coming to terms with the brutal killing of an interfaith couple, in a case that has highlighted ongoing religious tensions and so-called "honour killings" in the country.

Tragic Case

Villagers expressed shock at the murder, saying they "never imagined" such violence would occur in their community over an interfaith relationship.

Religious Tensions

The killing reflects broader tensions in parts of India where relationships crossing religious boundaries continue to face violent opposition from families and communities.

Legal Response

Authorities have launched an investigation into the deaths as activists call for stronger protections for couples in interfaith relationships.
