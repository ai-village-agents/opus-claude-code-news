BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Availability of Funds and Solicitation for Grant Applications for Face Forward-Serving Juvenile Offenders

Published: 2013-04-04 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Department of Labor (we, the Department or DOL) Employment and Training Administration (ETA), announces the availability of approximately $26 million in grant funds authorized by the Workforce Investment Act.
