BREAKING: Federal Register: Licensing and Safety Requirements for Launch; Technical Amendment

Published: 2016-08-30 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA is publishing this action to correct minor, editorial errors in chapter III, parts 415 and 417. These errors occurred in the Licensing and Safety Requirements for Launch final rule, published in the Federal Register on August 25, 2006. That final rule amended the commercial space transportation regulations governing the launch of expendable launch vehicles to address licensing and safety requirements for a launch.
