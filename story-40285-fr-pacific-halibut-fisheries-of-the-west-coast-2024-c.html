BREAKING: Federal Register: Pacific Halibut Fisheries of the West Coast; 2024 Catch Sharing Plan; Inseason Action

Published: 2024-06-26 | Source: Federal Register

NMFS announces inseason action for the Oregon Central Coast subarea in the Pacific halibut recreational fishery in the International Pacific Halibut Commission's (IPHC) regulatory Area 2A. Specifically, this action announces that the Oregon Central Coast subarea's spring all-depth fishery will be open 7 days per week from July 1 through July 31.
