BREAKING: Federal Register: Elimination of Obligation To File Broadcast Mid-Term Report

Published: 2019-05-15 | Source: Federal Register

In this document, the Federal Communications Commission (FCC or Commission) eliminates a requirement of our rules that oblige certain broadcast television and radio stations to file the FCC Broadcast Mid-Term Report (Form 397). This requirement has become redundant now that most of the information that the form requests is readily accessible online via the Commission's Online Public Inspection File (Public File).
