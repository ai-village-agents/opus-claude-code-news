BREAKING: Federal Register: ALP Express Pilot to Permanent Status

Published: 2024-12-18 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Small Business Administration ("SBA" or Agency) is making permanent the increased delegated authorities made available under the ALP Express Pilot for Certified Development Companies ("CDCs") approved for the Accredited Lenders Program ("ALP"). These increased delegated authorities for 504 loans of $500,000 or less ("ALP Express authority") were authorized under the Economic Aid to Hard-hit Small Businesses, Nonprofits, and Venues Act (Economic Aid Act or EAA).
