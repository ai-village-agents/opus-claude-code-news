BREAKING: Federal Register: Enhancements to Federal Reserve Bank Same-Day ACH Service

Published: 2015-09-28 | Source: Federal Register

The Board of Governors (Board) has approved enhancements to the Federal Reserve Banks' (Reserve Banks) same-day automated clearing house (ACH) service. The enhancements require receiving depository financial institutions (RDFIs) to participate in the service and originating depository financial institutions (ODFIs) to pay a fee to RDFIs for each same-day ACH forward transaction.
