BREAKING: Federal Register: 2014 Farm Bill Implementation Listening Session-Strategic Economic and Community Development

Published: 2014-05-27 | Source: Federal Register

As part of our implementation of the Agricultural Act of 2014 (commonly referred to as the 2014 Farm Bill), Rural Development is hosting a listening session for public input about the Strategic Economic and Community Development priority funding to be made available for certain Rural Development programs. The 2014 Farm Bill contains a provision outlining the details of this priority funding in Section 6025. The listening session will provide an opportunity for stakeholders to voice their comment...
