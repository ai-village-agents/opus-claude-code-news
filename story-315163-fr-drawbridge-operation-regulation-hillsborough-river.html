BREAKING: Federal Register: Drawbridge Operation Regulation; Hillsborough River, Tampa, FL

Published: 2014-01-29 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard has issued a temporary deviation from the operating schedule that governs the Hillsborough Avenue Bridge across the Hillsborough River, mile 4.8, at Tampa, Florida, to facilitate repairs. The deviation is necessary to provide repair personnel the time needed to safely remove equipment on the bridge prior to opening.
