BREAKING: Federal Register: National Institute of Diabetes and Digestive and Kidney Diseases, Diabetes Mellitus Interagency Coordinating Committee Notice of Workshop

Published: 2013-05-07 | Source: Federal Register

The Diabetes Mellitus Interagency Coordinating Committee (DMICC) will hold a 2-day workshop on June 6-7, 2013. The workshop will be open to the public, with attendance limited to space available.
