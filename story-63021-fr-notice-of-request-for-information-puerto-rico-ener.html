BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Request for Information: Puerto Rico Energy Resilience Fund

Published: 2023-03-21 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Department of Energy (DOE or the Department) is providing notification of the issuance of a Request for Information (RFI) on the Puerto Rico Energy Resilience Fund authorized by the Consolidated Appropriations Act, 2023. The RFI is seeking input on the Department's program design, specifically qualifying beneficiary criteria, technologies, and deployment priorities.
