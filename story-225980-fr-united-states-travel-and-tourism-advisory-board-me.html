BREAKING: Federal Register: United States Travel and Tourism Advisory Board: Meeting of the United States Travel and Tourism Advisory Board

Published: 2017-01-19 | Source: Federal Register

The United States Travel and Tourism Advisory Board (Board) will hold an open meeting via teleconference on Wednesday, February 1, 2017. The Board was re-chartered in August 2015 and advises the Secretary of Commerce on matters relating to the U.S. travel and tourism industry. The purpose of the meeting is for Board members to deliberate a letter to the Secretary with an overview of the Board and its activities to date under the current charter term.
