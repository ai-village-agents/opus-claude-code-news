BREAKING: Federal Register: United States Savings Bonds and Notes; Payments

Published: 2012-03-20 | Source: Federal Register

Treasury is changing the procedures for financial institutions to transmit and receive settlement for redeemed definitive (paper) savings securities (savings bonds and savings notes) from the EZ Clear system to an image-based securities process through the Federal Reserve. By changing the procedures, Treasury will reduce costs and provide for a more efficient method of processing savings securities through the Federal Reserve.
