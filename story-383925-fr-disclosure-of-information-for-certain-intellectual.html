BREAKING: Federal Register: Disclosure of Information for Certain Intellectual Property Rights Enforced at the Border

Published: 2012-04-24 | Source: Federal Register

This document amends, on an interim basis, the U.S. Customs and Border Protection (CBP) regulations pertaining to importations of merchandise bearing recorded trademarks or recorded trade names.
