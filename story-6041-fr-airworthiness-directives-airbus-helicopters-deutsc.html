BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Airbus Helicopters Deutschland GmbH Helicopters

Published: 2025-02-26 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA proposes to adopt a new airworthiness directive (AD) for all Airbus Helicopters Deutschland GmbH Model MBB-BK 117 C-2 and MBB-BK 117 D-2 helicopters. This proposed AD was prompted by reports of significant wear of the control rod assembly and pitch link assembly spherical bearings.
