BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Determinations; Culturally Significant Objects Imported for Exhibition-Determinations: “Mapa Wiya (Your Map's Not Needed): Australian Aboriginal Art From the Fondation Opale” Exhibition

Notice is hereby given of the following determinations: I hereby determine that certain objects to be included in the exhibition "Mapa Wiya (Your Map's Not Needed): Australian Aboriginal Art from the Fondation Opale," imported from abroad for temporary exhibition within the United States, are of cultural significance.
