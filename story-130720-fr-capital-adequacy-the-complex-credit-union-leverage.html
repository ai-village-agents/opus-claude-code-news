BREAKING: Federal Register: Capital Adequacy: The Complex Credit Union Leverage Ratio; Risk-Based Capital

Published: 2021-08-16 | Source: Federal Register

The NCUA is seeking comment on a proposed rule that would provide a simplified measure of capital adequacy for federally insured, natural-person credit unions (credit unions) classified as complex (those with total assets greater than $500 million). Under the proposed rule, a complex credit union that maintains a minimum net worth ratio, and that meets other qualifying criteria, will be eligible to opt into the complex credit union leverage ratio (CCULR) framework.
