BREAKING: Federal Register: Natural Resource Damages for Hazardous Substances

Published: 2024-01-05 | Source: Federal Register

The Office of Restoration and Damage Assessment is seeking comments and suggestions from State, Tribal, and Federal natural resource co-trustees, other affected parties, and the interested public on revising the simplified Type A procedures in the regulations for conducting natural resource damage assessment and restoration for hazardous substance releases.
