BREAKING: Federal Register: Importation of Tomatoes From the Economic Community of West African States Into the Continental United States

Published: 2012-06-12 | Source: Federal Register

We are amending the fruits and vegetables regulations to allow the importation of tomatoes from the member States of the Economic Community of West African States (ECOWAS) into the continental United States. As a condition of entry, tomatoes from the ECOWAS will be subject to a systems approach that includes requirements for pest exclusion at the production site, fruit fly trapping and monitoring, and procedures for packing the tomatoes.
