BREAKING: Federal Register: Presumptive Service Connection for Rare Respiratory Cancers Due to Exposure to Fine Particulate Matter

Published: 2023-11-03 | Source: Federal Register

This rulemaking adopts as final, without changes, an interim final rule amending the Department of Veterans Affairs (VA) adjudication regulations to establish presumptive service connection for nine rare respiratory cancers in association with presumed exposure to fine particulate matter.
