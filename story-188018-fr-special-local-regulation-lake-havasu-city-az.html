BREAKING: Federal Register: Special Local Regulation; Lake Havasu City, AZ

Published: 2019-09-05 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is establishing a temporary special local regulation for the Havapalooza 2019 marine event that will be held on the navigable waters of Lake Havasu, Lake Havasu City, AZ. This action is necessary to provide for the safety of life on these navigable waters on Thompson Bay in Lake Havasu during a waterside concert on September 14, 2019.
