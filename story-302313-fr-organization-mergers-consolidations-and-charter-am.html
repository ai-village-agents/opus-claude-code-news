BREAKING: Federal Register: Organization; Mergers, Consolidations, and Charter Amendments of Banks or Associations

Published: 2015-08-24 | Source: Federal Register

The Farm Credit Administration (FCA, Agency, we, or our) amends existing regulations related to mergers and consolidations of Farm Credit System (FCS or System) banks and associations to clarify the merger review and approval process and incorporate existing practices in the regulations.
