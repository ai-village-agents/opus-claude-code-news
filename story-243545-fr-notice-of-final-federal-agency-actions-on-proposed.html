BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Final Federal Agency Actions on Proposed Transportation Project in Florida

Published: 2017-09-13 | Source: Federal Register

These actions relate to a proposed highway project, the State Road (SR) 7 extension from SR 704/Okeechobee Boulevard to County Road (CR) 809/North Lake Boulevard, Federal Project No: 4752-030-P, in Palm Beach County, State of Florida.
