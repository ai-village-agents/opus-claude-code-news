BREAKING: Federal Register: Short-Term Investment Funds

Published: 2012-04-09 | Source: Federal Register

The OCC is requesting comment on a proposal that would revise the requirements imposed on banks pursuant to 12 CFR 9.18(b)(4)(ii)(B), the short-term investment fund (STIF) rule (STIF Rule).
