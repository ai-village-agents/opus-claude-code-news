BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Receipt of Requests for Amendments To Delete Uses in Certain Pesticide Registrations

Published: 2013-04-03 | Source: Federal Register

In accordance with the Federal Insecticide, Fungicide, and Rodenticide Act (FIFRA), EPA is issuing a notice of receipt of request for amendments by registrants to delete uses in certain pesticide registrations. FIFRA provides that a registrant of a pesticide product may at any time request that any of its pesticide registrations be amended to delete one or more uses.
