BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone; Mississippi River Between Mile 488.0 and 480.5; Lake Providence, LA

Published: 2015-10-22 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is establishing an emergency temporary safety zone for all waters of the Lower Mississippi River between mile 488.0 and 480.5. Transit into and through this area is prohibited beginning at 5:43 p.m. on September 9, 2015 and will continue through October 31, 2015 or until the width and depth of the navigational channel improves.
