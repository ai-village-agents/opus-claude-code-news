BREAKING: Federal Register: Release of Policy Assessment for the Ozone National Ambient Air Quality Standards

Published: 2020-05-22 | Source: Federal Register

On or about May 29, 2020, the Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) will make available the document, Policy Assessment for the Ozone National Ambient Air Quality Standards (PA, EPA-452/R-20-001). This document was prepared as part of the current review of the national ambient air quality standards (NAAQS) for photochemical oxidants including ozone (O3).
