BREAKING: Federal Register: List of Approved Spent Fuel Storage Casks: TN Americas LLC, NUHOMS® EOS Dry Spent Fuel Storage System, Certificate of Compliance No. 1042

Published: 2017-03-24 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Nuclear Regulatory Commission (NRC) is amending its spent fuel storage regulations by adding the TN Americas LLC (TN Americas), NUHOMS[supreg] Extended Optimized Storage (EOS) Dry Spent Fuel Storage System, to the "List of approved spent fuel storage casks" as Certificate of Compliance (CoC) No. 1042.
