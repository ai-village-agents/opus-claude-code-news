BREAKING: Federal Register: Emergency Clearance: Public Information Collection Requirements Submitted to the Office of Management and Budget (OMB)

Published: 2020-07-21 | Source: Federal Register

The Centers for Medicare and Medicaid Services (CMS) is requesting that a new information collection request (ICR) related to the Medicare Current Beneficiary Survey (MCBS) (OMB clearance 0938- 0568) be processed under the emergency clearance process. Due to CMS' determination that this collection of information is needed prior to the expiration of time periods established under its regulations, an emergency clearance is requested.
