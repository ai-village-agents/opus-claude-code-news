BREAKING: Federal Register: Proposed Settlement Agreement, Clean Air Act Petition for Review; Reopening of Comment Period

Published: 2017-06-13 | Source: Federal Register

EPA issued a notice in the Federal Register on January 19, 2017, soliciting comments on the proposed settlement agreement in Sierra Club v. EPA, No. 16-1158 (D.C. Cir.). The proposed settlement agreement would resolve Sierra Club's lawsuit challenging EPA's final action titled "Revisions to Ambient Monitoring Quality Assurance and Other Requirements" upon EPA's sending of two nonbinding guidance documents (described in the proposed settlement agreement) recommending public notification practices...
