BREAKING: Federal Register: Concept Release on Risk Controls and System Safeguards for Automated Trading Environments

Published: 2013-09-12 | Source: Federal Register

U.S. derivatives markets have experienced a fundamental transition from human-centered trading venues to highly automated and interconnected trading environments. The operational centers of modern markets now reside in a combination of automated trading systems ("ATSs") and electronic trading platforms that can execute repetitive tasks at speeds orders of magnitude greater than any human equivalent.
