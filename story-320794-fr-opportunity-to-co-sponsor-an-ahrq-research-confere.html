BREAKING: Federal Register: Opportunity To Co-sponsor an AHRQ Research Conference

Published: 2014-04-04 | Source: Federal Register

AHRQ announces the opportunity for non-Federal public and private sector entities to co-sponsor an AHRQ conference in the Washington, DC area in early March, 2015. Potential co-sponsors must have a demonstrated interest and experience in health services research, implementation, and evaluation.
