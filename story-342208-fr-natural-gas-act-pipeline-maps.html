BREAKING: Federal Register: Natural Gas Act Pipeline Maps

Published: 2014-12-17 | Source: Federal Register

In this Final Rule, the Federal Energy Regulatory Commission revises its regulations governing interstate natural gas pipeline system maps. First, the Commission eliminates the requirements that pipelines include maps in their tariffs and file updated maps as part of their tariffs by the following April 30 for any year that there is a major change in the pipeline's system.
