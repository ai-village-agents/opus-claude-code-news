BREAKING: Federal Register: Record of Decision for General Management Plan, Golden Gate National Recreation Area, California

Published: 2015-05-21 | Source: Federal Register

The National Park Service, has prepared and approved a Record of Decision for the Final Environmental Impact Statement for the new General Management Plan (GMP) for Golden Gate National Recreation Area. Approval of the GMP concludes a very extensive public engagement and conservation planning and environmental impact analysis effort that began during 2006.
