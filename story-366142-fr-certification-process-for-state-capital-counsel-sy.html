BREAKING: Federal Register: Certification Process for State Capital Counsel System

Published: 2013-09-23 | Source: Federal Register

Chapter 154 of title 28, United States Code, provides special procedures for Federal habeas corpus review of cases brought by indigent prisoners in State custody who are subject to a capital sentence.
