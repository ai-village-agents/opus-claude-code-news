BREAKING: Federal Register: Request for Information (RFI): Developing the National Public Health Strategy for the Prevention and Control of Vector-Borne Diseases in Humans

Published: 2021-04-27 | Source: Federal Register

The development of a national strategy on vector-borne diseases including tickborne diseases was mandated by Congress. To inform development of the national strategy to address vector-borne diseases, HHS is issuing this Request for Information (RFI).
