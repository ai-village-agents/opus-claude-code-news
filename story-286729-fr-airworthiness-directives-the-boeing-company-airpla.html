BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; The Boeing Company Airplanes

Published: 2015-02-18 | Source: Federal Register

We propose to supersede Airworthiness Directive (AD) 2012-24- 10, which applies to certain The Boeing Company Model 747-400 and -400F series airplanes. AD 2012-24-10 currently requires installing new software, replacing the duct assembly with a new duct assembly, making wiring changes, and routing certain wire bundles.
