BREAKING: Federal Register: Agency Response to Public Comments of Safety Measurement System Changes

Published: 2012-08-28 | Source: Federal Register

The Federal Motor Carrier Safety Administration (FMCSA) announces changes to the Carrier Safety Measurement System (SMS). A preview of the original improvements became available to motor carriers and law enforcement on March 27, 2012, and will remain available until the SMS changes become operational. The SMS improvements are now scheduled to be operational in December 2012. Comments to the preview were reviewed and considered.
