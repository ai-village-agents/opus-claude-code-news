BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Proposed Reinstatement of Terminated Oil and Gas Lease WYW180328, Wyoming

Published: 2013-01-11 | Source: Federal Register

Under the provisions of the Mineral Leasing Act of 1920, as amended, the Bureau of Land Management (BLM) received a petition for reinstatement from Fidelity Exploration & Production Company and Finley Resources, Inc., for noncompetitive oil and gas lease WYW180328 for lands in Sweetwater County, Wyoming. The petition was filed on time and was accompanied by all the rentals due since the date the lease terminated under the law.
