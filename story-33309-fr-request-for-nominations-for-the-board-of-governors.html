BREAKING: Federal Register: Request for Nominations for the Board of Governors of the Patient-Centered Outcomes Research Institute (PCORI)

Published: 2024-04-01 | Source: Federal Register

The Patient Protection and Affordable Care Act gave the Comptroller General of the United States responsibility for appointing up to 21 members to the Board of Governors of the Patient-Centered Outcomes Research Institute. In addition, the Directors of the Agency for Healthcare Research and Quality and the National Institutes of Health, or their designees, are members of the Board.
