BREAKING: Federal Register: Revisions and Clarifications in Requirements for the Processing of Donated Foods

Published: 2018-05-01 | Source: Federal Register

This rule revises and clarifies requirements for the processing of donated foods in order to: Incorporate successful processing options tested in demonstration projects into the regulations, ensure accountability for donated foods provided for processing, increase program efficiency and integrity, and support vendor and State operability.
