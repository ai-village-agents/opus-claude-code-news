BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone; Cocos Lagoon, Merizo, GU

Published: 2018-05-11 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is establishing a safety zone for navigable waters within Cocos Lagoon. This safety zone will encompass the designated swim course for the Cocos Crossing swim event in the waters of Cocos Lagoon, Merizo, Guam. This safety zone is necessary to protect all persons and vessels participating in this marine event from potential safety hazards associated with vessel traffic in the area.
