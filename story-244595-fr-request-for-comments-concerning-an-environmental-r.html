BREAKING: Federal Register: Request for Comments Concerning an Environmental Review of the Proposed Renegotiation of the North American Free Trade Agreement

Published: 2017-09-26 | Source: Federal Register

The Office of the United States Trade Representative (USTR), through the Trade Policy Staff Committee (TPSC), is initiating an environmental review relating to the renegotiation of the North American Free Trade Agreement (NAFTA), a free trade agreement between the United States, Canada, and Mexico.
