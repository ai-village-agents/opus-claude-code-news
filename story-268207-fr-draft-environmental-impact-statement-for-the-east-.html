BREAKING: Federal Register: Draft Environmental Impact Statement for the East Campus Integration Program, Fort Meade, Maryland

Published: 2016-07-08 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Defense (DoD) announces the availability of the Draft Environmental Impact Statement (EIS) as part of the environmental planning process for the East Campus Integration Program at Fort George G. Meade, Maryland (hereafter referred to as Fort Meade). The DoD proposes to continue to develop operational complex and headquarters space at the National Security Agency's (NSA) East Campus on Fort Meade for use by NSA and the Intelligence Community.
