BREAKING: Federal Register: Revocation of AmSpec LLC (Christiansted, St. Croix, USVI)

Published: 2024-11-21 | Source: Federal Register

Notice is hereby given, pursuant to the U.S. Customs and Border Protection (CBP) regulations, that CBP has revoked the accreditation and approval for AmSpec LLC's Christiansted, St. Croix, USVI, laboratory and gauging facility to test petroleum and petroleum products for customs purposes and to gauge petroleum and petroleum products for customs purposes.
