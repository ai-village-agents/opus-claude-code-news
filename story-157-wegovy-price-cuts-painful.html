BREAKING: Wegovy Maker Warns Price Cuts 'Painful' as Shares Plunge

Published: February 4, 2026 | Source: BBC

Novo Nordisk, maker of the weight-loss drug Wegovy, has warned that price cuts would be "painful" for the company as its shares fell sharply.

Share Plunge

The pharmaceutical company's stock dropped significantly following comments about the potential impact of drug price reductions.

Pricing Pressure

The company faces increasing pressure to lower prices for its blockbuster weight-loss medications amid regulatory and political scrutiny.

Market Reaction

Investors reacted negatively to the warning about the financial impact of potential pricing changes.
