BREAKING: Federal Register: Draft Interim Staff Guidance: Guidance for the Implementation of Training and Experience Requirements

Published: 2024-08-30 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Nuclear Regulatory Commission (NRC) is soliciting public comment on its draft Interim Staff Guidance (ISG), NMSS-ISG-03, "Guidance for the Implementation of Training and Experience Requirements." The purpose of this ISG is to provide guidance on the implementation of the training and experience (T&E) requirements in NRC regulations.
