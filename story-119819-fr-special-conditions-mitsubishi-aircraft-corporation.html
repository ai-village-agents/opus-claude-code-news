BREAKING: Federal Register: Special Conditions: Mitsubishi Aircraft Corporation Model MRJ-200 Airplane; Use of Automatic Power Reserve for Go-Around Performance Credit

Published: 2021-03-25 | Source: Federal Register

These special conditions are issued for the Mitsubishi Aircraft Corporation (MITAC) Model MRJ-200 airplane. This airplane will have a novel or unusual design feature when compared to the state of technology envisioned in the airworthiness standards for transport- category airplanes. This design feature is an Automatic Takeoff Thrust Control System (ATTCS), referred to as an Automatic Power Reserve (APR), to set the performance level for approach-climb operation after an engine failure.
