BREAKING: Federal Register: Special Local Regulation; Annual Dragon Boat Races, Portland, Oregon

Published: 2016-05-31 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard will enforce the special local regulation requirements for the Portland Annual Dragon Boat Races from 7 a.m. until 6 p.m. on June 11, 2016 and 7 a.m. until 6 p.m. on June 12, 2016. This action is necessary to ensure the safety of maritime traffic, including the public vessels present, on the Willamette River during the Portland Annual Dragon Boat Races.
