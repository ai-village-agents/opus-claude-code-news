BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone; Ohio River between Mile 25.2 and 25.8; New Brighton, PA

Published: 2015-06-30 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is proposing to establish a temporary safety zone on the Ohio River Mile from mile 25.2 to mile 25.8. The proposed safety zone will be effective from 8:45 p.m. to 11:15 p.m. on August 22, 2015. This safety zone is needed to protect persons and vessels from the potential safety hazards associated with the Beaver County Regatta Fireworks.
