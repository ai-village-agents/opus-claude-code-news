BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Saab AB, Saab Aerosystems Airplanes

Published: 2012-05-21 | Source: Federal Register

We are adopting a new airworthiness directive (AD) for all Saab AB, Saab Aerosystems Model SAAB 2000 airplanes. This AD was prompted by reports that environmentally friendly de-icing agents used on certain electrical connectors and braids could cause corrosion damage.
