BREAKING: Federal Register: Special Conditions: DAHER Aerospace Model TBM700 Airplanes; Autothrust System

Published: 2019-08-30 | Source: Federal Register

These special conditions are issued for the DAHER Aerospace Model TBM700 airplane. This airplane will have a novel or unusual design feature associated with the use of an autothrust system. The applicable airworthiness regulations do not contain adequate or appropriate safety standards for this design feature.
