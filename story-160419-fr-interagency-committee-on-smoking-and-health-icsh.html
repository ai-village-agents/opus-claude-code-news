BREAKING: Federal Register: Interagency Committee on Smoking and Health (ICSH)

Published: 2020-09-04 | Source: Federal Register

In accordance with the Federal Advisory Committee Act, the Centers for Disease Control and Prevention (CDC) announces the following meeting of the Interagency Committee on Smoking and Health (ICSH). This is a virtual meeting and is open to the public. The public is welcome to view the meeting via Zoom, limited only by the number of seats available, which is 500.
