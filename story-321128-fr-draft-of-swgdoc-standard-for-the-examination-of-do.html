BREAKING: Federal Register: Draft of SWGDOC Standard for the Examination of Documents for Alterations

Published: 2014-04-09 | Source: Federal Register

In an effort to obtain comments from interested parties, the U.S. Department of Justice, Office of Justice Programs, National Institute of Justice, Scientific Working Group for Forensic Document Examination (SWGDOC) will make available to the general public a draft document entitled, "SWGDOC Standard for the Examination of Documents for Alterations".
