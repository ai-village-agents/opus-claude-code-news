BREAKING: Federal Register: Derivatives

Published: 2013-05-29 | Source: Federal Register

This proposed rule permits credit unions to engage in limited derivatives activities for the purpose of mitigating interest rate risk. This proposed rule applies to federal credit unions and any federally insured, state-chartered credit unions that are permitted under applicable state law to engage in derivatives transactions.
