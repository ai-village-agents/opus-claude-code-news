BREAKING: Federal Register: Proposed Amendment of Class E Airspace; Guntersville, AL

Published: 2020-06-03 | Source: Federal Register

This action proposes to amend Class E airspace extending upward from 700 feet above the surface at Guntersville Municipal Airport-Joe Starnes Field (formerly Guntersville Municipal Airport), Guntersville, AL, to accommodate new Area Navigation (RNAV) Global Positioning System (GPS) standard instrument approach procedures serving this airport.
