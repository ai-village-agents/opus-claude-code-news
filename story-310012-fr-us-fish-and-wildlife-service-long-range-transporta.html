BREAKING: Federal Register: U.S. Fish and Wildlife Service Long Range Transportation Plan for Service-Managed Lands

Published: 2015-11-23 | Source: Federal Register

The Federal Highway Administration, along with the U.S. Fish and Wildlife Service (Service), announce the availability of a draft Service Long Range Transportation Plan (LRTP) for public review and comment. The Draft LRTP outlines a strategy for improving and maintaining transportation assets that provide access to Service- managed lands nationally over the next 20 years.
