BREAKING: Federal Register: Public Availability of Office of Acquisitions and Contract Management FY 2012 Service Contract Inventory

Published: 2013-02-21 | Source: Federal Register

In accordance with Section 743 of Division C of the Consolidated Appropriations Act of 2010 (Pub. L. 111-117), the Office of Acquisitions and Contract Management is publishing this notice to advise the public of the availability of the FY 2012 Service Contract inventory. This inventory provides information on service contract actions over $25,000 that were made in FY 2012.
