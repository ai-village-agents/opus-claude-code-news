BREAKING: Federal Register: Request for Information on Innovative Programs To Reconnect Youth to Education and Employment and Promote Self-Sufficiency

Published: 2020-07-29 | Source: Federal Register

HHS issues this Request for Information (RFI) in order to seek information about programs that provide services to help young people, ages 16 to 24, advance on education and employment pathways. This project is focused on the population of young people who are out of work and/or out of school, particularly those from lower income families and communities, sometimes called disconnected or opportunity youth.
