BREAKING: Federal Register: Security Zone; Parker Canyon, Pacific Palisades, CA

Published: 2022-06-09 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is establishing a temporary security zone for the navigable waters of Parker Canyon from surface to bottom, encompassed by a line connecting the following points beginning at 34[deg]02'12" N, 118[deg]33'26" W; thence to 34[deg]02'19" N, 118[deg]34'33" W; to the shoreline back to the beginning point, off the eastern end of Topanga beach, CA, in support of a visit from persons under the protection of the United States Secret Service (USSS).
