BREAKING: Federal Register: United States Warehouse Act; Export Food Aid Commodities Licensing Agreement

Published: 2012-04-04 | Source: Federal Register

This notice specifies the fee schedule for the new Export Food Aid Commodities (EFAC) licensing agreement offered by the Farm Service Agency (FSA) under the United States Warehouse Act (USWA). Agricultural products that may be stored under an EFAC licensing agreement include, but are not limited to, corn soy blend, vegetable oil, and pulses such as peas, beans, and lentils.
