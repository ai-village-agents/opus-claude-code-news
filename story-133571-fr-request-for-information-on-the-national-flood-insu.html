BREAKING: Federal Register: Request for Information on the National Flood Insurance Program's Community Rating System Extension of Comment Period

Published: 2021-09-22 | Source: Federal Register

The Federal Emergency Management Agency (FEMA) is extending the public comment period for its request for information published August 23, 2021 on the National Flood Insurance Program's (NFIP) Community Rating System (CRS).
