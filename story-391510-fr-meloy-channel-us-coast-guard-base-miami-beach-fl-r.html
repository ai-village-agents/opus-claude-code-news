BREAKING: Federal Register: Meloy Channel, U.S. Coast Guard Base Miami Beach, FL; Restricted Area

Published: 2012-07-20 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Army Corps of Engineers (Corps) is amending the regulations at 33 CFR part 334 to establish a new restricted area in the waters surrounding the U.S. Coast Guard Base Miami Beach, Florida (Base Miami Beach). Base Miami Beach is composed of multiple U.S. Coast Guard (USCG) units, both land and waterside.
