BREAKING: Federal Register: Fiscal Year (FY) 2023 Notice of Supplemental Funding Opportunity

This notice is to inform the public that the Substance Abuse and Mental Health Services Administration (SAMHSA) is supporting an administrative supplement, which is consistent with the scope of the initial FY 2018 award, of up to $375,000 for nine months to the only funded AI/AN ATTC recipient. This recipient was funded in FY 2018 under the AI/AN ATTC Cooperative Agreement NOFO TI-18-001 and has a project end date of December 29, 2022.
