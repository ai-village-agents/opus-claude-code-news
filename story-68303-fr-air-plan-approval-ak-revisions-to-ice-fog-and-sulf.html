BREAKING: Federal Register: Air Plan Approval; AK; Revisions to Ice Fog and Sulfur Dioxide Regulations

Published: 2023-05-24 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) proposes to approve a revision to the Alaska State Implementation Plan (SIP) submitted on May 16, 2022. In the submission, Alaska revised and repealed state regulations originally put in place to limit water vapor emissions that may contribute to ice fog and to address the use of high-sulfur marine fuels near the communities of St. Paul Island and Unalaska.
