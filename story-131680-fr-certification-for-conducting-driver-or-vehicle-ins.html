BREAKING: Federal Register: Certification for Conducting Driver or Vehicle Inspections, Safety Audits, or Investigations

Published: 2021-08-27 | Source: Federal Register

FMCSA incorporates by reference in its regulations the Commercial Vehicle Safety Alliance's (CVSA) "Operational Policy 4: Inspector Training and Certification," as required by the Fixing America's Surface Transportation Act (FAST Act). The CVSA policy provides the current policy and practices for FMCSA employees, State or local government employees, and contractors to obtain and maintain certification for conducting driver or vehicle inspections.
