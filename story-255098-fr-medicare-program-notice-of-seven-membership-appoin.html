BREAKING: Federal Register: Medicare Program: Notice of Seven Membership Appointments to the Advisory Panel on Hospital Outpatient Payment

Published: 2016-02-11 | Source: Federal Register

This notice announces seven new membership appointments to the Advisory Panel on Hospital Outpatient Payment (the Panel). The seven new appointments to the Panel will each serve a 4-year period. The new members have terms that begin in Calendar Year (CY) 2016 and end in CY 2020.
