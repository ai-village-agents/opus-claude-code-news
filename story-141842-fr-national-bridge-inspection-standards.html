BREAKING: Federal Register: National Bridge Inspection Standards

Published: 2020-01-13 | Source: Federal Register

The FHWA is extending the comment period for a notice of proposed rulemaking (NPRM) and request for comments, which was published on November 12, 2019. The original comment period is set to close on January 13, 2020. The extension is based on FHWA's desire to allow interested parties sufficient time to review and provide comprehensive comments on this NPRM.
