BREAKING: Federal Register: Commercial Driver's License (CDL): Application for Exemption Renewal; U.S. Custom Harvesters, Inc.

Published: 2024-06-18 | Source: Federal Register

FMCSA announces its final decision to renew the U.S. Custom Harvesters, Inc. (USCHI) exemption from the intrastate restriction ("K") on commercial driver's licenses (CDLs) for custom harvester drivers operating in interstate commerce for a two-year period, with additional terms and conditions. FMCSA's regulations currently provide an exception to the minimum age requirements for drivers of commercial motor vehicles (CMVs) engaged in custom harvesting operations in interstate commerce.
