BREAKING: Federal Register: COVID-19: Developing Drugs and Biological Products for Treatment or Prevention; Guidance for Industry; Availability

Published: 2020-05-19 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA or Agency) is announcing the availability of a final guidance for industry entitled "COVID-19: Developing Drugs and Biological Products for Treatment or Prevention." This guidance describes FDA's current recommendations regarding phase 2 or phase 3 trials for drugs or biological products under development for the treatment or prevention of COVID-19.
