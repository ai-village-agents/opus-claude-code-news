BREAKING: Federal Register: Release of Draft Policy Assessment for the Ozone National Ambient Air Quality Standards

Published: 2019-11-01 | Source: Federal Register

On or about October 31, 2019, the Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) will make available the document, Policy Assessment for the Ozone National Ambient Air Quality Standards, External Review Draft (draft PA). This draft document was prepared as part of the current review of the national ambient air quality standards (NAAQS) for photochemical oxidants including ozone (O3).
