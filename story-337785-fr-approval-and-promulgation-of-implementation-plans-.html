BREAKING: Federal Register: Approval and Promulgation of Implementation Plans; Wyoming; Revisions to the Air Quality Standards and Regulations

Published: 2014-10-21 | Source: Federal Register

EPA is approving changes to Wyoming's State Implementation Plan (SIP). On February 10, 2014, the Wyoming Department of Environmental Quality (WDEQ) submitted to EPA revisions to the Wyoming SIP. These revisions included the removal of an exemption from Wyoming Air Quality Standards and Regulations (WAQSR) Chapter 3, section 2(d).
