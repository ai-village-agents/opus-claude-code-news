BREAKING: Federal Register: Energy Conservation Program: Energy Conservation Standards for Small Electric Motors

Published: 2020-09-18 | Source: Federal Register

On April 30, 2020, the U.S. Department of Energy ("DOE") published in the Federal Register a Notification of Proposed Determination ("NOPD") and request for comment regarding energy conservation standards for small electric motors. DOE is announcing that a webinar will be held regarding this proposed determination and that the comment period is re-opened to accept additional written comments.
