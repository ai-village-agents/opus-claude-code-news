BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of a Determination Regarding the Swine Vesicular Disease Status of Certain Regions in Italy

Published: 2013-04-26 | Source: Federal Register

We are advising the public that we have determined that the Italian Regions of Lombardia, Emilia-Romagna, Veneto, and Piemonte and the autonomous provinces of Trento and Bolzano are free of swine vesicular disease.
