BREAKING: Federal Register: Special Local Regulations; Red Bull Flugtag Miami, Biscayne Bay; Miami, FL

Published: 2012-10-03 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is establishing a special local regulation on the waters of Biscayne Bay, east of Bayfront Park, in Miami, Florida during the Red Bull Flugtag. The Red Bull Flugtag is scheduled to take place on Saturday, November 3, 2012. The event consists of 30 participants launching self-propelled flying machines from a 30ft ramp to the water below. 150 spectator vessels are expected to attend the event.
