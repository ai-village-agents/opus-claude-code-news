BREAKING: Federal Register: Section 8 Housing Assistance Payments Program-Annual Adjustment Factors, Fiscal Year 2023

Published: 2023-03-09 | Source: Federal Register

The United States Housing Act of 1937 requires that certain assistance contracts signed by owners participating in the Department's Section 8 housing assistance payment programs provide annual adjustments to monthly rentals for units covered by the contracts. For owners subject to a Reserve for Replacement deposit requirement, HUD also requires that the amount of the required deposit be adjusted each year by the AAF.
