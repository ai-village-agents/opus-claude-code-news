BREAKING: Federal Register: Pipeline Safety: Public Workshop on Managing Pipeline Cracking Challenges

Published: 2014-07-01 | Source: Federal Register

The Pipeline and Hazardous Materials Safety Administration (PHMSA) and the National Association of Pipeline Safety Representatives are holding this public workshop to gather and disseminate information on the state-of-the-art of crack detection in hazardous liquid and natural gas pipelines.
