BREAKING: Federal Register: Privacy Act of 1974; System of Records

Published: 2022-05-12 | Source: Federal Register

In accordance with the Privacy Act of 1974, the DoD is establishing a new Department-wide system of records titled, "Defense Advisory Committees," DoD-0011. This system of records covers DoD's maintenance of records about proposed, appointed, and prior members of the DoD's Federal Advisory Committees, to include subcommittees, which are or not subject to the Federal Advisory Committee Act (FACA).
