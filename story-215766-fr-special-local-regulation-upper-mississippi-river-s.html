BREAKING: Federal Register: Special Local Regulation; Upper Mississippi River, St. Paul, MN

Published: 2018-09-07 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is establishing a temporary special local regulation for the navigable waters of the Upper Mississippi River from Mile Marker (MM) 846 to MM 847. The special local regulation is necessary to protect event participants, spectators, and vessels transiting the area from potential hazards during the WCCO-TV Pulling Together marine event.
