BREAKING: Federal Register: Public Inquiry on Competitive Products Fund

Published: 2013-07-25 | Source: Federal Register

The Commission is establishing a proceeding to review several issues concerning the Competitive Products Fund. These include inter- fund transfers (of amounts from the Postal Service Fund to the Competitive Products Fund); the use of amounts from the Competitive Products Fund to prepay certain costs; and calculation and transfer of the assumed federal income tax.
