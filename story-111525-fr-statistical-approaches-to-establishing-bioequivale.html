BREAKING: Federal Register: Statistical Approaches To Establishing Bioequivalence; Draft Guidance for Industry; Availability

Published: 2022-12-05 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA or Agency) is announcing the availability of a draft guidance for industry entitled "Statistical Approaches to Establishing Bioequivalence." This draft guidance provides recommendations to sponsors and applicants planning to use equivalence criteria in analyzing bioequivalence (BE) studies for investigational new drug applications (INDs), new drug applications (NDAs), abbreviated new drug applications (ANDAs), and supplements to these applications.
