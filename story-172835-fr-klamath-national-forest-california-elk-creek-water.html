BREAKING: Federal Register: Klamath National Forest; California; Elk Creek Watershed Project

Published: 2019-02-27 | Source: Federal Register

The Klamath National Forest is withdrawing the Notice of Intent (NOI) to prepare an Environmental Impact Statement for the Elk Creek Watershed Project. The original NOI was published in the Federal Register on October 2, 2017. The project is being canceled and will be combined with a new project for which an Environmental Assessment will be prepared.
