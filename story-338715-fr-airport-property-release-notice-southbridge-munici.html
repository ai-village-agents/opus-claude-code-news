BREAKING: Federal Register: Airport Property Release Notice; Southbridge Municipal Airport, Southbridge, MA

Published: 2014-10-31 | Source: Federal Register

The Federal Aviation Administration is considering a proposal to release approximately 30.29 acres of airport property for conservation land at the Southbridge Municipal Airport, Southbridge, MA. The acres to be released are currently not used for aeronautical purposes and are vacant wetlands. The released acres would be considered reimbursement for general funds the Town of Southbridge expended to provide the Airport with necessary services within the last 6 years.
