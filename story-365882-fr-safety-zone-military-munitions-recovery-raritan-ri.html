BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone; Military Munitions Recovery, Raritan River, Raritan, NJ

Published: 2013-09-19 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard proposes to establish a permanent safety zone within the waters of the Raritan River upstream of the Perth Amboy Railroad Bridge. This proposed safety zone is necessary to provide for the protection of the maritime public and safety of navigation during removal of underwater explosive hazards in the Raritan River.
