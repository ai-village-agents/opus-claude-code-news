BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; The Boeing Company Airplanes

Published: 2017-12-20 | Source: Federal Register

We are adopting a new airworthiness directive (AD) for certain The Boeing Company Model 757-200 series airplanes. This AD was prompted by a report indicating that the main cargo door (MCD) forward-most cam latch on the forward center cam latch pair broke during flight.
