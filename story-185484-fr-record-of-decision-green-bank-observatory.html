BREAKING: Federal Register: Record of Decision; Green Bank Observatory

Published: 2019-08-02 | Source: Federal Register

On July 26, 2019, the National Science Foundation (NSF) signed a Record of Decision (ROD) for the Green Bank Observatory in Green Bank, West Virginia. This important step concludes the agency's decision-making process with respect to the general path forward for facility operations in a budget-constrained environment.
