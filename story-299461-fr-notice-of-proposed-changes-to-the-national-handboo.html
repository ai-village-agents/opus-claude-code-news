BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Proposed Changes to the National Handbook of Conservation Practices for the Natural Resources Conservation Service

Published: 2015-07-21 | Source: Federal Register

Notice is hereby given of the intention of NRCS to issue a series of revised conservation practice standards in the National Handbook of Conservation Practices. These standards include: Channel Bed Stabilization (Code 584), Karst Sinkhole Treatment (Code 527), Open Channel (Code 582), Pond (Code 378), Surface Drain, Field Ditch (Code 607), Surface Drain, Main or Lateral (Code 608), Vertical Drain (Code 630) and Waste Hauling (Code 321).
