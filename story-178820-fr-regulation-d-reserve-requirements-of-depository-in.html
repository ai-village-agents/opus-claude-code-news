BREAKING: Federal Register: Regulation D: Reserve Requirements of Depository Institutions

Published: 2019-05-10 | Source: Federal Register

The Board of Governors of the Federal Reserve System ("Board") is amending Regulation D (Reserve Requirements of Depository Institutions) to revise the rate of interest paid on balances maintained to satisfy reserve balance requirements ("IORR") and the rate of interest paid on excess balances ("IOER") maintained at Federal Reserve Banks by or on behalf of eligible institutions.
