BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Airbus Airplanes

Published: 2018-06-05 | Source: Federal Register

We are adopting a new airworthiness directive (AD) for certain Airbus Model A350-941 airplanes. This AD requires a detailed inspection of the four retaining pins in the main landing gear support structure (MLGSS) trunnion block, left- and right-hand sides, and related investigative and corrective actions if necessary.
