BREAKING: Federal Register: Approval of Air Quality Implementation Plans; New York; Fuel Composition and Use-Sulfur Limitations

Published: 2018-08-23 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is approving a revision to the New York State Implementation Plan (SIP) concerning sulfur-in-fuel limits. The intended effect of this revision is to add a regulatory mechanism for meeting the existing obligations related to regional haze.
