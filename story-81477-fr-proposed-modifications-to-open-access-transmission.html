BREAKING: Federal Register: Proposed Modifications to Open Access Transmission Tariff; Public Hearing and Opportunities for Public Review and Comment

Published: 2023-11-07 | Source: Federal Register

Bonneville is initiating a proceeding pursuant to Bonneville's open access transmission tariff (Tariff) and the Federal Power Act to modify the non-rate terms and conditions for transmission and interconnection services in the Tariff.
