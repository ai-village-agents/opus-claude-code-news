BREAKING: Federal Register: Federal Housing Finance Board; Repeal of Federal Housing Finance Board Regulations

Published: 2018-04-03 | Source: Federal Register

The Federal Housing Finance Agency (FHFA) is proposing to repeal two parts of the Federal Housing Finance Board (Finance Board) regulations, one of which defines terms used in Finance Board regulations and one of which describes the process by which the Finance Board conducted its monthly interest rate survey (MIRS).
