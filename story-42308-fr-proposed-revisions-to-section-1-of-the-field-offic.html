BREAKING: Federal Register: Proposed Revisions to Section 1 of the Field Office Technical Guide for Several States

Published: 2024-07-22 | Source: Federal Register

NRCS is giving notice that it is proposing revisions to Section 1--General Resource References of the Field Office Technical Guides in Connecticut, Delaware, Florida, Kentucky, Maryland, Massachusetts, New York, North Carolina, Pennsylvania, Rhode Island, South Carolina, and Virginia to include State Offsite Methods for Food Security Act Wetland Identification (SOSM).
