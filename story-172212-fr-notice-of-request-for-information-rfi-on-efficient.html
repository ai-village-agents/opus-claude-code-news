BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Request for Information (RFI) on Efficient and Flexible Building Loads

Published: 2019-02-20 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Department of Energy (DOE) invites public comment on its Request for Information (RFI) number regarding Efficient and Flexible Building Loads. Through this RFI, the U.S. Department of Energy (DOE) Building Technologies Office (BTO) seeks input from industry to better understand where flexible building loads research goals can be refined to reflect market needs and inform related R&D activities.
