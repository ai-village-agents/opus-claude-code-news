BREAKING: Federal Register: Sierra National Forest, Bass Lake Ranger District, California, Whisky Ecosystem Restoration Project

Published: 2012-04-11 | Source: Federal Register

The Bass Lake Ranger District is proposing a series of ecological restoration treatments, east of the community of North Fork, California. This would be north of Cascadel Point, south of Shuteye Peak, and west of Whisky Ridge. Treatment areas have been initially identified to restore forest conditions to more closely resemble pre- 1900s stand structures which would result in forests that are more resilient and resistant to expected changes in climate and disturbance regimes.
