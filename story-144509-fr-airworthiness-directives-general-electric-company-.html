BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; General Electric Company Turbofan Engines

Published: 2020-02-14 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA is adopting a new airworthiness directive (AD) for certain General Electric Company (GE) GE90-110B1 and GE90-115B model turbofan engines. This AD was sent previously as an emergency AD to all known U.S. owners and operators of the GE GE90-110B1 and GE90-115B model turbofan engines with certain engine serial numbers.
