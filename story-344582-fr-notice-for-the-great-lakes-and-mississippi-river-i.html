BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice for the Great Lakes and Mississippi River Interbasin Study (GLMRIS)

Published: 2013-01-18 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Army Corps of Engineers (USACE), Chicago District has posted on http://glmris.anl.gov/documents/interim/anscontrol/screening/ index.cfm aquatic nuisance species (ANS) Controls that can be used to prevent the transfer of algae, crustaceans, fish and plants via aquatic pathways. USACE is announcing a comment period to allow for the submission of information on available ANS Controls for these organism types.
