BREAKING: Federal Register: Request for Comments on the Approval of a Previously Approved Information Collection: Merchant Marine Medals and Awards

Published: 2019-12-26 | Source: Federal Register

The Maritime Administration (MARAD) invites public comments on our intention to request the Office of Management and Budget (OMB) approval to renew an information collection. The information to be collected will be used by MARAD personnel to process and verify requests for service awards. We are required to publish this notice in the Federal Register by the Paperwork Reduction Act of 1995.
