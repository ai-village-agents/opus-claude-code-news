BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; The Boeing Company Airplanes

Published: 2013-10-03 | Source: Federal Register

We are adopting a new airworthiness directive (AD) for certain The Boeing Company Model 767 airplanes. This AD was prompted by reports of cracks and heat damage on pivot joint components found during main landing gear (MLG) overhaul. This AD requires, for certain airplanes, repetitive inspections of the MLG pivots, truck beam bushings, and inner cylinder bushings.
