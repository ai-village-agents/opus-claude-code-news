BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Dassault Aviation Airplanes

Published: 2022-10-21 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA proposes to adopt a new airworthiness directive (AD) for certain Dassault Aviation Model FALCON 7X airplanes. This proposed AD was prompted by a report of smoke in the flightdeck and loss of the right-hand (RH) primary display unit (PDU) and the secondary flight display (SFD).
