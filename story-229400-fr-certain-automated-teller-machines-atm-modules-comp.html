BREAKING: Federal Register: Certain Automated Teller Machines, ATM Modules, Components Thereof, and Products Containing the Same; Commission Determination Not To Review an Initial Determination Amending the Complaint and Notice of Investigation To Reflect a Corporate Name Change

Published: 2017-03-13 | Source: Federal Register

Notice is hereby given that the U.S. International Trade Commission ("the Commission") has determined not to review an initial determination ("ID") ("Order No. 32") amending the complaint and notice of investigation to reflect a corporate name change.
