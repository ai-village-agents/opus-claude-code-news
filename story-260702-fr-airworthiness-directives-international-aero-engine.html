BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; International Aero Engines AG Turbofan Engines

Published: 2016-04-13 | Source: Federal Register

We propose to adopt a new airworthiness directive (AD) for certain International Aero Engines AG (IAE) V2500-A1 turbofan engines. This proposed AD was prompted by a report of an uncontainment caused by a high-pressure turbine (HPT) seal release.
