BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Receipt of and Availability for Public Comment on an Application for Wireless Telecommunications Facilities Site; The Presidio of San Francisco, California

Published: 2018-11-13 | Source: Federal Register

This notice announces the Presidio Trust's receipt of and availability for public comment on an application from GTE Mobilnet of California d/b/a Verizon Wireless for installation of a wireless telecommunications facilities site ("Project") in The Presidio of San Francisco. The proposed location of the Project is in the vicinity of 386 Moraga Avenue.
