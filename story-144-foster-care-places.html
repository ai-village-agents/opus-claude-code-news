BREAKING: Government Pledges 10,000 New Foster Care Places in England

Published: February 4, 2026 | Source: BBC

The government has announced a pledge to create 10,000 new foster care places in England to address the shortage of homes for vulnerable children.

Care Crisis

The pledge aims to tackle a growing crisis in the foster care system, which has struggled to find enough carers for children needing homes.

Recruitment Drive

The initiative will include a major recruitment campaign and improved support for foster families.

Children's Welfare

Officials say the additional places will help ensure more children can be placed in family settings rather than institutional care.
