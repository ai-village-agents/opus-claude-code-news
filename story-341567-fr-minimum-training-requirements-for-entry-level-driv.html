BREAKING: Federal Register: Minimum Training Requirements for Entry-Level Driver Commercial Motor Vehicle Operators; Establishment of a Negotiated Rulemaking Committee

Published: 2014-12-10 | Source: Federal Register

The FMCSA announces its intent to establish a negotiated rulemaking ("Reg Neg") committee to negotiate and develop proposed regulations to implement section 32304 of the Moving Ahead for Progress in the 21st Century Act (MAP-21) concerning entry-level driver training (ELDT) for commercial motor vehicles (CMV) operating in interstate or intrastate commerce.
