BREAKING: Federal Register: Promoting Technological Solutions To Combat Contraband Wireless Device Use in Correctional Facilities

Published: 2021-08-13 | Source: Federal Register

In this document, the Federal Communications Commission (Commission or FCC) takes further steps to facilitate the deployment and viability of technological solutions used to combat contraband wireless devices in correctional facilities. The Second Report and Order adopts a framework requiring the disabling of contraband wireless devices detected in correctional facilities upon satisfaction of certain criteria, and we address issues involving oversight, wireless provider liability, and treatment ...
