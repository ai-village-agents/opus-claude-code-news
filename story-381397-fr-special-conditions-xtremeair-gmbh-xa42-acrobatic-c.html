BREAKING: Federal Register: Special Conditions: XtremeAir GmbH, XA42; Acrobatic Category Aerodynamic Stability

Published: 2012-03-26 | Source: Federal Register

These special conditions are issued for the XtremeAir GmbH XA42 airplane. The XA42 airplane has a novel or unusual design feature associated with its static stability. This airplane can perform at the highest level of aerobatic competition. To be competitive, the aircraft was designed with positive and, at some points, neutral stability within its flight envelope.
