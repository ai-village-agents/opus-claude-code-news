BREAKING: Federal Register: David Grant United States Air Force Medical Center Specialty Care Travel Reimbursement Demonstration Project

Published: 2013-02-14 | Source: Federal Register

This notice is to advise interested parties of a Military Health System (MHS) demonstration project under the authority of Title 10, United States Code, Section 1092, entitled David Grant United States Air Force Medical Center Specialty Care Travel Reimbursement Demonstration Project. This demonstration project is intended to test whether providing travel reimbursement will increase utilization of the direct care system by selected beneficiaries.
