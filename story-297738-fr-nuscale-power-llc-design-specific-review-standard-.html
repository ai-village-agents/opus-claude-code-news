BREAKING: Federal Register: NuScale Power, LLC, Design-Specific Review Standard and Safety Review Matrix

Published: 2015-06-30 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Nuclear Regulatory Commission (NRC) is soliciting public comment on the Design-Specific Review Standard (DSRS) and Safety Review Matrix for the NuScale Power, LLC, design (NuScale DSRS Scope and Safety Review Matrix).
