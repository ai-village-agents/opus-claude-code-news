BREAKING: Federal Register: Amendment of the Federal Docket Management System (EPA/GOV-2)

Published: 2014-02-18 | Source: Federal Register

Pursuant to the provisions of the Privacy Act of 1974 (5 USC 552a), the Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is giving notice that it is amending the Federal Docket Management System (FDMS) system of records to include additional categories of records. The amendment is required to address additional categories of information collected from Freedom of Information Act (FOIA) requesters by some participating agencies and information voluntarily provided, even when not required.
