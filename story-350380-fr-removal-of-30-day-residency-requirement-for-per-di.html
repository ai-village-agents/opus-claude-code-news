BREAKING: Federal Register: Removal of 30-Day Residency Requirement for Per Diem Payments

Published: 2013-03-26 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Veterans Affairs (VA) is withdrawing VA's proposed rulemaking, published in the Federal Register on September 27, 2012, which proposed to amend its regulations that govern VA payments to State homes for bed holds on behalf of veterans. Specifically, the regulation proposed to remove a 30-day residency requirement before VA would make such payments.
