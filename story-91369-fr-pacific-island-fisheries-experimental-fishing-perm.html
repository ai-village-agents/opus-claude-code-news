BREAKING: Federal Register: Pacific Island Fisheries; Experimental Fishing Permit

Published: 2022-03-18 | Source: Federal Register

NMFS is issuing an experimental fishing permit (EFP) to the Hawaii Longline Association (HLA) to evaluate the risk of seabird interactions in the Hawaii shallow-set longline fishery when setting fishing gear one hour before and one hour after local sunset and using tori lines instead of required blue-dyed bait and strategic offal discharge as seabird mitigation measures.
