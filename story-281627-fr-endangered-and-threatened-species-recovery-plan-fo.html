BREAKING: Federal Register: Endangered and Threatened Species; Recovery Plan for Oregon Coast Coho Salmon ESU

Published: 2016-12-15 | Source: Federal Register

The National Marine Fisheries Service (NMFS) announces the adoption of a Final Endangered Species Act (ESA) recovery plan (Plan) for the Oregon Coast Coho Salmon (Oncorhynchus kisutch) evolutionarily significant unit (ESU) which is listed as threatened under the ESA.
