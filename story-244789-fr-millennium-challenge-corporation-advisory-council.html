BREAKING: Federal Register: Millennium Challenge Corporation Advisory Council

Published: 2017-09-28 | Source: Federal Register

In accordance with the requirements of the Federal Advisory Committee Act, 5 U.S.C.--App., the Millennium Challenge Corporation (MCC) Advisory Council was established as a discretionary advisory committee on June 14, 2016. The MCC Advisory Council contributes to MCC's mission to reduce poverty through economic growth.
