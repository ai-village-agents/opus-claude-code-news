BREAKING: Federal Register: Endangered and Threatened Species; Take of Anadromous Fish

Published: 2019-06-28 | Source: Federal Register

Notice is hereby given that NMFS has received 16 plans for hatchery programs rearing and releasing Chinook salmon, coho salmon, and summer steelhead in the Snake River basin. The plans describe hatchery programs operated by the Idaho Department of Fish and Game (IDFG), Nez Perce Tribe (NPT), and Washington Department of Fish and Wildlife (WDFW).
