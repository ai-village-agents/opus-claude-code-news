BREAKING: Federal Register: Federal Management Regulation (FMR); Sleeping in Federal Buildings

Published: 2019-11-05 | Source: Federal Register

This bulletin reaffirms that sleeping in buildings under the jurisdiction, custody or control of GSA, including those buildings delegated to other Federal agencies by the Administrator of General Services, is prohibited, except when expressly authorized by an agency official.
