BREAKING: Federal Register: Privacy Act of 1974, as Amended; Notice of a New System of Records

Published: 2013-09-10 | Source: Federal Register

Pursuant to the provisions of the Privacy Act of 1974, as amended, the Department of the Interior is issuing a public notice regarding the Department of the Interior system of records titled "Oracle Federal Financials." The Oracle Federal Financials application is a financial software package that clients may use to perform budgeting, purchasing, and procurement functions.
