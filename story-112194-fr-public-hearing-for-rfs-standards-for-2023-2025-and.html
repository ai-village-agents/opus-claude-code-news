BREAKING: Federal Register: Public Hearing for RFS Standards for 2023-2025 and Other Changes

Published: 2022-12-13 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is announcing a virtual public hearing to be held on January 10, 2023, on its proposal for the "Renewable Fuel Standard (RFS) Program: Standards for 2023- 2025 and Other Changes," which was announced on November 30, 2022. An additional session will be held on January 11, 2023, if necessary, to accommodate the number of testifiers that sign-up to testify.
