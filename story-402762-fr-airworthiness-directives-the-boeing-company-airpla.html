BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; The Boeing Company Airplanes

Published: 2012-12-03 | Source: Federal Register

We are adopting a new airworthiness directive (AD) for certain The Boeing Company Model 737-100, -200, -200C, -300, -400, and -500 series airplanes. This AD was prompted by a report of a crack found in the fuselage skin under the aft drain mast.
