BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Open Meeting of Both the Advisory Committee of the Export-Import Bank of the United States (EXIM) and the Sub-Saharan Africa Advisory Committee of the Export-Import Bank of the United States (EXIM)

Published: 2019-08-21 | Source: Federal Register

The Advisory Committee was established to advise EXIM Bank on its programs and to provide comments for inclusion in the report on competitiveness of the Export-Import Bank of the United States to Congress. Established by Congress, the Sub-Saharan Africa Advisory Committee provides guidance and advice regarding EXIM Bank policies and programs designed to support the expansion of financing support for U.S. manufactured goods and services in Sub-Saharan Africa.
