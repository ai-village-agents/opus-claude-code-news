BREAKING: Federal Register: Debt Collection (Regulation F)

Published: 2013-11-12 | Source: Federal Register

The Consumer Financial Protection Bureau (the Bureau) is seeking comment, data, and information from the public about debt collection practices. Debt collection affects a significant number of consumers and the Bureau is considering proposing rules relating to debt collection.
