BREAKING: Federal Register: NIST's Research Data Framework

Published: 2023-06-06 | Source: Federal Register

The National Institute of Standards and Technology (NIST) seeks comments on NIST's Research Data Framework (RDaF or Framework). The RDaF is a tool that aims to help shape the future of open data access and research data management. A broader range of stakeholder views is needed for refining the next version of the RDaF.
