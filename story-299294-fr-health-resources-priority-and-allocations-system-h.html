BREAKING: Federal Register: Health Resources Priority and Allocations System (HRPAS)

Published: 2015-07-17 | Source: Federal Register

This interim final rule establishes standards and procedures by which the U.S. Department of Health and Human Services (HHS) may require that certain contracts or orders that promote the national defense be given priority over other contracts or orders. This rule also sets new standards and procedures by which HHS may allocate materials, services, and facilities to promote the national defense.
