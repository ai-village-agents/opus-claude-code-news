BREAKING: Federal Register: Advisory Committee on Supply Chain Competitiveness Solicitation of Nominations for Membership

Published: 2015-11-12 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Commerce, International Trade Administration (ITA), is requesting nominations to fill vacancies on the Advisory Committee on Supply Chain Competitiveness (Committee). The Committee was established under the Federal Advisory Committee Act, 5 U.S.C. App. The Committee was first chartered on November 21, 2011, and renewed on November 20, 2013.
