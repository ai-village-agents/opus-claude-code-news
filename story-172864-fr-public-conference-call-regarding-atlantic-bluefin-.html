BREAKING: Federal Register: Public Conference Call Regarding Atlantic Bluefin Tuna Management Strategy Evaluation

Published: 2019-02-27 | Source: Federal Register

NMFS is holding a stakeholder meeting by conference call to provide information on recent progress related to the International Commission for the Conservation of Atlantic Tunas (ICCAT) bluefin tuna management strategy evaluation (MSE). NMFS will also receive input on issues to be considered at an upcoming intersessional meeting of ICCAT's Panel 2, which will focus on initial operational management objectives for the bluefin tuna MSE. The conference call is open to the public.
