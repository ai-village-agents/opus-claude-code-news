BREAKING: Federal Register: 5-Decyne-4,7-Diol, 2,4,7,9-Tetramethyl- and 6-Dodecyne-5,8-Diol, 2,5,8,11-Tetramethyl-; Exemption From the Requirement of a Tolerance

Published: 2022-06-15 | Source: Federal Register

This regulation establishes an exemption from the requirement of a tolerance for residues of 5-decyne-4,7-diol, 2,4,7,9-tetramethyl- (CAS Reg. No. 126-86-3), herein referred to as TMDD, and 6-dodecyne- 5,8-diol, 2,5,8,11-tetramethyl- (CAS Reg. No. 68227-33-8), herein referred to as TMDDD, when used as inert ingredients (surfactants, related adjuvant of surfactants and carriers) in pesticide formulations applied to growing crops pre- and post-harvest, and applied in/on animals.
