BREAKING: Federal Register: Toluene Diisocyanates (TDI) and Related Compounds; Significant New Use Rule; Extension of Comment Period

Published: 2015-02-23 | Source: Federal Register

EPA issued a proposed rule in the Federal Register of January 15, 2015, concerning 2,4-toluene diisocyanate, 2,6-toluene diisocyanate, toluene diisocyanate unspecified isomers (these three chemical substances are hereafter referred to as toluene diisocyanates or TDI) and related compounds. This document extends the comment period for 45 days, from March 16, 2015, to April 30, 2015.
