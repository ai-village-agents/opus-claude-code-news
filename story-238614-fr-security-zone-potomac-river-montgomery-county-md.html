BREAKING: Federal Register: Security Zone; Potomac River, Montgomery County, MD

Published: 2017-07-10 | Source: Federal Register

This interim rule establishes a security zone encompassing certain waters of the Potomac River. This action is necessary to prevent waterside threats and incidents immediately before, during and after events held at the Trump National Golf Club at Potomac Falls, VA.
