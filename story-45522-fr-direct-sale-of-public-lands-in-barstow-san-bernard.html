BREAKING: Federal Register: Direct Sale of Public Lands in Barstow, San Bernardino County, California

Published: 2024-08-28 | Source: Federal Register

The Bureau of Land Management (BLM) is proposing a non- competitive (direct) sale of public lands near Barstow, San Bernardino County, California, to Burlington Northern Santa Fe (BNSF), LLC, a Delaware Corporation, under the provisions of the Federal Land Policy and Management Act of 1976 (FLPMA), as amended, and BLM land sale regulations.
