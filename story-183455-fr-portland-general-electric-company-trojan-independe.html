BREAKING: Federal Register: Portland General Electric Company; Trojan Independent Spent Fuel Storage Installation

Published: 2019-07-08 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Nuclear Regulatory Commission (NRC) is considering the renewal of Special Nuclear Materials (SNM) License SNM-2509 for the Trojan Nuclear Plant Independent Spent Fuel Storage Installation (ISFSI) (Trojan ISFSI) located in Columbia County, Oregon. The NRC has prepared an environmental assessment (EA) for this proposed license renewal in accordance with its regulations.
