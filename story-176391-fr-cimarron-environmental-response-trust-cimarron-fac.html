BREAKING: Federal Register: Cimarron Environmental Response Trust Cimarron Facility

Published: 2019-04-10 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Nuclear Regulatory Commission (NRC) has received a license amendment application from the licensee, Cimarron Environmental Response Trust (CERT) for the Cimarron Facility, located in Logan County, Oklahoma. The license authorizes possession of Byproduct, Source, and Special Nuclear Material (SNM-928).
