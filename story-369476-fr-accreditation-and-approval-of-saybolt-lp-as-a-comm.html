BREAKING: Federal Register: Accreditation and Approval of Saybolt, LP, as a Commercial Gauger

Published: 2013-11-06 | Source: Federal Register

Notice is hereby given, pursuant to CBP regulations, that Saybolt, LP, has been approved to gauge petroleum and petroleum products for customs purposes for the next three years as of July 11, 2013.
