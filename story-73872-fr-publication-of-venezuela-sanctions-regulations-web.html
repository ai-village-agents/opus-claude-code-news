BREAKING: Federal Register: Publication of Venezuela Sanctions Regulations Web General Licenses 5K and 5L.

Published: 2023-08-07 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of the Treasury's Office of Foreign Assets Control (OFAC) is publishing two general licenses (GLs) issued pursuant to the Venezuela Sanctions Regulations: GLs 5K and 5L, each of which was previously made available on OFAC's website.
