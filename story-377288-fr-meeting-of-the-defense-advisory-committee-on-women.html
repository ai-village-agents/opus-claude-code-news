BREAKING: Federal Register: Meeting of the Defense Advisory Committee on Women in the Services (DACOWITS)

Published: 2012-02-10 | Source: Federal Register

Pursuant to Section 10(a), Public Law 92-463, as amended, notice is hereby given of a forthcoming meeting of the Defense Advisory Committee on Women in the Services (DACOWITS). The purpose of the meeting is to receive briefings on sexual harassment programs, health issues in combat zone deployments, leadership accountability in the prevention of sexual assault and harassment, and an update on the Women in Services Restrictions report.
