BREAKING: Federal Register: Access to the Decommissioning Trust Fund for the Disposal of Large Components

Published: 2022-02-04 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Nuclear Regulatory Commission (NRC) is denying a petition for rulemaking, dated February 22, 2019, submitted by Gerard P. Van Noordennen on behalf of EnergySolutions, LLC (the petitioner). The petition was docketed by the NRC on March 20, 2019, and was assigned Docket No. PRM-50-119.
