BREAKING: Federal Register: Request for Comment; Notice of Development of Economic Statistics for Puerto Rico

Published: 2019-11-01 | Source: Federal Register

The Bureau of Economic Analysis (BEA) is soliciting comments from the public on its new prototype Economic Statistics for Puerto Rico, which cover consumer spending, business investment, and trade in goods for Puerto Rico. BEA seeks comments on the statistics' methodology, presentation, level of detail, and scope.
