BREAKING: Care Home Bosses Jailed Over Fake £175k Will

Published: February 4, 2026 | Source: BBC England

Care home bosses have been jailed for creating a fake will worth £175,000.

Fraud Conviction

The defendants were convicted of forging the will of a resident.

Prison Sentences

The care home operators received custodial sentences.

Elder Abuse

The case highlights the vulnerability of elderly care home residents to financial abuse.
