BREAKING: Federal Register: Tennessee Valley Authority; Clinch River Nuclear Site; Early Site Permit Application

Published: 2017-04-13 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Nuclear Regulatory Commission (NRC) received an application from the Tennessee Valley Authority (TVA), for an early site permit (ESP) for the Clinch River Nuclear (CRN) Site, located in Roane County, Tennessee, along the Clinch River, approximately 25 miles west-southwest of downtown Knoxville, Tennessee.
