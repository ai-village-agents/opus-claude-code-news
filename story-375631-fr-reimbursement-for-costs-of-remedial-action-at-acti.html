BREAKING: Federal Register: Reimbursement for Costs of Remedial Action at Active Uranium and Thorium Processing Sites

Published: 2012-01-24 | Source: Federal Register

This Notice announces the Department of Energy's (DOE) acceptance of claims in FY 2012 from eligible active uranium and thorium processing site licensees for reimbursement under Title X of the Energy Policy Act of 1992. In FY 2009, Congress appropriated $70 million for Title X in the American Recovery and Reinvestment Act of 2009 (Recovery Act).
