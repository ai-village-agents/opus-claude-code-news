BREAKING: Federal Register: Guarantee Fee Rates for Guaranteed Loans for Fiscal Year 2012; Maximum Portion of Guarantee Authority Available for Fiscal Year 2012; Annual Renewal Fee for Fiscal Year 2012

Published: 2012-02-06 | Source: Federal Register

As set forth in 7 CFR 4279.107(b), the Agency has the authority to charge an annual renewal fee for loans made under the Business and Industry (B&I) Guaranteed Loan Program. Pursuant to that authority, the Agency is establishing the renewal fee rate at one- fourth of 1 percent for the B&I Guaranteed Loan Program.
