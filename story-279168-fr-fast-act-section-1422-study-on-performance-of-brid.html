BREAKING: Federal Register: FAST Act Section 1422 Study on Performance of Bridges

Published: 2016-11-16 | Source: Federal Register

Section 1422 of the Fixing America's Surface Transportation (FAST) Act directs the Administrator of the Federal Highway Administration (FHWA) to commission the Transportation Research Board of the National Academy of Sciences to conduct a study on the performance of bridges that received funding under the Innovative Bridge Research and Construction program in meeting the goals of that program.
