BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Availability of Draft Guidelines for Human Exposure Assessment: Extension of Public Comment Period

Published: 2016-02-16 | Source: Federal Register

On January 7, 2016 the U.S. Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) announced the release of the external review draft of "Guidelines for Human Exposure Assessment" for public review and comment (81 FR 774-775). With this notice EPA is announcing the extension of the comment period to March 22, 2016. EPA is seeking public comment prior to external peer review.
