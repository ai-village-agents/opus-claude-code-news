BREAKING: Federal Register: Privacy Act of 1974; System of Records-Impact Evaluation of Race to the Top and School Improvement Grants

Published: 2012-11-16 | Source: Federal Register

On October 12, 2012, the Institute of Education Sciences in the U.S. Department of Education (Department) published in the Federal Register (77 FR 62228) a new system of records notice (SORN) entitled "Impact Evaluation of Race to the Top and School Improvement Grants" (18-13-32) (RTT-SIG). This notice corrects one error in terminology in the RTT-SIG SORN.
