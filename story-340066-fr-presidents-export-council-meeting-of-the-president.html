BREAKING: Federal Register: President's Export Council: Meeting of the President's Export Council

Published: 2014-11-19 | Source: Federal Register

The President's Export Council (Council) will hold a meeting to deliberate on recommendations related to promoting the expansion of U.S. exports and to convey a report to the President on the September 2014 fact-finding trip to Poland and Turkey by some members of the Council.
