BREAKING: Federal Register: Amendment of Class D and Class E Airspace; Saginaw, MI

Published: 2024-05-15 | Source: Federal Register

This action amends the Class D and Class E airspace at Saginaw, MI. This action is the result of an airspace review conducted due to the decommissioning of the Mount Pleasant very high frequency omnidirectional range (VOR) as part of the VOR Minimum Operating Network (MON) Program. The names and geographic coordinates of various airports are being updated to coincide with the FAA's aeronautical database.
