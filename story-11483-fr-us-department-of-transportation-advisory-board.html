BREAKING: Federal Register: U.S. Department of Transportation Advisory Board

Published: 2025-05-16 | Source: Federal Register

The Department announces the establishment of the U.S. Department of Transportation Advisory Board, which is intended to provide strategic vision and high-level guidance to modernize and enhance the United States transportation systems. In addition, the Department is soliciting nominations for membership of the Board.
