BREAKING: Federal Register: Schedules of Controlled Substances: Temporary Placement of Four Synthetic Cannabinoids Into Schedule I

Published: 2014-01-10 | Source: Federal Register

The Deputy Administrator of the Drug Enforcement Administration (DEA) is issuing this notice of intent to temporarily schedule four synthetic cannabinoids into Schedule I pursuant to the temporary scheduling provisions of the Controlled Substances Act (CSA).
