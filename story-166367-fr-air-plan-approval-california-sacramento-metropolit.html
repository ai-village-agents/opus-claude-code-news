BREAKING: Federal Register: Air Plan Approval; California; Sacramento Metropolitan Air Quality Management District

Published: 2020-11-19 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is taking final action to approve revisions to the Sacramento Metropolitan Air Quality Management District (SMAQMD) portion of the California State Implementation Plan (SIP). These revisions concern emissions of volatile organic compounds (VOCs) from the surface coating operations of plastic parts and products.
