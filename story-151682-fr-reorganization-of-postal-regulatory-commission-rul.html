BREAKING: Federal Register: Reorganization of Postal Regulatory Commission Rules; Correction

Published: 2020-05-15 | Source: Federal Register

On April 20, 2020, the Postal Regulatory Commission revised Commission rules. That document incorrectly listed a cross-reference. This document corrects the final regulations by removing the incorrect cross-reference.
