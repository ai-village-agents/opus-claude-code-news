BREAKING: Federal Register: Scientific Integrity Policy Draft for Public Comment

Published: 2024-01-24 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is announcing a 30-day public comment period on the draft updates to its Scientific Integrity (SI) Policy. In accordance with the requirements of the 2021 Presidential Memorandum on Restoring Trust in Government Through Scientific Integrity and Evidence-based Policymaking, EPA is revising our SI Policy.
