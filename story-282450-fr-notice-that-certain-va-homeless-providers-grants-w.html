BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice that Certain VA Homeless Providers Grants Will Be Terminated

Published: 2016-12-23 | Source: Federal Register

VA is announcing that all per diem funding for grants awarded during fiscal year (FY) 1994 through FY 2016 under VA's Homeless Providers GPD will be terminated, in accordance with the grant award agreements. This does not apply to special need grants and Transition in Place (TIP) grants.
