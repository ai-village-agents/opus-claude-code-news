BREAKING: Federal Register: Atlantic Highly Migratory Species; North and South Atlantic 2016 Commercial Swordfish Quotas

Published: 2016-07-26 | Source: Federal Register

This final rule adjusts the 2016 fishing season quotas for North and South Atlantic swordfish based upon 2015 commercial quota underharvests and international quota transfers consistent with the International Commission for the Conservation of Atlantic Tunas (ICCAT) Recommendations 13-02 and 13-03. This final rule also simplifies the annual North and South Atlantic quota adjustment process when the adjustment simply applies a previously-adopted formula or measure.
