BREAKING: Federal Register: Elimination of Copayment for Opioid Antagonists and Education on Use of Opioid Antagonists

Published: 2020-11-06 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Veterans Affairs (VA) is proposing to amend its medical regulations that govern copayments to conform with recent statutory requirements. VA would be eliminating the copayment requirement for opioid antagonists furnished to veterans who are at high risk of overdose of a specific medication or substance in order to reverse the effect of such an overdose.
