BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Publication to the Department of Labor's List of Goods Produced by Child Labor or Forced Labor, and Updates to the List of Products Requiring Federal Contractor Certification as to Forced or Indentured Child Labor

Published: 2024-09-06 | Source: Federal Register

This notice announces the publication on September 5, 2024, of an updated list of goods--along with countries of origin--that the Department of Labor (DOL) has reason to believe are produced with child labor or forced labor in violation of international standards (the TVPRA List).
