BREAKING: Federal Register: Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standards; Seat Belt Assembly Anchorages

Published: 2015-03-02 | Source: Federal Register

This document supplements NHTSA's March 2012 notice of proposed rulemaking (NPRM) to amend Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard (FMVSS) No. 210, "Seat belt assembly anchorages," to specify a force application device (FAD) for use as a testing interface to transfer loads onto the seat belt anchorage system during compliance tests of anchorage strength.
