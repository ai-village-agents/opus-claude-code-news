BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Airbus Airplanes

Published: 2016-07-07 | Source: Federal Register

We propose to supersede Airworthiness Directive (AD) 2011-17- 05, for certain Airbus Model A300 B2-1C, A300 B2-203, A300 B2K-3C, A300-B4-103, A300 B4-203, and A300 B4-2C airplanes. AD 2011-17-05 currently requires repetitive inspections in sections 13 through 18 of the fuselage between rivets of the longitudinal lap joints between frames (FR) 18 and 80 for cracking, and repair or modification if necessary.
