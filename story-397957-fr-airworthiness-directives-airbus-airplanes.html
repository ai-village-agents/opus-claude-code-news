BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Airbus Airplanes

Published: 2012-10-02 | Source: Federal Register

We propose to supersede an existing airworthiness directive (AD) that applies to all Airbus Model A330-200 and -300 series airplanes, and Model A340-200 and -300 series airplanes.
