BREAKING: Federal Register: Energy Conservation Program: Energy Conservation Standards for Consumer Furnaces

Published: 2022-01-28 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Department of Energy ("DOE") is initiating an effort to determine whether to amend the current energy conservation standards for certain classes of consumer furnaces. Under the Energy Policy and Conservation Act, as amended, DOE must review these standards at least once every six years and publish either a notice of proposed rulemaking ("NOPR") to propose new standards or a notification of determination that the existing standards do not need to be amended.
