BREAKING: Federal Register: Office of Women's Business Ownership: Women's Business Center Program

Published: 2019-11-25 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Small Business Administration (SBA) is codifying regulations for the Women's Business Center (WBC) Program as directed in section 29 of the Small Business Act (the Act). This final rule also codifies policy and procedural changes included in the WBC Program Announcement and Notice of Award (NOA).
