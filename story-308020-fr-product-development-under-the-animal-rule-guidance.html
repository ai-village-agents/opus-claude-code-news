BREAKING: Federal Register: Product Development Under the Animal Rule; Guidance for Industry; Availability

Published: 2015-10-28 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA or Agency) is announcing the availability of a guidance for industry entitled "Product Development Under the Animal Rule." When human efficacy studies are not ethical and field trials are not feasible, FDA may rely on adequate and well-controlled animal efficacy studies to support approval of a drug or licensure of a biological product under the Animal Rule.
