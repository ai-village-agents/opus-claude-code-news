BREAKING: Federal Register: Microbial and Disinfection Byproducts Rules: Public Meeting To Inform Potential Rule Revisions

Published: 2020-09-30 | Source: Federal Register

The Safe Drinking Water Act (SDWA) requires the U.S. Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) to review each national primary drinking water regulation (NPDWR) at least once every six years. As part of the "Six-Year Review", EPA evaluates any newly available data, information, and technologies to determine if any regulatory revisions are needed.
