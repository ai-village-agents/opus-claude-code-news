BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Final Federal Agency Action on the Authorization for the Sunrise Wind Energy Project Offshore New York

Published: 2024-06-28 | Source: Federal Register

USACE announces final agency action on the USACE authorization for the proposed construction and maintenance of the Sunrise Wind project offshore of Massachusetts, Rhode Island, and New York. USACE has issued a permit authorizing the construction and maintenance of the Sunrise Wind project under section 10 of the Rivers and Harbors Act of 1899 (RHA), section 404 of the Clean Water Act (CWA), and section 14 of the Rivers and Harbors Act of 1899.
