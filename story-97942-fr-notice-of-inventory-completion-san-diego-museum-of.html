BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Inventory Completion: San Diego Museum of Man, San Diego, CA; Correction

Published: 2022-06-09 | Source: Federal Register

The Museum of Us (formerly known as the San Diego Museum of Man) has corrected an inventory of human remains and associated funerary objects, published in a Notice of Inventory Completion in the Federal Register on August 8, 2018. This notice corrects the cultural affiliation.
