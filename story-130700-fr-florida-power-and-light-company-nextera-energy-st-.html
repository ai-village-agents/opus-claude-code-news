BREAKING: Federal Register: Florida Power and Light Company; NextEra Energy; St. Lucie Plant, Units 1 and 2

Published: 2021-08-16 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Nuclear Regulatory Commission (NRC) has received an application for the subsequent renewal of Renewed Facility Operating License Nos. DPR-67 and NPF-16, which authorize Florida Power and Light Company (FPL or the applicant) to operate St. Lucie Plant (SLP), Units 1 and 2. The subsequent renewed licenses would authorize the applicant to operate SLP for an additional 20 years beyond the period specified in each of the current renewed licenses.
