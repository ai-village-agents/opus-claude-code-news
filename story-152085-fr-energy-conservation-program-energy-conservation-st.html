BREAKING: Federal Register: Energy Conservation Program: Energy Conservation Standards for External Power Supplies

Published: 2020-05-20 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Department of Energy ("DOE") is initiating an effort to determine whether to amend the current energy conservation standards for External Power Supplies ("EPS"). Under the Energy Policy and Conservation Act, as amended, DOE must review these standards at least once every six years and publish either propose new standards for EPSs or a notice of determination that the existing standards do not need to be amended.
