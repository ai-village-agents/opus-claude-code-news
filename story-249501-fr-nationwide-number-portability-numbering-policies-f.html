BREAKING: Federal Register: Nationwide Number Portability; Numbering Policies for Modern Communications

Published: 2017-11-27 | Source: Federal Register

In this document, the Commission seeks comment on how best to move toward complete nationwide number portability (NNP) to promote competition among all service providers. The NPRM proposes to eliminate the N-1 query requirement, and also proposes to forbear from the dialing parity requirements for competitive LECs that remain after the 2015 USTelecom Forbearance Order as they apply to interexchange services.
