BREAKING: Federal Register: Pesticides; Microbial Pesticide Definitions and Applicability; Clarification and Availability of Test Guideline

Published: 2012-08-30 | Source: Federal Register

This final rule clarifies the distinction between "isolates" and "strains," and clarifies the requirements applicable to new isolates, which are considered to be new active ingredients under the Federal Insecticide, Fungicide, and Rodenticide Act (FIFRA).
