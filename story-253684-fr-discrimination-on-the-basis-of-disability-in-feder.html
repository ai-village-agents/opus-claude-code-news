BREAKING: Federal Register: Discrimination on the Basis of Disability in Federally Assisted and Federally Conducted Programs and Activities

Published: 2016-01-22 | Source: Federal Register

This rule revises the National Aeronautics and Space Administration (NASA) regulations implementing section 504 of the Rehabilitation Act of 1973 (section 504), which prohibits discrimination on the basis of disability in programs, services, and activities by recipients of Federal financial assistance from NASA as well as those programs, services, and activities conducted by NASA.
