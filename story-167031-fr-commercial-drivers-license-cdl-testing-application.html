BREAKING: Federal Register: Commercial Driver's License (CDL) Testing; Application for Exemption: State of Minnesota

Published: 2020-11-30 | Source: Federal Register

FMCSA announces that the State of Minnesota seeks reconsideration of its application for exemption from regulations governing commercial driver's license (CDL) skills testing procedures and practices that was denied on May 9, 2017. Minnesota believes it can deliver its CDL skills testing more efficiently in an alternative manner.
