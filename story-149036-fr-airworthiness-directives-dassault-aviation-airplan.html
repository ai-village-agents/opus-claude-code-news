BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Dassault Aviation Airplanes

Published: 2020-04-13 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA is superseding Airworthiness Directive (AD) 2016-16-09 and AD 2019-03-20, which applied to Dassault Aviation Model FALCON 7X airplanes. Those ADs required revising the existing maintenance or inspection program, as applicable, to incorporate new and more restrictive maintenance requirements and airworthiness limitations for airplane structures and systems.
