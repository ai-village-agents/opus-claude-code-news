BREAKING: Federal Register: Local Government Advisory Committee and Small Communities Advisory Subcommittee: Request for Nominations

Published: 2022-10-04 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Environmental Protection Agency's (EPA) Office of Intergovernmental Relations invites nominations from a diverse range of qualified candidates to be considered for appointment to its Local Government Advisory Committee (LGAC) and Small Communities Advisory Subcommittee (SCAS). Qualified nominees for the LGAC and SCAS hold elected positions with local, tribal, state, or territorial governments, or serve in a full-time government position appointed by an elected official.
