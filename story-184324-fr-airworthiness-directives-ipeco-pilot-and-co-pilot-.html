BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Ipeco Pilot and Co-Pilot Seats

Published: 2019-07-19 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA proposes to supersede airworthiness directive (AD) 2017-22-02, which applies to certain Ipeco Holdings Limited (Ipeco) pilot and co-pilot seats. AD 2017-22-02 requires modification and re- identification of the affected seats.
