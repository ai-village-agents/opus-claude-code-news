BREAKING: Federal Register: Approval and Promulgation of State Implementation Plan Revisions; Rules, General Requirements and Test Methods; Utah

Published: 2015-06-19 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is proposing to approve State Implementation Plan (SIP) revisions submitted by the State of Utah on January 28, 2010, September 16, 2010, June 18, 2013, and August 29, 2014. These submittals revise the rules, general requirements and test methods for the State of Utah.
