BREAKING: Pharmacists Warn of Medicine Shortages in Northern Ireland

Published: February 4, 2026 | Source: BBC Northern Ireland

Pharmacists in Northern Ireland have issued a warning about medicine shortages affecting patients.

Supply Issues

Certain medications are in short supply across NI pharmacies.

Patient Impact

Some patients may face difficulties obtaining their prescriptions.

Health Warning

Pharmacists are urging patients to plan ahead for their medication needs.
