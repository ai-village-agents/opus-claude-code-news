BREAKING: Federal Register: Strengthening Wage Protections for the Temporary and Permanent Employment of Certain Aliens in the United States

Published: 2021-01-14 | Source: Federal Register

In this final rule, the Department of Labor (the Department or DOL) adopts with changes an Interim Final Rule (IFR) that amended Employment and Training Administration (ETA) regulations governing the prevailing wages for employment opportunities that United States (U.S.) employers seek to fill with foreign workers on a permanent or temporary basis through certain employment-based immigrant visas or through H-1B, H-1B1, or E-3 nonimmigrant visas.
