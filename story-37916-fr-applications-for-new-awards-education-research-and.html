BREAKING: Federal Register: Applications for New Awards; Education Research and Special Education Research Grant Programs

Published: 2024-05-28 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Education (Department) is issuing a notice inviting applications for new awards for fiscal year (FY) 2025 for the Education Research and Special Education Research Grant Programs, Assistance Listing Numbers (ALNs) 84.305A, 84.305D, 84.324A, and 84.324B. This notice relates to the approved information collection under OMB control number 4040-0001.
