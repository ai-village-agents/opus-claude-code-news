BREAKING: Federal Register: 2022 National Strategy To Support Family Caregivers Available for Public Comments

Published: 2022-10-06 | Source: Federal Register

In accordance with the requirements of the Recognize, Assist, Include, Support and Engage (RAISE) Family Caregivers Act of 2017, the Administration for Community Living (ACL) is making the 2022 National Strategy to Support Family Caregivers (the Strategy) available for public comment for a period of 60 days.
