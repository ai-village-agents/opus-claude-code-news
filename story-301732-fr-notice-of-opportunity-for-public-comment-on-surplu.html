BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Opportunity for Public Comment on Surplus Property Release at Madras Municipal Airport, Madras, OR

Published: 2015-08-17 | Source: Federal Register

Under the provisions of Title 49, U.S.C. Section 47153(c), notice is being given that the FAA is considering a request from the City of Madras, OR, to waive the surplus property requirements for approximately 1.07 acres of airport property located at Madras Municipal Airport, in Madras, OR. The subject property is located away from the aeronautical area and currently vacant.
