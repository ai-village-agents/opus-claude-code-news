BREAKING: Federal Register: Circular Welded Non-Alloy Steel Pipe From the Republic of Korea: Final Results of Antidumping Duty Administrative Review; 2015-2016

Published: 2018-06-13 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Commerce (Commerce) determines that certain companies covered by this administrative review made sales of circular welded non-alloy steel pipe (CWP) from the Republic of Korea (Korea) at less than normal value during the period of review (POR) November 1, 2015, through October 31, 2016.
