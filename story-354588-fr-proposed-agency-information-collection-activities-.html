BREAKING: Federal Register: Proposed Agency Information Collection Activities: Proposed Collection Renewal; Comment Request Re Occasional Qualitative Surveys

Published: 2013-05-10 | Source: Federal Register

The FDIC, as part of its continuing effort to reduce paperwork and respondent burden, invites the general public and other Federal agencies to take this opportunity, as required by the Paperwork Reduction Act of 1995 (4 U.S.S. chapter 35), to comment on renewal of its generic information collection entitled, "Occasional Qualitative Surveys" (OMB No. 3064-0127).
