BREAKING: Federal Register: Exemptions From Certain Prohibited Transaction Restrictions

Published: 2014-04-10 | Source: Federal Register

This document contains exemptions issued by the Department of Labor (the Department) from certain of the prohibited transaction restrictions of the Employee Retirement Income Security Act of 1974 (ERISA or the Act) and/or the Internal Revenue Code of 1986 (the Code).
