BREAKING: Federal Register: Certain Cut-to-Length Carbon-Quality Steel Plate From the Republic of Korea: Preliminary Results and Preliminary Intent To Rescind, in Part, the Countervailing Duty Administrative Review; 2020

Commerce preliminarily determines that the mandatory respondent, Hyundai Steel Company (Hyundai Steel), received de minimis net countervailable subsidies during the POR.
