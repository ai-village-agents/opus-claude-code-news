BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Designation of Potential Wilderness as Wilderness, Fire Island National Seashore

Published: 2015-10-21 | Source: Federal Register

The Otis Pike Fire Island High Dunes Wilderness Act, Public Law 96-585, December 23, 1980, designated approximately 1,360 acres as wilderness in the Fire Island National Seashore. Due to existing boardwalks and a pit toilet, this Act also designated approximately 18 acres of potential wilderness within Fire Island National Seashore that could be re-designated as wilderness upon elimination of these non- conforming uses.
