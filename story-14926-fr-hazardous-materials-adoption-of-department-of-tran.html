BREAKING: Federal Register: Hazardous Materials: Adoption of Department of Transportation Special Permit 21478

Published: 2025-07-01 | Source: Federal Register

PHMSA proposes to adopt the provisions of DOT special permit (SP) 21478 into the Hazardous Materials Regulations (HMR). Adoption of this special permit would authorize intermediate bulk containers (IBCs) containing a residue of a certain hazardous materials to be transported without shipping papers, placards, and motor vehicle marking of the UN identification (ID) number subject to additional operational controls.
