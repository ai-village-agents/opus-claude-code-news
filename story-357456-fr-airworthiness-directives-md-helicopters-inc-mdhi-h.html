BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; MD Helicopters, Inc. (MDHI), Helicopters

Published: 2013-06-14 | Source: Federal Register

We propose to adopt a new airworthiness directive (AD) for the MDHI Model MD900 helicopters with certain main rotor blade (MRB) retention bolts (bolts) installed. This proposed AD would require a daily check of the position of each bolt and would also require a daily check and a repetitive inspection for a gap in each bolt.
