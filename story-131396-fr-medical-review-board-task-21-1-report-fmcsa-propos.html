BREAKING: Federal Register: Medical Review Board Task 21-1 Report: FMCSA Proposed Alternative Vision Standard

Published: 2021-08-24 | Source: Federal Register

In January 2021, FMCSA published a notice of proposed rulemaking (NPRM) to amend its regulations to permit individuals who cannot meet either the current distant visual acuity or field of vision standard, or both, in one eye to be physically qualified to operate a commercial motor vehicle (CMV) in interstate commerce.
