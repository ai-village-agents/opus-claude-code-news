BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Eurocopter France Helicopters

Published: 2013-04-19 | Source: Federal Register

We are adopting an airworthiness directive (AD) for Eurocopter France (Eurocopter) Model AS332C, AS332L, and AS332L1 helicopters and superseding an AD for Model AS332L2 and EC225LP helicopters. This AD requires inspecting the chip detector and modifying the chip collector, both installed on the main gearbox (MGB).
