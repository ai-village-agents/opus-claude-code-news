BREAKING: Federal Register: Margin Protection Program for Dairy

Published: 2016-04-13 | Source: Federal Register

This rule amends the regulations for the Margin Protection Program for Dairy (MPP-Dairy) to allow dairy operations to update their production history when a son, daughter, grandchild, or spouse of a child or grandchild of a current producer participating in the MPP- Dairy program joins the operation.
