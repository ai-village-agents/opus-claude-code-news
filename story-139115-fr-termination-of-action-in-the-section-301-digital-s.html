BREAKING: Federal Register: Termination of Action in the Section 301 Digital Services Tax Investigation of Turkey and Further Monitoring

Published: 2021-12-01 | Source: Federal Register

On October 8, 2021, Turkey joined the United States and 134 other jurisdictions participating in the OECD/G20 Inclusive Framework on Base Erosion and Profit Shifting in reaching a political agreement on a two-pillar solution to address tax challenges arising from the digitalization of the world economy.
