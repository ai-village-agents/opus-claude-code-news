BREAKING: Federal Register: Agency Information Collection Activities: Proposed Collection, Comment Request; National Flood Insurance Program-Ask the Advocate Web Form

Published: 2025-07-31 | Source: Federal Register

On Friday, June 6, 2025, FEMA published in the Federal Register a 60-Day notice of revision and request for comments for an information collection concerning the Office of the Flood Insurance Advocate's (OFIA) Ask the Advocate web form and the removal of two instruments that are no longer needed. This notice provides a correction to this information to be used in lieu of the information published June 6, 2025.
