BREAKING: Federal Register: Advisory Committee on the Medical Uses of Isotopes: Meeting Notice

Published: 2012-01-13 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Nuclear Regulatory Commission (NRC) will convene a teleconference meeting of the Advisory Committee on the Medical Uses of Isotopes (ACMUI) on February 7, 2012, to discuss the ACMUI Permanent Implant Brachytherapy Subcommittee Report. A copy of the agenda for the meeting will be available at http://www.nrc.gov/reading-rm/doc- collections/acmui/agenda.
