BREAKING: Federal Register: President's Advisory Council on Doing Business in Africa

Published: 2023-03-29 | Source: Federal Register

The President's Advisory Council on Doing Business in Africa will hold the first meeting of its 2022-2024 term to deliberate and adopt recommendations on actions the U.S. Government can take to strengthen U.S. commercial relationships in Africa. The PAC-DBIA will present recommendations focused on financing and infrastructure, digital and information and communications technology, healthcare, enery and environment, and agribusiness and food/water security.
