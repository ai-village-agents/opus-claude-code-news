BREAKING: Federal Register: Airline Passenger Rights; Withdrawal

Published: 2025-11-17 | Source: Federal Register

The Department is withdrawing the ANPRM on Airline Passenger Rights issued on December 11, 2024. The ANPRM sought public comment on a potential regulatory action that would require airlines to provide passengers affected by significant flight disruptions with a variety of costly measures.
