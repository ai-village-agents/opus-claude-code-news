BREAKING: Federal Register: Improving Transparency Into Pharmacy Benefit Manager Fee Disclosure

Published: 2026-01-30 | Source: Federal Register

The Department is proposing a regulation that would require providers of pharmacy benefit management services and affiliated providers of brokerage and consulting services to disclose information about their compensation to fiduciaries of self-insured group health plans subject to the Employee Retirement Income Security Act (ERISA).
