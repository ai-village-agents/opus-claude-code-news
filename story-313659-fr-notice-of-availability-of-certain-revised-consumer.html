BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Availability of Certain Revised Consumer Information Publications

Published: 2014-01-10 | Source: Federal Register

The Bureau of Consumer Financial Protection (Bureau or CFPB) announces the availability of three revised consumer publications, including a consumer information brochure and two booklets required under the Real Estate Settlement Procedures Act (RESPA), Regulation X, the Truth in Lending Act (TILA), and Regulation Z.
