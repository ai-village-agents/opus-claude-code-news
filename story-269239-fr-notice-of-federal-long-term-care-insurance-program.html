BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Federal Long Term Care Insurance Program Enrollee Decision Period for Current Enrollees

Published: 2016-07-21 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Office of Personnel Management (OPM) is announcing rules for current enrollees in the Federal Long Term Care Insurance Program (FLTCIP) who will be eligible to change coverage during a limited Enrollee Decision Period to be held this year. These rules pertain only to current eligible enrollees who may make certain changes because of premium rate increases that affect most enrollees.
