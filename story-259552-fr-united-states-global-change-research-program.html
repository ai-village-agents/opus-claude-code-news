BREAKING: Federal Register: United States Global Change Research Program

Published: 2016-03-31 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Global Change Research Program (USGCRP) is initiating an Interagency Special Report on Physical Climate Science (referred to as "the Report" throughout this notice). The focus of the Report will be an update to the physical climate science presented in the 2014 National Climate Assessment (NCA).
