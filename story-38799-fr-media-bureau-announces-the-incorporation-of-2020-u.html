BREAKING: Federal Register: Media Bureau Announces the Incorporation of 2020 U.S. Census Population Data Into the Commission's TVStudy Software and Requirement To Utilize Such Data Effective August 1, 2024

Published: 2024-06-06 | Source: Federal Register

The Media Bureau announces, that effective August 1, 2024 the Commission's TVStudy software will incorporate the most recent U.S. Census Bureau decennial Census block and population data (2020 Census Data). All television broadcast applications filed on or after August 1, 2024, will be required to utilize 2020 Census Data for purposes of conducting interference analyses. Failure to do so will require amendment and may result in dismissal of applications as defective.
