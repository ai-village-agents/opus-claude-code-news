BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Request for Information for the Proposed United States-East African Community Commercial Dialogue

Published: 2012-11-06 | Source: Federal Register

In June 2012, the East African Community (EAC) Secretariat, the EAC member country Governments, and the Government of the United States agreed to explore establishing a Commercial Dialogue as part of the U.S.-EAC Trade and Investment Partnership ("Partnership").
