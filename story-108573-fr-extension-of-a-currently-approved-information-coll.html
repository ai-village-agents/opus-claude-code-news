BREAKING: Federal Register: Extension of a Currently Approved Information Collection for the Weatherization Assistance Program

Published: 2022-10-26 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Energy (DOE), pursuant to the Paperwork Reduction Act of 1995, intends to extend for three years a currently approved collection of information with the Office of Management and Budget (OMB). The information collection request, Weatherization Assistance Program (WAP), was previously approved on May 31, 2020, under OMB Control No. 1910-5127 and its current expiration date is May 31, 2023.
