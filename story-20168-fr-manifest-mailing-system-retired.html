BREAKING: Federal Register: Manifest Mailing System Retired

Published: 2025-09-12 | Source: Federal Register

The Postal Service is proposing to amend Mailing Standards of the United States Postal Service, Domestic Mail Manual (DMM[supreg]) subsection 703.2.0 to retire the manifest mailing system (MMS).
