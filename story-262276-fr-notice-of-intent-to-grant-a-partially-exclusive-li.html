BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Intent To Grant a Partially Exclusive License; AEgis Technologies Group, Inc.

Published: 2016-04-29 | Source: Federal Register

In accordance with 35 U.S.C. 209(e) and 37 CFR part 404.7(a)(1)(i), the Department of the Army announces the intent to grant a revocable, non-assignable, partially exclusive license to AEgis Technologies Inc., Huntsville, Alabama, for the Joint Embedded Messaging System (JEMS) government-owned software for efficient manipulation of data.
