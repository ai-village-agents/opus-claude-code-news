BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; PILATUS AIRCRAFT LTD. Airplanes

Published: 2013-03-25 | Source: Federal Register

We are rescinding an airworthiness directive (AD) for all PILATUS AIRCRAFT LTD. Models PC-12, PC-12/45, and PC-12/47 airplanes. This AD results from mandatory continuing airworthiness information (MCAI) issued by the aviation authority of another country to identify and correct an unsafe condition on an aviation product.
