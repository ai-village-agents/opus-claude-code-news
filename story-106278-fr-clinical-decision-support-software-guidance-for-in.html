BREAKING: Federal Register: Clinical Decision Support Software; Guidance for Industry and Food and Drug Administration Staff; Availability

Published: 2022-09-28 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA or Agency) is announcing the availability of a final guidance entitled "Clinical Decision Support Software." This final guidance provides clarity on FDA's oversight of clinical decision support (CDS) software intended for health care professionals with the purpose of describing FDA's regulatory approach to CDS software functions.
