BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone; Annual Fireworks Displays Within the Sector Columbia River Captain of the Port Zone

Published: 2024-07-11 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard will enforce safety zone regulations at various locations in the Sector Columbia River Captain of the Port Zone from July 3, 2024 to July 27, 2024, to provide for the safety of life on navigable waters during fireworks displays. The regulation for fireworks displays within the Thirteenth Coast Guard District designates safety zones and identifies approximate dates for these events.
