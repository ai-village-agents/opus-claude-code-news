BREAKING: Federal Register: Energy Conservation Program: Energy Conservation Standards for Ceiling Fan Light Kits

Published: 2018-05-16 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Department of Energy (DOE) is publishing this final rule to amend the compliance date for energy conservation standards for ceiling fan light kits (CFLKs). The energy conservation standards for CFLKs were issued by DOE on January 6, 2016, and compliance with the standards was required on January 7, 2019.
