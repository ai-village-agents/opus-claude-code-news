BREAKING: Federal Register: Nomination of an In Vitro Test Method for the Identification of Contact Allergens: Request for Comments and Data

Published: 2012-07-23 | Source: Federal Register

On behalf of the Interagency Coordinating Committee on the Validation of Alternative Methods (ICCVAM), the NTP Interagency Center for the Evaluation of Alternative Toxicological Methods (NICEATM) requests public comment on an ICCVAM test method nomination for validation studies. The validation studies are proposed to determine the usefulness and limitations of an in vitro test method to identify electrophilic substances that have the potential to produce allergic contact dermatitis (ACD).
