BREAKING: Federal Register: Textile and Apparel Imports From China: Statistical Reports

Published: 2019-10-29 | Source: Federal Register

Following receipt of a request on October 3, 2019, from the Committee on Ways and Means of the U.S. House of Representatives (Committee) under section 332(g) of the Tariff Act of 1930 (19 U.S.C. 1332(g)), the U.S. International Trade Commission (Commission) has changed the format and publication frequency of the statistical reports it provides under Investigation No. 332-501, Textile and Apparel Imports from China: Statistical Reports.
