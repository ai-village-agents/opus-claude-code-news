BREAKING: Federal Register: Pacific Fishery Management Council; Public Meeting

Published: 2014-01-17 | Source: Federal Register

The Pacific Fishery Management Council's (Pacific Council) ad hoc Ecosystem Workgroup (EWG) will hold a public work session in Portland, OR. The meeting is open to the public, but is not intended as a public hearing. Public comments will be taken at the discretion of the EWG chair as time allows.
