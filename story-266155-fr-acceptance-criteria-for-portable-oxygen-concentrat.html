BREAKING: Federal Register: Acceptance Criteria for Portable Oxygen Concentrators Used On Board Aircraft; Correction

Published: 2016-06-14 | Source: Federal Register

This final rule replaces the existing process by which the Federal Aviation Administration (Agency or FAA) approves portable oxygen concentrators (POC) for use on board aircraft in air carrier operations, commercial operations, and certain other operations using large aircraft.
