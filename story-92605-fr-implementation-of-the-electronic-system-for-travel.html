BREAKING: Federal Register: Implementation of the Electronic System for Travel Authorization (ESTA) at U.S. Land Borders

Published: 2022-04-01 | Source: Federal Register

This rule amends Department of Homeland Security (DHS) regulations to implement the Electronic System for Travel Authorization (ESTA) requirements under section 711 of the Implementing Recommendations of the 9/11 Commission Act of 2007, for noncitizens who intend to enter the United States under the Visa Waiver Program (VWP) at land ports of entry.
