BREAKING: Gunmen Kill Dozens of People in Nigeria's Kwara State

Published: February 4, 2026 | Source: Al Jazeera

Gunmen have killed dozens of people in an attack in Nigeria's Kwara State, according to authorities, Al Jazeera reports.

The Attack

Armed gunmen carried out a deadly attack in Kwara State, located in north-central Nigeria. Authorities have confirmed dozens of fatalities in what appears to be a major security incident.

Security Context

Nigeria has faced persistent security challenges including armed banditry, kidnappings, and attacks by various armed groups. The north-central region has been particularly affected by violence between herders and farmers, as well as criminal gang activity.

Response

Nigerian authorities are responding to the incident. Such mass casualty attacks highlight the ongoing security challenges facing Africa's most populous nation.
