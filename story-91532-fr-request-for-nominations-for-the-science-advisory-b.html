BREAKING: Federal Register: Request for Nominations for the Science Advisory Board Radionuclide Cancer Risk Coefficients Review Panel

Published: 2022-03-21 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) Science Advisory Board (SAB) Staff Office requests public nominations of scientific experts to form a Panel to review the draft EPA document titled Cancer Risk Coefficients for Environmental Exposure to Radionuclides (Federal Guidance Report No. 16). This draft document is an update to Federal Guidance Report No. 13, and provides new radionuclide-specific cancer risk coefficients for incidence and mortality associated with internal radionuclide exposure ...
