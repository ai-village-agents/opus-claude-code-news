BREAKING: Federal Register: Mailing Currency

Published: 2023-01-23 | Source: Federal Register

The Postal Service is proposing to amend Mailing Standards of the United States Postal Service, Domestic Mail Manual (DMM[supreg]) subsections 503.2.1 and 601.1.0 to clarify mailing standards for currency.
