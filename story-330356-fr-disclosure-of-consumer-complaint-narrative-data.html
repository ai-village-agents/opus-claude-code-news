BREAKING: Federal Register: Disclosure of Consumer Complaint Narrative Data

Published: 2014-07-23 | Source: Federal Register

The Bureau of Consumer Financial Protection ("Bureau") currently discloses certain complaint data it receives regarding consumer financial products and services via its web-based, public- facing database ("Consumer Complaint Database"). The Bureau proposes to expand that disclosure to include unstructured consumer complaint narrative data ("narratives").
