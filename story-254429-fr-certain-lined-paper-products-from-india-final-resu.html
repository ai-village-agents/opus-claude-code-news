BREAKING: Federal Register: Certain Lined Paper Products From India: Final Results of Antidumping Duty Administrative Review; 2013-2014

Published: 2016-02-04 | Source: Federal Register

On October 7, 2015, the Department of Commerce (the Department) published the Preliminary Results of the administrative review of the antidumping duty order on certain lined paper products (CLPP) from India in accordance with section 751(a)(1)(B) of the Tariff Act of 1930, as amended (the Act).\1\ The period of review (POR) is September 1, 2013, through August 31, 2014.
