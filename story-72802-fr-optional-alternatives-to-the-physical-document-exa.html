BREAKING: Federal Register: Optional Alternatives to the Physical Document Examination Associated With Employment Eligibility Verification (Form I-9)

Published: 2023-07-25 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Homeland Security (DHS) is amending its regulations to create a framework under which the Secretary of Homeland Security (the Secretary) may, as an optional alternative to the in- person physical document examination method that employers have followed as part of the Form I-9 process set forth in current regulations, authorize alternative document examination procedures.
