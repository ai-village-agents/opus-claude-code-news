BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; The Boeing Company Airplanes

Published: 2018-10-24 | Source: Federal Register

We are superseding Airworthiness Directive (AD) 2017-01-02, which applied to certain The Boeing Company Model 787-8 and 787-9 airplanes. AD 2017-01-02 required an inspection for discrepant inboard and outboard trailing edge flap rotary actuators, and replacing the rotary actuator or doing related investigative and corrective actions if necessary.
