BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; General Electric Company Turbofan Engines

Published: 2023-03-10 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA is adopting a new airworthiness directive (AD) for certain General Electric Company (GE) GE90-110B1 and GE90-115B model turbofan engines. This AD was prompted by a manufacturer investigation which discovered that florescent penetrant inspections (FPI) were not performed on the dovetail pressure face of certain high-pressure compressor (HPC) rotor spools at overhaul.
