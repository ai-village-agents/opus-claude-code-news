BREAKING: Federal Register: Schedule for Rating Disabilities: The Digestive System

Published: 2022-01-11 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Veterans Affairs (VA) proposes to amend the Schedule for Rating Disabilities (VASRD or rating schedule) that addresses the Digestive System. These changes add medical conditions not currently in the rating schedule, revise the rating criteria to reflect medical advances that have occurred since the last revision, clarify existing rating criteria, and update medical terminology.
