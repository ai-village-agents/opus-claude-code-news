BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Inventory Completion: History Colorado, Formerly Colorado Historical Society, Denver, CO

Published: 2014-03-24 | Source: Federal Register

History Colorado, formerly Colorado Historical Society, has completed an inventory of human remains, in consultation with the appropriate Indian tribes or Native Hawaiian organizations, and has determined that there is no cultural affiliation between the human remains and any present-day Indian tribes or Native Hawaiian organizations.
