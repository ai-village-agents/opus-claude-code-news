BREAKING: Federal Register: Meeting of the COVID-19 Health Equity Task Force; Correction

Published: 2021-04-22 | Source: Federal Register

The Office of the Assistant Secretary for Health published a document in the Federal Register on April 19, 2020 giving notice that the COVID-19 Health Equity Task Force (Task Force) will hold a virtual meeting on April 30, 2021. In accordance with 41 CFR 102-3.150(b), less than 15 calendar days' notice is being given for this meeting due to the exceptional circumstances of the COVID-19 pandemic, the urgent need for the Task Force's advice during the COVID-19 pandemic, and scheduling difficulties...
