BREAKING: Federal Register: Revised Form FHWA-1273

Published: 2023-09-29 | Source: Federal Register

This notice announces the availability of revised Form FHWA- 1273 "Required Contract Provisions Federal-Aid Construction Contracts" (FHWA-1273). This form includes certain contract provisions that are required on all Federal-aid construction contracts. This form also includes proposal notices that Federal-aid recipients must incorporate or reference in all solicitation-for-bids or request-for- proposals documents for Federal-aid construction projects.
