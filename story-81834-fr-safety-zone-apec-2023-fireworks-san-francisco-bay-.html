BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone; APEC 2023 Fireworks; San Francisco Bay, San Francisco, CA

Published: 2023-11-13 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is establishing a temporary safety zone on the navigable water of the San Francisco Bay in San Francisco, CA in support of a fireworks display on November 15, 2023. The safety zone is necessary to protect personnel, vessels, and the marine environment from potential hazards created by pyrotechnics.
