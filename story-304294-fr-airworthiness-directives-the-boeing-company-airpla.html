BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; The Boeing Company Airplanes

Published: 2015-09-16 | Source: Federal Register

We are adopting a new airworthiness directive (AD) for all The Boeing Company Model 737-600, -700, -700C, -800, -900, and -900ER series airplanes. This AD was prompted by reports of latently failed fuel shutoff valves discovered during fuel filter replacement. This AD requires revising the maintenance or inspection program to include new airworthiness limitations.
