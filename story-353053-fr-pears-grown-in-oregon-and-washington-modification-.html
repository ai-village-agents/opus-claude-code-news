BREAKING: Federal Register: Pears Grown in Oregon and Washington; Modification of the Assessment Rate for Fresh Pears

Published: 2013-04-24 | Source: Federal Register

This rule increases the assessment rate established for the Fresh Pear Committee (Committee) for the 2012-2013 and subsequent fiscal periods from $0.366 to $0.449 per standard box or equivalent of summer/fall pears handled, and decreases the assessment rate from $0.471 to $0.449 per standard box or equivalent of fresh winter pears handled.
