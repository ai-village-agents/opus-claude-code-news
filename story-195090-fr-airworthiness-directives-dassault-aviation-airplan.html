BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Dassault Aviation Airplanes

Published: 2019-12-09 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA is superseding Airworthiness Directives (AD) 2017-19- 14 and AD 2014-16-27, which apply to certain Dassault Aviation Model FALCON 900EX airplanes. Those ADs require revising the existing maintenance or inspection program, as applicable, to incorporate new or more restrictive maintenance requirements and/or airworthiness limitations.
