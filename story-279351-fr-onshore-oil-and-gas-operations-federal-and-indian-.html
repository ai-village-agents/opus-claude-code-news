BREAKING: Federal Register: Onshore Oil and Gas Operations; Federal and Indian Oil and Gas Leases; Measurement of Gas

Published: 2016-11-17 | Source: Federal Register

This final rule updates and replaces Onshore Oil and Gas Order No. 5 (Order 5) with a new regulation codified in the Code of Federal Regulations (CFR). Like Order 5, this rule establishes minimum standards for accurate measurement and proper reporting of all gas removed or sold from Federal and Indian (except the Osage Tribe) leases, units, unit participating areas (PAs), and areas subject to communitization agreements (CAs).
