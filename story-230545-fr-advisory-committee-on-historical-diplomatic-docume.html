BREAKING: Federal Register: Advisory Committee on Historical Diplomatic Documentation-Notice of Closed and Open Meeting for 2017

Published: 2017-03-27 | Source: Federal Register

The Advisory Committee on Historical Diplomatic Documentation will meet on May 15, 2017, in open session to discuss unclassified matters concerning declassification and transfer of Department of State records to the National Archives and Records Administration and the status of the Foreign Relations series. The Committee will meet in open session from 9:30 a.m. until 10:30 a.m. in SA-4D Conference Room, Department of State, 2300 E Street NW., Washington DC, 20372 (Potomac Navy Hill Annex). RSVP ...
