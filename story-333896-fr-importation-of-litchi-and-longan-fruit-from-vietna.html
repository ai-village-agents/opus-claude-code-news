BREAKING: Federal Register: Importation of Litchi and Longan Fruit From Vietnam Into the Continental United States

Published: 2014-09-04 | Source: Federal Register

We are amending the fruits and vegetables regulations to allow the importation of litchi and longan fruit from Vietnam into the continental United States. As a condition of entry, litchi and longan fruit from Vietnam will be subject to a systems approach that includes requirements for treatment and inspection and restrictions on the distribution of the fruit.
