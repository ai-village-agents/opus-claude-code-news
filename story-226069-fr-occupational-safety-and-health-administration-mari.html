BREAKING: Federal Register: Occupational Safety and Health Administration Maritime Advisory Committee for Occupational Safety and Health (MACOSH)

Published: 2017-01-23 | Source: Federal Register

In accordance with the provisions of the Federal Advisory Committee Act (FACA), and after consultation with the General Services Administration, the Secretary of Labor is renewing the charter for the Maritime Advisory Committee for Occupational Safety and Health. The Committee will better enable OSHA to perform its duties under the Occupational Safety and Health Act (the OSH Act) of 1970.
