BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Intent of Waiver With Respect to Land; Freeman Municipal Airport, Seymour, IN

Published: 2020-07-27 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA is considering a proposal to change 6.592 acres of airport land from aeronautical use to non-aeronautical use and to authorize the sale of airport property located at Freeman Municipal Airport, Seymour, IN. The aforementioned land is not needed for aeronautical use. The land is located in the northeast portion of the airport just east of Airport Access Road and north of the Runway 23 runway protection zone.
