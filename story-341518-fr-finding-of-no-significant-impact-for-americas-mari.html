BREAKING: Federal Register: Finding of No Significant Impact for America's Marine Highway Program

Published: 2014-12-09 | Source: Federal Register

This Notice announces the availability of the Finding of No Significant Impact (FONSI) for the America's Marine Highway Program, which designates criteria, eligibility requirements, and information for applicants seeking to establish services on a "short sea" marine highway (America's Marine Highway).
