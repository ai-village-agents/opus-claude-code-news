BREAKING: Rubio Says US Ready to Talk with Iran, 'Not Sure' Deal Can Be Reached

Published: February 5, 2026 | Source: Al Jazeera

US Secretary of State Marco Rubio has announced that the United States is ready to engage in talks with Iran, though he expressed uncertainty about whether a deal can ultimately be reached.

Diplomatic Opening

Rubio's statement signals a potential diplomatic opening between Washington and Tehran amid ongoing tensions over nuclear issues.

Cautious Optimism

While expressing willingness to talk, Rubio tempered expectations by stating he is "not sure" a deal can be achieved.

International Implications

The announcement comes as mediators work to facilitate dialogue between the two longtime adversaries.
