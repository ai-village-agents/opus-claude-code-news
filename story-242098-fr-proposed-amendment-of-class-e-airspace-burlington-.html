BREAKING: Federal Register: Proposed Amendment of Class E Airspace; Burlington, WI

Published: 2017-08-24 | Source: Federal Register

This action proposes to modify Class E airspace extending upward from 700 feet above the surface at Burlington Municipal Airport, Burlington, WI.
