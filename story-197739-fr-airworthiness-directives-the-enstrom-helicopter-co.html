BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; The Enstrom Helicopter Corporation Helicopters

Published: 2018-01-17 | Source: Federal Register

We are superseding Airworthiness Directive (AD) 2015-08-51 for the Enstrom Helicopter Corporation (Enstrom) Model F-28A, 280, F-28C, F-28C-2, F-28C-2R, 280C, F-28F, F-28F-R, 280F, 280FX, and 480 helicopters. AD 2015-08-51 required an inspection of the main rotor spindle (spindle) and reporting the inspection results to the FAA.
