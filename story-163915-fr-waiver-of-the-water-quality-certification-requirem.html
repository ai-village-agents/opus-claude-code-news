BREAKING: Federal Register: Waiver of the Water Quality Certification Requirements of Section 401(a)(1) of the Clean Water Act

Published: 2020-10-19 | Source: Federal Register

Pursuant to the Environmental Protection Agency's Clean Water Act Section 401 Certification Rule and Executive Order 13868, the Federal Energy Regulatory Commission (Commission) is proposing rules to categorically establish a reasonable period of time for a certifying authority to act on a water quality certification request related to natural gas and liquified natural gas projects for which either an application filed pursuant to section 3 or section 7(c) of the Natural Gas Act (NGA) is pending...
