BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone; Navy Underwater Detonation (UNDET) Exercise, Apra Outer Harbor, GU

Published: 2017-06-08 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is establishing a temporary safety zone for navigable waters within Apra Outer Harbor, Guam. The safety zone will encompass a U.S. Navy render safe underwater detonation (UNDET) exercise. The Coast Guard believes this safety zone regulation is necessary to protect the public and exercise participants within the affected area from safety hazards associated with the exercise.
