BREAKING: Federal Register: Transdermal and Topical Delivery Systems-Product Development and Quality Considerations; Draft Guidance for Industry; Availability

Published: 2019-11-21 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA or Agency) is announcing the availability of a draft guidance for industry entitled "Transdermal and Topical Delivery Systems--Product Development and Quality Considerations." This guidance provides recommendations to applicants and manufacturers of transdermal and topical delivery systems (TDS) regarding the pharmaceutical development and quality information to include in new drug applications (NDAs) and abbreviated new drug applications (ANDAs).
