BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone, Festival of the Fish, Vermillion River, Vermillion, OH

Published: 2018-06-14 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is establishing a temporary safety zone for navigable waters within a 420-foot radius of the launch site located near the mouth of the Vermilion River, Vermilion, OH. This safety zone is intended to restrict vessels from a portion of Lake Erie and the Vermilion River during the Festival of the Fish fireworks display.
