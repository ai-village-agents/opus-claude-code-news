BREAKING: Federal Register: Special Local Regulation; Gulfport Grand Prix, Boca Ciego Bay, Gulfport, FL

Published: 2018-06-04 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is establishing a special local regulation on the waters of the Boca Ciego Bay in the vicinity of Gulfport, Florida, during the Gulfport Grand Prix High Speed Boat Race. Approximately 50 boats, 16 feet in length, traveling at speeds in excess of 120 miles per hour are expected to participate.
