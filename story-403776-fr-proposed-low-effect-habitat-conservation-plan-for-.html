BREAKING: Federal Register: Proposed Low-Effect Habitat Conservation Plan for the State-Route 99/Cartmill Avenue Interchange Improvements Project, City of Tulare, Tulare County, CA

Published: 2012-12-14 | Source: Federal Register

We, the U.S. Fish and Wildlife Service (Service), have received an application from the City of Tulare, Tulare County, California (applicant), for a 5-year incidental take permit for two species under the Endangered Species Act of 1973, as amended (Act). The application addresses the potential for "take" of two listed animals, the vernal pool fairy shrimp and the San Joaquin kit fox.
