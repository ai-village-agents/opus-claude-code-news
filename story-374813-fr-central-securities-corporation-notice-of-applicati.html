BREAKING: Federal Register: Central Securities Corporation; Notice of Application

Published: 2012-01-12 | Source: Federal Register

Summary of Application: Applicant requests an order to permit the adoption of an incentive compensation plan. The plan would permit the applicant to issue restricted shares of common stock, restricted stock units, shares of common stock granted as a bonus, and awards denominated in cash. Applicant: Central Securities Corporation ("Company").
