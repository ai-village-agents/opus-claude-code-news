BREAKING: Federal Register: Energy Conservation Program: Energy Conservation Standards for Dishwashers, Notification of Petition for Rulemaking

Published: 2018-04-24 | Source: Federal Register

On March 21, 2018, the Department of Energy (DOE) received a petition from the Competitive Enterprise Institute (CEI) to define a new product class under the Energy Policy and Conservation Act (EPCA) for residential dishwashers. The new product class would cover dishwashers with a cycle time of less than one hour from washing through drying.
