BREAKING: Federal Register: Amendment and Establishment of Class E Airspace; Columbus, NE

Published: 2018-08-06 | Source: Federal Register

This action modifies Class E airspace designated as a surface area and makes the airspace full-time and removes the airspace part- time status and language from the airspace legal description, amends Class E airspace extending upward from 700 feet above the surface, and establishes Class E airspace designated as an extension to the Class E surface area at Columbus Municipal Airport, Columbus, NE.
