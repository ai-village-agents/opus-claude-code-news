BREAKING: Federal Register: Agency Information Collection Extension

Published: 2020-06-03 | Source: Federal Register

DOE submitted an information collection request for extension as required by the Paperwork Reduction Act of 1995. The information collection requests a three-year extension of DOE's Form FE-746R, Import and Export of Natural Gas under OMB Control Number 1901-0294.
