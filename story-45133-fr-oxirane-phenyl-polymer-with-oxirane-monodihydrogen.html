BREAKING: Federal Register: Oxirane, phenyl-, polymer With oxirane, mono(dihydrogen phosphate), decyl ether in Pesticide Formulations; Tolerance Exemption

Published: 2024-08-23 | Source: Federal Register

This regulation establishes an exemption from the requirement of a tolerance for residues of oxirane, phenyl-, polymer with oxirane, mono(dihydrogen phosphate), decyl ether, minimum number average molecular weight 1300 Daltons (CAS Reg. No. 308336-53-0) when used as an inert ingredient in a pesticide chemical formulation.
