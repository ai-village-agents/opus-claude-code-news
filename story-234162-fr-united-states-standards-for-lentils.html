BREAKING: Federal Register: United States Standards for Lentils

Published: 2017-05-15 | Source: Federal Register

The United States Department of Agriculture's (USDA) Grain Inspection, Packers, and Stockyards Administration (GIPSA) is proposing a revision to the United States Standards for Lentils to (1) establish an additional grading factor, definition, grade requirements, and visual reference images for "wrinkled lentils," and (2) establish a special grade, definition, special grade requirements, designation, and visual reference images for "green lentils."
