BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Public Meeting Concerning Procurement Through Commercial e-Commerce Portals

Published: 2018-05-31 | Source: Federal Register

GSA and the Office of Management and Budget (OMB) are interested in conducting further dialogue with industry about Section 846 of the National Defense Authorization Act (NDAA) for Fiscal Year 2018, Procurement through Commercial e-Commerce Portals. A public meeting was held on January 9, 2018 to provide external stakeholders an initial opportunity to offer input on Phase I implementation as outlined in Section 846.
