BREAKING: Federal Register: Hazardous Materials: Adoption of Certain Special Permits and Competent Authorities Into Regulations

Published: 2014-03-18 | Source: Federal Register

The Pipeline and Hazardous Materials Safety Administration is amending the Hazardous Materials Regulations (HMR) to adopt provisions contained in certain widely used or longstanding special permits and certain competent authority approvals ("approvals") that have established safety records. Special permits allow a company or individual to package or ship a hazardous material in a manner that varies from the regulations provided an equivalent level of safety is maintained.
