BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Final Federal Agency Actions on Proposed Transportation Project in Florida

Published: 2024-11-27 | Source: Federal Register

The FHWA, on behalf of the FDOT, is issuing this notice to announce actions taken by FDOT and other Federal Agencies that are final agency actions. These actions relate to the proposed State Road (S.R.) 535/Vineland Road Project Development and Environment (PD&E) Study (Financial Management Number 437174-2).
