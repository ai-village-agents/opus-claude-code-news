BREAKING: Federal Register: Laminated Woven Sacks From the People's Republic of China: Final Results of Antidumping Duty Administrative Review; 2013-2014

Published: 2015-04-21 | Source: Federal Register

On January 28, 2015, the Department of Commerce (the "Department") published the Preliminary Results \1\ of the 2013-2014 administrative review of the antidumping duty order on laminated woven sacks ("sacks") from the People's Republic of China ("PRC"). The period of review ("POR") is August 1, 2013, through July 31, 2014. We gave interested parties an opportunity to comment on the Preliminary Results, but we received none.
