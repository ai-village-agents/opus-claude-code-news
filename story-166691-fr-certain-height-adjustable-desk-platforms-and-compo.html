BREAKING: Federal Register: Certain Height-Adjustable Desk Platforms and Components Thereof; Commission Determination Not To Review an Initial Determination Terminating the Investigation; Termination of the Investigation

Published: 2020-11-24 | Source: Federal Register

Notice is hereby given that the U.S. International Trade Commission ("Commission") has determined not to review an initial determination (Order No. 7) terminating the investigation based on withdrawal of the complaint. The investigation is terminated.
