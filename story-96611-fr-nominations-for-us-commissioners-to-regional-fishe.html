BREAKING: Federal Register: Nominations for U.S. Commissioners to Regional Fisheries Management Organizations

Published: 2022-05-24 | Source: Federal Register

NMFS is soliciting nominations, which may include self- nominations, for qualified individuals to serve as non-Federal U.S. Commissioners (Commissioners) to certain regional fisheries management organizations (RFMOs).
