BREAKING: Federal Register: Privacy Act of 1974; System of Records

Published: 2023-07-12 | Source: Federal Register

Pursuant to the Privacy Act of 1974, notice is hereby given that the Department of Veterans Affairs (VA) proposes to revise the system of records titled "Loan Guaranty Fee Personnel and Program Participant Records--VA" (17VA26). This system contains information pertaining to Fee Personnel and Program Participants who are authorized to conduct VA property appraisals and those that process, close, fund and guaranty VA loans respectively.
