BREAKING: Federal Register: Multistakeholder Process on Internet of Things Security Upgradability and Patching

Published: 2017-04-14 | Source: Federal Register

The National Telecommunications and Information Administration (NTIA) will convene a meeting of a multistakeholder process on Internet of Things Security Upgradability and Patching on April 26, 2016.
