BREAKING: Federal Register: Privacy Act of 1974; System of Records

Published: 2021-12-15 | Source: Federal Register

The United States Agency for International Development (USAID) proposes to modify a system of records titled, "USAID-19, Travel and Transportation Records," last published on December 30, 2014. USAID is modifying its SORN category of records as Social Security Numbers are no longer collected.
