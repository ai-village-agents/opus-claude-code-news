BREAKING: Federal Register: Finding Regarding Foreign Social Insurance or Pension System of Estonia

Published: 2022-05-16 | Source: Federal Register

We find that, under the Alien Nonpayment Provision of the Social Security Act (Act), citizens of Estonia may continue to receive Social Security benefits under title II, after 6 consecutive calendar months of absence from the United States. This finding is based on information and data we received about the social insurance system of Estonia and its laws.
