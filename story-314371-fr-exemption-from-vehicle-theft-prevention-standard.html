BREAKING: Federal Register: Exemption From Vehicle Theft Prevention Standard

Published: 2014-01-17 | Source: Federal Register

In this rulemaking action, NHTSA proposes to amend its procedures for obtaining an exemption from the vehicle theft prevention standard for vehicles equipped with immobilizers. NHTSA proposes to simplify the exemption procedure for immobilizer-equipped vehicles by adding performance criteria for immobilizers.
