BREAKING: Federal Register: Clarification of Controls on Radiation Hardened Integrated Circuits and Expansion of License Exception GOV

Published: 2024-03-13 | Source: Federal Register

The Bureau of Industry and Security (BIS) is amending the Export Administration Regulations (EAR) to clarify controls on radiation hardened integrated circuits, including controls on computer and telecommunications equipment incorporating such radiation hardened integrated circuits.
