BREAKING: Federal Register: Stakeholder Listening Session and Request for Information on the Value-Added Producer Grant Program

Published: 2021-08-24 | Source: Federal Register

The Rural Business-Cooperative Service (RBCS) is hosting a listening session and opening a request for information for public input about the Value-Added Producer Grant (VAPG) program. The VAPG program helps agricultural producers enter into value-added activities related to the processing and marketing of new products.
