BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Bell Helicopter Textron Canada (Bell) Helicopters

Published: 2013-07-12 | Source: Federal Register

We propose to supersede an existing airworthiness directive (AD) for the Bell Model 407 helicopters. The existing AD currently requires preflight checking and repetitively inspecting for a crack in certain tailbooms that have been redesigned, replacing the tailboom if there is a crack, modifying and re-identifying certain tailbooms, installing an improved horizontal stabilizer assembly, and assigning a 5,000 hour time-in-service (TIS) limit.
