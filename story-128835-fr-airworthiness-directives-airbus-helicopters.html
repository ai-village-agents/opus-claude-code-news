BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Airbus Helicopters

Published: 2021-07-22 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA proposes to adopt a new airworthiness directive (AD) for Airbus Helicopters Model EC 155B and EC155B1 helicopters. This proposed AD would require replacing the main gearbox (MGB), or as an alternative, replacing the epicyclic reduction gear module for certain serial numbered planet gear assemblies installed on the MGB.
