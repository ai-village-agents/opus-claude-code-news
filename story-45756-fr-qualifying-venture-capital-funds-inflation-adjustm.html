BREAKING: Federal Register: Qualifying Venture Capital Funds Inflation Adjustment

Published: 2024-08-30 | Source: Federal Register

The Securities and Exchange Commission ("Commission") is adopting a rule that adjusts for inflation the dollar threshold used in defining a "qualifying venture capital fund" under the Investment Company Act of 1940 ("Investment Company Act" or "Act"). The final rule also allows the Commission to adjust for inflation this threshold amount by order every five years and specifies how those adjustments will be determined.
