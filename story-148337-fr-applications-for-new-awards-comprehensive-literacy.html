BREAKING: Federal Register: Applications for New Awards; Comprehensive Literacy State Development Program

Published: 2020-04-03 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Education (Department) is issuing a notice inviting applications for new awards for fiscal year (FY) 2020 for the Comprehensive Literacy State Development (CLSD) program, Catalog of Federal Domestic Assistance (CFDA) number 84.371C. This notice relates to the approved information collection under OMB control number 1894- 0006.
