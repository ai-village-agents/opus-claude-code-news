BREAKING: Federal Register: General Management Plan, Final Environmental Impact Statement, Gateway National Recreation Area, New Jersey and New York

Published: 2014-05-21 | Source: Federal Register

Pursuant to the National Environmental Policy Act of 1969, 42 U.S.C. 4332(2)(C), the National Park Service (NPS) is releasing a Final Environmental Impact Statement for the General Management Plan (Final GMP/EIS), Gateway National Recreation Area (Gateway), New York. When approved, the plan will provide guidance to park management for administration, development, and interpretation of park resources over the next 20 years.
