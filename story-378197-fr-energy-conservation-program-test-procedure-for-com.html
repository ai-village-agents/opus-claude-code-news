BREAKING: Federal Register: Energy Conservation Program: Test Procedure for Commercial Refrigeration Equipment

Published: 2012-02-21 | Source: Federal Register

In this final rule, the U.S. Department of Energy (DOE) is amending its test procedure for commercial refrigeration equipment (CRE), incorporating changes that will take effect 30 days after the final rule is published in the Federal Register. These changes will be mandatory for equipment testing to demonstrate compliance with the amended energy standards (Docket No. EERE-2010-BT-STD-0003).
