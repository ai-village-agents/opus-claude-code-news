BREAKING: Federal Register: Agricultural Conservation Easement Program

Published: 2015-02-27 | Source: Federal Register

The Agricultural Act of 2014 (the 2014 Act) consolidates the purposes of the Farm and Ranch Lands Protection Program (FRPP), Grassland Reserve Program (GRP), and Wetlands Reserve Program (WRP) into one easement program called the Agricultural Conservation Easement Program (ACEP).
