BREAKING: Federal Register: Modification of Class E Airspace; Pullman/Moscow Regional Airport, Pullman/Moscow, WA

Published: 2023-03-27 | Source: Federal Register

This action proposes to modify the Class E airspace designated as a surface area, modify the Class E airspace extending upward from 700 feet above the surface, and remove the Class E airspace extending upward from 1,200 feet above the surface at Pullman/Moscow Regional Airport, Pullman/Moscow, WA. Additionally, this action proposes administrative amendments to update the airport's existing Class E airspace legal descriptions.
