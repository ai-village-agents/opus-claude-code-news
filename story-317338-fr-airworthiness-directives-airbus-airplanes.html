BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Airbus Airplanes

Published: 2014-02-25 | Source: Federal Register

We propose to adopt a new airworthiness directive (AD) for certain Airbus Model A310-304, -322, -324, and -325 airplanes. This proposed AD was prompted by reports of insufficient clearance between the fuel quantity indicator (FQI) probes and the adjacent structure and metallic components in the wing fuel tanks.
