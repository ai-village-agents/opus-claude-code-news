BREAKING: Federal Register: Special Conditions: Bombardier Inc. Model BD-700-2A12 and BD-700-2A13 Airplanes; Design Roll-Maneuver Condition

Published: 2015-04-30 | Source: Federal Register

These special conditions are issued for the Bombardier Inc. Model BD-700-2A12 and BD-700-2A13 airplanes. These airplanes will have a novel or unusual design feature associated with an electronic flight- control system that provides roll control of the airplanes through pilot inputs to the flight computers.
