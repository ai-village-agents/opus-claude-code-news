BREAKING: Federal Register: Certain Frozen Warmwater Shrimp From India: Rescission of Antidumping Duty Administrative Review; 2022-2023, in Part

Published: 2023-11-22 | Source: Federal Register

On April 11, 2023, the U.S. Department of Commerce (Commerce) initiated an administrative review of the antidumping duty (AD) order on certain frozen warmwater shrimp (shrimp) from India for the period of review (POR), February 1, 2022, through January 31, 2023, for 269 companies. Because all interested parties timely withdrew their requests for administrative review for certain companies, we are rescinding this administrative review with respect to those companies.
