BREAKING: Federal Register: Request for Information and Comments on the Preparation of the 2019-2024 National Outer Continental Shelf Oil and Gas Leasing Program MAA104000

Published: 2017-07-03 | Source: Federal Register

The Bureau of Ocean Energy Management (BOEM) is soliciting information and requesting comments on the preparation of a new five- year National Outer Continental Shelf Oil and Gas Leasing Program (National OCS Program) for 2019-2024 pursuant to the Outer Continental Shelf (OCS) Lands Act.
