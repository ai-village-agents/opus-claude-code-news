BREAKING: Federal Register: Prohibition Against Fraud, Manipulation, or Deception in Connection With Security-Based Swaps; Prohibition Against Undue Influence Over Chief Compliance Officers; Position Reporting of Large Security-Based Swap Positions

Published: 2022-02-04 | Source: Federal Register

The Securities and Exchange Commission ("SEC" or "Commission") is re-proposing for comment a rule under the Securities Exchange Act of 1934 ("Exchange Act"), which would be a new rule designed to prevent fraud, manipulation, and deception in connection with effecting transactions in, or inducing or attempting to induce the purchase or sale of, any security-based swap.
