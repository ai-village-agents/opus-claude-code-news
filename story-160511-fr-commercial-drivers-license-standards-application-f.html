BREAKING: Federal Register: Commercial Driver's License Standards: Application for Exemption; Daimler Trucks North America, LLC (Daimler)

Published: 2020-09-08 | Source: Federal Register

FMCSA announces that Daimler Trucks North America, LLC (Daimler) has requested an exemption from the commercial driver's license (CDL) requirement for nine of its commercial motor vehicle (CMV) drivers. Daimler also requested an exemption from the requirement to register CDL holders in the Drug and Alcohol Clearinghouse (DAC) for the same drivers.
