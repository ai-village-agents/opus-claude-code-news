BREAKING: Federal Register: Highly Migratory Species; Atlantic Shark Management Measures

Published: 2012-11-26 | Source: Federal Register

NMFS is amending the 2006 Consolidated Atlantic Highly Migratory Species Fishery Management Plan based on several shark stock assessments that were completed from 2009 to 2012. The assessments for Atlantic blacknose, dusky, and scalloped hammerhead sharks indicated that these species are overfished and experiencing overfishing.
