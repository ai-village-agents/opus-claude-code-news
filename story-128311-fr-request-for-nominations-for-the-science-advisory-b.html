BREAKING: Federal Register: Request for Nominations for the Science Advisory Board; Contaminant Candidate List 5 Panel

Published: 2021-07-15 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) Science Advisory Board (SAB) Staff Office requests public nominations of scientific experts to form a Panel to review the draft EPA document titled Contaminant Candidate List 5--Draft (CCL 5--Draft). This draft document presents a list of contaminants that are known or anticipated to occur in public water systems, and which may require future regulation under the Safe Drinking Water Act (SDWA).
