BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Airbus Helicopters

Published: 2022-06-29 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA proposes to adopt a new airworthiness directive (AD) for all Airbus Helicopters Model AS332C, AS332C1, AS332L, and AS332L1 helicopters. This proposed AD was prompted by review of maintenance instructions that showed conflicting methods of recording torque cycles for certain parts.
