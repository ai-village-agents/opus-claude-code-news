BREAKING: Federal Register: Intent To Award a Single-Source Supplement; National Consumer Voice for Quality Long-Term Care

Published: 2021-05-07 | Source: Federal Register

The Administration for Community Living (ACL) announces the intent to award a single-source supplement to the current cooperative agreement held by the National Consumer Voice for Quality Long-Term Care for the National Ombudsman Resource Center. The COVID-19 pandemic has significantly impacted residents of long-term care facilities, staff, families, and Long-Term Care Ombudsman programs.
