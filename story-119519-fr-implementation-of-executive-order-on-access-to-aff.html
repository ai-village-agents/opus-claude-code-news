BREAKING: Federal Register: Implementation of Executive Order on Access to Affordable Life-Saving Medications; Final Rule; Delay of Effective Date

Published: 2021-03-23 | Source: Federal Register

This final rule implements a further delay until July 20, 2021, of the effective date of the rule entitled "Implementation of Executive Order on Access to Affordable Life-saving Medications" published in the Federal Register on December 23, 2020. This rule was scheduled to take effect on March 22, 2021, after a delay from its original effective date of January 22, 2021.
