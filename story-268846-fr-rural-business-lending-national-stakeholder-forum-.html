BREAKING: Federal Register: Rural Business Lending National Stakeholder Forum 2016-Business and Industry Guaranteed Loan Program

Published: 2016-07-18 | Source: Federal Register

The Rural Business-Cooperative Service (RBS), an Agency within USDA Rural Development, is holding a forum to introduce the updated Business and Industry (B&I) Guaranteed Loanmaking and Servicing Regulations, as published in the Federal Register Friday, June 3, 2016. Major changes to the program include strengthened criteria for non- regulated lender participation, provisions for New Markets Tax Credit and Cooperative Stock Purchase Program, and modified loan scoring criteria.
