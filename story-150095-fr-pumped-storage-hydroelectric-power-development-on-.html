BREAKING: Federal Register: Pumped-Storage Hydroelectric Power Development on Seminoe Reservoir, Kendrick Project, Wyoming

Published: 2020-04-27 | Source: Federal Register

Current Federal policy allows non-Federal development of a potential electrical power resource on Federal water resource projects. This Notice provides background information, proposal content guidelines, and information concerning the selection of a non-Federal entity to develop pumped-storage hydroelectric power utilizing Seminoe Reservoir, a feature of the Kendrick Project, located in Wyoming.
