BREAKING: Federal Register: Gulf of Mexico, Outer Continental Shelf, Western Planning Area Oil and Gas Lease Sale 246

Published: 2015-07-17 | Source: Federal Register

BOEM has prepared a Record of Decision for proposed oil and gas WPA Lease Sale 246, which is scheduled for August 19, 2015. The proposed lease sale is in the Gulf of Mexico's WPA off the states of Texas and Louisiana. Proposed WPA Lease Sale 246 is the fourth WPA lease sale scheduled in the OCS Oil and Gas Leasing Program for 2012- 2017.
