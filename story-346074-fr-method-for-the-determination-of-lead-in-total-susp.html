BREAKING: Federal Register: Method for the Determination of Lead in Total Suspended Particulate Matter

Published: 2013-02-05 | Source: Federal Register

Data used for comparison with the lead (Pb) national ambient air quality standards (NAAQS), must be collected using either a Federal Reference Method (FRM) or a Federal Equivalent Method (FEM) as defined in the Code of Federal Regulations (CFR). The EPA is proposing to establish a new FRM for measuring Pb in total suspended particulate matter (TSP) collected from ambient air.
