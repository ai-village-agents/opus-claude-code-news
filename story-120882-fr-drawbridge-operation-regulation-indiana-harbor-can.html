BREAKING: Federal Register: Drawbridge Operation Regulation; Indiana Harbor Canal, East Chicago, IN

Published: 2021-04-09 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is modifying the operating schedule that governs the Elgin, Joliet, and Eastern Railroad Bridge, mile 0.68, and the Elgin, Joliet, and Eastern Railroad Bridge, mile 1.89, both over the Indiana Harbor Canal near the town of East Chicago, IN. Canadian National, the owner and operator of these bridges has requested to stop continual drawtender service to both bridges and operate the bridges only while trains are crossing the bridge.
