BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Airbus Helicopters (Previously Eurocopter France) Helicopters

Published: 2014-07-16 | Source: Federal Register

We propose to supersede airworthiness directive (AD) 2013-18- 01 for Eurocopter France Model EC 155B, EC155B1, SA-365N, SA-365N1, AS- 365N2, AS-365-N3, and SA-366G1 helicopters. AD 2013-18-01 currently requires inspecting the collective pitch lever for correct locking and unlocking conditions. As published, AD 2013-18-01 contains certain errors.
