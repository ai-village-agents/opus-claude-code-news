BREAKING: Federal Register: Effluent Limitations Guidelines and Standards for the Steam Electric Power Generating Point Source Category

Published: 2019-11-22 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (the EPA or the Agency) is proposing a regulation to revise the technology-based effluent limitations guidelines and standards (ELGs) for the steam electric power generating point source category applicable to flue gas desulfurization (FGD) wastewater and bottom ash (BA) transport water.
