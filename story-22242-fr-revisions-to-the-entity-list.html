BREAKING: Federal Register: Revisions to the Entity List

Published: 2025-11-12 | Source: Federal Register

The Bureau of Industry and Security (BIS) is removing one entity from the Entity List under the destination of China, People's Republic of (China). BIS is also removing six aliases associated with a different entity on the Entity List under the destination of China.
