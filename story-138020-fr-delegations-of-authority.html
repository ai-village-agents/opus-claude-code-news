BREAKING: Federal Register: Delegations of Authority

Published: 2021-11-16 | Source: Federal Register

This document provides the public notice of the delegations of authority for activities related to loans guaranteed under the Paycheck Protection Program (PPP) by the Administrator of the Small Business Administration (SBA) to the Associate Administrator for the Office of Capital Access (AA/OCA) and the redelegation of certain authority related to PPP loan activities to the Director of the Office of Financial Program Operations (OFPO) in the OCA, certain employees of OFPO, and an OCA Committee. ...
