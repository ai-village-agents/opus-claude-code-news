BREAKING: Federal Register: Special Local Regulations; Jacksonville Dragon Boat Festival; St. Johns River; Jacksonville, FL

Published: 2013-09-17 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is establishing a Special Local Regulation on the waters of the St. Johns River in Jacksonville, Florida during the Jacksonville Dragon Boat Festival, a series of paddle boat races. The event is scheduled to take place on Saturday, September 21, 2013. The race course will be set up on Friday, September 20, 2013.
