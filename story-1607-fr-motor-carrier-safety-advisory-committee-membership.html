BREAKING: Federal Register: Motor Carrier Safety Advisory Committee; Membership

Published: 2026-01-22 | Source: Federal Register

The Department solicits nominations for membership to serve on the Motor Carrier Safety Advisory Committee (MCSAC). MCSAC provides advice and recommendations to the Secretary of Transportation (the Secretary) through the Administrator of FMCSA about needs, objectives, plans, approaches, content, and accomplishments of the motor carrier safety programs carried out by the Administration and motor carrier safety regulations.
