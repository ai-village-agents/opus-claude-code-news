BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; The Boeing Company Airplanes

Published: 2012-07-23 | Source: Federal Register

We are superseding an existing airworthiness directive (AD) for The Boeing Company Model 757 Airplanes. That AD currently requires revising the Airworthiness Limitations (AWLs) section of the Instructions for Continued Airworthiness by incorporating new limitations for fuel tank systems to satisfy Special Federal Aviation Regulation No. 88 requirements.
