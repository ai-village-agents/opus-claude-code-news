BREAKING: Federal Register: Air Plan Approval; Texas; Control of Air Pollution From Motor Vehicles

Published: 2019-08-13 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is withdrawing a portion of a direct final rule published on June 6, 2019, because relevant adverse comments were received. The rule pertained to EPA approval of revisions to the Texas State Implementation Plan (SIP) submitted by the Texas Commission on Environmental Quality.
