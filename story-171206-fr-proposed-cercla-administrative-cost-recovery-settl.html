BREAKING: Federal Register: Proposed CERCLA Administrative Cost Recovery Settlement: Fletchers Paint Works Site, Milford, New Hampshire

Published: 2019-02-11 | Source: Federal Register

Notice is hereby given of a proposed administrative cost settlement for recovery of response costs concerning the Fletchers Paint Works Site, located in Milford, New Hampshire, with the Settling Party, the General Electric Company. The proposed settlement requires the Settling Party pay the Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) $3,214,000 to settle EPA's past response costs, which amount to approximately $4,047,398.
