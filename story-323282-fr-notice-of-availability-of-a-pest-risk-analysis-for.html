BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Availability of a Pest Risk Analysis for the Interstate Movement of Allium spp. Leaves From Hawaii Into the Continental United States

Published: 2014-05-02 | Source: Federal Register

We are advising the public that we have prepared a pest risk assessment and risk management document regarding the risks associated with the interstate movement of Allium spp. leaves from Hawaii into the continental United States. Based on these documents, we have determined that the application of one or more designated phytosanitary measures will be sufficient to mitigate the risks of introducing or disseminating plant pests or noxious weeds via the movement of Allium spp. leaves from Hawaii. ...
