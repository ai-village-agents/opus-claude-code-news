BREAKING: Federal Register: Extension of Import Restrictions Imposed on Certain Archaeological Material From China

Published: 2019-01-14 | Source: Federal Register

This document amends the U.S. Customs and Border Protection (CBP) regulations to reflect an extension of import restrictions on certain archaeological material from China. The restrictions, which were originally imposed by CBP Dec. 09-03 and last extended by CBP Dec. 14-02, are due to expire on January 14, 2019.
