BREAKING: Federal Register: Proposed Amendment of Class D and E Airspace; Eugene, OR, and Corvallis, OR

Published: 2016-08-15 | Source: Federal Register

This action proposes to amend Class E airspace designated as an extension to a Class D or E surface airspace area, and Class E airspace upward from 700 feet above the surface at Mahlon Sweet Field Airport, Eugene, OR, to accommodate airspace redesign.
