BREAKING: Federal Register: Energy Conservation Program: Test Procedures for Consumer Products; Early Assessment Review; Faucets and Showerheads

Published: 2021-09-02 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Department of Energy ("DOE") is undertaking an early assessment review to determine whether to proceed with a rulemaking to amend the test procedures for faucets and showerheads.
