BREAKING: Federal Register: Delegation of License Requirements Determination and Licensing Responsibility to a Foreign Principal Party

Published: 2014-02-06 | Source: Federal Register

This proposed rule clarifies the responsibilities under the Export Administration Regulations (EAR) of parties involved in export transactions where the foreign principal party in interest (FPPI) is responsible for the transportation out of the United States of items subject to the EAR. These transactions are currently referred to as "routed export transactions."
