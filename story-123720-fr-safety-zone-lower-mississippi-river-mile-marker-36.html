BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone; Lower Mississippi River, Mile Marker 365, Natchez, MS

Published: 2021-05-14 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is establishing a temporary safety zone for all navigable waters of the Lower Mississippi River (LMR), Mile Markers 364.5 through 365.5. The safety zone is needed to protect persons, property, and the marine environment from the potential safety hazards associated with a fireworks display in the vicinity of Natchez, MS.
