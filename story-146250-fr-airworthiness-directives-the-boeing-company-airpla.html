BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; The Boeing Company Airplanes

Published: 2020-03-09 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA proposes to supersede Airworthiness Directive (AD) 2019-03-06, which applies to certain The Boeing Company Model 737-300, -400, and -500 series airplanes. AD 2019-03-06 requires installing lanyard assemblies to the passenger service units (PSUs) and, for certain airplanes, on the life vest panels.
