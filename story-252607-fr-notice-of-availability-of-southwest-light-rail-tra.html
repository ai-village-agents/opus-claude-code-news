BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Availability of Southwest Light Rail Transit Project Amended Draft Section 4(f) Evaluation

Published: 2016-01-11 | Source: Federal Register

This notice announces the availability of the Southwest Light Rail Transit (LRT) Project Amended Draft Section 4(f) Evaluation, which includes preliminary Section 4(f) de minimis impact determinations for two newly identified Section 4(f) properties.
