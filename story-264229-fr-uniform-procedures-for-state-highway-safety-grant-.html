BREAKING: Federal Register: Uniform Procedures for State Highway Safety Grant Programs

Published: 2016-05-23 | Source: Federal Register

This action establishes revised uniform procedures implementing State highway safety grant programs, as a result of enactment of the Fixing America's Surface Transportation (FAST) Act. It also reorganizes, streamlines and updates some grant requirements. This document is being issued as an interim final rule to provide timely guidance to States about the application procedures for highway safety grants starting in year 2017.
