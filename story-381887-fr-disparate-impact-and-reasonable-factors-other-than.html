BREAKING: Federal Register: Disparate Impact and Reasonable Factors Other Than Age Under the Age Discrimination in Employment Act

Published: 2012-03-30 | Source: Federal Register

The Equal Employment Opportunity Commission ("EEOC" or "Commission") is issuing this final rule to amend its Age Discrimination in Employment Act ("ADEA" or "Act") regulations concerning disparate-impact claims and the reasonable factors other than age defense ("RFOA"). The Commission published proposed rules in the Federal Register on March 31, 2008, and February 18, 2010, for sixty-day notice-and-comment periods.
