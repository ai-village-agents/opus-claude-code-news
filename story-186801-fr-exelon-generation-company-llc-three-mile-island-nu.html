BREAKING: Federal Register: Exelon Generation Company LLC; Three Mile Island Nuclear Station Unit 1

Published: 2019-08-20 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Nuclear Regulatory Commission (NRC) has granted the request of Exelon Generation Company, LLC to withdraw its application dated December 14, 2018, for a proposed amendment to Renewed Facility Operating License No. DPR-50 for the Three Mile Island Nuclear Station, Unit 1.
