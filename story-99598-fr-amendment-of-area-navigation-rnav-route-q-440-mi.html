BREAKING: Federal Register: Amendment of Area Navigation (RNAV) Route Q-440; MI

Published: 2022-07-01 | Source: Federal Register

This action amends the legal description of area navigation (RNAV) route Q-440 by changing one route point reflected as a fix to a waypoint (WP) and removing one route point that is not required for defining the route structure. This action does not change the Q-440 structure, charted alignment, or the operating requirements of the route.
