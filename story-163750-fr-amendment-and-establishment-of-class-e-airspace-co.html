BREAKING: Federal Register: Amendment and Establishment of Class E Airspace; Coeur D'Alene, ID

Published: 2020-10-16 | Source: Federal Register

This action modifies the Class E surface area airspace and establishes Class E airspace extending upward from 700 feet above the surface at Coeur D'Alene-Pappy Boyington Field, Coeur D'Alene, ID, to support the Instrument Flight Rules (IFR) operations under standard instrument approach and departure procedures at the airport, for the safety and management of aircraft within the National Airspace System.
