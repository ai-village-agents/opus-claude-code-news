BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; The Boeing Company Airplanes

Published: 2017-08-25 | Source: Federal Register

We are adopting a new airworthiness directive (AD) for all The Boeing Company Model 737-300, -400, and -500 series airplanes. This AD was prompted by a manufacturer's review that showed that the fuel tank access door at a certain wing buttock line did not have an engineered ground path with the mating wing structure.
