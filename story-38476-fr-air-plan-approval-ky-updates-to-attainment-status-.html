BREAKING: Federal Register: Air Plan Approval; KY; Updates to Attainment Status Designations

Published: 2024-06-03 | Source: Federal Register

The Commonwealth of Kentucky, through the Kentucky Energy and Environment Cabinet (Cabinet), Kentucky Division for Air Quality (KDAQ), submitted a revision to the Kentucky State Implementation Plan (SIP) on November 29, 2022. The SIP revision updates, as of June 9, 2022, the geographical boundary description and attainment status designation for the Henderson-Webster SO2 nonattainment area for the 2010 primary SO2 National Ambient Air Quality Standards (NAAQS).
