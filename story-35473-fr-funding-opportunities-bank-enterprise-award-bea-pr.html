BREAKING: Federal Register: Funding Opportunities: Bank Enterprise Award (BEA) Program; FY 2024 Funding Round

Published: 2024-04-29 | Source: Federal Register

This NOFA is issued in connection with the fiscal year (FY) 2024 funding round of the Bank Enterprise Award Program (BEA Program). The BEA Program is administered by the U.S. Department of the Treasury's Community Development Financial Institutions Fund (CDFI Fund).
