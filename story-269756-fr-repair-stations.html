BREAKING: Federal Register: Repair Stations

Published: 2016-07-27 | Source: Federal Register

The Federal Aviation Administration (FAA) is revising its repair station rules to remove the requirement that a repair station with an airframe rating provide suitable permanent housing to enclose the largest type and model aircraft listed on its operations specifications.
