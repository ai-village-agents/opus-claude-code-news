BREAKING: Federal Register: Energy Conservation Program: Test Procedure for VRF Multi-Split Systems

Published: 2021-12-10 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Department of Energy ("DOE") proposes to amend the test procedure for variable refrigerant flow multi-split air conditioners and heat pumps ("VRF multi-split systems") to incorporate by reference the latest version of the industry test standard. DOE also proposes to adopt the integrated energy efficiency ratio metric in its test procedures for VRF multi-split systems.
