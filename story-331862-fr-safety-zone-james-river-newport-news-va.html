BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone, James River; Newport News, VA

Published: 2014-08-11 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is establishing a safety zone on the navigable waters of the James River in Newport News, VA for multiple periods, one hour in length each, on all weekdays from August 6 until August 15, 2014. This action will restrict vessel traffic movement in the designated area during the United States Navy's operation involving unmanned and remote-operated crafts.
