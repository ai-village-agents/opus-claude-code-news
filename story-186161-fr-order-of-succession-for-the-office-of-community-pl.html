BREAKING: Federal Register: Order of Succession for the Office of Community Planning and Development

Published: 2019-08-13 | Source: Federal Register

In this notice, the Secretary of HUD designates the Order of Succession for the Office of Community Planning and Development (CPD). This Order of Succession supersedes all prior Orders of Succession for the Assistant Secretary for Community Planning and Development, including the Order of Succession published on April 20, 2015.
