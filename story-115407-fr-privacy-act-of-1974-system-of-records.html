BREAKING: Federal Register: Privacy Act of 1974; System of Records

Published: 2021-01-27 | Source: Federal Register

In accordance with the Privacy Act of 1974 (Privacy Act), the U.S. Merit Systems Protection Board (MSPB or Board) proposes to establish a new MSPB system of records titled "Surveys for Special Studies of the Civil Service." This system of records contains information that MSPB collects, maintains, and uses in order to develop and administer the Merit Principles Survey (MPS) and other surveys for special studies of the civil service and to evaluate and distribute the results of such surveys.
