BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Airbus

Published: 2018-06-11 | Source: Federal Register

We propose to adopt a new airworthiness directive (AD) for certain Airbus Model A350-941 airplanes. This proposed AD was prompted by the discovery of inadequate corrosion protection in certain areas of the horizontal stabilizer and the rear fuselage cone structure.
