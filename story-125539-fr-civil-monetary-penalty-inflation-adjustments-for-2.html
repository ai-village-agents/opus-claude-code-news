BREAKING: Federal Register: Civil Monetary Penalty Inflation Adjustments for 2021; Correction

Published: 2021-06-09 | Source: Federal Register

On May 10, 2021, we published a final rule amending the U.S. Department of Agriculture's civil monetary penalty regulations by making inflation adjustments as mandated by the Federal Civil Penalties Inflation Adjustment Act Improvements Act of 2015. We are correcting an error that appeared in that final rule.
