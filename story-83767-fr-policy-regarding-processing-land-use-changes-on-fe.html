BREAKING: Federal Register: Policy Regarding Processing Land Use Changes on Federally Acquired or Federally Conveyed Airport Land

Published: 2023-12-08 | Source: Federal Register

This action finalizes the FAA's policy on the FAA's procedures for processing land use changes on federally acquired or federally conveyed airport land or in situations where a land use change impacts the safe and efficient operation of aircraft or safety of people and property on the ground related to aircraft operations.
