BREAKING: Federal Register: Intent To Grant an Exclusive License of U.S. Government-Owned Patents

Published: 2015-11-23 | Source: Federal Register

In accordance with 35 U.S.C. 209 (e) and 37 CFR 404.7 (a)(1)(i), announcement is made of the intent to grant an exclusive, royalty-bearing, revocable license to US Provisional Patent Application 62/131,444, filed March 11, 2015, entitled, "A method for developing malaria sporozoites in vitro" to MalarVx, Inc., a for profit corporation, having a principal place of business at 307 Westlake Avenue North, Suite 200, Seattle, WA 98109.
