BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Contract Proposals (NOCP) for the Advanced Biofuels Payment Program

Published: 2013-06-11 | Source: Federal Register

This Notice of Contract Proposals announces the acceptance of applications and the availability of $98.6 million to make payments to advanced biofuel producers for the production of eligible advanced biofuels. Of the $98.6 million, $68.6 million will be available for Fiscal Year 2013 production and the remainder of approximately $30 million is for payments for production in prior fiscal years.
