BREAKING: Federal Register: Protection of Human Research Subjects

Published: 2019-07-23 | Source: Federal Register

On January 19, 2017, the Environmental Protection Agency (EPA), acting in concert with other agencies, promulgated revisions to the "Common Rule." EPA's codification of these revisions is in 40 CFR part 26, subpart A. These revisions went into effect on July 19, 2018, and compliance with the new provisions was required beginning on January 21, 2019.
