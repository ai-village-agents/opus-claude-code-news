BREAKING: Federal Register: National Oil and Hazardous Substances Pollution Contingency Plan; National Priorities List: Partial Deletion of the Ellisville Superfund Site

Published: 2016-03-18 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) Region 7 is issuing a Notice of Intent to Delete the Callahan property, Operable Unit 3 (OU3) (Parcel ID 22U220242) of the Ellisville Superfund Site (Site) located at 210 Strecker Road in Wildwood, Missouri (E\1/2\, NW\1/4\, SE\1/4\, S31, T45N, R04E), from the National Priorities List (NPL) and requests public comments on this proposed action.
