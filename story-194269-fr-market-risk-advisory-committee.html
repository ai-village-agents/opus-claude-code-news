BREAKING: Federal Register: Market Risk Advisory Committee

Published: 2019-11-26 | Source: Federal Register

The Commodity Futures Trading Commission (CFTC) announces that on December 11, 2019, from 9:30 a.m. to 1:00 p.m., the Market Risk Advisory Committee (MRAC) will hold a public meeting in the Conference Center at the CFTC's Washington, DC, headquarters.
