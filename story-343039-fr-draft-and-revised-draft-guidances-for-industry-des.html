BREAKING: Federal Register: Draft and Revised Draft Guidances for Industry Describing Product-Specific Bioequivalence Recommendations; Availability

Published: 2014-12-30 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA) is announcing the availability of additional draft and revised draft product-specific bioequivalence (BE) recommendations. These recommendations provide product-specific guidance on the design of BE studies to support abbreviated new drug applications (ANDAs).
