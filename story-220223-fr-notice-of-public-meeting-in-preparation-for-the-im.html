BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Public Meeting in Preparation for the IMO's One Hundred Twenty First Session of IMO's Council

Published: 2018-11-02 | Source: Federal Register

The United States Coast Guard will conduct an open meeting in Washington, DC in preparation for the upcoming one hundred twenty first session of IMO Council to be held at the IMO Headquarters, United Kingdom, November 19-23, 2018.
