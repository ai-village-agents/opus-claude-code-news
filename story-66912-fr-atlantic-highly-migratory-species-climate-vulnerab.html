BREAKING: Federal Register: Atlantic Highly Migratory Species; Climate Vulnerability Assessment Public Meeting and Webinar

Published: 2023-05-08 | Source: Federal Register

NMFS will hold a 3-day Atlantic Highly Migratory Species (HMS) Climate Vulnerability Assessment (CVA) public workshop and webinar in May 2023. NMFS uses CVAs to identify which species may be most vulnerable based on their exposure to projected changes in the environment (e.g., warming oceans) and their sensitivity or adaptability to handle those changes based on their life history characteristics.
