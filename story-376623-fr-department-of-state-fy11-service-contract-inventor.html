BREAKING: Federal Register: Department of State FY11 Service Contract Inventory

Published: 2012-02-03 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of State has publically released its Service Contract Inventory for FY11 and its analysis of the FY10 inventory. They are available here: http://csm.state.gov/. Section 743 of Division C of the FY 2010 Consolidated Appropriations Act, Public Law 111-117, requires Department of State, and other civilian agencies, to submit an annual inventory of service contracts.
