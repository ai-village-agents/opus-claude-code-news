BREAKING: Federal Register: Amendments to the Regulatory Capital, Capital Plan, and Stress Test Rules

Published: 2018-04-25 | Source: Federal Register

The Board is inviting comment on a notice of proposed rulemaking (proposal) that would integrate the Board's regulatory capital rule (capital rule) and the Board's Comprehensive Capital Analysis and Review (CCAR) and stress test rules in order to simplify the capital regime applicable to firms subject to the capital plan rule.
