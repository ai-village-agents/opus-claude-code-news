BREAKING: Federal Register: Energy Conservation Program: Test Procedure for Dishwashers

Published: 2023-01-18 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Department of Energy ("DOE") is amending the current test procedures for dishwashers, adopting a new test procedure appendix, incorporating by reference Association of Home Appliance Manufacturers ("AHAM") standards--AHAM DW-1-2020 and DW-2-2020--and applying certain provisions of the industry standards to the test procedures appendices.
