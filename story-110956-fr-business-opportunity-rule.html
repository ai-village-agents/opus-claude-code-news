BREAKING: Federal Register: Business Opportunity Rule

Published: 2022-11-25 | Source: Federal Register

The Federal Trade Commission ("FTC" or "Commission") is requesting public comment on its "Business Opportunity Rule" ("Rule"), the trade regulation rule governing the sale of certain business opportunities. The Commission is soliciting comments about the efficiency, costs, benefits, and regulatory impact of the Rule, as part of its ten-year regulatory review plan.
