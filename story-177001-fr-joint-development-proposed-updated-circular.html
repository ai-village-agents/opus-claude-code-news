BREAKING: Federal Register: Joint Development: Proposed Updated Circular

Published: 2019-04-18 | Source: Federal Register

The Federal Transit Administration (FTA) has placed in the docket and on its website proposed changes to an existing Circular (7050.1A) on joint development projects using FTA funds or FTA-funded property.
