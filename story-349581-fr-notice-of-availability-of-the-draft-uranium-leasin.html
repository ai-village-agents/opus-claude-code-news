BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Availability of the Draft Uranium Leasing Program Programmatic Environmental Impact Statement

Published: 2013-03-15 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Department of Energy (DOE) announces the availability of the Draft Uranium Leasing Program Programmatic Environmental Impact Statement (Draft ULP PEIS, DOE/EIS-0472D), for public comment. DOE is also announcing the dates, times, and locations for public hearings to receive comments on the Draft ULP PEIS.
