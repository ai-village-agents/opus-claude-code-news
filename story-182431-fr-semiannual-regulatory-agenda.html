BREAKING: Federal Register: Semiannual Regulatory Agenda

Published: 2019-06-24 | Source: Federal Register

This document sets forth the regulatory agenda of the Committee for Purchase From People Who Are Blind or Severely Disabled. This agenda is issued in accordance with Executive Order 12866 and the Regulatory Flexibility Act. The agenda lists regulations that are currently under development or review or that the Committee expects to have under development or review during the next 12 months.
