BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Eurocopter France Helicopters

Published: 2012-07-26 | Source: Federal Register

We propose to adopt a new airworthiness directive (AD) for Eurocopter France (Eurocopter) Model AS350BA helicopters with certain AERAZUR emergency flotation gear container assemblies installed. This proposed AD would require replacing each affected emergency flotation gear container assembly (container assembly) at specified time limits based on the date of manufacture.
