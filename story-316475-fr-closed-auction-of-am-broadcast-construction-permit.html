BREAKING: Federal Register: Closed Auction of AM Broadcast Construction Permits Scheduled for May 6, 2014; Notice and Filing Requirements, Minimum Opening Bids, Upfront Payments, and Other Procedures for Auction 84

Published: 2014-02-13 | Source: Federal Register
