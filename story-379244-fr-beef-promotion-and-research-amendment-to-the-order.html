BREAKING: Federal Register: Beef Promotion and Research; Amendment to the Order

Published: 2012-03-02 | Source: Federal Register

This proposed rule would expand the contracting authority as established under the Beef Promotion and Research (Order). The Beef Research and Information Act (Act) requires that the Beef Promotion Operating Committee (BPOC) enter into contracts with established national non-profit industry-governed organizations including the Federation of State Beef Councils to implement programs of promotion, research, consumer information, and industry information.
