BREAKING: Federal Register: Toluene Diisocyanates (TDI) and Related Compounds; Significant New Use Rule

Published: 2015-01-15 | Source: Federal Register

Under the Toxic Substance Control Act (TSCA), EPA is proposing a significant new use rule (SNUR) for 2,4-toluene diisocyanate, 2,6- toluene diisocyanate, toluene diisocyanate unspecified isomers (these three chemical substances are hereafter referred to as toluene diisocyanates or TDI) and related compounds as identified in this proposed rule.
