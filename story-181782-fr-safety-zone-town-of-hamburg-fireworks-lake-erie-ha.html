BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone; Town of Hamburg Fireworks, Lake Erie, Hamburg, NY

Published: 2019-06-17 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is establishing a temporary safety zone for navigable waters within a 420-foot radius of the launch site located near Woodlawn Beach, Hamburg, NY. This safety zone is intended to restrict vessels from portions of Lake Erie during the Town of Hamburg fireworks display. The safety zone is necessary to protect mariners and vessels from potential hazards associated with a fireworks display.
