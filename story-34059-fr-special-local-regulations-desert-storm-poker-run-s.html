BREAKING: Federal Register: Special Local Regulations; Desert Storm Poker Run Shootout, Lake Havasu, AZ

Published: 2024-04-10 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard will enforce the Desert Storm Poker Run Shootout special local regulations on the waters of Lake Havasu, Arizona on April 26, 2024, through April 28, 2024. These special local regulations are necessary to provide for the safety of the participants, crew, spectators, sponsor vessels, and general users of the waterway.
