BREAKING: Federal Register: Opportunity for Co-Sponsorship of the President's Challenge Physical Activity and Fitness Awards Program

Published: 2013-12-17 | Source: Federal Register

The President's Council on Fitness, Sports, and Nutrition (PCFSN) announces the opportunity for non-federal public and private sector entities to co-sponsor and administer a series of financially self-sustaining activities related to the President's Challenge Physical Activity and Fitness Awards Program (President's Challenge).
