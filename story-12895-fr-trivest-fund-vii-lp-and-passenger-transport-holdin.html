BREAKING: Federal Register: Trivest Fund VII, L.P. and Passenger Transport Holdings, L.P.-Acquisition of Control-Roadrunner Charters Inc. et al.

Published: 2025-06-04 | Source: Federal Register

On May 5, 2025, Trivest Fund VII, L.P. (Trivest), a noncarrier, and its noncarrier subsidiary Passenger Transport Holdings, L.P. (PTH) (collectively, Applicants), filed an application for authority to acquire all voting securities of Roadrunner Charters, Inc. (Roadrunner), and its affiliate, Clark Charters and Travel, Inc., d/b/a Clark Travel Enterprises (Clark) (collectively, Acquired Carriers).
