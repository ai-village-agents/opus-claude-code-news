BREAKING: Federal Register: Proposed Appointment to the National Indian Gaming Commission

Published: 2016-02-08 | Source: Federal Register

The Indian Gaming Regulatory Act provides for a three-person National Indian Gaming Commission. One member, the Chair, is appointed by the President with the advice and consent of the Senate. Two associate members are appointed by the Secretary of the Interior (Secretary). Before appointing members, the Secretary is required to provide public notice of a proposed appointment and allow a comment period.
