BREAKING: Federal Register: United States-Israel Free Trade Area Implementation Act; Re-Designation of Qualifying Industrial Zones

Published: 2013-03-12 | Source: Federal Register

Under the United States-Israel Free Trade Area Implementation Act (IFTA Act), articles of qualifying industrial zones (QIZs) encompassing portions of Israel and Jordan or Israel and Egypt are eligible to receive duty-free treatment.
