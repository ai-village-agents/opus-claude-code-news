BREAKING: Federal Register: Forest River, Inc., Grant of Petition for Decision of Inconsequential Noncompliance

Published: 2019-03-01 | Source: Federal Register

Forest River, Inc. (Forest River), has determined that certain model year (MY) 2017-2018 Forest River buses and school buses do not fully comply with Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard (FMVSS) No. 205, Glazing Materials. Forest River filed two separate noncompliance reports, both dated November 30, 2017.
