BREAKING: Federal Register: Compensation, Retirement Programs, and Related Benefits

Published: 2013-02-19 | Source: Federal Register

On December 4, 2012, the Farm Credit Council (Council) filed a Petition for Regulatory Change (Petition) with the Farm Credit Administration (FCA, we, or our) on behalf of its Farm Credit System (System) members. The Council requested in the Petition that we repeal the provisions of the recently effective final rule regarding "Compensation, Retirement Programs, and Related Benefits," that require a non-binding, advisory vote on senior officer compensation.
