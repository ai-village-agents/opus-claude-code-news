BREAKING: Federal Register: Special Local Regulation; East Tawas Offshore Gran Prix, Tawas Bay; East Tawas, MI

Published: 2012-06-25 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is establishing a special local regulation on Tawas Bay, Michigan. This action is necessary and intended to ensure safety of life on the navigable waters immediately prior to, during, and immediately after the East Tawas Offshore Gran Prix boat race. This special local regulation will establish restrictions upon, and control movement of, vessels in a portion of Tawas Bay.
