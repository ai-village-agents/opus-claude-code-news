BREAKING: Federal Register: Request for Information on Computer Security Incident Coordination (CSIC)

Published: 2013-09-20 | Source: Federal Register

NIST is extending the deadline for submitting comments relating to Computer Security Incident Coordination. NIST experienced technical difficulties with receiving email comments from the time the notice was published on June 28, 2013 through July 31, 2013. Due to the technical difficulties experienced during the comment period, NIST may not have received all comments submitted via email on or before the closing date, July 29, 2013.
