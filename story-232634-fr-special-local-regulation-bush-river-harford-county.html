BREAKING: Federal Register: Special Local Regulation; Bush River, Harford County, MD

Published: 2017-04-24 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is establishing special local regulations for certain waters of the Bush River, including Otter Point Creek. This action is necessary to provide for the safety of life on these navigable waters located near Flying Point Park in Harford County, MD, during a high-speed power boat racing event on May 6, 2017, and May 7, 2017.
