BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Intent To Grant an Exclusive Patent License

Published: 2017-06-08 | Source: Federal Register

NASA hereby gives notice of its intent to grant an exclusive patent license in the United States to practice the invention described and claimed in U.S. Patent Application Serial No. 14/871,127 entitled "Magnetically Retained Relief Valve," KSC-13870, to MagPress Valves having its principal place of business in Round Rock, Texas.
