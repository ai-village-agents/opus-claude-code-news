BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone; Piscataqua River Turning Basin Dredge Project, Portsmouth, NH

Published: 2021-10-26 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is establishing two temporary safety zones for the navigable waters of the Piscataqua River in Portsmouth Harbor. The first safety zone will be a 100-yard radius around any vessel, barge, or dredging equipment engaged in dredging operations. The second safety zone will be a 500-yard radius around any vessel, barge, or dredging equipment engaged in blasting operations and any blasting worksites.
