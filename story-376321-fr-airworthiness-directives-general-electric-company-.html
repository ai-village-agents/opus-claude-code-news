BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; General Electric Company Turbofan Engines

Published: 2012-01-31 | Source: Federal Register

We are superseding two existing airworthiness directives (ADs) for General Electric Company (GE) CF6-45 and CF6-50 series turbofan engines with certain low-pressure turbine (LPT) rotor stage 3 disks installed. The existing ADs currently require inspections of high- pressure turbine (HPT) and LPT rotors, engine checks, and vibration surveys.
