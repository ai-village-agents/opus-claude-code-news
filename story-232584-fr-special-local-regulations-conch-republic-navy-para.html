BREAKING: Federal Register: Special Local Regulations; Conch Republic Navy Parade and Battle, Key West, FL

Published: 2017-04-21 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard will enforce the special local regulations for the Conch Republic Navy Parade and Battle in Key West, Florida, from 6:30 p.m. until 8:00 p.m. on April 28, 2017. Our regulation for Recurring Marine Events in Captain of the Port Key West Zone identifies the regulated area for this event.
