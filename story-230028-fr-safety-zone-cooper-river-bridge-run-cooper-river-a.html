BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone; Cooper River Bridge Run, Cooper River and Town Creek Reaches, Charleston, SC

Published: 2017-03-21 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is establishing a safety zone on the waters of the Cooper River and Town Creek Reaches in Charleston, South Carolina during the Cooper River Bridge Run. The Cooper River Bridge Run is a 10-K run across the Arthur Ravenel Bridge. The safety zone is necessary for the safety of event participants, spectators, and vessels transiting the navigable waters of the Cooper River and Town Creek Reaches during this event.
