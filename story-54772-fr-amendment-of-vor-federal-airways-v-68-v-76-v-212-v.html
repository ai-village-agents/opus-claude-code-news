BREAKING: Federal Register: Amendment of VOR Federal Airways V-68, V-76, V-212, V-222, and V-558, and United States Area Navigation Route T-220 in the Vicinity of Industry, TX

Published: 2024-12-09 | Source: Federal Register

This action amends Very High Frequency Omnidirectional Range (VOR) Federal Airways V-68, V-212, and V-222, and United States Area Navigation (RNAV) Route T-220; and revokes VOR Federal Airway V-558. The proposed V-76 airway amendment is removed from this action due to the amendment already being accomplished by a separate airspace docket action.
