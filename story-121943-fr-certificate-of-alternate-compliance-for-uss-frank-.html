BREAKING: Federal Register: Certificate of Alternate Compliance for USS FRANK E. PETERSON JR. (DDG 121)

Published: 2021-04-22 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Navy hereby announces that a Certificate of Alternate Compliance has been issued for USS FRANK E. PETERSON JR. (DDG 121).
