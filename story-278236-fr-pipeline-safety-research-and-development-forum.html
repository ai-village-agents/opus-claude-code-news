BREAKING: Federal Register: Pipeline Safety: Research and Development Forum

Published: 2016-11-03 | Source: Federal Register

This notice announces a PHMSA sponsored Pipeline Safety Research and Development Forum. PHMSA periodically holds this public forum to generate a national research agenda that fosters solutions for the many challenges with pipeline safety and with protecting the environment. This forum allows public, government, and industry pipeline stakeholders to develop a consensus on the technical gaps and challenges for future research.
