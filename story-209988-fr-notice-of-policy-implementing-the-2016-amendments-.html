BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Policy Implementing the 2016 Amendments to STCW in Support of the Polar Code

Published: 2018-06-22 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is announcing the availability of CG-MMC Policy Letter No. 02-18 titled "Guidelines for Qualifications of Personnel for Issuing STCW Endorsements for Basic and Advanced Polar Code Operations." This policy provides guidance for the issuance of Merchant Mariner Credential endorsements in accordance with the International Convention on Standards of Training, Certification and Watchkeeping for Seafarers, 1978, as amended, for Basic and Advanced Polar Code Operations.
