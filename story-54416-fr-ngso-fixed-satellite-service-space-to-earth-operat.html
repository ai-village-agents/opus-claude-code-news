BREAKING: Federal Register: NGSO Fixed-Satellite Service (Space-to-Earth) Operations in the 17.3-17.8 GHz Band

Published: 2024-12-05 | Source: Federal Register

In this document, the Federal Communications Commission (FCC or Commission) adopts rules to permit use of the 17.3-17.7 GHz band by non-geostationary satellite orbit (NGSO) space stations operating in the fixed-satellite service (FSS) in the space-to-Earth (downlink) direction.
