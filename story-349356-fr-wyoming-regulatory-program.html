BREAKING: Federal Register: Wyoming Regulatory Program

Published: 2013-03-14 | Source: Federal Register

We are announcing receipt of a proposed amendment to the Wyoming regulatory program (hereinafter, the "Wyoming program") under the Surface Mining Control and Reclamation Act of 1977 ("SMCRA" or "the Act"). Wyoming proposes revisions to rules concerning valid existing rights and individual civil penalties.
