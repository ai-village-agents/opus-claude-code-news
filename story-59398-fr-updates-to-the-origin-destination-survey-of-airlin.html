BREAKING: Federal Register: Updates to the Origin-Destination Survey of Airline Passengers

Published: 2023-01-31 | Source: Federal Register

DOT finalizes amendments to update the collection and processing of aviation traffic data in the Origin--Destination Survey of Airline Passenger Traffic (O&D). As part of this action, DOT is expanding the number of reporting air carriers, the sample size collected, and the scope of the data collected.
