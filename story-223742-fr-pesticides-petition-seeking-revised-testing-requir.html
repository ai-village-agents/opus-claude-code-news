BREAKING: Federal Register: Pesticides; Petition Seeking Revised Testing Requirements of Pesticides Prior to Registration; Request for Comment

Published: 2018-12-21 | Source: Federal Register

EPA is seeking public comment on a petition from the Center for Food Safety (CFS) requesting that the Agency revise testing requirements for pesticides prior to registration. The petitioner, CFS, requests that the Agency require testing for whole pesticide formulations to account for the toxicological effects of inert and adjuvant ingredients and the testing of tank mixes to assess the interaction between pesticide ingredients.
