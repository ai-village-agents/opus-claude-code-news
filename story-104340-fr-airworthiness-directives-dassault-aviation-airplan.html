BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Dassault Aviation Airplanes

Published: 2022-09-02 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA is adopting a new airworthiness directive (AD) for all Dassault Aviation Model FALCON 7X airplanes. This AD was prompted by a report of a failed extension of inboard slats during the landing phase, which was not indicated to the flightcrew by the crew alerting system.
