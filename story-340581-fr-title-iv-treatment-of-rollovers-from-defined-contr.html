BREAKING: Federal Register: Title IV Treatment of Rollovers From Defined Contribution Plans to Defined Benefit Plans

Published: 2014-11-25 | Source: Federal Register

In April 2014, PBGC proposed to amend its regulations to clarify the treatment of benefits resulting from a rollover distribution from a defined contribution plan to a defined benefit plan, if the defined benefit plan was terminated and trusteed by PBGC.
