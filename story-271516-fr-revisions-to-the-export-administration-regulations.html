BREAKING: Federal Register: Revisions to the Export Administration Regulations (EAR): Harmonization of the Destination Control Statements

Published: 2016-08-17 | Source: Federal Register

This final rule implements changes that were proposed on May 22, 2015, in a proposed rule entitled Revisions to the Export Administration Regulations (EAR): Harmonization of the Destination Control Statements.
