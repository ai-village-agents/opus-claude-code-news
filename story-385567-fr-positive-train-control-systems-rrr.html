BREAKING: Federal Register: Positive Train Control Systems (RRR)

Published: 2012-05-14 | Source: Federal Register

FRA amends the regulations implementing a provision of the Rail Safety Improvement Act of 2008 that requires certain passenger and freight railroads to install positive train control (PTC) systems.
