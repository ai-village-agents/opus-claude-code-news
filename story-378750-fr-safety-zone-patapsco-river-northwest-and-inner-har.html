BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone; Patapsco River, Northwest and Inner Harbors, Baltimore, MD

Published: 2012-02-27 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard proposes to establish a temporary safety zone upon certain waters of the Patapsco River, Northwest Harbor and Inner Harbor during the movement of the historic sloop-of-war USS CONSTELLATION on May 25, 2012.
