BREAKING: Federal Register: Meeting of the U.S. Naval Academy Board of Visitors

Published: 2013-05-17 | Source: Federal Register

The United States Naval Academy Board of Visitors will meet to make such inquiry, as the Board shall deem necessary, into the state of morale and discipline, the curriculum, instruction, physical equipment, fiscal affairs, and academic methods of the Naval Academy.
