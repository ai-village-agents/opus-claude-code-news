BREAKING: Federal Register: Measuring Educational Gain in the National Reporting System for Adult Education; Correction

Published: 2017-03-15 | Source: Federal Register

On March 9, 2017, the U.S. Department of Education published a 30-day comment period notice in the Federal Register (82 FR 13103) seeking public comment for an information collection entitled, "Measuring Educational Gain in the National Reporting System for Adult Education." The date of the comment period included in the notice was for 60 days after publication of the notice instead of 30 days.
