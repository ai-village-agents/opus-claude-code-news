BREAKING: Federal Register: Anchorage Grounds; Delaware Bay and Atlantic Ocean, Delaware

Published: 2019-11-29 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is considering amending its regulations to establish new anchorage grounds in the Delaware Bay and Atlantic Ocean. We are considering this action after receiving requests suggesting additional anchorage grounds are necessary to accommodate current and future vessel traffic, improve navigation safety, and because traditional anchorage areas may not be available due to planned or potential offshore wind energy development.
