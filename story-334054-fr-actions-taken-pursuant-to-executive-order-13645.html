BREAKING: Federal Register: Actions Taken Pursuant to Executive Order 13645

Published: 2014-09-05 | Source: Federal Register

The Treasury Department's Office of Foreign Assets Control ("OFAC") is publishing the names of three entities whose property and interests in property are blocked pursuant to Executive Order 13645 of June 3, 2013, as well as the names of three vessels in which one of the entities has a property interest.
