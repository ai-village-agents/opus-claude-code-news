BREAKING: Federal Register: Food and Drug Administration Small Business and Industry Assistance Regulatory Education for Industry Spring Conference; Public Conference

Published: 2017-04-25 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA) Center for Drug Evaluation and Research (CDER), together with the Center for Devices and Radiological Health (CDRH), is sponsoring a 2-day public conference entitled "FDA Small Business and Industry Assistance Regulatory Education for Industry (REdI) Spring Conference." The goal of this public conference is to provide direct, relevant, and helpful information on the key aspects of drug and medical device regulations in order to increase regulatory certainty...
