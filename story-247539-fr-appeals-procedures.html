BREAKING: Federal Register: Appeals Procedures

Published: 2017-10-30 | Source: Federal Register

The NCUA Board (Board) is adopting this final rule to establish procedures to govern appeals to the Board. The rule establishes a uniform procedure that will apply to agency regulations that currently have their own embedded appeals provisions. Accordingly, this final rule will replace those current provisions.
