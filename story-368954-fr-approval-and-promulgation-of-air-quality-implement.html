BREAKING: Federal Register: Approval and Promulgation of Air Quality Implementation Plans; Indiana; Indiana PM2.5 NSR

Published: 2013-11-01 | Source: Federal Register

EPA is proposing to approve revisions to Indiana's state implementation plan as requested by the Indiana Department of Environmental Management (IDEM) to EPA on July 12, 2012, and December 12, 2012.
