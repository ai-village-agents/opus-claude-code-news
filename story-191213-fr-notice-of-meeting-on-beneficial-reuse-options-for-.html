BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Meeting on Beneficial Reuse Options for Used Lubricating Oil

Published: 2019-10-16 | Source: Federal Register

The Office of Oil and Natural Gas will host a meeting on the subject of beneficial reuse options for used lubricating oil, including ways to increase the responsible collection and use of used oil (current and future use options); disseminate public information concerning sustainable reuse options for used oil; and promote sustainable reuse of used oil by Federal agencies, recipients of Federal grant funds, entities contracting with the Federal Government, and the general public.
