BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone, Barnegat Inlet; Barnegat Light, NJ

Published: 2014-03-21 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard has established a safety zone in Barnegat Inlet from March 7, 2014 to November 31, 2014 around the Barnegat Inlet north jetty repair project. The Army Corps of Engineers has contracted with Agate Construction to repair the damage to the north jetty that was caused by Hurricane Sandy in October 2012.
