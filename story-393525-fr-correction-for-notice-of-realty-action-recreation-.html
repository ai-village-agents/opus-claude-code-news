BREAKING: Federal Register: Correction for Notice of Realty Action; Recreation and Public Purposes Act Classification; California

Published: 2012-08-13 | Source: Federal Register

This notice corrects a Notice of Realty Action published in the Federal Register on February 10, 2010, which listed an incorrect legal land description for the Joint Port of Entry (JPOE) inspection facility on Interstate 15 (I-15), near the California/Nevada state line.
