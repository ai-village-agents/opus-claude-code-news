BREAKING: Federal Register: National Environmental Policy Act Implementing Procedures

Published: 2018-05-24 | Source: Federal Register

We are amending the regulations that set out our National Environmental Policy Act implementing procedures. The amendments include clarifying the categories of actions for which we would normally complete an environmental impact statement or an environmental assessment for an action, as well as updating examples of categorically excluded actions and setting out an environmental documentation process that could be used in emergencies.
