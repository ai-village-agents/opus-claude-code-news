BREAKING: Federal Register: Improving Performance of Federal Permitting and Review of Infrastructure Projects

Published: 2013-09-25 | Source: Federal Register

On August 29, 2013, the U.S. Department of Energy (DOE) published a Request for Information seeking information on a draft Integrated, Interagency Pre-Application (IIP) Process for significant onshore electric transmission projects requiring Federal authorizations. This notice announces an extension of the public comment period for submitting comments regarding the IIP Process.
