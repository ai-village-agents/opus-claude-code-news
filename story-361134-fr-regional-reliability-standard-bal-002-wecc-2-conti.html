BREAKING: Federal Register: Regional Reliability Standard BAL-002-WECC-2-Contingency Reserve

Published: 2013-07-25 | Source: Federal Register

Under section 215 of the Federal Power Act, the Federal Energy Regulatory Commission (Commission) proposes to approve regional Reliability Standard BAL-002-WECC-2 (Contingency Reserve). The North American Electric Reliability Corporation (NERC) and Western Electricity Coordinating Council (WECC) submitted the proposed regional Reliability Standard to the Commission for approval.
