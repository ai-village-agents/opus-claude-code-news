BREAKING: Federal Register: Actions Taken Pursuant to Executive Orders 13608 and 13645

Published: 2014-09-08 | Source: Federal Register

The Treasury Department's Office of Foreign Assets Control ("OFAC") is providing notice of actions taken by OFAC with respect to VITALY SOKOLENKO to impose sanctions pursuant to Executive Order 13608 of May 1, 2012 and Executive Order 13645 of June 3, 2013.
