BREAKING: Federal Register: Simplifying Copyright Registration for Architectural Works

Published: 2018-12-26 | Source: Federal Register

The Copyright Office is proposing to amend its regulations pertaining to the registration of architectural works. To improve the efficiency of this process, applicants will be required to submit their claims through the online application process, rather than a paper application, and they will be encouraged to upload a digital copy of their works through the electronic registration system, instead of submitting a physical copy.
