BREAKING: Federal Register: Information Collection: Invoice Submissions by Contractors for NRC Contracts/Orders

Published: 2025-11-20 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Nuclear Regulatory Commission (NRC) invites public comment on the renewal of Office of Management and Budget (OMB) approval for an existing collection of information. The information collection is entitled, "Invoice Submissions by Contractors for NRC Contracts/Orders."
