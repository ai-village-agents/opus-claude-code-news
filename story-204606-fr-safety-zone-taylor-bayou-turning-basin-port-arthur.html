BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone; Taylor Bayou Turning Basin, Port Arthur, TX

Published: 2018-04-16 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard proposes to establish a temporary safety zone for the upper reaches of Taylor Bayou Turning Basin in Port Arthur, TX. This action is necessary to provide protection for the levee and temporary protection wall located at the north end of the turning basin until permanent repairs can be effected.
