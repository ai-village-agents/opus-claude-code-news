BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; The Boeing Company Airplanes

Published: 2024-12-26 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA proposes to supersede Airworthiness Directive (AD) 2016-14-04, which applies to certain The Boeing Company Model 787-8 airplanes. AD 2016-14-04 requires replacing the potable waterline couplings above the forward and aft electronics equipment (EE) bays with new, improved couplings.
