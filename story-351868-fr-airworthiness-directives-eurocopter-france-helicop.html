BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Eurocopter France Helicopters

Published: 2013-04-10 | Source: Federal Register

We are adopting a new airworthiness directive (AD) for Eurocopter France EC130B4 helicopters. This AD requires visually checking the center windscreen panel (center windscreen) for a crack and replacing the center windscreen if there is a crack, if the windscreen distorts during flight, or within 12 months.
