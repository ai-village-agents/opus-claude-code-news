BREAKING: Federal Register: Entities Wholly Owned by Indian Tribal Governments

Published: 2025-12-16 | Source: Federal Register

This document contains final regulations regarding the Federal tax classification of entities wholly owned by Indian Tribal governments (Tribes). The final regulations provide that entities that are wholly owned by Tribes and organized or incorporated under the laws of one or more of the Tribes that own them generally are not recognized as separate entities for Federal tax purposes.
