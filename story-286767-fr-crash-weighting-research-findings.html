BREAKING: Federal Register: Crash Weighting Research Findings

Published: 2015-02-19 | Source: Federal Register

FMCSA extends the comment period for its January 23, 2015, notice. This notice shares information on the "Crash Weighting Analysis" which informs decision-making about the feasibility of using a motor carrier's role in crashes as an indicator of future crash risk. The January notice advised the public of the availability of the study report for review and comment, and requested feedback on what steps the Agency should take regarding crash and Police Accident Report (PAR) data quality.
