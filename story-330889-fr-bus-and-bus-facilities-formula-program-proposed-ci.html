BREAKING: Federal Register: Bus and Bus Facilities Formula Program: Proposed Circular

Published: 2014-07-30 | Source: Federal Register

The Federal Transit Administration (FTA) has placed in the docket and on its Web site, proposed guidance, in the form of a circular, to assist recipients in their implementation of the Section 5339 Bus and Bus Facilities Formula Program (Bus Program). The purpose of this proposed circular is to provide recipients of FTA financial assistance with instructions and guidance on program administration and the grant application process.
