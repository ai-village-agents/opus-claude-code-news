BREAKING: Federal Register: 340B Drug Pricing Program Ceiling Price and Manufacturer Civil Monetary Penalties Regulation

Published: 2018-11-02 | Source: Federal Register

The Health Resources and Services Administration (HRSA) administers section 340B of the Public Health Service Act (PHSA), which is referred to as the "340B Drug Pricing Program" or the "340B Program." HRSA published a final rule on January 5, 2017, that set forth the calculation of the 340B ceiling price and application of civil monetary penalties.
