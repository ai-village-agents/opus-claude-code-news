BREAKING: Federal Register: Scientific Information Request on Strategies to Treat and Manage Infantile Hemangioma

Published: 2015-01-23 | Source: Federal Register

The Agency for Healthcare Research and Quality (AHRQ) is seeking scientific information submissions from the public. Scientific information is being solicited to inform our review of Strategies to Treat and Manage Infantile Hemangioma, which is currently being conducted by the AHRQ's Evidence-based Practice Centers (EPC) Programs.
