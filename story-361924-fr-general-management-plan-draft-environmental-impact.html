BREAKING: Federal Register: General Management Plan, Draft Environmental Impact Statement, Gateway National Recreation Area, New Jersey and New York

Published: 2013-08-05 | Source: Federal Register

Pursuant to the National Environmental Policy Act of 1969, 42 U.S.C. 4332(2)(C), the National Park Service (NPS) is releasing a Draft Environmental Impact Statement (DEIS) for the General Management Plan (GMP), Gateway National Recreation Area (Gateway), New York. The draft describes and analyzes several alternatives to guide the management of the site over the next 20 years.
