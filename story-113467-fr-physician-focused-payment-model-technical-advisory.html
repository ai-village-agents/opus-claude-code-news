BREAKING: Federal Register: Physician-Focused Payment Model Technical Advisory Committee; Meetings

Published: 2022-12-29 | Source: Federal Register

This notice announces the 2023 meetings of the Physician- Focused Payment Model Technical Advisory Committee (PTAC). These meetings include deliberation and voting on proposals for physician- focused payment models (PFPMs) submitted by individuals and stakeholder entities and may include discussions on topics related to current or previously submitted PFPMs. All meetings are open to the public.
