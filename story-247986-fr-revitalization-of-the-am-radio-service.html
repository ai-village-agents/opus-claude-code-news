BREAKING: Federal Register: Revitalization of the AM Radio Service

Published: 2017-11-03 | Source: Federal Register

This document amends certain Commission rules applying to AM broadcast stations using directional antenna arrays. AM directional antenna arrays are multiple-tower installations designed to direct radio energy primarily in certain directions in order to avoid interfering with other AM broadcast stations.
