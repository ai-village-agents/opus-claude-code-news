BREAKING: Federal Register: Injurious Wildlife Species; Listing Salamanders Due to Risk of Salamander Chytrid Fungus

Published: 2016-01-13 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Fish and Wildlife Service is amending its regulations under the Lacey Act to add all species of salamanders from 20 genera, of which there are 201 species, to the list of injurious amphibians.
