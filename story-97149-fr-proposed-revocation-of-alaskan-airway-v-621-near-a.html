BREAKING: Federal Register: Proposed Revocation of Alaskan Airway V-621 Near Atqasuk, AK

Published: 2022-05-31 | Source: Federal Register

This action proposes to revoke the Alaskan VHF Omnidirectional Range (VOR) Federal airway V-621 (hereinafter referred to as Alaskan V- 621) due to the planned decommissioning of the Atqasuk, AK, (ATK) Non- Directional Beacon (NDB) Navigational Aid (NAVAID).
