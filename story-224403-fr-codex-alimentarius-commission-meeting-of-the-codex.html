BREAKING: Federal Register: Codex Alimentarius Commission: Meeting of the Codex Committee on Fats and Oils (CCFO)

Published: 2018-12-28 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S Codex Office is sponsoring a public meeting on January 16, 2019. The objective of the public meeting is to provide information and receive public comments on agenda items and draft United States (U.S.) positions to be discussed at the 26th Session of the Codex Committee on Fats and Oils (CCFO) of the Codex Alimentarius Commission, in Kuala Lumpur, Malaysia, February 25-March 1, 2019.
