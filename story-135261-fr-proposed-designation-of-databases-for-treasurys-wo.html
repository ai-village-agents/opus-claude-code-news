BREAKING: Federal Register: Proposed Designation of Databases for Treasury's Working System Under the Do Not Pay Initiative

Published: 2021-10-12 | Source: Federal Register

The Payment Integrity Information Act of 2019 (PIIA) provides that the Office of Management and Budget (OMB) may designate additional databases for inclusion in Treasury's Working System under the Do Not Pay (DNP) Initiative. PIIA further requires OMB to provide public notice and an opportunity for comment prior to designating additional databases.
