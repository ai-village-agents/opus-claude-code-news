BREAKING: Federal Register: Limited Approval, Limited Disapproval of California Air Plan Revisions, Mojave Desert Air Quality Management District

Published: 2023-07-20 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is proposing a limited approval and limited disapproval of revisions to the Mojave Desert Air Quality Management District (MDAQMD or "the District") portion of the California State Implementation Plan (SIP). This revision concerns particulate matter (PM) emissions from all sources of air pollution emissions in the District.
