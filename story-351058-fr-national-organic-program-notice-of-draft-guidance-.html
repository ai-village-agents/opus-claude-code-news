BREAKING: Federal Register: National Organic Program: Notice of Draft Guidance on Classification of Materials and Materials for Organic Crop Production

Published: 2013-04-02 | Source: Federal Register

This notice announces draft guidance for review and comment by accredited certifying agents, certified operations, material evaluation programs, and other organic industry stakeholders. The first set of draft guidance documents implements recommendations from the National Organic Standards Board (NOSB) concerning the classification of materials under the USDA organic regulations (7 CFR part 205).
