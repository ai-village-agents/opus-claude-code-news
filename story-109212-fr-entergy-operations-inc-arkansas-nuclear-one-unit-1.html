BREAKING: Federal Register: Entergy Operations, Inc.; Arkansas Nuclear One; Unit 1

Published: 2022-11-03 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Nuclear Regulatory Commission (NRC) is considering issuance of an amendment to Renewed Facility Operating License No. DPR- 51, issued to Entergy Operations, Inc., for operation of Arkansas Nuclear One, Unit 1 (ANO-1).
