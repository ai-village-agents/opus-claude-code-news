BREAKING: Federal Register: Request for Information-Environmental Research and Education, National Security, and Economic Competitiveness

Published: 2018-06-20 | Source: Federal Register

The purpose of this Request for Information (RFI) is to seek input from the public on possible future directions for fundamental environmental science and education. The RFI intends to gather views from external stakeholders on what environmental research and education is needed to further advance national security and economic competitiveness.
