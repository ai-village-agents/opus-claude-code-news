BREAKING: Federal Register: Atlantic Highly Migratory Species; Atlantic Shark Management Measures; 2020 Research Fishery

Published: 2020-03-03 | Source: Federal Register

On November 22, 2019, NMFS published a notice inviting qualified commercial shark permit holders to submit applications to participate in the 2020 shark research fishery. The shark research fishery allows for the collection of fishery-dependent data for future stock assessments and cooperative research with commercial fishermen to meet the shark research objectives of the Agency.
