BREAKING: Federal Register: Federal Implementation Plan for Contingency Measures for the Fine Particulate Matter Standards; San Joaquin Valley, California

Published: 2023-08-08 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is proposing to promulgate a Federal Implementation Plan (FIP) under the Clean Air Act (CAA) that consists of contingency measures for the 1997, 2006, and 2012 fine particulate matter (PM2.5) national ambient air quality standards (NAAQS or "standards") for the San Joaquin Valley PM2.5 nonattainment area.
