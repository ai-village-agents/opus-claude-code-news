BREAKING: Federal Register: User Fees for Agricultural Quarantine and Inspection Services

Published: 2023-03-17 | Source: Federal Register

We are amending our regulations governing the user fees charged for certain Agricultural Quarantine and Inspection (AQI) services. We are reducing the fees charged to remove surcharges that were intended to fund a reserve.
