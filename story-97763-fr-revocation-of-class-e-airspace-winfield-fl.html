BREAKING: Federal Register: Revocation of Class E Airspace; Winfield, FL

Published: 2022-06-07 | Source: Federal Register

This action removes Class E airspace in Winfield, FL, as I-10 Helistop Heliport has been abandoned and controlled airspace is no longer required. This action enhances the safety and management of controlled airspace within the national airspace system.
