BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; The Boeing Company Airplanes

Published: 2016-12-16 | Source: Federal Register

We propose to adopt a new airworthiness directive (AD) for certain The Boeing Company Model 787-8 airplanes. This proposed AD was prompted by a report indicating that during an airplane inspection in production, the variable frequency starter generator (VFSG) power feeder cables were found to contain terminal lugs incorrectly installed common to terminal blocks located in the wing front spar.
