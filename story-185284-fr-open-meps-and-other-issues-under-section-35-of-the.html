BREAKING: Federal Register: "Open MEPs" and Other Issues Under Section 3(5) of the Employee Retirement Income Security Act

Published: 2019-07-31 | Source: Federal Register

This document is a request for information regarding the definition of "employer" in section 3(5) of the Employee Retirement Income Security Act of 1974, as amended (ERISA). The document mainly seeks comments on whether to amend our regulations to facilitate the sponsorship of "open MEPs" by persons acting indirectly in the interests of unrelated employers whose employees would receive benefits under such arrangements.
