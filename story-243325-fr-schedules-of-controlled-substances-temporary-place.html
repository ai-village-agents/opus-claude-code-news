BREAKING: Federal Register: Schedules of Controlled Substances: Temporary Placement of FUB-AMB Into Schedule I

Published: 2017-09-11 | Source: Federal Register

The Administrator of the Drug Enforcement Administration is issuing this notice of intent to publish a temporary order to schedule the synthetic cannabinoid, Methyl 2-(1-(4-fluorobenzyl)-1H-indazole-3- carboxamido)-3-methylbutanoate [FUB-AMB, MMB-FUBINACA, AMB-FUBINACA], into Schedule I.
