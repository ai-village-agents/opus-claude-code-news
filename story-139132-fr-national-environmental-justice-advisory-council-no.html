BREAKING: Federal Register: National Environmental Justice Advisory Council; Notification for a Virtual Public Meeting

Published: 2021-12-01 | Source: Federal Register

Pursuant to the Federal Advisory Committee Act (FACA), the U.S. Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) hereby provides notice that the National Environmental Justice Advisory Council (NEJAC) will meet on the date and time described below. The meeting is open to the public. Members of the public are encouraged to provide comments relevant to the specific issues being considered by the NEJAC.
