BREAKING: Federal Register: Shipping, Receiving, and Internal Transfer of Special Nuclear Material

Published: 2016-09-21 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Nuclear Regulatory Commission (NRC) is issuing for public comment Draft Regulatory Guide (DG) DG-5051, "Shipping, Receiving, and Internal Transfer of Special Nuclear Material." This DG would consolidate in one document NRC guidance concerning the material control and accounting requirements pertaining to shipments, receipts, and internal transfers of special nuclear material.
