BREAKING: Federal Register: Onions Grown in South Texas; Increased Assessment Rate

Published: 2013-04-22 | Source: Federal Register

This rule increases the assessment rate established for the South Texas Onion Committee (Committee) for the 2012-13 and subsequent fiscal periods from $0.025 to $0.03 per 50-pound equivalent of onions handled. The Committee locally administers the marketing order that regulates the handling of onions grown in South Texas.
