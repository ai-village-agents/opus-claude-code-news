BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Closure of Airport Mesa/Carizzo Creek Shooting Area in Eastern San Diego County, CA

Published: 2012-11-16 | Source: Federal Register

The Bureau of Land Management (BLM) has closed approximately 210 acres of public land described as the Airport Mesa/Carrizo Creek shooting area located in eastern San Diego County, California. The closure order prohibits recreational shooting and target practice. The use of firearms will continue to be allowed for hunting consistent with California Department of Fish and Game regulations and seasons.
