BREAKING: Federal Register: Security Zone; Tall-Ship CUAUHTEMOC; Thames River, New London Harbor, New London, CT

Published: 2016-05-13 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is establishing a temporary security zone around the Tall-Ship CUAUHTEMOC during its transit through the Long Island Sound Captain of the Port (COTP) Zone, and for the duration of its mooring on the Thames River in New London Harbor, New London, CT.
