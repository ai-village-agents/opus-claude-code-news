BREAKING: Federal Register: Irish Potatoes Grown in Colorado; Decreased Assessment Rate for Area No. 2

Published: 2013-11-22 | Source: Federal Register

This rule decreases the assessment rate established for the Colorado Potato Administrative Committee, Area No. 2 (Committee), for the 2013-2014 and subsequent fiscal periods from $0.0051 to $0.0033 per hundredweight of potatoes handled. The Committee locally administers the marketing order, which regulates the handling of Irish potatoes grown in Colorado.
