BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone; Fireworks Display, Sea Otter Point, Port of Valdez, AK

Published: 2022-12-23 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is establishing a temporary safety zone in the navigable waters from the surface to seabed, within a 150 yard radius of the fireworks launching point located at Sea Otter Point in position 61[deg]07'22" North and 146[deg]21'13" West in the vicinity of the mouth of the Small Boat Harbor, Port of Valdez, Alaska, to limit access for the duration of the New Year's fireworks display.
