BREAKING: Federal Register: Billing and Collection by VA for Medical Care and Services

Published: 2019-10-28 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Veterans Affairs (VA) proposes to amend its regulations concerning collection and recovery by VA for medical care and services provided to an individual for treatment of a nonservice- connected disability.
