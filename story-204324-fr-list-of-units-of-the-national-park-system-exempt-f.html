BREAKING: Federal Register: List of Units of the National Park System Exempt From the Provisions of the National Parks Air Tour Management Act

Published: 2018-04-11 | Source: Federal Register

The National Parks Air Tour Management Act (NPATMA) requires the Federal Aviation Administration (FAA) and National Park Service (NPS) to develop an air tour management plan for units of the national park system where an operator has applied for authority to conduct commercial air tours. The FAA Modernization and Reform Act of 2012 amended various provisions of NPATMA.
