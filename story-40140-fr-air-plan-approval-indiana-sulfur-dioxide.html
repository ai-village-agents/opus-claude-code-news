BREAKING: Federal Register: Air Plan Approval; Indiana; Sulfur Dioxide

Published: 2024-06-24 | Source: Federal Register

In accordance with the Clean Air Act, the Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is proposing to approve a September 11, 2023, State Implementation Plan (SIP) submittal from the Indiana Department of Environmental Management (IDEM), with information supplemented by a March 11, 2024, letter to EPA. This SIP submittal requests EPA approval of a revision to the monitoring and compliance requirements for certain process heater stacks at Safety-Kleen Oil Recovery Company in Lake County, Indiana.
