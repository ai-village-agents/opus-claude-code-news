BREAKING: Federal Register: Homeland Security Academic Advisory Council; Establishment and Meeting

Published: 2012-03-01 | Source: Federal Register

The Secretary of Homeland Security has determined that the establishment of the Homeland Security Academic Advisory Council (HSAAC) is necessary and in the public interest in connection with the performance of duties of the Office of Academic Engagement. This determination follows consultation with the Committee Management Secretariat, General Services Administration. The committee will hold its inaugural meeting on March 20, 2012.
