BREAKING: Federal Register: Certain Steel Nails From the People's Republic of China: Final Results of Antidumping Duty Changed Circumstances Review

Published: 2019-09-20 | Source: Federal Register

On September 17, 2018, the Department of Commerce (Commerce) published a notice of initiation and the preliminary results of the changed circumstances review (CCR) of the antidumping duty (AD) order on certain steel nails (nails) from the People's Republic of China (China) in the Federal Register regarding certain scope exclusion language. Commerce has adopted the revised scope exclusion language in these final results.
