BREAKING: Federal Register: Inviting Applications for Agriculture Innovation Demonstration Center Grants

Published: 2022-12-05 | Source: Federal Register

The Rural Business-Cooperative Service (Agency), an agency of the United States Department of Agriculture (USDA), announces that it is accepting fiscal year (FY) 2023 applications for the Agriculture Innovation Demonstration Center (AIC) program. In FY 2023, the program has $8,005,621 available for grant funding.
