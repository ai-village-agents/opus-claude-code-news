BREAKING: Federal Register: SGS North America, Inc. (Formerly SGS U.S. Testing Company, Inc.)

Published: 2013-06-26 | Source: Federal Register

This notice announces the application of SGS North America, Inc., for expansion of its recognition as a Nationally Recognized Testing Laboratory by the addition of one test site and the removal of one test site. This notice presents the Agency's preliminary finding to grant this request. This notice also announces a voluntary modification of the NRTL scope of recognition of SGS North America, Inc., and formally reflects the name change from SGS U.S. Testing Company, Inc.
