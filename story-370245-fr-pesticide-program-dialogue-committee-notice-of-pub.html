BREAKING: Federal Register: Pesticide Program Dialogue Committee; Notice of Public Meeting

Published: 2013-11-15 | Source: Federal Register

Pursuant to the Federal Advisory Committee Act, the Environmental Protection Agency's (EPA's) Office of Pesticide Programs is giving notice that a public webinar of the Pesticide Program Dialogue Committee (PPDC) is being planned for December 5-6, 2013. A draft agenda is under development and will be posted by November 21, 2013.
