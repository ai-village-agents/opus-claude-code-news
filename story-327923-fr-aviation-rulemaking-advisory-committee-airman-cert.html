BREAKING: Federal Register: Aviation Rulemaking Advisory Committee Airman Certification System Working Group

Published: 2014-06-25 | Source: Federal Register

This notice announces the availability of draft Airman Certification Standards documents developed by the Airman Certification Standards Working Group for the commercial pilot certificate and the authorized instructor certificate. The Airman Certification Standards documents are designed as the foundation for transitioning to a more integrated and systematic approach to airman certification testing and training.
