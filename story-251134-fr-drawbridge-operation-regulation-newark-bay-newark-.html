BREAKING: Federal Register: Drawbridge Operation Regulation; Newark Bay, Newark, NJ

Published: 2017-12-19 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard has issued a temporary deviation from the operating schedule that governs the Lehigh Valley Railroad Bridge across the Newark Bay, mile 4.3, at Newark, New Jersey. The deviation is necessary to test a change to the drawbridge operation schedule to determine whether a permanent change to the schedule is needed.
