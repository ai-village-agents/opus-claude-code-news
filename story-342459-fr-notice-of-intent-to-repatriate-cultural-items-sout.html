BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Intent to Repatriate Cultural Items: Southern Oregon Historical Society, Medford, OR

Published: 2014-12-22 | Source: Federal Register

The Southern Oregon Historical Society in consultation with the appropriate Indian tribes or Native Hawaiian organizations has determined that the cultural items listed in this notice meet the definition of unassociated funerary objects. Lineal descendants or representatives of any Indian tribe or Native Hawaiian organization not identified in this notice that wish to claim these cultural items should submit a written request to the Southern Oregon Historical Society.
