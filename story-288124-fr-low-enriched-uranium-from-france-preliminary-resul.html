BREAKING: Federal Register: Low-Enriched Uranium From France; Preliminary Results of Antidumping Duty Administrative Review; 2013-2014

Published: 2015-03-09 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Commerce (the Department) is conducting an administrative review of the antidumping duty order on low-enriched uranium (LEU) from France.\1\ The period of review (POR) is February 1, 2013, through January 31, 2014. The review covers one producer/exporter of the subject merchandise, Eurodif S.A., AREVA NC, and AREVA NC, Inc. (collectively AREVA).
