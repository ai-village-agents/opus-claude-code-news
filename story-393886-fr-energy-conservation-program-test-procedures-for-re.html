BREAKING: Federal Register: Energy Conservation Program: Test Procedures for Residential Dishwashers and Cooking Products

Published: 2012-08-15 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Department of Energy (DOE) proposes to amend its test procedures for residential dishwashers to update certain obsolete dishware, flatware and food items, make minor amendments to the definition of the normal cycle, and update the ambient temperature and preconditioning requirements as well as the industry test method referenced in DOE's test procedure. DOE also proposes to add water pressure, drain height, rack position, loading, rinse aid container, and soil preparation specifications...
