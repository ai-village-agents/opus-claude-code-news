BREAKING: Federal Register: Access by EPA Subcontractors to Information Claimed as Confidential Business Information (CBI) Submitted Under Titles I and II of the Clean Air Act (CAA) and the Prevent Pollution From Ships Act (APPS)

Published: 2019-05-29 | Source: Federal Register

The Clean Air Act (CAA), Act to Prevent Pollution from Ships (APPS) and regulations require that the United States Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) provide notice to interested parties before any contractor may be provided access to confidential business information (CBI). EPA is providing notice that several subcontractors of a previously identified contractor will be given access to CBI.
