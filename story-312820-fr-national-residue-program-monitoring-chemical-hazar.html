BREAKING: Federal Register: National Residue Program: Monitoring Chemical Hazards

Published: 2015-12-29 | Source: Federal Register

The Food Safety and Inspection Service (FSIS; also Agency) is clarifying its approach within the National Residue Program's (NRP's) Tier 2 exploratory program when it tests tissue samples collected from livestock and poultry carcasses and detects chemicals that do not have established tolerances or other regulatory levels.
