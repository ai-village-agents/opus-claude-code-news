BREAKING: Federal Register: Request for Information to Make Access to the Innovation Ecosystem More Inclusive and Equitable

Published: 2022-06-03 | Source: Federal Register

The White House Office of Science and Technology Policy (OSTP), on behalf of the National Science and Technology Council (NSTC) Lab-to-Market (L2M) Subcommittee, seeks information to improve inclusive and equitable access to Federal programs and resources by broadly engaging stakeholders in the U.S. innovation ecosystem.
