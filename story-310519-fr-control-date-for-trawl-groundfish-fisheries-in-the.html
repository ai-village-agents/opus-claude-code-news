BREAKING: Federal Register: Control Date for Trawl Groundfish Fisheries in the Aleutian Islands

Published: 2015-11-30 | Source: Federal Register

At the request of the North Pacific Fishery Management Council (Council), this document announces a control date of December 31, 2015, that may be used as a reference date for a future management action to limit future access to the offshore sector of the trawl groundfish fisheries in the Aleutian Islands.
