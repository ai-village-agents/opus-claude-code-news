BREAKING: Federal Register: Draft Guidance for Industry on Complicated Urinary Tract Infections: Developing Drugs for Treatment; Availability

Published: 2012-02-24 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA) is announcing the availability of a draft guidance for industry entitled "Complicated Urinary Tract Infections: Developing Drugs for Treatment." The purpose of this guidance is to assist sponsors in the clinical development of drugs for the treatment of complicated urinary tract infections (cUTIs).
