BREAKING: Federal Register: Availability of Interaction Profile for Chlorinated Dibenzo-p -Dioxins, Polybrominated Diphenol Ethers, and Phthalates

Published: 2013-10-25 | Source: Federal Register

The Agency for Toxic Substances and Disease Registry (ATSDR) within the Department of Health and Human Services announces the availability of the interaction profile for Chlorinated Dibenzo-p- Dioxins, Polybrominated Diphenol Ethers, and Phthalates. This interaction profile evaluates a mixture of chemicals often found in human blood, adipose tissue, and breast milk.
