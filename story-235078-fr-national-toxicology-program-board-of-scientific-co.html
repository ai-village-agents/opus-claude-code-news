BREAKING: Federal Register: National Toxicology Program Board of Scientific Counselors; Announcement of Meeting; Request for Comments; Amended Notice

Published: 2017-05-25 | Source: Federal Register

This notice amends Federal Register notice 82 FR 20484, published May 2, 2017, announcing the National Toxicology Program (NTP) Board of Scientific Counselors (BSC) meeting and requesting comments. The deadline for registration has been changed to June 29, 2017. The BSC will provide input to the NTP on programmatic activities and issues.
