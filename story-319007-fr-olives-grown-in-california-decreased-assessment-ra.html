BREAKING: Federal Register: Olives Grown in California; Decreased Assessment Rate

Published: 2014-03-14 | Source: Federal Register

This rule decreases the assessment rate established for the California Olive Committee (Committee) for the 2014 and subsequent fiscal years from $21.16 to $15.21 per ton of assessable olives handled. The Committee locally administers the marketing order, which regulates the handling of olives grown in California.
