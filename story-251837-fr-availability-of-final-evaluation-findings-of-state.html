BREAKING: Federal Register: Availability of Final Evaluation Findings of State Coastal Programs and National Estuarine Research Reserves

Published: 2017-12-28 | Source: Federal Register

Notice is hereby given of the availability of final evaluation findings of state coastal programs and national estuarine research reserves. The NOAA Office for Coastal Management has completed review of the Coastal Zone Management Program evaluations for the states of New York, New Hampshire, Washington, and Maine.
