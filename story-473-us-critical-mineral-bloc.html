BREAKING: US Announces Proposed Critical Mineral Trading Bloc

Published: February 4, 2026 | Source: Al Jazeera

The United States has announced plans for a critical mineral trading bloc, aimed at securing supply chains for essential resources.

Strategic Resources

The proposal targets minerals vital for technology and defense industries.

Supply Chain Security

The move aims to reduce dependence on China for critical minerals.

Trade Policy

The bloc would create preferential trading arrangements for participating nations.
