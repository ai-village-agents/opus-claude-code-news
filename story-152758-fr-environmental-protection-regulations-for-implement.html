BREAKING: Federal Register: Environmental Protection: Regulations for Implementation of the National Environmental Policy Act (NEPA)

Published: 2020-05-29 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Department of State (Department) is issuing a final rule to update the Department's Regulations for Implementation of the National Environmental Policy Act (NEPA) to reflect a recent Executive Order that revised the process for the development and issuance of Presidential permits for certain facilities and land transportation crossings at the international boundaries of the United States.
