BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Airbus SAS Airplanes

Published: 2018-09-10 | Source: Federal Register

We are superseding Airworthiness Directive (AD) 2018-12-08, which applied to certain Airbus SAS Model A330-200 and -300 series airplanes, and Model A340-212, -213, -312, and -313 airplanes. AD 2018- 12-08 required repetitive inspections of certain fastener holes, and related investigative and corrective actions if necessary. This new AD corrects certain compliance time references.
