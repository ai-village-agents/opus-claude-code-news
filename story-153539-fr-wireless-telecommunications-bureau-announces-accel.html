BREAKING: Federal Register: Wireless Telecommunications Bureau Announces Accelerated Clearing in the 3.7-4.2 GHz Band

Published: 2020-06-09 | Source: Federal Register

(Claro), and Telesat Canada (Telesat) have committed to clearing the 3.7-4.0 GHz band on the accelerated timeline described in the 3.7 GHz Report and Order.
