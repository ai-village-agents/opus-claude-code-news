BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; General Electric Company Turbofan Engines

Published: 2017-04-06 | Source: Federal Register

We are superseding Airworthiness Directive (AD) 2013-24-17 for General Electric Company (GE) GE90-110B1 and GE90-115B turbofan engines with certain high-pressure compressor (HPC) rotor stage 2-5 spools installed. AD 2013-24-17 required removing these spools from service at times determined by a drawdown plan. This AD retains the same requirements as AD 2013-24-17.
