BREAKING: Federal Register: Sole Source Concession Contract for Gateway National Recreation Area, NY

Published: 2014-09-10 | Source: Federal Register

Pursuant to 36 CFR 51.25, public notice is hereby given that the National Park Service proposes to award a sole source concession contract for the conduct of certain visitor services within Gateway National Recreation Area, New York for a term not to exceed 5 years. The visitor services include marina operations and dry storage for boats at Great Kills Marina. This action is necessary to avoid interruption of visitor services.
