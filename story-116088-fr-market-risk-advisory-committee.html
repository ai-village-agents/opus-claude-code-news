BREAKING: Federal Register: Market Risk Advisory Committee

Published: 2021-02-05 | Source: Federal Register

The Commodity Futures Trading Commission (CFTC) announces that on February 23, 2021, from 9:30 a.m. to 2:00 p.m. (Eastern Standard Time), the Market Risk Advisory Committee (MRAC) will hold a public meeting via teleconference. At this meeting, the MRAC will receive reports from its subcommittees: Climate-related Market Risk, CCP Risk and Governance, Market Structure, and Interest Rate Benchmark Reform.
