BREAKING: Federal Register: Special Local Regulations; Great Western Tube Float, Parker, AZ

Published: 2021-05-21 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard will enforce the special local regulation for the Great Western Tube Float on June 12, 2021. These special local regulations are necessary to provide for the safety of the participants, crew, spectators, sponsor vessels, and general users of the waterway. Our regulation for marine events within the Eleventh Coast Guard District identifies the regulated area for this event in Parker, AZ.
