BREAKING: Federal Register: Media Bureau Reorganization

Published: 2020-09-18 | Source: Federal Register

In this document, the Commission eliminates the Engineering Division of the Media Bureau and folds it into the Bureau's Industry Analysis Division. We take this step to account for changes in the Engineering Division's duties and in the organizational structure of the Commission.
