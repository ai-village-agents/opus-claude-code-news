BREAKING: Federal Register: Workshop on Electronic Filing of Certificates as Included in Proposed Rule on Certificates of Compliance

Published: 2014-07-03 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Consumer Product Safety Commission (CPSC, Commission, or we) staff is holding a workshop on aspects of the Commission's proposed rule on Certificates of Compliance (certificates), which the Commission published on May 13, 2013.
