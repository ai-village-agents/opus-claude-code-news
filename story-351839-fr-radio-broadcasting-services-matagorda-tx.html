BREAKING: Federal Register: Radio Broadcasting Services; Matagorda, TX

Published: 2013-04-10 | Source: Federal Register

In this document the Commission requests comment on a petition filed by Tejas Broadcasting Ltd., LLP ("Petitioner"), licensee of FM Station KMJR, Channel 252C2, Odem, Texas. Petitioner proposes to amend the FM Table of Allotments by substituting Channel 291A for vacant Channel 252A, at Matagorda. The proposal is part of a contingently filed "hybrid" application and rule making petition.
