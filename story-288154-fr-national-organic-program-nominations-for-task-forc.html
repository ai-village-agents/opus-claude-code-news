BREAKING: Federal Register: National Organic Program; Nominations for Task Force Members

Published: 2015-03-09 | Source: Federal Register

The Agricultural Marketing Service (AMS) is soliciting nominees to participate in a task force to examine hydroponic and aquaponic practices and their alignment with the USDA organic regulations and the Organic Foods Production Act (OFPA). The USDA organic regulations do not include specific provisions for organic hydroponic or aquaponic production.
