BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Intent To Grant Exclusive Patent License; Nanoarmor, LLC

Published: 2019-04-09 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of the Navy hereby gives notice of its intent to grant to Nanoarmor, LLC a revocable, nonassignable, exclusive license to practice the Government-Owned invention described in U.S. Patent Application No. 11/157,751 (Navy Case No. 97280; U.S. Patent No. 8,220,378 titled "Composite Armor Panel and Method of Manufacturing Same"; and any continuations, divisionals, or re-issues thereof.
