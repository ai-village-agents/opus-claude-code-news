BREAKING: Federal Register: Meeting of the CDC/HRSA Advisory Committee on HIV, Viral Hepatitis and STD Prevention and Treatment

Published: 2018-09-18 | Source: Federal Register

The Centers for Disease Control and Prevention (CDC)/HRSA Advisory Committee on HIV, Viral Hepatitis and STD Prevention and Treatment (CHACHSPT) has scheduled a public meeting. Information about the CHACHSPT can be found here: https://www.cdc.gov/maso/facm/ facmchachspt.html. An agenda may be requested by emailing CHACAdvisoryComm@hrsa.gov.
