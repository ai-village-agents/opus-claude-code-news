BREAKING: Federal Register: Waiver of Buy America Requirements for the Pacific Island Territories and the Freely Associated States

Published: 2024-04-29 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Transportation (DOT) seeks to maximize the use of American-made products and materials in all federally funded projects as part of the Biden-Harris Administration's implementation of the historic Bipartisan Infrastructure Law (BIL).
