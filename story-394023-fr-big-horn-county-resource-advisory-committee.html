BREAKING: Federal Register: Big Horn County Resource Advisory Committee

Published: 2012-08-17 | Source: Federal Register

The Big Horn County Resource Advisory Committee will meet in Greybull, Wyoming. The committee is meeting as authorized under the Secure Rural Schools and Community Self-Determination Act (Pub. L. 112- 141) and in compliance with the Federal Advisory Committee Act. The purpose is to hold the sixth meeting and to vote on project proposals.
