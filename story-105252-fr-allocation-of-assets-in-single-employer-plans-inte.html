BREAKING: Federal Register: Allocation of Assets in Single-Employer Plans; Interest Assumptions for Valuing Benefits

Published: 2022-09-15 | Source: Federal Register

This final rule amends the Pension Benefit Guaranty Corporation's regulation on Allocation of Assets in Single-Employer Plans to prescribe interest assumptions under the asset allocation regulation for plans with valuation dates in the fourth quarter of 2022. These interest assumptions are used for valuing benefits under terminating single-employer plans and for other purposes.
