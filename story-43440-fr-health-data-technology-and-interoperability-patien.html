BREAKING: Federal Register: Health Data, Technology, and Interoperability: Patient Engagement, Information Sharing, and Public Health Interoperability

Published: 2024-08-05 | Source: Federal Register

This proposed rule seeks to advance interoperability, improve transparency, and support the access, exchange, and use of electronic health information through proposals for: standards adoption; adoption of certification criteria to advance public health data exchange; expanded uses of certified application programming interfaces, such as for electronic prior authorization, patient access, care management, and care coordination; and information sharing under the information blocking regulations. ...
