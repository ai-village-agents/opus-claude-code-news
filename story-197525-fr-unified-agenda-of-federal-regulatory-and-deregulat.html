BREAKING: Federal Register: Unified Agenda of Federal Regulatory and Deregulatory Actions

Published: 2018-01-12 | Source: Federal Register

We are publishing our semiannual regulatory agenda (the Agenda) in accordance with Public Law 96-354, "The Regulatory Flexibility Act," and Executive Order 12866, "Regulatory Planning and Review." The Agenda is a compilation of all rulemaking activities on which we have recently completed action or have proposed or are considering action.
