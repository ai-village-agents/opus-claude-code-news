BREAKING: Federal Register: Use of Salt Substitutes To Reduce the Sodium Content in Standardized Foods

Published: 2023-04-10 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA or we) is proposing to amend our standard of identity (SOI) regulations that specify salt (sodium chloride) as a required or optional ingredient to permit the use of salt substitutes in standardized foods, to reduce the sodium content. Reducing sodium may help reduce the risk of hypertension, a leading cause of heart disease and stroke.
