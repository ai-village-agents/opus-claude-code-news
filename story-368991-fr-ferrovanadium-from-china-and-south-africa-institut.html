BREAKING: Federal Register: Ferrovanadium From China and South Africa; Institution of Five-Year Reviews

Published: 2013-11-01 | Source: Federal Register

The Commission hereby gives notice that it has instituted reviews pursuant to section 751(c) of the Tariff Act of 1930 (19 U.S.C. 1675(c)) (the Act) to determine whether revocation of the antidumping duty orders on ferrovanadium from China and South Africa would be likely to lead to continuation or recurrence of material injury.
