BREAKING: Federal Register: Submission for OMB Review; Contractors' Purchasing Systems Reviews

Published: 2016-04-05 | Source: Federal Register

Under the provisions of the Paperwork Reduction Act, the Regulatory Secretariat Division will be submitting to the Office of Management and Budget (OMB) a request to review and approve an extension of a previously approved information collection requirement concerning contractors' purchasing systems reviews. A notice was published in the Federal Register at 81 FR 3135 on January 20, 2016. No comments were received.
