BREAKING: Federal Register: Final Waiver and Extension of the Project Period for the Literacy Information and Communication System Regional Professional Development Centers

Published: 2014-06-02 | Source: Federal Register

For the 36-month grant projects funded in fiscal year (FY) 2011, using FY 2010 funds, under the Literacy Information and Communication System (LINCS) Regional Professional Development Centers (RPDC) program, the Secretary hereby waives the restriction against project period extensions involving the obligation of additional Federal funds, and extends the project period of the four LINCS RPDC grants for an additional 12 months.
