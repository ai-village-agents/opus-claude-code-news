BREAKING: Federal Register: Inland Waterways Navigation Regulation: Sacramento River, CA

Published: 2013-04-23 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is confirming the removal of the Decker Island restricted anchorage area in the Sacramento River. The restricted anchorage area was needed in the past to prevent non-government vessels from transiting through or anchoring in the United States Army's tug and barge anchorage zones. The United States Army relinquished control of the Island in 1975.
