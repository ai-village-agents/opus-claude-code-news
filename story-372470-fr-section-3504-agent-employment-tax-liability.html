BREAKING: Federal Register: Section 3504 Agent Employment Tax Liability

Published: 2013-12-12 | Source: Federal Register

This document contains final regulations relating to agents authorized by the Secretary under section 3504 of the Internal Revenue Code to perform acts required of employers who are home care service recipients. The final regulations affect employers and their designated agents who pay wages for home care services, which are subject to taxes under the Federal Unemployment Tax Act.
