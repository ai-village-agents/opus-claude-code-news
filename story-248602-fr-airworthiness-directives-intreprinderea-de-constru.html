BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Intreprinderea De Constructii Aeronautice Gliders

Published: 2017-11-14 | Source: Federal Register

We propose to adopt a new airworthiness directive (AD) for Intreprinderea De Constructii Aeronautice Model IS-28B2 gliders. This proposed AD results from mandatory continuing airworthiness information (MCAI) originated by an aviation authority of another country to identify and correct an unsafe condition on an aviation product.
