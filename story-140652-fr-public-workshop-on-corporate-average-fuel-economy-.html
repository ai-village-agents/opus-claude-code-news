BREAKING: Federal Register: Public Workshop on Corporate Average Fuel Economy Reporting Templates

Published: 2021-12-22 | Source: Federal Register

This notice announces that the National Highway Traffic Safety Administration (NHTSA) will hold a workshop to present three new compliance reporting templates for the Corporate Average Fuel Economy (CAFE) Program. The workshop will provide a demonstration of the use of the templates which automobile manufacturers will use to provide NHTSA required compliance data.
