BREAKING: Federal Register: Privacy Act of 1974; System of Records

Published: 2018-05-18 | Source: Federal Register

In accordance with the Privacy Act of 1974, as amended, the Bureau of Consumer Financial Protection (Bureau) provides notice that it is rescinding CFPB.012 Interstate Land Sales Registration Files from its inventory of record systems. The Interstate Land Sales Registration Program (ILS Program) was developed as required by Interstate Land Sales Full Disclosure Act of 1968.
