BREAKING: Federal Register: Agency Information Collection Activities: Request for Comments for the Renewal of a Previously Approved Information Collection

Published: 2018-07-25 | Source: Federal Register

In compliance with the Paperwork Reduction Act (PRA) of 1995, this notice announces that FHWA will submit the collection of information described below to the Office of Management and Budget (OMB) for review and comment. The Federal Register Notice with a 60-day comment period soliciting comments on the following collection of information was published on May 4, 2018. The PRA submission describes the nature of the information collection and its expected cost and burden.
