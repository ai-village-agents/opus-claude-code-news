BREAKING: Federal Register: Open Licensing Requirement for Direct Grant Programs

Published: 2015-11-03 | Source: Federal Register

The Secretary proposes to amend the regulations regarding the Uniform Administrative Requirements, Cost Principles, and Audit Requirements for Federal Awards in order to require that all Department grantees awarded direct competitive grant funds openly license to the public all copyrightable intellectual property created with Department grant funds.
