BREAKING: Federal Register: Changes To Implement Provisions of the Trademark Modernization Act of 2020

Published: 2021-11-17 | Source: Federal Register

The United States Patent and Trademark Office (USPTO or Office) amends the rules of practice in trademark cases to implement provisions of the Trademark Modernization Act of 2020 (TMA).
