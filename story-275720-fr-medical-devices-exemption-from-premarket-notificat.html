BREAKING: Federal Register: Medical Devices; Exemption From Premarket Notification: Method, Metallic Reduction, Glucose (Urinary, Nonquantitative) Test System in a Reagent Tablet Format

Published: 2016-10-04 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA) is publishing an order denying a petition requesting exemption from the premarket notification requirements for "method, metallic reduction, glucose (urinary, nonquantitative)" devices that are in a reagent tablet format and are classified as class II devices as urinary glucose (nonquantitative) test system (hereinafter referred to as "copper reduction tablet test").
