BREAKING: Federal Register: Reorganization of the National Center for Injury Prevention and Control

Published: 2024-08-26 | Source: Federal Register

CDC has modified its structure. This notice announces the National Center for Injury Prevention and Control (NCIPC) reorganization. NCIPC established the Behavioral Integration Branch.
