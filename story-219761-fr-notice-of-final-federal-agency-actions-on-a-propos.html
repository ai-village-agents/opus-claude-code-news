BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Final Federal Agency Actions on a Proposed Highway Project in Wisconsin

Published: 2018-10-26 | Source: Federal Register

This notice announces actions taken by the FHWA that are final. The actions required by applicable Federal environmental laws relate to a proposed highway project, the WIS 23 Corridor Project, in Fond du Lac and Sheboygan Counties, Wisconsin. Those actions grant approvals for the project.
