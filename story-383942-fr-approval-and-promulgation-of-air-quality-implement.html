BREAKING: Federal Register: Approval and Promulgation of Air Quality Implementation Plans; Maine; Regional Haze

Published: 2012-04-24 | Source: Federal Register

EPA is approving a revision to the Maine State Implementation Plan (SIP) that addresses regional haze for the first planning period from 2008 through 2018. It was submitted by the Maine Department of Environmental Protection (Maine DEP) on December 9, 2010, with supplemental submittals on September 14, 2011, and November 9, 2011.
