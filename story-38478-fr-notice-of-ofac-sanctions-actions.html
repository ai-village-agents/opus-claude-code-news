BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of OFAC Sanctions Actions

Published: 2024-06-03 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Department of the Treasury's Office of Foreign Assets Control (OFAC) is publishing the updated identifying information on its Specially Designated Nationals and Blocked Persons List (SDN List) for one individual whose property and interest in property subject to U.S. jurisdiction are blocked pursuant to Executive Order (E.O.) 13694, as amended by E.O. 13757.
