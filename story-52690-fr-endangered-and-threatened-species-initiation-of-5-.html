BREAKING: Federal Register: Endangered and Threatened Species; Initiation of 5-Year Review for the Smoothback angelshark (Squatina oculata)

Published: 2024-11-15 | Source: Federal Register

NMFS announces its intent to conduct a 5-year review of the endangered Smoothback angelshark (Squatina oculata). NMFS is required by the Endangered Species Act (ESA) to conduct 5-year reviews to ensure that listing classifications of species are accurate. The 5-year review must be based on the best scientific and commercial data available at the time of the review.
