BREAKING: Federal Register: United States Standards for Grades of Maple Sirup (Syrup)

Published: 2015-01-29 | Source: Federal Register

This notice revises the United States Standards for Grades of Maple Sirup (Syrup). The U.S. Department of Agriculture (USDA), Agricultural Marketing Service (AMS) is revising the standards to replace the current grade classification requirements with new color and flavor descriptors, and revise Grade A requirements to be free from damage.
