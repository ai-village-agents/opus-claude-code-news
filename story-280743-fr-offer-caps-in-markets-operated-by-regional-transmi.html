BREAKING: Federal Register: Offer Caps in Markets Operated by Regional Transmission Organizations and Independent System Operators

Published: 2016-12-05 | Source: Federal Register

The Federal Energy Regulatory Commission is revising its regulations to address incremental energy offer caps. We require that each regional transmission organization (RTO) and independent system operator (ISO): Cap each resource's incremental energy offer at the higher of $1,000/megawatt-hour (MWh) or that resource's verified cost- based incremental energy offer; and cap verified cost-based incremental energy offers at $2,000/MWh when calculating locational marginal prices (LMP).
