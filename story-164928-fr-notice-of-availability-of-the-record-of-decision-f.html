BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Availability of the Record of Decision for the Proposed Willow Master Development Plan Project, Alaska

Published: 2020-11-02 | Source: Federal Register

The Bureau of Land Management (BLM), Alaska State Office, announces the availability of the Record of Decision (ROD) for the Final Environmental Impact Statement (EIS) for the Willow Master Development Plan (MDP) Project. The ROD includes a deferral on a decision for drill sites 4 and 5 and associated gravel roads and pipelines, at the request of the project proponent.
