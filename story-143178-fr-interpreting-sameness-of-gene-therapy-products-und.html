BREAKING: Federal Register: Interpreting Sameness of Gene Therapy Products Under the Orphan Drug Regulations; Draft Guidance for Industry; Availability

Published: 2020-01-30 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA or Agency) is announcing the availability of a draft document entitled "Interpreting Sameness of Gene Therapy Products Under the Orphan Drug Regulations." The draft guidance document provides FDA's current thinking on the criteria to determine sameness of human gene therapy products for the purpose of orphan drug designation and orphan drug exclusivity.
