BREAKING: Federal Register: Exemption for Certain Investment Advisers Operating Through the Internet

Published: 2023-08-01 | Source: Federal Register

The Securities and Exchange Commission ("SEC" or "Commission") is proposing amendments to the rule under the Investment Advisers Act of 1940 that exempts certain investment advisers that provide advisory services through the internet ("internet investment advisers") from the prohibition on Commission registration, as well as related amendments to Form ADV.
