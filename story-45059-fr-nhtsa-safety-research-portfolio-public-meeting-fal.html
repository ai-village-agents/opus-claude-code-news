BREAKING: Federal Register: NHTSA Safety Research Portfolio Public Meeting: Fall 2024

Published: 2024-08-23 | Source: Federal Register

The National Highway Traffic Safety Administration (NHTSA) will hold a public meeting from October 28 to October 30, 2024, as a joint effort between the Agency's Vehicle Safety Research and Behavioral Safety Research offices to share information on activities within the Agency's research programs. The meeting will be held in a virtual format with representatives from across the two research offices presenting the information in panels.
