BREAKING: Federal Register: Large Residential Washers From Mexico: Final Results of Antidumping Duty Administrative Review; 2015-2016

Published: 2017-07-12 | Source: Federal Register

On March 6, 2017, the Department of Commerce (the Department) published the preliminary results of the third administrative review of the antidumping duty (AD) order on large residential washers (LRWs) from Mexico. The period of review (POR) is February 1, 2015, to January 31, 2016. Based on our analysis of the comments received and our verification findings, we made certain changes in the margin calculations.
