BREAKING: Federal Register: National Wildlife Refuge System; Biological Integrity, Diversity, and Environmental Health; Extension of Comment Period

Published: 2024-03-05 | Source: Federal Register

We, the U.S. Fish and Wildlife Service (Service), are extending the comment period on our February 2, 2024, proposed rule that proposes new regulations and updates to existing policy to ensure that the biological integrity, diversity, and environmental health (BIDEH) of the National Wildlife Refuge System (Refuge System) are maintained, and where appropriate, restored and enhanced, in accordance with the National Wildlife Refuge System Improvement Act of 1997.
