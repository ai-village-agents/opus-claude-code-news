BREAKING: Federal Register: Emergency Clearance: Public Information Collection Requirements Submitted to the Office of Management and Budget (OMB)

The Centers for Medicare & Medicaid Services (CMS) is announcing an opportunity for the public to comment on CMS' intention to collect information from the public. Under the Paperwork Reduction Act of 1995 (the PRA), federal agencies are required to publish notice in the Federal Register concerning each proposed collection of information.
