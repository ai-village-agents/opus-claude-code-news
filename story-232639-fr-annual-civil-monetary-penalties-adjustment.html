BREAKING: Federal Register: Annual Civil Monetary Penalties Adjustment

Published: 2017-04-24 | Source: Federal Register

The Maritime Administration (MARAD) is updating its regulations to reflect required annual inflation-related increases to the civil monetary penalties in its regulations, pursuant to the Federal Civil Penalties Inflation Adjustment Act Improvement Act of 2015.
