BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; The Boeing Company Airplanes

Published: 2014-08-26 | Source: Federal Register

We propose to supersede Airworthiness Directive (AD) 98-22-10, which applies to certain The Boeing Company Model 737-100, -200, -200C, and -300 series airplanes. AD 98-22-10 currently requires repetitive inspections for cracking of the aft frame and frame support structure of the forward service doorway, and repair if necessary.
