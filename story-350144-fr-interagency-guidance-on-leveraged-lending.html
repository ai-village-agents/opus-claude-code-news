BREAKING: Federal Register: Interagency Guidance on Leveraged Lending

Published: 2013-03-22 | Source: Federal Register

The OCC, Board, and the FDIC (collectively, the "agencies") are issuing final guidance on leveraged lending. This guidance outlines for agency-supervised institutions high-level principles related to safe-and-sound leveraged lending activities, including underwriting considerations, assessing and documenting enterprise value, risk management expectations for credits awaiting distribution, stress- testing expectations, pipeline portfolio management, and risk management expectations for exposures ...
