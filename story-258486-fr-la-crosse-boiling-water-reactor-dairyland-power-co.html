BREAKING: Federal Register: La Crosse Boiling Water Reactor, Dairyland Power Cooperative, Consideration of Approval of Transfer of License and Conforming Amendment

Published: 2016-03-18 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Nuclear Regulatory Commission (NRC) received and is considering approval of an application filed by the Dairyland Power Cooperative (DPC) on October 8, 2015. The application seeks NRC approval of the direct transfer of Facility Operating License No. DPR- 45 for the La Crosse Boiling Water Reactor (LACBWR), from the current holder, DPC, to LaCrosseSolutions, LLC (LS) a wholly owned subsidiary of EnergySolutions (ES).
