BREAKING: Federal Register: Reasonable Charges for Inpatient Medical Severity-Diagnosis Related Groups and Skilled Nursing Facility Medical Services (v4.22); Fiscal Year 2022 Update

Published: 2021-09-13 | Source: Federal Register

This document updates the acute inpatient and Skilled Nursing Facility (SNF)/sub-acute inpatient facility charges. The updated charges are based on Medical Severity-Diagnosis Related Groups (MS-DRG) for fiscal year (FY) 2022.
