BREAKING: Federal Register: Rare Element Resources, Inc.; Upton Pilot Project

Published: 2023-08-03 | Source: Federal Register

(RER, the licensee) for its possession, and storage of source material in a pilot project for the recovery of rare earth elements (REE).
