BREAKING: Federal Register: Fisheries of the Northeastern United States; Mid-Atlantic Forage Species Omnibus Amendment

Published: 2017-04-24 | Source: Federal Register

NMFS proposes to approve and implement measures included in an omnibus amendment submitted by the Mid-Atlantic Fishery Management Council that would manage forage species within Mid-Atlantic Federal waters. This amendment would implement an annual landing limit, possession limits, and permitting and reporting requirements for Atlantic chub mackerel and certain previously unmanaged forage species and species groups within Mid-Atlantic Federal waters.
