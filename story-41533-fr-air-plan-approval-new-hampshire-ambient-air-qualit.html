BREAKING: Federal Register: Air Plan Approval; New Hampshire; Ambient Air Quality Standards

Published: 2024-07-11 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is proposing to approve most of a State Implementation Plan (SIP) revision submitted by the State of New Hampshire. This revision updates the State regulation containing ambient air quality standards.
