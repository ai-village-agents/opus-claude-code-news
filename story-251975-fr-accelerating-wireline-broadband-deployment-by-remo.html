BREAKING: Federal Register: Accelerating Wireline Broadband Deployment by Removing Barriers to Infrastructure Investment

Published: 2017-12-28 | Source: Federal Register

In this document, a Further Notice of Proposed Rulemaking (FNPRM) seeks comment on a number of actions aimed at removing unnecessary regulatory barriers to the deployment of high-speed broadband networks. The FNPRM seeks comment on pole attachment reforms, changes to the copper retirement and other network change notification processes, and changes to the section 214(a) discontinuance application process.
