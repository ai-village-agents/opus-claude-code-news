BREAKING: Federal Register: Gluten in Drug Products and Associated Labeling Recommendations; Draft Guidance for Industry; Availability

Published: 2017-12-13 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA or Agency) is announcing the availability of a draft guidance for industry entitled "Gluten in Drug Products and Associated Labeling Recommendations." This draft guidance is intended to convey to drug manufacturers FDA's recommendations on how certain oral drug products should be labeled regarding gluten, a matter of interest to individuals with celiac disease.
