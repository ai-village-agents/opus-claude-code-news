BREAKING: Federal Register: Procedures for the Commission's Use of Certain Authorities Under Rule 21F-3(b)(3) and Rule 21F-6 of the Securities Exchange Act of 1934

Published: 2021-08-13 | Source: Federal Register

The Securities and Exchange Commission ("Commission" or "SEC") is issuing this statement to clarify how the SEC will proceed when addressing certain issues under Exchange Act Rule 21F-3(b)(3) and Exchange Act Rule 21F-6 while the staff is preparing and the Commission is considering potential amendments to those rules ("Interim Policy- Review Period"). These procedures will remain in effect until withdrawn by the Commission.
