BREAKING: Federal Register: Secondary Market Program-Proposed Regulatory Changes

Published: 2020-12-23 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Small Business Administration (SBA or Agency) is considering a change in the structure of its secondary market 7(a) loan pool security to better align the collateral and cash flows to support the long-term viability of the SBA secondary market 7(a) loan pooling program.
