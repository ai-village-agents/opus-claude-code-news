BREAKING: Federal Register: Best Practices for Development and Application of Disease Progression Models; Public Workshop; Establishment of a Public Docket; Request for Comments

Published: 2021-02-24 | Source: Federal Register

One of the goals of the Prescription Drug User Fee Act of 2017 (PDUFA VI), part of the FDA Reauthorization Act of 2017 (FDARA), is advancing model-informed drug development (MIDD). The "Best Practices for Development and Application of Disease Progression Models" workshop fulfills FDA's performance commitment under PDUFA VI to hold a workshop.
