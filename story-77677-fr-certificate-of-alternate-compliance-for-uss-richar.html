BREAKING: Federal Register: Certificate of Alternate Compliance for USS Richard M. McCool (LPD-29)

Published: 2023-09-25 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Navy hereby announces that a Certificate of Alternate Compliance has been issued for USS Richard M. McCool (LPD-29).
