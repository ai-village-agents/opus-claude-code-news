BREAKING: Federal Register: Utility Allowance Submetering

Published: 2019-03-04 | Source: Federal Register

This document contains final regulations that amend the utility allowance regulations concerning the low-income housing credit under section 42 of the Internal Revenue Code (Code). These final regulations extend the principles of the current submetering rules. The current rules address situations in which a building owner purchases a utility from a utility company and then separately charges the tenants for the utility.
