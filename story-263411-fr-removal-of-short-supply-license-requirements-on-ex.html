BREAKING: Federal Register: Removal of Short Supply License Requirements on Exports of Crude Oil

Published: 2016-05-12 | Source: Federal Register

The Bureau of Industry and Security (BIS) publishes this final rule to amend the Export Administration Regulations (EAR) to remove the short supply license requirements that, prior to the entry into force of the "Consolidated Appropriations Act, 2016" on December 18, 2015, applied to exports of crude oil from the United States.
