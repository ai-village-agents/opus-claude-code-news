BREAKING: Federal Register: Process for Determining That an Individual Shall Not Be Deemed an Employee of the Public Health Service

Published: 2024-07-05 | Source: Federal Register

This rule sets forth a process by which the Attorney General or a designee may determine that an individual shall not be deemed an employee of the Public Health Service for purposes of medical malpractice coverage under the Public Health Service Act.
