BREAKING: Federal Register: Agency Information Collection Activities; Extension of Collection; Comment Request; Notification Requirements for Coal and Wood Burning Appliances

As required by the Paperwork Reduction Act of 1995, the Consumer Product Safety Commission (CPSC or Commission) requests comments on a proposed extension of approval of information collection regarding notification requirements for coal and wood burning appliances. The Office of Management and Budget (OMB) previously approved the collection of information under control number 3041-0040. OMB's most recent extension of approval will expire on March 31, 2025.
