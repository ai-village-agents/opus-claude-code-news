BREAKING: Federal Register: Agency Information Collection Activities; New Information Collection Request: Generic Clearance of Customer Satisfaction Surveys

Published: 2014-08-29 | Source: Federal Register

Executive Order 12862 directs Federal agencies to provide service to the public that matches or exceeds the best service available in the private sector. In order to work continuously to ensure that our programs are effective and meet our customers' needs, the Federal Motor Carrier Safety Administration (FMCSA) seeks to obtain OMB approval of a generic clearance to collect feedback on our service delivery.
