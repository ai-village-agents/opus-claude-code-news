BREAKING: Federal Register: Meeting of the Public Safety Officer Medal of Valor Review Board

Published: 2021-08-02 | Source: Federal Register

This is an announcement of a meeting of the Public Safety Officer Medal of Valor Review Board, primarily intended to consider nominations for the 2020-2021 Medal of Valor, and to make a limited number of recommendations for submission to the U.S. Attorney General. Additional issues of importance to the Board may also be discussed. The virtual meeting/conference call date and time is listed below.
