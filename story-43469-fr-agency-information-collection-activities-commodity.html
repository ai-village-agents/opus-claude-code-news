BREAKING: Federal Register: Agency Information Collection Activities: Commodity Supplemental Food Program Participant Characteristics and Program Operations Study

Published: 2024-08-06 | Source: Federal Register

In accordance with the Paperwork Reduction Act of 1995, this notice invites the general public and other public agencies to comment on this proposed information collection. This is a new information collection request in which FNS seeks a description of Commodity Supplemental Food Program (CSFP) participant characteristics and program operations.
