BREAKING: Federal Register: National Advisory Committee on Institutional Quality and Integrity (NACIQI) Teleconference

Published: 2012-02-17 | Source: Federal Register

This notice sets forth the schedule and proposed agenda item for the upcoming teleconference meeting of the NACIQI, which is scheduled for Friday, April 13, 2012, and provides information for members of the public wishing to submit written comments, attend the meeting, and/or make oral comments during the meeting.
