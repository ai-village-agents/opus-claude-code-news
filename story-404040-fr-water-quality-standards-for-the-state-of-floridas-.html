BREAKING: Federal Register: Water Quality Standards for the State of Florida's Streams and Downstream Protection Values for Lakes: Remanded Provisions

Published: 2012-12-18 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA or Agency) is proposing a rule that addresses an order by the U.S. District Court for the Northern District of Florida from February 18, 2012, which remanded to EPA two portions of its numeric water quality standards for nutrients in Florida that were promulgated and published on December 6, 2010.
