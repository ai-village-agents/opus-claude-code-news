BREAKING: Federal Register: Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standards; Compressed Natural Gas Fuel Container Integrity

Published: 2022-02-11 | Source: Federal Register

This final rule amends the visual inspection labeling requirement in Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard (FMVSS) No. 304, "Compressed natural gas fuel container integrity," by modifying the periodic inspection interval for compressed natural gas (CNG) fuel containers installed on vehicles with a gross vehicle weight rating (GVWR) greater than 4,536 kilograms (10,000 pounds).
