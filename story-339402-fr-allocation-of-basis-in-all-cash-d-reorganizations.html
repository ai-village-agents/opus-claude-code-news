BREAKING: Federal Register: Allocation of Basis in All Cash D Reorganizations

Published: 2014-11-12 | Source: Federal Register

This document contains final regulations regarding the determination of the basis of stock or securities in certain reorganizations where no stock or securities of the issuing corporation is issued and distributed in the transaction.
