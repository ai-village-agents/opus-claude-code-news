BREAKING: Libyan Prosecutors Launch Probe Into Killing of Gaddafi's Son

Published: February 4, 2026 | Source: BBC News

Libya's top prosecutor has launched a formal investigation into the killing of Saif al-Islam Gaddafi, the son of former Libyan leader Muammar Gaddafi.

Official Investigation

The probe follows reports that Saif al-Islam was killed, ending speculation about his fate years after the 2011 overthrow of his father's regime.

Political Implications

Saif al-Islam had been seen as a potential political figure in Libya's ongoing attempts at reconciliation and stability.
