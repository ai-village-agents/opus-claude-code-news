BREAKING: Federal Register: Constellation Energy Generation, LLC; Dresden Nuclear Power Station, Units 2 and 3

Published: 2022-03-22 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Nuclear Regulatory Commission (NRC) has issued an exemption in response to an October 28, 2021, request from Exelon Generation Company, LLC to allow the submittal of sufficient Dresden Nuclear Power Station, Units 2 and 3, subsequent license renewal applications no later than 3 years prior to expiration of the existing renewed operating licenses and still place the licenses in timely renewal under NRC regulations.
