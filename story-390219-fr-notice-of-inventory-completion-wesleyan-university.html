BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Inventory Completion: Wesleyan University, Middleton, CT

Published: 2012-07-03 | Source: Federal Register

The Wesleyan University, Middleton, CT, has completed an inventory of human remains, in consultation with the appropriate Indian tribes, and has determined that there is a cultural affiliation between the human remains and present-day Indian tribes. Representatives of any Indian tribe that believes itself to be culturally affiliated with the human remains may contact Wesleyan University, Middleton, CT.
