BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone; Cape Fear River, Wilmington, NC

Published: 2024-04-08 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is establishing a temporary safety zone for navigable waters of the Cape Fear River. This action is necessary for the safety of life on these navigable waters, in Wilmington, NC, during a fireworks display to be executed by Zambelli Fireworks.
