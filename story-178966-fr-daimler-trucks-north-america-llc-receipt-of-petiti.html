BREAKING: Federal Register: Daimler Trucks North America, LLC, Receipt of Petition for Decision of Inconsequential Noncompliance

Published: 2019-05-13 | Source: Federal Register

Daimler Trucks North America, LLC (DTNA), has determined that certain model year (MY) 2013-2018 Thomas Built Buses do not fully comply with Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard (FMVSS) No. 222, School Bus Passenger Seating and Crash Protection. DTNA filed a noncompliance report dated November 27, 2017.
