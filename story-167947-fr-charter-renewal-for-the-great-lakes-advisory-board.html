BREAKING: Federal Register: Charter Renewal for the Great Lakes Advisory Board

Published: 2020-12-11 | Source: Federal Register

Notice is hereby given that the Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) has determined that, in accordance with the provisions of the Federal Advisory Committee Act (FACA), the EPA Great Lakes Advisory Board is a necessary committee which is in the public's interest. Accordingly, the Advisory Board will be renewed for an additional two- year period.
