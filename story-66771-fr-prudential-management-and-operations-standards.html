BREAKING: Federal Register: Prudential Management and Operations Standards

Published: 2023-05-04 | Source: Federal Register

The Federal Housing Finance Agency (FHFA) is proposing to amend its prudential management and operations standards Rule (rule) to clarify that FHFA may establish prudential management and operations standards (Standards) as regulations as well as guidelines. The proposed amendments to the rule would also revise definitions and make other conforming changes. FHFA is not proposing to establish new Standards or to revise Standards already established.
