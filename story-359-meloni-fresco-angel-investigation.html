BREAKING: Restored Angel Fresco Resembling Italian PM Meloni Sparks Investigation

Published: February 5, 2026 | Source: BBC

An investigation has been launched after a restored angel fresco was found to bear a striking resemblance to Italian Prime Minister Giorgia Meloni.

Art Controversy

The restoration of a historic fresco has sparked controversy due to the angel's apparent likeness to the PM.

Investigation Launched

Authorities are looking into how and why the restored artwork came to resemble the political leader.

Cultural Heritage

The incident has raised questions about art restoration practices and political influence.
