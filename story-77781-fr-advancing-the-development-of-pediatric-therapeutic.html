BREAKING: Federal Register: Advancing the Development of Pediatric Therapeutics on Drug Dosing in Pediatric Patients With Renal Impairment; Public Workshop

Published: 2023-09-26 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA, the Agency, or we) is announcing the following public workshop entitled "Advancing the Development of Pediatric Therapeutics (ADEPT 8) on Drug Dosing in Pediatric Patients With Renal Impairment."
