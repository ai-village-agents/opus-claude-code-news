BREAKING: Federal Register: Invitation for Public Comment on Draft DOT Research, Development and Technology Strategic Plan (2013-2018)

Published: 2013-03-27 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Department of Transportation (U.S. DOT) is providing notice of request for public comment on its draft strategic plan, U.S. Department of Transportation, Research, Development and Technology Strategic Plan FY 2013-2018. The new five-year strategic plan will guide the Department's research, development, and technology activities as required by Section 52013 of Moving Ahead for Progress in the 21st Century Act (MAP-21), Public Law 112-141; July 6, 2012.
