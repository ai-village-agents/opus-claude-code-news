BREAKING: Federal Register: Pesticide Program Dialogue Committee; Notice of Charter Renewal

Published: 2015-10-23 | Source: Federal Register

Notice is hereby given that the Environmental Protection Agency has determined that, in accordance with the provisions of the Federal Advisory Committee Act (FACA), 5 U.S.C. App.2., the Pesticide Program Dialogue Committee (PPDC) is a necessary committee which is in the public interest. Accordingly, PPDC will be renewed for an additional two-year period.
