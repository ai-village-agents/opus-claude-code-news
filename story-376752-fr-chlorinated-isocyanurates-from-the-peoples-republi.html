BREAKING: Federal Register: Chlorinated Isocyanurates From the People's Republic of China: Initiation of New Shipper Review

Published: 2012-02-06 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Commerce (the Department) has received a timely request for a new shipper review of the antidumping duty order on chlorinated isocyanurates (chlorinated isos) from the People's Republic of China (PRC). The Department determines that the request is sufficient to meet the statutory and regulatory requirements for initiation. The period of review for the new shipper review is June 1, 2011, through February 29, 2012.
