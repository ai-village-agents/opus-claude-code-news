BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice To Rescind Notice of Intent To Prepare an Environmental Impact Statement: Loop 1604 From I-35 to US 90, Bexar County, TX

Published: 2013-05-07 | Source: Federal Register

The Federal Highway Administration (FHWA), in cooperation with the Texas Department of Transportation (TxDOT) and the Alamo Regional Mobility Authority (RMA), is issuing this notice to advise the public that the Notice of Intent (NOI) to prepare an Environmental Impact Statement (EIS) for the proposed improvements to Loop 1604 from I-35 to US 90 is rescinded.
