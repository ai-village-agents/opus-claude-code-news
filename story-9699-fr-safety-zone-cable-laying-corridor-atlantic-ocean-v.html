BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone; Cable Laying Corridor, Atlantic Ocean, Virginia Beach, Virginia

Published: 2025-04-21 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is establishing a temporary, moving safety zone to surround nearshore operations conducted by a cable laying barge. This action is necessary to provide for the safety of life on these navigable waters near Virginia Beach, Virginia. Cable lay and burial operations will create navigational hazards moving along a corridor from shore extending seaward 12 NM.
