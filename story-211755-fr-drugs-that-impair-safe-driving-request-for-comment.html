BREAKING: Federal Register: Drugs that Impair Safe Driving; Request for Comments

Published: 2018-07-17 | Source: Federal Register

NHTSA is reviewing the literature on drug use and driving with the aim of updating its Drugs and Human Performance Fact Sheets that are used by the criminal justice community and others as they address drug-impaired driving.
