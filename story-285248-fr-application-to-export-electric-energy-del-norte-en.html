BREAKING: Federal Register: Application To Export Electric Energy; Del Norte Energy LLC

Published: 2015-02-02 | Source: Federal Register

Del Norte Energy LLC (Del Norte or Applicant) has applied for authority to transmit electric energy from the United States to Mexico pursuant to section 202(e) of the Federal Power Act.
