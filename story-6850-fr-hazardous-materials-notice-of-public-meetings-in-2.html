BREAKING: Federal Register: Hazardous Materials: Notice of Public Meetings in 2025 for International Standards on the Transport of Dangerous Goods

Published: 2025-03-12 | Source: Federal Register

This notice announces that the Pipeline and Hazardous Materials Safety Administration (PHMSA) Office of Hazardous Materials Safety (OHMS) will host four public meetings during 2025 in advance of certain international meetings. The first meeting will be held in preparation of the International Civil Aviation Organization's (ICAO) Dangerous Goods Panel (DGP) Working Group 25 (WG/25) scheduled for April 21-25, 2025 in Delhi, India.
