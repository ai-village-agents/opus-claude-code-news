BREAKING: Federal Register: Announcement of Test Concerning Manifesting and Entry of Residue Found in Instruments of International Traffic (IITs)

Published: 2013-08-27 | Source: Federal Register

This document announces U.S. Customs and Border Protection's (CBP's) plan to conduct a test concerning the manifesting and entry of residual cargo found in containers arriving as Instruments of International Traffic (IIT). The document also announces that CBP will begin enforcement of HQ Ruling H026715, dated June 19, 2009, when the test announced in this document begins.
