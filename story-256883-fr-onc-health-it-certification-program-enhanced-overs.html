BREAKING: Federal Register: ONC Health IT Certification Program: Enhanced Oversight and Accountability

Published: 2016-03-02 | Source: Federal Register

This notice of proposed rulemaking ("proposed rule") introduces modifications and new requirements under the ONC Health IT Certification Program ("Program"), including provisions related to the Office of the National Coordinator for Health Information Technology (ONC)'s role in the Program.
