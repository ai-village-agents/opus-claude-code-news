BREAKING: Federal Register: Endangered and Threatened Wildlife and Plants; Designation of Critical Habitat for Mississippi Gopher Frog

Published: 2012-01-17 | Source: Federal Register

We, the U.S. Fish and Wildlife Service, announce the reopening of the public comment period on our September 27, 2011, revised proposed rule to designate critical habitat for the Mississippi gopher frog (Rana sevosa) [=Rana capito sevosa] under the Endangered Species Act of 1973, as amended (Act). We are reopening the comment period to announce changes in methodology from the revised proposed rule and to allow all interested parties another opportunity to comment on the revised proposed rule.
