BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Purchased/Referred Care Delivery Area Redesignation for the Northwestern Band of the Shoshone Nation

Published: 2020-07-10 | Source: Federal Register

This Notice advises the public that the Indian Health Service (IHS) proposes to expand the geographic boundaries of the Purchased/ Referred Care Delivery Area (PRCDA) for the Northwestern Band of the Shoshone Nation (NWBSN) in the State of Utah to include the Utah counties of Box Elder, Davis, Salt Lake, and Weber.
