BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Saab AB, Saab Aerosystems Airplanes

Published: 2014-08-12 | Source: Federal Register

We are revising an earlier proposed airworthiness directive (AD) for all Saab AB, Saab Aerosystems Model 340B airplanes. The NPRM proposed to require an inspection of the stick pusher rigging and an adjustment to the correct setting if necessary. The NPRM was prompted by a report that the elevator position quoted in an aircraft maintenance manual is incorrect and a report that the trunnion at the lower part of the control column was installed incorrectly.
