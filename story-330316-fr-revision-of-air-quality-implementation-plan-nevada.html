BREAKING: Federal Register: Revision of Air Quality Implementation Plan; Nevada; Clark County; Stationary Source Permits

Published: 2014-07-23 | Source: Federal Register

EPA is proposing to fully approve permitting related rules submitted by Nevada as a revision to the Clark County Department of Air Quality (Clark or DEQ) portion of the state implementation plan (SIP) for the State of Nevada. These rules were adopted by DEQ to regulate the construction and modification of stationary sources of air pollution within Clark County.
