BREAKING: Federal Register: Electric Reliability Organization Proposal To Retire Requirements in Reliability Standards

Published: 2013-06-28 | Source: Federal Register

Pursuant to section 215 of the Federal Power Act, the Commission proposes to approve the retirement of 34 requirements within 19 Reliability Standards identified by the North American Electric Reliability Corporation (NERC), the Commission-certified Electric Reliability Organization. The requirements proposed for retirement either: Provide little protection for Bulk-Power System reliability or are redundant with other aspects of the Reliability Standards.
