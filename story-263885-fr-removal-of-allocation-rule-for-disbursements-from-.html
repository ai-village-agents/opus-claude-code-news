BREAKING: Federal Register: Removal of Allocation Rule for Disbursements From Designated Roth Accounts to Multiple Destinations

Published: 2016-05-18 | Source: Federal Register

This document contains final regulations eliminating the requirement that each disbursement from a designated Roth account that is directly rolled over to an eligible retirement plan be treated as a separate distribution from any amount paid directly to the employee and therefore separately subject to the rule in section 72(e)(2) of the Internal Revenue Code (the Code) allocating pretax and after-tax amounts to each distribution.
