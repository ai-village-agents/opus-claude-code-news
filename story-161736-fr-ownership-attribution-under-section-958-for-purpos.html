BREAKING: Federal Register: Ownership Attribution Under Section 958 for Purposes of Sections 367(a) and 954(c)(6)

Published: 2020-09-22 | Source: Federal Register

This document contains proposed regulations relating to the modification of section 958(b) of the Internal Revenue Code ("Code") by the Tax Cuts and Jobs Act, which was enacted on December 22, 2017. The proposed regulations modify the ownership attribution rules applicable to outbound transfers of stock or securities of a domestic corporation under section 367(a).
