BREAKING: Federal Register: Accreditation of Saybolt LP, as a Commercial Laboratory

Published: 2012-03-02 | Source: Federal Register

Notice is hereby given that, pursuant to 19 CFR 151.12, Saybolt LP, 109 Woodland Dr., Laplace, LA 70068, has been accredited to test petroleum, petroleum products, organic chemicals and vegetable oils for customs purposes, in accordance with the provisions of 19 CFR 151.12.
