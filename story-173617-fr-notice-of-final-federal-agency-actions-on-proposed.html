BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Final Federal Agency Actions on Proposed Highway in Utah

Published: 2019-03-08 | Source: Federal Register

This notice announces certain actions taken by UDOT that are final Federal agency actions. These actions relate to a proposed highway project on Interstate 15 (I-15) at and in the vicinity of the Payson Main Street Interchange (exit 250) in the County of Utah, State of Utah. Those actions grant licenses, permits and/or approvals for the project.
