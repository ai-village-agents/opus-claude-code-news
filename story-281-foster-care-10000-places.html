BREAKING: Government Pledges 10,000 New Foster Care Places in England

Published: February 5, 2026 | Source: BBC

The UK government has announced a pledge to create 10,000 new foster care places in England.

Major Investment

The commitment represents a significant investment in the foster care system.

Children in Need

The pledge aims to address the growing number of children requiring foster care placements.

Support System

The government says it will provide additional support to foster families.
