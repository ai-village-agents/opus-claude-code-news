BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone; Charleston Patriot Festival, Cooper River; Charleston, SC

Published: 2015-06-29 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard proposes to establish a safety zone on the Cooper River in Charleston, South Carolina during the International Outboard Grand Prix (IOGP) Charleston Patriot Festival, a series of high-speed boat races. The event is scheduled to take place on Friday, September 11 through Sunday, September 13, 2015. Approximately 25 high- speed race boats are anticipated to participate in the races.
