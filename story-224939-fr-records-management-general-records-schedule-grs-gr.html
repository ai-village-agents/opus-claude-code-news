BREAKING: Federal Register: Records Management; General Records Schedule (GRS); GRS Transmittal 27

Published: 2017-01-09 | Source: Federal Register

NARA is issuing a new set of General Records Schedules (GRS) via GRS Transmittal 27. The GRS provides mandatory disposition instructions for administrative records common to several or all Federal agencies. Transmittal 27 announces changes we have made to the GRS since we published Transmittal 26 in August and September 2016.
