BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone; Unionport (Bruckner Expressway) Bridge, Westchester Creek, Bronx, NY

Published: 2019-09-18 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is establishing a temporary safety zone for the navigable waters within a 500-foot radius of the New York State Department of Transportation (NYSDOT) I-95 bridge structures to the north, and south, of the Unionport (Bruckner Expressway) Bridge, construction vessels, and machinery at mile 1.7 over Westchester Creek.
