BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone; Monongahela River Mile 28.0 to Mile 30.0, Pittsburgh, PA

Published: 2020-02-03 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is proposing to establish a temporary safety zone for mile 28.0 to mile 30.0 of the Monongahela River. This action is necessary to provide for the safety of life on these navigable waters near Mitchell Power Plant, Pittsburgh, PA, during an electrical conducter pull from March 2, 2020 through March 20, 2020.
