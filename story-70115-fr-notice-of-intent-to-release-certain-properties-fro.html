BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Intent To Release Certain Properties From All Terms, Conditions, Reservations and Restrictions of a Quitclaim Deed Agreement Between City of Palatka and the Federal Aviation Administration for the Palatka Municipal-Lt. Kay Larkin Field, Palatka, FL

Published: 2023-06-16 | Source: Federal Register

Kay Larkin Field, Palatka, FL from the conditions, reservations, and restrictions as contained in a Quitclaim Deed agreement between the FAA and the City of Palatka, dated February 24, 1947.
