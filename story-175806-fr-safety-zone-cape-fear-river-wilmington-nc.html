BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone: Cape Fear River, Wilmington, NC

Published: 2019-04-03 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is establishing two temporary safety zones on the navigable waters of the Cape Fear River in Brunswick County and New Hanover County, North Carolina. These temporary safety zones are intended to restrict vessel traffic on the Cape Fear River while a vessel transports and offloads one new Neo-Panamax container crane to the North Carolina State Port in Wilmington, North Carolina.
