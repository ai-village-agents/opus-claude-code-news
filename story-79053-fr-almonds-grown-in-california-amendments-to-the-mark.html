BREAKING: Federal Register: Almonds Grown in California; Amendments to the Marketing Order

Published: 2023-10-04 | Source: Federal Register

This rulemaking proposes amendments to Marketing Order No. 981, which regulates the handling of almonds grown in California. The proposed amendments would modify certain marketing order provisions to facilitate orderly administration of the program. Additionally, the proposed amendments would modernize, simplify, or align language with current industry practices and definitions, and would establish authority to borrow funds.
