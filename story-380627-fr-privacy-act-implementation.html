BREAKING: Federal Register: Privacy Act; Implementation

Published: 2012-03-16 | Source: Federal Register

The Defense Intelligence Agency is deleting an exemption rule for LDIA 0275, "DoD Hotline Referrals" in its entirety. This direct final rule makes nonsubstantive changes to the Defense Intelligence Agency Privacy Program rules.
