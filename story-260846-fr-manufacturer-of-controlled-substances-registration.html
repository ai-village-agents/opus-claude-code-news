BREAKING: Federal Register: Manufacturer of Controlled Substances Registration: Stepan Company

Published: 2016-04-14 | Source: Federal Register

Stepan Company applied to be registered as a manufacturer of certain basic classes of controlled substances. The Drug Enforcement Administration (DEA) grants Stepan Company registration as a manufacturer of those controlled substances.
