BREAKING: Federal Register: Security Zone; Potomac and Anacostia Rivers, Washington, DC

Published: 2012-01-18 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is establishing a temporary security zone encompassing certain waters of the Potomac River and Anacostia River in order to safeguard high-ranking public officials from terrorist acts and incidents. This action is necessary to ensure the safety of persons and property, and prevent terrorist acts or incidents.
