BREAKING: Federal Register: Labeling of Pesticide Products and Devices for Export; Clarification of Requirements

Published: 2014-07-11 | Source: Federal Register

EPA is proposing to amend the regulations that pertain to the labeling of pesticide products and devices that are intended solely for export.
