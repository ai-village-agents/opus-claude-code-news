BREAKING: Federal Register: Environmental Impact Statement; Pennington County, SD; Pennington County, Maine

Published: 2015-12-17 | Source: Federal Register

The FHWA is issuing this notice to advise the public that we are rescinding the Notice of Intent (NOI) to prepare an Environmental Impact Statement (EIS) for roadway improvements proposed for South Rochford Road in Pennington County, South Dakota. The NOI was published in the Federal Register on January 30, 2012. A Draft Environmental Impact Statement (DEIS) was not released.
