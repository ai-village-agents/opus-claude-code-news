BREAKING: Federal Register: Almonds Grown in California; Proposed Amendments to Marketing Order 981 and Referendum Order

Published: 2018-11-14 | Source: Federal Register

This rulemaking proposes amendments to Marketing Order No. 981, which regulates the handling of almonds in California. The Almond Board of California (Board) recommended changing the dates associated with the process to nominate members to the Board as well as the start of the term of office of members of the Board.
