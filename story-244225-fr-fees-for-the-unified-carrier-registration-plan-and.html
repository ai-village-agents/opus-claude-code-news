BREAKING: Federal Register: Fees for the Unified Carrier Registration Plan and Agreement

Published: 2017-09-21 | Source: Federal Register

FMCSA proposes to establish reductions in the annual registration fees collected from motor carriers, motor private carriers of property, brokers, freight forwarders, and leasing companies for the Unified Carrier Registration (UCR) Plan and Agreement for the registration years 2018, 2019 and subsequent years.
