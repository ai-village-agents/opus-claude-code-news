BREAKING: Federal Register: Request for Information on Data Collection and Tracking for Qualified Opportunity Zones

Published: 2019-05-01 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of the Treasury (Treasury Department) is publishing this notice and request for information to seek public input on the development of public information collection and tracking related to investment in qualified opportunity funds (QOFs).
