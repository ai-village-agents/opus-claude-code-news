BREAKING: Federal Register: Update and Clarify Regulatory Bars to Benefits Based on Character of Discharge

Published: 2021-09-09 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Veterans Affairs (VA) is requesting information to assist in the modification of the regulatory framework for discharges considered "dishonorable" for VA benefit eligibility purposes. On July 10, 2020, VA published a proposed rule for public notice and comment.
