BREAKING: Federal Register: NPDES Electronic Reporting Rule

Published: 2013-07-30 | Source: Federal Register

EPA is proposing a regulation that would require electronic reporting for current paper-based NPDES reports. This action will save time and resources for permittees, states, tribes, territories, and EPA while improving compliance and providing better protection of the Nation's waters.
