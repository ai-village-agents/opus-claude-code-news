BREAKING: Federal Register: Privacy Act of 1974; System of Records

Published: 2023-01-25 | Source: Federal Register

Pursuant to the Privacy Act of 1974, notice is hereby given that the Department of Veterans Affairs (VA) is modifying the system of records entitled "Voluntary Service Records--VA" (57VA10B2A).
