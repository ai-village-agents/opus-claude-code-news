BREAKING: Federal Register: Ripe Olives From Spain: Continuation of Antidumping and Countervailing Duty Orders; Correction

Published: 2024-09-05 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Department of Commerce (Commerce) published a notice in the Federal Register of August 9, 2024 in which Commerce issued the continuation of the antidumping duty (AD) order and countervailing duty (CVD) orders on ripe olives from Spain. This notice did not correctly state the applicable date.
