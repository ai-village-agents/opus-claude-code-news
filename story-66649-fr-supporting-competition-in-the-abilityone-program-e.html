BREAKING: Federal Register: Supporting Competition in the AbilityOne Program; Extension of Comment Period

Published: 2023-05-03 | Source: Federal Register

On March 13, 2023, the Committee for Purchase from People Who Are Blind or Severely Disabled (Committee), operating as the U.S. AbilityOne Commission (Commission), published a proposed rule, Supporting Competition in the AbilityOne Program, with a 60-day comment period ending on May 11, 2023. The Commission has determined that a 30- day extension of the comment period, until June 12, 2023, is appropriate.
