BREAKING: Federal Register: Medical Devices; Gastroenterology-Urology Devices; Classification of the Vibrator for Climax Control of Premature Ejaculation; Republication

Published: 2015-06-23 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA) is republishing in its entirety a final order entitled "Medical Devices; Gastroenterology- Urology Devices; Classification of the Vibrator for Climax Control of Premature Ejaculation" that published in the Federal Register on May 28, 2015 (80 FR 30353). FDA is republishing to correct an inadvertent omission of information. FDA is classifying the vibrator for climax control of premature ejaculation into class II (special controls).
