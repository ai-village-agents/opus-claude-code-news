BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Inventory Completion: Warren Anatomical Museum, Harvard University, Boston, MA

Published: 2020-08-03 | Source: Federal Register

The Peabody Museum of Archaeology and Ethnology and Warren Anatomical Museum, Harvard University, have completed an inventory of human remains, in consultation with the appropriate Indian Tribes, and have determined that there is a cultural affiliation between the human remains and present-day Indian Tribes.
