BREAKING: Federal Register: Thresholds Increase for the Major Assets Prohibition of the Depository Institution Management Interlocks Act Rules

Published: 2019-01-31 | Source: Federal Register

The OCC, the Board, and the FDIC (collectively, the agencies) are inviting comment on a proposed rule that would increase the major assets prohibition thresholds for management interlocks in the agencies' rules implementing the Depository Institution Management Interlocks Act (DIMIA).
