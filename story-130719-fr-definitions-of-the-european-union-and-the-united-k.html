BREAKING: Federal Register: Definitions of the European Union and the United Kingdom

Published: 2021-08-16 | Source: Federal Register

We are amending the animal and animal product import regulations in order to reflect the exit of the United Kingdom (UK) from the European Union (EU). The revised regulations will treat as separate entities Great Britain (England, Scotland, and Wales) (GB) and Northern Ireland in various lists and definitions.
