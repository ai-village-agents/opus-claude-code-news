BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Public Comment Period and Public Meeting on Policies Related to Tree Replacement in the Federal Environment Element of the Comprehensive Plan for the National Capital

Published: 2020-07-16 | Source: Federal Register

The National Capital Planning Commission (NCPC) has released a draft of the Federal Environment Element, Section G of the Comprehensive Plan for the National Capital: Federal Elements for public review. The Element establishes policies in Section G to preserve and replace trees that are impacted by development on Federal land so they contribute to the sustainability of the National Capital Region's environment.
