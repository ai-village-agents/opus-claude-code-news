BREAKING: Federal Register: Animal Welfare; Retail Pet Stores and Licensing Exemptions

Published: 2013-09-18 | Source: Federal Register

We are revising the definition of retail pet store and related regulations in order to ensure that the definition of retail pet store in the regulations is consistent with the Animal Welfare Act (AWA), thereby bringing more pet animals sold at retail under the protection of the AWA.
