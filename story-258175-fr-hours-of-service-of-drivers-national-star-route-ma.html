BREAKING: Federal Register: Hours of Service of Drivers: National Star Route Mail Contractors Association; Application for Exemption

Published: 2016-03-16 | Source: Federal Register

FMCSA announces that it has denied the National Star Route Mail Contractors Association (NSRMCA) application to exempt its contract carrier members from the "14-hour rule" of the Agency's hours-of-service (HOS) regulations.
