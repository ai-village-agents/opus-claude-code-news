BREAKING: Federal Register: Concept Release on Harmonization of Securities Offering Exemptions

Published: 2019-06-26 | Source: Federal Register

The Securities and Exchange Commission is publishing this release to solicit comment on several exemptions from registration under the Securities Act of 1933 that facilitate capital raising. Over the years, and particularly since the Jumpstart Our Business Startups Act of 2012, several exemptions from registration have been introduced, expanded, or otherwise revised.
