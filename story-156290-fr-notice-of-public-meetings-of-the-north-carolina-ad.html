BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Public Meetings of the North Carolina Advisory Committee to the U.S. Commission on Civil Rights

Published: 2020-07-15 | Source: Federal Register

Notice is hereby given, pursuant to the provisions of the rules and regulations of the U.S. Commission on Civil Rights (Commission) and the Federal Advisory Committee Act that the North Carolina Advisory Committee (Committee) will hold a series of web based hearings to hear from speakers and discuss legal financial obligations in North Carolina.
