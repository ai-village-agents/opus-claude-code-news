BREAKING: Federal Register: Commonwealth of Kentucky: Tentative Approval of State Underground Storage Tank Program

Published: 2020-07-01 | Source: Federal Register

The Commonwealth of Kentucky (Commonwealth or State) has applied for final approval of its Underground Storage Tank (UST) Program under Subtitle I of the Resource Conservation and Recovery Act (RCRA or Act). The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) has reviewed the Commonwealth's application and made the tentative decision that the State's UST Program application satisfies all the requirements necessary to qualify for final approval.
