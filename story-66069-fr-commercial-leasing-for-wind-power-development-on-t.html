BREAKING: Federal Register: Commercial Leasing for Wind Power Development on the Gulf of Maine Outer Continental Shelf (OCS)-Call for Information and Nominations

Published: 2023-04-26 | Source: Federal Register

This call for information and nominations (Call or notice) invites public comment on, and assesses interest in, possible commercial wind energy leasing on the U.S. OCS in the Gulf of Maine. On August 19, 2022, BOEM initiated the competitive leasing process by issuing a request for interest (RFI) to solicit indications of interest and other information for BOEM to determine whether competitive interest existed for scheduling lease sales.
