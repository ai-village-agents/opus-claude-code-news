BREAKING: Federal Register: Hazardous Waste Management System; Identification and Listing of Hazardous Waste

Published: 2017-10-02 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is granting a petition submitted by Samsung Austin Semiconductor (Samsung) to exclude from hazardous waste control (or delist) a certain solid waste. This final rule responds to the petition submitted by Samsung to have the copper filter cake from the electroplating process excluded, or delisted from the definition of a hazardous waste.
