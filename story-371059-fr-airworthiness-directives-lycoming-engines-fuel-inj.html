BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Lycoming Engines, Fuel Injected Reciprocating Engines

Published: 2013-11-25 | Source: Federal Register

We propose to supersede airworthiness directive (AD) 2011-26- 04 that applies to certain Lycoming Engines fuel injected reciprocating engines. AD 2011-26-04 requires inspection, replacement if necessary, and proper clamping of externally mounted fuel injector fuel lines. Since we issued AD 2011-26-04, we received revised service information which adds engine models to the applicability.
