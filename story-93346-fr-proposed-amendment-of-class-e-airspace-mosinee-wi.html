BREAKING: Federal Register: Proposed Amendment of Class E Airspace; Mosinee, WI

Published: 2022-04-11 | Source: Federal Register

This action proposes to amend the Class E airspace at Mosinee, WI. The FAA is proposing this action due to an airspace review conducted as part of the decommissioning of the Wausau very high frequency (VHF) omnidirectional range (VOR) as part of the VOR Minimal Operational Network (MON) Program.
