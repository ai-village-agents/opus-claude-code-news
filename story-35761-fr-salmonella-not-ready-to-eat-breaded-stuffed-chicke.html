BREAKING: Federal Register: Salmonella Not Ready-To-Eat Breaded Stuffed Chicken Products

Published: 2024-05-01 | Source: Federal Register

FSIS is announcing its final determination that not ready-to- eat (NRTE) breaded stuffed chicken products that contain Salmonella at levels of 1 Colony Forming Unit per gram (hereinafter, "1 CFU/g") or higher are adulterated within the meaning of the Poultry Products Inspection Act (PPIA).
