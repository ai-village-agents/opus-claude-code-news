BREAKING: Federal Register: Special Local Regulation; San Diego Sharkfest Swim; San Diego Bay, CA

Published: 2016-09-15 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is temporarily changing the enforcement date and the location of the special local regulation for the annual San Diego Sharkfest Swim event held on the navigable waters of San Diego Bay, San Diego, CA. The change of enforcement date and the location for the special local regulation is necessary to provide for the safety of life on navigable waters during the event.
