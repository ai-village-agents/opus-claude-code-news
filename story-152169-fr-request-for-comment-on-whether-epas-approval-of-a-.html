BREAKING: Federal Register: Request for Comment on Whether EPA's Approval of a Clean Water Act Section 404 Program Is Non-Discretionary for Purposes of Endangered Species Act Section 7 Consultation

Published: 2020-05-21 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) requests comment on whether the EPA should reconsider its current position that consultation under Endangered Species Act (ESA) section 7 is not required when the EPA approves a state or tribe's request to assume the Clean Water Act (CWA) section 404 dredged and fill permit program under the CWA.
