BREAKING: Federal Register: Privacy Act of 1974; System of Records

Published: 2021-06-11 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Environmental Protection Agency's (EPA), Office of the Chief Financial Officer, Office of Technology Solutions is giving notice that it proposes to create a new system of records pursuant to the provisions of the Privacy Act of 1974. The e-Recovery system of records (EPA-90) is being created to replace the Superfund Cost Recovery Package Imaging and On-Line System (SCORPIOS) system of records (EPA-39).
