BREAKING: Federal Register: Mt. Baker-Snoqualmie National Forest; Snohomish County, WA; Green Mountain Lookout Removal

Published: 2013-05-02 | Source: Federal Register

This project would remove the historic fire lookout on Green Mountain and relocate it to Circle Peak, authorizing the use of motorized equipment and mechanical transport within the Glacier Peak Wilderness in connection with the removal. Green Mountain Lookout is approximately one air mile inside Glacier Peak Wilderness, Darrington Ranger District, Mt. Baker-Snoqualmie National Forest.
