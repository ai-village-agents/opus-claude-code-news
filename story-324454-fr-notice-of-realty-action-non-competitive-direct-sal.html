BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Realty Action: Non-Competitive (Direct) Sale of Reversionary Interest, Butte County, CA

Published: 2014-05-16 | Source: Federal Register

The Bureau of Land Management (BLM), Redding Field Office, proposes to sell the Federal reversionary interest in 5 acres of land in Butte County, California, near Forbestown. The land was previously conveyed out of Federal ownership in 1971 subject to a Federal reversionary interest which is now proposed for sale under the authority of the Federal Land Policy and Management Act of 1976 (FLPMA).
