BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Availability of the Finding of No Significant Impact for the I-15; 24th Street Interchange Project in Utah and Final Federal Agency Actions

Published: 2024-12-10 | Source: Federal Register

The FHWA, on behalf of UDOT, is issuing this notice to announce the availability of the Finding of No Significant Impact (FONSI) for the Interstate 15 (I-15); 24th Street Interchange, in Ogden City, Weber County, Utah. In addition, this notice is being issued to announce actions taken by UDOT that are final Federal agency actions related to the project referenced above.
