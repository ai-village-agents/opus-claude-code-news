BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice on Penalty Inflation Adjustments for Civil Monetary Penalties for Violations of Provisions Pertaining to U.S. Investments in Certain National Security Technologies and Products in Countries of Concern

Published: 2025-01-29 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of the Treasury's Office of Investment Security is giving notice of its updated maximum civil monetary penalties in connection with the Outbound Investment Security Program. These amounts are effective through January 14, 2026. These figures represent an annual adjustment for inflation.
