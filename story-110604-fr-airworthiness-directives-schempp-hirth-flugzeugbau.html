BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Schempp-Hirth Flugzeugbau GmbH Gliders

Published: 2022-11-22 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA proposes to adopt a new airworthiness directive (AD) for all Schempp-Hirth Flugzeugbau GmbH Model Duo Discus and Duo Discus T gliders. This proposed AD results from mandatory continuing airworthiness information (MCAI) originated by an aviation authority of another country to identify and correct an unsafe condition on an aviation product.
