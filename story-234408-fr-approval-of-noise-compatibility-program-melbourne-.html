BREAKING: Federal Register: Approval of Noise Compatibility Program, Melbourne International Airport, Melbourne, FL

Published: 2017-05-17 | Source: Federal Register

The Federal Aviation Administration (FAA) announces its findings on the Noise Compatibility Program submitted by the Melbourne Airport Authority (MAA) under the Aviation Safety and Noise Abatement Act, hereinafter referred to as "the Act." These findings are made in recognition of the description of Federal and nonfederal responsibilities in Senate Report No. 96-52 (1980).
