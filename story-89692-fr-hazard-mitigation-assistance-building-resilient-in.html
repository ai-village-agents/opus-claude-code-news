BREAKING: Federal Register: Hazard Mitigation Assistance: Building Resilient Infrastructure and Communities

Published: 2022-02-25 | Source: Federal Register

The Federal Emergency Management Agency (FEMA) is issuing the Building Resilient Infrastructure and Communities Policy. This policy describes a new hazard mitigation grant program to assist States, territories, Tribes, and local governments with mitigating the impacts of natural hazards, including those created, aggravated, or amplified by climate change.
