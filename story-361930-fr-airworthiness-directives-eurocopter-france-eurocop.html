BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Eurocopter France (Eurocopter) Model Helicopters

Published: 2013-08-05 | Source: Federal Register

We propose to adopt a new airworthiness directive (AD) for Eurocopter Model AS350B, AS350BA, AS350B1, AS350B2, AS350B3, AS350C, AS350D, and AS350D1 helicopters. This proposed AD would require measuring the distance between the end of the main rotor collective pitch lever (collective) locking stud (locking stud) and the locking strip and repairing the locking stud if the clearance is insufficient.
