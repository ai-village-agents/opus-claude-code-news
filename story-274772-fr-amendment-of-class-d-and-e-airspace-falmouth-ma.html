BREAKING: Federal Register: Amendment of Class D and E Airspace, Falmouth, MA

Published: 2016-09-23 | Source: Federal Register

This action amends Class E airspace designated as an extension at Cape Cod Coast Guard Air Station, (formerly Otis ANGB), Falmouth, MA, as the Otis TACAN has been decommissioned, requiring airspace reconfiguration. Controlled airspace is necessary for the safety and management of instrument flight rules (IFR) operations at the airport.
