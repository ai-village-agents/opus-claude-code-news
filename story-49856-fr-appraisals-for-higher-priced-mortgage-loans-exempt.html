BREAKING: Federal Register: Appraisals for Higher-Priced Mortgage Loans Exemption Threshold

Published: 2024-10-15 | Source: Federal Register

The OCC, the Board, and the CFPB are finalizing amendments to the official interpretations for their regulations that implement section 129H of the Truth in Lending Act (TILA). Section 129H of TILA establishes special appraisal requirements for "higher-risk mortgages," termed "higher-priced mortgage loans" or "HPMLs" in the agencies' regulations.
