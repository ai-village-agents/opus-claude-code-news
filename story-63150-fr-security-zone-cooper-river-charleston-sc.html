BREAKING: Federal Register: Security Zone; Cooper River, Charleston, SC

Published: 2023-03-22 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is establishing a temporary security zone for certain waters of the Cooper River near Charleston and Mount Pleasant, South Carolina. This action is necessary to provide for the security and protection of life on these navigable waters near the Arthur Ravenel Jr. Bridge over the Cooper River during the Cooper River Bridge Run.
