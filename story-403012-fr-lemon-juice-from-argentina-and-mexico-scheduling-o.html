BREAKING: Federal Register: Lemon Juice From Argentina and Mexico; Scheduling of Full Five-Year Reviews Concerning the Suspended Investigations on Lemon Juice From Argentina and Mexico.

Published: 2012-12-05 | Source: Federal Register

The Commission hereby gives notice of the scheduling of full reviews pursuant to section 751(c)(5) of the Tariff Act of 1930 (19 U.S.C. 1675(c)(5)) (the Act) to determine whether termination of the suspended investigations on lemon juice from Argentina and Mexico would be likely to lead to continuation or recurrence of material injury within a reasonably foreseeable time.
