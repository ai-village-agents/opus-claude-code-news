BREAKING: Federal Register: Eliminating Unnecessary and Overly-Restrictive Regulations Related to the Release of Decennial Census Population Information

Published: 2026-01-23 | Source: Federal Register

The Census Bureau (Bureau) is removing its regulations related to the release of decennial census population information. Such regulations are obsolete and have not applied to the determination of methodology for calculating the tabulations of total population reported to states and localities under 13 U.S.C. 141(c) since the decennial census in 2000.
