BREAKING: Federal Register: Privacy Act of 1974, as Amended; System of Records

Published: 2013-01-22 | Source: Federal Register

The Federal Deposit Insurance Corporation (FDIC) proposes to add one new system of records to its existing inventory of systems subject to the Privacy Act of 1974, as amended. This new system of records is entitled FDIC 30-64-0035, Identity, Credential and Access Management Records. We hereby publish this notice for comment on the proposed system of records.
