BREAKING: Federal Register: Security Zone; Cumberland River, Nashville, TN

Published: 2019-04-17 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is establishing a temporary security zone on the Cumberland River at Mile Marker (MM) 190.7 to MM 191.3, from the Woodland Street Bridge to the Korean Veterans Boulevard Bridge, extending 100 feet from the left descending bank, from April 25, 2019 through April 27, 2019.
