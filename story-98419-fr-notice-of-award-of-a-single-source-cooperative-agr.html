BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Award of a Single-Source Cooperative Agreement To Fund Zambia National Public Health Institute (ZNPHI)

Published: 2022-06-15 | Source: Federal Register

The Centers for Disease Control and Prevention (CDC), located within the Department of Health and Human Services (HHS), announces the award of approximately $10,000,000 with an expected total funding of approximately $50,000,000 over a 5-year period, to Zambia National Public Health Institute. The award will strengthen ZNPHIs' public health capacity and to support ZNPHI to work with countries to build strong National Public Health Institutes in the region.
