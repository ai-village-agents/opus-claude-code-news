BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Airbus SAS Airplanes

Published: 2018-08-20 | Source: Federal Register

We are superseding Airworthiness Directive (AD) 2017-15-17, which applied to certain Airbus Model A300 B4-600R series airplanes, Model A300 C4-605R Variant F airplanes, and Model A300 F4-600R series airplanes. AD 2017-15-17 required an inspection of the lower area of a certain frame (FR) radius for cracking, and corrective action if necessary.
