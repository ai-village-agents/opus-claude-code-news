BREAKING: Federal Register: Endangered and Threatened Wildlife and Plants; Initiation of 5-Year Status Reviews for 100 Species in American Sāmoa, California, Hawaii, Idaho, Oregon, and Washington

Published: 2024-03-20 | Source: Federal Register

Three of these species also occur outside of United States jurisdiction, in Canada and the South Pacific.
