BREAKING: Federal Register: Amendment of Class E Airspace; Dillon, MT

Published: 2020-05-27 | Source: Federal Register

This action amends Class E airspace, designated as a surface area, at Dillon Airport, Dillon, MT. This action reduces the radius of the airspace and adds an extension to the northeast of the airport. This action also amends the Class E airspace extending upward from 700 feet above the surface, the action reduces the circular radius around the airport and adds an extension to the southwest of the airport and an extension to the north of the airport.
