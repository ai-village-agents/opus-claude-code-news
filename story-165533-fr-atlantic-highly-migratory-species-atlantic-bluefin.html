BREAKING: Federal Register: Atlantic Highly Migratory Species; Atlantic Bluefin Tuna Fisheries

Published: 2020-11-09 | Source: Federal Register

NMFS reopens the General category fishery for two days within the October through November 2020 General category subquota period. This action is intended to provide a reasonable opportunity to harvest the full annual U.S. bluefin tuna (BFT) quota without exceeding it, while maintaining an equitable distribution of fishing opportunities across time periods.
