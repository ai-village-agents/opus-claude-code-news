BREAKING: Federal Register: Revision of the Land Management Plan for the Lolo National Forest

Published: 2024-01-31 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Department of Agriculture (USDA) Forest Service is revising the land management plan for the Lolo National Forest and preparing an environmental impact statement (EIS). This notice announces the Forest Service's intent to prepare an EIS and initiates the scoping period on the proposed action.
