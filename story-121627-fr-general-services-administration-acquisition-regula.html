BREAKING: Federal Register: General Services Administration Acquisition Regulation; Remove Office of General Counsel Review for Final Payments

Published: 2021-04-19 | Source: Federal Register

GSA is proposing to amend the General Services Administration Acquisition Regulation (GSAR) to revise internal agency approval procedures for processing a final payment for construction and building service contracts where, after 60 days, a contracting officer is unable to obtain a release of claims from a contractor.
