BREAKING: Federal Register: Native American Language (NAL@ED) Program; Correction

Published: 2017-03-15 | Source: Federal Register

On March 9, 2017 the U.S. Department of Education published a 60-day comment period notice in the Federal Register (Page 13100, Column 2 and 3) seeking public comment for an information collection entitled, "Native American Language (NAL@ED) Program ". The date of the comment period included in the notice was for 60 days after publication of the notice instead of 30 days.
