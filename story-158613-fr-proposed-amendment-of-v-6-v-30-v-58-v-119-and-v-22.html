BREAKING: Federal Register: Proposed Amendment of V-6, V-30, V-58, V-119, and V-226 in the Vicinity of Clarion, PA

Published: 2020-08-13 | Source: Federal Register

This action proposes to amend VHF Omnidirectional Range (VOR) Federal airways V-6, V-30, V-58, V-119, and V-226 in the vicinity of Clarion, PA. The VOR Federal airway modifications are necessary due to the planned decommissioning of the VOR portion of the Clarion, PA, VOR/ Distance Measuring Equipment (VOR/DME) navigation aid (NAVAID) which provides navigation guidance for portions of the affected ATS routes.
