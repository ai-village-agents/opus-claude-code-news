BREAKING: Federal Register: Open Meeting of the Environmental Financial Advisory Board

Published: 2019-03-27 | Source: Federal Register

The EPA's Environmental Financial Advisory Board (EFAB) will hold a public meeting on April 17-18, 2019 in Washington, DC. The EFAB is an EPA advisory committee chartered under the Federal Advisory Committee Act to provide advice and recommendations to EPA on creative approaches to funding environmental programs, projects, and activities.
