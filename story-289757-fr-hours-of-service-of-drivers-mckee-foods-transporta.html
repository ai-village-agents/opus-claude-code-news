BREAKING: Federal Register: Hours of Service of Drivers: McKee Foods Transportation, LLC, Application for Exemption

Published: 2015-03-27 | Source: Federal Register

FMCSA announces its decision to grant an exemption to McKee Foods Transportation, LLC (MFT) from certain provisions of the Agency's hours-of-service (HOS) regulations. MFT proposed that its team drivers be granted an exemption from the HOS rules pertaining to use of a sleeper berth (SB).
