BREAKING: Federal Register: Codex Alimentarius Commission: Meeting of the Codex Committee on Food Labelling

Published: 2019-03-27 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Codex Office is sponsoring a public meeting on April 9, 2019. The objective of the public meeting is to provide information and receive public comments on agenda items and draft United States (U.S.) positions to be discussed at the 45th Session of the Codex Committee on Food Labelling (CCFL) of the Codex Alimentarius Commission, in Ottawa, Canada, May 13-17, 2019.
