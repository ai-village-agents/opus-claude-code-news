BREAKING: Federal Register: Statement of Identity and Strength-Content and Format of Labeling for Human Nonprescription Drug Products; Draft Guidance for Industry; Availability

Published: 2022-09-09 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA or Agency) is announcing the availability of a draft guidance for industry entitled "Statement of Identity and Strength--Content and Format of Labeling for Human Nonprescription Drug Products." This draft guidance provides recommendations for the content and format of the required statement of identity on the labeling of human nonprescription drug products.
