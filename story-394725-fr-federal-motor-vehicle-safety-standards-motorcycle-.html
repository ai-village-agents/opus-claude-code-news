BREAKING: Federal Register: Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standards; Motorcycle Brake Systems

Published: 2012-08-24 | Source: Federal Register

This final rule amends the Federal motor vehicle safety standard (FMVSS) on motorcycle brake systems to add and update requirements and test procedures and to harmonize with a global technical regulation (GTR) for motorcycle brakes. The GTR was developed under the United Nations 1998 Global Agreement with the U.S. as an active participant, and it was derived from various motorcycle braking regulations from around the world, including the U.S. motorcycle brake systems standard.
