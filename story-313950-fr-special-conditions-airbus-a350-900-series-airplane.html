BREAKING: Federal Register: Special Conditions: Airbus, A350-900 Series Airplane; Design Roll Maneuver

Published: 2014-01-14 | Source: Federal Register

These special conditions are issued for Airbus Model A350-900 series airplanes. These airplanes will have a novel or unusual design feature(s) associated with the airplane's response to the design roll maneuver. The applicable airworthiness regulations do not contain adequate or appropriate safety standards for this design feature.
