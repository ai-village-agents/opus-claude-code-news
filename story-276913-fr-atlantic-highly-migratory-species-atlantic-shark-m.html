BREAKING: Federal Register: Atlantic Highly Migratory Species; Atlantic Shark Management Measures; Proposed Amendment 5b

Published: 2016-10-18 | Source: Federal Register

NMFS is amending the 2006 Consolidated Atlantic Highly Migratory Species (HMS) Fishery Management Plan (FMP) based on the results of the 2016 stock assessment update for Atlantic dusky sharks. Based on this assessment, NMFS determined that the dusky shark stock remains overfished and is experiencing overfishing.
