BREAKING: Federal Register: New Pallet Preparation Standards for Periodicals

Published: 2012-09-24 | Source: Federal Register

The Postal Service will revise Mailing Standards of the United States Postal Service, Domestic Mail Manual (DMM[supreg]) 705.8.10.2 to align pallet preparation standards for Periodicals with those currently required for Periodicals prepared in sacks and similar containers.
