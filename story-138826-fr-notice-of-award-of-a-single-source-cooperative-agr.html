BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Award of a Single-Source Cooperative Agreement To Fund the United Nations Children's Fund (UNICEF)

Published: 2021-11-26 | Source: Federal Register

The Centers for Disease Control and Prevention (CDC), located within the Department of Health and Human Services (HHS) announces the award of $750,000 with an expected total funding of approximately $3,750,000 in funding to the United Nations Children's Fund (UNICEF) to plan and conduct assessments of micronutrient deficiencies burden and to design and implement systems to monitor and evaluate micronutrient and nutrition interventions in select countries, including Nepal, Ghana, Tanzania, Uganda...
