BREAKING: Federal Register: Methodology for Designation of Frontier and Remote Areas

Published: 2014-05-05 | Source: Federal Register

The Office of Rural Health Policy (ORHP) in the Health Resources and Services Administration (HRSA) published a 60-day public notice in the Federal Register on November 5, 2012 (Federal Register volume 77, number 214, 66471-66476) describing a methodology for designating U.S. frontier areas. The Frontier and Remote Area (FAR) Codes methodology was developed in a collaborative project between ORHP and the Economic Research Service (ERS) in the U.S. Department of Agriculture (USDA).
