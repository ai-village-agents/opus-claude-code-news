BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Southwest Idaho Resource Advisory Committee Meeting

Published: 2012-07-26 | Source: Federal Register

Pursuant to the authorities in the Federal Advisory Committee Act (Pub. L. 92-463) and under the Secure Rural Schools and Community Self-Determination Act of 2000, as amended, (Pub. L. 110-343), the Boise, Payette, Salmon-Challis, Sawtooth, and Wallowa-Whitman National Forests' Southwest Idaho Resource Advisory Committee will conduct a business meeting. The meeting is open to the public.
