BREAKING: Federal Register: Air Plan Approval; North Carolina: PSD Requirements for GHGs

Published: 2019-08-08 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is finalizing approval of two State Implementation Plan (SIP) revisions dated July 30, 2012, and January 12, 2018, submitted by the State of North Carolina through the North Carolina Department of Environmental Quality (NCDEQ). These SIP revisions are related to the State's Prevention of Significant Deterioration (PSD) permitting program requirements for greenhouse gases (GHGs).
