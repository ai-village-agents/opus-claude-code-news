BREAKING: Federal Register: Proposed Modification of the Atlanta Class B Airspace Area; GA

Published: 2012-02-03 | Source: Federal Register

This action proposes to modify the Atlanta, GA, Class B airspace area to ensure the containment of aircraft within Class B airspace, reduce controller workload and enhance safety in the Atlanta, GA, terminal area.
