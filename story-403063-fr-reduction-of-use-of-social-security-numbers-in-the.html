BREAKING: Federal Register: Reduction of Use of Social Security Numbers in the Department of Defense

Published: 2012-12-06 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Defense (DoD) published a proposed rule concerning the reduction of the use of social security numbers (SSN) in the Department on March 3, 2010 (75 FR 9548). The Department published the proposed rule because it intended to apply the SSN reduction policies and procedures to entities that contract with the Department.
