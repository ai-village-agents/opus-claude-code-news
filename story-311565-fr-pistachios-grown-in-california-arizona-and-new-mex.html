BREAKING: Federal Register: Pistachios Grown in California, Arizona, and New Mexico; Increased Assessment Rate

Published: 2015-12-14 | Source: Federal Register

This proposed rule would implement a recommendation from the Administrative Committee for Pistachios (Committee) to increase the assessment rate established for the 2015-16 and subsequent production years from $0.0005 to $0.0035 per pound of assessed weight pistachios handled under the marketing order for pistachios grown in California, Arizona, and New Mexico.
