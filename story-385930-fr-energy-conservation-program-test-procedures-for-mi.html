BREAKING: Federal Register: Energy Conservation Program: Test Procedures for Microwave Ovens

Published: 2012-05-16 | Source: Federal Register

On November 23, 2011, the U.S. Department of Energy (DOE) issued a supplemental notice of proposed rulemaking (SNOPR) to amend the test procedures for microwave ovens. That SNOPR proposed amendments to the DOE test procedure to incorporate provisions from the International Electrotechnical Commission (IEC) Standard 62301, "Household electrical appliances--Measurement of standby power," Edition 2.0 2011-01 (IEC Standard 62301 (Second Edition)).
