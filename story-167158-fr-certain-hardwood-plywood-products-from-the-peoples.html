BREAKING: Federal Register: Certain Hardwood Plywood Products From the People's Republic of China: Final Results of Antidumping Duty Administrative Review; 2017-2018

Published: 2020-12-01 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Commerce (Commerce) determines that certain producers and exporters of certain hardwood plywood products (hardwood plywood) from the People's Republic of China (China) made sales of the subject merchandise at prices below normal value (NV) during the period of review (POR), June 23, 2017 through December 31, 2018.
