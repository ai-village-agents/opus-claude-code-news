BREAKING: Federal Register: National Advisory Committee on Microbiological Criteria for Foods; Renewal

Published: 2014-12-30 | Source: Federal Register

In accordance with the Federal Advisory Committee Act, this notice is announcing the renewal of the charter of the National Advisory Committee on Microbiological Criteria for Foods (NACMCF). The Committee is being renewed in cooperation with the Department of Health and Human Services (DHHS).
