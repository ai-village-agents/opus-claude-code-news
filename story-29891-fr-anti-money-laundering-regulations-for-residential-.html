BREAKING: Federal Register: Anti-Money Laundering Regulations for Residential Real Estate Transfers

Published: 2024-02-16 | Source: Federal Register

FinCEN is issuing a proposed rule to require certain persons involved in real estate closings and settlements to submit reports and keep records on identified non-financed transfers of residential real property to specified legal entities and trusts on a nationwide basis. Transfers made directly to an individual would not be covered by this proposed rule.
