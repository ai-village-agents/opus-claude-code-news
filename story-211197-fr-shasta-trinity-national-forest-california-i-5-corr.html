BREAKING: Federal Register: Shasta-Trinity National Forest; California; I-5 Corridor Fuels Reduction Project

Published: 2018-07-09 | Source: Federal Register

The Shasta-Trinity National Forest is withdrawing its Notice of Intent issued on April 22, 2011, for preparation of an Environmental Impact Statement (EIS) for the I-5 Corridor Fuels Reduction Project. No significant issues were identified during this scoping period or any other opportunity to comment. Upon further evaluation, it also appears that there are no potential significant impacts to the human environment associated with the project.
