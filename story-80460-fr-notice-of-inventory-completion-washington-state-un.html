BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Inventory Completion: Washington State University, Museum of Anthropology, Pullman, WA

Published: 2023-10-25 | Source: Federal Register

In accordance with the Native American Graves Protection and Repatriation Act (NAGPRA), the Museum of Anthropology at Washington State University (WSU Museum of Anthropology) has completed an inventory of human remains and associated funerary objects and has determined that there is a cultural affiliation between the human remains and associated funerary objects and Indian Tribes or Native Hawaiian organizations in this notice.
