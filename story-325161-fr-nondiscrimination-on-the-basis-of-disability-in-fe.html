BREAKING: Federal Register: Nondiscrimination on the Basis of Disability in Federally Assisted Programs and Activities

Published: 2014-05-23 | Source: Federal Register

HUD is issuing this document to permit recipients of Federal financial assistance from HUD (HUD recipients) to use an alternative accessibility standard for purposes of complying with Section 504 of the Rehabilitation Act of 1973 (Section 504) and HUD's implementing regulation at 24 CFR part 8 (Section 504 regulation) until HUD formally revises its Section 504 regulation to adopt an updated accessibility standard.
