BREAKING: Federal Register: Guidance for Industry on Qualification Process for Drug Development Tools; Availability

Published: 2014-01-07 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA) is announcing the availability of a guidance for industry entitled "Qualification Process for Drug Development Tools." This guidance describes the qualification process for drug development tools intended for potential use, over time, in multiple drug development programs.
