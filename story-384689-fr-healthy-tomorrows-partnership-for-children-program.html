BREAKING: Federal Register: Healthy Tomorrows Partnership for Children Program

Published: 2012-05-02 | Source: Federal Register

The Health Resources and Services Administration (HRSA) will be issuing a non-competitive one-year extension with funds for the National Healthy Tomorrows Technical Assistance Resource Center at the American Academy of Pediatrics (AAP). Up to $176,855 will be awarded over a one-year extended project period.
