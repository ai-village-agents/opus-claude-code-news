BREAKING: Federal Register: Irish Potatoes Grown in Washington; Decreased Assessment Rate

Published: 2013-04-29 | Source: Federal Register

This rule decreases the assessment rate established for the State of Washington Potato Committee (Committee) for the 2013-2014 and subsequent fiscal periods from $0.003 to $0.0025 per hundredweight of potatoes handled. The Committee locally administers the marketing order which regulates the handling of Irish potatoes grown in Washington.
