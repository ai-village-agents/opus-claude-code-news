BREAKING: Federal Register: Department of Commerce Pre-Award Notification Requirements for Grants and Cooperative Agreements

Published: 2014-12-30 | Source: Federal Register

This notice replaces the U.S. Department of Commerce (DOC) Pre-Award Notification Requirements for Grants and Cooperative Agreements most recently published in the Federal Register on December 17, 2012 (77 FR 74634). This announcement constitutes notice of a recompilation of the Department of Commerce pre-award requirements for grants and cooperative agreements, including all amendments and revisions to date.
