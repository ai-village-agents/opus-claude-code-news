BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Inventory Completion: Office of the State Archaeologist, University of Iowa, Iowa City, IA

Published: 2016-06-08 | Source: Federal Register

The Office of the State Archaeologist Bioarchaeology Program, previously listed as the Office of the State Archaeologist Burials Program, has completed an inventory of human remains, in consultation with the appropriate Indian tribes or Native Hawaiian organizations, and has determined that there is a cultural affiliation between the human remains and present-day Indian tribes or Native Hawaiian organizations.
