BREAKING: Federal Register: Housing Opportunity Through Modernization Act of 2016: Initial Guidance

Published: 2016-10-24 | Source: Federal Register

On July 29, 2016, President Obama signed into law the Housing Opportunity Through Modernization Act of 2016 (HOTMA). This new statute provides updates and improvements to statutes that authorize and prescribe requirements for multiple HUD programs and the Department of Agriculture's single-family housing guaranteed loan program.
