BREAKING: Federal Register: Endangered and Threatened Species; Take of Anadromous Fish

Published: 2025-09-19 | Source: Federal Register

Notice is hereby given that NMFS has received a Hatchery and Genetics Management Plan (HGMP) for a hatchery program rearing and releasing summer-run chum salmon in the Dungeness River basin. The plan describes a hatchery program operated by Washington Department of Fish and Wildlife (WDFW) in collaboration with the Jamestown S'Klallam Tribe as co-managers.
