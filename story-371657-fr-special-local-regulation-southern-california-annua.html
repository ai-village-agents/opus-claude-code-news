BREAKING: Federal Register: Special Local Regulation; Southern California Annual Marine Events for the San Diego Captain of the Port Zone

Published: 2013-12-02 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard will enforce the Mission Bay Parade of Lights special local regulations during this year's parade held on December 14, 2013. This event occurs on Mission Bay in San Diego, CA. These special local regulations are necessary to provide for the safety of the participants, crew, spectators, sponsor vessels of the parade, and general users of the waterway.
