BREAKING: Federal Register: Change In Control: Driftwood LNG LLC

Published: 2024-09-10 | Source: Federal Register

The Office of Fossil Energy and Carbon Management (FECM) of the Department of Energy (DOE) gives notice of receipt of a Statement of Change in Control (Statement) filed by Driftwood LNG LLC (Driftwood LNG) on August 16, 2024. The Statement describes an expected change in Driftwood LNG's upstream ownership. The Statement was filed under the Natural Gas Act (NGA).
