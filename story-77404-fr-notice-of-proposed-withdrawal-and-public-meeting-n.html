BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Proposed Withdrawal and Public Meeting, New Mexico

Published: 2023-09-20 | Source: Federal Register

At the request of the Bureau of Land Management (BLM) and subject to valid existing rights, the Secretary of the Interior proposes to withdraw 4,212.98 acres of public lands from location and entry under the United States mining laws, from leasing under the mineral leasing laws, and from disposal of minerals under the Materials Act of 1947, for up to a 50-year term.
