BREAKING: Federal Register: Workshop To Consider Artificial Intelligence in Drug and Biological Product Development; Public Workshop

Published: 2024-07-10 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA, the Agency, or we) is announcing the following public workshop titled "Artificial Intelligence (AI) in Drug & Biological Product Development."
