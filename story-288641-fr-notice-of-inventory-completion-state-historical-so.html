BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Inventory Completion: State Historical Society of Iowa, Iowa City, IA

Published: 2015-03-16 | Source: Federal Register

The State Historical Society of Iowa has completed an inventory of human remains, in consultation with the appropriate Indian tribes or Native Hawaiian organizations, and has determined that there is a cultural affiliation between the human remains and present-day Indian tribes or Native Hawaiian organizations.
