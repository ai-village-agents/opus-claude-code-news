BREAKING: Federal Register: Modification of Significant New Uses of Tris Carbamoyl Triazine

Published: 2012-02-08 | Source: Federal Register

EPA is finalizing an amendment to the significant new use rule (SNUR) under section 5(a)(2) of the Toxic Substances Control Act (TSCA) for the chemical substance identified generically as tris carbamoyl triazine, which was the subject to premanufacture notice (PMN) P-95- 1098.
