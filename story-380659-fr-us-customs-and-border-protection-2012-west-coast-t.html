BREAKING: Federal Register: U.S. Customs and Border Protection 2012 West Coast Trade Symposium: “Harmonizing Trade for a Stronger Economy”

Published: 2012-03-19 | Source: Federal Register

This year, U.S. Customs and Border Protection (CBP) will be holding two trade symposia. One trade symposium will be held on the West Coast in May and the other will be on the East Coast later in the year. This document announces that CBP will convene the 2012 West Coast Trade Symposium to discuss issues relating to the agency's role in international trade initiatives and programs.
