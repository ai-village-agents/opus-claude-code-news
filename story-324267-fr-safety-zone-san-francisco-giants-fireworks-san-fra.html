BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone; San Francisco Giants Fireworks, San Francisco Bay, San Francisco, CA

Published: 2014-05-14 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard will enforce the safety zone for the San Francisco Giants Fireworks display in the Captain of the Port, San Francisco area of responsibility during the dates and times noted below. This action is necessary to protect life and property of the maritime public from the hazards associated with the fireworks display.
