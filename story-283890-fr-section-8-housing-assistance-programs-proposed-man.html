BREAKING: Federal Register: Section 8 Housing Assistance Programs Proposed Management and Occupancy Review Schedule

Published: 2015-01-14 | Source: Federal Register

Through this notice, FHA solicits comment from interested members of the public on HUD's proposed Management and Occupancy Review (MOR) schedule for the seven project-based Section 8 programs administered by the Office of Multifamily Housing. This proposed schedule will reduce the frequency of unnecessary MORs, thereby minimizing interruptions in property operations created by onsite reviews, preserving staff time, and reducing costs.
