BREAKING: Federal Register: Pilot Loading of Aeronautical Database Updates

Published: 2012-11-29 | Source: Federal Register

This action amends the maintenance regulations by removing from the preventive maintenance category the task of updating databases used in self-contained, front-panel or pedestal-mounted navigation equipment. Further, we are adding text to the maintenance regulations that describes which equipment and, under which conditions, may have aeronautical databases updated by pilots as a non-maintenance function.
