BREAKING: Federal Register: Response to Clean Air Act Section 126(b) Petitions From Delaware and Maryland

Published: 2018-10-05 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is denying four petitions submitted by the state of Delaware and one petition submitted by the state of Maryland under Clean Air Act (CAA or Act) section 126(b). The petitions were submitted between July and November 2016.
