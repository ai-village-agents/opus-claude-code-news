BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Funding Availability; Additional Payment to Approved Insurance Providers

Published: 2023-05-02 | Source: Federal Register

The Risk Management Agency (RMA), on behalf of the Federal Crop Insurance Corporation (FCIC), announces the availability of funding under the Additional Payment (ADD PAY) Program. The ADD PAY Program is a one-time additional payment to Approved Insurance Providers (AIP) administering eligible crop insurance contracts for 2021 reinsurance year specialty crops.
