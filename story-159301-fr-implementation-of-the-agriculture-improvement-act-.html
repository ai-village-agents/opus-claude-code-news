BREAKING: Federal Register: Implementation of the Agriculture Improvement Act of 2018

Published: 2020-08-21 | Source: Federal Register

The purpose of this interim final rule is to codify in the Drug Enforcement Administration (DEA) regulations the statutory amendments to the Controlled Substances Act (CSA) made by the Agriculture Improvement Act of 2018 (AIA), regarding the scope of regulatory controls over marihuana, tetrahydrocannabinols, and other marihuana-related constituents.
