BREAKING: Federal Register: Hazardous Materials: Compatibility With the Regulations of the International Atomic Energy Agency

Published: 2022-09-12 | Source: Federal Register

PHMSA, in coordination with the Nuclear Regulatory Commission, proposes to amend the Hazardous Materials Regulations to maintain alignment with international regulations and standards governing the transportation of Class 7 radioactive materials. Specifically, PHMSA proposes to adopt changes contained in the International Atomic Energy Agency standards.
