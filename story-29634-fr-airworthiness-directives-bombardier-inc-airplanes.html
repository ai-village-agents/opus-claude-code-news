BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Bombardier, Inc., Airplanes

Published: 2024-02-14 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA proposes to adopt a new airworthiness directive (AD) for certain Bombardier, Inc., Model BD-700-2A12 airplanes. This proposed AD was prompted by a report indicating that the fan in a transformer rectifier unit (TRU) can become inoperative in a manner that is not detectable by the fan monitoring circuit.
