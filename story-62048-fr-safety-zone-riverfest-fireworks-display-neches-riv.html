BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone; Riverfest Fireworks Display, Neches River, Port Neches, Texas

Published: 2023-03-08 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard will enforce a safety zone for the Riverfest fireworks display on the Neches River in Port Neches, TX from 8:30 p.m. through 10 p.m. on May 6, 2023 to provide for the safety of life on navigable waterways during this event. Our regulation for fireworks displays and other events within the Eighth Coast Guard District identifies the regulated area for this event in Port Neches, TX.
