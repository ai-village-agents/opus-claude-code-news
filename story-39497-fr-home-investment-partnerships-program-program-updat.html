BREAKING: Federal Register: HOME Investment Partnerships Program: Program Updates and Streamlining; Public Listening Sessions

Published: 2024-06-14 | Source: Federal Register

HUD will host three, two-part virtual public listening sessions to allow interested persons an opportunity to learn about and provide comments on the "HOME Investment Partnerships Program: Program Updates and Streamlining" proposed rule. The Part 1 listening sessions will address the topics of community housing development organizations, maximum subsidy amounts, green building incentives, homebuyer housing, and community land trusts.
