BREAKING: Federal Register: Ban of Crib Bumpers

Published: 2022-07-26 | Source: Federal Register

On May 16, 2022, the President signed into law the Safe Sleep for Babies Act of 2021, requiring that crib bumpers, "regardless of the date of manufacture, shall be considered a banned hazardous product" under the Consumer Product Safety Act (CPSA).
