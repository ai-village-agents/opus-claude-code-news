BREAKING: Federal Register: Positioning, Navigation, and Timing (PNT) Service for National Critical Infrastructure Resiliency

Published: 2016-11-30 | Source: Federal Register

This RFI provides an outline for the potential use by the Federal Government of one or more Positioning, Navigation, and Timing (PNT) technologies to back up signals from the Global Positioning System (GPS) and to ensure resiliency of PNT for U.S. Critical Infrastructure (CI) operations.
