BREAKING: Federal Register: Toxic Substance Control Act Chemical Substance Import Certification Process Revisions

Published: 2016-12-27 | Source: Federal Register

This document amends the U.S. Customs and Border Protection (CBP) regulations regarding the requirement to file a Toxic Substances Control Act (TSCA) certification when importing into the customs territory of the United States chemicals in bulk form or as part of mixtures and articles containing a chemical or mixture.
