BREAKING: Federal Register: VA Veteran-Owned Small Business (VOSB) Verification Guidelines

Published: 2013-05-13 | Source: Federal Register

The Office of Small and Disadvantaged Business Utilization (OSDBU) is currently reviewing its regulations governing the Department of Veterans Affairs (VA) Veteran-Owned Small Business (VOSB) Verification Program. OSDBU intends to improve the regulations to provide greater clarity, to streamline the program, and to encourage more VOSBs to apply for verification.
