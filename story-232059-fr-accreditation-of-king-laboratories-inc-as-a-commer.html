BREAKING: Federal Register: Accreditation of King Laboratories, Inc., as a Commercial Laboratory

Published: 2017-04-14 | Source: Federal Register

Notice is hereby given, pursuant to CBP regulations, that King Laboratories, Inc., has been accredited to test petroleum and certain petroleum products for customs purposes as of February 15, 2017.

Agency: Homeland Security Department, U.S. Customs and Border Protection
