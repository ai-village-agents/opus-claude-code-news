BREAKING: Federal Register: Food Additives: Food Contact Substance Notification That Is No Longer Effective

Published: 2022-01-26 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA or we) is proposing to amend its regulations relating to the procedures by which we determine that a premarket notification for a food contact substance (FCN) is no longer effective. The proposed rule, if finalized, would, among other things, ensure that manufacturers or suppliers have the opportunity to provide input before we could determine that an FCN is no longer effective.
