BREAKING: Federal Register: Duke Energy Carolinas, LLC; McGuire Generating Station, Units 1 and 2

Published: 2015-08-12 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Nuclear Regulatory Commission (NRC) has granted the request of Duke Energy Carolinas, LLC to withdraw its application dated February 22, 2012, as supplemented by letters dated November 13, 2012 and February 4, 2013, for a proposed amendment to Renewed Facility Operating License Nos. NPF-9 and NPF-17, for the McGuire Nuclear Station, Units 1 and 2. The proposed amendment would have revised the McGuire Technical Specification 3.7.7, "Nuclear Service Water System (NSWS)."
