BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Public Meeting of the South Carolina Advisory Committee to the U.S. Commission on Civil Rights

Published: 2022-09-29 | Source: Federal Register

Notice is hereby given, pursuant to the provisions of the rules and regulations of the U.S. Commission on Civil Rights (Commission) and the Federal Advisory Committee Act, that the South Carolina Advisory Committee (Committee) to the U.S. Commission on Civil Rights will hold a business meeting on Friday, October 14, 2022, at 12:00 p.m. (ET). The purpose of the meeting is to review, edit, and vote on the committee's draft report on civil asset forfeiture.
