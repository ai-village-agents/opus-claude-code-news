BREAKING: Federal Register: Boston Harbor Islands National Recreation Area Advisory Council

Published: 2015-08-21 | Source: Federal Register

This notice announces the annual meeting of the Boston Harbor Islands National Recreation Area Advisory Council (Council). The agenda includes results of the Council survey, planning for the 20th anniversary of the Boston Harbor Islands National Recreation Area, the National Park Service (NPS) Centennial, and the Boston Light Tricentennial in 2016, and membership of and planning for the next steps for the Council.
