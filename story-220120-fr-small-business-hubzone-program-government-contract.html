BREAKING: Federal Register: Small Business HUBZone Program; Government Contracting Programs

Published: 2018-10-31 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Small Business Administration (SBA or Agency) proposes to amend its regulations for the Historically Underutilized Business Zone (HUBZone) Program to reduce the regulatory burdens imposed on HUBZone small business concerns and government agencies, implement new statutory provisions, and eliminate ambiguities in the regulations.
