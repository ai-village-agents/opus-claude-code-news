BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Embraer S.A. Airplanes

Published: 2024-03-26 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA is adopting a new airworthiness directive (AD) for all Embraer S.A. Model EMB-545 and EMB-550 airplanes. This AD was prompted by a report of a hard landing event with substantial damage to the airplane, in which the angle of attack (AOA) limiter was engaged during the final approach phase in unstable air conditions and remained engaged until the airplane touched down on the runway.
