BREAKING: Federal Register: Determination of Pest-Free Areas in Australia

Published: 2014-08-29 | Source: Federal Register

We are advising the public that we are recognizing the Australian States of New South Wales, Northern Territory, Queensland, South Australia, Tasmania, and Victoria as free of Mediterranean fruit fly (Medfly) and the State of Western Australia as free of Queensland fruit fly.
