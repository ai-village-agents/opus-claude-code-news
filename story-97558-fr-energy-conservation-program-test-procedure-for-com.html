BREAKING: Federal Register: Energy Conservation Program: Test Procedure for Compressors; Extension of Comment Period

Published: 2022-06-06 | Source: Federal Register

On May 6, 2022, the U.S. Department of Energy ("DOE") published a request for information ("RFI") regarding test procedures for compressors. The RFI provided an opportunity for submitting written comments, data, and information by June 6, 2022. DOE received a request from the Compressed Air and Gas Institute on May 23, 2022 requesting an extension of the public comment period for an additional 60 days.
