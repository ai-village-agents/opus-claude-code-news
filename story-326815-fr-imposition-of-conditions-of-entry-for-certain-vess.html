BREAKING: Federal Register: Imposition of Conditions of Entry for Certain Vessels Arriving to the United States From Nigeria

Published: 2014-06-12 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard announces that it will impose conditions of entry on vessels arriving from the Federal Republic of Nigeria with the exception of vessels arriving from certain ports.
