BREAKING: Federal Register: Public Safety and Homeland Security Bureau Seeks Comment on Wireless Service Providers' Safety Measures for Their Customers During Disasters in Connection With the Consolidated Appropriations Act of 2021

Published: 2021-04-05 | Source: Federal Register

In this document, the Public Safety and Homeland Security Bureau (Bureau) seeks comment on recent efforts by mobile wireless service providers to improve network resiliency in order to inform a report to Congress.
