BREAKING: Federal Register: Uniform National Discharge Standards for Vessels of the Armed Forces-Phase II Batch One

Published: 2017-01-11 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) and the U.S. Department of Defense (DoD) are promulgating discharge performance standards for 11 discharges incidental to the normal operation of a vessel of the Armed Forces into the navigable waters of the United States, the territorial seas, and the contiguous zone.
