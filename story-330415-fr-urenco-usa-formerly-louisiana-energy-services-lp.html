BREAKING: Federal Register: URENCO USA (formerly Louisiana Energy Services, L.P.)

Published: 2014-07-24 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Nuclear Regulatory Commission (NRC) has received an application from URENCO USA for amendment of License No. SNM-2010, which authorizes it to operate a uranium enrichment facility. The license authorizes the production of enriched uranium up to a maximum of 5.0 percent U-235, using a gas centrifuge process.
