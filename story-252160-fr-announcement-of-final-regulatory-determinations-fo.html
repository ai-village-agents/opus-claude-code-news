BREAKING: Federal Register: Announcement of Final Regulatory Determinations for Contaminants on the Third Drinking Water Contaminant Candidate List

Published: 2016-01-04 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is announcing final regulatory determinations not to issue national primary drinking water regulations for four of the 116 contaminants listed on the Third Contaminant Candidate List. The Safe Drinking Water Act, as amended in 1996, requires the EPA to make regulatory determinations every five years on at least five unregulated contaminants.
