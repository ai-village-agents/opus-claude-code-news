BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Airbus Airplanes

Published: 2017-11-09 | Source: Federal Register

We are revising an earlier proposal for certain Airbus Model A318-112, A319-115, A320-214, A320-232, and A321-111 airplanes. This action revises the notice of proposed rulemaking (NPRM) by adding airplanes to the applicability and removing others that were inadvertently included due to typographical errors.
