BREAKING: Federal Register: Public Land Order No. 7941; Partial Withdrawal Revocation, Power Site Reserve No. 244; Colorado

Published: 2024-05-10 | Source: Federal Register

This order partially revokes a withdrawal created by Executive Order dated February 17, 1912, which established Power Site Reserve (PSR) No. 244, insofar as it affects 7.55 acres and releases these acres from applicability of section 24 of the Federal Power Act.
