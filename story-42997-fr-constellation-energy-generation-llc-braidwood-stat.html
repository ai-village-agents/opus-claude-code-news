BREAKING: Federal Register: Constellation Energy Generation, LLC; Braidwood Station, Units 1 and 2; Environmental Assessment and Finding of No Significant Impact

Published: 2024-07-31 | Source: Federal Register

NPF-72 and NPF-77, which authorize Constellation Energy Generation, LLC, (Constellation, the licensee) to operate Braidwood Station (Braidwood), Units 1 and 2.
