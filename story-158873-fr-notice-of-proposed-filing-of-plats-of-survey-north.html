BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Proposed Filing of Plats of Survey; North Dakota

Published: 2020-08-18 | Source: Federal Register

The plats of survey for the lands described in this notice are scheduled to be officially filed 30 calendar days after the date of this publication in the BLM Montana State Office, Billings, Montana. The surveys, which were executed at the request of the Bureau of Land Management, Montana State Office, Billings, Montana, are necessary for the management of these lands.
