BREAKING: Federal Register: Air Plan Approval; Kentucky: Revisions to Jefferson County VOC Definition

Published: 2020-07-06 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is proposing to approve a SIP revision to the Jefferson County portion of the Kentucky State Implementation Plan (SIP), submitted by the Commonwealth of Kentucky (Commonwealth), through the Energy and Environment Cabinet (Cabinet) on September 5, 2019. The revision was submitted by the Cabinet on behalf of the Louisville Metro Air Pollution Control District (LMAPCD) and makes changes to the definition of "volatile organic compound" (VOC).
