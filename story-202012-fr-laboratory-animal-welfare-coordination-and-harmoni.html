BREAKING: Federal Register: Laboratory Animal Welfare: Coordination and Harmonization of Regulations and Policies

Published: 2018-03-14 | Source: Federal Register

The National Institutes of Health (NIH) is seeking information to improve the coordination of regulations and policies with respect to research with laboratory animals as required by the 21st Century Cures Act, Section 2034(d).
