BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Cessna Aircraft Company Airplanes

Published: 2013-04-25 | Source: Federal Register

We are adopting a new airworthiness directive (AD) for certain Cessna Aircraft Company (Cessna) Model 525 airplanes equipped with certain part number (P/N) air conditioning (A/C) compressor motors. This AD was prompted by reports of smoke and/or fire in the tailcone caused by brushes wearing beyond their limits on the A/C motor.
