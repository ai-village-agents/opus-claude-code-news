BREAKING: Federal Register: Schedules of Controlled Substances: Extension of Temporary Placement of THJ-2201, AB-PINACA and AB-CHMINACA in Schedule I of the Controlled Substances Act

Published: 2017-01-27 | Source: Federal Register
