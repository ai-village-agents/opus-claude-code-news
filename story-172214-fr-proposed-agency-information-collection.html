BREAKING: Federal Register: Proposed Agency Information Collection

Published: 2019-02-20 | Source: Federal Register

Bonneville Power Administration (BPA) has submitted to the Office of Management and Budget (OMB) for clearance, a proposal for collection of information under the provisions of the Paperwork Reduction Act of 1995. The proposed collection will allow BPA to exercise management and oversight over the personal security of the public and physical security of its facilities. Incidents of property damage and loss are also reported and managed using these collections.
