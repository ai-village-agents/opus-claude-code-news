BREAKING: Federal Register: Shipping Safety Fairways Along the Atlantic Coast

Published: 2024-01-19 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is proposing to establish shipping safety fairways ("fairways") along the Atlantic Coast of the United States, identified in the Atlantic Coast Port Access Route Study. Fairways would preserve the safe and reliable transit of vessels along well- established traffic patterns and routes.
