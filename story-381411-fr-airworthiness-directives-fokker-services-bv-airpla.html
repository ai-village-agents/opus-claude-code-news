BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Fokker Services B.V. Airplanes

Published: 2012-03-27 | Source: Federal Register

We propose to adopt a new airworthiness directive (AD) for all Fokker Services B.V. Model F.28 Mark 0070 and 0100 airplanes. This proposed AD was prompted by a design review which revealed the absence of electrical insulation material between a wing or integral center wing tank (ICWT) fuel quantity indication system (FQIS) probe and the bottom of the tank structure.
