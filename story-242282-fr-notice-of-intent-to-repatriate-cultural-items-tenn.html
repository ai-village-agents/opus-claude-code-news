BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Intent To Repatriate Cultural Items: Tennessee Valley Authority, Knoxville, TN

Published: 2017-08-28 | Source: Federal Register

The Tennessee Valley Authority (TVA), in consultation with the appropriate Indian Tribes or Native Hawaiian organizations, has determined that the cultural items listed in this notice meet the definition of unassociated funerary objects. Further, TVA has determined that a cultural affiliation between the unassociated funerary objects and present-day federally recognized Indian Tribes can be reasonably traced.
