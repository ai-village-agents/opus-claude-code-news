BREAKING: Federal Register: Regulations Governing Practice Before the Internal Revenue Service

Published: 2012-09-17 | Source: Federal Register

This document proposes modifications of the regulations governing practice before the Internal Revenue Service (IRS). These proposed regulations affect individuals who practice before the IRS. These proposed regulations modify the standards governing written advice and update certain provisions as appropriate.
