BREAKING: Federal Register: Current Good Manufacturing Practice and Hazard Analysis and Risk-Based Preventive Controls for Human Food

Published: 2013-01-16 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA) is proposing to amend its regulation for Current Good Manufacturing Practice In Manufacturing, Packing, or Holding Human Food (CGMPs) to modernize it and to add requirements for domestic and foreign facilities that are required to register under the Federal Food, Drug, and Cosmetic Act (the FD&C Act) to establish and implement hazard analysis and risk- based preventive controls for human food.
