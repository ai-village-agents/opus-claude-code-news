BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Federal Review of the American Samoa Protection and Advocacy System (P&A)

Published: 2022-06-24 | Source: Federal Register

Representatives of the Administration on Disabilities (AoD), Administration for Community Living (ACL), will be conducting a federal review of the American Samoa Protection and Advocacy System (P&A) on September 19-23, 2022. AoD is soliciting comments from interested parties on your experiences with the program, and strategies employed by P&A in meeting the needs of individuals with developmental disabilities and their families in American Samoa.
