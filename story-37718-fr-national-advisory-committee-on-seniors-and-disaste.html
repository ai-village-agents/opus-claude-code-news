BREAKING: Federal Register: National Advisory Committee on Seniors and Disasters Meeting Notice; Cancellation

Published: 2024-05-23 | Source: Federal Register

The Administration for Strategic Preparedness and Response (ASPR) published a notice in the Federal Register concerning a public meeting of the National Advisory Committee on Seniors and Disasters (NACSD). The public meeting, scheduled for Monday, May 20, 2024, at 2:30 p.m. ET, has been cancelled and will be rescheduled for a later date. The notice is in the Federal Register on Thursday, May 2, 2024, in FR Document Number 2024-09584 on pages 35843-35844 (2 pages).
