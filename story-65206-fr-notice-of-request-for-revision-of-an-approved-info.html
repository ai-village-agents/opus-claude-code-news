BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Request for Revision of an Approved Information Collection (Public Health Information System)

In accordance with the Paperwork Reduction Act of 1995 and Office of Management and Budget (OMB) regulations, the Food Safety and Inspection Service (FSIS) is announcing its intention to revise the approved information collection regarding its Public Health Information System (PHIS).
