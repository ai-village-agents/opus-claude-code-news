BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; The Boeing Company Airplanes

Published: 2021-06-24 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA is withdrawing a notice of proposed rulemaking (NPRM) that proposed to adopt a new airworthiness directive (AD). That NPRM would have applied to certain The Boeing Company Model 737-700, -800, and -900ER series airplanes. The NPRM was prompted by a report of unshimmed gaps at a certain frame inner chord.
