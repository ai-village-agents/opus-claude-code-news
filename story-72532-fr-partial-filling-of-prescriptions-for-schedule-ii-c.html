BREAKING: Federal Register: Partial Filling of Prescriptions for Schedule II Controlled Substances

Published: 2023-07-21 | Source: Federal Register

On July 22, 2016, the Comprehensive Addiction and Recovery Act of 2016 became law. One provision of the Comprehensive Addiction and Recovery Act of 2016 amended the Controlled Substances Act to allow for the partial filling of prescriptions for schedule II controlled substances under certain conditions.
