BREAKING: Federal Register: Proposed Designation of Databases for Treasury's Working System Under the Do Not Pay Initiative

Published: 2017-09-13 | Source: Federal Register

Section 5(b)(1)(B) of the Improper Payments Elimination and Recovery Improvement Act of 2012 (IPERIA) provides that the Director of the Office of Management and Budget (OMB), in consultation with agencies, may designate additional databases for inclusion under the Do Not Pay (DNP) Initiative. IPERIA further requires OMB to provide public notice and an opportunity for comment prior to designating additional databases.
