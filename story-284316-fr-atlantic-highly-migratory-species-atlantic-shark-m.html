BREAKING: Federal Register: Atlantic Highly Migratory Species; Atlantic Shark Management Measures; 2015 Research Fishery

Published: 2015-01-21 | Source: Federal Register

NMFS announces its second request for applications for the 2015 shark research fishery from commercial shark fishermen with directed or incidental shark limited access permits. In this second request, NMFS is specifically requesting applications only from commercial shark fishermen who are fishing or plan to fish in the Gulf of Mexico fishing region.
