BREAKING: Federal Register: Connecting Minority Communities Pilot Program

Published: 2021-06-15 | Source: Federal Register

The Consolidated Appropriations Act of 2021 (the ``Act'') appropriated $285 million to the National Telecommunications and Information Administration (NTIA) to establish the Connecting Minority Communities (CMC) Pilot Program.
