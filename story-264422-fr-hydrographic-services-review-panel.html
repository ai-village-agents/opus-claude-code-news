BREAKING: Federal Register: Hydrographic Services Review Panel

Published: 2016-05-25 | Source: Federal Register

In accordance with the Hydrographic Service Improvements Act Amendments of 2002, Public Law 107-372, the Administrator of the National Oceanic and Atmospheric Administration (NOAA) is required to solicit nominations for membership at least once a year for the Hydrographic Services Review Panel (HSRP).
