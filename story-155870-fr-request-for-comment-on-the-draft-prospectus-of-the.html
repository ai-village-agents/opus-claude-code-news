BREAKING: Federal Register: Request for Comment on the Draft Prospectus of the Fifth National Climate Assessment

Published: 2020-07-10 | Source: Federal Register

With this notice, the U.S. Global Change Research Program (USGCRP) seeks public comment on the proposed themes and framework of the Fifth National Climate Assessment (NCA5) as indicated by the draft prospectus presented here. Based on input received from this notice, USGCRP will develop an annotated outline, which will be released for public comment at a later date.
