BREAKING: Federal Register: Approval and Promulgation of Air Quality Implementation Plans;

Published: 2022-04-06 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is proposing to approve a state implementation plan (SIP) revision, made in two separate submittals, by the State of Delaware. This revision removes requirements for gasoline vapor recovery systems installed on gasoline dispensers, the purpose of which are to capture emissions from vehicle refueling operations, otherwise known as Stage II vapor recovery.
