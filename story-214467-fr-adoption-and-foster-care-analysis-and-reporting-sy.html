BREAKING: Federal Register: Adoption and Foster Care Analysis and Reporting System

Published: 2018-08-21 | Source: Federal Register

The Children's Bureau will delay the compliance and effective dates in the Adoption and Foster Care Analysis and Reporting System (AFCARS) 2016 final rule for title IV-E agencies to comply with agency rules for an additional one fiscal year.
