BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; General Electric Company Turbofan Engines

Published: 2016-11-03 | Source: Federal Register

We propose to supersede airworthiness directive (AD) 2015-15- 03, which applies to all General Electric Company (GE) GEnx turbofan engine models. AD 2015-15-03 precludes the use of certain full authority digital engine control (FADEC) software on GEnx turbofan engines. Since we issued AD 2015-15-03, GE implemented final design changes that remove the unsafe condition.
