BREAKING: Federal Register: Inbound Competitive Multi-Service Agreements With Foreign Postal Operators

Published: 2023-06-23 | Source: Federal Register

The Commission is recognizing a recent Postal Service filing of a change in certain modifications to the Competitive Multi-Product Agreements with Foreign Postal Operators 1 products to be effective July 1, 2023. This notice informs the public of the filing, invites public comment, and takes other administrative steps.
