BREAKING: Federal Register: Department of Health and Human Services Acquisition Regulation-Electronic Submission and Processing of Payment Requests

Published: 2022-02-02 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Health and Human Services (HHS or the Department) finalizes its proposed regulation amending the Department's Federal Acquisition Regulation Supplement, the HHS Acquisition Regulation (HHSAR), to support the HHS Electronic Invoicing Implementation Project and HHS's transition to the Department of the Treasury's Invoice Processing Platform.
