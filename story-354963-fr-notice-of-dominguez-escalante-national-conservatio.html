BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Dominguez-Escalante National Conservation Area Advisory Council Meeting Cancellation and Reschedule

Published: 2013-05-15 | Source: Federal Register

In accordance with the Federal Land Policy and Management Act (FLPMA) and the Federal Advisory Committee Act of 1972 (FACA), notice is hereby given that the Dominguez-Escalante National Conservation Area Advisory Council meeting scheduled for May 29, 2013, at the Bill Heddles Recreation Center, 530 Gunnison River Drive, Delta, CO, has been cancelled and rescheduled to take place on June 26, 2013, at the Bill Heddles Recreation Center, 530 Gunnison River Drive, Delta, CO.
