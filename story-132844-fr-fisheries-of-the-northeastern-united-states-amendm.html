BREAKING: Federal Register: Fisheries of the Northeastern United States; Amendment 7 to the Atlantic Bluefish Fishery Management Plan

Published: 2021-09-13 | Source: Federal Register

NMFS proposes to approve and implement measures included in Amendment 7 to the Atlantic Bluefish Fishery Management Plan, as submitted by the Mid-Atlantic Fishery Management Council.
