BREAKING: Federal Register: Modernization of Qualification Requirements for Brewer's Notices

Published: 2022-06-08 | Source: Federal Register

In this notice of proposed rulemaking, the Alcohol and Tobacco Tax and Trade Bureau (TTB) proposes deregulatory amendments to its regulations to modernize and streamline the qualification requirements for Brewer's Notices. The proposed amendments also relax requirements associated with reporting certain changes to brewery businesses and other notification requirements.
