BREAKING: Federal Register: Security Zone; Atlantic Ocean, Kiawah Island, SC

Published: 2022-08-15 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is establishing a temporary security zone for certain navigable waters of the Atlantic Ocean in the vicinity of Kiawah Island, South Carolina to prevent waterside threats and incidents for persons under the protection of the United States Secret Service. The action is necessary to protect an official party, public, and surrounding waterways from terrorist acts, sabotage or other subversive acts, accidents or other events of a similar nature.
