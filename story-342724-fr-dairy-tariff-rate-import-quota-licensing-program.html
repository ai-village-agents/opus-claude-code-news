BREAKING: Federal Register: Dairy Tariff-Rate Import Quota Licensing Program

Published: 2014-12-23 | Source: Federal Register

This proposed rule would amend the regulation that provides for the issuance of licenses to import certain dairy articles under tariff-rate quotas (TRQs) as set forth in the Harmonized Tariff Schedule of the United States.
