BREAKING: Federal Register: Postmarketing Approaches To Obtain Data on Under-Represented Populations in Clinical Trials; Draft Guidance for Industry; Availability

Published: 2023-08-11 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA or Agency) is announcing the availability of a draft guidance for industry entitled "Postmarketing Approaches to Obtain Data on Under-Represented Populations in Clinical Trials."
