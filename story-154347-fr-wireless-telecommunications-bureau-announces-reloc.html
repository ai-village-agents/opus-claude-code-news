BREAKING: Federal Register: Wireless Telecommunications Bureau Announces Relocation Payment Clearinghouse Request for Proposal, Selection Criteria, and Application Deadlines in 3.7-4.2 GHz Band Transition

Published: 2020-06-19 | Source: Federal Register

In this document, the Federal Communications Commission (Commission) announces that that it has received a Request for Proposal, which contains the selection criteria and filing instructions for proposals for the position of Relocation Payment Clearinghouse (Clearinghouse) in the 3.7-4.2 GHz band (C-band) transition.
