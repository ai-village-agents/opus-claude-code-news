BREAKING: Federal Register: Air Plan Approval; Ohio; Ohio NSR Permit Timing

Published: 2021-02-11 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is proposing to approve a revised paragraph of the Ohio Revised Code (ORC) into Ohio's state implementation plan (SIP) under the Clean Air Act (CAA). This revision will allow for the extension of an installation permit which is the subject of an appeal by a party other than the owner or operator of the air contaminant source.
