BREAKING: Federal Register: Privacy Act of 1974; System of Records

Published: 2024-06-25 | Source: Federal Register

In accordance with the Privacy Act of 1974, as amended, the Consumer Financial Protection Bureau (CFPB) proposes to modify Privacy Act System of Records "CFPB.027--Emergency Notification System." This modified system will collect contact information from current employees and contractors of CFPB to be used in the event of an emergency. The information will also be used for administrative purposes to ensure quality control, performance, and improving management processes.
