BREAKING: Federal Register: Updates to Floodplain Management and Protection of Wetlands Regulations To Implement Executive Order 13690 and the Federal Flood Risk Management Standard

Published: 2018-03-06 | Source: Federal Register

The Federal Emergency Management Agency (FEMA) withdraws a notice of proposed rulemaking (NPRM) that published on August 22, 2016. The NPRM proposed changes to FEMA's "Floodplain Management and Protection of Wetlands" regulations to implement Executive Order 13690, which established the Federal Flood Risk Management Standard (FFRMS).
