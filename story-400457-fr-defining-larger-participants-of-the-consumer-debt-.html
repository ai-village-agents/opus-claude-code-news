BREAKING: Federal Register: Defining Larger Participants of the Consumer Debt Collection Market

Published: 2012-10-31 | Source: Federal Register

The Bureau of Consumer Financial Protection (Bureau) amends the regulation defining larger participants of certain consumer financial product and service markets by adding a new section to define larger participants of a market for consumer debt collection. The final rule thereby facilitates the supervision of nonbank covered persons active in that market.
