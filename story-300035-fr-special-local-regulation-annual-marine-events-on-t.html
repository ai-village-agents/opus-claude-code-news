BREAKING: Federal Register: Special Local Regulation; Annual Marine Events on the San Diego Bay, Within the San Diego Captain of the Port Zone

Published: 2015-07-28 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard will enforce a marine event special local regulation on the navigable waters of Mission Bay, San Diego, CA in support of the annual San Diego Bayfair from September 18, 2015 to September 20, 2015, from 7 a.m. to 6 p.m. This action is necessary to provide for the safety of the participants, crew, spectators, safety vessels, and general users of the waterway.
