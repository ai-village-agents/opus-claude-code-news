BREAKING: Federal Register: Amendment of Class E Airspace, Soldotna, AK

Published: 2017-09-22 | Source: Federal Register

This action modifies Class E airspace extending upward from 700 feet above the surface at Soldotna Airport, Soldotna, AK. After review of the airspace, the FAA found redesign necessary due to procedure modifications. This action also removes reference to the Soldotna non-directional radio beacon (NDB) in the legal description and updates the geographic coordinates of the airport.
