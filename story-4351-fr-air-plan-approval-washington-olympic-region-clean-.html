BREAKING: Federal Register: Air Plan Approval; Washington; Olympic Region Clean Air Agency, Recreational Fires

Published: 2025-01-17 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is approving a revision to the Washington State Implementation Plan (SIP) that was submitted by the Department of Ecology (Ecology) in coordination with the Olympic Region Clean Air Agency (ORCAA). In 2013, Ecology and ORCAA inadvertently submitted for incorporation into the SIP a ban on small, recreational fires in Thurston County.
