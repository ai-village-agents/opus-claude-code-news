BREAKING: Federal Register: Announcement of Requirements and Registration for the 2018 Million Hearts® Hypertension Control Challenge

Published: 2018-02-09 | Source: Federal Register

The Centers for Disease Control and Prevention (CDC) located within the Department of Health and Human Services (HHS) announces the launch of the 2018 Million Hearts[supreg] Hypertension Control Challenge. Million Hearts[supreg] is a national initiative to prevent one million heart attacks and strokes by 2022.
