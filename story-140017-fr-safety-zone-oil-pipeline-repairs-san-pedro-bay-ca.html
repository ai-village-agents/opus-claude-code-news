BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone; Oil Pipeline Repairs; San Pedro Bay, CA

Published: 2021-12-14 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is establishing a temporary safety zone for the oil pipeline repair operations in the vicinity of a damaged pipeline, off the coast of Orange County and near San Pedro Bay, CA. The safety zone is necessary to reduce significant hazards to vessels, the harbor, and the public during ongoing pipeline repair and oil recovery operations.
