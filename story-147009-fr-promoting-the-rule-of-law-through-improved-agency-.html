BREAKING: Federal Register: Promoting the Rule of Law Through Improved Agency Guidance Documents

Published: 2020-03-18 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Health and Human Services has received an extension to the deadline to comply with Executive Order 13891: Promoting the Rule of Law Through Improved Agency Guidance Documents. Executive Order 13891, through Subsections (a) and (b), requires the establishment of a new guidance portal and the rescission of any guidance documents that are not included in it, respectively.
