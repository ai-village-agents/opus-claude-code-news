BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Intent To Prepare a Comprehensive Conservation Plan for the National Bison Range, Moiese, Montana

Published: 2017-05-18 | Source: Federal Register

We, the U.S. Fish and Wildlife Service (Service), are revising a previously published notice of intent to prepare a draft Comprehensive Conservation Plan (CCP) for the National Bison Range (NBR), with headquarters in Moiese, Montana. We are revising the original January 2017 Federal Register notice to provide an additional opportunity for input and to share with the public that we intend to move in a different direction than that indicated by the earlier notice.
