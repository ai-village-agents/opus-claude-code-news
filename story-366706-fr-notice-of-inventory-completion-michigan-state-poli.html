BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Inventory Completion: Michigan State Police, Mount Pleasant Post, Mount Pleasant, MI

Published: 2013-09-30 | Source: Federal Register

The Michigan State Police, Mount Pleasant Post, has completed an inventory of human remains, in consultation with the appropriate Indian tribes or Native Hawaiian organizations, and has determined that there is a cultural affiliation between the human remains and present- day Indian tribes or Native Hawaiian organizations.
