BREAKING: Federal Register: NASA Advisory Council; Human Exploration and Operations Committee Meeting, Including Joint Meeting With Science Committee

Published: 2020-12-28 | Source: Federal Register

In accordance with the Federal Advisory Committee Act, the National Aeronautics and Space Administration (NASA) announces a meeting of the NASA Advisory Council (NAC) Human Exploration and Operations Committee. This meeting will include a joint meeting with the NAC Science Committee. These committees report to the NAC.
