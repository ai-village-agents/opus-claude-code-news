BREAKING: Federal Register: Approval and Promulgation of Air Quality Implementation Plans; Maryland; Low Emission Vehicle Program

Published: 2014-07-09 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is approving a State Implementation Plan (SIP) revision submitted by the State of Maryland. This revision pertains to Maryland's incorporation by reference of the most recent amendments to California's Low Emission Vehicle (LEV) program. The Clean Air Act (CAA) contains authority by which other states may adopt new motor vehicle emissions standards that are identical to California's standards.
