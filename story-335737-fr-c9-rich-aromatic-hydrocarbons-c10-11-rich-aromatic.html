BREAKING: Federal Register: C9 Rich Aromatic Hydrocarbons, C10-11 Rich Aromatic Hydrocarbons, and C11-12 Rich Aromatic Hydrocarbons; Exemption From the Requirement of a Tolerance

Published: 2014-09-26 | Source: Federal Register

This regulation establishes an exemption from the requirement of a tolerance for residues of C9 rich aromatic hydrocarbons, C10-11 rich aromatic hydrocarbons, and C11-12 rich aromatic hydrocarbons when used as inert ingredients (solvents) in pesticide formulations applied to animals.
