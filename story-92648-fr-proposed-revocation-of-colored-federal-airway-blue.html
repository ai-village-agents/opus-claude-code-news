BREAKING: Federal Register: Proposed Revocation of Colored Federal Airway Blue 37 (B-37); Level Island, AK

Published: 2022-04-04 | Source: Federal Register

This action proposes to revoke Colored Federal airway Blue 37 (B-37) in the vicinity of Level Island, AK due to the pending decommissioning of the Sumner Strait, AK, (SQM) Non-directional Beacon (NDB).
