BREAKING: Federal Register: Agency Information Collection Activities; Extension of Collection; Comment Request; Standard for Omnidirectional CB Base Antennas

Published: 2025-06-13 | Source: Federal Register

As required by the Paperwork Reduction Act of 1995, the Consumer Product Safety Commission (CPSC or Commission) requests comments on a proposed extension of approval of information collection requirements associated with the Safety Standard for Omnidirectional Citizens Band Base Station Antennas. The Office of Management and Budget (OMB) previously approved the collection of information under control number 3041-0006. OMB's most recent extension of approval will expire on September 30, 2025.
