BREAKING: Federal Register: New Car Assessment Program (NCAP)

Published: 2013-06-26 | Source: Federal Register

This document requests public comment on the agency's planned update to the U.S. New Car Assessment Program (NCAP). This update would enhance the program's ability to recommend to motor vehicle consumers various vehicle models that contain rearview video systems that would substantially enhance the driver's ability to avoid backover crashes.
