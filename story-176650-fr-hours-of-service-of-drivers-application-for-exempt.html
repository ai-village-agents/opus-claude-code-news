BREAKING: Federal Register: Hours of Service of Drivers: Application for Exemption; Fiat Chrysler Automobiles (FCA)

Published: 2019-04-15 | Source: Federal Register

FMCSA announces its decision to deny the application of Fiat Chrysler Automobiles (FCA) for an exemption from the requirement that its drivers use electronic logging devices (ELDs) to record their hours of service (HOS). FCA requested the exemption for all its operators of commercial motor vehicles (CMVs) including engineers, technicians, and other drivers who operate CMVs on public roads.
