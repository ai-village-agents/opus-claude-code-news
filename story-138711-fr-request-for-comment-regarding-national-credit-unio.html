BREAKING: Federal Register: Request for Comment Regarding National Credit Union Administration Draft Strategic Plan 2022-2026

Published: 2021-11-24 | Source: Federal Register

The NCUA Board (Board) is requesting comment on its Draft Strategic Plan 2022-2026. The draft plan provides the agency's proposed strategic goals and objectives for the next five years. The draft plan summarizes an analysis of the internal and external environment impacting NCUA and evaluates the agency's programs and risks.
