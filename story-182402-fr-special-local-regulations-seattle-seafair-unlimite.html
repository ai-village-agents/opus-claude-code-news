BREAKING: Federal Register: Special Local Regulations; Seattle Seafair Unlimited Hydroplane Race, Lake Washington, WA

Published: 2019-06-24 | Source: Federal Register

This year the Seattle Seafair Unlimited Hydroplane Race Special Local Regulation on Lake Washington, WA will be subject to enforcement from 7 a.m. until 7 p.m. daily, from August 1 through August 4. This action is necessary to ensure public safety from the inherent dangers associated with high-speed, hydroplane races, while allowing race area access for rescue personnel in the event of an emergency.
