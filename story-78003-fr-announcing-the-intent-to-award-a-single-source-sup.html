BREAKING: Federal Register: Announcing the Intent To Award a Single-Source Supplement the Link Center: Bridging Intellectual and/or Developmental Disabilities (I/DD) and Mental Health Systems Cooperative Agreement

The Administration for Community Living (ACL) announces the intent to award a single-source supplement to the current cooperative agreement held by the National Association for State Directors of Developmental Disabilities Services (NASDDDS) for the Link Center: Bridging I/DD and Mental Health Systems cooperative agreement.
