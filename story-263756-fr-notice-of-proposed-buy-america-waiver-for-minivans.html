BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Proposed Buy America Waiver for Minivans

Published: 2016-05-17 | Source: Federal Register

The Federal Transit Administration (FTA) received a formal request from the Pace Suburban Bus Division of the Regional Transportation Authority (Pace) for a Buy America non-availability waiver to purchase 188 Dodge Caravan minivans for its vanpool program.
