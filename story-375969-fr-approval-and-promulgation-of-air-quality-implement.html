BREAKING: Federal Register: Approval and Promulgation of Air Quality Implementation Plans; Virginia; Consumer and Commercial Products

Published: 2012-01-26 | Source: Federal Register

EPA is approving a State Implementation Plan (SIP) revision submitted by the Commonwealth of Virginia. The SIP revision adds a new chapter (9VAC5-45--Consumer and Commercial Products) in order to control volatile organic compounds (VOC) from portable fuel containers, consumer products, architectural and industrial (AIM) coatings, adhesives and sealants, and asphalt paving operations within the Northern Virginia and Fredericksburg VOC Emissions Control Areas.
