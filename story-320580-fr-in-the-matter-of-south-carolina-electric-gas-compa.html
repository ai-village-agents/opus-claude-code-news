BREAKING: Federal Register: In the Matter of South Carolina Electric & Gas Company, Virgil C. Summer Nuclear Station, Unit 1

Published: 2014-04-02 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Nuclear Regulatory Commission (NRC) is issuing a confirmatory order to South Carolina Electric & Gas Company (SCE&G or Licensee), the holder of License No. NPF-12, authorizing the operation of Virgil C. Summer Station (SNS), Unit 1. The facility is located on the Licensee's site in Jenkinsville, South Carolina.
