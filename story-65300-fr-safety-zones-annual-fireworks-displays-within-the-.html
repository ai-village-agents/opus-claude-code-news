BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zones; Annual Fireworks Displays Within the Captain of the Port, Puget Sound

Published: 2023-04-17 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard will enforce fourteen safety zones for annual firework displays in the Captain of the Port, Sector Puget Sound Zone during the dates and times noted under SUPPLEMENTARY INFORMATION. This action is necessary to prevent injury and to protect life and property of the maritime public from the hazards associated with the firework displays.
