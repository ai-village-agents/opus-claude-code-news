BREAKING: Federal Register: National Historic Landmark Nuclear Ship Savannah Available; Request for Information; Period Extension

Published: 2024-01-26 | Source: Federal Register

On October 30, 2023, the Maritime Administration (MARAD) published a Notice of Availability and Request for Information (NOA and RFI) in the Federal Register to determine preservation interest from entities that may wish to acquire the National Historic Landmark (NHL) Nuclear Ship Savannah (NSS).
