BREAKING: Federal Register: Certain Light-Emitting Diode Products, Fixtures, and Components Thereof; Notice of a Commission Determination To Institute a Rescission Proceeding and Grant a Joint Petition To Rescind Remedial Orders; Termination of Rescission Proceeding

Published: 2022-05-12 | Source: Federal Register

Notice is hereby given that the U.S. International Trade Commission ("the Commission") has determined to institute a rescission proceeding and grant a joint petition to rescind remedial orders issued in the underlying investigation. The rescission proceeding is terminated.
