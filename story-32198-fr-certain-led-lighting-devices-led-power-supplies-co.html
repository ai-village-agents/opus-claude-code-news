BREAKING: Federal Register: Certain LED Lighting Devices, LED Power Supplies, Components Thereof, and Products Containing Same; Notice of Commission Determination Not To Review an Initial Determination Terminating the Investigation Based on Settlement

Published: 2024-03-18 | Source: Federal Register

Notice is hereby given that the U.S. International Trade Commission has determined not to review an initial determination ("ID") (Order No. 15) issued by the presiding administrative law judge ("ALJ"), terminating the investigation in its entirety based on settlement.
