BREAKING: Federal Register: Reestablishment of the Agricultural Advisory Committee

Published: 2014-09-09 | Source: Federal Register

The Commodity Futures Trading Commission (Commission) is publishing this notice to announce the reestablishment of the Agricultural Advisory Committee (AAC). The Commission has determined that reestablishment of the AAC is necessary and in the public's interest.
