BREAKING: Federal Register: Exclusion of Particular Products From the Solar Products Safeguard Measure

Published: 2019-06-13 | Source: Federal Register

On January 23, 2018, the President imposed a safeguard measure on imports of certain solar products pursuant to a Section 201 investigation. On February 14, 2018, the United States Trade Representative (Trade Representative) established a procedure to request product-specific exclusions from application of the safeguard measure.
