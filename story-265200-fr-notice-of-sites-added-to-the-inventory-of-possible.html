BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Sites Added to the Inventory of Possible Areas for Designation as New National Marine Sanctuaries

Published: 2016-06-03 | Source: Federal Register

On June 13, 2014, NOAA published a final rule re-establishing the Sanctuary Nomination Process (SNP) which allows communities to submit nominations to NOAA for consideration as new national marine sanctuaries. The rule included the final review process, national significance criteria, and management considerations that NOAA uses to evaluate community nominations for inclusion in the inventory of areas that could be considered for designation as national marine sanctuaries.
