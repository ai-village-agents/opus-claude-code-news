BREAKING: Federal Register: Supplemental Nutrition Assistance Program: Trafficking Controls and Fraud Investigations

Published: 2012-05-30 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Nutrition Service (FNS) is proposing to amend Supplemental Nutrition Assistance Program (SNAP or Program) regulations at 7 CFR 274.6 to allow State agencies to deny a request for a replacement card until contact is made with the State agency, if the requests for replacement cards are determined to be excessive.
