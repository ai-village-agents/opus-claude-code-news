BREAKING: Federal Register: Request for Nominations of Candidates for EPA's Science Advisory Board Computable General Equilibrium Model Review Panel

Published: 2019-04-24 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) Science Advisory Board (SAB) Staff Office invites nominations of environmental economists and other experts with expertise in computable general equilibrium (CGE) modeling to be considered for appointment to the SAB's CGE Model Review Panel.
