BREAKING: Federal Register: Call for Applications for the International Buyer Program Calendar Year 2017

Published: 2015-11-30 | Source: Federal Register

In this notice, the U.S. Department of Commerce (DOC) International Trade Administration (ITA) announces that it will begin accepting applications for the International Buyer Program (IBP) for calendar year 2017 (January 1, 2017, through December 31, 2017). The announcement also sets out the objectives, procedures and application review criteria for the IBP.
