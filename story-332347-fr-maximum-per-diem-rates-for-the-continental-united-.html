BREAKING: Federal Register: Maximum Per Diem Rates for the Continental United States (CONUS)

Published: 2014-08-15 | Source: Federal Register

The General Services Administration's (GSA) Fiscal Year (FY) 2015 per diem review has resulted in lodging and meal allowance changes for certain locations within the Continental United States (CONUS) to provide for reimbursement of Federal employees' expenses covered by per diem.
