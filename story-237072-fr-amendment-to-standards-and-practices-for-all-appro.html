BREAKING: Federal Register: Amendment to Standards and Practices for All Appropriate Inquiries Under CERCLA

Published: 2017-06-20 | Source: Federal Register

EPA is proposing to amend the Standards and Practices for All Appropriate Inquiries to update an existing reference to a standard practice recently made available by ASTM International, a widely recognized standards development organization.
