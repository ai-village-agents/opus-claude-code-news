BREAKING: Federal Register: Significant New Use Rule on Certain Chemical Substances

Published: 2013-09-11 | Source: Federal Register

Under the Toxic Substances Control Act (TSCA), EPA is finalizing the significant new use rule (SNUR) for three chemical substances which were the subject of premanufacture notices (PMNs) and subject to a TSCA section 5(e) consent order issued by EPA.
