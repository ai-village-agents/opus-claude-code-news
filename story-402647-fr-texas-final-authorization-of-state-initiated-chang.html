BREAKING: Federal Register: Texas: Final Authorization of State-initiated Changes and Incorporation by Reference of State Hazardous Waste Management Program

Published: 2012-11-30 | Source: Federal Register

During a review of Texas' regulations, the EPA identified a variety of State-initiated changes to Texas' hazardous waste program under the Resource Conservation and Recovery Act, as amended (RCRA), for which the State had not previously sought authorization. The EPA proposes to authorize the State for the program changes.
