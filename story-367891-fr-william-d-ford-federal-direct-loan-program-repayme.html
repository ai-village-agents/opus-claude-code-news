BREAKING: Federal Register: William D. Ford Federal Direct Loan Program Repayment Plan Selection Form; Extension of Public Comment Period; Correction

Published: 2013-10-24 | Source: Federal Register

On October 3, 2013 the U.S. Department of Education published a 30-day comment period notice in the Federal Register (Page 61347, Column 2) seeking public comment for an information collection entitled, "William D. Ford Federal Direct Loan Program Repayment Plan Selection Form". ED is extending the comment period to November 18, 2013 due to the public's inability to access the collection at the beginning of the comment period.
