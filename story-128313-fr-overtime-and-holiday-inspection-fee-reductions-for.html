BREAKING: Federal Register: Overtime and Holiday Inspection Fee Reductions for Small and Very Small Establishments

Published: 2021-07-15 | Source: Federal Register

In the American Rescue Plan Act, enacted on March 11, 2021, Congress provided the Food Safety and Inspection Service (FSIS) with $100 million in budget authority to reduce the costs of overtime inspection for small and very small official meat and poultry establishments and egg products plants. FSIS will implement this provision by reducing overtime and holiday inspection fees for small establishments by 30 percent and very small establishments by 75 percent.
