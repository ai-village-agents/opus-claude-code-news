BREAKING: Federal Register: Amendment of Class D Airspace and Class E Airspace; Greenwood, MS

Published: 2018-05-17 | Source: Federal Register

This action amends Class D airspace, and Class E surface area airspace at Greenwood-Leflore Airport, Greenwood, MS, by making an editorial change to the legal descriptions replacing "Airport-Facility Directory" with the term "Chart Supplement". This action also removes the part-time Notice to Airmen (NOTAM) language from Class E airspace designated as an extension to Class D airspace.
