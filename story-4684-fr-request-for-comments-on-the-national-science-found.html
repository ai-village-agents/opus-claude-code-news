BREAKING: Federal Register: Request for Comments on the National Science Foundation's Proposed Intellectual Property Options

Published: 2025-01-24 | Source: Federal Register

In response to requests by prospective commenters that they would benefit from additional time to adequately consider and respond to the RFC, NSF has determined that an extension of the comment period until Friday, February 21, 2025, at 11:59 p.m. (eastern), is appropriate.
