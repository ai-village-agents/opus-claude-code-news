BREAKING: Federal Register: Energy Conservation Program: Energy Conservation Standards for Miscellaneous Gas Products

Published: 2022-06-14 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Department of Energy ("DOE" or "the Department") is initiating an effort to evaluate the potential for energy conservation standards for consumer miscellaneous gas products. DOE published a proposed coverage determination for these products in the Federal Register on February 7, 2022.
