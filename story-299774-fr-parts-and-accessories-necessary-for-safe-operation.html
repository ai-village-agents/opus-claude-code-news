BREAKING: Federal Register: Parts and Accessories Necessary for Safe Operation; Application for an Exemption From Volvo Trucks of North America

Published: 2015-07-24 | Source: Federal Register

The Federal Motor Carrier Safety Administration (FMCSA) requests public comment on an application for exemption from Volvo Trucks of North America (Volvo) to allow the placement of rain and ambient light detection sensors on Volvo commercial motor vehicles (CMV) lower in the windshield than is currently permitted by the Agency's regulations in order to utilize a mounting location that allows the sensor to function correctly.
