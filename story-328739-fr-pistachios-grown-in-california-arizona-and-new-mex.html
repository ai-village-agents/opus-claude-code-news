BREAKING: Federal Register: Pistachios Grown in California, Arizona, and New Mexico; Modification of Aflatoxin Regulations

Published: 2014-07-03 | Source: Federal Register

This rule revises the aflatoxin sampling regulations currently prescribed under the California, Arizona, and New Mexico pistachio marketing order (order). The order regulates the handling of pistachios grown in California, Arizona, and New Mexico, and is administered locally by the Administrative Committee for Pistachios (Committee).
