BREAKING: Federal Register: Endangered and Threatened Wildlife and Plants; 90-Day Findings on Five Petitions

Published: 2014-01-22 | Source: Federal Register

We, the U.S. Fish and Wildlife Service (Service), announce 90- day findings on five petitions to list 19 species as endangered or threatened, under the Endangered Species Act of 1973, as amended (Act). Based on our review, we find that these petitions present substantial scientific or commercial information indicating that the petitioned actions may be warranted.
