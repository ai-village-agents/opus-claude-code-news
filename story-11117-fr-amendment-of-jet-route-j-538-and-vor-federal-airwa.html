BREAKING: Federal Register: Amendment of Jet Route J-538 and VOR Federal Airway V-129; Establishment of Canadian RNAV Routes Q-828, Q-945, Q-971, and T-797; and Revocation of Jet Routes J-483 and J-562; Northcentral United States

Published: 2025-05-12 | Source: Federal Register
