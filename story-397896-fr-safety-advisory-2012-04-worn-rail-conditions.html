BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Advisory 2012-04; Worn Rail Conditions

Published: 2012-10-02 | Source: Federal Register

FRA is issuing Safety Advisory 2012-04 to remind track owners, railroads, and their track inspectors of the importance of complying with the applicable rail management programs and engineering procedures that address rail with severe rail head wear and rolling contact fatigue (RCF) conditions.
