BREAKING: Federal Register: Revising Abbreviated New Drug Application Labeling Following Revision of the Reference Listed Drug Labeling; Final Guidance for Industry; Availability

Published: 2024-01-25 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA or Agency) is announcing the availability of a final guidance for industry entitled "Revising ANDA Labeling Following Revision of the RLD Labeling."
