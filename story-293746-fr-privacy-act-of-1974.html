BREAKING: Federal Register: Privacy Act of 1974

Published: 2015-05-13 | Source: Federal Register

The Privacy Act of 1974, (5 U.S.C. 552a(e)(4)) requires that all agencies publish in the Federal Register a notice of the existence and character of their systems of records. Notice is hereby given that the Department of Veterans Affairs (VA) is establishing a new system of records entitled "Inquiry Routing & Information System (IRIS)-VA" (151VA005OP6).
