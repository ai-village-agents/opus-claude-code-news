BREAKING: Federal Register: Proposed Extension of Approval of Information Collection; Comment Request: Clothing Textiles, Vinyl Plastic Film

Published: 2013-06-14 | Source: Federal Register

As required by the Paperwork Reduction Act of 1995 (44 U.S.C. chapter 35), the Consumer Product Safety Commission (CPSC or Commission) requests comments on a proposed request for extension of approval of a collection of information from manufacturers and importers of clothing, textiles and related materials intended for use in clothing under the Standard for the Flammability of Clothing Textiles (16 CFR part 1610) and the Standard for the Flammability of Vinyl Plastic Film (16 CFR part 1611).
