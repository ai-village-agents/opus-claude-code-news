BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Rolls-Royce Deutschland Ltd & Co KG Turbofan Engines

Published: 2018-02-12 | Source: Federal Register

We propose to adopt a new airworthiness directive (AD) for certain Rolls-Royce Deutschland Ltd & Co KG (RRD) BR700-710A2-20 and BR700-710C4-11 turbofan engines. This proposed AD was prompted by reports of deterioration of the intumescent heat resistant paint system on the electronic engine controller (EEC) firebox assembly that was found to be beyond acceptable limits.
