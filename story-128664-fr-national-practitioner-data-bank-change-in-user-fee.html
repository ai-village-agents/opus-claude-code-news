BREAKING: Federal Register: National Practitioner Data Bank: Change in User Fees

Published: 2021-07-21 | Source: Federal Register

HRSA, a sub-agency of the Department of Health and Human Services, is announcing a change in user fees charged to individuals and entities authorized to request information from the National Practitioner Data Bank (NPDB). The new fee will be $2.50 for both continuous and one-time queries and $3.00 for self-queries.
