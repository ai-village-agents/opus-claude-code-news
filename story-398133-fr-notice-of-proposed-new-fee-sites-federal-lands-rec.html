BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Proposed New Fee Sites; Federal Lands Recreation Enhancement Act

Published: 2012-10-03 | Source: Federal Register

The Caribou-Targhee National Forest is proposing to begin charging fees at the Al Taylor Cabin. Facilities at the site have recently been reconstructed and amenities have been added to improve services and experiences. Fees are assessed based on the level of amenities and services provided, cost of operation and maintenance, market assessment, and public comment.
