BREAKING: Federal Register: Public Notice for a Change in Use of Aeronautical Property and Long-Term Lease Approval at Harrisburg International Airport (MDT), Middletown, PA

Published: 2013-05-28 | Source: Federal Register

The Federal Aviation Administration is requesting public comment on the Susquehanna Area Regional Airport Authority's request to change 22 acres of airport property from aeronautical use to non- aeronautical use. The request also solicits approval for entering into a long-term lease for 2.893 acres within this area for a retail convenience store.
