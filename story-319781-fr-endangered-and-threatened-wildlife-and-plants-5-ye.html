BREAKING: Federal Register: Endangered and Threatened Wildlife and Plants; 5-Year Status Reviews of 33 Southeastern Species

Published: 2014-03-25 | Source: Federal Register

We, the U.S. Fish and Wildlife Service (Service), are initiating 5-year status reviews of 33 species under the Endangered Species Act of 1973, as amended (Act). We conduct these reviews to ensure that the classification of species as threatened or endangered on the Lists of Endangered and Threatened Wildlife and Plants is accurate.
