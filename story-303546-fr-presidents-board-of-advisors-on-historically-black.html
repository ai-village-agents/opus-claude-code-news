BREAKING: Federal Register: President's Board of Advisors on Historically Black Colleges and Universities

Published: 2015-09-08 | Source: Federal Register

This notice sets forth the agenda for the September 23, 2015, meeting of the President's Board of Advisors on Historically Black Colleges and Universities (PBA) and provides information to members of the public on submitting written comments and on the process as to how to request time to make oral comments at the meeting.
