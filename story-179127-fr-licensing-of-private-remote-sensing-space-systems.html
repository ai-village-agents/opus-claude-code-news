BREAKING: Federal Register: Licensing of Private Remote Sensing Space Systems

Published: 2019-05-14 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Commerce (Commerce), through the National Oceanic and Atmospheric Administration (NOAA), licenses the operation of private remote sensing space systems under the Land Remote Sensing Policy Act of 1992. NOAA's existing regulations implementing the Act were last updated in 2006.
