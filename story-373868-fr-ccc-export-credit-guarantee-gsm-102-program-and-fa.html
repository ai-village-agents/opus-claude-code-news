BREAKING: Federal Register: CCC Export Credit Guarantee (GSM-102) Program and Facility Guarantee Program (FGP)

Published: 2013-12-27 | Source: Federal Register

This proposed rule would revise and amend the regulations that administer the Export Credit Guarantee (GSM-102) Program. Changes in this proposed rule incorporate program operational changes and information from press releases and notices to participants that have been implemented since the publication of the current rule, and include other administrative revisions to enhance clarity and program integrity.
