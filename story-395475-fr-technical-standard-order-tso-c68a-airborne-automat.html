BREAKING: Federal Register: Technical Standard Order (TSO)-C68a, Airborne Automatic Dead Reckoning Computer Equipment Utilizing Aircraft Heading and Doppler Ground Speed and Drift Angle Data (for Air Carrier Aircraft)

Published: 2012-09-04 | Source: Federal Register

This notice announces the FAA's cancellation of TSO-C68a. The effect of the cancelled TSO-C68a will result in no new TSO-C68a design or production approvals.
