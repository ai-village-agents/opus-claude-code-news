BREAKING: Federal Register: Modifying Emissions Limits for the 24.25-24.45 GHz and 24.75-25.25 GHz Bands

Published: 2024-01-29 | Source: Federal Register

In this document, the Federal Communications Commission (Commission) proposes to implement certain decisions regarding the 24.25-27.5 GHz band made in the World Radiocommunication Conference held by the International Telecommunication Union (ITU) in 2019 (WRC- 19). Specifically, the Commission proposes to align part 30 of the Commission's rules for mobile operations with the Resolution 750 limits on unwanted emissions into the passive 23.6-24.0 GHz band that were adopted at WRC-19.
