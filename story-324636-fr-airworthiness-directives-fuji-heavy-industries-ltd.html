BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Fuji Heavy Industries, Ltd. Airplanes

Published: 2014-05-19 | Source: Federal Register

We propose to adopt a new airworthiness directive (AD) for Fuji Heavy Industries, Ltd. Models FA-200-160, FA-200-180, and FA-200- 180AO airplanes. This proposed AD results from mandatory continuing airworthiness information (MCAI) originated by an aviation authority of another country to identify and correct an unsafe condition on an aviation product.
