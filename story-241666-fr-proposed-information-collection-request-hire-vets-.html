BREAKING: Federal Register: Proposed Information Collection Request; HIRE Vets Medallion Program

Published: 2017-08-18 | Source: Federal Register

VETS is publishing elsewhere in this issue of the Federal Register a Notice of Proposed Rulemaking to propose regulations implementing the Honoring Investments in Recruiting and Employing American Military Veterans Act of 2017. The proposal allows the public 30 days to comment on the proposed regulations including the proposal's collections of information contained in the rule.
