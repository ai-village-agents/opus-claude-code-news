BREAKING: Federal Register: Provisions Pertaining to Certain Investments in the United States by Foreign Persons

Published: 2018-10-11 | Source: Federal Register

This interim rule sets forth amendments to the regulations in part 800 of 31 CFR that implement, and make updates consistent with, certain provisions of the Foreign Investment Risk Review Modernization Act of 2018 (FIRRMA).
