BREAKING: Federal Register: Civil Monetary Penalty Adjustments for Inflation

Published: 2016-06-07 | Source: Federal Register

This interim final rule is being issued to adjust for inflation each civil monetary penalty (CMP) provided by law within the jurisdiction of the Department of Commerce (Commerce Department).
