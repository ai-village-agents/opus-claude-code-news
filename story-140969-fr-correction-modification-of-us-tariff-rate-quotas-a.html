BREAKING: Federal Register: Correction: Modification of U.S. Tariff-Rate Quotas and the Harmonized Tariff Schedule of the United States

Published: 2021-12-27 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Trade Representative published a notice in the Federal Register on July 6, 2021 (July 6 notice), modifying the Harmonized Tariff Schedule of the United States (HTSUS) to divide certain U.S. tariff-rate quotas (TRQs) currently allocated to the European Union (EU), between the EU and the United Kingdom (UK) as a result of Brexit and to reflect changes in the composition of the EU.
