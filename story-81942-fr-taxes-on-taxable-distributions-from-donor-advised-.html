BREAKING: Federal Register: Taxes on Taxable Distributions From Donor Advised Funds Under Section 4966

Published: 2023-11-14 | Source: Federal Register

This document contains proposed regulations regarding excise taxes on taxable distributions made by a sponsoring organization from a donor advised fund (DAF), and on the agreement of certain fund managers to the making of such distributions. The proposed regulations would provide guidance regarding DAFs and taxable distributions.
