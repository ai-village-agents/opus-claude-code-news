BREAKING: Federal Register: Guidance Under Section 1061

Published: 2021-01-19 | Source: Federal Register

This document contains final regulations that provide guidance under section 1061 of the Internal Revenue Code (Code). Section 1061 recharacterizes certain net long-term capital gains of a partner that holds one or more applicable partnership interests as short-term capital gains.
