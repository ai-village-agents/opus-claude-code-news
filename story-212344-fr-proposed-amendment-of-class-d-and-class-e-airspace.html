BREAKING: Federal Register: Proposed Amendment of Class D and Class E Airspace, and Establishment of Class E Airspace; Honolulu, HI

Published: 2018-07-24 | Source: Federal Register

This action proposes to modify Class D airspace, and Class E airspace extending upward from 700 feet above the surface, and establish Class E surface area airspace at Wheeler Army Airfield (AAF), Honolulu, HI. This action also would update the airport name and geographic coordinates in the associated Class D and E airspace areas to match the FAA's aeronautical database, and would replace outdated language in the airspace description.
