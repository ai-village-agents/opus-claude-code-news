BREAKING: Federal Register: Privacy Act of 1974; System of Records

Published: 2018-01-02 | Source: Federal Register

The Commodity Futures Trading Commission (CFTC or Commission) is establishing a new system of records under the Privacy Act of 1974: CFTC-50, LabCFTC. LabCFTC is the focal point for the CFTC's efforts to promote responsible Financial Technology (FinTech) innovation and fair competition for the benefit of the American public.
