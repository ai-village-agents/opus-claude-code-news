BREAKING: Federal Register: Minimum Internal Control Standards and Technical Standards

Published: 2012-07-24 | Source: Federal Register

On June 1, 2012, the National Indian Gaming Commission (NIGC) published in the Federal Register two notices of proposed rulemaking for public comment. The deadline for submission of public comments was July 31, 2012.
