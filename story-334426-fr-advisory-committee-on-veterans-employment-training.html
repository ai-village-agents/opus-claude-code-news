BREAKING: Federal Register: Advisory Committee on Veterans' Employment, Training and Employer Outreach (ACVETEO): Meeting

Published: 2014-09-10 | Source: Federal Register

This notice sets forth the schedule and proposed agenda of a forthcoming meeting of the ACVETEO. The ACVETEO will discuss the VETS core programs and services regarding efforts that assist veterans seeking employment and raise employer awareness as to the advantages of hiring veterans. There will be an opportunity for persons or organizations to address the committee. Any individual or organization that wishes to do so should contact Mr. Anthony Camilli at 202-693-4708.
