BREAKING: Federal Register: Ceramic Tile from the People's Republic of China: Rescission of Antidumping Duty Administrative Review; 2023-2024

Published: 2024-12-02 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Department of Commerce (Commerce) is rescinding the administrative review of the antidumping duty (AD) order on ceramic tile from the People's Republic of China (China) for the period of review (POR) June 1, 2023, through May 31, 2024.
