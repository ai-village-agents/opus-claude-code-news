BREAKING: Federal Register: Request for Public Comment on Waiver Revisions for Certain Medications and Suggestions for Risk Evaluation

Published: 2015-01-28 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is seeking public comment on the policy clarification proposed in this Notice regarding the criteria for granting medical waivers to mariners who require the use of potentially impairing prescription medications. The Coast Guard is also seeking input regarding effective methods of risk evaluation of such mariners.
