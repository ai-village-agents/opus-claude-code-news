BREAKING: Federal Register: Amendment of Class E Airspace; Missoula, MT

Published: 2020-03-03 | Source: Federal Register

This action amends Class E airspace designated as an extension to a Class D or Class E surface area at the Missoula International Airport, Missoula, MT. This action also amends the Class E airspace extending upward from 700 above the surface at the airport. Additionally, the action amends the Class E airspace extending upward from 1,200 above the surface at the airport. Further, this action removes the Missoula VORTAC from the legal description.
