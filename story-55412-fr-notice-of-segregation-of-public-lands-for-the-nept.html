BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Segregation of Public Lands for the Neptune Solar Project, Millard County, Utah

Published: 2024-12-16 | Source: Federal Register

The Bureau of Land Management (BLM) is segregating 1,920 acres of public lands as part of the right-of-way (ROW) application for the Neptune Solar Project, from appropriation under the public land laws, including location under the Mining Law of 1872, but not the Mineral Leasing Act or Material Sales Act, for a period of 2 years from the date of publication of this notice, subject to valid existing rights.
