BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of U.S. Administration Role Concerning U.S. Sector Members at the International Telecommunication Union (ITU)

Published: 2016-03-23 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of State informs all existing and prospective U.S. Sector Members of the ITU that the Department of State serves as the ITU membership approval authority for U.S. entities and reminds U.S. Sector Members and prospective members of their responsibilities for paying their membership fees and keeping their accounts current.
