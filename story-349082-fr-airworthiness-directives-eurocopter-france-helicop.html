BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Eurocopter France Helicopters

Published: 2013-03-11 | Source: Federal Register

We are adopting a new airworthiness directive (AD) for the Eurocopter France (Eurocopter) Model AS332C, AS332L, and AS332L1 helicopters. This AD requires modifying the main landing gear control panel (control panel) 33G, connector 100G, and wiring. It also requires tests to ensure that these modifications function correctly.
