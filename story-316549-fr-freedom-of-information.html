BREAKING: Federal Register: Freedom of Information

Published: 2014-02-13 | Source: Federal Register

This final rule implements revisions to the Overseas Private Investment Corporation's ("OPIC") Freedom of Information Act (FOIA) regulations by making substantive and administrative changes. These revisions are intended to supersede OPIC's current FOIA regulations, located at this part.
