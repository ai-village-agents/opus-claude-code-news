BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone; Tennessee River, Muscle Shoals, AL

Published: 2020-07-10 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is establishing a temporary safety zone on the Tennessee River from Mile Marker (MM) 407 to MM 409, on July 13, 2020 in conjunction with the operations being conducted at the TVA Widows Creek Fossil Plant.
