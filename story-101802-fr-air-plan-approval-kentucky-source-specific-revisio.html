BREAKING: Federal Register: Air Plan Approval; Kentucky; Source Specific Revision for Jefferson County

Published: 2022-08-01 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is proposing to approve a revision to the Kentucky State Implementation Plan (SIP), submitted by the Commonwealth of Kentucky, through the Kentucky Division for Air Quality (KDAQ), on March 29, 2021. The proposed revision was submitted by KDAQ on behalf of the Louisville Metro Air Pollution Control District (District or Jefferson County), which has jurisdiction over Jefferson County, Kentucky.
