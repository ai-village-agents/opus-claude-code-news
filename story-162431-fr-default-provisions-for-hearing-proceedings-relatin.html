BREAKING: Federal Register: Default Provisions for Hearing Proceedings Relating to the Revocation, Suspension, or Denial of a DEA Registration

Published: 2020-09-30 | Source: Federal Register

This proposed rulemaking would add provisions requiring a person served with an order to show cause issued pursuant to the Controlled Substances Act to file a request for a hearing no later than 15 days after the date of receipt of the order.
