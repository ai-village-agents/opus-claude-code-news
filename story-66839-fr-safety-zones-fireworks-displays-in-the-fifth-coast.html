BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zones; Fireworks Displays in the Fifth Coast Guard District-Philadelphia, PA

Published: 2023-05-05 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard will enforce the Delaware River, Philadelphia, PA Safety Zone on May 27, 2023, or on a rain date of May 28, 2023, to provide for the safety of life on navigable waterways during a barge- based fireworks display. Our regulation for marine events within the Fifth Coast Guard District identifies the boundaries of the regulated area.
