BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; The Boeing Company Airplanes

Published: 2018-02-23 | Source: Federal Register

We propose to adopt a new airworthiness directive (AD) for The Boeing Company Model 787 series airplanes powered by Rolls Royce Trent 1000 engines. This proposed AD was prompted by a report of failures of the inner fixed structure (IFS) forward upper fire seal and damage to thermal insulation blankets in the forward upper area of the thrust reverser (TR).
