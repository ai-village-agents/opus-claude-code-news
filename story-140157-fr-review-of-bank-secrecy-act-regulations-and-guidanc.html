BREAKING: Federal Register: Review of Bank Secrecy Act Regulations and Guidance

Published: 2021-12-15 | Source: Federal Register

The Financial Crimes Enforcement Network (FinCEN) is issuing this request for information (RFI) to solicit comment on ways to streamline, modernize, and update the anti-money laundering and countering the financing of terrorism (AML/CFT) regime of the United States.
