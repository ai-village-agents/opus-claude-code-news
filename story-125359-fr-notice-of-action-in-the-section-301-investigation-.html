BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Action in the Section 301 Investigation of India's Digital Services Tax

Published: 2021-06-07 | Source: Federal Register

On January 6, 2021, the U.S. Trade Representative announced a determination that India's Digital Services Tax (DST) is unreasonable or discriminatory and burdens or restricts U.S. commerce. This notice announces the U.S. Trade Representative's determination to take action in the form of additional duties of 25 percent on the products of India specified in Annex A to this notice.
