BREAKING: Federal Register: Gestamp West Virginia; Application for Permanent Variance and Interim Order; Grant of Interim Order; Request for Comments

Published: 2020-08-05 | Source: Federal Register

In this notice, OSHA announces the application of Gestamp West Virginia (Gestamp) for a permanent variance and interim order from the provision of OSHA standards that regulate the control of hazardous energy (lockout/tagout) and presents the agency's preliminary finding to grant the permanent variance.
