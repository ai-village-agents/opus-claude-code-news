BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Availability of Draft Scientific Assessment for Public Comment

Published: 2016-12-15 | Source: Federal Register

The National Oceanic and Atmospheric Administration (NOAA) is publishing this notice on behalf of the United States Global Change Research Program (USGCRP) to announce the availability of a draft assessment, the Climate Science Special Report, for a 45-day public review, collected comments will be carefully reviewed by the relevant chapter author teams.
