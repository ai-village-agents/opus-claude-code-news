BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Closure of Public Lands at Challis Bridge Recreation Site in Custer County, Idaho

Published: 2022-04-29 | Source: Federal Register

The Bureau of Land Management (BLM) is giving notice that 11.5 acres of public lands within the Challis Bridge Recreation Site is closed to all activities and forms of access. The closure is for the protection of public health and safety due to excessive flooding and ice jams within the recreation site.
