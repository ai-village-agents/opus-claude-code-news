BREAKING: Federal Register: Proposed Amendment of Class D and E Airspace; Yakima, WA

Published: 2020-07-06 | Source: Federal Register

This action proposes to amend Class D airspace at Yakima Air Terminal/McAllister Field Airport. This action also proposes to amend Class E airspace, designated as a surface area. Additionally, this action proposes to remove Class E airspace, designated as an extension to a Class D or Class E surface area.
