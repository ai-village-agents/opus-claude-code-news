BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of EPA's Action To Postpone the Effective Date of the EPA Region 1 Clean Water Act National Pollutant Discharge Elimination System General Permits for Stormwater Discharges From Small Municipal Separate Storm Sewer Systems in Massachusetts

Published: 2017-07-13 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is providing notice that it took action to postpone the effective date of its Clean Water Act (CWA) National Pollutant Discharge Elimination System (NPDES) General Permits for Stormwater Discharges from Small Municipal Separate Storm Sewer Systems (MS4s) in Massachusetts. By its action, EPA postponed the July 1, 2017 effective date of the permit for one year, to July 1, 2018. EPA's postponement is available at: https://www3.epa.gov/region1/npdes/stormwat...
