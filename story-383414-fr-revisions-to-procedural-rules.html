BREAKING: Federal Register: Revisions to Procedural Rules

Published: 2012-04-18 | Source: Federal Register

The Commission is establishing a docket to consider proposed changes in procedures for handling cases under 39 U.S.C. 3661. These cases involve changes in the nature of postal services which affect service on a nationwide or substantially nationwide basis. The Commission invites comments from interested persons on ways to improve and expedite its procedures, consistent with due process.
