BREAKING: Federal Register: Marine Highway Project Designation Open Season General Information Announcement

Published: 2019-03-01 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Department of Transportation (DOT) and Maritime Administration (MARAD) announce that the open season for Marine Highway Project Designation submissions is being extended to January 31, 2022 (Open Season). The purpose of this notice is to extend the invitation to interested parties to submit Marine Highway Project Designation applications to DOT for review and consideration.
