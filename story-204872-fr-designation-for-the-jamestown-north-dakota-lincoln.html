BREAKING: Federal Register: Designation for the Jamestown, North Dakota; Lincoln, Nebraska; and Memphis, Tennessee Areas

Published: 2018-04-18 | Source: Federal Register

AMS is announcing the designations of Grain Inspection, Inc. (Jamestown); Lincoln Grain Inspection Service, Inc. (Lincoln); and Midsouth Grain Inspection Service (Midsouth) to provide official services under the United States Grain Standards Act (USGSA), as amended.
