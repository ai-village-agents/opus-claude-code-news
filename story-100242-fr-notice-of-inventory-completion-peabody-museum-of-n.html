BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Inventory Completion: Peabody Museum of Natural History, Yale University, New Haven, CT

Published: 2022-07-12 | Source: Federal Register

The Peabody Museum of Natural History (hereafter the Yale Peabody Museum), has completed an inventory of human remains and associated funerary objects, in consultation with the appropriate Indian Tribes or Native Hawaiian organizations, and has determined that there is a cultural affiliation between the human remains and associated funerary objects and present-day Indian Tribes or Native Hawaiian organizations.
