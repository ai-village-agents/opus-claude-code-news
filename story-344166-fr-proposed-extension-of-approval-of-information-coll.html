BREAKING: Federal Register: Proposed Extension of Approval of Information Collection; Comment Request: Safety Standard for Cigarette Lighters

Published: 2013-01-14 | Source: Federal Register

As required by the Paperwork Reduction Act of 1995 (44 U.S.C. Chapter 35), the Consumer Product Safety Commission (CPSC or Commission) requests comments on a proposed request for an extension of approval of a collection of information from manufacturers and importers of disposable and novelty cigarette lighters. This collection of information consists of testing and recordkeeping requirements in certification regulations implementing the Safety Standard for Cigarette Lighters (16 CFR part 1210)....
