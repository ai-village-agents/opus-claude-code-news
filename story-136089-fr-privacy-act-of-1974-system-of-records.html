BREAKING: Federal Register: Privacy Act of 1974; System of Records

Published: 2021-10-21 | Source: Federal Register

In accordance with the Privacy Act of 1974, the U.S. Department of Homeland Security (DHS), Office of Inspector General (OIG), proposes to modify and reissue a current DHS system of records titled, "DHS/OIG-002 Investigative Records System of Records."
