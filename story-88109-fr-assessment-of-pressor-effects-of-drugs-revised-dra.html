BREAKING: Federal Register: Assessment of Pressor Effects of Drugs; Revised Draft Guidance for Industry; Availability

Published: 2022-02-04 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA or Agency) is announcing the availability of a revised draft guidance for industry entitled "Assessment of Pressor Effects of Drugs." This draft guidance is intended to advise sponsors on the premarketing assessment of a drug's effect on blood pressure. Elevated blood pressure is known to increase the risk of stroke, heart attack, and death.
