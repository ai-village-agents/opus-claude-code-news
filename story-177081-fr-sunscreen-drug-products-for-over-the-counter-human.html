BREAKING: Federal Register: Sunscreen Drug Products for Over-the-Counter Human Use; Extension of Comment Period

Published: 2019-04-18 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA or the Agency) is extending the comment period for the proposed rule that appeared in the Federal Register of February 26, 2019. In the proposed rule, FDA requested comments on its proposals relating to the regulation of over- the-counter (OTC) sunscreen monograph products.
