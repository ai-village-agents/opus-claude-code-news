BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Robinson Helicopter Company Helicopters

Published: 2019-04-12 | Source: Federal Register

We are adopting a new airworthiness directive (AD) for certain Robinson Helicopter Company (Robinson Helicopter) Model R66 helicopters. This AD requires inspecting the oil tank outlet's fitting color. If the fitting is blue, this AD requires replacing the tank with an oil tank that lacks a blue fitting.
