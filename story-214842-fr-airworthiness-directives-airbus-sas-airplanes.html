BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Airbus SAS Airplanes

Published: 2018-08-24 | Source: Federal Register

We propose to adopt a new airworthiness directive (AD) for certain Airbus SAS Model A330-200 series airplanes; Model A330-200 Freighter series airplanes; and Model A330-300 series airplanes. This proposed AD was prompted by revisions to certain airworthiness limitation item (ALI) documents, which specify more restrictive instructions and/or airworthiness limitations.
