BREAKING: Federal Register: Proposed Establishment of Class E Airspace; Pine Island, FL

Published: 2013-03-06 | Source: Federal Register

This action proposes to establish Class E Airspace at Pine Island, FL, to accommodate a new Area Navigation (RNAV) Global Positioning System (GPS) special Standard Instrument Approach Procedure (SIAP) serving Pine Island Heliport. This action would enhance the safety and airspace management of Instrument Flight Rules (IFR) operations within the National Airspace System.
