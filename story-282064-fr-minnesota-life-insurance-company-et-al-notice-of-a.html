BREAKING: Federal Register: Minnesota Life Insurance Company, et al; Notice of Application

Published: 2016-12-20 | Source: Federal Register

Notice of Application for an order pursuant to section 11(a) of the Investment Company Act of 1940, as amended (the "Act"), approving the terms of a proposed offer of exchange. Applicants request an order approving the terms of a proposed offer of exchange of a new flexible variable universal life insurance policy for certain outstanding flexible variable universal life insurance policies.
