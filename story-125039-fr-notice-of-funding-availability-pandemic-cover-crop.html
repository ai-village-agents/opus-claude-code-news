BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Funding Availability; Pandemic Cover Crop Program

Published: 2021-06-02 | Source: Federal Register

The Risk Management Agency (RMA), on behalf of the Federal Crop Insurance Corporation (FCIC), is announcing the availability of funding under the Pandemic Cover Crop Program (PCCP) to help agricultural producers impacted by the effects of the COVID-19 outbreak.
