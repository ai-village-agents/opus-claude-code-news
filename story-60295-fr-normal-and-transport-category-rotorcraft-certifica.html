BREAKING: Federal Register: Normal and Transport Category Rotorcraft Certification

Published: 2023-02-10 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA is amending the certification standards of normal and transport category rotorcraft. These changes are necessary to address modern designs currently used in the rotorcraft industry and will reduce the burden on applicants for certification of new rotorcraft designs. The changes will reduce or eliminate the need for certain special conditions currently required to obtain certification of modern rotorcraft.
