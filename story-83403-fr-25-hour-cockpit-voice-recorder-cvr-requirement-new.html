BREAKING: Federal Register: 25-Hour Cockpit Voice Recorder (CVR) Requirement, New Aircraft Production

Published: 2023-12-04 | Source: Federal Register

This rulemaking would increase the recording time of cockpit voice recorders from the mandated 2 hours to a proposed 25-hour recording time for all future manufactured aircraft.
