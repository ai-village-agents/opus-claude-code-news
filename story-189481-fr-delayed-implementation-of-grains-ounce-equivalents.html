BREAKING: Federal Register: Delayed Implementation of Grains Ounce Equivalents in the Child and Adult Care Food Program

Published: 2019-09-25 | Source: Federal Register

This action delays, from October 1, 2019 until October 1, 2021, the implementation date of the "ounce equivalents" requirement for crediting grains served in the Child and Adult Care Food Program (CACFP). The final rule, Child and Adult Care Food Program: Meal Pattern Revisions Related to the Healthy, Hunger-Free Kids Act of 2010, published on April 25, 2016, specified that meal planners must use ounce equivalents to determine the amount of creditable grain served as part of a reimbursable meal ...
