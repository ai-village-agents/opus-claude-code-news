BREAKING: Federal Register: Gastrointestinal Drugs Advisory Committee; Cancellation

Published: 2012-05-08 | Source: Federal Register

The meeting of the Gastrointestinal Drugs Advisory Committee scheduled for May 31, 2012, is canceled. This meeting was announced in the Federal Register of March 23, 2012 (77 FR 17078). The meeting is being canceled because the Agency no longer needs to discuss the issues that were originally under consideration in the review of the application.
