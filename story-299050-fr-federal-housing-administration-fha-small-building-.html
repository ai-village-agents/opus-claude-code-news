BREAKING: Federal Register: Federal Housing Administration (FHA): Small Building Risk Sharing Initiative Final Notice

Published: 2015-07-16 | Source: Federal Register

This Final Notice announces HUD's implementation of an Initiative under the Risk Sharing Program (the "Initiative"), authorized by Section 542(b) of the Housing and Community Development Act of 1992, to facilitate the financing of small multifamily properties.
