BREAKING: Federal Register: WTO Dispute Settlement Proceeding Regarding Indonesia-Importation of Horticultural Products, Animals and Animal Products

Published: 2015-06-30 | Source: Federal Register

The Office of the United States Trade Representative ("USTR") is providing notice that on May 20, 2015, at the request of the United States, the World Trade Organization (WTO) has established a dispute settlement panel under the Marrakesh Agreement Establishing the World Trade Organization ("WTO Agreement") concerning certain measures imposed by Indonesia on the importation of horticultural products, animals and animal products.
