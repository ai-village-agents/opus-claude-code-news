BREAKING: Federal Register: Request for Nominees for the Advisory Council on Wildlife Trafficking; Extension of Nominations Period

Published: 2015-09-10 | Source: Federal Register

On August 10, 2015, via a Federal Register notice, the Secretary of the Interior (Secretary), after consultation with the Co- Chairs of the Presidential Task Force on Wildlife Trafficking (Task Force), issued a call for nominations for individuals to serve on the Advisory Council on Wildlife Trafficking (Council). The due date for nominations is now extended.
