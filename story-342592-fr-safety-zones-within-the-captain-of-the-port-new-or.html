BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zones Within the Captain of the Port New Orleans Zone, Louisiana

Published: 2014-12-23 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is establishing two temporary safety zones for two fireworks display events within the Captain of the Port (COTP) New Orleans Zone. This action is necessary to protect persons and vessels from potential safety hazards associated with these events. Entry into these zones is prohibited unless specifically authorized by the Captain of the Port New Orleans or a designated representative.
