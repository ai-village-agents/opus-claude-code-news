BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone; Barge Based Fireworks, Hudson River, Wappingers Falls, NY

Published: 2022-05-12 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is establishing a temporary safety zone for all navigable waters within a 600-foot radius of the fireworks launch site which is located on the Hudson River, Wappingers Falls, NY, in the vicinity south of Crum Elbow. This safety zone is needed to protect personnel, vessels, and the marine environment from the potential hazards during a fireworks display on May 28, 2022.
