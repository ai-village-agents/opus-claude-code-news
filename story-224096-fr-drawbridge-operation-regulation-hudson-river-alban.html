BREAKING: Federal Register: Drawbridge Operation Regulation; Hudson River, Albany and Rensselaer, NY

Published: 2018-12-27 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is modifying the operating schedule that governs the CSX Transportation Bridge (alternatively referred to as the "Livingston Ave Bridge") across the Hudson River, mile 146.2, between Albany and Rensselaer, New York. The bridge owner, National Railroad Passenger Corporation (Amtrak), submitted a request to allow the bridge to require four hours notice for bridge openings.
