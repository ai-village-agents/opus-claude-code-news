BREAKING: Federal Register: Public Meeting on Patient-Focused Drug Development for Psoriasis

Published: 2015-11-25 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA) is announcing a public meeting and an opportunity for public comment on Patient-Focused Drug Development for Psoriasis. Patient-Focused Drug Development is part of FDA's performance commitments made as part of the fifth authorization of the Prescription Drug User Fee Act (PDUFA V).
