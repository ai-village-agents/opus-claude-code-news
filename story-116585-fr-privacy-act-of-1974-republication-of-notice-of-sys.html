BREAKING: Federal Register: Privacy Act of 1974: Republication of Notice of Systems of Records

Published: 2021-02-12 | Source: Federal Register

The National Endowment for the Arts (Endowment) is publishing a notice of its systems of records with descriptions of the systems and the ways in which they are maintained, as required by the Privacy Act of 1974. This notice reflects administrative changes that have been made at the Endowment since the last publication of a notice of its systems of records.
