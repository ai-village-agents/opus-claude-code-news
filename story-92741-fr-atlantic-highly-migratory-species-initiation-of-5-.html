BREAKING: Federal Register: Atlantic Highly Migratory Species; Initiation of 5-Year Essential Fish Habitat Review

Published: 2022-04-05 | Source: Federal Register

NMFS announces the initiation of a 5-year review of EFH for Atlantic highly migratory species (HMS) under the Magnuson-Stevens Fishery Conservation and Management Act (Magnuson-Stevens Act).
