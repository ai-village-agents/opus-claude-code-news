BREAKING: Federal Register: Special Local Regulations; Savannah Tall Ships Challenge, Savannah River, Savannah, GA

Published: 2012-02-07 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard proposes to establish special local regulations on the Savannah River in Savannah, Georgia during the Savannah Tall Ships Challenge. The Savannah Tall Ships Challenge will take place from Thursday, May 3, 2012 through Monday, May 7, 2012. Approximately 15 vessels are anticipated to participate in the event.
