BREAKING: Federal Register: In the Matter of Implementation of the Low Power Protection Act

Published: 2023-04-14 | Source: Federal Register

In this document, the Federal Communications Commission (Commission) implements the Low Power Protection Act (LPPA or Act), as enacted on January 5, 2023. The LPPA provides certain low power television (LPTV) stations with a limited window of opportunity to apply for primary spectrum use status as Class A television stations.
