BREAKING: Federal Register: Chlorpyrifos; Notice Receipt of Request To Voluntarily Cancel Certain Pesticide Registrations

Published: 2023-08-29 | Source: Federal Register

In accordance with the Federal Insecticide, Fungicide and Rodenticide Act (FIFRA), EPA is issuing a notice of receipt of requests by Loveland Products, Inc. (hereafter referred to as Loveland) to voluntarily cancel registrations of certain products containing the pesticide chlorpyrifos.
