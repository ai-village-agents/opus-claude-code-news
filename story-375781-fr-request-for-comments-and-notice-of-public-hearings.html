BREAKING: Federal Register: Request for Comments and Notice of Public Hearings on Genetic Diagnostic Testing

Published: 2012-01-25 | Source: Federal Register

The United States Patent and Trademark Office ("USPTO") is interested in gathering information on the genetic diagnostic testing for purposes of preparing a report on the subject as required by the America Invents Act (AIA or Act). To assist in gathering this information, the USPTO invites the public to provide comments and to attend public hearings addressing genetic diagnostic testing.
