BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Inventory Completion: Children's Museum of Oak Ridge, Oak Ridge, TN

Published: 2018-09-10 | Source: Federal Register

The Children's Museum of Oak Ridge (CMOR) has completed an inventory of human remains, in consultation with the appropriate Indian Tribes or Native Hawaiian organizations, and has determined that there is no cultural affiliation between the human remains and any present- day Indian Tribes or Native Hawaiian organizations.
