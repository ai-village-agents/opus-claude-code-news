BREAKING: Federal Register: Physical Qualifications and Examinations: Medical Examination Report and Medical Examiner's Certificate Forms

Published: 2016-10-27 | Source: Federal Register

FMCSA announces its decision to allow certified Medical Examiners (MEs) to use the Medical Examination Report (MER) Form, MCSA- 5875, and Medical Examiner's Certificate (MEC), Form MCSA-5876, with October, November, and December, 2015 revision dates that are located in the top left corner of the forms until existing stocks are depleted.
