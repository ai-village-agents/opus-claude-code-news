BREAKING: Federal Register: Lease and Interchange of Vehicles; Motor Carriers of Passengers

Published: 2018-09-20 | Source: Federal Register

FMCSA proposes to amend its May 27, 2015, Lease and Interchange of Vehicles; Motor Carriers of Passengers final rule in response to petitions for rulemaking and extend the January 1, 2019, compliance date to January 1, 2021.
