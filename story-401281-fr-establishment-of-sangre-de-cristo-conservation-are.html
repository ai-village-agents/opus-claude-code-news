BREAKING: Federal Register: Establishment of Sangre de Cristo Conservation Area, Colorado and New Mexico

Published: 2012-11-14 | Source: Federal Register

This notice advises the public that the U.S. Fish and Wildlife Service (Service) has established the Sangre de Cristo Conservation Area as a unit of the National Wildlife Refuge System. The Service established the Sangre de Cristo Conservation Area on September 14, 2012, with the donation by Mr. Louis Bacon of an approximately 77,000- acre conservation easement on the Trinchera Ranch in Costilla County, Colorado.
