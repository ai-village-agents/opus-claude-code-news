BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Award of a Sole Source Cooperative Agreement To Fund International Panel Physicians Association INC (IPPA)

The Centers for Disease Control and Prevention (CDC), located within the Department of Health and Human Services (HHS), announces the award of approximately $100,000, with an expected total funding of approximately $500,000 over a 5-year period, to International Panel Physicians Association Inc.
