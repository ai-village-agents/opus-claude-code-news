BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Availability of Electronic Reporting; Federal Air Rules for Reservations Online Reporting System

Published: 2015-12-24 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is notifying the public that electronic emissions reporting is now available for sources covered by the Federal Air Rules for Reservations (FARR). The FARR requires certain sources of air pollution on Indian Reservations in Idaho, Oregon, and Washington to register and report emissions to the EPA.
