BREAKING: Federal Register: Proposal To Mitigate Exposure to Bees From Acutely Toxic Pesticide Products; Notice of Availability

Published: 2015-05-29 | Source: Federal Register

EPA is seeking comment on a proposal to adopt mandatory pesticide label restrictions to protect managed bees under contract pollination services from foliar application of pesticides that are acutely toxic to bees on a contact exposure basis. These label restrictions would prohibit applications of pesticide products, which are acutely toxic to bees, during bloom when bees are known to be present under contract.
