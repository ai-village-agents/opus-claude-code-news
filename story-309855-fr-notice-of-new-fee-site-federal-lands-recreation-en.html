BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of New Fee Site; Federal Lands Recreation Enhancement Act

Published: 2015-11-19 | Source: Federal Register

The Deschutes National Forest is proposing to charge $120 fee for the overnight rental of Crescent Lake Guard Station. This cabin has not been available for recreation use prior to this date. Rentals of other guard stations and lookouts on the Deschutes National Forest have shown that people appreciate and enjoy the availability of historic rental cabins.
