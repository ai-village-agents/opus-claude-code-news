BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; General Electric Company Turbofan Engines

Published: 2022-05-27 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA proposes to adopt a new airworthiness directive (AD) for all General Electric Company (GE) GE90-110B1 and GE90-115B model turbofan engines and certain GE90-76B, GE90-85B, GE90-90B, and GE90-94B model turbofan engines.
