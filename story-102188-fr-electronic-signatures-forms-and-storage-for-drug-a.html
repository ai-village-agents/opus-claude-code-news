BREAKING: Federal Register: Electronic Signatures, Forms and Storage for Drug and Alcohol Testing Records

Published: 2022-08-05 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Transportation (DOT) requests public comment on how its regulations for conducting workplace drug and alcohol testing for the federally regulated transportation industry could be amended to allow electronic signatures on documents required to be created and utilized under the regulations, to be able to use electronic versions of forms, and to electronically store forms and data.
