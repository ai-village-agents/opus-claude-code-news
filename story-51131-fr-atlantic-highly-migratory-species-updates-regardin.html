BREAKING: Federal Register: Atlantic Highly Migratory Species; Updates Regarding Sea Turtle Careful Release Equipment and Techniques

Published: 2024-10-29 | Source: Federal Register

In this final rule, NMFS updates the Atlantic highly migratory species (HMS) regulations regarding the sea turtle safe handling and release requirements and equipment in the HMS pelagic and bottom longline fisheries. This action is based in part on two technical memoranda published by NMFS' Southeast Fisheries Science Center (SEFSC).
