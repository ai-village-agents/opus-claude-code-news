BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice To Reestablish the Transit Advisory Committee for Safety Charter

Published: 2022-01-18 | Source: Federal Register

The Federal Transit Administration (FTA) announces the intent to reestablish the Transit Advisory Committee for Safety (TRACS). The previous TRACS charter expired on September 12, 2020. The U.S. Department of Transportation and FTA are committed to ensuring the safety of the Nation's public transportation systems and reducing the safety risk to both riders and transit workers.
