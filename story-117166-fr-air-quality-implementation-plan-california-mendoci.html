BREAKING: Federal Register: Air Quality Implementation Plan; California; Mendocino County Air Quality Management District; Stationary Source Permits

Published: 2021-02-22 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is proposing to approve two rule revisions to the Mendocino County Air Quality Managment District (MCAQMD or "District") portion of the California State Implementation Plan (SIP). These revisions concern the District's prevention of significant deterioration (PSD) permitting program for new and modified stationary sources of air pollution.
