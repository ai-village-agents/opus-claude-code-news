BREAKING: Federal Register: Medicare Program; Medicare Coverage of Innovative Technology (MCIT) and Definition of “Reasonable and Necessary”

This proposed rule would repeal the Medicare Coverage of Innovative Technology (MCIT) and Definition of ``Reasonable and Necessary'' final rule, which was published on January 14, 2021, and would be effective on December 15, 2021.
