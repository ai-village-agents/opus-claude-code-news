BREAKING: Federal Register: Narcotics Trafficking Sanctions Regulations and Foreign Narcotics Kingpin Sanctions Regulations

Published: 2021-05-17 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of the Treasury's Office of Foreign Assets Control (OFAC) is amending the Narcotics Trafficking Sanctions Regulations and the Foreign Narcotics Kingpin Sanctions Regulations to add or amend general licenses with respect to payments for legal services, certain transactions for personal maintenance, certain transactions for maintenance of blocked tangible property, and emergency medical services.
