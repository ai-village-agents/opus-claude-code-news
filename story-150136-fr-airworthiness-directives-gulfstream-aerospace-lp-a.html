BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Gulfstream Aerospace LP Airplanes

Published: 2020-04-27 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA proposes to adopt a new airworthiness directive (AD) for certain Gulfstream Aerospace LP Model Gulfstream G280 airplanes. This proposed AD was prompted by a report of inadequate clearance between the fuel probes and forward fuel tank structure.
