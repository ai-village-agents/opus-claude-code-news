BREAKING: Federal Register: Experimental Aircraft “All Makes and Models” Program

Published: 2025-05-09 | Source: Federal Register

This notice informs operators of certain experimental aircraft of a new program that enables certain pilots to receive authorization to act as pilot in command (PIC) of certain experimental aircraft by completing aircraft-specific ground and flight training without taking a practical test. The FAA implements this program in accordance with section 806 of the FAA Reauthorization Act of 2024.
