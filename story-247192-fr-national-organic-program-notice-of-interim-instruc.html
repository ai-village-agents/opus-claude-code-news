BREAKING: Federal Register: National Organic Program: Notice of Interim Instruction, Maintaining the Integrity of Organic Imports

Published: 2017-10-25 | Source: Federal Register

The Agricultural Marketing Service (AMS) is announcing the availability of an interim instruction document intended for use by USDA-accredited organic certifying agents (certifiers). The interim instruction is entitled as follows: Maintaining the Integrity of Organic Imports (NOP 4013). This interim instruction explains the USDA organic regulations' current requirements for certifiers engaged in the oversight of organic products imported into the United States.
