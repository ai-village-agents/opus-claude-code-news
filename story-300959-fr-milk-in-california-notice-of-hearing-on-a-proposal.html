BREAKING: Federal Register: Milk in California; Notice of Hearing on a Proposal To Establish a Federal Milk Marketing Order

Published: 2015-08-06 | Source: Federal Register

A public hearing is being held to consider and take evidence on a proposal that would establish a Federal milk marketing order to regulate the handling of milk in California. The proposed marketing area would incorporate the entire state of California. USDA received four proposals from interested parties, some that include certain milk pricing and pooling provisions not found in current Federal milk orders.
