BREAKING: Federal Register: Indorsement and Payment of Checks Drawn on the United States Treasury

Published: 2023-02-01 | Source: Federal Register

The Bureau of the Fiscal Service (Fiscal Service) at the Department of the Treasury (Treasury) is proposing to amend its regulations governing the payment of checks drawn on the United States Treasury. Specifically, to prevent Treasury checks from being negotiated after cancellation by Treasury or a payment certifying agency--also known as payments over cancellation (POCs)--Fiscal Service is proposing amendments that would require financial institutions use the Treasury Check Verification System...
