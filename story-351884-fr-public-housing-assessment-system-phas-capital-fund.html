BREAKING: Federal Register: Public Housing Assessment System (PHAS): Capital Fund Final Scoring Notice

Published: 2013-04-11 | Source: Federal Register

This notice makes final an interim notice that advised public housing agencies (PHAs), as well as members of the public, that HUD intended to award 5 points for the occupancy sub-indicator of the Capital Fund indicator to all PHAs for the Capital Fund Indicator under the PHAS interim rule published February 23, 2011.
