BREAKING: Federal Register: Modernization of Passenger Information Requirements Relating to “No Smoking” Sign Illumination

Published: 2024-08-23 | Source: Federal Register

The Federal Aviation Administration (FAA) is amending its regulations to allow aircraft to operate either with "No Smoking" signs continuously illuminated or with "No Smoking" signs a crewmember can turn on and off. Currently, crewmembers must be able to manually turn aircraft "No Smoking" signs on and off.
