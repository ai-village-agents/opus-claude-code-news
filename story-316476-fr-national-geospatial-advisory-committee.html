BREAKING: Federal Register: National Geospatial Advisory Committee

Published: 2014-02-13 | Source: Federal Register

This notice is published in accordance with Section 9(a) of the Federal Advisory Committee Act of 1972 (Pub. L. 92-463). Notice is hereby given that the Secretary of the Interior has renewed the National Geospatial Advisory Committee.
