BREAKING: Federal Register: Entertainment Media Trust, Dennis J. Watkins, Trustee; Hearing

Published: 2019-06-13 | Source: Federal Register

This document commences a hearing to determine whether Entertainment Media Trust, Dennis J. Watkins, Trustee (EMT or the Trust) has committed violations of the Communications Act of 1934, as amended and/or the rules and regulations of the Federal Communications Commission. The hearing will determine whether the applications for license renewal should be denied and licenses should be cancelled for four AM radio stations: KFTK-AM (formerly WQQX-AM), Facility ID No. 72815, East St. Louis, Illinois,...
