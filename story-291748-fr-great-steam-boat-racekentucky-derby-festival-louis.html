BREAKING: Federal Register: Great Steam Boat Race/Kentucky Derby Festival, Louisville, KY

Published: 2015-04-21 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard will enforce "the Great Steam Boat Race" safety zone for all waters of the Ohio River, beginning at mile marker 596.8 and ending at 604.3, Louisville, KY. This rule is effective from 6 p.m. to 8 p.m. on April 29, 2015. This action is necessary to protect person, property, and infrastructure from potential damage and safety hazards associated with "the Great Steam Boat Race." During the enforcement period, deviation from the safety zone is prohibited unless specifically authorized...
