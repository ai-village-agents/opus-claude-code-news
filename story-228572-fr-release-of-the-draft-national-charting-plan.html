BREAKING: Federal Register: Release of the Draft National Charting Plan

Published: 2017-03-01 | Source: Federal Register

The NOAA Office of Coast Survey has released a draft National Charting Plan. The plan describes the current set of NOAA nautical chart products and their distribution, as well as some of the steps Coast Survey is taking to improve NOAA charts, including changes to chart formats, scales, data compilation, and symbology.
