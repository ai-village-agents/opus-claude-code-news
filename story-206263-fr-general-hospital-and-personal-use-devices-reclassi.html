BREAKING: Federal Register: General Hospital and Personal Use Devices; Reclassification of Sharps Needle Destruction Device

Published: 2018-05-04 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA or the Agency) is issuing a final order to reclassify the needle destruction device, renaming the device to "sharps needle destruction device," a postamendments class III device (regulated under product code MTV), into class II (special controls), subject to premarket notification.
