BREAKING: Federal Register: Public Scoping for Environmental Impact Statement-Real Estate Transaction and Related Utilities Infrastructure Upgrade at U.S. Army Garrison-West Point and Vicinity

Published: 2022-07-15 | Source: Federal Register

Pursuant to the National Environmental Policy Act of 1969 (NEPA), as implemented by Council on Environmental Quality regulations, the Department of the Army announces its intent to prepare an environmental impact statement (EIS) to analyze a real estate transaction and a related utilities infrastructure upgrade.
