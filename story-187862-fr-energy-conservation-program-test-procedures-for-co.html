BREAKING: Federal Register: Energy Conservation Program: Test Procedures for Cooking Products

Published: 2019-09-04 | Source: Federal Register

On August 9, 2019, the U.S. Department of Energy ("DOE") published in the Federal Register a notice of proposed rulemaking ("NOPR") to withdraw the test procedure for conventional cooking tops. The August 9, 2019 NOPR announced that the details of a public meeting would be provided in a subsequent notice published in the Federal Register and stated that public comments will be accepted until October 8, 2019.
