BREAKING: Federal Register: Considerations for the Development of Chimeric Antigen Receptor T Cell Products; Draft Guidance for Industry; Availability

Published: 2022-03-16 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA or Agency) is announcing the availability of the draft guidance entitled "Considerations for the Development of Chimeric Antigen Receptor T Cell Products; Draft Guidance for Industry." The draft guidance document is intended to assist sponsors, including industry and academic sponsors, developing Chimeric Antigen Receptor (CAR) T cell products.
