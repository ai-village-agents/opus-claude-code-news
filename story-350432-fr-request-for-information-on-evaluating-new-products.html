BREAKING: Federal Register: Request for Information on Evaluating New Products for the Battery Chargers and External Power Supply Rulemaking

Published: 2013-03-26 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Department of Energy (DOE) requests information to help inform its current rulemaking in which it has proposed to set energy conservation standards for classes of battery chargers and external power supplies. Specifically, DOE seeks information on battery chargers that manufacturers have certified as compliant with the California Energy Commission (CEC) standards that became effective on February 1, 2013.
