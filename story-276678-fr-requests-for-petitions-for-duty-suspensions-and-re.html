BREAKING: Federal Register: Requests for Petitions for Duty Suspensions and Reductions

Published: 2016-10-14 | Source: Federal Register

As required by section 3(b)(1) of the American Manufacturing Competitiveness Act of 2016, the Commission is publishing notice requesting members of the public who can demonstrate that they are likely beneficiaries of duty suspensions or reductions to submit petitions for duty suspensions and reductions.
