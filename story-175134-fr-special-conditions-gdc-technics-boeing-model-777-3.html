BREAKING: Federal Register: Special Conditions: GDC Technics, Boeing Model 777-300ER Airplane; the Use of Single-Passenger Side-Facing Seats Equipped With Multiple Airbag Systems

Published: 2019-03-27 | Source: Federal Register

These special conditions are issued for the Boeing Model 777- 300ER airplane. This airplane, as modified by GDC Technics, has novel or unusual design features when compared to the state of technology envisioned in the airworthiness standards for transport category airplanes.
