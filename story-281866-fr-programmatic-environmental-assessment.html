BREAKING: Federal Register: Programmatic Environmental Assessment

Published: 2016-12-19 | Source: Federal Register

NOAA is issuing this notice to advise the public that NOS is preparing a programmatic environmental assessment (PEA) in accordance with the National Environmental Policy Act (NEPA) to evaluate the environmental impacts of NOS hydroacoustic surveys, mapping, and other related data gathering activities.
