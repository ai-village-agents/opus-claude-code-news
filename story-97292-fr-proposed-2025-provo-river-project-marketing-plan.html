BREAKING: Federal Register: Proposed 2025 Provo River Project Marketing Plan

Published: 2022-06-01 | Source: Federal Register

Western Area Power Administration (WAPA), a federal Power Marketing Administration of the Department of Energy (DOE), is seeking comments on this proposed 2025 Provo River Project (PRP) Marketing Plan, including the general power marketing criteria to be used as the basis for marketing the hydroelectric generation of the PRP. The current PRP Marketing Plan expires September 30, 2024, and the proposed 2025 PRP Marketing Plan would take effect October 1, 2024.
