BREAKING: Federal Register: South Mt. Baker-Snoqualmie Resource Advisory Committee

Published: 2012-08-21 | Source: Federal Register

The South Mt. Baker-Snoqualmie (MBS) Resource Advisory Committee (RAC) will meet in North Bend, Washington on September 7, 2012. The committee is authorized under the Secure Rural Schools and Community Self-Determination Act (Pub. L. 112-141) (the Act) and operates in compliance with the Federal Advisory Committee Act.
