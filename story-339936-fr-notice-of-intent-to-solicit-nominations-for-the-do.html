BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Intent To Solicit Nominations for the Dominguez-Escalante National Conservation Area Advisory Council

Published: 2014-11-18 | Source: Federal Register

The Secretary of the Interior (Secretary) was directed by the Omnibus Public Lands Management Act of 2009 to establish the Dominguez- Escalante National Conservation Area (D-E NCA) Advisory Council (Council). The 10-member Council was formed in December 2010 to provide recommendations to the Secretary through the Bureau of Land Management (BLM) during the development of a resource management plan (RMP) for the D-E NCA.
