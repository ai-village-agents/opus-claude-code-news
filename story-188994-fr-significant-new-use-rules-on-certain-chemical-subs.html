BREAKING: Federal Register: Significant New Use Rules on Certain Chemical Substances

Published: 2019-09-18 | Source: Federal Register

EPA is issuing significant new use rules (SNURs) under the Toxic Substances Control Act (TSCA) for 26 chemical substances which are the subject of premanufacture notices (PMNs) and deferring action on one chemical substance. The chemical substances are subject to Orders issued by EPA pursuant to section 5(e) of TSCA.
