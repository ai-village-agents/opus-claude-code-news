BREAKING: Federal Register: National Organic Program; Periodic Residue Testing

Published: 2012-11-09 | Source: Federal Register

This final rule clarifies a provision of the Organic Foods Production Act of 1990 and the regulations issued thereunder that requires periodic residue testing of organically produced agricultural products by accredited certifying agents.
