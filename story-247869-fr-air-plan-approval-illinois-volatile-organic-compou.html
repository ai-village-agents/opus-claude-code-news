BREAKING: Federal Register: Air Plan Approval; Illinois; Volatile Organic Compounds Definition

Published: 2017-11-02 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is proposing to approve a state submission as a revision to the Illinois state implementation plan (SIP) for ozone. The revision, submitted on May 30, 2017, incorporates changes to the Illinois Administrative Code definition of volatile organic material, otherwise known as volatile organic compounds (VOC).
