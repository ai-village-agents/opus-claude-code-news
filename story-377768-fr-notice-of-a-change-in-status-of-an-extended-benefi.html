BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of a Change in Status of an Extended Benefit (EB) Period for New Mexico

Published: 2012-02-15 | Source: Federal Register

This notice announces a change in status of the payable periods under the EB program for New Mexico. The following change has occurred since the publication of the last notice regarding the State's EB status: The Federal authorization to have a three year look-back was recently extended to February 29, 2012.
