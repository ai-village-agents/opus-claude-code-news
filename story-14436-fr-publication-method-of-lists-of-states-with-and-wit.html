BREAKING: Federal Register: Publication Method of Lists of States With and Without State Meat or Poultry Inspection Programs

Published: 2025-06-26 | Source: Federal Register

FSIS is amending the Federal meat and poultry products inspection regulations to remove its lists of states that do not operate their own meat or poultry inspection (MPI) programs that are "at least equal to" FSIS' Federal inspection programs and have therefore been designated for FSIS' Federal inspection. FSIS is also amending the regulations to remove or revise related cross references to the lists. Going forward, FSIS will maintain lists of states with and without MPI programs on its website ...
