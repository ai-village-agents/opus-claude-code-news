BREAKING: Federal Register: Environmental Assessment and Habitat Conservation Plan; Heart of Texas Wind Project; McCulloch County, Texas

Published: 2017-03-27 | Source: Federal Register

Heart of Texas Wind, LLC (applicant), has applied to the U.S. Fish and Wildlife Service (Service) for an incidental take permit under the Endangered Species Act of 1973, as amended. If granted, the permit would be in effect for 30 years and would authorize incidental take of the black-capped vireo (covered species), a bird listed as endangered under the Act.
