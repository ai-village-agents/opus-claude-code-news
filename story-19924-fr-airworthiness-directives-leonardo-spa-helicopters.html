BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Leonardo S.p.A. Helicopters

Published: 2025-09-10 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA is adopting a new airworthiness directive (AD) for all Leonardo S.p.A. Model A109E, A109S, AW109SP, A119, and AW119 MKII helicopters. This AD was prompted by a report that certain manufacturer maintenance instructions for swashplate assembly installation specified incorrect torque values for certain swashplate nuts.
