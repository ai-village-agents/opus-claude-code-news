BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Public Meeting of the Virginia Advisory Committee; Cancellation

Published: 2023-02-28 | Source: Federal Register

The Commission on Civil Rights published a notice in the Federal Register concerning a virtual business meeting of the Virginia Advisory Committee. The meeting scheduled for Tuesday, February 28, 2023, at 12:00 p.m. (ET) is cancelled. The notice is in the Federal Register of Monday, February 13, 2023, in FR Doc. 2023-02998 in the first and second columns of page 9226.
