BREAKING: Federal Register: Interim Staff Guidance Specific Environmental Guidance for Integral Pressurized Water Reactors Reviews

Published: 2013-09-13 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S Nuclear Regulatory Commission (NRC) staff is issuing for use of, and to solicit public comment on, draft Interim Staff Guidance (ISG) ESP/COL-ISG-027, "Interim Staff Guidance Specific Environmental Guidance for iPWR Reviews."
