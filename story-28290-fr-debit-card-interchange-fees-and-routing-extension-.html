BREAKING: Federal Register: Debit Card Interchange Fees and Routing; Extension of Comment Period

Published: 2024-01-29 | Source: Federal Register

On November 14, 2023, the Board of Governors of the Federal Reserve System (Board) published in the Federal Register a proposal that would update all three components of Regulation II's interchange fee cap based on the latest data reported to the Board by large debit card issuers, update the interchange fee cap every other year going forward by directly linking the interchange fee cap to data from the Board's biennial survey of large debit card issuers, and implement a set of technical revisions...
