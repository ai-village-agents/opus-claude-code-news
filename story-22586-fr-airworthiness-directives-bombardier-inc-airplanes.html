BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Bombardier, Inc., Airplanes

Published: 2025-11-18 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA is adopting a new airworthiness directive (AD) for all Bombardier, Inc., Model CL-600-2B16 (604 Variant) airplanes. This AD was prompted by reports that the flap system on-board recorder (FSOBR) interfaces with the flap control unit signals and may result in the flap control unit monitors tripping, causing Flap Fail messages and possibly an uncommanded flap movement.
