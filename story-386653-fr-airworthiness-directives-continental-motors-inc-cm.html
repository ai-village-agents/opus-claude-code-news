BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Continental Motors, Inc. (CMI) Reciprocating Engines

Published: 2012-05-24 | Source: Federal Register

We are superseding an existing airworthiness directive (AD) for certain Continental Motors, Inc. (CMI) models TSIO-520, TSIO-550-K, TSIOF-550K, and IO-550-N series reciprocating engines with new or rebuilt CMI starter adapters installed between January 1, 2011 and November 20, 2011. That AD currently requires replacing affected CMI starter adapters with starter adapters eligible for installation.
