BREAKING: Federal Register: Shasta-Trinity National Forest; California; Elk Late-Successional Reserve Enhancement Project

Published: 2013-02-28 | Source: Federal Register

The Forest Service will prepare an environmental impact statement (EIS) to evaluate and disclose the predicted effects of the Elk Late-Successional Reserve Enhancement project, which would treat natural stands and plantations on approximately 2,930 acres to reduce the current and future risk of large-scale disturbance events within early, mid and late-successional habitat within the Elk Flat Late-Successional Reserve and nearby stands.
