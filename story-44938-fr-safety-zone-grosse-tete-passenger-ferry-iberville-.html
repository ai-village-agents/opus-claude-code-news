BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone; Grosse Tete Passenger Ferry, Iberville LA

Published: 2024-08-22 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is establishing a temporary safety zone for all navigable waters within a 300 yard radius of the Grosse Tete passenger ferry at MM 46 of the Port Allen Route, Iberville, LA. The safety zone is needed to protect ferry operations for crossing school students during morning and afternoon commutes as a result of the Grosse Tete bridge closure.
