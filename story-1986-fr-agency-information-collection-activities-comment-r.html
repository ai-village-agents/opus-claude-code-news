BREAKING: Federal Register: Agency Information Collection Activities; Comment Request; Segal Education Award-Related Information Collection Requests

Published: 2026-01-15 | Source: Federal Register

In accordance with the Paperwork Reduction Act of 1995, the Corporation for National and Community Service (operating as AmeriCorps) is proposing to renew three information collections associated with the Segal Education Award: the voucher and payment request form, the forbearance request form, and the interest accrual form.
