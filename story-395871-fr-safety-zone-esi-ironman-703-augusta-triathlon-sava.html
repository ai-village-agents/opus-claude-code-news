BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone, ESI Ironman 70.3 Augusta Triathlon, Savannah River; Augusta, GA

Published: 2012-09-07 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is establishing a temporary safety zone on the waters of the Savannah River in Augusta, Georgia during the ESI Ironman 70.3 Augusta Triathlon on Sunday, September 30, 2012. The event will include a 1.1 mile swim on the waters of the Savannah River. The temporary safety zone is necessary for the safety of the race participants, participant vessels, spectators, and the general public during the swim portion of the competition.
