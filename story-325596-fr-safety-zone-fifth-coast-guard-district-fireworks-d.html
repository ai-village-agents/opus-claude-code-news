BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone, Fifth Coast Guard District Fireworks Display Cape Fear River; Wilmington, NC

Published: 2014-05-29 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is temporarily changing the enforcement location of a safety zone for one specific recurring fireworks display in the Fifth Coast Guard District. This regulation applies to only one recurring fireworks event, held adjacent to the Cape Fear River, Wilmington, North Carolina. The fireworks display formerly originated from a location on land but this year will originate from a barge. The safety zone is necessary to provide for the safety of life on navigable waters during the event....
