BREAKING: Federal Register: Information Collection Request Submitted to OMB for Review and Approval; Comment Request; Cross-Media Electronic Reporting Rule (Renewal)

Published: 2018-07-16 | Source: Federal Register

This is a proposed extension of the ICR, which is currently approved through July 31, 2018.
