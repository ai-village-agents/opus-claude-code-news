BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; MD Helicopters, Inc. (MDHI) Helicopters

Published: 2016-03-11 | Source: Federal Register

We are adopting a new airworthiness directive (AD) for certain MDHI Model 369A (Army OH-6A), 369H, 369HE, 369HM, 369HS, 369D, 369E, 369F, 369FF, and 500N helicopters. This AD requires inspecting the auxiliary fuel pump (fuel pump) wire routing in the left-hand fuel cell and corrective action, if necessary.
