BREAKING: Federal Register: Proposed Establishment of Class E Airspace and Proposed Amendment of Class D Airspace; East Hampton, NY

Published: 2021-07-19 | Source: Federal Register

This action proposes to establish Class E surface airspace for East Hampton Airport, East Hampton, NY, to assist aircraft landing and departing the airport when the air traffic control tower is closed. In addition, this action would amend Class D airspace by replacing the outdated term Airport/Facility Directory with the term Chart Supplement in the airport description and amend the radius and ceiling as the Federal Aviation Administration (FAA) deemed the airspace adjustments were necessary.
