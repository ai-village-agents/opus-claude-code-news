BREAKING: Federal Register: Approval and Promulgation of Implementation Plans; Louisiana; Permitting of Greenhouse Gases

Published: 2016-05-06 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is proposing to partially approve and partially disapprove a revision to the Louisiana State Implementation Plan (SIP) submitted by the State of Louisiana on December 21, 2011. This revision outlines the State's program to regulate and permit emissions of greenhouse gases (GHGs) in the Louisiana Prevention of Significant Deterioration (PSD) program.
