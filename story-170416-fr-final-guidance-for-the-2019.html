BREAKING: Federal Register: Final Guidance for the 2019

Published: 2019-02-04 | Source: Federal Register

The Board and the FDIC (together, the "Agencies") are adopting this final guidance for the 2019 and subsequent resolution plan submissions by the eight largest, complex U.S. banking organizations ("Covered Companies" or "firms"). The final guidance is meant to assist these firms in developing their resolution plans, which are required to be submitted pursuant to the Dodd-Frank Wall Street Reform and Consumer Protection Act ("Dodd-Frank Act").
