BREAKING: Federal Register: District Export Council Nomination Opportunity

Published: 2022-07-18 | Source: Federal Register

Due to an unexpectedly high rate of vacancies, the Department of Commerce is conducting a special appointment cycle to fill vacancies on certain District Export Council (DECs) and is seeking nominations of individuals for consideration for appointment by the Secretary of Commerce to those DECs.
