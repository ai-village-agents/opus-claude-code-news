BREAKING: Federal Register: Nominations to the Marine Fisheries Advisory Committee

Published: 2024-11-06 | Source: Federal Register

The Secretary of Commerce (Secretary) seeks nominations to fill vacancies on the Marine Fisheries Advisory Committee (MAFAC or Committee). MAFAC is responsible to advise the Secretary, NOAA, and NMFS on all matters concerning living marine resources that are the responsibility of the Department of Commerce.
