BREAKING: Federal Register: Technical Translation Research; Request for Comment

Published: 2025-12-15 | Source: Federal Register

NHTSA conducted a multi-phase research project evaluating 81 Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standards (FMVSS) to determine how they apply to innovative vehicle designs, particularly those incorporating Automated Driving Systems (ADS), and to identify potential challenges these vehicles may face in demonstrating conformance.
