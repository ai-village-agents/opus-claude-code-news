BREAKING: Federal Register: Product-Specific Bioequivalence Recommendations; Draft and Revised Draft Guidances for Industry; Availability

Published: 2016-01-28 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA or Agency) is announcing the availability of additional draft and revised draft product-specific bioequivalence (BE) recommendations. The recommendations provide product-specific guidance on the design of BE studies to support abbreviated new drug applications (ANDAs).
