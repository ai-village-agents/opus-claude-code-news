BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice for Suspension of Small Area Fair Market Rent (Small Area FMR) Designations; Solicitation of Comment

Published: 2017-12-12 | Source: Federal Register

On August 10, 2017, pursuant to the authority provided in regulation, HUD suspended for two years the designation for the mandatory use of Small Area Fair Market Rents (Small Area FMRs) for certain metropolitan areas that had previously been designated for Small Area FMR use (Suspension). After HUD provided notice of this suspension, interested persons requested an opportunity to review and comment on the Suspension.
