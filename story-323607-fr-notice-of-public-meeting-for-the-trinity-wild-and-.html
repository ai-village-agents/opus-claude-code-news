BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Public Meeting for the Trinity Wild and Scenic River Withdrawal

Published: 2014-05-07 | Source: Federal Register

Pursuant to the Federal Land Policy and Management Act (FLPMA) (43 U.S.C. 1714) this notice schedules a public meeting to be conducted to provide information and solicit comments on the proposed withdrawal of lands along the Trinity Wild and Scenic River (TRWSR) in Trinity County, California.
