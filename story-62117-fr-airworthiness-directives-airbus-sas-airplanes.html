BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Airbus SAS Airplanes

Published: 2023-03-08 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA proposes to adopt a new airworthiness directive (AD) for all Airbus SAS Model A318, A319, A320, and A321 series airplanes. This proposed AD was prompted by reports where the passenger door external handle mechanism was not retrieving its normal, flush position when the door was being closed.
