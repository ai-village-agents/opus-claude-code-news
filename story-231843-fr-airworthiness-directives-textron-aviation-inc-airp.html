BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Textron Aviation Inc. Airplanes

Published: 2017-04-12 | Source: Federal Register

We propose to adopt a new airworthiness directive (AD) for all Textron Aviation Inc. Models A36TC and B36TC airplanes. This proposed AD was prompted by a fatal accident where the exhaust tailpipe fell off during takeoff.
