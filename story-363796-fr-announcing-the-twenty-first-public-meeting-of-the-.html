BREAKING: Federal Register: Announcing the Twenty First Public Meeting of the Crash Injury Research and Engineering Network (CIREN)

Published: 2013-08-23 | Source: Federal Register

This notice announces the Twenty First Public Meeting of members of the Crash Injury Research and Engineering Network. CIREN is a collaborative effort to conduct research on crashes and injuries at six Level I Trauma Centers across the United States linked by a computer network. The current CIREN model utilizes two types of centers, medical and engineering.
