BREAKING: Federal Register: Amendment of Class E Airspace; Ahoskie, NC

Published: 2024-11-27 | Source: Federal Register

This action proposes to amend Class E airspace extending upward from 700 feet above the surface for Ahoskie, NC, by adding airspace for ECU Health Roanoke Chowan Heliport, Ahoskie, NC. This action also proposes to amend the airport information for Tri-County at Henry Joyner Field Airport by updating the airport name and coordinates.
