BREAKING: Federal Register: U.S. Coral Reef Task Force Public Meeting and Public Comment

Published: 2016-08-15 | Source: Federal Register

We, the U.S. Department of the Interior, announce a public meeting of the U.S. Coral Reef Task Force and a request for written comments. This meeting, the 36th biannual meeting of the task force, provides a forum for coordinated planning and action among Federal agencies, State and territorial governments, and nongovernmental partners.
