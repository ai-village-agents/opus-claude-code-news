BREAKING: Federal Register: Taking and Importing Marine Mammals; Taking Marine Mammals Incidental to the Atlantic Shores Offshore Wind Energy Projects Offshore of New Jersey; Extension of Public Comment Period

Published: 2022-10-28 | Source: Federal Register
