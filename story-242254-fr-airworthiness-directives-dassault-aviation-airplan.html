BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Dassault Aviation Airplanes

Published: 2017-08-25 | Source: Federal Register

We are adopting a new airworthiness directive (AD) for certain Dassault Aviation Model FALCON 7X airplanes. This AD was prompted by a report indicating that, under certain operational takeoff conditions, the available thrust in relation with the N1 indication is less than a certified value, which could affect the safety margins with an engine failure during takeoff.
