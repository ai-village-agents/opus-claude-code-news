BREAKING: Federal Register: Cellular Service, Including Changes in Licensing of Unserved Area

Published: 2014-12-05 | Source: Federal Register

In this Report and Order ("R&O"), the Federal Communications Commission ("Commission") adopts new and revised rules governing the 800 MHz Cellular ("Cellular") Service, changing the licensing model from site-based to geographic-based and eliminating numerous filing requirements while preserving direct access to area not yet licensed ("Unserved Area").
