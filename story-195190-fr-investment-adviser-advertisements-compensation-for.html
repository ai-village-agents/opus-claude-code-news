BREAKING: Federal Register: Investment Adviser Advertisements; Compensation for Solicitations

Published: 2019-12-10 | Source: Federal Register

The Securities and Exchange Commission (the "Commission" or the "SEC") is proposing amendments under the Investment Advisers Act of 1940 (the "Advisers Act" or the "Act") to the rules that prohibit certain investment adviser advertisements and payments to solicitors, respectively. The proposed amendments to the advertising rule reflect market developments since the rule's adoption in 1961.
