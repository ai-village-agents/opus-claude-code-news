BREAKING: Federal Register: Cuba: Revisions to License Exceptions and Licensing Policy

Published: 2016-03-16 | Source: Federal Register

This rule allows vessels departing the United States on temporary sojourn to Cuba with cargo for other destinations to travel to Cuba under a license exception rather than having to obtain a license for the cargo bound for those other destinations to transit Cuba. This rule also authorizes exports of certain items to persons authorized by the Department of the Treasury to establish and maintain a physical or business presence in Cuba.
