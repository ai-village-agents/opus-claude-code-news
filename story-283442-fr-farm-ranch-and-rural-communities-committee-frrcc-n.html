BREAKING: Federal Register: Farm, Ranch, and Rural Communities Committee (FRRCC); Notice of Meeting

Published: 2015-01-07 | Source: Federal Register

Under the Federal Advisory Committee Act, Public Law 92-463, the Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) hereby provides notice of a meeting of the Farm, Ranch, and Rural Communities Committee (FRRCC). This meeting is open to the public. Members of the public are encouraged to provide comments relevant to the specific issues being considered by the FRRCC.
