BREAKING: Federal Register: Public Land Order No. 7806; Partial Revocation of Secretarial Order Dated September 24, 1942; Alaska

Published: 2012-11-26 | Source: Federal Register

This order revokes a Secretarial Order insofar as it affects approximately 1,905 acres of public lands withdrawn on behalf of the Federal Aviation Administration for Air Navigation Site No. 189 at Farewell, Alaska. The Federal Aviation Administration no longer needs the lands for air navigation purposes.
