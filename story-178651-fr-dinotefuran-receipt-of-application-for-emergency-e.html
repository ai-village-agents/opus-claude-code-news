BREAKING: Federal Register: Dinotefuran; Receipt of Application for Emergency Exemptions, Solicitation of Public Comment

Published: 2019-05-08 | Source: Federal Register

EPA has received specific exemption requests from the Virginia Department of Agriculture and Consumer Services (VDACS) to use the insecticide dinotefuran (CAS No. 165252-70-0) to treat up to 29,000 acres of pome fruits and stone fruits to control the brown marmorated stinkbug.
