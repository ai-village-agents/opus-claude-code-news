BREAKING: Federal Register: Withdrawal of Approval for Use of Phosphogypsum in Road Construction

Published: 2021-07-07 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is withdrawing its October 14, 2020 approval for use of phosphogypsum in government road projects. Under the Clean Air Act, the EPA may approve a request for other use of phosphogypsum if it includes certain prescribed information. Upon further review, EPA has determined that the approval was premature and should be withdrawn because the request did not contain all of the required information.
