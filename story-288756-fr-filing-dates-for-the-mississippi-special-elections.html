BREAKING: Federal Register: Filing Dates for the Mississippi Special Elections in the 1st Congressional District

Published: 2015-03-16 | Source: Federal Register

Mississippi has scheduled a Special General Election on May 12, 2015, to fill the U.S. House of Representatives seat held by the late Representative Alan Nunnelee. Under Mississippi law, a majority winner in a nonpartisan special election is declared elected. Should no candidate achieve a majority vote, a Special Runoff Election will be held on June 2, 2015, between the top two vote-getters.
