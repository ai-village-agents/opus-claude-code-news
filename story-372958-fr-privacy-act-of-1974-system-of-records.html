BREAKING: Federal Register: Privacy Act of 1974; System of Records

Published: 2013-12-19 | Source: Federal Register

As required by the Privacy Act of 1974, 5 U.S.C. 552a(e), notice is hereby given that the Department of Veterans Affairs (VA) is amending the system of records currently titled "Income Verification Records--VA" (89VA16) as set forth in the Federal Register (73 FR 26192-26197), dated May 8, 2008. VA is amending the System Number, System Location, Access, Routine Uses of Records Maintained in the System, Storage, Safeguards, and Records Source Categories.
