BREAKING: Federal Register: Determination That NASCOBAL (Cyanocobalamin) Nasal Spray, 0.5 Milligram/Spray, Was Not Withdrawn From Sale for Reasons of Safety or Effectiveness

Published: 2025-04-09 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA, Agency, or we) has determined that NASCOBAL (cyanocobalamin) nasal spray, 0.5 milligram (mg)/spray, was not withdrawn from sale for reasons of safety or effectiveness.
