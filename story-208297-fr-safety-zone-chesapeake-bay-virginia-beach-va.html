BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone; Chesapeake Bay, Virginia Beach, VA

Published: 2018-05-31 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard will enforce a safety zone for a fireworks display taking place over Chesapeake Bay, Virginia Beach, VA, on July 3, 2018. This action is necessary to ensure safety of life on navigable waterways during the fireworks display. Our regulation for Recurring Marine Events within the Fifth Coast Guard District identifies the regulated area for this fireworks display event.
