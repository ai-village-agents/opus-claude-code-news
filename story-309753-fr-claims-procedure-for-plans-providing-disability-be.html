BREAKING: Federal Register: Claims Procedure for Plans Providing Disability Benefits

Published: 2015-11-18 | Source: Federal Register

This document contains proposed amendments to claims procedure regulations for plans providing disability benefits under the Employee Retirement Income Security Act of 1974 (ERISA). The amendments would revise and strengthen the current rules primarily by adopting certain of the new procedural protections and safeguards made applicable to group health plans by the Affordable Care Act.
