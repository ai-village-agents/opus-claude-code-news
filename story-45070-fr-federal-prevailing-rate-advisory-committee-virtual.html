BREAKING: Federal Register: Federal Prevailing Rate Advisory Committee Virtual Public Meeting

Published: 2024-08-23 | Source: Federal Register

According to the provisions of section 10 of the Federal Advisory Committee Act, notice is hereby given that a virtual meeting of the Federal Prevailing Rate Advisory Committee will be held on Thursday, September 19, 2024. There will be no in-person gathering for this meeting.
