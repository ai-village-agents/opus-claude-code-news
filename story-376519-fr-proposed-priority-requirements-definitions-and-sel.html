BREAKING: Federal Register: Proposed Priority, Requirements, Definitions, and Selection Criteria-Arts in Education National Program (AENP)

Published: 2012-02-02 | Source: Federal Register

The Assistant Deputy Secretary proposes a priority, requirements, definitions, and selection criteria under the Arts in Education National Program (AENP). We may use the priority, requirements, definitions, and selection criteria for competitions in fiscal year (FY) 2012 and later years.
