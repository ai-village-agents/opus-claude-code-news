BREAKING: Federal Register: Energy Conservation Program: Test Procedure for Walk-in Coolers and Walk-in Freezers

Published: 2016-12-28 | Source: Federal Register

This final rule amends the test procedure for certain walk-in cooler and freezer components by improving the procedure's clarity, updating related certification and enforcement provisions to address the performance-based energy conservation standards for walk-in cooler and freezer equipment, and establishing labeling requirements to aid manufacturers in determining compliance with the relevant standards for walk-in cooler and freezer applications.
