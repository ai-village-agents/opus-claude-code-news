BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Intended Repatriation: Sinclair Community College, Dayton, OH

Published: 2026-01-07 | Source: Federal Register

In accordance with the Native American Graves Protection and Repatriation Act (NAGPRA), the Sinclair Community College intends to repatriate a certain cultural item that meets the definition of a sacred object and that has a cultural affiliation with the Indian Tribes or Native Hawaiian organizations in this notice.
