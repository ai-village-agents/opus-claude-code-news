BREAKING: Federal Register: Filing, Indexing and Service Requirements for Oil Pipelines

Published: 2012-10-29 | Source: Federal Register

The Federal Energy Regulatory Commission proposes to amend its regulations under the Interstate Commerce Act.\1\ The Commission proposes to rewrite, remove, and update its regulations governing the form, composition and filing of rates and charges by interstate oil pipelines for transportation in interstate commerce.
