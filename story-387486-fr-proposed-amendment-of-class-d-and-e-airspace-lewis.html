BREAKING: Federal Register: Proposed Amendment of Class D and E Airspace; Lewiston, ID

Published: 2012-06-04 | Source: Federal Register

This action proposes to modify Class E airspace at Lewiston- Nez Perce County Airport, Lewiston, ID. Controlled airspace is necessary to accommodate aircraft using Area Navigation (RNAV) Global Positioning System (GPS) standard instrument approach procedures at Lewiston-Nez Perce County Airport, Lewiston, ID.
