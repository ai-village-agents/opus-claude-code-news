BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone; Ohio River Mile Marker 0.3-1.5, Pittsburgh, PA

Published: 2022-11-22 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is establishing a temporary safety zone for the Ohio River mile marker 0.3-1.5. This action is necessary to provide for the safety of life on these navigable waters during drilling operations from November 28, 2022 until December 3, 2022.
