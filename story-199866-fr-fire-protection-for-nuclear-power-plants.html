BREAKING: Federal Register: Fire Protection for Nuclear Power Plants

Published: 2018-02-13 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Nuclear Regulatory Commission (NRC) is issuing Revision 3 to Regulatory Guide (RG) 1.189, "Fire Protection for Nuclear Power Plants." Revision 3 of RG 1.189 includes administrative changes involving editorial corrections that make the document consistent with existing policy. None of the revisions involve changes to the staff regulatory positions.
