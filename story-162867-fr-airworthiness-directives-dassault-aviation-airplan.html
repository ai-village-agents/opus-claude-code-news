BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Dassault Aviation Airplanes

Published: 2020-10-06 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA is adopting a new airworthiness directive (AD) for certain Dassault Aviation Model FALCON 7X, FALCON 900EX, and FALCON 2000EX airplanes. This AD was prompted by reports of primary display unit (PDU) data flickering on airplanes equipped with EASy software.
