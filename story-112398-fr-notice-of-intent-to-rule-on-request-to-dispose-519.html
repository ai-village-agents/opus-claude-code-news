BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Intent To Rule on Request To Dispose 5.19 Acres of Airport Land at Hanscom Field, Bedford, MA

Published: 2022-12-15 | Source: Federal Register

Notice is being given that the FAA is considering a request from the Massachusetts Port Authority to dispose of 5.19 acres of land at Hanscom Field, Bedford, MA. The disposal of 2 parcels is associated with a land swap with an adjacent property. The 2 parcels are not required for existing or future aviation development and are currently undeveloped.
