BREAKING: Federal Register: Harley-Davidson Motor Company, Inc., Receipt of Petition for Decision of Inconsequential Noncompliance

Published: 2017-04-07 | Source: Federal Register

Harley-Davidson Motor Company, Inc. (Harley-Davidson), has determined that certain model year (MY) 2016-2017 Harley-Davidson XL 1200XC Roadster motorcycles do not fully comply with Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard (FMVSS) No. 120, Tire selection and rims and motor home/recreation vehicle trailer load carrying capacity information for motor vehicles with a GVWR of more than 4,536 kilograms (10,000 pounds).
