BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Availability of the Willow Master Development Plan Final Environmental Impact Statement, Alaska

Published: 2020-08-14 | Source: Federal Register

In accordance with the National Environmental Policy Act of 1969, as amended, the Bureau of Land Management (BLM) has prepared a Final Environmental Impact Statement (EIS) for the Willow Master Development Plan, and by this notice is announcing its publication.
