BREAKING: Federal Register: Privacy Act of 1974; System of Records

Published: 2023-07-28 | Source: Federal Register

In accordance with the Privacy Act of 1974, as amended, the Corporation for National and Community Service (operating as AmeriCorps) proposes to establish a new system of records titled CNCS- 11-CPO-AHB-AmeriCorps Health Benefits System of Records. AmeriCorps will use this system of records to track, store, manage, and evaluate the paper and electronic records associated with the healthcare benefits offered to AmeriCorps VISTA, AmeriCorps NCCC, and AmeriCorps FEMA Corps members (Members).
