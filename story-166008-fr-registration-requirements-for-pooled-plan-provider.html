BREAKING: Federal Register: Registration Requirements for Pooled Plan Providers

Published: 2020-11-16 | Source: Federal Register

This final regulation establishes the requirements for registering with the Department of Labor as a "pooled plan provider" for "pooled employer plans" under the Employee Retirement Income Security Act of 1974, as amended (ERISA).
