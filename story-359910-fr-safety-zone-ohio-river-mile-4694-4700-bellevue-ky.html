BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety zone; Ohio River, Mile 469.4-470.0; Bellevue, KY

Published: 2013-07-11 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is establishing a temporary safety zone for all waters of the Ohio River, surface to bottom, extending from Ohio River mile 469.4 to mile 470.0. This temporary safety zone is necessary to protect commercial and recreational vessels from fireworks fallout associated with the City of Bellevue Beach Park Concert fireworks display.
