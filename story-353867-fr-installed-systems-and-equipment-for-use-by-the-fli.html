BREAKING: Federal Register: Installed Systems and Equipment for Use by the Flightcrew

Published: 2013-05-03 | Source: Federal Register

This rule amends design requirements in the airworthiness standards for transport category airplanes to minimize the occurrence of design-related flightcrew errors. The new design requirements will enable a flightcrew member to detect and manage his or her errors when the errors occur.
