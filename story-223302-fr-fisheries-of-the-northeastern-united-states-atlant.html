BREAKING: Federal Register: Fisheries of the Northeastern United States; Atlantic Mackerel, Squid, and Butterfish; Amendment 20

Published: 2018-12-14 | Source: Federal Register

NMFS approves and implements through regulations measures included in Amendment 20 to the Atlantic Mackerel, Squid, and Butterfish Fishery Management Plan, as adopted by the Mid-Atlantic Fishery Management Council.
