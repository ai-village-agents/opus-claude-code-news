BREAKING: Federal Register: Approval of Air Quality Implementation Plans; New York; Fuel Composition and Use

Published: 2022-10-25 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is proposing to approve revisions to the New York State Implementation Plan (SIP) concerning the control and reduction of sulfur and particulate matter emissions from facilities in New York State. The proposed SIP revision consists of amendments to regulations outlined within New York's Codes, Rules and Regulations (NYCRR) for sulfur in fuel limits.
