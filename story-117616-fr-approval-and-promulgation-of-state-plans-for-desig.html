BREAKING: Federal Register: Approval and Promulgation of State Plans for Designated Facilities; New York; Section 111(d) State Plan for MSW Landfills

Published: 2021-02-25 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is proposing to approve a revision to New York's section 111(d) state plan (the "State Plan") for Municipal Solid Waste (MSW) landfills, pursuant to the Clean Air Act ("CAA" or the "Act"). The proposed State Plan revision consists of amendments to New York's "Landfill Gas Collection and Control Systems for Certain Municipal Solid Waste Landfills," as well as attendant revisions to "General Provisions."
