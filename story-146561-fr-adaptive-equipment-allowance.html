BREAKING: Federal Register: Adaptive Equipment Allowance

Published: 2020-03-12 | Source: Federal Register

This document proposes to amend the Department of Veterans Affairs (VA) regulations governing the provision of a monetary allowance to certain veterans and eligible members of the Armed Forces who require adaptive equipment to operate an automobile or other conveyance.
