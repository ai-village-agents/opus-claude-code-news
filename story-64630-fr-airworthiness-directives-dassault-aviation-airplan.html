BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Dassault Aviation Airplanes

Published: 2023-04-07 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA is superseding Airworthiness Directive (AD) 2021-04- 02, which applied to certain Dassault Aviation Model FALCON 2000EX airplanes. AD 2021-04-02 required revising the existing maintenance or inspection program, as applicable, to incorporate new or more restrictive airworthiness limitations. This AD was prompted by a determination that new or more restrictive airworthiness limitations are necessary.
