BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; CFM International S.A. Turbofan Engines

Published: 2019-09-20 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA proposes to adopt a new airworthiness directive (AD) for all CFM International, S.A. (CFM) CFM56-5B and CFM56-7B model turbofan engines with a certain high-pressure turbine (HPT) inner stationary seal installed. This proposed AD was prompted by cracks found in the rotating air HPT front seal.
