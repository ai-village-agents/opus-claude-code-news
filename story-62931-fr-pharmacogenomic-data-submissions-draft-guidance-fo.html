BREAKING: Federal Register: Pharmacogenomic Data Submissions; Draft Guidance for Industry; Availability

Published: 2023-03-20 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA or Agency) is announcing the availability of a draft guidance for industry entitled "Pharmacogenomic Data Submissions." This draft guidance is intended to facilitate progress in the field of pharmacogenomics and the use of pharmacogenomic data in drug development.
