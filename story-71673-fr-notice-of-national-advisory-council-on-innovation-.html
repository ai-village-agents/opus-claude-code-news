BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of National Advisory Council on Innovation and Entrepreneurship Meeting

Published: 2023-07-11 | Source: Federal Register

The National Advisory Council on Innovation and Entrepreneurship (NACIE) will hold a virtual public meeting on Tuesday, July 27, 2023. In 2022, U.S. Secretary of Commerce Gina M. Raimondo appointed a cohort of 33 members to NACIE, and this will be this cohort's fourth meeting.
