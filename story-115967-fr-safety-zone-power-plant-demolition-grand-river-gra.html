BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone; Power Plant Demolition; Grand River, Grand Haven, MI

Published: 2021-02-04 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is establishing a temporary safety zone for all navigable waters within 1400 feet of a demolition site near the eastern bank of the Grand River in Grand Haven, MI. The safety zone is needed to protect personnel, vessels, and the marine environment from potential hazards created by the controlled implosion of the J.B. Sims power plant.
