BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zones; Recurring Events in Captain of the Port Boston Zone

Published: 2015-06-08 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard will enforce the safety zones in the Captain of the Port Boston Zone on the specified dates and times listed below. This action is necessary to ensure the protection of the maritime public and event participants from the hazards associated with these annual recurring events.
