BREAKING: Federal Register: Determination of Overfishing or an Overfished Condition

Published: 2016-07-21 | Source: Federal Register

This action serves as a notice that NMFS, on behalf of the Secretary of Commerce (Secretary), has found that the Atlantic bigeye tuna stock is subject to overfishing. In addition, Gulf of Mexico gray triggerfish and Gulf of Mexico red snapper continue to be overfished.
