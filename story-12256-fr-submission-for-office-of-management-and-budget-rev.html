BREAKING: Federal Register: Submission for Office of Management and Budget Review; American Relief Act 2025 Disaster Supplemental Funds for Child Care (New Collection)

Published: 2025-05-27 | Source: Federal Register

The Office of Child Care (OCC), Administration for Children and Families (ACF) is requesting applications for disaster relief funding. OCC will be requesting information from eligible Lead Agencies who are interested in receiving these funds.
