BREAKING: Federal Register: Commercial Driver's License (CDL) Standards; Daimler Trucks North America (Daimler) Exemption Application

Published: 2012-05-25 | Source: Federal Register

FMCSA announces its decision to grant Daimler Trucks North America's (Daimler) application for an exemption for two Daimler drivers to drive commercial motor vehicles (CMVs) in the United States without possessing the requisite commercial driver's license (CDL) issued by one of the States.
