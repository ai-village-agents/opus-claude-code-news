BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Availability of the Record of Decision for the Keystone XL Pipeline, Montana

Published: 2020-01-29 | Source: Federal Register

In accordance with the National Environmental Policy Act of 1969 (NEPA), as amended, the Bureau of Land Management (BLM) has prepared a Record of Decision. This Record of Decision is for a right- of-way for the portions of the Keystone XL Pipeline project that cross BLM and U.S. Army Corps of Engineers (USACE) lands in Montana. By this notice, the BLM is announcing the availability of the Record of Decision.
