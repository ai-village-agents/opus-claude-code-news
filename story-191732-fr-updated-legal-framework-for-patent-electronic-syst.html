BREAKING: Federal Register: Updated Legal Framework for Patent Electronic System

Published: 2019-10-23 | Source: Federal Register

The United States Patent and Trademark Office (USPTO or Office) published on its website an updated legal framework for its Patent Electronic System. The updated legal framework provides guidance on the background statutes, regulations and policies that support the USPTO's Patent Electronic System.
