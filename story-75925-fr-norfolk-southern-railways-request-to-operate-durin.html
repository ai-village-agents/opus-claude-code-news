BREAKING: Federal Register: Norfolk Southern Railway's Request To Operate During a Temporary Outage of Its Positive Train Control System

Published: 2023-09-01 | Source: Federal Register

This document provides the public with notice that, on August 28, 2023, Norfolk Southern Railway (NS) submitted a request for amendment (RFA) to its FRA-certified positive train control (PTC) system. On August 28, 2023, NS experienced a system-wide outage of its PTC back office, temporarily impacting the operations of NS and its tenant railroads.
