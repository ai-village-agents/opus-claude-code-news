BREAKING: Federal Register: Elimination of Firearms Transaction Record, ATF Form 4473 (Low Volume) (2008R-21P)

Published: 2014-08-04 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Justice is amending the regulations of the Bureau of Alcohol, Tobacco, Firearms, and Explosives (ATF) by eliminating the Firearms Transaction Record, ATF Form 4473 (Low Volume (LV)), Parts I and II. Federally licensed firearms dealers used this form as an alternate record for the receipt and disposition of firearms.
