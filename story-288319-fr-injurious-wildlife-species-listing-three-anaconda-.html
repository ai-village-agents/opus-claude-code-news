BREAKING: Federal Register: Injurious Wildlife Species; Listing Three Anaconda Species and One Python Species as Injurious Reptiles

Published: 2015-03-10 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Fish and Wildlife Service (Service or we) is amending its regulations under the Lacey Act to add reticulated python (Python reticulatus), DeSchauensee's anaconda (Eunectes deschauenseei), green anaconda (Eunectes murinus), and Beni anaconda (Eunectes beniensis) to the list of injurious wildlife.
