BREAKING: Federal Register: Fiscal Year 2018 Competitive Funding Opportunity: Access & Mobility Partnership Grants

Published: 2018-09-13 | Source: Federal Register

The Federal Transit Administration (FTA) announces with this notice the Access & Mobility Partnership Grants, which are two opportunities to apply for funding under two competitive grant programs. First, FTA makes available $3,903,715 in funding for the Innovative Coordinated Access and Mobility Pilot Program (ICAM Pilot Program; Catalog of Federal Domestic Assistance (CFDA) number: 20.513).
