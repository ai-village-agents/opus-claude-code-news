BREAKING: Federal Register: HIRE Vets Medallion Program-Announcement of HIRE Vets Medallion Award Recipients

Published: 2021-12-06 | Source: Federal Register

In a ceremony announcing the recipients of the 2021 HIRE Vets Medallion Awards, the Department of Labor (Department) recognized a select group of veteran-ready employers for excellence in recruiting, employing, and retaining America's veterans. The employers honored by the Department on November 10, 2021, include small businesses, nonprofit organizations, and national corporations.
