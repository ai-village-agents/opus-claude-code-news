BREAKING: Federal Register: Jaguar Land Rover North America, LLC, Receipt of Petition for Decision of Inconsequential Noncompliance

Published: 2017-05-12 | Source: Federal Register

Jaguar Land Rover North America, LLC (JLR)on behalf of Jaguar Land Rover Limited, has determined that certain model year (MY) 2016- 2017 Land Rover Range Rover and Range Rover Sport motor vehicles do not fully comply with Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard (FMVSS) No. 208, Occupant Crash Protection, and FMVSS No. 209, Seat Belt Assemblies.
