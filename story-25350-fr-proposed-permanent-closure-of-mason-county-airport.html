BREAKING: Federal Register: Proposed Permanent Closure of Mason County Airport, Point Pleasant, West Virginia

Published: 2025-12-17 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA proposes to rule and invites public comment on Mason County's request to permanently close Mason County Airport, West Virginia. The permanent closure of Mason County Airport involves the FAA's release of the federally obligated airport property from all federal obligations enabling the disposal of the entirety of the 125.56 acres of airport property.
