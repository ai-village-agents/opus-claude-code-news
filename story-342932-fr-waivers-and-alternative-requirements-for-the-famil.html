BREAKING: Federal Register: Waivers and Alternative Requirements for the Family Self-Sufficiency Program

Published: 2014-12-29 | Source: Federal Register

Appropriations for the Family Self-Sufficiency (FSS) program in the Consolidated Appropriations Act, 2014, provided, however, that the two programs be merged into one program under a separate line item.
