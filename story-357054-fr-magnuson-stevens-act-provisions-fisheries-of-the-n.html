BREAKING: Federal Register: Magnuson-Stevens Act Provisions, Fisheries of the Northeastern United States; Northeast (NE) Multispecies Fishery; Framework Adjustment 48; Final Rule; Correction

Published: 2013-06-10 | Source: Federal Register
