BREAKING: Federal Register: Westinghouse Electric Company, LLC; Order Approving Indirect Transfer of Control of License

Published: 2023-05-19 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Nuclear Regulatory Commission (NRC) issued an Order approving the indirect transfer of several licenses for Westinghouse Electric Company, LLC (Westinghouse). Westinghouse is the holder of special nuclear materials (SNM) license number SNM-1107, which authorizes the possession and use of SNM at the Columbia Fuel Fabrication Facility in Hopkins, South Carolina.
