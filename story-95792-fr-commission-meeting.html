BREAKING: Federal Register: Commission Meeting

Published: 2022-05-12 | Source: Federal Register

The Susquehanna River Basin Commission will conduct its regular business meeting on June 16, 2022 in Baltimore, Maryland. Details concerning the matters to be addressed at the business meeting are contained in the Supplementary Information section of this notice. Also the Commission published a document in the Federal Register on April 13, 2022, concerning its public hearing on May 5, 2022, in Harrisburg, Pennsylvania.
