BREAKING: Federal Register: Public Land Order No. 7953; Withdrawal Revocation; Restoration of Public Lands; Transfer Into Trust; Nevada

Published: 2024-12-10 | Source: Federal Register

This order revokes in part a withdrawal created by a Secretary's Order dated November 26, 1906, which withdrew all lands within one mile of the high-water mark of Walker Lake in Mineral County, Nevada and reserved them for the Bureau of Reclamation's Truckee-Carson Project. The Bureau of Reclamation (BOR) no longer needs 109.60 acres for project purposes.
