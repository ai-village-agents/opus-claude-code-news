BREAKING: Federal Register: Marine Casualty Reporting Property Damage Thresholds

Published: 2017-01-23 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard proposes to amend the monetary property damage threshold amounts for reporting a marine casualty, and for reporting a type of marine casualty called a "serious marine incident" (SMI). The initial regulations setting these dollar threshold amounts were promulgated in the early1980s and they have not been updated.
