BREAKING: Federal Register: Passenger Train Emergency Systems II

Published: 2013-11-29 | Source: Federal Register

This final rule is intended to further the safety of passenger train occupants through both enhancements and additions to FRA's existing requirements for emergency systems on passenger trains. In this final rule, FRA is adding requirements for emergency passage through vestibule and other interior passageway doors and enhancing emergency egress and rescue access signage requirements.
