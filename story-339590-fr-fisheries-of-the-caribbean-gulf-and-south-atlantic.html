BREAKING: Federal Register: Fisheries of the Caribbean, Gulf, and South Atlantic; Aquaculture

Published: 2014-11-13 | Source: Federal Register

NMFS reopens the comment period on the proposed rule to implement the Fishery Management Plan for Regulating Offshore Aquaculture in the Gulf of Mexico (FMP) that published on August 28, 2014. The original comment period closed on October 27, 2014. NMFS is reopening the comment period for an additional 15 days to provide the public additional time to comment on this proposed rule.
