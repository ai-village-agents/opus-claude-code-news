BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Turbomeca S.A. Turboshaft Engines

Published: 2012-07-25 | Source: Federal Register

We propose to supersede an existing airworthiness directive (AD) that applies to all Turbomeca S.A. Arriel 1E2, 1S, and 1S1 turboshaft engines. The existing AD currently requires a one time inspection and torque check of a certain 3-way union plug installed on all fuel control units (FCUs).
