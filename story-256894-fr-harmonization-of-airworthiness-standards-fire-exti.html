BREAKING: Federal Register: Harmonization of Airworthiness Standards-Fire Extinguishers and Class B and F Cargo Compartments; Correction

Published: 2016-03-02 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA is correcting a final rule published on February 16, 2016. In that rule, the FAA amended certain airworthiness regulations for transport category airplanes by upgrading fire safety standards for Class B cargo compartments; establishing fire safety standards for a new type of cargo compartment, Class F; and updating related standards for fire extinguishers.
