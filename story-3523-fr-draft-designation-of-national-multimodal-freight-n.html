BREAKING: Federal Register: Draft Designation of National Multimodal Freight Network and State Input Process

Published: 2025-01-13 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Department of Transportation (DOT or Department) is publishing a draft designation of the National Multimodal Freight Network (NMFN or Network) as required Federal law. The designation is informed by the comments received on a notice published by DOT on April 12, 2024. DOT is also using this notice to request comments or proposed modifications to the draft Network prior to designating the Network.
