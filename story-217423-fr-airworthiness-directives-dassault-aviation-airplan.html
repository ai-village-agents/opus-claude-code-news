BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Dassault Aviation Airplanes

Published: 2018-09-27 | Source: Federal Register

We are adopting a new airworthiness directive (AD) for certain Dassault Aviation Model FALCON 2000 and FALCON 2000EX airplanes. This AD was prompted by reports of metallic debris found in the wing slat piccolo tubes; investigation revealed that the debris originated from the flow guide of the ball joint of the wing anti-ice valve.
