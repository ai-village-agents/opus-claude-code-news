BREAKING: Federal Register: Scoping Period Extended for the Proposed Enbridge Energy Partners, Line 67 Capacity Expansion Project

Published: 2013-05-03 | Source: Federal Register

The Department has decided to extend by two weeks the scoping period for the Supplemental Environmental Impact Statement for the proposed Enbridge Energy, Limited Partnership (Enbridge), Line 67 Capacity Expansion Project. Scoping will now end at midnight EDT on May 13th, 2013.
