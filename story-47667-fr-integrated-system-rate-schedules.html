BREAKING: Federal Register: Integrated System Rate Schedules

Published: 2024-09-20 | Source: Federal Register

The Administrator of the Southwestern Power Administration (Southwestern) has approved and implemented Rate Order No. SWPA-85, which grants a temporary extension of Southwestern's current Integrated System rate schedules (P-13B, NFTS-13A, and EE-13) through September 30, 2025, unless superseded.
