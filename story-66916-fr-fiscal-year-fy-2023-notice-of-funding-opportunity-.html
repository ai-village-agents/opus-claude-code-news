BREAKING: Federal Register: Fiscal Year (FY) 2023 Notice of Funding Opportunity Modification

Published: 2023-05-08 | Source: Federal Register

This notice is to inform the public that the Substance Abuse and Mental Health Services Administration (SAMHSA) has modified the FY 2022 State Opioid Response (SOR) NOFO (TI-22-005). The modifications include adding a new appendix with the FY 2023 allocations based on increased funding and amending the funding limitations/restrictions to increase the administrative costs (i.e., indirect costs) in FY 2023.
