BREAKING: Federal Register: Guidelines for Appeals of Material Supervisory Determinations

Published: 2017-07-25 | Source: Federal Register

On July 18, 2017, the Federal Deposit Insurance Corporation (FDIC) Board of Directors (Board) adopted revised Guidelines for Appeals of Material Supervisory Determinations (Guidelines) to provide institutions with broader avenues of redress with respect to material supervisory determinations and enhance consistency with the appeals process of the other Federal banking agencies.
