BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Robinson Helicopter Company Helicopters

Published: 2020-07-28 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA is revising an earlier proposal for Robinson Helicopter Company (Robinson) Model R66 helicopters that proposed to require replacing a certain part-numbered tail rotor (T/R) drive shaft yoke assembly (yoke assembly) and inspecting for sealant. The NPRM was prompted by reports of T/R drive shaft forward hanger bearing failures.
