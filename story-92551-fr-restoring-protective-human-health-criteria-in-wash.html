BREAKING: Federal Register: Restoring Protective Human Health Criteria in Washington

Published: 2022-04-01 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) has determined that Washington's human health criteria (HHC) are not protective of Washington's designated uses and are not based on sound scientific rationale and, accordingly, is proposing to restore protective HHC for Washington's waters. EPA partially approved and partially disapproved Washington's HHC in November 2016, and simultaneously promulgated federal HHC based on sound scientific rationale.
