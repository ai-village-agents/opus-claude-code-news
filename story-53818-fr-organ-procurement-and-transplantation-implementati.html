BREAKING: Federal Register: Organ Procurement and Transplantation: Implementation of the HIV Organ Policy Equity (HOPE) Act

Published: 2024-11-27 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Health and Human Services (HHS) amends the regulations implementing the National Organ Transplant Act of 1984, as amended (NOTA), to remove clinical research and institutional review board (IRB) requirements ("research and IRB requirements") for transplantation of kidneys and livers from donors with human immunodeficiency virus (HIV) to recipients with HIV.
