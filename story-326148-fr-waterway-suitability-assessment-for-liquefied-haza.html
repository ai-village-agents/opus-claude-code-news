BREAKING: Federal Register: Waterway Suitability Assessment for Liquefied Hazardous Gas Facilities; Freeport, TX

Published: 2014-06-05 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard, at Sector Houston-Galveston, announces receipt of a Letter of Intent (LOI) and Waterways Suitability Assessment (WSA) for a proposed construction project to modify existing petroleum product import/export facilities to add Liquefied Hazardous Gas (LHG) operations in Freeport, Texas. The LOI and WSA were submitted by Phillips 66.
