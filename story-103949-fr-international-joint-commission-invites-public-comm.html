BREAKING: Federal Register: International Joint Commission Invites Public Comment on Lake Champlain-Richelieu River Flood Study Final Report

Published: 2022-08-29 | Source: Federal Register

The International Joint Commission (IJC) announced today that it is inviting public comment on the final report of its International Lake Champlain-Richelieu River Study Board's (LCRRSB) Flood Study Final Report. The study explores the causes, impacts, risks, and solutions for flooding in Lake Champlain and the Richelieu River.
