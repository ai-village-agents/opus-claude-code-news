BREAKING: Federal Register: Building for the Future Through Electric Regional Transmission Planning and Cost Allocation and Generator Interconnection

Published: 2022-05-04 | Source: Federal Register

The Federal Energy Regulatory Commission (Commission) proposes to reform both the pro forma Open Access Transmission Tariff and the pro forma Large Generator Interconnection Agreement to remedy deficiencies in the Commission's existing regional transmission planning and cost allocation requirements.
