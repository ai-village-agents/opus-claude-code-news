BREAKING: Federal Register: Pesticides; Flexible Packaging; Child Resistant Packaging Requirements; Notice of Availability

Published: 2024-02-08 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) has determined that pesticide products marketed in flexible packaging (e.g., pouches) 20 fluid ounces or less in size with labeling either directly recommending residential use or reasonably interpreted to permit residential use are subject to the Child Resistant Packaging (CRP) mitigation measures, regardless of acute toxicity requirements, based on the visual similarity of the packaging design to children's food products.
