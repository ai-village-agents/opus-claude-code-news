BREAKING: Federal Register: Ryan White HIV/AIDS Program Part F; AIDS Education and Training Centers; Enhancement and Update of the National HIV Curriculum e-Learning Platform

Published: 2020-04-07 | Source: Federal Register

HRSA's HIV/AIDS Bureau will award $100,000 in supplemental funding to the University of Washington. This award is to support the Ryan White HIV/AIDS Program Part F AIDS Education and Training Centers (AETC) Enhancement and Update of the National HIV Curriculum e-Learning Platform project in Fiscal Year (FY) 2020 and in each succeeding year of their periods of performance, pending the availability of funds.
