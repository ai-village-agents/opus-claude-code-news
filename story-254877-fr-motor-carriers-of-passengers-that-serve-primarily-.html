BREAKING: Federal Register: Motor Carriers of Passengers That Serve Primarily Urban Areas With High Passenger Loads

Published: 2016-02-09 | Source: Federal Register

This request for comments is related to the implementation of a specific provision in section 32707 of the Moving Ahead for Progress in the 21st Century Act (MAP-21) that requires an annual safety fitness assessment of certain motor carriers of passengers that serve primarily urban areas with high passenger loads.
