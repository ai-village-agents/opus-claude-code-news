BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone; M/V Zhenhuan 14 Wando Terminal Crane Movement; Charleston, SC

Published: 2016-08-05 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is establishing a 100 yard temporary moving safety zone around the M/V Zhenhuan 14 during its inbound and outbound transit as well as all movements in between the Charleston Harbor entrance buoy and the Wando Welch Terminal on the Charleston Harbor, and Wando River, Charleston, SC. The M/V Zhenhuan 14 will be transporting 5 gantry cranes between the dates of August 5, 2016 through August 17, 2016.
