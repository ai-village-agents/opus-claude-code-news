BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Airbus Airplanes

Published: 2013-09-04 | Source: Federal Register

We are adopting a new airworthiness directive (AD) for all Airbus Model A318, A319, A320, and A321 series airplanes. This AD was prompted by reports that certain trimmable horizontal stabilizer actuators (THSA) were found with corrosion that affected the ballscrew lower splines between the tie-bar and screw-jack.
