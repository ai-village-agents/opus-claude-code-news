BREAKING: Federal Register: Approval and Promulgation of Implementation Plans; Oregon: Interstate Transport of Ozone

Published: 2015-12-21 | Source: Federal Register

The Clean Air Act (CAA) requires each State Implementation Plan (SIP) to contain adequate provisions prohibiting air emissions that will have certain adverse air quality effects in other states. On June 28, 2010, the State of Oregon made a submittal to the Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) to address these requirements.
