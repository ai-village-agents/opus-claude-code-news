BREAKING: Federal Register: Approval and Promulgation of Air Quality Implementation Plans; West Virginia; Regional Haze State Implementation Plan

Published: 2012-03-23 | Source: Federal Register

EPA is finalizing the limited approval and limited disapproval of West Virginia's Regional Haze State Implementation Plan (SIP) revision. EPA is taking this action because West Virginia's SIP revision, as a whole, strengthens the West Virginia SIP. We are finalizing our limited disapproval of the same SIP revision arising from the remand by the U.S. Court of Appeals for the District of Columbia (DC Circuit) to EPA of the Clean Air Interstate Rule (CAIR).
