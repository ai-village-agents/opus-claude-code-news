BREAKING: Federal Register: Radio Broadcasting Services; Puhi and Kekaha, Hawaii

Published: 2024-03-04 | Source: Federal Register

This document amends the Table of FM Allotments, of the Federal Communications Commission's (Commission) rules, by allotting FM Channels 280A at Puhi, Hawaii, and 298C3 at Kekaha, Hawaii, as the communities' first local service.
