BREAKING: Federal Register: Award Management Requirements, Final Circular

Published: 2024-09-27 | Source: Federal Register

The Federal Transit Administration (FTA) has made available on its website the final updated Award Management Requirements Circular (C 5010.1). The updated circular combines requirements applicable to all FTA financial assistance awards (referred to as "cross-cutting" requirements) and supersedes the previous Award Management Requirements Circular C 5010.1E.
