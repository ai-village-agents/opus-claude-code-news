BREAKING: Federal Register: Softwood Lumber Research, Promotion, Consumer Education, and Information Order; Adjustment to Membership

Published: 2024-02-21 | Source: Federal Register

This proposal invites comments on the modification of the membership of the Softwood Lumber Board (Board) established under the Softwood Lumber Research, Promotion, Consumer Education and Industry Information Order (Order). This action would modify the membership of the Board by adding the alternate position for certain seats and a public member.
