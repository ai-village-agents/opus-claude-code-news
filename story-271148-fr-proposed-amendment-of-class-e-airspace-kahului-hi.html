BREAKING: Federal Register: Proposed Amendment of Class E Airspace, Kahului, HI

Published: 2016-08-12 | Source: Federal Register

This action proposes to modify Class E airspace designated as an extension to a Class C surface area, and modify Class E airspace extending upward from 700 feet above the surface at Kahului Airport, Kahului, HI.
