BREAKING: Federal Register: Fiscal Year 2019 Competitive Funding Opportunity; Grants for Buses and Bus Facilities Program

Published: 2019-05-15 | Source: Federal Register

The Federal Transit Administration (FTA) announces the opportunity to apply for approximately $423.35 million in fiscal year (FY) 2019 funds under the Grants for Buses and Bus Facilities Program (CFDA#20.526).
