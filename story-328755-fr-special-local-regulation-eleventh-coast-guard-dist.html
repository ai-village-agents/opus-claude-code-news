BREAKING: Federal Register: Special Local Regulation; Eleventh Coast Guard District Annual Marine Events

Published: 2014-07-03 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard will enforce the San Diego Maritime Museum Tall Ship Festival of Sail special local regulations during this year's event on August 29, 2014 through September 1, 2014. This event occurs on the navigable waters of the San Diego Bay in San Diego, CA. These special local regulations are necessary to provide for the safety of the participants, crew, spectators, sponsor vessels of the boat parade, and general users of the waterway.
