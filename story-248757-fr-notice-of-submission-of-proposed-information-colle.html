BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Submission of Proposed Information Collection to OMB

Published: 2017-11-16 | Source: Federal Register

In accordance with the Paperwork Reduction Act of 1995, as amended, this notice announces the Department of Transportation's (Department) intention to reinstate an Office of Management and Budget (OMB) control number for the collection and posting of certain aviation consumer protection-related information from U.S. carriers and foreign carriers.
