BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Turbomeca S.A. Turboshaft Engines

Published: 2013-02-07 | Source: Federal Register

We propose to supersede an existing airworthiness directive (AD) that applies to all Turbomeca S.A. Arrius Models 2B, 2B1, and 2F turboshaft engines. The existing AD currently requires replacement of injector manifolds and borescope-inspection of the flame tube and the high-pressure (HP) turbine area for possible damage.
