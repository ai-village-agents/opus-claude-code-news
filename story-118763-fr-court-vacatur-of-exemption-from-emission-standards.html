BREAKING: Federal Register: Court Vacatur of Exemption From Emission Standards During Periods of Startup, Shutdown, and Malfunction

Published: 2021-03-11 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is amending the Code of Federal Regulations (CFR) to reflect a court order regarding the General Provisions for National Emissions Standards for Hazardous Air Pollutants (NESHAP) issued on December 19, 2008, by the United States Court of Appeals for the District of Columbia Circuit (the court).
