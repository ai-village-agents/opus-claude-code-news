BREAKING: Federal Register: Amendment of Class E Airspace, Augusta, GA, and Establishment of Class E Airspace, Waynesboro, GA

Published: 2018-10-26 | Source: Federal Register

This action amends Class E airspace extending upward from 700 feet above the surface in Augusta, GA, by recognizing the name change of Augusta Regional Airport at Bush Field (formerly Augusta Regional at Bush Field Airport); removing Burke County Airport and Millen Airport from the airspace designation and establishing these two airports under Waynesboro, GA, designation; and updating the geographic coordinates of Daniel Field, Augusta, GA, and Millen Airport, Waynesboro, GA.
