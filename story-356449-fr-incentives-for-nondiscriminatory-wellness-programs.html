BREAKING: Federal Register: Incentives for Nondiscriminatory Wellness Programs in Group Health Plans

Published: 2013-06-03 | Source: Federal Register

This document contains final regulations, consistent with the Affordable Care Act, regarding nondiscriminatory wellness programs in group health coverage. Specifically, these final regulations increase the maximum permissible reward under a health-contingent wellness program offered in connection with a group health plan (and any related health insurance coverage) from 20 percent to 30 percent of the cost of coverage.
