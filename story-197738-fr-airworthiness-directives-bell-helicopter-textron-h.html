BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Bell Helicopter Textron Helicopters

Published: 2018-01-17 | Source: Federal Register

We are adopting a new airworthiness directive (AD) for Bell Helicopter Textron (Bell) Model 204B, 205A, and 205A-1 helicopters with a Helicopter Technology Company (HTC) main rotor (M/R) blade installed. This AD requires cleaning and visually inspecting the M/R blades, and depending on the outcome of the inspection, repairing or replacing the M/R blades.
