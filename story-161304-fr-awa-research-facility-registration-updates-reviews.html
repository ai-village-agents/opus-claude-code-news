BREAKING: Federal Register: AWA Research Facility Registration Updates, Reviews, and Reports

Published: 2020-09-17 | Source: Federal Register

We are proposing to amend the Animal Welfare Act (AWA) regulations governing research facilities by removing duplicative and unnecessary information requirements. We would remove the requirement that registered research facilities update their registration information every 3 years.
