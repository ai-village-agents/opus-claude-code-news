BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zones; Recurring Events in Captain of the Port Duluth Zone

Published: 2013-05-31 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard proposes to establish permanent safety zones for annually recurring marine events in the Coast Guard Marine Safety Unit Duluth Captain of the Port (COTP) Zone. The safety zones in this proposed rule are needed to protect both spectators and participants from the hazards associated with the events.
