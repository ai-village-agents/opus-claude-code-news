BREAKING: Federal Register: Endangered and Threatened Wildlife and Plants; 12-Month Finding for the Northern Spotted Owl

Published: 2020-12-15 | Source: Federal Register

We, the U.S. Fish and Wildlife Service (Service), announce a 12-month finding on a petition to list the northern spotted owl (Strix occidentalis caurina) as an endangered species under the Endangered Species Act of 1973, as amended (Act).
