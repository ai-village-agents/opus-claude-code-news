BREAKING: Federal Register: Drawbridge Operation Regulation; Ashtabula River, Ashtabula, OH

Published: 2024-02-29 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is modifying the operating schedule that governs the Fifth Street Bridge, mile 0.15, and the Norfolk Southern Railroad Bridge, mile 1.5, both over the Ashtabula River. The Coast Guard is also changing signaling and signage requirements for the Norfolk Southern Railroad Bridge, mile 1.5.
