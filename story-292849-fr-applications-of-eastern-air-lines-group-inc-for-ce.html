BREAKING: Federal Register: Applications of Eastern Air Lines Group, Inc. for Certificate Authority

Published: 2015-05-01 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Transportation is directing all interested persons to show cause why it should not issue orders finding Eastern Air Lines Group, Inc. fit, willing, and able, and awarding it certificates of public convenience and necessity authorizing it to engage in interstate and foreign charter air transportation of persons, property, and mail.
