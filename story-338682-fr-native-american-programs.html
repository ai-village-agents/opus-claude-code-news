BREAKING: Federal Register: Native American Programs

Published: 2014-10-31 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Health and Human Services, Administration for Children and Families, Administration for Native Americans (ANA) is seeking comments, data, and information from the public related to planned revisions and amendments to regulations implementing the Native American Programs Act of 1974 (the NAPA).
