BREAKING: Federal Register: Accident Source Term Methodologies and Corresponding Release Fractions

Published: 2020-08-24 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Nuclear Regulatory Commission (NRC) has received a petition for rulemaking from Brian Magnuson dated May 31, 2020, requesting that the NRC revise its regulations to codify the source term methodologies and corresponding release fractions recommended in a report issued by Sandia National Laboratories.
