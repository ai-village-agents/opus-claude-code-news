BREAKING: Federal Register: Electronic Filing in the Copyright Office of Notices of Intention To Obtain a Section 115 Compulsory License

Published: 2012-05-25 | Source: Federal Register

The Copyright Office is proposing to amend its regulations for filing Notices of Intention to obtain a Section 115 compulsory license with the Copyright Office to provide an option for electronically filing the notice. By law, such notices may be filed in the Office only when the public records of the Copyright Office do not identify the copyright owner of the musical work and include an address at which notice can be served.
