BREAKING: Federal Register: Federal Acquisition Regulation: Rerepresentation of Size and Socioeconomic Status

Published: 2025-01-03 | Source: Federal Register

DoD, GSA, and NASA are issuing a final rule amending the Federal Acquisition Regulation (FAR) to implement regulatory changes made by the Small Business Administration to order-level size and socioeconomic status rerepresentation requirements.

Agency: Defense Department, General Services Administration, National Aeronautics and Space Administration
