BREAKING: Federal Register: Office of the Secretary, Muscular Dystrophy Coordinating Committee Call for Committee Membership Nominations

Published: 2021-05-06 | Source: Federal Register

The Office of the Secretary of the Department of Health and Human Services (HHS) is seeking nominations for two individuals to serve as non-federal public members on the Muscular Dystrophy Coordinating Committee.
