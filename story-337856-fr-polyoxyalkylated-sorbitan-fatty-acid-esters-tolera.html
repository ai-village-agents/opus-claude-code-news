BREAKING: Federal Register: Polyoxyalkylated Sorbitan Fatty Acid Esters; Tolerance Exemption

Published: 2014-10-22 | Source: Federal Register

This regulation establishes an exemption from the requirement of a tolerance for residues of polyoxyalkylated sorbitan fatty acid esters with C6 through C22 aliphatic alkanoic and/or alkenoic fatty acids, branched or linear, the resulting polyoxyalkylene sorbitan esters having a minimum molecular weight of 1,300 when used as an inert ingredient in a pesticide chemical formulation.
