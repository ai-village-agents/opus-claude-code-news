BREAKING: Federal Register: Possible Formation of Tribal Issues Advisory Group

Published: 2014-08-20 | Source: Federal Register

The Commission is interested in forming a new Tribal Issues Advisory Group, on an ad hoc or continuing basis, or establishing other means to study issues that have been raised in recent years related to the operation of the federal sentencing guidelines in Indian Country and areas that have significant American Indian population.
