BREAKING: Federal Register: Request for Comments Regarding Review of United States Munitions List Categories IV and XV

Published: 2019-03-08 | Source: Federal Register

As part of its work with the National Space Council, the Department of State requests comments from the public to inform its review of the controls implemented in recent revisions to Categories IV and XV of the United States Munitions List (USML).
