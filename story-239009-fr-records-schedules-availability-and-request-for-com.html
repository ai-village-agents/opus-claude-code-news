BREAKING: Federal Register: Records Schedules; Availability and Request for Comments

Published: 2017-07-14 | Source: Federal Register

The National Archives and Records Administration (NARA) publishes notice at least once monthly of certain Federal agency requests for records disposition authority (records schedules). Once approved by NARA, records schedules provide mandatory instructions on what happens to records when agencies no longer need them for current Government business.
