BREAKING: Federal Register: Proposed Establishment of the Mount Pisgah, Polk County, Oregon Viticultural Area

Published: 2020-10-01 | Source: Federal Register

The Alcohol and Tobacco Tax and Trade Bureau (TTB) proposes to establish the approximately 5,850-acre "Mount Pisgah, Polk County, Oregon" viticultural area in Polk County, Oregon. The proposed viticultural area lies entirely within the Willamette Valley viticultural area. TTB designates viticultural areas to allow vintners to better describe the origin of their wines and to allow consumers to better identify wines they may purchase.
