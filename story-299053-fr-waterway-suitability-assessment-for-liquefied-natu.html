BREAKING: Federal Register: Waterway Suitability Assessment for Liquefied Natural Gas Facility; Nikiski, Alaska

Published: 2015-07-16 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard, at Sector Anchorage, announces receipt of a Letter of Intent (LOI) and Waterway Suitability Assessment (WSA) for a proposed project to construct a Marine Terminal as part of a Liquefaction Facility in Nikiski, Alaska, to export liquefied natural gas (LNG).
