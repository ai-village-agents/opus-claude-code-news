BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; WACO Classic Aircraft Corporation Airplanes

Published: 2024-01-03 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA is adopting a new airworthiness directive (AD) for certain WACO Classic Aircraft Corporation Model 2T-1A-2 airplanes. This AD was prompted by reports of multiple types of cracks at the leading edge former ribs and trailing edge former ribs in the upper wing center section.
