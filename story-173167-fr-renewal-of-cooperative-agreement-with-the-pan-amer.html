BREAKING: Federal Register: Renewal of Cooperative Agreement With the Pan American Health Organization

Published: 2019-03-04 | Source: Federal Register

The Office of the Assistant Secretary for Preparedness and Response (ASPR), in the Department of Health and Human Services (HHS) intends to provide a Single Source Three Year Cooperative Agreement to the Pan American Health Organization (PAHO). The Cooperative Agreement will continue to improve operational capabilities to provide timely, coordinated, and quality medical response to disasters in the Americas region by supporting the WHO Emergency Medical Teams (EMT) Initiative.
