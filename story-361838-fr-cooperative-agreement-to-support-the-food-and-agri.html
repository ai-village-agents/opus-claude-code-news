BREAKING: Federal Register: Cooperative Agreement to Support the Food and Agriculture Organization

Published: 2013-08-02 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA) is announcing its intention to receive and consider a single source application for award of a cooperative agreement in fiscal year 2013 to the Food and Agriculture Organization (FAO) of the United Nations to support global strategies that address food safety and public health.
