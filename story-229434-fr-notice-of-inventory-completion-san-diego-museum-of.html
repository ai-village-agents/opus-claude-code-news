BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Inventory Completion: San Diego Museum of Man, San Diego, CA; Correction

Published: 2017-03-13 | Source: Federal Register

The San Diego Museum of Man has corrected an inventory of human remains and associated funerary objects, published in a Notice of Inventory Completion in the Federal Register on January 27, 2016. This notice corrects the minimum number of individuals and number of associated funerary objects.
