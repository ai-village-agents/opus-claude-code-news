BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Inventory Completion: History Colorado, Denver, CO

Published: 2012-03-07 | Source: Federal Register

History Colorado (formerly the Colorado Historical Society) has completed an inventory of human remains and associated funerary objects, in consultation with the appropriate Indian tribes, and has determined that there is insufficient evidence to reasonably establish cultural affiliation between the human remains and associated funerary objects and present- day Indian tribes.
