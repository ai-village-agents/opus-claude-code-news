BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone: America's Cup World Series Finish-Line, San Francisco, CA

Published: 2012-10-05 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is establishing a temporary safety zone in the navigable waters of the San Francisco Bay in vicinity of San Francisco West Yacht Harbor Light 2, approximately 1,250 yards east of Anita Rock in San Francisco Bay, in support of the 2012 America's Cup World Series sailing events.
