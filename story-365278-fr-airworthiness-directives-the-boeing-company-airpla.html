BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; The Boeing Company Airplanes

Published: 2013-09-12 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA withdraws a notice of proposed rulemaking (NPRM) that proposed to rescind airworthiness directive (AD) 2008-06-03, which applies to certain The Boeing Company Model 737-600, -700, -700C, -800 and -900 series airplanes; and Model 757-200, -200PF, -200CB, and -300 series airplanes.
