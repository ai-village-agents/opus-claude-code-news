BREAKING: Federal Register: Pediatric Device Consortia Grant Program

Published: 2013-04-05 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA) is announcing the availability of grant funds for the support of the Office of Orphan Products Development (OOPD) Pediatric Device Consortia (PDC) Grant Program. The goal of the PDC Grant Program is to facilitate the development, production, and distribution of pediatric medical devices.
