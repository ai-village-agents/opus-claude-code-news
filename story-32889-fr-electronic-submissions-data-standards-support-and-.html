BREAKING: Federal Register: Electronic Submissions: Data Standards; Support and Requirement for the Clinical Data Interchange Standards Consortium Standard for Exchange of Nonclinical Data Implementation Guide-Animal Rule Version 1.0

Published: 2024-03-26 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration's (FDA or Agency) Center for Biologics Evaluation and Research (CBER) is announcing support for the Clinical Data Interchange Standards Consortium (CDISC) Standard for Exchange of Nonclinical Data Implementation Guide--Animal Rule Version 1.0 (SENDIG-AR v1.0) on March 26, 2024, and this standard will be required in submissions to CBER for studies that start after March 15, 2027.
