BREAKING: Federal Register: Waterway Suitability Assessment for Construction and Operation of Liquefied Gas Terminals; Orange, TX

Published: 2013-12-11 | Source: Federal Register

In accordance with Coast Guard regulations, INVISTA, S.a.r.l. has submitted a Letter of Intent and Preliminary Waterway Suitability Assessment to the Coast Guard Captain of the Port, Port Arthur, TX regarding the company's plans to construct, own and operate a waterfront facility handling and storing Liquefied Hazardous Gas (LHG) at its Orange, Texas facility.
