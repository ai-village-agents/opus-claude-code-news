BREAKING: Federal Register: Cyber Security for Byproduct Materials Licensees

Published: 2018-05-15 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Nuclear Regulatory Commission (NRC) is discontinuing the rulemaking activity that would have developed cyber security requirements for byproduct materials licensees possessing risk- significant quantities of radioactive materials. The purpose of this action is to inform members of the public of the discontinuation of the rulemaking activity and to provide a brief discussion of the NRC's decision.
