BREAKING: Federal Register: Tomatoes Grown in Florida; Decreased Assessment Rate

Published: 2015-11-25 | Source: Federal Register

This rule implements a recommendation from the Florida Tomato Committee (Committee) for a decrease in the assessment rate established for the 2015-16 and subsequent fiscal periods from $0.0375 to $0.03 per 25-pound carton of tomatoes handled under the marketing order (order). The Committee locally administers the order and is comprised of producers of tomatoes operating within the area of production.
