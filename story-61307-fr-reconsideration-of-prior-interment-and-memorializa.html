BREAKING: Federal Register: Reconsideration of Prior Interment and Memorialization Decisions

Published: 2023-02-27 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Veterans Affairs (VA) proposes to amend its regulations to implement VA's authority to reconsider a prior decision to inter or honor the memory of a person in a VA national cemetery. As of December 20, 2013, VA was authorized to reconsider a prior decision to inter or memorialize an individual who was convicted of a Federal or State capital crime or tier III sex offense.
