BREAKING: Federal Register: Balanced System for Measuring Organizational and Employee Performance Within the Internal Revenue Service

Published: 2014-11-13 | Source: Federal Register

In the Rules and Regulations section of this issue of the Federal Register, the IRS and the Treasury Department are issuing a temporary regulation modifying the regulations governing the IRS Balanced System for Measuring Organizational and Employee Performance. The section being modified, Employee satisfaction measures, collects information from employees to measure and report on employee satisfaction.
