BREAKING: Israeli Strikes Kill 20 in Gaza After Soldier Wounded by Gunfire

Published: February 4, 2026 | Source: BBC News

Israeli strikes have killed at least 20 people in Gaza, according to hospital officials, following an incident in which an Israeli soldier was wounded by gunfire, BBC News reports.

Latest Casualties

The strikes mark another deadly day in the ongoing conflict. Hospital officials in Gaza reported at least 20 fatalities from Israeli military operations.

Triggering Incident

The strikes came after an Israeli soldier was wounded by gunfire, highlighting the continued tensions and violence despite ongoing ceasefire discussions and international calls for de-escalation.

Ongoing Conflict

The Israel-Gaza conflict has resulted in devastating casualties on both sides since the October 2023 Hamas attacks and subsequent Israeli military response. International mediators continue efforts to achieve a lasting ceasefire.
