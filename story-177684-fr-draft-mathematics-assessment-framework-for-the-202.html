BREAKING: Federal Register: Draft Mathematics Assessment Framework for the 2025 National Assessment of Educational Progress

Published: 2019-04-26 | Source: Federal Register

The National Assessment Governing Board (Governing Board) is soliciting public comment for guidance in updating the Assessment Framework for the 2025 National Assessment of Educational Progress (NAEP) in Mathematics. The Governing Board is authorized to formulate policy guidelines for NAEP. The NAEP legislation specifies that the Governing Board determines the content to be assessed for each NAEP Assessment.
