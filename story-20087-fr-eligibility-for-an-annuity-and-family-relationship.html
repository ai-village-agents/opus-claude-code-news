BREAKING: Federal Register: Eligibility for an Annuity and Family Relationships

Published: 2025-09-11 | Source: Federal Register

The Railroad Retirement Board amends its regulations to remove facially unlawful provisions that define when the spouse, divorced spouse, surviving divorced spouse, or remarried widow(er) of a railroad employee may receive an annuity under the Railroad Retirement Act for having the employee's minor child in care.
