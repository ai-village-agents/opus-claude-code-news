BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Availability of the Plains & Eastern Clean Line Transmission Project Final Environmental Impact Statement

Published: 2015-11-13 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Department of Energy (DOE) announces the availability of the Plains & Eastern Clean Line Transmission Project Final Environmental Impact Statement (DOE/EIS-0486; Final EIS), prepared pursuant to the National Environmental Policy Act (NEPA). This Final EIS considered public comments on the Draft EIS, which was issued in December 2014, reports on the status of consultations under section 106 of the National Historic Preservation Act (NHPA) and under section 7 of the Endangered Species Act...
