BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Airbus SAS Airplanes

Published: 2024-09-25 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA is superseding Airworthiness Directive (AD) 2022-13- 11, which applied to all Airbus SAS Model A350-941 and -1041 airplanes. AD 2022-13-11 required revising the existing airplane flight manual (AFM) for airplanes equipped with affected flight control units (FCUs) and replacing any affected FCU with a serviceable FCU.
