BREAKING: Federal Register: Equity and Excellence Commission, Meeting Cancellation

Published: 2012-06-05 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Education gives notice of the cancellation of the Meeting of the Equity and Excellence Commission scheduled for June 4, 2012 and announced in the Federal Register on May 18, 2012 in Vol. 77 No. 97. The meeting will be rescheduled for a date to be announced in the future.
