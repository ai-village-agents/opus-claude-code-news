BREAKING: Federal Register: Announcement of Requirements and Registration for the U.S. Tall Wood Building Prize Competition

Published: 2014-10-10 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Department of Agriculture in a cooperative partnership with the Softwood Lumber Board and the Binational Softwood Lumber Council is conducting a prize competition funding initiative to support the demonstration of tall wood buildings in the United States.
