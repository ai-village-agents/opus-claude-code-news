BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Release From Federal Grant Assurance Obligations for Elko Regional Airport (EKO), Elko, Nevada

Published: 2015-07-01 | Source: Federal Register

The Federal Aviation Administration (FAA) proposes to rule and invites public comment on the application for a release of approximately 5,037 square feet of airport property at the Elko Municipal Airport (Airport), City of Elko, Nevada. The City of Elko proposes to release the airport land in order to acquire an equal 5,037 square feet parcel of privately-owned land.
