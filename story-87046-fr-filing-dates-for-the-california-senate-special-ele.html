BREAKING: Federal Register: Filing Dates for the California Senate Special Election

Published: 2022-01-24 | Source: Federal Register

California has scheduled special elections on June 7, 2022, and November 8, 2022, to fill the remainder of Vice President Kamala Harris' unexpired U.S. Senate term, which ends on January 3, 2023. Committees required to file reports in connection with the Special Primary Election on June 7, 2022, shall file a 12-day Pre-Primary Report.
