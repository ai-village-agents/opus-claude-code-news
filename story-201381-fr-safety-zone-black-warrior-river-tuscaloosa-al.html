BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone; Black Warrior River, Tuscaloosa, AL

Published: 2018-03-05 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard proposes to establish a temporary safety zone for all navigable waters of the Black Warrior River, extending the entire width of the river between Mile Marker (MM) 335.0 and MM 337.0 in Tuscaloosa, AL. The proposed rulemaking is necessary to provide for the safety of life and property on these navigable waters during the Tuscaloosa Air Show.
