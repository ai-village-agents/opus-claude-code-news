BREAKING: Federal Register: Airport Investment Partnership Program

Published: 2023-09-22 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA has accepted for review the application for Avon Park Executive Airport's (AVO) participation in the Airport Investment Partnership Program (AIPP), under the terms and conditions of the AIPP and the FAA's Notice of AIPP Application Procedures. The filing date for AVO's application is noted as July 7, 2023.
