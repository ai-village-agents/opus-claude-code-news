BREAKING: Federal Register: Air Plan Approval; California; Consumer Products Regulations

Published: 2020-09-16 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is taking final action to approve revisions to the California Air Resources Board's (CARB's) Consumer Products portion of the California State Implementation Plan (SIP). These revisions concern volatile organic compound (VOC) emissions from consumer products and a supporting test method.
