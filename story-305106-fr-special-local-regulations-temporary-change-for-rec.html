BREAKING: Federal Register: Special Local Regulations; Temporary Change for Recurring Marine Event in the Fifth Coast Guard District

Published: 2015-09-25 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is temporarily changing the enforcement periods of special local regulations for a recurring marine event in the Fifth Coast Guard District. These regulations apply to the Ocean City Maryland Offshore Grand Prix, a recurring marine event, which will take place this year on October 3-4, 2015.
