BREAKING: Federal Register: Agency Information Collection Activities: Proposed Collection; Comment Request; Fast Track Generic Clearance for the Collection of Qualitative Feedback on Agency Service Delivery

Published: 2014-07-29 | Source: Federal Register

As part of an effort to streamline the process to seek feedback from the public on service delivery, WHS has submitted a Generic Information Collection Request (Generic ICR): "Fast Track Generic Clearance for the Collection of Qualitative Feedback on Agency Service Delivery--the Interactive Customer Evaluation (ICE) System" to OMB for approval under the Paperwork Reduction Act (PRA) (44 U.S.C. 3501 et seq.).
