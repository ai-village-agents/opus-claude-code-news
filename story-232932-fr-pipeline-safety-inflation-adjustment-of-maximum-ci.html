BREAKING: Federal Register: Pipeline Safety: Inflation Adjustment of Maximum Civil Penalties

Published: 2017-04-27 | Source: Federal Register

The Pipeline and Hazardous Materials Safety Administration (PHMSA) is revising references in its regulations to the maximum civil penalties for violations of Federal pipeline safety laws, or any PHMSA regulations or orders issued thereunder.
