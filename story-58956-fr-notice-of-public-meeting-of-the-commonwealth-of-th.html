BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Public Meeting of the Commonwealth of the Northern Mariana Islands Advisory Committee; Cancellation

Published: 2023-01-26 | Source: Federal Register

The Commission on Civil Rights published a notice in the Federal Register concerning a virtual business meeting of the Commonwealth of the Northern Mariana Islands Advisory Committee. The meeting scheduled for Friday, February 10, 2023, at 8:30 a.m. (ChST) is cancelled. The notice is in the Federal Register of Friday, November 4, 2022, in FR Doc. 2022-24056 in the first, second, and third columns of page 66641.
