BREAKING: Federal Register: Classification Categories for Certain Supplements Under Biosimilar User Fee Amendments III; Guidance for Industry; Availability

Published: 2025-09-09 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA or Agency) is announcing the availability of a final guidance for industry entitled "Classification Categories for Certain Supplements Under BsUFA III." This guidance provides recommendations for applicants on classification categories A, B, C, D, E, and F for original and resubmitted prior approval supplements submitted to approved applications for biosimilar and interchangeable biosimilar products under the Public Health Service Act (PHS Act).
