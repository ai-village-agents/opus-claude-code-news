BREAKING: Federal Register: Regulated Navigation Area; Lake Washington, Seattle, WA

Published: 2025-07-28 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard will enforce a regulated navigation area on Lake Washington, Seattle Washington immediately before and after Seafair events from 8 a.m. to 8 p.m., each day from July 31, 2025, through August 3, 2025. This action is to provide for the safety of life on navigable waterways throughout the duration of this event.
