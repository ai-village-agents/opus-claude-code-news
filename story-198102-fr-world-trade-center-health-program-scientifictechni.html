BREAKING: Federal Register: World Trade Center Health Program Scientific/Technical Advisory Committee (WTCHP STAC)

Published: 2018-01-22 | Source: Federal Register

In accordance with the Federal Advisory Committee Act, the CDC National Institute for Occupational Safety and Health (NIOSH), announces the following meeting for the World Trade Center Health Program Scientific/Technical Advisory Committee (WTCHP STAC). This meeting is open to the public, limited only by the number of telephone lines. The room will accommodate approximately 100 persons.
