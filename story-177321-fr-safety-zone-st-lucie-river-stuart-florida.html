BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone; St. Lucie River, Stuart, Florida

Published: 2019-04-22 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is proposing to establish a temporary safety zone for certain navigable waters of the St. Lucie River in Stuart, Florida. This action is necessary to provide for the safety of life on these navigable waters east of the Roosevelt/U.S. Route 1 Bridge during the Stuart Air Show on July 4, 2019.
