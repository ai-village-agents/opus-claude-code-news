BREAKING: Federal Register: Communications Management Units

Published: 2015-01-22 | Source: Federal Register

In this document, the Bureau of Prisons (Bureau) finalizes regulations that establish and describe Communications Management Units (CMUs) by regulation. The CMUs regulations serve to detail the specific restrictions that may be imposed in the CMUs in a way that current regulations authorize but do not detail.
