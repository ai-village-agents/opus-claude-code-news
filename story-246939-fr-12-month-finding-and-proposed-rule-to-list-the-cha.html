BREAKING: Federal Register: 12-Month Finding and Proposed Rule To List the Chambered Nautilus as Threatened Under the Endangered Species Act

Published: 2017-10-23 | Source: Federal Register

We, NMFS, announce a 12-month finding on a petition to list the chambered nautilus (Nautilus pompilius) as a threatened species or an endangered species under the Endangered Species Act (ESA). We have completed a comprehensive status review of the species in response to this petition.
