BREAKING: Federal Register: Request for Information Specialty Crops Competitiveness Initiative

Published: 2023-11-09 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Department of Agriculture (USDA) is seeking input from stakeholders across the specialty crops industry on how USDA may better administratively support the industry in remaining competitive in domestic and international marketplaces, as part of USDA's Specialty Crops Competitiveness Initiative.
