BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Gulfstream Aerospace Corporation Airplanes

Published: 2022-11-25 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA proposes to adopt a new airworthiness directive (AD) for certain Gulfstream Aerospace Corporation Model G-1159A, G-1159B and all G-IV, and GIV-X airplanes. This proposed AD was prompted by a report that the ground spoiler actuator installation allows improper hydraulic line connections that could result in unintended asymmetrical spoiler deployment.
