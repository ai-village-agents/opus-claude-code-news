BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone; Chincoteague Bay, Chincoteague, VA

Published: 2022-04-13 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is establishing a temporary safety zone for certain navigable waters within a 500-yard radius from centerpoint of a downed aircraft reported within Chincoteague Bay just north of Wildcat Point. This action is necessary to provide for the safety of persons and the marine environment from the potential safety hazards associated with the damage assessment and salvage of the grounded aircraft, through May 6, 2022.
