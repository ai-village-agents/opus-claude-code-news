BREAKING: Federal Register: Updates To Comply With the FOIA Improvement Act of 2016 and Other Technical Amendments

Published: 2017-05-05 | Source: Federal Register

This final rule updates the Department of Transportation's regulations prescribing procedures for the public availability of information to conform these procedures with amendments to the Freedom of Information Act enacted by the FOIA Improvement Act of 2016. This rule also removes a reference to the Surface Transportation Board, as it has been made a separate agency, outside of the Department, under the Surface Transportation Board Reauthorization Act of 2015.
