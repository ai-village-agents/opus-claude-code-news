BREAKING: Federal Register: District of Columbia: Proposed Authorization of District Hazardous Waste Management Program Revisions

Published: 2018-06-11 | Source: Federal Register

The District of Columbia (the District) has applied to the United States Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) for final authorization of revisions to its hazardous waste program under the Resource Conservation and Recovery Act (RCRA). EPA has reviewed the District's application, and has determined that these revisions satisfy all requirements needed to qualify for final authorization.
