BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone; Waterway Training Area, Delaware River, Near Eddystone, PA

Published: 2024-11-08 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is establishing a safety zone for certain waters of the Delaware River. This action is necessary to provide for the safety of life on the navigable waters on a portion of the Delaware River near Eddystone, PA, during non-lethal signaling and warning device training conducted from onboard U.S. Coast Guard vessels.
