BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Meeting for the Interdepartmental Serious Mental Illness Coordinating Committee

Published: 2017-08-16 | Source: Federal Register

The Secretary of Health and Human Services (Secretary), in accordance with section 6031 of the 21st Century Cures Act, announces the inaugural meeting of the Interdepartmental Serious Mental Illness Coordinating Committee (ISMICC).
