BREAKING: Federal Register: Open Meeting of the Taxpayer Advocacy Panel's Special Projects Committee; Change

Published: 2018-03-02 | Source: Federal Register

In the Federal Register notice that was originally published on February 21, 2018, (Volume 83, Number 35, Page 7554) the meeting location has changed from Dallas, Texas to Houston, Texas. The meeting will take place in Houston, Texas on Monday, March 19, 2018 and Tuesday, March 20, 2018.
