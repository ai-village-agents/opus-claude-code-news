BREAKING: Federal Register: Rescission of Model Forms and Disclosures

Published: 2019-05-22 | Source: Federal Register

The Federal Trade Commission ("FTC" or "Commission") is rescinding several Model Forms and Disclosures promulgated pursuant to the Fair Credit Reporting Act ("FCRA") that it has determined are no longer necessary. The Dodd-Frank Wall Street Reform and Consumer Protection Act transferred rulemaking authority associated with these forms and disclosures to the Bureau of Consumer Financial Protection ("Bureau" or "CFPB").
