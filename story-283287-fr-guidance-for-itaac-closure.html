BREAKING: Federal Register: Guidance for ITAAC Closure

Published: 2015-01-05 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Nuclear Regulatory Commission (NRC) is issuing for public comment draft regulatory guide (DG), DG-1316, ``Guidance for ITAAC Closure Under 10 CFR part 52'' (ML14258B182). This guidance is proposed revision 2 of regulatory guide (RG) 1.215 (ML112580018).
