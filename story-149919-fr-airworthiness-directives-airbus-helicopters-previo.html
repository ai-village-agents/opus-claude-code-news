BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Airbus Helicopters (Previously Eurocopter France)

Published: 2020-04-23 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA is superseding Airworthiness Directive (AD) 2008-24-04 for Eurocopter France (now Airbus Helicopters) Model AS355E, AS355F, AS355F1, AS355F2, and AS355N helicopters. AD 2008-24-04 required repetitively inspecting the lubricating pump and checking the magnetic chip detector plug (chip detector) and the main gearbox (MGB) oil-sight glass.
