BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Submission for OMB Review; Institute of Education Sciences; Quick Response Information System (QRIS) 2012-2015 System Clearance

Published: 2012-03-26 | Source: Federal Register

The National Center for Education Statistics (NCES) Quick Response Information System (QRIS) consists of the Fast Response Survey System (FRSS) and the Postsecondary Education Quick Information System (PEQIS). The QRIS currently conducts surveys under OMB generic clearance 1850-0733, which expires in June 2012. This submission requests approval to continue the current clearance conditions through 2015.
