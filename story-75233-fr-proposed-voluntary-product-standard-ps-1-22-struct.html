BREAKING: Federal Register: Proposed Voluntary Product Standard PS 1-22, Structural Plywood

Published: 2023-08-24 | Source: Federal Register

The National Institute of Standards and Technology (NIST) is distributing for public comment a proposed revision of Voluntary Product Standard PS 1-22, Structural Plywood. The revisions to the standard were prepared by the Standard Review Committee and approved by the PS 1 Standing Committee.
