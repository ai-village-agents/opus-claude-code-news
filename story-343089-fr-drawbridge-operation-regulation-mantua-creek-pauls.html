BREAKING: Federal Register: Drawbridge Operation Regulation; Mantua Creek, Paulsboro, NJ

Published: 2014-12-30 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard proposes to change the operating regulation that governs the Conrail railroad bridge over Mantua Creek at mile marker 1.4 in Paulsboro, NJ. The bridge owner, Conrail, is modifying the operating system which controls the bridge operations. Cameras will be installed and the bridge will be remotely operated from Mt. Laurel, NJ.
