BREAKING: Federal Register: Changes to Patent Term Adjustment in View of the Federal Circuit Decision in Novartis v. Lee

Published: 2014-06-18 | Source: Federal Register

The United States Patent and Trademark Office (Office) is proposing changes to the rules of practice pertaining to the patent term adjustment provisions in view of the decision by the U.S. Court of Appeals for the Federal Circuit (Federal Circuit) in Novartis AG v. Lee.
