BREAKING: Federal Register: Revisions to the Public Notice Provisions in Clean Air Act Permitting Programs

Published: 2015-12-29 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) proposes to revise the public notice rule provisions for the New Source Review (NSR), title V and Outer Continental Shelf (OCS) permit programs of the Clean Air Act (CAA) and the corresponding onshore area (COA) determinations for implementation of the OCS air quality regulations.
