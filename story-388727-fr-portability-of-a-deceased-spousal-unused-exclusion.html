BREAKING: Federal Register: Portability of a Deceased Spousal Unused Exclusion Amount

Published: 2012-06-18 | Source: Federal Register

This document contains temporary regulations that provide guidance on the estate and gift tax applicable exclusion amount, in general, as well as on the applicable requirements for electing portability of a deceased spousal unused exclusion (DSUE) amount to the surviving spouse and on the applicable rules for the surviving spouse's use of this DSUE amount.
