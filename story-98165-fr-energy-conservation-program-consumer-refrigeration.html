BREAKING: Federal Register: Energy Conservation Program: Consumer Refrigeration and Miscellaneous Refrigeration Products

Published: 2022-06-13 | Source: Federal Register

On July 18, 2016, the U.S. Department of Energy published a final rule that amended the test procedure for refrigerators and refrigerator-freezers and established both coverage and procedures for testing miscellaneous refrigeration products ("MREFs").
