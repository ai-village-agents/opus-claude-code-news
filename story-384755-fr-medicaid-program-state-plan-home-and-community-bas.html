BREAKING: Federal Register: Medicaid Program; State Plan Home and Community-Based Services, 5-Year Period for Waivers, Provider Payment Reassignment, and Setting Requirements for Community First Choice; Correction

Published: 2012-05-03 | Source: Federal Register

This document corrects a technical error that appeared in the proposed rule published elsewhere in this Federal Register entitled "Medicaid Program; State Plan Home and Community-Based Services, 5- Year Period for Waivers, Provider Payment Reassignment, and Setting Requirements for Community First Choice." The proposed rule was intended to carry a 60-day comment period, but was submitted with a 30- day comment period. This document corrects that error.
