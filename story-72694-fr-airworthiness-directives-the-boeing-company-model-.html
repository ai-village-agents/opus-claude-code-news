BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; The Boeing Company Model Airplanes

Published: 2023-07-24 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA proposes to adopt a new airworthiness directive (AD) for certain The Boeing Company Model 757-200, -200CB, and -200PF series airplanes. This proposed AD was prompted by a crack growth analysis, which indicated that current inspections are not adequate to detect cracks in certain sections of the upper frame at the frame splice between certain stringers before a single frame fails.
