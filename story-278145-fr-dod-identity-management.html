BREAKING: Federal Register: DoD Identity Management

Published: 2016-11-02 | Source: Federal Register

This rulemaking establishes implementation guidelines for DS Logon to provide a secure means of authentication to applications containing personally identifiable information (PII) and personal health information (PHI). This will allow beneficiaries and other individuals with a continuing affiliation with DoD to update pay or health-care information in a secure environment.
