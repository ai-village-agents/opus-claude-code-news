BREAKING: Federal Register: Texas Central Railroad High-Speed Rail Safety Standards

Published: 2020-03-12 | Source: Federal Register

On March 10, 2020, FRA published a notice of proposed rulemaking (NPRM) that would establish safety standards for the Texas Central Railroad (TCRR) high speed rail system. FRA is announcing three public hearings to provide members of the public an opportunity to provide oral comments on the proposed safety requirements.
