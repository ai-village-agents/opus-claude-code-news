BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Intent To Release Airport Property for Non-Aeronautical Use; Hyannis, Massachusetts

Published: 2014-01-21 | Source: Federal Register

The Federal Aviation Administration (FAA) is considering a proposal to change 53.55 acres of airport land from aeronautical use to non-aeronautical use and to authorize the lease of the land for non- aeronautical purposes at the Barnstable Municipal Airport-Boardman/ Polando Field, Hyannis, Massachusetts.
