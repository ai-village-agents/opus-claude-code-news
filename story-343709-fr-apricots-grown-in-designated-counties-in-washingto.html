BREAKING: Federal Register: Apricots Grown in Designated Counties in Washington; Temporary Suspension of Handling Regulations

Published: 2013-01-08 | Source: Federal Register

This rule suspends the minimum grade, size, quality, maturity, and inspection requirements prescribed under the Washington apricot marketing order for the 2012-13 fiscal period. The marketing order regulates the handling of apricots grown in designated Counties in Washington and is administered locally by the Washington Apricot Marketing Committee (Committee).
