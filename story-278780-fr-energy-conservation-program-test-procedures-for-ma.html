BREAKING: Federal Register: Energy Conservation Program: Test Procedures for Manufactured Housing

Published: 2016-11-09 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Department of Energy (DOE) is publishing a proposed rule to establish test procedures for manufactured housing (MH). This test procedure would support standards DOE is directed to establish by the Energy Independence and Security Act of 2007.
