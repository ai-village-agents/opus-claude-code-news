BREAKING: Federal Register: Request for Comment on Proposed Statement of Policy on Bank Merger Transactions

Published: 2024-04-19 | Source: Federal Register

The FDIC invites comments on a proposed Statement of Policy (SOP) on Bank Merger Transactions (Proposed SOP) that is relevant to all insured depository institutions (IDIs). The Proposed SOP would replace the FDIC's current SOP on Bank Merger Transactions (Current SOP) and proposes a principles-based overview that describes the FDIC's administration of its responsibilities under the Bank Merger Act (BMA).
