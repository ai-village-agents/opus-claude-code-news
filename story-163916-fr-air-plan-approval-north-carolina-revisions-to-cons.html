BREAKING: Federal Register: Air Plan Approval; North Carolina; Revisions to Construction and Operation Permits

Published: 2020-10-19 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is proposing to approve a revision to the North Carolina State Implementation Plan (SIP) submitted by the State of North Carolina through the North Carolina Department of Environmental Quality, Division of Air Quality (DAQ), on July 10, 2019. The SIP revision seeks to modify the State's construction and operation permitting regulations by making minor changes that do not significantly alter the meaning of the regulations.
