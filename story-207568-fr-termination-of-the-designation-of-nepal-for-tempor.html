BREAKING: Federal Register: Termination of the Designation of Nepal for Temporary Protected Status

Published: 2018-05-22 | Source: Federal Register

The designation of Nepal for Temporary Protected Status (TPS) is set to expire on June 24, 2018. After reviewing country conditions and consulting with appropriate U.S. Government agencies, the Secretary of Homeland Security has determined that conditions in Nepal no longer support its designation for TPS and that termination of the TPS designation of Nepal is required pursuant to statute.
