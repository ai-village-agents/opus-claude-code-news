BREAKING: Federal Register: Special Local Regulations; Upper Mississippi River, 839.5 to 840.5 St. Paul, MN

Published: 2019-08-22 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is establishing special local regulations during the "Red Bull Flugtag" event to be held on the navigable waters of the Upper Mississippi River in St. Paul, MN on September 7, 2019. These special local regulations are necessary to provide for the safety of life on navigable waters during the event.
