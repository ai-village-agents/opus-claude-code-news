BREAKING: Federal Register: Unique Electronic Identification of Commercial Motor Vehicles

Published: 2022-09-23 | Source: Federal Register

FMCSA requests public comment on whether the agency should amend the Federal Motor Carrier Safety Regulations to require every commercial motor vehicle (CMV) operating in interstate commerce to be equipped with electronic identification (ID) technology capable of wirelessly communicating a unique ID number when queried by a Federal or State motor carrier safety enforcement personnel.
