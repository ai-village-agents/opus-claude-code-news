BREAKING: Federal Register: Best Practices for Continuous Monitoring of Temperature and Flow in Wadeable Streams

Published: 2013-10-31 | Source: Federal Register

EPA is announcing a 30-day public comment period for the draft document titled, "Best Practices for Continuous Monitoring of Temperature and Flow in Wadeable Streams" (EPA/600/R-13/170). The EPA also is announcing that either ERG or Versar, EPA contractors for external scientific peer review, will select an independent group of experts to conduct a letter peer review of the draft document.
