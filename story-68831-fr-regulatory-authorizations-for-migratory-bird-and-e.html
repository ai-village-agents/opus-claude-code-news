BREAKING: Federal Register: Regulatory Authorizations for Migratory Bird and Eagle Possession by the General Public, Educators, and Government Agencies

Published: 2023-06-01 | Source: Federal Register

We, the U.S. Fish and Wildlife Service (Service), propose to amend our regulations to revise current regulatory authorizations and add new regulatory authorizations for possession of migratory birds and eagles and other purposes.
