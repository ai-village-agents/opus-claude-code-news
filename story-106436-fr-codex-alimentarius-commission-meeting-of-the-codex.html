BREAKING: Federal Register: Codex Alimentarius Commission: Meeting of the Codex Alimentarius Commission

Published: 2022-09-29 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Codex Office is sponsoring a virtual public meeting on October 26, 2022. The objective of the public meeting is to provide information and receive public comments on agenda items and draft United States (U.S.) positions to be discussed at the 45th Session of the Codex Alimentarius Commission (CAC), which will take place from November 21-25, 2022, with the report adoption on December 12-13, 2022.
