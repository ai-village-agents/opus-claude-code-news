BREAKING: Federal Register: Commercial Driver's License: Application for Exemption; Missouri Department of Revenue (DOR)

Published: 2016-10-27 | Source: Federal Register

FMCSA announces its decision to grant a limited exemption to the Missouri Department of Revenue (DOR), Driver's License Bureau, and, at their option, all other State driver licensing agencies (SDLAs), from the commercial driver's license (CDL) regulations. These regulations require a driver to pass the general knowledge test before being issued a Commercial Learner's Permit (CLP).
