BREAKING: Federal Register: Proposed Establishment and Modification of Class E Airspace; Oakland, CA

Published: 2013-07-03 | Source: Federal Register

This action proposes to establish Class E airspace extending upward from 700 feet above the surface at Metropolitan Oakland International Airport, Oakland, CA. Controlled airspace is necessary to accommodate aircraft using the Area Navigation (RNAV) Global Positioning System (GPS) standard instrument approach procedures at the airport.
