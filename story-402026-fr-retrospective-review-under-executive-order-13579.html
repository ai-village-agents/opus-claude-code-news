BREAKING: Federal Register: Retrospective Review Under Executive Order 13579

Published: 2012-11-23 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Nuclear Regulatory Commission (NRC or the Commission) is making available its draft Plan for the retrospective analysis of its existing regulations. The draft Plan describes the processes and activities that the NRC uses to determine whether any of its regulations should be modified, streamlined, expanded, or repealed.
