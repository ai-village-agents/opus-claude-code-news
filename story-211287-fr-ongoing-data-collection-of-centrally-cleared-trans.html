BREAKING: Federal Register: Ongoing Data Collection of Centrally Cleared Transactions in the U.S. Repurchase Agreement Market

Published: 2018-07-10 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Department of the Treasury's Office of Financial Research (the "Office") is requesting comment on a proposed rule establishing a data collection covering centrally cleared transactions in the U.S. repurchase agreement market. This proposed collection will require daily reporting to the Office by covered central counterparties.
