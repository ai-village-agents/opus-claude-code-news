BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone; City of Erie 4th July Fireworks; Presque Isle Bay, Erie, PA

Published: 2019-06-26 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is establishing a temporary safety zone for navigable waters within a 280-foot radius of the launch site located near the Bicentennial Tower, Erie, PA. This safety zone is intended to restrict vessels from portions of Presque Isle Bay during the City of Erie 4th of July fireworks display.
