BREAKING: Federal Register: Disclosure of Order Execution Information

Published: 2024-04-15 | Source: Federal Register

The Securities and Exchange Commission ("Commission" or "SEC") is adopting amendments to a rule under the Securities Exchange Act of 1934 ("Exchange Act") that requires disclosures for order executions in national market system ("NMS") stocks. First, the amendments expand the scope of reporting entities subject to the preexisting rule that requires market centers to make available to the public monthly execution quality reports to encompass broker-dealers with a larger number of customers.
