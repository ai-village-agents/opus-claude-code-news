BREAKING: Federal Register: Request for Information; Direct Rental Assistance

Published: 2024-07-23 | Source: Federal Register

In the Housing Choice Voucher (HCV) program, rental housing subsidies are provided on behalf of an eligible low-income renter to the landlord of a rental unit. Recently, some researchers, advocates, policymakers, and public housing agencies have expressed interest in testing a "direct rental assistance" model.
