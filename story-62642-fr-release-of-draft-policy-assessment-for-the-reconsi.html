BREAKING: Federal Register: Release of Draft Policy Assessment for the Reconsideration of the Ozone National Ambient Air Quality Standards

This draft document was prepared as a part of the current reconsideration of the 2020 final decision on the national ambient air quality standards (NAAQS) for ozone (O3).
