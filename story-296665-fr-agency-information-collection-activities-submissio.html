BREAKING: Federal Register: Agency Information Collection Activities; Submission for OMB Review; Comment Request; O*NET Data Collection Program

Published: 2015-06-17 | Source: Federal Register

On June 30, 2015, the Department of Labor (DOL) will submit the Employment and Training Administration (ETA) sponsored information collection request (ICR) revision titled, "O*NET Data Collection Program" to the Office of Management and Budget (OMB) for review and approval for use in accordance with the Paperwork Reduction Act (PRA) of 1995 (44 U.S.C. 3501 et seq.). Public comments on the ICR are invited.
