BREAKING: Federal Register: National Climate Assessment and Development Advisory Committee

Published: 2013-03-22 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Commerce's Chief Financial Officer and Assistant Secretary for Administration has renewed the charter for the National Climate Assessment and Development Advisory Committee (NCADAC) for a six-month period, through July 10, 2013. The NCADAC is a federal advisory committee under the Federal Advisory Committee Act (Pub. L. 92-463).
