BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; The Boeing Company Airplanes

Published: 2022-12-22 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA is superseding Airworthiness Directive (AD) 2021-14- 20, which applied to all The Boeing Company Model 737 airplanes. AD 2021-14-20 required repetitive functional tests of the cabin altitude pressure switches, and on-condition actions, including replacement, if necessary. AD 2021-14-20 also required reporting test results.
