BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Airbus SAS Airplanes

Published: 2019-04-11 | Source: Federal Register

We are adopting a new airworthiness directive (AD) for all Airbus SAS Model A330-223, A330-223F, A330-321, A330-322, and A330-323 airplanes. This AD was prompted by a report of fatigue cracking in the latch beam gussets on a certain thrust reverser (T/R).
