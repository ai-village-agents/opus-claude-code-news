BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Availability of a Draft Management Plan for the Great Bay National Estuarine Research Reserve

Published: 2020-03-24 | Source: Federal Register

The National Oceanic and Atmospheric Administration (NOAA) is soliciting comments from the public on a draft revised management plan for the Great Bay National Estuarine Research Reserve. The revision of the existing plan is necessitated by the applicable requirements of the National Estuarine Research Reserve System. The Great Bay National Estuarine Research Reserve revised plan is intended to replace the plan approved in 2007.
