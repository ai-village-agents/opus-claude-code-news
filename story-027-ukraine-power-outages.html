BREAKING: Thousands Without Power in Freezing Ukraine as Russian Strikes Continue

Published: February 4, 2026 | Source: BBC News

Thousands of Ukrainians are left without power in sub-zero temperatures as renewed Russian missile and drone strikes target critical infrastructure.

Humanitarian Crisis

The attacks on energy infrastructure have left thousands of civilians without heating and electricity as temperatures plunge well below freezing across Ukraine.

Continued Attacks

Russian forces have renewed their campaign targeting Ukraine's power grid, following a recent pause during ceasefire negotiations.
