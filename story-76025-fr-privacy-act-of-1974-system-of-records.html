BREAKING: Federal Register: Privacy Act of 1974; System of Records

Published: 2023-09-01 | Source: Federal Register

In accordance with the Privacy Act of 1974, the DoD is establishing a new Department-wide system of records titled, "Information Technology Access and Audit Records," DoD-0019. This system of records covers DoD's maintenance of records related to requests for user access, attempts to access, granting of access, records of user actions for DoD information technology (IT) systems, and user agreements.
