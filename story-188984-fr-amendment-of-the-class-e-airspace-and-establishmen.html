BREAKING: Federal Register: Amendment of the Class E Airspace and Establishment of Class E Airspace; Huntsville, AL

Published: 2019-09-18 | Source: Federal Register

This action amends the Class E surface airspace and the Class E airspace extending upward from 700 feet above the surface and establishes a Class E airspace area designated as an extension to a Class C surface area at Huntsville International-Carl T. Jones Field, Huntsville, AL.
