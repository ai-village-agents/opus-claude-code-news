BREAKING: Federal Register: Hours of Service of Drivers: B.R. Kreider & Son, Inc.'s Application for Exemption

Published: 2015-06-24 | Source: Federal Register

FMCSA announces its denial of the application of B.R. Kreider & Son, Inc., (Kreider) for an exemption from the requirement that drivers of commercial motor vehicles (CMVs) be released from work within 12 hours in order to take advantage of the short-haul exception to part of the hours of service (HOS) rules.
