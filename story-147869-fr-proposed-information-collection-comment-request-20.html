BREAKING: Federal Register: Proposed Information Collection; Comment Request; 2020-2022 Report of Organization

Published: 2020-03-30 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Commerce, as part of its continuing effort to reduce paperwork and respondent burden, invites the general public and other Federal agencies to take this opportunity to comment on a proposed extension of the Report of Organization, as required by the Paperwork Reduction Act of 1995.
