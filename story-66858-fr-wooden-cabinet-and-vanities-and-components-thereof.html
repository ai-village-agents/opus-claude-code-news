BREAKING: Federal Register: Wooden Cabinet and Vanities and Components Thereof From the People's Republic of China: Preliminary Results, Preliminary Determination of No Shipments, and Partial Rescission of the Antidumping Duty Administrative Review; 2021-2022

Published: 2023-05-05 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Department of Commerce (Commerce) preliminarily determines that certain producers and or/exporters made sales of wooden cabinets and vanities and components thereof (cabinets) at less than normal value, and eight companies had no shipments of subject merchandise during the period of review (POR) April 1, 2021, through March 31, 2022.
