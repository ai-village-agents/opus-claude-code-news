BREAKING: Federal Register: Security Zone; Protection of Military Cargo, Captain of the Port Zone Puget Sound, WA

Published: 2013-02-01 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard will enforce the Sitcum Waterway Security Zone in Commencement Bay, Tacoma, Washington from 6 a.m. on February 1, 2013, through 11:59 p.m. on February 7, 2013, unless cancelled sooner by the Captain of the Port. This action is necessary for the security of Department of Defense assets and military cargo in the navigable waters of Puget Sound and adjacent waters.
