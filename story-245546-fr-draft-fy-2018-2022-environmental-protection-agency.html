BREAKING: Federal Register: Draft FY 2018-2022 Environmental Protection Agency Strategic Plan

Published: 2017-10-05 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is announcing the availability of the Draft FY 2018-2022 EPA Strategic Plan for public review and comment, which is being revised as required by the Government Performance and Results Act (GPRA) Modernization Act of 2010. The agency anticipates the final Strategic Plan will be submitted to Congress in February 2018.
