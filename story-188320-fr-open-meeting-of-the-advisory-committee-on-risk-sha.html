BREAKING: Federal Register: Open Meeting of the Advisory Committee on Risk-Sharing Mechanisms

Published: 2019-09-10 | Source: Federal Register

This notice announces that the U.S. Department of the Treasury's Advisory Committee on Risk-Sharing Mechanisms (ACRSM) will convene a meeting on Monday, September 30, 2019, in the Cash Room, Room 2121, U.S. Department of the Treasury, 1500 Pennsylvania Ave. NW, Washington, DC 20220, from 1:00 p.m.-3:00 p.m. Eastern Time. The meeting is open to the public, and the site is accessible to individuals with disabilities.
