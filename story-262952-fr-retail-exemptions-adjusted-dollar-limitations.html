BREAKING: Federal Register: Retail Exemptions Adjusted Dollar Limitations

Published: 2016-05-09 | Source: Federal Register

The Food Safety and Inspection Service (FSIS) is announcing the dollar limitations on the amount of meat and meat food products, poultry, and poultry products that a retail store can sell to hotels, restaurants, and similar institutions without disqualifying itself for exemption from Federal inspection requirements.
