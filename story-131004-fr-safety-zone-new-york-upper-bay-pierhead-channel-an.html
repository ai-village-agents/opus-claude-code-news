BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone; New York Upper Bay, Pierhead Channel, and Port Jersey Channel, Bayonne, NJ

Published: 2021-08-19 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is establishing a temporary safety zone for navigable waters of New York Upper Bay, Pierhead Channel, and Port Jersey Channel within 100 yards of the Bayonne Peninsula bulkhead. The safety zone is needed to protect personnel, vessels, and the marine environment from potential hazards created by two building demolitions on shore.
