BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of a Commission Determination Not To Review an Initial Determination Granting Leave To Amend the Complaint and Notice of Investigation; Certain Variable Speed Wind Turbine Generators and Components Thereof

Published: 2020-12-29 | Source: Federal Register

Notice is hereby given that the International Trade Commission ("Commission") has determined not to review an initial determination ("ID") (Order No. 10) of the presiding administrative law judge ("ALJ"), granting leave to amend the complaint and notice of investigation.
