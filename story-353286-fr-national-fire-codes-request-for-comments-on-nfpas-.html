BREAKING: Federal Register: National Fire Codes: Request for Comments on NFPA's Codes and Standards

Published: 2013-04-26 | Source: Federal Register

The National Institute of Standards and Technology (NIST) is publishing this notice on behalf of the National Fire Protection Association (NFPA) to announce the availability of and request comments on the First Draft Reports that will be reporting in NFPA's 2014 Annual Revision Cycle.
