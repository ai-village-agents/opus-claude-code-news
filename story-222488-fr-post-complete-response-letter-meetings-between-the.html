BREAKING: Federal Register: Post-Complete Response Letter Meetings Between the Food and Drug Administration and Abbreviated New Drug Application Applicants Under Generic Drug User Fee Amendments; Guidance for Industry; Availability

Published: 2018-12-04 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA or Agency) is announcing the availability of a final guidance for industry entitled "Post- Complete Response Letter Meetings Between FDA and ANDA Applicants Under GDUFA." This guidance is intended to clarify the criteria for granting post-complete response letter (CRL) meeting requests and the scope of discussions for granted meeting requests.
