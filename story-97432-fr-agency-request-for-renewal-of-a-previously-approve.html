BREAKING: Federal Register: Agency Request for Renewal of a Previously Approved Information Collection: Exemptions for Air Taxi Operations

Published: 2022-06-03 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Transportation (DOT) invites public comments about our intention to request the Office of Management and Budget (OMB)'s approval to renew an information collection. The collection involves a classification of air carriers known as air taxi operators and their filings of a one-page form that enables them to obtain economic authority from DOT.
