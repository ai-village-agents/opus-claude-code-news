BREAKING: Federal Register: Waybill Sample Reporting

Published: 2020-09-03 | Source: Federal Register

The Surface Transportation Board (Board) adopts a final rule that amends its Waybill Sample data collection regulations by increasing the sampling rates of certain non-intermodal carload shipments, specifying separate sampling strata and rates for intermodal shipments, and eliminating the manual system for reporting waybill data.
