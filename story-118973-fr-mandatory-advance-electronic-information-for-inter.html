BREAKING: Federal Register: Mandatory Advance Electronic Information for International Mail Shipments

Published: 2021-03-15 | Source: Federal Register

To address the threat of synthetic opioids and other dangerous items coming to the United States in international mail shipments and to implement the requirements of the Synthetics Trafficking and Overdose Prevention Act of 2018 (STOP Act), U.S. Customs and Border Protection (CBP) is amending its regulations to require the United States Postal Service (USPS) to transmit certain advance electronic information to CBP.
