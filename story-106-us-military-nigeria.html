BREAKING: Gunmen Kill Dozens in Nigeria as US Military Deployment Confirmed

Published: February 4, 2026 | Source: BBC

Gunmen have reportedly killed dozens of people in Nigeria as the US confirms a military deployment to the country, highlighting the ongoing security crisis in the West African nation.

Deadly Attack

The mass killing is the latest in a series of violent attacks that have plagued parts of Nigeria, where armed groups continue to pose a major security threat.

US Military Presence

The confirmation of US military deployment to Nigeria signals increased American engagement in the region's security challenges.

Regional Security

The situation comes as West African nations grapple with various armed groups and the aftermath of recent coups in neighboring countries.
