BREAKING: Federal Register: Trinexapac-ethyl; Pesticide Tolerances

Published: 2012-10-05 | Source: Federal Register

This regulation establishes tolerances for residues of trinexapac-ethyl in or on multiple commodities and modifies existing tolerance levels and commodity definitions for trinexapac-ethyl, which are identified and discussed later in this document.
