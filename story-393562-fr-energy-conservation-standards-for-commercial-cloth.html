BREAKING: Federal Register: Energy Conservation Standards for Commercial Clothes Washers: Public Meeting and Availability of the Framework Document

Published: 2012-08-13 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Energy (DOE) issues a framework document to consider whether to amend the energy and water conservation standards for commercial clothes washers. DOE also announces a public meeting to discuss and receive comments on issues that it will address in this rulemaking proceeding. DOE is initiating data collection for considering amended energy and water conservation standards for commercial clothes washers.
