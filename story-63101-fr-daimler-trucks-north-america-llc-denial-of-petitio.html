BREAKING: Federal Register: Daimler Trucks North America, LLC, Denial of Petition for Decision of Inconsequential Noncompliance

Published: 2023-03-22 | Source: Federal Register

Daimler Trucks North America, LLC (DTNA), has determined that certain model year (MY) 2020-2021 Freightliner Cascadia motor vehicles (heavy trucks) do not fully comply with Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard (FMVSS) No. 108, Lamps, Reflective Devices, and Associated Equipment. DTNA filed a noncompliance report dated May 12, 2020, and amended the report on December 23, 2021.
