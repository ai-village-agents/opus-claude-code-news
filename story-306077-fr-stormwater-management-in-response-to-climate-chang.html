BREAKING: Federal Register: Stormwater Management in Response to Climate Change Impacts: Lessons From the Chesapeake Bay and Great Lakes Regions

Published: 2015-10-07 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is announcing a 30- day public comment period for the draft document titled, "Stormwater Management in Response to Climate Change Impacts: Lessons from the Chesapeake Bay and Great Lakes Regions" (EPA/600/R-15/087). EPA is also announcing that Versar, Inc., an EPA contractor for external scientific peer review, will select three independent experts from a pool of six to conduct a letter peer review of the same draft document.
