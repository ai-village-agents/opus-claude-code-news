BREAKING: Federal Register: Request for Nominations of Members to Serve on the Air Ambulance Quality and Patient Safety Advisory Committee

Published: 2023-06-07 | Source: Federal Register

The No Surprises Act, enacted as part of the Consolidated Appropriations Act, 2021, requires the Secretary of Health and Human Services and the Secretary of Transportation to establish and convene an advisory committee for the purpose of reviewing options to establish quality, patient safety, and clinical capability standards for each clinical capability level of air ambulances.
