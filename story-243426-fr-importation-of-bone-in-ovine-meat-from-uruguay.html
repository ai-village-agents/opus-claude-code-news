BREAKING: Federal Register: Importation of Bone-In Ovine Meat From Uruguay

Published: 2017-09-12 | Source: Federal Register

We are amending the regulations governing the importation of certain animals, meat, and other animal products by allowing, under certain conditions, the importation of bone-in ovine meat from Uruguay. Based on the evidence in a risk assessment that we prepared, we believe that bone-in ovine meat can safely be imported from Uruguay provided certain conditions are met.
