BREAKING: Federal Register: Energy Conservation Program: Test Procedure for Commercial Warm Air Furnaces

Published: 2022-02-25 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Department of Energy ("DOE") proposes to amend the test procedures for commercial warm air furnaces ("CWAFs") to incorporate the latest versions of the industry standards that are currently incorporated by reference. DOE also proposes to establish a new metric, Thermal Efficiency Two ("TE2"), and corresponding test procedure.
