BREAKING: Federal Register: North Anna Power Station Independent Spent Fuel Storage Installation

Published: 2018-02-02 | Source: Federal Register

The NRC has prepared an environmental assessment (EA) for this proposed license renewal in accordance with its regulations in title 10 of the Code of Federal Regulations (10 CFR).
