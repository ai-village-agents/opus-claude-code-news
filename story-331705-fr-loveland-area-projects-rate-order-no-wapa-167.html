BREAKING: Federal Register: Loveland Area Projects-Rate Order No. WAPA-167

Published: 2014-08-08 | Source: Federal Register

The Western Area Power Administration (Western) Loveland Area Projects' (LAP) Rate Schedule L-F9 expires December 31, 2014. LAP consists of the Fryingpan-Arkansas Project (Fry-Ark) and the Pick-Sloan Missouri Basin Program (P-SMBP)--Western Division, which were integrated for marketing and rate-making purposes in 1989.
