BREAKING: Federal Register: Use of Electronic Signatures by Medical Licensees on Internal Documents

Published: 2018-08-30 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Nuclear Regulatory Commission (NRC) is seeking public comment on a draft regulatory issue summary (RIS). The draft RIS describes one means by which medical licensees can use electronic signatures to satisfy NRC's signature requirements on internal records that the NRC requires the licensee to maintain.
