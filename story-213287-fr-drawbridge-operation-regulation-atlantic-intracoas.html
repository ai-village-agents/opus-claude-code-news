BREAKING: Federal Register: Drawbridge Operation Regulation; Atlantic Intracoastal Waterway (AIWW), Wrightsville Beach, NC and Northeast Cape Fear River, Wilmington, NC

Holmes Bridge across the Northeast Cape Fear River, mile 1.0, at Wilmington, NC.
