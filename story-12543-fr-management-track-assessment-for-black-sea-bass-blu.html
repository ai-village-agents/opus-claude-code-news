BREAKING: Federal Register: Management Track Assessment for Black Sea Bass, Bluefish, Scup, and Summer Flounder; Public Meeting

Published: 2025-05-30 | Source: Federal Register

NMFS will convene the Management Track Assessment Peer Review Meeting for the purpose of reviewing black sea bass, bluefish, scup, and summer flounder stocks. The Management Track Assessment Peer Review is a formal scientific peer-review process for evaluating and presenting stock assessment results to managers for fish stocks in the offshore U.S. waters of the northwest Atlantic.
