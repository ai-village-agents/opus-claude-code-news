BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Advisory Council on Historic Preservation Quarterly Business Meeting

Published: 2022-10-17 | Source: Federal Register

Notice is hereby given that the Advisory Council on Historic Preservation (ACHP) will have its next quarterly meeting on Wednesday, October 26, 2022, from 1:30 p.m. to 4:30 p.m. Eastern Time. The meeting will be held through videoconferencing and a recording will be made available afterwards on www.achp.gov.
