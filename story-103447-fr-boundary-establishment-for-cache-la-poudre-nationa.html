BREAKING: Federal Register: Boundary Establishment for Cache la Poudre National Wild and Scenic River, Roosevelt National Forest and Rocky Mountain National Park, Larimer County, Colorado

Published: 2022-08-22 | Source: Federal Register

In accordance with Section 3(b) of the Wild and Scenic Rivers Act, the USDA Forest Service, Washington Office, is transmitting the final boundary for the Cache la Poudre National Wild and Scenic River to Congress.
