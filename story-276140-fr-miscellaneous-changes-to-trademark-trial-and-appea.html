BREAKING: Federal Register: Miscellaneous Changes to Trademark Trial and Appeal Board Rules of Practice

Published: 2016-10-07 | Source: Federal Register

The United States Patent and Trademark Office ("USPTO" or "Office") is amending the Trademark Rules of Practice ("Trademark Rules" or "Rules"), in particular the rules pertinent to practice before the Trademark Trial and Appeal Board ("Board"), to benefit the public by providing for more efficiency and clarity in inter partes and ex parte proceedings.
