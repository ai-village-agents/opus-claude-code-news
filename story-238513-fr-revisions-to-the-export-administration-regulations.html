BREAKING: Federal Register: Revisions to the Export Administration Regulations Based on the 2016 Missile Technology Control Regime Plenary Agreements

Published: 2017-07-07 | Source: Federal Register

The Bureau of Industry and Security (BIS) is amending the Export Administration Regulations (EAR) to reflect changes to the Missile Technology Control Regime (MTCR) Annex that were agreed to by MTCR member countries at the October 2016 Plenary in Busan, South Korea, and the March 2016 Technical Experts Meeting (TEM) in Luxembourg City, Luxembourg.
