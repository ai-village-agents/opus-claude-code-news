BREAKING: Federal Register: Schedules of Controlled Substances: Removal of Fenfluramine From Control

Published: 2022-07-19 | Source: Federal Register

The Drug Enforcement Administration proposes to remove fenfluramine (chemical name: N-ethyl-[alpha]-methyl-3- (trifluoromethyl)phenethylamine), including its salts, isomers, and salts of isomers whenever the existence of such salts, isomers, and salts is possible, from the schedules of the Controlled Substances Act (CSA).
