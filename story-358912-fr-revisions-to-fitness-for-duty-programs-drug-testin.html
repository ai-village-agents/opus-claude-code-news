BREAKING: Federal Register: Revisions to Fitness for Duty Programs' Drug Testing Requirements

Published: 2013-07-01 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Nuclear Regulatory Commission (NRC) is making available the regulatory basis for the ongoing proposed rulemaking effort to amend its regulations regarding drug testing requirements in NRC licensees' fitness for duty programs. The regulatory basis documents the reasoning upon which the NRC determined rulemaking was the appropriate course of action.
