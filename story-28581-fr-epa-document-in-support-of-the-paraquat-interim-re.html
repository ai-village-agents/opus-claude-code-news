BREAKING: Federal Register: EPA Document in Support of the Paraquat Interim Registration Review Decision; Notice of Availability

Published: 2024-02-01 | Source: Federal Register

In July 2021, the Environmental Protection Agency (EPA or the Agency) completed the Paraquat Dichloride Interim Registration Review Decision (Paraquat ID). The Paraquat ID finalized certain portions of EPA's analysis of paraquat's risks and benefits and determined that certain mitigation measures were necessary for paraquat to meet the Federal Insecticide, Fungicide, and Rodenticide Act (FIFRA) standard for registration.
