BREAKING: Federal Register: Agency Information Collection Activities: Announcement of Board Approval Under Delegated Authority and Submission to OMB

Published: 2020-12-22 | Source: Federal Register

The Board of Governors of the Federal Reserve System (Board) is adopting a proposal to extend for three years, with revision, the Reports of Deposits (FR 2900, FR 2915; OMB No. 7100-0087). The revisions to the weekly collection of the FR 2900 are effective for the report as-of-date April 12, 2021. The revisions to the FR 2915 are applicable for the report as-of-date June 21, 2021.
