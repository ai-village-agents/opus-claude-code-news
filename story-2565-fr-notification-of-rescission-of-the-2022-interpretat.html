BREAKING: Federal Register: Notification of Rescission of the 2022 Interpretation of Section 188 of the Workforce Innovation and Opportunity Act

Published: 2026-01-08 | Source: Federal Register

This notice rescinds guidance that defines the term "sex" for purposes of the Department of Labor's anti-discrimination provisions of its workforce funding and development programs.
