BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone; Recurring Safety Zone in Captain of the Port Sault Sainte Marie Zone

Published: 2021-05-11 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is proposing to amend its recurring safety zone regulations in the Captain of the Port Sault Sainte Marie Zone. This proposed rule would update one safety zone location and dates. This proposed amendment action is necessary to provide for the safety of life associated with annual marine events and firework displays on these navigable waters near Mackinaw City, MI.
