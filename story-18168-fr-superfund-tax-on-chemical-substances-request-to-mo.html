BREAKING: Federal Register: Superfund Tax on Chemical Substances; Request To Modify List of Taxable Substances; Notice of Filing for Ethylene-Propylene-Dicyclopentadiene Rubber (m=73.18, n=26.53, o=0.29)

Published: 2025-08-15 | Source: Federal Register

This notice of filing announces that a petition has been filed requesting that ethylene-propylene-dicyclopentadiene rubber ((C2H4)m- (C3H6)n- (C10H12)o; m=73.18, n=26.53, o=0.29) be added to the list of taxable substances. This notice of filing also requests comments on the petition. This notice of filing is not a determination that the list of taxable substances is modified.
