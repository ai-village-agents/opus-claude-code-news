BREAKING: Federal Register: Saline Valley Warm Springs Management Plan/Environmental Impact Statement, Death Valley National Park, Inyo County, CA

In accordance with Sec. 102(2)(C) of the National Environmental Policy Act of 1969, the National Park Service (NPS) is initiating the conservation planning and environmental impact analysis process for the Saline Valley Warm Springs Management Plan for Death Valley National Park.
