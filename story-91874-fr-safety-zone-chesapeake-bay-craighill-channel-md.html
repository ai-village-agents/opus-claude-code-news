BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone; Chesapeake Bay, Craighill Channel, MD

Published: 2022-03-24 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is extending the duration of a temporary safety zone on certain navigable waters of the Chesapeake Bay. This action is necessary to provide for the safety of persons and the marine environment from the potential safety hazards associated with the damage assessment and salvage of the grounded freight ship EVER FORWARD, through 9 p.m. on April 13, 2022.
