BREAKING: Federal Register: Emergent Biosolutions Inc.; Withdrawal of Approval of a Supplemental New Drug Application for NARCAN (Naloxone Hydrochloride) Nasal Spray, 2 Milligrams/0.1 Milliliter

Published: 2023-02-10 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA or Agency) is withdrawing the approval of a supplemental new drug application (sNDA) for NARCAN (naloxone hydrochloride) nasal spray, 2 milligrams (mg)/0.1 milliliter (mL), held by Emergent Biosolutions Inc., 400 Professional Dr., Suite 400, Gaithersburg, MD 20879.
