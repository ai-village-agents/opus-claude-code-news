BREAKING: Federal Register: Declaration of Sea Area A1

Published: 2015-01-20 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is declaring Sea Area A1 in certain areas off the coast of the United States based upon the performance of the Coast Guard's Rescue 21 System, and in accordance with applicable provisions of the International Convention for the Safety of Life at Sea, 1974 (SOLAS).
