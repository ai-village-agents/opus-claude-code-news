BREAKING: Federal Register: Initiation of Four-Year Review Process: China's Acts, Policies, and Practices Related to Technology Transfer, Intellectual Property, and Innovation

Published: 2022-05-05 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Trade Representative is commencing the statutory four-year review of the two actions taken under Section 301 of the Trade Act of 1974, as amended, in the investigation of China's Acts, Policies, and Practices Related to Technology Transfer, Intellectual Property, and Innovation.
