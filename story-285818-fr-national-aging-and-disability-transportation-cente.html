BREAKING: Federal Register: National Aging and Disability Transportation Center (NADTC) Under FTA's Technical Assistance Program

Published: 2015-02-06 | Source: Federal Register

The Federal Transit Administration (FTA) is soliciting proposals under the Moving Ahead for Progress in the 21st Century Act's Section 5314 Technical Assistance and Standards Development Program from national non-profit organizations for a cooperative agreement to fund a National Aging and Disability Transportation Center (NADTC).
