BREAKING: Federal Register: Solicitation of Input From Stakeholders Regarding the Food Safety Outreach Program

Published: 2016-12-12 | Source: Federal Register

As part of the National Institute of Food and Agriculture's (NIFA) strategy to successfully expand the Food Safety Outreach Program, NIFA will host a virtual listening session. The focus of the listening session is to gather stakeholder input to develop the priorities for the Request for Applications (RFA) in Fiscal Year (FY) 2018.
