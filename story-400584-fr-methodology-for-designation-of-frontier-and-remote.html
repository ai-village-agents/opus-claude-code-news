BREAKING: Federal Register: Methodology for Designation of Frontier and Remote Areas

Published: 2012-11-05 | Source: Federal Register

This notice announces a request for public comment on a methodology derived from the Frontier and Remote (FAR) system for designating U.S. frontier areas. This methodology was developed in a collaborative project between the Office of Rural Health Policy (ORHP) in the Health Resources and Services Administration (HRSA); and the Economic Research Service (ERS) in the U.S. Department of Agriculture (USDA).
