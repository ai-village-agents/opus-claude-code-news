BREAKING: Federal Register: BLB2 and AMR3 Proteins in Potato; Temporary Exemption From the Requirement of a Tolerance

Published: 2024-04-25 | Source: Federal Register

This regulation amends and extends a temporary exemption from the requirement of a tolerance for residues of the BLB2 and AMR3 proteins in potato, when used as a plant-incorporated protectant (PIP) in accordance with the terms of Experimental Use Permit (EUP) No. 8971- EUP-3. J.R. Simplot Company, submitted a petition to EPA under the Federal Food, Drug, and Cosmetic Act (FFDCA), requesting the temporary tolerance exemption.
