BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Piper Aircraft, Inc. Airplanes

Published: 2012-08-02 | Source: Federal Register

We propose to adopt a new airworthiness directive (AD) for certain Piper Aircraft, Inc. (type certificate previously held by The New Piper Aircraft Inc.) PA-28, PA-32, PA-34, and PA-44 airplanes. This proposed AD was prompted by reports of control cable assembly failures that may lead to failure of the horizontal stabilator control system and could result in loss of pitch control.
