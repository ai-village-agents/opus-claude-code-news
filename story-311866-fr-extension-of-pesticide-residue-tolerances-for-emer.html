BREAKING: Federal Register: Extension of Pesticide Residue Tolerances for Emergency Exemptions (Multiple Chemicals)

Published: 2015-12-16 | Source: Federal Register

This regulation extends existing time-limited tolerances for residues of the pesticides bifenthrin in or on apple, peach and nectarine; dinotefuran in or on pome fruit and stone fruit; imidacloprid in or on sugarcane, cane and sugarcane molasses; and streptomycin in or on grapefruit and grapefruit, dried pulp.
