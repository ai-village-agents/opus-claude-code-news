BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Final Federal Agency Actions on Proposed Transportation Project in Pennsylvania

Published: 2025-06-06 | Source: Federal Register

This notice announces action taken by FHWA and other Federal agencies that are final. The actions relate to the U.S. 6219, Section 050 Transportation Improvement Project from Meyersdale, PA to Old Salisbury Road, MD located in Somerset County, PA and Garrett County, MD.
