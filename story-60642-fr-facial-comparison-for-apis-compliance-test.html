BREAKING: Federal Register: Facial Comparison for APIS Compliance Test

Published: 2023-02-16 | Source: Federal Register

This document announces that U.S. Customs and Border Protection (CBP) plans to conduct a voluntary test in which participating commercial airlines and vessels use CBP's Traveler Verification Service (TVS) facial comparison service to comply with certain regulatory requirements regarding the Advance Passenger Information System (APIS).
