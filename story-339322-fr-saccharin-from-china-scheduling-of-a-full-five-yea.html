BREAKING: Federal Register: Saccharin From China; Scheduling of a Full Five-Year Review

Published: 2014-11-10 | Source: Federal Register

The Commission hereby gives notice of the scheduling of a full review pursuant to section 751(c)(5) of the Tariff Act of 1930 (19 U.S.C. 1675(c)(5)) (the Act) to determine whether revocation of the antidumping duty order on saccharin from China would be likely to lead to continuation or recurrence of material injury within a reasonably foreseeable time.
