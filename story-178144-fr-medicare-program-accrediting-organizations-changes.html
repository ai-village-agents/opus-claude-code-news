BREAKING: Federal Register: Medicare Program; Accrediting Organizations-Changes to Change of Ownership

Published: 2019-05-02 | Source: Federal Register

This proposed rule would add requirements and a specified process to address changes of ownership as they relate to the sale, transfer, and/or purchase of assets of Accrediting Organizations (AOs) with the Centers for Medicare & Medicaid Services (CMS)-approved accreditation programs.
