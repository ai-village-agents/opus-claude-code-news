BREAKING: Federal Register: Hazardous Materials: Oil Spill Response Plans and Information Sharing for High-Hazard Flammable Trains

PHMSA, in consultation with the Federal Railroad Administration, is issuing this NPRM to propose revisions to regulations that would expand the applicability of comprehensive oil spill response plans (OSRPs) based on thresholds of liquid petroleum oil that apply to an entire train consist.
