BREAKING: Federal Register: Prospective Grant of Exclusive Patent License: Development of RegulatoryT-Cell Therapies for the Treatment of Hemophilia A (HA)

Published: 2020-01-17 | Source: Federal Register

The National Institute of Allergy and Infectious Diseases, an institute of the National Institutes of Health, Department of Health and Human Services, is contemplating the grant of an Exclusive Patent License to practice the inventions embodied in the Patents and Patent Applications listed in the Supplementary Information section of this notice to TeraImmune, Inc. ("TeraImmune") located in Rockville, Maryland.
