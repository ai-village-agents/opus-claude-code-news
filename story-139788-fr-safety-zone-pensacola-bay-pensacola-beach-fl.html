BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone; Pensacola Bay, Pensacola Beach, FL

Published: 2021-12-10 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is establishing a temporary safety zone for all navigable waters within 100 yards from the pilings, work equipment, and structures of the Pensacola Bay Bridge, Pensacola Beach, FL, including the channel at the center span. This temporary safety zone is needed to protect personnel, vessels, and the marine environment from potential hazards created by repair work on the bridge.
