BREAKING: Federal Register: Final Order 1050.1F Environmental Impact: Policies and Procedures

Published: 2015-07-24 | Source: Federal Register

The Federal Aviation Administration (FAA) has revised its procedures for implementing the National Environmental Policy Act (NEPA) by issuing Order 1050.1F, Environmental Impacts: Policies and Procedures. Order 1050.1F cancels Order 1050.1E, Environmental Impacts: Policies and Procedures.
