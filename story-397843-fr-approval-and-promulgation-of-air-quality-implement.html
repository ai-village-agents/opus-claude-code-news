BREAKING: Federal Register: Approval and Promulgation of Air Quality Implementation Plans; Ohio; PBR and PTIO

Published: 2012-10-01 | Source: Federal Register

EPA is proposing to approve six Permit-by-Rule provisions, a Permit to Install and Operate program, two permanent exemptions from the Permit to Install requirement and a general permit program as additions to Ohio's State Implementation Plan under the Clean Air Act. The Ohio Environmental Protection Agency has requested these rule revisions to make its air pollution permit program more efficient.
