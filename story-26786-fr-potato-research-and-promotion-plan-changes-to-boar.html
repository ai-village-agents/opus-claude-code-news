BREAKING: Federal Register: Potato Research and Promotion Plan; Changes to Board Membership and Administrative Committee

Published: 2024-01-08 | Source: Federal Register

This rule implements recommendations from the National Potato Promotion Board (Board) to change its membership and organization by revising the formula used to determine the number of producer and importer seats on the Board, reducing the maximum number of importer seats on the Board, and indefinitely suspending the Administrative Committee.
