BREAKING: Federal Register: Public Affairs Liaison With Industry

Published: 2016-08-15 | Source: Federal Register

This final rule removes regulations concerning Public Affairs liaison with industry. These Code of Federal Regulations (CFR) provisions are outdated and no longer accurate or applicable as written. The guidance, as revised, sets forth internal standards for how DoD employees should reach out and engage with industry.
