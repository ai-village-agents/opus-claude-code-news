BREAKING: Federal Register: 510(k) Third Party Review Program and Third Party Emergency Use Authorization (EUA) Review; Draft Guidance for Industry, Food and Drug Administration Staff, and Third Party Review Organizations; Availability

Published: 2023-12-21 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA or Agency) is announcing the availability of the draft guidance entitled "510(k) Third Party Review Program and Third Party Emergency Use Authorization (EUA) Review." This draft guidance provides FDA's current thinking regarding the 510(k) Third Party (3P510k) Review Program and review of Emergency Use Authorizations (EUA) requests by a third party review organizations (3PEUA review).
