BREAKING: Federal Register: Regulatory Implementation of the Centers of Excellence and Expertise

Published: 2023-10-05 | Source: Federal Register

This document adopts as final, without change, interim amendments made to the U.S. Customs and Border Protection (CBP) regulations by CBP Decision 16-26, as modified by a subsequent technical correction, CBP Decision 19-11. The interim amendments established the Centers of Excellence and Expertise (Centers) as a permanent organizational component of the agency.
