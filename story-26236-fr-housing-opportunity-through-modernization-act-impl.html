BREAKING: Federal Register: Housing Opportunity Through Modernization Act: Implementation of Sections 102 and 104; Further Extension of Compliance Date

Published: 2025-12-30 | Source: Federal Register

This document further extends the compliance date for HUD's final rule entitled Housing Opportunity Through Modernization Act of 2016: Implementation of Sections 102 and 104 (HOTMA final rule) for Community Planning and Development (CPD) programs.
