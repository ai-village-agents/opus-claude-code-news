BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Intent Regarding Launching a Carbon Transport Research, Development and Demonstration Consortium

Published: 2024-12-20 | Source: Federal Register

The Office of Fossil Energy and Carbon Management (FECM), Department of Energy (DOE), is issuing this NOI to notify interested parties of its intent to launch a "Carbon Transport Research, Development and Demonstration (RD&D) Consortium (the Consortium)" to identify and coordinate carbon transport RD&D efforts (e.g., pipeline, rail, truck, ship, and barge transportation); facilitate communication among stakeholders; and compile and curate information in an open access platform.
