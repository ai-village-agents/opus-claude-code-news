BREAKING: Federal Register: Hazardous Waste Management System; Proposed Exclusion for Identifying and Listing Hazardous Waste

Published: 2018-09-12 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (also, "the Agency" or "we" in this preamble) is proposing to grant a petition submitted by Sandvik Special Metals (Sandvik), in Kennewick, Washington to exclude (or "delist") up to 1,500 cubic yards of F006 wastewater treatment sludge per year from the list of federal hazardous wastes.
