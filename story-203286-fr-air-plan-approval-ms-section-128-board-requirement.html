BREAKING: Federal Register: Air Plan Approval; MS; Section 128 Board Requirements for Infrastructure SIPs

Published: 2018-03-30 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is proposing to approve the draft State Implementation Plan (SIP) submissions, submitted by the State of Mississippi, through the Mississippi Department of Environmental Quality (MDEQ) for parallel processing, on June 23, 2017, and February 2, 2018. Together these draft submittals address specific Clean Air Act (CAA or Act) requirements applicable to Mississippi state boards or bodies that approve CAA permits and enforcement orders.
