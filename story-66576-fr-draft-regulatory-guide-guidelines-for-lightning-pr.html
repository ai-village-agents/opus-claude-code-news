BREAKING: Federal Register: Draft Regulatory Guide: Guidelines for Lightning Protection for Production and Utilization Facilities

Published: 2023-05-03 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Nuclear Regulatory Commission (NRC) is issuing for public comment a draft regulatory guide (DG), DG-1409, "Guidelines for Lightning Protection for Production and Utilization Facilities." This DG is the proposed Revision 1 of Regulatory Guide (RG) 1.204, "Guidelines for Lightning Protection of Nuclear Power Plants."
