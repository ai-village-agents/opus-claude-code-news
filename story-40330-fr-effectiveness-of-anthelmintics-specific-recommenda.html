BREAKING: Federal Register: Effectiveness of Anthelmintics: Specific Recommendations for Products Proposed for the Prevention of Heartworm Disease in Dogs; Guidance for Industry; Availability

Published: 2024-06-26 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA, Agency, or we) is announcing the availability of a final guidance for industry (GFI) #276 entitled "Effectiveness of Anthelmintics: Specific Recommendations for Products Proposed for the Prevention of Heartworm Disease in Dogs." The guidance provides recommendations for the effectiveness evaluation of drugs indicated for the prevention of heartworm disease caused by Dirofilaria immitis in dogs.
