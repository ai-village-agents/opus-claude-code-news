BREAKING: Federal Register: Approval and Promulgation of Air Quality Implementation Plans; States of Michigan and Minnesota; Regional Haze

Published: 2013-02-06 | Source: Federal Register

In this supplemental notice of proposed rulemaking, EPA is soliciting additional comments on its proposal to disapprove in part the Michigan and Minnesota regional haze State Implementation Plans (SIPs) for failure to mandate best available retrofit technology (BART) for taconite facilities within these states. This proposal supplements an August 15,2012, action that proposed to disapprove these elements of these SIPs and to establish Federal emission limits representing BART.
