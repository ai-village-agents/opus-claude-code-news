BREAKING: Federal Register: Hazardous Materials: Transportation of Lithium Batteries

Published: 2014-08-06 | Source: Federal Register

PHMSA, in consultation with the Federal Aviation Administration (FAA), is modifying the requirements governing the transportation of lithium cells and batteries.
