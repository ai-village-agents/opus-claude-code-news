BREAKING: Federal Register: Removal of the Date Restriction for Flight Training in Experimental Light Sport Aircraft

Published: 2018-10-24 | Source: Federal Register

The Federal Aviation Administration is proposing to revise its rules concerning the operation of experimental light sport aircraft. The current regulations prohibited the use of these aircraft for flight training for compensation or hire after January 31, 2010.
