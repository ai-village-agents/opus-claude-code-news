BREAKING: Federal Register: Appointments to a Recreational Fisheries Working Group by the Marine Fisheries Advisory Committee

Published: 2012-12-10 | Source: Federal Register

Nominations are being solicited for appointment to a Recreational Fisheries Working Group of the Marine Fisheries Advisory Committee (MAFAC). The members will be appointed by MAFAC in consultation with NOAA and will serve for an initial term of up to two years, with the option to apply to be re-appointed.
