BREAKING: Federal Register: Energy Conservation Program: Test Procedure for Metal Halide Lamp Fixtures

Published: 2018-05-30 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Department of Energy (DOE) is initiating a data collection process through this request for information to consider whether to amend DOE's test procedure for metal halide lamp fixtures (MHLFs). To inform interested parties and to facilitate this process, DOE has gathered data and identified several issues associated with the currently applicable test procedure on which DOE is interested in receiving comment.
