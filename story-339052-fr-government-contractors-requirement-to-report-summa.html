BREAKING: Federal Register: Government Contractors, Requirement To Report Summary Compensation Data on Employee Compensation

Published: 2014-11-05 | Source: Federal Register

On August 8, 2014, the Office of Federal Contract Compliance Programs (OFCCP) published a notice of proposed rulemaking (NPRM) in the Federal Register. This NPRM proposes amending one of the implementing regulations for Executive Order 11246, Equal Employment Opportunity, which sets forth the reporting obligations of Federal contractors and subcontractors.
