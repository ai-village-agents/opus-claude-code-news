BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone: Akadama Fireworks Display, Richmond Inner Harbor, Richmond, CA

Published: 2014-04-09 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is establishing a temporary safety zone in the navigable waters of Richmond Inner Harbor near Richmond, CA in support of the Akadama Fireworks Display on April 19, 2014. This safety zone is established to ensure the safety of participants and spectators from the dangers associated with the pyrotechnics.
