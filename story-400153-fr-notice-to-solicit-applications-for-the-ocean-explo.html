BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice To Solicit Applications for the Ocean Exploration Advisory Board

Published: 2012-10-29 | Source: Federal Register

The Office of Oceanic and Atmospheric Research publishes this notice to solicit applications and nominations of persons with appropriate education, interest, and/or experience to become a member of the Ocean Exploration Advisory Board.
