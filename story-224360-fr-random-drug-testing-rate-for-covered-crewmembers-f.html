BREAKING: Federal Register: Random Drug Testing Rate for Covered Crewmembers for 2019

Published: 2018-12-28 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard has set the calendar year 2019 minimum random drug testing rate at 50 percent of covered crewmembers.
