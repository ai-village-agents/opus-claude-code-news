BREAKING: Federal Register: Procedural Regulations of the Copyright Royalty Board Regarding Electronic Filing System (eCRB)

Published: 2020-12-28 | Source: Federal Register

The Copyright Royalty Judges propose to amend regulations governing the electronic filing of documents through the Copyright Royalty Board's electronic filing system (eCRB) to permit attorney designees with approved eCRB user accounts to file on behalf of attorneys. The Judges solicit comments on the proposed amendments.
