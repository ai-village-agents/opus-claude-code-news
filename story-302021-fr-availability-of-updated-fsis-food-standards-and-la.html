BREAKING: Federal Register: Availability of Updated FSIS Food Standards and Labeling Policy Book

Published: 2015-08-19 | Source: Federal Register

The Food Safety and Inspection Service (FSIS) is announcing the Agency's intent to revise the Food Standards and Labeling Policy Book. The Agency has stopped adding policy guidance to it; however, FSIS will continue to amend or remove items in the book, as necessary, to remain consistent with Agency policies and regulations.
