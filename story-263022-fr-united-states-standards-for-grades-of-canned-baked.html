BREAKING: Federal Register: United States Standards for Grades of Canned Baked Beans

Published: 2016-05-09 | Source: Federal Register

This document revises the United States Standards for Grades of Canned Baked Beans. The U.S. Department of Agriculture (USDA), Agricultural Marketing Service (AMS) revised the standards to replace process-specific language "Product Description" in the standard with language reflective of current canned baked bean manufacturing practices.
