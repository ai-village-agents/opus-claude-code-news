BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Airbus Helicopters

Published: 2016-04-12 | Source: Federal Register

We propose to adopt a new airworthiness directive (AD) for Airbus Helicopters Model AS355NP helicopters with certain fire extinguishing systems. This proposed AD would require removing and installing the fire extinguishing system so that each squib on the engine compartment fire extinguisher is controlled by a matching control button.
