BREAKING: Federal Register: Announcing DRAFT Revisions to Federal Information Processing Standard (FIPS) 186-3, Digital Signature Standard (DSS), and Request for Comments

Published: 2012-04-10 | Source: Federal Register

The National Institute of Standards and Technology (NIST) requests comments on revisions to Federal Information Processing Standard (FIPS) 186-3, Digital Signature Standard, which was approved in January 2009. The proposed revisions are available at http:// csrc.nist.gov/publications/PubsDrafts.html.
