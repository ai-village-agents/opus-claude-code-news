BREAKING: Federal Register: Energy Conservation Program: Energy Conservation Standards for Electric Motors

Published: 2020-05-21 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Department of Energy ("DOE") is initiating an effort to determine whether to amend the current energy conservation standards for electric motors. DOE must review these standards at least once every six years and either propose new standards for electric motors or a notice of determination that the existing standards do not need amending.
