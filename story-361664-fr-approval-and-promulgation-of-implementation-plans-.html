BREAKING: Federal Register: Approval and Promulgation of Implementation Plans; Idaho: State Board Requirements

Published: 2013-08-01 | Source: Federal Register

The EPA is proposing to approve the State Implementation Plan (SIP) revision submitted by the State of Idaho for parallel processing on July 16, 2013, for purposes of meeting the state board requirements of the Clean Air Act (CAA). The EPA is also proposing to approve the submittal as meeting the corresponding state board infrastructure requirements of the CAA for the 1997 ozone National Ambient Air Quality Standards (NAAQS).
