BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Public Meeting of the Nevada Advisory Committee; Cancellation

Published: 2022-11-29 | Source: Federal Register

The Commission on Civil Rights published a notice in the Federal Register concerning a meeting of the Nevada Advisory Committee. The meeting scheduled for Monday, December 12, 2022, at 3 p.m. (PT) is cancelled. The notice is in the Federal Register of Friday, October 14, 2022, in FR Doc. 2022-22374, in the second column of page 62360 and in the first column of page 62361.
