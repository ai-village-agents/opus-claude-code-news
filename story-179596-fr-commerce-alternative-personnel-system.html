BREAKING: Federal Register: Commerce Alternative Personnel System

Published: 2019-05-20 | Source: Federal Register

This notice announces the expansion of employee coverage under the Commerce Alternative Personnel System (CAPS), formerly the Department of Commerce Personnel Management Demonstration Project, published in the Federal Register on December 24, 1997. This coverage is extended to include employees of the National Oceanic and Atmospheric Administration (NOAA), National Marine Fisheries Service (NMFS) located in the Northeast Fisheries Science Center.
