BREAKING: Federal Register: Solicitation of Nominations for Membership on the National Vaccine Advisory Committee

Published: 2013-05-28 | Source: Federal Register

The National Vaccine Program Office (NVPO), a program office within the Office of the Assistant Secretary for Health, Department of Health and Human Services (HHS), is soliciting nominations of qualified candidates to be considered for appointment as members to the National Vaccine Advisory Committee (NVAC).
