BREAKING: Federal Register: Charging Standard Administrative Fees for Non-Program Information

Published: 2022-09-27 | Source: Federal Register

On August 22, 2012, we announced in the Federal Register a schedule of standard administrative fees we charge to the public. When authorized, we charge these fees to recover our full costs when we provide information and related services for non-program purposes. We are announcing an update to the previously published schedule of standard administrative fees.
