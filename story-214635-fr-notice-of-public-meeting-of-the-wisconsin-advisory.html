BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Public Meeting of the Wisconsin Advisory Committee for a Meeting To Discuss Civil Rights Concerns in the State

Published: 2018-08-23 | Source: Federal Register

Notice is hereby given, pursuant to the provisions of the rules and regulations of the U.S. Commission on Civil Rights (Commission) and the Federal Advisory Committee Act that the Wisconsin Advisory Committee (Committee) will hold a meeting on Friday, August 31, 2018, at 12:00 p.m. CST for the purpose of discussing civil rights concerns in the state.
