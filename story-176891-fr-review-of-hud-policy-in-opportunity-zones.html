BREAKING: Federal Register: Review of HUD Policy in Opportunity Zones

Published: 2019-04-17 | Source: Federal Register

Consistent with Executive Order 13853, "Establishing the White House Opportunity and Revitalization Council," this document informs the public that HUD intends to maximize the beneficial impact of investment in Opportunity Zones.
