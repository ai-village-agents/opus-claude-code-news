BREAKING: Federal Register: PCBs Bulk Product v. Remediation Waste

Published: 2012-02-29 | Source: Federal Register

EPA is seeking comment on a proposed reinterpretation of its position regarding the polychlorinated biphenyl (PCB) disposal regulations regarding PCB bulk product and PCB remediation waste. The proposed reinterpretation is in response to questions EPA received about the application of particular disposal and cleanup regulatory requirements regarding PCB- contaminated building materials designated for disposal.
