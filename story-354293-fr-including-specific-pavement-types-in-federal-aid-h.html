BREAKING: Federal Register: Including Specific Pavement Types in Federal-aid Highway Traffic Noise Analyses

Published: 2013-05-08 | Source: Federal Register

The FHWA requests input from stakeholders and interested parties on expanding the specific pavement types used in Federal-aid highway traffic noise analyses. Current highway traffic noise analyses rely on data from three pavement types: dense-graded asphaltic concrete (DGAC), open-graded asphaltic concrete (OGAC), and Portland cement concrete (PCC).
