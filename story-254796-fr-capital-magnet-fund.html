BREAKING: Federal Register: Capital Magnet Fund

Published: 2016-02-08 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of the Treasury is issuing an interim rule implementing the Capital Magnet Fund (CMF), administered by the Community Development Financial Institutions Fund (CDFI Fund). This interim rule incorporates updates to the definitions, requirements and parameters for CMF implementation and administration.
