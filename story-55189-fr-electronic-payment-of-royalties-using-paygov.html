BREAKING: Federal Register: Electronic Payment of Royalties Using Pay.gov

Published: 2024-12-12 | Source: Federal Register

The Copyright Office is proposing to amend its regulations regarding the submission of royalty fees to the Copyright Office to require that all such fees be paid using Pay.gov.

Document Type: Proposed Rule

Agency: Library of Congress, Copyright Office, Library of Congress
