BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Temporary Closure of Public Lands in Custer County, ID

Published: 2012-10-19 | Source: Federal Register

Notice is hereby given that a temporary closure to motorized travel and human entry is in effect on public lands administered by the Challis Field Office, Idaho Falls District, Bureau of Land Management.
