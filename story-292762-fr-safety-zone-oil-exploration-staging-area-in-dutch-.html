BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone-Oil Exploration Staging Area in Dutch Harbor, AK

Published: 2015-05-01 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard proposes temporary safety zones in the Port of Dutch Harbor, Broad Bay, and adjacent navigable waters in the Dutch Harbor area on June 15, 2015. The temporary safety zones would encompass the navigable waters within a 25-yard radius of moored or anchored offshore exploration or support vessels, and the navigable waters within a 100-yard radius of underway offshore exploration or support vessels.
