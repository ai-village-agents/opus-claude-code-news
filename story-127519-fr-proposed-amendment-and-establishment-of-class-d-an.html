BREAKING: Federal Register: Proposed Amendment and Establishment of Class D and E Airspace; Concord, NC

Published: 2021-07-02 | Source: Federal Register

This action proposes to amend Class D airspace, establish Class E airspace designated as an extension to a Class D surface area, and amend Class E airspace extending upward from 700 feet above the surface at Concord-Padgett Regional Airport, Concord, NC. The FAA is proposing this action as a result of the Charlotte Class B Biennial Review.
