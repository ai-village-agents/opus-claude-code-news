BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; International Aero Engines AG Engines

Published: 2025-11-18 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA proposes to adopt a new airworthiness directive (AD) for certain International Aero Engines AG (IAE AG) Model V2522-A5, V2524-A5, V2525-D5, V2527-A5, V2527E-A5, V2527M-A5, V2528-D5, V2530-A5, V2531-E5, and V2533-A5 engines. This proposed AD was prompted by a manufacturer investigation that revealed that certain 3rd stage HPC rotor blades were susceptible to shroud wear and blade failure.
