BREAKING: Federal Register: Use of Mobile Wireless Devices for Voice Calls on Aircraft

Published: 2016-12-14 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Transportation (DOT or the Department) is proposing to protect airline passengers from being unwillingly exposed to voice calls within the confines of an aircraft. Specifically, the Department proposes to require sellers of air transportation to provide adequate advance notice to passengers if the carrier operating the flight allows passengers to make voice calls using mobile wireless devices.
