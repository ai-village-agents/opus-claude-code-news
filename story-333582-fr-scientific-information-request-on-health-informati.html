BREAKING: Federal Register: Scientific Information Request on Health Information Exchange

Published: 2014-08-29 | Source: Federal Register

The Agency for Healthcare Research and Quality (AHRQ) is seeking scientific information submissions from the public. Scientific information is being solicited to inform our review of Health Information Exchange, which is currently being conducted by the Evidence-based Practice Centers for the AHRQ Effective Health Care Program.
