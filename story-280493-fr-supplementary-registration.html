BREAKING: Federal Register: Supplementary Registration

Published: 2016-12-01 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Copyright Office is proposing to amend the regulation governing supplementary registration to reflect certain technical upgrades that will soon be made to the electronic registration system. In most cases applicants will be required to submit an online application in order to correct or amplify the information set forth in a basic registration.
