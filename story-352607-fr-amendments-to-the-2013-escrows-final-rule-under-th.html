BREAKING: Federal Register: Amendments to the 2013 Escrows Final Rule Under the Truth in Lending Act (Regulation Z)

Published: 2013-04-18 | Source: Federal Register

This rule proposes clarifying and technical amendments to a final rule issued by the Bureau of Consumer Financial Protection (Bureau) on January 10, 2013, which, among other things, lengthens the time for which a mandatory escrow account established for a higher- priced mortgage loan (HPML) must be maintained.
