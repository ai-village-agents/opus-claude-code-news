BREAKING: Federal Register: Tolerance Crop Grouping Program V

Published: 2019-08-27 | Source: Federal Register

EPA is proposing revisions to its pesticide tolerance crop grouping regulations, which allow the establishment of tolerances for multiple related crops based on data from a representative set of crops. EPA is proposing to revise one commodity definition, add three new commodity definitions, and amend the current herbs and spices crop group currently provided in Crop Group 19.
