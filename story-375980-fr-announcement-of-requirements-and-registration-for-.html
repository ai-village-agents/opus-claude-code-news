BREAKING: Federal Register: Announcement of Requirements and Registration for “Discharge Follow-Up Appointment Challenge”

Published: 2012-01-27 | Source: Federal Register

The "Discharge Follow-Up Appointment Challenge" challenges software developers to create an easy-to-use web-based tool that will make post-discharge follow-up appointment scheduling a more effective and shared process for care providers, patients and caregivers. In addition, developers will need to articulate a plan for broader adoption at the community level.
