BREAKING: Federal Register: Revocation of Emergency Use of a Drug During the COVID-19 Pandemic; Availability

Published: 2023-06-07 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA) is announcing the revocation of the Emergency Use Authorization (EUA) issued to B. Braun Melsungen AG (B. Braun Melsungen) for Propofol-Lipuro 1% injectable emulsion (Propofol-Lipuro 1% emulsion). The EUA was issued on March 12, 2021. B. Braun Melsungen informed FDA that the inventory of the Propofol-Lipuro 1% emulsion within the United States has been depleted and that B.
