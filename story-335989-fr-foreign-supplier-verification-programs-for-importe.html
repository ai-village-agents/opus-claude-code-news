BREAKING: Federal Register: Foreign Supplier Verification Programs for Importers of Food for Humans and Animals

Published: 2014-09-29 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA) is revising certain provisions of the proposed rule, issued in July 2013, on foreign supplier verification programs (FSVPs) for importers of food for humans and animals. We are primarily revising the proposed requirements concerning compliance status review of food and foreign suppliers, hazard analysis, and supplier verification activities.
