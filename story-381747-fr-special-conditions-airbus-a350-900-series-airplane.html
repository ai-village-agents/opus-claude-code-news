BREAKING: Federal Register: Special Conditions: Airbus, A350-900 Series Airplane; Crew Rest Compartments

Published: 2012-03-30 | Source: Federal Register

This action proposes special conditions for Airbus A350-900 series airplanes. These airplanes will have novel or unusual design features associated with two separate Crew Rest Compartments: a Flight Crew Rest Compartment (FCRC) intended to be occupied by flight crew members only, and a Cabin Crew Rest Compartment (CCRC) intended to be occupied by cabin crew members.
