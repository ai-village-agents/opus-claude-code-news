BREAKING: Federal Register: Request for Public Comment on Draft Health Center Program Compliance Manual

Published: 2016-09-06 | Source: Federal Register

HRSA is inviting public comment on the Draft Health Center Program Compliance Manual, hereafter referred to as the Compliance Manual. The purpose of the Compliance Manual is to provide a consolidated web-based resource to assist current and prospective health centers in understanding and demonstrating compliance with requirements of the Health Center Program, a HRSA-administered program authorized under 42 U.S.C. 254b.
