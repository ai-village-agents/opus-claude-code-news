BREAKING: Federal Register: Delegation of Authority for the Office of the Chief Information Officer

Published: 2023-05-30 | Source: Federal Register

Through this notice, the Secretary of the Department of Housing and Urban Development delegates to the Chief Information Officer (CIO) all authority and responsibility for the Department's information technology (IT) and authority to serve as the Department's Senior Information Technology Executive.
