BREAKING: Federal Register: Establishment of Class E Airspace; Wiggins, MS

Published: 2022-07-18 | Source: Federal Register

This action corrects a final rule published in the Federal Register of May 23, 2022, that modifies Establishment of Class E airspace at Dean Griffin Memorial Airport, Wiggins, MS. The geographic coordinates of the airport are amended to be in concert with the FAA's aeronautical database.
