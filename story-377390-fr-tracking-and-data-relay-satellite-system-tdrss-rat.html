BREAKING: Federal Register: Tracking and Data Relay Satellite System (TDRSS) Rates for Non-U.S. Government Customers

Published: 2012-02-10 | Source: Federal Register

This direct final rule makes non-substantive changes to the policy governing the Tracking and Data Relay Satellite System (TDRSS) services provided to non-U.S. Government users and the reimbursement for rendering such services.
