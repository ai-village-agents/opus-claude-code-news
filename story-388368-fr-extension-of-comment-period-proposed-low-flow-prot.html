BREAKING: Federal Register: Extension of Comment Period-Proposed Low Flow Protection Policy

Published: 2012-06-13 | Source: Federal Register

At its regular meeting in Binghamton, New York on June 7, 2012, the Susquehanna River Basin Commission (SRBC) extended the comment deadline for its proposed Low Flow Protection Policy to July 16, 2012. The original comment deadline had been May 16, 2012. On March 15, 2012, SRBC's commissioners approved the release of the proposed Low Flow Protection Policy for public review and comment.
