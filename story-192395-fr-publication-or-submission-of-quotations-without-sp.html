BREAKING: Federal Register: Publication or Submission of Quotations Without Specified Information

Published: 2019-10-30 | Source: Federal Register

The Securities and Exchange Commission (the "SEC" or the "Commission") is proposing amendments to 17 CFR 240.15c2-11 (the "Rule") under the Securities Exchange Act of 1934 (the "Exchange Act"). The Rule governs the publication of quotations for securities in a quotation medium other than a national securities exchange, i.e., over-the-counter ("OTC") securities.
