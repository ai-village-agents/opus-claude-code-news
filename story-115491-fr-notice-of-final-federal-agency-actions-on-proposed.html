BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Final Federal Agency Actions on Proposed Highway in Utah

Published: 2021-01-27 | Source: Federal Register

The FHWA, on behalf of UDOT, is issuing this notice to announce actions taken by UDOT that are final Federal agency actions. The final agency actions relate to a proposed highway project that includes modifications to the existing interchange on Interstate 15 (I- 15) between approximate mileposts 337.5 and 339, at State Route 97 (5600 South); and roadway widening of 5600 South in Roy City, Weber County, Utah.
