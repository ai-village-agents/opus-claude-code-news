BREAKING: Federal Register: Student Pilot Application Requirements

Published: 2016-01-12 | Source: Federal Register

This action requires applicants to apply for a student pilot certificate through a Flight Standards District Office, designated pilot examiner, airman certification representative associated with a pilot school, or certified flight instructor. Aviation Medical Examiners will no longer issue a combination medical certificate and student pilot certificate.
