BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Award of a Single-Source Cooperative Agreement To Fund National Center for HIV/AIDS, Dermatology, and STDs

Published: 2023-03-15 | Source: Federal Register

The Centers for Disease Control and Prevention (CDC), in the Department of Health and Human Services (HHS), announces the award of approximately $1,500,000 for Year 1 funding to the National Center for HIV/AIDS, Dermatology, and STDS (NCHADS). This award will support implementing a comprehensive combination of HIV prevention, monitoring, diagnosis, and treatment systems; emergency preparedness and response; and reduce the infectious disease burden in Cambodia.
