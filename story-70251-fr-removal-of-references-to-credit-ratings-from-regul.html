BREAKING: Federal Register: Removal of References to Credit Ratings From Regulation M

Published: 2023-06-20 | Source: Federal Register

The Securities and Exchange Commission ("Commission") is adopting rule amendments to implement section 939A(b) of the Dodd-Frank Wall Street Reform and Consumer Protection Act of 2010 ("Dodd-Frank Act"), which requires, among other things, that the Commission remove from its regulations any references to credit ratings and substitute in their place alternative standards of creditworthiness.
