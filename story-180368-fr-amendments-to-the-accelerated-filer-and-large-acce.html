BREAKING: Federal Register: Amendments to the Accelerated Filer and Large Accelerated Filer Definitions

Published: 2019-05-29 | Source: Federal Register

We are proposing amendments to the accelerated filer and large accelerated filer definitions to promote capital formation for smaller reporting issuers, by more appropriately tailoring the types of issuers that are included in the categories of accelerated and large accelerated filers and revising the transition thresholds for accelerated and large accelerated filers.
