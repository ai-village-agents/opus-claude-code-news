BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of New Fee Sites; Federal Lands Recreation Enhancement Act

Published: 2018-09-14 | Source: Federal Register

The Coronado National Forest is proposing new recreation fees at 13 day use sites at $8 a day or $40 for an annual pass, four campgrounds at $15 a day, and six group camping sites and one group picnic site at $50 a day, plus $10 per vehicle per day. Fees are assessed based on the level of amenities and services provided, cost of operations and maintenance, and market assessment.
