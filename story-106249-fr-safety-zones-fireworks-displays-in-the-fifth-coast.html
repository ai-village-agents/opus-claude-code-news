BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zones; Fireworks Displays in the Fifth Coast Guard District

Published: 2022-09-28 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard will enforce the Delaware River, Philadelphia, PA; Safety Zone from 8:30 p.m. through 9:15 p.m. on September 30, 2022, to provide for the safety of life on navigable waterways during the Cooper Foundation Gala fireworks display. Our regulation for fireworks displays in the Fifth Coast Guard District identifies the regulated area for this event in Philadelphia, PA.
