BREAKING: Federal Register: Advisory Committee on the Medical Uses of Isotopes: Meeting Notice

Published: 2013-02-01 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Nuclear Regulatory Commission (NRC) will convene a teleconference meeting of the Advisory Committee on the Medical Uses of Isotopes (ACMUI) on March 5, 2013, to discuss the draft report of the ACMUI Rulemaking Subcommittee that was formed to provide comments to the NRC staff on the proposed changes to 10 CFR Part 35.
