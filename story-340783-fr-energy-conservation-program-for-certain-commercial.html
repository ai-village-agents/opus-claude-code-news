BREAKING: Federal Register: Energy Conservation Program for Certain Commercial and Industrial Equipment: Gas Compressors

Published: 2014-11-28 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Department of Energy (DOE) is considering establishing energy conservation standards for commercial and industrial compressors. To date, DOE has considered energy conservation standards only for compressors intended to compress air, rather than natural gas. As a result, DOE's current efforts have focused on air compressors.
