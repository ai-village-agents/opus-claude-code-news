BREAKING: Federal Register: Endangered and Threatened Wildlife and Plants; Revised Critical Habitat for the Northern Spotted Owl

Published: 2012-05-08 | Source: Federal Register

We, the U.S. Fish and Wildlife Service (Service), recently published a proposal to revise the designated critical habitat for the northern spotted owl (Strix occidentalis caurina), and announced the opening of a public comment period on the proposed revised rule through June 6, 2012. We now extend the public comment period to July 6, 2012.
