BREAKING: Federal Register: Washington Channel, Fort McNair, Washington, DC; Restricted Area

Published: 2020-12-29 | Source: Federal Register

On October 13, 2020, the U.S. Army Corps of Engineers (the Corps) published a proposed rule to establish a permanent restricted area in the Washington Channel adjacent to Fort McNair in Washington, DC. The comment period ended on November 12, 2020, and we received requests to extend the comment period. As it closed prior to the publication of this document, we are reopening the comment period.
