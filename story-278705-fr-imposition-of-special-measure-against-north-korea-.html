BREAKING: Federal Register: Imposition of Special Measure Against North Korea as a Jurisdiction of Primary Money Laundering Concern

Published: 2016-11-09 | Source: Federal Register

FinCEN is issuing this final rule to prohibit U.S. financial institutions from opening or maintaining a correspondent account for, or on behalf of, North Korean banking institutions.
