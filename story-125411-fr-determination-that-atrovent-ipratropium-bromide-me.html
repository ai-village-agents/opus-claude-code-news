BREAKING: Federal Register: Determination That ATROVENT (Ipratropium Bromide) Metered Spray, 0.021 Micrograms/Spray and 0.042 Micrograms/Spray, Were Not Withdrawn From Sale for Reasons of Safety or Effectiveness

Published: 2021-06-07 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA or Agency) has determined that ATROVENT (ipratropium bromide) metered spray, 0.021 micrograms (mcg)/spray and 0.042 mcg/spray, were not withdrawn from sale for reasons of safety or effectiveness.
