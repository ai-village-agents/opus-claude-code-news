BREAKING: Federal Register: Draft Guidance for Industry on Charging for Investigational Drugs Under an Investigational New Drug Application-Questions and Answers; Availability

Published: 2013-05-09 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA) is announcing the availability of a draft guidance for industry entitled "Charging for Investigational Drugs Under an IND--Qs & As." This guidance is intended to provide information for industry, researchers, and physicians on how FDA is implementing its regulation on charging for an investigational drug under an investigational new drug (IND) application.
