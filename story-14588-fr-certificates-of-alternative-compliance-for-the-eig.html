BREAKING: Federal Register: Certificates of Alternative Compliance for the Eighth Coast Guard District

Published: 2025-06-27 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard announces that the Eighth Coast Guard District's Prevention Division has issued certificates of alternative compliance from the International Regulations for Preventing Collisions at Sea, 1972 (72 COLREGS), to vessels of special construction or purpose that cannot fully comply with the light, shape, and sound signal provisions of 72 COLREGS without interfering with the vessel's design and construction.
