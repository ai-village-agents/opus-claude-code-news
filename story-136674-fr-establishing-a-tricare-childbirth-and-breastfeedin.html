BREAKING: Federal Register: Establishing a TRICARE Childbirth and Breastfeeding Support Demonstration

Published: 2021-10-29 | Source: Federal Register

The Assistant Secretary of Defense for Health Affairs issues this notice announcing the creation of a demonstration to cover the services of three new classes of extra-medical TRICARE-authorized providers: certified labor doulas (CLDs), certified lactation consultants, and certified lactation counselors.
