BREAKING: Federal Register: Freight Car Safety Standards Implementing the Infrastructure Investment and Jobs Act

Published: 2023-12-08 | Source: Federal Register

FRA is proposing to amend the Freight Car Safety Standards (FCSS) to implement section 22425 of the Infrastructure Investment and Jobs Act (Act). The Act places certain restrictions on newly built freight cars placed into service in the United States (U.S.) including limiting content that originates from a country of concern (COC) or is sourced from a state-owned enterprise (SOE) and prohibiting the use of sensitive technology that originates from a COC or SOE.
