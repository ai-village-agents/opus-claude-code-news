BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Bell Helicopter Textron Canada Limited Helicopters

Published: 2020-03-16 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA is adopting a new airworthiness directive (AD) for certain Bell Helicopter Textron Canada Limited (Bell Canada) Model 429 helicopters. This AD requires inspecting a certain part-numbered curvic coupling for proper engagement and depending on the inspection results, inspecting for play, inspecting the curvic coupling teeth, inspecting the flapping bearing teeth, replacing parts, performing a rigging check, and reporting information.
