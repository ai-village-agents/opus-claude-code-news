BREAKING: Federal Register: USPTO Officially Transitions to Issuing Electronic Patent Grants in 2023

Published: 2023-02-28 | Source: Federal Register

The United States Patent and Trademark Office (USPTO) is implementing electronic patent issuance to reduce pendency of patent applications, foster a green economy by reducing paper waste, and permit complete issued patents to be viewable and printable by both the applicants as well as the public immediately upon issuance in Patent Center, the USPTO's electronic patent application filing and management system.
