BREAKING: Federal Register: Medical Devices; Neurological Devices; Classification of the Brain Stimulation Programming Planning Software

Published: 2023-01-05 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA, Agency, or we) is classifying brain stimulation programming planning software into class II (special controls). The special controls that apply to the device type are identified in this order and will be part of the codified language for the brain stimulation programming planning software's classification.
