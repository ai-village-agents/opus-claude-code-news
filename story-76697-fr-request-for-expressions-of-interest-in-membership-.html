BREAKING: Federal Register: Request for Expressions of Interest in Membership on the Federal Insurance Office's Advisory Committee on Risk-Sharing Mechanisms

Published: 2023-09-12 | Source: Federal Register

The Federal Insurance Office (FIO) within the Department of the Treasury invites the public to submit expressions of interest in serving as members of the Advisory Committee on Risk-Sharing Mechanisms (ACRSM). Submissions must be received by FIO no later than October 15, 2023.
