BREAKING: Federal Register: Priority Zoonotic Animal Drug Designation and Review Process; Draft Guidance for Industry; Availability

Published: 2023-12-18 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA or Agency) is announcing the availability of a draft guidance for industry (GFI) #283 entitled "Priority Zoonotic Animal Drug Designation and Review Process." This draft guidance is intended to assist sponsors pursuing priority zoonotic animal drug (PZAD) designation for a new animal drug.
