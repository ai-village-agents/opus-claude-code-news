BREAKING: Federal Register: Recent Trends in U.S. Services Trade, 2016 Annual Report

Published: 2016-02-04 | Source: Federal Register

The Commission has prepared and published annual reports in this series under investigation No. 332-345, Recent Trends in U.S. Services Trade, since 1996. The 2016 report, which the Commission plans to publish in September 2016, will provide aggregate data on cross- border trade in services for the period ending in 2014, and transactions by affiliates based outside the country of their parent firm for the period ending in 2013.
