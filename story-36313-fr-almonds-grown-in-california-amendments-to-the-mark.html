BREAKING: Federal Register: Almonds Grown in California; Amendments to the Marketing Order

Published: 2024-05-07 | Source: Federal Register

This rulemaking amends Marketing Order No. 981, which regulates the handling of almonds grown in California. This final rule modifies the definition of almonds and shelled almonds, establishes a definition for "almond biomass," and changes dates when the Almond Board of California (Board) conducts elections to coincide with assessment collections and when the Board submits volume regulation recommendations to USDA.
