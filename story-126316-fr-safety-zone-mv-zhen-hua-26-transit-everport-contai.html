BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone; M/V ZHEN HUA 26 Transit; Everport Container Terminal, San Pedro, California

Published: 2021-06-17 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Coast Guard is establishing a temporary moving safety zone around the M/V ZHEN HUA 26 while it transits through the navigation channel during its transit to Everport Container Terminal, Berth 227, in San Pedro, California.
