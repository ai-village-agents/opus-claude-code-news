BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of GSA Live Webinar Regarding Data Center Sustainability

Published: 2022-03-07 | Source: Federal Register

GSA is hosting a Data Center Sustainability Summit to obtain information from federal and industry communities. The summit focuses on the energy, environment, and infrastructure benefits that result from IT modernization and a transition from government-owned data centers to commercially-owned facilities, cloud computing, or software- as-a-service that can help the Federal Government reach its sustainability goals.
