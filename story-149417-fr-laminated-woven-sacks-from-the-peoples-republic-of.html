BREAKING: Federal Register: Laminated Woven Sacks From the People's Republic of China: Preliminary Results of the Antidumping Duty Administrative Review; 2018-2019

Published: 2020-04-17 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Commerce (Commerce) preliminarily finds that 20 companies subject to the administrative review of the antidumping duty (AD) order on laminated woven sacks (LWS) from the People's Republic of China (China) are part of the China-wide entity because none filed a separate rate application (SRA) or separate rate certification (SRC). The period of review (POR) is August 1, 2018 through July 31, 2019. Interested parties are invited to comment on these preliminary results.
