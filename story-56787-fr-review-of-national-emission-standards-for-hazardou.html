BREAKING: Federal Register: Review of National Emission Standards for Hazardous Air Pollutants for Polyether Polyols Production Industry

Published: 2024-12-27 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is proposing amendments to the National Emission Standards for Hazardous Air Pollutants (NESHAP) under the Clean Air Act (CAA) that apply to the Polyether Polyols (PEPO) Production industry (referred to as the PEPO NESHAP in this document).
