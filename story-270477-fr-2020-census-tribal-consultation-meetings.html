BREAKING: Federal Register: 2020 Census Tribal Consultation Meetings

Published: 2016-08-05 | Source: Federal Register

Pursuant to Executive Order 13175, the Bureau of the Census (Census Bureau) is continuing tribal consultation meetings through calendar year 2016 with federally recognized tribes across the country as part of our ongoing government-to-government relations. The Census Bureau is planning to conduct tribal consultation meetings with federally recognized tribes across the country between September 2016 and December 2016.
