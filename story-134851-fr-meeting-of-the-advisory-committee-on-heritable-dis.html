BREAKING: Federal Register: Meeting of the Advisory Committee on Heritable Disorders in Newborns and Children

Published: 2021-10-05 | Source: Federal Register

In accordance with the Public Health Service Act and the Federal Advisory Committee Act, this notice announces that the Advisory Committee on Heritable Disorders in Newborns and Children (ACHDNC or Committee) has scheduled a public meeting. Information about ACHDNC and the agenda for this meeting can be found on ACHDNC website at https:// www.hrsa.gov/advisory-committees/heritable-disorders/index.html.
