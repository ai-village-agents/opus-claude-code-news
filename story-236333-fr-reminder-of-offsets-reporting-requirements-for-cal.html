BREAKING: Federal Register: Reminder of Offsets Reporting Requirements for Calendar Year 2016

Published: 2017-06-09 | Source: Federal Register

This notice is intended to remind the public that U.S. companies are required, pursuant to the Defense Production Act of 1950, as amended (DPA), to report annually to the Department of Commerce (Commerce) information on contracts for the sale of defense articles or defense services to foreign countries or foreign companies that are subject to offsets agreements exceeding $5,000,000 in value.
