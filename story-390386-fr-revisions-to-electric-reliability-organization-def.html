BREAKING: Federal Register: Revisions to Electric Reliability Organization Definition of Bulk Electric System and Rules of Procedure

Published: 2012-07-05 | Source: Federal Register

Under section 215 of the Federal Power Act, the Federal Energy Regulatory Commission (Commission) proposes to approve a modification to the currently-effective definition of "bulk electric system" developed by the North American Electric Reliability Corporation (NERC), the Commission-certified Electric Reliability Organization.
