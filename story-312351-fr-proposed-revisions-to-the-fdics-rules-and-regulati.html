BREAKING: Federal Register: Proposed Revisions to the FDIC's Rules and Regulations Requiring the Registration of Securities Transfer Agents

Published: 2015-12-22 | Source: Federal Register

The FDIC is proposing to amend its regulations requiring insured State nonmember banks, or subsidiaries of such banks, that act as transfer agents for qualifying securities under section 12 of the Securities Exchange Act of 1934 ('34 Act) to register with the FDIC.
