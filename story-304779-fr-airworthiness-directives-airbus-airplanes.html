BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Airbus Airplanes

Published: 2015-09-22 | Source: Federal Register

We are revising an earlier proposed airworthiness directive (AD) for all Airbus Model A318, A319, A320, and A321 series airplanes. The NPRM proposed to supersede Airworthiness Directives (AD) 2011-13-11 and AD 2013-16-09.
