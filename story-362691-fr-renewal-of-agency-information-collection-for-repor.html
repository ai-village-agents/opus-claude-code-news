BREAKING: Federal Register: Renewal of Agency Information Collection for Reporting Systems for Public Law 102-477 Demonstration Project

Published: 2013-08-13 | Source: Federal Register

In compliance with the Paperwork Reduction Act of 1995, the Assistant Secretary--Indian Affairs is seeking comments on the renewal of Office of Management and Budget (OMB) approval for the collection of information for the Reporting System for Public Law 102-477 Demonstration Project authorized by OMB Control Number 1076-0135. This information collection expires December 31, 2013.
