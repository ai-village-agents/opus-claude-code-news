BREAKING: Federal Register: Supervisory Review Committee; Procedures for Appealing Material Supervisory Determinations

Published: 2017-10-30 | Source: Federal Register

The NCUA Board (Board) is adopting regulatory procedures for appealing material supervisory determinations to the NCUA's Supervisory Review Committee (SRC). These procedures significantly expand the number of material supervisory determinations appealable to the SRC to include most agency decisions that could significantly affect capital, earnings, operating flexibility, or the nature or level of supervisory oversight of a federally insured credit union (FICU).
