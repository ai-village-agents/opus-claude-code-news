BREAKING: Federal Register: Hours of Service of Drivers: National Mobile Shower and Catering Association; Application for Exemption

Published: 2019-10-01 | Source: Federal Register

FMCSA announces its decision to grant the National Mobile Shower and Catering Association's (NMSCA) request for an exemption from various provisions of the Federal hours-of-service (HOS) rules for commercial motor vehicle (CMV) drivers.
