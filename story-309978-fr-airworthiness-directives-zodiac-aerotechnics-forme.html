BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Zodiac Aerotechnics (Formerly Intertechnique Aircraft Systems)

Published: 2015-11-20 | Source: Federal Register

We are adopting a new airworthiness directive (AD) for certain Zodiac Aerotechnics (formerly Intertechnique Aircraft Systems) flightcrew oxygen mask regulators as installed on, but not limited to, various transport and small airplanes. This AD was prompted by a report that improper maintenance on oxygen mask regulators was found. This AD requires the identification and replacement of all potentially affected units.
