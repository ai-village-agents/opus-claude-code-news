BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Proposed Supplementary Rules on Public Land in Water Canyon, Humboldt County, NV

Published: 2012-11-09 | Source: Federal Register

The Bureau of Land Management (BLM) is proposing supplementary rules relating to camping, the discharge of firearms, and the use of motor vehicles, to protect public safety and resources on public land within the Water Canyon Recreation Area.
