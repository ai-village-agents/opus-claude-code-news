BREAKING: Federal Register: Evaluation of American Samoa Coastal Management Program; Notice of Public Meeting; Request for Comments

Published: 2023-01-06 | Source: Federal Register

The National Oceanic and Atmospheric Administration (NOAA), Office for Coastal Management, will hold an in-person public meeting to solicit input on the performance evaluation of the American Samoa Coastal Management Program. NOAA also invites the public to submit written comments.
