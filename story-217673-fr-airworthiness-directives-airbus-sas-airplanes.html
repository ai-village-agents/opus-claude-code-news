BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Airbus SAS Airplanes

Published: 2018-10-01 | Source: Federal Register

We are adopting a new airworthiness directive (AD) for all Airbus SAS Model A330-200 Freighter, -200, and -300 series airplanes; and Airbus SAS Model A340-200, -300, -500, and -600 series airplanes. This AD was prompted by a report of deficient fatigue performance of high strength steel used in forgings.
