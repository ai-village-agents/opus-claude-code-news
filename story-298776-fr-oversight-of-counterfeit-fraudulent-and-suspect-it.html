BREAKING: Federal Register: Oversight of Counterfeit, Fraudulent, and Suspect Items in the Nuclear Industry

Published: 2015-07-13 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Nuclear Regulatory Commission (NRC) is issuing Regulatory Issue Summary (RIS) 2015-08, "Oversight of Counterfeit, Fraudulent, and Suspect Items in the Nuclear Industry." This RIS is intended to heighten awareness of existing NRC regulations and how they apply to the nuclear industry stakeholders' oversight of counterfeit, fraudulent, and suspect items (CFSI).
