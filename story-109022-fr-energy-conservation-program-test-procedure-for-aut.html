BREAKING: Federal Register: Energy Conservation Program: Test Procedure for Automatic Commercial Ice Makers

Published: 2022-11-01 | Source: Federal Register

In this final rule, the U.S. Department of Energy ("DOE") amends the test procedure for automatic commercial ice makers to update incorporated references to the latest version of the industry standards; establish a relative humidity test condition; provide additional detail regarding certain test conditions, settings, setup requirements, and calculations; include a voluntary measurement of potable water use; clarify certification and reporting requirements; and add enforcement provisions.
