BREAKING: Federal Register: Hydroelectric Power Development at Silver Jack Dam, Bostwick Park Project, Colorado

Published: 2018-12-18 | Source: Federal Register

Current Federal policy allows non-Federal development of electrical power resource potential on Federal water resource projects. The Bureau of Reclamation (Reclamation) will consider proposals for non-Federal development of hydroelectric power at Silver Jack Dam, a feature of the Bostwick Park Project, located in Colorado. Reclamation is considering such hydroelectric power development under a Lease of Power Privilege. No Federal funds will be available for such hydroelectric power development.
