BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice To Announce the National Eye Institute (NEI) Draft Strategic Plan; Request for Information

Published: 2021-05-17 | Source: Federal Register

This Request for Information (RFI) is intended to gather broad public input to assist the National Eye Institute (NEI), National Institutes of Health (NIH) in reviewing the draft strategic plan document accessible from the NEI Strategic Plan website. NEI invites input from vison researchers in academia and industry, health care professionals, patient advocates and advocacy organizations, scientific or professional organizations, federal agencies, and other interested members of the public.
