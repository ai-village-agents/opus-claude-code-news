BREAKING: Federal Register: Medical Devices; Gastroenterology-Urology Devices; Classification of the Fluid Jet System for Prostate Tissue Removal

Published: 2018-06-15 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA or we) is classifying the fluid jet system for prostate tissue removal into class II (special controls). The special controls that apply to the device type are identified in this order and will be part of the codified language for the fluid jet system for prostate tissue removal's classification.
