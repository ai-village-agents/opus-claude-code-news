BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Applications for Deregistration Under Section 8(f) of the Investment Company Act of 1940

Published: 2012-07-06 | Source: Federal Register

Applicant seeks an order declaring that it has ceased to be an investment company. The applicant has transferred its assets to Heitman REIT Fund, a series of FundVantage Trust, and, on June 4, 2012, made a final distribution to shareholders based on net asset value. Expenses of $104,000 incurred in connection with the reorganization were paid by Old Mutual Capital, applicant's investment adviser. Filing Date: The application was filed on June 5, 2012.
