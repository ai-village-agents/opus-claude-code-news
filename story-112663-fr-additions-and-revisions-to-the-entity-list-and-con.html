BREAKING: Federal Register: Additions and Revisions to the Entity List and Conforming Removal From the Unverified List

Published: 2022-12-19 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Commerce is amending the Export Administration Regulations (EAR) by adding thirty-six entities to the Entity List. These entities have been determined by the U.S. Government to be acting contrary to the national security or foreign policy interests of the United States and will be listed on the Entity List under the destinations of the People's Republic of China (China) and Japan.
