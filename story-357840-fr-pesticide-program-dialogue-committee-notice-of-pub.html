BREAKING: Federal Register: Pesticide Program Dialogue Committee; Notice of Public Meeting

Published: 2013-06-19 | Source: Federal Register

Pursuant to the Federal Advisory Committee Act, the Environmental Protection Agency's (EPA's) Office of Pesticide Programs (OPP) is giving notice that a public meeting of the Pesticide Program Dialogue Committee (PPDC) is being planned for July 10-11, 2013. A draft agenda is under development and will be posted by July 2, 2013.
