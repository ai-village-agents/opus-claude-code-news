BREAKING: Federal Register: Technical Advisory Committee; Request for Nominations

Published: 2017-12-06 | Source: Federal Register

The BLS is soliciting new members for the Technical Advisory Committee (TAC). Five current membership terms expire on April 19, 2018. The TAC provides advice to the Bureau of Labor Statistics on technical aspects of data collection and the formulation of economic measures and makes recommendations on areas of research.
