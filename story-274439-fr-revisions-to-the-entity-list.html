BREAKING: Federal Register: Revisions to the Entity List

Published: 2016-09-20 | Source: Federal Register

This final rule amends the Export Administration Regulations (EAR) by revising the license requirement to apply to all items subject to the EAR for twelve Chinese entities on the Entity List.
