BREAKING: Federal Register: Anti-Circumvention Inquiry of Antidumping Duty Order on Hydrofluorocarbon Blends From the People's Republic of China-HFC Components: Final Determination Not To Include Within the Scope of the Order

Published: 2020-08-19 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Commerce (Commerce) determines not to include hydrofluorocarbon (HFC) components R-32 (difluoromethane), R- 125 (pentafluoroethane), and R-143a (1,1,1,-trifluoroethane), imported into the United States from the People's Republic of China (China), within the scope of the antidumping duty (AD) order on HFC blends from the China.
