BREAKING: Federal Register: Air Plan Approval; District of Columbia; Update to Materials Incorporated by Reference

Published: 2017-03-21 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is updating the materials that are incorporated by reference (IBR) into the District of Columbia state implementation plan (SIP). The regulations affected by this update have been previously submitted by the District of Columbia Department of Energy and Environment (DoEE) and approved by EPA.
