BREAKING: Federal Register: Pension Benefit Guaranty Corporation Disaster Relief

Published: 2018-07-02 | Source: Federal Register

The Pension Benefit Guaranty Corporation (PBGC) is changing how it announces relief from filing deadlines and penalties when a disaster occurs. Under an Announcement made today, PBGC's disaster relief will be available at the same time the Internal Revenue Service issues disaster relief for taxpayers that includes filing extensions for the Form 5500 series.
