BREAKING: Federal Register: Additions to List of Section 241.4 Categorical Non-Waste Fuels

Published: 2014-04-14 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA or the Agency) is proposing amendments to the Non-Hazardous Secondary Materials (NHSM) regulation under the Resource Conservation and Recovery Act (RCRA). The NHSM rule generally established standards and procedures for identifying whether non-hazardous secondary materials are solid wastes when used as fuels or ingredients in combustion units.
