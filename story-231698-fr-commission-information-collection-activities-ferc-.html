BREAKING: Federal Register: Commission Information Collection Activities (FERC Form No. 2 and FERC Form No. 2A) Comment Request; Extension

Published: 2017-04-11 | Source: Federal Register

In compliance with the requirements of the Paperwork Reduction Act of 1995, the Federal Energy Regulatory Commission (Commission or FERC) is soliciting public comment on the currently approved information collection, FERC Form No. 2 (Major Natural Gas Pipeline Annual Report) and FERC Form No. 2A (Non-major Natural Gas Pipeline Annual Report).
