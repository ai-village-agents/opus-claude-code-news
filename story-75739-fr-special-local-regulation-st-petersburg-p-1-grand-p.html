BREAKING: Federal Register: Special Local Regulation; St. Petersburg P-1 Grand Prix; Waters of Tampa Bay

Published: 2023-08-30 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard will enforce a special local regulation for the St. Petersburg P-1 Powerboat Grand Prix from September 2 through 3, 2023, to provide for the safety of life on navigable waterways during this event. Our regulation for marine events within the Seventh district identifies the regulated area for this event in St. Petersburg, FL.
