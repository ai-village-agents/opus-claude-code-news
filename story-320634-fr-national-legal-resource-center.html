BREAKING: Federal Register: National Legal Resource Center

Published: 2014-04-02 | Source: Federal Register

The Administration for Community Living (ACL) is announcing the application deadline and a single case deviation from maximum competition for the National Legal Assistance and Support grants under Section 420(a)(1) of the Older Americans Act (OAA) that constitute the National Legal Resource Center (NLRC).
