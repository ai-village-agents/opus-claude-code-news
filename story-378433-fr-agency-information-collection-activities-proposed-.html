BREAKING: Federal Register: Agency Information Collection Activities; Proposed Collection; Comment Request; General Licensing Provisions; Section 351(k) Biosimilar Applications; Correction

Published: 2012-02-23 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA) is correcting a notice that appeared in the Federal Register of February 15, 2012 (77 FR 8880). The document announced an opportunity for public comment on the proposed collection of certain information by the Agency. The document was published with an incorrect docket number. This document corrects that error.
