BREAKING: Federal Register: Certain Cellular Communications Infrastructure Systems, Components Thereof, and Products Containing Same; Commission Determination Not To Review an Initial Determination Terminating the Investigation in Its Entirety Based on Settlement; Termination of Investigation

Published: 2021-08-13 | Source: Federal Register

Notice is hereby given that the U.S. International Trade Commission ("Commission") has determined not to review an initial determination ("ID") (Order No. 6) of the presiding administrative law judge ("ALJ"), terminating the investigation in its entirety based on settlement. This investigation is terminated.
