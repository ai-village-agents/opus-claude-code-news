BREAKING: Federal Register: Review of the Commission's Rules Governing 896-901/935-940 MHz Band

Published: 2020-07-16 | Source: Federal Register

In this document, the Commission adopts rules for broadband license operations in the 897.5-900.5/936.5-939.5 MHz segment of the 900 MHz band (896-901/935-940 MHz). The new rules are necessary because many 900 MHz licensees, including utilities and other industrial users, will require additional coverage and capacity to keep pace with the expanding need for enhanced connectivity.
