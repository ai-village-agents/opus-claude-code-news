BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Extension of Public Comment Period for the Preliminary Designation of Certain Stormwater Discharges in the State of New Mexico Under the National Pollutant Discharge Elimination System of the Clean Water Act

Published: 2015-05-06 | Source: Federal Register

The public comment period for the Environmental Protection Agency Region 6 (EPA Region 6) Preliminary Designation of certain stormwater discharges in Los Alamos County, New Mexico has been extended until June 15, 2015.
