BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone; San Pedro Bay, Los Angeles, CA

Published: 2024-11-20 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is establishing a temporary moving safety zone around the M/V ZHEN HUA 26 while it transits through San Pedro Bay to and from APM Terminal Berth LA 401. This safety zone is necessary to protect personnel, vessels, and the marine environment from potential hazards associated with oversized cargo transfer operations of three ship-to-shore gantry cranes, which will extend more than 200 feet out from the transiting vessels.
