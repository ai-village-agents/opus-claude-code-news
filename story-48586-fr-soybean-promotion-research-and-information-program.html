BREAKING: Federal Register: Soybean Promotion, Research, and Information Program: Results of Soybean Request for Referendum

Published: 2024-10-01 | Source: Federal Register

The results of the Agricultural Marketing Service's (AMS) Request for Referendum indicate that too few soybean producers wanted a referendum on the Soybean Promotion and Research Order (Order) for one to be conducted. The Request for Referendum was conducted from May 6, 2024, through May 31, 2024, at the U.S. Department of Agriculture's (USDA) Farm Service Agency County offices.
