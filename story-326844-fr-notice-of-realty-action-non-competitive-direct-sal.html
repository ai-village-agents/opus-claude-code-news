BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Realty Action: Non-Competitive (Direct) Sale and Release of Reversionary Interest, Clark County, NV (N-92814)

Published: 2014-06-12 | Source: Federal Register

The Bureau of Land Management (BLM), Las Vegas Field Office, proposes to sell the Federal reversionary interest in 2.5 acres of land in Las Vegas, Nevada. The land was conveyed out of Federal ownership in 1996 subject to the Federal reversionary interest that is now proposed for sale under the authority of Section 202 and Section 203 of the Federal Land Policy and Management Act of October 21, 1976 (FLPMA), as amended.
