BREAKING: Federal Register: Consideration of Current Operating Issues and Licensing Actions in License Renewal

Published: 2023-09-29 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Nuclear Regulatory Commission (NRC) is issuing Revision 1 to Regulatory Issue Summary (RIS) 2014-06, "Consideration of Current Operating Issues and Licensing Actions in License Renewal." Revision 1 to RIS 2014-06 provides information to the addressees on how the NRC considers current operating issues in the license renewal review under NRC regulations.
