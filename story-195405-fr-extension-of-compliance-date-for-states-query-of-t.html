BREAKING: Federal Register: Extension of Compliance Date for States' Query of the Drug and Alcohol Clearinghouse

Published: 2019-12-13 | Source: Federal Register

FMCSA extends the compliance date for the requirement established by the December 5, 2016, Commercial Driver's License Drug and Alcohol Clearinghouse (Clearinghouse) final rule that States request information from the Clearinghouse ("query") about individuals before completing certain commercial driver's license (CDL) transactions for those drivers.
