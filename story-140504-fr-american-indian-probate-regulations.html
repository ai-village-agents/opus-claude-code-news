BREAKING: Federal Register: American Indian Probate Regulations

Published: 2021-12-20 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of the Interior (Department) is finalizing updates to its regulations governing probate of property that the United States holds in trust or restricted status for American Indians, in an effort to continually improve the services the Department provides to individual Indians and Tribes.
