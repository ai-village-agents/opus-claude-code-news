BREAKING: Federal Register: Tests Determined To Be Suitable for Use in the National Reporting System for Adult Education; Correction

Published: 2023-08-24 | Source: Federal Register

On July 13, 2023, the Department of Education (Department) published a notice announcing tests, test forms, and delivery formats that the Secretary determined to be suitable for use in the National Reporting System for Adult Education (NRS). This notice corrects the name of one test. All other information in the notice remains the same.
