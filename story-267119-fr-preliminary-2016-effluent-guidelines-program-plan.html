BREAKING: Federal Register: Preliminary 2016 Effluent Guidelines Program Plan

Published: 2016-06-27 | Source: Federal Register

This notice announces the availability of the Environmental Protection Agency's (EPA) Preliminary 2016 Effluent Guidelines Program Plan (Preliminary 2016 Plan) and solicits public comment. Section 304(m) of the Clean Water Act (CWA) requires EPA to biennially publish a plan for new and revised effluent limitations guidelines, after public review and comment.
