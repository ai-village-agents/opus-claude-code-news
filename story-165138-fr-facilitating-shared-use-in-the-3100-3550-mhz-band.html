BREAKING: Federal Register: Facilitating Shared Use in the 3100-3550 MHz Band

Published: 2020-11-03 | Source: Federal Register

The Federal Communications Commission is correcting a final that appeared in the Federal Register on October 9, 2020. In the document, the Commission adopts changes to its rules to prepare the 3.45-3.55 GHz band for commercial wireless services. It removes the secondary, non-federal allocations in the 3.3-3.55 GHz band for radiolocation services and the amateur radio service.
