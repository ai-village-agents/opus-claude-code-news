BREAKING: Federal Register: Direct Final Rulemaking Procedures

Published: 2015-06-25 | Source: Federal Register

NHTSA is establishing direct final rulemaking (DFR) procedures for use in adopting amendments to its regulations on which the agency expects it would receive no adverse public comment were it to publish them as proposals in the Federal Register. This limitation means that NHTSA will not use direct final rule procedures for amendments involving complex or controversial issues.
