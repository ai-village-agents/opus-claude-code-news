BREAKING: Federal Register: Urban Agriculture and Innovative Production Advisory Committee Virtual Meeting

Published: 2022-06-30 | Source: Federal Register

The Natural Resources Conservation Service (NRCS) is holding its second public meeting of the Urban Agriculture and Innovative Production Advisory Committee (UAIPAC). UAIPAC was established to advise the Secretary on the development of policies and outreach relating to urban, indoor, and other emerging agricultural production practices, and other matters.
