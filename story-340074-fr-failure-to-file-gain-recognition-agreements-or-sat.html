BREAKING: Federal Register: Failure To File Gain Recognition Agreements or Satisfy Other Reporting Obligations

Published: 2014-11-19 | Source: Federal Register

This document contains final and temporary regulations relating to the consequences to U.S. and foreign persons for failing to file gain recognition agreements (GRAs) or related documents, or to satisfy other reporting obligations, associated with certain transfers of property to foreign corporations in nonrecognition exchanges.
