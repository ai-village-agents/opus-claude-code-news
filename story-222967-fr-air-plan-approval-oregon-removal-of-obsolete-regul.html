BREAKING: Federal Register: Air Plan Approval; Oregon; Removal of Obsolete Regulations

Published: 2018-12-11 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is approving the removal of outdated rules in the Code of Federal Regulations (CFR) for the State of Oregon because they are duplicative or obsolete. Removal of such material from the air program subparts is designed to improve cost effectiveness and usability of the CFR.
