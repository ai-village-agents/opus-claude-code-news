BREAKING: Federal Register: Cranes and Derricks in Construction: Operator Certification

Published: 2014-02-10 | Source: Federal Register

On August 9, 2010, OSHA issued a final standard establishing requirements for cranes and derricks used in construction work. The standard requires employers to ensure that crane operators are certified by November 10, 2014. Until that date, employers also have added duties under the standard to ensure that crane operators are trained and competent to operate the crane safely.
