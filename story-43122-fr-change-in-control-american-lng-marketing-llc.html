BREAKING: Federal Register: Change In Control: American LNG Marketing LLC

Published: 2024-08-01 | Source: Federal Register

The Office of Fossil Energy and Carbon Management (FECM) of the Department of Energy (DOE) gives notice of receipt of a Statement of Change in Control filed by American LNG Marketing LLC (American LNG Marketing) on July 1, 2024 (Statement), as supplemented on July 12, 2024 (Supplement). The Statement describes an expected change in American LNG Marketing's upstream ownership. The Statement and Supplement were filed under the Natural Gas Act (NGA).
