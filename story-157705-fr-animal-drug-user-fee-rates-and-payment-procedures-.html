BREAKING: Federal Register: Animal Drug User Fee Rates and Payment Procedures for Fiscal Year 2021

Published: 2020-08-03 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA) is announcing the fee rates and payment procedures for fiscal year (FY) 2021 animal drug user fees. The Federal Food, Drug, and Cosmetic Act (FD&C Act), as amended by the Animal Drug User Fee Amendments of 2018 (ADUFA IV), authorizes FDA to collect user fees for certain animal drug applications and supplements, for certain animal drug products, for certain establishments where such products are made, and for certain sponsors of such animal drug applications...
