BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Inventory Completion: John Michael Kohler Arts Center, Sheboygan, WI

Published: 2021-08-11 | Source: Federal Register

The John Michael Kohler Arts Center has completed an inventory of human remains, in consultation with the appropriate Indian Tribes or Native Hawaiian organizations and has determined that there is a cultural affiliation between the human remains and present-day Indian Tribes or Native Hawaiian organizations.
