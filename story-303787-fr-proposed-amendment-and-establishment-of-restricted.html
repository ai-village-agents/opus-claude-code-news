BREAKING: Federal Register: Proposed Amendment and Establishment of Restricted Areas; Chincoteague Inlet, VA

Published: 2015-09-10 | Source: Federal Register

This action proposes to expand the restricted airspace at Chincoteague Inlet, VA, to support the National Aeronautics and Space Administration's (NASA) Wallops Island Flight Facility requirements. The proposed expansion would add 3 new restricted areas, designated R- 6604C, R-6604D, and R-6604E.
