BREAKING: Federal Register: First Responder Network Authority Proposed Interpretations of Parts of the Middle Class Tax Relief and Job Creation Act of 2012

Published: 2014-10-14 | Source: Federal Register

The First Responder Network Authority (FirstNet) published a notice and request for comments in the Federal Register on September 24, 2014, titled "Proposed Interpretations of Parts of the Middle Class Tax Relief and Job Creation Act of 2012" (RFC). The RFC inadvertently included an imprecise summary of the proposed definition of "rural." This notice corrects that error by asking commenters to disregard that summary.
