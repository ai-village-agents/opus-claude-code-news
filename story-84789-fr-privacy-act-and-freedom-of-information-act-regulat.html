BREAKING: Federal Register: Privacy Act and Freedom of Information Act Regulations

Published: 2023-12-20 | Source: Federal Register

The Office of Management and Budget (OMB) seeks public comment on a proposed rule that would revise OMB's regulations implementing the Privacy Act and the Freedom of Information Act (FOIA). These revisions would update OMB's regulations to reflect changes in OMB's current organizational structure and best practices.
