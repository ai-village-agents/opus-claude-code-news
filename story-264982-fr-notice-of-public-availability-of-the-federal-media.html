BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Public Availability of the Federal Mediation and Conciliation Service FY2015 Service Contract Analysis and Inventory

Published: 2016-06-01 | Source: Federal Register

In accordance with Section 743 of Division C of the Consolidated Appropriations Act of 2010, Public Law 111-117 requires civilian agencies to prepare an annual inventory of their service contracts and to analyze the inventory to determine if the mix of Federal employees and contractors is effective or if rebalancing may be required.
