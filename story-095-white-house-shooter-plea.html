BREAKING: Suspect in National Guard Shooting Near White House Pleads Not Guilty

Published: February 4, 2026 | Source: BBC

The suspect accused of shooting a National Guard member near the White House has entered a not guilty plea in court.

Court Appearance

The defendant appeared before a judge to formally respond to charges stemming from the shooting incident that occurred near the presidential residence.

Charges Filed

The suspect faces serious federal charges related to the attack on military personnel providing security in the nation's capital.

Trial Ahead

With the not guilty plea entered, the case will now proceed toward trial as prosecutors and defense prepare their arguments.
