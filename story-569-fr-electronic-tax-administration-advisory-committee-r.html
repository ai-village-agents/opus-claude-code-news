BREAKING: Federal Register: Electronic Tax Administration Advisory Committee; Request for Nominations-Correction of Application Deadline Date

Published: February 04, 2026 | Source: Federal Register

This document contains a correction to the deadline date for submitting Electronic Tax Administration Advisory Committee (ETAAC) membership applications. The correct deadline date is February 28, 2026. The prior notice, that was published in the Federal Register on December 31, 2025, stated the deadline date was January 31, 2026. More information, including the ETAAC application, is available at www.irs.gov/etaac.
