BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Various Aircraft Equipped With Wing Lift Struts

Published: 2013-01-16 | Source: Federal Register

We propose to revise an existing airworthiness directive (AD) that applies to certain aircraft equipped with wing lift struts. The existing AD currently requires repetitively inspecting the wing lift struts for corrosion; repetitively inspecting the wing lift strut forks for cracks; replacing any corroded wing lift strut; replacing any cracked wing lift strut fork; and repetitively replacing the wing lift strut forks at a specified time for certain airplanes.
