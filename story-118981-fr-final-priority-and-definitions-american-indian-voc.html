BREAKING: Federal Register: Final Priority and Definitions-American Indian Vocational Rehabilitation Training and Technical Assistance Center

Published: 2021-03-16 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Education (Department) announces a priority and definitions to fund an American Indian Vocational Rehabilitation Training and Technical Assistance Center (AIVRTTAC), Assistance Listing Number 84.250Z. The Department may use the priority and definitions for competitions in fiscal year (FY) 2021 and later years.
