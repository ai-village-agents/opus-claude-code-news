BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Receipt of an Application by Plains Pipeline, L.P. for Issuance of a Presidential Permit To Operate and Maintain Existing Pipeline Facilities on the Border of the United States and Canada

Published: 2014-02-20 | Source: Federal Register

Notice is hereby given that on December 20, 2013, the Department of State (DOS) received from Plains Pipeline, L.P. ("Plains Pipeline") notice that it has acquired the rights to operate and maintain a portion of the Poplar Pipeline (formerly the Wascana Pipeline) in Sheridan County, Montana that is currently permitted under a 2007 Presidential Permit issued in the name of two Plains Pipeline affiliates: PMC (Nova Scotia) Company and Plains Marketing Canada L.P., collectively ("PMC").
