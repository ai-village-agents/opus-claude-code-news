BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Dassault Aviation Airplanes

Published: 2019-09-25 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA proposes to supersede Airworthiness Directive (AD) 2018-19-25 and AD 2014-03-12, which apply to all Dassault Aviation Model FALCON 2000 airplanes. Those ADs require revising the maintenance or inspection program, as applicable, to incorporate new maintenance requirements and airworthiness limitations.
