BREAKING: Federal Register: Air Plan Approval; NC: Readoption of Air Quality Rules and Removal of the Oxygenated Gasoline Rules

Published: 2019-04-10 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is taking final action to approve a State Implementation Plan (SIP) revision submitted by the North Carolina Department of Environmental Quality, Division of Air Quality (DAQ), on March 21, 2018, readopting and amending several air quality rules, and requesting to remove the rules for the oxygenated gasoline program.
