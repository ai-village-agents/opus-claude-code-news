BREAKING: Federal Register: Agency Information Collection Activities: Renewal of a Currently Approved Collection; Comment Request; Foreign Banks

The FDIC hereby gives notice that it is seeking comment on renewal of its "Foreign Banks" information collection (OMB No. 3064-0114).
