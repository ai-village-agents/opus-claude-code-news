BREAKING: Federal Register: Entry-Level Driver Training: SBL Truck Driving Academy, Inc.; Application for Exemption

Published: 2022-12-22 | Source: Federal Register

FMCSA announces its decision to deny the exemption application from SBL Truck Driving Academy, Inc. (SBL). SBL sought an exemption from the theory and behind-the-wheel (BTW) instructor requirements contained in the entry-level driver training (ELDT) regulations for two of its instructors.
