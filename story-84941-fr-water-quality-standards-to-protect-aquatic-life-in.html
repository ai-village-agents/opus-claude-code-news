BREAKING: Federal Register: Water Quality Standards To Protect Aquatic Life in the Delaware River

Published: 2023-12-21 | Source: Federal Register

On December 1, 2022, the U.S. Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) determined that revised water quality standards are necessary to protect aquatic life in certain water quality management zones of the Delaware River.
