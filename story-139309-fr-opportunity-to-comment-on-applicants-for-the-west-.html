BREAKING: Federal Register: Opportunity To Comment on Applicants for the West Lafayette, Indiana U.S. Grain Standards Act Designation Area

Published: 2021-12-03 | Source: Federal Register

In the June 28, 2021, Federal Register, AMS asked persons interested in providing official U.S. Grain Standards Act services in the West Lafayette, Indiana designation area to submit an application. There are two applicants for the West Lafayette, Indiana area: Champaign-Danville Grain Inspection Departments, Inc. (Champaign) and North Dakota Grain Inspection Service, Inc. (North Dakota).
