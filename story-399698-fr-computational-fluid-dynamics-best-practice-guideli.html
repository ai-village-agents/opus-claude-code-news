BREAKING: Federal Register: Computational Fluid Dynamics Best Practice Guidelines for Dry Cask Applications

Published: 2012-10-23 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Nuclear Regulatory Commission (NRC or the Commission) is requesting public comments on draft NUREG-2152, "Computational Fluid Dynamics Best Practice Guidelines for Dry Cask Applications." The draft NUREG-2152 report provides best practice guidelines for undertaking simulations used to evaluate the thermal response of dry casks.
