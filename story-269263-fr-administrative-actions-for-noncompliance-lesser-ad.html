BREAKING: Federal Register: Administrative Actions for Noncompliance; Lesser Administrative Actions; Confirmation of Effective Date

Published: 2016-07-21 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA) is confirming the effective date of August 17, 2016, for the direct final rule that appeared in the Federal Register of April 4, 2016. The direct final rule amends the regulations describing lesser administrative actions that may be imposed on an Institutional Review Board (IRB) that has failed to comply with applicable regulations.
