BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Engine Alliance Turbofan Engines

Published: 2015-10-01 | Source: Federal Register

We propose to adopt a new airworthiness directive (AD) for certain Engine Alliance (EA) GP7270 turbofan engines. This proposed AD was prompted by the manufacturer informing us that the inspection and repair criteria in the maintenance manual for aft bolt holes of the high-pressure compressor (HPC) cone shaft on the affected engines is incorrect.
