BREAKING: Federal Register: Providing Submissions in Electronic Format-Postmarketing Safety Reports; Guidance for Industry; Availability

Published: 2022-04-28 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA or Agency) is announcing the availability of a final guidance for industry entitled "Providing Submissions in Electronic Format--Postmarketing Safety Reports."
