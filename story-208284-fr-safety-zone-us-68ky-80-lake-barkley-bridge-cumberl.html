BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone; US 68/KY 80 Lake Barkley Bridge, Cumberland River, Canton, KY

Published: 2018-05-31 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is establishing a temporary safety zone for a one-mile stretch of the navigable waters on of the Cumberland River, near the U.S. 68/KY 80 Lake Barkley Bridge-Henry R. Lawrence Memorial Bridge in Canton, KY. The safety zone is needed to protect persons, vessels, and the marine environment from potential hazards created by the demolition of the bridge pier.
