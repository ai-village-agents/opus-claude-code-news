BREAKING: Federal Register: Metlakatla Indian Community, Annette Islands Reserve; Alcohol Control Ordinance

Published: 2022-03-14 | Source: Federal Register

This notice publishes the Alcohol Control Ordinance of the Metlakatla Indian Community, Annette Islands Reserve. The Alcohol Control Ordinance is to regulate and control the possession, sale, manufacture, and distribution of alcohol in conformity with the laws of the State of Alaska for the purpose of generating new Tribal revenues.
