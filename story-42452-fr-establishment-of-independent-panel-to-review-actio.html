BREAKING: Federal Register: Establishment of Independent Panel To Review Actions Relating to the Attempted Assassination of Former President Donald J. Trump

Published: 2024-07-24 | Source: Federal Register

In order to facilitate an effective review of the security provided by the U.S. Secret Service to Former President Donald J. Trump on July 13, 2024, the Department of Homeland Security (Department or DHS) is creating an Independent Review Panel (Panel).
