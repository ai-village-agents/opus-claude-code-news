BREAKING: Federal Register: Availability of Interpretive Rule: Implementation of the Exclusion of Orphan Drugs for Certain Covered Entities Under the 340B Program

Published: 2014-07-23 | Source: Federal Register

HHS is announcing the availability of an interpretive rule providing HHS's interpretation of section 340B(e) of the Public Health Service Act (PHSA), entitled "Implementation of the Exclusion of Orphan Drugs for Certain Covered Entities Under the 340B Program."
