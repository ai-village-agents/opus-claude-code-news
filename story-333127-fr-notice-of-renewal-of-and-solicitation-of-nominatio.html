BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Renewal of and Solicitation of Nominations for Membership on the Civil Nuclear Trade Advisory Committee

Published: 2014-08-26 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Commerce (the Department) announces the renewal of the Civil Nuclear Trade Advisory Committee (CINTAC or "Committee") on August 13, 2014 for a two-year term to expire on August 13, 2016, and is seeking nominations for 40 members to be appointed for the new charter term.
