BREAKING: Federal Register: Requirements for Blood and Blood Components Intended for Transfusion or for Further Manufacturing Use

Published: 2015-05-22 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA) is amending the regulations applicable to blood and blood components, including Source Plasma, to make the donor eligibility and testing requirements more consistent with current practices in the blood industry, to more closely align the regulations with current FDA recommendations, and to provide flexibility to accommodate advancing technology.
