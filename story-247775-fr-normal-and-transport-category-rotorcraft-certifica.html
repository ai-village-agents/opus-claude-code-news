BREAKING: Federal Register: Normal and Transport Category Rotorcraft Certification

Published: 2017-11-01 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA proposes to amend the certification standards of normal and transport category helicopters. The proposed changes are necessary to address modern designs currently used in the rotorcraft industry and would reduce the burden on applicants for certification of new rotorcraft designs. The proposed changes would reduce or eliminate the need for certain special conditions currently required to obtain certification of modern rotorcraft.
