BREAKING: Federal Register: Filing Dates for the Illinois Special Elections in the 18th Congressional District

Published: 2015-05-04 | Source: Federal Register

Illinois has scheduled special elections on July 7, 2015, and September 10, 2015, to fill the U.S. House of Representative seat in the 18th Congressional District vacated by Representative Aaron Schock. Committees required to file reports in connection with the Special Primary Election on July 7, 2015, shall file a 12-day Pre-Primary Report.
