BREAKING: Federal Register: Ocean Dumping; Withdrawal of Designated Disposal Sites; Nome, Alaska

Published: 2024-05-03 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is taking direct final action to withdraw from EPA regulation and management two designated ocean dredged material disposal sites, the Nome East and Nome West Sites (Sites), located near Nome, Alaska, pursuant to the Marine Protection, Research, and Sanctuaries Act (MPRSA), as amended.
