BREAKING: Federal Register: Common Formats for Patient Safety Data Collection and Event Reporting

Published: 2014-02-18 | Source: Federal Register

As authorized by the Secretary of HHS, AHRQ coordinates the development of common definitions and reporting formats (Common Formats) for reporting patient safety events to Patient Safety Organizations (PSOs) and other entities. The purpose of this notice is to announce the availability of a new type of Common Formats for public review and comment--Common Formats for Surveillance--Hospital.
