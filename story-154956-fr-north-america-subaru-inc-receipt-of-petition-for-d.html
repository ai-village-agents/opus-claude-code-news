BREAKING: Federal Register: North America Subaru, Inc., Receipt of Petition for Decision of Inconsequential Noncompliance

Published: 2020-06-29 | Source: Federal Register

North America Subaru, Inc., (NASI) on behalf of Subaru Corporation and Subaru of America, Inc. (Subaru) has determined that certain model year (MY) 2016--2020 Subaru Impreza motor vehicles do not fully comply with Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard (FMVSS) No. 108, Lamps, Reflective Devices, and Associated Equipment.
