BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zones; New Year's Fireworks Display, Hood Canal, Washington

Published: 2023-12-19 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard will a enforce safety zone surrounding the Alderbrook Resort dock involved in a fireworks display in Hood Canal, WA, from December 31, 2023, through January 1, 2024 to provide for the safety of life on navigable waterways during the event. Our regulation for marine events within the Thirteenth Coast Guard District identifies the regulated area for this event in Hood Canal, WA.
