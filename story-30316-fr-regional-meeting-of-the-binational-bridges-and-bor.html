BREAKING: Federal Register: Regional Meeting of the Binational Bridges and Border Crossings Group in Mexico City, Mexico

Published: 2024-02-23 | Source: Federal Register

Delegates from the United States and Mexican governments, the states of New Mexico and Texas, and the Mexican states of Chihuahua, Coahuila, Nuevo Laredo, and Tamaulipas will participate in a regional meeting of the U.S.-Mexico Binational Bridges and Border Crossings Group. The purpose of this meeting is to discuss operational matters involving existing and proposed international bridges and border crossings and their related infrastructure and to exchange technical information as well as views ...
