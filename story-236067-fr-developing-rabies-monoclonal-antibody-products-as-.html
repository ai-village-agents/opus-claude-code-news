BREAKING: Federal Register: Developing Rabies Monoclonal Antibody Products as a Component of Rabies Post-Exposure Prophylaxis; Public Workshop; Request for Comments

Published: 2017-06-07 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA, the Agency, or we) is announcing a public workshop regarding development of rabies monoclonal antibody products to be used in conjunction with licensed rabies vaccine as part of a rabies post-exposure prophylaxis (PEP) regimen.
