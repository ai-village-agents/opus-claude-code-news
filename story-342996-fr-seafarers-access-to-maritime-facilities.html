BREAKING: Federal Register: Seafarers' Access to Maritime Facilities

Published: 2014-12-29 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard proposes to require each owner or operator of a facility regulated by the Coast Guard to implement a system that provides seafarers and other individuals with access between vessels moored at the facility and the facility gate, in a timely manner and at no cost to the seafarer or other individual.
