BREAKING: Federal Register: Streamlining of Provisions on State Plans for Occupational Safety and Health

Published: 2015-12-18 | Source: Federal Register

On August 18, 2015 OSHA published in the Federal Register a direct final rule that streamlined provisions on State Plans. OSHA stated in that document that it would withdraw the companion proposed rule and confirm the effective date of the final rule if the Agency received no significant adverse comments on the direct final rule or the proposal.
