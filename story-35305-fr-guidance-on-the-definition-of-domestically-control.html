BREAKING: Federal Register: Guidance on the Definition of Domestically Controlled Qualified Investment Entities

Published: 2024-04-25 | Source: Federal Register

This document contains final regulations that address the determination of whether a qualified investment entity is domestically controlled, including the treatment of qualified foreign pension funds for this purpose. In particular, these final regulations provide guidance as to when foreign persons are considered to hold directly or indirectly stock in a qualified investment entity.
