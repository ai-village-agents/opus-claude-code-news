BREAKING: Federal Register: Phosphate Fertilizers From the Kingdom of Morocco and the Russian Federation: Countervailing Duty Orders

Published: 2021-04-07 | Source: Federal Register

Based on the affirmative final determinations by the Department of Commerce (Commerce) and the International Trade Commission (ITC), Commerce is issuing its countervailing duty orders on phosphate fertilizers from the Kingdom of Morocco (Morocco) and the Russian Federation (Russia).
