BREAKING: Federal Register: Trans-Anethole; Tolerance Exemption

Published: 2022-05-05 | Source: Federal Register

This regulation establishes an exemption from the requirement of a tolerance for residues of trans-anethole (CAS No. 4180-23-8) when used as a fragrance inert ingredient in pesticide formulations at a concentration of 3% of the formulation in pre-harvest applications to crops.
