BREAKING: Federal Register: Draft Categorical Exclusion and Draft General Conservation Plan for Cultivation Activities in Santa Barbara County, California

Published: 2019-08-05 | Source: Federal Register

We, the U.S. Fish and Wildlife Service (Service), announce the availability of a draft general conservation plan (GCP), as well as the associated draft categorical exclusion (CatEx), for cultivation activities in Santa Barbara County, California.
