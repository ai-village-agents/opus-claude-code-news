BREAKING: Federal Register: International Aviation Safety Assessment (IASA) Program Change

Published: 2013-03-08 | Source: Federal Register

This statement describes a policy change to the FAA's International Aviation Safety Assessment (IASA) program. The FAA wants to ensure that countries do not remain on this listing when the results of the FAA's IASA determinations as to those countries might no longer be accurate or reasonably current.
