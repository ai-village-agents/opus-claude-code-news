BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Eurocopter Deutschland GmbH Helicopters

Published: 2013-10-31 | Source: Federal Register

We are adopting a new airworthiness directive (AD) for Eurocopter Deutschland GmbH (Eurocopter) Model EC135 P1, P2, P2+, T1, T2, and T2+ helicopters. This AD requires inspecting each linear transducer bearing (bearing) for freedom of movement and replacing the bearing if there is binding or rough turning or if there is chafing or damage on the lower side of the floor.
