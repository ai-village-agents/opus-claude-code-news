BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Intent To Discontinue Use of Paper Applications for Airman Medical Certification

Published: 2012-03-08 | Source: Federal Register

The Federal Aviation Administration is providing public notice regarding its intent to discontinue use of the paper version of FAA Form 8500-8, the application form used to apply for FAA medical certification. Maintaining FAA Form 8500-8 for applicants to complete manually is burdensome not only in terms of the cost involved, but also in terms of the complex logistics and use of Agency resources involved.
