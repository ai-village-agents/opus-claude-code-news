BREAKING: Federal Register: National Advisory Committee

Published: 2022-02-18 | Source: Federal Register

The Census Bureau is giving notice of a virtual meeting of the National Advisory Committee (NAC). The Committee will address strategies for communicating the use of differential privacy for the 2020 Census data products.
