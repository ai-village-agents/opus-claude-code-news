BREAKING: Federal Register: Supporting the Head Start Workforce and Consistent Quality Programming

Published: 2023-11-20 | Source: Federal Register

We propose to add new requirements to the Head Start Program Performance Standards (HSPPS) to support and stabilize the Head Start workforce, including requirements for wages and benefits, breaks for staff, and enhanced supports for staff health and wellness. We also propose to enhance several existing requirements and add new requirements to promote consistent quality of services across Head Start programs.
