BREAKING: Federal Register: Alternate PCB Extraction Methods and Amendments to PCB Cleanup and Disposal Regulations; Extension of Comment Period

Published: 2021-12-20 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is extending the comment period for the proposed rule entitled "Alternate PCB Extraction Methods and Amendments to PCB Cleanup and Disposal Regulations." EPA published the proposed rule in the Federal Register on October 22, 2021, and the public comment period was scheduled to end on December 21, 2021.
