BREAKING: Federal Register: Operation in the 57-64 GHz Band

Published: 2013-09-30 | Source: Federal Register

This document modifies the Commission's rules for intentional radiators operating on an unlicensed basis in the 57-64 GHz frequency range (60 GHz).
