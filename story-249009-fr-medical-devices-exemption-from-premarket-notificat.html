BREAKING: Federal Register: Medical Devices; Exemption From Premarket Notification: Over-the-Counter Denture Repair Kits

Published: 2017-11-20 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA or Agency) is announcing that it has received a petition requesting exemption from the premarket notification requirements for over-the-counter (OTC) denture repair kits. These devices consist of material, such as a resin monomer system of powder and liquid glues, which is intended to be applied permanently to a denture to mend cracks or breaks.
