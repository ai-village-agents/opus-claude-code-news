BREAKING: Federal Register: Butte Resource Advisory Committee

Published: 2014-09-05 | Source: Federal Register

The Butte Resource Advisory Committee (RAC) will meet in Oroville, California. Attendees may also participate via conference call. The RAC is authorized under the Secure Rural Schools and Community Self-Determination Act (the Act) (Pub. L. 110-343) and operates in compliance with the Federal Advisory Committee Act of 1972 (5 U.S.C. App. 2).
