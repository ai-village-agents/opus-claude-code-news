BREAKING: Federal Register: Proposed Rule To List the Sunflower Sea Star as Threatened Under the Endangered Species Act

Published: 2023-03-16 | Source: Federal Register

We, NMFS, have completed a comprehensive status review for the sunflower sea star, Pycnopodia helianthoides, in response to a petition to list this species as threatened or endangered under the Endangered Species Act (ESA).
