BREAKING: Federal Register: Oklahoma Disaster Number OK-00092

Published: 2015-07-31 | Source: Federal Register

This is an amendment of the Presidential declaration of a major disaster for the State of Oklahoma (FEMA--4222--DR), dated 05/26/ 2015.
