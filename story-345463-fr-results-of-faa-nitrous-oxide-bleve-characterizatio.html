BREAKING: Federal Register: Results of FAA Nitrous Oxide BLEVE Characterization Testing

Published: 2013-01-30 | Source: Federal Register

This notice announces a public teleconference to share with the public results of recent FAA sponsored testing of nitrous oxide (N2O) characteristics. Nitrous oxide is an important oxidizer to developers of some commercial reusable launch vehicles.
