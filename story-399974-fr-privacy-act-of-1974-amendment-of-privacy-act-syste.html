BREAKING: Federal Register: Privacy Act of 1974; Amendment of Privacy Act System of Records

Published: 2012-10-25 | Source: Federal Register

The Merit Systems Protection Board (MSPB) is issuing public notice of its intent to amend a Government-wide system of records that it maintains subject to the Privacy Act of 1974 (5 U.S.C. 552a). MSPB/ GOVT-1, "Appeals and Case Records," is being amended to reflect that its location is in the Office of the Clerk of the Board.
