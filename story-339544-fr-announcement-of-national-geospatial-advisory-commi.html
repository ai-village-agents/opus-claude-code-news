BREAKING: Federal Register: Announcement of National Geospatial Advisory Committee Meeting

Published: 2014-11-13 | Source: Federal Register

The National Geospatial Advisory Committee (NGAC) will meet on December 3, 2014, from 1:00 p.m. to 4:00 p.m. EST. The meeting will be held via web conference and teleconference.
