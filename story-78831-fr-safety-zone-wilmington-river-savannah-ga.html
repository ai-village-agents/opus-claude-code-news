BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone; Wilmington River, Savannah, GA

Published: 2023-10-03 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is revising a temporary safety zone encompassing the Causton Bluff Bridge, on the Wilmington River, Savannah, GA. This action is necessary to provide for the safety of life on these navigable waters for the planned demolition and removal of structural components of the original bridge, in preparation of the construction of a new span.
