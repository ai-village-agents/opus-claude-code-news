BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives Airbus Helicopters (Previously Eurocopter France)

Published: 2015-09-25 | Source: Federal Register

We propose to supersede airworthiness directive (AD) 2014-12- 51 for Airbus Helicopters (previously Eurocopter France) Model EC130B4 and EC130T2 helicopters. AD 2014-12-51 currently requires repetitively inspecting the tailboom to Fenestron junction frame (junction frame) for a crack.
