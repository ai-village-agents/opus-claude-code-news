BREAKING: Federal Register: Addition of Nonylphenol Category; Community Right-To-Know Toxic Chemical Release Reporting

Published: 2014-09-30 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is adding a nonylphenol category to the list of toxic chemicals subject to reporting under section 313 of the Emergency Planning and Community Right-to-Know Act (EPCRA) of 1986 and section 6607 of the Pollution Prevention Act (PPA) of 1990.
