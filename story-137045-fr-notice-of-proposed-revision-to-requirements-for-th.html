BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Proposed Revision to Requirements for the Importation of Plums From Chile Into the United States

Published: 2021-11-03 | Source: Federal Register

We are advising the public that we have prepared a commodity import evaluation document (CIED) relative to the importation into the United States of plums from Chile. Chile plums are currently subject to irradiation, either in Chile or in the United States, as a mitigation for European grapevine moth (EGVM).
