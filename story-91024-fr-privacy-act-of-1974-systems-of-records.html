BREAKING: Federal Register: Privacy Act of 1974; Systems of Records

Published: 2022-03-14 | Source: Federal Register

The Equal Employment Opportunity Commission (hereinafter "EEOC" or "the Commission") proposes to create a new Religious Accommodation system of records to maintain information collected in response to a request for an accommodation based on a sincerely held religious belief, practice, or observance.
