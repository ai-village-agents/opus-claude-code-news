BREAKING: Federal Register: Meeting of the California Desert District Advisory Council

Published: 2013-05-09 | Source: Federal Register

Notice is hereby given, in accordance with Public Laws 92-463 and 94-579, that the California Desert District Advisory Council (DAC) to the Bureau of Land Management (BLM), U.S. Department of the Interior, will meet in formal session on Saturday, June 8, 2013, from 8 a.m. to 4:30 p.m. in Ridgecrest, Calif. at a location to be announced later.
