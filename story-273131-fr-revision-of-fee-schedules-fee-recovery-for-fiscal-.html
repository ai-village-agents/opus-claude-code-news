BREAKING: Federal Register: Revision of Fee Schedules; Fee Recovery for Fiscal Year 2016; Correction

Published: 2016-09-06 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Nuclear Regulatory Commission (NRC) published a final rule amending regulations that became effective August 23, 2016. The fiscal year (FY) 2016 final fee rule, published June 24, 2016, amended the licensing, inspection, special project, and annual fees charged to NRC applicants and licensees. This document corrects the annual fee for materials licensees in the category "Nuclear laundries" from the FY 2016 rate of $0 to the FY 2015 rate of $40,100.
