BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Austro Engine GmbH Engines

Published: 2023-11-06 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA is adopting a new airworthiness directive (AD) for certain Austro Engine GmbH Model E4 and E4P engines. This AD is prompted by reports of piston failures and the determination that certain batches of pistons were manufactured with a dimensional deviation in the piston pin bore and piston diameter.
