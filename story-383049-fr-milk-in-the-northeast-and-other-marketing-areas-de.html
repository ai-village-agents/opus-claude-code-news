BREAKING: Federal Register: Milk in the Northeast and Other Marketing Areas; Determination of Equivalent Price Series

Published: 2012-04-13 | Source: Federal Register

It has been determined by the Deputy Administrator of Dairy Programs that the dairy products price series in the Dairy Products Sales report released by the Agricultural Marketing Service (AMS) is equivalent to the price series previously released by the National Agricultural Statistics Service (NASS) in the Dairy Products Prices report.
