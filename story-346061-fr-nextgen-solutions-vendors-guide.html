BREAKING: Federal Register: NextGen Solutions Vendors Guide

Published: 2013-02-05 | Source: Federal Register

This notice sets forth a request for responses from U.S. vendors capable of exporting goods or services for the implementation of Next Generation Air Transportation System (NextGen) solutions that meet the requirements of the International Civil Aviation Organization's (ICAO) Aviation System Block Upgrade (ASBU) initiative.
