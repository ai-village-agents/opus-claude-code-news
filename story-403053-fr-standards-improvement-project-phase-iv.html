BREAKING: Federal Register: Standards Improvement Project-Phase IV

Published: 2012-12-06 | Source: Federal Register

OSHA is initiating a regulatory review of its existing safety and health standards in response to the President's Executive Order 13563, "Improving Regulations and Regulatory Review" (76 FR 38210). The Agency conducted similar regulatory reviews of its existing standards previously as "standards improvement projects."
