BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Airbus SAS Airplanes

Published: 2025-09-26 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA is superseding Airworthiness Directive (AD) 2024-22- 02, which applied to certain Airbus SAS Model A330-200, -200 Freighter, -300, -800, and -900 series airplanes. AD 2024-22-02 required revising the existing maintenance or inspection program, as applicable, to incorporate new or more restrictive airworthiness limitations.
