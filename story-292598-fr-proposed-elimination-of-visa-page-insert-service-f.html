BREAKING: Federal Register: Proposed Elimination of Visa Page Insert Service for U.S. Passport Book Holders

Published: 2015-04-29 | Source: Federal Register

Currently, all U.S. passport book applicants may apply for either a 28-page or 52-page passport book at no extra charge. U.S. passport book holders may then apply for additional visa pages while the passport book is still valid. The Department of State proposes eliminating the option to add visa pages in passports beginning January 1, 2016.
