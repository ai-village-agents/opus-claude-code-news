BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; The Boeing Company Airplanes

Published: 2020-03-09 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA is adopting a new airworthiness directive (AD) for certain The Boeing Company Model 747-400 series airplanes. This AD was prompted by a report of a certain modification that causes interference with inspections that are intended to detect fatigue cracks.
