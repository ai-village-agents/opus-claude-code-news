BREAKING: Federal Register: Special Enrollment Examination User Fee for Enrolled Agents

Published: 2016-10-25 | Source: Federal Register

This document withdraws a proposed regulation relating to the user fee for the special enrollment examination to become an enrolled agent. This document also proposes a new regulation to increase the user fee for the examination to recover the cost to the IRS of overseeing the administration of the examination.
