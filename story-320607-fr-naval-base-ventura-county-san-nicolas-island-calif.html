BREAKING: Federal Register: Naval Base Ventura County, San Nicolas Island, California; Restricted Area

Published: 2014-04-02 | Source: Federal Register

The Corps of Engineers (Corps) is amending its regulations to modify an existing permanent restricted area in the waters of the Pacific Ocean surrounding San Nicolas Island, California. The modifications realign subsections (designated Alpha, Bravo and Charlie) within the restricted area to better match the U.S. Navy's current operational requirements.
