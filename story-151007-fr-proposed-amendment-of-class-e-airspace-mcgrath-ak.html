BREAKING: Federal Register: Proposed Amendment of Class E Airspace; McGrath, AK

Published: 2020-05-07 | Source: Federal Register

This action proposes to amend Class E airspace, designated a surface area, at Mc Grath Airport, McGrath, AK. To properly size the area, it should be reduced from a 7.6-mile radius to a 5.6-mile radius of the airport. This action also proposes to properly size Class E airspace extending upward from 700 feet above the surface.
