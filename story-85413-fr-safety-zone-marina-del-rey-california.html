BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone; Marina Del Rey, California

Published: 2023-12-28 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is adding two events to the table regarding "Southern California Annual Firework Events for the Los Angeles Long Beach Captain of the Port Zone". The additions are temporary safety zones, one for the Marina del Rey Annual Boat Parade Fireworks Show and another for the Marina Del Rey New Year's Eve Fireworks Display.
