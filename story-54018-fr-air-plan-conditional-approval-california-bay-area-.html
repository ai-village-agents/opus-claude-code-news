BREAKING: Federal Register: Air Plan Conditional Approval; California; Bay Area Air Quality Management District

Published: 2024-11-29 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is proposing to conditionally approve revisions to the Bay Area Air Quality Management District (BAAQMD) portion of the California State Implementation Plan (SIP). These revisions concern emissions of oxides of nitrogen (NOX) from natural gas-fired furnaces and water heaters.
