BREAKING: Federal Register: General Directive 24-1: Required Actions Regarding Assaults on Transit Workers

Published: 2024-09-25 | Source: Federal Register

The Federal Transit Administration (FTA) is issuing a General Directive to address the significant and continuing national-level safety risk related to assaults on transit workers.
