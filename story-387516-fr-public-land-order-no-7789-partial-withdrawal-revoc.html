BREAKING: Federal Register: Public Land Order No. 7789; Partial Withdrawal Revocation and Transfer of Administrative Jurisdiction for Fort Vancouver National Historic Site, WA

Published: 2012-06-05 | Source: Federal Register

This order partially revokes a withdrawal created by a Secretarial Order, General Orders, and an Executive Order insofar as they affect approximately 33.75 acres of public land reserved for military purposes on behalf of the United States Department of the Army for the East and South Vancouver Barracks in Washington.
