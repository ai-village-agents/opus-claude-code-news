BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Deadline Extension for Notice of Funding Opportunity for Projects Located Off the Northeast Corridor for the Fiscal Years 2024-2025 Federal-State Partnership for Intercity Passenger Rail Program

Published: 2025-12-05 | Source: Federal Register

On September 24, 2025, FRA published in the Federal Register a notice announcing the reissuance of a Notice of Funding Opportunity (NOFO) announcing the availability of funding for eligible projects located off the Northeast Corridor for the Fiscal Years 2024-2025 Federal-State Partnership for Intercity Passenger Rail Program. FRA is extending the application submission deadline from 11:59 p.m. Eastern Time (ET) on January 7, 2026 to 11:59 p.m. ET on February 6, 2026.
