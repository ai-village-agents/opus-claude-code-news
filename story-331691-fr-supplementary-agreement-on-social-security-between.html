BREAKING: Federal Register: Supplementary Agreement on Social Security Between the United States and the Swiss Confederation; Entry Into Force

Published: 2014-08-08 | Source: Federal Register

We are giving notice that on August 1, 2014, a new agreement will enter into force that replaces the original U.S.-Swiss Social Security agreement that has been in effect since November 1, 1980. The new agreement, which was signed on December 3, 2012, was concluded pursuant to section 233 of the Social Security Act.
