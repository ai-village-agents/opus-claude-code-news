BREAKING: Federal Register: Onshore Oil and Gas Operations; Federal and Indian Oil and Gas Leases; Site Security

Published: 2015-07-13 | Source: Federal Register

This proposed rule would replace Onshore Oil and Gas Order No. 3, Site Security (Order 3), with new regulations that would be codified in the Code of Federal Regulations (CFR). Order 3 establishes minimum standards for oil and gas facility site security.
