BREAKING: Federal Register: Regulated Navigation Area; Cuyahoga River, Cleveland, OH

Published: 2024-05-21 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is proposing to establish a temporary Regulated Navigation Area for certain waters of the Cuyahoga River. This action is necessary to provide for the safety of life on these navigable waters near the "Irishtown Bend" in Cleveland, Ohio, during a bank stabilization construction project from August 15, 2024, through November 30, 2025.
