BREAKING: Federal Register: Proposed Reissuance of NPDES General Permit for Offshore Seafood Processors in Alaska (AKG524000)

Published: 2019-03-25 | Source: Federal Register

The Director, Office of Water and Watersheds, Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) Region 10, is proposing to reissue a National Pollutant Discharge Elimination System (NPDES) General Permit to Offshore Seafood Processors in Alaska.
