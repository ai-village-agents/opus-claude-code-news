BREAKING: Federal Register: Environmental Impact Statement for Allen Fossil Plant Ash Impoundment Closures

Published: 2018-11-30 | Source: Federal Register

The Tennessee Valley Authority (TVA) intends to prepare an Environmental Impact Statement (EIS) to address the potential environmental effects associated with the future management of coal combustion residual (CCR) material at the Allen Fossil Plant (ALF) located in Shelby County, Tennessee, southwest of the City of Memphis.
