BREAKING: Federal Register: Request for Public Comment on MBDA Information Clearinghouse

Published: 2024-12-26 | Source: Federal Register

The Minority Business Development Agency (MBDA) is accepting written comments from the public regarding the MBDA Information Clearinghouse, through February 28, 2025. The comments will inform MBDA of the types of data and other information sought, best practices or recommendations for studies, analysis and data about Minority Business Enterprises (MBEs) to be published, and ways to improve the user experience.
