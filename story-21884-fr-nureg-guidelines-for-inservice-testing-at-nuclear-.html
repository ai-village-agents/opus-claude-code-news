BREAKING: Federal Register: NUREG: Guidelines for Inservice Testing at Nuclear Power Plants-Inservice Testing of Pumps and Valves and Inservice Examination and Testing of Dynamic Restraints (Snubbers) at Nuclear Power Plants

Published: 2025-10-02 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Nuclear Regulatory Commission (NRC) is issuing NUREG- 1482, Revision 4, "Guidelines for Inservice Testing at Nuclear Power Plants--Inservice Testing of Pumps and Valves and Inservice Examination and Testing of Dynamic Restraints (Snubbers) at Nuclear Power Plants." NUREG-1482, Revision 4 provides a basic understanding of the regulatory basis for pump and valve inservice testing (IST) programs and dynamic restraint (snubbers) examination and testing programs.
