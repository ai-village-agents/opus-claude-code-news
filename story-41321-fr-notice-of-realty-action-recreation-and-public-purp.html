BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Realty Action: Recreation and Public Purposes Act Classification; Arizona

Published: 2024-07-09 | Source: Federal Register

The Bureau of Land Management (BLM), Hassayampa Field Office, examined two parcels consisting of approximately 640 acres of public land and determined that the parcels are suitable for classification for lease and/or conveyance under the provisions of the Recreation and Public Purpose Act, as amended (R&PP).
