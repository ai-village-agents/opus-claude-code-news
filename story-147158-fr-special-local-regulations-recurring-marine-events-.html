BREAKING: Federal Register: Special Local Regulations; Recurring Marine Events in the Lake Michigan Captain of the Port Zone

Published: 2020-03-19 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is proposing to amend the rules that regulate vessel traffic and control navigation on portions of waterways in the Lake Michigan Captain of the Port Zone during events that will introduce extra or unusual hazards to the safety of life on the navigable waters of the United States.
