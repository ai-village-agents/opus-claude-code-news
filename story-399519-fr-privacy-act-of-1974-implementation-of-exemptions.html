BREAKING: Federal Register: Privacy Act of 1974; Implementation of Exemptions

Published: 2012-10-19 | Source: Federal Register

The Consumer Financial Protection Bureau ("CFPB" or the "Bureau") is proposing to amend its regulations to exempt portions of its system of records entitled "CFPB.005--Consumer Response System" from certain provisions of the Privacy Act of 1974, as amended (the "Privacy Act").
