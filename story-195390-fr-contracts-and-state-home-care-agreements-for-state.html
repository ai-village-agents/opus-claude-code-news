BREAKING: Federal Register: Contracts and State Home Care Agreements for State Home Nursing Home Care

This rulemaking adopts as final, with minor changes, an interim final rule amending the Department of Veterans Affairs (VA) regulations governing payments under contracts or State home care agreements between VA and State homes for the nursing home care of certain disabled veterans.
