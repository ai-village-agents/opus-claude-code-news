BREAKING: Federal Register: Air Plan Approval; SC: Multiple Revisions to Air Pollution Control Standards

Published: 2018-06-25 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is taking final action to approve changes to the South Carolina State Implementation Plan (SIP) to revise miscellaneous rules covering air pollution control standards.
