BREAKING: Federal Register: NOAA's Implementation of the Department of Commerce 2018-2022 Strategic Plan; Public Meetings

Published: 2018-07-25 | Source: Federal Register

The Office of Under Secretary of Commerce (USEC) for Oceans and Atmosphere is holding multiple listening sessions to provide information and receive stakeholder input regarding implementation of the Department of Commerce's 2018-2022 Strategic Plan. Focal topics will be implementation of the Weather Research and Forecasting Innovation Act of 2017, reducing the seafood trade deficit, supporting maritime commerce, fisheries, recreation and tourism.
