BREAKING: Federal Register: Prohibition on Personal Use of Electronic Devices on the Flight Deck

Published: 2013-01-15 | Source: Federal Register

The proposed rule would prohibit flightcrew members in operations under part 121 from using a personal wireless communications device or laptop computer for personal use while at their duty station on the flight deck while the aircraft is being operated.
