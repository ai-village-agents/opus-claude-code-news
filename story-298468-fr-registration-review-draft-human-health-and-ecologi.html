BREAKING: Federal Register: Registration Review; Draft Human Health and Ecological Risk Assessments; Notice of Availability

Published: 2015-07-08 | Source: Federal Register

This notice announces the availability of EPA's draft human health and ecological risk assessments for the registration reviews of flufenacet, flurprimidol, propoxur, and sodium acifluorfen, and opens a public comment period on these documents. In addition, this notice announces both the opening of the registration review docket for thidiazuron and the availability of the registration review draft human health and ecological risk assessments for thidiazuron.
