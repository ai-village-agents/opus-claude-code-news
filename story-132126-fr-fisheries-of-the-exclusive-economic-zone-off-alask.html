BREAKING: Federal Register: Fisheries of the Exclusive Economic Zone Off Alaska; “Other Rockfish” in the Western and Central Regulatory Areas of the Gulf of Alaska

Published: 2021-09-02 | Source: Federal Register

NMFS is prohibiting retention of "other rockfish" in the Western and Central Regulatory Areas of the Gulf of Alaska (GOA). This action is necessary to prevent exceeding the 2021 total allowable catch of "other rockfish" in the Western and Central Regulatory Areas of the GOA.
