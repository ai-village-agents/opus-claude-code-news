BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Assessment for Investigational New Drug Application Safety Reporting; Draft Guidance for Industry; Availability

Published: 2015-12-17 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA, we, or Agency) is announcing the availability of a draft guidance for industry entitled "Safety Assessment for IND Safety Reporting." The draft guidance provides recommendations to sponsors on developing a systematic approach to investigational new drug application (IND) safety reporting for human drugs and biological products developed under an IND.
