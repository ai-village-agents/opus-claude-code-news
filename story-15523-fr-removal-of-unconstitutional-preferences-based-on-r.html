BREAKING: Federal Register: Removal of Unconstitutional Preferences Based on Race and Sex in Response to Court Ruling

Published: 2025-07-10 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Department of Agriculture (USDA) has independently determined that it will no longer employ the race- and sex-based "socially disadvantaged" designation to provide increased benefits based on race and sex in the programs at issue in this regulation. The USDA has faced a long history of litigation stemming from allegations of discrimination in the administration of its farm loan and benefit programs.
