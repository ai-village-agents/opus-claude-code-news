BREAKING: Federal Register: Uniform Procedures for State Highway Safety Grant Programs

Published: 2013-01-23 | Source: Federal Register

This action establishes new uniform procedures governing the implementation of State highway safety grant programs as amended by the Moving Ahead for Progress in the 21st Century Act (MAP-21). It also reorganizes and amends existing requirements to implement the provisions of MAP-21.
