BREAKING: Federal Register: Data Standards for Drug and Biological Product Submissions Containing Real-World Data; Guidance for Industry; Availability

Published: 2023-12-22 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA or Agency) is announcing the availability of a final guidance for industry entitled "Data Standards for Drug and Biological Product Submissions Containing Real- World Data."
