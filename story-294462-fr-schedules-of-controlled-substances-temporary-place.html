BREAKING: Federal Register: Schedules of Controlled Substances: Temporary Placement of Acetyl Fentanyl into Schedule I

Published: 2015-05-21 | Source: Federal Register

The Administrator of the Drug Enforcement Administration is issuing this notice of intent to temporarily schedule the synthetic opioid, N-(1-phenethylpiperidin-4-yl)-N-phenylacetamide (acetyl fentanyl), into schedule I pursuant to the temporary scheduling provisions of the Controlled Substances Act.
