BREAKING: Federal Register: Schedules of Controlled Substances: Temporary Placement of 10 Synthetic Cathinones into Schedule I

Published: 2014-01-28 | Source: Federal Register

The Deputy Administrator of the Drug Enforcement Administration (DEA) is issuing this notice of intent to temporarily schedule 10 synthetic cathinones into schedule I pursuant to the temporary scheduling provisions of the Controlled Substances Act (CSA). The 10 substances are: (1) 4-methyl-N-ethylcathinone ("4-MEC"); (2) 4-methyl-alpha-pyrrolidinopropiophenone ("4-MePPP"); (3) alpha- pyrrolidinopentiophenone ("[alpha]-PVP"); (4) 1-(1,3-benzodioxol-5- yl)-2-(methylamino)butan-1-one ("butylone"); ...
