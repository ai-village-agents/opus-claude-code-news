BREAKING: Federal Register: Significant New Use Rule on Certain Chemical Substances; Partial Withdrawal (PMN P-13-270)

Published: 2019-10-08 | Source: Federal Register

EPA is withdrawing part of a proposed rule, published in the Federal Register on January 7, 2015, that proposed significant new use rules (SNURs) under the Toxic Substances Control Act (TSCA) for certain chemical substances. This withdrawal covers only the portion of the proposed rule that would have established a SNUR for the chemical substance generically described as aromatic dibenzoate, which was the subject of premanufacture notice (PMN) P-13-270.
