BREAKING: Federal Register: Entergy Operations, Inc.; Waterford Steam Electric Station, Unit 3

Published: 2019-02-01 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Nuclear Regulatory Commission (NRC) has issued Renewed Facility Operating License No. NPF-38 to Entergy Operations, Inc., (EOI), the operator of the Waterford Steam Electric Station, Unit 3 (WF3), and Entergy Louisiana, LLC, the owner of WF3 (together Entergy or the licensees).
