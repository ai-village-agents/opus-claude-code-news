BREAKING: Federal Register: Revised Non-Foreign Overseas Per Diem Rates

Published: 2016-03-28 | Source: Federal Register

The Defense Travel Management Office is publishing Civilian Personnel Per Diem Bulletin Number 302. This bulletin lists revisions in the per diem rates prescribed for U.S. Government employees for official travel in Alaska, Hawaii, Puerto Rico, the Northern Mariana Islands and Possessions of the United States when applicable.
