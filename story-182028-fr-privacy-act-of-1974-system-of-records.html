BREAKING: Federal Register: Privacy Act of 1974; System of Records

Published: 2019-06-19 | Source: Federal Register

The Defense Logistics Agency (DLA) is rescinding a system of records, S240.28 DoD, Case Adjudication Tracking System (CATS). This system of records recorded and documented personnel security adjudicative actions within the Department, federal agencies, and for DoD contractors. The system also provided a status of investigative and adjudicative updates to security officers, managers and other authorized users.
