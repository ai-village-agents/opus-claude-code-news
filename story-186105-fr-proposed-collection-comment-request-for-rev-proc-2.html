BREAKING: Federal Register: Proposed Collection; Comment Request for Rev. Proc. 2001-29

Published: 2019-08-12 | Source: Federal Register

The Internal Revenue Service (IRS), as part of its continuing effort to reduce paperwork and respondent burden, invites the general public and other Federal agencies to take this opportunity to comment on information collections, as required by the Paperwork Reduction Act of 1995. The IRS is soliciting comments concerning Revenue Procedure 2001-29, Leveraged Leases.
