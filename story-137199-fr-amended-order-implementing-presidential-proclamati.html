BREAKING: Federal Register: Amended Order Implementing Presidential Proclamation on Advancing the Safe Resumption of Global Travel During the COVID-19 Pandemic

Published: 2021-11-05 | Source: Federal Register

On October 25, 2021, the President issued a Proclamation, "Advancing the Safe Resumption of Global Travel During the COVID-19 Pandemic." Pursuant to this Proclamation, the President has implemented a global suspension and restriction on entry for noncitizens who are nonimmigrants seeking to enter the United States by air travel and who are not fully vaccinated against COVID-19.
