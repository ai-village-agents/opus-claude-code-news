BREAKING: Federal Register: Designation of a Class of Employees for Addition to the Special Exposure Cohort

Published: 2012-12-28 | Source: Federal Register

HHS gives notice of a decision to designate a class of employees from Nuclear Metals, Inc. (or a subsequent owner) in West Concord, Massachusetts, as an addition to the Special Exposure Cohort (SEC) under the Energy Employees Occupational Illness Compensation Program Act of 2000.
