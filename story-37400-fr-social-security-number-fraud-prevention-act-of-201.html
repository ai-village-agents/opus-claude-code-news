BREAKING: Federal Register: Social Security Number Fraud Prevention Act of 2017

Published: 2024-05-20 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Energy (DOE or Department) revises its regulations regarding records maintained on individuals under the Privacy Act. The revisions would clarify and update procedural requirements pertaining to the inclusion of a Social Security number (SSN) on documents that the Department sends by mail.
