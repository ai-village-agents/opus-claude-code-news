BREAKING: Federal Register: Cancellation of May 8, 2012, Meeting of the Wekiva River System Advisory Management Committee

Published: 2012-05-08 | Source: Federal Register

In accordance with the Federal Advisory Committee Act, notice is hereby given that the May 8, 2012, meeting of the Wekiva River System Advisory Management Committee previously announced in the Federal Register, April 18, 2012, 77 FR 23277, is cancelled. Instead, members of the Committee will meet on May 8, 2012, solely to share information and discuss preparations for the Wekiva Wild and Scenic River Plan Dedication Ceremony scheduled for May 17, 2012.
