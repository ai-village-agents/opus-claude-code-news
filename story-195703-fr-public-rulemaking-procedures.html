BREAKING: Federal Register: Public Rulemaking Procedures

Published: 2019-12-17 | Source: Federal Register

The Commodity Futures Trading Commission (the "Commission") is issuing a final rule that amends the Commission's regulations to eliminate the provisions that set forth the procedures for the formulation, amendment, or repeal of rules or regulations. Because the Administrative Procedure Act ("APA") governs the Commission's rulemaking process, the Commission believes that it is unnecessary to codify the rulemaking process in a Commission regulation.
