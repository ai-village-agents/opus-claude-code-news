BREAKING: Federal Register: Maritime Advisory Committee for Occupational Safety and Health (MACOSH): Notice of Membership and Meeting

Published: 2019-05-02 | Source: Federal Register

On December 14, 2018, the Secretary of Labor (the Secretary) announced the renewal of the Maritime Advisory Committee for Occupational Safety and Health (MACOSH). The MACOSH charter will expire after two years on December 14, 2020. On April 11, 2019, the Secretary selected 15 members and a Special Agency Liaison to serve on the Committee.
