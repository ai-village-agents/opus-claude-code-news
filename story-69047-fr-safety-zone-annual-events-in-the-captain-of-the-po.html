BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone; Annual Events in the Captain of the Port Buffalo Zone

Published: 2023-06-05 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard will enforce a Safety Zone for the Boldt Castle Independence Day Fireworks on July 4, 2023, to provide for the safety of life on navigable waterways during this event. Our regulation for marine events within the Ninth Coast Guard District identifies the regulated area for this event in Alexandria Bay, NY.
