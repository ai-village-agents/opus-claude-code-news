BREAKING: Federal Register: Hours of Service of Drivers: Application for Exemption; Small Business in Transportation Coalition

Published: 2018-06-05 | Source: Federal Register

FMCSA announces that the Small Business in Transportation Coalition (SBTC) has requested an exemption from the electronic logging device (ELD) requirements for all motor carriers with fewer than 50 employees, including, but not limited to, one-person private and for- hire owner-operators of commercial motor vehicles used in interstate commerce.
