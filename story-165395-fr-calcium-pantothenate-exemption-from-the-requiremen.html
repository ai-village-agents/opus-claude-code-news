BREAKING: Federal Register: Calcium Pantothenate; Exemption From the Requirement of a Tolerance

Published: 2020-11-05 | Source: Federal Register

This regulation establishes an exemption from the requirement of a tolerance for residues of calcium pantothenate (CAS Reg. No. 137- 08-6) when used as an inert ingredient (enzyme cofactor) in pesticide products applied to/on all growing crops pre-harvest, limited to 0.1% (by weight) in pesticide formulations.
