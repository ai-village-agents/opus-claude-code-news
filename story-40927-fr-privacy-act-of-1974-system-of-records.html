BREAKING: Federal Register: Privacy Act of 1974; System of Records

Published: 2024-07-03 | Source: Federal Register

The "Communications Personnel Training Records, State-57" which is being rescinded, contains information which was used to determine current and future training requirements of those individuals who are professional communications personnel and who have been tasked to perform additional back-up communications duties at Foreign Service posts.
