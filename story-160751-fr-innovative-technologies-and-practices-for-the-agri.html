BREAKING: Federal Register: Innovative Technologies and Practices for the Agriculture Innovation Agenda

Published: 2020-09-10 | Source: Federal Register

The United States Department of Agriculture (USDA) is requesting comments and suggestions on the most innovative technologies and practices that can be readily deployed across U.S. agriculture to meet USDA's goal of increasing agricultural production by 40 percent to meet the needs of the global population in 2050 while cutting the environmental footprint of U.S. agriculture in half.
