BREAKING: Federal Register: Women's Preventive Services Guidelines

Published: 2018-02-27 | Source: Federal Register

Applicable as of December 29, 2017, HRSA updated the HRSA- supported Women's Preventive Services Guidelines for purposes of health insurance coverage for preventive services that address health needs specific to women based on clinical recommendations from the Women's Preventive Services Initiative.
