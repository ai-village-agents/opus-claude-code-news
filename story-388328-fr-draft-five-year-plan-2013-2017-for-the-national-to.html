BREAKING: Federal Register: Draft Five-Year Plan (2013-2017) for the National Toxicology Program Interagency Center for the Evaluation of Alternative Toxicological Methods and the Interagency Coordinating Committee on the Validation of Alternative Methods

Published: 2012-06-13 | Source: Federal Register

The National Toxicology Program (NTP) Interagency Center for the Evaluation of Alternative Toxicological Methods (NICEATM) in collaboration with the Interagency Coordinating Committee on the Validation of Alternative Methods (ICCVAM) has developed a draft NICEATM-ICCVAM Five-Year Plan. The plan describes four core strategies to foster and promote development, validation, and regulatory acceptance of scientifically sound alternative test methods by the Federal government and by other governments ...
