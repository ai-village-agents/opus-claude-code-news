BREAKING: Federal Register: Visual-Manual NHTSA Driver Distraction Guidelines for In-Vehicle Electronic Devices

Published: 2012-02-24 | Source: Federal Register

The National Highway Traffic Safety Administration (NHTSA) is concerned about the effects of distraction due to drivers' use of electronic devices on motor vehicle safety. Consequently, NHTSA is issuing nonbinding, voluntary NHTSA Driver Distraction Guidelines (NHTSA Guidelines) to promote safety by discouraging the introduction of excessively distracting devices in vehicles.
