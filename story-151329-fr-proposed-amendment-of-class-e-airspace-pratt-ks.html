BREAKING: Federal Register: Proposed Amendment of Class E Airspace; Pratt, KS

Published: 2020-05-12 | Source: Federal Register

This action amends the Class E airspace extending upward from 700 feet above the surface at Pratt Regional Airport, Pratt, KS. This action is the result of airspace review caused by the decommissioning of the Pratt non-directional beacon (NDB), which provided navigation information for the instrument procedures at this airport.
