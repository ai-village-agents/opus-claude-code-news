BREAKING: Federal Register: Cybersecurity Risk Management for Investment Advisers, Registered Investment Companies, and Business Development Companies

Published: 2022-03-09 | Source: Federal Register

The Securities and Exchange Commission is proposing new rules under the Investment Advisers Act of 1940 ("Advisers Act") and the Investment Company Act of 1940 ("Investment Company Act") to require registered investment advisers ("advisers") and investment companies ("funds") to adopt and implement written cybersecurity policies and procedures reasonably designed to address cybersecurity risks.
