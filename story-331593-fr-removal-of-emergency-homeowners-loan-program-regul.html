BREAKING: Federal Register: Removal of Emergency Homeowners' Loan Program Regulations

Published: 2014-08-07 | Source: Federal Register

Through this rule, HUD removes regulations for the Emergency Homeowners' Loan Program. The statutory authority to provide emergency assistance to homeowners under this program expired on September 30, 2011. Because these regulations are no longer operative, they are being removed by this final rule.
