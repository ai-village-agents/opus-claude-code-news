BREAKING: Federal Register: Fisheries Off West Coast States; the Highly Migratory Species Fishery; Closure

Published: 2015-06-03 | Source: Federal Register

NMFS is prohibiting fishing with large-mesh drift gillnet (DGN) gear (=14 inches mesh) off the coast of southern California east of 120[deg] W. meridian from the effective date of this rule through August 31, 2015. This prohibition is based on the Assistant Administrator for Fisheries' (AA) determination that El Ni[ntilde]o conditions are occurring off the coast of southern California.
