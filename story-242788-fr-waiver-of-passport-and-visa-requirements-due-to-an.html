BREAKING: Federal Register: Waiver of Passport and Visa Requirements Due to an Unforeseen Emergency

Published: 2017-09-05 | Source: Federal Register

This rule adopts as final proposed amendments to the Department of Homeland Security's (DHS) regulations describing the procedures for issuance of a discretionary waiver, on the basis of unforeseen emergency in individual cases, of certain documentary requirements for individuals seeking admission to the United States as a nonimmigrant.
