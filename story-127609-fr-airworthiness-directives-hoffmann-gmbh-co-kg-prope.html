BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Hoffmann GmbH & Co. KG Propellers

Published: 2021-07-06 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA proposes to supersede Airworthiness Directive (AD) 2020-25-05, which applies to all Hoffmann GmbH & Co. KG (Hoffmann) model HO-V 72 propellers. AD 2020-25-05 requires amending the existing aircraft flight manual (AFM) with abnormal propeller vibration instructions.
