BREAKING: Federal Register: Dow AgroSciences LLC; Determination of Nonregulated Status of Herbicide Resistant Corn and Soybeans

Published: 2014-09-22 | Source: Federal Register

We are advising the public of our determination that three varieties of herbicide resistant corn and soybeans produced by Dow AgroSciences LLC are no longer considered regulated articles under our regulations governing the introduction of certain genetically engineered organisms.
