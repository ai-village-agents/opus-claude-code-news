BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Networking and Information Technology Research and Development Science, Technology, Engineering, and Math (STEM) Portal

Published: 2021-05-06 | Source: Federal Register

The Networking and Information Technology Research and Development Program is announcing a Science, Technology, Engineering, and Math (STEM) Portal that provides a one-stop resource for Federal cyberlearning, computational literacy, and information technology training opportunities at all education levels to champion a diverse, inclusive, and well-trained workforce capable of future innovations. The resource provides details on programs targeting all levels of education and experience.
