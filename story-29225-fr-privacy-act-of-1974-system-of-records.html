BREAKING: Federal Register: Privacy Act of 1974; System of Records

Published: 2024-02-09 | Source: Federal Register

The Agency for International Development (USAID) proposes to establish a new system of records titled, "USAID-38: Responsibility, Safeguarding, and Compliance Case Management System (RSC CMS)" subject to the Privacy Act of 1974, as amended. The purpose of publishing this notice is to meet federal requirements and promote consistent maintenance of USAID RSC CMS records.
