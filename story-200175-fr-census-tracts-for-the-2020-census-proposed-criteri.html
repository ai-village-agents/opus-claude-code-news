BREAKING: Federal Register: Census Tracts for the 2020 Census-Proposed Criteria

Published: 2018-02-15 | Source: Federal Register

The Census Bureau is publishing this notice in the Federal Register to request comments from the public and other government agencies on census tracts. The Census Bureau will respond to the comments received as part of the publication of final criteria in the Federal Register.
