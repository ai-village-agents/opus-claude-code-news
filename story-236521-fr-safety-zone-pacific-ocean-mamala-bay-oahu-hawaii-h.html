BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone; Pacific Ocean, Mamala Bay, Oahu, Hawaii-Hokulea Arrival

Published: 2017-06-13 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is establishing a temporary 500 yard safety zone around the sailing canoe HOKULEA for her return to Oahu from a three year worldwide voyage. HOKULEA is a culturally significant double-hulled sailing canoe modeled after the vessels that brought the first Polynesians to Hawaii.
