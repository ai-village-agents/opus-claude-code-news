BREAKING: Federal Register: Clean Air Act Title V Operating Permit Program Revision; West Virginia

Published: 2015-10-05 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is proposing to approve a revision to the West Virginia Title V Operating Permit Program submitted by the State of West Virginia on June 17, 2015. The West Virginia Title V Operating Permit Program is implemented through its "Requirement for Operating Permits" rule, codified at Title 45, Series 30 of the West Virginia Code of State Regulations (45CSR30).
