BREAKING: Federal Register: Revisions to the Federal Seed Act Regulations

Published: 2020-07-07 | Source: Federal Register

This final rule revises the regulations that implement the Federal Seed Act (FSA). Revisions are made to seed labeling, testing, and certification requirements.
