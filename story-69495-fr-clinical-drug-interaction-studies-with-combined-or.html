BREAKING: Federal Register: Clinical Drug Interaction Studies With Combined Oral Contraceptives; Guidance for Industry; Availability

Published: 2023-06-09 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA or Agency) is announcing the availability of a final guidance for industry entitled "Clinical Drug Interaction Studies With Combined Oral Contraceptives."
