BREAKING: Federal Register: Administrative Simplification: Certification of Compliance for Health Plans; Withdrawal

Published: 2017-10-04 | Source: Federal Register

This document withdraws the January 2, 2014, proposed rule that would have required a controlling health plan (CHP) to submit information and documentation demonstrating that it is compliant with certain standards and operating rules adopted by the Secretary of Health and Human Services (the Secretary) under the Health Insurance Portability and Accountability Act of 1996 (HIPAA).
