BREAKING: Federal Register: Proposed Collection; Comment Request; Update and Renewal of the Bank Secrecy Act Designation of Exempt Person Report

Published: 2017-06-13 | Source: Federal Register

FinCEN, a bureau of the U.S. Department of the Treasury ("Treasury"), invites all interested parties to comment on its proposed update and renewal of the collection of information through its "Designation of Exempt Person" ("DoEP") report, used by banks and other depository institutions to designate eligible customers as exempt from the requirement to report transactions in currency over $10,000.
