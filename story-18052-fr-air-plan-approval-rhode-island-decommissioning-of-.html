BREAKING: Federal Register: Air Plan Approval; Rhode Island; Decommissioning of Stage II Vapor Recovery Systems

Published: 2025-08-14 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is proposing to approve a State Implementation Plan (SIP) revision submitted by the State of Rhode Island. This revision removes requirements for Stage II vapor recovery equipment at gasoline dispensing facilities (GDFs). This revision also includes minor updates to Stage I vapor recovery regulatory amendments.
