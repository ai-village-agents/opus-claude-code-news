BREAKING: Federal Register: Approval of Nebraska Air Quality Implementation Plans; Adoption of a New Chapter Under the Nebraska Administrative Code; Withdrawal of Direct Final Rule

Published: 2017-11-27 | Source: Federal Register

Due to adverse comments, the Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is withdrawing the direct final rule for "Approval of Nebraska Air Quality Implementation Plans; Adoption of a New Chapter Under the Nebraska Administrative Code" published in the Federal Register on October 5, 2017. Nebraska's SIP revision added a new chapter titled "Visibility Protection" which provides Nebraska authority to implement Federal regulations relating to Regional Haze and Best Available Retrofit Technology (BART).
