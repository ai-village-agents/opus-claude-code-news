BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Standard for Infant Bath Tubs

Published: 2022-06-24 | Source: Federal Register

In March 2017, the U.S. Consumer Product Safety Commission (CPSC) published a consumer product safety standard for infant bath tubs under section 104 of the Consumer Product Safety Improvement Act of 2008 (CPSIA). The standard incorporated by reference the 2017 ASTM voluntary standard for infant bath tubs that was in effect at the time.
