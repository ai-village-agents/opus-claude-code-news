BREAKING: Federal Register: Migratory Bird Permits; Authorizing the Incidental Take of Migratory Birds

Published: 2021-10-04 | Source: Federal Register

To better protect migratory bird populations and provide more certainty for the regulated public, the Service seeks to address human- caused migratory bird mortality by codifying our interpretation that the Migratory Bird Treaty Act (MBTA) prohibits incidental take of migratory birds and developing regulations that authorize incidental take under prescribed conditions.
