BREAKING: Federal Register: Approval and Promulgation of State Plans for Designated Facilities and Pollutants; Pinal County Air Quality Control District; Control of Emissions From Existing Municipal Solid Waste Landfills

Published: 2019-07-08 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is proposing to approve a state plan submitted by the Pinal County Air Quality Control District (PCAQCD). For the purposes of this plan, the PCAQCD is considered a "State" as defined in the "Standards of Performance for New Stationary Sources". This state plan submittal pertains to the regulation of landfill gas and its components, including methane, from existing municipal solid waste (MSW) landfills.
