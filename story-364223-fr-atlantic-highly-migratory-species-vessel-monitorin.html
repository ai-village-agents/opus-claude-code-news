BREAKING: Federal Register: Atlantic Highly Migratory Species; Vessel Monitoring Systems

Published: 2013-08-29 | Source: Federal Register

NMFS proposes to modify the declaration requirements for vessels required to use Vessel Monitoring System (VMS) units in Atlantic Highly Migratory Species (HMS) fisheries. This proposed rule would require operators of vessels that have been issued HMS permits and are required to use VMS to use their VMS units to provide hourly position reports 24 hours a day, 7 days a week (24/7).
