BREAKING: Federal Register: Veterans Health Administration; Funding Availability Under Supportive Services for Veteran Families Program

Published: 2014-02-03 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Veterans Affairs (VA) published a Notice of Funding Availability in the Federal Register on January 14, 2014, that contained an error. Specifically, the table on page 2539 mistakenly listed one of the priority 1 Continuums of Care (CoCs) as "GA-500 Atlanta/Roswell/DeKalb/Fulton." GA-500 has split into three CoCs: GA- 500--Atlanta, GA-502--Fulton County, and GA-508--DeKalb County.
