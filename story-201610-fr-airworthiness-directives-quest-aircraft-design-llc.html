BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Quest Aircraft Design, LLC Airplanes

Published: 2018-03-08 | Source: Federal Register

We propose to adopt a new airworthiness directive (AD) for all Quest Aircraft Design, LLC Model KODIAK 100 airplanes. This proposed AD was prompted by reports of cracks found in certain nose landing gear forks.
