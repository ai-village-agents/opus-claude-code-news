BREAKING: Federal Register: Privacy Act of 1974; System of Records

Published: 2018-06-22 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of the Army is modifying its system of records notice entitled "Army Career Tracker (ACT), A0350-1b TRADOC". The Army Career Tracker (ACT) enables Soldiers and Army civilians world- wide with career development and transition resources. ACT provides users with a more efficient and effective way to monitor their career development while allowing leaders to track and advise subordinates on personalized leadership development.
