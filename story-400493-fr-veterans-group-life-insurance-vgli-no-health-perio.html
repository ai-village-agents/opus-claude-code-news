BREAKING: Federal Register: Veterans' Group Life Insurance (VGLI) No-Health Period Extension

Published: 2012-11-01 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Veterans Affairs (VA) is issuing this final rule that amends the regulations governing eligibility for Veterans' Group Life Insurance (VGLI) to extend to 240 days the current 120-day "no-health" period during which veterans can apply for VGLI without proving that they are in good health for insurance purposes.
