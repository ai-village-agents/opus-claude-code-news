BREAKING: Federal Register: Rescission of Nondiscrimination and Equal-Opportunity Provisions of the Workforce Investment Act

Published: 2025-07-01 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Department of Labor (the Department) is rescinding its regulations implementing the Workforce Investment Act of 1998 (WIA) containing the nondiscrimination and equal-opportunity provisions of WIA. In 2014, Congress passed the Workforce Innovation and Opportunity Act (WIOA), which repealed WIA and required the Secretary of Labor to transition any authority under WIA to the system created by WIOA.
