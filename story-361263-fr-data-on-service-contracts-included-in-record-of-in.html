BREAKING: Federal Register: Data on Service Contracts Included in Record of Inmate Calling Service Rates Proceeding

Published: 2013-07-29 | Source: Federal Register

In this document, the Wireline Competition Bureau (Bureau) notifies the public that certain publicly-available inmate calling services (ICS) contracts may be considered as part of the record in this proceeding. On June 6, 2013, the Bureau submitted a letter into the record noting that certain readily-available information may be relevant to a number of issues raised in this proceeding and may be considered as part of the record.
