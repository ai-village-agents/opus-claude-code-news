BREAKING: Federal Register: Scope of the Copyright Royalty Judges' Continuing Jurisdiction

Published: 2015-05-04 | Source: Federal Register

The Copyright Royalty Judges ("CRJs"), acting pursuant to statute, referred novel material questions of substantive law to the Register of Copyrights for resolution. Those questions concerned the scope of the CRJs' authority, under the statutory grant of continuing jurisdiction over ratemaking determinations, to issue a clarifying interpretation of regulations adopted pursuant to such a determination.
