BREAKING: Federal Register: Federal Management Regulation (FMR); Mail Management; Requirements for Agencies

Published: 2014-06-11 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. General Services Administration (GSA) is amending the Federal Management Regulation (FMR) by revising its mail management policy. This amendment revises the term "commercial payment process" and removes the requirement that agencies pay the United States Postal Service (USPS) using only commercial payment processes.
