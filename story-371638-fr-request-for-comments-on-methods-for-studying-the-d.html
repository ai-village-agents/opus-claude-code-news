BREAKING: Federal Register: Request for Comments on Methods for Studying the Diversity of Patent Applicants

Published: 2013-12-02 | Source: Federal Register

The United States Patent and Trademark Office ("USPTO" or "the Office") is interested in gathering information on approaches for studying the diversity of patent applicants in accordance with research methodology developed as required by the America Invents Act (AIA or Act).
