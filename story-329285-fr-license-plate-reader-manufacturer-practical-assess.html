BREAKING: Federal Register: License Plate Reader Manufacturer Practical Assessment of Proposed Test Methods

Published: 2014-07-11 | Source: Federal Register

The National Institute of Justice (NIJ) is inviting manufacturers of vehicle-mounted license plate reader (LPR) systems to participate in a practical assessment of the proposed test methods in the tentatively titled Vehicle-mounted License Plate Recognition Systems for Law Enforcement standard under development.
