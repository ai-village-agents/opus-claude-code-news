BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone; Missouri River, Mile Markers 366.3 to 369.8, Kansas City, MO

Published: 2019-04-15 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard proposes to establish a temporary safety zone for the navigable waters of the Missouri River from mile marker (MM) 366.3 to MM 369.8. This action is necessary to provide for the safety of life on these navigable waters near Kansas City, MO, during an airshow from July 4 through July 7, 2019.
