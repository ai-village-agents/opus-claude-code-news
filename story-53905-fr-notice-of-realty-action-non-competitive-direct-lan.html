BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Realty Action: Non-Competitive (Direct) Land Sale in Eddy County, New Mexico

Published: 2024-11-29 | Source: Federal Register

The Bureau of Land Management (BLM) Carlsbad Field Office is proposing a non-competitive direct sale of 199.89 acres of public land in Eddy County, New Mexico, to the county. The purpose of the sale is for the county to build a light industrial, commercial, and retail activity area. The sale would be subject to the applicable provisions of the Federal Land Policy and Management Act of 1976 (FLPMA), as amended, and BLM land sale regulations.
