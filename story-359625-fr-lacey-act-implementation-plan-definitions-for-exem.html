BREAKING: Federal Register: Lacey Act Implementation Plan; Definitions for Exempt and Regulated Articles

Published: 2013-07-09 | Source: Federal Register

In response to recent amendments to the Lacey Act, we are establishing definitions for the terms "common cultivar" and "common food crop" and several related terms. The amendments to the Act expanded its protections to a broader range of plant species, extended its reach to encompass products, including timber, that derive from illegally harvested plants, and require that importers submit a declaration at the time of importation for certain plants and plant products.
