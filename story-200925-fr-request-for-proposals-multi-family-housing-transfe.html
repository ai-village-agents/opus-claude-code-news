BREAKING: Federal Register: Request for Proposals: Multi-Family Housing Transfer and Prepayment Technical Assistance Grants

Published: 2018-02-27 | Source: Federal Register

This Request for Proposal (RFP or Notice) announces an availability of funds and the timeframe to submit proposals for Multi- Family Housing (MFH) Transfer and Prepayment Technical Assistance (TA) grants. Section 771 of the Consolidated Appropriations Act, 2017 appropriated $1,000,000 to provide grants to qualified non-profit organizations and public housing authorities (PHA).
