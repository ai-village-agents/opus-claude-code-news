BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; International Aero Engines Turbofan Engines

Published: 2019-02-08 | Source: Federal Register

We are adopting a new airworthiness directive (AD) for certain International Aero Engines (IAE) PW1100G-JM turbofan engine models with certain low-pressure turbine (LPT) 1st- and 3rd-stage disks installed. This AD was prompted by a report of manufacturing defects found on delivered LPT 1st- and 3rd-stage disks.
