BREAKING: Federal Register: Agency Information Collection Activities; Proposed Collection; Comment Request

Published: 2015-06-16 | Source: Federal Register

The Commission plans to conduct a remedy study to update and expand on the divestiture study it conducted in the mid-1990s to: (1) Assess the effectiveness of the Commission's policies and practices regarding remedial orders where the Commission has permitted a merger but required a divestiture or other remedy, and (2) identify the factors that contributed to the Commission successfully or unsuccessfully achieving the remedial goals of the orders.
