BREAKING: Federal Register: Compliance Guide for Residue Prevention and Agency Testing Policy for Residues

Published: 2012-04-25 | Source: Federal Register

The Food Safety and Inspection Service (FSIS) is announcing the availability of a compliance guide for the prevention of violative residues in livestock slaughter establishments. FSIS has posted this compliance guide on its Web page and it may be used immediately. FSIS also welcomes comments on this compliance guide, which will be revised as needed.
