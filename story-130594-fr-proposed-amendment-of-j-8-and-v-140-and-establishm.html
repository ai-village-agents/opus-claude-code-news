BREAKING: Federal Register: Proposed Amendment of J-8 and V-140, and Establishment of T-422 in the Vicinity of Kingfisher, OK

Published: 2021-08-13 | Source: Federal Register

This action proposes to amend Jet Route J-8 and VHF Omnidirectional Range (VOR) Federal airway V-140, and establish Area Navigation (RNAV) route T-422 in the vicinity of Kingfisher, OK. The Air Traffic Service (ATS) route modifications are necessary due to the planned decommissioning of the VOR portion of the Kingfisher, OK, VOR/ Tactical Air Navigation (VORTAC) navigational aid (NAVAID) which provides navigational guidance for portions of J-8 and V-140.
