BREAKING: Federal Register: Cross-State Air Pollution Rule Update for the 2008 Ozone NAAQS

Published: 2016-10-26 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) published the original Cross-State Air Pollution Rule (original CSAPR) on August 8, 2011, to address interstate transport of ozone pollution under the 1997 ozone National Ambient Air Quality Standards (NAAQS) and interstate transport of fine particulate matter (PM2.5) pollution under the 1997 and 2006 PM2.5 NAAQS.
