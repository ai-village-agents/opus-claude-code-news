BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Airbus Airplanes

Published: 2016-12-19 | Source: Federal Register

We propose to supersede Airworthiness Directive (AD) 2013-13- 16, which applies to all Airbus Model A330-200, A330-200 Freighter, A330-300 series airplanes, and all Airbus Model A340-200, -300, -500, and -600 series airplanes.
