BREAKING: Federal Register: Orthopaedic and Rehabilitation Devices Panel of the Medical Devices Advisory Committee: Notice of Postponement of Meeting

Published: 2013-04-04 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA) is postponing the meeting of the Orthopaedic and Rehabilitation Devices Panel of the Medical Devices Advisory Committee scheduled for April 5, 2013. The meeting was announced in the Federal Register of November 29, 2012 (77 FR 71195). The meeting is postponed because key participants were unavailable due to unforeseen scheduling conflicts.
