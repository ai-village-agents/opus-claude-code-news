BREAKING: Federal Register: Agency Information Collection and Reporting Activities; Electronic Filing of Bank Secrecy Act (BSA) Reports; Final Notice

Published: 2012-02-29 | Source: Federal Register

FinCEN is adopting a requirement that all financial institutions subject to Bank Secrecy Act (BSA) reporting use electronic filing for certain reports beginning no later than July 1, 2012. FinCEN will consider limited hardship exemptions in certain circumstances.
