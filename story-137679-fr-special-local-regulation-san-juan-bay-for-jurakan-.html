BREAKING: Federal Register: Special Local Regulation; San Juan Bay for Jurakan Triathlon, San Juan, PR

Published: 2021-11-12 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is establishing a temporary special local regulation for navigable waters around the Isleta San Juan for the Jurakan triathlon event, San Juan, PR. The event includes a paddleboard competition around Isleta San Juan with approximately 30 competitive stand up paddleboard (SUP) participants.
