BREAKING: Federal Register: Pasta From Turkey: Final Results of Countervailing Duty Administrative Review; 2014

Published: 2016-12-15 | Source: Federal Register

On August 10, 2016, the Department of Commerce (the Department) published the preliminary results of the administrative review of the countervailing duty order on pasta from Turkey. The period of review (POR) is January 1, 2014, through December 31, 2014. The review covers one producer/exporter of subject merchandise: Bessan Makarna Gida San. Ve Tic. A.[Scedil]. (Bessan).
