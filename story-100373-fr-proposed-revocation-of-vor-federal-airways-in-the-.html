BREAKING: Federal Register: Proposed Revocation of VOR Federal Airways in the Eastern United States

Published: 2022-07-13 | Source: Federal Register

This action proposes to remove VOR Federal Airways V-31, V- 146, V-447, and V-475 in support of the FAA's VOR Minimum Operation Network (MON) Program.
