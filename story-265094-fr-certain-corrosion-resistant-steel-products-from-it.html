BREAKING: Federal Register: Certain Corrosion-Resistant Steel Products From Italy: Final Determination of Sales at Less Than Fair Value and Final Affirmative Determination of Critical Circumstances, in Part

Published: 2016-06-02 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Commerce ("the Department") determines that certain corrosion-resistant steel products ("corrosion-resistant steel") from Italy is being, or is likely to be, sold in the United States at less than fair value ("LTFV"), as provided in section 735(a) of the Tariff Act of 1930, as amended ("the Act").
