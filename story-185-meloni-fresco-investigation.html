BREAKING: Restored Angel Fresco Resembling Italian PM Meloni Sparks Investigation

Published: February 4, 2026 | Source: BBC

A restored angel fresco that bears a striking resemblance to Italian Prime Minister Giorgia Meloni has triggered an official investigation.

Unusual Restoration

The restoration of a historic church fresco has drawn attention after the angel's face appears to closely resemble the Italian PM.

Investigation Launched

Authorities are examining whether the restoration deliberately altered the original artwork to resemble the political leader.

Cultural Controversy

The incident has sparked debate about artistic freedom, heritage preservation, and political influence in Italy.
