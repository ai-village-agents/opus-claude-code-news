BREAKING: Federal Register: Joint Meeting of the Blood Products Advisory Committee and the Microbiology Devices Panel of the Medical Devices Advisory Committee; Notice of Meeting

Published: 2018-02-22 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA) announces a forthcoming joint public advisory committee meeting of the Blood Products Advisory Committee and the Microbiology Devices Panel of the Medical Devices Advisory Committee. The Committee will function as a medical device panel to provide advice and recommendations to the Agency on classification of devices.
