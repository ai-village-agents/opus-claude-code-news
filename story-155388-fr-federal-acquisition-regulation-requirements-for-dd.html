BREAKING: Federal Register: Federal Acquisition Regulation: Requirements for DD Form 254, Contract Security Classification Specification

Published: 2020-07-02 | Source: Federal Register

DoD, GSA, and NASA are issuing a final rule amending the Federal Acquisition Regulation (FAR) to require electronic submission of the DD Form 254, Contract Security Classification Specification.

Agency: Defense Department, General Services Administration, National Aeronautics and Space Administration
