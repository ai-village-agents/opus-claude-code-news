BREAKING: Federal Register: Olives Grown in California; Suspension and Revision of Incoming Size-Grade Requirements

Published: 2016-07-18 | Source: Federal Register

This rule implements a recommendation from the California Olive Committee (Committee) to suspend the incoming size-grade authority under the California olive marketing order (order), which regulates the handling of olives in California. The rule also makes conforming changes to the corresponding size-grade requirements in the order's rules and regulations to adapt them to the suspension.
