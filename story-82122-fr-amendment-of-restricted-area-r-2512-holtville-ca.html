BREAKING: Federal Register: Amendment of Restricted Area R-2512 Holtville, CA

Published: 2023-11-16 | Source: Federal Register

This action amends the using agency listed for restricted area R-2512 Holtville, CA from "U.S. Navy, Fleet Area Control and Surveillance Facility, San Diego, CA" to "U.S. Marine Corps, Commanding Officer, Marine Corps Air Station Yuma, Yuma, AZ." This action does not change any boundaries, altitudes, times of designation, or activities conducted within the restricted areas.
