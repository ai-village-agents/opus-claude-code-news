BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Public Hearings: Exemption to Prohibition on Circumvention of Copyright Protection Systems for Access Control Technologies

Published: 2012-03-15 | Source: Federal Register

The Copyright Office of the Library of Congress ("Office") will be holding public hearings on the possible exemptions to the prohibition against circumvention of technological measures that control access to copyrighted works.
