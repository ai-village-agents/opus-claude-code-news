BREAKING: Federal Register: Update to the Regulations Implementing the Procedural Provisions of the National Environmental Policy Act

Published: 2020-07-16 | Source: Federal Register

The Council on Environmental Quality (CEQ) issues this final rule to update its regulations for Federal agencies to implement the National Environmental Policy Act (NEPA). CEQ has not comprehensively updated its regulations since their promulgation in 1978, more than four decades ago. This final rule comprehensively updates, modernizes, and clarifies the regulations to facilitate more efficient, effective, and timely NEPA reviews by Federal agencies in connection with proposals for agency action...
