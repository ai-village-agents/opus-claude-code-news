BREAKING: Federal Register: Quadrennial Technology Review Framing Document

Published: 2015-01-21 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Department of Energy (DOE or Department) has initiated the second Quadrennial Technology Review (QTR). The DOE-QTR- 2015 Framing Document has been developed as a principle means of facilitating stakeholder engagement in the QTR process.
