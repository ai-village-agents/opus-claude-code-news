BREAKING: Federal Register: Special Local Regulation; Annual Les Cheneaux Islands Antique Wooden Boat Show, Hessel, MI

Published: 2021-05-04 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard will enforce the Annual Les Cheneaux Island Antique Wooden Boat Show special local regulation on the U.S. navigable waters of Hessel Marina, Hessel, MI on August 14, 2021. This action is necessary and intended to protect the safety of life and property on navigable waters prior to, during, and immediately after the boat show.
