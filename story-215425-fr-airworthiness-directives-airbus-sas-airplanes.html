BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Airbus SAS Airplanes

Published: 2018-08-31 | Source: Federal Register

We propose to adopt a new airworthiness directive (AD) for certain Airbus SAS Model A330-200, -200F, and -300 series airplanes. This proposed AD was prompted by a revision of the airworthiness limitations section (ALS), which provides new and more restrictive maintenance requirements and airworthiness limitations for airplane structures and systems.
