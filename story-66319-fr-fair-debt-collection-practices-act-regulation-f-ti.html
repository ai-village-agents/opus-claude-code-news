BREAKING: Federal Register: Fair Debt Collection Practices Act (Regulation F); Time-Barred Debt

Published: 2023-05-01 | Source: Federal Register

The Consumer Financial Protection Bureau (CFPB) is issuing this advisory opinion to affirm that the Fair Debt Collection Practices Act (FDCPA) and its implementing Regulation F prohibit a debt collector, as that term is defined in the statute and regulation, from suing or threatening to sue to collect a time-barred debt.
