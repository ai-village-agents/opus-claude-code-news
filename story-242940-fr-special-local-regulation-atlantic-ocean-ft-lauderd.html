BREAKING: Federal Register: Special Local Regulation; Atlantic Ocean, Ft. Lauderdale, FL

Published: 2017-09-06 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard proposes to establish a recurring special local regulation for navigable waters of the Atlantic Ocean in the vicinity of the Fort Lauderdale for the Grand Prix of the Seas. The Fort Lauderdale Grand Prix of the Seas race course is located east of South Beach Park and North of the Port Everglades inlet.
