BREAKING: Federal Register: Assistance to Firefighters Grant Program-COVID-19 Supplemental Round 2 (AFG-S 2)

Published: 2020-11-17 | Source: Federal Register

On March 27, 2020, the President signed into law the Coronavirus Aid, Relief, and Economic Security Act ("CARES Act"). This bill provides $100 million for AFG-S to prevent, prepare for, and respond to coronavirus, domestically or internationally. Specifically, AFG-S funds shall be for the purchase of Personal Protective Equipment (PPE) and related supplies, including reimbursements for previously purchased PPE back to January 1, 2020.
