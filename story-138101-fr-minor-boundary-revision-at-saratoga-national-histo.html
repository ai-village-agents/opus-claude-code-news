BREAKING: Federal Register: Minor Boundary Revision at Saratoga National Historical Park

Published: 2021-11-17 | Source: Federal Register

The boundary of Saratoga National Historical Park is modified to include four parcels of land totaling approximately 28.45 acres of land located in Saratoga County, New York, immediately adjoining the boundaries of Saratoga National Historical Park.
