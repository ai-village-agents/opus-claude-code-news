BREAKING: Federal Register: Air Plan Approval; Missouri; General Conformity Rescission

Published: 2022-08-16 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is taking final action to approve a revision to the Missouri State Implementation Plan (SIP). This final action will rescind the Missouri General Conformity Rule from the Missouri SIP. General Conformity ensures federal agency actions, such as airport construction, do not interfere with a state's plans to attain and maintain national standards for air quality.
