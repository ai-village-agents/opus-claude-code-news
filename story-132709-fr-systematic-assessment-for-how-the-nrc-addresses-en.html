BREAKING: Federal Register: Systematic Assessment for How the NRC Addresses Environmental Justice in Its Programs, Policies, and Activities

Published: 2021-09-10 | Source: Federal Register

On July 9, 2021, the U.S. Nuclear Regulatory Commission (NRC) requested comments as part of its systematic review for how NRC programs, policies, and activities address environmental justice. Specifically, the NRC requested input on how the agency is addressing environmental justice, considering the agency's mission and statutory authority.
