BREAKING: Federal Register: United States Standards for Grades of Processed Vegetables

Published: 2019-04-19 | Source: Federal Register

The Agricultural Marketing Service (AMS) of the Department of Agriculture (USDA) is revising the U.S. Standards for Grades of Canned Lima Beans, U.S. Standards for Grades of Canned Mushrooms, U.S. Standards for Grades of Pickles, and U.S. Standards for Grades of Green Olives. AMS is replacing the term "midget" with "petite" in the canned lima bean, canned mushroom, and pickle standards, and removing "midget" completely from the green olive standards as there is an alternative term.
