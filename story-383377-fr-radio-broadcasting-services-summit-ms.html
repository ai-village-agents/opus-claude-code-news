BREAKING: Federal Register: Radio Broadcasting Services; Summit, MS

Published: 2012-04-18 | Source: Federal Register

This document sets forth a proposal to amend the FM Table of Allotments. The Commission requests comment on a petition filed by Bowen Broadcasting, proposing to amend the Table of Allotments by allotting FM Channel 228A at Summit, Mississippi, as that community's first local broadcast service.
