BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone; Skagit River Bridge, Skagit River, Mount Vernon, WA

Published: 2013-07-11 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is establishing a safety zone around the Skagit River Bridge located in Mount Vernon, WA. This action is necessary to protect vessels and persons from dangers associated with the collapse of the Interstate 5 Skagit River Bridge and to ensure the safety of the emergency response, salvage, and construction crews on scene.
