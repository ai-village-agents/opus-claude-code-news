BREAKING: Federal Register: Revised Exhibit Submission Requirements for Commission Hearings

Published: 2015-03-25 | Source: Federal Register

The Commission proposes to amend Rule 508 of the Commission's Rules of Practice and Procedure to eliminate the requirement that participants in Commission trial-type evidentiary hearings must provide paper copies of all exhibits introduced as evidence.
