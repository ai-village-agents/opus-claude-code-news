BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Airbus SAS Airplanes

Published: 2022-05-31 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA proposes to supersede Airworthiness Directive (AD) 2017-19-13, AD 2018-24-04, and AD 2019-23-02, which apply to certain Airbus SAS Model A330-200 series, A330-200 Freighter series, and A330- 300 series airplanes. ADs 2017-19-13, 2018-24-04, and 2019-23-02 require revising the existing maintenance or inspection program, as applicable, to incorporate new or more restrictive airworthiness limitations.
