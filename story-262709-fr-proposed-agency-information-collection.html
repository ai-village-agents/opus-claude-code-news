BREAKING: Federal Register: Proposed Agency Information Collection

Published: 2016-05-05 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Energy (DOE) has submitted to the Office of Management and Budget (OMB) for clearance, a proposal for collection of information under the provisions of the Paperwork Reduction Act of 1995. The proposed collection will be used to generate an annual Energy and Jobs Report.
