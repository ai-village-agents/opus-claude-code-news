BREAKING: Federal Register: Applications for New Awards; Comprehensive Centers Program (CFDA 84.283B); Correction

Published: 2012-08-15 | Source: Federal Register

The 2012 notice erroneously listed deadline dates for intergovernmental review under Executive Order (EO) 12372 and its implementing regulations in 34 CFR part 79.
