BREAKING: Federal Register: Announcement of New Cushion of Credit Program Provisions

Published: 2019-06-07 | Source: Federal Register

The Rural Utilities Service (RUS), a Rural Development agency of the United States Department of Agriculture, is issuing this notice regarding the new provisions affecting RUS borrower participation in the Cushion of Credit (CoC) program pursuant to the Agriculture Improvement Act of 2018, (the 2018 Farm Bill).
