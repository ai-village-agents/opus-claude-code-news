BREAKING: Federal Register: Fisheries Off West Coast States; West Coast Groundfish Electronic Monitoring Program; Service Provider Revisions

Published: 2023-11-22 | Source: Federal Register

This final rule revises regulations governing the use of electronic monitoring (EM) in the Pacific Coast groundfish trawl fishery. This rulemaking modifies deadlines in Federal regulations for EM service providers to process EM data and submit vessel logbooks to NMFS.
