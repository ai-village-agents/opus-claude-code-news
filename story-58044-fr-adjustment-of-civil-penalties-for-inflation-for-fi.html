BREAKING: Federal Register: Adjustment of Civil Penalties for Inflation for Fiscal Year 2023

Published: 2023-01-13 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Nuclear Regulatory Commission (NRC) is amending its regulations to adjust the maximum civil monetary penalties it can assess under statutes enforced by the agency. These changes are mandated by the Federal Civil Penalties Inflation Adjustment Act of 1990, as amended by the Federal Civil Penalties Inflation Adjustment Act Improvements Act of 2015.
