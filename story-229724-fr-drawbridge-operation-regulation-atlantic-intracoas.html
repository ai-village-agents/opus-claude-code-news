BREAKING: Federal Register: Drawbridge Operation Regulation; Atlantic Intracoastal Waterway, St. Augustine, FL

Published: 2017-03-15 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is seeking comments and information concerning a proposal to change the operating schedule for the Bridge of Lions across the Atlantic Intracoastal Waterway, St. Augustine, Florida. The City of St. Augustine is concerned that vehicle traffic is becoming exponentially worse with each passing season and that on-demand bridge openings are contributing to vehicle traffic backups.
