BREAKING: Federal Register: Food and Drug Administration/American Glaucoma Society Workshop on the Validity, Reliability, and Usability of Glaucoma Imaging Devices

Published: 2012-09-13 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA) is announcing a public workshop entitled "FDA/American Glaucoma Society (AGS) Workshop on the Validity, Reliability, and Usability of Glaucoma Imaging Devices." FDA is co-sponsoring the workshop together with the AGS, a nonprofit organization that supports glaucoma specialists and scientists through the advancement of education and research.
