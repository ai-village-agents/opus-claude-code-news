BREAKING: Federal Register: Medical Malpractice Claims by Members of the Uniformed Services

Published: 2024-05-10 | Source: Federal Register

The DoD is finalizing amendments to apply offsets for payments made by the U.S. Government for medical malpractice claims to potential economic damages only and not to total potential damages. Under this rule total potential damages will no longer be reduced by offsetting most of the compensation otherwise provided or expected to be provided by DoD or the Department of Veterans Affairs (VA) for the same harm that is the subject of the medical malpractice claim.
