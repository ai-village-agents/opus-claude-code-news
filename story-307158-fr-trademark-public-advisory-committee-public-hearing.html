BREAKING: Federal Register: Trademark Public Advisory Committee Public Hearing on the Proposed Trademark Fee Schedule

Published: 2015-10-20 | Source: Federal Register

Under Section 10 of the America Invents Act (AIA), the United States Patent and Trademark Office (USPTO) may set or adjust by rule any patent or trademark fee established, authorized, or charged under Title 35 of the United States Code or the Trademark Act of 1946, respectively. The USPTO currently is planning to set or adjust trademark fees pursuant to its Section 10 fee setting authority.
