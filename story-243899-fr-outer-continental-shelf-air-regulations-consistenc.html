BREAKING: Federal Register: Outer Continental Shelf Air Regulations; Consistency Update for California

Published: 2017-09-18 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is finalizing the updates of the Outer Continental Shelf ("OCS") Air Regulations proposed in the Federal Register on June 17, 2016 and December 12, 2016. Requirements applying to OCS sources located within 25 miles of States' seaward boundaries must be updated periodically to remain consistent with the requirements of the corresponding onshore area ("COA"), as mandated by section 328(a)(1) of the Clean Air Act, as amended in 1990 ("the Act").
