BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Inventory Completion: University of Alabama Museums, Tuscaloosa, AL

Published: 2022-08-30 | Source: Federal Register

The University of Alabama Museums has completed an inventory of human remains and associated funerary objects from the Moundville archeological site (1Tu500) in Hale County, AL, as well as adjacent archeological sites in Hale and Tuscaloosa Counties, AL.
