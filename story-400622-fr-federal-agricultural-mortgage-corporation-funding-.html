BREAKING: Federal Register: Federal Agricultural Mortgage Corporation Funding and Fiscal Affairs; Farmer Mac Investment Management

Published: 2012-11-05 | Source: Federal Register

The Farm Credit Administration (FCA, Agency, us, or we) issues this final rule amending our regulations governing investment management practices of the Federal Agricultural Mortgage Corporation (Farmer Mac or Corporation). This final rule will help ensure that Farmer Mac maintains safe and sound non-program investment management practices in accordance with clearly articulated board-established guidance, streamlines the process for handling investments that fail to meet the eligibility criteria...
