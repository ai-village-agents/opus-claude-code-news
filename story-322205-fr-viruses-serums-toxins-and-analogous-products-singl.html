BREAKING: Federal Register: Viruses, Serums, Toxins, and Analogous Products; Single Label Claim for Veterinary Biological Products

Published: 2014-04-21 | Source: Federal Register

We are proposing to amend the Virus-Serum-Toxin Act regulations to provide for the use of a simpler labeling format that would better communicate product performance to the user. We intend to replace the current label format, which reflects any of four different levels of effectiveness, with a single, uniform label format.
