BREAKING: Federal Register: Certain Quartz Surface Products From the People's Republic of China: Expansion of the Period of Review and Supplemental Opportunity To Request Administrative Review; Correction

Published: 2024-03-12 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Department of Commerce (Commerce) published notice in the Federal Register of February 26, 2024, in which Commerce expanded the period of review (POR) for the current antidumping duty (AD) and countervailing duty (CVD) administrative reviews of certain quartz surface products (quartz surface products) from the People's Republic of China (China) to include entries suspended by the final scope ruling on Malaysian processed quartz slab prior to the current POR of the instant reviews.
