BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of HUD Vacant Loan Sales (HVLS 2017-2)

Published: 2017-06-08 | Source: Federal Register

This notice announces HUD's intention to competitively offer multiple residential reverse mortgage pools consisting of approximately 885 reverse mortgage notes secured by properties with an aggregate broker price opinion of approximately $120 million. The sale will consist of due and payable Secretary-held reverse mortgage loans.
