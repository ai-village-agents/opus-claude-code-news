BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Advisory 24-1 Proterra Bus/Phoenix Motorcars Technical Service Bulletin

Published: 2024-10-16 | Source: Federal Register

The Federal Transit Administration (FTA) is issuing Safety Advisory 24-1 to alert transit agencies that Phoenix Motorcars (Phoenix) is voluntarily recalling certain 2020-2021 800V Catalyst and 2020-2022 ZX5 transit buses. FTA is recommending corrective actions to avoid and mitigate the risk to public transportation posed by the safety defect.
