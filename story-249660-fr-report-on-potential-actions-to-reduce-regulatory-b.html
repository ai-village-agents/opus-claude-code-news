BREAKING: Federal Register: Report on Potential Actions To Reduce Regulatory Burdens on Domestic Energy Production

Published: 2017-11-28 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Army Corps of Engineers (Corps) has issued a report that examined actions it could take to modify existing regulations that potentially burden the development and use of domestically produced energy resources, such as oil, natural gas, coal, and nuclear energy, as well as renewable energy. The report was required by Executive Order 13783, Promoting Energy Independence and Economic Growth.
