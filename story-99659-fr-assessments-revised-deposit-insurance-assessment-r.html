BREAKING: Federal Register: Assessments, Revised Deposit Insurance Assessment Rates

Published: 2022-07-01 | Source: Federal Register

The FDIC is seeking comment on a proposed rule that would increase initial base deposit insurance assessment rates by 2 basis points, beginning with the first quarterly assessment period of 2023.
