BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Standard for Button Cell or Coin Batteries and Consumer Products Containing Such Batteries

Published: 2023-09-21 | Source: Federal Register

In February 2023, as required by Reese's Law, the U.S. Consumer Product Safety Commission (CPSC or Commission) issued a notice of proposed rulemaking (NPR) to eliminate or adequately reduce the risk of injury from ingestion of button cell or coin batteries by children six years old and younger. In the NPR the Commission preliminarily determined that no existing voluntary standard met the requirements in Reese's Law at that time.
