BREAKING: Federal Register: Extension of Import Restrictions on Archaeological Material of Chile

Published: 2025-10-02 | Source: Federal Register

This document amends the U.S. Customs and Border Protection (CBP) regulations to extend import restrictions on certain archaeological material from the Republic of Chile. The Acting Assistant Secretary for Educational and Cultural Affairs, United States Department of State, has made the requisite determinations for extending the import restrictions, which were originally imposed by CBP Decision 20-16.
