BREAKING: Federal Register: Energy Conservation Program: Test Procedures for Conventional Ovens

Published: 2015-07-02 | Source: Federal Register

On December 3, 2014, the U.S. Department of Energy (DOE) issued a supplemental notice of proposed rulemaking (SNOPR) to amend the test procedures for conventional cooking products. The oven-related procedures proposed in that rulemaking serve as the basis for this final rule.
