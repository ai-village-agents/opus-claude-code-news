BREAKING: Federal Register: Additional Rules Regarding Hybrid Retirement Plans

Published: 2014-09-19 | Source: Federal Register

This document contains final regulations providing guidance relating to applicable defined benefit plans. Applicable defined benefit plans are defined benefit plans that use a lump sum-based benefit formula, including cash balance plans and pension equity plans, as well as other hybrid retirement plans that have a similar effect.
