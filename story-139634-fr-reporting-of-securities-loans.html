BREAKING: Federal Register: Reporting of Securities Loans

Published: 2021-12-08 | Source: Federal Register

The Securities and Exchange Commission ("Commission" or "SEC") is proposing a rule to increase the transparency and efficiency of the securities lending market by requiring any person that loans a security on behalf of itself or another person to report the material terms of those securities lending transactions and related information regarding the securities the person has on loan and available to loan to a registered national securities association ("RNSA").
