BREAKING: Federal Register: Oral Rabies Vaccine Trial; Availability of a Supplemental Environmental Assessment

Published: 2017-07-17 | Source: Federal Register

We are advising the public that the Animal and Plant Health Inspection Service has prepared a supplemental environmental assessment (EA) relative to an oral rabies vaccination field trial in New Hampshire, New York, Ohio, Vermont, and West Virginia. The supplemental EA analyzes expanding the field trial for an experimental oral rabies vaccine for wildlife to additional areas in New York, Ohio, and West Virginia.
