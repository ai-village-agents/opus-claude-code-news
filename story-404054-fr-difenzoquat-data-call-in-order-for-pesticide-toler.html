BREAKING: Federal Register: Difenzoquat; Data Call-in Order for Pesticide Tolerances

Published: 2012-12-19 | Source: Federal Register

This order requires the submission of various data to support the continuation of the tolerances for the pesticide, difenzoquat. Pesticide tolerances are established under the Federal Food, Drug, and Cosmetic Act (FFDCA). Following publication of this order, persons who are interested in the continuation of the difenzoquat tolerances must notify the Agency by completing and submitting the required section 408(f) Order Response Form (available in the docket) within 90 days.
