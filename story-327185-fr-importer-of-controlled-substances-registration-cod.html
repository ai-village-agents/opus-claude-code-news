BREAKING: Federal Register: Importer of Controlled Substances Registration: CODY LABORATORIES, INC.

Published: 2014-06-17 | Source: Federal Register

Cody Laboratories, Inc. applied to be registered as an importer of certain basic classes of narcotic or non-narcotic controlled substances. The DEA grants Cody Laboratories, Inc. registration as an importer of those controlled substances.
