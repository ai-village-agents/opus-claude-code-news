BREAKING: Federal Register: Procedural Regulations for the Copyright Royalty Board Regarding Electronic Filing of Claims; Corrections

Published: 2022-06-14 | Source: Federal Register

On July 8, 2019, the Copyright Royalty Judges revised their regulations to move several sections from one part to a new part. At the time, the Judges inadvertently failed to revise other regulations that include cross-references to the old part. They are now revising regulations regarding the filing of claims to reflect the change.
