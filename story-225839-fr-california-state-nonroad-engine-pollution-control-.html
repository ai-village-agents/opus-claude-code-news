BREAKING: Federal Register: California State Nonroad Engine Pollution Control Standards; Evaporative Emission Standards and Test Procedures for Off-Highway Recreational Vehicles (OHRVs); Notice of Decision

Published: 2017-01-19 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency ("EPA") is granting the California Air Resources Board ("CARB") its request for an authorization of its amendments to its Off-Highway Recreational Vehicle regulation ("OHRV Amendments"). The OHRV Amendments establish new evaporative emission standards and test procedures for 2018 and subsequent model year OHRVs.
