BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Final Agency Actions on Proposed Railroad Project in California on Behalf of the California High Speed Rail Authority

Published: 2022-08-22 | Source: Federal Register

FRA, on behalf of the California High-Speed Rail Authority (Authority), is issuing this notice to announce actions taken by the Authority that are final. By this notice, FRA is advising the public of the time limit to file a claim seeking judicial review of the actions. The actions relate to the Stockton Diamond Grade Separation Project (Project).
