BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone; Patapsco River, Northwest and Inner Harbors; Baltimore, MD

Published: 2013-09-16 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is establishing a temporary safety zone upon certain waters of the Patapsco River, Northwest Harbor and Inner Harbor during the movement of the historic sloop-of-war USS CONSTELLATION on September 26, 2013. If necessary, due to inclement weather, the event will be rescheduled for October 3, 2013.
