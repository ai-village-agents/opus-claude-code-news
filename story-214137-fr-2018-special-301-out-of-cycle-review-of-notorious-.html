BREAKING: Federal Register: 2018 Special 301 Out-of-Cycle Review of Notorious Markets: Comment Request

Published: 2018-08-16 | Source: Federal Register

The Office of the United States Trade Representative (USTR) requests written comments that identify online and physical markets based outside the United States that should be included in the 2018 Notorious Markets List (List). Conducted under the auspices of the Special 301 program, the List identifies online and physical marketplaces that reportedly engage in and facilitate substantial copyright piracy and trademark counterfeiting.
