BREAKING: Federal Register: Xanthan Gum From the People's Republic of China: Final Results of Antidumping Duty Administrative Review; 2013-2014

Published: 2017-02-23 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Commerce ("the Department") is conducting an administrative review of the antidumping duty order on xanthan gum from the People's Republic of China ("PRC"). For these final results, we have treated Neimenggu Fufeng Biotechnologies Co., Ltd. (aka Inner Mongolia Fufeng Biotechnologies Co., Ltd.)/Shandong Fufeng Fermentation Co., Ltd./Xinjiang Fufeng Biotechnologies Co., Ltd. (collectively, "Fufeng"), Deosen Biochemical Ltd./Deosen Biochemical (Ordos) Ltd. (collectively, "Deosen")...
