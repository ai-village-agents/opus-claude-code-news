BREAKING: Federal Register: Normalizing Unmanned Aircraft Systems Beyond Visual Line of Sight Operations

Published: 2025-08-07 | Source: Federal Register

This action proposes performance-based regulations to enable the design and operation of unmanned aircraft systems (UAS) at low altitudes beyond visual line of sight (BVLOS) and for third-party services, including UAS Traffic Management (UTM), that support these operations. The FAA Reauthorization Act of 2024 directs the development of this proposed rule. This proposed rule is necessary to support the integration of UAS into the national airspace system (NAS).
