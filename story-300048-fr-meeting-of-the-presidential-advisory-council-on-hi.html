BREAKING: Federal Register: Meeting of the Presidential Advisory Council on HIV/AIDS

Published: 2015-07-28 | Source: Federal Register

As stipulated by the Federal Advisory Committee Act, the U.S. Department of Health and Human Service is hereby giving notice that the Presidential Advisory Council on HIV/AIDS (PACHA) will be holding a meeting to continue discussions and possibly develop recommendations regarding People Living with HIV/AIDS.
