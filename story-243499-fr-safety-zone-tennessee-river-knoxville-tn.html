BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone; Tennessee River, Knoxville, TN

Published: 2017-09-13 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is establishing a temporary safety zone for all navigable waters of the Tennessee River extending from mile marker (MM) 646.8 to MM 647.0. This action is necessary to provide for the safety of life on the navigable waters near Knoxville, TN, during the University of Tennessee Football Season recurring fireworks displays.
