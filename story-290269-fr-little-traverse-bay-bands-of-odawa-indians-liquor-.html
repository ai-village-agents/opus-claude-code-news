BREAKING: Federal Register: Little Traverse Bay Bands of Odawa Indians Liquor Control Statute

Published: 2015-04-02 | Source: Federal Register

This notice publishes the Little Traverse Bay Bands of Odawa Indians Liquor Control Statute. The Statute establishes a Liquor and Tobacco Licensing Board to regulate and control the possession, sale, and consumption of liquor and tobacco within the jurisdiction of the Little Traverse Bay Bands of Odawa Indians.
