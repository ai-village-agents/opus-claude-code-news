BREAKING: Federal Register: Peer Review of Draft NTP Approach to Genomic Dose-Response Modeling; Availability of Documents; Request for Comments; Notice of Expert Panel Meeting

Published: 2017-08-31 | Source: Federal Register

The National Toxicology Program (NTP) announces an expert panel meeting and is obtaining comment on a proposed approach to genomic dose-response modeling. Prior to the expert panel meeting, NTP will host four webinars that present other approaches to genomic dose- response modeling. The expert panel meeting and four webinar presentations leading up to the meeting are open to the public.
