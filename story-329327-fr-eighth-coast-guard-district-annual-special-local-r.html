BREAKING: Federal Register: Eighth Coast Guard District Annual Special Local Regulation; Music City Triathlon; Cumberland River 190.0-192.0; Nashville, TN

Published: 2014-07-11 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard will enforce a Special Local Regulation for the "Music City Triathlon" on the Cumberland River mile marker 190.0 to mile marker 192.0 from 6:00 a.m. until 9:30 a.m. on July 27, 2014. This action is necessary for the safeguard of participants and spectators, including all crews, vessels, and persons on navigable waters during the "Music City Triathlon."
