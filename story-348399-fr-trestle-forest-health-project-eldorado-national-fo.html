BREAKING: Federal Register: Trestle Forest Health Project, Eldorado National Forest, El Dorado County, CA

Published: 2013-03-04 | Source: Federal Register

The USDA Forest Service, Eldorado National Forest will prepare an Environmental Impact Statement (EIS) for a proposal to modify vegetation on approximately 7,000 acres of National Forest System land.
