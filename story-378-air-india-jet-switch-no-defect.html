BREAKING: No Defect Found in Switch of Jet Grounded by Air India - Regulator

Published: February 4, 2026 | Source: BBC

Aviation regulators have found no defect in the switch of a jet that was grounded by Air India, according to an official investigation.

Investigation Results

Regulators concluded their examination found no defects in the aircraft component.

Grounding Decision

Air India had grounded the jet over concerns about the switch's functionality.

Safety Clearance

The findings could allow the aircraft to return to service.
