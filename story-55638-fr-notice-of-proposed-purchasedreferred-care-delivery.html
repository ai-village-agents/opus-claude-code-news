BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Proposed Purchased/Referred Care Delivery Area Redesignation for the Chippewa Cree Tribe of the Rocky Boy's Reservation

Published: 2024-12-17 | Source: Federal Register

This Notice advises the public that the Indian Health Service (IHS) proposes to expand the geographic boundaries of the Purchased/ Referred Care Delivery Area (PRCDA) for the Chippewa Cree Tribe of the Rocky Boy's Reservation ("Chippewa Cree Tribe" or "Tribe") to include the Montana county of Cascade. The current PRCDA for the Chippewa Cree Tribe includes the Montana counties of Choteau, Hill and Liberty.
