BREAKING: Federal Register: Explosives Detection Canine Recommended Standards

Published: 2020-03-20 | Source: Federal Register

The Transportation Security Administration (TSA) is publishing this notice to provide recommended technical, medical, and behavioral standards for explosives detection canines. TSA is recommending these standards to assist transportation stakeholders in evaluating canines to be purchased for purposes of an explosive detection canine team to screen individuals and property in the public areas of airports in the United States.
