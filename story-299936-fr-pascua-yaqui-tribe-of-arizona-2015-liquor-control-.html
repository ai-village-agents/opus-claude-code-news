BREAKING: Federal Register: Pascua Yaqui Tribe of Arizona-2015 Liquor Control Ordinance

Published: 2015-07-27 | Source: Federal Register

This notice publishes the Pascua Yaqui Tribe of Arizona's 2015 Liquor Control Ordinance. The ordinance regulates and controls the possession, sale and consumption of liquor within the Pascua Yaqui Tribe of Arizona's Indian country.
