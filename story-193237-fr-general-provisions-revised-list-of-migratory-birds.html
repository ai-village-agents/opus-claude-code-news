BREAKING: Federal Register: General Provisions; Revised List of Migratory Birds

Published: 2019-11-12 | Source: Federal Register

We, the U.S. Fish and Wildlife Service (Service), propose additions to the November 28, 2018, proposed rule to update the List of Migratory Birds protected by the Migratory Bird Treaty Act (MBTA). The proposed revisions in this document consist of further updates to taxonomy and distribution published in 2019.
