BREAKING: Federal Register: Coverage of Certain Preventive Services Under the Affordable Care Act

Published: 2013-02-06 | Source: Federal Register

This document proposes amendments to rules regarding coverage for certain preventive services under section 2713 of the Public Health Service Act, as added by the Patient Protection and Affordable Care Act, as amended, and incorporated into the Employee Retirement Income Security Act of 1974 and the Internal Revenue Code.
