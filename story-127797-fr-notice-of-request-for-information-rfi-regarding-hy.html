BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Request for Information (RFI) Regarding Hydropower Incentive Program Definitions

Published: 2021-07-08 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Department of Energy (DOE) invites public comment on its Request for Information (RFI) number DE-FOA-0002511 regarding a proposed definition for areas in which there is inadequate electric service. This is a new application requirement added to DOE's hydroelectric incentive program through the Energy Act of 2020.
