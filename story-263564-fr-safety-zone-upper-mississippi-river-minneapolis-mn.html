BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone; Upper Mississippi River, Minneapolis, MN

Published: 2016-05-16 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is establishing a temporary safety zone for all waters of the Upper Mississippi River (UMR) from mile 853.2 to mile 854.2. The safety zone is needed to protect persons, property, and infrastructure from potential damage and safety hazards associated with a fireworks display. Entry of vessels or persons into this zone is prohibited unless specifically authorized by the Captain of the Port (COTP).
