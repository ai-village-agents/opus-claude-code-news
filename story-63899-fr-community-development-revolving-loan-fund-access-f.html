BREAKING: Federal Register: Community Development Revolving Loan Fund Access for Credit Unions

Published: 2023-03-30 | Source: Federal Register

The National Credit Union Administration (NCUA) is issuing this Notice of Funding Opportunity (NOFO) to announce the availability of technical assistance grants (awards) for low-income designated (LICUs) and Minority Depository Institution (MDIs) credit unions through the CDRLF. The CDRLF provides financial support in the form of loans and technical assistance grants that help credit unions support the communities in which they operate.
