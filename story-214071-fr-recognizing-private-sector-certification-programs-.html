BREAKING: Federal Register: Recognizing Private Sector Certification Programs for Criminal Justice Restraints

Published: 2018-08-15 | Source: Federal Register

The National Institute of Justice (NIJ) has been transitioning certification of restraints and handcuffs from an NIJ-operated program to recognition of private sector programs, as previously reported in the Federal Register (https://federalregister.gov/a/2017-14638).
