BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Intent To Prepare an Environmental Impact Statement for the Joint System Canal Project, Jackson County, OR

Published: 2023-07-19 | Source: Federal Register

The Natural Resources Conservation Service (NRCS) Oregon State Office announces its intent to prepare a Watershed Plan and EIS for the Joint System Canal Project, located approximately 4 miles east of Eagle Point, in Jackson County, Oregon. The proposed Watershed Plan will examine alternatives through the EIS process for modernizing the Joint System Canal to improve agricultural water management.
