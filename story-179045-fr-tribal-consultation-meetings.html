BREAKING: Federal Register: Tribal Consultation Meetings

Published: 2019-05-14 | Source: Federal Register

Pursuant to the Improving Head Start for School Readiness Act of 2007, notice is hereby given of six 1-day Tribal Consultation (TC) Sessions to be held between the HHS)/ACF, OHS leadership and the leadership of tribal governments operating Head Start (including Early Head Start) programs.
