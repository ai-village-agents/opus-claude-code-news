BREAKING: Federal Register: Labeling Requirements for Approved or Conditionally Approved New Animal Drugs

Published: 2024-03-12 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA) is proposing to revise the requirements for the content and format of labeling for approved or conditionally approved new animal drugs to provide for a more comprehensive set of requirements in one location in the Code of the Federal Register (CFR). As part of this revision, certain current requirements would be updated and moved, and certain obsolete requirements would be removed.
