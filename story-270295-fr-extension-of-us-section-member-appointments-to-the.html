BREAKING: Federal Register: Extension of U.S. Section Member Appointments to the United States-Brazil CEO Forum

Published: 2016-08-03 | Source: Federal Register

In March 2007, the Governments of the United States and Brazil established the U.S.-Brazil CEO Forum (Forum). Through a Federal Register notice on May 29, 2013 (78 FR 32,239), the Department of Commerce solicited applicants for appointment to the U.S. Section for a term of three years to expire August 13, 2016, and appointed individuals to all twelve Member positions.
