BREAKING: Federal Register: Outer Continental Shelf, Gulf of Mexico, Oil and Gas Western Planning Area Lease Sale 248

Published: 2015-09-04 | Source: Federal Register

BOEM is announcing the availability of a Draft Supplemental Environmental Impact Statement (EIS) for the proposed Gulf of Mexico (GOM) Outer Continental Shelf (OCS) oil and gas Western Planning Area (WPA) Lease Sale 248 (WPA Sale 248). WPA Sale 248 is tentatively scheduled for August 2016.
