BREAKING: Federal Register: Federal Acquisition Regulation; Information Collection; Public Voucher for Purchases and Services Other Than Personal

Published: 2014-07-10 | Source: Federal Register

Under the provisions of the Paperwork Reduction Act (44 U.S.C. chapter 35), the Regulatory Secretariat Division (MVCB) will be submitting to the Office of Management and Budget (OMB) a request to review and approve a new information collection requirement regarding Public Voucher for Purchases and Services other than Personal.
