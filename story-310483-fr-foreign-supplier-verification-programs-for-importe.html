BREAKING: Federal Register: Foreign Supplier Verification Programs for Importers of Food for Humans and Animals

Published: 2015-11-27 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA) is adopting a regulation on foreign supplier verification programs (FSVPs) for importers of food for humans and animals.
