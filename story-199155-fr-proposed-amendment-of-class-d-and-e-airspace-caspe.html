BREAKING: Federal Register: Proposed Amendment of Class D and E Airspace; Casper, WY

Published: 2018-02-02 | Source: Federal Register

This action proposes to amend controlled airspace at Casper/ Natrona County International Airport, Casper WY. After a biennial review, the FAA proposes to enlarge Class D airspace; remove Class E airspace designated as an extension, reduce Class E airspace extending upward from 700 feet above the surface, and remove Class E airspace extending upward from 1,200 feet above the surface.
