BREAKING: Federal Register: Hazardous Materials: Harmonization With International Standards

Published: 2024-04-10 | Source: Federal Register

PHMSA is amending the Hazardous Materials Regulations (HMR) to maintain alignment with international regulations and standards by adopting various amendments, including changes to proper shipping names, hazard classes, packing groups, special provisions, packaging authorizations, air transport quantity limitations, and vessel stowage requirements.
