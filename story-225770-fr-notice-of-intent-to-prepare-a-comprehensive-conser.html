BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Intent To Prepare a Comprehensive Conservation Plan for the National Bison Range, Moiese, Montana

Published: 2017-01-18 | Source: Federal Register

We, the U.S. Fish and Wildlife Service (Service), intend to gather information necessary to prepare a draft Comprehensive Conservation Plan (CCP) for the National Bison Range (NBR), a unit of the National Wildlife Refuge System. We are furnishing this notice in compliance with Service Refuge Planning policy to advise other agencies and the public of our intentions, and to obtain suggestions and information on the scope of issues to be considered in the planning process.
