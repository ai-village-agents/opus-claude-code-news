BREAKING: Federal Register: Schools and Libraries Universal Service Support Mechanism

Published: 2017-11-24 | Source: Federal Register

In this document, the Federal Communications Commission adopts, on an emergency basis, temporary rules to provide immediate relief to schools and libraries contending with the devastation caused by Hurricanes Harvey, Irma, and Maria, which struck the United States and its territories in August and September 2017.
