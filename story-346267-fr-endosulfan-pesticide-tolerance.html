BREAKING: Federal Register: Endosulfan; Pesticide Tolerance

Published: 2013-02-06 | Source: Federal Register

EPA has granted an objection to the timing of the revocation of the tolerance for endosulfan on tea. The objection was filed by the Chamber of Commerce of Zhejiang International Tea Industry. With this document, EPA is amending the tolerances for endosulfan to reestablish a time- limited tolerance for residues on tea.
