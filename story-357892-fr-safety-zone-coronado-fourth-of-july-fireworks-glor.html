BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone; Coronado Fourth of July Fireworks, Glorietta Bay; Coronado, CA

Published: 2013-06-19 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is establishing a temporary safety zone upon the navigable waters of Glorietta Bay for the Coronado Fourth of July Fireworks on July 4, 2013. This temporary safety zone is a modification of an existing permanent safety zone, made due to a change in location of the fireworks barge.
