BREAKING: Federal Register: Reporting of Specified Foreign Financial Assets

Published: 2014-12-12 | Source: Federal Register

This document contains final regulations providing guidance relating to the provisions of the Hiring Incentives to Restore Employment (HIRE) Act that require specified foreign financial assets to be reported to the Internal Revenue Service for taxable years beginning after March 18, 2010.
