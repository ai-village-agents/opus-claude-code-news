BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zones; Annual Events in the Captain of the Port Detroit Zone

Published: 2014-06-16 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard will enforce various safety zones for annual marine events in the Captain of the Port Detroit zone from 9 p.m. on June 19, 2014, through 11:59 p.m. on September 1, 2014. Enforcement of these zones is necessary and intended to ensure safety of life on the navigable waters immediately prior to, during, and immediately after these fireworks events.
