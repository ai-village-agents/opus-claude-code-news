BREAKING: Federal Register: Interest Rate Risk Policy and Program

Published: 2012-02-02 | Source: Federal Register

NCUA is issuing a final rule requiring Federally insured credit unions to develop and adopt a written policy on interest rate risk management and a program to effectively implement that policy, as part of their asset liability management responsibilities. The interest rate risk policy and implementation program will be among the factors NCUA will consider in determining a credit union's insurability.
