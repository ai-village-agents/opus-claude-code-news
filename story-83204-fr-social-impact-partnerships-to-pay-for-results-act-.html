BREAKING: Federal Register: Social Impact Partnerships To Pay for Results Act Projects

Published: 2023-11-30 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of the Treasury (Treasury) is issuing this Notice of Funding Availability (NOFA) to invite applications from State and local governments for awards under the Social Impact Partnerships to Pay for Results Act (the "Act"). An award recipient will receive payment if a specified outcome of the social impact partnership project is achieved as determined by the project's independent evaluator.
