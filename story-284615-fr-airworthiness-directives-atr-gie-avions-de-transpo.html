BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; ATR-GIE Avions de Transport Régional Airplanes

Published: 2015-01-23 | Source: Federal Register

We propose to adopt a new airworthiness directive (AD) for certain ATR-GIE Avions de Transport R[eacute]gional Model ATR42-500 and Model ATR72-212A airplanes. This proposed AD was prompted by a report indicating interference between a Type III Emergency Exit door and the surrounding passenger cabin furnishing during a production check.
