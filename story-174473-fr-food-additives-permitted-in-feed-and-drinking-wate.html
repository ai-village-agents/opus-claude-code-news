BREAKING: Federal Register: Food Additives Permitted in Feed and Drinking Water of Animals; Spent Bleaching Clay

Published: 2019-03-19 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA, the Agency, or we) is amending a notice of petition announcing that the Canadian Oilseed Processors Association has filed a petition proposing that the food additive regulations be amended to provide for the safe use of spent bleaching clay as a flow agent in canola meal for all livestock and poultry species.
