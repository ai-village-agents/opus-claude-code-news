BREAKING: Federal Register: Pesticides; Updated Draft Guidance for Pesticide Registrants on Plant Regulator Products and Claims, Including Plant Biostimulants; Notice of Availability and Request for Comment

Published: 2020-11-30 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is announcing the availability of and seeking public comment on an updated draft guidance document entitled "Guidance for Plant Regulator Products and Claims, including Plant Biostimulants," which was originally issued for public review and comment in March 2019.
