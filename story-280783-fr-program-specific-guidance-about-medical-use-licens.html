BREAKING: Federal Register: Program-Specific Guidance About Medical Use Licenses

Published: 2016-12-06 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Nuclear Regulatory Commission (NRC) is revising its licensing guidance for licenses authorizing medical use of byproduct material. The NRC is requesting public comment on draft NUREG-1556, Volume 9, Revision 3, "Consolidated Guidance About Materials Licenses: Program-Specific Guidance About Medical Use Licenses."
