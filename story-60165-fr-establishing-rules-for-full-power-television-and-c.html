BREAKING: Federal Register: Establishing Rules for Full Power Television and Class A Television Stations

Published: 2023-02-09 | Source: Federal Register

In this document, the Federal Communications Commission (Commission or FCC) seeks comment on comprehensively deleting, updating, or otherwise revising Commission rules for full power television and Class A television stations that no longer have any practical effect given the completion of the transition from analog to digital-only operations and the post incentive auction transition to a smaller television band with fewer channels.
