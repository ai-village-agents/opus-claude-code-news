BREAKING: Federal Register: National Volatile Organic Compound Emission Standards for Aerosol Coatings Amendments

Published: 2021-09-17 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is proposing to amend the National Volatile Organic Compound (VOC) Emission Standards for Aerosol Coatings, which establishes reactivity-based emission standards for the aerosol coatings category (aerosol spray paints) under the Clean Air Act (CAA).
