BREAKING: Federal Register: Authority To Accept Unsolicited Proposals for Research Partnerships

Published: 2014-03-21 | Source: Federal Register

This notice announces that HUD's Office of Policy Development and Research (PD&R) has the authority to accept unsolicited research proposals that address current research priorities. In accord with statutory requirements, the research projects must be funded at least 50 percent by philanthropic entities or federal, state or local government agencies.
