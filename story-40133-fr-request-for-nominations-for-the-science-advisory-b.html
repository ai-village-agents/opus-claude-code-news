BREAKING: Federal Register: Request for Nominations for the Science Advisory Board BenSPLASH Review Panel

Published: 2024-06-24 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) Science Advisory Board (SAB) Staff Office requests public nominations of scientific experts to form a Panel to review the Benefits Spatial Platform for Aggregating Socioeconomics and H2O Quality (BenSPLASH). BenSPLASH serves as one component in an integrated assessment model designed to quantify the economic benefits of changes in water quality.
