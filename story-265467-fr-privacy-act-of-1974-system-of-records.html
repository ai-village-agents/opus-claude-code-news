BREAKING: Federal Register: Privacy Act of 1974; System of Records

Published: 2016-06-07 | Source: Federal Register

The Office of the Secretary of Defense proposes to add a new system of records, DSCA 05, entitled "Defense Institute of Security Assistance Management (DISAM) Information System Mission (DISM)." The DISAM Information Mission System is a web based portal designed to hold several applications for the purposes of efficient administration of students, and the effective management of DISAM personnel and guest lecturers.
