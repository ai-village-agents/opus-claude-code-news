BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Solicitation of Applications for the Rural Business Development Grant Programs for Fiscal Year 2023

Published: 2022-11-23 | Source: Federal Register

This notice is given to invite applications for grants under the Rural Business Development Grant (RBDG) Program for fiscal year (FY) 2023, subject to the availability of funding. This notice is being issued prior to passage of a FY 2023 Appropriations Act in order to allow applicants sufficient time to leverage financing, prepare and submit their applications, and give the Agency time to process applications within FY 2023.
