BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Proposed Information Collection; Request for Comments for 1029-0116

Published: 2016-09-15 | Source: Federal Register

In compliance with the Paperwork Reduction Act of 1995, the Office of Surface Mining Reclamation and Enforcement (OSMRE) is announcing its intention to request renewed approval from the Office of Management and Budget (OMB) to continue the collection of information for the Revisions; Renewals; and Transfer, Assignment, or Sale of Permit Rights.
