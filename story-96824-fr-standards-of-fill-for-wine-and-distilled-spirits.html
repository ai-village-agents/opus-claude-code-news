BREAKING: Federal Register: Standards of Fill for Wine and Distilled Spirits

Published: 2022-05-25 | Source: Federal Register

In this document, the Alcohol and Tobacco Tax and Trade Bureau (TTB) proposes to amend the regulations governing wine and distilled spirits containers. TTB is proposing to add 10 additional authorized standards of fill for wine, along with related technical and other harmonizing changes.
