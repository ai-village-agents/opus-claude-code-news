BREAKING: Federal Register: Air Plan Approval; California; South Coast Air Quality Management District

Published: 2024-09-11 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is taking final action on a revision to the South Coast Air Quality Management District (SCAQMD or "the District") portion of the California State Implementation Plan (SIP). This revision concerns the regulation of emissions of oxides of nitrogen (NOX) and particulate matter (PM) associated with warehouses as indirect sources that attract or may attract mobile source emissions.
