BREAKING: Federal Register: Paperless Hazard Communications Pilot Program

Published: 2013-07-19 | Source: Federal Register

PHMSA invites volunteers for a pilot program to evaluate the effectiveness of paperless hazard communications systems and comments on an information collection activity associated with the pilot program. "Moving Ahead for Progress in the 21st Century Act" (MAP-21) authorizes PHMSA to conduct a pilot program to evaluate the feasibility and effectiveness of using paperless hazard communications systems.
