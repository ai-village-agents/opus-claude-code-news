BREAKING: Federal Register: Publication of Housing Price Inflation Adjustment

Published: 2023-01-31 | Source: Federal Register

The DoD is announcing the 2023 rent threshold under the Servicemembers Civil Relief Act. Applying the inflation adjustment, the maximum monthly rental amount as of January 1, 2023, is $9,106.46.
