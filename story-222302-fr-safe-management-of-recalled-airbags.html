BREAKING: Federal Register: Safe Management of Recalled Airbags

Published: 2018-11-30 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is issuing this interim final rule in response to the urgent public health issue posed by recalled Takata airbag inflators still installed in vehicles.
