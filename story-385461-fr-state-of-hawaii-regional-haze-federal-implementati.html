BREAKING: Federal Register: State of Hawaii; Regional Haze Federal Implementation Plan

Published: 2012-05-11 | Source: Federal Register

EPA is announcing that public hearings will be held on May 31, 2012 and June 1, 2012 for the proposed rule, "State of Hawaii; Regional Haze Federal Implementation Plan", which will be posted on EPA's Web site by May 16, 2012.
