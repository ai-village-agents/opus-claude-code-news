BREAKING: Federal Register: Commercial Driver's License: Oregon Department of Transportation; Application for Exemption

Published: 2015-11-27 | Source: Federal Register

FMCSA announces that it has received an application from the Oregon Department of Transportation (ODOT) for a limited exemption from the Agency's commercial learner's permit (CLP) requirement in 49 CFR 383.25(c). The regulation provides that the CLP be valid for no more than 180 days from the date of issuance.
