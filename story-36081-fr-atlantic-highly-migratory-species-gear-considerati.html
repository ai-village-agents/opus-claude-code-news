BREAKING: Federal Register: Atlantic Highly Migratory Species; Gear Considerations

Published: 2024-05-03 | Source: Federal Register

NMFS announces the availability of a scoping document to consider changes to gear regulations and requirements in fisheries targeting Atlantic highly migratory species (HMS). While management measures implemented since 1999 have helped achieve fishery management and conservation goals, the combination of over two decades of gear- specific measures may have had unanticipated consequences.
