BREAKING: Federal Register: Request for Nominations on the Pediatric Advisory Committee

Published: 2017-11-02 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA) is requesting that any industry organizations interested in participating in the selection of a nonvoting industry representative to serve on the Pediatric Advisory Committee for the Office of the Commissioner notify FDA in writing. FDA is also requesting nominations for a nonvoting industry representative(s) to serve on the Pediatric Advisory Committee.
