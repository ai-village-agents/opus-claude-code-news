BREAKING: Federal Register: Information Collection Request Submitted to OMB for Review and Approval; Comment Request; Renewable Fuel Standard Program

Published: 2018-11-13 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) has submitted an information collection request (ICR), Renewable Fuel Standard Program (EPA ICR No. 2546.01, OMB Control No. 2060-NEW) to the Office of Management and Budget (OMB) for review and approval in accordance with the Paperwork Reduction Act. This is a request for approval of an ICR that consolidates several existing collections.
