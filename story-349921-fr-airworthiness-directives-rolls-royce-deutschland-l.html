BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Rolls-Royce Deutschland Ltd & Co KG Turbofan Engines

Published: 2013-03-20 | Source: Federal Register

We are adopting a new airworthiness directive (AD) for certain Rolls-Royce Deutschland Ltd & Co KG (RRD) models Tay 620-15 and Tay 650-15 turbofan engines. This AD was prompted by RRD recalculating the Declared Safe Cyclic Life (DSCL) for certain low-pressure compressor (LPC) rotor disc assemblies operating to the Plan D Flight Mission.
