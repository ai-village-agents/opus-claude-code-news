BREAKING: Federal Register: Availability of Draft Toxicological Profile; Perfluoroalkyls

Published: 2015-09-02 | Source: Federal Register

The Agency for Toxic Substances and Disease Registry (ATSDR) located in the Department of Health and Human Services (HHS) announces the availability of the Toxicological Profile for Perfluoroalkyls for review and comment. Comments can include additional information or reports on studies about the health effects of perfluoroalkyls.
