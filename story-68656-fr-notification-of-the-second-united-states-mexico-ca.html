BREAKING: Federal Register: Notification of the Second United States-Mexico-Canada Agreement Labor Council Meeting

Published: 2023-05-31 | Source: Federal Register

The Parties to the United States-Mexico-Canada Agreement (USMCA) intend to hold the second meeting of the Labor Council on June 28-29, 2023. The meeting will include a closed in-person government-to- government Labor Council session in Mexico City, Mexico, and a virtual public session on implementation of the USMCA labor chapter.
