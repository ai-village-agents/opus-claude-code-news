BREAKING: Federal Register: Dairy Tariff-Rate Quota Import Licensing Program

Published: 2015-07-27 | Source: Federal Register

This final rule amends the regulation that provides for the issuance of licenses to import certain dairy articles under tariff-rate quotas (TRQs) as set forth in the Harmonized Tariff Schedule of the United States.
