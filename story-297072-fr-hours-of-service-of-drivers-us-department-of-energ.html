BREAKING: Federal Register: Hours of Service of Drivers: U.S. Department of Energy (DOE); Application for Renewal of Exemption

Published: 2015-06-22 | Source: Federal Register

FMCSA announces its decision to grant the U.S. Department of Energy's (DOE) request for a renewal of its exemption from the minimum 30-minute rest break provision of the Agency's hours-of-service (HOS) regulations for commercial motor vehicle (CMV) drivers. The exemption will enable DOE's contract motor carriers and their employee-drivers transporting security-sensitive radioactive materials to be treated the same as drivers transporting explosives.
