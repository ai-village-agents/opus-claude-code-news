BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Bell Helicopter Textron, Inc. (BHTI) Helicopters

Published: 2014-06-19 | Source: Federal Register

We are superseding Airworthiness Directive (AD) 2003-01-04 for BHTI Model 204B, 205A, 205A-1, 205B, and 212 helicopters. AD 2003-01-04 required inspecting the main rotor grip (grip) and reporting certain inspection results to the FAA.
