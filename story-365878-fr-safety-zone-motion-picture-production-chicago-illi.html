BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone; Motion Picture Production; Chicago, Illinois

Published: 2013-09-19 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard will enforce the temporary safety zone for motion picture filming in Calumet Harbor, Chicago, IL from 9 p.m. until 6 a.m., from September 15 through September 29, 2013. This action is necessary and intended to ensure safety of life on navigable waters during nighttime filming of a motion picture in Calumet Harbor.
