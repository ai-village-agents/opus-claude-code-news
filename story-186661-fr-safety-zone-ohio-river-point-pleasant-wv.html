BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone; Ohio River, Point Pleasant, WV

Published: 2019-08-19 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is establishing a temporary safety zone for the navigable waters of the Ohio River, from mile marker (MM) 264.6 to MM 265.6, near Point Pleasant on August 31, 2019 for a Labor Day fireworks display. The safety zone is needed to protect personnel, vessels, and the marine environment from potential hazards created by the fireworks display.
