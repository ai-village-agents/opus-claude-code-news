BREAKING: Federal Register: Physical Protection of Irradiated Reactor Fuel in Transit

Published: 2013-05-20 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Nuclear Regulatory Commission (NRC) is amending its security regulations for the transport of irradiated reactor fuel (the terms "irradiated reactor fuel" and "spent nuclear fuel" are used interchangeably in this rule).
