BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Extension To Solicit Transit Advisory Committee for Safety Member Applications

Published: 2022-03-15 | Source: Federal Register

The Federal Transit Administration (FTA) is extending the solicitation seeking applications for individuals to serve as members, for two-year terms, on the Transit Advisory Committee for Safety (TRACS), which was published on February 7, 2022, with the original solicitation closing date of March 9, 2022.
