BREAKING: Federal Register: Granting Petitions To Add n-Propyl Bromide to the List of Hazardous Air Pollutants

Published: 2017-03-06 | Source: Federal Register

On January 9, 2017, the Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) published a draft notice of the rationale for granting petitions to add n-propyl bromide (nPB), also known as 1-bromopropane (1-BP) (Chemical Abstract Service No. 106-94-5), to the list of hazardous air pollutants contained in section 112(b)(1) of the Clean Air Act.
