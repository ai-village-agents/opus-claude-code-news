BREAKING: Federal Register: Privacy Act; System of Records: State-59, Refugee Case Records

Published: 2012-02-06 | Source: Federal Register

Notice is hereby given that the Department of State proposes to consolidate two existing systems of records, Refugee Case Records, State-59 and Refugee Processing Center Records, State-60, pursuant to the provisions of the Privacy Act of 1974, as amended (5 U.S.C. 552a) and Office of Management and Budget Circular No. A-130, Appendix I. The consolidated system will be titled State-59, Refugee Case Records.
