BREAKING: Federal Register: Colo Void Clause Coalition; Antenna Systems Co-Location; Voluntary Best Practices

Published: 2022-07-05 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA announces an amendment to its Colo Void policy. The FAA last revised its policy regarding the notification requirements and processes for evaluation of potential electromagnetic interference (EMI) for co-location of antenna systems on existing structures previously studied by the FAA on November 21, 2007.
