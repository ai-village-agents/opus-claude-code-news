BREAKING: Federal Register: Climate and Economic Justice Screening Tool Beta Version

Published: 2022-04-25 | Source: Federal Register

On February 23, 2022, the Council on Environmental Quality published a request for information (RFI) to solicit feedback on the beta version of the Climate and Economic Justice Screening Tool. This notice extends the deadline date for receiving comments until May 25, 2022.
