BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone; United States and Canadian Military Exercise Jump Training, Lake Erie, Hamburg, NY

Published: 2014-06-26 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is establishing a temporary safety zone on Lake Erie, Hamburg, NY. This rule is necessary to protect the United States military and Canadian military participants as well as mariners and vessels from the navigational and safety hazards associated with the airborne deployment of U.S. and Canadian military personnel and their associated equipment.
