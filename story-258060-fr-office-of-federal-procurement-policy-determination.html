BREAKING: Federal Register: Office of Federal Procurement Policy; Determination of Statutory Formula Benchmark Compensation Amount for Certain Executives and Contractor Employees

Published: 2016-03-15 | Source: Federal Register

The Office of Management and Budget is publishing the attached memorandum to the Heads of Executive Departments and Agencies announcing that the "benchmark compensation amount" for certain executives and contractor employees in terms of costs allowable under Federal Government covered contracts during the contractor's fiscal years 2013 and 2014 is $980,796 and $1,144,888, respectively.
