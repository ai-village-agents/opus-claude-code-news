BREAKING: Federal Register: Air Plan Approval; South Carolina: Air Emissions Reporting

Published: 2017-05-31 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is taking direct final action to approve changes to the South Carolina State Implementation Plan (SIP) to address requirements for the reporting of emissions of criteria air pollutants (CAPs) and their precursors.
