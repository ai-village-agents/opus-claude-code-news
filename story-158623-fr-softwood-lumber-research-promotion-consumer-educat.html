BREAKING: Federal Register: Softwood Lumber Research, Promotion, Consumer Education and Industry Information Order; Assessment Rate Increase

Published: 2020-08-13 | Source: Federal Register

This proposal invites comments on amending the Softwood Lumber Research, Promotion, Consumer Education and Industry Information Order (Order) to increase the assessment rate from $0.35 to $0.41 per thousand board feet (mbf). The Order is administered by the Softwood Lumber Board (Board) with oversight by the U.S. Department of Agriculture (USDA).
