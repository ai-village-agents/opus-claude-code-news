BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Airbus SAS Airplanes

Published: 2024-07-23 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA proposes to supersede Airworthiness Directive (AD) 2023-07-13, which applies to certain Airbus SAS Model A350-941 and - 1041 airplanes. AD 2023-07-13 requires repetitive detailed inspections of the lower attachment studs on the AFT galley complex and, depending on findings, replacement of the lower attachment studs.
