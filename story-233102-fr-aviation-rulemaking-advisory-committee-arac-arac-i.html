BREAKING: Federal Register: Aviation Rulemaking Advisory Committee (ARAC)-ARAC Input To Support Regulatory Reform of Aviation Regulations-New Task

Published: 2017-04-28 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA assigned the Aviation Rulemaking Advisory Committee (ARAC) a new task to consider (1) recommendations on existing regulations that are good candidates for repeal, replacement, or modification and (2) recommendations on regulatory action identified in FAA's regulatory agenda.
