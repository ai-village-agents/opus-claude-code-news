BREAKING: Federal Register: Privacy Act of 1974; System of Records

Published: 2024-09-09 | Source: Federal Register

The United States Postal Service[supreg] (USPS[supreg]) is proposing to create one Customer Privacy Act System of Records (SOR) 1225.000. This new SOR will formalize the Postal Service program known as USPS Operation Santa[supreg] and promote transparency.
