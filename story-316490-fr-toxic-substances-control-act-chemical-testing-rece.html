BREAKING: Federal Register: Toxic Substances Control Act Chemical Testing; Receipt of Test Data

Published: 2014-02-13 | Source: Federal Register

EPA is announcing its receipt of test data submitted pursuant to a test rule issued by EPA under section 4 of the Toxic Substances Control Act (TSCA). This document identifies each chemical substance or mixture for which test data have been received; lists uses or intended uses of such chemical substance or mixture; and describes the nature of the test data received.
