BREAKING: Federal Register: Developing a Unified Intercarrier Compensation Regime

Published: 2018-04-03 | Source: Federal Register

In this document, the Commission reconsiders rules adopted in the Rate-of-Return Reform Order. Specifically, the Commission replaces the surrogate cost methods for Consumer Only Broadband Loops, revises CBOL imputation rules, and lastly, clarifies matters concerning reductions in the Connect America Fund Broadband Loop Support.
