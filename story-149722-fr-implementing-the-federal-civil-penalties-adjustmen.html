BREAKING: Federal Register: Implementing the Federal Civil Penalties Adjustment Act Improvements Act of 2015

Published: 2020-04-21 | Source: Federal Register

The National Endowment for the Humanities (NEH) is adjusting the civil monetary penalties it imposes for violations of NEH's New Restrictions on Lobbying regulation, pursuant to the Federal Civil Penalties Inflation Adjustment Act Improvements Act of 2015 (the 2015 Act).
