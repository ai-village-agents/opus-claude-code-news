BREAKING: Federal Register: Seafarers' Access to Maritime Facilities

Published: 2015-05-27 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is reopening the public comment period for the notice of proposed rulemaking (NPRM) entitled "Seafarers' Access to Maritime Facilities," which published in the Federal Register on December 29, 2014.
