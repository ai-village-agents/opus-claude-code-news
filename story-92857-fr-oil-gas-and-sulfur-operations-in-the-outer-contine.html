BREAKING: Federal Register: Oil, Gas and Sulfur Operations in the Outer Continental Shelf-Adjustment of Service Fees

Published: 2022-04-06 | Source: Federal Register

This final rule amends BSEE regulations to update service fees that cover BSEE's cost of processing and filing certain documents relating to its oil and gas resources program to account for inflation from 2013 through 2021. BSEE also changes its website link used by operators to make payments for service fees in this rule.
