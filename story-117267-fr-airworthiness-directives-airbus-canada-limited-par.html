BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Airbus Canada Limited Partnership (Type Certificate Previously Held by C Series Aircraft Limited Partnership (CSALP); Bombardier, Inc.) Airplanes

Published: 2021-02-23 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA is superseding Airworthiness Directive (AD) 2019-23- 15, which applied to certain Airbus Canada Limited Partnership Model BD-500-1A10 and BD-500-1A11 airplanes. AD 2019-23-15 required revising the existing maintenance or inspection program, as applicable, to incorporate new or more restrictive airworthiness limitations.
