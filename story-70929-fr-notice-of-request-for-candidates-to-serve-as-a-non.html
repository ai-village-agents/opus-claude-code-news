BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Request for Candidates To Serve as a Non-Federal Member of the Federal Accounting Standards Advisory Board

Published: 2023-06-28 | Source: Federal Register

Notice is hereby given that the Federal Accounting Standards Advisory Board (FASAB) is currently seeking candidates (candidates must not currently be Federal employees) to serve as a non-Federal member of FASAB. FASAB is the body designated to establish generally accepted accounting principles for Federal Government entities.
