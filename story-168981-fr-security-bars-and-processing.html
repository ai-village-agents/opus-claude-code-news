BREAKING: Federal Register: Security Bars and Processing

Published: 2020-12-23 | Source: Federal Register

On July 9, 2020, DHS and DOJ (collectively, "the Departments") published a notice of proposed rulemaking ("NPRM") clarifying that the danger to the security of the United States statutory bar to eligibility for asylum and withholding of removal may encompass emergency public health concerns.
