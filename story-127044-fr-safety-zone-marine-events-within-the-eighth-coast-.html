BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone; Marine Events Within the Eighth Coast Guard District

Published: 2021-06-29 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard will enforce a safety zone for the Madisonville Old Fashioned 4th of July fireworks display July 3, 2021, from 8 p.m. through 9 p.m. to provide for the safety of life on navigable waterways during this event. Our regulation for marine events within the Eighth Coast Guard District identifies the regulated area for this event on the Tchefuncte River, near Madisonville, Louisiana.
