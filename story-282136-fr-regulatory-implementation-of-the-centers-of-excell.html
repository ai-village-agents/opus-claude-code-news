BREAKING: Federal Register: Regulatory Implementation of the Centers of Excellence and Expertise

Published: 2016-12-20 | Source: Federal Register

In 2012, U.S. Customs and Border Protection (CBP) developed a test to incrementally transition the operational trade functions that traditionally reside with port directors to the Centers of Excellence and Expertise (Centers).
