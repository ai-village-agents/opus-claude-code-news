BREAKING: Federal Register: Acquisition Regulations; Buy Indian Act; Procedures for Contracting

Published: 2022-04-08 | Source: Federal Register

This final rule revises the Department of the Interior's regulations implementing the Buy Indian Act, which provides the Department of Interior (DOI) with authority to set aside procurement contracts for Indian-owned and controlled businesses.
