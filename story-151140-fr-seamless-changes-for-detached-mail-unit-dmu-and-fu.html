BREAKING: Federal Register: Seamless Changes for Detached Mail Unit (DMU) and Full-Service Mailings

Published: 2020-05-08 | Source: Federal Register

The Postal ServiceTM is revising Mailing Standards of the United States Postal Service, Domestic Mail Manual (DMM[supreg]) to require Detached Mail Unit (DMU) mailers and mailers that enter full-service mailings at a Business Mail Entry Unit (BMEU) to participate in Seamless Parallel by June 1, 2020.
