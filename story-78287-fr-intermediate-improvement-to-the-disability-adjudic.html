BREAKING: Federal Register: Intermediate Improvement to the Disability Adjudication Process: Including How We Consider Past Work

Published: 2023-09-29 | Source: Federal Register

We propose to revise the time period that we consider when determining whether an individual's past work is relevant for purposes of making disability determinations and decisions. Specifically, we would revise the definition of past relevant work (PRW) by reducing the relevant work period from 15 to 5 years.
