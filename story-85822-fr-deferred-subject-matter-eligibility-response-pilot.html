BREAKING: Federal Register: Deferred Subject Matter Eligibility Response Pilot Program

Published: 2022-01-06 | Source: Federal Register

The United States Patent and Trademark Office (USPTO or Office) is implementing a pilot program to evaluate the effects of permitting applicants to defer responding to subject matter eligibility (SME) rejections in certain patent applications.
