BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Eurocopter France Helicopters

Published: 2013-12-20 | Source: Federal Register

We are adopting a new airworthiness directive (AD) for Eurocopter France (Eurocopter) Model AS332C, AS332L, AS332L1, AS332L2, and EC225LP helicopters. This AD requires visually inspecting each jettisonable emergency exit window panel (window) for sealant, and removing any sealant that exists in the window's extruded sections.
