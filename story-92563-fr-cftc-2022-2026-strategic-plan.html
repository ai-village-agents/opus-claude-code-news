BREAKING: Federal Register: CFTC 2022-2026 Strategic Plan

Published: 2022-04-01 | Source: Federal Register

The Commodity Futures Trading Commission (CFTC or Commission) is providing notice that it is seeking public comments on its draft 2022-2026 Strategic Plan. This Commission-approved version of the Strategic Plan includes the CFTC's mission, strategic goals, and strategic objectives.
