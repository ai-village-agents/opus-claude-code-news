BREAKING: Federal Register: Drawbridge Operation Regulations; Hutchinson River (Eastchester Creek), Bronx, NY

Published: 2012-02-07 | Source: Federal Register

The Commander, First Coast Guard District, has issued a temporary deviation from the regulation governing the operation of the Amtrak Pelham Bay Railroad Bridge, mile 0.5, across the Hutchinson River (Eastchester Creek) at the Bronx, New York. The deviation is necessary to facilitate scheduled maintenance at the bridge. This deviation allows the bridge to remain in the closed position during two separate phases.
