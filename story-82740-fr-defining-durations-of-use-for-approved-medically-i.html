BREAKING: Federal Register: Defining Durations of Use for Approved Medically Important Antimicrobial Drugs Fed to Food-Producing Animals; Draft Guidance for Industry; Extension of the Comment Period

Published: 2023-11-24 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA or the Agency) is extending the comment period for the notice of availability that published in the Federal Register of September 26, 2023. In that notice, FDA requested comments on the draft guidance for industry (GFI) #273 entitled "Defining Durations of Use for Approved Medically Important Antimicrobial Drugs Fed to Food-Producing Animals."
