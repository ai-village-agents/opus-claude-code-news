BREAKING: Federal Register: Marine Mammal Protection Act and Wild Bird Conservation Act; Receipt of Permit Applications

Published: 2022-11-10 | Source: Federal Register

We, the U.S. Fish and Wildlife Service (Service), invite the public to comment on species for which the Service has jurisdiction under the Marine Mammal Protection Act (MMPA) and foreign bird species covered under the Wild Bird Conservation Act (WBCA). With some exceptions, the MMPA and WBCA prohibit activities with listed species unless Federal authorization is issued that allows such activities.
