BREAKING: Federal Register: Specific Listing for 1-boc-4-piperidone, a Currently Controlled List I Chemical

Published: 2025-10-02 | Source: Federal Register

The Drug Enforcement Administration (DEA) is establishing a specific listing and DEA Chemical Code Number for tert-butyl 4- oxopiperidine-1-carboxylate (also known as 1-boc-4-piperidone; and CAS Number: 79099-07-3) and its salts as a list I chemical under the Controlled Substances Act (CSA).
