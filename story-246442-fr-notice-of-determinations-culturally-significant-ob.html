BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Determinations; Culturally Significant Objects Imported for Exhibition Determinations: “Painted in Mexico, 1700-1790: Pinxit Mexici” Exhibition

Published: 2017-10-17 | Source: Federal Register

Notice is hereby given of the following determinations: I hereby determine that certain objects to be included in the exhibition "Painted in Mexico, 1700-1790: Pinxit Mexici," imported from abroad for temporary exhibition within the United States, are of cultural significance. The objects are imported pursuant to loan agreements with the foreign owners or custodians.
