BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Embraer S.A. Airplanes

Published: 2013-06-04 | Source: Federal Register

We are adopting a new airworthiness directive (AD) for certain Embraer S.A. Model ERJ 190 airplanes. This AD was prompted by reports of cracks on the side stay of the main landing gear (MLG).
