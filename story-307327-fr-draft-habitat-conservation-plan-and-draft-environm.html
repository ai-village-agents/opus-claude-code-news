BREAKING: Federal Register: Draft Habitat Conservation Plan and Draft Environmental Assessment; Kaufman Properties, Thurston County, Washington

Published: 2015-10-21 | Source: Federal Register

We, the U.S. Fish and Wildlife Service (Service), have received an application from Kaufman Real Estate LLC, Kaufman Holdings Inc., and Liberty Leasing & Construction, Inc. (applicants), for an incidental take permit (ITP) pursuant to the Endangered Species Act of 1973, as amended (ESA). The applicants request a 20-year ITP that would authorize "take" of five listed species incidental to otherwise lawful land development and habitat conservation activities on parcels they own in Thurston County,...
