BREAKING: Federal Register: Endangered and Threatened Wildlife and Plants; Listing All Chimpanzees as Endangered

Published: 2013-06-12 | Source: Federal Register

We, the U.S. Fish and Wildlife Service, propose to list all chimpanzees (Pan troglodytes) as endangered under the Endangered Species Act of 1973, as amended (Act). We are taking this action in response to a petition to list the entire species, whether in the wild or in captivity, as endangered under the Act.
