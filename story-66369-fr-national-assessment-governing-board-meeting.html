BREAKING: Federal Register: National Assessment Governing Board; Meeting

Published: 2023-05-01 | Source: Federal Register

This notice sets forth the agenda, time, and instructions to access or participate in the National Assessment Governing Board (hereafter referred to as Governing Board or Board) meetings scheduled for May 18-19, 2023, in Los Angeles, CA. This is a hybrid meeting, accessible both in person and virtually by advance registration 5 working days prior to each meeting via www.nagb.gov.
