BREAKING: Federal Register: Petition for Rulemaking To Adopt Revised Competitive Switching Rules

Published: 2012-07-31 | Source: Federal Register

The Surface Transportation Board (the Board) is commencing a proceeding to consider a proposal submitted by The National Industrial Transportation League (NITL) to increase rail-to-rail competition. The Board is seeking empirical evidence about the impact of the proposal, if it were to be adopted.
