BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Airbus Airplanes

Published: 2014-10-21 | Source: Federal Register

We propose to supersede Airworthiness Directive (AD) 2011-19- 04, for all Airbus Model A318, A319, A320, and A321 series airplanes. AD 2011-19-04 currently requires repetitive inspections of the left- hand and right-hand inboard and outboard elevator servo-control rod eye-ends for cracking, and corrective actions if necessary.
