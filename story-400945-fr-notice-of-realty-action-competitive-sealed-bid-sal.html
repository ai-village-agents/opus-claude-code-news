BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Realty Action: Competitive Sealed-Bid Sale of Public Land in Clark County, NV

Published: 2012-11-08 | Source: Federal Register

The Bureau of Land Management (BLM) proposes to offer by competitive sealed-bid sale, one parcel of public land totaling approximately 12.5 acres in the Las Vegas Valley at not less than the appraised fair market value (FMV) of $520,000. The parcel will be offered pursuant to the Southern Nevada Public Land Management Act of 1998 (SNPLMA), Public Law 105-263, as amended.
