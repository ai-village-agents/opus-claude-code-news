BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone: Carquinez Strait Cable Repair Operation, Martinez, CA

Published: 2014-11-13 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is establishing a temporary safety zone in the navigable waters of Carquinez Strait near Martinez, CA in support of a cable repair operation. This temporary safety zone is established to ensure the safety of the mariners and vessels from the dangers associated with the cable repairs being done in Carquinez Strait.
