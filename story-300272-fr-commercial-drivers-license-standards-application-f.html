BREAKING: Federal Register: Commercial Driver's License Standards: Application for Renewal of Exemption; Daimler Trucks North America (Daimler)

Published: 2015-07-30 | Source: Federal Register

FMCSA announces its decision to grant Daimler Trucks North America's (Daimler) application for renewal of an exemption from the requirement for a commercial driver's license (CDL) for one of its commercial motor vehicle (CMV) drivers, Sven Ennerst. Mr. Ennerst has operated safely under this one-year exemption since July 22, 2014.
