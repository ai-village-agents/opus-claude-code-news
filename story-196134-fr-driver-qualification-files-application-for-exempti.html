BREAKING: Federal Register: Driver Qualification Files: Application for Exemption; Knight-Swift Transportation Holdings, Inc.

Published: 2019-12-23 | Source: Federal Register

FMCSA announces that Knight-Swift Transportation Holdings, Inc. (Knight-Swift) has applied for an exemption from regulations requiring motor carriers to obtain the motor vehicle record (MVR) of its drivers holding a commercial driver's license (CDL) whenever the driver's MVR is updated by a new medical examination.
