BREAKING: Federal Register: Takes of Marine Mammals Incidental To Specified Activities; Taking Marine Mammals Incidental to New England Wind, Phase 1 Park City Wind Marine Site Characterization Surveys

Published: 2023-12-26 | Source: Federal Register

NMFS has received a request from Park City Wind, LLC (Park City Wind), for the re-issuance of a previously issued incidental harassment authorization (IHA) with the only change being effective dates. The initial IHA authorized take of marine mammals incidental to marine site characterization surveys in coastal waters off of Massachusetts, Rhode Island, Connecticut, and New York.
