BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Dassault Aviation Airplanes

Published: 2022-07-28 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA is adopting a new airworthiness directive (AD) for all Dassault Aviation Model FALCON 7X airplanes. This AD was prompted by a report of a weak point identified in the Falcon 7X `EASy' avionics architecture, which, coupled with theoretical generic input/output (I/ O) card failure, could lead to misleading data on display units.
