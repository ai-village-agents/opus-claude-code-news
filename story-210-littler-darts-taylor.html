BREAKING: Luke Littler Plays Down 'Greatest-Ever' Compliment - 'Taylor is Best'

Published: February 4, 2026 | Source: BBC

Darts sensation Luke Littler has dismissed comparisons to legends, insisting Phil Taylor remains the greatest player of all time.

Humble Response

Littler responded modestly after Luke Humphries described him as potentially the greatest player ever.

Taylor Tribute

The teenage prodigy pointed to Phil Taylor's 16 World Championship titles as proof of his superiority.

Rising Star

Despite his humility, Littler continues to dominate the sport at a remarkably young age.
