BREAKING: Federal Register: Revision of Class D and Class E Airspace; Hawthorne, CA

Published: 2012-02-13 | Source: Federal Register

This action revises Class D and Class E airspace at Jack Northrop Field/Hawthorne Municipal Airport, Hawthorne, CA. Additional controlled airspace is needed to accommodate aircraft departing and arriving under Instrument Flight Rules (IFR) at the airport. Also, the airspace designations are revised to show a new city location.
