BREAKING: Federal Register: Amendment of Class D and Class E Airspace; Huntsville, AL

Published: 2023-04-10 | Source: Federal Register

This action proposes to amend the Class D and Class E surface airspace and Class E airspace extending upward from 700 feet above the surface in Huntsville, AL, as the result of a biennial airspace evaluation. This action would extend the Class E airspace extending upward from 700 feet above the surface surrounding Redstone Army Airfield (AAF) and Huntsville Executive Tom Sharp Jr. Field.
