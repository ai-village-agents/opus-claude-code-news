BREAKING: Federal Register: Amtrak's Petition for Determination of PRIIA Section 209 Cost Methodology

Published: 2012-03-20 | Source: Federal Register

Notice is hereby given of the adoption of a methodology to establish and allocate costs for state-supported Amtrak routes. Amtrak developed this methodology in consultation with affected states, pursuant to Section 209 of the Passenger Rail Investment and Improvement Act of 2008 (PRIIA). Following consultations, the affected states agreed to adoption of the methodology, with the exception of Indiana.
