BREAKING: Federal Register: Privacy Act of 1974; System of Records

Published: 2020-10-29 | Source: Federal Register

The OSD is modifying a system of records titled, "Science, Mathematics, and Research for Transformation (SMART) Information Management System," DUSDA 14. This system of records enables the SMART program officials to select qualified scholarship applicants and monitor their progress in the program.
