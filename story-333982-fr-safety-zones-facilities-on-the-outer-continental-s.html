BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zones, Facilities on the Outer Continental Shelf in the Gulf of Mexico

Published: 2014-09-04 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is establishing safety zones around four Chevron North America (Chevron) facilities located on the Outer Continental Shelf (OCS) in the Gulf of Mexico. The facilities are listed in the Supplementary Information.The purpose of these safety zones is to protect each facility from vessels operating outside the normal shipping channels and fairways.
