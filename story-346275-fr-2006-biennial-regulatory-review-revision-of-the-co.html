BREAKING: Federal Register: 2006 Biennial Regulatory Review-Revision of the Commission's Rules

Published: 2013-02-06 | Source: Federal Register

In this document, the Federal Communications Commission amends various provisions of the Commission's rules pertaining to licensing and operation of satellite service radio stations. With two exceptions, the amendments are non-substantive; that is, they neither impose new requirements nor eliminate or alter existing requirements.
