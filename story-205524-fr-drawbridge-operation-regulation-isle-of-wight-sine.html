BREAKING: Federal Register: Drawbridge Operation Regulation; Isle of Wight (Sinepuxent) Bay, Ocean City, MD

Published: 2018-04-26 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard has issued a temporary deviation from the operating schedule that governs the US 50/Harry W. Kelly Memorial Bridge, which carries US 50 and Ocean Gateway across the Isle of Wight (Sinepuxent) Bay, mile 0.5, at Ocean City, MD. The deviation is necessary to facilitate the "Island to Island" Half Marathon. This deviation allows the bridge to remain in the closed-to-navigation position.
