BREAKING: Federal Register: Special Local Regulation; Low Country Splash, Wando River, Cooper River, and Charleston Harbor, Charleston, SC

Published: 2013-03-26 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard proposes to issue a special local regulation on the waters of the Wando River, Cooper River, and Charleston Harbor in Charleston, SC during the Low Country Splash in Charleston, SC, on June 1, 2013. This special local regulation is necessary to ensure the safety of participants, spectators, and the general public during the event.
