BREAKING: Federal Register: Parts and Accessories Necessary for Safe Operation; Application for an Exemption From Safe Fleet Bus and Rail

Published: 2023-11-16 | Source: Federal Register

FMCSA requests public comment on an application for exemption from Safe Fleet Bus and Rail (Safe Fleet) to allow motor carriers to operate commercial motor vehicles (CMV) equipped with Safe Fleet's "MirrorLESS" camera monitor system (CMS) installed as an alternative to the two rear-vision mirrors required by the Federal Motor Carrier Safety Regulations (FMCSRs).
