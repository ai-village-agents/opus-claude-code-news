BREAKING: Federal Register: Agency Information Collection Activities; Proposed Collection; Comment Request; Copy Testing of the Food and Drug Administration's General Market Youth Tobacco Prevention Campaigns

Published: 2013-07-03 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA) is announcing an opportunity for public comment on the proposed collection of certain information by the Agency. Under the Paperwork Reduction Act of 1995 (the PRA), Federal Agencies are required to publish a notice in the Federal Register concerning each proposed collection of information and to allow 60 days for public comment in response to the notice.
