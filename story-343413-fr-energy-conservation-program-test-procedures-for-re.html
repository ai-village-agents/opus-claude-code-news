BREAKING: Federal Register: Energy Conservation Program: Test Procedures for Residential Clothes Dryers

Published: 2013-01-02 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Department of Energy (DOE) proposes to revise its test procedures for residential clothes dryers established under the Energy Policy and Conservation Act. The proposed amendments to appendix D1 would include testing methods for more accurately measuring the effects of automatic cycle termination.
