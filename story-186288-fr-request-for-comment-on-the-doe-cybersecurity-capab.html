BREAKING: Federal Register: Request for Comment on the DOE Cybersecurity Capability Maturity Model Version 2.0

Published: 2019-08-14 | Source: Federal Register

Through this notice, the Department of Energy (DOE) seeks comments and information from the public on enhancements to the Cybersecurity Capability Maturity Model (C2M2) Version 2.0. C2M2 Version 2.0 incorporates enhancements to align model domains and functional questions with internationally-recognized cyber standards and best practices, including the NIST Cybersecurity Framework Version 1.1 released in April 2018.
