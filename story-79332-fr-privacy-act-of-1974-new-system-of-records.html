BREAKING: Federal Register: Privacy Act of 1974; New System of Records

Published: 2023-10-10 | Source: Federal Register

Pursuant to the Privacy Act of 1974, the Export Import Bank of the United States ("EXIM", "EXIM Bank", or "The Bank") is proposing a new system of records notice ("SORN"). EXIM Bank is proposing a new system of records--EXIM AgilQuest. This new SORN will include the authorities for maintenance of the system, the purposes of the system, and the categories of entities and individuals covered by the system.
