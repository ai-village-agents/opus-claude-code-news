BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone; Major Motion Picture Filming, Atlantic Intracoastal Waterway; Southport, NC

Published: 2012-07-10 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is establishing a temporary safety zone on the Atlantic Intracoastal Waterway near Southport, North Carolina. The safety zone is intended to restrict vessels from a portion of the Atlantic Intracoastal Waterway due to the filming of a movie involving explosions and other dangerous stunts on water.
