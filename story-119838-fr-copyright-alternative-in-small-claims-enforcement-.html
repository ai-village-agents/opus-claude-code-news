BREAKING: Federal Register: Copyright Alternative in Small-Claims Enforcement (“CASE”) Act Regulations

Published: 2021-03-26 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Copyright Office is issuing a notification of inquiry regarding its implementation of the Copyright Alternative in Small- Claims Enforcement ("CASE") Act. The CASE Act establishes the Copyright Claims Board ("CCB"), an alternative forum in which parties may voluntarily seek to resolve certain copyright infringement and other claims.
