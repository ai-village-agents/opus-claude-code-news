BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Suspension and Commencement of Proposed Debarment Proceedings; Federal Lifeline Program

Published: 2024-12-12 | Source: Federal Register

The Enforcement Bureau (Bureau) gives notice of the suspension of Q Link Wireless LLC (Q Link) from the federal Lifeline program (Lifeline Program) and all federal universal service support mechanisms. Additionally, the Bureau gives notice that debarment proceedings are commencing against Q Link.
