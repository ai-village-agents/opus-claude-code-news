BREAKING: Federal Register: Ammonium Acetate; Exemption From the Requirement of a Tolerance

Published: 2015-12-23 | Source: Federal Register

This regulation establishes an exemption from the requirement of a tolerance for residues of ammonium acetate (CAS No. 631-61-8) when used as an inert ingredient (buffering agent) limited to 15% in pesticide formulations applied to pre-harvested crops.
