BREAKING: Federal Register: Fund of Funds Arrangements

Published: 2020-11-19 | Source: Federal Register

The Securities and Exchange Commission (the "Commission") is adopting a new rule under the Investment Company Act of 1940 ("Investment Company Act" or "Act") to streamline and enhance the regulatory framework applicable to funds that invest in other funds ("fund of funds" arrangements).
