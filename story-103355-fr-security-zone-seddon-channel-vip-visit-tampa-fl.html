BREAKING: Federal Register: Security Zone; Seddon Channel, VIP Visit, Tampa, FL

Published: 2022-08-22 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is establishing a temporary security zone in the vicinity of the Tampa Convention Center, Tampa, FL, during a Government ceremony featuring several high-level officials. The security zone will cover all navigable waters of the Seddon Channel and Hillsborough River within 100 yards of the Tampa Convention Center, Tampa, FL. The security zone is necessary to protect the official party, the public, and the surrounding waterway from terrorist acts, sabotage, or other subversive acts,...
