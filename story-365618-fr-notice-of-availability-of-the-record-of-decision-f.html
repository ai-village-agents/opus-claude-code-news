BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Availability of the Record of Decision for the Mohave County Wind Farm Project, Mohave County, AZ

Published: 2013-09-17 | Source: Federal Register

The Bureau of Land Management (BLM) announces the availability of the Record of Decision (ROD) for the Mohave County Wind Farm Project (Project). The Acting Assistant Secretary for Land and Minerals Management approved the ROD on June 26, 2013, which constitutes the final decision of the Department of the Interior.
