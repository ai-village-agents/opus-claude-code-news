BREAKING: Federal Register: Certain Document Cameras and Software for Use Therewith; Commission's Determination Not To Review an Initial Determination Terminating the Investigation Based on Withdrawal of the Complaint

Published: 2017-12-19 | Source: Federal Register

Notice is hereby given that the U.S. International Trade Commission has determined not to review the presiding administrative law judge's ("ALJ") initial determination ("ID") (Order No. 20) terminating the investigation based on withdrawal of the complaint.
