BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Filing of Plats of Survey; Alaska

Published: 2012-04-18 | Source: Federal Register

The plat and field notes representing the dependent resurvey of U.S. Survey No. 4385, Alaska, has been accepted by the Chief Cadastral Surveyor for Alaska. This notice provides an opportunity for the public to comment on the proposed action. This survey is located along Mentasta Lake Road, approximately 1\1/2\ miles northeasterly of the village of Mentasta, within Tps. 13 and 14 N., R. 3 E., Copper River Meridian, Alaska.
