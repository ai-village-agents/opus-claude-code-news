BREAKING: Federal Register: Removal of Published Rules To Align Published Policy with Current Sources of Law

Published: 2015-03-17 | Source: Federal Register

The Executive Office of the President, Office of Administration, is removing regulations from the Code of Federal Regulations related to the status of records created and maintained by the Executive Office of the President.
