BREAKING: Federal Register: Regulated Navigation Area; Columbus Day Weekend, Biscayne Bay, Miami, FL

Published: 2012-07-25 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard proposes to establish a regulated navigation area on Biscayne Bay in Miami, Florida. The regulated navigation area will be enforced annually from Saturday of the second week through Monday of the third week in October (Columbus Day weekend). The regulated navigation area will include certain waters of Biscayne Bay between Rickenbacker Causeway Bridge and Coon Point on Elliot Key.
