BREAKING: Federal Register: Mt. Baker-Snoqualmie National Forest; Snohomish County, WA; Green Mountain Lookout Removal

Published: 2013-06-03 | Source: Federal Register

The Mt. Baker-Snoqualmie National Forest hereby gives notice that it is extending the public scoping comment period for the Green Mountain Lookout Removal Project. A notice was originally published in the Federal Register on May 2, 2013 (Volume 78, No. 85), beginning a 30 day comment period. Please see the Notice of Intent (FR Doc. 2013- 10322) for more information related to the project.
