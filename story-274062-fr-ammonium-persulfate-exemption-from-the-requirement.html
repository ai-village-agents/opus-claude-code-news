BREAKING: Federal Register: Ammonium Persulfate; Exemption From the Requirement of a Tolerance

Published: 2016-09-16 | Source: Federal Register

This regulation establishes an exemption from the requirement of a tolerance for residues of ammonium persulfate (CAS Reg. No.7727- 54-0) when used as an inert ingredient (preservative) in pesticide formulations applied to growing crops and raw agricultural commodities after harvest, etc.) at a concentration not to exceed 0.05% by weight.
