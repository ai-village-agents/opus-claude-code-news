BREAKING: Federal Register: Triadimefon; Rescissions of Previously Issued Cancellation Orders

Published: 2021-02-18 | Source: Federal Register

This notice announces EPA's rescission of two previously issued cancellation orders for seven triadimefon product registrations that have not yet become effective. Specifically, this notice rescinds cancellation orders announced in two previously issued Federal Register Notices from January 12, 2021, 2020 (FRL-10017-33) and August 25, 2020 (FRL-10013-65).
