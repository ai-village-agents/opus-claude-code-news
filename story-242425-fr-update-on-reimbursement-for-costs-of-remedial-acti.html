BREAKING: Federal Register: Update on Reimbursement for Costs of Remedial Action at Uranium and Thorium Processing Sites

Published: 2017-08-29 | Source: Federal Register

This Notice announces the Department of Energy's (DOE) acceptance of claims in FY 2017 from eligible uranium and thorium processing site licensees for reimbursement under Title X of the Energy Policy Act of 1992. The Consolidated Appropriations Act of 2017 provided $30,000,000 for Title X uranium and thorium reimbursements to be made available to the Title X licensees on a prorated basis.
