BREAKING: Federal Register: Drawbridge Operation Regulation; Pelican Island Causeway, Galveston Channel, TX

Published: 2014-06-09 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is extending the temporary final rule changing the operating schedule that governs the Pelican Island Causeway bridge across Galveston Channel mile 4.5 (GIWW mile 356.1), at Galveston, Texas. Extending this temporary final rule allows the bridge to remain closed to navigation for an additional six months while major repairs are conducted to the mechanical portions of the bascule span.
