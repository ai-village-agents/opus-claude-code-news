BREAKING: Federal Register: Privacy Act of 1974; System of Records

Published: 2024-01-17 | Source: Federal Register

Pursuant to the Privacy Act of 1974, the Consumer Product Safety Commission (CPSC) is proposing changes to one system of records notice (SORN). CPSC is proposing to amend CPSC-25--FOIAXpress System of Records (FOIAXpress).
