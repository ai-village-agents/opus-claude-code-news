BREAKING: Federal Register: Environmental Impact Statement for Dallas-Houston High Speed Passenger Rail Corridor

Published: 2014-06-25 | Source: Federal Register

FRA is issuing this notice to advise the public that FRA and the Texas Department of Transportation (TxDOT) intend to prepare an EIS pursuant to the National Environmental Policy Act of 1969 (NEPA) for the impacts of constructing and operating a dedicated high-speed rail (HSR) system (Proposed Action) proposed by the private company, Texas Central High-Speed Railway (TCR), for the Central Texas High-Speed Rail Corridor.
