BREAKING: Federal Register: Small Business Size Standards: Construction

Published: 2013-12-23 | Source: Federal Register

The United States Small Business Administration (SBA) is increasing two small business size standards in North American Industry Classification System (NAICS) Sector 23, Construction, and retaining the current standards for the 30 remaining industries in that Sector.
