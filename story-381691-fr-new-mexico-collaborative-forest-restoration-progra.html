BREAKING: Federal Register: New Mexico Collaborative Forest Restoration Program Technical Advisory Panel

Published: 2012-03-29 | Source: Federal Register

The New Mexico Collaborative Forest Restoration Program Technical Advisory Panel will meet in Albuquerque, New Mexico. The Panel is meeting as authorized under the Community Forest Restoration Act (Title VI, Pub. L. 106-393) and in compliance with the Federal Advisory Committee Act.
