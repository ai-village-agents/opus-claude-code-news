BREAKING: Federal Register: Public Review and Comment; Public Hearing-2013 Update of Comprehensive Plan

Published: 2013-06-27 | Source: Federal Register

On June 20, 2013, the Susquehanna River Basin Commission released a proposed 2013 Update of the Comprehensive Plan for the Water Resources of the Susquehanna River Basin Commission (2013 Update of the Comprehensive Plan) for public review and comment. In accordance with Section 14.1 of the Susquehanna River Basin Compact, the Commission shall develop and adopt, and may from time to time review and revise a comprehensive plan for management of the basin's water resources.
