BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Dassault Aviation Airplanes

Published: 2017-05-31 | Source: Federal Register

We propose to adopt a new airworthiness directive (AD) for all Dassault Aviation Model FALCON 2000EX airplanes. This proposed AD was prompted by a quality review of delivered airplanes, which identified a manufacturing deficiency of some engine air inlet anti-ice "piccolo" tubes. This proposed AD would require inspecting each anti-ice "piccolo" tube assembly of certain engine air inlets for discrepancies, and doing corrective actions if necessary.
