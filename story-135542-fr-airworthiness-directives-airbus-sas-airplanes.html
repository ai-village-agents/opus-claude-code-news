BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Airbus SAS Airplanes

Published: 2021-10-14 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA is superseding Airworthiness Directive (AD) 2019-19- 06, which applied to certain Airbus SAS Model A330-202, -243, -243F, - 302, -323, and -343 airplanes. AD 2019-19-06 required an inspection to determine the part number and serial number of the slat geared rotary actuators (SGRAs), and replacement of each affected SGRA with a serviceable part.
