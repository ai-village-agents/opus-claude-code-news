BREAKING: Federal Register: Science Advisory Committee on Chemicals; Establishment of a Federal Advisory Committee; Request for Nominations

Published: 2016-08-26 | Source: Federal Register

As required by section 9(a)(2) of the Federal Advisory Committee Act (FACA), EPA is giving notice that, pursuant to section 2625(o) of the Frank R. Lautenberg Chemical Safety for the 21st Century Act, the Agency is establishing the Science Advisory Committee on Chemicals (SACC).
