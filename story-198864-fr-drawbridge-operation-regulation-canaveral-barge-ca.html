BREAKING: Federal Register: Drawbridge Operation Regulation Canaveral Barge Canal, Canaveral, FL

Published: 2018-01-31 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is modifying the operating schedule that governs the SR 401 Drawbridges across the Canaveral Barge Canal, mile 5.5, at Port Canaveral, Florida. This modification is necessary to reduce vehicular traffic congestion and to ensure the safety of roadways while passengers are transiting to and from the cruise ship terminals.
