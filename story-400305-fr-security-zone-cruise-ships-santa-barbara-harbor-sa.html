BREAKING: Federal Register: Security Zone; Cruise Ships, Santa Barbara Harbor, Santa Barbara, CA

Published: 2012-10-30 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard has established fixed security zones around and under any cruise ships visiting Santa Barbara Harbor, Santa Barbara, California. This regulation was created for national security reasons to protect cruise ships, vessels, users of the waterway and the port from potential terrorist acts.
