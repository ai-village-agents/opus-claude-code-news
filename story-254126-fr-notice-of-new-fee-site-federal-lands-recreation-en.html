BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of New Fee Site Federal Lands Recreation Enhancement Act

Published: 2016-02-01 | Source: Federal Register

The Flathead National Forest is proposing to charge a $75 fee for the overnight rental for the Anna Creek Cabin. This site has not been available for recreation use prior to this date. Rentals of other cabins on the Flathead National Forest have shown that people appreciate and enjoy the availability of historic rental lookouts and cabins.
