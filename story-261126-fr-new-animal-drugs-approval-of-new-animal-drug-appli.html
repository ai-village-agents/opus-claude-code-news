BREAKING: Federal Register: New Animal Drugs; Approval of New Animal Drug Applications; Changes of Sponsorship

Published: 2016-04-18 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA, we) is amending the animal drug regulations to reflect application-related actions for new animal drug applications (NADAs) and abbreviated new animal drug applications (ANADAs) during January and February 2016. FDA is also informing the public of the availability of summaries of the basis of approval and of environmental review documents, where applicable.
