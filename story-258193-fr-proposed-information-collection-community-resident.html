BREAKING: Federal Register: Proposed Information Collection (Community Residential Care) Activity: Comment Request

Published: 2016-03-16 | Source: Federal Register

The Veterans Health Administration (VHA) is announcing an opportunity for public comment on the proposed collection of certain information by the agency. Under the Paperwork Reduction Act (PRA) of 1995, Federal agencies are required to publish notice in the Federal Register concerning each proposed collection of information, including each new collection of a currently approved collection, and allow 60 days for public comment in response to the notice.
