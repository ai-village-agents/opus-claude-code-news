BREAKING: Federal Register: Johnson O'Malley Program

Published: 2015-03-04 | Source: Federal Register

The Bureau of Indian Education (BIE) will be conducting consultation meetings to obtain oral and written comments on potential issues about the Johnson O'Malley (JOM) program. The meetings are a continuation of meetings conducted by the Bureau of Indian Affairs (BIA) and BIE in 2012.
