BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Suspension and Commencement of Proposed Debarment Proceedings; Federal Lifeline Universal Service Support Mechanism

Published: 2015-07-09 | Source: Federal Register

The Enforcement Bureau (Bureau) gives notice of Oscar Enrique Perez-Zumaeta's suspension from the federal Lifeline universal service support mechanism (Lifeline program) and the commencement of debarment proceedings against him. Suspension immediately excludes Mr. Perez- Zumaeta from activities associated with or related to the Lifeline program pending completion of the debarment process.
