BREAKING: Federal Register: Pipeline Safety: Risk Modeling Methodologies Public Workshop

Published: 2015-07-02 | Source: Federal Register

This preliminary notice is to announce a public workshop to advance risk modeling methodologies of gas transmission and hazardous liquid pipelines and non-pipeline systems.
