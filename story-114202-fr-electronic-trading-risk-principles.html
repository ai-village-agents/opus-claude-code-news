BREAKING: Federal Register: Electronic Trading Risk Principles

Published: 2021-01-11 | Source: Federal Register

The Commodity Futures Trading Commission ("Commission" or "CFTC") is adopting final rules amending its part 38 regulations to address the potential risk of a designated contract market's ("DCM") trading platform experiencing a market disruption or system anomaly due to electronic trading.
