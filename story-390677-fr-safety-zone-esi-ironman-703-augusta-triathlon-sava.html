BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone; ESI Ironman 70.3 Augusta Triathlon, Savannah River; Augusta, GA

Published: 2012-07-10 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard proposes to establish a temporary safety zone on the Savannah River in Augusta, Georgia during the ESI Ironman 70.3 Augusta Triathlon on Sunday, September 30, 2012. The temporary safety zone would be necessary for the safety of the race participants, participant vessels, spectators, and the general public during the 1.1 mile swim portion of the competition.
