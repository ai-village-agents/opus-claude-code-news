BREAKING: Federal Register: Recommendations for Microbial Vectors Used for Gene Therapy; Draft Guidance for Industry; Availability

Published: 2015-10-14 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA or Agency) is announcing the availability of a draft document entitled "Recommendations for Microbial Vectors Used for Gene Therapy; Draft Guidance for Industry." The draft guidance provides investigational new drug application (IND) sponsors, with recommendations concerning IND submissions for microbial vectors used for gene therapy (MVGTs) in early-phase clinical trials.
