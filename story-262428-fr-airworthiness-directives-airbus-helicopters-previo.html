BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Airbus Helicopters (Previously Eurocopter France)

Published: 2016-05-02 | Source: Federal Register

We are superseding airworthiness directive (AD) 2014-12-51 for Airbus Helicopters (previously Eurocopter France) Model EC130B4 and EC130T2 helicopters. AD 2014-12-51 required repetitively inspecting the tailboom to Fenestron junction frame (junction frame) for a crack.
