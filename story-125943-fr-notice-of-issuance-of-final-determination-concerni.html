BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Issuance of Final Determination Concerning Certain Fixed and Portable Patient Ceiling Lift Systems

Published: 2021-06-14 | Source: Federal Register

This document provides notice that U.S. Customs and Border Protection (CBP) has issued a final determination concerning the country of origin of certain fixed and portable patient ceiling lift systems that will be installed at a patient's residence or healthcare setting.
