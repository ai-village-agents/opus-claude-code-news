BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone; Sector Key West COTP Zone Post Storm Recovery, Atlantic Ocean, FL

Published: 2017-10-12 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard has established a temporary safety zone for certain waters within the Sector Key West Captain of the Port (COTP) Zone. Vessels are prohibited from entering into, anchoring, loitering, or movement within a safety zone around salvage or pollution removal vessels in the Florida Keys. These temporary regulations are necessary for the safety of persons, vessels, and property due to the large volume of debris, sunken vessels and salvage operations associated with Hurricane Irma.
