BREAKING: Federal Register: Potassium Salts of Hops Beta Acids; Exemption From the Requirement of a Tolerance

Published: 2015-10-21 | Source: Federal Register

This regulation establishes an exemption from the requirement of a tolerance for residues of the biochemical pesticide potassium salts of hops beta acids in or on honey and honeycomb for the control of Varroa mites in accordance with label directions and good agricultural practices.
