BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Public Meetings: Mojave-Southern Great Basin Resource Advisory Council, Nevada

Published: 2017-02-22 | Source: Federal Register

In accordance with the Federal Land Policy and Management Act and the Federal Advisory Committee Act of 1972, the Bureau of Land Management (BLM) Mojave-Southern Great Basin Resource Advisory Council (RAC) will meet as indicated below. The meeting is open to the public.
