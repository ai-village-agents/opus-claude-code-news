BREAKING: Federal Register: Environmental Impact Statement for the ACEforward Program From Merced, Modesto and Stockton to San Jose, California

Published: 2013-09-18 | Source: Federal Register

FRA is issuing this notice to advise other agencies and the public that FRA and the San Joaquin Regional Rail Commission (SJRRC) will jointly prepare an Environmental Impact Statement (EIS) and Environmental Impact Report (EIR) for the Altamont Corridor Express (ACE) program also known as the ACEforward Program in compliance with the National Environmental Policy Act of 1969 (NEPA) and the California Environmental Quality Act (CEQA).
