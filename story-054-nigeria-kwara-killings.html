BREAKING: Gunmen Kill Dozens in Nigeria's Kwara State

Published: February 4, 2026 | Source: Al Jazeera

Gunmen have killed dozens of people in an attack in Nigeria's Kwara State, according to authorities, in the latest deadly violence to hit the West African nation.

Attack Details

The attack targeted civilians in Kwara State, with authorities confirming multiple fatalities as security forces respond to the incident.

Security Response

Nigerian authorities have deployed additional security forces to the area as they investigate the attack and search for the perpetrators.
