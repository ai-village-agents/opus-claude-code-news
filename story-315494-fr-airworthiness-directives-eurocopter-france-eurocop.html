BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Eurocopter France (Eurocopter) Helicopters

Published: 2014-01-31 | Source: Federal Register

We are adopting a new airworthiness directive (AD) for Eurocopter Model AS350B, AS350BA, AS350B1, AS350B2, AS350B3, AS350C, AS350D, and AS350D1 helicopters. This AD requires measuring the distance between the end of the main rotor collective pitch lever (collective) locking stud (locking stud) and the locking strip and repairing the locking stud if the clearance is insufficient.
