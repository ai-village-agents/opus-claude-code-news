BREAKING: Federal Register: Changes to Production Certificates and Approvals; Correction

Published: 2016-01-13 | Source: Federal Register

The Federal Aviation Administration (FAA) is correcting a final rule correction published on December 17, 2015. In that correction, the FAA changed the effective date of the final rule to permit an earlier implementation of the rule's provisions that allow production approval holders to issue authorized release documents for aircraft engines, propellers, and articles.
