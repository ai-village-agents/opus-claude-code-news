BREAKING: Federal Register: Consolidated Guidance About Materials Licenses: Program-Specific Guidance About Licenses of Broad Scope

Published: 2017-04-05 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Nuclear Regulatory Commission (NRC) has issued Revision 1 to NUREG-1556, Volume 11, "Consolidated Guidance about Materials Licenses: Program-Specific Guidance about Licenses of Broad Scope," which updates licensing guidance for broad scope licenses. This document has been revised to include information on updated regulatory requirements, safety culture, security of radioactive materials, protection of sensitive information, and changes in regulatory policies and practices.
