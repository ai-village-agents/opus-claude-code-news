BREAKING: Federal Register: Waterway Suitability Assessment for Expansion of Liquefied Natural Gas Facility; Ingleside, TX

Published: 2016-08-15 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard, at Sector Corpus Christi, announces receipt of a Letter of Intent (LOI) and Waterways Suitability Assessment (WSA) for a construction project expanding a Federal Energy Regulatory Commission (FERC) approved Liquefied Natural Gas (LNG) facility in Ingleside, Texas. The LOI and WSA were submitted by Rodino, Inc. on behalf of Cheniere's Corpus Christi Liquefaction (CCL) Project.
