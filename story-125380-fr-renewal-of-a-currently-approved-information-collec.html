BREAKING: Federal Register: Renewal of a Currently Approved Information Collection for the Energy Efficiency and Conservation Block Grant Financing Programs

Published: 2021-06-07 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Energy (DOE), pursuant to the Paperwork Reduction Act of 1995, intends to extend for three years a currently approved collection of information with the Office of Management and Budget (OMB). The information collection request, Energy Efficiency and Conservation Block Grant Program, was previously approved under OMB Control No. 1910-5150 and its current expiration date is September 30, 2021.
