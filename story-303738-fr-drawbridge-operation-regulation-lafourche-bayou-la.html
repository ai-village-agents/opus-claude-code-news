BREAKING: Federal Register: Drawbridge Operation Regulation; Lafourche Bayou, Larose, LA

Published: 2015-09-09 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard has issued a temporary deviation from the operating schedule that governs the operation of the T-Bois bridge, a vertical lift-span bridge across Lafourche Bayou, mile 40.4, at Larose, Louisiana. The deviation is necessary in order to allow for the smooth flow of increased vehicular traffic caused by the temporary closure of the LA 1 bridge across the Gulf Intracoastal Waterway.
