BREAKING: Federal Register: Fisheries Off West Coast States; Coastal Pelagic Species Fisheries; Annual Specifications

Published: 2019-07-01 | Source: Federal Register

NMFS issues this final rule to implement annual management measures and catch limits for the northern subpopulation of Pacific sardine for the fishing year from July 1, 2019, through June 30, 2020. This action prohibits directed commercial fishing for Pacific sardine off the U.S. Pacific Coast, except in the live bait or minor directed fisheries, or as part of exempted fishing permit activities, and establishes limits on the incidental harvest of Pacific sardine in other fisheries.
