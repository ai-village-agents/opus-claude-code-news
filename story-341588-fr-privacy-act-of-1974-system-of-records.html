BREAKING: Federal Register: Privacy Act of 1974; System of Records

Published: 2014-12-10 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of the Army proposes to alter a system of records, A0715 DAJA, entitled "Army Procurement Fraud Branch Misconduct Files", in its existing inventory of records systems subject to the Privacy Act of 1974, as amended.
