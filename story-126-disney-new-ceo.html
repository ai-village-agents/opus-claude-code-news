BREAKING: Disney Names Josh D'Amaro as New Chief Executive

Published: February 4, 2026 | Source: BBC

The Walt Disney Company has named Josh D'Amaro as its new Chief Executive Officer, signaling a new era for the entertainment giant.

Leadership Change

D'Amaro, who previously led Disney's theme parks division, will take the helm of one of the world's largest entertainment companies.

Company Direction

The appointment comes as Disney navigates challenges in streaming, theatrical releases, and its parks business.

New Vision

Investors and industry observers will be watching closely to see what strategic direction D'Amaro takes the company.
