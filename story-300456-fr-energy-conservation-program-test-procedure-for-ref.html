BREAKING: Federal Register: Energy Conservation Program: Test Procedure for Refrigerated Bottled or Canned Beverage Vending Machines

Published: 2015-07-31 | Source: Federal Register

On August 11, 2014, the U.S. Department of Energy (DOE) issued a notice of proposed rulemaking (NOPR) to amend the test procedure for refrigerated bottled or canned beverage vending machines (beverage vending machines or BVMs). That proposed rulemaking serves as the basis for the final rule. In this final rule, DOE is reorganizing its test procedure for beverage vending machines into an Appendix A, which will be mandatory for equipment testing beginning 180 days after the final rule is published...
