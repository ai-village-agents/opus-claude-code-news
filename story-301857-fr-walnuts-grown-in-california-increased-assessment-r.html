BREAKING: Federal Register: Walnuts Grown in California; Increased Assessment Rate

Published: 2015-08-18 | Source: Federal Register

This proposed rule would implement a recommendation from the California Walnut Board (Board) to increase the assessment rate established for the 2015-16 and subsequent marketing years from $0.0189 to $0.0379 per kernelweight pound of assessable walnuts. The Board locally administers the marketing order and is comprised of growers and handlers of walnuts operating within the area of production.
