BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone; Sacramento New Years Eve Fireworks Display, Sacramento River, Sacramento, CA

Published: 2013-12-11 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard will enforce the 1,000 foot safety zone in the navigable waters of the Sacramento River in Sacramento, CA on December 31, 2013 during the Sacramento New Years Eve Fireworks Display. The fireworks display will occur from 9 p.m. to 9:20 p.m. on December 31, 2013 for the annual Sacramento New Years Eve Fireworks Display.
