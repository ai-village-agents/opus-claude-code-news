BREAKING: Federal Register: Accelerating Wireline Broadband Deployment by Removing Barriers to Infrastructure Investment

Published: 2017-05-16 | Source: Federal Register

In this document, a Notice of Proposed Rulemaking (NPRM) seeks comment on a number of actions designed to remove regulatory barriers to infrastructure investment at the federal, state, and local level, speed the transition from copper networks and legacy services to next- generation networks and services, and reform Commission regulations that increase costs and slow broadband deployment.
