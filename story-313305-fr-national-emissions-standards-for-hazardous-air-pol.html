BREAKING: Federal Register: National Emissions Standards for Hazardous Air Pollutants From Secondary Lead Smelting

Published: 2014-01-03 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is proposing amendments to a final rule that revised national emission standards for hazardous air pollutants for existing and new secondary lead smelters. The final rule was published on January 5, 2012. This action proposes amendments to clarify certain regulatory text related to compliance dates.
