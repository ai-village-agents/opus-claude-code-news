BREAKING: Federal Register: Kiwifruit Grown in California; Order Amending Marketing Order No. 920

Published: 2014-05-28 | Source: Federal Register

This final rule amends Marketing Order No. 920 (order), which regulates the handling of kiwifruit grown in California. The amendments were proposed by the Kiwifruit Administrative Committee (Committee or KAC), which is responsible for the local administration of the order.
