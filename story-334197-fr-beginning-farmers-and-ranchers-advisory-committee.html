BREAKING: Federal Register: Beginning Farmers and Ranchers Advisory Committee

Published: 2014-09-08 | Source: Federal Register

Pursuant to the Federal Advisory Committee Act (FACA), the Office of Advocacy and Outreach (OAO) is announcing a meeting of the Beginning Farmers and Ranchers Advisory Committee (Committee). The Committee is being convened to consider issues involving communications, service, and advocacy in identifying barriers for beginning farmers and ranchers.
