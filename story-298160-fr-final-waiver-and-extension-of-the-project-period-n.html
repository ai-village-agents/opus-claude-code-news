BREAKING: Federal Register: Final Waiver and Extension of the Project Period; Native American Career and Technical Education Program

Published: 2015-07-06 | Source: Federal Register

For the 24-month projects funded in fiscal year (FY) 2013 under the Native American Career and Technical Education Program (NACTEP), the Secretary waives the requirements that generally prohibit project period extensions involving the obligation of additional Federal funds. The Secretary also extends the project periods of these grants for up to an additional 24 months.
