BREAKING: Federal Register: Aluminum Extrusions From the People's Republic of China: Initiation of Changed Circumstances Reviews and Consideration of Revocation of the Antidumping and Countervailing Duty Orders in Part

Published: 2013-08-20 | Source: Federal Register

In response to a request by 3M Company (3M), a U.S. importer of certain rectangular wire, the Department of Commerce (the Department) is initiating changed circumstances reviews of the antidumping duty (AD) and countervailing (CVD) duty orders on aluminum extrusions from the People's Republic of China. Interested parties are invited to comment on this notice of initiation.
