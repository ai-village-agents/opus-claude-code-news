BREAKING: Federal Register: Disguised Payments for Services

Published: 2015-07-23 | Source: Federal Register

This document contains proposed regulations relating to disguised payments for services under section 707(a)(2)(A) of the Internal Revenue Code. The proposed regulations provide guidance to partnerships and their partners regarding when an arrangement will be treated as a disguised payment for services.
