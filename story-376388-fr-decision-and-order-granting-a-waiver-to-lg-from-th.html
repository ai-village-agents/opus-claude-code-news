BREAKING: Federal Register: Decision and Order Granting a Waiver to LG From the Department of Energy Clothes Washer Test Procedure

Published: 2012-02-01 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Department of Energy (DOE) gives notice of the decision and order (Case No. CW-022) that grants to LG Electronics U.S.A., Inc. (LG) a waiver from the DOE clothes washer test procedure for determining the energy consumption of clothes washers for the basic models set forth in its petition for waiver.
