BREAKING: Federal Register: Electricity Subsector Cybersecurity Risk Management Process Guideline

Published: 2012-03-07 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Energy (DOE) invites public comment on DOE's intent to publish the Electricity Subsector Cybersecurity Risk Management Process guideline. The guideline describes a risk management process that is targeted to the specific needs of electricity sector organizations.
