BREAKING: Federal Register: Reports by Air Carriers on Incidents Involving Animals During Air Transport

Published: 2014-07-03 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Transportation (DOT or Department) is issuing a final rule to amend the requirement for air carriers to report incidents involving the loss, injury, or death of an animal during air transport.
