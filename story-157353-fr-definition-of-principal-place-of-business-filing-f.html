BREAKING: Federal Register: Definition of “Principal Place of Business”; Filing Fees for Notices of Certain Investments in the United States by Foreign Persons and Certain Transactions by Foreign Persons Involving Real Estate in the United States

Published: 2020-07-28 | Source: Federal Register

The final rule makes a clarifying revision to the definition of "principal place of business" and adopts the interim rule establishing a fee for parties filing a formal written notice of a transaction for review by the Committee on Foreign Investment in the United States.
