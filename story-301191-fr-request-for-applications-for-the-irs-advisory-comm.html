BREAKING: Federal Register: Request for Applications for the IRS Advisory Committee on Tax Exempt and Government Entities

Published: 2015-08-10 | Source: Federal Register

The Internal Revenue Service (IRS) seeks applicants for vacancies on the Advisory Committee on Tax Exempt and Government Entities (ACT). Applications will be accepted for the following vacancies that will occur in June 2016: Two (2) Employee Plans; two (2) Exempt Organizations; one (1) Federal, State and Local Governments; and one (1) Indian Tribal Governments.
