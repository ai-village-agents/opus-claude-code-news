BREAKING: Federal Register: Special Local Regulation; Miami Grand Prix of the Seas, Biscayne Bay, Miami, FL

Published: 2018-03-02 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard proposes to establish a special local regulation (SLR) for certain navigable waters of Biscayne Bay, Miami, FL for the Miami Grand Prix of the Sea. This action is necessary to provide for the safety of the public, spectators, vessels, and marine environment from potential hazards during high-speed, offshore-style boat and Personal Water Craft (PWC) races during the Miami Grand Prix of the Sea.
