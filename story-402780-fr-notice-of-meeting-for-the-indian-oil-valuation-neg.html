BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Meeting for the Indian Oil Valuation Negotiated Rulemaking Committee

Published: 2012-12-04 | Source: Federal Register

The Office of Natural Resources Revenue (ONRR) announces additional meetings for the Indian Oil Valuation Negotiated Rulemaking Committee (Committee). The seventh through ninth meetings of the Committee will take place on January 15 and 16, March 5 and 6, and April 17 and 18, 2013, in Building 85 of the Denver Federal Center.
