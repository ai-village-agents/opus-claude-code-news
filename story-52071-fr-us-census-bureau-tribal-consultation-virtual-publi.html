BREAKING: Federal Register: U.S. Census Bureau Tribal Consultation; Virtual Public Meeting

Published: 2024-11-12 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Census Bureau (Census Bureau) will conduct two virtual Tribal consultations on updating the race/ethnicity code list for the American Community Survey and the 2030 Census on December 11, 2024, and January 15, 2025. The two virtual Tribal consultation webinars will reflect the Census Bureau's continuous commitment to strengthen government-to-government relationships with federally recognized Tribes.
