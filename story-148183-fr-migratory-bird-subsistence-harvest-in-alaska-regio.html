BREAKING: Federal Register: Migratory Bird Subsistence Harvest in Alaska; Region-Specific Regulations

Published: 2020-04-02 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Fish and Wildlife Service (Service or we) is establishing regulations for the subsistence harvest of migratory birds in Alaska for the 2020 season and beyond. These regulations allow for the continuation of customary and traditional subsistence uses of migratory birds in Alaska and prescribe regional information on when and where the harvesting of birds may occur.
