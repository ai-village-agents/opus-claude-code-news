BREAKING: Federal Register: Pass-Through Share Insurance for Interest on Lawyers Trust Accounts

Published: 2015-12-28 | Source: Federal Register

The NCUA Board (Board) is amending its share insurance regulations to implement statutory amendments to the Federal Credit Union Act (FCU Act or the Act) resulting from the recent enactment of the Credit Union Share Insurance Fund Parity Act (Insurance Parity Act). The statutory amendments require NCUA to provide enhanced, pass- through share insurance for interest on lawyers trust accounts (IOLTA) and other similar escrow accounts.
