BREAKING: Federal Register: Extension of Port Limits of Indianapolis, IN

Published: 2012-04-25 | Source: Federal Register

U.S. Customs and Border Protection (CBP) is proposing to extend the geographical limits of the port of entry of Indianapolis, Indiana. The proposed extension will make the boundaries more easily identifiable to the public and will allow for uniform and continuous service to the extended area of Indianapolis, Indiana.
