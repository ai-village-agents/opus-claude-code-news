BREAKING: Federal Register: Obsolete Grant and Contract Regulations

Published: 2025-08-18 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Labor (Department) is confirming the effective date of September 2, 2025, for the direct final rule that was published in the Federal Register on July 1, 2025. This direct final rule removed obsolete grant and contract regulations in the Department of Labor's regulations.
