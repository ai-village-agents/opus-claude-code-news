BREAKING: Federal Register: The World Radiocommunication Conference Advisory Committee Schedules Its Fifth Meeting on February 15, 2022

Published: 2022-01-04 | Source: Federal Register

In accordance with the Federal Advisory Committee Act, this notice advises interested persons that the fifth meeting of the World Radiocommunication Conference Advisory Committee (WAC or Advisory Committee) meeting will be held on Tuesday, February 15, 2022 at 11:00 EST. Due to exceptional circumstances, the fifth WAC meeting will be convened as a virtual meeting with remote participation only. The meeting is open to the public.
