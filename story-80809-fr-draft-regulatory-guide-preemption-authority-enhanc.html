BREAKING: Federal Register: Draft Regulatory Guide: Preemption Authority, Enhanced Weapons Authority, and Firearms Background Checks

Published: 2023-10-30 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Nuclear Regulatory Commission (NRC) is issuing for public comment a draft Regulatory Guide (DG), DG-5081, "Preemption Authority, Enhanced Weapons Authority, and Firearms Background Checks." This DG-5081 is proposed Revision 1 of Regulatory Guide (RG) 5.86 of the same name.
