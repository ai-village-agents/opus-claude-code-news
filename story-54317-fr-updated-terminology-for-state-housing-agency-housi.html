BREAKING: Federal Register: Updated Terminology for State Housing Agency Housing Assistance Payments Contracts

Published: 2024-12-04 | Source: Federal Register

This final rule revises HUD's regulations for Housing Assistance Payments contracts that were initially issued and administered by a State Housing Finance Agency. This final rule clarifies the meaning of the terms "HFA (Housing Finance Agency)" and "State Agency (Agency)" when HUD either assumes contract administration responsibilities or assigns the contract administration responsibilities to a Performance-Based Contract Administrator.
