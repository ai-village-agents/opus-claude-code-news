BREAKING: Federal Register: Cooperative Research and Development Agreement: Cellular Phone Geolocation Development

Published: 2017-12-01 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard announces its intent to enter into a Cooperative Research and Development Agreement (CRADA) with TriaSys Technologies Corp, to investigate the potential operational use of cellular phone direction finding technology. The intent to enter in a potential CRADA with TriaSys Corp is based on market research and visits to vendors with advertised expertise in this unique application of technology in the maritime environment for Search and Rescue.
