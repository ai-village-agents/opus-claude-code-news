BREAKING: Federal Register: Final Waiver and Extension of the Project Period for the Opportunity Scholarship Program

Published: 2018-04-03 | Source: Federal Register

For projects funded under the DC Opportunity Scholarship Program (OSP), the Secretary: (1) Waives the requirements of the Education Department General Administrative Regulations that generally prohibit project period extensions involving the obligation of additional Federal funds; and (2) extends the project period for the current OSP grantee for up to an additional 24 months under the existing program authority.
