BREAKING: Federal Register: Approval and Promulgation of Implementation Plans; State of California; Sacramento Metro Area; Attainment Plan for 1997 8-Hour Ozone Standard

Published: 2014-10-15 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is proposing to approve state implementation plan (SIP) revisions submitted by the State of California to provide for attainment of the 1997 8-hour ozone national ambient air quality standard ("standard" or NAAQS) in the Sacramento Metro nonattainment area.
