BREAKING: Federal Register: Meetings of the Small Communities Advisory Subcommittee and the Local Government Advisory Committee

Published: 2012-01-18 | Source: Federal Register

The Small Communities Advisory Subcommittee (SCAS) will meet via teleconference on Monday, February 6, 2012, 1 p.m.-2 p.m. (ET). The Subcommittee will discuss sustainable communities, drinking water regulations and other issues and recommendations to the Administrator regarding environmental issues affecting small communities.
