BREAKING: Federal Register: Coast Guard Sector Juneau; Sector Name Conforming Amendment

Published: 2023-10-05 | Source: Federal Register

This rule makes non-substantive changes to Coast Guard regulations in association with a change in the Coast Guard's internal organization. The purpose of this rule is to reflect that U.S. Coast Guard Sector Juneau has been renamed U.S. Coast Guard Sector Southeast Alaska. This rule will have no substantive effect on the regulated public.
