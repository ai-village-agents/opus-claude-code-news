BREAKING: Federal Register: Endangered and Threatened Wildlife and Plants; Endangered Species Status for Amur Sturgeon

Published: 2022-04-04 | Source: Federal Register

We, the U.S. Fish and Wildlife Service (Service), are reopening the public comment period on our August 25, 2021, proposed rule to list the Amur sturgeon (Acipenser schrenckii), a fish species from the Amur River basin in Russia and China, as an endangered species under the Endangered Species Act of 1973, as amended (Act).
