BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Bombardier, Inc., Airplanes

Published: 2012-03-27 | Source: Federal Register

We propose to adopt a new airworthiness directive (AD) for certain Bombardier, Inc., Model DHC-8-400, -401, and -402 airplanes. This proposed AD was prompted by reports of cracking of certain fuel access panels of the outer wing.
