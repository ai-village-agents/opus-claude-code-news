BREAKING: Federal Register: South Carolina: Final Authorization of State Hazardous Waste Management Program Revisions

Published: 2020-11-20 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency is granting South Carolina final authorization for changes to its hazardous waste program under the Resource Conservation and Recovery Act (RCRA). The Agency published a Proposed Rule on September 11, 2020 and provided for public comment. The Agency received one comment in support of authorizing the South Carolina program changes.
