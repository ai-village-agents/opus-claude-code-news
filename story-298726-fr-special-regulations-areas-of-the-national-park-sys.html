BREAKING: Federal Register: Special Regulations, Areas of the National Park System, Lake Chelan National Recreation Area, Solid Waste Disposal

Published: 2015-07-13 | Source: Federal Register

The National Park Service proposes to authorize a solid waste transfer station near Stehekin, Washington, within the boundary of Lake Chelan National Recreation Area, that does not meet all regulatory siting criteria and accepts solid waste generated within the boundary of the recreation area from non-NPS activities.
