BREAKING: Federal Register: Energy Conservation Program: Energy Conservation Program: Energy Conservation Standards for Microwave Ovens

Published: 2025-05-16 | Source: Federal Register

DOE is proposing to rescind the energy conservation standards for microwave ovens. The rescission will eliminate the efficiency requirements for microwave ovens.
