BREAKING: Federal Register: Update to the Department of Homeland Security STEM Designated Degree Program List

Published: 2023-07-12 | Source: Federal Register

This notice announces that the Secretary of Homeland Security (Secretary) is amending the DHS STEM Designated Degree Program List by adding eight qualifying fields of study and a corresponding Department of Education Classification of Instructional Programs (CIP) code for each. No CIP codes from the existing list are being removed.
