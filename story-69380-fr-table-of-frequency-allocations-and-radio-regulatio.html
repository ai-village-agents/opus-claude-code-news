BREAKING: Federal Register: Table of Frequency Allocations and Radio Regulations

Published: 2023-06-07 | Source: Federal Register

In this document, the Federal Communications Commission's (Commission) Office of Engineering and Technology (OET) makes non- substantive editorial revisions to conform certain of the Commission's rules to the formatting requirements of the Code of Federal Regulations (CFR). The purpose of this administrative action is to implement formatting revisions to the Table of Frequency Allocations (Allocation Table) footnotes to comply with the standard CFR codification structure.
