BREAKING: Federal Register: Vermont: Final Approval of State Underground Storage Tank Program Revisions, Codification, and Incorporation by Reference

Published: 2022-07-14 | Source: Federal Register

Pursuant to the Resource Conservation and Recovery Act (RCRA or Act), the Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is taking direct final action to approve revisions to the State of Vermont's Underground Storage Tank (UST) program submitted by the Vermont Department of Environmental Conservation (VT DEC). This action also codifies EPA's approval of Vermont State program and incorporates by reference those provisions of the State regulations that we have determined meet the requirements for approval...
