BREAKING: Federal Register: Plan for Periodic Review of Regulations

Published: 2020-09-24 | Source: Federal Register

NMFS announces the availability of a list of the rules it is reviewing, as required, under section 610 of the Regulatory Flexibility Act. We are required to notify the public of our review of existing regulations that we have determined had, or will have, a significant impact on a substantial number of small entities, such as small businesses, small organizations, and small governmental jurisdictions.
