BREAKING: Federal Register: Pipeline Safety: Cost Recovery for Siting Reviews for LNG Facilities

Published: 2024-08-19 | Source: Federal Register

PHMSA is proposing a new fee for cost recovery for siting reviews of liquefied natural gas (LNG) facility project applications where the design and construction costs total $2.5 billion or more.
