BREAKING: Federal Register: Record of Decision for Legislative Environmental Impact Statement Regarding Training and Public Land Withdrawal Extension, Fort Irwin, California

Published: 2024-08-16 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of the Army (Army) signed the Record of Decision (ROD) on February 29, 2024, for the Legislative Environmental Impact Statement (LEIS) Regarding Training and Public Land Withdrawal Extension, Fort Irwin, California. The Army selected a program of training infrastructure and range improvement, along with new uses for its Western Training Area (WTA).
