BREAKING: Federal Register: Custom Declaration Exceptions

Published: 2023-05-10 | Source: Federal Register

The Postal Service proposes amending Mailing Standards of the United States Postal Service, Domestic Mail Manual (DMM[supreg]) in various subsections to remove the "known mailer" and "official mail" exceptions for customs declarations for mail to or from overseas military and diplomatic post office addresses.
