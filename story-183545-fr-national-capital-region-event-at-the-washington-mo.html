BREAKING: Federal Register: National Capital Region; Event at the Washington Monument

Published: 2019-07-09 | Source: Federal Register

The National Park Service is temporarily revising a regulation for the National Capital Region. This revision will allow the projection of government-selected film footage and associated imagery of the Apollo 11 Mission onto the Washington Monument for an official celebration of the fiftieth anniversary of the Apollo 11 lunar landing.
