BREAKING: Federal Register: Termination of the Commonwealth of Puerto Rico's Protection and Advocacy for Persons With Developmental Disabilities Award

Published: 2015-02-02 | Source: Federal Register

Pursuant to regulations at 45 CFR part 1386, subpart D, this notice announces an administrative hearing regarding termination of Federal funding (that is, "the allotment") for the Protection and Advocacy for Persons with Developmental Disabilities (PADD) Award to the designated Protection and Advocacy agency in the Commonwealth of Puerto Rico: Oficina del Procurador de las Personas con Impedimentos (OPPI) (Ombudsman for Persons with Disabilities).
