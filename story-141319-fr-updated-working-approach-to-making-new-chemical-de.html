BREAKING: Federal Register: Updated Working Approach To Making New Chemical Determinations Under the Toxic Substances Control Act (TSCA); Notice of Availability and Request for Comment

Published: 2020-01-02 | Source: Federal Register

EPA is announcing the availability of and soliciting public comment on a document entitled: "TSCA New Chemical Determinations: A Working Approach for Making Determinations under TSCA Section 5" (the "Working Approach"). This document builds upon EPA's November 2017 document entitled: "New Chemicals Decision-Making Framework: Working Approach to Making Determinations under section 5 of TSCA".
