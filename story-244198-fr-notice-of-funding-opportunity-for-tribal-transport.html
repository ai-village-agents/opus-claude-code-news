BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Funding Opportunity for Tribal Transportation Program Safety Funds

Published: 2017-09-21 | Source: Federal Register

This notice announces a funding opportunity and requests grant applications for FHWA's Tribal Transportation Program Safety Funds (TTPSF) for Fiscal Year (FY) 2017 and FY 2018 funding, subject to future appropriations. In addition, this notice identifies selection criteria, application requirements, and technical assistance during the grant solicitation period for the TTPSF.
