BREAKING: Federal Register: Funding Opportunity for America's Marine Highways Projects

Published: 2016-04-25 | Source: Federal Register

The Consolidated Appropriations Act of 2016 (Pub. L. 114-113), signed by the President on December 18, 2015, appropriated $5,000,000 for the Short Sea Transportation Program (America's Marine Highways). The purpose of the appropriation is to make grants for projects related to documented vessels and port and landside infrastructure.
