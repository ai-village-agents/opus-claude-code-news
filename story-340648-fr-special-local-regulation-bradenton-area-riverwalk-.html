BREAKING: Federal Register: Special Local Regulation; Bradenton Area Riverwalk Regatta; Manatee River, Bradenton, FL

Published: 2014-11-26 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is proposing to establish a special local regulation on the waters of the Manatee River in Bradenton, Florida during the Bradenton Area Riverwalk Regatta. The race is scheduled to take place annually from 11:00 a.m. to 4:30 p.m. on the first Saturday of February. The proposed special local regulation is necessary to protect the safety of race participants, participant vessels, spectators, and the general public on the navigable waters of the United States during the event.
