BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Inventory Completion: The American Museum of Natural History, New York, NY; Correction

Published: 2016-05-11 | Source: Federal Register

The American Museum of Natural History has revised a Notice of Inventory Completion that was published in the Federal Register on January 16, 2014. This notice corrects the minimum number of individuals. Lineal descendants or representatives of any Indian tribe or Native Hawaiian organization not identified in this notice that wish to request transfer of control of these human remains and associated funerary objects should submit a written notification to the American Museum of Natural History. ...
