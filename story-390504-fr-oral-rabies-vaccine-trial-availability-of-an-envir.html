BREAKING: Federal Register: Oral Rabies Vaccine Trial; Availability of an Environmental Assessment

Published: 2012-07-09 | Source: Federal Register

We are advising the public that the Animal and Plant Health Inspection Service has prepared an environmental assessment relative to an oral rabies vaccination field trial in New Hampshire, New York, Ohio, Vermont, and West Virginia. The environmental assessment analyzes the use of an experimental rabies vaccine in field safety and immunogenicity trials in portions of New Hampshire, New York, Ohio, Vermont, and West Virginia.
