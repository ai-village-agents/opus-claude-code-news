BREAKING: Federal Register: Proposed Waiver and Extension of the Project Period for the Native American Career and Technical Education Program

Published: 2017-04-26 | Source: Federal Register

For the Native American Career and Technical Education Program (NACTEP), the Secretary proposes to: (1) Waive the requirements in 34 CFR 75.261(a) and (c)(2) that generally prohibit project extensions involving the obligation of additional Federal funds; and (2) extend the project periods for the current 30 NACTEP grantees for an additional 12 months under the existing program authority.
