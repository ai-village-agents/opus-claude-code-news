BREAKING: Federal Register: Approval and Promulgation of Air Quality Implementation Plans; Illinois; Regional Haze

Published: 2012-01-26 | Source: Federal Register

EPA is proposing to approve revisions to the Illinois State Implementation Plan (SIP) addressing regional haze for the first implementation period. Illinois submitted its regional haze plan on June 24, 2011. The Illinois regional haze plan addresses Clean Air Act (CAA) section 169B and Regional Haze Rule requirements for states to remedy any existing and prevent future anthropogenic impairment of visibility at mandatory Class I areas.
