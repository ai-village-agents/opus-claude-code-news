BREAKING: Federal Register: Accreditation of Nexeo Solutions LLC, as a Commercial Laboratory

Published: 2015-04-13 | Source: Federal Register

Notice is hereby given, pursuant to CBP regulations, that Nexeo Solutions LLC, has been accredited to test petroleum and certain petroleum products for customs purposes for the next three years as of September 24, 2014.
