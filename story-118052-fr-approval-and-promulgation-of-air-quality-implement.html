BREAKING: Federal Register: Approval and Promulgation of Air Quality Implementation Plan; Mashantucket Pequot Tribal Nation

Published: 2021-03-03 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) approves the Mashantucket Pequot Tribal Nation's (MPTN or the Tribe) Tribal Implementation Plan (TIP) under the Clean Air Act (CAA) to regulate air pollution within the exterior boundaries of the Tribe's reservation. The TIP is one of two CAA regulatory programs that comprise the Tribe's Clean Air Program (CAP).
