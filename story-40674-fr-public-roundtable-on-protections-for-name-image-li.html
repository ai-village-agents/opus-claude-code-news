BREAKING: Federal Register: Public Roundtable on Protections for Name, Image, Likeness, Other Indicia of Identity, and Reputation

Published: 2024-07-01 | Source: Federal Register

The United States Patent and Trademark Office (USPTO) will hold a roundtable to seek public input regarding: whether existing laws protecting an individual's reputation and existing laws prohibiting unauthorized use of an individual's name, image, voice, likeness, or other indicia of identity (collectively referred to within this Notice as "NIL") are sufficient given the development and proliferation of artificial intelligence (AI) technology.
