BREAKING: Federal Register: Rural Opportunities To Use Transportation for Economic Success: Request for Information

Published: 2019-12-31 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Transportation (DOT) extends the comment period for its November 27, 2019 request for information (RFI) on unmet transportation infrastructure needs in rural transportation, barriers that rural communities face in addressing these needs, stakeholders' experiences with applying to and using DOT discretionary grant and credit programs, and opportunities for DOT to improve its services and technical assistance to rural communities in relation to these grant and credit programs.
