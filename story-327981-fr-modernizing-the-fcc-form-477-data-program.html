BREAKING: Federal Register: Modernizing the FCC Form 477 Data Program

Published: 2014-06-26 | Source: Federal Register

In this document the Federal Communications Commission (Commission) released a Report and Order which revised the Commission's Form 477 collection to include data on deployment of fixed and mobile broadband networks and mobile voice networks, as well as company identification and emergency contact information.
