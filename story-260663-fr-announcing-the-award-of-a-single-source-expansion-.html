BREAKING: Federal Register: Announcing the Award of a Single Source Expansion Supplement to Community Action Partnership in Washington, DC

Published: 2016-04-13 | Source: Federal Register

The Administration for Children and Families (ACF), Office of Community Services (OCS), Division of State Assistance announces a single-source program expansion supplement in the amount of $50,000 to Community Action Partnership in Washington, DC 20036. The supplement will support the creation of a new learning cluster focused on addressing child poverty in rural communities and tribal lands and increasing access to multi-generational programs.
