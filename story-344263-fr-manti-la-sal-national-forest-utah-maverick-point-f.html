BREAKING: Federal Register: Manti-La Sal National Forest, Utah; Maverick Point Forest Health Project

Published: 2013-01-15 | Source: Federal Register

The Forest Service will prepare an Environmental Impact Statement (EIS) to document the analysis and disclose the environmental impacts of the Maverick Point Forest Health Project. This project is designed to achieve goals of increasing aspen and ponderosa pine forest stand resilience and resistance to insects, disease, drought, and wildfire by altering stand density, species composition, and age class structure via use of timber harvesting and prescribed fire.
