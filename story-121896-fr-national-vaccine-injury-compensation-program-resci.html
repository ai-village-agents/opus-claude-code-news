BREAKING: Federal Register: National Vaccine Injury Compensation Program: Rescission of Revisions to the Vaccine Injury Table

Published: 2021-04-22 | Source: Federal Register

This action rescinds in its entirety the rule entitled "National Vaccine Injury Compensation Program: Revisions to the Vaccine Injury Table," published in the Federal Register on January 21, 2021 (January 21, 2021 Final Rule).
