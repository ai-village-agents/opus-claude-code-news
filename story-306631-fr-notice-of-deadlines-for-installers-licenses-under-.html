BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Deadlines for Installers' Licenses Under the HUD Manufactured Housing Installation Program

Published: 2015-10-14 | Source: Federal Register

The National Manufactured Housing Construction and Safety Standards Act of 1974, as amended, is intended to protect the quality, safety, durability, and affordability of manufactured homes. In order to accomplish those objectives, the Act requires HUD to establish and implement manufactured home installation programs for States that choose not to operate their own installation programs.
