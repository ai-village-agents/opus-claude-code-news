BREAKING: Federal Register: Emergency Mergers-Chartering and Field of Membership

Published: 2017-07-31 | Source: Federal Register

The NCUA Board (Board) proposes to amend in its Chartering and Field of Membership Manual the definition of the term "in danger of insolvency" for emergency merger purposes. The current definition requires a credit union to fall into at least one of three net worth categories over a period of time to be "in danger of insolvency."
