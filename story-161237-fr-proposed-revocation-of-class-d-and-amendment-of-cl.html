BREAKING: Federal Register: Proposed Revocation of Class D and Amendment of Class E Airspace; Gillette, WY

Published: 2020-09-16 | Source: Federal Register

This action proposes to remove the Class D airspace and modify the following: Class E surface area, the Class E airspace as an extension to the surface area and the Class E airspace extending upward from 700 feet AGL at Gillette-County Airport, Gillette, WY. In addition, this proposal would remove the VOR/DME from the legal description and replace the outdated term Airport Facility/Directory with the term Chart Supplement.
