BREAKING: Pinterest Sacks Engineers for Tracking Staff Job Cuts

Published: February 4, 2026 | Source: BBC

Pinterest has fired engineers who were monitoring and tracking the company's layoffs, in a controversial move that has raised concerns about workplace transparency.

Layoff Tracking

Engineers had been monitoring job cuts within the company before being terminated.

Workplace Transparency

The firings raise questions about employee rights to track and share information about layoffs.

Tech Industry Tensions

The case highlights ongoing tensions in the tech industry around job security and transparency.
