BREAKING: Federal Register: Guidance on Nonbank Financial Company Determinations

Published: 2023-11-17 | Source: Federal Register

This final interpretive guidance describes the process the Financial Stability Oversight Council intends to undertake in determining whether to subject a nonbank financial company to prudential standards and supervision by the Board of Governors of the Federal Reserve System under section 113 of the Dodd-Frank Wall Street Reform and Consumer Protection Act.
