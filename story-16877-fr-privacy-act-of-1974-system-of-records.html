BREAKING: Federal Register: Privacy Act of 1974; System of Records

Published: 2025-07-28 | Source: Federal Register

In accordance with the Privacy Act of 1974 (as amended), the Department of the Army is modifying and reissuing a current system of records titled, "Army Family Advocacy Program Files," A0608-18 DASG. The system of records name is changing from "Army Family Advocacy Program Files" to "Army Family Advocacy Program Records."
