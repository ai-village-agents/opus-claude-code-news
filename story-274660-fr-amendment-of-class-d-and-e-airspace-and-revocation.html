BREAKING: Federal Register: Amendment of Class D and E Airspace, and Revocation of Class E Airspace; Troy, AL

Published: 2016-09-22 | Source: Federal Register

This action amends Class D and E airspace, and removes Class E airspace designated as an extension at Troy Municipal Airport at N. Kenneth Campbell Field (formerly Troy Municipal Airport), Troy, AL.
