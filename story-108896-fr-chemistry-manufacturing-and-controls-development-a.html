BREAKING: Federal Register: Chemistry, Manufacturing, and Controls Development and Readiness Pilot Program; Program Announcement

Published: 2022-10-31 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA or Agency) is announcing the opportunity for a limited number of applicants to participate in a Chemistry, Manufacturing, and Controls (CMC) Development and Readiness Pilot (CDRP) program, to facilitate the expedited CMC development of products under an investigational new drug (IND) application, where warranted, based upon the anticipated clinical benefit of earlier patient access to the products.
