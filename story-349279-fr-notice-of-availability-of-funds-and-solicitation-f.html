BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Availability of Funds and Solicitation for Grant Applications for the Workforce Data Quality Initiative

Published: 2013-03-13 | Source: Federal Register

The Employment and Training Administration (ETA), U.S. Department of Labor, announces the availability of approximately $6 million for grants to State Workforce Agencies (SWA) to develop the Workforce Data Quality Initiative (WDQI). Grants awarded will provide SWAs the opportunity to develop or expand State workforce longitudinal administrative data systems.
