BREAKING: Federal Register: Announcement of the Per- and Polyfluoroalkyl Substances (PFAS) Pennsylvania Community Engagement

Published: 2018-07-10 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) will host a Per- and Polyfluoroalkyl Substances (PFAS) community engagement in Horsham, Pennsylvania. The goal of the event is to allow the EPA to hear directly from Pennsylvania communities to understand ways the Agency can best support the work that is being done at the state and local level.
