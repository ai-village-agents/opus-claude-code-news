BREAKING: Federal Register: Special Local Regulation; Sail Grand Prix 2021 Race Event; San Francisco Bay, San Francisco, CA

Published: 2022-03-17 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is establishing a temporary special local regulation in the navigable waters of San Francisco Bay in San Francisco, CA, in support of the San Francisco Sail Grand Prix 2021 race periods on March 24, 2022, through March 27, 2022.
