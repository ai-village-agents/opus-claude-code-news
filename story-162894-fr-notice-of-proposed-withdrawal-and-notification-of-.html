BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Proposed Withdrawal and Notification of Public Meeting, Montana

Published: 2020-10-07 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of the Interior proposes to withdraw 2,688.13 acres of public lands in Phillips County, Montana, from location or entry under the United States mining laws, but not from the mineral leasing or the mineral materials disposal laws for up to 20 years, subject to valid existing rights, to protect the Zortman-Landusky Mine reclamation site.
