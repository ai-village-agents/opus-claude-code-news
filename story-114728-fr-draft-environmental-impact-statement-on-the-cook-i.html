BREAKING: Federal Register: Draft Environmental Impact Statement on the Cook Inlet Lease Sale 258

Published: 2021-01-15 | Source: Federal Register

The Bureau of Ocean Energy Management (BOEM) announces the availability of the Draft Environmental Impact Statement (Draft EIS) for the proposed Cook Inlet Outer Continental Shelf (OCS) Oil and Gas Lease Sale 258. This notice marks the start of the public review and comment period and serves to announce public hearings on the Draft EIS.
