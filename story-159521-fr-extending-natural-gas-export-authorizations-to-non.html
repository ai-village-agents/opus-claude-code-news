BREAKING: Federal Register: Extending Natural Gas Export Authorizations to Non-Free Trade Agreement Countries Through the Year 2050

Published: 2020-08-25 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Department of Energy's (DOE) Office of Fossil Energy (FE) will act on applications and amendments requesting to export domestically produced natural gas--including liquefied natural gas (LNG), compressed natural gas, and compressed gas liquid--from the lower-48 states to non-free trade agreement (non-FTA) countries for a term ending on December 31, 2050, discontinuing its practice of issuing standard 20-year export terms.
