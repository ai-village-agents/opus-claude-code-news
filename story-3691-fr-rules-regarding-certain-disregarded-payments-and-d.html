BREAKING: Federal Register: Rules Regarding Certain Disregarded Payments and Dual Consolidated Losses

Published: 2025-01-14 | Source: Federal Register

This document contains final regulations regarding certain disregarded payments that give rise to deductions for foreign tax purposes and avoid the application of the dual consolidated loss ("DCL") rules. The final regulations affect domestic corporate owners that make or receive such payments.
