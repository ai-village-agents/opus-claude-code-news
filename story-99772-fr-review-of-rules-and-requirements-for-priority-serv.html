BREAKING: Federal Register: Review of Rules and Requirements for Priority Services

Published: 2022-07-05 | Source: Federal Register

In this document, the Federal Communications Commission (Commission) adopts changes to its priority services rules to reflect today's marketplace and governance framework and to explicitly authorize the prioritization of IP-based technologies. Specifically, it removes outdated language that may cause confusion or otherwise impede the use of IP-based technologies to support the provision of priority services for voice, data, and video communications.
