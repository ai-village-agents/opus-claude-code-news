BREAKING: Federal Register: Updated Means of Providing Recall Notification

Published: 2025-01-10 | Source: Federal Register

In accordance with the Moving Ahead for Progress in the 21st Century Act (MAP-21) and the Fixing America's Surface Transportation Act (FAST Act), NHTSA is proposing to amend the means of required recall notification to include notification by electronic means, in addition to first-class mail, and proposing certain other attendant obligations related to this requirement.
