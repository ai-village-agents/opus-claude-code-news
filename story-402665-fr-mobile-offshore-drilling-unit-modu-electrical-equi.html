BREAKING: Federal Register: Mobile Offshore Drilling Unit (MODU) Electrical Equipment Certification Guidance

Published: 2012-12-03 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is providing guidance regarding electrical equipment installed in hazardous areas on foreign-flagged Mobile Offshore Drilling Units (MODUs) that have never operated, but intend to operate, on the U.S. Outer Continental Shelf (OCS).
