BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Embraer S.A. (Type Certificate Previously Held by Empresa Brasileira de Aeronáutica S.A.) Airplanes

Published: 2020-06-16 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA is superseding airworthiness directive (AD) 2011-20-01 for certain Empresa Brasileira de Aeron[aacute]utica S.A. (now Embraer S.A.) Model EMB-505 airplanes. AD 2011-20-01 required replacing the bolts that attach the balance mass weights to the elevator structure.
