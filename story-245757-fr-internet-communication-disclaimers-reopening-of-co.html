BREAKING: Federal Register: Internet Communication Disclaimers; Reopening of Comment Period

On October 13, 2011, the Federal Election Commission published an Advance Notice of Proposed Rulemaking ("ANPRM") seeking comment on whether to begin a rulemaking to revise its regulations concerning disclaimers on certain internet communications and, if so, on what changes should be made to those rules.
