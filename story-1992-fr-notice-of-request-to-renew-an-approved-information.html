BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Request To Renew an Approved Information Collection: Advanced Meat Recovery

Published: 2026-01-15 | Source: Federal Register

In accordance with the Paperwork Reduction Act of 1995 and Office of Management and Budget (OMB) regulations, FSIS is announcing its intention to renew an approved information collection regarding the regulatory requirements associated with the production of meat from advanced meat recovery (AMR) systems. The approval for this information collection will expire on June 30, 2026. FSIS is making no changes to the information collection.
