BREAKING: Federal Register: Petition for Exemption From the Federal Motor Vehicle Motor Theft Prevention Standard; Toyota

Published: 2013-01-15 | Source: Federal Register

This document grants in full Toyota Motor North America, Inc.'s (Toyota) petition for an exemption of the RAV4 vehicle line in accordance with 49 CFR part 543, Exemption from the Theft Prevention Standard.
