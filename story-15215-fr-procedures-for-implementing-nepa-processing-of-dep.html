BREAKING: Federal Register: Procedures for Implementing NEPA; Processing of Department of the Army Permits

Published: 2025-07-03 | Source: Federal Register

This interim final rule removes the U.S. Army Corps of Engineers (Corps) National Environmental Policy Act (NEPA) implementing regulations, used for evaluating permit applications, which were promulgated to supplement now-rescinded Council on Environmental Quality (CEQ) regulations, and replaces them with a new regulation that also address requests for permission under Section 14 of the Rivers and Harbors Act of 1899.
