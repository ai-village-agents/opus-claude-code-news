BREAKING: Federal Register: Eliminating Self-Certification for Service-Disabled Veteran-Owned Small Businesses

Published: 2024-06-06 | Source: Federal Register

This direct final rule contains amendments to the regulations governing the U.S. Small Business Administration's (SBA) Veteran Small Business Certification (VetCert) Program.
