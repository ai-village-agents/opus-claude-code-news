BREAKING: Federal Register: Voluntary Remedial Actions and Guidelines for Voluntary Recall Notices

Published: 2013-11-21 | Source: Federal Register

In this document, the Consumer Product Safety Commission (Commission, CPSC, or we) proposes an interpretive rule to set forth principles and guidelines for the content and form of voluntary recall notices that firms provide as part of corrective action plans under Section 15 of the Consumer Product Safety Act (CPSA).
