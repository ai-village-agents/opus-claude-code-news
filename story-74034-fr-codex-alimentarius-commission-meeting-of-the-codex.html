BREAKING: Federal Register: Codex Alimentarius Commission: Meeting of the Codex Committee on General Principles

Published: 2023-08-09 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Codex Office is sponsoring a public meeting on September 14, 2023, from 2:00-4:00 p.m. EDT. The objective of the public meeting is to provide information and receive public comments on agenda items and draft U.S. positions to be discussed at the 33rd Session of the Codex Committee on General Principles (CCGP) of the Codex Alimentarius Commission (CAC), which will meet in Bordeaux, France from October 2-6, 2023.
