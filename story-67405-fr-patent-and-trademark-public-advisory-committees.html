BREAKING: Federal Register: Patent and Trademark Public Advisory Committees

Published: 2023-05-12 | Source: Federal Register

The United States Patent and Trademark Office (USPTO)-- America's Innovation Agency--seeks nominations for up to three members of its Patent Public Advisory Committee (PPAC) to advise the Under Secretary of Commerce for Intellectual Property and Director of the USPTO on patent policy, and for up to three members of its Trademark Public Advisory Committee (TPAC) to advise the Director on trademark policy.
