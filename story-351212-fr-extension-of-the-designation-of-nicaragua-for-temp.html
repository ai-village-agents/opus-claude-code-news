BREAKING: Federal Register: Extension of the Designation of Nicaragua for Temporary Protected Status

Published: 2013-04-03 | Source: Federal Register

This Notice announces that the Secretary of Homeland Security (Secretary) is extending the designation of Nicaragua for Temporary Protected Status (TPS) for 18 months from July 6, 2013 through January 5, 2015. The extension allows currently eligible TPS beneficiaries to retain TPS through January 5, 2015.
