BREAKING: Federal Register: Additional PortaCount® Quantitative Fit-Testing Protocols: Amendment to Respiratory Protection Standard

Published: 2016-10-07 | Source: Federal Register

OSHA is proposing to add two modified PortaCount[supreg] quantitative fit-testing protocols to its Respiratory Protection Standard. The proposed protocols would apply to employers in general industry, shipyard employment, and the construction industry. Both proposed protocols are variations of the existing OSHA-accepted PortaCount[supreg] protocol, but differ from it by the exercise sets, exercise duration, and sampling sequence.
