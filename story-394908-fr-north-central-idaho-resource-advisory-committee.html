BREAKING: Federal Register: North Central Idaho Resource Advisory Committee

Published: 2012-08-28 | Source: Federal Register

The North Central Idaho RAC will be meeting via a conference call. The committee is meeting as authorized under the Secure Rural Schools and Community Self-Determination Act (Pub. L. 110-343) and in compliance with the Federal Advisory Committee Act. The purpose of the meeting is for RAC Members to recommend additional FY 2013 Title II projects (under the one year Secure Rural Schools extension) for approval. Meetings are always open to the public.
