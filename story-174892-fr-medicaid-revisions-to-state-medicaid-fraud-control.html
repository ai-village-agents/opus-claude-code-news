BREAKING: Federal Register: Medicaid; Revisions to State Medicaid Fraud Control Unit Rules

Published: 2019-03-22 | Source: Federal Register

This final rule amends the regulation governing State Medicaid Fraud Control Units (MFCUs or Units). The rule incorporates statutory changes affecting the Units as well as policy and practice changes that have occurred since the regulation was initially issued in 1978.
