BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone; Upper Mississippi River Between Mile 44 and 46; Thebes, IL

Published: 2014-12-01 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is establishing a temporary safety zone for all waters of the Upper Mississippi River, extending the entire width from mile 44 and 46. This safety zone is needed to protect persons, property, and infrastructure from potential damage and safety hazards associated with the removal of two 16 inch Enterprise pipelines in the navigation channel.
