BREAKING: Federal Register: Drawbridge Operation Regulation; Drawbridge Operation Regulation; Erie Canal, Part of the New York State Canal System, in Brockport, NY

Published: 2023-04-14 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard proposes to temporarily change the operating schedule that governs the E-182 Main Street Bridge, mile 278.93, over the Erie Canal, in Brockport, NY to allow contractors to rehabilitate the bridge. The roadway has been closed since last fall and vehicles are unable to cross the bridge until repairs are completed.
