BREAKING: Federal Register: Access to Federal Employees Health Benefits (FEHB) for Employees of Certain Tribally Controlled Schools

Published: 2021-09-03 | Source: Federal Register

The Office of Personnel Management (OPM) is issuing an interim final rule to expand eligibility for enrollment in the Federal Employees Health Benefits (FEHB) Program to additional tribal employees.
