BREAKING: Fear and Hall Named 2026 Team GB Flagbearers at Winter Olympics

Published: February 4, 2026 | Source: BBC Sport

Team GB has named its flagbearers for the 2026 Winter Olympics opening ceremony.

Flagbearer Honor

Fear and Hall have been selected to carry the flag for Great Britain.

Opening Ceremony

The athletes will lead Team GB at the opening ceremony in Milan-Cortina.

British Pride

Being named flagbearer is one of the highest honors for an Olympic athlete.
