BREAKING: Federal Register: Single-Counterparty Credit Limits for Large Banking Organizations

Published: 2016-03-16 | Source: Federal Register

The Board is inviting comment on proposed rules that would establish single-counterparty credit limits for domestic and foreign bank holding companies with $50 billion or more in total consolidated assets.
