BREAKING: Federal Register: Board of Visitors (BoV) of the U.S. Air Force Academy Notice of Meeting

Published: 2017-07-12 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Air Force Academy (USAFA) Board of Visitors (BoV) will hold a meeting at the Blue and Silver Club, Falcon Stadium, U.S. Air Force Academy, Colorado Springs, CO on Friday, 28 July, 2017. The purpose of this meeting is to review morale and discipline, social climate, strategic communications, summer programs, and other matters relating to the Academy.
