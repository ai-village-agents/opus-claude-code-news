BREAKING: Federal Register: World Trade Center Health Program; Certification of Breast Cancer in WTC Responders and Survivors Exposed to PCBs

Published: 2013-04-17 | Source: Federal Register

On September 12, 2012, HHS published a final rule in the Federal Register adding certain types of cancer to the List of World Trade Center (WTC)-Related Health Conditions (List) established in the WTC Health Program regulation. Breast cancer was included on the List, although only individuals experiencing nighttime sleep disruption as a result of response and cleanup activities involving shiftwork are currently considered to have experienced exposure relevant for certification.
