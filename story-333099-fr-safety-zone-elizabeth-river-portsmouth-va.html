BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone; Elizabeth River; Portsmouth, VA

Published: 2014-08-25 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard proposes to establish a safety zone on the navigable waters of the Elizabeth River in the vicinity of the Midtown Tunnel, Portsmouth, Virginia for 12 periods of 48 hours each beginning at midnight on January 1, January 12, January 19, January 22, January 27, February 9, March 2, March 5, March 10, March 23, March 30 and April 6, 2015.
