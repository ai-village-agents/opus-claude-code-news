BREAKING: Federal Register: Crooked River Valley Rehabilitation Project

Published: 2015-08-20 | Source: Federal Register

This notice announces the availability of the ROD to fund implementation of Crooked River Valley Rehabilitation Project in Idaho County, Idaho. BPA has decided to fund implementation of Alternative 2 identified in the Crooked River Valley Rehabilitation Project Final Environmental Impact Statement (DOE/EIS-0506, June 2015).
