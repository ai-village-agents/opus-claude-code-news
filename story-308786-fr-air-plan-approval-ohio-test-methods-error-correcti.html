BREAKING: Federal Register: Air Plan Approval; Ohio; Test Methods; Error Correction

Published: 2015-11-05 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is determining that a portion of an October 26, 2010, action was in error and is making a correction pursuant to section 110(k)(6) of the Clean Air Act. The October 26, 2010, EPA action approved various revisions to Ohio regulations in the EPA approved state implementation plan (SIP).
