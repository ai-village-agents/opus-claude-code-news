BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Proposed Purchased/Referred Care Delivery Area Re-Designation for the Pokagon Band of Potawatomi Indians of Michigan and Indiana

Published: 2024-05-17 | Source: Federal Register

This Notice advises the public that the Indian Health Service (IHS) proposes to expand the geographic boundaries of the Purchased/ Referred Care Delivery Area (PRCDA) for the Pokagon Band of Potawatomi Indians of Michigan and Indiana to include the counties of Kalamazoo, Kent, and Ottawa in the State of Michigan.
