BREAKING: Federal Register: Privacy Act of 1974; System of Records

Published: 2024-07-24 | Source: Federal Register

Pursuant to the Privacy Act of 1974, notice is hereby given that VA is establishing a new system of records titled, "Law Enforcement Officer Evaluations (LEO Evals)--VA" (216VA10). This system will be used to assist with the occupational placements of VA police officer candidates and officers undergoing psychological evaluations for hire and annually thereafter.
