BREAKING: Federal Register: Announcement of Grant Application Deadlines and Funding Levels for the Assistance to High Energy Cost Rural Communities

Published: 2012-06-28 | Source: Federal Register

The Rural Utilities Service (RUS), an agency of the United States Department of Agriculture (USDA), announces the availability of up to $7 million in Fiscal Year 2012 for competitive grants to assist communities with extremely high energy costs. This grant program is authorized under section 19 of the Rural Electrification Act of 1936 (RE Act) (7 U.S.C. 918a) and program regulations at 7 CFR part 1709.
