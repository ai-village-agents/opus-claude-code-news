BREAKING: Federal Register: Section 542(c) Housing Finance Agency Risk Sharing Program

Published: 2020-12-22 | Source: Federal Register

Through the Section 542(c) Housing Finance Agency (HFA) Risk Sharing program, HUD enters into risk-sharing agreements with qualified state and local HFAs so they can provide FHA (Federal Housing Administration) mortgage insurance and credit enhancement for new loans on multifamily affordable housing properties.
