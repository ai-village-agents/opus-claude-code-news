BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone; Northern Atlantic Ocean, Nahant, MA

Published: 2020-07-24 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is establishing a temporary safety zone for navigable waters within a 100-yard radius of the DREDGE 200 salvage vessels and machinery located in the Northern Atlantic Ocean approximately 2 miles southeast of Nahant, Massachusetts. The safety zone is needed to protect personnel, vessels, and the marine environment from potential hazards created by salvage operations.
