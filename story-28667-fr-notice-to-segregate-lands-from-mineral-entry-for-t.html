BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice To Segregate Lands From Mineral Entry for the Proposed Libra Solar Project in Mineral County, Nevada

Published: 2024-02-02 | Source: Federal Register

Through this notice, the Bureau of Land Management (BLM) is segregating public lands for the Libra Solar Project right-of-way application from appropriation under the public land laws, including the Mining Law, for a period of 2 years from the date of publication of this notice in the Federal Register, subject to valid existing rights.
