BREAKING: Federal Register: Establishment of the National Quantum Initiative Advisory Committee

Published: 2019-09-11 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Department of Energy (DOE) or (the Department) announces the establishment of the National Quantum Initiative Advisory Committee (Committee), pursuant to, the National Quantum Initiative Act, and in accordance with the Federal Advisory Committee Act (FACA), as delegated to the Department by the President under Executive Order 13885.
