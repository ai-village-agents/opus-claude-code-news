BREAKING: Federal Register: Investment Company Act Release No. 34636

Published: 2022-06-30 | Source: Federal Register

Applicant, a closed-end investment company, seeks an order declaring that it has ceased to be an investment company. On December 15, 2021, applicant made liquidating distributions to its shareholders based on net asset value. Expenses of $4,111 incurred in connection with the liquidation were paid by the applicant. Filing Date: The application was filed on May 26, 2022. Applicant's Address: roykim@chapman.com.
