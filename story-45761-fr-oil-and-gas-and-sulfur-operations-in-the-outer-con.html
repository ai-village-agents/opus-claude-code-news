BREAKING: Federal Register: Oil and Gas and Sulfur Operations in the Outer Continental Shelf-High Pressure High Temperature Updates

Published: 2024-08-30 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of the Interior (DOI or Department), through the Bureau of Safety and Environmental Enforcement (BSEE), is adding requirements for new or unusual technology, including equipment used in high pressure high temperature (HPHT) environments; revising and reorganizing the information submission requirements for a project's Conceptual Plans and Deepwater Operations Plan (DWOP); and requiring independent third parties to review certain information prior to submission to BSEE.
