BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Airbus Helicopters Deutschland GmbH (AHD) Helicopters

Published: 2022-01-26 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA is adopting a new airworthiness directive (AD) for all Airbus Helicopters Deutschland GmbH (AHD) Model MBB-BK 117 C-2, MBB-BK 117 D-2, and MBB-BK 117 D-3 helicopters. This AD was prompted by reports of engine flame out following prolonged operations in falling snow on helicopters with an inlet barrier filter (IBF) system installed.
