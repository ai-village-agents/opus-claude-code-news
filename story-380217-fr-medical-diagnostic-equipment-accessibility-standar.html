BREAKING: Federal Register: Medical Diagnostic Equipment Accessibility Standards

Published: 2012-03-13 | Source: Federal Register

The Architectural and Transportation Barriers Compliance Board (Access Board) announces its intent to establish an advisory committee to advise the Board on matters addressed in the notice of proposed rulemaking (NPRM) published in the February 9, 2012 edition of the Federal Register, 77 FR 6916, on accessibility standards for medical diagnostic equipment and issues raised in the public comments on the NPRM.
