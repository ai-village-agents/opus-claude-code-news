BREAKING: Federal Register: Solicitation of Nominations for Membership To Serve on the Council on Graduate Medical Education

Published: 2023-03-21 | Source: Federal Register

HRSA is seeking nominations of qualified candidates for consideration for appointment as members of the Council on Graduate Medical Education (COGME or Council). COGME provides advice and recommendations on policy, program development, and other matters of significance concerning the physician training and the physician workforce.
