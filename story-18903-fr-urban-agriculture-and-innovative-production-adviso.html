BREAKING: Federal Register: Urban Agriculture and Innovative Production Advisory Committee Meeting

Published: 2025-08-26 | Source: Federal Register

The Natural Resources Conservation Service (NRCS) will hold three public meetings for the Urban Agriculture and Innovative Production Advisory Committee (UAIPAC). UAIPAC will discuss proposed recommendations for the Secretary of Agriculture on the development of policies and outreach for urban, indoor, and other emerging agriculture production practices.
