BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; The Boeing Company Airplanes

Published: 2021-06-30 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA proposes to supersede Airworthiness Directive (AD) 2005-05-18, which applies to certain The Boeing Company Model 737-600, -700, -700C, -800, and -900 series airplanes. AD 2005-05-18 requires repetitive inspections for cracking of the webs of the aft pressure bulkhead at a certain body station, and corrective action if necessary.
