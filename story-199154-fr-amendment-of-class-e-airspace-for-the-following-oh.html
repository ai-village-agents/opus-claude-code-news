BREAKING: Federal Register: Amendment of Class E Airspace; for the Following Ohio Towns; Millersburg, OH and Coshocton, OH

Published: 2018-02-02 | Source: Federal Register

This action amends Class E airspace extending upward from 700 feet above the surface at Holmes County Airport, Millersburg, OH; and at Richard Downing Airport, Coshocton, OH due to the decommissioning of Tiverton VHF Omnidirectional Range (VOR) and Distance Measuring Equipment (DME), cancellation of the VOR approaches, and implementation of area navigation (RNAV) procedures have made this action necessary for the safety and management of instrument flight rules (IFR) operations at these airports...
