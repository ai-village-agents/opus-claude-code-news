BREAKING: Federal Register: Entry-Level Driver Training: Application for Exemption; Western Area Career and Technology Center

Published: 2022-06-15 | Source: Federal Register

FMCSA announces that Western Area Career and Technology Center (WACTC) has requested an exemption from the Theory and Behind-the-Wheel (BTW) instructor requirements contained in the entry-level driver training (ELDT) regulations for one prospective instructor.
