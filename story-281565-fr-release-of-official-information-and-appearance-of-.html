BREAKING: Federal Register: Release of Official Information and Appearance of Witnesses in Litigation

Published: 2016-12-14 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of the Army proposes to amend its regulation concerning policies and procedures for release of official information and testimony of Army witnesses in federal and state courts where the Army or Department of Defense (DoD) has an interest in the matter. This regulation was last published in the Federal Register on July 29, 1994 (59 FR 38236). At that time, a complete Army Regulation was codified.
