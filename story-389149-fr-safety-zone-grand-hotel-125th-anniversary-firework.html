BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone; Grand Hotel 125th Anniversary Fireworks Celebration, Mackinaw Island, MI

Published: 2012-06-21 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is establishing a temporary safety zone near Mackinaw Island, Michigan. This safety zone is intended to restrict vessels from a portion of Lake Huron due to a fireworks display. This temporary safety zone is necessary to protect the surrounding public and vessels from the hazards associated with a fireworks display.
