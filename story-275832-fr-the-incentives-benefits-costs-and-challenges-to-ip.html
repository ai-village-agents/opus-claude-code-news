BREAKING: Federal Register: The Incentives, Benefits, Costs, and Challenges to IPv6 Implementation

Published: 2016-10-05 | Source: Federal Register

On August 18, 2016, the National Telecommunications and Information Administration (NTIA) issued a notice and request for public comments seeking input to guide NTIA in future Internet Protocol version 6 (IPv6) promotional activities.
