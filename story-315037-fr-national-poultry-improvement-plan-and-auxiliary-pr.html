BREAKING: Federal Register: National Poultry Improvement Plan and Auxiliary Provisions

Published: 2014-01-28 | Source: Federal Register

We are proposing to amend the National Poultry Improvement Plan (NPIP, the Plan) and its auxiliary provisions by removing the descriptions of specific tests and sanitation procedures from the regulations. Instead, we would require tests to be performed and sanitation to be maintained in a manner approved by the Administrator.
