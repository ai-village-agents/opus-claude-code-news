BREAKING: Federal Register: Standards for the Growing, Harvesting, Packing, and Holding of Produce for Human Consumption

Published: 2013-01-16 | Source: Federal Register

To minimize the risk of serious adverse health consequences or death from consumption of contaminated produce, the Food and Drug Administration (FDA) is proposing to establish science-based minimum standards for the safe growing, harvesting, packing, and holding of produce, meaning fruits and vegetables grown for human consumption.
