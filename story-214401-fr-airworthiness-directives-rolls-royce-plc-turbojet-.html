BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Rolls-Royce plc Turbojet Engines

Published: 2018-08-21 | Source: Federal Register

We are superseding Airworthiness Directive (AD) 2016-03-03 for all Rolls-Royce plc (RR) Viper Mk. 521, Viper Mk. 522, and Viper Mk. 601-22 turbojet engines. AD 2016-03-03 required reducing the life of certain critical parts. This AD requires reducing the life of certain critical parts and adds additional engine parts to the applicability.
