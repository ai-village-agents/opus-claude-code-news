BREAKING: Federal Register: Competitive Postal Products

Published: 2025-10-15 | Source: Federal Register

The Commission is recognizing a recent Postal Service filing requesting to add a new Mid-Market Non-Published Rates (MMNPR-3) to the Competitive Product List. This notice informs the public of the filing, invites public comment, and takes other administrative steps.
