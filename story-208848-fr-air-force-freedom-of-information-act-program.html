BREAKING: Federal Register: Air Force Freedom of Information Act Program

Published: 2018-06-07 | Source: Federal Register

This final rule removes the Department of the Air Force's regulation concerning the Freedom of Information Act program. On February 6, 2018, the DoD published a revised FOIA program rule as a result of the FOIA Improvement Act of 2016. When the DoD FOIA program rule was revised, it included DoD component information and removed the requirement for component supplementary rules.
