BREAKING: Federal Register: Wine Treating Materials and Related Regulations

Published: 2022-08-24 | Source: Federal Register

The Alcohol and Tobacco Tax and Trade Bureau (TTB) is amending its regulations pertaining to the production of wine to add to the list of materials and processes authorized for the treatment of wine and of the juice from which wine is made, and to expand the authorized uses of certain materials already authorized under the regulations.
