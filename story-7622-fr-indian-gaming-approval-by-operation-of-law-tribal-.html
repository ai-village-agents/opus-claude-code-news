BREAKING: Federal Register: Indian Gaming; Approval by Operation of Law Tribal-State Class III Gaming Compact Amendment Between the Chitimacha Tribe of Louisiana and the State of Louisiana

Published: 2025-03-20 | Source: Federal Register

This notice announces the approval by operation of law of an amendment to the Tribal-State compact for the conduct of class III gaming between the Chitimacha Tribe of Louisiana and the State of Louisiana governing the operation and regulation of class III gaming activities.
