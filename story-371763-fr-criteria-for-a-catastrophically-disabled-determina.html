BREAKING: Federal Register: Criteria for a Catastrophically Disabled Determination for Purposes of Enrollment

Published: 2013-12-03 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Veterans Affairs (VA) is amending its regulation concerning the manner in which VA determines that a veteran is catastrophically disabled for purposes of enrollment in priority group 4 for VA health care.
