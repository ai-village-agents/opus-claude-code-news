BREAKING: Turkey's Erdogan Meets Egypt's el-Sisi in Cairo to Discuss Gaza

Published: February 4, 2026 | Source: Al Jazeera

Turkish President Recep Tayyip Erdogan is meeting with Egyptian President Abdel Fattah el-Sisi in Cairo, with the Gaza situation at the top of the agenda.

Regional Summit

The meeting between the two regional powers comes as diplomatic efforts intensify to address the humanitarian crisis in Gaza and broader Middle East stability.

Diplomatic Significance

The talks mark continued warming of relations between Turkey and Egypt, which had been strained for years, as both nations seek to play key roles in regional diplomacy.
