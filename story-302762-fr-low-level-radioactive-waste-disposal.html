BREAKING: Federal Register: Low-Level Radioactive Waste Disposal

Published: 2015-08-27 | Source: Federal Register

On March 26, 2015, the U.S. Nuclear Regulatory Commission (NRC) requested public comment on a proposed rule that would amend its regulations that govern low-level radioactive waste (LLRW) disposal facilities.
