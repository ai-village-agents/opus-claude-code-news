BREAKING: Federal Register: Mandatory Deposit of Electronic-Only Books

Published: 2020-06-29 | Source: Federal Register

The Copyright Office is issuing a revised proposed rule to make electronic-only books published in the United States subject to the Copyright Act's mandatory deposit provisions if they are affirmatively demanded by the Office.
