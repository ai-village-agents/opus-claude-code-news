BREAKING: Federal Register: Federal Management Regulation (FMR); Transportation Management; Transportation Reporting

Published: 2014-07-17 | Source: Federal Register

GSA is proposing to amend the Federal Management Regulation (FMR) to recommend that agencies annually submit a Federal Transportation Summary for prior fiscal year transportation activities for freight and cargo, including household goods (HHG). Every process improvement effort relies on data to provide a factual basis for making decisions.
