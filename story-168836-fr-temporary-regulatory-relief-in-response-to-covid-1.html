BREAKING: Federal Register: Temporary Regulatory Relief in Response to COVID-19-Extension

Published: 2020-12-22 | Source: Federal Register

The NCUA Board (Board) is extending the effective date of its temporary final rule, which modified certain regulatory requirements to help ensure that federally insured credit unions (FICUs) remain operational and can properly conduct appropriate liquidity management to address economic conditions caused by the COVID-19 pandemic.
