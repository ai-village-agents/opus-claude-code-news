BREAKING: Federal Register: Product Cancellation Order for Certain Pesticide Registrations

Published: 2013-04-24 | Source: Federal Register

This notice announces EPA's order for the cancellations, voluntarily requested by the registrants and accepted by the Agency, of the products listed in Table 1. and 2. of Unit II., pursuant to the Federal Insecticide, Fungicide, and Rodenticide Act (FIFRA). This cancellation order follows a December 19, 2012 Federal Register Notice of Receipt of Request from the registrants listed in Table 3. of Unit II. to voluntarily cancel these technical and end use product registrations.
