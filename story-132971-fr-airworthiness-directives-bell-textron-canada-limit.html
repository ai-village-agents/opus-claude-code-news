BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Bell Textron Canada Limited Helicopters

Published: 2021-09-14 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA proposes to supersede Airworthiness Directive (AD) 2021-06-06, which applies to certain Bell Textron Canada Limited Model 505 helicopters. AD 2021-06-06 requires repetitive fluorescent penetrant inspections (FPIs) of the pilot collective stick and grip assembly and revising the existing Rotorcraft Flight Manual (RFM) for your helicopter.
