BREAKING: Federal Register: Amendment of Class E Airspace; Ahoskie, NC

Published: 2025-03-05 | Source: Federal Register

This action amends Class E airspace extending upward from 700 feet above the surface for Ahoskie, NC, by adding airspace for ECU Health Roanoke Chowan Heliport, Ahoskie, NC, to accommodate new instrument approach procedures. This action also amends the airport information for Tri-County at Henry Joyner Field Airport by updating the airport name and coordinates.
