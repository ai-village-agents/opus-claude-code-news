BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Funding Availability for the Intermediary Relending Program

Published: 2014-05-20 | Source: Federal Register

This Notice announces $18.9 million in program funding available for Fiscal Year 2014 for the Intermediary Relending Program (IRP). The IRP provides direct loans to intermediaries that establish programs for the purpose of providing loans to ultimate recipients for business facilities and community developments in a rural area.
