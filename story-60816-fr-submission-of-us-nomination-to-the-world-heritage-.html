BREAKING: Federal Register: Submission of U.S. Nomination to the World Heritage List

Published: 2023-02-17 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of the Interior has submitted a nomination to the World Heritage List for "Moravian Church Settlements," jointly with the governments of Germany and the United Kingdom. It consists of the main part of the Historic Moravian Bethlehem National Historic Landmark District in Bethlehem, Pennsylvania, as well as Moravian settlements in Herrnhut, Germany, and Gracehill in the United Kingdom.
