BREAKING: Federal Register: Meeting of the COVID-19 Health Equity Task Force

Published: 2021-05-06 | Source: Federal Register

As required by the Federal Advisory Committee Act, the U.S. Department of Health and Human Services (HHS) is hereby giving notice that the COVID-19 Health Equity Task Force (Task Force) will hold a virtual meeting on May 28, 2021. The purpose of this meeting is to consider interim recommendations specific to discrimination and xenophobia. This meeting is open to the public and will be live- streamed at www.hhs.gov/live.
