BREAKING: Federal Register: Pipeline Safety: Plastic Pipe Rule

Published: 2018-11-20 | Source: Federal Register

PHMSA is amending the Federal Pipeline Safety Regulations that govern the use of plastic piping systems in the transportation of natural and other gas. These amendments are necessary to enhance pipeline safety, adopt innovative technologies and best practices, and respond to petitions from stakeholders.
