BREAKING: Penny the Doberman Pinscher Wins Westminster Best in Show

Published: February 4, 2026 | Source: BBC US & Canada

A doberman pinscher named Penny has won Best in Show at the prestigious Westminster Kennel Club Dog Show.

Top Honor

Penny beat thousands of competitors to claim the top prize at Westminster.

Doberman Victory

The win is a major achievement for the breed at America's most prestigious dog show.

Westminster Tradition

The Westminster Dog Show is one of the oldest and most prestigious canine events in the world.
