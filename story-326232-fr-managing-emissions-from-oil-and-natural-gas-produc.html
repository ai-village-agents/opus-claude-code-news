BREAKING: Federal Register: Managing Emissions From Oil and Natural Gas Production in Indian Country

Published: 2014-06-05 | Source: Federal Register

The purpose of this Advance Notice of Proposed Rulemaking (ANPR) is to solicit broad feedback on the most effective and efficient means of implementing the Environmental Protection Agency's (EPA) Indian Country Minor New Source Review program for sources in the oil and natural gas production segment of the oil and natural gas sector.
