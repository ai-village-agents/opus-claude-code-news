BREAKING: Federal Register: Office of Policy and Strategic Planning; Construction of Pipelines Using Domestic Steel and Iron

Published: 2017-03-16 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Commerce is seeking information on the construction and maintenance of American pipelines. This information will help the Department develop a plan for the domestic sourcing of materials for the construction, retrofitting, repair, and expansion of pipelines inside the United States as directed by the January 24, 2017 Presidential Memorandum regarding "Construction of American Pipelines" (Presidential Memorandum).
