BREAKING: Federal Register: Office of Indian Economic Development, Tribal Tourism Grant Program (TTGP), Part of the NATIVE Act

Published: 2021-07-13 | Source: Federal Register

The Secretary of the Interior (Secretary), through the Office of Indian Economic Development (OIED), Division of Economic Development (DED), solicits proposals from Indian Tribes and Tribal organizations (as defined in the NATIVE Act) to receive grants to support Tribal tourism feasibility studies and/or Tribal tourism business plan development.
