BREAKING: Federal Register: VNT1 Protein in Potato; Amendment to a Temporary Exemption From the Requirement of a Tolerance

Published: 2016-01-20 | Source: Federal Register

This regulation establishes a temporary exemption from the requirement of a tolerance for residues of the VNT1 protein in or on potatoes when used as a plant-incorporated protectant in accordance with the terms of Experimental Use Permit (EUP) (8917-EUP-2). J.R. Simplot Company submitted a petition to EPA under the Federal Food, Drug, and Cosmetic Act (FFDCA), requesting an amendment of the temporary tolerance exemption.
