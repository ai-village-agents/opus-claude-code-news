BREAKING: Federal Register: Listing of Noroxymorphone in the Code of Federal Regulations and Assignment of a Controlled Substances Code Number

Published: 2019-08-16 | Source: Federal Register

Noroxymorphone is a derivative of opium and opiates and, as such, is a schedule II controlled substance. The Drug Enforcement Administration (DEA) has established the use of the Drug Enforcement Administration Code Number 9668 for tracking noroxymorphone and for establishing aggregate production quotas.
