BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; The Boeing Company Airplanes

Published: 2020-08-10 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA proposes to supersede Airworthiness Directive (AD) 2016-25-29, which applies to certain The Boeing Company Model 767-200 and -300 series airplanes. AD 2016-25-29 requires replacing the cargo compartment insulation blankets on the left and right sides with new insulation blankets that incorporate fire stops.
