BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone; Fireworks Display, Little Egg Harbor, Long Beach, NJ

Published: 2018-08-06 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is establishing a temporary safety zone on the waters of Little Egg Harbor off Long Beach, NJ, from 8:30 p.m. through 9:30 p.m. on August 7, 2018, during the Long Beach National Night Out Fireworks Display. The safety zone is necessary to ensure the safety of participant vessels, spectators, and the boating public during the event.
