BREAKING: Federal Register: Cardiac Troponin Assays; Public Workshop; Request for Comments

Published: 2017-07-31 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA, the Agency, or we) is announcing the following public workshop entitled "Cardiac Troponin Assays." The purpose of the workshop is to discuss the development of innovative troponin assays designed to aid in the diagnosis of myocardial infarction (MI) and additional clinical uses of these assays.
