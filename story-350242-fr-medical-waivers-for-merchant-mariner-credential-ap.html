BREAKING: Federal Register: Medical Waivers for Merchant Mariner Credential Applicants With a History of Seizure Disorders

Published: 2013-03-25 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is seeking public comment regarding criteria for granting medical waivers to merchant mariners with a history of seizure disorders. Coast Guard regulations provide that convulsive disorders (also known as seizure disorders) are conditions that may lead to disqualification for a merchant mariner credential (MMC).
