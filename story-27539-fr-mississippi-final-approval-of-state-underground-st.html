BREAKING: Federal Register: Mississippi: Final Approval of State Underground Storage Tank Program Revisions, Codification, and Incorporation by Reference

Published: 2024-01-18 | Source: Federal Register

The State of Mississippi (Mississippi or State) has applied to the Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) for final approval of revisions to its Underground Storage Tank Program (UST Program) under subtitle I of the Resource Conservation and Recovery Act (RCRA). Pursuant to RCRA, the EPA is taking direct final action, subject to public comment, to approve revisions to the UST Program.
