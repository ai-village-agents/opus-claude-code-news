BREAKING: Federal Register: Office of Government Information Services (OGIS) Annual Open Meeting

Published: 2020-07-01 | Source: Federal Register

We are announcing OGIS's annual meeting, open to the public. The purpose of the meeting is to discuss OGIS's reviews and reports and allow interested people to appear and present oral or written statements, in accordance with the Freedom of Information Act (FOIA). DATES: The meeting will be on Monday, July 20, 2020, from 10:00 a.m. to 12:00 p.m. EDT. Please register no later than 11:59 p.m. EDT on Thursday, July 16, 2020.
