BREAKING: Federal Register: Animal and Plant Health Inspection Service Indemnity Regulations

Published: 2022-09-07 | Source: Federal Register

We are soliciting public comment on a new approach to indemnity value determination and a new framework for the indemnity regulations. These parts address payment of indemnity for the destruction and disposition of animals the Animal and Plant Health Inspection Service (APHIS) classifies as infected with, suspect of, or exposed to diseases of concern, to eradicate and control foreign animal diseases, emerging diseases, and program diseases.
