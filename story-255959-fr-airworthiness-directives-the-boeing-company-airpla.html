BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; The Boeing Company Airplanes

Published: 2016-02-22 | Source: Federal Register

We are adopting a new airworthiness directive (AD) for all The Boeing Company Model 767 airplanes. This AD was prompted by reports of cracking at a central part of the structure. This AD requires repetitive inspections of the skin hidden by the upper and lower splice fittings on both sides of the fuselage, and corrective action if necessary.
