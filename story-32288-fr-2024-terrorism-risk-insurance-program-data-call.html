BREAKING: Federal Register: 2024 Terrorism Risk Insurance Program Data Call

Published: 2024-03-19 | Source: Federal Register

Pursuant to the Terrorism Risk Insurance Act of 2002, as amended (TRIA), insurers that participate in the Terrorism Risk Insurance Program (TRIP or Program) are directed to submit information for the 2024 TRIP Data Call, which covers the reporting period from January 1, 2023 to December 31, 2023. Participating insurers are required to register and report information in a series of forms approved by the Office of Management and Budget (OMB).
