BREAKING: Federal Register: Credit for Indian Coal Production and Inflation Adjustment Factor for Calendar Year 2015

Published: 2016-01-20 | Source: Federal Register

The 2015 inflation adjustment factor is used in determining the availability of the credit for Indian coal production under section 45. Section 186 of Division Q of the Consolidated Appropriations Act, 2016 (Pub. L. 114-113) extends the credit period for the Indian coal production credit from a 9-year period beginning on January 1, 2006, to an 11-year period beginning on January 1, 2006.
