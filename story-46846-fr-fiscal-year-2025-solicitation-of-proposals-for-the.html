BREAKING: Federal Register: Fiscal Year 2025 Solicitation of Proposals for the National Rural Transit Assistance Program

Published: 2024-09-12 | Source: Federal Register

The Federal Transit Administration (FTA) announces the opportunity to apply for $3,250,723 in Fiscal Year (FY) 2024 Rural Transportation Assistance Program funds through a competitive cooperative agreement award. FTA is soliciting proposals under the agency's Formula Grants for Rural Areas Program to select an entity to administer a National Rural Transit Assistance Program (National RTAP).
