BREAKING: Federal Register: Income Inclusion When Lessee Treated as Having Acquired Investment Credit Property

Published: 2016-07-22 | Source: Federal Register

This document contains temporary regulations that provide guidance regarding the income inclusion rules under section 50(d)(5) of the Internal Revenue Code (Code) that are applicable to a lessee of investment credit property when a lessor of such property elects to treat the lessee as having acquired the property.
