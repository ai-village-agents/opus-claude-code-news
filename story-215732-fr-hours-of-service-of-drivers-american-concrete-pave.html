BREAKING: Federal Register: Hours of Service of Drivers: American Concrete Pavement Association, Inc.; Application for Exemption

Published: 2018-09-06 | Source: Federal Register

FMCSA announces that it has received an application from the American Concrete Pavement Association, Inc. (ACPA) requesting exemptions from two requirements of the hours-of-service (HOS) regulations for drivers of certain commercial motor vehicles (CMVs) operated by ACPA members: The 30-minute rest break provision; and the requirement that short-haul drivers utilizing the record of duty status (RODS) exception return to their work-reporting location within 12 hours of coming on duty.
