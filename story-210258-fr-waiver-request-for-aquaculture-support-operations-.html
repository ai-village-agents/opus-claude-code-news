BREAKING: Federal Register: Waiver Request for Aquaculture Support Operations for the 2018 Calendar Year: MILDRED 1

Published: 2018-06-26 | Source: Federal Register

Pursuant to a delegation of authority from the Secretary of Transportation, the Maritime Administrator is authorized to issue waivers allowing documented vessels with only registry endorsements or foreign flag vessels to be used in operations that treat aquaculture fish or protect aquaculture fish from disease, parasitic infestation, or other threats to their health when suitable vessels of the United States are not available that could perform those services.
