BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Determinations; Culturally Significant Object Imported for Exhibition-Determinations: “Manet and Modern Beauty” Exhibition

Published: 2018-11-27 | Source: Federal Register

Notice is hereby given of the following determinations: I hereby determine that a certain object to be included in the exhibition "Manet and Modern Beauty," imported from abroad for temporary exhibition within the United States, is of cultural significance.
