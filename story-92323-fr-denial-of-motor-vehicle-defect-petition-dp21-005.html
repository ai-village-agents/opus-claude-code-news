BREAKING: Federal Register: Denial of Motor Vehicle Defect Petition, DP21-005

Published: 2022-03-30 | Source: Federal Register

This notice sets forth the reasons for the denial of a petition submitted on September 27, 2021, by Mr. James Lamb to NHTSA's Office of Defects Investigation (ODI). The petition requests that the Agency initiate an investigation into alleged "defects in the 2006 J1939 databus," citing a 2016 research paper published through the University of Michigan.
