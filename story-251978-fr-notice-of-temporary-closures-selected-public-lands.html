BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Temporary Closures: Selected Public Lands in La Paz County, AZ

Published: 2017-12-29 | Source: Federal Register

Notice is hereby given that temporary closures will be in effect on public lands administered by the Bureau of Land Management (BLM), Lake Havasu Field Office, during the Best in the Desert (BITD) Racing Association GMZ Utility Terrain Vehicle Winter Nationals Parker "250" and BlueWater Resort Parker "425" official permitted off- highway-vehicle (OHV) events.
