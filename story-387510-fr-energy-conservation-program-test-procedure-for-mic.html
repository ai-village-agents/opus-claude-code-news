BREAKING: Federal Register: Energy Conservation Program: Test Procedure for Microwave Ovens

Published: 2012-06-05 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Department of Energy (DOE) initiated a test procedure rulemaking to develop active mode testing methodologies for residential microwave ovens. DOE conducted testing to evaluate potential test procedure amendments to provide methods of measuring energy use for microwave ovens, including both microwave-only ovens and convection microwave cooking ovens.
