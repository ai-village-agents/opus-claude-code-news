BREAKING: Federal Register: Endangered and Threatened Species; Initiation of 5-Year Reviews for Black Abalone and White Abalone

Published: 2023-07-07 | Source: Federal Register

NMFS announces its intent to conduct 5-year reviews for black abalone (Haliotis cracherodii) and white abalone (H. sorenseni), both listed as endangered under the Endangered Species Act (ESA). NMFS is required by the ESA to conduct 5-year reviews to ensure that the listing classifications of species are accurate.
