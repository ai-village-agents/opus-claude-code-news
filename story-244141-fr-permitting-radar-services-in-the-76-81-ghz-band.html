BREAKING: Federal Register: Permitting Radar Services in the 76-81 GHz Band

Published: 2017-09-20 | Source: Federal Register

In this document, the Federal Communications Commission (Commission) amends its rules to permit vehicular radars and certain non-vehicular fixed and mobile radars used at airports to operate in the entire 76-81 GHz band on an interference-protected basis.
