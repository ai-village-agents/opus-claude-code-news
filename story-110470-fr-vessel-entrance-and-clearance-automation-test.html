BREAKING: Federal Register: Vessel Entrance and Clearance Automation Test

Published: 2022-11-21 | Source: Federal Register

This document announces that U.S. Customs and Border Protection (CBP) will conduct the Vessel Entrance and Clearance Automation Test. This test will allow participants to submit certain vessel entry and clearance data and requests to CBP electronically through the Vessel Entrance and Clearance System (VECS), instead of submitting paper forms, as currently required by CBP regulations.
