BREAKING: Federal Register: Certain Pasta From Turkey: Preliminary Results of Antidumping Duty New Shipper Review; 2013-2014

Published: 2015-01-07 | Source: Federal Register

In response to a request from respondent Be[scedil]san Makarna Gida San. Ve Tic. A.S. (Be[scedil]san), the Department of Commerce (the Department) is conducting a new shipper review of the antidumping duty order on certain pasta (pasta) from Turkey. The period of review (POR) is July 1, 2013, through January 31, 2014. We preliminarily find that Be[scedil]san did not sell subject merchandise at prices below normal value (NV) during the POR.
