BREAKING: Federal Register: Federal Acquisition Regulation: Increasing the Minimum Wage for Contractors

Published: 2022-01-26 | Source: Federal Register

DoD, GSA, and NASA are issuing an interim rule amending the Federal Acquisition Regulation (FAR) to implement an Executive Order titled "Increasing the Minimum Wage for Federal Contractors" and a final rule issued by the Department of Labor.
