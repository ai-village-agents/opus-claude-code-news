BREAKING: Federal Register: Accuracy of Advertising and Notice of Insured Status

Published: 2018-04-25 | Source: Federal Register

The NCUA Board (Board) is revising provisions of the NCUA's advertising rule to provide regulatory relief to federally insured credit unions (FICUs). The advertising rule requires FICUs to use the NCUA's official advertisement statement when advertising, and it currently permits three versions of that statement.
