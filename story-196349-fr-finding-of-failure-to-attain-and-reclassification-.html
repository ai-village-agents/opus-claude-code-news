BREAKING: Federal Register: Finding of Failure To Attain and Reclassification of Denver Area for the 2008 Ozone National Ambient Air Quality Standard

Published: 2019-12-26 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is determining that the Denver-Boulder-Greeley-Ft. Collins-Loveland, Colorado nonattainment area (Denver Area) failed to attain the 2008 ozone National Ambient Air Quality Standard (NAAQS) by the applicable attainment date for "Moderate" nonattainment areas. The effect of failing to attain by the attainment date is that the area will be reclassified by operation of law to "Serious" upon the effective date of this final reclassification action.
