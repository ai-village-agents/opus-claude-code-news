BREAKING: Federal Register: Generalized System of Preferences (GSP): Change in Hearing Date for the 2011 Annual GSP Product Review

Published: 2012-03-16 | Source: Federal Register

The date of the hearing for the 2011 Annual GSP Product Review is changed to Thursday, March 29, 2012. Post-hearing comments are due Monday, April 16, 2012.
