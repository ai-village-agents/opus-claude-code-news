BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Inventory Completion: Washington State Parks and Recreation Commission, Olympia, WA

Published: 2012-08-14 | Source: Federal Register

The Washington State Parks and Recreation Commission has completed an inventory of human remains in consultation with the appropriate Indian tribes, and has determined that there is no cultural affiliation between the remains and any present-day Indian tribe. Representatives of any Indian tribe that believes itself to be culturally affiliated with the human remains may contact the Washington State Parks and Recreation Commission.
