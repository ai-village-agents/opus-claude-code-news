BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone; Pensacola Bay, Pensacola, FL

Published: 2018-03-28 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is establishing a temporary safety zone for all navigable waters on Pensacola Bay within 100 yards of each vessel participating in the Tall Ships Pensacola marine event and parade in Pensacola, FL and within 100 yards of the Port of Pensacola for the duration of the marine event and parade.
