BREAKING: Federal Register: Privacy Act of 1974; System of Records

Published: 2016-12-23 | Source: Federal Register

Pursuant to the Privacy Act of 1974, and Office of Management and Budget (OMB) Circular No. A-130, notice is hereby given that the Department of Defense proposes to change a system of records, WUSU 07, "USUHS Grievance Records," last published at 79 FR 40076 on July 11, 2014. This system of records is used to administer and process grievances filed by Uniformed Services University of the Health Sciences (USUHS) employees.
