BREAKING: Federal Register: Regulation of Fuel and Fuel Additives: Modification to Octamix Waiver (TOLAD MFA-10A)

Published: 2012-11-01 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency reconsidered a portion of a fuel waiver granted under section 211(f) of the Clean Air Act to the Texas Methanol Corporation (Texas Methanol) on June 14, 2012, by issuing a notice to approve the use of an alternative corrosion inhibitor, TOLAD MFA-10A, in Texas Methanol's gasoline-alcohol fuel, OCTAMIX.
