BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Rolls-Royce Deutschland Ltd. & Co. KG Engines

Published: 2023-09-21 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA is adopting a new airworthiness directive (AD) for all Rolls-Royce Deutschland Ltd. & Co. KG (RRD) Model RB211 Trent 768-60, 772-60, and 772B-60 engines. This AD was prompted by reports of cracks on affected intermediate-pressure compressor (IPC) rotor shaft balance lands.
