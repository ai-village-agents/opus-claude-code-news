BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Proposed New Fee Sites for the Ouachita National Forest

Published: 2019-09-13 | Source: Federal Register

The Ouachita National Forest, in Arkansas, is proposing to charge new fees at two recreation sites: Little Missouri Falls Day Use Site and Wolf Pen Gap Off-Highway Vehicle (OHV) Trail Complex. These sites have had additional amenities added to improve services and experiences. Fees are assessed based on the level of amenities and service provided, cost of operation and maintenance, market assessment, and public comment.
