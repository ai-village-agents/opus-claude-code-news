BREAKING: Federal Register: Privacy Act of 1974; System of Records

Published: 2025-08-01 | Source: Federal Register

Pursuant to the provisions of the Privacy Act of 1974, as amended, the Department of the Housing and Urban Development (HUD), Office of the Chief Financial Officer (OCFO) Accounting Operations Center, is modifying a system of records titled, "Financial Data Mart." Financial Data Mart (FDM) is a warehouse of data extracted from various HUD systems and is supported by several query tools for improved financial and program data reporting.
