BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; ATR-GIE Avions de Transport Régional Airplanes

Published: 2018-03-29 | Source: Federal Register

We propose to adopt a new airworthiness directive (AD) for all ATR-GIE Avions de Transport R[eacute]gional Model ATR42 and Model ATR72 airplanes. This proposed AD was prompted by reports of cracking in main landing gear (MLG) universal joints (U-joints). This proposed AD would require repetitive detailed inspections of the affected U-joints for cracks, and replacement if necessary.
