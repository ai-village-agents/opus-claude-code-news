BREAKING: Federal Register: Americans With Disabilities Act: Proposed Circular Amendment 1

Published: 2014-02-19 | Source: Federal Register

The Federal Transit Administration (FTA) has placed in the docket and on its Web site proposed guidance in the form of four additional circular chapters to help transportation providers meet the requirements of the U.S. Department of Transportation's (DOT) Americans with Disabilities Act (ADA) regulations.
