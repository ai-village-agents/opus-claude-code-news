BREAKING: Federal Register: Extension of the Application Deadline Date; Applications for the Supplemental Support Under the American Rescue Plan (SSARP)

Published: 2022-03-31 | Source: Federal Register

On February 3, 2022, the Department of Education (Department) published in the Federal Register a notice inviting applications for the Supplemental Support under the American Rescue Plan (NIA), Assistance Listing Number (ALN) 84.425T. The NIA established a deadline date of April 4, 2022, for the transmittal of applications. This notice extends the deadline date for transmittal of applications until April 8, 2022.
