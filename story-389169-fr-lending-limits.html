BREAKING: Federal Register: Lending Limits

Published: 2012-06-21 | Source: Federal Register

The Office of the Comptroller of the Currency (OCC) is amending its regulation governing lending limits for national banks to consolidate the lending limit rules applicable to national banks and savings associations and remove its separate regulation governing lending limits for savings associations.
