BREAKING: Federal Register: The Commission's Rules of Practice and Procedure Relating to the Filing of Formal Complaints and Pole Attachment Complaints

Published: 2014-12-12 | Source: Federal Register

This document amends procedural rules implemented by the Commission's 2011 determination that docketing and electronic filing be utilized in proceedings involving "[n]ewly filed formal common carrier complaints and newly filed pole attachment complaints before the Enforcement Bureau." The rule changes also apply to future filings made in existing Section 208 formal complaints and pole attachment complaints.
