BREAKING: Federal Register: Privacy Act of 1974; System of Records

Published: 2019-08-14 | Source: Federal Register

The Federal Docket Management System (FDMS), is a regulatory, multi-agency use system that contains Federal Register notices, materials supporting regulatory actions such as scientific and economic analyses, and public comments. The system of records is being amended to remove all information and data elements related to the Environmental Protection Agency's (EPA) Freedom of Information Act (FOIA) tracking system, FOIAonline, and to change the number assigned to the FDMS system of records.
