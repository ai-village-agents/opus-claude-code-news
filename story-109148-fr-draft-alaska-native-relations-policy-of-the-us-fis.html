BREAKING: Federal Register: Draft Alaska Native Relations Policy of the U.S. Fish and Wildlife Service

Published: 2022-11-03 | Source: Federal Register

We, the U.S. Fish and Wildlife Service (Service), are making available for public comment our draft Alaska Native Relations policy. The purpose of this policy is to build on the Service's existing Native American policy by providing additional clarity for employees on the Service's relationships with Tribes in Alaska, Alaska Native organizations, and Alaska Native corporations.
