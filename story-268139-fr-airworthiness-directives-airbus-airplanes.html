BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Airbus Airplanes

Published: 2016-07-07 | Source: Federal Register

We are revising an earlier proposed airworthiness directive (AD) for all Airbus Model A300 B4-603, B4-620, B4-622, B4-605R, B4- 622R, F4-605R, F4-622R, and C4-605R variant F airplanes. The NPRM proposed to require repetitive detailed inspections of the lower frame fittings, related investigative actions, and corrective actions if necessary.
