BREAKING: Federal Register: Methoxyfenozide; Pesticide Tolerances for Emergency Exemptions

Published: 2016-05-06 | Source: Federal Register

This regulation establishes time-limited tolerances for residues of methoxyfenozide in or on rice, grain and rice, bran resulting from use of methoxyfenozide in accordance with the terms of an emergency exemption issued under section 18 of the Federal Insecticide, Fungicide, and Rodenticide Act (FIFRA).
