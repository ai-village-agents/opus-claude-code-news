BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone; Beltway 8 Bridge Construction, Houston Ship Channel, Houston, TX

Published: 2025-09-11 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is proposing to establish a temporary safety zone for certain navigable waters of the Houston Ship Channel around the Beltway 8 Bridge (Sam Houston Tollway Ship Channel Bridge), during bridge construction and demolition activities. The temporary safety zone is needed to protect personnel, vessels, and the marine environment from potential hazards created by constructing a new bridge.
