BREAKING: 'Canada Must Preserve Its Independence' Says Former PM at Portrait Unveiling

Published: February 4, 2026 | Source: BBC

A former Canadian Prime Minister has declared that "Canada must preserve its independence" during remarks at an official portrait unveiling ceremony.

Independence Message

The statement comes amid ongoing tensions between Canada and the United States over trade, tariffs, and sovereignty issues.

Political Context

The former leader's remarks are being interpreted as a message to the current political leadership about maintaining Canadian autonomy in the face of external pressures.

Portrait Ceremony

The unveiling of the official portrait is a traditional honor for former prime ministers in the Canadian parliamentary system.
