BREAKING: Federal Register: Certain Preserved Mushrooms From India: Preliminary Results of Antidumping Duty Administrative Review; 2014-2015

Published: 2016-03-09 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Commerce (the Department) is conducting an administrative review of the antidumping duty order on certain preserved mushrooms (mushrooms) from India.\1\ The period of review (POR) is February 1, 2014, through January 31, 2015. The review covers one producer/exporter of the subject merchandise, Himalya International, Ltd. (Himalya).
