BREAKING: Federal Register: Publication of Offender Tracking System Standard, NIJ Standard-1004.00, and Request for Expressions of Interest From Manufacturers and Conformity Assessment Bodies

Published: 2016-07-15 | Source: Federal Register

The National Institute of Justice (NIJ) announces publication of Offender Tracking System Standard, NIJ Standard-1004.00. The document can be found here: https://www.ncjrs.gov/pdffiles1/nij/ 249810.pdf. NIJ is currently exploring strategies for conformity assessment of offender tracking systems in accordance with this new standard.
