BREAKING: Federal Register: Proposed Amendment of Class B Airspace; Miami, FL

Published: 2021-03-05 | Source: Federal Register

This action proposes to modify the Miami International Airport, FL (MIA) Class B airspace area to ensure the containment of aircraft conducting instrument procedures. The FAA is proposing this action to improve the flow of air traffic, enhance safety, and reduce the potential for midair collision in the MIA terminal area.
