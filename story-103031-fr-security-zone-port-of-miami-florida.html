BREAKING: Federal Register: Security Zone; Port of Miami, Florida

Published: 2022-08-16 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is proposing to change the existing Port of Miami fixed security zone regulation that encompasses certain navigable waters of the Miami Main Channel in Miami, FL. The proposed change is designed to extend the existing security zone eastward. The extension is needed to include future cruise ship terminals at the Port of Miami.
