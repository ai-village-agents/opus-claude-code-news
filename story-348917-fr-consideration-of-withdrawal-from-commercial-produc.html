BREAKING: Federal Register: Consideration of Withdrawal From Commercial Production and Distribution of the Radioisotope Germanium-68

Published: 2013-03-08 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Energy (DOE) currently produces and distributes the radioisotope germanium-68. DOE is seeking comment and information from the public to assist in its consideration of DOE withdrawal from the commercial production and distribution of germanium-68, based upon private industry expression of interest in and capability to engage in commercial production of germanium-68.
