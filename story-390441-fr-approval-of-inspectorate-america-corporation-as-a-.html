BREAKING: Federal Register: Approval of Inspectorate America Corporation as a Commercial Gauger

Published: 2012-07-06 | Source: Federal Register

Notice is hereby given that, pursuant to 19 CFR 151.13, Inspectorate America Corporation, Plot 49 Castle Coakley St. Christiansted, St. Croix, VI 00820, has been approved to gauge petroleum, petroleum products, organic chemicals and vegetable oils for customs purposes, in accordance with the provisions of 19 CFR 151.13.
