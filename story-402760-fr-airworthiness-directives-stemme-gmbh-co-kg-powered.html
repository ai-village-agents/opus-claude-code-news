BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Stemme GmbH & Co. KG Powered Sailplanes

Published: 2012-12-03 | Source: Federal Register

We are adopting a new airworthiness directive (AD) for all Stemme GmbH & Co. KG Models S10, S10-V, and S10-VT powered sailplanes. This AD results from mandatory continuing airworthiness information (MCAI) issued by an aviation authority of another country to identify and correct an unsafe condition on an aviation product.
