BREAKING: Federal Register: Policy on the Temporary Closure of Airports for Nonaeronautical Purposes

Published: 2018-05-29 | Source: Federal Register

This notice announces a proposed update of the FAA policy regarding approval of the temporary closure of a federally obligated airport for a nonaeronautical purpose. Under Federal law, an airport operator that has accepted Federal grants is obligated to maintain the airport for public aviation use. In a limited exception to the general requirement, an airport operator may temporarily close the airport or part of the airport for a nonaeronautical event, if the FAA approves that closure in advance...
