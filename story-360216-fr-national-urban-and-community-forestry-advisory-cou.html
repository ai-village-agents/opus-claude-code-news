BREAKING: Federal Register: National Urban and Community Forestry Advisory Council

Published: 2013-07-16 | Source: Federal Register

The National Urban and Community Forestry Advisory Council, (NUCFAC) will be filling five positions that will expire December 31, 2013. Interested applicants may download a copy of the application and position descriptions from the U.S. Department of Agriculture, Forest Service's Urban and Community Forestry Web site: www.fs.fed.us/ucf/ nucfac.
