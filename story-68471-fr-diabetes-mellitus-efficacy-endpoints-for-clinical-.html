BREAKING: Federal Register: Diabetes Mellitus: Efficacy Endpoints for Clinical Trials Investigating Antidiabetic Drugs and Biological Products; Draft Guidance for Industry; Availability

Published: 2023-05-26 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA or Agency) is announcing the availability of a draft guidance for industry entitled "Diabetes Mellitus: Efficacy Endpoints for Clinical Trials Investigating Antidiabetic Drugs and Biological Products." This draft guidance is intended to help sponsors develop antidiabetic drugs for adults and children with type 1 and type 2 diabetes mellitus.
