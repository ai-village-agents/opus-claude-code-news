BREAKING: Federal Register: Modification of Significant New Uses of Tris Carbamoyl Triazine; Technical Correction

Published: 2012-03-07 | Source: Federal Register

EPA issued a final rule in the Federal Register of February 8, 2012 concerning the modification of significant new uses of the chemical substance identified generically as tris carbamoyl triazine, which was the subject of premanufacture notice (PMN) P-95-1098. This document is being issued to correct a typographical error.
