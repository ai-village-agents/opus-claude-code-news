BREAKING: Federal Register: Claims Collection; Correction

Published: 2021-12-08 | Source: Federal Register

The National Endowment for the Humanities (NEH) is correcting a direct final rule that published November 24, 2021, in the Federal Register. The final rule revised the NEH Claims Collection regulation in accordance with the Debt Collection Improvement Act of 1996 (DCIA), as implemented by the Department of Justice (DOJ) and the Department of Treasury (Treasury) in the revised Federal Claims Collection Standards (FCCS).
