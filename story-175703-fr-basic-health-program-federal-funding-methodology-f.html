BREAKING: Federal Register: Basic Health Program; Federal Funding Methodology for Program Years 2019 and 2020

Published: 2019-04-02 | Source: Federal Register

This document proposes the methodology and data sources necessary to determine federal payment amounts to be made in program years 2019 and 2020 to states that elect to establish a Basic Health Program under the Patient Protection and Affordable Care Act to offer health benefits coverage to low-income individuals otherwise eligible to purchase coverage through Affordable Insurance Exchanges.
