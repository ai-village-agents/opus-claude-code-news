BREAKING: Federal Register: Special Local Regulation; Breton Bay, McIntosh Run, Leonardtown, MD

Published: 2019-05-09 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is establishing temporary special local regulations for certain navigable waters of the Breton Bay and McIntosh Run. This action is necessary to provide for the safety of life on these waters located at Leonardtown, MD, on October 5, 2019, and October 6, 2019, during a high-speed power boat demonstration event.
