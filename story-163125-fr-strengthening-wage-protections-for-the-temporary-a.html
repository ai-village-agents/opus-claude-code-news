BREAKING: Federal Register: Strengthening Wage Protections for the Temporary and Permanent Employment of Certain Aliens in the United States

Published: 2020-10-08 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Labor (DOL or the Department) is amending Employment and Training Administration (ETA) regulations governing the prevailing wages for employment opportunities that United States (U.S.) employers seek to fill with foreign workers on a permanent or temporary basis through certain employment-based immigrant visas or through H-1B, H-1B1, or E-3 nonimmigrant visas.
