BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Realty Action: Modified Competitive Sale of Two Parcels of Public Land in Lincoln County, NV

Published: 2021-05-18 | Source: Federal Register

The Bureau of Land Management (BLM) proposes to offer two parcels of public land totaling 80 acres in Lincoln County, Nevada, by modified competitive sale at not less than each parcel's appraised Fair Market Value (FMV) pursuant to the Lincoln County Conservation, Recreation, and Development Act of 2004 (LCCRDA).
