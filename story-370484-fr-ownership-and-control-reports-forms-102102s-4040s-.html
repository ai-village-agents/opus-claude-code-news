BREAKING: Federal Register: Ownership and Control Reports, Forms 102/102S, 40/40S, and 71

Published: 2013-11-18 | Source: Federal Register

The Commodity Futures Trading Commission ("Commission" or "CFTC") is adopting new rules and related forms to enhance its identification of futures and swap market participants. These final rules will leverage the Commission's current position and transaction reporting programs by requiring the electronic submission of trader identification and market participant data on amended Forms 102 and 40, and on new Form 71.
