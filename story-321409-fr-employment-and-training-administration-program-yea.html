BREAKING: Federal Register: Employment and Training Administration Program Year (PY) 2014 Workforce Investment Act (WIA) Allotments; PY 2014 Wagner-Peyser Act Final Allotments and PY 2014 Workforce Information Grants

Published: 2014-04-11 | Source: Federal Register

This notice announces allotments for PY 2014 for WIA Title I Youth, Adults and Dislocated Worker Activities programs; final allotments for Employment Service (ES) activities under the Wagner- Peyser Act for PY 2014 and Workforce Information Grants allotments for PY 2014. WIA allotments for States and the State final allotments for the Wagner-Peyser Act are based on formulas defined in their respective statutes.
