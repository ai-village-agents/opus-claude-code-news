BREAKING: Federal Register: Confidentiality of Interim Results in Cardiovascular Outcome Safety Trials; Public Hearing; Request for Comments

Published: 2014-07-15 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA) is announcing a public hearing that will provide a forum to discuss confidentiality of interim results for certain cardiovascular outcomes trials (CVOTs) submitted to the Agency while the trials are still ongoing.
