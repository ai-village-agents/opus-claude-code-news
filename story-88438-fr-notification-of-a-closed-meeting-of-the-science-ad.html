BREAKING: Federal Register: Notification of a Closed Meeting of the Science Advisory Board 2021 Scientific and Technological Achievement Awards Panel

Published: 2022-02-09 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) Science Advisory Board (SAB) Staff Office announces a two-day meeting of the Scientific and Technological Achievement Awards (STAA) Panel. The purpose of the meeting is to review the 2021 STAA nominations and to make recommendations for awards. The meeting is closed to the public.
