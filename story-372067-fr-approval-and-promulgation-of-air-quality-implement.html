BREAKING: Federal Register: Approval and Promulgation of Air Quality Implementation Plans; Maryland; State Boards Requirements

Published: 2013-12-06 | Source: Federal Register

EPA proposes to approve the State Implementation Plan (SIP) revision submitted by the State of Maryland for the purpose of addressing the State Boards' requirements for all criteria pollutants of the National Ambient Air Quality Standards (NAAQS). In the Final Rules section of this Federal Register, EPA is approving the State's SIP submittal as a direct final rule without prior proposal because the Agency views this as a noncontroversial submittal and anticipates no adverse comments.
