BREAKING: Federal Register: Utility Scale Wind Towers From the Socialist Republic of Vietnam: Preliminary Results of Antidumping Duty Administrative Review; 2013-2014

Published: 2015-03-09 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Commerce ("the Department") is conducting an administrative review of the antidumping duty order on utility scale wind towers ("wind towers"), from the Socialist Republic of Vietnam ("Vietnam"). The period of review ("POR") is February 13, 2013, through January 31, 2014. The review covers one mandatory respondent, CS Wind Vietnam and CS Wind Corporation ("collectively, CS Wind Group").
