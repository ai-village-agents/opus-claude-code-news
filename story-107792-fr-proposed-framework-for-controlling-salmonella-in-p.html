BREAKING: Federal Register: Proposed Framework for Controlling Salmonella in Poultry

Published: 2022-10-17 | Source: Federal Register

FSIS is hosting a virtual public meeting to discuss a regulatory framework that the Agency is considering for a new strategy to control Salmonella in poultry products and more effectively reduce foodborne Salmonella infections linked to these products. The framework under consideration has been shaped by months of information-gathering and discussions with a wide range of stakeholders, researchers, and scientists.
