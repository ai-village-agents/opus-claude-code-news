BREAKING: Federal Register: Final Environmental Impact Statement, Pure Water San Diego Program, North City Project; San Diego County, California

Published: 2018-04-27 | Source: Federal Register

The Bureau of Reclamation and the City of San Diego have completed a final Environmental Impact Report/Environmental Impact Statement for the North City Project, the first phase of the Pure Water San Diego Program--a water and wastewater facilities plan to produce potable water from recycled water. The Bureau of Reclamation is recommending the Miramar Alternative as the preferred alternative for approval.
