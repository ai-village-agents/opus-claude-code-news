BREAKING: Federal Register: Energy Conservation Program: Test Procedures for Dehumidifiers

Published: 2014-05-21 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Department of Energy (DOE) proposes to revise its test procedures for dehumidifiers, by adding clarifications for equipment setup during testing and correcting the calculations of active mode energy use and an efficiency metric, integrated energy factor (IEF).
