BREAKING: Federal Register: Delay in Issuing 2014 Standards for the Renewable Fuel Standard Program

Published: 2014-12-09 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is announcing that it will not be finalizing 2014 applicable percentage standards under the Renewable Fuel Standard (RFS) program before the end of 2014. In light of this delay in issuing the 2014 RFS standards, the compliance demonstration deadline for the 2013 RFS standards will take place in 2015.
