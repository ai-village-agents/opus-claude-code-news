BREAKING: Federal Register: Acute Ischemic Stroke Medical Devices Trials Workshop; Public Workshop; Request for Comments

Published: 2015-07-09 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA) is announcing the following public workshop entitled "Acute Ischemic Stroke Medical Device Trials Workshop". Acute ischemic stroke medical devices are intended to remove blood clots from the cerebral neurovasculature by mechanical, laser, ultrasound, or a combination of technologies. The purpose of this workshop is to obtain public input and feedback on scientific, clinical, and regulatory considerations associated with acute ischemic stroke medical devices...
