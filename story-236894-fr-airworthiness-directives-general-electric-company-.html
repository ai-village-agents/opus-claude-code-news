BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; General Electric Company Turboshaft Engines

Published: 2017-06-16 | Source: Federal Register

We propose to adopt a new airworthiness directive (AD) for certain General Electric Company (GE) CT7-8A and CT7-9B model turboshaft engines. This proposed AD was prompted by reports from the manufacturer that the high-pressure compressor (HPC) impeller installed on these engines may have suffered from material degradation during the manufacturing process.
