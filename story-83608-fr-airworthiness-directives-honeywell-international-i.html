BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Honeywell International Inc. Engines

Published: 2023-12-06 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA is adopting a new airworthiness directive (AD) for all Honeywell International Inc. (Honeywell) Model AS907-1-1A and AS907-2- 1G engines. This AD was prompted by reports of compressor surge, including a dual engine compressor surge, during takeoff climb out through a steep temperature inversion, causing a loss of engine thrust control.
