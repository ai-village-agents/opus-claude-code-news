BREAKING: Federal Register: Avocados Grown in South Florida; Increased Assessment Rate

Published: 2014-01-16 | Source: Federal Register

This rule increases the assessment rate established for the Avocado Administrative Committee (Committee) for the 2013-14 and subsequent fiscal periods from $0.25 to $0.30 per 55-pound bushel container of Florida avocados handled. The Committee locally administers the marketing order, which regulates the handling of avocados grown in South Florida.
