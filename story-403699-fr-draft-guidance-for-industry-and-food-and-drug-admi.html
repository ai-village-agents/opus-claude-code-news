BREAKING: Federal Register: Draft Guidance for Industry and Food and Drug Administration Staff; Design Considerations for Devices Intended for Home Use; Availability

Published: 2012-12-13 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA) is announcing the availability of the draft guidance entitled "Design Considerations for Devices Intended for Home Use." This document is intended to assist manufacturers in designing and developing home use medical devices that comply with applicable standards of safety and effectiveness and other regulatory requirements.
