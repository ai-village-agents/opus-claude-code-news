BREAKING: Federal Register: Debit Card Interchange Fees and Routing

Published: 2012-08-03 | Source: Federal Register

The Board has amended the provisions in Regulation II (Debit Card Interchange Fees and Routing) that govern adjustments to debit card interchange transaction fees to make an allowance for fraud- prevention costs incurred by issuers.
