BREAKING: Federal Register: Request for Information Regarding Complaints From Private Education Loan Borrowers

Published: 2012-06-14 | Source: Federal Register

Section 1035 of the Dodd-Frank Wall Street Reform and Consumer Protection Act of 2010 (Dodd-Frank Act) established a Private Education Loan Ombudsman (Ombudsman) within the Consumer Financial Protection Bureau (Bureau) to provide timely assistance to borrowers of private education loans.
