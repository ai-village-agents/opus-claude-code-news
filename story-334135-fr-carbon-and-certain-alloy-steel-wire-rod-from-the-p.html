BREAKING: Federal Register: Carbon and Certain Alloy Steel Wire Rod From the People's Republic of China: Preliminary Determination of Sales at Less Than Fair Value and Preliminary Affirmative Determination of Critical Circumstances, in Part

Published: 2014-09-08 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Commerce ("Department") preliminarily determines that carbon and certain alloy steel wire rod ("steel wire rod") from the People's Republic of China ("PRC") is being, or is likely to be, sold in the United States at less than fair value ("LTFV"), as provided in section 733(b) of the Tariff Act of 1930, as amended ("the Act").
