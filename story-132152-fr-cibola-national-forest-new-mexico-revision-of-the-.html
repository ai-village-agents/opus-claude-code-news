BREAKING: Federal Register: Cibola National Forest; New Mexico; Revision of the Land Management Plan for the Cibola National Forest

Published: 2021-09-02 | Source: Federal Register

The Forest Service is revising the Cibola National Forest's Land Management Plan (Forest Plan). The Forest Service has prepared a Final Environmental Impact Statement (FEIS) for its revised Forest Plan and a draft Record of Decision (ROD).
