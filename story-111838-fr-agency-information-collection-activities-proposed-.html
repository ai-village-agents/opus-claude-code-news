BREAKING: Federal Register: Agency Information Collection Activities: Proposed Collection; Comment Request

Published: 2022-12-08 | Source: Federal Register

NARA is proposing to request that the Office of Management and Budget (OMB) renew approval of an information collection used by researchers who wish to do biomedical statistical research in archival records containing highly personal information. We invite you to comment on this proposed information collection.
