BREAKING: Federal Register: Request for Comment: U.S. Elections Survey-Election Office Staffing

As part of its continuing effort to reduce paperwork burdens, and as required by the Paperwork Reduction Act of 1995 (PRA), the EAC announces an information collection and seeks public comment on the provisions thereof. The EAC intends to submit this proposed information collection (U.S. Elections Survey--Election Office Staffing) to the Director of the Office of Management and Budget for approval.
