BREAKING: Federal Register: HHS-Operated Risk Adjustment Data Validation Stakeholder Meeting

Published: 2013-05-29 | Source: Federal Register

This notice announces a public meeting on the Affordable Care Act HHS-operated risk adjustment data validation process. The purpose of this public meeting is to provide opportunity to discuss the HHS risk adjustment data validation process that will be conducted when HHS operates the risk adjustment program on behalf of a state under the Affordable Care Act.
