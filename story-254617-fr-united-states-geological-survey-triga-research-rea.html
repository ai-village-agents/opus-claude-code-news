BREAKING: Federal Register: United States Geological Survey, TRIGA Research Reactor

Published: 2016-02-05 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Nuclear Regulatory Commission (NRC) is considering an application for the renewal of Facility Operating License No. R-113, which authorizes the United States Geological Survey (USGS or the licensee) to operate the USGS Training, Research, Isotope Production, General Atomics (TRIGA) Research Reactor (GSTR) at a maximum steady- state thermal power of 1.0 megawatts (MW).
