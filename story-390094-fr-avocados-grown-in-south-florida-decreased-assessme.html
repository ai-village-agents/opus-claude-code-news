BREAKING: Federal Register: Avocados Grown in South Florida; Decreased Assessment Rate

Published: 2012-07-02 | Source: Federal Register

This rule decreases the assessment rate established for the Avocado Administrative Committee (Committee) for the 2012-13 and subsequent fiscal periods from $0.37 to $0.25 per 55-pound bushel container of Florida avocados handled. The Committee locally administers the marketing order which regulates the handling of avocados grown in South Florida.
