BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone; Chicago to Mackinac Race; Lake Michigan; Chicago, IL

Published: 2013-06-25 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard will enforce the safety zone on Lake Michigan near Chicago, Illinois for the 105th Race to Mackinac. This zone will be enforced from 2 p.m. until 4:30 p.m. on July 12, and from 9 a.m. until 3 p.m. on July 13, 2013. This action is necessary and intended to ensure safety of life on the navigable waters during the 105th Race to Mackinac.
