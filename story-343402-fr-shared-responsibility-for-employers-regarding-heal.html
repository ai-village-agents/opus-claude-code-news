BREAKING: Federal Register: Shared Responsibility for Employers Regarding Health Coverage

Published: 2013-01-02 | Source: Federal Register

This document contains proposed regulations providing guidance under section 4980H of the Internal Revenue Code (Code) with respect to the shared responsibility for employers regarding employee health coverage. These proposed regulations would affect only employers that meet the definition of "applicable large employer" as described in these proposed regulations.
