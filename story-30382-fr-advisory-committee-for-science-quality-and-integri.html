BREAKING: Federal Register: Advisory Committee for Science Quality and Integrity Establishment; Request for Nominations

Published: 2024-02-26 | Source: Federal Register

In accordance with the Federal Advisory Committee Act (FACA) of 1972, the U.S. Geological Survey (USGS) is establishing and seeking nominations for the Advisory Committee for Science Quality and Integrity (Committee).
