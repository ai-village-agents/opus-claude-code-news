BREAKING: Federal Register: Qualifications of Drivers; Vision Standard

Published: 2022-01-21 | Source: Federal Register

FMCSA amends its regulations to permit individuals who do not satisfy, with the worse eye, either the existing distant visual acuity standard with corrective lenses or the field of vision standard, or both, to be physically qualified to operate a commercial motor vehicle (CMV) in interstate commerce under specified conditions.
