BREAKING: Federal Register: Establishment of the Ocean Research Advisory Panel and Solicitation of Nominations for Membership

Published: 2022-06-29 | Source: Federal Register

(Mac) Thornberry National Defense Authorization Act for Fiscal Year 2021 (H.R. 6395) Act and the Federal Advisory Committee Act (FACA), the NOAA Administrator and the Co-Chairs of the Ocean Policy Committee (OPC) announce the establishment of the Ocean Research Advisory Panel (ORAP).
