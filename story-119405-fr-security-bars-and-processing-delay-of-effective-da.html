BREAKING: Federal Register: Security Bars and Processing; Delay of Effective Date

Published: 2021-03-22 | Source: Federal Register

On December 23, 2020, the Department of Homeland Security ("DHS") and the Department of Justice ("DOJ") (collectively, "the Departments") published a final rule ("Security Bars rule") to clarify that the "danger to the security of the United States" standard in the statutory bar to eligibility for asylum and withholding of removal encompasses certain emergency public health concerns and to make certain other changes; that rule was scheduled to take effect on January 22, 2021.
