BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Intent to Suspend Certain Pesticide Registrations

Published: 2012-05-30 | Source: Federal Register

Pursuant to section 6(f)(2) of the Federal Insecticide, Fungicide and Rodenticide Act (FIFRA), EPA is publishing this Notice of Intent to Suspend affecting several pesticide products. This Notice is issued by EPA pursuant to section 3(c)(2)(B) of FIFRA.
