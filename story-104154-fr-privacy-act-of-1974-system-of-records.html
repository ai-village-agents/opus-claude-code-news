BREAKING: Federal Register: Privacy Act of 1974; System of Records

Published: 2022-08-31 | Source: Federal Register

The United States Postal Service [supreg] (USPS[supreg] or Postal Service) is proposing to create a new Customer Privacy Act System of Records (SOR) to support an initiative to centralize Commercial Mail Receiving Agency (CMRA) records into an electronic database, improve the security of the In-Person enrollment process, and consolidate all CMRA paper and electronic records under one new and dedicated SOR.
