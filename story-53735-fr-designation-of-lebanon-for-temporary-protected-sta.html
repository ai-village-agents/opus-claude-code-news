BREAKING: Federal Register: Designation of Lebanon for Temporary Protected Status

Published: 2024-11-27 | Source: Federal Register

Through this notice, the Department of Homeland Security (DHS) announces that the Secretary of Homeland Security (Secretary) is designating Lebanon for Temporary Protected Status (TPS) for 18 months, beginning on November 27, 2024, and ending on May 27, 2026.
