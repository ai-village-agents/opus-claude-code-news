BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Bae Systems (Operations) Limited Airplanes

Published: 2014-02-07 | Source: Federal Register

We are adopting a new airworthiness directive (AD) for all Bae Systems (Operations) Limited Model BAe 146 series airplanes and Model Avro 146-RJ series airplanes. This AD was prompted by reports of excess solder deposited during overhaul on the frangible plug of a fire extinguisher, which prevented the release of the extinguishant.
