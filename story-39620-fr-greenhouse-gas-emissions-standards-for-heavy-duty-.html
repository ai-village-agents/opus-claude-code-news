BREAKING: Federal Register: Greenhouse Gas Emissions Standards for Heavy-Duty Vehicles-Phase 3; Correction

Published: 2024-06-17 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is issuing a correction to a final rule published in the Federal Register of Monday, April 22, 2024, which will be effective June 21, 2024. The final rule established new emission standards for heavy-duty highway vehicles, along with several amendments for a wide range of highway and nonroad engines and vehicles.
