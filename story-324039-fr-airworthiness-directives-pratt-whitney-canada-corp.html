BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Pratt & Whitney Canada Corp. Turboprop Engines

Published: 2014-05-12 | Source: Federal Register

We are revising an earlier proposed airworthiness directive (AD) for all Pratt & Whitney Canada Corp. (P&WC) PT6A-114 and PT6A-114A turboprop engines. The NPRM proposed to require initial and repetitive borescope inspections (BSIs) of compressor turbine (CT) blades, and the removal from service of blades that fail inspection.
