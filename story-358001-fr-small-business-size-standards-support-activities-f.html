BREAKING: Federal Register: Small Business Size Standards: Support Activities for Mining

Published: 2013-06-20 | Source: Federal Register

The United States Small Business Administration (SBA) is increasing the small business size standards for three of the four industries in North American Industry Classification System (NAICS) Subsector 213, Support Activities for Mining, that are based on average annual receipts.
