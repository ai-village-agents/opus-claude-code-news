BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; The Boeing Company Airplanes

Published: 2013-07-22 | Source: Federal Register

We are adopting a new airworthiness directive (AD) for all The Boeing Company Model 727 airplanes. This AD was prompted by reports of spanwise cracks and corrosion in the wing center box upper skin and rear spar upper chord between left buttock line (LBL) 70.50 and right buttock line (RBL) 70.50 at body station (STA) 870.
