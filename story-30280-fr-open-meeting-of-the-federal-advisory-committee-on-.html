BREAKING: Federal Register: Open Meeting of the Federal Advisory Committee on Insurance

Published: 2024-02-23 | Source: Federal Register

This notice announces that the U.S. Department of the Treasury's Federal Advisory Committee on Insurance (FACI) will meet in the Cash Room at the U.S. Department of the Treasury, 1500 Pennsylvania Ave. NW, Washington, DC, and also via videoconference on Wednesday, March 20, 2024, from 1:30 p.m.-4:30 p.m. Eastern Time. The meeting will be open to the public. The FACI provides non-binding recommendations and advice to the Federal Insurance Office (FIO) in the U.S. Department of the Treasury.
