BREAKING: PepsiCo to Cut Some US Snack Prices After Backlash

Published: February 4, 2026 | Source: BBC

PepsiCo has announced it will reduce prices on some of its snack products in the US following consumer backlash over high costs.

Price Reductions

The food and beverage giant is responding to growing complaints from shoppers about the cost of its Frito-Lay snack products.

Consumer Pressure

The move comes as consumers push back against perceived "greedflation" practices by food companies that raised prices beyond inflation costs.

Industry Trend

PepsiCo joins other food companies reconsidering pricing strategies amid concerns about losing price-sensitive customers.
