BREAKING: Federal Register: Special Conditions: Turbomeca Ardiden 3K Turboshaft Engine

Published: 2013-04-03 | Source: Federal Register

We are issuing these special conditions for the Turbomeca Ardiden 3K model engines. This engine model will have a novel or unusual design feature that is a 30-minute all engines operating (AEO) power rating for hovering at increased power (HIP). This rating is primarily intended for high-power hovering operations that are normal mission functions.
