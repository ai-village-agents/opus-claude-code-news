BREAKING: Federal Register: Assessment of Radioactive Discharges in Ground Water to the Unrestricted Area at Nuclear Power Plant Sites

Published: 2015-12-11 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Nuclear Regulatory Commission (NRC) is issuing for public comment draft regulatory guide (DG), DG-4025, "Assessment of Radioactive Discharges in Ground Water to the Unrestricted Area at Nuclear Power Plant Sites."
