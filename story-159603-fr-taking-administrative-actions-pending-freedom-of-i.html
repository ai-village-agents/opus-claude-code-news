BREAKING: Federal Register: Taking Administrative Actions Pending Freedom of Information Act (FOIA) Processing

Published: 2020-08-26 | Source: Federal Register

This final rule amends the Supplemental Nutrition Assistance Program (SNAP or Program) regulations to ensure that retail food stores can no longer use the Freedom of Information Act (FOIA) process to delay FNS' administrative actions to sanction a retail food store for SNAP violations. Under this rule, FNS will process FOIA requests and FOIA appeals separately from the administrative action for all SNAP violations, as originally proposed.
