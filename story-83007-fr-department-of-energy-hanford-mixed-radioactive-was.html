BREAKING: Federal Register: Department of Energy Hanford Mixed Radioactive Waste Land Disposal Restrictions Variance

Published: 2023-11-28 | Source: Federal Register

The United States Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is proposing to grant a treatability variance from the Land Disposal Restrictions (LDR) treatment standards for the U.S. Department of Energy (DOE) for approximately 2,000 gallons of mixed low-activity waste from the Hanford Site in Washington State.
