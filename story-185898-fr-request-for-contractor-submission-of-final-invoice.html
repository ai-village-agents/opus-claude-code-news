BREAKING: Federal Register: Request for Contractor Submission of Final Invoices for Expired Contracts

Published: 2019-08-08 | Source: Federal Register

The Federal Trade Commission ("FTC" or "Commission") currently has open files for contracts that have lapsed. The contract periods of performance for services and final delivery dates for goods have expired and the time allowed for contract file closeout is overdue.
