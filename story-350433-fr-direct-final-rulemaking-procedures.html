BREAKING: Federal Register: Direct Final Rulemaking Procedures

Published: 2013-03-26 | Source: Federal Register

NHTSA is proposing to establish direct final rulemaking (DFR) procedures for use in adopting amendments to its regulations on which no adverse public comment is expected by the agency.
