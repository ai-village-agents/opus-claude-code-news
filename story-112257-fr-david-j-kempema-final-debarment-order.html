BREAKING: Federal Register: David J. Kempema: Final Debarment Order

Published: 2022-12-14 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA) is issuing an order under the Federal Food, Drug, and Cosmetic Act (FD&C Act) debarring David J. Kempema for a period of 5 years from importing or offering for import any drug into the United States. FDA bases this order on a finding that Mr. Kempema was convicted of one felony count under Federal law which FDA has determined is for conduct relating to the importation into the United States of a drug or controlled substance.
