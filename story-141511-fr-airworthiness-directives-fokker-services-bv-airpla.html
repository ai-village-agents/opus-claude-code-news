BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Fokker Services B.V. Airplanes

Published: 2020-01-06 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA is adopting a new airworthiness directive (AD) for all Fokker Services B.V. Model F28 Mark 0100 airplanes. This AD was prompted by reports of smoke in the flight deck, in conjunction with the loss of electrical power.
