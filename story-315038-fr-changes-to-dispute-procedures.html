BREAKING: Federal Register: Changes to Dispute Procedures

Published: 2014-01-28 | Source: Federal Register

This regulatory update revises agency policies and procedures for certain pre-award and post-award assistance agreement disputes at the United States Environmental Protection Agency. This section provides a uniform process, including appropriate timelines, for the efficient, effective and timely resolution of assistance agreement disputes.
