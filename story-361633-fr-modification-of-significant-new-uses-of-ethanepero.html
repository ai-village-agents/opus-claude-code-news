BREAKING: Federal Register: Modification of Significant New Uses of Ethaneperoxoic Acid, 1,1-Dimethylpropyl Ester

Published: 2013-07-31 | Source: Federal Register

Under the Toxic Substances Control Act (TSCA), EPA is finalizing an amendment to the significant new use rule (SNUR) for the chemical substance identified as ethaneperoxoic acid, 1,1- dimethylpropyl ester, which was the subject of premanufacture notice (PMN) P-85-680. This action requires persons who intend to manufacture or process the chemical substance for a use that is designated as a significant new use by this final rule to notify EPA at least 90 days before commencing that activity.
