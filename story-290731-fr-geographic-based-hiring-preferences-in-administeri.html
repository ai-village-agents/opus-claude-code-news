BREAKING: Federal Register: Geographic-Based Hiring Preferences in Administering Federal Awards

Published: 2015-04-08 | Source: Federal Register

This action extends the comment period for the Notice of Proposed Rulemaking (NPRM) proposing to amend the DOT's implementation of the Government-wide Uniform Administrative Requirements, Cost Principles, and Audit Requirements for Federal Awards to permit recipients and subrecipients to impose geographic-based hiring preferences whenever not otherwise prohibited by Federal statute.
