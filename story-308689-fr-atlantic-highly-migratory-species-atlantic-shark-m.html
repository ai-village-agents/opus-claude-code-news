BREAKING: Federal Register: Atlantic Highly Migratory Species; Atlantic Shark Management Measures; 2016 Research Fishery

Published: 2015-11-05 | Source: Federal Register

NMFS announces its request for applications for the 2016 shark research fishery from commercial shark fishermen with directed or incidental shark limited access permits. The shark research fishery allows for the collection of fishery-dependent and biological data for future stock assessments and to meet the research objectives of the Agency.
