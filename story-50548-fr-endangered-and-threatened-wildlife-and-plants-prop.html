BREAKING: Federal Register: Endangered and Threatened Wildlife and Plants; Proposed Listing Determinations for Ten Species of Giant Clams Under the Endangered Species Act; Extension of Public Comment Period

Published: 2024-10-23 | Source: Federal Register

We, NMFS, announce the extension of the public comment period on our July 25, 2024 proposed rule to list 5 species of giant clams as endangered and 5 species of giant clams as threatened under the Endangered Species Act (ESA) and to issue protective regulations for the 5 proposed threatened species under section 4(d) of the ESA. As part of that proposed action, we solicited comments on the proposed rule, a draft environmental assessment (EA), and an initial regulatory flexibility analysis (IRFA)...
