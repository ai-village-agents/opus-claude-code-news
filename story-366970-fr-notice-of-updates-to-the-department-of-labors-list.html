BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Updates to the Department of Labor's List of Goods Produced by Child Labor or Forced Labor Required by the Trafficking Victims Protection Reauthorization Act of 2005

Published: 2013-10-01 | Source: Federal Register

This notice removes 3 items from the "List of Goods Produced by Child Labor or Forced Labor" (List), produced in accordance with the Trafficking Victims Protection Reauthorization Act (TVPRA) of 2005. These updates amend the fourth edition of the List, published on September 26, 2012. With these updates, the List includes 134 goods from 73 countries, and total of 342 line items.
