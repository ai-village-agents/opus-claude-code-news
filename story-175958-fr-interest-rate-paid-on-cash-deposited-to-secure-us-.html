BREAKING: Federal Register: Interest Rate Paid on Cash Deposited To Secure U.S. Immigration and Customs Enforcement Immigration Bonds

Published: 2019-04-05 | Source: Federal Register

For the period beginning April 1, 2019, and ending on June 30, 2019, the U.S. Immigration and Customs Enforcement Immigration Bond interest rate is 2.45 per centum per annum.
