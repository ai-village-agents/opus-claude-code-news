BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone; Independence Day Fireworks, Kings Beach, CA

Published: 2014-06-05 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard will enforce the safety zone for the Independence Day Fireworks, Kings Beach, CA in the Captain of the Port, San Francisco area of responsibility on July 3, 2014. This action is necessary to protect life and property of the maritime public from the hazards associated with the fireworks display.
