BREAKING: Federal Register: Meetings: Homeland Security Information Network Advisory Committee (HSINAC)

Published: 2014-07-10 | Source: Federal Register

The Homeland Security Information Network Advisory Council (HSINAC) will meet August 6, 2014 from 1 p.m.-3 p.m. EDT virtually through HSIN Connect, an online web-conferencing tool, and via teleconference. The meeting will be open to the public.
