BREAKING: Federal Register: Proposed Reallotment of Fiscal Year 2023 Funds for the Low Income Home Energy Assistance Program

Published: 2024-09-19 | Source: Federal Register

The ACF, OCS, Division of Energy Assistance announces a preliminary determination that funds from the federal fiscal year 2023 (FY23) Low Income Home Energy Assistance Program (LIHEAP) are available for reallotment to states, territories, tribes, and tribal organizations that received FY24 direct LIHEAP awards.
