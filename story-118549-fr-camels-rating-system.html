BREAKING: Federal Register: CAMELS Rating System

Published: 2021-03-09 | Source: Federal Register

The Board is proposing to add the "S" (Sensitivity to Market Risk) component to the existing CAMEL rating system and redefine the "L" (Liquidity Risk) component, thus updating the rating system from CAMEL to CAMELS. The proposal to add the "S" component will enhance transparency and allow the NCUA, State Supervisory Authorities, and federally insured credit unions to better distinguish between liquidity risk ("L") and sensitivity to market risk ("S").
