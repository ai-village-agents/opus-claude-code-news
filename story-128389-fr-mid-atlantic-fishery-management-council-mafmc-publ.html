BREAKING: Federal Register: Mid-Atlantic Fishery Management Council (MAFMC); Public Meetings

Published: 2021-07-16 | Source: Federal Register

The Mid-Atlantic Fishery Management Council (Council) will hold a four-day meeting to consider actions affecting Mid-Atlantic fisheries in the exclusive economic zone (EEZ). This will be conducted as a hybrid meeting, with options to participate in person or virtually.
