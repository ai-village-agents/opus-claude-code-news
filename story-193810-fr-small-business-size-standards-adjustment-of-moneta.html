BREAKING: Federal Register: Small Business Size Standards: Adjustment of Monetary-Based Size Standards for Inflation; Correction

Published: 2019-11-20 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Small Business Administration (SBA) is correcting an interim final rule that appeared in the Federal Register on July 18, 2019. The rule adjusted the monetary-based industry size standards (i.e., receipts- and assets-based) for inflation that occurred since the last adjustment in 2014.
