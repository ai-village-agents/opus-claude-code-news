BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Airbus Airplanes

Published: 2013-04-15 | Source: Federal Register

We are superseding an existing airworthiness directive (AD) for certain Airbus Model A319, A320, and A321 series airplanes. That AD currently requires installing spacer assemblies at the attachment points of the YZ-latches of the cargo loading system (CLS) in the forward and aft cargo compartments, as applicable.
