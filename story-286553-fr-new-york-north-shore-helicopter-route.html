BREAKING: Federal Register: New York North Shore Helicopter Route

Published: 2015-02-17 | Source: Federal Register

On June 23, 2014, the FAA published a final rule to extend the requirement for an additional two years for pilots operating civil helicopters under Visual Flight Rules to use the New York North Shore Helicopter Route when operating along the north shore of Long Island, New York. The final rule extended the expiration date to August 6, 2016.
