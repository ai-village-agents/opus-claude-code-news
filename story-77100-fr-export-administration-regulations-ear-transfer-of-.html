BREAKING: Federal Register: Export Administration Regulations (EAR): Transfer of Access Information and Release of Software (Source Code and Object Code)

Published: 2023-09-18 | Source: Federal Register

In this final rule, the Bureau of Industry and Security (BIS) amends the Export Administration Regulations (EAR) to make a technical correction that also serves to clarify provisions of the EAR pertaining to the release of "software" as set out in the sections on release and transfer of access information.
