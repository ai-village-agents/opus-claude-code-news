BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Airbus SAS Airplanes

Published: 2020-09-17 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA proposes to adopt a new airworthiness directive (AD) for certain Airbus SAS Model A320-271N, A321-211 and A321-271N airplanes. This proposed AD was prompted by reports of missing overhead stowage compartment (OHSC) X-fixation brackets or brackets that were incorrectly installed during assembly.
