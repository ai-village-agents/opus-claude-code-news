BREAKING: Federal Register: Approval and Promulgation of Implementation Plans; Oregon: Permitting and General Rule Revisions

Published: 2017-03-22 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) proposes to approve, and incorporate by reference, specific changes to Oregon's State Implementation Plan (SIP) submitted on April 22, 2015. The changes relate to the criteria pollutants for which the EPA has established national ambient air quality standards--carbon monoxide, lead, nitrogen dioxide, ozone, particulate matter, and sulfur dioxide.
