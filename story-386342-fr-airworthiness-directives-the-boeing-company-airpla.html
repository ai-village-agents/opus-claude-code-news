BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; The Boeing Company Airplanes

Published: 2012-05-21 | Source: Federal Register

We are superseding an existing airworthiness directive (AD) for certain The Boeing Company Model 747 series airplanes. That AD currently requires repetitive inspections for cracks of the fuselage skin lap splice between body station (BS) 400 and BS 520 at stringers S-6L and S-6R, and repair if necessary.
