BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Pratt & Whitney Division (PW) Turbofan Engines

Published: 2018-11-19 | Source: Federal Register

We propose to adopt a new airworthiness directive (AD) for certain Pratt & Whitney Division (PW) PW4158 turbofan engines. This proposed AD was prompted by several reports of high cycle fatigue (HCF) cracks found in the fuel nozzle supply manifold. This proposed AD would require replacement of the affected fuel nozzles and fuel nozzle manifold supply assemblies with parts eligible for installation.
