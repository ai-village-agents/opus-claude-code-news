BREAKING: Federal Register: Public Land Order No. 7791; Extension of Public Land Order No. 6928; Wyoming

Published: 2012-06-05 | Source: Federal Register

This Order extends the withdrawal created by Public Land Order No. 6928 for an additional 20-year period. This extension is necessary to continue the protection of the significant capital investments and improvements made by the U.S. Forest Service associated with the Crandall Creek Administrative Site.
