BREAKING: Federal Register: Returning a Decommissioning Plant to Operating Status

Published: 2024-09-19 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Nuclear Regulatory Commission (NRC) has received a petition for rulemaking from Alan Blind and Roger Rapoport, dated July 1, 2024, requesting that the NRC revise its regulations to include a Commission-approved process for returning a decommissioning plant to operational status. The petition was docketed by the NRC on July 23, 2024, and has been assigned Docket No. PRM-50-125.
