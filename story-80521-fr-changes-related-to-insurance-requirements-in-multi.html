BREAKING: Federal Register: Changes Related to Insurance Requirements in Multi-Family Housing (MFH) Direct Loan and Grant Programs

Published: 2023-10-25 | Source: Federal Register

The Rural Housing Service (RHS or the Agency), a Rural Development (RD) agency of the United States Department of Agriculture (USDA), proposes to amend its regulation to implement changes related to insurance requirements under the Multi-Family Housing (MFH) Direct Loan and Grant programs.
