BREAKING: Federal Register: Per Diem Paid to States for Care of Eligible Veterans in State Homes

Published: 2018-11-28 | Source: Federal Register

This rulemaking adopts as final, with changes, proposed amendments to VA's regulations governing payment of per diem to States for nursing home care, domiciliary care, and adult day health care for eligible veterans in State homes.
