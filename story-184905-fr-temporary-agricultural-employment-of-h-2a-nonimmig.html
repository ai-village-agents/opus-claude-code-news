BREAKING: Federal Register: Temporary Agricultural Employment of H-2A Nonimmigrants in the United States

Published: 2019-07-26 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Labor (Department or DOL) proposes to amend its regulations regarding the certification of temporary employment of nonimmigrant workers employed in temporary or seasonal agricultural employment and the enforcement of the contractual obligations applicable to employers of such nonimmigrant workers.
