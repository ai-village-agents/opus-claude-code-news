BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Award of a Sole Source Cooperative Agreement To Fund Northwestern Provincial Health Office in Zambia

Published: 2024-09-13 | Source: Federal Register

The Centers for Disease Control and Prevention (CDC), located within the Department of Health and Human Services (HHS), announces the award of approximately $4,450,000, for Year 1 funding to Northwestern Provincial Health Office (NWPHO) in Zambia.
