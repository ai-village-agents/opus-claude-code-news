BREAKING: Federal Register: Proposed Establishment of the Los Olivos District Viticultural Area

Published: 2015-03-03 | Source: Federal Register

The Alcohol and Tobacco Tax and Trade Bureau (TTB) proposes to establish the approximately 22,820-acre "Los Olivos District" viticultural area in Santa Barbara County, California. The proposed viticultural area lies entirely within the Santa Ynez Valley viticultural area and the larger, multicounty Central Coast viticultural area.
