BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of HUD Non-Vacant Loan Sales (HNVLS 2026-1)

Published: 2026-01-21 | Source: Federal Register

This notice announces HUD's intention to offer approximately 2,500 home equity conversion mortgages (HECM, or reverse mortgage loans) secured by occupied properties with a loan balance of approximately $730 million. The sale will consist of due and payable Secretary-held reverse mortgage loans.
