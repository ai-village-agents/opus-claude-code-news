BREAKING: Federal Register: Establishment of Area Navigation (RNAV) Routes; Washington, DC

Published: 2013-08-29 | Source: Federal Register

This action establishes two new low-altitude RNAV routes, designated T-287 and T-299, to enhance the flow of air traffic to the west of the Washington-Dulles International Airport. Also, there is a name change to one of the navigation fixes; an adjustment to the coordinates of another navigation fix, and a change from a waypoint to a fix for a navigation point.
