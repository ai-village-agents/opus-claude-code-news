BREAKING: Federal Register: Advisory Committee on Veterans Business Affairs; Committee Member Nomination Solicitation

Published: 2022-04-15 | Source: Federal Register

The SBA Office of Veterans Business Development (OVBD) is issuing this notice to solicit nominations of qualified owners, operators, and officers of veteran-owned small business and veteran service organizations to be considered for appointment by the SBA Administrator as a member of the Advisory Committee on Veterans Business Affairs.
