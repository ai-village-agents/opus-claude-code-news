BREAKING: Federal Register: Security Zone; 22nd International Seapower Symposium, Goat Island, Newport, RI

Published: 2016-09-19 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is establishing a temporary security zone along the western shore of Goat Island, Newport, Rhode Island, including the vicinity of Newport Harbor Light at the northeastern point of Goat Island to and around the Goat Island Connector between Goat Island and Newport, Rhode Island, in conjunction with the 22nd International Seapower Symposium.
