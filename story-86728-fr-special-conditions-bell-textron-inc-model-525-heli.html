BREAKING: Federal Register: Special Conditions: Bell Textron Inc. Model 525 Helicopter; Fly-By-Wire Flight Control System

Published: 2022-01-19 | Source: Federal Register

These special conditions are issued for the Bell Textron Inc. (Bell) Model 525 helicopter. This helicopter will have a novel or unusual design feature associated with a fly-by-wire (FBW) flight control system (FCS). The applicable airworthiness regulations do not contain adequate or appropriate safety standards for this design feature.
