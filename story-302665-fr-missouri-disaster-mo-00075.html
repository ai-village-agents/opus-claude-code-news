BREAKING: Federal Register: Missouri Disaster #MO-00075

Published: 2015-08-26 | Source: Federal Register

This is a notice of an Administrative declaration of a disaster for the State of Missouri Dated 08/18/15. Incident: Severe storms, tornadoes, straight-line winds and flooding. Incident Period: 05/15/2015 through 07/27/2015. Effective Date: 08/18/2015. Physical Loan Application Deadline Date: 10/19/2015. Economic Injury (EIDL) Loan Application Deadline Date: 05/18/2016.
