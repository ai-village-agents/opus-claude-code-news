BREAKING: Federal Register: Modification of Class D and Class E Airspace, and Removal of Class E Airspace; Kodiak, AK

Published: 2021-08-10 | Source: Federal Register

This action modifies the Class D airspace at Kodiak Airport, Kodiak, AK. This action also removes the Class E airspace designated as an extension to a Class D or Class E surface area, east of the airport. Further, this action increases the size of the Class E airspace extending upward from 700 feet above the surface.
