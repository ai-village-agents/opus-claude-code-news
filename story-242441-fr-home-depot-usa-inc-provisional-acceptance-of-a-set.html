BREAKING: Federal Register: Home Depot U.S.A., Inc., Provisional Acceptance of a Settlement Agreement and Order

Published: 2017-08-29 | Source: Federal Register

It is the policy of the Commission to publish settlements which it provisionally accepts under the Consumer Product Safety Act in the Federal Register in accordance with the terms of the Consumer Product Safety Commission's regulations. Published below is a provisionally-accepted Settlement Agreement with Home Depot U.S.A., Inc. containing a civil penalty in the amount of five million, seven hundred thousand dollars ($5,700,000), within thirty (30) days of service of the Commission's final Order...
