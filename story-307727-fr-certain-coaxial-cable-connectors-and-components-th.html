BREAKING: Federal Register: Certain Coaxial Cable Connectors and Components Thereof and Products Containing Same Commission Determination Not To Review an Initial Determination Terminating the Investigation in Its Entirety Based Upon Withdrawal of the Complaint

Published: 2015-10-26 | Source: Federal Register

Notice is hereby given that the U.S. International Trade Commission has determined not to review an initial determination ("ID") (Order No. 10) of the presiding administrative law judge ("ALJ") granting complainant's motion to withdraw the complaint and terminate the investigation in its entirety.
