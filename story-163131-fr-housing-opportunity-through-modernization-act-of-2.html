BREAKING: Federal Register: Housing Opportunity Through Modernization Act of 2016-Housing Choice Voucher (HCV) and Project-Based Voucher Implementation; Additional Streamlining Changes

Published: 2020-10-08 | Source: Federal Register

HOTMA made numerous changes that affect either the Housing Choice Voucher (HCV) tenant- based program or the Project-Based Voucher (PBV) program, or both.
