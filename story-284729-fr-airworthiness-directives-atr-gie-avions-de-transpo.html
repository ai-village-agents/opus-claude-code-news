BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; ATR-GIE Avions de Transport Régional Airplanes

Published: 2015-01-26 | Source: Federal Register

We propose to adopt a new airworthiness directive (AD) for all ATR--GIE Avions de Transport R[eacute]gional Model ATR42 airplanes. This proposed AD was prompted by several reports of a cracked floor beam at frame (FR) 26, and of discrepancies in certain wing inspection tasks in maintenance documents that could lead to errors in scheduling inspection intervals of structurally significant items (SSIs).
