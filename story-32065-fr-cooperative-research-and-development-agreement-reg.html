BREAKING: Federal Register: Cooperative Research and Development Agreement: REGENT Craft, Inc.

Published: 2024-03-15 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is announcing its intent to enter into a Cooperative Research and Development Agreement (CRADA) with REGENT Craft, Inc. (REGENT) to evaluate advancements in Wing-In-Ground (WIG) maritime aircraft for applicability to USCG missions. REGENT's sea gliders are representative of that technology.
