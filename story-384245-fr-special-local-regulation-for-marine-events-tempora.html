BREAKING: Federal Register: Special Local Regulation for Marine Events; Temporary Change of Dates for Recurring Marine Events in the Fifth Coast Guard District, Ocean City Maryland Offshore Grand Prix, Ocean City, MD

Published: 2012-04-27 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is temporarily changing the enforcement period for a special local regulation for one recurring marine event in the Fifth Coast Guard District, specifically, the "Ocean City Maryland Offshore Grand Prix," hydroplane races on the North Atlantic Ocean near Ocean City, Maryland.
