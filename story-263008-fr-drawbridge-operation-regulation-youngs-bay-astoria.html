BREAKING: Federal Register: Drawbridge Operation Regulation; Youngs Bay, Astoria, OR

Published: 2016-05-09 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is temporarily changing the operating schedule that governs the Oregon State (Old Youngs Bay) highway bridge, mile 2.4, across Youngs Bay foot of Fifth Street at Astoria, OR. The Oregon Department of Transportation (ODOT) requested to change the operating schedule of the Old Youngs Bay Bridge for work on both bascule lifts.
