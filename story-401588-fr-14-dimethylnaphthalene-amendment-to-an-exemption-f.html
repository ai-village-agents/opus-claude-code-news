BREAKING: Federal Register: 1,4-Dimethylnaphthalene; Amendment to an Exemption From the Requirement of a Tolerance

Published: 2012-11-16 | Source: Federal Register

This regulation amends the existing exemption from the requirement of a tolerance for residues of the plant growth regulator, 1,4-dimethylnaphthalene (1,4-DMN) by expanding the current exemption to include all sprouting root and tuber vegetables (EPA Crop Group 01) and all bulb vegetables (EPA Crop Group 03).
