BREAKING: Federal Register: Gulf of Mexico Citizen Advisory Committee; Notice of Charter Renewal

Published: 2012-09-26 | Source: Federal Register

Notice is hereby given that the Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) has determined that in accordance with the provisions of the Federal Advisory Committee Act (FACA), 5 U.S.C. App.2, the Gulf of Mexico Citizen Advisory Committee (GMCAC) is a necessary committee which is in the public interest. Accordingly, the GMCAC is renewed for an additional two year period.
