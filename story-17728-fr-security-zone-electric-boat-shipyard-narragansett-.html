BREAKING: Federal Register: Security Zone; Electric Boat Shipyard, Narragansett Bay, Quonset Point, North Kingstown, RI

Published: 2025-08-08 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is establishing a security zone in the waters adjacent to the General Dynamics Electric Boat Corporation Quonset Point facility in Narragansett Bay, North Kingstown, RI. This is necessary to protect the facility, material, and adjacent areas from sabotage or other subversive acts, accidents or incidents of a similar nature.
