BREAKING: Federal Register: Social Security Number Card Applications

Published: 2015-08-10 | Source: Federal Register

This final rule adopts the notice of proposed rulemaking (NPRM) we published in the Federal Register on February 26, 2015. This rule revises our regulations to allow applicants for a Social Security number (SSN) card to apply by completing a prescribed application and submitting the required evidence.
