BREAKING: Federal Register: Market Dominant Price Adjustment

Published: 2016-12-13 | Source: Federal Register

The Commission is noticing a recently filed Postal Service notice revising one of its inflation-based rate adjustments affecting market dominant products. The adjustment and other changes are scheduled to take effect January 22, 2017. This notice informs the public of the filing, invites public comment, and takes other administrative steps.
