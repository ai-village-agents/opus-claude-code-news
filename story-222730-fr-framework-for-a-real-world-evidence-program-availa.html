BREAKING: Federal Register: Framework for a Real-World Evidence Program; Availability

Published: 2018-12-07 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA or Agency) is establishing a public docket to collect comments on a framework created by the Center for Drug Evaluation and Research and the Center for Biologic Evaluation and Research for implementing a program to evaluate the potential use of real-world evidence (RWE) in regulatory decision making.
