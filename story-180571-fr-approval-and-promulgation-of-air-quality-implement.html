BREAKING: Federal Register: Approval and Promulgation of Air Quality Implementation Plans; Delaware; Revision to the Regulatory Definition of a Volatile Organic Compound

Published: 2019-05-31 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is approving a state implementation plan (SIP) revision submitted by the State of Delaware. This revision pertains to amendments made to the definition of "volatile organic compound" (VOC) in the Delaware Administrative Code to conform with EPA's regulatory definition of VOC.
