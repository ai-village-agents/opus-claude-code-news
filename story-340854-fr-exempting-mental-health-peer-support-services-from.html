BREAKING: Federal Register: Exempting Mental Health Peer Support Services From Copayments

Published: 2014-11-28 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Veterans Affairs (VA) is proposing to amend its regulation that sets forth the VA services that are not subject to copayment requirements for inpatient hospital care or outpatient medical care. Specifically, the regulation would be amended to exempt mental health peer support services from having any required copayment.
