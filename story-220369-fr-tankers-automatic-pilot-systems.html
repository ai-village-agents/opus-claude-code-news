BREAKING: Federal Register: Tankers-Automatic Pilot Systems

Published: 2018-11-05 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard will permit tankers with automatic pilot systems that meet certain international standards to operate using those systems in shipping safety fairways and traffic separation schemes specified in 33 CFR parts 166 and 167, respectively.
