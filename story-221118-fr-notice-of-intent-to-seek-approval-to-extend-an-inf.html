BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Intent To Seek Approval To Extend an Information Collection

Published: 2018-11-15 | Source: Federal Register

The National Science Foundation (NSF) is announcing plans to request a new, one time data collection. The primary purpose of this data collection is to provide critical evidence for the Evaluation of the Centers for Chemical Innovation (CCI) Program. The National Science Foundation (NSF) has submitted this information collection requirement to OMB for review and clearance under the Paperwork Reduction Act of 1995.
