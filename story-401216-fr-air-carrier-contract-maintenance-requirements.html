BREAKING: Federal Register: Air Carrier Contract Maintenance Requirements

Published: 2012-11-13 | Source: Federal Register

The Federal Aviation Administration (FAA) proposes to amend the maintenance regulations for domestic, flag, and supplemental operations, and commuter and on-demand operations for aircraft type certificated with a passenger seating configuration of 10 seats or more (excluding any pilot seat).
