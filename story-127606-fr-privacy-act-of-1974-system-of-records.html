BREAKING: Federal Register: Privacy Act of 1974; System of Records

Published: 2021-07-06 | Source: Federal Register

In accordance with the Privacy Act of 1974, the U.S. Department of Agriculture (USDA or Department) proposes to modify and reissue the current system of records entitled "Department of Agriculture, Forest Service, Mineral Lessees and Permittees." This system allows the Forest Service Minerals and Geology program to support Forests and Grasslands to manage locatable minerals and mineral materials.
