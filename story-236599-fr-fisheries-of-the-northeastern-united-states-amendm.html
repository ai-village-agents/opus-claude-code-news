BREAKING: Federal Register: Fisheries of the Northeastern United States; Amendment 6 to the Tilefish Fishery Management Plan

Published: 2017-06-14 | Source: Federal Register

NMFS announces that the Mid-Atlantic Fishery Management Council has submitted Amendment 6 to the Tilefish Fishery Management Plan for review and approval by the Secretary of Commerce. We are requesting comments from the public on the amendment.
