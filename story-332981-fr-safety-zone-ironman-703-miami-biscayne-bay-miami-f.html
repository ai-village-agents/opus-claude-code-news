BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone, Ironman 70.3 Miami, Biscayne Bay; Miami, FL

Published: 2014-08-25 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard proposes to establish a temporary safety zone on the waters of Biscayne Bay, east of Bayfront Park, in Miami, Florida during the 2014 Ironman 70.3 Miami, a triathlon. The Ironman 70.3 Miami is scheduled to take place on October 26, 2014. Approximately 2,500 participants are anticipated to participate in the swim portion of the event.
