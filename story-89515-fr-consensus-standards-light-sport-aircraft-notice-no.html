BREAKING: Federal Register: Consensus Standards, Light-Sport Aircraft, Notice No. NOA-21-01

Published: 2022-02-23 | Source: Federal Register

This notice announces the availability of one new and two revised consensus standards relating to the provisions of the Certification of Aircraft and Airmen for the Operation of Light-Sport Aircraft rule. ASTM International (ASTM) Committee F37 on Light-Sport Aircraft developed the new and revised standards with FAA participation.
