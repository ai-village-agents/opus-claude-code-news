BREAKING: Federal Register: Appeal Proceedings Before the Commission

Published: 2012-09-25 | Source: Federal Register

The National Indian Gaming Commission (NIGC or Commission) promulgates this final rule to enhance and clarify appeal proceedings before the Commission. This final rule removes three parts concerning appeals, and adds a new subchapter concerning appeal proceedings before the Commission. This final rule has six parts.
