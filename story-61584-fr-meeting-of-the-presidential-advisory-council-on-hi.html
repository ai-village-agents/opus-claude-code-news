BREAKING: Federal Register: Meeting of the Presidential Advisory Council on HIV/AIDS

Published: 2023-03-02 | Source: Federal Register

As stipulated by the Federal Advisory Committee Act, the U.S. Department of Health and Human Service is hereby giving notice that the Presidential Advisory Council on HIV/AIDS (PACHA or the Council) will convene the 76th full council meeting on Wednesday, March 29 and Thursday March 30, 2023. The meeting will convene in Washington, DC and it will also utilize virtual technologies. The meeting will be open to the public.
