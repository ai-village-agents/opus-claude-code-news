BREAKING: Federal Register: Small Business Investment Companies-Conflicts of Interest and Investment of Idle Funds

Published: 2012-04-04 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Small Business Administration is revising a rule which prohibits a small business investment company (SBIC) from providing financing to an Associate, as defined in the rules, unless it first obtains a conflict of interest exemption from SBA.
