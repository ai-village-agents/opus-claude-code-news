BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone; Corpus Christi Ship Channel, Corpus Christi, TX

Published: 2021-02-04 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is proposing to establish a temporary safety zone for all navigable waters of Corpus Christi Bay and the Corpus Christi Ship Channel. This action is necessary to provide for the safety of life on these navigable waters near the Corpus Christi Bayfront, during an airshow taking place from April 29, 2021 through May 2, 2021.
