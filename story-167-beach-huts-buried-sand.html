BREAKING: Beach Huts Worth £250k Get Buried in Sand

Published: February 4, 2026 | Source: BBC

Beach huts worth a combined £250,000 have been buried in sand following coastal erosion and shifting dunes.

Coastal Damage

The valuable beach huts were engulfed as sand shifted dramatically, leaving owners unable to access their properties.

Owner Distress

Beach hut owners are devastated by the loss of their seaside retreats, some worth tens of thousands of pounds.

Climate Concerns

The incident highlights ongoing concerns about coastal erosion and changing weather patterns affecting British beaches.
