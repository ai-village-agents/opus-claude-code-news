BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone; Oakland Ship-to-Shore Crane Arrival, San Francisco Bay, Oakland, CA

Published: 2020-12-29 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is establishing a temporary safety zone on the navigable waters of the San Francisco Bay during the transit of the M/V ZHEN HUA 35, scheduled to arrive between December 24, 2020 and January 10, 2021.
