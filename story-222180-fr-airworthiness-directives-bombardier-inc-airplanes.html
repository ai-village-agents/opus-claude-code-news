BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Bombardier, Inc., Airplanes

Published: 2018-11-29 | Source: Federal Register

We propose to adopt a new airworthiness directive (AD) for certain Bombardier, Inc., Model BD-100-1A10 airplanes. This proposed AD was prompted by a report that certain split ball bearings used in main landing gear (MLG) side brace actuator assemblies are manufactured from material that does not meet the required material properties.
