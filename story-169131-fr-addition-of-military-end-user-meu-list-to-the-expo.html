BREAKING: Federal Register: Addition of `Military End User' (MEU) List to the Export Administration Regulations and Addition of Entities to the MEU List

Published: 2020-12-23 | Source: Federal Register

In this final rule, the Bureau of Industry and Security (BIS) amends the Export Administration Regulations (EAR) by adding a new `Military End User' (MEU) List that includes the first tranche of entities.
