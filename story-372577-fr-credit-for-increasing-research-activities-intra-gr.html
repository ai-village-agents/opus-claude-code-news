BREAKING: Federal Register: Credit for Increasing Research Activities: Intra-Group Gross Receipts

Published: 2013-12-13 | Source: Federal Register

This document contains proposed regulations under section 41 of the Internal Revenue Code (Code) relating to the treatment of qualified research expenditures (QREs) and gross receipts resulting from transactions between members of a controlled group of corporations or a group of trades or businesses under common control (intra-group transactions) for purposes of determining the credit under section 41 for increasing research activities (research credit).
