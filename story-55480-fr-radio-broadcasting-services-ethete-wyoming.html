BREAKING: Federal Register: Radio Broadcasting Services; Ethete, Wyoming

Published: 2024-12-16 | Source: Federal Register

This document requests comments on a petition for rule making filed by The Business Council of the Northern Arapaho, the non-gaming Tribal governmental agency of the Northern Arapaho Tribe, proposing to amend the FM Table of Allotments, by allotting Channel 260C0 at Ethete, Wyoming, as a Tribal allotment and the community's first local service.
