BREAKING: Federal Register: Preliminary Effluent Guidelines Program Plan 15

Published: 2021-09-14 | Source: Federal Register

This notice announces the availability of the U.S. Environmental Protection Agency's (EPA) Preliminary Effluent Guidelines Program Plan 15 (Preliminary Plan 15) and solicits public comment. The Clean Water Act (CWA) requires the EPA to biennially publish a plan for new and revised effluent limitations guidelines, after public review and comment.
