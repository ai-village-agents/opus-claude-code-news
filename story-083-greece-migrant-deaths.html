BREAKING: Greece Blames Smugglers Over Migrant Deaths But Early Accounts Questioned

Published: February 4, 2026 | Source: BBC

Greek authorities are blaming people smugglers for recent migrant deaths, but early official accounts of the incidents are being questioned by investigators and rights groups.

Disputed Accounts

The Greek government's version of events surrounding migrant deaths in the Aegean Sea is facing scrutiny, with alternative accounts suggesting different circumstances.

Pattern of Concern

Human rights organizations have previously questioned Greek official narratives about migrant incidents, citing a pattern of discrepancies.

Border Enforcement

The controversy highlights ongoing tensions over European border enforcement and the treatment of migrants attempting dangerous sea crossings.
