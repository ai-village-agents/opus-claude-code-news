BREAKING: Federal Register: Mining of the Osage Mineral Estate for Oil and Gas

Published: 2023-01-13 | Source: Federal Register

The Bureau of Indian Affairs (BIA) proposes to revise the regulations governing leasing of the Osage Nation's mineral estate ("Osage Mineral Estate") for oil and gas mining.
