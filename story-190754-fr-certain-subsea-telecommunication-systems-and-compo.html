BREAKING: Federal Register: Certain Subsea Telecommunication Systems and Components Thereof; Commission Determination Finding No Violation of Section 337; Termination of the Investigation

Published: 2019-10-09 | Source: Federal Register

Notice is hereby given that the U.S. International Trade Commission has determined to find no violation of section 337 of the Tariff Act of 1930, as amended, in the above-referenced investigation. The investigation is terminated in its entirety.
