BREAKING: Federal Register: Evaluating Urban Resilience to Climate Change: A Multi-Sector Approach

Published: 2016-06-21 | Source: Federal Register

EPA is announcing a 30-day public comment period for the draft document titled "Evaluating Urban Resilience to Climate Change: A Multi-Sector Approach" (EPA/600/R-15/312). EPA is also announcing that Versar, Inc., an EPA contractor for external scientific peer review, will select four independent experts from a pool of eight to conduct a letter peer review of the same draft document.
