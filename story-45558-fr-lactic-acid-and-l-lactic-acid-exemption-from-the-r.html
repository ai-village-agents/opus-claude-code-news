BREAKING: Federal Register: Lactic Acid and L-Lactic Acid; Exemption From the Requirement of a Tolerance

Published: 2024-08-29 | Source: Federal Register

This regulation removes the duplicative exemption from the requirement of a tolerance for residues of lactic acid and l-lactic acid, herein referred to as l-lactic acid, when applied/used in dairy processing equipment and food processing equipment and utensils. In addition, the Agency is amending an existing exemption from the requirement of a tolerance for lactic acid to also include l-lactic acid.
