BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Bell Helicopter Textron Canada Limited (Bell) Helicopters

Published: 2013-06-20 | Source: Federal Register

We propose to adopt a new airworthiness directive (AD) for Bell Model 206L, L-1, L-3, and L-4 helicopters. This proposed AD would require measuring each main rotor (M/R) blade spar space to determine whether it is oversized and reidentifying the blade and reducing the life limit of the blade if the spar spacer is oversized.
