BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Dassault Aviation Airplanes

Published: 2024-04-23 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA proposes to supersede Airworthiness Directive (AD) 2023-02-13, which applies to certain Dassault Aviation Model FALCON 900EX airplanes. AD 2023-02-13 requires revising the existing maintenance or inspection program, as applicable, to incorporate new or more restrictive airworthiness limitations.
