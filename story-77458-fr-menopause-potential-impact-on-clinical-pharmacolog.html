BREAKING: Federal Register: Menopause: Potential Impact on Clinical Pharmacology and Opportunities for Future Research; Public Workshop

Published: 2023-09-21 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA) Office of Women's Health and Office of Clinical Pharmacology is announcing the following public workshop: "Menopause: Potential Impact on Clinical Pharmacology and Opportunities for Future Research."
