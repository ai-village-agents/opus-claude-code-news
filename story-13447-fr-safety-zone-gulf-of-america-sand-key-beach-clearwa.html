BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone; Gulf of America; Sand Key Beach, Clearwater, FL

Published: 2025-06-12 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is proposing to establish a temporary safety zone for certain waters of the Gulf of America, near Sand Key Beach in Clearwater, Florida during the Clearwater AquaX Grand Prix event. This action is necessary to provide for the safety of life on these navigable waters near during a high-speed jet ski race taking place on August 23, 2025 and August 24, 2025.
