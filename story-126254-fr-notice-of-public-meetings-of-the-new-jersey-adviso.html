BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Public Meetings of the New Jersey Advisory Committee; (1) Cancellation of Meeting Date and (2) Change of Meeting Date

Published: 2021-06-17 | Source: Federal Register

The Commission on Civil Rights published a notice in the Federal Register concerning meetings of the New Jersey Advisory Committee. The meeting scheduled for Friday, June 18, 2021 at 1:00 p.m. (ET) is cancelled. The meeting scheduled for Wednesday, September 1 at 1:00 p.m. (ET) is changed to Thursday, September 2 at 1:00 p.m. (ET).
