BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone; Annual Swim for Alligator Reef Lighthouse, Islamorada, FL

Published: 2018-08-31 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard will enforce the temporary safety zone for the 6th Annual Swim for Alligator Reef Lighthouse, Islamorada, Florida from 6:30 a.m. until 4:30 p.m. on September 15, 2018. Our regulation for Recurring Safety Zones in Captain of the Port Key West Zone identifies the regulated area for this event.
