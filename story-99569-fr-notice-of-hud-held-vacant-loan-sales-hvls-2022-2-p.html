BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of HUD-Held Vacant Loan Sales (HVLS 2022-2, Parts 1 and 2)

Published: 2022-07-01 | Source: Federal Register

This notice reports HUD's recent June 8th competitive sale offering of 1,406 reverse mortgage loans secured by vacant or abandoned properties with a loan balance of approximately $336 million (June sale or HVLS 2022-2, Part 1). The June sale consisted of one national pool and one single-asset pool secured by a vacant lot in Omaha, Nebraska (Single asset pool or Land only asset).
