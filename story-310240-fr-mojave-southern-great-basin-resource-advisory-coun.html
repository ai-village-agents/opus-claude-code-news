BREAKING: Federal Register: Mojave-Southern Great Basin Resource Advisory Council Call for Nominees To Complete Elected Official Term

Published: 2015-11-25 | Source: Federal Register

The purpose of this notice is to open the request for public nominations for the Elected Official representative on the Mojave- Southern Great Basin Resource Advisory Council (RAC). The selected individual would complete the remaining two years of the current three- year term that concludes on May 6, 2017.
