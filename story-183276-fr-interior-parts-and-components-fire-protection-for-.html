BREAKING: Federal Register: Interior Parts and Components Fire Protection for Transport Category Airplanes

Published: 2019-07-03 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA is proposing to amend certain airworthiness regulations for fire protection of interior compartments on transport category airplanes. This proposal would convert those flammability regulations from detailed, prescriptive requirements into simpler, performance-based standards.
