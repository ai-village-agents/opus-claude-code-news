BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Bombardier, Inc. Airplanes

Published: 2012-07-25 | Source: Federal Register

We propose to supersede an existing airworthiness directive (AD) that applies to certain Bombardier, Inc. Model DHC-8-400 series airplanes. The existing AD currently requires a modification to trim the edge of the bumper plate, including performing an inspection for damage or cracks of the bumper plate and base fitting, and replacing any damaged or cracked part.
