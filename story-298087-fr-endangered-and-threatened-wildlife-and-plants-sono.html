BREAKING: Federal Register: Endangered and Threatened Wildlife and Plants; Sonoran Pronghorn Draft Recovery Plan

Published: 2015-07-02 | Source: Federal Register

We, the Fish and Wildlife Service (Service), announce the availability of our draft recovery plan, second revision, for the Sonoran pronghorn, which is listed as endangered under the Endangered Species Act of 1973, as amended (Act). This pronghorn is currently found in southwestern Arizona and northwestern Sonora, Mexico.
