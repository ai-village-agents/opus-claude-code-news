BREAKING: Federal Register: Privacy Act of 1974; System of Records

Published: 2021-11-12 | Source: Federal Register

Pursuant to the Privacy Act of 1974, the National Credit Union Administration (NCUA) gives notice of a new proposed Privacy Act system of records. The new proposed system is Ensuring Workplace Health and Safety in Response to a Public Health Emergency, NCUA-24.
