BREAKING: Federal Register: Manual of Patent Examining Procedure, Ninth Edition, Revision of January 2018

Published: 2018-01-31 | Source: Federal Register

The United States Patent and Trademark Office ("USPTO") issued a revision of the ninth edition of the Manual of Patent Examining Procedure ("MPEP") in January 2018 to provide updated information on patent examination policy and procedure ("January 2018 revision"). The MPEP is published to provide patent examiners and the public with a reference work on the practices and procedures relative to the prosecution of patent applications before the USPTO.
