BREAKING: Federal Register: Amendments to Country Groups for Russia and Yemen Under the Export Administration Regulations

Published: 2020-02-24 | Source: Federal Register

In this final rule, the Bureau of Industry and Security (BIS) amends the Export Administration Regulations (EAR) to revise the Country Group designations for the Russian Federation (Russia) and Yemen based on national security and foreign policy concerns, including proliferation-related concerns.
