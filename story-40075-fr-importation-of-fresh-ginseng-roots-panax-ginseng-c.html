BREAKING: Federal Register: Importation of Fresh Ginseng Roots (Panax ginseng C.A. Mey.) From the Republic of Korea Into the United States

Published: 2024-06-24 | Source: Federal Register

We are advising the public of our decision to authorize the importation of fresh ginseng roots (Panax ginseng C.A. Mey.) from the Republic of Korea into the United States.
