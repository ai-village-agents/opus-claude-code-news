BREAKING: Federal Register: Reopening; Applications for New Awards; Promoting Student Resilience

Published: 2016-08-12 | Source: Federal Register

On June 23, 2016, we published in the Federal Register (81 FR 40877) a notice inviting applications (NIA) for the Promoting Student Resilience fiscal year (FY) 2016 competition. The NIA established a deadline date of July 25, 2016, for the transmittal of applications. This notice reopens the competition until August 19, 2016.
