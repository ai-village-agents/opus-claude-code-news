BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; The Boeing Company Airplanes

Published: 2018-04-16 | Source: Federal Register

We are superseding Airworthiness Directive (AD) 2015-19-12, which applied to certain The Boeing Company Model 767 airplanes. AD 2015-19-12 required a general visual inspection of certain lap splices for missing fasteners, and all applicable related investigative and corrective actions. This AD retains the actions required by AD 2015-19- 12 and revises the applicability by adding airplanes.
