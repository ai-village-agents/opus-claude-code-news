BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Intent To Prepare an Environmental Impact Statement for a Proposed Highway Project in Arkansas

Published: 2021-07-01 | Source: Federal Register

FHWA, in coordination with the Arkansas Department of Transportation (ARDOT), is issuing this Notice of Intent (NOI) to solicit comments and advise the public, agencies, and stakeholders of an Environmental Impact Statement (EIS) that will be prepared to study the effects of a highway project under consideration for the Highway 67 corridor in Clay, Greene, Lawrence, and Randolph counties, Arkansas.
