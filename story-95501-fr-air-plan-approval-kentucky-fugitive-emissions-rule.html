BREAKING: Federal Register: Air Plan Approval; Kentucky; Fugitive Emissions Rule

Published: 2022-05-09 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is finalizing approval of a State Implementation Plan (SIP) revision submitted by the Commonwealth of Kentucky (Commonwealth), through the Energy and Environmental Cabinet (Cabinet) on October 15, 2020. The SIP revision updates the Commonwealth's regulation for the control of fugitive emissions.
