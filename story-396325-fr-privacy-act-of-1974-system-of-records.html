BREAKING: Federal Register: Privacy Act of 1974; System of Records

Published: 2012-09-13 | Source: Federal Register

The United States Postal Service[supreg] is proposing to modify seven General and Customer Privacy Act Systems of Records. These modifications are being made to account for updates to the system location, system manager(s) and address, and notification procedures due to an organizational re-design.
