BREAKING: Federal Register: Payette National Forest, Idaho; Huckleberry Landscape Restoration Project

Published: 2016-09-30 | Source: Federal Register

The Payette National Forest will prepare an Environmental Impact Statement (EIS) for the Huckleberry Landscape Restoration Project. The Huckleberry Landscape Restoration Project is located approximately 15 miles west of New Meadows, Idaho. Proposed treatments include timber harvest, thinning, prescribed fire, road treatments and road decommissioning, and recreation improvements.
