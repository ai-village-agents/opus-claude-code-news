BREAKING: Federal Register: Public Availability of Agency Records and Informational Materials

Published: 2018-06-20 | Source: Federal Register

The General Services Administration (GSA) is issuing a proposed rule to amend its regulations implementing the Freedom of Information Act (FOIA). The regulations are being revised to update and streamline the language of several procedural provisions and to incorporate certain changes brought about by the amendments to the FOIA under both statutory and nonstatutory authorities.
