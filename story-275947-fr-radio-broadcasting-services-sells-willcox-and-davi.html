BREAKING: Federal Register: Radio Broadcasting Services; Sells, Willcox, and Davis-Monthan Air Force Base, Arizona

While the AFR was pending, KZLZ filed a minor modification application to change the community of license of Station KWCX-FM from Willcox to Tanque Verde, Arizona.
