BREAKING: Federal Register: Silverado Stages, Inc.-Acquisition of Control-Michelangelo Leasing, Inc. and Ryan's Express Transportation Services, Inc.

Published: 2016-07-21 | Source: Federal Register

(Michelangelo) and Ryan's Express Transportation Services, Inc. (Ryan), a corporation wholly owned and controlled by Michelangelo.
