BREAKING: Federal Register: National Advisory Committee on Microbiological Criteria for Foods; Reestablishment

Published: 2013-01-23 | Source: Federal Register

In accordance with the Federal Advisory Committee Act, this notice is announcing the reestablishment of the National Advisory Committee on Microbiological Criteria for Foods (NACMCF). The Committee is being reestablished in cooperation with the Department of Health and Human Services (DHHS).
