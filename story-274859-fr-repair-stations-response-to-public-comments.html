BREAKING: Federal Register: Repair Stations; Response to Public Comments

Published: 2016-09-26 | Source: Federal Register

This action confirms the effective date and adopts as final the interim final rule published on July 27, 2016, and responds to the comments received on that interim final rule. The rule removed the requirement that a repair station with an airframe rating provide suitable permanent housing to enclose the largest type and model aircraft listed on its operations specifications.
