BREAKING: Federal Register: Gallatin County Resource Advisory Committee

Published: 2013-12-31 | Source: Federal Register

The Gallatin County Resource Advisory Committee (RAC) will meet in Bozeman, Montana. The RAC is authorized under the Secure Rural Schools and Community Self-Determination Act (the Act) (Pub. L. 110- 343) and operates in compliance with the Federal Advisory Committee Act (FACA) (5 U.S.C. App. II). Additional information concerning the RAC can be found by visiting the Act's Web site at www.fs.usda.gov/gallatin and clicking on the right-hand highlight that denotes Gallatin County RAC.
