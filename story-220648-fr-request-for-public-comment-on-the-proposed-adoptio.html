BREAKING: Federal Register: Request for Public Comment on the Proposed Adoption of Administration for Native Americans Program Policies and Procedures

Published: 2018-11-08 | Source: Federal Register

Pursuant to the Native American Programs Act of 1974 (NAPA), as amended, the Administration for Native Americans (ANA) is required to provide members of the public an opportunity to comment on proposed changes in interpretive rules and general statements of policy and to give notice of the final adoption of such changes no less than 30 days before such changes become effective.
