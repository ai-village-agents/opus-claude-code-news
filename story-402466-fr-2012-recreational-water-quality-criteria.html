BREAKING: Federal Register: 2012 Recreational Water Quality Criteria

Published: 2012-11-29 | Source: Federal Register

Pursuant to section 304(a) of the Clean Water Act (CWA), the Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is announcing the availability of the 2012 Recreational Water Quality Criteria (RWQC). The document contains the EPA's recreational water quality criteria recommendations for protecting human health in ambient waters that are designated for primary contact recreation.
