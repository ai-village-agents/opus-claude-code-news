BREAKING: Federal Register: Commercial Driver's License Standards: Application for Exemption; Daimler Trucks North America

Published: 2022-04-27 | Source: Federal Register

FMCSA announces its decision to grant an exemption to Daimler Trucks North America (Daimler) for nine of its commercial motor vehicle (CMV) drivers, identified below. Under this exemption, the nine drivers are not subject to the commercial driver's license (CDL) requirements and therefore are exempt from the requirements of the Agency's drug and alcohol regulations.
