BREAKING: Federal Register: Privacy Act of 1974: Systems of Records

Published: 2019-03-26 | Source: Federal Register

Pursuant to the Privacy Act of 1974, the National Credit Union Administration (NCUA) gives notice of a new proposed Privacy Act system of records. The new proposed system is the Examination and Supervision System (ESS), NCUA-22. The ESS will be used for NCUA's statutorily mandated examination and supervision activities, including the coordination and conduct of examinations of credit unions, supervisory evaluations and analyses, enforcement actions and Federal court actions.
