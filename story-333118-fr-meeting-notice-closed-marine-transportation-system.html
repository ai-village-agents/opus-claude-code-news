BREAKING: Federal Register: Meeting Notice (Closed)-Marine Transportation System National Advisory Council

Published: 2014-08-26 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Transportation and the Maritime Administration (MARAD) announces that the Marine Transportation System National Advisory Council (MTSNAC) will meet on September 11, 2014. In January 2014, MARAD held a three-day National Maritime Strategy Symposium to discuss issues facing U.S.-flag vessels.
