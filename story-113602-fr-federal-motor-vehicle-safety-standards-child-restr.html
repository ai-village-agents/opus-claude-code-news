BREAKING: Federal Register: Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standards; Child Restraint Systems, Incorporation by Reference

Published: 2021-01-04 | Source: Federal Register

NHTSA has received petitions asking the Agency to extend the comment period for a notice of proposed rulemaking (NPRM) to update Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard (FMVSS) No. 213, "Child restraint systems." The NPRM proposed to incorporate a new test sled assembly to conduct compliance tests of child restraints and update a number of the test procedures of the standard.
