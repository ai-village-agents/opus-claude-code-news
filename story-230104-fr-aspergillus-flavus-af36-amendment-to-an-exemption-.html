BREAKING: Federal Register: Aspergillus flavus AF36; Amendment to an Exemption From the Requirement of a Tolerance

Published: 2017-03-22 | Source: Federal Register

This regulation amends the existing tolerance exemption for Aspergillus flavus AF36 by establishing an exemption from the requirement of a tolerance for residues of Aspergillus flavus AF36 in or on almond and fig when used in accordance with label directions and good agricultural practices.
