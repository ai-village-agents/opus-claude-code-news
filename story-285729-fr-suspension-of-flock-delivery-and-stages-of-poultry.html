BREAKING: Federal Register: Suspension of Flock Delivery and Stages of Poultry Production

Published: 2015-02-05 | Source: Federal Register

This final rule removes certain regulations promulgated under the Packers and Stockyards Act, 1921 (P&S Act). Under the authority granted to the Secretary of Agriculture (Secretary) and delegated to the Grain Inspection, Packers and Stockyards Administration (GIPSA), GIPSA is authorized to issue regulations necessary to carry out the provisions of the P&S Act.
