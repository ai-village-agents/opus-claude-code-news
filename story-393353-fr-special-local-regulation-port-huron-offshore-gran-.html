BREAKING: Federal Register: Special Local Regulation; Port Huron Offshore Gran Prix, St. Clair River; Port Huron, MI

Published: 2012-08-09 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is establishing a special local regulation on the St. Clair River, Port Huron, Michigan. This action is necessary and intended to ensure safety of life on the navigable waters immediately prior to, during, and immediately after the Port Huron Offshore Gran Prix boat race. This special local regulation will establish restrictions upon, and control movement of, vessels in a portion of the St. Clair River.
