BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone; Chesapeake Bay, Approaches to Baltimore Harbor, MD

Published: 2024-04-08 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is establishing a temporary safety zone for navigable waters within a 2000-yard radius of the center span of the Francis Scott Key Bridge, in Baltimore, MD. The safety zone is needed to protect personnel, vessels, and the marine environment from potential hazards associated with salvage work on the bridge, which partially collapsed when it was hit by the M/V DALI, and on the M/V DALI itself.
