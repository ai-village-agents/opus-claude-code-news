BREAKING: Federal Register: Approval and Promulgation of Air Quality Implementation Plans; State of Florida; Regional Haze State Implementation Plan

Published: 2012-11-29 | Source: Federal Register

The EPA is finalizing a full approval of the Best Available Retrofit Technology (BART) determinations addressed in the Agency's May 25, 2012, proposed rulemaking action on a regional haze state implementation plan (SIP) submitted by the State of Florida, through the Florida Department of Environmental Protection (FDEP). These BART determinations were submitted to the EPA in a draft regional haze SIP on April 13, 2012, for parallel processing, and re-submitted in final form on September 17, 2012....
