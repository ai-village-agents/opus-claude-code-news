BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice to LSC Grantees of Application Process For Subgranting Special Grant Funds

Published: 2019-08-15 | Source: Federal Register

The Legal Services Corporation (LSC) is the national organization charged with administering Federal funds provided for civil legal services to low-income people. LSC is announcing the submission dates for applications to make subgrants of its Special Grant funds. LSC is also providing information about where applicants may locate subgrant application forms and directions for providing the information required in the application.
