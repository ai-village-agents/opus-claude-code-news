BREAKING: Federal Register: Federal Management Regulation (FMR); Mail Management; Financial Requirements for All Agencies

Published: 2013-05-13 | Source: Federal Register

The General Services Administration (GSA) is proposing to amend the Federal Management Regulation (FMR) by revising its mail management policy. A major part of the proposed revision involves the removal of the agency requirement to pay the United States Postal Service (USPS) using commercial payment processes.
