BREAKING: Federal Register: Common Instructions for Applicants to Department of Education Discretionary Grant Programs

Published: 2018-02-12 | Source: Federal Register

As part of a broader effort to reduce barriers for applicants seeking funds under a Department of Education (Department) discretionary grant competition, the Department is issuing a common set of instructions for applicants. It will be referenced in individual notices inviting applications (NIAs).
