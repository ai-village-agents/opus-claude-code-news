BREAKING: Federal Register: Amended Trade Mission Application Deadline and Updated Schedule to the Executive-Led Cybersecurity Business Development Mission to Taiwan, South Korea, and Japan

Published: 2023-07-18 | Source: Federal Register

The United States Department of Commerce, International Trade Administration (ITA), is organizing an Executive-Led Cybersecurity Business Development Mission to Taiwan, South Korea, and Japan on September 18-26, 2023. This notice is to update the prior Federal Register notice to reflect that the application deadline is now extended to July 21, 2023 and the proposed timetable has been updated to include additional events.
