BREAKING: Federal Register: Federal Housing Administration (FHA): Homeowners Armed With Knowledge (HAWK) for New Homebuyers

Published: 2014-05-15 | Source: Federal Register

This notice solicits public comment on a proposed four-year, two-phase, housing counseling pilot, "HAWK for New Homebuyers." HAWK stands for Homeowners Armed With Knowledge, and serves as an umbrella term for several initiatives to link HUD's Housing Counseling program with FHA-insured mortgage origination and servicing.
