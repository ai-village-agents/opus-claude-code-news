BREAKING: Federal Register: Carbon Tetrachloride (CTC); Regulation Under the Toxic Substances Control Act (TSCA)

Published: 2023-07-28 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is proposing to address the unreasonable risk of injury to human health presented by carbon tetrachloride (CTC) under its conditions of use as documented in EPA's 2020 Risk Evaluation for Carbon Tetrachloride and 2022 Revised Unreasonable Risk Determination for Carbon Tetrachloride pursuant to the Toxic Substances Control Act (TSCA).
