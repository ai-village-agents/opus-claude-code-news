BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of HUD Vacant Loan Sales (HVLS 2024-2)

Published: 2024-04-11 | Source: Federal Register

This notice announces HUD's intention to competitively offer approximately 1,265 home equity conversion mortgages (HECM, or reverse mortgage loans) secured by vacant properties with an updated loan balance of approximately $346 million. The sale will consist of due and payable Secretary-held reverse mortgage loans.
