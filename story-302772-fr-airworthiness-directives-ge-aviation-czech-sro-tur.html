BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; GE Aviation Czech s.r.o. Turboprop Engines

Published: 2015-08-27 | Source: Federal Register

We are adopting a new airworthiness directive (AD) for certain serial number (S/N) GE Aviation Czech s.r.o. M601E-11, M601E-11A, and M601F turboprop engines with certain part number (P/N) gas generator turbine (GGT) blades, installed. This AD requires removing from service any affected engine with certain GGT blades installed. This AD was prompted by the determination that certain GGT blades are susceptible to blade failure.
