BREAKING: Federal Register: Terrorism Risk Insurance Program

Published: 2016-04-01 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of the Treasury (Treasury) is issuing these proposed rules to implement changes to the Terrorism Risk Insurance Program (TRIP or Program) required by the Terrorism Risk Insurance Program Reauthorization Act of 2015 (2015 Reauthorization Act). In addition, Treasury proposes for the first time a Civil Penalties rule under TRIP, pursuant to authority granted by Congress in the Terrorism Risk Insurance Act of 2002 (TRIA).
