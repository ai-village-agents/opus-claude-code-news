BREAKING: Federal Register: Location-Based Routing for Wireless 911 Calls

Published: 2024-09-26 | Source: Federal Register

In this document, the Federal Communications Commission (Commission) announces that the Office of Management and Budget (OMB) has approved information collections associated with certain rules adopted in the Location-Based Routing for Wireless 911 Calls Report and Order. The Commission also announces that compliance with the rules is now required.
