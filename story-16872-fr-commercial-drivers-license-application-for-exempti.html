BREAKING: Federal Register: Commercial Driver's License: Application for Exemption; Bianco Trucking Services, LLC d.b.a. CDL and Operator Training

Published: 2025-07-28 | Source: Federal Register

FMCSA announces its decision to deny the application from Bianco Trucking Services, LLC d.b.a. CDL and Operator Training ("Bianco") for an exemption to allow students between the ages of 18 and 20 holding a "K" restricted commercial learners permit (CLP) issued by another State to attend its driver training facility in Michigan.
