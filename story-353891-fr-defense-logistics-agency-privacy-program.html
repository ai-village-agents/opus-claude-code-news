BREAKING: Federal Register: Defense Logistics Agency Privacy Program

Published: 2013-05-03 | Source: Federal Register

The Defense Logistics Agency (DLA) is revising its Privacy Act procedural and exemption rules. The DLA Privacy Offices have been repositioned under the DLA General Counsel; therefore, responsibilities have been updated to reflect the repositioning. In addition, DLA has adopted revisions to the DoD Privacy Program.
