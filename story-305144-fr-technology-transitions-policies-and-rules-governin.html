BREAKING: Federal Register: Technology Transitions, Policies and Rules Governing Retirement of Copper Loops by Incumbent Local Exchange Carriers and Special Access for Price Cap Local Exchange Carriers

Published: 2015-09-25 | Source: Federal Register
