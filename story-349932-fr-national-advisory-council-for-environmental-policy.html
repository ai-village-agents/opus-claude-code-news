BREAKING: Federal Register: National Advisory Council for Environmental Policy and Technology

Published: 2013-03-21 | Source: Federal Register

Under the Federal Advisory Committee Act, Pub. L. 92463, EPA gives notice that the public meeting for the National Advisory Council for Environmental Policy and Technology (NACEPT) initially scheduled for March 7-8, 2013, was postponed due to the predicted inclement weather that closed the Federal Government.
