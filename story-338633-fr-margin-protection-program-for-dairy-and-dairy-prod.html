BREAKING: Federal Register: Margin Protection Program for Dairy and Dairy Product Donation Program

Published: 2014-10-30 | Source: Federal Register

The Commodity Credit Corporation (CCC) and the Farm Service Agency (FSA) published a final rule on August 29, 2014, implementing regulations for the Margin Protection Program for Dairy (MPP-Dairy) and the Dairy Product Donation Program (DPDP) as authorized in subtitle D of the Agricultural Act of 2014 (the 2014 Farm Bill).
