BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of an Open Meeting of the Advisory Committee on Water Information

Published: 2017-02-09 | Source: Federal Register

Notice is hereby given of a meeting of the ACWI. This meeting is to discuss broad policy-related topics relating to national water initiatives, and the development and dissemination of water information, through reports from ACWI subgroups.
