BREAKING: Federal Register: Special Local Regulation: Seminole Hard Rock Winterfest Holiday Boat Parade

Published: 2019-11-05 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard will enforce a special local regulation on December 14, 2019 to provide for the safety and security of navigable waterways during the Seminole Hard Rock Winterfest Holiday Boat Parade. During the enforcement period, all non-participant persons and vessels will be prohibited from entering, transiting, anchoring in, or remaining within the regulated area unless authorized by the Captain of the Port Miami or a designated representative.
