BREAKING: Federal Register: Amendments to the Marine Radar Observer Refresher Training Regulations

Published: 2019-06-07 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is revising its merchant mariner credentialing regulations to remove obsolete portions of the radar observer requirements and harmonize the radar observer endorsement with the merchant mariner credential. These revisions will reduce an unnecessary financial burden on mariners required to hold a radar observer endorsement.
