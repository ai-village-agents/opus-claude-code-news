BREAKING: Federal Register: Chemical Safety Advisory Committee; Establishment of a Federal Advisory Committee; Request for Nominations

Published: 2015-06-12 | Source: Federal Register

As required by section 9(a)(2) of the Federal Advisory Committee Act (FACA), we are giving notice that EPA recently established the Chemical Safety Advisory Committee (CSAC). The purpose of the CSAC is to provide expert scientific advice, information, and recommendations to the Office of Pollution Prevention and Toxics (OPPT). The major objective is to provide advice and recommendations on: The scientific basis for risk assessments, methodologies, and pollution prevention measures or approaches....
