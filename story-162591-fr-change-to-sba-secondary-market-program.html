BREAKING: Federal Register: Change to SBA Secondary Market Program

Published: 2020-10-02 | Source: Federal Register

The purpose of this Notice is to inform the public that the Small Business Administration (SBA) is making a change to its Secondary Market Loan Pooling Program. SBA is decreasing the minimum maturity ratio for both SBA Standard Pools and Weighted-Average Coupon (WAC) Pools by 500 basis points, to 89.0%.
