BREAKING: Federal Register: Privacy Act of 1974; Systems of Records; Amendment to General Routine Uses

Published: 2021-11-10 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Environmental Protection Agency's (EPA) Office of Mission Support is giving notice that it proposes to amend its current list of general routine uses for EPA systems of records in accordance with the provisions of the Privacy Act of 1974, as amended.
