BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone; Hemingway Sunset Run & Paddleboard Race, Key West, FL

Published: 2019-05-08 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard will enforce the temporary safety zone for the Hemingway Sunset Run & Paddleboard Race, Key West, Florida on July 20, 2019. Our regulation for Recurring Safety Zones in Captain of the Port Key West Zone identifies the regulated area for this event. This action is necessary to ensure the safety of event participants and spectators.
