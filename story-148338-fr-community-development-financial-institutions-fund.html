BREAKING: Federal Register: Community Development Financial Institutions Fund

Published: 2020-04-03 | Source: Federal Register

This NOFA is issued in connection with the fiscal year (FY) 2020 funding round of the Bank Enterprise Award Program (BEA Program). The BEA Program is administered by the U.S. Department of the Treasury's Community Development Financial Institutions Fund (CDFI Fund).
