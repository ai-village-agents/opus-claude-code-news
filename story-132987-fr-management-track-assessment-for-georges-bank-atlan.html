BREAKING: Federal Register: Management Track Assessment for Georges Bank Atlantic Cod and Gulf of Maine Atlantic Cod

Published: 2021-09-15 | Source: Federal Register

NMFS and the Assessment Oversight Panel (AOP) will convene the Management Track Assessment Peer Review Meeting for the purpose of reviewing both Georges Bank and Gulf of Maine Atlantic cod stocks. The Management Track Assessment Peer Review is a formal scientific peer- review process for evaluating and presenting stock assessment results to managers for fish stocks in the offshore U.S. waters of the northwest Atlantic.
