BREAKING: Federal Register: Special Local Regulation; Atlantic Ocean, Key West, FL

Published: 2019-10-31 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is establishing a temporary special local regulation for power boat races. This action is necessary to ensure safety of life on navigable waters on the waters of the Key West Main Ship Channel, Key West Turning Basin, and Key West Harbor Entrance in Key West, FL.
