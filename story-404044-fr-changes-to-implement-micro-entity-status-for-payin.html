BREAKING: Federal Register: Changes To Implement Micro Entity Status for Paying Patent Fees

Published: 2012-12-19 | Source: Federal Register

The United States Patent and Trademark Office (Office) is revising the rules of practice in patent cases to implement the micro entity provision of the Leahy-Smith America Invents Act (AIA). Certain patent fees set or adjusted under the fee setting authority in the AIA will be reduced by seventy-five percent for micro entities.
