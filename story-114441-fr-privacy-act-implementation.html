BREAKING: Federal Register: Privacy Act; Implementation

Published: 2021-01-13 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Health and Human Services (HHS or Department) proposes to exempt confidential source identifying information in a system of records maintained by the National Institutes of Health (NIH) from certain requirements of the Privacy Act. The affected system of records is 09-25-0165, "National Institutes of Health (NIH) Office of Loan Repayment and Scholarship (OLRS) Record System, HHS/NIH/OD" (to be renamed "NIH Loan Repayment Records").
