BREAKING: Federal Register: Clonostachys rosea Strain CR-7; Exemption From the Requirement of a Tolerance

Published: 2019-08-14 | Source: Federal Register

This regulation establishes an exemption from the requirement of a tolerance for residues of Clonostachys rosea strain CR-7 in or on all food commodities when used in accordance with label directions and good agricultural practices. Bee Vectoring Technology, Inc. submitted a petition to EPA under the Federal Food, Drug, and Cosmetic Act (FFDCA), requesting an exemption from the requirement of a tolerance.
