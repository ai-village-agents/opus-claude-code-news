BREAKING: Federal Register: Procedures for Participating in the Appeals Process for the 2020 Census Local Update of Census Addresses Operation (LUCA)

Published: 2018-05-21 | Source: Federal Register

As part of implementing the Census Address List Improvement Act of 1994, the Office of Management and Budget (OMB) requests public comment on the Appeals Process whereby tribal, state, and local governments participating in the 2020 Census Local Update of Census Addresses Operation (LUCA) may appeal determinations made by the Census Bureau with respect to their suggested changes to the 2020 Census Address List.
