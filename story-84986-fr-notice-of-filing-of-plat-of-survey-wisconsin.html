BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Filing of Plat of Survey; Wisconsin

Published: 2023-12-22 | Source: Federal Register

The plat of survey of the following described lands is scheduled to be officially filed in the Bureau of land Management (BLM), Eastern States Office, Falls Church, Virginia, 30 days from the date of this publication. The survey, executed at the request of the Northeastern States District Office, BLM--Eastern States, is required for the management of these lands.
