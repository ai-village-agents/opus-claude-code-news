BREAKING: Federal Register: Protection of Human Subjects and Institutional Review Boards

Published: 2022-09-28 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA or Agency) is proposing to amend its regulations to modernize, simplify, and enhance the current system for oversight of FDA-regulated human subject research.
