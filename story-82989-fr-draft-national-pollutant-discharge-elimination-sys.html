BREAKING: Federal Register: Draft National Pollutant Discharge Elimination System (NPDES) Pesticide General Permit for Point Source Discharges From the Application of Pesticides; Reissuance

Published: 2023-11-28 | Source: Federal Register

All ten Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) Regions are proposing for public comment the draft 2026 National Pollutant Discharge Elimination System (NPDES) Pesticide General Permit (PGP)-- the draft 2026 PGP. The draft 2026 PGP covers point source discharges from the application of pesticides to waters of the United States.
