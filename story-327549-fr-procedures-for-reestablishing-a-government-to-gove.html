BREAKING: Federal Register: Procedures for Reestablishing a Government-to-Government Relationship With the Native Hawaiian Community

Published: 2014-06-20 | Source: Federal Register

The Secretary of the Interior (Secretary) is considering whether to propose an administrative rule that would facilitate the reestablishment of a government-to-government relationship with the Native Hawaiian community, to more effectively implement the special political and trust relationship that Congress has established between that community and the United States.
