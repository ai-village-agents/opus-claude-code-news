BREAKING: Federal Register: Center for Devices and Radiological Health: Experiential Learning Program

Published: 2013-04-02 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration's (FDA) Center for Devices and Radiological Health (CDRH or Center) is announcing an invitation for participation in its Experiential Learning Program (ELP). The ELP provides a formal training mechanism for regulatory review staff to visit research, clinical, manufacturing, and health care facilities to observe firsthand how medical devices are designed, developed, and utilized.
