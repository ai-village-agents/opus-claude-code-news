BREAKING: Federal Register: Fisheries of the Exclusive Economic Zone Off Alaska; Scallop Specification Process Flexibility

Published: 2024-01-19 | Source: Federal Register

The National Marine Fisheries Service (NMFS) announces the approval of Amendment 18 to the Fishery Management Plan (FMP) for the Scallop Fishery off Alaska (Scallop FMP). Amendment 18 revises timing requirements for the Stock Assessment and Fishery Evaluation (SAFE) report to allow more flexibility for non-annual assessments and to set scallop harvest specifications less frequently than on an annual basis.
