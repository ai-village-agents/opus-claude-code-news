BREAKING: Federal Register: Filing Dates for the Georgia Senate Special Election

Published: 2020-03-09 | Source: Federal Register

Georgia has scheduled a Special General Election on November 3, 2020, to fill the U.S. Senate seat vacated by Senator Johnny Isakson. Under Georgia law, a majority winner in a Special General Election is declared elected. Should no candidate achieve a majority vote, a Special Runoff Election will be held on January 5, 2021, between the top two vote-getters.
