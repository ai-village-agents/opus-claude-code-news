BREAKING: Federal Register: Modification to Content Published by Import Administration in the Federal Register

Published: 2012-04-30 | Source: Federal Register

Due to the mounting costs of publishing notices in the Federal Register and widespread access to the internet, Import Administration intends to modify the manner in which its determinations in antidumping and countervailing duty proceedings are made available to the public.
