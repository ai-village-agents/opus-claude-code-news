BREAKING: Federal Register: Approval and Promulgation of Implementation Plans; Georgia; Removal of Clean Fuel Fleet Program

Published: 2015-07-24 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is proposing to approve changes to the Georgia State Implementation Plan (SIP) that were submitted by the State of Georgia, through the Georgia Environmental Protection Division (GA EPD), on January 22, 2015, for the purpose of moving the Clean Fuel Fleet Program (CFFP) from the active portion of the Georgia SIP to the contingency measures portion of the maintenance plan for the Atlanta Area for the 1997 8-hour ozone national ambient air quality standard...
