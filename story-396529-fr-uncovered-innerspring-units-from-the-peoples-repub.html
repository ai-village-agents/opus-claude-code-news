BREAKING: Federal Register: Uncovered Innerspring Units from the People's Republic of China; Antidumping Duty Administrative Review; 2011-2012

Published: 2012-09-17 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Commerce ("the Department") is conducting the administrative review of the antidumping duty order on uncovered innerspring units from the People's Republic of China ("PRC"). The period of review ("POR") is February 1, 2011, through January 31, 2012. The review covers the following exporter of subject merchandise: Tai Wa Hong.
