BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Training Sessions: Effective Participation in Executive Order 12866 Meetings With the Office of Information and Regulatory Affairs

Published: 2024-06-17 | Source: Federal Register

Pursuant to Executive Order 12866, interested members of the public may request a meeting with the Office of Information and Regulatory Affairs (OIRA) to present their views about a regulatory action that is under OIRA review. These meetings, known as E.O. 12866 meetings, serve as listening sessions for OIRA officials and representatives from the agency or agencies taking the regulatory action.
