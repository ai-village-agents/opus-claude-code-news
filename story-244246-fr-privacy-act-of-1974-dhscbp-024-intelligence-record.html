BREAKING: Federal Register: Privacy Act of 1974; DHS/CBP-024 Intelligence Records System (CIRS) System of Records

Published: 2017-09-21 | Source: Federal Register

In accordance with the Privacy Act of 1974, the Department of Homeland Security (DHS)/U.S. Customs and Border Protection (CBP) proposes to establish a new DHS CBP system of records titled, "DHS/ CBP-024 CBP Intelligence Records System (CIRS)." As a new SORN in the CBP inventory, CBP will carefully consider public comments, apply appropriate revisions, and republish the CIRS SORN, if necessary, within 180 days of receipt of comments.
