BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Empresa Brasileira de Aeronautica S.A. (Embraer) Airplanes

Published: 2014-08-15 | Source: Federal Register

We are adopting a new airworthiness directive (AD) for all Embraer S.A. Model ERJ 190-100 STD, -100 LR, -100 ECJ, -100 IGW, -200 STD, -200 LR, and -200 IGW airplanes. This AD requires, for certain airplanes, retorquing and replacing the pylon outboard and inboard lower link fittings. For all airplanes, this AD requires repetitive retorquing of the pylon outboard and inboard lower link fittings.
