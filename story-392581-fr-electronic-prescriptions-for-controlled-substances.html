BREAKING: Federal Register: Electronic Prescriptions for Controlled Substances Notice of Approved Certification Process

Published: 2012-08-01 | Source: Federal Register

DEA is announcing a new DEA-approved certification process for Electronic Prescriptions for Controlled Substances (EPCS). Certifying organizations with a certification process approved by DEA pursuant to 21 Code of Federal Regulations (CFR) 1311.300(e) are posted on DEA's Web site once approved.
