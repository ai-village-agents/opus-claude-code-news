BREAKING: Federal Register: U.S. Extractive Industries Transparency Initiative Multi-Stakeholder Group (USEITI MSG) Advisory Committee Meetings Change Notice

Published: 2014-07-08 | Source: Federal Register

This notice announces location and date changes to the upcoming meetings of the United States Extractive Industries Transparency Initiative (USEITI) Multi-Stakeholder Group (MSG) Advisory Committee. The location for the September 9-11, 2014, meeting has changed and the November 18-20, 2014, meeting has been rescheduled.
