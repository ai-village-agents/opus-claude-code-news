BREAKING: Federal Register: Public Notice For Waiver of Aeronautical Land-Use Assurance Mankato Regional Airport, Mankato, MN

Published: 2016-03-22 | Source: Federal Register

The Federal Aviation Administration (FAA) is considering a proposal to authorize the release of 2.35 acres of the airport property at the Mankato Regional Airport, Mankato MN. The City is proposing a land swap to exchange this 2.35 acre parcel for another parcel of 2.0 acres. The acreage being released is not needed for aeronautical use as currently identified on the Airport Layout Plan.
