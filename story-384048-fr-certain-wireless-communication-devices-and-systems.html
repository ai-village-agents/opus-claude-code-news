BREAKING: Federal Register: Certain Wireless Communication Devices and Systems, Components Thereof, and Products Containing Same; Notice of Commission Determination Not To Review Initial Determination Terminating Investigation

Published: 2012-04-25 | Source: Federal Register

Notice is hereby given that the U.S. International Trade Commission has determined not to review an initial determination ("ID") (Order No. 24) of the presiding administrative law judge ("ALJ") terminating the investigation based upon withdrawal of the complaint.
