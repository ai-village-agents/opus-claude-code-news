BREAKING: Federal Register: Taking and Importing Marine Mammals; Taking Marine Mammals Incidental to Fisheries Research

Published: 2016-10-18 | Source: Federal Register

NMFS' Office of Protected Resources has received a request from the NMFS Alaska Fisheries Science Center (AFSC) for authorization to take small numbers of marine mammals incidental to conducting fisheries research, over the course of five years from the date of issuance.
