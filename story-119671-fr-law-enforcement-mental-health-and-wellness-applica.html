BREAKING: Federal Register: Law Enforcement Mental Health and Wellness Application Software Market Survey

Published: 2021-03-24 | Source: Federal Register

The National Institute of Justice (NIJ) is soliciting information for use in an upcoming Criminal Justice Testing and Evaluation Consortium (CJTEC) report that will provide a landscape view of application software for mental health and wellness in the law enforcement community. The report will highlight the vendors/developers creating mental health and wellness application software products (apps) directed to law enforcement end users and other first responders.
