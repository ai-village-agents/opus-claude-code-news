BREAKING: Federal Register: Pacific Gas and Electric Company; Diablo Canyon Nuclear Power Plant, Unit 2

Published: 2023-11-22 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Nuclear Regulatory Commission (NRC, the Commission) is considering issuance of an amendment to Facility Operating License No. DPR-82, issued to Pacific Gas and Electric Company (PG&E, the licensee), for operation of the Diablo Canyon Nuclear Power Plant (Diablo Canyon), Unit 2.
