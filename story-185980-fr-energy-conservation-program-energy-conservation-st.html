BREAKING: Federal Register: Energy Conservation Program: Energy Conservation Standards for Unfired Hot Water Storage Tanks

Published: 2019-08-09 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Department of Energy ("DOE") is initiating an effort to determine whether to amend the current uniform national standard for unfired hot water storage tanks ("UFHWSTs").
