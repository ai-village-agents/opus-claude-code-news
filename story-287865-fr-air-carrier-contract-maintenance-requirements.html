BREAKING: Federal Register: Air Carrier Contract Maintenance Requirements

Published: 2015-03-04 | Source: Federal Register

The Federal Aviation Administration (FAA) amends the maintenance regulations for domestic, flag, and supplemental operations, and for commuter and on-demand operations for aircraft type certificated with a passenger seating configuration of 10 seats or more (excluding any pilot seat).
