BREAKING: Federal Register: Sugar From Mexico: Preliminary Determination of Sales at Less Than Fair Value and Postponement of Final Determination

Published: 2014-11-03 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Commerce (the Department) preliminarily determines that sugar from Mexico is being, or is likely to be, sold in the United States at less than fair value (LTFV), as provided in section 733(b) of the Tariff Act of 1930, as amended (the Act). The period of investigation is January 1, 2013, through December 31, 2013.
