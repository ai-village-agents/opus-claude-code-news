BREAKING: Federal Register: Salicylaldehyde; Exemption From the Requirement of a Tolerance

Published: 2016-03-30 | Source: Federal Register

This regulation establishes an exemption from the requirement of a tolerance for residues of salicylaldehyde (2-hydroxybenzaldehyde, CAS Reg. No. 90-02-8) when used as an inert ingredient (penetration aid) in pesticide formulations applied to growing crops and raw agricultural commodities under 40 CFR 180.910 at a concentration not to exceed 14% by weight of the pesticide formulation.
