BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Funding Opportunity for the Rural Energy Pilot Grants Program (REPP) for Fiscal Year 2022

Published: 2022-01-19 | Source: Federal Register

The Rural Business-Cooperative Service (Agency) announces the availability of up to $10 million in competitive grants awarded to Rural Energy Community Partnerships (RECP) to further develop renewable energy to help meet our nation's energy needs and combat climate change while prioritizing environmental justice, racial equity, and economic opportunity.
