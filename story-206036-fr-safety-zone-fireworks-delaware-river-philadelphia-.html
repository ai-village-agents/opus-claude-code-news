BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone; Fireworks, Delaware River, Philadelphia, PA

Published: 2018-05-02 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard proposes to establish a temporary safety zone for multiple fireworks events launched in the vicinity of Penn's Landing, Philadelphia, Pennsylvania, for waters of the Delaware River, Philadelphia, PA. Establishment of this safety zone is necessary to enhance safety of life on navigable waters immediately prior to, during, and immediately after these fireworks events.
