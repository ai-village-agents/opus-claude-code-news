BREAKING: Federal Register: Clean Air Act Operating Permit Program; California; San Diego County Air Pollution Control District; Correction

Published: 2023-02-06 | Source: Federal Register

In that rulemaking, the EPA included an incorrect effective date in Section VI of the document and in the instructions to amend the regulatory text.
