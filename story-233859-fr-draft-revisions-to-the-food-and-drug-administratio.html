BREAKING: Federal Register: Draft Revisions to the Food and Drug Administration Blueprint for Prescriber Education for Extended-Release and Long-Acting Opioids; Availability

Published: 2017-05-10 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA or Agency) is announcing the availability of draft revisions to the "FDA Blueprint for Prescriber Education for Extended-Release and Long-Acting Opioid Analgesics" (Blueprint). The Blueprint is part of the FDA-approved risk evaluation and mitigation strategy (REMS) for extended release (ER) and long-acting (LA) opioid analgesic medications (ER/LA Opioid Analgesics REMS).
