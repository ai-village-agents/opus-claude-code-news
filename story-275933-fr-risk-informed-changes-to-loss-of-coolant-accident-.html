BREAKING: Federal Register: Risk-Informed Changes to Loss-of-Coolant Accident Technical Requirements

Published: 2016-10-06 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Nuclear Regulatory Commission (NRC) is discontinuing a rulemaking activity titled, "Risk-Informed Changes to Loss-Of- Coolant Accident Technical Requirements." The purpose of this action is to inform members of the public of the discontinuation of this rulemaking and to provide a brief discussion of the NRC's decision to discontinue it.
