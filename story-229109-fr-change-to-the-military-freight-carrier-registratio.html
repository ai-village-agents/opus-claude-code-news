BREAKING: Federal Register: Change to the Military Freight Carrier Registration Program (FCRP) Open Season

Published: 2017-03-08 | Source: Federal Register

The Military Surface Deployment and Distribution Command (SDDC) is providing notice that it will, effective 01 Mar 17, no longer accept domestic motor transportation service provider (TSP) registrations until further notice. This will affect domestic motor TSPs only (common, contract, logistics management, freight forwarders, and brokers). This does not apply to registration of air, rail, ocean, pipeline, and barge TSPs.
