BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Standard for Infant Bath Seats

Published: 2024-10-09 | Source: Federal Register

The standard incorporated by reference ASTM F1967-19, Standard Consumer Safety Specification for Infant Bath Seats, the voluntary standard for infant bath seats that was in effect at the time.
