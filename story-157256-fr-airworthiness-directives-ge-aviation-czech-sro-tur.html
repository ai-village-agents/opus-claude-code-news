BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; GE Aviation Czech s.r.o. Turboprop Engines

Published: 2020-07-27 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA is superseding Airworthiness Directive (AD) 2016-07-13 and AD 2018-03-22 which apply to certain GE Aviation Czech s.r.o. M601D-11, M601E-11, M601E-11A, M601E-11AS, M601E-11S, and M601F model turboprop engines. AD 2016-07-13 required inspection of the engine power turbine (PT) disk and, if found damaged, its replacement with a part eligible for installation.
