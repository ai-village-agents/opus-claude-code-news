BREAKING: Federal Register: Announcement of Scientific Earthquake Studies Advisory Committee Meeting

Published: 2016-12-09 | Source: Federal Register

Pursuant to Public Law 106-503, the Scientific Earthquake Studies Advisory Committee (SESAC) advises the Director of the U.S. Geological Survey (USGS) on matters relating to the USGS's participation in the National Earthquake Hazards Reduction Program. The Committee, which is comprised of members from academia, industry, and State government, will hold its next meeting by teleconference on January 5, 2017, as specified below.
