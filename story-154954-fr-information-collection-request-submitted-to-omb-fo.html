BREAKING: Federal Register: Information Collection Request Submitted to OMB for Review and Approval; Comment Request; Regional Haze Regulations (Renewal)

Published: 2020-06-29 | Source: Federal Register

This is a proposed extension of the ICR, which is currently approved through August 31, 2020.
