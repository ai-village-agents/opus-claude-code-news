BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone; Tall Ships Duluth 2016-Giant Duck, Lake Superior, Duluth, MN

Published: 2016-08-18 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is establishing a safety zone around the Giant Duck barge and its corresponding tug during the Tall Ships Duluth 2016 parade of sail in Lake Superior near Duluth, MN. This safety zone will provide for the regulation of vessel traffic in the vicinity of the tow in the navigable waters of the United States.
