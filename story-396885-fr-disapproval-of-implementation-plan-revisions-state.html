BREAKING: Federal Register: Disapproval of Implementation Plan Revisions; State of California; South Coast VMT Emissions Offset Demonstrations

Published: 2012-09-19 | Source: Federal Register

EPA is proposing to withdraw its final approvals of state implementation plan revisions submitted by the State of California to meet the vehicle-miles-traveled emissions offset requirement under the Clean Air Act for the Los Angeles-South Coast Air Basin 1-hour and 8- hour ozone nonattainment areas. EPA is also proposing to disapprove the same plan revisions.
