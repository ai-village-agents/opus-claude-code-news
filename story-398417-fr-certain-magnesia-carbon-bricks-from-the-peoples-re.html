BREAKING: Federal Register: Certain Magnesia Carbon Bricks From the People's Republic of China: Antidumping Duty Administrative Review; 2010-2011

Published: 2012-10-09 | Source: Federal Register

In response to requests from interested parties, the Department of Commerce ("Department") is conducting the administrative review of the antidumping duty order on certain magnesia carbon bricks from the People's Republic of China ("PRC"), covering the period of review ("POR") of March 12, 2010, through August 31, 2011.
