BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone; Pacific Ocean; Santa Catalina Island, California

Published: 2023-09-20 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Coast Guard is establishing a temporary safety zone for the navigable waters in the Pacific Ocean on the East end of Santa Catalina Island, California. This safety zone is needed to protect personnel, vessels, and the marine environment from potential hazards created by ongoing recovery operations relating to the grounding of the 62-foot F/V PACIFIC KNIGHT.
