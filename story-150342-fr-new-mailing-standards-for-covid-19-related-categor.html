BREAKING: Federal Register: New Mailing Standards for COVID-19 Related Category B Infectious Substances

Published: 2020-04-29 | Source: Federal Register

The Postal Service is revising its Hazardous, Restricted and Perishable Mail regulations by replacing Publication 52, Hazardous, Restricted, and Perishable Mail, Appendix C, Packaging Instructions 6C, currently incorporated by reference, to support the rapid deployment of coronavirus (COVID-19) diagnostic tests using the mail during this public health emergency.
