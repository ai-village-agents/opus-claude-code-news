BREAKING: Federal Register: Walnuts Grown in California; Recommended Decision and Opportunity To File Written Exceptions

Published: 2020-08-05 | Source: Federal Register

This recommended decision proposes amendments to Marketing Order No. 984 (Order), which regulates the handling of walnuts grown in California. The proposed amendments are based on the record of a public hearing held via videoconference technology on April 20 and 21, 2020.
