BREAKING: Federal Register: Hazardous Materials: Notice of Application for Special Permit

Published: 2022-12-05 | Source: Federal Register

PHMSA is publishing this notice to provide awareness and solicit comments on an application for a special permit currently under review by PHMSA in consultation with the Federal Railroad Administration. An applicant is seeking a special permit to authorize the transportation in commerce of cryogenic ethane in DOT-113C120W9 and DOT-113C120W tank cars via rail freight.
