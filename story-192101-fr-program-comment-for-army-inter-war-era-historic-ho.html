BREAKING: Federal Register: Program Comment for Army Inter-War Era Historic Housing (1919-1940)

Published: 2019-10-28 | Source: Federal Register

On September 19, 2019 the Department of the Army notified the Advisory Council on Historic Preservation (ACHP) of the Army's intent to request a Program Comment for Army Inter-War Era Historic Housing 1919-1940 in accordance with the National Historic Preservation Act (NHPA) Section 106, and 36 CFR 800.14(e).
