BREAKING: Federal Register: Privacy Act of 1974; System of Records

Published: 2020-06-02 | Source: Federal Register

Pursuant to the provisions of the Privacy Act of 1974, as amended, the Department of the Interior (DOI) is issuing a public notice of its intent to create a DOI Privacy Act system of records titled, "INTERIOR/DOI-21, eRulemaking Program." This system of records helps DOI manage an eRulemaking Program and the associated rulemaking documents, public comments, and supporting materials submitted on its rulemakings and Federal Register notices.
