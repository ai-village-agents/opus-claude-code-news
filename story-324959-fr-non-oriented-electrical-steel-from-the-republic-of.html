BREAKING: Federal Register: Non-Oriented Electrical Steel From the Republic of Korea: Preliminary Affirmative Determination of Sales at Less Than Fair Value, Negative Preliminary Determination of Critical Circumstances, and Postponement of Final Determination

Published: 2014-05-22 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Commerce (Department) preliminarily determines that non-oriented electrical steel (NOES) from the Republic of Korea (Korea) is being sold, or is likely to be sold in the United States at less than fair value (LTFV), as provided in section 733(b) of the Tariff Act of 1930, as amended (the Act).
