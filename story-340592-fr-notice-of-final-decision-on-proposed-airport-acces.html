BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Final Decision on Proposed Airport Access Restriction

Published: 2014-11-25 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA has completed its review of the application for an airport noise and access restriction submitted by Los Angeles World Airports (LAWA) for the Los Angeles International Airport (LAX). The FAA determined that the application does not provide substantial evidence that the proposed restriction meets three of the six statutory conditions for approval under the Airport Noise and Capacity Act of 1990 (ANCA).
