BREAKING: Federal Register: Guidance Documents Issued by the Office of Government Ethics

Published: 2020-08-20 | Source: Federal Register

Pursuant to Executive Order 13891, the U.S. Office of Government Ethics (OGE) is issuing a regulation to detail the process for the issuance of, modifications to, and petitions regarding guidance documents, as they are defined by the Executive Order. Additionally, the regulation states that OGE will post all guidance documents in a new guidance portal on its website.
