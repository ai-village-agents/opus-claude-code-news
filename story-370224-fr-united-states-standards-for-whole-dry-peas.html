BREAKING: Federal Register: United States Standards for Whole Dry Peas

Published: 2013-11-14 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Agriculture's (USDA) Grain Inspection, Packers and Stockyards Administration (GIPSA) is soliciting comments on proposed revisions to the United States Standards for Whole Dry Peas. The proposal would establish an additional color grading factor requirement for the Whole Dry Peas class "Smooth Yellow Dry Peas" and establish a definition for "fair color yellow peas."
