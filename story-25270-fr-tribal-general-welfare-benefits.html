BREAKING: Federal Register: Tribal General Welfare Benefits

Published: 2025-12-16 | Source: Federal Register

This document contains final regulations regarding the exclusion from gross income of certain Tribal general welfare benefits. The regulations address the requirements that apply to determine whether the benefits an Indian Tribal government program provides qualify as Tribal general welfare benefits.
