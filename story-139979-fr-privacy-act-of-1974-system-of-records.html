BREAKING: Federal Register: Privacy Act of 1974; System of Records

Published: 2021-12-14 | Source: Federal Register

Pursuant to the provisions of the Privacy Act of 1974, the National Aeronautics and Space Administration is issuing public notice of its proposal to alter a previously noticed system of records NASA Health Information Management System/NASA 10HIMS. This adds a retention schedule and revises NASA standard routine uses, as set forth below under the caption SUPPLEMENTARY INFORMATION.
