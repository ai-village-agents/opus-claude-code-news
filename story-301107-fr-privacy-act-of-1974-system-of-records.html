BREAKING: Federal Register: Privacy Act of 1974: System of Records

Published: 2015-08-10 | Source: Federal Register

SBA is amending its Privacy Act system of records notice titled, Business and Community Initiatives Resource Files, SBA-5 to clarify the categories of individuals and categories of records that are covered by that systems of records and also to change the title of the system of records.
