BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone; FKCC Swim Around Key West, Key West, FL

Published: 2018-05-22 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard will enforce the temporary safety zone for the Annual FKCC Swim Around Key West, in Key West, Florida from 9 a.m. until 6 p.m. on June 30, 2018. Our regulation for Recurring Safety Zones in Captain of the Port Key West Zone identifies the regulated area for this event. This action is necessary to ensure the safety of event participants and spectators.
