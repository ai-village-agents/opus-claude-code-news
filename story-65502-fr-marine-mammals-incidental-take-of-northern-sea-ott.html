BREAKING: Federal Register: Marine Mammals; Incidental Take of Northern Sea Otters During Specified Activities; the Gulf of Alaska

Published: 2023-04-19 | Source: Federal Register

In accordance with the Marine Mammal Protection Act of 1972, as amended, and its implementing regulations, we, the U.S. Fish and Wildlife Service, finalize incidental take regulations that facilitate the authorization of nonlethal, incidental, unintentional take by harassment of small numbers of northern sea otters during marine construction and pile driving in the Gulf of Alaska coastal waters.
