BREAKING: Federal Register: United States Travel and Tourism Advisory Board Charter Renewal

Published: 2021-08-25 | Source: Federal Register

The Chief Financial Officer and Assistant Secretary of Commerce for Administration, with the concurrence of the General Services Administration, renewed the Charter for the United States Travel and Tourism Advisory Board on August 16, 2021.
