BREAKING: Federal Register: Orders Granting Authority To Import and Export Natural Gas, To Import and Export Liquefied Natural Gas, and Vacating Authority During March 2017

Published: 2017-04-28 | Source: Federal Register

The Office of Fossil Energy (FE) of the Department of Energy gives notice that during March 2017, it issued orders granting authority to import and export natural gas, to import and export liquefied natural gas (LNG), and vacating authority. These orders are summarized in the attached appendix and may be found on the FE Web site at http://energy.gov/fe/listing-doefe-authorizationsorders-issued-2017.
