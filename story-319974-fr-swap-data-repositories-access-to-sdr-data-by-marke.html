BREAKING: Federal Register: Swap Data Repositories-Access to SDR Data by Market Participants

Published: 2014-03-26 | Source: Federal Register

The Commodity Futures Trading Commission ("Commission" or "CFTC") is adopting an interim final rule to clarify the scope of permissible access by market participants to swap data and information maintained by a registered swap data repository ("SDR").
