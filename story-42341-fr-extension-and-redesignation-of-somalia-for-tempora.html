BREAKING: Federal Register: Extension and Redesignation of Somalia for Temporary Protected Status

Published: 2024-07-22 | Source: Federal Register

Through this notice, the Department of Homeland Security (DHS) announces that the Secretary of Homeland Security (Secretary) is extending the designation of Somalia for Temporary Protected Status (TPS) and redesignating Somalia for TPS for 18 months, beginning on September 18, 2024, and ending on March 17, 2026.
