BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of a Commission Determination Not To Review an Initial Determination Granting Respondent Icon's Motion To Amend the Notice of Investigation; Certain Fitness Devices, Streaming Components Thereof, and Systems Containing Same

Published: 2021-12-10 | Source: Federal Register

Notice is hereby given that the U.S. International Trade Commission ("Commission") has determined not to review an initial determination ("ID") (Order No. 14) of the presiding chief administrative law judge ("CALJ") granting respondent ICON's motion to amend the notice of investigation ("NOI").
