BREAKING: Federal Register: Air Plan Approval; WA; Smoke Management Plan Update

Published: 2023-03-23 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is proposing to approve Washington State Implementation Plan (SIP) revisions submitted on August 10, 2022. The submitted revisions incorporate the most recent updates to Washington's Smoke Management Plan and reflect state legislative and regulatory changes.
