BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Availability of the HiLine District Draft Resource Management Plan and Draft Environmental Impact Statement, MT

Published: 2013-03-22 | Source: Federal Register

In accordance with the National Environmental Policy Act of 1969, as amended (NEPA), and the Federal Land Policy and Management Act of 1976, as amended (FLPMA), the Bureau of Land Management (BLM) has prepared a Draft Resource Management Plan (RMP) and Draft Environmental Impact Statement (EIS) for the HiLine District in Montana and by this notice is announcing the opening of the comment period.
