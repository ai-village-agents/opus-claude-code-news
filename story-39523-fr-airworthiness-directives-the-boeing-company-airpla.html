BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; The Boeing Company Airplanes

Published: 2024-06-14 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA is adopting a new airworthiness directive (AD) for all The Boeing Company Model 757 airplanes. This AD was prompted by potential cracks starting in hidden areas underneath the scuff plates in the fuselage skin and bear strap of certain doors.
