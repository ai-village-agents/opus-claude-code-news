BREAKING: Federal Register: Determination That SULAR (Nisoldipine) Extended-Release Tablets, 10 Milligrams, 20 Milligrams, 25.5 Milligrams, 30 Milligrams, and 40 Milligrams, Were Not Withdrawn From Sale for Reasons of Safety or Effectiveness

Published: 2014-08-25 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA) has determined that SULAR (nisoldipine) extended-release tablets, 10 milligrams (mg), 20 mg, 25.5 mg, 30 mg, and 40 mg, were not withdrawn from sale for reasons of safety or effectiveness.
