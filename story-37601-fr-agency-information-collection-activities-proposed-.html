BREAKING: Federal Register: Agency Information Collection Activities: Proposed Collection; Comment Request

Published: 2024-05-22 | Source: Federal Register

This notice announces the intention of the Agency for Healthcare Research and Quality (AHRQ) to request that the Office of Management and Budget (OMB) approve the proposed information collection project "Implementation and Testing of Diagnostic Safety Resources." This proposed information collection was previously published in the Federal Register on March 7th, 2024 and allowed 60 days for public comment.
