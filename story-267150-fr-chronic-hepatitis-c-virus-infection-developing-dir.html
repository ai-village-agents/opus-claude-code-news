BREAKING: Federal Register: Chronic Hepatitis C Virus Infection: Developing Direct-Acting Antiviral Drugs for Treatment; Draft Guidance for Industry; Extension of the Comment Period

Published: 2016-06-27 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA or we) is extending the comment period for the notice of availability, published in the Federal Register of May 4, 2016 (81 FR 26805), announcing the draft guidance for industry entitled "Chronic Hepatitis C Virus Infection: Developing Direct-Acting Antiviral Drugs for Treatment." We are taking this action due to maintenance on the Federal eRulemaking portal from July 1 through July 5, 2016.
