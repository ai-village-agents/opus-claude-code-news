BREAKING: Federal Register: Air Plan Revisions; California; Technical Amendments

Published: 2020-07-15 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is taking final action to delete various local rules from the California State Implementation Plan (SIP) that were approved in error. These rules include general nuisance provisions, Federal New Source Performance Standards or National Emission Standards for Hazardous Air Pollutant requirements, hearing board procedures, variance provisions, and local fee provisions.
