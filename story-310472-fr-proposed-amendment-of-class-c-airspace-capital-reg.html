BREAKING: Federal Register: Proposed Amendment of Class C Airspace; Capital Region International Airport, MI

Published: 2015-11-27 | Source: Federal Register

This action proposes to modify Class C airspace at Capital Region International Airport, formerly Lansing Capital City Airport, Lansing, MI, by removing a cutout from the surface area that was put in place to accommodate operations around Davis Airport, now permanently closed. Also, this proposal would update the airport's name and geographic coordinates to reflect the current information in the FAA's aeronautical database.
