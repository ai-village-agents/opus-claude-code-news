BREAKING: Federal Register: Aggregation of Basis for Partnership Distributions Involving Equity Interests of a Partner

Published: 2015-06-12 | Source: Federal Register

This document contains proposed regulations that would allow consolidated group members that are partners in the same partnership to aggregate their bases in stock distributed by the partnership for the purpose of limiting the application of rules that might otherwise cause basis reduction or gain recognition.
