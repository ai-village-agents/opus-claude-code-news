BREAKING: Federal Register: President's Commission on White House Fellowships Advisory Committee: Closed Meeting

Published: 2016-05-05 | Source: Federal Register

The President's Commission on White House Fellowships (PCWHF) was established by an Executive Order in 1964. The PCWHF is an advisory committee composed of Special Government Employees appointed by the President. The Advisory Committee meets in June to interview potential candidates for recommendation to become a White House Fellow. The meeting is closed.
