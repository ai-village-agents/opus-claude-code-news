BREAKING: Federal Register: Renewal of Academic Research Council

Published: 2021-03-18 | Source: Federal Register

The Bureau of Consumer Financial Protection (Bureau), after consultation with the Committee Management Secretariat of the General Services Administration, will renew the Academic Research Council (the committee or the ARC) effective on March 18, 2021.
