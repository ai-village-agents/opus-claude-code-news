BREAKING: Federal Register: Modification of Regulations Regarding the Steel Import Monitoring and Analysis System

Published: 2020-03-30 | Source: Federal Register

On May 17, 2019, the United States announced joint understandings with Canada and Mexico, respectively, to eliminate tariffs imposed under Section 232 of the Trade Expansion Act of 1962, as amended, on imports of steel and aluminum products from Canada and Mexico and to establish a process for monitoring such imports.
