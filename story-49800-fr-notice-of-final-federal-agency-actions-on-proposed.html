BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Final Federal Agency Actions on Proposed Transportation Project in Florida

Published: 2024-10-15 | Source: Federal Register

The FHWA, on behalf of the FDOT, is issuing this notice to announce actions taken by FDOT and other Federal Agencies that are final agency actions. These actions relate to the proposed Truck Parking Central Florida--Westbound Volusia County Site (Financial Management Number 446445-4). The Westbound Volusia County Site 1B is located along Interstate 4 (I-4) Westbound approximately 4.5 miles west of the I-95 interchange with I-4.
