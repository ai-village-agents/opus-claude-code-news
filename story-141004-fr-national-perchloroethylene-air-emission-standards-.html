BREAKING: Federal Register: National Perchloroethylene Air Emission Standards for Dry Cleaning Facilities Technology Review

Published: 2021-12-27 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is proposing amendments to the National Emission Standards for Hazardous Air Pollutants (NESHAP) for dry cleaning facilities using perchloroethylene (PCE) as the cleaning solvent (PCE Dry Cleaning NESHAP). The proposed amendments address the results of the technology review for the PCE Dry Cleaning NESHAP, in accordance with section 112 of the Clean Air Act (CAA).
