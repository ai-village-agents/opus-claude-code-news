BREAKING: Federal Register: Electronic Study Data Submission; Data Standards; Support for Study Data Tabulation Model Implementation Guide Version 3.2

Published: 2015-08-18 | Source: Federal Register

The Center for Biologics Evaluation and Research (CBER) and the Center for Drug Evaluation and Research (CDER) are announcing support for the 3.2 version (see section II. Exceptions) of Clinical Data Interchange Standards Consortium (CDISC) Study Data Tabulation Model Implementation Guide (SDTM IG 3.2), an update to the FDA Data Standards Catalog (Catalog), and availability of validation rules for the 3.2 version.
