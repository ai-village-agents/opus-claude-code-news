BREAKING: Federal Register: Social Security Number Fraud Prevention Act Requirements

Published: 2024-04-12 | Source: Federal Register

The Office of Personnel Management (OPM) is publishing this direct final rule to implement the requirements of the Social Security Number Fraud Prevention Act of 2017 (Act). In accordance with the Act, OPM is amending its privacy procedures to prohibit the inclusion of Social Security numbers (SSNs) on any document sent through the mail unless the Director of OPM deems it necessary.
