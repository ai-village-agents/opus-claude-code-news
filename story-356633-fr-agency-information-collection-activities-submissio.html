BREAKING: Federal Register: Agency Information Collection Activities; Submission for OMB Review; Comment Request; Examination Questionnaire

Published: 2013-06-05 | Source: Federal Register

The OCC, as part of its continuing effort to reduce paperwork and respondent burden, invites the general public and other Federal agencies to comment on a continuing information collection, as required by the Paperwork Reduction Act of 1995 (44 U.S.C. chapter 35) (PRA). Under the PRA, Federal agencies are required to publish notice in the Federal Register concerning each proposed collection of information, including each proposed extension of an existing collection of information.
