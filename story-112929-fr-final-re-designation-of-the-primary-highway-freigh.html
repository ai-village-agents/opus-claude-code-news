BREAKING: Federal Register: Final Re-Designation of the Primary Highway Freight System (PHFS)

Published: 2022-12-22 | Source: Federal Register

This notice announces the re-designated PHFS to meet the statutory requirements of the authorizing law. This notice presents a final, re-designated PHFS, provides summary analysis of input received for PHFS re-designation, FHWA responses to comments, the methodology applied, and changes made for the re-designation of the PHFS.
