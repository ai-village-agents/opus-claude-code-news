BREAKING: Federal Register: Record of Decision for F35A Training Basing Final Environmental Impact Statement

Published: 2012-08-10 | Source: Federal Register

On August 1, 2012, the United States Air Force signed the ROD for the F35A Training Basing Final Environmental Impact Statement (FEIS). The ROD states the Air Force decision to implement the Preffered Alternative to beddown 72 F35A Primary aircraft authorized (PAA) training aircraft at Luke Air Force Base, Arizona.
