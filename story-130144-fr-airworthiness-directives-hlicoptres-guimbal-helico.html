BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Hélicoptères Guimbal Helicopters

Published: 2021-08-09 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA proposes to adopt a new airworthiness directive (AD) for H[eacute]licopt[egrave]res Guimbal (HG) Model Cabri G2 helicopters with certain part-numbered aluminum cooling fans (cooling fan) installed. This proposed AD was prompted by reports of two occurrences of in-flight shutdowns due to a crack and subsequent failure of the cooling fan.
