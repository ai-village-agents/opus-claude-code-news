BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Plans for Department of Veterans Affairs to Assess Exposures and Conditions of Interest for Veterans Who Served At Karshi-Khanabad Air Base and To Solicit Public Comment

Published: 2024-11-27 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Veterans Affairs (VA) announces that it plans to conduct an assessment of scientific literature and historical claims data to determine whether there is an association between military environmental exposure to toxic substances related to military service at Camp Stronghold Freedom in Karshi-Khanabad (K2) in Uzbekistan from 2001 to 2005 and medical conditions.
