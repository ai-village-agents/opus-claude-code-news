BREAKING: Federal Register: NASA Advisory Council; Meeting Postponement

Published: 2014-11-26 | Source: Federal Register

In accordance with the Federal Advisory Committee Act, Public Law 92-463, as amended, NASA announces a postponement of the previously announced meeting of the NASA Advisory Council (NAC). The meeting had been scheduled to be held on December 8 and 9, 2014.
