BREAKING: Federal Register: Center for Drug Evaluation and Research Center for Clinical Trial Innovation

Published: 2024-04-16 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA) is publishing this notice to announce the establishment of the Center for Drug Evaluation and Research (CDER) Center for Clinical Trial Innovation (C3TI). C3TI aims to be a central hub within CDER that supports innovative approaches to clinical trials that are designed to improve the quality and efficiency of drug development and regulatory decision making.
