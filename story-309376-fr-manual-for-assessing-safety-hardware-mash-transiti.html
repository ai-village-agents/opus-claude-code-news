BREAKING: Federal Register: Manual for Assessing Safety Hardware (MASH) Transition

Published: 2015-11-13 | Source: Federal Register

In issuing Federal-aid eligibility letters for roadside safety hardware, FHWA currently makes determinations of continued eligibility for modifications to devices tested to the National Cooperative Highway Research Program Report 350 (NCHRP 350).
