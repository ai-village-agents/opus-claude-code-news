BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Realty Action: Proposed Non-Competitive Lease of Public Land in Johnson County, WY

Published: 2017-09-28 | Source: Federal Register

The Bureau of Land Management (BLM), Buffalo Field Office, proposes to lease a parcel of public land totaling 0.87 acres in Johnson County, Wyoming, for residential purposes. The subject parcel was inadvertently developed by the adjacent landowner (proposed lessee) as a residence without authorization. The area has a long history of occupancy and the proposed lease would provide the BLM with a reasonable option to resolve the continued unauthorized use of the affected public land.
