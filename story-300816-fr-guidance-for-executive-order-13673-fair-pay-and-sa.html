BREAKING: Federal Register: Guidance for Executive Order 13673, “Fair Pay and Safe Workplaces”

Published: 2015-08-05 | Source: Federal Register

On May 28, 2015, the Department of Labor (DOL) published proposed guidance to assist federal agencies and the contracting community in implementing Executive Order 13673, "Fair Pay and Safe Workplaces," which is designed to improve contractor compliance with labor laws and increase efficiency and cost savings in Federal contracting.
