BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; The Boeing Company Airplanes

Published: 2014-07-01 | Source: Federal Register

We are revising an earlier proposed airworthiness directive (AD) for certain The Boeing Company Model 757-200, -200PF, -200CB, and -300 series airplanes. The NPRM proposed to require, for certain airplanes, installing new relays adjacent to two of the spoiler control modules.
