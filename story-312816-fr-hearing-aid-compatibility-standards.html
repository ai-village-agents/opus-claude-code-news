BREAKING: Federal Register: Hearing Aid Compatibility Standards

Published: 2015-12-28 | Source: Federal Register

In this document, the Commission proposes to amend its hearing aid compatibility (HAC) rules to enhance equal access to the national telecommunications network by people with hearing loss and implement the Twenty-First Century Communications and Video Accessibility.
