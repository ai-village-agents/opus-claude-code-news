BREAKING: Federal Register: Surface Transportation Environment and Planning Cooperative Research Program (STEP)

Published: 2012-06-28 | Source: Federal Register

Section 5207 of the Safe, Accountable, Flexible, Efficient Transportation Equity Act: A Legacy for Users (SAFETEA-LU) established the Surface Transportation Environment and Planning Cooperative Research Program (STEP). The FHWA anticipates that the STEP or a similar program to provide resources for national research on issues related to planning, environment, and realty will be included in future surface transportation legislation.
