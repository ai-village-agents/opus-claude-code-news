BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; The Boeing Company Airplanes

Published: 2014-08-05 | Source: Federal Register

We are adopting a new airworthiness directive (AD) for all The Boeing Company Model 737-600, -700, -700C, -800, -900 and -900ER series airplanes. This AD was prompted by reports of incorrectly installed bolts common to the rear spar termination fitting on the horizontal stabilizer.
