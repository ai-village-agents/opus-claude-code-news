BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone; Homewood Wedding Fireworks Display, Lake Tahoe, Homewood, CA

Published: 2019-07-30 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is establishing a temporary safety zone on the navigable waters of McKinney Bay in Lake Tahoe in support of the Homewood Wedding Fireworks Display on August 2, 2019. This safety zone is necessary to protect personnel, vessels, and the marine environment from the dangers associated with pyrotechnics.
