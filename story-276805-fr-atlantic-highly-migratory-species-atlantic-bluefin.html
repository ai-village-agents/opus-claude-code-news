BREAKING: Federal Register: Atlantic Highly Migratory Species; Atlantic Bluefin Tuna Fisheries

Published: 2016-10-18 | Source: Federal Register

NMFS is transferring 18 metric tons (mt) of Atlantic bluefin tuna (BFT) quota from the Harpoon category and 67 mt from the Reserve category to the General category for the remainder of the 2016 fishing year. These transfers result in adjusted 2016 quotas of 676.7 mt, 20.6 mt, and 8.6 mt for the General, Harpoon, and Reserve category quotas, respectively.
