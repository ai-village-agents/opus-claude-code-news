BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Honeywell International Inc. Turboprop and Turboshaft Engines

Published: 2018-06-25 | Source: Federal Register

We propose to supersede Airworthiness Directive (AD) 2018-02- 14, which applies to certain Honeywell International Inc. (Honeywell) TPE331 turboprop and TSE331 turboshaft engines. AD 2018-02-14 requires inspection of the affected combustion chamber case assembly, replacement of those assemblies found cracked, and removal of affected assemblies on certain TPE331 and TSE331 engines.
