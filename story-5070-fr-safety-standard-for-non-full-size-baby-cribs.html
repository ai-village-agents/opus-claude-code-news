BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Standard for Non-Full-Size Baby Cribs

Published: 2025-01-31 | Source: Federal Register

In December 2010, the U.S. Consumer Product Safety Commission (CPSC or Commission) published a consumer product safety standard for non-full-size baby cribs (NFS cribs) pursuant to section 104 of the Consumer Product Safety Improvement Act of 2008 (CPSIA).
