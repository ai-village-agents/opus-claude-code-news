BREAKING: Federal Register: Central Florida Rail Corridor's Request To Amend Its Positive Train Control System

Published: 2024-02-26 | Source: Federal Register

This document provides the public with notice that, on February 15, 2024, the Central Florida Rail Corridor (CFRC) submitted a request for amendment (RFA) to its FRA-certified positive train control (PTC) system in order to temporarily disable its PTC system between Milepost (MP) 749.60 and MP 755.4 to perform infrastructure upgrades to better facilitate vehicular travel across a crossing.
