BREAKING: Federal Register: Swine Vesicular Disease Status of the Regions of Tuscany and Umbria, Italy

Published: 2024-06-12 | Source: Federal Register

We are advising the public that we are recognizing the regions of Tuscany and Umbria, Italy as being free of swine vesicular disease. This recognition is based on a risk evaluation we prepared in connection with this action and made available to the public for review and comment through a previous notice.
