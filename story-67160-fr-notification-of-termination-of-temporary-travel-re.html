BREAKING: Federal Register: Notification of Termination of Temporary Travel Restrictions Applicable to Land Ports of Entry and Ferries Service Between the United States and Mexico

Published: 2023-05-10 | Source: Federal Register

This Notification announces the decision of the Secretary of Homeland Security ("Secretary"), after consulting with interagency partners, to terminate temporary restrictions on travel by certain noncitizens into the United States at land ports of entry ("land POEs"), including ferry terminals, along the United States-Mexico border.
