BREAKING: Federal Register: Standards for Safeguarding Customer Information

Published: 2019-04-04 | Source: Federal Register

The Federal Trade Commission ("FTC" or "Commission") requests public comment on its proposal to amend the Standards for Safeguarding Customer Information ("Safeguards Rule" or "Rule"). The proposal contains five main modifications to the existing Rule.
