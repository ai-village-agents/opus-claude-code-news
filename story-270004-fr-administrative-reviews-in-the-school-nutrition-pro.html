BREAKING: Federal Register: Administrative Reviews in the School Nutrition Programs

Published: 2016-07-29 | Source: Federal Register

As required by the Healthy, Hunger-Free Kids Act of 2010, this final rule revises the State agency's administrative review process in the National School Lunch Program and School Breakfast Program to establish a unified accountability system designed to ensure that school food authorities offering school meals comply with program requirements.
