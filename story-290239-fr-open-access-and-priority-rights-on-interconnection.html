BREAKING: Federal Register: Open Access and Priority Rights on Interconnection Customer's Interconnection Facilities

Published: 2015-04-01 | Source: Federal Register

In this Final Rule, the Federal Energy Regulatory Commission is amending its regulations to waive the Open Access Transmission Tariff requirements, the Open Access Same-Time Information System requirements, and the Standards of Conduct requirements, under certain conditions, for the ownership, control, or operation of Interconnection Customer's Interconnection Facilities (ICIF).
