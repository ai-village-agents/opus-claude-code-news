BREAKING: Federal Register: Request for Information on Research in Dedicated Habitat Research Areas; Fisheries of the Northeastern United States; Essential Fish Habitat

Published: 2022-07-20 | Source: Federal Register

NMFS is requesting information about ongoing and proposed research in the Stellwagen and Georges Bank Dedicated Habitat Research Areas, which were established in 2018. The regulations require the NMFS Regional Administrator to initiate a review, consult with the New England Fishery Management Council about, and evaluate the use of the Dedicated Habitat Research Areas 3 years after their establishment.
