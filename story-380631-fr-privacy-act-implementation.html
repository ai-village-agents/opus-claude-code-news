BREAKING: Federal Register: Privacy Act; Implementation

Published: 2012-03-16 | Source: Federal Register

The National Security Agency/Central Security Service (NSA/ CSS) is adding a new exemption rule for GNSA 29 to exempt those records that are presently exempt from certain requirements of the Privacy Act. This direct final rule makes nonsubstantive changes to the National Security Agency/Central Security Service Privacy Program rules.
