BREAKING: Federal Register: TWIC-Reader Requirements; Second Delay of Effective Date

Published: 2024-10-31 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is further delaying the effective date for certain facilities affected by the final rule entitled "Transportation Worker Identification Credential (TWIC)--Reader Requirements," published in the Federal Register on August 23, 2016. On March 9, 2020, the Coast Guard published a rule, delaying the implementation date to May 8, 2023.
