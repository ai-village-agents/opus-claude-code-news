BREAKING: Federal Register: Air Plan Approval; Oregon: Lane County Permitting and General Rule Revisions

Published: 2018-07-31 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) proposes to approve, and incorporate by reference, specific changes to the Oregon State Implementation Plan as it applies in Lane County, Oregon. The local air agency in Lane County, Lane Regional Air Protection Agency, has revised its rules to align with recent changes to Oregon state regulations.
