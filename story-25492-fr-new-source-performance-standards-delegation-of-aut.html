BREAKING: Federal Register: New Source Performance Standards; Delegation of Authority to Oklahoma

Published: 2025-12-18 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is approving updates to the Code of Federal Regulations that reflect Oklahoma's current New Source Performance Standards (NSPS) delegation status and the mailing address for the Oklahoma Department of Environmental Quality (ODEQ). The ODEQ submitted updated regulations for receiving delegation of EPA authority for implementation and enforcement of certain NSPS.
