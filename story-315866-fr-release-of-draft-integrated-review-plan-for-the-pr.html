BREAKING: Federal Register: Release of Draft Integrated Review Plan for the Primary National Ambient Air Quality Standards for Oxides of Nitrogen

Published: 2014-02-06 | Source: Federal Register

On or about February 10, 2014, the Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is making available for public review the draft titled, Integrated Review Plan for the Primary National Ambient Air Quality Standards for Nitrogen Dioxide (draft IRP). This document contains the plans for the review of the air quality criteria for oxides of nitrogen and national ambient air quality standards (NAAQS) for nitrogen dioxide (NO2).
