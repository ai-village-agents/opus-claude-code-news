BREAKING: Federal Register: Drawbridge Operation Regulation; Atlantic Intracoastal Waterway, at Jupiter, FL

Published: 2023-10-20 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard proposes to temporarily modify the operating schedule that governs the Indiantown Road Bridge across the Atlantic Intracoastal Waterway (AICW), mile 1006.2, at Jupiter, Florida. This action is necessary to alleviate vehicle traffic congestion on the Indiantown Road Bridge caused by the replacement and closure of a nearby bridge.
