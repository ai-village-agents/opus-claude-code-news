BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zones; Fireworks Displays in the Sector Columbia River Captain of the Port Zone Columbia River Zone

Published: 2013-05-16 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard will enforce the safety zones listed in this notice for fireworks displays in the Sector Columbia River Captain of the Port Zone from May 2013 until October 2013. This action is necessary to ensure the safety of the crews onboard the vessels displaying the fireworks, the maritime public, and all other observers.
