BREAKING: Federal Register: Special Local Regulation; San Diego Bay, San Diego, CA

Published: 2021-10-29 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is establishing a temporary special local regulation (SLR) in the navigable waters of San Diego Bay, California during the Baja Ha-Ha XXVII Pre-Rally Parade marine event. This regulation is necessary to provide for the safety of the participants, crew, spectators, sponsor vessels of the parade, and general users of the waterway during the event, which will be held on November 1, 2021.
