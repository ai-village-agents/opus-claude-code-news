BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Inventory Completion Amendment: S'edav Va'aki Museum, City of Phoenix, Phoenix, AZ

Published: 2026-01-07 | Source: Federal Register

In accordance with the Native American Graves Protection and Repatriation Act (NAGPRA), the S'edav Va'aki Museum (formerly Pueblo Grande Museum), City of Phoenix has amended a notice of inventory completion published in the Federal Register on February 8, 2019. This notice amends the Indian Tribes or Native Hawaiian organizations with cultural affiliation.
