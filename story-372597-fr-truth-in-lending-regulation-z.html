BREAKING: Federal Register: Truth in Lending (Regulation Z)

Published: 2013-12-16 | Source: Federal Register

The Bureau of Consumer Financial Protection (Bureau) is publishing this final rule amending the regulatory text and official interpretations for Regulation Z, which implements the Truth in Lending Act (TILA).
