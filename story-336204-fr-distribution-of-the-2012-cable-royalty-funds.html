BREAKING: Federal Register: Distribution of the 2012 Cable Royalty Funds

Published: 2014-10-01 | Source: Federal Register

The Copyright Royalty Judges are soliciting comments on a motion of Phase I claimants for partial distribution in connection with the 2012 cable royalty funds. The Judges are also requesting comments as to the existence of Phase I and Phase II controversies with respect to the distribution of 2012 cable royalty funds.
