BREAKING: Federal Register: Public Meeting of the Missouri Advisory Committee To Discuss Civil Rights Topics in the State

Published: 2018-12-21 | Source: Federal Register

Notice is hereby given, pursuant to the provisions of the rules and regulations of the U.S. Commission on Civil Rights (Commission) and the Federal Advisory Committee Act that the Missouri Advisory Committee (Committee) will hold a meeting on Friday, January 11, 2019, at 3:00 p.m. (Central) for the purpose discussing civil rights topics in the state.
