BREAKING: Federal Register: Multilayered Wood Flooring From the People's Republic of China: Preliminary Rescission of 2014-2015 Antidumping Duty New Shipper Reviews

Published: 2016-05-31 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Commerce ("the Department") is conducting two new shipper reviews ("NSRs") of the antidumping duty order on multilayered wood flooring from the People's Republic of China ("PRC"). The NSRs cover two exporters and producers of subject merchandise, Dongtai Zhangshi Wood Industry Co., Ltd. ("Zhangshi") and Huzhou Muyun Wood Co., Ltd. ("Muyun").
