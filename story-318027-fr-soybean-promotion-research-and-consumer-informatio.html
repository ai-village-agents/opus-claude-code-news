BREAKING: Federal Register: Soybean Promotion, Research, and Consumer Information Program: Amendment of Procedures and Notification of Request for Referendum

Published: 2014-03-04 | Source: Federal Register

This interim final rule would amend the procedures to Request a Referendum by removing the specific number of soybean producers eligible to request a referendum under the Soybean Promotion, Research, and Consumer Information program, commonly known as the Soybean Checkoff Program. The number of soybean producers will be replaced with language that allows the Secretary of Agriculture (Secretary) to update this number based on information provided by USDA.
