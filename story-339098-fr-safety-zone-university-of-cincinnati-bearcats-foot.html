BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone; University of Cincinnati Bearcats Football Fireworks; Ohio River, Mile 470.4-470.8; Cincinnati, OH

Published: 2014-11-06 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is establishing a temporary safety zone for all waters of the Ohio River, surface to bottom, extending from Ohio River mile 470.4 to mile 470.8 at Cincinnati, Ohio. This temporary safety zone is necessary to protect persons and property from potential damage and safety hazards during the University of Cincinnati Bearcats Football Fireworks.
