BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Eurocopter France (Eurocopter) Model Helicopters

Published: 2013-06-17 | Source: Federal Register

We propose to supersede an existing airworthiness directive (AD) for Eurocopter Model AS350B, BA, B1, B2, C, D, and D1 helicopters and Model AS355E, F, F1, F2, and N helicopters with certain part- numbered tail gearbox (TGB) control levers installed. The existing AD currently requires repetitive visual inspections of the TGB control lever for a crack and replacing a cracked TGB control lever with an airworthy TGB control lever.
