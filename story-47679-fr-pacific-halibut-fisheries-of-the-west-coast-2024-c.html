BREAKING: Federal Register: Pacific Halibut Fisheries of the West Coast; 2024 Catch Sharing Plan; Inseason Action

Published: 2024-09-20 | Source: Federal Register

NMFS announces inseason action for the Pacific halibut recreational fishery in the International Pacific Halibut Commission's (IPHC) regulatory Area 2A off Washington, Oregon, and California. Specifically, this action transfers 12,000 pounds (lb; 5.4 metric tons (mt)) of the Area 2A Pacific halibut recreational allocation, in net pounds, from the Oregon recreational fishery to the Washington recreational fishery.
