BREAKING: Federal Register: Approval and Promulgation of Air Quality Implementation Plans; Washington; Update to Materials Incorporated by Reference

Published: 2016-03-31 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is updating the materials that are incorporated by reference (IBR) into the Washington State Implementation Plan (SIP). The regulations affected by this update have been previously submitted by the Washington State Department of Ecology (Ecology) and approved by the EPA.
