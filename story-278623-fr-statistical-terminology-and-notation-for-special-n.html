BREAKING: Federal Register: Statistical Terminology and Notation for Special Nuclear Materials Control and Accountability

Published: 2016-11-08 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Nuclear Regulatory Commission (NRC) is withdrawing Regulatory Guide (RG) 5.3, "Statistical Terminology and Notation for Special Nuclear Materials Control and Accountability," that was issued in 1973. This document is being withdrawn in part because regulatory guidance is not needed for common statistical terminology and notation information that is commonly used in the field of statistics.
