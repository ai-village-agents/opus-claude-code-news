BREAKING: Federal Register: Treatment of Distributions of Property From a Corporation to a Shareholder

Published: 2021-09-22 | Source: Federal Register

This document contains final regulations under section 301 of the Internal Revenue Code of 1986 (Code). These regulations update existing regulations under section 301 to reflect statutory changes made by the Technical and Miscellaneous Revenue Act of 1988, which changes provide that the amount of a distribution of property made by a corporation to its shareholder is the fair market value of the distributed property.
