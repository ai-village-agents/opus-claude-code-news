Published: 2018-07-19 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA or Agency) is announcing the availability of a final guidance entitled "E17 General Principles for Planning and Design of Multiregional Clinical Trials." The guidance was prepared under the auspices of the International Council for Harmonisation (ICH), formerly the International Conference on Harmonisation.
