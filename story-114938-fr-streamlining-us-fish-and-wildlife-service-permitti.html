BREAKING: Federal Register: Streamlining U.S. Fish and Wildlife Service Permitting of Rights-of-Way

Published: 2021-01-19 | Source: Federal Register

We, the U.S. Fish and Wildlife Service (FWS), propose to revise and streamline FWS regulations for permitting of rights-of-way by aligning FWS processes more closely with those of other Department of the Interior bureaus, consistent with applicable law and to the extent practicable.
