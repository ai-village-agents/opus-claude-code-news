BREAKING: Federal Register: Medical Student Education Program; Notice of Deviation to Maximum Competition Requirements

Published: 2020-04-20 | Source: Federal Register

HRSA is requesting a deviation from competition requirements in order to fund additional applications received in fiscal year 2019 under HRSA-19-101, Medical Student Education (MSE) Program, and to fund supplements to current award recipients. In fiscal year 2020, Congress provided $50,000,000 to fund the MSE program.
