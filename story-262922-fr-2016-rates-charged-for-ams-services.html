BREAKING: Federal Register: 2016 Rates Charged for AMS Services

Published: 2016-05-06 | Source: Federal Register

The Agricultural Marketing Service (AMS) is announcing the 2016 rates it will charge for voluntary grading, inspection, certification, auditing and laboratory services for a variety of agricultural commodities including meat and poultry, fruits and vegetables, eggs, dairy products, and cotton and tobacco.
