BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Agusta S.p.A. Helicopters

Published: 2012-05-22 | Source: Federal Register

We propose to adopt a new airworthiness directive (AD) for certain Agusta S.p.A. (Agusta) Model AW139 helicopters. This proposed AD is prompted by the discovery of improper installation of solder splices on the co-pilot audio system causing intermittent noise through the audio system during flight.
