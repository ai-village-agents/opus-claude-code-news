BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Availability for the Draft Environmental Impact Statement and Announcement of Public Hearings for the Proposed Champlain Hudson Power Express Transmission Line Project

DOE is also announcing four public hearings to receive comments on the Draft EIS.
