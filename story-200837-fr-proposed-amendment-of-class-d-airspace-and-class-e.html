BREAKING: Federal Register: Proposed Amendment of Class D Airspace and Class E Airspace; Erie, PA

Published: 2018-02-26 | Source: Federal Register

This action proposes to amend Class D airspace, Class E surface airspace, and Class E airspace designated as an extension to a Class D surface area, by updating the name to Erie International Airport/Tom Ridge Field, Erie, PA. This action also proposes to amend Class E airspace extending upward from 700 feet above the surface in Erie, PA, by updating the name to St. Vincent Health Center Heliport.
