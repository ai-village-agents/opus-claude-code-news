BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; The Boeing Company Airplanes

Published: 2016-11-25 | Source: Federal Register

We are adopting a new airworthiness directive (AD) for all The Boeing Company Model 737-400 series airplanes. This AD was prompted by reports of cracks in the upper chord of the overwing stub beams at body station (STA) 578 emanating from the rivet location common to the crease beam inner chord and the overwing stub beam upper chord.
