BREAKING: Federal Register: Removal of Class E Airspace and Modification of Class D and Class E Airspace; Point Mugu NAS (Naval Base Ventura Co) Airport, CA

Published: 2022-08-23 | Source: Federal Register

This action removes the Class E airspace, designated as an extension to a Class D or Class E surface area, at Point Mugu Naval Air Station (NAS) Airport, Oxnard, CA. This action also modifies the Class E airspace extending upward from 700 feet above the surface.
