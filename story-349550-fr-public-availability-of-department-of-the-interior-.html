BREAKING: Federal Register: Public Availability of Department of the Interior FY 2012 Service Contract Inventory and FY 2011 Service Contract Inventory Report

Published: 2013-03-15 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of the Interior is publishing this notice to advise the public of the availability of the fiscal year (FY) 2012 Service Contract Inventory and the FY 2011 Service Contract Inventory Report, in accordance with Section 743 of Division C of the Consolidated Appropriations Act of 2010 (Pub. L. 111-117). The inventory provides information on service contract actions over $25,000 that the Department made in FY 2012.
