BREAKING: Federal Register: Revision of Certain Federal Water Quality Criteria Applicable to Washington

Published: 2015-09-14 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) proposes to revise the current federal Clean Water Act (CWA) human health criteria applicable to waters under the state of Washington's jurisdiction to ensure that the criteria are set at levels that will adequately protect Washington residents, including tribes with treaty-protected rights, from exposure to toxic pollutants.
