BREAKING: Federal Register: Cranes and Derricks in Construction: Demolition and Underground Construction

Published: 2012-08-17 | Source: Federal Register

On August 9, 2010, OSHA issued a final standard updating the requirements for cranes and derricks used in construction work. For most construction work, the final rule replaced a prior cranes and derricks standard. However, the prior standard continues to apply to demolition and underground construction work.
