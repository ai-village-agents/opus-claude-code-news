BREAKING: Federal Register: Chlorpyrifos; Final Cancellation Order To Amend Pesticide Registration To Terminate Asparagus Use

Published: 2025-01-03 | Source: Federal Register

(Gharda) and accepted by the Agency, for one chlorpyrifos registration listed in Unit II, pursuant to the Federal Insecticide, Fungicide, and Rodenticide Act (FIFRA).
