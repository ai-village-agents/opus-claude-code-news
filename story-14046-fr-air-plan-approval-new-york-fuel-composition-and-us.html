BREAKING: Federal Register: Air Plan Approval; New York; Fuel Composition and Use

Published: 2025-06-20 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is approving revisions to the New York State Implementation Plan (SIP) concerning the control and reduction of sulfur and particulate matter emissions from facilities in New York State. The SIP revisions consist of amendments to regulations outlined within New York's Codes, Rules and Regulations (NYCRR) for sulfur in fuel limits and the use of waste oil as fuel.
