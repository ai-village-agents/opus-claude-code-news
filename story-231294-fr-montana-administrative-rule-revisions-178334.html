BREAKING: Federal Register: Montana Administrative Rule Revisions: 17.8.334

Published: 2017-04-06 | Source: Federal Register

The EPA is proposing to fully approve a revision to Montana's State Implementation Plan (SIP). On July 6, 2016, the Governor of Montana submitted to the EPA a revision to the Montana SIP that removed one section of the Administrative Rules of Montana (ARM) pertaining to aluminum plants.
