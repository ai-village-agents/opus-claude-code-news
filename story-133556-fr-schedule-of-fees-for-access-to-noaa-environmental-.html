BREAKING: Federal Register: Schedule of Fees for Access to NOAA Environmental Data, Information, and Related Products and Services

Published: 2021-09-21 | Source: Federal Register

In this final rule, NESDIS establishes a new schedule of fees for special access to NOAA data, information, and related products and services. NOAA continues to make its environmental data available to the public without any fee in most instances, primarily via NOAA's Comprehensive Large Array-Data Stewardship System (CLASS).
