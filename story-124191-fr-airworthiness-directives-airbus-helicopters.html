BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Airbus Helicopters

Published: 2021-05-20 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA is adopting a new airworthiness directive (AD) for all Airbus Helicopters Model SA330J helicopters. This AD was prompted by the failure of a second stage planet gear installed in the main gearbox (MGB).
