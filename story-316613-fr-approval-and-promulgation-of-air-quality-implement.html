BREAKING: Federal Register: Approval and Promulgation of Air Quality Implementation Plans; West Virginia; Minor New Source Review

Published: 2014-02-14 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is proposing to approve a State Implementation Plan (SIP) revision submitted on July 20, 2009 by the State of West Virginia. The proposed revision will expedite the processing of certain preconstruction permits issued under West Virginia's minor New Source Review (NSR) Program.
