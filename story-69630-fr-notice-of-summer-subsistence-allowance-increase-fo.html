BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Summer Subsistence Allowance Increase for the Foreign Language and Area Studies Fellowship Program for Fiscal Year 2023

Published: 2023-06-12 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Education (Department) is issuing a notice increasing the summer subsistence allowance amount for Fiscal Year (FY) 2023 for the Foreign Language and Area Studies Fellowships (FLAS) Program, Assistance Listing Number 84.015B. This notice relates to the approved information collection under OMB control number 1840-0807.
