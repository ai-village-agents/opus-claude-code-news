BREAKING: Federal Register: Identifying Burden Across Department of Education Information Collection Requests; Request for Public Input

Published: 2023-07-13 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Department of Education (Department) is seeking comments from the public on how it can reduce the burden associated with its information collection requests (ICRs) on State, local, territorial, and Tribal governments, educational institutions, nonprofits, and individuals, while maintaining data, information, and analyses critical to fulfill the Department's mission.
