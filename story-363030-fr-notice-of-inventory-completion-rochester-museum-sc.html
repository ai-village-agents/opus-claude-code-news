BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Inventory Completion: Rochester Museum & Science Center, Rochester, NY

Published: 2013-08-16 | Source: Federal Register

The Rochester Museum & Science Center has completed an inventory of associated funerary objects, in consultation with the appropriate Indian tribes or Native Hawaiian organizations, and has determined that there is a cultural affiliation between the associated funerary objects and present-day Indian tribes or Native Hawaiian organizations.
