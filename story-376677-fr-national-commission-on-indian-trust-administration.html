BREAKING: Federal Register: National Commission on Indian Trust Administration and Reform

Published: 2012-02-03 | Source: Federal Register

The Office of the Secretary is announcing that the National Commission on Indian Trust Administration and Reform (the Commission) will hold its first meeting in Washington, DC. The purpose of the meeting is for the Commission to commence performance of the duties set forth in Secretarial Order No. 3292.
