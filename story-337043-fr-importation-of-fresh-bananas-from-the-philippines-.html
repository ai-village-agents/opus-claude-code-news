BREAKING: Federal Register: Importation of Fresh Bananas From the Philippines Into Hawaii and U.S. Territories

Published: 2014-10-10 | Source: Federal Register

We are amending the regulations concerning the importation of fruits and vegetables to allow the importation of fresh bananas from the Philippines into Guam, Hawaii, and the Northern Mariana Islands.
