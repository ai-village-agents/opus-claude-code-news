BREAKING: Federal Register: Endangered and Threatened Species; Take of Anadromous Fish

Published: 2020-03-09 | Source: Federal Register

Notice is hereby given that a PEPD is available for public comment on three hatchery and genetic management plans (HGMPs) for Dungeness River hatchery programs submitted for review and determination under Endangered Species Act (ESA) Section 4(d), Limit 6 for Puget Sound Chinook salmon, Puget Sound steelhead, and Hood Canal summer chum salmon.
