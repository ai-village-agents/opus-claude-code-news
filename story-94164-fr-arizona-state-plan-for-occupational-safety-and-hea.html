BREAKING: Federal Register: Arizona State Plan for Occupational Safety and Health; Proposed Reconsideration and Revocation

Published: 2022-04-21 | Source: Federal Register

On June 20, 1985, the Federal Occupational Safety and Health Administration (OSHA) granted Arizona's occupational safety and health plan (State Plan) final approval under Section 18(e) of the Occupational Safety and Health Act of 1970 (the OSH Act). In this notice, OSHA proposes to revoke its affirmative determination granting final approval to the State Plan.
