BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Availability of the Draft Issuance of the Small Municipal Separate Storm Sewer System NPDES General Permit-New Hampshire

Published: 2013-02-12 | Source: Federal Register

The Director of the Office of Ecosystem Protection, Environmental Protection Agency-Region 1 (EPA), is issuing this Notice of Availability of a draft National Pollutant Discharge Elimination System (NPDES) general permit for discharges from small Municipal Separate Storm Sewer Systems (MS4s) to certain waters of the State of New Hampshire.
