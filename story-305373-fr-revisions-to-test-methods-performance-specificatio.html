BREAKING: Federal Register: Revisions to Test Methods, Performance Specifications, and Testing Regulations for Air Emission Sources

Published: 2015-09-30 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is announcing a public hearing for the proposed rule titled, "Revisions to Test Methods, Performance Specifications, and Testing Regulations for Air Emission Sources," that was published in the Federal Register on September 8, 2015. The hearing will be held in Research Triangle Park, North Carolina. The EPA is proposing technical and editorial corrections and revisions to regulations related to source testing of emissions.
