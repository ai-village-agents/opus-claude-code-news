BREAKING: Federal Register: Federal Employees' Retirement System; Government Costs

Published: 2017-10-25 | Source: Federal Register

The Office of Personnel Management amends this rule to clarify the manner OPM uses for determining a supplemental liability under the Federal Employees' Retirement System (FERS), and to clarify the process by which the U.S. Postal Service (USPS) and the U.S. Department of the Treasury (Treasury) may request reconsideration of OPM's valuation of the supplemental liability.
