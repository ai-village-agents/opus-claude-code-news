BREAKING: Federal Register: Significant New Use Rule on Certain Chemical Substances

Published: 2014-02-04 | Source: Federal Register

EPA is finalizing a significant new use rule (SNUR) under the Toxic Substances Control Act (TSCA) for chemical substances identified generically as complex strontium aluminate, rare earth doped, which were the subject of several premanufacture notices (PMNs).
