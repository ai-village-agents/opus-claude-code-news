BREAKING: Federal Register: Application for Recertification of Cook Inlet Regional Citizens Advisory Council

Published: 2023-06-20 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard announces the availability of, and seeks comments on, the recertification of the Cook Inlet Regional Citizens Advisory Council (CIRCAC) for September 1, 2023 through August 31, 2024. Under the Oil Pollution Act of 1990 (OPA 90), the Coast Guard may certify on an annual basis the CIRCAC. This advisory group monitors the activities of terminal facilities and crude oil tankers under the Cook Inlet program established by the statute.
