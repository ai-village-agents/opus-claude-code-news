BREAKING: Federal Register: Fishery Management Plan of Puerto Rico; Triggerfish Management Measures

Published: 2025-06-25 | Source: Federal Register

NMFS issues this final rule to implement management measures described in Framework Action 3 under the Puerto Rico Fishery Management Plan (FMP) (Framework Action 3). Specifically, this final rule modifies the annual catch limits (ACLs) for the triggerfish stock complex in Federal waters off Puerto Rico.
