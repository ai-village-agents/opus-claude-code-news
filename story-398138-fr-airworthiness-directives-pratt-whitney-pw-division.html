BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Pratt & Whitney (P&W) Division Turbofan Engines

Published: 2012-10-03 | Source: Federal Register

We are adopting a new airworthiness directive (AD) for certain P&W PW4000 series turbofan engines. This AD was prompted by reports of 3rd and 4th stage vane fractures in the low-pressure turbine (LPT) of certain PW4000-94" and PW4000-100" turbofan engines.
