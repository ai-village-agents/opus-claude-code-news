BREAKING: Federal Register: Standards for the Growing, Harvesting, Packing, and Holding of Produce for Human Consumption Relating to Agricultural Water; Proposed Rule; Public Meetings; Request for Comments

Published: 2022-01-07 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA, the Agency, or we) is announcing two virtual public meetings entitled "Standards for the Growing, Harvesting, Packing, and Holding of Produce for Human Consumption Relating to Agricultural Water."
