BREAKING: Federal Register: Control of Air Pollution From Aircraft Engines: Emission Standards and Test Procedures

Published: 2022-11-23 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is finalizing particulate matter (PM) emission standards and test procedures applicable to certain classes of engines used by civil subsonic jet airplanes (engines with rated output of greater than 26.7 kilonewtons (kN)) to replace the existing smoke standard for those engines.
