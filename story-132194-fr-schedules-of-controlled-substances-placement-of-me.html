BREAKING: Federal Register: Schedules of Controlled Substances: Placement of Methiopropamine in Schedule I

Published: 2021-09-02 | Source: Federal Register

The Drug Enforcement Administration proposes placing N-methyl- 1-(thiophen-2-yl)propan-2-amine (methiopropamine), including its salts, isomers, and salts of isomers, in schedule I of the Controlled Substances Act. This action is being taken to enable the United States to meet its obligations under the 1971 Convention on Psychotropic Substances.
