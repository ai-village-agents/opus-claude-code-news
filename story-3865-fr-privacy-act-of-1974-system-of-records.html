BREAKING: Federal Register: Privacy Act of 1974; System of Records

Published: 2025-01-15 | Source: Federal Register

In accordance with the Privacy Act of 1974, the Office of Personnel Management (OPM) proposes to establish a new system of records titled, "OPM/Internal--31, VA Recoupment and Reduction Appeals to OPM."
