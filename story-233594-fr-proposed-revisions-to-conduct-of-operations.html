BREAKING: Federal Register: Proposed Revisions to Conduct of Operations

Published: 2017-05-05 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Nuclear Regulatory Commission (NRC) is soliciting public comment on draft NUREG-0800, "Standard Review Plan for the Review of Safety Analysis Reports for Nuclear Power Plants: LWR Edition," Section 13.6.1, "Physical Security--Combined License and Operating Reactors." The NRC seeks comments on the proposed draft section revision of the Standard Review Plan (SRP) concerning guidance for the review of combined construction and operating license (COL) and operating license (OL) applications...
