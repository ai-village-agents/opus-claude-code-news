BREAKING: Federal Register: Proposed Amendment of Class E Airspace; Dumas, AR

Published: 2020-11-19 | Source: Federal Register

This action proposes to amend the Class E airspace extending upward from 700 feet above the surface at Billy Free Municipal Airport, Dumas, AR. The FAA is proposing this action as the result of airspace reviews caused by the decommissioning of the Monticello VHF omnidirectional range (VOR) navigation aid as part of the VOR Minimum Operational Network (MON) Program.
