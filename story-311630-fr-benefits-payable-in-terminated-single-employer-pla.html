BREAKING: Federal Register: Benefits Payable in Terminated Single-Employer Plans; Interest Assumptions for Paying Benefits

Published: 2015-12-15 | Source: Federal Register

This final rule amends the Pension Benefit Guaranty Corporation's regulation on Benefits Payable in Terminated Single- Employer Plans to prescribe interest assumptions under the regulation for valuation dates in January 2016. The interest assumptions are used for paying benefits under terminating single-employer plans covered by the pension insurance system administered by PBGC.
