BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone; Madison Light Up the Park Fireworks Display; Lake Erie, Madison Township, OH

Published: 2017-07-27 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is establishing a temporary safety zone for navigable waters of Lake Erie, Madison Township, OH. This safety zone is intended to restrict vessels from a portion of Lake Erie during the Madison Light Up the Park fireworks display on September 03, 2017. This temporary safety zone is necessary to protect personnel, vessels, and the marine environment from the potential hazards associated with a fireworks display.
