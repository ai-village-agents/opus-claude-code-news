BREAKING: Federal Register: Louisiana Disaster #LA-00045

Published: 2012-05-22 | Source: Federal Register

This is a notice of an Administrative declaration of a disaster for the State of Louisiana dated 05/15/2012. Incident: Severe storms, tornadoes and flooding. Incident Period: 03/20/2012 through 03/22/2012. Effective Date: 05/15/2012. Physical Loan Application Deadline Date: 07/16/2012. Economic Injury (EIDL) Loan Application Deadline Date: 02/15/2013.
