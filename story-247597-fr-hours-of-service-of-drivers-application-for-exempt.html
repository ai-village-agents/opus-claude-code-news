BREAKING: Federal Register: Hours of Service of Drivers: Application for Exemption; Hub Group Trucking Inc.

Published: 2017-10-31 | Source: Federal Register

FMCSA announces that the Hub Group Trucking Inc. (HGT) has requested an exemption from the electronic logging device (ELD) requirements to permit an alternative grandfather period for any commercial motor vehicles added to HGT's fleet after the December 18, 2017, compliance date. HGT reports that all of its 2,700 trucks are equipped with automatic on-board recording devices (AOBRDs) and it expects to add at least 160 trucks to its fleet in 2018.
