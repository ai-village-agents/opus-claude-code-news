BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Intent of Waiver With Respect to Land; John Glenn Columbus International Airport, Columbus, OH

Published: 2024-05-02 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA is considering a proposal to change approximately 2.392 acres of airport land from aeronautical use to non-aeronautical use and to authorize the sale of airport property located at John Glenn Columbus International Airport, Columbus, OH. The property is located in the northwest portion of the airport along the north side of Johnstown Road, west of the Runway Protection Zone for Runway 10L.
