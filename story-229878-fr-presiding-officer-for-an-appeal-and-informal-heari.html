BREAKING: Federal Register: Presiding Officer for an Appeal and Informal Hearing; Technical Amendments

Published: 2017-03-17 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA, the Agency, or we) is making revisions to Chapter I of its regulations. These revisions are necessary to reflect changes to the Agency's organizational structure, including the dissolution of the Regional Food and Drug Director position.
