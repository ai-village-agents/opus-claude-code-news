BREAKING: Federal Register: Advisory Committee on Evidence Rules; Meeting of the Judicial Conference

Published: 2021-04-14 | Source: Federal Register

The Advisory Committee on Evidence Rules will hold a virtual meeting on April 30, 2021 starting at 9:30 a.m. (Eastern) rather than 10:00 a.m. The meeting is open to the public. When a meeting is held virtually, members of the public may join by telephone or videoconference to observe but not participate.
