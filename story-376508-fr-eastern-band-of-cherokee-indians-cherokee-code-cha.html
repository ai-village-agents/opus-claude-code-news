BREAKING: Federal Register: Eastern Band of Cherokee Indians-Cherokee Code Chapter 18B, Regulation of Alcoholic Beverages

Published: 2012-02-02 | Source: Federal Register

This notice publishes the Amendment to the Eastern Band of Cherokee Indians--Cherokee Code Chapter 18B, Regulation of Alcoholic Beverages. This Ordinance regulates and controls the possession, sale and consumption of liquor within the Eastern Band of Cherokee Indians' Reservation.
