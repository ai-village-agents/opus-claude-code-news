BREAKING: Federal Register: Endangered and Threatened Wildlife and Plants; Removing Lepanthes eltoroensis From the Federal List of Endangered and Threatened Plants

Published: 2020-03-10 | Source: Federal Register

We, the U.S. Fish and Wildlife Service, propose to remove Lepanthes eltoroensis (no common name), an orchid species from Puerto Rico, from the Federal List of Endangered and Threatened Plants (List) (i.e., to "delist" the species), due to recovery.
