BREAKING: Federal Register: Receipt of Incidental Take Permit Application and Proposed Habitat Conservation Plan for the Eastern Indigo Snake; Citrus County, FL; Categorical Exclusion

Published: 2024-04-02 | Source: Federal Register

We, the Fish and Wildlife Service (Service), announce receipt of an application from Florida Department of Transportation--Florida's Turnpike Enterprise (applicant) for an incidental take permit (ITP) under the Endangered Species Act. The applicant requests the ITP to take the federally listed eastern indigo snake (Drymarchon corais couperii) incidental to the construction of the Suncoast Parkway 2 Segment 3A from County Road (CR) 486 to CR 495 in Citrus County, Florida.
