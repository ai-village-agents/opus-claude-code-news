BREAKING: Federal Register: Proposed Establishment of the Crest of the Blue Ridge Henderson County Viticultural Area

Published: 2018-12-06 | Source: Federal Register

The Alcohol and Tobacco Tax and Trade Bureau (TTB) proposes to establish the approximately 215-square mile "Crest of the Blue Ridge Henderson County" viticultural area in Henderson County, North Carolina. The proposed viticultural area is not located within, nor does it contain, any other established viticultural area.
