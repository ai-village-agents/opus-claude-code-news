BREAKING: Federal Register: Government Securities: Call for Large Position Reports

Published: 2022-07-21 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Department of the Treasury (Treasury) called for the submission of Large Position Reports by entities whose positions in the 1\1/2\% Treasury Note of February 2024 equaled or exceeded $5.2 billion as of Thursday, March 24, 2022, or Friday, April 1, 2022.
