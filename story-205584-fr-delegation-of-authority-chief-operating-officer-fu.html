BREAKING: Federal Register: Delegation of Authority; Chief Operating Officer Functions Delegated to Chief of Staff

Published: 2018-04-27 | Source: Federal Register

This is notice that the Administrator of the Small Business Administration (SBA) has delegated to the Chief of Staff of SBA the functions of the SBA Chief Operating Officer.
