BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Memorandum of Agreement

Published: 2019-02-11 | Source: Federal Register

The National Capital Planning Commission (NCPC) and the Smithsonian Institution (Smithsonian) have entered into a Memorandum of Agreement (MOA) effective December 20, 2018. The MOA details the process the two agencies will follow to comply with the National Environmental Policy Act (NEPA) 42 U.S.C. 4371).
