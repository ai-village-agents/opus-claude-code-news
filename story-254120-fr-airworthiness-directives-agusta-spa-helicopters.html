BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Agusta S.p.A. Helicopters

Published: 2016-02-01 | Source: Federal Register

We are publishing a new airworthiness directive (AD) for Agusta S.p.A. (Agusta) Model A109A and A109AII helicopters, which was sent previously to all known U.S. owners and operators of these helicopters. This AD requires checking and inspecting each main rotor blade (blade) for a crack and replacing any cracked blade before further flight.
