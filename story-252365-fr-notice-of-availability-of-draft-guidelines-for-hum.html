BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Availability of Draft Guidelines for Human Exposure Assessment

Published: 2016-01-07 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is announcing a 45- day public comment period for the External Review Draft of the "Guidelines for Human Exposure Assessment." EPA is seeking public comment prior to external peer review. The document will undergo review during an expert peer review meeting, which will be convened, organized, and conducted by an independent contractor.
