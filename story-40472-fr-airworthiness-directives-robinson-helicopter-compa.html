BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Robinson Helicopter Company Helicopters

Published: 2024-06-27 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA proposes to adopt a new airworthiness directive (AD) for certain Robinson Helicopter Company Model R44 and R44 II helicopters. This proposed AD was prompted by several reports of failed clutch actuators and failed rivets attaching the belt tension clutch actuator brackets (bracket) to the fan scroll housing.
