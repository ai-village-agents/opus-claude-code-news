BREAKING: Federal Register: Yuhaaviatam of San Manuel Nation; Liquor Act Amendment

Published: 2025-08-25 | Source: Federal Register

This notice publishes an amendment to the Liquor Act of the Yuhaaviatam of San Manuel Nation. The Yuhaaviatam of San Manuel Nation Liquor Act, as amended, regulates and controls the possession, sale, manufacture, and distribution of alcohol in conformity with the laws of the State of California.
