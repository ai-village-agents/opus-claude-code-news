BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Bombardier, Inc. Airplanes

Published: 2013-06-04 | Source: Federal Register

We are adopting a new airworthiness directive (AD) for certain Bombardier, Inc. Model BD-100-1A10 (Challenger 300) airplanes. This AD was prompted by reports of failure of a screw cap or end cap of the hydraulic system accumulator while on the ground, which resulted in loss of use of that hydraulic system and high-energy impact damage to adjacent systems and structures.
