BREAKING: Federal Register: Guidelines for Home Energy Professionals: Standard Work Specifications for Single Family Energy Upgrades

Published: 2012-04-18 | Source: Federal Register

A notice of availability and request for comments on a revised version of The Guidelines for Home Energy Professionals: Standard Work Specifications for Single Family Energy Upgrades was published in the Federal Register on March 29, 2012. 77 FR 19008. A number of commenters indicated that because of the extent of changes to the revised version from the version previously made publically available, additional time should be provided for review and comment.
