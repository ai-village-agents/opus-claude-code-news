BREAKING: Federal Register: U.S. Department of Energy Idaho Operations Office; Three Mile Island Unit 2; Independent Spent Fuel Storage Installation

Published: 2019-09-25 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Nuclear Regulatory Commission (NRC) has issued a renewed license to the U.S. Department of Energy Idaho Operations Office for Special Nuclear Materials (SNM) License No. SNM-2508 for the receipt, possession, transfer, and storage of radioactive material from the Three Mile Island Unit 2 (TMI-2) reactor core in the TMI-2 independent spent fuel storage installation (ISFSI).
