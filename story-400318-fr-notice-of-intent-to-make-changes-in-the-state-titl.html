BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Intent To Make Changes in the State Title V Maternal and Child Health Block Grant Allocations

Published: 2012-10-30 | Source: Federal Register

The Health Resources and Services Administration's (HRSA) Maternal and Child Health Bureau (MCHB) plans to move forward in implementing annual changes to the State Title V MCH Block Grant allocations, beginning in Federal Fiscal Year (FY) 2013, using the U.S. Census Bureau's 3-year American Community Survey (ACS) poverty estimates.
