BREAKING: Federal Register: Amendment of Class E Airspace and Revocation of Class E Airspace; Multiple Minnesota Towns

Published: 2021-02-23 | Source: Federal Register

This action amends the Class E airspace extending upward from 700 feet above the surface at multiple Minnesota Towns and revokes the Class E airspace extending upward from 700 feet above the surface at Silver Bay Municipal Airport, Silver Bay, MN. This action is the result of airspace reviews caused by the decommissioning of multiple non- federal non-directional beacons (NDBs) within Minnesota.
