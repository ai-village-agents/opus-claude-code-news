BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Inventory Completion: University of Washington, Department of Anthropology, Seattle, WA

Published: 2013-02-19 | Source: Federal Register

The University of Washington, Department of Anthropology, has completed an inventory of human remains and associated funerary objects, in consultation with the appropriate Indian tribes, and has determined that there is no cultural affiliation between the remains and associated funerary objects and any present-day Indian tribe.
