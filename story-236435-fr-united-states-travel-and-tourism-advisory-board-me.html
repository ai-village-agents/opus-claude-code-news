BREAKING: Federal Register: United States Travel and Tourism Advisory Board: Meeting of the United States Travel and Tourism Advisory Board

Published: 2017-06-12 | Source: Federal Register

The United States Travel and Tourism Advisory Board (Board or TTAB) will hold an open meeting via teleconference on Wednesday, June 28, 2017. The Board advises the Secretary of Commerce on matters relating to the U.S. travel and tourism industry. The purpose of the meeting is for Board members to deliberate on and potentially adopt a letter to the Secretary containing recommendations related to the importance of international travel and tourism to the United States.
