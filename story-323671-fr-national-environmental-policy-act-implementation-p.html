BREAKING: Federal Register: National Environmental Policy Act Implementation Procedures

Published: 2014-05-07 | Source: Federal Register

The Missile Defense Agency (MDA or the Agency) plans to issue procedures to implement the National Environmental Policy Act (NEPA), Executive Order (E.O.) 11514, and Council on Environmental Quality (CEQ) regulations on implementing NEPA. Pursuant to CEQ regulations, the MDA is soliciting comments on its proposed procedures.
