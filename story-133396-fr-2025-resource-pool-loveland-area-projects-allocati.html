BREAKING: Federal Register: 2025 Resource Pool-Loveland Area Projects, Allocation Procedures and Call for Applications

Published: 2021-09-20 | Source: Federal Register

WAPA's Rocky Mountain Region (RMR) published its Loveland Area Projects (LAP)--2025 Power Marketing Initiative (2025 PMI) in the Federal Register on December 30, 2013, to be effective January 29, 2014.
