The Centers for Disease Control and Prevention (CDC) in the Department of Health and Human Services (HHS) announces the opening of a docket to obtain comment on the FY 2024 annual Vessel Sanitation Program (VSP) public meeting. The August 5, 2024, annual meeting is a forum for CDC to update cruise industry representatives and other interested persons on programmatic activities and future plans.
