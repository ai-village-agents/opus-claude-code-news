BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone; Barge Launches; Gulfport Lake; Gulfport, MS

Published: 2013-12-24 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is establishing a safety zone for Gulfport Lake, Gulfport, MS. This action is necessary for the protection of persons and vessels on navigable waters during the launching of barges in Gulfport Lake, Gulfport, MS, particularly small craft in the area that risk being swamped.
