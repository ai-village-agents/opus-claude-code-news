BREAKING: Federal Register: Clarification of Bales Made Available for Shipment by CCC-Approved Warehouses

Published: 2014-12-01 | Source: Federal Register

This rule amends the regulations that specify the requirements for the Commodity Credit Corporation (CCC)-approved warehouses storing cotton, which are administered by the Farm Service Agency (FSA). FSA is changing the definition of Bales Made Available for Shipment (BMAS). CCC-approved cotton warehouses are currently required to report BMAS, among other data, to FSA every week.
