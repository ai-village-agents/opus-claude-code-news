BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone; NW Natural PGM Site, Willamette River, Portland, OR

Published: 2019-05-17 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is proposing to establish a temporary safety zone for certain waters of the Willamette River. This action is necessary to provide for the safety of life on these navigable waters while Oregon Department of Environmental Quality oversees the construction of an engineered sediment cap and other remedial environmental actions.
