BREAKING: Federal Register: Commodity Pool Operator Financial Reports

Published: 2016-11-25 | Source: Federal Register

The Commodity Futures Trading Commission (Commission or CFTC) is amending certain of its regulations applicable to the financial reports that each person registered or required to be registered as a commodity pool operator (CPO) must provide for each commodity pool that it operates.
