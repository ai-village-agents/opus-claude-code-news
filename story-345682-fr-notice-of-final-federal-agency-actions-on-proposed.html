BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Final Federal Agency Actions on Proposed Interchange Project in Massachusetts

Published: 2013-01-31 | Source: Federal Register

This notice announces actions taken by the FHWA and the USACE that are final within the meaning of 23 U.S.C. 139(l)(1). The actions relate to the proposed Route 79/I-195 Interchange Project in Fall River--Bristol County, Massachusetts. The actions grant permits and approvals for the project.
