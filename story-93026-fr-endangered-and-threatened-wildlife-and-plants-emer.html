BREAKING: Federal Register: Endangered and Threatened Wildlife and Plants; Emergency Listing of the Dixie Valley Toad as Endangered

Published: 2022-04-07 | Source: Federal Register

We, the U.S. Fish and Wildlife Service (Service), exercise our authority pursuant to the Endangered Species Act of 1973, as amended (Act), to emergency list the Dixie Valley toad (Anaxyrus williamsi) as endangered. Due to the imminent development of a geothermal project in Dixie Meadows, Nevada, and the potential resulting effects to the geothermal springs relied upon by the Dixie Valley toad, there is a significant risk to the well-being of the species.
