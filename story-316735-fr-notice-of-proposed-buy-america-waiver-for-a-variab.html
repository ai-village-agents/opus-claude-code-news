BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Proposed Buy America Waiver for a Variable Refrigerant Flow HVAC System

Published: 2014-02-18 | Source: Federal Register

The Federal Transit Administration (FTA) received a request for a waiver to permit the purchase of a Variable Refrigerant Flow (VRF) HVAC system that is non-compliant with Buy America requirements using FTA funding. The request is from the Rock Island County Metropolitan Mass Transit District (MetroLINK) for its Rock Island Transfer Station.
