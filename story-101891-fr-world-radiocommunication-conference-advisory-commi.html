BREAKING: Federal Register: World Radiocommunication Conference Advisory Committee Schedules Its Sixth Meeting on September 12, 2022

Published: 2022-08-02 | Source: Federal Register

In accordance with the Federal Advisory Committee Act, this notice advises interested persons that the sixth meeting of the World Radiocommunication Conference Advisory Committee (WAC) will be held on Monday, September 12, 2022 at 11:00 EDT. Due to exceptional circumstances, the sixth WAC meeting will be convened as a virtual meeting with remote participation only. The meeting is open to the public.
