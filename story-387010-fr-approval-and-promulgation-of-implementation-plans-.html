BREAKING: Federal Register: Approval and Promulgation of Implementation Plans; State of Hawaii; Regional Haze Federal Implementation Plan

Published: 2012-05-29 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is proposing to promulgate a Federal Implementation Plan (FIP) to address regional haze in the State of Hawaii. EPA proposes to determine that the FIP meets the requirements of the Clean Air Act (CAA or "the Act") and EPA's rules concerning reasonable progress towards the national goal of preventing any future and remedying any existing man-made impairment of visibility in mandatory Class I areas.
