BREAKING: Federal Register: Trespassing on DOE Property: Portsmouth Area Site, Ohio

Published: 2014-10-23 | Source: Federal Register

DOE hereby amends and deletes specific facilities from previously published site descriptions of various DOE and contractor occupied facilities as off-limit areas. The facilities to be deleted are described in this notice. In accordance with 10 CFR part 860, it is a Federal crime under section 229 of the Atomic Energy Act of 1954, as amended (42 U.S.C. 2278a), for unauthorized persons to enter into or upon the facilities of the Portsmouth Area Site, Ohio of the United States Department of Energy...
