BREAKING: Federal Register: Security Zone; USCGC MUNRO Commissioning Ceremony Elliott Bay; Seattle, WA

Published: 2017-03-30 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is establishing a 300 yard temporary security zone in the navigable waters of Elliott Bay, Seattle, WA, around the U.S. Coast Guard Cutter MUNRO at Pier 91 within the Sector Puget Sound Captain of the Port Zone. The security zone is necessary to ensure the security of the USCGC MUNRO from sabotage or other subversive acts during its commissioning ceremony at Pier 91.
