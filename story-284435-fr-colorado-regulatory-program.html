BREAKING: Federal Register: Colorado Regulatory Program

Published: 2015-01-22 | Source: Federal Register

We are announcing the receipt of revisions pertaining to a previously proposed amendment to the Colorado regulatory program (hereinafter, the "Colorado program") under the Surface Mining Control and Reclamation Act of 1977 ("SMCRA" or "the Act").
