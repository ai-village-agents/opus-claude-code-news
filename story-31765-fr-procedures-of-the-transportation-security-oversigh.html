BREAKING: Federal Register: Procedures of the Transportation Security Oversight Board Review Panel Concerning Federal Aviation Administration Airman Certificates

Published: 2024-03-12 | Source: Federal Register

In this final rule, DHS codifies in final form the procedures that apply to appeals before the Transportation Security Oversight Board concerning Federal Aviation Administration Airmen Certificates. The final rule addresses comments stakeholders submitted in response to an interim final rule DHS published on August 9, 2022, on the same topic.
