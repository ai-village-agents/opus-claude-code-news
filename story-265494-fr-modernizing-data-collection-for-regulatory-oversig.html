BREAKING: Federal Register: Modernizing Data Collection for Regulatory Oversight of Credit Unions

Published: 2016-06-07 | Source: Federal Register

The National Credit Union Administration (NCUA) is conducting a comprehensive review of two vehicles used to collect information for regulatory oversight of federally insured credit unions (FICUs)--the 5300 Call Report (Call Report) and Form 4501A Profile (Profile).
