BREAKING: Federal Register: Approval and Promulgation of Air Quality Implementation Plans; Minnesota; Regional Haze

Published: 2012-01-25 | Source: Federal Register

EPA is proposing to approve the Minnesota State Implementation Plan addressing regional haze for the first implementation period. Minnesota submitted its regional haze plan on December 30, 2009. A supplemental submission was made on January 5, 2012. The Minnesota regional haze plan addresses Clean Air Act (CAA) and Regional Haze Rule (RHR) requirements to remedy any existing and prevent future anthropogenic visibility impairment at mandatory Class I areas.
