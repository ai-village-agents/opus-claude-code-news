BREAKING: Federal Register: Office of Economic Adjustment; Announcement of Federal Funding Opportunity (FFO)

Published: 2015-03-09 | Source: Federal Register

This notice announces an opportunity to request funding from the Office of Economic Adjustment (OEA), a Department of Defense (DoD) field activity, for community planning assistance and economic diversification in response to major reductions or cancellations in DoD spending, including the cancellation or termination of DoD contracts or failure to proceed with a major weapon system program.
