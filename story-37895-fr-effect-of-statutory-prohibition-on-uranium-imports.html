BREAKING: Federal Register: Effect of Statutory Prohibition on Uranium Imports From the Russian Federation

Published: 2024-05-24 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Nuclear Regulatory Commission (NRC) is issuing a notice explaining how recently enacted legislation that prohibits imports of certain uranium products (natural uranium and low-enriched uranium) from the Russian Federation affects imports under NRC licenses.
