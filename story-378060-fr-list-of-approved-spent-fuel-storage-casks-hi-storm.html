BREAKING: Federal Register: List of Approved Spent Fuel Storage Casks: HI-STORM 100, Revision 8

Published: 2012-02-17 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Nuclear Regulatory Commission (NRC or the Commission) is proposing to amend its spent fuel storage cask regulations by revising the Holtec International HI-STORM 100 dry cask storage system listing within the "List of Approved Spent Fuel Storage Casks" to include Amendment No. 8 to Certificate of Compliance (CoC) No. 1014.
