BREAKING: Federal Register: Ferrovanadium From the Republic of Korea: Final Results of Antidumping Duty Administrative Review and Final Determination of No Shipments in Part; 2018-2019

Published: 2020-07-10 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Commerce (Commerce) continues to find that two of the three companies under review had no shipments of subject merchandise during the period of review (POR), May 1, 2018 through April 30, 2019, and continues to base the dumping margin for the third company on total adverse facts available (AFA).
