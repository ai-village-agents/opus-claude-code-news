BREAKING: Federal Register: Privacy Act of 1974; System of Records

Published: 2019-02-01 | Source: Federal Register

The Defense Information Systems Agency (DISA) proposes to establish a new system of records entitled "Electronic Security System (ESS), K890.28" to control physical access to DISA Headquarters. DISA, as a classified collateral open storage area, has security responsibilities mandated by DoD Manual 5200.01, volume 3, DoD Information Security Program: Protection of Classified Information.
