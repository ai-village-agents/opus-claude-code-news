BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Bell Helicopter Textron

Published: 2016-05-05 | Source: Federal Register

We propose to supersede airworthiness directive (AD) 75-26-05 for Bell Helicopter Textron (Bell) Model 204B, 205A-1 and 212 helicopters. AD 75-26-05 currently requires removing and visually inspecting each main rotor (M/R) blade and, depending on the inspection's outcome, repairing or replacing the M/R blades.
