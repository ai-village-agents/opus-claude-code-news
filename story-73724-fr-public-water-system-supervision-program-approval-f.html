BREAKING: Federal Register: Public Water System Supervision Program Approval for the State of Ohio

Published: 2023-08-04 | Source: Federal Register

Notice is hereby given that the Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) has approved the State of Ohio's revisions to the State's Public Water System Supervision (PWSS) Program under the federal Safe Drinking Water Act (SDWA) for adoption of the federal Ground Water Rule.
