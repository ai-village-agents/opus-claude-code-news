BREAKING: Federal Register: Certification Program for Access to the Death Master File

Published: 2014-12-30 | Source: Federal Register

The National Technical Information Service (NTIS) issues a proposed rule that would, if implemented, establish a program pursuant to Section 203 of the Bipartisan Budget Act of 2013 (Act) through which persons may become "certified" and thereby be eligible to obtain access to Death Master File (DMF) information about an individual within three years of that individual's death ("Limited Access DMF," as defined in the proposed rule).
