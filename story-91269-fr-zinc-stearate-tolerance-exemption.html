BREAKING: Federal Register: Zinc Stearate; Tolerance Exemption

Published: 2022-03-17 | Source: Federal Register

This regulation establishes an exemption from the requirement of a tolerance for residues of zinc stearate (CAS No. 557-05-1) when used as an inert ingredient (lubricant) in pesticide formulations at rates of no more than 6 percent by weight of the formulation in pre- and post-harvest applications to crops.
