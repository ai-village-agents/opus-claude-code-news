BREAKING: Federal Register: Health Workforce Research Center Cooperative Agreement Program

Published: 2013-09-11 | Source: Federal Register

The Bureau of Health Professions (BHPr) is announcing a change to its Health Workforce Research Center cooperative agreement program. Funding Opportunity Announcement (FOA) HRSA-13-185, issued on November 26, 2012, announced HRSA's intent to fund Health Workforce Research Centers (HWRCs) focusing on research and technical assistance (TA).
