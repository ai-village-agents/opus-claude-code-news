BREAKING: Federal Register: Reporting Requirements Regarding Findings of Harassment, Sexual Harassment, Other Forms of Harassment, or Sexual Assault

Published: 2019-07-17 | Source: Federal Register

The National Aeronautics and Space Administration (NASA) is correcting a document that appeared in the Federal Register on July 10, 2019, concerning request for public comment on the Agency's proposed implementation of new reporting requirements regarding harassment among recipients of NASA financial assistance. The document that published was an incorrect version. This document is the correct version.
