BREAKING: Federal Register: Accessibility Guidelines for Pedestrian Facilities in the Public Right-of-Way

Published: 2023-08-08 | Source: Federal Register

The Architectural and Transportation Barriers Compliance Board (Access Board or Board) issues its final rule that provides minimum guidelines for the accessibility of pedestrian facilities in the public right-of-way. These guidelines, once adopted, would ensure that facilities used by pedestrians, such as sidewalks and crosswalks, constructed or altered in the public right-of-way by Federal, state, and local Governments are readily accessible to and usable by pedestrians with disabilities.
