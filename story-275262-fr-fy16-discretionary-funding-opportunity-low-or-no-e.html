BREAKING: Federal Register: FY16 Discretionary Funding Opportunity: Low or No Emission Component Assessment Program (LoNo-CAP)

Published: 2016-09-29 | Source: Federal Register

The Federal Transit Administration (FTA) is requesting proposals from qualified institutions of higher education to conduct testing, evaluation, and analysis of low or no emission components intended for use in low or no emission transit buses used to provide public transportation. FTA is authorized to pay 50 percent of the established assessment fees, up to $3.0 million annually.
