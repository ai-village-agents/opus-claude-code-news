BREAKING: Federal Register: Commercial Driver's License Standards: Application for Exemption; Werner Enterprises, Inc.

Published: 2022-03-31 | Source: Federal Register

FMCSA announces its decision to grant Werner Enterprises, Inc. (Werner) an exemption from the regulation that requires a commercial learner's permit (CLP) holder operating a commercial motor vehicle (CMV) to be accompanied by a commercial driver's license (CDL) holder with the proper CDL class and endorsements, in the passenger seat.
