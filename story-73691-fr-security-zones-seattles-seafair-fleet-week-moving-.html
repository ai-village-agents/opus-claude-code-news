BREAKING: Federal Register: Security Zones; Seattle's Seafair Fleet Week Moving Vessels, Puget Sound, WA

Published: 2023-08-04 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard will enforce Seattle's Seafair Fleet Week Moving Vessels security zones from August 1 through August 7, 2023 to ensure the security of the vessels from sabotage or other subversive acts during Seafair Fleet Week Parade of Ships. Our regulation for marine events within the Thirteenth Coast Guard District identifies the regulated area for this event in Seattle, WA.
