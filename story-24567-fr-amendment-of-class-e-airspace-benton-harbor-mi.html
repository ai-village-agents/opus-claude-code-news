BREAKING: Federal Register: Amendment of Class E Airspace; Benton Harbor, MI

Published: 2025-12-08 | Source: Federal Register

This action proposes to amend the Class E airspace at Benton Harbor, MI. The name and geographic coordinates of the Southwest Michigan Regional Airport, Benton Harbor, MI, would also be updated to coincide with the FAA's aeronautical database. The FAA is proposing this action as the result of an airspace review conducted due to the decommissioning of the Keeler very high frequency omnidirectional range (VOR) as part of the VOR Minimum Operational Network (MON) Program.
