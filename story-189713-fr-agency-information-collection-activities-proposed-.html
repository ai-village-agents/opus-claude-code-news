BREAKING: Federal Register: Agency Information Collection Activities; Proposed Collection; Comment Request

Published: 2019-09-27 | Source: Federal Register

The FTC plans to ask the Office of Management and Budget (OMB) to extend for an additional three years the current Paperwork Reduction Act (PRA) clearance for information collection requirements contained in the FTC's portion of the information collection requirements contained in the Consumer Financial Protection Bureau's Regulation N (the Mortgage Acts and Practices--Advertising Rule). The FTC generally shares enforcement of Regulation N with the Consumer Financial Protection Bureau ("CFPB"). ...
