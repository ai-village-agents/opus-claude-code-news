BREAKING: Federal Register: Proposed Establishment of the Wanapum Village Viticultural Area; Proposed Name Change to Beverly, Washington

Published: 2023-10-05 | Source: Federal Register

The Alcohol and Tobacco Tax and Trade Bureau (TTB) is soliciting comments on a proposal to change the name of the proposed "Wanapum Village" American viticultural area (AVA) to "Beverly, Washington." The proposed AVA area is located in Grant County, Washington and is entirely within the existing Columbia Valley AVA.
