BREAKING: Federal Register: Melamine From the People's Republic of China: Final Results of the Expedited Sunset Review of the Antidumping Duty Order

Published: 2021-03-09 | Source: Federal Register

As a result of this expedited sunset review, the Department of Commerce (Commerce) finds that revocation of the antidumping duty (AD) order on melamine from the People's Republic of China (China) would be likely to lead to continuation or recurrence of dumping at the levels indicated in the "Final Results of Review" section of this notice.
