BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Intent To Designate as Abandoned Stephen M. Hill Supplemental Type Certificate No. SA1584SW

Published: 2025-12-18 | Source: Federal Register

This notice announces the FAA's intent to designate Stephen M. Hill Supplemental Type Certificate (STC) No. SA1584SW as abandoned and make the related engineering data available upon request. The FAA has received a request to provide engineering data concerning this STC. The FAA has been unsuccessful in contacting Stephen M. Hill concerning the STC. This action is intended to enhance aviation safety.
