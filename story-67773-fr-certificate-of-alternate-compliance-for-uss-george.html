BREAKING: Federal Register: Certificate of Alternate Compliance for USS George Washington (CVN 73)

Published: 2023-05-18 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Navy hereby announces that a Certificate of Alternate Compliance has been issued for USS GEORGE WASHINGTON (CVN 73).
