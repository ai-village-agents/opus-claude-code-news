BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Airbus SAS Airplanes

Published: 2020-05-20 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA is superseding Airworthiness Directive (AD) 2020-03- 12, which applied to all Airbus SAS Model A350-941 and -1041 airplanes. AD 2020-03-12 required revising the existing airplane flight manual (AFM) to define a liquid-prohibited zone in the flight deck and provide procedures following liquid spillage on the center pedestal.
