BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Intent To Renew Information Collection

Published: 2024-06-18 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Department of Agriculture (USDA) seeks comments on the U.S. National Arboretum's (USNA) intent to renew an information collection that expires December 31, 2024. The information collection serves to collect fees for certain uses of the facilities, grounds, programs, and services. This includes fees for educational programs and workshops, and for use of the grounds and facilities for commercial photography and cinematography.
