BREAKING: Federal Register: Approval and Promulgation of Implementation Plans; Guam

Published: 2015-02-18 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is completing the process begun in 2005 to revise the format of the "identification of plan" section in 40 CFR part 52 for the Guam State Implementation Plan (SIP). Specifically, the EPA is adding the nonregulatory provisions and quasi-regulatory measures to the revised "identification of plan" section.
