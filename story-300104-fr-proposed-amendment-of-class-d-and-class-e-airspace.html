BREAKING: Federal Register: Proposed Amendment of Class D and Class E Airspace, Revocation of Class E Airspace; Mountain Home, ID

Published: 2015-07-28 | Source: Federal Register

This action proposes to modify Class D airspace, Class E surface area airspace, Class E airspace extending upward from 700 feet above the surface, and remove Class E surface area airspace designated as an extension at Mountain Home AFB, Mountain Home, ID.
