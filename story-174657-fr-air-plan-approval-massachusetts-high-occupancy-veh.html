BREAKING: Federal Register: Air Plan Approval; Massachusetts; High Occupancy Vehicle Lanes

Published: 2019-03-20 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is approving a State Implementation Plan (SIP) revision submitted by the Commonwealth of Massachusetts. This revision provides for the Massachusetts Department of Transportation (MassDOT) to construct and operate specified transit facilities and high occupancy vehicle (HOV) lanes established therein.
