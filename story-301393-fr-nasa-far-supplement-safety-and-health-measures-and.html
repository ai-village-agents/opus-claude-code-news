BREAKING: Federal Register: NASA FAR Supplement: Safety and Health Measures and Mishap Reporting

Published: 2015-08-12 | Source: Federal Register

NASA proposes to amend the NASA FAR Supplement (NFS) to revise a current clause related to safety and health measures and mishaps reporting by narrowing the application of the clause, resulting in a decrease in the reporting burden on contractors while reinforcing the measures contractors at NASA facilities must take to protect the safety of their workers, NASA employees, the public, and high value assets.
