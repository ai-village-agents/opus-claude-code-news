BREAKING: Federal Register: Draft Fiscal Years 2014-2018 Strategic Plan

Published: 2014-03-05 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Nuclear Regulatory Commission (NRC) is requesting comment on draft NUREG-1614, Volume 6, "U.S. Nuclear Regulatory Commission Strategic Plan, Fiscal Years 2014-2018." The draft Strategic Plan provides the agency's long-term, results-focused goals and objectives and its proposed strategies for achieving them for the planning period.
