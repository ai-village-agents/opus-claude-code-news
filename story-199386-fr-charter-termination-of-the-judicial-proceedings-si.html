BREAKING: Federal Register: Charter Termination of the Judicial Proceedings Since Fiscal Year 2012 Amendments Panel

Published: 2018-02-07 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Defense is publishing this notice to announce that it is terminating the Judicial Proceedings Since Fiscal Year 2012 Amendments Panel, effective January 26, 2018.
