BREAKING: Federal Register: Drawbridge Operation Regulation; Taunton River, Fall River and Somerset, MA

Published: 2013-05-24 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard proposes to modify the operating schedule that governs the Veterans Memorial Bridge across the Taunton River, mile 2.1, between Fall River and Somerset, Massachusetts. The bridge owner, Massachusetts Department of Transportation, submitted a request to reduce the hours the bridge is crewed based upon infrequent requests to open the draw.
