BREAKING: Federal Register: Request for Comments; CISS Expansion

Published: 2023-04-05 | Source: Federal Register

On November 15, 2021, Congress passed the Bipartisan Infrastructure Law (BIL). Under Sec. 24108(e) Congress authorizes the Secretary of Transportation to enhance the collection of crash data by upgrading the Crash Investigation Sampling System (CISS) to include-- (1) additional program sites; (2) an expanded scope that includes all crash types; and (3) on-scene investigation protocols.
