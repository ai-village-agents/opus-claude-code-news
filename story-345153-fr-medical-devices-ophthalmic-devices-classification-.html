BREAKING: Federal Register: Medical Devices; Ophthalmic Devices; Classification of the Scleral Plug

Published: 2013-01-25 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA or Agency) is proposing to classify the scleral plug into class II (special controls), and proposing to exempt the scleral plugs composed of surgical grade stainless steel (with or without coating in gold, silver, or titanium) from premarket notification (510(k)) and to continue to require premarket notification (510(k)) for all other scleral plugs in order to provide a reasonable assurance of safety and effectiveness of the device.
