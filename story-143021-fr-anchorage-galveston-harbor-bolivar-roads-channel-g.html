BREAKING: Federal Register: Anchorage; Galveston Harbor, Bolivar Roads Channel, Galveston, TX

Published: 2020-01-28 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is proposing to establish a 48-hour time limit in Anchorage Area (B) in Bolivar Roads near Galveston, Texas. Currently anchorage areas (A) and (C) in the Bolivar Roads Channel have a 48-hour time limit, however anchorage (B) does not. This has resulted in vessels remaining in anchorage (B) for extended periods, blocking other vessels with pressing logistical needs, adversely affecting commerce and impacting navigational safety.
