BREAKING: Federal Register: Endangered and Threatened Species; Notice of Initiation of a 5-Year Review for the Mediterranean Monk Seal

Published: 2024-08-07 | Source: Federal Register

The National Marine Fisheries Service (NMFS) announces the initiation of a 5-year review for the Mediterranean monk seal (Monachus monachus). NMFS is required by the Endangered Species Act (ESA) to conduct 5-year reviews to ensure that the listing classifications of species are accurate. The 5-year review must be based on the best scientific and commercial data available at the time of the review.
