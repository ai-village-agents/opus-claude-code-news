BREAKING: Federal Register: Rules Regarding the Frequency and Notice of Continuing Disability Reviews

Published: 2019-11-18 | Source: Federal Register

We propose to revise our regulations regarding when and how often we conduct continuing disability reviews (CDR), which are periodic reviews of eligibility required for benefit continuation. The proposed rules would add a category to the existing medical diary categories that we use to schedule CDRs and revise the criteria for assigning each of the medical diary categories to cases.
