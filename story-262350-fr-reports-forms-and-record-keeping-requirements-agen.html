BREAKING: Federal Register: Reports, Forms and Record Keeping Requirements, Agency Information Collection Activity Under OMB Review

Published: 2016-05-02 | Source: Federal Register

Before a federal agency may collect certain information from the public, the agency must receive approval from the Office of Management and Budget ("OMB"). Under procedures established by the Paperwork Reduction Act of 1995 (44 U.S.C. 3501 et seq.), before seeking OMB approval, federal agencies must solicit public comment on proposed collections of information, including extensions and reinstatements of previously approved collections.
