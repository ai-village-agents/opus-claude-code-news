BREAKING: Federal Register: Request for Nominations for the Advisory Committee on Training in Primary Care Medicine and Dentistry

Published: 2018-08-24 | Source: Federal Register

HRSA is requesting nominations to fill vacancies on the Advisory Committee on Training in Primary Care Medicine and Dentistry (ACTPCMD). The ACTPCMD is authorized by Section 749 of the Public Health Service (PHS) Act.
