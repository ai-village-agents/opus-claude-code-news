BREAKING: Federal Register: Agency Information Collection Activities: Proposed Collection; Cancellation

Published: 2014-12-22 | Source: Federal Register

On December 4, 2014, NARA published a notice (79 FR 72033) that the agency was proposing to reinstate the information collection National Historical Publications and Records Commission (NHPRC) Grant Program, Budget Form and Instructions (OMB number 3095-0013, agency form number NA Form 17001), which NHPRC uses in its grant program. NARA invited the public to comment on the proposed information collection pursuant to the Paperwork Reduction Act of 1995. However, NARA is now cancelling that notice...
