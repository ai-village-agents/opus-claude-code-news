BREAKING: Federal Register: Import Requirements for the Importation of Fresh Fragrant Pears From China Into the United States

Published: 2020-03-27 | Source: Federal Register

We are advising the public of our decision to revise the import requirements for the importation of fresh fragrant pears from China into the United States and to authorize importation from an additional area of production.
