BREAKING: Federal Register: National Center for Family/Professional Partnerships Cooperative Agreement

Published: 2015-09-03 | Source: Federal Register

HRSA announces its intent to award a program expansion supplement in the amount of $118,700 for the National Center for Family/Professional Partnerships (NCFPP) cooperative agreement. The purpose of the NCFPP cooperative agreement, as stated in the funding opportunity announcement, is to improve the health delivery system and quality of life for children (and youth) with special health care needs (CSHCN) and their families.
