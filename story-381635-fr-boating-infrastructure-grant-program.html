BREAKING: Federal Register: Boating Infrastructure Grant Program

Published: 2012-03-28 | Source: Federal Register

We, the U.S. Fish and Wildlife Service (Service), propose changes in the regulations governing the administration of the national Boating Infrastructure Grant Program (BIG). We are updating the regulations to reflect changes in policy and practice that have occurred since the program's inception in 1998.
