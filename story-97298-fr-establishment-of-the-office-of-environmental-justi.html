BREAKING: Federal Register: Establishment of the Office of Environmental Justice

Published: 2022-06-01 | Source: Federal Register

The Office of the Assistant Secretary for Health (OASH) has modified its structure. This notice announces the establishment of the Office of Environmental Justice in OASH's Office of Climate Change and Health Equity.
