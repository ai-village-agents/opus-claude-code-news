BREAKING: Federal Register: Finding of No Significant Impact for the Commercial Disposal of Defense Waste Processing Facility Recycle Wastewater From the Savannah River Site

Published: 2020-08-10 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Energy (DOE) has completed the Final Environmental Assessment for the Commercial Disposal of Defense Waste Processing Facility Recycle Wastewater from the Savannah River Site (Final EA).
