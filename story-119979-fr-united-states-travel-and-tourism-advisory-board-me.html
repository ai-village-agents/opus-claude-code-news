BREAKING: Federal Register: United States Travel and Tourism Advisory Board; Meeting of the United States Travel and Tourism Advisory Board

Published: 2021-03-29 | Source: Federal Register

The United States Travel and Tourism Advisory Board (Board or TTAB) will hold a meeting on Wednesday, March 31, 2021. The Board advises the Secretary of Commerce (Secretary) on matters relating to the U.S. travel and tourism industry.
