BREAKING: Federal Register: Air Plan Approval; Connecticut; Sulfur Content of Fuel Oil Burned in Stationary Sources

Published: 2016-05-25 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is approving a State Implementation Plan (SIP) revision submitted by the State of Connecticut on April 22, 2014, with supplemental submittals on June 18, 2015 and September 25, 2015. This revision establishes sulfur in fuel oil content limits for use in stationary sources.
