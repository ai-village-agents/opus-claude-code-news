BREAKING: Federal Register: Announcement of National Geospatial Advisory Committee Meeting

Published: 2015-11-12 | Source: Federal Register

The National Geospatial Advisory Committee (NGAC) will meet on December 4, 2015, from 12:30 p.m. to 3:30 p.m. EST. The meeting will be held via web conference and teleconference.
