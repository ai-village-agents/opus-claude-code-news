BREAKING: Federal Register: Special Local Regulation; Ohio River, Owensboro, KY

Published: 2020-07-07 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is proposing to establish a temporary special local regulation for all navigable waters of the Ohio River from mile marker (MM) 754 .0 to MM 759.0. This action is necessary to provide for the safety of life on these navigable waters near Owensboro, KY, during a high speed boat race on August 14, 2020 through August 16, 2020.
