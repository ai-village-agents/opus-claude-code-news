BREAKING: Federal Register: LPTV, TV Translator, and FM Broadcast Station Reimbursement

Published: 2019-03-26 | Source: Federal Register

In this document, the Commission adopts rules to implement Congress's directive in the 2018 Reimbursement Expansion Act (REA) that the Commission reimburse certain Low Power Television and television translator stations and FM broadcast stations, for costs incurred as a result of the Commission's broadcast television spectrum incentive auction.
