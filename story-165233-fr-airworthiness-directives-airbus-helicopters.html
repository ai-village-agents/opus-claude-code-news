BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Airbus Helicopters

Published: 2020-11-04 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA proposes to supersede Airworthiness Directive (AD) 2018-05-09, which applies to all Airbus Helicopters Model AS332C, AS332C1, AS332L, and AS332L1 helicopters. AD 2018-05-09 requires inspecting the tail rotor (T/R) flapping hinge link (hinge) and reporting the results.
