BREAKING: Federal Register: Amendment of Class D and Class E Airspace; Aspen, CO; and Pueblo, CO

Published: 2017-06-07 | Source: Federal Register

This action withdraws the final rule, technical amendment published in the Federal Register on April 25, 2017. In that action, the FAA amended Class E Airspace at Aspen Pitkin County/Sardy Field, Aspen, CO, and Pueblo Memorial Airport, Pueblo, CO.
