BREAKING: Federal Register: Filing Dates for the Louisiana Special Elections in the 5th Congressional District

Published: 2013-08-29 | Source: Federal Register

Louisiana has scheduled a Special General Election on October 19, 2013, to fill the U.S. House of Representatives seat vacated by Representative Rodney Alexander. Under Louisiana law, a majority winner in an open special election is declared elected. Should no candidate achieve a majority vote, a Special Runoff Election will be held on November 16, 2013, between the top two vote-getters.
