BREAKING: Federal Register: Certain Television Sets, Television Receivers, Television Tuners, and Components Thereof; Commission Determination Not To Review an Initial Determination Granting a Complainant's Motion for Leave To Amend the Complaint and Notice of Investigation

Published: 2014-06-12 | Source: Federal Register

Notice is hereby given that the U.S. International Trade Commission has determined not to review an initial determination ("ID") (Order No. 12) of the presiding administrative law judge ("ALJ") granting complainant's motion for leave to amend the complaint and notice of investigation.
