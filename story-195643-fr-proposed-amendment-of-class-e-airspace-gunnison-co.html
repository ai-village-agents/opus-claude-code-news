BREAKING: Federal Register: Proposed Amendment of Class E Airspace; Gunnison, CO

Published: 2019-12-16 | Source: Federal Register

This action proposes to amend the Class E airspace designated as a surface area, at Gunnison-Crested Butte Regional Airport, Gunnison, CO. The proposal would increase the circular radius of the Class E surface area and add an extension to the west of the airport. Also, this action proposes to amend the Class E airspace by adding an airspace area designated as an extension to a Class D or Class E surface area, to the southwest of the airport.
