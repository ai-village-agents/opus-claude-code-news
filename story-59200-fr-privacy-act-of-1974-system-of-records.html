BREAKING: Federal Register: Privacy Act of 1974; System of Records

Published: 2023-01-30 | Source: Federal Register

Pursuant to the provisions of the Privacy Act of 1974, notice is given that the Board of Governors of the Federal Reserve System (Board) proposes to rescind an existing system of records, entitled BGFRS-8, "FRB--Travel Records."
