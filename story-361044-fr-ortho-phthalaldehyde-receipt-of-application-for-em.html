BREAKING: Federal Register: Ortho-Phthalaldehyde; Receipt of Application for Emergency Exemption, Solicitation of Public Comment

Published: 2013-07-24 | Source: Federal Register

EPA has received a specific exemption request from the National Aeronautics and Space Administration (NASA) to use the pesticide ortho-phthalaldehyde (OPA) (CAS No. 643-79-8) to treat the International Space Station internal active thermal control system (IATCS) coolant including the United States (U.S.) Laboratory, the Japanese Experiment Module, the Columbus and Node with a total volume of 829 L with a maximum of 1,974 cm\3\ OPA resin/year to control aerobic/microaerophilic water bacteria.
