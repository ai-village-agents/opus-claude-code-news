BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Bell Helicopter Textron Canada Limited (Bell) Model Helicopters

Published: 2013-05-13 | Source: Federal Register

We propose to adopt a new airworthiness directive (AD) for Bell Model 206A, 206B, 206L, 206L-1, 206L-3, 206L-4, 222, 222B, 222U, 230, 407, 427, and 430 helicopters. This proposed AD would require inspecting each bearing to determine if it has been properly staked and replacing the bearing or assembly if it has not been staked properly.
