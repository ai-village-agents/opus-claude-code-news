BREAKING: Federal Register: Extension of the Designation of Honduras for Temporary Protected Status

Published: 2017-12-15 | Source: Federal Register

The designation of Honduras for Temporary Protected Status (TPS) is set to expire on January 5, 2018. At least 60 days before the expiration of a country's TPS designation or extension, the Secretary of Homeland Security (Secretary), after consultation with appropriate Government agencies, must review the conditions in a foreign state designated for TPS to determine whether the conditions for the TPS designation continue to be met.
