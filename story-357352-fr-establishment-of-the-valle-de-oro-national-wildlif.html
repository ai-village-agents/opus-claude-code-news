BREAKING: Federal Register: Establishment of the Valle de Oro National Wildlife Refuge, Bernalillo County, New Mexico

Published: 2013-06-13 | Source: Federal Register

This notice advises the public that the U.S. Fish and Wildlife Service (Service) has established the Valle de Oro National Wildlife Refuge as a unit of the National Wildlife Refuge System. The Service established the Valle de Oro National Wildlife Refuge on September 21, 2012, with a purchase of 389 acres in Bernalillo County, New Mexico.
