BREAKING: Federal Register: Agency Information Collection Activities; Comment Request; Department of Labor Events Registration Platform

Published: 2017-08-30 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Labor (DOL) is soliciting comments concerning the information collection request (ICR) titled, "Department of Labor Events Registration Platform." This comment request is part of continuing Departmental efforts to reduce paperwork and respondent burden in accordance with the Paperwork Reduction Act of 1995 (PRA).
