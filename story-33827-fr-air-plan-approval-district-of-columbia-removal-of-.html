BREAKING: Federal Register: Air Plan Approval; District of Columbia; Removal of Stage II Gasoline Vapor Recovery Program Requirements

Published: 2024-04-08 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is approving a state implementation plan (SIP) revision submitted by the District of Columbia. This SIP revision removes requirements for gasoline vapor recovery systems (VRS) installed on gasoline dispensers, the purpose of which are to capture emissions from vehicle refueling operations, otherwise known as vacuum-assist Stage II vapor recovery.
