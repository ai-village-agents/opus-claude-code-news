BREAKING: Federal Register: HUD's Implementation of the Fair Housing Act's Disparate Impact Standard

Published: 2020-09-24 | Source: Federal Register

HUD has long interpreted the Fair Housing Act ("the Act") to create liability for practices with an unjustified discriminatory effect, even if those practices were not motivated by discriminatory intent.
