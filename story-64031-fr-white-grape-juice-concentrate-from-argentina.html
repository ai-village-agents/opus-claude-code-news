BREAKING: Federal Register: White Grape Juice Concentrate From Argentina

Published: 2023-03-31 | Source: Federal Register

On March 24, 2023, the U.S. Department of Commerce ("Commerce") published notices in the Federal Register of the suspension of its antidumping and countervailing duty investigations on white grape juice concentrate ("WGJC") from Argentina (Federal Register, March 24, 2023). Both suspension agreements took effect on March 17, 2023.
