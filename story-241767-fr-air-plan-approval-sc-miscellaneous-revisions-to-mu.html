BREAKING: Federal Register: Air Plan Approval; SC: Miscellaneous Revisions to Multiple Rules

Published: 2017-08-21 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is proposing to approve changes to the South Carolina State Implementation Plan (SIP) to revise several miscellaneous rules, covering definitions, source tests, credible evidence, open burning, air pollution episodes, and fugitive particulate matter.
