BREAKING: Federal Register: Updates to Cross-References to Bank Secrecy Act Regulations

Published: 2014-01-14 | Source: Federal Register

The Commodity Futures Trading Commission ("Commission") is adopting technical amendments to the Commission's regulations that correct cross-references to regulations administered by the Financial Crimes Enforcement Network ("FinCEN"), a bureau of the U.S. Department of Treasury, under the Bank Secrecy Act ("BSA").
