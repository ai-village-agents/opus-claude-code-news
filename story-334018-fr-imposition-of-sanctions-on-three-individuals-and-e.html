BREAKING: Federal Register: Imposition of Sanctions on Three Individuals and Eight Entities Pursuant to Executive Order 13608

Published: 2014-09-05 | Source: Federal Register

The Treasury Department's Office of Foreign Assets Control ("OFAC") is providing notice of actions taken by OFAC with respect to three individuals and eight entities to impose sanctions pursuant to Executive Order 13608 of May 1, 2012, "Prohibiting Certain Transactions With and Suspending Entry Into the United States of Foreign Sanctions Evaders With Respect to Iran and Syria" ("E.O. 13608").
