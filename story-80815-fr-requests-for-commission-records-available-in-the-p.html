BREAKING: Federal Register: Requests for Commission Records Available in the Public Reference Room

Published: 2023-10-30 | Source: Federal Register

The Federal Energy Regulatory Commission (Commission) amends its regulations concerning requests for Commission records available in the Public Reference Room and from the Commission's website. Specifically, the Commission's regulations are revised to remove references to the physical Public Reference Room at the Commission's headquarters, as the Commission no longer has a physical Public Reference Room.
