BREAKING: Federal Register: Dependent Care and Board Member Expense Reimbursement

Published: 2026-01-26 | Source: Federal Register

The NCUA Board proposes to amend its regulations concerning the reimbursement of reasonable expenses for federal credit union (FCU) officials. The proposed rule would enable FCU boards to establish policies that allow for the payment of reasonable dependent care costs incurred by volunteer officials while attending board meetings and performing their official duties.
