BREAKING: Federal Register: Government Securities Act Regulations; Replacement of References to Credit Ratings and Technical Amendments

Published: 2014-07-08 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of the Treasury (Treasury) is issuing in final form an amendment to the regulations issued under the Government Securities Act of 1986, as amended (GSA), to replace references to credit ratings in the regulations with alternative requirements.
