BREAKING: Federal Register: Proposed Agency Information Collection

Published: 2014-01-30 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Energy (DOE) has submitted to the OMB for clearance, a proposal for collection of information pursuant to the Paperwork Reduction Act of 1995. The collection would be used to measure the impact and progress of DOE's Workplace Charging Challenge (Challenge).
