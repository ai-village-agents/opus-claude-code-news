BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Intent To Establish and Call for Nominations for the San Juan Islands National Monument Advisory Committee, Washington

Published: 2014-02-06 | Source: Federal Register

The Bureau of Land Management (BLM) is publishing this notice in accordance with the Federal Land Policy and Management Act (FLPMA) and the Federal Advisory Committee Act (FACA). The BLM gives notice that the Secretary of the Interior is establishing the San Juan Islands National Monument Advisory Committee.
