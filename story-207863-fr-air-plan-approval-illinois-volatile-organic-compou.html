BREAKING: Federal Register: Air Plan Approval; Illinois; Volatile Organic Compounds Definition

Published: 2018-05-24 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is approving a state submission as a revision to the Illinois state implementation plan (SIP). The revision, submitted on May 30, 2017, incorporates changes to the Illinois Administrative Code (IAC) definition of "volatile organic material" or "volatile organic compounds" (VOC).
