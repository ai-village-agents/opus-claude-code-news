BREAKING: Federal Register: Announcement of Funding Awards; Service Coordinators in Multifamily Housing Program, Fiscal Year (FY) 2012

Published: 2013-06-17 | Source: Federal Register

In accordance with Section 102(a)(4)(C) of the Department of Housing and Urban Development Reform Act of 1989, this announcement notifies the public of funding decisions made by the Department in a competition for funding under the Fiscal Year (FY) 2010 Notice of Funding Availability (NOFA) for the Service Coordinators in Multifamily Housing Programs. This announcement contains the names of the awardees and the amounts of the awards made available by HUD.
