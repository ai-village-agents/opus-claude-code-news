BREAKING: Federal Register: In the Matter of Wolf Creek Nuclear Operating Corporation; Wolf Creek Generating Station

Published: 2019-08-01 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Nuclear Regulatory Commission (NRC) issued a confirmatory order to Wolf Creek Nuclear Operating Corporation on July 18, 2019. The purpose of the confirmatory order was to document commitments that were made as part of a settlement agreement between Wolf Creek Nuclear Operating Corporation and the NRC to address an apparent violation related to craft personnel deliberately falsifying records regarding a work order associated with the cleaning and inspection of control rod drive mechanism...
