BREAKING: Federal Register: Taking and Importing of Marine Mammals

Published: 2018-05-31 | Source: Federal Register

The NMFS Assistant Administrator (Assistant Administrator) has issued a five-year affirmative finding for the Government of El Salvador under the Marine Mammal Protection Act (MMPA).
