BREAKING: Federal Register: Request for Comments on the 5-Year Review of NOAA's Policy on Partnerships in the Provision of Environmental Information

Published: 2012-03-07 | Source: Federal Register

The National Weather Service of the National Oceanic and Atmospheric Administration publishes this notice to request comments on NOAA's Policy on Partnerships in the Provision of Environmental Information. This request for comments is being made as part of a period periodic review of the Policy's effectiveness.
