BREAKING: Federal Register: Applications for Exemption: Commercial Driver's License (CDL) and Hours-of-Service (HOS) of Drivers

Published: 2012-07-06 | Source: Federal Register

FMCSA announces denials of several separate applications for exemption from certain provisions of the Federal Motor Carrier Safety Regulations (FMCSRs) submitted by five motor carriers on behalf of their commercial motor vehicle (CMV) drivers. The Agency reviewed each application and any public comments received, and rendered each decision based upon the merits of the application.
