BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Release and Permanent Closure of the St. Clair Regional Airport, St. Clair, Missouri

Published: 2017-10-13 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA is publishing this notice of a pending action required by statute. Public Law 113-285 requires the FAA to release the City of St. Clair, Missouri, from all restrictions, conditions, and limitations on the use, encumbrance, conveyance, and closure of the St. Clair Regional Airport upon the satisfaction of certain conditions of the St. Clair Regional Airport (K39).
