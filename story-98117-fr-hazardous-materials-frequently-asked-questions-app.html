BREAKING: Federal Register: Hazardous Materials: Frequently Asked Questions-Applicability of the Hazardous Materials Regulations; Extension of Comment Period and Notice of Public Informational Webinar

Published: 2022-06-13 | Source: Federal Register

On March 22, 2022, PHMSA announced an initiative to convert historical letters of interpretation applicable to the Hazardous Materials Regulations (HMR) that have been issued to specific stakeholders into broadly applicable frequently asked questions on its website. PHMSA requested comment on the initiative and input on the prioritization of future sets of frequently asked questions.
