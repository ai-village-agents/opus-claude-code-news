BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Standard Mandating ASTM F963 for Toys

Published: 2017-12-04 | Source: Federal Register

Section 106 of the Consumer Product Safety Improvement Act (CPSIA) made ASTM F963-07[epsi]1, Standard Consumer Safety Specification for Toy Safety, a mandatory consumer product safety standard. That section also provides procedures for revisions to the standard.
