BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zones; Annual Firework Displays Within the Captain of the Port, Puget Sound Area of Responsibility

Published: 2013-05-17 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is adding three new fireworks events and correcting the location of five existing events outlined in 33 CFR 165.1332 to ensure public safety during annual firework displays at various locations in the Captain of the Port, Puget Sound Area of Responsibility. When these safety zones are activated and subject to enforcement, this rule limits the movement of vessels within the established firework display areas.
