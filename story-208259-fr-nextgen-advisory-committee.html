BREAKING: Federal Register: NextGen Advisory Committee

Published: 2018-05-31 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA is issuing this notice to advise the public of the establishment of the NAC for 2 years. The Federal Aviation Administration (FAA) is establishing the NAC under agency authority in accordance with the provisions of the Federal Advisory Committee Act (FACA). The NAC is necessary and in the public interest.
