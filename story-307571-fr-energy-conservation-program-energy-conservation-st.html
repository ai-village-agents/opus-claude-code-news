BREAKING: Federal Register: Energy Conservation Program: Energy Conservation Standards for Refrigerated Beverage Vending Machines

Published: 2015-10-23 | Source: Federal Register

On August 19, 2015, the U.S. Department of Energy (DOE) published a notice of proposed rulemaking (NOPR) in the Federal Register regarding energy conservation standards for refrigerated beverage vending machines (BVM ECS NOPR). DOE also held a public meeting on September 29, 2015. The comment period was scheduled to end October 19, 2015.
