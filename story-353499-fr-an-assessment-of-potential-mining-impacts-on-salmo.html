BREAKING: Federal Register: An Assessment of Potential Mining Impacts on Salmon Ecosystems of Bristol Bay, Alaska

Published: 2013-04-30 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is announcing a public comment period for the revised draft document titled, "An Assessment of Potential Mining Impacts on Salmon Ecosystems of Bristol Bay, Alaska" (EPA-910-R-12-004Ba-c). The document was revised by the EPA after reviewing comments received from the public between May 18 and July 23, 2012 and input from the peer review panel held in August 2012.
