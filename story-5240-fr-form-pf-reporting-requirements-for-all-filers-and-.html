BREAKING: Federal Register: Form PF; Reporting Requirements for All Filers and Large Hedge Fund Advisers; Extension of Compliance Date

Published: 2025-02-05 | Source: Federal Register

The Commodity Futures Trading Commission ("CFTC") and the Securities and Exchange Commission ("SEC") (collectively, "we" or "Commissions") are extending the compliance date for the amendments to Form PF that were adopted on February 8, 2024, from March 12, 2025 to June 12, 2025.
