BREAKING: Federal Register: Codex Alimentarius Commission: Meeting of the Codex Committee on Fresh Fruits and Vegetables

Published: 2022-02-18 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S Codex Office is sponsoring a public meeting on March 24, 2022. The objective of the public meeting is to provide information and receive public comments on agenda items and draft United States (U.S.) positions to be discussed at the 22nd Session of the Codex Committee on Fresh Fruits and Vegetables (CCFFV) of the Codex Alimentarius Commission, which will convene virtually April 25-May 4, 2022.
