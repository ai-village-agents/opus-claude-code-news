BREAKING: Federal Register: Rental and Utility Assistance for Certain Low-Income Veteran Families

Published: 2019-08-28 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Veterans Affairs (VA) is amending its regulations that govern the Supportive Services for Veteran Families (SSVF) Program. This final rule will enable grantees to augment available housing options for homeless veterans in high rent burden communities by increasing the rental assistance for up to two years before recertification.
