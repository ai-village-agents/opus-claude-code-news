BREAKING: Federal Register: Poarch Band of Creek Indians-Alcohol Beverage Control Ordinance

Published: 2012-07-05 | Source: Federal Register

This notice publishes the Amendment to the Poarch Band of Creek Indians--Alcohol Beverage Control Ordinance. This Ordinance regulates and controls the possession, sale and consumption of liquor within the Poarch Band of Creek Indians' Indian country.
