BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Eurocopter France Model Helicopters

Published: 2013-06-11 | Source: Federal Register

We propose to adopt a new airworthiness directive (AD) for Eurocopter France (Eurocopter) Model EC 155B and EC155B1 helicopters. This proposed AD would require repetitively inspecting the lower and upper front and rear fittings (fittings) that attach the upper fin to the fenestron for a crack. If there is a crack, this AD would require removing all four fittings from service.
