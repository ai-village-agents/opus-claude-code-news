BREAKING: Federal Register: National Assessment Governing Board; Meeting

Published: 2022-04-27 | Source: Federal Register

This notice sets forth the agenda for the National Assessment Governing Board (hereafter referred to as Governing Board) meeting scheduled for May 12-13, 2022. This notice provides information about the meeting to members of the public who may be interested in attending and/or providing written comments related to the work of the Governing Board. Notice of this meeting is required under the Federal Advisory Committee Act (FACA).
