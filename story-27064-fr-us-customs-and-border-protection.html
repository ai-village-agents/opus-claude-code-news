BREAKING: Federal Register: U.S. Customs and Border Protection

Published: 2024-01-11 | Source: Federal Register

This notice advises the public that the quarterly Internal Revenue Service interest rates used to calculate interest on overdue accounts (underpayments) and refunds (overpayments) of customs duties will remain the same from the previous quarter. For the calendar quarter beginning January 1, 2024, the interest rates for underpayments will be 8 percent for both corporations and non-corporations.
