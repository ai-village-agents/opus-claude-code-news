BREAKING: Federal Register: Privacy Act of 1974; System of Records

Published: 2023-02-23 | Source: Federal Register

In accordance with the requirements of the Privacy Act of 1974, the National Aeronautics and Space Administration is providing public notice of a modification to a previously announced system of records, Harassment Report Case Files/NASA 10HRCF. The most significant modification is that the system of records will now be exempt from certain subsections of the Privacy Act. A statement of purpose for maintaining the records was also added.
