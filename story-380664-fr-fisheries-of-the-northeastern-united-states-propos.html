BREAKING: Federal Register: Fisheries of the Northeastern United States; Proposed 2012 Spiny Dogfish Fishery Specifications

Published: 2012-03-19 | Source: Federal Register

This rule proposes a catch limit, commercial quota, and trip limit for the spiny dogfish fishery for the 2012 fishing year. The proposed action was developed by the Mid-Atlantic and New England Fishery Management Councils pursuant to the fishery specification requirements of the Spiny Dogfish Fishery Management Plan.
