BREAKING: Federal Register: Crash Preventability Determination Program

Published: 2023-04-13 | Source: Federal Register

To address industry concerns about all crashes being used in the Agency's Safety Measurement System (SMS), since May 2020 FMCSA has been operating the Crash Preventability Determination Program (CPDP). This program reviews 16 specific crash types and modifies information in the SMS to distinguish not preventable crashes.
