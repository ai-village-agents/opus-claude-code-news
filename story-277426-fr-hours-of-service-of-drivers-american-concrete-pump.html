BREAKING: Federal Register: Hours of Service of Drivers: American Concrete Pumping Association (ACPA); Application for Exemption

Published: 2016-10-25 | Source: Federal Register

FMCSA announces that it has received an application from the American Concrete Pumping Association (ACPA) for an exemption from the 30-minute rest break provision of the Agency's hours-of-service (HOS) regulations for commercial motor vehicle (CMV) drivers. ACPA requests that concrete pump operators be allowed to use 30 minutes or more of on-duty "waiting time" to satisfy the requirement for the 30-minute rest break, provided they do not perform any other work during the break.
