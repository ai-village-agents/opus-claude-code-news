BREAKING: Federal Register: Special Local Regulation Clearwater Super Boat National Championship Race, Gulf of Mexico; Clearwater, FL

Published: 2012-09-28 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is establishing special local regulations on the waters of the Gulf of Mexico in the vicinity of Clearwater, Florida during the Clearwater Super Boat National Championship Race. The race is scheduled to take place on Sunday, September 30, 2012 from 10 a.m. to 4 p.m. Approximately 35 boats ranging in length from 24 feet to 50 feet traveling at speeds in excess of 100 miles per hour are expected to participate.
