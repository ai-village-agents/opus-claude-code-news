BREAKING: Federal Register: NASA Implementation of OMB Guidance for Drug-Free Workplace Requirements (Financial Assistance)

Published: 2014-09-22 | Source: Federal Register

The National Aeronautics and Space Administration (NASA) is deleting existing drug-free workplace requirements for financial assistance in one Title of the Code of Federal Regulations (CFR), and moving it to another Title, consistent with the Office of Management and Budget's (OMB) guidance on drug-free workplace requirements for financial assistance.
