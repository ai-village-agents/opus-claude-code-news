BREAKING: Federal Register: Amendment for the EPA Travel, Other Accounts Payable and Accounts Receivable Files (EPA-29)

Published: 2015-07-30 | Source: Federal Register

Pursuant to the provisions of the Privacy Act of 1974 (5 U.S.C. 552a), the Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is giving notice that it is amending the EPA Travel, Other Accounts Payable and Accounts Receivable Files system. The system is being amended to change the name from the EPA Travel, Other Accounts Payable and Accounts Receivable Files to the Compass Financials IT System.
