BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; American Champion Aircraft Corp.

Published: 2017-04-12 | Source: Federal Register

We are adopting a new airworthiness directive (AD) for certain American Champion Aircraft Corp. Model 8KCAB airplanes. This AD requires fabrication and installation of a placard to prohibit aerobatic flight, inspection of the aileron hinge rib and support, and a reporting requirement of the inspection results to the FAA.
