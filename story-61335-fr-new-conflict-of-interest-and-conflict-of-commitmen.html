BREAKING: Federal Register: New Conflict of Interest and Conflict of Commitment Policy for Recipients of NASA Financial Assistance Awards

Published: 2023-02-27 | Source: Federal Register

To address undue foreign influence in NASA-supported research and ensure responsible stewardship of taxpayer dollars, NASA has developed a new conflict of interest (COI) and conflict of commitment (COC) disclosure policy and an associated term and condition applicable to entities implementing NASA financial assistance awards that will be implemented in NASA's Grant and Cooperative Agreement Manual (GCAM).
