BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Engine Alliance Turbofan Engines

Published: 2013-01-28 | Source: Federal Register

We are adopting a new airworthiness directive (AD) for all Engine Alliance GP7270 and GP7277 turbofan engines. This AD requires initial and repetitive borescope inspections and removal from service before further flight if one or more burn holes are detected, in certain high-pressure turbine (HPT) stage 2 nozzles.
