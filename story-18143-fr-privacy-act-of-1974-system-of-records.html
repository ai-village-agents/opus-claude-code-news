BREAKING: Federal Register: Privacy Act of 1974; System of Records

Published: 2025-08-15 | Source: Federal Register

Pursuant to the Privacy Act of 1974, notice is hereby given that the VA is modifying the system of records titled, "Veteran, Patient, Employee, and Volunteer Research and Development Project Records-VA" (34VA10). This system of records is used to determine eligibility for research funding, to determine handling of intellectual properties, to manage proposed and/or approved research endeavors, and to evaluate the research and development program.
