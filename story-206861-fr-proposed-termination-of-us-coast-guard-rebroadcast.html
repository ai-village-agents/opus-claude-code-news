BREAKING: Federal Register: Proposed Termination of U.S. Coast Guard Rebroadcast of HYDROLANT and HYDROPAC Information

Published: 2018-05-14 | Source: Federal Register

The United States Coast Guard may cease rebroadcasting HYDROLANT and HYDROPAC (defined below) navigational warnings from the National Geospatial-Intelligence Agency (NGA) over HF SITOR (defined below). There is not a requirement for the Coast Guard to rebroadcast this information, although the Coast Guard has been voluntarily doing so for a number of years, and doing so is duplicative of NGA's broadcast.
