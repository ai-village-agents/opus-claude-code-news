BREAKING: Federal Register: Solicitation of Nominations for Appointment to the Advisory Committee on Minority Health

Published: 2015-01-15 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Health and Human Services (HHS), Office of Minority Health (OMH), is seeking nominations of qualified candidates to be considered for appointment as a member of the Advisory Committee on Minority Health (hereafter referred to as the "Committee or ACMH").
