BREAKING: Federal Register: Application To Export Electric Energy; ConocoPhillips Company

Published: 2013-02-19 | Source: Federal Register

ConocoPhillips Company (CoP) has applied to renew its authority to transmit electric energy from the United States to Mexico pursuant to section 202(e) of the Federal Power Act.
