BREAKING: Federal Register: Export and Reexport License Requirements for Certain Microwave and Millimeter Wave Electronic Components: Correction

Published: 2012-01-24 | Source: Federal Register

This correction adds a compliance date of February 9, 2012, to a final rule published on January 9, 2012 (77 FR 1017). That final rule imposed a license requirement on exports and reexports to all destinations other than Canada of two types of microwave and millimeter wave electronic components.
