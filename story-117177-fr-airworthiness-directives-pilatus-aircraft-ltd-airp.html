BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Pilatus Aircraft Ltd. Airplanes

Published: 2021-02-22 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA is adopting a new airworthiness directive (AD) for all Pilatus Aircraft Ltd. Model PC-7 airplanes. This AD results from mandatory continuing airworthiness information (MCAI) originated by an aviation authority of another country to identify and correct an unsafe condition on an aviation product.
