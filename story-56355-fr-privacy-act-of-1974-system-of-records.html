BREAKING: Federal Register: Privacy Act of 1974; System of Records

Published: 2024-12-23 | Source: Federal Register

In accordance with the Privacy Act of 1974, the DoD is modifying and reissuing a current system of records titled, "Pentagon Facilities Parking Program," DWHS D04. This system of records was originally established to manage the Pentagon Facilities Parking Program for DoD civilian, military, and contractor personnel applying for and in receipt of Pentagon parking permits.
