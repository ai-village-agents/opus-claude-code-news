BREAKING: Federal Register: Computation of Time During Emergencies

Published: 2019-02-14 | Source: Federal Register

The Federal Energy Regulatory Commission (Commission or Agency) is amending its Rules of Practice and Procedure to update its provisions regarding the computation of time. This final rule will modify the Commission's regulations to cover situations in which the Commission is closed due to adverse conditions--including inclement weather--even though some official duties may continue through telework-ready employees.
