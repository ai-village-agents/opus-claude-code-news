BREAKING: Federal Register: Special Local Regulation, Seattle Seafair Unlimited Hydroplane Race

Published: 2024-08-01 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard will enforce the special local regulations for the Seattle Seafair Unlimited Hydroplane Race from 8 a.m. to 5 p.m. each day from August 2 through August 4, 2024, to provide for the safety of life on navigable waterways during this event. The regulation for this event identifies the regulated area on Lake Washington, Seattle, Washington.
