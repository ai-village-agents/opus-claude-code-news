BREAKING: Federal Register: Removal of Personally Identifiable Information From Registration Records

Published: 2016-09-15 | Source: Federal Register

The United States Copyright Office ("Office") is proposing new rules related to personally identifiable information ("PII") that may be found in the Office's registration records.
