BREAKING: Federal Register: Update to Investigative and Enforcement Procedures; Correction

Published: 2019-03-18 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA is correcting an NPRM published on February 12, 2019. In that document, the FAA proposed to amend its regulations to the procedural rules governing Federal Aviation Administration investigations and enforcement actions. This document corrects two errors in the FOR FURTHER INFORMATION CONTACT section of that document and one error in the footnote of the "Subpart A--General Authority To Re-Delegate and Investigative Procedures" section of that document.
