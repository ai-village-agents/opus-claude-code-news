BREAKING: Federal Register: Arms Sales Notification; Withdrawal

Published: 2024-07-12 | Source: Federal Register

On Thursday, June 6, 2024, the DoD published the unclassified text of an arms sales notification in the Federal Register. The published notice contained a Policy Justification for the North Atlantic Treaty Organization (NATO) and a Policy Justification for the Taipei Economic and Cultural Representative Office in the United States (TECRO).
