BREAKING: Federal Register: Availability of the Draft IRIS Toxicological Review of Inorganic Arsenic

Published: 2023-10-16 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is announcing a 60- day public comment period associated with release of the draft Integrated Risk Information System (IRIS) Toxicological Review of Inorganic Arsenic. The draft document was prepared by the Center for Public Health and Environmental Assessment (CPHEA) within EPA's Office of Research and Development (ORD).
