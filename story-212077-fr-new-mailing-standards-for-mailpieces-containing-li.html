BREAKING: Federal Register: New Mailing Standards for Mailpieces Containing Liquids: Extension of Comment Period

Published: 2018-07-20 | Source: Federal Register

On July 9, 2018, the United States Postal Service (USPS[reg]) published a Federal Register proposed rule titled, "New Mailing Standards for Mailpieces Containing Liquids." The USPS has received several requests to extend the comment period for this proposed rule and is, accordingly, extending the comment period scheduled to close on August 8, 2018, until September 30, 2018.
