BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone; Tug Kimberly Anne and Barge Big Digger Operating in the Straits of Mackinac, MI

Published: 2020-05-13 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is establishing a temporary safety zone for navigable waters within a 500-yard radius of a tug and barge in the Straits of Mackinac. The safety zone is needed to protect personnel, vessels, and the marine environment from potential hazards created by the work, inspection, and surveying of underwater infrastructure.
