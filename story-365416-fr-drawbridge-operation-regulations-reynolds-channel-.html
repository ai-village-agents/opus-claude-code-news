BREAKING: Federal Register: Drawbridge Operation Regulations; Reynolds Channel, Lawrence, NY

Published: 2013-09-13 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is canceling the temporary deviation from the regulations published on June 11, 2013, (78 FR 34893) governing the operation of the Atlantic Beach Bridge, mile 0.4, across Reynolds Channel, at Lawrence, New York. The owner of the bridge, Nassau County Bridge Authority, requested six, five hour closure periods, scheduled to occur during the effective period of the above temporary deviation.
