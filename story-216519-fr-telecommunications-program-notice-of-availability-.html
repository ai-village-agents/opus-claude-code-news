BREAKING: Federal Register: Telecommunications Program: Notice of availability of a Finding of No Significant Impact

Published: 2018-09-17 | Source: Federal Register

The Rural Utilities Service (RUS), an agency of the United States Department of Agriculture, has issued a Finding of No Significant Impact (FONSI) in association with the "Broadband Deployment to Rural America" Programmatic Environmental Assessment (PEA) published on March 1, 2016.
