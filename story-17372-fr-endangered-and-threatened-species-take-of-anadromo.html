BREAKING: Federal Register: Endangered and Threatened Species; Take of Anadromous Fish

Published: 2025-08-04 | Source: Federal Register

Notice is hereby given that NMFS has received applications for renewal of two enhancement permit applications pursuant to the Endangered Species Act (ESA) for hatchery operations rearing and releasing Snake River Fall-run Chinook salmon in the Snake River basin of Idaho. The applications are in the form of two existing hatchery and genetic management plans (HGMPs), and two addendums.
