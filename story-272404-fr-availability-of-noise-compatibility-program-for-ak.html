BREAKING: Federal Register: Availability of Noise Compatibility Program for Akron-Canton Airport, North Canton, Ohio

Published: 2016-08-26 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA announces its determination that the noise exposure maps submitted by the Akron-Canton Airport Authority for Akron-Canton Airport under the provisions of 49 U.S.C. 47501 et. Seq. (formerly the Aviation Safety and Noise Abatement Act, hereinafter referred to as "the Act") and 14 CFR part 150 (hereinafter referred to as "Part 150") are in compliance with applicable requirements.
