BREAKING: Federal Register: Performance Review Board Membership

Published: 2024-10-17 | Source: Federal Register

This notice announces the members of the Department of Agriculture's (USDA) Senior Executive Service (SES) and Senior Level (SL) and Scientific or Professional (ST) Performance Review Boards. Agriculture has two PRBs with representatives from each USDA Mission Area. The PRB provides a written recommendation to the Secretary for final approval of each executive's performance rating, performance- based pay adjustment, and performance award.
