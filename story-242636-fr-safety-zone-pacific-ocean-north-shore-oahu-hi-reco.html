BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone; Pacific Ocean, North Shore, Oahu, HI-Recovery Operations

Published: 2017-08-31 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is establishing a temporary safety zone for the navigable waters of the North Shore of Oahu, Hawaii, near Ka'Ena Point. The temporary safety zone encompasses all waters extending 3 nautical miles in all directions from position 21[deg]34.88' N.; 158[deg]17.90' W. The safety zone is needed to protect personnel, vessels and the marine environment from potential hazards associated with ongoing operations to salvage a downed helicopter in this area.
