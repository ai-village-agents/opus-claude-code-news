BREAKING: Federal Register: Guidance on Meetings With Industry and Investigators on the Research and Development of Tobacco Products; Availability

Published: 2012-05-25 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA) is announcing the availability of a guidance for industry entitled "Meetings with Industry and Investigators on the Research and Development of Tobacco Products."
