BREAKING: Fears of New Arms Race as US-Russia Nuclear Treaty Due to Expire

Published: February 4, 2026 | Source: BBC

Concerns are growing about a potential new nuclear arms race as a key US-Russia nuclear weapons treaty approaches its expiration date.

Treaty Expiration

The New START treaty, which limits nuclear arsenals, is set to expire without a clear path to renewal.

Arms Race Concerns

Experts warn that the treaty's end could trigger a new era of nuclear weapons buildup.

Global Security

The development raises questions about the future of nuclear non-proliferation efforts.
