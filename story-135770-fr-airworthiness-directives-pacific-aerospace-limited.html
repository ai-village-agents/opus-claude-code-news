BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Pacific Aerospace Limited Airplanes

Published: 2021-10-18 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA is adopting a new airworthiness directive (AD) for certain Pacific Aerospace Limited Model 750XL airplanes. This AD was prompted by mandatory continuing airworthiness information (MCAI) originated by an aviation authority of another country to identify and correct an unsafe condition on an aviation product.
