BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Bell Helicopter Textron, Inc. (Bell) Helicopters

Published: 2014-02-19 | Source: Federal Register

We are adopting a new airworthiness directive (AD) for Bell Model 204B helicopters with a certain cable assembly installed. This AD requires inspecting the tail rotor (T/R) cable assembly for an incorrectly machined body. This AD is prompted by a report from Bell that a defective body on the cable prevents the barrel assembly from fully engaging in the body cavity.
