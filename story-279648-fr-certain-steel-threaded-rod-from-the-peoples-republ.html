BREAKING: Federal Register: Certain Steel Threaded Rod From the People's Republic of China: Final Results of Antidumping Duty Administrative Review; 2014-2015

Published: 2016-11-22 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Commerce (the "Department") published the Preliminary Results of the sixth administrative review of the antidumping duty order on certain steel threaded rod from the People's Republic of China ("PRC") on May 13, 2016. The period of review ("POR") is April 1, 2014, through March 31, 2015.
