BREAKING: Federal Register: Pipeline Safety: Request for Special Permit; Tennessee Gas Pipeline, LLC (TGP)

Published: 2025-10-10 | Source: Federal Register

On June 26, 2025, PHMSA published a notice to solicit public comment on a request for a special permit from Tennessee Gas Pipeline, LLC (TGP), a subsidiary of Kinder Morgan, Inc. The comment period closed on July 28, 2025. PHMSA is issuing this notice to extend the comment period for an additional 15 days to give the public more time to review the proposed special permit action and updated documents.
