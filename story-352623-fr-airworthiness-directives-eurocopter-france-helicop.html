BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Eurocopter France Helicopters

Published: 2013-04-18 | Source: Federal Register

We are adopting a new airworthiness directive (AD) for Eurocopter France (ECF) Model AS332C, L, and L1 helicopters to require an initial and repetitive inspections of the outer skin, butt strap, and fuselage frame for a crack and modification of the helicopter.
