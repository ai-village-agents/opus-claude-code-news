BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Funding Availability for the Rural Community Development Initiative (RCDI) for Fiscal Year 2024

Published: 2024-06-10 | Source: Federal Register

The Rural Housing Service (RHS or the Agency), a Rural Development (RD) agency of the United States Department of Agriculture (USDA), announces the acceptance of applications under the Rural Community Development Initiative (RCDI) program for fiscal year (FY) 2024. Up to $5 million in funding is available for fiscal year (FY) 2024.
