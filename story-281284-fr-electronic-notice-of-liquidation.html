BREAKING: Federal Register: Electronic Notice of Liquidation

Published: 2016-12-12 | Source: Federal Register

This document adopts as a final rule, with changes, proposed amendments to the U.S. Customs and Border Protection (CBP) regulations reflecting that official notice of liquidation, suspension of liquidation, and extension of liquidation will be posted electronically on the CBP Web site.
