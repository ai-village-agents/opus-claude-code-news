BREAKING: Federal Register: Extension of Approval of Information Collection, OMB Control Number 1004-0137

Published: 2014-06-11 | Source: Federal Register

In compliance with the Paperwork Reduction Act, the Bureau of Land Management (BLM) invites public comments on, and plans to request approval to continue, the collection of information from those who wish to participate in the exploration, development, production, and utilization of oil and gas operations on BLM-managed public lands. The Office of Management and Budget (OMB), has assigned control number 1004-0137 to this information collection.
