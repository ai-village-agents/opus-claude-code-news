BREAKING: Federal Register: Addition of Lebanon to the List of Regions Affected by Highly Pathogenic Avian Influenza

Published: 2017-01-23 | Source: Federal Register

We are advising the public that we added Lebanon to the Animal and Plant Health Inspection Service (APHIS) list maintained on the APHIS Web site of regions considered affected with highly pathogenic avian influenza (HPAI). We are taking this action because of the confirmation of HPAI detection in Lebanon.
