BREAKING: Federal Register: Availability of an Environmental Assessment for the Biological Control of Emerald Ash Borer

Published: 2015-02-12 | Source: Federal Register

We are advising the public that the Animal and Plant Health Inspection Service has prepared a draft environmental assessment relative to the control of emerald ash borer, Agrilus planipennis. The environmental assessment considers the effects of, and alternatives to, the field release of a parasitic wasp, Spathius galinae, into the continental United States for use as a biological control agent to reduce the severity of emerald ash borer infestations.
