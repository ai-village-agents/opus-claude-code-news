BREAKING: Federal Register: Agency Information Collection Activities; Proposed New Collection (EPA ICR No. 2532.01); Comment Request

Published: 2016-03-30 | Source: Federal Register

In compliance with the Paperwork Reduction Act (PRA), this document announces that EPA is planning to submit an Information Collection Request (ICR) to the Office of Management and Budget (OMB). The ICR, entitled: "Use of Mercury and Mercury Compounds in Products and Processes" and identified by EPA ICR No. 2532.01 and OMB Control No. 2070-NEW, represents a new request.
