BREAKING: Federal Register: Diabetes Outcome Measures Beyond Hemoglobin A1c: CDER Public Workshop

Published: 2016-05-19 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration's (FDA) Center for Drug Evaluation and Research (CDER), is sponsoring a public workshop entitled "Diabetes Outcome Measures Beyond Hemoglobin A1c (HbA1c)."
