BREAKING: Federal Register: Boating Infrastructure Grant Program

Published: 2014-04-25 | Source: Federal Register

We, the U.S. Fish and Wildlife Service (Service), propose changes in the regulations governing the administration of the national Boating Infrastructure Grant Program (BIG). We published a proposed rule in the Federal Register on March 28, 2012. We received 22 responses from the public during the 60-day comment period that ended May 29, 2012.
