BREAKING: Federal Register: Lists of Designated Primary Medical Care, Mental Health, and Dental Health Professional Shortage Areas

Published: 2024-01-02 | Source: Federal Register

This is the second of two planned notices informing the public of the availability of the complete lists of all geographic areas, population groups, and facilities designated as primary medical care, dental health, and mental health professional shortage areas (HPSA). This notice includes the lists of HPSAs in a designated status as of December 2, 2023. The lists are available on the shortage area topic page on HRSA's data.hrsa.gov website.
