BREAKING: Federal Register: Violence Against Women Reauthorization Act of 2013: Implementation in HUD Housing Programs

Published: 2016-11-16 | Source: Federal Register

This final rule implements in HUD's regulations the requirements of the 2013 reauthorization of the Violence Against Women Act (VAWA), which applies for all victims of domestic violence, dating violence, sexual assault, and stalking, regardless of sex, gender identity, or sexual orientation, and which must be applied consistent with all nondiscrimination and fair housing requirements.
