BREAKING: Federal Register: Media Bureau Announces Incentive Auction Eligible Facilities and Deadline for Filing Pre-Auction Technical Certification Form

Published: 2015-07-08 | Source: Federal Register

This document announces each full power and Class A station facility eligible for protection in the repacking process and for relinquishment in the reverse auction (i.e., "eligible facility"), as well as the date by which a licensee with a eligible facility must file a Pre-Auction Technical Certification Form (FCC Form 2100, Schedule 381) (approved under OMB control under 3060-1206).
