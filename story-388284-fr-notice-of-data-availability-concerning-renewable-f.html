BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Data Availability Concerning Renewable Fuels Produced From Grain Sorghum Under the RFS Program

Published: 2012-06-12 | Source: Federal Register

This notice of data availability provides an opportunity to comment on EPA's analyses of grain sorghum used as a feedstock to produce ethanol under the Renewable Fuel Standard (RFS) program. EPA's analysis shows that ethanol from grain sorghum has estimated lifecycle greenhouse gas (GHG) emission reductions of 32% compared to the baseline petroleum fuel it would replace.
