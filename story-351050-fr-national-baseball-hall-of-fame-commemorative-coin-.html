BREAKING: Federal Register: National Baseball Hall of Fame Commemorative Coin Program Design Competition

Published: 2013-04-02 | Source: Federal Register

The United States Mint announces the opening of a national coin design competition that will culminate in the Secretary of the Treasury's selection of the image for the obverse (heads side) of the 2014 National Baseball Hall of Fame Commemorative Coins. The competition, which is open to all United States citizens and permanent residents ages 14 and over, begins on April 11, 2013, at 12 noon Eastern Daylight Time (EDT).
