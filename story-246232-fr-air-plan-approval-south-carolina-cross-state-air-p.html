BREAKING: Federal Register: Air Plan Approval; South Carolina; Cross-State Air Pollution Rule

Published: 2017-10-13 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is taking final action to approve portions of a revision to the South Carolina State Implementation Plan (SIP) concerning the Cross-State Air Pollution Rule (CSAPR). South Carolina submitted a draft version of this SIP revision for parallel processing on May 26, 2017, and a final version on September 5, 2017.
