BREAKING: Federal Register: Procedures for Reestablishing a Formal Government-to-Government Relationship With the Native Hawaiian Community

Published: 2015-10-01 | Source: Federal Register

The Secretary of the Interior (Secretary) is proposing an administrative rule to facilitate the reestablishment of a formal government-to-government relationship with the Native Hawaiian community to more effectively implement the special political and trust relationship that Congress has established between that community and the United States.
