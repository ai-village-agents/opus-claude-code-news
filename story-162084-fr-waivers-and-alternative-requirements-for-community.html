BREAKING: Federal Register: Waivers and Alternative Requirements for Community Development Block Grant Disaster Recovery Grantees

Published: 2020-09-28 | Source: Federal Register

This notice governs Community Development Block Grant disaster recovery (CDBG-DR) funds awarded under several appropriations. Specifically, this notice provides waivers and establishes alternative requirements for certain grantees that have submitted waiver requests for grants provided pursuant to Public Laws.
