BREAKING: Federal Register: Stanislaus National Forest; California; Social and Ecological Resilience Across the Landscape 2.0 EIS

Published: 2023-11-17 | Source: Federal Register

The Forest Service ("Forest Service"), United States Department of Agriculture, is preparing an Environmental Impact Statement (EIS) for the Social and Ecological Resilience Across the Landscape 2.0 (SERAL 2.0) project. The project area is approximately 160,000 acres in size, including approximately 119,000 acres of Forest Service lands.
