BREAKING: Federal Register: Release of the Policy Assessment for the Reconsideration of the National Ambient Air Quality Standards for Particulate Matter

Published: 2022-05-26 | Source: Federal Register

On or about May 23, 2022, the Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) will make available the document, Policy Assessment for the Reconsideration of the National Ambient Air Quality Standards for Particulate Matter (PA). This document was prepared as a part of the current reconsideration of the 2020 final decision on the National Ambient Air Quality Standards (NAAQS) for Particulate Matter (PM).
