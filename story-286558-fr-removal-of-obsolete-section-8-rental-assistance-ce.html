BREAKING: Federal Register: Removal of Obsolete Section 8 Rental Assistance Certificate Program Regulations

Published: 2015-02-17 | Source: Federal Register

This final rule removes from regulations obsolete references to the Section 8 Tenant-Based Rental Assistance Certificate program (Certificate Program). In accordance with Executive Order 13563, "Improving Regulation and Regulatory Review," HUD reviewed its regulations to identify regulations that are "outmoded, ineffective, insufficient or excessively burdensome."
