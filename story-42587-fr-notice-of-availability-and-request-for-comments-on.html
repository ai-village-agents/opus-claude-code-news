BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Availability and Request for Comments on Draft Director's Order #71C Concerning Consultation With Indian and Alaska Native Tribes

Published: 2024-07-25 | Source: Federal Register

The National Park Service (NPS) has prepared Director's Order #71C to set forth its policies and procedures regarding consultation with Indian and Alaska Native Tribes. Once adopted, the policies and procedures in Director's Order #71C and its accompanying Reference Manual will supersede and replace any conflicting policies and procedures.
