BREAKING: Federal Register: Compensation, Retirement Programs, and Related Benefits; Effective Date

Published: 2012-12-27 | Source: Federal Register

The Farm Credit Administration (FCA or Agency), through the FCA Board (Board), issued a final rule amending its regulations for Farm Credit System banks and associations to require disclosure of pension benefit and supplemental retirement plans and a discussion of the link between senior officer compensation and performance.
