BREAKING: Federal Register: Prevention of Workplace Violence in Healthcare and Social Assistance

Published: 2016-12-07 | Source: Federal Register

Workplace violence against employees providing healthcare and social assistance services is a serious concern. Evidence indicates that the rate of workplace violence in the industry is substantially higher than private industry as a whole.
