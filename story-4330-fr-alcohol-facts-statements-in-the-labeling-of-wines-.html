BREAKING: Federal Register: Alcohol Facts Statements in the Labeling of Wines, Distilled Spirits, and Malt Beverages

Published: 2025-01-17 | Source: Federal Register

The Alcohol and Tobacco Tax and Trade Bureau (TTB) proposes to require disclosure of per-serving alcohol, calorie, and nutrient content information in an "Alcohol Facts" statement on all alcohol beverage labels subject to TTB's regulatory authority under the Federal Alcohol Administration Act (FAA Act).
