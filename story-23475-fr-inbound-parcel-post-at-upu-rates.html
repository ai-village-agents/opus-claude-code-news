BREAKING: Federal Register: Inbound Parcel Post (at UPU Rates)

Published: 2025-11-26 | Source: Federal Register

The Commission is recognizing a recent Postal Service filing of a change in rates not of general applicability for Inbound Parcel Post (at Universal Postal Union rates) to be effective January 1, 2026. This document informs the public of the filing, invites public comment, and takes other administrative steps.
