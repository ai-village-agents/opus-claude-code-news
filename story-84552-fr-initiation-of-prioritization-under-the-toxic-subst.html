BREAKING: Federal Register: Initiation of Prioritization Under the Toxic Substances Control Act (TSCA); Request for Comment

Published: 2023-12-18 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is announcing the initiation of and soliciting public comment on the prioritization process for five chemical substances as candidates for designation as High-Priority Substances for risk evaluation under the Toxic Substances Control Act (TSCA) and related implementing regulations.
