BREAKING: Federal Register: Medical Review Board Public Meeting

Published: 2014-07-11 | Source: Federal Register

FMCSA announces that the Medical Review Board will meet on Tuesday and Wednesday, July 29--30, 2014. The MRB will continue its deliberations on Schedule II Controlled Substances and their effect on commercial motor vehicle (CMV) drivers' ability to operate safely. The entire meeting is open to the public and there will be a public comment period at the end of each day.
