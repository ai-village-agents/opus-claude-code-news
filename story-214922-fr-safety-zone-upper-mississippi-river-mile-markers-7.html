BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone; Upper Mississippi River, Mile Markers 751.2 to 751.8, Alma, WI

Published: 2018-08-27 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is establishing a temporary safety zone for certain navigable waters of the Upper Mississippi River. The safety zone is necessary to protect persons, vessels, and the marine environment from potential hazards created by a demolition project taking place on the bank of the Upper Mississippi River near Alma, WI.
