BREAKING: Federal Register: Taxable Year of Income Inclusion Under an Accrual Method of Accounting; Correction

Published: 2019-10-09 | Source: Federal Register

This document contains a correction to a notice of proposed rulemaking (REG-104870-18) that was published in the Federal Register on September 9, 2019. The proposed regulations are regarding the timing of income inclusion under section 451 of the Internal Revenue Code. The proposed regulations reflect changes made by the Tax Cuts and Jobs Act.
