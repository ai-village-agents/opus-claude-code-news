BREAKING: Federal Register: Aviation Maintenance Technician Schools

Published: 2015-11-19 | Source: Federal Register

This action extends the comment period for an NPRM published on October 2, 2015. In that document, the FAA proposes to amend the regulations governing the curriculum and operations of FAA-certificated Aviation Maintenance Technician Schools. These amendments would modernize and reorganize the required curriculum subjects in the appendices of the current regulations.
