BREAKING: Federal Register: Best Practices in Developing Proprietary Names for Human Prescription Drug Products; Guidance for Industry; Availability

Published: 2020-12-09 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA or Agency) is announcing the availability of a final guidance for industry entitled "Best Practices in Developing Proprietary Names for Human Prescription Drug Products." This document provides guidance to sponsors on the development and selection of proposed proprietary names.
