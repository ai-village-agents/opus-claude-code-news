BREAKING: Federal Register: Rules Regarding Dual Consolidated Losses and the Treatment of Certain Disregarded Payments; Hearing

Published: 2024-11-08 | Source: Federal Register

This document provides a notice of public hearing on the proposed rule (REG-105128-23) that was published in the Federal Register on Wednesday, August 7, 2024. The proposed regulations relate to certain issues arising under the dual consolidated loss rules, including the effect of intercompany transactions and items arising from stock ownership in calculating a dual consolidated loss.
