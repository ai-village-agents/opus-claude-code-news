BREAKING: Federal Register: Public Water System Supervision Program Approval for the State of Michigan

Published: 2020-06-10 | Source: Federal Register

Notice is hereby given that the Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) has tentatively approved a revision to the state of Michigan's Public Water System Supervision Program under the federal Safe Drinking Water Act (SDWA) by adopting the Revised Total Coliform Rule. The EPA has determined this revision is no less stringent than the corresponding federal regulation.
