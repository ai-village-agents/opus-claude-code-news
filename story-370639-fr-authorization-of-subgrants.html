BREAKING: Federal Register: Authorization of Subgrants

Published: 2013-11-20 | Source: Federal Register

This notice authorizes the use of subgrants with Scholarships for Opportunity and Results Act (SOAR Act) funds awarded under CFDA 84.370C to the District of Columbia Office of the State Superintendent of Education (OSSE), for the purpose of carrying out its proposed activities in support of quality charter schools.
