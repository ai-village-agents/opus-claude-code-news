BREAKING: Federal Register: Hours of Service: Exemption Application From Flat Top Transport, LLC

Published: 2023-02-22 | Source: Federal Register

FMCSA announces its decision to deny Flat Top Transport's request for an exemption from the hours-of-service (HOS) regulations. Flat Top Transport requested a four-month exemption for "immediate and emergency delivery of dry and bulk food grade products to locations that supply stores and distribution centers nationally."
