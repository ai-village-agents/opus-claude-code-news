BREAKING: Federal Register: Approval and Promulgation of Air Quality Implementation Plans; Washington; Determination of Clean Data for the 2006 24-Hour Fine Particulate Standard for the Tacoma, Pierce County Nonattainment Area

Published: 2012-07-05 | Source: Federal Register

EPA is proposing to determine that the Tacoma, Pierce County nonattainment area (hereafter referred to as "Tacoma, Pierce County" or "the area") for the 2006 24-hour fine particulate matter (PM2.5) National Ambient Air Quality Standard (NAAQS) has clean data for the 2006 24-hour PM2.5 NAAQS.
