BREAKING: Federal Register: Special Local Regulations; Temporary Change for Recurring Marine Event in the Fifth Coast Guard District

Published: 2015-07-16 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard proposes to temporarily change the enforcement periods of special local regulations for a recurring marine event in the Fifth Coast Guard District. This regulation applies to the "Ocean City Maryland Offshore Grand Prix" power boat race, a recurring marine event held on the North Atlantic Ocean near Ocean City, MD, and would be effective from October 3, 2015, to October 4, 2015.
