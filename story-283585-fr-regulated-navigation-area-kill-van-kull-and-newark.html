BREAKING: Federal Register: Regulated Navigation Area, Kill Van Kull and Newark Bay; Bayonne, NJ, NY

Published: 2015-01-09 | Source: Federal Register

The United States Coast Guard proposes to establish a permanent Regulated Navigation Area (RNA) on the navigable waters of Kill Van Kull and Newark Bay surrounding the Bayonne Bridge. In response to a planned Bayonne Bridge construction project, this proposed rule would establish a speed restriction in the waters surrounding the Bayonne Bridge.
