BREAKING: Federal Register: Air Plan Approval; Indiana; Volatile Organic Compounds; Cold Cleaner Degreasing

Published: 2023-12-18 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is approving revisions to the volatile organic compound (VOC) rules contained in the Indiana State Implementation Plan (SIP). The Indiana Department of Environmental Management (Indiana) modified its rules to allow the use of a VOC control device for cold cleaning degreasing operations as a compliance option to using low vapor pressure solvent for cleaning or degreasing machine parts.
