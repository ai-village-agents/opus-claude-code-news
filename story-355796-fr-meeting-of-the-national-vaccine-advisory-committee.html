BREAKING: Federal Register: Meeting of the National Vaccine Advisory Committee

Published: 2013-05-24 | Source: Federal Register

As stipulated by the Federal Advisory Committee Act, the Department of Health and Human Services (HHS) is hereby giving notice that the National Vaccine Advisory Committee (NVAC) will hold a meeting. The meeting is open to the public. Pre-registration is required for both public attendance and comment.
