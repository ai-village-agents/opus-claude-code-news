BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Continued Suspension of Imports of Zimbabwe Elephant Trophies Taken On or After April 4, 2014

Published: 2015-07-17 | Source: Federal Register

On March 26, 2015, the U.S. Fish and Wildlife Service (Service) made a determination that the suspension on the import of sport-hunted African elephant trophies taken in Zimbabwe on or after April 4, 2014, would be continued until further notice.
