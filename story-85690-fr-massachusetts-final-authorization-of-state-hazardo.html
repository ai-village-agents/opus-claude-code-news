BREAKING: Federal Register: Massachusetts: Final Authorization of State Hazardous Waste Management Program Revisions

Published: 2022-01-04 | Source: Federal Register

Massachusetts has applied to the EPA for final authorization of revisions to its hazardous waste program under the Resource Conservation and Recovery Act (RCRA), as amended. The EPA proposes to grant final authorization to Massachusetts for these revisions by a direct final rule, which can be found in the "Rules and Regulations" section of this issue of the Federal Register.
