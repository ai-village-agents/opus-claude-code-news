BREAKING: Federal Register: Voluntary Self-Reported Exit (VSRE) Pilot

Published: 2025-03-19 | Source: Federal Register

This notice announces that U.S. Customs and Border Protection (CBP) will conduct a Voluntary Self-Reported Exit (VSRE) Pilot to collect facial images from certain aliens, specifically aliens subject to Form I-94 (Arrival/Departure Record) requirements, exiting the United States from any location.
