BREAKING: Federal Register: Russian Sanctions: Addition of Certain Entities to the Entity List

Published: 2017-06-22 | Source: Federal Register

The Bureau of Industry and Security (BIS) amends the Export Administration Regulations (EAR) by adding ten entities to the Entity List. The ten entities that are added to the Entity List have been determined by the U.S. Government to be acting contrary to the national security or foreign policy interests of the United States.
