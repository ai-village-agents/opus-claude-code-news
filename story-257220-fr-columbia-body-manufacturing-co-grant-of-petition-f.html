BREAKING: Federal Register: Columbia Body Manufacturing Co.; Grant of Petition for Temporary Exemption From FMVSS No. 224

Published: 2016-03-07 | Source: Federal Register

In accordance with 49 U.S.C. 30113 and 49 CFR part 555, NHTSA is granting a petition from Columbia Body Manufacturing Co. ("Columbia Body" or "petitioner"), a small volume manufacturer, for a temporary exemption from Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard (FMVSS) No. 224, Rear impact protection, for certain gravity feed dump body trailers ("dump body trailers").
