BREAKING: Federal Register: Air Plan Approval; TN; Updates to References to Appendix W Modeling Guideline

Published: 2022-10-24 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is approving a State Implementation Plan (SIP) revision submitted by the State of Tennessee on April 9, 2021. Specifically, EPA is approving updates to the incorporation by reference of Federal guidelines on air quality modeling in the Tennessee SIP.
