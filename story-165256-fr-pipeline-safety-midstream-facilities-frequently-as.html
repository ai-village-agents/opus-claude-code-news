BREAKING: Federal Register: Pipeline Safety: Midstream Facilities Frequently Asked Questions

Published: 2020-11-04 | Source: Federal Register

PHMSA is making available for comment a set of draft frequently asked questions (FAQs) regarding federal oversight of midstream processing facilities. Specifically, this guidance will delineate where PHMSA and the Occupational Safety and Health Administration (OSHA) will each perform inspection and enforcement activities for midstream processing facilities where there is overlapping authority.
