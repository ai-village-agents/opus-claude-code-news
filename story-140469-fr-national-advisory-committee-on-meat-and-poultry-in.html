BREAKING: Federal Register: National Advisory Committee on Meat and Poultry Inspection; Nominations for Membership

Published: 2021-12-20 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Department of Agriculture (USDA) is soliciting nominations for membership for the National Advisory Committee on Meat and Poultry Inspection (NACMPI). The full Committee consists of 20 members, and each person selected is expected to serve a 2-year term. The USDA is announcing Committee vacancies to minority businesses and organizations, consumer groups, businesses, media, local and state governments, and academia to attract and appoint diverse candidates.
