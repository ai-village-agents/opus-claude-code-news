BREAKING: Federal Register: Lambda-Cyhalothrin; Receipt of Application for Emergency Exemption, Solicitation of Public Comment

Published: 2020-08-13 | Source: Federal Register

EPA has received a specific exemption request from the California Department of Pesticide Regulation to use the pesticide lambda-cyhalothrin (CAS No. 91465-08-6) to treat up to 3,000 acres of asparagus to control the European asparagus aphid. The applicant proposes a use which is supported by the Interregional (IR)-4 program and has been requested in 5 or more previous years, and a petition for tolerance has not yet been submitted to the Agency.
