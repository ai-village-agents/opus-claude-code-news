BREAKING: Federal Register: Certain Lined Paper Products From India: Initiation of Changed Circumstances Review

Published: 2014-01-22 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Commerce (the Department) has received information sufficient to warrant initiation of a changed circumstances review of the antidumping duty order on certain lined paper products (CLPP) from India.
