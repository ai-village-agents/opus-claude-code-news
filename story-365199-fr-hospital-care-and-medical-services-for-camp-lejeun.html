BREAKING: Federal Register: Hospital Care and Medical Services for Camp Lejeune Veterans

Published: 2013-09-11 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Veterans Affairs (VA) proposes to amend its regulations to implement a statutory mandate that VA provide health care to certain veterans who served at Camp Lejeune, North Carolina, for at least 30 days during the period beginning on January 1, 1957, and ending on December 31, 1987.
