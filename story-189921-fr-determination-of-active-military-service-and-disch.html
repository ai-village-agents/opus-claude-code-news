BREAKING: Federal Register: Determination of Active Military Service and Discharge for Civilian or Contractual Groups

Published: 2019-09-30 | Source: Federal Register

This final rule removes the Department of the Air Force's regulation containing procedures for processing discharge applications of civilians or contractors claiming prior active military service with the Air Force or a predecessor organization. The content of this part is addressed in a DoD-level regulation, and it is unnecessary.
