BREAKING: Federal Register: Extension and Redesignation of Somalia for Temporary Protected Status

Published: 2021-07-22 | Source: Federal Register

Through this notice, the Department of Homeland Security (DHS) announces that the Secretary of Homeland Security (Secretary) is extending the designation of Somalia for Temporary Protected Status (TPS) for 18 months, from September 18, 2021, through March 17, 2023, and redesignating Somalia for 18 months, effective September 18, 2021 through March 17, 2023.
