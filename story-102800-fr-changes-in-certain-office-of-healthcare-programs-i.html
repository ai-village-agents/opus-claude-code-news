BREAKING: Federal Register: Changes in Certain Office of Healthcare Programs Insurance Premiums; Correction

Published: 2022-08-12 | Source: Federal Register

On May 19, 2022, HUD published a notice in the Federal Register entitled, "Changes in Certain Office of Healthcare Programs Insurance Premiums." The notice contained a chart showing FHA Office of Health Care Facilities Insurance Premiums by Rate & Category. That chart incorrectly listed current upfront capitalized mortgage insurance premium (MIP) basis points for some of the listed programs.
