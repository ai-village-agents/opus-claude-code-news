BREAKING: Federal Register: Department of Health and Human Services Policy for the Protection of Human Research Subjects: Update to the Additional Protections for Specific Populations

Published: 2024-10-24 | Source: Federal Register

In this final rule, the Department of Health and Human Services (HHS) is amending its regulations that govern the protection of human subjects for conformity with 2018 revisions made to the Federal policy for protection of human research subjects (the "Common Rule"), as well as to maintain consistency with the prior version of the Common Rule for research that remains subject to those requirements.
