BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone, Cuyahoga River, Cleveland, OH

Published: 2024-08-23 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is proposing to establish a temporary safety zone for certain waters of the Cuyahoga River. This action is necessary to provide for the safety of life on these navigable waters from position 41-29.716' N, 081-42.137' W to position 41-29.927' N, 081- 42.356' W during the Cleveland Dragon Boat Festival on or about September 21, 2024.
