BREAKING: Federal Register: Update of Statistical Disclosures for Bank and Savings and Loan Registrants

Published: 2019-10-03 | Source: Federal Register

We are proposing rules to update our statistical disclosures for banking registrants. These registrants currently provide many disclosures in response to the items set forth in Industry Guide 3 ("Guide 3"), Statistical Disclosure by Bank Holding Companies, which are not Commission rules.
