BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone; Ohio River, Newburgh, IN

Published: 2019-08-12 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is establishing a temporary safety zone for all navigable waters of the Ohio River, extending the entire width of the river, from mile marker (MM) 777.3 to MM 778.3. This action is necessary to provide for the safety of life on these navigable waters near Newburgh, Indiana, during the City of Newburgh fireworks display on August 31, 2019.
