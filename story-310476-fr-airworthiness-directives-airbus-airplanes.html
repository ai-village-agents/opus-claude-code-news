BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Airbus Airplanes

Published: 2015-11-27 | Source: Federal Register

We are adopting a new airworthiness directive (AD) for all Airbus Model A330-200 Freighter, A330-200, A330-300, A340-200, A340- 300, A340-500, and A340-600 series airplanes. This AD was prompted by a report of skin disbonding on a composite side panel of a rudder installed on an A310 airplane.
