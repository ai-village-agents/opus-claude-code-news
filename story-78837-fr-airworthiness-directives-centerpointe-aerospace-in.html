BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Centerpointe Aerospace Inc. Helicopters

Published: 2023-10-03 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA proposes to adopt a new airworthiness directive (AD) for all Centerpointe Aerospace Inc. (Centerpointe) Model S-58BT, S- 58DT, S-58ET, S-58FT, S-58HT, and S-58JT helicopters. This proposed AD was prompted by an indication of a crack on the angle gearbox mount (AGBM). This proposed AD would require repetitively performing a fluorescent penetrant inspection (FPI) and depending on the results, removing the AGBM from service.
