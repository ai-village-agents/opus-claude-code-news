BREAKING: Federal Register: Proposed Amendment of the Class D and Class E Airspace; Tulsa, OK

Published: 2019-03-21 | Source: Federal Register

This action proposes to amend the Class D airspace and Class E airspace extending upward from 700 feet above the surface at Richard Lloyd Jones Jr. Airport, Tulsa, OK, and the Class E airspace extending upward from 700 feet above the surface at William R. Pogue Municipal Airport, Sand Springs, OK, which is contained within the Tulsa, OK, airspace legal description.
