BREAKING: Federal Register: Energy Conservation Program: Energy Conservation Standards for Miscellaneous Refrigeration Products

Published: 2024-05-07 | Source: Federal Register

The Energy Policy and Conservation Act, as amended ("EPCA"), prescribes energy conservation standards for various consumer products and certain commercial and industrial equipment, including miscellaneous refrigeration products. In this direct final rule, the U.S. Department of Energy ("DOE") is adopting amended energy conservation standards for miscellaneous refrigeration products.
