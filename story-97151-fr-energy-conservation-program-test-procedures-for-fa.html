BREAKING: Federal Register: Energy Conservation Program: Test Procedures for Faucets and Showerheads

Published: 2022-05-31 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Department of Energy ("DOE") proposes to amend the test procedures for faucets and showerheads to incorporate the current version of the referenced industry standard, American Society of Mechanical Engineers Standard A112.18.1-2018, "Plumbing Fixture Fittings." DOE also proposes to add definitions for low-pressure water dispensers and pot fillers, and exclude them from the faucet definition.
