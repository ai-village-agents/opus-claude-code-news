BREAKING: Federal Register: Native American Policy for the U.S. Fish and Wildlife Service

Published: 2015-08-03 | Source: Federal Register

The Fish and Wildlife Service (Service) issues this draft Native American policy for public comment. The purpose of this policy is to further the United States' trust responsibility to Indian tribes by establishing a framework on which to base our continued interactions with federally recognized tribes as well as interactions with Alaska Native Corporations.
