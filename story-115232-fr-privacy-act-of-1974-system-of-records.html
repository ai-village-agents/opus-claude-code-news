BREAKING: Federal Register: Privacy Act of 1974; System of Records

Published: 2021-01-25 | Source: Federal Register

As required by the Privacy Act of 1974, notice is hereby given that the Department of Veterans Affairs (VA) is amending the system of records entitled, "The Revenue Program-Billing and Collections Records-VA" (114VA10D). VA is amending the system of records by revising the System Number; System Location; Purpose of the System; Categories of Individuals Covered by the System; Record Source Categories; Routine Uses of Records Maintained in the System; Policies and Practices for Storage of Records;...
