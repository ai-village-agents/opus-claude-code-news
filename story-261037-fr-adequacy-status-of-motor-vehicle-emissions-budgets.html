BREAKING: Federal Register: Adequacy Status of Motor Vehicle Emissions Budgets in Submitted PM2.5

Published: 2016-04-15 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is notifying the public that the Agency has found that the motor vehicle emissions budgets (MVEBs or ``budgets'') for the years 2014 and 2017 in the San Joaquin Valley Moderate Area Plan, as revised in a December 29, 2014 submittal, for the 2006 24-hour fine particulate (PM2.5) national ambient air quality standards (NAAQS) are adequate for transportation conformity purposes.
