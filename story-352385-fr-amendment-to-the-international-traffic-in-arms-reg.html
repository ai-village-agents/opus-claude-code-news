BREAKING: Federal Register: Amendment to the International Traffic in Arms Regulations: Initial Implementation of Export Control Reform

Published: 2013-04-16 | Source: Federal Register

As part of the President's Export Control Reform (ECR) effort, the Department of State is amending the International Traffic in Arms Regulations (ITAR) to revise four U.S Munitions List (USML) categories and provide new definitions and other changes. Additionally, policies and procedures regarding the licensing of items moving from the export jurisdiction of the Department of State to the Department of Commerce are provided.
