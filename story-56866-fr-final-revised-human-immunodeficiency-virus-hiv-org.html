BREAKING: Federal Register: Final Revised Human Immunodeficiency Virus (HIV) Organ Policy Equity Act Safeguards and Research Criteria for Transplantation of Organs From Donors With HIV

Published: 2024-12-30 | Source: Federal Register

Kidney and liver transplants from donors with HIV no longer require institutional review board (IRB)-approved research protocols or compliance with HHS research criteria per a November 27, 2024, final rule.
