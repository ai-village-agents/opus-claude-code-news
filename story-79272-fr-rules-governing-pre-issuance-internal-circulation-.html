BREAKING: Federal Register: Rules Governing Pre-Issuance Internal Circulation and Review of Decisions Within the Patent Trial and Appeal Board

Published: 2023-10-06 | Source: Federal Register

The United States Patent and Trademark Office ("USPTO" or "Office") proposes regulations to govern the pre-issuance circulation and review of decisions within the Patent Trial and Appeal Board ("PTAB" or "Board"). The Office proposes these provisions to refine the current interim process in light of stakeholder feedback received in response to a Request for Comments (RFC).
