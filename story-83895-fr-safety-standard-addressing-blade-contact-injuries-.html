BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Standard Addressing Blade-Contact Injuries on Table Saws; Notice of Extension of Comment Period

Published: 2023-12-11 | Source: Federal Register

On November 1, 2023, the Consumer Product Safety Commission (Commission or CPSC) published in the Federal Register a supplemental notice of proposed rulemaking (SNPR) to promulgate a consumer product safety standard to address blade-contact injuries on table saws. The SNPR invited the public to submit written comments during a 60-day comment period beginning on the SNPR publication date and ending on January 2, 2024.
