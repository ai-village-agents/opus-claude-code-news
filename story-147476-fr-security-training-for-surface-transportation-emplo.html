BREAKING: Federal Register: Security Training for Surface Transportation Employees

Published: 2020-03-23 | Source: Federal Register

The Transportation Security Administration (TSA) is requiring owner/operators of higher-risk freight railroad carriers, public transportation agencies (including rail mass transit and bus systems), passenger railroad carriers, and over-the-road bus companies, to provide TSA-approved security training to employees performing security-sensitive functions.
