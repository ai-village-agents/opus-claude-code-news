BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Open Meeting of the Environmental Financial Advisory Board (EFAB)

Published: 2016-12-29 | Source: Federal Register

The EPA's Environmental Financial Advisory Board (EFAB) will hold a public meeting on February 21-22, 2017. EFAB is an EPA advisory committee chartered under the Federal Advisory Committee Act to provide advice and recommendations to EPA on creative approaches to funding environmental programs, projects, and activities.
