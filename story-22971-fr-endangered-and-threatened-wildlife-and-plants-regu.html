BREAKING: Federal Register: Endangered and Threatened Wildlife and Plants; Regulations for Designating Critical Habitat

Published: 2025-11-21 | Source: Federal Register

We, the U.S. Fish and Wildlife Service (FWS or the Service), propose to amend portions of our regulations for section 4 of the Endangered Species Act of 1973, as amended (Act or ESA). Specifically, we propose to revise regulations related to section 4(b)(2) of the Act.
