BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Dowty Propellers Constant Speed Propellers

Published: 2014-03-19 | Source: Federal Register

We propose to revise airworthiness directive (AD) 2010-17- 11R1, which applies to all Dowty Propellers R408/6-123-F/17 model propellers. AD 2010-17-11R1 requires initial and repetitive application of sealant between the bus bar assembly and the backplate assembly of certain line-replaceable units (LRUs).
