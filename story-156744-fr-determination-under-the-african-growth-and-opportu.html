BREAKING: Federal Register: Determination Under the African Growth and Opportunity Act (AGOA)

Published: 2020-07-21 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Trade Representative has determined that Mali has adopted an effective visa system and related procedures to prevent the unlawful transshipment of textile and apparel articles and the use of counterfeit documents in connection with the shipment of such articles, and has implemented and follows, or is making substantial progress towards implementing and following, the custom procedures required by the African Growth and Opportunity Act (AGOA).
