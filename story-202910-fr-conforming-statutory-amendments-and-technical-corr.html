BREAKING: Federal Register: Conforming Statutory Amendments and Technical Corrections to Small Business Government Contracting Regulations

Published: 2018-03-26 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Small Business Administration (SBA or Agency) is amending its regulations to incorporate a provision of the National Defense Authorization Act of 2018 (NDAA 2018) and to update and provide several technical corrections to SBA's regulations. Specifically, the NDAA 2018 amended the Small Business Act by replacing fixed dollar amount thresholds with references to the micro-purchase and simplified acquisition thresholds.
