BREAKING: Federal Register: Radio Broadcasting Services; Custer, Michigan

Published: 2014-09-08 | Source: Federal Register

The Audio Division, at the request of Roy E. Henderson, substitutes FM Channel 260A for FM Channel 263A as the vacant allotment at Custer, Michigan. Channel 260A can be allotted at Custer, consistent with the minimum distance separation requirements of the Commission's rules, at coordinates 43-58-16 NL and 86-19-42 WL.
