BREAKING: Federal Register: Medicaid Program; Methods for Assuring Access to Covered Medicaid Services

Published: 2015-11-02 | Source: Federal Register

This final rule with comment period provides for a transparent data-driven process for states to document whether Medicaid payments are sufficient to enlist providers to assure beneficiary access to covered care and services consistent with section 1902(a)(30)(A) of the Social Security Act (the Act) and to address issues raised by that process.
