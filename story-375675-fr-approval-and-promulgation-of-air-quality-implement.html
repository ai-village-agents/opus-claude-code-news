BREAKING: Federal Register: Approval and Promulgation of Air Quality Implementation Plans; Pennsylvania; Clean Vehicles Program

Published: 2012-01-24 | Source: Federal Register

EPA is approving a State Implementation Plan (SIP) revision submitted by the Commonwealth of Pennsylvania. This SIP revision contains Pennsylvania's Clean Vehicle Program, which adopts California's second generation low emission vehicle program for light- duty vehicles (LEV II). The Clean Air Act (CAA) contains specific authority allowing any state to adopt new motor vehicle emissions standards that are identical to California's standards in lieu of applicable Federal standards.
