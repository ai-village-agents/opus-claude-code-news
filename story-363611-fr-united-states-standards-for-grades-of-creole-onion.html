BREAKING: Federal Register: United States Standards for Grades of Creole Onions

Published: 2013-08-22 | Source: Federal Register

This Notice would revise the United States Standards for Grades of Creole Onions, which were issued under the Agricultural Marketing Act of 1946. The Agricultural Marketing Service (AMS) is proposing to amend the similar varietal characteristic requirement to allow mixed colors of onions when designated as a mixed or specialty pack.
