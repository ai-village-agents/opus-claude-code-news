BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Public Meeting of the Minnesota Advisory Committee; Cancellation

Published: 2024-04-09 | Source: Federal Register

The Commission on Civil Rights published a notice in the Federal Register concerning a meeting of the Minnesota Advisory Committee. The meeting, scheduled for Wednesday, April 10, 2024, at 1:00 p.m. CT, has been cancelled. The notice is in the Federal Register on Tuesday, February 20, 2024, in FR Document Number 2024-03390 on page 12821.
