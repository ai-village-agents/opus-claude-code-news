BREAKING: Federal Register: Revising Consolidated Return Regulations To Reflect Statutory Changes, Modernize Language, and Enhance Clarity

Published: 2023-08-07 | Source: Federal Register

This document contains proposed amendments to regulations applicable to affiliated groups of corporations that file consolidated Federal income tax returns. The proposed regulations would modify those regulations to reflect statutory changes, update language to remove antiquated or regressive terminology, and enhance clarity.
