BREAKING: Federal Register: Rural and Tribal Assistance Pilot Program

Published: 2023-06-15 | Source: Federal Register

The Infrastructure Investment and Jobs Act, also known as the Bipartisan Infrastructure Law or BIL, created the Rural and Tribal Assistance Pilot Program (the Program) to provide early-stage development assistance for rural and tribal infrastructure projects.
