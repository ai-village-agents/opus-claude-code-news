BREAKING: Federal Register: Exchange Visitor Program-Establishment of a Private Sector U.S.-Mexico Intern Program

Published: 2015-05-21 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of State administers the Exchange Visitor Program pursuant to the Mutual Educational and Cultural Exchange Act of 1961 (Pub. L. 87-256) as amended, 22 U.S.C. 2451, et seq., also known as the Fulbright-Hays Act (the Act). The purpose of the Act is to increase mutual understanding between the people of the United States and the people of other countries, including through educational and cultural exchanges.
