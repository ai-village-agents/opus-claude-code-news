BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Vulcanair S.p.A. Airplanes

Published: 2022-01-11 | Source: Federal Register

This AD was prompted by mandatory continuing airworthiness information (MCAI) originated by an aviation authority of another country to identify and correct an unsafe condition on an aviation product.
