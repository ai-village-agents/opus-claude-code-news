BREAKING: Federal Register: Health and Human Services Acquisition Regulation

Published: 2013-01-10 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Health and Human Services (HHS) is proposing to amend its Federal Acquisition Regulation (FAR) Supplement--the HHS Acquisition Regulation (HHSAR)--to add two clauses, "Patent Rights-- Exceptional Circumstances" and "Rights in Data--Exceptional Circumstances," and their prescriptions.
