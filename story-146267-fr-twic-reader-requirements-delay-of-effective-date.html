BREAKING: Federal Register: TWIC-Reader Requirements; Delay of Effective Date

Published: 2020-03-09 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is delaying the effective date for three categories of facilities affected by the final rule entitled, "Transportation Worker Identification Credential (TWIC)--Reader Requirements," published in the Federal Register on August 23, 2016. These three categories are: Facilities that handle certain dangerous cargoes in bulk, but do not transfer these cargoes to or from a vessel; facilities that handle certain dangerous cargoes in bulk, and do transfer these cargoes to or from a vessel...
