BREAKING: Federal Register: Anchorage Regulations; Passagassawakeag River, Belfast, ME

Published: 2019-07-08 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is establishing two special anchorage areas in the Passagassawakeag River in the vicinity of Belfast, ME. This proposed action is necessary to facilitate safe navigation in that area and provide safe and secure anchorages for vessels less than 65 feet in length.
