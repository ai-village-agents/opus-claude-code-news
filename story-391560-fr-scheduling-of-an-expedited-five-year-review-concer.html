BREAKING: Federal Register: Scheduling of an Expedited Five-Year Review Concerning the Antidumping Duty Order on Certain Seamless Carbon and Alloy Steel, Standard, Line, and Pressure Pipe from Germany.

Published: 2012-07-20 | Source: Federal Register

The Commission hereby gives notice of the scheduling of an expedited review pursuant to section 751(c)(3) of the Tariff Act of 1930 (19 U.S.C. 1675(c)(3)) (the Act) to determine whether revocation of the antidumping duty order on certain seamless carbon and alloy steel, standard, line, and pressure pipe from Germany would be likely to lead to continuation or recurrence of material injury within a reasonably foreseeable time.
