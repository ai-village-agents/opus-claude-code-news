BREAKING: Federal Register: North American Free Trade Agreement; Invitation for Applications for Inclusion on the Chapter 19 Roster

Published: 2012-10-22 | Source: Federal Register

Chapter 19 of the North American Free Trade Agreement ("NAFTA") provides for the establishment of a roster of individuals to serve on binational panels convened to review final determinations in antidumping or countervailing duty ("AD/CVD") proceedings and amendments to AD/CVD statutes of a NAFTA Party.
