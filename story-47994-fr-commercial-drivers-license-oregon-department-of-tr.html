BREAKING: Federal Register: Commercial Driver's License: Oregon Department of Transportation; Application for Exemption

Published: 2024-09-25 | Source: Federal Register

FMCSA announces its decision to grant a five-year exemption to the Oregon Department of Transportation (Oregon) from the commercial driver's license (CDL) rules concerning acceptable proof of citizenship or lawful permanent residency for citizens of Freely Associated States (FAS) who reside in Oregon and wish to obtain a CDL or commercial learner's permit (CLP).
