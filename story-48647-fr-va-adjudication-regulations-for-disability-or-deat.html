BREAKING: Federal Register: VA Adjudication Regulations for Disability or Death Benefit Claims Based on Toxic Exposure

Published: 2024-10-01 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Veterans Affairs (VA) is proposing to amend its adjudication regulations to implement provisions of the Sergeant First Class Heath Robinson Honoring our Promise to Address Comprehensive Toxics Act of 2022 (PACT Act or Act). The statute amended procedures applicable to claims based on toxic exposure and modified or established presumptions of service connection related to toxic exposure.
