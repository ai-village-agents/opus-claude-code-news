BREAKING: Federal Register: Special Local Regulation; Lake Havasu, Lake Havasu City, AZ

Published: 2024-10-07 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is establishing a special local regulation in the navigable waters of the Bridgewater Channel, Lake Havasu, AZ during the Annual Bridgewater Channel Underwater Cleanup marine event. This regulation is necessary to provide for the safety of the participants, crew, supporting vessels, and general users of the waterway during the event, which will be held on October 19, 2024.
