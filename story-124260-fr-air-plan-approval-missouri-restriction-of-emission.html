BREAKING: Federal Register: Air Plan Approval; Missouri; Restriction of Emissions From Lithographic and Letterpress Printing Operations

Published: 2021-05-21 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is proposing to approve revisions to the Missouri State Implementation Plan (SIP) received on November 10, 2020. The submission revises a Missouri regulation that restricts volatile organic compound emissions from lithographic and letterpress printing operations in the St. Louis Metropolitan Area.
