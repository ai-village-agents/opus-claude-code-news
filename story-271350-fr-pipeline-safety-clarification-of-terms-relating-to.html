BREAKING: Federal Register: Pipeline Safety: Clarification of Terms Relating to Pipeline Operational Status

Published: 2016-08-16 | Source: Federal Register

PHMSA is issuing this advisory bulletin to all owners and operators (operators) of hazardous liquid, carbon dioxide, and gas pipelines, as defined in 49 Code of Federal Regulations Parts 192 and 195, to clarify the regulatory requirements that may vary depending on the operational status of a pipeline. Further, this advisory bulletin identifies regulatory requirements operators must follow for the abandonment of pipelines.
