BREAKING: Federal Register: FDIC Official Sign and Advertising Requirements, False Advertising, Misrepresentation of Insured Status, and Misuse of the FDIC's Name or Logo; Extension of Comment Period

Published: 2023-02-01 | Source: Federal Register

On December 21, 2022, the FDIC published in the Federal Register a Notice of Proposed Rulemaking (NPR) seeking comment on proposed changes to the FDIC's regulations relating to the FDIC's official sign, the FDIC's official advertising statement, and misrepresentations of deposit insurance coverage. The NPR provided for a 60-day comment period, which would have closed on February 21, 2023.
