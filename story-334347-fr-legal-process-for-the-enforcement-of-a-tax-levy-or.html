BREAKING: Federal Register: Legal Process for the Enforcement of a Tax Levy or Criminal Restitution Order Against a Participant Account

Published: 2014-09-10 | Source: Federal Register

The Federal Retirement Thrift Investment Board (Agency) proposes to amend its regulations to explain the Board's procedures for responding to tax levies and criminal restitution orders that comply with the statutory requirements.
