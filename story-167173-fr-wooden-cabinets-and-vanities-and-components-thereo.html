BREAKING: Federal Register: Wooden Cabinets and Vanities and Components Thereof From the People's Republic of China: Initiation of Antidumping Duty New Shipper Review

Published: 2020-12-01 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Commerce (Commerce) has determined that a request for a new shipper review (NSR) of the antidumping duty order on wooden cabinets and vanities and components thereof (wooden cabinets and vanities) from the People's Republic of China (China) meets the statutory and regulatory requirements for initiation. The period of review (POR) for the NSR is April 1, 2020 through September 30, 2020.
