BREAKING: Federal Register: National Environmental Policy Act Categorical Exclusions

Published: 2014-04-29 | Source: Federal Register

The First Responder Network Authority (FirstNet) publishes this notice of its categorical exclusions (CEs) of actions that FirstNet has determined do not individually or cumulatively have a significant effect on the human environment and, thus, should be categorically excluded from the requirement to prepare an environmental assessment or environmental impact statement under the National Environmental Policy Act (NEPA).
