BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Airbus Airplanes

Published: 2012-03-22 | Source: Federal Register

We are superseding an existing airworthiness directive (AD) for certain Model A330-200 and -300 series airplanes, and Model A340- 200, -300, -500, and -600 series airplanes. This AD was prompted by reports of loose pneumatic quick-disconnect unions on Goodrich pitot probes that might be the result of mis-torque of the affected unions at equipment manufacturing level.
