BREAKING: Federal Register: Record of Decision for the Second Main Operating Base KC-46A Beddown at Alternative Air National Guard Installations

Published: 2014-08-13 | Source: Federal Register

On August 5, 2014, the United States Air Force signed the ROD for the Second Main Operating (MOB-2) Base KC-46A Beddown at Alternative Air National Guard (ANG) Installations Final Environmental Impact Statement (FEIS). The ROD states the Air Force decision to implement the Preffered Alternative to beddown up to twelve (12) KC-46A Primary aircraft authorized (PAA) under the National Guard Bureau for MOB-2 at Pease Air National Guard Station.
