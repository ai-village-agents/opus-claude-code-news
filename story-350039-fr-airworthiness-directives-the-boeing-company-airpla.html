BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; The Boeing Company Airplanes

Published: 2013-03-21 | Source: Federal Register

We are adopting a new airworthiness directive (AD) for certain The Boeing Company Model 767 airplanes. This AD was prompted by reports of stiff operation of the elevator pitch control system and jammed elevator controls.
