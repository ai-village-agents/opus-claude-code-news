BREAKING: Federal Register: Final Action Under the NIH Guidelines for Research Involving Recombinant or Synthetic Nucleic Acid Molecules (NIH Guidelines)

Published: 2024-04-05 | Source: Federal Register

This notice sets forth final changes to NIH Guidelines for Research Involving Recombinant or Synthetic Nucleic Acid Molecules (NIH Guidelines) as initially outlined in a Federal Register notice issued on August 10, 2023. Following solicitation of public comments, the NIH is amending the NIH Guidelines to include specific considerations and requirements for conducting research involving gene drive modified organisms (GDMOs) in contained research settings.
