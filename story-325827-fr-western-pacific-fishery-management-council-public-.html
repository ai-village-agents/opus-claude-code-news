BREAKING: Federal Register: Western Pacific Fishery Management Council; Public Meetings

Published: 2014-06-02 | Source: Federal Register

The Western Pacific Fishery Management Council (Council) will hold meetings of its 116th Scientific and Statistical Committee (SSC) and its 160th Council meeting to take actions on fishery management issues in the Western Pacific Region.
