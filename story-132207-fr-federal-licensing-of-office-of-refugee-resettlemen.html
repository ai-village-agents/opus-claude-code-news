BREAKING: Federal Register: Federal Licensing of Office of Refugee Resettlement Facilities Request for Information

Published: 2021-09-03 | Source: Federal Register

The Unaccompanied Children (UC) Program is responsible for the administration of childcare facilities throughout the country that care for unaccompanied children arriving in the United States prior to those children being placed with viable sponsors in the United States. To inform a strategic and impactful plan for the administration of these facilities HHS is issuing this Request for Information (RFI).
