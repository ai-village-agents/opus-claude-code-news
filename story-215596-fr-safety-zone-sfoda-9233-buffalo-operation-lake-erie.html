BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone; SFODA 9233 Buffalo Operation; Lake Erie, Hamburg, NY

Published: 2018-09-05 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is establishing a temporary safety zone for navigable waters within a 500-foot radius of the water insertion site located offshore of Hamburg Beach/Town Park, Lake Erie, Hamburg, NY. This safety zone is intended to restrict vessels from portions of Lake Erie during the SFODA 9233 Buffalo Operation.
