BREAKING: Federal Register: Proposed Amendment of the Class E Airspace; Mexia, TX

Published: 2022-04-11 | Source: Federal Register

This action proposes to amend the Class E airspace at Mexia, TX. The FAA is proposing this action due to an airspace review conducted as part of the decommissioning of the Mexia non-directional beacon (NDB). The geographic coordinates of the airport would also be updated to coincide with the FAA's aeronautical database.
