BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zones; Recurring Events in Captain of the Port Duluth Zone

Published: 2017-03-31 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard proposes to amend its safety zones regulations for annual events in the Captain of the Port Duluth Zone. This final rule would update the locations for seven safety zones, add two new safety zones, increase the safety zone radius of six existing fireworks events, and modify the format of the regulation to list the annual events and corresponding safety zones in table form.
