BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Intent to Grant Exclusive Patent License; Harvest Optimization LLC

Published: 2014-02-10 | Source: Federal Register

Notice is hereby given to an intent to grant to Harvest Optimization LLC of Rigby, Idaho, an exclusive license to practice the inventions described in U.S. Patent No. 7,311,013 entitled "Complex Pendulum Biomass Sensor" and U.S. Patent No. 8,469,784 entitled "Autonomous Grain Combine Control System." The inventions are owned by the United States of America, as represented by the U.S. Department of Energy (DOE).
