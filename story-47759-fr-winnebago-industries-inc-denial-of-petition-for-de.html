BREAKING: Federal Register: Winnebago Industries, Inc., Denial of Petition for Decision of Inconsequential Noncompliance

Published: 2024-09-23 | Source: Federal Register

Winnebago Industries, Inc., (Winnebago or petitioner), has determined that certain model year (MY) 2013-2023 Winnebago motorhomes do not fully comply with Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard (FMVSS) No. 108, Lamps, Reflective Devices, and Associated Equipment. Winnebago filed a noncompliance report dated November 11, 2022, and amended the report on December 2, 2022, and May 17, 2023.
