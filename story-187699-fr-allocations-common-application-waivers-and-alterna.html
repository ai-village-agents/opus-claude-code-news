BREAKING: Federal Register: Allocations, Common Application, Waivers, and Alternative Requirements for Community Development Block Grant Mitigation Grantees

Published: 2019-08-30 | Source: Federal Register

This notice allocates $6.875 billion in Community Development Block Grant Mitigation (CDBG-MIT) funds to grantees recovering from qualifying 2015, 2016, and 2017 disasters. Funds allocated by this notice were made available by the Further Additional Supplemental Appropriations for Disaster Relief Requirements Act, 2018 (approved February 9, 2018) (the "Appropriations Act").
