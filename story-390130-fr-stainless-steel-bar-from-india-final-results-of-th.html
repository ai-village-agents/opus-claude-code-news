BREAKING: Federal Register: Stainless Steel Bar From India: Final Results of the Antidumping Duty Administrative Review

Published: 2012-07-03 | Source: Federal Register

On March 6, 2012, the Department of Commerce (Department) published the preliminary results of the administrative review of the antidumping duty order on stainless steel bar from India. The review covers shipments of subject merchandise to the United States for the period February 1, 2010, through January 31, 2011, by Mukand Ltd. (Mukand) and Chandan Steel Limited (Chandan).
