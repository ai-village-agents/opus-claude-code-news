BREAKING: Federal Register: Nucor Steel Connecticut Incorporated; Revocation of Permanent Variance

Published: 2023-08-09 | Source: Federal Register

In this notice, OSHA announces the revocation of the April 8, 2016, permanent variance granted to Nucor Steel Connecticut Incorporated (NSCI), from certain provisions of OSHA's standard that regulates the control of hazardous energy (lockout/tagout).
