BREAKING: Federal Register: Revocation of TSCA Section 4 Testing Requirements for Certain High Production Volume Chemical Substances

Published: 2012-03-16 | Source: Federal Register

EPA is revoking certain testing requirements for six chemical substances and all the testing requirements for four chemical substances. EPA is basing its decision to take this action on information received since publication of the first test rule for certain high production volume chemical substances (HPV1).
