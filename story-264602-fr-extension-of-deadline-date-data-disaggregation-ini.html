BREAKING: Federal Register: Extension of Deadline Date; Data Disaggregation Initiative Program

Published: 2016-05-26 | Source: Federal Register

On May 4, 2016, we published in the Federal Register (81 FR 26780) a notice inviting applications for new awards for fiscal year (FY) 2016 for the Data Disaggregation Initiative Program. The notice established July 5, 2016, as the deadline date for eligible applicants to apply for funding under the program.
