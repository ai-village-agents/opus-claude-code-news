BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zones; Tug Nancy Anne, Jack-Up Barge JUB-100, and M/V Highland Eagle Operating in the Straits of Mackinac, MI

Published: 2019-06-20 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is establishing two temporary safety zones in the Captain of the Port, Sault Sainte Marie zone. These temporary safety zones are necessary to protect the public, and contractors from potential hazards associated with geotechnical sampling by persons conducting bore hole drilling in the Straits of Mackinac.
