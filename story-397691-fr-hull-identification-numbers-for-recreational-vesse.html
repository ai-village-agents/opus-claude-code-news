BREAKING: Federal Register: Hull Identification Numbers for Recreational Vessels

Published: 2012-09-28 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard announces that it is requesting public comments regarding the existing regulatory requirement to indicate a boat's model year as part of the 12-character Hull Identification Number (HIN). Under current regulations in 33 CFR part 181, the HIN must consist of 12 characters, the last two of which indicate the boat's model year.
