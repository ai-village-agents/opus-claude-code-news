BREAKING: Federal Register: Codex Alimentarius Commission: Meeting of the Codex Committee on Pesticide Residues

Published: 2022-03-02 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Codex Office is sponsoring a public meeting on June 2, 2022. The objective of the public meeting is to provide information and receive public comments on agenda items and draft United States (U.S.) positions to be discussed at the 53rd Session of the Codex Committee on Pesticide Residues (CCPR) of the Codex Alimentarius Commission (CAC), which will convene virtually, July 4-July 13, 2022.
