BREAKING: Federal Register: Presidential Declaration of a Major Disaster for Public Assistance Only for the State of Washington

Published: 2022-04-07 | Source: Federal Register

This is a Notice of the Presidential declaration of a major disaster for Public Assistance Only for the State of Washington (FEMA- 4650-DR), dated 03/29/2022. Incident: Severe Winter Storms, Straight-line Winds, Flooding, Landslides, and Mudslides. Incident Period: 01/01/2022 through 01/15/2022.
