BREAKING: Federal Register: Report on Carcinogens Evaluation of Pentachlorophenol; Request for Nominations of Scientific Experts

Published: 2012-10-17 | Source: Federal Register

The National Toxicology Program (NTP), Division of the National Toxicology Program (DNTP), National Institute of Environmental Health Sciences (NIEHS), is initiating development of the Report on Carcinogens (RoC) monograph on pentachlorophenol.
