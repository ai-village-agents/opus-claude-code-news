BREAKING: Federal Register: Designation of 4-Piperidone as a List I Chemical

Published: 2022-09-22 | Source: Federal Register

The Drug Enforcement Administration is proposing the control of 4-piperidone, its acetals, its amides, its carbamates, its salts, and salts of its acetals, its amides, and its carbamates, whenever the existence of such is possible, as a list I chemical under the Controlled Substances Act.
