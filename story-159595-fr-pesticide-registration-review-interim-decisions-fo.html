BREAKING: Federal Register: Pesticide Registration Review; Interim Decisions for Several Pesticides; Notice of Availability

Published: 2020-08-26 | Source: Federal Register

This notice announces the availability of EPA's interim registration review decisions for the following chemicals: 2,4-DP-p, Aliphatic alcohols C6-C16, Alkylbenzene Sulfonates (ABS), Bacillus pumilus, Bromacil, Dimethyl Disulfide (DMDS), Fatty Acid Monoesters with Glycerol or Propanediol, Harpin Proteins, Linuron, Pyroxsulam, Thiencarbazone-methyl (TCM).
