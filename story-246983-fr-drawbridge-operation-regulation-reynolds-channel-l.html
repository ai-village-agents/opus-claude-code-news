BREAKING: Federal Register: Drawbridge Operation Regulation; Reynolds Channel, Lawrence, NY

Published: 2017-10-24 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is modifying the operating schedule that governs the Atlantic Beach Bridge across Reynolds Channel, mile 0.4 at Lawrence, New York. This action is necessary to allow for an unexpected delay in the reconstruction and painting of the bascule leaves. A temporary deviation was previously granted for a length of 180 days.
