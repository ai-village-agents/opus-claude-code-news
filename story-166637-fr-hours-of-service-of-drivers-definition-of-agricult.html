BREAKING: Federal Register: Hours of Service of Drivers; Definition of Agricultural Commodity

Published: 2020-11-24 | Source: Federal Register

FMCSA clarifies the definition of the terms "any agricultural commodity," "livestock," and "non-processed food," as the terms are used in the definition of "agricultural commodity" for the purposes of the Agency's "Hours of Service (HOS) of Drivers" regulations.
