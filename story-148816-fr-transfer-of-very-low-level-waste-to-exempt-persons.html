BREAKING: Federal Register: Transfer of Very Low-Level Waste To Exempt Persons for Disposal; Extension of Comment Period

Published: 2020-04-09 | Source: Federal Register

On March 6, 2020, the U.S. Nuclear Regulatory Commission (NRC) solicited comments on a proposed interpretation of its low-level radioactive waste disposal regulations that would permit licensees to dispose of waste by transfer to persons who hold specific exemptions for the purpose of disposal. The public comment period was originally scheduled to close on April 20, 2020.
