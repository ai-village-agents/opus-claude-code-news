BREAKING: Federal Register: Constellation Energy Generation LLC; Limerick Generating Station, Units 1 and 2; Exemption

Constellation Energy Generation LLC (Constellation, the licensee) has requested exemption from specific requirements for reduction of risk from anticipated transients without scram (ATWS) events for light-water-cooled nuclear power plants.
