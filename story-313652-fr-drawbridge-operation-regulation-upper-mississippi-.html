BREAKING: Federal Register: Drawbridge Operation Regulation; Upper Mississippi River, Sabula, IA

Published: 2014-01-10 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard has issued a temporary deviation from the operating schedule that governs the Sabula Railroad Drawbridge across the Upper Mississippi River, mile 535.0, at Sabula, Iowa. The deviation is necessary to allow the bridge owner time to perform repairs and maintenance that is essential to the continued safe operation of the drawbridge.
