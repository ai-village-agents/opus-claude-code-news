BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Proposed Revisions for the LSC Grant Assurances for Calendar Year 2017 Basic Field Grants

Published: 2016-04-05 | Source: Federal Register

The Legal Services Corporation ("LSC") intends to revise the LSC Grant Assurances for calendar year 2017 Basic Field Grants and is soliciting public comment on the proposed changes. The proposed revisions affect Grant Assurances 7, 15, 20, and 22. The proposed LSC Grant Assurances for calendar year 2017 Basic Field Grants, in redline format indicating the proposed changes to the current "LSC 2016 Grant Assurances," are available at http://grants.lsc.gov/sites/default/ files/Grants/ReferenceMater...
