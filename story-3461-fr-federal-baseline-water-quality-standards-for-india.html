BREAKING: Federal Register: Federal Baseline Water Quality Standards for Indian Reservations; Withdrawal of Proposed Rule

Published: 2025-01-10 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (the EPA or agency) is withdrawing the proposed rule entitled "Federal Baseline Water Quality Standards for Indian Reservations," which published in the Federal Register on May 5, 2023. The EPA is electing to withdraw and not finalize the proposed rule at this time.
