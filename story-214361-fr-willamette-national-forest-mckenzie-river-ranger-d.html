BREAKING: Federal Register: Willamette National Forest, McKenzie River Ranger District; Oregon; Flat Country Project

Published: 2018-08-20 | Source: Federal Register

The Flat Country Project is located on the western slope of the Cascades, extending from Scott Mountain to the upper reach of the McKenzie River, eight miles east of McKenzie Bridge, Oregon. The project area is approximately 74,063 acres. There is a high amount of mid-seral stands, moving the seral distribution away from historic levels and limiting stand structure and species diversity across the landscape.
