BREAKING: Federal Register: Enhancing Participation in NRC Public Meetings

Published: 2016-08-31 | Source: Federal Register

To further clarify and enhance participation in public meetings conducted by the U.S. Nuclear Regulatory Commission (NRC), the NRC is proposing to revise its public meeting policy. The revised policy statement redefines the three categories of public meetings and identifies the level of public participation offered at each type of meeting.
