In accordance with Section 743 of Division C of the Consolidated Appropriations Act of 2010, Public Law 111-117, Department of Transportation is publishing this notice to advise the public of the availability of the FY 14 Service Contract Inventory and the FY 13 Service Contract Inventory Analysis Report.
