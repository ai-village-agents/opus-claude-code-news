BREAKING: Federal Register: Bulk Manufacturer of Controlled Substances Applications: Bulk Manufacturers of Marihuana

Published: 2019-08-27 | Source: Federal Register

The Drug Enforcement Administration (DEA) is providing notice of certain applications it has received from entities applying to be registered to manufacture in bulk a basic class of controlled substances listed in schedule I. Prior to making decisions on these pending applications, DEA intends to promulgate regulations that govern the program of growing marihuana for scientific and medical research under DEA registration.
