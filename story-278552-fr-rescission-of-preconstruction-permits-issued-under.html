BREAKING: Federal Register: Rescission of Preconstruction Permits Issued Under the Clean Air Act

Published: 2016-11-07 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is promulgating amendments to the EPA's federal Prevention of Significant Deterioration (PSD) regulations to remove a date restriction from the Permit Rescission provision. Other than removing the date restriction, this final rule does not alter the criteria under which a new source review (NSR) permit may be rescinded.
