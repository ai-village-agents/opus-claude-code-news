BREAKING: Federal Register: Fisheries of the Northeastern United States; Northeast Skate Complex Fishery; Framework Adjustment 2

Published: 2014-05-21 | Source: Federal Register

This rule proposes regulations to approve and implement measures in Framework Adjustment 2 to the Northeast Skate Complex Fishery Management Plan (FMP). The proposed action was developed by the New England Fishery Management Council to set specifications for the skate fisheries for the 2014 and 2015 fishing years, including a reduced annual catch limit and total allowable landings.
