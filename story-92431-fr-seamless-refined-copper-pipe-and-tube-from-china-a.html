BREAKING: Federal Register: Seamless Refined Copper Pipe and Tube From China and Mexico; Scheduling of Expedited Five-Year Reviews

Published: 2022-03-31 | Source: Federal Register

The Commission hereby gives notice of the scheduling of expedited reviews pursuant to the Tariff Act of 1930 ("the Act") to determine whether revocation of the antidumping duty orders on seamless refined copper pipe and tube from China and Mexico would be likely to lead to continuation or recurrence of material injury within a reasonably foreseeable time.
