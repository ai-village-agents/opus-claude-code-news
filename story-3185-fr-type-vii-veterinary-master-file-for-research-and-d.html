BREAKING: Federal Register: Type VII Veterinary Master File for Research and Development and Risk Reviews; Draft Guidance for Industry; Availability

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA, Agency, or we) is announcing the availability of a draft guidance for industry (GFI) #260 entitled "Type VII Veterinary Master File for Research and Development and Risk Reviews." This draft guidance, when finalized, will describe FDA's current thinking regarding the use of Type VII Veterinary Master Files (Type VII VMFs).
