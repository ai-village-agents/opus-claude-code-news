BREAKING: Federal Register: Authority To Solicit Gifts and Donations

Published: 2016-09-22 | Source: Federal Register

On July 11, 2016, the Department of Veterans Affairs (VA) published a direct final rule amending its regulation that governs soliciting contributions from the public by officials and employees of NCA, or authorizing the use of officials' or employees' names, name of the Secretary, or the name of VA for the purpose of making a gift or donation to VA.
