BREAKING: Federal Register: Certain Oil Country Tubular Goods From India: Final Results of Countervailing Duty Administrative Review; 2013-2014

(Jindal SAW). The period of review (POR) is December 23, 2013, through December 31, 2014. On October 14, 2016, the Department published the preliminary results of the administrative review.
