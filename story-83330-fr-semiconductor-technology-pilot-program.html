BREAKING: Federal Register: Semiconductor Technology Pilot Program

Published: 2023-12-01 | Source: Federal Register

The United States Patent and Trademark Office (USPTO) is implementing the Semiconductor Technology Pilot Program, which is designed to accelerate improvements in the semiconductor industry by expediting examination of patent applications for certain semiconductor manufacturing innovations.
