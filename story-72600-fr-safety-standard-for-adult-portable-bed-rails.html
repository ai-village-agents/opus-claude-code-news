BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Standard for Adult Portable Bed Rails

Published: 2023-07-21 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Consumer Product Safety Commission (Commission or CPSC) has determined that there is an unreasonable risk of injury and death associated with entrapment and other hazards from adult portable bed rails (APBRs). CPSC has identified 284 fatal incidents related to entrapment by APBRs between January 2003 and December 2021.
