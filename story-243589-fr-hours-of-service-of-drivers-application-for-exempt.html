BREAKING: Federal Register: Hours of Service of Drivers: Application for Exemption; Power and Communication Contractors Association (PCCA)

Published: 2017-09-14 | Source: Federal Register

FMCSA announces that the Power and Communication Contractors Association (PCCA) has requested an exemption from the requirement that a motor carrier install and require each of its drivers to use an electronic logging device (ELD) to record the driver's hours-of-service (HOS) no later than December 18, 2017.
