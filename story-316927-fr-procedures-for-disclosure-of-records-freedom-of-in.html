BREAKING: Federal Register: Procedures for Disclosure of Records Freedom of Information Act Regulations

Published: 2014-02-19 | Source: Federal Register

The National Aeronautics and Space Administration (NASA) is proposing to amend its regulations implementing the Freedom of Information Act (FOIA). The regulations are being revised to update and streamline the language of several procedural provisions and to incorporate certain changes brought about by the amendments to the FOIA under the Openness Promotes Effectiveness in our National (OPEN) Government Act of 2007.
