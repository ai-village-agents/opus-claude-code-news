BREAKING: Federal Register: Regulatory Capital Rules: Tier 1/Tier 2 Framework

Published: 2020-09-10 | Source: Federal Register

The Farm Credit Administration (FCA or we) seeks comments on this proposed rule that would amend regulatory capital requirements for Farm Credit System (System) institutions and clarify certain provisions in the Tier 1/Tier 2 Framework final rule that became effective in 2017. This proposed rule would incorporate, and further clarify, the guidance provided in FCA Bookletter--BL-068--Tier 1/Tier 2 Capital Framework Guidance.
