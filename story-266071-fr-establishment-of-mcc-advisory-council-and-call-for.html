BREAKING: Federal Register: Establishment of MCC Advisory Council and Call for Nominations

Published: 2016-06-14 | Source: Federal Register

In accordance with the requirements of the Federal Advisory Committee Act, 5 U.S.C.--App., MCC intends to establish the MCC Advisory Council ("Advisory Council"), and is hereby soliciting representative nominations.
