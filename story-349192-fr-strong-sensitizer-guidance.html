BREAKING: Federal Register: Strong Sensitizer Guidance

Published: 2013-03-12 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Consumer Product Safety Commission (CPSC or Commission) is announcing the availability of a document prepared by CPSC staff titled, "Strong Sensitizer Guidance." This guidance document is intended to clarify the "strong sensitizer" definition, assist manufacturers in understanding how CPSC staff would assess whether a substance and/or product containing that substance should be considered a "strong sensitizer," and how the Commission would make such a determination.
