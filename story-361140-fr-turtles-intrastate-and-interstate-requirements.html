BREAKING: Federal Register: Turtles Intrastate and Interstate Requirements

Published: 2013-07-25 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA) is amending its regulations regarding the prohibition on the sale, or other commercial or public distribution, of viable turtle eggs and live turtles with a carapace length of less than 4 inches to remove procedures for destruction as FDA believes it is not necessary to routinely demand this destruction to achieve the purpose of the regulations.
