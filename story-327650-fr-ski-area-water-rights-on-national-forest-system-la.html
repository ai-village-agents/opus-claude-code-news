BREAKING: Federal Register: Ski Area Water Rights on National Forest System Lands

Published: 2014-06-23 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Forest Service (Forest Service or Agency) is proposing to amend its internal directives for ski area concessions by adding two clauses to the Special Uses Handbook, FSH 2709.11, chapter 50, to address water rights necessary for and that primarily support operation of ski areas on National Forest System (NFS) lands.
