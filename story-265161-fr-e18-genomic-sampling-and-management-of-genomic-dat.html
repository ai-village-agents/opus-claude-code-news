BREAKING: Federal Register: E18 Genomic Sampling and Management of Genomic Data; International Council for Harmonisation; Draft Guidance for Industry; Availability

Published: 2016-06-03 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA or Agency) is announcing the availability of a draft guidance entitled "E18 Genomic Sampling and Management of Genomic Data." The draft guidance was prepared under the auspices of the International Council for Harmonisation (ICH), formerly the International Conference on Harmonisation.
