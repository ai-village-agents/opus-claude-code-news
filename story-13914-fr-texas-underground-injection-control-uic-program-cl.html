BREAKING: Federal Register: Texas Underground Injection Control (UIC) Program; Class VI Primacy

Published: 2025-06-17 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Environmental Protection Agency (EPA or the Agency) has received a complete Underground Injection Control (UIC) program revision application from the State of Texas, requesting primary enforcement responsibility (primacy) for Class VI injection wells under Safe Drinking Water Act (SDWA) section 1422.
