BREAKING: Federal Register: Implementation of the Privacy Act of 1974, as Amended; Amendment to Existing System of Records-Pay and Leave Records of Employees

Published: 2014-11-03 | Source: Federal Register

The Department's Office of the Chief Human Capital Officer (OCHCO) is proposing to amend its "Pay and Leave Records of Employees" system of records. The system of records consists of payroll and time and attendance systems for fulfillment of payroll, timekeeping and leave functions.
