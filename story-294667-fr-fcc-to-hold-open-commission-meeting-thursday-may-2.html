BREAKING: Federal Register: FCC To Hold Open Commission Meeting Thursday, May 21, 2015

Published: 2015-05-22 | Source: Federal Register

The Commission will consider an Order to extend the National Deaf-Blind Equipment Distribution Program and a Notice of Proposed Rulemaking to permanently extend the program.
