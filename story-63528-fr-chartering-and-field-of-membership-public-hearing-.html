BREAKING: Federal Register: Chartering and Field of Membership-Public Hearing; Community Charter Application for Area With a Population of 2.5 Million or More

Published: 2023-03-27 | Source: Federal Register

The NCUA will hold a public hearing on a request from Dade County Federal Credit Union, submitted on November 8, 2022, seeking to expand its existing community charter. The American Community Survey estimates the proposed community's population is 4,624,664 people.
