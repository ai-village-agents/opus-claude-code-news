BREAKING: Federal Register: Provisions To Improve the Supplemental Nutrition Assistance Program's Quality Control System

Published: 2023-09-19 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Department of Agriculture (the Department) is issuing this notice of proposed rulemaking to improve the Food and Nutrition Service's (FNS) Supplemental Nutrition Assistance Program (SNAP) quality control (QC) system as required in the Agriculture Improvement Act of 2018 (2018 Farm Bill).
