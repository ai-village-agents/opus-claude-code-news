BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Stemme AG Gliders

Published: 2021-10-08 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA proposes to adopt a new airworthiness directive (AD) for certain Stemme AG Model Stemme S 12 gliders. This proposed AD was prompted by mandatory continuing airworthiness information (MCAI) issued by the aviation authority of another country to identify and correct an unsafe condition on an aviation product.
