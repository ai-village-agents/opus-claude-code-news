BREAKING: Federal Register: Science Advisory Board Satellite Task Force; Availability of Draft Report and Request for Comments

Published: 2012-10-10 | Source: Federal Register

NOAA Office of Oceanic and Atmospheric Research (OAR) publishes this notice on behalf of the NOAA Science Advisory Board (SAB) to announce the availability of the draft report of the SAB Satellite Task Force (here called the SATTF) for public comment. The draft report of the SATTF has been prepared at NOAA's request made in September 2011.
