BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Alexander Schleicher GmbH & Co. Segelflugzeugbau Gliders

Published: 2022-03-23 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA proposes to adopt a new airworthiness directive (AD) for all Alexander Schleicher GmbH & Co. Segelflugzeugbau Model ASW-15 gliders. This proposed AD was prompted by mandatory continuing airworthiness information (MCAI) originated by an aviation authority of another country to identify and correct an unsafe condition on an aviation product.
