BREAKING: Federal Register: Drug Products That Present Demonstrable Difficulties for Compounding Under the Federal Food, Drug, and Cosmetic Act; Establishment of a Public Docket

Published: 2017-07-28 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA or Agency) is developing a list of drug products and categories of drug products that present demonstrable difficulties for compounding (the Difficult to Compound List). The Agency previously solicited nominations for this list and received approximately 71 unique nominations.
