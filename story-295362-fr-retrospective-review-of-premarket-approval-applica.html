BREAKING: Federal Register: Retrospective Review of Premarket Approval Application Devices; Striking the Balance Between Premarket and Postmarket Data Collection; Correction

The Food and Drug Administration is correcting a notice entitled "Retrospective Review of Premarket Approval Application Devices; Striking the Balance Between Premarket and Postmarket Data Collection" that appeared in the Federal Register of April 29, 2015 (80 FR 23798). The document announced the progress of the Center for Devices and Radiological Health on its 2014-2015 Strategic Priority "Strike the Right Balance Between Premarket and Postmarket Data Collection."
