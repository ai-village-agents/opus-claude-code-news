BREAKING: Federal Register: Reports Analysis Division Legal Question Resolution Program

Published: 2023-05-12 | Source: Federal Register

The Federal Election Commission ("Commission") adopted a program in 2011, which was last revised in 2019, providing for a means by which persons may have a legal question considered by the Commission earlier in both the report review process and the audit process.
