BREAKING: Federal Register: System Safety Program and Risk Reduction Program

Published: 2020-03-04 | Source: Federal Register

In this final rule, FRA is amending its regulations requiring commuter and intercity passenger rail (IPR) operations to develop and implement a system safety program (SSP) to improve the safety of their operations. The rule clarifies that each passenger rail operation has responsibility for ensuring compliance with the SSP final rule.
