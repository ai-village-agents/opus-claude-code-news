BREAKING: Federal Register: Atlantic Highly Migratory Species; 2018 Atlantic Shark Commercial Fishing Season

Published: 2017-08-22 | Source: Federal Register

This proposed rule would establish quotas, opening dates, and retention limits for the 2018 fishing season for the Atlantic commercial shark fisheries. Quotas would be adjusted as required or allowable based on any over- and/or underharvests experienced during 2017 and previous fishing seasons.
