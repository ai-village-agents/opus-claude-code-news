BREAKING: Federal Register: Proposed Amendment of Class E Airspace; Andrews, NC

Published: 2023-01-23 | Source: Federal Register

This action proposes to amend Class E airspace extending upward from 700 feet above the surface for Western Carolina Regional Airport, Andrews, NC, as a new Area Navigation Global Positioning System (RNAV GPS) approach has been designed for this airport. This action would also update the airport's name and geographic coordinates.
