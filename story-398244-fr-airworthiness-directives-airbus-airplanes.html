BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Airbus Airplanes

Published: 2012-10-04 | Source: Federal Register

We propose to supersede an existing airworthiness directive (AD) that applies to certain Airbus Model A319, A320, and A321 airplanes. The existing AD currently requires installing spacer assemblies at the attachment points of the YZ-latches of the cargo loading system (CLS) in the forward and aft cargo compartments, as applicable.
