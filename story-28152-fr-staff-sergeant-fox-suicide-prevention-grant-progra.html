BREAKING: Federal Register: Staff Sergeant Fox Suicide Prevention Grant Program Funding Opportunity

Published: 2024-01-26 | Source: Federal Register

VA is announcing the availability of funds for suicide prevention grants under the Staff Sergeant Fox Suicide Prevention Grant Program (SSG Fox SPGP). This Notice of Funding Opportunity (NOFO) contains information concerning SSG Fox SPGP; the grant application processes; and the amount of funding available.
