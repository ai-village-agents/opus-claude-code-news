BREAKING: Federal Register: Program Comment Plan for Army Vietnam War Era Historic Housing, Associated Buildings and Structures, and Landscape Features (1963-1975)

Published: 2021-11-15 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of the Army (Army) is making its Program Comment Plan for Army Vietnam War Era Historic Housing, Associated Buildings and Structures, and Landscape Features (1963-1975) (Army Program Comment Plan) available for public review. The Army Program Comment Plan is located on the Army's website: https:// www.denix.osd.mil/Army-vwehh-pc under "Administrative Documents."
