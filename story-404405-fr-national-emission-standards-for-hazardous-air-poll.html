BREAKING: Federal Register: National Emission Standards for Hazardous Air Pollutants for Chemical Manufacturing Area Sources

Published: 2012-12-21 | Source: Federal Register

On January 30, 2012, the EPA proposed revisions to several provisions of the final National Emission Standards for Hazardous Air Pollutants for Chemical Manufacturing Area Sources. The proposed revisions were made, in part, in response to a petition for reconsideration received by the Administrator following the promulgation of the October 29, 2009, final rule ("2009 final rule").
