BREAKING: Federal Register: Endangered and Threatened Wildlife and Plants; Draft Revised Recovery Plan for Pallid Sturgeon

Published: 2013-03-15 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Fish and Wildlife Service (Service) announces the availability of a draft revised recovery plan for the pallid sturgeon (Scaphirhynchus albus). This species is federally listed as endangered under the Endangered Species Act of 1973, as amended (Act). The Service solicits review and comment from the public on this draft revised plan.
