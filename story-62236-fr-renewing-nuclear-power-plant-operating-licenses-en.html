BREAKING: Federal Register: Renewing Nuclear Power Plant Operating Licenses-Environmental Review

Published: 2023-03-10 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Nuclear Regulatory Commission (NRC) plans to hold comment-gathering public meetings on a proposed rule to amend its environmental protection regulations by updating the Commission's 2013 findings on the environmental effect of renewing the operating license of a nuclear power plant.
