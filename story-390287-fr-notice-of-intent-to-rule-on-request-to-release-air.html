BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Intent To Rule on Request To Release Airport Property at Portland-Hillsboro Airport, Hillsboro, OR

Published: 2012-07-05 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA proposes to rule and invites public comment on the release of land at Portland--Hillsboro Airport under the provisions of Section 125 of the Wendell H. Ford Aviation Investment Reform Act for the 21st Century (AIR 21), now 49 U.S.C. 47107(h)(2). The FAA is considering a proposal to change a portion of the airport from aeronautical use to nonaeronautical use and to authorize the sale and/or conversion of the airport property.
