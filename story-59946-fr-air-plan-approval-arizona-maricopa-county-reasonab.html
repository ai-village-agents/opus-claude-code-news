BREAKING: Federal Register: Air Plan Approval; Arizona; Maricopa County; Reasonably Available Control Technology-Combustion Sources

Published: 2023-02-07 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is taking final action to approve a revision to the Maricopa County Air Quality Department's (MCAQD or "County") portion of the Arizona State Implementation Plan (SIP). This revision concerns emissions of oxides of nitrogen (NOX) and particulate matter (PM) from combustion equipment and internal combustion engines.
