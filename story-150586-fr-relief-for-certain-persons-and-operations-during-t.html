BREAKING: Federal Register: Relief for Certain Persons and Operations During the Coronavirus Disease 2019 (COVID-19) Outbreak

Published: 2020-05-04 | Source: Federal Register

This Special Federal Aviation Regulation (SFAR) provides regulatory relief to persons who have been unable to comply with certain training, recent experience, testing, and checking requirements due to the Coronavirus Disease 2019 (COVID-19) outbreak. This relief allows operators to continue to use pilots and other crewmembers in support of essential operations during this period.
