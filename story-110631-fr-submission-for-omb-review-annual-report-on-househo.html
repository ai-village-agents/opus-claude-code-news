BREAKING: Federal Register: Submission for OMB Review; Annual Report on Households Assisted by the Low Income Home Energy Assistance Program (OMB #0970-0060)

Published: 2022-11-22 | Source: Federal Register

Grant recipients complete the Household Report on an annual basis, completing either the Long Form or the Short Form version of the report.
