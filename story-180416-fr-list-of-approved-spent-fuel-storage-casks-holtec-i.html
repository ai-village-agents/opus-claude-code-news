BREAKING: Federal Register: List of Approved Spent Fuel Storage Casks: Holtec International HI-STORM 100 Multipurpose Canister Cask System, Certificate of Compliance No. 1014, Amendment Nos. 12 and 13; Corrections

Published: 2019-05-30 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Nuclear Regulatory Commission (NRC) published two direct final rules in the Federal Register, on December 12, 2018, and February 26, 2019, which amended its spent fuel storage regulations by revising the Holtec International HI-STORM 100 Multipurpose Canister Cask System listing within the "List of approved spent fuel storage casks" to include Amendment Nos. 12 and 13 to Certificate of Compliance No. 1014 respectively.
