BREAKING: Federal Register: Mailing of Tickets Under the Ticket to Work Program

Published: 2012-01-12 | Source: Federal Register

The Social Security Act (Act) states that we may issue a Ticket to Work (Ticket) to disabled beneficiaries for participation in the Ticket to Work program (Ticket program). Under our current rules, however, we mail initial Ticket notices to all Ticket-eligible beneficiaries, regardless of whether they are likely to participate in the program, immediately after they begin receiving benefits.
