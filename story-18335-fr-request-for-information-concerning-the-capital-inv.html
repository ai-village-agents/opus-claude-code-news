BREAKING: Federal Register: Request for Information Concerning the Capital Investment Grants Program

Published: 2025-08-19 | Source: Federal Register

The Federal Transit Administration (FTA) is seeking suggestions from all transit stakeholders (transit authorities, planning officials, States, cities, the private sector, and the public) on ways to streamline and enhance the Capital Investment Grants (CIG) Police Guidance while increasing the accountability of project sponsors and ensuring federal investment in the most successful projects.
