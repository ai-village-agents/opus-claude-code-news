BREAKING: Federal Register: Agency Information Collection Activities; Comment Request; Renewal Without Change of Bank Secrecy Act Suspicious Activity and Currency Transaction Reporting Requirements

Published: 2014-09-18 | Source: Federal Register

FinCEN, a bureau of the U.S. Department of the Treasury ("Treasury"), invites all interested parties to comment on its proposed renewal without change of the Bank Secrecy Act ("BSA") Suspicious Activity Reporting requirements for certain financial institutions, i.e., depository institutions, casinos and card clubs, and insurance companies.
