BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; GA 8 Airvan (Pty) Ltd Airplanes

Published: 2015-07-16 | Source: Federal Register

We are revising an airworthiness directive (AD) 2015-06-02 for GA 8 Airvan (Pty) Ltd Model GA8-TC320 airplanes. This AD results from mandatory continuing airworthiness information (MCAI) issued by an aviation authority of another country to identify and correct an unsafe condition on an aviation product.
