BREAKING: Federal Register: Operations in Rural Areas Under the Truth in Lending Act (Regulation Z); Interim Final Rule

Published: 2016-03-25 | Source: Federal Register

This interim final rule amends certain provisions of Regulation Z in light of title LXXXIX of the Fixing America's Surface Transportation Act, entitled the Helping Expand Lending Practices in Rural Communities Act, Public Law 114-94.
