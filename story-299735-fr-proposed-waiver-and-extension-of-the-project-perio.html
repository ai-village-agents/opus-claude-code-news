BREAKING: Federal Register: Proposed Waiver and Extension of the Project Period for the Literacy Information and Communication System Regional Professional Development Centers

Published: 2015-07-24 | Source: Federal Register

The Secretary proposes to waive the requirements that generally prohibit project period extensions involving the obligation of additional Federal funds, and to extend the project period of the four currently-funded Literacy Information and Communication System (LINCS) Regional Professional Development Centers (RPDC) program grants for one year.
