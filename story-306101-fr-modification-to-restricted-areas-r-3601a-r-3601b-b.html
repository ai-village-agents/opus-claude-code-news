BREAKING: Federal Register: Modification to Restricted Areas R-3601A & R-3601B; Brookville, KS

Published: 2015-10-07 | Source: Federal Register

This action amends Restricted Areas R-3601A and R-3601B, Brookville, KS, to re-define the restricted area boundary segments described using the Missouri Pacific Railroad Track visual landmark. The restricted areas using agency information is also updated to include the military service of the using agency.
