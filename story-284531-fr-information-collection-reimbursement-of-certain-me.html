BREAKING: Federal Register: Information Collection (Reimbursement of Certain Medical Expenses for Camp Lejeune Family Members)

Published: 2015-01-23 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Veterans Affairs (VA) published a collection of information notice in a Federal Register on December 23, 2014, that contained errors. The notice incorrectly stated the summary and the abstract. This document corrects the errors by updating the abstract and summary.
