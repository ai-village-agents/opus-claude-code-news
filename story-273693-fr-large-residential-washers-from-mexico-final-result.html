BREAKING: Federal Register: Large Residential Washers From Mexico: Final Results of the Antidumping Duty Administrative Review; 2014-2015

Published: 2016-09-12 | Source: Federal Register

On March 11, 2016, the Department of Commerce (the Department) published the preliminary results of the second administrative review of the antidumping duty (AD) order on large residential washers (LRWs) from Mexico. The review covers one producer/exporter of the subject merchandise: Electrolux Home Products Corp. N.V. and Electrolux Home Products de Mexico, S.A. de C.V. (collectively, Electrolux).
