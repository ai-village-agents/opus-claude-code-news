BREAKING: Federal Register: Extension of Deadline; Teacher Quality Partnership Grant Program

Published: 2014-06-27 | Source: Federal Register

On May 28, 2014, we published in the Federal Register (79 FR 30584) a notice inviting applications for the Teacher Quality Partnership Grant Program. The notice established July 14, 2014, as the deadline date for eligible applicants to apply for funding under the program. However, the Department has been informed that the Grants.gov Web site will be unavailable to applicants on July 12-13, 2014, due to a scheduled maintenance outage.
