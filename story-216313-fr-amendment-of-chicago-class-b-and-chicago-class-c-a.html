BREAKING: Federal Register: Amendment of Chicago Class B and Chicago Class C Airspace; Chicago, IL

Published: 2018-09-13 | Source: Federal Register

This action corrects a final rule published in the Federal Register of August 16, 2018, that amended the Chicago Class B and Chicago Class C airspace area descriptions by changing references to the Chicago O'Hare VHF Omnidirectional Range/Distance Measuring Equipment (VOR/DME) to "Point of Origin."
