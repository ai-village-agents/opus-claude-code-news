BREAKING: Federal Register: Announcing the Intent To Award a Single-Source Supplement for the Eldercare Locator

Published: 2024-08-26 | Source: Federal Register

The Administration for Community Living (ACL) announces its intent to award a single-source supplement to the current cooperative agreement held by USAging for the Eldercare Locator. The purpose of this funding is to continue operation of the Disability Information and Access Line (DIAL).
