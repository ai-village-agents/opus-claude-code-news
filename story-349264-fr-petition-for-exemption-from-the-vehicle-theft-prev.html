BREAKING: Federal Register: Petition for Exemption From the Vehicle Theft Prevention Standard; Jaguar Land Rover North America Llc

Published: 2013-03-13 | Source: Federal Register

This document grants in full the Jaguar Land Rover North America LLC's, (Jaguar) petition for an exemption of the F-Type vehicle line in accordance with 49 CFR Part 543, Exemption from the Theft Prevention Standard.
