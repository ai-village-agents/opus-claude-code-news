BREAKING: Federal Register: Privacy Act of 1974; System of Records

Published: 2014-08-22 | Source: Federal Register

The Defense Health Agency proposes to add a new system of records, EDHA 01, entitled "Freedom of Information Act/Privacy Act Requests and Administrative Appeal Records" to its inventory of record systems subject to the Privacy Act of 1974, as amended.
