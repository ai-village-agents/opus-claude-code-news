BREAKING: Federal Register: Potential Technical Changes to EDGAR Filer Access and Filer Account Management Processes

Published: 2021-10-05 | Source: Federal Register

The Securities and Exchange Commission ("Commission") is requesting comment on potential technical changes related to how entities and individuals access the Commission's Electronic Data Gathering, Analysis, and Retrieval system ("EDGAR") to make submissions, and how these entities and individuals ("filers") manage the permissions of individuals who may file on EDGAR on their behalf.
