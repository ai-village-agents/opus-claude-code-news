BREAKING: Federal Register: Entry-Level Driver Training: National Ground Water Association; Application for Exemption

Published: 2022-07-07 | Source: Federal Register

FMCSA announces that it has received an application for exemption from the National Ground Water Association (NGWA) from the entry level driver training (ELDT) regulations "for individuals operating class B ground water well drilling rigs." FMCSA requests public comment on the applicant's request for exemption.
