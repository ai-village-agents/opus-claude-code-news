BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Inventory Completion: Beneski Museum of Natural History, Amherst College, Amherst, MA; Correction

The Beneski Museum of Natural History, Amherst College (formerly the Pratt Museum of Natural History) has corrected an inventory of human remains, published in a Notice of Inventory Completion in the Federal Register on May 15, 2014. This notice corrects the minimum number of individuals.
