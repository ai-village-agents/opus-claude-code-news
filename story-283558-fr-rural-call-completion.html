BREAKING: Federal Register: Rural Call Completion

Published: 2015-01-08 | Source: Federal Register

The Commission published in the Federal Register of December 10, 2014, a document concerning an Order on Reconsideration (Order) affirming the Commission's commitment to ensuring that high quality telephone service must be available to all Americans. In the Order, the Commission established rules to combat extensive problems with successfully completing calls to rural areas, and created a framework to improve the ability to monitor call problems and take appropriate enforcement action.
