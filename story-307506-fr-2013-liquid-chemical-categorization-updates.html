BREAKING: Federal Register: 2013 Liquid Chemical Categorization Updates

Published: 2015-10-22 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard proposes additional updates and revisions to regulatory tables that were amended by an interim rule published in August 2013. The tables list liquid hazardous materials, liquefied gases, and compressed gases approved for maritime transportation in bulk, and indicate how each cargo is categorized by its pollution risk and safe carriage requirements.
