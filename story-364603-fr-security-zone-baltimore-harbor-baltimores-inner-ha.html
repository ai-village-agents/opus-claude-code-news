BREAKING: Federal Register: Security Zone, Baltimore Harbor, Baltimore's Inner Harbor; Baltimore, MD

Published: 2013-09-04 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is establishing a temporary security zone encompassing certain waters of Baltimore Harbor, Baltimore's Inner Harbor, at Baltimore, Maryland. This action is necessary to safeguard persons and property, and prevent terrorist acts or incidents.
