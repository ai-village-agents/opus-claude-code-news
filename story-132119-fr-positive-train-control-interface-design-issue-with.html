BREAKING: Federal Register: Positive Train Control Interface Design Issue With Locomotive and Cab Car Braking Systems

Published: 2021-09-02 | Source: Federal Register

FRA is issuing Safety Advisory 2021-01 to make the rail industry, including railroads and railroad employees, aware of a recently identified interface design issue relating to how positive train control (PTC) systems in use throughout the United States interface with locomotive and cab car braking systems.
