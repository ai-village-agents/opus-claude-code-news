BREAKING: Federal Register: Air Plan Approval; Arizona; Stationary Sources; New Source Review Updates

Published: 2020-12-23 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is proposing to approve revisions to the Arizona Department of Environmental Quality's (ADEQ) portion of the Arizona State Implementation Plan (SIP). These revisions are primarily intended to make corrections to the ADEQ's SIP- approved rules for the issuance of New Source Review (NSR) permits for stationary sources under the Clean Air Act (CAA or Act).
