BREAKING: Federal Register: National Water Program 2012 Strategy: Response to Climate Change

Published: 2012-12-26 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is publishing the final "National Water Program 2012 Strategy: Response to Climate Change" (2012 Strategy). The Strategy describes a set of long-term visions and goals for the management of water resources in light of climate change and charts key strategic actions to be taken to achieve the goals in 2012 and subsequent years.
