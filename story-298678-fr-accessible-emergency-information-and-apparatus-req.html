BREAKING: Federal Register: Accessible Emergency Information, and Apparatus Requirements for Emergency Information and Video Description

Published: 2015-07-10 | Source: Federal Register

In this document, the Commission adopts additional rules under the authority of the Twenty-First Century Communications and Video Accessibility Act of 2010 (CVAA) to make emergency information in video programming accessible to individuals who are blind or visually impaired.
