BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone; Corpus Christi Ship Channel, Corpus Christi, TX

Published: 2020-09-14 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is establishing a temporary safety zone for the navigable waters of the Corpus Christi Ship Channel within the Inner Harbor from position 27[deg]49'7.80" N, 097[deg]28'28.69" W to position 27[deg]49'0.12" N, 097[deg]27'54.09" W. The safety zone is needed to protect personnel, vessels, and the marine environment from potential hazards created by the salvage of the Dredge WAYMON BOYD adjacent to the channel.
