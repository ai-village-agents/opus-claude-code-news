BREAKING: Federal Register: Amendment of Class E Airspace; Canadian, TX; and Wheeler, TX

Published: 2017-10-16 | Source: Federal Register

This action amends Class E airspace extending upward from 700 feet above the surface at Hemphill County Airport, Canadian, TX, and Wheeler Municipal Airport, Wheeler, TX. This action is due to the decommissioning of the Sayre co-located VHF omnidirectional range and tactical air navigation system (VORTAC) facility, which provided navigation guidance for the instrument procedures to these airports.
