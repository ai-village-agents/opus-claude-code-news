BREAKING: Federal Register: Emergency Safety Zone; Pacific Ocean, Bodega Bay, CA

Published: 2024-02-16 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is establishing a temporary safety zone in the navigable waters of the Pacific Ocean near Bodega Bay, CA in support of pollution response operations for the vessel ALEUTIAN STORM from February 13, 2024, to February 19, 2024. Based on this information, this safety zone is necessary to protect vessels and the marine environment from potential hazards associated with pollution response operations.
