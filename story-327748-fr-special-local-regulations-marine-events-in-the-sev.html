BREAKING: Federal Register: Special Local Regulations; Marine Events in the Seventh Coast Guard District

Published: 2014-06-24 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard will enforce the special local regulations for two events, the Patriots Point Fireworks and the City of North Charleston Fireworks, from 8:15 p.m. through 10:15 p.m. on July 4, 2014. This action is necessary to ensure safety of life on navigable waters of the United States during the Fourth of July fireworks displays.
