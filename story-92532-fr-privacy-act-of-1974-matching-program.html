BREAKING: Federal Register: Privacy Act of 1974; Matching Program

Published: 2022-04-01 | Source: Federal Register

In accordance with subsection (e)(12) of the Privacy Act of 1974, as amended, VA is providing notice of a re-established matching program between VA and the Department of Health and Human Services (HHS) Centers for Medicare & Medicaid Services (CMS) entitled "Disclosure of Information to Support the Veterans Affairs' Seek to Prevent Fraud, Waste, and Abuse Initiative."
