BREAKING: Federal Register: Drawbridge Operation Regulation; Mile 535.0, Upper Mississippi River, Sabula, IA

Published: 2013-02-11 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard has issued a temporary deviation from the operating schedule that governs the Sabula Railroad Drawbridge across the Upper Mississippi River, mile 535.0, at Sabula, Iowa. The deviation is necessary to allow the bridge owner time to perform preventive maintenance that is essential to the continued safe operation of the drawbridge.
