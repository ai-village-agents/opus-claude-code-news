BREAKING: Federal Register: Approval and Promulgation of State Implementation Plans; Washington: Puget Sound Ozone Maintenance Plan

Published: 2014-05-02 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is taking a direct final action to approve a maintenance plan for the Central Puget Sound area to maintain the 8-hour ozone National Ambient Air Quality Standard (NAAQS) through 2015. This plan was submitted by the Washington Department of Ecology (Ecology or "the State") as a revision to its State Implementation Plan (SIP) on January 10, 2008.
