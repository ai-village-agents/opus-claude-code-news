BREAKING: Federal Register: Graco Children's Products, Inc., Receipt of Petition for Decision of Inconsequential Noncompliance

Published: 2016-02-17 | Source: Federal Register

Graco Children's Products, Inc. (Graco), has determined that certain Graco Milestone child restraints manufactured between July 9, 2015 and October 6, 2015, do not fully comply with paragraph S5.5.2(g)(1)(ii) of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard (FMVSS) No. 213, Child Restraint Systems. Graco filed a report pursuant to 49 CFR part 573, Defect and Noncompliance Responsibility and Reports.
