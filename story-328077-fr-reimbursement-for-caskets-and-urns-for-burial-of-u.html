BREAKING: Federal Register: Reimbursement for Caskets and Urns for Burial of Unclaimed Remains in a National Cemetery

Published: 2014-06-26 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Veterans Affairs National Cemetery Administration (NCA) proposes to amend its regulations to establish a new program to furnish caskets and urns for the interment of the remains of veterans with no known next- of-kin (NOK) where sufficient financial resources are not available for this purpose.
