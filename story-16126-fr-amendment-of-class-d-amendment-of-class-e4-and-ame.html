BREAKING: Federal Register: Amendment of Class D, Amendment of Class E4, and Amendment of Class E5 Airspace; Nantucket, MA

Published: 2025-07-17 | Source: Federal Register

This action amends Class D airspace at Nantucket Memorial Airport, Nantucket, MA, due to the currently designated airspace not properly containing instrument flight rule (IFR) operations. Additionally, this action amends Class E4 airspace at Nantucket Memorial Airport, Nantucket, MA, due to portions no longer meeting the requirements of its designation.
