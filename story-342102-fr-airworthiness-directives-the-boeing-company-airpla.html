BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; The Boeing Company Airplanes

Published: 2014-12-16 | Source: Federal Register

We are adopting a new airworthiness directive (AD) for certain The Boeing Company Model 737-100, -200, -200C, -300, -400, and -500 series airplanes. This AD was prompted by reports of fatigue cracking in the skin assembly and bear strap of the aft lower corner of the forward airstair stowage doorway.
