BREAKING: Federal Register: Redesignation of Portions of Westmoreland and Cambria Counties, Pennsylvania for the 2010 Sulfur Dioxide (SO2) National Ambient Air Quality Standards (NAAQS): Notification of Availability and Public Comment Period

Published: 2024-02-12 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA or the Agency) is providing notice of our intent to redesignate portions of Westmoreland County and Cambria County, Pennsylvania, to "nonattainment" for the 2010 sulfur dioxide (SO2) national ambient air quality standard (NAAQS or standard). Westmoreland County is currently designated "attainment/unclassifiable," and Cambria County is currently designated "unclassifiable."
