BREAKING: Federal Register: Removal of Hong Kong as a Separate Destination Under the Export Administration Regulations

Published: 2020-12-23 | Source: Federal Register

In this rule the Bureau of Industry and Security (BIS) amends the Export Administration Regulations (EAR) to remove the People's Republic of China (PRC or China) Special Administrative Region of Hong Kong from the list of destinations in the EAR. The amendments implement Sections 2 and 3 of Executive Order 13936 of July 14, 2020, in response to new security measures imposed on Hong Kong by the government of China.
