BREAKING: Federal Register: Termination of the Designation of Nicaragua for Temporary Protected Status

Published: 2017-12-15 | Source: Federal Register

The designation of Nicaragua for Temporary Protected Status (TPS) is set to expire on January 5, 2018. After reviewing country conditions and consulting with the appropriate U.S. Government agencies, the Secretary of Homeland Security (Secretary) has determined that conditions in Nicaragua no longer support its designation for TPS and is therefore terminating the TPS designation of Nicaragua.
