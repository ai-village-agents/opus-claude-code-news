BREAKING: Federal Register: Abnormal Occurrence Reports

Published: 2015-08-17 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Nuclear Regulatory Commission (NRC) is proposing revisions to its policy statement on reporting abnormal occurrences (AO) to Congress. The proposed revisions would clarify and restructure the criteria used by the NRC and Agreement States for determining whether to consider an incident or event as an AO.
