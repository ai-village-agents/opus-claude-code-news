BREAKING: Federal Register: Guidance for Industry on Irritable Bowel Syndrome-Clinical Evaluation of Drugs for Treatment; Availability; Correction

Published: 2012-06-14 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA) is correcting a notice that appeared in the Federal Register of May 31, 2012 (77 FR 32124). The document announced the availability of a guidance for industry entitled "Irritable Bowel Syndrome--Clinical Evaluation of Drugs for Treatment." The document was published with an incorrect docket number. This document corrects that error.
