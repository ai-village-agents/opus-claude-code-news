BREAKING: Federal Register: AmeriCorps Seniors Regulation Updates

Published: 2024-02-14 | Source: Federal Register

The Corporation for National and Community Service (operating as AmeriCorps) proposes to revise its regulations governing AmeriCorps Seniors programs. This proposed rule would remove barriers to service for individuals and increase flexibility for sponsors to determine the best mix of staffing and resources to accomplish project goals.
