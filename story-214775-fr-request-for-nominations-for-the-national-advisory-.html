BREAKING: Federal Register: Request for Nominations for the National Advisory Council on Nurse Education and Practice

Published: 2018-08-24 | Source: Federal Register

HRSA is requesting nominations to fill vacancies on the National Advisory Council on Nurse Education and Practice (NACNEP). The NACNEP is authorized by Section 851 of the Public Health Service (PHS) Act, as amended.
