BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; General Electric Company Engines

Published: 2024-12-05 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA proposes to adopt a new airworthiness directive (AD) for all General Electric Company (GE) Model CT7-2E1 engines. This proposed AD was prompted by a revised analysis using an updated stress model, which calculated that the actual life limit of the CT7-2E1 stage 2 turbine aft cooling plate is less than the current life limit.
