BREAKING: Federal Register: Request for Information and Stakeholder Listening Sessions on Farm Labor Stabilization and Protection Pilot Program

Published: 2022-09-23 | Source: Federal Register

The Farm Service Agency (FSA) is hosting three listening sessions for public input about a recently announced planned farm labor stabilization and protection pilot grant program focused on improving the resiliency of our food and agricultural supply chain by addressing current labor shortages in agriculture, reducing irregular migration through increased use of legal pathways, and improving labor protections for farmworkers.
