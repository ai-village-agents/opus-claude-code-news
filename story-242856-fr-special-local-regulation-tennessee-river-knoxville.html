BREAKING: Federal Register: Special Local Regulation; Tennessee River, Knoxville, TN

Published: 2017-09-05 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is establishing a temporary special local regulation for all navigable waters of the Tennessee River from mile marker (MM) 641 to MM 648.7. This special local regulation is necessary to provide safety for the participants of the Bridges to Bluffs marine event in Knoxville, TN.
