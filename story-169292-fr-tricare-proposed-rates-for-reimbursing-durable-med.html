BREAKING: Federal Register: TRICARE; Proposed Rates for Reimbursing Durable Medical Equipment, Prosthetics, Orthotics, and Supplies (DMEPOS) and Parenteral and Enteral Nutrition (PEN) Items Not on the Medicare DMEPOS and PEN Fee Schedule

Published: 2020-12-29 | Source: Federal Register

This notice is to advise interested parties of a Military Health System reimbursement change to certain DMEPOS and PEN items not included in Medicare's fee schedule. For these items, the Defense Health Agency (DHA) will create a TRICARE-specific fee schedule based on similar payment rules, to the extent practicable, as Medicare's DMEPOS and PEN fee schedule.
