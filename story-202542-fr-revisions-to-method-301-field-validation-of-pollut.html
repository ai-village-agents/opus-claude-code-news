BREAKING: Federal Register: Revisions to Method 301: Field Validation of Pollutant Measurement Methods From Various Waste Media

Published: 2018-03-20 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is publishing editorial and technical revisions to the EPA's Method 301 "Field Validation of Pollutant Measurement Methods from Various Waste Media" to correct and update the method. In addition, the EPA is clarifying the regulatory applicability of Method 301 as well as its suitability for use with other regulations.
