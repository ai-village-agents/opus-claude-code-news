BREAKING: Federal Register: Federal Records Management: Digitizing Permanent Records and Reviewing Records Schedules

Published: 2023-05-04 | Source: Federal Register

The National Archives and Records Administration (NARA) is amending our records management regulations to add a subpart containing standards for digitizing permanent Federal records so that agencies may dispose of the source records, when appropriate and in accordance with the Federal Records Act amendments of 2014.
