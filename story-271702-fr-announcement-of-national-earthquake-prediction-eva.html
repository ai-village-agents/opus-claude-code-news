BREAKING: Federal Register: Announcement of National Earthquake Prediction Evaluation Council

Published: 2016-08-19 | Source: Federal Register

Pursuant to Public Law 106-503, the National Earthquake Prediction Evaluation Council (NEPEC) will hold its next meeting by phone. The Committee is comprised of members from academia and the Federal government. The Committee provides advice and recommendations to the Director of the USGS on earthquake predictions and related scientific research.
