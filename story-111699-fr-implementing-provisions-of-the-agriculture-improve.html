BREAKING: Federal Register: Implementing Provisions of the Agriculture Improvement Act of 2018

Published: 2022-12-06 | Source: Federal Register

The Agriculture Improvement Act of 2018 (2018 Farm Bill) amended several sections of the Rural Electrification Act of 1936 (RE Act) that are carried out by the Electric Program at the USDA--Rural Utilities Service (RUS). Section 6501 extends refinancing authority to RUS for loans made or guaranteed by the Secretary. Section 6503 ended the Cushion of Credit Payment Program.
