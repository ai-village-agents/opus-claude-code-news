BREAKING: Federal Register: Mevinphos; Order Revoking Tolerances

Published: 2012-02-29 | Source: Federal Register

EPA is revoking all the tolerances for the pesticide mevinphos. EPA previously required data to be submitted to support these tolerances. However, no person submitted timely notice to EPA of intent to provide the required data.
