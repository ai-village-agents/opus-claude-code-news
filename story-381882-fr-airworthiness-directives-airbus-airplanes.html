BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Airbus Airplanes

Published: 2012-03-30 | Source: Federal Register

We are adopting a new airworthiness directive (AD) for certain Airbus Model A321-131, -211, -212, and -231 airplanes. This AD requires a rotating probe inspection for cracking of the lower panel bore holes of the center wing box (CWB), and corrective actions if necessary.
