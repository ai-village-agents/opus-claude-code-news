BREAKING: Federal Register: Drawbridge Operation Regulations; Mystic River, Charlestown and Everett, MA

Published: 2013-11-04 | Source: Federal Register

The Commander, First Coast Guard District, has issued a temporary deviation from the regulation governing the operation of the Alford Street (Rt-99) Bridge across the Mystic River, mile 1.4, between Boston and Everett, Massachusetts. The bridge owner, the City of Boston, and Massachusetts Department of Transportation will be performing electrical repairs and structural rehabilitation at the bridge.
