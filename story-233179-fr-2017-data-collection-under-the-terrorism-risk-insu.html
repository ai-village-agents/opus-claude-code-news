BREAKING: Federal Register: 2017 Data Collection Under the Terrorism Risk Insurance Program

Published: 2017-05-01 | Source: Federal Register

The Terrorism Risk Insurance Act requires the Secretary of the Treasury to collect, from insurers that participate in the Terrorism Risk Insurance Program, information regarding insurance coverage for terrorism losses. The information is to be used by the Secretary in connection with reports analyzing various aspects of the Program.
