BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Public Meeting of the California Advisory Committee

Published: 2021-06-09 | Source: Federal Register

Notice is hereby given, pursuant to the provisions of the rules and regulations of the U.S. Commission on Civil Rights (Commission) and the Federal Advisory Committee Act that the California Advisory Committee (Committee) will hold a meeting via web teleconference on Thursday, July 22, 2021, from 12:30 p.m.-2:00 p.m. Pacific Time for the purpose of discussing the topic and focus of their next project.
