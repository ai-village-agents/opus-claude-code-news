BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of EB-5 Regional Center Integrity Fund Fee

Published: 2023-03-02 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Citizenship and Immigration Services (USCIS) is announcing a fee to be collected by USCIS. The EB-5 Reform and Integrity Act of 2022 (the 2022 Act) requires USCIS to establish a special fund to be known as the EB-5 Integrity Fund to be primarily used by USCIS in the administration of the Regional Center Program. USCIS must collect a fee of $20,000 or $10,000, depending on certain factors established by the 2022 Act, to finance the EB-5 Integrity Fund from each designated regional center...
