BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Goodrich Externally-Mounted Hoist Assemblies

Published: 2022-03-16 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA is adopting a new airworthiness directive (AD) for various model helicopters with certain part-numbered Goodrich externally-mounted hoist assemblies (hoists) installed. This AD was prompted by hoists failing lower load limit inspections.
