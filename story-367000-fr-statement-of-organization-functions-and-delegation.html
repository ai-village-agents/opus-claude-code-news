BREAKING: Federal Register: Statement of Organization, Functions, and Delegations of Authority

Published: 2013-10-02 | Source: Federal Register

Statement of organization, functions, and delegations of authority. The Administration for Children and Families (ACF) has reorganized the Office of Child Support Enforcement (OCSE). This reorganization realigns the functions of the Office of Child Support Enforcement. It eliminates the Office of Automation and Program Operations and moves the functions to the Division of Federal Systems.
