BREAKING: Federal Register: Migratory Bird Hunting; Normal Agricultural Operations

Published: 2019-08-08 | Source: Federal Register

The Agriculture Improvement Act of 2018 includes a provision that directs the Secretary of the Interior to revise the Federal migratory bird hunting regulations in part 20 of title 50 of the Code of Federal Regulations. The provision directs the Secretary to clarify that rice ratooning and post-disaster flooding, when carried out as part of a normal agricultural operation, do not constitute baiting.
