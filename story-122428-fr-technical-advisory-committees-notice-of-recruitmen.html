BREAKING: Federal Register: Technical Advisory Committees; Notice of Recruitment of Members

Published: 2021-04-28 | Source: Federal Register

The Bureau of Industry and Security (BIS), Department of Commerce is announcing its recruitment of candidates to serve on one of its seven Technical Advisory Committees ("TACs" or "Committees"). TAC members advise the Department of Commerce on the technical parameters for export controls applicable to dual-use items (commodities, software, and technology) and on the administration of those controls.
