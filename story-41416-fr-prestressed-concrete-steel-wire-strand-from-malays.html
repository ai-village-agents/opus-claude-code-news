BREAKING: Federal Register: Prestressed Concrete Steel Wire Strand From Malaysia: Preliminary Results and Preliminary Partial Rescission of Antidumping Duty Administrative Review; 2022-2023

Published: 2024-07-10 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Department of Commerce (Commerce) is conducting an administrative review of the antidumping duty order on prestressed concrete steel wire strand (PC strand) from Malaysia for the period of review (POR) June 1, 2022, through May 31, 2023. Commerce preliminarily finds that PC Strand from Malaysia was sold in the United States at prices below normal value (NV) during the POR.
