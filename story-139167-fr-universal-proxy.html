BREAKING: Federal Register: Universal Proxy

Published: 2021-12-01 | Source: Federal Register

The Securities and Exchange Commission ("Commission") is amending the Federal proxy rules to enhance the ability of shareholders to elect directors though the proxy process in a manner consistent with their ability to vote in person at a shareholder meeting. Specifically, the Commission is requiring the use of a universal proxy card in all non-exempt solicitations involving director election contests, except those involving registered investment companies and business development companies.
