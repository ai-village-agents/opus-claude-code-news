BREAKING: Federal Register: Isoamyl Acetate; Exemption From the Requirement of a Tolerance

Published: 2017-03-22 | Source: Federal Register

This regulation establishes an exemption from the requirement of a tolerance for residues of isoamyl acetate (CAS Reg. No. 123-92-2) when used as an inert ingredient (buffering agent) in pesticide formulations applied to growing crops and raw agricultural commodities after harvest.
