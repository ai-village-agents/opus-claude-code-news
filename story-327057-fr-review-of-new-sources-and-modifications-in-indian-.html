BREAKING: Federal Register: Review of New Sources and Modifications in Indian Country Amendments to the Registration and Permitting Deadlines for True Minor Sources

Published: 2014-06-16 | Source: Federal Register

EPA is issuing final amendments to the "Federal Minor New Source Review Program in Indian Country" (we refer to this new source review rule as the "Indian Country Minor NSR Rule"). We are amending the Indian Country Minor NSR Rule in two ways. First, we are extending the NSR minor source permitting deadline for true minor sources in the oil and natural gas sector from September 2, 2014, to March 2, 2016.
