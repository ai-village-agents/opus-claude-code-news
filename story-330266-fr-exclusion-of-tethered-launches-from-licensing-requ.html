BREAKING: Federal Register: Exclusion of Tethered Launches From Licensing Requirements

Published: 2014-07-22 | Source: Federal Register

On August 23, 2012, the FAA published a notice of proposed rulemaking (NPRM) to exclude certain tethered launches from the FAA's licensing and permitting requirements. The FAA is issuing this SNPRM because a commenter raised an issue regarding toxic propellants that was not discussed in the NPRM, but should be addressed.
