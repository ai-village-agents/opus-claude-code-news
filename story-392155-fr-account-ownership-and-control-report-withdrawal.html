BREAKING: Federal Register: Account Ownership and Control Report; Withdrawal

Published: 2012-07-26 | Source: Federal Register

On July 19, 2010, the Commission published for public comment a Notice of Proposed Rulemaking that proposed to collect certain account ownership and control information for all trading accounts active on U.S. futures exchanges and other reporting entities ("OCR NPRM").
