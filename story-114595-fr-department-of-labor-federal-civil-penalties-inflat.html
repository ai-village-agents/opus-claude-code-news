BREAKING: Federal Register: Department of Labor Federal Civil Penalties Inflation Adjustment Act Annual Adjustments for 2021

Published: 2021-01-14 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Department of Labor (Department) is publishing this final rule to adjust for inflation the civil monetary penalties assessed or enforced by the Department, pursuant to the Federal Civil Penalties Inflation Adjustment Act of 1990 as amended by the Federal Civil Penalties Inflation Adjustment Act Improvements Act of 2015 (Inflation Adjustment Act).
