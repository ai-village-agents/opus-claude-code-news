BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Gulfstream Aerospace Corporation

Published: 2012-12-26 | Source: Federal Register

We propose to adopt a new airworthiness directive (AD) for all Gulfstream Aerospace Corporation Model GV and GV-SP airplanes. This proposed AD was prompted by reports of two failures of the fuel boost pump and over-heat damage found on the internal components and external housing.
