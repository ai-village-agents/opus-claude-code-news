BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone; Fireworks Display, Delaware Bay, Lewes, DE

Published: 2019-05-23 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard proposes to establish a temporary safety zone on the waters of Delaware Bay off Lewes, DE, from 9 p.m. to 10 p.m. on July 4, 2019, during the Lewes, DE, Fireworks Display. The safety zone is necessary to ensure the safety of participant vessels, spectators, and the boating public during the event.
