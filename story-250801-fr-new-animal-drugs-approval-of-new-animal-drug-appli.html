BREAKING: Federal Register: New Animal Drugs; Approval of New Animal Drug Applications

Published: 2017-12-13 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA or we) is amending the animal drug regulations to reflect application-related actions for a new animal drug application (NADA) and abbreviated new animal drug applications (ANADAs) during May and June 2017. FDA is informing the public of the availability of summaries of the basis of approval and of environmental review documents, where applicable.
