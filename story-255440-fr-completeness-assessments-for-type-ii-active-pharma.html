BREAKING: Federal Register: Completeness Assessments for Type II Active Pharmaceutical Ingredient Drug Master Files Under the Generic Drug User Fee Amendments of 2012; Guidance for Industry; Availability

Published: 2016-02-16 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA or Agency) is announcing the availability of a guidance for industry entitled "Completeness Assessments for Type II API DMFs Under GDUFA". It finalizes the draft guidance entitled "Initial Completeness Assessments for Type II API DMFs Under GDUFA", which published on October 2, 2012.
