BREAKING: Federal Register: Buy America Handbook-Conducting Pre-Award and Post-Delivery Audits for Rolling Stock Procurements

Published: 2017-01-17 | Source: Federal Register

The Federal Transit Administration (FTA) has placed in the docket and on its Web site guidance, in the form of a Handbook, on complying with FTA's Buy America pre-award and post-delivery audit requirements for revenue service rolling stock procurements, from the solicitation phase through final acceptance of the rolling stock.
