BREAKING: Federal Register: Record of Decision for the Emergency Beddown of the F-22 Formal Training Unit and Associated T-38 Aircraft From Tyndall Air Force Base to Eglin Air Force Base, Florida Special Environmental Assessment

Published: 2019-05-07 | Source: Federal Register

On April 25, 2019, the United States Air Force signed a Record of Decision (ROD) on the Emergency Beddown of the F-22 Formal Training Unit and Associated T-38 Aircraft from Tyndall Air Force Base to Eglin Air Force Base, Florida, Special Environmental Assessment.
