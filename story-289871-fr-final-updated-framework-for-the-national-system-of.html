BREAKING: Federal Register: Final Updated Framework for the National System of Marine Protected Areas and Response to Comments

Published: 2015-03-30 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Commerce and the Department of the Interior (DOI) jointly propose an updated Framework for the National System of Marine Protected Areas of the United States (Framework). The Framework is required by Executive Order 13158 on Marine Protected Areas (MPAs).
