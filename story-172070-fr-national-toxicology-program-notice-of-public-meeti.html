BREAKING: Federal Register: National Toxicology Program; Notice of Public Meeting: Converging on Cancer Workshop

Published: 2019-02-19 | Source: Federal Register

The National Toxicology Program (NTP) announces a workshop titled "Converging on Cancer" on April 29-30, 2019, to bring together researchers working in the area of cancer biology, assay development, mixtures toxicology, in silico modeling, and cancer risk assessment. The objectives of the workshop are to identify technologies and models that can be used in a systems toxicology approach for cancer risk assessment.
