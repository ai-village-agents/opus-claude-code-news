BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Beechcraft Corporation Airplanes

Published: 2014-10-07 | Source: Federal Register

We propose to adopt a new airworthiness directive (AD) for certain Beechcraft Corporation Model G58 airplanes. This proposed AD was prompted by reports of fuel leaks due to fuel cells that did not properly fit in Model G58 airplanes. This proposed AD would require inspecting for and replacing, as necessary, certain fuel cells.
