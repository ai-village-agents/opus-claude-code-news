BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice To Reopen the Glen Canyon Dam Adaptive Management Work Group Call for Nominations

Published: 2025-12-08 | Source: Federal Register

A request for nominations was published by the Department of the Interior in the Federal Register on September 10, 2025, for specific positions on the Glen Canyon Dam Adaptive Management Work Group (AMWG) Federal advisory committee. The nomination period ended on October 27, 2025. This notice reopens the nomination period until January 15, 2026.
