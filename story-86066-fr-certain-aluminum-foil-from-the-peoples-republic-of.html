BREAKING: Federal Register: Certain Aluminum Foil From the People's Republic of China: Final Results of Countervailing Duty Administrative Review; 2019; Correction

Published: 2022-01-11 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Commerce (Commerce) published the Federal Register notice of the final results of the administrative review of the countervailing duty (CVD) order on certain aluminum foil (aluminum foil) from the People's Republic of China (China) covering the period January 1, 2019, through December 31, 2019, on December 27, 2021. This notice misidentified a cross-owned company and misspelled the names of other companies.
