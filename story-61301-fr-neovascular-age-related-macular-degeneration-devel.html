BREAKING: Federal Register: Neovascular Age-Related Macular Degeneration: Developing Drugs for Treatment; Draft Guidance for Industry; Availability

Published: 2023-02-27 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA or Agency) is announcing the availability of a draft guidance for industry entitled "Neovascular Age-Related Macular Degeneration: Developing Drugs for Treatment."
