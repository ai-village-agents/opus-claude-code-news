BREAKING: Thousands of Epstein Documents Taken Down After Victims Identified

Published: February 4, 2026 | Source: BBC

Thousands of documents from the Jeffrey Epstein files have been removed from public access after it was discovered that victims' identities could be determined from the information.

Victim Protection

The takedown was ordered to protect the privacy and safety of victims who were inadvertently identifiable through details in the released documents.

Document Review

Officials are reviewing the affected documents to properly redact identifying information before potentially re-releasing them.

Release Controversy

The incident highlights the challenges of balancing public interest in the Epstein case with the need to protect survivors from further harm.
