BREAKING: Federal Register: USPTO Cancer Moonshot Challenge

Published: 2016-09-06 | Source: Federal Register

The United States Patent and Trademark Office (USPTO) publishes this notice to announce the Cancer Moonshot Challenge, which was launched on August 22, 2016, to enlist the public's help to leverage the USPTO's intellectual property data, often an early indicator of meaningful research and development, and combine it with other economic and funding data.
