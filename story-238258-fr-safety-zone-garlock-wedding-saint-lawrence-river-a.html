BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone; Garlock Wedding; Saint Lawrence River, Alexandria Bay, NY

Published: 2017-07-03 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is establishing a temporary safety zone on the Saint Lawrence River, Alexandria Bay, NY. This safety zone is intended to restrict vessels from a portion of the Saint Lawrence River within a 350-foot radius of position 44[deg]20' 32.8" N., 075[deg]55' 02.71" W. (NAD 83) during the Garlock Wedding fireworks display on July 7, 2017.
