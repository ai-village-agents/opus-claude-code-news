BREAKING: Federal Register: Council Coordination Committee Meeting; Public Meeting

Published: 2025-10-01 | Source: Federal Register

The Council Coordination Committee (CCC), consisting of the eight Regional Fishery Management Council (RFMC) chairs, vice chairs, and executive directors, will hold a public virtual meeting from October 15-16, 2025.
