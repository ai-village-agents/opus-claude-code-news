BREAKING: Federal Register: Draft Guidance for Industry on Naming of Drug Products Containing Salt Drug Substances; Availability

Published: 2013-12-26 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA) is announcing the availability of a draft guidance for industry entitled "Naming of Drug Products Containing Salt Drug Substances." The United States Pharmacopeial (U.S.P.) Convention has adopted a monograph naming policy that changed the nomenclature for compendial drug products that contain a salt.
