BREAKING: Federal Register: New Animal Drugs; Approval of New Animal Drug Applications; Withdrawal of Approval of New Animal Drug Applications; Change of Sponsor; Change of Sponsors' Name and Addresses

Published: 2020-04-01 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA or we) is amending the animal drug regulations to reflect application-related actions for new animal drug applications (NADAs) and abbreviated new animal drug applications (ANADAs) during October, November, and December 2019. FDA is informing the public of the availability of summaries of the basis of approval and of environmental review documents, where applicable.
