BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice Regarding the Applicability of NHTSA FMVSS Test Procedures to Certifying Manufacturers

Published: 2020-12-21 | Source: Federal Register

The National Traffic and Motor Vehicle Safety Act (Safety Act) prohibits the sale, manufacture for sale, import or introduction into interstate commerce of a motor vehicle or item of motor vehicle equipment, unless fully compliant with all applicable Federal motor vehicle safety standards (FMVSS). The FMVSS set a threshold of performance that a vehicle or equipment item must attain, at a minimum, to meet the need for safety.
