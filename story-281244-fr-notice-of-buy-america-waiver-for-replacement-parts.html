BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Buy America Waiver for Replacement Parts on Diesel Multiple Unit Rail Vehicles

Published: 2016-12-12 | Source: Federal Register

The Federal Transit Administration (FTA) received a request from the North County Transit District (NCTD) in California for a Buy America non-availability waiver for the procurement of replacement parts for Diesel Multiple Unit (DMU) rail vehicles. The 12 DMU rail vehicles were manufactured by Siemens as a part of their Desiro series and were placed in revenue service in 2008.
