BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; GROB-WERKE Airplanes

Published: 2013-09-06 | Source: Federal Register

This document withdraws a notice of proposed rulemaking (NPRM) that would have applied to GROB-WERKE GMBH & CO KG Model G 115E airplanes. The proposed airworthiness directive (AD) would have required a one-time inspection to verify correct cable routing behind the LH cockpit instrument panel and, depending on findings, correction and replacement of damaged parts.
