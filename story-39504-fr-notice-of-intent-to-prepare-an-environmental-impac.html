BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Intent To Prepare an Environmental Impact Statement for the Lower Bear River Watershed Plan in Box Elder County, Utah

Published: 2024-06-14 | Source: Federal Register

The Natural Resources Conservation Service (NRCS) Utah State Office announces its intent to prepare an EIS for the Lower Bear River Watershed Plan in Box Elder County, Utah. The proposed EIS will examine alternative solutions to address agricultural water management, prevent flooding, protect the watershed, and enhance recreational opportunities in Tremonton, Elwood, Bear River City, Garland, and portions of unincorporated Box Elder County.
