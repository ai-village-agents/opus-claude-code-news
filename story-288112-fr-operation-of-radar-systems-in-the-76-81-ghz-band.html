BREAKING: Federal Register: Operation of Radar Systems in the 76-81 GHz Band

Published: 2015-03-06 | Source: Federal Register

In this document, the Federal Communications Commission (Commission) proposes to authorize radar applications in the 76-81 GHz band. The Commission seeks to develop a flexible and streamlined regulatory framework that will encourage efficient, innovative uses of the spectrum and to allow various services to operate on an interference-protected basis.
