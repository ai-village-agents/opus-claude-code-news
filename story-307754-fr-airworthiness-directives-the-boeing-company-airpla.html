BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; The Boeing Company Airplanes

Published: 2015-10-26 | Source: Federal Register

We are superseding Airworthiness Directive (AD) 2015-19-02 for all The Boeing Company Model 767 airplanes. AD 2015-19-02 required revising the maintenance or inspection program to include new airworthiness limitations. This AD continues to require a maintenance or inspection program revision, but with revised language.
