BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Opportunity for Public Comment on a Proposed Change of Airport Property Land Use From Aeronautical To Non-Aeronautical Use at Russellville Regional Airport, Russellville, AR

Published: 2022-04-13 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA is considering a request from the City of Russellville, Arkansas to change 39.14 acres, located on the northwest side of the airport bordered largely by East 16th Street and Airport Road, from aeronautical use to non-aeronautical use and to authorize the conversion of the airport property.
