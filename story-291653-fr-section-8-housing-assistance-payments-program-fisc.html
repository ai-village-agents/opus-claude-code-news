BREAKING: Federal Register: Section 8 Housing Assistance Payments Program-Fiscal Year (FY) 2015 Inflation Factors for Public Housing Agency (PHA) Renewal Funding

Published: 2015-04-20 | Source: Federal Register

The Consolidated Appropriations Act, 2015 requires that HUD apply "an inflation factor as established by the Secretary, by notice published in the Federal Register" to adjust FY 2015 renewal funding for the Tenant-based Rental Assistance Program or Housing Choice Voucher (HCV) Program of each PHA. HUD began using Renewal Funding Inflation Factors in FY 2012.
