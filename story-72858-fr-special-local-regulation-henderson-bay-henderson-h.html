BREAKING: Federal Register: Special Local Regulation; Henderson Bay, Henderson Harbor, NY

Published: 2023-07-26 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is establishing a permanent special local regulation for certain waters of Henderson Bay in Henderson Harbor, NY, in support of the Christmas in July festival. This action is necessary to provide for the safety of life on these navigable waters near Henderson Bay, Henderson Harbor, NY, during a boat parade.
