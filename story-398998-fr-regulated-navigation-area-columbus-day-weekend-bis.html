BREAKING: Federal Register: Regulated Navigation Area; Columbus Day Weekend, Biscayne Bay, Miami, FL

Published: 2012-10-15 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is amending the Columbus Day weekend regulated navigation area on Biscayne Bay in Miami, Florida. The amended regulated navigation area alters the boundaries of the area and expands the enforcement period. These regulations are necessary to protect the public during Columbus Day weekend; a period that has historically had a significant concentration of persons and vessels on the waters of Biscayne Bay.
