BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zones; Recurring Events in Captain of the Port Boston Zone

Published: 2013-06-14 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard proposes to add three new permanent safety zones to the Coast Guard Captain of the Port Boston Zone annual recurring marine events. When activated and subject to enforcement, these permanent safety zones would restrict vessels from portions of water areas during annual recurring events.
