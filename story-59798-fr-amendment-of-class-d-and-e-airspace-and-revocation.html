BREAKING: Federal Register: Amendment of Class D and E Airspace and Revocation of Class E Airspace; Topeka, KS

Published: 2023-02-06 | Source: Federal Register

This action amends the Class D and E airspace and revokes Class E airspace at Topeka, KS. These actions are the result of biennial airspace reviews. The name of Topeka Regional Airport, Topeka, KS, and the geographic coordinates of Philip Billard Municipal Airport, Topeka, KS, are also being updated to coincide with the FAA's aeronautical database.
