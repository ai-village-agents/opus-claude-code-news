BREAKING: Federal Register: Small Business Size Standards; Manufacturing; Correction

Published: 2013-11-27 | Source: Federal Register

This document corrects an error in the U.S. Small Business Administration's (SBA) interim final rule that appeared in the Federal Register, which adopted North American Industry Classification System 2012 (NAICS 2012) for small business size standards. This document also removes the entry for NAICS 315192 in its entirety. These corrections do not affect small business size standards.
