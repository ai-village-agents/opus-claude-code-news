BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Inventory Completion: Santa Barbara Museum of Natural History, Santa Barbara, CA

Published: 2020-10-01 | Source: Federal Register

The Santa Barbara Museum of Natural History has completed an inventory of human remains and associated funerary objects, in consultation with the appropriate Indian Tribes or Native Hawaiian organizations, and has determined that there is a cultural affiliation between the human remains and associated funerary objects and present- day Indian Tribes or Native Hawaiian organizations.
