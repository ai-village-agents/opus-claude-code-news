BREAKING: Federal Register: Transportation Worker Identification Credential (TWIC)-Reader Requirements; Second Delay of Effective Date

Published: 2022-12-06 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard proposes to further delay the effective date for certain facilities affected by the final rule entitled "Transportation Worker Identification Credential (TWIC)--Reader Requirements," published in the Federal Register on August 23, 2016. The current effective date for the final rule is May 8, 2023.
