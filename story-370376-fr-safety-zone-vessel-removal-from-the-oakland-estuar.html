BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone: Vessel Removal From the Oakland Estuary, Alameda, CA

Published: 2013-11-18 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is establishing a temporary safety zone in the navigable waters of the Oakland Estuary just north of the Park Street Bridge in Alameda, CA in support of the Oakland Estuary Closure for the Vessel Removal Project on November 4, 2013 through November 22, 2013.
