BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Issuance of Final Determination Concerning Certain Carbon Dioxide Sampling Line Products

Published: 2014-07-14 | Source: Federal Register

This document provides notice that U.S. Customs and Border Protection ("CBP") has issued a final determination concerning the country of origin of certain carbon dioxide sampling line products known as "FilterLine" and "CapnoLine." Based upon the facts presented, CBP has concluded that Israel is the country of origin for purposes of U.S. Government procurement.
