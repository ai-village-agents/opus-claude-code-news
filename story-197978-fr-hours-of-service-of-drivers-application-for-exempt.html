BREAKING: Federal Register: Hours of Service of Drivers: Application for Exemption; Motion Picture Association of America

Published: 2018-01-19 | Source: Federal Register

FMCSA grants the Motion Picture Association of America (MPAA) a five-year exemption from the electronic logging device (ELD) requirements for all commercial motor vehicle (CMV) drivers providing transportation to or from a theatrical or television motion picture production site. MPAA requested this exemption to allow these drivers to complete paper records of duty status (RODS) instead of using an ELD device.
