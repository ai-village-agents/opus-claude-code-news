BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone; Lake Tahoe Dive Operations, Lake Tahoe, CA

Published: 2021-10-08 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Coast Guard is establishing a safety zone, within Lake Tahoe in Lake Tahoe, CA, in support of Navy Special Warfare Group (NSWG-1) dive operations. This safety zone is necessary to provide for the safety of the divers while performing dive operations and to protect personnel, vessels, and the marine environment from potential hazards created by the NSWG-1 on-water training and associated operations.
