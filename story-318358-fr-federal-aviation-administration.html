BREAKING: Federal Register: Federal Aviation Administration

Published: 2014-03-10 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA proposes to rule and invite public comment on the release of land at the Ocean County Airport, Toms River, New Jersey under the provision 49 U.S.C. 47125(a). The request consists of a permanent release of six (6) parcels of land (145.90 acres) that are no longer needed for aeronautical purposes.
