BREAKING: Federal Register: Irish Potatoes Grown in Colorado; Modification of the Handling Regulation for Area No. 2

Published: 2013-01-02 | Source: Federal Register

This rule modifies the grade requirements for potatoes handled under the Colorado potato marketing order, Area No. 2 (order). The order regulates the handling of Irish potatoes grown in Colorado and is administered locally by the Colorado Potato Administrative Committee, Area No. 2 (Committee).
