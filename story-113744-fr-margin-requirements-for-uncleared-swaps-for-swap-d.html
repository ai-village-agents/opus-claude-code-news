BREAKING: Federal Register: Margin Requirements for Uncleared Swaps for Swap Dealers and Major Swap Participants

Published: 2021-01-05 | Source: Federal Register

The Commodity Futures Trading Commission ("Commission" or "CFTC") is adopting amendments ("Final Rule") to its margin requirements for uncleared swaps for swap dealers ("SDs") and major swap participants ("MSPs") for which there is not a prudential regulator ("CFTC Margin Rule").
