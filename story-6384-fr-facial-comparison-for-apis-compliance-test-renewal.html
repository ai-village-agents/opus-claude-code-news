BREAKING: Federal Register: Facial Comparison for APIS Compliance Test: Renewal of Test

Published: 2025-03-04 | Source: Federal Register

This notice announces that U.S. Customs and Border Protection (CBP) is renewing the Facial Comparison for Advance Passenger Information System (APIS) Compliance Test. Commercial air and sea carriers that voluntarily participate in this test use CBP's Traveler Verification Service (TVS) to comply with APIS regulatory requirements.
