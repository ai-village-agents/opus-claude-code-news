BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Establishment of Emergency Relief Docket for Calendar Year 2026

Published: 2026-01-30 | Source: Federal Register

By this notice, the Federal Transit Administration (FTA) is establishing an Emergency Relief Docket for calendar year 2026, so grantees and subgrantees affected by a national or regional emergency or disaster may request temporary relief from FTA administrative and statutory requirements.
