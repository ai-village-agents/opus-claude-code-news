BREAKING: Federal Register: Comprehensive Review of Licensing and Operating Rules for Satellite Services

Published: 2014-10-31 | Source: Federal Register

In this document, the Federal Communications Commission (Commission) proposes to amend its rules for licensing and operation of space stations and earth stations for communication by radio.
