BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Airbus Airplanes

Published: 2013-11-20 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA withdraws a notice of proposed rulemaking (NPRM) that proposed a new airworthiness directive (AD) for all Airbus Model A330 series airplanes. The NPRM would have required replacement of the elevator servo-controls with new servo-controls when the existing parts have reached their operational life limit.
