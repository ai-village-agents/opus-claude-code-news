BREAKING: Federal Register: Endangered Species; Marine Mammals; Seismic Survey Design and Impacts to Maternal Polar Bear Dens

Published: 2020-02-18 | Source: Federal Register

We, the U.S. Fish and Wildlife Service, announce the availability of a peer-reviewed scientific manuscript and associated model regarding seismic survey design and potential impacts to maternal polar bear dens. This manuscript contains information, including a methodology and model that may be used for evaluating the effects of future seismic survey proposals for their potential impacts to maternal polar bear dens.
