BREAKING: Federal Register: Railroads' Joint Request To Amend Their Positive Train Control Safety Plans

Published: 2023-11-13 | Source: Federal Register

This document provides the public with notice that on November 3, 2023, twenty host railroads submitted a joint request for amendment (RFA) to their FRA-approved Positive Train Control Safety Plans (PTCSP) to implement on-board software changes to the human-machine interface (HMI) which requires amendments to positive train control (PTC) training for train crews.
