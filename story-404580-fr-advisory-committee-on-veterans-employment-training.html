BREAKING: Federal Register: Advisory Committee on Veterans' Employment, Training and Employer Outreach (ACVETEO): Meeting

Published: 2012-12-26 | Source: Federal Register

This notice sets forth the schedule and proposed agenda of a forthcoming meeting of the Advisory Committee on Veterans' Employment, Training and Employer Outreach (ACVETEO). The ACVETEO will discuss Department of Labor's Veterans' Employment and Training Services' (VETS) core programs and new initiatives regarding efforts that assist veterans seeking employment and raise employer awareness as to the advantages of hiring veterans.
