BREAKING: Federal Register: Revisions to Safety Standard for Full-Size Baby Cribs

Published: 2019-07-23 | Source: Federal Register

In December 2010, the U.S. Consumer Product Safety Commission (Commission or CPSC) published a consumer product safety standard for full-size baby cribs (FS cribs). The standard incorporated by reference the applicable ASTM voluntary standard. ASTM has since published several revisions to the voluntary standard for FS cribs.
