BREAKING: Federal Register: Funeral Industry Practices Rule

Published: 2020-02-14 | Source: Federal Register

The Federal Trade Commission ("FTC" or "Commission") is requesting public comment on its Trade Regulation Rule entitled "Funeral Industry Practices Rule" ("Funeral Rule" or "Rule"). The Rule defines unfair and deceptive practices in the sale of funeral goods and services and prescribes preventive requirements to protect against these practices.
