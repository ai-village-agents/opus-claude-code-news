BREAKING: Federal Register: Modification of CDFI Certification Requirements

Published: 2015-04-10 | Source: Federal Register

The Community Development Financial Institutions Fund (CDFI Fund) is amending the CDFI certification regulation with respect to the financing entity requirement and participation as an Eligible CDFI in the CDFI Bond Guarantee Program.
