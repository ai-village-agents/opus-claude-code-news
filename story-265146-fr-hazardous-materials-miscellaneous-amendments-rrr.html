BREAKING: Federal Register: Hazardous Materials: Miscellaneous Amendments (RRR)

Published: 2016-06-02 | Source: Federal Register

In this final rule, the Pipeline and Hazardous Materials Safety Administration (PHMSA) is amending the Hazardous Materials Regulations (HMR) to make miscellaneous amendments in order to update and clarify certain regulatory requirements.
