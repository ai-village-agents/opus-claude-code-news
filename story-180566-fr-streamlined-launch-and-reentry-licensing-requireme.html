BREAKING: Federal Register: Streamlined Launch and Reentry Licensing Requirements; Extension of Comment Period

Published: 2019-05-31 | Source: Federal Register

This action extends the comment period for an NPRM that was published on April 15, 2019. In that document, the FAA proposed to streamline and increase the flexibility in the FAA's commercial space launch and reentry regulations. This action would consolidate and revise multiple regulatory parts and apply a single set of licensing and safety regulations across several types of operations and vehicles.
