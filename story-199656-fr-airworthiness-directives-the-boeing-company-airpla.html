BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; The Boeing Company Airplanes

Published: 2018-02-09 | Source: Federal Register

We propose to adopt a new airworthiness directive (AD) for all The Boeing Company Model 787 series airplanes. This proposed AD was prompted by reports that, under certain conditions, the automatic dependent surveillance--broadcast (ADS-B) out function and air traffic control/traffic alert and collision avoidance system (ATC/TCAS) functions can transmit incorrect data.
