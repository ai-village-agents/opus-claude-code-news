BREAKING: Federal Register: Transactions Other Than Contracts, Grants, or Cooperative Agreements for Prototype Projects

Published: 2024-09-04 | Source: Federal Register

DoD is proposing revisions to its regulations on Other Transaction (OT) agreements for prototype projects to implement changes in statutory authority enacted by Congress since the last update in 2004.
