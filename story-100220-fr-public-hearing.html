BREAKING: Federal Register: Public Hearing

Published: 2022-07-12 | Source: Federal Register

The Susquehanna River Basin Commission will hold a public hearing on August 11, 2022. The Commission will hold this hearing in- person and telephonically. At this public hearing, the Commission will hear testimony on the projects listed in the Supplementary Information section of this notice. The Commission will also hear testimony on two proposed policies, SRBC Civil Penalty Matrix and Policy and Guidance Statement for the Settlement of Civil Penalties/Enforcement Actions.
