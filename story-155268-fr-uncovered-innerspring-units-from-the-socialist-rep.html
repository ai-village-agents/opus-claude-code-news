BREAKING: Federal Register: Uncovered Innerspring Units From the Socialist Republic of Vietnam: Preliminary Results of the Antidumping Duty Administrative Review; 2018-2019

Published: 2020-07-01 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Commerce (Commerce) preliminarily determines that the sole company subject to this administrative review is part of the Vietnam-wide entity because it did not file a separate rate application (SRA). The period of review (POR) is December 1, 2018 through November 31, 2019. We invite interested parties to comment on these preliminary results.
