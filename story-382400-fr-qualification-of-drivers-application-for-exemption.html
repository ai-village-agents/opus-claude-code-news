BREAKING: Federal Register: Qualification of Drivers; Application for Exemptions; Implantable Cardioverter Defibrillators

Published: 2012-04-06 | Source: Federal Register

FMCSA announces receipt of applications from four individuals for an exemption from the prohibition against persons with an implantable cardioverter defibrillator (ICD) in the Federal Motor Carrier Safety Regulations (FMCSRs), due to syncope or likelihood of causing any loss of ability to operate a commercial motor vehicle (CMV) safely.
