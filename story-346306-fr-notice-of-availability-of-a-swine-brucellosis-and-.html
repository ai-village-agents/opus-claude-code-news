BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Availability of a Swine Brucellosis and Pseudorabies Proposed Action Plan

Published: 2013-02-07 | Source: Federal Register

We are making a proposed action plan describing a potential new approach to managing swine brucellosis and pseudorabies available for public review and comment. Swine brucellosis and pseudorabies have been eliminated from commercial swine herds within the United States, but potential sources of introduction of these diseases exist and we believe program modifications are necessary to address these risks.
