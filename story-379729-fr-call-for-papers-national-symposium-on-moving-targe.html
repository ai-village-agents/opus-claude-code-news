BREAKING: Federal Register: Call for Papers: National Symposium on Moving Target Research

Published: 2012-03-07 | Source: Federal Register

This Call for Papers is being issued by the National Coordination Office for the Networking and Information Technology Research and Development (NITRD) Program to initiate the National Symposium on Moving Target Research. The Symposium intends to bring together and publish the work of the Moving Target cybersecurity research community to provide a basis for building on the current state of the art.
