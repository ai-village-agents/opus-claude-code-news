BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Anjou Aeronautique Torso Restraint Systems

Published: 2019-12-31 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA proposes to supersede airworthiness directive (AD) 2017-16-04 which applies to certain Anjou Aeronautique (formerly Romtex Anjou Aeronautique) Model 358 torso restraint systems (restraint systems). AD 2017-16-04 required inspection of the restraint system, placarding if it is found to be inoperative, and replacement of the affected restraint system with a part eligible for installation.
