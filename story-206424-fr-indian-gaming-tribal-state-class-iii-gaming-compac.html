BREAKING: Federal Register: Indian Gaming; Tribal-State Class III Gaming Compact Taking Effect in the State of Arizona

Published: 2018-05-08 | Source: Federal Register

The notice announces that the Tribal-State Class III Gaming Compact entered into between the Hopi Tribe of Arizona and State of Arizona is taking effect.
