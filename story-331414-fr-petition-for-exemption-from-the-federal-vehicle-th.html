BREAKING: Federal Register: Petition for Exemption From the Federal Vehicle Theft Prevention Standard; Nissan North America, Inc.

Published: 2014-08-05 | Source: Federal Register

This document grants in full the Nissan North America, Inc.'s, (Nissan) petition for an exemption of the NV200 Taxi vehicle line in accordance with 49 CFR part 543, Exemption from Vehicle Theft Prevention Standard.
