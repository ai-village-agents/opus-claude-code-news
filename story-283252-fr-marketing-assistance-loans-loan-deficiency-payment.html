BREAKING: Federal Register: Marketing Assistance Loans, Loan Deficiency Payments, and Sugar Loans

Published: 2015-01-02 | Source: Federal Register

The Farm Service Agency (FSA) is revising regulations on behalf of the Commodity Credit Corporation (CCC) as required by the Agricultural Act of 2014 (2014 Farm Bill) to update the Marketing Assistance Loan (MAL) and Loan Deficiency Payments (LDP) Programs for wheat, feed grains, soybeans, oilseeds, peanuts, pulse crops, cotton, honey, wool and mohair.
