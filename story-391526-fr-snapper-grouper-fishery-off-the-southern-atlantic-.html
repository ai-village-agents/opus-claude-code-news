BREAKING: Federal Register: Snapper-Grouper Fishery Off the Southern Atlantic States; Snapper-Grouper Management Measures

Published: 2012-07-20 | Source: Federal Register

NMFS publishes this proposed rule to implement a regulatory amendment (Regulatory Amendment 12) to the Fishery Management Plan for the Snapper-Grouper Fishery of the South Atlantic Region (FMP), as prepared by the South Atlantic Fishery Management Council (Council).
