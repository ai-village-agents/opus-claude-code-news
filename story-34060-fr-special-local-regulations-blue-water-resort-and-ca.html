BREAKING: Federal Register: Special Local Regulations; Blue Water Resort and Casino Spring Classic; Parker, Arizona

Published: 2024-04-10 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard will enforce the Blue Water Resort and Casino Spring Classic special local regulations on the waters of Parker, AZ from April 13, 2024, to April 14, 2024. These special local regulations are necessary to provide for the safety of the participants, crew, spectators, sponsor vessels, and general users of the waterway.
