BREAKING: Federal Register: Request for Comments and Announcement of Workshop on Pet Medications Issues

Published: 2012-09-24 | Source: Federal Register

The FTC is reopening the period for filing public comments in connection with a workshop to examine competition and consumer protection issues in the pet medications industry.
