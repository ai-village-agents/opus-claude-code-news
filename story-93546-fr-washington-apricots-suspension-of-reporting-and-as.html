BREAKING: Federal Register: Washington Apricots; Suspension of Reporting and Assessment Requirements

Published: 2022-04-13 | Source: Federal Register

This final rule suspends the reporting and assessment requirements prescribed under the marketing order regulating apricots grown in designated counties in Washington (Marketing Order No. 922). In a separate meeting, the State of Washington Apricot Marketing Committee also unanimously recommended terminating Marketing Order No. 922.
