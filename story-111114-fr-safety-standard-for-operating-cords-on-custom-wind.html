BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Standard for Operating Cords on Custom Window Coverings

Published: 2022-11-28 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Consumer Product Safety Commission (Commission or CPSC) has determined that custom window coverings with accessible operating cords longer than 8 inches pose an unreasonable risk of strangulation to children 8 years old and younger.
