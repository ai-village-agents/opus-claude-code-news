BREAKING: Federal Register: Schedules of Controlled Substances: Temporary Placement of Three Synthetic Phenethylamines Into Schedule I

Published: 2013-10-10 | Source: Federal Register

The Deputy Administrator of the Drug Enforcement Administration (DEA) is issuing this notice of intent to temporarily schedule three synthetic phenethylamines into the Controlled Substances Act (CSA) pursuant to the temporary scheduling provisions of 21 U.S.C. 811(h).
