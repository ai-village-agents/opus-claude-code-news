BREAKING: Federal Register: Clearing Exemption for Swaps Between Certain Affiliated Entities

Published: 2012-08-21 | Source: Federal Register

The Commodity Futures Trading Commission ("CFTC" or "Commission") is proposing a rule to exempt swaps between certain affiliated entities within a corporate group from the clearing requirement (the "inter-affiliate clearing exemption" or the "proposed exemption") under Section 2(h)(1)(A) of the Commodity Exchange Act ("CEA").
