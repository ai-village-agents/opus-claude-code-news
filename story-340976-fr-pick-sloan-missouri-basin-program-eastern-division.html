BREAKING: Federal Register: Pick-Sloan Missouri Basin Program-Eastern Division-Rate Order No. WAPA-168

Published: 2014-12-02 | Source: Federal Register

This action extends the existing transmission and ancillary services rates for the Pick-Sloan Missouri Basin Program--Eastern Division (P-SMB--ED) through December 31, 2016. The existing Rate Schedules UGP-NT1, UGP-FPT1, UGP-NFPT1, UGP-AS1, UGP-AS2, UGP-AS3, UGP- AS4, UGP-AS5, UGP-AS6, UGP-AS7, and UGP-TSP1 are set to expire on December 31, 2014.
