BREAKING: Federal Register: Establishment of and Modification to Restricted Areas; Fort Sill, OK

Published: 2016-11-15 | Source: Federal Register

This action establishes 2 new restricted areas (R-5601G and R- 5601H) to the special use airspace (SUA) complex located at Fort Sill, OK, to provide additional maneuvering airspace for current and planned hazardous training activities.
