BREAKING: Federal Register: Karis Copper Delong: Debarment Order

Published: 2017-02-17 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA or Agency) is issuing an order under the Federal Food, Drug, and Cosmetic Act (the FD&C Act) debarring Karis Copper Delong for a period of 12 years from providing services in any capacity to a person that has an approved or pending drug product application.
