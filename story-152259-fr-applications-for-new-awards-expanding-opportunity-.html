BREAKING: Federal Register: Applications for New Awards; Expanding Opportunity Through Quality Charter Schools Program (CSP)--Grants to Charter School Developers for the Opening of New Charter Schools and for the Replication and Expansion of High-Quality Charter Schools (Developer Grants)

Published: 2020-05-22 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Education (Department) is issuing a notice inviting applications for new awards for fiscal year (FY) 2020 for CSP--Developer Grants, Catalog of Federal Domestic Assistance (CFDA) numbers 84.282B (for the opening of new charter schools) and 84.282E (for the replication and expansion of high-quality charter schools). This notice relates to the approved information collection under OMB control number 1894-0006.
