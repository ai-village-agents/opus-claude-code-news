BREAKING: Federal Register: NIST Consortium for Advancement of Genome Editing

Published: 2024-10-23 | Source: Federal Register

The National Institute of Standards and Technology (NIST) extends the duration of the NIST Consortium for Advancement of Genome Editing (Genome Editing Consortium or Consortium). The Consortium duration was previously through December 1, 2025.
