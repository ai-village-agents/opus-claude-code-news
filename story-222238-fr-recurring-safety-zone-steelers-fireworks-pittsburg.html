BREAKING: Federal Register: Recurring Safety Zone; Steelers Fireworks, Pittsburgh, PA

Published: 2018-11-30 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard will enforce the safety zone for the Pittsburgh Steelers Fireworks to provide for the safety of persons, vessels, and the marine environment on the navigable waters of the Allegheny, Ohio, and Monongahela Rivers during this event. Our regulation for marine events within the Eighth Coast Guard District identifies the regulated area for this event in Pittsburgh, PA.
