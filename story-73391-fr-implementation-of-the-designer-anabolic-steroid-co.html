BREAKING: Federal Register: Implementation of the Designer Anabolic Steroid Control Act of 2014

Published: 2023-08-01 | Source: Federal Register

On December 18, 2014, the Designer Anabolic Steroid Control Act of 2014 (DASCA) became law. The Act amended the Controlled Substances Act to revise and add specified substances to the definition of "anabolic steroid." The Act provided a new mechanism for temporary and permanent scheduling of anabolic steroids, and added specific labeling requirements for products containing anabolic steroids.
