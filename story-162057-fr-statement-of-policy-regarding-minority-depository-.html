BREAKING: Federal Register: Statement of Policy Regarding Minority Depository Institutions

Published: 2020-09-25 | Source: Federal Register

The FDIC is proposing to revise its Statement of Policy Regarding Minority Depository Institutions. Section 308 of the Financial Institutions Reform, Recovery and Enforcement Act of 1989 established several goals related to encouraging, assisting, and preserving minority depository institutions.
