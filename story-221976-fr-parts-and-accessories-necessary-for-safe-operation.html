BREAKING: Federal Register: Parts and Accessories Necessary for Safe Operation; Application for an Exemption From SmartDrive Systems, Inc.

Published: 2018-11-27 | Source: Federal Register

The Federal Motor Carrier Safety Administration (FMCSA) requests public comment on an application for exemption from SmartDrive Systems, Inc. (SmartDrive) to allow an Advanced Driver Assistance Systems (ADAS) camera to be mounted lower in the windshield than is currently permitted. Mounting the camera in this location does not meet the driver's field of view requirements for windshields.
