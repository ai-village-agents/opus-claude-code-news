BREAKING: Federal Register: Mississippi Disaster Number MS-00064

Published: 2013-03-08 | Source: Federal Register

This is an amendment of the Presidential declaration of a major disaster for the State of Mississippi (FEMA-4101-DR), dated 02/ 13/2013. Incident: Severe storms, tornadoes, and flooding. Incident Period: 02/10/2013 through 02/22/2013. Effective Date: 03/01/2013. Physical Loan Application Deadline Date: 04/15/2013. EIDL Loan Application Deadline Date: 11/13/2013.
