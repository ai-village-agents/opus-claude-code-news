BREAKING: Federal Register: Draft List of Bird Species to Which the Migratory Bird Treaty Act Does Not Apply

Published: 2018-11-28 | Source: Federal Register

We, the U.S. Fish and Wildlife Service, are publishing a draft list of the nonnative bird species that have been introduced by humans into the United States or U.S. territories and to which the Migratory Bird Treaty Act (MBTA) does not apply.
