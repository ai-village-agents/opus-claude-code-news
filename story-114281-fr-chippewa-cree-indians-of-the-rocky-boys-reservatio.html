BREAKING: Federal Register: Chippewa Cree Indians of the Rocky Boy's Reservation; Amendment to Liquor Control Ordinance

Published: 2021-01-12 | Source: Federal Register

This notice publishes an amendment to the Liquor Ordinance of the Chippewa Cree Indians of the Rocky Boy's Indian Reservation of Montana (Chippewa Cree Tribe). The Chippewa Cree Tribe and the State of Montana have agreed to enter into a Montana Alcoholic Beverages Tax Agreement.
