BREAKING: Federal Register: Waybill Data Released in Three-Benchmark Rail Rate Proceedings

Published: 2012-03-19 | Source: Federal Register

When a shipper files a formal complaint that a railroad's rate is too high, the Surface Transportation Board (Board) must determine whether the challenged rate is reasonable. To present its case using the Board's procedures for small cases, the complaining shipper needs to obtain from the Board confidential information that the Board collects regarding the rates that the defendant railroad charges other shippers for similar shipments.
