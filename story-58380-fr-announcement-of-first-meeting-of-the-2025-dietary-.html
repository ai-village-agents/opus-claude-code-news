BREAKING: Federal Register: Announcement of First Meeting of the 2025 Dietary Guidelines Advisory Committee and Request for Comments

Published: 2023-01-19 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Departments of Health and Human Services and Agriculture announce the first meeting of the newly appointed 2025 Dietary Guidelines Advisory Committee (Committee). This meeting will be open to the public virtually. Additionally, this notice opens a public comment period that will remain open until late 2024, throughout the Committee's deliberations.
