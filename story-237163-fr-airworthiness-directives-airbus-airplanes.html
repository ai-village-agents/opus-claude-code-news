BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Airbus Airplanes

Published: 2017-06-20 | Source: Federal Register

We propose to adopt a new airworthiness directive (AD) for all Airbus Model A310-203, -204, -221, -222, -304, -322, -324, and -325 airplanes. This proposed AD was prompted by reports of cracking in the drainage holes on the lower skin panel in the center wing box between frames (FR) 42 and FR 46.
