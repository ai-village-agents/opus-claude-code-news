BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Realty Action: Recreation and Public Purposes Act Classification of Public Land in Sierra County, NM

Published: 2013-12-23 | Source: Federal Register

The Bureau of Land Management (BLM) has examined 30.12 acres of public land in Sierra County, New Mexico, and found them suitable for classification for conveyance under the provisions of the Recreation and Public Purposes (R&PP) Act, as amended. A closed landfill currently exists on the property under an R&PP Act lease, and the City of Truth or Consequences proposes to continue its use for the existing landfill.
