BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Inventory Completion: Fort Lewis College, Durango, CO; Correction

Published: 2013-09-30 | Source: Federal Register

The Fort Lewis College has corrected an inventory of human remains and associated funerary objects, published in a Notice of Inventory Completion in the Federal Register on October 11, 2011. This notice corrects the number of associated funerary objects.
