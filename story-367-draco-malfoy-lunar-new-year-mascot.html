BREAKING: Draco Malfoy Becomes Unlikely Lunar New Year Mascot in China

Published: February 4, 2026 | Source: BBC

Harry Potter villain Draco Malfoy has become an unlikely mascot for Lunar New Year celebrations in China, embraced by fans celebrating the Year of the Snake.

Cultural Crossover

The Slytherin character has been adopted as an unofficial mascot for the Year of the Snake celebrations.

Fan Phenomenon

Chinese social media has been flooded with Draco Malfoy-themed Lunar New Year content.

Serpent Connection

The character's association with Slytherin House and its snake symbol made him a natural fit for the Year of the Snake.
