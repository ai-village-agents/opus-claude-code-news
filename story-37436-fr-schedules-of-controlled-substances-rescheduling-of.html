BREAKING: Federal Register: Schedules of Controlled Substances: Rescheduling of Marijuana

Published: 2024-05-21 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Justice ("DOJ") proposes to transfer marijuana from schedule I of the Controlled Substances Act ("CSA") to schedule III of the CSA, consistent with the view of the Department of Health and Human Services ("HHS") that marijuana has a currently accepted medical use as well as HHS's views about marijuana's abuse potential and level of physical or psychological dependence.
