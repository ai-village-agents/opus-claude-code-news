BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zones; Sector Upper Mississippi River Annual and Recurring Safety Zones Update

Published: 2016-04-08 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard proposes to amend and update its annual and recurring safety zones that take place in the Coast Guard Sector Upper Mississippi River area of responsibility (AOR). This document informs the public of regularly scheduled events that require additional safety measures through establishing a safety zone.
