BREAKING: Federal Register: Outsourcing Facility Fee Rates for Fiscal Year 2015

Published: 2014-08-01 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA or the Agency) is announcing the rates for fiscal year (FY) 2015 for the establishment and reinspection fees related to human drug compounding outsourcing facilities (outsourcing facilities) that elect to register under the Federal Food, Drug, and Cosmetic Act (the FD&C Act).
