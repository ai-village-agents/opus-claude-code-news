BREAKING: Federal Register: Determination Concerning a Petition to Add a Class of Employees to the Special Exposure Cohort

Published: 2014-11-06 | Source: Federal Register

HHS gives notice of a determination concerning a petition to add a class of employees from the Simonds Saw and Steel Company in Lockport, New York, to the Special Exposure Cohort (SEC) under the Energy Employees Occupational Illness Compensation Program Act of 2000 (EEOICPA).
