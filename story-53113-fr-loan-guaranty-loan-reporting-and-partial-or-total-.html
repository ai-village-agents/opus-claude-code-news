BREAKING: Federal Register: Loan Guaranty: Loan Reporting and Partial or Total Loss of Guaranty or Insurance

Published: 2024-11-20 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Veterans Affairs (VA) proposes to amend its regulations governing loan reporting requirements for lenders that participate in the VA-guaranteed home loan program and circumstances when VA would assert a defense for partial or total loss of guaranty or insurance for lenders and holders.
