BREAKING: Federal Register: Proposed Amendment of Class E Airspace; Pratt, KS

Published: 2019-12-16 | Source: Federal Register

This action proposes to amend the Class E airspace extending upward from 700 feet above the surface at Pratt Regional Airport, Pratt, KS. The FAA is proposing this action as the result of an airspace review caused by the decommissioning of the Pratt non- directional beacon (NDB), which provided navigation information for the instrument procedures at this airport.
