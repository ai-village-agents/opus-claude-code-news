BREAKING: Federal Register: Special Local Regulations; Marine Events in the Coast Guard Sector Detroit Captain of the Port Zone

Published: 2019-06-28 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard will enforce various special local regulations for annual regattas and marine parades in the Captain of the Port Detroit zone. Enforcement of these regulations is necessary and intended to protect the safety of life on the navigable waters immediately prior to, during, and after these regattas or marine parades.
