BREAKING: Federal Register: Real Estate Appraisals

Published: 2019-11-29 | Source: Federal Register

The NCUA Board (Board) proposes to amend the agency's regulation requiring appraisals for certain real estate-related transactions. The proposed rule would increase the threshold level below which appraisals would not be required for residential real estate-related transactions from $250,000 to $400,000.
