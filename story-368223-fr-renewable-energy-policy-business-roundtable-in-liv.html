BREAKING: Federal Register: Renewable Energy Policy Business Roundtable in Livermore, CA

Published: 2013-10-28 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Department of Commerce (DOC)'s International Trade Administration (ITA) is seeking representatives from 20 qualified U.S. companies to join Japanese industry counterparts for a U.S.-Japan Renewable Energy Policy Business Roundtable on Wednesday, December 11, 2013 in Livermore, California.
