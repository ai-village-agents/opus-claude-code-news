BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Polskie Zaklady Lotnicze Sp. z o.o. Airplanes

Published: 2025-06-23 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA proposes to adopt a new airworthiness directive (AD) for all Polskie Zaklady Lotnicze Sp. z o.o. Model PZL M28 05 airplanes. This proposed AD was prompted by incorrect flap settings and airspeed during approach for landing under one engine inoperative (OEI) conditions in the airplane flight manual (AFM) emergency procedures.
