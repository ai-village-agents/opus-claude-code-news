BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; The Boeing Company Airplanes

Published: 2020-08-25 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA is superseding Airworthiness Directive (AD) 2019-03- 06, which applied to certain The Boeing Company Model 737-300, -400, and -500 series airplanes. AD 2019-03-06 required installing lanyard assemblies on the passenger service units (PSUs) and, for certain airplanes, on the life vest panels.
