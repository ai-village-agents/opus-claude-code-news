BREAKING: Federal Register: Gulf of Mexico, Outer Continental Shelf (OCS), Western Planning Area (WPA) and Central Planning Area (CPA), Oil and Gas Lease Sales for 2012-2017

Published: 2012-07-06 | Source: Federal Register

BOEM has prepared a Multisale FEIS on oil and gas lease sales tentatively scheduled during the period 2012-2017 in the WPA and CPA offshore the States of Texas, Louisiana, Mississippi, and Alabama. Under the Proposed Final Outer Continental Shelf Oil and Gas Leasing Program: 2012-2017 (Five-Year Program), five annual areawide lease sales are scheduled for the WPA and five annual areawide lease sales are scheduled for the CPA.
