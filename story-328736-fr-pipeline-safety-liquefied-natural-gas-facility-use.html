BREAKING: Federal Register: Pipeline Safety: Liquefied Natural Gas Facility User Fee Rate Increase

Published: 2014-07-03 | Source: Federal Register

This notice is to advise all liquefied natural gas facility (LNG) operators subject to PHMSA user fee billing of a change in the LNG user fee rates to align these rates with the actual allocation of PHMSA resources to LNG program costs. Specifically, the LNG user fee rates will increase to 5 percent of the total gas program costs.
