BREAKING: Federal Register: Temporary Changes to Requirements Affecting H-2B Nonimmigrants Due to the COVID-19 National Emergency

Published: 2020-05-14 | Source: Federal Register

As a result of disruptions and uncertainty to the U.S. economy and international travel caused by the global novel Coronavirus Disease 2019 (COVID-19) public health emergency, the Department of Homeland Security (the Department or DHS), U.S. Citizenship and Immigration Services (USCIS), has decided to temporarily amend the regulations regarding certain temporary nonagricultural workers, and their U.S. employers, within the H-2B nonimmigrant classification.
