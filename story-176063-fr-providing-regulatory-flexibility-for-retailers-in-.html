BREAKING: Federal Register: Providing Regulatory Flexibility for Retailers in the Supplemental Nutrition Assistance Program (SNAP)

Published: 2019-04-05 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Nutrition Service (FNS, or the Agency) proposes to make changes to the Supplemental Nutrition Assistance Program (SNAP) regulations pertaining to the eligibility of certain SNAP retail food stores.
