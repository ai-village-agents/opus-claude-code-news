BREAKING: Federal Register: Mass Digitization Pilot Program; Request for Comments

Published: 2015-06-09 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Copyright Office is developing a limited pilot program and corresponding draft legislation that would establish a legal framework known as extended collective licensing for certain mass digitization activities that are currently beyond the reach of the Copyright Act.
