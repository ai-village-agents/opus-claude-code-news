BREAKING: Federal Register: Implement the Infrastructure Investment and Jobs Act: Prevention and Elimination of Digital Discrimination

Published: 2024-02-01 | Source: Federal Register

In this document, the Federal Communications Commission (Commission) proposes rules regarding affirmative obligations for broadband providers, through: annual reports that facilitate greater transparency regarding substantial broadband projects recently completed by providers, and internal compliance programs requiring periodic evaluation of the demographics of communities served--and not served--by such recently completed projects, as well as pending and planned substantial projects.
