BREAKING: Federal Register: Drawbridge Operation Regulation; Pass Manchac, Manchac, LA

Published: 2016-11-03 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard has issued a temporary deviation from the operating schedule that governs the Canadian National (CN) Railroad automated bascule span drawbridge across Pass Manchac, mile 6.7 at Manchac, between St. John and Tangipahoa Parishes, Louisiana. The deviation is necessary to accommodate bridge repair work essential for the continued operation of the bridge.
