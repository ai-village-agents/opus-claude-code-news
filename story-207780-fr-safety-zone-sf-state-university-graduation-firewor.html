BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone: SF State University Graduation Fireworks Display, San Francisco Bay, San Francisco, CA

Published: 2018-05-24 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is establishing a temporary moving safety zone in the navigable waters of the San Francisco Bay near AT&T Park in support of the San Francisco State University Fireworks Display on May 24, 2018. This safety zone is established to ensure the safety of participants and spectators from the dangers associated with pyrotechnics.
