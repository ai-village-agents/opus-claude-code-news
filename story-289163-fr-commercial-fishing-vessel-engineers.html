BREAKING: Federal Register: Commercial Fishing Vessel Engineers

Published: 2015-03-20 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard announces the availability of an updated policy letter entitled "Engineer Officer Endorsements on Uninspected Commercial Fishing Industry Vessels." The letter provides guidance to Coast Guard officers with respect to the upcoming enforcement of existing requirements for the proper credentialing of engineering officers.
