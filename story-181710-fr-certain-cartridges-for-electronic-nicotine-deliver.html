BREAKING: Federal Register: Certain Cartridges for Electronic Nicotine Delivery Systems and Components Thereof; Commission Determination Not To Review an Initial Determination To Amend the Complaint and Notice of Investigation To Correct the Name of a Corporate Respondent

Published: 2019-06-17 | Source: Federal Register

Notice is hereby given that the U.S. International Trade Commission (the "Commission") has determined not to review an initial determination ("ID") (Order No. 29) to amend the complaint and notice of investigation to correct the name of a corporate respondent.
