BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone; San Diego Symphony Summer Pops, San Diego Bay; San Diego, CA

Published: 2014-07-02 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard will enforce the 2014 San Diego Symphony Summer Pops fireworks display safety zone on Saturday and Sunday evenings from June 28, 2014 thru August 31, 2014, as well as on Friday August 29, 2014. The brief fireworks displays are scheduled to occur between 9 p.m. to 10 p.m., to coincide with the end of the concert.
