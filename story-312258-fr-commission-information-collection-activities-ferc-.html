BREAKING: Federal Register: Commission Information Collection Activities (FERC-725K); Comment Request

Published: 2015-12-22 | Source: Federal Register

In compliance with the requirements of the Paperwork Reduction Act of 1995, 44 U.S.C. 3507(a)(1)(D), the Federal Energy Regulatory Commission (Commission or FERC) is submitting its information collection FERC-725K (Mandatory Reliability Standards for the SERC Region) to the Office of Management and Budget (OMB) for review of the information collection requirements.
