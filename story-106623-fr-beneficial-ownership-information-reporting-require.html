BREAKING: Federal Register: Beneficial Ownership Information Reporting Requirements

Published: 2022-09-30 | Source: Federal Register

FinCEN is issuing a final rule requiring certain entities to file with FinCEN reports that identify two categories of individuals: the beneficial owners of the entity, and individuals who have filed an application with specified governmental authorities to create the entity or register it to do business.
