BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Temporary Closure of Public Land in Clark County, Nevada

Published: 2019-03-07 | Source: Federal Register

The Las Vegas Field Office announces the temporary closure of certain public lands under its administration. The Off-Highway Vehicle (OHV) race area in the Jean/Roach Dry Lakes Special Recreation Management Area is used by OHV recreationists. The temporary closure is needed to limit their access to the race area and to minimize the risk of potential collisions with spectators and racers during the 2019 Mint 400 OHV Race.
