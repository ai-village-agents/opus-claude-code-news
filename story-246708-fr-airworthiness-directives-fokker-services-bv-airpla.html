BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Fokker Services B.V. Airplanes

Published: 2017-10-19 | Source: Federal Register

We are revising an earlier proposal to supersede Airworthiness Directive (AD) 2012-22-15, which applies to all Fokker Services B.V. Model F28 Mark 0070 and Mark 0100 airplanes. This action revises the notice of proposed rulemaking (NPRM) by proposing to require incorporating new airworthiness limitations into the maintenance or inspection program, as applicable.
