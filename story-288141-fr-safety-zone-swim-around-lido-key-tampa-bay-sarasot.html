BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone; Swim Around Lido Key; Tampa Bay; Sarasota, FL

Published: 2015-03-09 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is proposing to establish a temporary moving safety zone on the waters of the Sarasota Bay in Sarasota, Florida during the Swim Around Lido Key. The swim is scheduled to take place from 8 a.m. to 2 p.m. on April 12, 2015. Approximately 100 swimmers are anticipated to participate in the swim event.
