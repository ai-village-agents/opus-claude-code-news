BREAKING: Federal Register: National Advisory Council on Indian Education (NACIE)

Published: 2025-06-05 | Source: Federal Register

On May 5, 2025, the Department of Education (Department) published in the Federal Register a notice of an open meeting for June 18-19, 2025. The Department is amending the dates to June 17-18, 2025. This notice provides information about the meeting updates to members of the public who may be interested in virtually attending the meeting and how to provide written and/or oral comment for the meeting. Notice of this meeting is required by the Federal Advisory Committee Act.
