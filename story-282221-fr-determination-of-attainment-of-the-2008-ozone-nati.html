BREAKING: Federal Register: Determination of Attainment of the 2008 Ozone National Ambient Air Quality Standards; Mariposa County, California

Published: 2016-12-21 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is taking direct final action to determine that the Mariposa County, California Moderate Nonattainment Area (NAA) has attained the 2008 8-hour ozone National Ambient Air Quality Standards (NAAQS or "standards"). This determination is based on complete, quality-assured and certified data for 2013-2015.
