BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Request for Information; National Strategic Plan for Advanced Manufacturing

Published: 2025-09-16 | Source: Federal Register

The Office of Science and Technology Policy published a document in the Federal Register on June 20, 2025, requesting input from all interested parties on the development of a National Strategic Plan for Advanced Manufacturing. This document extends the deadline for submission of responses.
