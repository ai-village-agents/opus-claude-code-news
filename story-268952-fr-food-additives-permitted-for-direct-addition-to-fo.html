BREAKING: Federal Register: Food Additives Permitted for Direct Addition to Food for Human Consumption; Vitamin D2 and Vitamin D3

Published: 2016-07-18 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA or we) is amending the food additive regulations to expand the safe uses of vitamin D2 as a nutrient supplement in edible plant-based beverages intended for use as milk alternatives and in edible plant-based yogurt alternatives and vitamin D3 as a nutrient supplement in milk at levels higher than those currently permitted.
