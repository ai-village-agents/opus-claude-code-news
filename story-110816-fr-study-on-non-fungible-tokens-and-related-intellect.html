BREAKING: Federal Register: Study on Non-Fungible Tokens and Related Intellectual Property Law Issues

Published: 2022-11-23 | Source: Federal Register

The United States Patent and Trademark Office (USPTO) and the United States Copyright Office (USCO) (collectively, the Offices) are conducting a joint study regarding issues of intellectual property (IP) law and policy associated with non-fungible tokens (NFTs). The Offices seek public comments on these matters to assist in their work on IP policy related to NFTs and in conducting the study.
