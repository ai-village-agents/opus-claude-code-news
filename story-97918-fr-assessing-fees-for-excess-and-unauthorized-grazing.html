BREAKING: Federal Register: Assessing Fees for Excess and Unauthorized Grazing

Published: 2022-06-09 | Source: Federal Register

The Forest Service (Agency), U.S. Department of Agriculture, hereby adopts this final rule to amend existing regulations for the provision of an option to waive excess and unauthorized grazing fees when excess or unauthorized grazing is determined to be a result of unforeseen or uncontrollable circumstances.
