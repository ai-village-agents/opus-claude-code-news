BREAKING: Federal Register: Nixon Presidential Historical Materials: Opening of Materials

Published: 2015-04-17 | Source: Federal Register

The National Archives and Records Administration's (NARA) Richard Nixon Presidential Library and Museum is opening additional Nixon Presidential Historical Materials for public access. These materials include previously restricted materials and select White House Central Files, Name Files.
