BREAKING: Federal Register: Full Approval of Revised Clean Air Act Operating Permit Program; North Dakota

Published: 2021-02-24 | Source: Federal Register

With this direct final rule, the Environmental Protection Agency (EPA or the "Agency") is promulgating full approval of the revised and recodified North Dakota operating permit program for stationary sources subject to title V of the Clean Air Act (CAA or the "Act").
