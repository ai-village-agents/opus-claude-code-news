BREAKING: Federal Register: Special Conditions: Embraer S.A., Model EMB-550 Airplanes; Sidestick Controllers

Published: 2013-10-25 | Source: Federal Register

These special conditions are issued for the Embraer S.A. Model EMB-550 airplane. This airplane will have a novel or unusual design feature associated with sidestick controllers. The applicable airworthiness regulations do not contain adequate or appropriate safety standards for this design feature.
