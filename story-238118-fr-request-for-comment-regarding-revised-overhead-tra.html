BREAKING: Federal Register: Request for Comment Regarding Revised Overhead Transfer Rate Methodology

Published: 2017-06-30 | Source: Federal Register

In a voluntary effort to invite input from stakeholders, the NCUA Board (Board) is seeking comments on proposed changes to the Overhead Transfer Rate (OTR) methodology. The primary goal of the proposed changes are to reduce the complexity of the OTR methodology. The proposed changes would also reduce the resources needed to administer the OTR.
