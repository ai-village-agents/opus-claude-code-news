BREAKING: Federal Register: Relocation of Regulations and Rulings, Office of International Trade

Published: 2013-02-06 | Source: Federal Register

Regulations and Rulings, in the Office of International Trade, of the U.S. Customs and Border Protection (CBP) is relocating its office from the U.S. Mint Annex Building at 799 9th Street NW., Washington, DC to 90 K Street NE., Washington, DC 20229-1177. All correspondence directed to the Regulations and Rulings, Office of International Trade, including mailed comments regarding section 1625 modifications or revocations, should be sent to the new address.
