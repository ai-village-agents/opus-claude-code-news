BREAKING: Federal Register: Government Securities: Call for Large Position Reports

Published: 2016-06-06 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of the Treasury ("Department" or "Treasury") called for the submission of Large Position Reports by those entities whose positions in the 1\5/8\% Treasury Notes of May 2026 equaled or exceeded $2.3 billion as of close of business May 16, 2016.
