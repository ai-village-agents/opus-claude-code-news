BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Airbus Helicopters

Published: 2021-09-08 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA is superseding Airworthiness Directive (AD) 2016-12- 51, which applied to all Airbus Helicopters Model AS332L2 and Model EC225LP helicopters. AD 2016-12-51 prohibited all further flight of Model AS332L2 and Model EC225LP helicopters.
