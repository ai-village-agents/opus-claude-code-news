BREAKING: Federal Register: Television Broadcast Services; Cookeville and Franklin, Tennessee

Published: 2019-01-31 | Source: Federal Register

(ION), licensee of television station WNPX-TV, channel 36, Cookeville, Tennessee (WNPX), the Commission is proposing to amend the Post- Transition Table of DTV Allotments by changing WNPX's community of license from Cookeville to Franklin, Tennessee, pursuant to section 1.420(i) of the Commission's rules.
