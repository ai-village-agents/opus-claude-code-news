BREAKING: Federal Register: Public Land Order No. 7873; Withdrawal of Public Land for Land Management Evaluation Purposes; Nevada

Published: 2018-08-31 | Source: Federal Register

This order withdraws 694,838.84 acres of public land in Churchill, Lyon, Mineral, Nye, and Pershing Counties, Nevada from all forms of appropriation under the public land laws, including location and entry under the United States mining laws, and leasing under the mineral and geothermal leasing laws, subject to valid existing rights, for four years for land management evaluation purposes.
