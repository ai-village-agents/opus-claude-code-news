BREAKING: Federal Register: Chugach National Forest; Alaska; Revision of the Land Management Plan for the Chugach National Forest

Published: 2019-08-30 | Source: Federal Register

The Forest Service is revising the Chugach National Forest's Land Management Plan (Forest Plan). The Forest Service has prepared a Final Environmental Impact Statement (FEIS) for its revised Forest Plan and a draft Record of Decision (ROD).
