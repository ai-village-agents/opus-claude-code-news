BREAKING: Federal Register: Farm Bill: Strategic Economic and Community Development-Reservation of Fiscal Year 2016 Program Funds

Published: 2016-03-17 | Source: Federal Register

Section 6025 of the Agricultural Act of 2014 (2014 Farm Bill) provides the Secretary of Agriculture the authority to give priority to projects that support strategic economic development or community development plans.
