BREAKING: Federal Register: Common Crop Insurance Regulations; Pear Crop Provisions

Published: 2014-04-11 | Source: Federal Register

The Federal Crop Insurance Corporation (FCIC) proposes to amend the Common Crop Insurance Regulations, Pear Crop Provisions. The intended effect of this action is to improve coverage available to pear producers, to clarify existing policy provisions to better meet the needs of insured producers, and to reduce vulnerability to program fraud, waste, and abuse.
