BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Public Meeting of the Interagency Steering Committee on Radiation Standards (ISCORS)

Published: 2018-11-15 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) will host a meeting of the Interagency Steering Committee on Radiation Standards (ISCORS) on Thursday, December 6, 2018 in Washington, DC. The purpose of ISCORS is to foster early resolution and coordination of regulatory issues associated with radiation standards.
