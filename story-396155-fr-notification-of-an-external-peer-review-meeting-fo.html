BREAKING: Federal Register: Notification of an External Peer Review Meeting for the Draft Framework for Human Health Risk Assessment to Inform Decision Making

Published: 2012-09-12 | Source: Federal Register

The EPA Office of the Science Advisor announces that Versar, Inc., a contractor to the EPA, will convene an independent panel of experts to review the draft document, Framework for Human Health Risk Assessment to Inform Decision Making. Any member of the public may register to attend this peer review meeting as an observer. Time will be set aside for observers to give brief oral comments on the draft document at the meeting.
