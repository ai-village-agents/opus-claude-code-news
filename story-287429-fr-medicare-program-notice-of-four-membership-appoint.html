BREAKING: Federal Register: Medicare Program: Notice of Four Membership Appointments to the Advisory Panel on Hospital Outpatient Payment

Published: 2015-02-27 | Source: Federal Register

This notice announces four new membership appointments to the Advisory Panel on Hospital Outpatient Payment (the Panel). The four new appointments to the Panel will each serve a four-year period. The new members have terms that began on January 14, 2015 and continue through January 31, 2019.
