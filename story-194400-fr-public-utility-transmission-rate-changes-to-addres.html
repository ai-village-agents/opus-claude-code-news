BREAKING: Federal Register: Public Utility Transmission Rate Changes To Address Accumulated Deferred Income Taxes

Published: 2019-11-27 | Source: Federal Register

In this final rule, the Federal Energy Regulatory Commission (Commission) is requiring public utility transmission providers with transmission formula rates under an Open Access Transmission Tariff, a transmission owner tariff, or a rate schedule to revise those transmission formula rates to account for changes caused by the Tax Cuts and Jobs Act of 2017.
