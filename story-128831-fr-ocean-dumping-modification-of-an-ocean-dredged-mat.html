BREAKING: Federal Register: Ocean Dumping; Modification of an Ocean Dredged Material Disposal Site Offshore Port Everglades, Florida

Published: 2021-07-22 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is expanding the size of the EPA designated ocean dredged material disposal site (ODMDS) offshore of Port Everglades, Florida (referred to hereafter as the existing Port Everglades ODMDS), pursuant to the Marine Protection, Research and Sanctuaries Act (MPRSA). The primary purpose for the site modification is to enlarge the site to provide for the long-term disposal capacity to dump suitable material dredged from the Port Everglades Harbor in ocean water...
