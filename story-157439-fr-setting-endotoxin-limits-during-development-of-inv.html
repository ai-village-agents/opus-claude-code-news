BREAKING: Federal Register: Setting Endotoxin Limits During Development of Investigational Oncology Drugs and Biological Products; Draft Guidance for Industry; Availability

Published: 2020-07-29 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA or Agency) is announcing the availability of a draft guidance for industry entitled "Setting Endotoxin Limits During Development of Investigational Oncology Drugs and Biological Products." This guidance describes FDA's current recommendations about endotoxin limits in certain investigational oncology drugs and biological products.
