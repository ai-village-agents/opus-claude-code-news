BREAKING: Federal Register: Investigator Responsibilities-Safety Reporting for Investigational Drugs and Devices; Draft Guidance for Industry; Availability

Published: 2021-09-30 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA or Agency) is announcing the availability of a draft guidance for industry entitled "Investigator Responsibilities--Safety Reporting for Investigational Drugs and Devices." The draft guidance provides recommendations to help clinical investigators comply with the safety reporting requirements of investigational new drug application (IND) studies and investigational device exemption (IDE) studies.
