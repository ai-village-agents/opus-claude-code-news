BREAKING: Federal Register: Schedules of Controlled Substances: Temporary Placement of Acetyl Fentanyl Into Schedule I

Published: 2015-07-17 | Source: Federal Register

The Administrator of the Drug Enforcement Administration is issuing this final order to temporarily schedule the synthetic opioid, N-(1-phenethylpiperidin-4-yl)-N-phenylacetamide (acetyl fentanyl), and its optical, positional, and geometric isomers, salts and salts of isomers, into schedule I pursuant to the temporary scheduling provisions of the Controlled Substances Act.
