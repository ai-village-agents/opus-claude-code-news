BREAKING: Federal Register: Approval and Promulgation of Implementation Plans; State of Iowa

Published: 2014-03-14 | Source: Federal Register

Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is approving revisions to the State Implementation Plan (SIP) for the State of Iowa. These revisions will amend the SIP to include revisions to Iowa air quality rule, Chapter 33, "Special Regulations and Construction Permit Requirements for Major Stationary Sources--Prevention of Significant Deterioration (PSD) of Air Quality."
