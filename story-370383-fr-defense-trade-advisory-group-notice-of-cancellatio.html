BREAKING: Federal Register: Defense Trade Advisory Group; Notice of Cancellation of Open Meeting

Published: 2013-11-18 | Source: Federal Register

The notice references Public Notice 8515 published in the November 12, 2013 Federal Register, announcing the public meeting for Defense Trade Advisory Group (DTAG) on November 22, 2013 to discuss current defense trade issues and topics for further study has been cancelled. It will be rescheduled at a later date.
