BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Receipt of Requests to Voluntarily Cancel Certain Pesticide Registrations (January 2025)

Published: 2025-05-30 | Source: Federal Register

In accordance with the Federal Insecticide, Fungicide, and Rodenticide Act (FIFRA), this document announces the Agency's receipt of and solicits comment on requests by registrants to voluntarily cancel their pesticide registration of certain products. EPA provides a periodic notice of receipt addressing requests received by EPA since the last notice of receipt was issued and uses the month and year in the title to help distinguish one document from the other.
