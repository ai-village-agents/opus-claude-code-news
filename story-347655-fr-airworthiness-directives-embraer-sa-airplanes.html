BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Embraer S.A. Airplanes

Published: 2013-02-22 | Source: Federal Register

We propose to adopt a new airworthiness directive (AD) for certain Embraer S.A. Model ERJ 170 and ERJ 190 airplanes. This proposed AD was prompted by reports of chafing between the auxiliary power unit (APU) electronic starter controller (ESC) power cables and the airplane tail cone firewall.
