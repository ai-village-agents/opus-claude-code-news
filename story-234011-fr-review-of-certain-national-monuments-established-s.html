BREAKING: Federal Register: Review of Certain National Monuments Established Since 1996; Notice of Opportunity for Public Comment

Published: 2017-05-11 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Department of the Interior is conducting a review of certain National Monuments designated or expanded since 1996 under the Antiquities Act of 1906 in order to implement Executive Order 13792 of April 26, 2017.
