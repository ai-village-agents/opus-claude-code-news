BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone; Delaware River Fireworks Display, Delaware River, Philadelphia, PA

Published: 2018-08-28 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is establishing a temporary safety zone for navigable waters of the Delaware River in the vicinity of Penn's Landing, Philadelphia, PA, from 8:30 p.m. through 9:30 p.m. on September 1, 2018, during the Delaware River Waterfront Corp Fireworks Display. The safety zone is necessary to ensure the safety of participant vessels, spectators, and the boating public during the event.
