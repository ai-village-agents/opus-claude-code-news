BREAKING: Federal Register: Importation of Fresh Barhi Dates From Israel Into the United States

Published: 2013-01-09 | Source: Federal Register

We are advising the public that we have prepared a pest risk analysis that evaluates the risks associated with the importation of fresh dates of the cultivar Barhi from Israel into the United States. Based on that analysis, we have concluded that the application of one or more designated phytosanitary measures will be sufficient to mitigate the pest risk.
