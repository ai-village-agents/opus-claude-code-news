BREAKING: Federal Register: Hours of Service of Drivers: Trailways Companies Application for Exemption

Published: 2013-05-31 | Source: Federal Register

FMCSA announces its decision to grant Adirondack Trailways, Pine Hill Trailways, New York Trailways ("Trailways") and all other regular-route passenger carriers and their drivers an exemption from the hours-of-service (HOS) record of duty status (RODS) requirement to enter a change in duty status on the daily log for breaks in driving time of 10 minutes or less, for the limited purpose of picking up or dropping off passengers, baggage, or small express packages.
