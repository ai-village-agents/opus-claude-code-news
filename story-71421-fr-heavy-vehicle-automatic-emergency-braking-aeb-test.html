BREAKING: Federal Register: Heavy Vehicle Automatic Emergency Braking; AEB Test Devices

Published: 2023-07-06 | Source: Federal Register

This NPRM proposes to adopt a new Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard (FMVSS) to require automatic emergency braking (AEB) systems on heavy vehicles, i.e., vehicles with a gross vehicle weight rating greater than 4,536 kilograms (10,000 pounds).
