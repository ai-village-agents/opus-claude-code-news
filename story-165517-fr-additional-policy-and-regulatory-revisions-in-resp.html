BREAKING: Federal Register: Additional Policy and Regulatory Revisions in Response to the COVID-19 Public Health Emergency

Published: 2020-11-06 | Source: Federal Register

This interim final rule with request for comments (IFC) discusses CMS's implementation of section 3713 of the Coronavirus Aid, Relief, and Economic Security Act (CARES Act), which established Medicare Part B coverage and payment for Coronavirus Disease 2019 (COVID-19) vaccine and its administration.
