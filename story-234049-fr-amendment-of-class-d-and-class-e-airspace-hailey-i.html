BREAKING: Federal Register: Amendment of Class D and Class E Airspace; Hailey, ID

Published: 2017-05-12 | Source: Federal Register

This action modifies Class D airspace, Class E surface area airspace, and Class E airspace extending upward from 700 feet above the surface at Friedman Memorial Airport, Hailey, ID, to support the implementation of new Area Navigation (RNAV) Global Positioning System (GPS) standard instrument approach procedures for Instrument Flight Rules (IFR) at the airport.
