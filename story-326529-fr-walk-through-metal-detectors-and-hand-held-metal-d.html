BREAKING: Federal Register: Walk-Through Metal Detectors and Hand-Held Metal Detectors Test Method Validation

Published: 2014-06-10 | Source: Federal Register

National Institute of Justice has recently developed updated versions of its minimum performance standards for walk-through metal detectors and hand-held metal detectors. In order to ensure that the test methods in the standards are properly documented, NIJ is requesting proposals (including price quotes) for test method validation efforts from testing laboratories.
