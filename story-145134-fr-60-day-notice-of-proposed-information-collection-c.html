BREAKING: Federal Register: 60-Day Notice of Proposed Information Collection: Compliance Inspection Report and Mortgagee's Assurance of Completion

Published: 2020-02-25 | Source: Federal Register

HUD is seeking approval from the Office of Management and Budget (OMB) for the information collection described below. The purpose of this notice is to allow for 60 days of public comment.
