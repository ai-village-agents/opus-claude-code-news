BREAKING: Federal Register: Award Format for DoD Grants and Cooperative Agreements

Published: 2020-08-19 | Source: Federal Register

This final rule is the second of a sequence of six final rules in this issue of the Federal Register that collectively establish for DoD grants and cooperative agreements an updated interim implementation of Government wide guidance on administrative requirements, cost principles, and audit requirements for Federal awards and make other needed updates to the DoD Grant and Agreement Regulations (DoDGARs).
