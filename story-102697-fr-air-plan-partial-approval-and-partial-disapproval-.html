BREAKING: Federal Register: Air Plan Partial Approval and Partial Disapproval; Missouri; Construction Permits Required

Published: 2022-08-11 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is taking final action to partially approve and partially disapprove revisions to the Missouri State Implementation Plan (SIP) received on March 20, 2019 and June 10, 2021. The SIP revisions in the 2019 and 2021 submittals incorporate updates to construction permit requirement regulations for stationary and portable air sources in Missouri that help ensure ambient air quality standards are met.
