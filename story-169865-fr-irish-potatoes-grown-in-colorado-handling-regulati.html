BREAKING: Federal Register: Irish Potatoes Grown in Colorado; Handling Regulation for Area No. 2

Published: 2019-01-31 | Source: Federal Register

This proposed rule invites comments on a recommendation to revise the size requirements currently prescribed under the federal marketing order for Irish potatoes grown in Colorado. This action would revise the minimum size requirement for U.S. No. 2 or better grade round potatoes to align with the current size requirements for all other types of U.S. No. 2 or better grade potatoes.
