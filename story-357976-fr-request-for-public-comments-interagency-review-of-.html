BREAKING: Federal Register: Request for Public Comments: Interagency Review of Exclusion Order Enforcement Process

Published: 2013-06-20 | Source: Federal Register

The Executive Office of the President, through the U.S. Intellectual Property Enforcement Coordinator ("IPEC"), is beginning an interagency review directed at strengthening the procedures and practices used during enforcement of exclusion orders issued by the U.S. International Trade Commission ("ITC").
