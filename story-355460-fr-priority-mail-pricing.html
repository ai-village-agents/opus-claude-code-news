BREAKING: Federal Register: Priority Mail Pricing

Published: 2013-05-21 | Source: Federal Register

The Commission is noticing a recently-filed Postal Service notice announcing a proposal to include, for no additional charge, insurance up to $100 on most Priority Mail pieces. This notice addresses procedural steps associated with this filing.
