BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; The Boeing Company Airplanes

Published: 2014-02-25 | Source: Federal Register

We are adopting a new airworthiness directive (AD) for certain The Boeing Company Model 737-100, -200, -200C, -300, -400, and -500 series airplanes. This AD was prompted by reports of chaffing, arcing, and burning damage to the control cabin overhead wiring and ducting with smoke and fire caused by metal clamps installed on certain hoses.
