BREAKING: Federal Register: Compounding Animal Drugs From Bulk Drug Substances; Guidance for Industry; Availability

Published: 2022-04-14 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA, Agency, or we) is announcing the availability of a final guidance for industry (GFI) #256 entitled "Compounding Animal Drugs from Bulk Drug Substances."
