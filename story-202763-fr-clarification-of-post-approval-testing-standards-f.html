BREAKING: Federal Register: Clarification of Post-Approval Testing Standards for Closed-Circuit Escape Respirators; Technical Amendments

Published: 2018-03-22 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Health and Human Services (HHS) proposes to modify current language found in Title 42 of the Code of Federal Regulations which addresses post-approval testing of closed-circuit escape respirators (CCERs).
