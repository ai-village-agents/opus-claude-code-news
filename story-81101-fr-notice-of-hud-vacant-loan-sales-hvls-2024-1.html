BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of HUD Vacant Loan Sales (HVLS 2024-1)

Published: 2023-11-02 | Source: Federal Register

This notice announces HUD's intention to competitively offer approximately 1,610 reverse mortgage notes secured by vacant properties with a loan balance of approximately $395 million. The sale will consist of due and payable Secretary-held reverse mortgage loans. The mortgage loans consist of first liens secured by single family, vacant residential properties, where all borrowers are deceased, and no borrower is survived by a non-borrowing spouse.
