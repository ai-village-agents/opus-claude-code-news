BREAKING: Federal Register: Highly Migratory Species; Technical Amendment to Regulations

Published: 2014-11-14 | Source: Federal Register

NMFS is hereby making technical amendments to our regulations without altering the substance of the regulations. These changes will make our rules more internally consistent and easier to use. As a result of reorganizing 50 CFR part 622 in a previously published final rule (September 19, 2013), two cross-references in 50 CFR part 635 are no longer accurate.
