BREAKING: Federal Register: HIV/AIDS Surveillance and Service Data Analysis in the Republic of Haiti Under the President's Emergency Plan for AIDS Relief (PEPFAR)

Published: 2014-10-01 | Source: Federal Register

The Centers for Disease Control and Prevention (CDC), located within the Department of Health and Human Services (HHS) announces intent to award additional expansion supplement funds to National Alliance of State & Territorial AIDS Directors (NASTAD) Cooperative Agreement Number PS001842 in the amount of $1,250,000.
