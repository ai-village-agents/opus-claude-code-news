BREAKING: Federal Register: Human-System Interface Design Review Guidelines

Published: 2019-02-13 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Nuclear Regulatory Commission (NRC) is issuing for public comment a draft NUREG entitled, "Human-System Interface Design Review Guidelines" (NUREG-0700, Revision 3). NUREG-0700 was first published in 1981 following the accident at the Three Mile Island Nuclear Power Plant.
