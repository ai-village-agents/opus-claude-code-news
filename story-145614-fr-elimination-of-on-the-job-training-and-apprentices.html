BREAKING: Federal Register: Elimination of On-the-Job Training and Apprenticeship Trainee Certification

Published: 2020-02-28 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Veterans Affairs (VA) proposes to amend its regulations that contain the requirements for certification of attendance at on-the-job training and apprenticeship programs under the Veterans Apprenticeship and Labor Opportunity Reform Act (VALOR Act).
