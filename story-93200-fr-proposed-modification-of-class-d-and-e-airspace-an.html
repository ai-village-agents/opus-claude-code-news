BREAKING: Federal Register: Proposed Modification of Class D and E Airspace, and Proposed Removal of Class E Airspace; King Salmon Airport, AK

Published: 2022-04-08 | Source: Federal Register

This action proposes to modify the Class D and Class E surface area airspace, and the Class E airspace extending upward from 700 feet above the surface at King Salmon Airport, King Salmon, AK. Additionally, the FAA proposes to remove the Class E airspace designated as an extension to Class D or Class E airspace, as it is no longer required.
