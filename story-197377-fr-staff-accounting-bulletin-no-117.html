BREAKING: Federal Register: Staff Accounting Bulletin No. 117

Published: 2018-01-11 | Source: Federal Register

This staff accounting bulletin modifies portions of the interpretive guidance included in the Staff Accounting Bulletin Series in order to make the relevant interpretive guidance consistent with authoritative accounting guidance and Securities and Exchange Commission rules and regulations.
