BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Correction

Published: 2018-05-23 | Source: Federal Register

HRSA is correcting a notice published in the September 26, 2017 issue of the Federal Register entitled Improving Care for Children and Youth--Incentive Prize. This correction amends the subject of the challenge and the timeline. Please note, however, that this correction notice, along with future updates, as needed and pursuant to recent changes to the applicable law, will be posted on challenge.gov and mchbgrandchallenges.hrsa.gov.
