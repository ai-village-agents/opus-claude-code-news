BREAKING: Federal Register: Texas Central Railroad High-Speed Rail Safety Standards

Published: 2020-11-03 | Source: Federal Register

This final rule of particular applicability (RPA) establishes safety standards for the Texas Central Railroad (TCRR or the railroad) high-speed rail (HSR) system. These standards are not intended for general application in the railroad industry, but apply only to the TCRR system planned for development in the State of Texas.
