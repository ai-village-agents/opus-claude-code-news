BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Intent To Release Airport Property; Southwest Florida International Airport, Fort Myers, FL.

Published: 2016-09-08 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA hereby provides notice of intent to release approximately 0.521 acres of airport property at the Southwest Florida International Airport, Fort Myers, FL, from the terms, conditions, reservations, and restrictions as contained in federal grant assurances. The release of property will allow Lee County Port Authority to dispose of the property for other than aeronautical purposes.
