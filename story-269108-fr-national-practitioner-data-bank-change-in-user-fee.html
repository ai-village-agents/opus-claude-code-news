BREAKING: Federal Register: National Practitioner Data Bank: Change in User Fees

Published: 2016-07-20 | Source: Federal Register

The Health Resources and Services Administration, Department of Health and Human Services, is announcing a decrease in user fees charged to individuals and entities authorized to request information from the National Practitioner Data Bank (NPDB). The new fee will be $2.00 for both continuous and one-time queries and $4.00 for self- queries.
