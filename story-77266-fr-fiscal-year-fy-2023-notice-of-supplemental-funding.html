BREAKING: Federal Register: Fiscal Year (FY) 2023 Notice of Supplemental Funding Opportunity

Published: 2023-09-19 | Source: Federal Register

This notice is to inform the public that the Substance Abuse and Mental Health Services Administration (SAMHSA) is supporting administrative supplements (in scope of the parent award) for the 58 Community Mental Health Services Block Grant (MHBG) recipients funded in FY 2023. The total amount of available funding is $8,292,318. The 58 MHBG recipients are eligible to receive up to $142,971 for a total of $8,292,318.
