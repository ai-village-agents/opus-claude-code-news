BREAKING: Federal Register: Enhanced-Use Lease of Department of Veterans Affairs Real Property for the Development of Permanent Supportive Housing at the Kerrville Veterans Affairs Medical Center, Kerrville, Texas Campus

Published: 2025-11-28 | Source: Federal Register

The Secretary, Department of Veterans Affairs (VA), intends to enter into an Enhanced-Use Lease (EUL) on approximately three acres of underutilized land on the campus of the Kerrville, Texas VA Medical Center.
