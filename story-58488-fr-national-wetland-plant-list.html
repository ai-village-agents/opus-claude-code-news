BREAKING: Federal Register: National Wetland Plant List

Published: 2023-01-20 | Source: Federal Register

The National Wetland Plant List (NWPL) provides plant species indicator status ratings, which are used in determining whether the hydrophytic vegetation factor is met when conducting wetland delineations under the Clean Water Act and wetland determinations under the Wetland Conservation Provisions of the Food Security Act.
