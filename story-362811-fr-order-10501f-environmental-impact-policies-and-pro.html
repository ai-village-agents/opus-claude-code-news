BREAKING: Federal Register: Order 1050.1F Environmental Impact: Policies and Procedures

Published: 2013-08-14 | Source: Federal Register

The Federal Aviation Administration (FAA) proposes to update, reorganize, and revise its order that contains policies and procedures for implementing the National Environmental Policy Act (NEPA), in accordance with regulations issued by the Council on Environmental Quality (40 CFR parts 1500-1508).
