BREAKING: Federal Register: Revising the National Drug Code Format and Drug Label Barcode Requirements

Published: 2022-07-25 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA, the Agency, or we) is proposing to amend our regulations governing the format of the National Drug Code (NDC). The NDC is an FDA standard for uniquely identifying drug products marketed in the United States. This action, if finalized, will standardize the format of all NDCs.
