BREAKING: Federal Register: Opportunities and Challenges of Artificial Intelligence (AI) in Transportation; Request for Information

Published: 2024-05-03 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Department of Transportation's Advanced Research Projects Agency--Infrastructure (ARPA-I) is seeking input from interested parties on the potential applications of artificial intelligence (AI) in transportation, as well as emerging challenges and opportunities in creating and deploying AI technologies in applications across all modes of transportation.
