BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone; Upper Mississippi River, Crystal City, MO

Published: 2017-08-09 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard proposes to establish a temporary safety zone for navigable waters of the Upper Mississippi River (UMR) from mile 147.5 to 148.5. This action is necessary to protect persons, property, and infrastructure from potential damage and safety hazards associated with work being completed on new power lines across the river.
