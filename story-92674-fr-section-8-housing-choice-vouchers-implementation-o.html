BREAKING: Federal Register: Section 8 Housing Choice Vouchers: Implementation of the Housing Choice Voucher Mobility Demonstration for Awarded PHAs, Supplementary Notice for Demonstration Participants

Through that Implementation Notice, HUD made available up to $50,000,000 to participating Public Housing Agencies ("PHAs") to implement housing mobility programs.
