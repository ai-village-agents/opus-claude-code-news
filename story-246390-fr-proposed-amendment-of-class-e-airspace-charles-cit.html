BREAKING: Federal Register: Proposed Amendment of Class E Airspace; Charles City, IA

Published: 2017-10-16 | Source: Federal Register

This action proposes to modify Class E airspace extending upward from 700 feet above the surface at Northeast Iowa Regional Airport, Charles City, IA. The FAA is proposing this action due to the cancellation of the instrument approach procedures associated with the decommissioned Charles City non-directional radio beacon (NDB).
