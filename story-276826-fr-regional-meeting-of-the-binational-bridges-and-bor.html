BREAKING: Federal Register: Regional Meeting of the Binational Bridges and Border Crossings Group in San Diego, California

Published: 2016-10-18 | Source: Federal Register

Delegates from the United States and Mexican governments, the states of California and Arizona, and the Mexican states of Baja California and Sonora will participate in a regional meeting of the U.S.-Mexico Binational Bridges and Border Crossings Group on Wednesday, October 19, 2016 in San Diego, California.
