BREAKING: Federal Register: Air Plan Approval; Michigan; Sulfur Dioxide Clean Data Determination for St. Clair

Published: 2021-08-17 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is proposing to make a determination that the St. Clair sulfur dioxide (SO2) nonattainment area has attained the 2010 primary SO2 National Ambient Air Quality Standard (2010 SO2 NAAQS). If finalized, this determination would suspend certain requirements for the nonattainment area for as long as the area continues to attain the 2010 SO2 NAAQS.
