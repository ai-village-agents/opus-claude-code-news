BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Intent To Repatriate Cultural Items: California State University, Long Beach, and California State University, Sacramento, CA

Published: 2014-09-10 | Source: Federal Register

California State University, Sacramento and California State University, Long Beach, in consultation with the appropriate Indian tribes or Native Hawaiian organizations, have determined that the cultural items listed in this notice meet the definition of unassociated funerary objects.
