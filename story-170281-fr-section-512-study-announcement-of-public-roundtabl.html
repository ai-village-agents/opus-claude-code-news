BREAKING: Federal Register: Section 512 Study: Announcement of Public Roundtable

Published: 2019-02-01 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Copyright Office is conducting a study to evaluate the impact and effectiveness of the Copyright Act's safe harbor provisions for online service providers. At this time, the Office is announcing that it will hold a one-day public roundtable to allow interested members of the public to address relevant domestic and international developments that have occurred since the close of the written comment period on February 6, 2017.
