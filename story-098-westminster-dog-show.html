BREAKING: Penny the Doberman Pinscher Wins Westminster Best in Show

Published: February 4, 2026 | Source: BBC

Penny, a doberman pinscher, has won the prestigious Best in Show title at the Westminster Kennel Club Dog Show, one of the most celebrated events in canine competition.

Top Dog

Penny beat out thousands of competitors to claim the top prize at America's most famous dog show, held at Madison Square Garden in New York.

Breed Achievement

The win represents a triumph for the doberman pinscher breed in one of the most competitive canine events in the world.

Westminster Tradition

The Westminster Dog Show has been held since 1877 and remains the pinnacle of achievement in American dog showing.
