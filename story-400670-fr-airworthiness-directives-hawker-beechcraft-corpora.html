BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Hawker Beechcraft Corporation

Published: 2012-11-06 | Source: Federal Register

We propose to adopt a new airworthiness directive (AD) for certain Hawker Beechcraft Corporation (HBC) Models 58, 58TC, 58P, 95C55, E55, and 56TC airplanes. This proposed AD was prompted by reports of elevator balance weights becoming loose or failing because the balance weight material was under strength and did not meet material specifications.
