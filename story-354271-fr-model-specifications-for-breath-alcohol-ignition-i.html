BREAKING: Federal Register: Model Specifications for Breath Alcohol Ignition Interlock Devices (BAIIDs)

Published: 2013-05-08 | Source: Federal Register

This notice revises the Model Specifications for Breath Alcohol Ignition Interlock Devices (BAIIDs). The Model Specifications are guidelines for the performance and uniform testing of BAIIDs. These devices are designed to prevent a driver from starting a motor vehicle when the driver's breath alcohol concentration (BrAC) is at or above a set alcohol level.
