BREAKING: Federal Register: 2-Methyl-1,3-propanediol; Exemption From the Requirement of a Tolerance

Published: 2012-08-01 | Source: Federal Register

This regulation establishes an exemption from the requirement of a tolerance for residues of 2-methyl-1,3-propanediol (CAS Reg. No. 2163-42-0) when used as an inert ingredient component of food contact sanitizing solutions applied to all food contact surfaces in public eating places, dairy-processing equipment, and food-processing equipment and utensils.
