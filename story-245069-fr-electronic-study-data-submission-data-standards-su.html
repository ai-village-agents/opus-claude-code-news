BREAKING: Federal Register: Electronic Study Data Submission; Data Standards; Support for Analysis Data Model Implementation Guide Version 1.1

Published: 2017-10-02 | Source: Federal Register

(See http://www.fda.gov/forindustry/ datastandards/studydatastandards/default.htm). ADaM IG V1.1 has been available from CDISC (www.cdisc.org) since February 12, 2016.
