BREAKING: Federal Register: Advisory Committee on Earthquake Hazards Reduction Meeting

Published: 2015-05-22 | Source: Federal Register

The Advisory Committee on Earthquake Hazards Reduction (ACEHR or Committee), will hold an open meeting via webinar on Friday, June 12, 2015, from 1:00 p.m. to 3:00 p.m. Eastern Time. The primary purpose of this meeting is to finalize the Committee's 2015 Report on the Effectiveness of the National Earthquake Hazards Reduction Program (NEHRP).
