BREAKING: Federal Register: 2020 Census Tribal Consultation; Virtual Public Meeting

Published: 2021-10-19 | Source: Federal Register

The Census Bureau will conduct a tribal consultation on the next set of 2020 Census Data Products on November 18, 2021, through a national webinar. The tribal consultation meeting reflects the Census Bureau's continuous commitment to strengthen government-to-government relationships with federally recognized tribes.
