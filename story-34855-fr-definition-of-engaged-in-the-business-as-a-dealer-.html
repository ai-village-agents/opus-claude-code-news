BREAKING: Federal Register: Definition of “Engaged in the Business” as a Dealer in Firearms

Published: 2024-04-19 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Justice ("Department") is amending Bureau of Alcohol, Tobacco, Firearms, and Explosives ("ATF") regulations to implement the provisions of the Bipartisan Safer Communities Act that broaden the definition of when a person is considered "engaged in the business" ("EIB") as a dealer in firearms other than a gunsmith or pawnbroker.
