BREAKING: Federal Register: Handling of Almonds Grown in California

Published: 2018-07-06 | Source: Federal Register

This proposed rule invites comments on proposed amendments to Marketing Order No. 981, which regulates the handling of almonds grown in California. The proposed amendments would change the dates associated with the process to nominate members to the Almond Board of California (Board) as well as the start of the term of office of members of the Board.
