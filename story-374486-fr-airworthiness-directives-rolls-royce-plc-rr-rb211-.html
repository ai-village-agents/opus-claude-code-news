BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Rolls-Royce plc (RR) RB211-524 Series Turbofan Engines

Published: 2012-01-09 | Source: Federal Register

We are superseding an existing airworthiness directive (AD) for the products listed above. That AD currently requires initial and repetitive borescope inspections of the head section and meterpanel assembly of the combustion liner, and replacement if necessary. This new AD requires those same inspections, and replacement. This AD also expands the applicability to include part numbers (P/N) of additional combustion liners.
