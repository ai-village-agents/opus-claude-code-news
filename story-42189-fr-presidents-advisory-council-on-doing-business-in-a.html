BREAKING: Federal Register: President's Advisory Council on Doing Business in Africa

Published: 2024-07-19 | Source: Federal Register

The President's Advisory Council on Doing Business in Africa will hold a meeting to deliberate and adopt recommendations on the reauthorization and modernization of the Africa Growth and Opportunity Act (AGOA).
