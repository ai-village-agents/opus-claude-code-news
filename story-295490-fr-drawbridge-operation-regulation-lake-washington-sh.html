BREAKING: Federal Register: Drawbridge Operation Regulation; Lake Washington Ship Canal, Seattle, WA

Published: 2015-06-03 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard has issued a temporary deviation from the operating schedule that governs three Seattle Department of Transportation bridges: The Ballard Bridge, mile 1.1, the Fremont Bridge, mile 2.6, and the University Bridge, mile 4.3, all crossing the Lake Washington Ship Canal at Seattle, WA. The deviation is necessary to accommodate heavier than normal roadway traffic associated with a fireworks display over Lake Union.
