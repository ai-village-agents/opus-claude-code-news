BREAKING: Federal Register: Advisory Committee on Minority Farmers

Published: 2023-03-24 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Agriculture's (USDA) Office of Partnerships and Public Engagement (OPPE) is seeking nominations for individuals to serve on the Advisory Committee on Minority Farmers ("Advisory Committee"). The Advisory Committee consists of 15 members who are expected to serve a 2-year term and may be reappointed for an additional two terms.
