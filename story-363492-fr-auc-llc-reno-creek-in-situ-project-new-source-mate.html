BREAKING: Federal Register: AUC, LLC Reno Creek, In Situ Project, New Source Material License Application

Published: 2013-08-21 | Source: Federal Register

By letter dated October 3, 2012, AUC, LLC (AUC) submitted to the U.S. Nuclear Regulatory Commission (NRC) an application for a new source material license. The requested license, or the proposed action, would authorize the construction and operation, and decommissioning of AUC's proposed in-situ uranium recovery (ISR, also known as in-situ leach) facilities, and would require restoration of the aquifer from which the uranium would be extracted.
