BREAKING: Federal Register: Schedule for Rating Disabilities: Mental Disorders

Published: 2022-02-15 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Veterans Affairs (VA) proposes to amend the portion of the rating schedule dealing with mental disorders, including revising the General Rating Formula for Mental Disorders and combining currently separate General Rating Formula for Mental Disorders with the General Rating Formula for Eating Disorders in the VA Schedule for Rating Disabilities (VASRD or rating schedule).
