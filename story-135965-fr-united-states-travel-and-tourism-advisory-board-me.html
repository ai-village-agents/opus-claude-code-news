BREAKING: Federal Register: United States Travel and Tourism Advisory Board: Meeting of the United States Travel and Tourism Advisory Board

Published: 2021-10-20 | Source: Federal Register

The United States Travel and Tourism Advisory Board (Board or TTAB) will hold a meeting on Friday, November 5, 2021. The Board advises the Secretary of Commerce on matters relating to the U.S. travel and tourism industry.
