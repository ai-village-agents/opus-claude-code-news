BREAKING: Federal Register: Privacy Act of 1974; System of Records

Published: 2023-04-25 | Source: Federal Register

Pursuant to the Privacy Act of 1974, notice is hereby given that the VA is modifying the system of records entitled "Disaster Emergency Medical Personnel System (DEMPS)-VA" (98VA104).
