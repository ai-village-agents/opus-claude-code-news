BREAKING: Federal Register: Privacy Act of 1974; System of Records

Published: 2025-11-28 | Source: Federal Register

In accordance with the Privacy Act of 1974 and in compliance with Executive Order 14249, Protecting America's Bank Account Against Fraud, Waste, and Abuse, and Office of Management and Budget Memorandum M-25-32, Preventing Improper Payments and Protecting Privacy Through Do Not Pay, the Commodity Futures Trading Commission (CFTC or Commission) is modifying four Privacy Act systems of records by adding a new routine use to permit the disclosure of records to the Department of the Treasury.
