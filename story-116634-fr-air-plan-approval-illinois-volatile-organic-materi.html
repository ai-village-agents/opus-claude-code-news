BREAKING: Federal Register: Air Plan Approval; Illinois; Volatile Organic Material Definition Update

Published: 2021-02-12 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is proposing to approve a revision to the Illinois State Implementation Plan (SIP). The revision will amend the Illinois Administrative Code (IAC) by updating the definition of volatile organic material (VOM) and volatile organic compounds (VOC) to exclude (Z)-1,1,1,4,4,4-hexafluorobut-2-ene.
