BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; The Boeing Company Airplanes

Published: 2024-08-20 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA is adopting a new airworthiness directive (AD) for all The Boeing Company Model 787-8, 787-9, and 787-10 airplanes. This AD was prompted by a report of heat damage on multiple engine inlets around the engine anti-ice (EAI) duct within the inlet aft compartment.
