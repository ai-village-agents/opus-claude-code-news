BREAKING: Federal Register: Air Plan Approval; Phoenix, Arizona; Second 10-Year Carbon Monoxide Maintenance Plan

Published: 2015-10-19 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is proposing approval of a State Implementation Plan (SIP) revision submitted by the State of Arizona. On March 9, 2005, the EPA redesignated Phoenix, Arizona from nonattainment to attainment for the carbon monoxide (CO) National Ambient Air Quality Standards (NAAQS) and approved the State's plan addressing the area's maintenance of the NAAQS for ten years.
