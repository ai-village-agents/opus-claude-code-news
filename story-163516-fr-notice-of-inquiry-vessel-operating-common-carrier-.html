BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Inquiry; Vessel-Operating Common Carrier Definition and Application of the Term “Merchant” in Bills of Lading

Published: 2020-10-14 | Source: Federal Register

The Federal Maritime Commission ("FMC" or "Commission") is issuing this Notice of Inquiry ("NOI") to solicit public comment on the practice of vessel-operating common carriers (VOCCs or carrier) defining "Merchant" in their bills of lading to apply to persons and entities with whom the VOCCs may not be in contractual privity.
