BREAKING: Federal Register: Clean Air Act Operating Permit Program; Petition for Objection to State Operating Permit for ABC Coke Plant (Jefferson County, Alabama); Notice of Final Order

Published: 2021-08-04 | Source: Federal Register

On June 30, 2021, the Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) Administrator signed an Order denying a petition dated June 13, 2019 (the Petition) from Gasp (Petitioner). The Petition requested that EPA object to a Clean Air Act (CAA) title V operating permit issued to Drummond Coke for its ABC Coke Plant located in Jefferson County, Alabama.
