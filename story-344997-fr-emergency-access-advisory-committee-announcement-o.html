BREAKING: Federal Register: Emergency Access Advisory Committee; Announcement of Charter Extension

Published: 2013-01-24 | Source: Federal Register

This document announces the extension of the charter of the Emergency Access Advisory Committee (Committee or EAAC). This extension of the charter will enable the EAAC to complete the important work it began in early 2011 pursuant to the Twenty-First Century Communications and Video Accessibility Act of 2010 (CVAA).
