BREAKING: Federal Register: Proposed Amendment of V-53, V-115, V-140, V-339, T-215, and T-323, and Revocation of V-339 in the Vicinity of Hazard, KY

Published: 2020-07-24 | Source: Federal Register

This action proposes to amend three VHF Omnidirectional Range (VOR) Federal airways, V-53, V-115, and V-140; amend two Area Navigation (RNAV) T-routes, T-215 and T-323; and remove one VOR Federal airway, V-339, in the vicinity of Hazard, KY.
