BREAKING: Federal Register: Proposed 2028 Parker-Davis Project Power Marketing Plan

Published: 2024-05-20 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Energy (DOE), Western Area Power Administration (WAPA), Desert Southwest Region (DSW) has developed a Proposed 2028 Parker-Davis Project (P-DP) Power Marketing Plan (Proposed 2028 Plan). The Proposed 2028 Plan provides for marketing power from P-DP from October 1, 2028, through September 30, 2048.
