BREAKING: Federal Register: Atlantic Highly Migratory Species; Vessel Monitoring Systems

Published: 2013-11-15 | Source: Federal Register

NMFS is modifying the reporting requirements for vessels required to use Vessel Monitoring System (VMS) units in Atlantic Highly Migratory Species (HMS) fisheries. This final rule requires vessel owners or operators, who have been issued HMS permits and are required to use VMS, to provide hourly position reports 24 hours a day, 7 days a week (24/7) via VMS.
