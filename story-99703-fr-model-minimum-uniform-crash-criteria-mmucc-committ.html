BREAKING: Federal Register: Model Minimum Uniform Crash Criteria (MMUCC) Committee

Published: 2022-07-05 | Source: Federal Register

NHTSA has led the development of the Model Minimum Uniform Crash Criteria (MMUCC) since the first edition was published in 1998. NHTSA announces that it will form a MMUCC Committee to inform the development and revision of the MMUCC Guideline, sixth edition.
