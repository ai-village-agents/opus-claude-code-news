BREAKING: Federal Register: Regulated Navigation Area; Oregon Inlet Channel, Marc Basnight Bridge, Dare County, NC

Published: 2022-07-08 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is establishing a temporary regulated navigational area (RNA) for navigable waters of Oregon Inlet Channel. Due to severe shoaling in the Oregon Inlet Channel, the navigational channel will be moved until shoaling can be mitigated by dredging crews. The new, temporary, channel through the Marc Basnight Bridge may not be suitable for vessels of 100 gross tons (GT) or greater.
