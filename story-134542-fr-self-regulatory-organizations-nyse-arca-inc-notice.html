BREAKING: Federal Register: Self-Regulatory Organizations; NYSE Arca, Inc.; Notice of Filing and Immediate Effectiveness of Proposed Rule Change To Adopt a New Historical Market Data Product To Be Known as the NYSE Options Open-Close Volume Summary

Published: 2021-10-01 | Source: Federal Register

End of Day Volume Summary and Intra-Day Volume Summary. The End of Day Volume Summary will contain historical data from the previous trading day and would be available after the end of each trading day, generally on a T+1 basis. The Intra-Day Volume Summary would include "snapshots" taken every 10 minutes throughout the trading day and would be available within five minutes of the conclusion of each 10-minute period.
