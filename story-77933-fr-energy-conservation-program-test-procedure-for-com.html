BREAKING: Federal Register: Energy Conservation Program: Test Procedure for Commercial Refrigerators, Refrigerator-Freezers, and Freezers

Published: 2023-09-26 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Department of Energy ("DOE") amends the test procedures for commercial refrigerators, refrigerator-freezers, and freezers ("CRE") to reference the latest versions of the applicable industry standards.
