BREAKING: Federal Register: Agriculture Acquisition Regulation, Fire Suspension Suppression and Liability

Published: 2014-05-22 | Source: Federal Register

The Office of Procurement and Property Management (OPPM) of the Department of Agriculture (USDA) amends the Agriculture Acquisition Regulation (the "AGAR") by adding a new clause entitled "Fire Suppression and Liability." Section 8205 of the Agricultural Act of 2014 (2014 Act) provided the USDA Forest Service with permanent authority for stewardship end results contracting by adding a new section 604 to the Healthy Forests Restoration Act of 2003.
