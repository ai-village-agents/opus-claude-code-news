BREAKING: Federal Register: Clean Air Act Title V Operating Permit Program Revision; New Jersey

Published: 2016-10-28 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency is approving a revision to the New Jersey Operating Permit Program related to the permitting of stationary sources subject to title V of the Clean Air Act (CAA) in the state of New Jersey. The revision consists of amendments to Subchapter 22 of Chapter 27 of Title 7 of the New Jersey Administrative Code, "Operating Permits."
