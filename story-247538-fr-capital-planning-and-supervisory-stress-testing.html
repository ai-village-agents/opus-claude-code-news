BREAKING: Federal Register: Capital Planning and Supervisory Stress Testing

Published: 2017-10-30 | Source: Federal Register

The NCUA Board ("Board") proposes to amend its regulations regarding capital planning and stress testing for federally insured credit unions with $10 billion or more in assets (covered credit unions). The proposal would reduce regulatory burden by removing some of the capital planning and stress testing requirements currently applicable to certain covered credit unions.
