BREAKING: Federal Register: Virtual Public Meeting; Federal Salary Council

Published: 2022-07-08 | Source: Federal Register

The Federal Salary Council will meet virtually on Friday, August 5, 2022, at the time shown below. There will be no in-person gathering for this meeting. The Council is an advisory body composed of representatives of Federal employee organizations and experts in the fields of labor relations and pay policy.
