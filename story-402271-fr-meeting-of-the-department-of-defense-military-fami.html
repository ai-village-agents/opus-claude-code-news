BREAKING: Federal Register: Meeting of the Department of Defense Military Family Readiness Council (MFRC); Cancellation of Meeting and Rescheduling of Meeting

Published: 2012-11-27 | Source: Federal Register

On October 17, 2012 (77 FR 63805-63806), the Department of Defense Military Family Readiness Council (MFRC) announced a meeting to be held on Tuesday, December 11, 2012, from 1:00 p.m. to 4:00 p.m. at Pentagon Conference Center B6. The meeting on December 11 has been cancelled. The meeting is rescheduled for January 31, 2013, from 1:00 p.m. to 3:00 p.m. at Pentagon Conference Center B6.
