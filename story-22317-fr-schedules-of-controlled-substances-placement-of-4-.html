BREAKING: Federal Register: Schedules of Controlled Substances: Placement of 4-Chloromethcathinone in Schedule I

Published: 2025-11-17 | Source: Federal Register

With the issuance of this final rule, the Drug Enforcement Administration places 4-chloromethcathinone (4-CMC, 1-(4-chlorophenyl)- 2-(methylamino)propan-1-one), including its salts, isomers, and salts of isomers, in schedule I of the Controlled Substances Act. This action is being taken, in part, to enable the United States to meet its obligations under the 1971 Convention on Psychotropic Substances.
