BREAKING: Federal Register: Minimum Training Requirements for Entry-Level Commercial Motor Vehicle Operators; Public Listening Session

Published: 2013-02-28 | Source: Federal Register

FMCSA announces that it will hold a public listening session to solicit ideas and information on the issue of entry-level training for drivers of commercial motor vehicles (CMVs). Specifically, the Agency solicits input on factors, issues, and data it should consider in anticipation of a rulemaking to implement the entry-level driver training (ELDT) provisions in the Moving Ahead for Progress in the 21st Century Act.
