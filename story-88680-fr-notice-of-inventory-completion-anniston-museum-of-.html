BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Inventory Completion: Anniston Museum of Natural History, Anniston, AL; Correction

Published: 2022-02-11 | Source: Federal Register

The Anniston Museum of Natural History has corrected an inventory of human remains and associated funerary objects, published in a Notice of Inventory Completion in the Federal Register on August 10, 2018. This notice corrects the cultural affiliation.
