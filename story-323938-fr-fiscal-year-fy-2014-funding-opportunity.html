BREAKING: Federal Register: Fiscal Year (FY) 2014 Funding Opportunity

Published: 2014-05-12 | Source: Federal Register

This notice is to inform the public that the Substance Abuse and Mental Health Services Administration (SAMHSA) intends to award $520,000 (total costs) per year for the remaining two years of the APAIT Health Center grant. The original grant was awarded to APAIT Health Center in FY 2013 in response to the Request for Applications (RFA) TI-13-011: Substance Abuse Treatment for Racial/Ethnic Minority Women at High Risk for HIV/AIDS (TCE:HIV Minority Women).
