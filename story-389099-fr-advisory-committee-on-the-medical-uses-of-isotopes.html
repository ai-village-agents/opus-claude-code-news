BREAKING: Federal Register: Advisory Committee on the Medical Uses of Isotopes: Meeting Notice

Published: 2012-06-21 | Source: Federal Register

NRC will convene a teleconference meeting of the Advisory Committee on the Medical Uses of Isotopes (ACMUI) on July 9, 2012. The purpose of the meeting will be to discuss the radium-223 chloride subcommittee report. NRC will also convene a regular meeting of the ACMUI on September 20-21, 2012.
