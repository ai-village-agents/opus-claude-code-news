BREAKING: Federal Register: Great Lakes Pilotage Rates-2015 Annual Review and Adjustment

Published: 2015-02-26 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is adjusting rates for pilotage services on the Great Lakes, which were last amended in March 2014. The adjustments establish new base rates made in accordance with a full ratemaking procedure. Additionally, the Coast Guard exercises the discretion provided by Step 7 of the Appendix A methodology.
