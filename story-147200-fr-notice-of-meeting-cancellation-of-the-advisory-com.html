BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Meeting Cancellation of the Advisory Committee on Commercial Remote Sensing

Published: 2020-03-19 | Source: Federal Register

On February 28, 2020, the National Oceanic and Atmospheric Administration (NOAA) published a notice that announced the public meeting of The Advisory Committee on Commercial Remote Sensing ("ACCRES" or "the Committee") on March 18, 2020. This meeting has been cancelled due to developments of COVID-19 (Coronavirus) in the region.
