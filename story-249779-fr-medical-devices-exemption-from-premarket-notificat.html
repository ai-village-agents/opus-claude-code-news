BREAKING: Federal Register: Medical Devices; Exemption From Premarket Notification: Class II Devices; Surgical Apparel; Request for Comments

Published: 2017-11-30 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA or Agency) is announcing its intention to exempt certain subtypes of surgical apparel from premarket notification requirements, subject to conditions and limitations.
