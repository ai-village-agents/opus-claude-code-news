BREAKING: Federal Register: Facility Security Clearance and Safeguarding of National Security Information and Restricted Data

Published: 2013-08-07 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Nuclear Regulatory Commission (NRC) is updating its regulations to standardize the frequency of required security education training for employees of NRC licensees possessing security clearances so that such training will be conducted annually consistent with the objectives of Executive Order 13526, Classified National Security Information.
