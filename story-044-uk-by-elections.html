BREAKING: UK By-Elections Test Labour's Grip on Heartland Voters

Published: February 4, 2026 | Source: Al Jazeera

By-elections in Gorton and Denton are testing the Labour Party's hold on traditional heartland constituencies in Greater Manchester.

Electoral Test

The votes are being closely watched as a barometer of public sentiment toward the Starmer government and Labour's performance since taking power.

Political Stakes

A poor showing could signal trouble for Labour in areas it has traditionally dominated, while strong results would reinforce the party's position.
