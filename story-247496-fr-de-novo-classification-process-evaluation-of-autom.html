BREAKING: Federal Register: De Novo Classification Process (Evaluation of Automatic Class III Designation); Guidance for Industry and Food and Drug Administration Staff; Availability

Published: 2017-10-30 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA or Agency) is announcing the availability of the guidance entitled "De Novo Classification Process (Evaluation of Automatic Class III Designation)." The purpose of this document is to provide guidance on the process for the submission and review of a De Novo classification request (hereafter a "De Novo request") under the Federal Food, Drug, and Cosmetic Act (the FD&C Act), also known as the De Novo classification process.
