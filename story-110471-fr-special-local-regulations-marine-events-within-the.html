BREAKING: Federal Register: Special Local Regulations; Marine Events Within the Captain of the Port Charleston

Published: 2022-11-21 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard will enforce the special local regulation to provide for the safety and security of certain navigable waterways of Charleston Harbor during the Charleston Parade of Boats. Our regulation for marine events within the Captain of the Port Charleston identifies the regulated area for this event in the Charleston Harbor, SC.
