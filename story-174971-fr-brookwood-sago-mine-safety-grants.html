BREAKING: Federal Register: Brookwood-Sago Mine Safety Grants

Published: 2019-03-25 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Department of Labor (DOL), Mine Safety and Health Administration (MSHA), is making up to $400,000 available in grant funds for education and training programs to help identify, avoid, and prevent unsafe working conditions in and around mines.
