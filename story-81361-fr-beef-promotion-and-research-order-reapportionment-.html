BREAKING: Federal Register: Beef Promotion and Research Order; Reapportionment and Technical Amendment

Published: 2023-11-06 | Source: Federal Register

This rule adjusts representation on the Cattlemen's Beef Promotion and Research Board (Board), established under the Beef Promotion and Research Act of 1985 (Act), to reflect changes in domestic cattle inventories as well as changes in levels of imported cattle, beef, and beef products that have occurred since the Board was last reapportioned in July 2020.
