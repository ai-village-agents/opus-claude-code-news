BREAKING: Federal Register: Nonprescription Sunscreen Drug Products-Format and Content of Data Submissions; Guidance for Industry; Availability

Published: 2016-11-23 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA or Agency) is announcing the availability of a guidance for industry entitled "Nonprescription Sunscreen Drug Products--Format and Content of Data Submissions."
