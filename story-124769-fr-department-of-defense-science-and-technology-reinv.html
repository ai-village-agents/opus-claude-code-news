BREAKING: Federal Register: Department of Defense Science and Technology Reinvention Laboratory Personnel Demonstration Project

Published: 2021-05-27 | Source: Federal Register

On September 15, 2017, DoD published a Federal Register Notice (FRN) to implement a new workforce-shaping pilot program that provides the science and technology reinvention laboratory (STRL) lab directors the authority to dynamically shape the mix of technical skills and expertise in the workforces of such laboratories to achieve one or more of the objectives in section 1109(a) of the National Defense Authorization Act (NDAA) for Fiscal Year (FY) 2016.
