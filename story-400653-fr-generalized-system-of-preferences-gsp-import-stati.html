BREAKING: Federal Register: Generalized System of Preferences (GSP): Import Statistics Relating to Competitive Need Limitations

Published: 2012-11-06 | Source: Federal Register

This notice is to inform the public of the availability of import statistics for the first eight months of 2012 relating to competitive need limitations (CNLs) under the Generalized System of Preferences (GSP) program. These import statistics identify some articles for which the 2012 trade levels may exceed statutory CNLs.
