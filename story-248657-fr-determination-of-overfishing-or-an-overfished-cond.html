BREAKING: Federal Register: Determination of Overfishing or an Overfished Condition

Published: 2017-11-15 | Source: Federal Register

This action serves as a notice that NMFS, on behalf of the Secretary of Commerce (Secretary), has found that the following stocks are subject to overfishing or approaching an overfished condition. The Stillaguamish coho salmon stock in Puget Sound is now subject to overfishing.
