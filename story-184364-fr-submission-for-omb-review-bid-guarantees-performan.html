BREAKING: Federal Register: Submission for OMB Review; Bid Guarantees, Performance and Payment Bonds, and Alternative Payment Protections

Published: 2019-07-19 | Source: Federal Register

Under the provisions of the Paperwork Reduction Act, the Regulatory Secretariat Division has submitted to the Office of Management and Budget (OMB) a request to review and approve a renewal of a previously approved information collection requirement regarding bid guarantees, performance and payment bonds, and alternative payment protections.
