BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; AlliedSignal Inc. and Rajay Inc. Oil Scavenge Pumps

Published: 2015-06-19 | Source: Federal Register

(CMI) IO-470 and TSIO-520 reciprocating engines and on Lycoming Engines, Inc., (Lycoming) IO-360, IO-540, and O-360 reciprocating engines.
