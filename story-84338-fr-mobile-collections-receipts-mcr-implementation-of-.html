BREAKING: Federal Register: Mobile Collections & Receipts (MCR): Implementation of Phase Two

Published: 2023-12-15 | Source: Federal Register

U.S. Customs and Border Protection (CBP) is conducting a test to allow for the payment of certain commercial vessel taxes and fees with electronic methods, including credit cards. Payment can be made through the existing Mobile Collections & Receipts (MCR) system's payment portal at eCBP (https://e.cbp.dhs.gov) or at the ports of entry for any commercial vessel arriving at a maritime port of entry.
