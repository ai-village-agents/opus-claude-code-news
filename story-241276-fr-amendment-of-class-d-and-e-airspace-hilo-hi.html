BREAKING: Federal Register: Amendment of Class D and E Airspace; Hilo, HI

Published: 2017-08-14 | Source: Federal Register

This action corrects a final rule, technical amendment published in Federal Register on June 22, 2017, that amends Class E airspace designated as an extension at Hilo International, General Lyman Field, Hilo, HI. The airport name is corrected to Hilo International Airport, Hilo, HI, removing "General Lyman Field" from the airport name to match the FAA's aeronautical database.
