BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Cameron Balloons Ltd. Fuel Cylinders

Published: 2023-08-29 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA is superseding Airworthiness Directive (AD) 2022-13- 03, which applied to a certain Cameron Balloons Ltd. (Cameron) fuel cylinder installed on hot air balloons. AD 2022-13-03 required removing any installed fuel cylinder part number (P/N) CB2990 (Alugas) from service before further flight. Since the FAA issued AD 2022-13-03, the fuel cylinder part number has been identified as CB2990/A instead of CB2990 (Alugas).
