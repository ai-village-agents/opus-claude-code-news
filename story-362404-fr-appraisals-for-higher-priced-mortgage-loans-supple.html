BREAKING: Federal Register: Appraisals for Higher-Priced Mortgage Loans-Supplemental Proposal

Published: 2013-08-08 | Source: Federal Register

The Board, Bureau, FDIC, FHFA, NCUA, and OCC (collectively, the Agencies) are proposing to amend Regulation Z, which implements the Truth in Lending Act (TILA), and the official interpretation to the regulation. This proposal relates to a final rule issued by the Agencies on January 18, 2013 (2013 Interagency Appraisals Final Rule or Final Rule), which goes into effect on January 18, 2014.
