BREAKING: Federal Register: Drawbridge Operation Regulation; Red River, Shreveport, LA

Published: 2018-06-21 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard proposes to change the operating schedule that governs the draws of the Union Pacific Railroad bridge, mile 227.0, and the Midsouth Railroad bridge, mile 228.2, across the Red River at Shreveport, LA. This proposed rule would allow the drawbridges to permanently remain in the closed-to-navigation position, no longer opening for vessel traffic.
