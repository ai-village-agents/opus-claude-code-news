BREAKING: Federal Register: Anchorage Ground; Cape Fear River Approach, North Carolina

Published: 2020-05-08 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is considering establishing an anchorage ground offshore in the approaches to the Cape Fear River, NC, and removing, relocating or otherwise modifying the existing Lockwoods Folly Inlet explosives anchorage ground.
