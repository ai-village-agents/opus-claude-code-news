BREAKING: Federal Register: Confirmatory Order; Issuance; In the Matter of ProTechnics Division of Core Laboratories LP

Published: 2023-12-08 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Nuclear Regulatory Commission (NRC) issued a Confirmatory Order to ProTechnics Division of Core Laboratories LP (ProTechnics) to document commitments made as part of a settlement agreement made between the NRC and ProTechnics following an alternative dispute resolution mediation session held on October 12, 2023.
