BREAKING: Federal Register: Affordable Connectivity Program; Emergency Broadband Benefit Program

Published: 2022-02-14 | Source: Federal Register

In this Report and Order, the Federal Communications Commission (Commission or FCC) adopts final rules for the Affordable Connectivity Program, established by Congress in the Infrastructure Investment and Jobs Act (Infrastructure Act). The Affordable Connectivity Program is designed to make broadband service and connected devices available to eligible low-income households at affordable, discounted prices from providers that opt to participate in the program.
