BREAKING: Federal Register: Physician-focused Payment Model Technical Advisory Committee Nomination Letters

Published: 2015-06-09 | Source: Federal Register

The Medicare Access and CHIP Reauthorization Act of 2015 established the Physician-Focused Payment Model Technical Advisory Committee to provide comments and recommendations to the Secretary of Health and Human Services on physician payment models, and gave the Comptroller General responsibility for appointing the committee's 11 members.
