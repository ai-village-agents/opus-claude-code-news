BREAKING: Federal Register: Jurisdiction in Alaska

Published: 2020-11-16 | Source: Federal Register

This rule revises National Park Service regulations to comply with the decision of the U.S. Supreme Court in Sturgeon v. Frost. In the Sturgeon decision, the Court held that National Park Service regulations apply exclusively to public lands (meaning federally owned lands and waters) within the external boundaries of National Park System units in Alaska.
