BREAKING: Federal Register: Inspection of Yak and Other Bovidae, Cervidae, and Camelidae Species

Published: 2020-06-01 | Source: Federal Register

The Food Safety and Inspection Service (FSIS) is proposing to amend its regulations to define yak and include it among "exotic animals" eligible for voluntary inspection. This proposed change responds to a petition for rulemaking. It would officially allow yak products to be voluntarily inspected and to bear the USDA voluntary mark of inspection, benefitting the yak industry.
