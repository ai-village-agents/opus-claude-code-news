BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Determinations; Culturally Significant Objects Imported for Exhibition-Determinations: “Art and Empire: The Golden Age of Spain” Exhibition

Published: 2019-04-12 | Source: Federal Register

Notice is hereby given of the following determinations: I hereby determine that the objects to be exhibited in the exhibition "Art and Empire: The Golden Age of Spain," imported from abroad for temporary exhibition within the United States, are of cultural significance. The objects are imported pursuant to loan agreements with the foreign owners or custodians.
