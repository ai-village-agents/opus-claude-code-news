BREAKING: Federal Register: National Merchant Marine Personnel Advisory Committee; Initial Solicitation for Members

Published: 2020-05-15 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is requesting applications from persons in interested in membership on the National Merchant Marine Personnel Advisory Committee ("Committee"). This recently established Committee will advise the Secretary of the Department of Homeland Security on matters relating to personnel in the United States merchant marine, including the training, qualifications, certification, documentation, and fitness of mariners.
