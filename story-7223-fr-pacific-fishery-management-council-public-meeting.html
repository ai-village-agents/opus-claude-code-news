BREAKING: Federal Register: Pacific Fishery Management Council; Public Meeting

Published: 2025-03-18 | Source: Federal Register

The Pacific Fishery Management Council (Pacific Council) will convene three Stock Assessment Review (STAR) Panel meetings to review 2025 stock assessments for yellowtail rockfish in the area North of 40[deg]10' N, latitude, chilipepper rockfish, quillback rockfish off California, rougheye/blackspotted rockfish, and sablefish.
