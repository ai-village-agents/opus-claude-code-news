BREAKING: Federal Register: Amendments to the Low-Income Housing Credit Compliance-Monitoring Regulations

Published: 2019-02-26 | Source: Federal Register

This document contains final regulations that amend the compliance monitoring regulations concerning the low-income housing credit under section 42 of the Internal Revenue Code (Code). These final regulations revise and clarify the requirement to conduct physical inspections and review low-income certifications and other documentation.
