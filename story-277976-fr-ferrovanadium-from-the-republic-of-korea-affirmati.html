BREAKING: Federal Register: Ferrovanadium From the Republic of Korea: Affirmative Preliminary Determination of Sales at Less Than Fair Value and Postponement of Final Determination and Extension of Provisional Measures

Published: 2016-11-01 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Commerce ("the Department") preliminarily determines that imports of ferrovanadium from the Republic of Korea ("Korea") are being, or are likely to be, sold in the United States at less than fair value ("LTFV"). The period of investigation ("POI") is January 1, 2015, through December 31, 2015.
