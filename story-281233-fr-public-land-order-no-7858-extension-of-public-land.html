BREAKING: Federal Register: Public Land Order No. 7858; Extension of Public Land Order No. 7233, Rogue River; Oregon

Published: 2016-12-12 | Source: Federal Register

This order extends the duration of the withdrawal created by Public Land Order No. 7233 for an additional 20-year period, which would otherwise expire on January 1, 2017. Public Land Order No. 7233 withdrew 2,090 acres of National Forest System lands within the Rogue River-Siskiyou National Forest from location and entry under the United States mining laws, but not from leasing under the mineral leasing laws.
