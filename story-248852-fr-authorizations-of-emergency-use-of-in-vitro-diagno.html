BREAKING: Federal Register: Authorizations of Emergency Use of In Vitro Diagnostic Devices for Detection of Zika Virus; Availability

Published: 2017-11-17 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA) is announcing the issuance of two Emergency Use Authorizations (EUAs) (the Authorizations) for in vitro diagnostic devices for detection of the Zika virus in response to the Zika virus outbreak in the Americas. FDA issued these Authorizations under the Federal Food, Drug, and Cosmetic Act (the FD&C Act), as requested by Siemens Healthcare Diagnostics, Inc. and Chembio Diagnostic Systems, Inc.
