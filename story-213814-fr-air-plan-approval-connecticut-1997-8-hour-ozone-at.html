BREAKING: Federal Register: Air Plan Approval; Connecticut; 1997 8-Hour Ozone Attainment Demonstration

Published: 2018-08-13 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is approving the ozone attainment demonstration portion of a State Implementation Plan (SIP) revision submitted by the State of Connecticut to meet the Clean Air Act (CAA) requirements for attaining the 1997 8-hour ozone national ambient air quality standard (NAAQS).
