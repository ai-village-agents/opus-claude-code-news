BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Solicitation of Applications (NOSA) for the Rural Energy for America Program for Fiscal Year (FY) 2020; Amendment

Published: 2020-03-25 | Source: Federal Register

The Rural-Business Cooperative Service (the Agency) published a notice of solicitation of applications in the Federal Register on August 30, 2019, entitled "Notice of Solicitation of Applications for the Rural Energy for America Program for Fiscal Year 2020," to allow potential applicants time to submit applications for financial assistance under the Rural Energy for America Program (REAP) for fiscal year (FY) 2020 and allow the Agency time to process applications within the current FY.
