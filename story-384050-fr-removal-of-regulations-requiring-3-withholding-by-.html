BREAKING: Federal Register: Removal of Regulations Requiring 3% Withholding by Government Entities

Published: 2012-04-25 | Source: Federal Register

This document provides a Treasury decision that removes the final regulations contained in TD 9524 relating to withholding by government entities on payments to persons providing property or services, and makes conforming amendments to regulations to reflect the removal of these regulations.
