BREAKING: Federal Register: Florida Keys National Marine Sanctuary: Establishment of Temporary Special Use Area for Coral Nursery

Published: 2023-11-02 | Source: Federal Register

On September 6, 2023, the National Oceanic and Atmospheric Administration (NOAA) issued a final temporary rule establishing a special use area of a 0.07 square mile area approximately five miles southeast of the community of Tavernier, on the island of Key Largo, within Federal waters of the Florida Keys National Marine Sanctuary (FKNMS) from September 6, 2023, through November 6, 2023.
