BREAKING: Federal Register: Request for Citizens Coinage Advisory Committee Membership Application

Published: 2015-09-24 | Source: Federal Register

Pursuant to United States Code, Title 31, section 5135 (b), the United States Mint is accepting applications for appointment to the Citizens Coinage Advisory Committee (CCAC) for a new member specially qualified to serve on the CCAC by virtue of his or her education, training, or experience in numismatics.
