BREAKING: Federal Register: Designation of Potential Wilderness as Wilderness, Point Reyes National Seashore

Published: 2012-12-04 | Source: Federal Register

This notice informs the public that all uses in Point Reyes National Seashore that are prohibited by the Wilderness Act (Pub. L. 88-577) have ceased and certain Federal lands that were previously designated as potential wilderness are, upon publication of this notice, designated as wilderness.
