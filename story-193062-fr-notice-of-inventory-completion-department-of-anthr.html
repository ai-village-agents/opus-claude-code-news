BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Inventory Completion: Department of Anthropology, San Jose State University, San Jose, CA

Published: 2019-11-08 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Anthropology at San Jose State University has completed an inventory of human remains and associated funerary objects, in consultation with the appropriate Indian Tribes or Native Hawaiian organizations, and has determined that there is a cultural affiliation between the human remains and associated funerary objects and present-day Indian Tribes or Native Hawaiian organizations.
