BREAKING: Federal Register: Prevailing Rate Systems; Abolishment of the Washington, DC, Special Wage Schedule for Printing Positions

Published: 2012-12-14 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Office of Personnel Management is issuing a final rule to abolish the Washington, DC, Federal Wage System (FWS) special wage schedule for printing and lithographic positions. Printing and lithographic employees in the Washington, DC, wage area will now be paid from the regular Washington, DC, appropriated fund FWS wage schedule.
