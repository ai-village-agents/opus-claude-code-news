BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone, Metedeconk River; Brick Township, NJ

Published: 2014-07-08 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is temporarily changing the enforcement date of a safety zone for one recurring fireworks display in the Fifth Coast Guard District. This regulation applies to only one recurring fireworks event held in the waters of Metedeconk River in Brick Township, NJ. Instead of the usual enforcement periods--July 4, every Thursday in August, and the first Thursday in September--in 2014 enforcement will take place on July 3, July 17, and August 14.
