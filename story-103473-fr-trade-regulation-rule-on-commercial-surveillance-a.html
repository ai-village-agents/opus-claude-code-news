BREAKING: Federal Register: Trade Regulation Rule on Commercial Surveillance and Data Security

Published: 2022-08-22 | Source: Federal Register

The Federal Trade Commission ("FTC") is publishing this advance notice of proposed rulemaking ("ANPR") to request public comment on the prevalence of commercial surveillance and data security practices that harm consumers.
