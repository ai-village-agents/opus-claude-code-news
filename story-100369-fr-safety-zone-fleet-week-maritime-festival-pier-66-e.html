BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone; Fleet Week Maritime Festival, Pier 66, Elliott Bay, Seattle, WA

Published: 2022-07-13 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard will enforce a safety zone for the Fleet Week Maritime Festival Pier 66 Safety Zone in Elliott Bay, Seattle, WA thirty minutes prior to the beginning, during, and thirty minutes following the conclusion of the parade of ships on August 1, 2022. This action is necessary to provide for the safety of life on navigable waterways during this event.
