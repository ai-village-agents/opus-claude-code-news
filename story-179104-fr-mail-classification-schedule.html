BREAKING: Federal Register: Mail Classification Schedule

Published: 2019-05-14 | Source: Federal Register

The Commission is proposing an amendment to its rules involving the information the Postal Service must provide when updating the size and weight limitations applicable to market dominant mail matter. The Commission invites public comment on the proposed revisions.
