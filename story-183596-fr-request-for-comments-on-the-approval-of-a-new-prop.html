BREAKING: Federal Register: Request for Comments on the Approval of a New Proposed Information Collection: Exercise Breakout 2019 Survey

Published: 2019-07-09 | Source: Federal Register

The Maritime Administration (MARAD) invites public comments on our intention to request the Office of Management and Budget (OMB) approval to of a new information collection. The information that will be collected from this survey pertains to merchant mariners training and familiarity with Naval systems and procedures.
