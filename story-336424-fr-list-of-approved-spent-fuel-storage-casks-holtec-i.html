BREAKING: Federal Register: List of Approved Spent Fuel Storage Casks: Holtec International HI-STORM FLOOD/WIND System; Certificate of Compliance No. 1032, Amendment No. 1

Published: 2014-10-03 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Nuclear Regulatory Commission (NRC) is proposing to amend its spent fuel storage regulations by revising the Holtec International HI-STORM FLOOD/WIND (FW) System listing within the "List of approved spent fuel storage casks" to include Amendment No. 1 to Certificate of Compliance (CoC) No. 1032.
