BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zones; Fireworks Displays in the Fifth Coast Guard District

Published: 2019-06-07 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard will enforce two safety zones for fireworks displays: The first taking place over the Chester River, at Kent Island Narrows, MD, on July 2, 2019, (an alternate date on July 3, 2019) and the second over the Chesapeake Bay, at Chesapeake Beach, MD, on July 3, 2019, (an alternate date on July 5, 2019).
