BREAKING: Federal Register: Approval of California Air Plan Revisions, Imperial County Air Pollution Control District

Published: 2015-11-12 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is taking final action to approve a revision to the Imperial County Air Pollution Control District (ICAPCD) portion of the California State Implementation Plan (SIP). This revision concerns the District's reasonably available control technology (RACT) requirements under the 1997 8-hour National Ambient Air Quality Standards (NAAQS) for ozone.
