BREAKING: Federal Register: Clarification and Update of the Trade Fair Certification Program

Published: 2016-07-29 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Department of Commerce (Commerce) is intending to update the Trade Fair Certification Program, which recognizes and endorses U.S. participation in selected, privately organized, foreign trade fairs, in the coming months. Proposed changes will be announced through the Federal Register and comments will be solicited and reviewed before a final rule is issued.
