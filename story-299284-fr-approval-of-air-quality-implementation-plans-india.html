BREAKING: Federal Register: Approval of Air Quality Implementation Plans; Indiana; Lead Rule Revisions

Published: 2015-07-17 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is proposing to approve a request submitted on March 14, 2013, and supplemented on November 17, 2014, by the Indiana Department of Environmental Management to revise the state implementation plan for lead. The submittal updates Indiana's lead rule at Title 326 of the Indiana Administrative Code (IAC), Article 15.
