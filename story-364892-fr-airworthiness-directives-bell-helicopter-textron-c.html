BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Bell Helicopter Textron Canada Inc. Helicopters

Published: 2013-09-06 | Source: Federal Register

We are adopting a new airworthiness directive (AD) for certain Bell Helicopter Textron Canada Inc. (BHT) Model 206A, 206B, and 206L helicopters. This AD requires replacing certain part-numbered engine auto-relight kit control boxes. This AD was prompted by a design review that revealed the control box chipset did not meet the required temperature range requirements, which could cause the control box to malfunction, disabling the engine auto-relight system.
