BREAKING: Federal Register: Office of Health Assessment and Translation Webinar on Lessons Learned in Application of the OHAT Framework for Systematic Review and Evidence Integration to Case Studies; Notice of Public Webinar and Registration Information

Published: 2014-06-25 | Source: Federal Register

The National Toxicology Program (NTP) announces a public webinar, "Lessons Learned in Application of the Office of Health Assessment and Translation (OHAT) Framework for Systematic Review and Evidence Integration to Case Studies." The OHAT, Division of the National Toxicology Program (DNTP), National Institute of Environmental Health Sciences (NIEHS) will host the web-based meeting and the public can register to participate at http://ntp.niehs.nih.gov/go/41629.
