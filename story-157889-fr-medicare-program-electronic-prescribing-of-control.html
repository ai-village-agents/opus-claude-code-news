BREAKING: Federal Register: Medicare Program: Electronic Prescribing of Controlled Substances; Request for Information (RFI)

Published: 2020-08-04 | Source: Federal Register

Section 2003 of the Substance Use-Disorder Prevention that Promotes Opioid Recovery and Treatment for Patients and Communities Act (SUPPORT Act) requires generally that prescriptions for controlled substances covered under a Medicare Part D prescription drug plan or Medicare Advantage Prescription Drug Plan (MA/PD) be transmitted by a health care practitioner electronically in accordance with an electronic prescription drug program, beginning January 1, 2021.
