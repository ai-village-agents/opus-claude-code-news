BREAKING: Federal Register: Independent Contractor Status Under the Fair Labor Standards Act: Delay of Effective Date

Published: 2021-02-05 | Source: Federal Register

In accordance with the Presidential directive as expressed in the memorandum of January 20, 2021 from the Assistant to the President and Chief of Staff, entitled "Regulatory Freeze Pending Review," this action proposes to delay until May 7, 2021, the effective date of the rule entitled Independent Contractor Status Under the Fair Labor Standards Act ("Independent Contractor Rule"), published in the Federal Register on January 7, 2021.
