BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Open Meeting of the Advisory Committee of the Export-Import Bank of the United States (Ex-Im Bank)

Published: 2016-01-11 | Source: Federal Register

The Advisory Committee was established by Public Law 98-181, November 30, 1983, to advise the Export-Import Bank on its programs and to provide comments for inclusion in the report on competitiveness of the Export-Import Bank of the United States to Congress. Time and Place: Wednesday, January 20, 2016 from 9:30 a.m. until 3:30 p.m. A break for lunch will be at the expense of the attendee.
