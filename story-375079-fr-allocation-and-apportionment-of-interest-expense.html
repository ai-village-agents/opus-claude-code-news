BREAKING: Federal Register: Allocation and Apportionment of Interest Expense

Published: 2012-01-17 | Source: Federal Register

This document contains temporary regulations that provide guidance regarding the allocation and apportionment of interest expense. These temporary regulations provide guidance concerning the allocation and apportionment of interest expense by corporations owning a 10 percent or greater interest in a partnership, as well as the allocation and apportionment of interest expense using the fair market value method.
