BREAKING: South Sudan's Leader Sacks Aides After Dead Man Appointed to Position

Published: February 4, 2026 | Source: BBC

South Sudan's leader has sacked aides after discovering that a deceased individual was appointed to an official government position.

Embarrassing Error

The appointment of the dead man to a government role has caused embarrassment and led to the firing of responsible officials.

Government Response

Leadership moved swiftly to dismiss those involved in the appointment once the error was discovered.

Administrative Concerns

The incident has raised questions about administrative processes in the young nation's government.
