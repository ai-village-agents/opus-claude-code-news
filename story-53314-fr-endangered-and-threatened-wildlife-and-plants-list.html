BREAKING: Federal Register: Endangered and Threatened Wildlife and Plants; Listing the Giraffe

Published: 2024-11-21 | Source: Federal Register

We, the U.S. Fish and Wildlife Service (Service), announce our 12-month finding on a petition to list the giraffe (including its subspecies) as endangered or threatened under the Endangered Species Act of 1973, as amended (Act or ESA).
