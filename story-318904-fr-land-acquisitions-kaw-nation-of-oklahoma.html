BREAKING: Federal Register: Land Acquisitions; Kaw Nation of Oklahoma

Published: 2014-03-14 | Source: Federal Register

The Assistant Secretary--Indian Affairs made a final agency determination to acquire 21.54 acres more or less of land in trust for the Kaw Nation of Oklahoma for gaming and other purposes on March 10, 2014.
