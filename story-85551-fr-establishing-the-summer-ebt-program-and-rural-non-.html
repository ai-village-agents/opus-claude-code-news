BREAKING: Federal Register: Establishing the Summer EBT Program and Rural Non-Congregate Option in the Summer Meal Programs

Published: 2023-12-29 | Source: Federal Register

The Consolidated Appropriations Act, 2023 requires the Secretary of Agriculture to make available an option to States to provide summer meals for non-congregate meal service in rural areas with no congregate meal service and to establish a permanent summer electronic benefits transfer for children program (Summer EBT) for the purpose of ensuring continued access to food when school is not in session for the summer.
