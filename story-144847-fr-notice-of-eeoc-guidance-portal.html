BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of EEOC Guidance Portal

Published: 2020-02-20 | Source: Federal Register

Pursuant to Executive Order 13891 and OMB Memorandum M-20-02, the Equal Employment Opportunity Commission (EEOC) is noticing the February 28, 2020 launch of a single, searchable, indexed database containing all EEOC guidance documents currently in effect.
