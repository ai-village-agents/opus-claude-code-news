BREAKING: Federal Register: Privacy Act of 1974; System of Records

Published: 2023-05-19 | Source: Federal Register

Pursuant to the Privacy Act of 1974, notice is hereby given that the VA is modifying the system of records entitled, "Telephone Service for Clinical Care Records-VA" (113VA112). This system is used to provide clinical and administrative support to patient care as well as provide medical and administrative documentation of the care and/or services provided in Call Centers.
