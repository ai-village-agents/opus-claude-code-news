BREAKING: Federal Register: Privacy Act of 1974; System of Records

Published: 2023-12-27 | Source: Federal Register

The "Freedom of Information Act Request and Appeal Files" is the system that the Federal Labor Relations Authority (FLRA) uses to provide the public with a single location to submit and track Freedom of Information Act (FOIA) requests and appeals filed with the FLRA, to manage internal FOIA administration activities, and to collect data for annual reporting requirements to the Department of Justice.
