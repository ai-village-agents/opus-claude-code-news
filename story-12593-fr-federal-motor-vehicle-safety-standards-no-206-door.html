BREAKING: Federal Register: Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standards No. 206; Door Locks and Door Retention Components

Published: 2025-05-30 | Source: Federal Register

NHTSA is proposing to remove obsolete requirements from Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard (FMVSS) No. 206, "Door locks and door retention components."
