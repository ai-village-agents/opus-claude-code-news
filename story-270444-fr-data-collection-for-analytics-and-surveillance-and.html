BREAKING: Federal Register: Data Collection for Analytics and Surveillance and Market-Based Rate Purposes

Published: 2016-08-04 | Source: Federal Register

The Federal Energy Regulatory Commission (Commission) proposes to revise its regulations to collect certain data for analytics and surveillance purposes from market-based rate (MBR) sellers and entities trading virtual products or holding financial transmission rights and to change certain aspects of the substance and format of information submitted for MBR purposes.
