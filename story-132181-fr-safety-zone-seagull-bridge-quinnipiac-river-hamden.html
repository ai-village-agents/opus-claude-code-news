BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone; Seagull Bridge, Quinnipiac River, Hamden, CT

Published: 2021-09-02 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is establishing a temporary safety zone for all navigable waters within a 25-yard radius of any foundation, support, stanchion, pier or abutment of the Seagull Bridge located on the Quinnipiac River, Hamden, CT. The safety zone is needed to protect personnel and property from potential hazards created by falling debris.
