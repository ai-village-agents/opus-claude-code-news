BREAKING: Federal Register: Filing Dates for the Florida Special Elections in the 19th Congressional District

Published: 2014-02-13 | Source: Federal Register

Florida has scheduled special elections on April 22, 2014, and June 24, 2014, to fill the U.S. House of Representatives seat vacated by Representative Trey Radel. Committees required to file reports in connection with the Special Primary Election on April 22, 2014, shall file a 12-day Pre-Primary Report.
