BREAKING: Federal Register: Air Plan Disapproval; Missouri; Control of Sulfur Dioxide Emissions

Published: 2023-01-04 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is taking final action to disapprove revisions to the Missouri State Implementation Plan (SIP) submitted by Missouri on March 7, 2019. In its submission, Missouri requested rescinding a regulation addressing sulfur compounds from the SIP and replacing it with a new regulation that establishes requirements for units emitting sulfur dioxide (SO2).
