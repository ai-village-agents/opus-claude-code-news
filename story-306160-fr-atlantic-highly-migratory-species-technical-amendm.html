BREAKING: Federal Register: Atlantic Highly Migratory Species; Technical Amendment to Regulations

Published: 2015-10-07 | Source: Federal Register

NMFS is hereby making technical amendments to the regulations for Atlantic highly migratory species--specifically, to several restricted fishing areas--without altering the substance of the regulations. Also, this action re-inserts the longstanding statutorily required limit on length of gillnets that was erroneously removed from the regulations in late 2012, and corrects the end date of the Spring Gulf of Mexico gear restricted areas from May 30 to May 31.
