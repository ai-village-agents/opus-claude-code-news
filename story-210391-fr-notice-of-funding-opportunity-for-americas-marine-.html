BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Funding Opportunity for America's Marine Highway Projects

Published: 2018-06-27 | Source: Federal Register

The Consolidated Appropriations Act, 2018, signed by the President on March 23, 2018, appropriated $7,000,000 to the Short Sea Transportation Program, commonly referred to as the America's Marine Highway Program (AMHP). The purpose of the appropriation is to make grants to previously designated Marine Highway Projects that support the development and expansion of documented vessels, or of port and landside infrastructure.
