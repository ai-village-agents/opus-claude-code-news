BREAKING: Federal Register: Review of Administrative Rules

Published: 2018-05-03 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Nuclear Regulatory Commission (NRC) is initiating a retrospective review of administrative requirements to identify outdated or duplicative administrative requirements that may be eliminated without an adverse effect on public health or safety, common defense and security, protection of the environment, or regulatory efficiency and effectiveness.
