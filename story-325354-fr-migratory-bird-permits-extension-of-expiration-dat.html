BREAKING: Federal Register: Migratory Bird Permits; Extension of Expiration Dates for Double-Crested Cormorant Depredation Orders

Published: 2014-05-28 | Source: Federal Register

We, the U.S. Fish and Wildlife Service (FWS), revise the two depredation orders for double-crested cormorants (Phalacrocorax auritus, DCCOs). We extend the expiration dates for the orders for 5 years to allow State and Tribal resource management agencies to continue to manage DCCO problems and gather data on the effects of DCCO control actions.
