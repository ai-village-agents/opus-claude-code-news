BREAKING: Federal Register: Administrative Priority and Definitions for Discretionary Grant Programs

Published: 2020-09-08 | Source: Federal Register

The Secretary of Education proposes to establish a priority and definitions for discretionary grant programs that would promote the use of the Department of Education's (the Department's) discretionary grants funds to support remote learning (as defined in this notice).
