BREAKING: Federal Register: Availability of Final Evaluation Findings of State Coastal Programs and National Estuarine Research Reserves

Published: 2022-04-28 | Source: Federal Register

Notice is hereby given of the availability of final evaluation findings for three state coastal programs, Illinois, Indiana, and Massachusetts, and two national estuarine research reserves, Apalachicola and Delaware, under Sections 312 and 315 of the Coastal Zone Management Act (CZMA).
