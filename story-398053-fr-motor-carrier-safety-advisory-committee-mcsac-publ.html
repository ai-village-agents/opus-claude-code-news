BREAKING: Federal Register: Motor Carrier Safety Advisory Committee (MCSAC): Public Subcommittee Meeting

Published: 2012-10-03 | Source: Federal Register

FMCSA announces that the CSA Subcommittee of the MCSAC will hold a meeting on Tuesday--Wednesday, October 16-17, 2012. Established at the August 2012 meeting of the MCSAC in accordance with Task 12-03, the subcommittee will provide the MCSAC with concepts, ideas, and recommendations on the CSA program. The entire subcommittee meeting will be open to the public, with a public comment period to occur during the last one-half hour of each day.
