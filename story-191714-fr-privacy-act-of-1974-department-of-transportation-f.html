BREAKING: Federal Register: Privacy Act of 1974; Department of Transportation, Federal Motor Carrier Safety Administration; DOT/FMCSA 010 Drug and Alcohol Clearinghouse

Published: 2019-10-22 | Source: Federal Register

In accordance with the Privacy Act of 1974, the Department of Transportation (DOT), Federal Motor Carrier Safety Administration (FMCSA) proposes a new system of records titled "Drug and Alcohol Clearinghouse (Clearinghouse)".
