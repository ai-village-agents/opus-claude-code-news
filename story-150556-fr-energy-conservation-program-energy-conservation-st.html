BREAKING: Federal Register: Energy Conservation Program: Energy Conservation Standards for General Service Fluorescent Lamps and Incandescent Reflector Lamps

Published: 2020-05-01 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Department of Energy ("DOE") is initiating an effort to determine whether to amend the current energy conservation standards for general service fluorescent lamps ("GSFLs") and incandescent reflector lamps ("IRLs").
