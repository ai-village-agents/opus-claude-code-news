BREAKING: Federal Register: Proposed Amendment of Class E Airspace, Hamilton, NY

Published: 2018-02-09 | Source: Federal Register

This action proposes to amend Class E airspace at Hamilton Municipal Airport (formerly Elisha Payne Airport), Hamilton, NY, to accommodate airspace reconfiguration due to the decommissioning of the Georgetown VHF omni-directional radio range tactical air navigation aid (VORTAC), and cancellation of the VORTAC approach.
