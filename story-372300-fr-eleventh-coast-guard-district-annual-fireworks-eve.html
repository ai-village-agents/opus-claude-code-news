BREAKING: Federal Register: Eleventh Coast Guard District Annual Fireworks Events

Published: 2013-12-10 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard proposes to amend several permanent safety zones located in the Eleventh Coast Guard District that are established to protect public safety during annual firework displays. These amendments will standardize the safety zone language, update listed events, delete events that are no longer occurring, add new annual fireworks events, and establish a standardized format using a table to list these recurring annual fireworks events.
