BREAKING: Federal Register: Monetary Threshold for Reporting Rail Equipment Accidents/Incidents for Calendar Year 2016

Published: 2015-12-28 | Source: Federal Register

This rule maintains the rail equipment accident/incident monetary reporting threshold at $10,500 for railroad accidents/ incidents involving property damage that occur during calendar year (CY) 2016 that FRA's accident/incident reporting regulations require to be reported to the agency.
