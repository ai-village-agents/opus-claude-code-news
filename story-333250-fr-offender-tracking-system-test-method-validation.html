BREAKING: Federal Register: Offender Tracking System Test Method Validation

Published: 2014-08-27 | Source: Federal Register

National Institute of Justice has recently developed draft minimum performance standards for offender tracking systems. In order to ensure that the test methods in the standards are adequate and properly documented, NIJ is requesting proposals (including price quotes) for test method validation efforts from testing laboratories.
