BREAKING: Federal Register: Standards for Business Practices of Interstate Natural Gas Pipelines

Published: 2015-07-24 | Source: Federal Register

The Federal Energy Regulatory Commission (Commission) is proposing to amend its regulations to incorporate by reference, with certain enumerated exceptions, the latest version (Version 3.0) of business practice standards adopted by the Wholesale Gas Quadrant of the North American Energy Standards Board (NAESB) applicable to natural gas pipelines.
