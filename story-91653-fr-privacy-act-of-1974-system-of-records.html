BREAKING: Federal Register: Privacy Act of 1974; System of Records

Published: 2022-03-22 | Source: Federal Register

We propose revising Appendix A of our existing Privacy Act inventory of systems subject to the Privacy Act of 1974, which contains the common routine uses that apply to some or all of our systems of records. We propose to revise routine use H, which permits sharing information when there has been a data breach and it's necessary to respond to the breach.
