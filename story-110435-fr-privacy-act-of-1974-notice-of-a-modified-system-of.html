BREAKING: Federal Register: Privacy Act of 1974; Notice of a Modified System of Records

Published: 2022-11-21 | Source: Federal Register

GSA proposes to modify a system of records subject to the Privacy Act of 1974, as amended. Login.gov is a secure sign-in service with the capability to authenticate and identity proof users before the user is granted access to participating government websites or applications.
