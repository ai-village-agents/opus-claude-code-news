BREAKING: Federal Register: Announcement of the Intent To Award a Supplement to Upwardly Global in New York, NY

Published: 2021-11-12 | Source: Federal Register

The ACF, ORR, Division of Refugee Services (DRS) announces the intent to award a supplement in the amount of up to $1,500,000 to Upwardly Global in New York, NY. The purpose of this award is to enable the recipient to provide program services to Afghan Special Immigrant Visa holders and humanitarian parolees currently housed on U.S. military installations awaiting resettlement.
