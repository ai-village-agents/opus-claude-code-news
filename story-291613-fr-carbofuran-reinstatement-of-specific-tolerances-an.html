BREAKING: Federal Register: Carbofuran; Reinstatement of Specific Tolerances and Removal of Expired Tolerances

Published: 2015-04-17 | Source: Federal Register

EPA is amending its regulations to reinstate four import tolerances for carbofuran, in order to comply with a DC Circuit decision and order vacating the Agency's revocation of those tolerances. EPA is also removing several carbofuran time-limited tolerances that have already expired.
