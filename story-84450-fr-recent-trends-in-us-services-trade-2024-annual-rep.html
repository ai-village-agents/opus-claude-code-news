BREAKING: Federal Register: Recent Trends in U.S. Services Trade, 2024 Annual Report

Published: 2023-12-15 | Source: Federal Register

The Commission has prepared and published annual reports in this series since 1996 under the investigation title Recent Trends in U.S. Services Trade. The 2024 report, which the Commission plans to publish in May 2024, will provide aggregate data on cross-border trade in services for the period ending in 2022, and transactions by affiliates based outside the country of their parent firm for the period ending in 2021.
