BREAKING: Federal Register: Changes in Flood Hazard Determinations

Published: 2022-07-06 | Source: Federal Register

On June 1, 2022, FEMA published in the Federal Register a changes in flood hazard determination notice that contained an erroneous table. This notice provides corrections to that table to be used in lieu of the erroneous information. The table provided here represents the changes in flood hazard determinations and communities affected for the City of Margaret and Unincorporated Areas of St. Clair County, Alabama.
