BREAKING: Federal Register: Public Availability of Fiscal Year 2016 Agency Inventories Under the Federal Activities Inventory Reform Act

Published: 2018-02-20 | Source: Federal Register

The Federal Activities Inventory Reform (FAIR) Act, requires agencies to develop inventories each year of activities performed by their employees that are not inherently governmental functions. The FAIR Act further requires OMB to review the inventories in consultation with the agencies. Once that review is complete, agencies are required to make the list available to the public and OMB must publish a notice of public availability in the Federal Register.
