BREAKING: Federal Register: Action Level for Inorganic Arsenic in Apple Juice: Guidance for Industry; Availability

Published: 2023-06-02 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA or we) is announcing the availability of a final guidance for industry entitled "Action Level for Inorganic Arsenic in Apple Juice." The guidance identifies for industry an action level for inorganic arsenic in apple juice that is intended to help protect public health by reducing exposure to inorganic arsenic and is achievable with the use of current good manufacturing practices.
