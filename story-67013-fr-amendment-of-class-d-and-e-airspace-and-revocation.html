BREAKING: Federal Register: Amendment of Class D and E Airspace and Revocation of Class E Airspace; Kalamazoo, MI

Published: 2023-05-08 | Source: Federal Register

This action proposes to amend the Class D and Class E airspace and remove Class E airspace at Kalamazoo, MI. The FAA is proposing this action as the result of an airspace review due to the decommissioning of the Kalamazoo very high frequency omnidirectional range (VOR) as part of the VOR Minimum Operating Network (MON) Program.
