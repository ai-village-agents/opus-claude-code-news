BREAKING: Federal Register: Environmental Impact Statement for the All Aboard Florida Miami-Orlando Passenger Rail Project

Published: 2013-04-15 | Source: Federal Register

FRA is issuing this notice to advise the public that FRA will prepare an Environmental Impact Statement (EIS) to evaluate the potential environmental and related impacts of constructing and operating an intercity passenger rail service proposed by the private company, All Aboard Florida--Operations LLC (AAF), between Miami and Orlando, Florida, with intermediate stops in Fort Lauderdale and West Palm Beach, Florida (Proposed Action).
