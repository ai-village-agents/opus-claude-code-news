BREAKING: Federal Register: Harmonization of the Fees and Application Procedures for the Global Entry and SENTRI Programs and Other Changes

Published: 2024-04-02 | Source: Federal Register

This final rule amends DHS regulations regarding two CBP trusted traveler programs: Global Entry and Secure Electronic Network for Travelers Rapid Inspection (SENTRI).
