BREAKING: Federal Register: Request for Information: Establish a Public-Private Collaboration, “Drug Development Initiative” (DDI), for New Pharmacological Treatments for Post-Traumatic Stress Disorder (PTSD)

Published: 2012-11-29 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Veterans Affairs (VA) ORD publishes this Request for Information (RFI) to solicit interest in forming public- private collaborations to develop new pharmacological treatments for PTSD. ORD is interested in developing collaborations with organizations that are interested in pursuing clinical trials specifically focused on PTSD.
