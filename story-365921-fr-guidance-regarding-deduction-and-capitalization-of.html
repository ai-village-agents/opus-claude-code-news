BREAKING: Federal Register: Guidance Regarding Deduction and Capitalization of Expenditures Related to Tangible Property

Published: 2013-09-19 | Source: Federal Register

This document contains final regulations that provide guidance on the application of sections 162(a) and 263(a) of the Internal Revenue Code (Code) to amounts paid to acquire, produce, or improve tangible property. The final regulations clarify and expand the standards in the current regulations under sections 162(a) and 263(a).
