BREAKING: Federal Register: Submission for OMB Review; State Temporary Assistance for Needy Families Case Studies (New Collection)

Published: 2019-07-16 | Source: Federal Register

The Office of Planning, Research, and Evaluation (OPRE) is proposing a data collection activity as part of the State Temporary Assistance for Needy Families (TANF) Case Studies project. This study seeks to document innovative employment and training programs for low- income individuals including TANF recipients and examine the ways the programs provide or link families to wraparound services.
