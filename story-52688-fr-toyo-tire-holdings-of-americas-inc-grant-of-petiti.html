BREAKING: Federal Register: Toyo Tire Holdings of Americas Inc., Grant of Petition for Decision of Inconsequential Noncompliance

Published: 2024-11-15 | Source: Federal Register

Toyo Tire Holdings of Americas, Inc., (Toyo) has determined that certain Open Country R/T light truck tires, do not fully comply with Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard (FMVSS) No. 119, New Pneumatic Tires for Motor Vehicles with a GVWR of More Than 4,536 Kilograms (10,000 Pounds) and Motorcycles. Toyo filed a noncompliance report dated March 15, 2021, and later amended it on April 2, 2021.
