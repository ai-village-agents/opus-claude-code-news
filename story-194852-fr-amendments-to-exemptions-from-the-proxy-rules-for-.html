BREAKING: Federal Register: Amendments to Exemptions From the Proxy Rules for Proxy Voting Advice

Published: 2019-12-04 | Source: Federal Register

The Securities and Exchange Commission ("Commission") is proposing amendments to its rules governing proxy solicitations to help ensure that investors who use proxy voting advice receive more accurate, transparent, and complete information on which to make their voting decisions, in a manner that does not impose undue costs or delays that could adversely affect the timely provision of proxy voting advice.
