BREAKING: Federal Register: Draft Guidance for Nationally Consistent Coastal Zone Area Contingency Plan Architecture

Published: 2022-11-10 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard announces the availability, in the docket, of a draft guidance document intended to establish a new, nationally consistent architecture for coastal zone Area Contingency Plans (ACPs). ACPs are required by the Clean Water Act and demonstrate the planning for oil and hazardous substance incident response at the local level.
