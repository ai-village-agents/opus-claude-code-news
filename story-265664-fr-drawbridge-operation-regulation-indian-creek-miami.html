BREAKING: Federal Register: Drawbridge Operation Regulation; Indian Creek, Miami Beach, FL

Published: 2016-06-08 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is changing the operating schedule that governs the 63rd Street Bridge across Indian Creek, mile 4.0, at Miami Beach, FL. This rule implements restrictions that allow the bridge to remain closed during peak vehicle traffic times. Bridge openings during peak vehicle traffic times cause major traffic jams that may be avoided without negatively impacting vessel traffic on the Indian Creek.
