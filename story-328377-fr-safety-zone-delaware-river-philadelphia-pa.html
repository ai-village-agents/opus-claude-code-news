BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone, Delaware River; Philadelphia, PA

Published: 2014-07-01 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is establishing a safety zone on the Delaware River in Philadelphia, PA, from 8:30 p.m. to 9:30 p.m. on July 1, 2014.
