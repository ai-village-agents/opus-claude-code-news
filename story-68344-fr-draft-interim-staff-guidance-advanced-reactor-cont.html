BREAKING: Federal Register: Draft Interim Staff Guidance: Advanced Reactor Content of Application Project, “Risk-informed Inservice Inspection/Inservice Testing”

Published: 2023-05-25 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Nuclear Regulatory Commission (NRC) is soliciting public comment on its draft Interim Staff Guidance (ISG) DANU-ISG-2022- 07, "Risk-informed Inservice Inspection/Inservice Testing."
