BREAKING: Federal Register: Use of Nanomaterials in Food for Animals; Guidance for Industry; Availability

Published: 2015-08-05 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA) is announcing the availability of guidance for industry #220 entitled "Use of Nanomaterials in Food for Animals." The guidance describes FDA's current thinking regarding the use of nanomaterials or the application of nanotechnology in food for animals.
