BREAKING: Federal Register: Pacific Alternating Current Intertie Point-to-Point Transmission Service-Rate Order No. WAPA-211

Published: 2026-01-26 | Source: Federal Register

The formula rates for the Sierra Nevada (SN) region Pacific Alternating Current Intertie (PACI) point-to-point transmission service have been confirmed, approved, and placed into effect on an interim basis (Provisional Formula Rates). These new formula rates supersede rates for short-term sales on the PACI, PACI-T4 REV, PACI-T5 REV and PACI DLAP-T6 (approved under Delegation Order No. S3-DEL-WAPA1-2023, Section 1.4.B).
