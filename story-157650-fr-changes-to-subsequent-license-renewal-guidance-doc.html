BREAKING: Federal Register: Changes to Subsequent License Renewal Guidance Documents; Correction

Published: 2020-07-31 | Source: Federal Register

On July 2, 2020, the U.S. Nuclear Regulatory Commission (NRC) solicited comments on three draft interim staff guidance documents (ISGs) that propose changes to the NRC's subsequent license renewal guidance documents. Several aging management review (AMR) line items were inadvertently omitted from the draft mechanical ISG. These omitted AMR line items are provided in an errata document.
