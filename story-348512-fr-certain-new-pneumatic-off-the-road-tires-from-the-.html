BREAKING: Federal Register: Certain New Pneumatic Off-the-Road Tires From the People's Republic of China: Preliminary Results of Antidumping Duty New Shipper Review; 2011-2012

Published: 2013-03-05 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Commerce ("the Department") is conducting a new shipper review of the antidumping duty order on certain new pneumatic off-the-road tires ("OTR tires") from the People's Republic of China ("PRC"). The period of review ("POR") is September 1, 2011, through February 29, 2012. The review covers a single entry of subject merchandise exported by Trelleborg Wheel Systems (Xingtai) China, Co. Ltd.
