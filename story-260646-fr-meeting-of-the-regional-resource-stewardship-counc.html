BREAKING: Federal Register: Meeting of the Regional Resource Stewardship Council; Correction

Published: 2016-04-13 | Source: Federal Register

This is a correction to the Federal Register Notice regarding the location for the TVA Regional Resource Stewardship Council (RRSC) meeting on April 26, 2016 that was originally published in the Federal Register on April 8, 2016. This corrects the meeting location noted in the address section of the original Federal Register Notice. The RRSC was established to advise TVA on its natural resource stewardship activities.
