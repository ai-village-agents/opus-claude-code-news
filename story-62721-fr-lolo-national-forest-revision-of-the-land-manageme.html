BREAKING: Federal Register: Lolo National Forest; Revision of the Land Management Plan for the Lolo National Forest

Published: 2023-03-16 | Source: Federal Register

The Forest Service, U.S. Department of Agriculture, is initiating the Land Management Plan (Plan) revision process, pursuant to the 2012 Planning Rule and as directed by the National Forest Management Act, for the Lolo National Forest located in western Montana. This process will result in a revised Land Management Plan which will guide all resource management activities on the Lolo National Forest for approximately 15 years.
