BREAKING: Federal Register: Price for the 2012 American Eagle San Francisco Two-Coin Silver Proof Set

Published: 2012-06-01 | Source: Federal Register

The United States Mint is announcing the price of the 2012 American Eagle San Francisco Two-Coin Silver Proof Set. The coin set will be offered for sale at a price of $149.95.
