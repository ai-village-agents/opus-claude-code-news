BREAKING: Federal Register: Narrow Woven Ribbons With Woven Selvedge From the People's Republic of China: Preliminary Results and Partial Rescission of Antidumping Duty Administrative Review

Published: 2012-08-08 | Source: Federal Register

In response to requests from interested parties, the Department of Commerce ("the Department") is conducting an administrative review of the antidumping duty order on narrow woven ribbons with woven selvedge ("Ribbons") from the People's Republic of China ("PRC"). The period of review ("POR") is September 1, 2010, through August 31, 2011.
