BREAKING: Federal Register: Recordkeeping for Custodial Accounts

Published: 2024-10-02 | Source: Federal Register

The Federal Deposit Insurance Corporation (FDIC) is proposing requirements that would strengthen FDIC-insured depository institutions' (IDI) recordkeeping for custodial deposit accounts with transactional features and preserve beneficial owners' and depositors' entitlement to the protections afforded by Federal deposit insurance.
