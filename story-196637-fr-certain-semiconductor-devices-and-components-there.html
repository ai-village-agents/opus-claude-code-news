BREAKING: Federal Register: Certain Semiconductor Devices and Components Thereof (I); Commission Determination Not To Review an Initial Determination (Order No. 7) Terminating an Investigation Due to a Settlement Agreement; Termination of Investigation

Published: 2019-12-30 | Source: Federal Register

Notice is hereby given that the U.S. International Trade Commission (the "Commission") has determined not to review an initial determination ("ID") (Order No. 7) terminating the investigation due to a settlement agreement. The investigation is terminated.
