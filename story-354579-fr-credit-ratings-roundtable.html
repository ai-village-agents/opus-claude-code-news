BREAKING: Federal Register: Credit Ratings Roundtable

Published: 2013-05-10 | Source: Federal Register

This notice announces the change of location of the May 14, 2013, Credit Ratings Roundtable that was published in the April 29, 2013 Federal Register (78 FR 25101 through 25102). The new location of the roundtable discussion is Room L-002 (the Auditorium) at the Securities and Exchange Commission's headquarters located at 100 F Street NE., in Washington, DC 20549.
