BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; General Electric Company Turbofan Engines

Published: 2013-08-06 | Source: Federal Register

We are superseding emergency airworthiness directive (AD) 2013-14-51 for General Electric Company (GE) GE90-110B1 and GE90-115B turbofan engines with affected transfer gearbox assembly (TGB) radial gearshafts installed. AD 2013-14-51 was sent previously to all known U.S. owners and operators of GE90-110B1 and GE90-115B turbofan engines.
