BREAKING: Federal Register: Mesothelioma Registry Feasibility; Request for Information; Extension of Comment Period

Published: 2019-07-10 | Source: Federal Register

On April 8, 2019, the National Institute for Occupational Safety and Health (NIOSH), within the Centers for Disease Control and Prevention (CDC), opened a docket to obtain information on the feasibility of a registry designed to track mesothelioma cases in the United States, as well as recommendations on enrollment, data collection, confidentiality, and registry maintenance.
