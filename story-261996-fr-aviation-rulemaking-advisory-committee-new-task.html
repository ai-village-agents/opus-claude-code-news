BREAKING: Federal Register: Aviation Rulemaking Advisory Committee-New Task

Published: 2016-04-27 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA assigned the Aviation Rulemaking Advisory Committee (ARAC) a new task to provide recommendations regarding bird strike protection rulemaking, policy, and guidance for normal category rotorcraft and to provide recommendations to enhance the existing bird strike protection standards for transport category rotorcraft.
