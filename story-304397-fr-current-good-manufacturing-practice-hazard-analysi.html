BREAKING: Federal Register: Current Good Manufacturing Practice, Hazard Analysis, and Risk-Based Preventive Controls for Human Food

Published: 2015-09-17 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA or we) is amending our regulation for Current Good Manufacturing Practice In Manufacturing, Packing, or Holding Human Food in two fundamental ways. First, we are modernizing the long-standing current good manufacturing practice requirements. Second, we are adding requirements for domestic and foreign facilities that are subject to our regulation for Registration of Food Facilities to establish and implement hazard analysis and risk- based preventive controls...
