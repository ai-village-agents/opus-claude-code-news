BREAKING: Federal Register: National Advisory Committee

Published: 2021-10-08 | Source: Federal Register

The Census Bureau is giving notice of a virtual meeting of the National Advisory Committee (NAC). The Committee will address policy, research, and technical issues relating to a full range of Census Bureau programs and activities, including decennial, economic, field operations, information technology, and statistics.
