BREAKING: Federal Register: Automated Driving Systems: A Vision for Safety

Published: 2017-09-15 | Source: Federal Register

NHTSA is releasing new voluntary guidance on automated driving systems--Automated Driving Systems: A Vision for Safety. The new voluntary guidance is based on public comments received on the Federal Automated Vehicles Policy (FAVP) released in September 2016.
