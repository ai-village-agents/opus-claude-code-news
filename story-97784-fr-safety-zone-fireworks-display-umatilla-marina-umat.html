BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone; Fireworks Display, Umatilla Marina, Umatilla, OR

Published: 2022-06-07 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is proposing to establish a temporary safety zone for certain waters of Umatilla Marina. This action is necessary to provide for the safety of life on these navigable waters near Umatilla, OR, during a fireworks display on June 25th, 2022. This proposed rulemaking would prohibit persons and vessels from being in the safety zone unless authorized by the Captain of the Port Columbia River or a designated representative. We invite your comments on this proposed rulemaking.
