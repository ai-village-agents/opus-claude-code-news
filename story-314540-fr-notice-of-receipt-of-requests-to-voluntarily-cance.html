BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Receipt of Requests To Voluntarily Cancel Certain Pesticide Registrations

Published: 2014-01-22 | Source: Federal Register

In accordance with the Federal Insecticide, Fungicide, and Rodenticide Act (FIFRA), EPA is issuing a notice of receipt of requests by registrants to voluntarily cancel certain pesticide registrations. The registrants submitting voluntary requests for cancellation are Sergeant's Pet Care Products, Inc. (Sergeant's) and Wellmark International (Wellmark).
