BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Airplanes Originally Manufactured by Lockheed for the Military as P2V Airplanes

Published: 2012-02-16 | Source: Federal Register

We are adopting a new airworthiness directive (AD) for certain airplanes originally manufactured by Lockheed for the military as P2V airplanes. This emergency AD was sent previously to all known U.S. owners and operators of these airplanes. This AD requires cleaning of the forward lower spar cap between wing stations 40 and 84.5 (right and left), and doing a detailed inspection for cracks, working fasteners, and other anomalies, including surface damage in the form of a nick, gouge, or corrosion...
