BREAKING: Federal Register: Supervisory Review Committee; Procedures for Appealing Material Supervisory Determinations

Published: 2017-06-07 | Source: Federal Register

The NCUA Board (Board) proposes to amend its procedures for appealing material supervisory determinations to the NCUA Supervisory Review Committee (SRC) to enhance due process and to be more consistent with the practices of the federal banking agencies. The proposed rule would expand the number of supervisory determinations appealable to the SRC and provide credit unions with the opportunity for additional review by the Director of the Office of Examinations and Insurance (E&I).
