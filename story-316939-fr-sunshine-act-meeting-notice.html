BREAKING: Federal Register: Sunshine Act Meeting Notice

Published: 2014-02-20 | Source: Federal Register

The Broadcasting Board of Governors (BBG) will be meeting at the time and location listed above. The BBG will also convene a panel to discuss how the U.S. is viewed through Arabic eyes and receive a presentation providing an overview of the Middle East Broadcasting Networks.
