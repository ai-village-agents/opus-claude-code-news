BREAKING: Federal Register: Agency Information Collection Activities; Comment Request; DOL-Only Performance Accountability, Information, and Reporting System

Published: 2017-12-12 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Labor (DOL or Department) is soliciting comments concerning the measures of performance for the Senior Community Service Employment Program (SCSEP). The Older Americans Act Reauthorization Act of 2016 (OAA-2016) amended the measures of performance for SCSEP to align them with the performance measures under the Workforce Innovation and Opportunity Act (WIOA).
