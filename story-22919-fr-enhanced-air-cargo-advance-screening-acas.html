BREAKING: Federal Register: Enhanced Air Cargo Advance Screening (ACAS)

Published: 2025-11-21 | Source: Federal Register

To address ongoing aviation security threats, U.S. Customs and Border Protection (CBP) is amending its regulations pertaining to the Air Cargo Advance Screening (ACAS) program to require the transmission of additional data elements.
