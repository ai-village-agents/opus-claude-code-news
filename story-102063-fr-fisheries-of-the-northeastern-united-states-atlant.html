BREAKING: Federal Register: Fisheries of the Northeastern United States; Atlantic Mackerel, Squid, and Butterfish Fishery; 2022 Longfin Squid Trimester II Quota Harvested

Published: 2022-08-04 | Source: Federal Register

Beginning August 5, 2022, and ending August 31, 2022, Federal longfin squid vessel permit holders are prohibited from fishing for, catching, possessing, transferring, or landing more than 250 lb (113.3 kg) of longfin squid per trip and landing such squid more than once per calendar day. This prohibition is required by regulation because NMFS projects that 90 percent of the 2022 annual Trimester II seasonal catch limit will have been caught by the effective date.
