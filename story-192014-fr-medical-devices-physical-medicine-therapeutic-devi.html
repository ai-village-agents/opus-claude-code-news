BREAKING: Federal Register: Medical Devices; Physical Medicine Therapeutic Devices; Classification of the Internal Therapeutic Massager

Published: 2019-10-25 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA or we) is classifying the internal therapeutic massager into class II (special controls). The special controls that apply to the device type are identified in this order and will be part of the codified language for the internal therapeutic massager's classification.
