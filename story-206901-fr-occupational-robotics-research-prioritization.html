BREAKING: Federal Register: Occupational Robotics Research Prioritization

Published: 2018-05-14 | Source: Federal Register

The National Institute for Occupational Safety and Health of the Centers for Disease Control and Prevention has recently established the Center for Occupational Robotics Research. NIOSH is requesting information to guide the prioritization of research to be undertaken by the Center.
