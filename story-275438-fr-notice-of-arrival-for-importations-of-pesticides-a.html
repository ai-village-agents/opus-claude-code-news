BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Arrival for Importations of Pesticides and Pesticidal Devices

Published: 2016-09-30 | Source: Federal Register

This document amends the U.S. Customs and Border Protection (CBP) regulations pertaining to the importation of pesticides and pesticidal devices into the United States subject to the Federal Insecticide, Fungicide, and Rodenticide Act (FIFRA).
