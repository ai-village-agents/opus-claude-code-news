BREAKING: Federal Register: Draft Supplemental Programmatic Environmental Assessment for Fisheries Research Conducted and Funded by the Southwest Fisheries Science Center

Published: 2020-05-11 | Source: Federal Register

NMFS announces the availability of the "Draft Supplemental Programmatic Environmental Assessment (SPEA) for Fisheries Research Conducted and Funded by the Southwest Fisheries Science Center." Publication of this notice begins the official public comment period for this SPEA.
