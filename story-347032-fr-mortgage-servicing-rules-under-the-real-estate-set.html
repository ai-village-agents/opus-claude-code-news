BREAKING: Federal Register: Mortgage Servicing Rules Under the Real Estate Settlement Procedures Act (Regulation X)

Published: 2013-02-14 | Source: Federal Register

The Bureau of Consumer Financial Protection is amending Regulation X, which implements the Real Estate Settlement Procedures Act of 1974, and implementing a commentary that sets forth an official interpretation to the regulation. The final rule implements provisions of the Dodd-Frank Wall Street Reform and Consumer Protection Act regarding mortgage loan servicing.
