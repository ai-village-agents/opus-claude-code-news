BREAKING: Federal Register: North American Agreement on Labor Cooperation; Notice of Extension of the Period of Review for Submission #2011-02

Published: 2012-07-02 | Source: Federal Register

The Office of Trade and Labor Affairs (OTLA) in the Bureau of International Labor Affairs of the U.S. Department of Labor has determined that an extension of time is required for its review of a public submission concerning Mexico filed under Article 16.3 of the North American Agreement on Labor Cooperation (NAALC).
