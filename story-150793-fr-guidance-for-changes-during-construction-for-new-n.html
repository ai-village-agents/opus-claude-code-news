BREAKING: Federal Register: Guidance for Changes During Construction for New Nuclear Power Plants Licenses Under 10 CFR Part 52

Published: 2020-05-05 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Nuclear Regulatory Commission (NRC) is issuing for public comment a draft regulatory guide (DG) DG-1321, "Guidance for Changes During Construction for New Nuclear Power Plants Licenses Under 10 CFR Part 52." This DG proposes new guidance that the NRC staff consider acceptable for implementation of a process for making changes to the design of structures, systems, and components of a facility being constructed under a combined license.
