BREAKING: Federal Register: Standards and Practices for All Appropriate Inquiries

Published: 2022-12-15 | Source: Federal Register

EPA is taking final action to amend the Standards and Practices for All Appropriate Inquiries to reference a standard practice recently made available by ASTM International, a widely recognized standards developing organization.
