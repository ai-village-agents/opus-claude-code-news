BREAKING: Federal Register: TRICARE; Off-Label Uses of Devices; Partial List of Examples of Unproven Drugs, Devices, Medical Treatments, or Procedures

Published: 2012-06-27 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Defense is publishing this final rule to revise the definition of "unlabeled or off-label drug" to "off-label use of a drug or device." This provision codifies the coverage of those medically necessary indications for which there are demonstrations from medical literature, national organizations, or technology assessment bodies that the off-label use is safe and effective and in accordance with nationally accepted standards of practice in the medical community.
