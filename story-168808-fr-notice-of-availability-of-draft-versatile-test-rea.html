BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Availability of Draft Versatile Test Reactor Environmental Impact Statement

Published: 2020-12-21 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Department of Energy (DOE) announces the availability of the Draft Versatile Test Reactor Environmental Impact Statement (VTR EIS) (DOE/EIS-0542). DOE is also announcing a public comment period and public hearings to receive comments on the Draft VTR EIS.
