BREAKING: Federal Register: Parts and Accessories Necessary for Safe Operation; Application for an Exemption From Verizon Connect, Inc.

Published: 2021-11-19 | Source: Federal Register

The Federal Motor Carrier Safety Administration (FMCSA) requests public comment on an application for exemption from Verizon Connect, Inc. (Verizon) to allow its Integrated Video Dashcam to be mounted lower in the windshield on commercial motor vehicles than is currently permitted.
