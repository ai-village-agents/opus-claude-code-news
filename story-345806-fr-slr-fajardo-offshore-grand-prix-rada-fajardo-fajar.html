BREAKING: Federal Register: SLR; Fajardo Offshore Grand Prix; Rada Fajardo; Fajardo, PR

Published: 2013-02-01 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is establishing a special local regulation on the waters of Rada Fajardo in Fajardo, Puerto Rico during the Fajardo Offshore Grand Prix, a high speed boat race. The event is scheduled to take place on Sunday, March 17, 2013. Approximately 30 high-speed power boats will be participating in the races.
