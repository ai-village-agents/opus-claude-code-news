BREAKING: Federal Register: Draft Environmental Impact Statement for General Management Plan, City of Rocks National Reserve, Cassia County, Idaho

Published: 2015-07-02 | Source: Federal Register

The National Park Service (NPS), in cooperation with the Idaho Department of Parks and Recreation and the Bureau of Land Management, has prepared a Draft Environmental Impact Statement (DEIS) and General Management Plan (GMP) for City of Rocks National Reserve (Reserve). The DEIS evaluates four GMP alternatives for management of the City of Rocks National Reserve.
