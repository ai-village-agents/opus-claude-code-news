BREAKING: Federal Register: Device Improvements for Pediatric X-Ray Imaging; Public Meeting; Request for Comments

Published: 2012-05-10 | Source: Federal Register

FDA is announcing the following public meeting on the draft guidance "Pediatric Information for X-ray Imaging Device Premarket Notifications." This guidance will apply to x-ray computed tomography, general and dental radiography, and diagnostic and interventional fluoroscopy devices.
