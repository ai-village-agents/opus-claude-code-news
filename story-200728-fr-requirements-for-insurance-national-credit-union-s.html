BREAKING: Federal Register: Requirements for Insurance; National Credit Union Share Insurance Fund Equity Distributions

Published: 2018-02-23 | Source: Federal Register

The NCUA Board (Board) is adopting amendments to its share insurance requirements rule to provide stakeholders with greater transparency regarding the calculation of each eligible financial institution's pro rata share of a declared equity distribution from the National Credit Union Share Insurance Fund (NCUSIF).
