BREAKING: Federal Register: Procurement, Management, and Administration of Engineering and Design Related Services

Published: 2024-01-30 | Source: Federal Register

This proposed rule would update the regulations governing the procurement, management, and administration of engineering and design related services directly related to a highway construction project that is funded through a discretionary grant administered by FHWA. The intent of the proposed rule is to clarify how the regulations apply to recipients other than State transportation agencies (STA).
