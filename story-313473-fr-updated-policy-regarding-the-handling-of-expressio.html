BREAKING: Federal Register: Updated Policy Regarding the Handling of Expressions of Interest

Published: 2014-01-07 | Source: Federal Register

This notice is to inform the public about a policy document that the Bureau of Land Management (BLM) issued on October 28, 2013, that states that BLM will publish information on Expression of Interest (EOI) submissions that BLM receives after January 1, 2014.
