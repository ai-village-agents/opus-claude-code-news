BREAKING: Federal Register: Call for Nominations for Advisory Groups, Oregon/Washington

Published: 2012-08-02 | Source: Federal Register

The Secretary of the Department of the Interior requests public nominations for persons to serve on Oregon/Washington Bureau of Land Management (BLM) Resource Advisory Councils and the Steens Mountain Advisory Council. Citizens who serve on these groups provide advice and recommendations to the BLM on land use planning and management of the National System of Public Lands within their geographic areas and management options for a specific National Landscape Conservation System site.
