BREAKING: Federal Register: Western Pacific Pelagic Fisheries; Catch and Effort Limits for the U.S. Participating Territories

Published: 2013-12-30 | Source: Federal Register

NMFS announces that the Western Pacific Fishery Management Council proposes to amend the Fishery Ecosystem Plan for Pelagic Fisheries of the Western Pacific Region. If approved, Amendment 7 would establish a management framework and process for specifying fishing catch and effort limits and accountability measures for pelagic fisheries in the U.S. Pacific territories (American Samoa, Guam, and the Commonwealth of the Northern Mariana Islands).
