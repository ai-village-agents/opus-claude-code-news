BREAKING: Federal Register: Urban Area Criteria for the 2020 Census-Final Criteria

Published: 2022-03-24 | Source: Federal Register

This notice provides the Census Bureau's final criteria for defining urban areas based on the results of the 2020 Decennial Census. This notice also provides a summary of comments received in response to the proposed criteria published in the Federal Register on February 19, 2021, as well as the Census Bureau's responses to those comments.
