BREAKING: Federal Register: Public Notice of AIP Property Release Idaho Falls Regional Airport, Idaho Falls, Idaho

Published: 2025-01-29 | Source: Federal Register

Notice is being given that the FAA is considering a request from the City of Idaho Falls, Idaho to waive the AIP property requirements and dispose of approximately 0.617 acres of airport property located at Idaho Falls Regional Airport, in Idaho Falls, Idaho.
