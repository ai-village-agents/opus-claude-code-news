BREAKING: Federal Register: Certificates of Alternative Compliance for the EAGLE II and Q-OCEAN SERVICE

Published: 2019-08-20 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard announces that the Chief of Prevention Division, Seventh District has issued a certificate of alternative compliance from the International Regulations for Preventing Collisions at Sea, 1972 (72 COLREGS), for the EAGLE II (O.N. 1294433) and Q-OCEAN SERVICE (O.N. 1284182). We are issuing this notice because its publication is required by statute.
