BREAKING: Federal Register: Changes or Corrections to Mail Classification Schedule

Published: 2014-11-24 | Source: Federal Register

The Commission is proposing rules addressing changes and corrections to the Mail Classification Schedule (MCS). The proposed rules establish separate procedures for material changes in services offered in connection with products and corrections to product descriptions.
