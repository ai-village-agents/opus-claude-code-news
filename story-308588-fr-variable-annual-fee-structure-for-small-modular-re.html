BREAKING: Federal Register: Variable Annual Fee Structure for Small Modular Reactors

Published: 2015-11-04 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Nuclear Regulatory Commission (NRC) is proposing to amend its licensing, inspection, and annual fee regulations to establish a variable annual fee structure for light-water small modular reactors (SMR). Under the proposed variable annual fee structure, an SMR's annual fee would be calculated as a function of its licensed thermal power rating.
