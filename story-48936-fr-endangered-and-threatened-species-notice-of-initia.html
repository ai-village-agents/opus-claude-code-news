BREAKING: Federal Register: Endangered and Threatened Species; Notice of Initiation of a 5-Year Review for the Chinese River Dolphin

Published: 2024-10-03 | Source: Federal Register

The National Marine Fisheries Service (NMFS) announces the initiation of a 5-year review for the Chinese river dolphin, also called the Baiji or the Yangtze River dolphin, (Lipotes vexillifer). NMFS is required by the Endangered Species Act (ESA) to conduct 5-year reviews to ensure that the listing classifications of species are accurate.
