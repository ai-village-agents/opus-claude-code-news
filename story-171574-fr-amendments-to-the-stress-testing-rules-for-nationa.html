BREAKING: Federal Register: Amendments to the Stress Testing Rules for National Banks and Federal Savings Associations

Published: 2019-02-12 | Source: Federal Register

The OCC is requesting comment on a proposed rule that would amend the OCC's company-run stress testing requirements for national banks and Federal savings associations, consistent with section 401 of the Economic Growth, Regulatory Relief, and Consumer Protection Act (EGRRCPA).
