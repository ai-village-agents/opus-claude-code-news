BREAKING: Federal Register: Application for Recertification of Prince William Sound Regional Citizens' Advisory Council

Published: 2026-02-02 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard announces the availability of, and seeks comments on, the recertification of the Prince William Sound Regional Citizen's Advisory Council (PWSRCAC) for a period of March 1, 2026 through February 28, 2027. Under the Oil Pollution Act of 1990 (OPA 90), the Coast Guard may recertify the PWSRCAC on an annual basis.
