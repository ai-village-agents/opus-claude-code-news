BREAKING: Federal Register: Walnuts Grown in California; Proposed Amendment to Marketing Order

Published: 2016-09-16 | Source: Federal Register

This proposed rule invites public comments on a proposed amendment to Marketing Order No. 984, which regulates the handling of walnuts grown in California. The California Walnut Board (Board), which is responsible for the local administration of the order and is comprised of walnut producers and handlers operating within the production area, recommended an amendment that would authorize the Board to borrow from a commercial lending institution to fund operations and marketing/research expenses. ...
