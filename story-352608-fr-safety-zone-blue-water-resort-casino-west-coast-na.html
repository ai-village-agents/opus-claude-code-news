BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone; Blue Water Resort & Casino West Coast Nationals; Parker, AZ

Published: 2013-04-18 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is establishing a temporary safety zone within the Lake Moovalya region of the navigable waters of the Colorado River in Parker, Arizona for the Blue Water Resort & Casino West Coast Nationals. This temporary safety zone is necessary to provide for the safety of the participants, crew, spectators, and participating vessels.
