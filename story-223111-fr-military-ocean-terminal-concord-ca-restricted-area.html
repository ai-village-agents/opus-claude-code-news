BREAKING: Federal Register: Military Ocean Terminal Concord, CA; Restricted Area

Published: 2018-12-13 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Army Military Surface Deployment and Distribution Command (SDDC) requested that the U.S. Army Corps of Engineers (Corps) change the name of a restricted area from "Suisun Bay at Naval Weapons Station, Concord; restricted area" to "Military Ocean Terminal Concord (MOTCO); restricted area." The restricted area is located in Suisun Bay, north of the City of Concord, California.
