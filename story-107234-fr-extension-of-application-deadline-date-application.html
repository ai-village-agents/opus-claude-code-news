BREAKING: Federal Register: Extension of Application Deadline Date; Applications for New Awards; Postsecondary Student Success Program

Published: 2022-10-11 | Source: Federal Register

On August 12, 2022, the Department of Education (Department) published in the Federal Register a notice inviting applications (NIA) for new awards for fiscal year (FY) 2022 for the Postsecondary Student Success Program, Assistance Listing Number 84.116M. The NIA established a deadline date of October 11, 2022, for the transmittal of applications.
