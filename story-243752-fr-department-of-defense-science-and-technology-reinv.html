BREAKING: Federal Register: Department of Defense Science and Technology Reinvention Laboratory (STRL) Personnel Management Demonstration (Demo) Project Program

Published: 2017-09-15 | Source: Federal Register

STRLs may implement a new workforce shaping pilot program which provides the STRL lab directors the authority to dynamically shape the mix of technical skills and expertise in the workforces of such laboratories to achieve one or more of the objectives in section 1109(a) of the NDAA for FY2016.
