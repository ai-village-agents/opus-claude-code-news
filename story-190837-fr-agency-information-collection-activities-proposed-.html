BREAKING: Federal Register: Agency Information Collection Activities: Proposed Collection; Comment Request

Published: 2019-10-10 | Source: Federal Register

NARA proposes to renew an information collection under the Paperwork Reduction Act. The information collection includes NA Form 16016, Limited Facility Report, which we use to review the facility, environment, and staffing capabilities of non-NARA organizations that wish to borrow a National Archives traveling exhibit. We invite you to comment on this information collection.
