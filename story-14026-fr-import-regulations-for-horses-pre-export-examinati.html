BREAKING: Federal Register: Import Regulations for Horses; Pre-Export Examination

Published: 2025-06-20 | Source: Federal Register

We are proposing to remove the requirement that horses offered for importation to the United States be accompanied by documentation of pre-export examination occurring within 48 hours of departure from the port of embarkation endorsed by a salaried veterinary medical officer.
