BREAKING: Federal Register: Short-Term Investment Funds

Published: 2012-10-09 | Source: Federal Register

This final rule revises the requirements imposed on national banks pursuant to the OCC's short-term investment fund (STIF) rule (STIF Rule). Regulations governing Federal savings associations (FSAs) require compliance with the national bank STIF Rule.
