BREAKING: Federal Register: Solicitation of Nominations for Appointment to the MyVA Advisory Committee

Published: 2017-05-30 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Veterans Affairs (VA), Office of Enterprise Integration--MyVA Task Force, is seeking nominations of qualified candidates to be considered for appointment as a member of the MyVA Advisory Committee (Committee).
