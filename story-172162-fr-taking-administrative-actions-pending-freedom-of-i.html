BREAKING: Federal Register: Taking Administrative Actions Pending Freedom of Information Act (FOIA) Processing

Published: 2019-02-19 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Nutrition Service (FNS) seeks to prevent firms authorized to participate in the Supplemental Nutrition Assistance Program (SNAP or the Program) from delaying administrative actions, such as disqualification or civil money penalties, through submission of Freedom of Information Act (FOIA) requests or appeals.
