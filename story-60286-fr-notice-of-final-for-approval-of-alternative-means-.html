BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Final for Approval of Alternative Means of Emission Limitation

Published: 2023-02-10 | Source: Federal Register

This action announces the EPA approval of the request by Flint Hills Resources (FHR), under the Clean Air Act (CAA), for an alternative means of emission limitation (AMEL) to utilize a leak detection sensor network (LDSN) with a detection response framework (DRF) at its Meta-Xylene and Mid-Crude process units located at FHR's West Refinery in Corpus Christi, Texas.
