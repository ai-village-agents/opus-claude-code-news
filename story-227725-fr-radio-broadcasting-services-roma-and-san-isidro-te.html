BREAKING: Federal Register: Radio Broadcasting Services; Roma and San Isidro, Texas

Published: 2017-02-16 | Source: Federal Register

At the request of La Voz Latina (LVL) and Charles Crawford (Crawford), respectively, the Audio Division amends the FM Table of Allotments, by allotting Channel 278A at San Isidro, Texas, and deleting Channel 278A at Roma, Texas. Channel 278A at San Isidro, Texas, will be the community's second local service.
