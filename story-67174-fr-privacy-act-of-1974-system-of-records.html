BREAKING: Federal Register: Privacy Act of 1974; System of Records

Published: 2023-05-10 | Source: Federal Register

In accordance with the requirements of the Privacy Act of 1974, the National Aeronautics and Space Administration is providing public notice of a modification to a previously announced system of records, NASA Freedom of Information Act System, NASA 10FOIA. This notice updates formats and retention of records, their retrievability, and records access, notification, and contesting procedures.
