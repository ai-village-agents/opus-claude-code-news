BREAKING: Federal Register: Announcement of Requirements and Registration for “Propose New Ideas For Prescription Drugs Oral Overdose Protection”

Published: 2013-05-16 | Source: Federal Register

Prescription drug abuse is a growing drug problem for America. The "Propose New Ideas For Prescription Drugs Oral Overdose Protection" is a Challenge to find new and creative ways that diminish or eliminate overconsumption of intact opioid pills. This notice provides information about the requirements and registration for the Challenge.
