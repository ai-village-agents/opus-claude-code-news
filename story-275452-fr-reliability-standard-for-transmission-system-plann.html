BREAKING: Federal Register: Reliability Standard for Transmission System Planned Performance for Geomagnetic Disturbance Events

Published: 2016-09-30 | Source: Federal Register

The Federal Energy Regulatory Commission (Commission) approves Reliability Standard TPL-007-1 (Transmission System Planned Performance for Geomagnetic Disturbance Events). The North American Electric Reliability Corporation (NERC), the Commission-certified Electric Reliability Organization, submitted Reliability Standard TPL-007-1 for Commission approval in response to a Commission directive in Order No. 779.
