BREAKING: Federal Register: Prevailing Rate Systems; Special Wage Schedules for Nonappropriated Fund Automotive Mechanics

Published: 2012-06-12 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Office of Personnel Management is issuing a proposed rule to establish special wage schedules for the Department of Defense's (DOD's) nonappropriated fund (NAF) automotive mechanics. These special wage schedules would replace the current commission pay practice covering DOD's NAF automotive mechanics with a flat rate pay system.
