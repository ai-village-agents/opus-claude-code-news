BREAKING: Federal Register: Amending the Administrative Procedures With Respect to the Import and Export of Natural Gas

Published: 2025-05-16 | Source: Federal Register

This DFR amends the regulations for the administrative procedures with respect to the import and export of natural gas to update and streamline the general requirements for filing documents with the Office of Fossil Energy (FE).
