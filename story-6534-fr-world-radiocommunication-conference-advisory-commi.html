BREAKING: Federal Register: World Radiocommunication Conference Advisory Committee Schedules Its Third Meeting on April 15, 2025

Published: 2025-03-06 | Source: Federal Register

This notice advises interested persons that the third meeting of the World Radiocommunication Conference Advisory Committee (WRC Advisory Committee) will be held on April 15, 2025 at the Federal Communications Commission (FCC). This third meeting of the WRC Advisory Committee will consider status reports and recommendations from its Informal Working Groups (IWG) concerning preparation for the 2027 World Radiocommunication Conference (WRC-27).
