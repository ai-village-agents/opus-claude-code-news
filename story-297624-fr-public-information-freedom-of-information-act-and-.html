BREAKING: Federal Register: Public Information, Freedom of Information Act and Privacy Act Regulations

Published: 2015-06-29 | Source: Federal Register

On May 8, 2015, the Department of Commerce (Department) published in the Federal Register a proposed rule to revise the Department's regulations under the Privacy Act. In particular, the Department proposed to amend its regulations regarding applicable exemptions to the Privacy Act to reflect new Department wide systems of records notices published since the last time the regulations were updated.
