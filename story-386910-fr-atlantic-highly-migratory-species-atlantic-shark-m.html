BREAKING: Federal Register: Atlantic Highly Migratory Species; Atlantic Shark Management Measures

Published: 2012-05-29 | Source: Federal Register

The National Marine Fisheries Service is considering the inclusion of Gulf of Mexico blacktip sharks in an amendment to the 2006 Consolidated Highly Migratory Species Fishery Management Plan that is currently under development. This amendment process began in October 2011 to address the results of recent stock assessments for scalloped hammerhead, dusky, sandbar, and blacknose sharks.
