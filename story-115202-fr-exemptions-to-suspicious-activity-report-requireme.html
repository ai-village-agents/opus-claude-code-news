BREAKING: Federal Register: Exemptions to Suspicious Activity Report Requirements

Published: 2021-01-22 | Source: Federal Register

The OCC is inviting comment on a proposed rule that would modify the requirements for national banks and federal savings associations to file Suspicious Activity Reports. The proposed rule would amend the OCC's Suspicious Activity Report regulations to allow the OCC to issue exemptions from the requirements of those regulations.
