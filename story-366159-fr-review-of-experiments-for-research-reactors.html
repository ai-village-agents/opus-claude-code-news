BREAKING: Federal Register: Review of Experiments for Research Reactors

Published: 2013-09-24 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Nuclear Regulatory Commission (NRC) is withdrawing Regulatory Guide (RG) 2.4, "Review of Experiments for Research Reactors." The guide is being withdrawn because the industry standard which it referred to has been withdrawn, and corresponding information is available in other NRC guidance.
