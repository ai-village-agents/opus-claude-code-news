BREAKING: Federal Register: NASA Protective Services Enforcement

Published: 2014-09-15 | Source: Federal Register

NASA is amending its regulations by adding a subpart for traffic enforcement regulations, authorities, and procedures at all NASA Centers and component facilities. Changes are being made to align this part with NASA objectives in the protection of its people and property.
