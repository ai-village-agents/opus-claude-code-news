BREAKING: Federal Register: Energy Conservation Program: Energy Conservation Standards for Manufactured Housing; Enforcement

Published: 2023-12-26 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Department of Energy (DOE) is proposing to establish enforcement procedures for its energy conservation standards for manufactured housing. DOE recently amended the compliance date for these standards in a final rule to delay compliance.
