BREAKING: Federal Register: Privacy Act; Implementation

Published: 2015-12-21 | Source: Federal Register

The Office of the Secretary of Defense (OSD) is amending its regulations to exempt portions of a system of records from certain provisions of the Privacy Act. Specifically, the Department proposes to exempt portions of DMDC 16 DoD, entitled "Identity Management Engine for Security and Analysis (IMESA)" from one or more provisions of the Privacy Act because of criminal, civil, and administrative enforcement requirements.
