BREAKING: Federal Register: Extension of Waiver to Panasonic Appliances Refrigeration Systems Corporation of America (PAPRSA) From the Department of Energy Consumer Refrigerator and Refrigerator-Freezer Test Procedures

Published: 2017-08-04 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Department of Energy ("DOE") is granting a waiver extension (Case No. RF-047) to Panasonic Appliances Refrigeration Systems Corporation of America ("PAPRSA") to waive the requirements of the DOE refrigerator and refrigerator-freezer test procedures for determining the energy consumption of a specific combination cooler- refrigerator basic model, PR5181WBC.
