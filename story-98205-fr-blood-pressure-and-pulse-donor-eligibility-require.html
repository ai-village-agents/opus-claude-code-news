BREAKING: Federal Register: Blood Pressure and Pulse Donor Eligibility Requirements: Compliance Policy; Draft Guidance for Industry; Correction

Published: 2022-06-13 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA or Agency) is correcting a notice that appeared in the Federal Register of Tuesday, May 24, 2022. The document announced the availability of a draft guidance entitled "Blood Pressure and Pulse Donor Eligibility Requirements: Compliance Policy; Draft Guidance for Industry." The draft guidance document was published with incorrect information of a comment period due date. This document corrects that error.
