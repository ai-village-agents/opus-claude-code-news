BREAKING: Federal Register: Designation of Product Categories for Federal Procurement

Published: 2019-07-05 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Department of Agriculture (USDA) is amending the Guidelines for Designating Biobased Products for Federal Procurement (Guidelines) to add 30 sections that will designate the product categories within which biobased products would be afforded procurement preference by Federal agencies and their contractors.
