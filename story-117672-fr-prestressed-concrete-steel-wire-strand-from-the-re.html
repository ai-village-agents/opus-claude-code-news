BREAKING: Federal Register: Prestressed Concrete Steel Wire Strand From the Republic of Turkey: Notice of Correction to the Final Affirmative Determination of Sales at Less Than Fair Value

Published: 2021-02-26 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Department of Commerce (Commerce) is issuing a correction to a previously published Federal Register notice pertaining to the final determination of sales at less than fair value (LTFV) of prestressed concrete steel wire strand (PC strand) from the Republic of Turkey (Turkey).
