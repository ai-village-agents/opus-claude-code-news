BREAKING: Federal Register: Water Quality Standards for the State of Florida's Lakes and Flowing Waters; Proposed Rule; Stay

Published: 2012-12-14 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) proposes to temporarily stay our regulation the "Water Quality Standards for the State of Florida's Lakes and Flowing Waters; Final Rule" (inland waters rule) to November 15, 2013. EPA's inland waters rule currently includes an effective date of January 6, 2013, for the entire regulation except for the site-specific alternative criteria provision, which took effect on February 4, 2011.
