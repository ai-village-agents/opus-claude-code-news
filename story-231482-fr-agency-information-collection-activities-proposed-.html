BREAKING: Federal Register: Agency Information Collection Activities: Proposed Collection; Comment Request

Published: 2017-04-07 | Source: Federal Register

NARA proposes to request an extension from the Office of Management and Budget (OMB) of approval to use the following three information collections. We use the first information collection form to advise requesters of (1) the procedures they should follow to request certified copies of records for use in civil litigation or criminal actions in courts of law, and (2) the information they need to provide us so that we can identify the correct records.
