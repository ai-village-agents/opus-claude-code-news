BREAKING: Federal Register: Approval Criteria for Rates Charged for Community Residential Care

Published: 2019-07-15 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Affairs (VA) adopts as final, with changes, a proposed rule amending its regulation governing standards applicable to a community residential care facility (CRC) approved by VA. This rule also addresses the amount that a veteran may be charged for residence in a CRC and how VA determines whether that rate is appropriate.
