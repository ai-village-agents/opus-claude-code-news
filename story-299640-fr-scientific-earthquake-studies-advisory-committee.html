BREAKING: Federal Register: Scientific Earthquake Studies Advisory Committee

Published: 2015-07-23 | Source: Federal Register

Pursuant to Public Law 106-503, the Scientific Earthquake Studies Advisory Committee (SESAC) will hold its next meeting on September 1-2, 2015, in the Ernst & Young Gallery of the Fincher Building, Cox School of Business, at the Southern Methodist University in Dallas, Texas. The Committee is comprised of members from academia, industry, and State government.
