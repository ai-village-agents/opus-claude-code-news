BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Intent To Repatriate Cultural Items Amendment: California State University, Sacramento, Sacramento, CA

Published: 2024-03-08 | Source: Federal Register

In accordance with the Native American Graves Protection and Repatriation Act (NAGPRA), the California State University, Sacramento has amended a Notice of Intent to Repatriate Cultural Items published in the Federal Register on March 2, 2023. This notice amends the cultural affiliation in a collection removed from Amador County, CA.
