BREAKING: Federal Register: Modification of Significant New Uses of Certain Chemical Substances

Published: 2015-06-30 | Source: Federal Register

EPA is amending the significant new use rules (SNURs) under section 5(a)(2) of the Toxic Substances Control Act (TSCA) for 21 chemical substances which were the subject of premanufacture notices (PMNs). This action amends the SNURs to allow certain uses without requiring a significant new use notice (SNUN), and extends SNUN requirements to certain additional uses.
