BREAKING: Federal Register: Extension of Solicitation of Nominations for Membership in the Renewable Energy and Energy Efficiency Advisory Committee

Published: 2022-08-12 | Source: Federal Register

As announced in the June 15, 2022 Federal Register, the Department of Commerce renewed the Renewable Energy and Energy Efficiency Advisory Committee (the Committee) for the 2022-2024 term and requested nominations for membership. This notice extends the solicitation period for membership on the Committee in order to widen the pool of qualified candidates and to ensure that diverse points of view are represented.
