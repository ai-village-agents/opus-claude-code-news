BREAKING: Federal Register: Agency Information Collection Activities; Proposed Renewal of an Existing Collection (EPA ICR No. 2330.04 and OMB Control No. 2070-0179); Comment Request

The ICR, entitled: "Pesticide Registration Fees Program" and identified by EPA ICR No. 2330.04 and OMB Control No. 2070-0179, represents the renewal of an existing ICR that is scheduled to expire on September 30, 2020.
