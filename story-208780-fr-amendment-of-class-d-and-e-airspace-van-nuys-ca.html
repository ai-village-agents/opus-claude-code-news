BREAKING: Federal Register: Amendment of Class D and E Airspace; Van Nuys, CA

Published: 2018-06-06 | Source: Federal Register

This action removes the Notice to Airmen (NOTAM) part-time status from the legal description of the Class E airspace area designated as an extension at Van Nuys Airport, Van Nuys, CA, and adds NOTAM part-time status information to Class E surface area airspace. These actions bring the airspace descriptions in line with the airspace hours listed in the applicable Chart Supplement.
