BREAKING: Federal Register: Random Drug Testing Rate for Covered Crewmembers for 2016

Published: 2016-01-12 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard has set the calendar year 2016 minimum random drug testing rate at 25 percent of covered crewmembers.
