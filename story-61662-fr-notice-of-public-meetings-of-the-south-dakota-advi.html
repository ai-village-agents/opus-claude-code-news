BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Public Meetings of the South Dakota Advisory Committee to the U.S. Commission on Civil Rights; Revision to Time and Agenda Item

Published: 2023-03-02 | Source: Federal Register

The Commission on Civil Rights published a notice in the Federal Register of Tuesday, January 10, 2023, concerning meetings of the South Dakota Advisory Committee. The notice is in FR Doc. 2023- 00230, in the first and second columns of page 1348. The document contains a meeting time that is now revised. Also, in the second column, Agenda item three should be revised to read: Discussion: Report on Voting Rights and Voter Access.
