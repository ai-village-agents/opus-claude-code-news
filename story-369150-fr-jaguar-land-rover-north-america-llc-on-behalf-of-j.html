BREAKING: Federal Register: Jaguar Land Rover North America, LLC, on behalf of Jaguar Cars Limited, Grant of Petition for Decision of Inconsequential Noncompliance

Published: 2013-11-04 | Source: Federal Register

Jaguar Land Rover North America, LLC, on behalf of Jaguar Cars Limited (collectively referred to as "Jaguar") has determined that model year 2010 and certain 2011 Jaguar XJ passenger cars manufactured between September 11, 2009 and March 28, 2011, do not fully comply with paragraphs S5.2.1 and S5.5.2 of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard (FMVSS) No. 101, Controls and displays, regarding brake system-related telltales.
