BREAKING: Federal Register: Regulatory Reform Initiative: Small Disadvantaged Businesses

Published: 2020-05-08 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Small Business Administration (SBA) is removing from the Code of Federal Regulations (CFR) 16 regulations that are no longer necessary because they are either redundant or obsolete. This action will assist the public by simplifying SBA's regulations.
