BREAKING: Federal Register: Fees for Instantaneously Declined Transactions; Withdrawal of Proposed Rule

Published: 2025-01-14 | Source: Federal Register

The Consumer Financial Protection Bureau (CFPB) is withdrawing its proposed rule to prohibit banks and other financial institutions from charging certain nonsufficient funds (NSF) fees, such as those for declined debit card purchases, Automated Teller Machine (ATM) withdrawals, and some person-to-person payments.
