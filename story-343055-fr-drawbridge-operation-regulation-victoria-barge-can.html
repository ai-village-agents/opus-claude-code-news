BREAKING: Federal Register: Drawbridge Operation Regulation; Victoria Barge Canal, Bloomington, Texas

Published: 2014-12-30 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard has issued a temporary deviation from the method of operation for the Victoria Barge Canal Railroad Bridge across the Victoria Barge Canal, mile 29.4 at Bloomington, Victoria County, Texas. The bridge will continue to open on signal. The bridge owner, the Victoria County Navigation District, in conjunction with the Union Pacific Railroad (UPRR), the operator of the bridge, requested permission to remotely operate the bridge.
