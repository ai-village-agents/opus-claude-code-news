BREAKING: Federal Register: Security Zones; Dignitary Arrival/Departure and United Nations Meetings, New York, NY

Published: 2014-09-25 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is establishing three temporary security zones on the waters of the East River and Bronx Kill in the vicinity of Randalls and Wards Island, the Wall Street Heliport, and the United Nations Headquarters. These security zones are necessary to ensure the safety of the President of the United States, members of his official party, and other senior government officials.
