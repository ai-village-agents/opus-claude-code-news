BREAKING: Federal Register: Certain Off-the-Road Tires From China; Termination of Five-Year Reviews

Published: 2019-06-21 | Source: Federal Register

The Commission instituted the subject five-year reviews in January 2019 to determine whether revocation of the antidumping and countervailing duty orders on certain off-the-road tires from China would be likely to lead to continuation or recurrence of material injury.
