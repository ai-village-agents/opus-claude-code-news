BREAKING: Federal Register: Lifesaving Devices-Uninspected Commercial Barges and Sailing Vessels

Published: 2014-11-17 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard published a final rule in the Federal Register on September 10, 2014, requiring the use of wearable personal flotation devices for individuals on board uninspected non-passenger commercial barges and sailing vessels. In the final rule, the Coast Guard amended several tables to reflect this requirement.
