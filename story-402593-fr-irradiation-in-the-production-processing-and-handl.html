BREAKING: Federal Register: Irradiation in the Production, Processing and Handling of Food

Published: 2012-11-30 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA) is amending the food additive regulations to increase the maximum dose of ionizing radiation permitted in the treatment of poultry products, to include specific language intended to clarify the poultry products covered by the regulations, and to remove the limitation that any packaging used during irradiation of poultry shall not exclude oxygen.
