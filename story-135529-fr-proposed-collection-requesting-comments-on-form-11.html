BREAKING: Federal Register: Proposed Collection; Requesting Comments on Form 1127

Published: 2021-10-14 | Source: Federal Register

The Internal Revenue Service, as part of its continuing effort to reduce paperwork and respondent burden, invites the general public and other federal agencies to take this opportunity to comment on proposed and/or continuing information collections, as required by the Paperwork Reduction Act of 1995. The IRS is soliciting comments concerning Form 1127, Application for Extension of Time for Payment of Tax Due to Undue Hardship.
