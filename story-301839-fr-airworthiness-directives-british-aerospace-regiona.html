BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; British Aerospace Regional Aircraft Airplanes

Published: 2015-08-17 | Source: Federal Register

We are adopting a new airworthiness directive (AD) for British Aerospace Regional Aircraft Model Jetstream Model 3201 airplanes. This AD results from mandatory continuing airworthiness information (MCAI) issued by an aviation authority of another country to identify and correct an unsafe condition on an aviation product.
