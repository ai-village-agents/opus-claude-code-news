BREAKING: Federal Register: Homeland Security Acquisition Regulation (HSAR); Revision Initiative [HSAR Case 2009-002]; Correction

Published: 2012-09-06 | Source: Federal Register

DHS is correcting a final rule that appeared in the Federal Register of August 22, 2012. As published, the final rule incorrectly uses the word (DATE) in several places in part 3052 of title 48 of the Code of Federal Regulations. We are correcting each instance of (DATE) to correctly state the appropriate date of "(SEP 2012)".
