BREAKING: Federal Register: Payday-Alternative Loans

Published: 2012-09-27 | Source: Federal Register

The NCUA Board (Board) is currently reviewing its regulation governing payday-alternative loans (PAL or PAL loans), formerly known as short-term, small amount loans. The Board intends to improve the regulation to encourage more federal credit unions (FCUs) to offer PAL loans and believes it may be necessary to amend the regulation.
