BREAKING: Federal Register: Amendment of Class E Airspace; Douglas, WY

Published: 2015-09-24 | Source: Federal Register

This action modifies Class E airspace at Converse County Airport, Douglas, WY, to accommodate new Standard Instrument Approach Procedures (SIAPs) at Converse County Airport, and addresses an inaccuracy identified by FAA Airspace Policy and Support that V-19, which is no longer located in the area, is used in the legal description of the airspace.
