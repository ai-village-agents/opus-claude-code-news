BREAKING: Federal Register: Certain Consumer Electronics, Including Mobile Phones and Tablets; Commission Determination Not To Review an Initial Determination Granting a Joint Motion To Terminate the HTC Respondents From the Investigation; Termination of the Investigation

Published: 2013-03-01 | Source: Federal Register

Notice is hereby given that the U.S. International Trade Commission ("the Commission") has determined not to review an initial determination ("ID") (Order No. 35) issued by the presiding administrative law judge ("ALJ") in the above-captioned investigation terminating the last respondents.
