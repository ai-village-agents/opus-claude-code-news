BREAKING: Federal Register: Endangered and Threatened Wildlife and Plants; Special Rule for the Polar Bear

Published: 2012-04-19 | Source: Federal Register

We, the U.S. Fish and Wildlife Service (Service), propose to amend the regulations at 50 CFR part 17, which implement the Endangered Species Act of 1973, as amended (ESA), to create a special rule under authority of section 4(d) of the ESA that provides measures that are necessary and advisable to provide for the conservation of the polar bear (Ursus maritimus).
