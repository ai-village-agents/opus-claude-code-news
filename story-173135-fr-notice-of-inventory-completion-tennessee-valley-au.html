BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Inventory Completion: Tennessee Valley Authority, Knoxville, TN; Correction

Published: 2019-03-01 | Source: Federal Register

The Tennessee Valley Authority (TVA) has corrected an inventory of human remains and associated funerary objects published in a Notice of Inventory Completion in the Federal Register on August 22, 2017. This notice corrects the minimum number of individuals. Lineal descendants or representatives of any Indian Tribe or Native Hawaiian organization not identified in this notice that wish to request transfer of control of these human remains should submit a written request to the TVA.
