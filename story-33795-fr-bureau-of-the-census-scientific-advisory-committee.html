BREAKING: Federal Register: Bureau of the Census Scientific Advisory Committee

Published: 2024-04-08 | Source: Federal Register

The Census Bureau is publishing this notice to announce the renewal of the Bureau of the Census Scientific Advisory Committee (CSAC or Committee). The purpose of the Committee is to provide advice to the Director of the Census Bureau on the full range of Census Bureau programs and activities including communications, decennial, demographic, economic, field operations, geography, information technology, and statistics.
