BREAKING: Federal Register: Lease and Interchange of Vehicles; Motor Carriers of Passengers

Published: 2016-09-27 | Source: Federal Register

FMCSA announces it will hold a roundtable discussion on October 31, 2016, as a follow-up to its August 31, 2016, notice of intent concerning the petitions for reconsideration of the final rule, titled "Lease and Interchange of Vehicles; Motor Carriers of Passengers," which published May 27, 2015. The meeting will be open to the public. Individuals with diverse experience, expertise, and perspectives are encouraged to attend.
