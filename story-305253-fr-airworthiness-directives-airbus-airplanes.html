BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Airbus Airplanes

Published: 2015-09-28 | Source: Federal Register

We propose to adopt a new airworthiness directive (AD) for all Airbus Model A318, A319, A320, and A321 series airplanes. This proposed AD was prompted by an evaluation by the design approval holder (DAH) indicating that the fuselage skin repairs are subject to widespread fatigue damage (WFD).
