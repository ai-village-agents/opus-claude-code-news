BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone; Fireworks Displays, Little Egg Harbor, Beach Haven, NJ

Published: 2019-05-15 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is establishing a temporary safety zone on a portion of Little Egg Harbor in Beach Haven, NJ. This action is necessary to protect the surrounding public and vessels on these navigable waters during a series of fireworks displays on the following dates: May 18, May 25, June 1, June 7, July 20, July 27, August 10, September 1, October 5, and October 12, 2019.
