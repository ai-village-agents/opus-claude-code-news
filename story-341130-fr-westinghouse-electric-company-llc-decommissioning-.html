BREAKING: Federal Register: Westinghouse Electric Company, LLC; Decommissioning Project, Hematite, Missouri

Published: 2014-12-03 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Nuclear Regulatory Commission (NRC) has received an application from Westinghouse Electric Company, LLC dated July 11, 2014, and subsequently modified by submittal dated September 25, 2014, for amendment of the Hematite Fuel Cycle Facility license (License No. SNM-33), which authorizes decommissioning of the facility.
