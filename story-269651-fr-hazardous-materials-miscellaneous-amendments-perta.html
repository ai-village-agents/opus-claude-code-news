BREAKING: Federal Register: Hazardous Materials: Miscellaneous Amendments Pertaining to DOT-Specification Cylinders (RRR)

Published: 2016-07-26 | Source: Federal Register

The Pipeline and Hazardous Materials Safety Administration (PHMSA) is proposing to amend the Hazardous Materials Regulations to revise certain requirements applicable to the manufacture, use, and requalification of DOT-specification cylinders. PHMSA is taking this action in response to petitions for rulemaking submitted by stakeholders and to agency review of the compressed gas cylinders regulations.
