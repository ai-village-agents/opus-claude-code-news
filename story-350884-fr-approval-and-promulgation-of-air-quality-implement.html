BREAKING: Federal Register: Approval and Promulgation of Air Quality Implementation Plans; Ohio; Particulate Matter Standards

Published: 2013-03-29 | Source: Federal Register

EPA is taking direct final action to convert a conditional approval of specified provisions of the Ohio state implementation plan (SIP) to a full approval. Ohio submitted a request to approve revised particulate matter (PM) rules on February 23, 2012.
