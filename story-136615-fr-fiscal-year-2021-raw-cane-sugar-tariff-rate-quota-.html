BREAKING: Federal Register: Fiscal Year 2021 Raw Cane Sugar Tariff-Rate Quota Extension of the Entry Period

Published: 2021-10-29 | Source: Federal Register

The Foreign Agricultural Service of the U.S. Department of Agriculture is providing notice of an extension of the tariff-rate quota (TRQ) entry period for the fiscal year (FY) 2021 raw cane sugar TRQ.
