BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone; Apache Pier Labor Day Fireworks; Myrtle Beach, SC

Published: 2012-08-24 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is establishing a temporary safety zone on the waters of the Atlantic Ocean in the vicinity of Apache Pier in Myrtle Beach, SC, during the Labor Day fireworks demonstration. This regulation is necessary to protect life and property on the navigable waters of the Atlantic Ocean off the coast of Myrtle Beach, SC.
