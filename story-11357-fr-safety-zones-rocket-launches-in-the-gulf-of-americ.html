BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zones; Rocket Launches in the Gulf of America and South Bay, Boca Chica Beach, TX

Published: 2025-05-15 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard proposes to establish two permanent safety zones to protect personnel, vessels, and the marine environment from potential hazards created by commercial spaceflight activities. The proposed permanent safety zones are located in navigable waters of South Bay, TX and in navigable waters of the Gulf of America.
