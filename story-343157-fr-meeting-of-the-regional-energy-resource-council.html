BREAKING: Federal Register: Meeting of the Regional Energy Resource Council

Published: 2014-12-30 | Source: Federal Register

The TVA Regional Energy Resource Council (RERC) will hold a meeting on Monday, February 2 and Tuesday, February 3, 2015, regarding regional energy related issues in the Tennessee Valley. The RERC was established to advise TVA on its energy resource activities and the priorities among competing objectives and values.
