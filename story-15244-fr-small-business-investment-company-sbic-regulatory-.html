BREAKING: Federal Register: Small Business Investment Company (SBIC) Regulatory Amendments

Published: 2025-07-07 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Small Business Administration ("SBA" or "Agency") is proposing to modify or remove from the Code of Federal Regulations ("CFR") regulations that are obsolete, inefficient, or otherwise unnecessarily impede the licensing of small business investment companies ("SBICs").
