BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice To Announce Request for Comments on the National Endowment for the Arts' Draft 2022-2026 Strategic Plan

Published: 2021-08-20 | Source: Federal Register

The National Endowment for the Arts (NEA) is in the process of developing a new strategic plan for fiscal years 2022 through 2026. The NEA Office of Research & Analysis is soliciting public comments on the agency's draft 2022-2026 Strategic Plan. We encourage you to read the draft Strategic Plan (linked below) and provide any comments you may have via email (see ADDRESSES).
