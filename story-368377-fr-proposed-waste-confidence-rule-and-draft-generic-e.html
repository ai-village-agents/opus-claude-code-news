BREAKING: Federal Register: Proposed Waste Confidence Rule and Draft Generic Environmental Impact Statement

Published: 2013-10-29 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Nuclear Regulatory Commission (NRC) has rescheduled to November 12 the Waste Confidence public meeting it initially planned to hold in Oak Brook, Illinois, on October 24, 2013. The NRC postponed this meeting as a result of lapsed appropriations.
