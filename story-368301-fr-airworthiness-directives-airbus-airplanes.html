BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Airbus Airplanes

Published: 2013-10-28 | Source: Federal Register

We are superseding airworthiness directive (AD) 2000-12-11, for certain Model A300 B4-600 and Model A300 B4-600R series airplanes. AD 2000-12-11 required repetitive inspections to detect cracks in the bolt holes inboard and outboard of rib 9 on the bottom booms of the front and rear wing spars, and repair if necessary.
