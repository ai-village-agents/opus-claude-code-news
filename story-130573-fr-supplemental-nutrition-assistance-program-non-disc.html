BREAKING: Federal Register: Supplemental Nutrition Assistance Program: Non-Discretionary Quality Control Provisions of the Agricultural Improvement Act of 2018

Published: 2021-08-13 | Source: Federal Register

The U. S. Department of Agriculture (the Department) is issuing this interim final rule to strengthen and improve the integrity and accuracy of the Food and Nutrition Service (FNS) Supplemental Nutrition Assistance Program (SNAP) Quality Control (QC) system by codifying statutory requirements enacted by the Agriculture Improvement Act of 2018 (2018 Farm Bill) that was signed into law on December 20, 2018.
