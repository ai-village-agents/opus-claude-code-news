BREAKING: Federal Register: Establishment of the Marine and Coastal Area-Based Management Advisory Committee and Solicitation of Nominations for Membership

Published: 2022-11-17 | Source: Federal Register

Pursuant to the Federal Advisory Committee Act, the Under Secretary of Commerce for Oceans and Atmosphere (Under Secretary) announces the establishment of the Marine and Coastal Area-based Management Advisory Committee (MCAM AC). The MCAM AC shall advise the Under Secretary on science-based approaches to area-based protection, conservation, restoration, and management in coastal and marine areas, including the Great Lakes.
