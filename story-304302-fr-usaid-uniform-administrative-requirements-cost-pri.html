BREAKING: Federal Register: USAID Uniform Administrative Requirements, Cost Principles, and Audit Requirements for Federal Awards

Published: 2015-09-17 | Source: Federal Register

USAID is issuing a final rule adopting with amendments the "Uniform Administrative Requirements, Cost Principles, and Audit Requirements for Federal Awards," issued by the Office of Management and Budget and published in Federal Register on December 26, 2013. Consistent with the OMB rule, USAID's rule supersedes USAID's "Administration of Assistance Awards to U.S. Non-Governmental Organizations."
