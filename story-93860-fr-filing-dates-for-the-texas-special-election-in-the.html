BREAKING: Federal Register: Filing Dates for the Texas Special Election in the 34th Congressional District

Published: 2022-04-18 | Source: Federal Register

Texas has scheduled a Special General Election on June 14, 2022, to fill its U.S. House of Representatives seat in the 34th Congressional District vacated by Representative Filemon B. Vela. There are two possible elections, but only one may be necessary. Under Texas law, all qualified candidates, regardless of party affiliation, will appear on the ballot.
