BREAKING: Federal Register: Product Cancellation Order for Certain Pesticide Registrations; Correction

Published: 2014-03-12 | Source: Federal Register

EPA issued a notice in the Federal Register of August 21, 2013, and October 30, 2013, concerning receipt of requests to voluntarily cancel certain pesticide registrations and its follow-up product cancellation order, respectively. In both notices, EPA inadvertently listed the pesticide product Paraquat Dichloride Technical (EPA Reg. No. 083558-00005). The registrant did not request voluntary cancellation for this product.
