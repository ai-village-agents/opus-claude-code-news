BREAKING: Federal Register: Endangered and Threatened Wildlife and Plants; Endangered Species Status for Amur Sturgeon

Published: 2021-08-25 | Source: Federal Register

We, the U.S. Fish and Wildlife Service (Service), announce a 12-month finding on a petition to list the Amur sturgeon (Acipenser schrenckii), a fish species from the Amur River basin in Russia and China, as an endangered species under the Endangered Species Act of 1973, as amended (Act). After a review of the best scientific and commercial information available, we find that listing the species is warranted.
