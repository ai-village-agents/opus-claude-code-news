BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Intent To Prepare an Integrated Feasibility Report and Environmental Impact Statement for the St. Tammany Parish, Louisiana Feasibility Study

Published: 2020-06-19 | Source: Federal Register

Pursuant to the National Environmental Policy Act (NEPA), the U.S. Army Corps of Engineers, New Orleans District (CEMVN) intends to prepare an Integrated Feasibility Report and Environmental Impact Statement (IFR-EIS) to evaluate potential coastal storm risk management measures and flood risk management measures in St. Tammany Parish as part of the St. Tammany Parish, Louisiana Feasibility Study.
