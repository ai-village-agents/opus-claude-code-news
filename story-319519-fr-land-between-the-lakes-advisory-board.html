BREAKING: Federal Register: Land Between the Lakes Advisory Board

Published: 2014-03-21 | Source: Federal Register

The Land Between The Lakes Advisory Board (Board) will meet in Cadiz, Kentucky. The Board is established consistent with the Federal Advisory Committee Act of 1972 (5 U.S.C. App 2). Additional information concerning the Board can be found by visiting the Board's Web site at: http://www.lbl.org/LRMPadvisoryboard.html.
