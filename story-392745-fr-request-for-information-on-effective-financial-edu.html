The Dodd-Frank Wall Street Reform and Consumer Protection Act of 2010 ("Dodd-Frank") established the Office of Financial Education ("OFE") within the Bureau of Consumer Financial Protection ("CFPB") to develop and launch initiatives that will educate consumers and help them make better informed financial decisions.
