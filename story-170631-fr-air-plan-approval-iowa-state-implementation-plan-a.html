BREAKING: Federal Register: Air Plan Approval; Iowa; State Implementation Plan and Operating Permits Program

Published: 2019-02-05 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is approving revisions to the Iowa State Implementation Plan (SIP) and the Operating Permits Program. The revisions include updating definitions, clarifying permit rule exemptions and permit-by-rule regulations, revising methods and procedures for performance test/stack test and continuous monitoring systems, and updating the Prevention of Significant Deterioration (PSD) regulations and Operating Permits Program.
