BREAKING: Federal Register: Past Performance Ratings for Small Business Joint Venture Members and Small Business First-Tier Subcontractors

Published: 2022-07-22 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Small Business Administration (SBA) is amending its regulations to implement new provisions of the National Defense Authorization Act (NDAA) Fiscal Year (FY) 2021. The final rule provides new methods for small business government contractors to obtain past performance ratings to be used with offers on prime contracts with the Federal Government.
