BREAKING: Federal Register: Federal Acquisition Regulation: Small Business Participation on Certain Multiple-Award Contracts

Published: 2025-06-12 | Source: Federal Register

OFPP, DoD, GSA, and NASA (collectively referred to as the Federal Acquisition Regulatory Council, or FAR Council) are withdrawing the proposed rule to amend the Federal Acquisition Regulation (FAR) titled: Small Business Participation on Certain Multiple-Award Contracts.

Document Type: Proposed Rule

Agency: Management and Budget Office, Federal Procurement Policy Office, Defense Department, General Services Administration, National Aeronautics and Space Administration
