BREAKING: Federal Register: Availability of E-Tag Information to Commission Staff

Published: 2012-03-02 | Source: Federal Register

On April 21, 2011, the Commission issued a Notice of Proposed Rulemaking (76 FR 23516) proposing to require the Commission-certified Electric Reliability Organization to make available to Commission staff, on an ongoing basis, access to complete electronic tagging data used to schedule the transmission of electric power in transmission markets.
