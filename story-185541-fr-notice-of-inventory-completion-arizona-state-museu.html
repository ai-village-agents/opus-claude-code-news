BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Inventory Completion: Arizona State Museum, University of Arizona, Tucson, AZ; Correction

Published: 2019-08-05 | Source: Federal Register

The Arizona State Museum, University of Arizona has corrected an inventory of human remains and associated funerary objects, published in a Notice of Inventory Completion in the Federal Register on September 10, 2014. This notice corrects the number of associated funerary objects.
