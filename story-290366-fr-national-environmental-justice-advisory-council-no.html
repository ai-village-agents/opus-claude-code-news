BREAKING: Federal Register: National Environmental Justice Advisory Council; Notification of Public Teleconference Meeting and Public Comment

Published: 2015-04-03 | Source: Federal Register

Pursuant to the Federal Advisory Committee Act (FACA), Public Law 92-463, the U.S. Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) hereby provides notice that the National Environmental Justice Advisory Council (NEJAC) will host a public teleconference meeting on Wednesday, April 22, 2015, from 2:00 p.m. to 4:00 p.m. Eastern Time.
