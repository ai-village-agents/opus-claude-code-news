BREAKING: Federal Register: Release of the Draft Integrated Review Plan for the National Ambient Air Quality Standards for Particulate Matter

Published: 2016-04-19 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is announcing the availability for public review of the draft document titled Draft Integrated Review Plan for the National Ambient Air Quality Standards for Particulate Matter (draft IRP). The draft IRP contains the current plans for the review of the air quality criteria for particulate matter (PM) and the primary and secondary national ambient air quality standards (NAAQS) for PM.
