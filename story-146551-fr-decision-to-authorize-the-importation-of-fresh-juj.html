BREAKING: Federal Register: Decision To Authorize the Importation of Fresh Jujube From China Into the Continental United States

Published: 2020-03-12 | Source: Federal Register

We are advising the public of our decision to authorize the importation of fresh jujube fruit from China into the continental United States.
