BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of OFAC Sanctions Action

Published: 2022-07-12 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of the Treasury's Office of Foreign Assets Control (OFAC) is publishing updates to the identifying information of one person on OFAC's List of Specially Designated Nationals and Blocked Persons (SDN List), whose property and interests in property are blocked pursuant to Executive Order 13405 of June 16, 2006, "Blocking Property of Certain Persons Undermining Democratic Processes or Institutions in Belarus," 71 FR 35485 (June 20, 2016) (E.O. 13405).
