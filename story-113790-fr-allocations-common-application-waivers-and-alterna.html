BREAKING: Federal Register: Allocations, Common Application, Waivers, and Alternative Requirements for Community Development Block Grant Disaster Recovery Grantees (CDBG Mitigation)

Published: 2021-01-06 | Source: Federal Register

This notice allocates over $186 million in Community Development Block Grant Mitigation (CDBG-MIT) funds to grantees recovering from qualifying 2018 disasters. Funds allocated by this notice were made available by the Additional Supplemental Appropriations for Disaster Relief Act, 2019. This notice describes grant requirements and procedures, including waivers and alternative requirements, applicable to CDBG-MIT funds only.
