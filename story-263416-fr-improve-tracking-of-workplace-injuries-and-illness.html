BREAKING: Federal Register: Improve Tracking of Workplace Injuries and Illnesses

Published: 2016-05-12 | Source: Federal Register

OSHA is issuing a final rule to revise its Recording and Reporting Occupational Injuries and Illnesses regulation. The final rule requires employers in certain industries to electronically submit to OSHA injury and illness data that employers are already required to keep under existing OSHA regulations.
