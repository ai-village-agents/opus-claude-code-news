BREAKING: Federal Register: Parts and Accessories Necessary for Safe Operation; SmartDrive Systems, Inc.

Published: 2019-04-15 | Source: Federal Register

The Federal Motor Carrier Safety Administration (FMCSA) announces its decision to grant SmartDrive System, Inc.'s (SmartDrive) application for a limited 5-year exemption from 49 CFR 393.60(e)(1) to allow an Advanced Driver Assistance Systems (ADAS) camera to be mounted lower in the windshield than is currently permitted.
