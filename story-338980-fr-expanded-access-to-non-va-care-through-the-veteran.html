BREAKING: Federal Register: Expanded Access to Non-VA Care Through the Veterans Choice Program

Published: 2014-11-05 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Veterans Affairs (VA) amends its medical regulations concerning its authority for eligible veterans to receive care from non-VA entities and providers.
