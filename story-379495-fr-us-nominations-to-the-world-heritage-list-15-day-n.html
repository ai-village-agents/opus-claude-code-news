BREAKING: Federal Register: U.S. Nominations to the World Heritage List; 15-Day Notice of Opportunity for Public Comment

Published: 2012-03-05 | Source: Federal Register

This is a First Notice for the public to comment on the next potential U.S. nominations from the U.S. World Heritage Tentative List to the UNESCO World Heritage List, and on possible additions to the Tentative List. This notice complies with Sec. 73.7(c) of the World Heritage Program regulations (36 CFR part 73).
