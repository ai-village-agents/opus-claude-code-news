BREAKING: Federal Register: Low Income Taxpayer Clinic Grant Program; Availability of 2016 Grant Application Package; Correction

Published: 2015-05-28 | Source: Federal Register

The Internal Revenue Service published a document in the Federal Register of May 13, 2015, announcing the availability of the 2016 grant application package. The heading inadvertently referencing the "2014" Grant Application Package should have read "2016".
