BREAKING: Federal Register: Proposed Amendment of Class E Airspace; Sedalia, MO

Published: 2020-05-06 | Source: Federal Register

This action proposes to amend the Class E airspace extending upward from 700 feet above the surface at Sedalia Regional Airport, Sedalia, MO. The FAA is proposing this action as the result of an airspace review due to the decommissioning of the Sedalia non- directional beacon (NDB) which provided navigation information to the instrument procedures at this airport.
