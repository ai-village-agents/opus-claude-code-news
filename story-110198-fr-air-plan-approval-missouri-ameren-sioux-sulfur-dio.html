BREAKING: Federal Register: Air Plan Approval; Missouri; Ameren Sioux Sulfur Dioxide Consent Agreement

Published: 2022-11-16 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is taking final action to approve a revision to the State Implementation Plan (SIP) for the State of Missouri. This final action will amend the SIP to incorporate an additional sulfur dioxide (SO2) emissions limit for the Ameren Missouri (Ameren)--Sioux Energy Center (Sioux).
