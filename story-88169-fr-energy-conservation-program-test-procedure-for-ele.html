BREAKING: Federal Register: Energy Conservation Program: Test Procedure for Electric Motors; Extension of Comment Period

Published: 2022-02-04 | Source: Federal Register

On December 17, 2021, the U.S. Department of Energy ("DOE") published a notice of proposed rulemaking ("NOPR") on potential amendments to its test procedure for electric motors. The NOPR provided an opportunity for submitting written comments, data, and information on the proposal by February 15, 2022.
