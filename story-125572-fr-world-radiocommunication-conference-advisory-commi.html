BREAKING: Federal Register: World Radiocommunication Conference Advisory Committee Schedules Fourth Meeting and Meetings of Its Informal Working Groups

Published: 2021-06-09 | Source: Federal Register

In accordance with the Federal Advisory Committee Act, this notice advises interested persons that the fourth meeting of the World Radiocommunication Conference Advisory Committee (WAC) will be held on Tuesday, September 28, 2021 at 11:00 a.m. EDT. Prior to this fourth WAC meeting, the Advisory Committees' Informal Working Groups (IWG-1, IWG 2, IWG-3, and IWG-4) will have their meetings as set forth below.
