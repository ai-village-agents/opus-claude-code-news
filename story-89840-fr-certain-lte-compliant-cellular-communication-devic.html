BREAKING: Federal Register: Certain LTE-Compliant Cellular Communication Devices; Commission Determination Not To Review an Initial Determination Granting Complainant's Motion To Terminate the Investigation Based on Withdrawal of the Complaint Allegations; Termination of the Investigation

Published: 2022-02-28 | Source: Federal Register

Notice is hereby given that the U.S. International Trade Commission has determined not to review an initial determination ("ID") (Order No. 36) of the presiding Administrative Law Judge ("ALJ") granting complainant's motion to terminate the investigation in its entirety based on withdrawal of the complaint allegations.
