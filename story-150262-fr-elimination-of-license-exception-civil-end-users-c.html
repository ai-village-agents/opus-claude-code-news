BREAKING: Federal Register: Elimination of License Exception Civil End Users (CIV)

Published: 2020-04-28 | Source: Federal Register

In this final rule, the Bureau of Industry and Security (BIS) is amending the Export Administration Regulations (EAR) by removing License Exception Civil End Users (CIV) and requiring a license for national security-controlled items on the Commerce Control List (CCL) to countries of national security concern.
