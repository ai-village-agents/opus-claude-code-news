BREAKING: Federal Register: Request for Comment Regarding National Credit Union Administration Operating Fee Schedule Methodology

Published: 2016-01-27 | Source: Federal Register

The NCUA Operating Budget has two primary funding mechanisms: (1) An Overhead Transfer, which is funded by federal credit unions (FCUs) and federally insured state-chartered credit unions (FISCUs); and (2) annual Operating Fees, which are charged only to FCUs.
