BREAKING: Federal Register: Law Enforcement Support Activities

Published: 2014-07-31 | Source: Federal Register

This final rule revises regulations regarding removal of obstructions, impoundment of personal property, payment of rewards, and payment for information and evidence in furtherance of an investigation. The revisions will clarify and concisely state the Agency's authority for setting reward amounts and will streamline the rules regarding payment for information and evidence in furtherance of an investigation.
