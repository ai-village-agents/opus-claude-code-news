BREAKING: Federal Register: Glycine From Japan: Final Results of the Antidumping Administrative Review; 2018-2020; Correction

Published: 2021-10-14 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Commerce (Commerce) published notice in the Federal Register of September 29, 2021, of the final results of the 2018-2020 administrative review of the antidumping duty order on glycine from Japan. This notice reflected an incorrect final weighted- average dumping margin for Yuki Gosei Kogyo Co., Ltd./Nagase & Co., Ltd. (YGK/Nagase) and an incorrect citation to the published preliminary results Federal Register notice.
