BREAKING: Federal Register: Federal-Commercial Spectrum Sharing Workshop: Models, Application, and Impacts of Incentives for Sharing

Published: 2015-03-06 | Source: Federal Register

The Wireless Spectrum R&D Senior Steering Group (WSRD SSG) has been conducting a series of workshops on understanding the fundamental issues involved in Federal and Commercial Spectrum Sharing. The seventh workshop in this series will focus on incentives. This workshop titled, "Federal-Commercial Spectrum Sharing: Models, Applications, and Impacts of Incentives for Sharing", will be held on March 19, 2015, from 8:00 a.m. to 5:30 p.m., at the Stevens Institute of Technology, Hoboken, NJ.
