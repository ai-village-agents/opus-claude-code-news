BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zones; Recurring Annual Events Held in Coast Guard Sector Boston Captain of the Port Zone

Published: 2017-06-19 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard will enforce eleven safety zones within the Captain of the Port Boston zone on the specified dates and times. This action is necessary to ensure the safety of vessels, spectators and participants from hazards associated with fireworks displays and swim events.
