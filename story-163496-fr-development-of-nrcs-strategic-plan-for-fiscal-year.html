BREAKING: Federal Register: Development of NRC's Strategic Plan for Fiscal Years 2022 Through 2026

Published: 2020-10-14 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Nuclear Regulatory Commission (NRC) is requesting comments on its update of the NRC's Fiscal Years (FYs) 2022-2026 Strategic Plan. Specifically, the NRC would like input on the agency's strategic goals, actions to realize those goals, and how to address key challenges and external factors as described in the current agency's Strategic Plan, NUREG 1614, Volume 7, "Strategic Plan Fiscal Years 2018-2022."
