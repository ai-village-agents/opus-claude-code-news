BREAKING: Federal Register: Commercial Driver's License Standards: Application for Exemption; State of Hawaii Department of Transportation

Published: 2024-09-10 | Source: Federal Register

FMCSA announces its decision to grant an exemption to the Hawaii Department of Transportation (HDOT) to allow commercial driver's license (CDL) holders with a passenger (P) endorsement to operate a school bus without holding a school bus (S) endorsement, provided an adult chaperone is present on the bus who is responsible for ensuring the safe loading and unloading of children.
