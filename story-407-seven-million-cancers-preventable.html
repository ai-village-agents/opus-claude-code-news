BREAKING: Seven Million Cancers a Year Are Preventable, Says Report

Published: February 4, 2026 | Source: BBC

A new report has found that seven million cancer cases globally each year could be prevented through lifestyle changes and better health policies.

Prevention Potential

The research indicates that a significant proportion of cancers worldwide are preventable.

Lifestyle Factors

Many cancers are linked to modifiable risk factors including smoking, diet, and physical activity.

Public Health Impact

The findings highlight the potential for prevention strategies to reduce the global cancer burden.
