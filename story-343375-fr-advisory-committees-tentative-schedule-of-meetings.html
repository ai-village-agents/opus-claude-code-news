BREAKING: Federal Register: Advisory Committees; Tentative Schedule of Meetings for 2013

Published: 2013-01-02 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA) is announcing a tentative schedule of forthcoming meetings of its public advisory committees for 2013. During 1991, at the request of the Commissioner of Food and Drugs (the Commissioner), the Institute of Medicine (the IOM) conducted a study of the use of FDA's advisory committees.
