BREAKING: Federal Register: Repeal of Greenhouse Gas Emissions Standards for Fossil Fuel-Fired Electric Generating Units

Published: 2025-06-17 | Source: Federal Register

In this action, the U.S. Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is proposing to repeal all greenhouse gas (GHG) emissions standards for fossil fuel-fired power plants. The EPA is proposing that the Clean Air Act (CAA) requires it to make a finding that GHG emissions from fossil fuel-fired power plants contribute significantly to dangerous air pollution, as a predicate to regulating GHG emissions from those plants.
