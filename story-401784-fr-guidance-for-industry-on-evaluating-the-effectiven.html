BREAKING: Federal Register: Guidance for Industry on Evaluating the Effectiveness of Anticoccidial Drugs in Food-Producing Animals; Availability

Published: 2012-11-20 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA) is announcing the availability of guidance for industry 217 entitled "Evaluating the Effectiveness of Anticoccidial Drugs in Food-Producing Animals."
