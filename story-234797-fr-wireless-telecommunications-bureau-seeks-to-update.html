BREAKING: Federal Register: Wireless Telecommunications Bureau Seeks To Update the Record in the Wireless Radio Services Reform Proceeding

Published: 2017-05-22 | Source: Federal Register

In this document, the Wireless Telecommunications Bureau (Bureau) invites interested parties to update the record in the WRS Reform NPRM and Order proceeding, which the Commission initiated in May 2010.
