BREAKING: Federal Register: Low Enriched Uranium From France; Termination of Five-Year Review

Published: 2019-05-31 | Source: Federal Register

The Commission instituted the subject five-year review in November 2018 to determine whether revocation of the antidumping duty order on low enriched uranium from France would be likely to lead to continuation or recurrence of material injury. On March 15, 2019, the Department of Commerce published notice that it was revoking the order effective December 24, 2018, because it did not receive a Notice of Intent to Participate from domestic interested parties.
