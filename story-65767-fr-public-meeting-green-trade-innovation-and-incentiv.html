BREAKING: Federal Register: Public Meeting: Green Trade Innovation and Incentives Forum

Published: 2023-04-21 | Source: Federal Register

U.S. Customs and Border Protection (CBP) will host a public Green Trade Innovation and Incentives Forum and invites interested parties to participate. CBP is announcing this public meeting to solicit and share ideas related to green trade innovation and incentivization of clean and sustainable supply chains and trade decarbonization.
