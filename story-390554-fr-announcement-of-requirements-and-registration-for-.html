BREAKING: Federal Register: Announcement of Requirements and Registration for Million Hearts TM Caregiver Video Challenge

Published: 2012-07-09 | Source: Federal Register

The Centers for Disease Control and Prevention (CDC) within the Department of Health and Human Services, in partnership with Million Hearts TM announces the launch of The Million Hearts TM Caregiver Video Challenge. We invite people who play a role in helping to prevent or control high blood pressure or maintain the heart health of a loved one to share their stories of caregiving by creating original, compelling videos that are less than 2 minutes long.
