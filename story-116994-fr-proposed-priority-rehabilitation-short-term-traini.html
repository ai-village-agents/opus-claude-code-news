BREAKING: Federal Register: Proposed Priority-Rehabilitation Short-Term Training-Client Assistance Program (CAP Training)

Published: 2021-02-19 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Education (Department) proposes a priority under the Rehabilitation Short-Term Training program, Assistance Listing Number 84.246K. We may use this priority for competitions in fiscal year (FY) 2021 and later years.
