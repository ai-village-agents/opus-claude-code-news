BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Filing of Plat of Survey; Eastern States

Published: 2019-11-18 | Source: Federal Register

On Monday, August 26, 2019, there was published in the Federal Register, Volume 84, Number 165, on page 44632 a notice entitled "Eastern States: Filing of Plats of Survey". In said notice was a plat depicting the dependent resurvey and corrective dependent resurvey of portions of the boundaries of the "Public Settlement Lands" recited in the Act of August 18, 1987, (Pub. L. 100-95) held in trust for the Mashpee Wampanoag Tribe, in the Town of Aquinnah, Dukes County, in the State of Massachusetts...
