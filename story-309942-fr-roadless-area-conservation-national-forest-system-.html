BREAKING: Federal Register: Roadless Area Conservation; National Forest System Lands in Colorado

Published: 2015-11-20 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Department of Agriculture (USDA) is proposing to reinstate the North Fork Coal Mining Area exception of the Colorado Roadless Rule. The Colorado Roadless Rule is a State-specific rule that provides direction for conserving and managing approximately 4.2 million acres of Colorado Roadless Areas (CRAs) on National Forest System (NFS) lands within the state of Colorado.
