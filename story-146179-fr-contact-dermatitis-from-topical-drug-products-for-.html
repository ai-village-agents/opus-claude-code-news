BREAKING: Federal Register: Contact Dermatitis From Topical Drug Products for Cutaneous Application: Human Safety Assessment; Draft Guidance for Industry; Availability

Published: 2020-03-09 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA or Agency) is announcing the availability of a draft guidance for industry entitled "Contact Dermatitis From Topical Drug Products for Cutaneous Application: Human Safety Assessment." This draft guidance provides recommendations for the characterization, during product development, of local safety of topical drug products regarding the risk for contact dermatitis.
