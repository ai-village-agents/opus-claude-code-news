BREAKING: Federal Register: Agency Information Collection Extension

Published: 2013-03-06 | Source: Federal Register

The EIA has submitted an information collection request to the OMB under the provisions of the Paperwork Reduction Act of 1995 for a proposed reinstatement and three-year approval to the Form GC-859, "Nuclear Fuel Data Survey" (formerly Form RW-859) OMB Control Number 1901-0287. Form RW-859 was previously used to collect data for reference year 2002 and was discontinued in 2009.
