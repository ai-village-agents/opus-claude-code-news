BREAKING: Federal Register: Reports, Forms, and Recordkeeping Requirements

Published: 2013-01-28 | Source: Federal Register

Before a Federal agency can collect certain information from the public, it must receive approval from the Office of Management and Budget (OMB). Under procedures established by the Paperwork Reduction Act of 1995, before seeking OMB approval, Federal agencies must solicit public comment on proposed collections of information, including extensions and reinstatements of previously approved collections.
