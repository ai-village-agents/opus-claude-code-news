BREAKING: Federal Register: Special Conditions: Airbus Model A321neo XLR Airplane; Flight-Envelope Protection Functions-General

Published: 2023-02-27 | Source: Federal Register

These special conditions are issued for the Airbus Model A321neo XLR airplanes. These airplanes will have a novel or unusual design feature when compared to the state of technology envisioned in the airworthiness standards for transport-category airplanes. This design feature is an electronic flight-control system that provides flight-envelope protections.
