BREAKING: Federal Register: Technical Considerations for Demonstrating Reliability of Emergency-Use Injectors Submitted Under a Biologics License Application, New Drug Application, or Abbreviated New Drug Application; Draft Guidance for Industry and Food and Drug Administration; Availability

Published: 2020-04-22 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA or Agency) is announcing the availability of a draft guidance for industry and FDA entitled "Technical Considerations for Demonstrating Reliability of Emergency- Use Injectors Submitted under a BLA, NDA or ANDA." For injectable drug or biological products that are intended to treat emergent, life- threatening conditions, it is essential to ensure that the emergency- use injector will reliably deliver the drug or biological product as intended.
