BREAKING: Federal Register: Study on the Right of Making Available; Comments and Public Roundtable

Published: 2014-02-25 | Source: Federal Register

The United States Copyright Office is undertaking a study at the request of Congress to assess the state of U.S. law recognizing and protecting "making available" and "communication to the public" rights for copyright holders.
