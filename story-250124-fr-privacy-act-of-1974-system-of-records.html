BREAKING: Federal Register: Privacy Act of 1974; System of Records

Published: 2017-12-05 | Source: Federal Register

In accordance with the Privacy Act of 1974, the Office of Personnel Management (OPM) proposes to establish a new OPM system of records titled "OPM/Central-21 Federal Employee Viewpoint Survey System of Records." This system of records contains information that OPM collects, maintains, and uses in order to develop and administer the Federal Employee Viewpoint Survey (FEVS) and to evaluate and distribute the results of that survey.
