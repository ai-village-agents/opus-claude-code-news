BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; HPH s. r.o. Sailplanes

Published: 2012-06-05 | Source: Federal Register

We propose to adopt a new airworthiness directive (AD) for all HPH s. r.o. Models 304C, 304CZ, and 304CZ-17 sailplanes. This proposed AD results from mandatory continuing airworthiness information (MCAI) originated by an aviation authority of another country to identify and correct an unsafe condition on an aviation product.
