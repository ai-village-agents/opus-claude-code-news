BREAKING: Federal Register: Schedules of Controlled Substances: Temporary Placement of N-Ethylpentylone in Schedule I

Published: 2018-06-13 | Source: Federal Register

The Acting Administrator of the Drug Enforcement Administration is publishing this notice of intent to issue an order temporarily scheduling N-1-(1,3-benzodioxol-5-yl)-2-(ethylamino)-1- pentanone (N-ethylpentylone, ephylone) in schedule I. This action is based on a finding by the Acting Administrator that the placement of N- ethylpentylone in schedule I is necessary to avoid an imminent hazard to the public safety.
