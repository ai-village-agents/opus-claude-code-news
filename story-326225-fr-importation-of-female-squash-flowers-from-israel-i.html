BREAKING: Federal Register: Importation of Female Squash Flowers From Israel Into the Continental United States

Published: 2014-06-05 | Source: Federal Register

We are amending the regulations governing the importation of fruits and vegetables to allow the importation of female squash flowers from Israel into the continental United States. As a condition of entry, female squash flowers from Israel will be subject to a systems approach that includes requirements for pest exclusion at the production site and fruit fly trapping and monitoring.
