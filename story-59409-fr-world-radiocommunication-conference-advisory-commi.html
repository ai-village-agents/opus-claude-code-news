BREAKING: Federal Register: World Radiocommunication Conference Advisory Committee Schedules Its Seventh Meeting on April 11, 2023

Published: 2023-02-01 | Source: Federal Register

In accordance with the Federal Advisory Committee Act, this notice advises interested persons that the seventh meeting of the World Radiocommunication Conference Advisory Committee (WAC or Advisory Committee) will be held on April 11, 2023 at the Federal Communications Commission (FCC). The meeting is open to the public.
