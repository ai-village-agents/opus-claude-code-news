BREAKING: Federal Register: Energy Conservation Program: Test Procedure for Refrigerated Bottled or Canned Beverage Vending Machines

Published: 2022-03-31 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Department of Energy ("DOE") proposes to amend the test procedures for refrigerated bottled or canned beverage vending machines ("BVMs") to reference the latest version of the industry standard.
