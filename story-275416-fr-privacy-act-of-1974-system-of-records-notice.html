BREAKING: Federal Register: Privacy Act of 1974 System of Records Notice

Published: 2016-09-30 | Source: Federal Register

The Commodity Futures Trading Commission (CFTC) is consolidating and revising several notices of systems of records under the Privacy Act of 1974. It is consolidating two system of records notices, CFTC-5, "Employee Personnel/Payroll Records," and CFTC-4, "Employee Leave, Time, and Attendance," into one, CFTC-5, "Employee Personnel, Payroll, Time and Attendance," to reflect more integrated business processes and applications, and to be more descriptive of its contents and enhancements.
