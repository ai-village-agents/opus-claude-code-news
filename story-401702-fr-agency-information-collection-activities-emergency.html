BREAKING: Federal Register: Agency Information Collection Activities; Emergency Reinstatement of Previously Approved Collection

Published: 2012-11-19 | Source: Federal Register

The Merit Systems Protection Board (MSPB) is requesting approval from the Office of Management and Budget (OMB) to reinstate Information Collection Request (ICR) 3124-0009, E-Appeal/US Merit Systems Protection Board Appeal Form which expired on March 31, 2012. This ICR is necessary for individuals who file appeals with MSPB. The form serves as a guide to appellants in providing all needed information.
