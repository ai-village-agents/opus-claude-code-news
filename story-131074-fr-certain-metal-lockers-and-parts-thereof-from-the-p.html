BREAKING: Federal Register: Certain Metal Lockers and Parts Thereof From the People's Republic of China: Antidumping and Countervailing Duty Orders

Published: 2021-08-20 | Source: Federal Register

Based on the affirmative final determinations by the Department of Commerce (Commerce) and the International Trade Commission (ITC), Commerce is issuing antidumping duty (AD) and countervailing duty (CVD) orders on certain metal lockers and parts thereof (metal lockers) from the People's Republic of China (China).
