BREAKING: Federal Register: Broker and Freight Forwarder Financial Responsibility

Published: 2018-09-27 | Source: Federal Register

FMCSA announces that it is initiating rulemaking action pertaining to the implementation of the Moving Ahead for Progress in the 21st Century Act (MAP-21). MAP-21 raised the financial security amount for brokers to $75,000 and, for the first time, established financial security requirements for freight forwarders.
