BREAKING: Federal Register: Emergency Assistance for Livestock, Honeybees, and Farm-Raised Fish Program (ELAP)

Published: 2024-07-01 | Source: Federal Register

This rule makes changes to ELAP to provide financial assistance to dairy producers who face milk losses due to H5N1 infection of their dairy herds.
