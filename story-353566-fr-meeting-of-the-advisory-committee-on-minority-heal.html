BREAKING: Federal Register: Meeting of the Advisory Committee on Minority Health

Published: 2013-04-30 | Source: Federal Register

As stipulated by the Federal Advisory Committee Act, the Department of Health and Human Services (DHHS) is hereby giving notice that a meeting of the Advisory Committee on Minority Health (ACMH) will be held virtually. This virtual meeting will take place via webinar and audio video conferencing technology. This meeting will be open to the public.
