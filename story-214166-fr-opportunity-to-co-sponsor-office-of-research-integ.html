BREAKING: Federal Register: Opportunity to Co-Sponsor Office of Research Integrity Workshops

Published: 2018-08-16 | Source: Federal Register

The Office of Research Integrity (ORI) announces the opportunity for non-federal public and private sector entities to co- sponsor ORI conferences or workshops (ORI Workshops). Potential co- sponsors must have a demonstrated interest and experience in the responsible conduct of research (RCR) or the handling of research misconduct allegations.
