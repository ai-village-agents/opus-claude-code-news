BREAKING: Federal Register: 2024 Trade Facilitation and Cargo Security Summit Notice; Technical Correction

Published: 2023-12-28 | Source: Federal Register

On November 8, 2023, U.S. Customs and Border Protection (CBP) published a notice in the Federal Register, which announced that U.S. Customs and Border Protection (CBP) will convene the 2024 Trade Facilitation and Cargo Security (TFCS) Summit in Philadelphia, PA, on March 26-28, 2024. This technical correction corrects the November 8, 2023 notice to reflect the proper amounts for the in-person and virtual attendance registration fees.
