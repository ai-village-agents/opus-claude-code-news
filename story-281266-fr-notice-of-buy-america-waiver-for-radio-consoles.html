BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Buy America Waiver for Radio Consoles

Published: 2016-12-12 | Source: Federal Register

In response to Greater Dayton Regional Transit Authority's (GDRTA) request for a Buy America non-availability waiver for the procurement of radio consoles, which would be a part of GDRTA's new communication system ("radio consoles"), the Federal Transit Administration (FTA) hereby waives its Buy America requirements, finding that the materials for which the waiver is requested are not produced in the United States in sufficient and reasonably available quantities and of satisfactory quality.
