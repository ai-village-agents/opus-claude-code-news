BREAKING: Federal Register: Fiscal Year (FY) 2021 Competitive Funding Opportunity: Standards Development for Bus Exportable Power Systems (BEPS)

Published: 2022-04-12 | Source: Federal Register

The Federal Transit Administration (FTA) announces the opportunity to apply for a total of $1,000,000 of Fiscal Year (FY) 2021 Technical Assistance and Workforce Development Program funds for projects that develop interoperable standards for Bus Exportable Power Systems (BEPS). BEPS enable public transportation agencies, communities, and States to access resilient and flexible power options through bus fleet vehicles during major power disruptions.
