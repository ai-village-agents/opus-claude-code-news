BREAKING: Federal Register: National Emission Standards for Hazardous Air Pollutants for Clay Ceramics Manufacturing

Published: 2018-08-20 | Source: Federal Register

This action proposes amendments to the National Emission Standards for Hazardous Air Pollutants (NESHAP) for Clay Ceramics Manufacturing. The proposed amendments are in response to a petition for reconsideration filed by industry stakeholders on the final rule promulgated on October 26, 2015, as well as our review of the 2015 rule with respect to other issues raised by stakeholders.
