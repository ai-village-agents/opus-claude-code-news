BREAKING: Federal Register: Proposed Information Collection (Casket/Urn Reimbursement) Activity: Withdrawal

Published: 2015-02-06 | Source: Federal Register

On Wednesday January 28, 2015, The National Cemetery Administration (NCA), Department of Veterans Affairs (VA), published a notice in the Federal Register announcing an opportunity for public comment on the proposed collection Casket/Urn Reimbursement. This notice (FR Vol. 80, Number 18, January 28, 2015) was published in error; therefore this document corrects that error by withdrawing this FR notice, document number 2015-01581.
