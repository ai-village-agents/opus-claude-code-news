BREAKING: Federal Register: Notification of the Fourteenth CAFTA-DR Environmental Affairs Council Meeting

Published: 2021-09-03 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of State and the Office of the United States Trade Representative are providing notice that the parties to CAFTA-DR intend to hold the fourteenth meeting of the Environmental Affairs Council (the Council) established under Chapter 17 (Environment Chapter) of that agreement in a virtual meeting on October 14, 2021, hosted by Costa Rica.
