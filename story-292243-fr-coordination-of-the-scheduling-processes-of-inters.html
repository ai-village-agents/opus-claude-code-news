BREAKING: Federal Register: Coordination of the Scheduling Processes of Interstate Natural Gas Pipelines and Public Utilities

Published: 2015-04-24 | Source: Federal Register

In this Final Rule, the Federal Energy Regulatory Commission (Commission) is revising its regulations to better coordinate the scheduling of wholesale natural gas and electricity markets in light of increased reliance on natural gas for electric generation, as well as to provide additional scheduling flexibility to all shippers on interstate natural gas pipelines.
