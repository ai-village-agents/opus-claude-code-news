BREAKING: Federal Register: Small Business Size Standards: Finance and Insurance and Management of Companies and Enterprises

Published: 2012-09-11 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Small Business Administration (SBA) proposes to increase small business size standards for 37 industries in North American Industry Classification System (NAICS) Sector 52, Finance and Insurance, and for two industries in NAICS Sector 55, Management of Companies and Enterprises. In addition, SBA proposes to change the measure of size from average assets to average receipts for NAICS 522293, International Trade Financing.
