BREAKING: Federal Register: Establishment of Class E Airspace and Amendment of Class E Airspace; Ephrata, WA

Published: 2019-02-13 | Source: Federal Register

This action establishes Class E airspace extending upward from 700 feet above the surface, and modifies Class E surface area airspace at Ephrata Municipal Airport, Ephrata, WA. This action also updates the geographic coordinates of the airport in the associated Class E airspace areas to match the FAA's aeronautical database.
