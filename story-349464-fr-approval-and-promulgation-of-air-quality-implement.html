BREAKING: Federal Register: Approval and Promulgation of Air Quality Implementation Plans; West Virginia; Prevention of Significant Deterioration

Published: 2013-03-15 | Source: Federal Register

EPA is proposing to disapprove a narrow portion of a State Implementation Plan (SIP) revision submitted by the State of West Virginia on August 31, 2011. EPA is proposing this action because a narrow portion of the submittal does not satisfy the Federal requirement for the inclusion of condensable emissions of particulate matter (condensables) within the definition of "regulated new source review (NSR) pollutant."
