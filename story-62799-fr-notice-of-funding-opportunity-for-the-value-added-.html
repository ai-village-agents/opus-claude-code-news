BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Funding Opportunity for the Value-Added Producer Grants for Fiscal Year 2023

Published: 2023-03-17 | Source: Federal Register

The Rural Business-Cooperative Service (RBCS, Agency), a Rural Development (RD) agency of the United States Department of Agriculture (USDA), announces acceptance of applications under the Value-Added Producer Grant (VAPG) program for Fiscal Year (FY) 2023, subject to the availability of funding. This Notice is being issued in order to allow applicants sufficient time to leverage financing, prepare and submit their applications, and give the Agency time to process applications within FY 2023.
