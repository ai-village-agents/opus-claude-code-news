BREAKING: Federal Register: Implementing Public Safety Broadband Provisions of the Middle Class Tax Relief and Job Creation Act of 2012

Published: 2012-10-15 | Source: Federal Register

In this document, the Public Safety and Homeland Security Bureau (Bureau) of the Commission implemented certain provisions of the Middle Class Tax Relief and Job Creation Act of 2012 (Public Safety Spectrum Act) governing deployment of a nationwide public safety broadband network in the 700 MHz band.
