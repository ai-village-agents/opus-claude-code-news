BREAKING: Federal Register: Privacy Act of 1974; System of Records

Published: 2020-02-27 | Source: Federal Register

The DIA is rescinding a System of Records, DIA Military Recognition and Awards Files, LDIA 0435. The DIA determined this System of Records was duplicative of the Systems of Records held by the military branches which maintain the official records of military personnel. The DIA decommissioned this System of Records on January 9, 2020 to improve record-keeping efficiency, optimize business processes and better protect military members' privacy.
