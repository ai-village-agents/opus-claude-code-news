BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Opportunity for Public Comment on Surplus Property Release at Beverly Airport in Beverly, MA

Published: 2016-02-26 | Source: Federal Register

Under the provisions of Title 49, U.S.C. Section 47153(d), notice is being given that the FAA is considering a request from Beverly Airport in Beverly, MA to change the use of a certain parcel of land and associated building from Aeronautical to Non-Aeronautical Land for future non-aeronautical revenue generation at Beverly Airport in Beverly, MA. The subject parcel contains Building #45 and 19 automobile parking spaces which is currently vacant and is landside with no access to aviation areas. ...
