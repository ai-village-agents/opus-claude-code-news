BREAKING: Federal Register: Request for Medicaid and CHIP Payment and Access Commission (MACPAC) Nominations

Published: 2023-01-03 | Source: Federal Register

The Children's Health Insurance Program Reauthorization Act of 2009 (CHIPRA) established MACPAC to review Medicaid and CHIP access and payment policies and to advise Congress on issues affecting Medicaid and CHIP. CHIPRA gave the Comptroller General of the United States responsibility for appointing MACPAC's members.
