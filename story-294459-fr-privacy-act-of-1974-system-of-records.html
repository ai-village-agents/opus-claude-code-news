BREAKING: Federal Register: Privacy Act of 1974; System of Records

Published: 2015-05-21 | Source: Federal Register

The Office of the Secretary of Defense proposes to alter a system of records, DWHS E02, entitled "Freedom of Information Act Case Files" in its inventory of record systems subject to the Privacy Act of 1974, as amended.
