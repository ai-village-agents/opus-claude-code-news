BREAKING: Federal Register: Amendment of V-6, V-10, V-30, V-100, and V-233 in the Vicinity of Litchfield, MI

Published: 2023-01-17 | Source: Federal Register

This action amends VHF Omnidirectional Range (VOR) Federal airways V-6, V-10, V-30, V-100, and V-233 in the vicinity of Litchfield, MI. The airway modifications are necessary due to the planned decommissioning of the VOR portion of the Litchfield, MI, VOR/ Distance Measuring Equipment (VOR/DME) navigational aid (NAVAID) which provides navigational guidance for portions of the affected VOR Federal airways listed above.
