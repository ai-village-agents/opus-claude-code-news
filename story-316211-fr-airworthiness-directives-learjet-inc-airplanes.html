BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Learjet Inc. Airplanes

Published: 2014-02-10 | Source: Federal Register

We propose to adopt a new airworthiness directive (AD) for certain Learjet Inc. Model 45 airplanes. This proposed AD was prompted by a report of two cases of premature corrosion found on the structural support flange for the engine thrust reverser.
