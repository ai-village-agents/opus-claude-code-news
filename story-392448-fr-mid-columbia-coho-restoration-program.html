BREAKING: Federal Register: Mid-Columbia Coho Restoration Program

Published: 2012-07-31 | Source: Federal Register

This notice announces the availability of the ROD for the Mid- Columbia Coho restoration program, sponsored by the Confederated Tribes and Bands of the Yakama Nation and based on the Mid-Columbia Coho Restoration Program EIS (DOE/EIS-0425, March 2012). The purpose of the program is to re-establish naturally spawning coho populations to harvestable levels in tributaries of the Wenatchee and Methow basins in north-central Washington State (Okanogan and Chelan Counties).
