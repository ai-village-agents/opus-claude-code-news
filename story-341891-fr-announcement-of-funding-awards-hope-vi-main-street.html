BREAKING: Federal Register: Announcement of Funding Awards HOPE VI Main Street Grant Program Fiscal Year (FY) 2014

Published: 2014-12-15 | Source: Federal Register

In accordance with Section 102(a)(4)(C) of the Department of Housing and Urban Development Reform Act of 1989, this announcement notifies the public of funding decisions made by the Department in a competition for funding under the FY 2014 Notice of Funding Availability (NOFA) for the HOPE VI Main Street Program. This announcement contains the name and address of the award recipient under said NOFA.
