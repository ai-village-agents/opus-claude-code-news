BREAKING: Federal Register: Special Local Regulation; Sail Grand Prix 2021 Race Event; San Francisco, CA

Published: 2021-12-16 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is proposing to establish a temporary special local regulation in the navigable waters of San Francisco Bay in San Francisco, CA in support of the San Francisco Sail Grand Prix 2021 race periods on March 24, 2022 through March 27, 2022.
