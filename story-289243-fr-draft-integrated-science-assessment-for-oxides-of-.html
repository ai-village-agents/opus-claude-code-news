BREAKING: Federal Register: Draft Integrated Science Assessment for Oxides of Nitrogen-Health Criteria; Extension of Comment Period

Published: 2015-03-20 | Source: Federal Register

EPA is announcing an extension of the public comment period for the second external review draft of a document titled, "Second External Review Draft Integrated Science Assessment for Oxides of Nitrogen--Health Criteria" (EPA/600/R-14/006). The original Federal Register document announcing the public comment period was published on January 30, 2015 (80 FR 5110). With this extension, the comment period ends on April 30, 2015.
