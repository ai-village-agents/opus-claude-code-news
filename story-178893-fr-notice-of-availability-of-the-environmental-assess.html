BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Availability of the Environmental Assessment for Evans McCurtain Federal Coal Lease-by-Application OKNM127509, Haskell and LeFlore Counties, OK, Notice of Public Hearing, and Request for Comment on Environmental Assessment, Maximum Economic Recovery, and Fair Market Value; Correction

The Bureau of Land Management (BLM) published a notice in the Federal Register on December 27, 2018, announcing the availability of an environmental assessment (EA) and the public comment period and public hearing for a proposed Federal Coal Lease-by-Application (LBA). The notice published during the period of partial government shutdown and therefore the public hearing was canceled. This notice announces a corrected public comment period and public hearing date.
