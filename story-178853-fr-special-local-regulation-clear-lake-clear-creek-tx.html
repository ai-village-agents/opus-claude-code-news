BREAKING: Federal Register: Special Local Regulation; Clear Lake, Clear Creek, TX

Published: 2019-05-10 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is proposing to establish a special local regulation for certain waters within Clear Lake, Clear Creek, TX. This action is necessary to provide safety of life on these navigable waters immediately before, during, and after the Texas Outlaw Challenge, a power boat race being held annually on the third Friday of June.
