BREAKING: Federal Register: Discontinue Use of Form CD-492, Justification for Other Than Full and Open Competition

Published: 2024-07-23 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Commerce (Commerce) is issuing this final rule to discontinue use of Form CD-492, Justification for Other Than Full and Open Competition, and to make an editorial change to the associated regulations.
