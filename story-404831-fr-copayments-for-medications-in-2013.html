BREAKING: Federal Register: Copayments for Medications in 2013

Published: 2012-12-31 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Veterans Affairs (VA) amends its medical regulations concerning the copayment required for certain medications. But for this rulemaking, beginning on January 1, 2013, the copayment amount would increase based on a formula set forth in regulation. The maximum annual copayment amount payable by veterans would also increase.
