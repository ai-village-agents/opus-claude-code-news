BREAKING: Federal Register: Aspergillus flavus NRRL 21882; Amendment to an Exemption From the Requirement of a Tolerance

Published: 2020-09-25 | Source: Federal Register

This regulation amends the existing tolerance exemptions for residues of Aspergillus flavus NRRL 21882 in or on corn and peanut commodities by clarifying that the exemption covers all food and feed commodities of these crops and by establishing an exemption for all food and feed commodities of almond and pistachio.
