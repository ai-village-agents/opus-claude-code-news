BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; The Boeing Company Airplanes

Published: 2013-02-08 | Source: Federal Register

We propose to adopt a new airworthiness directive (AD) for certain The Boeing Company Model 747-100, 747-100B, 747-100B SUD, 747-200B, 747-200C, 747-300, 747-400, 747-400D, and 747SP series airplanes. This proposed AD was prompted by reports of worn or incorrectly assembled latches on main deck escape slides installed on airplane doors.
