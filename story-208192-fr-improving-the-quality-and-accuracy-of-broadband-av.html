BREAKING: Federal Register: Improving the Quality and Accuracy of Broadband Availability Data

Published: 2018-05-30 | Source: Federal Register

The National Telecommunications and Information Administration (NTIA), on behalf of the Department of Commerce (Department), is requesting comment on actions that can be taken to improve the quality and accuracy of broadband availability data, particularly in rural areas, as part of the activities directed by Congress in the Consolidated Appropriations Act of 2018.
