BREAKING: Federal Register: Patient Protection and Affordable Care Act; Program Integrity: Exchange, SHOP, and Eligibility Appeals

Published: 2013-08-30 | Source: Federal Register

This final rule implements provisions of the Patient Protection and Affordable Care Act and the Health Care and Education Reconciliation Act of 2010 (collectively referred to as the Affordable Care Act). Specifically, this final rule outlines Exchange standards with respect to eligibility appeals, agents and brokers, privacy and security, issuer direct enrollment, and the handling of consumer cases.
