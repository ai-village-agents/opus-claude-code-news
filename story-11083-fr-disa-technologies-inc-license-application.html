BREAKING: Federal Register: Disa Technologies, Inc; License Application

Published: 2025-05-12 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Nuclear Regulatory Commission (NRC) has received a license application from Disa Technologies, Inc (Disa) for a multi-site service provider license for its high-pressure slurry ablation (HPSA) technology to remediate abandoned uranium mine (AUM) waste. Disa's request (Docket #040-38417) is to use HPSA technology to perform remediation at certain AUM sites after additional site-specific safety and environmental information is provided to and approved by the NRC.
