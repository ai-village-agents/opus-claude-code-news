BREAKING: Federal Register: Authorization for Non-VA Medical Services; Withdrawal

Published: 2013-12-16 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Veterans Affairs (VA) published a direct final rule in the Federal Register on November 28, 2012, that would have amended its regulations regarding payment by VA for medical services under VA's statutory authority to provide non-VA medical care. VA sought to remove an outdated regulatory limitation on veterans' eligibility to be referred for non-VA medical care.
