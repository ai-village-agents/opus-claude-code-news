BREAKING: Federal Register: National Cybersecurity Center of Excellence (NCCoE) Securing Property Management Systems for the Hospitality Sector

Published: 2017-11-24 | Source: Federal Register

This notice is the initial step for the National Cybersecurity Center of Excellence (NCCoE) in collaborating with technology companies to address cybersecurity challenges identified under the Hospitality Sector program.
