BREAKING: Federal Register: Sodium lauroyl sarcosinate; Exemption From the Requirement of a Tolerance

Published: 2021-05-12 | Source: Federal Register

This regulation establishes an exemption from the requirement of a tolerance for residues of sodium lauroyl sarcosinate when used as an inert ingredient in antimicrobial pesticide formulations applied to food-contact surfaces in public eating places, dairy-processing equipment, and food-processing equipment and utensils at an end-use concentration not to exceed 10,000 parts per million (ppm).
