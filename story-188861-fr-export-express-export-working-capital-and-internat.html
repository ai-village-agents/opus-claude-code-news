BREAKING: Federal Register: Export Express, Export Working Capital, and International Trade Loan Programs

Published: 2019-09-17 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Small Business Administration (SBA or Agency) is seeking comments on potential changes to the regulations governing its Export Loan Programs (the Export Express, Export Working Capital, and International Trade Loan Programs). SBA is soliciting comments on how the Agency can improve the products, procedures, forms, and reporting requirements of the Export Loan Programs.
