BREAKING: Federal Register: Aging Management of Loss of Coating Integrity for Internal Service Level III (Augmented) Coatings

Published: 2014-01-10 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Nuclear Regulatory Commission (NRC) requests public comment on the draft License Renewal Interim Staff Guidance (LR-ISG), LR-ISG-2013-01, "Aging Management of Loss of Coating Integrity for Internal Service Level III (augmented) Coatings." The draft LR-ISG proposes to add a new NRC staff-recommended aging management program (AMP), new aging management review (AMR) items, and new age-related definitions in NUREG-1801, Revision 2, "Generic Aging Lessons Learned (GALL) Report."
