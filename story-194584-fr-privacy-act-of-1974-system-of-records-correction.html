BREAKING: Federal Register: Privacy Act of 1974; System of Records; Correction

Published: 2019-12-02 | Source: Federal Register

On Wednesday, October 16, 2019, the DoD published a notice titled "Privacy Act of 1974; System of Records" that modified a System of Records titled, "Defense Enrollment Eligibility Reporting System (DEERS), DMDC 02 DoD." Subsequent to the publication of the notice, DoD discovered an error. This notice corrects the error.
