BREAKING: Federal Register: Programmatic Environmental Assessment

Published: 2013-04-30 | Source: Federal Register

We, the Fish and Wildlife Service (Service), intend to prepare a programmatic environmental assessment (PEA) to evaluate the effects of the cultivation and use of genetically modified crops (GMCs) on lands that are part of the National Wildlife Refuge System in the Southeast Region (Refuge System lands).
