BREAKING: Federal Register: Commission Information Collection Activities (FERC-725R); Comment Request; Revision

Published: 2020-12-29 | Source: Federal Register

In compliance with the requirements of the Paperwork Reduction Act of 1995, the Federal Energy Regulatory Commission (Commission or FERC) is soliciting public comment on the renewal with revisions of currently approved FERC- 725R (Mandatory Reliability Standards: BAL Reliability Standards). The Commission is submitting FERC-725R with revisions to the Office of Management and Budget (OMB) for review.
