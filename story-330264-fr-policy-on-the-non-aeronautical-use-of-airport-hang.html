BREAKING: Federal Register: Policy on the Non-aeronautical Use of Airport Hangars

Published: 2014-07-22 | Source: Federal Register

Under Federal law, airport operators that have accepted federal grants and/or those that have obligations contained in property deeds for property transferred under various Federal laws such as the Surplus Property Act generally may use airport property only for aviation-related purposes unless otherwise approved by the FAA.
