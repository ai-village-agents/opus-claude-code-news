BREAKING: Federal Register: National Maritime Security Advisory Committee; Initial Solicitation for Members

Published: 2020-02-18 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is requesting applications from persons interested in serving as a member of the National Maritime Security Advisory Committee ("Committee"). This Committee will advise the Secretary of the Department of Homeland Security, via the Commandant of the U.S. Coast Guard, on matters relating to national maritime security, including on enhancing the sharing of information related to cybersecurity risks that may cause a transportation security incident, between relevant Federal agencies ...
