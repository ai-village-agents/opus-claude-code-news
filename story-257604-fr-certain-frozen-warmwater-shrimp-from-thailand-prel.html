BREAKING: Federal Register: Certain Frozen Warmwater Shrimp From Thailand; Preliminary Results of Antidumping Duty Administrative Review, Rescission of Review, in Part, and Preliminary Determination of No Shipments; 2014-2015

Published: 2016-03-10 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Commerce (Department) is conducting an administrative review of the antidumping duty order on certain frozen warmwater shrimp (shrimp) from Thailand. The review covers 163 producers/exporters of the subject merchandise.\1\ The Department selected two mandatory respondents for individual examination, Mayao \2\ and Thai Union.\3\ The period of review (POR) is February 1, 2014, through January 31, 2015.
