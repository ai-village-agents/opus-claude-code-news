BREAKING: Federal Register: Suspension of September 1, 2015 Digital Transition Date for Low Power Television and TV Translator Stations

Published: 2015-05-15 | Source: Federal Register

In this document, the Media Bureau of the Federal Communications Commission (Commission) announced that, effective May 15, 2015, the September 1, 2015 digital transition date for low power television (LPTV) and TV translator stations is hereby suspended. The Commission will decide on a new transition date in the rulemaking proceeding in MB Docket No. 03-185.
