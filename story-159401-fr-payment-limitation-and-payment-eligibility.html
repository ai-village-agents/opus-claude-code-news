BREAKING: Federal Register: Payment Limitation and Payment Eligibility

Published: 2020-08-24 | Source: Federal Register

This rule implements the mandatory changes required by the Agriculture Improvement Act of 2018 (2018 Farm Bill) and other changes made by the Farm Service Agency (FSA) on behalf of CCC.
