BREAKING: Federal Register: Expanding Consumers' Video Navigation Choices; Commercial Availability of Navigation Devices

Published: 2016-03-16 | Source: Federal Register

In this document, we propose new rules to empower consumers to choose how they wish to access the multichannel video programming to which they subscribe, and promote innovation in the display, selection, and use of this programming and of other video programming available to consumers.
