BREAKING: Federal Register: Examining Computer-Implemented Functional Claim Limitations for Compliance With 35 U.S.C. 112

Published: 2019-01-07 | Source: Federal Register

This guidance will assist United States Patent and Trademark Office (USPTO) personnel in the examination of claims in patent applications that contain functional language, particularly patent applications where functional language is used to claim computer- implemented inventions. Part I of this guidance addresses issues related to the examination of computer-implemented functional claims having means-plus-function limitations.
