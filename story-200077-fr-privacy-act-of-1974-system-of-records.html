BREAKING: Federal Register: Privacy Act of 1974; System of Records.

Published: 2018-02-14 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Health and Human Services (HHS), Centers for Medicare & Medicaid Services (CMS) proposes to establish a new system of records subject to the Privacy Act, System No. 09-70-0539, titled "Quality Payment Program (QPP)."
