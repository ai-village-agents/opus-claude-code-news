BREAKING: Federal Register: Request for Information on FEMA Programs, Regulations, and Policies

Published: 2021-04-22 | Source: Federal Register

The Federal Emergency Management Agency (FEMA) is issuing this Request for Information (RFI) to receive input from the public on specific FEMA programs, regulations, collections of information, and policies for the agency to consider modifying, streamlining, expanding, or repealing in light of recent Executive orders.
