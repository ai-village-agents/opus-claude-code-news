BREAKING: Federal Register: Standardization of the Patent Term Adjustment Statement Regarding Information Disclosure Statements

Published: 2022-11-18 | Source: Federal Register

The United States Patent and Trademark Office (USPTO) is reopening the comment period for the proposed rule titled "Standardization of the Patent Term Adjustment Statement Regarding Information Disclosure Statements" that was published in the Federal Register on July 12, 2022. The proposed rule's comment period, which ended on September 12, 2022, is extended until December 2, 2022.
