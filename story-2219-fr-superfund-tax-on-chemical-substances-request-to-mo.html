BREAKING: Federal Register: Superfund Tax on Chemical Substances; Request To Modify List of Taxable Substances; Notice of Filing for Poly(styrene-divinylbenzene-ethylvinylbenzene), With Styrene Content of Greater than 50% (x=2.02x1017, y=2.98x1016, z=7.35x1015)

Published: 2026-01-14 | Source: Federal Register
