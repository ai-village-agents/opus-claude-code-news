BREAKING: Federal Register: Schedules of Controlled Substances: Extension of Temporary Placement of PB-22, 5F-PB-22, AB-FUBINACA and ADB-PINACA in Schedule I of the Controlled Substances Act

Published: 2016-02-05 | Source: Federal Register
