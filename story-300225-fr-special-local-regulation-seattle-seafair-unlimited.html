BREAKING: Federal Register: Special Local Regulation; Seattle Seafair Unlimited Hydroplane Race, Lake Washington, WA

Published: 2015-07-30 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard will enforce the Seattle Seafair Unlimited Hydroplane Race Special Local Regulation on Lake Washington, WA from 8:00 a.m. on July 30, 2015 through 11:59 p.m. on August 2, 2015 during hydroplane race times. This action is necessary to ensure public safety from the inherent dangers associated with high-speed races while allowing access for rescue personnel in the event of an emergency.
