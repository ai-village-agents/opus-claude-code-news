BREAKING: Federal Register: Anchorage Grounds, Hudson River; Yonkers, NY to Kingston, NY

Published: 2023-07-25 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is withdrawing the advance notice of proposed rulemaking titled "Anchorage Grounds, Hudson River; Yonkers, NY to Kingston, NY" published in the Federal Register on June 9, 2016. After a review of comments, the Coast Guard suspended rulemaking action in 2017 to allow for further study and analysis of the need, impact, and appropriateness of the requested anchorage grounds.
