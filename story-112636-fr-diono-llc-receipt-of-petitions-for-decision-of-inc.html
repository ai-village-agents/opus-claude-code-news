BREAKING: Federal Register: Diono LLC, Receipt of Petitions for Decision of Inconsequential Noncompliance

Published: 2022-12-19 | Source: Federal Register

Diono LLC, (Diono), has determined that certain models of its child restraint systems do not fully comply with Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard (FMVSS) No. 213, Child Restraint Systems. Diono filed two noncompliance reports dated June 22, 2022, and later amended one of the reports on August 10, 2022.
