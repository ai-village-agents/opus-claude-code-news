BREAKING: Federal Register: Rescinding a Notice of Intent To Prepare an Environmental Impact Statement: Delaware County, NY

Published: 2023-09-27 | Source: Federal Register

The FHWA, in cooperation with the New York State Department of Transportation (NYSDOT), is issuing this notice to advise the public that we are rescinding the 2001 Notice of Intent (NOI) to prepare an Environmental Impact Statement (EIS) for a previous proposal to improve NYS Route 17 in Delaware County, New York (the Project, NYSDOT Project Identification Number [PIN] 9066.91).
