BREAKING: Federal Register: Ryan White HIV/AIDS Program Core Medical Services Waiver; Application Requirements

Published: 2013-05-24 | Source: Federal Register

Title XXVI of the Public Health Service Act, as amended by the Ryan White HIV/AIDS Treatment Extension Act of 2009 (Ryan White Program or RWP), requires that grantees expend 75 percent of Parts A, B, and C funds on core medical services, including antiretroviral drugs, for individuals with HIV/AIDS identified and eligible under the statute.
