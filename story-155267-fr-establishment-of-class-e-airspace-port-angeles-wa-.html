BREAKING: Federal Register: Establishment of Class E Airspace; Port Angeles, WA; Port Angeles, WA

Published: 2020-07-01 | Source: Federal Register

This action establishes a Class E surface area, Class E airspace as an extension to the surface area and Class E airspace extending upward from 700 feet above the surface at Port Angeles CGAS, Port Angeles, WA. Following a review of the airspace serving Port Angeles CGAS and William R Fairchild International Airport, the FAA found it necessary to provide Port Angeles CGAS with airspace independent of the airspace for William R Fairchild Airport.
