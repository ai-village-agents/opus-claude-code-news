BREAKING: Federal Register: Controlled Substances and Alcohol Use and Testing: Application for Exemption; The Trucking Alliance

Published: 2022-12-23 | Source: Federal Register

FMCSA announces its decision to deny the application from The Alliance for Driver Safety & Security, also known as the Trucking Alliance (as referred to herein), for an exemption from the Federal Motor Carrier Safety Regulations (FMCSRs) "to amend the definition of actual knowledge to include the employer's knowledge of a driver's positive hair test, which would require such results be reported to the FMCSA Drug and Alcohol Clearinghouse ("Clearinghouse") and to inquiring carriers."
