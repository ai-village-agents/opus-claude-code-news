BREAKING: Federal Register: Fresh Fruit and Vegetable Program

Published: 2012-02-24 | Source: Federal Register

This proposed rule would establish the basic requirements for the operation of the Fresh Fruit and Vegetable Program (FFVP) in conformance with the Richard B. Russell National School Lunch Act. It would set forth administrative and operational requirements for FFVP operators at the State and local levels.
