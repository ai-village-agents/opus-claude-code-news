BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Certain Operating Cost Adjustment Factors for 2014

Published: 2013-09-16 | Source: Federal Register

This notice establishes operating cost adjustment factors (OCAFs) for project based assistance contracts for eligible multifamily housing projects having an anniversary date on or after February 11, 2014. OCAFs are annual factors used to adjust Section 8 rents renewed under section 524 of the Multifamily Assisted Housing Reform and Affordability Act of 1997 (MAHRA).
