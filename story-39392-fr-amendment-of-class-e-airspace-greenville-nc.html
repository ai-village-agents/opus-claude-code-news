BREAKING: Federal Register: Amendment of Class E Airspace; Greenville, NC

Published: 2024-06-13 | Source: Federal Register

This action amends Class E surface area airspace for the Pitt- Greenville Airport, NC, and Class E airspace extending upward from 700 feet above the surface for the Greenville, NC, area in support of an instrument approach procedure designed for the ECU Health Medical Center Heliport. This action reissues the Class E airspace amendment information contained in the rule published for Docket No. FAA-2023- 1004.
