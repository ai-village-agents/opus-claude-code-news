BREAKING: Federal Register: Questions and Answers Regarding Mandatory Cosmetics Recalls: Draft Guidance for Industry; Availability

Published: 2025-12-18 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA or Agency) is announcing the availability of a draft guidance document entitled "Questions and Answers Regarding Mandatory Cosmetics Recalls: Guidance for Industry." The draft guidance document, when finalized, will provide answers to common questions that might arise about FDA's mandatory recall authority for cosmetics.
