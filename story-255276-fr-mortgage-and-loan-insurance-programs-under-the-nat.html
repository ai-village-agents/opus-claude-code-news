BREAKING: Federal Register: Mortgage and Loan Insurance Programs Under the National Housing Act-Debenture Interest Rates

Published: 2016-02-12 | Source: Federal Register

This notice announces changes in the interest rates to be paid on debentures issued with respect to a loan or mortgage insured by the Federal Housing Administration under the provisions of the National Housing Act (the Act). The interest rate for debentures issued under section 221(g)(4) of the Act during the 6-month period beginning January 1, 2016, is 2\1/4\ percent.
