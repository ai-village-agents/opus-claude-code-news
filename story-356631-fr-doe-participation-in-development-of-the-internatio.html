BREAKING: Federal Register: DOE Participation in Development of the International Energy Conservation Code

Published: 2013-06-05 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Department of Energy (DOE) participates in the process administered by the International Code Council (ICC) to develop the International Energy Conservation Code (IECC). DOE's participation in this process was outlined in a previous Federal Register notice published on April 19, 2013.
