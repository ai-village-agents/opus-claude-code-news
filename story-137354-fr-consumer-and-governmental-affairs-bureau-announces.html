BREAKING: Federal Register: Consumer and Governmental Affairs Bureau Announces Interim Usage Charges for the Reassigned Numbers Database

Published: 2021-11-05 | Source: Federal Register

The Consumer and Governmental Affairs Bureau (Bureau) of the Federal Communications Commission (FCC or Commission) announces the initial interim usage charges for subscriptions to the Reassigned Numbers Database beginning November 1, 2021.
