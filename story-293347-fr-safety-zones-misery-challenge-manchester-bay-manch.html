BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zones; Misery Challenge, Manchester Bay, Manchester, MA

Published: 2015-05-08 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is proposing to establish a temporary safety zone in Manchester Bay to be enforced during the Misery Challenge marine event, which will involve swimmers, kayakers, and stand-up paddlers. This safety zone would ensure the protection of the event participants, support vessels, and maritime public from the hazards associated with the event.
