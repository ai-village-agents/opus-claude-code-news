BREAKING: Federal Register: Drawbridge Operation Regulations; Tchefuncta River, Madisonville, LA

Published: 2016-11-04 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard has issued a temporary deviation from the operating schedule that governs the State Route 22 Bridge (Madisonville (SR22) swing span bridge) across the Tchefuncta River, mile 2.5, at Madisonville, St. Tammany Parish, Louisiana. This deviation will test a change to the drawbridge operation schedule to determine whether a permanent change to the schedule is needed.
