BREAKING: Federal Register: Special Local Regulation; Red Bull Air Race-San Diego 2017; San Diego Bay, CA

Published: 2017-04-13 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is temporarily establishing special local regulations for the Red Bull Air Race--San Diego 2017 event held on the navigable waters of San Diego Bay, California. This action is necessary to provide for the safety of life on navigable waters during the event.
