BREAKING: Federal Register: Air Plan Approval; North Carolina: New Source Review for Fine Particulate Matter (PM2.5)

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is approving changes to the North Carolina State Implementation Plan (SIP), submitted by the North Carolina Department of Environmental Quality (NC DEQ) through the Division of Air Quality, to EPA through a letter dated October 17, 2017.
