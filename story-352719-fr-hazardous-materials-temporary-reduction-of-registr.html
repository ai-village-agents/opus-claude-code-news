BREAKING: Federal Register: Hazardous Materials; Temporary Reduction of Registration Fees

Published: 2013-04-19 | Source: Federal Register

The Federal hazardous materials transportation law requires DOT to adjust the amount of the annual registration fee to account for any unexpended balance in the Hazardous Materials Emergency Preparedness (HMEP) Fund.
