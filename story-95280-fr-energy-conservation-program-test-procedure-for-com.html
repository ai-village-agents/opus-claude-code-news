BREAKING: Federal Register: Energy Conservation Program: Test Procedure for Compressors

Published: 2022-05-06 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Department of Energy ("DOE") is initiating a review through this RFI to consider whether to amend DOE's test procedure for compressors. To inform interested parties and to facilitate this process, DOE has identified certain issues associated with the currently applicable test procedure on which DOE is interested in receiving comment.
