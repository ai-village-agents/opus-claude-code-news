BREAKING: Federal Register: Reporting, Procedures and Penalties Regulations; Transnational Criminal Organizations Sanctions Regulations

Published: 2012-01-12 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of the Treasury's Office of Foreign Assets Control ("OFAC") is issuing regulations to implement Executive Order 13581 of July 24, 2011 ("Blocking Property of Transnational Criminal Organizations"). OFAC intends to supplement this part 590 with a more comprehensive set of regulations, which may include additional interpretive and definitional guidance and additional general licenses and statements of licensing policy.
