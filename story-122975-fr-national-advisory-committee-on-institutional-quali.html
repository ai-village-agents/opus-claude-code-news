BREAKING: Federal Register: National Advisory Committee on Institutional Quality and Integrity (NACIQI)

Published: 2021-05-05 | Source: Federal Register

At least one member of the National Advisory Committee on Institutional Quality and Integrity (NACIQI) must be a student who, at the time of the appointment by the Secretary of Education, is attending an institution of higher education.
