BREAKING: Federal Register: Chartering and Field of Membership Manual

Published: 2016-11-09 | Source: Federal Register

The NCUA Board proposes to amend its chartering and field of membership rules to give applicants for community charter approval, expansion or conversion the option, in lieu of a presumptive community, to submit a narrative to establish common interests or interaction among residents of the area it proposes to serve, thus qualifying the area as a well-defined local community.
