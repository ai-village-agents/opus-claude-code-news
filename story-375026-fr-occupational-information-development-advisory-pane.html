BREAKING: Federal Register: Occupational Information Development Advisory Panel

Published: 2012-01-17 | Source: Federal Register

Notice is hereby given that on January 6, 2012; our Commissioner renewed the Charter for the Occupational Information Development Advisory Panel (Panel). This discretionary Panel will provide independent advice and recommendations on plans and activities to create an occupational information system (OIS) tailored specifically for our disability programs and adjudicative needs.
