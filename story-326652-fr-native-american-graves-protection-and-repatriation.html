BREAKING: Federal Register: Native American Graves Protection and Repatriation Act Regulations, Definition of Indian Tribe

Published: 2014-06-11 | Source: Federal Register

The Department is confirming the interim final rule published and effective on July 5, 2011, removing the definition of Indian tribe because it is inconsistent with the statutory definition of that term. The July 5, 2011, publication stated that we would review comments on the interim final rule and either confirm the rule or initiate a proposed rulemaking. We are confirming the rule without change.
