BREAKING: Federal Register: 2-Isobutyl-2-methyl-1,3-dioxolane-4-methanol; Exemption From the Requirement of a Tolerance

Published: 2022-02-07 | Source: Federal Register

This regulation establishes an exemption from the requirement of a tolerance for residues of 2-isobutyl-2-methyl-1,3-dioxolane-4- methanol (CAS Reg. No. 5660-53-7) when used as an inert ingredient (solvent/cosolvent) on growing crops and raw agricultural commodities after harvest, and in antimicrobial formulations applied to food- contact surfaces in public eating places, dairy-processing equipment, and food-processing equipment and utensils.
