BREAKING: Federal Register: Setting the Manner for the Appearance of Parties and Witnesses at a Hearing

Published: 2019-12-18 | Source: Federal Register

We are publishing a final rule we proposed in November 2018 regarding setting the time, place, and manner of appearance for hearings at the administrative law judge (ALJ) level of our administrative review process, with modifications. Our final rule states that we (the agency) will determine how parties and witnesses will appear at a hearing before an ALJ, and that we will set the time and place for the hearing accordingly.
