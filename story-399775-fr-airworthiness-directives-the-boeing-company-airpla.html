BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; The Boeing Company Airplanes

Published: 2012-10-23 | Source: Federal Register

We are adopting a new airworthiness directive (AD) for certain The Boeing Company Model 767-200, -300, -300F, and -400ER series airplanes. This AD was prompted by a design review following a ground fire incident and reports of flammable fluid leaks from the wing leading edge area onto the engine exhaust area.
