BREAKING: Federal Register: Protection of Stratospheric Ozone: Notice of Revocation and Voluntary Withdrawals of Programs From EPA's Section 608 Technician Certifying Program

Published: 2016-05-10 | Source: Federal Register

EPA is removing programs that were revoked and those that submitted voluntary withdrawals from its list of Section 608 Technician Certification Programs approved to provide the technician certification exam. EPA's list is available at: http://www.epa.gov/section608/ section-608-technician-certification-programs.
