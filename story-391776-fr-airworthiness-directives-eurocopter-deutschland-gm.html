BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Eurocopter Deutschland GmbH Helicopters

Published: 2012-07-23 | Source: Federal Register

We are adopting a new airworthiness directive (AD) for Eurocopter Deutschland GmbH (ECD) Model MBB-BK 117 (all versions) and BO-105LS A-3 helicopters. This AD requires inspecting the tail rotor pitch link spherical bearing for proper swaging. This AD is prompted by a report of a tail rotor pitch link with a spherical bearing that had migrated out of the bearing bore.
