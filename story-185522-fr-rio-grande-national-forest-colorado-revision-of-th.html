BREAKING: Federal Register: Rio Grande National Forest; Colorado; Revision of the Land Management Plan for the Rio Grande National Forest

Published: 2019-08-02 | Source: Federal Register

The Forest Service is revising the Rio Grande National Forest's Land Management Plan (Forest Plan). The Forest Service has prepared a Final Environmental Impact Statement (FEIS) for its revised Forest Plan and a draft Record of Decision (ROD). This notice is to inform the public that the Rio Grande National Forest is initiating a 60-day period where individuals or entities with specific concerns about the Rio Grande National Forest's revised Forest Plan and the associated FEIS may file objection...
