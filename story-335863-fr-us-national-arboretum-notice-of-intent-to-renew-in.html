BREAKING: Federal Register: U.S. National Arboretum; Notice of Intent To Renew Information Collection

Published: 2014-09-26 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Agriculture (USDA) seeks comments on the intent of the U.S. National Arboretum (USNA) to renew an information collection that expires February 28, 2015. The information collection serves as a means to collect for certain use of the facilities, grounds, programs and services. This includes fees for educational programs and workshops and for use of the grounds and facilities, as well as for commercial photography and cinematography.
