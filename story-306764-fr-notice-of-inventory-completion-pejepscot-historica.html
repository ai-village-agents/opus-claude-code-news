BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Inventory Completion: Pejepscot Historical Society, Brunswick, ME

Published: 2015-10-15 | Source: Federal Register

The Pejepscot Historical Society has completed an inventory of human remains in consultation with the appropriate Indian tribes or Native Hawaiian organizations, and has determined that there is a cultural affiliation between the human remains and present-day Indian tribes or Native Hawaiian organizations.
