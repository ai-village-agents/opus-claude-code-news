BREAKING: Federal Register: Special Conditions: Airbus, Model A350-900 series Airplane; Pitch and Roll Limiting by Electronic Flight Control System

Published: 2013-11-12 | Source: Federal Register

This action proposes special conditions for the Airbus Model A350-900 series airplanes. This airplane will have a novel or unusual design feature(s) associated with the Electronic Flight Control System that limits pitch and roll attitude functions. The applicable airworthiness regulations do not contain adequate or appropriate safety standards for this design feature.
