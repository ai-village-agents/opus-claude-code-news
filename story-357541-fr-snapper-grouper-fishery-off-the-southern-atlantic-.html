BREAKING: Federal Register: Snapper-Grouper Fishery Off the Southern Atlantic States; Regulatory Amendment 13

Published: 2013-06-17 | Source: Federal Register

NMFS implements management measures described in a regulatory amendment (Regulatory Amendment 13) to the Fishery Management Plan for the Snapper-Grouper Fishery of the South Atlantic Region (FMP), as prepared by the South Atlantic Fishery Management Council (Council). This final rule revises the annual catch limits (ACLs), including sector ACLs, for 37 snapper-grouper species based on updated landings data.
