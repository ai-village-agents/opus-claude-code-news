BREAKING: Federal Register: Customs Broker Verification of an Importer's Identity

Published: 2019-08-14 | Source: Federal Register

This rule proposes to amend the U.S. Customs and Border Protection (CBP) regulations to require customs brokers to collect certain information from importers to enable the customs brokers to verify the identity of importers, including nonresident importers.
