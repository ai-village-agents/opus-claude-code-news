BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zones; Fireworks Displays in the Fifth Coast Guard District, Brick Township, NJ

Published: 2025-07-08 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard will enforce a safety zone for the Township of Brick Summer Fest fireworks displays on four dates in July. This action is being taken to provide for the safety of life on navigable water during each of the three separate land-based fireworks displays. Our regulation for marine events within the Fifth Coast Guard District identifies the regulated area for this event, located in in Brick, NJ.
