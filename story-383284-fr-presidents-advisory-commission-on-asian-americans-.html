BREAKING: Federal Register: President's Advisory Commission on Asian Americans and Pacific Islanders

Published: 2012-04-17 | Source: Federal Register

This notice sets forth the schedule and agenda of the meeting of the President's Advisory Commission on Asian Americans and Pacific Islanders (Commission). The notice also describes the functions of the Commission. Notice of the meeting is required by section 10 (a) (2) of the Federal Advisory Committee Act and intended to notify the public of its opportunity to attend. Date: May 7, 2012.
