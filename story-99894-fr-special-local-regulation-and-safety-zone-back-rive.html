BREAKING: Federal Register: Special Local Regulation and Safety Zone; Back River, Baltimore County, MD

Published: 2022-07-07 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is establishing temporary regulations for certain waters of the Back River. This action is necessary to provide for the safety of life on these navigable waters near Baltimore County, MD, during a fireworks display on July 16, 2022. This regulation prohibits persons and vessels from being in the regulated area and safety zone unless authorized by the Captain of the Port, Maryland- National Capital Region or a designated representative.
