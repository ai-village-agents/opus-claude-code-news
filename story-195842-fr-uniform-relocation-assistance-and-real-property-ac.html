BREAKING: Federal Register: Uniform Relocation Assistance and Real Property Acquisition for Federal and Federally Assisted Programs

Published: 2019-12-18 | Source: Federal Register

The FHWA is proposing to amend its Uniform Relocation Assistance and Real Property Acquisition Policies Act of 1970 (Uniform Act) regulations. The revisions are prompted by enactment of the Moving Ahead for Progress in the 21st Century Act (MAP-21), which increases statutory relocation benefits and reduces length of occupancy requirements.
