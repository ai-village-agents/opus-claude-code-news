BREAKING: Federal Register: Approval and Promulgation of Air Quality Implementation Plans; Indiana; Consent Decree Requirements

Published: 2014-01-17 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is approving a portion of Indiana's construction permit rule for sources subject to the state operating permit program regulations. These provisions authorize the state to incorporate terms from Federal consent decrees and Federal district court orders into these construction permits.
