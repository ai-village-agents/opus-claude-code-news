BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Bell Helicopter Textron Inc. Helicopters

Published: 2018-08-02 | Source: Federal Register

We propose to supersede airworthiness directive (AD) 2015-04- 04 for Bell Helicopter Textron Inc. (Bell) Model 412 and 412EP helicopters. AD 2015-04-04 requires revising the Rotorcraft Flight Manual (RFM) and installing a placard to limit flights to visual flight rules (VFR) and prohibiting night operations because of failing inverters.
