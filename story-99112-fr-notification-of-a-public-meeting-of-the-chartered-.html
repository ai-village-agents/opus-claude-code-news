BREAKING: Federal Register: Notification of a Public Meeting of the Chartered Science Advisory Board

Published: 2022-06-27 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) Science Advisory Board (SAB) Staff Office announces a public meeting of the chartered Science Advisory Board. The chartered SAB will meet to conduct quality reviews of two draft SAB reports: Review of EPA's Analyses to Support EPA's National Primary Drinking Water Rulemaking for PFAS, and Review of the EPA's Draft Fifth Contaminant Candidate List (CCL 5).
