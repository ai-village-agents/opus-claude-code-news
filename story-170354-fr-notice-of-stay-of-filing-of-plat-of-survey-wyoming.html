BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Stay of Filing of Plat of Survey, Wyoming

Published: 2019-02-04 | Source: Federal Register

The Bureau of Land Management (BLM) has placed a stay on the filing of a plat of survey of the following described land, pending consideration of the protest and/or appeal that was filed within 30 calendar days of publication in this Federal Register. A plat will not be officially filed until after disposition of protest and/or appeal.
