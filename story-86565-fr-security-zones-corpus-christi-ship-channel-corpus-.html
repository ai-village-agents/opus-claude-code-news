BREAKING: Federal Register: Security Zones; Corpus Christi Ship Channel, Corpus Christi, TX

Published: 2022-01-18 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is establishing two temporary, 500-yard radius, moving security zones for certain Liquefied Natural Gas Carriers (LNGC) within the Corpus Christi Ship Channel and La Quinta Channel. The security zones are needed to protect the vessels and the marine environment from potential hazards created by Liquefied Natural Gas (LNG) cargo aboard the vessels.
