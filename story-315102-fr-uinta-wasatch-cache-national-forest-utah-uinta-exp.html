BREAKING: Federal Register: Uinta-Wasatch-Cache National Forest; Utah; Uinta Express Pipeline Project

Published: 2014-01-29 | Source: Federal Register

Uinta Express Pipeline Company LLC, a subsidiary of Tesoro Refining & Marketing Company LLC, has submitted a special use application requesting authorization to construct and maintain a crude oil pipeline from the Uinta Basin to Salt Lake City, Utah. The project is known as the Uinta Express Pipeline ("Project").
