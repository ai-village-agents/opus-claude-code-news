BREAKING: Federal Register: Drug Interaction Information in Human Prescription Drug and Biological Product Labeling-Content and Format; Draft Guidance for Industry; Availability

Published: 2024-10-22 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA or Agency) is announcing the availability of a draft guidance for industry entitled "Drug Interaction Information in Human Prescription Drug and Biological Product Labeling." The draft guidance is intended to assist applicants in developing the DRUG INTERACTIONS section of labeling as described in FDA regulations for the content and format of labeling for human prescription drug and biological products.
