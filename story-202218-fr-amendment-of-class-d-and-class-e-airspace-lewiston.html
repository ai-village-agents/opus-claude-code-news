BREAKING: Federal Register: Amendment of Class D and Class E Airspace; Lewiston, ID

Published: 2018-03-15 | Source: Federal Register

This actions amends controlled airspace at Lewiston-Nez Perce County Airport, Lewiston, ID, by enlarging Class D airspace, and Class E surface airspace, and reducing Class E airspace designated as an extension, and Class E airspace extending upward from 700 feet above the surface. Also, this action removes the part-time Notice to Airmen (NOTAM) status from Class E airspace designated as an extension.
