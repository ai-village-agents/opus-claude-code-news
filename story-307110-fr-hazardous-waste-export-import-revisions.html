BREAKING: Federal Register: Hazardous Waste Export-Import Revisions

Published: 2015-10-19 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is proposing to amend our existing regulations in regards to the export and import of hazardous wastes from and into the United States.
