BREAKING: Federal Register: Disturbance Control Standard-Contingency Reserve for Recovery From a Balancing Contingency Event Reliability Standard

Published: 2016-05-26 | Source: Federal Register

The Federal Energy Regulatory Commission proposes to approve Reliability Standard BAL-002-2 (Disturbance Control Standard-- Contingency Reserve for Recovery from a Balancing Contingency Event) submitted by the North American Electric Reliability Corporation (NERC). Proposed Reliability Standard BAL-002-2 is designed to ensure that applicable entities balance resources and demand and return their Area Control Error to defined values following a Reportable Balancing Contingency Event.
