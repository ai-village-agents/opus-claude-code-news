BREAKING: Federal Register: Mexican Hass Avocado Import Program

Published: 2015-02-18 | Source: Federal Register

Commercial consignments of Hass avocado fruit are currently authorized entry into the continental United States, Hawaii, and Puerto Rico from the Mexican State of Michoac[aacute]n under a systems approach to mitigate against quarantine pests of concern.
