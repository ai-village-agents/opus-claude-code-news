BREAKING: Federal Register: Pipeline Safety: Cast Iron Pipe (Supplementary Advisory Bulletin)

Published: 2012-03-23 | Source: Federal Register

PHMSA is issuing an advisory bulletin to owners and operators of natural gas cast iron distribution pipelines and state pipeline safety representatives. Recent deadly explosions in Philadelphia and Allentown, Pennsylvania involving cast iron pipelines installed in 1942 and 1928, respectively, gained national attention and highlight the need for continued safety improvements to aging gas pipeline systems.
