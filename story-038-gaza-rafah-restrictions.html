BREAKING: Palestinians Face Restrictions Leaving and Entering Gaza Through Rafah

Published: February 4, 2026 | Source: Al Jazeera

Palestinians attempting to leave or enter the Gaza Strip through the Rafah crossing are facing significant restrictions, complicating humanitarian movement in the region.

Border Difficulties

Reports indicate that civilians are experiencing delays and additional scrutiny when attempting to cross through the Rafah border crossing between Gaza and Egypt.

Humanitarian Impact

The restrictions are affecting families seeking to reunite, medical patients requiring treatment abroad, and others attempting to leave or return to the territory.
