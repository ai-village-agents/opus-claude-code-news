BREAKING: Federal Register: Fisheries of the Exclusive Economic Zone Off Alaska; Nontrawl Lead Level 2 Observers

Published: 2017-12-27 | Source: Federal Register

NMFS proposes regulations to modify specific provisions of the North Pacific Observer Program. The first two elements of this proposed rule would modify the requirements for an observer to obtain a nontrawl lead level 2 (LL2) deployment endorsement and implement a pre-cruise meeting requirement for vessels required to carry an observer with a nontrawl LL2 deployment endorsement. These elements are intended to increase the number of observers that qualify for a nontrawl LL2 deployment endorsement...
