BREAKING: Federal Register: Interim Final Determination To Stay Sanctions, Imperial County Air Pollution Control District

Published: 2013-01-07 | Source: Federal Register

EPA is making an interim final determination to stay imposition of sanctions based on a proposed approval of revisions to the Imperial County Air Pollution Control District (ICAPCD) portion of the California State Implementation Plan (SIP) published elsewhere in this Federal Register.
