BREAKING: Federal Register: Pipeline Safety: Pipeline Transportation; Hydrogen and Emerging Fuels Research and Development (R&D) Public Meeting and Forum

Published: 2021-10-21 | Source: Federal Register

This notice announces a virtual public meeting and forum titled: "Pipeline Transportation and Emerging Fuels R&D Public Meeting and Forum." The public meeting and forum will serve as an opportunity for pipeline stakeholders to discuss research gaps and challenges in pipeline safety and emerging fuels, including hydrogen transportation.
