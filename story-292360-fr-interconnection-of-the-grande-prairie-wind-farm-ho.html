BREAKING: Federal Register: Interconnection of the Grande Prairie Wind Farm, Holt County, Nebraska (DOE/EIS-0485)

Published: 2015-04-28 | Source: Federal Register

Western Area Power Administration (Western) received a request from Grande Prairie Wind, LLC (Grande Prairie Wind), a subsidiary of Geronimo Wind Energy, LLC d.b.a. Geronimo Energy, LLC to interconnect their proposed Grande Prairie Wind Farm (Project) to Western's power transmission system.
