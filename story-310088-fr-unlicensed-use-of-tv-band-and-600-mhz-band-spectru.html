BREAKING: Federal Register: Unlicensed Use of TV Band and 600 MHz Band Spectrum

Published: 2015-11-23 | Source: Federal Register

The Commission recently adopted rules to repurpose broadcast television spectrum for new wireless services via an incentive auction. This document modifies Commission rules for unlicensed wireless devices and wireless microphones in the reconstituted TV bands and the new 600 MHz band.
