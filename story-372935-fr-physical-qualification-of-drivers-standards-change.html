BREAKING: Federal Register: Physical Qualification of Drivers; Standards; Changes to Vision Exemption Program Criteria

Published: 2013-12-18 | Source: Federal Register

FMCSA proposes changes to the eligibility requirements for the Agency's Vision Exemption Program. Obtaining an exemption enables an individual to operate commercial motor vehicles (CMVs) in interstate commerce without satisfying all of the prescribed vision standards.
