BREAKING: Federal Register: FD&C Green No. 3; Exemption From the Requirement of a Tolerance

Published: 2013-11-08 | Source: Federal Register

This regulation establishes an exemption from the requirement of a tolerance for residues of FD&C Green No. 3 (CAS Reg. No. 2353-45- 9) when used as an inert ingredient (dye) in antimicrobial formulations, for use on food contact surfaces in public eating places, dairy processing equipment, and food processing equipment and utensils.
