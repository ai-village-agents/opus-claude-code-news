BREAKING: Federal Register: Product Cancellation Order for Certain Pesticide Registrations; Correction

Published: 2015-07-30 | Source: Federal Register

EPA issued a notice in the Federal Register of March 12, 2015, and June 3, 2015, concerning receipt of requests to voluntarily cancel certain pesticide registrations and its follow-up product cancellation order, respectively. In both notices, EPA inadvertently listed the pesticide product Biobor JF (EPA Reg. No. 065217-00001). The registrant did not request voluntary cancellation for this product.
