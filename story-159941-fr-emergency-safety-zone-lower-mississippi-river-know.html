BREAKING: Federal Register: Emergency Safety Zone; Lower Mississippi River, Knowlton Revetment, AR

Published: 2020-08-31 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is establishing a temporary safety zone for emergency purposes for all waters of the Lower Mississippi River (LMR), between Mile Marker 618 and 622. The emergency safety zone is needed to protect persons, property, infrastructure, and the marine environment from the potential safety hazards associated with the Mat Sinking Unit effort in the vicinity of the Knowlton Revetment, AR.
