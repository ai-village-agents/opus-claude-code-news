BREAKING: Federal Register: Military Credit Monitoring

Published: 2019-07-01 | Source: Federal Register

The Federal Trade Commission ("FTC" or "Commission") is publishing a final rule to implement the credit monitoring provisions applicable to active duty military consumers in section 302 of the Economic Growth, Regulatory Relief, and Consumer Protection Act, which amends the Fair Credit Reporting Act ("FCRA").
