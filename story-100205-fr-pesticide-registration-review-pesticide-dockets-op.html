BREAKING: Federal Register: Pesticide Registration Review; Pesticide Dockets Opened for Review and Comment; Notice of Availability

Published: 2022-07-12 | Source: Federal Register

This notice announces the availability of the EPA's preliminary work plans for the following chemicals: Ametoctradin, Fenpyrazamine, Picoxystrobin, and Trichoderma species. With this document, the EPA is opening the public comment period for registration review for these chemicals.
