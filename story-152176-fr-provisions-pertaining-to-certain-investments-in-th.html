BREAKING: Federal Register: Provisions Pertaining to Certain Investments in the United States by Foreign Persons

Published: 2020-05-21 | Source: Federal Register

This proposed rule would modify certain provisions in the regulations of the Committee on Foreign Investment in the United States that implement section 721 of the Defense Production Act of 1950, as amended by the Foreign Investment Risk Review Modernization Act of 2018.
