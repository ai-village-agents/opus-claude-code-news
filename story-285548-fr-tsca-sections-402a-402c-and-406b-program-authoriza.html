BREAKING: Federal Register: TSCA Sections 402(a), 402(c), and 406(b) Program Authorization

Published: 2015-02-04 | Source: Federal Register

On May 19, 2014, the Bois Forte Band of Chippewa (Bois Forte) submitted a complete application under section 404 of the Toxic Substances Control Act (TSCA) requesting authorization to administer and enforce the requirements for TSCA sections 402(a), 402(c), and 406(b) in accordance with the provisions of TSCA for trust lands located within the exterior boundaries of the reservation.
