BREAKING: Federal Register: Proposed Procedures for Conducting Hearings on Whether Acceptance Criteria in Combined Licenses Are Met

Published: 2014-04-18 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Nuclear Regulatory Commission (NRC) is developing generic procedures for conducting hearings on whether acceptance criteria in combined licenses are met. These acceptance criteria are part of the inspections, tests, analyses, and acceptance criteria (ITAAC) included in the combined license for a nuclear reactor.
