BREAKING: Federal Register: Perchloroethylene (PCE); Regulation Under the Toxic Substances Control Act (TSCA)

Published: 2024-12-18 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA or Agency) is finalizing a rule to address the unreasonable risk of injury to health presented by perchloroethylene (PCE) under its conditions of use. TSCA requires that EPA address by rule any unreasonable risk of injury to health or the environment identified in a TSCA risk evaluation and apply requirements to the extent necessary so that the chemical no longer presents unreasonable risk.
