BREAKING: Federal Register: Approval and Promulgation of Air Quality Implementation Plans; Vermont; Regional Haze

Published: 2012-02-28 | Source: Federal Register

EPA is proposing approval of a revision to the Vermont State Implementation Plan (SIP) submitted by the Vermont Department of Environmental Conservation (VT DEC) on August 26, 2009, with a supplemental submittal on January 3, 2012, that addresses regional haze for the first planning period from 2008 through 2018. This revision addresses the requirements of the Clean Air Act (CAA) and EPA's rules that require States to prevent any future, and remedy any existing, manmade impairment of visibility ...
