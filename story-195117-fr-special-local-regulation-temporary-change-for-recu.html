BREAKING: Federal Register: Special Local Regulation; Temporary Change for Recurring Marine Event in the Seventh Coast Guard District

Published: 2019-12-10 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is temporarily changing the enforcement period of a special local regulation for a recurring marine event in the Seventh Coast Guard District and adding a temporary safety zone for this event. These regulations apply to the St. Croix Christmas Boat Parade and Fireworks Display in the vicinity of Protestant Cay in St. Croix, USVI, which will take place this year on December 14, 2019.
