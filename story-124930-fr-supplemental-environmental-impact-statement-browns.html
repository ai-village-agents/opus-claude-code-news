BREAKING: Federal Register: Supplemental Environmental Impact Statement-Browns Ferry Nuclear Site Subsequent License Renewal

Published: 2021-06-01 | Source: Federal Register

The Tennessee Valley Authority (TVA) intends to prepare a Supplemental Environmental Impact Statement (SEIS) to address the potential environmental effects associated with obtaining subsequent license renewals (SLR) for the Browns Ferry Nuclear Plant (BFN) Units 1, 2, and 3 located in Limestone County, Alabama.
