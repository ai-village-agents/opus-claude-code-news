BREAKING: Federal Register: Reopening; Application Deadline for Fiscal Year 2015; Performance Partnership Pilots

Published: 2016-07-12 | Source: Federal Register

On April 26, 2016, we published in the Federal Register (81 FR 24573) a notice inviting applications (NIA) for the Fiscal Year (FY) 2015 Performance Partnership Pilots (P3) competition. The NIA established a deadline date of June 27, 2016, for the transmittal of applications. This notice reopens the competition until July 19, 2016.
