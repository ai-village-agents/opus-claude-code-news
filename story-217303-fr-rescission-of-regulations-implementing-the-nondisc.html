BREAKING: Federal Register: Rescission of Regulations Implementing the Nondiscrimination and Equal Opportunity Provisions of the Job Training Partnership Act of 1982

Published: 2018-09-26 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Department of Labor proposes to remove regulations for an inoperative program while continuing to require non- discrimination and equal-employment opportunity under its programs. The Department is undergoing a process of identifying regulations that are "outdated" and "unnecessary." The regulations to be rescinded by the proposed rule are "outdated" because they administer a program that no longer exists.
