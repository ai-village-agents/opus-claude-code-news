BREAKING: Federal Register: Determination of Attainment by the Attainment Date; Michigan; St. Clair 2010 Sulfur Dioxide Nonattainment Area

Published: 2024-09-26 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is proposing to determine that the St. Clair, MI sulfur dioxide (SO2) nonattainment area attained the 2010 1-hour primary SO2 national ambient air quality standard (NAAQS) by the date of September 12, 2021.
