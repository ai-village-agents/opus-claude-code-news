BREAKING: Federal Register: Tennessee Valley Authority; Clinch River Nuclear Site, Unit 1; Construction Permit Application

Published: 2025-07-15 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Nuclear Regulatory Commission (NRC) has accepted for review and docketed an application from Tennessee Valley Authority (TVA) for a construction permit for the Clinch River Nuclear Site, Unit 1 reactor to be built in Roane County, Tennessee. In addition, the NRC is providing notice that an uncontested hearing will be held on the TVA construction permit application at a time and place to be set in the future by the presiding officer for the uncontested hearing.
