BREAKING: Federal Register: Identification of Basket Contract Transactions as Listed Transactions

Published: 2024-07-12 | Source: Federal Register

This document contains proposed regulations that would identify transactions that are the same as, or substantially similar to, certain basket contract transactions as listed transactions, a type of reportable transaction. Material advisors and certain participants in these listed transactions would be required to file disclosures with the IRS and would be subject to penalties for failure to disclose.
