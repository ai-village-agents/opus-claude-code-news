BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Funds Availability Inviting Applications for the Rural Veterans Coordination; Amendment

Published: 2013-09-24 | Source: Federal Register

This notice extends the Department of Veterans Affairs (VA) application deadline for funds available under the Rural Veterans Coordination Pilot (RVCP) program. VA published a Notice of Funds Availability (NOFA) in the Federal Register on June 4, 2013 (78 FR 33472) to announce the availability of funds for applications beginning June 4, 2013 through July 19, 2013, 5 pm eastern standard time.
