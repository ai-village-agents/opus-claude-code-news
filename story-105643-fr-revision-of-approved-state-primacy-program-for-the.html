BREAKING: Federal Register: Revision of Approved State Primacy Program for the State of Arizona

Published: 2022-09-20 | Source: Federal Register

Notice is hereby given that the State of Arizona revised its approved State primacy program under the federal Safe Drinking Water Act (SDWA) by adopting regulations that effectuate the federal Filter Backwash Recycling Rule (FBRR), Ground Water Rule (GWR), and Radionuclides Rule.
