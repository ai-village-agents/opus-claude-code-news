BREAKING: Federal Register: Regulated Navigation Area; Monongahela, Allegheny, and Ohio Rivers, Pittsburgh, PA

Published: 2019-07-01 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard proposes to establish a regulated navigation area for certain waters of the Monongahela, Allegheny, and Ohio Rivers near Pittsburgh, Pennsylvania. This action is necessary to provide for the safety of persons, vessels, and the marine environment on these navigable waters due to the high volume of vessels navigating the area.
