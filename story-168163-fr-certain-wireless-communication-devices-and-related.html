BREAKING: Federal Register: Certain Wireless Communication Devices, and Related Components Thereof

Published: 2020-12-14 | Source: Federal Register

Notice is hereby given that the U.S. International Trade Commission ("Commission") has determined not to review an initial determination ("ID") (Order No. 48) of the presiding administrative law judge ("ALJ"), terminating the investigation as to respondents HTC Corporation and HTC America, Inc. This investigation is hereby terminated.
