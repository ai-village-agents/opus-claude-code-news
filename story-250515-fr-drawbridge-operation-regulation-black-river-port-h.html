BREAKING: Federal Register: Drawbridge Operation Regulation; Black River, Port Huron, MI

Published: 2017-12-11 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard proposes to modify the operating schedule that governs the Military Street Bridge, mile 0.33, the Seventh Street Bridge, mile 0.50, the Tenth Street Bridge, mile 0.94, and the Canadian National Railroad Bridge, mile 1.56, across the Black River at Port Huron, MI. The City of Port Huron requested the winter hours to be expanded for City-operated highway bridges.
