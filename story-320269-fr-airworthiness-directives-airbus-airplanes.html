BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Airbus Airplanes

Published: 2014-03-28 | Source: Federal Register

We are superseding Airworthiness Directive (AD) 2008-08-04 for certain Airbus Model A318, A319, A320, and A321 series airplanes. AD 2008-08-04 required repetitive inspections for cracking in the forward lug of the support rib 5 fitting of the left and right main landing gear (MLG), and repair or replacement of any cracked MLG fitting if necessary.
