BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Bombardier, Inc. Airplanes

Published: 2016-07-15 | Source: Federal Register

We propose to adopt a new airworthiness directive (AD) for certain Bombardier, Inc. Model BD-700-1A10 and BD-700-1A11 airplanes. This proposed AD was prompted by a determination that the existing instruction in a certain task in the aircraft maintenance manual (AMM) will not accomplish the intent of a certification maintenance requirement (CMR).
