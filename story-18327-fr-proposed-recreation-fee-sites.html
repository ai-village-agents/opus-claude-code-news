BREAKING: Federal Register: Proposed Recreation Fee Sites

Published: 2025-08-19 | Source: Federal Register

The Coconino National Forest is proposing to establish several recreation fee sites and a special recreation permit. Proposed recreation fees collected at the proposed recreation fee sites and for the proposed special recreation permit would be used for operation, maintenance, and improvement of the sites and the special recreation use covered by the proposed special recreation permit.
