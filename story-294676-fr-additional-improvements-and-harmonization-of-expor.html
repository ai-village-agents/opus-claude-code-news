BREAKING: Federal Register: Additional Improvements and Harmonization of Export Clearance Provisions

Published: 2015-05-22 | Source: Federal Register

The Bureau of Industry and Security (BIS) in this advanced notice of proposed rulemaking (ANPR) requests comments for how the export clearance requirements under the Export Administration Regulations (EAR) can be improved, including how the EAR export clearance provisions can be better harmonized with the export clearance requirements under the International Traffic in Arms Regulations (ITAR).
