BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Inventory Completion: Yale Peabody Museum of Natural History, New Haven, CT

Published: 2013-03-29 | Source: Federal Register

The Yale Peabody Museum of Natural History has completed an inventory of human remains and associated funerary objects, in consultation with the appropriate Indian tribes, and has determined that there is a cultural affiliation between the human remains and associated funerary objects and present-day Indian tribes.
