BREAKING: Federal Register: Light-Duty Vehicle GHG Program Technical Amendments

Published: 2018-10-01 | Source: Federal Register

EPA is proposing two technical corrections to the light-duty vehicle greenhouse gas (GHG) emissions standards regulations finalized in the 2012 rulemaking that established standards for model years 2017- 2025 light-duty vehicles. First, EPA proposes to correct regulations pertaining to how auto manufacturers must calculate credits for the GHG program's optional advanced technology incentives.
