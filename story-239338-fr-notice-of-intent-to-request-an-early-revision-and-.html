BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Intent To Request an Early Revision and Merger of Two Currently Approved Information Collections

Published: 2017-07-19 | Source: Federal Register

In accordance with the Paperwork Reduction Act of 1995 this notice announces the intention of the National Agricultural Statistics Service (NASS) to request revision to the currently approved information collection, the Bee and Honey survey docket (0535-0153). In addition NASS plans to merge this docket with the currently approved Colony Loss survey docket (0535-0255).
