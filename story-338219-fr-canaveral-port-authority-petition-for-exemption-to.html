BREAKING: Federal Register: Canaveral Port Authority-Petition for Exemption To Construct and Operate a Rail Line Extension to Port Canaveral, Florida

The Canaveral Port Authority (CPA) plans to file a request with the Surface Transportation Board (Board) pursuant to 49 U.S.C. 10502 for authority to construct and operate approximately 11 miles of new rail line to Port Canaveral (Port) in Brevard County, Florida.
