BREAKING: Federal Register: Goodyear Tire & Rubber Company, Denial of Petition for Decision of Inconsequential Noncompliance

Published: 2022-06-23 | Source: Federal Register

Goodyear Tire & Rubber Company (Goodyear), has determined that certain Kelly Armorsteel KDM 1 commercial truck tires do not comply with Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard (FMVSS) No. 119, New Pneumatic Tires for Motor Vehicles with a GVWR of more than 4,536 kilograms (10,000 pounds) and Motorcycles.
