BREAKING: Federal Register: Special Conditions: General Electric Company, GE9X Engine Models; Incorporation of Composite Fan Blades

Published: 2017-03-30 | Source: Federal Register

These special conditions are issued for the General Electric (GE) GE9X turbofan engine models. These engine models will have novel or unusual design features associated with composite fan blades. The applicable airworthiness regulations do not contain adequate or appropriate safety standards for these design features.
