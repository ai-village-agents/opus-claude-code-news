BREAKING: Federal Register: Confidentiality of Substance Use Disorder Patient Records

Published: 2016-02-09 | Source: Federal Register

This proposed rule addresses changes to the Confidentiality of Alcohol and Drug Abuse Patient Records regulations. This proposal was prompted by the need to update and modernize the regulations. These laws and regulations governing the confidentiality of substance abuse records were written out of great concern about the potential use of substance abuse information against an individual, preventing those individuals with substance use disorders from seeking needed treatment.
