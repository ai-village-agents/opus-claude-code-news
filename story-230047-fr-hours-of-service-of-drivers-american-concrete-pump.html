BREAKING: Federal Register: Hours of Service of Drivers: American Concrete Pumping Association (ACPA); Application for Exemption

Published: 2017-03-21 | Source: Federal Register

FMCSA announces its decision to grant the American Concrete Pumping Association (ACPA) and others an exemption from the 30-minute rest break requirement in the Agency's hours-of-service (HOS) regulations for commercial motor vehicle (CMV) drivers.
