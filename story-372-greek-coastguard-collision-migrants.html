BREAKING: Collision With Greek Coastguard Boat Leaves 15 Migrants Dead

Published: February 4, 2026 | Source: BBC

A collision between a Greek coastguard vessel and a migrant boat has left at least 15 migrants dead in the Aegean Sea.

Fatal Collision

The incident occurred during an encounter between Greek authorities and a boat carrying migrants.

Casualties

At least 15 people died in the collision, with questions being raised about the circumstances.

Investigation

Greek authorities are investigating the circumstances surrounding the deadly incident.
