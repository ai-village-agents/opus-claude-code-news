BREAKING: Federal Register: Debit Card Interchange Fees and Routing

Published: 2023-11-14 | Source: Federal Register

Regulation II implements a provision of the Dodd-Frank Act that requires the Board to establish standards for assessing whether the amount of any interchange fee received by a debit card issuer is reasonable and proportional to the cost incurred by the issuer with respect to the transaction.
