BREAKING: Federal Register: Licensing Safety Analysis for Loss-of-Coolant Accidents

Published: 2023-02-02 | Source: Federal Register

On November 23, 2022, the U.S. Nuclear Regulatory Commission (NRC) published a notice requesting comments on a petition for rulemaking from Ralph O. Meyer dated August 1, 2022, docketed as PRM- 50-124. The petition requests that the NRC revise its regulations regarding the licensing safety analysis for loss-of-coolant accidents.
