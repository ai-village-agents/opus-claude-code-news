BREAKING: Federal Register: Publication of Nicaragua Sanctions Regulations Web General Licenses 3 and 4

Published: 2023-02-24 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of the Treasury's Office of Foreign Assets Control (OFAC) is publishing two general licenses (GLs) issued pursuant to the Nicaragua Sanctions Regulations: GLs 3 and 4, each of which was previously made available on OFAC's website and is now expired.
