BREAKING: Federal Register: Agency Information Collection Activities: Assessing Equity in Work Requirements and SNAP Employment & Training

Published: 2023-09-06 | Source: Federal Register

In accordance with the Paperwork Reduction Act of 1995, this notice invites the general public and other public agencies to comment on this proposed information collection for the Assessing Equity in Work Requirements and SNAP [Supplemental Nutrition Assistance Program] Employment & Training study.
