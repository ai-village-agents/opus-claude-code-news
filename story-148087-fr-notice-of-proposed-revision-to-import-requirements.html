BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Proposed Revision to Import Requirements for the Importation of Fresh Citrus From South Africa Into the United States

Published: 2020-04-01 | Source: Federal Register

We are advising the public that we have prepared a commodity import evaluation document (CIED) relative to the importation into the United States of citrus (grapefruit, lemon, mandarin orange, sweet orange, tangelo, and Satsuma mandarin) fruit from South Africa. Based on the findings of the CIED, we are proposing to remove restrictions on the ports of entry into which citrus from South Africa may be imported into the United States.
