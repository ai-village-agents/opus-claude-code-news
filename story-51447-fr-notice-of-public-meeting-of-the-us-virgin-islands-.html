BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Public Meeting of the U.S. Virgin Islands Advisory Committee to the U.S. Commission on Civil Rights; Revision to Meeting Date and Time

Published: 2024-11-01 | Source: Federal Register

The Commission on Civil Rights published a notice in the Federal Register on October 7, 2024, regarding the November 12th business meeting of the U.S. Virgin Islands Advisory Committee. The notice can be found in FR Doc. 2024-23121, located in the second and third columns of page 81039 and continuing into the first column of page 81040. This document provides the correct date and time for the Committee's next meeting.
