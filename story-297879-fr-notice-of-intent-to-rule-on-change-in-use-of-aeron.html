BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Intent To Rule on Change in Use of Aeronautical Property at Sumner County Regional Airport, Gallatin, Tennessee

Published: 2015-07-01 | Source: Federal Register

The Federal Aviation Administration (FAA) is requesting public comment on a request by the Sumner County Regional Airport Authority of Gallatin, Tennessee, owner of the Sumner County Regional Airport, to change a portion of airport property from aeronautical to non- aeronautical use at the Sumner County Regional Airport.
