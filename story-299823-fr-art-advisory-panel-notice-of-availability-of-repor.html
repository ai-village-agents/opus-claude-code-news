BREAKING: Federal Register: Art Advisory Panel-Notice of Availability of Report of 2014 Closed Meetings

Published: 2015-07-24 | Source: Federal Register

Pursuant to 5 U.S.C. App. 2, section 10(d), of the Federal Advisory Committee Act, and 5 U.S.C. 552b, of the Government in the Sunshine Act, a report summarizing the closed meeting activities of the Art Advisory Panel during Fiscal Year 2014 has been prepared. A copy of this report has been filed with the Assistant Secretary for Management of the Department of the Treasury.
