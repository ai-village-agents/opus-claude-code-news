BREAKING: Federal Register: Extension of Compliance Dates for Medical Examiner's Certification Integration

Published: 2018-06-21 | Source: Federal Register

FMCSA amends its regulations to delay the compliance date from June 22, 2018, to June 22, 2021, for several provisions of its April 23, 2015 Medical Examiner's Certification Integration final rule.
