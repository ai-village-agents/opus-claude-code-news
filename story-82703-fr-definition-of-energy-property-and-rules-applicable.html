BREAKING: Federal Register: Definition of Energy Property and Rules Applicable to the Energy Credit

Published: 2023-11-22 | Source: Federal Register

This document contains proposed regulations that would amend the regulations relating to the energy credit for the taxable year in which eligible energy property is placed in service. This document also withdraws and reproposes, for additional clarity, portions of previously proposed regulations regarding the increased energy credit amount available if prevailing wage and registered apprenticeship requirements are met.
