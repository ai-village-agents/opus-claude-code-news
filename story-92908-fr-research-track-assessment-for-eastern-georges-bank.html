BREAKING: Federal Register: Research Track Assessment for Eastern Georges Bank and Georges Bank Haddock

Published: 2022-04-06 | Source: Federal Register

NMFS will convene the Research Track Assessment Peer Review Meeting for the purpose of reviewing Eastern Georges Bank (EGB) and Georges Bank (GB) haddock stocks. The Research Track Assessment Peer Review is a formal scientific peer-review process for evaluating and presenting stock assessment results to managers for fish stocks in the offshore U.S. and Canadian waters of the northwest Atlantic.
