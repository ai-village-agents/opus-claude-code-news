BREAKING: Federal Register: Clinical Trials Registration and Results Information Submission

Published: 2016-09-21 | Source: Federal Register

This final rule details the requirements for submitting registration and summary results information, including adverse event information, for specified clinical trials of drug products (including biological products) and device products and for pediatric postmarket surveillances of a device product to ClinicalTrials.gov, the clinical trial registry and results data bank operated by the National Library of Medicine (NLM) of the National Institutes of Health (NIH).
