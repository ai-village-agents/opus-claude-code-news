BREAKING: Federal Register: Certain Frozen Warmwater Shrimp From India: Rescission of Antidumping Duty Administrative Review; 2018-2019, in Part

Published: 2019-11-15 | Source: Federal Register

On May 2, 2019, the Department of Commerce (Commerce) initiated an administrative review on frozen warmwater shrimp from India for the period February 1, 2018, through January 31, 2019, for 254 companies. Because all interested parties timely withdrew their requests for administrative review for certain companies, we are rescinding this administrative review with respect to those companies.
