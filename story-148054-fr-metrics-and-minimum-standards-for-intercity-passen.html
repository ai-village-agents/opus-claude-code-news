BREAKING: Federal Register: Metrics and Minimum Standards for Intercity Passenger Rail Service

Published: 2020-03-31 | Source: Federal Register

FRA proposes metrics and minimum standards for measuring the performance and service quality of intercity passenger train operations. Consistent with the statutory mandate, FRA and Amtrak jointly developed the proposed metrics and minimum standards.
