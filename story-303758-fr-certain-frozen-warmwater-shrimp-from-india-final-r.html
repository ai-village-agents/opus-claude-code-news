BREAKING: Federal Register: Certain Frozen Warmwater Shrimp From India: Final Results of Antidumping Duty Administrative Review; 2013-2014

Published: 2015-09-10 | Source: Federal Register

On March 6, 2015, the Department of Commerce (the Department) published the preliminary results of the administrative review of the antidumping duty order on certain frozen warmwater shrimp from India.\1\ The period of review (POR) is February 1, 2013, through January 31, 2014. For the final results, we continue to find that all companies involved in this review sold subject merchandise at less than normal value. ---------------------------------------------------------------------------
