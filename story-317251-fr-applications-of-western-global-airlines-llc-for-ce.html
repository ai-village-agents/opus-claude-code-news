BREAKING: Federal Register: Applications of Western Global Airlines, LLC for Certificate Authority

Published: 2014-02-24 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Transportation is directing all interested persons to show cause why it should not issue orders finding Western Global Airlines, LLC fit, willing, and able, and awarding it certificates of public convenience and necessity authorizing it to engage in interstate and foreign charter air transportation of property and mail.
