BREAKING: Federal Register: Amendments to the Marine Radar Observer Refresher Training Regulations

Published: 2018-06-11 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard proposes to revise its merchant mariner credentialing regulations to remove obsolete portions of the radar observer requirements and harmonize the radar observer endorsement with the merchant mariner credential.
