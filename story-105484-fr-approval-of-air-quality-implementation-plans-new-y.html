BREAKING: Federal Register: Approval of Air Quality Implementation Plans; New York; Consumer Products

Published: 2022-09-16 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is approving a revision to the New York State Implementation Plan (SIP) for the purposes of implementing control of air pollution for volatile organic compounds (VOC). The SIP revision consists of amendments to New York's Codes, Rules and Regulations (NYCRR) that implement control measures for Consumer Products.
