BREAKING: Federal Register: Quinclorac; Pesticide Tolerances

Published: 2020-07-20 | Source: Federal Register

This regulation amends the tolerance for residues of quinclorac in or on rice, grain. BASF Corporation requested this tolerance amendment under the Federal Food, Drug, and Cosmetic Act (FFDCA).
