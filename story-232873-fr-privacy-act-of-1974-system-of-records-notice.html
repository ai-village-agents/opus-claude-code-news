BREAKING: Federal Register: Privacy Act of 1974; System of Records Notice

Published: 2017-04-26 | Source: Federal Register

The National Endowment for the Humanities ("NEH") is publishing a notice of its systems of records with descriptions of the systems and the ways in which they are maintained, as required by the Privacy Act of 1974. NEH's mission has not changed since its inception in 1965: It extends federal financial assistance to support humanities-related projects, conducted by both individuals and organizations.
