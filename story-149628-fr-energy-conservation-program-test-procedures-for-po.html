BREAKING: Federal Register: Energy Conservation Program: Test Procedures for Portable Air Conditioners; Correction

Published: 2020-04-20 | Source: Federal Register

On June 1, 2016, the U.S. Department of Energy ("DOE") published a final rule adopting test procedures for portable air conditioners ("June 2016 final rule"). A correction rule was subsequently published on October 14, 2016 ("October 2016 correction rule"), to correct typographical errors in the June 2016 final rule that were included in the regulatory text.
