BREAKING: Federal Register: Affordable Housing Program Amendments

Published: 2018-11-28 | Source: Federal Register

The Federal Housing Finance Agency (FHFA or Agency) is amending its regulation addressing requirements for the Federal Home Loan Banks' (Banks) Affordable Housing Program (AHP or Program).
