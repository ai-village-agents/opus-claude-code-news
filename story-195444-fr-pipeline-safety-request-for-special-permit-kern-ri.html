BREAKING: Federal Register: Pipeline Safety: Request for Special Permit; Kern River Gas Transmission Company

Published: 2019-12-13 | Source: Federal Register

PHMSA is publishing this notice to solicit public comments on two (2) requests received from the Kern River Gas Transmission Company to renew previously issued special permits. These special permit renewal requests are seeking relief from compliance with certain requirements in the Federal pipeline safety regulations.
