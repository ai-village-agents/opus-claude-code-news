BREAKING: Federal Register: Publication Method for Lists of Foreign Countries Eligible To Export Meat, Poultry, or Egg Products to the United States

Published: 2019-11-27 | Source: Federal Register

The Food Safety and Inspection Service (FSIS) is amending its regulations to remove lists of foreign countries eligible to export meat, poultry, and egg products to the United States. FSIS will maintain a single list of eligible foreign countries on its website. The criteria FSIS uses to evaluate whether a foreign country is eligible to export meat, poultry, or egg products has not changed.
