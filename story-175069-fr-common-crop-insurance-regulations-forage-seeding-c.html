BREAKING: Federal Register: Common Crop Insurance Regulations; Forage Seeding Crop Insurance Provisions; Withdrawal

Published: 2019-03-26 | Source: Federal Register

This action informs the public that the Federal Crop Insurance Corporation (FCIC) is withdrawing a final rule with request for comments published in the Federal Register on December 10, 2018, titled Common Crop Insurance Regulations; Forage Seeding Crop Insurance Provisions.
