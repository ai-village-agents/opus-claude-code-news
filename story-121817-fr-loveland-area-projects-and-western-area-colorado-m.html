BREAKING: Federal Register: Loveland Area Projects and Western Area Colorado Missouri Balancing Authority-Rate Order No. WAPA-197

Published: 2021-04-21 | Source: Federal Register

The Rocky Mountain Region (RMR) of the Western Area Power Administration (WAPA) proposes to convert its existing rates for short- term sales for RMR to use under the WEIS Market to new long-term formula rates for use October 1, 2021, through September 30, 2024. The existing formula rates (approved under the WAPA Administrator's short- term rate authority) expire on September 30, 2021.
