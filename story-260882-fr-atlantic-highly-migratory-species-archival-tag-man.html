BREAKING: Federal Register: Atlantic Highly Migratory Species; Archival Tag Management Measures

Published: 2016-04-14 | Source: Federal Register

NMFS is proposing to revise the regulations that currently require persons surgically implanting archival tags in Atlantic highly migratory species (HMS) or externally affixing archival tags to such species to obtain written authorization from NMFS and that require fishermen to report their catches of Atlantic HMS with such tags to NMFS.
