BREAKING: Federal Register: Default Electronic Disclosure by Employee Pension Benefit Plans Under ERISA

Published: 2019-10-23 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Labor is proposing in this document a new, additional safe harbor for the use of electronic media by employee benefit plans to furnish information to participants and beneficiaries of plans subject to the Employee Retirement Income Security Act of 1974 (ERISA). The proposal, if adopted, would allow plan administrators who satisfy specified conditions to provide participants and beneficiaries with a notice that certain disclosures will be made available on a website.
