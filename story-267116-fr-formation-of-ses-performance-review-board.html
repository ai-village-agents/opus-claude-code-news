BREAKING: Federal Register: Formation of SES Performance Review Board

Published: 2016-06-27 | Source: Federal Register

Section 4314(c)(1) through (5) of title 5 of the United States Code, requires each agency to establish in accordance with regulations prescribed by the Office of Personnel Management, one or more SES Performance Review Boards. Section 4314(c)(4) of title 5 requires that notice of appointment of board members be published in the Federal Register.
