BREAKING: Federal Register: 2022-2024 Single-Family and 2022 Multifamily Enterprise Housing Goals

Published: 2021-12-28 | Source: Federal Register

The Federal Housing Finance Agency (FHFA) is issuing a final rule on the single-family housing goals for Fannie Mae and Freddie Mac (the Enterprises) for 2022 through 2024, as well as the multifamily housing goals for 2022. The Federal Housing Enterprises Financial Safety and Soundness Act of 1992 (the Safety and Soundness Act) requires FHFA to establish annual housing goals for mortgages purchased by the Enterprises.
