BREAKING: Federal Register: Proposed Expansion of the Outer Coastal Plain Viticultural Area

Published: 2016-09-20 | Source: Federal Register

The Alcohol and Tobacco Tax and Trade Bureau (TTB) proposes to expand the approximately 2.25 million-acre "Outer Coastal Plain" viticultural area in southeastern New Jersey by approximately 32,932 acres. The established Outer Coastal Plain viticultural area and the proposed expansion area do not lie within any other viticultural area. TTB designates viticultural areas to allow vintners to better describe the origin of their wines and to allow consumers to better identify wines they may purchase....
