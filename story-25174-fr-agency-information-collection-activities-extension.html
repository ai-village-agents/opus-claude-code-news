BREAKING: Federal Register: Agency Information Collection Activities; Extension of Collection; Comment Request; Publicly Available Consumer Product Safety Information Database

Published: 2025-12-16 | Source: Federal Register

As required by the Paperwork Reduction Act of 1995 (PRA), the Consumer Product Safety Commission (CPSC or Commission) requests comments on a proposed extension of approval of information collection requirements associated with the Publicly Available Consumer Product Safety Information Database. The Office of Management and Budget (OMB) previously approved the collection of information under control number 3041-0146. OMB's approval will expire on March 31, 2026.
