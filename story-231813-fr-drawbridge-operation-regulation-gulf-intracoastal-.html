BREAKING: Federal Register: Drawbridge Operation Regulation; Gulf Intracoastal Waterway, Belle Chasse, Louisiana

Published: 2017-04-12 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard has issued a temporary deviation from the operating schedule that governs the State Route 23 Bridge across the Gulf Intracoastal Waterway (Algiers Alternate Route), mile 3.8, at Belle Chasse, Plaquemines Parish, Louisiana. The deviation is necessary to facilitate movement of vehicular traffic for the 2017 New Orleans Air Show to be held at the U.S. Naval Air Station, Joint Reserve Base at Belle Chasse, Louisiana.
