BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; The Boeing Company Airplanes

Published: 2013-08-05 | Source: Federal Register

We propose to adopt a new airworthiness directive (AD) for certain The Boeing Company Model 737-600, -700, -800, -900, and -900ER airplanes modified by particular supplemental type certificates (STC). This proposed AD was prompted by reports of cracks found during inspections of the in-flight entertainment system radome assembly.
