BREAKING: Federal Register: Southwestern Region: Invasive Plant Control Project, Carson and Santa Fe National Forests, New Mexico

Published: 2013-10-30 | Source: Federal Register

On December 15, 2000, the USDA Forest Service published a notice of intent (NOI) in the Federal Register (65 FR 78464) to prepare an environmental impact statement (EIS) for controlling invasive plants in the Carson and Santa Fe National Forests. The agency published a notice of availability (NOA) for the Draft EIS in the Federal Register (69 FR 42722) on July 16, 2004.
