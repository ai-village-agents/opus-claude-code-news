BREAKING: Federal Register: Labeling for Biosimilar Products; Guidance for Industry; Availability

Published: 2018-07-19 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA or Agency) is announcing the availability of a final guidance for industry entitled "Labeling for Biosimilar Products." This guidance is intended to help applicants develop draft labeling for proposed biosimilar products.
