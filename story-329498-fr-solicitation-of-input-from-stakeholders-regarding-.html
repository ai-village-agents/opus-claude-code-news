BREAKING: Federal Register: Solicitation of Input From Stakeholders Regarding Centers of Excellence, Implementation

Published: 2014-07-15 | Source: Federal Register

As part of the National Institute of Food and Agriculture's (NIFA) strategy to successfully implement Section 7214 of Public Law 113-79, the Agricultural Act of 2014, NIFA is soliciting stakeholder input on how it will provide priority to Centers of Excellence in the receipt of funding from its competitive research or extension programs.
