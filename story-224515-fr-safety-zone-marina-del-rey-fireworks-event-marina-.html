BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone; Marina Del Rey Fireworks Event; Marina Del Rey, California

Published: 2018-12-31 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Coast Guard is establishing a temporary safety zone in Marina Del Rey Harbor around the fireworks launch site located at the south jetty. This temporary safety zone is necessary to provide for the safety of the waterway users by keeping them clear of potentially harmful debris within the fall out zone during the fireworks displays scheduled to take place within Marina Del Rey harbor on December 31, 2018 and January 1, 2019.
