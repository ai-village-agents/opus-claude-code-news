BREAKING: Federal Register: Drawbridge Operation Regulation; Atlantic Intracoastal Waterway, Ormond Beach, FL

Published: 2018-01-19 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard has issued a temporary deviation from the operating schedule that governs the Highbridge Road (Knox) Bridge across the Atlantic Intracoastal Waterway (Halifax River), mile 816.0, at Ormond Beach, FL. The deviation is necessary to accommodate the replacement of trunnion bearings for the west bascule leaf. This deviation allows the bridge single-leaf operations, reducing the horizontal clearance to 45 feet.
