BREAKING: Federal Register: Effluent Limitations Guidelines and Standards for the Steam Electric Power Generating Point Source Category

Published: 2015-11-03 | Source: Federal Register

This final rule, promulgated under the Clean Water Act (CWA), protects public health and the environment from toxic metals and other harmful pollutants, including nutrients, by strengthening the technology-based effluent limitations guidelines and standards (ELGs) for the steam electric power generating industry.
