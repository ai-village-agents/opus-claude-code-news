BREAKING: Federal Register: Advisory Committee on the Medical Uses of Isotopes; Call for Nominations

Published: 2019-02-20 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Nuclear Regulatory Commission (NRC) is advertising for nominations for the Nuclear Medicine Physician position and the Patients' Rights Advocate position on the Advisory Committee on the Medical Uses of Isotopes (ACMUI). Nuclear Medicine Physician nominees should currently be practicing nuclear medicine in a clinical setting.
