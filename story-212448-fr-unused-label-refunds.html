BREAKING: Federal Register: Unused Label Refunds

Published: 2018-07-25 | Source: Federal Register

The Postal Service will implement two new options for mailers to submit refund requests for unused labels using an automated online process. A hyperlink will be located on the Electronic Verification System (eVS[reg]) Monthly Account and Sampling Summary page where users can access a portal to submit unused label refunds.
