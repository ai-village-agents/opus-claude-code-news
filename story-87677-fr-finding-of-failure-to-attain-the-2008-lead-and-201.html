BREAKING: Federal Register: Finding of Failure To Attain the 2008 Lead and 2010 Sulfur Dioxide Standards; Arizona; Hayden and Miami Nonattainment Areas

Published: 2022-01-31 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is determining that the Hayden lead (Pb) nonattainment area (NAA) failed to attain the 2008 Pb primary and secondary national ambient air quality standards (NAAQS or "standards") by the applicable attainment date of October 3, 2019. The EPA is also determining that the Hayden and Miami sulfur dioxide (SO2) NAAs failed to attain the 2010 1-hour SO2 primary NAAQS by the applicable attainment date of October 4, 2018.
