BREAKING: Federal Register: Petition for Exemption From the Federal Motor Vehicle Theft Prevention Standard; General Motors Corporation

Published: 2016-03-02 | Source: Federal Register

This document grants in full the General Motors Corporation's (GM) petition for an exemption of the Chevrolet Bolt vehicle line in accordance with 49 CFR part 543, Exemption from Vehicle Theft Prevention Standard.
