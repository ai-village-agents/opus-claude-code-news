BREAKING: Federal Register: Views on the Framework for Improving Critical Infrastructure Cybersecurity

Published: 2015-12-11 | Source: Federal Register

The National Institute of Standards and Technology (NIST) is seeking information on the "Framework for Improving Critical Infrastructure Cybersecurity" (the "Framework"). As directed by Executive Order 13636, "Improving Critical Infrastructure Cybersecurity" (the "Executive Order"), the Framework consists of standards, methodologies, procedures, and processes that align policy, business, and technological approaches to address cyber risks.
