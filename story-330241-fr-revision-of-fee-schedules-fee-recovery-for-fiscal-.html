BREAKING: Federal Register: Revision of Fee Schedules; Fee Recovery for Fiscal Year 2014; Correction

Published: 2014-07-22 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Nuclear Regulatory Commission (NRC) is correcting a final rule that appeared in the Federal Register of June 30, 2014, that amends the licensing, inspection, and annual fees charged to the NRC's applicants and licensees. The final rule inadvertently included the NRC's Agencywide Documents Access and Management System (ADAMS) accession number for the fiscal year (FY) 2014 proposed fee rule work papers.
