BREAKING: Federal Register: Circular Welded Carbon Quality Steel Line Pipe From the People's Republic of China: Continuation of Antidumping and Countervailing Duty Orders

Published: 2014-05-20 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Commerce (the "Department") has determined that revocation of the antidumping duty ("AD") order on circular welded carbon quality steel line pipe from the People's Republic of China ("PRC") would likely lead to continuation or recurrence of dumping, and that revocation of the countervailing duty ("CVD") order on circular welded carbon quality steel line pipe from the PRC would likely lead to continuation or recurrence of a countervailable subsidy.
