BREAKING: Federal Register: Peanut Promotion, Research, and Information Order; Change in Assessment Rate Computation

Published: 2018-06-14 | Source: Federal Register

This rule changes the assessment rate computation under the Agricultural Marketing Service's (AMS) regulations regarding a national research and promotion program (program) for U.S. peanuts. This rule changes the basis for assessment under the regulations from value to volume (per ton). Two rates of assessment are established instead of using the formula currently specified in the regulations.
