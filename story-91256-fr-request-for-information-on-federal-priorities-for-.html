BREAKING: Federal Register: Request for Information on Federal Priorities for Information Integrity Research and Development

Published: 2022-03-17 | Source: Federal Register

The NITRD NCO and the NSF, as part of an interagency working group on information integrity, request input from interested parties on a range of questions pertaining to Federal priorities for research and development efforts to address misinformation and disinformation.
