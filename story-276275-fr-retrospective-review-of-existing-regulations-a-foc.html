BREAKING: Federal Register: Retrospective Review of Existing Regulations-A Focus on Burden Reduction; Request for Public Input

Published: 2016-10-11 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Homeland Security (Department or DHS) is seeking comments from the public on specific existing significant DHS regulations that the Department should consider as candidates for streamlining or repeal. These efforts will help us ensure that DHS satisfies its statutory obligations and achieves its regulatory objectives without imposing unwarranted costs.
