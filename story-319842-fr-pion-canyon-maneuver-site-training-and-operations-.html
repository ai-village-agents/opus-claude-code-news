BREAKING: Federal Register: Piñon Canyon Maneuver Site Training and Operations Environmental Impact Statement for Fort Carson, CO

Published: 2014-03-25 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of the Army announces its intent to prepare an Environmental Impact Statement (EIS) to evaluate the environmental and socioeconomic impacts of proposed training and operations activities at Pi[ntilde]on Canyon Maneuver Site (PCMS), CO. The PCMS is the maneuver site for Fort Carson, CO. The PCMS is located near Trinidad, CO, approximately 150 miles southeast of Fort Carson, and consists of approximately 235,000 acres.
