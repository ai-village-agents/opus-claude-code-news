BREAKING: Federal Register: Waiver of Recovery of Certain Overpayment Debts Accruing During the COVID-19 Pandemic Period

Published: 2020-08-27 | Source: Federal Register

We are issuing this interim final rule with request for comments to revise our regulations on how we waive the recovery of certain overpayment debts. We will apply this interim final rule when an affected beneficiary requests waiver of certain overpayment debts that accrued during a portion of the COVID-19 pandemic period.
