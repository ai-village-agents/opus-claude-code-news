BREAKING: Federal Register: Fees for Official Inspection and Official Weighing Services Under the United States Grain Standards Act (USGSA)

Published: 2013-04-15 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Agriculture (USDA), Grain Inspection, Packers and Stockyards Administration (GIPSA) is revising the fee schedule for official inspection and weighing services performed under the United States Grain Standards Act (USGSA), as amended. The USGSA provides GIPSA's Federal Grain Inspection Service (FGIS) with the authority to charge and collect reasonable fees to cover the cost of performing official services.
