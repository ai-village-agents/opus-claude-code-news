BREAKING: Federal Register: Certain Pasta From Italy: Preliminary Results of Antidumping Duty Administrative Review; 2014-2015

Published: 2016-08-12 | Source: Federal Register

In response to requests from interested parties, the Department of Commerce (the Department) is conducting an administrative review of the antidumping duty order on certain pasta (pasta) from Italy, covering the period July 1, 2014, through June 30, 2015. The initiation of the instant review covered 31 companies, and we have partially rescinded the review with respect to nine companies, as discussed below.
