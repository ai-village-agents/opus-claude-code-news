BREAKING: Federal Register: Evaluation of Safety Sensitive Personnel for Moderate-to-Severe Obstructive Sleep Apnea

Published: 2016-03-10 | Source: Federal Register

The Federal Motor Carrier Safety Administration (FMCSA) and Federal Railroad Administration (FRA) request data and information concerning the prevalence of moderate-to-severe obstructive sleep apnea (OSA) among individuals occupying safety sensitive positions in highway and rail transportation, and on its potential consequences for the safety of rail and highway transportation.
