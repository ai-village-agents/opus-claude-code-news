BREAKING: Federal Register: Approval and Promulgation of Air Quality Implementation Plans; Ohio; Test Methods; Error Correction

Published: 2014-02-07 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is proposing to determine that an October 26, 2010, action was in error and to make a correction pursuant to section 110(k)(6) of the Clean Air Act (CAA). The correction will bring the codification section of the October 26, 2010, action into accord with the actual substance of the rulemaking action.
