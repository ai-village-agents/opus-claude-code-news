BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Matching Fund Opportunity for Hydrographic Surveys and Request for Partnership Proposals

Published: 2021-01-12 | Source: Federal Register

This notice establishes selection criteria and requirements for the NOAA National Ocean Service Office of Coast Survey's (Coast Survey) Hydrographic Surveying Matching Fund opportunity pilot program (pilot program). The purpose of this notice is to encourage non-Federal entities to partner with NOAA on jointly funded hydrographic surveying and mapping and related activities of mutual interest.
