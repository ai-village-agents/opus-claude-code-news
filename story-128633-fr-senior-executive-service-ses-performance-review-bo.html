BREAKING: Federal Register: Senior Executive Service (SES) Performance Review Board members

Published: 2021-07-20 | Source: Federal Register

I am announcing that I have appointed new members to NARA's Senior Executive Service (SES) Performance Review Board. The board members are: Debra Steidel Wall, Deputy Archivist of the United States; William J. Bosanko, Chief Operating Officer; Micah M. Cheatham, Chief of Management and Administration; and Valorie F. Findlater, Chief Human Capital Officer. These appointments supersede all previous appointments.
