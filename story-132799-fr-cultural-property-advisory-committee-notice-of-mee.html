BREAKING: Federal Register: Cultural Property Advisory Committee; Notice of Meeting

Published: 2021-09-13 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of State announces the location, date, time, and agenda for the next meeting of the Cultural Property Advisory Committee. The Cultural Property Advisory Committee ("the Committee") will meet October 5, 6, and 8, 2021, from 9:00 a.m. to 5:00 p.m. (EDT) via videoconference. The Committee will hold an open session on October 5, 2021, at 2:00 p.m. (EDT), which will last approximately one hour. Participation: You may participate in the open session by videoconference.
