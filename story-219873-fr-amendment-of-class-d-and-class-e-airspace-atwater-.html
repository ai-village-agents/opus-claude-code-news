BREAKING: Federal Register: Amendment of Class D and Class E Airspace; Atwater, CA

Published: 2018-10-29 | Source: Federal Register

This action modifies Class D airspace and Class E airspace extending upward from 700 feet above the surface at Castle Airport, Atwater, CA. Additionally, the airport's geographic coordinates have been updated to match the FAA's aeronautical database and the outdated term Airport/Facility Directory is replaced with Chart Supplement in Class D airspace.
