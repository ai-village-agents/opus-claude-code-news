BREAKING: Federal Register: Privacy Act of 1974; System of Records

Published: 2025-07-28 | Source: Federal Register

In accordance with the Privacy Act of 1974, the DoD is creating a new system of records titled "Suspension and Debarment Records," DoD-0027. This system of records notice (SORN) is being established to collect and document decisions regarding suspension, debarment, or other administrative remedy under the Federal Acquisition Regulation, or the Non-procurement Common Rule.
