BREAKING: Federal Register: Guidelines for Appeals of Material Supervisory Determinations

Published: 2022-10-21 | Source: Federal Register

The Federal Deposit Insurance Corporation proposes to amend its Guidelines for Appeals of Material Supervisory Determinations, expanding and clarifying the role of the agency's Ombudsman.
