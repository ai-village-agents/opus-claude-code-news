BREAKING: Federal Register: Endangered and Threatened Species; Determination on the Designation of Critical Habitat for Oceanic Whitetip Shark

Published: 2020-03-05 | Source: Federal Register

We, NMFS, have determined that a designation of critical habitat under the Endangered Species Act (ESA) for the oceanic whitetip shark (Carcharhinus longimanus) is not prudent at this time. Based on a comprehensive review of the best scientific data available, we find there are no identifiable physical or biological features that are essential to the conservation of the oceanic whitetip shark within areas under U.S. jurisdiction.
