BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; General Electric Company Turbofan Engines

Published: 2021-09-17 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA is adopting a new airworthiness directive (AD) for all General Electric Company (GE) CF34-8 model turbofan engines with a certain outer shell combustion liner (combustion outer liner shell) installed. This AD was prompted by two in-flight engine shutdowns (IFSDs) that occurred as a result of failures of the combustion outer liner shell.
