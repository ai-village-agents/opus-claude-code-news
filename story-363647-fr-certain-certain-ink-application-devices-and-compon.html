BREAKING: Federal Register: Certain Certain Ink Application Devices and Components Thereof and Methods of Using the Same; Commission Determination Not to Review an Initial Determination Finding RespondentT-Tech Tattoo Device Inc. in Default; Request for Submissions on Remedy, Public Interest, and Bonding

Published: 2013-08-22 | Source: Federal Register

Notice is hereby given that the U.S. International Trade Commission has determined not to review the presiding administrative law judge's ("ALJ") initial determination ("ID") (Order No. 5) finding respondent T-Tech Tattoo Device Inc. of Ontario, Canada ("T- Tech") in default.
