BREAKING: Federal Register: Proposed Establishment of the Elkton Oregon Viticultural Area

Published: 2012-06-19 | Source: Federal Register

The Alcohol and Tobacco Tax and Trade Bureau (TTB) proposes to establish the approximately 74,900-acre "Elkton Oregon" viticultural area in Douglas County, Oregon. The proposed viticultural area lies totally within the Umpqua Valley viticultural area and the multi-county Southern Oregon viticultural area.
