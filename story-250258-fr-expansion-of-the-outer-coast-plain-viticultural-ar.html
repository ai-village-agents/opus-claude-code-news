BREAKING: Federal Register: Expansion of the Outer Coast Plain Viticultural Area

Published: 2017-12-07 | Source: Federal Register

The Alcohol and Tobacco Tax and Trade Bureau (TTB) is expanding the approximately 2.25 million-acre "Outer Coastal Plain" viticultural area of southeastern New Jersey by approximately 32,932 acres. The Outer Coastal Plain AVA includes all or portions of Atlantic, Burlington, Camden, Cape May, Cumberland, Gloucester, Monmouth, Ocean, and Salem counties.
