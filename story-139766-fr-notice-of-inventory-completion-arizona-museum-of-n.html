BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Inventory Completion: Arizona Museum of Natural History, Mesa, AZ

Published: 2021-12-10 | Source: Federal Register

The Arizona Museum of Natural History has completed an inventory of human remains and an associated funerary object, in consultation with the appropriate Indian Tribes or Native Hawaiian organizations, and has determined that there is a cultural affiliation between the human remains and associated funerary object and present- day Indian Tribes or Native Hawaiian organizations.
