BREAKING: Federal Register: Request for Information on Update to the 2016 Federal Cybersecurity Research and Development Strategic Plan

Published: 2018-11-13 | Source: Federal Register

Pursuant to the Cybersecurity Enhancement Act of 2014, Federal agencies must update the Federal cybersecurity research and development (R&D) strategic plan every four years. The NITRD NCO seeks public input for the 2019 update of the Federal cybersecurity R&D strategic plan.
