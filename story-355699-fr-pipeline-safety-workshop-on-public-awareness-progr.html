BREAKING: Federal Register: Pipeline Safety: Workshop on Public Awareness Programs

Published: 2013-05-23 | Source: Federal Register

PHMSA is sponsoring a two-day public awareness workshop on June 19 and June 20, 2013, at the Hyatt Regency North Dallas hotel in Richardson, Texas. The workshop serves as an opportunity to bring pipeline safety stakeholders together to discuss ways to improve public awareness outreach.
