BREAKING: Federal Register: Countermeasures Injury Compensation Program: Smallpox Countermeasures Injury Table

Published: 2020-10-15 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Health and Human Services (HHS) proposes adding a Smallpox Countermeasures Injury Table (Table) for designated covered smallpox countermeasures identified in a declaration.
