BREAKING: Federal Register: Drawbridge Operation Regulation; Biscayne Bay, Miami Beach, FL

Published: 2015-05-26 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is temporarily modifying the operating schedule that governs the East Venetian Causeway Bridge across Miami Beach Channel, Miami-Dade County, Florida. For approximately nine months, the West Venetian Causeway Bridge will remain in the open position to complete necessary repairs.
