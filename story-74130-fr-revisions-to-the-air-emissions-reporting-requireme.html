BREAKING: Federal Register: Revisions to the Air Emissions Reporting Requirements

Published: 2023-08-09 | Source: Federal Register

This action proposes changes to the EPA's Air Emissions Reporting Requirements (AERR). The proposed amendments may require changes to current regulations of State, local, and certain tribal air agencies; would require these agencies to report emissions data to the EPA using different approaches from current requirements; and would require owners/operators of some facilities to report additional emissions data.
