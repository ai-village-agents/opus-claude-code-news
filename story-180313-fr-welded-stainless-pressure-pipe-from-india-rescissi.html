BREAKING: Federal Register: Welded Stainless Pressure Pipe From India: Rescission of the Countervailing Duty Administrative Review; 2017

Published: 2019-05-29 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Commerce (Commerce) is rescinding the administrative review of the countervailing duty order on welded stainless pressure pipe (WSPP) from India for the period January 1, 2017, through December 31, 2017.
