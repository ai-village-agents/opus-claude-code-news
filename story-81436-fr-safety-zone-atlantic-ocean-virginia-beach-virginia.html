BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone; Atlantic Ocean, Virginia Beach, Virginia

Published: 2023-11-07 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is establishing a temporary safety zone for navigable waters within a 550-yard radius of the M/V HOS MYSTIQUE and accompanying machinery while the vessel is conducting a subsea survey for potential unexploded ordinance (pUXO) in the Atlantic Ocean within three nautical miles of the shores of the State Military Reservation, Virginia Beach.
