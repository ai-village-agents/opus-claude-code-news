BREAKING: Federal Register: Temporary Restrictions of Specific Uses on Public Lands Within the Black Rock Desert-High Rock Canyon Emigrant Trails National Conservation Area (Black Rock Desert Playa) Humboldt County, Pershing County, and Washoe County, NV

Published: 2021-08-18 | Source: Federal Register

Under the authority of the Federal Land Policy and Management Act of 1976, as amended (FLPMA), the Bureau of Land Management (BLM) Winnemucca District, Black Rock Field Office, will implement temporary restrictions to protect public safety and resources on public lands both within and adjacent to the Black Rock Desert playa.
