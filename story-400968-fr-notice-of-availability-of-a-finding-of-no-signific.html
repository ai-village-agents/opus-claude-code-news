BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Availability of a Finding of No Significant Impact for the Port of Guam Modernization Program

Published: 2012-11-08 | Source: Federal Register

The purpose of this Notice is to make available to the public the Finding of No Significant Impact (FONSI) derived from the Environmental Assessment (EA) regarding the Port of Guam Modernization Program (Program). The objective of this Program is to improve or replace existing port facilities, reconfigure operations, expand storage capacity, and upgrade existing infrastructure.
