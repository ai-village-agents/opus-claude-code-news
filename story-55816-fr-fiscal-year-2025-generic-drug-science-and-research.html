BREAKING: Federal Register: Fiscal Year 2025 Generic Drug Science and Research Initiatives Workshop; Public Workshop; Request for Comments

Published: 2024-12-18 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA or the Agency) is announcing the following public workshop entitled "FY 2025 Generic Drug Science and Research Initiatives Workshop." The purpose of the public workshop is to provide an overview of the status of science and research initiatives for generic drugs and an opportunity for public input on these initiatives.
