BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; The Boeing Company Airplanes

Published: 2013-11-27 | Source: Federal Register

We are adopting a new airworthiness directive (AD) for The Boeing Company Model 747-8 and 747-8F series airplanes and Model 787-8 airplanes powered by GEnx engines. This AD requires revising the airplane flight manual to advise the flight crew of potential ice crystal icing (ICI) conditions at high altitudes, and to prohibit operation in moderate and severe ICI conditions.
