BREAKING: Federal Register: Water Resources Policies and Authorities: Federal Participation in Covered Flood Control Channels

Published: 2021-01-11 | Source: Federal Register

This final rule removes the U.S. Army Corps of Engineers' part titled Water Resources Policies and Authorities: Federal Participation in Covered Flood Control Channels. Each removed section of this part is outdated in reference to engineering criteria and requirements, and covers internal agency operations that have no public compliance component or adverse public impact.
