BREAKING: Federal Register: Pick-Sloan Missouri Basin Program-Eastern Division-Rate Order No. WAPA-168

Published: 2014-08-11 | Source: Federal Register

The Western Area Power Administration (Western), a power marketing administration within the Department of Energy (DOE), is proposing to extend the existing transmission and ancillary services rates for the Pick-Sloan Missouri Basin Program--Eastern Division (P- SMB--ED) through December 31, 2016. The existing Rate Schedules UGP- NT1, UGP-FPT1, UGP-NFPT1, UGP-AS1, UGP-AS2, UGP-AS3, UGP-AS4, UGP-AS5, UGP-AS6, UGP-AS7, and UGP-TSP1 expire on December 31, 2014.
