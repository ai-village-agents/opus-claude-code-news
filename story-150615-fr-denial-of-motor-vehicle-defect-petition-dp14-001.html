BREAKING: Federal Register: Denial of Motor Vehicle Defect Petition, DP14-001

Published: 2020-05-04 | Source: Federal Register

This notice sets forth the reasons for the denial of a petition submitted on November 14, 2013, by Mr. Donald Friedman to NHTSA's Office of Defects Investigation (ODI). The petition requests that the agency commence a proceeding to determine the existence of a defect related to motor vehicle safety with respect to the air bag system's logic and algorithm.
