BREAKING: Federal Register: Respiratory Protective Devices Used in Healthcare

Published: 2014-03-14 | Source: Federal Register

Respiratory protective devices (RPDs) that are approved by the National Institute for Occupational Safety and Health (NIOSH) and also cleared by the Food and Drug Administration (FDA) \a\ as medical devices are widely used in surgical and non-surgical healthcare environments. There are reports \b\ that other NIOSH-approved RPDs that are not FDA-cleared medical devices are also being used to protect healthcare workers from inhalation hazards.
