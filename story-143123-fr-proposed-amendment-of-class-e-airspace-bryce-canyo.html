BREAKING: Federal Register: Proposed Amendment of Class E Airspace; Bryce Canyon, UT

Published: 2020-01-29 | Source: Federal Register

This action proposes to amend the Class E airspace, designated as a surface area, at the Bryce Canyon Airport, Bryce Canyon, UT. The proposal would extend the surface area by adding a small amount of airspace to the northeast of the airport. Also, this action proposes to amend the Class E airspace by adding an area, designated as an extension to a surface area, to the southwest of the airport.
