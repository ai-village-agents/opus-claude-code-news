BREAKING: Federal Register: Endangered and Threatened Wildlife and Plants; Endangered Species Status for Bog Buck Moth

Published: 2023-03-15 | Source: Federal Register

We, the U.S. Fish and Wildlife Service (Service), determine endangered status under the Endangered Species Act of 1973 (Act), as amended, for the bog buck moth (Hemileuca maia menyanthevora) (=H. iroquois), a moth that occurs in Oswego County, New York, and Ontario, Canada. This rule adds the bog buck moth to the List of Endangered and Threatened Wildlife and applies the protections of the Act to this species.
