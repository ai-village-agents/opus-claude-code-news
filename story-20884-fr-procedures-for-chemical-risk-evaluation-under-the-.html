BREAKING: Federal Register: Procedures for Chemical Risk Evaluation Under the Toxic Substances Control Act (TSCA)

Published: 2025-09-23 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Environmental Protection Agency (EPA, "the Agency") is proposing to amend the procedural framework rule for conducting existing chemical risk evaluations under the Toxic Substances Control Act (TSCA).
