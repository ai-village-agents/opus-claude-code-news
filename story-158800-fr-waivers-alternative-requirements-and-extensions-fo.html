BREAKING: Federal Register: Waivers, Alternative Requirements and Extensions for Community Development Block Grant Disaster Recovery Grantees

Published: 2020-08-17 | Source: Federal Register

This notice governs Community Development Block Grant disaster recovery (CDBG-DR) funds awarded under several appropriations. Specifically, this notice provides waivers and establishes alternative requirements and extensions for grants provided pursuant to Public Laws 114-113, 114-223, 114-254, 115-31, 115-56, 115-123, 115-254, and 116-20 in connection with HUD's obligation or use by the recipient of these funds.
