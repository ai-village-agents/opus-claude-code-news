BREAKING: Federal Register: BMW of North America, LLC, Denial of Petition for Decision of Inconsequential Noncompliance

Published: 2016-08-24 | Source: Federal Register

BMW of North America, LLC (BMW), a subsidiary of BMW AG in Munich, Germany, has determined that certain model year (MY) 2014-2015 BMW R nineT motorcycles do not fully comply with paragraph S6.4.3(a) (Table V-b) of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard (FMVSS) No. 108, Lamps, Reflective Devices and Associated Equipment.
