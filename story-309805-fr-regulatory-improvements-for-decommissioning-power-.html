BREAKING: Federal Register: Regulatory Improvements for Decommissioning Power Reactors

Published: 2015-11-19 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Nuclear Regulatory Commission (NRC) is issuing this advance notice of proposed rulemaking (ANPR) to obtain input from stakeholders on the development of a draft regulatory basis. The draft regulatory basis would support potential changes to the NRC's regulations for the decommissioning of nuclear power reactors.
