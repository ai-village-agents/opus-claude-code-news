BREAKING: Federal Register: Presidential Permits: Express Pipeline, LLC

Published: 2015-07-31 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of State issued a Presidential Permit to Express Pipeline, LLC on July 9, 2015, to connect, operate, and maintain existing pipeline facilities at the border of the United States and Canada that transport crude oil between Canada and the United States. The Department of State determined that issuance of this permit would serve the national interest.
