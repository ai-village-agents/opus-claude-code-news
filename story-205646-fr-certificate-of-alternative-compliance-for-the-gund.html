BREAKING: Federal Register: Certificate of Alternative Compliance for the Gunderson Marine LLC Hull 116

Published: 2018-04-27 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard announces that the Thirteenth Coast Guard District has issued a certificate of alternative compliance from the International Regulations for Preventing Collisions at Sea, 1972 (72 COLREGS), for the Gunderson Marine LLC Hull 116. We are issuing this notice because its publication is required by statute.
