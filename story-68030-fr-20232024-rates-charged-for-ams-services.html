BREAKING: Federal Register: 2023/2024 Rates Charged for AMS Services

Published: 2023-05-22 | Source: Federal Register

The Agricultural Marketing Service (AMS) is announcing the 2023/2024 rates it will charge for voluntary grading, inspection, certification, auditing, and laboratory services for a variety of agricultural commodities including meat and poultry, fruits and vegetables, eggs, dairy products, rice, and cotton and tobacco.
