BREAKING: Federal Register: Title IV-E Program; Correction

Published: 2022-07-15 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Health and Human Services published an interim final rule in the Federal Register on January 6, 2012, that revised regulations for the title IV-E program. The interim final rule inadvertently included incorrect numbering of one paragraph. This document corrects the numbering of that paragraph.
