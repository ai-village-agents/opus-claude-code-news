BREAKING: Federal Register: Extension of Suspension of Applications for Federal Long Term Care Insurance Program (FLTCIP) Coverage

Published: 2024-11-13 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Office of Personnel Management (OPM) is announcing an extension of the suspension of applications for FLTCIP coverage that began on December 19, 2022.
