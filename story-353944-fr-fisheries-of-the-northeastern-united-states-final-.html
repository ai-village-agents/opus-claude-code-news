BREAKING: Federal Register: Fisheries of the Northeastern United States; Final 2013-2015 Spiny Dogfish Fishery Specifications

Published: 2013-05-03 | Source: Federal Register

This rule implements specifications and management measures for the spiny dogfish fishery for the 2013-2015 fishing years consistent with the recommendations of the Mid-Atlantic and New England Fishery Management Councils. The approved commercial quotas represent increases over status quo levels, and the possession limit is also increased.
