BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zones; Fireworks Displays in the Fifth Coast Guard District

Published: 2018-08-30 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard will enforce a safety zone for a fireworks display taking place over the Patapsco River, Baltimore, MD, on September 15, 2018. This action is necessary to ensure safety of life on navigable waterways during the fireworks display. Our regulation for recurring safety zones for fireworks displays within the Fifth Coast Guard District identifies the regulated area for this fireworks display.
