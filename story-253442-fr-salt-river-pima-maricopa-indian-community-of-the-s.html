BREAKING: Federal Register: Salt River Pima-Maricopa Indian Community of the Salt River Reservation Liquor Ordinance

Published: 2016-01-21 | Source: Federal Register

This notice publishes the Salt River Pima-Maricopa Indian Community of the Salt River Reservation Liquor Ordinance (Ordinance). The Ordinance certifies the Salt River Pima-Maricopa Indian Community's Liquor licensing laws to regulate and control the possession, sale and consumption of liquor within the jurisdiction of the Salt River Pima- Maricopa Indian Community.
