BREAKING: Federal Register: Announcement of Requirements and Registration for the Career and Technical Education Makeover Challenge

Published: 2016-03-09 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Department of Education (the Department) is announcing the Career and Technical Education (CTE) Makeover Challenge (the Challenge), a prize competition funded by the Carl D. Perkins Career and Technical Education Act of 2006 (Perkins IV or Act). The Challenge calls upon eligible high schools to design models of makerspaces that strengthen career and technical skills through making (models of CTE makerspaces).
