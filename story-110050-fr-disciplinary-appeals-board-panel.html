BREAKING: Federal Register: Disciplinary Appeals Board Panel

Published: 2022-11-15 | Source: Federal Register

Section 203 of the Department of Veterans Affairs (VA) Health- Care Personnel Act of 1991 revised the disciplinary grievance and appeal procedures for employees appointed under Federal law. It also required the periodic designation of VA employees who are qualified to serve on the Disciplinary Appeals Board.
