BREAKING: Federal Register: Business Loan Program Temporary Changes; Paycheck Protection Program as Amended by Economic Aid Act

Published: 2021-01-14 | Source: Federal Register

On April 2, 2020, the U.S. Small Business Administration (SBA) posted an interim final rule announcing the implementation of sections 1102 and 1106 of the Coronavirus Aid, Relief, and Economic Security Act (CARES Act). Section 1102 of the CARES Act temporarily adds a new program, titled the "Paycheck Protection Program," to the SBA's 7(a) Loan Program.
