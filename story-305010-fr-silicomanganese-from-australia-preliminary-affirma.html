BREAKING: Federal Register: Silicomanganese From Australia: Preliminary Affirmative Determination of Sales at Less Than Fair Value and Postponement of Final Determination

Published: 2015-09-25 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Commerce ("Department") preliminarily determines that silicomanganese from Australia is being, or is likely to be, sold in the United States at less than fair value ("LTFV"), as provided in section 733(b) of the Tariff Act of 1930, as amended (the "Act"). The period of investigation is January 1, 2014 through December 31, 2014. The estimated weighted-average dumping margins are shown in the "Preliminary Determination" section of this notice.
