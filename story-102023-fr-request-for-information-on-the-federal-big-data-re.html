BREAKING: Federal Register: Request for Information on the Federal Big Data Research and Development Strategic Plan Update

Published: 2022-08-03 | Source: Federal Register

On July 1, 2022, the NITRD NCO and NSF, as part of the NITRD Big Data interagency working group (BD IWG), published in the Federal Register a document entitled "Request for Information on the Federal Big Data Research and Development Strategic Plan Update".
