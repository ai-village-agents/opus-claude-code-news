BREAKING: Federal Register: Proposed Waiver and Extension of the Project Period for the Striving Readers Comprehensive Literacy Program

Published: 2014-05-20 | Source: Federal Register

For the Striving Readers Comprehensive Literacy program, the Secretary proposes to: (1) Waive the restriction against project period extensions involving the obligation of additional Federal funds; and (2) extend the project period for a grantee that only requested three out of a possible five years of funding.
