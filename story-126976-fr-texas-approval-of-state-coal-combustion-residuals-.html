BREAKING: Federal Register: Texas: Approval of State Coal Combustion Residuals Permit Program

Published: 2021-06-28 | Source: Federal Register

Pursuant to the Resource Conservation and Recovery Act (RCRA), the Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is approving the Texas Commission on Environmental Quality's partial State Coal Combustion Residuals (CCR) Permit Program, which will now operate in lieu of the Federal CCR program, with the exception of certain provisions for which the State did not seek approval.
