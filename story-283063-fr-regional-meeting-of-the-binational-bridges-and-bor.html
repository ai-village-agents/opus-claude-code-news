BREAKING: Federal Register: Regional Meeting of the Binational Bridges and Border Crossings Group in Fort Worth, Texas

Published: 2016-12-29 | Source: Federal Register

Delegates from the United States and Mexican governments, the states of Texas and New Mexico, and the Mexican states of Chihuahua, Coahuila, Nuevo Leon, and Tamaulipas will participate in a regional meeting of the U.S.-Mexico Binational Bridges and Border Crossings Group on Wednesday, January 25, 2017 in Fort Worth, Texas.
