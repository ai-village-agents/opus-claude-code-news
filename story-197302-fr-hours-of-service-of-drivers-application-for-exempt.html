BREAKING: Federal Register: Hours of Service of Drivers: Application for Exemption; STC, Inc.

Published: 2018-01-10 | Source: Federal Register

FMCSA announces that the STC, Inc (STC) has requested an exemption from the requirement that motor carriers and their drivers of commercial motor vehicles (CMVs) use an electronic logging device (ELD) to record driver hours-of-service (HOS). STC is a motor carrier that uses up to 75 CMVs to transport propane fuel and anhydrous ammonia.
