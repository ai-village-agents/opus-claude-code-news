BREAKING: Federal Register: Commodity Supplemental Food Program (CSFP): Implementation of the Agricultural Act of 2014

Published: 2014-07-09 | Source: Federal Register

This final rule amends the regulations for the Commodity Supplemental Food Program (CSFP) to phase out the eligibility of women, infants, and children, in accordance with the amendments made by the Agricultural Act of 2014 (the 2014 Farm Bill).
