BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Rolls-Royce Corporation Turboshaft Engines

Published: 2022-05-24 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA is superseding Airworthiness Directive (AD) 2017-18- 14, which applied to certain Rolls-Royce Corporation (RRC) 250 model turboshaft engines. AD 2017-18-14 required repetitive visual inspections and fluorescent penetrant inspections (FPIs) of the 3rd- stage turbine wheel and removal from service of the 4th-stage turbine wheel.
