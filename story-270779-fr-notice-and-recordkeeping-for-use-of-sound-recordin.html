BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice and Recordkeeping for Use of Sound Recordings Under Statutory License; Technical Amendment

Published: 2016-08-10 | Source: Federal Register

On June 21, 2016, the Copyright Royalty Judges (Judges) published in the Federal Register a technical amendment to regulations that govern reporting requirements for noncommercial educational webcasters that pay no more than the minimum fee for their use of sound recordings under the applicable statutory licenses.
