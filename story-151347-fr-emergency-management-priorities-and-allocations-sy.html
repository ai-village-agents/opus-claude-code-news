BREAKING: Federal Register: Emergency Management Priorities and Allocations System (EMPAS)

Published: 2020-05-13 | Source: Federal Register

This interim final rule establishes standards and procedures by which the Federal Emergency Management Agency (FEMA) may require certain contracts or orders that promote the national defense be given priority over other contracts or orders.
