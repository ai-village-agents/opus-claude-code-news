BREAKING: Federal Register: Endangered and Threatened Wildlife and Plants; 12-Month Findings on Petitions To Delist U.S. Captive Populations of the Scimitar-horned Oryx, Dama Gazelle, and Addax

Published: 2013-06-05 | Source: Federal Register

We, the U.S. Fish and Wildlife Service ("Service"), announce 12-month findings on two petitions to remove the U.S. captive-bred and U.S. captive "populations" of three antelope species, the scimitar- horned oryx (Oryx dammah), dama gazelle (Gazella dama), and addax (Addax nasomaculatus), from the List of Endangered and Threatened Wildlife as determined under the Endangered Species Act of 1973, as amended (Act).
