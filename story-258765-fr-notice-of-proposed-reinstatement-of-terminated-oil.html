BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Proposed Reinstatement of Terminated Oil and Gas Lease WYW179123, Wyoming

Published: 2016-03-23 | Source: Federal Register

Per the Mineral Leasing Act of 1920, Douglas C. McLeod filed a petition for reinstatement of competitive oil and gas lease WYW179123 for land in Crook County, Wyoming. The petition was filed on time, and the lessee paid the required rentals accruing from the date of termination. No leases that affect these lands were issued before the petition was filed.
