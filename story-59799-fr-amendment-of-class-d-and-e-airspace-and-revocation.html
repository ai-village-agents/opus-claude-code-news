BREAKING: Federal Register: Amendment of Class D and E Airspace and Revocation of Class E Airspace; Alton/St. Louis, IL

Published: 2023-02-06 | Source: Federal Register

This action amends the Class D and Class E airspace and revokes Class E airspace at Alton/St. Louis, IL. This is action due to an airspace review conducted as part of the decommissioning of the Civic Memorial non-directional beacon (NDB).
