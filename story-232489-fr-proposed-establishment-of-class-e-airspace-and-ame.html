BREAKING: Federal Register: Proposed Establishment of Class E Airspace, and Amendment of Class E Airspace; St. George, UT

Published: 2017-04-20 | Source: Federal Register

This action proposes to establish Class E airspace designated as an extension to a Class E surface area, establish Class E en route airspace, and modify Class E airspace extending upward from 700 feet above the surface at St. George Regional Airport (formerly, St. George Municipal Airport), St. George, UT.
