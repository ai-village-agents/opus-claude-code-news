BREAKING: Federal Register: False Advertising, Misrepresentation of Insured Status, and Misuse of the FDIC's Name or Logo

The Federal Deposit Insurance Corporation is adopting a final rule to implement section 18(a)(4) of the Federal Deposit Insurance Act.
