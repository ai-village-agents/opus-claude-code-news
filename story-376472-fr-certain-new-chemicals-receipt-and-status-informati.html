BREAKING: Federal Register: Certain New Chemicals; Receipt and Status Information

Published: 2012-02-01 | Source: Federal Register

Section 5 of the Toxic Substances Control Act (TSCA) requires any person who intends to manufacture (defined by statute to include import) a new chemical (i.e., a chemical not on the TSCA Chemical Substances Inventory (TSCA Inventory)) to notify EPA and comply with the statutory provisions pertaining to the manufacture of new chemicals.
