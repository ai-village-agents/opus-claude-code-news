BREAKING: Federal Register: NRC Position on the Relationship Between General Design Criteria and Technical Specification Operability

Published: 2012-07-31 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Nuclear Regulatory Commission (NRC or the Commission) is holding a public meeting to discuss a draft regulatory issue summary (RIS) that clarifies the NRC staff's position on the relationship between the general design criteria (GDC) for nuclear power plants and technical specification operability.
