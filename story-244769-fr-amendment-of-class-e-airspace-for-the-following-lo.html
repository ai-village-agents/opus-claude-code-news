BREAKING: Federal Register: Amendment of Class E Airspace for the Following Louisiana Towns; Leesville, LA; and Patterson, LA

Published: 2017-09-27 | Source: Federal Register

This action amends Class E airspace extending upward from 700 feet above the surface at Leesville City Airport, Leesville, LA, and Harry P. Williams Memorial Airport, Patterson, LA. Airspace redesign is necessary due to the decommissioning of the Leesville non-directional radio beacon (NDB), and the Patterson radio beacon (RBN), and cancellation of NDB and RBN approaches, and for the safe management of instrument flight rules (IFR) operations at these airports.
