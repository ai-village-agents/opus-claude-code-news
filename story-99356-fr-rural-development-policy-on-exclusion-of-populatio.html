BREAKING: Federal Register: Rural Development Policy on Exclusion of Populations

Published: 2022-06-29 | Source: Federal Register

The Rural Housing Service, Rural Business-Cooperative Service, and Rural Utilities Service, agencies in the United States Department of Agriculture (USDA) Rural Development Mission area, are issuing a final rule to implement the exclusion of certain populations from the definition of "Rural area."
