BREAKING: Federal Register: Copyright Claims Board: District Court Referrals; Proof of Service Forms; Default Proceedings; Law Student Representation

Published: 2023-07-31 | Source: Federal Register

Pursuant to the Copyright Alternative in Small-Claims Enforcement Act, the U.S. Copyright Office is adopting as final a December 19, 2022 interim rule allowing the Copyright Claims Board to modify or suspend certain rules when a claim is referred by a district court and, in cases that are first filed before the Copyright Claims Board, accept alternative proof of service forms.
