BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Determination of the Highly Pathogenic Avian Influenza and Newcastle Disease Status of Romania

Published: 2020-08-26 | Source: Federal Register

We are advising the public that we are recognizing Romania as being free of highly pathogenic avian influenza and Newcastle disease. This recognition is based on a risk evaluation we prepared and made available for public review and comment.
