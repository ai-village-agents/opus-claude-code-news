BREAKING: Federal Register: National Environmental Policy Act Implementing Procedures

Published: 2016-07-20 | Source: Federal Register

We are proposing to amend the regulations that set out our National Environmental Policy Act implementing procedures. The amendments include clarifying and amending the categories of action for which we would normally complete an environmental impact statement or an environmental assessment for an action, expanding the list of actions subject to categorical exclusion from further environmental documentation, and setting out an environmental documentation process that could be used in emergencies...
