BREAKING: Federal Register: Evaluating Drug Effects on the Ability To Operate a Motor Vehicle; Guidance for Industry; Availability

Published: 2017-11-09 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA or Agency) is announcing the availability of a guidance for industry entitled "Evaluating Drug Effects on the Ability to Operate a Motor Vehicle." The purpose of this guidance is to assist sponsors in the evaluation of the effects of psychoactive drugs on the ability to operate a motor vehicle.
