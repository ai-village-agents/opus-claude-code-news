BREAKING: Federal Register: Approval of Air Quality Implementation Plans; California; Imperial County; Ozone Precursor Emissions Inventories

Published: 2014-10-23 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is approving revisions to California's State Implementation Plan (SIP) for Imperial County that address Clean Air Act (CAA) requirements concerning ozone precursor emissions inventories of volatile organic compounds and oxides of nitrogen.
