BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Airbus SAS Airplanes

Published: 2025-06-27 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA proposes to adopt a new airworthiness directive (AD) for all Airbus SAS Model A350-941 and -1041 airplanes. This proposed AD was prompted by reports of electronic centralized aircraft monitor (ECAM) messages requiring flight control remote module (FCRM) replacement linked to solder structural fatigue.
