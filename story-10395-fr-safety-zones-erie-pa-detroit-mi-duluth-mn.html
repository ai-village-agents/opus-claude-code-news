BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zones; Erie, PA; Detroit, MI; Duluth, MN

Published: 2025-05-01 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is proposing to establish temporary safety zones around each tall ship visiting the Great Lakes during the Tall Ships Challenge 2025 race series beginning on July 10, 2025 through October 1, 2025. These safety zones will provide for the regulation of vessel traffic in the vicinity of each tall ship in the navigable waters of the United States.
