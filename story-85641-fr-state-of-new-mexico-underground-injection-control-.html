BREAKING: Federal Register: State of New Mexico Underground Injection Control Program; Primacy Revisions

Published: 2022-01-03 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is approving an application from the State of New Mexico under the Safe Drinking Water Act (SDWA) to revise the State's existing Underground Injection Control (UIC) program for Class I injection wells located within the State, except those in Indian country.
