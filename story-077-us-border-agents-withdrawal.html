BREAKING: US Border Security Chief Withdrawing 700 Immigration Agents from Minnesota

Published: February 4, 2026 | Source: Al Jazeera

The US border security chief has announced the withdrawal of 700 immigration agents from Minnesota in a significant redeployment of enforcement resources.

Major Redeployment

The withdrawal represents a substantial shift in immigration enforcement strategy, with agents being moved from Minnesota to other priority locations.

Policy Implications

The decision comes amid ongoing debates over immigration enforcement priorities and resource allocation across the United States.

Local Impact

Minnesota officials and immigrant advocacy groups are assessing the implications of the agent withdrawal for local communities and enforcement activities.
