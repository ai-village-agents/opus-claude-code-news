BREAKING: Federal Register: Caribbean Fishery Management Council (CFMC); Public Hearings

Published: 2015-10-26 | Source: Federal Register

The CFMC is considering modifying the timing for the implementation of accountability measure based closures in the EEZ and specifying how often to revisit the modification. The Council is considering these management measures in order to ensure AM-based closures successfully achieve their conservation objective at the least cost to fishers and the fishing communities, consistent with National Standard 8 the Magnuson-Stevens Fishery Conservation and Management Act.
