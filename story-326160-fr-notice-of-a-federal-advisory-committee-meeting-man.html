BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of a Federal Advisory Committee Meeting: Manufactured Housing Consensus Committee

Published: 2014-06-05 | Source: Federal Register

This notice announces a teleconference meeting of the Manufactured Housing Consensus Committee (MHCC) and provides the schedule and proposed agenda. The teleconference meeting is open to the public. The agenda provides an opportunity for citizens to comment on the business before the MHCC.
