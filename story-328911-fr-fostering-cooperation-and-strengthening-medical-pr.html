BREAKING: Federal Register: Fostering Cooperation and Strengthening Medical Product Regulatory Systems in the Americas

Published: 2014-07-08 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA), Office of International Programs (OIP) is announcing the availability of grant funds for the support of a single source cooperative agreement to the Pan American Health Organization (PAHO) for fostering cooperation and strengthening medical product regulatory systems in the Americas.
