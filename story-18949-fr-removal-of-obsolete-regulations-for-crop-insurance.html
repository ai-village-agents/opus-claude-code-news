BREAKING: Federal Register: Removal of Obsolete Regulations for Crop Insurance Provisions

Published: 2025-08-27 | Source: Federal Register

Risk Management Agency (RMA), on behalf of the Federal Crop Insurance Corporation (FCIC), is in the process of reviewing all regulations within its purview to reduce regulatory burdens and costs. Pursuant to this review, FCIC has identified obsolete, unnecessary, and outdated provisions in title 7 of the Code of Federal Regulation (CFR).
