BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Final Federal Agency Action on the Authorization for the Coastal Virginia Offshore Wind Commercial Project

Published: 2024-04-09 | Source: Federal Register

USACE announces final agency action on the USACE authorization for the proposed construction and maintenance of the Coastal Virginia Offshore Wind (CVOW) Commercial Project offshore Virginia. USACE has issued a permit authorizing the construction and maintenance of the CVOW Project under sections 10 and 14 of the Rivers and Harbors Act of 1899 (RHA) and section 404 of the Clean Water Act (CWA).
