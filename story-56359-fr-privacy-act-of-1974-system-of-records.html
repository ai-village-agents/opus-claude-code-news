BREAKING: Federal Register: Privacy Act of 1974; System of Records

Published: 2024-12-23 | Source: Federal Register

In accordance with the Privacy Act of 1974, the DoD is modifying a system of records titled, "Joint Advertising, Market Research & Studies Recruiting Database (JAMRS)," DHRA 04. The JAMRS Recruiting Database compiles, processes, and distributes files of individuals, ages 16 to 18 years, to the Services to assist them in their direct marketing recruiting efforts.
