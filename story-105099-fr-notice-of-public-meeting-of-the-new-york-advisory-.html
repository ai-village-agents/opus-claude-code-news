BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Public Meeting of the New York Advisory Committee; Cancellation

Published: 2022-09-13 | Source: Federal Register

The Commission on Civil Rights published a notice in the Federal Register concerning a virtual business meeting of the New York Advisory Committee. The meeting scheduled for Friday, September 16, 2022, at 1 p.m. (ET) is cancelled. The notice is in the Federal Register of Friday, July 22, 2022, in FR Doc. 2022-15638 in the first and second columns of page 43784.
