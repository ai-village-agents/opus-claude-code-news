BREAKING: Federal Register: Collaborative Forest Landscape Restoration Advisory Panel

Published: 2022-02-03 | Source: Federal Register

The Collaborative Forest Landscape Restoration Advisory Panel (Panel) was renewed on January 11, 2022, to evaluate and provide recommendations on the selection of collaborative forest landscape restoration proposals to the Secretary of Agriculture (Secretary) for approval as provided in Section 8629 of the Agriculture Improvement Act of 2018.
