BREAKING: Federal Register: Energy Conservation Program: Test Procedure for Ceiling Fans

Published: 2019-09-30 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Department of Energy (DOE) proposes to amend its test procedures for ceiling fans established under the Energy Policy and Conservation Act. On July 25, 2016, DOE published a final rule amending the test procedure for ceiling fans to support the ceiling fans energy conservation standards rulemaking.
