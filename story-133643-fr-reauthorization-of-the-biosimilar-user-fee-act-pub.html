BREAKING: Federal Register: Reauthorization of the Biosimilar User Fee Act; Public Meeting; Request for Comments

Published: 2021-09-22 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA or Agency) is hosting a virtual public meeting to discuss proposed recommendations for the reauthorization of the Biosimilar User Fee Act (BsUFA) for fiscal years (FYs) 2023 through 2027. The BsUFA authorizes FDA to collect user fees to support the process for the review of biosimilar biological product applications.
