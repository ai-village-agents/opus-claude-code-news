BREAKING: Federal Register: Applicability of National Environmental Policy Act (NEPA) to Federal Aviation Administration (FAA) Review of Airport Wildlife Hazard Management Plans

Published: 2016-12-01 | Source: Federal Register

FAA is extending the comment period on a Notice published in the Federal Register of October 19, 2016, entitled "Applicability of National Environmental Policy Act (NEPA) to Federal Aviation Administration (FAA) Review of Airport Wildlife Hazard Management Plans." In that notice, the FAA requested comments be received by November 18, 2016.
