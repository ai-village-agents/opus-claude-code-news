BREAKING: Federal Register: Air Force Academy Preparatory School

Published: 2020-02-24 | Source: Federal Register

This final rule removes the regulation concerning how the Department of the Air Force accesses individuals into the Air Force Academy Preparatory School. This part is outdated, contains internal guidance, reiterates statutory law, and is otherwise subject to the military function exemption to rulemaking.
