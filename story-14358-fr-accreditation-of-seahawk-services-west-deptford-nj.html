BREAKING: Federal Register: Accreditation of Seahawk Services (West Deptford, NJ) as a Commercial Laboratory

Published: 2025-06-25 | Source: Federal Register

Notice is hereby given, pursuant to CBP regulations, that Seahawk Services (West Deptford, NJ), has been accredited to test petroleum and certain petroleum products for customs purposes for the next three years as of March 8, 2023.
