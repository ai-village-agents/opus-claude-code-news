BREAKING: Federal Register: Next Generation Delivery Vehicles Acquisitions

Published: 2022-03-15 | Source: Federal Register

To replace existing delivery vehicles nationwide that have reached the end of their service life, the U.S. Postal Service ("Postal Service") has determined that it will implement the Preferred Alternative. The Preferred Alternative is the purchase and deployment over a ten-year period of 50,000 to 165,000 purpose-built, right-hand drive NGDV vehicles consisting of a mix of internal combustion engine ("ICE") and battery electric vehicle ("BEV") powertrains, with at least ten percent BEVs.
