BREAKING: Federal Register: Hours of Service of Drivers: Specialized Carriers & Rigging Association (SC&RA); Application for Exemption

Published: 2014-11-24 | Source: Federal Register

FMCSA announces that it has received an application from the Specialized Carriers & Rigging Association (SC&RA) for an exemption from the 30-minute rest break provision of the Agency's hours-of- service (HOS) regulations for commercial motor vehicle (CMV) drivers.
