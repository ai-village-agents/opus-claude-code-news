BREAKING: Federal Register: Affirmative Action for Individuals With Disabilities in the Federal Government

Published: 2016-02-24 | Source: Federal Register

The Equal Employment Opportunity Commission ("EEOC" or "Commission") proposes to amend its regulations requiring the federal government to engage in affirmative action for individuals with disabilities. These changes will clarify the obligations that the Rehabilitation Act of 1973 imposes on federal agencies as employers, in addition to the obligation not to discriminate on the basis of disability.
