BREAKING: Federal Register: Special Local Regulations; Key West World Championship, Atlantic Ocean, Key West, FL

Published: 2017-11-08 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard will enforce the Key West World Championship Special Local Regulation from 9:30 a.m. until 4:30 p.m. on November 8, 10, and 12, 2017. This action is necessary to ensure safety of life on navigable waters of the United States and to protect race participants, participant vessels, spectators, and the general public from the hazards associated with high-speed boat races.
