BREAKING: Federal Register: Effects of Extending Foreign Policy-Based Export Controls

Published: 2015-09-14 | Source: Federal Register

The Bureau of Industry and Security (BIS) is seeking public comments on the effect of existing foreign policy-based export controls in the Export Administration Regulations. Section 6 of the Export Administration Act requires BIS to consult with industry on the effect of such controls and to report the results of the consultations to Congress.
