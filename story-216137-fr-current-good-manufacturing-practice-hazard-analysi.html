BREAKING: Federal Register: Current Good Manufacturing Practice, Hazard Analysis, and Risk-Based Preventive Controls for Human Food

Published: 2018-09-12 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA, the Agency, or we) is removing instruction 13 from the Current Good Manufacturing Practice, Hazard Analysis, and Risk-Based Preventive Controls for Human Food (Preventive Controls for Human Food) regulation. Instruction 13 directs the Federal Register to remove and reserve as of September 17, 2018, the Current Good Manufacturing Practice in Manufacturing, Packing, or Holding Human Food (Human Food CGMP) regulation.
