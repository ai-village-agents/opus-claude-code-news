BREAKING: Federal Register: Vocational Rehabilitation Services Projects for American Indians With Disabilities

Published: 2014-06-23 | Source: Federal Register

The Secretary proposes to amend the definition of "reservation" under the regulations governing the American Indian Vocational Rehabilitation Services (AIVRS) program in one of two ways. The first proposed amendment, "Alternative A," would conform the definition to the Department's current interpretation and practices.
