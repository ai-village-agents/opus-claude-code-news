BREAKING: Federal Register: The Critical Infrastructure Partnership Advisory Council

Published: 2013-03-19 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Homeland Security (DHS) announced the establishment of the Critical Infrastructure Partnership Advisory Council (CIPAC) in a Federal Register Notice (71 FR 14930-14933) dated March 24, 2006, which identified the purpose of CIPAC, as well as its membership.
