BREAKING: Federal Register: Single-Ingredient, Immediate-Release Drug Products Containing Oxycodone for Oral Administration and Labeled for Human Use; Enforcement Action Dates

Published: 2012-07-06 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA or Agency) is announcing its intention to take enforcement action against all unapproved single- ingredient, immediate-release drug products that contain oxycodone hydrochloride (hereinafter "oxycodone") for oral administration and are labeled for human use, and persons who manufacture or cause the manufacture or distribution of such products in interstate commerce.
