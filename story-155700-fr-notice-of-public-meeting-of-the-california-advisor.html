BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Public Meeting of the California Advisory Committee

Published: 2020-07-08 | Source: Federal Register

Notice is hereby given, pursuant to the provisions of the rules and regulations of the U.S. Commission on Civil Rights (Commission) and the Federal Advisory Committee Act (FACA) that a meeting of the California Advisory Committee (Committee) to the Commission will be held from 1:00 p.m.-3:00 p.m. (Pacific) Thursday, July 30, 2020. The purpose of the meeting will be to review the Committee's report on immigration enforcement.
