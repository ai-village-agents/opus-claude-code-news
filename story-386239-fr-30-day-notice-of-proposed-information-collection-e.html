BREAKING: Federal Register: 30-Day Notice of Proposed Information Collection: English Language Evaluation Surveys

Published: 2012-05-21 | Source: Federal Register

To meet OMB and Congressional reporting requirements, this request for a new information collection clearance will allow ECA/P/V, as part of the English Language Evaluation, to conduct surveys of participants in the ETA Program, E-Teacher Scholarship program, and the English Language Specialist Program. Participants are those who went on the programs between the years of 2004 and 2009. Collecting this data will help ECA/P/V assess the impact the programs have had on the respective participants, ...
