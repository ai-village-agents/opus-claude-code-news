BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; The Boeing Company Airplanes

Published: 2021-03-15 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA is superseding Airworthiness Directive (AD) 2017-26- 10, which applied to certain The Boeing Company Model 757 airplanes. AD 2017-26-10 required deactivating the spoiler control module (SCM) relays and capping and stowing the associated wiring on certain airplanes. This AD requires repetitive operational tests of the spoiler inhibit function.
