BREAKING: Federal Register: Cooperative Research and Development Agreement: Western Rivers e-AtoN Technology Demonstration

Published: 2015-02-19 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard announces its intent to enter into a cooperative research and development agreement (CRADA) to prepare for a demonstration, the "Ohio River eNav Technology Demonstration" of electronic navigation (eNav) technology to be conducted by the Coast Guard, U.S. Army Corps of Engineers, and the National Oceanic Atmospheric Administration.
