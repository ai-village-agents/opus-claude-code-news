BREAKING: Federal Register: Security Zones; Dignitary Arrival/Departure and United Nations Meetings, New York, NY

Published: 2018-05-22 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard proposes to modify the Marine Air Terminal, LaGuardia Airport Security Zone. The modification of the security zone would expand the existing security zone boundary north along the Rikers Island Bridge to the intersecting point on the southern tip of Rikers Island then east to the western end of LaGuardia Airport.
