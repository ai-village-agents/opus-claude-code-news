BREAKING: Federal Register: Rescissions Proposals Pursuant to the Congressional Budget and Impoundment Control Act of 1974

Published: 2018-05-15 | Source: Federal Register

Pursuant to section 1014(d) of the Congressional Budget and Impoundment Control Act of 1974, enclosed for publication in the Federal Register is a special message from the President reflecting the proposals for rescission under section 1012 of that Act that were transmitted to the Congress for consideration on May 8, 2018.
