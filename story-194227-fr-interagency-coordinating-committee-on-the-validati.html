BREAKING: Federal Register: Interagency Coordinating Committee on the Validation of Alternative Methods Communities of Practice Webinar on Use of Animal-Free Affinity Reagents; Notice of Public Webinar; Registration Information

Published: 2019-11-26 | Source: Federal Register

The Interagency Coordinating Committee on the Validation of Alternative Methods (ICCVAM) announces a public webinar "Use of Animal-free Affinity Reagents." The webinar is organized on behalf of ICCVAM by the National Toxicology Program Interagency Center for the Evaluation of Alternative Toxicological Methods (NICEATM).
