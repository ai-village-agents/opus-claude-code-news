BREAKING: Federal Register: Request for Information on the Bioeconomy

Published: 2019-09-10 | Source: Federal Register

The Office of Science Technology and Policy (OSTP) requests input from all interested parties on the U.S. Bioeconomy. The Bioeconomy represents the infrastructure, innovation, products, technology, and data derived from biologically-related processes and science that drive economic growth, promote health, and increase public benefit.
