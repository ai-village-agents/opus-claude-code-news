BREAKING: Federal Register: Exclusion of Orphan Drugs for Certain Covered Entities Under 340B Program

Published: 2013-07-23 | Source: Federal Register

HHS is issuing this final rule to clarify how section 340B(e) of the Public Health Service Act (PHSA) will be implemented. The final rule applies section 340B(e) of the PHSA only to drugs transferred, prescribed, sold, or otherwise used for the rare condition or disease for which the orphan drug was designated under section 526 of the Federal Food, Drug, and Cosmetic Act (FFDCA).
