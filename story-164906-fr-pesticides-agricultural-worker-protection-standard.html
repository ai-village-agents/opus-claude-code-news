BREAKING: Federal Register: Pesticides; Agricultural Worker Protection Standard; Revision of the Application Exclusion Zone Requirements

Published: 2020-10-30 | Source: Federal Register

EPA is finalizing revisions to the Agricultural Worker Protection Standard (WPS) to clarify and simplify the application exclusion zone (AEZ) requirements. This rulemaking is responsive to feedback received from stakeholders and the Agency's efforts to reduce regulatory burden, while providing the necessary protections for agricultural workers and the public.
