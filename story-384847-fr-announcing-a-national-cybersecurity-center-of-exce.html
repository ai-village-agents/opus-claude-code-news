BREAKING: Federal Register: Announcing a National Cybersecurity Center of Excellence (NCCoE) Workshop

Published: 2012-05-04 | Source: Federal Register

NIST announces a National Cybersecurity Center of Excellence (NCCoE) Workshop to be held on Tuesday, June 26, 2012. This is an initial informational NCCoE workshop. The goals of this workshop are to provide a venue for discussion of the NCCoE public-private partnership structure, and to describe and gather input from individual participants on possible case studies that are expected to form a central focus of collaborative efforts.
