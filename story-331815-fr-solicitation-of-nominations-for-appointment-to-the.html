BREAKING: Federal Register: Solicitation of Nominations for Appointment to the Advisory Committee on Homeless Veterans

Published: 2014-08-11 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Veterans Affairs (VA), Veterans Health Administration (VHA) Homeless Programs, is seeking nominations of qualified candidates to be considered for appointment as a member of the Advisory Committee on Homeless Veterans (herein-after in this section referred to as "the Committee"). In accordance with 38 U.S.C. Sec. 2066, the Committee provides advice to the Secretary, through the Under Secretary for Health, on the provision of benefits and services to homeless Veterans.
