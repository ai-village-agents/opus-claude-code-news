BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Intent To Request Public Comment on Draft Exemption From Historic Preservation Review for Electric Vehicle Supply Equipment

Published: 2022-05-05 | Source: Federal Register

The Advisory Council on Historic Preservation (ACHP) proposes an exemption that would relieve Federal agencies from the requirement of taking into account the effects of their undertakings on historic properties that involve the installation of certain electric vehicle supply equipment (EVSE).
