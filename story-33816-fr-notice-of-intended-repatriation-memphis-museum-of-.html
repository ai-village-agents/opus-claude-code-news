BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Intended Repatriation: Memphis Museum of Science and History, Memphis, TN

Published: 2024-04-08 | Source: Federal Register

In accordance with the Native American Graves Protection and Repatriation Act (NAGPRA), the Memphis Museum of Science and History (MoSH), intends to repatriate certain cultural items that meet the definition of unassociated funerary objects and that have a cultural affiliation with the Indian Tribes or Native Hawaiian organizations in this notice.
