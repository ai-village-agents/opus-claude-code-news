BREAKING: Federal Register: Reasonable Charges for Inpatient MS-DRGs and SNF Medical Services; v3.24, Fiscal Year 2019 Update

Published: 2018-09-19 | Source: Federal Register

This document updates the acute inpatient and the skilled nursing facility (SNF)/sub-acute inpatient facility charges. The updated charges are based on the 2019 Medicare severity diagnosis related groups (MS-DRG).
