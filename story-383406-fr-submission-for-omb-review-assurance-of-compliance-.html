BREAKING: Federal Register: Submission for OMB Review; Assurance of Compliance-Civil Rights Certificate

Published: 2012-04-18 | Source: Federal Register

The Office for Civil Rights (OCR) has enforcement responsibilities under several civil rights laws, including Title VI, Title IX, Section 504, the Age Discrimination Act, and the Boy Scouts of America Equal Access Act. To meet these responsibilities, OCR collects assurances of compliance from applicants for Federal financial assistance from, and applicants for funds made available through, the U.S. Department of Education, as required by regulations.
