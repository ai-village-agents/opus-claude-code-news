BREAKING: Federal Register: Vietnam War Commemoration Advisory Committee; Notice of Federal Advisory Committee Meeting; Amendment

Published: 2019-02-05 | Source: Federal Register

On Wednesday, December 26, 2018, the Department of Defense (DoD) published a notice to announce that the following Federal Advisory Committee meeting of the Vietnam War Commemoration Advisory Committee will be held on February 8, 2019. Subsequent to the publication of this notice, the start time for this meeting has changed. The public is asked to arrive no later than 10:15 a.m. The meeting will begin promptly at 10:30 a.m. All other information in the December 26, 2018 notice remains the same.
