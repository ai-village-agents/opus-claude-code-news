BREAKING: Federal Register: Reorganization of the Office of Readiness and Response

Published: 2023-07-12 | Source: Federal Register

CDC has modified its structure. This notice announces the reorganization of the Office of Readiness and Response (ORR). ORR reorganized to improve rapid response to disease outbreaks and public health emergencies within the United States and around the world.
