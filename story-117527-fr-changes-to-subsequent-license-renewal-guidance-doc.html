BREAKING: Federal Register: Changes to Subsequent License Renewal Guidance Documents

Published: 2021-02-25 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Nuclear Regulatory Commission (NRC) is issuing three Interim Staff Guidance (ISG) documents that update aging management criteria for mechanical, structural, and electrical structures and components in the NRC's subsequent license renewal (SLR) guidance documents.
