BREAKING: Federal Register: Cyber Incident Notification Requirements for Federally Insured Credit Unions

Published: 2022-07-27 | Source: Federal Register

Due to the increased frequency and severity of cyberattacks on the financial services sector, the NCUA Board is proposing to require a federally insured credit union that experiences a reportable cyber incident to report the incident to the NCUA as soon as possible and no later than 72 hours after the federally insured credit union reasonably believes that it has experienced a reportable cyber incident.
