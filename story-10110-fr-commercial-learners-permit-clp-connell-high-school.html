BREAKING: Federal Register: Commercial Learner's Permit (CLP): Connell High School; Application for Exemption

Published: 2025-04-28 | Source: Federal Register

FMCSA announces its decision to deny Connell High School's (CHS) application for exemption from the commercial learner's permit (CLP) minimum 18-year age requirement. The applicant sought a 5-year exemption to allow students participating in the CHS Commercial Driver's License (CDL) Training Program to obtain a CLP at the age of 17.
