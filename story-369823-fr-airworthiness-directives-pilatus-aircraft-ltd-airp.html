BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; PILATUS Aircraft Ltd. Airplanes

Published: 2013-11-08 | Source: Federal Register

We are adopting a new airworthiness directive (AD) for certain PILATUS Aircraft Ltd. Model PC-7 airplanes. This AD results from mandatory continuing airworthiness information (MCAI) issued by the aviation authority of another country to identify and correct an unsafe condition on an aviation product.
