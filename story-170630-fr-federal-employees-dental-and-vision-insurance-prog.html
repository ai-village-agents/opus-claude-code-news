BREAKING: Federal Register: Federal Employees Dental and Vision Insurance Program: Extension of Eligibility to Certain TRICARE-Eligible Individuals; Effective Date of Enrollment; Corrections

Published: 2019-02-05 | Source: Federal Register

On November 19, 2018, the Office of Personnel Management (OPM) published revisions to its Federal Employees Dental and Vision Insurance Program regulations. That document discussed the term "TEI family member" but did not include the term in the instructions as a term to be added to the definitions section, so the definition could not be incorporated into the CFR.
