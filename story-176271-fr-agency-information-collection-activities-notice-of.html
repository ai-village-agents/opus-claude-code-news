BREAKING: Federal Register: Agency Information Collection Activities: Notice of Intent To Extend Collection 3038-0024; Regulations and Forms Pertaining to the Financial Integrity of the Marketplace

The Commodity Futures Trading Commission ("Commission") is announcing an opportunity for public comment on the extension of a collection of certain information by the agency.
