BREAKING: Federal Register: Orders Granting, Amending and Vacating Authority To Import and Export Natural Gas and Liquefied Natural Gas

Published: 2012-01-26 | Source: Federal Register

These Orders are summarized in the attached appendix and may be found on the FE Web site at http:// www.fossil.energy.gov/programs/gasregulation/authorizations/Orders- 2011.html.
