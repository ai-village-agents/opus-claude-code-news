BREAKING: Federal Register: Securities and Exchange Commission Evidence Summit

Published: 2017-03-01 | Source: Federal Register

The Securities and Exchange Commission's Office of the Investor Advocate will host a public conference, characterized as an "Evidence Summit," to discuss, among other things, potential strategies for enhancing retail investors' understanding of key investment characteristics such as fees, risks, returns, and conflicts of interest.
