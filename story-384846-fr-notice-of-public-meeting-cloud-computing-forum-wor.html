BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Public Meeting-Cloud Computing Forum & Workshop V

Published: 2012-05-04 | Source: Federal Register

NIST announces the Cloud Computing Forum & Workshop V to be held on Tuesday, Wednesday and Thursday, June 5, 6 and 7, 2012. The format is a two-day forum followed by a one-day hands-on workshop. This workshop will provide information on the U.S. Government (USG) Cloud Computing Technology Roadmap initiative.
