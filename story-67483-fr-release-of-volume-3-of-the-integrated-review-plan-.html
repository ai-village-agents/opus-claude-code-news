BREAKING: Federal Register: Release of Volume 3 of the Integrated Review Plan in the Review of the Lead National Ambient Air Quality Standards

Published: 2023-05-15 | Source: Federal Register

On or about May 12, 2023, the Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is making available to the public, Volume 3 of the Integrated Review Plan for the Lead National Ambient Air Quality Standards (IRP). The national ambient air quality standards (NAAQS) for lead (Pb) are set to protect the public health and the public welfare from Pb in ambient air.
