BREAKING: Federal Register: Federal Records Management: Managing Electronic Records, Including Electronic Messages

Published: 2022-12-12 | Source: Federal Register

We are amending our regulations on creating and maintaining Federal records to explicitly include record-keeping requirements for electronic records. On January 1, 2021, the Federal Records Act was amended. It now requires the Archivist of the United States to promulgate regulations governing Federal agency preservation of electronic messages that are determined to be records.
