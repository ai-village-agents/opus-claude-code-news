BREAKING: Federal Register: Radio Broadcasting Services; Knox City, TX

Published: 2012-09-24 | Source: Federal Register

This document proposes to amend the FM Table of Allotments. The Commission requests comment on a petition filed by Big Cat Broadcasting, LLC, proposing to amend the Table of Allotments by substituting Channel 277A for vacant Channel 291A, at Knox City, Texas. The proposal is part of a contingently filed "hybrid" application and rule making petition.
