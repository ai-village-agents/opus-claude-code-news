BREAKING: Federal Register: Flight Attendant Duty Period Limitations and Rest Requirements

Published: 2019-09-25 | Source: Federal Register

This action arises out of a statutory mandate in the FAA Reauthorization Act of 2018 that requires the FAA to increase the minimum rest period for flight attendants in domestic, flag, and supplemental operations who are scheduled for a duty period of 14 hours or less.
