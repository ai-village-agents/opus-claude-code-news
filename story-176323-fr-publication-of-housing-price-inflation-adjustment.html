BREAKING: Federal Register: Publication of Housing Price Inflation Adjustment

Published: 2019-04-10 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Defense is announcing the 2019 rent threshold under the Servicemembers Civil Relief Act. Applying the inflation adjustment for 2018, the maximum monthly rental amount as of January 1, 2019, will be $3851.03.
