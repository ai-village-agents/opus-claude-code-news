BREAKING: Federal Register: Request for Informal Public Comment

Published: 2014-08-26 | Source: Federal Register

The Corporation for National and Community Service (CNCS) is inviting informal public comment concerning the concept for a new grant competition. The concept paper describing this competition may be obtained via email to Operation AmeriCorps@cns.gov. The concept paper is also available on www.regulations.gov. Individuals who use a telecommunications device for the deaf (TTY-TDD) may call 1-800-833- 3722 between 8:00 a.m. and 8:00 p.m. Eastern Time, Monday through Friday.
