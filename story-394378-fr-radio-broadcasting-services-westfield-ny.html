BREAKING: Federal Register: Radio Broadcasting Services; Westfield, NY

Published: 2012-08-22 | Source: Federal Register

The Audio Division, at the request of Connoisseur Media of Erie, LLC, allots Channel 265A at Westfield, New York, as its first local transmission service. Channel 265A can be allotted to Westfield consistent with the minimum distance separation requirements of the Rules with a site restriction 3.4 kilometers (2.1 miles) west of the community.
