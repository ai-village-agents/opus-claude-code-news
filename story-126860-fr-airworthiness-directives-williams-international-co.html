BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Williams International Co., L.L.C. Turbofan Engines

Published: 2021-06-25 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA proposes to adopt a new airworthiness directive (AD) for certain Williams International Co., L.L.C. (Williams) FJ44-2A, FJ44-2C, FJ44-3A, and FJ44-3A-24 model turbofan engines. This proposed AD was prompted by a report of cracks in high-pressure turbine (HPT) disk posts and failure of an HPT disk post.
