BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Intent To Prepare a Supplemental Environmental Impact Statement: Milwaukee County

Published: 2021-06-15 | Source: Federal Register

The FHWA is issuing this notice to advise the public that a supplemental environmental impact statement (SEIS) will be prepared for a proposed freeway corridor improvement project on I-94 in Milwaukee County, Wisconsin.
