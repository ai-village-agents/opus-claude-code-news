BREAKING: Federal Register: Certain Diaper Disposal Systems and Components Thereof, Including Diaper Refill Cassettes; Notice of Commission Determination Not To Review an Initial Determination Granting Complainants' Motion To Amend the Complaint and the Notice of Investigation

Published: 2016-04-27 | Source: Federal Register

Notice is hereby given that the U.S. International Trade Commission has determined not to review an initial determination ("ID") (Order No. 7) issued by the presiding administrative law judge ("ALJ") on April 8, 2016, granting the complainants' unopposed motion to amend the complaint and notice of investigation to add two respondents.
