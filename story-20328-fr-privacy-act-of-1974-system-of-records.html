BREAKING: Federal Register: Privacy Act of 1974; System of Records

Published: 2025-09-15 | Source: Federal Register

As required by the Privacy Act of 1974, notice is hereby given that VA is modifying the system of records titled "VHA Corporate Data Warehouses-VA" (172VA10). This system is used for clinical decision support, mobile applications presenting patient data, and statistical analysis to produce various management, workload tracking, and follow- up reports.
