BREAKING: Federal Register: Drawbridge Operation Regulation; Canaveral Barge Canal, Canaveral, FL

Published: 2017-09-21 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard has issued a temporary deviation from the operating schedule that governs the SR 401 Drawbridge, mile 5.5 at Port Canaveral, Florida. The deviation is necessary to allow fuel trucks a less restrictive access to and from Port Canaveral to pick up and deliver fuel due to the critical fuel supply in the region.
