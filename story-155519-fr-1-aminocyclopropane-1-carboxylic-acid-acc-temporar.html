BREAKING: Federal Register: 1-Aminocyclopropane-1-carboxylic Acid (ACC); Temporary Exemption From the Requirement of a Tolerance

Published: 2020-07-06 | Source: Federal Register

This regulation establishes a temporary exemption from the requirement of a tolerance for residues of 1-aminocyclopropane-1- carboxylic acid (ACC) in or on apples and stone fruits when used in accordance with the terms of the Experimental Use Permit (EUP) under EPA Number 73049-EUP-12.
