BREAKING: Federal Register: Atlantic Highly Migratory Species; Charter/Headboat Permit Commercial Sale Provision

Published: 2017-12-06 | Source: Federal Register

This final rule creates a separate permit endorsement provision for the commercial sale of Atlantic highly migratory species (HMS) by HMS Charter/Headboat permit holders.
