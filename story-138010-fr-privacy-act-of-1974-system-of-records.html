BREAKING: Federal Register: Privacy Act of 1974; System of Records

Published: 2021-11-16 | Source: Federal Register

Pursuant to the Privacy Act of 1974 the Export-Import Bank of the United States (EXIM) is proposing a new system of records notice (SORN). EXIM is proposing EXIM 21-02 Reasonable Accommodation Records.
