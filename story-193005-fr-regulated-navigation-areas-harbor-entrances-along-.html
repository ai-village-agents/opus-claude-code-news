BREAKING: Federal Register: Regulated Navigation Areas; Harbor Entrances Along the Coast of Northern California

Published: 2019-11-07 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is proposing to establish Regulated Navigation Areas (RNAs) at the harbor bar entrances to Crescent City Harbor, Humboldt Bay, Noyo River, and Morro Bay.
