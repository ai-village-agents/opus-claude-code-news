BREAKING: Federal Register: Endangered and Threatened Wildlife and Plants; Technical Correction for Tonkin Snub-Nosed Monkey

Published: 2017-09-06 | Source: Federal Register

We, the U.S. Fish and Wildlife Service, are making a technical correction to remove the endangered Tonkin snub-nosed monkey (Rhinopithecus avunculus) from certain regulations that apply to certain threatened primates. These regulations apply only to threatened species, and Tonkin snub-nosed monkeys were reclassified as an endangered species in 1990.
