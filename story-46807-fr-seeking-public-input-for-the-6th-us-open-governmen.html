BREAKING: Federal Register: Seeking Public Input for the 6th U.S. Open Government National Action Plan

Published: 2024-09-12 | Source: Federal Register

This notice informs the public that the United States Federal Government is initiating the co-creation process for its 6th U.S. Open Government National Action Plan. The GSA is inviting input from a wide and diverse array of stakeholders from the public, private, advocacy, not-for-profit, and philanthropic sectors, including State, local, Tribal, and territorial governments.
