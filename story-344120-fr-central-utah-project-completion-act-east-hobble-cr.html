BREAKING: Federal Register: Central Utah Project Completion Act; East Hobble Creek Restoration Project Draft Environmental Assessment

Published: 2013-01-14 | Source: Federal Register

The draft environmental assessment for the East Hobble Creek Restoration Project is available for public review and comment. The assessment analyzes the anticipated environmental effects of a proposed restoration effort on a portion of Lower Hobble Creek, near Springville, Utah.
