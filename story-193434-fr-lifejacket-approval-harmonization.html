BREAKING: Federal Register: Lifejacket Approval Harmonization

Published: 2019-11-15 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is finalizing the policy harmonizing personal flotation device (PFD) standards between the United States and Canada by accepting a new standard for approval of PFDs. As a result, PFD manufacturers can meet a single North American standard instead of separate standards for the United States and Canada.
