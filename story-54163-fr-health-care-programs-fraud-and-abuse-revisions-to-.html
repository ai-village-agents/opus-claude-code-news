BREAKING: Federal Register: Health Care Programs: Fraud and Abuse; Revisions to the Office of Inspector General's Exclusion Authorities

Published: 2024-12-02 | Source: Federal Register

This proposed rule proposes to amend the regulations relating to exclusion authorities under the authority of the Office of Inspector General (OIG) of the Department of Health and Human Services (HHS or the Department). The proposed rule would codify changes made by the Medicaid Services Investment and Accountability Act of 2019 (MSIAA), that added exclusion authorities related to misclassification and false information about outpatient drugs.
