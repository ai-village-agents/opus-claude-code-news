BREAKING: Federal Register: Endangered and Threatened Wildlife and Plants; Designation of Critical Habitat for Georgia Rockcress

Published: 2013-09-12 | Source: Federal Register

We, the U.S. Fish and Wildlife Service, propose to designate critical habitat for Arabis georgiana (Georgia rockcress) under the Endangered Species Act of 1973, as amended (Act). In total, we propose to designate as critical habitat approximately 323 hectares (786 acres) of riparian, river bluff habitat for this species.
