BREAKING: Federal Register: Nissan North America, Inc., Denial of Petition for Decision of Inconsequential Noncompliance

Published: 2022-04-11 | Source: Federal Register

Nissan North America, Inc. (Nissan) has determined that certain model year (MY) 2020 Nissan Sentra motor vehicles do not fully comply with Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard (FMVSS) No. 108, Lamps, Reflective Devices, and Associated Equipment. Nissan filed a noncompliance report dated August 26, 2020.
