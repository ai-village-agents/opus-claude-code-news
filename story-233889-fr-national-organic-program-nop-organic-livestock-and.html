BREAKING: Federal Register: National Organic Program (NOP); Organic Livestock and Poultry Practices Second Proposed Rule

Published: 2017-05-10 | Source: Federal Register

This proposed rule sets forth the U.S. Department of Agriculture's (USDA) intention to pursue one of several actions on the Organic Livestock and Poultry Practices Final Rule (FR) published in the Federal Register on January 19, 2017, by USDA's Agricultural Marketing Service (AMS). USDA is asking the public to comment on the possible actions USDA should take in regards to the disposition of the FR.
