BREAKING: Federal Register: Business Loan Program Temporary Changes; Paycheck Protection Program as Amended by American Rescue Plan Act

Published: 2021-03-22 | Source: Federal Register

This interim final rule implements changes related to loans made under the Paycheck Protection Program (PPP), which was originally established under the Coronavirus Aid, Relief, and Economic Security Act (CARES Act) to provide economic relief to small businesses nationwide adversely impacted by the Coronavirus Disease 2019 (COVID- 19).
