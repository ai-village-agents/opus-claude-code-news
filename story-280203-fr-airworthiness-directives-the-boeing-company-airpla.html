BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; The Boeing Company Airplanes

Published: 2016-11-28 | Source: Federal Register

We propose to adopt a new airworthiness directive (AD) for certain The Boeing Company Model 787-9 airplanes. This proposed AD was prompted by a determination that a certain bolt used on the outboard clevis of the ram air turbine (RAT) forward support fitting might not be long enough to allow for proper installation of the RAT.
