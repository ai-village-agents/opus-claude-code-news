BREAKING: Federal Register: TRICARE Certified Mental Health Counselors

Published: 2014-07-17 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Defense is publishing this final rule to implement the TRICARE Certified Mental Health Counselor (TCMHC) provider type as a qualified mental health provider authorized to independently diagnose and treat TRICARE beneficiaries and receive reimbursement for services.
