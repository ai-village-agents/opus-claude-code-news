BREAKING: Federal Register: Expanding Opportunities To Appear Before the Patent Trial and Appeal Board

Published: 2022-10-18 | Source: Federal Register

In this request for comments, the United States Patent and Trademark Office (USPTO or Office) seeks public input on the requirements to practice before the Patent Trial and Appeal Board (PTAB or Board). The Office seeks to ensure quality representation in PTAB proceedings under the Leahy-Smith America Invents Act (AIA) without creating undue restrictions or barriers to entry for practitioners wishing to appear before the PTAB.
