BREAKING: Federal Register: Medgar and Myrlie Evers Home National Monument

Published: 2020-12-04 | Source: Federal Register

As authorized by the John D. Dingell, Jr. Conservation, Management, and Recreation Act, the National Park Service announces that the Secretary of the Interior (Secretary) has established, in the State of Mississippi, Medgar and Myrlie Evers Home National Monument (National Monument) as a unit of the National Park System.
