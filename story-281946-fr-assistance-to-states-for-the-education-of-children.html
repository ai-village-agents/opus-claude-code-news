BREAKING: Federal Register: Assistance to States for the Education of Children With Disabilities; Preschool Grants for Children With Disabilities

Published: 2016-12-19 | Source: Federal Register

The Secretary amends the regulations under Part B of the Individuals with Disabilities Education Act (IDEA) governing the Assistance to States for the Education of Children with Disabilities program and the Preschool Grants for Children with Disabilities program.
