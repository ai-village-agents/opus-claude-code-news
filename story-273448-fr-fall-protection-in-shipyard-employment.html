BREAKING: Federal Register: Fall Protection in Shipyard Employment

Published: 2016-09-08 | Source: Federal Register

OSHA is considering revising and updating its safety standards that address access and egress (including stairways and ladders), fall and falling object protection, and scaffolds in shipbuilding, ship repair, shipbreaking, and other shipyard related employment (collectively referred to as "shipyard employment" in this document).
