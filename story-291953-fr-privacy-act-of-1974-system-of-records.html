BREAKING: Federal Register: Privacy Act of 1974; System of Records

Published: 2015-04-22 | Source: Federal Register

The Office of the Secretary of Defense proposes to add a new system of records, DWHS P51, entitled "WHS DefenseReady" to its inventory of record systems subject to the Privacy Act of 1974, as amended. The system will provide human resource information and system support for the OSD/WHS civilian and military workforce; and track the status of personnel actions, benefit queries, in-processing, out- processing, and military billets.
