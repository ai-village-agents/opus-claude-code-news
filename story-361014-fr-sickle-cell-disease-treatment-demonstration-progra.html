BREAKING: Federal Register: Sickle Cell Disease Treatment Demonstration Program

Published: 2013-07-24 | Source: Federal Register

HRSA currently has nine programs that are funded through competitive grant awards under the Sickle Cell Disease Treatment Demonstration Program. Three of these awards will end on August 31, 2013, prior to the end of the other six awards. HRSA intends to implement a non-competitive extension of the project period for the three grants that will end in 2013.
