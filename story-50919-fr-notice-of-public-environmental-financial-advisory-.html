BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Public Environmental Financial Advisory Board Webinar

Published: 2024-10-25 | Source: Federal Register

The purpose of the webinar is to explore strategies for mobilizing private capital in distributed energy generation projects to leverage the government's recent historic $27 billion Greenhouse Gas Reduction Fund (GGRF).
