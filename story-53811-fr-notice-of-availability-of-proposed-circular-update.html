BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Availability of Proposed Circular Updates and Request for Comments

Published: 2024-11-27 | Source: Federal Register

The Federal Transit Administration (FTA) has placed in the docket and on its website proposed guidance in the form of an updated circular pertaining to procurements financed in whole or part with Federal assistance awarded by FTA through grants or cooperative agreements (i.e., "third party" procurements).
