BREAKING: Federal Register: Determination That PARAFON FORTE DSC (Chlorzoxazone) Tablets, 500 Milligrams, Was Not Withdrawn From Sale for Reasons of Safety or Effectiveness

Published: 2016-07-14 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA or Agency) has determined that PARAFON FORTE DSC (chlorzoxazone) tablets, 500 milligrams (mg), were not withdrawn from sale for reasons of safety or effectiveness.
