BREAKING: Federal Register: Power Reactor In-Core Monitoring

Published: 2015-07-16 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Nuclear Regulatory Commission (NRC) has received a petition for rulemaking (PRM) requesting that the NRC amend its "Domestic Licensing of Production and Utilization Facilities" regulations to require all nuclear power plant (NPP) licensees to use in-core monitoring devices at different elevations and radial positions throughout the reactor core.
