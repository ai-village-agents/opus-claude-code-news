BREAKING: Federal Register: Special Local Regulation, Jacksonville Grand Prix of the Seas; St. Johns River, Jacksonville, FL

Published: 2016-04-19 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard proposes to establish a temporary special local regulation on the waters of the St. Johns River near downtown Jacksonville, FL during the 3rd Annual Jacksonville Grand Prix of the Seas, a series of high-speed boat races. This action is necessary to provide for the safety of life on the navigable waters during the event.
