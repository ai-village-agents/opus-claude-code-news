BREAKING: Federal Register: Food Labeling: Revision of the Nutrition and Supplement Facts Labels; Reopening of the Comment Period

Published: 2015-10-20 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA or we) is announcing the reopening of the comment period for certain documents associated with the proposed rule to amend FDA's labeling regulations for conventional foods and dietary supplements to provide updated nutrition information on the Nutrition Facts and Supplement Facts labels to assist consumers in maintaining healthy dietary practices.
