BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Public Comment Period and Public Meeting on the Revisions to the Submission Guidelines

Published: 2019-09-11 | Source: Federal Register

The National Capital Planning Commission (NCPC) has released revisions to the Submission Guidelines to update aspects of submission requirements related to transportation planning. Federal and non- federal agency applicants whose development proposals and plan are subject to statutory mandated Commission plan and project review must submit their proposals to the Commission following a process laid out in the Submission Guidelines.
