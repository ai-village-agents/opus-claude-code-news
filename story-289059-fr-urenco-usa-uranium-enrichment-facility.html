BREAKING: Federal Register: URENCO USA, Uranium Enrichment Facility

Published: 2015-03-19 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Nuclear Regulatory Commission (NRC) is considering approval of the URENCO USA (UUSA) license amendment request 12-10 (LAR- 12-10) that would authorize capacity expansion of the UUSA enrichment facility near Eunice, New Mexico.
