BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; The Boeing Company Airplanes

Published: 2012-05-09 | Source: Federal Register

We propose to adopt a new airworthiness directive (AD) for all The Boeing Company Model 717-200 airplanes. This proposed AD was prompted by multiple reports of cracks of overwing frames. This proposed AD would require repetitive inspections for cracking of the overwing frames, and related investigative and corrective actions if necessary.
