BREAKING: Federal Register: Schedule of Fees for Consular Services-Fee Change for Certain Border Crossing Cards

Published: 2021-06-15 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of State amends the Schedule of Fees for Consular Services (Schedule) for visa fees. More specifically, the rule amends the Border Crossing Card fee paid by a Mexican citizen under age 15 whose parent or guardian has or is applying for a border crossing card (the ``reduced Border Crossing Card fee'').
