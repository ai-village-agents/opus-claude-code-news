BREAKING: Federal Register: Atlantic Highly Migratory Species; Notice of Public Scoping Meetings

Published: 2012-06-08 | Source: Federal Register

NMFS has scheduled an additional scoping meeting for Amendment 7 to the 2006 Consolidated Atlantic Highly Migratory Species Fishery Management Plan, which will focus on management issues related to Atlantic bluefin tuna. A Notice of Intent published on April 23, 2012, which notified the public that NMFS intended to hold public scoping meetings and that it anticipated preparing a draft environmental impact statement.
