BREAKING: Federal Register: HUBZone and National Defense Authorization Act for Fiscal Year 2016 Amendments

Published: 2016-08-04 | Source: Federal Register

This direct final rule contains several amendments to the regulations governing the HUBZone Program. The U.S. Small Business Administration (SBA) is making changes to its regulations to implement section 866 of the National Defense Authorization Act for Fiscal Year 2016 (2016 NDAA).
