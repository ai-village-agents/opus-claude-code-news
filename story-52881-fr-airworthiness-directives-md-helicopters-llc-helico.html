BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; MD Helicopters, LLC, Helicopters

Published: 2024-11-19 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA is adopting a new airworthiness directive (AD) for certain MD Helicopters, LLC (MDHI), Model 369, 369A, 369D, 369E, 369F, 369FF, 369H, 369HE, 369HM, 369HS, 500N, and 600N helicopters. This AD was prompted by a report of a seized and damaged roller bearing in the pilot interconnecting cyclic torque tube (torque tube) assembly.
