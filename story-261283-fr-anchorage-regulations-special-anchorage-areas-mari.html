BREAKING: Federal Register: Anchorage Regulations; Special Anchorage Areas, Marina del Rey Harbor, California

Published: 2016-04-20 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard announces a public meeting to receive comments on a supplemental notice of proposed rulemaking (NPRM) to revise the special anchorage in Marina del Rey Harbor, California. Based on the comments received in response to the NPRM that was published in the Federal Register on May 28, 2014, we published a supplemental NPRM proposing to amend the shape and reduce the size of the special anchorage in Marina del Rey Harbor, California.
