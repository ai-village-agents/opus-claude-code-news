BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone; Atlantic Ocean, Cape Canaveral Offshore Launch Area, FL

Published: 2023-03-08 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is establishing a temporary safety zone for waters of the Atlantic Ocean, adjacent to Cape Canaveral, FL. This safety zone would implement a special activities provision of the William M. (Mac) Thornberry National Defense Authorization Act for Fiscal Year 2021. The Coast Guard is establishing this safety zone for the launch of the Terran I rocket, which is being launched by Relativity Space.
