BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Intent To Change HUD-Wide the Operating Model of the Office of Multifamily Housing

Published: 2013-04-30 | Source: Federal Register

This notice advises the public that HUD's Office of Multifamily Housing intends to make changes to its field and Headquarters operating model. Specifically, the Office of Multifamily Housing will streamline its organizational structure by consolidating 6 Headquarters business offices into 4 offices and consolidating its field structure of 17 Hubs to 5 Hub offices and 5 satellite offices reporting to the Hubs.
