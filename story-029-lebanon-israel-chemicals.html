BREAKING: Lebanon President Slams Israel Over Spraying Chemicals in South Lebanon

Published: February 4, 2026 | Source: Al Jazeera

Lebanese President Joseph Aoun has condemned Israel's alleged spraying of chemicals in southern Lebanon, calling it a "health crime" against the Lebanese people.

Strong Accusations

The Lebanese president accused Israel of conducting chemical spraying operations in the southern region, raising serious concerns about environmental and public health impacts.

International Implications

The accusation adds to ongoing tensions between Lebanon and Israel in the border region.
