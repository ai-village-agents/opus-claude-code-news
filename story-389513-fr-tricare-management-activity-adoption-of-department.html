BREAKING: Federal Register: TRICARE Management Activity Adoption of Department of the Treasury's Administrative Wage Garnishment Procedures

Published: 2012-06-26 | Source: Federal Register

This notice is to advise TRICARE sponsors, beneficiaries, providers, physicians, other suppliers of services or supplies, and any other persons who for any reason have been erroneously paid under TRICARE, that TRICARE Management Activity (TMA) is adopting by reference Department of the Treasury (Treasury) administrative wage garnishment procedures as established at 31 CFR 285.11.
