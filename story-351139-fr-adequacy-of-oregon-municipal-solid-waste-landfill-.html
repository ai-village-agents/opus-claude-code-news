BREAKING: Federal Register: Adequacy of Oregon Municipal Solid Waste Landfill Permit Program

Published: 2013-04-03 | Source: Federal Register

This action approves a modification to the State of Oregon's approved Municipal Solid Waste Landfill (MSWLF) permit program. The approved modification allows the State to issue Research, Development, and Demonstration (RD&D) Permits to owners and operators of MSWLF units in accordance with its State law.
