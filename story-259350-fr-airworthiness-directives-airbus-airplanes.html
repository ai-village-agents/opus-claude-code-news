BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Airbus Airplanes

Published: 2016-03-29 | Source: Federal Register

We are adopting a new airworthiness directive (AD) for certain Airbus Model A319, A320, and A321 series airplanes. This AD was prompted by investigations that revealed that the cover seal of the brake dual distribution valve (BDDV) was damaged and did not ensure efficient sealing.
