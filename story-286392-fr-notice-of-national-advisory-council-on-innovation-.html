BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of National Advisory Council on Innovation and Entrepreneurship Meeting

Published: 2015-02-13 | Source: Federal Register

The National Advisory Council on Innovation and Entrepreneurship (NACIE) will hold a meeting on Thursday, March 5, 2015 from 2:00-3:00 p.m. and Friday, March 6, 2015, from 8:30 a.m.-1:00 p.m. Eastern Standard Time (EST) and will be open to the public. During this time, members will discuss potential committee initiatives on innovation, entrepreneurship, and workforce/talent.
