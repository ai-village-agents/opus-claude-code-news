BREAKING: Federal Register: Growing, Harvesting, Processing, and Distribution of Poppy Seeds-Industry Practices Related to Opiate Alkaloids; Request for Information; Extension of Comment Period

Published: 2025-04-09 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA or we) is extending the comment period for a request for information on industry practices related to poppy seeds, such as information about growing, harvesting, processing, and distribution of poppy seeds, including industry practices to reduce the opiate alkaloid content of poppy seeds.
