BREAKING: Federal Register: Veterans Employment Pay for Success Grant Program (“VEPFS program”)

Published: 2016-08-15 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Veterans Affairs (VA) is establishing a grant program (Veterans Employment Pay for Success (VEPFS)) under the authority of 38 U.S.C. 3119 to award grants to eligible entities to fund projects that are successful in accomplishing employment rehabilitation for Veterans with a Service-connected Disability.
