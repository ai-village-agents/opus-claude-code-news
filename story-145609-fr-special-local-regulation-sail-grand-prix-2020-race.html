BREAKING: Federal Register: Special Local Regulation; Sail Grand Prix 2020 Race Event; San Francisco, CA

Published: 2020-02-28 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is proposing to establish a temporary special local regulation in the navigable waters of San Francisco Bay in San Francisco, CA in support of the San Francisco Sail Grand Prix 2020 official practice and race periods between April 30, 2020 and May 3, 2020. This special local regulation is necessary to ensure the safety of mariners transiting the area from the dangers of high-speed sailing activities associated with the Sail Grand Prix 2020 race event.
