BREAKING: Federal Register: Codex Alimentarius Commission: Meeting of the Codex Committee on Food Additives

Published: 2023-02-01 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Codex Office is sponsoring a public meeting on February 21, 2023, from 9-12 p.m. EST. The objective of the public meeting is to provide information and receive public comments on agenda items and draft United States (U.S.) positions to be discussed at the 53rd Session of the Codex Committee on Food Additives (CCFA) of the Codex Alimentarius Commission, in Hong Kong, People's Republic of China on March 27-31, 2023.
