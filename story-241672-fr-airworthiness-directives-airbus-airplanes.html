BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Airbus Airplanes

Published: 2017-08-18 | Source: Federal Register

We are superseding Airworthiness Directive (AD) 2015-22-06 which applied to all Airbus Model A318, A319, A320, and A321 series airplanes. AD 2015-22-06 required revising the After Start Normal Procedures section of the airplane flight manual (AFM) to provide procedures that address latent failures in the spoiler and elevator computer (SEC).
