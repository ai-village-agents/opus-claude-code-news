BREAKING: Federal Register: Medical Devices; General Hospital and Personal Use Devices; Classification of the Alternate Controller Enabled Infusion Pump

Published: 2022-02-04 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA, Agency, or we) is classifying the alternate controller enabled infusion pump into class II (special controls). The special controls that apply to the device type are identified in this order and will be part of the codified language for the alternate controller enabled infusion pump's classification.
