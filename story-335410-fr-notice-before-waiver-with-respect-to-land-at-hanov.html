BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice Before Waiver With Respect To Land at Hanover County Municipal Airport, Hanover, Virginia

Published: 2014-09-23 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA is publishing notice of proposed release of 2.94 acres of land at the Hanover County Airport, Hanover, Virginia to the Virginia Department of Transportation for construction of the I-95 and Lewistown Road Intersection improvements. An additional 1.07 Acres will be permanently utilized by Virginia Department of Transportation & Dominion Power within utility, and drainage easements.
