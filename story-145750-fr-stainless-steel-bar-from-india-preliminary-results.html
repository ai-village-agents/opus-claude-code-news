BREAKING: Federal Register: Stainless Steel Bar From India: Preliminary Results of Antidumping Duty Administrative Review; and Rescission of Review in Part; 2018-2019

Published: 2020-03-03 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Commerce (Commerce) preliminarily determines that producers/exporters subject to this review made sales of stainless steel bar (SS Bar) from India at less than fair value (LTFV) during the period of review (POR) February 1, 2018 through January 31, 2019. Interested parties are invited to comment on these preliminary results.
