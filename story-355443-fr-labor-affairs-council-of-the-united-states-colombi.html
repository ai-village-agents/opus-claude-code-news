BREAKING: Federal Register: Labor Affairs Council of the United States-Colombia Trade Promotion Agreement; Notice of Public Session Meeting

Published: 2013-05-21 | Source: Federal Register

Pursuant to Article 17.5 of the U.S.-Colombia Trade Promotion Agreement (TPA), the International Labor Affairs Bureau (ILAB) of the U.S. Department of Labor gives notice of the public session of the meeting of the Labor Affairs Council ("Council" or "LAC"). The LAC public session has been rescheduled and will be held the morning of June 5, 2013.
