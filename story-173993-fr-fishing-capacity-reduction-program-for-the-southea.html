BREAKING: Federal Register: Fishing Capacity Reduction Program for the Southeast Alaska Purse Seine Salmon Fishery

Published: 2019-03-13 | Source: Federal Register

In 2018 the Southeast Revitalization Association (SRA) conducted a bid selection process accepting 36 bids to remove Southeast Alaska Purse Seine salmon permits under a proposed second fishing capacity reduction loan program. In accordance with regulations, the SRA submitted a reduction plan to NMFS to implement the proposed second loan reduction program.
