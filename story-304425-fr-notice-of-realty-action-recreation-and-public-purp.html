BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Realty Action; Recreation and Public Purposes Act Classification for Lease in Chaffee County, Colorado

Published: 2015-09-18 | Source: Federal Register

The Bureau of Land Management (BLM) has examined and found suitable for classification for lease under the Recreation and Public Purposes (R&PP) Act, as amended (43 U.S.C. 860 et seq.), approximately 19.34 acres of public land in Chaffee County, Colorado. The BLM proposes to amend the Colorado Parks and Wildlife's (CPW) existing R&PP Lease (COC-49757) to include the following 19.34 acres in the BLM Salida East Recreation area.
