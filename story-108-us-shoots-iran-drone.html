BREAKING: US Says It Shot Down Iranian Drone Flying Towards Aircraft Carrier

Published: February 4, 2026 | Source: BBC

The United States military has shot down an Iranian drone that was flying towards a US aircraft carrier, in a significant escalation of tensions between the two nations.

Military Intercept

US forces destroyed the drone before it could reach the carrier, though details about the exact location and circumstances remain limited.

Rising Tensions

The incident comes amid already heightened tensions between the US and Iran, with diplomatic talks about Iran's nuclear program ongoing.

Regional Concerns

The confrontation raises fears of potential military escalation in a region already on edge over multiple conflicts.
