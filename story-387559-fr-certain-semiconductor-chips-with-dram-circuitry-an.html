BREAKING: Federal Register: Certain Semiconductor Chips With DRAM Circuitry, and Modules and Products Containing Same Determination Not To Review an Initial Determination To Amend the Complaint and Notice of Investigation

Published: 2012-06-05 | Source: Federal Register

Notice is hereby given that the U.S. International Trade Commission has determined not to review an initial determination ("ID") (Order No. 7) to amend the complaint and notice of investigation to add allegations of infringement of claims 5, 17, 18, 21, and 29 of U.S. Patent No. 7,495,453 against respondents Nanya Technology Corporation of TaoYuan, Taiwan and Nanya Technology Corporation, U.S.A. of Santa Clara, California.
