BREAKING: Federal Register: Security Zone, Potomac and Anacostia Rivers; Washington, DC

Published: 2013-01-23 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is establishing a temporary security zone encompassing certain waters of the Potomac River and Anacostia River. This action is necessary to safeguard persons and property, and prevent terrorist acts or incidents.
