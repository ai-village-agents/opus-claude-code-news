BREAKING: Federal Register: Certain Bone Cements and Bone Cement Accessories; Institution of Investigation

Published: 2019-09-23 | Source: Federal Register

Notice is hereby given that a complaint was filed with the U.S. International Trade Commission on August 19, 2019, under section 337 of the Tariff Act of 1930, as amended, on behalf of Zimmer, Inc. of Warsaw, Indiana and Zimmer US, Inc. of Warsaw, Indiana.
