BREAKING: Federal Register: Protection of Stratospheric Ozone: Revisions To Reporting and Recordkeeping for Imports and Exports

Published: 2016-02-09 | Source: Federal Register

EPA is taking direct final action on minor conforming edits to the stratospheric protection regulations to implement the International Trade Data System. This system allows businesses to transmit the transactional data required by multiple Federal agencies for the import and export of cargo through a single "window."
