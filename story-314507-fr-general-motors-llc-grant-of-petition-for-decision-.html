BREAKING: Federal Register: General Motors, LLC, Grant of Petition for Decision of Inconsequential Noncompliance

Published: 2014-01-21 | Source: Federal Register

General Motors, LLC (GM) has determined that certain model year (MY) 2013 Chevrolet Cruze, Chevrolet Volt, and Buick Verano passenger cars manufactured between November 15, 2012 and January 11, 2013, do not fully comply with paragraph S4.2.6 of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard (FMVSS) No. 202a, Head Restraints; Mandatory Applicability Begins on September 1, 2009.
