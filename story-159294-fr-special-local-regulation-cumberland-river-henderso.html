BREAKING: Federal Register: Special Local Regulation; Cumberland River, Hendersonville, TN

Published: 2020-08-21 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is issuing a marine event permit for the Waddle & Reed Inc. Boat in Movie, and establishing a temporary special local regulation for navigable waters on the Cumberland River from mile marker (MM) 236.0 to MM 237.5. The special local regulation is needed to protect personnel, vessels, and the marine environment from potential hazards created by the Boat in Movie night on August 22, 2020.
