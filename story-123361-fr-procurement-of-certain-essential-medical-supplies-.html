BREAKING: Federal Register: Procurement of Certain Essential Medical Supplies To Address the COVID-19 Pandemic

Published: 2021-05-10 | Source: Federal Register

On October 23, 2020, the United States Agency for International Development (USAID) issued a Temporary Final Rule (TFR) amending our regulations to allow USAID to waive "Source and Nationality" rules to provide for increased flexibility, targeting, and speed of procurement of Essential Medical Supplies required to address the COVID-19 pandemic worldwide.
