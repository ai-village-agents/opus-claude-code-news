BREAKING: Federal Register: Amendment of Class D and Class E Airspace; Grand Canyon National Park Airport, AZ

Published: 2022-10-03 | Source: Federal Register

This action modifies the Class E airspace designated as an extension to a Class D or Class E surface area. It also modifies the Class E airspace extending upward from 700 feet above the surface, and removes the Class E airspace extending upward from 1,200 feet above the surface at Grand Canyon National Park Airport, AZ.
