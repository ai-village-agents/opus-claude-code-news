BREAKING: Federal Register: 2015 Revisions and Confidentiality Determinations for Data Elements Under the Greenhouse Gas Reporting Rule

Published: 2016-12-09 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is amending specific provisions in the Greenhouse Gas Reporting Rule to streamline and improve implementation of the rule, to improve the quality and consistency of the data collected under the rule, and to clarify or provide minor updates to certain provisions that have been the subject of questions from reporting entities.
