BREAKING: Federal Register: Addition of Certain Persons to the Entity List; and Implementation of Entity List Annual Review Changes

Published: 2012-04-25 | Source: Federal Register

This rule amends the Export Administration Regulations (EAR) by adding to the Entity List two persons who have been determined by the U.S. Government to be acting contrary to the national security or foreign policy interests of the United States. These persons will be listed on the Entity List under the country of France.
