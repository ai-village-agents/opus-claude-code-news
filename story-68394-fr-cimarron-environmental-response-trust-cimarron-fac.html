BREAKING: Federal Register: Cimarron Environmental Response Trust; Cimarron Facility

Published: 2023-05-25 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Nuclear Regulatory Commission (NRC) has received a license amendment application from Cimarron Environmental Response Trust (CERT or the applicant) for the Cimarron Facility, located in the city of Guthrie, which is in Logan County, Oklahoma. The license authorizes Possession of Byproduct, Source, and Special Nuclear Material (SNM-928).
