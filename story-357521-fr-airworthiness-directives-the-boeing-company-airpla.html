BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; The Boeing Company Airplanes

Published: 2013-06-14 | Source: Federal Register

We are adopting a new airworthiness directive (AD) for certain The Boeing Company Model 777-200 and -300 series airplanes. This AD was prompted by reports of hydraulic fluid contamination (including contamination caused by hydraulic fluid in its liquid, vapor, and/or solid (coked) form) found in the strut forward dry bay.
