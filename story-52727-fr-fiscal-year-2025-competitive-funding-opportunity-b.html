BREAKING: Federal Register: Fiscal Year 2025 Competitive Funding Opportunity: Bus Safety and Accessibility Research Program

Published: 2024-11-18 | Source: Federal Register

The Federal Transit Administration (FTA) announces the opportunity to submit applications for the Bus Safety and Accessibility Research Program for up to $10,000,000 under the Public Transportation Innovation Program in multiple fiscal years. The strategic goal for this program is to make existing and new buses safer for their operators and vulnerable road users, and safer and more accessible for their passengers.
