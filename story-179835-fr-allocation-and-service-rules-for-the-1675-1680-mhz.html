BREAKING: Federal Register: Allocation and Service Rules for the 1675-1680 MHz Band

Published: 2019-05-22 | Source: Federal Register

In this document, the Federal Communications Commission proposes to reallocate the 1675-1680 MHz band for shared use between incumbent federal operations and new, non-federal flexible wireless (fixed or mobile) use operations. The Commission seeks comment on the appropriate sharing mechanisms that will protect incumbent federal operations while making the spectrum available for new, non-federal use.
