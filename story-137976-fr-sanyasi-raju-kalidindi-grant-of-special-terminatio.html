BREAKING: Federal Register: Sanyasi Raju Kalidindi; Grant of Special Termination

Published: 2021-11-16 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA) has issued an order under the Federal Food, Drug, and Cosmetic Act (FD&C Act) granting special termination of the debarment of Sanyasi Raju Kalidindi.
