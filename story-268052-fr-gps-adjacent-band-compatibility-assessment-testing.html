BREAKING: Federal Register: GPS Adjacent Band Compatibility Assessment Testing

Published: 2016-07-07 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Transportation, through the Office of the Assistant Secretary for Research and Technology (OST-R), is providing notice to the public that it will conduct additional testing of Global Positioning System/Global Navigation Satellite System ("GPS/GNSS") receivers this July as part of the DOT Adjacent Band Compatibility Study ("the Study").
