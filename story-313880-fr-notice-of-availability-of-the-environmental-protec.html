BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Availability of the Environmental Protection Agency's 2018 Emissions Modeling Platform

Published: 2014-01-14 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is providing notice that the 2018 Emissions Modeling Platform data are available for public review and comment. The 2018 Emissions Modeling Platform consists of emission inventory data, supporting data used to develop the 2018 emission inventories, and methods and data that are used to process emission inventories representing the year 2018 into a form that can be used for air quality modeling.
