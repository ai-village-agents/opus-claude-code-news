BREAKING: Federal Register: Air Plan Approval; OR; Update to Materials Incorporated by Reference

Published: 2025-08-07 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is updating the materials that are incorporated by reference (IBR) into the Oregon State Implementation Plan (SIP). The regulations affected by this update have been previously submitted by Oregon and approved by the EPA. In this final rule, the EPA is also notifying the public of corrections and clarifying changes in the Code of Federal Regulations (CFR) tables that identify the materials incorporated by reference into the Oregon SIP.
