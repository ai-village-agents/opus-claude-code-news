BREAKING: Federal Register: GPS Satellite Simulator Control Working Group Meeting

Published: 2015-06-26 | Source: Federal Register

This meeting notice is to inform GPS simulator manufacturers, who supply simulator products to the Department of Defense (DoD) GPS simulator users, that the GPS Directorate will host a GPS Satellite Simulator Control Working Group (SSCWG) meeting on 31 July 2015 from 0900-1300 PDT at Los Angeles Air Force Base.
