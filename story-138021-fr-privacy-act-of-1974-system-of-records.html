BREAKING: Federal Register: Privacy Act of 1974; System of Records

Published: 2021-11-16 | Source: Federal Register

In accordance with the Privacy Act of 1974, as amended, the Architectural and Transportation Barriers Compliance Board (Access Board or Board) is proposing to establish a new system of records titled, "USAB.001--Reasonable Accommodation Records." This system of records covers records related to the administration, adjudication, and implementation of reasonable accommodation requests by agency employees and applicants for employment.
