BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Inventory Completion: Sheboygan County Historical Museum, Sheboygan, WI

Published: 2012-04-19 | Source: Federal Register

The Sheboygan County Historical Museum has completed an inventory of human remains, in consultation with the appropriate Indian tribes, and has determined that there is no cultural affiliation between the remains and any present-day Indian tribe. Representatives of any Indian tribe that believes itself to be culturally affiliated with the human remains may contact the Sheboygan County Historical Museum.
