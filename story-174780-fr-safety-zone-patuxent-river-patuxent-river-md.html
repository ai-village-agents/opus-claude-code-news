BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone; Patuxent River, Patuxent River, MD

Published: 2019-03-22 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is establishing a temporary safety zone for certain waters of the Patuxent River. This action is necessary to provide for the safety of life on these navigable waters of the Patuxent River at Patuxent River, MD, during salvage operations of the vessel YP-702 on March 24, 2019 (with alternate of March 25, 2019, or March 26, 2019).
