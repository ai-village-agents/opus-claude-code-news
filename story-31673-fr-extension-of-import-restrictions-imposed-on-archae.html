BREAKING: Federal Register: Extension of Import Restrictions Imposed on Archaeological and Ecclesiastical Ethnological Material From Honduras

Published: 2024-03-12 | Source: Federal Register

This document amends the U.S. Customs and Border Protection (CBP) regulations to extend import restrictions on certain archaeological and ecclesiastical ethnological material from Honduras. The Assistant Secretary for Educational and Cultural Affairs, United States Department of State, has made the requisite determinations for extending the import restrictions, which were originally imposed by CBP Decision (CBP Dec.) 04-08 and last extended by CBP Dec. 19-03.
