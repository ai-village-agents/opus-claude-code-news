BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; The Boeing Company Airplanes

Published: 2021-12-28 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA proposes to adopt a new airworthiness directive (AD) for certain The Boeing Company Model 777-200 and -300 series airplanes. This proposed AD was prompted by reports of three incidents involving in-flight fan blade failures on certain Pratt & Whitney engines ("fan blades" are also known as "1st-stage low-pressure compressor (LPC) blades"--these terms are used interchangeably in this proposed AD).
