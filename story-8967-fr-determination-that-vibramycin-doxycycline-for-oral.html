BREAKING: Federal Register: Determination That VIBRAMYCIN (Doxycycline) for Oral Suspension, Equivalent 25 Milligrams Base/5 Milliliter, Was Not Withdrawn From Sale for Reasons of Safety or Effectiveness

Published: 2025-04-09 | Source: Federal Register
