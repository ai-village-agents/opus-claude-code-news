BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Beechcraft Corporation and Hawker Beechcraft Corporation

Published: 2013-08-20 | Source: Federal Register

We are adopting a new airworthiness directive (AD) for certain Beechcraft Corporation (type certificate previously held by Hawker Beechcraft Corporation) Models 58, 95-C55, E55, and 56TC airplanes; and Hawker Beechcraft Corporation Models 58P and 58TC airplanes (both type certificates previously held by Raytheon Aircraft Company).
