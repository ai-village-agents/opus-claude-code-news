BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Final Federal Agency Action on the Authorization for the Port of Corpus Christi Authority Channel Deepening Project

Published: 2024-07-17 | Source: Federal Register

USACE announces final agency action on the USACE authorization for the proposed deepening of the Port of Corpus Christi Authority Channel Deepening Project (CDP) located in and along the existing Corpus Christi Ship Channel.
