BREAKING: Federal Register: Classification Categories for Certain Supplements Under the Biosimilar User Fee Amendments of 2022; Draft Guidance for Industry; Availability

Published: 2023-08-11 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA or Agency) is announcing the availability of a draft guidance for industry entitled "Classification Categories for Certain Supplements Under BsUFA III." This draft guidance provides recommendations for applicants and FDA review staff on classification categories A, B, C, D, E, and F for original and resubmitted prior approval supplements submitted to approved applications under the Public Health Service Act (PHS Act).
