BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone; Delaware River and Schuylkill River, Philadelphia, PA

Published: 2019-10-22 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is establishing a temporary safety zone for navigable waters within a 200-yard radius of the derrick boat ELIZABETH for the duration of object removal operations within the Delaware and Schuylkill Rivers. The safety zone is needed to protect personnel, vessels, and the marine environment from potential hazards created by submerged object removal operations.
