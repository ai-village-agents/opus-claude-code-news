BREAKING: Federal Register: Medical Devices; Validated Instructions for Use and Validation Data Requirements for Certain Reusable Medical Devices in Premarket Notifications

Published: 2017-06-09 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA or Agency) has determined that it is necessary for manufacturers of certain reusable medical devices to include in their premarket notifications (510(k)s) instructions for use which have been validated and validation data regarding cleaning, disinfection, and sterilization, for which a substantial equivalence determination may be based.
