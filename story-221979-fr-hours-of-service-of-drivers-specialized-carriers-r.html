BREAKING: Federal Register: Hours of Service of Drivers: Specialized Carriers & Rigging Association (SC&RA); Application for Exemption

Published: 2018-11-27 | Source: Federal Register

FMCSA announces its decision to renew the Specialized Carriers & Rigging Association's (SC&RA) exemption from the 30-minute rest break rule of the Agency's hours-of-service (HOS) regulations for certain commercial motor vehicle (CMV) drivers. SC&RA currently holds an exemption valid through November 1, 2018. The exemption renewal is for five years.
