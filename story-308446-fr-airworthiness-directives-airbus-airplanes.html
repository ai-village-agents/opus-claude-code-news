BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Airbus Airplanes

Published: 2015-11-02 | Source: Federal Register

We propose to adopt a new airworthiness directive (AD) for certain Airbus Model A330-200, -200 Freighter, and -300 series airplanes; and Model A340-200 and -300 series airplanes. This proposed AD was prompted by reports that cracks were found on an adjacent hole of certain frames of the center wing box (CWB).
