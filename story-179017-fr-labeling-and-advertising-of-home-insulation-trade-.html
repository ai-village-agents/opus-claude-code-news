BREAKING: Federal Register: Labeling and Advertising of Home Insulation: Trade Regulation Rule

Published: 2019-05-13 | Source: Federal Register

The Federal Trade Commission ("Commission") amends its Trade Regulation Rule Concerning the Labeling and Advertising of Home Insulation ("R-value Rule" or "Rule") to clarify, streamline, and improve existing requirements.
