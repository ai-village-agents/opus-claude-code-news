BREAKING: Federal Register: Television Broadcasting Services Portland, Oregon

Published: 2020-10-29 | Source: Federal Register

The Commission has before it a petition for rulemaking filed by Sander Operating Co. III LLC (Sander), licensee of KGW, requesting the substitution of channel 26 for channel 8 at Portland in the DTV Table of Allotments.
