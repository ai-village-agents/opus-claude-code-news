BREAKING: Federal Register: Availability of Program Application Instructions for Medicare Improvements for Patients and Providers Act (MIPPA) Program Funds

Published: 2020-05-22 | Source: Federal Register

The purpose of MIPPA funding is to enhance statewide and local coalition building focused on outreach, education, and one-to-one assistance activities to Medicare beneficiaries likely to be eligible for the Low Income Subsidy program (LIS) or the Medicare Savings Programs (MSP), above and beyond those regular activities planned in response to other funding.
