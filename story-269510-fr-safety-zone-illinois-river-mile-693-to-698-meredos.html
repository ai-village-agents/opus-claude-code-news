BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone; Illinois River Mile 69.3 to 69.8; Meredosia, IL

Published: 2016-07-25 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is establishing a temporary safety zone for certain waters of the Illinois River from mile 69.3 to mile 69.8. This safety zone is needed to protect persons, property and infrastructure from potential damage and safety hazards associated with work being performed on new power lines across the river.
