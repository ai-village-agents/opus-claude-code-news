BREAKING: Federal Register: Availability of Draft National Toxicology Program Technical Reports; Request for Comments; Notice of Meeting

Published: 2014-03-18 | Source: Federal Register

The National Toxicology Program (NTP) announces the availability of four draft NTP Technical Reports (TRs) scheduled for peer review: bromodichloroacetic acid, CIMSTAR 3800, green tea extract, and indole-3-carbinol. The peer-review meeting is open to the public. Registration is requested for both public attendance and oral comment and required to access the webcast. Information about the meeting and registration are available at http://ntp.niehs.nih.gov/go/36051.
