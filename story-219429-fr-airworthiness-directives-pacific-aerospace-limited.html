BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Pacific Aerospace Limited Airplanes

Published: 2018-10-23 | Source: Federal Register

We propose to supersede Airworthiness Directive (AD) AD 2018- 04-09 for Pacific Aerospace Limited Model 750XL airplanes. This proposed AD results from mandatory continuing airworthiness information (MCAI) originated by an aviation authority of another country to identify and correct an unsafe condition on an aviation product.
