BREAKING: Federal Register: Sugar From Mexico: Amendment to the Agreement Suspending the Antidumping Duty Investigation

Published: 2017-07-11 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Commerce (the Department) and a representative of the signatory sugar producers/exporters accounting for substantially all imports of sugar from Mexico have signed an amendment to the Agreement Suspending the Antidumping Duty Investigation on Sugar from Mexico (AD Suspension Agreement).
