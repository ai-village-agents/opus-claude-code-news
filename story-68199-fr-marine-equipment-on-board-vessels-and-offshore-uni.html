BREAKING: Federal Register: Marine Equipment on Board Vessels and Offshore Units or Facilities

Published: 2023-05-23 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard proposes to revise regulations associated with the approval, carriage, and maintenance of certain safety equipment required on board vessels and offshore units or facilities. We are taking this action to align the regulations with the current industry practice and provide more transparent regulations for the regulated industry.
