BREAKING: Federal Register: Sweet Cherries Grown in Designated Counties in Washington; Decreased Assessment Rate

Published: 2012-12-06 | Source: Federal Register

This rule decreases the assessment rate established for the Washington Cherry Marketing Committee (Committee) for the 2012-2013 and subsequent fiscal periods from $0.40 to $0.18 per ton of sweet cherries handled. The Committee locally administers the marketing order which regulates the handling of sweet cherries grown in designated counties in Washington.
