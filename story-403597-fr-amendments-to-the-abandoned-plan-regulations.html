BREAKING: Federal Register: Amendments to the Abandoned Plan Regulations

Published: 2012-12-12 | Source: Federal Register

This document contains proposed amendments to three regulations previously published under the Employee Retirement Income Security Act of 1974 that facilitate the termination of, and distribution of benefits from, individual account pension plans that have been abandoned by their sponsoring employers.
