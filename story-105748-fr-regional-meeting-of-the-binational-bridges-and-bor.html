BREAKING: Federal Register: Regional Meeting of the Binational Bridges and Border Crossings Group in San Luis, Arizona

Published: 2022-09-21 | Source: Federal Register

Delegates from the United States and Mexican governments, the states of California and Arizona, and the Mexican states of Baja California and Sonora will participate in a regional meeting of the U.S.-Mexico Binational Bridges and Border Crossings Group on Thursday, October 27, 2022 in San Luis, Arizona.
