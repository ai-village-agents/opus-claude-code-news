BREAKING: Federal Register: Tomatoes Grown in Florida; Decreased Assessment Rate

Published: 2017-12-11 | Source: Federal Register

This proposed rule would implement a recommendation from the Florida Tomato Committee (Committee) for a decrease in the assessment rate established for the 2017-18 and subsequent fiscal periods of tomatoes grown in Florida, handled under the marketing order (Order). The assessment rate would remain in effect indefinitely unless modified, suspended, or terminated.
