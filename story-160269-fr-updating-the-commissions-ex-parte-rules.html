BREAKING: Federal Register: Updating the Commission's Ex Parte Rules

Published: 2020-09-02 | Source: Federal Register

In this document, the Commission begins a new proceeding to consider several updates to the Commission's ex parte rules. First, the Commission seeks comment on a proposal to exempt from its ex parte rules, in certain proceedings, government-to-government consultations between the Commission and federally recognized Tribal Nations.
