BREAKING: Federal Register: Sauk-Suiattle Indian Tribe Alcohol Control Ordinance

Published: 2019-12-19 | Source: Federal Register

This notice publishes the Alcohol Control Ordinance of the Sauk-Suiattle Indian Tribe. The alcohol control ordinance is to regulate and control the possession, sale, manufacture, and distribution of alcohol in conformity with the laws of the State of Washington for the purpose of generating new Tribal revenues.
