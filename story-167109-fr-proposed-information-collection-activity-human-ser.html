BREAKING: Federal Register: Proposed Information Collection Activity; Human Services Programs in Rural Contexts Study

Published: 2020-11-30 | Source: Federal Register

The Administration for Children and Families (ACF) at the U.S. Department of Health and Human Services is proposing to collect data on the challenges and unique opportunities of administering human services programs in rural contexts.
