BREAKING: Federal Register: Air Plan Approval; New Hampshire; Nonattainment Plan for the Central New Hampshire SO2 Nonattainment Area

Published: 2017-09-28 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is proposing to approve the State Implementation Plan (SIP) revision that the State of New Hampshire submitted to EPA on January 31, 2017 for attaining the 1- hour sulfur dioxide (SO2) primary national ambient air quality standard (NAAQS) for the Central New Hampshire Nonattainment Area. This plan (herein called a "nonattainment plan") includes New Hampshire's attainment demonstration and other elements required under the Clean Air Act (CAA). ...
