BREAKING: Federal Register: Clean Air Act Operating Permit Program; Petition for Objection to State Operating Permit for Duke Energy Indiana-Edwardsport Generating Station

Published: 2012-02-01 | Source: Federal Register

This document announces that the EPA Administrator has denied a petition from the Sierra Club, Valley Watch, and Citizen Action Coalition of Indiana (Petitioners) asking EPA to object to a Clean Air Act (Act) Title V operating permit for Duke Energy Indiana--Edwardsport Generating Station (Duke) issued by the Indiana Department of Environmental Management (IDEM).
