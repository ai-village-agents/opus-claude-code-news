BREAKING: Federal Register: Acceptance Criteria for Portable Oxygen Concentrators Used On Board Aircraft

Published: 2014-09-19 | Source: Federal Register

This rulemaking would replace Special Federal Aviation Regulation No. 106 with acceptance criteria for portable oxygen concentrators to be used by passengers in air carrier operations, commercial operations and certain other operations using large aircraft. Currently, the agency assesses each portable oxygen concentrator on a case-by-case basis to determine whether it is safe for use on board aircraft.
