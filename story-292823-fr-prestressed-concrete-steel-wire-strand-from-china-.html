BREAKING: Federal Register: Prestressed Concrete Steel Wire Strand From China; Institution of a Five-Year Review

Published: 2015-05-01 | Source: Federal Register

The Commission hereby gives notice that it has instituted a review pursuant to the Tariff Act of 1930 (the Act) to determine whether revocation of the antidumping and countervailing duty orders on prestressed concrete steel wire strand from China would be likely to lead to continuation or recurrence of material injury.
