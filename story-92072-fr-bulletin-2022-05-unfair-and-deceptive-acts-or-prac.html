BREAKING: Federal Register: Bulletin 2022-05: Unfair and Deceptive Acts or Practices That Impede Consumer Reviews

Published: 2022-03-28 | Source: Federal Register

Reviews of products and services help to promote fair, transparent, and competitive markets. When firms frustrate the ability of consumers to post honest reviews of products and services that they use, they may be engaged in conduct prohibited by the Consumer Financial Protection Act (CFPA).
