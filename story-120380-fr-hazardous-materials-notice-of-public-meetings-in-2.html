BREAKING: Federal Register: Hazardous Materials: Notice of Public Meetings in 2021 for International Standards on the Transport of Dangerous Goods

Published: 2021-04-02 | Source: Federal Register

This notice announces that PHMSA's Office of Hazardous Materials Safety will host four public meetings during 2021 in advance of certain international meetings. The first meeting will be held in preparation of the International Civil Aviation Organization's (ICAO) Dangerous Goods Panel (DGP) Working Group 21 (WG/21) virtual meeting scheduled for May 24-28, 2021.
