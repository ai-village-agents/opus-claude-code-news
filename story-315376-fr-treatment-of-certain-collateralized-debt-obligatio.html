BREAKING: Federal Register: Treatment of Certain Collateralized Debt Obligations Backed Primarily by Trust Preferred Securities With Regard to Prohibitions and Restrictions on Certain Interests in, and Relationships With, Hedge Funds and Private Equity Funds

Published: 2014-01-31 | Source: Federal Register

The OCC, Board, FDIC, CFTC and SEC (individually, an "Agency," and collectively, "the Agencies") are each adopting a common interim final rule that would permit banking entities to retain investments in certain pooled investment vehicles that invested their offering proceeds primarily in certain securities issued by community banking organizations of the type grandfathered under section 171 of the Dodd-Frank Wall Street Reform and Consumer Protection Act ("Dodd- Frank Act").
