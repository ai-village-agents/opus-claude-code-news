BREAKING: Federal Register: Guidance on Expanded Access to TSCA Confidential Business Information; Notice of Availability

Published: 2018-06-27 | Source: Federal Register

The amendments to the Toxic Substances Control Act in June 2016 expanded the categories of people to whom EPA may disclose TSCA confidential business information (CBI) by specifically authorizing EPA to disclose TSCA CBI to state, tribal, and local governments; environmental, health, and medical professionals; and emergency responders, under certain conditions, including consistency with guidance that EPA is required to develop.
