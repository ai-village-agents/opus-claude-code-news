BREAKING: Federal Register: Annual Stress Test-Technical and Conforming Changes

Published: 2017-10-27 | Source: Federal Register

The Office of the Comptroller of the Currency (OCC) is inviting comment on a proposed rule that would make several revisions to its stress testing rule. The proposed rule would change the range of possible "as-of" dates used in the global market shock component to conform to changes recently made by the Board of Governors of the Federal Reserve System (Board) to its stress testing regulations.
