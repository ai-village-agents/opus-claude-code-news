BREAKING: Federal Register: Commercial Customs Operations Advisory Committee (COAC) Charter Renewal

Published: 2018-07-10 | Source: Federal Register

The Secretaries of the Department of the Treasury and the Department of Homeland Security approved the renewal of the charter for the Commercial Customs Operations Advisory Committee (COAC). The committee's charter is effective May 15, 2018, and expires May 15, 2020. Section 109 of the Trade Facilitation and Trade Enforcement Act of 2015 (TFTEA) established the COAC.
