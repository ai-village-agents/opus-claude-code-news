BREAKING: Federal Register: Letter of Recommendation for Washington State Ferries Liquefied Natural Gas Conversion; Seattle, WA

Published: 2014-11-12 | Source: Federal Register

Coast Guard Sector Puget Sound received a Letter of Intent (LOI) and Waterway Suitability Assessment (WSA) from Washington State Ferries for a proposal to convert six Issaquah Class Ferries to Liquefied Natural Gas (LNG) propulsion.
