BREAKING: Federal Register: Establishment of Restricted Areas R-5305A, R-5305B, and R-5305C; Camp Lejeune, NC; and Restricted Areas R-5307A, R-5307B, and R-5307C; Cherry Point, NC

This action provides a second extension of the comment period for a NPRM that was originally published on March 20, 2025.
