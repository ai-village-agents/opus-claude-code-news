BREAKING: Federal Register: Drawbridge Operation Regulation; Canaveral Barge Canal, Canaveral, FL

Published: 2017-10-23 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard proposes to modify the operating schedule that governs the SR 401 Drawbridge, mile 5.5 at Port Canaveral, Florida. This modified regulation is necessary to reduce vehicular traffic congestion and to ensure the safety of the roadways while passengers are transiting to and from Cruise Terminal 10, which is used by Norwegian Cruise Line at Port Canaveral.
