BREAKING: Federal Register: Draft Guidance for Industry and Food and Drug Administration Staff; Safety Considerations for 510(k) Submissions To Mitigate the Risks of Misconnections With Small-Bore Connectors Intended for Enteral Applications; Availability

Published: 2012-07-27 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA) is announcing the availability of the draft guidance entitled "Safety Considerations for 510(k) Submissions to Mitigate the Risks of Misconnections with Small- bore Connectors Intended for Enteral Applications." The use of common connector designs, such as luer connectors, has led to unintended connections between devices that have different intended uses and has resulted in serious and sometimes fatal consequences to patients.
