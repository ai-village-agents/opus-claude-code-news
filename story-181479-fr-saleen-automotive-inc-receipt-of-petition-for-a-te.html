BREAKING: Federal Register: Saleen Automotive Inc.; Receipt of Petition for a Temporary Exemption From all Requirements of FMVSS No. 126 and the Air Bag Requirements of FMVSS No. 208

Published: 2019-06-12 | Source: Federal Register

Saleen Automotive Inc. has petitioned NHTSA for a temporary exemption from all requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard (FMVSS) No. 126, "Electronic stability control for light vehicles," and from the air bag requirements of FMVSS No. 208, "Occupant crash protection," for its newly-designed S1 model.
