BREAKING: Federal Register: Chattahoochee River National Recreation Area; Bicycling

Published: 2024-12-23 | Source: Federal Register

The National Park Service amends the special regulations for Chattahoochee River National Recreation Area to allow for bicycle use on approximately 20.6 miles of trails.

Document Type: Rule

Agency: Interior Department, National Park Service
