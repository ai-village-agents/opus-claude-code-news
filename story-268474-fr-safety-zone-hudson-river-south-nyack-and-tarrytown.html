BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone; Hudson River, South Nyack and Tarrytown, NY

Published: 2016-07-12 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is establishing a temporary moving safety zone for navigable waters of the Hudson River within a 200-yard radius of the LEFT COAST LIFTER crane barge during heavy lift operations. The safety zone is needed to protect personnel, vessels, and the marine environment from potential hazards created by heavy lift operations conducted by the crane barge in the vicinity of the Tappan Zee Bridge.
