BREAKING: Federal Register: Air Plan Approval; Connecticut; Regional Haze Five Year Progress Report

Published: 2019-09-25 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is proposing to approve the Connecticut regional haze progress report submitted as a State Implementation Plan (SIP) revision on June 30, 2015. This revision addresses the provisions of the Clean Air Act and its implementing regulations that require states to submit periodic reports describing progress on reasonable progress goals established for regional haze and a determination of adequacy of the state's existing regional haze SIP.
