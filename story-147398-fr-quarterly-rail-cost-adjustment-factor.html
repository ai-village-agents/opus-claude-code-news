BREAKING: Federal Register: Quarterly Rail Cost Adjustment Factor

Published: 2020-03-23 | Source: Federal Register

The Board has approved the second quarter 2020 Rail Cost Adjustment Factor (RCAF) and cost index filed by the Association of American Railroads. The second quarter 2020 RCAF (Unadjusted) is 1.051. The second quarter 2020 RCAF (Adjusted) is 0.442. The second quarter 2020 RCAF-5 is 0.417.
