BREAKING: Federal Register: Energy Conservation Program: Test Procedure for Commercial Refrigeration Equipment

Published: 2013-10-28 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Department of Energy (DOE) proposes to revise and reorganize its test procedure for commercial refrigeration equipment (CRE) in order to clarify certain terms, procedures, and compliance dates.
