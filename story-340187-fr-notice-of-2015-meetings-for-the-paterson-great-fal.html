BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of 2015 Meetings for the Paterson Great Falls National Historical Park Advisory Commission

Published: 2014-11-20 | Source: Federal Register

As required by the Federal Advisory Committee Act (5 U.S.C. Appendix 1-16), the National Park Service is hereby giving notice of the 2015 schedule of meetings for the Paterson Great Falls National Historical Park Advisory Commission. The Commission is authorized by the Omnibus Public Land Management Act of 2009 (16 U.S.C. 410lll(e)(2)), ". . . to advise the Secretary in the development and implementation of the management plan."
