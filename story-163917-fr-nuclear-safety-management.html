BREAKING: Federal Register: Nuclear Safety Management

Published: 2020-10-19 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Energy (DOE or the Department) is amending its regulations concerning nuclear safety management. These regulations govern the conduct of DOE contractors, DOE personnel, and other persons conducting activities (including providing items and services) that affect, or may affect, the safety of DOE nuclear facilities.
