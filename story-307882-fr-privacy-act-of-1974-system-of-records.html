BREAKING: Federal Register: Privacy Act of 1974; System of Records

Published: 2015-10-27 | Source: Federal Register

The Office of the Secretary of Defense proposes to add a new system of records, DPA 02, entitled "AFNConnect (AFNC)" which will document the eligibility and continued validation of authorized individuals Outside the Continental United States (OCONUS) who register an American Forces Network satellite decoder.
