BREAKING: Federal Register: Reorganization of Regulations on the Adjudication of Department of Homeland Security Practitioner Disciplinary Cases

Published: 2012-01-13 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Justice is amending its regulations governing the discipline of immigration practitioners as follows. First, the Department is removing unnecessary regulations and adding appropriate references to applicable regulations of the Department of Homeland Security (DHS).
