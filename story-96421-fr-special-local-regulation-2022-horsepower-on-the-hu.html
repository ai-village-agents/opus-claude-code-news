BREAKING: Federal Register: Special Local Regulation; 2022 Horsepower on the Hudson, Hudson River, Castleton, NY

Published: 2022-05-20 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is establishing temporary special local regulations on certain waters of the Hudson River in the vicinity of Castleton-on-the-Hudson, New York, in support of the Horsepower on the Hudson event on August 6, 2022. This action is necessary to ensure the safety of participants, participant vessels, spectators, and mariners transiting the area from the dangers associated with vessels operating at high-speeds during the Horsepower on the Hudson event.
