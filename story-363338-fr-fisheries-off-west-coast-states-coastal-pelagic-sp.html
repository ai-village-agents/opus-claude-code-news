BREAKING: Federal Register: Fisheries Off West Coast States; Coastal Pelagic Species Fisheries; Closure

Published: 2013-08-20 | Source: Federal Register

NMFS is prohibiting directed fishing for Pacific sardine off the coasts of Washington, Oregon and California. This action is necessary because the directed harvest allocation total for the second seasonal period (July 1-September 14) is projected to be reached by the effective date of this rule.
