BREAKING: Federal Register: Approval and Promulgation of Implementation Plans; Washington: General Regulations for Air Pollution Sources

Published: 2014-10-03 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is approving revisions to the Washington State Implementation Plan (SIP) submitted by the Department of Ecology (Ecology) on January 27, 2014. These revisions were submitted in accordance with the requirements of section 110 of the Clean Air Act, which requires states to develop a plan for the implementation, maintenance, and enforcement of the National Ambient Air Quality Standards (NAAQS).
