BREAKING: Federal Register: Nitric Acid; Exemption From the Requirement of a Tolerance

Published: 2012-08-31 | Source: Federal Register

This regulation establishes an exemption from the requirement of a tolerance for residues of nitric acid (CAS Reg. No. 7697-37-2) when used as an inert ingredient in antimicrobial pesticide formulations applied to food-contact surfaces in public eating places, dairy processing equipment, and food-processing equipment and utensils at a maximum level in the end-use concentration of 1,000 parts per million (ppm).
