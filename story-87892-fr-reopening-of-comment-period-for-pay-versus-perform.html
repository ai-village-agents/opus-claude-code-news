BREAKING: Federal Register: Reopening of Comment Period for Pay Versus Performance

Published: 2022-02-02 | Source: Federal Register

The Securities and Exchange Commission ("Commission") is reopening the comment period for its proposal to implement Section 953(a) of the Dodd-Frank Wall Street Reform and Consumer Protection Act of 2010 ("Dodd-Frank Act"). The proposed rule would amend the current executive compensation disclosure rule to require a description of how executive compensation actually paid by a registrant related to the financial performance of that company ("proposed rules").
