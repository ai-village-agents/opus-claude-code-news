BREAKING: Federal Register: Annual Pay Ranges for Physicians and Dentists of the Veterans Health Administration

Published: 2014-09-18 | Source: Federal Register

As required by the "Department of Veterans Affairs Health Care Personnel Enhancement Act of 2004" (Pub. L. 108-445, dated December 3, 2004) the Department of Veterans Affairs (VA) is hereby giving notice of annual pay ranges for Veterans Health Administration (VHA) physicians and dentists as prescribed by the Secretary for VA- wide applicability.
