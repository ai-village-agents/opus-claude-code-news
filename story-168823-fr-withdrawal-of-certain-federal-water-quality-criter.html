BREAKING: Federal Register: Withdrawal of Certain Federal Water Quality Criteria Applicable to Maine

Published: 2020-12-21 | Source: Federal Register

The United States Environmental Protection Agency (EPA or Agency) is taking final action to amend the Federal regulations to withdraw human health criteria (HHC) for toxic pollutants applicable to waters in the State of Maine. EPA is taking this action because Maine adopted, and EPA approved, HHC that the Agency determined are protective of the designated uses for these waters.
