BREAKING: Federal Register: Microgrid Program Strategy

Published: 2022-10-25 | Source: Federal Register

The Office of Electricity (OE) of the U.S. Department of Energy (DOE) requests comment from the public on its Microgrid Program Strategy located at https://www.energy.gov/oe/microgrid-strategy-call- public-comment. Response to this notice is voluntary. Responses to this notice may be used by the government for program planning on a non- attribution basis. OE therefore requests that no business proprietary information or copyrighted information be submitted in response to this notice. Please note...
