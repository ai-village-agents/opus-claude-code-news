BREAKING: Federal Register: Privacy Act of 1974; System of Records

Published: 2022-07-11 | Source: Federal Register

The Animal and Plant Health Inspection Service proposes to add a system of records to its inventory of records systems subject to the Privacy Act of 1974, as amended. The system of records is the Smuggling Interdiction and Trade Compliance (SITC) National Information Communication Activity System (SNICAS), USDA/APHIS-21.
