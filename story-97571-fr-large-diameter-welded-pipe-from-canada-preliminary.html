BREAKING: Federal Register: Large Diameter Welded Pipe From Canada: Preliminary Results of Antidumping Duty Administrative Review and Partial Rescission of Administrative Review; 2020-2021

Published: 2022-06-06 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Department of Commerce (Commerce) is conducting an administrative review of the antidumping duty (AD) order on large diameter welded pipe (welded pipe) from Canada. The period of review (POR) is May 1, 2020, through April 30, 2021. The review covers 46 producers or exporters of the subject merchandise including the sole mandatory respondent, Evraz.
