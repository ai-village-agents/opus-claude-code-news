BREAKING: Federal Register: Assessing the Resource Needs of the Generic Drug User Fee Amendments; Publication of Report; Request for Comments

Published: 2020-08-03 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA or Agency) is announcing the publication of a report, entitled "Independent Evaluation of the GDUFA Resource Capacity Planning Adjustment Methodology," providing options and recommendations for a new methodology to assess accurately changes in the resource needs of the generic drug review program.
