BREAKING: Federal Register: Special Conditions: Airbus, A350-900 Series Airplane; Crashworthiness-Emergency Landing Conditions

Published: 2014-01-08 | Source: Federal Register

This action proposes special conditions for the Airbus Model A350-900 series airplanes. These airplanes will have a novel or unusual design feature associated with crashworthiness of carbon fiber reinforced plastic used in the construction of the fuselage. The applicable airworthiness regulations do not contain adequate or appropriate safety standards for this design feature.
