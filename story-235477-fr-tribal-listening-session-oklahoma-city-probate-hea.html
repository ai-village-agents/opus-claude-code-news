BREAKING: Federal Register: Tribal Listening Session; Oklahoma City Probate Hearings Division Field Office

Published: 2017-05-31 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of the Interior's (Department) Office of Hearings and Appeals (OHA), Probate Hearings Division (Ph.D.) is responsible for probating Indian trust estates. OHA plans to close the Ph.D. Oklahoma City Field Office at the end of this fiscal year, September 30, 2017.
