BREAKING: Federal Register: Agency Forms Undergoing Paperwork Reduction Act Review; Data Calls for the Laboratory Response Network; Cancellation

Published: 2014-01-31 | Source: Federal Register

The Centers for Disease Control and Prevention is cancelling the 30-Day Information Collection Request, 14-0881, concerning the Data Calls for the Laboratory Response Network (79 FR 4165), published January 24, 2014. The purpose behind this notice cancellation is that a 60-day FRN was previously published on December 2, 2013 (78 FR 27087).
