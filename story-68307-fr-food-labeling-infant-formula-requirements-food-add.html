BREAKING: Federal Register: Food Labeling, Infant Formula Requirements, Food Additives and Generally Recognized as Safe Substances, New Dietary Ingredient Notification; Technical Amendments; Correction

Published: 2023-05-24 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration is correcting a final rule entitled "Food Labeling, Infant Formula Requirements, Food Additives and Generally Recognized as Safe Substances, New Dietary Ingredient Notification; Technical Amendments" that appeared in the Federal Register of March 24, 2023. The final rule corrected typographical errors, corrected errors in sample labels, restored inadvertent omissions, and updated office and organization names, addresses, and other references.
