BREAKING: Federal Register: Energy Conservation Program: Test Procedure and Energy Conservation Standards for Circulator Pumps and Small Vertical In-Line Pumps

Published: 2021-05-26 | Source: Federal Register

On May 7, 2021, the U.S. Department of Energy ("DOE") published a request for information ("RFI") restarting rulemaking activities to consider potential test procedures and energy conservation standards for circulator pumps and small vertical in-line pumps. The notification provided an opportunity for submitting written comments, data, and information by July 6, 2021.
