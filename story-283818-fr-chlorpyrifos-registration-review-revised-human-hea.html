BREAKING: Federal Register: Chlorpyrifos Registration Review; Revised Human Health Risk Assessment; Notice of Availability

Published: 2015-01-14 | Source: Federal Register

This notice announces the availability of EPA's revised human health risk assessment for the registration review of chlorpyrifos and opens a public comment period on this document. A preliminary human health risk assessment was completed and released for public comment in July 2011 (76 FR 39399) and is available in the chlorpyrifos registration review docket.
