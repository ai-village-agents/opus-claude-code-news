BREAKING: Federal Register: FirstEnergy Nuclear Operating Company; Davis-Besse Nuclear Power Station, Unit No. 1

Published: 2019-08-09 | Source: Federal Register

(FES) to withdraw the FirstEnergy Nuclear Operating Company (FENOC, the licensee) application dated February 5, 2019, for a proposed amendment to Renewed Facility Operating License No. NPF-3.
