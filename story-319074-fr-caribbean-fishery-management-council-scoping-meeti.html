BREAKING: Federal Register: Caribbean Fishery Management Council; Scoping Meetings

Published: 2014-03-17 | Source: Federal Register

The Caribbean Fishery Management Council (Council) is transitioning from species specific fishery management to island- specific fisheries management for the exclusive economic zones of Puerto Rico, St. Thomas/St. John and St. Croix separately. This transition is in response to the numerous requests received by the Council to consider the differences among the islands in the U.S. Caribbean.
