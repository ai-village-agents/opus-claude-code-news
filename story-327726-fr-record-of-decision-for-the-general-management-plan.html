BREAKING: Federal Register: Record of Decision for the General Management Plan, Fort Raleigh National Historic Site, North Carolina

Published: 2014-06-24 | Source: Federal Register

Pursuant to 42U.S.C. 4332(2)(C) of the National Environmental Policy Act of 1969, the National Park Service (NPS) announces the availability of the Record of Decision (ROD) for the General Management Plan (GMP) for Fort Raleigh National Historic Site (National Historic Site). On April 25, 2014, the Regional Director, Southeast Region, approved the ROD for the project.
