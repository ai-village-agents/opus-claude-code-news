BREAKING: Federal Register: Revision of Land Management Plan for the Nantahala and Pisgah National Forests

Published: 2014-03-12 | Source: Federal Register

As directed by the National Forest Management Act, the USDA Forest Service is preparing the Nantahala and Pisgah National Forests' revised land management plan (forest plan) and will also prepare an Environmental Impact Statement (EIS) for this revised forest plan. This notice briefly describes the nature of the decision to be made, a general proposed action based on the preliminary identified need to change the existing plan, and information concerning public participation.
