BREAKING: Federal Register: Default Electronic Disclosure by Employee Pension Benefit Plans Under ERISA

Published: 2020-05-27 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Labor is adopting in this document a new, additional safe harbor for employee benefit plan administrators to use electronic media, as a default, to furnish information to participants and beneficiaries of plans subject to the Employee Retirement Income Security Act of 1974 (ERISA).
