BREAKING: Federal Register: Atlantic Highly Migratory Species; Large Coastal and Small Coastal Atlantic Shark Management Measures

Published: 2015-08-18 | Source: Federal Register

This final rule implements Amendment 6 to the 2006 Consolidated Highly Migratory Species (HMS) Fishery Management Plan (FMP) (Amendment 6) to increase management flexibility to adapt to the changing needs of the Atlantic shark fisheries; prevent overfishing while achieving on a continuing basis optimum yield; and rebuild overfished shark stocks.
