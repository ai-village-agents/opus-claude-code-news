BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Saab AB, Saab Aerosystems Airplanes

Published: 2012-01-03 | Source: Federal Register

We are superseding an existing airworthiness directive (AD) for all Saab AB, Saab Aerosystems Model 340A (SAAB/SF340A) and SAAB 340B airplanes. That AD currently requires an inspection of the main landing gear (MLG) separation bolt harness for broken wires and corroded connectors, and corrective actions if necessary; and for certain airplanes, a modification of the MLG separation bolt's electrical harness.
