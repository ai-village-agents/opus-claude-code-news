BREAKING: Federal Register: Technical Amendment To List of User Fee Airports: Addition of Three Airports, Removal of Two Airports

Published: 2021-09-23 | Source: Federal Register

This document amends U.S. Customs and Border Protection (CBP) regulations by revising the list of user fee airports. User fee airports are airports that have been approved by the Commissioner of CBP to receive, for a fee, the customs services of CBP officers for processing aircraft, passengers, and cargo entering the United States, but that do not qualify for designation as international or landing rights airports.
