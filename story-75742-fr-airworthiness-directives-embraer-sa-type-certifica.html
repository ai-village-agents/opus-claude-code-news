Published: 2023-08-30 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA proposes to adopt a new airworthiness directive (AD) for certain Embraer S.A. Model EMB-135BJ airplanes. This proposed AD was prompted by reports of missing sealant on the rivets installed in the interface between rib 3 and the wing skin.
