BREAKING: Federal Register: Privacy Act of 1974; System of Records Notice

Published: 2015-10-20 | Source: Federal Register

In accordance with the requirements of the Privacy Act of 1974, as amended (5 U.S.C. 552a), the Office of Medicare Hearings and Appeals (OMHA) within the Office of the Secretary of Health and Human Services (HHS) is establishing a new system of records, System No. 09- 90-1501, entitled "Administrative Law Judge (ALJ) Working File, Office of Medicare Hearings and Appeals," to cover OMHA ALJ working files previously maintained as part of the Social Security Administration's (SSA) ALJ Working File ...
