BREAKING: Federal Register: Public Comment on EPA's National Enforcement Initiatives for Fiscal Years 2017-2019

Published: 2015-09-15 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is soliciting public comment and recommendations on national enforcement initiatives (NEI) for fiscal years 2017-2019. EPA selects these initiatives every three years in order to focus federal resources on the most important environmental problems where noncompliance is a significant contributing factor and where federal enforcement attention can make a difference.
