BREAKING: Federal Register: Atlantic Highly Migratory Species; Archival Tag Management Measures

Published: 2016-08-19 | Source: Federal Register

This final rule revises the regulations that currently require persons surgically implanting or externally affixing archival tags on Atlantic highly migratory species (HMS) to obtain written authorization from NMFS, and that require fishermen to report their catches of Atlantic HMS with such tags to NMFS.
