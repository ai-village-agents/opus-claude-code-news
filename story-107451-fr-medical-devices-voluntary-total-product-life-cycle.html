BREAKING: Federal Register: Medical Devices; Voluntary Total Product Life Cycle Advisory Program Pilot

Published: 2022-10-12 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration's (FDA, Agency, or we) Center for Devices and Radiological Health (CDRH or Center) is announcing its voluntary Total Product Life Cycle (TPLC) Advisory Program (TAP) Pilot that will begin in fiscal year (FY) 2023 with the initial phase, hereafter referred to as the TAP Pilot Soft Launch.
