BREAKING: Federal Register: Proposed Amendment of Class E Airspace; Medford, WI and Waupaca, WI

Published: 2017-06-07 | Source: Federal Register

This action proposes to modify Class E airspace extending up to 700 feet above the surface at Taylor County Airport, Medford, WI and Waupaca Municipal Airport, Waupaca, WI, to accommodate new standard instrument approach procedures (SIAPS) for instrument flight rules (IFR) operations at these airports. This action is necessary due to the decommissioning of the Medford and Waupaca non directional radio beacons (NDB), and cancellation of NDB approaches.
