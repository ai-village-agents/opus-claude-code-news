BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Proposed New Fee Site; Federal Lands Recreation Enhancement Act

Published: 2016-06-14 | Source: Federal Register

The Hoosier National Forest is proposing to charge a fee at Saddle Lake Recreation Area Campground. This includes a $5 fee at Saddle Lake Campground per site per night year round. Fees are assessed based on the level of amenities and services provided, cost of operations and maintenance, and market assessment.
