BREAKING: Federal Register: Amendment of Class D and Class E Airspace; Saginaw, MI

Published: 2024-02-27 | Source: Federal Register

This action proposes to amend the Class D and Class E airspace at Saginaw, MI. The FAA is proposing this action as the result of airspace reviews conducted as part of the decommissioning of the Mount Pleasant very high frequency omnidirectional range (VOR) as part of the VOR Minimum Operational Network (MON) Program.
