BREAKING: Federal Register: Parts and Accessories Necessary for Safe Operation; Application for an Exemption From Laydon Composites Ltd.

Published: 2020-05-18 | Source: Federal Register

The FMCSA announces its decision to grant Laydon Composites Ltd.'s (Laydon) application for a limited 5-year exemption to allow motor carriers to operate certain commercial motor vehicles (CMVs) that are equipped with Laydon's OptiTail\TM\ aerodynamic device with rear identification lamps and rear clearance lamps that are mounted lower than currently permitted by the Agency's regulations.
