BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone; Woldenburg Park, Mississippi River, New Orleans, LA

Published: 2013-01-31 | Source: Federal Register

The Captain of the Port New Orleans, under the authority of the Ports and Waterways Safety Act, has established a temporary safety zone on the Mississippi River in the vicinity of Woldenburg Park, mile marker 94 to mile marker 96, extending out 300 feet from the East Bank of the Mississippi River during Super Bowl 2013 celebratory events.
