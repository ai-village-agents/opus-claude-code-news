BREAKING: Federal Register: Freedom of Information Act “Still Interested” Inquiry

Published: 2025-09-16 | Source: Federal Register

The Freedom of Information Act (5 U.S.C. 552) provides that agencies shall make records available to the public, subject to certain exemptions. In line with the Department of Justice (DOJ) Office of Information Policy (OIP) guidance, agencies may confirm whether requesters are "still interested" in pending FOIA requests before continuing processing, particularly when circumstances have changed significantly.
