BREAKING: Federal Register: Timekeeping Requirement

Published: 2021-05-19 | Source: Federal Register

The Legal Services Corporation (LSC) is adopting a final rule amending its regulation related to the timekeeping requirements of employees at LSC funding recipients. The final rule makes multiple changes to how recipients keep time.
