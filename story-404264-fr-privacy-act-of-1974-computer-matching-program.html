BREAKING: Federal Register: Privacy Act of 1974: Computer Matching Program

Published: 2012-12-20 | Source: Federal Register

Notice is hereby given that the Department of Veterans Affairs (VA) intends to conduct a recurring computer matching program. This will match personnel records of the Department of Defense (DoD) with VA records of benefit recipients under the Montgomery GI Bill and Post-9/ 11 GI Bill.
