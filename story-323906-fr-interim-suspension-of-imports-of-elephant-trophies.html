BREAKING: Federal Register: Interim Suspension of Imports of Elephant Trophies From Zimbabwe

Published: 2014-05-12 | Source: Federal Register

On April 4, 2014, the U.S. Fish and Wildlife Service (Service) announced an interim suspension on importation of sport-hunted African elephant trophies taken in Zimbabwe during the 2014 season (on April 17, 2014, the Service revised this finding, primarily to clarify that the suspension applied only to elephants hunted on or after April 4, 2014).
