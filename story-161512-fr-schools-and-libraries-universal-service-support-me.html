BREAKING: Federal Register: Schools and Libraries Universal Service Support Mechanism

Published: 2020-09-21 | Source: Federal Register

In this document, the Wireline Competition Bureau (Bureau) adopts, on an emergency basis, temporary rules to provide immediate relief to schools that participate in the E-Rate program as they continue to contend with the ongoing disruptions caused by the pandemic.
