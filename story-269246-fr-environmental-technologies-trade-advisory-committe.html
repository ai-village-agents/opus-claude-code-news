BREAKING: Federal Register: Environmental Technologies Trade Advisory Committee (ETTAC), Request for Nominations

Published: 2016-07-21 | Source: Federal Register

This notice sets forth a request for nominations to serve on the Environmental Technologies Trade Advisory Committee (ETTAC). The ETTAC was established pursuant to provisions under Title IV of the Jobs Through Trade Expansion Act, 22. U.S.C. 2151, and under the Federal Advisory Committee Act, 5 U.S.C. App. 2.
