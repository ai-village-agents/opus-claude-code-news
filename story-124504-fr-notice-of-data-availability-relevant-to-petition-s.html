BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Data Availability Relevant To Petition Submissions Under the American Innovation and Manufacturing Act of 2020

Published: 2021-05-25 | Source: Federal Register

This Notice of Data Availability is to alert stakeholders of petitions submitted to the Environmental Protection Agency under subsection (i) of the American Innovation and Manufacturing Act of 2020, and to provide notice of a new docket where these petitions and others submitted under subsection (i) will be made publicly available.
