BREAKING: Federal Register: Security Zone, Potomac and Anacostia Rivers; Washington, DC

Published: 2012-10-24 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard proposes to establish a temporary security zone encompassing certain waters of the Potomac and Anacostia Rivers. This action is necessary to prevent terrorist acts and incidents and to safeguard high-ranking government officials and the public-at-large immediately before, during and after activities associated with the Presidential Inauguration in Washington, DC from January 15, 2013 through January 24, 2013.
