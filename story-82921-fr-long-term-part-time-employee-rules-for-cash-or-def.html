BREAKING: Federal Register: Long-Term, Part-Time Employee Rules for Cash or Deferred Arrangements Under Section 401(k)

Published: 2023-11-27 | Source: Federal Register

This document sets forth a proposed regulation that would amend the rules applicable to plans that include cash or deferred arrangements under section 401(k) to provide guidance with respect to long-term, part-time employees. The proposed regulation reflects statutory changes made by the SECURE Act and the SECURE 2.0 Act that relate to long-term, part-time employees.
