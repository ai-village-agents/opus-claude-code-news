BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Airbus SAS Airplanes

Published: 2020-04-27 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA proposes to adopt a new airworthiness directive (AD) for all Airbus SAS Model A350-941 airplanes. This proposed AD was prompted by reports that the latches for the forward and aft pressure relief doors could be opened during exposure to fire, leading to a breach in the engine core firewall.
