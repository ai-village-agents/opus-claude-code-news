BREAKING: Federal Register: Determinations of Attainment by the Attainment Date, Extensions of the Attainment Date, and Reclassification of Several Areas Classified as Moderate for the 2008 Ozone National Ambient Air Quality Standards

Published: 2018-11-14 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is proposing three actions related to the attainment date for 11 areas classified as "Moderate" for the 2008 ozone National Ambient Air Quality Standards (NAAQS). First, the agency is proposing to determine that two areas-- the Baltimore, Maryland, and Mariposa County, California, nonattainment areas--attained the standard by the July 20, 2018, attainment date.
