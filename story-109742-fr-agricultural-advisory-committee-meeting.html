BREAKING: Federal Register: Agricultural Advisory Committee; Meeting

Published: 2022-11-10 | Source: Federal Register

The Commodity Futures Trading Commission (CFTC) announces that on December 7, 2022 from 9:00 a.m. to 12:45 p.m. (Eastern Standard Time), the Agricultural Advisory Committee (AAC or Committee) will hold an in-person public meeting at the CFTC's Washington, DC headquarters with options for the public to attend virtually.
