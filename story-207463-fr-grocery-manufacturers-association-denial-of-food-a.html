BREAKING: Federal Register: Grocery Manufacturers Association; Denial of Food Additive Petition

Published: 2018-05-21 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA or we) is denying a food additive petition (FAP 5A4811), submitted by the Grocery Manufacturers Association (GMA), requesting that the food additive regulations be amended to provide for the safe use of partially hydrogenated vegetable oils (PHOs) in certain food applications.
