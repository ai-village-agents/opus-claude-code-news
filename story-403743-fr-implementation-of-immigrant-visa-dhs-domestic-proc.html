BREAKING: Federal Register: Implementation of Immigrant Visa DHS Domestic Processing Fee

Published: 2012-12-14 | Source: Federal Register

U.S. Citizenship and Immigration Services (USCIS) announces that as of February 1, 2013, USCIS will begin to collect a $165 Immigrant Visa DHS Domestic Processing Fee (USCIS Immigrant Fee) from individuals who have been issued immigrant visas by the U.S. Department of State (DOS) and are applying for admission to the United States.
