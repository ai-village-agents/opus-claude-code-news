BREAKING: Federal Register: Special Local Regulations; Ironman 70.3 Augusta Triathlon, Savannah River

Published: 2016-09-16 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard will enforce the Ironman 70.3 Augusta Triathlon, Savannah River, Special Local Regulation from 7 a.m. through 11 a.m. on September 25, 2016. This action is necessary to ensure safety of life on navigable waterways of the United States during this event.
