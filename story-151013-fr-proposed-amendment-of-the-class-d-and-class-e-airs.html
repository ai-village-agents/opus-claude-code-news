BREAKING: Federal Register: Proposed Amendment of the Class D and Class E Airspace and Revocation of Class E Airspace; Erie and Corry, PA

Published: 2020-05-07 | Source: Federal Register

This action proposes to amend the Class D airspace, Class E surface area airspace, and Class E airspace extending upward from 700 feet above the surface at Erie International Airport/Tom Ridge Field, Erie, PA; revoke the Class E airspace area designated as an extension to Class D and Class E surface area at Erie International Airport/Tom Ridge Field; and amend the Class E airspace extending upward from 700 feet above the surface at Corry-Lawrence Airport, Corry, PA.
