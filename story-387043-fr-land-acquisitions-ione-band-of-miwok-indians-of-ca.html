BREAKING: Federal Register: Land Acquisitions; Ione Band of Miwok Indians of California

Published: 2012-05-30 | Source: Federal Register

The Acting Assistant Secretary--Indian Affairs made a final agency determination to acquire 228.04 acres of land into trust for the Ione Band of Miwok Indians of California on May 24, 2012.
