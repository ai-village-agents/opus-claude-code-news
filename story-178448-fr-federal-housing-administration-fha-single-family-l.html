BREAKING: Federal Register: Federal Housing Administration (FHA): Single-Family Loan Sale Program; Advance Notice of Proposed Rulemaking and Request for Public Comment

Published: 2019-05-06 | Source: Federal Register

This notice seeks comments regarding FHA's Single-Family Loan Sale Program (the Program). The Program has been operating under demonstration and general disposition authority, through which eligible, single-family mortgage loans assigned to FHA in exchange for claim payment and mortgage notes are sold competitively to maximize recoveries and strengthen the FHA Mutual Mortgage Insurance Fund ("MMIF").
