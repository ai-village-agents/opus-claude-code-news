BREAKING: Federal Register: Approval and Promulgation of Implementation Plans; Idaho: Stationary Source Permitting Revisions

Published: 2016-06-09 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) proposes to approve, and incorporate by reference, revisions to the Idaho State Implementation Plan submitted on May 21, 2015. In the submission, Idaho revised stationary source permitting rules, including the addition of facility-wide emission limits and nonmetallic mineral processing plant regulations.
