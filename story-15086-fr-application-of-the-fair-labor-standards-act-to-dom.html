BREAKING: Federal Register: Application of the Fair Labor Standards Act to Domestic Service

Published: 2025-07-02 | Source: Federal Register

In 1974, Congress applied the Fair Labor Standards Act (FLSA) to "domestic service" employees, but exempted employees who provide "companionship services" from the minimum wage and overtime requirements and also exempted live-in domestic service employees from overtime. In 1975, the Department promulgated regulations defining companionship services and permitting third party employers to claim these exemptions.
