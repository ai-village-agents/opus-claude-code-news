BREAKING: Federal Register: Allocation of Creditable Foreign Taxes

Published: 2016-02-04 | Source: Federal Register

This document contains temporary regulations that provide guidance relating to the allocation by a partnership of creditable foreign tax expenditures. These temporary regulations are necessary to improve the operation of an existing safe harbor rule that is used for determining whether allocations of creditable foreign tax expenditures are deemed to be in accordance with the partners' interests in the partnership.
