BREAKING: Federal Register: Meeting of the Public Safety Officer Medal of Valor Review Board

Published: 2022-06-16 | Source: Federal Register

This is an announcement of a meeting (via WebEx/conference call-in) of the Public Safety Officer Medal of Valor Review Board to cover a range of issues of importance to the Board, to include but not limited to: Membership/terms; nomination eligibility; pending 2021-2022 nominations; program marketing and outreach.
