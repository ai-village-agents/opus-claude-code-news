BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Intent To Review and Update the Generic Environmental Impact Statement for License Renewal of Nuclear Plants

Published: 2020-08-04 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Nuclear Regulatory Commission (NRC) intends to gather information through the public scoping process to support the review to determine whether to update NUREG-1437, "Generic Environmental Impact Statement for License Renewal of Nuclear Plants--Final Report" (LR GEIS). The NRC is seeking public input on the proposed action and has scheduled public scoping meetings.
