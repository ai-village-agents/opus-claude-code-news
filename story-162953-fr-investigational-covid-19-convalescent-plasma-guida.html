BREAKING: Federal Register: Investigational COVID-19 Convalescent Plasma; Guidance for Industry; Withdrawal of Guidance; Correction

Published: 2020-10-07 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA) is correcting a notice that published in the Federal Register of September 21, 2020. The document announced the withdrawal of a final guidance for industry entitled "Investigational COVID-19 Convalescent Plasma," which was issued in April 2020 and updated in May 2020.
