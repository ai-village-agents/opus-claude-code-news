BREAKING: Federal Register: Designation of Cameroon for Temporary Protected Status

Published: 2022-06-07 | Source: Federal Register

Through this notice, the Department of Homeland Security (DHS) announces that the Secretary of Homeland Security (Secretary) is designating Cameroon for Temporary Protected Status (TPS) for 18 months, effective June 7, 2022, through December 7, 2023.
