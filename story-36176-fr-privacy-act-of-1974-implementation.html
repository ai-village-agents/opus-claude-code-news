BREAKING: Federal Register: Privacy Act of 1974; Implementation

Published: 2024-05-06 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Defense (DoD or Department) is giving concurrent notice of a new system of records titled "All-domain Anomaly Resolution Office (AARO) Report System," AARO-0001, and this rulemaking, which exempts portions of this system of records from certain provisions of the Privacy Act of 1974, as amended, because of national security.
