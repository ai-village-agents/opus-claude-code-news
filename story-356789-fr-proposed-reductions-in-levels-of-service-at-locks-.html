BREAKING: Federal Register: Proposed Reductions in Levels of Service at Locks and Dams on the J Bennett Johnston Waterway (Red River)

Published: 2013-06-06 | Source: Federal Register

It is proposed that the hours of availability at Lindy C. Boggs and John H. Overton Locks on the J Bennett Johnston Waterway (Red River) will remain at the current schedule of 24 hours per day, 7 days per week, 365 days per year.
