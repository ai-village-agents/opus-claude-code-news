BREAKING: Federal Register: Andean Trade Preference Act: Impact on the U.S. Economy and on Andean Drug Crop Eradication

Published: 2014-05-20 | Source: Federal Register

Section 206 of the ATPA (19 U.S.C. 3204) requires the Commission to report biennially to the Congress by September 30 of each reporting year on the economic impact of the Act on U.S. industries and U.S. consumers, as well as on the effectiveness of the Act in promoting drug related crop eradication and crop substitution efforts by beneficiary countries.
