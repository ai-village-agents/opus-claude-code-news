BREAKING: Federal Register: Federal Credit Union Ownership of Fixed Assets

Published: 2014-08-11 | Source: Federal Register

The NCUA Board (Board) proposes to amend its regulation governing federal credit union (FCU) ownership of fixed assets to provide regulatory relief and to help FCUs better manage their fixed assets. The proposed rule provides greater flexibility to FCUs by removing the waiver requirement for FCUs to exceed the five percent aggregate limit on investments in fixed assets.
