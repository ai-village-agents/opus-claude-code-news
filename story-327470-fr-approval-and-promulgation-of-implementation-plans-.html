BREAKING: Federal Register: Approval and Promulgation of Implementation Plans; State of Nevada; Update to Materials Incorporated by Reference

Published: 2014-06-19 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is updating the materials submitted by the State of Nevada that are incorporated by reference (IBR) into the Nevada State Implementation Plan (SIP). In this action, EPA is also notifying the public of the correction of certain typographical errors within the IBR table.
