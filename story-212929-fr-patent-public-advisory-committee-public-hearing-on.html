BREAKING: Federal Register: Patent Public Advisory Committee Public Hearing on the Proposed Patent Fee Schedule

Published: 2018-08-01 | Source: Federal Register

Under Section 10 of the America Invents Act (AIA), the United States Patent and Trademark Office (USPTO) may set or adjust by rule any patent or trademark fee established, authorized, or charged, respectively. The USPTO currently is planning to propose to set or adjust patent fees pursuant to its Section 10 fee setting authority.
