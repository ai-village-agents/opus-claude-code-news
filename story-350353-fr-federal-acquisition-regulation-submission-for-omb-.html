BREAKING: Federal Register: Federal Acquisition Regulation; Submission for OMB Review; Taxpayer Identification Number Information

Published: 2013-03-26 | Source: Federal Register

Under the provisions of the Paperwork Reduction Act, the Regulatory Secretariat will be submitting to the Office of Management and Budget (OMB) a request to review and approve an extension of a previously approved information collection requirement concerning Taxpayer Identification Number Information. A notice was published in the Federal Register at 77 FR 51782, on August 27, 2012. No comments were received.
