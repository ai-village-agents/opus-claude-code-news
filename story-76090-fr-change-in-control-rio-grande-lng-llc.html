BREAKING: Federal Register: Change In Control: Rio Grande LNG, LLC

Published: 2023-09-05 | Source: Federal Register

The Office of Fossil Energy and Carbon Management (FECM) of the Department of Energy (DOE) gives notice of receipt of a Statement and Notice of Change in Control (Notice) filed by Rio Grande LNG, LLC (Rio Grande LNG) on August 16, 2023. The Notice describes changes in Rio Grande LNG's upstream ownership. The Notice was filed under the Natural Gas Act (NGA).
