BREAKING: Federal Register: Delegation of Authority for the Office of Lead Hazard Control and Healthy Homes

Published: 2016-12-12 | Source: Federal Register

Under the Residential Lead- Based Paint Hazard Reduction Act of 1992, the Office of Lead Hazard Control and Healthy Homes (OLHCHH) is authorized to develop, demonstrate, and promote measures to correct lead-based paint-related health and safety hazards in the home environment that affect children, particularly of low-income families.
