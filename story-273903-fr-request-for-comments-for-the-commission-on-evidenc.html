BREAKING: Federal Register: Request for Comments for the Commission on Evidence-Based Policymaking

Published: 2016-09-14 | Source: Federal Register

The Evidence-Based Policymaking Commission Act of 2016 (Pub. L. 114-140), enacted March 30, 2016, established a 15-member Commission on Evidence-Based Policymaking. The Commission is charged with examining strategies to increase the availability and use of government data, in order to build evidence related to government programs and policies, while protecting the privacy and confidentiality of the data.
