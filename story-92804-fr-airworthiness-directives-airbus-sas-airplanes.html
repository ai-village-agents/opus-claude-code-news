BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Airbus SAS Airplanes

Published: 2022-04-05 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA is adopting a new airworthiness directive (AD) for all Airbus SAS Model A350-941 and -1041 airplanes. This AD was prompted by a report that certain overheat detection system (OHDS) sensing elements may not properly detect thermal bleed leak events due to a quality escape during the manufacturing process.
