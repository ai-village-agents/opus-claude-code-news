BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; BAE SYSTEMS (OPERATIONS) LIMITED Airplanes

Published: 2012-02-08 | Source: Federal Register

We propose to adopt a new airworthiness directive (AD) for all BAE SYSTEMS (OPERATIONS) LIMITED Model BAe 146 and Avro 146-RJ airplanes. This proposed AD was prompted by reports of baggage bay fire bottles that can be misassembled such that two squib electrical connectors can be cross-connected.
