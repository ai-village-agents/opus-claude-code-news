BREAKING: Federal Register: Unmanned Aircraft Systems Integration Pilot Program-Announcement of Establishment of Program and Request for Applications

Published: 2017-11-08 | Source: Federal Register

Through the FAA, the DOT announces a new pilot program that encourages State, local, and tribal governments, in partnership with UAS operators and other private sector stakeholders, to conduct advanced operations safely and with public support in affected communities.
