BREAKING: Federal Register: Regulation of Fuels and Fuel Additives: 2012 Renewable Fuel Standards

Published: 2012-01-09 | Source: Federal Register

Under the Clean Air Act Section 211(o), the Environmental Protection Agency is required to set the renewable fuel standards each November for the following year. In general the standards are designed to ensure that the applicable volumes of renewable fuel specified in the statue are used.
