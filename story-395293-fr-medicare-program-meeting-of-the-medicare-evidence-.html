BREAKING: Federal Register: Medicare Program; Meeting of the Medicare Evidence Development and Coverage Advisory Committee-November 14, 2012

Published: 2012-08-31 | Source: Federal Register

This notice announces that a public meeting of the Medicare Evidence Development & Coverage Advisory Committee (MEDCAC) ("Committee") will be held on Wednesday, November 14, 2012. The Committee generally provides advice and recommendations concerning the adequacy of scientific evidence needed to determine whether certain medical items and services can be covered under the Medicare statute.
