BREAKING: Federal Register: Privacy Act of 1974; Department of Transportation, Office of the Secretary of Transportation; DOT/ALL-25; Department of Transportation Biographies System of Records

Published: 2013-05-08 | Source: Federal Register

In accordance with the Privacy Act of 1974, the Department of Transportation's Office of the Secretary of Transportation (DOT/OST) intends to establish a DOT-wide System of Records of Transportation system of records titled, "DOT/ALL-25, Department of Transportation Biographies System of Records."
