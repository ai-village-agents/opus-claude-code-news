BREAKING: Federal Register: Investment Adviser Marketing

Published: 2021-03-05 | Source: Federal Register

The Securities and Exchange Commission (the "Commission" or the "SEC") is adopting amendments under the Investment Advisers Act of 1940 (the "Advisers Act" or the "Act") to update rules that govern investment adviser marketing. The amendments will create a merged rule that will replace both the current advertising and cash solicitation rules.
