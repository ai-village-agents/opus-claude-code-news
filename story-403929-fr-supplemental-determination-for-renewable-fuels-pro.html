BREAKING: Federal Register: Supplemental Determination for Renewable Fuels Produced Under the Final RFS2 Program From Grain Sorghum

Published: 2012-12-17 | Source: Federal Register

EPA is issuing a supplemental rule associated with the Renewable Fuel Standard (RFS) program. This final rule contains a lifecycle GHG analysis for grain sorghum ethanol and a regulatory determination that grain sorghum ethanol qualifies as a renewable fuel under the RFS Program.
