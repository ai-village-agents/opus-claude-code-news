BREAKING: Federal Register: Changes To Implement Derivation Proceedings

Published: 2012-09-11 | Source: Federal Register

The United States Patent and Trademark Office (Office or USPTO) is revising the rules of practice to implement the provisions of the Leahy-Smith America Invents Act (AIA) that create a new derivation proceeding to be conducted before the Patent Trial and Appeal Board (Board).
