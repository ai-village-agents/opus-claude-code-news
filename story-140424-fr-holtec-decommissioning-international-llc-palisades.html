BREAKING: Federal Register: Holtec Decommissioning International, LLC; Palisades Nuclear Plant

Published: 2021-12-20 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Nuclear Regulatory Commission (NRC) is issuing an exemption in response to a December 23, 2020, request from Holtec Decommissioning International, LLC (HDI). The exemption permits HDI to make withdrawals from the Palisades Nuclear Plant (PNP) Decommissioning Trust Fund (DTF) for spent fuel management and site restoration activities at PNP without prior notification to the NRC.
