BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; The Boeing Company Airplanes

Published: 2017-02-07 | Source: Federal Register

We are adopting a new airworthiness directive (AD) for all The Boeing Company Model 737-100, -200, -200C, -300, -400, and -500 series airplanes. This AD was prompted by reports of skin cracking found at the corners of the aft entry and aft galley doorways.
