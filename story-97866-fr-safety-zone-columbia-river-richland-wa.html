BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone; Columbia River, Richland, WA

Published: 2022-06-08 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is establishing a temporary safety zone for certain waters of the Columbia River. This action is necessary to provide for the safety of participants and the maritime public during a high-speed boat race from June 24, 2022 through June 26, 2022 on these navigable waters of the Columbia River in Richland, WA.
