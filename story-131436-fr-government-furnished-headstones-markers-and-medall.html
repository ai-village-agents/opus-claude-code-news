BREAKING: Federal Register: Government-Furnished Headstones, Markers, and Medallions; Unmarked Graves

Published: 2021-08-25 | Source: Federal Register

On August 6, 2021, the Department of Veterans Affairs (VA) published in the Federal Register a final rule that amended regulations regarding the provision of Government-furnished headstones, markers, and medallions to eligible individuals. This correction addresses minor technical errors in the published final rule and revises the amendatory text to correct a section reference.
