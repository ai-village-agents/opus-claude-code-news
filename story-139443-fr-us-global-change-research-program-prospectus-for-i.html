BREAKING: Federal Register: U.S. Global Change Research Program Prospectus for Its National Global Change Research Plan 2022-2031

Published: 2021-12-06 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Global Change Research Program (USGCRP), in collaboration with the Office of Science and Technology Policy (OSTP), requests comments from the public on the draft prospectus of its upcoming decadal Strategic Plan. The USGCRP is nearing the end of the implementation phase of its current plan, the National Global Change Research Plan 2012-2021; the Global Change Research Act calls for a 10- year plan with periodic updates.
