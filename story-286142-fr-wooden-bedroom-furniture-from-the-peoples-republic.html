BREAKING: Federal Register: Wooden Bedroom Furniture From the People's Republic of China: Preliminary Results of Antidumping Duty Administrative Review and New Shipper Reviews; 2013

Published: 2015-02-11 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Commerce ("the Department") is conducting the ninth administrative review ("AR") and a new shipper review ("NSR") of the antidumping duty order on wooden bedroom furniture ("WBF") from the People's Republic of China ("PRC"). The AR covers 28 exporters of subject merchandise, of which the Department selected one company for individual examination. The NSR covers one exporter and producer of subject merchandise.
