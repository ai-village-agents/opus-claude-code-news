BREAKING: Federal Register: Hazardous Materials Packaging-Composite Cylinder Standards; Public Meeting

Published: 2013-03-13 | Source: Federal Register

This notice is to advise interested persons that PHMSA will conduct a public meeting to provide further clarification regarding PHMSA's intent to modify certain special permits in accordance with 49 CFR 107.121. These special permits authorize the manufacture, marking, sale and use of non-DOT specification composite cylinders.
