BREAKING: Federal Register: Extension of, and New Combined Petition Option for Participation in, the Expanded Collaborative Search Pilot Program

Published: 2023-01-18 | Source: Federal Register

To speed up patent examination and give applicants more comprehensive prior art by combining the search expertise of United States Patent and Trademark Office (USPTO), Japan Patent Office (JPO), and Korean Intellectual Property Office (KIPO) examiners before issuing a first Office action, the USPTO, in partnership with the JPO and the KIPO, is extending the Expanded Collaborative Search Pilot (CSP) program for an additional two years, through October 31, 2024.
