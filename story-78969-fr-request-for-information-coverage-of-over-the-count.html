BREAKING: Federal Register: Request for Information; Coverage of Over-the-Counter Preventive Services

Published: 2023-10-04 | Source: Federal Register

This document is a request for information (RFI) regarding the application of the preventive services requirements under section 2713 of the Public Health Service Act (PHS Act) to over-the-counter (OTC) preventive items and services available without a prescription by a health care provider.
