BREAKING: Federal Register: Special Local Regulations; Southern California Annual Marine Events for the Los Angeles-Long Beach Captain of the Port Zone

Published: 2023-11-24 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard will enforce multiple special local regulations codified in federal regulations for recurring marine events taking place in December 2023 located in the Los Angeles--Long Beach Captain of the Port Zone. This action is necessary and intended to provide for the safety of life and property on navigable waterways during these events.
