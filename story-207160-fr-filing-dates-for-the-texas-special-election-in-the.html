BREAKING: Federal Register: Filing Dates for the Texas Special Election in the 27th Congressional District

Published: 2018-05-16 | Source: Federal Register

Texas has scheduled a special general election on June 30, 2018, to fill the U.S. House of Representatives seat in the 27th Congressional District vacated by Representative Blake Farenthold. There are two possible elections, but only one may be necessary. Under Texas law, all qualified candidates, regardless of party affiliation, will appear on the ballot.
