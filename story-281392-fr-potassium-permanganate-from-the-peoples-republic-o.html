BREAKING: Federal Register: Potassium Permanganate From the People's Republic of China: Preliminary Results of the 2015 Antidumping Duty Administrative Review

Published: 2016-12-13 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Commerce (the "Department") is conducting an administrative review of the antidumping duty ("AD") order on potassium permanganate from the People's Republic of China (the "PRC"). The period of review ("POR") is January 1, 2015 through December 31, 2015.
