BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Intent To Prepare Environmental Impact Statement for Alcatraz Ferry Embarkation Site, Golden Gate National Recreation Area, San Francisco and Marin Counties, CA

Published: 2012-06-01 | Source: Federal Register

In accordance with Sec. 102(2)(C) of the National Environmental Policy Act (NEPA) of 1969, and pursuant to the Council on Environmental Quality's regulations (40 CFR parts 1500-08), the National Park Service (NPS) is initiating the conservation planning and environmental impact analysis process for the proposed establishment of a long-term ferry embarkation site for passenger ferry service between the northern San Francisco waterfront and Alcatraz Island.
