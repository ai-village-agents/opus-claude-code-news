BREAKING: Federal Register: Automation of CBP Form I-418 for Vessels

Published: 2021-12-28 | Source: Federal Register

This rule amends the regulations in title 8 and title 19 of the Code of Federal Regulations (CFR) regarding the submission of U.S. Customs and Border Protection (CBP) Form I-418, Passenger List--Crew List (Form I-418) in paper form.
