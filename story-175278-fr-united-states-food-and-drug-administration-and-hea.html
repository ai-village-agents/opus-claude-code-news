BREAKING: Federal Register: United States Food and Drug Administration and Health Canada Joint Regional Consultation on the International Council for Harmonisation of Technical Requirements for Pharmaceuticals for Human Use; Public Meeting; Request for Comments

Published: 2019-03-28 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA or Agency) is announcing a regional public meeting entitled "U.S. Food and Drug Administration and Health Canada Joint Regional Consultation on the International Council for Harmonisation of Technical Requirements for Pharmaceuticals for Human Use (ICH)."
