BREAKING: Federal Register: Universal Proxy

Published: 2016-11-10 | Source: Federal Register

We are proposing amendments to the federal proxy rules to require the use of universal proxies in all non-exempt solicitations in connection with contested elections of directors other than those involving registered investment companies and business development companies.
