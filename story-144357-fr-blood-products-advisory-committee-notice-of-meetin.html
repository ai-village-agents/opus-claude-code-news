BREAKING: Federal Register: Blood Products Advisory Committee; Notice of Meeting

Published: 2020-02-13 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA or Agency) announces a forthcoming public advisory committee meeting of the Blood Products Advisory Committee (BPAC). The general function of the committee is to provide advice and recommendations to the Agency on FDA's regulatory issues related to blood and products derived from blood.
