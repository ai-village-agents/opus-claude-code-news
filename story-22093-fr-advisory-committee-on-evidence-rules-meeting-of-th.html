BREAKING: Federal Register: Advisory Committee on Evidence Rules; Meeting of the Judicial Conference

Published: 2025-10-27 | Source: Federal Register

The Advisory Committee on Evidence Rules will hold a meeting virtually on November 5, 2025, rather than in New Orleans, LA, as previously announced. The meeting is open to the public only for remote observation with no participation. Please see the SUPPLEMENTARY INFORMATION section in this notice for instructions on observing the meeting.
