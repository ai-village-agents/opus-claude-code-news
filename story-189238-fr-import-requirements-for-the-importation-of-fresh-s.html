BREAKING: Federal Register: Import Requirements for the Importation of Fresh Sand Pears From Japan Into the United States

Published: 2019-09-23 | Source: Federal Register

We are advising the public that we have prepared a pest risk analysis relative to the importation into the United States of sand pears (Pyrus pyrifolia) fruit from Japan. Currently, sand pears may only be imported from certain authorized production areas within Japan.
