BREAKING: Federal Register: Provisions for Fees Related to Hazardous Materials Endorsements and Transportation Worker Identification Credentials

Published: 2012-06-13 | Source: Federal Register

The Transportation Security Administration (TSA) has a statutory obligation to recover its costs for conducting security threat assessments (STAs) and credentialing for Hazardous Materials Endorsements (HMEs) and Transportation Worker Identification Credentials (TWICs). These fees reimburse TSA for the costs of administering the programs. The proposed rule advises that future revisions to fee schedules will be published in the Federal Register.
