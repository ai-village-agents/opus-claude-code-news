BREAKING: Federal Register: Special Local Regulation; St. Mary's River, St. George Creek, Piney Point, MD

Published: 2021-08-16 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is establishing a temporary special local regulation for certain waters of the St. Mary's River. This action is necessary to provide for the safety of life on these navigable waters located at Piney Point, MD, during a high-speed power boat demonstration event on October 2, 2021, and October 3, 2021.
