BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone; COVID-19 NorCal Maritime 2020, San Francisco Bay, CA

Published: 2020-03-19 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is establishing a temporary safety zone on the navigable waters of San Francisco Bay surrounding the cruise ship GRAND PRINCESS due to the presence of at least 21 people onboard reportedly testing positive for COVID-19. Federal, state, and local health officials have determined that the vessel presents a risk of spreading communicable disease within the United States.
