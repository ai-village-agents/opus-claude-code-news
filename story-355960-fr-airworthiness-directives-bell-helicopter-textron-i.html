BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Bell Helicopter Textron, Inc., Helicopters

Published: 2013-05-28 | Source: Federal Register

We are revising an earlier proposed airworthiness directive (AD) for Bell Helicopter Textron, Inc. (Bell), Model 214B and B-1 helicopters, which proposed to require inspecting certain pylon support spindle assemblies (spindles) for any corrosion, or a nick, scratch, dent, or crack, and repairing or replacing any unairworthy spindle before further flight.
