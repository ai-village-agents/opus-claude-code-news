BREAKING: Federal Register: Privacy Act of 1974; System of Records; Correction

Published: 2014-04-18 | Source: Federal Register

On April 9, 2014 (79 FR 19589), DoD published a notice deleting a Privacy Act System of Records notice, K270-01, DoD Digital Certificate Records. The Reason paragraph was written inaccurately, and this notice corrects the error.
