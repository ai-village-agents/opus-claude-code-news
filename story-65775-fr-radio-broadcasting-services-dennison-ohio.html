BREAKING: Federal Register: Radio Broadcasting Services; Dennison, Ohio

Published: 2023-04-21 | Source: Federal Register

This document amends the FM Table of Allotments, of the Federal Communications Commission's (Commission) rules, by allotting Channel 272A at Dennison, Ohio, as the community's first local service. A staff engineering analysis indicates that Channel 272A can be allotted to Dennison, Ohio, consistent with the minimum distance separation requirements of the Commission's rules, with a site restriction of 2.7 km (1.7 miles) west of the community.
