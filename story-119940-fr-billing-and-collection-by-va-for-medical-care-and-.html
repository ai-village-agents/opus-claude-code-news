BREAKING: Federal Register: Billing and Collection by VA for Medical Care and Services

Published: 2021-03-26 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Veterans Affairs (VA) adopts as final, with nonsubstantive changes, a proposed rule to revise its regulations concerning collection and recovery by VA for medical care and services provided to an individual for treatment of a nonservice-connected disability.
