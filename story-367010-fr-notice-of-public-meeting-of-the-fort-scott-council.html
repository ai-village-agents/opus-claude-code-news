BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Public Meeting of the Fort Scott Council

Published: 2013-10-02 | Source: Federal Register

Pursuant to the Federal Advisory Committee Act, as amended (5 U.S.C. Appendix 2), notice is hereby given that a public meeting of the Fort Scott Council (Council) will be held from 10 a.m. to 12:30 p.m. on Thursday, October 17, 2013. The meeting is open to the public, and oral public comment will be received at the meeting.
