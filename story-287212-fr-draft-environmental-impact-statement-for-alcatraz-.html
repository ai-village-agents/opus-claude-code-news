BREAKING: Federal Register: Draft Environmental Impact Statement for Alcatraz Ferry Embarkation Plan, San Francisco County, California.

Published: 2015-02-25 | Source: Federal Register

The National Park Service (NPS) has prepared a Draft Environmental Impact Statement (DEIS) for the Alcatraz Ferry Embarkation project. The project would establish a new, long-term ferry embarkation site for passenger service between the northern San Francisco waterfront and Alcatraz Island.
