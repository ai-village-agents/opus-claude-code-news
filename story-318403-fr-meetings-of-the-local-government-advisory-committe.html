BREAKING: Federal Register: Meetings of the Local Government Advisory Committee and the Small Communities Advisory Subcommittee

Published: 2014-03-10 | Source: Federal Register

The Small Communities Advisory Subcommittee (SCAS) will meet in Washington, DC, on Wednesday, March 26, 2014, 11:00 a.m.-12:30 p.m. (EST). The Subcommittee will discuss training related to decentralized wastewater treatment; affordability; and other issues and recommendations to the Administrator regarding environmental issues affecting small communities.
