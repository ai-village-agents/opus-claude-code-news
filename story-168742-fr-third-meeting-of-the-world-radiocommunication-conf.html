BREAKING: Federal Register: Third Meeting of the World Radiocommunication Conference Advisory Committee

Published: 2020-12-21 | Source: Federal Register

In accordance with the Federal Advisory Committee Act, this notice advises interested persons that the third meeting of the World Radiocommunication Conference Advisory Committee (WAC) will be held on February 23, 2021. Due to exceptional circumstances, the Advisory Committee meeting will be convened as a virtual meeting with remote participation only.
