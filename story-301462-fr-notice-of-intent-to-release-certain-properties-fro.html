BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Intent To Release Certain Properties From All Terms, Conditions, Reservations and Restrictions of a Quitclaim Deed Agreement Between the City of Orlando and the Federal Aviation Administration for the Orlando International Airport, Orlando, FL

Published: 2015-08-13 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA hereby provides notice of intent to release approximately 44.30 acres at the Orlando International Airport, Orlando, FL from the conditions, reservations, and restrictions as contained in a Quitclaim Deed agreement between the FAA and the City of Orlando, dated September 27,1976. The release of property will allow the City of Orlando to dispose of the property for other than aeronautical purposes.
