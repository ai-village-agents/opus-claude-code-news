BREAKING: Federal Register: Privacy Act of 1974; System of Records

Published: 2023-02-02 | Source: Federal Register

The FCC's Consumer and Governmental Affairs Bureau (CGB) Stakeholder Database stores the personally identifiable information of individuals who voluntarily submit contact information to CGB. FCC/CGB- 5 covers the personally identifiable information (PII) contained in a database of CGB stakeholders which is used to facilitate outreach about FCC public events and recent developments.
