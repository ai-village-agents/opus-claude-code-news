BREAKING: Federal Register: Electronic Submission of Applications

Published: 2019-05-06 | Source: Federal Register

Pursuant to 15 CFR 400.21(i), applications to the Foreign- Trade Zones Board henceforth should be submitted solely in electronic form via email to ftz@trade.gov.
