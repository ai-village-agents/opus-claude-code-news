BREAKING: Federal Register: Paper and Paper-Based Packaging Promotion, Research and Information Order

Published: 2013-01-02 | Source: Federal Register

This proposed rule invites comments on a proposed Paper and Paper-Based Packaging Promotion, Research and Information Order (Order). The purpose of the program would be to maintain and expand markets for paper and paper-based packaging. A referendum would be held among eligible manufacturers and importers to determine whether they favor implementation of the program prior to it going into effect.
