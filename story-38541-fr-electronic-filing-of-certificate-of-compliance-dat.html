BREAKING: Federal Register: Electronic Filing of Certificate of Compliance Data: Announcement of Expansion of PGA Message Set Test and Request for Additional Participants

Published: 2024-06-04 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Consumer Product Safety Commission (CPSC), in consultation with U.S. Customs and Border Protection (CBP), announce their joint intent to expand the current Partner Government Agency (PGA) Message Set test (Beta Pilot) to include up to 2,000 additional participants.
