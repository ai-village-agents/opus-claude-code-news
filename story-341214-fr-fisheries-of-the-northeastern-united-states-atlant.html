BREAKING: Federal Register: Fisheries of the Northeastern United States; Atlantic Herring Fishery; Framework Adjustment 3

Published: 2014-12-04 | Source: Federal Register

NMFS implements final regulations to establish a process for setting river herring (alewife and blueback) and shad (American and hickory) catch caps for the herring fishery. This action also sets these catch caps for the 2014 and 2015 fishing years. The river herring and shad caps in the herring fishery will limit how much of these species will be caught in the herring fishery.
