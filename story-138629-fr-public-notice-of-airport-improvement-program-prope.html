BREAKING: Federal Register: Public Notice of Airport Improvement Program Property Release; Orcas Island Airport, Eastsound, Washington

Published: 2021-11-23 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA is considering a request from the Port of Orcas, Washington to waive the Airport Improvement Program property requirements for approximately 1,120 square feet of airport property located at Orcas Island Airport, in Eastsound, Washington. The subject property is located in the east section of the airport.
