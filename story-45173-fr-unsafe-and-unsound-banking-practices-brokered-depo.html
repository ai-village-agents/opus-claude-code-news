BREAKING: Federal Register: Unsafe and Unsound Banking Practices: Brokered Deposits Restrictions

Published: 2024-08-23 | Source: Federal Register

The Federal Deposit Insurance Corporation (FDIC) is inviting comment on proposed revisions to its regulations relating to the brokered deposits restrictions that apply to less than well-capitalized insured depository institutions. The proposed rule would revise the "deposit broker" definition and would amend the analysis of the "primary purpose" exception to the "deposit broker" definition.
