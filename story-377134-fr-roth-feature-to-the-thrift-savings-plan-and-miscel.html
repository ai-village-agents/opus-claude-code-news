BREAKING: Federal Register: Roth Feature to the Thrift Savings Plan and Miscellaneous Uniformed Services Account Amendments

Published: 2012-02-08 | Source: Federal Register

The Federal Retirement Thrift Investment Board (Agency) proposes to amend its regulations to add a Roth feature to the Thrift Savings Plan. The Agency also proposes to reorganize regulatory provisions pertaining to uniformed services accounts.
