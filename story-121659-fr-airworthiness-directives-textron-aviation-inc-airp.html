BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Textron Aviation Inc. Airplanes

Published: 2021-04-20 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA is superseding Airworthiness Directive (AD) 97-06-10 for certain Raytheon Aircraft Company (type certificate now held by Textron Aviation Inc. (Textron)) Model 76 airplanes. AD 97-06-10 required repetitively inspecting the main landing gear (MLG) "A" frame assemblies for cracks and replacing any cracked assembly.
