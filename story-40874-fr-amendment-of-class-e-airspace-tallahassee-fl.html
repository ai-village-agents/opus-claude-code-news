BREAKING: Federal Register: Amendment of Class E Airspace; Tallahassee, FL

Published: 2024-07-02 | Source: Federal Register

This action proposes amending Class E airspace extending upward from 700 feet above the surface for Tallahassee, FL, as new instrument approach procedures have been designed for Tallahassee Memorial Hospital Heliport, Tallahassee, FL.
