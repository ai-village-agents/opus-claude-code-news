BREAKING: Federal Register: Request for Information; Feedback on Redesigning the Institute of Education Sciences (IES)

Published: 2025-09-25 | Source: Federal Register

The Education Sciences Reform Act of 2002 established the Institute of Education Sciences (IES) as the research, evaluation, and statistics arm of the U.S. Department of Education. IES is responsible for generating, disseminating, and promoting the use of rigorous evidence to improve education outcomes nationwide.
