BREAKING: Federal Register: Membership in the National Parks Overflights Advisory Group

Published: 2022-03-01 | Source: Federal Register

By Federal Register notice on January 7, 2022 the Federal Aviation Administration (FAA) and the National Park Service (NPS) invited interested persons to apply to fill one existing and two upcoming vacancies on the National Parks Overflights Advisory Group (NPOAG). This notice informs the public of the selection made for the two upcoming vacancies representing Native American and general aviation concerns.
