BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Temporary Closures of Public Lands in La Paz County, AZ

Published: 2013-01-15 | Source: Federal Register

The Bureau of Land Management (BLM) Lake Havasu Field Office will close certain public lands during the Best in the Desert (BITD) Racing Association "BITD PARKER 250" and "BITD 425" events.
