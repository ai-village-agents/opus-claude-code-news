BREAKING: Federal Register: Parts and Accessories Necessary for Safe Operation; Exemption Renewal for Groendyke Transport, Inc.

Published: 2024-11-20 | Source: Federal Register

FMCSA announces its final decision to grant an exemption renewal requested by Groendyke Transport, Inc. (Groendyke) to allow the use of an amber brake-activated pulsating lamp on the rear of its trailers in addition to the steady-burning brake lamps required by the Federal Motor Carrier Safety Regulations (FMCSR).
