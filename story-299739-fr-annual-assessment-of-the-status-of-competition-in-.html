BREAKING: Federal Register: Annual Assessment of the Status of Competition in the Market for the Delivery of Video Programming

Published: 2015-07-24 | Source: Federal Register

The Commission is required to report annually to Congress on the status of competition in markets for the delivery of video programming. This document solicits data, information, and comment on the status of competition in the market for the delivery of video programming for the Commission's Seventeenth Report (17th Report).
