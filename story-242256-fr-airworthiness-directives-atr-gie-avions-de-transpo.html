BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; ATR-GIE Avions de Transport Régional Airplanes

Published: 2017-08-25 | Source: Federal Register

We are superseding Airworthiness Directive (AD) 2015-23-12, which applied to all ATR-GIE Avions de Transport R[eacute]gional Model ATR42 and ATR72 airplanes. AD 2015-23-12 required identifying the serial number and part number of the main landing gear (MLG) rear hinge pins, and replacing pins or the MLG if necessary.
