BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zones; Recurring Events in Captain of the Port Duluth Zone

Published: 2013-08-12 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard will establish 8 permanent safety zones for annually recurring marine events in the Coast Guard Captain of the Port (COTP) Duluth zone. These safety zones are needed to protect both spectators and participants from the hazards associated with the events.
