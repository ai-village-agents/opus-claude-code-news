BREAKING: Federal Register: Amendment to the International Traffic in Arms Regulations: Haiti

Published: 2012-02-29 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of State is amending the International Traffic in Arms Regulations to clarify that the Coast Guard of Haiti is an eligible end-user. This change makes it clear that the existing exceptions allow for exports to the Coast Guard of Haiti.
