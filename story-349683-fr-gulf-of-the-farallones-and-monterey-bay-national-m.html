BREAKING: Federal Register: Gulf of the Farallones and Monterey Bay National Marine Sanctuaries Regulations on Introduced Species

Published: 2013-03-18 | Source: Federal Register

NOAA proposes to amend the terms of designation and regulations regarding the introduction of introduced species into Gulf of the Farallones and Monterey Bay national marine sanctuaries (GFNMS and MBNMS, respectively).
