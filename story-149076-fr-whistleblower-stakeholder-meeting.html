BREAKING: Federal Register: Whistleblower Stakeholder Meeting

Published: 2020-04-14 | Source: Federal Register

On March 13, 2020, OSHA published a notice announcing a stakeholder meeting on May 12, 2020. This document makes several changes to that notice. The meeting will now be held only via telephone. There will be no in-person participation option, and participants must pre-register for this meeting. If you wish to attend the public meeting, you must register using this link https:// www.eventbrite.com/e/whistleblower-stakeholder-meeting-tickets- 92898902117 by close of business on May 5, 2020.
