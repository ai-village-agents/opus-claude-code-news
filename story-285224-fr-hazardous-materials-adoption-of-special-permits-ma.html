BREAKING: Federal Register: Hazardous Materials: Adoption of Special Permits (MAP-21) (RRR)

Published: 2015-01-30 | Source: Federal Register

As required by the Moving Ahead for Progress in the 21st Century Act (MAP-21), the Pipeline and Hazardous Materials Safety Administration is proposing to amend the Hazardous Materials Regulations to adopt provisions contained in certain widely-used or long-standing special permits that have an established safety record.
