BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; The Boeing Company Airplanes

Published: 2014-01-10 | Source: Federal Register

We propose to adopt a new airworthiness directive (AD) for all The Boeing Company Model 777 airplanes. This proposed AD was prompted by reports of latently failed fuel shutoff valves discovered during fuel filter replacement. This proposed AD would require revising the maintenance or inspection program to include a new airworthiness limitation.
