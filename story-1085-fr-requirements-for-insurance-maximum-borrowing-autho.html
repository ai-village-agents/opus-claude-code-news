BREAKING: Federal Register: Requirements for Insurance; Maximum Borrowing Authority

Published: 2026-01-28 | Source: Federal Register

The NCUA Board (Board) seeks comment on a proposed rule to remove the maximum borrowing authority from the NCUA's regulations that establish the requirements for obtaining and maintaining federal share insurance with the National Credit Union Share Insurance Fund (Share Insurance Fund). This provision applies to all federally insured credit unions (FICUs).
