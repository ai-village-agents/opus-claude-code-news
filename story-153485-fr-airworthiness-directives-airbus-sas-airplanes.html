BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Airbus SAS Airplanes

Published: 2020-06-08 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA is withdrawing a supplemental notice of proposed rulemaking (SNPRM) that proposed to adopt a new airworthiness directive (AD) that would have applied to all Airbus SAS Model A330-200, A330-200 Freighter, A330-300, A340-200, A340-300, A340-500, and A340-600 series airplanes.
