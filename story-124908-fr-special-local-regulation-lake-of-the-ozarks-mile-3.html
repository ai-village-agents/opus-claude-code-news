BREAKING: Federal Register: Special Local Regulation; Lake of the Ozarks, Mile 34.5, Sunrise Beach, MO

Published: 2021-05-28 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard proposes to establish a temporary special local regulation for all navigable waters of Sunrise Beach in Francis Hollow at mile marker 34.5 on the Lake of the Ozarks, Missouri. This action is necessary to provide for the safety of life on these navigable waters during a waterside concert on June 26, 2021.
