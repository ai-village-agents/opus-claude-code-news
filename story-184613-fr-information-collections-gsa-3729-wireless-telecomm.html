BREAKING: Federal Register: Information Collections; GSA 3729 Wireless Telecommunications Company Application

Published: 2019-07-23 | Source: Federal Register

The data collection previously approved under OMB Control Number 3090-0307, GSA 3729 Wireless Telecommunications Company Application (GSA 3729) is no longer active and has been removed from GSA's website. The SF-299, Application for Transportation and Utility Systems and Facilities on Federal Lands, will be modified to incorporate the information contained in the GSA 3729 and should be used in its current format, until the revised form is approved for use.
