BREAKING: Federal Register: Fisheries of the Exclusive Economic Zone Off Alaska; Pacific Salmon

Published: 2012-12-21 | Source: Federal Register

NMFS issues regulations to implement Amendment 12 to the Fishery Management Plan for Salmon Fisheries in the EEZ off the Coast of Alaska (FMP). Amendment 12 comprehensively revises and updates the FMP to reflect the North Pacific Fishery Management Council's (Council) salmon management policy and to comply with Federal law.
