BREAKING: Federal Register: Fisheries of the Northeastern United States; Golden Tilefish Fishery; Extension of Emergency Action

Published: 2021-06-25 | Source: Federal Register

This temporary rule extends emergency measures that allow a limited one-time carryover of up to 5 percent of unharvested fishing quota from the 2020 fishing year into the 2021 fishing year. This action is necessary to allow the golden tilefish individual fishing quota shareholders that were eligible for carryover under the emergency measures, but have not yet fully harvested that carryover, an opportunity to use it.
