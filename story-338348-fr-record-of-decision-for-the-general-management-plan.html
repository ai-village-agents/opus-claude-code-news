BREAKING: Federal Register: Record of Decision for the General Management Plan, Canaveral National Seashore

Published: 2014-10-28 | Source: Federal Register

Pursuant to the National Environmental Policy Act of 1969, Section 42 U.S.C. 4332(2)(C), the National Park Service (NPS) announces the availability of the Record of Decision (ROD) for the General Management Plan (GMP) for Canaveral National Seashore (Seashore). On August 12, 2014 the Regional Director, Southeast Region, approved the ROD for the project.
