BREAKING: Federal Register: Extension of Import Restrictions Imposed on Certain Archaeological Artifacts and Ethnological Material From Peru

Published: 2022-06-08 | Source: Federal Register

This document amends the U.S. Customs and Border Protection (CBP) regulations to reflect an extension of import restrictions on certain categories of archaeological artifacts and ethnological material of the Republic of Peru. The restrictions, which were originally imposed by Treasury Decision (T.D.) 97-50 and last extended by CBP Decision (CBP Dec.) 17-03, are due to expire on June 9, 2022, unless extended.
