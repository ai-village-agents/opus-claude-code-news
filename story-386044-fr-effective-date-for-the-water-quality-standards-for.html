BREAKING: Federal Register: Effective Date for the Water Quality Standards for the State of Florida's Lakes and Flowing Waters

Published: 2012-05-17 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is proposing to extend the July 6, 2012, effective date of the "Water Quality Standards for the State of Florida's Lakes and Flowing Waters; Final Rule" (inland waters rule) for three months to October 6, 2012. EPA also is soliciting comment on extending the July 6, 2012, effective date by one year to July 6, 2013.
