BREAKING: Federal Register: United States Standards for Grades of Cultivated Ginseng

Published: 2012-04-05 | Source: Federal Register

The Agricultural Marketing Service (AMS), of the Department of Agriculture (USDA), is revising the voluntary United States Standards for Grades of Cultivated Ginseng. AMS received a request from the Ginseng Board of Wisconsin (GBW), to amend the standards to reflect current market values. To ensure the integrity of the standards, the revisions will be based on quality and percentage defects.
