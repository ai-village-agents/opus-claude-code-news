BREAKING: Federal Register: Atlantic Highly Migratory Species; North and South Atlantic 2013 Commercial Swordfish Quotas

Published: 2013-05-16 | Source: Federal Register

This final rule adjusts the 2013 fishing season quotas for North and South Atlantic swordfish based upon 2012 commercial quota underharvests and international quota transfers consistent with the International Commission for the Conservation of Atlantic Tunas (ICCAT) Recommendations 11-02 and 12-01. This final rule will affect commercial and recreational fishing for swordfish in the Atlantic Ocean, including the Caribbean Sea and Gulf of Mexico.
