BREAKING: Federal Register: Pathways to Global Unity; Public Workshop

Published: 2018-03-16 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA), in collaboration with the Association of Food and Drug Officials (AFDO), is announcing the following public workshop entitled "Pathways to Global Unity." This 2\1/2\-day public workshop is intended to provide information about FDA drug and device regulation to the regulated industries.
