BREAKING: Federal Register: Pipeline Safety: Public Workshop on Underground Natural Gas Storage Safety

Published: 2016-06-24 | Source: Federal Register

This notice announces a public meeting to solicit input and obtain background information concerning underground natural gas storage safety. PHMSA and the National Association of Pipeline Safety Representatives (NAPSR) are co-sponsoring this one-day workshop.
