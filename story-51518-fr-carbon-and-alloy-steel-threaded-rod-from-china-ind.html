BREAKING: Federal Register: Carbon and Alloy Steel Threaded Rod From China, India, Taiwan, and Thailand; Institution of Five-Year Reviews

Published: 2024-11-01 | Source: Federal Register

The Commission hereby gives notice that it has instituted reviews pursuant to the Tariff Act of 1930 ("the Act"), as amended, to determine whether revocation of the countervailing duty orders on carbon and alloy steel threaded rod from China and India, and the antidumping duty orders on carbon and alloy steel threaded rod from China, India, Taiwan, and Thailand would be likely to lead to continuation or recurrence of material injury.
