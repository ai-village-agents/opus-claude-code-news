BREAKING: Federal Register: SBA Lender Risk Rating System

Published: 2021-02-16 | Source: Federal Register

This notice implements changes to the Small Business Administration's (SBA's) Risk Rating System. The Risk Rating System is an internal tool to assist SBA in assessing the risk of the SBA loan operations and loan portfolio of each active 7(a) Lender and Certified Development Company (CDC).
