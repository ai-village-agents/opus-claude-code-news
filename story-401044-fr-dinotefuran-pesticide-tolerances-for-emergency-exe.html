BREAKING: Federal Register: Dinotefuran; Pesticide Tolerances for Emergency Exemptions

Published: 2012-11-09 | Source: Federal Register

This regulation establishes time-limited tolerances for residues of dinotefuran in or on pome fruits and stone fruits. This action is in response to EPA's granting of emergency exemptions under the Federal Insecticide, Fungicide, and Rodenticide Act (FIFRA) authorizing use of the pesticide on pome fruits and stone fruits.
