BREAKING: Federal Register: Crow Butte Resources, Inc.

Published: 2016-11-21 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Nuclear Regulatory Commission (NRC) is issuing an exemption to Crow Butte Resources, Inc. (CBR) for the purpose of complying with occupational dose limits in response to a request from CBR dated September 21, 2015.
