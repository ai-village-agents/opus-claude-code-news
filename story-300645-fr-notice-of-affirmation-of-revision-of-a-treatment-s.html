BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Affirmation of Revision of a Treatment Schedule for Hot Water Treatment of Mangoes

Published: 2015-08-04 | Source: Federal Register

We are affirming our earlier determination that it was necessary to immediately amend hot water treatment schedule T102-a in the Plant Protection and Quarantine Treatment Manual to extend the applicability of the treatment to additional mango commodities.
