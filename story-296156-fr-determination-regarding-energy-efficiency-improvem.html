BREAKING: Federal Register: Determination Regarding Energy Efficiency Improvements in the 2015 International Energy Conservation Code (IECC)

Published: 2015-06-11 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Department of Energy (DOE) has determined that the 2015 edition of the International Energy Conservation Code (IECC) would improve energy efficiency in buildings subject to the code compared to the 2012 edition.
