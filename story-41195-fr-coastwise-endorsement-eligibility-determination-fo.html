BREAKING: Federal Register: Coastwise Endorsement Eligibility Determination for a Foreign-Built Vessel: TRANQUILO (SAIL); Invitation for Public Comments

Published: 2024-07-08 | Source: Federal Register

The Secretary Of Transportation, As Represented By The Maritime Administration (Marad), Is Authorized To Issue Coastwise Endorsement Eligibility Determinations For Foreign-Built Vessels Which Will Carry No More Than Twelve Passengers For Hire. A Request For Such A Determination Has Been Received By Marad.
