BREAKING: Federal Register: Agency Information Collection Activities; Proposed Collection; Comment Request

Published: 2015-09-02 | Source: Federal Register

In compliance with the Paperwork Reduction Act (PRA), this document announces that EPA is planning to submit an Information Collection Request (ICR) to the Office of Management and Budget (OMB). The ICR, entitled: "TSCA Section 5(a)(2) Significant New Use Rules for Existing Chemicals" and identified by EPA ICR No. 1188.12 and OMB Control No. 2070-0038, represents the renewal of an existing ICR that is scheduled to expire on July 31, 2016.
