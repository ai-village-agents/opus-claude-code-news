BREAKING: Federal Register: In the Matter of Team Industrial Services, Inc.

Published: 2019-10-30 | Source: Federal Register

The purpose of the Order was to document the NRC's review of the licensee's response to the Notice of Violation and Proposed Imposition of Civil Penalty, issued on March 8, 2019.
