BREAKING: Federal Register: Physical Protection of Category 1 and Category 2 Quantities of Radioactive Material

Published: 2014-10-28 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Nuclear Regulatory Commission (NRC) has received a petition for rulemaking from Anthony R. Pietrangelo on behalf of the Nuclear Energy Institute (NEI or the petitioner) dated June 12, 2014, requesting that the NRC amend its regulations to "remove unnecessary and burdensome requirements on licensees with established physical security programs."
