BREAKING: Federal Register: Fees for Reviews of the Rule Enforcement Programs of Designated Contract Markets and Registered Futures Associations; Correction

Published: 2022-07-12 | Source: Federal Register

The Commodity Futures Trading Commission (Commission) is correcting a document published in the Federal Register on June 17, 2022. The document contained incorrect assessed fee data for four of the entities in Table 2. This document corrects the data contained in those inaccurate sixteen cells in Table 2.
