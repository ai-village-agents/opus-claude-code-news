BREAKING: Federal Register: Certain Pasta From Italy: Final Results of Antidumping Duty Administrative Review; 2015-2016

The period of review (POR) is July 1, 2015, through June 30, 2016. As a result of our analysis of the comments and information received, these final results differ from the Preliminary Results with respect to Ghigi 1870 S.p.A. and Pasta Zara S.p.A.
