BREAKING: Federal Register: Amateur Radio Use of the Allocation at 5 MHz

Published: 2012-02-03 | Source: Federal Register

This document amends the Commission's rules to facilitate more efficient and effective use by the Amateur Radio Service of five channels in the 5330.5-5406.4 kHz band (the 60 meter band).
