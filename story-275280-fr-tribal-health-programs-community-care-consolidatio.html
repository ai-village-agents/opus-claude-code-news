BREAKING: Federal Register: Tribal Health Programs-Community Care Consolidation

Published: 2016-09-29 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Veterans Affairs (VA), Veterans Health Administration (VHA) will facilitate a Tribal Consultation on the VHA's effort to improve continuity of care and health care access for Veterans by consolidating multiple community care programs, previously known as non-VA care, into one standard program with standard rates.
