BREAKING: Federal Register: Revocation and Amendment of Class E Airspace; Philipsburg, PA

Published: 2018-07-05 | Source: Federal Register

This action corrects a final rule published in the Federal Register on June 11, 2018, amending Class E airspace extending upward from 700 feet or more above the surface for Mid-State Airport, Phillipsburg, PA, by adding the word `side' to the legal description.
