BREAKING: Federal Register: South Atlantic Fishery Management Council; Public Meetings

Published: 2018-09-17 | Source: Federal Register

A meeting of the South Atlantic Fishery Management Council originally scheduled for September 16-21, 2018 has been postponed due to the threat of Hurricane Florence. The meeting has been rescheduled for September 30--October 5, 2018.
