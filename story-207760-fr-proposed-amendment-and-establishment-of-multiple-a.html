BREAKING: Federal Register: Proposed Amendment and Establishment of Multiple Air Traffic Service (ATS) Routes; Western United States

Published: 2018-05-24 | Source: Federal Register

This action proposes to amend six United States Area Navigation (RNAV) routes (Q-88, Q-90, Q-114, Q-126, Q-136, and Q-150) and establish one RNAV route (Q-92) in the western United States. The routes would support standard instrument departures (SIDs) and standard terminal arrival routes (STARs) for Denver International Airport.
