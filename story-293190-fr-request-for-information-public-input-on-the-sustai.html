BREAKING: Federal Register: Request for Information: Public Input on the Sustained Assessment Process of the U.S. National Climate Assessment

Published: 2015-05-06 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Global Change Research Program (USGCRP) has a legal mandate to conduct a National Climate Assessment (NCA) not less frequently than every four years. Under its current decadal strategic plan (http://go.usa.gov/3qGU4), USGCRP is building sustained assessment capacity to support the Nation's ability to understand, anticipate, and respond to global change impacts and vulnerabilities.
