BREAKING: Federal Register: Privacy Act of 1974; System of Records

Published: 2012-12-18 | Source: Federal Register

Pursuant to the Privacy Act of 1974 (5 U.S.C. 552a), the Foreign Claims Settlement Commission (Commission), Department of Justice, proposes to establish a new system of records to enable the Commission to carry out its statutory responsibility to determine the validity and amount of the claims submitted to the Commission against Iraq ("Claims Against Iraq System").
