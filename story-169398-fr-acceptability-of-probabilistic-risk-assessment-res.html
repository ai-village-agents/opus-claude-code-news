BREAKING: Federal Register: Acceptability of Probabilistic Risk Assessment Results for Risk-Informed Activities

Published: 2020-12-29 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Nuclear Regulatory Commission (NRC) is issuing Revision 3 to Regulatory Guide (RG) 1.200, "Acceptability of Probabilistic Risk Assessment Results for Risk-Informed Activities." Revision 3 describes one acceptable approach for determining whether a base probabilistic risk assessment (PRA), in total or the portions that are used to support an application, is acceptable to provide confidence in the results, such that the PRA can be used in regulatory decisionmaking for light-water reactors...
