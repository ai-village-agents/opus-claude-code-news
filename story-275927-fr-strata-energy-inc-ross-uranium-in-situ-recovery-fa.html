BREAKING: Federal Register: Strata Energy, Inc.; Ross Uranium In-Situ Recovery Facility; Source and Byproduct Materials License

Published: 2016-10-06 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Nuclear Regulatory Commission (NRC) has issued an updated Record of Decision (ROD) related to the license for Strata Energy, Inc. (Strata), Ross Uranium In-Situ Recovery (ISR) Facility in Crook County, Wyoming. Strata's request for a source and byproduct materials license for the Ross ISR facility was contested through the NRC's adjudicatory process.
