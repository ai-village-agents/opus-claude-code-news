BREAKING: Federal Register: Security Training for Surface Transportation Employees

Published: 2016-12-16 | Source: Federal Register

The Transportation Security Administration (TSA) is proposing to require security training for employees of higher-risk freight railroad carriers, public transportation agencies (including rail mass transit and bus systems), passenger railroad carriers, and over-the- road bus (OTRB) companies.
