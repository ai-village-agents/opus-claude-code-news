BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone; Bahia de Ponce, Ponce, PR

Published: 2020-12-01 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is proposing to establish a permanment safety zone for certain waters of the Bahia de Ponce, Ponce, Puerto Rico. This action is necessary to provide for the safety of life on these navigable waters during ship-to-ship liquefied natural gas transfer operations between liquefied gas carriers.
