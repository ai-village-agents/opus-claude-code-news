BREAKING: Federal Register: Acetamiprid; Pesticide Tolerances for Emergency Exemption

Published: 2017-04-10 | Source: Federal Register

This regulation establishes time-limited tolerances for residues of acetamiprid in or on sugarcane, cane and sugarcane, molasses. This action is associated with the issuance of a crisis exemption under the Federal Insecticide, Fungicide, and Rodenticide Act (FIFRA) authorizing use of the pesticide on sugarcane.
