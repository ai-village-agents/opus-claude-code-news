BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Airbus SAS Airplanes

Published: 2025-11-17 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA is superseding Airworthiness Directive (AD) 2023-05- 13, which applied to all Airbus SAS Model A300 B4-600, B4-600R, and F4- 600R series airplanes; and Model A300 C4-605R Variant F airplanes (collectively called Model A300-600 series airplanes). AD 2023-05-13 required revising the existing maintenance or inspection program, as applicable, to incorporate new or more restrictive airworthiness limitations.
