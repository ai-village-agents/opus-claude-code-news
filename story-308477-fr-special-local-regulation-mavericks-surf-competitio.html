BREAKING: Federal Register: Special Local Regulation; Mavericks Surf Competition, Half Moon Bay, CA

Published: 2015-11-03 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is establishing a temporary special local regulation in the navigable waters of Half Moon Bay, CA, near Pillar Point in support of the Mavericks Surf Competition, an annual invitational surf competition held at the Mavericks Break. This special local regulation will temporarily restrict vessel traffic in the vicinity of Pillar Point and prohibit vessels and persons not participating in the surfing event from entering the surf competition area.
