BREAKING: Federal Register: Deepwater Horizon Oil Spill; Proposal of Future Early Restoration Projects and Environmental Reviews

Published: 2013-05-06 | Source: Federal Register

The federal and state natural resource trustees for the Deepwater Horizon oil spill (Trustees) intend to propose the additional early restoration projects described below to continue the process of using early restoration funding to restore natural resources, ecological services, and human use services injured or lost as a result of the Deepwater Horizon oil spill disaster.
