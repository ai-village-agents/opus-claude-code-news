BREAKING: Federal Register: Delayed Verification Sampling of Not Ready-to-Eat Breaded Stuffed Chicken Products

Published: 2025-04-11 | Source: Federal Register

FSIS is announcing that it is delaying the date it will begin sampling not ready-to-eat (NRTE) breaded stuffed chicken products for Salmonella and delaying the date establishments need to reassess their Hazard Analysis and Critical Control Point (HACCP) plans for these products from May 1, 2025, to November 3, 2025.
