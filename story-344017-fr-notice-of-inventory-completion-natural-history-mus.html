BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Inventory Completion: Natural History Museum of Utah, Salt Lake City, UT

Published: 2013-01-11 | Source: Federal Register

The Natural History Museum of Utah has completed an inventory of human remains and associated funerary objects, in consultation with the appropriate Indian tribes, and has determined that there is a cultural affiliation between the human remains and associated funerary objects and a present-day Indian tribe.
