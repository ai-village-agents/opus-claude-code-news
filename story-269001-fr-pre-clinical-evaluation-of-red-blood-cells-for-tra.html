BREAKING: Federal Register: Pre-Clinical Evaluation of Red Blood Cells for Transfusion; Public Workshop

Published: 2016-07-19 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA) is announcing a public workshop entitled "Pre-Clinical Evaluation of Red Blood Cells for Transfusion." The purpose of the public workshop is to discuss new methodologies for pre-clinical evaluation of the safety and efficacy of red blood cell transfusion products.
