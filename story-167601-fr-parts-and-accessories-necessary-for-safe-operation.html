BREAKING: Federal Register: Parts and Accessories Necessary for Safe Operation; Application for an Exemption From Grote Industries, LLC

Published: 2020-12-07 | Source: Federal Register

The Federal Motor Carrier Safety Administration (FMCSA) announces its decision to grant Grote Industries, LLC's (Grote) application for a limited 5-year exemption to allow motor carriers operating trailers and van body trucks to install amber brake-activated pulsating warning lamps on the rear of trailers and van body trucks in addition to the steady-burning brake lamps required by the Federal Motor Carrier Safety Regulations (FMCSRs).
