BREAKING: Federal Register: Customer Clearing Documentation, Timing of Acceptance for Clearing, and Clearing Member Risk Management

Published: 2012-04-09 | Source: Federal Register

The Commodity Futures Trading Commission ("Commission" or "CFTC") is adopting rules to implement new statutory provisions enacted by Title VII of the Dodd-Frank Wall Street Reform and Consumer Protection Act.
