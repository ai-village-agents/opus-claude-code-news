BREAKING: Federal Register: Agency Information Collection Activities: Declaration of Person Who Performed Repairs

Published: 2012-02-23 | Source: Federal Register

As part of its continuing effort to reduce paperwork and respondent burden, CBP invites the general public and other Federal agencies to comment on an information collection requirement concerning the: Declaration of a Person Who Performed Repairs. This request for comment is being made pursuant to the Paperwork Reduction Act of 1995 (Pub. L. 104-13).
