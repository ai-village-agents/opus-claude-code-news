BREAKING: Federal Register: FY 2020 Competitive Funding Opportunity: Low or No Emission Grant Program

Published: 2020-01-24 | Source: Federal Register

The Federal Transit Administration (FTA) announces the opportunity to apply for $130 million in competitive grants under the fiscal year (FY) 2020 Low or No Emission Grant Program (Low-No Program) (Catalog of Federal Domestic Assistance number: 20.526).
