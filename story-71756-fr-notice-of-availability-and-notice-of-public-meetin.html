BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Availability and Notice of Public Meeting for the U.S. Antarctic Program (USAP) South Pole Station Master Plan Charrette

Published: 2023-07-12 | Source: Federal Register

The National Science Foundation (NSF) is developing a Station Master Plan for the USAP South Pole Station, Antarctica. A planning charrette will provide a forum for the scientific community to engage in conversations about science conducted at the South Pole currently and capabilities in the future. Planners will facilitate discussions around facility requirements, siting criteria, infrastructure and utility needs, and phasing priorities.
