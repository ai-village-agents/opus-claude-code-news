BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Final Federal Agency Actions on Proposed Highway in California

Published: 2013-04-24 | Source: Federal Register

The FHWA, on behalf of Caltrans, is issuing this notice to announce actions taken by Caltrans, and other Federal Agencies: US Army Corps of Engineers (USACE), US Fish and Wildlife, USDA Six Rivers National Forest: Smith River National Recreation Area, US National Park Service, NOAA National Marine Fisheries, that are final within the meaning of 23 U.S.C. 139(l)(1).
