BREAKING: Federal Register: Drawbridge Operation Regulations; Industrial Seaway Canal, MS

Published: 2020-05-13 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard proposes to change the operating schedule that governs the State Route 605 highway, Wilkes Road bascule bridge across the Industrial Seaway Canal mile 11.3, Hansboro, Harrison County, Mississippi. The Mississippi Department of Transportation (MDOT) submitted this request for a change in schedule, which would allow the bridge to close to vessel traffic.
