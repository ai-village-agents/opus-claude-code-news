BREAKING: Federal Register: Public Meeting on Innovation Driven by Artificial Intelligence

Published: 2023-01-26 | Source: Federal Register

The United States Patent and Trademark Office (USPTO) plays an important role in incentivizing and protecting innovation, including innovation driven by artificial intelligence (AI), to ensure continued U.S. leadership in AI and other emerging technologies (ET). The USPTO's Texas Regional Office will host a public meeting on innovation driven by AI on February 8, 2023, at 11 a.m. CT.
