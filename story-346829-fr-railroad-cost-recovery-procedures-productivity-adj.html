BREAKING: Federal Register: Railroad Cost Recovery Procedures-Productivity Adjustment

Published: 2013-02-13 | Source: Federal Register

In a decision served on February 8, 2013, we proposed to adopt 1.009 (0.9% per year) as the measure of average change in railroad productivity for the 2007-2011 (5-year) averaging period. This represents a 0.1% increase over the average for the 2006-2010 period. The Board's February 8, 2013 decision in this proceeding stated that comments may be filed addressing any perceived data and computational errors in our calculation.
