BREAKING: Federal Register: Sunlamp Products; Proposed Amendment to Performance Standard

Published: 2015-12-22 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA or Agency) is proposing to amend the performance standard for sunlamp products and ultraviolet (UV) lamps intended for use in these products. This standard was last amended in 1985. The current amendments seek to improve consumer safety by requiring more effective communication regarding the risks posed by these products.
