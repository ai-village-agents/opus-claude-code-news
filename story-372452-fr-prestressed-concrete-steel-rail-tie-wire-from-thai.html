BREAKING: Federal Register: Prestressed Concrete Steel Rail Tie Wire From Thailand: Preliminary Determination of Sales at Not Less Than Fair Value and Postponement of Final Determination

Published: 2013-12-12 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Commerce ("Department") preliminarily determines that prestressed concrete steel rail tire wire ("PC tie wire") from Thailand is not being, or likely to be, sold in the United States at less than fair value ("LTFV"), as provided in section 733(b) of the Tariff Act of 1930, as amended ("the Act").
