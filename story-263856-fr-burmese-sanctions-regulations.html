BREAKING: Federal Register: Burmese Sanctions Regulations

Published: 2016-05-18 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of the Treasury's Office of Foreign Assets Control (OFAC) is amending the Burmese Sanctions Regulations to add a general license authorizing certain transactions related to U.S. persons residing in Burma.
