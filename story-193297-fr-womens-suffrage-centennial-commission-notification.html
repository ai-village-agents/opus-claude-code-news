BREAKING: Federal Register: Women's Suffrage Centennial Commission; Notification of Public Meeting

Published: 2019-11-13 | Source: Federal Register

Notice is being provided according to the requirements of the Federal Advisory Committee Act. This notice provides the schedule and agenda for the December 3, 2019, telephonic meeting of the Women's Suffrage Centennial Commission (Commission), and the March 3, 2020, in- person meeting of the Commission. These meetings are open to the public.
