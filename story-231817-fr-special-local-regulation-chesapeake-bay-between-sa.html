BREAKING: Federal Register: Special Local Regulation; Chesapeake Bay, Between Sandy Point and Kent Island, MD

Published: 2017-04-12 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is establishing special local regulations for certain waters of the Chesapeake Bay. This action is necessary to provide for the safety of life on the navigable waters located between Sandy Point, Anne Arundel County, MD and Kent Island, Queen Anne's County, MD, during a paddling event on April 29, 2017.
