BREAKING: Federal Register: Multiemployer Plans; Valuation and Notice Requirements

Published: 2014-01-29 | Source: Federal Register

PBGC is proposing to amend its multiemployer regulations to make the provision of information to PBGC and plan participants more efficient and effective and to reduce burden on plans and sponsors.
