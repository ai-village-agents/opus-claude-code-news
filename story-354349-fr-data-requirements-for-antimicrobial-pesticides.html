BREAKING: Federal Register: Data Requirements for Antimicrobial Pesticides

Published: 2013-05-08 | Source: Federal Register

EPA is revising the data requirements for antimicrobial pesticide products to reflect current scientific and regulatory practice, and to provide the regulated community with clearer and transparent information about the data needed to support pesticide registration decisions for antimicrobial products.
