BREAKING: Federal Register: Khapra Beetle; New Regulated Countries and Regulated Articles

Published: 2014-12-29 | Source: Federal Register

We are amending the khapra beetle regulations by adding additional regulated articles and regulated countries. We are also updating the regulations to reflect changes in industry practices that have affected the risk of khapra beetle being imported into the United States and country names that have changed since the regulations were originally published.
