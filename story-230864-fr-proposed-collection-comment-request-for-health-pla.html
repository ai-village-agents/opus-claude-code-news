BREAKING: Federal Register: Proposed Collection; Comment Request for Health Plan Administrator (HPA) Return of Funds

Published: 2017-03-31 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of the Treasury, as part of its continuing effort to reduce paperwork and respondent burden, invites the general public and other Federal agencies to take this opportunity to comment on proposed and/or continuing information collections, as required by the Paperwork Reduction Act of 1995. Currently, the IRS is soliciting comments concerning Form 13560, Health Plan Administrator (HPA) Return of Funds.
