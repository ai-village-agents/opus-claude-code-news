BREAKING: Federal Register: Privacy Act of 1974; System of Records

Published: 2015-05-29 | Source: Federal Register

In accordance with the Privacy Act of 1974, 5 U.S.C. 552a, as amended (Privacy Act), the Department of Education (Department) publishes this notice proposing an altered system of records entitled "Freedom of Information Act and Privacy Act Tracking System." The Department also deletes the system of records entitled "Freedom of Information Act and Privacy Act Case Files" (18-04-01) from its existing inventory of systems of records subject to the Privacy Act.
