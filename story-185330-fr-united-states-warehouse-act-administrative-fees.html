BREAKING: Federal Register: United States Warehouse Act Administrative Fees

Published: 2019-08-01 | Source: Federal Register

The Agricultural Marketing Service (AMS) is announcing the fees it will charge warehouse operators for voluntary services associated with the administration of the United States Warehouse Act. This action establishes the license action fees, service license fees, inspection fees, and annual user fees for warehouse services for fiscal year 2020, which begins October 1, 2019.
