BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Charter Renewal a Federal Advisory Committee

Published: 2024-07-03 | Source: Federal Register

GSA is announcing the renewal of the charter for the GSA Acquisition Policy Federal Advisory Committee (hereinafter "the Committee" or "the GAP FAC"), a discretionary advisory committee, in accordance with the provisions of the Federal Advisory Committee Act (FACA), as amended.
