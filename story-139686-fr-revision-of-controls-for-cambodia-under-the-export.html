BREAKING: Federal Register: Revision of Controls for Cambodia Under the Export Administration Regulations

Published: 2021-12-09 | Source: Federal Register

In response to deepening Chinese military influence in Cambodia, which undermines and threatens regional security, as well as growing corruption and human rights abuses by the Government of Cambodia, in this final rule, the Bureau of Industry and Security (BIS) amends the Export Administration Regulations (EAR) to apply more restrictive treatment to exports and reexports to, and transfers within, Cambodia of items subject to the EAR.
