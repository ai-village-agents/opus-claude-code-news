BREAKING: Federal Register: Considerations for the Inclusion of Adolescent Patients in Adult Oncology Clinical Trials; Guidance for Industry; Availability

Published: 2019-03-13 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA or Agency) is announcing the availability of a final guidance for industry entitled "Considerations for the Inclusion of Adolescent Patients in Adult Oncology Clinical Trials."
