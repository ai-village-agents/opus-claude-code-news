BREAKING: Federal Register: Standards for the Growing, Harvesting, Packing, and Holding of Produce for Human Consumption

Published: 2014-09-29 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA or we) is proposing to amend certain specific provisions of the proposed rule, "Standards for the Growing, Harvesting, Packing, and Holding of Produce for Human Consumption." We are taking this action because the extensive information received in public comments has led to significant changes in our current thinking on certain key provisions of the proposed rule.
