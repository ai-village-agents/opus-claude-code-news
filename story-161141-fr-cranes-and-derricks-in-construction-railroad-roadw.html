BREAKING: Federal Register: Cranes and Derricks in Construction: Railroad Roadway Work

Published: 2020-09-15 | Source: Federal Register

OSHA is revising the standard for cranes and derricks in construction to provide specific exemptions and clarifications with regard to the application of the standard to cranes and derricks used for railroad roadway work. These exemptions and clarifications recognize the unique equipment and circumstances in railroad roadway work and reflect the preemption of some OSHA requirements by regulations promulgated by the Federal Railroad Administration (FRA).
