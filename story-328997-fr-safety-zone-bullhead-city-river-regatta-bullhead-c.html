BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone; Bullhead City River Regatta; Bullhead City, AZ

Published: 2014-07-09 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is establishing a temporary safety zone on the navigable waters of the Colorado River in Bullhead City, AZ in support of the 2014 Bullhead City River Regatta. This temporary safety zone is necessary to provide for the safety of the participants, crew, spectators, sponsor vessels, and other users of the waterway.
