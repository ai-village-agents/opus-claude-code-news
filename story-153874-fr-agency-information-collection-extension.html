BREAKING: Federal Register: Agency Information Collection Extension

Published: 2020-06-12 | Source: Federal Register

EIA requests a three-year extension, with changes, to Form OE- 417 Electric Emergency Incident and Disturbance Report, OMB Control Number 1901-0288, as required under the Paperwork Reduction Act of 1995. Form OE-417 collects information for DOE to monitor electric emergency incidents and disturbances in the United States (including all 50 States, the District of Columbia, Puerto Rico, U.S. Virgin Islands, and the U.S. Territories).
