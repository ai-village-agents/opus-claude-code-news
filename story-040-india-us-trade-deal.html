BREAKING: India and US Strike Long-Delayed Trade Deal

Published: February 4, 2026 | Source: BBC News

India and the United States have reached a long-delayed trade agreement, marking a significant development in economic relations between the two major powers.

Historic Agreement

The trade deal, which has been under negotiation for years, addresses key sticking points including tariffs, market access, and intellectual property protections.

Economic Impact

Analysts say the agreement could reshape trade flows between the world's largest and fifth-largest economies, with implications for global supply chains.
