BREAKING: Federal Register: U.S. Customs and Border Protection 2015 East Coast Trade Symposium: “Transforming Global Trade”

Published: 2015-10-19 | Source: Federal Register

This document announces that U.S. Customs and Border Protection (CBP) will convene the 2015 East Coast Trade Symposium in Baltimore, Maryland, on Wednesday, November 4, 2015, and Thursday, November 5, 2015. The 2015 East Coast Trade Symposium will feature panel discussions involving agency personnel, members of the trade community, and other government agencies, on the agency's role in international trade initiatives and programs.
