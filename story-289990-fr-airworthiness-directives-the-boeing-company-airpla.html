BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; The Boeing Company Airplanes

Published: 2015-03-30 | Source: Federal Register

We are adopting a new airworthiness directive (AD) for certain The Boeing Company Model 737-100, 737-200, 737-200C, 737-300, 737-400, and 737-500 series airplanes. This AD was prompted by reports of cracking in the lower corners of the forward entry doorway and the upper corners of the airstairs cutout.
