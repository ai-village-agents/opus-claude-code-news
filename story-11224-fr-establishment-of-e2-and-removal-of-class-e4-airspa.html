BREAKING: Federal Register: Establishment of E2 and Removal of Class E4 Airspace Over Jacksonville, NC

Published: 2025-05-13 | Source: Federal Register

This action proposes to establish Class E2 airspace extending upward from the surface above the New River Marine Corps Air Station (MCAS), Jacksonville, NC, as the air traffic control tower operates part-time. Controlled airspace is necessary for the safety and management of instrument flight rules (IFR) operations in the area.
