BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Tribal Consultation on the Department of Veterans Affairs' State Home Programs

Published: 2021-05-19 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Veterans Affairs (VA) is seeking Tribal consultation to assist VA in implementing the requirements of section 3004 of the Johnny Isakson and David P. Roe, M.D. Veterans Health Care and Benefits Improvement Act of 2020 (the Act), The Act amended the statutory authorities of two VA programs (the State Veterans Home Construction Grant Program and the State Home Per Diem Program) so that Indian tribes were included in definitions for "state" and "state home."
