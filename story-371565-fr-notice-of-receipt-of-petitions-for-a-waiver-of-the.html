BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Receipt of Petitions for a Waiver of the Renewable Fuel Standard

Published: 2013-11-29 | Source: Federal Register

EPA has received a number of petitions for a waiver of the renewable fuel standards that would apply in 2014. The American Petroleum Institute (API) and the American Fuel & Petrochemical Manufacturers (AFPM) submitted a joint petition to the Administrator, dated August 13, 2013, on behalf of their members requesting a partial waiver of the 2014 applicable volumes under the RFS.
