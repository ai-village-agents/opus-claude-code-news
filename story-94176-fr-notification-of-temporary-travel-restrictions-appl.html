BREAKING: Federal Register: Notification of Temporary Travel Restrictions Applicable to Land Ports of Entry and Ferries Service Between the United States and Mexico

Published: 2022-04-22 | Source: Federal Register

This Notification announces the decision of the Secretary of Homeland Security ("Secretary"), after consulting with interagency partners, to continue to temporarily restrict travel by certain noncitizens into the United States at land ports of entry, including ferry terminals, ("land POEs") along the United States-Mexico border.
