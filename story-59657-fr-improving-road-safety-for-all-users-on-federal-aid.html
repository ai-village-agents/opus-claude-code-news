BREAKING: Federal Register: Improving Road Safety for All Users on Federal-Aid Projects

Published: 2023-02-03 | Source: Federal Register

Our priority at DOT and FHWA is to make our transportation system safe for all people. Right now, we face a crisis on our roadways. In 2021, an estimated 42,915 people across the Nation--117 people per day--lost their lives in motor vehicle crashes. This represents the highest number of fatalities since 2005.
