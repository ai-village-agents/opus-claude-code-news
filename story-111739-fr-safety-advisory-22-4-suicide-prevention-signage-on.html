BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Advisory 22-4 Suicide Prevention Signage on Public Transit

Published: 2022-12-07 | Source: Federal Register

The Federal Transit Administration (FTA) is issuing Safety Advisory 22-4 to encourage rail transit agencies to implement, update, or expand suicide prevention signage and messaging campaigns that apply best practices for deterring suicide attempts. This safety advisory provides guidance on suicide prevention signage and mental health intervention campaigns and provides supporting resources for transit agencies.
