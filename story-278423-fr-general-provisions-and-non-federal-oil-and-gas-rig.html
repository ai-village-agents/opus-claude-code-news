BREAKING: Federal Register: General Provisions and Non-Federal Oil and Gas Rights

Published: 2016-11-04 | Source: Federal Register

We are updating our service-wide regulations governing the exercise of non-federal oil and gas rights, to improve our ability to protect park resources, values, and visitors from potential impacts associated with nonfederal oil and gas operations located within National Park Service units outside Alaska.
