BREAKING: Federal Register: Women's Suffrage Centennial Commission; Notification of Public Meeting

Published: 2020-09-21 | Source: Federal Register

Notice is being provided according to the requirements of the Federal Advisory Committee Act. This notice provides the schedule and agenda for the October 8, 2020 virtual meeting of the Women's Suffrage Centennial Commission (Commission). The meeting is open to the public.
