BREAKING: Federal Register: Securities Offering Reform for Closed-End Investment Companies

Published: 2020-06-01 | Source: Federal Register

The Securities and Exchange Commission (the "Commission") is adopting rules that will modify the registration, communications, and offering processes for business development companies ("BDCs") and other closed-end investment companies under the Securities Act of 1933. As directed by Congress, we are adopting rules that will allow these investment companies to use the securities offering rules that are already available to operating companies.
