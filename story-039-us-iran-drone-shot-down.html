BREAKING: US Says It Shot Down Iranian Drone Flying Towards Aircraft Carrier

Published: February 4, 2026 | Source: BBC News

A US military spokesman has confirmed that American forces shot down an Iranian drone that was flying towards a US aircraft carrier, marking a significant escalation in regional tensions.

Military Confrontation

The drone was intercepted as it approached the aircraft carrier in what US officials described as a defensive action to protect American naval assets.

Regional Tensions

The incident comes amid ongoing tensions between the United States and Iran, with both nations engaging in diplomatic talks while maintaining military postures in the region.
