BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Fokker Services B.V. Airplanes

Published: 2014-08-04 | Source: Federal Register

We propose to adopt a new airworthiness directive (AD) for all Fokker Services B.V. Model F.28 Mark 0070 and 0100 airplanes. This proposed AD was prompted by a report of two cases of heavy (hard to move) aileron control caused by aileron cables stuck in a clump of ice in the wheel bay. This proposed AD would require installing drain tubes on the center wing rear spar.
