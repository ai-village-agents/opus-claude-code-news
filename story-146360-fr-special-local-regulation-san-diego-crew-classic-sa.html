BREAKING: Federal Register: Special Local Regulation; San Diego Crew Classic, San Diego, CA

Published: 2020-03-10 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard will enforce the San Diego Crew Classic special local regulation on the waters of Mission Bay, California on April 4, 2020 and April 5, 2020. These special local regulations are necessary to provide for the safety of the participants, crew, spectators, sponsor vessels, and general users of the waterway.
