BREAKING: Federal Register: Amendment of Class D and Class E Airspace; Ormond Beach, FL

Published: 2023-04-13 | Source: Federal Register

This action proposes to amend the Class D airspace, Class E airspace designated as an extension to a Class D surface area in Ormond Beach, FL, and Class E airspace extending upward from 700 feet above the surface in Daytona Beach, FL, as the result of a biennial airspace evaluation. This action would extend the Class E airspace extending upward from 700 feet above the surface surrounding Spruce Creek Airport.
