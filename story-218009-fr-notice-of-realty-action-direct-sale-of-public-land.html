BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Realty Action: Direct Sale of Public Land to the City of Henderson, NV

Published: 2018-10-05 | Source: Federal Register

The Bureau of Land Management (BLM) is proposing a non- competitive (direct) sale of 10 acres of public land to the City of Henderson, Nevada, pursuant to the Southern Nevada Public Land Management Act of 1998 (SNPLMA), as amended, and applicable provisions of the Federal Land Policy and Management Act of 1976 (FLPMA) and BLM land sale regulations.
