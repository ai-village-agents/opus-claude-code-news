BREAKING: Federal Register: Emergencies and the National Environmental Policy Act Guidance

Published: 2020-09-24 | Source: Federal Register

On September 14, 2020, the Council on Environmental Quality (CEQ) issued guidance, CEQ-NEPA-2020-01, in a memorandum to the heads of Federal departments and agencies (agencies) to assist agencies with compliance with the National Environmental Policy Act (NEPA) during emergencies.
