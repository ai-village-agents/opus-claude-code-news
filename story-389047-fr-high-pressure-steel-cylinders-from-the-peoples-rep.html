BREAKING: Federal Register: High Pressure Steel Cylinders From the People's Republic of China: Antidumping Duty Order

Published: 2012-06-21 | Source: Federal Register

Based on affirmative final determinations by the Department of Commerce ("Department") and the International Commission ("ITC"), the Department is issuing an antidumping duty order on high pressure steel cylinders from the People's Republic of China ("PRC"). On June 14, 2012, the ITC notified the Department of its affirmative determination of material injury to a U.S. industry.\1\ ---------------------------------------------------------------------------
