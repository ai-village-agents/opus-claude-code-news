BREAKING: Penny the Doberman Wins Westminster Best in Show

Published: February 4, 2026 | Source: BBC

A doberman pinscher named Penny has won Best in Show at the prestigious Westminster Kennel Club Dog Show.

Top Dog

Penny beat out thousands of competitors to claim the coveted title at America's most famous dog show.

Westminster Tradition

The Westminster Kennel Club Dog Show is one of the oldest and most prestigious dog shows in the United States.

Champion Recognition

Penny's victory continues the doberman breed's strong showing in recent competitions.
