BREAKING: Federal Register: Telecommunications Carriers Eligible for Support; Lifeline and Link Up Reform and Several Petitions for Forbearance

Published: 2013-05-08 | Source: Federal Register

In this order, the Federal Communications Commission (Commission) grants limited forbearance from the requirement of the Commission's rules that the service area of an eligible telecommunications carrier (ETC) conform to the service area of any rural telephone company serving the same area.
