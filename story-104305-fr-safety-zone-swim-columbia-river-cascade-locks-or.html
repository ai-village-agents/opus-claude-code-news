BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone; Swim, Columbia River, Cascade Locks, OR

Published: 2022-09-01 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is establishing a temporary safety zone for certain waters of the Columbia River. This action is necessary to provide for the safety of participants and the maritime public during a cross-channel swim on the Columbia River near Cascade Locks, Oregon, to Stevenson, Washington, on the morning of September 5, 2022.
