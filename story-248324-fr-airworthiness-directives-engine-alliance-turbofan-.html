BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Engine Alliance Turbofan Engines

Published: 2017-11-09 | Source: Federal Register

We are superseding Emergency Airworthiness Directive (AD) 2017-21-51 for all Engine Alliance (EA) GP7200 series turbofan engines. AD 2017-21-51 was sent previously to all known U.S. owners and operators of GP7200 series turbofan engines. AD 2017-21-51 required visual inspections of all fan hubs for damage.
