BREAKING: Federal Register: Proposed Amendment of Class D Airspace and Class E Airspace; Biloxi, MS, and Gulfport, MS

Published: 2018-02-26 | Source: Federal Register

This action proposes to amend Class D airspace, Class E surface airspace, Class E airspace designated as an extension (by removing NOTAM part-time status), and Class E airspace extending upward from 700 feet above the surface at Keesler Air Force Base (AFB), Biloxi, MS, and Gulfport-Biloxi International Airport, (formerly Gulfport-Biloxi Regional Airport), Gulfport, MS.
