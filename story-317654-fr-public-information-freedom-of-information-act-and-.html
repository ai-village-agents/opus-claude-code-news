BREAKING: Federal Register: Public Information, Freedom of Information Act and Privacy Act Regulations

Published: 2014-02-27 | Source: Federal Register

This rule proposes revisions to the Department of Commerce's (Department) regulations under the Freedom of Information Act (FOIA) and Privacy Act.
