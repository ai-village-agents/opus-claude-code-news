BREAKING: Federal Register: Certain Radiotherapy Systems and Treatment Planning Software, and Components Thereof; Notice of Request for Statements on the Public Interest

Published: 2016-11-03 | Source: Federal Register

Notice is hereby given that the presiding administrative law judge ("ALJ") has issued a recommended determination on remedy and bonding in the above-captioned investigation. The Commission is soliciting submissions from the public on any public interest issues raised by the recommended relief. The ALJ recommended that a limited exclusion order issue against certain radiotherapy systems and treatment planning software, and components thereof, imported by respondents Elekta AB of Stockholm, Sweden...
