BREAKING: Federal Register: Modernizing the Delivery of Housing Counseling Services

Published: 2024-09-16 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Housing and Urban Development (HUD) is issuing this final rule to update HUD's regulations that require participating agencies to provide in-person counseling to clients that prefer this format to reflect advances in technology, align with client engagement preferences, and preserve consumer protections.
