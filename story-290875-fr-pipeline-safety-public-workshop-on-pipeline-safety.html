BREAKING: Federal Register: Pipeline Safety: Public Workshop on Pipeline Safety Management Systems

Published: 2015-04-09 | Source: Federal Register

This notice is announcing a one-day public workshop to discuss the recent Pipeline Safety Management Systems (SMS) national consensus standard. The meeting will include participation from all major pipeline sectors, state and Federal regulators, and public safety advocates. This workshop will detail the development process of the SMS standard.
