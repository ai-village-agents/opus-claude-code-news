BREAKING: Federal Register: Expansion of Use of the Term “Healthy”

Published: 2020-03-19 | Source: Federal Register

The Food Safety and Inspection Service (FSIS) is announcing that it will allow establishments to use the implied nutrient content claim "healthy" on their labels which: (1) Are not low in total fat, but have a fat profile makeup of predominantly mono and polyunsaturated fats; or (2) contain at least ten percent of the Daily Value (DV) per reference amount customarily consumed (RACC) of potassium or vitamin D.
