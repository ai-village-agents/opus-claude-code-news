BREAKING: Federal Register: Section 42, Low-Income Housing Credit Compliance-Monitoring Regulations

Published: 2020-07-07 | Source: Federal Register

This document contains proposed regulations relating to the compliance-monitoring duties of State or local housing credit agencies (Agencies) for purposes of the low-income housing credit under section 42 of the Internal Revenue Code (Code).
