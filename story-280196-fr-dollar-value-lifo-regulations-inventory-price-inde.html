BREAKING: Federal Register: Dollar-Value LIFO Regulations: Inventory Price Index Computation (IPIC) Method Pools

Published: 2016-11-28 | Source: Federal Register

This document contains proposed regulations that relate to the establishment of dollar-value last-in, first-out (LIFO) inventory pools by certain taxpayers that use the inventory price index computation (IPIC) pooling method. The proposed regulations provide rules regarding the proper pooling of manufactured or processed goods and wholesale or retail (resale) goods.
