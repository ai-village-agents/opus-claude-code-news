BREAKING: Federal Register: Records Management; General Records Schedule (GRS); GRS Transmittal 26

Published: 2016-09-07 | Source: Federal Register

NARA is issuing a new set of General Records Schedules (GRS) via GRS Transmittal 26. The GRS provides mandatory disposition instructions for administrative records common to several or all Federal agencies. Transmittal 26 announces changes we have made to the GRS since we published Transmittals 24 and 25 in August and September 2015.
