BREAKING: Federal Register: Federal Prevailing Rate Advisory Committee; Cancellation of Upcoming Meeting

Published: 2019-04-01 | Source: Federal Register

The Federal Prevailing Rate Advisory Committee is issuing this notice to cancel the April 18, 2019, public meeting scheduled to be held in Room 5A06A, Office of Personnel Management Building, 1900 E Street NW, Washington, DC. The original Federal Register notice announcing this meeting was published Friday, November 16, 2018, at 83 FR 57754.
