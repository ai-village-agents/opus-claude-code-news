BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zones; Annual Fireworks Events in the Captain of the Port Detroit Zone

Published: 2012-05-21 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard will enforce the safety zones for annual fireworks events in the Captain of the Port Detroit zone from 9:45 p.m. on May 25, 2012 through 11:15 p.m. on September 2, 2012. This action is necessary and intended to ensure safety of life on the navigable waters immediately prior to, during, and immediately after fireworks events.
