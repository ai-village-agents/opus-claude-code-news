BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Airbus SAS Airplanes

Published: 2020-02-06 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA is adopting a new airworthiness directive (AD) for certain Airbus SAS Model A319-112, A319-115, A319-132, A320-214, A320- 216, A320-232, A320-233, A320-251N, A320-271N, A321-211, A321-231, A321-232, A321-251N, and A321-253N airplanes. This AD was prompted by reports of finding container/galley end stop bumpers damaged in service.
