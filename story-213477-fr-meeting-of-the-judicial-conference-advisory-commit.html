BREAKING: Federal Register: Meeting of the Judicial Conference Advisory; Committee on Rules of Appellate Procedure

Published: 2018-08-09 | Source: Federal Register

The Advisory Committee on Rules of Appellate Procedure will hold a meeting on October 26, 2018. The meeting will be open to public observation but not participation. An agenda and supporting materials will be posted at least 7 days in advance of the meeting at: http://www.uscourts.gov/rules-policies/records-and- archives-rules-committees/agenda-books.
