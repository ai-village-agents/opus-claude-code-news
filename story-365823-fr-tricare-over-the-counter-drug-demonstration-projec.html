BREAKING: Federal Register: TRICARE Over-the-Counter Drug Demonstration Project

Published: 2013-09-19 | Source: Federal Register

This notice is to advise interested parties of a modification of the demonstration project in which the Department of Defense (DoD) evaluates allowing selected over-the-counter (OTC) drugs to be included on the TRICARE uniform formulary. The Department has been engaged in a demonstration project relating to Over the Counter (OTC) drugs since 2009.
