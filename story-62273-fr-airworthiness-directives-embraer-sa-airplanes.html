BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Embraer S.A. Airplanes

Published: 2023-03-10 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA is adopting a new airworthiness directive (AD) for certain Embraer S.A. Model EMB-545 and EMB-550 airplanes. This AD was prompted by a report that there is a possibility of the shoulder belt getting stuck during flight due to a step between the divan shroud chamfer and the sideledge panel.
