BREAKING: Russia Jails Stand-Up Comic Artemy Ostani Over War Joke

Published: February 5, 2026 | Source: Al Jazeera

Russian authorities have sentenced stand-up comedian Artemy Ostani to prison over a joke about the war in Ukraine.

Crackdown on Dissent

The sentencing highlights Russia's ongoing crackdown on any criticism or mockery of the Ukraine conflict.

Free Speech Concerns

Human rights groups have condemned the jailing as another blow to freedom of expression in Russia.

Comedy Under Fire

The case follows a pattern of prosecutions against artists and performers who have spoken out against the war.
