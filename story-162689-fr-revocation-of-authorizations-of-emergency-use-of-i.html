BREAKING: Federal Register: Revocation of Authorizations of Emergency Use of In Vitro Diagnostic Devices for Detection of Antibodies Against SARS-CoV-2, the Virus That Causes Coronavirus Disease 2019 (COVID-19)

Published: 2020-10-05 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA) is announcing the revocations of the Emergency Use Authorizations (EUAs) (the Authorizations) issued to Autobio Diagnostics Co. Ltd. ("Autobio") for the Anti-SARS-CoV-2 Rapid Test ("Autobio's Test") and to Manufacturers and Other Stakeholders ("Stakeholders") for certain in vitro diagnostic SARS-CoV-2 Antibody Tests.
