BREAKING: Federal Register: Countermeasures Injury Compensation Program: Smallpox Countermeasures Injury Table

Published: 2021-08-16 | Source: Federal Register

HHS is establishing the Smallpox Countermeasures Injury Table (Table) as authorized by the Public Readiness and Emergency Preparedness Act of 2005 (PREP Act). Through this final rule, the Secretary of the U.S. Department of Health and Human Services (Secretary) adds the Smallpox Countermeasures Injury Table to the agency's regulations.
