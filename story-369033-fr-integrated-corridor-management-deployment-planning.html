BREAKING: Federal Register: Integrated Corridor Management Deployment Planning Grants

Published: 2013-11-01 | Source: Federal Register

This notice invites States, Metropolitan Planning Organizations (MPO), and local governments that intend to initiate or continue Integrated Corridor Management (ICM) development with their partners, such as arterial management agencies, tolling authorities, and transit authorities, to apply for deployment planning grants.
