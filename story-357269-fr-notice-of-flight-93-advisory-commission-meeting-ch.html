BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Flight 93 Advisory Commission Meeting Change

Published: 2013-06-12 | Source: Federal Register

Notice is hereby given of a meeting of the Flight 93 Advisory Commission, on September 10, 2013, Flight 93 National Memorial Office, 109 West Main Street, Somerset, PA 15501. This is a date change from what was announced in the Federal Register on January 29, 2013.
