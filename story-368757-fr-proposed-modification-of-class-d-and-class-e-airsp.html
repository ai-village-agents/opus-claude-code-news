BREAKING: Federal Register: Proposed Modification of Class D and Class E Airspace; Kailua-Kona, HI

Published: 2013-10-31 | Source: Federal Register

This action proposes to modify Class D and E airspace at Kona International Airport at Keahole, Kailua-Kona, HI. Controlled airspace is necessary to accommodate the Area Navigation (RNAV) Global Positioning System (GPS) and the Instrument Landing System (ILS) or Localizer (LOC) standard instrument approach procedures at the airport.
