BREAKING: Federal Register: Boulder Canyon Project

Published: 2023-08-31 | Source: Federal Register

The Deputy Secretary confirms, approves, and places into effect on a final basis the Boulder Canyon Project base charge and rates for fiscal year 2024 under Rate Schedule BCP-F11. The base charge increased 11.3 percent from $66.8 million in FY 2023 to $74.3 million in FY 2024.
