BREAKING: Federal Register: Office of the National Coordinator for Health Information Technology; Announcement of Requirements and Registration for “Consumer Health Data Aggregator Challenge”

Published: 2016-03-03 | Source: Federal Register

The Consumer Health Data Aggregator Challenge is intended to spur the development of third-party, consumer-facing applications that use open, standardized Application Programming Interfaces (APIs) to help consumers aggregate their data in one place and under their control.
