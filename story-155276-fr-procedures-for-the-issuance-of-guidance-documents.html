BREAKING: Federal Register: Procedures for the Issuance of Guidance Documents

Published: 2020-07-01 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Department of Energy (DOE) proposes to establish procedures for the issuance of DOE guidance documents in accordance with Executive Order 13891. The proposed rule would establish internal agency requirements for the contents of guidance documents, and procedures for providing notice of, and soliciting public comment on, certain guidance documents.
