BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Hélicoptères Guimbal Helicopters

Published: 2022-04-05 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA is superseding Airworthiness Directive (AD) 2021-02- 20, which applied to certain H[eacute]licopt[egrave]res Guimbal (HG) Model Cabri G2 helicopters. AD 2021-02-20 required initial and repetitive inspections of certain rotating and non-rotating scissor fittings, and depending on the results, replacing the affected assembly.
