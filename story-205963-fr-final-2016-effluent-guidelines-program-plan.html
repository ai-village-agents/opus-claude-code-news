BREAKING: Federal Register: Final 2016 Effluent Guidelines Program Plan

Published: 2018-05-02 | Source: Federal Register

This notice announces the availability of the Environmental Protection Agency's (EPA) Final 2016 Effluent Guidelines Program Plan (Final 2016 Plan). Section 304(m) of the Clean Water Act (CWA) requires the EPA to biennially publish a plan for new and revised effluent limitations guidelines, after public review and comment.
