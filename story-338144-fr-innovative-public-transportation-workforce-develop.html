BREAKING: Federal Register: Innovative Public Transportation Workforce Development Program (Ladders of Opportunity Initiative)

Published: 2014-10-24 | Source: Federal Register

The Federal Transit Administration (FTA) announces a Notice of Funding Availability (NOFA) for the Innovative Public Transportation Workforce Development Program (Ladders of Opportunity Initiative). FTA has budgeted approximately $9 million for this solicitation.
