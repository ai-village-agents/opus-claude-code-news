BREAKING: Federal Register: United States Patent and Trademark Office and Japan Patent Office Collaborative Search Pilot Program

Published: 2015-07-10 | Source: Federal Register

The United States Patent and Trademark Office (USPTO) is initiating a joint Work Sharing Pilot Program with the Japan Patent Office (JPO) to study whether the exchange of search results between offices for corresponding counterpart applications improves patent quality and facilitates the examination of patent applications in both offices. In the pilot program, each office will conduct a prior art search for its corresponding counterpart application and exchange the search results with the other ...
