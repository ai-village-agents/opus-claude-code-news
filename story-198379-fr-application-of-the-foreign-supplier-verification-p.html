BREAKING: Federal Register: Application of the Foreign Supplier Verification Program Regulation to Importers of Grain Raw Agricultural Commodities: Guidance for Industry; Availability

Published: 2018-01-25 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA, we, or Agency) is announcing the availability of a guidance for industry entitled "Application of the Foreign Supplier Verification Program Regulation to Importers of Grain Raw Agricultural Commodities: Guidance for Industry."
