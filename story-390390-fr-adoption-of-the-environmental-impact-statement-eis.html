BREAKING: Federal Register: Adoption of the Environmental Impact Statement (EIS) and Participation in the Section 106 Programmatic Agreement for the East Side Access Project

Published: 2012-07-06 | Source: Federal Register

FRA is issuing this notice to advise the public and interested agencies that FRA is adopting the Federal Transit Administration (FTA) March 2001 Final Environmental Impact Statement and subsequent FTA reevaluations (collectively, the "2001 EIS") for the East Side Access project proposed by the Metropolitan Transportation Authority (MTA) in the State of New York.
