BREAKING: Federal Register: Project Financing Loans

Published: 2013-06-05 | Source: Federal Register

The Rural Utilities Service (RUS or Agency) is considering regulatory reforms to codify statutory changes related to "project financing" requirements to advance the agency's rural development mission and improve its ability to finance electric infrastructure projects, including those that use renewable sources of energy.
