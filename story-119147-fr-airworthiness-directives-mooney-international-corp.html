BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Mooney International Corporation Airplanes

Published: 2021-03-17 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA is correcting a notice of proposed rulemaking (NPRM) that published in the Federal Register. The NPRM proposed to issue an airworthiness directive (AD) that would apply to certain Mooney International Corporation (Mooney) Model M20V airplanes. As published, the docket number referenced throughout is incorrect. This document corrects that error.
