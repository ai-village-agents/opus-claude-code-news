BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Inventory Completion: Museum of Natural History and Planetarium, Roger Williams Park, Providence RI

Published: 2017-06-30 | Source: Federal Register

The Museum of Natural History and Planetarium, Roger Williams Park, has completed an inventory of human remains and associated funerary objects, in consultation with the appropriate Indian tribes or Native Hawaiian organizations, and has determined that there is a cultural affiliation between the human remains and associated funerary objects and present-day Indian tribes or Native Hawaiian organizations.
