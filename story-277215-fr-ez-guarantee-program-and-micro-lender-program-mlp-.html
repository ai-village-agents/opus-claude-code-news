BREAKING: Federal Register: EZ Guarantee Program and Micro Lender Program (MLP) Status

Published: 2016-10-21 | Source: Federal Register

The Farm Service Agency (FSA) is amending the guaranteed Farm Loan Programs (FLP) regulations to implement an EZ Guarantee Program and establish an additional lender status. The EZ Guarantee Program will help lenders reduce costs of underwriting and servicing loans to help meet the unique financing needs of small farm operations.
