BREAKING: Federal Register: Technical Correction to Centers of Excellence and Expertise Regulations

Published: 2019-09-05 | Source: Federal Register

On December 20, 2016, U.S. Customs and Border Protection (CBP) published an interim final rule in the Federal Register, which established the Centers of Excellence and Expertise (Centers) as a permanent organizational component of the agency and transitioned certain operational trade functions to the Center directors that traditionally resided with the port directors.
