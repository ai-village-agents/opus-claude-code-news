BREAKING: Federal Register: Group Registration of Serials

Published: 2019-11-12 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Copyright Office is amending its regulation governing the group registration option for serials. Under the current regulation, applicants may complete and submit the online application designated for a group of serial issues, or they may complete and submit a paper application using Form SE/Group, provided the Office receives the paper form by December 30, 2019.
