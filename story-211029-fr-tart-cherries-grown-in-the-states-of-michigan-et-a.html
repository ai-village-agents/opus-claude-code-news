BREAKING: Federal Register: Tart Cherries Grown in the States of Michigan, et al.; Revision of Exemption Requirements

Published: 2018-07-06 | Source: Federal Register

This rule implements a recommendation from the Cherry Industry Administrative Board (Board) to revise the exemption provisions for tart cherries grown in Michigan, New York, Pennsylvania, Oregon, Utah, Washington, and Wisconsin. This rule changes the number of years that new product, new market development, and market expansion projects are eligible for handler diversion credit.
