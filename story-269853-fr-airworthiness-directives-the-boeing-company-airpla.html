BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; The Boeing Company Airplanes

Published: 2016-07-28 | Source: Federal Register

This proposed AD was prompted by an evaluation by the design approval holder (DAH) indicating that the nose wheel well is subject to widespread fatigue damage (WFD).
