BREAKING: Federal Register: Data Portability

Published: 2016-10-07 | Source: Federal Register

Many modern service providers give people access to their own data in machine readable format to download and use as they see fit. Proponents of increased data portability point to numerous, significant benefits for users, service providers, and the broader public.
