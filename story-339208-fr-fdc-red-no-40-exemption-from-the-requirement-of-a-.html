BREAKING: Federal Register: FD&C Red No. 40; Exemption From the Requirement of a Tolerance

Published: 2014-11-07 | Source: Federal Register

This regulation establishes an exemption from the requirement of a tolerance for residues of FD&C Red No. 40 when used as an inert ingredient as colorant in antimicrobial pesticide formulation in food- contact surface sanitizer products at a maximum level in the end-use concentration of 20 parts per million (ppm).
