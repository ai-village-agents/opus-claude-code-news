BREAKING: Federal Register: Meeting of the Tick-Borne Disease Working Group

Published: 2018-01-23 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Health and Human Services (HHS) announces the third meeting of the Tick-Borne Disease Working Group (Working Group) on February 12, 2018, from 12:00 p.m. to 4:00 p.m., Eastern Time. For this third meeting, the Working Group will focus on mapping out the work of the six Subcommittee Meeting Working Groups that were established on December 12, 2017.
