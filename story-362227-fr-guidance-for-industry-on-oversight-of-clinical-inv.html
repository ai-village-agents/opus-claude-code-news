BREAKING: Federal Register: Guidance for Industry on Oversight of Clinical Investigations-A Risk-Based Approach to Monitoring; Availability

Published: 2013-08-07 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA) is announcing the availability of a guidance for industry entitled "Oversight of Clinical Investigations--A Risk-Based Approach to Monitoring." This guidance assists sponsors in developing risk-based monitoring strategies and plans for clinical investigations of human drugs, biologics, medical devices, and combinations thereof.
