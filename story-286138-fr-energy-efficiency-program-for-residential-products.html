BREAKING: Federal Register: Energy Efficiency Program for Residential Products: Energy Conservation Standards for Miscellaneous Refrigeration Products

Published: 2015-02-11 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Department of Energy (DOE) has published a preliminary technical support document (TSD) that analyzes the potential economic impacts and energy savings that could result from potential energy conservation standards for miscellaneous refrigeration products. DOE published this analysis so stakeholders can review the relevant outputs and the underlining assumptions and calculations.
