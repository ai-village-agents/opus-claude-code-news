BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Proposed Filing of Plats of Survey, South Dakota

Published: 2023-07-05 | Source: Federal Register

The plats of surveys for the lands described in this notice are scheduled to be officially filed 30 calendar days after the date of this publication in the BLM Montana State Office, Billings, Montana. The surveys, which were executed at the request of the Superintendent, Crow Creek Agency, Bureau of Indian Affairs dated February 27, 2020, and Interagency Agreement No. 4500146692, dated July 30, 2020, are necessary for the management of these lands.
