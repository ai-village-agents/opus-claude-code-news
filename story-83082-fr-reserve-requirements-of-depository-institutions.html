BREAKING: Federal Register: Reserve Requirements of Depository Institutions

Published: 2023-11-29 | Source: Federal Register

The Board is amending Regulation D, Reserve Requirements of Depository Institutions, to reflect the annual indexing of the reserve requirement exemption amount and the low reserve tranche for 2024. The annual indexation of these amounts is required notwithstanding the Board's action in March 2020 of setting all reserve requirement ratios to zero.
