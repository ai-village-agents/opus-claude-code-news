BREAKING: Federal Register: Approval of Air Quality Implementation Plans; Missouri State Implementation Plan for the 2008 Lead Standard

Published: 2016-02-29 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) proposes to grant full approval of Missouri's attainment demonstration State Implementation Plan (SIP) for the lead National Ambient Air Quality Standard (NAAQS) for the Exide Technologies Canon Hollow facility in Forest City, Missouri, received by EPA on October 20, 2014.
