BREAKING: Federal Register: Joint Report: Differences in Accounting and Capital Standards Among the Federal Banking Agencies as of December 31, 2019; Report to Congressional Committees

Published: 2020-07-13 | Source: Federal Register

The OCC, the Board, and the FDIC (collectively, the agencies) have prepared this report pursuant to section 37(c) of the Federal Deposit Insurance Act. Section 37(c) requires the agencies to jointly submit an annual report to the Committee on Financial Services of the U.S. House of Representatives and to the Committee on Banking, Housing, and Urban Affairs of the U.S. Senate describing differences among the accounting and capital standards used by the agencies for insured depository institutions...
