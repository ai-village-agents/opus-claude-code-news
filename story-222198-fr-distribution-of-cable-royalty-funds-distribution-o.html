BREAKING: Federal Register: Distribution of Cable Royalty Funds; Distribution of Satellite Royalty Funds

Published: 2018-11-30 | Source: Federal Register

The Copyright Royalty Judges (Judges) announce the final distribution of cable and satellite royalty funds for the years 2010, 2011, 2012, and 2013. The determination is a result of agreement among the participants that claim shares of the cable and satellite royalty funds to be allocated to the Program Suppliers Claimant category.
