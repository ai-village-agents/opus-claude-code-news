BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; the Boeing Company Airplanes

Published: 2013-08-13 | Source: Federal Register

We propose to adopt a new airworthiness directive (AD) for certain The Boeing Company Model 707 airplanes; and Model 720 and 720B series airplanes. This proposed AD was prompted by reports indicating that a standard access door was located where an impact-resistant access door was required, and stencils were missing from some impact- resistant access doors.
