BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone; Navy Week New Orleans 2022 Fireworks Display Event, New Orleans, LA

Published: 2022-04-04 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard will enforce a temporary safety zone for a fireworks display located on the navigable waters of the Lower Mississippi River between Mile Marker (MM) 94.5 and MM 95.5. This action is needed to provide for the safety of life on these navigable waterways during the event. During the enforcement periods, the operator of any vessel in the regulated area must comply with directions from the Captain of the Port or designated representative.
