BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Roundtable on the Renewal of a Continuing Information Collection

Published: 2013-11-13 | Source: Federal Register

In accordance with the Paperwork Reduction Act (PRA), the United States Patent and Trademark Office (USPTO) published a notice inviting written public comment on the renewal of information collection 0651- 0032, Initial Patent Applications. The USPTO plans to conduct a roundtable to obtain additional public input regarding the burden associated with the Initial Patent Applications collection and ways to potentially reduce it.
