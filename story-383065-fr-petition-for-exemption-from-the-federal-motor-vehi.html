BREAKING: Federal Register: Petition for Exemption From the Federal Motor Vehicle Motor Theft Prevention Standard; TESLA

Published: 2012-04-13 | Source: Federal Register

This document grants in full the petition of Tesla Motors Inc's. (Tesla) for an exemption of the Model S vehicle line in accordance with 49 CFR Part 543, Exemption from the Theft Prevention Standard.
