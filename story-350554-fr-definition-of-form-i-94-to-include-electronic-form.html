BREAKING: Federal Register: Definition of Form I-94 To Include Electronic Format

Published: 2013-03-27 | Source: Federal Register

The Form I-94 is issued by the Department of Homeland Security (DHS) to certain aliens and is used for various purposes such as documenting status in the United States, the approved length of stay, and departure. DHS generally issues the Form I-94 to aliens at the time they lawfully enter the United States.
