BREAKING: Federal Register: Small Entity Government Use License Exception

Published: 2020-12-21 | Source: Federal Register

The United States Patent and Trademark Office (USPTO or Office) is amending the rules of practice in patent cases to clarify and expand exceptions to the rule pertaining to government use licenses and their effect on small entity status for purposes of paying reduced patent fees.
