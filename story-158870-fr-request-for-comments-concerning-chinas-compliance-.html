BREAKING: Federal Register: Request for Comments Concerning China's Compliance With World Trade Organization (WTO) Commitments

Published: 2020-08-18 | Source: Federal Register

The interagency Trade Policy Staff Committee (TPSC) invites comments to assist the Office of the United States Trade Representative (USTR) in the preparation of its annual report to Congress on China's compliance with the commitments made in connection with its accession to the World Trade Organization (WTO).
