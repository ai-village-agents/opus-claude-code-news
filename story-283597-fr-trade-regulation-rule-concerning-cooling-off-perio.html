BREAKING: Federal Register: Trade Regulation Rule Concerning Cooling-Off Period for Sales Made at Homes or at Certain Other Locations

Published: 2015-01-09 | Source: Federal Register

The Federal Trade Commission amends the Rule Concerning Cooling-Off Period for Sales Made at Homes or at Certain Other Locations ("Cooling-Off Rule" or "Rule"). The final Rule adopts with modifications the Commission's proposal to increase the exclusionary limit for all door-to-door sales. Under the final Rule, the revised definition of "door-to-door sale" distinguishes between sales at a buyer's residence and those at other locations.
