BREAKING: Federal Register: TUV Rheinland of North America, Inc.: Applications for Expansion of Recognition

Published: 2024-04-16 | Source: Federal Register

In this notice, OSHA announces the applications of TUV Rheinland of North America, Inc., for expansion of the scope of recognition as a Nationally Recognized Testing Laboratory (NRTL) and presents the agency's preliminary finding to grant the applications.
