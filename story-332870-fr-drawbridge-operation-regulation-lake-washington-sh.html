BREAKING: Federal Register: Drawbridge Operation Regulation; Lake Washington Ship Canal, Seattle, WA

Published: 2014-08-22 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard has issued a temporary deviation from the operating schedule that governs the Seattle Department of Transportation (SDOT) Fremont Bridge, across the Lake Washington Ship Canal, mile 2.6, at Seattle, WA. This deviation is necessary to allow the bridge to operate in single leaf mode while work crews are onsite, and to only provide a double leaf opening with a five hour advance notice.
