BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Availability of Revised Methodology for Determining Average Prime Offer Rates

Published: 2016-08-10 | Source: Federal Register

The Bureau of Consumer Financial Protection (Bureau) announces the availability of a revised methodology statement, entitled the "Methodology for Determining Average Prime Offer Rates." The methodology statement describes the data and the methodology used to calculate average prime offer rates for purposes of Regulation C and Regulation Z.
