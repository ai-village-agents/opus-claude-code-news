BREAKING: Federal Register: Privacy Act of 1974; Systems of Records

Published: 2021-01-25 | Source: Federal Register

As required by the Privacy Act of 1974, notice is hereby given that the Department of Veterans Affairs is amending the system of records currently entitled "Veterans, Dependents of Veterans, and VA Beneficiary Survey Records (43VA008)" as set forth in the Federal Register. VA is amending the System Manager, Notification Procedure, organizational information, updating existing Routine Uses to use VA standard language and adding Handbook for Secure Connections 6513.
