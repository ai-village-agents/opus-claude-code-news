BREAKING: Federal Register: Request for Applicants for the Appointment to the United States-India CEO Forum

Published: 2019-06-27 | Source: Federal Register

This notice announces membership opportunities for the appointment of two additional U.S. representatives to the existing U.S. Section of the U.S.-India CEO Forum. The U.S. Department of Commerce is seeking applicants from the aerospace/defense and healthcare sectors.
