BREAKING: Federal Register: Proposed Modification of Class E Airspace; Gold Beach Municipal Airport, OR

Published: 2021-11-19 | Source: Federal Register

This action proposes to modify the Class E airspace at Gold Beach Municipal Airport, Gold Beach, OR. This action would ensure the safety and management of instrument flight rules (IFR) operations at the airport.
