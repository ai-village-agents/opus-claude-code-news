BREAKING: Federal Register: Agenda and Notice of Public Meeting of the Rhode Island Advisory Committee

Published: 2019-05-16 | Source: Federal Register

Notice is hereby given, pursuant to the provisions of the rules and regulations of the U.S. Commission on Civil Rights (Commission), and the Federal Advisory Committee Act (FACA), that a briefing meeting of the Rhode Island Advisory Committee to the Commission will convene at 12:00 p.m. (EDT) on Tuesday, June 11, 2019, at Hinckley Allen & Snyder LLP, 100 Westminster Street, Providence, RI 02903. The purpose of the briefing is to hear from advocates and officials about hate crimes in Rhode Island...
