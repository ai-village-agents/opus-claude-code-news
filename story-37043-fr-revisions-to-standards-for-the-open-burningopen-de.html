BREAKING: Federal Register: Revisions to Standards for the Open Burning/Open Detonation of Waste Explosives; Extension of Comment Period

Published: 2024-05-16 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is extending the comment period for the proposed rule entitled "Revisions to Standards for the Open Burning/Open Detonation of Waste Explosives." EPA published the proposed rule in the Federal Register on March 20, 2024 (89 FR 19952) and the public comment period was scheduled to end on May 20, 2024.
