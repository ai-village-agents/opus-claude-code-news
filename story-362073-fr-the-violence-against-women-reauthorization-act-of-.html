BREAKING: Federal Register: The Violence Against Women Reauthorization Act of 2013: Overview of Applicability to HUD Programs

Published: 2013-08-06 | Source: Federal Register

This notice provides an overview of the applicability to HUD programs of the recently enacted Violence Against Women Reauthorization Act of 2013. The 2013 law expands the number of HUD programs subject to the statute's protections beyond HUD's public housing and section 8 tenant-based and project-based programs.
