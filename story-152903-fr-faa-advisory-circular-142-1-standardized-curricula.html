BREAKING: Federal Register: FAA Advisory Circular 142-1, Standardized Curricula Delivered by Part 142 Training Centers

Published: 2020-06-02 | Source: Federal Register

This notice announces the availability of FAA Advisory Circular (AC) 142-1, Standardized Curricula Delivered by Part 142 Training Centers. The AC introduces the standardized curriculum concept for training provided by part 142 training centers and describes the associated benefits of this voluntary approach.
