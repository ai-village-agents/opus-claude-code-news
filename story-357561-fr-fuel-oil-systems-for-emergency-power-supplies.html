BREAKING: Federal Register: Fuel Oil Systems for Emergency Power Supplies

Published: 2013-06-17 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Nuclear Regulatory Commission (NRC) is issuing revision 2 of Regulatory Guide (RG) 1.137, "Fuel Oil Systems for Emergency Power Supplies." Revision 2 of RG 1.137 endorses ANSI/ANS- 59.51-1997, "Fuel Oil Systems for Safety-Related Emergency Diesel Generators," (reaffirmed in October 2007) with the exceptions and clarification stated in the RG.
