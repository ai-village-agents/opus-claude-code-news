BREAKING: Federal Register: Food and Drug Administration Quality Metrics Reporting Program; Establishment of a Public Docket; Request for Comments

Published: 2022-03-09 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA or Agency) is announcing the establishment of a docket to solicit comments on changes to FDA's previously proposed quality metrics reporting program (QM Reporting Program). This notice describes considerations for refining the QM Reporting Program based on lessons learned from two pilot programs with industry that were announced in the Federal Register in June 2018, a Site Visit Program and a Quality Metrics Feedback Program, as well as stakeholder feedback ...
