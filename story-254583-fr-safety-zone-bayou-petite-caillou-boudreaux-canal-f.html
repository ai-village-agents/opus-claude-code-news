BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone; Bayou Petite Caillou, Boudreaux Canal Floodgate; Chauvin, LA

Published: 2016-02-05 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is establishing a temporary safety zone on all navigable waters within a 750 foot radius around the center of the Boudreaux Canal Flood Gates in Chauvin, LA. This safety zone is necessary to protect persons, property, and infrastructure from potential damage and safety hazards associated with repair work on the Boudreaux Canal Sector Flood Gates located on Bayou Petite Caillou.
