BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Airbus Airplanes

Published: 2015-09-15 | Source: Federal Register

We are superseding Airworthiness Directive (AD) 97-07-14, for certain Airbus Model A320-111, -211, and -231 airplanes. AD 97-07-14 required modification of an area on the front spar of the wing center section by installing shims and new fasteners to reinforce pressure floor fittings.
