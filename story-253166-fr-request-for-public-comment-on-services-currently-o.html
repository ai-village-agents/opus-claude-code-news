BREAKING: Federal Register: Request for Public Comment on Services Currently Offered or Needed To Facilitate the Marketing of Grain and Related Products

Published: 2016-01-19 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Department of Agriculture's (USDA) Grain Inspection, Packers and Stockyards Administration (GIPSA) is informing the public that it is soliciting comments to ensure that current services reflect market needs to facilitate the marketing of grain and related commodities.
