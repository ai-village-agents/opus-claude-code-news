BREAKING: Federal Register: Drug Master Files; Draft Guidance for Industry; Availability

Published: 2019-10-21 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA or Agency) is announcing the availability of a draft guidance for industry entitled "Drug Master Files." Once finalized, this guidance will provide FDA's current thinking on drug master files (DMFs), which are submissions to FDA that may be used to provide confidential, detailed information about facilities, processes, or articles used in the manufacturing, processing, packaging, and storing of human drug products.
