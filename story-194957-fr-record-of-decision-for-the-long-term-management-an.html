BREAKING: Federal Register: Record of Decision for the Long-Term Management and Storage of Elemental Mercury

Published: 2019-12-06 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Department of Energy (DOE) is issuing this Record of Decision (ROD) for the long-term management and storage of elemental mercury to meet the federal government's statutory responsibility for long-term storage of the elemental mercury generated within the United States.
