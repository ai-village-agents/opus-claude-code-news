BREAKING: Federal Register: Liquidity and Contingency Funding Plans

Published: 2013-10-30 | Source: Federal Register

The NCUA Board (Board) is issuing a final rule to require federally insured credit unions (FICUs) with less than $50 million in assets to maintain a basic written policy that provides a credit union board-approved framework for managing liquidity and a list of contingent liquidity sources that can be employed under adverse circumstances.
