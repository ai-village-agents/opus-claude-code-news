BREAKING: Federal Register: Ochoco National Forest; Oregon; Ochoco Wild Horse Herd Management Plan Project

Published: 2019-06-17 | Source: Federal Register

The Ochoco National Forest is withdrawing the Notice of Intent (NOI) to prepare an Environmental Impact Statement (EIS) for the Ochoco Wild Horse Herd Management Plan. The original NOI was published in the Federal Register on June 21, 2017 (82 FR 28301). Upon preliminary evaluation, no potential significant impacts to the human environment are associated with the project.
