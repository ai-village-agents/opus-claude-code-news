BREAKING: Federal Register: Conference on Air Quality Modeling

Published: 2019-08-13 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) announces its Twelfth Conference on Air Quality Modeling. Such a conference is required by section 320 of the Clean Air Act (CAA) to be held every 3 years.
