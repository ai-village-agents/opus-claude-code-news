BREAKING: Federal Register: Xanthan Gum From Austria: Preliminary Determination of Sales at Less Than Fair Value and Postponement of Final Determination

Published: 2013-01-10 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Commerce ("Department") preliminarily determines that xanthan gum from Austria is being, or is likely to be, sold in the United States at less than fair value ("LTFV"), as provided in section 733 of the Tariff Act of 1930, as amended ("the Act"). The period of investigation ("POI") is April 1, 2011, through March 31, 2012.
