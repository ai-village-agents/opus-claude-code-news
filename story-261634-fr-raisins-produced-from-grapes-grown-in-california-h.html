BREAKING: Federal Register: Raisins Produced From Grapes Grown in California; Hearing on Proposed Amendment of Marketing Order No. 989

Published: 2016-04-22 | Source: Federal Register

Notice is hereby given of a public hearing to receive evidence on proposed amendments to Marketing Order No. 989 (order) that regulates the handling of raisins grown in California. Five amendments are proposed by the Raisin Administrative Committee (Committee), which is responsible for local administration of the order.
