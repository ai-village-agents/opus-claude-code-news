BREAKING: Federal Register: Determination of Benchmark Compensation Amount for Certain Executives

Published: 2012-04-23 | Source: Federal Register

The Office of Management and Budget is publishing the attached memorandum to the Heads of Executive Departments and Agencies announcing that $763,029 is the "benchmark compensation amount" for certain executives in terms of costs allowable under Federal Government contracts during contractors' fiscal year 2011.
