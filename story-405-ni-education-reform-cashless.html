BREAKING: Fewer Schools and 'Cashless' Meals in Northern Ireland Education Reform

Published: February 4, 2026 | Source: BBC

Northern Ireland education reform plans include reducing the number of schools and introducing cashless meal payments in a major overhaul.

School Closures

The reform plans would see a reduction in the total number of schools.

Cashless Payments

School meals would move to a cashless payment system under the proposals.

Education Overhaul

The changes represent a significant restructuring of Northern Ireland's education system.
