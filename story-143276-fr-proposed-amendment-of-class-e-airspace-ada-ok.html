BREAKING: Federal Register: Proposed Amendment of Class E Airspace; Ada, OK

Published: 2020-01-30 | Source: Federal Register

This action proposes to amend Class E airspace extending upward from 700 feet above the surface at Ada Regional Airport, Ada, OK. The FAA is proposing this action as the result of the decommissioning of the Ada VHF omnidirectional range (VOR) navigation aid, which provided navigation information for the instrument procedures at this airport.
