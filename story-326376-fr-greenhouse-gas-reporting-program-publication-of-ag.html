BREAKING: Federal Register: Greenhouse Gas Reporting Program: Publication of Aggregated Greenhouse Gas Data

Published: 2014-06-09 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) intends to publish aggregated versions of certain data collected under the Greenhouse Gas Reporting Program, including totals, averages, and other aggregated versions of data submitted by individual reporters. The data that we intend to aggregate were determined in previous rulemakings to be confidential business information in their original, non-aggregated form.
