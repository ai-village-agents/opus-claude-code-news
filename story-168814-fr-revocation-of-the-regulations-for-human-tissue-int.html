BREAKING: Federal Register: Revocation of the Regulations for Human Tissue Intended for Transplantation and Human Dura Mater

Published: 2020-12-21 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA, the Agency, or we) is proposing to revoke the regulations for human tissue intended for transplantation and human dura mater recovered prior to May 25, 2005. The proposed revocation does not affect the regulations for human cells, tissues, and cellular and tissue-based products (HCT/Ps) recovered on or after May 25, 2005.
