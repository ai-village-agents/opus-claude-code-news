BREAKING: Federal Register: Privacy Act of 1974; System of Records

Published: 2022-05-31 | Source: Federal Register

In accordance with the Privacy Act of 1974, the DoD is modifying and reissuing a current system of records titled, "Defense Enrollment Eligibility Reporting Systems (DEERS)," DMDC 02 DoD. This system of records was originally established by the Defense Manpower Data Center to collect and maintain records that are used to manage the issuance of DoD identification cards, manage physical and logical access to DoD facilities and to provide a database for determining DoD entitlements and privileges. ...
