BREAKING: Federal Register: National Resource Centers Program and Foreign Language and Area Studies Fellowships Program

Published: 2024-08-27 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Department of Education (Department) amends the regulations that govern the National Resource Centers (NRC) Program, Assistance Listing Number 84.015A, and the Foreign Language and Area Studies (FLAS) Fellowships Program, Assistance Listing Number 84.015B. These regulations clarify interpretations of statutory language, redesign the selection criteria, and make necessary updates based upon program management experience.
