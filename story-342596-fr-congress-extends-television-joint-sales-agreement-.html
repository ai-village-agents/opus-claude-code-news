BREAKING: Federal Register: Congress Extends Television Joint Sales Agreement Compliance Deadline

Published: 2014-12-23 | Source: Federal Register

In this Public Notice, the Media Bureau announces that recently enacted legislation has extended the compliance deadline for parties to certain attributable television joint sales agreements (JSAs) to come into compliance with the Commission's broadcast ownership limits.
