BREAKING: Federal Register: The President's Commission on Election Administration (PCEA); Upcoming Public Advisory Meeting

Published: 2013-06-07 | Source: Federal Register

The President's Commission on Election Administration (PCEA), a Federal Advisory Committee established in accordance with the Federal Advisory Committee Act (FACA), 5 U.S.C., App., and Executive Order 13639, as amended by EO 13644, will hold a meeting open to the public on Friday, June 21, 2013.
