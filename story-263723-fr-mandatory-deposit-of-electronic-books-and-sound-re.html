BREAKING: Federal Register: Mandatory Deposit of Electronic Books and Sound Recordings Available Only Online

Published: 2016-05-17 | Source: Federal Register

In 2010, the U.S. Copyright Office, acting pursuant to section 407 of title 17 and following a public rulemaking process, adopted an interim rule governing mandatory deposit of electronic works that are not available in a physical format. The interim rule refers to such works as "electronic works published in the United States and available only online" (or "online-only works").
