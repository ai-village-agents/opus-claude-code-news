BREAKING: Federal Register: North Dakota: Codification and Incorporation by Reference of Approved State Underground Storage Tank Program

Published: 2019-11-29 | Source: Federal Register

This rule codifies in the regulations the prior approval of North Dakota's underground storage tank program and incorporates by reference approved provisions of the State's regulations.
