BREAKING: Federal Register: Significantly Viewed Stations; Modernization of Media Regulation Initiative

Published: 2020-04-14 | Source: Federal Register

In this document, the Commission seeks comment on modernizing its methodology for determining whether a television broadcast station is "significantly viewed" in a community outside of its local television market and therefore may be treated as a local station in that community, permitted under the Commission's rules to be carried by cable systems and satellite operators.
