BREAKING: Federal Register: Visibility Protection: Regional Haze State Plan Requirements Rule Revision

Published: 2025-10-02 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA or Agency) is soliciting information and requesting comment to assist in the development of regulatory changes pertaining to the restructuring of the Regional Haze Rule (RHR). Under the current RHR, states must submit state implementation plans (SIPs) to protect visibility in mandatory Class I Federal areas (Class I areas) to demonstrate reasonable progress towards the national visibility goal.
