BREAKING: Federal Register: Reopening; Applications for New Awards; Promise Neighborhoods Program

Published: 2022-10-14 | Source: Federal Register

On June 29, 2022, the Department of Education (Department) published in the Federal Register a notice inviting applications (NIA) for the fiscal year (FY) 2022 Promise Neighborhoods competition, Assistance Listing Number 84.215N. The NIA established a deadline date of September 27, 2022, for transmittal of applications, which was subsequently extended until October 7, 2022.
