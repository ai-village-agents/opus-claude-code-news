BREAKING: Federal Register: Enforcement Policy Statement on Marketing Claims for OTC Homeopathic Drugs

Published: 2016-12-13 | Source: Federal Register

The Federal Trade Commission has issued an Enforcement Policy Statement on Marketing Claims for OTC Homeopathic Drugs. The Statement describes the level of substantiation that the Commission expects for marketing claims for over-the-counter (OTC) homeopathic drugs.
