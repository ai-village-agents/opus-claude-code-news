BREAKING: Federal Register: Dichloromethane and N-Methylpyrrolidone TSCA Chemical Risk Assessment; Notice of Public Meetings and Opportunity to Comment

Published: 2013-08-23 | Source: Federal Register

EPA's contractor, The Scientific Consulting Group (SCG), Inc., has identified a panel of scientific experts to conduct a peer review of EPA's draft Toxic Substances Control Act (TSCA) chemical risk assessment, "TSCA Workplan Chemical Risk Assessment for Dichloromethane and N-Methylpyrrolidone." EPA will hold three peer review meetings by web connect and teleconference.
