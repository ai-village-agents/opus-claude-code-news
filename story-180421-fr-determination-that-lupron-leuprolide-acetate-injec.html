BREAKING: Federal Register: Determination That LUPRON (Leuprolide Acetate) Injection, 1 Milligram/0.2 Milliliter, Was Not Withdrawn From Sale for Reasons of Safety or Effectiveness

Published: 2019-05-30 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA or Agency) has determined that LUPRON (leuprolide acetate) injection, 1 milligram (mg)/0.2 milliliter (mL), was not withdrawn from sale for reasons of safety or effectiveness.
