BREAKING: Federal Register: General Schedule Locality Pay Areas

Published: 2018-12-07 | Source: Federal Register

On behalf of the President's Pay Agent, the Office of Personnel Management (OPM) is issuing final regulations to establish six new General Schedule locality pay areas, make certain changes to the definitions of existing locality pay areas, and make minor clarifying changes to the names of two locality pay areas.
