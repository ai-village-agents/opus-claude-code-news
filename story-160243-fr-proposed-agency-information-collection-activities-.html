BREAKING: Federal Register: Proposed Agency Information Collection Activities; Comment Request

Published: 2020-09-02 | Source: Federal Register

The Board of Governors of the Federal Reserve System (Board) invites comment on a proposal to extend for three years, with revision, the Reports of Deposits: Report of Transaction Accounts, Other Deposits, and Vault Cash; Annual Report of Deposits and Reservable Liabilities; Report of Foreign (Non-U.S.) Currency Deposits; and Allocation of Low Reserve Tranche and Reservable Liabilities Exemption (FR 2900, FR 2910a, FR 2915, FR 2930; OMB No. 7100-0087).
