BREAKING: Federal Register: Improving Continuity for Religious Organizations and Their Employees

Published: 2026-01-16 | Source: Federal Register

This interim final rule (IFR) amends U.S. Department of Homeland Security (DHS) regulations to remove the requirement that R-1 religious workers who have reached the maximum period of stay must reside abroad and be physically present outside the United States for one year before being eligible for readmission in R-1 status after departing from the United States upon reaching the maximum admission period.
