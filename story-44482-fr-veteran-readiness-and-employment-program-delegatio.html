BREAKING: Federal Register: Veteran Readiness and Employment Program: Delegation of Concurrence for Entitlement Extensions

Published: 2024-08-16 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Veterans Affairs (VA) is amending its regulations to authorize VA Regional Office (RO) Veteran Readiness and Employment Officers (VREO) to delegate their concurrence authority to extend a Veteran's entitlement to a rehabilitation program. The inability to delegate can delay the delivery of services if a VREO is unexpectedly out of the office for an extended period.
