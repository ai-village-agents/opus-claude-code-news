BREAKING: Federal Register: Federal Home Loan Bank Housing Goals Amendments

Published: 2018-11-02 | Source: Federal Register

The Federal Housing Finance Agency (FHFA) is proposing to amend the existing Federal Home Loan Bank Housing Goals regulation. FHFA proposes to replace the existing four separate retrospective housing goals with a single prospective mortgage purchase housing goal as well as establish a separate small member participation housing goal.
