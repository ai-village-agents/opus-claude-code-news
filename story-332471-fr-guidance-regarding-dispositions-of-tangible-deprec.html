BREAKING: Federal Register: Guidance Regarding Dispositions of Tangible Depreciable Property

Published: 2014-08-18 | Source: Federal Register

This document contains final regulations regarding dispositions of property subject to depreciation under section 168 of the Internal Revenue Code (Code) (Modified Accelerated Cost Recovery System (MACRS) property). The final regulations also amend the general asset account regulations and the accounting for MACRS property regulations.
