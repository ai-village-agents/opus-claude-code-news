BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Aerospace & Defense Oxygen Systems SaS (Part of Safran Aerosystems) (Formerly Known as Air Liquide)

Published: 2025-12-30 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA is superseding Airworthiness Directive (AD) 2025-25- 02, which applied to all aircraft equipped with certain Aerospace & Defense Oxygen Systems SaS portable breathing equipment (PBE). AD 2025- 25-02 required incorporating revised procedures for donning the PBE.
