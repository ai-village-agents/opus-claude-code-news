BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice and Request for Comment on Benefit-Cost Analysis Guidance for Rail Projects

Published: 2016-06-02 | Source: Federal Register

Section 11313(b) of the Fixing America's Surface Transportation (FAST) Act, Public Law 114-94 (December 4, 2015), requires the Secretary of Transportation (Secretary) to "enhance the usefulness of assessments of benefits and costs for intercity passenger rail and freight rail projects." As directed by Section 11313, FRA is issuing benefit-cost analysis (BCA) guidance (BCA Guidance) to fulfill these requirements and is requesting comments on the BCA Guidance.
