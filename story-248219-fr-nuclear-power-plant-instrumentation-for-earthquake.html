BREAKING: Federal Register: Nuclear Power Plant Instrumentation for Earthquakes

Published: 2017-11-08 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Nuclear Regulatory Commission (NRC) is issuing Revision 3 of Regulatory Guide (RG) 1.12, "Nuclear Power Plant Instrumentation for Earthquakes." This revision to the guide addresses new reactor plant configurations and the state of practice of seismic instrumentation since Revision 2 of RG 1.12 in 1997.
