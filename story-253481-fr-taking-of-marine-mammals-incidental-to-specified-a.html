BREAKING: Federal Register: Taking of Marine Mammals Incidental to Specified Activities; Coupeville Timber Towers Preservation Project

Published: 2016-01-21 | Source: Federal Register

NMFS has received a request from the Washington State Department of Transportation (WSDOT) for an authorization to take small numbers of 10 species of marine mammals, by Level B harassment, incidental to proposed construction activities for the Coupeville Timber Tower Preservation Project in Washington State.
