BREAKING: Federal Register: All Aboard America! Holdings, Inc.-Acquisition of Control-Lux Bus America Co.

Published: 2018-09-21 | Source: Federal Register

On August 14, 2018, All Aboard America! Holdings, Inc. (AAAHI), a noncarrier, filed an application under 49 U.S.C. 14303 for AAAHI to acquire a 100% equity interest in Lux Bus America Co. (Lux Bus), an interstate motor carrier of passengers. On August 23, 2018, AAAHI filed a supplement to its application.
