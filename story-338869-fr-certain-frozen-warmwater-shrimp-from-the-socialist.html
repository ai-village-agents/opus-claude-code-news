BREAKING: Federal Register: Certain Frozen Warmwater Shrimp From the Socialist Republic of Vietnam: Amended Final Results of Antidumping Duty Administrative Review, 2012-2013

Published: 2014-11-04 | Source: Federal Register

On September 24, 2014, the Department of Commerce ("Department") published in the Federal Register the Final Results of the eighth administrative review of the antidumping duty ("AD") Order \1\ on certain warmwater shrimp from the Socialist Republic of Vietnam ("Vietnam").\2\ The Department is amending the Final Results of the administrative review covering the period February 1, 2012 through January 31, 2013, to correct two ministerial errors. ---------------------------------------------------...
