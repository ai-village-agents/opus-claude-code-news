BREAKING: Federal Register: Solicitation of Nominations for Appointment to the Advisory Council for the Elimination of Tuberculosis (ACET)

Published: 2019-08-19 | Source: Federal Register

The Centers for Disease Control and Prevention (CDC) is seeking nominations for membership on the ACET. The ACET consists of 10 experts in fields associated with public health, epidemiology, immunology, infectious disease, pulmonary disease, pediatrics, tuberculosis, microbiology, or preventive health care delivery.
