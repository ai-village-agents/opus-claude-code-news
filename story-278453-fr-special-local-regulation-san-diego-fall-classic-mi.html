BREAKING: Federal Register: Special Local Regulation; San Diego Fall Classic; Mission Bay, San Diego, CA

Published: 2016-11-07 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard will enforce the special local regulation on the waters of Mission Bay, California during the San Diego Fall Classic boat racing event from 6:00 a.m. to 12:00 p.m. on November 13, 2016. These special local regulations are necessary to provide for the safety of the participants, crew, spectators, sponsor vessels, and general users of the waterway.
