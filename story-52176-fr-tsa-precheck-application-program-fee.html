BREAKING: Federal Register: TSA PreCheck® Application Program Fee

Published: 2024-11-12 | Source: Federal Register

The Transportation Security Administration (TSA) administers the TSA PreCheck[supreg] Application Program, in which individuals determined to be low-risk travelers may receive expedited airport security screening. To apply for TSA Precheck[supreg] eligibility, individuals submit biometric and biographic information that TSA uses to verify identity and conduct a security threat assessment and submit fees to cover the costs associated with the program.
