BREAKING: Federal Register: Guidance on Performing a Seismic Margin Assessment

Published: 2012-09-10 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Nuclear Regulatory Commission (NRC) is issuing for public comment the draft Japan Lessons-Learned Project Directorate Interim Staff Guidance (JLD-ISG), JLD-ISG-2012-04, "Guidance on Performing a Seismic Margin Assessment in response to the March 2012 Request for Information Letter."
