BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Inventory Completion: The University of Oregon Museum of Natural and Cultural History, Eugene, OR

Published: 2023-12-13 | Source: Federal Register

The University of Oregon Museum of Natural and Cultural History has completed an inventory of human remains and associated funerary objects, in consultation with the appropriate Indian Tribes or Native Hawaiian organizations, and has determined that there is a cultural affiliation between the human remains and associated funerary objects and present-day Indian Tribes or Native Hawaiian organizations.
