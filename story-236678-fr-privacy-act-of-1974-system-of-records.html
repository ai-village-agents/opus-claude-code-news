BREAKING: Federal Register: Privacy Act of 1974; System of Records

Published: 2017-06-15 | Source: Federal Register

The FTC is adopting in final form a new routine use that permits disclosure of the agency's Freedom of Information Act ("FOIA") request and appeal records to the Office of Government Information Services ("OGIS"), in order for OGIS to assist FOIA requesters in the processing and resolution of their requests and appeals.
