BREAKING: Federal Register: Native American Housing Assistance and Self-Determination Act; Revisions to the Indian Housing Block Grant Program Formula

Published: 2016-05-31 | Source: Federal Register

This proposed rule would revise the Indian Housing Block Grant (IHBG) Program allocation formula authorized by section 302 of the Native American Housing Assistance and Self-Determination Act of 1996, as amended (NAHASDA). Through the IHBG Program, HUD provides federal housing assistance for Indian tribes in a manner that recognizes the right of Indian self-determination and tribal self-government.
