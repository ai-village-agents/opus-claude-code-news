BREAKING: Federal Register: Scoping Meeting for Protective Regulations for Killer Whales in the Inland Waters of Washington State

Published: 2019-10-24 | Source: Federal Register

This notice informs the public of an upcoming scoping process, including a scoping meeting, to solicit public comments on whether, based on best available information, existing National Marine Fisheries Service (NMFS) regulations and other measures adequately protect killer whales from the impacts of vessels and noise in the inland waters of Washington State and, if not, what actions NMFS should take.
