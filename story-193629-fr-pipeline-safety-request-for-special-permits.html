BREAKING: Federal Register: Pipeline Safety: Request for Special Permits

Published: 2019-11-19 | Source: Federal Register

PHMSA is publishing this notice to solicit public comment on several requests for special permit from Williams --Transcontinental Gas Pipeline Company (Transco) and Williams--Northwest Pipeline Company (Northwest), seeking relief from compliance with certain requirements in the Federal Pipeline Safety Regulations. After the 30-day comment period, PHMSA will review the comments received from this notice as part of its evaluation to grant or deny the special permit requests.
