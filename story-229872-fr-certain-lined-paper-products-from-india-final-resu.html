BREAKING: Federal Register: Certain Lined Paper Products From India: Final Results of Antidumping Duty Administrative Review; 2014-2015

Published: 2017-03-17 | Source: Federal Register

On October 14, 2016, the Department of Commerce (the Department) published the Preliminary Results of the administrative review of the antidumping duty order on certain lined paper products (CLPP) from India. The period of review (POR) is September 1, 2014, through August 31, 2015. This review covers two mandatory respondents, Kokuyo Riddhi Paper Products Private Limited (Kokuyo Riddhi) and Navneet Education Limited (Navneet).
