BREAKING: Federal Register: Pipeline Safety: Class Location Change Requirements

Published: 2020-10-14 | Source: Federal Register

In response to public input received as part of the rulemaking process, PHMSA is proposing to revise the Federal Pipeline Safety Regulations to amend the requirements for gas transmission pipeline segments that experience a change in class location.
