BREAKING: Federal Register: Announcement of National Geospatial Advisory Committee Meeting

Published: 2016-05-25 | Source: Federal Register

The National Geospatial Advisory Committee (NGAC) will meet on June 14-15, 2016 at the U.S. Department of Transportation (DOT) Building, 1200 New Jersey Avenue SE., Washington, DC 20590. The meeting will be held in the DOT Conference Center, in the Oklahoma Room.
