BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Request for Information (RFI) Re: Photovoltaic Module Recycling

Published: 2014-10-21 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Energy (DOE) today gives notice of a request for information regarding the possible technical areas of research in the area of photovoltaic module recycling. DOE intends to understand the current state of recycling technology and the areas of research that could lead to impactful recycling technologies to support the developing photovoltaic industry.
