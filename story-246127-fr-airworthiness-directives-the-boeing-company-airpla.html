BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; The Boeing Company Airplanes

Published: 2017-10-12 | Source: Federal Register

We are adopting a new airworthiness directive (AD) for all The Boeing Company Model 737-100, -200, and -200C series airplanes. This AD was prompted by a report of incidents involving fatigue cracking in transport category airplanes that are approaching or have exceeded their design service objective and a structural reevaluation that was conducted by the manufacturer.
