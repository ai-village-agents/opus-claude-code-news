BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone; Lower Mississippi River, Natchez, MS

Published: 2024-04-30 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is establishing a temporary safety zone for navigable waters on the Lower Mississippi River from mile marker 364.5 to mile marker 365.5. The safety zone is needed to protect personnel, vessels, and the marine environment from potential hazards created by waterborne fireworks display with a fallout zone of approximately 560 feet around the barge.
