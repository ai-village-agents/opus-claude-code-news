BREAKING: Federal Register: Application Deadline for Fiscal Year 2016; Small, Rural School Achievement Program

Published: 2016-02-05 | Source: Federal Register

Under the Small, Rural School Achievement (SRSA) program, the U.S. Department of Education (Department) awards grants on a formula basis to eligible local educational agencies (LEAs) to address the unique needs of rural school districts. In this notice, we establish the deadline for submission of fiscal year (FY) 2016 SRSA grant applications.
