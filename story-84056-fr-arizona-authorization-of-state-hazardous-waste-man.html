BREAKING: Federal Register: Arizona: Authorization of State Hazardous Waste Management Program Revisions; Proposed Correction

Published: 2023-12-12 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) approved revisions to Arizona's federally authorized hazardous waste program by publishing proposed and direct final rules in the Federal Register on September 28, 2021. EPA inadvertently omitted a citation to Checklist 233 in the text of the proposed and direct final rule.
