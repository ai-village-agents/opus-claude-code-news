BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Austro Engine GmbH Engines

Published: 2024-03-05 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA is adopting a new airworthiness directive (AD) for certain Austro Engine GmbH Model E4 and E4P engines. This AD was prompted by reports of engine failures and the determination that certain batches of cap screws, installed on the inner main bearing positions of the engine, were manufactured at the lower end of the material strength tolerance.
