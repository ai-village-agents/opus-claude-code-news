BREAKING: Federal Register: Acceptance of Retrocession of Jurisdiction for the Skokomish Nation

Published: 2024-11-26 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of the Interior (Department) has accepted retrocession to the United States of partial criminal jurisdiction over the Skokomish Nation from the State of Washington.

Agency: Interior Department, Indian Affairs Bureau
