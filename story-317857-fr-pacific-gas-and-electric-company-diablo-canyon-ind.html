BREAKING: Federal Register: Pacific Gas and Electric Company; Diablo Canyon Independent Spent Fuel Storage Installation

Published: 2014-03-03 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Nuclear Regulatory Commission (NRC) is amending Materials License No. SNM-2511 for the Diablo Canyon (DC) independent spent fuel storage installation (ISFSI).
