BREAKING: Federal Register: Air Plan Approval; Wisconsin; 2017 Revisions to NR 400 and 406

Published: 2017-11-07 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is proposing to approve a revision to the Wisconsin State Implementation Plan (SIP) submitted by the Wisconsin Department of Natural Resources (WDNR) to EPA on May 16, 2017. The revision replaces the definition of "emergency electric generator" with a broader definition of "restricted internal combustion engine".
