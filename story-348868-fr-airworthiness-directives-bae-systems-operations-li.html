BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; BAE SYSTEMS (OPERATIONS) LIMITED Airplanes

Published: 2013-03-07 | Source: Federal Register

We are adopting a new airworthiness directive (AD) for all BAE SYSTEMS (OPERATIONS) LIMITED Model BAe 146 and Avro 146-RJ series airplanes. This AD was prompted by a report that certain ceramic terminal blocks, through which the wiring for the engine fire extinguishers, fire detection circuits, and engine and intake anti-ice system are routed, have been found to have moisture ingress, which can degrade the insulation resistance of the ceramic terminal blocks.
