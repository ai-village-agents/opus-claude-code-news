BREAKING: Federal Register: Determination That LOTENSIN (Benazepril Hydrochloride) Tablets, 5 Milligrams, Were Not Withdrawn From Sale for Reasons of Safety or Effectiveness

Published: 2023-02-01 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA, Agency, or we) has determined that LOTENSIN (benazepril hydrochloride) tablets, 5 milligrams (mg), were not withdrawn from sale for reasons of safety or effectiveness.
