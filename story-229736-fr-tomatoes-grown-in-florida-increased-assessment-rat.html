BREAKING: Federal Register: Tomatoes Grown in Florida; Increased Assessment Rate

Published: 2017-03-15 | Source: Federal Register

This rule implements a recommendation from the Florida Tomato Committee (Committee) for an increase in the assessment rate established for the 2016-17 and subsequent fiscal periods from $0.03 to $0.035 per 25-pound carton of tomatoes handled under the marketing order (order). The Committee locally administers the order and is comprised of producers of tomatoes operating within the area of production.
