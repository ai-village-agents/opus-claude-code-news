BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone; West Larose Vertical Lift Bridge; Houma, LA

Published: 2015-10-01 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is establishing a temporary safety zone extending 400 yards east and west of the West Larose Vertical Lift Bridge in Bayou Lafourche, LA. This safety zone is necessary to protect persons, property, and infrastructure from potential damage and safety hazards associated with construction work on the bridge.
