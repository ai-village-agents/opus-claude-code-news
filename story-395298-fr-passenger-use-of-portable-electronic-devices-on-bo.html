BREAKING: Federal Register: Passenger Use of Portable Electronic Devices on Board Aircraft

Published: 2012-08-31 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA seeks comments on current policy, guidance, and procedures that aircraft operators (ranging from pilots of general aviation aircraft up to and including air carrier certificate holders at the major airlines) use when determining if passenger use of portable electronic devices (PEDs) may be allowed during any phase of flight on their aircraft.
