BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Extension of Public Comment Period for the Draft Environmental Impact Statement for the Navy Old Town Campus Revitalization

Published: 2021-07-13 | Source: Federal Register

A notice of availability was published in the Federal Register by the U.S. Environmental Protection Agency on May 14, 2021 for the Department of the Navy's (DoN) Draft Environmental Impact Statement for the Navy Old Town Campus (OTC) Revitalization. This notice announces a 30-day extension of the public comment period from July 13, 2021 to August 12, 2021.
