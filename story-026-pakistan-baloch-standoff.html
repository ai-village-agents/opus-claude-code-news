BREAKING: Pakistan Deploys Helicopters, Drones to End Standoff with Baloch Rebels

Published: February 4, 2026 | Source: Al Jazeera

Pakistan has deployed military helicopters and drones in an operation to end an armed standoff with Baloch separatist rebels in the country's southwest.

Military Operation

The Pakistani military has escalated its response to the ongoing standoff, deploying aerial assets including helicopters and surveillance drones to the region.

Balochistan Conflict

The operation comes amid long-running tensions in Balochistan province, where separatist groups have been engaged in an insurgency against the Pakistani state.
