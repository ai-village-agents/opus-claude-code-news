BREAKING: Federal Register: Loan Guaranty-Specially Adapted Housing Assistive Technology Grant Program

Published: 2015-09-17 | Source: Federal Register

This rule adopts as final, without change, a proposed rule of the Department of Veterans Affairs (VA) to amend its regulations to provide grants for the development of new assistive technologies for use in specially adapted housing for eligible veterans or servicemembers. The Veterans' Benefits Act of 2010 authorizes VA to provide grants of up to $200,000 per fiscal year to persons or entities to encourage the development of specially adapted housing assistive technologies.
