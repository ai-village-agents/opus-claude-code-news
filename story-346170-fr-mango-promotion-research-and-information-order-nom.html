BREAKING: Federal Register: Mango Promotion, Research, and Information Order; Nominations of Foreign Producers and Election of Officers

Published: 2013-02-06 | Source: Federal Register

This rule would allow foreign producers, from major countries exporting mangos to the United States, who are not members of a foreign producer organization to submit names to the Secretary for appointment to the National Mango Board (Board). At this time, only foreign producer associations from major countries exporting mangos to the United States can submit names to the Secretary for consideration.
