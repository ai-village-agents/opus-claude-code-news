BREAKING: Federal Register: Kentucky Disaster #KY-00062

Published: 2016-09-08 | Source: Federal Register

This is a Notice of the Presidential declaration of a major disaster for Public Assistance Only for the State of Kentucky (FEMA- 4278-DR), dated 08/26/2016. Incident: Severe Storms, Tornadoes, Flooding, Landslides, and Mudslides. Incident Period: 07/02/2016 through 07/09/2016. Effective Date: 08/26/2016.
