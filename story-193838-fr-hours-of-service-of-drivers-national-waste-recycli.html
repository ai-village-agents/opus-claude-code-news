BREAKING: Federal Register: Hours of Service of Drivers: National Waste & Recycling Association; Application for Exemption

Published: 2019-11-21 | Source: Federal Register

FMCSA announces its decision to grant the National Waste & Recycling Association's (NWRA) request for exemption from the requirement that short-haul drivers utilizing the records of duty status (RODS) exception return to their normal work-reporting location within 12 hours after coming on duty.
