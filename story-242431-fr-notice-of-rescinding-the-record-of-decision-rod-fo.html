BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Rescinding the Record of Decision (ROD) for the State Route 704, Cross Base Highway Project in Pierce County, Washington

Published: 2017-08-29 | Source: Federal Register

The Federal Highway Administration is issuing this notice to advise the public that the Record of Decision for the proposed State Route 704 Cross Base Highway in Pierce County, Washington is being rescinded.

Document Type: Notice

Agency: Transportation Department, Federal Highway Administration
