BREAKING: Federal Register: Announcing Upcoming Virtual Meeting on Biofuel Greenhouse Gas Modeling

Published: 2021-12-28 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency announces an upcoming workshop on biofuel greenhouse gas (GHG) modeling. This is a virtual meeting and open to the public. The purpose of this workshop is to solicit information on the current scientific understanding of greenhouse gas modeling of land-based biofuels used in the transportation sector.
