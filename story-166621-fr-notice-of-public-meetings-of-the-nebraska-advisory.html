BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Public Meetings of the Nebraska Advisory Committee

Published: 2020-11-24 | Source: Federal Register

Notice is hereby given, pursuant to the provisions of the rules and regulations of the U.S. Commission on Civil Rights (Commission) and the Federal Advisory Committee Act that the Nebraska Advisory Committee (Committee) will hold a series of meetings via Webex on Monday, December 14th, Wednesday, December 16th, Thursday, January 7th at 9:00 a.m. Central Time for the purpose of gathering testimony on the Impact of Native American Mascot names in Nebraska.
