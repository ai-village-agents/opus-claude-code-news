BREAKING: Federal Register: Applications for New Awards; Centers of Excellence for Veteran Student Success Program; Correction

Published: 2020-10-23 | Source: Federal Register

On October 5, 2020, the Department of Education (Department) published in the Federal Register a notice inviting applications (NIA) for new awards for fiscal year (FY) 2020 for the Centers of Excellence for Veteran Student Success Program, Catalog of Federal Domestic Assistance (CFDA) number 84.116G. We are correcting the deadline for intergovernmental review. All other information in the NIA, including the November 4, 2020, deadline for transmittal of applications, remains the same.
