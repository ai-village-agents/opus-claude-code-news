BREAKING: Federal Register: JPSS Polar Satellite-Gap Mitigation-Request for Public Comment

In addition to reports from Inspector General and the Government Accountability Office on this topic, NOAA has also convened an Independent Review Team (IRT) to review the progress of our satellite programs and identify the challenges ahead.
