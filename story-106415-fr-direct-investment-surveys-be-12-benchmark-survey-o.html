BREAKING: Federal Register: Direct Investment Surveys: BE-12, Benchmark Survey of Foreign Direct Investment in the United States

Published: 2022-09-29 | Source: Federal Register

The final rule amends regulations of the Department of Commerce's Bureau of Economic Analysis (BEA) to set forth the reporting requirements for the 2022 BE-12, Benchmark Survey of Foreign Direct Investment in the United States. The BE-12 survey is conducted every five years; the prior survey covered 2017.
