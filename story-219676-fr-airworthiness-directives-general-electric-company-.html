BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; General Electric Company Turbofan Engines

Published: 2018-10-26 | Source: Federal Register

We are superseding Airworthiness Directive (AD) 2017-07-04 for General Electric Company (GE) GE90-110B1 and GE90-115B turbofan engines with certain high-pressure compressor (HPC) rotor stage 2-5 spools installed. AD 2017-07-04 required removing certain HPC rotor stage 2-5 spools from service at times determined by a drawdown plan.
