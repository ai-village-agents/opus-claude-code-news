BREAKING: Federal Register: Adequacy Status of the Knoxville, TN 1997 Annual PM2.5 Maintenance Plan Motor Vehicle Emission Budgets for Transportation Conformity Purposes

Published: 2017-03-10 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is notifying the public that it has found that the motor vehicle emissions budgets (MVEBs) contained in the State Implementation Plan (SIP) revision pertaining to the Knoxville, Tennessee 1997 Annual fine particulate matter (PM2.5) nonattainment area are adequate for transportation conformity purposes.
