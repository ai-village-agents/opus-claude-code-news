BREAKING: Federal Register: Tobacco Transition Program; Final Assessment Procedures

Published: 2013-08-02 | Source: Federal Register

This notice provides information about the final quarterly assessments for the Tobacco Transition Program (TTP). Through the Tobacco Transition Payment Program (TTPP), which is part of the TTP, eligible former tobacco quota holders and tobacco producers receive payments in 10 annual installments in fiscal years (FY) 2005 through 2014.
