BREAKING: Federal Register: Management of Federal Agency Disbursements

Published: 2020-05-01 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of the Treasury (Treasury), Bureau of the Fiscal Service (Fiscal Service or "we"), is adopting the changes proposed in its Notice of Proposed Rulemaking for its regulation that requires electronic delivery of all Federal payments aside from tax payments. The final rule eliminates obsolete references in the regulation, including references to the Electronic Transfer Account (ETA\SM\).
