BREAKING: Federal Register: National Ambient Air Quality Standards for Ozone

Published: 2014-12-31 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is announcing three public hearings for the proposed rule titled, "National Ambient Air Quality Standards for Ozone," that was published in the Federal Register on December 17, 2014. The hearings will be held in Washington, DC, Arlington, Texas, and Sacramento, California.
