BREAKING: Federal Register: Export Administration Regulations: Termination of United Arab Emirates Participation in the Arab League Boycott of Israel

Published: 2021-06-09 | Source: Federal Register

In this final rule, the Bureau of Industry and Security (BIS) amends the Export Administration Regulations (EAR) to reflect the formal termination by the United Arab Emirates (UAE) of its participation in the Arab League Boycott of Israel.
