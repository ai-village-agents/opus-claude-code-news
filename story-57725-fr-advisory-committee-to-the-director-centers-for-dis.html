BREAKING: Federal Register: Advisory Committee to the Director, Centers for Disease Control and Prevention

Published: 2023-01-11 | Source: Federal Register

In accordance with the Federal Advisory Committee Act, the CDC announces the following meeting for the Advisory Committee to the Director, Centers for Disease Control and Prevention (ACD, CDC). This meeting is open to the public. The meeting will be webcast live via the World Wide Web.
