BREAKING: Federal Register: Certain Dynamic Random Access Memory and NAND Flash Memory Devices and Products Containing Same; Commission Determination Not To Review Initial Determinations Terminating the Investigation as to All Remaining Respondents; Termination of the Investigation

Published: 2012-12-13 | Source: Federal Register

Notice is hereby given that the U.S. International Trade Commission has determined not to review initial determinations ("IDs") (Order Nos. 70, 71, and 72) of the presiding administrative law judge ("ALJ") terminating the above-captioned investigation as to all remaining respondents based on settlement and license agreements.
