BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Standard for Clothing Storage Units

Published: 2023-05-04 | Source: Federal Register

In November 2022, the U.S. Consumer Product Safety Commission published a consumer product safety standard for clothing storage units (CSUs) to protect children from tip-over-related death or injury, with an effective date of May 24, 2023.
