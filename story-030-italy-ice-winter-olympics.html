BREAKING: Italy Says ICE Agents Have No Operational Police Role in Winter Olympics

Published: February 4, 2026 | Source: Al Jazeera

Italy has clarified that US Immigration and Customs Enforcement (ICE) agents will have no operational police role at the upcoming Winter Olympics being hosted in the country.

Official Statement

Italian authorities have stated that any ICE presence at the Winter Olympics will be limited and will not involve operational policing duties.

International Context

The statement comes amid global debates over ICE operations and their international reach.
