BREAKING: Federal Register: Aluminum Extrusions From the People's Republic of China: Initiation of Anti-Circumvention and Scope Inquiries on the Antidumping Duty and Countervailing Duty Orders

Published: 2019-05-06 | Source: Federal Register

In response to a request from Air Master Awning LLC (Air Master), the Department of Commerce (Commerce) is initiating anti- circumvention inquiries to determine whether imports of aluminum jalousie shutters that are processed in the Dominican Republic from window frame extrusions produced in the People's Republic of China (China), are circumventing the antidumping duty (AD) and countervailing duty (CVD) orders on aluminum extrusions from China.
