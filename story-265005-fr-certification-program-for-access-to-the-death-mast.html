BREAKING: Federal Register: Certification Program for Access to the Death Master File

Published: 2016-06-01 | Source: Federal Register

The National Technical Information Service (NTIS) issues this final rule establishing a program through which persons may become eligible to obtain access to Death Master File (DMF) information about an individual within three years of that individual's death.
