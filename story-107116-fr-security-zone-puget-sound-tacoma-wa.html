BREAKING: Federal Register: Security Zone; Puget Sound, Tacoma, WA

Published: 2022-10-07 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is extending the effective period of a temporary, 500-yard radius, moving security zone for a vessel carrying Certain Dangerous Cargo (CDC) within Puget Sound. This temporary security zone is needed to protect the vessel, the CDC, and the surrounding waterway from terrorist acts, sabotage, or other subversive acts, accidents, or other events of a similar nature.
