BREAKING: Federal Register: Amendment of the Emergency Alert System

Published: 2016-05-06 | Source: Federal Register

In this document, the Federal Communications Commission (FCC or Commission) revises its rules governing the Emergency Alert System (EAS) to incorporate new multilingual alerting reporting requirements into its State EAS Plan reporting requirements.
