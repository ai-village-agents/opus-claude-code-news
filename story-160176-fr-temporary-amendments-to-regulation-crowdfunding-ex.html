BREAKING: Federal Register: Temporary Amendments to Regulation Crowdfunding; Extension

Published: 2020-09-02 | Source: Federal Register

The Securities and Exchange Commission ("Commission") is extending the effective date and applicability dates of our temporary final rules under Regulation Crowdfunding to facilitate capital formation for small businesses impacted by coronavirus disease 2019 (COVID-19).
