BREAKING: Federal Register: Submission for OMB Review; Home-Based Child Care Practices and Experiences Study (New Collection)

Published: 2023-05-12 | Source: Federal Register

The Administration for Children and Families (ACF) Office of Planning, Research, and Evaluation is proposing a new primary data collection to examine the experiences, strengths, resources, and strategies used by home-based child care providers to serve and support equitable outcomes for children and families.
