BREAKING: Federal Register: Special Local Regulation and Safety Zones; Marine Events in Captain of the Port Sector Long Island Sound Zone

Published: 2012-04-04 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard proposes to establish temporary special local regulations and safety zones for marine events on the navigable waters within the Captain of the Port (COTP) Sector Long Island Sound zone for regattas, fireworks displays and swim events. This action is necessary to provide for the safety of life on navigable waters during the events.
