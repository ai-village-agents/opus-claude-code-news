BREAKING: Federal Register: Accidental Release Reporting

Published: 2020-02-21 | Source: Federal Register

The enabling statute of the Chemical Safety and Hazard Investigation Board (CSB) provides that the CSB shall establish by regulation requirements binding on persons for reporting accidental releases into the ambient air subject to the Board's investigative jurisdiction. The final rule is intended to satisfy this statutory requirement.
