BREAKING: Federal Register: Challenge Competition: Using Technology to Prevent Childhood Obesity in Low-Income Families and Communities

Published: 2017-09-26 | Source: Federal Register

The Health Resources and Services Administration's (HRSA's) Maternal and Child Health Bureau (MCHB) announces a prize competition to support the development of low-cost, scalable technology-based innovations to promote healthy weight for low-income children and families in the socio-cultural and environmental contexts of their communities.
