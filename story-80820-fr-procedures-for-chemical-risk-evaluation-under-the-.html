BREAKING: Federal Register: Procedures for Chemical Risk Evaluation Under the Toxic Substances Control Act (TSCA)

Published: 2023-10-30 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA, "the Agency") is proposing to amend the procedural framework rule for conducting risk evaluations under the Toxic Substances Control Act (TSCA).
