BREAKING: Federal Register: Assistance to Foreign Atomic Energy Activities

Published: 2019-11-04 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Department of Energy ("DOE") is extending the public comment period for its proposed rule establishing procedures for the imposition of civil penalties for violations of certain provisions of the Atomic Energy Act of 1954 (AEA). The notice of proposed rulemaking (NOPR) provided for a public comment period ending November 4, 2019.
