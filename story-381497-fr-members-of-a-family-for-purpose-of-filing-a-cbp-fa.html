BREAKING: Federal Register: Members of a Family for Purpose of Filing a CBP Family Declaration

Published: 2012-03-27 | Source: Federal Register

U.S. Customs and Border Protection (CBP) is proposing to revise its regulations regarding U.S. returning residents who are eligible to file a single customs declaration for members of a family traveling together upon arrival in the United States. Specifically, CBP is proposing to expand the definition of the term "members of a family residing in one household" to allow more U.S. returning residents to file a family customs declaration for articles acquired abroad.
