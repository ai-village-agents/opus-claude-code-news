BREAKING: Federal Register: Idaho: Final Authorization of State Hazardous Waste Management Program; Revision

Published: 2012-06-11 | Source: Federal Register

Idaho applied to EPA for final authorization of certain changes to its hazardous waste program under the Resource Conservation and Recovery Act, as amended (RCRA). On February 29, 2012, EPA published a proposed rule to authorize the changes and opened a public comment period under Docket ID No. EPA-R10-RCRA-2011-0973.
