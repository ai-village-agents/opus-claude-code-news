BREAKING: Federal Register: Sanitary Transportation of Human and Animal Food; Public Meetings on Proposed Rule

Published: 2014-02-14 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA or we) is announcing three public meetings to discuss the proposed rule that would establish requirements for shippers, carriers by motor vehicle and rail vehicle, and receivers engaged in the transportation of food, including food for animals, to use sanitary transportation practices to help ensure the safety of the food they transport.
