BREAKING: Federal Register: Government Securities: Call for Large Position Reports

Published: 2023-07-14 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Department of the Treasury (Treasury) called for the submission of Large Position Reports by entities whose positions in the Treasury Bill of June 8, 2023 equaled or exceeded $10.2 billion as of Friday, April 28, 2023, or Friday, May 5, 2023. This Bill has CUSIP 912796ZP7 and was originally auctioned as a 182-Day (i.e., 26-Week) Bill on December 5, 2022, and issued on December 8, 2022, with a maturity date of June 8, 2023.
