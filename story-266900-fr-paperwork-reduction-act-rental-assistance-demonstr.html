BREAKING: Federal Register: Paperwork Reduction Act-Rental Assistance Demonstration (RAD) Documents

Published: 2016-06-23 | Source: Federal Register

The Rental Assistance Demonstration allows Public Housing, Moderate Rehabilitation (Mod Rehab), Rent Supplement (Rent Supp), and Rental Assistance Payment (RAP) properties to convert to long-term project-based Section 8 rental assistance contracts. The documents that subject to this notice are those used to process and complete the conversion process for Public Housing, Mod Rehab, Rent Supp, and RAP properties.
