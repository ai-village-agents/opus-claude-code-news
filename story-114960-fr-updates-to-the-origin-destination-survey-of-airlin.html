BREAKING: Federal Register: Updates to the Origin-Destination Survey of Airline Passengers

Published: 2021-01-19 | Source: Federal Register

The Department is proposing to update the method of collecting and processing aviation traffic data in the Origin-Destination Survey of Airline Passenger Traffic (O&D Survey), as well as to expand the number of reporting air carriers, the sample size collected, and the scope of the data reported.
