BREAKING: Federal Register: WRC-07 Implementation Report and Order and WRC-12 Order

Published: 2015-07-07 | Source: Federal Register

In this document, the Commission implemented allocation changes from the World Radiocommunication Conference (Geneva, 2007) (WRC-07) and updated related service rules. The Commission took this action in order to conform its rules, to the extent practical, to the decisions that the international community made at WRC-07.
