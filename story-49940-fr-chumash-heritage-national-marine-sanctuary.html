BREAKING: Federal Register: Chumash Heritage National Marine Sanctuary

Published: 2024-10-16 | Source: Federal Register

NOAA is designating Chumash Heritage National Marine Sanctuary (CHNMS) in the waters along and offshore of the coast of central California to recognize the national significance of the area's ecological, historical, archaeological, and cultural resources and to manage this special place as part of the National Marine Sanctuary System.
