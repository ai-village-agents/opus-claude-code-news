BREAKING: Federal Register: Northeast Corridor Safety Committee; Notice of Meeting

Published: 2012-01-23 | Source: Federal Register

FRA announced the first meeting of the Northeast Corridor Safety Committee, a Federal Advisory Committee mandated by Section 212 of the Passenger Rail Investment and Improvement Act of 2008 (PRIIA), on June 6, 2011 (See 76 FR 32391). This initial meeting was postponed until further notice (See 76 FR 34139) and is now rescheduled.
