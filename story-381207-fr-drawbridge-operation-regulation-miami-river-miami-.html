BREAKING: Federal Register: Drawbridge Operation Regulation; Miami River, Miami, FL

Published: 2012-03-23 | Source: Federal Register

The Commander, Seventh Coast Guard District, has issued a temporary deviation from the regulation governing the operation of the N.W. 12th Avenue Bridge across the Miami River, mile 2.1, in Miami, Florida. This deviation will test a change to the drawbridge operation schedule to determine whether a permanent change to the schedule is needed.
