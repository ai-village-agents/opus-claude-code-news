BREAKING: Federal Register: Proposal Regarding Labeling Wines Containing Added Distilled Spirits

Published: 2022-06-13 | Source: Federal Register

The Alcohol and Tobacco Tax and Trade Bureau (TTB) proposes to amend its wine labeling and advertising regulations to remove an existing regulatory prohibition against statements which indicate that a wine contains distilled spirits.
