BREAKING: Federal Register: Innovations in Medical Evidence Development and Surveillance-Methods Research Agenda

Published: 2015-04-15 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA) is announcing the availability of grant funds for the support of Center for Drug Evaluation and Research (CDER). The goal of the CDER is to support the development of appropriate methodologies to conduct medical product safety surveillance in large electronic databases.
