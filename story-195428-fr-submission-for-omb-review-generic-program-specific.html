BREAKING: Federal Register: Submission for OMB Review; Generic Program-Specific Performance Progress Report (0970-0490)

Published: 2019-12-13 | Source: Federal Register

This Notice describes the proposal to extend data collection under the Administration for Children and Families (ACF) Generic Program-Specific Performance Progress Report (PPR) (0970-0490). This overarching generic allows ACF program offices to collect performance and progress data from recipients and sub-recipients who receive funding from ACF under a discretionary grant or cooperative agreement.
