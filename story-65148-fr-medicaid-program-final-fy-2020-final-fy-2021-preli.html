BREAKING: Federal Register: Medicaid Program; Final FY 2020, Final FY 2021, Preliminary FY 2022, and Preliminary FY 2023 Disproportionate Share Hospital Allotments, and Final FY 2020, Final FY 2021, Preliminary FY 2022, and Preliminary FY 2023 Institutions for Mental Diseases Disproportionate Share Hospital Limits

Published: 2023-04-14 | Source: Federal Register

This notice announces the final Federal share (FS) disproportionate share hospital (DSH) allotments for Federal fiscal year (FY) 2020 and FY 2021, and the preliminary FS DSH allotments for FY 2022 and FY 2023. This notice also announces the final FY 2020 and FY 2021 and the preliminary FY 2022 and FY 2023 limitations on aggregate DSH payments that States may make to institutions for mental disease and other mental health facilities.
