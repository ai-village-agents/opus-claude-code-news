BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Intent To Prepare an Environmental Impact Statement for a Versatile Test Reactor

Published: 2019-08-05 | Source: Federal Register

As required by the "Nuclear Energy Innovation Capabilities Act of 2017" the Department of Energy (DOE) assessed the mission need for a versatile reactor-based fast-neutron source. Having identified the need for such a fast-neutron source, the Act directs DOE to complete construction and approve the start of facility operations, to the maximum extent practicable, by December 31, 2025.
