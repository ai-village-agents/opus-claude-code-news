BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone; Columbia River, Sand Island, WA

Published: 2016-09-22 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is establishing a temporary safety zone for the navigable waters of the Columbia River within a 500-yard radius of the small boat "Nessy," while in the area of Sand Island, near Chinook, WA, and all involved associated vessels in support of the Double-Crested Cormorant removal operations conducted by the U.S. Army Corps of Engineers and U.S. Department of Agriculture Wildlife Services.
