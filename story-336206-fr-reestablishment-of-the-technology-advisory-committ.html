BREAKING: Federal Register: Reestablishment of the Technology Advisory Committee

Published: 2014-10-01 | Source: Federal Register

The Commodity Futures Trading Commission (Commission) is publishing this notice to announce the reestablishment of the Technology Advisory Committee (TAC). The Commission has determined that reestablishment of the TAC is necessary and in the public's interest.
