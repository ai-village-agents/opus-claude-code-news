BREAKING: Federal Register: Recognition of Tribal Organizations for Representation of VA Claimants

Published: 2016-03-10 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Veterans Affairs (VA) is considering issuing a proposed rulemaking to amend its regulations concerning recognition of certain national, State, and regional or local organizations for purposes of VA claims representation.
