BREAKING: Federal Register: Addition of Certain Persons and Revisions to Entries on the Entity List; and Removal of a Person From the Entity List

Published: 2017-01-10 | Source: Federal Register

This rule amends the Export Administration Regulations (EAR) by adding five persons to the Entity List. The five persons who are added to the Entity List have been determined by the U.S. Government to be acting contrary to the national security or foreign policy interests of the United States. These five persons will be listed on the Entity List under the destination of Turkey.
