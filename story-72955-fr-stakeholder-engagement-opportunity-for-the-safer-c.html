BREAKING: Federal Register: Stakeholder Engagement Opportunity for the Safer Choice and Design for the Environment (DfE) Programs' Potential Expansion Into New Product Categories

Published: 2023-07-27 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is announcing an opportunity for public input on the Safer Choice and Design for the Environment (DfE) programs' potential expansion of their certification to new product categories. Safer Choice helps consumers, businesses, and purchasers find products that perform and contain ingredients that are safer for human health and the environment.
