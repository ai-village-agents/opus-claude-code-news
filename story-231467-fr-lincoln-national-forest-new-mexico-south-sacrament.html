BREAKING: Federal Register: Lincoln National Forest; New Mexico; South Sacramento Restoration Project

Published: 2017-04-07 | Source: Federal Register

The Lincoln National Forest will prepare an Environmental Impact Statement (EIS) to document and publicly disclose environmental effects of its management strategy for restoring forest health on approximately 140,000 acres in the southern Sacramento Mountains of New Mexico. The restoration strategy would include a variety of management tools including mechanical methods and prescribed fire to achieve forest health and fuel reduction goals.
