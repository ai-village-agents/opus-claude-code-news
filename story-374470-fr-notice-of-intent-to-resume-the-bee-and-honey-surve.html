BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Intent To Resume the Bee and Honey Surveys and All Associated Reports

Published: 2012-01-09 | Source: Federal Register

This notice announces the intention of the National Agricultural Statistics Service (NASS) to resume a currently approved information collection for bee and honey data and all associated reports.
