BREAKING: Federal Register: Air Plan Approval; Indiana; Emissions Reporting Rule

Published: 2021-06-16 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is approving a revision to the Indiana State Implementation Plan (SIP) submitted on July 16, 2020, by the Indiana Department of Environmental Management (IDEM). The revision incorporates changes to Indiana's existing emission reporting rule to be consistent with the emissions statement requirements in the Clean Air Act (CAA).
