BREAKING: Federal Register: Worker Walkaround Representative Designation Process

Published: 2023-10-16 | Source: Federal Register

On August 30, 2023, OSHA published a notice of proposed rulemaking (NPRM) titled "Worker Walkaround Representative Designation Process." The period for submitting public comments is being extended by two weeks to allow stakeholders additional time to comment.
