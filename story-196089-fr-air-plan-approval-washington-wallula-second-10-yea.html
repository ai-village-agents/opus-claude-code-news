BREAKING: Federal Register: Air Plan Approval; Washington; Wallula Second 10-Year Maintenance Plan

Published: 2019-12-20 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is proposing to approve a plan for the Wallula area in Washington State that addresses the second 10-year maintenance period for particulate matter with an aerodynamic diameter less than or equal to a nominal 10 micrometers (PM10). This plan relies upon the control measures contained in the first 10-year maintenance plan, with revisions to reflect updated permits and agreements, also proposed for approval in this action.
