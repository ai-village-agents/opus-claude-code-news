BREAKING: Federal Register: Proposed Amendment VOR Federal Airway V-487; Eastern New York and Northern Vermont

Published: 2021-03-05 | Source: Federal Register

This action proposes to amend VOR Federal airway V-487 in the vicinity of Glens Falls, NY, and Burlington, VT. The proposed change would substitute a radial from the Burlington, VT, VOR/DME (BTV), in place of the current Glens Falls, NY, VOR/DME (GFL) radial, for defining a navigation fix along the route.
