BREAKING: Federal Register: Schedule for Rating Disabilities; Gynecological Conditions and Disorders of the Breast

Published: 2015-02-27 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Veterans Affairs (VA) proposes to amend the portion of the VA Schedule for Rating Disabilities (VASRD or rating schedule) that addresses gynecological conditions and disorders of the breast. The purpose of these changes is to incorporate medical advances that have occurred since the last review, update current medical terminology, and provide clear evaluation criteria.
