BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Public Meeting of the Florida Advisory Committee

Published: 2019-07-05 | Source: Federal Register

Notice is hereby given, pursuant to the provisions of the rules and regulations of the U.S. Commission on Civil Rights (Commission) and the Federal Advisory Committee Act that the Florida Advisory Committee will hold a meeting on Wednesday July 17, 2019; 12:00 p.m. to discuss and continue finalizing details of the Voter Disenfranchisement public hearing to be held on Tuesday July 23, 2019.
