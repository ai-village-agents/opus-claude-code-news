BREAKING: Federal Register: Ryan White HIV/AIDS Program Part F; AIDS Education and Training Centers National Coordinating Resource Center

Published: 2020-04-06 | Source: Federal Register

HRSA's HIV/AIDS Bureau will award $200,000 in supplemental funding to Rutgers, the State University of New Jersey, Biomedical and Health Sciences (Rutgers), to support the Ryan White HIV/AIDS Program Part F AIDS Education and Training Centers' (AETC) National Coordinating Resource Center (NCRC) project in Fiscal Year (FY) 2020.
