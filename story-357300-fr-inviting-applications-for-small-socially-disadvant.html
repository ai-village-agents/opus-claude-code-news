BREAKING: Federal Register: Inviting Applications for Small Socially-Disadvantaged Producer Grants

Published: 2013-06-12 | Source: Federal Register

The Rural Business--Cooperative Service announces the availability of $2,855,222 in competitive grant funds for the FY 2013 Small Socially-Disadvantaged Producer Grants (SSDPG) program as authorized by H.R. 933. We are requesting proposals from applicants that will provide technical assistance to small, socially-disadvantaged agricultural producers in rural areas.
