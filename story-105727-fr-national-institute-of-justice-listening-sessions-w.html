BREAKING: Federal Register: National Institute of Justice Listening Sessions With Stakeholder Organizations

Published: 2022-09-21 | Source: Federal Register

The National Institute of Justice (NIJ) plans to host a series of listening sessions with stakeholder organizations. The purpose of the listening sessions is for the NIJ Director to (1) to gather fact and information from stakeholder organizations about current challenges in the criminal justice and juvenile justice ecosystems that research could address and (2) to explain NIJ's priorities in regard to those challenges.
