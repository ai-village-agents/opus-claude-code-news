BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Leonardo S.p.A. Helicopters

Published: 2020-07-30 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA is adopting a new airworthiness directive (AD) for certain Leonardo S.p.A. (Leonardo) Model AB139, AW139, AW169, and AW189 helicopters. This AD was prompted by reports of uncommanded deployment of the emergency flotation system (EFS) due to improper accomplishment of the reset procedure of the shape memory alloy (SMA) inflation system actuation device.
