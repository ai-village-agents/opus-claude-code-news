BREAKING: Federal Register: 2020 Decennial Census Residence Rule and Residence Situations

Published: 2015-05-20 | Source: Federal Register

The Bureau of the Census (U.S. Census Bureau) requests public comment on the 2010 Census Residence Rule and Residence Situations. The Residence Rule is applied to living situations to determine where people should be counted during the decennial Census. Specific Residence Situations have been included with the Residence Rule to illustrate how the Rule is applied.
