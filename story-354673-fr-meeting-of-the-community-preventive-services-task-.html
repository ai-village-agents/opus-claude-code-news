BREAKING: Federal Register: Meeting of the Community Preventive Services Task Force (Task Force)

Published: 2013-05-13 | Source: Federal Register

The Centers for Disease Control and Prevention (CDC) announces the next meeting of the Community Preventive Services Task Force (Task Force). The Task Force is independent and nonfederal. Its members are nationally known leaders in public health practice, policy, and research, and are appointed by the CDC Director.
