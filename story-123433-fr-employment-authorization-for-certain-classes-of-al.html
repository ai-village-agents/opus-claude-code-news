BREAKING: Federal Register: Employment Authorization for Certain Classes of Aliens With Final Orders of Removal; Withdrawal

Published: 2021-05-10 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Department of Homeland Security (DHS) is withdrawing a notice of proposed rulemaking (NPRM) that published on November 19, 2020. The NPRM proposed to revise DHS regulations governing employment authorization for individuals who have a final order of removal and are released from DHS custody on an order of supervision.
