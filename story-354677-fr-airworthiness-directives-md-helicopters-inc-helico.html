BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; MD Helicopters Inc. Helicopters

Published: 2013-05-13 | Source: Federal Register

(MDHI) Model 369A, 369D, 369E, 369H, 369HE, 369HM, 369HS, 369F and 369FF helicopters with certain MDHI or Helicopter Technology Company (HTC) tail rotor blades installed.
