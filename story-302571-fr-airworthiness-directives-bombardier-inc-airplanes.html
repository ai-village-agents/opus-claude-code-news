BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Bombardier, Inc. Airplanes

Published: 2015-08-25 | Source: Federal Register

We are adopting a new airworthiness directive (AD) for certain Bombardier, Inc. Model BD-700-1A10 and BD-700-1A11 airplanes. This AD was prompted by a report of several events where pilots experienced difficulty in lateral control of the airplane after doing a climb through heavy rain conditions and a determination that the cause was water ingress in the aileron control pulley assembly.
