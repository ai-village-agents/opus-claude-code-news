BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; EVECTOR, spol. s.r.o. Gliders

Published: 2016-03-04 | Source: Federal Register

Models L 13 SEH VIVAT and L 13 SDM VIVAT gliders (type certificate previously held by AEROTECHNIK s.r.o.) that would supersede AD 2000-20-12. This proposed AD results from mandatory continuing airworthiness information (MCAI) originated by an aviation authority of another country to identify and correct an unsafe condition on an aviation product.
