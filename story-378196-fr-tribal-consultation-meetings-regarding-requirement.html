BREAKING: Federal Register: Tribal Consultation Meetings Regarding Requirements Applicable to Title IV-B Child and Family Services Plan

Published: 2012-02-21 | Source: Federal Register

The title IV-B regulations regarding the title IV-B plan and fiscal requirements are outdated due to statutory changes over the last 15 years. The Children's Bureau (CB) is deciding whether to revise the regulations accordingly. Per the ACF Tribal Consultation Policy (76 FR 55678, published September 8, 2011), we request comments from Indian Tribes that operate a title IV-B, subpart 1 and/or title IV-B, subpart 2 program and any other interested party.
