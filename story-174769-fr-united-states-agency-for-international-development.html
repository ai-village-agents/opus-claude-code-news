BREAKING: Federal Register: United States Agency for International Development Acquisition Regulation (AIDAR): Security and Information Technology Requirements

Published: 2019-03-21 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Agency for International Development (USAID) seeks public comment on a proposed rule that would amend the USAID Acquisition Regulation (AIDAR) to incorporate a revised definition of information technology and other requirements relating to information security and information technology approvals.
