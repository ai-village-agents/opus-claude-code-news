BREAKING: Federal Register: National Institute on Disability and Rehabilitation Research; Long-Range Plan for Fiscal Years 2013-2017

Published: 2013-04-04 | Source: Federal Register

The Assistant Secretary for Special Education and Rehabilitative Services announces the publication of the final Long- Range Plan for Fiscal Years (FY) 2013-2017 (Plan) for the National Institute on Disability and Rehabilitation Services (NIDRR). This Plan provides an overview of NIDRR's goals and objectives, identifies contributions that NIDRR research has made to improve the lives of individuals with disabilities, and presents NIDRR's specific goals and objectives for the next five years.
