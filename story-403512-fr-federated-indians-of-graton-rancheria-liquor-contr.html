BREAKING: Federal Register: Federated Indians of Graton Rancheria-Liquor Control Statute

Published: 2012-12-12 | Source: Federal Register

This notice publishes the Federated Indians of Graton Rancheria--Liquor Control Statute (Ordinance). The Ordinance regulates and controls the sale, consumption and possession of liquor within the Graton Rancheria's Indian country.
