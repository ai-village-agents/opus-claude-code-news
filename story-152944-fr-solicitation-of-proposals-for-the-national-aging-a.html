BREAKING: Federal Register: Solicitation of Proposals for the National Aging and Disability Transportation Center

Published: 2020-06-02 | Source: Federal Register

The Federal Transit Administration (FTA) is soliciting proposals under FTA's Technical Assistance and Workforce Development Program, to select an entity to administer the National Aging and Disability Transportation Center (NADTC).
