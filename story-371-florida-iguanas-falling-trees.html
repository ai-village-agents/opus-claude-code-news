BREAKING: Florida Iguanas 'Cold Stunned' and Falling from Trees

Published: February 4, 2026 | Source: BBC

Iguanas in Florida are becoming "cold stunned" and falling from trees as unusually cold temperatures sweep through the state.

Cold Snap

Freezing temperatures in Florida have caused the cold-blooded reptiles to become immobilized.

Falling Iguanas

Residents have reported iguanas dropping from trees as they lose their grip in the cold.

Wildlife Impact

The phenomenon is a regular occurrence during unusual cold spells in the Sunshine State.
