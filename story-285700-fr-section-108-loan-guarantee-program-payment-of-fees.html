BREAKING: Federal Register: Section 108 Loan Guarantee Program: Payment of Fees To Cover Credit Subsidy Costs

Published: 2015-02-05 | Source: Federal Register

This proposed rule would amend HUD's Section 108 Loan Guarantee Program (Section 108 Program) regulations to permit HUD, in accordance with statutory authority, to collect fees from Section 108 borrowers to offset the costs of Section 108 loan guarantees. HUD is proposing this rule to ensure that it can begin to make Section 108 loan guarantee commitments without appropriated subsidy.
