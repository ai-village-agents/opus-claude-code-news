BREAKING: Federal Register: Privacy Act of 1974; System of Records

Published: 2013-11-04 | Source: Federal Register

The Defense Logistics Agency proposes to add a new system of records in its inventory of record systems subject to the Privacy Act of 1974 (5 U.S.C. 552a), as amended. This notice adds S890.11, Defense Agencies Initiative (DAI) Civilian Time and Labor Records.
