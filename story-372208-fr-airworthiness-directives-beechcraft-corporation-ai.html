BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Beechcraft Corporation Airplanes

Published: 2013-12-09 | Source: Federal Register

We are adopting a new airworthiness directive (AD) for certain Beechcraft Corporation (type certificate previously held by Hawker Beechcraft Corporation) Models 1900, 1900C, and 1900D airplanes. This AD was prompted by reports of cracking in the front spar cap angles and hat section structure of the vertical stabilizer.
