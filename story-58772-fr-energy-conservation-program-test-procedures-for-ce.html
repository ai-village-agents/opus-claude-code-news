BREAKING: Federal Register: Energy Conservation Program: Test Procedures for Central Air Conditioners and Heat Pumps

Published: 2023-01-24 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Department of Energy ("DOE") is undertaking the preliminary stages of a rulemaking to consider amendments to the test procedure for central air conditioners and heat pumps.
