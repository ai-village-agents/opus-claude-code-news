BREAKING: Federal Register: Revisions to the California State Implementation Plan, Mojave Desert Air Quality Management District

Published: 2012-10-25 | Source: Federal Register

EPA is finalizing a limited approval and limited disapproval of revisions to the Mojave Desert Air Quality Management District (MDAQMD) portion of the California State Implementation Plan (SIP). This action was proposed in the Federal Register on February 28, 2012 and concerns oxides of nitrogen (NOX) emissions from stationary gas turbines.
