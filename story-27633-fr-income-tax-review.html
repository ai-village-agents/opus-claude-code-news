BREAKING: Federal Register: Income Tax Review

Published: 2024-01-19 | Source: Federal Register

The Commission is recognizing a recent Postal Service filing concerning the calculation of the assumed Federal income tax on competitive products income for Fiscal Year 2023. This notice informs the public of the filing, invites public comment, and takes other administrative steps.
