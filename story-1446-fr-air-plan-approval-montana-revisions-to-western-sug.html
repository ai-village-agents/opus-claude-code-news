BREAKING: Federal Register: Air Plan Approval; Montana; Revisions to Western Sugar Stipulation

Published: 2026-01-23 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is proposing to approve revisions to the Montana State Implementation Plan (SIP). These revisions specifically address sulfur dioxide (SO2) emission limits and associated requirements related to the Western Sugar Cooperative facility in Billings, Montana. The EPA is taking this action pursuant to the Clean Air Act (CAA).
