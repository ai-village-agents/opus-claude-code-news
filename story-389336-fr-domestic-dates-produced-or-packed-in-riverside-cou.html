BREAKING: Federal Register: Domestic Dates Produced or Packed in Riverside County, CA; Order Amending Marketing Order 987

Published: 2012-06-25 | Source: Federal Register

This final rule amends Marketing Agreement and Order No. 987 (order), which regulates the handling of domestic dates produced or packed in Riverside County, California. The amendments approved by producers in referendum were proposed by the California Date Administrative Committee (CDAC or committee), which is responsible for local administration of the order.
