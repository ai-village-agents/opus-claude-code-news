BREAKING: Federal Register: Special Local Regulation; California Half Ironman Triathlon, Oceanside, CA

Published: 2020-03-10 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard will enforce the California Half Ironman Triathlon special local regulation on the waters offshore Oceanside and within Oceanside Harbor, California on April 4, 2020. These special local regulations are necessary to provide for the safety of the participants, crew, spectators, sponsor vessels of the triathlon, and general users of the waterway.
