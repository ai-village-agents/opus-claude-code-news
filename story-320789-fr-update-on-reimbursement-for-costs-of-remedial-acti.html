BREAKING: Federal Register: Update on Reimbursement for Costs of Remedial Action at Uranium and Thorium Processing Sites

Published: 2014-04-04 | Source: Federal Register

In light of the passage of the Consolidated Appropriations Act, 2014 (Pub. L. 113-76), funds were not made available in FY 2014 to the Department of Energy (DOE) for reimbursement for cleanup work performed by licensees at eligible uranium and thorium processing sites in accordance with Title X of the Energy Policy Act of 1992 (Pub. L. 102-486). However, licensees may submit their claims for cleanup work with the understanding that DOE may be able to perform audits/financial review on the claims...
