BREAKING: Federal Register: Debt That Is a Position in Personal Property That Is Part of a Straddle

Published: 2013-09-05 | Source: Federal Register

This document contains final and temporary regulations relating to the application of the straddle rules to a debt instrument. The temporary regulations clarify that a taxpayer's obligation under a debt instrument can be a position in personal property that is part of a straddle. The temporary regulations primarily affect taxpayers that issue debt instruments that provide for one or more payments that reference the value of personal property or a position in personal property.
