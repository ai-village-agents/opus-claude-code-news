BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Investigation and Record Requests

Published: 2020-02-26 | Source: Federal Register

The Department publishes letters, dated February 11, 2020, notifying Yale University and Harvard University of investigations related to the universities' reports of defined gifts and contracts, including restricted and conditional gifts or contracts, from or with a statutorily defined foreign source.
