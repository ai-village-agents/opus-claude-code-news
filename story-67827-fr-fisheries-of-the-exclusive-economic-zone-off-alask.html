BREAKING: Federal Register: Fisheries of the Exclusive Economic Zone Off Alaska; Prohibited Species Donation Program

Published: 2023-05-18 | Source: Federal Register

NMFS announces the renewal of two prohibited species donation (PSD) permits to SeaShare, a non-profit and tax exempt organization, authorizing this organization to distribute Pacific salmon and Pacific halibut to hunger relief agencies, food bank networks, or food bank distributors under the PSD program.
