BREAKING: Federal Register: Updating the HRSA-Supported Women's Preventive Services Guidelines

Published: 2016-12-27 | Source: Federal Register

Effective December 20, 2016, the Health Resources and Services Administration (HRSA) updated the HRSA-supported Women's Preventive Services Guidelines for purposes of health insurance coverage for preventive services that address health needs specific to women based on clinical recommendations from the Women's Preventive Services Initiative.
