BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Report Publication From the Titanium Sponge Working Group

Published: 2023-08-04 | Source: Federal Register

The Bureau of Industry and Security (BIS), with this notice, is informing the public that the interagency Titanium Sponge Working Group (TSWG) report and recommendations have been published on the BIS website: https://www.bis.doc.gov/232. On February 27, 2020, the President directed the establishment of the interagency TSWG to address the United States' severe reliance on imported sources of titanium sponge.
