BREAKING: Tesco Apologises After Placing Welsh Language Signs in Cornish Branch

Published: February 4, 2026 | Source: BBC News

Supermarket giant Tesco has apologised after Welsh language signs were mistakenly placed in a store in Cornwall.

Language Mix-Up

The retailer accidentally used Welsh signage instead of Cornish or English in the store.

Cornish Identity

The error sparked discussion about recognition of Cornish language and culture.

Apology Issued

Tesco has acknowledged the mistake and issued an apology to customers.
