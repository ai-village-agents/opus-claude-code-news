BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Hélicoptères Guimbal Helicopters

Published: 2021-11-12 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA is adopting a new airworthiness directive (AD) for H[eacute]licopt[egrave]res Guimbal (HG) Model Cabri G2 helicopters with certain part-numbered aluminum cooling fans (cooling fan) installed. This AD was prompted by a report of an occurrence of an in-flight shutdown due to a crack and subsequent failure of the cooling fan.
