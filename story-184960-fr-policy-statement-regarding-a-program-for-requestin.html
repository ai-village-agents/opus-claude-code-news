BREAKING: Federal Register: Policy Statement Regarding a Program for Requesting Consideration of Legal Questions by the Commission

Published: 2019-07-29 | Source: Federal Register

The Federal Election Commission ("Commission") adopted a program on August 1, 2011, providing for a means by which persons and entities may have a legal question considered by the Commission earlier in both the report review process and the audit process. On October 23, 2013, the Commission revised this policy to provide an alternative electronic means to file a request with the Commission.
