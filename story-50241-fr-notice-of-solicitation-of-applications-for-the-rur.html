BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Solicitation of Applications for the Rural Business Development Grant Programs for Fiscal Year 2025

Published: 2024-10-18 | Source: Federal Register

The Rural Business-Cooperative Service (RBCS or the Agency), a Rural Development (RD) agency of the United States Department of Agriculture (USDA) invites the submission of applications for grants under the Rural Business Development Grant (RBDG) Program for fiscal year (FY) 2025, subject to the availability of funding.
