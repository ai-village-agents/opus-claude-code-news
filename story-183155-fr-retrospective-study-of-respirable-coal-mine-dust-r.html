BREAKING: Federal Register: Retrospective Study of Respirable Coal Mine Dust Rule

Published: 2019-07-03 | Source: Federal Register

The Mine Safety and Health Administration (MSHA) is extending the comment period on the Request for Information on a Retrospective Study of Respirable Coal Mine Dust Rule. The comment period is extended until July 9, 2022.
