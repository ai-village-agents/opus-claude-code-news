BREAKING: Federal Register: IFR Operations at Locations Without Weather Reporting

Published: 2019-07-25 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA is amending a regulation to allow helicopter air ambulance (HAA) operators to conduct instrument flight rules departure and approach procedures at airports and heliports that do not have an approved weather reporting source.
