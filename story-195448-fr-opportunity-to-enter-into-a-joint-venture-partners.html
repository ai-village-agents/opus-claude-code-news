BREAKING: Federal Register: Opportunity To Enter Into a Joint Venture Partnership With the National Technical Information Service for Data Innovation Support

Published: 2019-12-13 | Source: Federal Register

The National Technical Information Service (NTIS) requests proposals from interested private-sector organizations to assist Federal agencies in the development and implementation of innovative ways to collect, connect, access, secure, analyze, disseminate and enable effective and efficient use of data to address unique and complex national data priorities. Specifically, NTIS is interested in partnering with organizations that have specialized skills and capabilities in applied data science areas...
