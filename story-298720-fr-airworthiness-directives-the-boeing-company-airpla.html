BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; The Boeing Company Airplanes

Published: 2015-07-13 | Source: Federal Register

We are adopting a new airworthiness directive (AD) for The Boeing Company Model 747 airplanes equipped with a main deck side cargo door (MDSCD). This AD was prompted by recent testing that indicates that intermodal containers, when loaded as cargo, under certain flight- load conditions, can shift and impact the adjacent fuselage frames.
