BREAKING: Federal Register: Certain Food Processing Equipment and Packaging Materials Thereof; Issuance of a General Exclusion Order; Termination of the Investigation

Published: 2020-06-18 | Source: Federal Register

Notice is hereby given that the U.S. International Trade Commission has issued a general exclusion order ("GEO") prohibiting the unlicensed entry of certain food processing equipment and packaging materials thereof that are falsely advertised through the unlicensed use of one or more certification marks of U.S. Trademark Registration No. 1,976,117; U.S. Trademark Registration No. 5,189,919; or U.S. Trademark Registration No. 5,554,628 (collectively, "the Certification Marks").
