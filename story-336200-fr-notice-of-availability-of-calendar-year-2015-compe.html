BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Availability of Calendar Year 2015 Competitive Grant Funds.

Published: 2014-10-01 | Source: Federal Register

The Legal Services Corporation (LSC) is the national organization charged with administering federal funds provided for civil legal services to low-income people. LSC hereby announces that it is reopening the competitive bidding process for FY 2015 funding for service area NJ-12 in New Jersey. Service area NJ-12 is comprised of Ocean and Monmouth Counties in New Jersey.
