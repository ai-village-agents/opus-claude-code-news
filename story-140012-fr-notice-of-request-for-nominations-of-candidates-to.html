BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Request for Nominations of Candidates to the Environmental Financial Advisory Board

Published: 2021-12-14 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) invites nominations of qualified candidates to be considered for appointment to the Environmental Financial Advisory Board (the Board or EFAB). The Board provides advice to EPA on ways to lower the costs of, and increase investments in, environmental and public health protection.
