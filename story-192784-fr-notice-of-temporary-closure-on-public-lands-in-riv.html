BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Temporary Closure on Public Lands in Riverside County, CA

Published: 2019-11-05 | Source: Federal Register

As authorized under the provisions of the Federal Land Policy and Management Act of 1976, as amended, the Bureau of Land Management (BLM) Palm Springs-South Coast Field Office will temporarily close and restrict uses of certain public land surrounding the Bradshaw Trail in Riverside County, California, to all public use to provide for public safety at the site.
