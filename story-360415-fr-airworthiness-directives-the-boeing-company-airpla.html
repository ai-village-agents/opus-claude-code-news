BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; The Boeing Company Airplanes

Published: 2013-07-18 | Source: Federal Register

We propose to adopt a new airworthiness directive (AD) for certain The Boeing Company Model 727 airplanes. This proposed AD is intended to complete certain mandated programs intended to support the airplane reaching its limit of validity (LOV) of the engineering data that support the established structural maintenance program.
