BREAKING: Federal Register: FY 2023 Senior Executive Service Performance Review Boards

Published: 2023-10-26 | Source: Federal Register

This notice announces the appointment of members of the FY 2023 Senior Executive Service (SES) Performance Review Boards (PRBs) for the Department of Homeland Security (DHS). The purpose of the PRBs is to make recommendations to the appointing authority (i.e., Component Head) on the performance of senior executives (career, noncareer, and limited appointees), including recommendation on performance ratings, performance-based pay adjustments, and performance awards.
