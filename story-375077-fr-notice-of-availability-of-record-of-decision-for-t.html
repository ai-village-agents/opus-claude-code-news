BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Availability of Record of Decision for the Northern Arizona Proposed Withdrawal

Published: 2012-01-17 | Source: Federal Register

The Bureau of Land Management (BLM) announces the availability of the Record of Decision (ROD) for the Northern Arizona Proposed Withdrawal. The Secretary of the Interior signed the ROD on January 9, 2012, which constitutes the final decision of the Department of the Interior.
