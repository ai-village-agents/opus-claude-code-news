BREAKING: Federal Register: Eighth Coast Guard District Annual Safety Zones; Bob O'Connor Foundation Fireworks; Ohio River Mile 0.0 to 0.1; Pittsburgh, PA

Published: 2014-08-19 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard will enforce a safety zone for the Bob O'Connor Foundation Fireworks on the Ohio River from mile 0.0 to 0.1. This zone will be in effect on August 20, 2014 from 8:30 p.m. until 9:40 p.m. This zone is needed to protect vessels transiting the area and event spectators from the hazards associated with the Bob O'Connor Foundation Fireworks.
