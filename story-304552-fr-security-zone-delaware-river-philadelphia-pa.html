BREAKING: Federal Register: Security Zone, Delaware River; Philadelphia, PA

Published: 2015-09-21 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is establishing security zones in the waters of the Delaware River and Schuylkill River in Philadelphia, PA. These zones are intended to restrict vessels from portions of the Delaware River and Schuylkill River during the 2015 Papal visit on September 26, 2015, and September 27, 2015.
