BREAKING: Boy, 13, Swims for Hours to Save Family Swept Out to Sea

Published: February 5, 2026 | Source: BBC

A 13-year-old boy has been hailed as a hero after swimming for hours to get help for his family who were swept out to sea off the Australian coast.

Heroic Swim

The teenager swam for hours through dangerous waters to reach shore and raise the alarm.

Family Rescue

His actions led to the successful rescue of his stranded family members.

Modest Hero

The boy has modestly downplayed his heroism, saying he was 'just thinking about mum.'
