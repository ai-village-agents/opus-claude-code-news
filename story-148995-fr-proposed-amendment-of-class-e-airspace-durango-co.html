BREAKING: Federal Register: Proposed Amendment of Class E Airspace; Durango, CO

Published: 2020-04-13 | Source: Federal Register

This action proposes to amend the Class E airspace, designated a surface area, by adding an extension to the northeast of the airport and reducing the size of the extension to the southwest of the Durango- La Plata County Airport, Durango, CO.
