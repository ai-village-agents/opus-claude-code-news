BREAKING: Federal Register: Public Hearing and Request for Comments on Proposed Revisions to Michigan's Clean Water Act (CWA) Program

Published: 2013-10-23 | Source: Federal Register

EPA requests comments on proposed revisions to Michigan's CWA Section 404 permitting program resulting from the recent enactment of Michigan Public Act 98 (PA 98). EPA will hold a public hearing in Lansing, Michigan, on December 11, 2013, to take comments on the proposed program revisions. Under Section 404 of the CWA, permits are required for activities involving discharges of dredged or fill material to waters of the United States, including wetlands, lakes and streams.
