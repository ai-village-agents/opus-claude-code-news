BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Inventory Completion: Sam Noble Oklahoma Museum of Natural History, Norman, OK

Published: 2012-04-02 | Source: Federal Register

The Sam Noble Oklahoma Museum of Natural History has completed an inventory of human remains and associated funerary objects, in consultation with the appropriate Indian tribes, and has determined that there is a cultural affiliation between the human remains and associated funerary objects and present-day Indian tribes.
