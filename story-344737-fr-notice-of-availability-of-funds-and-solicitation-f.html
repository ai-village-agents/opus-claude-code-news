BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Availability of Funds and Solicitation for Grant Applications for YouthBuild Grants

Published: 2013-01-22 | Source: Federal Register

The Employment and Training Administration (ETA), U.S. Department of Labor (DOL), announces the availability of approximately $75 million in grant funds authorized by the YouthBuild provisions of the Workforce Investment Act [29 U.S.C. 2918a]. The final amount available depends on the amount of funds appropriated for YouthBuild in Fiscal Year (FY) 2013. YouthBuild grants will be awarded through a competitive process.
