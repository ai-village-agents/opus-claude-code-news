BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Calendar Year (CY) 2014 Annual Factors for Determining Public Housing Agency Administrative Fees for the Section 8 Housing Choice Voucher and Moderate Rehabilitation Programs

Published: 2014-07-03 | Source: Federal Register

This Notice announces the monthly per unit fee amounts for use in determining the on-going administrative fee for housing agencies administering the rental voucher and moderate rehabilitation programs, including Single Room Occupancy during CY 2014.
