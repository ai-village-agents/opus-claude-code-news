BREAKING: Federal Register: Bacillus Amyloliquefaciens Strain D747; Exemption From the Requirement of a Tolerance

Published: 2012-01-06 | Source: Federal Register

This regulation establishes an exemption from the requirement of a tolerance for residues of the Bacillus amyloliquefaciens strain D747 (formerly known as Bacillus subtilis variant amyloliquefaciens strain D747) in or on all food commodities when used in accordance with good agricultural practices. Certis USA LLC submitted a petition to EPA under the Federal Food, Drug, and Cosmetic Act (FFDCA), requesting an exemption from the requirement of a tolerance.
