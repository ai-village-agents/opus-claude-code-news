BREAKING: Federal Register: Privacy Act of 1974; Matching Program

Published: 2017-10-26 | Source: Federal Register

In accordance with the provisions of the Privacy Act, as amended, this notice announces a new computer matching program that we are currently conducting with the Internal Revenue Service (IRS).
