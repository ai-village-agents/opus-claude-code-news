BREAKING: Federal Register: Privacy Act of 1974; System of Records

Published: 2022-10-11 | Source: Federal Register

In accordance with the Privacy Act of 1974, as amended, the Department of the Treasury, Alcohol and Tobacco Tax and Trade Bureau, proposes to modify a current Treasury system of records titled "Department of the Treasury, Alcohol and Tobacco Tax and Trade Bureau (TTB) .001--Regulatory Enforcement System of Records."
