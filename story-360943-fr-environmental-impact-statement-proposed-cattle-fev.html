BREAKING: Federal Register: Environmental Impact Statement; Proposed Cattle Fever Tick Control Barrier in South Texas

Published: 2013-07-24 | Source: Federal Register

We are advising the public that the Animal and Plant Health Inspection Service has prepared a draft environmental impact statement (DEIS) to analyze the effects that may result from installing game fencing as a barrier to keep animals that carry cattle fever ticks and southern cattle ticks out of areas which are free of them and which are beyond the permanent tick quarantine zone in South Texas.
