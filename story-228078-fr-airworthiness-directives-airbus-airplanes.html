BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Airbus Airplanes

Published: 2017-02-22 | Source: Federal Register

We are superseding Airworthiness Directive (AD) 2012-22-12 for all Airbus Model A330-243, -243F, -341, -342, and -343 airplanes. AD 2012-22-12 required inspecting piccolo tubes and mount links, the aft side of the forward bulkhead, and outer boundary angles (OBAs); and doing corrective actions if necessary.
