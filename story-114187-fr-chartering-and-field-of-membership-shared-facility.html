BREAKING: Federal Register: Chartering and Field of Membership-Shared Facility Requirements

Published: 2021-01-11 | Source: Federal Register

The NCUA Board (Board) proposes to amend its chartering and field of membership ("FOM") rules to modernize requirements related to service facilities for multiple common bond ("MCB") federal credit unions ("FCUs"). The Board is proposing to include any shared branch, shared ATM, or shared electronic facility in the definition of "service facility" for an FCU that participates in a shared branching network.
