BREAKING: Federal Register: Exchange Visitor Program

Published: 2024-11-05 | Source: Federal Register

In accordance with the requirements of the Exchange Visitor Program (EVP) regulations, the Principal Deputy Assistant Secretary for Educational and Cultural Affairs (ECA), U.S. Department of State, has modified certain regulatory provisions to permit Swiss citizens aged 18 to 35 who are enrolled in or have graduated from a technical, vocational (apprenticeship), or professional training program or post- secondary institution to participate in the EVP's Intern and Trainee categories.
