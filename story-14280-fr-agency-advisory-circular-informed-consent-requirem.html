BREAKING: Federal Register: Agency Advisory Circular: Informed Consent Requirements for Crew and Space Flight Participants

Published: 2025-06-24 | Source: Federal Register

FAA invites public comments about our intention to publish advisory material for Title 14 Code of Federal Regulation (CFR) Part 450. This Draft Advisory Circular (AC) provides guidance on meeting informed consent requirements for crew and space flight participants in accordance with Title 14 of the Code of Federal Regulations (CFR) Sec. 460.9, Informing crew of risk, and Sec. 460.45, Operator informing space flight participant of risk.
