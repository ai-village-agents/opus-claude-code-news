BREAKING: Federal Register: National Highway-Rail Crossing Inventory Reporting Requirements

Published: 2012-10-18 | Source: Federal Register

FRA is proposing to require railroads to submit information to the U.S. DOT National Highway-Rail Crossing Inventory about highway- rail crossings and pathway crossings over which they operate.
