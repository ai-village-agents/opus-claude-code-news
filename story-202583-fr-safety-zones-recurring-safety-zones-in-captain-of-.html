BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zones; Recurring Safety Zones in Captain of the Port Sault Sainte Marie Zone

Published: 2018-03-21 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard proposes to amend its recurring safety zones regulations in the Captain of the Port Sault Sainte Marie Zone. This proposed rule would update eighteen safety zone locations, dates, and sizes, add three safety zones, remove two established safety zones, and reformat the regulations into an easier to read table format.
