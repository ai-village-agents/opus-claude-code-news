BREAKING: Federal Register: Utility Scale Wind Towers From Canada, Indonesia, South Korea, and Vietnam; Institution of Five-Year Reviews

Published: 2025-07-01 | Source: Federal Register

The Commission hereby gives notice that it has instituted reviews pursuant to the Tariff Act of 1930 ("the Act"), as amended, to determine whether revocation of the countervailing duty orders on utility scale wind towers from Canada and Vietnam and the antidumping duty orders on utility scale wind towers from Canada, Indonesia, South Korea, and Vietnam would be likely to lead to continuation or recurrence of material injury.
