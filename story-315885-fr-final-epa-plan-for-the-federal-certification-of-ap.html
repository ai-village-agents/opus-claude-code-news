BREAKING: Federal Register: Final EPA Plan for the Federal Certification of Applicators of Restricted Use Pesticides Within Indian Country; Notice of Implementation

Published: 2014-02-06 | Source: Federal Register

In the Federal Register of May 18, 2011, EPA issued a notice of intent to implement a Federal program to certify applicators of restricted use pesticides (RUPs) in Indian country where no other certification plan applies. The program will be administered by EPA. In that notice, EPA solicited comments from the public on EPA's Proposed Federal Plan for Certifying Applicators of Restricted Use Pesticides within Indian Country (EPA plan).
