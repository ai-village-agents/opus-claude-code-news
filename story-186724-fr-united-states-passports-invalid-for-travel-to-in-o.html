BREAKING: Federal Register: United States Passports Invalid for Travel to, in, or Through the Democratic People's Republic of Korea

Published: 2019-08-20 | Source: Federal Register

On September 1, 2017, all United States passports were declared invalid for travel to, in, or through the Democratic People's Republic of Korea (DPRK) unless specially validated for such travel. The restriction was extended for one year in 2018, and, if not renewed, the restriction is set to expire on August 31, 2019. This notice extends the restriction until August 31, 2020 unless extended or revoked by the Secretary of State.
