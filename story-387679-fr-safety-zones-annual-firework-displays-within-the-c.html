BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zones; Annual Firework Displays Within the Captain of the Port, Puget Sound Area of Responsibility

Published: 2012-06-06 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard will enforce the safety zones for annual firework displays in the Captain of the Port, Puget Sound area of responsibility during the dates and times noted below. This action is necessary to prevent injury and to protect life and property of the maritime public from the hazards associated with the firework displays.
