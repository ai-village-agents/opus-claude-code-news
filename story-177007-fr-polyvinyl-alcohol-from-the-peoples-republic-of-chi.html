BREAKING: Federal Register: Polyvinyl Alcohol From the People's Republic of China: Notice of Final Results of Antidumping Duty Changed Circumstances Review

Published: 2019-04-18 | Source: Federal Register

On March 4, 2019, the Department of Commerce (Commerce) initiated, and published the preliminary results of, the changed circumstances review of the antidumping duty order on polyvinyl alcohol (PVA) from the People's Republic of China (China). For these final results, Commerce continues to find that Sinopec Chongqing SVW Chemical Co., Ltd. (SVW) is the successor-in-interest to Sinopec Sichuan Vinylon Works (Sichuan SVW).
