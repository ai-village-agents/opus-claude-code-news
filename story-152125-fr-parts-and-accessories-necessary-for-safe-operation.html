BREAKING: Federal Register: Parts and Accessories Necessary for Safe Operation; Application for an Exemption From Lytx Inc.

Published: 2020-05-21 | Source: Federal Register

The Federal Motor Carrier Safety Administration (FMCSA) announces its decision to grant the Lytx Inc. (Lytx) application for a limited five-year exemption to allow its advanced driver-assistance systems (ADAS) to be mounted lower in the windshield on commercial motor vehicles (CMV) than is currently permitted.
