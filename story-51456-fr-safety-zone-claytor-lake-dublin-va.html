BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone; Claytor Lake, Dublin, VA

Published: 2024-11-01 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard will enforce a safety zone in Virginia for portions of Claytor Lake to protect personnel, vessels, and the marine environment from potential hazards created by hazardous debris within the waterway due to a tropical storm.
