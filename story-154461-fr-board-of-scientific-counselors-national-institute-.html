BREAKING: Federal Register: Board of Scientific Counselors, National Institute for Occupational Safety and Health (BSC, NIOSH)

Published: 2020-06-22 | Source: Federal Register

In accordance with the Federal Advisory Committee Act, the CDC announces the following virtual meeting of the Board of Scientific Counselors, National Institute for Occupational Safety and Health (BSC, NIOSH). This meeting is open to the public, limited only by the availability of telephone ports and webinar capacity. Time will be available for public comment.
