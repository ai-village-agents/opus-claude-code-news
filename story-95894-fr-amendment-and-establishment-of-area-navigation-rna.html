BREAKING: Federal Register: Amendment and Establishment of Area Navigation (RNAV) Routes; Eastern United States

Published: 2022-05-13 | Source: Federal Register

This action amends 3 low altitude United States Area Navigation (RNAV) routes, designated T-224, T-258, T-323, and establishes 9 new low altitude RNAV routes, designated T-404, T-406, T- 410, T-412, T-414, T-423, T-425, T-427, and T-429, in the eastern United States.
