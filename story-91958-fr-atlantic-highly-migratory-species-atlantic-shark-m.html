BREAKING: Federal Register: Atlantic Highly Migratory Species; Atlantic Shark Management Measures; 2022 Research Fishery

Published: 2022-03-25 | Source: Federal Register

On November 19, 2021, NMFS published a notice inviting qualified commercial shark permit holders to submit applications to participate in the 2022 Shark Research Fishery. The Shark Research Fishery allows for the collection of fishery-dependent data for future stock assessments and cooperative research with commercial fishermen to meet the shark research objectives of the Agency.
