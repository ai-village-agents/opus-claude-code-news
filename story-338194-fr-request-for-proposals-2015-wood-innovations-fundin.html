BREAKING: Federal Register: Request for Proposals: 2015 Wood Innovations Funding Opportunity

Published: 2014-10-27 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Forest Service (Forest Service) requests proposals to substantially expand and accelerate wood energy and wood products markets throughout the United States to support forest management needs on National Forest System and other forest lands.
