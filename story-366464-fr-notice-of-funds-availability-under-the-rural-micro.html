BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Funds Availability Under the Rural Microentrepreneur Assistance Program

Published: 2013-09-26 | Source: Federal Register

The Rural Business-Cooperative Service announced the fiscal year (FY) 2013 funding available under the Rural Microentrepreneur Assistance Program (RMAP) in the Federal Register Vol. 78, No. 157 on Wednesday, August 14, 2013. This Notice extends the application deadline for initial loans with a technical assistance grant, initial or subsequent loan only, and technical assistance-only grants from September 13, 2013, to October 31, 2013.
