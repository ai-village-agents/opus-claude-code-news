BREAKING: Federal Register: NTIA 2019 Spectrum Policy Symposium

Published: 2019-08-13 | Source: Federal Register

The National Telecommunications and Information Administration (NTIA), U.S. Department of Commerce, will host a symposium on September 10, 2019, focusing on the development and implementation of a National Spectrum Strategy, pursuant to the Presidential Memorandum, released on October 25, 2018, on "Developing a Sustainable Spectrum Strategy for America's Future."
