BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone; Upper Mississippi River, Crystal City, MO

Published: 2017-10-04 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is establishing a safety zone for all navigable waters on the Upper Mississippi River between mile marker (MM) 147.5 and MM 148.5. This action is necessary to provide for the safety of life, vessels and property on these navigable waters near Crystal City, MO while construction work is completed on new power lines extending across the river.
