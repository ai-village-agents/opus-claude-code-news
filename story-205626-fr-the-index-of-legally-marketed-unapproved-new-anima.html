BREAKING: Federal Register: The Index of Legally Marketed Unapproved New Animal Drugs for Minor Species; Guidance for Industry; Availability

Published: 2018-04-27 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA) is announcing the availability of guidance for industry #210 entitled "The Index of Legally Marketed Unapproved New Animal Drugs for Minor Species." This final guidance describes the process for adding a new animal drug to the Index of Legally Marketed Unapproved New Animal Drugs for Minor Species (the Index).
