BREAKING: Federal Register: Deregistration Under Section 8(f) of the Investment Company Act of 1940

Published: 2026-01-07 | Source: Federal Register

Applicant seeks an order declaring that it has ceased to be an investment company. On September 4, 2025, and October 15, 2025, applicant made a liquidating distribution to its shareholders based on net asset value. Expenses of $56,755.98 incurred in connection with the liquidation were paid by the applicant. Filing Date: The application was filed on October 22, 2025. Applicant's Address: 275 Wiregrass Parkway, West Henrietta, New York 14586.
