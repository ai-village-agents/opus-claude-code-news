BREAKING: Federal Register: Winnebago Industries, Inc., Receipt of Petition for Decision of Inconsequential Noncompliance

Published: 2023-08-01 | Source: Federal Register

Winnebago Industries, Inc., (Winnebago), has determined that certain model year (MY) 2013-2023 Winnebago motorhomes do not fully comply with Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard (FMVSS) No. 108, Lamps, Reflective Devices, and Associated Equipment. Winnebago filed an original noncompliance report dated November 11, 2022, and amended the report on December 2, 2022, and May 17, 2023.
