BREAKING: Federal Register: Over-the-Counter Monograph Order Requests: Format and Content; Draft Guidance for Industry; Availability

Published: 2023-04-13 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA or Agency) is announcing the availability of a draft guidance for industry entitled "Over-the- Counter Monograph Order Requests (OMORs): Format and Content." This draft guidance provides recommendations on the format and content of the information that a requestor should provide in an over-the-counter (OTC) monograph order request (OMOR) and identifies relevant guidance documents to assist requestors in preparing their OMORs.
