BREAKING: Federal Register: Survey Methodologies To Assess Risk Evaluation and Mitigation Strategies Goals That Relate to Knowledge; Draft Guidance for Industry; Availability

Published: 2019-02-01 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA or Agency) is announcing the availability of a draft guidance for industry entitled "Survey Methodologies to Assess REMS Goals That Relate to Knowledge; Draft Guidance For Industry." This draft guidance provides recommendations to industry on conducting risk evaluation and mitigation strategies (REMS) assessment surveys used to evaluate respondent knowledge of REMS-related information.
