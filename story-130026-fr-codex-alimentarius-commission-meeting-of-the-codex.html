BREAKING: Federal Register: Codex Alimentarius Commission: Meeting of the Codex Committee on Fats and Oils

Published: 2021-08-06 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Codex Office is sponsoring a public meeting on September 20, 2021. The objective of the public meeting is to provide information and receive public comments on agenda items and draft United States (U.S.) positions to be discussed at the 27th Session of the Codex Committee on Fats and Oils (CCFO) of the Codex Alimentarius Commission, which will convene virtually, October 18-26, 2021.
