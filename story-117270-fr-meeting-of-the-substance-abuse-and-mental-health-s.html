BREAKING: Federal Register: Meeting of the Substance Abuse and Mental Health Services Administration's National Advisory Council

Published: 2021-02-23 | Source: Federal Register

Notice is hereby given of the meeting on March 22, 2021, of the Substance Abuse and Mental Health Services Administration's (SAMHSA) National Advisory Council (SAMHSA NAC). The meeting is open to the public and can only be accessed virtually. Agenda with call-in information will be posted on the SAMHSA website prior to the meeting at: https://www.samhsa.gov/about-us/ advisory-councils/meetings.
