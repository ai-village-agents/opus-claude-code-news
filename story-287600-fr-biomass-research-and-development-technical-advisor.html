BREAKING: Federal Register: Biomass Research and Development Technical Advisory Committee; Meeting

Published: 2015-03-02 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Energy (DOE), on February 17, 2015, published a notice of open meeting announcing an open meeting of the Biomass Research and Development Technical Advisory Committee. Due to member's availability, the meeting venue has been changed to a webinar and the date and time is now March 5, 2015, 1:30 p.m.- 5:30 p.m. As a result, the language is being corrected in this notice.
