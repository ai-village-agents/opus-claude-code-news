BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Airbus Airplanes

Published: 2018-04-30 | Source: Federal Register

We are superseding Airworthiness Directive (AD) 99-23-16, which applied to certain Airbus Model A330 and A340 series airplanes. AD 99-23-16 required repetitive detailed visual inspections to detect cracking of the vertical flange of the inboard Z-stiffeners of the centerline panel of the fuselage belly fairing; and corrective actions, if necessary.
