BREAKING: Federal Register: Multiemployer Plans; Valuation and Notice Requirements

Published: 2014-05-28 | Source: Federal Register

This final rule amends the Pension Benefit Guaranty Corporation's (PBGC) multiemployer regulations to make the provision of information to PBGC and plan participants more efficient and effective and to reduce burden on plans and sponsors.
