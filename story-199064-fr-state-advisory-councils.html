BREAKING: Federal Register: State Advisory Councils

Published: 2018-02-01 | Source: Federal Register

This proposed rulemaking would remove the Legal Services Corporation's (LSC) regulation on state advisory councils. LSC believes this action is appropriate because the state advisory councils are no longer active and their oversight functions have been replaced adequately by other offices and processes established by Congress or LSC.
