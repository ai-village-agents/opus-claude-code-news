BREAKING: Federal Register: Removal of Transferred OTS Regulation Regarding Deposits

Published: 2019-08-26 | Source: Federal Register

The Federal Deposit Insurance Corporation (FDIC) proposes to rescind and remove the "Deposits" regulations because they are unnecessary and duplicative of currently applicable provisions of law with respect to the maintenance of deposit account records at State savings associations.
