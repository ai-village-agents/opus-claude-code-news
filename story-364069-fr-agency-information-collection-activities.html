BREAKING: Federal Register: Agency Information Collection Activities

Published: 2013-08-28 | Source: Federal Register

The Whistleblower Protection Enhancement Act of 2012 (WPEA) was signed into law on 27 November 2012 (Pub. L. 112-199). The law strengthens the protections for federal employees who disclose evidence of waste, fraud, or abuse. In addition, the WPEA modifies rules on the use of nondisclosure policies, forms, or agreements by government agencies.
