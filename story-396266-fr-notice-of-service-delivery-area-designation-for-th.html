BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Service Delivery Area Designation for the Mashpee Wampanoag Indian Tribe

Published: 2012-09-13 | Source: Federal Register

This Notice advises the public that the Indian Health Service (IHS) established the geographic boundaries of the Service Delivery Area (SDA) for the newly recognized Mashpee Wampanoag Indian Tribe. The SDA was established on December 22, 2008 and services were provided to eligible beneficiaries beginning on January 19, 2009.
