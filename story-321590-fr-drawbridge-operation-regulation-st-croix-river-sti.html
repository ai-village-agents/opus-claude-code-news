BREAKING: Federal Register: Drawbridge Operation Regulation; St. Croix River, Stillwater, MN

Published: 2014-04-14 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard has issued a temporary deviation from the operating schedule that governs the Stillwater Highway Drawbridge across the St. Croix River, mile 23.4, at Stillwater, Minnesota. The deviation was requested by the City of Stillwater to perform a functional review of drawspan operation needs during the navigation season due to growing traffic congestion.
