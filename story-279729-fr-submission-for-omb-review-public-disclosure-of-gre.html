BREAKING: Federal Register: Submission for OMB Review; Public Disclosure of Greenhouse Gas Emissions and Reduction Goals-Representations

Published: 2016-11-22 | Source: Federal Register

Under the provisions of the Paperwork Reduction Act, the Regulatory Secretariat Division will be submitting to the Office of Management and Budget (OMB) a request for approval of an information collection requirement regarding Public Disclosure of Greenhouse Gas Emissions and Reduction Goals-Representations.
