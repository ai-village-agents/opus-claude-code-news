BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; The Boeing Company Airplanes

Published: 2015-08-25 | Source: Federal Register

We propose to adopt a new airworthiness directive (AD) for all The Boeing Company Model 777-200, -200LR, -300, and -300ER series airplanes. This proposed AD was prompted by reports of fractured forward attach fittings of the inboard flap outboard aft flap track. The fractured fittings were determined to be the result of corrosion pits forming on the inside diameter of the fittings.
