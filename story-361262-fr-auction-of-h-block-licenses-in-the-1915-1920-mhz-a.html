BREAKING: Federal Register: Auction of H Block Licenses in the 1915-1920 MHz and 1995-2000 MHz Bands; Comment Sought on Competitive Bidding Procedures for Auction 96

Published: 2013-07-29 | Source: Federal Register

This document announces the intention to hold an auction of H Block licenses in the 1915-1920 and 1995-2000 MHz bands. This document also seeks comment on competitive bidding procedures for Auction 96.
