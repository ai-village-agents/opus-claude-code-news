BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Library Changes

Published: 2020-12-21 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is issuing this notice to advise the public of upcoming changes to the Region 4 Library. Region 4 will be reducing the size of its library space, decreasing the amount of print materials maintained in its collection, and ceasing all on-site library support services.
