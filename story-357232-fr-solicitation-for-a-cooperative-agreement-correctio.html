BREAKING: Federal Register: Solicitation for a Cooperative Agreement-Corrections-Specific Resources for Extended Stakeholder Audiences

Published: 2013-06-12 | Source: Federal Register

The National Institute of Corrections (NIC) is soliciting proposals from organizations, groups, or individuals to enter into a cooperative agreement for an 18-month period to begin no later than September 15, 2013. Work under this cooperative agreement will involve the development of a series of resources dedicated to addressing the corrections-specific frequently asked questions of its extended stakeholder audiences, including family and community-based constituency groups.
