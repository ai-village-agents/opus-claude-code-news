BREAKING: Federal Register: Schedule of Fees for Access to NOAA Environmental Data, Information, and Related Products and Services

Published: 2012-12-19 | Source: Federal Register

In this final rule, NESDIS establishes a new schedule of fees for the sale of its data, information, and related products and services to users. NESDIS is revising the fee schedule that has been in effect since 2011 to ensure that the fees accurately reflect the costs of providing access to the environmental data, information, and related products and services.
