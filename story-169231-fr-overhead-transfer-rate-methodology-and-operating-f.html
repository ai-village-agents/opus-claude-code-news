BREAKING: Federal Register: Overhead Transfer Rate Methodology and Operating Fee Schedule Methodology

Published: 2020-12-28 | Source: Federal Register

In July 2020, the NCUA Board (Board) invited comment on the methodology used to determine the Overhead Transfer Rate (OTR). The Board also requested comment on proposed changes to the methodology it uses to determine how it apportions operating fees charged to federal credit unions (FCUs).
