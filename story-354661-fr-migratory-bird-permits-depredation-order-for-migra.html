BREAKING: Federal Register: Migratory Bird Permits; Depredation Order for Migratory Birds in California

Published: 2013-05-13 | Source: Federal Register

We propose to revise the regulations that allow control of depredating birds in some counties in California. We propose to specify the counties in which this order is effective, to better identify which species may be taken under the order, to add a requirement that landowners attempt nonlethal control, to add a requirement for use of nontoxic ammunition, and to revise the reporting required.
