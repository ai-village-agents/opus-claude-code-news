BREAKING: Federal Register: Energy Conservation Program: Energy Conservation Standards for Direct Heating Equipment

Published: 2020-12-01 | Source: Federal Register

The Energy Policy and Conservation Act, as amended (EPCA), prescribes energy conservation standards for various consumer products, including direct heating equipment (DHE). EPCA also requires the U.S. Department of Energy (DOE) to periodically determine whether more- stringent, amended standards would be technologically feasible and economically justified, and would result in significant energy savings.
