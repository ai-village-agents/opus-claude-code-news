BREAKING: Federal Register: Schedules of Controlled Substances: Temporary Placement of Three Synthetic Cannabinoids Into Schedule I

Published: 2015-01-30 | Source: Federal Register

The Administrator of the Drug Enforcement Administration is issuing this final order to temporarily schedule three synthetic cannabinoids (SCs) into schedule I pursuant to the temporary scheduling provisions of the Controlled Substances Act (CSA).
