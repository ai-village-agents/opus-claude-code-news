BREAKING: Federal Register: Drawbridge Operation Regulation; Gulf Intracoastal Waterway, Treasure Island, FL

Published: 2013-11-13 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard proposes to change the operating schedule that governs the Treasure Island Causeway Bridge, mile 119.0, Treasure Island, Florida. The Treasure Island Bridge is a double-leaf bascule bridge that provides a vertical clearance of 21 feet in the closed position. The Treasure Island Bridge crosses the Gulf Intracoastal Waterway at mile 119.0, Treasure Island, Pinellas County, Florida.
