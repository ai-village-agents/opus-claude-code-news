BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone; Lake Charles, Lake Charles, LA

Published: 2021-06-29 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is establishing a temporary safety zone for all navigable waters of Lake Charles west of 93[deg]13'51.2" W, east of 93[deg]14'8.3" W, and extending 500 yards south from the northern shore of Lake Charles. This safety zone is necessary to protect persons and vessels from hazards associated with a Pro Watercross event on August 28 and 29, 2021 in Lake Charles, LA.
