BREAKING: Federal Register: Staff Sergeant Fox Suicide Prevention Grant Program Funding Opportunity

Published: 2025-05-15 | Source: Federal Register

VA is announcing the availability of funds for suicide prevention grants under the Staff Sergeant Fox Suicide Prevention Grant Program (SSG Fox SPGP). The SSG Fox SPGP directs efforts to reduce Veteran suicide by awarding grants to community-based organizations to provide or coordinate the provision of primarily non-clinical suicide prevention services, including outreach and linkage to VA and community resources, to eligible individuals and their families.
