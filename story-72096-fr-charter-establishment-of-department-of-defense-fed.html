BREAKING: Federal Register: Charter Establishment of Department of Defense Federal Advisory Committees-Department of the Navy Science and Technology Board

Published: 2023-07-17 | Source: Federal Register

The DoD gives notice that it is establishing the Department of the Navy Science and Technology Board (DoN S&T Board) as a discretionary Federal advisory committee.
