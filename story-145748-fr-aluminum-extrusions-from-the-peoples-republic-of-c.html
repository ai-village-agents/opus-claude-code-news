BREAKING: Federal Register: Aluminum Extrusions From the People's Republic of China: Rescission of Antidumping and Countervailing Duty Circumvention Inquiries

Published: 2020-03-03 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Commerce (Commerce) is rescinding the anti- circumvention inquiries on aluminum extrusions from the People's Republic of China (China) that were initiated on May 6, 2019.
