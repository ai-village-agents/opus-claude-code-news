BREAKING: Federal Register: Environmental Impact Statement in Erie County, NY

Published: 2019-12-20 | Source: Federal Register

The FHWA is issuing this notice to advise the public that an Environmental Impact Statement (EIS) will be prepared for a proposed project known as the New York State (NYS) Route 5 (Buffalo Skyway) Project, in Erie County, New York.
