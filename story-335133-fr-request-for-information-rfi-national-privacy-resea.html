BREAKING: Federal Register: Request for Information (RFI)-National Privacy Research Strategy

Published: 2014-09-18 | Source: Federal Register

Agencies of the Federal Networking and Information Technology Research and Development (NITRD) Program are planning to develop a joint National Privacy Research Strategy. On behalf of the agencies, the Cyber Security and Information Assurance Research and Development Senior Steering Group seeks public input on the vital privacy objectives that should be considered for the goals of the strategy.
