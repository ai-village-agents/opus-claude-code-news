BREAKING: Federal Register: Small Business Size Standards: Educational Services

Published: 2012-09-24 | Source: Federal Register

The United States Small Business Administration (SBA) is increasing the small business size standards for nine industries in North American Industry Classification System (NAICS) Sector 61, Educational Services, and retaining the current size standards for the remaining eight industries and one sub-industry ("exception") in this Sector.
