BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone, Coast Guard Exercise Area, Hood Canal, Washington

Published: 2013-02-05 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Coast Guard is establishing a safety zone around vessels involved in Coast Guard training exercises in Hood Canal, WA. This is necessary to ensure the safety of the maritime public during these exercises, which involve fast moving surface vessels, smoke machines, pyrotechnics, and other elements which could create safety concerns for waterway users.
