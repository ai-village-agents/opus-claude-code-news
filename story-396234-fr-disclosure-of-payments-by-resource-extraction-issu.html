BREAKING: Federal Register: Disclosure of Payments by Resource Extraction Issuers

Published: 2012-09-12 | Source: Federal Register

We are adopting new rules and an amendment to a new form pursuant to Section 1504 of the Dodd-Frank Wall Street Reform and Consumer Protection Act relating to disclosure of payments by resource extraction issuers.
