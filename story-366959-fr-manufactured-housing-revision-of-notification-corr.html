BREAKING: Federal Register: Manufactured Housing: Revision of Notification, Correction, and Procedural Regulations

Published: 2013-10-01 | Source: Federal Register

This final rule amends HUD regulations that establish procedures for manufacturers and others to address reports of problems with manufactured homes. These "Subpart I" regulations establish a system of protections with respect to imminent safety hazards and violations of the federal construction and safety standards, assuring a minimum of formality and delay, while protecting the rights of all parties.
