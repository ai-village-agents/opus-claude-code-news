BREAKING: Federal Register: Security Zone; Senior Government Official's Visit to Cleveland, Lake Erie, Cleveland, OH

Published: 2018-11-05 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is establishing a temporary security zone for navigable waters on Lake Erie for a senior government official's visit to Cleveland, OH. The security zone is necessary to protect the official party, the public and surrounding waterways from terrorist acts, sabotage or other subversive acts, accidents, or other causes of a similar nature.
