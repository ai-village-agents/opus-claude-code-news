BREAKING: Federal Register: USPS Marketing Mail Content Standards

Published: 2018-08-23 | Source: Federal Register

The Postal Service is contemplating amendment of the Mailing Standards of the United States Postal Service, Domestic Mail Manual (DMM[supreg]), to revise content standards for USPS Marketing Mail[supreg] letter-size and flat-size pieces regardless of level of sortation.
