BREAKING: Federal Register: Micro-Captive Listed Transactions and Micro-Captive Transactions of Interest

Published: 2023-04-11 | Source: Federal Register

This document contains proposed regulations that identify transactions that are the same as, or substantially similar to, certain micro-captive transactions as listed transactions, a type of reportable transaction, and certain other micro-captive transactions as transactions of interest, another type of reportable transaction.
