BREAKING: Federal Register: Air Plan Approval; Illinois; NAAQS Update

Published: 2019-11-05 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is approving a revision to the Illinois State implementation plan (SIP). The revision, submitted on May 30, 2019, incorporates several revisions to the Illinois air pollution control rules entitled "Part 243--Ambient Air Quality Standards" related to the National Ambient Air Quality Standards (NAAQS).
