BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Opportunity To Comment on an Analysis of the Greenhouse Gas Emissions Attributable to Production and Transport of Cotton (Gossypium spp.) Seed Oil for Use in Biofuel Production

Published: 2015-07-14 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is inviting comment on its analysis of the greenhouse gas (GHG) emissions attributable to the production and transport of Gossypium spp. seed oil ("cottonseed oil") feedstock for use in making biofuels such as biodiesel, renewable diesel, and jet fuel.
