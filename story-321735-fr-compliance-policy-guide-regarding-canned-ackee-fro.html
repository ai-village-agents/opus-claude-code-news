BREAKING: Federal Register: Compliance Policy Guide Regarding Canned Ackee, Frozen Ackee, and Other Ackee Products-Hypoglycin A Toxin; Availability

Published: 2014-04-15 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA or we) is announcing the availability of the Compliance Policy Guide (CPG) Sec. 550.050 Canned Ackee, Frozen Ackee, and Other Ackee Products--Hypoglycin A Toxin. The CPG provides guidance for FDA staff on our enforcement criteria for canned ackee, frozen ackee, and other ackee products that contain hypoglycin A.
