BREAKING: Federal Register: Advisory Committee on the Medical Uses of Isotopes: Meeting Notice

Published: 2019-09-24 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Nuclear Regulatory Commission (NRC) will convene a teleconference meeting of the Advisory Committee on the Medical Uses of Isotopes (ACMUI) on October 17, 2019, to discuss the draft report of the ACMUI Training and Experience Subcommittee.
