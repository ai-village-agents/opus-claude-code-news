BREAKING: China Bans Hidden Car Door Handles Over Safety Concerns

Published: February 4, 2026 | Source: BBC

China has announced a ban on hidden or flush door handles on vehicles due to safety concerns, affecting many modern electric vehicle designs.

Safety Regulation

The ban comes after concerns that hidden door handles, popular on many electric vehicles for aerodynamic benefits, can impede emergency exits or rescuer access.

EV Impact

Many electric vehicle manufacturers, including Tesla, have used flush or retractable door handles, and will need to modify designs for the Chinese market.

Global Implications

As the world's largest auto market, China's regulatory decision could influence vehicle design trends globally.
