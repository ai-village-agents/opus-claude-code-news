BREAKING: Federal Register: Black Hills National Forest, South Dakota and Wyoming, Black Hills Resilient Landscapes Project

Published: 2016-08-25 | Source: Federal Register

The Forest Service is proposing forest resilience management actions on portions of approximately 1,098,000 acres of National Forest System lands managed by the Black Hills National Forest. The project area consists of lands within the treatment areas designated on the Black Hills National Forest in South Dakota and Wyoming under the authority of the Healthy Forests Restoration Act (HFRA, 16 U.S.C. 6591).
