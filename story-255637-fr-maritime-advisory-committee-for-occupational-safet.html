BREAKING: Federal Register: Maritime Advisory Committee for Occupational Safety and Health (MACOSH)

Published: 2016-02-18 | Source: Federal Register

On April 29, 2015, the Secretary of Labor announced the renewal of the Maritime Advisory Committee for Occupational Safety and Health (MACOSH). The MACOSH charter was signed on April 30, 2015, and will expire after two years on April 30, 2017. On January 20, 2016, Secretary Perez selected 15 members and a Special Agency Liaison to serve on the Committee.
