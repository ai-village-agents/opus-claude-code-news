BREAKING: Federal Register: Format and Content of a Risk Evaluation and Mitigation Strategy Document; Revised Draft Guidance for Industry; Availability

Published: 2017-10-12 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA or Agency) is announcing the availability of a revised draft guidance for industry entitled "Format and Content of a REMS Document." A Risk Evaluation and Mitigation Strategy (REMS) document, which is part of a REMS that is required by FDA, establishes the goals and requirements of the REMS.
