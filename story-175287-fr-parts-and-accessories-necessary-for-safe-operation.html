BREAKING: Federal Register: Parts and Accessories Necessary for Safe Operation; Laydon Composites Ltd. Application for an Exemption

Published: 2019-03-28 | Source: Federal Register

FMCSA requests public comment on an application for exemption from Laydon Composites Ltd. (Laydon) to allow motor carriers to operate commercial motor vehicles (CMVs) that are equipped with Laydon's OptiTail\TM\ aerodynamic device with rear identification lamps and rear clearance lamps that are mounted lower than currently permitted by the Agency's regulations.
