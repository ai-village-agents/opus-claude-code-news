BREAKING: Federal Register: Air Plan Approval; IA; Electronic Submittal of Air Quality Information

Published: 2023-01-24 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is proposing to approve revisions to the Iowa State Implementation Plan (SIP) and the Operating Permit Program for the State of Iowa. The revisions require the electronic submittal of air emissions reporting, construction permit applications, and Title V permit applications, and make administrative updates.
