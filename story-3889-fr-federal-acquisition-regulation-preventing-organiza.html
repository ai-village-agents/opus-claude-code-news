BREAKING: Federal Register: Federal Acquisition Regulation: Preventing Organizational Conflicts of Interest in Federal Acquisition

Published: 2025-01-15 | Source: Federal Register

DoD, GSA, and NASA are proposing to amend the Federal Acquisition Regulation (FAR) to implement the Preventing Organizational Conflicts of Interest in Federal Acquisition Act. The statute requires the FAR to provide and update definitions, guidance, and examples related to organizational conflicts of interest, including the creation of solicitation provisions and contract clauses to avoid or mitigate organizational conflicts of interest.
