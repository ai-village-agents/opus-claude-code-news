BREAKING: Federal Register: Bovine Spongiform Encephalopathy; Importation of Bovines and Bovine Products

Published: 2012-03-16 | Source: Federal Register

We are proposing to amend the regulations that govern the importation of animals and animal products to revise the conditions for the importation of live bovines and products derived from bovines with regard to bovine spongiform encephalopathy (BSE). We are proposing to base importation conditions on the inherent risk of BSE infectivity in specified commodities, as well as on the BSE risk status of the region from which the commodities originate.
