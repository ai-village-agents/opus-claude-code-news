BREAKING: Federal Register: Controlled Import Permits

Published: 2013-05-02 | Source: Federal Register

We are amending the regulations concerning the importation of plants and plant products by establishing the controlled import permit as a single type of authorization for the importation into the United States of otherwise prohibited or restricted plant material for experimental, therapeutic, or developmental purposes.
