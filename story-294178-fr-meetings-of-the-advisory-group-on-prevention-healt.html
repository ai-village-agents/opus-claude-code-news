BREAKING: Federal Register: Meetings of the Advisory Group on Prevention, Health Promotion, and Integrative and Public Health

Published: 2015-05-19 | Source: Federal Register

In accordance with Section 10(a) of the Federal Advisory Committee Act, Public Law 92-463, as amended (5 U.S.C. App.), notice is hereby given that two meetings are scheduled for the Advisory Group on Prevention, Health Promotion, and Integrative and Public Health (the "Advisory Group"). The meetings will be open to the public. Information about the Advisory Group and the agendas for these meetings can be obtained by accessing the following Web site: http:// www.surgeongeneral.gov/initiatives/pre...
