BREAKING: Federal Register: Regulated Navigation Area and Safety Zone; Hampton River, Hampton, NH

Published: 2025-07-07 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is establishing a temporary regulated navigation area and a safety zone on the Hampton River at the inlet to Hampton Harbor, Hampton, NH, in response to hazardous conditions created by construction activities for the Neil R. Underwood Seabrook- Hampton Harbor Bridge replacement project. The RNA will establish a speed restriction and a traffic pattern.
