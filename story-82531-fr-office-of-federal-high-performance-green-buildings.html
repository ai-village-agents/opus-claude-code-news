BREAKING: Federal Register: Office of Federal High-Performance Green Buildings; Green Building Advisory Committee; Notification of Upcoming Public Meeting

Published: 2023-11-21 | Source: Federal Register

In accordance with the requirements of the Federal Advisory Committee Act, this notice provides the agenda for a Web-based meeting of the Green Building Advisory Committee (the Committee). This meeting will be focused on finalizing and voting on Task Group topics for FY2024. The meeting is open to the public to observe; online attendees are required to register in advance to attend as instructed below.
