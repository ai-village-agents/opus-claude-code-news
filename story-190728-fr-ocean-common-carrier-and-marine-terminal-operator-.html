BREAKING: Federal Register: Ocean Common Carrier and Marine Terminal Operator Agreements Subject to the Shipping Act of 1984

Published: 2019-10-09 | Source: Federal Register

The Federal Maritime Commission published a Notice of Proposed Rulemaking on August 15, 2016, which set forth proposed modifications to its rules governing agreements by or among ocean common carriers and/or marine terminal operators subject to the Shipping Act of 1984 and its rules on the delegation of authority to and redelegation of authority by the Director, Bureau of Trade Analysis.
