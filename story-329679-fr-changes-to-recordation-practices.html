BREAKING: Federal Register: Changes to Recordation Practices

Published: 2014-07-16 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Copyright Office is proposing to amend its regulations for the recordation of copyright transfers and other documents. The proposed rule is intended to reduce the amount of time the Office requires to process certain types of documents submitted for recordation and help to alleviate remitter concerns regarding the receipt of documents for processing.
