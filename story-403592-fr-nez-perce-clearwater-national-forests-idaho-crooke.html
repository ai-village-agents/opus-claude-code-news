BREAKING: Federal Register: Nez Perce-Clearwater National Forests; Idaho; Crooked River Valley Rehabilitation Project

Published: 2012-12-12 | Source: Federal Register

The forest gives notice of its intent to prepare an Environmental Impact Statement for the Crooked River Valley Rehabilitation Project. The Red River Ranger District of the Nez Perce- Clearwater National Forests is undergoing planning efforts to restore the lower Crooked River valley near Elk City, Idaho.
