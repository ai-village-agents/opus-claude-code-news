BREAKING: Federal Register: Estes to Flatiron Substation Transmission Lines Rebuild Project, Larimer County, CO

Published: 2012-04-17 | Source: Federal Register

Western Area Power Administration currently owns and operates two 115-kilovolt transmission lines on two separate rights-of-way (ROW) located between Flatiron Reservoir (near Loveland, Colorado) and the town of Estes Park, Colorado. Each transmission line is approximately 16 miles long. Western is proposing to remove one transmission line and abandon the ROW.
