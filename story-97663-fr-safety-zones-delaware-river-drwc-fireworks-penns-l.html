BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zones; Delaware River; DRWC Fireworks; Penn's Landing

Published: 2022-06-07 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard will enforce the Penn's Landing, Delaware River, Philadelphia, PA; Safety Zone from 8:30 p.m. through 9:15 p.m. on June 8, 2022 to provide for the safety of life on navigable waterways during this firework event. Our regulation for marine events within the Fifth Coast Guard District identifies the regulated area for this event in Philadelphia, PA.
