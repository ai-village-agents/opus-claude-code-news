BREAKING: Federal Register: Drawbridge Operation Regulation; Swinomish Channel, Whitmarsh, WA

Published: 2017-06-30 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard has issued a temporary deviation from the operating schedule that governs the Northern Santa Fe Railroad Company (BNSF) Railroad Swing Span Drawbridge 12A across Swinomish Channel, mile 8.4, near Whitmarsh, WA. This deviation is necessary to accommodate replacement of the bridge protective fendering system.
