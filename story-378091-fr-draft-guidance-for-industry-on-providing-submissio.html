BREAKING: Federal Register: Draft Guidance for Industry on Providing Submissions in Electronic Format-Standardized Study Data; Availability

Published: 2012-02-21 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA) is announcing the availability of a draft guidance for industry entitled "Providing Submissions in Electronic Format--Standardized Study Data." This draft guidance establishes FDA's recommendation that sponsors and applicants submit nonclinical and clinical study data in a standardized electronic format.
