BREAKING: Federal Register: Golden Nematode; Removal of Regulated Areas in Livingston and Steuben Counties, NY

Published: 2013-01-09 | Source: Federal Register

We are amending the golden nematode regulations by removing areas in Livingston and Steuben Counties in New York from the list of generally infested areas. Surveys and other data have shown that certain areas in these two counties are free of golden nematode, and we have determined that regulation of these areas is no longer necessary.
