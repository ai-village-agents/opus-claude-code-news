BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Airbus Helicopters

Published: 2022-12-02 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA is adopting a new airworthiness directive (AD) for certain Airbus Helicopters (Airbus) Model SA330J helicopters. This AD was prompted by a report of restricted movement of the collective lever caused by incidental contact of the secondary stop cover due to a loosened rivet.
