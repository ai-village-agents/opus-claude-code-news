BREAKING: Federal Register: Approval and Promulgation of Air Quality Implementation Plans; Virginia; Transportation Conformity Regulations

Published: 2013-04-01 | Source: Federal Register

EPA proposes to approve the State Implementation Plan (SIP) revision submitted by the Commonwealth of Virginia for the purpose of amending existing regulation 9VAC5 Chapter 151 in order to incorporate Federal revisions to transportation conformity requirements. In the Final Rules section of this Federal Register, EPA is approving the Commonwealth's SIP submittal as a direct final rule without prior proposal because EPA views this as a noncontroversial submittal and anticipates no adverse comment...
