BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Hélicoptères Guimbal Helicopters

Published: 2022-01-31 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA proposes to supersede Airworthiness Directive (AD) 2021-02-20, which applies to certain H[eacute]licopt[egrave]res Guimbal Model Cabri G2 helicopters. AD 2021-02-20 requires initial and repetitive inspections of certain rotating and non-rotating scissor fittings, and depending on the results, replacing the affected assembly.
