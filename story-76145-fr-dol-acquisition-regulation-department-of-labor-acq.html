BREAKING: Federal Register: DOL Acquisition Regulation: Department of Labor Acquisition Regulation System

Published: 2023-09-05 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Labor (DOL) is proposing to revise the Department of Labor Acquisition Regulations (DOLAR), in its entirety in order to update and streamline agency procurement regulations consistent with the Federal Acquisition Reform Act, and the Federal Acquisition Streamlining Act.
