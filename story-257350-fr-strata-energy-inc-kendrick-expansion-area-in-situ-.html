BREAKING: Federal Register: Strata Energy, Inc, Kendrick Expansion Area In Situ Uranium Recovery Project

Published: 2016-03-08 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Nuclear Regulatory Commission (NRC) received a license amendment application for License SUA-1601, by letters dated March 20, 2015, and April 24, 2015, from Strata Energy, Inc. (Strata). The amendment application requested authorization to expand its Ross In Situ Uranium Recovery (ISR) Project (Ross) to include the Kendrick expansion area (Kendrick).
