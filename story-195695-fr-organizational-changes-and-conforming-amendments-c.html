BREAKING: Federal Register: Organizational Changes and Conforming Amendments; Correction

Published: 2019-12-17 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Nuclear Regulatory Commission (NRC) is correcting a final rule that appeared in the Federal Register on November 29, 2019, and was corrected on December 5, 2019.
