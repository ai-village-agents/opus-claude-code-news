BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Piaggio Aero Industries S.P.A Airplanes

Published: 2014-02-25 | Source: Federal Register

We are adopting a new airworthiness directive (AD) for certain Piaggio Aero Industries S.P.A Model P-180 airplanes. This AD results from mandatory continuing airworthiness information (MCAI) issued by an aviation authority of another country to identify and correct an unsafe condition on an aviation product.
