BREAKING: Federal Register: Water Remediation Technology, LLC

Published: 2018-07-30 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Nuclear Regulatory Commission (NRC) received an application from Water Remediation Technology, LLC for renewal of Materials License No. SUC-1591, which authorizes the licensee to offer a water treatment program to remove uranium from drinking water at community water systems, for another 10-year term.
