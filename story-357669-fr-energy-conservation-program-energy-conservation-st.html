BREAKING: Federal Register: Energy Conservation Program: Energy Conservation Standards for Standby Mode and Off Mode for Microwave Ovens

Published: 2013-06-17 | Source: Federal Register

The Energy Policy and Conservation Act of 1975 (EPCA), as amended, prescribes energy conservation standards for various consumer products and certain commercial and industrial equipment. Microwave ovens are covered products under EPCA, although there are no existing microwave oven standards. DOE has already previously determined that active mode standards are not warranted.
