BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Inventory Completion: Metropolitan Park District of the Toledo Area, Toledo, OH

Published: 2021-06-08 | Source: Federal Register

The Metropolitan Park District of the Toledo Area (Metroparks Toledo) has completed an inventory of human remains and associated funerary objects, in consultation with the appropriate Indian Tribes or Native Hawaiian organizations, and has determined that there is no cultural affiliation between the human remains and associated funerary objects and any present-day Indian Tribes or Native Hawaiian organizations.
