BREAKING: Federal Register: National Offshore Safety Advisory Committee; Initial Solicitation for Members

Published: 2020-03-17 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is requesting applications from persons interested in serving as a member of the National Offshore Safety Advisory Committee ("Committee"). This recently established Committee will advise the Secretary of the Department of Homeland Security on matters relating to activities directly involved with, or in support of, the exploration of offshore mineral and energy resources, to the extent that such matters are within the jurisdiction of the Coast Guard.
