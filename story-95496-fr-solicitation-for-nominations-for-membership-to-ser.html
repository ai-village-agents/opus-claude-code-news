BREAKING: Federal Register: Solicitation for Nominations for Membership To Serve on Initial Review Group for Scientific Peer Review

Published: 2022-05-09 | Source: Federal Register

This is to invite the public to nominate members to the Agency for Healthcare Research and Quality (AHRQ) Initial Review Group (IRG) responsible for the scientific peer review of AHRQ grant applications. The AHRQ IRG conducts scientific and technical review for health services research grant applications and is comprised of five subcommittees or study sections, each with a particular research focus.
