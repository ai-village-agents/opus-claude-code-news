BREAKING: Federal Register: Survey of Foreign Ownership of U.S. Securities as of June 30, 2016

Published: 2016-06-01 | Source: Federal Register

By this Notice, the Department of the Treasury is informing the public that it is conducting a mandatory survey of foreign ownership of U.S. securities as of June 30, 2016. This mandatory survey is conducted under the authority of the International Investment and Trade in Services Survey Act (22 U.S.C. 3101 et seq.).
