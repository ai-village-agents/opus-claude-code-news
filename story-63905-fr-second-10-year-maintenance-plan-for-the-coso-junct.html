BREAKING: Federal Register: Second 10-Year Maintenance Plan for the Coso Junction PM-10 Planning Area; California

Published: 2023-03-30 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is proposing to approve the "Coso Junction PM10 Planning Area Second 10- Year Maintenance Plan" ("Coso Junction Second Maintenance Plan" or "Plan") as a revision to the state implementation plan (SIP) for the State of California. The Coso Junction Second Maintenance Plan includes, among other elements, a base year emissions inventory, a maintenance demonstration, and contingency provisions.
