BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone; Navy Underwater Detonation (UNDET) Exercises, GU

Published: 2018-05-21 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is establishing two recurring safety zones for navigable waters of Apra Outer Harbor and Piti, Guam. The safety zones will encompass sites designated for U.S. Navy underwater detonation (UNDET) exercises. The Coast Guard believes this safety zone regulation is necessary to protect the public and exercise participants within the affected area from possible safety hazards associated with these exercises.
