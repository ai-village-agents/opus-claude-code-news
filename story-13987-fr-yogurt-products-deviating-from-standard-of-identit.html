BREAKING: Federal Register: Yogurt Products Deviating From Standard of Identity; Temporary Permit for Market Testing

Published: 2025-06-18 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA or we) is announcing the amendment to the temporary permit issued to Chobani, LLC, (Chobani) to market test yogurt deviating from the yogurt standard of identity and lower fat yogurt products deviating from the general definition and standard of identity in 21 CFR 130.10 by using ultrafiltered nonfat milk as a basic dairy ingredient.
