BREAKING: Federal Register: Pipeline Safety: Gas and Hazardous Liquid Pipeline Risk Models

Published: 2018-08-30 | Source: Federal Register

PHMSA published a notice in the Federal Register to seek public comments on a report developed to support improvements in gas and hazardous liquid pipeline risk models titled "Pipeline Risk Modeling--Overview of Methods and Tools for Improved Implementation" (Pipeline Risk Modeling Report). PHMSA has received a request to extend the comment period to allow stakeholders more time to evaluate the notice.
