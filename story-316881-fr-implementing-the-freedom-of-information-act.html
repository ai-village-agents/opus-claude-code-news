BREAKING: Federal Register: Implementing the Freedom of Information Act

Published: 2014-02-19 | Source: Federal Register

IMLS issues this final rule to implement the Freedom of Information Act (FOIA), as amended. The regulations both describe how IMLS processes requests for records under FOIA and reaffirm the agency's commitment to providing the fullest possible disclosure of records to the public.
