BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Mooney International Corporation Airplanes

Published: 2021-03-09 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA proposes to adopt a new airworthiness directive (AD) for certain Mooney International Corporation (Mooney) Model M20V airplanes. This proposed AD was prompted by reports of short circuit and arcing of the alternator main power cable in the engine compartment. This condition, if unaddressed, could result in a fire hazard, loss of engine thrust control, and reduced control of the airplane.
