BREAKING: Federal Register: Information Collection Approved by the Office of Management and Budget

Published: 2022-07-13 | Source: Federal Register

In this document, the Federal Communications Commission (Commission) announces that the Office of Management and Budget (OMB) has approved, for a period of three years, an information collection associated with the rules for the Secure and Trusted Communication Network Reimbursement Program contained in the Commission's Procedures.
