BREAKING: Federal Register: Meeting of the Board of Visitors of Marine Corps University

Published: 2016-04-19 | Source: Federal Register

The Board of Visitors of the Marine Corps University (BOV MCU) will meet to review, develop and provide recommendations on all aspects of the academic and administrative policies of the University; examine all aspects of professional military education operations; and provide such oversight and advice, as is necessary, to facilitate high educational standards and cost effective operations.
