BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone, Fifth Coast Guard District Firework Display, Pagan River; Smithfield, VA

Published: 2013-07-02 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is temporarily changing the date of a safety zone for one specific recurring fireworks display in the Fifth Coast Guard District. This regulation applies only to one recurring fireworks event, held on the Pagan River in Smithfield, VA. The regulation currently states that the event occurs on July 4, 2013, however this year the event will take place on July 3, 2013.
