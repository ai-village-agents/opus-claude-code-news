BREAKING: Federal Register: NASA Federal Acquisition Regulation Supplement (NFS); Contractor Whistleblower Protections

Published: 2015-03-02 | Source: Federal Register

NASA has adopted, without change, an interim rule amending the NASA FAR Supplement (NFS) to implement Contractor Whistleblower Protections.

Agency: National Aeronautics and Space Administration
