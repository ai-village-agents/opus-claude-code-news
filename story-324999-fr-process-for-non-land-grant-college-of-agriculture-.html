BREAKING: Federal Register: Process for Non-Land Grant College of Agriculture (NLGCA) Designation

Published: 2014-05-22 | Source: Federal Register

Section 7101 of the Agricultural Act of 2014 requires the National Institute of Food and Agriculture (NIFA) to establish a process through which institutions may apply for designation as a NLGCA. Designation as a NLGCA is one way an institution may qualify to receive an exemption from the new matching fund requirement described in Section 7128 of the Agricultural Act of 2014.
