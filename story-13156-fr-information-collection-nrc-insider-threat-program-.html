BREAKING: Federal Register: Information Collection: NRC Insider Threat Program for Licensees and Other Requiring Access to Classified Information

Published: 2025-06-09 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Nuclear Regulatory Commission (NRC) invites public comment on the renewal of Office of Management and Budget (OMB) approval for an existing collection of information. The information collection is entitled, "NRC Insider Threat Program for Licensees and Other Requiring Access to Classified Information."
