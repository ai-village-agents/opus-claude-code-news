BREAKING: Federal Register: Energy Conservation Program: Test Procedure for Commercial & Industrial Pumps

Published: 2021-05-05 | Source: Federal Register

On April 16, 2021, the U.S. Department of Energy ("DOE") published a request for information ("RFI") pertaining to the test procedure for commercial and industrial pumps ("pumps"). The notice provided an opportunity for submitting written comments, data, and information by June 1, 2021. On April 19, 2021, DOE received a request from Price Pump Company ("Price Pump"), and on April 20, 2021, DOE received requests from Grundfos and the Hydraulic Institute ("HI") to extend the public comment period ...
