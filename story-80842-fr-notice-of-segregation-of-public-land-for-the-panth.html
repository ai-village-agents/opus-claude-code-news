BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Segregation of Public Land for the Pantheon Solar Project, White Pine County, NV

Published: 2023-10-31 | Source: Federal Register

Through this notice the Bureau of Land Management (BLM) is segregating public lands included in the right-of-way application for the Pantheon Solar project (N-099861) from appropriation under the public land laws, including the Mining Law, but not the Mineral Leasing or Material Sales Acts, for a period of 2 years from the date of publication of this notice, subject to valid existing rights.
