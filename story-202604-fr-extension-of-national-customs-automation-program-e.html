BREAKING: Federal Register: Extension of National Customs Automation Program; eBond Test

Published: 2018-03-21 | Source: Federal Register

This document announces the extension of U.S. Customs and Border Protection's (CBP's) National Customs Automation Program (NCAP) test concerning the automation of CBP's bond program (eBond test). CBP announced the eBond test in a Federal Register notice published on November 28, 2014. The test program has run continuously and without interruption since it commenced on January 3, 2015, and continues to run currently.
