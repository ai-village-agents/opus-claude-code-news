BREAKING: Federal Register: Next Meeting of the North American Numbering Council

Published: 2019-04-29 | Source: Federal Register

In this document, the Commission released a public notice announcing the meeting of the North American Numbering Council (NANC). At this meeting, which will be conducted by conference call for those unable to attend in person, the NANC will consider and vote on reports from its Nationwide Number Portability Issues Working Group and its Numbering Administration Oversight Working Group.
