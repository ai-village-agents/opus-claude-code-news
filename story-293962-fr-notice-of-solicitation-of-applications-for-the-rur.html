BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Solicitation of Applications for the Rural Community Development Initiative for Fiscal Year 2015

Published: 2015-05-15 | Source: Federal Register

The Rural Housing Service (RHS), an agency within the USDA Rural Development mission area herein referred to as the Agency announces the acceptance of applications under the Rural Community Development Initiative (RCDI) program. Applicants must provide matching funds in an amount at least equal to the Federal grant.
