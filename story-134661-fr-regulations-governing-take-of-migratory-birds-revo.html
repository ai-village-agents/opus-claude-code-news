BREAKING: Federal Register: Regulations Governing Take of Migratory Birds; Revocation of Provisions

Published: 2021-10-04 | Source: Federal Register

On January 7, 2021, we, the U.S. Fish and Wildlife Service (we, the Service, or USFWS), published a final rule (January 7 rule) defining the scope of the Migratory Bird Treaty Act (MBTA) as it applies to conduct resulting in the injury or death of migratory birds protected by the MBTA. We now revoke that rule for the reasons set forth below.
