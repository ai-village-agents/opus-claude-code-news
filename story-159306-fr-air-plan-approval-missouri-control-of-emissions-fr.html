BREAKING: Federal Register: Air Plan Approval; Missouri; Control of Emissions From Production of Pesticides and Herbicides

Published: 2020-08-21 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is taking final action to approve a revision to the State Implementation Plan (SIP) for its rule related to control of emissions from production of pesticides and herbicides in the Kansas City area. This final action will amend the SIP to remove certain provisions from the rule, consolidate requirements, include incorporations by reference and revise restrictive language.
