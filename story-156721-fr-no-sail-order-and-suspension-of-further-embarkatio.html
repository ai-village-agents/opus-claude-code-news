BREAKING: Federal Register: No Sail Order and Suspension of Further Embarkation; Second Modification and Extension of No Sail Order and Other Measures Related to Operations

Published: 2020-07-21 | Source: Federal Register

The Centers for Disease Control and Prevention (CDC), a component of the U.S. Department of Health and Human Services (HHS), announces a second modification and extension of the No Sail Order and Other Measures Related to Operations that was issued on April 15, 2020.
