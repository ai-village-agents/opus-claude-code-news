BREAKING: Federal Register: Methomyl; Cancellation Order for Amendments to Terminate Uses

This notice announces EPA's order for the amendments to terminate uses, voluntarily requested by the registrants and accepted by the Agency, of products containing methomyl, pursuant to the Federal Insecticide, Fungicide, and Rodenticide Act (FIFRA).
