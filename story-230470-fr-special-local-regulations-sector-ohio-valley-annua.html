BREAKING: Federal Register: Special Local Regulations; Sector Ohio Valley Annual and Recurring Special Local Regulations Update

Published: 2017-03-27 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is amending and updating its special local regulations relating to recurring marine parades, regattas, and other events that take place in the Coast Guard Sector Ohio Valley area of responsibility (AOR). This notice informs the public of regularly scheduled events that require additional safety measures through establishing a special local regulation.
