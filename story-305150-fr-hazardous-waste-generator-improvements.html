BREAKING: Federal Register: Hazardous Waste Generator Improvements

Published: 2015-09-25 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA or the Agency) is proposing to revise the hazardous waste generator regulations under the Resource Conservation and Recovery Act (RCRA) to improve compliance and thereby enhance protection of human health and the environment.
