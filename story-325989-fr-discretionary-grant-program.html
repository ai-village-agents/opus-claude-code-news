BREAKING: Federal Register: Discretionary Grant Program

Published: 2014-06-04 | Source: Federal Register

HRSA will be issuing non-competitive awards under the F2F HIC program. Approximately $5 million will be made available in the form of a grant to current grantees (see below) covering the period of 6/1/ 2014-5/31/2015.
