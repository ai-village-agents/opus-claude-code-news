BREAKING: Federal Register: National Dairy Promotion and Research Program; National Dairy Promotion and Research Board Importer Representation

Published: 2020-08-05 | Source: Federal Register

This document maintains the number of National Dairy Promotion and Research Board (Dairy Board) importer members at one member, as is stated in the Dairy Promotion and Research Order (Dairy Order). The Dairy Order requires that at least once every three years, after the initial appointment of importer members on the Dairy Board, the Secretary shall review the average volume of domestic production of dairy products compared to the average volume of imports of dairy products into the United States...
