BREAKING: Federal Register: Highway Safety Improvement Program

Published: 2014-05-28 | Source: Federal Register

The FHWA is extending the comment period for a notice of proposed rulemaking (NPRM) and request for comments, which was published on March 28, 2014. The original comment period is set to close on May 27, 2014.
