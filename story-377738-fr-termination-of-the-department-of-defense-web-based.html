BREAKING: Federal Register: Termination of the Department of Defense Web-Based TRICARE Assistance Program Demonstration

Published: 2012-02-15 | Source: Federal Register

This notice is to advise interested parties of the termination of the Military Health System (MHS) demonstration project, under authority of Title 10, U.S. Code, Section 1092, entitled Web-Based TRICARE Assistance Program (TRIAP).
