BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Proposed Rulemaking: Definitions of Transmittal of Funds and Funds Transfer

Published: 2012-12-06 | Source: Federal Register

The Financial Crimes Enforcement Network (FinCEN), a bureau of the Department of the Treasury, and the Board of Governors of the Federal Reserve System (Board) are proposing amendments to the regulatory definitions of "funds transfer" and "transmittal of funds" under the regulations implementing the Bank Secrecy Act.
