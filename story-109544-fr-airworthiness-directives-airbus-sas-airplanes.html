BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Airbus SAS Airplanes

Published: 2022-11-08 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA is adopting a new airworthiness directive (AD) for certain Airbus SAS Model A350-941 and A350-1041 airplanes. This AD was prompted by a determination that in the event of rapid decompression at a specific location of the airplane, possible deflections of the passenger floor cross beams may result in wiring damages, leading to potential system losses.
