BREAKING: Federal Register: Pipeline Safety: Requests for Special Permit

Published: 2012-06-11 | Source: Federal Register

Pursuant to the Federal pipeline safety laws, PHMSA is publishing this notice of special permit requests we have received from Norgasco, Inc., and BreitBurn Energy Company LP, two natural gas pipeline operators, seeking relief from compliance with certain requirements in the Federal pipeline safety regulations.
