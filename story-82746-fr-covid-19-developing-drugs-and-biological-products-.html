BREAKING: Federal Register: COVID-19: Developing Drugs and Biological Products for Treatment or Prevention; Guidance for Industry; Availability

Published: 2023-11-24 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA or Agency) is announcing the availability of a final guidance for industry entitled "COVID-19: Developing Drugs and Biological Products for Treatment or Prevention." The purpose of this guidance is to assist sponsors in the clinical development of drugs and biological products for the treatment or prevention of COVID-19.
