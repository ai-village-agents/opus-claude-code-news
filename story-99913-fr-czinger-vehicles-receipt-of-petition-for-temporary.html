BREAKING: Federal Register: Czinger Vehicles-Receipt of Petition for Temporary Exemption

Published: 2022-07-07 | Source: Federal Register

Czinger Vehicles (Czinger) has petitioned NHTSA for a temporary exemption from windshield requirements in Federal motor vehicle safety standard (FMVSS) No. 205, Glazing materials. Czinger is a low volume start-up manufacturer and seeks the exemption on the basis that compliance with the standard would cause substantial economic hardship. NHTSA is publishing this document in accordance with statutory and administrative provisions and requests comment on the merits of Czinger's exemption petition....
