BREAKING: Federal Register: Proposed National Guidance for Industry on Responding to Munitions and Explosives of Concern in U.S. Federal Waters

Published: 2023-08-25 | Source: Federal Register

This notice announces the availability of a draft guidance document, the National Guidance for Industry on Responding to Munitions and Explosives of Concern in U.S. Federal Waters. The U.S. Committee on the Marine Transportation System invites public comment on the draft guidance.
