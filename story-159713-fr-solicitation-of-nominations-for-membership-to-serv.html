BREAKING: Federal Register: Solicitation of Nominations for Membership To Serve on Tribal Advisory Council

Published: 2020-08-27 | Source: Federal Register

HRSA is seeking additional nominations of qualified tribal officials as candidates for consideration for appointment as voluntary delegate members of the HRSA Tribal Advisory Council (TAC), which is being established. Specifically, HRSA requests submissions of nominations of qualified tribal officials from the Indian Health Service (IHS) geographic areas of: Alaska; Albuquerque; Billings; Navajo; Phoenix; and Tucson.
