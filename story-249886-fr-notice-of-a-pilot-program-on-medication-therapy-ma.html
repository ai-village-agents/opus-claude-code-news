BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of a Pilot Program on Medication Therapy Management Under the TRICARE Program

Published: 2017-12-01 | Source: Federal Register

Per Section 726 of the National Defense Authorization Act (NDAA) for Fiscal Year (FY) 2015, the Department of Defense (DoD) is implementing a 2-year Pilot Program, "Pilot Program on Medication Therapy Management Under TRICARE Program".
