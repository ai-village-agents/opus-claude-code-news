BREAKING: Federal Register: Methodology and Formulas for Allocation of Loan and Grant Program Funds

Published: 2014-03-18 | Source: Federal Register

The Rural Business-Cooperative Service (RBS) is proposing to amend its regulations found in 7 CFR part 1940, subpart L for allocating program funds to its State Offices. RBS is proposing to amend 7 CFR part 1940, subpart L to add three programs--the Rural Energy for America Program, the Value-Added Producer Grant program, and the Intermediary Relending Program.
