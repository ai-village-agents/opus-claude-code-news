BREAKING: Federal Register: Information Session; Implementation of the Water Infrastructure Finance and Innovation Act of 2014

Published: 2017-10-31 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is announcing plans to hold information sessions on: November 8, 2017 in Denver, Colorado; November 16, 2017 in Nashville, Tennessee; and November 29, 2017 in Lenexa, Kansas. In addition, EPA will host a series of webinars covering the same topics on December 6, 13, and 20, 2017.
