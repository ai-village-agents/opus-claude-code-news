BREAKING: Federal Register: Certain Polyester Staple Fiber From the People's Republic of China: Preliminary Results of the Antidumping Duty Administrative Review; 2012-2013

Published: 2014-07-25 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Commerce ("the Department") is conducting an administrative review of the antidumping duty order on certain polyester staple fiber from the People's Republic of China ("PRC"). The period of review ("POR") is June 1, 2012, through May 31, 2013. The Department preliminarily determines that Takayasu Industrial (Jiangyin) Co., Ltd. ("Takayasu") did not sell subject merchandise in the United States at prices below normal value ("NV") during the POR June 1, 2012, through May 31, 2013...
