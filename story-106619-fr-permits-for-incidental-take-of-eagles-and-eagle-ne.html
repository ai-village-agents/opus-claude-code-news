BREAKING: Federal Register: Permits for Incidental Take of Eagles and Eagle Nests

Published: 2022-09-30 | Source: Federal Register

We, the U.S. Fish and Wildlife Service (Service or USFWS), propose the following revisions to regulations authorizing the issuance of permits for eagle incidental take and eagle nest take. The purpose of these revisions is to increase the efficiency and effectiveness of permitting, facilitate and improve compliance, and increase the conservation benefit for eagles.
