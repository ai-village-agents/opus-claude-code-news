BREAKING: Federal Register: Citizens Coinage Advisory Committee Meeting

Published: 2018-11-07 | Source: Federal Register

The United States Mint announces the Citizens Coinage Advisory Committee (CCAC) public meeting scheduled for November 14, 2018. Date: November 14, 2018. Time: 1:00 p.m. to 2:30 p.m. Location: 8th Floor Board Room, United States Mint, 801 9th Street NW, Washington, DC 20220. Subject: Review and discussion of candidate designs for the American Veterans Medal. Interested members of the public may either attend the meeting in person or dial in to listen to the meeting at (866) 564-9287/Access Code: ...
