BREAKING: Federal Register: Unique Entity ID Standard for Awards Management

Published: 2019-07-10 | Source: Federal Register

Notice of this new Unique Entity ID for Federal awards management includes the final technical specification for the identifier standard. IAE is hosting a meeting to provide information on the new Unique Entity ID standard. The meeting is open to current and potential federal awardees (contracts, grants, loan recipients, etc.) and the public.
