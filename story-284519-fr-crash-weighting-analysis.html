BREAKING: Federal Register: Crash Weighting Analysis

Published: 2015-01-23 | Source: Federal Register

FMCSA announces a study to inform decision making about the feasibility of using a motor carrier's role in crashes as an indicator of future crash risk in response to stakeholder interest and as part of the Agency's commitment to continuous improvement.
