BREAKING: Federal Register: World Radiocommunication Conference Advisory Committee Schedules Its Fourth Meeting on February 19, 2026

Published: 2026-01-26 | Source: Federal Register

This notice advises interested persons that the fourth meeting of the World Radiocommunication Conference Advisory Committee (WRC Advisory Committee) will be held on February 19, 2026, at the Federal Communications Commission (FCC). This fourth meeting of the WRC Advisory Committee will consider status reports and recommendations from its Informal Working Groups (IWG) concerning preparation for the 2027 World Radiocommunication Conference (WRC-27).
