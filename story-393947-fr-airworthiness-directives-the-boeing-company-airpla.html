BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; The Boeing Company Airplanes

Published: 2012-08-16 | Source: Federal Register

We propose to supersede an existing airworthiness directive (AD) that applies to certain The Boeing Company Model 757 airplanes equipped with Rolls-Royce RB211-535E engines. The existing AD currently requires repetitive inspections for signs of damage of the aft hinge fittings and attachment bolts of the thrust reversers, and related investigative and corrective actions if necessary.
