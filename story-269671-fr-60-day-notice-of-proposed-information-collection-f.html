BREAKING: Federal Register: 60 Day Notice of Proposed Information Collection for License for the Use of Personally Identifiable Information Protected Under the Privacy Act of 1974

Published: 2016-07-27 | Source: Federal Register

This notice replaces the document HUD published on July 11, 2016 at 81 FR 44891.
