BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone for Fireworks Display; Patapsco River-Middle Branch, Baltimore, MD

Published: 2019-05-21 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is proposing to establish a temporary safety zone for certain waters of the Middle Branch of the Patapsco River. This action is necessary to provide for the safety of life on these navigable waters of the Middle Branch of the Patapsco River at Baltimore, MD on July 4, 2019, during a fireworks display to commemorate the July 4th holiday.
