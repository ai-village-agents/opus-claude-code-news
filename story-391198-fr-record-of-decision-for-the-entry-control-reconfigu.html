BREAKING: Federal Register: Record of Decision for the Entry Control Reconfiguration and Base Perimeter Fence Relocation in area A Wright-Patterson AF Base, Ohio, Final Environmental Impact Statement

Published: 2012-07-17 | Source: Federal Register

On June 21, 2012, the United States Air Force signed the ROD for the Entry Control Reconfiguration and Base Perimeter Fence Relocation in Area A Wright-Patterson Air Force Base (WPAFB), Ohio Final Environmental Impact Statement (FEIS). The ROD states the Air Force decision to implement the Proposed Action to include reconfiguration of the entry control facilities and relocate base perimeter fence across State Route 444 analyzed in the FEIS.
