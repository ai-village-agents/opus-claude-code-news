BREAKING: Federal Register: Revisions of the Section 232 Steel and Aluminum Tariff Exclusions Process

Published: 2023-08-28 | Source: Federal Register

This proposed rule revises aspects of the process for requesting exclusions from the duties and quantitative limitations on imports of aluminum and steel discussed in five previous Bureau of Industry and Security ("BIS") interim final rules implementing the exclusion process authorized by the President under section 232 of the Trade Expansion Act of 1962, as amended ("Section 232").
