BREAKING: Federal Register: Fisheries of the Exclusive Economic Zone Off Alaska; At-Sea Scales Requirements

Published: 2014-04-29 | Source: Federal Register

NMFS announces a workshop to solicit input from owners and operators of catcher/processor vessels (C/Ps) and motherships that are required to weigh catch at sea. The workshop concerns proposed changes to equipment and operational requirements for motion compensating scales that weigh catch at sea.
