BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Inventory Completion: The Michigan State University Museum, East Lansing, MI

Published: 2013-09-30 | Source: Federal Register

The Michigan State University Museum (MSUM) has completed an inventory of human remains, in consultation with the appropriate Indian tribes or Native Hawaiian organizations, and has determined that there is no cultural affiliation between the human remains and any present- day Indian tribes or Native Hawaiian organizations.
