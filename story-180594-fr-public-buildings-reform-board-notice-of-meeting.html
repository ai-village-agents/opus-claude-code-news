BREAKING: Federal Register: Public Buildings Reform Board; Notice of Meeting

Published: 2019-06-03 | Source: Federal Register

As provided by the Federal Assets Sale and Transfer Act of 2016 (FASTA), the Public Buildings Reform Board (PBRB) is holding its first public meeting. At this meeting, the Board will hear from government and private sector representatives to gain their perspectives on implementation of FASTA and FASTA's opportunities and challenges.
