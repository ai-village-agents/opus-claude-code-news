BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Inventory Completion: Texas Archeological Research Laboratory, Austin, TX

Published: 2016-07-11 | Source: Federal Register

The Texas Archeological Research Laboratory (TARL) has completed an inventory of human remains and associated funerary objects, in consultation with the appropriate Indian tribes or Native Hawaiian organizations, and has determined that there is no cultural affiliation between the human remains and associated funerary objects and present-day Indian tribes or Native Hawaiian organizations.
