BREAKING: Federal Register: Hispanic Council on Federal Employment

Published: 2016-05-13 | Source: Federal Register

The Hispanic Council on Federal Employment (Council) meeting will be held on Wednesday, June 29th at the location shown below from 10:00 a.m. to 11:30 a.m. The Council is an advisory committee composed of representatives from Hispanic organizations and senior government officials. Along with its other responsibilities, the Council shall advise the Director of the Office of Personnel Management on matters involving the recruitment, hiring, and advancement of Hispanics in the Federal workforce.
