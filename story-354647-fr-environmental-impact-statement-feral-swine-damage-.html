BREAKING: Federal Register: Environmental Impact Statement; Feral Swine Damage Management

Published: 2013-05-13 | Source: Federal Register

We are advising the public that the Animal and Plant Health Inspection Service plans to prepare an environmental impact statement (EIS) to address the need for a national feral swine damage management program to protect agriculture, natural resources, property, and human health and safety.
