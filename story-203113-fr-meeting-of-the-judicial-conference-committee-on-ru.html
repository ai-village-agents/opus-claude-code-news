BREAKING: Federal Register: Meeting of The Judicial Conference; Committee on Rules of Practice and Procedure

Published: 2018-03-28 | Source: Federal Register

The Committee on Rules of Practice and Procedure will hold a meeting on June 12, 2018. The meeting will be open to public observation but not participation. An agenda and supporting materials will be posted at least 7 days in advance of the meeting at: http:// www.uscourts.gov/rules-policies/records-and-archives-rules-committees/ agenda-books.
