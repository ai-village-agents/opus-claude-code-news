BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; the Boeing Company Airplanes

Published: 2015-01-06 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA withdraws a notice of proposed rulemaking (NPRM) that proposed to revise Airworthiness Directive (AD) 2012-12-15, which applies to all The Boeing Company Model 757 airplanes.
