BREAKING: Federal Register: OSHA Training Institute Education Center; Notice of Competition and Request for Applications

Published: 2012-04-13 | Source: Federal Register

This notice announces the opportunity for interested non- profit organizations, including qualifying trade associations, labor unions, and community-based and faith-based organizations that are not an agency of a state or local government to submit applications to become an OSHA Training Institute Education Center and deliver standard classroom instruction on a regional basis.
