BREAKING: Federal Register: Privacy Act of 1974; System of Records

Published: 2024-04-09 | Source: Federal Register

In accordance with the Privacy Act of 1974, the Office of the Secretary of Defense is modifying and reissuing a current system of records titled, "Joint Duty Assignment Management Information System (JDAMIS)," DMDC 05. The records are used for the purpose of tracking military officers in joint duty assignments and to document Joint Qualified Officer levels.
