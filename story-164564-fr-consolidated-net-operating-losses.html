BREAKING: Federal Register: Consolidated Net Operating Losses

Published: 2020-10-27 | Source: Federal Register

This document contains final regulations under sections 1502 and 1503 of the Internal Revenue Code (Code). These regulations provide guidance implementing recent statutory amendments to section 172 of the Code relating to the absorption of consolidated net operating loss (CNOL) carryovers and carrybacks.
