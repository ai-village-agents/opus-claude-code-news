BREAKING: Federal Register: Revision to the Research, Development and Demonstration Permits Rule for Municipal Solid Waste Landfills

Published: 2016-05-10 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is publishing a final rule to revise the maximum permit term for Municipal Solid Waste Landfill (MSWLF) units operating under Research, Development and Demonstration (RD&D) permits.
