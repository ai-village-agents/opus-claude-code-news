BREAKING: Federal Register: Tentative Determination To Approve Site Specific Flexibility for Closure and Monitoring of the Picacho Landfill

Published: 2016-04-07 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency, Region IX, is making a tentative determination to approve two Site Specific Flexibility Requests (SSFRs) from Imperial County (County or Imperial County) to close and monitor the Picacho Solid Waste Landfill (Picacho Landfill or Landfill). The Picacho Landfill is a commercial municipal solid waste landfill (MSWLF) operated by Imperial County from 1977 to the present on the Quechan Indian Tribe of the Fort Yuma Indian Reservation in California.
