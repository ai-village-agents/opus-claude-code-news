BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Availability of Funds and Solicitation for Grant Applications for Pay for Success Pilot Projects

Published: 2012-06-15 | Source: Federal Register

The Employment and Training Administration (ETA), U.S. Department of Labor, announces the availability of approximately $20 million in Pay for Success grants, funded out of the Workforce Innovation Fund in the Department of Labor Appropriations Act, 2012 (Pub. L. 112-74, Div. F, Tit. I).
