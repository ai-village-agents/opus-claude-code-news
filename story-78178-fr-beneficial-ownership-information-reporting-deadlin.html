BREAKING: Federal Register: Beneficial Ownership Information Reporting Deadline Extension for Reporting Companies Created or Registered in 2024

Published: 2023-09-28 | Source: Federal Register

FinCEN is proposing to amend the beneficial ownership information (BOI) reporting rule (Reporting Rule) to extend the filing deadline for certain BOI reports. Under the Reporting Rule, entities created or registered on or after the rule's effective date of January 1, 2024, must file initial BOI reports with FinCEN within 30 days of notice of their creation or registration.
