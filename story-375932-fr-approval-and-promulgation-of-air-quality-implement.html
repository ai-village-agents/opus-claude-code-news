BREAKING: Federal Register: Approval and Promulgation of Air Quality Implementation Plans; Indiana; Regional Haze

Published: 2012-01-26 | Source: Federal Register

EPA is proposing a limited approval of revisions to the Indiana State Implementation Plan (SIP) addressing regional haze for the first implementation period. Indiana submitted its regional haze plan on January 14, 2011, and supplemented it on March 10, 2011.
