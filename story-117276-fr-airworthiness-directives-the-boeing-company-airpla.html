BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; The Boeing Company Airplanes

Published: 2021-02-23 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA is superseding Airworthiness Directive (AD) 2019-02- 03, which applied to all The Boeing Company Model 787-8, 787-9, and 787-10 airplanes. AD 2019-02-03 required revising the existing maintenance or inspection program, as applicable, to incorporate new or more restrictive airworthiness limitations.
