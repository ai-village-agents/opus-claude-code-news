BREAKING: Federal Register: Revised Geographic Eligibility for Federal Office of Rural Health Policy Grants

Published: 2020-09-23 | Source: Federal Register

HRSA's Federal Office of Rural Health Policy (FORHP) has sought to identify clear, consistent, and data-driven methods of defining rural areas in the United States. FORHP uses the Office of Management and Budget (OMB)'s list of counties designated as part of a Metropolitan Statistical Area (MSA) as the basis for determining eligibility to apply for or receive services funded by its rural health grant programs.
