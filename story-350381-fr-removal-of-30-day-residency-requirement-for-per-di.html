BREAKING: Federal Register: Removal of 30-Day Residency Requirement for Per Diem Payments

Published: 2013-03-26 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Veterans Affairs (VA) published a direct final rule amending its regulations concerning per diem payments to State homes for the provision of nursing home care to veterans. Specifically, this rule removes the requirement that a veteran must have resided in a State home for 30 consecutive days before VA will pay per diem for that veteran when there is no overnight stay.
