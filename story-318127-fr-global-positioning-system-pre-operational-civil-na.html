BREAKING: Federal Register: Global Positioning System Pre-Operational Civil Navigation; Message Continuous Broadcast

Published: 2014-03-05 | Source: Federal Register

The purpose of this notice is to seek comment from the public and industry regarding plans by the United States Air Force to broadcast pre-operational L2C and L5 civil navigation (CNAV) messages from certain Global Positioning System (GPS) satellites beginning in April 2014. These messages will be formatted in accordance with Interface Specifications IS-GPS-200G and IS-GPS-705C, each dated 31 Jan 2013.
