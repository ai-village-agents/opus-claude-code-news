BREAKING: Federal Register: Bell Bend, LLC; Combined License Application for Bell Bend Nuclear Power Plant

Published: 2015-12-24 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Nuclear Regulatory Commission (NRC) is issuing an exemption in a response to an October 7, 2015, letter from Bell Bend, LLC, which requested an exemption from Final Safety Analysis Report (FSAR) updates included in their combined license (COL) application.
