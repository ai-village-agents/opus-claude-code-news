BREAKING: Federal Register: Drafting the NIST Privacy Framework: Workshop #2

Published: 2019-05-03 | Source: Federal Register

The National Institute of Standards and Technology (NIST) will be hosting the second in a series of public workshops on the development of the Privacy Framework: An Enterprise Risk Management Tool ("Privacy Framework") on May 13-14, 2019 in Atlanta, Georgia. In this two-day event, attendees will have an opportunity to actively engage in facilitated discussions to advance the development of the Privacy Framework.
