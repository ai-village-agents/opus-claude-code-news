BREAKING: Federal Register: Authorization of Radiofrequency Equipment

Published: 2013-05-03 | Source: Federal Register

This document proposes certain changes to the Commission's equipment authorization processes to ensure that they continue to operate efficiently and effectively. In particular, it addresses the role of TCBs in certifying RF equipment and post-market surveillance, as well as the Commission's role in assessing TCB performance.
