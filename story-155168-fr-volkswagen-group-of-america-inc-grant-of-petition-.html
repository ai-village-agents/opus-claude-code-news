BREAKING: Federal Register: Volkswagen Group of America, Inc., Grant of Petition for Decision of Inconsequential Noncompliance

Published: 2020-07-01 | Source: Federal Register

Volkswagen Group of America, Inc. (Volkswagen), has determined that certain MY 2017-2019 Audi A3 motor vehicles do not comply with Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard (FMVSS) No. 101, Controls and Displays. Volkswagen filed a noncompliance report dated February 18, 2019, and later amended it on September 13, 2019.
