BREAKING: Federal Register: Unlicensed National Information Infrastructure (U-NII) Devices in the 5 GHz Band

Published: 2013-04-10 | Source: Federal Register

This document proposes to amend the Commission's rules governing the operation of Unlicensed National Information Infrastructure (U-NII) devices in the 5 GHz band. The Commission has gained much experience with U-NII devices since it first made spectrum available in the 5 GHz band for U-NII in 1997. The Commission believes that the time is now right to revisit the rules.
