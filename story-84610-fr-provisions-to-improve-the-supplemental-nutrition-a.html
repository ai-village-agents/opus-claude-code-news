BREAKING: Federal Register: Provisions To Improve the Supplemental Nutrition Assistance Program's Quality Control System

Published: 2023-12-19 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Department of Agriculture proposed to make changes to the Supplemental Nutrition Assistance Program's Quality Control (SNAP QC) system to strengthen and improve the integrity and accuracy of the system and to better align SNAP QC with requirements in the Payment Integrity Information Act of 2019 (PIIA).
