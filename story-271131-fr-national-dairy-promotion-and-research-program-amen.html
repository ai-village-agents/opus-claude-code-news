BREAKING: Federal Register: National Dairy Promotion and Research Program; Amendments to the Order

Published: 2016-08-12 | Source: Federal Register

This final rule amends the Dairy Promotion and Research Order (Dairy Order). The amendment modifies the number of National Dairy Promotion and Research Board (Dairy Board) importer members. The total number of importer members would be reduced from 2 members to 1 member, and the domestic Dairy Board members would remain the same at 36.
