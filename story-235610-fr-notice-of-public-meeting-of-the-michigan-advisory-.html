BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Public Meeting of the Michigan Advisory Committee for New Committee Member Orientation and To Discuss Civil Rights Concerns in Michigan as Potential Topics of Committee Study

Published: 2017-06-01 | Source: Federal Register

Notice is hereby given, pursuant to the provisions of the rules and regulations of the U.S. Commission on Civil Rights (Commission) and the Federal Advisory Committee Act that the Michigan Advisory Committee (Committee) will hold a meeting on Wednesday, June 21, 2017, at 12pm EST for the purpose of hosting new Committee orientation and discussing civil rights concerns in the state.
