BREAKING: Federal Register: Radio Broadcasting Services; Big Coppitt Key, Florida

Published: 2022-09-02 | Source: Federal Register

This document amends the FM Table of Allotments, of the Commission's rules, by adding Channel 265C3 at Big Coppitt Key, Florida. Channel 265C3 would provide a first local service at Big Coppitt Key, Florida. A staff engineering analysis indicates that Channel 265C3 can be allotted to Big Coppitt Key, Florida, consistent with the minimum distance separation requirements of the Commission's rules, with a site restriction of 14.5 km (9.0 miles) northeast of the community.
