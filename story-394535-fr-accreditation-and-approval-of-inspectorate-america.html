BREAKING: Federal Register: Accreditation and Approval of Inspectorate America Corporation, as a Commercial Gauger and Laboratory

Published: 2012-08-23 | Source: Federal Register

Notice is hereby given that, pursuant to 19 CFR 151.12 and 19 CFR 151.13, Inspectorate America Corporation, 2184 Jefferson Hwy, Lutcher, LA 70071, has been approved to gauge and accredited to test petroleum and petroleum products, organic chemicals and vegetable oils for customs purposes, in accordance with the provisions of 19 CFR 151.12 and 19 CFR 151.13.
