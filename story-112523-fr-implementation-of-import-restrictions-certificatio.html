BREAKING: Federal Register: Implementation of Import Restrictions; Certification of Admissibility for Certain Fish Products From New Zealand

Published: 2022-12-16 | Source: Federal Register

Under the authority of the Marine Mammal Protection Act (MMPA), and pursuant to a court order, the NMFS Assistant Administrator for Fisheries (Assistant Administrator) has implemented import restrictions on the products harvested in certain Government of New Zealand (GNZ) regulated fisheries: West Coast North Island multi- species set-net fishery, and West Coast North Island multi-species trawl fishery.
