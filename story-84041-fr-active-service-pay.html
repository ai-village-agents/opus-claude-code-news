BREAKING: Federal Register: Active Service Pay

Published: 2023-12-12 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Veterans Affairs (VA) amends its adjudication regulations to permit VA to adjust disability compensation payments under certain circumstances upon receipt of notice from the Department of Defense (DoD) that the veteran has received or is receiving active service pay.
