BREAKING: Federal Register: Address Quality Census Measurement and Assessment Process

Published: 2017-10-24 | Source: Federal Register

The Postal Service is revising Mailing Standards of the United States Postal Service, Domestic Mail Manual (DMM[supreg]), to include a new method of Move Update verification and assessment procedure for evaluating address quality, the "Address Quality Census Measurement and Assessment Process."
