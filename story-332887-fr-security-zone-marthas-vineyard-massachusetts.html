BREAKING: Federal Register: Security Zone: Martha's Vineyard, Massachusetts

Published: 2014-08-22 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is establishing two 1000-yard temporary security zones around the President of the United States (POTUS) and/or the first family in conjunction with their visit to Martha's Vineyard, Massachusetts. Vessels and people will be prohibited from entering these security zones during the effective period.
