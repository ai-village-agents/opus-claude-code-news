BREAKING: Federal Register: Differential Privacy Methodology for County Business Patterns Data

Published: 2023-04-03 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Census Bureau (Census Bureau) has been working to implement modernized methods to continue to ensure the privacy protections of its information products and seeks public engagement and comment on these efforts. The Census Bureau is targeting the release the 2022 County Business Patterns (CBP) data using differential privacy methodology for disclosure avoidance.
