BREAKING: Federal Register: Energy Conservation Program: Energy Conservation Standards for Dishwashers

Published: 2012-10-01 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Department of Energy (DOE) published a direct final rule to establish amended energy conservation standards for dishwashers in the Federal Register on May 30, 2012. DOE has determined that the adverse comments received in response to the direct final rule were not sufficiently "adverse" as to provide a reasonable basis for withdrawing the direct final rule. Therefore, DOE provides this document confirming adoption of the energy conservation standards established in the direct final rule...
