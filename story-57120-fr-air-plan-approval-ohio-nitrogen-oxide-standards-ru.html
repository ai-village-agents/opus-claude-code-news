BREAKING: Federal Register: Air Plan Approval; Ohio; Nitrogen Oxide Standards Rules

Published: 2024-12-30 | Source: Federal Register

Ohio EPA requested that EPA approve the revised rules for nitrogen oxide standards in the Ohio Administrative Code (OAC) into Ohio's SIP.
