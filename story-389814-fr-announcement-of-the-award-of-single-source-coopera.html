BREAKING: Federal Register: Announcement of the Award of Single-Source Cooperative Agreement to Rubicon Programs, Inc., in Richmond, CA

Published: 2012-06-28 | Source: Federal Register

The Administration for Children and Families (ACF), Office of Family Assistance (OFA), Division of State and Territory TANF Management (DSTTM) announces the award of a single-source cooperative agreement of $1,500,000 to Rubicon Programs, Inc., in Richmond, CA. The cooperative agreement will support a demonstration pilot project for responsible fatherhood activities authorized by the Claims Resolution Act of 2010 (Pub. L. 111-291).
