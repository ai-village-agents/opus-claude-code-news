BREAKING: Federal Register: Gifford Pinchot National Forest; Washington; Spirit Lake Outflow Safety Improvement Project

Published: 2024-09-30 | Source: Federal Register

The USDA Forest Service is preparing an environmental impact statement for the Spirit Lake Outflow Safety Improvement Project (Project). Spirit Lake is within the Mount St. Helens National Volcanic Monument within the Gifford Pinchot National Forest, about 52 miles northeast of Portland, Oregon. The purpose of the Project is to provide for the safety of downstream communities by reducing the risk of flooding and mudflows from a failure of the Spirit Lake debris blockage.
