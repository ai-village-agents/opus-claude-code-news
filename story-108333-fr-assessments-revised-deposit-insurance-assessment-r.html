BREAKING: Federal Register: Assessments, Revised Deposit Insurance Assessment Rates

Published: 2022-10-24 | Source: Federal Register

The FDIC is adopting a final rule to increase initial base deposit insurance assessment rate schedules by 2 basis points, beginning the first quarterly assessment period of 2023.
