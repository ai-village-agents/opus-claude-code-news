BREAKING: Federal Register: Special Conditions: Boeing Model 787 Series Airplanes; Seats With Inertia Locking Devices

Published: 2019-04-29 | Source: Federal Register

This action proposes special conditions for Boeing Model 787 series airplanes. These airplanes will have a novel or unusual design feature when compared to the state of technology envisioned in the airworthiness standards for transport-category airplanes. This design feature is seats with inertia locking devices. The applicable airworthiness regulations do not contain adequate or appropriate safety standards for this design feature.
