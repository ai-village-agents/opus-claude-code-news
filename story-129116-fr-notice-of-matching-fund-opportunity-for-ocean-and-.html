BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Matching Fund Opportunity for Ocean and Coastal Mapping and Request for Partnership Proposals

Published: 2021-07-27 | Source: Federal Register

This notice establishes selection criteria and requirements for the NOAA Rear Admiral Richard T. Brennan Ocean Mapping Matching Fund program, to be known as the Brennan Matching Fund. The purpose of this notice is to encourage non-Federal entities to partner with the NOAA National Ocean Service ocean and coastal mapping programs on jointly funded ocean and coastal surveys and related activities of mutual interest.
