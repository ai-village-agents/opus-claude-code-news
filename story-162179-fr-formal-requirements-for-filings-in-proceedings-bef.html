BREAKING: Federal Register: Formal Requirements for Filings in Proceedings Before the Commission

Published: 2020-09-28 | Source: Federal Register

In this order, the Federal Energy Regulatory Commission (Commission) acknowledges a request for clarification of Order No. 862 or, in the alternative, rehearing of that Order. Order No. 862 amended the Commission's regulations to require that the filings and submissions to be delivered to the Commission, other than by the United States Postal Service, are instead to be sent to the Commission's off-site security screening facility.
