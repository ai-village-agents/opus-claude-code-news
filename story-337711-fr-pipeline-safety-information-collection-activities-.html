BREAKING: Federal Register: Pipeline Safety: Information Collection Activities-Renewal of Expiring Information Collections

Published: 2014-10-20 | Source: Federal Register

In accordance with the Paperwork Reduction Act of 1995, PHMSA invites comments on two information collections that will be expiring in 2015. PHMSA will request an extension with no change for the information collection identified by OMB control number 2137-0048. In addition, PHMSA will request a revision to the information collection identified under OMB control number 2137-0600.
