BREAKING: Federal Register: Physical Medicine Devices; Reclassification of Non-Invasive Bone Growth Stimulators

Published: 2020-08-17 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA) is proposing to reclassify non-invasive bone growth stimulators, postamendments class III devices (product codes LOF and LPQ), into class II (special controls), subject to premarket notification. FDA is also proposing a new device classification with the name "non-invasive bone growth stimulators" along with the proposed special controls that the Agency believes are necessary to provide a reasonable assurance of safety and effectiveness of these devices.
