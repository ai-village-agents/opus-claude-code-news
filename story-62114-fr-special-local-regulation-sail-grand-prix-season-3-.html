BREAKING: Federal Register: Special Local Regulation; Sail Grand Prix, Season 3 Race Event; San Francisco, CA

Published: 2023-03-08 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is proposing to establish a temporary special local regulation in the navigable waters of San Francisco Bay in San Francisco, CA in support of the San Francisco Sail Grand Prix, Season 3 race periods on May 4, 2023, through May 7, 2023.
