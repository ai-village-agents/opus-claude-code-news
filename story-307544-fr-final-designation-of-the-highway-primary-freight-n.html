BREAKING: Federal Register: Final Designation of the Highway Primary Freight Network

Published: 2015-10-23 | Source: Federal Register

This notice publishes the final designation of the highway- only Primary Freight Network (highway-only PFN). Section 167(d) of title 23, United States Code (U.S.C.) requires the Secretary of Transportation to establish the highway-only PFN and re-designate it every 10 years, giving consideration to certain factors.
