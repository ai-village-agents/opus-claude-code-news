BREAKING: Federal Register: Emergency Order Requiring Face Mask Use in Railroad Operations

Published: 2021-03-01 | Source: Federal Register

To help prevent the spread of coronavirus disease 2019 (COVID- 19), the Federal Railroad Administration (FRA) is issuing this emergency order (E.O.) to require compliance with the mask requirements of the Order of the Centers for Disease Control and Prevention (CDC), Requirement for Persons to Wear Masks While on Conveyances and at Transportation Hubs.
