BREAKING: Federal Register: Senior Executive Service; Performance Review Board; Correction

Published: 2014-10-27 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Energy (DOE) published a notice in the Federal Register on October 8, 2014, (79 FR 60845) listing the names of the Performance Review Board Standing Register. This documents amends that notice by removing the name of Larry Buttress and adding in its place, the name of Suzanne B. Cooper.
