BREAKING: Federal Register: Housing Choice Voucher Program: Streamlining the Portability Process

Published: 2015-08-20 | Source: Federal Register

This final rule amends HUD's regulations governing portability in the Housing Choice Voucher (HCV) program. Portability is a feature of the HCV program that allows an eligible family with a housing choice voucher to use that voucher to lease a unit anywhere in the United States where there is a public housing agency (PHA) operating an HCV program.
