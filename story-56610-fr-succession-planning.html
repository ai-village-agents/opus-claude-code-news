BREAKING: Federal Register: Succession Planning

Published: 2024-12-26 | Source: Federal Register

The NCUA Board (Board) is issuing this final rule to further strengthen succession planning efforts for all consumer federally insured credit unions (FICUs). This final rule requires that a FICU board of directors establish a written succession plan that addresses specified positions and contains certain information.
