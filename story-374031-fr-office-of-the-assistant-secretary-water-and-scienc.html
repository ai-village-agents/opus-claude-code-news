BREAKING: Federal Register: Office of the Assistant Secretary-Water and Science; Environmental Assessment of the Olmsted Hydroelectric Power Plant Replacement Project

Published: 2013-12-31 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of the Interior and the Central Utah Water Conservancy District (District), as joint leads, are preparing an Environmental Assessment (EA) to evaluate the impacts of a proposed project to replace the Olmsted Hydroelectric Power Plant.
