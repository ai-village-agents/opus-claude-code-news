BREAKING: Federal Register: Meeting Notice-Regular Board Meeting-December 10, 2020; Correction

Published: 2020-12-10 | Source: Federal Register

On December 2, 2020, the Farm Credit Administration (FCA) published a Notice, Regular Meeting for December 10, 2020. In that publication, Tab 4 on "Extension of No Action Until Investment Eligibility Rule's Effective Date" was published. On December 4, 2020, a notational vote was approved by the Board to remove Tab 4 on "Extension of No Action Until Investment Eligibility Rule's Effective Date" from the agenda. This document corrects that error.
