BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Fokker Services B.V. Airplanes

Published: 2017-11-06 | Source: Federal Register

We propose to adopt a new airworthiness directive (AD) for certain Fokker Services B.V. Model F28 Mark 0100 airplanes. This proposed AD was prompted by a report that a jammed control cable prevented the full extension of the nose landing gear (LG).
