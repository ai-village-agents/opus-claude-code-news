BREAKING: Federal Register: Polyethylene Terephthalate Film, Sheet, and Strip From India and Taiwan; Institution of Five-Year Reviews

Published: 2013-04-01 | Source: Federal Register

The Commission hereby gives notice that it has instituted reviews pursuant to section 751(c) of the Tariff Act of 1930 (19 U.S.C. 1675(c)) (the Act) to determine whether revocation of the countervailing duty order on polyethylene terephthalate film, sheet, and strip ("PET film") from India and the antidumping duty orders on PET film from India and Taiwan would be likely to lead to continuation or recurrence of material injury.
