BREAKING: Federal Register: Land Acquisitions; the Forest County Potawatomi Community, Wisconsin

Published: 2019-08-20 | Source: Federal Register

The Assistant Secretary--Indian Affairs has made a final determination to acquire 92.75 acres, more or less, into trust for the Forest County Potawatomi Community, Wisconsin on July 12, 2019.
