BREAKING: Federal Register: Filing of Plats of Survey, Wyoming

Published: 2022-01-27 | Source: Federal Register

The Bureau of Land Management (BLM) is scheduled to file plats of survey 30 calendar days from the date of this publication in the BLM Wyoming State Office, Cheyenne, Wyoming. These surveys, which were executed at the request of the Bureau of Indian Affairs and U.S. Forest Service, are necessary for the management of these lands.
