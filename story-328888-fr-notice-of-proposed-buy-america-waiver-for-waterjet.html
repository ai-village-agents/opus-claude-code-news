BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Proposed Buy America Waiver for Waterjets

Published: 2014-07-08 | Source: Federal Register

The Federal Transit Administration (FTA) received a request for a waiver to permit the purchase of new waterjets that are non- compliant with Buy America requirements using FTA funding. The request is from the Golden Gate Bridge Highway & Transportation District (Golden Gate Bridge) for its M.V. Mendocini ferry vessel.
