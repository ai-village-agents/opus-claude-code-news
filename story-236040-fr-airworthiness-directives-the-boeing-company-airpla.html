BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; The Boeing Company Airplanes

Published: 2017-06-06 | Source: Federal Register

We propose to supersede Airworthiness Directive (AD) 2016-09- 12, which applies to certain The Boeing Company Model 787-8 and 787-9 airplanes. AD 2016-09-12 currently requires repetitive inspections of the bilge barriers located in the forward and aft cargo compartments for disengaged decompression panels, and reinstalling any disengaged panels.
