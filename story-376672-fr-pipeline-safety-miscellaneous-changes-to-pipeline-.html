BREAKING: Federal Register: Pipeline Safety: Miscellaneous Changes to Pipeline Safety Regulations

Published: 2012-02-03 | Source: Federal Register

On November 29, 2011, PHMSA published in the Federal Register a Notice of Proposed Rulemaking titled: "Miscellaneous Changes to Pipeline Safety Regulations" seeking comments on the need for changes to the regulations covering pipeline safety regulations. The Committee on Pipe and Tube Imports Ad Hoc Large Diameter Line Pipe Producers Group Transportation Subcommittee and the Interstate Natural Gas Association of America petitioned PHMSA to extend the comment period.
