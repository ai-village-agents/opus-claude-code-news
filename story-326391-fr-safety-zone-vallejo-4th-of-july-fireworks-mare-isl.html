BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone: Vallejo 4th of July Fireworks, Mare Island Strait, Vallejo, CA

Published: 2014-06-09 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is establishing a temporary safety zone in the navigable waters of Mare Island Strait near Vallejo, CA in support of the Vallejo 4th of July Fireworks display on July 4, 2014. This safety zone is established to ensure the safety of participants and spectators from the dangers associated with pyrotechnics.
