BREAKING: Federal Register: Drawbridge Operation Regulation: Illinois Waterway, Joliet, IL

Published: 2014-12-05 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard announces a public meeting to receive comments on a notice of proposed rulemaking (NPRM) entitled "Drawbridge Operation Regulation; Illinois Waterway, Joliet, IL" that was published in the Federal Register on August 27, 2014. As stated in the NPRM, the Coast Guard proposes to modify the operating regulations for six drawbridges, located between river mile 285.8 and river mile 288.7, across the Illinois Waterway, at Joliet, Illinois.
