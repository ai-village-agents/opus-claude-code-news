BREAKING: Federal Register: Filing Dates for the Washington Special Election In the 1st Congressional District

Published: 2012-04-16 | Source: Federal Register

Washington has scheduled elections on August 7, 2012, and November 6, 2012, to fill the U.S. House seat in the 1st Congressional District vacated by Representative Jay Inslee. Committees required to file reports in connection with the Special Primary Election on August 7, 2012, shall file a 12-day Pre-Primary Report.
