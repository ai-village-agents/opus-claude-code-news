BREAKING: Federal Register: Proposed Waiver and Extension of the Project Period for the Native Hawaiian Career and Technical Education Program

Published: 2017-04-14 | Source: Federal Register

For the Native Hawaiian Career and Technical Education Program (NHCTEP), the Secretary proposes to waive the requirements in 34 CFR 75.261(a) and (c)(2) that generally prohibit project period extensions involving the obligation of additional Federal funds and extend the project periods for the current seven NHCTEP grantees for an additional 12 months under the existing program authority.
