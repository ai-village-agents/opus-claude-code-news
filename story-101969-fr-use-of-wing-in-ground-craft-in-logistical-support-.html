BREAKING: Federal Register: Use of Wing-in-Ground Craft in Logistical Support of Offshore Platform Operations

Published: 2022-08-03 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Coast Guard seeks input from the public on wing-in- ground (WIG) craft. This information will support the Coast Guard's compliance with Section 8431 of the William M. (Mac) Thornberry National Defense Authorization Act for Fiscal Year 2021. In addition, public input will help in assessing the current state of WIG craft development and the technology to provide transportation support to offshore energy facilities on the U.S. Outer Continental Shelf.
