BREAKING: Federal Register: Eighth Coast Guard District Annual Safety Zones; Niceville July 4th Fireworks Show; Boggy Bayou; Niceville, FL

Published: 2012-07-03 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard will enforce a Safety Zone for the Niceville July 4th Fireworks Show in Boggy Bayou, Niceville, Florida from 8 p.m. until 9 p.m. on July 4, 2012. This action is necessary for the safeguard of participants and spectators, including all crews, vessels, and persons on navigable waters during the Niceville July 4th Fireworks Show.
