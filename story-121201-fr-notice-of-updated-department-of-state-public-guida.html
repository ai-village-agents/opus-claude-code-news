BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Updated Department of State Public Guidance for the Protecting Europe's Energy Security Act (PEESA), as Amended

Published: 2021-04-14 | Source: Federal Register

The Protecting Europe's Energy Security Act (PEESA) was amended by the FY21 National Defense Authorization Act (NDAA) on January 1, 2021. The Department of State is issuing updated, clarifying public guidance and Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs) for PEESA, as amended, on April 09, 2021, to provide public notice of the Administration's implementation of PEESA, as amended, and the range of activities captured under the amended legislation.
