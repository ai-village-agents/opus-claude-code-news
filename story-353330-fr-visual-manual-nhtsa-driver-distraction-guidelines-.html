BREAKING: Federal Register: Visual-Manual NHTSA Driver Distraction Guidelines for In-Vehicle Electronic Devices

Published: 2013-04-26 | Source: Federal Register

The National Highway Traffic Safety Administration (NHTSA) is concerned about the effects of distraction on motor vehicle safety due to drivers' use of electronic devices. Consequently, NHTSA is issuing nonbinding, voluntary Driver Distraction Guidelines (NHTSA Guidelines) to promote safety by discouraging the introduction of excessively distracting devices in vehicles.
