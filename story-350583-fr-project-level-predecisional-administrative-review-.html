BREAKING: Federal Register: Project-Level Predecisional Administrative Review Process

Published: 2013-03-27 | Source: Federal Register

The United States Department of Agriculture (the Department) is issuing this final rule to establish the sole process by which the public may file objections seeking predecisional administrative review for proposed projects and activities implementing land management plans and documented with a Record of Decision (ROD) or Decision Notice (DN).
