BREAKING: Federal Register: Civil Penalty Inflation Adjustments

Published: 2019-01-31 | Source: Federal Register

The Bureau of Consumer Financial Protection (Bureau) is amending its rule that specifies the time period for which adjusted civil penalty amounts would be applied to conduct within its jurisdiction and is also adjusting specific civil penalty amounts in that rule to account for inflation.
