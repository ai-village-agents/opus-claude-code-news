BREAKING: Federal Register: Regulations Governing Take of Migratory Birds

Published: 2020-02-03 | Source: Federal Register

We, the U.S. Fish and Wildlife Service (FWS, Service, we), propose to adopt a regulation that defines the scope of the Migratory Bird Treaty Act (MBTA or Act) as it applies to conduct resulting in the injury or death of migratory birds protected by the Act.
