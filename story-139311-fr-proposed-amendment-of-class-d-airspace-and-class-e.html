BREAKING: Federal Register: Proposed Amendment of Class D Airspace and Class E Airspace; Fort Worth and Dallas-Fort Worth, TX

Published: 2021-12-03 | Source: Federal Register

This action proposes to amend the Class D airspace at Fort Worth, TX, and the Class E airspace at Dallas-Fort Worth, TX. The FAA is proposing this action as the result of an airspace review due to the cancellation of the instrument procedures and implementation on new instrument procedures at Granbury Regional Airport, Granbury, TX, contained within the Dallas-Fort Worth, TX, Class E airspace legal description.
