BREAKING: Federal Register: Approval of Noise Compatibility Program for Akron-Canton Airport, North Canton, Ohio

Published: 2017-07-13 | Source: Federal Register

The Federal Aviation Administration (FAA) announces its findings on the noise compatibility program submitted by Akron-Canton Airport. On July 22, 2016, the FAA determined that the noise exposure maps submitted by Akron-Canton Airport Authority were in compliance with applicable requirements. On January 13, 2017 the FAA approved the Akron-Canton Airport noise compatibility program. All of the recommendations of the program were approved.
