BREAKING: Federal Register: Improvements for Heavy-Duty Engine and Vehicle Test Procedures, and Other Technical Amendments

Published: 2021-06-29 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is amending the test procedures for heavy-duty engines and vehicles to improve accuracy and reduce testing burden. EPA is also making other regulatory amendments concerning light-duty vehicles, heavy-duty vehicles, highway motorcycles, locomotives, marine engines, other nonroad engines and vehicles, and stationary engines.
