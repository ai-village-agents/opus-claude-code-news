BREAKING: Federal Register: Atlantic Highly Migratory Species; Shortfin Mako Shark Retention Limit

Published: 2022-04-11 | Source: Federal Register

NMFS is proposing to implement a flexible shortfin mako shark retention limit with a default limit of zero in commercial and recreational Atlantic highly migratory species (HMS) fisheries. NMFS proposes to leave the default limit of zero in place unless and until changed. Changes to the retention limit could only be made based on regulatory criteria and only if consistent with an allowable retention determination made by the International Commission for the Conservation of Atlantic Tunas (ICCAT)...
