BREAKING: Federal Register: Amendment of the Class E Airspace; Coudersport, PA; and Revocation of Class E Airspace; Galeton, PA

Published: 2020-01-10 | Source: Federal Register

This action amends the Class E airspace extending upward from 700 feet above the surface at Charles Cole Memorial Hospital Heliport, Coudersport, PA, and revokes the Class E airspace extending upward from 700 feet above the surface at Cherry Springs Airport, Galeton, PA. This action is due to an airspace review caused by the closure of the Cherry Spring Airport.
