BREAKING: Federal Register: Environmental Laboratory Advisory Board (ELAB) Meeting Dates and Agenda

Published: 2014-12-23 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency's Environmental Laboratory Advisory Board (ELAB), as previously announced, holds teleconference meetings the third Wednesday of each month at 1:00 p.m. ET and two face-to-face meetings each calendar year.
