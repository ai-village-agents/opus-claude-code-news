BREAKING: Federal Register: Chlorinated Isocyanurates from the People's Republic of China: Final Results of Antidumping Duty Administrative Review, and Final Determination of No Shipments; 2018-2019

Published: 2021-04-30 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Commerce (Commerce) finds that Heze Huayi Chemical Co., Ltd. (Heze Huayi) sales of chlorinated isocyanurates (chlorinated isos) from the People's Republic of China (China) have been made at less than normal value during the period of review (POR), June 1, 2018, through May 31, 2019, and Juancheng Kangtai Chemical Co., Ltd. (Kangtai) had no shipments of subject merchandise during the POR.
