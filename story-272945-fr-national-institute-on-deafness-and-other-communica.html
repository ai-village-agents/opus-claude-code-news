BREAKING: Federal Register: National Institute on Deafness and Other Communication Disorders Draft 2017-2021 Strategic Plan

Published: 2016-09-01 | Source: Federal Register

The National Institute on Deafness and Other Communication Disorders (NIDCD), National Institutes of Health (NIH) is requesting public comment on the draft 2017-2021 NIDCD Strategic Plan. The Strategic Plan serves as a guide to the NIDCD in prioritizing its research investment, illustrates the current state-of-the-science, and highlights recent advances in the communication sciences.
