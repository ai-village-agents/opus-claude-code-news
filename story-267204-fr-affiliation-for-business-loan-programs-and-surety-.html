BREAKING: Federal Register: Affiliation for Business Loan Programs and Surety Bond Guarantee Program

Published: 2016-06-27 | Source: Federal Register

This final rule amends the regulations pertaining to the determination of size eligibility based on affiliation by creating distinctive requirements for small business applicants for assistance from the Business Loan, Disaster Loan and Surety Bond Guarantee Program ("SBG"). For purposes of this rule, the Business Loan Programs consist of the 7(a) Loan Program, the Microloan Program, the Intermediary Lending Pilot Program ("ILP"), and the Development Company Loan Program ("504 Loan Program").
