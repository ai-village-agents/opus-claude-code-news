BREAKING: Federal Register: Compensation, Retirement Programs, and Related Benefits

Published: 2012-03-21 | Source: Federal Register

The Farm Credit Administration (FCA, us, we, or our) published a proposed rule to amend our regulations related to Farm Credit System (System) bank and association disclosures to shareholders and investors. The proposed rule would require enhanced reporting of senior officer compensation and retirement programs and reporting to shareholders of significant events that occur between annual reporting periods.
