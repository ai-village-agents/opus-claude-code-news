BREAKING: Federal Register: Legal Services for Veterans-Legal Assistance for Access to VA Programs Grant Program

Published: 2024-10-02 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Veterans Affairs (VA) proposes to amend its regulations to implement a new authority requiring VA to establish a pilot program to assess the feasibility of awarding grants to eligible entities to establish new legal assistance clinics or enhance existing legal assistance clinics and pro bono efforts to provide certain legal assistance to veterans and individuals who served in the Armed Forces.
