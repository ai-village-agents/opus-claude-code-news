BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Gulfstream Aerospace Corporation Airplanes

Published: 2019-11-08 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA is adopting a new airworthiness directive (AD) for certain Gulfstream Aerospace Corporation (Gulfstream) Model G-IV and Model GIV-X airplanes. This AD was prompted by a revision to the airworthiness limitations section (ALS) of the aircraft maintenance manual (AMM) based on fatigue and damage tolerance testing and updated analysis.
