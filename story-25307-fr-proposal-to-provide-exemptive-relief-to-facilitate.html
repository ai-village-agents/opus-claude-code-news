BREAKING: Federal Register: Proposal To Provide Exemptive Relief To Facilitate Cross-Margining of Customer Positions Cleared at Chicago Mercantile Exchange, Inc. and Fixed Income Clearing Corporation

Published: 2025-12-17 | Source: Federal Register

The Commodity Futures Trading Commission ("CFTC" or "Commission") is proposing to issue an order pursuant to the Commodity Exchange Act ("CEA") that would provide exemptive relief from the CEA and Commission regulations related to segregation and protection of futures customer funds.
