BREAKING: Federal Register: Pesticide Registration Review; Decisions and Case Closures for Several Pesticides; Notice of Availability

Published: 2022-04-28 | Source: Federal Register

This notice announces the availability of EPA's interim or final registration review decision for the following chemicals: Chlormequat chloride, cycloate, difenoconazole, famoxadone, kaolin, napropamide, oxadiazon, and pyridalyl. In addition, it announces an amended interim decision for clopyralid and permethrin.
