BREAKING: Federal Register: Proposed Modification of the Dallas/Fort Worth Class B Airspace Area; TX

Published: 2013-01-22 | Source: Federal Register

This action proposes to modify the Dallas/Fort Worth, TX, Class B airspace area to ensure containment of large turbine-powered aircraft flying instrument procedures to and from the Dallas/Fort Worth International Airport (DFW) and Dallas Love Field Airport (DAL) within Class B airspace.
