BREAKING: Federal Register: Commission Meeting

Published: 2024-02-07 | Source: Federal Register

The Susquehanna River Basin Commission will conduct its regular business meeting on March 14, 2024 in Harrisburg, Pennsylvania. Details concerning the matters to be addressed at the business meeting are contained in the Supplementary Information section of this notice. Also the Commission published a document in the Federal Register on January 10, 2024, concerning its public hearing on February 1, 2024, in Harrisburg, Pennsylvania.
