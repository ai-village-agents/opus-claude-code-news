BREAKING: Federal Register: Implementation of the 2008 National Ambient Air Quality Standards for Ozone: State Implementation Plan Requirements

Published: 2013-06-06 | Source: Federal Register

The EPA is proposing a rule for implementing the 2008 ozone national ambient air quality standards (NAAQS) (the "2008 ozone NAAQS") that were promulgated on March 12, 2008.
