BREAKING: Federal Register: Common Alloy Aluminum Sheet From Indonesia and Romania: Postponement of Final Determinations of Less-Than-Fair-Value Investigations

Published: 2020-11-06 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Commerce (Commerce) is postponing the deadline for issuing the final determinations in the less-than-fair- value (LTFV) investigations of imports of common alloy aluminum sheet (aluminum sheet) from Indonesia and Romania, until March 1, 2021. Commerce is also extending the provisional measures from a four-month period to a period of not more than six months.
