BREAKING: Federal Register: Amortization Limits

Published: 2020-12-21 | Source: Federal Register

The Farm Credit Administration (FCA or we) is repealing the regulatory requirement that production credit associations (PCAs) amortize their loans in 15 years or less, while requiring Farm Credit System (FCS or System) associations to address amortization through their credit underwriting standards and internal controls.
