BREAKING: Federal Register: Providing Submissions in Electronic Format-Postmarketing Safety Reports for Vaccines; Guidance for Industry; Availability

Published: 2015-08-18 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA or Agency) is announcing the availability of a document entitled "Providing Submissions in Electronic Format--Postmarketing Safety Reports for Vaccines; Guidance for Industry."
