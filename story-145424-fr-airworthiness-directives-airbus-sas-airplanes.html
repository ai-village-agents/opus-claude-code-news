BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Airbus SAS Airplanes

Published: 2020-02-27 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA is adopting a new airworthiness directive (AD) for all Airbus SAS Model A350-941 and -1041 airplanes. This AD was prompted by reports of loss of retention of the regulator inlet filter retainer on certain crew oxygen cylinder assemblies.
