BREAKING: Federal Register: Request for Nominations for the Acadia National Park Advisory Commission

Published: 2014-10-08 | Source: Federal Register

The National Park Service, U.S. Department of the Interior, is seeking nominations for members at large to the Acadia National Park Advisory Commission (Commission). The purpose of the Commission is to advise the Secretary of the Interior (Secretary) and the National Park Service (NPS) on matters relating to management and development of Acadia National Park.
