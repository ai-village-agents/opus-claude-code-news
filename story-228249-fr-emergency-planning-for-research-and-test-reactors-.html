BREAKING: Federal Register: Emergency Planning For Research and Test Reactors and Other Non-Power Production and Utilization Facilities

Published: 2017-02-24 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Nuclear Regulatory Commission (NRC) is issuing for public comment draft regulatory guide (DG), DG-2004, "Emergency Planning for Research and Test Reactors and Other Non-Power Production and Utilization Facilities." This DG is proposed Revision 2 of Regulatory Guide (RG) 2.6, "Emergency Planning for Research and Test Reactors."
