BREAKING: Federal Register: Drawbridge Operation Regulation; Mianus River, Greenwich, CT

Published: 2023-01-31 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard proposes to modify the operating schedule that governs the Metro-North (Cos Cob) Bridge, across the Mianus River, mile 1.0, at Greenwich, CT. The bridge owner, Metro-North (MNR), submitted a request on May 5, 2022 to modify the regulation to align with the Metro-North "WALK" Bridge train schedule and avoid bridge openings during peak transit hours.
