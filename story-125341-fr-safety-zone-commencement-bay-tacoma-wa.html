BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone; Commencement Bay, Tacoma, WA

Published: 2021-06-07 | Source: Federal Register

The notice of enforcement of regulation published May 5, 2021 in the Federal Register, is rescinded and replaced by this notice of enforcement of regulation. The Coast Guard will enforce safety zone regulations for the Tacoma Freedom Fair Air Show on Commencement Bay from 2 p.m. on July 2 through 12:30 a.m. on July 4, 2021.
