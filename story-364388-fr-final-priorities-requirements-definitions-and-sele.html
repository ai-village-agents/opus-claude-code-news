BREAKING: Federal Register: Final Priorities, Requirements, Definitions, and Selection Criteria; Race to the Top-Early Learning Challenge

Published: 2013-08-30 | Source: Federal Register

The Secretary of Education and the Secretary of Health and Human Services (hereafter "the Secretaries") announce priorities, requirements, definitions, and selection criteria for the Race to the Top--Early Learning Challenge (RTT-ELC) program. The Secretaries may use one or more of these priorities, requirements, definitions, and selection criteria for competitions in fiscal year (FY) 2013 and later years.
