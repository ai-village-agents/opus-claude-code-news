BREAKING: Federal Register: Exemption From Certain Prohibited Transaction Restrictions Involving UBS AG (UBS), Located in Zurich, Switzerland

Published: 2025-01-15 | Source: Federal Register

This exemption provides conditional prospective relief that allows current and future asset managers under the UBS corporate umbrella (UBS QPAMs) to continue to rely on PTE 84-14 for the five-year period from June 12, 2024, through June 11, 2029, notwithstanding four judgments of conviction involving entities within the UBS and CSAG (Credit Suisse AG) corporate umbrellas.
