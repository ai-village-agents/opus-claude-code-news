BREAKING: Federal Register: Policy on the Non-Aeronautical Use of Airport Hangars

Published: 2016-06-15 | Source: Federal Register

This action clarifies the FAA's policy regarding storage of non-aeronautical items in airport facilities designated for aeronautical use. Under Federal law, airport operators that have accepted federal grants and/or those that have obligations contained in property deeds for property transferred under various Federal laws such as the Surplus Property Act generally may use airport property only for aviation-related purposes unless otherwise approved by the FAA.
