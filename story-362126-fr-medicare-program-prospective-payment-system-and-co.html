BREAKING: Federal Register: Medicare Program; Prospective Payment System and Consolidated Billing for Skilled Nursing Facilities for FY 2014

Published: 2013-08-06 | Source: Federal Register

This final rule updates the payment rates used under the prospective payment system for skilled nursing facilities (SNFs) for fiscal year (FY) 2014. In addition, it revises and rebases the SNF market basket, revises and updates the labor related share, and makes certain technical and conforming revisions in the regulations text.
