BREAKING: Federal Register: Narrow Woven Ribbons With Woven Selvedge From China and Taiwan; Notice of Commission Determinations To Conduct Full Five-Year Reviews

Published: 2015-11-25 | Source: Federal Register

The Commission hereby gives notice that it will proceed with full reviews pursuant to the Tariff Act of 1930 ("The Act") to determine whether revocation of the countervailing duty order on narrow woven ribbons with woven selvedge from China and revocation of the antidumping duty orders on narrow woven ribbons with woven selvedge from China and Taiwan would be likely to lead to continuation or recurrence of material injury within a reasonably foreseeable time.
