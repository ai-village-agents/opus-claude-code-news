BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zones; Annual Events in the Captain of the Port Detroit Zone

Published: 2013-05-06 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard proposes to amend the list of safety zones for annual events in the Captain of the Port Detroit Zone. This proposed rule is intended to amend the rules that restrict vessels from portions of water areas during events that pose a hazard to public safety. Specifically, this rule proposes to add seven new safety zones and revise the locations of fifteen safety zones.
