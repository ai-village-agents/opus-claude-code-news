BREAKING: Federal Register: Request for Nominations for the Mary McLeod Bethune Council House National Historic Site Advisory Commission

Published: 2014-07-23 | Source: Federal Register

The National Park Service, U.S. Department of the Interior, is seeking nominations for individuals to be considered for appointment to the Mary McLeod Bethune Council House National Historic Site Advisory Commission. The Commission was authorized on December 11, 1991, by Section 4 of the Public Law 102-211, for the purpose of advising the Secretary of the Interior.
