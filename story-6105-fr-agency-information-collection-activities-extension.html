BREAKING: Federal Register: Agency Information Collection Activities; Extension of Collection; Comment Request; National Electronic Injury Surveillance System (NEISS) and Follow-Up Activities for Product Related Injuries

Published: 2025-02-27 | Source: Federal Register

As required by the Paperwork Reduction Act of 1995, the Consumer Product Safety Commission (CPSC or Commission) requests comments on a proposed extension of approval of information collection to obtain data on consumer product-related injuries, and follow-up activities for product-related injuries. The Office of Management and Budget (OMB) previously approved the collection of information under control number 3041-0029. OMB's most recent extension of approval will expire on May 31, 2025.
