BREAKING: Federal Register: Air Plan Approval; Missouri; Construction Permit Exemptions

Published: 2022-05-26 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is proposing to approve revisions to the Missouri State Implementation Plan (SIP) received on June 10, 2021. The submission revises Missouri's regulation on construction permit exemptions. These revisions include updates to incorporations by reference, remove unnecessary words, and make minor clarifications and grammatical changes.
