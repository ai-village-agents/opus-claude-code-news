BREAKING: Federal Register: Poly(oxy-1,2-ethanediyl), α-(3-(1,3,3,3-tetramethyl-1-((trimethylsilyl) oxy) disiloxanyl) propyl)-ω-hydroxy-; Exemption From the Requirement of a Tolerance

Published: 2019-10-03 | Source: Federal Register

No. 67674-67-3) when used as an inert ingredient (surfactant) applied to animals.
