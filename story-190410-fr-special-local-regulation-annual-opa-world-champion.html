BREAKING: Federal Register: Special Local Regulation; Annual OPA World Championships, Gulf of Mexico; Englewood Beach, FL

Published: 2019-10-04 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard will enforce special local regulations for the OPA World Championships from November 22, 2019 through November 24, 2019, to provide for the safety of life on navigable waterways during this event. Our regulation for the annual OPA World Championships identifies the regulated area for this event in Englewood, FL.
