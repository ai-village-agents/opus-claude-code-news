BREAKING: Federal Register: Privacy Act of 1974; Matching Program

Published: 2018-04-26 | Source: Federal Register

Pursuant to 5 U.S.C. 552a, the Privacy Act of 1974, as amended, and the Office of Management and Budget (OMB) Guidelines on the Conduct of Matching Programs, notice is hereby given that the Department of Veterans Affairs (VA) intends to conduct a computer matching program with the Social Security Administration (SSA).
