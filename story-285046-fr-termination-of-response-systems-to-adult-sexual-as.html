BREAKING: Federal Register: Termination of Response Systems to Adult Sexual Assault Crimes Panel

Published: 2015-01-29 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Defense is publishing this notice to announce that it is terminating the Response Systems to Adult Sexual Assault Crimes Panel, effective July 27, 2014.
