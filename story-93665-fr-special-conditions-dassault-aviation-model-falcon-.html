BREAKING: Federal Register: Special Conditions: Dassault Aviation Model Falcon 6X Airplane; Dynamic Test Requirements for Multiple-Occupant Side-Facing Seats With Inflatable Restraints

Published: 2022-04-14 | Source: Federal Register

These special conditions are issued for the Dassault Aviation (Dassault) Model Falcon 6X airplane. This airplane will have a novel or unusual design feature when compared to the state of technology envisioned in the airworthiness standards for transport-category airplanes. This design feature is multiple-occupant side-facing seats with inflatable restraints.
