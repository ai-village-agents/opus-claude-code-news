BREAKING: Federal Register: Final Requirements-Race to the Top-Early Learning Challenge; Phase 2

Published: 2012-09-20 | Source: Federal Register

The Secretary of Education and the Secretary of Health and Human Services (hereafter "the Secretaries") announce requirements for Phase 2 of the Race to the Top--Early Learning Challenge (RTT-ELC) program. In Phase 2, we will make awards to certain States that applied for, but did not receive, funding under the RTT-ELC competition held in fiscal year (FY) 2011 (FY 2011 RTT-ELC competition).
