BREAKING: Federal Register: Radio Experimentation and Market Trials

Published: 2015-08-31 | Source: Federal Register

In this document, the Commission proposes to modify the rules for program experimental licenses to permit experimentation for radio frequency (RF)-based medical devices, if the device being tested is designed to comply with all applicable service rules in Part 18, Industrial, Scientific, and Medical Equipment; Part 95, Personal Radio Services Subpart H--Wireless Medical Telemetry Service; or Part 95, Subpart I--Medical Device Radiocommunication Service.
