BREAKING: Federal Register: Pick-Sloan Missouri Basin Program-Eastern Division-Rate Order No. WAPA-203

The existing formula rates for these services, under Rate Schedules P-SED-F13, P- SED-FP13, and P-SED-M1, expire on December 31, 2022.
