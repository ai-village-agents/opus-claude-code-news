BREAKING: Federal Register: Approval and Promulgation of Air Quality Implementation Plans; Illinois; VOM Definition

Published: 2015-02-20 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is approving a request submitted by the Illinois Environmental Protection Agency (Illinois EPA) on June 10, 2014, to revise the Illinois State Implementation Plan (SIP). The submission amends the Illinois Administrative Code (IAC) by updating the definition of "volatile organic material (VOM) or volatile organic compound (VOC)" to add five compounds to the list of exempted compounds.
