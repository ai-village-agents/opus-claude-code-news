BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone; Luna Pier Fireworks, Luna Pier, MI

Published: 2013-08-13 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard will enforce the safety zone for the Luna Pier Fireworks Show, Luna Pier, MI, from 9:25 p.m. until 10:25 p.m. on August 24, 2013. Enforcement of this zone is necessary to ensure the safety of life on the navigable waters of the United States immediately prior to, during, and immediately after the associated marine event.
