BREAKING: Federal Register: Special Conditions: Dassault Aviation Model Falcon 6X Airplane; Interaction of Systems and Structures

Published: 2022-03-24 | Source: Federal Register

These special conditions are issued for the Dassault Aviation (Dassault) Model Falcon 6X airplane. This airplane will have a novel or unusual design feature when compared to the state of technology envisioned in the airworthiness standards for transport category airplanes. This design feature is installed systems that, directly or as a result of failure or malfunction, affect airplane structural performance.
