BREAKING: Federal Register: Qualification and Training of Personnel for Nuclear Power Plants

Published: 2018-02-12 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Nuclear Regulatory Commission (NRC) is issuing for public comment draft regulatory guide (DG), DG-1329, "Qualification and Training of Personnel for Nuclear Power Plants." The proposed revision describes methods acceptable to the NRC staff for complying with those portions of the Commission's regulations associated with the selection, qualifications, and training for nuclear power plant personnel.
