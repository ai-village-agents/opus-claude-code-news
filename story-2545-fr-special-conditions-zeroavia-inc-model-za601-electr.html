Published: 2026-01-08 | Source: Federal Register

This action proposes special conditions for ZeroAvia, Inc. (ZeroAvia) Model ZA601 electric engines that operate using electrical technology installed on the aircraft for use as an aircraft engine. These engines will have a novel or unusual design feature when compared to the state of technology envisioned in the airworthiness standards for aircraft engines.
