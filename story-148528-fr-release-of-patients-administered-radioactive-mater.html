BREAKING: Federal Register: Release of Patients Administered Radioactive Material

Published: 2020-04-07 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Nuclear Regulatory Commission (NRC) is issuing Revision 1 to Regulatory Guide (RG) 8.39, "Release of Patients Administered Radioactive Material." This RG (Revision 1) provides licensees with more detailed instructions to provide to patients before and after they have been administered radioactive material than was in Revision 0.
