BREAKING: Federal Register: Equal Access to Justice Act Implementation Rule

Published: 2014-02-10 | Source: Federal Register

On June 29, 2012, the Consumer Financial Protection Bureau (Bureau) published in the Federal Register an interim final rule implementing the Equal Access to Justice Act (EAJA or the Act). EAJA requires agencies that conduct adversary adjudications to award attorney fees and other litigation expenses to certain parties other than the United States in certain circumstances.
