BREAKING: Federal Register: Request for Information Regarding Diagnostic Excellence Measurement; Reopening of Comment Period

Published: 2025-03-10 | Source: Federal Register

The Agency for Healthcare Research and Quality (AHRQ) invites public comment in response to this Request for Information (RFI) on the development of measures of diagnostic excellence that may be calculated using administrative data or electronic health record (EHR) data. The purpose of diagnostic excellence measurement is to identify potential opportunities to improve the diagnostic process at a health system or geographic level.
