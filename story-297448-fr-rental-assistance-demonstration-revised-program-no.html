BREAKING: Federal Register: Rental Assistance Demonstration: Revised Program Notice

Published: 2015-06-26 | Source: Federal Register

On July 26, 2012, HUD announced through notice in the Federal Register the implementation of the statutorily authorized Rental Assistance Demonstration (RAD), which provides the opportunity to test the conversion of public housing and other HUD-assisted properties to long-term, project-based section 8 rental assistance.
