BREAKING: Federal Register: Notification of Deletion of System of Records; Office of Criminal Enforcement, Forensics & Training, National Enforcement Investigations Center, Master Tracking System (EPA-46)

Published: 2013-07-08 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency is removing the Master Tracking System (EPA-46), published in the Federal Register on October 1, 2011, from its inventory of Privacy Act systems. A personal identifier is no longer used to retrieve the information in the system.
