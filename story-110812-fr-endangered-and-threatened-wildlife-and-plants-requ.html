BREAKING: Federal Register: Endangered and Threatened Wildlife and Plants; Request for New Information for the North American Wolverine Species Status Assessment

Published: 2022-11-23 | Source: Federal Register

We, the U.S. Fish and Wildlife Service (Service), notify the public that we are requesting new information to update the Species Status Assessment (SSA) for the North American Wolverine (Gulo gulo luscus) occurring in the contiguous United States to make a final determination whether to list this species under the Endangered Species Act of 1973, as amended (Act).
