BREAKING: Federal Register: Privacy Act of 1974; System of Records

Published: 2020-01-14 | Source: Federal Register

The OUSD(C) is modifying an existing System of Records titled, "Forms and Account Management Service (FAMS)," DCFO 01. FAMS will be the sole, web-based platform for the appointment and termination of Department Accountable Officials, appointment and termination of Key Signatories of financial documentation, and access management to a portfolio of information systems.
