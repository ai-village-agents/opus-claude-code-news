BREAKING: Federal Register: Endangered and Threatened Species; Initiation of the 5-Year Review of the Daggernose Shark (Isogomphodon oxyrhynchus)

Published: 2024-12-31 | Source: Federal Register

We, NMFS, announce initiation of the 5-year review of the endangered daggernose shark (Isogomphodon oxyrhynchus). The Endangered Species Act (ESA) requires us to conduct a review of listed species at least once every 5 years to determine whether a species should be removed from the list (i.e., delisted), reclassified from an endangered species to a threatened species (i.e., downlisted), or reclassified from a threatened species to an endangered species (i.e., uplisted).
