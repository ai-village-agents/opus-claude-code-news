BREAKING: Federal Register: Safe Harbor Agreement for the Reintroduction of the Amargosa Vole (Microtus californicus scirpensis) in Shoshone, CA, and Draft Environmental Action Statement, Inyo County, CA

Published: 2020-07-17 | Source: Federal Register

We, the U.S. Fish and Wildlife Service (Service), have received an application from Susan Sorrells (applicant) for an enhancement of survival permit (permit) under the Endangered Species Act. The application includes a draft safe harbor agreement (SHA) to facilitate reintroduction and recovery of the federally endangered Amargosa vole on non-Federal land in California.
