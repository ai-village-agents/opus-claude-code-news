BREAKING: Federal Register: Digital Health Technologies for Remote Data Acquisition in Clinical Investigations; Guidance for Industry, Investigators, and Other Stakeholders; Availability

Published: 2023-12-22 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA or Agency) is announcing the availability of a final guidance for industry, investigators, and other stakeholders entitled "Digital Health Technologies for Remote Data Acquisition in Clinical Investigations." This guidance provides recommendations on the use of digital health technologies (DHTs) to acquire data remotely from participants in clinical investigations that evaluate medical products.
