BREAKING: Federal Register: Reassignment of Post Office Box Section 98025 to Competitive Fee Group, and of Sections 87325 and 87326 to Market Dominant Fee Groups

Published: 2016-07-01 | Source: Federal Register

The Postal Service hereby provides notice that Post Office[supreg] Box service for ZIP Code[supreg] 98025 is reassigned from its market dominant fee group to a competitive fee group, and Post Office Box services for ZIP Code 87325 and ZIP Code 87326 are reassigned from their competitive fee groups to market dominant fee groups.
