BREAKING: Federal Register: Membership of the Performance Review Board for the Office of the Secretary

Published: 2016-10-03 | Source: Federal Register

In accordance with 5 U.S.C. 4314(c)(4), the Office of the Secretary, Department of Commerce (DOC), announces the appointment of those individuals who have been selected to serve as members of the Performance Review Board.
