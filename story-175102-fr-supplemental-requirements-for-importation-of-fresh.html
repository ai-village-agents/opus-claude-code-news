BREAKING: Federal Register: Supplemental Requirements for Importation of Fresh Citrus From Colombia Into the United States

Published: 2019-03-26 | Source: Federal Register

We are affirming the supplemental requirements we added for the importation of fresh sweet orange, grapefruit, mandarin, clementine, and tangerine fruit from Colombia into the United States. In a previous notice, we made available to the public for review and comment supplemental requirements for mitigating pest risks posed by the importation of those commodities from Colombia into the United States.
