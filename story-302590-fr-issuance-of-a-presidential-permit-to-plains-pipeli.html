BREAKING: Federal Register: Issuance of a Presidential Permit to Plains Pipeline, L.P.

Published: 2015-08-26 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of State issued a Presidential Permit to Plains Pipeline, L.P. on August 3, 2015 to connect, operate, and maintain existing pipeline facilities acquired by that company at the border of the United States and Canada for the transport of crude oil between the United States and Canada. The Department of State determined that issuance of this permit would serve the national interest.
