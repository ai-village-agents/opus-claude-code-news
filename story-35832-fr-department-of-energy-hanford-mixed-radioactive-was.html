BREAKING: Federal Register: Department of Energy Hanford Mixed Radioactive Waste Land Disposal Restrictions Variance

Published: 2024-05-01 | Source: Federal Register

The United States Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is granting a treatment variance, requested by the U.S. Department of Energy (DOE) in an August 1, 2023, petition, from the Land Disposal Restrictions (LDR) treatment standards for approximately 2,000 gallons of mixed hazardous low-activity radioactive waste from DOE's Test Bed Initiative (TBI) for the Hanford Site in Washington State.
