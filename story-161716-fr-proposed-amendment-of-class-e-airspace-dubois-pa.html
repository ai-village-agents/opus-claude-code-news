BREAKING: Federal Register: Proposed Amendment of Class E Airspace; DuBois, PA

Published: 2020-09-22 | Source: Federal Register

This action proposes to amend Class E surface airspace and Class E airspace extending upward from 700 feet above the surface in DuBois, PA, due to the decommissioning of the Clarion VORTAC and cancellation of the associated approach at DuBois Regional Airport. This action would also update the name of the airport, as well as the name and geographic coordinates of Penn Highlands Healthcare-DuBois Heliport.
