BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Airbus SAS Airplanes

Published: 2019-05-10 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA is correcting an airworthiness directive (AD) that published in the Federal Register. That AD applies to all Airbus SAS Model A318 series airplanes; Model A319 series airplanes; Model A320- 211, -212, -214, -216, -231, -232, and -233 airplanes; and Model A321- 111, -112, -131, -211, -212, -213, -231, and -232 airplanes.
