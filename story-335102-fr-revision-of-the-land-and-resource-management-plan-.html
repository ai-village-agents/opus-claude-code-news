BREAKING: Federal Register: Revision of the Land and Resource Management Plan for El Yunque National Forest, Puerto Rico

Published: 2014-09-18 | Source: Federal Register

As directed by the National Forest Management Act (NFMA), the U.S. Forest Service is preparing the El Yunque National Forest's revised land management plan (forest plan) and will also prepare an Environmental Impact Statement (EIS) for this revised forest plan. This notice briefly describes the nature of the decision to be made, a proposed action based on the preliminary identified need to change the existing plan, and information concerning public participation.
