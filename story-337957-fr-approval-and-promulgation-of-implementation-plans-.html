BREAKING: Federal Register: Approval and Promulgation of Implementation Plans; North Dakota; Revisions to the Air Pollution Control Rules

Published: 2014-10-22 | Source: Federal Register

EPA is approving changes to North Dakota's State Implementation Plan (SIP). On January 23, 2013, the Governor of North Dakota submitted to EPA revisions to several chapters of the North Dakota SIP. These revisions included the removal of subsections 33-15- 03-04.4 and 33-15-05-01.2.a(l) of the North Dakota Administrative Code (NDAC).
