BREAKING: Federal Register: Immigration Benefits Business Transformation, Increment I; Corrections

Published: 2024-08-09 | Source: Federal Register

On August 29, 2011, the Department of Homeland Security published a final rule titled "Immigration Benefits Business Transformation, Increment I," which, in part, amended DHS regulations to remove references to form numbers and titles. Two of the amendatory instructions were inadvertently not followed, resulting in errors in the Code of Federal Regulations (CFR).
