BREAKING: Federal Register: Servicemembers' Group Life Insurance and Veterans' Group Life Insurance Information Access

Published: 2013-09-23 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Veterans Affairs (VA) proposes to amend its regulations governing Servicemembers' Group Life Insurance (SGLI), Family SGLI, SGLI Traumatic Injury Protection, and Veterans' Group Life Insurance (all hereafter referred to as SGLI).
