BREAKING: Federal Register: Agency Information Collection Activities; Submission for OMB Review; Comment Request

Published: 2015-02-24 | Source: Federal Register

The FTC intends to conduct an evaluation of Admongo, its advertising literacy program for children ages 8-12. The evaluation will involve a randomized controlled trial of the Admongo online game, using an Internet panel recruited by a market research company. This research will be conducted to further the FTC's mission of protecting consumers from unfair and deceptive marketing.
