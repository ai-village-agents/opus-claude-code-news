BREAKING: Federal Register: Temporary Extension and Modification of Framework for Conditional Sailing Order (CSO) for Cruise Ships Operating or Intending To Operate in U.S. Waters

Published: 2021-10-28 | Source: Federal Register

The Centers for Disease Control and Prevention (CDC), a component of the Department of Health and Human Services (HHS), announces a temporary extension and modification of the Framework for Conditional Sailing Order (CSO).
