BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; The Boeing Company Airplanes

Published: 2014-03-03 | Source: Federal Register

We propose to adopt a new airworthiness directive (AD) for The Boeing Company Model airplanes equipped with Rolls-Royce Trent 800 series engines. This proposed AD was prompted by reports of in-flight separation of the aft plug from the forward plug, which are the two parts of the turbine exhaust plug assembly.
