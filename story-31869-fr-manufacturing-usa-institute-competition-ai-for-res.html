BREAKING: Federal Register: Manufacturing USA Institute Competition: AI for Resilient Manufacturing

Published: 2024-03-13 | Source: Federal Register

The Office of Advanced Manufacturing within the National Institute of Standards and Technology, an agency of the U.S. Department of Commerce, intends to announce an open competition for a new Manufacturing USA institute. The expected competition will seek to establish a Manufacturing USA institute focused on the use of Artificial Intelligence to improve resilience of U.S. manufacturing.
