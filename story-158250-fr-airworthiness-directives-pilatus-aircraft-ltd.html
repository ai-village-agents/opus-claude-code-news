BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Pilatus Aircraft Ltd

Published: 2020-08-07 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA proposes to adopt a new airworthiness directive (AD) for certain Pilatus Aircraft Ltd Model PC-24 airplanes. This proposed AD was prompted by mandatory continuing airworthiness information (MCAI) issued by the aviation authority of another country to identify and correct an unsafe condition on an aviation product.
