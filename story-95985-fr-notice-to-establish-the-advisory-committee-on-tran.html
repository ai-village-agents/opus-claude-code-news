BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice To Establish the Advisory Committee on Transportation Equity

Published: 2022-05-16 | Source: Federal Register

The Office of the Secretary of Transportation (OST) announces the establishment of the Advisory Committee on Transportation Equity. The Secretary has determined that establishing the Advisory Committee on Transportation Equity is necessary and in the public interest.
