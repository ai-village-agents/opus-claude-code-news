BREAKING: Federal Register: Military Pay Allotment Policy Change Notice

Published: 2014-11-28 | Source: Federal Register

The Secretary of Defense approved establishment of policy that prohibits the use of discretionary military pay allotments to acquire personal property. His purpose in doing so is to eliminate one mechanism that predatory lenders use to take advantage of service members who finance purchases using the military pay allotment system.
