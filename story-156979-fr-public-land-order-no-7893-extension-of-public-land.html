BREAKING: Federal Register: Public Land Order No. 7893; Extension of Public Land Order No. 7422, Diamond Fork System, Bonneville Unit of the Central Utah Project; Utah; Correction

Published: 2020-07-23 | Source: Federal Register

The Bureau of Land Management published a document in the Federal Register on December 4, 2019, concerning a Public Land Order (PLO) that extended the duration of the withdrawal created by an earlier PLO for an additional 20-year term. The document's subject heading incorrectly stated the new PLO number.
