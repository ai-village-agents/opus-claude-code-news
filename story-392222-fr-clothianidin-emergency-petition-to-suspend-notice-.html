BREAKING: Federal Register: Clothianidin; Emergency Petition To Suspend; Notice of Availability

Published: 2012-07-27 | Source: Federal Register

PANNA and others submitted a request for the EPA to immediately suspend Clothianidin and take other actions affecting the registration. The EPA is announcing the decision to deny the suspension request and is inviting the public to comment on the decision and the remainder of the petition.
