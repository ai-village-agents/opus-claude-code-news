BREAKING: Federal Register: Tomatoes Grown in Florida; Increased Assessment Rate

Published: 2012-07-26 | Source: Federal Register

This rule increases the assessment rate established for the Florida Tomato Committee (Committee) for the 2011-12 and subsequent fiscal periods from $0.0275 to $0.037 per 25-pound carton of tomatoes handled. The Committee locally administers the marketing order which regulates the handling of tomatoes grown in Florida.
