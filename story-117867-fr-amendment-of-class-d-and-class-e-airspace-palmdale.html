BREAKING: Federal Register: Amendment of Class D and Class E Airspace; Palmdale, CA

Published: 2021-03-01 | Source: Federal Register

This action modifies the Class E airspace, designated as an extension to a Class D or Class E surface area, at Palmdale USAF Plant 42 Airport. This action also modifies the Class E airspace, extending upward from 700 feet above the surface. Additionally, this action removes the Class E airspace, extending upward from 1,200 feet above the surface.
