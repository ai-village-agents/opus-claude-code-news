BREAKING: Federal Register: Changes To Implement the Inventor's Oath or Declaration Provisions of the Leahy-Smith America Invents Act

Published: 2012-01-06 | Source: Federal Register

The United States Patent and Trademark Office (Office) proposes changes to the existing rules of practice to implement the inventor's oath or declaration provisions of the Leahy-Smith America Invents Act. The Office proposes to revise and clarify the rules of practice relating to the inventor's oath or declaration, including reissue oaths or declarations, assignments containing oath or declaration statements from inventors, and oaths or declarations signed by parties other than the inventors.
