BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone, PNSY Entrance Structure Heavy Lift Project-Piscataqua River, Portsmouth, NH

Published: 2021-06-25 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is establishing a temporary safety zone for certain waters in the Piscataqua River, Portsmouth, NH. This action is necessary to provide for the safety of persons, property, and the marine environment from the potential safety hazards associated with the construction and heavy lift operations at the Portsmouth Naval Shipyard Dry Dock No. 1.
