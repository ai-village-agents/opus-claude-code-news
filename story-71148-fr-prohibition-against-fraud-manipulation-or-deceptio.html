BREAKING: Federal Register: Prohibition Against Fraud, Manipulation, or Deception in Connection With Security-Based Swaps; Prohibition Against Undue Influence Over Chief Compliance Officers

Published: 2023-06-30 | Source: Federal Register

The Securities and Exchange Commission ("SEC" or "Commission") is adopting a final rule, under the Securities Exchange Act of 1934 ("Exchange Act"), that is designed to prevent fraud, manipulation, and deception in connection with effecting any transaction in, or attempting to effect any transaction in, or purchasing or selling, or inducing or attempting to induce the purchase or sale of, any security-based swap.
