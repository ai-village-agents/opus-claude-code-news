BREAKING: Federal Register: Transmission Relay Loadability Reliability Standard

Published: 2012-03-21 | Source: Federal Register

Pursuant to section 215 of the Federal Power Act, the Commission approves Reliability Standard PRC-023-2 (Transmission Relay Loadability) submitted by the North American Electric Reliability Corporation (NERC), the Electric Reliability Organization certified by the Commission.
