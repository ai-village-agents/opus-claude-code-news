BREAKING: Federal Register: FirstEnergy Nuclear Operating Company; Beaver Valley Power Station, Unit Nos. 1 and 2

Published: 2015-06-10 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Nuclear Regulatory Commission (NRC) is considering issuance of an amendment to Renewed License Nos. DPR-66 and NPF-73, issued on November 5, 2009, and held by FirstEnergy Nuclear Operating Company for the operation of Beaver Valley Power Station, Unit Nos. 1 and 2 (BVPS). The proposed action would revise the Emergency Preparedness Plan (EPP) to modify the boundary of the 10-mile Emergency Planning Zone (EPZ).
