BREAKING: Federal Register: Visas: Documentation of Immigrants under the Immigration and Nationality Act, as Amended

Published: 2013-06-03 | Source: Federal Register

This rule amends the Department of State's regulations relating to adoptions in countries party to the Hague Convention on the Protection of Children and Co-operation in Respect of Intercountry Adoption to include a new adoption provision from the International Adoption Simplification Act.
