BREAKING: Federal Register: Centralized Partnership Audit Regime: Adjusting Tax Attributes

Published: 2018-02-02 | Source: Federal Register

This document contains proposed regulations implementing section 1101 of the Bipartisan Budget Act of 2015, which was enacted into law on November 2, 2015. The Bipartisan Budge Act repeals the current rules governing partnership audits and replaces them with a new centralized partnership audit regime that, in general, determines, assesses and collects tax at the partnership level.
