BREAKING: Federal Register: Employer-Provided Survey Wage Methodology for the Temporary Non-Agricultural Employment H-2B Program

Published: 2024-11-18 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Labor (Department or DOL) proposes to amend its regulations for employer-provided wage surveys for the H-2B temporary labor certification program. The regulations were published in the Wage Methodology for the Temporary Non-Agricultural Employment H-2B Program final rule (2015 Wage Rule).
