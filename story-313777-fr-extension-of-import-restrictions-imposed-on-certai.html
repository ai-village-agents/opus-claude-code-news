BREAKING: Federal Register: Extension of Import Restrictions Imposed on Certain Archaeological Material From China

Published: 2014-01-13 | Source: Federal Register

This final rule amends the U.S. Customs and Border Protection (CBP) regulations to reflect the extension of import restrictions on certain archaeological material from the People's Republic of China (China) and makes a technical change to the regulations to clarify that the restriction to monumental sculpture and wall art at least 250 years old should be calculated as of January 14, 2009, the date the Memorandum of Understanding (MOU) became effective.
