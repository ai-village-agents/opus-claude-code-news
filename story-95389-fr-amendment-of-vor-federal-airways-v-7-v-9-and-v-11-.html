BREAKING: Federal Register: Amendment of VOR Federal Airways V-7, V-9, and V-11; Eastern United States

Published: 2022-05-06 | Source: Federal Register

This action modifies VHF Omnidirectional Range (VOR) Federal airway V-11. This action is necessary due to the planned decommissioning of the Dyersburg, TN, (DYR); and the Holly Springs, MS (HLI) VOR Tactical Air Navigation (VORTAC) facilities, under the FAA's VOR Minimum Operational Network (MON) program, which provide navigation guidance for segments of the routes.
