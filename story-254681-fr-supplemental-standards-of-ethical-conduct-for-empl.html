BREAKING: Federal Register: Supplemental Standards of Ethical Conduct for Employees of the Department of Homeland Security

Published: 2016-02-05 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Homeland Security (DHS), with the concurrence of the U.S. Office of Government Ethics (OGE), is finalizing this rule to supplement the OGE Standards of Ethical Conduct for Employees of the Executive Branch (OGE Standards) for DHS employees.
