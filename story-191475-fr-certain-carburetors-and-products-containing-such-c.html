BREAKING: Federal Register: Certain Carburetors and Products Containing Such Carburetors; Commission Decision To Review in Part an Initial Determination Finding Complainant Failed To Satisfy the Economic Prong of the Domestic Industry Requirement; Termination of the Investigation

Published: 2019-10-18 | Source: Federal Register

Notice is hereby given that the U.S. International Trade Commission has determined to review in part the administrative law judge's ("ALJ") initial determination ("ID"), which grants respondents' motion for summary determination that the complainant failed to satisfy the economic prong of the domestic industry requirement as to U.S. Patent Nos. 6,394,424 ("the '424 patent"); 6,439,547 ("the '547 patent"); 6,533,254 ("the '254 patent"); and 7,070,173 ("the '173 patent).
