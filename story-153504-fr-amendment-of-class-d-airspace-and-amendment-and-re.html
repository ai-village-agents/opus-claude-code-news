BREAKING: Federal Register: Amendment of Class D Airspace and Amendment and Revocation of the Class E Airspace; Multiple Texas Towns

Published: 2020-06-08 | Source: Federal Register

This action affects several airports in Texas by amending Class D airspace and Class E surface airspace; revoking Class E airspace designated as an extension to a Class E surface area; amending Class E airspace extending upward from 700 feet above the surface; and removing Class E airspace extending upward from 700 feet above the surface.
