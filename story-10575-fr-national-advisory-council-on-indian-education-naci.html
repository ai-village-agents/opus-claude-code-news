BREAKING: Federal Register: National Advisory Council on Indian Education (NACIE)

Published: 2025-05-05 | Source: Federal Register

This notice sets forth the agenda, time, and instructions to access or participate in the June 18-19, 2025, virtual meeting of NACIE. This notice provides information about the meeting to members of the public who may be interested in virtually attending the meeting and how to provide written and/or oral comment for the meeting.
