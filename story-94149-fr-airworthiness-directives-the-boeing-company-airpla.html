BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; The Boeing Company Airplanes

Published: 2022-04-21 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA is adopting a new airworthiness directive (AD) for certain The Boeing Company Model 757-200, -200CB, and -300 series airplanes. This AD was prompted by a report indicating the passenger service units (PSUs) and life vest panels became separated from their attachments during several survivable accident sequences.
