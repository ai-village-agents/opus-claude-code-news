BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Purchased/Referred Care Delivery Area Redesignation for the Hoh Tribe in the State of Washington

Published: 2023-01-18 | Source: Federal Register

This Notice advises the public that the Indian Health Service (IHS) proposes to expand the geographic boundaries of the Purchased/ Referred Care Delivery Area (PRCDA) for the Hoh Tribe in the State of Washington to include the county of Clallam in the State of Washington. The current PRCDA for the Hoh Tribe includes the Washington county of Jefferson.
