BREAKING: Federal Register: Tongass Advisory Committee

Published: 2014-03-13 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Department of Agriculture (USDA) is extending the call for nominations an additional fifteen days, from February 27, 2014, to March 14, 2014, for the solicitation of membership to the newly established Tongass Advisory Committee (Committee). The USDA published a Notice of Intent in the Federal Register on January 13, 2014, (79 FR 2147). The USDA requested nominations and applications for individuals to be considered as Committee members.
