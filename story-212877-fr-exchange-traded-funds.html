BREAKING: Federal Register: Exchange-Traded Funds

Published: 2018-07-31 | Source: Federal Register

The Securities and Exchange Commission (the "Commission") is proposing a new rule under the Investment Company Act of 1940 (the "Investment Company Act" or the "Act") that would permit exchange- traded funds ("ETFs") that satisfy certain conditions to operate without the expense and delay of obtaining an exemptive order.
