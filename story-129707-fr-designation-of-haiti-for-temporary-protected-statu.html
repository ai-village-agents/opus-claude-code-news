BREAKING: Federal Register: Designation of Haiti for Temporary Protected Status

Published: 2021-08-03 | Source: Federal Register

Through this Notice, the Department of Homeland Security (DHS) announces that the Secretary of Homeland Security (Secretary) is designating Haiti for Temporary Protected Status (TPS) for 18 months, effective August 3, 2021, through February 3, 2023.
