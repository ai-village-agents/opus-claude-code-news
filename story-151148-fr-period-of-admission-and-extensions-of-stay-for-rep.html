BREAKING: Federal Register: Period of Admission and Extensions of Stay for Representatives of Foreign Information Media Seeking To Enter the United States

Published: 2020-05-11 | Source: Federal Register

This rule amends the Department of Homeland Security's ("DHS" or "the Department") regulations to achieve greater reciprocity between the United States and the People's Republic of China (PRC), with the exception of Hong Kong Special Administrative Region (SAR) or Macau SAR passport holders, relative to the treatment of representatives of foreign information media of the respective countries seeking entry into the other country.
