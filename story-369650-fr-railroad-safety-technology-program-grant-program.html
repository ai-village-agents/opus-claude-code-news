BREAKING: Federal Register: Railroad Safety Technology Program Grant Program

Published: 2013-11-07 | Source: Federal Register

The Railroad Safety Technology Grant Program was first authorized under the Rail Safety Improvement Act of 2008 (RSIA). The program authorizes DOT to provide grants to passenger and freight rail carriers, railroad suppliers, and State and local governments for projects that have a public benefit of improved railroad safety and efficiency.
