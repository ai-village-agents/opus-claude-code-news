BREAKING: Federal Register: Food Labeling; Calorie Labeling of Articles of Food in Vending Machines

Published: 2014-12-01 | Source: Federal Register

To implement the vending machine food labeling provisions of the Patient Protection and Affordable Care Act of 2010 (ACA), the Food and Drug Administration (FDA or we) is establishing requirements for providing calorie declarations for food sold from certain vending machines.
