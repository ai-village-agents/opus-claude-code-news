BREAKING: Federal Register: Privacy Act of 1974; System of Records

Published: 2020-03-11 | Source: Federal Register

In accordance with the Privacy Act of 1974, the Department of Homeland Security (DHS) proposes to establish a new DHS system of records titled, "DHS/ALL-045 Statistical Immigration Data Production and Reporting System of Records." This system of records allows DHS/ Office of Immigration Statistics (OIS) to collect and maintain records on members of the public for whom federal agencies have collected information related to individuals' interactions with the immigration system.
