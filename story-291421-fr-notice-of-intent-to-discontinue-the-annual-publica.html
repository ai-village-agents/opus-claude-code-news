BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Intent To Discontinue the Annual Publication of the Prices Paid Data

Published: 2015-04-16 | Source: Federal Register

This notice announces the intention of the National Agricultural Statistics Service (NASS) to discontinue the publication of annual prices paid data. These data include prices paid for fuels, feed, seeds, fertilizer, machinery, and chemicals. These prices were published each year in the April Agricultural Prices report.
