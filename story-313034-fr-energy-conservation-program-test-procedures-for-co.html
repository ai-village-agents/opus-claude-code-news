BREAKING: Federal Register: Energy Conservation Program: Test Procedures for Commercial Prerinse Spray Valves

Published: 2015-12-30 | Source: Federal Register

On June 23, 2015, the U.S. Department of Energy (DOE) issued a notice of proposed rulemaking (NOPR) to amend the test procedure for commercial prerinse spray valves. That proposed rulemaking serves as the basis for this final rule.
