BREAKING: Federal Register: Transitional Amendments To Satisfy the Market Rate of Return Rules for Hybrid Retirement Plans

Published: 2015-11-16 | Source: Federal Register

This document contains final regulations that provide guidance regarding certain amendments to applicable defined benefit plans. Applicable defined benefit plans are defined benefit plans that use a lump sum-based benefit formula, including cash balance plans and pension equity plans, as well as other plans that have formulas with an effect similar to a lump sum-based benefit formula.
