BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of New Fee Site; Federal Lands Recreation Enhancement Act

Published: 2018-12-18 | Source: Federal Register

The Ozark-St. Francis National Forests in Arkansas, will be implementing new fees at Richland Creek Campground at $10 per night for a single site or $20 per night for a double site. This site has had additional amenities added to improve services and experiences that include new grills, tables, lantern poles, tent pads, parking spaces, new toilet, water system upgrades and trashcans.
