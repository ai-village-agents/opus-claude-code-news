BREAKING: Federal Register: Approval and Promulgation of Implementation Plans; New York State Ozone Implementation Plan Revision

Published: 2014-03-04 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is approving a revision to the New York State Implementation Plan (SIP) for ozone concerning the control of volatile organic compounds. The SIP revision consists of amendments to Part 228, "Surface Coating Processes, Commercial and Industrial Adhesives, Sealants and Primers."
