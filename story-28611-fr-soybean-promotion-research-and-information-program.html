BREAKING: Federal Register: Soybean Promotion, Research, and Information Program: Opportunity To Request a Referendum

Published: 2024-02-02 | Source: Federal Register

The Agricultural Marketing Service (AMS) announces that soybean producers may request a referendum to determine if producers want the Secretary to conduct a referendum on the Soybean Promotion and Research Order (Order), as authorized under the Soybean Promotion, Research, and Consumer Information Act (Act).
