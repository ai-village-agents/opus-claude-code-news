BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Centennial Challenges 2015 Sample Return Robot (SRR) Challenge

Published: 2014-09-19 | Source: Federal Register

This notice is issued in accordance with 51 U.S.C. 20144(c). The Sample Return Robot (SRR) Challenge is scheduled and teams that wish to compete may now register. Centennial Challenges is a program of prize competitions to stimulate innovation in technologies of interest and value to NASA and the nation.
