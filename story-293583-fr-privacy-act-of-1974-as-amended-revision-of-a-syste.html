BREAKING: Federal Register: Privacy Act of 1974, As Amended; Revision of a System of Records

Published: 2015-05-12 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of the Interior (DOI) is issuing public notice of its intent to revise a system of records in its current inventory, Migratory Bird Population and Harvest Surveys--Interior, FWS-26, subject to the Privacy Act of 1974. This action is necessary to meet the requirements of the Privacy Act to publish in the Federal Register a notice of amended systems of records.
