BREAKING: Federal Register: Air Plan Approval; AK; Juneau's Mendenhall Valley Second 10-Year PM10 Limited Maintenance Plan

Published: 2021-08-11 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is proposing to approve the Juneau, Mendenhall Valley, Alaska (AK) limited maintenance plan (LMP) submitted on November 10, 2020, by the Alaska Department of Environmental Conservation (ADEC or "the State"). This plan addresses the second 10-year maintenance period beyond redesignation for particulate matter with an aerodynamic diameter less than or equal to a nominal 10 micrometers (PM10).
