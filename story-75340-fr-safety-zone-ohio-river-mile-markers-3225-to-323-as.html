BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone; Ohio River, Mile Markers 322.5 to 323, Ashland, KY

Published: 2023-08-25 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is establishing a temporary safety zone for a fireworks display occurring on September 12, 2023, on the Ohio River, Ashland, KY. The safety zone will cover all navigable waters between mile marker 322.5 and 323 to protect personnel, vessels, and the marine environment from potential hazards associated with a fireworks event.
