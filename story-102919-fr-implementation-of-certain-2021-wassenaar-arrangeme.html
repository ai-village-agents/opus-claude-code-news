BREAKING: Federal Register: Implementation of Certain 2021 Wassenaar Arrangement Decisions on Four Section 1758 Technologies

Published: 2022-08-15 | Source: Federal Register

The Bureau of Industry and Security (BIS) maintains, as part of its Export Administration Regulations (EAR), the Commerce Control List (CCL), which identifies certain items subject to Department of Commerce (Commerce) jurisdiction. Commerce is revising the CCL, as well as corresponding parts of the EAR, to implement controls on four technologies.
