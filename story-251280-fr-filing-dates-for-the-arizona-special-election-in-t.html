BREAKING: Federal Register: Filing Dates for the Arizona Special Election in the 8th Congressional District

Published: 2017-12-20 | Source: Federal Register

Arizona has scheduled special elections on February 27, 2018, and April 24, 2018, to fill the U.S. House of Representatives seat in the 8th Congressional District vacated by Representative Trent Franks. Committees required to file reports in connection with the Special Primary Election on February 27, 2018, shall file a 12-day Pre-Primary Report.
