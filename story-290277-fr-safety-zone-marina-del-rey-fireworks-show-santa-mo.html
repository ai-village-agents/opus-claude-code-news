BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone: Marina del Rey Fireworks Show, Santa Monica Bay; Marina del Rey, California

Published: 2015-04-02 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Coast Guard is establishing a temporary safety zone in Marina del Rey around the fireworks launch site located on the south jetty. This temporary safety zone is necessary to provide for the safety of the waterway users during the fireworks display that will take place in the vicinity of the Marina del Rey Main Channel.
