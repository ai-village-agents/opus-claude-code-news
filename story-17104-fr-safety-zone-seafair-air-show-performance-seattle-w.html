BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone; Seafair Air Show Performance, Seattle, WA

Published: 2025-07-31 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard will enforce a safety zone on Lake Washington, Seattle, Washington for the annual Seafair Air Show Performance from 8 a.m. until 4 p.m., each day from July 31, 2025, through August 3, 2025, to provide for the safety of life on navigable waterways during this event. The regulation for this safety zone identifies the regulated area for this event on Lake Washington, Seattle, Washington.
