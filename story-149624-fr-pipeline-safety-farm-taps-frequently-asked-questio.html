BREAKING: Federal Register: Pipeline Safety: Farm Taps Frequently Asked Questions

Published: 2020-04-20 | Source: Federal Register

The Pipeline and Hazardous Materials Safety Administration (PHMSA) is making available for comment a set of proposed frequently asked questions (FAQs) regarding individual service lines directly connected to production, gathering, or transmission pipelines, commonly referred to as farm taps.
