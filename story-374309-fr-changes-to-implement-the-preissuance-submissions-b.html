BREAKING: Federal Register: Changes To Implement the Preissuance Submissions by Third Parties Provision of the Leahy-Smith America Invents Act

Published: 2012-01-05 | Source: Federal Register

The United States Patent and Trademark Office (Office) is proposing changes to the rules of patent practice to implement the preissuance submissions by third parties provision of the Leahy-Smith America Invents Act.
