BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Public Meetings of the Kansas Advisory Committee

Published: 2022-07-11 | Source: Federal Register

Notice is hereby given, pursuant to the provisions of the rules and regulations of the U.S. Commission on Civil Rights (Commission) and the Federal Advisory Committee Act that the Kansas Advisory Committee (Committee) will hold a meeting via web conference on, July 19, 2022, at 12:00 p.m. Central Time. The purpose of the meeting is for the committee to discuss potential topics and panelists for the upcoming briefing(s).
