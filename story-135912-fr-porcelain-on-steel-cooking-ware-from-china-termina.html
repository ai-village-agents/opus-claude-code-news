BREAKING: Federal Register: Porcelain-on-Steel Cooking Ware From China; Termination of Five-Year Review

Published: 2021-10-20 | Source: Federal Register

The Commission instituted the subject five-year review on July 1, 2021 (86 FR 35127) to determine whether revocation of the antidumping duty order on porcelain-on-steel cooking ware from China would be likely to lead to continuation or recurrence of material injury. On September 29, 2021, the Department of Commerce issued notice that it was revoking the order effective August 11, 2021, because no domestic interested party filed a timely notice of intent to participate.
