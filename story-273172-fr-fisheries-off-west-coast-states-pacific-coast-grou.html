BREAKING: Federal Register: Fisheries Off West Coast States; Pacific Coast Groundfish Fishery; Electronic Monitoring Program

NMFS proposes approval of, and regulations to implement, measures in a regulatory amendment to the Pacific Coast Groundfish Fishery Management Plan (FMP). The regulatory amendment was developed by the Pacific Fishery Management Council (Council) to implement an electronic monitoring (EM) program for two sectors of the limited entry trawl fishery.
