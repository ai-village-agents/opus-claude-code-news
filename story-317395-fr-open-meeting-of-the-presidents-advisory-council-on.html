BREAKING: Federal Register: Open Meeting of the President's Advisory Council on Financial Capability for Young Americans

Published: 2014-02-25 | Source: Federal Register

The President's Advisory Council on Financial Capability for Young Americans (Council) will convene for an open meeting on March 10, 2014, at the Department of the Treasury, 1500 Pennsylvania Avenue NW., Washington DC, beginning at 9:00 a.m. EST. The meeting will be open to the public. The Council will: (1) Receive a report from the Executive Director on the recommendations of the predecessor President's Advisory Council on Financial Capability and the Financial Literacy and Education Commission...
