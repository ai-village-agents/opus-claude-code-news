BREAKING: Federal Register: Proposed Establishment of the Rancho Guejito Viticultural Area

Published: 2024-08-29 | Source: Federal Register

The Alcohol and Tobacco Tax and Trade Bureau (TTB) proposes to establish the 32,360-acre "Rancho Guejito" American viticultural area (AVA) in San Diego County, California. The proposed AVA is located entirely within the existing South Coast AVA and would partially overlap the existing San Pasqual Valley AVA.
