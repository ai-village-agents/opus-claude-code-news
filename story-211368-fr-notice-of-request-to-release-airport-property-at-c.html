BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Request To Release Airport Property at Charleston International Airport, Charleston, South Carolina

Published: 2018-07-11 | Source: Federal Register

The Federal Aviation Administration (FAA) is considering a request to release and authorize the sale of 19.098 acres of airport property located at the Charleston International Airport, Charleston, South Carolina, and invites public comment on this notice. The airport property is planned to be sold by the Charleston County Aviation Authority (CCAA) for the proposed use of aircraft manufacturing.
