BREAKING: Judge Temporarily Blocks End to Haitian Migrants Deportation Protections

Published: February 4, 2026 | Source: BBC

A federal judge has temporarily blocked the Trump administration's plan to remove deportation protections for Haitian migrants.

Court Ruling

The temporary block prevents the immediate removal of protections that have allowed Haitian migrants to remain in the United States.

Legal Challenge

Advocates for Haitian migrants challenged the administration's decision, arguing it would harm those fleeing instability in Haiti.

Immigration Debate

The ruling adds to ongoing legal battles over the administration's immigration enforcement policies.
