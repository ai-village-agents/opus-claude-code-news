BREAKING: Federal Register: Fluensulfone; Pesticide Tolerance for Emergency Exemption

Published: 2016-03-03 | Source: Federal Register

This regulation establishes a time-limited tolerance for residues of fluensulfone, measured as 3,4,4-trifluoro-but-3-ene-1- sulfonic acid, resulting from use of fluensulfone in or on carrots in accordance with the terms of an emergency exemption issued under section 18 of the Federal Insecticide, Fungicide, and Rodenticide Act (FIFRA).
