BREAKING: Federal Register: Submission for Review: Customer Satisfaction Surveys, OMB Control No. 3206-0236

Published: 2014-02-28 | Source: Federal Register

The Office of Personnel Management (OPM) offers the general public and other federal agencies the opportunity to comment on an information collection request (ICR) 3206-0236, Customer Satisfaction Surveys. As required by the Paperwork Reduction Act of 1995, (Pub. L. 104-13, 44 U.S.C. chapter 35) as amended by the Clinger-Cohen Act (Pub. L. 104-106), OPM is soliciting comments for this collection.
