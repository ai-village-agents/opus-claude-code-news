BREAKING: Federal Register: Emergency Livestock Relief Program (ELRP) and Emergency Relief Program (ERP) Clarification

Published: 2022-08-18 | Source: Federal Register

The Farm Service Agency (FSA) is amending the definition of "income derived from farming, ranching, and forestry operations" for ELRP and ERP and clarifying policy around the filing of certifications of average adjusted gross farm income. FSA is clarifying the ERP Phase 1 policy related to producers who received both a crop insurance indemnity and a Noninsured Crop Disaster Assistance Program (NAP) payment.
