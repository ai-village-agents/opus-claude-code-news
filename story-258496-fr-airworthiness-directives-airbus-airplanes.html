BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Airbus Airplanes

Published: 2016-03-18 | Source: Federal Register

We are adopting a new airworthiness directive (AD) for all Airbus Model A319-131, -132, and -133 airplanes; Model A320-232 and - 233 airplanes; and Model A321-131, -231, and -232 airplanes. This AD was prompted by reports of forward engine mount attachment pins that were manufactured from discrepant raw material.
