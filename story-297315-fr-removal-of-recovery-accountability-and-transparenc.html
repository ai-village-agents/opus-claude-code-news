BREAKING: Federal Register: Removal of Recovery Accountability and Transparency Board Regulations

Published: 2015-06-24 | Source: Federal Register

The Recovery Accountability and Transparency Board (Board) previously issued regulations pertaining to the Privacy Act of 1974, Public Information and Requests, and the Board's Official Seal. These are the only Board issued regulations codified in the Code of Federal Regulations. On September 30, 2015, the Board is required by the American Recovery and Reinvestment Act of 2009, as amended, to terminate.
