BREAKING: Federal Register: Consumer Information Regulations; Fees for Use of Traction Skid Pads

Published: 2012-02-01 | Source: Federal Register

This document amends NHTSA's consumer information regulations on uniform tire quality grading standards by updating the fees currently charged for use of the traction skid pads at NHTSA's San Angelo Test Facility, formerly called the Uniform Tire Quality Grading Test Facility, in San Angelo, Texas, and by eliminating fees for course monitoring tires, which are no longer supplied by NHTSA.
