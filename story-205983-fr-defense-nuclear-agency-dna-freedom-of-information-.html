BREAKING: Federal Register: Defense Nuclear Agency (DNA) Freedom of Information Act Program

Published: 2018-05-02 | Source: Federal Register

This final rule removes DoD's regulation concerning the Defense Threat Reduction Agency (DTRA), formerly the Defense Nuclear Agency (DNA) Freedom of Information Act program. On February 6, 2018, the DoD published a revised FOIA program rule as a result of the FOIA Improvement Act of 2016. When the DoD FOIA program rule was revised, it included DoD component information and removed the requirement for component supplementary rules.
