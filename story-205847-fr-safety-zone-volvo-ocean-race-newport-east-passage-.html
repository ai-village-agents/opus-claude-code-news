BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone, Volvo Ocean Race Newport; East Passage, Narragansett Bay, RI

Published: 2018-05-01 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is establishing a temporary safety zone in the navigable waters of the East Passage, Narragansett Bay, RI, during the Volvo Ocean Race Newport marine event from May 17 to May 21, 2018. This safety zone is intended to safeguard mariners from the hazards associated with high-speed, high-performance sailing vessels competing in inshore races on the waters of the East Passage, Narragansett Bay, RI.
