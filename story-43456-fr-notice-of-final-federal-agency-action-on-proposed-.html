BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Final Federal Agency Action on Proposed Interchange in Georgia, Interstate 16 (I-16) at Old Cuyler Road, Bryan County, Georgia

Published: 2024-08-06 | Source: Federal Register

This notice announces actions taken by FHWA and other Federal agencies that are final. This final agency action relates to a proposed new interchange project, the I-16 at Old Cuyler Road Interchange Project, along I-16 at Old Cuyler Road southeast of the existing County Road (CR) 11/Jernigan Road overpass.
