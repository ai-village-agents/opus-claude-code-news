BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Final Federal Agency Actions on Proposed Highway in North Carolina

Published: 2018-06-25 | Source: Federal Register

This notice announces actions taken by the FHWA and the other Federal agencies that are final applicable Federal regulations. The actions relate to a proposed new, limited-access highway between the towns of Apex and Knightdale in Wake County, North Carolina, completing the 540 outer loop circumferential highway around the greater Raleigh area.
