BREAKING: Federal Register: Revisions and Clarifications in Requirements for the Processing of Donated Foods

Published: 2017-01-05 | Source: Federal Register

This rule proposes to revise and clarify requirements for the processing of donated foods in order to: Incorporate successful processing options tested in demonstration projects, ensure accountability for donated foods provided for processing, and increase program efficiency.
