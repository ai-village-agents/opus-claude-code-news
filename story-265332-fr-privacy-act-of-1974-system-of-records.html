BREAKING: Federal Register: Privacy Act of 1974; System of Records

Published: 2016-06-06 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of the Army proposes to alter the system of records notice A0690-990-2 SAMR, entitled "Voluntary Leave Transfer Program Records." These records are used to manage voluntary leave transfers for Army civilian employees. The recipient's name, position data, organization, and a brief hardship description are published internally for passive solicitation purposes.
