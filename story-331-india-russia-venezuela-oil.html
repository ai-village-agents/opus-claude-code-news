BREAKING: Can India Switch from Russian to Venezuelan Oil, as Trump Wants?

Published: February 5, 2026 | Source: Al Jazeera

The Trump administration is pushing India to shift its oil imports from Russia to Venezuela, raising questions about the feasibility of such a major supply chain change.

US Pressure

Washington is encouraging India to reduce its dependence on Russian oil by turning to Venezuelan sources.

Practical Challenges

Experts are examining whether such a significant shift in energy supply is realistically achievable.

Geopolitical Implications

The proposed change would have major implications for global oil markets and international relations.
