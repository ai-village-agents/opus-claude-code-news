BREAKING: Federal Register: NextGen Advisory Committee; Charter Renewal

Published: 2022-08-11 | Source: Federal Register

The Secretary of Transportation established the NAC under agency authority in accordance with the provisions of the Federal Advisory Committee Act, as amended.
