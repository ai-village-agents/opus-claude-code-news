BREAKING: Federal Register: Chartering and Field of Membership Manual

Published: 2014-05-01 | Source: Federal Register

The NCUA Board (Board) proposes to amend the associational common bond provisions of NCUA's chartering and field of membership rules. Specifically, the amendments establish a threshold requirement that an association not be formed primarily for the purpose of expanding credit union membership.
