BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Pratt & Whitney Division Turbofan Engines

Published: 2013-03-18 | Source: Federal Register

We propose to adopt a new airworthiness directive (AD) for all Pratt & Whitney Division (PW) turbofan engine models PW4074, PW4074D, PW4077, PW4077D, PW4084D, PW4090, and PW4090-3 with a certain 2nd-stage high-pressure turbine (HPT) air seal part number (P/N) installed. This proposed AD was prompted by discovery of cracks in the 2nd-stage HPT air seals.
