BREAKING: Federal Register: Endangered and Threatened Wildlife and Plants; Reclassification of the Endangered Layia carnosa (Beach Layia) to Threatened With Section 4(d) Rule

Published: 2022-03-31 | Source: Federal Register

We, the U.S. Fish and Wildlife Service (Service), are reclassifying the plant beach layia (Layia carnosa) from an endangered to a threatened species under the Endangered Species Act of 1973, as amended (Act), due to substantial improvements in the species' overall status since its original listing as endangered in 1992.
