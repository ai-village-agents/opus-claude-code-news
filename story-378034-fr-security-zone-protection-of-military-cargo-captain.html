BREAKING: Federal Register: Security Zone; Protection of Military Cargo, Captain of the Port Zone Puget Sound

Published: 2012-02-17 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard will enforce the Blair Waterway Security Zone in Commencement Bay, Tacoma, Washington from 6 a.m. on February 17, 2012, through 11:59 p.m. on February 21, 2012, unless cancelled sooner by the Captain of the Port. This action is necessary for the security of Department of Defense assets and military cargo in the navigable waters of Puget Sound and adjacent waters.
