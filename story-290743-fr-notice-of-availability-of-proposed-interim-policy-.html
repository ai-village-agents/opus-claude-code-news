BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Availability of Proposed Interim Policy Guidance for the Capital Investment Grant Program

Published: 2015-04-08 | Source: Federal Register

The Federal Transit Administration (FTA) invites public comment on interim policy guidance the agency is proposing for the Capital Investment Grant (CIG) program. The proposed interim guidance has been placed in the docket and posted on the FTA Web site.
