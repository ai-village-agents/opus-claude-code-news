BREAKING: Federal Register: National Merchant Mariner Medical Advisory Committee; August 2022 Virtual Meeting

Published: 2022-07-13 | Source: Federal Register

The National Merchant Mariner Medical Advisory Committee (Committee) will conduct virtual meetings over a series of 3 days to discuss matters relating to medical certification determinations for issuance of licenses, certificates of registry, and merchant mariners' documents, medical standards and guidelines for the physical qualifications of operators of commercial vessels, medical examiner education, and medical research.
