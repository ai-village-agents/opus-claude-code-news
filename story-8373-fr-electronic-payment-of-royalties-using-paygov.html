BREAKING: Federal Register: Electronic Payment of Royalties Using Pay.gov

Published: 2025-03-31 | Source: Federal Register

The Copyright Office is issuing a final rule to amend its regulations regarding the submission of royalty fees to the Copyright Office to require that all such fees be paid using Pay.gov.

Document Type: Rule

Agency: Library of Congress, Copyright Office, Library of Congress
