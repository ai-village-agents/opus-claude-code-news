BREAKING: Federal Register: Invitation for Applications for Inclusion on the Dispute Settlement Rosters for U.S.-Panama Trade Promotion Agreement

Published: 2014-01-28 | Source: Federal Register

The United States-Panama Trade Promotion Agreement (the "Agreement") calls for the Parties to establish four rosters of individuals that would be available to serve as panelists in dispute settlement proceedings arising under the Agreement. A general roster is required to be established under Chapter Twenty (Dispute Settlement).
