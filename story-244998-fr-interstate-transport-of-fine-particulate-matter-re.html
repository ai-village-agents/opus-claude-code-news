BREAKING: Federal Register: Interstate Transport of Fine Particulate Matter: Revision of Federal Implementation Plan Requirements for Texas

Published: 2017-09-29 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is finalizing withdrawal of the federal implementation plan (FIP) provisions that require affected electricity generating units (EGUs) in Texas to participate in Phase 2 of the Cross-State Air Pollution Rule (CSAPR) trading programs for annual emissions of sulfur dioxide (SO2) and nitrogen oxides (NOX).
