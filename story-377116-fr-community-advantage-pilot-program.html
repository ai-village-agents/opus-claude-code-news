BREAKING: Federal Register: Community Advantage Pilot Program

Published: 2012-02-08 | Source: Federal Register

On February 18, 2011, SBA published a notice introducing the Community Advantage Pilot Program. In that notice, SBA provided an overview of the Community Advantage Pilot Program requirements, including the application process to participate, and SBA modified or waived as appropriate certain regulations, which otherwise apply to the 7(a) loan program, for the Community Advantage Pilot Program.
