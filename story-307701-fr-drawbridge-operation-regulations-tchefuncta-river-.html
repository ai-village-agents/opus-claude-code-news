BREAKING: Federal Register: Drawbridge Operation Regulations; Tchefuncta River, Madisonville, LA

Published: 2015-10-26 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard has issued a temporary deviation from the operating schedule that governs the SR 22 Bridge over the Tchefuncta River, mile 2.5, at Madisonville, St. Tammany Parish, Louisiana. This deviation is necessary to complete scheduled maintenance of the bridge.
