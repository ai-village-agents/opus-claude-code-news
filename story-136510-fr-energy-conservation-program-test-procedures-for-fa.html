BREAKING: Federal Register: Energy Conservation Program: Test Procedures for Fans and Blowers

Published: 2021-10-27 | Source: Federal Register

On October 1, 2021, the U.S. Department of Energy ("DOE") published a request for information ("RFI") undertaking the preliminary stages of a rulemaking to consider potential test procedures for fans and blowers, including air circulating fan heads. The RFI provided an opportunity for submitting written comments, data, and information by November 1, 2021.
