BREAKING: Federal Register: Codex Alimentarius Commission: Meeting of the Codex Committee on Methods of Analysis and Sampling

Published: 2020-02-24 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S Codex Office is sponsoring a public meeting on April 16, 2020. The objective of the public meeting is to provide information and receive public comments on agenda items and draft United States (U.S.) positions to be discussed at the 41th Session of the Codex Committee on Methods of Analysis (CCMAS) of the Codex Alimentarius Commission, in Budapest, Hungary, May 11-15, 2020.
