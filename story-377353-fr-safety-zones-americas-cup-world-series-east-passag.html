BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zones; America's Cup World Series, East Passage, Narragansett Bay, RI

Published: 2012-02-10 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard proposes to establish two temporary safety zones in the navigable waters of the East Passage, Narragansett Bay, Rhode Island, during the America's Cup World Series sailing vessel racing event. This safety zone is intended to safeguard mariners from the hazards associated with high-speed, high-performance sailing vessels competing in America's Cup-class races on the waters of the East Passage, Narragansett Bay, Rhode Island.
