BREAKING: Federal Register: Determination of Overfishing or an Overfished Condition

Published: 2016-10-31 | Source: Federal Register

This action serves as a notice that NMFS, on behalf of the Secretary of Commerce (Secretary), has found that the following stocks are subject to overfishing--Hood Canal coho salmon and Pribilof Islands blue king crab; the following salmon stocks are approaching an overfished condition--Quillayute Fall coho and Snohomish coho; and the following stocks are still both overfished and subject to overfishing-- Western and Central North Pacific striped marlin and Atlantic and Gulf of Mexico dusky shark...
