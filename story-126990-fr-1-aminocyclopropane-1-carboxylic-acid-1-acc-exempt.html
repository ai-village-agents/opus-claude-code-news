BREAKING: Federal Register: 1-Aminocyclopropane-1-Carboxylic Acid (1-ACC); Exemption From the Requirement of a Tolerance

Published: 2021-06-28 | Source: Federal Register

This regulation establishes an exemption from the requirement of a tolerance for residues of the plant growth regulator 1- aminocyclopropane-1-carboxylic acid (1-ACC) in or on apples and stone fruit when used in accordance with good agricultural practices.
