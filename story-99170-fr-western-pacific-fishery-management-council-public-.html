BREAKING: Federal Register: Western Pacific Fishery Management Council; Public Meetings

Published: 2022-06-28 | Source: Federal Register

The Western Pacific Fishery Management Council (Council) and NMFS will convene a Western Pacific Stock Assessment Review (WPSAR) of Level 1 and Level 2 Essential Fish Habitat (EFH) Models for the Main Hawaiian Islands gray jobfish, or uku (Aprion virescens).
