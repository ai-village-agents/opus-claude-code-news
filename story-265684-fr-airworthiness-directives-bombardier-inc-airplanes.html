BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Bombardier, Inc. Airplanes

Published: 2016-06-08 | Source: Federal Register

We propose to supersede Airworthiness Directive (AD) 2015-03- 01, for all Bombardier, Inc. Model CL-600-2B19 (Regional Jet Series 100 & 440) airplanes. AD 2015-03-01 currently requires installing additional attaching hardware on the left and right fan cowl access panels and the nacelle attaching structures.
