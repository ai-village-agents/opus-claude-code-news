BREAKING: Federal Register: Temporary Suspension of Dogs Entering the United States From High-Risk Rabies Countries

Published: 2021-06-16 | Source: Federal Register

The Centers for Disease Control and Prevention (CDC) within the Department of Health and Human Services (HHS) announces a temporary suspension in the importation of dogs from high-risk rabies-enzootic countries (hereinafter referred to as high-risk country or countries) into the United States.
