BREAKING: Federal Register: Announcement of the Intent To Award a Sole-Source Cooperative Agreement to the Welcoming Initiative for Newcomers in San Diego, CA

Published: 2025-12-30 | Source: Federal Register

ACF, Office of Refugee Resettlement (ORR) announces the intent to award a single-source cooperative agreement in the amount of up to $1,000,000 to the Welcoming Initiative for Newcomers (WIN) in San Diego, CA to conduct a baseline assessment of state readiness and capacity to implement a state-centered refugee resettlement framework under the Program of Initial Resettlement.
