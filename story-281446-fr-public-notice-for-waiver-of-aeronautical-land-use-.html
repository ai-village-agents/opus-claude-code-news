BREAKING: Federal Register: Public Notice for Waiver of Aeronautical Land-Use Assurance

Published: 2016-12-13 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA is considering a proposal to change 162.71 acres of airport land from aeronautical use to non-aeronautical use and to authorize the sale of airport property located at the former Bowman Municipal Airport, BPP, Bowman, ND. The aforementioned land is not needed for aeronautical use because the new Bowman Regional Airport, BWW, Bowman, ND is open and available for public aeronautical use.
