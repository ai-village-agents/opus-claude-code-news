BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; CFM International, S.A. Turbofan Engines

Published: 2012-02-09 | Source: Federal Register

We are adopting a new airworthiness directive (AD) for certain CFM International, S.A. model CFM56-5B series turbofan engines. This AD was prompted by a normal quality sampling at CFM International, S.A. that isolated a production batch of fan blades with nonconforming geometry of mid-span shroud tips of the fan blades.
