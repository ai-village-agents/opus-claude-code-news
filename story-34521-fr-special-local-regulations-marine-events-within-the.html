BREAKING: Federal Register: Special Local Regulations; Marine Events Within the Fifth Coast Guard District

Published: 2024-04-17 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard will enforce special local regulations for a recurring marine event in the Fifth Coast Guard District to provide for the safety of life on navigable waterways from June 7 to 9, 2024. Our regulation for marine events within the Fifth Coast Guard District identifies the regulated area for this event (the Ocean City Offshore Grand Prix) in Ocean City, MD.
