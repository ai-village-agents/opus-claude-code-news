BREAKING: Federal Register: Inventory of Mercury Supply, Use, and Trade in the United States 2020 Report; Notice of Availability

Published: 2020-04-02 | Source: Federal Register

EPA is announcing the availability of the 2020 mercury inventory report, which summarizes information on U.S. mercury supply, use, and trade required to be reported by rule directly from mercury manufacturers, importers, and processors.
