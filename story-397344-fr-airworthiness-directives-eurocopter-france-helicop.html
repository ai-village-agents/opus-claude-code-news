BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Eurocopter France Helicopters

Published: 2012-09-25 | Source: Federal Register

We propose to adopt a new airworthiness directive (AD) for the Eurocopter France (Eurocopter) Model AS332C, AS332L, and AS332L1 helicopters. This proposed AD is prompted by reports of electro-valve power supply disruptions while a helicopter is on the ground, causing the landing gear to retract and the helicopter nose to drop.
