BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Realty Action: Competitive Sale of 28 Parcels of Public Land in Clark County, NV

Published: 2013-09-12 | Source: Federal Register

The Bureau of Land Management (BLM) proposes to offer 28 parcels of public land totaling approximately 440.42 acres in the Las Vegas Valley by competitive sale, at not less than the appraised fair market values (FMV). The sale parcels would be offered for sale pursuant to the Southern Nevada Public Land Management Act of 1998 (SNPLMA), as amended.
