BREAKING: Federal Register: Indiana Regulatory Program

Published: 2012-07-16 | Source: Federal Register

We, the Office of Surface Mining Reclamation and Enforcement (OSM), are approving amendments to the Indiana regulatory program (Indiana program) under the Surface Mining Control and Reclamation Act of 1977 (SMCRA or the Act).
