BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; The Boeing Company Airplanes

Published: 2022-09-26 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA is adopting a new airworthiness directive (AD) for all The Boeing Company Model 777 airplanes. This AD was prompted by a report of a crack found in a front spar lower chord undergoing an underwing longeron replacement.
