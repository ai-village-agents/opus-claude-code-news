BREAKING: Federal Register: Geographically Updated Population Certification Program

Published: 2025-04-04 | Source: Federal Register

The Census Bureau will modify the Geographically Updated Population Certification Program (GUPCP) by removing the expedited processing option for certified decennial census population and housing counts associated with updated government boundaries. The GUPCP is a fee-based service that was reinstated on January 1, 2024.
