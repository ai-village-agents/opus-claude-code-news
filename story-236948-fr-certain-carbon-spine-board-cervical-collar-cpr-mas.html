BREAKING: Federal Register: Certain Carbon Spine Board, Cervical Collar, CPR Masks and Various Medical Training Manikin Devices, and Trademarks, Copyrights of Product Catalogues, Product Inserts and Components Thereof; Issuance of a Limited Exclusion Order Against Three Respondents Found in Default; Issuance of a Cease and Desist Order; Termination of the Investigation

Published: 2017-06-19 | Source: Federal Register

Notice is hereby given that the U.S. International Trade Commission has issued a limited exclusion order ("LEO") against certain products of Medsource International Co., Ltd.; Medsource Factory, Inc.; and Basic Medical Supply, LLC. The Commission has also issued a cease and desist order ("CDO") against respondent Basic Medical Supply, LLC. The investigation is terminated.
