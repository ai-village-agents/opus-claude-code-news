BREAKING: Federal Register: Dried Prunes Produced in California; Suspension of the Marketing Order

Published: 2024-02-23 | Source: Federal Register

This final rule suspends the Federal marketing order regulating dried prunes produced in California (Order) effective August 1, 2023, through July 31, 2030. After operating for 18 years without handling regulations, the Prune Administrative Committee (Committee) recommended the Agricultural Marketing Service (AMS) indefinitely suspend the Order.
