BREAKING: Federal Register: Rent Adjustments in the Mark-to-Market Program

Published: 2024-02-28 | Source: Federal Register

The Mark-to-Market program preserves affordability and availability of affordable rental multifamily properties with federally insured mortgages, reducing rents to market levels by restructuring existing debt to levels supportable by these rents.
