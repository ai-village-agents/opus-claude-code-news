BREAKING: Federal Register: Medical Devices; 510(k) Sterility Change Master File Pilot Program

Published: 2022-05-20 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration's (FDA, Agency, or we) Center for Devices and Radiological Health (CDRH or Center) is announcing its 510(k) Sterility Change Master File Pilot Program ("510(k) Sterility Pilot Program"). The 510(k) Sterility Pilot Program is voluntary and intends to give interested companies that terminally sterilize single- use devices ("sterilization providers") using certain sterilization methods a pathway to submit a Master File for FDA's review.
