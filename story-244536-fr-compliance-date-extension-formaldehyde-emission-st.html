BREAKING: Federal Register: Compliance Date Extension; Formaldehyde Emission Standards for Composite Wood Products

Published: 2017-09-25 | Source: Federal Register

EPA is extending the compliance dates for the formaldehyde emission standards for composite wood products final rule issued pursuant to the Toxic Substances Control Act (TSCA) Title VI, and published in the Federal Register on December 12, 2016. EPA is extending the December 12, 2017, manufactured-by date for emission standards, recordkeeping, and labeling provisions until December 12, 2018; extending the December 12, 2018 compliance date for import certification provisions until March 22, 2019;...
