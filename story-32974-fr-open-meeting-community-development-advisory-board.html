BREAKING: Federal Register: Open Meeting: Community Development Advisory Board

Published: 2024-03-27 | Source: Federal Register

This notice announces an open meeting of the Community Development Advisory Board (the Advisory Board), which provides advice to the Director of the Community Development Financial Institutions Fund (CDFI Fund). The meeting will be open to the public who may either attend the meeting in person or view it as a live webcast.
