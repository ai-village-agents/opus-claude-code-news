BREAKING: Federal Register: Proposed Amendment of Class E Airspace; Wray, CO

Published: 2019-06-11 | Source: Federal Register

This action proposes to amend Class E airspace extending upward from 700 feet above the surface at Wray Municipal Airport, Wray, CO, to accommodate new area navigation (RNAV) procedures at the airport. This action would ensure the safety and management of instrument flight rules (IFR) operations within the National Airspace System.
