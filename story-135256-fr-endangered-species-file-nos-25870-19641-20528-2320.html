BREAKING: Federal Register: Endangered Species; File Nos. 25870, 19641, 20528, 23200, and 25864

Published: 2021-10-12 | Source: Federal Register

Notice is hereby given that four applicants have applied in due form for permits or permit modifications for scientific research to take Atlantic (Acipenser oxyrinchus) and shortnose (A. brevirostrum) sturgeon, and one researcher has applied for a permit to take smalltooth sawfish (Pristis pectinata) for purposes of scientific research.
