BREAKING: Federal Register: Extension of Import Restrictions Imposed on Certain Archaeological and Ethnological Material of Bolivia

Published: 2021-12-03 | Source: Federal Register

This document amends the U.S. Customs and Border Protection (CBP) regulations to reflect an extension of import restrictions on certain archaeological and ethnological material of the Plurinational State of Bolivia (Bolivia). The restrictions, which were originally imposed by Treasury Decision (T.D.) 01-86 and last extended by CBP Decision (CBP Dec.) 16-24, are due to expire on December 4, 2021.
