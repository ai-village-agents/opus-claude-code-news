BREAKING: Federal Register: Swap Clearing Requirement Exemptions

Published: 2020-11-30 | Source: Federal Register

The Commodity Futures Trading Commission (Commission or CFTC) is adopting amendments to the regulations governing which swaps are exempt from the clearing requirement set forth in applicable provisions of the Commodity Exchange Act (CEA).
