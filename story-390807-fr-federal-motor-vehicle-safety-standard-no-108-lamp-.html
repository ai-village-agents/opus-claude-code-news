BREAKING: Federal Register: Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard No. 108; Lamp, Reflective Devices, and Associated Equipment

Published: 2012-07-11 | Source: Federal Register

This notice requests comments on a technical report which evaluates new approaches for the regulation of motor vehicle lighting performance. Since 1968, the lighting standard in the United States has been updated incrementally, while lighting technologies have in some ways changed dramatically. We are requesting comments on the general approaches and specific technical merits presented in this report.
