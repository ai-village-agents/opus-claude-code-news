BREAKING: Federal Register: Hazardous Materials; Miscellaneous Amendments (RRR)

Published: 2012-04-26 | Source: Federal Register

PHMSA proposes to make miscellaneous amendments to the Hazardous Materials Regulations to update and clarify certain regulatory requirements. These proposed amendments are designed to promote safer transportation practices; eliminate unnecessary regulatory requirements; address a petition for rulemaking; incorporate a special permit into the Hazardous Materials Regulations; facilitate international commerce; and simplify the regulations.
