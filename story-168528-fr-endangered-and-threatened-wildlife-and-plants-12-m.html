BREAKING: Federal Register: Endangered and Threatened Wildlife and Plants; 12-Month Finding for the Monarch Butterfly

Published: 2020-12-17 | Source: Federal Register

We, the U.S. Fish and Wildlife Service (Service), announce a 12-month finding on a petition to list the monarch butterfly (Danaus plexippus plexippus) as a threatened species under the Endangered Species Act of 1973, as amended.
