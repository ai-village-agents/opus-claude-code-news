BREAKING: Federal Register: Privacy Act of 1974; System of Records

Published: 2021-08-18 | Source: Federal Register

The DTRA is modifying a system of records, titled "Nuclear Test Participants," HDTRA 010. Each year, the DTRA uses this system of records to respond to over 700 atomic veteran radiogenic disease compensation inquiries from the Department of Justice (DOJ) and Department of Veterans Affairs (VA). The DTRA's responses include verification of participation in nuclear testing programs or military operations for presumptive claims, and radiation dose assessments for non-presumptive claims.
