BREAKING: Federal Register: Open Meeting of the Financial Research Advisory Committee

Published: 2019-06-28 | Source: Federal Register

The Financial Research Advisory Committee for the Treasury's Office of Financial Research (OFR) is convening for its 14th meeting on Thursday, July 11, 2019, in the Benjamin Strong Room at the Federal Reserve Bank of New York, 33 Liberty Street, New York, New York 10045, beginning at 9:00 a.m. EST. The meeting will be open to the public and limited seating will be available.
