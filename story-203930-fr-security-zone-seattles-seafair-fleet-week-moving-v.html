BREAKING: Federal Register: Security Zone, Seattle's Seafair Fleet Week Moving Vessels, Puget Sound, WA

Published: 2018-04-06 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard proposes to amend its Seattle Seafair Fleet Week Moving Vessels Security Zone regulation. This amendment would change the information in annual notices of enforcement that are published both in the Federal Register and Local Notice to Mariners.
