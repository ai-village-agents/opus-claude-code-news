BREAKING: Federal Register: Documentation Related to Goods Imported From U.S. Insular Possessions

Published: 2014-01-14 | Source: Federal Register

This document proposes to amend the U.S. Customs and Border Protection (CBP) regulations to eliminate the requirement that a customs official at the port of export verify and sign CBP Form 3229, Certificate of Origin for U.S. Insular Possessions, and to require only that the importer present this form, upon CBP's request, rather than with each entry as is currently required.
