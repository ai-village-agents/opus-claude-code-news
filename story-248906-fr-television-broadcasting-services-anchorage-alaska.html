BREAKING: Federal Register: Television Broadcasting Services; Anchorage, Alaska

Published: 2017-11-17 | Source: Federal Register

The Commission grants the request by Gray Television License, LLC (Gray) to substitute channel 7 for channel 5 for station KYES-TV, Anchorage, Alaska. Gray filed comments reaffirming its interest in the proposed channel substitution and stating that if the proposal is granted, it will promptly file an application for the facilities specified in the rulemaking petition and construct the station.
