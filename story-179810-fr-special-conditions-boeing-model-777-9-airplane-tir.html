BREAKING: Federal Register: Special Conditions: Boeing Model 777-9 Airplane; Tire Debris Penetration of Fuel Tank Structure

Published: 2019-05-22 | Source: Federal Register

These special conditions are issued for The Boeing Company (Boeing) Model 777-9 airplane. This airplane will have a novel or unusual design feature when compared to the state of technology envisioned in the airworthiness standards for transport category airplanes. This design feature is composite fuel tanks that may be subject to tire-debris penetration of the fuel tanks.
