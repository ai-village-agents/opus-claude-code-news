BREAKING: Federal Register: National Practitioner Data Bank Temporary Waiver of User Fees for Eligible Entities

Published: 2020-04-17 | Source: Federal Register

Pursuant to its authority under federal regulations for the National Practitioner Data Bank (NPDB), HRSA's Division of Practitioner Data Bank announces a temporary waiver of user fees for NPDB queries from March 1, 2020, through May 31, 2020, to support our eligible entities in making credentialing, hiring, privileging, and licensing decisions in combatting the COVID-19 pandemic.
