BREAKING: Federal Register: Mercury and Air Toxics Standards (MATS) Electronic Reporting Requirements

Published: 2017-04-06 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is amending the electronic reporting requirements for the National Emission Standards for Hazardous Air Pollutants: Coal- and Oil-Fired Electric Utility Steam Generating Units (also known as the Mercury and Air Toxics Standards (MATS)) to allow for the temporary submission, through June 30, 2018, of certain reports using the portable document file (PDF) format and to correct inadvertent errors.
