BREAKING: Federal Register: Application for a Presidential Permit To Operate and Maintain Pipeline Facilities on the Border of the United States and Canada

Published: 2012-11-08 | Source: Federal Register

Notice is hereby given that the Department of State (DOS) has received from NOVA Chemicals Inc. ("NOVA Inc.") notice that by way of corporate succession, NOVA Inc. now owns, operates, and maintains three pipeline facilities (Lines 16, 18 and 19) previously owned by Polysar Hydrocarbons Inc. ("Polysar") and permitted under a 1986 Presidential Permit issued to Polysar. NOVA Inc. requests a new Presidential Permit be issued under its name with respect to Lines 16, 18 and 19.
