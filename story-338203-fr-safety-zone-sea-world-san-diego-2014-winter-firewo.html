BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone; Sea World San Diego 2014 Winter Fireworks, Mission Bay; San Diego, CA

Published: 2014-10-27 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard will enforce the Sea World San Diego 2014 Firework safety zone on November 15, December 12, and December 31, 2014. These reoccurring annual firework display events occur on the navigable waters of Mission Bay in San Diego, California. This action is necessary to provide for the safety of the marine event crew, spectators, safety vessels, and general users of the waterway.
