BREAKING: Federal Register: Rescinding Requirements Regarding Federal-Aid Contracts for Appalachian Contracts

Published: 2025-12-03 | Source: Federal Register

FHWA rescinds the regulations related to Federal-Aid Contracts (Appalachian Contracts), issued on September 30, 1974 because they are unnecessary, outdated, and duplicative of other statutory and regulatory requirements.

Agency: Transportation Department, Federal Highway Administration
