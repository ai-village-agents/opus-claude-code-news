BREAKING: Federal Register: Report: Strategies for Serving Our Women Veterans

Published: 2013-05-07 | Source: Federal Register

On May 14, 2012, the Department of Veterans Affairs (VA) published a notice in the Federal Register inviting public comment on the Draft Strategy Report (DSR) titled, Strategies for Serving Our Women Veterans. This document responds to the public comments received and affirms as final, with two identified changes, to the DSR.
