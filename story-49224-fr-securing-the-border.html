BREAKING: Federal Register: Securing the Border

Published: 2024-10-07 | Source: Federal Register

On June 3, 2024, the President signed a Proclamation under sections 212(f) and 215(a) of the Immigration and Nationality Act ("INA") suspending and limiting the entry of certain noncitizens into the United States during emergency border circumstances. DHS and DOJ ("the Departments") issued a complementary interim final rule ("IFR") shortly thereafter.
