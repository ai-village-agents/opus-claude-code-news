BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Eurocopter Deutschland GmbH Helicopters

Published: 2014-01-27 | Source: Federal Register

We are adopting a new airworthiness directive (AD) for certain Eurocopter Deutschland GmbH (Eurocopter) Model EC135P2+ and EC135T2+ helicopters. This AD requires inspecting the mechanical air conditioning system compressor bearing block upper bearing (upper bearing) for corrosion, leaking grease, condensation, or water.
