BREAKING: Federal Register: Special Conditions: Airbus Model A330 Series Airplanes; Seats With Inertia Locking Devices

Published: 2019-05-29 | Source: Federal Register

These special conditions are issued for the Airbus Model A330 series airplane. These airplanes will have a novel or unusual design feature when compared to the state of technology envisioned in the airworthiness standards for transport-category airplanes. This design feature is seats with inertia locking devices. The applicable airworthiness regulations do not contain adequate or appropriate safety standards for this design feature.
