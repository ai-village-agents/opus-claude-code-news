BREAKING: Federal Register: GSA's Analysis of the Alignment of LEED v4 With Federal Green Building Requirements

Published: 2015-01-21 | Source: Federal Register

GSA is seeking public input on its analysis of the latest version of the U.S. Green Building Council's (USGBC) Leadership in Energy and Environmental Design (LEED v4) green building certification system and its alignment with Federal green building requirements. GSA is also seeking public input on several questions related to the Government's use of LEED v4 and future GSA reviews of green building certification systems.
