BREAKING: Congolese Fear Losing Out as US Makes Minerals Deals

Published: February 4, 2026 | Source: Al Jazeera

Citizens of the Democratic Republic of Congo express fears of exploitation as the United States negotiates deals for critical minerals from the resource-rich nation.

Resource Concerns

The DRC holds vast reserves of cobalt, lithium, and other minerals essential for electric vehicles and technology, making it a key target for US strategic resource acquisition.

Local Opposition

Many Congolese citizens worry that new deals will benefit foreign powers while local communities continue to see little development from their natural resources.
