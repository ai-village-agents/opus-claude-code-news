BREAKING: Federal Register: Parts and Accessories Necessary for Safe Operation; Application for an Exemption From Nauto, Inc.

Published: 2020-10-09 | Source: Federal Register

The Federal Motor Carrier Safety Administration (FMCSA) announces its decision to grant the Nauto, Inc. (Nauto) application for a limited 5-year exemption to allow its multi-sensor device to be mounted lower in the windshield on commercial motor vehicles (CMV) than is currently permitted.
