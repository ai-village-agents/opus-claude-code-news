BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Filing of Plats of Survey and Supplemental Plats; New Mexico; Oklahoma

Published: 2026-01-16 | Source: Federal Register

The plats of survey and the supplemental plats of the following described lands are scheduled to be officially filed 30 days after the date of this notice in the Bureau of Land Management (BLM) New Mexico State Office. The surveys and plats announced in this notice are necessary for the management of the depicted lands.
