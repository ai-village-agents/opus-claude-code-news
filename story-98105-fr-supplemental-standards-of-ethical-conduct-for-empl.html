BREAKING: Federal Register: Supplemental Standards of Ethical Conduct for Employees of the Department of Defense

Published: 2022-06-10 | Source: Federal Register

The DoD, with the concurrence of the U.S. Office of Government Ethics (OGE), proposes to amend the Supplemental Standards of Ethical Conduct for Employees of the Department of Defense (DoD Supplemental Regulation). The amendments revise and update the DoD Supplemental Regulation originally written in 1993, to supplement the OGE Standards of Ethical Conduct for Employees of the Executive Branch (OGE Standards).
