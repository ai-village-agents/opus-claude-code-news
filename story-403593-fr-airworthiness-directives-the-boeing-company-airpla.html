BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; The Boeing Company Airplanes

Published: 2012-12-12 | Source: Federal Register

We are adopting a new airworthiness directive (AD) for certain The Boeing Company Model 757 airplanes. This AD was prompted by a report of in-flight fracture of the right windshield (window 1) on the flight deck and multiple reports of electrical arcs at the terminal blocks of the flight deck windshields resulting in smoke and fire.
