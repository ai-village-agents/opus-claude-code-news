BREAKING: Federal Register: UPDATE-Meeting of the Community Preventive Services Task Force (Task Force)

Published: 2013-10-23 | Source: Federal Register

The Centers for Disease Control and Prevention (CDC) announces an update to the meeting of the Community Preventive Services Task Force (Task Force). The in-person Task Force meeting is being replaced by an abbreviated conference call as a result of the lapse in the FY 2014 appropriation, which limited CDC's ability to complete the necessary scientific and logistical support for the meeting.
