BREAKING: Federal Register: Submission for Office of Management and Budget Review; ACF-800: Child Care and Development Fund (CCDF) Annual Aggregate Report (Office of Management and Budget #0970-0150)

Published: 2025-05-27 | Source: Federal Register

The Office of Child Care (OCC), Administration for Children and Families (ACF) is requesting a 3-year extension of the form ACF- 800: CCDF Annual Aggregate Report (Office of Management and Budget (OMB) #0970-0150, expiration March 31, 2025). OCC proposes to delete questions related to COVID-19 supplemental funding that are no longer relevant.
