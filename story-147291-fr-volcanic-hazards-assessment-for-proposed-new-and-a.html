BREAKING: Federal Register: Volcanic Hazards Assessment for Proposed New and Advanced Nuclear Power Plants

Published: 2020-03-20 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Nuclear Regulatory Commission (NRC) is issuing for public comment draft regulatory guide (DG), DG-4028, "Volcanic Hazards Assessment for Proposed Nuclear Power Reactor Sites." This DG proposes new guidance for facilitating NRC staff reviews of volcanic hazard assessments performed by applicants to support the siting and licensing of new nuclear power reactors.
