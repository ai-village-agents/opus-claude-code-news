BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Inventory Completion: Bureau of Land Management, Green River District, Vernal Field Office, Vernal, UT

Published: 2014-07-21 | Source: Federal Register

The Bureau of Land Management (BLM), Green River District, Vernal Field Office, Vernal, UT, has completed an inventory of human remains and associated funerary objects, in consultation with the appropriate Indian tribes or Native Hawaiian organizations, and has determined that there is a cultural affiliation between the human remains and associated funerary objects and present-day Indian tribes or Native Hawaiian organizations.
