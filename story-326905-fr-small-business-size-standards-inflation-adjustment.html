BREAKING: Federal Register: Small Business Size Standards: Inflation Adjustment to Monetary Based Size Standards

Published: 2014-06-12 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Small Business Administration (SBA or Agency) is adjusting the monetary based industry size standards (i.e., receipts, assets, net worth, and net income) for inflation that has occurred since the last adjustment in 2008. These adjustments are in addition to the recent revisions to size standards as part of the current comprehensive size standards review, as mandated by the Small Business Jobs Act of 2010 (Jobs Act).
