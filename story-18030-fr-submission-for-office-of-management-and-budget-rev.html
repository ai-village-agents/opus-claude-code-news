BREAKING: Federal Register: Submission for Office of Management and Budget Review; Unaccompanied Alien Children Sponsor Application Packet

Published: 2025-08-14 | Source: Federal Register

The Office of Refugee Resettlement (ORR), Administration for Children and Families (ACF), U.S. Department of Health and Human Services, is inviting public comments on revisions to an approved information collection. The request consists of several instruments that allow the Unaccompanied Alien Children Bureau to assess the suitability of potential sponsors for unaccompanied alien children.
