BREAKING: Federal Register: Soo Line Corporation-Control-Central Maine & Quebec Railway, Inc.

Published: 2020-01-16 | Source: Federal Register

The Surface Transportation Board (Board) is accepting for consideration the application filed on December 17, 2019, by Soo Line Corporation (Soo Line Corp.) and Central Maine & Quebec Railway US Inc. (CMQR US) (collectively, Applicants). The application seeks Board approval for Soo Line Corp., an indirect wholly owned holding company subsidiary of Canadian Pacific Railway Company (CP), to acquire control of CMQR US.
