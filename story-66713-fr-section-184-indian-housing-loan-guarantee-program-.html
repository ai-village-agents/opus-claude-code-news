BREAKING: Federal Register: Section 184 Indian Housing Loan Guarantee Program; Reduction to the Upfront and Annual Loan Guarantee Fees

Published: 2023-05-04 | Source: Federal Register

The Section 184 Indian Housing Loan Guarantee program ("Section 184 program") is a home mortgage program specifically designed for American Indian and Alaska Native families, Alaska villages, Tribes, or Tribally Designated Housing Entities. Congress established this program in 1992 to facilitate homeownership and increase access to capital in Native American communities.
