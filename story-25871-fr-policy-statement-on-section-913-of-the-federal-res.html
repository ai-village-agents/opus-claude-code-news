BREAKING: Federal Register: Policy Statement on Section 9(13) of the Federal Reserve Act

Published: 2025-12-22 | Source: Federal Register

The Board of Governors of the Federal Reserve System (Board) is rescinding its 2023 policy statement interpreting section 9(13) of the Federal Reserve Act (FRA) (2023 Policy Statement), which set out a presumption for how the Board would exercise its authority under that provision and elaborated on supervisory expectations at that time related to "novel and unprecedented" activities.
