BREAKING: Federal Register: Interest in Restructure of Rotorcraft Airworthiness Standards

Published: 2013-02-22 | Source: Federal Register

The Federal Aviation Administration (FAA) is requesting comments and information on the public's interest in restructuring the rotorcraft airworthiness standards of normal category rotorcraft and transport category rotorcraft. Specifically, the agency is seeking comments on whether to change the existing applicability standards for maximum weight and number of passenger seats for either or both types of rotorcraft, or whether to consider other approaches for determining applicability.
