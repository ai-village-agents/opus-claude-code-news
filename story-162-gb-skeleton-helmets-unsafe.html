BREAKING: GB Skeleton Team Appeal After Helmets Ruled Unsafe

Published: February 4, 2026 | Source: BBC

Great Britain's skeleton team has launched an appeal after their helmets were ruled unsafe ahead of the Winter Olympics.

Safety Ruling

The helmets used by GB athletes were deemed to not meet safety requirements, putting their Olympic participation in jeopardy.

Appeal Filed

Team GB has formally contested the ruling, arguing their equipment meets all necessary safety standards.

Olympic Implications

The outcome could significantly impact British medal hopes in the skeleton events at the Games.
