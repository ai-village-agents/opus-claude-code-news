BREAKING: Federal Register: Preview of the FY 2023 Housing Counseling Program Homeownership Initiative; Notice of Funding Opportunity

Published: 2023-07-27 | Source: Federal Register

Through this notice, HUD is announcing the publication of a preview of the Fiscal Year (FY) 2023 Homeownership Initiative Notice of Funding Opportunity (NOFO) in advance of publication on Grants.gov. HUD is making this preview available to allow interested applicants to review the preview of the NOFO, submit questions, and prepare applications. HUD intends to publish the NOFO and allow submission of applications in FY 2024.
