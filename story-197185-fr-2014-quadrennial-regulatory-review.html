BREAKING: Federal Register: 2014 Quadrennial Regulatory Review

Published: 2018-01-08 | Source: Federal Register

In this document, an Order on Reconsideration repeals and modifies several of the Commission's broadcast ownership rules. Specifically, this document repeals the Newspaper/Broadcast Cross- Ownership Rule, the Radio/Television Cross-Ownership Rule, and the attribution rule for television joint sales agreements.
