BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; BAE Systems (Operations) Limited Airplanes

Published: 2022-04-14 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA is superseding Airworthiness Directive (AD) 2017-12- 08, which applied to all BAE Systems (Operations) Limited Model BAe 146-100A, -200A, and -300A airplanes; and Model Avro 146-RJ70A, 146- RJ85A, and 146-RJ100A airplanes. AD 2017-12-08 required revising the maintenance or inspection program, as applicable, to incorporate new or revised structural inspection requirements.
