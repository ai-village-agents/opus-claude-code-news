BREAKING: Federal Register: 2014 Standards for the Renewable Fuel Standard Program

Published: 2013-11-29 | Source: Federal Register

Under section 211(o) of the Clean Air Act, the Environmental Protection Agency is required to set the renewable fuel percentage standards each November for the following year. Today's action proposes the annual percentage standards for cellulosic biofuel, biomass-based diesel, advanced biofuel, and renewable fuels that would apply to all motor vehicle gasoline and diesel produced or imported in the year 2014.
