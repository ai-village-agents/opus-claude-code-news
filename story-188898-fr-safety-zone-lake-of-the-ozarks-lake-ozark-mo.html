BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone; Lake of the Ozarks, Lake Ozark, MO

Published: 2019-09-17 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is establishing a temporary safety zone for navigable waters of the Lake of the Ozarks within 300 feet of the fireworks barge in the vicinity of mile marker 7.0. This action is necessary to provide for the safety of life on these navigable waters near the Camden on the Lake Resort, Lake Ozark, MO, during a fireworks display.
