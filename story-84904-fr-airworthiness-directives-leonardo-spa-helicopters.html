BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Leonardo S.p.a. Helicopters

Published: 2023-12-21 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA proposes to adopt a new airworthiness directive (AD) for all Leonardo S.p.a Model AW169 helicopters. This proposed AD was prompted by a determination that new or more restrictive airworthiness limitations are necessary.
