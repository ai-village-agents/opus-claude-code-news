BREAKING: Federal Register: Draft Risk Profile on Pathogens and Filth in Spices: Availability; Extension of Comment Period

Published: 2013-12-18 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA or we) is extending the comment period for the notice entitled "Draft Risk Profile on Pathogens and Filth in Spices: Availability" that appeared in the Federal Register of November 4, 2013 (78 FR 66010). In the notice, FDA requested comments that can help improve the data and information used; the analytical analyses employed; and the clarity and transparency of the draft risk profile.
