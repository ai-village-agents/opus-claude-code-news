BREAKING: Federal Register: Proposed Fee Schedule for Commercial Filming and Still Photography Permits

Published: 2013-08-22 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of the Interior and the Department of Agriculture propose to adopt a fee schedule for commercial filming and still photography conducted on public lands under their jurisdiction. The proposed fee schedule would establish land-use fees for commercial filming and still photography that are consistent for the National Park Service, the U.S. Fish and Wildlife Service, the Bureau of Land Management and U.S. Forest Service.
