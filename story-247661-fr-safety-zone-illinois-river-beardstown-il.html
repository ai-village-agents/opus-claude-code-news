BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone; Illinois River, Beardstown, IL

Published: 2017-11-01 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is establishing a temporary safety zone for all navigable waters on the Illinois River between mile marker (MM) 83.0 and MM 87.0. This action is necessary to provide for the safety of life and property on all navigable waters near Beardstown, IL while construction work is completed on new power lines extending across the river.
