BREAKING: Federal Register: Expansion of Provisional Unlawful Presence Waivers of Inadmissibility

Published: 2015-07-22 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Homeland Security (DHS) proposes to expand eligibility for provisional waivers of certain grounds of inadmissibility based on the accrual of unlawful presence to all aliens who are statutorily eligible for a waiver of such grounds, are seeking such a waiver in connection with an immigrant visa application, and meet other conditions.
