BREAKING: Federal Register: Lightweight Thermal Paper From Germany: Preliminary Results of Antidumping Duty Administrative Review; 2012-2013

Published: 2014-12-01 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Commerce (the Department) is conducting an administrative review of the antidumping duty order on lightweight thermal paper (LWTP) from Germany. The period of review (POR) is November 1, 2012, through October 31, 2013. The review covers one producer and exporter of the subject merchandise, Papierfabrik August Koehler SE (Koehler).
