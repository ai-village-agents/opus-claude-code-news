BREAKING: Federal Register: Amendment of Class D Airspace and Class E Airspace; Aberdeen, MD

Published: 2018-07-10 | Source: Federal Register

This amends Class D airspace, Class E airspace designated as an extension to a Class D surface area, and Class E airspace area extending upward from 700 feet or more above the surface at Phillips Army Air Field (AAF), Aberdeen, MD. This action accommodates airspace reconfiguration due to the decommissioning of Aberdeen non-directional radio beacon (NDB), and cancellation of the NDB approaches.
