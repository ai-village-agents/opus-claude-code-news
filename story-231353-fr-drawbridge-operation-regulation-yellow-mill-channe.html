BREAKING: Federal Register: Drawbridge Operation Regulation; Yellow Mill Channel, Bridgeport, CT

Published: 2017-04-06 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard has issued a temporary deviation from the operating schedule that governs the Stratford Avenue Bridge across Yellow Mill Channel, mile 0.3 at Bridgeport, Connecticut. This deviation is necessary to complete electrical repairs, install new rebar and concrete, repair/replace structural steel, and retrofit navigation lighting.
