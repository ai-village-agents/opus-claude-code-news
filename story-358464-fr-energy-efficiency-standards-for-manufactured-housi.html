BREAKING: Federal Register: Energy Efficiency Standards for Manufactured Housing

Published: 2013-06-25 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Department of Energy (DOE) has initiated the process to develop and publish energy standards for manufactured housing, as directed by the Energy Independence and Security Act of 2007 (EISA). To facilitate this process, enhance the quality of the standards and supporting documentation, and to allow interested parties to provide suggestions, comments, and information, DOE is publishing this request for information.
