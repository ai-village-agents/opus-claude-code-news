BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Availability of Programmatic Assessment of Greenhouse Gas Emissions From Transit Projects

The Federal Transit Administration (FTA) announces the availability of a draft Programmatic Assessment of Greenhouse Gas Emissions from Transit Projects (Programmatic Assessment) and an accompanying Greenhouse Gas Emissions (GHG) Estimator Tool (Estimator Tool).
