BREAKING: Federal Register: Common Law Tort Claims Concerning Design and Marking of DOT Specification 39 Compressed Gas Cylinders

Published: 2012-07-03 | Source: Federal Register

Federal hazardous material transportation law preempts a private cause of action which seeks to create or establish a State common law requirement applicable to the design, manufacture, or marking of a packaging, container, or packaging component that is represented, marked, certified, or sold as qualified for use in transporting hazardous material in commerce when that State common law requirement would not be substantively the same as the requirements in the HMR.
