BREAKING: Federal Register: Shoshone Resource Advisory Committee

Published: 2012-08-09 | Source: Federal Register

The Shoshone Resource Advisory Committee (Committee) will hold a meeting on September 19, 2012. The Committee is meeting as authorized under the Secure Rural Schools and Community Self-Determination Act (Pub. L. 110-343) and in compliance with the Federal Advisory Committee Act. The purpose of the meeting is to select projects to recommend for 2012 Title II funds.
