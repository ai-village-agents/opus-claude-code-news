BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Airbus Airplanes

Published: 2012-03-30 | Source: Federal Register

We are adopting a new airworthiness directive (AD) for certain Airbus Model A330-200, A330-300, A340-500, and A340-600 series airplanes. This AD requires a detailed inspection for cracked and missing nuts, and replacement of cracked or missing nuts with new nuts having the same part number. This AD was prompted by reports of cracked nuts detected during production.
