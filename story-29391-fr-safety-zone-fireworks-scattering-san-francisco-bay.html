BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone; Fireworks Scattering; San Francisco Bay, San Francisco, CA

Published: 2024-02-12 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is establishing a temporary safety zone on the navigable waters of the San Francisco Bay, off Treasure Island, CA in support of a fireworks display on February 10, 2024. The safety zone is necessary to protect persons, vessels, and the marine environment from potential hazards caused by pyrotechnics.
