BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Piper Aircraft, Inc. Airplanes

Published: 2021-04-16 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA is superseding Airworthiness Directive (AD) 2021-04-07 which applied to certain Piper Aircraft, Inc., (Piper) Models PA-46- 350P (Malibu Mirage), PA-46R-350T (Malibu Matrix), and PA-46-500TP (Malibu Meridian) airplanes. AD 2021-04-07 required identifying and correcting nonconforming stall warning heat control systems.
