BREAKING: Federal Register: Uniform Administrative Requirements, Cost Principles, and Audit Requirements

Published: 2025-11-26 | Source: Federal Register

This document announces the availability of the 2025 Compliance Supplement (2025 Supplement) for the Office of Management and Budget's guidance on uniform administrative requirements, cost principles, and audit requirements for Federal awards. This document also offers interested parties an opportunity to comment on the 2025 Supplement to inform sound decision-making regarding guidance provided through the 2026 Compliance Supplement.
