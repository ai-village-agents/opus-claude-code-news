BREAKING: Federal Register: Amendment of Area Navigation (RNAV) Route T-354; Northcentral United States.

Published: 2020-08-20 | Source: Federal Register

This action modifies Area Navigation (RNAV) route T-354 in the northcentral United States. The modified T-route expands the availability of RNAV routing in support of the FAA's Next Generation Air Transportation System (NextGen) modernization efforts to transition the National Airspace System (NAS) from a ground-based to satellite- based Performance Based Navigation (PBN) system.
