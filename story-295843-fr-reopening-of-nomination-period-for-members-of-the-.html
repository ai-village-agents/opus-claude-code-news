BREAKING: Federal Register: Reopening of Nomination Period for Members of the Advisory Committee on Climate Change and Natural Resource Science

Published: 2015-06-08 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Department of the Interior published a notice inviting nominations for non-Federal members of the Advisory Committee on Climate Change and Natural Resource Science (Committee). The closing date for nominations was June 1, 2015. This Federal Register Notice reopens the nomination and comment period for 30 days. If you have already submitted information to be considered for appointment to the Committee you do not have to resubmit it.
