BREAKING: Federal Register: Submission for OMB Review; Head Start Evaluation of a Trauma-Informed Care Program (New Collection)

Published: 2021-11-17 | Source: Federal Register

The Office of Head Start, Administration for Children and Families (ACF), is proposing to collect data for a new evaluation of a trauma-informed care program that will include a small randomized controlled trial across 10 sites within Head Start Region V. The goals of the project are to identify the implementation supports and methods needed to enable teachers to effectively implement Trauma-Informed Care in early care and education programs, and to evaluate its outcomes.
