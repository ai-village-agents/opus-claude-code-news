BREAKING: Federal Register: Disclosure to Shareholders; Pension Benefit Disclosures

Published: 2014-11-17 | Source: Federal Register

The Farm Credit Administration (FCA, we or our) is proposing to amend our regulations related to Farm Credit System (System) bank and association disclosures to shareholders and investors. The proposed rule would exclude certain employees and their associated compensation amounts from the reporting requirement in our regulations.
