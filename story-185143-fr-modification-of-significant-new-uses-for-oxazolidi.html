BREAKING: Federal Register: Modification of Significant New Uses for Oxazolidine, 3,3′-Methylenebis[5-methyl-,

Published: 2019-07-30 | Source: Federal Register

EPA is amending a significant new use rule (SNUR) under section 5(a)(2) of the Toxic Substances Control Act (TSCA) for oxazolidine, 3,3'-methylenebis[5-methyl-, which was the subject of premanufacture notice (PMN) P-03-325 and significant new use notice (SNUN) S-17-4. The chemical substance is also subject to an Order issued by EPA pursuant to TSCA section 5(e).
