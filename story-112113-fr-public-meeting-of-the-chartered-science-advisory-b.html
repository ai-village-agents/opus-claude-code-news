BREAKING: Federal Register: Public Meeting of the Chartered Science Advisory Board

Published: 2022-12-12 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) Science Advisory Board (SAB) Staff Office announces a public meeting of the chartered Science Advisory Board. The chartered SAB will meet to: (1) deliberate on the adequacy of the scientific and technical basis of the proposed rule titled Emission Standards for New, Reconstructed, and Modified Sources and Emissions Guidelines for Existing Sources: Oil and Natural Gas Sector Climate Review; and (2) discuss recommendations received from the SAB Work Group ...
