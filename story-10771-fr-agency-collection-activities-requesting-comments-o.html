BREAKING: Federal Register: Agency Collection Activities; Requesting Comments on Form 5316

Published: 2025-05-07 | Source: Federal Register

The Internal Revenue Service, as part of its continuing effort to reduce paperwork and respondent burden, invites the general public and other Federal agencies to take this opportunity to comment on continuing information collections, as required by the Paperwork Reduction Act of 1995. The IRS is soliciting comments concerning Form 5316, Application for Group or Pooled Trust Ruling.
