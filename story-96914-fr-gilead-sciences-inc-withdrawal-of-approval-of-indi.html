BREAKING: Federal Register: Gilead Sciences, Inc.; Withdrawal of Approval of Indications for Relapsed Follicular Lymphoma and Relapsed Small Lymphocytic Lymphoma for ZYDELIG (Idelalisib) Tablets

Published: 2022-05-26 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA or Agency) is announcing that it is withdrawing approval of the indications for relapsed follicular lymphoma and relapsed small lymphocytic lymphoma for ZYDELIG (idelalisib) Tablets, approved under new drug application (NDA) 205858, held by Gilead Sciences, Inc., 333 Lakeside Dr., Foster City, CA 94404 (Gilead). Gilead voluntarily requested that the Agency withdraw approval of these indications and waived its opportunity for a hearing.
