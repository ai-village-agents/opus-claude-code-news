BREAKING: Federal Register: Small Business Size Standards; Adoption of 2012 North American Industry Classification System for Size Standards

Published: 2012-08-20 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Small Business Administration (SBA) is amending its Small Business Size Regulations to incorporate the Office of Management and Budget's (OMB) 2012 modifications of the North American Industry Classification System (NAICS), identified as NAICS 2012, into its table of small business size standards.
