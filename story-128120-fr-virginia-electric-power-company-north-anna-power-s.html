BREAKING: Federal Register: Virginia Electric Power Company; North Anna Power Station, Units 1 and 2

Published: 2021-07-13 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Nuclear Regulatory Commission (NRC) is considering issuance of an amendment to Facility Operating License Nos. NPF-4 and NPF-7, issued to Virginia Electric Power Company (VEPCO), for operation of the North Anna Power Station, Units 1 and 2. The proposed amendment would add a new requirement to isolate Primary Grade water from the reactor coolant system within 1 hour following a reactor shutdown from Mode 2.
