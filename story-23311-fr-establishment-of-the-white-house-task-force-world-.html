BREAKING: Federal Register: Establishment of the White House Task Force World Cup 2026 Commission

Published: 2025-11-25 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Homeland Security, through the Office of Partnership and Engagement, is establishing the White House Task Force World Cup 2026 Commission. The Commission is an advisory body made of representatives from the private sector that provide information, advice, and recommendations to the Executive Director of the White House Task Force on the FIFA World Cup 2026 ("Task Force"). The Commission's advice and recommendations are to be specific, actionable, timely, strategic, and targeted...
