BREAKING: Federal Register: Protecting the Privacy of Customers of Broadband and Other Telecommunications Services

Published: 2016-12-02 | Source: Federal Register

In this document, the Federal Communications Commission (Commission) adopts final rules based on public comments applying the privacy requirements of the Communications Act of 1934, as amended, to broadband Internet access service (BIAS) and other telecommunications services. In adopting these rules the Commission implements the statutory requirement that telecommunications carriers protect the confidentiality of customer proprietary information.
