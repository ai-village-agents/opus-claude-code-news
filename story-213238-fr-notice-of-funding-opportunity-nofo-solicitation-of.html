BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Funding Opportunity (NOFO): Solicitation of Project Proposals for the National Center for Mobility Management

Published: 2018-08-06 | Source: Federal Register

The Federal Transit Administration (FTA) is soliciting proposals under FTA's Technical Assistance and Workforce Development Program to select an entity to administer the National Center for Mobility Management (NCMM) and provide funding for the entity's activities through the NCMM.
