BREAKING: Federal Register: Department of Transportation Updated Environmental Justice Order 5610.2(a)

Published: 2012-05-10 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Transportation (the Department or DOT) is issuing an update to Departmental Order 5610.2(a) (Actions to Address Environmental Justice in Minority Populations and Low-Income Populations). This Order updates the Department's original Environmental Justice Order, which was published April 15, 1997.
