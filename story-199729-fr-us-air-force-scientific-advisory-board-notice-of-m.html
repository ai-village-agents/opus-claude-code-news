BREAKING: Federal Register: U.S. Air Force Scientific Advisory Board Notice of Meeting

Published: 2018-02-12 | Source: Federal Register

The previous Air Force Scientific Advisory Federal Register Notice, Vol. 82, No. 247, published on Wednesday December 27, 2017, is hereby withdrawn. The United States Air Force Scientific Advisory Board Meeting to be held on 23 January 2018 is cancelled. Due to the lapse in appropriations for the Department of Defense (DoD), the Designated Federal Officer for the U.S. Air Force Scientific Advisory Board along with the DoD was unable to provide public notification required by 41 CFR 102-3.150(a) ...
