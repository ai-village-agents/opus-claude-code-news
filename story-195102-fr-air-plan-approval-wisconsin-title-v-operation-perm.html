BREAKING: Federal Register: Air Plan Approval; Wisconsin; Title V Operation Permit Program

Published: 2019-12-09 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is approving updates and revisions to the Wisconsin title V Operation Permit Program, submitted by Wisconsin pursuant to subchapter V of the Clean Air Act (Act). The revisions were submitted to update the title V program since the final approval of the program in 2001 and to change the permit fee schedule for subject facilities.
