BREAKING: Federal Register: Withdrawal of Proposed Rule on Supplemental Applications Proposing Labeling Changes for Approved Drugs and Biological Products

Published: 2018-12-14 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA, the Agency, or we) is announcing the withdrawal of the proposed rule on "Supplemental Applications Proposing Labeling Changes for Approved Drugs and Biological Products" that published in the Federal Register of November 13, 2013. FDA is taking this action in light of concerns expressed by commenters and considerations regarding Agency resources.
