BREAKING: Federal Register: Operating Permit Program: Notice of Reporting Requirements for Compliance Certifications

Published: 2014-09-15 | Source: Federal Register

Under the state operating permit programs compliance certification requirements, facilities subject to the Title V operating permit program are required to submit compliance certifications to the Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) and the permitting authority. EPA is providing notice that submittal of Title V compliance certifications to the Region 5 authorized State permitting authorities, except Michigan, fulfills the reporting requirements.
