BREAKING: Federal Register: Cuba: Revisions to License Exceptions

Published: 2016-10-17 | Source: Federal Register

This rule amends a license exception to allow cargo aboard aircraft to transit Cuba when that cargo is bound for destinations other than Cuba. This rule also authorizes export and reexport of certain items sold directly to individuals in Cuba under a license exception. Finally, this rule revises the lists of ineligible Cuban officials for purposes of certain license exceptions.
