BREAKING: Federal Register: Approval of Nebraska's Air Quality Implementation Plans

Published: 2016-10-11 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) proposes to approve the State Implementation Plan (SIP) revision submitted by the State of Nebraska. This action will amend the SIP to include revisions to Title 129 of the Nebraska Air Quality Regulations, Chapter 4, "Ambient Air Quality Standards"; Chapter 19, "Prevention of Significant Deterioration of Air Quality"; and Chapter 22, "Incinerators; Emission Standards".
