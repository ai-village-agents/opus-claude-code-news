BREAKING: Federal Register: Oregon: Final Approval of State Underground Storage Tank Program Revisions, Codification and Incorporation by Reference

Published: 2019-07-26 | Source: Federal Register

Pursuant to the Resource Conservation and Recovery Act (RCRA or Act), the Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is taking direct final action to approve revisions to the State of Oregon's Underground Storage Tank (UST) program submitted by the State. The EPA has determined that these revisions satisfy all requirements needed for program approval.
