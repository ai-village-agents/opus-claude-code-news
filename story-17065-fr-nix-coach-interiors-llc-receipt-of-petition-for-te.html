BREAKING: Federal Register: Nix Coach Interiors, LLC-Receipt of Petition for Temporary Exemption From Shoulder Belt Requirements for Side-Facing Seats on Motorcoaches

Published: 2025-07-30 | Source: Federal Register

NHTSA has received a request for a temporary exemption from Nix Coach Interiors, LLC, (petitioner) seeking a temporary exemption from a shoulder belt requirement of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard (FMVSS) No. 208, "Occupant crash protection," for side- facing seats on motorcoaches. The petitioner seeks an exemption to allow them to install Type 1 seat belts (lap belt only) at side-facing seating positions, instead of Type 2 seat belts (lap and shoulder belts) required by FMVSS No. 208.
