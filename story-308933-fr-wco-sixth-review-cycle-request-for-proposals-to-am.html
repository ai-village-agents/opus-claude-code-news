BREAKING: Federal Register: WCO Sixth Review Cycle: Request for Proposals To Amend the International Harmonized System for Implementation in 2022

Published: 2015-11-09 | Source: Federal Register

The Commission, pursuant to section 1210(b) of the Omnibus Trade and Competitiveness Act of 1988 (19 U.S.C. 3010(b), is requesting proposals from interested persons and agencies to amend the International Harmonized Commodity Description and Coding System (Harmonized System or HS) in connection with the Sixth Review Cycle of the World Customs Organization (WCO).
