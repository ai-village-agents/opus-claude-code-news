BREAKING: Federal Register: Disclosure of Information for Certain Intellectual Property Rights Enforced at the Border

Published: 2015-09-18 | Source: Federal Register

This document adopts as a final rule, with changes, interim amendments to the U.S. Customs and Border Protection (CBP) regulations pertaining to importations of merchandise bearing suspected counterfeit trademarks or trade names that are recorded with CBP.
