BREAKING: Federal Register: Privacy Act of 1974; System of Records

Published: 2024-01-12 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of the Interior (DOI) is issuing a public notice of its intent to rescind four Bureau of Land Management (BLM) Privacy Act systems of records notices (SORNs): INTERIOR/BLM-10, Vehicle Use Authorization; INTERIOR/BLM-18, Criminal Case Investigation; INTERIOR/BLM-19, Civil Trespass Case Investigations; and INTERIOR/BLM-30, Uniform Accountability System, from its existing inventory.
