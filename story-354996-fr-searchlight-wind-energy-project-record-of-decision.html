BREAKING: Federal Register: Searchlight Wind Energy Project Record of Decision (DOE/EIS-0413)

Published: 2013-05-16 | Source: Federal Register

Western Area Power Administration (Western), an agency within the U.S. Department of Energy (DOE), received a request from Searchlight Wind Energy, LLC (Searchlight) to interconnect its proposed Searchlight Wind Energy Project (Project) to Western's Davis-Mead 230- kilovolt (kV) transmission line. The Project would be located in southern Clark County, Nevada, near the town of Searchlight.
