BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Availability of the Draft Environmental Impact Statement for the Proposed Jordan Cove Energy Project, Correction

Published: 2019-04-12 | Source: Federal Register

This document corrects the Notice of Availability of the Draft Environmental Impact Statement for the proposed Jordan Cove Energy Project (CP17-495-000 and CP17-494-000) which published in the Federal Register on Friday, April 5, 2019. The attachment to the Notice, which contained the Forest Service Planning Rule Requirements, was inadvertently omitted. This corrects the omission by republishing the Notice in its entirety with the attachment.
