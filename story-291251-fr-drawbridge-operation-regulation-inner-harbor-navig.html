BREAKING: Federal Register: Drawbridge Operation Regulation; Inner Harbor Navigation Canal, New Orleans, LA

Published: 2015-04-14 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard has issued a temporary deviation from the operating schedule that governs the Senator Ted Hickey (Leon C. Simon Blvd./Seabrook) bascule bridge across the Inner Harbor Navigation Canal, mile 4.6, at New Orleans, Louisiana. The deviation is necessary to ensure the safety of participants in the Ochsner Ironman 70.3 New Orleans event as they travel across the bridge as part of the bike and run courses.
