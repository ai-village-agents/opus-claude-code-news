BREAKING: Federal Register: Freedom of Information Act Predisclosure Notice

Published: 2023-03-09 | Source: Federal Register

This notice informs submitters who reported COVID-19 data in 2020 for the High-Impact Area Distribution that HRSA received a Freedom of Information Act (FOIA) request for data reported to HHS that was used in determining COVID-19 High-Impact Area Distribution payments under the Provider Relief Fund. Specifically, the request seeks certain information pertaining to providers who did not receive COVID-19 High- Impact Area Distribution payments.
