BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Austro Engine GmbH Engines

Published: 2021-09-14 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA proposes to supersede Airworthiness Directive (AD) 2021-09-04, which applies to all Austro Engine GmbH E4 and E4P model diesel piston engines. AD 2021-09-04 requires replacing a certain oil pump as well as the oil filter and engine oil.
