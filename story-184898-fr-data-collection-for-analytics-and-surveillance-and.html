BREAKING: Federal Register: Data Collection for Analytics and Surveillance and Market-Based Rate Purposes

Published: 2019-07-26 | Source: Federal Register

The Federal Energy Regulatory Commission (Commission) is revising its regulations governing market-based rates for public utilities. The Commission will collect certain information currently filed in the electric market-based rate program in a consolidated and streamlined manner through a relational database.
