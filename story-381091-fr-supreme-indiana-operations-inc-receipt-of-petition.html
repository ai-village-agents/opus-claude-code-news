BREAKING: Federal Register: Supreme Indiana Operations, Inc., Receipt of Petition for Decision of Inconsequential Noncompliance

Published: 2012-03-22 | Source: Federal Register

Startrans, a division of Supreme Indiana Operations, Inc., (Startrans) \1\ has determined that certain Startrans trucks, buses, and multifunction school activity buses (MFSAB) manufactured from 2006 through 2011, do not fully comply with paragraph S5.3 of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard (FMVSS) No. 120, Tire selection and rims and motor home/recreation vehicle trailer load carrying capacity information for motor vehicles with a GVWR of more than 4,536 kilograms (10,000 pounds).
