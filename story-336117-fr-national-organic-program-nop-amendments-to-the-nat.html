BREAKING: Federal Register: National Organic Program (NOP); Amendments to the National List of Allowed and Prohibited Substances (Crops and Processing)

Published: 2014-09-30 | Source: Federal Register

This final rule amends the U.S. Department of Agriculture's (USDA's) National List of Allowed and Prohibited Substances (National List) to reflect a recommendation submitted to the Secretary of Agriculture (Secretary) by the National Organic Standards Board (NOSB) on October 18, 2012, and removes two previously expired substances.
