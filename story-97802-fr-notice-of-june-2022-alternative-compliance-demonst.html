BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of June 2022 Alternative Compliance Demonstration Approach for Certain Small Refineries Under the Renewable Fuel Standard Program

Published: 2022-06-08 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is providing notice of its action to provide an alternative compliance demonstration approach (the "June 2022 Compliance Action") to certain small refineries whose 2016, 2017, and/or 2018 petitions for small refinery exemptions (SREs) under the Renewable Fuel Standard (RFS) program were denied in April and June 2022 after being judicially remanded to EPA for reconsideration.
