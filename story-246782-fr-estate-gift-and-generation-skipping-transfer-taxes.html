BREAKING: Federal Register: Estate, Gift, and Generation-Skipping Transfer Taxes; Restrictions on Liquidation of an Interest

Published: 2017-10-20 | Source: Federal Register

This document withdraws proposed regulations concerning the estate, gift and generation-skipping transfer (GST) tax treatment of lapses of liquidation rights in family-controlled entities, as well as the valuation of interests in family-controlled corporations and partnerships for estate, gift, and GST tax purposes.
