BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Availability of Proposed National Pollutant Discharge Elimination System (NPDES) General Permit for Bulk Fuel Facilities in Guam

Published: 2025-04-17 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) Region 9 is proposing to reissue a NPDES general permit authorizing discharges of treated effluent from bulk fuel storage facilities located in Guam (Permit No. GUG000001). EPA proposes to reissue the permit upon completion of the notice and comment period and after consideration of all comments received. Upon issuance, the permit will be valid for five years.
