BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Piaggio Aero Industries S.p.A.

Published: 2020-09-29 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA is adopting a new airworthiness directive (AD) for Piaggio Aero Industries S.p.A. Model P-180 airplanes. This AD results from mandatory continuing airworthiness information (MCAI) issued by an aviation authority of another country to identify and correct an unsafe condition on an aviation product.
