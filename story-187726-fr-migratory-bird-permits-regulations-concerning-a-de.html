BREAKING: Federal Register: Migratory Bird Permits; Regulations Concerning a Depredation Order

Published: 2019-09-03 | Source: Federal Register

We are following up on a 2013 proposal to remove regulations that set forth a means for controlling damage caused by certain depredating scrub jays and Steller's jays. We had proposed to remove the regulations that set forth a depredation order for these species to protect nut crops in certain counties in Washington and Oregon.
