BREAKING: Federal Register: Adoption and Recirculation of the Final Environmental Impact Statement for the Wilton Rancheria Fee-to-Trust and Casino Project

Published: 2018-09-14 | Source: Federal Register

The National Indian Gaming Commission (NIGC) is adopting the Bureau of Indian Affairs (BIA), Department of the Interior, December 2016 Final Environmental Impact Statement (the "BIA EIS") for the Wilton Rancheria (Tribe) Fee-to-Trust and Casino Project in Elk Grove, California.
