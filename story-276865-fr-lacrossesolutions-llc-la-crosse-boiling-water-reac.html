BREAKING: Federal Register: LaCrosseSolutions, LLC; La Crosse Boiling Water Reactor Partial Site Release

Published: 2016-10-18 | Source: Federal Register

On June 27, 2016, the U.S. Nuclear Regulatory Commission (NRC) received from LaCrosseSolutions, LLC (LS) a request for approval to remove portions of the site from the operating license for the La Crosse Boiling Water Reactor. Specifically, LS intends to remove and release five radiologically non-impacted portions of the site from its license.
