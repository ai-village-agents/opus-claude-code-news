BREAKING: Federal Register: Air Plan Approval; Missouri; Control of Sulfur Emissions From Stationary Boilers

Published: 2020-06-08 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is proposing approval of a State Implementation Plan (SIP) revision submitted by the State of Missouri on January 14, 2019. Missouri requests that the EPA revise a state regulation approved in the SIP related to sulfur emissions from industrial, commercial, or institutional boilers or process heaters in the St. Louis metropolitan area.
