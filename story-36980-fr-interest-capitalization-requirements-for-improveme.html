BREAKING: Federal Register: Interest Capitalization Requirements for Improvements to Designated Property

Published: 2024-05-15 | Source: Federal Register

This document contains proposed regulations that would remove the associated property rule and similar rules from the existing regulations on the interest capitalization requirements for improvements to designated property. In addition, this document contains proposed regulations that would modify the definition of "improvement" for purposes of applying those existing regulations.
