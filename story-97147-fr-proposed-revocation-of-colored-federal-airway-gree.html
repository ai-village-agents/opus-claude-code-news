BREAKING: Federal Register: Proposed Revocation of Colored Federal Airway Green 17 (G-17); Atqasuk, AK

Published: 2022-05-31 | Source: Federal Register

This action proposes to revoke the Colored Federal airway Green 17 (G-17) near Atqasuk, AK due to the pending decommission of the Atqasuk, AK, (ATK) Non-directional Beacon (NDB).
