BREAKING: Federal Register: Agency Information Collection Activities: Requests for Comments; Clearance of a New or Renewed Approval of Information Collection: Bird/Other Wildlife Strike Report

Published: 2025-09-24 | Source: Federal Register

In accordance with the Paperwork Reduction Act of 1995, FAA invites public comments about our intention to request the Office of Management and Budget (OMB) approval to renew an information collection. The collection involves voluntary reporting of bird/other wildlife strike information following a wildlife strike incident with aircraft.
