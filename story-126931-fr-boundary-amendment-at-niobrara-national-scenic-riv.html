BREAKING: Federal Register: Boundary Amendment at Niobrara National Scenic River

Published: 2021-06-28 | Source: Federal Register

Pursuant to the Judgment in Simmons v. Jarvis, 8:13CV98, Doc #120 (D. Neb. 2016), notice is hereby given that the boundary of Niobrara National Scenic River is amended to remove one tract of unimproved, non-Federal land, which is a portion of Tract 00001 containing 12.45 acres more or less, located in Cherry County, Nebraska, currently within the Scenic Riverway boundary.
