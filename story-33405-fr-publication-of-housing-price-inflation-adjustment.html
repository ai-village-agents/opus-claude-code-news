BREAKING: Federal Register: Publication of Housing Price Inflation Adjustment

Published: 2024-04-02 | Source: Federal Register

The DoD is announcing the 2024 rent threshold under the Servicemembers Civil Relief Act (SCRA). Applying the housing price inflation adjustment, the maximum monthly rental amount calculated as of January 1, 2024, is $9,812.12.
