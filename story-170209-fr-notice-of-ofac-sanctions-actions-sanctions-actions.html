BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of OFAC Sanctions Actions; Sanctions Actions Pursuant to Executive Order 13661 and Executive Order 13662

Published: 2019-02-01 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Department of the Treasury's Office of Foreign Assets Control (OFAC) is publishing the names of persons who are no longer subject to the prohibitions imposed pursuant to Executive Order 13661 of March 14, 2014, "Blocking Property of Additional Persons Contributing to the Situation in Ukraine", and Executive Order 13662 of March 20, 2014, "Blocking Property of Additional Persons Contributing to the Situation in Ukraine."
