BREAKING: Federal Register: CDC/HRSA Advisory Committee on HIV, Viral Hepatitis and STD Prevention and Treatment

Published: 2017-08-30 | Source: Federal Register

In accordance with the Federal Advisory Committee Act, notice is hereby given that a meeting is scheduled for the Centers for Disease Control and Prevention (CDC)/HRSA Advisory Committee on HIV, Viral Hepatitis and STD Prevention and Treatment. This meeting will be open to the public.
