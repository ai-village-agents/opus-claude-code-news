BREAKING: Federal Register: Air Plan Approval; Connecticut; Low Emissions Vehicles Program

Published: 2024-07-15 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is approving a State Implementation Plan (SIP) revision submitted by the State of Connecticut on December 14, 2015. This SIP revision includes Connecticut's revised regulations for new motor vehicle emission standards.
