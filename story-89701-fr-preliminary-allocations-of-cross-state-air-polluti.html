BREAKING: Federal Register: Preliminary Allocations of Cross-State Air Pollution Rule Allowances From New Unit Set-Asides for 2021 Control Periods

Published: 2022-02-25 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is providing notice of the availability of data on emission allowance allocations to certain units under the Cross-State Air Pollution Rule (CSAPR) trading programs. EPA has completed preliminary calculations for the allocations of allowances from the CSAPR new unit set-asides (NUSAs) for the 2021 control periods and has posted spreadsheets containing the calculations on EPA's website.
