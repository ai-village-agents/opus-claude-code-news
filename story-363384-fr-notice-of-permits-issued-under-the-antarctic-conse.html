BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Permits Issued Under the Antarctic Conservation Act of 1978

Published: 2013-08-20 | Source: Federal Register

On March 31, 2003, the National Science Foundation (NSF) issued a final rule that authorized the collection of meteorites in Antarctica for scientific purposes only. In addition the regulations provide requirements for appropriate collection, handling, documentation, and curation of Antarctic meteorites to preserve their scientific value.
