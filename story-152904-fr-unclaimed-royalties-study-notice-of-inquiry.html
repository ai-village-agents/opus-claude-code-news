BREAKING: Federal Register: Unclaimed Royalties Study: Notice of Inquiry

Published: 2020-06-02 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Copyright Office is undertaking a study as directed by the Music Modernization Act to evaluate best practices that the newly-established mechanical licensing collective ("MLC") may implement to: Identify and locate musical work copyright owners and unclaimed accrued royalties held by the collective; encourage musical work copyright owners to claim their royalties; and reduce the incidence of unclaimed royalties.
