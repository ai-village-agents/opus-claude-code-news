BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of a National Public Stakeholder Forum on the Rural Energy for America Program

Published: 2015-02-03 | Source: Federal Register

The Rural Business-Cooperative Service (RBS), an Agency within the USDA Rural Development Mission area, will hold an informational meeting, entitled "Rural Energy for America Program (REAP) National Stakeholder Forum," associated with the recently published REAP rule and notice. The Stakeholder forum is open to the public, and participation can be in person or via webinar.
