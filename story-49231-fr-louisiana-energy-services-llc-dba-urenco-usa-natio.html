BREAKING: Federal Register: Louisiana Energy Services, LLC, dba Urenco USA; National Enrichment Facility; License Amendment Application

Published: 2024-10-08 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Nuclear Regulatory Commission (NRC) staff accepted and docketed an application for the amendment of Special Nuclear Materials License No. SNM-2010, submitted by Louisiana Energy Services, LLC, dba Urenco USA dated November 30, 2023. The amended license would authorize the applicant to modify the National Enrichment Facility, located in Eunice, New Mexico, to increase enrichment from 5.5 weight percent Uranium-235 (U-235) to less than 10 weight percent U-235.
