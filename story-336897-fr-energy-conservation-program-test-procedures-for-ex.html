BREAKING: Federal Register: Energy Conservation Program: Test Procedures for External Power Supplies

Published: 2014-10-09 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Department of Energy is proposing to revise its test procedure for external power supplies. These proposed revisions, if adopted, would harmonize the instrumentation resolution and uncertainty requirements with the second edition of the International Electrotechnical Commission (IEC) 62301 standard when measuring standby power along with other international standards programs.
