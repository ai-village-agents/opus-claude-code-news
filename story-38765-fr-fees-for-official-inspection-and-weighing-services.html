BREAKING: Federal Register: Fees for Official Inspection and Weighing Services Under the United Stated Grain Standards Act

Published: 2024-06-06 | Source: Federal Register

The Agricultural Marketing Service (AMS), Federal Grain Inspection Service (FGIS or the Service) is revising the fee schedule for official inspection and weighing services performed under the United States Grain Standards Act (USGSA), as amended. Specifically, this interim rule announces the new rates for the remainder of fiscal year 2024, and until such time as new fees are set by a final rule.
