BREAKING: Federal Register: Semiannual Regulatory Agenda

Published: 2025-09-22 | Source: Federal Register

The Consumer Financial Protection Bureau (CFPB) is publishing this agenda as part of the Spring 2025 Unified Agenda of Federal Regulatory and Deregulatory Actions. The CFPB reasonably anticipates having the regulatory matters identified below under consideration during the period from June 2025 to May 2026.
