BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Availability of Draft NPDES General Permits for Hydroelectric Generating Facilities in Massachusetts and New Hampshire: The HYDRO General Permit

Published: 2018-08-20 | Source: Federal Register

The Director of the Office of Ecosystem Protection, U.S. Environmental Protection Agency (EPA)--Region 1, is providing a Notice of Availability of Draft National Pollutant Discharge Elimination System (NPDES) General Permits for certain discharges from hydroelectric generating facilities to certain waters of the Commonwealth of Massachusetts and the State of New Hampshire.
