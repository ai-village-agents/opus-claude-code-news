BREAKING: Federal Register: Pilot Program for Dental Health Care Access

Published: 2019-12-13 | Source: Federal Register

Upon Congressional approval, VA intends to develop and implement a pilot program designed to increase veteran access to health care and support services at no additional cost to VA or veterans. The initial demonstration project VA proposes under this pilot program is to improve dental health care access for veterans by connecting them with community-based, pro bono or discounted, dental service providers.
