BREAKING: Federal Register: Universal LNG Solutions Inc.; Noble Americas Gas & Power Corp.; Shell NA LNG LLC; Toyota Motor Engineering & Manufacturing North America, Inc.; Conocophillips Alaska Natural Gas Corporation; BG LNG Services, LLC: Orders Granting Authority To Import and Export Natural Gas, To Import and Export Liquefied Natural Gas and Vacating Prior Authority During April 2014

Published: 2014-05-28 | Source: Federal Register

The Office of Fossil Energy (FE) of the Department of Energy gives notice that during March 2014, it issued orders granting authority to import and export natural gas, to import and export liquefied natural gas and to vacate prior authority. These orders are summarized in the attached appendix and may be found on the FE Web site at http://www.fossil.energy.gov/programs/gasregulation/authorizations/ Orders-2014.html.
