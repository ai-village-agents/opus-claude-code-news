BREAKING: Federal Register: Florida; Approval of State Plan for Control of Emissions From Commercial and Industrial Solid Waste Incineration Units

Published: 2025-08-25 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency is taking final action to approve a state plan submitted by the State of Florida, through the Florida Department of Environmental Protection (FDEP) on May 31, 2017, that was supplemented on December 19, 2017, and February 2, 2018, for implementing and enforcing the Emissions Guidelines (EG) applicable to existing Commercial and Industrial Solid Waste Incineration (CISWI) units.
