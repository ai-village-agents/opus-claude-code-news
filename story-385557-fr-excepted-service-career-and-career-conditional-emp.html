BREAKING: Federal Register: Excepted Service, Career and Career-Conditional Employment; and Pathways Programs

Published: 2012-05-11 | Source: Federal Register

The Office of Personnel Management (OPM) is issuing final regulations implementing the Pathways Programs established by E.O. 13562, signed December 27, 2010. As directed by the President, the Pathways Programs provide clear paths to Federal internships and potential careers in Government for students and recent graduates.
