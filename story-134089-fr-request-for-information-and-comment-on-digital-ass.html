BREAKING: Federal Register: Request for Information and Comment on Digital Assets and Related Technologies

Published: 2021-09-28 | Source: Federal Register

On July 27, 2021, the NCUA Board (Board) published in the Federal Register a document entitled "Request for Information and Comment on Digital Assets and Related Technologies" (RFI) and invited comments from interested parties regarding the current and potential impact of activities connected to digital assets and related technologies on federally insured credit unions (FICUs), related entities, and the NCUA.
