BREAKING: Man Sentenced to Life in Prison for 2024 Attempt on Trump's Life

Published: February 4, 2026 | Source: BBC

A man has been sentenced to life in prison for his 2024 attempt on Donald Trump's life, a court has ruled.

Sentencing

The defendant received the maximum sentence of life imprisonment for the assassination attempt on the former and current president.

2024 Attempt

The attack occurred during the 2024 election campaign, when Trump survived an assassination attempt.

Justice Served

Prosecutors had sought the maximum penalty for what they described as an attack on democracy.
