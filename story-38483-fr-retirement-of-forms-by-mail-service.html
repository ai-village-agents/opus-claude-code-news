BREAKING: Federal Register: Retirement of Forms by Mail Service

Published: 2024-06-03 | Source: Federal Register

Through this notice, the Department of Homeland Security (DHS), United States Citizenship and Immigration Services (USCIS) announces the retirement of the Forms by Mail service on July 18, 2024. The Forms by Mail service provides paper copies of available USCIS forms by mail to individuals who submit a request online using the uscis.gov website (accessible at https://www.uscis.gov/forms/forms- information/forms-by-mail) or by mail.
