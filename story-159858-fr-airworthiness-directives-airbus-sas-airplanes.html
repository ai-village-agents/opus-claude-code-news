BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Airbus SAS Airplanes

Published: 2020-08-28 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA is superseding Airworthiness Directive (AD) 2019-03- 11, which applied to certain Airbus SAS Model A350-941 and -1041 airplanes. AD 2019-03-11 required revising the existing maintenance or inspection program, as applicable, to incorporate new or more restrictive maintenance requirements and airworthiness limitations.
