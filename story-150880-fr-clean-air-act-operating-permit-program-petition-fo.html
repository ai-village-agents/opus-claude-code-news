BREAKING: Federal Register: Clean Air Act Operating Permit Program; Petition for Objection to State Operating Permit for the U.S. Department of Energy-Hanford Operations, Benton County, Washington

Published: 2020-05-06 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) Administrator signed an Order, dated February 19, 2020, denying a petition dated July 18, 2019, filed by Mr. Bill Green of Richland, Washington. The Petition requested that the EPA object to a Clean Air Act (CAA) title V operating permit (Permit No. 00-05-006, Renewal 3) issued by the Washington State Department of Ecology (Ecology) to the U.S. Department of Energy--Hanford Operations (DOE) for the Hanford site located in Benton County, Washington.
