BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone; Chesapeake Bay, Baltimore, MD

Published: 2025-09-25 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is establishing a temporary safety zone for navigable waters of the Chesapeake Bay within 500 yards of the M/V W- SAPPHIRE. The safety zone is needed to protect personnel, vessels, and the marine environment from potential hazards created by the presence of explosive cargo on board the vessel.
