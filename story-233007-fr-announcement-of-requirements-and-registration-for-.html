BREAKING: Federal Register: Announcement of Requirements and Registration for Healthy Behavior Challenge

Published: 2017-04-28 | Source: Federal Register

The Centers for Disease Control and Prevention (CDC) located within the Department of Health and Human Services (HHS) announces the launch of the Healthy Behavior Data Challenge. The Healthy Behavior Data Challenge responds to the call for new ways to address the challenges and limitations of self-reported health surveillance information and tap into the potential of innovative data sources and alternative methodologies for public health surveillance.
