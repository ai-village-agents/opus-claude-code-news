BREAKING: Federal Register: Fisheries of the Northeastern United States; Atlantic Surfclam and Ocean Quahog Fisheries

Published: 2015-07-20 | Source: Federal Register

This final rule implements an information collection program for the Atlantic surfclam and ocean quahog fisheries. The information collection program is intended to obtain more detailed information about individuals and businesses that hold fishery quota allocation in these individual transferable quota fisheries.
