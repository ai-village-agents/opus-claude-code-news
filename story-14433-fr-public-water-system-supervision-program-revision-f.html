BREAKING: Federal Register: Public Water System Supervision Program Revision for New York; Notice of Approval and Opportunity for Public Comment and Public Hearing

Published: 2025-06-26 | Source: Federal Register

Public notice is hereby given that the state of New York has revised its approved Public Water System Supervision Program. New York has adopted drinking water regulations for the Stage 2 Disinfection Byproducts Rule. The EPA has determined that New York's revised regulations meet all minimum federal requirements, and that they are no less stringent than the corresponding federal regulations.
