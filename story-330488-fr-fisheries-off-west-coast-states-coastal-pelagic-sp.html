BREAKING: Federal Register: Fisheries Off West Coast States; Coastal Pelagic Species Fisheries; Annual Specifications

Published: 2014-07-25 | Source: Federal Register

NMFS issues this final rule to implement the annual catch limit (ACL), and associated annual reference points for Pacific sardine in the U.S. exclusive economic zone (EEZ) off the Pacific coast for the fishing season of July 1, 2014, through June 30, 2015. The 2014-2015 ACL for Pacific sardine is 23,293 metric tons (mt).
