BREAKING: Federal Register: Determination That Levitra (Vardenafil Hydrochloride) Oral Tablets, 5 Milligrams, 10 Milligrams, and 20 Milligrams, Were Not Withdrawn From Sale for Reasons of Safety or Effectiveness

Published: 2023-05-03 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA or Agency) has determined that Levitra (vardenafil hydrochloride) oral tablets, 5 milligrams (mg), 10 mg, and 20 mg, were not withdrawn from sale for reasons of safety or effectiveness.
