BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Intent To Grant Exclusive License Between National Energy Technology Laboratory and Corrosion Solutions

Published: 2012-11-16 | Source: Federal Register

This notice is issued in accordance with 35 U.S.C. 209(c)(1) and 37 CFR 404.7(a)(1)(i). The National Energy Technology Laboratory (NETL) hereby gives notice of its intent to grant an exclusive license to practice the inventions described and claimed in U.S. Patent No 7,553,517, issued June 30, 2009, entitled "Method of applying a cerium diffusion coating to a metallic alloy," to Corrosion Solutions having its principal place of business in Eugene, Oregon.
