BREAKING: Federal Register: Household Water Well System Grant Program Announcement of Application Deadlines

Published: 2014-03-26 | Source: Federal Register

The Rural Utilities Service (RUS) announces its Household Water Well System Grant Program (HWWS) funding availability and application window for Fiscal Year (FY) 2014. RUS will make grants to qualified private non-profit organizations to establish lending programs for homeowners to borrow up to $11,000 to construct or repair household water wells for an existing home.
