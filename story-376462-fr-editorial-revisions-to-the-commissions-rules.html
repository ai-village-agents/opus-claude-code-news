BREAKING: Federal Register: Editorial Revisions to the Commission's Rules

Published: 2012-02-01 | Source: Federal Register

This document makes a number of non-substantive, editorial revisions to the Commission's rules. These revisions are made to delete certain rule provisions that are without current legal effect and therefore are obsolete. These non-substantive revisions are part of the Commission's ongoing examination and improvement of FCC processes and procedures.
