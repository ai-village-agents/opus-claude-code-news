BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone; Chicago Match Cup Race; Lake Michigan; Chicago, IL

Published: 2013-06-24 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard will enforce the safety zone on Lake Michigan near Chicago, Illinois for the 2013 AWMRT Chicago Match Cup Race. This zone will be enforced from 8 a.m. until 8 p.m. on each day of August 6, 7, 8, 9, 10, and 11, 2013. This action is necessary and intended to ensure safety of life on the navigable waters during the 2013 AWMRT Chicago Match Cup.
