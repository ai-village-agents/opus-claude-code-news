BREAKING: Federal Register: Modification of Class D and Class E Airspace; Idaho Falls Regional Airport, ID

Published: 2022-10-03 | Source: Federal Register

This action modifies the Class D and E surface areas, the Class E airspace area designated as an extension to a Class D or Class E surface area, the Class E airspace extending upward from 700 feet above the surface, and the Class E airspace extending upward from 1,200 feet above the surface at Idaho Falls Regional Airport, ID.
