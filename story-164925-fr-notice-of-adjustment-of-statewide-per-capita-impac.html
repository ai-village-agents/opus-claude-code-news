BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Adjustment of Statewide per Capita Impact Indicator

Published: 2020-11-02 | Source: Federal Register

FEMA gives notice that the statewide per capita impact indicator under the Public Assistance program for disasters declared on or after October 1, 2020, will be increased.
