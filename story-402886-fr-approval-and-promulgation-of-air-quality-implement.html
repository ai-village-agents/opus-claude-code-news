BREAKING: Federal Register: Approval and Promulgation of Air Quality Implementation Plans; Delaware; Control of Stationary Generator Emissions

Published: 2012-12-04 | Source: Federal Register

EPA is taking direct final action to approve a revision to the Delaware Department of Natural Resources and Environmental Control (DNREC) State Implementation Plan (SIP). The revision amends Regulation 1102--PERMITS, Appendix A to provide permit exemptions for certain internal combustion engines. EPA is approving this revision in accordance with the requirements of the Clean Air Act (CAA).
