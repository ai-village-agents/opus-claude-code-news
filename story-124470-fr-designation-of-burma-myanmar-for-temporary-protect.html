BREAKING: Federal Register: Designation of Burma (Myanmar) for Temporary Protected Status

Published: 2021-05-25 | Source: Federal Register

Through this Notice, DHS announces that the Secretary of Homeland Security is designating Burma for TPS for 18 months, effective May 25, 2021, through November 25, 2022.
