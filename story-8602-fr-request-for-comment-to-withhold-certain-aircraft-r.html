BREAKING: Federal Register: Request for Comment To Withhold Certain Aircraft Registration Information From Public Dissemination

Published: 2025-04-03 | Source: Federal Register

The Federal Aviation Administration (FAA) is seeking comment on the impacts of removing certain aircraft registration data from public display on the FAA website, including through current search functions and published reports.
