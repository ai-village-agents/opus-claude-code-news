BREAKING: Federal Register: Wilderness Eligibility Reclassifications, Wrangell-St. Elias National Park and Preserve

Published: 2012-04-17 | Source: Federal Register

Wrangell-St. Elias National Park and Preserve has completed an analysis per NPS Management Policies 2006 6.2.1 for the reclassification of lands currently deemed to be eligible wilderness based on the 1986 eligibility review conducted as part of the park's General Management Plan (GMP).
