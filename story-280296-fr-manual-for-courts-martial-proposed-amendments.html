BREAKING: Federal Register: Manual for Courts-Martial; Proposed Amendments

Published: 2016-11-29 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Defense requests comments on proposed changes to the Manual for Courts-Martial, United States (2012 ed.) (MCM). The proposed changes concern the rules of procedure and evidence applicable in trials by courts-martial. The approval authority for these changes is the President.
