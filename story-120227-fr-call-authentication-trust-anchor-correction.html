BREAKING: Federal Register: Call Authentication Trust Anchor; Correction

Published: 2021-03-31 | Source: Federal Register

This document corrects the comment due date for the Paperwork Reduction Act requirements of a proposed rule published in the Federal Register of February 17, 2021, regarding STIR/SHAKEN caller ID authentication.
