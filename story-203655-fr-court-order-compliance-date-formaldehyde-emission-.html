BREAKING: Federal Register: Court Order; Compliance Date; Formaldehyde Emission Standards for Composite Wood Products

Published: 2018-04-04 | Source: Federal Register

On March 13, 2018, the United States District Court for the Northern District of California issued an order in the case of Sierra Club and A Community Voice-Louisiana vs. Scott Pruitt, which resulted in the compliance date for emission standards, recordkeeping, and labeling (i.e., the manufactured-by date or import-by date) becoming June 1, 2018, rather than December 12, 2018.
