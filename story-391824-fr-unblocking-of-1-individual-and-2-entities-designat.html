BREAKING: Federal Register: Unblocking of 1 Individual and 2 Entities Designated Pursuant to Executive Order 13315

Published: 2012-07-24 | Source: Federal Register

The Treasury Department's Office of Foreign Assets Control ("OFAC") is removing the names of 1 individual and 2 entities whose property and interests in property are blocked pursuant to Executive Order 13315 of August 28, 2003, "Blocking Property of the Former Iraqi Regime, Its Senior Officials and Their Family Members, and Taking Certain Other Actions" from the list of Specially Designated Nationals and Blocked Persons ("SDN List").
