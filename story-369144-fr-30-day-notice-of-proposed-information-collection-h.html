BREAKING: Federal Register: 30-Day Notice of Proposed Information Collection: HUD-Owned Real Estate-Sales Contract and Addendums

Published: 2013-11-04 | Source: Federal Register

On October 25, 2013 at 78 FR 64145, HUD inadvertently published a 30 day notice of proposed information collection entitled HUD-Owned Real Estate-Sales Contract and Addendums (2502-0306). HUD will republish the notice in the Federal Register at a later date. This notice withdraws the notice published on October 25, 2013.
