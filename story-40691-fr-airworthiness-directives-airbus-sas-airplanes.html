BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Airbus SAS Airplanes

Published: 2024-07-01 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA proposes to adopt a new airworthiness directive (AD) for all Airbus SAS Model A350-941 and A350-1041 airplanes. This proposed AD was prompted by a report the thrust reverser and pylon thermal blankets were found damaged due to air leaking from the pre- cooler exchanger (PCE).
