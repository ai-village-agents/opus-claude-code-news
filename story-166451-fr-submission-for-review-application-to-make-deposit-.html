BREAKING: Federal Register: Submission for Review: Application To Make Deposit or Redeposit (CSRS)-SF 2803 and Application To Make Service Credit Payment for Civilian Service (FERS)-SF 3108

Published: 2020-11-20 | Source: Federal Register

The Retirement Services, Office of Personnel Management (OPM) offers the general public and other Federal agencies the opportunity to comment on a revised information collection, Application to Make Deposit or Redeposit (CSRS)--SF 2803 and Application to Make Service Credit Payment for Civilian Service (FERS)--SF 3108, and includes revision to the Privacy Act Statement.
