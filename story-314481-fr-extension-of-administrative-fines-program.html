BREAKING: Federal Register: Extension of Administrative Fines Program

Published: 2014-01-21 | Source: Federal Register

Under the Federal Election Commission's Administrative Fines Program ("AFP"), the Commission may assess civil monetary penalties for certain violations of the reporting requirements of the Federal Election Campaign Act of 1971, as amended ("FECA"). Congress recently amended FECA to extend the end date of the statutory authorization for the AFP to December 31, 2018.
