BREAKING: Federal Register: Summary of Commission Practice Relating to Administrative Protective Orders

Published: 2020-02-10 | Source: Federal Register

Since February 1991, the U.S. International Trade Commission ("Commission") has published in the Federal Register reports on the status of its practice with respect to breaches of its administrative protective orders ("APOs") under title VII of the Tariff Act of 1930, in response to a direction contained in the Conference Report to the Customs and Trade Act of 1990.
