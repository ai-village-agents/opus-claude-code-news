BREAKING: Federal Register: Revisions to the California State Implementation Plan, Feather River Air Quality Management District; Correction

Published: 2019-07-31 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) published a direct final rule in the Federal Register on July 8, 2015, that approved revisions to the Feather River Air Quality Management District portion of the California State Implementation Plan (SIP) but did not include all the necessary amendatory language to list all the SIP revisions that were being approved. This document corrects this error.
