BREAKING: Federal Register: Draft Revised Human Immunodeficiency Virus (HIV) Organ Policy Equity Act Safeguards and Research Criteria for Transplantation of Organs Infected With HIV

Published: 2024-11-27 | Source: Federal Register

The HOPE Act requires the Secretary of Health and Human Services (the Secretary) to develop and publish criteria for research involving the transplantation of organs from donors with HIV to recipients with HIV. In 2015, the National Institutes of Health (NIH), and the U.S. Department of Health and Human Services (HHS) published research criteria applicable to such transplants, which have been in effect for all transplants involving organs from donors with HIV as authorized by the HOPE Act.
