BREAKING: Federal Register: Agreement on Social Security Between the United States and the Federative Republic of Brazil; Entry Into Force

Published: 2018-10-16 | Source: Federal Register

We are giving notice of an agreement coordinating the United States (U.S.) and Brazilian social security programs effective on October 1, 2018.
