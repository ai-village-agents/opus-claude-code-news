BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone; Fireworks Displays Within the Fifth Coast Guard District

Published: 2023-11-28 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard will enforce a safety zone for a fireworks display at The Wharf DC on December 2, 2023, to provide for the safety of life on navigable waterways during this event. Our regulation for Fireworks Displays within the Fifth Coast Guard District identifies the safety zone for this event in Washington, DC.
