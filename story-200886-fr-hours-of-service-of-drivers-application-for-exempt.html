BREAKING: Federal Register: Hours of Service of Drivers: Application for Exemption; G4S Secure Solutions (USA), Inc. (G4S)

Published: 2018-02-27 | Source: Federal Register

FMCSA announces its decision to deny the application of G4S Secure Solutions (USA), Inc. (G4S), for an exemption from the requirement that its drivers use electronic logging devices (ELDs) to record their hours of service (HOS). G4S requested the exemption for all its drivers of customer/government-owned vehicles used intermittently to perform passenger transportation.
