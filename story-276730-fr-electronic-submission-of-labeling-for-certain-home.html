BREAKING: Federal Register: Electronic Submission of Labeling for Certain Home-Use Medical Devices

Published: 2016-10-17 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA) is proposing to implement provisions of the Federal Food, Drug, and Cosmetic Act (the FD&C Act) to require electronic submission of the device label and package insert of certain home-use devices when these devices are listed with FDA.
