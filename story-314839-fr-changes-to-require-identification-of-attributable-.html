BREAKING: Federal Register: Changes To Require Identification of Attributable Owner

Published: 2014-01-24 | Source: Federal Register

The United States Patent and Trademark Office (Office) is proposing changes to the rules of practice to facilitate the examination of patent applications and to provide greater transparency concerning the ownership of patent applications and patents.
