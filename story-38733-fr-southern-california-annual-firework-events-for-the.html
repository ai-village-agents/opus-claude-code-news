BREAKING: Federal Register: Southern California Annual Firework Events for the Los Angeles Long Beach Captain of the Port Zone

Published: 2024-06-06 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard will enforce safety zones for annually reoccurring firework events taking place the first week of July 2024 in the Los Angeles-Long Beach Captain of the Port Zone. This action is necessary and intended to provide for the safety of life and property on the navigable waterways during these events.
