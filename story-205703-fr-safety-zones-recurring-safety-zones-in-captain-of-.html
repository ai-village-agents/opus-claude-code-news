BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zones; Recurring Safety Zones in Captain of the Port Sault Sainte Marie Zone

Published: 2018-04-27 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is updating its recurring safety zones regulations in the Captain of the Port Sault Sainte Marie Zone. This rule updates eighteen safety zone locations, dates, and sizes, adds three safety zones, removes two established safety zones, and reformats the regulations into an easier to read table format.
