BREAKING: Federal Register: Noise Compatibility Program for Hollywood Burbank Airport, Burbank, Los Angeles County, California

Published: 2025-11-25 | Source: Federal Register

The Federal Aviation Administration (FAA) announces its determination that the noise exposure maps submitted by Burbank- Glendale-Pasadena Airport Authority (BGPAA) for Hollywood Burbank Airport is compliant with applicable statutory and regulatory requirements.
