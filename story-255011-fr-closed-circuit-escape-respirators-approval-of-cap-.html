BREAKING: Federal Register: Closed-Circuit Escape Respirators; Approval of Cap 3 Device for Underground Coal Mining

Published: 2016-02-10 | Source: Federal Register

The National Institute for Occupational Safety and Health (NIOSH) and the Mine Safety and Health Administration (MSHA) have approved the first large-capacity (Cap 3) closed-circuit escape respirator (CCER) for use in underground coal mining, under the NIOSH new regulatory standard. Accordingly, respirator manufacturers may continue to manufacture, label, and sell large-capacity CCERs approved under the former regulatory standard (those CCERs with a rated service time of greater than 50 minutes) ...
