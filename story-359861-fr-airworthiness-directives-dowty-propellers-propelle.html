BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Dowty Propellers Propellers

Published: 2013-07-10 | Source: Federal Register

We are revising an existing airworthiness directive (AD) that applies to all Dowty Propellers R408/6-123-F/17 model propellers. That AD currently requires initial applications of sealant between the bus bar assembly and the backplate assembly of certain line-replaceable units, and repetitive applications of sealant on all R408/6-123-F/17 model propellers.
