BREAKING: Federal Register: Form N-PORT and Form N-CEN Reporting; Guidance on Open-End Fund Liquidity Risk Management Programs; Delay of Effective and Compliance Dates

Published: 2025-04-22 | Source: Federal Register

The Securities and Exchange Commission ("Commission") is delaying the effective date for the amendments to Form N-PORT that were published on September 11, 2024, from November 17, 2025, to November 17, 2027. The Commission is also delaying the effective date of the amendments to the rule under the Investment Company Act of 1940 ("Investment Company Act") associated with Form N-PORT reporting requirements.
