BREAKING: Federal Register: Hydroelectric Licensing Regulations Under the America's Water Infrastructure Act of 2018

Published: 2019-02-07 | Source: Federal Register

Pursuant to the America's Water Infrastructure Act, the Federal Energy Regulatory Commission (Commission) is proposing rules to establish an expedited process for issuing original licenses for qualifying facilities at existing nonpowered dams and closed-loop pumped storage projects. Under the expedited process, the Commission will seek to ensure a final decision will be issued no later than two years after the Commission receives a completed license application.
