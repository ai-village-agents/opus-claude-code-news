BREAKING: Federal Register: North American Free Trade Agreement; Invitation for Applications for Inclusion on the Chapter 19 Roster

Published: 2016-10-26 | Source: Federal Register

Chapter 19 of the North American Free Trade Agreement (NAFTA) provides for the establishment of a roster of individuals to serve on binational panels convened to review final determinations in antidumping or countervailing duty (AD/CV") proceedings and amendments to AD/CVD statutes of a NAFTA Party. The United States annually renews its selections for the Chapter 19 roster.
