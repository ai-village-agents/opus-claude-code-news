BREAKING: Federal Register: Waiver of Passport and Visa Requirements Due to an Unforeseen Emergency

Published: 2016-03-08 | Source: Federal Register

This rule proposes to reinstate a 1996 amendment to a regulation in title 8 of the Code of Federal Regulations regarding a discretionary waiver of certain documentary requirements for nonimmigrants seeking admission to the United States.
