BREAKING: Federal Register: Federal Prevailing Rate Advisory Committee; Cancellation of Upcoming Virtual Public Meeting

Published: 2021-01-28 | Source: Federal Register

The Federal Prevailing Rate Advisory Committee is issuing this notice to cancel the February 18, 2021, public meeting scheduled to be held via teleconference, beginning at 10:00 a.m. (EDT). The Federal Register notice announcing this virtual public meeting was published Wednesday, January 13, 2021, at 86 FR 2709.
