BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone; North Atlantic Ocean, Ocean City, NJ

Published: 2017-08-07 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard will enforce the North Atlantic Ocean, Ocean City, NJ, safety zone from 9:00 p.m. through 11:59 p.m. on October 10, 2017. This action is necessary to ensure safety of life on the navigable waters of the United States immediately prior to, during, and immediately after a fireworks display event.
