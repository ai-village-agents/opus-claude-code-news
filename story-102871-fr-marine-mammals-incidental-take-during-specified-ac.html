BREAKING: Federal Register: Marine Mammals; Incidental Take During Specified Activities: The Gulf of Alaska

Published: 2022-08-15 | Source: Federal Register

We, the U.S. Fish and Wildlife Service, in response to a request from the United States Coast Guard, propose to issue regulations authorizing the nonlethal, incidental, unintentional take by harassment of small numbers of northern sea otters during marine construction and pile driving in the Gulf of Alaska coastal waters.
