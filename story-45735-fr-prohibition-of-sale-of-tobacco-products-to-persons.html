BREAKING: Federal Register: Prohibition of Sale of Tobacco Products to Persons Younger Than 21 Years of Age

Published: 2024-08-30 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration is issuing a final rule to make conforming changes as required by the Further Consolidated Appropriations Act, 2020 (Appropriations Act), which established a new Federal minimum age of sale for tobacco products.
