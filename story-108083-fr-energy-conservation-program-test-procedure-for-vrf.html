BREAKING: Federal Register: Energy Conservation Program: Test Procedure for VRF Multi-Split Systems

Published: 2022-10-20 | Source: Federal Register

This final rule amends the test procedure for variable refrigerant flow ("VRF") multi-split air conditioners and heat pumps ("VRF multi-split systems") to incorporate by reference the latest version of the applicable industry test standard. This final rule also adopts the integrated energy efficiency ratio metric in its test procedures for VRF multi-split systems.
