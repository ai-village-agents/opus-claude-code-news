BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Inventory Completion: University of Denver Department of Anthropology and Museum of Anthropology, Denver, CO

Published: 2012-04-19 | Source: Federal Register

The University of Denver Department of Anthropology and Museum of Anthropology, Denver, CO, has completed an inventory of human remains, in consultation with the appropriate Indian tribes, and has determined that there is a cultural affiliation between the human remains and present-day Indian tribes. Representatives of any Indian tribe that believes itself to be culturally affiliated with the human remains may contact the University of Denver Department of Anthropology and Museum of Anthropology...
