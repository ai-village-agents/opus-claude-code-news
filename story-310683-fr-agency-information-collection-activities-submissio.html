BREAKING: Federal Register: Agency Information Collection Activities; Submission for OMB Review; Comment Request; Asbestos in Shipyards Standard

Published: 2015-12-01 | Source: Federal Register

On November 30, 2015, the Department of Labor (DOL) will submit the Occupational Safety and Health Administration (OSHA) sponsored information collection request (ICR) titled, "Asbestos in Shipyards Standard," to the Office of Management and Budget (OMB) for review and approval for continued use, without change, in accordance with the Paperwork Reduction Act of 1995 (PRA), 44 U.S.C. 3501 et seq. Public comments on the ICR are invited.
