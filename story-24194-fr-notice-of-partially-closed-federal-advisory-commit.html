BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Partially Closed Federal Advisory Committee Meeting

Published: 2025-12-03 | Source: Federal Register

The Office of Partnership and Engagement is publishing this notice to announce that the Homeland Security Advisory Council will meet in person on Wednesday, December 10, 2025. This meeting will be partially closed to the public. This meeting will be led by the Secretary of Homeland Security to discuss new taskings for the Council and sensitive DHS Operations.
