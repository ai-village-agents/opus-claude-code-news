BREAKING: Federal Register: Privacy Act of 1974; System of Records

Published: 2018-07-19 | Source: Federal Register

Pursuant to the provisions of the Privacy Act of 1974, as amended, the Department of the Interior proposes to modify the Department of the Interior system of records titled, "Payroll, Attendance, Retirement, and Leave Records--Interior, DOI-85."
