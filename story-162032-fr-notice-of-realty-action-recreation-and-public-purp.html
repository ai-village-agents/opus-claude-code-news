BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Realty Action: Recreation and Public Purposes (R&PP) Act Classification and Conveyance, Wyoming

Published: 2020-09-25 | Source: Federal Register

The Bureau of Land Management (BLM) has examined and found suitable for classification to convey 227.5 acres of public land to Park County under the provisions of the Recreation and Public Purposes (R&PP) Act, as amended. As a political subdivision of the State of Wyoming, Park County is a qualified applicant under the R&PP Act.
