BREAKING: Federal Register: Non-Power Production or Utilization Facility License Renewal

Published: 2024-12-30 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Nuclear Regulatory Commission (NRC) is amending its regulations that govern the license renewal process for certain production or utilization facilities. In this final rule, the NRC collectively refers to these facilities as non-power production or utilization facilities (NPUFs). This final rule revises the definitions of "non-power reactor," "research reactor," and "testing facility."
