BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Airbus SAS Airplanes

Published: 2024-09-11 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA proposes to supersede Airworthiness Directive (AD) 2023-05-02, which applies to certain Airbus SAS Model A318, A319, A320 and A321 series airplanes. AD 2023-05-02 requires revising the existing maintenance or inspection program, as applicable, to incorporate new or more restrictive airworthiness limitations.
