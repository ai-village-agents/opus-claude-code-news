BREAKING: Federal Register: Amendment of Class E Airspace; Edenton, NC

Published: 2025-04-01 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA is correcting a final rule that appeared in the Federal Register on March 10, 2025. The final rule amended Class E airspace extending upward from 700 feet above the surface for ECU Health Chowan Hospital Heliport, Edenton, NC, to accommodate new area navigation (RNAV) global positioning system (GPS) standard instrument approach procedures serving the heliport.
