BREAKING: Federal Register: Privacy Act of 1974; System of Records

Published: 2021-03-22 | Source: Federal Register

In accordance with the Privacy Act of 1974, the U.S. Department of Homeland Security (DHS) proposes to establish a new DHS system of records titled, "DHS/Federal Emergency Management Agency (FEMA)-015 Fraud Investigations System of Records." This system of records allows DHS/FEMA to collect and maintain records on individuals who are being investigated for or involved in an investigation relating to the misuse of federal disaster funds and/or benefits.
