BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone, MBTA Railroad Bridge Replacement Project-Annisquam River, Gloucester, MA

Published: 2019-08-19 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard proposes to establish a temporary safety zone for the navigable waters within 100 yards of the Massachusetts Bay Transportation Authority (MBTA) Railroad Bridge, at mile 0.7, across the Annisquam River, Gloucester, Massachusetts, from November 1, 2019 through June 30, 2023. The temporary safety zone is necessary to protect personnel, vessels and the marine environment from potential hazards created during the replacement project of the MBTA Railroad Bridge.
