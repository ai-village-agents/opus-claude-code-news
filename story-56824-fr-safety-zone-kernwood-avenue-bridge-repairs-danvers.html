BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone; Kernwood Avenue Bridge Repairs-Danvers River, Salem, MA, and Beverly, MA

Published: 2024-12-30 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is amending the current temporary safety zone in the vicinity of the Kernwood Avenue Bridge between Salem, MA and Beverly, MA. We are extending the effective period by 11 months to November 30, 2025, and are updating the enforcement schedule of the safety zone. The temporary safety zone is necessary to protect personnel, vessels, and the marine environment from potential hazards created during emergency bridge repairs.
