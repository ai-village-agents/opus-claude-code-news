BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone; Canalside 4th of July, Lake Erie, Buffalo, NY

Published: 2019-07-01 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is establishing a temporary safety zone for navigable waters within a 560-foot radius of the launch site located near the Buffalo Outer Harbor, Buffalo, NY. This safety zone is intended to restrict vessels from portions of the Buffalo Outer Harbor and Lake Erie during the Canalside 4th of July fireworks display.
