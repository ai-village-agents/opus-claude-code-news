BREAKING: Federal Register: Meeting Notice (Closed)-Marine Transportation System National Advisory Council

Published: 2014-07-25 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Department of Transportation (DOT) and Maritime Administration (MARAD) announce that the Marine Transportation System National Advisory Council (MTSNAC) will hold a closed door meeting on August 14, 2014. After a review of all comments received from the interested public during its two national maritime symposiums, MARAD is now planning to provide the council a compilation of select concepts for further consideration.
