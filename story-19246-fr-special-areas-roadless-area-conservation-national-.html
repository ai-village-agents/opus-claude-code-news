BREAKING: Federal Register: Special Areas; Roadless Area Conservation; National Forest System Lands

Published: 2025-08-29 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Department of Agriculture is initiating an environmental impact statement (EIS) and rulemaking concerning management of inventoried roadless areas on approximately 44.7 million acres of National Forest System lands, including in Alaska.
