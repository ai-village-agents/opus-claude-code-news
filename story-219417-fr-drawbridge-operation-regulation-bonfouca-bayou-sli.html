BREAKING: Federal Register: Drawbridge Operation Regulation; Bonfouca Bayou, Slidell, LA

Published: 2018-10-23 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard has issued a temporary deviation from the operating schedule that governs the State Route 433 Bridge across Bonfouca Bayou, mile 7.0, at Slidell, St. Tammany Parish, Louisiana. This deviation is necessary to perform maintenance. This deviation allows the bridge to remain in the closed-to-navigation position during nighttime hours for approximately 42 days.
