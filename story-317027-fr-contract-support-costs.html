BREAKING: Federal Register: Contract Support Costs

Published: 2014-02-21 | Source: Federal Register

The Assistant Secretary--Indian Affairs, in conjunction with the Acting Director, Indian Health Service (IHS), will conduct a consultation session with Indian tribes to work together to identify long-term solutions concerning contract support costs (CSC) as it relates to the Fiscal Year (FY) 2014 Consolidated Appropriations Act.
