BREAKING: Federal Register: Dual Shipping Labels Discontinued

Published: 2024-10-15 | Source: Federal Register

The Postal Service is proposing to amend Mailing Standards of the United States Postal Service, Domestic Mail Manual (DMM[supreg]) to discontinue the use of dual shipping labels.

Agency: Postal Service
