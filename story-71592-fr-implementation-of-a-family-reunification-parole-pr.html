BREAKING: Federal Register: Implementation of a Family Reunification Parole Process for Colombians

Published: 2023-07-10 | Source: Federal Register

This notice announces the U.S. Department of Homeland Security's (DHS) creation and implementation of a family reunification parole process (FRP) for Colombians.
