BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Intent to License Government-Owned Inventions; Intent to License on a Partially-Exclusive Basis

Published: 2012-11-30 | Source: Federal Register

The inventions listed below are assigned to the United States Government as represented by the Secretary of the Army. The US Army Edgewood Chemical Biological Center intends to license these inventions on a partially-exclusive basis to Lighthouse Worldwide Solutions, Inc, a California Corporation with principal offices at, 47300 Landing Parkway, Fremont, CA 94538.
