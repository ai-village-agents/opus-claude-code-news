BREAKING: Federal Register: Draft General Management Plan and Draft Environmental Impact Statement, Fort Raleigh National Historic Site, North Carolina

Published: 2013-04-02 | Source: Federal Register

Pursuant to the National Environmental Policy Act of 1969, 42 U.S.C. 4332(2)(C), the National Park Service announces the availability of a Draft Environmental Impact Statement for the General Management Plan (EIS/GMP) for Fort Raleigh National Historic Site, North Carolina. The draft describes and analyzes several alternatives to guide the management of the site over the next 15 to 20 years.
