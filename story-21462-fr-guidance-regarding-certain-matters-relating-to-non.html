BREAKING: Federal Register: Guidance Regarding Certain Matters Relating to Nonrecognition of Gain or Loss in Corporate Separations, Incorporations, and Reorganizations; Multi-Year Reporting Requirements for Corporate Separations and Related Transactions; Withdrawal

Published: 2025-09-30 | Source: Federal Register

This document withdraws a notice of proposed rulemaking containing proposed regulations relating to corporate separations, incorporations, and reorganizations qualifying, in whole or in part, for nonrecognition of gain or loss. This document also withdraws a notice of proposed rulemaking containing proposed regulations that would have required multi-year tax reporting for corporate separations and related transactions.
