BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Bombardier, Inc. Airplanes

Published: 2012-12-04 | Source: Federal Register

We propose to supersede an existing airworthiness directive (AD) that applies to certain Bombardier, Inc. Model DHC-8-400 series airplanes. The existing AD currently requires a free-play check for excessive free-play of the shaft swaged bearing installed in the tailstock end of each elevator power control unit (PCU), and replacing any PCU on which the bearing exceeds allowable limits with a serviceable PCU.
