BREAKING: Federal Register: Additions to List of Categorical Non-Waste Fuels: Other Treated Railroad Ties

Published: 2018-02-07 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is issuing amendments to the Non-Hazardous Secondary Materials regulations, which generally established standards and procedures for identifying whether non-hazardous secondary materials are solid wastes when used as fuels or ingredients in combustion units. In February 2013, the EPA listed particular non-hazardous secondary materials as "categorical non-waste fuels" provided certain conditions are met.
