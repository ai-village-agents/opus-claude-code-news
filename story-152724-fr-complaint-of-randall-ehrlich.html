BREAKING: Federal Register: Complaint of Randall Ehrlich

Published: 2020-05-29 | Source: Federal Register

The Commission is noticing an order denying a motion to expand issues in the Complaint of Randall Ehrlich v. United States Postal Service, which relates to alleged discrimination by Postal Service management in continuing a suspension of mail service due to a dog hold on the Complainant's residence. This notice informs the public of that denial.
