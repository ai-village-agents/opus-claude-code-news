BREAKING: Federal Register: Environmental Technologies Trade Advisory Committee (ETTAC), Request for Nominations from U.S. State Officials

Published: 2012-12-26 | Source: Federal Register

This notice sets forth a request for nominations from U.S. state officials, or representatives from associations that represent U.S. states, to serve on the Environmental Technologies Trade Advisory Committee (ETTAC). One person will be appointed under this notice increasing the total number of members to 36.
