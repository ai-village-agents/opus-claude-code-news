BREAKING: Federal Register: Implementation of Executive Order on Access to Affordable Life-Saving Medications; Rescission of Regulation

Published: 2021-10-01 | Source: Federal Register

HHS is rescinding the final rule entitled "Implementation of Executive Order on Access to Affordable Life-Saving Medications," published in the December 23, 2020, Federal Register (2020 Rule). HHS is rescinding the 2020 Rule due to the excessive administrative costs and burdens that implementation would have imposed on health centers.
