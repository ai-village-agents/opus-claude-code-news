BREAKING: Federal Register: Board of Directors; Request for Nomination

Published: 2025-04-11 | Source: Federal Register

The Unified Carrier Registration (UCR) Plan Board of Directors (Board) is requesting nominations of qualified individuals for possible appointment by the FMCSA to one of the Board's motor carrier industry positions. Each nominee must be a representative of "a motor carrier that falls within the smallest fleet fee bracket." The term for this position will begin immediately upon appointment and expire on May 31, 2027.
