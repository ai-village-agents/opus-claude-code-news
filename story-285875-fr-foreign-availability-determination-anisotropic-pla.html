BREAKING: Federal Register: Foreign Availability Determination: Anisotropic Plasma Dry Etching Equipment

Published: 2015-02-09 | Source: Federal Register

This notice announces that the Under Secretary for Industry and Security has determined that foreign availability exists for anisotropic plasma dry etching equipment controlled for national security reasons under Export Control Classification Number (ECCN) 3B001.c on the Commerce Control List. This foreign availability determination is in response to a submission from the Semiconductor Equipment and Materials International (SEMI) industry association.
