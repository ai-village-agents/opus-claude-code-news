BREAKING: Federal Register: Relief From HUD Public Housing and Section 8 Requirements Available During CY 2019 to Public Housing Agencies To Assist With Recovery and Relief Efforts on Behalf of Families Affected by Presidentially Declared Major Disasters

Published: 2019-10-09 | Source: Federal Register

This notification advises the public that HUD, in order to more effectively and expeditiously respond to Presidentially declared Major Disaster Declarations (MDD), is establishing for calendar year (CY) 2019 an expedited process for the review of requests for relief from HUD regulatory and/or administrative requirements ("HUD requirements") for Public Housing Agencies (PHAs) located in counties that are included in MDDs.
