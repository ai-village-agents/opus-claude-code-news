BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Public Meeting of the Connecticut Advisory Committee; Correction to Date

Published: 2022-01-31 | Source: Federal Register

The Commission on Civil Rights is holding a briefing of the Connecticut Advisory Committee on Tuesday, February 15, 2022, at 4:00 p.m. ET. This notice corrects the previous date of Monday, February 14, 2022, to Tuesday, February 15, 2022, at 4:00 p.m. ET. The notice is in the Federal Register of Friday, January 21, 2022, in FR Doc. 2022- 01119, in the second and third columns of page 3279.
