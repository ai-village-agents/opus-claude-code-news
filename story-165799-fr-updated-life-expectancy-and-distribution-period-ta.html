BREAKING: Federal Register: Updated Life Expectancy and Distribution Period Tables Used for Purposes of Determining Minimum Required Distributions

This document sets forth final regulations providing guidance relating to the life expectancy and distribution period tables that are used to calculate required minimum distributions from qualified retirement plans, individual retirement accounts and annuities, and certain other tax-favored employer-provided retirement arrangements.
