BREAKING: Federal Register: Proposed Class II Reinstatement of Terminated Oil and Gas Lease NDM 105578, Williams County, ND

Published: 2025-08-04 | Source: Federal Register

In accordance with the Mineral Leasing Act of 1920, as amended, Sapazi Holdings, LLC and TDB Resources LP filed a timely petition for reinstatement of oil and gas lease NDMT105396602 (NDM 105578) for land in Williams County, North Dakota. The petition was accompanied by all required rentals and royalties accruing from September 1, 2021, the date of termination. No leases were issued that affect these lands.
