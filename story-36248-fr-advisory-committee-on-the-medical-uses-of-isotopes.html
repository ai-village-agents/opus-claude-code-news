BREAKING: Federal Register: Advisory Committee on the Medical Uses of Isotopes: Meeting Notice

Published: 2024-05-07 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Nuclear Regulatory Commission (NRC) will convene a meeting of the Advisory Committee on the Medical Uses of Isotopes (ACMUI) on June 5, 2024 to discuss the ACMUI's draft subcommittee report on the NRC's draft interim staff guidance for the implementation of training and experience requirements in the NRC's regulations, "Medical Use of Byproduct Material."
