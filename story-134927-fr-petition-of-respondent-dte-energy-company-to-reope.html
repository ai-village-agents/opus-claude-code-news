BREAKING: Federal Register: Petition of Respondent DTE Energy Company To Reopen and Modify Decision and Order

Published: 2021-10-06 | Source: Federal Register

DTE Energy Company ("DTE" or "the company") has requested that the Federal Trade Commission ("FTC" or "Commission") reopen and modify the Commission's Decision and Order entered on November 21, 2019 (the "Order"), concerning the purchase of a natural gas pipeline and related assets. DTE requests that the Commission relieve the company of all continuing obligations under the Order because DTE has exited the relevant market addressed by the Order and its successor remains under the Order.
