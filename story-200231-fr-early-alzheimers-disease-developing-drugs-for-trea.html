BREAKING: Federal Register: Early Alzheimer's Disease: Developing Drugs for Treatment; Draft Guidance for Industry; Availability

Published: 2018-02-16 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA or Agency) is announcing the availability of a draft guidance for industry entitled "Early Alzheimer's Disease: Developing Drugs for Treatment." This guidance is intended to assist sponsors in the clinical development of drugs for the treatment of the stages of sporadic Alzheimer's disease (AD) that occur before the onset of overt dementia.
