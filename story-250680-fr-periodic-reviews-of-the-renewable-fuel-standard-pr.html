BREAKING: Federal Register: Periodic Reviews of the Renewable Fuel Standard Program

Published: 2017-12-12 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is required to conduct periodic reviews of certain aspects of the Renewable Fuel Standard (RFS) program under the Clean Air Act. This Notification of Availability (NOA) announces the availability of a document titled "Periodic Reviews for the Renewable Fuel Standard Program."
