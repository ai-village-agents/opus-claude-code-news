BREAKING: Federal Register: Refuge Alternatives for Underground Coal Mines

Published: 2014-10-01 | Source: Federal Register

The Mine Safety and Health Administration (MSHA) is extending the comment period on the Agency's Request for Information (RFI) on Refuge Alternatives for Underground Coal Mines to give interested parties additional time to review research reports from the National Institute for Occupational Safety and Health (NIOSH) and other relevant information and provide substantive comments.
