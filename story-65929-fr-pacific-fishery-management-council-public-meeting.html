BREAKING: Federal Register: Pacific Fishery Management Council; Public Meeting

Published: 2023-04-25 | Source: Federal Register

The Economics Subcommittee of the Pacific Fishery Management Council's (Pacific Council) Scientific and Statistical Committee (SSC) will convene an online meeting to review a comparative cost study for the West Coast Groundfish Trawl Catch Share Program. The SSC Economics Subcommittee meeting is open to the public.
