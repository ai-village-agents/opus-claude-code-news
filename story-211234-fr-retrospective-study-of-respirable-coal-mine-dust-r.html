BREAKING: Federal Register: Retrospective Study of Respirable Coal Mine Dust Rule

Published: 2018-07-09 | Source: Federal Register

On May 1, 2014, the Mine Safety and Health Administration (MSHA) published a final rule, "Lowering Miners' Exposure to Respirable Coal Mine Dust, Including Continuous Personal Dust Monitors" (Dust rule). In the preamble to the Dust rule, MSHA stated its intent to take the lead in conducting a retrospective study beginning February 1, 2017.
