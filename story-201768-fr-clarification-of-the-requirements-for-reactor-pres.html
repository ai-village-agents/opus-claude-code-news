BREAKING: Federal Register: Clarification of the Requirements for Reactor Pressure Vessel Upper Head Bare Metal Visual Examinations

Published: 2018-03-09 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Nuclear Regulatory Commission (NRC) is seeking public comment on a draft regulatory issue summary (RIS). The draft RIS informs all addressees of a clarification of the requirements for the bare metal visual examination.
