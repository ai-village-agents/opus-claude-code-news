BREAKING: Federal Register: Reporting Independent Expenditures

Published: 2022-06-14 | Source: Federal Register

The Federal Election Commission is removing a regulation requiring that certain persons making independent expenditures disclose on their reports the identification of each person who made a contribution over $200 to the persons filing such reports "for the purpose of furthering the reported independent expenditure."
