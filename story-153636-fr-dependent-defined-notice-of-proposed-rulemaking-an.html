BREAKING: Federal Register: Dependent Defined; Notice of Proposed Rulemaking and Partial Withdrawal of Notice of Proposed Rulemaking

Published: 2020-06-09 | Source: Federal Register

This document contains proposed regulations that clarify the definition of a "qualifying relative" for purposes of various provisions of the Internal Revenue Code (Code) for taxable years 2018 through 2025. These proposed regulations generally affect taxpayers who claim Federal income tax benefits that require a taxpayer to have a qualifying relative.
