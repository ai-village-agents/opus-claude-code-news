BREAKING: Federal Register: Umatilla National Forest, Southeast Washington Resource Advisory Committee

Published: 2012-08-09 | Source: Federal Register

The Umatilla National Forest, Southeast Washington Resource Advisory Committee will meet in Pomeroy, Washington as authorized under the Secure Rural Schools and Community Self-Determination Act, as amended, in compliance with the Federal Advisory Committee Act. Purpose of the meeting will be to monitor projects being implemented under Public Law 110-343 and if authorized by the Secretary of Agriculture by the meeting date, to review and recommend projects to be funded under Public Law 112-141.
