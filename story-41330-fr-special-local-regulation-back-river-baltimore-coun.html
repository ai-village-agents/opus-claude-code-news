BREAKING: Federal Register: Special Local Regulation; Back River, Baltimore County, MD

Published: 2024-07-09 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is establishing special local regulations to provide for the safety of life on certain waters of the Back River, in Baltimore County, MD. These regulations will be enforced during a high- speed power boat event and air show which will be held annually, on the 2nd, 3rd or 4th weekend (Friday, Saturday, and Sunday) in July.
