BREAKING: Federal Register: Modification of the National Customs Automation Program Test Regarding Periodic Monthly Statements

Published: 2025-10-16 | Source: Federal Register

This document announces a change to the U.S. Customs and Border Protection's (CBP) National Customs Automation Program (NCAP) test concerning Periodic Monthly Statements to reflect that test participants must transmit the payment of supplemental duty bills resulting from an underpayment of estimated duties, taxes, and fees electronically via Automated Clearinghouse (ACH).
