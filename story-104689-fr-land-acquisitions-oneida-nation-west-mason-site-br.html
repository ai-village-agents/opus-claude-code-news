BREAKING: Federal Register: Land Acquisitions; Oneida Nation, West Mason Site, Brown County, Wisconsin

Published: 2022-09-08 | Source: Federal Register

The Assistant Secretary--Indian Affairs made a final agency determination to acquire in trust 1.411 acres, more or less, of land known as the West Mason Site in Green Bay, Brown County, Wisconsin, (Site) for the Oneida Nation, (Nation) for gaming and other purposes.
