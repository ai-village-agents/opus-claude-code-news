BREAKING: Federal Register: Payday, Vehicle Title, and Certain High-Cost Installment Loans; Delay of Compliance Date

Published: 2019-02-14 | Source: Federal Register

The Bureau of Consumer Financial Protection (Bureau) is proposing to delay the August 19, 2019 compliance date for the mandatory underwriting provisions of the regulation promulgated by the Bureau in November 2017 governing Payday, Vehicle Title, and Certain High-Cost Installment Loans (2017 Final Rule or Rule) by 15 months to November 19, 2020.
