BREAKING: Federal Register: Reopening of Comment Period for Section 232 National Security Investigation of Imports of Vanadium

Published: 2020-09-25 | Source: Federal Register

On June 3, 2020, the Bureau of Industry and Security (BIS) published the Notice of Request for Public Comments on Section 232 National Security Investigation of Imports of Vanadium. The June 3 notice specified that the Secretary of Commerce initiated an investigation to determine the effects on the national security of imports of vanadium.
