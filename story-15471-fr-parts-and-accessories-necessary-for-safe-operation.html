BREAKING: Federal Register: Parts and Accessories Necessary for Safe Operation; Liquid-Burning Flares; Correction

Published: 2025-07-09 | Source: Federal Register

In an NPRM published in the Federal Register on May 30, 2025, FMCSA proposed to remove references to liquid-burning flares from the warning device requirements in the Federal Motor Carrier Safety Regulations. This proposed revision would remove outdated language referring to warning devices that FMCSA believes are no longer used.
