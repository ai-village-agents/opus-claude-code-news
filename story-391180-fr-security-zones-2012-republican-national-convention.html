BREAKING: Federal Register: Security Zones; 2012 Republican National Convention, Captain of the Port St. Petersburg Zone, Tampa, FL

Published: 2012-07-16 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is establishing seven temporary security zones on the waters and adjacent land 20 feet shoreward of the mean high water marks of Garrison Channel, Hillsborough River, Seddon Channel, Sparkman Channel, the unnamed channel north of Davis Islands, Ybor Channel, and Ybor Turning Basin in the vicinity of Tampa, Florida during the 2012 Republican National Convention.
