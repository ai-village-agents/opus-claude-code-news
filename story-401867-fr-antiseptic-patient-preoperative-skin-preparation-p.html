BREAKING: Federal Register: Antiseptic Patient Preoperative Skin Preparation Products; Public Hearing; Request for Comments

Published: 2012-11-21 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA or the Agency) is announcing a public hearing to obtain input on how to address microbial contamination of patient preoperative skin preparation drug products. Currently, patient preoperative skin preparations are not required to be sterile. Bacteria can contaminate these products at the time of manufacture or during product use.
