BREAKING: Federal Register: Agricultural Advisory Committee Meeting

Published: 2013-07-10 | Source: Federal Register

The Commodity Futures Trading Commission's (CFTC) Agricultural Advisory Committee (AAC) is providing notice that it will hold a public meeting on Thursday, July 25, 2013, from 9:00 a.m. to 2:30 p.m., at the CFTC's Washington, DC, headquarters. The AAC will discuss issues related to customer protection and the Dodd-Frank Wall Street Reform and Consumer Protection Act.
