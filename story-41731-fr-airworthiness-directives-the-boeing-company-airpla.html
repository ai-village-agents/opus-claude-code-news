BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; The Boeing Company Airplanes

Published: 2024-07-15 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA is correcting a notice of proposed rulemaking (NPRM) that was published in the Federal Register. The NPRM proposed to issue an airworthiness directive (AD) that would apply to all The Boeing Company Model 767-200, -300, and -300F series airplanes. As published, the docket number referenced throughout the NPRM is incorrect. This document corrects that error.
