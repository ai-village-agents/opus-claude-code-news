BREAKING: Federal Register: "Ruby Chocolate" Deviating From Identity Standard; Temporary Permit for Market Testing

Published: 2021-09-03 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA or we) is announcing the extension of a temporary permit issued to Barry Callebaut U.S.A. LLC (the applicant) to market test products (designated as "ruby chocolate") that deviate from the U.S. standards of identity for cacao products.
