BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zones; Vieques Unexploded Ordnance Operations, East Vieques; Vieques, Puerto Rico

Published: 2021-04-01 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is establishing permanent safety zones for certain waters of Vieques, Puerto Rico. This action is necessary to provide for the safety of life on the navigable waters near the island of Vieques, Puerto Rico due to unexploded ordnances. This rulemaking will prohibit mariners from anchoring, dredging, or trawling in the designated areas.
