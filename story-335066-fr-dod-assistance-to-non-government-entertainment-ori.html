BREAKING: Federal Register: DoD Assistance to Non-Government, Entertainment-Oriented Media Productions

Published: 2014-09-17 | Source: Federal Register

This rule establishes policy, assigns responsibilities, and prescribes procedures for DoD assistance to non-Government entertainment media productions such as feature motion pictures, episodic television programs, documentaries, and computer-based games.
