BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Termination of the San Rafael Swell Master Leasing Plan, Utah; Correction

Published: 2018-11-06 | Source: Federal Register

On July 13, 2018, the Bureau of Land Management published a Notice in the Federal Register terminating the San Rafael Swell Master Leasing Plan process. The name of the master leasing plan in the Notice was incorrect. This Notice corrects the name of the master leasing plan.
