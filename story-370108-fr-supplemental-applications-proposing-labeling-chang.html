BREAKING: Federal Register: Supplemental Applications Proposing Labeling Changes for Approved Drugs and Biological Products

Published: 2013-11-13 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA or the Agency) is proposing to amend its regulations to revise and clarify procedures for application holders of an approved drug or biological product to change the product labeling to reflect certain types of newly acquired information in advance of FDA's review of the change.
