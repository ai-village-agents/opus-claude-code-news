BREAKING: Federal Register: Grapes Grown in a Designated Area of Southeastern California; Increased Assessment Rate

Published: 2014-05-13 | Source: Federal Register

This rule increases the assessment rate established for the California Desert Grape Administrative Committee (Committee) for the 2014 and subsequent fiscal periods from $0.0165 to $0.0200 per 18-pound lug of grapes handled. The Committee locally administers the marketing order, which regulates the handling of grapes grown in a designated area of southeastern California.
