BREAKING: Federal Register: Proposed Establishment of Class E Airspace and Amendment of Class D and Class E Airspace; Olympia, WA

Published: 2018-01-10 | Source: Federal Register

This action proposes to establish Class E airspace extending upward from 700 feet above the surface and modify Class E airspace designated as an extension at Olympia Regional Airport (formerly Olympia Airport). This action would remove the Notice to Airmen (NOTAM) part-time status for Class E airspace designated as an extension, and would update the airport name and geographic coordinates in the associated Class D and E airspace areas to match the FAA's aeronautical database.
