BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Opportunity for Public Comment on Surplus Property Release at Brunswick-Golden Isles Airport, Brunswick, GA

Published: 2013-02-11 | Source: Federal Register

Under the provisions of Title 49, U.S.C. 47153(c), notice is being given that the FAA is considering a request from the Glynn County Airport Commission to waive the requirement that a 0.739-acre parcel of surplus property, located on Glynn County airport owned and operated land adjacent to, but not contiguace with, Brunswick--Golden Isles Airport, be used for aeronautical purposes.
