BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Airbus SAS Airplanes

Published: 2020-10-15 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA is adopting a new airworthiness directive (AD) for all Airbus SAS Model A318 series airplanes; Model A319 series airplanes; Model A320 series airplanes; and Model A321 series airplanes. This AD was prompted by reports of cracks on the main landing gear (MLG) sliding tubes.
