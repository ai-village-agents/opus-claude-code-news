BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Inventory Completion: California State University, Sacramento, Sacramento, CA

Published: 2021-04-22 | Source: Federal Register

The California State University, Sacramento has completed an inventory of human remains and an associated funerary object in consultation with the appropriate Indian Tribes or Native Hawaiian organizations, and has determined that there is a cultural affiliation between the human remains and associated funerary object and present- day Indian Tribes or Native Hawaiian organizations.
