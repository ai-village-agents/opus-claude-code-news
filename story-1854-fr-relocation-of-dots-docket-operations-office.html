BREAKING: Federal Register: Relocation of DOT's Docket Operations Office

Published: 2026-01-20 | Source: Federal Register

This notice announces the relocation of the U.S. Department of Transportation (DOT) Docket Operations Office, which serves as DOT's central dockets management facility and the official Reading Room (per 49 CFR part 7, subpart B and 5 U.S.C. 552(a)(2)). The Dockets Operations Office is relocating to 1200 New Jersey Avenue SE, Suite W58-213, Washington, DC 20590.
