BREAKING: Federal Register: Consumer Information; Uniform Tire Quality Grading Standards

Published: 2013-11-06 | Source: Federal Register

The Uniform Tire Quality Grading Standards (UTQGS) contain detailed testing procedures for generating consumer information about the treadwear, traction, and temperature resistance of passenger car tires. To ensure the uniformity of treadwear grades, the grading procedures specify a 400-mile test course located near San Angelo, Texas.
