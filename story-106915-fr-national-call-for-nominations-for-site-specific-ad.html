BREAKING: Federal Register: National Call for Nominations for Site-Specific Advisory Committees

Published: 2022-10-05 | Source: Federal Register

The purpose of this notice is to request public nominations for five of the Bureau of Land Management's (BLM) citizens' advisory committees affiliated with specific sites on the BLM's National Conservation Lands. The five advisory committees provide advice and recommendations to the BLM on the development and implementation of management plans in accordance with the statute under which the sites were established.
