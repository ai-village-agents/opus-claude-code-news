BREAKING: Federal Register: National Construction Safety Team Advisory Committee Meeting

Published: 2012-12-18 | Source: Federal Register

The National Construction Safety Team (NCST) Advisory Committee (Committee), will hold a meeting via teleconference on Friday, January 11, 2013 from 1 p.m. to 3 p.m. Eastern Time. The primary purpose of this meeting is to discuss the NCST Advisory Committee's draft annual report to Congress. A copy of the draft report will be posted prior to the meeting on the NCST Advisory Committee's Web site at http:// www.nist.gov/el/disasterstudies/ncst/index.cfm.
