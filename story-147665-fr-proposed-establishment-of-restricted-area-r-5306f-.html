BREAKING: Federal Register: Proposed Establishment of Restricted Area R-5306F; Cherry Point, NC

Published: 2020-03-25 | Source: Federal Register

This action proposes to establish restricted area R-5306F at Marine Corps Air Station (MCAS) Cherry Point, NC. R-5306F would extend from flight level (FL) 180 to FL 290. The proposed restricted area would overlie the existing restricted area R-5306A, and the adjacent Core Military Operations Area (MOA).
