BREAKING: Federal Register: Proposed Information Collection Activity: Submission for OMB Review; Comment Request

Published: 2012-07-09 | Source: Federal Register

The NTSB is announcing that it will submit an Information Collection Request (ICR) to the Office of Management and Budget (OMB) for approval, in accordance with the Paperwork Reduction Act. This ICR proposes voluntary completion of a form to collect contact information of victims of transportation accidents, and/or victims' family members and friends.
