BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Realty Action: Application for Conveyance of Federally Owned Mineral Interests in Escambia County, FL

Published: 2016-09-16 | Source: Federal Register

The Bureau of Land Management (BLM) is processing an application under the Federal Land Policy and Management Act of October 21, 1976 (FLPMA) to convey the undivided mineral interest owned by the United States in 70 acres located in Escambia County, Florida, to the surface owner, Airway Development, LLC.
