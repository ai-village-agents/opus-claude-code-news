BREAKING: Federal Register: Towing Safety Advisory Committee; January 2017 Teleconference

Published: 2016-12-23 | Source: Federal Register

The Towing Safety Advisory Committee will meet, via teleconference, to discuss the five current tasks of the Committee. The Committee is expected to receive the final report from the subcommittee on Electronic Charting Systems and an interim final report from the subcommittee on Implementation of Subchapter M.
