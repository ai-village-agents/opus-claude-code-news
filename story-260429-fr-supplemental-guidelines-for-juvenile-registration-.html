BREAKING: Federal Register: Supplemental Guidelines for Juvenile Registration Under the Sex Offender Registration and Notification Act

Published: 2016-04-11 | Source: Federal Register

The Sex Offender Registration and Notification Act (SORNA) requires registration of individuals convicted of sex offenses as adults and, in addition, registration of juveniles adjudicated delinquent for certain serious sex offenses. SORNA also provides for a reduction of justice assistance funding to eligible jurisdictions that fail to "substantially implement" SORNA's requirements, including the juvenile registration requirement, in their sex offender registration programs.
