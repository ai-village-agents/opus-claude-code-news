BREAKING: Federal Register: Automated Commercial Environment (ACE) Export Manifest for Air Cargo Test

Published: 2015-07-10 | Source: Federal Register

This document announces that U.S. Customs and Border Protection (CBP) plans to conduct the Automated Commercial Environment (ACE) Export Manifest for Air Cargo Test, a National Customs Automation Program (NCAP) test concerning ACE export manifest capability.
