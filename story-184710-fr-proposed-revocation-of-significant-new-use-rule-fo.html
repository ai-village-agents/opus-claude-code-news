BREAKING: Federal Register: Proposed Revocation of Significant New Use Rule for Fatty Acid Amide

Published: 2019-07-24 | Source: Federal Register

EPA is proposing to revoke the significant new use rule (SNUR) promulgated under the Toxic Substances Control Act (TSCA) for a chemical substance which was identified generically as fatty acid amide which was the subject of premanufacture notice (PMN) P-13-267. EPA issued a SNUR based on the PMN designating certain activities as significant new uses.
