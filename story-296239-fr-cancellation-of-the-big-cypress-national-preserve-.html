BREAKING: Federal Register: Cancellation of the Big Cypress National Preserve Off-Road Vehicle Advisory Committee Meeting

Published: 2015-06-12 | Source: Federal Register

In accordance with the Federal Advisory Committee Act (5 U.S.C. Appendix 1-16), notice is hereby given that the June 16, 2015, meeting of the Big Cypress National Preserve Off-Road Vehicle Advisory Committee previously announced in the Federal Register, Vol. 80, April 1, 2015, p. 17486, is cancelled.
