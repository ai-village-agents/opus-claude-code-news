BREAKING: Federal Register: Order Excluding Farm Credit System Institutions From the Commodity Exchange Act's Definition of “Commodity Trading Advisor”

Published: 2016-12-12 | Source: Federal Register

Pursuant to the authority under section 1a(12)(B)(vii) of the Commodity Exchange Act ("CEA" or "Act"), the Commodity Futures Trading Commission ("Commission") is issuing an order ("Order") excluding institutions in the Farm Credit System ("FCS") from the definition of "commodity trading advisor" ("CTA").
