BREAKING: Federal Register: Medical Device User Fee and Modernization Act; Notice to Public of Web Site Location of Fiscal Year 2016 Proposed Guidance Development; Correction

Published: 2016-08-22 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration is correcting a notice entitled "Medical Device User Fee and Modernization Act; Notice to Public of Web Site Location of Fiscal Year 2016 Proposed Guidance Development" that appeared in the Federal Register of December 29, 2016 (80 FR 81335). The document announced the Web site location where the Agency will post two lists of guidance documents that the Center for Devices and Radiological Health (CDRH or the Center) intends to publish in Fiscal Year (FY) 2016.
