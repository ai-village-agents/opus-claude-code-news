BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Solicitation of Applications for the Section 533 Housing Preservation Grants for Fiscal Year 2022

Published: 2022-05-26 | Source: Federal Register

The Rural Housing Service (Agency), a Rural Development (RD) agency of the United States Department of Agriculture (USDA), announces that it is soliciting competitive preapplications under the Housing Preservation Grant (HPG) program for fiscal year (FY) 2022, to make available grant funds to sponsoring organizations for the repair or rehabilitation of housing owned or occupied by low- and very-low-income rural citizens under the HPG Program.
