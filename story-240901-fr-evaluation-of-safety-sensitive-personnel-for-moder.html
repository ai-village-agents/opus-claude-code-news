BREAKING: Federal Register: Evaluation of Safety Sensitive Personnel for Moderate-to-Severe Obstructive Sleep Apnea

Published: 2017-08-08 | Source: Federal Register

The Federal Motor Carrier Safety Administration (FMCSA) and Federal Railroad Administration (FRA) (collectively, the Agencies) withdraw the March 10, 2016, advance notice of proposed rulemaking (ANPRM) concerning the prevalence of moderate-to-severe obstructive sleep apnea (OSA) among individuals occupying safety sensitive positions in highway and rail transportation, and its potential consequences for the safety of highway and rail transportation.
