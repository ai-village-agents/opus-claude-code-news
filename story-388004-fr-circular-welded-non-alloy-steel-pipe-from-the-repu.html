BREAKING: Federal Register: Circular Welded Non-Alloy Steel Pipe From the Republic of Korea: Final Results of the Antidumping Duty Administrative Review

Published: 2012-06-11 | Source: Federal Register

On December 7, 2011, the Department of Commerce (the "Department") published the preliminary results of the administrative review of the antidumping duty order on circular welded non-alloy steel pipe ("CWP") from the Republic of Korea ("Korea"). The review covers the period November 1, 2009, through October 31, 2010, and two producers/exporters of the subject merchandise to the United States: SeAH Steel Corporation ("SeAH") and Hyundai HYSCO ("HYSCO").
