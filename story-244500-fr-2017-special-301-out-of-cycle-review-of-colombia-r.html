BREAKING: Federal Register: 2017 Special 301 Out-of-Cycle Review of Colombia: Request for Public Comment

Published: 2017-09-25 | Source: Federal Register

In the 2017 Special 301 Report, the Office of the United States Trade Representative (USTR) announced that, in order to monitor progress on specific intellectual property rights (IPR) issues, USTR would conduct an out-of-cycle review of Colombia.
