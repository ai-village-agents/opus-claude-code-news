BREAKING: Federal Register: Solicitation of Nomination for Appointment to the Veterans' Advisory Committee on Education (VACOE)

Published: 2016-09-19 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Veterans Affairs (VA), Veterans Benefits Administration (VBA), Education Service is seeking nominations of qualified candidates to be considered for appointment as a member of the Veterans' Advisory Committee on Education (VACOE). The Committee is authorized by statute, 38 U.S.C. 3692, and operates under the provisions of the Federal Advisory Committee Act, as amended, 5 U.S.C. App. 2.
