BREAKING: Federal Register: General Motors, LLC, Grant of Petition for Decision of Inconsequential Noncompliance

Published: 2018-04-16 | Source: Federal Register

General Motors, LLC (GM), has determined that certain model year (MY) 2014-2016 GM motor vehicles do not fully comply with Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard (FMVSS) No. 110, Tire Selection and Rims and Motor Home/Recreation Vehicle Trailer Load Carrying Capacity Information for Motor Vehicles with a GVWR of 4,536 kilograms (10,000 pounds)or Less.
