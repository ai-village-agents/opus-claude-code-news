BREAKING: Federal Register: International Code Council: The Update Process for the International Codes and Standards

Published: 2013-03-26 | Source: Federal Register

The International Code Council (ICC), promulgator of the International Codes and Standards, maintains a process for updating the entire family of International Codes based on receipt of proposals from interested individuals and organizations involved in the construction industry as well as the general public.
