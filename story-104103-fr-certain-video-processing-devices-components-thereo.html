BREAKING: Federal Register: Certain Video Processing Devices, Components Thereof, and Digital Smart Televisions Containing the Same; Notice of a Commission Determination Not To Review an Order Denying Respondents' Motion for Sanctions and To Deny Complainant's Motion for Sanctions; Termination of Sanctions Proceedings

Published: 2022-08-30 | Source: Federal Register

Notice is hereby given that the U.S. International Trade Commission (the "Commission") has determined not to review an order (Order No. 75) issued by the presiding administrative law judge ("ALJ") denying the respondents' motion for sanctions and to deny a motion for sanctions filed by complainant DivX, LLC. Both sanctions proceedings are hereby terminated.
