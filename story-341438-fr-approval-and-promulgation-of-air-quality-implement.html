BREAKING: Federal Register: Approval and Promulgation of Air Quality Implementation Plans; Indiana; Open Burning Rule

Published: 2014-12-09 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is approving a November 14, 2011, request by Indiana to revise the state implementation plan (SIP) to update the open burning provisions in Title 326 of the Indiana Administrative Code (IAC), Article 4, Rule 1 (326 IAC 4-1), Open Burning Rule. This action applies statewide, with the exception of Clark, Floyd, Lake and Porter counties.
