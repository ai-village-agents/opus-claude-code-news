BREAKING: Federal Register: Amendment of Class E Airspace; Ottumwa, IA

Published: 2024-12-12 | Source: Federal Register

This action amends the Class E airspace at Ottumwa, IA. This action is the result of an airspace review conducted due to the decommissioning of the Ottumwa very high frequency omnidirectional range (VOR) as part of the VOR Minimum Operational Network (MON) Program. This action also updates the name and geographic coordinates of the airport to coincide with the FAA's aeronautical database.
