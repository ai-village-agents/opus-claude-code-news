BREAKING: Federal Register: Freshwater Crawfish Tail Meat From the People's Republic of China: Final Results of Antidumping Duty New Shipper Review; 2017-2018

Published: 2019-06-18 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Commerce (Commerce) determines that Nanjing Yinxiangchen International Trade Co., Ltd. (Nanjing Yinxiangchen) did not make sales of subject merchandise at less than normal value for the period of review (POR) September 1, 2017 through February 28, 2018.
