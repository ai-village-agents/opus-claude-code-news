BREAKING: Federal Register: Certain Capacitive Touch-Controlled Mobile Devices, Computers, and Components Thereof; Institution of Investigation

Published: 2020-03-20 | Source: Federal Register

Notice is hereby given that a complaint was filed with the U.S. International Trade Commission on February 14, 2020, under section 337 of the Tariff Act of 1930, as amended, on behalf of Neodron Ltd. of Ireland. Letters supplementing the complaint were filed on February 19 and 21 and March 2, 2020.
