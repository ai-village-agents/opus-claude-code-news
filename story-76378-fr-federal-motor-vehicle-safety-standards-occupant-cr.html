BREAKING: Federal Register: Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standards; Occupant Crash Protection, Seat Belt Reminder Systems

Published: 2023-09-07 | Source: Federal Register

The Moving Ahead for Progress in the 21st Century Act of 2012 (MAP-21) directs NHTSA to initiate a rulemaking proceeding to amend Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard (FMVSS) No. 208, "Occupant crash protection," to require a seat belt use warning system for rear seats.
