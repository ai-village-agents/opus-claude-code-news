BREAKING: Federal Register: Requirements for Contacts with Federal Credit Unions

Published: 2013-12-24 | Source: Federal Register

The NCUA Board (Board) proposes to amend part 701 of its regulations to require examinations and other contacts between NCUA staff and staff or officials of a federal credit union (FCU) occur in an FCU's business offices or other public location. This does not include a private residence.
