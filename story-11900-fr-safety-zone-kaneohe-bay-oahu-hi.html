BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone; Kaneohe Bay, Oahu, HI

Published: 2025-05-21 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is proposing to establish a temporary safety zone while the U.S. Navy Blue Angels Squadron conducts aerobatic performances over Kaneohe Bay, Oahu, Hawaii. This safety zone is necessary to protect watercrafts and the general public from hazards associated with the U.S. Navy Blue Angels aircraft performing low flying, high powered jet aerobatics over open water.
