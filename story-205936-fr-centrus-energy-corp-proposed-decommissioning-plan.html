BREAKING: Federal Register: Centrus Energy Corp.; Proposed Decommissioning Plan

Published: 2018-05-01 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Nuclear Regulatory Commission (NRC) has received an application from Centrus Energy Corp. (Centrus) to amend Material License Number SNM-7003 to authorize decommissioning of its American Centrifuge Lead Cascade Facility (LCF) located in Piketon, Ohio. The NRC has accepted the application for technical review and evaluation of the decommissioning plan.
