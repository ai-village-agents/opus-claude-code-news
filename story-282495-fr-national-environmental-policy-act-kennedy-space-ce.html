BREAKING: Federal Register: National Environmental Policy Act: Kennedy Space Center-Center Master Plan

Published: 2016-12-23 | Source: Federal Register

NASA has prepared and issued a Final Programmatic Environmental Impact Statement (FPEIS) for implementation of the Kennedy Space Center (KSC) Center Master Plan (CMP). The purpose of the CMP is to provide overall management guidance for KSC from 2016 to 2032. Implementation of the CMP will facilitate a two-decade transformation from a single, government-user launch complex to a multi-user spaceport.
