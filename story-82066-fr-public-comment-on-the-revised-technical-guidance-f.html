BREAKING: Federal Register: Public Comment on the Revised Technical Guidance for Assessing Environmental Justice in Regulatory Analysis

Published: 2023-11-15 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is announcing a 60- day public comment period on the draft revision of the Technical Guidance for Assessing Environmental Justice in Regulatory Analysis (EJ Technical Guidance). The EJ Technical Guidance was first published in 2016. The EPA is updating it to reflect the state of the science; new peer-reviewed agency guidance; and new terminology, priorities, and direction, including Executive Order 14096.
