BREAKING: Federal Register: Cooperative Research and Development Agreement (CRADA)-“Very High Frequency Data Exchange System (VDES) Satellite Connectivity and Service”

Published: 2025-06-23 | Source: Federal Register

The United States Coast Guard (USCG) is announcing its intent to enter into a Cooperative Research and Development Agreement (CRADA) with SPACE NORWAY to test and evaluate the suitability of implementing a VDES satellite (VDE-SAT) component for USCG operations. In addition, the USCG also invites other potential non-Federal participants, who have the interest and capability to bring similar contributions to this type of research, to consider submitting proposals for consideration in similar CRADA...
