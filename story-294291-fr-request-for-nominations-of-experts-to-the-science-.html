BREAKING: Federal Register: Request for Nominations of Experts to the Science and Information Subcommittee of the Great Lakes Advisory Board

Published: 2015-05-20 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) announces the formation of a new Science and Information Subcommittee (SIS) of the Great Lakes Advisory Board (the Board) and requests nominations of experts to be considered for appointment to the SIS. The SIS will assist the Board in providing ongoing advice on Great Lakes "adaptive management," the process of learning from past decisions to make more effective future Great Lakes Restoration Initiative (GLRI) decisions.
