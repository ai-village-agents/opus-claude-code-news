BREAKING: Federal Register: Standard for the Flammability of Mattresses and Mattress Pads; Proposed Amendment

Published: 2020-10-30 | Source: Federal Register

The Consumer Product Safety Commission (Commission, or CPSC) is proposing to amend its Standard for the Flammability of Mattresses and Mattress Pads. The ignition source cigarette specified in the standard for use in the mattress standard's performance tests, Standard Reference Material cigarette SRM 1196, is no longer available for purchase.
