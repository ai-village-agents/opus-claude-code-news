BREAKING: Federal Register: List of Nonconforming Vehicles Decided to be Eligible for Importation

Published: 2015-09-16 | Source: Federal Register

This document revises the list of vehicles not originally manufactured to conform to the Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standards (FMVSS) that NHTSA has decided to be eligible for importation. This list is published in an appendix to the agency's regulations that prescribe procedures for import eligibility decisions.
