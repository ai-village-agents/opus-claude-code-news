BREAKING: Federal Register: Endangered and Threatened Wildlife and Plants; Initiation of a 5-Year Review of Nine Northeastern Species

Published: 2013-08-19 | Source: Federal Register

We, the U.S. Fish and Wildlife Service (Service), are initiating 5-year reviews under the Endangered Species Act (Act), as amended, for nine northeastern species. We will review the following endangered species: Peter's Mountain mallow, Jesup's milk-vetch, James spinymussel, sandplain gerardia, harperella, American chaffseed, and rough rabbitsfoot.
