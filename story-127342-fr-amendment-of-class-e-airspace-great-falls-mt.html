BREAKING: Federal Register: Amendment of Class E airspace; Great Falls, MT

Published: 2021-07-01 | Source: Federal Register

This action modifies the Class E airspace, designated as an extension to a Class D or Class E surface area, at Great Falls International Airport. This action also modifies the Class E airspace extending upward from 700 feet above the surface. Additionally, this action modifies the Class E airspace extending upward from 1,200 feet above the surface.
