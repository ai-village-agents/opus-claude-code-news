BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Rolls-Royce plc Turbofan Engines

Published: 2016-12-01 | Source: Federal Register

We propose to supersede airworthiness directive (AD) 2015-17- 19 that applies to all Rolls-Royce plc (RR) RB211 Trent 768-60, 772-60, and 772B-60 turbofan engines. AD 2015-17-19 requires inspection of the fan case low-pressure (LP) fuel tubes and associated clips and the fuel oil heat exchanger (FOHE) mounts and associated hardware.
