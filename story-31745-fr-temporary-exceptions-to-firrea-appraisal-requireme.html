BREAKING: Federal Register: Temporary Exceptions to FIRREA Appraisal Requirements in Maui County as Affected by Hawaii Wildfires

Published: 2024-03-12 | Source: Federal Register

The Depository Institutions Disaster Relief Act of 1992 (DIDRA) authorizes the agencies to make exceptions to statutory and regulatory appraisal requirements under Title XI of the Financial Institutions Reform, Recovery, and Enforcement Act of 1989 (FIRREA) relating to transactions involving real property located within an area in a state or territory declared to be a major disaster by the President.
