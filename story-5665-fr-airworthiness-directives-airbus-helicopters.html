BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Airbus Helicopters

Published: 2025-02-18 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA is superseding Airworthiness Directive (AD) 2021-04- 18, which applied to Airbus Helicopters Model EC225LP helicopters. AD 2021-04-18 required repetitively inspecting the bearing in the swashplate assembly of certain main rotor (M/R) mast assemblies and, depending on the findings, replacing the M/R mast assembly.
