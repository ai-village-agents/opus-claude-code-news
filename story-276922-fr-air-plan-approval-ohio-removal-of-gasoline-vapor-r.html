BREAKING: Federal Register: Air Plan Approval; Ohio; Removal of Gasoline Vapor Recovery Requirements

Published: 2016-10-18 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is approving, as a revision under the Clean Air Act (CAA) to the Ohio state implementation plan (SIP), submittals from the Ohio Environmental Protection Agency (Ohio EPA) dated July 15, 2015, and February 29, 2016. The revision addresses the state's Stage II vapor recovery (Stage II) program for the Cleveland, Cincinnati, and Dayton ozone areas in Ohio.
