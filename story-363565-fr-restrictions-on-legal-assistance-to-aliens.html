BREAKING: Federal Register: Restrictions on Legal Assistance to Aliens

Published: 2013-08-21 | Source: Federal Register

This proposed rule updates the Legal Services Corporation (LSC or Corporation) regulation on legal assistance to aliens. The revisions are intended to implement three statutory changes on aliens eligible for legal assistance from LSC grant recipients that have been enacted since the pertinent provisions of the existing regulation were last revised in 1997.
