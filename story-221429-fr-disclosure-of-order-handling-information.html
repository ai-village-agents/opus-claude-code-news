BREAKING: Federal Register: Disclosure of Order Handling Information

Published: 2018-11-19 | Source: Federal Register

The Securities and Exchange Commission ("Commission" or "SEC") is adopting amendments to Regulation National Market System ("Regulation NMS") under the Securities Exchange Act of 1934 ("Exchange Act") to require additional disclosures by broker-dealers to customers regarding the handling of their orders.
