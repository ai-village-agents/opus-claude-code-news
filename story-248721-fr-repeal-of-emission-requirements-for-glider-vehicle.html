BREAKING: Federal Register: Repeal of Emission Requirements for Glider Vehicles, Glider Engines, and Glider Kits

Published: 2017-11-16 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is proposing to repeal the emission standards and other requirements for heavy-duty glider vehicles, glider engines, and glider kits based on a proposed interpretation of the Clean Air Act (CAA) under which glider vehicles would be found not to constitute "new motor vehicles" within the meaning of CAA section 216(3), glider engines would be found not to constitute "new motor vehicle engines" within the meaning of CAA section 216(3), and glider kits would...
