BREAKING: Federal Register: Superfund Chemical Taxes

Published: 2023-03-29 | Source: Federal Register

This document contains proposed regulations relating to the excise taxes imposed on certain chemicals and certain imported substances, effective July 1, 2022. Such taxes are known as the Superfund chemical taxes. The excise tax on taxable chemicals is imposed on the sale or use of taxable chemicals by manufacturers, producers, and importers of such chemicals.
