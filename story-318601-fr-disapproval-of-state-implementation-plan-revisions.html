BREAKING: Federal Register: Disapproval of State Implementation Plan Revisions; Clark County, Nevada

Published: 2014-03-11 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is finalizing disapproval of revisions to the Clark County portion of the Nevada State Implementation Plan (SIP). This action concerns affirmative defense provisions applicable to violations related to excess emissions from sources during equipment startup, shutdown and malfunction (SSM) events.
