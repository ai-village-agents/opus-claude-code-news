BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Schempp-Hirth Flugzeugbau GmbH Gliders

Published: 2015-11-09 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA is correcting an airworthiness directive (AD) that published in the Federal Register. That AD applies to certain Schempp- Hirth Flugzeugbau GmbH Models Duo Discus and Duo Discus T gliders. The Model Duo Discus gliders were incorrectly referenced as powered sailplanes in the Applicability section.
