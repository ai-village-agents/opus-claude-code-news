BREAKING: Federal Register: Meeting of the United States-Republic of Korea Environmental Affairs Council and Environmental Cooperation Commission

Published: 2024-07-08 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Department of State and the Office of the United States Trade Representative (USTR) are providing notice that the United States and the Republic of Korea (ROK) intend to hold the fourth meetings of the Environmental Affairs Council (EAC) and Environmental Cooperation Commission (ECC) in Seoul, ROK on July 11 and 12, 2024.
