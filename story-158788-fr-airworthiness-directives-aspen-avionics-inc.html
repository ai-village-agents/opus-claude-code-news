BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Aspen Avionics, Inc.

Published: 2020-08-17 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA is correcting an airworthiness directive (AD) that published in the Federal Register. The AD applies to certain Aspen Avionics, Inc., Evolution Flight Display (EFD) EFD1000 Emergency Backup Display, EFD1000 Multi-Function Display, and EFD1000 Primary Flight Display systems installed on various airplanes.
