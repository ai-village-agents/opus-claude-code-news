BREAKING: Federal Register: Approval and Promulgation of Implementation Plans; State of California; Sacramento Metro Area; Attainment Plan for 1997 8-Hour Ozone Standard

Published: 2015-01-29 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is taking final action to approve state implementation plan (SIP) revisions submitted by the State of California that provide for attainment of the 1997 8- hour ozone national ambient air quality standard ("standard" or NAAQS) in the Sacramento Metro nonattainment area.
