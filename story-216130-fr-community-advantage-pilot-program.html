BREAKING: Federal Register: Community Advantage Pilot Program

Published: 2018-09-12 | Source: Federal Register

The Community Advantage ("CA") Pilot Program is a pilot program to increase SBA-guaranteed loans to small businesses in underserved areas. The Small Business Administration ("SBA") continues to refine and improve the design of the Community Advantage Pilot Program.
