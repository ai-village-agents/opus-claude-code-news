BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of October 15-16, 2013, Meeting of the National Park System Advisory Board

Published: 2013-08-20 | Source: Federal Register

Notice is hereby given in accordance with the Federal Advisory Committee Act, 5 U.S.C. Appendix, and parts 62 and 65 of title 36 of the Code of Federal Regulations, that the National Park System Advisory Board will meet October 15-16, 2013, in Washington, DC. The agenda will include the review of proposed actions regarding the National Historic Landmarks Program and the National Natural Landmarks Program.
