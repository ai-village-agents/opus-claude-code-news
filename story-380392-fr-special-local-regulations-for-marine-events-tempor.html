BREAKING: Federal Register: Special Local Regulations for Marine Events; Temporary Change of Dates for Recurring Marine Events in the Fifth Coast Guard District, Bogue Sound; Morehead City, NC

Published: 2012-03-15 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard proposes to temporarily change the enforcement period of a special local regulation for a recurring marine event in the Fifth Coast Guard District. This change applies only to the "Crystal Coast Super Boat Grand Prix" conducted on the waters of Bogue Sound near Morehead City, North Carolina.
