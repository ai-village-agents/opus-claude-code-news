BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Inventory Completion: Center for Big Bend Studies, Sul Ross State University, Alpine, TX

Published: 2024-04-11 | Source: Federal Register

In accordance with the Native American Graves Protection and Repatriation Act (NAGPRA), the Center for Big Bend Studies, Sul Ross State University has completed an inventory of human remains and has determined that there is a known lineal descendant connected to the human remains in this notice.
