BREAKING: Federal Register: Patient and Medical Professional Perspectives on the Return of Genetic Test Results and Interpretations; Public Workshop; Request for Comments

Published: 2016-01-14 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA) is announcing the following public workshop entitled "Patient and Medical Professional Perspectives on the Return of Genetic Test Results." The purpose of this public workshop is to understand patient and provider perspectives on receiving potentially medically relevant genetic test results.
