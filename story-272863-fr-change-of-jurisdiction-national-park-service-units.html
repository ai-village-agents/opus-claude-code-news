BREAKING: Federal Register: Change of Jurisdiction-National Park Service Units Within the Commonwealth of Kentucky

Published: 2016-09-01 | Source: Federal Register

On behalf of the United States, the National Park Service accepted exclusive jurisdiction from the Commonwealth of Kentucky over certain lands and waters administered by the National Park Service within Mammoth Cave National Park.
