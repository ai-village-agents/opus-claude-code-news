BREAKING: Federal Register: Implementation of the Privacy Act of 1974, as Amended; New System of Records, Rent Reform Demonstration

Published: 2015-04-02 | Source: Federal Register

The Department's Office of Policy Development and Research (PD&R) is proposing to create a new system of records (SORN), the "Rent Reform Demonstration." The Department's Office of PD&R is responsible for maintaining current information on housing needs, market conditions and existing programs, as well as conducting research on priority housing and community development issues.
