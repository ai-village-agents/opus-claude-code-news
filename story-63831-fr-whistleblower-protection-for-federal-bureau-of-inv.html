BREAKING: Federal Register: Whistleblower Protection for Federal Bureau of Investigation Employees

Published: 2023-03-29 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Justice ("Department") proposes to update its regulations on the protection of whistleblowers in the Federal Bureau of Investigation ("FBI").
