BREAKING: Federal Register: Atlantic Highly Migratory Species; Electronic Reporting Requirements

Published: 2023-05-12 | Source: Federal Register

In this advance notice of proposed rulemaking, NMFS is considering management options to modify or expand reporting requirements for Atlantic highly migratory species (HMS). All HMS reporting would require electronic submission using an online or mobile reporting application.
