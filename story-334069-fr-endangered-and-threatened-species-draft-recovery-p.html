BREAKING: Federal Register: Endangered and Threatened Species; Draft Recovery Plan for Staghorn and Elkhorn Corals

Published: 2014-09-05 | Source: Federal Register

The National Marine Fisheries Service (NMFS) announces the availability for public review of the draft Recovery Plan (Plan) for elkhorn coral (Acropora palmata) and staghorn coral (Acropora cervicornis). The Plan is available on the NMFS Web site at http:// www.nmfs.noaa.gov/pr/recovery/plans.htm and the Southeast Regional Office Web site at http://sero.nmfs.noaa.gov/ protected_resources/coral/elkhorn_coral/document/ Key_Docs/DraftAcroporaRecoveryPlan.pdf.
