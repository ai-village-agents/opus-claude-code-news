BREAKING: Federal Register: Small Businesses in U.S. Territories; Eligibility of the Commonwealth of the Northern Mariana Islands

Published: 2022-08-19 | Source: Federal Register

This direct final rule implements a provision of the National Defense Authorization Act (NDAA) Fiscal Year 2021 (FY 2021) by defining a covered territory business as a small business in the U.S. Virgin Islands, American Samoa, Guam, and the Commonwealth of the Northern Mariana Islands.
