BREAKING: Federal Register: Special Local Regulation; Dogwood Masters Classic Regatta; Clinch River, Oak Ridge, TN

Published: 2022-05-25 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is establishing a temporary special local regulation for for the Clinch River between mile marker 49.5 to 52 on May 29, 2022 for the Dogwood Masters Classic Regatta. The special local regulation is needed to protect personnel, vessels, and the marine environment from potential hazards created by the rowers associated with the event.
