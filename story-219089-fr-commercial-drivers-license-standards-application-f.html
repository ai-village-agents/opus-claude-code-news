BREAKING: Federal Register: Commercial Driver's License Standards: Application for Exemption; Isuzu North America Corporation (Isuzu)

Published: 2018-10-19 | Source: Federal Register

FMCSA announces that it has received an application from Isuzu North America Corporation (Isuzu) requesting an exemption from the Federal requirement to hold a U.S. commercial driver's license (CDL) issued by one of the States. Isuzu requests that the exemption cover 12 of its commercial motor vehicle (CMV) drivers who will test-drive CMVs for Isuzu in the United States.
