BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Dassault Aviation Airplanes

Published: 2021-07-22 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA is adopting a new airworthiness directive (AD) for certain Dassault Aviation Model FALCON 7X airplanes and Model FALCON 2000EX airplanes. This AD was prompted by a report that non-certified ANCRA seat tracks were installed on some airplanes and that those seat tracks might not sustain required loads during an emergency landing.
