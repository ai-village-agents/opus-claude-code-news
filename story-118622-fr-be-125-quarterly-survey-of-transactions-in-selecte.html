BREAKING: Federal Register: BE-125: Quarterly Survey of Transactions in Selected Services and Intellectual Property With Foreign Persons

Published: 2021-03-10 | Source: Federal Register

By this Notice, the Bureau of Economic Analysis (BEA), Department of Commerce, is informing the public that it is conducting the mandatory survey titled Quarterly Survey of Transactions in Selected Services and Intellectual Property with Foreign Persons (BE- 125). The data collected on the BE-125 survey are needed to measure U.S. trade in services and to analyze the impact of U.S. trade on the U.S. and foreign economies.
