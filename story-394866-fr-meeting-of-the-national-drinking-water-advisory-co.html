BREAKING: Federal Register: Meeting of the National Drinking Water Advisory Council

Published: 2012-08-28 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Environmental Protection Agency (EPA or agency) is announcing a meeting of the National Drinking Water Advisory Council (NDWAC or Council), established under the Safe Drinking Water Act (SDWA). This meeting was originally scheduled (and announced in a Federal Register notice) for September 12 and 13, 2012, in Chicago, Illinois.
