BREAKING: Federal Register: Exportation of Live Animals, Hatching Eggs, and Animal Germplasm From the United States

Published: 2015-02-26 | Source: Federal Register

We are proposing to revise the regulations pertaining to the exportation of livestock from the United States. Among other things, we propose to remove most of the requirements for export health certifications, tests, and treatments from the regulations, and instead would direct exporters to follow the requirements of the importing country regarding such processes and procedures.
