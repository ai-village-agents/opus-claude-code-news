BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Dominguez-Escalante National Conservation Area Advisory Council Meeting Cancellation and Change of Location

Published: 2013-04-11 | Source: Federal Register

In accordance with the Federal Land Policy and Management Act (FLPMA) and the Federal Advisory Committee Act of 1972 (FACA), notice is hereby given that the Dominguez-Escalante National Conservation Area Advisory Council meeting scheduled for May 1, 2013, at the Delta County Courthouse, Room 234, 501 Palmer Street, Delta, CO, has been cancelled.
