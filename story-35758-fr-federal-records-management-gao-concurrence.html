BREAKING: Federal Register: Federal Records Management: GAO Concurrence

Published: 2024-05-01 | Source: Federal Register

The National Archives and Records Administration (NARA) is amending our records management regulations to limit the role of the Government Accountability Office (GAO) in approving certain deviations in agency records schedules.
