BREAKING: Federal Register: Privacy Act of 1974; System of Records

Published: 2022-06-24 | Source: Federal Register

In accordance with the Privacy Act of 1974, the Department of Defense (DoD) is establishing a new Department-wide system of records titled, "Counterintelligence Functional Services," DoD-0010. This system of records covers DoD's maintenance of records about counterintelligence functional services (CIFS).
