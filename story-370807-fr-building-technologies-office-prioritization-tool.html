BREAKING: Federal Register: Building Technologies Office Prioritization Tool

Published: 2013-11-21 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Department of Energy's (DOE) Building Technologies Office (BTO) developed the Prioritization Tool to improve its programmatic decision-making. The tool provides an objective framework for most energy-saving measures and scenarios, as well as methodology, comparing long-term benefits and end-user costs applied to various markets, end-uses, and lifetimes.
