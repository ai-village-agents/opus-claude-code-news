BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone: Giants Enterprises Fireworks Display, San Francisco Bay, San Francisco, CA

Published: 2015-04-24 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is establishing a temporary safety zone in the navigable waters of the San Francisco Bay near AT&T Park in support of Giants Enterprises Fireworks Display on May 22, 2015. This safety zone is established to ensure the safety of participants and spectators from the dangers associated with pyrotechnics.
