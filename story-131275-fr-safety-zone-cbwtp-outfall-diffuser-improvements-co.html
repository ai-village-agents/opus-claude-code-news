BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone; CBWTP Outfall Diffuser Improvements, Columbia River, Portland, OR

Published: 2021-08-23 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is proposing to establish a temporary safety zone for certain navigable waters of the Columbia River. This action is necessary to provide for the safety of life on these navigable waters near Portland, OR, at Columbia River Mile 105.6 from October 1, 2021, through February 28, 2022. This proposed rulemaking would prohibit persons and vessels from being in the safety zone unless authorized by the Captain of the Port Sector Columbia River or a designated representative.
