BREAKING: Federal Register: Extension of the Public Comment Period for the Draft Uranium Leasing Program Programmatic Environmental Impact Statement

Published: 2013-04-23 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Department of Energy (DOE) is extending the public comment period for the Draft Uranium Leasing Program Programmatic Environmental Impact Statement (Draft ULP PEIS, DOE/EIS-0472D), made available for public comment on March 15, 2013 (78 FR 16500). The public comment period for the Draft ULP PEIS was to complete on May 16, 2013, and has now been extended to close on May 31, 2013. This extension is being made in response to a public request for additional review time.
