BREAKING: Federal Register: Grant Agreements for the Development of Foreign Markets for U.S. Agricultural Commodities

Published: 2021-12-06 | Source: Federal Register

The Office of Management and Budget (OMB) revised and renumbered certain provisions in its regulation establishing uniform administrative requirements, cost principles, and audit requirements for Federal awards to non-Federal entities. This final rule amends the USDA regulations on Grant Agreements for the Development of Foreign Markets for U.S. Agricultural Commodities ("MAP regulation") to make technical corrections to reflect the revised OMB regulations.
