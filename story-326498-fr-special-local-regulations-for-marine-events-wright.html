BREAKING: Federal Register: Special Local Regulations for Marine Events, Wrightsville Channel; Wrightsville Beach, NC

Published: 2014-06-09 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is proposing to establish a Special Local Regulation for the "Swim the Loop/Motts Channel Sprint" swim event, to be held on the waters adjacent to and surrounding Harbor Island in Wrightsville Beach, North Carolina. This Special Local Regulation is necessary to provide for the safety of life on the navigable waters during the event.
