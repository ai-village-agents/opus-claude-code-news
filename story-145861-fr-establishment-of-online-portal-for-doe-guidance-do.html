BREAKING: Federal Register: Establishment of Online Portal for DOE Guidance Documents

Published: 2020-03-04 | Source: Federal Register

In accordance with an Executive Order and Office of Management and Budget (OMB) Memorandum M-20-02, the U.S. Department of Energy (DOE) established an online portal for the public to access DOE guidance documents. The portal provides links to DOE program web pages containing guidance documents, as required by the Executive Order.
