BREAKING: Federal Register: Coastal and Marine Ecological Classification Standard

Published: 2012-08-31 | Source: Federal Register

The Federal Geographic Data Committee (FGDC) has endorsed the Coastal and Marine Ecological Classification Standard (CMECS) as the first-ever comprehensive federal data standard for classifying and describing coastal and marine ecosystems. CMECS provides a means of classifying ecological and habitat units using a common terminology.
