BREAKING: Federal Register: Endangered and Threatened Wildlife and Plants; Revision to the Section 4(d) Rule for the African Elephant

Published: 2022-11-17 | Source: Federal Register

We, the U.S. Fish and Wildlife Service (Service or FWS), propose to revise the rule for the African elephant (Loxodonta africana) promulgated under section 4(d) of the Endangered Species Act of 1973, as amended (ESA).
