BREAKING: Federal Register: Uranerz Energy Corporation

Published: 2014-09-09 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Nuclear Regulatory Commission (NRC) is issuing a temporary exemption from certain NRC financial assurance requirements to Uranerz Energy Corporation, (Uranerz) in response to its annual financial assurance update for the Nichols Ranch in-situ recovery (ISR) Project.
