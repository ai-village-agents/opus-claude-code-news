BREAKING: Federal Register: Federal Acquisition Regulation; Submission for OMB Review; Drug-Free Workplace (FAR 52.223-6)

Under the provisions of the Paperwork Reduction Act, the Regulatory Secretariat will be submitting to the Office of Management and Budget (OMB) a request to review and approve an extension of a previously approved information collection requirement concerning drug- free workplace. A notice was published in the Federal Register at 77 FR 52696, on August 30, 2012. No comments were received.
