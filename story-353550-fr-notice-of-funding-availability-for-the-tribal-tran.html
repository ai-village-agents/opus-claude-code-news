BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Funding Availability for the Tribal Transportation Program Safety Funds; and Request for Comments

Published: 2013-04-30 | Source: Federal Register

This notice announces the availability of funding for Indian tribal governments for Tribal Transportation Program Safety Funds (TTPSF) authorized within the Tribal Transportation Program (TTP) under the Moving Ahead for Progress in the 21st Century Act (MAP-21). The FHWA will distribute these funds as described in this notice on a competitive basis in a manner consistent with the eligibilities of this program.
