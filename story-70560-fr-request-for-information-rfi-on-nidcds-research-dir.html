BREAKING: Federal Register: Request for Information (RFI) on NIDCD's Research Directions To Support Communication in Minimally Verbal/Non-Speaking People

Published: 2023-06-23 | Source: Federal Register

The National Institute on Deafness and Other Communication Disorders (NIDCD) asks for input on research directions to support communication in minimally verbal/non-speaking people. NIDCD invites anyone with interests in communication in minimally verbal/non-speaking people to provide input from a personal, service delivery, or research view.
