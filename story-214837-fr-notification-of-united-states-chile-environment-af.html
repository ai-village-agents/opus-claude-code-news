BREAKING: Federal Register: Notification of United States-Chile Environment Affairs Council and Joint Commission on Environmental Cooperation Meetings

Published: 2018-08-24 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of State and the Office of the United States Trade Representative are providing notice that the parties to the U.S.- Chile Free Trade Agreement (FTA) intend to hold the eighth meeting of the Environment Affairs Council (Council) established under Chapter 19 of the FTA, as well as the sixth meeting of the U.S.-Chile Joint Commission on Environmental Cooperation (Commission) established under the U.S.-Chile Environmental Cooperation Agreement (ECA), on Tuesday September 4, 2018.
