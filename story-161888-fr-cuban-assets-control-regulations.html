BREAKING: Federal Register: Cuban Assets Control Regulations

Published: 2020-09-24 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of the Treasury's Office of Foreign Assets Control (OFAC) is amending the Cuban Assets Control Regulations to further implement portions of the President's foreign policy toward Cuba to deny the Cuban regime sources of revenue.
