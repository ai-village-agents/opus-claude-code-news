BREAKING: Federal Register: Application of Federal Law to LSC Recipients

Published: 2015-04-20 | Source: Federal Register

This final rule updates the Legal Services Corporation (LSC or Corporation) regulation on the application of Federal law to LSC recipients. The FY 1996 appropriations act (incorporated in LSC's appropriations by reference annually thereafter) subjects LSC recipients and its employees and board members to Federal law relating to the proper use of Federal funds.
