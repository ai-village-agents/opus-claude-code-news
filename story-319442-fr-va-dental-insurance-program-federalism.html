BREAKING: Federal Register: VA Dental Insurance Program-Federalism

Published: 2014-03-20 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Veterans Affairs (VA) published a direct final rule in the Federal Register on October 22, 2013, amending its regulations related to the VA Dental Insurance Program (VADIP), a pilot program to offer premium-based dental insurance to enrolled veterans and certain survivors and dependents of veterans.
