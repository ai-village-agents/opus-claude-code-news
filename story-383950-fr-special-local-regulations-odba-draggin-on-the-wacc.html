BREAKING: Federal Register: Special Local Regulations; ODBA Draggin on the Waccamaw, Atlantic Intracoastal Waterway, Bucksport, SC

Published: 2012-04-24 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard proposes to establish special local regulations on the Atlantic Intracoastal Waterway in Bucksport, South Carolina during the ODBA Draggin on the Waccamaw, a series of high- speed boat races. The event is scheduled to take place on Saturday, June 23, 2012, and Sunday, June 24, 2012. Approximately 40 high-speed race boats are anticipated to participate in the races.
