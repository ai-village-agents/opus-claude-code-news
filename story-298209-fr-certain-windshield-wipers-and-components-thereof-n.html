BREAKING: Federal Register: Certain Windshield Wipers and Components Thereof; Notice of a Commission Determination not to Review an Initial Determination Terminating Investigation as to Federal-Mogul Respondents Based on a Settlement Agreement

Published: 2015-07-06 | Source: Federal Register

Notice is hereby given that the U.S. International Trade Commission has determined not to review an initial determination ("ID") (Order No. 24) of the presiding administrative law judge ("ALJ") terminating the investigation as to Federal-Mogul respondents based on a settlement agreement.
