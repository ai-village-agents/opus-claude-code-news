BREAKING: Federal Register: Request for Applications; Federal Public Defenders Advisory Group

Published: 2016-06-17 | Source: Federal Register

The Commission has decided to establish a Federal Public Defenders Advisory Group as a standing advisory group pursuant to 28 U.S.C. 995 and Rule 5.4 of the Commission's Rules of Practice and Procedure. Having adopted a formal charter for the Federal Public Defenders Advisory Group, the Commission is constituting the initial voting membership of the advisory group under that charter.
