BREAKING: Federal Register: Wireline Competition Bureau Announces Release of Final Lifeline Biennial Audit Plan

Published: 2014-04-18 | Source: Federal Register

In this document, the Wireline Competition Bureau (Bureau) in conjunction with the Office of Managing Director (OMD), developed standard procedures for independent biennial audits of eligible telecommunications carriers (ETCs).
