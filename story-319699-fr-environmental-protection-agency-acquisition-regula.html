BREAKING: Federal Register: Environmental Protection Agency Acquisition Regulation (EPAAR); Contractor Performance Information

Published: 2014-03-24 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is issuing a direct final rule to rescind EPAARs, which include EPA's policies for collecting and maintaining contractor past performance information. A final rule updating the Federal Acquisition Regulations (FAR) was published in Federal Register on August 1, 2013.
