BREAKING: Federal Register: President's Council of Advisors on Science and Technology (PCAST)

Published: 2014-12-24 | Source: Federal Register

This notice sets forth the schedule and summary agenda for a partially-closed meeting of the President's Council of Advisors on Science and Technology (PCAST), and describes the functions of the Council. The Federal Advisory Committee Act (Pub. L. 92-463, 86 Stat. 770) requires that public notice of this meeting be announced in the Federal Register.
