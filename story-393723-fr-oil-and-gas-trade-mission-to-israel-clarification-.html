BREAKING: Federal Register: Oil and Gas Trade Mission to Israel-Clarification and Amendment

Published: 2012-08-14 | Source: Federal Register

The United States Department of Commerce, International Trade Administration, U.S. and Foreign Commercial Service (CS) is publishing this amendment to the Notice of the Oil and Gas Trade Mission to Israel, 77 FR 21748, April 11, 2012, to amend the Notice to reflect minor changes in the timeline and agenda. The revised notice will include the new dates of October 28-November 1, 2012 and the resulting agenda changes.
