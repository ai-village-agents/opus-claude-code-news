BREAKING: Federal Register: Onions Grown in South Texas; Continuance Referendum

Published: 2014-10-29 | Source: Federal Register

This document directs that a referendum be conducted among eligible producers of onions grown in South Texas to determine whether they favor continuance of the marketing order that regulates the handling of onions produced in the production area. This is the second referendum being conducted this year; the first was conducted in May 2014.
