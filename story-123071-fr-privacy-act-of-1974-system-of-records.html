BREAKING: Federal Register: Privacy Act of 1974; System of Records

Published: 2021-05-05 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Environmental Protection Agency's (EPA), Office of Land and Emergency Management (OLEM) is giving notice that it proposes to modify a system of records pursuant to the provisions of the Privacy Act of 1974. Environmental Assessments of Residential Properties (EARP) is being modified to clarify the nature of the information, and the ways in which that information may be used and shared with parties who are part of the evaluation and coordination process.
