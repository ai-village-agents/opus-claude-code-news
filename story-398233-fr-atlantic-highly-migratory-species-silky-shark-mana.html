BREAKING: Federal Register: Atlantic Highly Migratory Species; Silky Shark Management Measures

Published: 2012-10-04 | Source: Federal Register

NMFS implements the International Commission for the Conservation of Atlantic Tunas (ICCAT) Recommendation 11-08, which prohibits retaining, transshipping, or landing of silky sharks (Carcharhinus falciformis) caught in association with ICCAT fisheries. In order to facilitate domestic compliance and enforcement, NMFS also prohibits the storing, selling, and purchasing of the species.
