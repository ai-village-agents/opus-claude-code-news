BREAKING: Federal Register: Extension of Generic Clearance for the Collection of Qualitative Feedback on Agency Service Delivery

The Department of Energy (DOE), as part of its continuing effort to reduce paperwork and respondent burden, invites the general public to take this opportunity to comment on the "Generic Clearance for the Collection of Qualitative Feedback on Agency Service Delivery" for approval under the Paperwork Reduction Act (PRA).
