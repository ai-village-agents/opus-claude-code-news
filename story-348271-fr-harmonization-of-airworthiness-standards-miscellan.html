BREAKING: Federal Register: Harmonization of Airworthiness Standards-Miscellaneous Structures Requirements

Published: 2013-03-01 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA proposes to amend certain airworthiness regulations for transport category airplanes, based on recommendations from the Aviation Rulemaking Advisory Committee (ARAC). Adopting this proposal would eliminate regulatory differences between the airworthiness standards of the FAA and European Aviation Safety Agency (EASA).
