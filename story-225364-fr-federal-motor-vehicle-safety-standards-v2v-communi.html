BREAKING: Federal Register: Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standards; V2V Communications

Published: 2017-01-12 | Source: Federal Register

This document proposes to establish a new Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard (FMVSS), No. 150, to mandate vehicle-to-vehicle (V2V) communications for new light vehicles and to standardize the message and format of V2V transmissions. This will create an information environment in which vehicle and device manufacturers can create and implement applications to improve safety, mobility, and the environment.
