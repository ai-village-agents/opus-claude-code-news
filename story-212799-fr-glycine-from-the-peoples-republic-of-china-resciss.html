BREAKING: Federal Register: Glycine From the People's Republic of China: Rescission of Antidumping Duty Administrative Review; 2017-2018

Published: 2018-07-31 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Commerce (Commerce) is rescinding the administrative review of the antidumping duty order on glycine from the People's Republic of China (China) for the period March 1, 2017, through February 28, 2018, based on the timely withdrawal of the request for review.
