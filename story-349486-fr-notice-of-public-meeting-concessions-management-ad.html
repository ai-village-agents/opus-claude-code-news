BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Public Meeting: Concessions Management Advisory Board

Published: 2013-03-15 | Source: Federal Register

On February 14, 2013, the National Park Service announced that a public meeting of the Concessions Management Advisory Board would be held March 20, 2013 in Washington, DC. This meeting has been cancelled. A future meeting date for this Board may be scheduled and would be announced in the Federal Register.
