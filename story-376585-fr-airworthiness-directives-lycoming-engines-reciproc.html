BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Lycoming Engines Reciprocating Engines

Published: 2012-02-02 | Source: Federal Register

We are rescinding an airworthiness directive (AD) for Lycoming Engines model TIO-540-A series reciprocating engines. The existing AD, AD 71-13-01, was prompted by a report of a failed fuel injector tube assembly. Since we issued AD 71-13-01, we became aware that Lycoming Engines no longer supports Service Bulletin (SB) No. 335A, which was incorporated by reference in AD 71-13-01.
