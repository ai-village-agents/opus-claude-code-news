BREAKING: Federal Register: BE-45: Quarterly Survey of Insurance Transactions by U.S. Insurance Companies With Foreign Persons

Published: 2021-03-10 | Source: Federal Register

By this Notice, the Bureau of Economic Analysis (BEA), Department of Commerce, is informing the public that it is conducting the mandatory survey titled Quarterly Survey of Insurance Transactions by U.S. Insurance Companies with Foreign Persons (BE-45). The data collected on the BE-45 survey are needed to measure U.S. trade in insurance services and to analyze the impact of U.S. trade on the U.S. and foreign economies.
