BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; The Boeing Company Airplanes

Published: 2025-08-06 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA is superseding Airworthiness Directive (AD) 2024-15- 03, which applied to all The Boeing Company Model 777 airplanes. AD 2024-15-03 required identifying the part number, and the serial number if applicable, of the Captain's and First Officer's seats and applicable on-condition actions for affected seats.
