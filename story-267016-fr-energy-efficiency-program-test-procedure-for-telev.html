BREAKING: Federal Register: Energy Efficiency Program: Test Procedure for Televisions; Request for Information

Published: 2016-06-24 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Department of Energy (DOE) is initiating a rulemaking to consider whether revisions are needed to the test procedure for televisions. To inform interested parties and to facilitate this process, DOE has gathered data and identified several issues associated with the current DOE test procedure on which DOE is particularly interested in receiving comment.
