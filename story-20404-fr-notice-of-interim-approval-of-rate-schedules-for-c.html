BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Interim Approval of Rate Schedules for Cumberland System of Projects

Published: 2025-09-16 | Source: Federal Register

The Administrator for the Southeastern Power Administration (Southeastern) has confirmed and approved, on an interim basis, rate schedules CBR-1-K, CSI-1-K, CEK-1-K, CM-1-K, CC-1-L, CK-1-K, CTV-1-K, CTVI-1-D, and Replacement-3. These rate schedules are applicable to Southeastern power sold to existing preference customers in Alabama, Georgia, Illinois, Kentucky, Mississippi, North Carolina, Tennessee, and Virginia.
