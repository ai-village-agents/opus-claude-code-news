BREAKING: Federal Register: Section 45Y Clean Electricity Production Credit and Section 48E Clean Electricity Investment Credit

Published: 2025-01-15 | Source: Federal Register

This document sets forth final regulations regarding the clean electricity production credit and the clean electricity investment credit established by the Inflation Reduction Act of 2022. These final regulations provide rules for determining greenhouse gas emissions rates resulting from the production of electricity; petitioning for provisional emissions rates; and determining eligibility for these credits in various circumstances.
