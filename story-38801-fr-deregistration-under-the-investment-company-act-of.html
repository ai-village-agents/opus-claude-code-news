BREAKING: Federal Register: Deregistration Under the Investment Company Act of 1940

Published: 2024-06-06 | Source: Federal Register

Applicant seeks an order declaring that it has ceased to be an investment company. The applicant has transferred its assets to First American Funds Trust, and on December 19, 2023, made a final distribution to its shareholders based on net asset value. Expenses of $2,240,310 incurred in connection with the reorganization were paid by the applicant's investment adviser.
