BREAKING: Federal Register: Monosodium Glutamate From the Republic of Indonesia: Final Results of Antidumping Duty Administrative Review; 2019-2020

Published: 2022-03-31 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Commerce (Commerce) determines that PT. Miwon Indonesia (Miwon) made sales of subject merchandise below normal value, and that PT. Cheil Jedang Indonesia (CJ Indonesia) did not. The period of review (POR) is November 1, 2019, through October 31, 2020.
