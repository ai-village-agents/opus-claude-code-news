BREAKING: Federal Register: Advisory Committee on Transportation Equity

Published: 2016-11-04 | Source: Federal Register

Pursuant to Section 9(a)(2) of the Federal Advisory Committee Act (FACA), and in accordance with Title 41, Code of Federal Regulations, Section 102-3.65, and following consultation with the Committee Management Secretariat, General Services Administration, notice is hereby given that the ACTE will be established for a 2-year period.
