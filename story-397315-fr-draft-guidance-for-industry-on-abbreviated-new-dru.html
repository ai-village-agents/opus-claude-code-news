BREAKING: Federal Register: Draft Guidance for Industry on Abbreviated New Drug Applications: Stability Testing of Drug Substances and Products; Availability

Published: 2012-09-25 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA) is announcing the availability of a draft guidance for industry entitled "ANDAs: Stability Testing of Drug Substances and Products." FDA is recommending that generic drug manufacturers follow the stability testing recommendations in the International Conference on Harmonisation (ICH) guidances Q1A(R2) through Q1E.
