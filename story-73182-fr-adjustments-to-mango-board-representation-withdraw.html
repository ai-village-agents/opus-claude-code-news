BREAKING: Federal Register: Adjustments to Mango Board Representation; Withdrawal of Proposed Rule

Published: 2023-07-31 | Source: Federal Register

The Agricultural Marketing Service (AMS) withdraws a proposed rule recommended by the National Mango Board (Board) that would have adjusted the Mango Promotion, Research, and Information Order (Order) to allocate the seven foreign producer Board seats to the top five mango exporting countries, based on mango volume exported to the United States, with an additional seat allocated to the top exporting country, and one at-large seat.
