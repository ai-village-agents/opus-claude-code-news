BREAKING: Federal Register: Certain Obstructive Sleep Apnea Treatment Mask Systems and Components Thereof; Termination of Investigation on the Basis of Settlement

Published: 2019-04-17 | Source: Federal Register

Notice is hereby given that the U.S. International Trade Commission has determined not to review the presiding administrative law judge's ("ALJ") initial determination ("ID") (Order No. 10), granting a joint motion to terminate the investigation on the basis of settlement. The investigation is terminated.
