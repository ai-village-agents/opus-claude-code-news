BREAKING: Federal Register: Migraine: Developing Drugs for Acute Treatment; Draft Guidance for Industry; Availability

Published: 2014-10-22 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA) is announcing the availability of a draft guidance for industry entitled "Migraine: Developing Drugs for Acute Treatment." The purpose of this guidance is to assist sponsors in the clinical development of drugs for the acute treatment of migraine. This guidance focuses on specific drug development and trial design issues that are unique to the study of drugs for the acute treatment of migraine.
