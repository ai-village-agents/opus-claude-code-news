BREAKING: Federal Register: Pipeline Safety: Public Workshop on Public Awareness

Published: 2016-06-10 | Source: Federal Register

This notice is announcing a one-day public workshop PHMSA is sponsoring on public awareness to bring pipeline safety stakeholders together to review the findings from the joint Public Awareness Program Working Group's (PAPWG) Strengths, Weaknesses, Opportunities, and Threats (SWOT) Report and explore future actions that can be taken to expand public awareness and stakeholder engagement efforts.
