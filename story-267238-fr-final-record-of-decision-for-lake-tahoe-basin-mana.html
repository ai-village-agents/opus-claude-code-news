BREAKING: Federal Register: Final Record of Decision for Lake Tahoe Basin Management Unit Land Management Plan

Published: 2016-06-28 | Source: Federal Register

Regional Forester Randy Moore signed the final Record of Decision (ROD) for the Lake Tahoe Basin Management Unit (LTBMU) revised Land Management Plan (Plan) on June 20, 2016. The final ROD documents the Regional Forester's decision and rationale for approving the revised Plan.
