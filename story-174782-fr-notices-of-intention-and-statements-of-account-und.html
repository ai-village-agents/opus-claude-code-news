BREAKING: Federal Register: Notices of Intention and Statements of Account Under Compulsory License To Make and Distribute Phonorecords of Musical Works

Published: 2019-03-22 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Copyright Office is issuing final regulations pursuant to the Musical Works Modernization Act, title I of the Orrin G. Hatch-Bob Goodlatte Music Modernization Act. This rule adopts previously issued interim regulations as final.
