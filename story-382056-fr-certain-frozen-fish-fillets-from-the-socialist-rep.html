BREAKING: Federal Register: Certain Frozen Fish Fillets From the Socialist Republic of Vietnam: Initiation of Antidumping Duty New Shipper Reviews

Published: 2012-04-03 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Commerce (the "Department") has determined that requests for four new shipper reviews ("NSRs") of the antidumping duty order on certain frozen fish fillets ("fish") from the Socialist Republic of Vietnam ("Vietnam") meet the statutory and regulatory requirements for initiation. The period of review ("POR") for these NSRs is August 1, 2011, through January 31, 2012.
