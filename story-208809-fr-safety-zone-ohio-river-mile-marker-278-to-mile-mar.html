BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone; Ohio River, Mile Marker 27.8 to Mile Marker 28.2, Vanport, PA

Published: 2018-06-07 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is establishing a temporary safety zone for the navigable waters of the Ohio River from mile marker 27.8 to mile marker 28.2 near the Vanport Highway Bridge. The safety zone is necessary to protect personnel, vessels, and the marine environment from potential hazards created by a cargo movement near the Vanport Highway Bridge in Vanport, PA.
