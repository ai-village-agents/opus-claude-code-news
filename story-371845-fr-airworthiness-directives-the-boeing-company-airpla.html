BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; The Boeing Company Airplanes

Published: 2013-12-04 | Source: Federal Register

We propose to adopt a new airworthiness directive (AD) for all The Boeing Company Model 717-200 airplanes. This proposed AD was prompted by multiple reports of cracking in the overwing frames. This proposed AD would require repetitive inspections for cracking in the overwing frames, and corrective actions if necessary.
