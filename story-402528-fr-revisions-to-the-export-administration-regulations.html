BREAKING: Federal Register: Revisions to the Export Administration Regulations (EAR) To Make the Commerce Control List (CCL) Clearer

Published: 2012-11-29 | Source: Federal Register

On December 9, 2010, the Bureau of Industry and Security (BIS) published an advance notice of proposed rulemaking entitled Commerce Control List: Revising Descriptions of Items and Foreign Availability as part of the President's Export Control Reform (ECR) Initiative. The December 9, 2010 notice sought, among other things, public comments on how descriptions of items controlled on the Commerce Control List (CCL) could be made clearer.
