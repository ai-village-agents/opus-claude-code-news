BREAKING: Federal Register: Privacy Act of 1974; System of Records

Published: 2021-03-22 | Source: Federal Register

In accordance with the Privacy Act of 1974, the U.S. Department of Homeland Security (DHS) proposes to modify and reissue a current DHS system of records titled, "DHS/U.S. Customs and Border Protection (CBP)-018 Customs Trade Partnership Against Terrorism System of Records." This system of records allows DHS/CBP to collect and maintain records about members of the trade community related to CBP's Customs Trade Partnership Against Terrorism (CTPAT) Program.
