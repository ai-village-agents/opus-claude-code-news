BREAKING: Federal Register: Federal Student Loan Program Deferment Request Forms; Correction

Published: 2024-09-30 | Source: Federal Register

On September 23, 2024, a request for a 30-day public comment period was published in the Federal Register. The abstract incorrectly included language about forbearances when the collection request is about deferments. The corrected abstract should read: These forms serve as the means by which borrowers in the William D. Ford Federal Direct Loan (Direct Loan), Federal Family Education Loan (FFEL) and the Federal Perkins Loan (Perkins Loan) Programs may request deferment of repayment on their loan...
