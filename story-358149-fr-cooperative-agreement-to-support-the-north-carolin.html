BREAKING: Federal Register: Cooperative Agreement To Support the North Carolina State University, Prestage Department of Poultry Science and the Piedmont Research Station

Published: 2013-06-21 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA) is announcing its intention to receive and consider a single-source application for the award of a cooperative agreement in fiscal year 2013 (FY13) to the North Carolina State University, Prestage Department of Poultry Science and the Piedmont Research Station Poultry Unit located in Salisbury, NC.
