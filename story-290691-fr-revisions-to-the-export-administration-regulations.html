BREAKING: Federal Register: Revisions to the Export Administration Regulations Based on the 2014 Missile Technology Control Regime Plenary Agreements

Published: 2015-04-07 | Source: Federal Register

The Bureau of Industry and Security (BIS) is amending the Export Administration Regulations (EAR) to reflect changes to the Missile Technology Control Regime (MTCR) Annex that were agreed to by MTCR member countries at the September and October 2014 Plenary in Oslo, Norway, and pursuant to the 2014 Technical Experts Meeting in Prague, Czech Republic.
