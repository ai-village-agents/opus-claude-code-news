BREAKING: Federal Register: Atlantic Highly Migratory Species; Electronic Dealer Reporting System Workshop

Published: 2012-07-30 | Source: Federal Register

On June 28, 2011, NMFS published a proposed rule that considered requiring, among other things, Federal Atlantic swordfish, shark, and tunas dealers (except for dealers reporting Atlantic bluefin tuna) to report commercially-harvested Atlantic sharks, swordfish, and bigeye, albacore, yellowfin, and skipjack (BAYS) tunas through one centralized electronic reporting system.
