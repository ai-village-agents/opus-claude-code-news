BREAKING: Federal Register: Approval of Other Solid Waste Incineration Units State Plan for Designated Facilities and Pollutants: Indiana

Published: 2015-02-26 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is approving Indiana's State Plan to control air pollutants from "Other Solid Waste Incineration" (OSWI) units. The Indiana Department of Environmental Management (IDEM) submitted the State Plan to EPA on November 27, 2007. The State Plan is consistent with Emission Guidelines (EG) promulgated by EPA on December 16, 2005.
