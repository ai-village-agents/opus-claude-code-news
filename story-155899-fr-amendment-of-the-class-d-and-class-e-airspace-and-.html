BREAKING: Federal Register: Amendment of the Class D and Class E Airspace and Revocation of Class E Airspace; Erie and Corry, PA

Published: 2020-07-10 | Source: Federal Register

This action amends the Class D airspace, Class E surface area airspace, and Class E airspace extending upward from 700 feet above the surface at Erie International Airport/Tom Ridge Field, Erie, PA; revokes the Class E airspace area designated as an extension to Class D and Class E surface area at Erie International Airport/Tom Ridge Field; and amends the Class E airspace extending upward from 700 feet above the surface at Corry-Lawrence Airport, Corry, PA.
