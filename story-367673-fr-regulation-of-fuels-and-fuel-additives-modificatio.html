BREAKING: Federal Register: Regulation of Fuels and Fuel Additives: Modifications to Renewable Fuel Standard Program

Published: 2013-10-22 | Source: Federal Register

In this final rule EPA is amending the definition of "heating oil" in the regulations for the Renewable Fuel Standard (RFS) program under section 211(o) of the Clean Air Act.
