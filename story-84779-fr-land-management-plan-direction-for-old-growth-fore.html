BREAKING: Federal Register: Land Management Plan Direction for Old-Growth Forest Conditions Across the National Forest System

Published: 2023-12-20 | Source: Federal Register

The United States Department of Agriculture (Department) is proposing to amend all land management plans for units of the National Forest System (128 plans in total) to include consistent direction to conserve and steward existing and recruit future old-growth forest conditions and to monitor their condition across planning areas of the National Forest System.
