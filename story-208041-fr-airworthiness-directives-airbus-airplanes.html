BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Airbus Airplanes

Published: 2018-05-29 | Source: Federal Register

We propose to adopt a new airworthiness directive (AD) for all Airbus Model A330-200 Freighter series airplanes, Airbus Model A330-200 and -300 series airplanes, and Airbus Model A340-200 and -300 series airplanes. This proposed AD was prompted by reports of cracked slat tracks at the location of the front stop attachment to the track.
