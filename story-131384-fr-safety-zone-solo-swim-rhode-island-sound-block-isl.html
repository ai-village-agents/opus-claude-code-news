BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone; Solo Swim, Rhode Island Sound, Block Island, RI

Published: 2021-08-24 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is establishing a temporary safety zone on the navigable waters of Rhode Island Sound within 500 yards of the swimmer crossing the channel from Block Island, Rhode Island to East Matunuck, Rhode Island. This safety zone is needed to protect the swimmer, event sponsors' safety vessels, and others in the maritime community from the safety hazards that may arise during his event.
