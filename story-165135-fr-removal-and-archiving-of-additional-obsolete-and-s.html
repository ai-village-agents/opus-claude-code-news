BREAKING: Federal Register: Removal and Archiving of Additional Obsolete and Superseded Guidance Documents

Published: 2020-11-03 | Source: Federal Register

This notice announces that the Department has completed a second review of administrative guidance and identified guidance documents that should no longer be in effect or are deemed unnecessary and obsolete as part of the Department's implementation of Executive Order 13891, "Promoting the Rule of Law Through Improved Agency Guidance Documents." HUD is removing and archiving these documents on HUD's program websites to reduce compliance burdens, promote regulatory analysis, provide fair notice, ...
