BREAKING: Federal Register: 2020 Census Tribal Consultation; Virtual Public Meeting

Published: 2021-01-21 | Source: Federal Register

The Bureau of the Census (Census Bureau) will conduct its first calendar year 2021 tribal consultation meeting on February 18 via national webinar. The tribal consultation meeting reflects the Census Bureau's commitment to strengthen government-to-government relationships with federally recognized tribes.
