BREAKING: Federal Register: Nonalcoholic Steatohepatitis With Compensated Cirrhosis: Developing Drugs for Treatment; Draft Guidance for Industry; Availability

Published: 2019-06-07 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA or Agency) is announcing the availability of a draft guidance for industry entitled "Nonalcoholic Steatohepatitis with Compensated Cirrhosis: Developing Drugs for Treatment." The purpose of this draft guidance is to provide the Agency's current recommendations regarding the important components of a drug development program for nonalcoholic steatohepatitis (NASH) with compensated cirrhosis.
