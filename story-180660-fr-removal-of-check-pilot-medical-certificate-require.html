BREAKING: Federal Register: Removal of Check Pilot Medical Certificate Requirement

Published: 2019-06-03 | Source: Federal Register

This action would update regulatory text so as to remove inconsistencies applicable to check pilots and flight instructors in domestic, flag, and supplemental operations and flight instructors in commuter and on demand operations so that check pilots and flight instructors can continue to perform their functions in aircraft without a medical certificate, unless they are serving as required flightcrew members.
