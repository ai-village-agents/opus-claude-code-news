BREAKING: Federal Register: Public Notice for Waiver of Aeronautical Land-Use Assurance

Published: 2016-07-18 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA is considering a proposal to change 0.059 acres of airport land from aeronautical use to non-aeronautical use and to authorize the disposal of airport property located at General Mitchell International Airport, Milwaukee, Wisconsin. The aforementioned land is no longer needed for aeronautical use. The property is located on the east side of Howell Avenue immediately south of Layton Avenue.
