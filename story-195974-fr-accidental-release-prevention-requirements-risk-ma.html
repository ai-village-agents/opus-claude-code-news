BREAKING: Federal Register: Accidental Release Prevention Requirements: Risk Management Programs Under the Clean Air Act

Published: 2019-12-19 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is revising regulations that are designed to reduce the risk of accidental releases of hazardous chemicals. These regulations are part of the EPA's Risk Management Program (RMP), which the Agency established under authority in the Clean Air Act and recently amended on January 13, 2017.
