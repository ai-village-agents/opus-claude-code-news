BREAKING: Federal Register: Endangered and Threatened Wildlife and Plants; Termination of the Southern Sea Otter Translocation Program

Published: 2012-12-19 | Source: Federal Register

We, the U.S. Fish and Wildlife Service (Service), are removing the regulations that govern the southern sea otter (Enhydra lutris nereis) translocation program, including the establishment of an experimental population of southern sea otters, and all associated management actions. Removal of the regulations terminates the program.
