BREAKING: Federal Register: Modification of the National Customs Automation Program Test Regarding Post-Summary Corrections for Extensions of Liquidation

Published: 2019-08-14 | Source: Federal Register

This document announces U.S. Customs and Border Protection's (CBP's) modification to the National Customs Automation Program (NCAP) test pertaining to the processing of post-summary corrections (PSCs). The modification in this notice expands the time period in which a PSC must be filed by allowing a PSC to be transmitted up to 15 days prior to the scheduled date of liquidation when liquidation has been extended.
