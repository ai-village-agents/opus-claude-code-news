BREAKING: Federal Register: Privacy Act of 1974; System of Records; Correction

Published: 2023-09-01 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of the Housing and Urban Development (HUD) published a document in the Federal Register of August 28, 2023, a rescindment of a systems of records notice concerning the Integrated Real Estate Management System (iREMS). The document did not contain a comment closing date. This notice establishes a due date of September 27, 2023.
