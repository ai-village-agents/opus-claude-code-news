BREAKING: Federal Register: Changes to the Representation of Others Before the United States Patent and Trademark Office

Published: 2023-07-14 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Patent and Trademark Office (USPTO or Office) amends the rules of practice in patent cases and the rules regarding the representation of others before the USPTO to better protect the public and improve compliance with USPTO requirements. This final rule formalizes the USPTO's Diversion Pilot Program for patent and trademark practitioners whose physical or mental health issues or law practice management issues resulted in minor misconduct.
