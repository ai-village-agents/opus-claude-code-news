BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zones; Fireworks Displays in the Fifth Coast Guard District-Brick Township, NJ

Published: 2023-06-27 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard will enforce the Metedeconk River, Brick Township, NJ Safety Zone for fireworks displays on four separate periods. The safety zone will be enforced on July 6, 2023, July 13, 2023, July 20, 2023, and July 27, 2023, or on a rain date of August 10, 2023, to provide for the safety of life on navigable waterways during each of the four separate land-based fireworks displays.
