BREAKING: Federal Register: Funding Availability Under Supportive Services for Veteran Families Program

Published: 2014-10-22 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Veterans Affairs (VA) published a Notice of Funding Availability in the Federal Register on October 10, 2014, that contained an error. Specifically, it incorrectly stated the phone number for the VA point of contact, John Kuhn. The correct phone number is (877) 737-0111.
