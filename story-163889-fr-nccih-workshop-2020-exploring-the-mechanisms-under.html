BREAKING: Federal Register: NCCIH Workshop 2020: Exploring the Mechanisms Underlying Analgesic Properties of Minor Cannabinoids and Terpenes

Published: 2020-10-19 | Source: Federal Register

The National Center for Complementary and Integrative Health (NCCIH) will hold a workshop on October 23, 2020. The topic of this workshop will be "Exploring the Mechanisms Underlying Analgesic Properties of Minor Cannabinoids and Terpenes".
