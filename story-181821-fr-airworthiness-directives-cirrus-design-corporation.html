BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Cirrus Design Corporation

Published: 2019-06-17 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA is adopting a new airworthiness directive (AD) for all Cirrus Design Corporation (Cirrus) Model SF50 airplanes. This AD was sent previously as an emergency AD to all known U.S. owners and operators of these airplanes. This AD requires replacing the angle of attack (AOA) sensors with improved AOA sensors.
