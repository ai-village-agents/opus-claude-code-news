BREAKING: Federal Register: Testimony and Production of Records

Published: 2022-01-04 | Source: Federal Register

The National Endowment for the Humanities (NEH) is proposing to issue regulations to be followed when an NEH employee receives a demand or request to provide testimony or produce records in a legal proceeding.
