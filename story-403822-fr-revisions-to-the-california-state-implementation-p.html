BREAKING: Federal Register: Revisions to the California State Implementation Plan, South Coast Air Quality Management District

Published: 2012-12-14 | Source: Federal Register

In this action, EPA is finalizing approval of South Coast Air Quality Management District (SCAQMD) Rule 317, "Clean Air Act Non- Attainment Fee," as a revision to SCAQMD's portion of the California State Implementation Plan (SIP). This action was proposed in the Federal Register on January 12, 2012 and concerns volatile organic compounds (VOC) and oxides of nitrogen (NOX).
