BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Bombardier, Inc. Airplanes

Published: 2015-04-01 | Source: Federal Register

We propose to adopt a new airworthiness directive (AD) for certain Bombardier, Inc. Model DHC-8-400, -401, and -402 airplanes. This proposed AD was prompted by a report of a main landing gear (MLG) parking brake becoming dislodged from its mounting bracket due to an improperly installed quick release pin of the hand pump lever.
