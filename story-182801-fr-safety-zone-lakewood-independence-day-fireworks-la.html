BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone; Lakewood Independence Day Fireworks; Lake Erie, Lakewood, OH

Published: 2019-06-28 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is establishing a temporary safety zone for navigable waters within a 420-foot radius of the launch site located at position 41[deg]29'50" N, 081[deg]47'52" W at Lakewood Park, Lakewood, OH. This safety zone is needed to restrict vessels from a portion of Lake Erie during the Lakewood Independence Day fireworks display.
