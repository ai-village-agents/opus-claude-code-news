BREAKING: Federal Register: World Trade Center Health Program; Petition 014-Autoimmune Diseases; Finding of Insufficient Evidence

Published: 2017-02-21 | Source: Federal Register

On September 29, 2016, the Administrator of the World Trade Center (WTC) Health Program received a petition to add autoimmune diseases, including rheumatoid arthritis, to the List of WTC-Related Health Conditions (List). Upon reviewing the information provided by the petitioner, the Administrator has determined that Petition 014 is not substantially different from Petitions 007, 008, 009, 011, and 013, which also requested the addition of autoimmune diseases, including various subtypes.
