BREAKING: Federal Register: Wildlife and Fisheries; Compensatory Mitigation Mechanisms

Published: 2022-07-27 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Fish and Wildlife Service (Service) seeks comments to assist us in developing a proposed rule establishing objectives, measurable performance standards, and criteria for use, consistent with the Endangered Species Act, for species conservation banking. The terms "you" or "your" in this document refer to those members of the public from whom we seek response. The terms "we" and "us" refer to the Service.
