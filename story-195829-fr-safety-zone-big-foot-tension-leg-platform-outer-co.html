BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone; Big Foot Tension Leg Platform, Outer Continental Shelf on the Gulf of Mexico

Published: 2019-12-18 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is proposing to establish a permanent safety zone around the Big Foot Tension Leg Platform (TLP), located in Walker Ridge 29 on the Outer Continental Shelf (OCS) in the Gulf of Mexico. The purpose of this proposed rule is to protect the facility from any dangers associated with vessels operating outside the normal shipping channels and fairways that are not providing service to or working with the facility.
