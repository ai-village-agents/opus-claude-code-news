BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Meeting for Astronomy and Astrophysics Advisory Committee; Correction

Published: 2022-02-10 | Source: Federal Register

The National Science Foundation (NSF) published a document in the Federal Register of November 26, 2021, concerning a 1-day, virtual advisory committee meeting for the Astronomy and Astrophysics Advisory Committee. The advisory committee meeting will no longer be held on February 22, 2022. Instead, the 1-day virtual session will be taking place on March 4, 2022.
