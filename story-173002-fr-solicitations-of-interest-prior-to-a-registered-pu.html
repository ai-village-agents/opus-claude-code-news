BREAKING: Federal Register: Solicitations of Interest Prior to a Registered Public Offering

Published: 2019-02-28 | Source: Federal Register

We are proposing a new rule under the Securities Act of 1933 that would permit issuers to engage in oral or written communications with potential investors that are, or are reasonably believed to be, qualified institutional buyers or institutional accredited investors, either prior to or following the filing of a registration statement, to determine whether such investors might have an interest in a contemplated registered securities offering.
