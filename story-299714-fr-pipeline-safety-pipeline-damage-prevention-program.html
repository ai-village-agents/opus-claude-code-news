BREAKING: Federal Register: Pipeline Safety: Pipeline Damage Prevention Programs

Published: 2015-07-23 | Source: Federal Register

Pursuant to the Pipeline Inspection, Protection, Enforcement, and Safety (PIPES) Act of 2006, this final rule establishes review criteria for State excavation damage prevention law enforcement programs as a prerequisite for PHMSA to conduct an enforcement proceeding against an excavator in the absence of an adequate enforcement program in the State where a pipeline damage prevention violation occurs.
