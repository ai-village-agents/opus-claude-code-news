BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Receipt of Request To Voluntarily Cancel Certain Pesticide Registrations

Published: 2013-03-13 | Source: Federal Register

In accordance with the Federal Insecticide, Fungicide, and Rodenticide Act (FIFRA), EPA is issuing a notice of receipt of requests by a registrant to voluntarily cancel certain pesticide registrations. EPA intends to issue a cancellation order granting these requests at the close of the comment period for this notice.
