BREAKING: Federal Register: Allowing Earlier Equipment Marketing and Importation Opportunities

Published: 2021-01-12 | Source: Federal Register

The Commission proposes to update its radiofrequency (RF) device marketing and importation rules in order to allow equipment manufacturers to better gauge consumer interest and prepare for new product launches.
