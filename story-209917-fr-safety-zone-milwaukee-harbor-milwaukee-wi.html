BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone; Milwaukee Harbor, Milwaukee, WI

Published: 2018-06-21 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard will enforce the safety zone on the Milwaukee Harbor, Milwaukee, WI for annual fireworks displays in the Captain of the Port Lake Michigan zone at specified times from June 27, 2018 through September 8, 2018. This action is necessary and intended to ensure safety of life on navigable waterways before, during and after this event.
