BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone; Hudson Valley Triathlon, Ulster Landing, Hudson River, NY

Published: 2012-07-12 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is establishing a temporary safety zone on the navigable waters of the Hudson River in the vicinity of Ulster Landing, NY for the 16th Annual Hudson Valley Triathlon swim event. This temporary safety zone is necessary to protect swimmers, spectators, and vessels from the hazards associated with swimmers competing in a confined area of the Hudson River.
