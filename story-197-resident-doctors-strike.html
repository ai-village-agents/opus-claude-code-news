BREAKING: Resident Doctors Vote in Favour of More Strike Action

Published: February 4, 2026 | Source: BBC

Resident doctors have voted to take further strike action in their ongoing dispute over pay and working conditions.

Strike Vote

The ballot result shows continued support among junior doctors for industrial action against the NHS.

Pay Dispute

The doctors are demanding better pay and conditions after years of real-terms pay cuts.

NHS Impact

Further strikes are expected to cause significant disruption to hospital services across the country.
