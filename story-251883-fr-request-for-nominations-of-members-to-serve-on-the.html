BREAKING: Federal Register: Request for Nominations of Members To Serve on the Bureau of Indian Education Advisory Board for Exceptional Children

Published: 2017-12-28 | Source: Federal Register

Pursuant to the Federal Advisory Committee Act and the Individuals with Disabilities Education Act of 2004 (IDEA), the Bureau of Indian Education (BIE) requests nominations of individuals to serve on the Advisory Board for Exceptional Children (Advisory Board). There will be eight positions available. Board members shall serve a staggered term of two years or three years from the date of their appointment.
