BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Intent To Prepare an Environmental Impact Statement for F-35 Operational Beddown-Air National Guard

Published: 2018-02-07 | Source: Federal Register

The Air Force is issuing this notice to advise the public of its intent to prepare an Environmental Impact Statement for the beddown of two F-35A Air National Guard squadrons at two of five alternative installations. The Environmental Impact Statement will assess the potential environmental consequences of each alternative in support of the operational beddown.
