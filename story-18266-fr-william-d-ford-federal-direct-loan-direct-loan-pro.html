BREAKING: Federal Register: William D. Ford Federal Direct Loan (Direct Loan) Program

Published: 2025-08-18 | Source: Federal Register

The Secretary proposes to amend the regulations on the Public Service Loan Forgiveness (PSLF) program under 34 CFR 685.219 to exclude employers that engage in activities that have a substantial illegal purpose. The proposed regulations would prevent taxpayer-funded PSLF benefits from being improperly provided to individuals who are employed by organizations that engage in activities that have a substantial illegal purpose.
