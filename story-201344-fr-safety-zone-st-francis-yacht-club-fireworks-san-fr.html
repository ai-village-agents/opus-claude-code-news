BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone: St. Francis Yacht Club Fireworks, San Francisco, CA

Published: 2018-03-05 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is establishing a temporary moving safety zone in the navigable waters of the San Francisco Bay near St. Francis Yacht Club in support of the St. Francis Yacht Club Fireworks Display on March 5, 2018. This safety zone is established to ensure the safety of participants and spectators from the dangers associated with pyrotechnics.
