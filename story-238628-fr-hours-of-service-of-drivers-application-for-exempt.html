BREAKING: Federal Register: Hours of Service of Drivers: Application for Exemption; MBI Energy Services

Published: 2017-07-10 | Source: Federal Register

FMCSA announces that MBI Energy Services (MBI) has requested an exemption from the requirement that a motor carrier install and require each of its drivers to use an electronic logging device (ELD) to record the driver's hours-of-service (HOS) no later than December 18, 2017.
