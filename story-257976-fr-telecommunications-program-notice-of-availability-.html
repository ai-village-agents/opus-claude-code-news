BREAKING: Federal Register: Telecommunications Program: Notice of Availability of a Programmatic Environmental Assessment

Published: 2016-03-14 | Source: Federal Register

The Rural Utilities Service (RUS, Agency), an agency of the United States Department of Agriculture, issued a Programmatic Environmental Assessment (PEA) for the development of a more efficient and effective environmental review process for the RUS Telecommunications Program on March 1, 2016. The Notice of Availability of a Programmatic Environmental Assessment was published on March 2, 2016, in the Federal Register at 81 FR 10575.
