BREAKING: Federal Register: Transitional Program for Covered Business Method Patents-Definition of Technological Invention

Published: 2012-02-10 | Source: Federal Register

The United States Patent and Trademark Office (Office or USPTO) proposes a new rule to implement the provision of the Leahy- Smith America Invents Act that requires the Office to issue regulations for determining whether a patent is for a technological invention in a transitional post-grant review proceeding for covered business method patents.
