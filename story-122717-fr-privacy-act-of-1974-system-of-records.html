BREAKING: Federal Register: Privacy Act of 1974; System of Records

Published: 2021-05-03 | Source: Federal Register

In accordance with the Privacy Act of 1974, the DoD is modifying and reissuing a current system of records titled, "DoD Media Pool and Pentagon Correspondent Files," DPAD 12.0.
