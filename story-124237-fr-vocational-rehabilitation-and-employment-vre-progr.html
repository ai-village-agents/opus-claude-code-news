BREAKING: Federal Register: Vocational Rehabilitation and Employment (VR&E) Program: Name Change

Published: 2021-05-20 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Veterans Affairs (VA) proposes to amend the regulations pertaining to the name of the Chapter 31 Vocational Rehabilitation and Employment program. VA provides benefits and services under the program name of ``Vocational Rehabilitation and Employment''. VA is proposing to amend the name to ``Veteran Readiness and Employment'' (VR&E).
