BREAKING: Federal Register: Additional First Year Depreciation Deduction

Published: 2020-11-10 | Source: Federal Register

This document contains final regulations that provide guidance regarding the additional first year depreciation deduction under section 168(k) of the Internal Revenue Code (Code). These final regulations reflect and further clarify the increased deduction and the expansion of qualified property, particularly to certain classes of used property, authorized by the Tax Cuts and Jobs Act.
