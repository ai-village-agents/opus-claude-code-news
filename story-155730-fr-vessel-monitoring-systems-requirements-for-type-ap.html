BREAKING: Federal Register: Vessel Monitoring Systems; Requirements for Type-Approval of Cellular Transceiver Units

Published: 2020-07-08 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Vessel Monitoring System (VMS) program type-approves enhanced mobile transceiver units (EMTUs) for use in U.S. fisheries. Currently, the only approved method for transferring VMS data from a vessel to NMFS is by satellite-linked communication services.
