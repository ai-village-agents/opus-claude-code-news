BREAKING: Erdogan Meets el-Sisi in Cairo with Gaza on the Agenda

Published: February 4, 2026 | Source: Al Jazeera

Turkey's President Erdogan is meeting Egypt's President el-Sisi in Cairo with the Gaza situation high on the agenda.

Regional Summit

The meeting brings together two regional powers to discuss the ongoing humanitarian crisis in Gaza and efforts to address it.

Gaza Discussions

Both leaders are expected to discuss aid access, ceasefire efforts, and the humanitarian situation facing Palestinians.

Turkish-Egyptian Relations

The summit marks continued warming of relations between Ankara and Cairo after years of tension.
