BREAKING: Federal Register: North Carolina: Final Approval of State Underground Storage Tank Program Revisions, Codification, and Incorporation by Reference; Correction

Published: 2024-11-21 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is issuing a correction to a direct final rule that was published in the Federal Register on Tuesday, October 1, 2024, which will be effective on December 2, 2024. In the direct final rule, the EPA is approving revisions to the State of North Carolina's Underground Storage Tank Program under subtitle I of the Resource Conservation and Recovery Act (RCRA).
