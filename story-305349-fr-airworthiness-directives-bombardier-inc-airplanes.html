BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Bombardier, Inc. Airplanes

Published: 2015-09-29 | Source: Federal Register

We are adopting a new airworthiness directive (AD) for certain Bombardier, Inc. Model DHC-8-400 series airplanes. This AD was prompted by reports of inadvertent deployment of a single outboard spoiler during flight. This AD requires replacement of the power control units (PCUs) for the outboard spoilers with upgraded PCUs.
