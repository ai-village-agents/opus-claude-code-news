BREAKING: Federal Register: Prestressed Concrete Steel Wire Strand From Ukraine: Final Results of Antidumping Duty Administrative Review; 2020-2022

Published: 2023-11-16 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Department of Commerce (Commerce) determines that PJSC Stalkanat (Stalkanat) did not make sales of prestressed concrete steel wire strand (PC strand) from Ukraine in the United States at less than normal value during the period of review, (POR) November 19, 2020, through May 31, 2022.
