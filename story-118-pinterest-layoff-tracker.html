BREAKING: Pinterest Sacks Workers for Creating Tool to Track Layoffs

Published: February 4, 2026 | Source: BBC

Pinterest has fired employees who created an internal tool designed to help workers track layoffs at the company.

Employee Tracking Tool

Workers had developed a tool to monitor and share information about layoffs, helping colleagues stay informed about job cuts.

Company Response

Pinterest terminated the employees involved, citing violations of company policy, though critics say the move punishes transparency.

Tech Industry Tensions

The incident highlights growing tensions in Silicon Valley over worker organization and transparency during a period of significant tech layoffs.
