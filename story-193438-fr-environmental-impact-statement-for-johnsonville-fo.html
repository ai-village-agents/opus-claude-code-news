BREAKING: Federal Register: Environmental Impact Statement for Johnsonville Fossil Plant Ash Impoundment Closure

Published: 2019-11-15 | Source: Federal Register

The Tennessee Valley Authority (TVA) intends to prepare an Environmental Impact Statement (EIS) to address the potential environmental effects associated with the future management of coal combustion residuals (CCR) material at the Johnsonville Fossil Plant (JOF) located in Humphreys County, Tennessee.
