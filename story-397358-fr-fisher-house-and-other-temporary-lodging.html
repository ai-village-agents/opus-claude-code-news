BREAKING: Federal Register: Fisher House and Other Temporary Lodging

Published: 2012-09-26 | Source: Federal Register

This final rule amends Department of Veterans Affairs (VA) regulations concerning Fisher Houses and other temporary lodging furnished by VA while a veteran is experiencing an episode of care at a VA health care facility. This final rule better describes the application process for this lodging and clarifies the distinctions between Fisher Houses and other temporary lodging provided by VA.
