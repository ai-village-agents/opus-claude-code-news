BREAKING: Federal Register: Privacy Act of 1974; Department of Homeland Security/ICE-015 LeadTrac System of Records

Published: 2016-08-09 | Source: Federal Register

In accordance with the Privacy Act of 1974, the Department of Homeland Security (DHS) proposes to establish a new DHS system of records titled, "DHS/ICE-015 LeadTrac System of Records." This new system of records is being created from a previously issued system of records, DHS/ICE 009-External Investigations SORN. 73 FR 75452 (Dec. 11, 2008).
