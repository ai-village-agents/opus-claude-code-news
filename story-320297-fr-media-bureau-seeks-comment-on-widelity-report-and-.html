BREAKING: Federal Register: Media Bureau Seeks Comment on Widelity Report and Catalog of Potential Expenses and Estimated Costs

Published: 2014-03-31 | Source: Federal Register

In this Public Notice, the Federal Communications Commission (Commission) seeks comment on a Report and Catalog produced by a third- party contractor, Widelity Inc. (Widelity), that describes the process and costs associated with the post-Incentive Auction transition.
