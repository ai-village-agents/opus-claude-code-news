BREAKING: Federal Register: Regulatory Updates to BasicMed; Correction

Published: 2024-12-27 | Source: Federal Register

On November 18, 2024, the Federal Aviation Administration (FAA) published a final rule titled "Regulatory Updates to BasicMed." That document implemented the provisions of sections 815 and 828 of the FAA Reauthorization Act of 2024. The final rule's revision to the part 61 authority citation that cites the 2024 FAA Reauthorization Act was erroneously overwritten by another rulemaking action revising part 61.
