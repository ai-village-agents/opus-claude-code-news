BREAKING: Federal Register: Endangered and Threatened Species; Initiation of 5-Year Review

Published: 2014-09-08 | Source: Federal Register

NMFS announces a 5-year review of the southern right whale (Eubalaena australis) and sperm whale (Physeter macrocephalus) under the Endangered Species Act of 1973, as amended (ESA). A 5-year review is a periodic process conducted to ensure that the listing classification of a species is accurate.
