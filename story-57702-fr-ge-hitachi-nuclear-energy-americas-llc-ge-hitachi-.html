BREAKING: Federal Register: GE-Hitachi Nuclear Energy Americas, LLC; GE-Hitachi Nuclear Test Reactor

Published: 2023-01-10 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Nuclear Regulatory Commission (NRC) staff accepts, dockets, and is considering an application for the renewal of Facility Operating License No. R-33, submitted by GE-Hitachi Nuclear Energy Americas, LLC (the licensee), dated November 19, 2020, as supplemented. The renewed license would authorize the licensee to operate the GE- Hitachi Nuclear Test Reactor at a maximum steady-state thermal power of 100 kilowatts for an additional 20 years from the date of issuance.
