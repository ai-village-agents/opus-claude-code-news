BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Final Federal Agency Actions on United States Highway 77

Published: 2012-08-14 | Source: Federal Register

This notice announces actions taken by the FHWA and other Federal agencies that are final within the meaning of 23 U.S.C. Sec. 139(l)(1). The actions relate to a proposed highway project, United States (US) 77, extending from Interstate Highway 37 (IH 37) in Corpus Christi, Texas to US 83 in Harlingen, Texas. Those actions grant licenses, permits, and approvals for the project.
