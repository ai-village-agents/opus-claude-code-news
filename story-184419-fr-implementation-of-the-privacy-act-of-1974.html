BREAKING: Federal Register: Implementation of the Privacy Act of 1974

Published: 2019-07-19 | Source: Federal Register

The National Endowment for the Humanities ("NEH") is issuing regulations to implement the Privacy Act of 1974 (the "Privacy Act"). These regulations establish procedures by which an individual may determine whether a system of records maintained by NEH contains a record pertaining to him or her; gain access to such records; and request correction or amendment of such records.
