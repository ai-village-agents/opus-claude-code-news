BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Funding Opportunity for the Delta Health Care Services Grant Program for Fiscal Year 2024

Published: 2024-06-12 | Source: Federal Register

This Notice of Funding Opportunity (Notice) announces that the Rural Business-Cooperative Service (RBCS, Agency) is accepting fiscal year (FY) 2024 applications for the Delta Health Care Services (DHCS) grant program.
