BREAKING: Federal Register: Change to Certification Authority for the Alien Physician Category of the Exchange Visitor Program

Published: 2021-04-19 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of State (Department) is changing the certification authority for alien physicians from the American Board of Medical Specialties (ABMS) to the Accreditation Council for Graduate Medical Education (ACGME).
