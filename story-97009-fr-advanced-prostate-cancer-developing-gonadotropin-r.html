BREAKING: Federal Register: Advanced Prostate Cancer: Developing Gonadotropin-Releasing Hormone Analogues; Guidance for Industry; Availability

Published: 2022-05-27 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA or Agency) is announcing the availability of a final guidance for industry entitled "Advanced Prostate Cancer: Developing Gonadotropin-Releasing Hormone Analogues." This guidance describes FDA's current recommendations regarding the overall development program and clinical trial designs for developing gonadotropin-releasing hormone (GnRH) analogues to treat advanced prostate cancer.
