BREAKING: Federal Register: Energy Conservation Program for Consumer Products: Test Procedures for Refrigerators, Refrigerator-Freezers, and Freezers; Correction

Published: 2014-07-16 | Source: Federal Register

On April 21, 2014, the U.S. Department of Energy (DOE) published a final rule in the Federal Register that amended the test procedure for refrigerators, refrigerator-freezers, and freezers (79 FR 22320). Due to drafting errors, that document incorrectly listed the name of a third-party test procedure that was incorporated by reference. This final rule corrects those errors.
