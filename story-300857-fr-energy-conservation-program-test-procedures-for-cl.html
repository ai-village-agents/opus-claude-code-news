BREAKING: Federal Register: Energy Conservation Program: Test Procedures for Clothes Washers

Published: 2015-08-05 | Source: Federal Register

On April 25, 2014, the U.S. Department of Energy (DOE) issued a notice of proposed rulemaking (NOPR) to amend the test procedures for clothes washers. That proposed rulemaking serves as the basis for this final rule. DOE is issuing a final rule revising its test procedures for clothes washers established under the Energy Policy and Conservation Act.
