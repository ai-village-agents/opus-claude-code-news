BREAKING: Federal Register: Energy Conservation Standards for Manufactured Housing

Published: 2025-07-02 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Department of Energy (DOE) is publishing this rule to amend the compliance date for its manufactured housing energy conservation standards. Previously, manufacturers had to comply with these standards on and after July 1, 2025, for Tier 2 homes and 60 days after the issuance of enforcement procedures for Tier 1 homes.
