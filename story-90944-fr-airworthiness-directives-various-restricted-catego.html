BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Various Restricted Category Helicopters

Published: 2022-03-14 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA is superseding Airworthiness Directive (AD) 2021-15-52 which applied to various restricted category helicopters originally manufactured by Bell Textron Inc. with a certain main rotor hub strap pin (pin) installed. AD 2021-15-52 required removing certain outboard pins from service and prohibited installing them on any helicopter.
