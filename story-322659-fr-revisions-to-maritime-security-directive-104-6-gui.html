BREAKING: Federal Register: Revisions to Maritime Security Directive 104-6; Guidelines for U.S. Vessels Operating in High Risk Waters

Published: 2014-04-25 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard announces the availability of Revision 7 to Maritime Security (MARSEC) Directive 104-6, which provides guidelines for U.S. vessels operating in high risk waters (HRW) where acts of terrorism, piracy, and armed robbery against ships are prevalent, as well as specific guidance for offshore supply vessels and liftboats operating in the Gulf of Guinea.
