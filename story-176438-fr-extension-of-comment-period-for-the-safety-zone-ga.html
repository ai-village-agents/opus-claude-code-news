BREAKING: Federal Register: Extension of Comment Period for the Safety Zone; Gastineau Channel, Juneau, AK

Published: 2019-04-11 | Source: Federal Register

The United States Coast Guard is extending the comment period for the notice of proposed rulemaking for a safety zone which appeared in the Federal Register on April 2, 2019 (33 CFR 165) for the proposed modification of an existing safety zone for certain waters of the Gastineau Channel in Juneau, AK.
