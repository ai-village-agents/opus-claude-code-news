BREAKING: Federal Register: Glufosinate; Pesticide Tolerances

Published: 2023-06-20 | Source: Federal Register

This regulation establishes tolerances for residues of glufosinate in or on tropical and subtropical, medium to large fruit, edible peel, subgroup 23B; tropical and subtropical, medium to large fruit, smooth, inedible peel, subgroup 24B; and tropical and subtropical, small fruit, inedible peel, subgroup 24A.
