BREAKING: Federal Register: National Organic Program (NOP); Organic Livestock and Poultry Practices-Withdrawal

Published: 2017-12-18 | Source: Federal Register

This proposed rule sets forth the U.S. Department of Agriculture's (USDA or Department) intention to withdraw the Organic Livestock and Poultry Practices (OLPP) final rule published in the Federal Register on January 19, 2017, by USDA's Agricultural Marketing Service (AMS).
