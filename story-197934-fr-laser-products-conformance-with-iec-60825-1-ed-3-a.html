BREAKING: Federal Register: Laser Products-Conformance With IEC 60825-1 Ed. 3 and IEC 60601-2-22 Ed. 3.1 (Laser Notice No. 56); Draft Guidance for Industry and Food and Drug Administration Staff; Availability

Published: 2018-01-19 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA or Agency) is announcing the availability of the draft guidance entitled "Conformance with IEC 60825-1 Ed. 3 and IEC 60601-2-22 Ed. 3.1 (Laser Notice No. 56)." This draft guidance describes the Agency's proposed approach regarding compliance with FDA's performance standards for laser products.
