BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Airbus SAS Airplanes

Published: 2023-01-24 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA proposes to supersede Airworthiness Directive (AD) 2021-08-08, which applies to all Airbus SAS Model A350-941 and -1041 airplanes. AD 2021-08-08 requires replacing affected bleed duct assemblies and bleed gimbals at the wing-to-pylon interface, and prohibits the installation of affected parts.
