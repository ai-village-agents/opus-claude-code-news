BREAKING: Federal Register: Amendment to Emergency Relief Exemptions Pursuant to the Reliable Home Heating (RHH) Act

Published: 2014-10-22 | Source: Federal Register

FMCSA adopts, as final, certain amendments to the Federal Motor Carrier Safety Regulations (FMCSRs) required by the Reliable Home Heating (RHH) Act. Currently, the FMCSRs include a provision which enables motor carriers providing direct assistance in responding to an emergency declared by a governor to do so without having to comply with certain Federal safety regulations.
