BREAKING: Federal Register: Recordkeeping for Custodial Accounts; Extension of Comment Period

Published: 2024-11-20 | Source: Federal Register

On October 2, 2024, the FDIC published in the Federal Register a proposed rule that would strengthen FDIC-insured depository institutions' (IDI) recordkeeping for custodial deposit accounts with transactional features and preserve beneficial owners' and depositors' entitlement to the protections afforded by Federal deposit insurance.
