BREAKING: Federal Register: Accreditation and Approval of Camin Cargo Control, Inc., as a Commercial Gauger and Laboratory.

Published: 2014-02-10 | Source: Federal Register

Notice is hereby given, pursuant to CBP regulations, that Camin Cargo Control, Inc., has been approved to gauge and accredited to test petroleum and petroleum products, organic chemicals and vegetable oils for customs purposes for the next three years as of September 27, 2013.
