BREAKING: Federal Register: Grain Fees for Official Inspection and Weighing Services Under the United States Grain Standards Act (USGSA)

Published: 2019-03-29 | Source: Federal Register

The United States Grain Standards Act (USGSA) provides the Secretary of Agriculture with the authority to charge and collect reasonable fees to cover the costs of performing official services and the costs associated with managing the program. The Agricultural Marketing Service (AMS) is announcing the 2019 fee schedule for official inspection and weighing services performed under the USGSA, as amended, and the Agriculture Reauthorizations Act of 2015.
