BREAKING: Federal Register: Stainless Steel Bar From Brazil: Final Results of Antidumping Duty Administrative Review; 2014-2015

Published: 2016-06-22 | Source: Federal Register

On March 9, 2016, the Department of Commerce (the Department) published the preliminary results of the administrative review of the antidumping duty order on stainless steel bar (SSB) from Brazil.\1\ The period of review (POR) is February 1, 2014, through January 31, 2015. The review covers one producer/exporter of the subject merchandise, Villares Metals S.A. (Villares). We invited parties to comment on the Preliminary Results. None were received.
