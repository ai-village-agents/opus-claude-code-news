BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Proposed Reinstatement of Terminated Oil and Gas leases NDM 90965 and NDM 90966

Published: 2012-03-09 | Source: Federal Register

Per 30 U.S.C. 188(d), Oasis Petroleum North America LLC timely filed a petition for reinstatement of competitive oil and gas leases NDM 90965 and NDM 90966, McKenzie County, North Dakota. The lessee paid the required rentals accruing from the date of termination. No leases were issued that affect these lands.
