BREAKING: Federal Register: Chemical Facility Anti-Terrorism Standards

Published: 2014-08-18 | Source: Federal Register

Section 550 of the Department of Homeland Security Appropriations Act of 2007 provides the Department of Homeland Security (DHS or Department) with the authority to regulate the security of high risk chemical facilities. To implement this authority, DHS issued the Chemical Facility Anti-Terrorism Standards (CFATS) regulation in 2007.
