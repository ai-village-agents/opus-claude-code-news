BREAKING: Federal Register: Air Plan Approval; FL: Revisions to New Source Review, Definitions and Small Business Assistance Programs

Published: 2017-07-03 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is taking direct final action to approve changes to the Florida State Implementation Plan (SIP) to update definitions and make administrative edits to regulations for the Plantwide Applicability Limits (PALs) and Florida's Small Business Assistance program (SBA).
