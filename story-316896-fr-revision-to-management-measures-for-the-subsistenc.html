BREAKING: Federal Register: Revision to Management Measures for the Subsistence Taking of Northern Fur Seals on St. George Island, AK

Published: 2014-02-19 | Source: Federal Register

NMFS announces its intent to prepare a supplemental environmental impact statement (SEIS) in accordance with the National Environmental Policy Act of 1969. The SEIS will evaluate proposed changes in the management of the northern fur seal subsistence harvest on St. George Island, AK. The SEIS will supplement the 2005 Final Environmental Impact Statement for Setting the Annual Subsistence Harvest of Northern Fur Seals on the Pribilof Islands.
