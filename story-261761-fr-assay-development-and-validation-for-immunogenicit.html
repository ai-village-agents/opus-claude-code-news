BREAKING: Federal Register: Assay Development and Validation for Immunogenicity Testing of Therapeutic Protein Products; Revised Draft Guidance for Industry; Availability

Published: 2016-04-25 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA or Agency) is announcing the availability of a revised draft guidance for industry entitled "Assay Development and Validation for Immunogenicity Testing of Therapeutic Protein Products." This guidance provides recommendations to facilitate industry's development and validation of immune assays for assessment of the immunogenicity of therapeutic protein products during clinical trials.
