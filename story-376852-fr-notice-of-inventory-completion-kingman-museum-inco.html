BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Inventory Completion: Kingman Museum, Incorporated, Battle Creek, MI; Correction

Published: 2012-02-06 | Source: Federal Register

This notice corrects the cultural affiliation of the human remains of two individuals described in a Notice of Inventory Completion previously published in the Federal Register (73 FR 20941- 20942, Thursday, April 17, 2008), and also corrects the identity of the category of cultural item for a scalp, from human remains to associated funerary object.
