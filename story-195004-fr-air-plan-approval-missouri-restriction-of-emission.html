BREAKING: Federal Register: Air Plan Approval; Missouri; Restriction of Emissions From Batch-Type Charcoal Kilns

Published: 2019-12-06 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is proposing to approve revisions to the Missouri State Implementation Plan (SIP) received on March 7, 2019. The submission revises a Missouri regulation that establishes emission limits for batch-type charcoal kilns based on operational parameters to reduce emissions of particulate matter (PM10), volatile organic compounds (VOCs) and carbon monoxide (CO).
