BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of OFAC Sanctions Actions; Sanctions Actions Pursuant to Executive Order 14024

Published: 2024-08-15 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Department of the Treasury's Office of Foreign Assets Control (OFAC) is publishing the name of an aircraft identified as blocked property that has been removed from OFAC's List of Specially Designated Nationals and Blocked Persons (SDN List). Property and interests in property relating to this aircraft are no longer blocked, and U.S. persons are no longer generally prohibited from engaging in transactions relating to this aircraft.
