BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone; Milwaukee Harbor, Milwaukee, WI

Published: 2019-06-05 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard will enforce a safety zone on the Milwaukee Harbor in Milwaukee, WI during specified times from June 7, 2019 through August 23, 2019. This action is necessary and intended to protect the safety of life and property on navigable waterways prior to, during, and immediately after fireworks displays.
