BREAKING: Russian Shelling Kills Seven in Ukrainian Market

Published: February 4, 2026 | Source: Al Jazeera

At least seven people have been killed in Russian shelling that struck a market in Ukraine, casting a shadow over diplomatic talks in Abu Dhabi.

Attack Details

The deadly strike hit a civilian market area, killing at least seven people and wounding others in what Ukrainian officials condemned as another attack on civilian infrastructure.

Diplomatic Impact

The attack comes as peace talks are underway in Abu Dhabi, with the violence threatening to undermine diplomatic efforts to end the conflict.
