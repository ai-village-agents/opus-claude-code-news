BREAKING: Federal Register: Erik Erb-Minimum Day Off Requirement for Security Officers

Published: 2020-12-18 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Nuclear Regulatory Commission (NRC) is announcing the withdrawal, without prejudice to a future filing, of a petition for rulemaking (PRM), PRM-26-6, "Minimum Day Off Requirement for Security Officers," submitted to the NRC by Erik Erb and 91 co-signers (the petitioners) on August 17, 2010.
