BREAKING: Federal Register: Privacy Act of 1974; System of Records

Published: 2017-12-05 | Source: Federal Register

In accordance with the Privacy Act of 1974, the Office of Personnel Management proposes to establish a new OPM system of records titled "OPM/Central-20 National CFC System of Records." This system of records contains information that OPM collects and maintains about individuals who make charitable contributions to eligible non-profit organizations through the Combined Federal Campaign (CFC).
