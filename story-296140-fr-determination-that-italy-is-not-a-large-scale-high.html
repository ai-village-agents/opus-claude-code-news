BREAKING: Federal Register: Determination That Italy Is Not a Large-Scale High Seas Driftnet Nation

Published: 2015-06-11 | Source: Federal Register

Under the High Seas Driftnet Fisheries Enforcement Act and the Dolphin Protection Consumer Information Act (DPCIA), the Secretary of Commerce has determined that Italy no longer has vessels that use large-scale driftnets to fish on the high seas.
