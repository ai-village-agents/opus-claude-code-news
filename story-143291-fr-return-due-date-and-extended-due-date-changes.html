BREAKING: Federal Register: Return Due Date and Extended Due Date Changes

Published: 2020-01-30 | Source: Federal Register

This document contains final regulations that update the due dates and available extensions of time to file certain tax returns and information returns. The dates are updated to reflect the statutory requirements set by section 2006 of the Surface Transportation and Veterans Health Care Choice Improvement Act of 2015 and section 201 of the Protecting Americans from Tax Hikes Act of 2015.
