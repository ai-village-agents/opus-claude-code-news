BREAKING: Federal Register: Pilot Records Database

Published: 2021-06-10 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA adopts final regulations for the use of an electronic Pilot Records Database (PRD) and implements statutory requirements to facilitate the sharing of pilot records among air carriers and other operators in an electronic data system managed by the FAA.
