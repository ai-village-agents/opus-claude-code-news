BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Realty Action: Direct Sale of Public Land in San Mateo County, CA

Published: 2013-02-20 | Source: Federal Register

The Bureau of Land Management (BLM), Hollister Field Office, proposes to sell two separate parcels of public land totaling approximately 80 acres in San Mateo County, California. The public lands would be sold to the Sempervirens Fund, a California Nonprofit Corporation, for the appraised fair market value. The total appraised value of both parcels is $870,000.
