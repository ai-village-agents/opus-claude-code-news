BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone; Pyro Spectaculars for USS MIDWAY Museum, San Diego, CA

Published: 2014-08-12 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard will enforce the Pyro Spectaculars USS MIDWAY Museum firework display safety zone on August 28, 2014. This marine event occurs on the navigable waters of San Diego Bay, immediately to the west of the USS MIDWAY, in San Diego, California. This action is necessary to provide for the safety of the participants, crew, spectators, safety vessels, and general users of the waterway.
