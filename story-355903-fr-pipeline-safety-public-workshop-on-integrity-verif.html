BREAKING: Federal Register: Pipeline Safety: Public Workshop on Integrity Verification Process

Published: 2013-05-28 | Source: Federal Register

This notice is announcing a public workshop to be held on the concept of "Integrity Verification Process." The Integrity Verification Process shares similar characteristics with fitness for service processes.
