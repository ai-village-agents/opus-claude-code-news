BREAKING: Federal Register: Dynamic Positioning Operations Guidance for Vessels Other Than Mobile Offshore Drilling Units Operating on the U.S. Outer Continental Shelf

Published: 2012-10-12 | Source: Federal Register

On May 4, 2012 the Coast Guard published a notice of recommended interim voluntary guidance titled "Mobile Offshore Drilling Unit Dynamic Positioning Guidance". The notice recommended owners and operators of Mobile Offshore Drilling Units (MODUs) follow Marine Technology Society (MTS) Dynamic Positioning (DP) operations guidance for MODUs.
