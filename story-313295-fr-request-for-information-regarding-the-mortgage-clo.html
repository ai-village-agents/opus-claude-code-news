BREAKING: Federal Register: Request for Information Regarding the Mortgage Closing Process

Published: 2014-01-03 | Source: Federal Register

This notice requests information from the public about mortgage closing. Specifically, the Consumer Financial Protection Bureau (CFPB) seeks information on key consumer "pain points" associated with mortgage closing and how those pain points might be addressed by market innovations and technology.
