BREAKING: Federal Register: Provision of Certain Temporary Sanctions Relief

Published: 2014-02-14 | Source: Federal Register

The United States Government (USG) is issuing temporary waivers of certain sanctions to allow for a discrete range of transactions related to the provision of satellite connectivity services to the Islamic Republic of Iran Broadcasting (IRIB).
