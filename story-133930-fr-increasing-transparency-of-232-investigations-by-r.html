BREAKING: Federal Register: Increasing Transparency of 232 Investigations by Requiring a Public Submission for an Application for an Investigation

Published: 2021-09-24 | Source: Federal Register

This final rule revises the regulations governing a request or application for an investigation under Section 232 of the Trade Expansion Act of 1962, as amended ("Section 232"). The changes in this final rule will increase the transparency of Section 232 investigations by requiring the submission of a public version of an application for an investigation from an interested party.
