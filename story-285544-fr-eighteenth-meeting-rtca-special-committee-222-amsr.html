BREAKING: Federal Register: Eighteenth Meeting: RTCA Special Committee 222, AMS(R)S

Published: 2015-02-04 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA is issuing this notice to advise the public of the eighteenth meeting of the RTCA Special Committee 222, Inmarsat AMS(R)S. The purpose of this meeting is threefold. First, we will consider the draft Change 4 to DO-210D. The draft will be submitted to the workspace no later than close of business Eastern time on January 12.
