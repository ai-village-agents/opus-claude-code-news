BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Pratt & Whitney Turbofan Engines

Published: 2015-03-02 | Source: Federal Register

We propose to adopt a new airworthiness directive (AD) for all Pratt & Whitney (PW) JT8D-217C and JT8D-219 turbofan engines. This proposed AD was prompted by reports of cracking in the low-pressure turbine (LPT) shaft. This proposed AD establishes a new lower life limit for these parts and would require removing affected LPT shafts from service using a drawdown plan.
