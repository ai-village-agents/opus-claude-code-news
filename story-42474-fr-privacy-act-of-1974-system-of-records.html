BREAKING: Federal Register: Privacy Act of 1974; System of Records

Published: 2024-07-24 | Source: Federal Register

In accordance with the Privacy Act of 1974, the Consumer Financial Protection Bureau (CFPB or Bureau) proposes to establish a new CFPB system of records titled, "CFPB.030--Nonbank Registry" (Nonbank Registry). Generally, "nonbanks" are institutions involved in the offering or provision of consumer financial products or services that are not insured depository institutions or insured credit unions (hereinafter referred to as "nonbanks").
