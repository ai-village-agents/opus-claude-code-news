BREAKING: Federal Register: Petition for Exemption From the Federal Motor Vehicle Motor Theft Prevention Standard; General Motors Corporation

Published: 2012-10-30 | Source: Federal Register

This document grants in full General Motors Corporation's (GM) petition for an exemption of the Cadillac ATS vehicle line in accordance with 49 CFR Part 543, Exemption from the Theft Prevention Standard.
