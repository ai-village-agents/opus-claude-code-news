BREAKING: Federal Register: Addition of a Subsurface Intrusion Component to the Hazard Ranking System

Published: 2016-02-29 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is proposing to add a subsurface intrusion (SsI) component to the Hazard Ranking System (HRS) which is the principal mechanism that EPA uses to evaluate sites for placement on the National Priorities List (NPL).
