BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone; French Quarter Festival Fireworks Display, New Orleans LA

Published: 2023-03-29 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard will enforce a safety zone for the French Quarter Festival fireworks display from 7:30 to 8:45 p.m. on April 13, 2023. The safety zone will be enforced for all navigable waters of the Lower Mississippi River, New Orleans, LA from Mile Marker (MM) 94 to MM 95. This action is needed to provide for the safety of life on the navigable waterways during this event.
