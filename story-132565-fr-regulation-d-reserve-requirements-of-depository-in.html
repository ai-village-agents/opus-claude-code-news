BREAKING: Federal Register: Regulation D: Reserve Requirements of Depository Institutions

Published: 2021-09-08 | Source: Federal Register

The Board of Governors of the Federal Reserve System ("Board") is amending Regulation D, Reserve Requirements of Depository Institutions, to revise the rate of interest paid on balances maintained at Federal Reserve Banks by or on behalf of eligible institutions ("IORB" rate). The final amendments specify that the IORB rate is 0.15 percent, an 0.05 percentage point increase from its prior level.
