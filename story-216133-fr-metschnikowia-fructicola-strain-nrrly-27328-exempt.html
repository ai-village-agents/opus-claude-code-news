BREAKING: Federal Register: Metschnikowia Fructicola Strain NRRLY-27328; Exemption From the Requirement of a Tolerance

Published: 2018-09-12 | Source: Federal Register

This regulation establishes an exemption from the requirement of a tolerance for residues of Metschnikowia fructicola strain NRRL Y- 27328 in or on the stone fruit group (group 12-12); the small fruit vine climbing subgroup, except fuzzy kiwifruit (subgroup 13-07F); and the low growing berry subgroup (subgroup 13-07G) when used in accordance with label directions and good agricultural practices.
