BREAKING: Federal Register: Resource Management Planning

Published: 2016-04-22 | Source: Federal Register

On February 25, 2016, the Bureau of Land Management (BLM) published in the Federal Register a proposed rule to amend existing regulations that establish the procedures used to prepare, revise, or amend land use plans pursuant to the Federal Land Policy and Management Act (FLPMA).
