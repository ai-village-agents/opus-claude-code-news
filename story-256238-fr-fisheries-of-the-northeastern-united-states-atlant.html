BREAKING: Federal Register: Fisheries of the Northeastern United States; Atlantic Sea Scallop Fishery; Framework Adjustment 27

Published: 2016-02-24 | Source: Federal Register

NMFS proposes to approve and implement measures included in Framework Adjustment 27 to the Atlantic Sea Scallop Fishery Management Plan, which the New England Fishery Management Council adopted and submitted to NMFS for approval. The purpose of Framework 27 is to prevent overfishing, improve yield-per-recruit, and improve the overall management of the Atlantic sea scallop fishery.
