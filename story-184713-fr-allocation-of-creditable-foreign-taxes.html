BREAKING: Federal Register: Allocation of Creditable Foreign Taxes

Published: 2019-07-24 | Source: Federal Register

This document contains final regulations with respect to a provision of the Internal Revenue Code (Code) that addresses the allocation by a partnership of foreign income taxes. These regulations are necessary to improve the operation of an existing safe harbor rule that determines whether allocations of creditable foreign tax expenditures are deemed to be in accordance with the partners' interests in the partnership.
