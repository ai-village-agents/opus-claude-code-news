BREAKING: Federal Register: Amendment of Class D and Class E Airspace; Moses Lake, WA

Published: 2018-12-14 | Source: Federal Register

This action modifies Class D airspace, Class E surface area airspace, Class E airspace designated as an extension, and Class E airspace extending upward from 700 and 1,200 feet above the surface at Grant County International Airport (formerly Grant County Airport), Moses Lake, WA.
