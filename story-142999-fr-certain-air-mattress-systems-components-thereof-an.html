BREAKING: Federal Register: Certain Air Mattress Systems, Components Thereof, and Methods of Using the Same; Commission Determination Vacating the Final Determination Relating to United States Patent No. 5,904,172 and the Limited Exclusion Order Based Thereon; Termination of the Investigation

Published: 2020-01-28 | Source: Federal Register

Notice is hereby given that the U.S. International Trade Commission has determined to vacate the portion of its final determination relating to United States Patent No. 5,904,172 ("the '172 patent") and its limited exclusion order based thereon.
