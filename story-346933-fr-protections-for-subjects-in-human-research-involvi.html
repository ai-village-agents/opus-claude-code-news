BREAKING: Federal Register: Protections for Subjects in Human Research Involving Pesticides

Published: 2013-02-14 | Source: Federal Register

EPA is finalizing narrowly tailored amendments to the portions of its rules for the protection of human subjects of research applying to third parties who conduct or support research with pesticides involving intentional exposure of human subjects and to persons who submit the results of human research with pesticides to EPA.
