BREAKING: Federal Register: Medical Devices; Quality System Regulation Amendments; Correction

Published: 2024-10-15 | Source: Federal Register

In that final rule, FDA amended the device current good manufacturing practice (CGMP) requirements of the Quality System (QS) regulation to harmonize and modernize the device CGMP.
