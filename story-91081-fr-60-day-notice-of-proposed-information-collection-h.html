BREAKING: Federal Register: 60-Day Notice of Proposed Information Collection: HECM Counseling Client Survey, OMB Control No.: 2502-0585

Published: 2022-03-15 | Source: Federal Register

HUD is seeking approval from the Office of Management and Budget (OMB) for the information collection OMB 2502-0585. OHC is seeking to reinstate OMB Collection 2502-0585 so that an OMB 83-D form can be submitted to discontinue this collection. HUD form 92911 is obsolete and hasn't been used in over 7 years.
