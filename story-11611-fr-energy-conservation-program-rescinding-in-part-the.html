BREAKING: Federal Register: Energy Conservation Program: Rescinding in Part the Amended Energy Conservation Standards for Dehumidifiers

Published: 2025-05-16 | Source: Federal Register

DOE is proposing to rescind the amended energy conservation standards for all dehumidifiers (excluding whole-home dehumidifiers) hereinafter ("dehumidifiers" or "non-whole-home dehumidifiers"), which constitute a distinct class of dehumidifier for which energy conservation standards are not statutorily prescribed.
