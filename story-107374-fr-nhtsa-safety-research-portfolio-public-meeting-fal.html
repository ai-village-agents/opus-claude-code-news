BREAKING: Federal Register: NHTSA Safety Research Portfolio Public Meeting: Fall 2022

Published: 2022-10-12 | Source: Federal Register

NHTSA will hold a Public Meeting from November 1-3, 2022, as a joint effort between the Agency's Vehicle Safety Research and Behavioral Safety Research offices to share information on activities within the Agency's research programs. The meeting will be held in a virtual format with representatives from across the two research offices presenting the information in a panel format.
