BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Public Meeting of the Ohio Advisory Committee for a Meeting To Discuss the Committee's Next Topic of Civil Rights Study: Equal Access to Education

Published: 2017-03-28 | Source: Federal Register

Notice is hereby given, pursuant to the provisions of the rules and regulations of the U.S. Commission on Civil Rights (Commission) and the Federal Advisory Committee Act that the Ohio Advisory Committee (Committee) will hold a meeting on Wednesday, April 26, 2017, at 3:00 p.m. EST for the purpose of discussing a proposal to study civil rights and equal access to education in Ohio.
