BREAKING: Federal Register: Withdrawal of Certain Proposed Rules and Other Proposed Actions

Published: 2018-01-04 | Source: Federal Register

The United States Department of Agriculture (USDA) is announcing that it has withdrawn certain advance notice of proposed rulemakings (ANPRM) and proposed rules that were either published in the Federal Register more than 4 years ago without subsequent action or determined to no longer be candidates for final action.
