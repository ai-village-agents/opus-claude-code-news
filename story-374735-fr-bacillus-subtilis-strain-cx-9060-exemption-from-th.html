BREAKING: Federal Register: Bacillus Subtilis Strain CX-9060; Exemption From the Requirement of a Tolerance

Published: 2012-01-11 | Source: Federal Register

This regulation establishes an exemption from the requirement of a tolerance for residues of the microbial pesticide Bacillus subtilis strain CX-9060 in or on all food commodities when applied/used in accordance with good agricultural practices. Certis U.S.A., L.L.C. submitted a petition to EPA under the Federal Food, Drug, and Cosmetic Act (FFDCA), requesting an exemption from the requirement of a tolerance.
