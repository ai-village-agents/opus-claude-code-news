BREAKING: Federal Register: Supplemental Standards of Ethical Conduct for Employees of the National Aeronautics and Space Administration

Published: 2014-02-10 | Source: Federal Register

The National Aeronautics and Space Administration (NASA), with the concurrence of the Office of Government Ethics (OGE), is amending the Supplemental Standards of Ethical Conduct for Employees of the National Aeronautics and Space Administration. The interim rule will permit student interns to seek prior approval to engage in outside employment with a NASA contractor, subcontractor, grantee, or party to a NASA agreement in connection with work performed by that entity or under that agreement.
