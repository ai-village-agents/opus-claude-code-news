BREAKING: Federal Register: Request for Information: Summer Meal Programs Data Reporting Requirements

Published: 2015-03-09 | Source: Federal Register

The purpose of this Request for Information is to help the Food and Nutrition Service (FNS) better understand what sponsors and State agencies could regularly report to FNS to more adequately track participation in the summer meal programs. The current data reporting system for sponsors and State agencies was designed primarily to process meal claims and not to track program participation.
