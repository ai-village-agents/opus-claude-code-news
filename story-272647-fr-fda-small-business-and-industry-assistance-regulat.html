BREAKING: Federal Register: FDA Small Business and Industry Assistance Regulatory Education for Industry Fall Conference

Published: 2016-08-30 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration's (FDA) Center for Drug Evaluation and Research (CDER) and the Center for Devices and Radiological Health (CDRH) are sponsoring a 2 day conference entitled "FDA Small Business and Industry Assistance Regulatory Education for Industry (REdI) Fall Conference." The goal of this conference is to provide direct, relevant, and helpful information on the key aspects of drug and device regulations.
