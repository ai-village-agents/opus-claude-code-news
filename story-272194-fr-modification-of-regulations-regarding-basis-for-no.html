BREAKING: Federal Register: Modification of Regulations Regarding Basis for Normal Value

Published: 2016-08-25 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Commerce ("the Department") proposes to modify the regulations pertaining to the use of constructed value or third country sales for purposes of determining normal value, where the exporting country does not constitute a viable market, and is seeking comments from parties. This modification, if adopted, will specify that, where the exporting country does not constitute a viable market, the Department normally will calculate normal value based upon constructed value.
