BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Filing of Plat of Survey, New Mexico

Published: 2019-09-26 | Source: Federal Register

The plat of survey of the following described land is scheduled to be officially filed 30 days after the date of this publication in the Bureau of Land Management (BLM), New Mexico Office, Santa Fe, New Mexico. The survey announced in this notice is necessary for the management of lands administered by the agency indicated.
