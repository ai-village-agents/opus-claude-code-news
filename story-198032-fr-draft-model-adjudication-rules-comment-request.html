BREAKING: Federal Register: Draft Model Adjudication Rules; Comment Request

Published: 2018-01-22 | Source: Federal Register

The Office of the Chairman of the Administrative Conference of the United States is revising its 1993 Model Adjudication Rules and is inviting public comment on the draft revised Rules. The current draft of the revised Rules is available at https://www.acus.gov/model-rules/ model-adjudication-rules.
