BREAKING: Federal Register: Combating Auto Retail Scams Trade Regulation Rule

Published: 2024-02-22 | Source: Federal Register

On January 4, 2024, the Federal Trade Commission ("FTC" or "Commission") published a Final Rule in the Federal Register, titled "Combating Auto Retail Scams Trade Regulation Rule" ("CARS Rule," "Rule," or "Final Rule"), in order to curtail certain unfair or deceptive acts or practices by motor vehicle dealers.
