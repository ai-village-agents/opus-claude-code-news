BREAKING: Federal Register: Removal of the Date Restriction for Flight Training in Experimental Light Sport Aircraft; Withdrawal

Published: 2023-06-23 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA is withdrawing a previously published notice of proposed rulemaking that proposed to amend the regulations governing the operating limitations for certain experimental light-sport aircraft. The rulemaking proposed to remove the date restriction that currently prevents flight training on these aircraft and add language to permit training in certain experimental light-sport aircraft for compensation or hire through existing deviation authority.
