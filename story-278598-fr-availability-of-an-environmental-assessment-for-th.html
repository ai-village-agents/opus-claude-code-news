BREAKING: Federal Register: Availability of an Environmental Assessment for the Biological Control of Giant Reed

Published: 2016-11-08 | Source: Federal Register

We are advising the public that the Animal and Plant Health Inspection Service has prepared a draft environmental assessment relative to the control of giant reed (Arundo donax). The environmental assessment considers the effects of, and alternatives to, the field release of a gall-forming fly, Lasioptera donacis, into the continental United States for use as a biological control agent to reduce the severity of giant reed infestations.
