BREAKING: Federal Register: Energy Conservation Program: Test Procedures for Dehumidifiers

Published: 2015-02-04 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Department of Energy (DOE) proposes to amend the test procedure proposals presented in a notice of proposed rulemaking (NOPR), published on May 21, 2014. The proposed revisions include modifications to the whole-home dehumidifier test setup and conduct, and revisions to the measurement of energy use in fan-only operation first proposed in the May 2014 NOPR.
