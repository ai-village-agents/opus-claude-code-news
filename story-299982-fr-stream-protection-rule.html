BREAKING: Federal Register: Stream Protection Rule

Published: 2015-07-27 | Source: Federal Register

We, the Office of Surface Mining Reclamation and Enforcement (OSMRE or OSM), are proposing to revise our regulations, based on, among other things, advances in science, to improve the balance between environmental protection and the Nation's need for coal as a source of energy.
