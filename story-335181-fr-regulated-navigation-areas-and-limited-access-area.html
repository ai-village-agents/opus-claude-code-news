BREAKING: Federal Register: Regulated Navigation Areas and Limited Access Areas; Waterway Management of Apra Harbor, Guam

Published: 2014-09-19 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard proposes to revise and consolidate existing regulated navigation areas, security zones and safety zones currently in place in Apra Harbor, Guam. This action is intended to replace a number of redundant, potentially confusing and outdated navigation regulations with a cogent regulatory framework.
