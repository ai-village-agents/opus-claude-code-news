BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Public Meeting on Developments in Trade Secret Protection

Published: 2017-04-11 | Source: Federal Register

The United States Patent and Trademark Office (USPTO) will be holding a public symposium on issues relevant to the protection of trade secrets. Since our last symposium on these issues in January 2015, the area of trade secret protection has continued to develop, most notably with the May 11, 2016, enactment of the Defend Trade Secrets Act (DTSA).
