BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Availability of an Environmental Assessment; Importation of Plants in Approved Growing Media Into the United States

Published: 2019-04-10 | Source: Federal Register

We are advising the public that the Animal and Plant Health Inspection Service is making available a draft programmatic environmental assessment for the importation of plants in approved growing media. The programmatic environmental assessment considers the potential environmental effects of a standardized set of pest risk mitigations for routine market requests to import plants in approved growing media.
