BREAKING: Federal Register: Announcement of Preliminary Regulatory Determinations for Contaminants on the Fourth Drinking Water Contaminant Candidate List; Extension of Comment Period

Published: 2020-04-30 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is extending the comment period for the document issued in the Federal Register on March 10, 2020, titled "Announcement of Preliminary Regulatory Determinations for Contaminants on the Fourth Drinking Water Contaminant Candidate List." In response to stakeholder requests, the EPA is extending the comment period an additional 30 days from May 11, 2020 to June 10, 2020.
