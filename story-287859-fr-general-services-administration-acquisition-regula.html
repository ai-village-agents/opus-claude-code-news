BREAKING: Federal Register: General Services Administration Acquisition Regulation (GSAR); Transactional Data Reporting

Published: 2015-03-04 | Source: Federal Register

The General Services Administration (GSA) announces a public meeting and request for comment on its proposal to amend the General Services Administration Acquisition Regulation (GSAR) to include clauses that would require vendors to report transactional data from orders and prices paid by ordering activities.
