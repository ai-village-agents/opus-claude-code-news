BREAKING: Federal Register: Proposed Amendment and Establishment of Class E Airspace; Columbus, NE

Published: 2018-03-29 | Source: Federal Register

This action proposes to amend Class E airspace designated as a surface area and remove the Notice to Airmen (NOTAM) part-time status, amend Class E airspace extending upward from 700 feet above the surface, and establish Class E airspace designated as an extension to the Class E surface area at Columbus Municipal Airport, Columbus, NE.
