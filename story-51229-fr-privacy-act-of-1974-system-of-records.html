BREAKING: Federal Register: Privacy Act of 1974; System of Records

Published: 2024-10-30 | Source: Federal Register

Pursuant to the provisions of the Privacy Act of 1974, as amended, the Department of the Housing and Urban Development (HUD), proposes a new Privacy Act System of Records titled, Customer Relationship Management, to include all "Customer Relationship Management" systems in use by HUD. This notice incorporates the One Stop Customer Service, HUD Central, and Microsoft Dynamics systems.
