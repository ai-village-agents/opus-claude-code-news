BREAKING: Federal Register: Privacy Act of 1974; System of Records

Published: 2021-01-14 | Source: Federal Register

The United States Agency for International Development proposes to significantly modify the "United States Agency for International Development, Partner-Vetting Enhancement Project, USAID- 27, Partner-Vetting System, System of Records." The purpose of the system is to support the vetting of directors, officers, and other employees of non-governmental organizations that apply to USAID for contracts, grants, cooperative agreements, or other funding.
