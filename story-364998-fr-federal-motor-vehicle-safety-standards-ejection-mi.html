BREAKING: Federal Register: Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standards; Ejection Mitigation

Published: 2013-09-09 | Source: Federal Register

This document responds to petitions for reconsideration of a 2011 final rule that established Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard (FMVSS) No. 226, "Ejection mitigation." The standard is intended to reduce complete and partial ejections of vehicle occupants through side windows in crashes, particularly rollover crashes.
