BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Airbus Helicopters Deutschland GmbH (AHD) Helicopters

Published: 2021-04-19 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA proposes to adopt a new airworthiness directive (AD) for certain Airbus Helicopters Deutschland GmbH (AHD) Model BO-105A, BO-105C, BO-105S, and BO-105LS A-3 helicopters equipped with a certain hoist system. This AD was prompted by an uncommanded activation of the hoist cable cutter function on an MBB-BK117 C-1 helicopter, which prompted a design review of the BO105 hoist control grip with coiled cable.
