BREAKING: Federal Register: Hours of Service of Drivers: National Pork Producers Council; Granting of Application for Exemption

Published: 2014-06-11 | Source: Federal Register

FMCSA announces the granting of a limited one-year exemption from the 30-minute rest break provision of the Agency's hours-of- service (HOS) regulations for commercial motor vehicle (CMV) drivers transporting livestock.
