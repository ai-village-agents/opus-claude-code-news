BREAKING: Federal Register: Special Local Regulations; SUP3Rivers the Southside Outside, Pittsburgh, PA

Published: 2017-08-01 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard will enforce a special local regulation for navigable waters of the Allegheny and Monongahela Rivers during the SUP3Rivers the Southside Outside event. This regulation is needed to provide for the safety of life during the marine event.
