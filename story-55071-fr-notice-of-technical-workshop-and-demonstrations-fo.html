BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Technical Workshop and Demonstrations for Vehicle Classification Test Procedure: Extension of Comment Period

Published: 2024-12-12 | Source: Federal Register

NHTSA is extending the comment period for the notice NHTSA published on October 29, 2024, seeking public comment on draft test procedure (TP) number TP-523-00, which is intended to assess vehicles for compliance with certain off-road capabilities requirements for vehicle classification within the Corporate Average Fuel Economy (CAFE) program. The comment period for the RFC notice was scheduled to end on November 30, 2024. NHTSA is extending the comment period for the notice to February 28, 2025,...
