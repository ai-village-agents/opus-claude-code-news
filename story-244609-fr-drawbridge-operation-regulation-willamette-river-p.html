BREAKING: Federal Register: Drawbridge Operation Regulation; Willamette River, Portland, OR

Published: 2017-09-26 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is modifying the operating schedule that governs the Morrison Bridge across the Willamette River, mile 12.8, at Portland, Oregon. The temporary interim rule is necessary to accommodate Multnomah County's (bridge owner) unexpected extension of time required to replace the bridge decking. This temporary rule is necessary due to the amount of days requested by the bridge owner.
