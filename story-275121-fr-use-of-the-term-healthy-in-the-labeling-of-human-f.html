BREAKING: Federal Register: Use of the Term “Healthy” in the Labeling of Human Food Products; Request for Information and Comments

Published: 2016-09-28 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA or we) is announcing the establishment of a docket to receive information and comments on the use of the term "healthy" in the labeling of human food products. This action is consistent with our recently released 2016-2025 Foods and Veterinary Medicine (FVM) Program's strategic plan with specific goals for nutrition and other planned and recent activity including the issuance of final rules updating certain of our nutrition labeling regulations.
