BREAKING: Federal Register: Exchange Visitor Program-Use of Forms DS-2019 in the Summer Work Travel Program

Published: 2016-07-05 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of State (the Department) will permit current sponsors in the Summer Work Travel (SWT) program category to apply to the Department for a program adjustment by allocation of Forms DS-2019 that were previously allocated to SWT sponsors in business for the full 2011 calendar year, but which no longer operate in the SWT program category.
