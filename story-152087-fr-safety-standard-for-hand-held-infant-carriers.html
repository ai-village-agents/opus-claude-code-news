BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Standard for Hand-Held Infant Carriers

Published: 2020-05-20 | Source: Federal Register

In December 2013, the U.S. Consumer Product Safety Commission (CPSC) issued a consumer product safety standard for hand-held infant carriers. The standard incorporated by reference the applicable ASTM voluntary standard, with one modification in the definition of the product, to clarify that semi-rigid carriers fall within the scope of the standard.
