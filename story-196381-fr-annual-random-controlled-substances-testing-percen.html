BREAKING: Federal Register: Annual Random Controlled Substances Testing Percentage Rate for Calendar Year 2020

Published: 2019-12-27 | Source: Federal Register

The FMCSA announces that it is increasing the minimum annual percentage rate for random controlled substances testing for drivers of commercial motor vehicles (CMVs) requiring a commercial driver's license (CDL) from the current rate of 25 percent of the average number of driver positions to 50 percent of the average number of driver positions, effective in calendar year 2020.
