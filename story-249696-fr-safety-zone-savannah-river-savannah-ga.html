BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone, Savannah River, Savannah, GA

Published: 2017-11-29 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is establishing a temporary safety zone for navigable waters around the M/V SWAN during its transit from the Savannah River entry point to Garden City Terminal in Savannah, GA and during cargo operations. The safety zone is needed to protect personnel, vessels, and the marine environment from potential hazards created by the transport and offloading of oversized cranes.
