BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Quest Aircraft Design, LLC Airplanes

Published: 2015-11-05 | Source: Federal Register

We propose to adopt a new airworthiness directive (AD) for certain Quest Aircraft Design, LLC Model KODIAK 100 airplanes. This proposed AD was prompted by a report of limited control yoke movement of the elevator control system due to cushion edging jammed in the elevator control anti-rotation guide slot.
