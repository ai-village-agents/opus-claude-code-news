BREAKING: Federal Register: Privacy Act of 1974; System of Records

Published: 2019-10-29 | Source: Federal Register

In accordance with the Privacy Act of 1974, as amended (Privacy Act), the U.S. Department of Education (Department) modifies the system of records entitled "Federal Student Aid Application File" (18-11-01). The Federal Student Aid Application File system of records contains information provided by applicants for title IV of the Higher Education Act of 1965, as amended, (HEA) program assistance, which is collected from the Free Application for Federal Student Aid (FAFSA).
