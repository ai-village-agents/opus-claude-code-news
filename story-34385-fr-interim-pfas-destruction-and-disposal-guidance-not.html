BREAKING: Federal Register: Interim PFAS Destruction and Disposal Guidance; Notice of Availability for Public Comment

Published: 2024-04-16 | Source: Federal Register

The National Defense Authorization Act for Fiscal Year 2020 (FY 2020 NDAA) was signed into law on December 19, 2019. Section 7361 of the FY 2020 NDAA directs the U.S. Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) to publish interim guidance on the destruction and disposal of perfluoroalkyl and polyfluoroalkyl substances (PFAS) and materials containing PFAS and to update the guidance at least every three years, as appropriate.
