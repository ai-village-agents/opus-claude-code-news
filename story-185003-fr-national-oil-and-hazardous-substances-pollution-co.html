BREAKING: Federal Register: National Oil and Hazardous Substances Pollution Contingency Plan; National Priorities List: Partial Deletion of the Cleburn Street Well Superfund Site

Published: 2019-07-29 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) Region 7 is publishing a direct final Notice of Partial Deletion of the Cleburn Street Well Superfund Site (Site), located in Grand Island, Nebraska, from the National Priorities List (NPL) for of the Operable Unit (OU) 1 and OU4. This partial deletion pertains to OU1--Contaminated sub- surface soil at former One-Hour Martinizing (OHM) and OU4--Soil and Groundwater at Ideal Cleaners.
