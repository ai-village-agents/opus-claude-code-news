BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Public Meeting of the Connecticut Advisory Committee to the U.S. Commission on Civil Rights

Published: 2023-03-23 | Source: Federal Register

Notice is hereby given, pursuant to the provisions of the rules and regulations of the U.S. Commission on Civil Rights (Commission) and the Federal Advisory Committee Act that the Connecticut Advisory Committee (Committee) to the U.S. Commission on Civil Rights will convene a business meeting on Thursday, April 6, 2023, 12 p.m. Eastern Time. The purpose of the meeting is to plan next steps on its report on civil rights implications of algorithms.
