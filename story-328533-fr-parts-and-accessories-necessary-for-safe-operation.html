BREAKING: Federal Register: Parts and Accessories Necessary for Safe Operation; Application for an Exemption From Mobileye, Inc.

Published: 2014-07-02 | Source: Federal Register

The Federal Motor Carrier Safety Administration (FMCSA) requests public comment on an application for exemption from Mobileye, Inc. (Mobileye) to allow interstate motor carrier to install Mobileye's camera-based collision avoidance system (CAS) system at either the bottom or top of the windshield, within the swept area of the windshield wipers.
