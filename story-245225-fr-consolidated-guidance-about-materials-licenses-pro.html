BREAKING: Federal Register: Consolidated Guidance About Materials Licenses: Program-Specific Guidance About Service Provider Licenses

Published: 2017-10-03 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Nuclear Regulatory Commission (NRC) has issued Revision 1 to NUREG-1556, Volume 18, "Consolidated Guidance about Materials Licenses: Program-Specific Guidance about Service Provider Licenses," which updates licensing guidance for service provider licenses. This document has been revised to include information on updated regulatory requirements, safety culture, security of radioactive materials, protection of sensitive information, and changes in regulatory policies and practices.
