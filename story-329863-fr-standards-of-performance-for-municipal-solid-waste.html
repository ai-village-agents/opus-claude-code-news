BREAKING: Federal Register: Standards of Performance for Municipal Solid Waste Landfills

Published: 2014-07-17 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is proposing a new subpart, 40 CFR part 60, subpart XXX that updates the Standards of Performance for Municipal Solid Waste Landfills. Under section 111 of the Clean Air Act, the EPA must review, and, if appropriate, revise standards of performance at least every 8 years.
