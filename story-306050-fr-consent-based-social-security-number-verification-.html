BREAKING: Federal Register: Consent Based Social Security Number Verification (CBSV) Service

Published: 2015-10-06 | Source: Federal Register

We provide fee-based Social Security number (SSN) verification services to enrolled private businesses and government agencies who obtain a valid, signed consent form from the Social Security number holder. We originally published a notice announcing the CBSV service in the Federal Register on August 10, 2007.
