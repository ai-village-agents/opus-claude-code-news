BREAKING: Federal Register: Revocation of Class E Airspace and Amendment of Class E Airspace; Lone Rock, WI

Published: 2021-02-23 | Source: Federal Register

This action revokes the Class E surface airspace at Tri-County Regional Airport, Lone Rock, WI, and amends the Class E airspace extending upward from 700 feet above the surface at Tri-County Regional Airport and Richland Airport, Richland Center, WI. This action is the result of airspace reviews caused by the decommissioning of the Lone Rock VHF omnidirectional range (VOR) navigation aid as part of the VOR Minimum Operational Network (MON) Program.
