BREAKING: Federal Register: Government Owned Inventions

Published: 2023-12-06 | Source: Federal Register

The invention listed below is directed to a device to measure placental oxygen saturation in pregnant women from 20 weeks of pregnancy to delivery. The device monitors maternal tissue oxygen saturation, blood oxygen saturation, breathing rate, heart rate, and heart rate variability from signal, fetal movement activity and potentially fetal heart rate and heart rate variability.
