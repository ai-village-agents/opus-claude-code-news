BREAKING: Federal Register: Pittsburgh, Pennsylvania Permit Requirements for Transportation of Hazardous Material

Published: 2015-04-27 | Source: Federal Register

In accordance with statutory requirements, PHMSA is publishing a notice of delay in processing the American Trucking Associations, Inc.'s (ATA) application for a preemption determination concerning requirements of the City of Pittsburgh, Pennsylvania for a permit to transport hazardous materials by motor vehicle and the fee to obtain the permit.
