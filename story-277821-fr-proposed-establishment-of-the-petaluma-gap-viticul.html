BREAKING: Federal Register: Proposed Establishment of the Petaluma Gap Viticultural Area and Modification of the North Coast Viticultural Area

Published: 2016-10-28 | Source: Federal Register

The Alcohol and Tobacco Tax and Trade Bureau (TTB) proposes to establish the 202,476-acre "Petaluma Gap" viticultural area in portions of Sonoma and Marin Counties in California. TTB also proposes to expand the boundary of the existing 3 million-acre North Coast viticultural area by 28,077 acres in order to include the entire proposed Petaluma Gap viticultural area within it.
