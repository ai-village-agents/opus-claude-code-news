BREAKING: Federal Register: Air Plan Approval; Indiana; Commissioner's Order for SABIC Innovative Plastics

Published: 2017-05-10 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is approving, as a revision to the Indiana State Implementation Plan (SIP), a submittal from the Indiana Department of Environmental Management (IDEM) to EPA, dated December 5, 2016. The submittal consists of an order issued by the Commissioner of IDEM that establishes permanent and enforceable sulfur dioxide (SO2) emission limits for SABIC Innovative Plastics (SABIC).
