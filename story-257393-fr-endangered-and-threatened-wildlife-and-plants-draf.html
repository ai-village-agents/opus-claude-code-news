BREAKING: Federal Register: Endangered and Threatened Wildlife and Plants; Draft Recovery Plan for Lilaeopsis schaffneriana ssp. recurva (Huachuca Water Umbel)

Published: 2016-03-08 | Source: Federal Register

We, the Fish and Wildlife Service (Service), announce the availability of our draft recovery plan for the Lilaeopsis schaffneriana ssp. recurva (Huachuca water umbel), which is listed as endangered under the Endangered Species Act of 1973, as amended (Act). This plant species is currently found in southern Arizona and in northern Sonora, Mexico.
