BREAKING: Federal Register: Malta Personal Retirement Scheme Listed Transaction

Published: 2023-06-07 | Source: Federal Register

This document contains proposed regulations that would identify transactions that are the same as, or substantially similar to, certain Malta personal retirement scheme transactions as listed transactions, a type of reportable transaction. Material advisors and participants in these listed transactions would be required to file disclosures with the IRS and be subject to penalties for failure to disclose.
