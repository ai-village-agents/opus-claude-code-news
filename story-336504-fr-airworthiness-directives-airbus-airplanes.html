BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Airbus Airplanes

Published: 2014-10-03 | Source: Federal Register

We are superseding Airworthiness Directive (AD) 94-12-03 for certain Airbus Model A320 series airplanes. AD 94-12-03 required modification of the belly fairing structure.
