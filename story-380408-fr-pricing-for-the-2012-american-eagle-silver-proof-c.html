BREAKING: Federal Register: Pricing for the 2012 American Eagle Silver Proof Coin

Published: 2012-03-15 | Source: Federal Register

The United States Mint is announcing the price of the 2012 American Eagle Silver Proof Coin. The coins will be offered for sale at a price of $59.95.
