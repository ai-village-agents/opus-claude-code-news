BREAKING: Federal Register: Schedule of Fees for Consular Services-Elimination of the “Return Check Processing Fee”

Published: 2022-03-24 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of State (the Department) is adjusting the Schedule of Fees for Consular Services (Schedule) by removing Item Number 74, a $25 return check processing fee. Domestically, the Bureau of Consular Affairs, Office of Passport Services (CA/PPT), has charged customers this fee when the instruments they have used to submit payment for a passport application could not be processed due to insufficient funds, closed accounts, stop payments, and altered/ fictious checks or money orders.
