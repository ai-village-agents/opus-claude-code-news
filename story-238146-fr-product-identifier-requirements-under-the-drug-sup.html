BREAKING: Federal Register: Product Identifier Requirements Under the Drug Supply Chain Security Act-Compliance Policy; Draft Guidance for Industry; Availability

Published: 2017-07-03 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA or Agency) is announcing the availability of a draft guidance for industry entitled "Product Identifier Requirements Under the Drug Supply Chain Security Act-- Compliance Policy." This draft guidance describes FDA's intention with regard to enforcement of requirements related to product identifiers under the Drug Supply Chain Security Act (DSCSA).
