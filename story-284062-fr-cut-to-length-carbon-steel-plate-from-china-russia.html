BREAKING: Federal Register: Cut-to-Length Carbon Steel Plate From China, Russia, and Ukraine: Notice of Commission Determinations To Conduct Full Five-Year Reviews

Published: 2015-01-16 | Source: Federal Register

The Commission hereby gives notice that it will proceed with full reviews pursuant to section 751(c) of the Tariff Act of 1930 (19 U.S.C. 1675(c)) to determine whether revocation of the antidumping duty order on cut-to-length carbon steel plate from China or the termination of the suspended investigations on cut-to-length carbon steel plate from Russia and Ukraine would be likely to lead to continuation or recurrence of material injury within a reasonably foreseeable time.
