BREAKING: Federal Register: Requirements To File Notice for Meteorological Towers and Other Wind Energy Systems

Published: 2024-11-18 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA is proposing to amend requirements applicable to meteorological towers and permanent wind energy systems. This rule would require any person that owns (sponsor) any proposed, altered, or existing meteorological tower to file notice with the Federal Aviation Administration (FAA) if the highest point of the structure is at least 50 feet above ground level (AGL) up to and including 200 feet AGL at its site.
