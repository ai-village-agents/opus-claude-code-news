BREAKING: Federal Register: Discontinuation of the Qualitative (30 mL) Campylobacter Analysis for Young Chickens

Published: 2014-02-21 | Source: Federal Register

The Food Safety and Inspection Service (FSIS) is discontinuing the use of its 30-mL qualitative analysis for Campylobacter for young chickens. The Agency suspended this analysis on June 3, 2013. FSIS evaluated the available Campylobacter data, and its analysis suggested that the performance standard based on an analysis of the 1-mL sample volume is sufficiently sensitive to identify establishments whose process control is substandard.
