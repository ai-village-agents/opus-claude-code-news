BREAKING: Federal Register: Possible Modifications to the International Harmonized System Nomenclature

Published: 2019-04-03 | Source: Federal Register

The Commission is requesting proposals from interested persons and agencies to amend the International Harmonized Commodity Description and Coding System (Harmonized System or HS) in connection with the Seventh Review Cycle of the World Customs Organization (WCO), with a view to keeping the Harmonized System current with changes in technology and trade patterns.
