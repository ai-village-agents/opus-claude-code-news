BREAKING: Federal Register: Statewide Longitudinal Data Systems; Reopening Fiscal Year (FY) 2012 Competition

Published: 2012-01-06 | Source: Federal Register

On September 20, 2011, we published in the Federal Register (76 FR 58252) a notice inviting applications for the Statewide Longitudinal Data Systems FY 2012 competition. That notice established a December 15, 2012, deadline date for eligible applicants to apply for funding under this program. As a result of the notice published on September 20, 2011, 31 eligible entities submitted applications.
