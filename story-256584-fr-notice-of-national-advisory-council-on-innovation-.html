BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of National Advisory Council on Innovation and Entrepreneurship Meeting

Published: 2016-02-29 | Source: Federal Register

The National Advisory Council on Innovation and Entrepreneurship (NACIE) will hold a public meeting on Thursday, March 3, 2016, 2:00--2:45 p.m. Central Standard Time (CST) and Friday, March 4, 2016, 8:45 a.m.--12:00 p.m. CST. During this time, members will continue to work on various Council initiatives which include: Innovation, entrepreneurship and workforce talent.
