BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone; Wine and Walleye Festival Fireworks; Ashtabula River, Ashtabula, OH

Published: 2018-06-26 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is establishing a temporary safety zone for navigable waters within a 280-foot radius of the launch site at the R.W. Sidley Facility, Ashtabula, OH. This safety zone is intended to restrict vessels from portions of the Ashtabula River during the Wine and Walleye Festival fireworks display.
