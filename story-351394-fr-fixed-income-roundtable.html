BREAKING: Federal Register: Fixed Income Roundtable

Published: 2013-04-05 | Source: Federal Register

The Securities and Exchange Commission will host a one day roundtable to discuss the current market structure and potential ways to improve the transparency, liquidity, efficiency, and other aspects of fixed income markets. The roundtable will focus on the municipal securities, corporate bonds, and asset-backed securities markets.
