BREAKING: Federal Register: National Emission Standards for Hazardous Air Pollutant Emissions: Petroleum Refinery Sector Amendments

Published: 2016-02-09 | Source: Federal Register

This action proposes amendments to the National Emission Standards for Hazardous Air Pollutants (NESHAP) Refinery MACT 1 and Refinery MACT 2 regulations and the New Source Performance Standards (NSPS) for petroleum refineries, which were published on December 1, 2015. In that action, as a result of a risk and technology review, the Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) finalized amendments to Refinery MACT 1 and Refinery MACT 2.
