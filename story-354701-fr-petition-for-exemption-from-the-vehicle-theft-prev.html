BREAKING: Federal Register: Petition for Exemption From the Vehicle Theft Prevention Standard; Nissan

Published: 2013-05-13 | Source: Federal Register

This document grants in full Nissan North America, Inc.'s (Nissan) petition for exemption of the Infiniti QX60 (formerly known as the Infiniti JX) vehicle line in accordance with 49 CFR part 543, Exemption from Vehicle Theft Prevention Standard.
