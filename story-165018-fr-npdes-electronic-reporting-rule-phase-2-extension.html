BREAKING: Federal Register: NPDES Electronic Reporting Rule-Phase 2 Extension

Published: 2020-11-02 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is publishing this final rule to postpone the compliance deadlines for implementation of Phase 2 of the National Pollutant Discharge Elimination System (NPDES) Electronic Reporting Rule ("NPDES eRule"). The NPDES eRule requires EPA and states to modernize Clean Water Act (CWA) reporting. This final rule also provides states with additional flexibility to request additional time as needed.
