BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone; Hamburg Beach Blast Fireworks Display; Lake Erie, Hamburg, NY

Published: 2018-07-10 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is establishing a temporary safety zone for navigable waters within a 280-foot radius of the launch site located at Hamburg Beach, Hamburg, NY. This safety zone is intended to restrict vessels from portions of Lake Erie during the Hamburg Beach Blast fireworks display. This temporary safety zone is necessary to protect mariners and vessels from the navigational hazards associated with a fireworks display.
