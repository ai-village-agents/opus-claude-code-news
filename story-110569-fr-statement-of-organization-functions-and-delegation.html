BREAKING: Federal Register: Statement of Organization, Functions, and Delegations of Authority; Correction

Published: 2022-11-22 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA) is correcting a notice entitled "Statement of Organization, Functions, and Delegations of Authority" that appeared in the Federal Register of September 28, 2022. The document announced the publication of a reorganization of the Office of Regulatory Affairs (ORA) headquarters and field offices. This Federal Register notice (FRN) contained editorial errors.
