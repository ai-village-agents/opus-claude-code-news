BREAKING: Federal Register: Special Conditions: Delta Flight Products, Non-Rechargeable Lithium Batteries and Battery System Installations

Published: 2025-06-04 | Source: Federal Register

These special conditions are issued for a supplemental type certificate (STC) to install non-rechargeable lithium batteries and battery systems on certain transport-category airplanes. These airplanes, as modified by Delta Flight Products, will have a novel or unusual design feature when compared to the state of technology envisioned in the airworthiness standards for transport-category airplanes.
