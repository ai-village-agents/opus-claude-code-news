BREAKING: Federal Register: Hours of Service of Drivers: Application for Exemption; Truck Renting and Leasing Association, Inc. (TRALA)

Published: 2017-03-22 | Source: Federal Register

FMCSA announces that the Truck Renting and Leasing Association, Inc. (TRALA) has requested an exemption from the requirement that a motor carrier install and require each of its drivers to use an electronic logging device (ELD) to record the driver's hours-of-service (HOS) no later than December 18, 2017.
