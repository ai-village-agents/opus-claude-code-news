BREAKING: Federal Register: Proposed Template Safe Harbor Agreement for the Solano County Water Agency in Yolo and Solano Counties, California

Published: 2013-10-23 | Source: Federal Register

This notice advises the public that the Solano County Water Agency and the U.S. Fish and Wildlife Service (Service) have developed a Template Safe Harbor Agreement (Agreement) for the federally threatened valley elderberry longhorn beetle (Desmocerus californicus dimorphus).
