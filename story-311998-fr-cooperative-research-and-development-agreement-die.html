BREAKING: Federal Register: Cooperative Research and Development Agreement: Diesel Outboard Engine Development

Published: 2015-12-18 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard announces its intent to enter into a Cooperative Research and Development Agreement (CRADA) with Mercury Marine (Mercury) to evaluate and test the advantages, disadvantages, required technology enhancements, performance, costs, and other issues associated with diesel outboard engine technology.
