BREAKING: Federal Register: Schedules of Controlled Substances: Temporary Placement of Ethyleneoxynitazene, Methylenedioxynitazene, 5-Methyl Etodesnitazene, N-Desethyl Etonitazene, N-Desethyl Protonitazene, N,N-Dimethylamino Etonitazene, and N-Pyrrolidino Isotonitazene in Schedule I

Published: 2025-10-15 | Source: Federal Register

The Drug Enforcement Administration issues this temporary order to schedule seven benzimidazole-opioids, as identified in this order, in schedule I of the Controlled Substances Act. DEA bases this action on a finding that placing these substances in schedule I is necessary to avoid imminent hazard to public safety.
