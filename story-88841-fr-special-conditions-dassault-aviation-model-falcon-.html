BREAKING: Federal Register: Special Conditions: Dassault Aviation Model Falcon 6X Airplane; Electronic Flight-Control System: Lateral-Directional and Longitudinal Stability and Low-Energy Awareness

Published: 2022-02-14 | Source: Federal Register

These special conditions are issued for the Dassault Aviation (Dassault) Model Falcon 6X airplane. This airplane will have a novel or unusual design feature when compared to the state of technology envisioned in the airworthiness standards for transport category airplanes. This design feature is an electronic flight-control system (EFCS) associated with lateral-directional and longitudinal stability, and low-energy awareness.
