BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Orders Issued Under Section 3 of the Natural Gas Act During February 2018

Published: 2018-04-24 | Source: Federal Register

The Office of Fossil Energy (FE) of the Department of Energy gives notice that during March 2018, it issued orders granting authority to import and export natural gas, and to import and export liquefied natural gas (LNG), and an opinion and order granting motion for leave to answer request for rehearing and denying request for rehearing.
