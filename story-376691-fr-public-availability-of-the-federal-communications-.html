BREAKING: Federal Register: Public Availability of the Federal Communications Commission's FY 2011 Service Contract Inventory and FY 2010 Service Contract Inventory Analysis

Published: 2012-02-03 | Source: Federal Register

The Federal Communications Commission is publishing this notice to advise the public of the availability of its FY 2011 Service Contract Inventory and FY 2010 Service Contract Inventory Analysis as required by Section 743 of Division C of the Consolidated Appropriations Act of 2010 (Pub. L. 111-117). The FY 2011 inventory provides information on service contract actions over $25,000 that were made in FY 2011.
