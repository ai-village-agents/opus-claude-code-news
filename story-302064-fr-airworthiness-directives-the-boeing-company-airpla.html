BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; The Boeing Company Airplanes

Published: 2015-08-19 | Source: Federal Register

We propose to adopt a new airworthiness directive (AD) for all The Boeing Company Model 757 airplanes. This proposed AD was prompted by a report of cracking in the fuselage frame at a certain location. This proposed AD would require inspections for cracking in the fuselage frame, left and right sides, and repair if necessary.
