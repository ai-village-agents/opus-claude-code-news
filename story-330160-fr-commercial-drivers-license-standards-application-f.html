BREAKING: Federal Register: Commercial Driver's License Standards: Application for Exemption; Daimler Trucks North America (Daimler)

Published: 2014-07-22 | Source: Federal Register

FMCSA announces its decision to grant Daimler Trucks North America's (Daimler) application for an exemption for one Daimler driver to drive commercial motor vehicles (CMV) in the United States without possessing a commercial driver's license (CDL) issued by one of the States. Sven Ennerst is an engineering executive who will test-drive Daimler vehicles on U.S. roads to better understand product requirements for these vehicles in "real world" environments and verify results.
