BREAKING: Federal Register: Temporary Flight Restrictions in the Proximity of Launch and Reentry Operations

Published: 2015-09-02 | Source: Federal Register

This proposed rulemaking would expand the temporary flight restriction provisions for launch, reentry, and amateur rocket operations and make such temporary flight restrictions applicable to all aircraft--including non-U.S. registered aircraft. The FAA also proposes revised language for consistency with other temporary flight restriction provisions and commercial space regulations and definitions.
