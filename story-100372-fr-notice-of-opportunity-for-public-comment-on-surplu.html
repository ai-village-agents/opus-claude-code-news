BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Opportunity for Public Comment on Surplus Property Land Swap and Release at the Cyril E. King Airport, St. Thomas, United States Virgin Islands

Published: 2022-07-13 | Source: Federal Register

Notice is being given that the FAA is considering a request from the Virgin Islands Port Authority to release and exchange 0.822 acres of airport property with the University of the Virgin Islands. The current Port Authority parcel is isolated from the rest of airport property and currently houses a shopping center. The property will be exchanged for a parcel adjacent to the general aviation area and allow aeronautical development.
