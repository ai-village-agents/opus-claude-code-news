BREAKING: Federal Register: Changes To Implement the Inventor's Oath or Declaration Provisions of the Leahy-Smith America Invents Act

Published: 2012-08-14 | Source: Federal Register

The United States Patent and Trademark Office (Office) is revising the rules of practice to implement the inventor's oath or declaration provisions of the Leahy-Smith America Invents Act (AIA). The AIA permits a person to whom the inventor has assigned, or is under an obligation to assign, the invention, or who otherwise shows sufficient proprietary interest in the matter, to make the application for patent.
