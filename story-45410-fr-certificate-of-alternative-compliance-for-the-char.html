BREAKING: Federal Register: Certificate of Alternative Compliance for the CHARYBDIS

Published: 2024-08-28 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard announces that the Chief of the Prevention Division, Fifth Coast Guard District, has issued a certificate of alternative compliance from the International Regulations for Preventing Collisions at Sea, 1972 (72 COLREGS), for the CHARYBDIS, O.N. 1346442. We are issuing this notice because its publication is required by statute.
