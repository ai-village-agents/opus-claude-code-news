BREAKING: Federal Register: Privacy Act of 1974; System of Records

Published: 2014-10-15 | Source: Federal Register

The Office of the Secretary of Defense proposes to add a new system of records, DPR 45 DoD, entitled "Military OneSource (MOS) Case Management System (CMS)" to its inventory of record systems subject to the Privacy Act of 1974, as amended. The MOS CMS allows the documentation of an individual's eligibility; identification of the caller's inquiry or issue to provide a warm hand-off, referral and/or requested information; the development towards a final solution and referral information.
