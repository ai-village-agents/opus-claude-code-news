BREAKING: Federal Register: Conservation Stewardship Program (CSP)

Published: 2020-10-09 | Source: Federal Register

This final rule adopts, with minor changes, an interim rule published in the Federal Register on November 12, 2019. The interim rule implemented changes to CSP that were necessitated by enactment of the Agriculture Improvement Act of 2018 (2018 Farm Bill) or that were required to implement administrative improvements and clarifications.
