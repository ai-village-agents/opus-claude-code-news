BREAKING: Federal Register: Northern Mariana Islands Disaster #MP-00006

Published: 2015-08-13 | Source: Federal Register

This is a Notice of the Presidential declaration of a major disaster for Public Assistance Only for the State of Northern Mariana Islands (FEMA-4235-DR), dated 08/05/2015. Incident: Typhoon Soudelor. Incident Period: 08/01/2015 through 08/03/2015. Effective Date: 08/05/2015. Physical Loan Application Deadline Date: 10/05/2015. Economic Injury (EIDL) Loan Application Deadline Date: 05/05/2016.
