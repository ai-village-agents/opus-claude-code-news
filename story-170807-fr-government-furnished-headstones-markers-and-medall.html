BREAKING: Federal Register: Government-Furnished Headstones, Markers, and Medallions; Unmarked Graves

Published: 2019-02-06 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Veterans Affairs (VA) proposes to amend its regulations related to the provision of government-furnished headstones, markers, and medallions.
