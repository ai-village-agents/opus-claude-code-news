BREAKING: Federal Register: Determination on the Exclusion of Bifacial Solar Panels From the Safeguard Measure on Solar Products

Published: 2020-04-17 | Source: Federal Register

On February 14, 2018, the U.S. Trade Representative established procedures for interested persons to request product- specific exclusions from application of the safeguard measure and to comment on the submitted requests.
