BREAKING: Federal Register: Persulfates From the People's Republic of China: Final Results of Expedited Third Sunset Review of Antidumping Duty Order

Published: 2013-07-08 | Source: Federal Register

On March 1, 2013, the Department of Commerce ("the Department") initiated a sunset review of the antidumping duty order on persulfates from the People's Republic of China ("PRC"). On the basis of a notice of intent to participate, and an adequate substantive response filed on behalf of domestic interested parties, as well as a lack of response from any respondent interested parties, the Department conducted an expedited (120-day) sunset review.
