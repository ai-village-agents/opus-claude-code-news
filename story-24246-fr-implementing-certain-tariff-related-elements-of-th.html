BREAKING: Federal Register: Implementing Certain Tariff-Related Elements of the U.S.-Korea Strategic Trade and Investment Deal

Published: 2025-12-04 | Source: Federal Register

The Joint Fact Sheet sets out steps the United States and the ROK will take to further the U.S.-ROK Alliance under the U.S.-ROK Deal.
