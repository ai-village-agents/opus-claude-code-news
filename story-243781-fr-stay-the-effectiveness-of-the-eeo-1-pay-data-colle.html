BREAKING: Federal Register: Stay the Effectiveness of the EEO-1 Pay Data Collection

Published: 2017-09-15 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Equal Employment Opportunity Commission (EEOC) announces that, until further notice, filers subject to the EEO-1 reporting requirement should not submit aggregate data about W-2 (Box 1) income and hours worked, which is the information required by "Component 2" of the EEO-1 report as approved on September 29, 2016.
