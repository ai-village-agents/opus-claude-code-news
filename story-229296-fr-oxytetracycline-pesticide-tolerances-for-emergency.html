BREAKING: Federal Register: Oxytetracycline; Pesticide Tolerances for Emergency Exemptions

Published: 2017-03-10 | Source: Federal Register

This regulation establishes a time-limited tolerance for residues of oxytetracycline in or on fruit, citrus, group 10-10. This action is in response to EPA's granting of an emergency exemption under the Federal Insecticide, Fungicide, and Rodenticide Act (FIFRA) authorizing use of the pesticide in citrus production.
