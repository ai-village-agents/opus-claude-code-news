BREAKING: Federal Register: Pennsylvania Regulatory Program

Published: 2024-08-08 | Source: Federal Register

We, the Office of Surface Mining Reclamation and Enforcement (OSMRE), are approving an amendment to the Pennsylvania regulatory program under the Surface Mining Control and Reclamation Act of 1977 (SMCRA or the Act). The amendment authorizes and implements a land reclamation financial guarantee program as a new alternative bonding option for operators that meet certain requirements.
