BREAKING: Federal Register: Endangered and Threatened Species; Recovery Plans

Published: 2012-02-10 | Source: Federal Register

On January 5, 2012, NMFS announced the release of the Draft Southern Oregon/Northern California Coast Recovery Plan (Draft Plan) for public review and comment. The Draft Plan addresses the Southern Oregon/Northern California Coast Coho Salmon (Oncorhynchus kisutch) Evolutionarily Significant Unit (ESU).
