BREAKING: Federal Register: Chartering and Field of Membership Manual

Published: 2015-12-10 | Source: Federal Register

The NCUA Board proposes to comprehensively amend its chartering and field of membership rules to put them in a more efficient framework and to maximize access to federal credit union services to the extent permitted by law.
