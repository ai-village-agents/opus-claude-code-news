BREAKING: Federal Register: Implementation of Amended Section 203(a)(1)(B) of the Federal Power Act

Published: 2018-11-29 | Source: Federal Register

Pursuant to "An Act to amend section 203 of the Federal Power Act" (Act), the Federal Energy Regulatory Commission (Commission) proposes to revise its regulations relating to mergers or consolidations by a public utility.
