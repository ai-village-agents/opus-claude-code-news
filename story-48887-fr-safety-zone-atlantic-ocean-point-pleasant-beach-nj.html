BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone; Atlantic Ocean, Point Pleasant Beach, NJ

Published: 2024-10-03 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is establishing a temporary safety zone around salvage operations for a sunken vessel. The safety zone encompasses navigable waters on a portion of the Atlantic Ocean in the vicinity of Point Pleasant Beach, NJ within a 500-yard radius of the salvage operations. The safety zone is needed to protect personnel, vessels, and the marine environment from potential hazards created by the salvage operations of the sunken vessel.
