BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone; Fleet Week Maritime Festival, Pier 66, Elliot Bay, Seattle, Washington

Published: 2019-06-21 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard does not plan to enforce the Fleet Week Maritime Festival's Pier 66 Safety Zone in Elliott Bay, WA in 2019. The Captain of the Port has determined that enforcement of this regulation is not presently needed to ensure participant and spectator safety for this year's Fleet Week Maritime Festival.
