BREAKING: Federal Register: Agency Information Collection Activities; Approval of a New Information Collection Request: FMCSA Registration System (FRS)

Published: 2024-08-28 | Source: Federal Register

In accordance with the Paperwork Reduction Act of 1995, FMCSA announces its plan to submit the Information Collection Request (ICR) described below to the Office of Management and Budget (OMB) for review and approval. FMCSA is replacing its Unified Registration System (URS), with a new, online registration system, which will be named the "FMCSA Registration System" (FRS).
