BREAKING: Fears of New Arms Race as US-Russia Nuclear Treaty Due to Expire

Published: February 4, 2026 | Source: BBC

International concerns are mounting over the potential for a new nuclear arms race as a key US-Russia weapons treaty approaches its expiration date without renewal talks.

Treaty Background

The New START treaty, which limits both nations' deployed strategic nuclear warheads, is set to expire amid deteriorating relations between Washington and Moscow.

Global Security Implications

Without a replacement agreement, both nations could resume unrestricted nuclear weapons production, potentially triggering a new arms race with global security implications.
