BREAKING: Federal Register: North Dakota: Final Authorization of State Hazardous Waste Management Program Revisions

Published: 2018-12-19 | Source: Federal Register

North Dakota has applied for final authorization of a revision to its hazardous waste program under the Resource Conservation and Recovery Act (RCRA). The revision has two parts, both of which relate directly to the creation of the North Dakota Department of Environmental Quality (NDDEQ) by the North Dakota Legislature, and the impending transfer of authority, power, and duties relating to environmental quality from the North Dakota Department of Health (NDDH) to the NDDEQ.
