BREAKING: Federal Register: Notification of Citizens Coinage Advisory Committee, Public Meeting

Published: 2019-06-17 | Source: Federal Register

The United States Mint announces the Citizens Coinage Advisory Committee (CCAC) public meeting scheduled for June 18, 2019. Date: June 18, 2019. Time: 9:30 a.m. to 12:30 p.m. Location: 2nd Floor Conference Room A&B, United States Mint, 801 9th Street NW, Washington, DC 20220. Subject: Review and discussion of candidate designs for the Anwar El Sadat Congressional Gold Medal, the Steve Gleason Congressional Gold Medal, and the reverse of the 2020 Basketball Hall of Fame Commemorative Coin Program...
