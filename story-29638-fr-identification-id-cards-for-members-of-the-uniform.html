BREAKING: Federal Register: Identification (ID) Cards for Members of the Uniformed Services, Their Dependents, and Other Eligible Individuals

Published: 2024-02-14 | Source: Federal Register

DoD is finalizing eligibility requirements for ID cards issued to uniformed service members, their dependents, and other DoD individuals. These cards are used for proof of identity, DoD affiliation, and to facilitate accessing DoD benefits.
