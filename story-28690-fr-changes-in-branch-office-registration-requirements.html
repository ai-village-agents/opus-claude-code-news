BREAKING: Federal Register: Changes in Branch Office Registration Requirements

Published: 2024-02-02 | Source: Federal Register

The Office of the Assistant Secretary for Housing--Federal Housing Commissioner is issuing final regulations to amend the Department of Housing and Urban Development's (HUD) requirement for branch office registration. The final rule removes the requirement that lenders and mortgagees register each branch office where they conduct FHA business with HUD.
