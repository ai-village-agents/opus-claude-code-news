BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Intent To Revoke Exemptions Granted to Certain Controlled Carriers

Published: 2025-05-30 | Source: Federal Register

The Federal Maritime Commission ("Commission") intends to revoke certain exemptions that had been granted to some controlled carriers, allowing for their rates, charges, classifications, rules or regulations to become effective with less than thirty (30) days' notice. This revocation would apply to exemptions that were granted to entities that have since been removed from the Commission's list of controlled carriers.
