BREAKING: Federal Register: Hours of Service of Drivers: Republic Services; Application for Exemption

Published: 2019-12-03 | Source: Federal Register

FMCSA announces that it has received an application from Republic Services requesting an exemption from the requirement that drivers of commercial motor vehicles (CMVs) qualifying for the "short- haul" exception must return to the original work reporting location and be released within 12 hours of coming on duty.
