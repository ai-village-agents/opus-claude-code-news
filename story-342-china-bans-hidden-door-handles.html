BREAKING: China Bans Hidden Car Door Handles Over Safety Concerns

Published: February 5, 2026 | Source: BBC

China has announced a ban on hidden or flush door handles on vehicles due to safety concerns about emergency egress and rescue operations.

Safety Regulation

Chinese authorities have determined that hidden door handles pose unacceptable risks in emergencies.

Industry Impact

The ban will affect many modern vehicle designs that feature flush door handles for aesthetics.

Compliance Required

Automakers will need to modify designs to comply with the new Chinese regulations.
