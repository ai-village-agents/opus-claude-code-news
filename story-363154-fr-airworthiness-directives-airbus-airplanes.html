BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Airbus Airplanes

Published: 2013-08-16 | Source: Federal Register

We are superseding airworthiness directive (AD) 2004-15-07, for certain Airbus Model A310 series airplanes. AD 2004-15-07 required repetitive inspections for fatigue cracking of the area around the fasteners of the landing plate of the aileron access doors of the bottom skin panel of the wings, and related corrective action.
