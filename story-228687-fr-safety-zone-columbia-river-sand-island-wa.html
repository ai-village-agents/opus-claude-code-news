BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone; Columbia River, Sand Island, WA

Published: 2017-03-03 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is establishing a temporary safety zone on the Columbia River within a 500-yard radius of the small boat "Nessy" while in the area of Sand Island, near Chinook, WA, during Double- Crested Cormorant removal operations being conducted by the U.S. Army Corps of Engineers and U.S. Department of Agriculture Wildlife Services.
