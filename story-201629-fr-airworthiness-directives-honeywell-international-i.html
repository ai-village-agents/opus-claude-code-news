BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Honeywell International Inc. Turbofan Engines

Published: 2018-03-08 | Source: Federal Register

We are adopting a new airworthiness directive (AD) for certain Honeywell International Inc. AS907 series turbofan engines. This AD was prompted by seven loss-of-thrust-control events attributed to water intrusion of the engine electronic control unit (ECU). This AD requires applying sealant to identified areas of the ECU and requires inserting a copy of certain airplane operating procedures into the applicable flight manuals.
