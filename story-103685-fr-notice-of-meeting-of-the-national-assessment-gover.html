BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Meeting of the National Assessment Governing Board's Executive Committee

Published: 2022-08-24 | Source: Federal Register

This notice sets forth the agenda and instructions to submit written comment for an August 29, 2022 closed teleconference meeting of the National Assessment Governing Board's (Governing Board) Executive Committee. This notice provides information to members of the public who may be interested in providing written comments related to the work of the Governing Board.
