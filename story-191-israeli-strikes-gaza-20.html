BREAKING: Israeli Strikes Kill 20 in Gaza After Soldier Wounded by Gunfire

Published: February 4, 2026 | Source: BBC

Israeli air strikes have killed 20 people in Gaza, according to hospital officials, following an incident in which an Israeli soldier was wounded by gunfire.

Deadly Strikes

The attacks targeted multiple locations in Gaza, with hospitals reporting at least 20 fatalities.

Soldier Wounded

The strikes followed an incident where an Israeli soldier was shot and wounded inside the territory.

Ceasefire Tensions

The violence raises concerns about the fragility of the ceasefire agreement between Israel and Hamas.
