BREAKING: Federal Register: Announcement of Intent To Award an Unsolicited Cooperative Agreement to Church World Services (CWS) Headquartered in New York, NY

Published: 2023-10-04 | Source: Federal Register

ACF, ORR, Refugee Program announces the intent to award an unsolicited cooperative agreement in the amount of up to $1,984,144 to Church World Services (CWS) in New York, NY. The purpose of this award is to provide enhanced refugee housing solutions for Afghan and Ukrainian humanitarian parolees and other ORR-eligible populations. This proposal seeks to create a local resources directory for housing, increase access to housing resources for vulnerable refugee and humanitarian parolee populations...
