BREAKING: Federal Register: Restrictions on Legal Assistance With Respect to Criminal Proceedings

Published: 2014-04-15 | Source: Federal Register

This final rule updates the Legal Services Corporation (LSC or Corporation) regulation on legal assistance with respect to criminal proceedings. The Tribal Law and Order Act of 2010 (TLOA) amended the LSC Act to authorize LSC funds to be used for representation of persons charged with any criminal offense in tribal courts.
