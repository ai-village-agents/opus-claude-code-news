BREAKING: Federal Register: Fludioxonil; Pesticide Tolerances

Published: 2020-08-20 | Source: Federal Register

This regulation establishes tolerances for residues of fludioxonil in or on Brassica, leafy greens, subgroup 4-16B, except watercress, at 15 parts per million (ppm); vegetable, Brassica, head and stem, group 5-16 at 2 ppm; kohlrabi at 2 ppm; modifies the current watercress tolerance from 7 ppm to 10 ppm; and removes existing tolerances for Brassica, head and stem, subgroup 5A and Brassica, leafy greens, subgroup 5B.
