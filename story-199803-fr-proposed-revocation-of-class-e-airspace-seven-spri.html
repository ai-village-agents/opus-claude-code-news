BREAKING: Federal Register: Proposed Revocation of Class E Airspace; Seven Springs, PA, and Somerset, PA

Published: 2018-02-12 | Source: Federal Register

This action proposes to remove Class E airspace at Seven Springs, PA, as Seven Springs Borough Airport has been abandoned, and controlled airspace is no longer required. This proposal would also remove reference to the Seven Springs, PA, Class E airspace area from the Somerset County Airport, Somerset, PA, description, and update the geographic coordinates of Somerset County Airport to coincide with the FAA's database.
