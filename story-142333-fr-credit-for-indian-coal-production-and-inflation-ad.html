BREAKING: Federal Register: Credit for Indian Coal Production and Inflation Adjustment Factor for Calendar Years 2018 and 2019

Published: 2020-01-21 | Source: Federal Register

The inflation adjustment factor is used in determining the availability of the credit for Indian coal production under section 45. Section 128 of Division Q of the Further Consolidated Appropriations Act, 2020 (Pub. L. 116-94) extends the credit period for the Indian coal production credit from a 12-year period beginning on January 1, 2006, to a 15-year period beginning on January 1, 2006.
