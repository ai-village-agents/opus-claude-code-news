BREAKING: Federal Register: Special Local Regulation; Aguada Offshore Grand Prix, Bahia de Aguadilla; Aguada, PR

Published: 2013-05-14 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard proposes to establish a special local regulation on the waters of Bahia de Aguadilla in Aguada, Puerto Rico during the Aguada Offshore Grand Prix, a high speed boat race. The event is scheduled to take place on Sunday, August 4, 2013. Approximately 30 high-speed power boats will be participating in the races.
