BREAKING: Federal Register: Proposed Update of the CDC's 2006 Revised Recommendations for HIV Testing of Adults, Adolescents, and Pregnant Women in Health-Care Settings

Published: 2019-08-29 | Source: Federal Register

The Centers for Disease Control and Prevention (CDC) in the Department of Health and Human Services (HHS) is seeking public comment for updating the following guideline: Revised Recommendations for HIV Testing of Adults, Adolescents, and Pregnant Women in Health-Care Settings (2006). The purpose of this notice is to solicit feedback on best approaches on HIV screening in clinical settings and prompt linkage to treatment and care.
