BREAKING: Federal Register: Processed Raspberry Promotion, Research, and Information Order; Late Payment and Interest Charges on Past Due Assessments

Published: 2015-08-06 | Source: Federal Register

This rule prescribes late payment and interest charges on past due assessments under the Processed Raspberry Promotion, Research, and Information Order (Order). The Order is administered by the National Processed Raspberry Council (Council) with oversight by the U.S. Department of Agriculture (USDA). Under the Order, assessments are collected from domestic producers and importers and used for research and promotion projects designed to maintain and expand the market for processed raspberries.
