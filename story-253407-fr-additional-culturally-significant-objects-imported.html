BREAKING: Federal Register: Additional Culturally Significant Objects Imported for Exhibition Determinations: “Bellissima: Italy and High Fashion 1945-1968” Exhibition

Published: 2016-01-21 | Source: Federal Register

On January 11, 2016, notice was published on page 1274 of the Federal Register (volume 81, number 6) of determinations made by the Department of State pertaining to certain objects imported for temporary display in the exhibition "Bellissima: Italy and High Fashion 1945-1968." The referenced notice is corrected here to include additional objects as part of the exhibition.
