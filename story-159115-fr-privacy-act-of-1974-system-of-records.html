BREAKING: Federal Register: Privacy Act of 1974; System of Records

Published: 2020-08-20 | Source: Federal Register

In accordance with the Privacy Act of 1974, the Office of Personnel Management (OPM) proposes to establish a new OPM system of records titled "Federal Competency Assessment Records." This system of records contains information that OPM collects, maintains, and uses in the operation of competency assessment tools, including the OPM Competency Exploration for Development and Readiness (CEDAR) tool.
