BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Intent To Grant an Exclusive License

Published: 2015-07-14 | Source: Federal Register

The Notice is hereby given that the U.S. Geological Survey intends to grant to Glosten Associates, 1201 Western Avenue, Suite 200, Seattle, WA 98101, an exclusive license to practice the following: A system, method, and apparatus for treating ship ballast water.
