BREAKING: Federal Register: Request for Nominations for the Science Advisory Board Environmental Justice Screen (EJScreen) Review Panel

Published: 2022-08-30 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) Science Advisory Board (SAB) Staff Office requests public nominations of scientific experts to form a Panel to review the updated EJScreen methodology. EJScreen is an environmental justice mapping and screening tool that provides EPA with a nationwide consistent approach for combining environmental and demographic indicators to identify areas with potential environmental justice (EJ) concerns.
