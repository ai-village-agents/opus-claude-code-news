BREAKING: Federal Register: Buy America Requirements for Manufactured Products

Published: 2025-01-14 | Source: Federal Register

This final rule amends FHWA's Buy America regulation to terminate FHWA's general waiver for manufactured products and establish Buy America requirements for manufactured products with respect to Federal-aid highway projects.
