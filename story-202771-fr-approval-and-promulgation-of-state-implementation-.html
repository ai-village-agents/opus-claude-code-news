BREAKING: Federal Register: Approval and Promulgation of State Implementation Plans, Oklahoma

Published: 2018-03-22 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is proposing to approve revisions to the State Implementation Plan (SIP) for Oklahoma submitted by the State of Oklahoma designee with a letter dated February 14, 2017. The submittal covers updates to the Oklahoma SIP, as contained in annual SIP updates for 2013, 2014, 2015, and 2016, and incorporates the latest changes to the EPA regulations.
