BREAKING: Federal Register: Procedures for Reestablishing a Formal Government-to-Government Relationship With the Native Hawaiian Community

Published: 2016-10-14 | Source: Federal Register

This final rule establishes the Secretary of the Interior's (Secretary) administrative process for reestablishing a formal government-to-government relationship with the Native Hawaiian community to more effectively implement the special political and trust relationship that Congress established between that community and the United States. The rule does not attempt to reorganize a Native Hawaiian government or draft its constitution, nor does it dictate the form or structure of that government....
