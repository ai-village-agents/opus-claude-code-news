BREAKING: Federal Register: Schools and Libraries Universal Service Support Mechanism

Published: 2022-02-14 | Source: Federal Register

In this document, the Federal Communications Commission (Commission) takes steps to address one of the barriers to participation and clarify the eligibility of Tribal libraries for E- Rate program support by updating the definition of "library" in its E-Rate program rules to include Tribal libraries.
