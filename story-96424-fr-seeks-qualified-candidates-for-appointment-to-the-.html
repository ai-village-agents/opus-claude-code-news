BREAKING: Federal Register: Seeks Qualified Candidates for Appointment to the Advisory Committee on Reactor Safeguards

Published: 2022-05-20 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Nuclear Regulatory Commission (NRC) seeks qualified candidates for appointment to the Advisory Committee on Reactor Safeguards (ACRS or Committee). Submit resumes to Ms. Jamila Perry and Ms. Sandra Walker, ACRS, Mail Stop: T2B50, U.S. Nuclear Regulatory Commission, Washington, DC 20555-0001, or email Jamila.Perry@nrc.gov and Sandra.Walker@nrc.gov. This announcement replaces the request for resumes that NRC issued in the Federal Register on September 8, 2021, in 86 FR 50380.
