BREAKING: Federal Register: Privacy Act of 1974; System of Records

Published: 2018-11-29 | Source: Federal Register

ODNI provides notice that it is establishing one new Privacy Act system of records at the National Counterintelligence and Security Center (NCSC). This new system of records is titled the NCSC Continuous Evaluation System, also identified as ODNI/NCSC-003. This notice is necessary to inform the public of the existence and character of records that the agency maintains.
