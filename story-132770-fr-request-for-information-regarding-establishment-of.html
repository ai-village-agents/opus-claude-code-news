BREAKING: Federal Register: Request for Information Regarding Establishment of the Department of Energy Uranium Reserve Program

Published: 2021-09-13 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Energy (DOE) published the Request for information (RFI) to invite public comment on topics related to the Establishment of the DOE's Uranium Reserve program on August 11, 2021. Through this notice, DOE announces the reopening of the Uranium Reserve RFI comment period for an additional 30 days.
