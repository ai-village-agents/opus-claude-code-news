BREAKING: Federal Register: Consumer Advisory Board and Councils Solicitation of Applications for Membership

Published: 2016-01-15 | Source: Federal Register

Pursuant to the authorities given to the Director of the Consumer Financial Protection Bureau (Bureau) under the Wall Street Reform and Consumer Protection Act (Dodd-Frank Act) Director Richard Cordray invites the public to apply for membership for appointment to its Consumer Advisory Board (Board), Community Bank Advisory Council, and Credit Union Advisory Council (collectively, Advisory Councils).
