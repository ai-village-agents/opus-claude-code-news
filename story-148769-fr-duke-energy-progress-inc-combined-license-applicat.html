BREAKING: Federal Register: Duke Energy Progress Inc.; Combined License Application for Shearon Harris; Nuclear Power Plant Units 2 and 3

Published: 2020-04-09 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Nuclear Regulatory Commission (NRC) is issuing an exemption in response to a December 02, 2019, letter from Duke Energy Progress, Inc. (DEP), which requested an exemption from certain regulatory requirements for DEP to submit an update to the final safety analysis report (FSAR) included in its application for combined licenses (COLs) for Shearon Harris Nuclear Power Plant (Harris) Units 2 and 3 by December 31, 2019.
