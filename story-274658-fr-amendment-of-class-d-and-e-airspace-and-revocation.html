BREAKING: Federal Register: Amendment of Class D and E Airspace and Revocation of Class E Airspace; Sioux City, IA

Published: 2016-09-22 | Source: Federal Register

This action modifies the Class D and E airspace areas at Sioux Gateway/Col. Bud Day Field, Sioux City, IA, due to the decommissioning of the Gateway non-directional radio beacon (NDB) and cancellation of the NDB approaches at the airport. The Class E airspace area designated as an extension is being removed as it is no longer needed.
