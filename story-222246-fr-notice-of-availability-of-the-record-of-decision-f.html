BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Availability of the Record of Decision for the Final Missouri River Recovery Management Plan and Environmental Impact Statement

Published: 2018-11-30 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Army Corps of Engineers (USACE) announces the availability of the Record of Decision (ROD) for the Final Missouri River Recovery Management Plan and Environmental Impact Statement (MRRMP-FEIS) published in the Federal Register on Friday, August 31, 2018. The USACE Northwestern Division Commander signed the ROD on November 20, 2018.
