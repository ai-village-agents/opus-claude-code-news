BREAKING: Federal Register: Improving Wireless Emergency Alerts and Community-Initiated Alerting

Published: 2015-12-14 | Source: Federal Register

This document proposes revisions to Wireless Emergency Alert (WEA) rules designed to improve the clarity of WEA messages, ensure that WEA alerts reach only those individuals to whom a WEA alert is relevant, and establish a WEA testing program that will improve the effectiveness of the system for public safety officials and the public.
