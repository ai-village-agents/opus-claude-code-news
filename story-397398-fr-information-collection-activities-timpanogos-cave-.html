BREAKING: Federal Register: Information Collection Activities: Timpanogos Cave National Monument Visitor and Community Survey

Published: 2012-09-26 | Source: Federal Register

We (National Park Service) will ask the Office of Management and Budget (OMB) to approve the Information Collection (IC) described below. This collection will consist of a survey and a focus group script that will be used to collect visitors and local community members' perceptions and evaluations of four management issues (1) Cave tour size and frequency; (2) ticketing procedure and fees; (3) concession service quality and selection; and (4) safety concerns on park trails.
