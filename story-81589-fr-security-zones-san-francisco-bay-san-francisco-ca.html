BREAKING: Federal Register: Security Zones; San Francisco Bay, San Francisco, CA

Published: 2023-11-09 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is establishing temporary security zones in the navigable waters of the San Francisco Bay, and the Pacific Ocean within the San Francisco Captain of the Port (COTP) zone. The security zones are along San Francisco Pier 15/17, approximately 1,000 yards from shore, and the Legion of Honor, approximately 450 yards from shore.
