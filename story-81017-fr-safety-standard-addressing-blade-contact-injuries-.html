BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Standard Addressing Blade-Contact Injuries on Table Saws

Published: 2023-11-01 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Consumer Product Safety Commission (Commission or CPSC) has determined preliminarily that there may be an unreasonable risk of blade-contact injuries associated with table saws.
