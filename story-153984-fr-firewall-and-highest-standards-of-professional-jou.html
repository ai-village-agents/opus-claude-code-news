BREAKING: Federal Register: Firewall and Highest Standards of Professional Journalism

Published: 2020-06-15 | Source: Federal Register

The Broadcasting Board of Governors is revising its regulations to clarify the practical meaning and impact of the statutory firewall contained within the United States International Broadcasting Act of 1994, as amended, upon Agency operations. Consistent with this action, this rule makes appropriate conforming changes.
