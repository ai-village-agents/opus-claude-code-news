BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; PZL Swidnik S.A. Helicopters

Published: 2021-04-26 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA is adopting a new airworthiness directive (AD) for PZL Swidnik S.A. (PZL) Model W-3A helicopters. This AD requires repetitive inspections of a certain part-numbered stainless steel cable (cable) installed on a certain part-numbered hoist assembly, and depending on those inspection results, removing certain parts from service and reporting the results.
