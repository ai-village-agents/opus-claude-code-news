BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Availability of an Evaluation of the Swine Vesicular Disease Status of Certain Regions in Italy

Published: 2012-12-18 | Source: Federal Register

We are advising the public that we have determined that the Italian Regions of Lombardia, Emilio-Romagna, Veneto, and Piemonte and the autonomous provinces of Trento and Bolzano are free of swine vesicular disease. This determination is based on our review of the documentation submitted by the Government of Italy in support of its request and the findings of our own animal health risk evaluation.
