BREAKING: Federal Register: M15 General Principles for Model-Informed Drug Development; International Council for Harmonisation; Draft Guidance for Industry; Availability

Published: 2024-12-30 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA or Agency) is announcing the availability of a draft guidance for industry entitled "M15 General Principles for Model-Informed Drug Development." The draft guidance was prepared under the auspices of the International Council for Harmonisation of Technical Requirements for Pharmaceuticals for Human Use (ICH).
