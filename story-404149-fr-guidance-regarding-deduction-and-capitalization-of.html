BREAKING: Federal Register: Guidance Regarding Deduction and Capitalization of Expenditures Related to Tangible Property

Published: 2012-12-19 | Source: Federal Register

This document contains correcting amendments to the temporary regulations (TD 9564), which were published in the Federal Register on Tuesday, December 27, 2011, relating to guidance regarding deduction and capitalization of expenditures related to tangible property. These amendments revise the general asset account regulations to provide the time and manner of making a general asset account election.
