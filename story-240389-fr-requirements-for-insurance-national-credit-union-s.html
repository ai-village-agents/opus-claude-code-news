BREAKING: Federal Register: Requirements for Insurance; National Credit Union Share Insurance Fund Equity Distributions

Published: 2017-08-01 | Source: Federal Register

The NCUA Board (Board) proposes to amend its share insurance requirements rule to provide federally insured credit unions (FICUs) with greater transparency regarding the calculation of a FICU's proportionate share of a declared equity distribution from the National Credit Union Share Insurance Fund (NCUSIF) and to add a temporary provision to govern NCUSIF equity distributions resulting from the Corporate System Resolution Program.
