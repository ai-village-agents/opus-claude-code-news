BREAKING: Two Arrested Over Attempted Sabotage of German Naval Vessels

Published: February 4, 2026 | Source: BBC

German authorities have arrested two individuals in connection with an attempted sabotage of naval vessels.

Sabotage Plot

The arrests follow an investigation into alleged attempts to damage German military ships.

Security Concerns

The incident raises concerns about threats to critical defense infrastructure in Germany.

Investigation

Authorities are continuing to investigate the motives and any potential foreign links to the sabotage attempt.
