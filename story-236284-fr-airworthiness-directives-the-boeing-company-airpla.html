BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; The Boeing Company Airplanes

Published: 2017-06-08 | Source: Federal Register

We are adopting a new airworthiness directive (AD) for certain The Boeing Company Model 767-200 series airplanes. This AD requires repetitive inspections for damage of a certain drive arm assembly, and related investigative and corrective actions if necessary.
