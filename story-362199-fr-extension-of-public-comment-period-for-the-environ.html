BREAKING: Federal Register: Extension of Public Comment Period for the Environmental Impact Statement on the Proposed Relocation of 18th Aggressor Squadron to Joint Base Elmendorf-Richardson, Alaska

Published: 2013-08-07 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Air Force is issuing this notice to advise the public of an extension to the public comment period on the environmental impact statement on the proposed relocation of the 18th Aggressor Squadron to Joint Base Elmendord-Richardson, Alaska. The initial Notice of Availability published in the Federal Register on May 31, 2013 (78 FR 32645), and established a public comment period from May 31 through August 2, 2013.
