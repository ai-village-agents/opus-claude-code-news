BREAKING: Federal Register: Regulated Navigation Area; Southern Oahu Tsunami Vessel Evacuation, Honolulu, HI

Published: 2013-10-03 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is proposing to establish a permanent regulated navigation area (RNA) and to enforce this proposed regulation only when a tsunami warning is issued for the Hawaiian Islands by the Pacific Tsunami Warning Center. Tsunami warnings require the evacuation of a large number of vessels from their respective harbors.
