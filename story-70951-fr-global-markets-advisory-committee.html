BREAKING: Federal Register: Global Markets Advisory Committee

Published: 2023-06-29 | Source: Federal Register

The Commodity Futures Trading Commission (CFTC) announces that on July 17, 2023, from 9 a.m. to 4 p.m. (Eastern Daylight Time), the Global Markets Advisory Committee (GMAC or Committee) will hold an in- person meeting for GMAC members at the New York Stock Exchange, 11 Wall Street, New York, New York, with options for the public to attend virtually.
