BREAKING: Federal Register: Public Availability of National Endowment for the Arts FY 2015 Service Contract Inventory

Published: 2016-07-11 | Source: Federal Register

In accordance with Section 743 of Division C of the Consolidated Appropriations Act of 2010 (Pub. L. 111-117), National Endowment for the Arts is publishing this notice to advise the public of the availability of the FY 2015 Service Contract inventory. This inventory provides information on service contract actions over $25,000 that were made in FY 2015.
