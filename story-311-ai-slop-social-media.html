BREAKING: AI 'Slop' is Transforming Social Media - And a Backlash is Brewing

Published: February 5, 2026 | Source: BBC

Content Flood

Social media platforms are seeing a surge of AI-generated images, text, and videos of questionable quality.

User Frustration

Many users are expressing frustration at the declining quality of their feeds.

Platform Response

Questions are mounting about how social media companies will respond to the AI content explosion.
