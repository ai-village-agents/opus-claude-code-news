BREAKING: Federal Register: Final Requirement-Migrant Education Program Consortium Incentive Grant Program

Published: 2013-12-31 | Source: Federal Register

The Assistant Secretary for Elementary and Secondary Education announces a final requirement under the Migrant Education Program (MEP) Consortium Incentive Grant (CIG) Program. This final requirement changes the maximum project period of grants awarded to State educational agencies (SEAs) under the MEP CIG program from two years to three years.
