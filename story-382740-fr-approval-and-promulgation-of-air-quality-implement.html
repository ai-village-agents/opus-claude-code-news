BREAKING: Federal Register: Approval and Promulgation of Air Quality Implementation Plans; Colorado; Revisions to New Source Review Rules

Published: 2012-04-10 | Source: Federal Register

EPA is approving those revisions adopted by the State of Colorado on April 16, 2004 to Regulation No. 3 (Stationary Source Permitting and Air Pollutant Emission Notice Requirements) that incorporate EPA's December 31, 2002 NSR Reforms. Colorado submitted the request for approval of these rule revisions into the State Implementation Plan (SIP) on July 11, 2005 and supplemented its request on October 25, 2005.
