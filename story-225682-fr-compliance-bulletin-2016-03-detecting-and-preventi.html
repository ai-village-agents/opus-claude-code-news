BREAKING: Federal Register: Compliance Bulletin 2016-03: Detecting and Preventing Consumer Harm From Production Incentives

Published: 2017-01-18 | Source: Federal Register

The Bureau recognizes that many supervised entities may choose to implement incentive programs to achieve business objectives. When properly implemented and monitored, reasonable incentives can benefit consumers and the financial marketplace as a whole. This bulletin compiles guidance that has previously been given by the CFPB in other contexts and highlights examples from the CFPB's supervisory and enforcement experience in which incentives contributed to substantial consumer harm.
