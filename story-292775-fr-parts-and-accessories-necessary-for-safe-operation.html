BREAKING: Federal Register: Parts and Accessories Necessary for Safe Operation; Application for an Exemption From the Entertainer Motorcoach Council

Published: 2015-05-01 | Source: Federal Register

FMCSA requests public comment on an application for exemption from the Entertainer Motorcoach Council (EMC) to allow its members to operate certain vehicles that do not meet the emergency exit requirements in the Federal Motor Carrier Safety Regulations (FMCSRs).
