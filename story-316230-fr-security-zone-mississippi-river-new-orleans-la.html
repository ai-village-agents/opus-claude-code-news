BREAKING: Federal Register: Security Zone; Mississippi River, New Orleans, LA

Published: 2014-02-10 | Source: Federal Register

The Captain of the Port of New Orleans (COTP New Orleans), under the authority of the Magnuson Act, established a Moving Security Zone on the Mississippi river from mile marker 90.0 to mile marker 106.0 above head of passes (AHP), extending 100 yards in all directions from vessels being escorted by one or more Coast Guard asset or other federal, state, or local law enforcement agency assets.
