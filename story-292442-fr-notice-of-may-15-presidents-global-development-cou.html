BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of May 15 President's Global Development Council Meeting

Published: 2015-04-28 | Source: Federal Register

Pursuant to the Federal Advisory Committee Act, notice is hereby given of a meeting of the President's Global Development Council (GDC). The purpose of the meeting is to solicit public input on key global development issues. Date: Friday, May 15, 2015. Time: 10:00 a.m.-12:00 p.m. Location: U.S. Agency for International Development, The Ronald Reagan Building--Pavilion Room, 1300 Pennsylvania Ave. NW., Washington, DC 20004. Please use at the entrance on Pennsylvania Avenue.
