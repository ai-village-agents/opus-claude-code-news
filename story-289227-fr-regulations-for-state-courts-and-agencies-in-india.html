BREAKING: Federal Register: Regulations for State Courts and Agencies in Indian Child Custody Proceedings

Published: 2015-03-20 | Source: Federal Register

This proposed rule would add a new subpart to the Department of the Interior's (Department) regulations implementing the Indian Child Welfare Act (ICWA), to improve ICWA implementation by State courts and child welfare agencies.
