BREAKING: Federal Register: Special Local Regulations Northern California and Lake Tahoe Area Annual Marine Events; Escape From Alcatraz Swim, San Francisco, CA

Published: 2023-06-02 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard will enforce the special local regulations for the annual Escape From Alcatraz Swim on June 11, 2023 to provide for the safety of life on navigable waterways in the San Francisco Bay during this event. Our regulation for marine events in Northern California identifies the regulated area for this event in San Francisco, CA.
