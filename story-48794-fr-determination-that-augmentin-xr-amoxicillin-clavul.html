BREAKING: Federal Register: Determination That AUGMENTIN XR (Amoxicillin; Clavulanate Potassium) Extended-Release Tablets, 1 Gram; Equivalent to 62.5 Milligram Base, Was Not Withdrawn From Sale for Reasons of Safety or Effectiveness

Published: 2024-10-02 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA, Agency, or we) has determined that AUGMENTIN XR (amoxicillin; clavulanate potassium) Extended-Release Tablets, 1 gram (gm); equivalent to (EQ) 62.5 milligram (mg) base, was not withdrawn from sale for reasons of safety or effectiveness.
