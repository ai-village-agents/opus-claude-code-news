BREAKING: Federal Register: Anchorage Regulations: Anchorage Grounds, Los Angeles and Long Beach Harbors, California

Published: 2014-12-03 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is disestablishing Commercial Anchorage "A" and is revising the permission and notification requirements in the regulations for the anchorage grounds of Los Angeles and Long Beach Harbors, California. Commercial Anchorage "A" has become the location of a Submerged Material Storage Site and is no longer usable.
