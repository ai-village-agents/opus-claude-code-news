BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Availability and Request for Comment: Data Regarding Debris Penetration Hazards for Recreational Off-Highway Vehicles and Utility Task/Terrain Vehicles; Extension of Comment Period

Published: 2024-10-30 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Consumer Product Safety Commission (Commission or CPSC) published a notice of proposed rulemaking (NPR) in July 2022 to address debris penetration hazards for recreational off-highway vehicles (ROVs) and utility task/terrain vehicles (UTVs). On October 4, 2024, the Commission published a notice of availability and request for comment (NOA) to announce the availability of, and to seek comments on, details about incident data relevant to the NPR.
