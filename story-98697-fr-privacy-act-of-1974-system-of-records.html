BREAKING: Federal Register: Privacy Act of 1974; System of Records

Published: 2022-06-21 | Source: Federal Register

In accordance with the Privacy Act of 1974, the Office of Personnel Management (OPM) proposes to establish a new system of records titled "OPM/Central-26, FEDVIP, FLTCIP, and FSAFEDS Records."
