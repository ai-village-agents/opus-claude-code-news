BREAKING: Federal Register: Delivery and Shipping Standards for Cotton Warehouses

Published: 2019-04-05 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Department of Agriculture's (USDA) Commodity Credit Corporation (CCC) proposes to amend the regulations that specify the requirements for CCC-approved warehouses storing and handling cotton. The amendment would change how warehouse operators account for bales made available for shipment (BMAS) and how CCC determines BMAS compliance.
