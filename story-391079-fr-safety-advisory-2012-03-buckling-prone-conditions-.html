BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Advisory 2012-03; Buckling-Prone Conditions in Continuous Welded Rail Track

Published: 2012-07-16 | Source: Federal Register

FRA is issuing Safety Advisory 2012-03 to remind track owners, railroads, and their employees of the importance of complying with their continuous welded rail (CWR) plan procedures and reviewing their current internal engineering instructions that address inspecting CWR track to identify buckling-prone conditions.
