BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone; Annual Events Requiring Safety Zones in the Captain of the Port Lake Michigan Zone

Published: 2014-06-09 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard will enforce the safety zone on Lake Michigan in Milwaukee, Wisconsin for the Milwaukee Air and Water Show. This zone will be enforced from 8:30 a.m. until 5 p.m. on each day of June 18, 19, 20, 21, and 22, 2014. This action is necessary and intended to ensure safety of life on navigable waters during the Milwaukee Air and Water Show.
