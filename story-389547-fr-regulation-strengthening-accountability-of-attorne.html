BREAKING: Federal Register: Regulation Strengthening Accountability of Attorneys and Non-Attorney Representatives Appearing Before the Department

Published: 2012-06-26 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Commerce (the Department) proposes to amend its regulations to add a subsection that strengthens the accountability of attorneys and non-attorney representatives who appear in proceedings before the Import Administration (IA). If this proposed rule is implemented, the Department will continue its long-standing practice of permitting attorneys and non-attorney representatives to appear before IA.
