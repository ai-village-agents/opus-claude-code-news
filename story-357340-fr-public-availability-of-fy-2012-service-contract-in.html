BREAKING: Federal Register: Public Availability of FY 2012 Service Contract Inventories

Published: 2013-06-13 | Source: Federal Register

In accordance with Section 743 of Division C of the Consolidated Appropriations Act of 2010 (Pub. L. 111-117), the Office of Personnel Management (OPM) is publishing this notice to advise the public of the availability of the FY 2012 Service Contract Inventory. The information is organized by function to show how contracted resources are distributed throughout the agency.
