BREAKING: Federal Register: Trade Adjustment Assistance for Workers

Published: 2020-08-21 | Source: Federal Register

The Employment and Training Administration (ETA) of the Department of Labor (Department) is expanding protection and support for U.S. workers adversely impacted by foreign trade by revising its Trade Adjustment Assistance (TAA) for Workers program (TAA Program) regulations.
