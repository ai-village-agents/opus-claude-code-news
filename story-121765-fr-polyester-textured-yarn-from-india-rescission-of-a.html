BREAKING: Federal Register: Polyester Textured Yarn from India: Rescission of Antidumping Duty and Countervailing Duty Administrative Reviews; 2019-2020

Published: 2021-04-21 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Commerce (Commerce) is rescinding the administrative reviews of the antidumping duty (AD) and countervailing duty (CVD) orders on polyester textured yarn from India for the periods of review (POR) July 1, 2019, through December 31, 2020 (AD) and May 3, 2019, through December 31, 2020 (CVD), based on the timely withdrawal of the requests for review.
