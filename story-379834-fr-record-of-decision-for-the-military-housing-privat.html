BREAKING: Federal Register: Record of Decision for the Military Housing Privatization Initiative Hurlburt Field and Eglin Air Force Base, Florida, Final Environmental Impact Statement

The MHPI ROD documents the Air Force Decision selecting Alternative 4, Mix Alternative (FEIS Sec. 2.3.6) in the FEIS along with the project commenalities described in the FEIS (FEIS Sec. 2.1).
