BREAKING: Federal Register: Certain Automated Media Library Devices; Notice of Commission Decision Remanding the Investigation as To U.S. Patent Nos. 6,328,766 and 6,353,581; Extension of Target Date

Published: 2012-10-31 | Source: Federal Register

Notice is hereby given that the U.S. International Trade Commission has determined to remand to the presiding administrative law judge ("ALJ") with respect to U.S. Patent Nos. 6,328,766 ("the '766 patent") and 6,353,581 ("the '581 patent"), and the target date for completion of the investigation is extended to March 25, 2013.
