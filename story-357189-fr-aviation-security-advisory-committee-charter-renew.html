BREAKING: Federal Register: Aviation Security Advisory Committee Charter Renewal and Request for Applicants

Published: 2013-06-11 | Source: Federal Register

The Transportation Security Administration (TSA) announces the renewal of the charter for the Aviation Security Advisory Committee (ASAC). The Secretary of Homeland Security has determined that the ASAC is necessary and in the public interest in connection with the performance of duties of TSA.
