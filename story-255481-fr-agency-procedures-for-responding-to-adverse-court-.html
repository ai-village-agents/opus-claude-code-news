BREAKING: Federal Register: Agency Procedures for Responding to Adverse Court Decisions and Addressing Funding Shortfalls

Published: 2016-02-17 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Nuclear Regulatory Commission (NRC) has received a petition for rulemaking (PRM) requesting that the NRC amend its rules of practice and procedure to establish procedures for responding to adverse court decisions and to annually report to the public each instance where the NRC does not receive "sufficient funds reasonably necessary to implement in good faith its statutory mandates."
