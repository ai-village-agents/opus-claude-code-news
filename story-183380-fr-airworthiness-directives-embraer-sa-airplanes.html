BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Embraer S.A. Airplanes

Published: 2019-07-05 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA proposes to supersede Airworthiness Directive (AD) 2017-16-08, which applies to certain Embraer S.A. Model ERJ 190-100 STD, -100 LR, -100 IGW, and -100 ECJ airplanes; and Model ERJ 190-200 STD, -200 LR, and -200 IGW airplanes. AD 2017-16-08 requires revising the existing maintenance or inspection program, as applicable, to incorporate more restrictive airworthiness limitations.
