BREAKING: Federal Register: Privacy Act of 1974; System of Records

Published: 2022-01-28 | Source: Federal Register

We propose to revise a system of records in our existing inventory of systems subject to the Privacy Act of 1974. The system is NARA 44, Reasonable Accommodation Request Records. We are revising it to make it more clear that the system includes records of requests for religious accommodations under Title VII of the Civil Rights Act of 1964.
