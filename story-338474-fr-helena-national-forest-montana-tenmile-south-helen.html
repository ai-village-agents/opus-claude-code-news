BREAKING: Federal Register: Helena National Forest, Montana, Tenmile-South Helena Vegetation Project

Published: 2014-10-29 | Source: Federal Register

The Helena National Forest is going to prepare an environmental impact statement for vegetation management actions in the Tenmile Municipal Watershed and the South Hills of the City of Helena east of the Continental Divide.
