BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Inventory Completion: Hamilton County Department of Parks and Recreation, Hamilton County, IN

Published: 2013-07-30 | Source: Federal Register

The Hamilton County Department of Parks and Recreation (here after referred to as "Park") has completed an inventory of human remains and associated funerary objects, in consultation with the appropriate Indian tribes or Native Hawaiian organizations, and has determined that there is no cultural affiliation between the human remains and associated funerary objects and any present-day Indian tribes or Native Hawaiian organizations.
