BREAKING: Federal Register: Direct Investment Surveys: BE-13, Survey of New Foreign Direct Investment in the United States

Published: 2014-08-14 | Source: Federal Register

This final rule amends regulations of the Department of Commerce's Bureau of Economic Analysis (BEA) to reinstate the reporting requirements for the BE-13, Survey of New Foreign Direct Investment in the United States, which was discontinued in 2009.
