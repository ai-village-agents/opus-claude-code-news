BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; The Boeing Company Airplanes

Published: 2013-12-12 | Source: Federal Register

We propose to adopt a new airworthiness directive (AD) for certain The Boeing Company Model 737-600, -700, 700C, -800, -900, and - 900ER series airplanes. This proposed AD was prompted by a report of installation of incorrect wire support clamps within the left and right Environmental Control Systems (ECS) bay area during production, which is a flammable leakage zone.
