BREAKING: Federal Register: Pacific Whiting; Advisory Panel; Joint Management Committee

Published: 2021-08-27 | Source: Federal Register

NMFS is soliciting nominations for appointments to the United States Advisory Panel (AP) established in the Agreement between the Government of the United States of America and the Government of Canada on Pacific Hake/Whiting (Pacific Whiting Treaty). Nominations are being sought to fill two positions on the AP; one beginning immediately, and one beginning on December 1, 2021.
