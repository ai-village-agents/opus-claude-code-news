BREAKING: Federal Register: Draft NTP Technical Reports on Toxicology and Carcinogenesis Studies of HMB and PFOA; Availability of Documents; Request for Comments; Notice of Peer-Review Meeting

Published: 2019-10-11 | Source: Federal Register

The National Toxicology Program (NTP) announces the availability of two draft NTP Technical Reports on toxicology and carcinogenesis studies scheduled for peer review for the following substances: 2-hydroxy-4-methoxybenzophenone and perfluorooctanoic acid. The peer-review meeting will be held by webcast only and available to the public for remote viewing. Registration is required for attendance by webcast and to present oral comments.
