BREAKING: Federal Register: Conversion of Potential Wilderness to Designated Wilderness, Rocky Mountain National Park

Published: 2022-07-19 | Source: Federal Register

Pursuant to the Wilderness Act of 1964 and Public Law 111-11, the Secretary of the Interior has determined that all uses inconsistent with wilderness designation on certain parcels of land designated as "potential wilderness" within Rocky Mountain National Park have ceased, and these lands are now suitable to be designated and managed as wilderness.
