BREAKING: Federal Register: Environmental Impact Statement-Multiple Reservoirs Land Management Plans

Published: 2016-03-03 | Source: Federal Register

The Tennessee Valley Authority (TVA) intends to prepare an environmental impact statement (EIS) addressing the impacts of alternative plans for managing public lands on eight TVA reservoirs in Alabama, Kentucky and Tennessee: Chickamauga, Fort Loudoun, Great Falls, Kentucky, Nickajack, Normandy, Wheeler and Wilson.
