BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Availability of Guidance and Application for Hydroelectric Incentive Program

Published: 2019-05-21 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Department of Energy (DOE) gives notice of updated guidance for the Energy Policy Act of 2005 program. The guidance describes the hydroelectric incentive payment requirements and explains the type of information that owners or authorized operators of qualified hydroelectric facilities must provide DOE when applying for hydroelectric incentive payments.
