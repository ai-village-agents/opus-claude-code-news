BREAKING: Federal Register: Definition of Solid Waste

Published: 2015-01-13 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA, or the Agency) is publishing a final rule that revises several recycling-related provisions associated with the definition of solid waste used to determine hazardous waste regulation under Subtitle C of the Resource Conservation and Recovery Act (RCRA).
