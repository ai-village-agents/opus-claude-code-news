BREAKING: Federal Register: Van Pool Transportation LLC and AG Van Pool Holdings, LP-Acquisition of Control-George M. Carroll Transportation, Inc.

Published: 2025-09-17 | Source: Federal Register

On August 18, 2025, Van Pool Transportation LLC (Van Pool) and AG Van Pool Holdings, LP (AG Holdings) (collectively, Applicants), both noncarriers, filed an application to acquire control of an interstate passenger motor carrier, George M. Carroll Transportation, Inc. dba George M. Carroll Inc. (GMCT), from its sole shareholder, John Schindler (Seller). The Board is tentatively approving and authorizing the transaction.
