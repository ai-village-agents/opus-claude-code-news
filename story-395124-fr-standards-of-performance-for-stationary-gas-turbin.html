BREAKING: Federal Register: Standards of Performance for Stationary Gas Turbines; Standards of Performance for Stationary Combustion Turbines

Published: 2012-08-29 | Source: Federal Register

The EPA is proposing to amend the new source performance standards (NSPS) for stationary gas turbines and stationary combustion turbines. These amendments are primarily in response to issues raised by the regulated community. On July 6, 2006, the EPA promulgated amendments to the new source performance standards for stationary combustion turbines.
