BREAKING: Federal Register: Federal Civil Penalties Inflation Adjustment Act Amendments

Published: 2020-02-07 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Veterans Affairs (VA) is providing public notice of inflationary adjustments to the maximum civil monetary penalties assessed or enforced by VA, as implemented by the Federal Civil Penalties Inflation Adjustment Act Improvements Act of 2015, for calendar year 2020. VA may impose civil monetary penalties for false loan guaranty certifications.
