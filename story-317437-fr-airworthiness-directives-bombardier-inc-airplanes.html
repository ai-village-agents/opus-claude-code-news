BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Bombardier, Inc. Airplanes

Published: 2014-02-25 | Source: Federal Register

We are adopting a new airworthiness directive (AD) for certain Bombardier, Inc. Model BD-700-1A10 airplanes. This AD was prompted by a report that the manufacturer has determined that some completion centers used the heater/brake monitoring unit (HBMU) logic circuit to control the line voltage of the drain mast heaters.
