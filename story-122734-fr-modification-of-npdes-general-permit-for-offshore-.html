BREAKING: Federal Register: Modification of NPDES General Permit for Offshore Seafood Processors in Alaska (AKG524000)

Published: 2021-05-03 | Source: Federal Register

The Director, Water Division, Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) Region 10, is modifying a National Pollutant Discharge Elimination System (NPDES) General Permit for offshore seafood processors operating in federal waters off the coast of Alaska.
