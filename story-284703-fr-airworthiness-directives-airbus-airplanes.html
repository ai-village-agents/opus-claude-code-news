BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Airbus Airplanes

Published: 2015-01-26 | Source: Federal Register

We are superseding Airworthiness Directive (AD) AD 2011-14-06 for all Airbus Model A318, A319, A320, and A321 series airplanes. AD 2011-14-06 required revising the maintenance program. This new AD requires revising the maintenance program to incorporate new, more restrictive limitations. This AD was prompted by the determination that more restrictive limitations are necessary.
