BREAKING: Federal Register: National Biodefense Science Board; Call for Nominees

Published: 2013-06-11 | Source: Federal Register

The Office of the Secretary is accepting application submissions from qualified individuals who wish to be considered for membership on the National Biodefense Science Board (NBSB). Six members have membership expiration dates of December 31, 2013; therefore six new voting members will be selected for the Board.
