BREAKING: Federal Register: Partial Approval and Disapproval of Nevada Air Plan Revisions, Clark County

Published: 2016-01-14 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is finalizing a partial approval and partial disapproval of revisions to the Clark County portion of the Nevada State Implementation Plan (SIP). The SIP revisions include rescission of four local rules that collectively apply to sources that emit volatile organic compounds (VOCs), oxides of sulfur (SOX), and particulate matter (PM).
