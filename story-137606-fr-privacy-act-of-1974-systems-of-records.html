BREAKING: Federal Register: Privacy Act of 1974; Systems of Records

Published: 2021-11-10 | Source: Federal Register

The US. Office of Government Ethics (OGE) proposes to revise four and rescind one of its existing internal systems of records under the Privacy Act. The system of records to be rescinded is OGE/INTERNAL-2, which covers telephone call detail records that were used to verify employee telephone usage and to resolve billing discrepancies.
