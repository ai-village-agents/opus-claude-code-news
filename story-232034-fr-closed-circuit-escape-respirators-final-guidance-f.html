BREAKING: Federal Register: Closed-Circuit Escape Respirators; Final Guidance for Industry; Availability

Published: 2017-04-14 | Source: Federal Register

On December 28, 2016, the National Institute for Occupational Safety and Health (NIOSH), within the Centers for Disease Control and Prevention, Department of Health and Human Services, published a notice in the Federal Register announcing the availability of an interim guidance document addressing the availability of closed-circuit escape respirators (CCERs) for purchase, and the readiness of respirator manufacturers to comply with the regulatory provisions addressing these respirators.
