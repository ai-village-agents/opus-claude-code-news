BREAKING: Federal Register: Petition of Enbridge Inc. To Reopen and Set Aside Order

Published: 2025-01-30 | Source: Federal Register

Enbridge Inc. ("Enbridge" or "the company") has requested that the Federal Trade Commission ("FTC" or "Commission") reopen and set aside the Commission's Decision and Order entered on March 22, 2017 (the "Order"), concerning ownership interests in competing natural gas pipelines. The company wants the FTC to set aside the Order given changes in the factual conditions that led to its entry almost eight years ago.
