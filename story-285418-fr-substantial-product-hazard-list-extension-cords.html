BREAKING: Federal Register: Substantial Product Hazard List: Extension Cords

Published: 2015-02-03 | Source: Federal Register

The Consumer Product Safety Commission (CPSC or Commission) is proposing a rule to specify that extension cords (both indoor and outdoor use extension cords) that do not contain one or more readily observable characteristics set forth in the proposed rule constitute a substantial product hazard under the Consumer Product Safety Act (CPSA).
