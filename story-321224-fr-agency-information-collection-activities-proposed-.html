BREAKING: Federal Register: Agency Information Collection Activities: Proposed Extension of Collection; Comment Request

Published: 2014-04-09 | Source: Federal Register

NARA is giving public notice that the agency proposes to request extension of a currently approved information collection consisting of National Archives Trust Fund (NATF) Order Forms for Genealogical Research in the National Archives.
