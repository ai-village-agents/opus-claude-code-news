BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Funds Availability; Emergency Relief Program 2022 (ERP 2022)

Published: 2023-10-31 | Source: Federal Register

The Farm Service Agency (FSA) is issuing this notice announcing ERP 2022, which will provide payments to eligible crop producers for losses due to qualifying disaster events including wildfires, hurricanes, floods, derechos, excessive heat, tornadoes, winter storms, freeze (including a polar vortex), smoke exposure, excessive moisture, qualifying drought, and related conditions that occurred in calendar year 2022.
