BREAKING: Federal Register: Spirodiclofen; Rescinding Cancellation Order for Certain Pesticide Registrations

Published: 2022-06-22 | Source: Federal Register

In the Federal Register of November 19, 2021, EPA amended the effective date of the cancellation for the two spirodiclofen registrations, EPA Registration Nos. 10163-382 and 10163-383, as requested by the registrant (Gowan), for the two registrations from December 31, 2021, until June 30, 2022.
