BREAKING: Federal Register: National Emission Standards for Hazardous Air Pollutants; Delegation of Authority to Oklahoma

Published: 2015-04-21 | Source: Federal Register

On February 24, 2015, the Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) published a direct final rule approving the updated delegation of EPA authority for implementation and enforcement of National Emission Standards for Hazardous Air Pollutants (NESHAPs) for all sources (both part 70 and non-part 70 sources) to the Oklahoma Department of Environmental Quality (ODEQ).
