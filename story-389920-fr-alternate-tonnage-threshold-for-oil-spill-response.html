BREAKING: Federal Register: Alternate Tonnage Threshold for Oil Spill Response Vessels

Published: 2012-06-29 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is establishing an alternate size threshold based on the measurement system established under the International Convention on Tonnage Measurement of Ships, 1969, for oil spill response vessels, which are properly certificated under 46 CFR chapter I, subchapter L. The present size threshold of 500 gross register tons is based on the U.S. regulatory measurement system.
