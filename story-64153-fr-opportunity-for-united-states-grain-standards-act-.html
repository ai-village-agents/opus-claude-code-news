BREAKING: Federal Register: Opportunity for United States Grain Standards Act (USGSA) Designation in the Texas Central Area

Published: 2023-04-03 | Source: Federal Register

The Agricultural Marketing Service (AMS) is seeking persons or governmental agencies interested in providing official services in the area noted below to submit an application for designation. Designation provides for private entities or state governmental agencies to be an integral part of the official grain inspection system (https:// www.ams.usda.gov/services/fgis/official-grain-inspection-weighing- system).
