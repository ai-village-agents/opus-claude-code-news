BREAKING: Federal Register: Prohibited Transactions Involving Pooled Employer Plans Under the SECURE Act and Other Multiple Employer Plans

Published: 2020-06-18 | Source: Federal Register

The Setting Every Community Up for Retirement Enhancement Act (SECURE Act) amended the Employee Retirement Income Security Act of 1974 (ERISA) to allow for pooled employer plans (PEPs). PEPs are required to designate a pooled plan provider who is a named fiduciary of the PEP.
