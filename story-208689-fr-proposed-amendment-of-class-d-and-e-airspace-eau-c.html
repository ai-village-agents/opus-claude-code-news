BREAKING: Federal Register: Proposed Amendment of Class D and E Airspace; Eau Claire, WI

Published: 2018-06-05 | Source: Federal Register

This action proposes to amend Class D airspace, Class E airspace designated as a surface area, and Class E airspace extending upward from 700 feet above the surface at Chippewa Valley Regional Airport, Eau Claire, WI. The FAA is proposing this action as a result of an airspace review caused by the decommissioning of the Eau Claire nondirectional radio beacon (NDB)/outer compass locator (LOM).
