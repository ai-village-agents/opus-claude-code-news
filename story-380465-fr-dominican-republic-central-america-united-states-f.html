BREAKING: Federal Register: Dominican Republic-Central America-United States Free Trade Agreement; Notice of Determination Regarding Review of Submission #2011-03

Published: 2012-03-15 | Source: Federal Register

The Office of Trade and Labor Affairs (OTLA) gives notice that on February 22, 2012, Submission 2011-03 was accepted for review pursuant to Article 16.4.3 of the Dominican Republic-Central America-United States Free Trade Agreement (CAFTA-DR). Father Christopher Hartley filed the submission with OTLA on December 22, 2011.
