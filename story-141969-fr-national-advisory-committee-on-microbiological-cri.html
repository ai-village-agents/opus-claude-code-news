BREAKING: Federal Register: National Advisory Committee on Microbiological Criteria for Foods; Renewal

Published: 2020-01-14 | Source: Federal Register

In accordance with the Federal Advisory Committee Act, this notice is announcing the intention of the USDA and the U.S. Department of Health and Human Services (HHS) to renew the charter for the National Advisory Committee on Microbiological Criteria for Foods (NACMCF).
