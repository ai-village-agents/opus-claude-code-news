BREAKING: Federal Register: Standards for the Growing, Harvesting, Packing, and Holding of Produce for Human Consumption; Extension of Comment Period for Information Collection Provisions

Published: 2013-02-19 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA or "we") is extending the comment period for the information collection provisions of the proposed rule on "Standards for the Growing, Harvesting, Packing, and Holding of Produce for Human Consumption" that appeared in the Federal Register of January 16, 2013.
