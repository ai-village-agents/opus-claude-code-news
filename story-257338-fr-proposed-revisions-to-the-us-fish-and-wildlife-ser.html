BREAKING: Federal Register: Proposed Revisions to the U.S. Fish and Wildlife Service Mitigation Policy

Published: 2016-03-08 | Source: Federal Register

We, the U.S. Fish and Wildlife Service (Service), announce proposed revisions to our Mitigation Policy, which has guided Service recommendations on mitigating the adverse impacts of land and water developments on fish, wildlife, plants, and their habitats since 1981. The revisions are motivated by changes in conservation challenges and practices since 1981, including accelerating loss of habitats, effects of climate change, and advances in conservation science.
