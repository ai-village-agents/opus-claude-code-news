BREAKING: Federal Register: Amendment of Class E Airspace; Minocqua-Woodruff, WI

Published: 2019-08-16 | Source: Federal Register

This action modifies Class E airspace areas extending upward from 700 feet or more above the surface of the earth at Lakeland/Nobel F. Lee Memorial Field Airport in Minocqua-Woodruff, WI. The FAA is taking this action as the result of an airspace review caused by the decommissioning of the Arbor Vitae non-directional radio beacon (NDB).
