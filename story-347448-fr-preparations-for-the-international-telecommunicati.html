BREAKING: Federal Register: Preparations for the International Telecommunication Union World Telecommunication Development Conference (ITU WTDC 2014)

Published: 2013-02-21 | Source: Federal Register

This notice announces meetings of an ad hoc group of the Department of State's International Telecommunication Advisory Committee (ITAC) to begin preparations for the ITU World Telecommunication Development Conference (WTDC 2014). The meetings are announced to the ITAC committee and referred to the ITAC-D ad hoc for action. The process for joining the ITAC-D ad hoc is described.
