BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Standard for Debris Penetration Hazards

Published: 2022-07-21 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Consumer Product Safety Commission (Commission or CPSC) has determined preliminarily that there is an unreasonable risk of injury and death associated with debris penetration in off-highway vehicles (OHVs), including recreational off-highway vehicles (ROVs) and utility task/terrain vehicles (UTVs).
