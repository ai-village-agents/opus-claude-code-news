BREAKING: Federal Register: Liquidity Coverage Ratio: Treatment of U.S. Municipal Securities as High-Quality Liquid Assets

Published: 2015-05-28 | Source: Federal Register

The Board of Governors of the Federal Reserve System (Board) invites public comment on a proposed rule (proposed rule) that would amend the Board's liquidity coverage ratio requirement (LCR) to include certain U.S. municipal securities as high-quality liquid assets (HQLA).
