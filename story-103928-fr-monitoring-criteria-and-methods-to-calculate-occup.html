BREAKING: Federal Register: Monitoring Criteria and Methods To Calculate Occupational Radiation Doses

Published: 2022-08-29 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Nuclear Regulatory Commission (NRC) is issuing Revision 1 to Regulatory Guide (RG) 8.34, "Monitoring Criteria and Methods to Calculate Occupational Radiation Doses." This revised guidance is an approach that is acceptable to the staff of the NRC for monitoring and determining the dose to occupationally exposed individuals.
