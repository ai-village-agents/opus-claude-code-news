BREAKING: Federal Register: Tests Determined To Be Suitable for Use in the National Reporting System for Adult Education

Published: 2021-12-06 | Source: Federal Register

On October 21, 2021, the Department of Education (Department) published in the Federal Register a notice announcing tests, test forms, and delivery formats that the Secretary determines to be suitable for use in the National Reporting System for Adult Education (NRS notice). To address formatting issues arising in the publication of the NRS notice, the Office of the Federal Register published a correction on November 15, 2021.
