BREAKING: Federal Register: United States Standards for Grades of Canned Vegetables

Published: 2016-06-17 | Source: Federal Register

The Agricultural Marketing Service (AMS) of the Department of Agriculture (USDA) proposes to revise 18 U.S. grade standards for canned vegetables issued on or before August 3, 1998. AMS is proposing to replace the two-term grading system (dual nomenclature) with a single term to describe each quality level for the grade standards identified in this notice.
