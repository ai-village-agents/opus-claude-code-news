BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone; Isabel Holmes Bridge, Wilmington, NC

Published: 2019-12-16 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is establishing two temporary safety zones on the navigable waters of the Cape Fear River at the Isabel Holmes Bridge in Wilmington, North Carolina. These temporary safety zones are intended to restrict vessel traffic on the Cape Fear River from December 15, 2019, through February 15, 2020, while work crews repair the bridge and replace power cables crossing under the river.
