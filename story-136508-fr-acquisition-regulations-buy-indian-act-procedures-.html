BREAKING: Federal Register: Acquisition Regulations; Buy Indian Act; Procedures for Contracting

Published: 2021-10-27 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of the Interior proposes to revise regulations implementing the Buy Indian Act, which provides the Department with authority to set aside procurement contracts for Indian-owned and controlled businesses.
