BREAKING: Federal Register: Transfer of Very Low-Level Waste To Exempt Persons for Disposal

Published: 2020-05-08 | Source: Federal Register

On April 9, 2020, the U.S. Nuclear Regulatory Commission (NRC) extended the comment period on a proposed interpretation of its low- level radioactive waste disposal regulations that would permit licensees to dispose of waste by transfer to persons who hold specific exemptions for the purpose of disposal.
