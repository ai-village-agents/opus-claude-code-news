BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Final Adoption and Effective Date for Updates to the Submission Guidelines

Published: 2026-01-13 | Source: Federal Register

The National Capital Planning Commission (NCPC) adopted updates to the Submission Guidelines on January 8, 2026. Agencies that are subject to plan and project review submit development proposals to the Commission by following the process laid out in the Submission Guidelines. The Submission Guidelines are available online at: https:// www.ncpc.gov/review/guidelines/.
