BREAKING: Federal Register: Center for Biologics Evaluation and Research eSubmitter Pilot Evaluation Program for Investigational New Drug Applications

Published: 2013-04-08 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration's (FDA's) Center for Biologics Evaluation and Research (CBER) is announcing an invitation to sponsors of investigational new drug (IND) applications to participate in a pilot evaluation program for CBER's eSubmitter Program (eSubmitter).
