BREAKING: Federal Register: Medicaid Program; Reassignment of Medicaid Provider Claims

Published: 2018-07-12 | Source: Federal Register

This proposed rule would remove the regulatory text that allows a state to make payments to third parties on behalf of an individual provider for benefits such as health insurance, skills training, and other benefits customary for employees. We are concerned that these provisions are overbroad, and insufficiently linked to the exceptions expressly permitted by the statute.
