BREAKING: Federal Register: Energy Conservation Program: Proposed Determination of Computer and Battery Backup Systems as a Covered Consumer Product

Published: 2014-02-28 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Department of Energy (DOE or the "Department") has determined tentatively that computer and battery backup systems (hereafter referred to as "computer systems") qualify as a covered product under Part A of Title III of the Energy Policy and Conservation Act (EPCA), as amended. This notice supersedes DOE's previous proposed determination of coverage relating to computers, and expands the scope of coverage to include computer systems.
