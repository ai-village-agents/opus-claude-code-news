BREAKING: Federal Register: Ferrovanadium From the Republic of Korea: Antidumping Duty Order

Published: 2017-05-15 | Source: Federal Register

Based on an affirmative final determination by the Department of Commerce (the Department) and an affirmative final determination by the International Trade Commission (the ITC), the Department is issuing an antidumping duty order on ferrovanadium from the Republic of Korea (Korea).
