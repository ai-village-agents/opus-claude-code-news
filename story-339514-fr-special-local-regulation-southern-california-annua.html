BREAKING: Federal Register: Special Local Regulation; Southern California Annual Marine Events for the San Diego Captain of the Port Zone

Published: 2014-11-13 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard will enforce the Hanohano Ocean Challenge special local regulations on January 24, 2015. This marine event occurs on the navigable waters of Mission Bay, in San Diego, California. This action is necessary to provide for safety of the participants, crew, spectators, safety vessels, and general users of the waterway.
