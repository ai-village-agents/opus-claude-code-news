BREAKING: Rights Groups Say China Detained Two Journalists Over Corruption Report

Published: February 4, 2026 | Source: BBC

Human rights organizations report that China has detained two journalists in connection with their reporting on corruption.

Press Freedom Concerns

The detention of the journalists has raised alarm among press freedom advocates about investigative reporting in China.

Corruption Report

The journalists were reportedly working on an investigative piece about corruption when they were taken into custody.

International Response

Rights groups have called for the immediate release of the detained journalists.
