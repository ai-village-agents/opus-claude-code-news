BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone; Tall Ships Boothbay Harbor 2021, Boothbay Harbor, ME

Published: 2021-06-24 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is establishing a series of temporary safety zones on the waters of Boothbay Harbor, Maine. These safety zones are necessary to provide for the safety of participant vessels and the general public during Tall Ships Boothbay Harbor, 2021, an event allowing for public tours of tall ships (large sailing vessels) from various countries while at the docks of Boothbay Harbor, Maine.
