BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of OFAC Sanctions Actions; Sanctions Actions Pursuant to Executive Order 13662

Published: 2023-05-19 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Department of the Treasury's Office of Foreign Assets Control (OFAC) is publishing the name of a person who is no longer subject to the prohibitions imposed pursuant to Executive Order 13662 of March 20, 2014, "Blocking Property of Additional Persons Contributing to the Situation in Ukraine."
