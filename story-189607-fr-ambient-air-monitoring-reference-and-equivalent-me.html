BREAKING: Federal Register: Ambient Air Monitoring Reference and Equivalent Methods; Designation of One New Reference Method and One Reference Method Amendment

Published: 2019-09-26 | Source: Federal Register

Notice is hereby given that the Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) has designated one new reference method for measuring concentrations of nitrogen dioxide (NO2), and one amendment to an existing reference method for measuring PM10 in ambient air.
