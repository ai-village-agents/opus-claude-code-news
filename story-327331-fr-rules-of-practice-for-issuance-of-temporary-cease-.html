BREAKING: Federal Register: Rules of Practice for Issuance of Temporary Cease-and-Desist Orders

Published: 2014-06-18 | Source: Federal Register

On September 26, 2013, 78 FR 59163, the Consumer Financial Protection Bureau (Bureau) published in the Federal Register an interim final rule establishing procedures for the issuance of a temporary cease-and-desist order (TCDO) pursuant to section 1053(c) of the Dodd- Frank Wall Street Reform and Consumer Protection Act (Dodd-Frank Act), which requires the Bureau to prescribe rules establishing procedures for the conduct of adjudication proceedings.
