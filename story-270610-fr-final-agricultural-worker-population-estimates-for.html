BREAKING: Federal Register: Final Agricultural Worker Population Estimates for Basic Field-Agricultural Worker/Migrant Grants

Published: 2016-08-08 | Source: Federal Register

The Legal Services Corporation (LSC) announces implementation of new population estimates of agricultural workers that LSC will use for distribution of funds among grants for providing civil legal services to those workers and their dependents (Agricultural Worker Grants, formerly referred to as Migrant Grants).
