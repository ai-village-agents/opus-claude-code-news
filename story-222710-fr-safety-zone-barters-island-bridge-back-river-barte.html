BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone; Barters Island Bridge, Back River, Barters Island, ME

Published: 2018-12-07 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is establishing a temporary safety zone for the navigable waters within a 50 yard radius from the center point of the Barters Island Bridge, on the Back River, ME, approximately 4.6 miles north of the mouth of the waterway.
