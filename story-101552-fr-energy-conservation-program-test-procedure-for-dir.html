BREAKING: Federal Register: Energy Conservation Program: Test Procedure for Direct Expansion-Dedicated Outdoor Air Systems

Published: 2022-07-27 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Department of Energy ("DOE") is publishing a final rule to establish definitions for "direct expansion-dedicated outdoor air systems" ("DX-DOASes") and "unitary dedicated outdoor air systems" ("unitary DOASes"). Unitary DOASes are a category of small, large, and very large commercial package air conditioning and heating equipment under the Energy Policy and Conservation Act, as amended.
