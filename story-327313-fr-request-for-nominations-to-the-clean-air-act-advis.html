BREAKING: Federal Register: Request for Nominations to the Clean Air Act Advisory Committee (CAAAC)

Published: 2014-06-18 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) invites nominations from a diverse range of qualified candidates to be considered for appointment to its Clean Air Act Advisory Committee (CAAAC). Vacancies are anticipated to be filled by February 2015.
