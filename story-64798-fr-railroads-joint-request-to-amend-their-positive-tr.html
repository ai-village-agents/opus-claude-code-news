BREAKING: Federal Register: Railroads' Joint Request To Amend Their Positive Train Control Safety Plans and Positive Train Control Systems

Published: 2023-04-11 | Source: Federal Register

This document provides the public with notice that on March 22, 2023, twenty host railroads submitted a joint request for amendment (RFA) to their FRA-approved Positive Train Control Safety Plans (PTCSP) reflecting the updated PTC onboard software, I-ETMS On-Board 6.5.0 and On-Board 6.5.1.
