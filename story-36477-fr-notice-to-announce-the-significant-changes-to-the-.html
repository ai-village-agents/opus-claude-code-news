BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice To Announce the Significant Changes to the NIH Grants Policy Statement for Fiscal Year 2024

Published: 2024-05-09 | Source: Federal Register

The National Institutes of Health (NIH) announces publication of the updated significant changes that have already been made to the NIH Grants Policy Statement (GPS) in fiscal year 2023 that will be reflected in the GPS for fiscal year 2024. The NIH GPS provides both up-to-date policy guidance that serves as NIH standard terms and conditions of award for all NIH grants and cooperative agreements, and extensive guidance to those who are interested in pursuing NIH grants.
