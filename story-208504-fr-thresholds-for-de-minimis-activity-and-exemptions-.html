BREAKING: Federal Register: Thresholds for De Minimis Activity and Exemptions From Licensing Under the Animal Welfare Act

Published: 2018-06-04 | Source: Federal Register

We are amending the Animal Welfare Act (AWA) regulations to implement amendments to the Act that broadened the scope of the exemptions from the licensing requirements for dealers and exhibitors.
