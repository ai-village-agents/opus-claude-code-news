BREAKING: Federal Register: Advisory Committee on the Medical Uses of Isotopes: Meeting Notice

Published: 2020-04-16 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Nuclear Regulatory Commission (NRC) will convene a teleconference meeting of the Advisory Committee on the Medical Uses of Isotopes (ACMUI) on April 30, 2020, to discuss the draft recommendations of the ACMUI COVID-19 Subcommittee. The ACMUI subcommittee's recommendations will include its review of the impact of COVID-19 on the medical use community and potential regulatory relief measures as it relates to the medical uses of radioactive material.
