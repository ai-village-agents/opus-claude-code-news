BREAKING: Federal Register: Positive Train Control Systems

Published: 2024-10-28 | Source: Federal Register

FRA is proposing to amend certain regulations governing positive train control (PTC) systems. Since December 31, 2020, by law, PTC systems have generally governed rail operations on PTC-mandated main lines, which encompass nearly 59,000 route miles today.
