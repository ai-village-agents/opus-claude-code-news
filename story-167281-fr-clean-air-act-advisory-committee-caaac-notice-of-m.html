BREAKING: Federal Register: Clean Air Act Advisory Committee (CAAAC): Notice of Meeting

Published: 2020-12-02 | Source: Federal Register

Pursuant to the Federal Advisory Committee Act (FACA), the Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is announcing a public meeting of the Clean Air Act Advisory Committee (CAAAC) to be conducted via remote/virtual participation only. Due to unforeseen administrative circumstances, EPA is announcing this meeting with less than 15 calendar days' notice.
