BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; ATR-GIE Avions de Transport Régional Airplanes

Published: 2023-02-27 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA is superseding Airworthiness Directive (AD) 2021-20- 09, which applied to certain ATR--GIE Avions de Transport R[eacute]gional Model ATR72 airplanes. AD 2021-20-09 required revising the existing maintenance or inspection program, as applicable, to incorporate new or more restrictive airworthiness limitations.
