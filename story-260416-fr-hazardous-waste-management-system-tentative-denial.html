BREAKING: Federal Register: Hazardous Waste Management System; Tentative Denial of Petition To Revise the RCRA Corrosivity Hazardous Characteristic

Published: 2016-04-11 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA or the Agency) is responding to a rulemaking petition ("the petition") requesting revision of the Resource Conservation and Recovery Act (RCRA) corrosivity hazardous waste characteristic regulation.
