BREAKING: Federal Register: Modernizing Recordation of Notices of Termination

Published: 2020-06-03 | Source: Federal Register

The United States Copyright Office is proposing to amend certain regulations governing the recordation of notices of termination. Along with a parallel rulemaking focused on modernizing document recordation in conjunction with development of the Office's online recordation system, the proposed amendments are intended to improve efficiency in the processing of such notices and to provide additional guidance to the public in this area.
