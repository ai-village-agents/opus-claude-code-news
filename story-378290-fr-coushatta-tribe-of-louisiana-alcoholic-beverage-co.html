BREAKING: Federal Register: Coushatta Tribe of Louisiana-Alcoholic Beverage Control Ordinance

Published: 2012-02-22 | Source: Federal Register

This notice publishes the Coushatta Tribe of Louisiana Alcoholic Beverage Control Ordinance. The Ordinance regulates and controls the possession, sale and consumption of liquor within the Coushatta Tribe's Indian country.
