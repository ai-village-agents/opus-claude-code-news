BREAKING: Federal Register: Corrections and Clarifications to the Export Administration Regulations

Published: 2014-12-29 | Source: Federal Register

The Bureau of Industry and Security (BIS) is correcting certain provisions of the Export Administration Regulations (EAR) that were amended in past rulemakings appearing in the Federal Register between November 5, 2007 and October 14, 2014. This final rule makes corrections to certain provisions to ensure consistency and clarity in the regulations.
