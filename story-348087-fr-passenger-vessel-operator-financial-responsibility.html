BREAKING: Federal Register: Passenger Vessel Operator Financial Responsibility Requirements for Nonperformance of Transportation

Published: 2013-02-27 | Source: Federal Register

The Federal Maritime Commission amends its rules regarding the establishment of passenger vessel financial responsibility for nonperformance of transportation.
