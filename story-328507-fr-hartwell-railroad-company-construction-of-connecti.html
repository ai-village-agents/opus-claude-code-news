BREAKING: Federal Register: Hartwell Railroad Company-Construction of Connecting Track Exemption-in Elbert County, GA

Published: 2014-07-01 | Source: Federal Register

The Surface Transportation Board's (Board) Office of Environmental Analysis (OEA) has prepared an Environmental Assessment (EA) in response to a verified notice of exemption filed on May 27, 2014 by the Hartwell Railroad Company (Hartwell). Hartwell seeks to construct approximately 1,360 feet of connecting railroad track, pursuant to 49 CFR 1150.36.
