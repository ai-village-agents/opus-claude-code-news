BREAKING: Federal Register: Certain Radio Frequency Micro-Needle Dermatological Treatment Devices and Components Thereof; Commission Determination Not To Review an Initial Determination Terminating the Investigation in Its Entirety Based on a Settlement Agreement; Termination of the Investigation

Published: 2019-10-09 | Source: Federal Register

Notice is hereby given that the U.S. International Trade Commission has determined not to review an initial determination ("ID") (Order No. 45) of the presiding administrative law judge ("ALJ") terminating the investigation as to the remaining respondents, EndyMed Medical Inc. of New York City, New York and EndyMed Medical Ltd. of Caesarea, Israel ("Endy"), based on a settlement agreement. The investigation is terminated.
