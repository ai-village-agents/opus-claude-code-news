BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone; Marina Del Rey New Year's Eve Fireworks Display, Marina Del Rey, California

Published: 2022-12-12 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is establishing a temporary safety zone near the South Jetty on Ballona Creek, Marina Del Rey, CA, for the duration of the fireworks display on December 31, 2022. This temporary safety zone is necessary to provide for the safety of the waterway users to keep them clear of potential harmful debris within the fall out zone during the fireworks display within Marina Del Rey, CA. Entry of persons or vessels into this temporary safety zone is prohibited unless specifically authorized ...
