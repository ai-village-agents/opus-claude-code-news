BREAKING: Federal Register: U.S. Flag Compliance With MARPOL Annex VI International Energy Efficiency (IEE) Requirements

Published: 2013-05-13 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard announces that International Maritime Organization (IMO) Resolution MEPC.203(62) amended Annex VI to the International Convention for the Prevention of Pollution from Ships (MARPOL). On July 15, 2011, the IMO formally adopted Resolution MEPC.203(62), which entered into force on January 1, 2013, and amends MARPOL Annex VI by adding Chapter 4 and amending existing regulations in Annex VI.
