BREAKING: Federal Register: Kiwifruit Grown in California; Proposed Amendments to Marketing Order

Published: 2013-02-08 | Source: Federal Register

This rule invites comments on five proposed amendments to Marketing Order No. 920 (order) which regulates the handling of kiwifruit grown in California. The amendments were proposed by the Kiwifruit Administrative Committee (Committee or KAC), which is responsible for local administration of the order.
