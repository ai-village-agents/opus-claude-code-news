BREAKING: Federal Register: Qualified Business Income Deduction

Published: 2018-08-16 | Source: Federal Register

This document contains proposed regulations concerning the deduction for qualified business income under section 199A of the Internal Revenue Code (Code). The regulations will affect individuals, partnerships, S corporations, trusts, and estates engaged in domestic trades or businesses. The proposed regulations also contain an anti- avoidance rule under section 643 of the Code to treat multiple trusts as a single trust in certain cases.
