BREAKING: Federal Register: Amendments to Registration of Food Facilities

Published: 2015-04-09 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA or we) is proposing to amend its regulation for registration of food facilities that requires domestic and foreign facilities that manufacture/process, pack, or hold food for human or animal consumption in the United States to register with FDA.
