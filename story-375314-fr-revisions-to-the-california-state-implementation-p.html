BREAKING: Federal Register: Revisions to the California State Implementation Plan, Placer County Air Pollution Control District

Published: 2012-01-19 | Source: Federal Register

EPA is finalizing a limited approval and limited disapproval of revisions to the Placer County Air Pollution Control District (PCAPCD) portion of the California State Implementation Plan (SIP). This action was proposed in the Federal Register on September 6, 2011 and concerns oxides of nitrogen (NOX) emissions from biomass fuel-fired boilers.
