BREAKING: Federal Register: Final Notice of Job Corps Center for Closure

Published: 2014-10-09 | Source: Federal Register

The Employment and Training Administration (ETA) of the U.S. Department of Labor (Department or DOL) issues this notice to announce its final decision to close the Treasure Lake Job Corps Center. The Office of Job Corps (OJC) in ETA published a proposed methodology for selecting centers for closure at 78 FR 2284 on January 10, 2013.
