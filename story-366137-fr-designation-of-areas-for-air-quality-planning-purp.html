BREAKING: Federal Register: Designation of Areas for Air Quality Planning Purposes; California; San Joaquin Valley, South Coast Air Basin, Coachella Valley, and Sacramento Metro Ozone Nonattainment Areas; Reclassification

Published: 2013-09-23 | Source: Federal Register

EPA is withdrawing a proposed action to reclassify the Indian country pertaining to the Morongo Band of Mission Indians (Morongo Reservation) from "severe-17" to "extreme" for the 1997 eight-hour ozone standard.
