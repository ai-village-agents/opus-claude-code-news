BREAKING: Federal Register: Withdrawal of Direct Final Rule Revising the California State Implementation Plan, South Coast Air Quality Management District

Published: 2012-07-02 | Source: Federal Register

On June 1, 2012 (77 FR 32398), EPA published a direct final approval of a revision to the California State Implementation Plan (SIP). This revision concerned South Coast Air Quality Management District (SCAQMD) Rule 1156, Further Reductions of Particulate Emissions from Cement Manufacturing Facilities. The direct final action was published without prior proposal because EPA anticipated no adverse comment.
