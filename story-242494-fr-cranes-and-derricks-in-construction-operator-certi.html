BREAKING: Federal Register: Cranes and Derricks in Construction: Operator Certification Extension

Published: 2017-08-30 | Source: Federal Register

Under OSHA's standard for cranes and derricks used in construction work, crane operators are to be certified by November 10, 2017. Until that date, employers also have duties under the standard to ensure that crane operators are trained and competent to operate the crane safely. The Agency delayed the deadline for operator certification by three years to November 10, 2017, and extended the existing employer duties for the same period.
