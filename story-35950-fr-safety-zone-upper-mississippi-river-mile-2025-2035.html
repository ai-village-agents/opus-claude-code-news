BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone; Upper Mississippi River Mile 202.5-203.5 Near Alton, IL

Published: 2024-05-02 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is proposing to establish a temporary safety zone for Upper Mississippi River at mile marker 202.5 to 203.2. This action is necessary to provide for the safety of life on these navigable waters near Alton, IL, during a power boat championship race on June 21, 2024, through June 24, 2024.
