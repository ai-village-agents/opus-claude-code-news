BREAKING: Federal Register: Revision of Freedom of Information Act Regulation

Published: 2015-08-17 | Source: Federal Register

This final rule amends HUD's regulations implementing the Freedom of Information Act (FOIA) to update and streamline HUD's current FOIA regulation. Specifically, it updates HUD's regulations to reflect statutory changes to the FOIA, current HUD organizational structure, and current HUD policies and practices with respect to the FOIA.
