BREAKING: Federal Register: Adequacy Status of the Submitted 2009, 2017 and 2025 PM2.5 Motor Vehicle Emission Budgets for Transportation Conformity Purposes for New York

Published: 2013-09-03 | Source: Federal Register

In this action, EPA is notifying the public that we have found the motor vehicle emissions budgets for PM2.5 and NOX in the submitted maintenance plan for the New York portions of the New York-Northern New Jersey-Long Island, NY-NJ-CT PM2.5 nonattainment areas to be adequate for transportation conformity purposes.
