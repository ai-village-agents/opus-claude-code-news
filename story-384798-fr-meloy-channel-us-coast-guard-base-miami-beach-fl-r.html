BREAKING: Federal Register: Meloy Channel, U.S. Coast Guard Base Miami Beach, FL; Restricted Area

Published: 2012-05-03 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Army Corps of Engineers (Corps) is proposing to amend its regulations to establish a new restricted area in the waters surrounding the U.S. Coast Guard Base Miami Beach, Florida (Base Miami Beach). Base Miami Beach is composed of multiple U.S. Coast Guard (USCG) units, both land and waterside. The facility has one of the highest operational tempos in the USCG for both routine and emergency operations.
