BREAKING: Snooker Legend and TV Star John Virgo Dies

Published: February 4, 2026 | Source: BBC

John Virgo, the beloved snooker player and television personality known for his trick shots on Big Break, has died.

Snooker Career

Virgo was a talented professional snooker player who won the UK Championship in 1979 and reached the World Championship semi-finals.

TV Fame

He became a household name as co-presenter of the BBC game show Big Break alongside Jim Davidson, entertaining audiences with his trick shots.

Tributes

Tributes are pouring in from the snooker world and beyond for the 'special' player who brought joy to millions.
