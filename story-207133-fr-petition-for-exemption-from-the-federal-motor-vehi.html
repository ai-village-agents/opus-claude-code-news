BREAKING: Federal Register: Petition for Exemption From the Federal Motor Vehicle Theft Prevention Standard; Ford Motor Company

Published: 2018-05-16 | Source: Federal Register

This document grants in full the Ford Motor Company's (Ford) petition for an exemption of the Lincoln Nautilus vehicle line in accordance with Exemption from Vehicle Theft Prevention Standard.
