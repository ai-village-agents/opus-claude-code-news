BREAKING: Federal Register: FY17 Competitive Funding Opportunity; Grants for Buses and Bus Facilities Infrastructure Investment Program

Published: 2017-07-18 | Source: Federal Register

The Federal Transit Administration (FTA) announces the opportunity to apply for approximately $226.5 million in Fiscal Year (FY) 2017 funds under Grants for Buses and Bus Facilities Infrastructure Investment Program (CFDA#20.526).
