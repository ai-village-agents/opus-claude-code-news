BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Proposed Withdrawal Extension, Red Rock Canyon State Park; California

Published: 2016-03-16 | Source: Federal Register

The Assistant Secretary for Land and Minerals Management proposes to extend the duration of Public Land Order (PLO) No. 7260 for an additional 20-year term.
