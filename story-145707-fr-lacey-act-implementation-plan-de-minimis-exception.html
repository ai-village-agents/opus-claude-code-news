BREAKING: Federal Register: Lacey Act Implementation Plan: De Minimis Exception

Published: 2020-03-02 | Source: Federal Register

The Food, Conservation, and Energy Act of 2008 amended the Lacey Act to provide, among other things, that importers submit a declaration at the time of importation for certain plants and plant products. The declaration requirement of the Lacey Act became effective on December 15, 2008, and enforcement of that requirement is being phased in.
