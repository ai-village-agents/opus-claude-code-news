BREAKING: Federal Register: Disclosure of Payments by Resource Extraction Issuers

Published: 2021-01-15 | Source: Federal Register

We are adopting a rule under the Securities Exchange Act of 1934 ("Exchange Act") and an amendment to Form SD to implement Section 13(q) of the Exchange Act. Section 13(q) directs the Commission to issue rules requiring resource extraction issuers to include in an annual report information relating to payments made to a foreign government or the Federal Government for the purpose of the commercial development of oil, natural gas, or minerals.
