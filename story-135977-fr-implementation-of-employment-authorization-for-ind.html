BREAKING: Federal Register: Implementation of Employment Authorization for Individuals Covered by Deferred Enforced Departure for Hong Kong

Published: 2021-10-21 | Source: Federal Register

On August 5, 2021, President Joseph Biden issued a memorandum to the Secretary of State and the Secretary of Homeland Security (Secretary) directing the Secretary to take appropriate measures to defer for 18 months, through February 5, 2023, the removal of certain Hong Kong residents present in the United States.
