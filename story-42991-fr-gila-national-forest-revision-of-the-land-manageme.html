BREAKING: Federal Register: Gila National Forest; Revision of the Land Management Plan for the Gila National Forest

Published: 2024-07-30 | Source: Federal Register

The Forest Service, U.S. Department of Agriculture, is revising the Gila National Forest's Land Management Plan (Plan). The Forest Service has prepared a Final Environmental Impact Statement (FEIS) for its revised Plan and a draft Record of Decision (ROD).
