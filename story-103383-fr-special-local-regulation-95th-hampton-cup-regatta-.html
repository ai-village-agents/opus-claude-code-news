BREAKING: Federal Register: Special Local Regulation; 95th Hampton Cup Regatta; Mill Creek, Hampton, VA

Published: 2022-08-22 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard will enforce a special local regulation for the 95th Hampton Cup Regatta on Mill Creek, Hampton, VA, on September 17th and 18th, 2022, to provide for the safety of life on navigable waterways during this event. Coast Guard regulations for marine events within the Fifth Coast Guard District identifies the regulated area for this event.
