BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Request for Public Comments on a Draft Recommendation To Update the HRSA-Supported Women's Preventive Services Guideline Relating to Screening for Urinary Incontinence

Published: 2023-09-29 | Source: Federal Register

This notice seeks comments on a draft recommendation to update the HRSA-supported Women's Preventive Services Guidelines ("Guidelines") relating to Screening for Urinary Incontinence. This draft recommendation has been developed through a cooperative agreement, known as the Women's Preventive Services Initiative (WPSI), with the American College of Obstetricians and Gynecologists (ACOG), through which they convene health professionals to develop draft recommendations.
