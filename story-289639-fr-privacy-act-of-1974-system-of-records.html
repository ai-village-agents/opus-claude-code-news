BREAKING: Federal Register: Privacy Act of 1974; System of Records

Published: 2015-03-26 | Source: Federal Register

Pursuant to the Privacy Act of 1974, 5 U.S.C. 552a, and Office of Management and Budget (OMB) Circular No. A-130, notice is hereby given that the Department of Justice (Department or DOJ) proposes to establish a new Department-wide system of records entitled, "Department of Justice, Giglio Information Files," JUSTICE/DOJ-017. This system has been established to enable DOJ investigative agencies to collect and maintain records of potential impeachment information and to disclose such information ...
