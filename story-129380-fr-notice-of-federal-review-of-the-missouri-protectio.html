BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Federal Review of the Missouri Protection and Advocacy System (P&A)

Published: 2021-07-29 | Source: Federal Register

Representatives of the Administration on Disabilities (AoD), Administration for Community Living (ACL), will be conducting a federal review of the Missouri Protection and Advocacy System (P&A) on September 13-17, 2021. AoD is soliciting comments from interested parties on your experiences with the program, and strategies employed by P&A in meeting the needs of individuals with developmental disabilities and their families in Missouri.
