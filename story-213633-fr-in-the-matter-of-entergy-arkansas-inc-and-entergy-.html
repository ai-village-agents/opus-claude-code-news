BREAKING: Federal Register: In the Matter of Entergy Arkansas, Inc. and Entergy Operations, Inc. Arkansas Nuclear One, Units 1 and 2, and Independent Spent Fuel Storage Installation Facility

DPR-51 and NPF-6 for Arkansas Nuclear One, Units 1 and 2, and the general license for the independent spent fuel storage installation facility, to a new limited liability company named Entergy Arkansas, LLC.
