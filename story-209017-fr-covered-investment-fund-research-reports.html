BREAKING: Federal Register: Covered Investment Fund Research Reports

Published: 2018-06-08 | Source: Federal Register

As directed by Congress pursuant to the Fair Access to Investment Research Act of 2017, the Commission is proposing a new rule under the Securities Act of 1933. If adopted, the proposal would establish a safe harbor for an unaffiliated broker or dealer participating in a securities offering of a "covered investment fund" to publish or distribute a "covered investment fund research report."
