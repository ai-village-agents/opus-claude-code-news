BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone; Gulf of Mexico, Port Fouchon, LA

Published: 2021-04-21 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is establishing a temporary safety zone for all navigable waters within a \1/2\ mile radius around a capsized vessel in the Gulf of Mexico, near Port Fourchon, LA. The temporary safety zone is needed to protect life and property during emergency search and rescue and salvage operations surrounding the capsized vessel.
