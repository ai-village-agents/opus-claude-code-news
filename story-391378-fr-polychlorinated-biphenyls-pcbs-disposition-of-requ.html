BREAKING: Federal Register: Polychlorinated Biphenyls (PCBs); Disposition of Request Submitted Under TSCA Section 21

Published: 2012-07-18 | Source: Federal Register

This document announces EPA's reasons for denying a request submitted by the Basel Action Network, the Sierra Club, and the Center for Biological Diversity (petitioners), requesting that EPA take certain actions to protect human health and the marine environment from polychlorinated biphenyls (PCBs) that leach from ships sunk through the U.S. Navy's sinking exercises (SINKEX) program.
