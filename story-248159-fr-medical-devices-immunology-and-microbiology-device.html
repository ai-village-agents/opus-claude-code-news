BREAKING: Federal Register: Medical Devices; Immunology and Microbiology Devices; Classification of the Genetic Health Risk Assessment System

Published: 2017-11-07 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA, the Agency, or we) is classifying the genetic health risk assessment system into class II (special controls). The special controls that apply to the device type are identified in this order and will be part of the codified language for the genetic health risk assessment system's classification.
