BREAKING: Federal Register: Wooden Cabinets and Vanities and Components Thereof From the People's Republic of China: Recission of Antidumping Duty New Shipper Review; 2020

Published: 2021-11-12 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Commerce (Commerce) determines that Dalian Hualing Wood Co., Ltd. (Hualing) does not qualify as a new shipper. Therefore, we are rescinding this new shipper review (NSR).
