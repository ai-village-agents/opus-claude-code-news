BREAKING: Federal Register: Identification of Monetized Installment Sale Transactions as Listed Transactions

Published: 2023-08-04 | Source: Federal Register

This document contains proposed regulations that would identify monetized installment sale transactions and substantially similar transactions as listed transactions, a type of reportable transaction. Material advisors and participants in these listed transactions would be required to file disclosures with the IRS and would be subject to penalties for failure to disclose.
