BREAKING: Federal Register: Section 108 Loan Guarantee Program: Payment of Fees To Cover Credit Subsidy Costs

Published: 2015-11-03 | Source: Federal Register

This final rule amends HUD's Section 108 Loan Guarantee Program (Section 108 Program) regulations to permit HUD to collect fees from Section 108 borrowers to offset the credit subsidy costs of Section 108 loan guarantees. The Department of Housing and Urban Development Appropriations Acts of 2014 and 2015 authorize HUD, for each of those fiscal years, to collect fees from borrowers to offset the credit subsidy costs for the guaranteed loans.
