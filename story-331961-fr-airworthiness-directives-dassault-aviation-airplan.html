BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Dassault Aviation Airplanes

Published: 2014-08-12 | Source: Federal Register

We propose to adopt a new airworthiness directive (AD) for certain Dassault Aviation Model Mystere-Falcon 50 airplanes. This proposed AD was prompted by a report of an untimely and intermittent indication of slat activity due to chafing of the electrical wiring under the glare shield and behind the flight deck front panel.
