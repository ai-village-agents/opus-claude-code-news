BREAKING: Federal Register: Statewide Data Indicators and National Standards for Child and Family Services Reviews

Published: 2014-04-23 | Source: Federal Register

The Children's Bureau reviews a state's substantial conformity with titles IV-B and IV-E of the Social Security Act through the Child and Family Services Reviews (CFSRs). Statewide data indicators are used to inform the Children's Bureau's determination of a state's substantial conformity relative to certain safety and permanency outcomes.
