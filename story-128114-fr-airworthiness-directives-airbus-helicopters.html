BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Airbus Helicopters

Published: 2021-07-13 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA is superseding Airworthiness Directive (AD) 2017-17- 01, which applied to certain Airbus Helicopters Model AS332L2 and EC225LP helicopters. AD 2017-17-01 required repetitive inspections of the main rotor blade (MRB) attachment pins.
