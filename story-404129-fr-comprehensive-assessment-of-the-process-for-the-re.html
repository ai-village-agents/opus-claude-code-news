BREAKING: Federal Register: Comprehensive Assessment of the Process for the Review of Device Submissions; Request for Comments

Published: 2012-12-19 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA) is announcing an opportunity for public comment on the statement of work for an assessment of the process for the review of medical device submissions. The assessment is part of the FDA performance commitments relating to the Medical Device User Fee Amendments of 2012 (MDUFA III), which reauthorized device user fees for fiscal years 2013-2017.
