BREAKING: Federal Register: Television Broadcasting Services Fort Bragg, California

Published: 2022-01-21 | Source: Federal Register

On September 20, 2021, the Media Bureau, Video Division (Bureau) issued a Notice of Proposed Rulemaking (NPRM) in response to a petition for rulemaking filed by One Ministries, Inc. (Petitioner), requesting the allotment of reserved noncommercial educational channel *4 to Fort Bragg, California, in the Table of Allotments as the community's second local service.
