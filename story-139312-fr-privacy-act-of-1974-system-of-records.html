BREAKING: Federal Register: Privacy Act of 1974; System of Records

Published: 2021-12-03 | Source: Federal Register

Pursuant to the provisions of the Privacy Act of 1974, as amended, the FDIC is establishing FDIC-037, FDITECH Information. This system of records maintains information collected by FDITECH, FDIC's innovation arm. FDITECH is the focal point for the FDIC's efforts to promote responsible innovation in the financial services sector.
