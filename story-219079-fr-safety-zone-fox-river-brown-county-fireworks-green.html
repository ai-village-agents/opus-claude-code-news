BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone; Fox River, Brown County Fireworks, Green Bay, WI

Published: 2018-10-19 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is establishing a temporary safety zone on the Fox River in Green Bay, WI, for all navigable waters within a 210-foot radius of the approximate launch position at 44[deg]31.016' N, 088[deg]01.016' W (NAD 83). This action is necessary to protect spectators, mariners, vessels, and property from potential hazards associated with a fireworks display.
