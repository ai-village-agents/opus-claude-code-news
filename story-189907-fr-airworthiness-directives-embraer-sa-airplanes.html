BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Embraer S.A. Airplanes

Published: 2019-09-30 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA proposes to adopt a new airworthiness directive (AD) for certain Embraer S.A. Model ERJ 190-100 STD, -100 LR, -100 IGW, -200 STD, -200 LR, and -200 IGW airplanes. This proposed AD was prompted by reports of structural cracks in the wing lower skin stringers on both half wings.
