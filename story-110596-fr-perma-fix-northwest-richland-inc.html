BREAKING: Federal Register: Perma-Fix Northwest Richland, Inc.

Published: 2022-11-22 | Source: Federal Register

On October 17, 2022, the U.S. Nuclear Regulatory Commission (NRC) received an application to amend an export license (XW027/01), from Perma-Fix Northwest Richland, Inc. (PFNW), authorizing the export of low-level radioactive waste to Eckert and Ziegler Nuclitech GmbH located in Germany. The request seeks the NRC's approval to amend an existing license authorizing the export of radioactive waste to Germany.
