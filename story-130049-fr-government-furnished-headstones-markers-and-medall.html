BREAKING: Federal Register: Government-Furnished Headstones, Markers, and Medallions; Unmarked Graves

Published: 2021-08-06 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Veterans Affairs (VA) amends its regulations regarding the provision of Government-furnished headstones, markers, and medallions to eligible individuals.
