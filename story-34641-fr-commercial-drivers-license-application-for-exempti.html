BREAKING: Federal Register: Commercial Driver's License: Application for Exemption; Florida Department of Highway Safety and Motor Vehicles

Published: 2024-04-18 | Source: Federal Register

FMCSA announces its denial of an application for exemption, filed by the Florida Department of Highway Safety and Motor Vehicles (FLHSMV), from the commercial driver's license (CDL) skills testing regulation requiring the three-part CDL skills test to be administered and successfully completed in the following order: pre-trip inspection, basic vehicle control skills, and on-road skills.
