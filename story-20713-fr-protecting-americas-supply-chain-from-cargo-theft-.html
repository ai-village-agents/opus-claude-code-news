BREAKING: Federal Register: Protecting America's Supply Chain From Cargo Theft-Request for Information

Published: 2025-09-19 | Source: Federal Register

Cargo theft is a growing concern for the U.S. transportation system, costing the economy billions annually. These crimes involve opportunistic "straight thefts" of trailers, containers, and loads at truck stops or multimodal distribution hubs and highly coordinated operations conducted by organized criminal networks.
