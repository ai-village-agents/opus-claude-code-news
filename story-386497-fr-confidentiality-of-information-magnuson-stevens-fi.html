BREAKING: Federal Register: Confidentiality of Information; Magnuson-Stevens Fishery Conservation and Management Reauthorization Act

Published: 2012-05-23 | Source: Federal Register

The National Marine Fisheries Service (NMFS) proposes revisions to existing regulations governing the confidentiality of information submitted in compliance with any requirement or regulation under the Magnuson-Stevens Fishery Conservation and Management Act (Magnuson-Stevens Act or MSA).
