BREAKING: Federal Register: Modification and Expansion of CBP Centers of Excellence and Expertise Test To Include Six Additional Centers

Published: 2013-04-04 | Source: Federal Register

This document announces U.S. Customs and Border Protection's (CBP's) plan to modify and expand its test for the Centers of Excellence and Expertise (CEEs), originally published in the Federal Register on August 28, 2012.
