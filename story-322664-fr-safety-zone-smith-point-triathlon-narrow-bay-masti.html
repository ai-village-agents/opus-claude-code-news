BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone: Smith Point Triathlon, Narrow Bay; Mastic Beach, NY

Published: 2014-04-25 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard proposes to establish a temporary safety zone on the navigable waters of Narrow Bay near Smith Point Park in Mastic Beach, NY for the swim event associated with the Smith Point Triathlon. The safety zone will provide protective measures for the event participants and the maritime public from the hazards associated with vessels operating in close proximity to the swim event.
