BREAKING: Federal Register: Protecting the Public and Our Employees in Our Hearing Process

Published: 2012-02-23 | Source: Federal Register

We are clarifying our regulations to ensure the safety of the public and our employees in our hearing process. Due to increasing reports of threats to our hearing office employees, we are taking steps to explicitly increase the level of protection we provide to our staff and to the public during the hearing process.
