BREAKING: Federal Register: Special Conditions: Boeing Model 787-9 Airplane; Structure-Mounted Airbags

Published: 2015-11-10 | Source: Federal Register

These special conditions are issued for the Boeing Model 787-9 airplane. This airplane will have a novel or unusual design feature when compared to the state of technology envisioned in the airworthiness standards for transport-category airplanes. This design feature is airbags mounted to structure to prevent serious injury.
