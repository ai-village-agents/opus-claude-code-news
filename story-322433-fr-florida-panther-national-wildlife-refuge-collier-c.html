BREAKING: Federal Register: Florida Panther National Wildlife Refuge, Collier County, Florida

Published: 2014-04-23 | Source: Federal Register

We, the Fish and Wildlife Service (Service), intend to prepare a comprehensive conservation plan (CCP) revision and associated National Environmental Policy Act (NEPA) documents for Florida Panther National Wildlife Refuge (NWR), located in Collier County in southwest Florida.
