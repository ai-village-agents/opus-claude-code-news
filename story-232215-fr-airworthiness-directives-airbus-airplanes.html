BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Airbus Airplanes

Published: 2017-04-17 | Source: Federal Register

We are adopting a new airworthiness directive (AD) for all Airbus Model A318, A319, A320-211, -212, -214, -231, -232, and -233 airplanes, and A321 series airplanes. This AD was prompted by the discovery of corroded circlips in fuel vent protectors (FVP) having a certain part number.
