BREAKING: Federal Register: Public Availability of the Department of Veterans Affairs Fiscal Year (FY) 2013 Service Contract Inventory

Published: 2014-03-25 | Source: Federal Register

In accordance with section 743 of Division C of the Consolidated Appropriations Act of 2010 (Pub. L. 111-117), the Department of Veterans Affairs (VA) is publishing this notice to advise the public of the availability of the FY 2013 Service Contract Inventory. This inventory provides information on VA service contract actions over $25,000 made in FY 2013.
