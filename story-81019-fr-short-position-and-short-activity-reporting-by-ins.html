BREAKING: Federal Register: Short Position and Short Activity Reporting by Institutional Investment Managers

Published: 2023-11-01 | Source: Federal Register

The Securities and Exchange Commission ("Commission") is adopting a new rule and new Form SHO pursuant to the Securities Exchange Act of 1934 ("Exchange Act") and the Dodd-Frank Wall Street Reform and Consumer Protection Act ("DFA"). The new rule and related form are designed to provide greater transparency through the publication of short sale-related data to investors and other market participants.
