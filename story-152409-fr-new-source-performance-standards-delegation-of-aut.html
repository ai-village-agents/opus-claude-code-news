BREAKING: Federal Register: New Source Performance Standards; Delegation of Authority to Oklahoma

Published: 2020-05-26 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is proposing to update the Code of Federal Regulations (CFR) to reflect Oklahoma's current New Source Performance Standards (NSPS) delegation status and mailing address for the Oklahoma Department of Environmental Quality (ODEQ). The ODEQ has submitted updated regulations for delegation of the EPA authority for implementation and enforcement of certain NSPS.
