BREAKING: Federal Register: Trichloroethylene; Regulation Under the Toxic Substances Control Act (TSCA); Compliance Date Extension

Published: 2025-09-17 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA or Agency) is taking interim final action on the Regulation of Trichloroethylene (TCE) under the Toxic Substances Control Act (TSCA) to revise certain compliance deadlines finalized in 2024.
