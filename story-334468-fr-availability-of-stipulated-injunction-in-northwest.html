BREAKING: Federal Register: Availability of Stipulated Injunction in Northwest Center for Alternatives to Pesticides v. EPA litigation

Published: 2014-09-10 | Source: Federal Register

This notice announces to the public the availability of an Order (stipulated injunction) issued by the U.S. District Court for the Western District of Washington that, among other things, would reinstitute streamside no-spray buffer zones to protect endangered or threatened Pacific salmon and steelhead in California, Oregon, and Washington.
