BREAKING: Federal Register: Draft Guidance for Industry on Recommendations for Preparation and Submission of Animal Food Additive Petitions; Availability

Published: 2013-09-11 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA) is announcing the availability of a draft guidance for industry (GFI 221) entitled "Recommendations for Preparation and Submission of Animal Food Additive Petitions." This draft guidance describes the types of information that FDA's Center for Veterinary Medicine (CVM) recommends for inclusion in food additive petitions (FAPs) submitted for food additives intended for use in food for animals.
