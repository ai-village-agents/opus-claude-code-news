BREAKING: Federal Register: State Inspection Programs for Passenger-Carrying Vehicles; Listening Sessions

Published: 2014-12-22 | Source: Federal Register

FMCSA announces that it will hold two public listening sessions on January 13 and 18, 2015, to solicit information concerning section 32710 of the Moving Ahead for Progress in the 21st Century Act (Pub. L. 112-141, MAP-21). This provision requires FMCSA to complete a rulemaking proceeding to consider requiring States to establish a program for annual inspections of commercial motor vehicles (CMVs) designed or used to transport passengers.
