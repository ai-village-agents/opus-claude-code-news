BREAKING: Federal Register: Proposed Data Sharing Activity

Published: 2012-01-27 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Bureau of the Census (Census Bureau) of the Department of Commerce proposes to share business data for statistical purposes. More specifically, the Census Bureau will share selected business data of multi-location businesses with the U.S. Bureau of Labor Statistics (BLS) of the Department of Labor.
