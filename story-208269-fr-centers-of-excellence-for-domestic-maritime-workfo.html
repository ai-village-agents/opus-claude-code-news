BREAKING: Federal Register: Centers of Excellence for Domestic Maritime Workforce Training and Education

Published: 2018-05-31 | Source: Federal Register

This notice provides interested parties with the opportunity to comment on the Maritime Administration's (MARAD) application process to designate training entities as Centers of Excellence for Domestic Maritime Workforce Training and Education (CoE). The Secretary of Transportation, acting through the Maritime Administrator, may make CoE designations and enter Cooperative Agreements with CoE designees.
