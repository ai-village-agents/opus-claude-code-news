BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Intent of Waiver With Respect to Land; Indianapolis Metropolitan Airport, Indianapolis, Indiana

Published: 2020-03-06 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA is considering a proposal to change 210.253 acres of airport land from aeronautical use to non-aeronautical use of airport property located at Indianapolis Metropolitan Airport, Indianapolis, Indiana. The aforementioned land is not needed for aeronautical use. The land is made up of four parcels.
