BREAKING: Federal Register: Importation of Kiwi From Chile Into the United States

Published: 2015-09-17 | Source: Federal Register

We are amending the fruits and vegetables regulations to list kiwi (Actinidia deliciosa and Actinidia chinensis) from Chile as eligible for importation into the United States subject to a systems approach. Under this systems approach, the fruit will have to be grown in a place of production that is registered with the Government of Chile and certified as having a low prevalence of Brevipalpus chilensis.
