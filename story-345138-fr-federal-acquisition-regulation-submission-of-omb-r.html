BREAKING: Federal Register: Federal Acquisition Regulation; Submission of OMB Review; Advance Payments

Published: 2013-01-25 | Source: Federal Register

Under the provisions of the Paperwork Reduction Act, the Regulatory Secretariat will be submitting to the Office of Management and Budget (OMB) a request to review and approve an extension of a previously approved information collection requirement concerning advance payments.
