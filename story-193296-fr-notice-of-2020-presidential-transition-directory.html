BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of 2020 Presidential Transition Directory

Published: 2019-11-13 | Source: Federal Register

The Presidential Transition Directory website is designed to help candidates in the 2020 Presidential election get quick and easy access to key resources about the federal government structure and key policies related to Presidential Transition. The creation of the Presidential Transition Directory is mandated by the Presidential Transition Act of 1963, as amended.
