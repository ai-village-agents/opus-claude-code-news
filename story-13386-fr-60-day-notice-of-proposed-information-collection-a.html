BREAKING: Federal Register: 60-Day Notice of Proposed Information Collection: Application for the Community Development Block Grant (ICDBG) Program for Indian

Published: 2025-06-11 | Source: Federal Register

HUD is seeking approval from the Office of Management and Budget (OMB) for the information collection described below. The purpose of this notice is to allow for 60 days of public comment.
