BREAKING: Federal Register: Omaha Public Power District; Fort Calhoun Station, Unit No. 1

Published: 2015-04-13 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Nuclear Regulatory Commission (NRC) has granted the request of Omaha Public Power District to withdraw its application dated April 25, 2014, as supplemented by letter dated February 13, 2015, for a proposed amendment to Renewed Facility Operating License No. DPR-40.
