BREAKING: Federal Register: Use of Truncated Taxpayer Identification Numbers on Forms W-2, Wage and Tax Statement, Furnished to Employees

Published: 2017-09-20 | Source: Federal Register

This document contains proposed amendments to the regulations under sections 6051 and 6052 of the Internal Revenue Code (Code).
