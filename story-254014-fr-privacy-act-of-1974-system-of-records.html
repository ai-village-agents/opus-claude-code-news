BREAKING: Federal Register: Privacy Act of 1974; System of Records

Published: 2016-01-27 | Source: Federal Register

The National Guard Bureau proposes to add a new system of records, INGB 005, entitled "Special Investigation Reports and Files". Information is collected and maintained for the purpose of conducting investigations on allegations of sexual assault, fraud, or other complex incidents involving National Guard forces when requested by an Adjutant General of a State, Territory, or the District of Columbia or by other appropriate authority and approved IAW Chief of the National Guard Bureau authorities...
