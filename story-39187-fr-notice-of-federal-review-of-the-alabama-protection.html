BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Federal Review of the Alabama Protection and Advocacy System (P&A)

Published: 2024-06-12 | Source: Federal Register

Representatives of the Administration on Disabilities (AoD), Administration for Community Living (ACL), will be conducting a federal review of the Alabama Protection and Advocacy System (P&A), Alabama Disability Advocacy Program (ADAP) on July 22-26, 2024. AoD is soliciting comments from interested parties on your experiences with the program, and strategies employed by the P&A ADAP in meeting the needs of individuals with developmental disabilities and their families in Alabama.
