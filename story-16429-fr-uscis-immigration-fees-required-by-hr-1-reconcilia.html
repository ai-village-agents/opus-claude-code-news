BREAKING: Federal Register: USCIS Immigration Fees Required by HR-1 Reconciliation Bill

Published: 2025-07-22 | Source: Federal Register

U.S. Citizenship and Immigration Services (USCIS) is announcing a series of fees to be collected by USCIS. Recently enacted legislation that provided for reconciliation pursuant to Title II of House Concurrent Resolution 14, titled HR-1, establishes specific fees for various immigration-related forms, benefits, statuses, petitions, applications, and requests administered by multiple government agencies.
