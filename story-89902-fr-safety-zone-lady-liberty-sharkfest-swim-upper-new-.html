BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone; Lady Liberty Sharkfest Swim, Upper New York Harbor, Liberty Island, NY

Published: 2022-03-01 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard proposes to establish a temporary safety zone on the navigable waters of Upper New York Bay, in the vicinity of Liberty Island, within a 100-yard radius of each swimmer during the Lady Liberty Sharkfest Swim on July 16, 2022. The safety zone is needed to protect the maritime public and event participants from the hazards associated with swim events taking place in a high vessel traffic area.
