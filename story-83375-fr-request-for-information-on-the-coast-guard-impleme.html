BREAKING: Federal Register: Request for Information on the Coast Guard Implementation of a Western Alaska Oil Spill Planning Criteria Program

Published: 2023-12-04 | Source: Federal Register

The Don Young Coast Guard Authorization Act of 2022 mandated the Coast Guard create planning criteria for vessel response plans (VRPs) distinct to the Western Alaska and Prince William Sound Captain of the Port zones. These criteria must include minimum response times, improvements to wildlife response, and consideration of prevention and mitigation measures.
