BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; The Boeing Company Airplanes

Published: 2013-10-31 | Source: Federal Register

We are adopting a new airworthiness directive (AD) for certain The Boeing Company Model 747-400 series airplanes. This AD was prompted by reports of fasteners missing on an airplane undergoing a passenger- to-freighter conversion. This AD requires doing a general visual inspection of the station 1920 splice clip for correct fastener installation, and related investigative and corrective actions if necessary.
