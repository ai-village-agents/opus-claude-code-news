BREAKING: Federal Register: Federal Travel Regulation (FTR); Relocation Allowance-Relocation Income Tax (RIT) Allowable Tables

Published: 2013-05-07 | Source: Federal Register

The GSA published FTR Amendment 2008-04, in the Federal Register on June 25, 2008 (73 FR 35952), specifying that GSA would no longer publish the RIT Allowance tables in Title 41 of the Code of Federal Regulation (CFR) part 302-17, Appendices A through D; instead, the tables would be available on a GSA Web site.
