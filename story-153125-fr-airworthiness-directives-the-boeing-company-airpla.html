BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; The Boeing Company Airplanes

Published: 2020-06-03 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA proposes to adopt a new airworthiness directive (AD) for all The Boeing Company Model 737 series airplanes, excluding Model 737-100, -200, -200C, -300, -400, and -500 series airplanes. This proposed AD was prompted by reports of cracked or completely severed lugs in the upper aft corner stop fitting assembly of the forward entry door.
