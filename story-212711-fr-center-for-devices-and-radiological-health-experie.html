BREAKING: Federal Register: Center for Devices and Radiological Health: Experiential Learning Program

Published: 2018-07-30 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration's (FDA) Center for Devices and Radiological Health (CDRH or Center) is announcing the 2019 Experiential Learning Program (ELP). This training is intended to provide CDRH and other FDA staff with an opportunity to understand laboratory practices, quality system management, patient perspective/ input, and challenges that impact the medical device development life cycle.
