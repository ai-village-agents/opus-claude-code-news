BREAKING: Federal Register: Informed Consent: Guidance for Institutional Review Boards, Clinical Investigators, and Sponsors; Availability

Published: 2023-08-16 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA or Agency) is announcing the availability of a final guidance for industry entitled "Informed Consent: Guidance for Institutional Review Boards, Clinical Investigators, and Sponsors." The guidance announced in this notice is intended to assist institutional review boards (IRBs), clinical investigators, and sponsors involved in clinical investigations of FDA- regulated products in carrying out their responsibilities related to informed consent.
