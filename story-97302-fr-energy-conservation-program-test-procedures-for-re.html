BREAKING: Federal Register: Energy Conservation Program: Test Procedures for Residential and Commercial Clothes Washers

Published: 2022-06-01 | Source: Federal Register

This final rule amends the U.S. Department of Energy's ("DOE") test procedures for residential and commercial clothes washers to further specify test conditions, instrument specifications, and test settings; address large clothes container capacities; add product-specific enforcement provisions; delete obsolete provisions; and consolidate all test cloth-related provisions and codify additional test cloth material verification procedures used by industry.
