BREAKING: Federal Register: Public Hearing for Greenhouse Gas Emissions Standards for Heavy-Duty Vehicles-Phase 3

Published: 2023-04-17 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is announcing a two- day virtual public hearing to be held May 2 and May 3, 2023, on its proposal titled "Greenhouse Gas Emissions Standards for Heavy-Duty Vehicles--Phase 3," which was signed by Administrator Regan on April 11, 2023. An additional session may be held on May 4, 2023, if necessary to accommodate the number of testifiers that sign up to testify.
