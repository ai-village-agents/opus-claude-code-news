BREAKING: Federal Register: Certain Uncoated Paper From Portugal: Preliminary Results of Antidumping Duty Administrative Review; 2015-2017

Published: 2018-04-06 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Commerce (Commerce) preliminarily determines that certain uncoated paper (uncoated paper) from Portugal is not being, or is not likely to be sold, at less than normal value during the period of review (POR), August 26, 2015, through February 28, 2017.
