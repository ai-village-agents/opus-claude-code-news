BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Diamond Aircraft Industries GmbH Airplanes

Published: 2017-08-01 | Source: Federal Register

We are adopting a new airworthiness directive (AD) for certain Diamond Aircraft Industries GmbH Model DA 42 airplanes. This AD requires installing engine exhaust pipe clamps with spring washers, repetitively inspecting the engine exhaust pipe clamps for cracks, and replacing the clamps if found cracked.
