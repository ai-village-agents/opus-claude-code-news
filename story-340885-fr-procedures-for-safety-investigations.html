BREAKING: Federal Register: Procedures for Safety Investigations

Published: 2014-12-01 | Source: Federal Register

The Defense Nuclear Facilities Safety Board (Board) is promulgating a final rule which establishes procedures for conducting preliminary and formal safety investigations of events or practices at Department of Energy (DOE) defense nuclear facilities that the Board determines have adversely affected, or may adversely affect, public health and safety.
