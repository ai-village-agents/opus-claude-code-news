BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Public Meeting of the Louisiana Advisory Committee To Discuss the Selection of a Civil Rights Topic in Louisiana for the Committee's Next Project

Published: 2020-02-10 | Source: Federal Register

Notice is hereby given, pursuant to the provisions of the rules and regulations of the U.S. Commission on Civil Rights (Commission) and the Federal Advisory Committee Act that the Louisiana Advisory Committee (Committee) will hold a meeting on Friday, February 21, 2020 at 12:00 p.m. (Central) for discussions on civil rights topics in Louisiana.
