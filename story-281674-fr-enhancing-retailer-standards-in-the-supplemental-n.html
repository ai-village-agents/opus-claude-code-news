BREAKING: Federal Register: Enhancing Retailer Standards in the Supplemental Nutrition Assistance Program (SNAP)

Published: 2016-12-15 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Nutrition Service (FNS or the Agency) is updating Supplemental Nutrition Assistance Program (SNAP or the Program) regulations pertaining to the eligibility criteria for retail food stores to participate in the Program by finalizing a proposed rule that was published on February 17, 2016.
