BREAKING: Federal Register: Medical Devices; General and Plastic Surgery Devices; Classification of the Light Based Energy Source Device for Topical Application

Published: 2018-10-19 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA or we) is classifying the light based energy source device for topical application into class II (special controls). The special controls that apply to the device type are identified in this order and will be part of the codified language for the light based energy source device for topical application's classification.
