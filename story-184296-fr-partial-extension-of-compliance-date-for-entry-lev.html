BREAKING: Federal Register: Partial Extension of Compliance Date for Entry-Level Driver Training

Published: 2019-07-18 | Source: Federal Register

FMCSA proposes to amend its December 8, 2016, final rule, "Minimum Training Requirements for Entry-Level Commercial Motor Vehicle Operators" (ELDT final rule), by extending the compliance date for two provisions from the rule.
