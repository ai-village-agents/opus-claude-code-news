BREAKING: Federal Register: Electronic Fund Transfers (Regulation E); Correction

Published: 2012-07-10 | Source: Federal Register

The Bureau of Consumer Financial Protection (Bureau) is correcting a final rule with an official interpretation (Final Rule) that appeared in the Federal Register of February 7, 2012. The Final Rule provides new protections, including disclosures and error resolution and cancellation rights, to consumers who send remittance transfers to other consumers or businesses in a foreign country.
