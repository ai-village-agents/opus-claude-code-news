BREAKING: Federal Register: Measuring, Evaluating, and Reporting Radioactive Material in Liquid and Gaseous Effluents and Solid Waste

Published: 2021-01-05 | Source: Federal Register

The Nuclear Regulatory Commission (NRC) is issuing for public comment draft regulatory guide (DG), DG-1377, "Measuring, Evaluating, and Reporting Radioactive Material in Liquid and Gaseous Effluents and Solid Waste." This DG is proposed revision 3 of Regulatory Guide (RG) 1.21 of the same name.
