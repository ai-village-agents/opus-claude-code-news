BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Affirmation of Addition of a Treatment Schedule for Methyl Bromide Fumigation of Blueberries

Published: 2013-11-13 | Source: Federal Register

We are affirming our earlier determination that it was necessary to immediately add to the Plant Protection and Quarantine Treatment Manual a treatment schedule for methyl bromide fumigation of blueberries for Mediterranean fruit fly and South American fruit fly.
