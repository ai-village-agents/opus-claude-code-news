BREAKING: Federal Register: Energy Conservation Standards for Residential Boilers: Availability of Analytical Results and Modeling Tools

Published: 2014-02-11 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Department of Energy (DOE) has completed a provisional analysis that estimates the potential economic impacts and energy savings that could result from promulgating amended energy conservation standards for residential boilers. At this time, DOE is not proposing any amendments to the energy conservation standards for residential boilers.
