BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone and Regulated Navigation Area; Chicago Sanitary and Ship Canal, Romeoville, IL

Published: 2013-07-15 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is issuing this Interim Rule to address two omissions from the regulatory text of the Safety zone and Regulated Navigation Area in the Chicago Sanitary and Ship Canal, Romeoville, IL. These omissions include requirements for the regulated navigation area that vessels must be greater than twenty feet in length and must not be a personal or human powered watercraft of any kind (e.g. jet skis, wave runners, kayaks, row boats, etc.).
