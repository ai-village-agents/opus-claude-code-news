BREAKING: Federal Register: Miscellaneous Corrections

Published: 2022-04-08 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Nuclear Regulatory Commission (NRC) is amending its regulations to make miscellaneous corrections. These changes include correcting an office title, a reference, a misspelling, and two administrative errors, and updating the street address for the NRC's Region I office. This document is necessary to inform the public of these non-substantive amendments to the NRC's regulations.
