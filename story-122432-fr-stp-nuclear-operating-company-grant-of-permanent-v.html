BREAKING: Federal Register: STP Nuclear Operating Company; Grant of Permanent Variance

Published: 2021-04-28 | Source: Federal Register

In this notice, OSHA grants a permanent variance to South Texas Project Nuclear Operating Company (STP Nuclear) from the OSHA standard that requires the isolation of permit-required confined spaces.
