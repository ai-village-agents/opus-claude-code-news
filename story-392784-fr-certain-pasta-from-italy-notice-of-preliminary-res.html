BREAKING: Federal Register: Certain Pasta From Italy: Notice of Preliminary Results of Antidumping Duty Administrative Review, Preliminary No Shipment Determination and Preliminary Intent To Revoke Order, in Part

Published: 2012-08-03 | Source: Federal Register

In response to requests by interested parties, the Department of Commerce ("the Department") is conducting an administrative review of the antidumping duty order on certain pasta ("pasta") from Italy for the period of review ("POR") July 1, 2010, through June 30, 2011. This review covers the following seven companies: Botticelli Mediterraneo S.a.r.l. ("Botticelli"), Fiamma Vesuviana S.r.L. ("Fiamma"), Industria Alimentare Filiberto Bianconi 1947 S.p.A.
