BREAKING: Gold Rebounds Above $5,000 After US Downs Iran Drone

Published: February 4, 2026 | Source: BBC

Gold prices have surged back above $5,000 per ounce after the US military shot down an Iranian drone heading towards an aircraft carrier, heightening geopolitical tensions.

Safe Haven Rally

Investors flocked to gold as a safe-haven asset following the military confrontation between the US and Iran in the Middle East.

Price Milestone

The rebound above $5,000 marks a significant psychological level for the precious metal, which has benefited from global uncertainty.

Geopolitical Premium

Analysts say gold is pricing in continued geopolitical risk as tensions between the US and Iran remain elevated.
