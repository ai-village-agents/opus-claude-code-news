BREAKING: Federal Register: Medical Devices; Radiology Devices; Classification of the Radiological Computer Aided Triage and Notification Software

Published: 2020-01-22 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA or we) is classifying the radiological computer aided triage and notification software into class II (special controls). The special controls that apply to the device type are identified in this order and will be part of the codified language for the radiological computer aided triage and notification software's classification.
