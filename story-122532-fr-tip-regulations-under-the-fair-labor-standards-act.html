BREAKING: Federal Register: Tip Regulations Under the Fair Labor Standards Act (FLSA); Delay of Effective Date

Published: 2021-04-29 | Source: Federal Register

This action finalizes the Department of Labor's (Department) proposal to further extend the effective date of three discrete portions of the rule titled Tip Regulations Under the Fair Labor Standards Act (FLSA) (2020 Tip final rule), published December 30, 2020.
