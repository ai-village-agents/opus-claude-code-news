BREAKING: Federal Register: Nonmetallic Thermal Insulation for Austenitic Stainless Steel

Published: 2014-10-06 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Nuclear Regulatory Commission (NRC) is issuing for public comment draft regulatory guide (DG), DG-1312, "Nonmetallic Thermal Insulation for Austenitic Stainless Steel," also known as Regulatory Guide (RG) 1.36. The NRC is proposing to revise the guidance toreflect the most current versions of voluntary consensus standards since the initial publication of RG 1.36 in February 1973.
