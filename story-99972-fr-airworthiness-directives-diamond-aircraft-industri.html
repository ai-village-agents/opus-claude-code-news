BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Diamond Aircraft Industries Inc. Airplanes

Published: 2022-07-07 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA is adopting a new airworthiness directive (AD) for all Diamond Aircraft Industries Inc. Model DA 40, DA 40 F, and DA 40 NG airplanes. This AD was prompted by mandatory continuing airworthiness information (MCAI) originated by an aviation authority of another country to identify and correct an unsafe condition on an aviation product.
