BREAKING: Federal Register: Publication of Russian Harmful Foreign Activities Sanctions Regulations Web General Licenses 45 and 46

Published: 2022-08-03 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of the Treasury's Office of Foreign Assets Control (OFAC) is publishing two general licenses (GLs) issued pursuant to the Russian Harmful Foreign Activities Sanctions Regulations: GL 45 and GL 46, each of which was previously issued on OFAC's website.
