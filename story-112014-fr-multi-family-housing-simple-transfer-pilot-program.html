BREAKING: Federal Register: Multi-Family Housing Simple Transfer Pilot Program

Published: 2022-12-09 | Source: Federal Register

The Rural Housing Service (RHS or the Agency), a Rural Development (RD) agency of the United States Department of Agriculture (USDA), is announcing the implementation of a pilot program for simple transfers of USDA Section 515 Rural Rental Housing properties.
