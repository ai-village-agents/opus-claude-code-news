BREAKING: Federal Register: Special Conditions: Embraer S.A., Model EMB-550 Airplane; Interaction of Systems and Structures

Published: 2012-11-28 | Source: Federal Register

This action proposes special conditions for the Embraer S.A. Model EMB-550 airplane. This airplane will have a novel or unusual design feature(s) associated with the interaction of systems and structures. The applicable airworthiness regulations do not contain adequate or appropriate safety standards for this design feature.
