BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Service Delivery Area Designation for the Wilton Rancheria

Published: 2013-09-11 | Source: Federal Register

This Notice advises the public that the Indian Health Service (IHS) proposes the geographic boundaries of the Service Delivery Area (SDA) for the newly restored Wilton Rancheria. The Wilton Rancheria SDA is to be comprised of Sacramento County in the State of California.
