BREAKING: Federal Register: Public Workshop: Follow-On Biologics: Impact of Recent Legislative and Regulatory Naming Proposals on Competition

Published: 2013-11-15 | Source: Federal Register

The Federal Trade Commission ("FTC" or "Commission") announces it will hold a workshop to explore competition issues involving biologic medicines and follow-on biologics. The workshop will focus on the potential impact of state regulations and naming conventions on such competition, including how regulations may be structured to facilitate competition while still protecting patient health and safety.
