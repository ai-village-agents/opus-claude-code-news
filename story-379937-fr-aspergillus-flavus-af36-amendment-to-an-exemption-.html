BREAKING: Federal Register: Aspergillus flavus AF36; Amendment to an Exemption From the Requirement of a Tolerance

Published: 2012-03-09 | Source: Federal Register

This regulation amends the existing temporary tolerance exemption for Aspergillus flavus AF36 by establishing a permanent exemption from the requirement of a tolerance for residues of the microbial pesticide, Aspergillus flavus AF36, in or on pistachio when applied as an antifungal agent and used in accordance with good agricultural practices.
