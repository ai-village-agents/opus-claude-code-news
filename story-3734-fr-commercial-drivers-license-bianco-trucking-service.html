BREAKING: Federal Register: Commercial Driver's License: Bianco Trucking Services, LLC d.b.a. CDL and Operator Training; Application for Exemption

Published: 2025-01-15 | Source: Federal Register

The Federal Motor Carrier Safety Administration (FMCSA) requests public comment on the application from Bianco Trucking Services, LLC d.b.a. CDL and Operator Training ("Bianco") for an exemption to allow drivers aged 18, 19, and 20 years with a Wisconsin State-issued commercial learners permit (CLP) and a "K" restriction on their CLP to attend its driver training facility in Michigan.
