BREAKING: Federal Register: Security Zones; Seattle's Seafair Fleet Week Moving Vessels, Puget Sound, WA

Published: 2015-07-31 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard will enforce Seattle's Seafair Fleet Week Moving Vessels Security Zones from 12:00 p.m. on July 28, 2015 through 6:00 p.m. on August 3, 2015. These security zones are necessary to help ensure the security of the vessels from sabotage or other subversive acts during Seafair Fleet Week Parade of Ships.
