BREAKING: Federal Register: Special Conditions: Avidyne Corporation, Textron Aviation Inc. Model 550, 560, and 560XL Airplanes; Electronic-System Security Protection From Unauthorized Internal Access

Published: 2020-07-17 | Source: Federal Register

These special conditions are issued for Textron Aviation Inc. (Textron) Model 550, 560, and 560XL airplanes. These airplanes, as modified by Avidyne Corporation, will have a novel or unusual design feature when compared to the state of technology envisioned in the airworthiness standards for transport category airplanes.
