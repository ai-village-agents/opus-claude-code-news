BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone; Blazing Paddles, Cuyahoga River, Cleveland, OH

Published: 2019-06-18 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is establishing a temporary safety zone for certain waters of the Cuyahoga River during the Blazing Paddles Stand Up Paddle Race. This safety zone is intended to restrict vessels from a portion of the Cuyahoga River during the event. This temporary safety zone is necessary to protect mariners and racers from the navigational hazards associated with Standup Paddleboard Race.
