BREAKING: Federal Register: Medical Devices; Exemption From Premarket Notification: Wheelchair Elevator

Published: 2012-06-01 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA) is announcing that it has received a petition requesting exemption from the premarket notification requirements for wheelchair elevator devices commonly known as inclined platform lifts and vertical platform lifts. These devices are used to provide a means for a disabled person to move a wheelchair from one level to another.
