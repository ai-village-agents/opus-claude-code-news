BREAKING: Federal Register: Atlantic Highly Migratory Species; 2017 Atlantic Shark Commercial Fishing Season

Published: 2016-11-23 | Source: Federal Register

This final rule establishes the opening date for all Atlantic shark fisheries, including the fisheries in the Gulf of Mexico and Caribbean. This final rule also establishes the quotas for the 2017 fishing season based on over- and/or underharvests experienced during 2016 and previous fishing seasons.
