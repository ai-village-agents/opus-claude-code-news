BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Bombardier, Inc., Airplanes

Published: 2020-02-12 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA is superseding Airworthiness Directive (AD) 2003-09-04 R1, which applied to certain Bombardier, Inc., Model CL-600-2B19 (Regional Jet series 100 & 440) airplanes. AD 2003-09-04 R1 required revising the airworthiness limitations for certain structural inspections; repair if necessary; and submission of inspection findings to the airplane manufacturer.
