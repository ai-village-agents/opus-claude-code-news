BREAKING: Federal Register: Formic Acid and Sodium Formate; Exemption From the Requirement of a Tolerance

Published: 2020-06-19 | Source: Federal Register

This regulation establishes exemptions from the requirement of a tolerance for residues of formic acid when used as an inert ingredient limited to 25% in pesticide formulations applied to growing crops pre- and post-harvest (adjuvant, pH buffering agent, or pH adjuster) and applied in/on animals (pH adjuster).
