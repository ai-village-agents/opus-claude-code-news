BREAKING: Federal Register: Exemption Permit for the M/V SUPPLIER or M/V TRANSPORTER

Published: 2025-06-04 | Source: Federal Register

Commander, Seventeenth Coast Guard District, has issued a permit exempting two vessels from requirements in the Code of Federal Regulations when transporting cargo, including bulk fuel, within Alaska. Permits temporarily waive vessel inspection requirements to facilitate the movement of cargo, including bulk fuel, to remote Alaskan sites when inspected cargo vessels are not reasonably available.
