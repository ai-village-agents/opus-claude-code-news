BREAKING: Federal Register: Periodic Reporting

Published: 2020-09-11 | Source: Federal Register

The Commission initiates an informal rulemaking proceeding to change how the Postal Service determines incremental costs and how it accounts for peak-season costs in its periodic reports. This notice informs the public of the filing, invites public comment, and takes other administrative steps.
