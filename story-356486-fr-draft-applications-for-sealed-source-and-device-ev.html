BREAKING: Federal Register: Draft Applications for Sealed Source and Device Evaluation and Registration

Published: 2013-06-04 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Nuclear Regulatory Commission (NRC) is revising its licensing guidance for applications for sealed source and device evaluation and registration. The NRC is requesting public comment on draft NUREG-1556, Volume 3, Revision 2, "Consolidated Guidance about Materials Licenses: Applications for Sealed Source and Device Evaluation and Registration."
