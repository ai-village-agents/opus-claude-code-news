BREAKING: Federal Register: Country Code Top-Level Domain (ccTLD) for the United States; Policies and Requirements; Comments Request

Published: 2013-01-31 | Source: Federal Register

The United States Department of Commerce's (Department) National Telecommunications and Information Administration (NTIA) administers the contract for the country code top-level domain (ccTLD) for the United States, ".us" (usTLD). The current contract expires on August 31, 2013. Given the expiration date of this contract, NTIA is seeking input from interested parties on the policies and requirements that should govern the usTLD.
