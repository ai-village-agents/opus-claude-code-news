BREAKING: Federal Register: Certain Polybrominated Diphenylethers; Significant New Use Rule and Test Rule; Extension of Comment Period

Published: 2012-05-24 | Source: Federal Register

EPA issued a proposed rule in the Federal Register of April 2, 2012, that would amend the Toxic Substances Control Act (TSCA) section 5(a) Significant New Use Rule (SNUR) for certain polybrominated diphenylethers (PBDEs), and that would require persons that manufacture, import, or process any of three commercial PBDEs, including in articles, for any use after December 31, 2013, to conduct testing under TSCA section 4(a).
