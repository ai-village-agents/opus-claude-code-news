BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Embraer S.A. Airplanes

Published: 2013-11-08 | Source: Federal Register

We are adopting a new airworthiness directive (AD) for Embraer S.A. Model EMB-505 airplanes. This AD results from mandatory continuing airworthiness information (MCAI) issued by the aviation authority of another country to identify and correct an unsafe condition on an aviation product.
