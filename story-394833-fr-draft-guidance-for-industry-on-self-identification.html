BREAKING: Federal Register: Draft Guidance for Industry on Self-Identification of Generic Drug Facilities, Sites, and Organizations; Availability

Published: 2012-08-27 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA) is announcing the availability of a draft guidance for industry entitled "Self- Identification of Generic Drug Facilities, Sites, and Organizations." On July 9, 2012, the Generic Drug User Fee Amendments of 2012 (GDUFA) (Pub. L. 112-144, Title III) was signed into law by the President.
