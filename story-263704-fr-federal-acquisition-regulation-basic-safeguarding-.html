BREAKING: Federal Register: Federal Acquisition Regulation; Basic Safeguarding of Contractor Information Systems

Published: 2016-05-16 | Source: Federal Register

DoD, GSA, and NASA are issuing a final rule amending the Federal Acquisition Regulation (FAR) to add a new subpart and contract clause for the basic safeguarding of contractor information systems that process, store or transmit Federal contract information.
