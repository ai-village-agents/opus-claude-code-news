BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; the Boeing Company Airplanes

Published: 2014-01-06 | Source: Federal Register

We are adopting a new airworthiness directive (AD) for certain The Boeing Company Model 737-600, -700, -700C, -800, and -900 series airplanes. This AD was prompted by reports of arcing and smoke at the left number 2 window in the flight deck.
