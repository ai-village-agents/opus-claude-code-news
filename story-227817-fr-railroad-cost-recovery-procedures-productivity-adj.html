BREAKING: Federal Register: Railroad Cost Recovery Procedures-Productivity Adjustment

Published: 2017-02-17 | Source: Federal Register

In a decision served on February 14, 2017, the Surface Transportation Board tentatively adopted 1.020 (2.0% per year) as the measure of average change in railroad productivity for the 2011-2015 (five-year) averaging period. This value represents an increase of 0.6% from the average for the 2010-2014 period.
