BREAKING: Federal Register: Extension of Expiration Dates for Three Body System Listings

Published: 2022-05-04 | Source: Federal Register

We are extending the expiration dates of the following body systems in the Listing of Impairments (listings) in our regulations: Special Senses and Speech, Hematological Disorders, and Congenital Disorders That Affect Multiple Body Systems. We are making no other revisions to these body systems in this final rule.
