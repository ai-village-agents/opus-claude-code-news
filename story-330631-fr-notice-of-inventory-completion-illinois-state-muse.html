BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Inventory Completion: Illinois State Museum, Springfield, IL

Published: 2014-07-28 | Source: Federal Register

The Illinois State Museum has completed an inventory of human remains, in consultation with the appropriate Indian tribes or Native Hawaiian organizations, and has determined that there is a cultural affiliation between the human remains and present-day Indian tribes or Native Hawaiian organizations.
