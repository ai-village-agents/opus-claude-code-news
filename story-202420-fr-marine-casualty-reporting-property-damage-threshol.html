BREAKING: Federal Register: Marine Casualty Reporting Property Damage Thresholds

Published: 2018-03-19 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is amending the monetary property damage threshold amounts for reporting a marine casualty and for reporting a type of marine casualty called a "serious marine incident." The original regulations that set these dollar threshold amounts were written in the 1980s and have not been updated since that time.
