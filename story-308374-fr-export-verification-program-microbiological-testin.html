BREAKING: Federal Register: Export Verification Program: Microbiological Testing of Ready-To-Eat Products Destined for Canada

Published: 2015-11-02 | Source: Federal Register

The Food Safety and Inspection Service (FSIS) is announcing the establishment of an FSIS and Agricultural Marketing Service (AMS) Export Verification (EV) Program. The program is designed to verify establishments' control of closed-faced sandwiches destined for Canada. Among other things, Canada is requiring that closed-faced sandwiches be produced under a Hazard Analysis and Critical Control Point (HACCP) plan.
