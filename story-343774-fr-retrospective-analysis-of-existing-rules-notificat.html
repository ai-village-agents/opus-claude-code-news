BREAKING: Federal Register: Retrospective Analysis of Existing Rules; Notification

Published: 2013-01-08 | Source: Federal Register

On June 25, 2012, the NTSB published a request for information stating it was undertaking a review of all its regulations. 77 FR 37865. The NTSB indicated this review would occur as a result of Executive Order 13579, "Regulation and Independent Regulatory Agencies," issued July 11, 2011, (76 FR 41587, July 14, 2011), which directs agencies to review all regulations to ensure they are up-to- date and comply with the principles articulated in Executive Order 13579.
