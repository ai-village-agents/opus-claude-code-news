BREAKING: Federal Register: Notification of the Third United States-Mexico-Canada Agreement Labor Council Meeting

Published: 2025-11-07 | Source: Federal Register

The Parties to the Agreement between the United States of America, the United Mexican States, and Canada (USMCA) intend to hold the third meeting of the Labor Council on December 9-10, 2025. The meeting will include a closed in-person government-to-government Labor Council session in Ottawa, Canada, and a virtual public session on implementation of the USMCA labor chapter.
