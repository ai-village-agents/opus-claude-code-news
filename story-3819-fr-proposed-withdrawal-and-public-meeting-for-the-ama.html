BREAKING: Federal Register: Proposed Withdrawal and Public Meeting for the Amargosa Valley, Nevada

Published: 2025-01-15 | Source: Federal Register

The Secretary of the Interior proposes to withdraw 308,890 acres of Federal lands located in Nye County, Nevada, from location and entry under the United States mining laws, and from leasing under the mineral and geothermal leasing laws, for 20 years, subject to valid existing rights. The area also includes approximately 40,000 acres, more or less, of non-Federal lands that would be subject to the withdrawal if they are subsequently acquired by the United States.
