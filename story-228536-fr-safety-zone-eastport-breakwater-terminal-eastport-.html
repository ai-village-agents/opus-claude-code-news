BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone: Eastport Breakwater Terminal, Eastport, Maine

Published: 2017-03-01 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is extending the effective period of a safety zone in the vicinity of the Eastport Breakwater Terminal, Eastport, Maine. This safety zone was established on January 9, 2015 (80 FR 1344). This rule will extend the effective period of the existing temporary final rule from January 30, 2017 until October 1, 2017.
