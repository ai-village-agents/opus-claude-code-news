BREAKING: Federal Register: Approval and Promulgation of Air Quality Implementation Plans; Maryland; State Board Requirements

Published: 2016-05-02 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is taking direct final action to approve revisions to the Maryland State Implementation Plan (SIP). The SIP revision removes the current SIP approved state board requirements and replaces them with an updated version of the requirements. The new provisions continue to address state board requirements for all the National Ambient Air Quality Standards (NAAQS).
