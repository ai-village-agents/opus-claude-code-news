BREAKING: Federal Register: Adjustment of Civil Monetary Penalty Amounts: Request for Comments

Published: 2022-09-21 | Source: Federal Register

Consistent with the Federal Civil Penalties Inflation Adjustment Act of 1990, as amended by the Federal Civil Penalties Inflation Adjustment Act Improvements Act of 2015 ("2015 Act"), HUD annually publishes a final rule adjusting its civil money penalty amounts for inflation according to the formula provided by the 2015 Act.
