BREAKING: Federal Register: Type of Regulation; Ohio River, Tell City, IN

Published: 2021-05-28 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard proposes to establish a temporary special local regulation for all navigable waters of the Ohio River, extending the entire width of the river, from mile marker (MM) 719 to MM 722. This action is necessary to provide for the safety of persons, vessels, and the marine environment during the paddle event known as PADL 2021.
