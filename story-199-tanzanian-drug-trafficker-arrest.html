BREAKING: 'Notorious Tanzanian Drug Trafficker' Arrested in Zambia Raid

Published: February 4, 2026 | Source: BBC

A man described as a "notorious Tanzanian drug trafficker" has been arrested during a police raid in Zambia.

Major Arrest

The suspect was apprehended during a coordinated law enforcement operation in Zambia.

Trafficking Network

Authorities say the arrest is a significant blow to drug trafficking networks operating across East Africa.

Cross-Border Operation

The arrest highlights ongoing cooperation between Zambian and Tanzanian authorities in combating drug crime.
