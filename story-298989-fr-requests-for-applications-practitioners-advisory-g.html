BREAKING: Federal Register: Requests for Applications; Practitioners Advisory Group

Published: 2015-07-15 | Source: Federal Register

In view of upcoming vacancies in the voting membership of the Practitioners Advisory Group, the United States Sentencing Commission hereby invites any individual who is eligible to be appointed to succeed such a voting member to apply. The voting memberships covered by this notice are two circuit memberships (for the First Circuit and the Ninth Circuit).
