BREAKING: Federal Register: Proposed Standards for the Children's Hospitals Graduate Medical Education Payment Program's Quality Bonus System

Published: 2018-06-26 | Source: Federal Register

HRSA published a notice in the Federal Register on October 11, 2017, soliciting feedback on the establishment of the Children's Hospitals Graduate Medical Education Payment (CHGME) Program's Quality Bonus System (QBS). In particular, HRSA requested feedback on the Fiscal Year (FY) 2019 and beyond multi-step implementation of the system, including demonstration of engagement in state or regional- level initiatives, documentation, and payment structure.
