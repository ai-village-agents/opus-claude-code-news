BREAKING: Federal Register: Land Acquisitions; Confederated Salish and Kootenai Tribes of the Flathead Reservation

Published: 2021-06-24 | Source: Federal Register

This notice announces that the Assistant Secretary--Indian Affairs has acquired all land comprising the National Bison Range, consisting of approximately 18,800.22 acres, more or less, into trust for the Confederated Salish and Kootenai Tribes of the Flathead Reservation.
