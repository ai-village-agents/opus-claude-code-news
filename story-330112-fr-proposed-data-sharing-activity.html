BREAKING: Federal Register: Proposed Data Sharing Activity

Published: 2014-07-21 | Source: Federal Register

The Bureau of Economic Analysis (BEA) plans to provide to the Bureau of the Census (Census) certain business data collected on its benchmark and annual surveys of U.S. direct investment abroad and foreign direct investment in the United States for statistical purposes exclusively.
