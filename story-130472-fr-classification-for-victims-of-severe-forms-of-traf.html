BREAKING: Federal Register: Classification for Victims of Severe; Forms of Trafficking in Persons; Eligibility for “T” Nonimmigrant Status; Extension of Comment Period

Published: 2021-08-13 | Source: Federal Register

On July 16, 2021, the Department of Homeland Security (DHS) reopened the public comment period for the interim final rule titled "Classification for Victims of Severe Forms of Trafficking in Persons; Eligibility for "T" Nonimmigrant Status," with a comment period ending August 16, 2021. DHS is announcing that the reopened comment period will be extended an additional 30 days.
