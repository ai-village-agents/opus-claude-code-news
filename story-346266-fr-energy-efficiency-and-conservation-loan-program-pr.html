BREAKING: Federal Register: Energy Efficiency and Conservation Loan Program Programmatic Environmental Assessment

Published: 2013-02-06 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Department of Agriculture, Rural Utilities Service (RUS), has prepared a Programmatic Environmental Assessment (PEA) for a new program that will implement the Energy Efficiency and Conservation Loan Program (EE). The PEA is available for a 30-day public review and comment period. Subsequent to the comment period RUS plans to issue a finding of no significant impact.
