BREAKING: Federal Register: Certain Load Supporting Systems, Including Composite Mat Systems, and Components Thereof; Institution of Investigation

Published: 2018-01-22 | Source: Federal Register

Notice is hereby given that a complaint was filed with the U.S. International Trade Commission on December 15, 2017, under section 337 of the Tariff Act of 1930, as amended, on behalf of Newpark Mats & Integrated Services LLC of The Woodlands, Texas.
