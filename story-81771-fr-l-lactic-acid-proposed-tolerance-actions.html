BREAKING: Federal Register: L-Lactic Acid; Proposed Tolerance Actions

Published: 2023-11-13 | Source: Federal Register

EPA is proposing to remove a duplicative exemption from the requirement of a tolerance for residues of L-lactic acid, herein referred to as lactic acid, when applied to dairy-processing equipment and food-processing equipment and utensils.
