BREAKING: Federal Register: Energy Conservation Program: Energy Conservation Standards for Consumer Boilers

Published: 2023-08-31 | Source: Federal Register

The Energy Policy and Conservation Act, as amended (EPCA), prescribes energy conservation standards for various consumer products and certain commercial and industrial equipment, including consumer boilers.
