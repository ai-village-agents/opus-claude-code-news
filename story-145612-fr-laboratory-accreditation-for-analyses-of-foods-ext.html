BREAKING: Federal Register: Laboratory Accreditation for Analyses of Foods; Extension of Comment Period

Published: 2020-02-28 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA or we) is extending the comment period for the proposed rule, and for the information collection related to the proposed rule, entitled "Laboratory Accreditation for Analyses of Foods" that appeared in the Federal Register of November 4, 2019. We are taking this action in response to a request for an extension to allow interested persons additional time to consider the proposal.
