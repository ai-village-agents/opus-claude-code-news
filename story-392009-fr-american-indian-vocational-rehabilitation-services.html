BREAKING: Federal Register: American Indian Vocational Rehabilitation Services Program; Proposed Waivers and Extensions of the Project Periods

Published: 2012-07-25 | Source: Federal Register

For 60-month projects initially funded in fiscal year (FY) 2007 under the American Indian Vocational Rehabilitation Services (AIVRS) Program, the Department proposes to waive the regulations that generally limit project periods to 60 months and that restrict project period extensions involving the obligation of additional Federal funds.
