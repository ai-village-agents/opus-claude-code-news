BREAKING: Federal Register: Yogurt Products Deviating From Standard of Identity; Temporary Permit for Market Testing

Published: 2023-03-28 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA or we) is announcing that we have issued a temporary permit to Chobani, LLC (the applicant) to market test lower fat yogurt products deviating from the general definition and standard of identity for yogurt with modified milkfat and fat-containing flavoring ingredients, and yogurt deviating from the yogurt standard of identity by using ultrafiltered nonfat milk as a basic dairy ingredient.
