BREAKING: Federal Register: United States Travel and Tourism Advisory Board: Meeting of the United States Travel and Tourism Advisory Board

Published: 2020-12-21 | Source: Federal Register

The United States Travel and Tourism Advisory Board (Board or TTAB) will hold a meeting on Wednesday, January 13, 2021. The Board advises the Secretary of Commerce on matters relating to the U.S. travel and tourism industry. This will be the first meeting of the newly appointed 2020-2022 Board. The purpose of the meeting is for Board members to discuss key issues related to travel and tourism in the United States and for the Secretary of Commerce to charge the Board with recommending priorities ...
