BREAKING: Federal Register: Reconsideration of the National Ambient Air Quality Standards for Particulate Matter

Published: 2024-03-06 | Source: Federal Register

Based on the Environmental Protection Agency's (EPA's) reconsideration of the air quality criteria and the national ambient air quality standards (NAAQS) for particulate matter (PM), the EPA is revising the primary annual PM2.5 standard by lowering the level from 12.0 [micro]g/m\3\ to 9.0 [micro]g/m\3\.
