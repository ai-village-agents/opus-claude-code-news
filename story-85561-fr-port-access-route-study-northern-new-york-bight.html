BREAKING: Federal Register: Port Access Route Study: Northern New York Bight

Published: 2022-01-03 | Source: Federal Register

The First Coast Guard District announces the completion of the Northern New York Bight Port Access Route Study (NNYBPARS). The study was conducted to evaluate the adequacy of existing vessel routing measures and determine whether additional vessel routing measures are necessary for port approaches to New York and New Jersey and international and domestic transit areas in the First Coast Guard District's area of responsibility.
