BREAKING: Federal Register: Request for Information on Vaping Products Associated With Lung Injuries; Extension of Comment Period

Published: 2020-04-14 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA or the Agency) is extending the comment period for the request for information (RFI) entitled "Request for Information on Vaping Products Associated with Lung Injuries" that appeared in the Federal Register of February 18, 2020. In the RFI, FDA opened a docket to obtain data and information related to the use of vaping products that are associated with recent lung injuries.
