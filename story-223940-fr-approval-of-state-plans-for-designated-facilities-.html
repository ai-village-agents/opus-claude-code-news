BREAKING: Federal Register: Approval of State Plans for Designated Facilities and Pollutants; Kansas; Sewage Sludge Incineration Units

Published: 2018-12-26 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is proposing to accept the negative declaration submitted by the State of Kansas, for Sewage Sludge Incineration (SSI) units. This negative declaration submitted by the Kansas Department of Health and Environment (KDHE) certifies that SSI units subject to sections 111(d) and 129 of the Clean Air Act (CAA) do not exist within the jurisdiction of the State of Kansas.
