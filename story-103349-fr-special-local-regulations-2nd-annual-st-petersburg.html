BREAKING: Federal Register: Special Local Regulations; 2nd Annual St. Petersburg P1 Powerboat Gran Prix; Tampa Bay, FL

Published: 2022-08-22 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard will enforce a special local regulation for certain waters of Tampa Bay, FL. in the vicinity of the St. Petersburg Pier during the Annual St. Petersburg P1 Powerboat Grand Prix. Our regulation for marine events within the Seventh Coast Guard District, Sector St. Petersburg identifies the regulated area for this event.
