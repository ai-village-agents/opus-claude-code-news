BREAKING: Federal Register: Yemen Sanctions Regulations

Published: 2012-11-09 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of the Treasury's Office of Foreign Assets Control ("OFAC") is issuing regulations to implement Executive Order 13611 of May 16, 2012 ("Blocking Property of Persons Threatening the Peace, Security, or Stability of Yemen"). OFAC intends to supplement this part 552 with a more comprehensive set of regulations, which may include additional interpretive and definitional guidance and additional general licenses and statements of licensing policy.
