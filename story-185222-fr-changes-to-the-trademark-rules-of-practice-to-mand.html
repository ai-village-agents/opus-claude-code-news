BREAKING: Federal Register: Changes to the Trademark Rules of Practice To Mandate Electronic Filing

Published: 2019-07-31 | Source: Federal Register

The United States Patent and Trademark Office (USPTO or Office) amends the Rules of Practice in Trademark Cases and the Rules of Practice in Filings Pursuant to the Protocol Relating to the Madrid Agreement Concerning the International Registration of Marks to mandate electronic filing of trademark applications and all submissions associated with trademark applications and registrations, and to require the designation of an email address for receiving USPTO correspondence, with limited exception...
