BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Embraer S.A. Airplanes

Published: 2022-07-13 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA proposes to adopt a new airworthiness directive (AD) for certain Embraer S.A. Model EMB-545 and EMB-550 airplanes. This proposed AD was prompted by a report that there is a possibility of the shoulder belt getting stuck during flight due to a step between the divan shroud chamfer and the sideledge panel.
