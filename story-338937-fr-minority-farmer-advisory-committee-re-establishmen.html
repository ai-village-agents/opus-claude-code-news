BREAKING: Federal Register: Minority Farmer Advisory Committee; Re-Establishment of Charter

Published: 2014-11-04 | Source: Federal Register

We are giving notice that the Secretary of Agriculture intends to re-establish the charter of the Advisory Committee on Minority Farmers (the "Committee") for a 2-year term.
