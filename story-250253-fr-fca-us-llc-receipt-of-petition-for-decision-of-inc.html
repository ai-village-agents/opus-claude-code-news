BREAKING: Federal Register: FCA US LLC, Receipt of Petition for Decision of Inconsequential Noncompliance

Published: 2017-12-07 | Source: Federal Register

FCA US LLC (FCA US), (f/k/a Chrysler Group LLC) has determined that certain Mopar Service seat belt assemblies sold to FCA dealers as replacement equipment in certain model year (MY) 1992-2018 FCA US motor vehicles do not fully comply with Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard (FMVSS) No. 209, Seat Belt Assemblies.
