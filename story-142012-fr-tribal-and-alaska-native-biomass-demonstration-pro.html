BREAKING: Federal Register: Tribal and Alaska Native Biomass Demonstration Projects; Eligibility Criteria

Published: 2020-01-15 | Source: Federal Register

The Bureau of Land Management (BLM) is establishing eligibility and selection criteria for Tribal Biomass Demonstration Project and Alaska Native Biomass Demonstration Project proposals submitted for BLM-managed lands, as authorized by the Indian Tribal Energy Development and Self-Determination Act Amendments of 2017.
