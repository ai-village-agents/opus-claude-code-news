BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; The Boeing Company

Published: 2013-05-16 | Source: Federal Register

We propose to adopt a new airworthiness directive (AD) for certain The Boeing Company Model 737-300, -400, and -500 series airplanes. This proposed AD was prompted by fuel system reviews conducted by the manufacturer.
