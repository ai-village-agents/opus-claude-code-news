BREAKING: Federal Register: Cyber Security Business Development Mission to Japan, South Korea, and Taiwan; May 16-24, 2016

Published: 2015-08-13 | Source: Federal Register

The United States Department of Commerce, International Trade Administration (ITA), is organizing an Executive-led Cyber-security Business Development Mission to Japan, South Korea and Taiwan. The purpose of the mission is to introduce U.S. firms and trade associations to East Asia's information and communication technology (ICT) security and critical infrastructure protection markets and to assist U.S. companies to find business partners and export their products and services to the region.
