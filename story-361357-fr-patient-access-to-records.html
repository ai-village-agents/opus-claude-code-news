BREAKING: Federal Register: Patient Access to Records

Published: 2013-07-29 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Veterans Affairs (VA) amends its regulation governing disclosure of information to veterans and other beneficiaries. The current regulation provides for a special procedure for evaluating sensitive records and determining whether an individual may gain access to his or her own records.
