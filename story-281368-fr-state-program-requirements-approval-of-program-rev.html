BREAKING: Federal Register: State Program Requirements; Approval of Program Revisions to Michigan's Clean Water Act Section 404 Program

Published: 2016-12-13 | Source: Federal Register

In a July 5, 2013, letter, the Michigan Department of Environmental Quality (MDEQ) requested that the Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) approve revisions to the State's Clean Water Act (CWA) Section 404 permitting program that resulted from the enactment of Michigan Public Act 98 (PA 98). CWA Section 404 requires permits for dredge and fill activities in wetlands subject to federal jurisdiction.
