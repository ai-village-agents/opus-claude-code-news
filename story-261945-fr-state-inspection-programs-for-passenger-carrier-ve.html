BREAKING: Federal Register: State Inspection Programs for Passenger-Carrier Vehicles

Published: 2016-04-27 | Source: Federal Register

FMCSA announces that it is considering a rulemaking that would require the States to establish a program for annual inspections of commercial motor vehicles (CMVs) designed or used to transport passengers (or, passenger-carrying CMVs).
