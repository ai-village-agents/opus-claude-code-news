BREAKING: Federal Register: Announcement of National Geospatial Advisory Committee Meeting

Published: 2014-09-09 | Source: Federal Register

The National Geospatial Advisory Committee (NGAC) will meet on September 23-24, 2014 at the National Conservation Training Center, 698 Conservation Way, Shepherdstown, WV 25443. The meeting will be held in Room #201 Instructional East.
