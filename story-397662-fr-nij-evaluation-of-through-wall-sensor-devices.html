BREAKING: Federal Register: NIJ Evaluation of Through-Wall Sensor Devices

Published: 2012-09-28 | Source: Federal Register

The National Institute of Justice (NIJ) is soliciting interest in supplying through-wall sensor devices for participation in an evaluation by the NIJ Sensor, Surveillance, and Biometric Technologies Center of Excellence (SSBT CoE). The evaluation is focused on field operation in civilian law enforcement scenarios.
