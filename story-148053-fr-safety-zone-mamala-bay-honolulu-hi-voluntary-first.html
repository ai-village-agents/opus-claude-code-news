BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone; Mamala Bay, Honolulu, HI-Voluntary First Amendment Zone

Published: 2020-03-31 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is establishing a temporary safety zone for navigable waters within Mamala Bay, Honolulu Hawaii. The safety zone is needed to protect personnel and vessels who may want to exercise their First Amendment in the vicinity of Honolulu Harbor and Honolulu Channel.
