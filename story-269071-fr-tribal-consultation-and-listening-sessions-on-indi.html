BREAKING: Federal Register: Tribal Consultation and Listening Sessions on Indian Trust Asset Reform Act

Published: 2016-07-20 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of the Interior (Interior) is hosting a listening session and tribal consultation sessions with Indian Tribes, appropriate Indian organizations, and individual Indians on the implementation of Title III of the Indian Trust Asset Reform Act recently passed by Congress.
