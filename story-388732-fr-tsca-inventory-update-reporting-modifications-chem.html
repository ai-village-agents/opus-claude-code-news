BREAKING: Federal Register: TSCA Inventory Update Reporting Modifications; Chemical Data Reporting; 2012 Submission Period Extension

Published: 2012-06-18 | Source: Federal Register

EPA is amending the Toxic Substances Control Act (TSCA) Chemical Data Reporting (CDR) regulations by extending the submission deadline for 2012 reports from June 30, 2012 to August 13, 2012. This is a one-time extension for the 2012 submission period only.
