BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Airbus Airplanes

Published: 2016-06-21 | Source: Federal Register

We propose to adopt a new airworthiness directive (AD) for certain Airbus Model A330-200, -200 Freighter, and -300 series airplanes; and Model A340-500 and -600 series airplanes. This proposed AD was prompted by a quality control review on the final assembly line, which determined that the wrong aluminum alloy was used to manufacture several structural parts.
