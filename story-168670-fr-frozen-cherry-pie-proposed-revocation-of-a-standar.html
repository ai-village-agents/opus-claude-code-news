BREAKING: Federal Register: Frozen Cherry Pie; Proposed Revocation of a Standard of Identity and a Standard of Quality

Published: 2020-12-18 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA or we) proposes to revoke the standard of identity and the standard of quality for frozen cherry pie. This action, in part, responds to a citizen petition submitted by the American Bakers Association (ABA). We tentatively conclude that these standards are no longer necessary to promote honesty and fair dealing in the interest of consumers.
