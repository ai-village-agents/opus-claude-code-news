BREAKING: Federal Register: Federal Acquisition Regulation; Submission for OMB Review; Notice of Radioactive Materials

Published: 2012-11-05 | Source: Federal Register

Under the provisions of the Paperwork Reduction Act, the Regulatory Secretariat will be submitting to the Office of Management and Budget (OMB) a request to review and approve an extension of a previously approved information collection requirement concerning Notice of Radioactive Materials. A notice was published in the Federal Register at 77 FR 45612, on August 1, 2012. No comments were received.
