BREAKING: Federal Register: Senior Executive Service; Departmental Performance Review Board Members

Published: 2012-10-16 | Source: Federal Register

Pursuant to 5 U.S.C. 4314(c)(4), this notice announces the appointment of members of the Departmental PRB. The purpose of this PRB is to review and make recommendations concerning proposed performance appraisals, ratings, bonuses and other appropriate personnel actions for incumbents of SES positions for which the Secretary or Deputy Secretary is the appointing authority.
