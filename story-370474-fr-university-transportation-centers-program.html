BREAKING: Federal Register: University Transportation Centers Program

Published: 2013-11-18 | Source: Federal Register

The United States Department of Transportation is publishing this notice to give eligible nonprofit institutions of higher education located in Federal Regions 3 and 10 advance notice that they will have an opportunity to submit applications for a grant as a Regional Center in the University Transportation Centers (UTCs) program.
