BREAKING: Federal Register: Security Zones; Seattle's Seafair Fleet Week Moving Vessels, 2017, Puget Sound, WA

Published: 2017-07-27 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard will enforce Seattle's Seafair Fleet Week Moving Vessels security zones from 10 a.m. on Aug 1, 2017, through 6 p.m. on August 7, 2017. These security zones are necessary to help ensure the security of the vessels from sabotage or other subversive acts during Seafair Fleet Week Parade of Ships.
