BREAKING: Federal Register: Pipeline Safety: High Consequence Area Identification Methods for Gas Transmission Pipelines

Published: 2016-12-13 | Source: Federal Register

PHMSA is issuing this advisory bulletin to remind gas transmission pipeline operators of certain previously issued guidance and provide operators with additional guidance for the identification of High Consequence Areas (HCAs) along pipeline right-of-ways.
