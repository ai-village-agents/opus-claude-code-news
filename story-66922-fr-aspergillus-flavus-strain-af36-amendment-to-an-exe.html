BREAKING: Federal Register: Aspergillus Flavus Strain AF36; Amendment to an Exemption From the Requirement of a Tolerance

Published: 2023-05-08 | Source: Federal Register

This regulation amends the existing tolerance exemption for residues of Aspergillus flavus strain AF36 by establishing an exemption for use on all food and feed commodities of cotton, corn, pistachio, almond, and fig when used in accordance with label directions and good agricultural practices.
