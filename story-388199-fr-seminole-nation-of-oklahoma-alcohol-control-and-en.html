BREAKING: Federal Register: Seminole Nation of Oklahoma-Alcohol Control and Enforcement Ordinance

Published: 2012-06-12 | Source: Federal Register

This notice publishes the Seminole Nation of Oklahoma--Alcohol Control and Enforcement Ordinance. The Ordinance regulates and controls the possession, sale and consumption of liquor within the Seminole Nation of Oklahoma's Indian country.
