BREAKING: Federal Register: Air Plan Approval; Missouri; Revision to Emission Data, Emission Fees and Process Information Rule

Published: 2019-08-22 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is taking final action to approve a State Implementation Plan (SIP) and Operating Permits Program revision submitted by the State of Missouri on January 15, 2019. The revisions add definitions, remove language referring to outdated emission fees, and update incorporations by reference in the rule.
