BREAKING: Federal Register: National Environmental Policy Act Implementing Regulations Revisions

Published: 2021-10-07 | Source: Federal Register

The Council on Environmental Quality (CEQ) is proposing to modify certain aspects of its regulations for implementing the procedural provisions of the National Environmental Policy Act (NEPA) to generally restore regulatory provisions that were in effect for decades before being modified in 2020.
