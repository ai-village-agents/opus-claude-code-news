BREAKING: Federal Register: Medicaid Program; Reassignment of Medicaid Provider Claims

Published: 2021-08-03 | Source: Federal Register

This proposed rule would reinterpret the scope of the general requirement that state payments for Medicaid services under a state plan must be made directly to the individual practitioner providing services, in the case of a class of practitioners for which the Medicaid program is the primary source of revenue.
