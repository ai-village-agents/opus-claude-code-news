BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; The Boeing Company Airplanes

Published: 2021-11-08 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA is superseding Airworthiness Directive (AD) 2005-05- 18, which applied to certain The Boeing Company Model 737-600, -700, - 700C, -800, and -900 series airplanes. AD 2005-05-18 required repetitive inspections for cracking of the webs of the aft pressure bulkhead at a certain body station, and corrective action if necessary.
