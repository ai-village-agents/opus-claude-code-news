BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Fokker Services B.V. Airplanes

Published: 2018-02-27 | Source: Federal Register

We are superseding Airworthiness Directive (AD) 2012-22-15, which applied to all Fokker Services B.V. Model F28 Mark 0070 and Mark 0100 airplanes. AD 2012-22-15 required revising the maintenance program to incorporate the limitations, tasks, thresholds, and intervals specified in certain revised Fokker maintenance review board (MRB) documents.
