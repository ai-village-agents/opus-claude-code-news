BREAKING: Federal Register: Special Regulations of the National Park Service; Glen Canyon National Recreation Area; Motor Vehicles

Published: 2018-02-28 | Source: Federal Register

The National Park Service proposes to amend its special regulations for Glen Canyon National Recreation Area to manage the use of motor vehicles on and off park roads. The rule would require a permit to operate a motor vehicle off roads in selected locations, designate routes and areas where motor vehicles may be used off roads, and allow the superintendent to establish closures and restrictions based upon specific criteria.
