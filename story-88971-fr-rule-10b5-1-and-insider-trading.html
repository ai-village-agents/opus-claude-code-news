BREAKING: Federal Register: Rule 10b5-1 and Insider Trading

Published: 2022-02-15 | Source: Federal Register

The Securities and Exchange Commission ("Commission") is proposing amendments to its rules under the Securities Exchange Act of 1934. The proposed amendments would add new conditions to the availability of an affirmative defense under an Exchange Act rule that are designed to address concerns about abuse of the rule to opportunistically trade securities on the basis of material nonpublic information in ways that harm investors and undermine the integrity of the securities markets.
