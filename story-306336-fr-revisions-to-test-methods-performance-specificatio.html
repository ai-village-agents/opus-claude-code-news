BREAKING: Federal Register: Revisions to Test Methods, Performance Specifications, and Testing Regulations for Air Emission Sources; Extension of Comment Period

Published: 2015-10-09 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is extending the comment period for the proposed rule titled, "Revisions to Test Methods, Performance Specifications, and Testing Regulations for Air Emission Sources," that was published in the Federal Register on September 8, 2015. The 60-day comment period in the proposed rule is scheduled to end on November 9, 2015. The extended comment period will close on December 9, 2015.
