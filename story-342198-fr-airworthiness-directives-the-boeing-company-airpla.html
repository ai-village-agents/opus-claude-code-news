BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; The Boeing Company Airplanes

Published: 2014-12-17 | Source: Federal Register

We propose to adopt a new airworthiness directive (AD) for certain The Boeing Company Model 747-8 and 747-8F airplanes. This proposed AD was prompted by an analysis, which determined that in a limited flight envelope with specific conditions, divergent flutter could occur during a high g-load maneuver in combination with certain system failures.
