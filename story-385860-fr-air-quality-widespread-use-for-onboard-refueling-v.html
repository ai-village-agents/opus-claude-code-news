BREAKING: Federal Register: Air Quality: Widespread Use for Onboard Refueling Vapor Recovery and Stage II Waiver

Published: 2012-05-16 | Source: Federal Register

The EPA has determined that onboard refueling vapor recovery (ORVR) technology is in widespread use throughout the motor vehicle fleet for purposes of controlling motor vehicle refueling emissions, and, therefore, by this action, the EPA is waiving the requirement for states to implement Stage II gasoline vapor recovery systems at gasoline dispensing facilities in nonattainment areas classified as Serious and above for the ozone national ambient air quality standards (NAAQS).
