BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone; City of Charleston Independence Celebration, Charleston, WV

Published: 2016-06-30 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard will enforce safety zone for the City of Charleston Independence Celebration Fireworks on the Kanawha River from mile marker 58.1 to mile marker 59.1, in Charleston, WV on July 3, 2016. This action is needed to provide for the safety of life on navigable waterways during a fireworks display on or over the waterway.
