BREAKING: Federal Register: Expansion of Provisional Unlawful Presence Waivers of Inadmissibility

Published: 2016-07-29 | Source: Federal Register

This final rule, consistent with the Immigration and Nationality Act (INA), expands the class of individuals who may be eligible for a provisional waiver of certain grounds of inadmissibility based on the accrual of unlawful presence in the United States.
