BREAKING: Federal Register: Extra-Schedular Evaluations for Individual Disabilities

Published: 2017-12-08 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Veterans Affairs (VA) amends its adjudication regulation pertaining to extra-schedular consideration of a service-connected disability in exceptional compensation cases. This rule clarifies that an extra-schedular evaluation is to be applied to an individual service-connected disability when the disability is so exceptional or unusual that it makes application of the regular rating schedule impractical.
