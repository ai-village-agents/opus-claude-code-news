BREAKING: Federal Register: Redesignation of Regulations for Securing the Information and Communications Technology and Services Supply Chain

Published: 2024-07-18 | Source: Federal Register

This rule redesignates regulations governing the procedures for the review of certain transactions involving information and communications technology and services (ICTS) designed, developed, manufactured, or supplied by persons owned by, controlled by, or subject to the jurisdiction or direction of a foreign adversary and which pose or may pose undue or unacceptable risks to the United States or U.S. persons.
