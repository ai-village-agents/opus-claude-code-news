BREAKING: Federal Register: Special Load Line Exemption for Lake Michigan: Petition for Rulemaking

Published: 2014-05-27 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard announces the availability of several requests for a rulemaking, and requests public comment accordingly. The requesters are petitioning the Coast Guard to establish a special load line-exempted route on Lake Michigan. This exemption would allow non- load line river barges to transit along the eastern shore of Lake Michigan, between Chicago (Calumet Harbor), IL, and Muskegon, MI.
