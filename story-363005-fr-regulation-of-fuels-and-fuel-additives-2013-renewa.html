BREAKING: Federal Register: Regulation of Fuels and Fuel Additives: 2013 Renewable Fuel Standards

Published: 2013-08-15 | Source: Federal Register

Under section 211(o) of the Clean Air Act, the Environmental Protection Agency is required to set the renewable fuel percentage standards each November for the following year. Today's action sets the annual percentage standards for cellulosic biofuel, biomass-based diesel, advanced biofuel, and renewable fuels that apply to all motor vehicle gasoline and diesel produced or imported in the year 2013.
