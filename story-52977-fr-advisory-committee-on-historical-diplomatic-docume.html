BREAKING: Federal Register: Advisory Committee on Historical Diplomatic Documentation; Notice of Closed and Open Meetings for 2025

Published: 2024-11-20 | Source: Federal Register

The Advisory Committee on Historical Diplomatic Documentation will meet, in open and closed sessions, to discuss matters concerning declassification and transfer of Department of State records to the National Archives and Records Administration and the status of the Foreign Relations series.
