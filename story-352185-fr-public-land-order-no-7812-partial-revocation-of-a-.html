BREAKING: Federal Register: Public Land Order No. 7812; Partial Revocation of a Secretarial Order Dated April 27, 1905; CO

Published: 2013-04-15 | Source: Federal Register

This order partially revokes a withdrawal created by a Secretarial Order insofar as it affects 35.89 acres of public land withdrawn on behalf of the Bureau of Reclamation for the Gore Canyon Reservoir, Colorado River Storage Project.
