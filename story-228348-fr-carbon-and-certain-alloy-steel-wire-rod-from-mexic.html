BREAKING: Federal Register: Carbon and Certain Alloy Steel Wire Rod From Mexico: Notice of Partial Rescission of the Antidumping Duty Administrative Review; 2015-2016

Published: 2017-02-27 | Source: Federal Register

On December 16, 2016, the Department of Commerce ("Department") published a notice of initiation of an administrative review of the antidumping duty order on carbon and certain alloy steel wire rod ("wire rod") from Mexico. Based on Nucor Corporation's ("Nucor") timely withdrawal of the request for review of Ternium Mexico S.A. de C.V. ("Ternium"), we are rescinding this administrative review with respect to Ternium. The instant review will continue with respect to ArcelorMittal Las Truchas S.A....
