BREAKING: Federal Register: Traylor-Sundt Joint Venture: Grant of Permanent Variance

Published: 2023-11-28 | Source: Federal Register

In this notice, OSHA grants a permanent variance to Traylor- Sundt Joint Venture (SUNDTJV) related to work in compressed-air environments.
