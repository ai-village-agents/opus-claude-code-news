BREAKING: Federal Register: Endangered and Threatened Wildlife and Plants; 90-Day Findings on 10 Petitions

Published: 2015-04-10 | Source: Federal Register

We, the U.S. Fish and Wildlife Service (Service), announce 90- day findings on various petitions to list eight species, reclassify one species, and delist one species under the Endangered Species Act of 1973, as amended (Act). Based on our review, we find that these 10 petitions present substantial scientific or commercial information indicating that the petitioned actions may be warranted.
