BREAKING: Federal Register: Naturally-Occurring and Accelerator-Produced Radioactive Materials

Published: 2017-08-23 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Nuclear Regulatory Commission (NRC) has received a petition for rulemaking from Matthew McKinley on behalf of the Organization of Agreement States (OAS) (the Petitioner) dated April 14, 2017, requesting that the NRC revise its regulations to add radionuclides and their corresponding activities to the list of "Quantities of Licensed Material Requiring Labeling."
