BREAKING: Federal Register: Importation of Dianthus spp. From Kenya

Published: 2019-05-09 | Source: Federal Register

We are notifying the public that we propose to make changes to the import requirements in the U.S. Department of Agriculture (USDA) Plants for Planting Manual for imports of Dianthus spp. (carnation) cuttings from Kenya. Currently, Dianthus spp. cuttings from Kenya require mandatory postentry quarantine within the United States.
