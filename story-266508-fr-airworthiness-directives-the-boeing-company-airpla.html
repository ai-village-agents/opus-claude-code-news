BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; The Boeing Company Airplanes

Published: 2016-06-17 | Source: Federal Register

We are adopting a new airworthiness directive (AD) for certain The Boeing Company Model 777-200 and -300 series airplanes equipped with Rolls-Royce Model RB211-Trent 800 engines. This AD was prompted by reports of thrust reverser (T/R) events related to thermal damage of the T/R inner wall.
