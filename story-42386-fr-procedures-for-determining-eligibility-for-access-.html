BREAKING: Federal Register: Procedures for Determining Eligibility for Access to Classified Matter or Special Nuclear Material

Published: 2024-07-23 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Energy (DOE) publishes a final rule to amend its regulations, which set forth the policies and procedures for resolving questions concerning eligibility for DOE access authorizations.
