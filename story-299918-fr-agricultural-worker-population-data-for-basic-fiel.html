BREAKING: Federal Register: Agricultural Worker Population Data for Basic Field-Migrant Grants

Published: 2015-07-27 | Source: Federal Register

The Legal Services Corporation (LSC) provides special population grants to effectively and efficiently fund civil legal aid services to address the legal needs of agricultural workers and their dependents through grants entitled "Basic Field--Migrant." The funding for these grants is based on data regarding the eligible client population to be served.
