BREAKING: Federal Register: Request for Nominations on the National Mammography Quality Assurance Advisory Committee

Published: 2016-01-26 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA) is requesting that any industry organizations interested in participating in the selection of nonvoting industry representatives to serve on the National Mammography Quality Assurance Advisory Committee (NMQAAC) for the Center for Devices and Radiological Health (CDRH) notify FDA in writing.
