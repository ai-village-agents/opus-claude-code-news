BREAKING: Federal Register: Columbia Generating Station; Issuance of License Amendment Revising Technical Specification 3.8.7

Published: 2020-09-16 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Nuclear Regulatory Commission (NRC) approved a request by Energy Northwest (the licensee) for an amendment to Renewed Facility Operating License No. NPF-21, issued to the licensee for operation of the Columbia Generating Station, located in Benton County, Washington.
