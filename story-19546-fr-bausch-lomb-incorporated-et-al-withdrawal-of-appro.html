BREAKING: Federal Register: Bausch & Lomb Incorporated, et al.; Withdrawal of Approval of Eight Abbreviated New Drug Applications; Correction

Published: 2025-09-04 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA) is correcting a notice that appeared in the Federal Register on March 14, 2025 (90 FR 49), appearing on page 12163 in FR Doc. 2025-04106. The document announced the withdrawal of approval of eight abbreviated new drug applications (ANDAs) from multiple applicants, withdrawn as of April 14, 2025. The document indicated that FDA was withdrawing approval of the ANDA 075819 for amantadine hydrochloride syrup, 50 milligrams/5 milliliters, held by CMP Pharma, Inc...
