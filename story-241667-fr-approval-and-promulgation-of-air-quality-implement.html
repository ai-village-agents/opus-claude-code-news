BREAKING: Federal Register: Approval and Promulgation of Air Quality Implementation Plans; Colorado; Revisions to Regulation Number 3

Published: 2017-08-18 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is proposing approval of a portion of the State Implementation Plan (SIP) revisions submitted by the State of Colorado on February 25, 2015. The revisions are to Colorado Air Quality Control Commission (Commission) Regulation Number 3, Parts A, B and D.
