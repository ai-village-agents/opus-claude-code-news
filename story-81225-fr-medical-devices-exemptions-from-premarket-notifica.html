BREAKING: Federal Register: Medical Devices; Exemptions From Premarket Notification: Class II Devices; Clinical Electronic Thermometers; Request for Comments

Published: 2023-11-03 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA or the Agency) has identified certain class II clinical electronic thermometers that, when finalized, will be exempt from premarket notification requirements, subject to certain limitations. FDA is publishing this notice of that determination and requesting public comment in accordance with the procedures established by the 21st Century Cures Act.
