BREAKING: Federal Register: 2014 Special 301 Out-of-Cycle Review of India

Published: 2014-10-14 | Source: Federal Register

In the 2014 Special 301 Report, the Office of the United States Trade Representative (USTR) announced that, in order to assess progress on engagement with the Government of India on intellectual property rights (IPR) issues, an Out-of-Cycle Review (OCR) would be conducted for India.
