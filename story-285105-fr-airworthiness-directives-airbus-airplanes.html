BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Airbus Airplanes

Published: 2015-01-29 | Source: Federal Register

We are adopting a new airworthiness directive (AD) for certain Airbus Model A300 B4-601, B4-603, B4-605R, F4-605R, and C4-605R Variant F airplanes. This AD was prompted by reports of cracking found in the pylon box, which was due to the stresses resulting from the pressure applied by the thrust reverser cowl bumpers.
