BREAKING: Federal Register: Indian Oil Valuation Negotiated Rulemaking Committee

Published: 2012-07-18 | Source: Federal Register

The Office of Natural Resources Revenue (ONRR) announces additional meetings for the Indian Oil Valuation Negotiated Rulemaking Committee (Committee). The third through sixth meetings of the Committee will take place on August 1 and 2, September 5 and 6, October 24 and 25, and December 11 and 12, 2012, in Building 85 of the Denver Federal Center.
