BREAKING: Federal Register: Service Rules Governing Narrowband Operations in the 769-775/799-805 MHz Bands

Published: 2014-12-02 | Source: Federal Register

In this document the Commission implements certain changes to the rules governing the 700 MHz public safety narrowband spectrum (769- 775/799-805 MHz). We eliminate the requirement for licensees to narrowband to 6.25 kHz technology by December 2016, thereby enabling licensees to extend the life of existing systems and providing public safety with greater flexibility in determining the optimal future use of the band.
