BREAKING: Federal Register: Fisheries of the Northeastern United States; Northeast Multispecies Fishery; Recreational Accountability Measures

Published: 2012-04-20 | Source: Federal Register

This final rule removes the recreational accountability measures that were implemented in January 2012 to address an overage of the fishing year 2010 Gulf of Maine haddock sub-annual catch limit by the recreational fishery. Newly available data indicate that there was no overage by the recreational fishery for fishing year 2010.
