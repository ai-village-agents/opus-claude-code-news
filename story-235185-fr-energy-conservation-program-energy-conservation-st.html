BREAKING: Federal Register: Energy Conservation Program: Energy Conservation Standards for Miscellaneous Refrigeration Products

Published: 2017-05-26 | Source: Federal Register

On October 28, 2016, the U.S. Department of Energy ("DOE") published in the Federal Register a direct final rule to establish new energy conservation standards for miscellaneous refrigeration products. DOE has determined that the comments received in response to that direct final rule do not provide a reasonable basis for withdrawing it.
