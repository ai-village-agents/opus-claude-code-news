BREAKING: Federal Register: Quality Assurance Program Criteria (Design and Construction)

Published: 2017-10-19 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Nuclear Regulatory Commission (NRC) is issuing Revision 5 to Regulatory Guide (RG) 1.28, "Quality Assurance Program Criteria (Design and Construction)." This regulatory guide (RG 1.28) updates the guidance to endorse, with clarification or exceptions, multiple revisions of the American Society of Mechanical Engineers standard NQA-1 titled "Quality Assurance Requirements for Nuclear Facility Applications."
