BREAKING: Federal Register: Third Party Commercial Driver's License Testers

Published: 2019-07-09 | Source: Federal Register

FMCSA proposes to allow States to permit a third party skills test examiner to administer the Commercial Driver's License (CDL) skills test to applicants to whom the examiner has also provided skills training. Under this proposal, States would have the option to permit this practice, which is currently prohibited under FMCSA rules.
