BREAKING: Federal Register: Fisheries of the Northeastern United States; Bluefish Fishery; Scoping Process

Published: 2020-02-06 | Source: Federal Register

The Mid-Atlantic Fishery Management Council announces its intent to prepare, in cooperation with the National Marine Fisheries Service and the Atlantic States Marine Fisheries Commission, an amendment to the Atlantic Bluefish Fishery Management Plan. An Environmental Impact Statement may be necessary for the amendment under the National Environmental Policy Act to analyze the impacts of any proposed management measures.
