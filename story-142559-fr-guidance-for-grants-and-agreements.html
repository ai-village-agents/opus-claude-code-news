BREAKING: Federal Register: Guidance for Grants and Agreements

Published: 2020-01-22 | Source: Federal Register

The Office of Management and Budget is proposing to revise sections of Title 2 of the Code of Federal Regulations (CFR) Subtitle A-OMB Guidance for Grants and Agreements.
