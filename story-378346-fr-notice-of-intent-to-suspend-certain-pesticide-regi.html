BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Intent To Suspend Certain Pesticide Registrations

Published: 2012-02-22 | Source: Federal Register

Pursuant to section 6(f)(2) of the Federal Insecticide, Fungicide and Rodenticide Act (FIFRA), EPA is printing this Notice of Intent to Suspend Drexel Basic Copper Sulfate Technical. This notice is issued by EPA pursuant to section 3(c)(2)(B) of FIFRA.
