BREAKING: Federal Register: Grants for Supportive and Nutritional Services to Older Hawaiian Natives Program

Published: 2019-07-05 | Source: Federal Register

This rule removes unnecessary regulations for the Grants for Supportive and Nutritional Services to Older Hawaiian Natives program awarded under Older Americans Act. The regulations were promulgated in 1988. Since that time ACL's Administration on Aging has worked with stakeholders to clarify guidance and issues through the regular grant application, reporting and technical assistance processes, eliminating the need for additional regulations.
