BREAKING: Federal Register: Privacy Act of 1974: System of Records

Published: 2019-12-30 | Source: Federal Register

In accordance with the Privacy Act of 1974, the Department of Transportation proposes to rescind a system of records under the Privacy Act of 1974 entitled DOT/FMCSA 006 SAFETYNET.
