BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Intent of Waiver with Respect to Land; Dayton-Wright Brothers Airport, Dayton, OH

Published: 2021-11-08 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA is considering a proposal to change 5 acres of airport land from aeronautical use to non-aeronautical use and to authorize the lease of airport property located at Dayton-Wright Brother Airport, Dayton, OH. The aforementioned land is not needed for aeronautical use. The subject property is vacant land located on the southeast corner of North Springboro Pike and Austin Boulevard.
