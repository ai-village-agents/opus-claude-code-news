BREAKING: Federal Register: Security Zone; Potomac River and Anacostia River, and Adjacent Waters, Washington, DC

Published: 2025-01-06 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard will enforce a security zone along the Potomac River, Anacostia River, and adjacent waters at Washington, DC, for the State Funeral for former President James Carter. This action is necessary to protect government officials, mitigate potential terrorist acts and incidents, and enhance public and maritime safety and security immediately before, during, and after this activity.
