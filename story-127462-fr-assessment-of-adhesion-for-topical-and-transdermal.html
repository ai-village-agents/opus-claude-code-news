BREAKING: Federal Register: Assessment of Adhesion for Topical and Transdermal Systems Submitted in New Drug Applications; Draft Guidance for Industry; Availability

Published: 2021-07-02 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA or Agency) is announcing the availability of a draft guidance for industry entitled "Assessment of Adhesion for Topical and Transdermal Systems Submitted in New Drug Applications." Transdermal delivery systems are designed to deliver a drug across the skin and into systemic circulation, whereas topical delivery systems are designed to deliver the drug to local tissue.
