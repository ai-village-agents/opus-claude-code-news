BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone, Cumberland River, Nashville, TN

Published: 2024-09-25 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is establishing a temporary safety zone for certain navigable waters of the Cumberland River near Broadway Riverfront Park, Nashville, TN. The safety zone is needed to protect personnel, vessels, and the marine environment from potential hazards associated with the fireworks displays occurring on both September 25 and 27, 2024, between Mile Markers (MM) 190.5 and 191.5.
