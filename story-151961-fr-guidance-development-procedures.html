BREAKING: Federal Register: Guidance Development Procedures

Published: 2020-05-19 | Source: Federal Register

This final rule responds to an Executive order titled "Promoting the Rule of Law Through Improved Agency Guidance Documents" (October 9, 2019). The central principle of the E.O. is that agency guidance documents should clarify existing obligations. Guidance documents are not permitted to impose new, binding requirements on the public.
