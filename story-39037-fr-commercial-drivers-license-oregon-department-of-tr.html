BREAKING: Federal Register: Commercial Driver's License: Oregon Department of Transportation; Application for Exemption

Published: 2024-06-11 | Source: Federal Register

FMCSA announces that the Oregon Department of Transportation (Oregon) has applied for a limited exemption from the commercial driver's license (CDL) rules concerning acceptable proof of citizenship or lawful permanent residency for citizens of Freely Associated States (FAS) who now reside in Oregon and wish to obtain a CDL.
