BREAKING: Federal Register: Federal Advisory Committee National Emergency Medical Services Advisory Council (NEMSAC); Notice of Meeting

Published: 2018-09-25 | Source: Federal Register

The NHTSA announces a meeting of the NEMSAC to be held in the Metropolitan Washington, DC, area. This notice announces the date, time, and location of the meeting, which will be open to the public, as well as opportunities for public input to the NEMSAC.
