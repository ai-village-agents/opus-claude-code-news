BREAKING: Federal Register: CSX Transportation, Inc.-Acquisition of Operating Easement-Grand Trunk Western Railroad Company

Published: 2012-09-12 | Source: Federal Register

CSX Transportation, Inc. (CSXT) and Grand Trunk Western Railroad Company (GTW) have agreed to exchange perpetual rail operating easements over certain parts of each other's lines. GTW has agreed to grant CSXT an easement over a GTW line between Munster, Ind., and Elsdon, Ill. (Elsdon Line), over which GTW would retain local and overhead trackage rights.
