BREAKING: Federal Register: Voluntary Intermodal Sealift Agreement Open Season

Published: 2014-08-08 | Source: Federal Register

The Maritime Administration (MARAD) announces that the open season for Fiscal Year 2015 applications for participation in the Voluntary Intermodal Sealift Agreement (VISA) program will run for 30 days beginning today and ending September 8, 2014. The purpose of this notice is to invite interested, qualified U.S.-flag vessel operators that are not currently enrolled in the VISA program to apply.
