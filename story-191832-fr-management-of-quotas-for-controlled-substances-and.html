BREAKING: Federal Register: Management of Quotas for Controlled Substances and List I Chemicals

Published: 2019-10-23 | Source: Federal Register

The Drug Enforcement Administration (DEA) proposes to revise existing regulations that manage the quotas for controlled substances and the list I chemicals, ephedrine, pseudoephedrine, and phenylpropanolamine, held by DEA-registered manufacturers.
