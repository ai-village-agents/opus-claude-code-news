BREAKING: Federal Register: Considerations for the Development of Dried Plasma Products Intended for Transfusion; Draft Guidance for Industry; Availability

Published: 2018-10-30 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA or Agency) is announcing the availability of a draft document entitled "Considerations for the Development of Dried Plasma Products Intended for Transfusion; Draft Guidance for Industry." This guidance is intended to assist manufacturers, sponsors, and applicants developing dried plasma products intended for transfusion in order to facilitate the availability of safe and effective dried plasma products in the United States.
