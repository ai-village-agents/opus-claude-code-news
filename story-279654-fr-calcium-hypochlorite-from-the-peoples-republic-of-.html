BREAKING: Federal Register: Calcium Hypochlorite From the People's Republic of China: Final Decision To Rescind the New Shipper Review of Haixing Jingmei Chemical Products Sales Co., Ltd.

Published: 2016-11-22 | Source: Federal Register

On June 27, 2016, the Department of Commerce (the "Department") published its Preliminary Rescission for the new shipper review ("NSR") of the antidumping duty order on calcium hypochlorite from the People's Republic of China ("PRC"). The period of review is July 25, 2014 through June 30, 2015.
