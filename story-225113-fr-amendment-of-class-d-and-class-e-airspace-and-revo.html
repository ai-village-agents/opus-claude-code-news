BREAKING: Federal Register: Amendment of Class D and Class E Airspace and Revocation of Class E Airspace; Roswell, NM

Published: 2017-01-10 | Source: Federal Register

This action modifies Class D airspace, Class E surface area airspace, and Class E airspace extending upward from 700 feet above the surface at Roswell, NM. This action is necessary due to advances Global Positioning System (GPS) capabilities and implementation of area navigation (RNAV) procedures at Roswell International Air Center, Roswell, NM.
