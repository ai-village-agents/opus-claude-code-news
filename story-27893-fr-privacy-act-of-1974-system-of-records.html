BREAKING: Federal Register: Privacy Act of 1974; System of Records

Published: 2024-01-24 | Source: Federal Register

VA is rescinding an outdated system of records titled, "Veteran, Employee and Citizen Health Care Facility Investigation Records--VA" (32VA10Q). This system was used to conduct statistical studies and analyses which supported the formulation of departmental policies and plans by identifying the total current health care usage of the VA patient population.
