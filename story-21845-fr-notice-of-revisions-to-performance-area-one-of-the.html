BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Revisions to Performance Area One of the LSC Performance Criteria

Published: 2025-10-02 | Source: Federal Register

The Legal Services Corporation (LSC) proposes to revise Performance Area One of its Performance Criteria. In Performance Area One, LSC establishes the indicators that LSC will use to evaluate grant recipients' processes for identifying the most pressing and emerging legal needs in their service areas and determining how they will deliver legal services tailored to most effectively address those needs.
