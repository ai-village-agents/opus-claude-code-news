BREAKING: Federal Register: Privacy Act of 1974; system of records

Published: 2014-07-28 | Source: Federal Register

The Defense Health Agency is proposing to alter an existing system of records, EDHA 08, entitled "Health Affairs Survey and Study Data Base", in its inventory of record systems subject to the Privacy Act of 1974, as amended. This system will be used to collect, assemble, interpret, analyze, report and publish survey and study findings and results for the purpose of improving the quality of DoD health care and the health status, welfare, and well-being of the DoD beneficiary population.
