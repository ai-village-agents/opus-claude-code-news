BREAKING: Federal Register: Music Licensing Study: Notice and Request for Public Comment

Published: 2014-03-17 | Source: Federal Register

The United States Copyright Office announces the initiation of a study to evaluate the effectiveness of existing methods of licensing music. To aid this effort, the Office is seeking public input on this topic. The Office will use the information it gathers to report to Congress.
