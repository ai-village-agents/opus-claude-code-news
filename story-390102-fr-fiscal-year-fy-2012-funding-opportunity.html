BREAKING: Federal Register: Fiscal Year (FY) 2012 Funding Opportunity

Published: 2012-07-02 | Source: Federal Register

This notice is to inform the public that the Substance Abuse and Mental Health Services Administration (SAMHSA) intends to award a programmatic supplement of approximately $800,000 (total costs) for up to one year to the current grantee of the Suicide Prevention Resource Center program. The current grantee is the Education Development Center, Inc. in Newton, Massachusetts. This is not a formal request for applications.
