BREAKING: Federal Register: Domestic Quarantine Regulations; Quarantined Areas and Regulated Articles

Published: 2022-06-14 | Source: Federal Register

We are proposing to revise the regulations that govern domestic quarantines for various plant pests by removing lists of quarantined areas and regulated articles from the regulations in order to maintain these lists on web pages maintained by the Agency. We are proposing these amendments because they would allow the Agency to be more responsive in updating the lists.
