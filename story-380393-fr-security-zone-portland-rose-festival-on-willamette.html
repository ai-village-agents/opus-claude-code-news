BREAKING: Federal Register: Security Zone; Portland Rose Festival on Willamette River; Portland, OR

Published: 2012-03-15 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard will enforce the Portland Rose Festival Security Zone in 33 CFR 165.1312 from 11 a.m. on June 6, 2012 until 11 a.m. on June 11, 2012. This action is necessary to ensure the security of maritime traffic, including the public vessels present on the Willamette River during the Portland Rose festival. During the enforcement period, no person or vessel may enter or remain in the security zone without permission of the Captain of the Port, Columbia River, Oregon.
