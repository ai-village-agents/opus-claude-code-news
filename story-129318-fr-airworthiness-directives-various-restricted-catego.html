BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Various Restricted Category Helicopters

Published: 2021-07-29 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA is adopting a new airworthiness directive (AD) for various restricted category helicopters originally manufactured by Bell Textron Inc. This AD was prompted by a fatal accident in which an outboard main rotor hub strap pin (pin) sheared off during flight, resulting in the main rotor blade and the main rotor head detaching from the helicopter.
