BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Airbus Airplanes

Published: 2016-08-30 | Source: Federal Register

We propose to adopt a new airworthiness directive (AD) for all Airbus Model A330-223F, -223, -321, -322, and -323 airplanes. The proposed AD was prompted by fatigue load analysis that determined the need for certain reduced inspection intervals and updated torque values of the forward mount pylon bolts.
