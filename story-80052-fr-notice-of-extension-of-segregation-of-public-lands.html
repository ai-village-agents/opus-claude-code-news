BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Extension of Segregation of Public Lands for the Copper Rays Solar Project, Nye County, NV

Published: 2023-10-20 | Source: Federal Register

The Bureau of Land Management (BLM) on October 21, 2021, segregated public lands included in the right-of-way application (N- 089655) for the Copper Rays Solar Project from appropriation under the public land laws, including the Mining Law, but not the Mineral Leasing or Material Sales Acts, for a period of 2 years subject to valid existing rights.
