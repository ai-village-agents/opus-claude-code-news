BREAKING: Federal Register: Artificial Island Anchorage No. 2 Partial Closure, Delaware River; Salem, NJ

Published: 2013-02-15 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard will be establishing a temporary safety zone around the southern portion of Anchorage 2 (Artificial Island Anchorage) below position 39[deg]29'20" N-075[deg]33'30" W to position 39[deg]29'12.5" N-075[deg]33'0" W due to dredging operations. The hopper dredge STUYVESANT will be working in the area, and 4,000 feet of submerged pipe line will cross the closed portion of the anchorage.
