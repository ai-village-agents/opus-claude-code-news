BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Airbus Airplanes

Published: 2013-12-02 | Source: Federal Register

We are adopting a new airworthiness directive (AD) for certain Airbus Model A318-112, A319-111, A319-112, A319-115, A319-132, and A319-133 airplanes. This AD was prompted by a report that a fastener, which connects the cargo door keel beam foot to the circumferential butt-strap and the section 13-14 lower shell panel, was not installed on airplanes during production.
