BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Airbus SAS Airplanes

Published: 2025-11-25 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA is superseding Airworthiness Directive (AD) 2023-14- 09, which applied to certain Airbus SAS Model A350-941 and -1041 airplanes. AD 2023-14-09 required an inspection for missing or incorrectly applied sealant in the wing tanks, applicable corrective actions, and a modification to restore two independent layers of lightning strike protection.
