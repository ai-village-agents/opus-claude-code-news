BREAKING: Federal Register: Contact Lens Rule

Published: 2015-09-03 | Source: Federal Register

The Commission is requesting public comments on the Contact Lens Rule, which requires that eyecare prescribers provide a copy of a consumer's prescription to the consumer upon completion of a contact lens fitting and verify or provide prescriptions to authorized third parties.
