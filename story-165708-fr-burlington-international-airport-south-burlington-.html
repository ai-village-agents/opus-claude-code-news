BREAKING: Federal Register: Burlington International Airport, South Burlington VT; FAA Approval of Noise Compatibility Program

Published: 2020-11-10 | Source: Federal Register

The Federal Aviation Administration (FAA) announces its findings on the noise compatibility program submitted by the City of Burlington, Vermont under the provisions of Title I of the Aviation Safety and Noise Abatement Act of 1979. These findings are made in recognition of the description of federal and non-federal responsibilities in Senate Report No. 96-52 (1980).
