BREAKING: Federal Register: Habitat Conservation Plan for Lorraine Tveten Single-Family Residence, Thurston County, Washington

Published: 2021-04-27 | Source: Federal Register

We, the U.S. Fish and Wildlife Service, received an application from Ms. Lorraine Tveten for an incidental take permit (ITP) pursuant to the Endangered Species Act of 1973, as amended. The ITP would authorize "take" of the Yelm pocket gopher, incidental to otherwise lawful activities during construction of a single-family home and agricultural buildings in Thurston County, Washington.
