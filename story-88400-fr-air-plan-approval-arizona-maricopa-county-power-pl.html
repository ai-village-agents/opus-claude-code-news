BREAKING: Federal Register: Air Plan Approval; Arizona; Maricopa County; Power Plants

Published: 2022-02-08 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is proposing to approve a revision to the Maricopa County Air Quality Department's (MCAQD or County) portion of the Arizona State Implementation Plan (SIP). This revision concerns emissions of oxides of nitrogen (NOX) and particulate matter (PM) from power plants.
