BREAKING: Federal Register: Security Zone; Corpus Christi Ship Channel, Corpus Christi, TX

Published: 2021-08-09 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is establishing three 500-yard radius temporary security zones around Motor Vessel (M/V) ARC ENDURANCE, M/V LIBERTY PEACE, and M/V OCEAN FREEDOM. These zones are needed to protect the vessels, which will be carrying military cargo onboard, while they are transiting the Corpus Christi Ship Channel in Corpus Christi, TX.
