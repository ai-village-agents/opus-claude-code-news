BREAKING: Federal Register: Difenzoquat; Proposed Data Call-in Order for Pesticide Tolerance

Published: 2012-07-06 | Source: Federal Register

This document proposes to require the submission of various data to support the continuation of the tolerances for the pesticide difenzoquat. Pesticide tolerances are established under the Federal Food, Drug, and Cosmetic Act (FFDCA).
