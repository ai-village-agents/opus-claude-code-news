BREAKING: Federal Register: International Workshop on Alternative Methods for Leptospira Vaccine Potency Testing: State of the Science and the Way Forward

Published: 2012-07-26 | Source: Federal Register

The NTP Interagency Center for the Evaluation of Alternative Toxicological Methods (NICEATM) announces an "International Workshop on Alternative Methods for Leptospira Vaccine Potency Testing: State of the Science and the Way Forward." This workshop, the second in a series of specialized vaccine workshops, will review recent advances and innovations in science and technology that can be applied to Leptospira vaccine potency testing.
