BREAKING: Federal Register: National Practitioner Data Bank Continues Temporary Waiver of User Fees for Eligible Entities

Published: 2020-07-06 | Source: Federal Register

Pursuant to its authority under Federal regulations for the National Practitioner Data Bank (NPDB), HRSA's Division of Practitioner Data Bank announces a continuation of its temporary waiver of user fees for NPDB queries from March 1, 2020, through September 30, 2020, to support our eligible entities in making credentialing, hiring, privileging, and licensing decisions in combatting the COVID-19 pandemic.
