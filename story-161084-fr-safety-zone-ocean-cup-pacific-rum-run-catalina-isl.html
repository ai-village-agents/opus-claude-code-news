BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone; Ocean Cup, Pacific Rum Run, Catalina Island, California

Published: 2020-09-15 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Coast Guard proposes to establish a one-time, temporary safety zone near Ship Rock, Catalina Island, in support of the Ocean Cup Pacific Rum Run. This action is necessary to protect the area near Ship Rock, Catalina Island, public vessels, and the high speed vessels participating in the event.
