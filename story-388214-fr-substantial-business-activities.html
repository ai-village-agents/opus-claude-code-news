BREAKING: Federal Register: Substantial Business Activities

Published: 2012-06-12 | Source: Federal Register

This document contains temporary regulations regarding whether a foreign corporation has substantial business activities in a foreign country. These regulations affect certain domestic corporations and partnerships (and certain parties related thereto), and foreign corporations that acquire substantially all of the properties of such domestic corporations or partnerships.
