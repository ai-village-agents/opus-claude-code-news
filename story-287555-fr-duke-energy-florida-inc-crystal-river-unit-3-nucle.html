BREAKING: Federal Register: Duke Energy Florida, Inc.; Crystal River Unit 3 Nuclear Generating Plant

Published: 2015-03-02 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Nuclear Regulatory Commission (NRC) is considering issuance of exemptions in response to a request from Duke Energy Florida, Inc. (DEF, the licensee) that would permit the licensee to reduce its emergency planning (EP) activities at the Crystal River Unit 3 Nuclear Generating Plant (CR-3). CR-3 has been shut down since September 26, 2009, and the final removal of fuel from the reactor vessel was completed on May 28, 2011.
