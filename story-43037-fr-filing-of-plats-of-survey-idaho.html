BREAKING: Federal Register: Filing of Plats of Survey: Idaho

Published: 2024-07-31 | Source: Federal Register

The plats of survey of the following described lands are scheduled to be officially filed in the Bureau of Land Management (BLM), Idaho State Office (ISO), Boise, Idaho, 30 days from the date of this publication. The plats were prepared to meet administrative needs of various Federal agencies.
