BREAKING: Federal Register: Approval of International Marine Consultants (San Juan, PR) as a Commercial Gauger

Published: 2025-08-20 | Source: Federal Register

Notice is hereby given, pursuant to CBP regulations, that International Marine Consultants (San Juan, PR), has been approved to gauge petroleum and certain petroleum products for customs purposes for the next three years as of October 2, 2024.
