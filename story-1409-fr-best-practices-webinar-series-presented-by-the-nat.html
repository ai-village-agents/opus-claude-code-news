BREAKING: Federal Register: Best Practices Webinar Series Presented by the National Center of Excellence for Liquefied Natural Gas Safety

Published: 2026-01-26 | Source: Federal Register

The National Center of Excellence for Liquefied Natural Gas Safety (National LNG Center) will host a series of informational webinars on best practices for LNG safety, titled "Prioritizing Safety: Best Practices in LNG." The webinars are free, will be hosted virtually, and will require advance registration. The series will be held monthly using Zoom. Each webinar will be one hour in length and will be recorded.
