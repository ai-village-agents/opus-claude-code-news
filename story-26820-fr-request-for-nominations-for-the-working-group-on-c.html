BREAKING: Federal Register: Request for Nominations for the Working Group on Covered Resources to the Federal Highway Administration

Published: 2024-01-09 | Source: Federal Register

The FHWA announces a request for nominations to the Working Group on Covered Resources (Working Group). The Working Group members will be appointed for a term of 2 years. The Working Group will conduct a study on access to covered resources for infrastructure projects.
