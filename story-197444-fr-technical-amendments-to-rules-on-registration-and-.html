BREAKING: Federal Register: Technical Amendments to Rules on Registration and Review of Exchange Disciplinary, Access Denial, or Other Adverse Actions

Published: 2018-01-12 | Source: Federal Register

The Commodity Futures Trading Commission ("CFTC" or "Commission") is adopting certain amendments to its rules that, respectively, govern registration of intermediaries and relate to the Commission's review of exchange disciplinary, access denial, or other adverse actions. Generally speaking, these amendments are technical in nature. The amendments to both areas of the rules integrate existing advisory guidance.
