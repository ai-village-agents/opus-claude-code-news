BREAKING: Federal Register: Air Plan Approval; ID, Crop Residue Burning; Revision to Ozone Requirement

Published: 2018-01-22 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is proposing to approve revisions to the portions of Idaho's State Implementation Plan (SIP) related to agricultural crop residue burning. The Director of the Idaho Department of Environmental Quality (IDEQ) submitted the revisions to EPA on September 22, 2017. IDEQ supplemented the original submission with photochemical modeling analyses on October 23, 2017.
