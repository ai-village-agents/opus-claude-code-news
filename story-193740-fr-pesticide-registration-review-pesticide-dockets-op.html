BREAKING: Federal Register: Pesticide Registration Review; Pesticide Dockets Opened for Review and Comment; Notice of Availability

Published: 2019-11-20 | Source: Federal Register

This notice announces the availability of the EPA's preliminary work plans for the following chemicals: Pyrimethanil and saflufenacil. With this document, the EPA is opening the public comment period for registration review for these chemicals. This notice also announces the availability of EPA's draft ecological risk assessment for the pesticide pyrimethanil and opens a 60-day public comment period on the draft risk assessment.
