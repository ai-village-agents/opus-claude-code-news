BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Public Meetings of the Oklahoma Advisory Committee

Published: 2020-06-29 | Source: Federal Register

Notice is hereby given, pursuant to the provisions of the rules and regulations of the U.S. Commission on Civil Rights (Commission) and the Federal Advisory Committee Act that the Oklahoma Advisory Committee (Committee) will hold a series of meetings via teleconference on Tuesday, July 28, Monday, August 17, 2020 at 2:00pm Central Time. The purpose of discussing the Committee's project proposal on policing.
