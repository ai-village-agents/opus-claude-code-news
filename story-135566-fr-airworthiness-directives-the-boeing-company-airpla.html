BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; The Boeing Company Airplanes

Published: 2021-10-14 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA proposes to adopt a new airworthiness directive (AD) for certain The Boeing Company Model 747-400, -400D, and -400F series airplanes.
