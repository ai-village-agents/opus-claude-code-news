BREAKING: Federal Register: Georgia Disaster #GA-00090

Published: 2017-02-06 | Source: Federal Register

Incident Period: 01/21/2017 through 01/22/2017. Effective Date: 01/26/2017. Physical Loan Application Deadline Date: 03/27/2017. Economic Injury (EIDL) Loan Application Deadline Date: 10/26/2017.
