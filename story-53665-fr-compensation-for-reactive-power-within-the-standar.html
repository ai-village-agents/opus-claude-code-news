BREAKING: Federal Register: Compensation for Reactive Power Within the Standard Power Factor Range

Published: 2024-11-26 | Source: Federal Register

In this final determination, the Federal Energy Regulatory Commission (Commission) finds that allowing transmission providers to charge transmission customers for a generating facility's provision of reactive power within the standard power factor range is unjust and unreasonable.
