BREAKING: Federal Register: Alternative Personnel Management System (APMS) at the National Institute of Standards and Technology

Published: 2022-04-28 | Source: Federal Register

This notice modifies the National Institute of Standards and Technology (NIST) Alternative Personnel Management System (APMS) published October 21, 1997 to allow NIST to implement a term appointment with a minimum duration of more than one year that may be extended up to six years, for research positions in the Scientific and Engineering (ZP) career path at the Pay Band III-level and above.
