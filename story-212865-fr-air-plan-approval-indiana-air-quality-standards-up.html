BREAKING: Federal Register: Air Plan Approval; Indiana; Air Quality Standards Update for the 2015 Ozone Standard

Published: 2018-07-31 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is approving a September 7, 2017, request by the Indiana Department of Environmental Management (IDEM) to revise the Indiana state implementation plan (SIP) for ozone. IDEM revised its ozone standard in order to be consistent with EPA's 2015 revisions to the 8-hour national ambient air quality standards (NAAQS).
