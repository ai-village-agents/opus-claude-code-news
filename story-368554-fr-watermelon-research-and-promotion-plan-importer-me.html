BREAKING: Federal Register: Watermelon Research and Promotion Plan; Importer Membership Requirements and Referendum Order

Published: 2013-10-29 | Source: Federal Register

This proposed rule would amend the Watermelon Research and Promotion Plan (Plan) importer membership requirements to serve on the National Watermelon Promotion Board (Board). The Board recommended eliminating the requirement that an importer import more than 50 percent of the total volume handled and imported in order to qualify as an importer member.
