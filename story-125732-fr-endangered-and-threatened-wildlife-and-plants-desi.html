BREAKING: Federal Register: Endangered and Threatened Wildlife and Plants; Designating Texas Hornshell Critical Habitat

Published: 2021-06-10 | Source: Federal Register

We, the U.S. Fish and Wildlife Service (Service), propose to designate critical habitat for the Texas hornshell (Popenaias popeii), a freshwater mussel, under the Endangered Species Act (Act). In total, the proposed critical habitat designation includes approximately 463.6 river miles (745.9 kilometers) in Eddy County, New Mexico, and in Culberson, Brewster, Terrell, Val Verde, Kinney, Maverick, and Webb Counties, Texas.
