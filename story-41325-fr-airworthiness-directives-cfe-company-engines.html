BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; CFE Company Engines

Published: 2024-07-09 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA is adopting a new airworthiness directive (AD) for certain CFE Company (CFE) Model CFE738-1-1B engines. This AD was prompted by a manufacturer investigation that revealed certain high- pressure turbine (HPT) stage 1 and stage 2 disks were manufactured from powder metal material suspected to contain iron inclusion.
