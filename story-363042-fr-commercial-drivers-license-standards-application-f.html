BREAKING: Federal Register: Commercial Driver's License Standards: Application for Exemption; Miami Nice Tours

Published: 2013-08-16 | Source: Federal Register

FMCSA announces that Miami Nice Tours (Miami) has applied for an exemption from the commercial driver's license (CDL) provisions of part 383 of the Federal Motor Carrier Safety Regulations (FMCSRs) (49 CFR 350-399) for itself and 50 European drivers. Miami, a motor carrier, would employ the 50 European drivers to conduct approximately 87 motorcoach tours in the United States annually.
