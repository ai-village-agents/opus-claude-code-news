BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; The Boeing Company Airplanes

Published: 2025-11-14 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA is adopting a new airworthiness directive (AD) for all The Boeing Company Model MD-11 and MD-11F airplanes. This AD was prompted by an accident where the left-hand engine and pylon detached from the airplane during takeoff. This AD prohibits further flight until the airplane is inspected and all applicable corrective actions are performed using a method approved by the FAA.
