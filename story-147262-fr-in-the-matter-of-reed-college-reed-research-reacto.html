BREAKING: Federal Register: In the Matter of Reed College, Reed Research Reactor, Portland, Oregon

Published: 2020-03-20 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Nuclear Regulatory Commission (NRC) is issuing a Confirmatory Order (CO) to Reed College, Portland, Oregon as a result of a successful alternative dispute resolution (ADR) mediation session held on January 23, 2020.
