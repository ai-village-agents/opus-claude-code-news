BREAKING: Federal Register: Implementing Public Safety Broadband Provisions of the Middle Class Tax Relief and Job Creation Act of 2012

Published: 2016-09-16 | Source: Federal Register

In this document, the Federal Communications Commission (Commission) addresses the 758-769/788-799 MHz band, which the Commission licensed to the First Responder Network Authority (FirstNet) on a nationwide basis pursuant to the provisions of the Middle Class Tax Relief and Job Creation Act of 2012. We provide a mechanism to facilitate the relocation of the public safety narrowband incumbents currently operating on FirstNet's spectrum.
