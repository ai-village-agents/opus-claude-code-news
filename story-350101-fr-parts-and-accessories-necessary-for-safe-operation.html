BREAKING: Federal Register: Parts and Accessories Necessary for Safe Operation; Exemption Renewal for Greyhound Lines, Inc.

Published: 2013-03-22 | Source: Federal Register

FMCSA renews Greyhound Lines, Inc.'s (Greyhound) exemption which allows the placement of video event recorders at the top of the windshields on its buses. Greyhound may continue to use the video event recorders to increase safety through (1) Identification and remediation of risky driving behaviors such as distracted driving and drowsiness; (2) enhanced monitoring of passenger behavior; and (3) enhanced collision review and analysis.
