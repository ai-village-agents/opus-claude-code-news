BREAKING: Federal Register: Civil Monetary Penalty Adjustments for Inflation

Published: 2022-01-04 | Source: Federal Register

This final rule is being issued to adjust for inflation each civil monetary penalty (CMP) provided by law within the jurisdiction of the United States Department of Commerce (Department of Commerce). The Department of Commerce's 2022 adjustments for inflation to CMPs apply only to CMPs with a dollar amount, and will not apply to CMPs written as functions of violations.
