BREAKING: Federal Register: Proposed Establishment of the Palos Verdes Peninsula Viticultural Area

Published: 2020-05-26 | Source: Federal Register

The Alcohol and Tobacco Tax and Trade Bureau (TTB) proposes to establish the approximately 15,900-acre "Palos Verdes Peninsula" viticultural area in the southwestern coastal region of Los Angeles County, California. The proposed viticultural area does not lie within, nor does it contain, any other established viticultural area.
