BREAKING: Federal Register: Termination of Global Patent Prosecution Highway With Rospatent

Published: 2022-04-04 | Source: Federal Register

The United States Patent and Trademark Office (USPTO or Office) will no longer grant requests to participate in the Global Patent Prosecution Highway (GPPH) at the USPTO when such requests are based on work performed by Rospatent as an Office of Earlier Examination under the GPPH.
