BREAKING: Federal Register: Department of State FY 2015 Service Contract Inventory

Published: 2016-04-22 | Source: Federal Register

Acting in compliance with Sec. 743 of Division C of the Consolidated Appropriations Act of 2010 (Pub. L. 111-117), the Department of State is publishing this notice to advise the public of the availability of the FY 2015 Service Contract Inventory. The FY 2015 Service Contract Inventory includes the Planned Analysis, and Summary, Detailed, and Supplement Reports. The FY 2014 Meaningful Analysis is also available.
