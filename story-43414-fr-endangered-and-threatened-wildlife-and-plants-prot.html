BREAKING: Federal Register: Endangered and Threatened Wildlife and Plants; Protective Regulations for the Oceanic Whitetip Shark (Carcharhinus longimanus), Public Hearings

Published: 2024-08-05 | Source: Federal Register

We, NMFS, will hold two public hearings related to our proposed rule to apply protective regulations to the oceanic whitetip shark (Carcharhinus longimanus) under the Endangered Species Act (ESA).
