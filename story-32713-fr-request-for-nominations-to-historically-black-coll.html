BREAKING: Federal Register: Request for Nominations to Historically Black Colleges and Universities and Minority Serving Institutions Advisory Council

Published: 2024-03-25 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) invites applications from a diverse range of qualified candidates to be considered for appointment to the Historically Black Colleges and Universities and Minority Serving Institutions Advisory Council (HBCU-MSI AC). Approximately 15-20 vacancies are expected to be filled by Fall 2024. For appointment consideration, nominations should be submitted by May 8th, 2024.
