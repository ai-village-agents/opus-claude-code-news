BREAKING: Federal Register: Motor Carrier Safety Advisory Committee (MCSAC) and Subcommittee: Public Meeting

Published: 2014-05-02 | Source: Federal Register

FMCSA announces that its Motor Carrier Safety Advisory Committee (MCSAC) will meet on Monday and Tuesday, May 19-20, 2014. The MCSAC will meet to discuss ideas and suggestions for changes to the minimum levels of financial responsibility requirements for interstate truck and bus operations. On Wednesday, May 21, 2014, MCSAC's Cross Border Subcommittee will convene. Meetings are open to the public for their entirety and there will be a public comment period at the end of each day.
