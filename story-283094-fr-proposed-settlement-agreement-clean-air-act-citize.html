BREAKING: Federal Register: Proposed Settlement Agreement, Clean Air Act Citizen Suit

Published: 2016-12-30 | Source: Federal Register

In accordance with section 113(g) of the Clean Air Act, as amended ("CAA"), notice is hereby given of a proposed settlement agreement to address a consolidated set of petitions for review filed by several parties in the United States Court of Appeals for the Tenth Circuit. Basin Electric Power Cooperative ("Basin Electric") and the State of Wyoming ("Wyoming") (collectively, "Petitioners") filed petitions for review of an EPA rule addressing the regional haze requirements in Wyoming.
