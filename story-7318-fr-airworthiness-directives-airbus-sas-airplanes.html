BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Airbus SAS Airplanes

Published: 2025-03-18 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA is adopting a new airworthiness directive (AD) for all Airbus SAS Model A350-941 and A350-1041 airplanes. This AD was prompted by a report indicating that the thrust reverser and pylon thermal blankets were found damaged due to air leaking from the pre-cooler exchanger (PCE).
