BREAKING: Federal Register: Approval and Promulgation of Air Quality Implementation Plans; Rhode Island; Transportation Conformity

Published: 2015-03-10 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is approving a State Implementation Plan (SIP) revision submitted by the State of Rhode Island on February 21, 2014. This revision includes a regulation adopted by Rhode Island that establishes procedures to follow for transportation conformity determinations.
