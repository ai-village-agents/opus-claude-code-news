BREAKING: Federal Register: Railroad Workplace Safety; Roadway Worker Protection Miscellaneous Revisions (RRR)

Published: 2012-08-20 | Source: Federal Register

FRA is proposing to amend its regulations on railroad workplace safety to resolve interpretative issues that have arisen since the 1996 promulgation of the original Roadway Worker Protection (RWP) regulation.
