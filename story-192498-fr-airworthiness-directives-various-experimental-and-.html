BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Various Experimental and Restricted Category Helicopters

Published: 2019-10-31 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA proposes to adopt a new airworthiness directive (AD) for various helicopters operating under experimental airworthiness certificates and various type certificated restricted category helicopters. This proposed AD was prompted by multiple accidents and incidents involving failure of the tail boom attachment structure and bolts.
