BREAKING: Federal Register: Trinity Adaptive Management Working Group; Public Meeting and Teleconference

Published: 2014-02-20 | Source: Federal Register

We, the U.S. Fish and Wildlife Service, announce a public meeting and teleconference of the Trinity Adaptive Management Working Group (TAMWG). The TAMWG is a Federal advisory committee that affords stakeholders the opportunity to give policy, management, and technical input concerning Trinity River (California) restoration efforts to the Trinity Management Council (TMC).
