BREAKING: Federal Register: Golden Nematode; Removal of Regulated Areas in Orleans, Nassau, and Suffolk Counties, New York

Published: 2015-10-02 | Source: Federal Register

We are amending the golden nematode regulations by removing areas in Orleans, Nassau, and Suffolk Counties in the State of New York from the list of generally infested areas. Surveys and other data have shown that certain areas in these counties are free of golden nematode, and we have determined that regulation of these areas is no longer necessary.
