BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Termination of Draft Desert Plan Amendment and Draft Environmental Impact Statement, California

Published: 2021-03-12 | Source: Federal Register

By this notice, the Bureau of Land Management (BLM) is announcing the termination of the land use planning process described in the Draft Land Use Plan Amendment (LUPA) and Draft Environmental Impact Statement (EIS) for an amendment to the California Desert Conservation Area (CDCA) Plan and the Bakersfield and Bishop Resource Management Plans (RMPs).
