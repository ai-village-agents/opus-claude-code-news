BREAKING: Federal Register: Recurring Safety Zone; EQT 4th of July Celebration, Pittsburgh, PA

Published: 2018-06-22 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard will enforce the safety zone for the EQT 4th of July Celebration to provide for the safety of persons, vessels, and the marine environment on the navigable waters of the Ohio, Allegheny, and Monongahela Rivers during this event. Our regulation for marine events within the Eighth Coast Guard District identifies the regulated area for this event in Pittsburgh, PA.
