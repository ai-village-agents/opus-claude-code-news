BREAKING: Federal Register: Waivers and Alternative Requirements for Implementation of the HOME American Rescue Plan (HOME-ARP) Program

This notice publishes the waivers and alternative requirements that apply to a grantee's use of HOME Investment Partnerships Program funds made available under Section 3205 of the American Rescue Plan Act (ARP) of 2021 ("HOME-ARP").
