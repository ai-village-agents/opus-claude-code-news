BREAKING: Federal Register: Public Notice for Waiver of Aeronautical Land-Use Assurance

Published: 2013-06-14 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA is considering a proposal to change a 3.03-acre portion of Parcel AA of airport land from aeronautical use to non- aeronautical use (for lease), located at Quad City International Airport, Moline, Illinois. The subject 3.03-acre portion of Parcel AA (10.84 total acres) is located in the north quadrant of the airport along Airport Drive and currently not being used directly for aeronautical purposes other than for the protection of FAR Part 77 surfaces and compatible land use.
