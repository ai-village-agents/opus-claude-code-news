BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Airbus SAS Airplanes

Published: 2019-02-07 | Source: Federal Register

We propose to adopt a new airworthiness directive (AD) for all Airbus SAS Model A319-113 and -114 airplanes, and Model A320-211 and - 212 airplanes. This proposed AD was prompted by a report that a life- limit of 64,000 flight cycles has been established for certain titanium crossbeams of the forward engine mount.
