BREAKING: Federal Register: Energy Conservation Program: Test Procedure for Portable Air Conditioners

Published: 2023-05-15 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Department of Energy ("DOE") amends the current test procedure for portable air conditioners ("portable ACs") to incorporate a measure of variable-speed portable AC performance, generally consistent with previously granted waivers, and to make minor clarifying edits. DOE also establishes a new test procedure for portable ACs that provides more representative measures of cooling capacity and energy consumption.
