BREAKING: Federal Register: Proposed Modification of Class D and Class E Airspace, and Proposed Establishment of Class E Airspace; Southwest Oregon Regional Airport, OR

Published: 2021-10-28 | Source: Federal Register

This action proposes to modify the Class D and Class E surface airspace at Southwest Oregon Regional Airport, North Bend, OR. It also proposes to modify the Class E airspace by establishing an area that is designated as an extension to a Class D or Class E surface area, and to modify the Class E airspace extending upward from 700 feet above the surface.
