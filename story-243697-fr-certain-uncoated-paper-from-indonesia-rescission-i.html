BREAKING: Federal Register: Certain Uncoated Paper From Indonesia: Rescission, in Part, of Countervailing Duty Administrative Review; 2015-2017

Published: 2017-09-15 | Source: Federal Register

On May 9, 2017, the Department of Commerce (the Department) initiated an administrative review of the countervailing duty (CVD) order on certain uncoated paper (uncoated paper) from Indonesia for two companies for the period June 29, 2015, through December 31, 2016. Based on a timely withdrawal of a request for review, we are now rescinding this administrative review with respect to one company, PT. Indah Kiat Pulp and Paper Tbk, PT.
