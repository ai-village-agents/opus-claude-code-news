BREAKING: Federal Register: Emergency Clearance: Public Information Collection Requirements Submitted to the Office of Management and Budget (OMB)

Published: 2020-08-17 | Source: Federal Register

The Centers for Medicare and Medicaid Services (CMS) is requesting that a new information collection request (ICR) associated with the Temporary Policy on 2020 Premium Credits Associated with the COVID-19 Public Health Emergency be processed under the emergency clearance process. Due to agencies inability to update CMS systems for IRS reporting purposes in time for tax season if the normal non- emergency clearance procedures are followed, an emergency clearance is requested.
