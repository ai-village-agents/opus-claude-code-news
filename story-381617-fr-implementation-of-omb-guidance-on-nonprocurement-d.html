BREAKING: Federal Register: Implementation of OMB Guidance on Nonprocurement Debarment and Suspension

Published: 2012-03-28 | Source: Federal Register

The Secretary of the Department of Education (Department) establishes a new part in 2 CFR that adopts the Office of Management and Budget's (OMB's) guidance, as supplemented by this new part, as the Department's regulations for nonprocurement debarment and suspension. The Secretary removes regulations that contain the Department's current implementation of the Governmentwide common rule on nonprocurement debarment and suspension.
