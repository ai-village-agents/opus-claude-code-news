BREAKING: Federal Register: Menstrual Products-Performance Testing and Labeling Recommendations; Draft Guidance for Industry and Food and Drug Administration Staff; Availability

Published: 2025-11-17 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA or Agency) is announcing the availability of the draft guidance entitled "Menstrual Products-- Performance Testing and Labeling Recommendations." This draft guidance document provides recommendations for performance testing, labeling, and information for inclusion in premarket notification (510(k)) submissions, when necessary, for certain menstrual products.
