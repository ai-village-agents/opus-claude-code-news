BREAKING: Federal Register: Submission for OMB Review; Comment Request

Published: 2012-02-29 | Source: Federal Register

The Bureau of Consumer Financial Protection (Bureau), as part of its continuing effort to reduce paperwork burdens, invites the general public and other Federal agencies to comment on a proposed information collection, as required by the Paperwork Reduction Act of 1995, Public Law 104-13. The Bureau is soliciting comments regarding the information collection requirements contained in 12 CFR part 1082, State Official Notification Rule that has been submitted to the Office of Management and Budget...
