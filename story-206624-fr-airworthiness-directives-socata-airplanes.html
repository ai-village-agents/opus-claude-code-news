BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; SOCATA Airplanes

Published: 2018-05-09 | Source: Federal Register

We propose to supersede Airworthiness Directive (AD) 98-16-03 for SOCATA Models TB 9 and TB 10 airplanes. This proposed AD results from mandatory continuing airworthiness information (MCAI) originated by an aviation authority of another country to identify and correct an unsafe condition on an aviation product.
