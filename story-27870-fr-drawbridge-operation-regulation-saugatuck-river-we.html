BREAKING: Federal Register: Drawbridge Operation Regulation; Saugatuck River, Westport, CT

Published: 2024-01-24 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is altering the operating schedule that governs the Metro-North (SAGA) Bridge, across the Saugatuck River, mile 1.1, at Westport, CT. The bridge owner, Metro-North (MNR), submitted a request on May 5, 2022, to modify the regulation to align with the Metro-North "WALK" Bridge train schedule and avoid bridge openings during peak transit hours.
