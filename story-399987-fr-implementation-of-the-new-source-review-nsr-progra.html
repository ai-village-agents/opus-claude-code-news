BREAKING: Federal Register: Implementation of the New Source Review (NSR) Program for Particulate Matter Less Than 2.5 Micrometers (PM2.5): Amendment to the Definition of “Regulated NSR Pollutant” Concerning Condensable Particulate Matter

Published: 2012-10-25 | Source: Federal Register

The EPA is issuing a final rule that revises the definition of "regulated NSR pollutant" contained in two sets of Prevention of Significant Deterioration (PSD) regulations and in the EPA's Emission Offset Interpretative Ruling. The revision corrects an inadvertent error made in 2008 when the EPA issued its rule to implement the New Source Review (NSR) program for fine particles with an aerodynamic diameter of less than or equal to 2.5 micrometers (PM2.5).
