BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Bombardier, Inc. Airplanes

Published: 2015-03-23 | Source: Federal Register

We are superseding Airworthiness Directive (AD) 2014-06-08 for certain Bombardier, Inc. Model DHC-8-100, -200, and -300 series airplanes. AD 2014-06-08 required repetitive functional checks of the nose and main landing gear, and corrective actions if necessary; and also provided optional terminating action modification for the repetitive functional checks.
