BREAKING: Federal Register: New Dates for the April and October 2021 Customs Broker's License Examinations

Published: 2020-12-31 | Source: Federal Register

This document announces that U.S. Customs and Border Protection has changed the dates on which the semi-annual examination for an individual broker's license will be held in April and October 2021.
