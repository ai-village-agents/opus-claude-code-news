BREAKING: Federal Register: Drawbridge Operation Regulation; Saugus River, Revere and Lynn, MA

Published: 2014-04-25 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard proposes to modify the operating schedule that governs the General Edwards SR1A Bridge across the Saugus River at mile 1.7, between Revere and Lynn, Massachusetts. The bridge owner, Massachusetts Department of Transportation, submitted a request to require a two-hour advance notice for bridge openings at all times based upon infrequent requests to open the draw during past years.
