BREAKING: Federal Register: Entergy Operations, Inc; Waterford Steam Electric Station; Unit 3

Published: 2021-12-29 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Nuclear Regulatory Commission (NRC) has issued an exemption in response to an October 12, 2021, request from Entergy Operations, Inc., as supplemented on December 13, 2021. The licensee requested a one-time schedular exemption for Waterford Steam Electric Station, Unit 3, to postpone the current scheduled Emergency Preparedness (EP) biennial exercise until Calendar Year 2022.
