BREAKING: Federal Register: Head Start Performance Standards; Extension of Comment Period

Published: 2015-08-12 | Source: Federal Register

The Administration for Children and Families extends the comment period for the notice of proposed rulemaking entitled, "Head Start Performance Standards." We take this action to respond to requests from the public for more time to submit comments. The notice of proposed rulemaking and our request for comments appeared in the Federal Register on June 19, 2015. We initially set August 18, 2015 as the deadline for the comment period.
