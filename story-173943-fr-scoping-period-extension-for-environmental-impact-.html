BREAKING: Federal Register: Scoping Period Extension for Environmental Impact Statement for Implementing Test Releases From Fort Peck Dam, Montana

Published: 2019-03-13 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Army Corps of Engineers (USACE), Omaha District, is extending the scoping period through March 26, 2019. The scoping period was originally scheduled to end on March 11, 2019.
