BREAKING: Federal Register: Special Conditions: Peregrine; Installed Rechargeable Lithium Batteries

Published: 2022-01-26 | Source: Federal Register

These special conditions are issued for a supplemental type certificate to install rechargeable lithium batteries in the Emergency Exit Light (EEL) power supply on certain transport category airplanes. These airplanes, as modified by Peregrine, will have a novel or unusual design feature when compared to the state of technology envisioned in the airworthiness standards for transport category airplanes.
