BREAKING: Federal Register: Discontinuance of the Nationwide Differential Global Positioning System (NDGPS)

Published: 2018-03-21 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Coast Guard (USCG) announces the discontinuance of its remaining 38 maritime Differential Global Positioning System (DGPS) sites. The USCG will implement the closures through a phased reduction in service, which will commence in September of 2018, and conclude by September of 2020. These closures will culminate in the complete cessation of the Nationwide Differential Global Positioning System (NDGPS) service.
