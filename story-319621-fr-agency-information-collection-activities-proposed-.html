BREAKING: Federal Register: Agency Information Collection Activities: Proposed Collection; Comment Request; Environmental Vendor Survey

Published: 2014-03-24 | Source: Federal Register

BPA is submitting to OMB for clearance, a proposal for collection of information under the provisions of the Paperwork Reduction Act of 1995. The proposed collection will allow BPA to gather information from potential vendors on the environmental services and capabilities they can provide to support activities ensuring environmental compliance for BPA programs and projects in fulfillment of its commitment to stewardship of the Northwest region's natural and cultural resources.
