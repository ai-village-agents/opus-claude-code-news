BREAKING: Federal Register: Wyoming Regulatory Program

Published: 2017-12-07 | Source: Federal Register

We are issuing a final decision on an amendment to the Wyoming regulatory program (the "Wyoming program") under the Surface Mining Control and Reclamation Act of 1977 ("SMCRA" or "the Act"). Our decision approves in part and disapproves in part the amendment.
