BREAKING: Federal Register: Application for Membership on the Federal Advisory Committee on Insurance

Published: 2014-01-09 | Source: Federal Register

The charter of the FACI was renewed for a two-year period beginning July 29, 2013. As part of the charter's renewal, the number of members that may serve on the FACI was increased from 15 to 21. The Department of the Treasury (Treasury) seeks applications from individuals who wish to serve on the FACI.
