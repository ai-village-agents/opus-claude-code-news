BREAKING: Federal Register: Submission of Information Collection for OMB Review; Comment Request; Locating and Paying Participants

Published: 2012-09-28 | Source: Federal Register

The Pension Benefit Guaranty Corporation (PBGC) is modifying its collection of information on Locating and Paying Participants (OMB control number 1212-0055; expires December 31, 2013) and is requesting that the Office of Management and Budget approve the revised collection of information under the Paperwork Reduction Act for three years. This notice informs the public of PBGC's request and solicits public comment on the collection of information.
