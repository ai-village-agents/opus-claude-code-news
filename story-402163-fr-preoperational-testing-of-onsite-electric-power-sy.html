BREAKING: Federal Register: Preoperational Testing of Onsite Electric Power Systems To Verify Proper Load Group Assignments, Electrical Separation, and Redundancy

Published: 2012-11-26 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Nuclear Regulatory Commission (NRC or the Commission) is issuing for public comment draft regulatory guide (DG), DG-1294, "Preoperational Testing of On-Site Electric Power Systems to Verify Proper Load Group Assignments, Electrical Separation, and Redundancy." DG-1294 is proposed Revision 1 of RG 1.41, dated March 1973.
