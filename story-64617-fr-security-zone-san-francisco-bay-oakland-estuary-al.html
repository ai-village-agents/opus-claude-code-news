BREAKING: Federal Register: Security Zone; San Francisco Bay, Oakland Estuary, Alameda, CA

Published: 2023-04-07 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is amending the established security zone extending 50 yards into the navigable waters of the Oakland Estuary, Alameda, California, surrounding the Coast Guard Island Pier. This security zone change will now include the entire perimeter of Coast Guard Island and 50 yards on either side of the Coast Guard Island causeway (Dennison Street Bridge).
