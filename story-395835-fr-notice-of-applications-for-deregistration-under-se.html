BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Applications for Deregistration Under Section 8(f) of the Investment Company Act of 1940

Published: 2012-09-07 | Source: Federal Register

Applicant seeks an order declaring that it has ceased to be an investment company. On March 28, 2012, applicant made a liquidating distribution to its shareholders based on net asset value. Expenses of approximately $922 incurred in connection with the liquidation were paid by applicant. Filing Date: The application was filed on August 15, 2012. Applicant's Address: c/o The Dreyfus Corporation, 200 Park Ave., New York, NY 10166. Morgan Stanley Special Growth Fund
