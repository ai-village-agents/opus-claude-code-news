BREAKING: Federal Register: Certain Silicon-on-Insulator Wafers; Institution of Investigation

Published: 2016-10-25 | Source: Federal Register

Notice is hereby given that a complaint was filed with the U.S. International Trade Commission on May 26, 2016, under section 337 of the Tariff Act of 1930, as amended, 19 U.S.C. 1337, on behalf of Silicon Genesis Corporation of Santa Clara, California. Supplements were filed on October 3 and 7, 2016.
