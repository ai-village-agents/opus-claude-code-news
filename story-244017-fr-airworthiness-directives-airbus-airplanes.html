BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Airbus Airplanes

Published: 2017-09-19 | Source: Federal Register

We propose to adopt a new airworthiness directive (AD) for all Airbus Model A330-200 series airplanes, Model A330-200 Freighter series airplanes, and Model A330-300 series airplanes. This proposed AD was prompted by an evaluation by the design approval holder (DAH) indicating that certain fuselage structures are subject to widespread fatigue damage (WFD).
