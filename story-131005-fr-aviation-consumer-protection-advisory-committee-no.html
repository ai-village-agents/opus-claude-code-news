BREAKING: Federal Register: Aviation Consumer Protection Advisory Committee: Notice of Solicitation of Nominations for Appointment for the Anti-Discrimination Subcommittee

Published: 2021-08-19 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Department of Transportation (Department) has established a subcommittee of the Aviation Consumer Protection Advisory Committee (ACPAC) to focus on preventing discrimination and ensuring air travelers are treated equally and without bias. The Department invites interested persons to submit applications or nominations for membership to this subcommittee, which has been named the Anti- Discrimination Subcommittee.
