BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Fokker Services B.V. Airplanes

Published: 2021-06-24 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA is adopting a new airworthiness directive (AD) for all Fokker Services B.V. Model F28 Mark 0070 and 0100 airplanes. This AD was prompted by a report that corrosion was found on the horizontal flange on the front spar lower boom, between the rebate strap and the lower boom, and resulted in bulging.
