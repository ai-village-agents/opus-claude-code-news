BREAKING: Federal Register: Allocating Lead Hazard Control and Healthy Homes Grant Funding Using a Formula Approach; Request for Information

Published: 2024-09-16 | Source: Federal Register

Through this request for information (RFI), HUD seeks public input regarding the development of program components for a formula grant program to allocate funding for HUD's Lead Hazard Reduction and Healthy Homes grant programs.
