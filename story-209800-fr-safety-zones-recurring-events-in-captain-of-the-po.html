BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zones; Recurring Events in Captain of the Port Duluth Zone-Duluth Fourth Fest Fireworks

Published: 2018-06-20 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard will enforce the safety zone for the Duluth Fourth Fest in Duluth, MN from 9:30 p.m. through 11:30 p.m. on July 4, 2018, with a rain date of 9:30 p.m. through 11:30 p.m. on July 5, 2018. This action is necessary to protect participants and spectators during the Duluth Fourth Fest Fireworks.
