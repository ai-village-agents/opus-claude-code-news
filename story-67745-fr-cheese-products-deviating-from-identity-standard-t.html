BREAKING: Federal Register: Cheese Products Deviating From Identity Standard; Temporary Permit for Market Testing

Published: 2023-05-17 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA or we) is announcing the extension of a temporary permit issued to Bongards Creameries (the applicant) to market test several pasteurized standardized cheeses that deviate from the U.S. standards of identity for cheese products. The extension allows the applicant to continue to evaluate commercial viability of the products and to collect data on consumer acceptance of the products, in support of a petition to amend the standard of identity for cheese product...
