BREAKING: Federal Register: Hazardous Waste Management System; Modification of the Hazardous Waste Manifest System; Electronic Manifests

Published: 2014-02-07 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA or the Agency) is establishing new requirements that will authorize the use of electronic manifests (or e-Manifests) as a means to track off-site shipments of hazardous waste from a generator's site to the site of the receipt and disposition of the hazardous waste.
