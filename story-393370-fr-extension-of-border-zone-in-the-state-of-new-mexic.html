BREAKING: Federal Register: Extension of Border Zone in the State of New Mexico

Published: 2012-08-09 | Source: Federal Register

Under current Department of Homeland Security (DHS) regulations, certain nonimmigrant Mexican nationals presenting a Border Crossing Card, or other proper immigration documentation, are not required to obtain a CBP Form I-94 (Form I-94), Arrival/Departure Record, if they remain within 25 miles of the border (75 miles in Arizona).
