BREAKING: Federal Register: Privacy Act of 1974; System of Records

Published: 2020-12-28 | Source: Federal Register

In accordance with the Privacy Act of 1974, the DoD proposes to establish a new system of records covering all DoD components titled, "Defense Training Records" DoD 0005. This system of records describes the DoD's collection, use, and maintenance of records about training delivered to DoD Service Members, civilian personnel, and other DoD-affiliated individuals.
