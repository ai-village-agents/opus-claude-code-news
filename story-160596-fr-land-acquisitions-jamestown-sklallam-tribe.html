BREAKING: Federal Register: Land Acquisitions; Jamestown S'Klallam Tribe

Published: 2020-09-08 | Source: Federal Register

The Assistant Secretary--Indian Affairs has made a final determination to acquire 44.10 acres, more or less, into trust for the Jamestown S'Klallam Tribe.
