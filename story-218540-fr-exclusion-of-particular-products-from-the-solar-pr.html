BREAKING: Federal Register: Exclusion of Particular Products From the Solar Products Safeguard Measure; Correction

Published: 2018-10-12 | Source: Federal Register

The Office of the United States Trade Representative published a document in the Federal Register of September 19, 2018, concerning a determination to exclude certain products from the safeguard measure on solar products. This notice corrects an error in that document--the time the modifications became applicable should have referenced Eastern Daylight Time (EDT).
