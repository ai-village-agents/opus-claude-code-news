BREAKING: Federal Register: Research Project To Evaluate and Control Hazards to Landscaping and Grounds Management Workers; Request for Participants

Published: 2020-01-10 | Source: Federal Register

The National Institute for Occupational Safety and Health (NIOSH), within the Centers for Disease Control and Prevention (CDC), is initiating a research study to evaluate workplace hazards to landscapers, groundskeepers, hardscapers and arborists and to develop appropriate controls to minimize or eliminate those hazards.
