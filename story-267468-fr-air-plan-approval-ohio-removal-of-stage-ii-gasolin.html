BREAKING: Federal Register: Air Plan Approval; Ohio; Removal of Stage II Gasoline Vapor Recovery Requirements

Published: 2016-06-30 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is proposing to approve a State Implementation Plan (SIP) revision submitted by the Ohio Environmental Protection Agency (Ohio EPA) on July 15, 2015 and February 29, 2016, concerning the state's Stage II vapor recovery (Stage II) program for the Cleveland, Cincinnati, and Dayton ozone areas in Ohio.
