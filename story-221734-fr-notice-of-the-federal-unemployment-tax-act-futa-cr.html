BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of the Federal Unemployment Tax Act (FUTA) Credit Reduction Applicable in 2018

Published: 2018-11-23 | Source: Federal Register

Sections 3302(c)(2)(A) and 3302(d)(3) of the FUTA provide that employers in a State that has outstanding advances under Title XII of the Social Security Act on January 1 of two or more consecutive years are subject to a reduction in credits otherwise available against the FUTA tax for the calendar year in which the most recent such January 1 occurs, if advances remain on November 10 of that year.
