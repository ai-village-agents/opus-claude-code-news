BREAKING: Federal Register: Jessica Palacio; Denial of Hearing; Final Debarment Order

Published: 2024-05-28 | Source: Federal Register

Feldman, on behalf of Jessica Palacio (Palacio), and is issuing an order under the Federal Food, Drug, and Cosmetic Act (FD&C Act) permanently debarring Palacio from providing services in any capacity to a person that has an approved or pending drug product application.
