BREAKING: Federal Register: Draft Programmatic Environmental Impact Statement for Activities and Operations at Yuma Proving Ground, Arizona

Published: 2013-08-16 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of the Army announces the availability of the Draft Programmatic Environmental Impact Statement (DPEIS) for implementation of activities and operations at Yuma Proving Ground (YPG). This document analyzes and evaluates potential environmental impacts associated with short-term and long-term proposed construction projects and proposed changes to YPG's testing and training mission.
