BREAKING: Federal Register: Withdrawal of Bifacial Solar Panels Exclusion to the Solar Products Safeguard Measure

Published: 2019-10-09 | Source: Federal Register

On January 23, 2018, the President imposed a safeguard measure on imports of certain solar products pursuant to a section 201 investigation. On February 14, 2018, the U.S. Trade Representative established procedures for interested persons to request product- specific exclusions from application of the safeguard measure.
