BREAKING: Federal Register: Revision of the America's Marine Highway Program Regulations

Published: 2017-12-01 | Source: Federal Register

The Maritime Administration (MARAD) is amending its America's Marine Highway Program (AMHP) regulations to implement provisions of the Coast Guard and Maritime Transportation Act of 2012 (CGMTA), the National Defense Authorization Act of 2016 (NDAA), and to clarify AMHP processes.
