BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; The Boeing Company Airplanes

Published: 2013-04-15 | Source: Federal Register

We are superseding an existing airworthiness directive (AD) for certain The Boeing Company Model 777-200, -200LR, -300, and -300ER series airplanes. That AD currently requires inspecting for scribe lines in the skin along lap joints, butt joints, certain external doublers, and the large cargo door hinges, and doing related investigative and corrective actions if necessary.
