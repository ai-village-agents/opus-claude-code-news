BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Inventory Completion: Department of Anthropology, University of Massachusetts, Amherst, MA

Published: 2012-12-18 | Source: Federal Register

The University of Massachusetts Amherst, Department of Anthropology has completed an inventory of human remains, in consultation with the appropriate Indian tribes, and has determined that there is a cultural affiliation between the human remains and present-day Indian tribes. Representatives of any Indian tribe that believes itself to be culturally affiliated with the human remains may contact the University of Massachusetts Amherst, Department of Anthropology.
