BREAKING: Federal Register: 2025 Resource Pool-Sierra Nevada Customer Service Region

Published: 2019-02-12 | Source: Federal Register

Western Area Power Administration announces proposed power allocations from the 2025 Resource Pool for the Central Valley and Washoe projects. Under the final 2025 Power Marketing Plan, 2 percent of the existing marketable resource, otherwise known as Base Resource, of the Central Valley and Washoe Projects will be allocated to new and existing eligible preference customers beginning January 1, 2025 and ending December 31, 2054.
