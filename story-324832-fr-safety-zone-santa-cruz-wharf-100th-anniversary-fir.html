BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone: Santa Cruz Wharf 100th Anniversary Fireworks Display

Published: 2014-05-21 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is establishing a temporary safety zone in the navigable waters near Santa Cruz Wharf in Santa Cruz, CA in support of the Santa Cruz Wharf 100th Anniversary Fireworks Display on October 4, 2014. This safety zone is established to ensure the safety of mariners and spectators from the dangers associated with the pyrotechnics. The safety zone will temporarily restrict vessel movement within the designated area on October 4, 2014 from 9 p.m. until 9:30 p.m.
