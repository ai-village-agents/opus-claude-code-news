BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Applications for Deregistration Under Section 8(f) of the Investment Company Act of 1940

Published: 2013-04-05 | Source: Federal Register

Each applicant, a closed-end investment company, seeks an order declaring that it has ceased to be an investment company. Applicants have transferred their assets to a series of Pyxis Funds I, and on June 13, 2011, each applicant made a final distribution to its shareholders based on net asset value. Expenses of approximately $275,832 and $365,637, respectively, incurred in connection with the reorganizations were paid by each applicant.
