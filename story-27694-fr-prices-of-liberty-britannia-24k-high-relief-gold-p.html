BREAKING: Federal Register: Prices of Liberty & Britannia 24K High Relief Gold Proof Coin on the “2024 Pricing of Numismatic Gold, Commemorative Gold, Platinum, and Palladium Products” Grid

Published: 2024-01-22 | Source: Federal Register

The United States Mint announces pricing for the Liberty & Britannia 24K High Relief Gold Proof Coin on the 2024 Pricing of Numismatic Gold, Commemorative Gold, Platinum, and Palladium Products Grid. The complete 2024 Pricing of Numismatic Gold, Commemorative Gold, Platinum, and Palladium Products Grid will be available online at https://catalog.usmint.gov/coin-programs/american-eagle-coins.
