BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Hawker Beechcraft Corporation Airplanes

Published: 2012-10-23 | Source: Federal Register

We are adopting a new airworthiness directive (AD) for certain Hawker Beechcraft Corporation Model C90GTi (King Air) airplanes. This AD was prompted by reports of incorrect gauge wires used in certain wiring bundles for the cockpit electrical power for backlighting and instrument panel components.
