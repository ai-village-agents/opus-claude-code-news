BREAKING: Federal Register: Regulated Navigation Area; Housatonic River, Bridge Replacement Operations; Stratford, CT

Published: 2013-01-08 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is establishing a regulated navigation area (RNA) on the navigable waters of the Housatonic River surrounding the Interstate 95 (I-95) Bridge, between Stratford and Milford, CT. This RNA allows the Coast Guard to enforce speed and wake restrictions and prohibit all vessel traffic through the RNA during bridge replacement operations, both planned and unforeseen, that could pose an imminent hazard to persons and vessels operating in the area.
