BREAKING: Federal Register: Fluctuating Workweek Method of Computing Overtime

Published: 2019-11-05 | Source: Federal Register

This proposed rulemaking would revise the Department of Labor's (Department) regulation for computing overtime compensation for salaried nonexempt employees who work hours that vary each week (fluctuating workweek) under the Fair Labor Standards Act (FLSA or the Act).
