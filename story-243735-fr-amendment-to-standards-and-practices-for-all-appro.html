BREAKING: Federal Register: Amendment to Standards and Practices for All Appropriate Inquiries Under CERCLA

Published: 2017-09-15 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Environmental Protection Agency (EPA or the agency) is taking final action to amend the Standards and Practices for All Appropriate Inquiries to update an existing reference to a standard practice revised by ASTM International, a widely recognized standards development organization.
