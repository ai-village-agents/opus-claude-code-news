BREAKING: Federal Register: Availability of the Updated Problem Formulation and Protocol for the Inorganic Arsenic IRIS Assessment

Published: 2019-05-28 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is announcing a 30- day public comment period associated with the release of the Updated Problem Formulation and Protocol for the Inorganic Arsenic IRIS Assessment. This document summarizes the Agency needs for the assessment and presents the refined focus based on problem formulation activities conducted since the last assessment plan released to the National Academy of Sciences, Engineering, and Medicine (NAS) in 2015.
