BREAKING: Federal Register: Specific Listing for 1-boc-4-AP, a Currently Controlled List I Chemical

Published: 2022-11-09 | Source: Federal Register

The Drug Enforcement Administration (DEA) is establishing a specific listing and DEA Chemical Control Number for tert-butyl 4- (phenylamino)piperidine-1-carboxylate (also known as 1-boc-4-AP; and CAS Number: 125541-22-2) and its salts as a list I chemical under the Controlled Substances Act.
