BREAKING: Federal Register: Fisheries of the Northeastern United States; Northeast Skate Complex; Rescission of Control Dates

Published: 2021-08-31 | Source: Federal Register

NMFS announces the rescission of the July 30, 2009, and March 31, 2014, skate wing and bait fishery control dates. The New England Fishery Management Council has decided to discontinue development of limited access alternatives for both the skate wing and skate bait fisheries as part of Amendment 5 to the Northeast Skate Complex Fishery Management Plan.
