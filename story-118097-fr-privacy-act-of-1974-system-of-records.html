BREAKING: Federal Register: Privacy Act of 1974; System of Records

Published: 2021-03-04 | Source: Federal Register

In accordance with the Privacy Act of 1974, as amended, the Department of Health and Human Services (HHS) is modifying a department-wide system of records titled HHS Correspondence, Customer Service, and Contact List Records, system no. 09-90-1901, to make certain updates and to more clearly include records about individuals who provide comments and supporting documents to HHS in response to HHS rulemakings and other docketed proceedings.
