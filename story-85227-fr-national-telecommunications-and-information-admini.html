BREAKING: Federal Register: National Telecommunications and Information Administration 2024 NTIA Spectrum Policy Symposium

Published: 2023-12-27 | Source: Federal Register

The National Telecommunications and Information Administration (NTIA), U.S. Department of Commerce, will host a public symposium on February 1, 2024, focusing on implementation of the National Spectrum Strategy, which the Executive Office of the President released on November 13, 2023.
