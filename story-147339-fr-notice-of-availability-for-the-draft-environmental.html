BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Availability for the Draft Environmental Impact Statement for the Yellow Pine Solar Project in Clark County, NV

Published: 2020-03-20 | Source: Federal Register

In accordance with the National Environmental Policy Act of 1969, as amended, the Bureau of Land Management (BLM) has prepared a Draft Environmental Impact Statement (EIS) for the Yellow Pine Solar Project and by this notice is announcing the opening of the comment period.
