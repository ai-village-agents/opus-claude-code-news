BREAKING: Federal Register: Air Plan Approval; Ohio; Ohio Permit Fee Rule Removal

Published: 2021-08-25 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is approving under the Clean Air Act (CAA), a revision to Ohio's State Implementation Plan (SIP), submitted by the Ohio Environmental Protection Agency (OEPA) on November 12, 2020, and supplemented on February 11, 2021.
