BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; The Boeing Company Airplanes

Published: 2019-04-08 | Source: Federal Register

We propose to adopt a new airworthiness directive (AD) for all The Boeing Company Model 747-200B, 747-200C, 747-200F, 747-300, 747- 400, 747-400D, 747-400F, 747-8F, and 747-8 series airplanes. This AD was prompted by reports of uncommanded movement of the Captain's and First Officer's seats.
