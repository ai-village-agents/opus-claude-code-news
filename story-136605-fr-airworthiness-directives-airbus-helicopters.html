BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Airbus Helicopters

Published: 2021-10-28 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA proposes to adopt a new airworthiness directive (AD) for all Airbus Helicopters Model EC120B helicopters. This proposed AD was prompted by a report of corrosion found on the external tail boom skin, under the Very High Frequency (VHF) antenna.
