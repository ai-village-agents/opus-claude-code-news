BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone; Boston Harbor's Rock Removal Project, Boston Inner Harbor, Boston, MA

Published: 2012-08-23 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is establishing a temporary safety zone within Sector Boston's Captain of the Port (COTP) Zone for the drilling, blasting, and dredging operation on the navigable waters of Boston Inner Harbor, in the main ship channel near Castle Island.
