BREAKING: Federal Register: Loans in Areas Having Special Flood Hazards; Interagency Questions and Answers Regarding Private Flood Insurance

Published: 2021-03-18 | Source: Federal Register

The OCC, Board, FDIC, FCA, and NCUA (collectively, the Agencies) propose to supplement the Interagency Questions and Answers Regarding Flood Insurance with new questions and answers regarding the acceptance of flood insurance policies issued by private insurers pursuant to the Agencies' private flood insurance final rule issued in February 2019.
