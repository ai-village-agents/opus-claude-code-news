BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Inventory Completion: Maxey Museum, Whitman College, Walla Walla, WA

Published: 2012-03-07 | Source: Federal Register

Maxey Museum has completed an inventory of human remains and associated funerary objects, in consultation with the appropriate Indian tribes, and has determined that there is a cultural affiliation between the human remains and associated funerary objects and present- day Indian tribes. Representatives of any Indian tribe that believes itself to be culturally affiliated with the human remains and associated funerary objects may contact Maxey Museum.
