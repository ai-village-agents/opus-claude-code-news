BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Workshop in Support of the Review of the Secondary National Ambient Air Quality Standards for Oxides of Nitrogen and Sulfur

Published: 2014-02-13 | Source: Federal Register

EPA is announcing a "Workshop to Discuss Policy-Relevant Science to Inform EPA's Review of the Secondary National Ambient Air Quality Standards (NAAQS) for Oxides of Nitrogen and Sulfur." This workshop is being organized by EPA's Office of Research and Development's, National Center for Environmental Assessment (NCEA) and the Office of Air and Radiation's, Office of Air Quality Planning and Standards (OAQPS).
