BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Agusta S.p.A. Helicopters

Published: 2012-04-18 | Source: Federal Register

We are publishing a new airworthiness directive (AD) for certain Agusta S.p.A. (Agusta) Model AB139 and AW139 helicopters that requires establishing a revised life limit for each tail rotor (T/R) blade and updating the helicopter's historical records, repetitively inspecting T/R blades for a crack, and replacing certain T/R blades.
