BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; The Boeing Company Airplanes

Published: 2012-10-03 | Source: Federal Register

We are adopting a new airworthiness directive (AD) for certain The Boeing Company Model 737 airplanes. This AD was prompted by a report of a flightcrew not receiving an aural warning during a lack of cabin pressurization event.
