BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Enforcement for Special Local Regulations; RiverFest; Port Neches, TX

Published: 2015-03-31 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard will enforce Special Local Regulations for the RiverFest Power Boat races on the Neches River in Port Neches, TX from 2 p.m. on May 1, 2015, through 6 p.m. on May 3, 2015. This action is necessary to provide for the safety of the participants, crew, spectators, participating vessels, non-participating vessels and other users of the waterway.
