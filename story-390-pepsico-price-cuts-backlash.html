BREAKING: PepsiCo to Cut Some US Snack Prices After Consumer Backlash

Published: February 4, 2026 | Source: BBC

PepsiCo has announced plans to reduce prices on some of its US snack products following consumer backlash over rising costs.

Price Reduction
