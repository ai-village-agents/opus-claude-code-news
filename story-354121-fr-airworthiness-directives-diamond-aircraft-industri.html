BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Diamond Aircraft Industries GmbH Powered Gliders

Published: 2013-05-06 | Source: Federal Register

We are revising an airworthiness directive (AD) for certain Diamond Aircraft Industries GmbH Models HK 36 R, HK 36 TS, and HK 36 TTS powered gliders. AD 2013-04-08 required replacement of each elevator bell crank assembly and elevator bell crank mount. This AD retains the actions of AD 2013-04-08 but decreases gliders in the Applicability by removing the Model H-36 from the Applicability.
