BREAKING: Federal Register: Submission for Review: Verification of Who Is Getting Payments, RI 38-107 and RI 38-147

Published: 2021-03-18 | Source: Federal Register

Retirement Services, Office of Personnel Management (OPM) offers the general public and other federal agencies the opportunity to comment on an expiring information collection (ICR) with minor edits, Verification of Who is Getting Payments, RI 38-107 and RI 38-147. This ICR has been revised in the following manner: (1) The display of the OMB control number and (2) an updated edition date.
