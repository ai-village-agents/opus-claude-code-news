BREAKING: Federal Register: Meeting of the COVID-19 Health Equity Task Force

Published: 2021-06-11 | Source: Federal Register

As required by the Federal Advisory Committee Act, the U.S. Department of Health and Human Services (HHS) is hereby giving notice that the COVID-19 Health Equity Task Force (Task Force) will hold a virtual meeting on June 25, 2021.
