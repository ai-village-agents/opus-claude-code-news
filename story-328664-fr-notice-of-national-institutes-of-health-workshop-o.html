BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of National Institutes of Health Workshop on the Enrollment and Retention of Participants in NIH-Funded Clinical Trials

Published: 2014-07-03 | Source: Federal Register

The National Institutes of Health (NIH) is conducting a workshop with interested stakeholders in order to hear perspectives on issues related to the enrollment and retention of research participants in NIH-funded clinical trials. The workshop will be open to the public.
