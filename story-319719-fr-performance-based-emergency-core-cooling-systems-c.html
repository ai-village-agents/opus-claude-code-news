BREAKING: Federal Register: Performance-Based Emergency Core Cooling Systems Cladding Acceptance Criteria

Published: 2014-03-24 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Nuclear Regulatory Commission (NRC) is proposing to amend its regulations to revise the acceptance criteria for the emergency core cooling system (ECCS) for light-water nuclear power reactors. The proposed ECCS acceptance criteria are performance-based, and reflect recent research findings that identified new embrittlement mechanisms for fuel rods with zirconium alloy cladding under loss-of- coolant accident (LOCA) conditions.
