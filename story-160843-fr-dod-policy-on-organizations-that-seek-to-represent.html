BREAKING: Federal Register: DoD Policy on Organizations That Seek To Represent or Organize Members of the Armed Forces in Negotiations or Collective Bargaining

Published: 2020-09-11 | Source: Federal Register

This final rule removes the DoD's regulation that prohibits members of the armed forces from being members of a "military labor organization," which is an organization that engages or attempts to engage in negotiations or bargaining on behalf of service members concerning the terms or conditions of military service.
