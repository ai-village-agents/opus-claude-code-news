BREAKING: Federal Register: Unsafe and Unsound Banking Practices: Brokered Deposits and Interest Rate Restrictions

Published: 2021-01-22 | Source: Federal Register

The FDIC is finalizing revisions to its regulations relating to the brokered deposits and interest rate restrictions that apply to less than well capitalized insured depository institutions. For brokered deposits, the final rule establishes a new framework for analyzing certain provisions of the "deposit broker" definition, including "facilitating" and "primary purpose."
