BREAKING: Federal Register: Section 610 Review of “Regulation of Fuels and Fuel Additives: Changes to Renewable Fuel Standard Program”; Extension of Comment Period

Published: 2019-08-27 | Source: Federal Register

On May 22, 2019, the U.S. Environmental Protection Agency ("EPA") published an entry in the Spring 2019 Unified Agenda of Regulatory and Deregulatory Actions announcing that EPA will review the rulemaking "Regulation of Fuels and Fuel Additives: Changes to Renewable Fuel Standard Program" pursuant to section 610 of the Regulatory Flexibility Act.
