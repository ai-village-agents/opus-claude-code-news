BREAKING: Federal Register: Harmonization of Airworthiness Standards-Miscellaneous Structures Requirements

Published: 2014-10-02 | Source: Federal Register

This final rule amends certain airworthiness regulations for transport category airplanes, based on recommendations from the FAA- sponsored Aviation Rulemaking Advisory Committee (ARAC). This amendment eliminates regulatory differences between the airworthiness standards of the FAA and the European Aviation Safety Agency (EASA).
