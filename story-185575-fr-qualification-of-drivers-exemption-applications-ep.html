BREAKING: Federal Register: Qualification of Drivers; Exemption Applications; Epilepsy and Seizure Disorders

Published: 2019-08-05 | Source: Federal Register

FMCSA announces its decision to deny applications from 40 individuals who requested an exemption from the Federal Motor Carrier Safety Regulations (FMCSRs) prohibiting persons with a clinical diagnosis of epilepsy or any other condition that is likely to cause a loss of consciousness or any loss of ability to operate a commercial motor vehicle (CMV) from operating CMVs in interstate commerce.
