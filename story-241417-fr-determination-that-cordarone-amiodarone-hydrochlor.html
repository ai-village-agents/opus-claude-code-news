BREAKING: Federal Register: Determination That CORDARONE (Amiodarone Hydrochloride) Tablets, 200 milligrams, Were Not Withdrawn From Sale for Reasons of Safety or Effectiveness

Published: 2017-08-16 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA or Agency) has determined that CORDARONE (amiodarone hydrochloride) tablets, 200 milligrams (mg), were not withdrawn from sale for reasons of safety or effectiveness.
