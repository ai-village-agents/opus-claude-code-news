BREAKING: Federal Register: Announcement of Requirements and Registration for a Prize Competition Titled: Arsenic Sensor Challenge-Stage 1

Published: 2016-12-13 | Source: Federal Register

This Challenge seeks to identify new or improved sensors, devices, or test kits to test for arsenic in water within natural and engineered systems. Solutions must improve on the current arsenic measurement methods. Areas of needed improvement include: performance, ease of use, reduction in hazardous waste production, data interpretation, and cost.
