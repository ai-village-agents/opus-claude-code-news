BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone; Thames River, Groton, CT

Published: 2024-05-23 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is establishing a temporary safety zone for certain waters of the Thames River, Groton, CT. This action is necessary to provide for the safety of life on the navigable waters in the vicinity of Fort Trumbull. New London, CT to the south of the Pfizer Pier in Groton, CT during installation of submerged cable and the removal and replacement of a floating drydock.
