BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Dassault Aviation Airplanes

Published: 2023-11-20 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA is superseding Airworthiness Directive (AD) 2020-23- 10, which applied to certain Dassault Aviation Model FAN JET FALCON and FAN JET FALCON SERIES C, D, E, F, and G airplanes. AD 2020-23-10 required revising the existing maintenance or inspection program, as applicable, to incorporate new or more restrictive airworthiness limitations.
