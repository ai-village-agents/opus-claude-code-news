BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone; Bullhead City Regatta, Bullhead City, AZ

Published: 2013-06-07 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is proposing a temporary safety zone on the navigable waters of the Colorado River in Bullhead City, Arizona for the Bullhead City Regatta on August 10, 2013. This temporary safety zone is necessary to provide for the safety of the participants, crew, spectators, participating vessels, and other vessels and users of the waterway.
