BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Release of the Department of State's FY 2021 Service Contract Inventory

Published: 2023-10-04 | Source: Federal Register

Acting in compliance with section 743 of Division C of the Consolidated Appropriations Act of 2010, the Department of State is publishing this notice to advise the public of the availability of the FY 2021 Service Contract Inventory. The FY 2021 Service Contract Inventory includes the FY 2021 Planned Analysis, and the FY 2020 Meaningful Analysis.
