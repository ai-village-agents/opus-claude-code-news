BREAKING: Federal Register: Payment or Reimbursement for Certain Medical Expenses for Camp Lejeune Family Members

Published: 2017-05-05 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Veterans Affairs (VA) adopts as final an interim final rule addressing payment or reimbursement of certain medical expenses for family members of Camp Lejeune veterans.
