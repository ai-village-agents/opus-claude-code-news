BREAKING: Federal Register: Central Arizona Project-Rate Order No. WAPA-158

Published: 2012-08-07 | Source: Federal Register

The Western Area Power Administration (Western), a power marketing administration with the Department of Energy (DOE), is proposing to extend the existing formula rates for Central Arizona Project (CAP) transmission services on the CAP 115-kilovolt (kV) and 230-kV transmission facilities through December 31, 2015.
