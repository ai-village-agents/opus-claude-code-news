BREAKING: Federal Register: NTIA Listening Session on Kids' Excessive Screen Time

Published: 2025-11-26 | Source: Federal Register

The National Telecommunications and Information Administration (NTIA) will convene a virtual listening session on the drivers and consequences of excessive screen use in schools, including the growing reliance on individual devices and educational technology services and applications. The discussion will also examine how federal law, broadband funding, and other federal programs may influence school- based device and digital platform use.
