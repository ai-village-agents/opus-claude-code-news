BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Airbus SAS Airplanes

Published: 2024-05-06 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA proposes to adopt a new airworthiness directive (AD) for certain Airbus SAS Model A330-243, -302, -343, and -941 airplanes. This proposed AD was prompted by a determination that a certain aft bulkhead cover panel may have been made with a non-conforming material.
