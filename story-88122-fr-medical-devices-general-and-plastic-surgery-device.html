BREAKING: Federal Register: Medical Devices; General and Plastic Surgery Devices; Classification of the Carbon Dioxide Gas Controlled Tissue Expander

Published: 2022-02-04 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA or we) is classifying the carbon dioxide gas controlled tissue expander into class II (special controls). The special controls that apply to the device type are identified in this order and will be part of the codified language for the carbon dioxide gas controlled tissue expander's classification.
