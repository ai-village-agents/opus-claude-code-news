BREAKING: Federal Register: Distributional Effects of Trade and Trade Policy on U.S. Workers

Published: 2022-01-19 | Source: Federal Register

The Commission has established a schedule and procedure, set forth below, for conducting roundtables, an academic symposium, and a hearing in connection with this investigation. The Commission will hold seven roundtable discussions between March 1 and April 1, 2022, an academic symposium on April 5-6, 2022, and a hearing on April 19, 2022.
