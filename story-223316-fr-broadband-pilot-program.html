BREAKING: Federal Register: Broadband Pilot Program

Published: 2018-12-14 | Source: Federal Register

The Rural Utilities Service (RUS) announces its general policy and application procedures for funding under the broadband pilot program established pursuant to the Consolidated Appropriations Act, 2018 which provides loans, grants, and loan/grant combinations to facilitate broadband deployment in rural areas.
