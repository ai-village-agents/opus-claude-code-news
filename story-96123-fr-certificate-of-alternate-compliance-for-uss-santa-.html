BREAKING: Federal Register: Certificate of Alternate Compliance for USS SANTA BARBARA (LCS 32)

Published: 2022-05-17 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Navy hereby announces that a Certificate of Alternate Compliance has been issued for USS SANTA BARBARA (LCS 32).
