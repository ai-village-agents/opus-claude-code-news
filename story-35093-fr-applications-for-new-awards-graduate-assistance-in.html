BREAKING: Federal Register: Applications for New Awards; Graduate Assistance in Areas of National Need (GAANN)

Published: 2024-04-24 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Education (Department) is issuing a notice inviting applications for new awards for fiscal year (FY) 2024 for the Graduate Assistance in Areas of National Need (GAANN) Program, Assistance Listing Number 84.200A. This notice relates to the approved information collection under OMB control number 1840-0604.
