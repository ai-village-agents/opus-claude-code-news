BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone; Los Angeles County Annual New Years Eve Fireworks Event

Published: 2024-12-09 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard will enforce safety zones for the Los Angeles County Holiday Fireworks on December 14, 2024, and the Los Angeles County New Years Eve Fireworks on December 31, 2024 through January 1, 2025. These safety zones are to provide for the safety of life and property on the navigable waterways during these events.
