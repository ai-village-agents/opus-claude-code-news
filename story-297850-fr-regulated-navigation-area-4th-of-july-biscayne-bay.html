BREAKING: Federal Register: Regulated Navigation Area; 4th of July, Biscayne Bay, Miami, FL

Published: 2015-07-01 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is establishing a regulated navigation area on Biscayne Bay in Miami, Florida, for multiple 4th of July fireworks displays throughout the Miami area. This regulation is necessary to protect the public from hazards associated with boating traffic expected during 4th of July firework displays throughout the Miami area.
