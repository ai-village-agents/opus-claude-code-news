BREAKING: Federal Register: Control of Non-Volatile Particulate Matter From Aircraft Engines: Emission Standards and Test Procedures; Correction

Published: 2024-05-07 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA is correcting a final rule published on April 24, 2024. In that document the FAA adopts standards for measuring non- volatile particulate matter (nvPM) exhaust emissions from aircraft engines.
