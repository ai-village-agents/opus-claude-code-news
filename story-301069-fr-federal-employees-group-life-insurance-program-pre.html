BREAKING: Federal Register: Federal Employees' Group Life Insurance Program; Premium Changes and Open Season

Published: 2015-08-07 | Source: Federal Register

The Office of Personnel Management (OPM) is announcing an upcoming FEGLI Open Season and changes in premium rates for certain Federal Employees' Group Life Insurance (FEGLI) categories in accordance with sections 870.401(a)(2) and 870.402(a)(3) of title 5 of the Code of Federal Regulations. These include changes to premium rates for Option A (most age bands), Option B (most age bands), Option C (most age bands), and Post-Retirement Basic Insurance.
