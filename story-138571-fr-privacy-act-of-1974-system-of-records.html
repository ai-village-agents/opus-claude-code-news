BREAKING: Federal Register: Privacy Act of 1974; System of Records

Published: 2021-11-23 | Source: Federal Register

Pursuant to the Privacy Act of 1974, notice is hereby given that the Department of Veterans Affairs (VA) proposes to establish a new system of records entitled VA Emergency Alerting and Accountability System (VA EAAS). The purpose is to document the enterprise-wide system used for alerting and accountability purposes. The system is a method to send rapid, reliable, and widespread notifications and collect the safety status of all VA employees, contractors, and affiliates in times of an emergency...
