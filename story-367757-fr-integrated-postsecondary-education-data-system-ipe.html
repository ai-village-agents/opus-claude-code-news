BREAKING: Federal Register: Integrated Postsecondary Education Data System (IPEDS) 2013-2016; Extension of Public Comment Period; Correction

Published: 2013-10-23 | Source: Federal Register

On October 2, 2013, the U.S. Department of Education published a 30-day comment period notice in the Federal Register (Page 60864, Column 2) seeking public comment for an information collection entitled, "Integrated Postsecondary Education Data System (IPEDS) 2013-2016". The comment period for this information collection request has been extended to November 14, 2013.
