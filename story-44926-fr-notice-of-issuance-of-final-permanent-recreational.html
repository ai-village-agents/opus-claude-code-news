BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Issuance of Final Permanent Recreational Shooting Order in the Hyalite Creek Drainage of the Bozeman Ranger District of the Custer Gallatin National Forest

Published: 2024-08-21 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Department of Agriculture, Forest Service (Agency), is issuing a final permanent order prohibiting recreational shooting in the Hyalite Creek Drainage, which covers 34,018 acres in Gallatin County, Montana, on the Bozeman Ranger District of the Custer Gallatin National Forest.
