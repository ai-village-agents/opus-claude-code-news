BREAKING: Federal Register: Affirmatively Furthering Fair Housing Revisions

Published: 2025-03-03 | Source: Federal Register

This interim final rule revises HUD's regulation governing the Fair Housing Act's mandate that the Secretary administer HUD's program and activities in a manner that affirmatively furthers fair housing. This interim final rule returns to the original understanding of what the statutory AFFH certification was prior to 1994--a general commitment that grantees will take active steps to promote fair housing.
