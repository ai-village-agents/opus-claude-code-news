BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone: Petaluma River Closure for Highway Widening, Petaluma River, Petaluma, CA

Published: 2014-06-12 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is establishing a temporary safety zone in the navigable waters of the Petaluma River near the Highway 101 Bridge in support of the Petaluma River closure for the highway widening project taking place from June 9, 2014 through June 21, 2014. This safety zone is established to ensure the safety of mariners transiting the area from the dangers associated with overhead bridge construction.
