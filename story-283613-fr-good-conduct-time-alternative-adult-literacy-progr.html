BREAKING: Federal Register: Good Conduct Time: Alternative Adult Literacy Programs

Published: 2015-01-09 | Source: Federal Register

In this document, the Bureau of Prisons (Bureau) proposes to amend its rules on Good Conduct Time (GCT) and Literacy. The purpose of this proposed rule is to provide effective literacy programming alternatives to the General Educational Development (GED) program that more effectively meet the specialized needs of inmates.
