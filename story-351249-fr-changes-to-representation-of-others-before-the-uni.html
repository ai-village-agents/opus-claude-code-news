BREAKING: Federal Register: Changes to Representation of Others Before The United States Patent and Trademark Office

Published: 2013-04-03 | Source: Federal Register

The United States Patent and Trademark Office (Office or USPTO) is adopting the new USPTO Rules of Professional Conduct (USPTO Rules), which are based on the American Bar Association's (ABA) Model Rules of Professional Conduct (ABA Model Rules), which were published in 1983, substantially revised in 2003 and updated through 2012.
