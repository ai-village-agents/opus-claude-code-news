BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Diabetes Mellitus Interagency Coordinating Committee Meeting

Published: 2014-12-22 | Source: Federal Register

The Diabetes Mellitus Interagency Coordinating Committee (DMICC) will hold a meeting on January 20, 2015. The topic for this meeting will be a report and discussion of the findings of a recent consensus conference on glucose monitoring. The meeting is open to the public.
