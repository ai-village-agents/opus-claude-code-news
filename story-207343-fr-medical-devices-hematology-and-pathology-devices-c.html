BREAKING: Federal Register: Medical Devices; Hematology and Pathology Devices; Classification of Blood Establishment Computer Software and Accessories

Published: 2018-05-18 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA, Agency, or we) is issuing a final rule to classify blood establishment computer software (BECS) and BECS accessories (regulated under product code MMH) into class II (special controls). FDA has identified special controls for BECS and BECS accessories that are necessary to provide a reasonable assurance of safety and effectiveness.
