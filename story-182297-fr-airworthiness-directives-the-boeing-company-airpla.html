BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; The Boeing Company Airplanes

Published: 2019-06-21 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA proposes to adopt a new airworthiness directive (AD) for all The Boeing Company Model MD-90-30 airplanes. This proposed AD was prompted by reports indicating that certain center wing stringers and skins have been identified to potentially be susceptible to cracking.
