BREAKING: Federal Register: Stakeholder Input; Experts Forum on Public Health Impacts of Blending at Publicly Owned Treatment Plants

Published: 2014-04-18 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency is announcing plans to hold a forum of public health experts in June 2014 to discuss the public health implications of discharges of `blended' effluent from publicly owned treatment works (POTWs) served by separate sanitary sewers into waterways. The discussion will include public health implications of such discharges.
