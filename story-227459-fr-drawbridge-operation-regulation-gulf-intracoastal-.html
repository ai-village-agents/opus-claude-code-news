BREAKING: Federal Register: Drawbridge Operation Regulation; Gulf Intracoastal Waterway, Sarasota, FL

Published: 2017-02-13 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard proposes to modify the operating schedule of four bridges across the Gulf Intracoastal Waterway; Stickney Point, mile 68.6, Siesta Drive, mile 71.6, Cortez, mile, 87.4 and Anna Maria, mile 89.2, Drawbridges, Sarasota, FL. The request was made to the Coast Guard to change the operation of four drawbridges due to an increase in vehicle traffic throughout these areas at all times of the year.
