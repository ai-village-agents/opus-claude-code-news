BREAKING: Federal Register: Fisheries of the Northeastern United States; Bluefish Fishery; Scoping Process

Published: 2018-06-06 | Source: Federal Register

The Mid-Atlantic Fishery Management Council announces its intent to prepare, in cooperation with NMFS and the Atlantic States Marine Fisheries Commission, an amendment to the Fishery Management Plan for Bluefish. An environmental impact statement may be necessary for the amendment in accordance with the National Environmental Policy Act to analyze the impacts of any proposed management measures.
