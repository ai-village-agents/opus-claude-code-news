BREAKING: Federal Register: Section 8 Housing Assistance Payments Program-Fiscal Year (FY) 2013 Inflation Factors for Public Housing Agency (PHA) Renewal Funding

Published: 2013-06-10 | Source: Federal Register

The FY 2013 HUD Appropriations Act requires that HUD apply "an inflation factor as established by the Secretary, by notice published in the Federal Register" to adjust FY 2013 renewal funding for the Tenant-based Rental Assistance Program or Housing Choice Voucher (HCV) Program of each PHA. For FY 2011 and FY 2010, renewal funding was based on annual adjustment factors (AAFs) and HUD published separate Renewal Funding AAFs for this purpose.
