BREAKING: Federal Register: Fire Safety of Small Passenger Vessels

Published: 2021-12-27 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is issuing an interim rule as the first step to implementing the statutorily mandated requirements for fire safety on certain covered small passenger vessels. This statutory mandate is in response to the fire and loss of life on the dive boat CONCEPTION off the coast of California on September 2, 2019.
