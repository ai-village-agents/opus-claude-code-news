BREAKING: Federal Register: Request for Information

Published: 2020-10-07 | Source: Federal Register

The Foundations for Evidence-Based Policymaking Act of 2018 requires federal agencies to develop evidence-building plans to identify and address questions relevant to Agency policies, programs, regulations, management, and operations. Through this Request for Information (RFI), the National Science Foundation (NSF), seeks to expand ongoing efforts to identify priority questions that can guide evidence-building activities by soliciting input from the public.
