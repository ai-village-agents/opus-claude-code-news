BREAKING: Federal Register: Privacy Act of 1974; System of Records

Published: 2017-11-06 | Source: Federal Register

The Council of the Inspectors General on Integrity and Efficiency (CIGIE) proposes to establish a system of records that is subject to the Privacy Act of 1974. Specifically, the Correspondence Tracking system of records will enable CIGIE to more efficiently track correspondence received from and sent to entities and individuals, both within and external to the Federal government.
