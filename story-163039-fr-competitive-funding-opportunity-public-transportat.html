BREAKING: Federal Register: Competitive Funding Opportunity: Public Transportation COVID-19 Research Demonstration Grant Program

Published: 2020-10-08 | Source: Federal Register

The Coronavirus Disease 2019 (COVID-19) public health emergency has had a significant impact on transit operations. During a series of FTA listening sessions held over the last three months, transit agencies asked FTA to support research to identify solutions to address the operational challenges that they are facing as a result of COVID- 19.
