BREAKING: Federal Register: Protecting Americans From Harmful Data Broker Practices (Regulation V)

Published: 2024-12-13 | Source: Federal Register

The Consumer Financial Protection Bureau (CFPB) is issuing a proposed rule for public comment to amend Regulation V, which implements the Fair Credit Reporting Act (FCRA). The proposed rule would implement the FCRA's definitions of consumer report and consumer reporting agency as well as certain of the FCRA's provisions governing when consumer reporting agencies may furnish, and users may obtain, consumer reports.
