BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; AgustaWestland S.p.A. Helicopters

Published: 2014-08-05 | Source: Federal Register

We are publishing a new airworthiness directive (AD) for certain AgustaWestland S.p.A. Model AB139 and AW139 helicopters. This AD requires repetitively inspecting the Main Rotor (M/R) Rotating Scissors for play of the Lower Half Scissor Spherical Bearing (bearing) and removing the bearing if there is play beyond allowable limits.
