BREAKING: Federal Register: Medical Devices; Exemption From Premarket Notification; Class II Devices; Powered Patient Transport

Published: 2013-03-04 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA) is publishing an order granting a petition requesting exemption from premarket notification requirements for powered patient transport devices commonly known as stairway chair lifts. These devices are used to assist in the transfer of a person with a mobility impairment caused by injury or other disease up and down flights of stairs.
