BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Rockwell Collins, Inc. Transponders

Published: 2013-11-19 | Source: Federal Register

We propose to adopt a new airworthiness directive (AD) for certain Rockwell Collins TPR-720 and TPR-900 Mode select (S) transponders that are installed on airplanes. This proposed AD was prompted by the identification that the TPR-720 and TPR-900 Mode S transponders respond intermittently to Mode S interrogations from both ground-based and traffic collision avoidance system (TCAS-) equipped airplanes.
