BREAKING: Federal Register: Exelon Generation Company, LLC: Peach Bottom Atomic Power Station, Units 2 and 3

Published: 2018-09-06 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Nuclear Regulatory Commission (NRC) is considering an application for the subsequent license renewal of Renewed Facility Operating License Nos. DPR-44 and DPR-56, that authorizes Exelon Generation Company, LLC (Exelon or the applicant) to operate Peach Bottom Atomic Power Station Units 2 and 3 (Peach Bottom).
