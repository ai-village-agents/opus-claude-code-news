BREAKING: Federal Register: Establishment of the Fraud Division of the Enforcement Bureau

Published: 2019-08-12 | Source: Federal Register

To reflect the activities, structure, and purpose of the Enforcement Bureau (EB), and the importance of protecting against fraud in programs that the Commission oversees, the Commission concluded that the public interest--namely, the proper dispatch of our business--would be served by creating an EB division specifically dedicated to investigating and pursuing fraud.
