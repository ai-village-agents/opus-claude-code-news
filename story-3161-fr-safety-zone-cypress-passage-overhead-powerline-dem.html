BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone; Cypress Passage Overhead Powerline Demolition and Removal, Atchafalaya River, LA

Published: 2025-01-07 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is establishing a temporary safety zone for navigable waters within a 500-yard radius of 29[deg]47'38.18" N, 91[deg]21'50.52" W, approximately MM 105.5, on the Atchafalaya River, locally known as Cypress Passage, during an overhead electrical powerline structure demolition operation on Thursday, January 9, 2025.
