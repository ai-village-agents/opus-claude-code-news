BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Airbus SAS Airplanes

Published: 2023-02-06 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA is adopting a new airworthiness directive (AD) for all Airbus SAS Model A350-941 airplanes. This AD was prompted by reports of main landing gear (MLG) bogie pivot pins with damaged high velocity oxygen fuel (HVOF) coating, which resulted from heating caused by friction between the MLG bogie pivot pin and the bushes.
