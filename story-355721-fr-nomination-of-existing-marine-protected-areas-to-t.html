BREAKING: Federal Register: Nomination of Existing Marine Protected Areas to the National System of Marine Protected Areas

Published: 2013-05-23 | Source: Federal Register

In January 2013, NOAA and the Department of the Interior (DOI) invited federal, state, commonwealth, and territorial marine protected area (MPA) programs with potentially eligible existing MPAs to nominate their sites to the National System of MPAs (national system).
