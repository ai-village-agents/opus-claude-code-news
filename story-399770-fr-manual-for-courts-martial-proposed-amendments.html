BREAKING: Federal Register: Manual for Courts-Martial; Proposed Amendments

Published: 2012-10-23 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Defense is proposing changes to the Manual for Courts-Martial, United States (2012 ed.) (MCM). The proposed changes concern the rules of procedure and evidence and the punitive articles applicable in trials by courts-martial.
