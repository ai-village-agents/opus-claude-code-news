BREAKING: Federal Register: Amendment of Class E Airspace; Muscle Shoals, AL

Published: 2021-08-27 | Source: Federal Register

This action amends Class E airspace extending upward from 700 feet above the surface in Muscle Shoals, AL, due to the decommissioning of the Muscle Shoals Very High Frequency Omni-Directional Radio Range Tactical Air Navigation Aid (VORTAC), and cancellation of the associated approach at Northwest Alabama Regional Airport.
