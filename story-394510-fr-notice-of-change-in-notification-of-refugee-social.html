BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Change in Notification of Refugee Social Services and Targeted Assistance Formula Grant Allocations

Published: 2012-08-23 | Source: Federal Register

The Office of Refugee Resettlement, Administration for Children and Families (ACF), is changing the notification to States and Wilson/Fish Alternative Project grantees of final Social Services and Targeted Assistance formula grant awards. Under these two programs, formula grants are allotted to States based on the eligible population in each State.
