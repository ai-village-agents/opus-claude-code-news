BREAKING: Federal Register: Environmental Impact Statement for Predator Damage Management in Oregon

Published: 2021-01-21 | Source: Federal Register

The Animal and Plant Health Inspection Service is preparing an environmental impact statement analyzing alternatives for predator damage management in Oregon. This notice proposes issues and alternatives for consideration in the environmental impact statement and requests public comments to further delineate the scope of the alternatives, the environmental issues, and other issues of public concern to be considered.
