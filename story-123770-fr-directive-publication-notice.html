BREAKING: Federal Register: Directive Publication Notice

Published: 2021-05-14 | Source: Federal Register

The USDA Forest Service provides direction to employees through issuances in its Directive System, comprised of the Forest Service Manual and Forest Service Handbooks. The Agency must provide notice of and opportunity for the public to comment on any directives that formulate standards, criteria, or guidelines applicable to Forest Service programs.
