BREAKING: Federal Register: Advisory Committee on Earthquake Hazards Reduction Meeting

Published: 2017-04-12 | Source: Federal Register

The Advisory Committee on Earthquake Hazards Reduction (ACEHR or Committee), will meet on Monday, July 24, 2017 from 8:30 a.m. to 5:00 p.m. Mountain Time and Tuesday, July 25, 2017, from 8:30 a.m. to 2:30 p.m. Mountain Time. The primary purpose of this meeting is to discuss the Committee's 2017 Report on the Effectiveness of the National Earthquake Hazards Reduction Program (NEHRP).
