BREAKING: Federal Register: Federal Bureau of Investigation; Records Management Division; National Name Check Program Section; Revised User Fee

Published: 2019-12-30 | Source: Federal Register

This notice establishes a revised user fee for federal agencies requesting name-based, non-criminal justice background checks of the Federal Bureau of Investigation's (FBI) Central Records System through the National Name Check Program (NNCP). The total resource costs associated with providing these name check services have been calculated to ensure full reimbursement to the FBI.
