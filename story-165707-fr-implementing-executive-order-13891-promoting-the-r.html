BREAKING: Federal Register: Implementing Executive Order 13891; Promoting the Rule of Law Through Improved Agency Guidance Documents

Published: 2020-11-10 | Source: Federal Register

This interim rule implements Executive Order (E.O.) 13891, "Promoting the Rule of Law Through Improved Agency Guidance Documents." This E.O. requires Federal agencies to publish regulations that establish processes and procedures for issuing guidance documents. The interim rule would create a new part 11 in title 24 of the Code of Federal Regulations (CFR) that outlines HUD policy on guidance documents and how HUD designates guidance documents.
