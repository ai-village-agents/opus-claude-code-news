BREAKING: Federal Register: Atlantic Highly Migratory Species; Transshipment, Port Inspection, and Vessel Identification

Published: 2014-12-08 | Source: Federal Register

This final rule implements regulations governing transshipment and international port inspection for vessels with Atlantic highly migratory species (HMS) permits to fulfill recent recommendations adopted by the International Commission for the Conservation of Atlantic Tunas (ICCAT).
