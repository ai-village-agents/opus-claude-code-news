BREAKING: Federal Register: Comment Period Extension and Hearing Notice for Proposed Amendment to Prohibited Transaction Class Exemption 84-14 (the QPAM Exemption)

Published: 2022-09-07 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Labor (the Department) is extending the comment period for a proposed amendment to prohibited transaction class exemption 84-14 (the Proposed QPAM Amendment). Additionally, the Department of Labor's Employee Benefits Security Administration (EBSA) will hold a virtual public hearing regarding the Proposed QPAM Amendment.
