BREAKING: Federal Register: Proposed Modification of Class D and Class E Airspace; Pueblo, CO

Published: 2013-02-21 | Source: Federal Register

This action proposes to modify Class D airspace and the Class E airspace areas at Pueblo Memorial Airport, Pueblo, CO. Controlled airspace is necessary to accommodate aircraft using VHF Omni- Directional Radio Range/Distance Measuring Equipment (VOR/DME) standard instrument approach procedures at Pueblo Memorial Airport.
