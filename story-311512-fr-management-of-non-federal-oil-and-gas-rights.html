BREAKING: Federal Register: Management of Non-Federal Oil and Gas Rights

Published: 2015-12-11 | Source: Federal Register

We, the U.S. Fish and Wildlife Service (Service), are proposing to revise regulations governing the exercise of non-Federal oil and gas rights in order to improve our ability to protect refuge resources, visitors, and the general public's health and safety from potential impacts associated with non-Federal oil and gas operations located within U.S. Fish and Wildlife Service refuge units.
