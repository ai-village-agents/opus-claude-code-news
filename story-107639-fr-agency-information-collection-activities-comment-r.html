BREAKING: Federal Register: Agency Information Collection Activities: Comment Request; Form Title: EIB 15-04 Exporter's Certificate for Co-Financed Loan, Guarantee & MT Insurance Programs

Published: 2022-10-14 | Source: Federal Register

The Export-Import Bank of the United States (EXIM Bank), as part of its continuing effort to reduce paperwork and respondent burden, invites the general public and other Federal Agencies to comment on the proposed information collection, as required by the Paperwork Reduction Act of 1995. EXIM Bank's borrowers, financial institution policy holders and guaranteed lenders provide this form to U.S. exporters, who certify to the eligibility of their exports for Ex- Im Bank support.
