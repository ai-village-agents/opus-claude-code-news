BREAKING: Federal Register: Hours of Service of Drivers-Restart Provisions

Published: 2019-09-12 | Source: Federal Register

FMCSA amends its hours-of-service (HOS) requirements applicable to drivers of property-carrying commercial motor vehicles (CMVs) to remove provisions requiring that a 34-hour restart include two periods between 1 a.m. and 5 a.m. and limiting use of a restart to once every 168 hours--provisions that were promulgated in December 2011.
