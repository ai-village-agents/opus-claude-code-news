BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; The Boeing Company Airplanes

Published: 2019-06-06 | Source: Federal Register

We are adopting a new airworthiness directive (AD) for all The Boeing Company Model 737-100, -200, -200C, -300, -400, and -500 series airplanes. This AD was prompted by reports of cracks caused by corrosion of the edge of the bore of the spot face and corrosion of the lug bore of certain side-strut support fitting lugs.
