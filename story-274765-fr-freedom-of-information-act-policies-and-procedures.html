BREAKING: Federal Register: Freedom of Information Act Policies and Procedures

Published: 2016-09-23 | Source: Federal Register

As part of a comprehensive review of agency practices related to the disclosure of records and information, the Office of the United States Trade Representative (USTR) is updating its implementing rule under the Freedom of Information Act (FOIA).
