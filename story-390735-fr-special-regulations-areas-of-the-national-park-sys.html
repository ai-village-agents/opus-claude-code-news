BREAKING: Federal Register: Special Regulations; Areas of the National Park System, Chattahoochee River National Recreation Area, Bicycle Routes

Published: 2012-07-10 | Source: Federal Register

The National Park Service proposes to designate certain multi- use pathways in Chattahoochee River National Recreation Area as routes for bicycle use. National Park Service regulations require promulgation of a special regulation to designate routes for bicycle use off park roads and outside developed areas.
