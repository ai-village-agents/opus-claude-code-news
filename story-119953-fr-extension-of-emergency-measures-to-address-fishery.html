BREAKING: Federal Register: Extension of Emergency Measures To Address Fishery Observer Coverage During the Coronavirus Pandemic

Published: 2021-03-29 | Source: Federal Register

NMFS promulgates this interim final rule to revise the termination date of an emergency action that provides authority to waive observer coverage requirements. NMFS is taking this action to address public health concerns that relate to the ongoing Coronavirus (COVID-19) pandemic.
