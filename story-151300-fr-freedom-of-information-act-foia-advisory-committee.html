BREAKING: Federal Register: Freedom of Information Act (FOIA) Advisory Committee; Meeting and Request for Public Comments

Published: 2020-05-12 | Source: Federal Register

We are announcing an upcoming Freedom of Information Act (FOIA) Advisory Committee meeting in accordance with the Federal Advisory Committee Act and the second United States Open Government National Action Plan. We are also providing an opportunity for the public to provide comments on the FOIA Advisory Committee's final draft report to the Archivist of the United States.
