BREAKING: Federal Register: Meetings of the United States-Panama Environmental Affairs Council and Environmental Cooperation Commission

Published: 2018-09-25 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of State and the Office of the United States Trade Representative (USTR) are providing notice that the United States and Panama intend to hold the second meetings of the Environmental Affairs Council (the "Council") and the Environmental Cooperation Commission (the "Commission") on October 3, 2018.
