BREAKING: Federal Register: The NCUA Staff Draft 2026-2027 Budget

Published: 2025-10-21 | Source: Federal Register

The NCUA's staff draft "detailed business-type budget" for 2026 and 2027 was made available for public review in the Federal Register as required by federal statute on September 29, 2025, as docket number NCUA-2025-0543. The NCUA will hold an in-person budget hearing to discuss its staff draft budget on November 6, 2025, at 1 p.m. Eastern. Further information about the hearing schedule and comment instructions are included in the SUPPLEMENTARY INFORMATION section.
