BREAKING: Federal Register: Proposed Priority-Promise Zones

Published: 2013-10-25 | Source: Federal Register

The Secretary of Education (Secretary) proposes a priority that the Department of Education (Department) may use for any appropriate discretionary grant program in fiscal year (FY) 2014 and future years. We take this action to focus Federal financial assistance on expanding the number of Department programs and projects that support activities in designated Promise Zones.
