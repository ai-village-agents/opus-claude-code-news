BREAKING: Federal Register: Multiple Employer Plans

Published: 2019-07-03 | Source: Federal Register

This document sets forth proposed regulations relating to the tax qualification of plans maintained by more than one employer. These plans, maintained pursuant to section 413(c) of the Internal Revenue Code (Code), are often referred to as multiple employer plans or MEPs.
