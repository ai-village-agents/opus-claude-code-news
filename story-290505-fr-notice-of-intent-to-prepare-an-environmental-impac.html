BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Intent To Prepare an Environmental Impact Statement: Dane County, Wisconsin

Published: 2015-04-06 | Source: Federal Register

The FHWA is issuing this notice to advise the public that an EIS will be prepared for a proposed freeway interchange improvement project on I-39/90 from the County N interchange in the south to the I- 39/90/94/WIS 30 interchange (Badger Interchange) in the north and on US 12/18 from the West Broadway interchange in the west to the County AB intersection in the east in Dane County in south-central Wisconsin.
