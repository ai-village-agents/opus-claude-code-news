BREAKING: Federal Register: Benefits of Neonicotinoid Seed Treatment to Soybean Production; Reopening of Comment Period

Published: 2014-12-24 | Source: Federal Register

EPA issued a notice in the Federal Register of October 22, 2014, concerning the assessment the Agency conducted as part of its ongoing re-evaluation of clothianidin, imidacloprid, and thiamethoxam under the registration review program. This assessment examines the use of clothianidin, imidacloprid, and thiamethoxam seed treatments in terms of the extent of use and the pests targeted in order to characterize overall benefits to soybean production nationwide.
