BREAKING: Federal Register: Statistical Approaches To Evaluate Analytical Similarity; Draft Guidance for Industry; Availability

Published: 2017-09-22 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA or Agency) is announcing the availability of a draft guidance for industry entitled "Statistical Approaches to Evaluate Analytical Similarity." This draft guidance, when finalized, will provide advice on the evaluation of analytical similarity to sponsors interested in developing biosimilar products.
