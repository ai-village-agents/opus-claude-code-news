BREAKING: Federal Register: Certain Movable Barrier Operator Systems and Components Thereof; Notice of a Commission Determination To Adopt in Part an Advisory Opinion; Termination of Advisory Opinion Proceeding

The Commission has determined to adopt the IAO's finding of non-infringement of claims 1 and 21 of U.S. Patent Nos. 7,755,223.
