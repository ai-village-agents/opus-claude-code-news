BREAKING: Federal Register: Request for Information on the 2023 Federal Cybersecurity Research and Development Strategic Plan

Published: 2023-02-21 | Source: Federal Register

On February 7, 2023, the NITRD NCO and NSF, on behalf of the NITRD Cyber Security and Information Assurance Interagency Working Group (CSIA IWG), published in the Federal Register a document entitled "Request for Information on the 2023 Federal Cybersecurity Research and Development Strategic Plan." Through this RFI, the NITRD NCO seeks input from the public on Federal priorities in cybersecurity R&D.
