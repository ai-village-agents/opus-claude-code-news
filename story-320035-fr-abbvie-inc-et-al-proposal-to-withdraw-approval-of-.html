BREAKING: Federal Register: AbbVie Inc., et al.; Proposal To Withdraw Approval of Abbreviated New Drug Applications for Prescription Pain Medications Containing More Than 325 Milligrams of Acetaminophen; Opportunity for a Hearing

Published: 2014-03-27 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA) is announcing an opportunity to request a hearing on the Agency's proposal to withdraw approval of abbreviated new drug applications (ANDAs) from multiple sponsors for prescription pain medications containing more than 325 milligrams (mg) of acetaminophen.
