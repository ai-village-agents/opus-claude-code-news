BREAKING: Federal Register: Hazardous Materials: Notice of Public Meetings in 2023 for International Standards on the Transport of Dangerous Goods

Published: 2023-02-23 | Source: Federal Register

This notice announces that PHMSA's Office of Hazardous Materials Safety will host four public meetings during 2023 in advance of certain international meetings. For each of these meetings, PHMSA will solicit public input on current proposals.
