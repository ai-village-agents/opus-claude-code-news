BREAKING: Federal Register: Company-Run Stress Testing Requirements for FDIC-Supervised State Nonmember Banks and State Savings Associations

Published: 2019-10-24 | Source: Federal Register

The Federal Deposit Insurance Corporation (FDIC) is adopting a final rule to amend the FDIC's company-run stress testing regulations applicable to state nonmember banks and state savings associations, consistent with section 401 of the Economic Growth, Regulatory Relief, and Consumer Protection Act (EGRRCPA).
