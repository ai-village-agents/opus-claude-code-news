BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Funds Availability; Emergency Relief Program (ERP)

Published: 2022-05-18 | Source: Federal Register

The Farm Service Agency (FSA) is issuing this notice announcing ERP. ERP will provide assistance to producers for losses to crops, trees, bushes, and vines due to wildfires, hurricanes, floods, derechos, excessive heat, winter storms, freeze (including a polar vortex), smoke exposure, excessive moisture, qualifying drought, and related conditions occurring in calendar years 2020 and 2021.
