BREAKING: Federal Register: Financial Assurance Measures for Hydroelectric Projects

Published: 2021-01-26 | Source: Federal Register

The Federal Energy Regulatory Commission (Commission) is inviting comments on what changes, if any, the Commission should make to its practices for requiring financial assurance measures in licenses and other authorizations for hydroelectric projects.
