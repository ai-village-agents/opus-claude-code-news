BREAKING: Federal Register: Malheur National Forest, Prairie City Ranger District; Oregon; Cliff Knox Project

Published: 2018-07-20 | Source: Federal Register

The Malheur National Forest will prepare an environmental impact statement (EIS) to disclose the environmental effects of proposed vegetation and fuels treatments, wildlife habitat designations, and road activities in the Cliff Knox project area located on the Prairie City and Emigrant Creek Ranger Districts. Proposed actions include timber harvest, small diameter thinning, aspen and mountain mahogany restoration, landscape underburning, road activities to support vegetation and fuels treatments...
