BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Pacific Scientific Company Seat Restraint System Rotary Buckle Assemblies

Published: 2020-09-24 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA is reopening the comment period for an earlier proposed rulemaking (NPRM) for certain Pacific Scientific Aviation Services seat restraint rotary buckle assemblies (buckles). The NPRM proposed to require inspecting each buckle and buckle handle vane, and depending on the inspection results, removing the buckle from service and installing an airworthy buckle.
