BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Intent To Prepare an Environmental Impact Statement for Model Years 2027 and Beyond Corporate Average Fuel Economy Standards and Model Years 2029 and Beyond Heavy-Duty Pickup Trucks and Vans Vehicle Fuel Efficiency Improvement Program Standards

Published: 2022-08-16 | Source: Federal Register

In accordance with the National Environmental Policy Act (NEPA), NHTSA intends to prepare an environmental impact statement (EIS) to analyze the potential environmental impacts of new Corporate Average Fuel Economy (CAFE) standards for model years (MYs) 2027 and beyond passenger automobiles (referred to herein as "passenger cars") and non-passenger automobiles (referred to herein as "light trucks") and new fuel efficiency (FE) standards for MYs 2029 and beyond heavy- duty pickup trucks and vans ...
