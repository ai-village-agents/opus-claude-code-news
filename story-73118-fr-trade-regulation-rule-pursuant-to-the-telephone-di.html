BREAKING: Federal Register: Trade Regulation Rule Pursuant to the Telephone Disclosure and Dispute Resolution Act of 1992

Published: 2023-07-28 | Source: Federal Register

On March 12, 1997, the Federal Trade Commission initiated a review of the effectiveness of its Pay-Per-Call Rule. The Commission sought comment on whether to expand the scope of this rule to cover audio information and entertainment services accessed by dialing telephone numbers that begin with numbers other than "900."
