BREAKING: Federal Register: Prevailing Rate Systems; Conduct of Local Wage Surveys

Published: 2021-03-01 | Source: Federal Register

The Office of Personnel Management is issuing an interim final rule to amend regulations to allow for additional options to collect wage data during Federal Wage System full-scale and wage change surveys, namely, by personal visit, telephone, mail, or electronic means.
