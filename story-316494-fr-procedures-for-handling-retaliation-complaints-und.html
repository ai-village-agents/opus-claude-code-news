BREAKING: Federal Register: Procedures for Handling Retaliation Complaints Under Section 402 of the FDA Food Safety Modernization Act

Published: 2014-02-13 | Source: Federal Register

This document provides the interim final regulations governing the employee protection (whistleblower) provision found at section 402 of the FDA Food Safety Modernization Act (FSMA), which added section 1012 to the Federal Food, Drug, and Cosmetic Act.
