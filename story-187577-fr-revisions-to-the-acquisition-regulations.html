BREAKING: Federal Register: Revisions to the Acquisition Regulations

Published: 2019-08-29 | Source: Federal Register

In this direct final rule (DFR), the Department of Labor (Department) is amending three definitions in the Department of Labor Acquisition Regulation (DOLAR) in order to provide the Secretary of Labor greater flexibility and a streamlined procedure to delegate procurement authority and appoint procurement officials.
