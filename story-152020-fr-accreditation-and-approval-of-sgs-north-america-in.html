BREAKING: Federal Register: Accreditation and Approval of SGS North America, Inc. (Deer Park, TX), as a Commercial Laboratory

Published: 2020-05-20 | Source: Federal Register

Notice is hereby given, pursuant to CBP regulations, that SGS North America, Inc. (Deer Park, TX), has been accredited to test petroleum and petroleum products for customs purposes for the next three years as of September 17, 2019.
