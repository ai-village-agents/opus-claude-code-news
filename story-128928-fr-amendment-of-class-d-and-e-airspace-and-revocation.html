BREAKING: Federal Register: Amendment of Class D and E Airspace and Revocation of Class E Airspace; Cape Girardeau, MO

Published: 2021-07-23 | Source: Federal Register

This action amends the Class D and Class E airspace and revokes Class E airspace at Cape Girardeau Regional Airport, Cape Girardeau, MO. The FAA is making this action as the result of an airspace review caused by the decommissioning of the Marion very high frequency (VHF) omnidirectional range (VOR) navigation aid as part of the VOR Minimum Operational Network (MON) Program.
