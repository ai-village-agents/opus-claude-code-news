BREAKING: Federal Register: Retirement Security Rule: Definition of an Investment Advice Fiduciary

Published: 2024-04-25 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Labor (Department) is adopting a final rule defining when a person renders "investment advice for a fee or other compensation, direct or indirect" with respect to any moneys or other property of an employee benefit plan, for purposes of the definition of a "fiduciary" in the Employee Retirement Income Security Act of 1974 (Title I of ERISA or the Act).
