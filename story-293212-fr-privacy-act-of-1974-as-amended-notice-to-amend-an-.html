BREAKING: Federal Register: Privacy Act of 1974, as Amended; Notice To Amend an Existing System of Records

Published: 2015-05-07 | Source: Federal Register

Pursuant to the provisions of the Privacy Act of 1974, as amended, the Department of the Interior is issuing public notice of its intent to amend the Office of the Secretary Privacy Act system of records, "Hearings and Appeals Files--Interior, OS-09".
