BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Availability of a Final General Conformity Determination for the California High-Speed Train System Merced to Fresno Section

Published: 2012-10-18 | Source: Federal Register

FRA is issuing this notice to advise the public that it issued a Final General Conformity Determination for the Merced to Fresno Section of the California High-Speed Train (HST) System on September 18, 2012. FRA is the lead Federal agency for compliance with federal environmental review requirements including the National Environmental Policy Act (NEPA) and is also the Federal agency with responsibility for complying with the Clean Air Act General Conformity requirements.
