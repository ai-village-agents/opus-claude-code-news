BREAKING: Federal Register: License Renewal of Nuclear Power Plants; Generic Environmental Impact Statement and Standard Review Plans for Environmental Reviews

Published: 2013-06-20 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Nuclear Regulatory Commission (NRC) is updating and revising NUREG-1437, "Generic Environmental Impact Statement for License Renewal of Nuclear Plants" (GEIS), which the NRC issued in May 1996. The revised GEIS provides the technical basis for amending certain NRC environmental protection regulations.
