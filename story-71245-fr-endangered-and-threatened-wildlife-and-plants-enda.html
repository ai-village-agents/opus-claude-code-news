BREAKING: Federal Register: Endangered and Threatened Wildlife and Plants; Endangered Species Status for the Dunes Sagebrush Lizard

Published: 2023-07-03 | Source: Federal Register

We, the U.S. Fish and Wildlife Service (Service), propose to list the dunes sagebrush lizard (Sceloporus arenicolus), a species found only in southeastern New Mexico and west Texas, as an endangered species under the Endangered Species Act of 1973, as amended (Act). This determination also serves as our 12-month finding on a petition to list the dunes sagebrush lizard.
