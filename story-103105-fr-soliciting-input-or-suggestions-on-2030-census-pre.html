BREAKING: Federal Register: Soliciting Input or Suggestions on 2030 Census Preliminary Research

Published: 2022-08-17 | Source: Federal Register

Early planning for the 2030 Census program began in Fiscal Year 2019 with building the program foundation and preparing for the official program kick-off and start of the Design Selection Phase in October 2021. The primary goal of the Design Selection Phase is to conduct the research, testing, and operational planning and design work to inform the selection of the 2030 Census operational design.
