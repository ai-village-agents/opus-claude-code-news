BREAKING: Federal Register: 30-Day Notice of Proposed Information Collection: Production of Material or Provision of Testimony by HUD in Response to Demands in Legal Proceedings Among Private Litigants

Published: 2015-05-22 | Source: Federal Register

This notice corrects the notice HUD published on May 18, 2015 at 80 FR 28294. HUD has submitted the proposed information collection requirement described below to the Office of Management and Budget (OMB) for review, in accordance with the Paperwork Reduction Act. The purpose of this notice is to allow for an additional 30 days of public comment. The OMB number will be changed from 2501-0022 to 2510-0014.
