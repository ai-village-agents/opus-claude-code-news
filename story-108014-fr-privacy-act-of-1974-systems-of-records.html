BREAKING: Federal Register: Privacy Act of 1974: Systems of Records

Published: 2022-10-20 | Source: Federal Register

Pursuant to the Privacy Act of 1974, the National Credit Union Administration (NCUA) gives notice of a proposal to revise an existing Privacy Act system of records. The revised system is the Examination and Supervision System (ESS), NCUA-22.
