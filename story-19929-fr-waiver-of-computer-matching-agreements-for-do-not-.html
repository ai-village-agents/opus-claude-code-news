BREAKING: Federal Register: Waiver of Computer Matching Agreements for Do Not Pay

Published: 2025-09-10 | Source: Federal Register

Under the Do Not Pay statute, the Secretary of the Treasury (Secretary) may waive statutory computer matching requirements, in consultation with the Director of the Office of Management and Budget (OMB), where legally permissible. OMB has issued guidance for agencies which outlines waiver eligibility criteria and specifies conditions agencies must meet to obtain the waiver.
