BREAKING: Federal Register: Charter Renewal for the Advisory Commission on Childhood Vaccines

Published: 2022-07-21 | Source: Federal Register

In accordance with the Federal Advisory Committee Act (FACA), HHS is hereby giving notice that the charter for the Advisory Commission on Childhood Vaccines (ACCV) has been renewed. The effective date of the renewed charter is July 21, 2022.
