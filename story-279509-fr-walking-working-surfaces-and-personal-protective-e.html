BREAKING: Federal Register: Walking-Working Surfaces and Personal Protective Equipment (Fall Protection Systems)

Published: 2016-11-18 | Source: Federal Register

OSHA is revising and updating its general industry standards on walking-working surfaces to prevent and reduce workplace slips, trips, and falls, as well as other injuries and fatalities associated with walking-working surface hazards. The final rule includes revised and new provisions addressing, for example, fixed ladders; rope descent systems; fall protection systems and criteria, including personal fall protection systems; and training on fall hazards and fall protection systems.
