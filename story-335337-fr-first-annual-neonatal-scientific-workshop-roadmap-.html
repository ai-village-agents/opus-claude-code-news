BREAKING: Federal Register: First Annual Neonatal Scientific Workshop-Roadmap for Applying Regulatory Science to Neonates; Notice of Public Workshop

Published: 2014-09-22 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA) is announcing a public scientific workshop to discuss the roadmap for applying regulatory science to neonates. This public scientific workshop is being co- sponsored with the FDA, the Critical Path Institute (C-Path) and the Burroughs Welcome Fund (BWF).
