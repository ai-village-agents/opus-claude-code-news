BREAKING: Federal Register: Air Plan Approval; Missouri; Control of Emissions From Lithographic and Letterpress Printing Operations

Published: 2020-07-22 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is taking final action to approve a revision to the State Implementation Plan (SIP) for the State of Missouri for a regulation that controls emissions from lithographic and letterpress printing operations.
