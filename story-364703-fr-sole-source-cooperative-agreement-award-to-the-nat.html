BREAKING: Federal Register: Sole Source Cooperative Agreement Award to the National Association of County and City Health Officials (NACCHO)

Published: 2013-09-05 | Source: Federal Register

ASPR requires collaboration with local health departments and officials to ensure that local governments, communities, private sector entities, non-governmental organizations, academia, and individuals can optimally coordinate their respective national health security roles and responsibilities to achieve community health resilience and strengthen health care, public health, and emergency management systems.
