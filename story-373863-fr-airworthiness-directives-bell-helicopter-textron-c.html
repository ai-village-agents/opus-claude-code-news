BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Bell Helicopter Textron Canada Limited Helicopters

Published: 2013-12-27 | Source: Federal Register

We are adopting a new airworthiness directive (AD) for certain serial-numbered Bell Helicopter Textron Canada Limited (BHTC) Model 206L, 206L-1, 206L-3, and 206L-4 helicopters with a certain tailboom upper left attachment fitting (fitting). This AD requires inspecting the fitting for a crack and other conditions.
