BREAKING: Federal Register: Management Fee Policy

Published: 2015-09-14 | Source: Federal Register

On December 30, 2014, the National Science Foundation (NSF) published at 79 FR 78497 a notice and request for comments on NSF's revised policy on management fee. The payment of a small but appropriate management fee has been a long standing practice at NSF in limited circumstances related to the construction and operation of major facility projects.
