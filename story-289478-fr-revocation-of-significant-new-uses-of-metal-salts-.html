BREAKING: Federal Register: Revocation of Significant New Uses of Metal Salts of Complex Inorganic Oxyacids

Published: 2015-03-24 | Source: Federal Register

EPA is revoking the significant new use rule (SNUR) promulgated under section 5(a)(2) of the Toxic Substances Control Act (TSCA) for two chemical substances that were identified generically as metal salts of complex inorganic oxyacids, which were the subject of premanufacture notices (PMNs) P-89-576 and P-89-577.
