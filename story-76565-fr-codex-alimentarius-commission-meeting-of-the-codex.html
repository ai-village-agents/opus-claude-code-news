BREAKING: Federal Register: Codex Alimentarius Commission: Meeting of the Codex Alimentarius Commission

Published: 2023-09-11 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Codex Office is sponsoring a public meeting on October 18, 2023, from 1:00-4:00 p.m. EDT. The objective of the public meeting is to provide information and receive public comments on agenda items and draft U.S. positions to be discussed at the 46th Session of the Codex Alimentarius Commission (CAC46), which will meet in Rome, Italy from November 27, 2023 to December 2, 2023.
