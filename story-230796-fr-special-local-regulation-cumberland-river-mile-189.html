BREAKING: Federal Register: Special Local Regulation; Cumberland River, Mile 189.0 to 193.0; Nashville, TN

Published: 2017-03-30 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard proposes to establish a special local regulation for all waters of the Cumberland River beginning at mile marker 189.0 and ending at mile marker 193.0 from 11 a.m. until 6 p.m. on May 13, 4 a.m. until 6 p.m. on May 14, and 4 a.m. until 3 p.m. on May 15, 2017. This proposed special regulation is necessary to provide safety for the participants in the "ACRA Henley" marine event.
