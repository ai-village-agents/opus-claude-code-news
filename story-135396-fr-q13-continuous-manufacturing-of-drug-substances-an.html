BREAKING: Federal Register: Q13 Continuous Manufacturing of Drug Substances and Drug Products; International Council for Harmonisation; Draft Guidance for Industry; Availability

Published: 2021-10-14 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA or the Agency) is announcing the availability of a draft guidance for industry entitled "Q13 Continuous Manufacturing of Drug Substances and Drug Products." The draft guidance was prepared under the auspices of the International Council for Harmonisation (ICH), formerly the International Conference on Harmonisation.
