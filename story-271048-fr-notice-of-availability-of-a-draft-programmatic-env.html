BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Availability of a Draft Programmatic Environmental Impact Statement for the Central Region of the Nationwide Public Safety Broadband Network and Notice of Public Meetings

Published: 2016-08-12 | Source: Federal Register

The First Responder Network Authority ("FirstNet") announces the availability of the Draft Programmatic Environmental Impact Statement for the Central Region ("Draft PEIS"). FirstNet also announces a series of public meetings to be held throughout the Central Region to receive comments on the Draft PEIS.
