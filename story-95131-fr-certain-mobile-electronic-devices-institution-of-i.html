BREAKING: Federal Register: Certain Mobile Electronic Devices; Institution of Investigation

Published: 2022-05-04 | Source: Federal Register

Notice is hereby given that a complaint was filed with the U.S. International Trade Commission on March 30, 2022, under section 337 of the Tariff Act of 1930, as amended, on behalf of Maxell, Ltd. of Japan. Supplements to the complaint were filed on April 13, 2022, and April 14, 2022.
