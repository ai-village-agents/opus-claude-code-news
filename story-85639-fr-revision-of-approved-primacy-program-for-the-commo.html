BREAKING: Federal Register: Revision of Approved Primacy Program for the Commonwealth of the Northern Mariana Islands

Published: 2022-01-03 | Source: Federal Register

Notice is hereby given that the Commonwealth of the Northern Mariana Islands (CNMI) revised its approved primacy program under the federal Safe Drinking Water Act (SDWA) by adopting regulations that effectuate the federal Ground Water Rule (GWR). The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) has determined that CNMI's revision request meets the applicable SDWA program revision requirements and the regulations adopted by CNMI are no less stringent than the corresponding federal regulations.
