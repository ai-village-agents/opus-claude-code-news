BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Standard for Bassinets and Cradles; Notice of Comment Period Extension

Published: 2024-06-18 | Source: Federal Register

On April 16, 2024, the Consumer Product Safety Commission (CPSC) published in the Federal Register a notice of proposed rulemaking (NPR) to amend the existing regulation for bassinets and cradles, to ensure that the rule addresses identified hazards and that these sleep products for young infants provide the highest level of safety feasible.
