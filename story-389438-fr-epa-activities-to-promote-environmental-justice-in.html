BREAKING: Federal Register: EPA Activities To Promote Environmental Justice in the Permit Application Process

Published: 2012-06-26 | Source: Federal Register

As part of its ongoing efforts under Plan EJ 2014 to integrate environmental justice into all of its programs, the Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is soliciting public comment on ways that EPA and permit applicants can meaningfully engage communities in the permitting process.
