BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; the Boeing Company Airplanes

Published: 2014-08-05 | Source: Federal Register

We are superseding Airworthiness Directive (AD) 89-12-10, for certain The Boeing Company Model 747 airplanes. AD 89-12-10 required replacement of certain underwing fuel tank access doors with stronger, fire-resistant doors.
