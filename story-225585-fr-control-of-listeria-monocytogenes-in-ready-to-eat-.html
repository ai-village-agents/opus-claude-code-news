BREAKING: Federal Register: Control of Listeria monocytogenes in Ready-To-Eat Foods: Revised Draft Guidance for Industry; Availability

Published: 2017-01-17 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA, we, or Agency) is announcing the availability of a revised draft guidance for industry entitled "Control of Listeria monocytogenes in Ready-To-Eat Foods." The revised draft guidance is intended for any person who is subject to our regulation entitled "Current Good Manufacturing Practice, Hazard Analysis, and Risk-Based Preventive Controls for Human Food" and who manufactures, processes, packs, or holds ready-to-eat (RTE) foods.
