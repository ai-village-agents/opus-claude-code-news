BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Idaho Panhandle Resource Advisory Committee Meeting

Published: 2012-07-30 | Source: Federal Register

Pursuant to the authorities in the Federal Advisory Committee Act (Pub. L. 112-141) and under the Secure Rural Schools and Community Self-Determination Act of 2000 (Pub. L. 110-343) the Idaho Panhandle Resource Advisory Committee will meet Friday, August 24, 2012, at 9:00 a.m. in Coeur d'Alene, Idaho for a business meeting. The business meeting is open to the public.
