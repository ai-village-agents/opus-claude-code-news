BREAKING: Federal Register: Pilot Certification and Qualification Requirements for Air Carrier Operations; Technical Amendment

Published: 2013-12-24 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA is correcting a final rule published on July 15, 2013 (78 FR 42324). In that rule, the FAA amended its regulations to create new certification and qualification requirements for pilots in air carrier operations.
