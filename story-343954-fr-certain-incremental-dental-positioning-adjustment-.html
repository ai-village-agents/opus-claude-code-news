BREAKING: Federal Register: Certain Incremental Dental Positioning Adjustment Appliances and Methods of Producing Same (Enforcement Proceeding); Commission Determination To Review and Reverse an Initial Determination of the Presiding Administrative Law Judge; Termination of the Enforcement Proceeding

Published: 2013-01-10 | Source: Federal Register

Notice is hereby given that the U.S. International Trade Commission has determined to review and reverse an initial determination ("ID") (Order No. 57) of the presiding administrative law judge in the above-captioned enforcement proceeding. The enforcement proceeding is hereby terminated.
