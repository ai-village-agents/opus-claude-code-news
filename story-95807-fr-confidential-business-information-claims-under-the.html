BREAKING: Federal Register: Confidential Business Information Claims Under the Toxic Substances Control Act (TSCA)

Published: 2022-05-12 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is proposing new and amended requirements concerning the assertion and treatment of confidential business information (CBI) claims for information reported to or otherwise obtained by EPA under the Toxic Substances Control Act (TSCA). Amendments to TSCA in 2016 included many new provisions concerning the assertion, Agency review, and treatment of confidentiality claims.
