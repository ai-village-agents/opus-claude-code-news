BREAKING: Federal Register: Hazardous Materials: Harmonization With International Standards (RRR)

Published: 2017-03-30 | Source: Federal Register

PHMSA is issuing a final rule to amend the Hazardous Materials Regulations (HMR) to maintain consistency with international regulations and standards by incorporating various amendments, including changes to proper shipping names, hazard classes, packing groups, special provisions, packaging authorizations, air transport quantity limitations, and vessel stowage requirements.
