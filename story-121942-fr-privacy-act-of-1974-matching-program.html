BREAKING: Federal Register: Privacy Act of 1974; Matching Program

Published: 2021-04-22 | Source: Federal Register

In accordance with the provisions of the Privacy Act, as amended, this notice announces a new matching program with the Centers for Medciare & Medicaid Services (CMS) in which CMS will disclose to the Security Administration (SSA) certain individuals' admission and discharge information for care received in a nursing care facility.
