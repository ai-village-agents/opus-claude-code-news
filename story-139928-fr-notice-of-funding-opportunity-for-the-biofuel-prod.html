BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Funding Opportunity for the Biofuel Producer Program for Fiscal Year 2021

Published: 2021-12-13 | Source: Federal Register

The Rural Business-Cooperative Service (RBCS or Agency), an agency of the United States Department of Agriculture (USDA), announces the application window, application requirements and the availability of up to $700 million in payments to eligible biofuel producers for unexpected market losses as a result of COVID-19 in order to maintain a viable and significant biofuels market for agricultural producers that supply biofuel producers.
