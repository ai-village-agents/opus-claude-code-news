BREAKING: Federal Register: Chlorinated Isocyanurates from Spain: Rescission of Antidumping Duty Administrative Review: 2019-2020

Published: 2020-10-22 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Commerce (Commerce) is rescinding the administrative review of the antidumping duty (AD) order on chlorinated isocyanurates from Spain for the period of review (POR) June 1, 2019, through May 31, 2020, based on the timely withdrawal of the request for review.
