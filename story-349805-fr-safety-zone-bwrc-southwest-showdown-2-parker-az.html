BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone; BWRC Southwest Showdown 2, Parker, AZ

Published: 2013-03-20 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is establishing a temporary safety zone within the Lake Moolvalya region of the navigable waters of the Colorado River in Parker, Arizona for the Blue Water Resort and Casino Southwest Showdown. This temporary safety zone is necessary to provide for the safety of the participants, crew, spectators, participating vessels, and other vessels and users of the waterway.
