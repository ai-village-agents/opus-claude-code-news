BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Inventory Completion: Michigan State University, East Lansing, MI

Published: 2022-08-30 | Source: Federal Register

Michigan State University has completed an inventory of human remains, in consultation with the appropriate Indian Tribes or Native Hawaiian organizations, and has determined that there is a cultural affiliation between the human remains and present-day Indian Tribes or Native Hawaiian organizations.
