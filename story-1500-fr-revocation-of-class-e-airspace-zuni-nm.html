BREAKING: Federal Register: Revocation of Class E Airspace; Zuni, NM

Published: 2026-01-23 | Source: Federal Register

This action proposes to revoke the Class E airspace at Zuni, NM. The FAA is proposing this action due to the closure of Black Rock Airport at Zuni Pueblo, NM.

Document Type: Proposed Rule

Agency: Transportation Department, Federal Aviation Administration
