BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone; Bullhead City River Regatta; Bullhead City, AZ

Published: 2014-05-20 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard proposes to establish a temporary safety zone on the navigable waters of the Colorado River in Bullhead City, Arizona. The safety zone is necessary to provide for the safety of the Bullhead City River Regatta marine event participants. The safety zone will temporarily restrict vessel movement and public waterway use within the designated area.
