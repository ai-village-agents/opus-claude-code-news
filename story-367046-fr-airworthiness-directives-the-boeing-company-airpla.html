BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; The Boeing Company Airplanes

Published: 2013-10-02 | Source: Federal Register

We are adopting a new airworthiness directive (AD) for certain The Boeing Company Model 747-100, -100B, -100B SUD, -200B, -200C, - 200F, -300, -400, -400D, -400F, and 747SR series airplanes. This AD was prompted by reports of cracking at the aft upper corner of the main entry door (MED) 5 cutout.
