BREAKING: Federal Register: Regulated Navigation Area; NW Natural PGM Site, Willamette River, Portland OR

Published: 2022-01-11 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is establishing a regulated navigation area (RNA) at the NW Natural PGM Site on the Willamette River in Portland, OR. This action is necessary to preserve the integrity of an engineered sediment cap as part of an Oregon Department of Environmental Quality (DEQ) required remedial action.
