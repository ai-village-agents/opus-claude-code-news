BREAKING: Federal Register: Polyethylene Terephthalate (PET) Sheet from Korea and Oman; Scheduling of the Final Phase of Anti-Dumping Duty Investigations

Published: 2020-03-19 | Source: Federal Register

The Commission hereby gives notice of the scheduling of the final phase of antidumping investigations Nos. 731-TA-1455 and 731-TA- 1457 (Final) pursuant to the Tariff Act of 1930 ("the Act") to determine whether an industry in the United States is materially injured or threatened with material injury, or the establishment of an industry in the United States is materially retarded, by reason of imports of polyethylene terephthalate (PET) sheet from Korea and Oman, provided for in subheading 3920....
