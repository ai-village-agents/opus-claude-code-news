BREAKING: Federal Register: Proposed 2022 Clean Water Act Financial Capability Assessment Guidance

Published: 2022-02-23 | Source: Federal Register

When municipal discharges cause violations of the Clean Water Act (CWA), EPA sets a schedule for the municipality to address them as soon as possible. When developing schedules to implement the control measures, EPA considers factors such as public health, environmental protection, and a community's financial capability.
