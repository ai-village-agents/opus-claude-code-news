BREAKING: Federal Register: Special Conditions: Airbus Model A380; Stairways Between Decks

Published: 2019-05-03 | Source: Federal Register

This action proposes an amendment to special conditions for the Airbus A380 airplane. This airplane will have novel or unusual design features when compared to the state of technology envisioned in the airworthiness standards for transport category airplanes. This design feature is associated with the complex systems and the configuration of the airplane, including its full-length double deck.
