BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Fokker Services B.V. Airplanes

Published: 2016-01-04 | Source: Federal Register

We propose to supersede Airworthiness Directive (AD) 2011-17- 10 for all Fokker Services B.V. Model F.28 Mark 1000, 2000, 3000, and 4000 airplanes. AD 2011-17-10 currently requires inspecting for a by- pass wire between the housing of each in-tank fuel quantity indication (FQI) cable plug and the cable shield, and corrective actions if necessary.
