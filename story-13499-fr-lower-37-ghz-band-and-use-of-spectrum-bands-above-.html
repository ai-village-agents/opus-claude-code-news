BREAKING: Federal Register: Lower 37 GHz Band and Use of Spectrum Bands Above 24 GHz for Mobile Radio Service

Published: 2025-06-12 | Source: Federal Register

In this document, the Federal Communications Commission (Commission) makes available 600 megahertz of high-frequency spectrum for flexible fixed and mobile use by establishing a mechanism for access to the 37-37.6 GHz band (Lower 37 GHz band). This spectrum, which is shared between non-Federal and Federal operators, can be used for fixed wireless broadband, Internet of Things (IoT), or other innovative services.
