BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Airbus SAS Airplanes

Published: 2020-12-30 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA is adopting a new airworthiness directive (AD) for certain Airbus SAS Model A350-941 airplanes. This AD was prompted by a report that during the assembly of a certain section of the fuselage, the gaps found on self-aligning nuts for eight fasteners were out of tolerance.
