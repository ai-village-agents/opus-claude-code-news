BREAKING: Federal Register: Schedules of Controlled Substances: Removal of Samidorphan From Control

Published: 2021-04-19 | Source: Federal Register

With the issuance of this final rule, the Acting Administrator of the Drug Enforcement Administration removes samidorphan (3- carboxamido-4-hydroxy naltrexone) and its salts from the schedules of the Controlled Substances Act. This scheduling action is pursuant to the Controlled Substances Act which requires that such actions be made on the record after opportunity for a hearing through formal rulemaking.
