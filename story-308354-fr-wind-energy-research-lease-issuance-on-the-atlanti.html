BREAKING: Federal Register: Wind Energy Research Lease Issuance on the Atlantic Outer Continental Shelf Offshore Virginia

Published: 2015-11-02 | Source: Federal Register

BOEM has issued a wind energy research lease to the Commonwealth of Virginia, Department of Mines, Minerals and Energy for an area situated on the Outer Continental Shelf offshore Virginia. The purpose of this notice is to inform the public of the availability of the executed lease, Renewable Energy Lease No. OCS-A-0497.
