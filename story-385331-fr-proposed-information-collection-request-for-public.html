BREAKING: Federal Register: Proposed Information Collection; Request for Public Comment: Indian Health Service Loan Repayment Program (LRP)

Published: 2012-05-10 | Source: Federal Register

In compliance with Section 3506(c)(2)(A) of the Paperwork Reduction Act of 1995, which requires 30 days for public comment on proposed information collection projects, the Indian Health Service (IHS) has submitted to the Office of Management and Budget (OMB) a request to review and approve the information collection listed below. This proposed information collection project was previously published in the Federal Register (77 FR 11558) on February 27, 2012 and allowed 60 days for public comment....
