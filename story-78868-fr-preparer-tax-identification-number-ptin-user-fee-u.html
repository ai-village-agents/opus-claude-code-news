BREAKING: Federal Register: Preparer Tax Identification Number (PTIN) User Fee Update

Published: 2023-10-04 | Source: Federal Register

This document contains interim final regulations relating to the imposition of certain user fees on tax return preparers. These regulations reduce the amount of the user fee to apply for or renew a preparer tax identification number (PTIN) and affect individuals who apply for or renew a PTIN. The Independent Offices Appropriation Act of 1952 authorizes the charging of user fees.
