BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone; Upper Galveston Bay, Kemah, TX

Published: 2024-09-05 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is updating the location and description of a safety zone, and adding two annually recurring dates, for events at the Kemah Boardwalk Fireworks Display, in the Upper Galveston Bay in Kemah, Texas. The safety zone is needed to protect personnel vessels, and the marine environment from potential hazards created by the fireworks show.
