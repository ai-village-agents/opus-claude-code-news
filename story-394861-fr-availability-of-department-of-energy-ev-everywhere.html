BREAKING: Federal Register: Availability of Department of Energy EV Everywhere Grand Challenge Initial Framing Document and Request for Public Comment

Published: 2012-08-28 | Source: Federal Register

The EV Everywhere Grand Challenge is a U.S. Department of Energy "Clean Energy Grand Challenge" with the goal of enabling U.S. companies to be the first in the world to produce plug-in electric vehicles (PEVs) that are as affordable and convenient for the average American family as today's gasoline-powered vehicles within the next 10 years.
