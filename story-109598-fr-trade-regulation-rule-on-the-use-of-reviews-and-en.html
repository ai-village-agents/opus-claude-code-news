BREAKING: Federal Register: Trade Regulation Rule on the Use of Reviews and Endorsements

Published: 2022-11-08 | Source: Federal Register

The Federal Trade Commission (the "Commission") proposes to commence a rulemaking proceeding to address certain deceptive or unfair uses of reviews and endorsements. The Commission is soliciting written comment, data, and arguments concerning the need for such a rulemaking to prevent unfair or deceptive marketing utilizing reviews and endorsements.
