BREAKING: Federal Register: POSTNET Barcode

Published: 2018-10-11 | Source: Federal Register

The Postal Service is amending Mailing Standards of the United States Postal Service, Domestic Mail Manual (DMM[supreg]) to remove all references to the POSTNETTM barcode.

Agency: Postal Service
