BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Request for Renewal of Previously Approved Collection; Short Term Lending Program-Application for Loan Guarantee

Published: 2012-05-31 | Source: Federal Register

In compliance with the Paperwork Reduction Act of 1995, Public Law 104-13, (44 U.S.C. 3501 et seq.) this notice announces that the Information Collection Request, abstracted below, will be forwarded to the Office of Management and Budget for the renewal of the Short Term Lending Program--Application for Loan Guarantee. A 60 day Federal Register Notice (77 FR 14459) was published March 9, 2012 (DOT-OST 2008-0244). The agency did not receive any comments.
