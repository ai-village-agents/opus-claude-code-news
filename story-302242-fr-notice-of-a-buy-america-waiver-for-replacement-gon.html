BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of a Buy America Waiver for Replacement Gondola Components

Published: 2015-08-21 | Source: Federal Register

The Federal Transit Administration (FTA) received a Buy America waiver request from the Colorado Department of Transportation on behalf of the Town of Mountain Village for replacement parts for a gondola rehabilitation project. A non-availability waiver is needed because Mountain Village intends to rehabilitate the gondola system with FTA funding and the replacement parts do not comply with Buy America requirements.
