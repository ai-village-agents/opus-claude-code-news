BREAKING: Federal Register: Elimination of the Voluntary Trichinae Certification Program

Published: 2021-09-24 | Source: Federal Register

We are amending the regulations to eliminate the Animal and Plant Health Inspection Service (APHIS) Voluntary Trichinae Certification Program and remove the regulations associated with the program. This action also notifies the public that APHIS will no longer maintain any activity associated with the program, such as training for qualified accredited veterinarians, on-farm audits, or any other administrative process associated with program maintenance and support.
