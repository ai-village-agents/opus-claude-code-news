BREAKING: Federal Register: Special Local Regulations; Riverfest Power Boat Races, Neches River, Port Neches, Texas

Published: 2023-03-08 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard will enforce special local regulation for the Riverfest boat races on the Neches River in Port Neches, TX from May 5, 2023 through May 7, 2023 to provide for the safety of life on navigable waterways during this event. Our regulation for marine events within the Eighth Coast Guard District identifies the regulated area for this event in Port Neches, TX.
