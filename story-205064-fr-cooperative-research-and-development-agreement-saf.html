BREAKING: Federal Register: Cooperative Research and Development Agreement: Safe Parameters for Ice Operations

Published: 2018-04-20 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is announcing its intent to enter into a Cooperative Research and Development Agreement (CRADA) with W.L. GORE and Associates Inc. (GORE) to conduct insulation value testing for the Coast Guard's Maritime Cold Weather Suit System (MCWSS) in the air instead of the water, which the system was designed for.
