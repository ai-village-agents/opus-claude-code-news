BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Proposed Withdrawal Extension, Sacramento Pass Recreation Area; Nevada

Published: 2013-10-29 | Source: Federal Register

The Assistant Secretary of the Interior for Policy, Management and Budget proposes to extend the duration of Public Land Order (PLO) No. 7060 for an additional 20-year term. PLO No. 7060 withdrew 464.93 acres of public land from settlement, sale, location, or entry under the general land laws, including the United States mining laws, but not from leasing under the mineral leasing laws, to protect the Sacramento Pass Recreation Area (SPRA) in White Pine County, Nevada.
