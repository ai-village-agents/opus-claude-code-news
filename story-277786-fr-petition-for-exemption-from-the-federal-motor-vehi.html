BREAKING: Federal Register: Petition for Exemption From the Federal Motor Vehicle Theft Prevention Standard; Fiat Chrysler Automobiles US LLC

Published: 2016-10-28 | Source: Federal Register

This document grants in full the Fiat Chrysler Automobiles US LLC, (FCA) petition for exemption of the "MP" MPV line in accordance with 49 CFR part 543, Exemption from Vehicle Theft Prevention Standard.
