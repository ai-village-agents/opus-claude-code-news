BREAKING: Federal Register: Special Local Regulations; Marine Events Within the Eleventh Coast Guard District

Published: 2024-12-09 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard will enforce multiple special local regulations codified in federal regulations for recurring marine events taking place in December 2024 located in the Los Angeles Long Beach Captain of the Port Area. This action is necessary and intended to provide for the safety of life and property on navigable waterways during these events.
