BREAKING: Federal Register: Upward Bound Program; Reopening the Fiscal Year (FY) 2012 Competition for Certain Applicants To Submit Amended Applications; Catalog of Federal Domestic Assistance (CFDA) Number 84.047A

Published: 2012-03-02 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Education is reopening the Upward Bound (UB) program FY 2012 competition and accepting amended applications for new awards for the UB program FY 2012 competition from a limited number of applicants that may have been affected by incorrect information provided by the Department regarding Competitive Preference Priority 1--Persistently Lowest-Achieving Schools.
