BREAKING: Federal Register: Draft Advisory Circular for the Type Certification of Powered-Lift

Published: 2024-08-09 | Source: Federal Register

On June 12, 2024, the FAA published in the Federal Register a notice of availability for draft Advisory Circular (AC) 21.17-04, "Type Certification of Powered-lift". The comment period for this document expires on August 12, 2024. By letter dated August 1, 2024, the General Aviation Manufacturers Association (GAMA) requested that the FAA extend the public-comment period deadline to September 12, 2024.
