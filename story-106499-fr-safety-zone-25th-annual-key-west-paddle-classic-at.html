BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone; 25th Annual Key West Paddle Classic, Atlantic Ocean, Key West, FL

Published: 2022-09-30 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is establishing a temporary safety zone on certain navigable waters of the Atlantic Ocean and adjoining waterways, surrounding Key West, Florida, during the 25th Annual Key West Paddle Classic event. The safety zone is necessary to ensure the safety of event participants and spectators.
