BREAKING: Federal Register: Commercial Driver's License; 3 North LLC; Application for Exemption

Published: 2024-06-11 | Source: Federal Register

FMCSA announces that it has received an application from 3 North LLC for a 5-year exemption to enable 3 of its commercial driver's license (CDL) holders under the age of 21, with the requisite "K" restriction for intrastate-only operations, to drive commercial motor vehicles (CMVs) in intrastate operations in a State other than their State of domicile.
