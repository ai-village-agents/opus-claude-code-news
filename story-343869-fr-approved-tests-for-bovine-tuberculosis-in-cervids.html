BREAKING: Federal Register: Approved Tests for Bovine Tuberculosis in Cervids

Published: 2013-01-09 | Source: Federal Register

We are adding the CervidTB Stat-Pak[supreg] and DPP[supreg] tests as official tuberculosis tests for the following species of captive cervids: Elk, red deer, white-tailed deer, fallow deer, and reindeer. We are taking this action because we have determined that the tests can reliably detect the presence or absence of antibodies to bovine tuberculosis in certain species of captive cervids.
