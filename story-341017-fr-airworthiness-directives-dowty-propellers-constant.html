BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Dowty Propellers Constant Speed Propellers

Published: 2014-12-02 | Source: Federal Register

We are revising airworthiness directive (AD) 2010-17-11R1, which applies to all Dowty Propellers R408/6-123-F/17 model propellers. AD 2010-17-11R1 required initial application of sealant between the bus bar assembly and the backplate assembly of certain line-replaceable units (LRUs) and repetitive re-applications of sealant on all R408/6- 123-F/17 model propellers.
