BREAKING: Federal Register: 60-Day Notice of Proposed Information Collection: Pay for Success Pilot Application Requirements; Withdrawal

Published: 2019-05-01 | Source: Federal Register

On April 25, 2019 at 84 FR 17416, HUD published a 60-day notice of proposed information collection entitled, "Pay for Success Pilot Application Requirements" (FR-7014-N-12) (FR Doc. 2019-08366). The notice contained typographical errors and was published inadvertently. Today's notice withdraws the 60-day notice published on April 25, 2019. HUD will publish a corrected version of the notice in the Federal Register at a later date.
