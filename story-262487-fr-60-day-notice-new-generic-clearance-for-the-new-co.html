BREAKING: Federal Register: 60-Day Notice: New Generic Clearance for the New Collection of Quantitative Feedback on Agency Service Delivery (Bureau of Prisons)

Published: 2016-05-03 | Source: Federal Register

The Bureau of Prisons (Department of Justice) as part of its continuing effort to reduce paperwork and respondent burden, invites the general public to take this opportunity to comment on the "Generic Clearance for the Collection of Quantitative Feedback on Agency Service Delivery " for approval under the Paperwork Reduction Act (PRA) (44 U.S.C. 3501 et seq.).
