BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Bombardier, Inc., Airplanes

Published: 2023-07-03 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA is superseding Airworthiness Directive (AD) 2020-07- 13, which applied to certain Bombardier, Inc., Model BD-100-1A10 airplanes. AD 2020-07-13 required revising the existing airplane flight manual (AFM) to provide the flightcrew with new warnings for "Autoflight" and "Engine Failure in Climb During ALTS CAP."
