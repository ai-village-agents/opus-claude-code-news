BREAKING: Federal Register: Raw Flexible Magnets From China and Taiwan; Institution of Five-Year Reviews

Published: 2019-01-02 | Source: Federal Register

The Commission hereby gives notice that it has instituted reviews pursuant to the Tariff Act of 1930 ("the Act"), as amended, to determine whether the countervailing duty order on raw flexible magnets from China and the revocation of the antidumping duty orders on raw flexible magnets from China and Taiwan would be likely to lead to continuation or recurrence of material injury.
