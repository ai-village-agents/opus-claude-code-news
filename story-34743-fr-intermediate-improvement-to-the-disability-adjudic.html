BREAKING: Federal Register: Intermediate Improvement to the Disability Adjudication Process, Including How We Consider Past Work

Published: 2024-04-18 | Source: Federal Register

We are finalizing our proposed regulation to revise the time period that we consider when determining whether an individual's past work is relevant for the purposes of making disability determinations and decisions. We are revising the definition of past relevant work (PRW) by reducing the relevant work period from 15 to 5 years.
