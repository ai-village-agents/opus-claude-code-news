BREAKING: Federal Register: Meeting of the Advisory Commission on Childhood Vaccines

Published: 2025-12-16 | Source: Federal Register

In accordance with the Federal Advisory Committee Act, this notice announces that the Advisory Commission on Childhood Vaccines (ACCV) will hold public meetings for calendar year (CY) 2025. Information about the ACCV, agendas, and materials for these meetings can be found on the ACCV website at https://www.hrsa.gov/advisory- committees/vaccines/index.html.
