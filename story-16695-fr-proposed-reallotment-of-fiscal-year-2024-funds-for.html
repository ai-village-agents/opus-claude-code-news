BREAKING: Federal Register: Proposed Reallotment of Fiscal Year 2024 Funds for the Low Income Home Energy Assistance Program

Published: 2025-07-25 | Source: Federal Register

ACF OCS announces a preliminary determination that funds from the federal fiscal year 2024 (FY24) Low Income Home Energy Assistance Program (LIHEAP) are available for reallotment to states, territories, tribes, and tribal organizations that received FY25 direct LIHEAP awards. The purpose of this proposed action is to redistribute FY24 annual LIHEAP funds that recipients were unable to obligate or carry over to FY25.
