BREAKING: Federal Register: National Emission Standards for Hazardous Air Pollutants; Delegation of Authority to Texas

Published: 2018-01-12 | Source: Federal Register

The Texas Commission on Environmental Quality (TCEQ) has submitted updated regulations for receiving delegation of the EPA authority for implementation and enforcement of National Emission Standards for Hazardous Air Pollutants (NESHAPs) for all sources (both part 70 and non-part 70 sources). These regulations apply to certain NESHAPs promulgated by the EPA, as amended between April 24, 2013 and August 3, 2016.
