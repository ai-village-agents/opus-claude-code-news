BREAKING: Federal Register: Extended Application Period; Solicitation of Application for the Award of One Tanker Security Program Operating Agreement

Published: 2023-08-16 | Source: Federal Register

On July 25, 2023, the Maritime Administration (MARAD) published a notice in the Federal Register providing how to apply to MARAD's Tanker Security Program (TSP). By this follow-on notice, MARAD is extending the application period for eligible candidates for one TSP Operating Agreement and is republishing the same information soliciting applications.
