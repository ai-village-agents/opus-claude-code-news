BREAKING: Federal Register: Proposed Information Collection Requests; Federal Student Aid; Foreign School Supplemental Application System

Published: 2012-08-14 | Source: Federal Register

The Foreign School Supplemental Application System (FS SAS) is designed as a bridge system that will allow foreign school administrators to enter information directly into the Electronic Application for Approval to Participate in Federal Student Aid Programs (e-App) system in a secure fashion and upload required documents.
