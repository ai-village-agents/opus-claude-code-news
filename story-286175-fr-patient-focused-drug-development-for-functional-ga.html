BREAKING: Federal Register: Patient-Focused Drug Development for Functional Gastrointestinal Disorders; Public Meeting; Request for Comments

Published: 2015-02-11 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA or Agency) is announcing a public meeting and an opportunity for public comment on Patient- Focused Drug Development for functional gastrointestinal (GI) disorders, including irritable bowel syndrome, gastroparesis, chronic persistent symptomatic gastroesophageal reflux despite standard therapeutic interventions, and chronic idiopathic constipation.
