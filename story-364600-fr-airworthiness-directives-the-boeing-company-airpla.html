BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; The Boeing Company Airplanes

Published: 2013-09-04 | Source: Federal Register

We are superseding airworthiness directive (AD) 90-23-14 for certain The Boeing Company Model 747 series airplanes. AD 90-23-14 required inspections of the fuselage skin lap splice between body station (BS) 340 and BS 400 at stringers (S)-6L and S-6R, and repair if necessary.
