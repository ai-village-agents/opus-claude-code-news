BREAKING: Federal Register: DOE Response to Defense Nuclear Facilities Safety Board Recommendation 2023-1, Onsite Transportation Safety

Published: 2024-05-24 | Source: Federal Register

On January 26, 2024, the Defense Nuclear Facilities Safety Board issued Recommendation 2023-1, Onsite Transportation Safety, to the Department of Energy (DOE). In accordance with the Atomic Energy Act of 1954, as amended, the Secretary of Energy's response to the Recommendation is provided in this notice.
