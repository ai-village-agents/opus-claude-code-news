BREAKING: Federal Register: Certain Mobile Device Holders and Components Thereof; Commission's Determination To Review In-Part a Final Initial Determination Finding a Violation of Section 337; Request for Written Submissions

Published: 2017-11-17 | Source: Federal Register

Notice is hereby given that the U.S. International Trade Commission has determined to review in-part the final initial determination ("ID") issued by the presiding administrative law judge ("ALJ") on September 12, 2017, finding a violation of section 337 in the above-captioned investigation. Specifically, the Commission has determined to review the ID's analysis and findings with respect to the economic prong of the domestic industry.
