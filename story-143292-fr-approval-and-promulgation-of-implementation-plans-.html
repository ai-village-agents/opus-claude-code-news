BREAKING: Federal Register: Approval and Promulgation of Implementation Plans; State of Montana; State Implementation Plan Revisions for Open Burning

Published: 2020-01-30 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is approving a State Implementation Plan (SIP) revision submitted by the State of Montana on May 24, 2018. The revisions remove a prohibition on the open burning of asbestos and asbestos-containing materials located in the SIP-approved Administrative Rules of Montana (ARM) Title 17, chapter 8, subchapter 6 and the similar provision in the SIP-approved Lincoln County Air Pollution Control Program.
