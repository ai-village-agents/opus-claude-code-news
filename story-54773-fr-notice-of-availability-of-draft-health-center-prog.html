BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Availability of Draft Health Center Program Scope Policy Manual Guidance

Published: 2024-12-09 | Source: Federal Register

HRSA requests public comments on the Draft Health Center Program Scope of Project Manual (draft Scope Policy Manual). The draft Scope Policy Manual provides updated policy guidance on what constitutes the Health Center Program scope of project under the Public Health Service Act (PHS Act).
