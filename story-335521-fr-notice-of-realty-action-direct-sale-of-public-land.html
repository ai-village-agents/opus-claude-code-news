BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Realty Action: Direct Sale of Public Land, Oklahoma County, OK

Published: 2014-09-24 | Source: Federal Register

The Bureau of Land Management (BLM) is offering to sell a parcel of public land totaling 2.78 acres as a non-competitive direct sale at not less than the appraised fair market value (FMV) of $175,000, to the City of Oklahoma City. The sale is pursuant to Section 203 of the Federal Land Policy and Management Act of 1976 (FLPMA), and BLM regulations.
