BREAKING: Federal Register: Proposed Transfer and Re-Use of Abandoned Pipelines

Published: 2020-04-20 | Source: Federal Register

The Bureau of Safety and Environmental Enforcement (BSEE) is considering whether to authorize the transfer of ownership and reuse of certain pipelines that were decommissioned-in-place in the Gulf of Mexico (GOM).
