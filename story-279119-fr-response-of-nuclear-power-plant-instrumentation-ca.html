BREAKING: Federal Register: Response of Nuclear Power Plant Instrumentation Cables When Exposed to Fire Conditions-Test Plan

Published: 2016-11-16 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Nuclear Regulatory Commission (NRC) is requesting public comment on the draft test plan entitled, "Response of Nuclear Power Plant Instrumentation Cables When Exposed to Fire Conditions-- Test Plan," in order to receive feedback from the widest range of interested parties and to ensure that all information relevant to developing this document is available to the NRC staff.
