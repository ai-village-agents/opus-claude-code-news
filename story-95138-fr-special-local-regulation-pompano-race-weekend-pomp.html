BREAKING: Federal Register: Special Local Regulation: Pompano Race Weekend, Pompano Beach, FL

Published: 2022-05-04 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is establishing a temporary special local regulation (SLR) on certain navigable waters of the Atlantic Ocean off Pompano Beach, FL, in connection with the Pompano Race Weekend event. The SLR is needed to protect personnel, vessels, and the marine environment from potential hazards associated with the high speed jet ski race.
