BREAKING: Federal Register: Defense Science Board; Notice of Advisory Committee Meetings

Published: 2012-01-23 | Source: Federal Register

The Defense Science Board will meet in closed session on February 22-23, 2012, at the Pentagon. The mission of the Defense Science Board is to advise the Secretary of Defense and the Under Secretary of Defense for Acquisition, Technology & Logistics on scientific and technical matters as they affect the perceived needs of the Department of Defense.
