BREAKING: Federal Register: Schedules for Atlantic Shark Identification Workshops and Safe Handling, Release, and Identification Workshops; Correction

Published: 2020-04-01 | Source: Federal Register

NMFS announces that the date for the Atlantic Shark Identification workshop originally scheduled for April 2, 2020, in Wilmington, NC has been changed to July 2, 2020. Also, NMFS is postponing the Safe Handling, Release, and Identification workshops originally scheduled for Houston, TX on March 17, 2020; Kitty Hawk, NC on April 1, 2020; and Revere, MA on April 3, 2020.
