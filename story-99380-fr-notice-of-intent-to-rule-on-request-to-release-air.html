BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Intent To Rule on Request To Release Airport Property at the Colorado Air and Space Port, Watkins, Colorado

Published: 2022-06-29 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA proposes to rule and invite public comment on the release of land at the Colorado Air and Space Port under the provisions of Section 125 of the Wendell H. Ford Aviation Investment Reform Act for the 21st Century (AIR 21).
