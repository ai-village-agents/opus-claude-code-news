BREAKING: Federal Register: Revocation and Amendment of Class E Airspace; Orange City and Le Mars, IA

Published: 2021-03-12 | Source: Federal Register

This action removes the Class E airspace extending upward from 700 feet above the surface at Orange City Municipal Airport, Orange City, IA and amends the Class E airspace extending upward from 700 feet above the surface at the Le Mars Municipal Airport, Le Mars, IA.
