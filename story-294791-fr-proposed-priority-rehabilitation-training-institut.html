BREAKING: Federal Register: Proposed Priority-Rehabilitation Training: Institute on Rehabilitation Issues

Published: 2015-05-26 | Source: Federal Register

The Assistant Secretary for Special Education and Rehabilitative Services proposes a priority to establish a topical Institute on Rehabilitation Issues (IRI). The Assistant Secretary may use this priority for competitions in fiscal year (FY) 2015 and later years.
