BREAKING: Federal Register: Western Pacific Pelagic Fisheries; U.S. Territorial Catch and Fishing Effort Limits

Published: 2014-01-08 | Source: Federal Register

NMFS seeks public comment on two proposed actions. First, NMFS proposes to establish a management framework for specifying catch and fishing effort limits and accountability measures for pelagic fisheries in the U.S. Pacific territories (American Samoa, Guam, and the Northern Mariana Islands).
