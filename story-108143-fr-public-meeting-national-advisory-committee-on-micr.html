BREAKING: Federal Register: Public Meeting; National Advisory Committee on Microbiological Criteria for Foods

Published: 2022-10-21 | Source: Federal Register

This notice is announcing that the National Advisory Committee on Microbiological Criteria for Foods (NACMCF) will hold a public meeting of the full Committee on November 15, 2022. The Committee will discuss and vote on adopting the following report: Enhancing Salmonella Control in Poultry Products.
