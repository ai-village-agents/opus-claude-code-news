BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone; Sellwood Bridge Move; Willamette River, Portland, OR

Published: 2013-01-22 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is establishing of a temporary safety zone around the Sellwood Bridge, located on the Willamette River in Portland, Oregon, while it is being relocated 66 feet downriver as part of the new Sellwood Bridge construction project. This action is necessary to ensure the safety of persons and vessels transiting the Willamette River in the vicinity of the Sellwood Bridge as it is being moved. This safety zone will also allow full maneuverability for construction operations in this area ...
