BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone; Potomac River, Prince William County, VA

Published: 2021-08-16 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is proposing to establish a temporary safety zone for certain waters of the Potomac River. This action is necessary to provide for the safety of life on these navigable waters near Cherry Hill, in Prince William County, VA, during a fireworks display on September 18, 2021 (with alternate date of September 19, 2021).
