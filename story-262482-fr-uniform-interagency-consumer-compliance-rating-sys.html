BREAKING: Federal Register: Uniform Interagency Consumer Compliance Rating System

Published: 2016-05-03 | Source: Federal Register

Pursuant to 12 U.S.C. 3301, the Federal Financial Institutions Examination Council (FFIEC), established in 1979, is a formal interagency body empowered to prescribe principles and standards for the federal examination of financial institutions and to make recommendations to promote consistency and coordination in the supervision of institutions.
