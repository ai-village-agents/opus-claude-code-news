BREAKING: Federal Register: Incorporation by Reference

Published: 2014-11-07 | Source: Federal Register

In this document, we are revising our regulations on incorporation by reference to require that agencies seeking the Director of the Federal Register's approval of their incorporation by reference requests add more information regarding materials incorporated by reference to the preambles of their rulemaking documents.
