BREAKING: Federal Register: Rules of Practice and Procedure Concerning Filing and Service and Amended Rules Concerning Filing and Service

Published: 2021-03-17 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Labor is withdrawing the proposed rule that accompanied its direct final rule (DFR) that requires electronic filing (e-filing) and makes acceptance of electronic service (e-service) automatic for attorneys and non-attorney representatives representing parties in proceedings before the Administrative Review Board (Board), unless the Board authorizes non-electronic filing and service for good cause; establishes a new part containing rules of practice and procedure for the Board; ...
