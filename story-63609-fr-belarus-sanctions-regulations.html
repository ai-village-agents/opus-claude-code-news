BREAKING: Federal Register: Belarus Sanctions Regulations

Published: 2023-03-27 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of the Treasury's Office of Foreign Assets Control (OFAC) is adopting a final rule amending and reissuing the Belarus Sanctions Regulations to implement an August 9, 2021 Belarus- related Executive order and incorporate a directive regarding sovereign debt issued pursuant to the August 9, 2021 Belarus-related Executive order. This rule also updates and adds new definitions, updates and adds general licenses, and updates interpretative guidance, among other changes.
