BREAKING: Federal Register: Environmental Impact Statement: Kenosha, Racine, Milwaukee, Waukesha, Washington, Dodge, Fond Du Lac, Winnebago, Outagamie and Brown Counties, Wisconsin

Published: 2013-06-21 | Source: Federal Register

The FHWA is issuing this revised notice to advise the public that FHWA and Wisconsin Department of Transportation (WisDOT) will not prepare a Tier 1 Environmental Impact Statement (EIS) for the proposed Interstate conversion of U.S. Highway 41 in Milwaukee, Waukesha, Washington, Dodge, Fond du Lac, Winnebago, Outagamie, and Brown Counties, Wisconsin. A Notice of Intent to prepare an EIS was published in the Federal Register on July 26, 2011.
