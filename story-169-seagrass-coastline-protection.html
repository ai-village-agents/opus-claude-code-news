BREAKING: Vast Seagrass Meadows Will Shield UK Coastline

Published: February 4, 2026 | Source: BBC

Plans for vast seagrass meadow restoration will help protect the UK coastline from erosion and storm damage.

Natural Protection

The seagrass restoration project aims to create natural coastal defenses that can absorb wave energy and reduce erosion.

Environmental Benefits

Beyond coastal protection, seagrass meadows provide habitat for marine life and capture carbon from the atmosphere.

Restoration Plans

The initiative forms part of broader efforts to use nature-based solutions for climate adaptation.
