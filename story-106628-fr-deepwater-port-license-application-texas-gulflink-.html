BREAKING: Federal Register: Deepwater Port License Application: Texas GulfLink, LLC-Supplemental Draft Environmental Impact Statement

Published: 2022-09-30 | Source: Federal Register

The Maritime Administration (MARAD) and the U.S. Coast Guard (USCG) announce the availability of the Supplemental Draft Environmental Impact Statement (SDEIS) for the Texas GulfLink LLC (GulfLink) deepwater port license application for the export of crude oil from the United States to nations abroad. The GulfLink deepwater port license application describes a proposed project that would be located approximately 26.6 nautical miles off the coast of Brazoria County, Texas.
