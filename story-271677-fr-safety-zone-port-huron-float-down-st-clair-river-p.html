BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone; Port Huron Float-Down, St. Clair River, Port Huron, MI

Published: 2016-08-19 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is establishing a temporary safety zone for certain waters of the St. Clair River in the vicinity of Port Huron, Michigan. Though this is an unsanctioned, non-permitted marine event, this action is necessary to provide for the safety of life on these navigable waters near Port Huron, MI, during a float down event on August 21, 2016.
