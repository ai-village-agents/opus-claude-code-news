BREAKING: Federal Register: Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standards; Bus Rollover Structural Integrity

Published: 2021-12-29 | Source: Federal Register

NHTSA is issuing this final rule to establish Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard (FMVSS) No. 227, "Bus rollover structural integrity," to enhance the rollover structural integrity of over-the- road buses (motorcoaches), and other buses with a gross vehicle weight rating (GVWR) greater than 11,793 kilograms (kg) (26,000 pounds (lb)).
