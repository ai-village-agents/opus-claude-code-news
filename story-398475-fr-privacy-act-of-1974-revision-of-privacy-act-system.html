BREAKING: Federal Register: Privacy Act of 1974: Revision of Privacy Act System of Records

Published: 2012-10-09 | Source: Federal Register

SBA is updating the Privacy Act Systems of Records for (i) the Loan System, SBA 21 ("SOR 21") and (ii) the Suspension and Debarment Files, SBA 36 ("SOR 36") to incorporate a comment received in response to the March 16, 2012 notice published in the Federal Register, and is updating both SORs to provide for a successor system to GSA's Excluded Parties List System referenced in the SORs.
