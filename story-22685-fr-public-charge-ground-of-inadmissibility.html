BREAKING: Federal Register: Public Charge Ground of Inadmissibility

Published: 2025-11-19 | Source: Federal Register

DHS proposes to rescind the 2022 public charge ground of inadmissibility regulations. The 2022 regulations are not the best implementation of the statute, inconsistent with congressional intent, unduly restrictive, and hamper DHS's ability to make accurate, precise, and reliable determinations of whether certain aliens are likely at any time to become a public charge.
