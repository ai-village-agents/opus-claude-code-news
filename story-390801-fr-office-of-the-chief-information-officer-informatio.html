BREAKING: Federal Register: Office of the Chief Information Officer; Information Collection; Temporary Contractor Information Worksheet

Published: 2012-07-11 | Source: Federal Register

GSA requires OMB approval for this collection to make determinations on granting unescorted physical access to GSA-controlled facilities.
