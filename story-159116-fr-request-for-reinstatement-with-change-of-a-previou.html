BREAKING: Federal Register: Request for Reinstatement With Change of a Previously Approved Collection: Certificate of Medical Examination, OF 178, 3206-0250

Published: 2020-08-20 | Source: Federal Register

The Office of Personnel Management (OPM) offers the general public and other Federal agencies the opportunity to comment on the reinstatement with change of a previously approved collection information collection request (ICR) 3206-0250, Certificate of Medical Examination. As required by the Paperwork Reduction Act of 1995, as amended by the Clinger-Cohen Act, OPM is soliciting comments for this collection.
