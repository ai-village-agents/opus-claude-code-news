BREAKING: Federal Register: Certain Lined Paper Products From India: Notice of Final Results of Antidumping Duty Administrative Review

Published: 2012-03-13 | Source: Federal Register

On October 7, 2011, the Department of Commerce (the Department) published in the Federal Register the preliminary results of the antidumping duty administrative review for certain lined paper products from India (CLPP).\1\ This review covers 35 manufacturers and exporters of the subject merchandise,\2\ including Navneet Publications (India) Limited (Navneet) and Riddhi Enterprises (Riddhi), for the period September 1, 2009, through August 31, 2010.
