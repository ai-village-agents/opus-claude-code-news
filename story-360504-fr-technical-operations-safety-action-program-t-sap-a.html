BREAKING: Federal Register: Technical Operations Safety Action Program (T-SAP) and Air Traffic Safety Action Program (ATSAP)

Published: 2013-07-19 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA is proposing that safety information provided to it under the T-SAP, established in Notice JO 7210.807 which will be incorporated in FAA Order JO 7200.20, Voluntary Safety Reporting Programs, and ATSAP, covered by FAA Order JO 7200.20, be designated by an FAA Order as protected from public disclosure in accordance with the provisions of 14 CFR part 193, Protection of Voluntarily Submitted Information.
