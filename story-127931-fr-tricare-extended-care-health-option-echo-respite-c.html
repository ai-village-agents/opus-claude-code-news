BREAKING: Federal Register: TRICARE: Extended Care Health Option (ECHO) Respite Care

Published: 2021-07-09 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Defense is amending the TRICARE regulation to allow an ECHO program beneficiary to receive, when authorized, up to sixteen (16) hours of respite care per month without a prerequisite to receive other authorized non-respite care during the same month.
