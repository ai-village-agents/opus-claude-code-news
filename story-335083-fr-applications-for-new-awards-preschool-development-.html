BREAKING: Federal Register: Applications for New Awards; Preschool Development Grants-Expansion Grants; Correction

Published: 2014-09-18 | Source: Federal Register

On August 18, 2014, the Departments of Education and Health and Human Services published in the Federal Register (79 FR 48874) a notice inviting applications for new awards for fiscal year 2014 for the Preschool Development Grants--Expansion Grants program. This notice corrects the Executive Summary Selection Criterion (A)(7)(b).
