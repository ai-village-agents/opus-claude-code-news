BREAKING: Federal Register: Air Plan Approval; Oklahoma; Revisions to Air Pollution Control Rules

Published: 2023-12-28 | Source: Federal Register

Pursuant to the Federal Clean Air Act (CAA or the Act), the Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is approving revisions to the State Implementation Plan (SIP) for Oklahoma, submitted to the EPA by the State of Oklahoma designee ("the State") on January 30, 2023.
