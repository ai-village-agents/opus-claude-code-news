BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Vacancies on the United States-Mexico Energy Business Council

Published: 2019-06-17 | Source: Federal Register

In 2016, the Governments of the United States and Mexico established the U.S.-Mexico Energy Business Council (the "Council"). This notice announces three membership opportunities for appointment as U.S. representatives to the U.S. Section of the Council for a term ending in June 2020.
