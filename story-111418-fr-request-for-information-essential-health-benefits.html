BREAKING: Federal Register: Request for Information; Essential Health Benefits

Published: 2022-12-02 | Source: Federal Register

This request for information (RFI) solicits public comment on issues related to the Essential Health Benefits (EHB) under the Patient Protection and Affordable Care Act (the Affordable Care Act or ACA). CMS is issuing this RFI to gather input from the public regarding a variety of topics related to the coverage of benefits in health plans subject to the EHB requirements of the ACA.
