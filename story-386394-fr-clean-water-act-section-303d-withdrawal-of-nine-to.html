BREAKING: Federal Register: Clean Water Act Section 303(d): Withdrawal of Nine Total Maximum Daily Loads (TMDLs)

Published: 2012-05-22 | Source: Federal Register

The EPA hereby withdraws nine final Total Maximum Daily Loads (TMDLs) for Chloride, Sulfate, and Total Dissolved Solids (TDS) for the Bayou de L'Outre Watershed in Arkansas. The EPA withdraws the Bayou de L'Outre TMDLs due to the discovery of inconsistencies in the values used to derive the flow and load duration curves, resulting in the calculation of TMDLs which do not accurately reflect the loading capacity of the segments.
