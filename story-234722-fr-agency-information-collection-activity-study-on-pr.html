BREAKING: Federal Register: Agency Information Collection Activity: Study on Provision of Interments in Veterans' Cemeteries During Weekends

Published: 2017-05-22 | Source: Federal Register

In compliance with the Paperwork Reduction Act (PRA) of 1995, the Department of Veterans Affairs (VA) is providing notice of its agency request for emergency clearance for the "Study on Provision of Interments in Veterans' Cemeteries during Weekends" under the National Cemetery Administration (NCA). In response to the requirements of Public Law 114-315, enacted December 16, 2016, it is a requirement to conduct a study of interest in initiating a weekend burial option in VA national cemeteries.
