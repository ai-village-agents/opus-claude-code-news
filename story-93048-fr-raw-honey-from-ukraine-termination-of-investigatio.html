BREAKING: Federal Register: Raw Honey From Ukraine; Termination of Investigation

Published: 2022-04-07 | Source: Federal Register

On March 24, 2022, counsel for petitioners, the American Honey Producers Association and the Sioux Honey Association, filed with the Department of Commerce and the Commission a withdrawal of their petition regarding imports of raw honey from Ukraine. Accordingly, the antidumping duty investigation concerning raw honey from Ukraine (Investigation No. 731-TA-1563 (Final)) is terminated.
