BREAKING: Federal Register: Pipeline Safety: Notice of Public Meeting on Pipeline Data

Published: 2012-10-11 | Source: Federal Register

PHMSA and the National Association of Pipeline Safety Representatives (NAPSR) are sponsoring this public meeting to discuss how pipeline data is currently used by stakeholders and identify potential improvements in pipeline safety performance measures. PHMSA pipeline safety regulations require integrity management program performance measures for gas distribution, gas transmission, and hazardous liquids pipelines.
