BREAKING: Federal Register: Public Notice for Waiver of Aeronautical Land-Use Assurance; Willow Run Airport; Detroit, MI

Published: 2012-01-18 | Source: Federal Register

The Federal Aviation Administration (FAA) is considering a proposal to authorize the release of approximately 29.5 acres of airport property for sale. The land consists of portions of the original airport parcels. These parcels were acquired by the Wayne County Road Commissioners of Wayne County, Michigan, its successors and assigns (Wayne County Airport Authority) from the U.S. Government, General Services Administration without federal participation.
