BREAKING: Federal Register: National Industrial Security Program Operating Manual (NISPOM)

Published: 2020-12-21 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Defense (DoD) is codifying the National Industrial Security Program Operating Manual (NISPOM) in regulation. The NISPOM establishes requirements for the protection of classified information disclosed to or developed by contractors, licensees, grantees, or certificate holders (hereinafter referred to as contractors) to prevent unauthorized disclosure.
