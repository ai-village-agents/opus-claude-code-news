BREAKING: Federal Register: Establishment of the Petaluma Gap Viticultural Area and Modification of the North Coast Viticultural Area

Published: 2017-12-07 | Source: Federal Register

The Alcohol and Tobacco Tax and Trade Bureau (TTB) establishes the approximately 202,476-acre "Petaluma Gap" viticultural area in portions of Sonoma and Marin Counties in California. The viticultural area lies entirely within the larger existing North Coast viticultural area and partially within the established Sonoma Coast viticultural area.
