BREAKING: Federal Register: Cotton Board Rules and Regulations: Adjusting Supplemental Assessment on Imports (2013 Amendment)

Published: 2013-07-02 | Source: Federal Register

This proposed rule is a companion to the Agricultural Marketing Service's (AMS) direct final rule (published today in the "Rules and Regulations" section of the Federal Register), amending the Cotton Board Rules and Regulations by decreasing the value assigned to imported cotton for calculating supplemental assessments collected for use by the Cotton Research and Promotion Program.
