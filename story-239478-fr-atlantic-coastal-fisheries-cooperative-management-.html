BREAKING: Federal Register: Atlantic Coastal Fisheries Cooperative Management Act Provisions; Summer Flounder Fishery

Published: 2017-07-20 | Source: Federal Register

This notice announces the Secretary of Commerce's compliance decision under the Atlantic Coastal Fisheries Cooperative Management Act regarding New Jersey recreational management of summer flounder. This notice is necessary to alert the public that the Secreatary of Commerce has made a final determination and will not implement a Federal moratorium on fishing, possession, and landing of summer flounder in New Jersey's state waters.
