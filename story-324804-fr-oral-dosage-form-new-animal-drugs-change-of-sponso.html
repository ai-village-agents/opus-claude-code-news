BREAKING: Federal Register: Oral Dosage Form New Animal Drugs; Change of Sponsor

Published: 2014-05-20 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA) is amending the animal drug regulations to reflect a change of sponsor for 172 approved new animal drug applications (NADAs) and 14 approved abbreviated new animal drug applications (ANADAs) for oral dosage form new animal drug products from Pfizer, Inc., including its several subsidiaries and divisions, to Zoetis, Inc.
