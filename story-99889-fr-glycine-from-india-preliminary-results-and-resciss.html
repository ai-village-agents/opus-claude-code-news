BREAKING: Federal Register: Glycine From India: Preliminary Results and Rescission, in Part, of Countervailing Duty Administrative Review; 2020

Published: 2022-07-07 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Department of Commerce (Commerce) is conducting an administrative review of the countervailing duty (CVD) order on glycine from India for the period of review (POR) January 1, 2020, through December 31, 2020. Commerce preliminarily determines that countervailable subsidies are being provided to producers and exporters of glycine from India.
