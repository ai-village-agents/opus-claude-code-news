BREAKING: Federal Register: Pacific Fishery Management Council; Public Meeting

Published: 2019-11-08 | Source: Federal Register

Participants in the Pacific Fishery Management Council's (Pacific Council's) 2019 groundfish stock assessment process will hold a meeting via webinar to review and evaluate the 2019 stock assessment review (STAR) process. The goal of the webinar is to solicit process improvements to recommend for future groundfish stock assessments and STAR panel reviews.
