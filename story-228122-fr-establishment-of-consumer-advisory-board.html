BREAKING: Federal Register: Establishment of Consumer Advisory Board

Published: 2017-02-23 | Source: Federal Register

The Consumer Financial Protection Bureau ("Bureau"), after consultation with the Committee Management Secretariat of the General Services Administration, has established the Consumer Advisory Board ("the Board") as required by statute.
