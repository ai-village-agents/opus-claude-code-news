BREAKING: Federal Register: Housing Choice Voucher Program-New Administrative Fee Formula

Published: 2016-07-06 | Source: Federal Register

This rule proposes a new methodology for determining the amount of funding a public housing agency (PHA) will receive for administering the Housing Choice Voucher (HCV) program--one that uses factors that a recently completed study demonstrates are more reflective of how much it costs to administer the HCV program.
