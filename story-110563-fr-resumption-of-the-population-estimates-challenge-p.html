BREAKING: Federal Register: Resumption of the Population Estimates Challenge Program and Proposed Changes to the Program

Published: 2022-11-22 | Source: Federal Register

The Bureau of the Census (Census Bureau) is proposing to amend the regulations for the Population Estimates Challenge Program which provides eligible general-purpose governmental entities (units) with the opportunity to file requests for the review of their population estimates for 2021 and subsequent years in forthcoming estimates series, beginning with the Vintage 2022 series that is scheduled to be published in 2023.
