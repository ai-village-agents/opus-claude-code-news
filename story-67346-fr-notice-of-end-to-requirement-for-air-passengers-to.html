BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of End to Requirement for Air Passengers To Provide Proof of COVID-19 Vaccination Before Boarding a Flight to the United States

Published: 2023-05-12 | Source: Federal Register

The Centers for Disease Control and Prevention (CDC), within the Department of Health and Human Services (HHS), announces that CDC's Amended Order: Implementing Presidential Proclamation on Safe Resumption of Global Travel During the COVID-19 Pandemic no longer will be in effect beginning at 12:01 a.m. eastern daylight time on May 12, 2023.
