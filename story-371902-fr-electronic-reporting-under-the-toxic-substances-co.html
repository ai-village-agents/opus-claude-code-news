BREAKING: Federal Register: Electronic Reporting Under the Toxic Substances Control Act

Published: 2013-12-04 | Source: Federal Register

EPA is amending certain reporting requirements that were promulgated under the Toxic Substances Control Act (TSCA) to require the use electronic reporting. EPA is requiring the use of electronic reporting in order to minimize the paperwork burden associated with the underlying regulatory requirements and to minimize the cost to the Federal Government of the creation, collection, maintenance, use, dissemination, and disposition of information.
