BREAKING: Federal Register: Electronic Delivery of MVPD Communications; Modernization of Media Regulation Initiative

Published: 2018-12-26 | Source: Federal Register

In this document, the Federal Communications Commission (FCC or Commission) provides that certain written notices from MVPDs to subscribers may be provided electronically via verified email, so long as the MVPD complies with certain consumer safeguards. In addition, we authorize cable operators to respond to consumer requests and complaints via email in certain circumstances, and eliminate a portion of our rules because they are outdated.
