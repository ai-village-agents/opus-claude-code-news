BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Realty Action: Direct Sale of Public Land in Doña Ana County, NM

Published: 2013-08-30 | Source: Federal Register

The Bureau of Land Management (BLM), Las Cruces District Office, proposes to sell two parcels of public land totaling 2.47 acres in Do[ntilde]a Ana County, New Mexico. The public land would be sold to the Union Pacific Railroad for $11,000 which is more than the appraised fair market value.
