BREAKING: Federal Register: Meeting of the Defense Advisory Committee on Women in the Services (DACOWITS)

Published: 2012-11-20 | Source: Federal Register

Pursuant to Section 10(a), Public Law 92-463, as amended, notice is hereby given of a forthcoming meeting of the Defense Advisory Committee on Women in the Services (DACOWITS). The meeting is open to the public, subject to the availability of space.
