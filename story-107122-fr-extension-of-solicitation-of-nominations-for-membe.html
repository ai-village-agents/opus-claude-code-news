BREAKING: Federal Register: Extension of Solicitation of Nominations for Membership to the Organization Designation Authorizations for Transport Airplanes Expert Review Panel

Published: 2022-10-07 | Source: Federal Register

On September 9, 2022, the FAA published in the Federal Register a notice to solicit nominations for membership on the ODA for Transport Airplanes Expert Review Panel ("Review Panel"). This notice extends the solicitation period for membership on the Review Panel to provide additional opportunities for interested persons to submit their nomination applications.
