BREAKING: Federal Register: Implementation of OMB Guidance on Drug-Free Workplace Requirements

Published: 2016-01-22 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Department of Commerce (Commerce) is removing its regulation implementing the prior government-wide common rule on drug- free workplace requirements for financial assistance, and issuing a new regulation to adopt updated Office of Management and Budget (OMB) guidance on the topic.
