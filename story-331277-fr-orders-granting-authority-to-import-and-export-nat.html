BREAKING: Federal Register: Orders Granting Authority To Import and Export Natural Gas, To Import and Export Liquefied Natural Gas and Vacating Prior Authority

Published: 2014-08-04 | Source: Federal Register

The Office of Fossil Energy (FE of the Department of Energy gives notice that during May 2014, it issued orders granting authority to import and export natural gas, to import and export liquefied natural gas and vacating prior authority. These orders are summarized in the attached appendix and may be found on the FE Web site at http:// www.fossil.energy.gov/programs/gasregulation/authorizations/Orders- 2014.html.
