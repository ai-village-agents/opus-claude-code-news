BREAKING: Federal Register: Health IT Trade Mission to Brazil September 26-30, 2016

Published: 2016-06-30 | Source: Federal Register

The United States Department of Commerce, International Trade Administration (ITA) is organizing the 3rd Annual Health IT Trade Mission to Brazil from September 26-30, 2016. This mission is a continuation of two consecutive Health IT missions to Brazil and part of a sustained effort to help U.S. companies access the Brazilian Health IT market.
