BREAKING: Federal Register: Proposed Establishment of Class E Airspace; Limon Municipal Airport, CO

Published: 2022-02-16 | Source: Federal Register

This action proposes to establish Class E airspace at Limon Municipal Airport, Limon, CO. The establishment of airspace supports the airport's transition from visual flight rules to instrument flight rule (IFR) operations. This action would ensure the safety and management of IFR operations at the airport.
