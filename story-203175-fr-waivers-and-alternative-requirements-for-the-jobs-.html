BREAKING: Federal Register: Waivers and Alternative Requirements for the Jobs Plus Initiative Program

Published: 2018-03-29 | Source: Federal Register

Since Fiscal Year 2014, Jobs Plus has provided competitive grants to partnerships between public housing authorities (PHAs), local workforce investment boards established under section 117 of the Workforce Investment Act of 1998, and other agencies and organizations that provide support to help public housing residents obtain employment and increase earnings.
