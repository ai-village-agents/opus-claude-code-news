BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Eurocopter Deutschland GmbH (ECD) Helicopters

Published: 2013-06-20 | Source: Federal Register

We propose to adopt a new airworthiness directive (AD) for ECD Model BO105C (C-2 and CB-2 Variants) and BO105S (CS-2 and CBS-2 Variants) helicopters with a certain third stage turbine wheel installed. This proposed AD would require installing a placard on the instrument panel and revising the limitations section of the rotorcraft flight manual (RFM).
