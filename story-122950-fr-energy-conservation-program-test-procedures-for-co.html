BREAKING: Federal Register: Energy Conservation Program: Test Procedures for Consumer Products; Early Assessment Review: Ceiling Fan Light Kits

Published: 2021-05-04 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Department of Energy ("DOE") is undertaking an early assessment review to determine whether amendments are warranted for the test procedure for ceiling fan light kits ("CFLKs"). DOE has identified certain issues associated with the currently applicable test procedure on which DOE is interested in receiving comment.
