BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Airbus Airplanes

Published: 2012-11-19 | Source: Federal Register

We propose to adopt a new airworthiness directive (AD) for certain Airbus Model A310-204, -222, -304, -322, and -324 airplanes. This proposed AD was prompted by the manufacturer re-classifying slat extension eccentric bolts as principle structural elements (PSE) with replacement due at or before newly calculated fatigue life limits.
