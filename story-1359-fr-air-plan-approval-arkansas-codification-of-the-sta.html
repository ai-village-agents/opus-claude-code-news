BREAKING: Federal Register: Air Plan Approval; Arkansas; Codification of the State Implementation Plan

Published: 2026-01-26 | Source: Federal Register

Pursuant to the Federal Clean Air Act (CAA or the Act), the Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is approving revisions to the Arkansas State Implementation Plan (SIP) submitted by the Arkansas Department of Energy and Environment (ADEE) to the EPA on February 4, 2025. The revisions address the codification of the Arkansas Pollution Control and Ecology Commission (APC & EC) Rule 19 and Regulations 9, 26, and 31 into the Code of Arkansas Rules (CAR).
