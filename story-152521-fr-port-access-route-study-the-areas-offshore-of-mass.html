BREAKING: Federal Register: Port Access Route Study: The Areas Offshore of Massachusetts and Rhode Island

Published: 2020-05-27 | Source: Federal Register

The United States Coast Guard (USCG) announces the completion of The Areas Offshore of Massachusetts and Rhode Island Port Access Route Study. The study focused on the seven adjacent leased areas of the outer continental shelf south of Martha's Vineyard, Massachusetts, and east of Rhode Island that together constitute the Massachusetts/ Rhode Island Wind Energy Area (MA/RI WEA).
