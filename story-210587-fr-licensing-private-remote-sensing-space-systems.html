BREAKING: Federal Register: Licensing Private Remote Sensing Space Systems

Published: 2018-06-29 | Source: Federal Register

Commerce is considering revisions to its regulations for the licensing of private remote sensing space systems, currently administered by NOAA. These revisions would facilitate the continued growth of this critical industry and update the regulatory regime to address significant technological developments, new business models, and increased foreign competition since their last update in 2006.
