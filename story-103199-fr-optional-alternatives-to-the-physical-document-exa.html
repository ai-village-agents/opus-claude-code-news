BREAKING: Federal Register: Optional Alternatives to the Physical Document Examination Associated With Employment Eligibility Verification (Form I-9)

Published: 2022-08-18 | Source: Federal Register

DHS is proposing to allow for alternative procedures for documents required by the Form I-9, Employment Eligibility Verification. This proposed rule would create a framework under which the Secretary of Homeland Security (the Secretary) could authorize alternative options for document examination procedures with respect to some or all employers.
