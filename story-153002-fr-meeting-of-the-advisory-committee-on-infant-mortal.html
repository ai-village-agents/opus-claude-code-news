BREAKING: Federal Register: Meeting of the Advisory Committee on Infant Mortality

Published: 2020-06-02 | Source: Federal Register

This notice announces a correction to the time of the Secretary's Advisory Committee on Infant Mortality (ACIM) scheduled public meeting. The ACIM meeting, originally scheduled for June 17, 2020, 11:00 a.m.-6:00 p.m. Eastern Time (ET) and June 18, 2020, 11:00 a.m.-3:00 p.m. ET, has been extended by 30 minutes on June 18, 2020.
