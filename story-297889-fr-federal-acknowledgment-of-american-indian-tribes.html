BREAKING: Federal Register: Federal Acknowledgment of American Indian Tribes

Published: 2015-07-01 | Source: Federal Register

This rule revises regulations governing the process and criteria by which the Secretary acknowledges an Indian tribe. The revisions seek to make the process and criteria more transparent, promote consistent implementation, and increase timeliness and efficiency, while maintaining the integrity and substantive rigor of the process.
