BREAKING: Federal Register: Double Hull Tanker Escorts on the Waters of Prince William Sound, Alaska

Published: 2013-08-19 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is amending the escort requirements for certain tankers operating on the waters of Prince William Sound, Alaska (PWS). This interim rule is necessary to implement section 711 of the Coast Guard Authorization Act of 2010 (Act), which mandates two tug escorts for double hull tankers over 5,000 gross tons transporting oil in bulk in PWS.
