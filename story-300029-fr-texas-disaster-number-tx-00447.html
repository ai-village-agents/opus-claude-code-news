BREAKING: Federal Register: Texas Disaster Number TX-00447

Published: 2015-07-28 | Source: Federal Register

This is an amendment of the Presidential declaration of a major disaster for the State of Texas (FEMA-4223-DR), dated 05/29/2015. Incident: Severe storms, tornadoes, straight-line winds and flooding. Incident Period: 05/04/2015 through 06/22/2015. Effective Date: 07/21/2015. Physical Loan Application Deadline Date: 08/27/2015. EIDL Loan Application Deadline Date: 02/29/2016.
