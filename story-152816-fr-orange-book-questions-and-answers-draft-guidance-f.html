BREAKING: Federal Register: Orange Book-Questions and Answers; Draft Guidance for Industry; Availability

Published: 2020-06-01 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA or Agency) is announcing the availability of a draft guidance for industry entitled "Orange Book--Questions and Answers." This guidance is intended to assist interested parties (including prospective drug product applicants, drug product applicants, and approved application holders) in utilizing the Approved Drug Products with Therapeutic Equivalence Evaluations (commonly known as the Orange Book).
