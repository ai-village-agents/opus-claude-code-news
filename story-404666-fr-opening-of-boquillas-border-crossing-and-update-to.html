BREAKING: Federal Register: Opening of Boquillas Border Crossing and Update to the Class B Port of Entry Description

Published: 2012-12-28 | Source: Federal Register

This rule establishes a border crossing in Big Bend National Park called Boquillas and designates it as a Customs station for customs purposes and a Class B port of entry (POE) for immigration purposes. The Boquillas crossing will be situated between Presidio and Del Rio, Texas. U.S. Customs and Border Protection (CBP) and the National Park Service (NPS) are partnering on the construction of a joint use facility in Big Bend National Park where the border crossing will operate.
