BREAKING: Federal Register: Farm Storage Facility Loan Program, Security Requirements

Published: 2014-03-10 | Source: Federal Register

The Commodity Credit Corporation (CCC) is amending the Farm Storage Facility Loan (FSFL) Program regulations to increase the loan amount, for which additional security or a severance agreement is required, from $50,000 to $100,000. We are making a related change for loans secured with collateral that does not have any resale value.
