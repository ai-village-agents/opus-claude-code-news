BREAKING: Federal Register: Takes of Marine Mammals Incidental to Specified Activities; Seabird Research Activities in Central California, 2015-2016

Published: 2015-10-13 | Source: Federal Register

We, NMFS, have received a request from Point Blue Conservation Science (Point Blue) to revise an issued Incidental Harassment Authorization (Authorization) to take marine mammals, by harassment, incidental to conducting seabird research activities on Southeast Farallon Island, A[ntilde]o Nuevo Island, and Point Reyes National Seashore in central California.
