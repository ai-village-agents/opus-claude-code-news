BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Schempp-Hirth Flugzeugbau GmbH Gliders

Published: 2023-06-09 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA proposes to adopt a new airworthiness directive (AD) for Schempp-Hirth Flugzeugbau GmbH (Schempp-Hirth) Model Ventus-2a and Ventus-2b gliders. This proposed AD results from mandatory continuing airworthiness information (MCAI) originated by an aviation authority of another country to identify and correct an unsafe condition on an aviation product.
