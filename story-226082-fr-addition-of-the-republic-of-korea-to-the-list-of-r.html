BREAKING: Federal Register: Addition of the Republic of Korea to the List of Regions Affected by Contagious Equine Metritis

Published: 2017-01-23 | Source: Federal Register

We are advising the public that we have added the Republic of Korea to the Animal and Plant Health Inspection Service (APHIS) list maintained on the APHIS Web site of regions considered affected with contagious equine metritis (CEM). We took this action because of the confirmation of CEM in the Republic of Korea.
