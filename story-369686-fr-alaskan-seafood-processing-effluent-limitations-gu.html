BREAKING: Federal Register: Alaskan Seafood Processing Effluent Limitations Guidelines

Published: 2013-11-07 | Source: Federal Register

This notice makes available for public review and comment additional data and information gathered recently by the Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) from seafood processing facilities in Alaska and other publicly available sources. These data relate to the applicability of and discharge requirements for the Alaskan seafood subcategories of the Canned and Preserved Seafood Processing effluent limitations guidelines.
