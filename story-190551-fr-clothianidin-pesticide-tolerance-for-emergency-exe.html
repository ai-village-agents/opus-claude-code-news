BREAKING: Federal Register: Clothianidin; Pesticide Tolerance for Emergency Exemptions

Published: 2019-10-07 | Source: Federal Register

This regulation establishes a time-limited tolerance for residues of clothianidin in or on rice, grain. This action is in response to EPA's granting of an emergency exemption under the Federal Insecticide, Fungicide, and Rodenticide Act (FIFRA) authorizing use of thiamethoxam on rice.
