BREAKING: Federal Register: Issuance of a Presidential Permit Authorizing the State of Michigan To Construct, Connect, Operate, and Maintain at the Border of the United States a Bridge Linking Detroit, Michigan, and Windsor, Ontario

Published: 2013-05-01 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of State published a document in the Federal Register of April 18, 2013 concerning the issuance of a Presidential Permit. The summary section incorrectly stated that the permit issued on April 11, 2013 when, in fact, the permit issued on April 12, 2013.
