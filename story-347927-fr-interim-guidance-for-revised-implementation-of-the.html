BREAKING: Federal Register: Interim Guidance for Revised Implementation of the International Convention for the Prevention of Pollution From Ships (MARPOL), Annex V

Published: 2013-02-26 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard announces the availability of CG-CVC Policy Letter 13-01, "Interim Guidance for Revised MARPOL Annex V Implementation." On July 15, 2011, the International Maritime Organization's (IMO) Marine Environment Protection Committee (MEPC) formally adopted Resolution MEPC.201(62), which amends the International Convention for the Prevention of Pollution from Ships (MARPOL) Annex V, by establishing a general prohibition on discharges of garbage into the sea.
