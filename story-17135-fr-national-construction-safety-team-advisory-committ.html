BREAKING: Federal Register: National Construction Safety Team Advisory Committee Meeting

Published: 2025-07-31 | Source: Federal Register

The National Construction Safety Team (NCST) Advisory Committee (Committee) will hold an open virtual meeting via web conference on Tuesday, September 9, 2025, and Tuesday, September 16, 2025, from 10:00 a.m. to 4:00 p.m. Eastern Time. The primary purposes of this meeting are to update the Committee on the progress of the NCST investigation focused on the impacts of Hurricane Maria in Puerto Rico, progress of the NCST investigation focused on the Champlain Towers South partial building collapse ...
