BREAKING: Federal Register: Privacy Act of 1974; System of Records

Published: 2024-05-09 | Source: Federal Register

GSA proposes to modify a system of records subject to the Privacy Act of 1974, as amended. The system of records was established to collect and maintain records needed by the Office of Inspections to carry out its responsibilities pursuant to the Inspector General Act of 1978, as amended.
