BREAKING: Federal Register: Streamlining and Implementation of Economic Growth, Regulatory Relief, and Consumer Protection Act Changes to Family Self-Sufficiency (FSS) Program

Published: 2020-09-21 | Source: Federal Register

The Economic Growth, Regulatory Relief, and Consumer Protection Act ("the Economic Growth Act") was signed into law on May 24, 2018, amending HUD's Family Self-Sufficiency (FSS) program.
