BREAKING: Federal Register: Antimicrobial Susceptibility Test System Devices-Updating Breakpoints in Device Labeling; Guidance for Industry; Availability

Published: 2023-09-29 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA or we) is announcing the availability of a final guidance for industry and FDA staff entitled "Antimicrobial Susceptibility Test (AST) System Devices--Updating Breakpoints in Device Labeling." This guidance is intended to provide industry and FDA staff with information regarding updating susceptibility test interpretative criteria (STIC)/breakpoints and associated performance data in device labeling for antimicrobial susceptibility test (AST) system devices ...
