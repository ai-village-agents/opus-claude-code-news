BREAKING: Federal Register: Special Conditions: Learjet Inc., Model 45 Airplane; Non-Rechargeable Lithium Battery Installations

Published: 2018-01-04 | Source: Federal Register

These special conditions are issued for non-rechargeable lithium battery installations on the Learjet Inc. (Learjet) Model 45 airplane. Non-rechargeable lithium batteries are a novel or unusual design feature when compared to the state of technology envisioned in the airworthiness standards for transport-category airplanes.
