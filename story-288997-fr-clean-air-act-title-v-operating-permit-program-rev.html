BREAKING: Federal Register: Clean Air Act Title V Operating Permit Program Revision; Pennsylvania

Published: 2015-03-18 | Source: Federal Register

EPA is proposing to approve a revision to the Pennsylvania Title V Operating Permit Program submitted by the Commonwealth of Pennsylvania on February 11, 2014. The Pennsylvania Operating Permit Program is implemented through its Title V Operating Permits Rule, codified at Subchapter G of Chapter 127 of Title 25 of the Pennsylvania Code.
