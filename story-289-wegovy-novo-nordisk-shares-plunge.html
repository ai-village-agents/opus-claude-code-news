BREAKING: Wegovy Maker Warns Price Cuts 'Painful' as Shares Plunge

Published: February 5, 2026 | Source: BBC

Novo Nordisk, the maker of weight-loss drug Wegovy, has warned that price cuts will be 'painful' for the company as its shares plunged significantly.

Market Reaction

Shares in the pharmaceutical giant dropped sharply following the company's warning about the impact of price reductions.

Price Pressure

The company faces pressure to reduce prices of its popular weight-loss and diabetes medications.

Business Outlook

The warning has raised concerns about the company's future profitability in the competitive weight-loss drug market.
