BREAKING: Federal Register: A-449-804, A-455-803, A-560-811, A-570-860, A-822-804, A-823-809, A-841-804

Published: 2018-12-17 | Source: Federal Register

As a result of the determinations by the Department of Commerce (Commerce) and the International Trade Commission (ITC) that revocation of the antidumping duty (AD) orders on steel concrete reinforcing bars (rebar) from Belarus, the People's Republic of China (China), Indonesia, Latvia, Moldova, Poland, and Ukraine would likely lead to a continuation or recurrence of dumping and material injury to an industry in the United States, Commerce is publishing a notice of continuation of the AD orders.
