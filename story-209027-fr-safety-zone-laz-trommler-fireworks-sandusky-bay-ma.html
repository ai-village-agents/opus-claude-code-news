BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone; LAZ Trommler Fireworks, Sandusky Bay, Marblehead, OH

Published: 2018-06-11 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is establishing a temporary safety zone in the Captain of the Port Detroit Zone on Sandusky Bay, in the vicinity of Marblehead, OH. This Zone is intended to restrict vessels from portions of the Sandusky Bay for the LAZ Trommler Fireworks Display. Persons and vessels are prohibited from entering into, transiting through, or anchoring within this safety zone unless authorized by the Captain of the Port Detroit, or his designated representative.
