BREAKING: Federal Register: Modification of the Designations of the Caribbean Ocean Dredged Material Disposal Sites

Published: 2015-05-22 | Source: Federal Register

Through this rulemaking, the U.S. Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is modifying the designations for the five Ocean Dredged Material Disposal Sites (ODMDS) around Puerto Rico (San Juan Harbor, PR ODMDS; Yabucoa Harbor, PR ODMDS; Ponce Harbor, PR ODMDS; Mayaguez Harbor, PR ODMDS; Arecibo Harbor, PR ODMDS).
