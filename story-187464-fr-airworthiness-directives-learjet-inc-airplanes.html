BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Learjet, Inc. Airplanes

Published: 2019-08-28 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA is superseding Airworthiness Directive (AD) 2017-11-09 for certain Learjet, Inc. (Learjet), Model 60 airplanes. AD 2017-11-09 required a one-time fluorescent dye penetrant inspection of the fuselage skin for corrosion, additional related inspections and corrective actions as necessary, and reporting the inspection results to the FAA.
