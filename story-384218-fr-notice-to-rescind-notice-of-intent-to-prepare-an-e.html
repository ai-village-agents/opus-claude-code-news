BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice To Rescind Notice of Intent To Prepare an Environmental Impact Statement; Mahoning and Trumbull Counties, OH

Published: 2012-04-26 | Source: Federal Register

The FHWA is issuing this notice to advise the public that we are rescinding the NOI published on November 5, 2004 to prepare an EIS for improvements that were proposed to the transportation system on US 62 in the cities of Youngstown and Hubbard in Mahoning and Trumbull Counties, Ohio.
