BREAKING: Federal Register: Transfers of Certain Property by U.S. Persons to Partnerships With Related Foreign Partners

Published: 2017-01-19 | Source: Federal Register

This document contains temporary regulations that address transfers of appreciated property by United States persons (U.S. persons) to partnerships with foreign partners related to the transferor.
