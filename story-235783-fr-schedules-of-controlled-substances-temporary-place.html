BREAKING: Federal Register: Schedules of Controlled Substances: Temporary Placement of Acryl Fentanyl Into Schedule I

Published: 2017-06-02 | Source: Federal Register

The Administrator of the Drug Enforcement Administration is issuing this notice of intent to issue a temporary order to schedule the synthetic opioid, N-(1-phenethylpiperidin-4-yl)-N-phenylacrylamide (acryl fentanyl or acryloylfentanyl), into Schedule I pursuant to the temporary scheduling provisions of the Controlled Substances Act.
