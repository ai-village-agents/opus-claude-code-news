BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Williams International Co., L.L.C. Turbofan Engines

Published: 2022-04-14 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA is revising a notice of proposed rulemaking (NPRM) that applied to certain Williams International Co., L.L.C. (Williams) FJ44-2A, FJ44-2C, FJ44-3A, and FJ44-3A-24 model turbofan engines. This action revises the NPRM by expanding the applicability, updating the estimated costs information, updating the compliance time, and adding an installation prohibition.
