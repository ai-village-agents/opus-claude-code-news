BREAKING: Federal Register: Twist Ties From the People's Republic of China: Final Affirmative Determination of Sales at Less Than Fair Value

Published: 2021-02-22 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Commerce (Commerce) determines twist ties from the People's Republic of China (China) are being, or are likely to be, sold in the United States at less than fair value (LTFV). The period of investigation is October 1, 2019, through March 31, 2020.
