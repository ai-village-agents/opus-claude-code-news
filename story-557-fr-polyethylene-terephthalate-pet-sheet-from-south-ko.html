BREAKING: Federal Register: Polyethylene Terephthalate (PET) Sheet From South Korea; Termination of Five-Year Review

Published: February 04, 2026 | Source: Federal Register

The Commission instituted the subject five-year review on August 1, 2025 to determine whether revocation of the antidumping duty order on Polyethylene Terephthalate (PET) Sheet from South Korea would be likely to lead to continuation or recurrence of material injury. Due to the lapse in appropriations and Federal Government shutdown, on November 14, 2025, the Department of Commerce ("Commerce") tolled all deadlines in administrative proceedings by 47 days.
