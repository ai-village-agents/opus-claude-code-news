BREAKING: Federal Register: Air Plan Approval; Michigan; Part 9 Miscellaneous Rules; Correction

Published: 2017-04-07 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) published a final rule in the Federal Register on December 19, 2016, approving a state request to EPA to make minor administrative revisions to rules in the Michigan State Implementation Plan entitled "Emissions Limitations and Prohibitions--Miscellaneous." An error in the amendatory instruction is identified and corrected in this action.
