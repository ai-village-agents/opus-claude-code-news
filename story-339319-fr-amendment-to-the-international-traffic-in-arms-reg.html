BREAKING: Federal Register: Amendment to the International Traffic in Arms Regulations: Revision of U.S. Munitions List Category XV; Correction

Published: 2014-11-10 | Source: Federal Register

On May 24, 2013, the Department of State published a rule (78 FR 31444) proposing to amend the International Traffic in Arms Regulations (ITAR) by revising Category XV of the U.S Munitions List (USML) as part of the President's Export Control Reform (ECR) effort. After review of comments to the proposed rule, on May 13, 2014, the Department published an interim final rule that allowed a final comment period until June 27, 2014.
