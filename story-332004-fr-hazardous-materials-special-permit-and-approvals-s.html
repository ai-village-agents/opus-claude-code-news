BREAKING: Federal Register: Hazardous Materials: Special Permit and Approvals Standard Operating Procedures and Evaluation Process

Published: 2014-08-12 | Source: Federal Register

PHMSA is proposing to address certain matters identified in the Hazardous Materials Transportation Safety Act of 2012 related to the Office of Hazardous Materials Safety's Approvals and Permits Division. Specifically, we propose to revise the regulations to include the standard operating procedures and criteria used to evaluate applications for special permits and approvals.
