BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Final Federal Agency Actions on Transportation Project in Washington State

Published: 2013-07-30 | Source: Federal Register

This notice announces actions taken by the FHWA and other Federal agencies that are final within the meaning of 23 U.S.C. 139(l)(1). The actions relate to the State Route 167 Puyallup to SR 509, Puyallup River Bridge Replacement Project, located in the City of Puyallup (Milepost [MP] 6.40) in Pierce County, Washington.
