BREAKING: Federal Register: Bridger-Teton National Forest; Wyoming; Teton to Snake Fuels Management Project

Published: 2013-02-27 | Source: Federal Register

The Forest Service is preparing an environmental impact statement (EIS) to document the potential effects of the Teton to Snake Fuels Management Project. The analysis will evaluate and disclose the effects of treating National Forest land to reduce the potential fire behavior within the wildland-urban interface to better protect threatened values, to improve firefighter safety, and to allow fire to play a more natural role in the ecosystem.
