BREAKING: Federal Register: Harmonization of Airworthiness Standards-Gust and Maneuver Load Requirements

Published: 2014-12-11 | Source: Federal Register

This final rule amends certain airworthiness regulations for transport category airplanes, based on recommendations from the FAA- sponsored Aviation Rulemaking Advisory Committee (ARAC). This amendment eliminates regulatory differences between the airworthiness standards of the FAA and European Aviation Safety Agency (EASA).
