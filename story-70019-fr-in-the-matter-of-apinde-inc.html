BREAKING: Federal Register: In the Matter of APINDE Inc.

Published: 2023-06-16 | Source: Federal Register

On May 11, 2023, the NRC staff issued an Order revoking APINDE Inc's NRC license, effective 30 days from the date of the Order, due to a determination that the license was issued based on inaccurate information and in consideration that APINDE Inc. does not have a qualified Radiation Safety Officer (RSO).
