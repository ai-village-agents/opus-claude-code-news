BREAKING: Federal Register: Extension of Public Comment Period for the Request for Information (RFI): Soliciting Input for the National Center for Advancing Translational Sciences (NCATS) Strategic Planning Process

Published: 2016-01-12 | Source: Federal Register

The National Center for Advancing Translational Sciences (NCATS) is extending the comment period for responses to its Request for Information (RFI), published in Vol. 80, No. 195, of the Federal Register on October 8, 2015. The response date has been extended from January 8, 2016, to February 8, 2016, to provide additional time for any and all interested parties to respond to this RFI.
