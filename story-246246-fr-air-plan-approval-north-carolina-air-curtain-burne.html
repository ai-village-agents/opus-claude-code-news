BREAKING: Federal Register: Air Plan Approval; North Carolina; Air Curtain Burners

Published: 2017-10-13 | Source: Federal Register

Due to receipt of an adverse comment, the Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is withdrawing the August 17, 2017, direct final rule that approves portions of North Carolina State Implementation Plan (SIP) revisions related to changes to an air curtain burner regulation. EPA stated in the direct final rules that if EPA received adverse comments by the close of the public comment period, the rules would be withdrawn and not take effect.
