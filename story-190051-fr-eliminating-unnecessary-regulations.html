BREAKING: Federal Register: Eliminating Unnecessary Regulations

Published: 2019-10-01 | Source: Federal Register

The United States Patent and Trademark Office (USPTO or Office) hereby amends the Rules of Practice in Patent Cases and Trial Practice Before the Patent Trial and Appeal Board (PTAB) by removing provisions in the Code of Federal Regulations that are no longer necessary.
