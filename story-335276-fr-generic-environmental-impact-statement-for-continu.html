BREAKING: Federal Register: Generic Environmental Impact Statement for Continued Storage of Spent Nuclear Fuel

Published: 2014-09-19 | Source: Federal Register

Notice is hereby given that the U.S. Nuclear Regulatory Commission (NRC) has published the final generic environmental impact statement (GEIS), NUREG-2157, "Generic Environmental Impact Statement for Continued Storage of Spent Nuclear Fuel."
