BREAKING: Federal Register: Public Meeting Regarding NHTSA's Research Portfolio

Published: 2018-03-01 | Source: Federal Register

NHTSA is announcing a public meeting where the agency's Vehicle Safety Research and Behavioral Safety Research offices will present information on activities related to priority research programs in vehicle and behavioral safety, including a focused overview session on NHTSA's research in Automated Driving Systems (ADSs).
