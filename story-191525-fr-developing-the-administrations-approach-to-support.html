BREAKING: Federal Register: Developing the Administration's Approach To Supporting Economic Recovery in Venezuela

Published: 2019-10-18 | Source: Federal Register

On behalf of the U.S. Administration, the International Trade Administration (ITA) is requesting comments on ways the Administration can support economic recovery following leadership transition in Venezuela. This request supplements on-going outreach the Administration is conducting with the private sector intended to inform our engagement going forward.
