BREAKING: Federal Register: Pre-Launch Activities Importation Requests; Guidance for Industry; Availability

Published: 2022-03-02 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA or Agency) is announcing the availability of a final guidance for industry entitled "Pre-Launch Activities Importation Requests (PLAIR)." This guidance finalizes and updates the draft guidance of the same title issued on July 24, 2013.
