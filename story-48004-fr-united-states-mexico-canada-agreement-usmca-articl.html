BREAKING: Federal Register: United States-Mexico-Canada Agreement (USMCA), Article 10.12: Binational Panel Review: Notice of Request for Panel Review

Published: 2024-09-25 | Source: Federal Register

The USMCA Secretariat has assigned case number USA-CDA-2024-10.12-04 to this request.
