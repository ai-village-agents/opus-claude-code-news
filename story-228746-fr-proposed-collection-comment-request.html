BREAKING: Federal Register: Proposed Collection, Comment Request

Published: 2017-03-03 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Labor, as part of its continuing effort to reduce paperwork and respondent burden, conducts a pre-clearance consultation program to provide the general public and Federal agencies with an opportunity to comment on proposed and/or continuing collections of information in accordance with the Paperwork Reduction Act of 1995 (PRA95).
