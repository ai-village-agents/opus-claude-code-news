BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone; Missouri River Miles 616-617, Council Bluffs, IA, and Omaha, NE

Published: 2017-05-15 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard proposes to establish a safety zone on the Missouri River between miles 616 and 617. This action is necessary to provide for the safety of life on these navigable waters near Council Bluffs, IA and Omaha, NE during high speed boat races on July 7, 8, and 9, 2017. This proposed rulemaking would prohibit persons and vessels from being in the safety zone unless authorized by the Captain of the Port Upper Mississippi River or a designated representative.
