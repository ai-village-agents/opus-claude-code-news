BREAKING: Federal Register: Energy Conservation Program: Energy Efficiency Standards for Manufactured Housing

Published: 2015-02-11 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Department of Energy (DOE) has initiated the process to develop and publish energy efficiency standards for manufactured housing, as directed by the Energy Independence and Security Act of 2007 (EISA). To facilitate this process, enhance the quality of the standards and supporting documentation, and to allow interested parties to provide comments and information, DOE is publishing this request for information (RFI).
