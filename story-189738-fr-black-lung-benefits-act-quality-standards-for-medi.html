BREAKING: Federal Register: Black Lung Benefits Act: Quality Standards for Medical Testing

Published: 2019-09-27 | Source: Federal Register

The Black Lung Benefits Act provides benefits to miners who are totally disabled due to pneumoconiosis arising out of coal mine employment and to certain miners' survivors. Determining benefits entitlement necessarily entails evaluating the miner's physical condition, particularly his or her respiratory system.
