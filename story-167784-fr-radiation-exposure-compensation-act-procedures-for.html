BREAKING: Federal Register: Radiation Exposure Compensation Act: Procedures for Claims Submitted at the Statutory Filing Deadline

Published: 2020-12-09 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Justice ("the Department") is publishing this document to inform the public of the Department's procedures for filing claims under the Radiation Exposure Compensation Act ("RECA") at the statutory filing deadline. RECA requires that claims shall be barred unless filed within 22 years after the date of enactment of the Radiation Exposure Compensation Act Amendments of 2000.
