BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone; Clear Lake, Clear Creek, TX

Published: 2021-06-24 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is establishing a temporary safety zone for navigable waters within 100 feet of the Pre-Stage Zone, Approach Zone, Course Run Zone and Shut-Down Zone during the Texas Outlaw Challenge in Clear Lake, Clear Creek, TX. The safety zone is needed to protect personnel, vessels, and the marine environment from potential hazards created by the power boat race.
