BREAKING: Federal Register: Clean Fuels Grant Program, Augmented With Discretionary Bus and Bus Facilities Program Funds

Published: 2012-10-02 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Department of Transportation's (DOT) Federal Transit Administration (FTA) announces the selection of projects for the Clean Fuels Grant program enhanced with Section 5309 Bus and Bus Facilities program funds. On February 7, 2012, FTA published a Notice of Funding Availability (NOFA) for its Clean Fuels Grant program (77 FR 6178).
