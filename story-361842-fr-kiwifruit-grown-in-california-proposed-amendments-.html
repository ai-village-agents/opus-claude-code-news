BREAKING: Federal Register: Kiwifruit Grown in California; Proposed Amendments to Marketing Order 920 and Referendum Order

Published: 2013-08-02 | Source: Federal Register

This rule proposes five amendments to Marketing Order No. 920 (order), which regulates the handling of kiwifruit grown in California, and provides growers with the opportunity to vote in a referendum to determine if they favor the changes. The amendments are based on proposals by the Kiwifruit Administrative Committee (Committee or KAC), which is responsible for the local administration of the order.
