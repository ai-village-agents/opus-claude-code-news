BREAKING: Federal Register: Approval of Missouri's Air Quality Implementation Plans; Open Burning Requirements

Published: 2016-09-08 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is proposing to approve revisions to the State Implementation Plan (SIP) for the State of Missouri related to open burning. On November 24, 2009, the Missouri Department of Natural Resources (MDNR) requested to amend the SIP to replace four area specific open burning rules into one rule that is area specific and applicable state-wide.
