BREAKING: Federal Register: Media Outlets for Publication of Legal and Action Notices in the Southern Region

Published: 2015-11-13 | Source: Federal Register

This notice lists the newspapers that will be used by all U.S. Forest Service Ranger Districts, Forests, and the Regional Office of the Southern Region to publish legal notices required under 36 CFR 214, 218, and 219. The intended effect of this action is to inform interested members of the public which newspapers the Forest Service will use to publish notices of proposed actions, notices of decision, and notices of opportunity to file an appeal/objection.
