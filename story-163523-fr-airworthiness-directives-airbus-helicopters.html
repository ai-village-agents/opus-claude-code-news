BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Airbus Helicopters

Published: 2020-10-14 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA proposes to supersede airworthiness directive (AD) 82- 20-05 for Societe Nationale Industrielle Aerospatiale (now Airbus Helicopters) Model AS-350 and AS-355 series helicopters. AD 82-20-05 requires inspecting and establishing a life limit for the tail rotor (TR) drive shaft bearing (bearing).
