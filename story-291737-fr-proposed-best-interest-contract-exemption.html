BREAKING: Federal Register: Proposed Best Interest Contract Exemption

Published: 2015-04-20 | Source: Federal Register

This document contains a notice of pendency before the U.S. Department of Labor of a proposed exemption from certain prohibited transactions provisions of the Employee Retirement Income Security Act of 1974 (ERISA) and the Internal Revenue Code (the Code).
