BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zones; Coast Guard Sector Upper Mississippi River Annual and Recurring Safety Zones Update

Published: 2018-04-17 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard proposes to amend its safety zone regulations for annual events in Coast Guard Sector Upper Mississippi River. This proposed rule would add one new recurring safety zone, remove four safety zones, and amend the sponsor/name, date, and/or safety zones for three recurring safety zones already listed in the current table.
