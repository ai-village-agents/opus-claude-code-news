BREAKING: Federal Register: Procedures for Changes in Control Affecting Applications and Authorizations To Import or Export Natural Gas

Published: 2014-11-05 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Department of Energy's (DOE or the Department) regulations require applications to export natural gas from the United States to identify "all the participants in the transaction, including the parent company, if any, and identification of any corporate or other affiliations among the participants."
