BREAKING: Federal Register: Privacy of Consumer Information (Regulation P)

Published: 2014-05-29 | Source: Federal Register

The Board of Governors of the Federal Reserve System (Board) is repealing its Regulation P, 12 CFR part 216, which was issued to implement the privacy provisions of the Gramm-Leach-Bliley Act (GLB Act).
