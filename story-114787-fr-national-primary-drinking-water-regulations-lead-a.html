BREAKING: Federal Register: National Primary Drinking Water Regulations: Lead and Copper Rule Revisions

Published: 2021-01-15 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is publishing final regulatory revisions to the National Primary Drinking Water Regulation (NPDWR) for lead and copper under the authority of the Safe Drinking Water Act (SDWA). These revised requirements provide greater and more effective protection of public health by reducing exposure to lead and copper in drinking water.
