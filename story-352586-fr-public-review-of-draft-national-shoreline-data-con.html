BREAKING: Federal Register: Public Review of Draft National Shoreline Data Content Standard

Published: 2013-04-18 | Source: Federal Register

The Federal Geographic Data Committee (FGDC) is conducting a public review of the draft National Shoreline Data Content Standard. The FGDC has developed a draft National Shoreline Data Content Standard that provides a framework for shoreline data development, sharing of data, and shoreline data transformation and fusion.
