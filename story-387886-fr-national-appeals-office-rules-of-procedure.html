BREAKING: Federal Register: National Appeals Office Rules of Procedure

Published: 2012-06-08 | Source: Federal Register

This proposed rule would establish procedures governing the National Appeals Office (NAO), a division of NMFS' Office of Management and Budget within NOAA. NAO's central mission is to provide an efficient means of adjudicating appeals by providing due process and consistency to NMFS' administrative decisions.
