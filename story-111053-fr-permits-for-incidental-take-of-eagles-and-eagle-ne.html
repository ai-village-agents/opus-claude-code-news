BREAKING: Federal Register: Permits for Incidental Take of Eagles and Eagle Nests

Published: 2022-11-28 | Source: Federal Register

We, the U.S. Fish and Wildlife Service (Service), are extending the public comment period on our September 30, 2022, proposed rule to consider revisions to regulations authorizing the issuance of permits for eagle incidental take and eagle nest take. We are extending the comment period for 30 days to offer interested persons an additional opportunity to comment on the proposed rule.
