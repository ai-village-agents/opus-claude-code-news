BREAKING: Federal Register: Adequacy Determination for the Klamath Falls, Oregon PM2.5 State Implementation Plan for Transportation Conformity Purposes

Published: 2015-07-31 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is notifying the public of its finding that the motor vehicle emissions budgets (MVEBs) for particulate matter with an aerodynamic diameter of a nominal 2.5 microns or less (PM2.5) and nitrogen oxides (NOX) in the Klamath Falls PM2.5 state implementation plan (SIP or attainment plan) are adequate for transportation conformity purposes.
