BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone; Motus Myrtle Beach Triathlon, Myrtle Beach, SC

Published: 2022-04-29 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is establishing a temporary safety zone on certain waters of the Atlantic Intracoastal Waterway, near Myrtle Beach, SC. This action is necessary to ensure the safety of life on navigable waters of the United States during the Motus Myrtle Beach Triathlon Swim event.
