BREAKING: Federal Register: Changes in Flood Hazard Determinations

Published: 2020-07-31 | Source: Federal Register

On July 10, 2020, FEMA published in the Federal Register a changes in flood hazard determination notice that contained an erroneous table. This notice provides corrections to that table to be used in lieu of the information published. The table provided here represents the changes in flood hazard determinations and communities affected for Mobile County, Alabama.
