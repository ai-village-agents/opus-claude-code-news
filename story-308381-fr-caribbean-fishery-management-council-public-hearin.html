BREAKING: Federal Register: Caribbean Fishery Management Council; Public Hearings; Correction

Published: 2015-11-02 | Source: Federal Register

The Caribbean Fishery Management Council (CFMC) is considering modifying the timing for the implementation of accountability measure (AM)-based closures in the Caribbean Exclusive Economic Zone (EEZ) and specifying how often to revisit the modification.
