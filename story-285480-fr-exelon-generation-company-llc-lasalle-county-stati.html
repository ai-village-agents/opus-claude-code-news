BREAKING: Federal Register: Exelon Generation Company, LLC; LaSalle County Station, Units 1 and 2

Published: 2015-02-03 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Nuclear Regulatory Commission (NRC) is considering an application for the renewal of operating license numbers NPF-11 and NPF-18, which authorize Exelon Generation Company, LLC to operate LaSalle County Station (LSCS), Units 1 and 2. The renewed licenses would authorize the applicant to operate LSCS, Units 1 and 2 for an additional 20 years beyond the period specified in each of the current licenses.
