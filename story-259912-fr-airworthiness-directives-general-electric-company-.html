BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; General Electric Company Turbofan Engines

Published: 2016-04-04 | Source: Federal Register

We are adopting a new airworthiness directive (AD) for all General Electric Company (GE) CF6-80E1 turbofan engines with rotating compressor discharge pressure (CDP) seal, part number (P/N) 1669M73P02, installed. This AD was prompted by reports from the manufacturer of cracks in the teeth of two rotating CDP seals found during engine shop visits.
