BREAKING: Federal Register: Environmental Impact Statement; Grasshopper and Mormon Cricket Suppression Program

Published: 2016-09-01 | Source: Federal Register

We are advising the public that the Animal and Plant Health Inspection Service plans to prepare an environmental impact statement to analyze the effects of a program to suppress populations of grasshoppers and Mormon cricket from 17 States in the western United States.
