BREAKING: Federal Register: Endangered and Threatened Wildlife and Plants; 90-Day Findings on Three Petitions; Correction

Published: 2016-12-05 | Source: Federal Register

On November 30, 2016, we, the U.S. Fish and Wildlife Service (Service), published a document in the Federal Register announcing 90- day findings on three petitions to list or reclassify wildlife or plants under the Endangered Species Act of 1973, as amended (Act). That document included a not-substantial finding for Tetraneuris verdiensis (Verde four-nerve daisy).
