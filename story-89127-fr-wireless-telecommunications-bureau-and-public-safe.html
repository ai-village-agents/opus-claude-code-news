BREAKING: Federal Register: Wireless Telecommunications Bureau and Public Safety and Homeland Security Bureau Extend the Date for the Acceptance and Processing of Certain Applications for 470-512 MHz (T-Band) Spectrum

Published: 2022-02-17 | Source: Federal Register

In this Public Notice, the Public Safety and Homeland Security Bureau and Wireless Telecommunications Bureau (collectively the Bureaus) extend the period for accepting certain applications for 470- 512 MHz (T-Band) spectrum until April 1, 2022.
