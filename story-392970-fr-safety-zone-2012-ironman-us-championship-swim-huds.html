BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone; 2012 Ironman US Championship Swim, Hudson River, Fort Lee, NJ

Published: 2012-08-06 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is establishing a temporary safety zone on the navigable waters of the Hudson River in the vicinity of Englewood Cliffs and Fort Lee, NJ for the 2012 Ironman US Championship swim event. This temporary safety zone is necessary to protect the maritime public and event participants from the hazards associated with swim events.
