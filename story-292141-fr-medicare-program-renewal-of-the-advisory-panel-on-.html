BREAKING: Federal Register: Medicare Program: Renewal of the Advisory Panel on Hospital Outpatient Payment

Published: 2015-04-24 | Source: Federal Register

This notice announces the renewal of the Advisory Panel (the Panel) on Hospital Outpatient Payment (HOP) charter. The charter was approved on November 6, 2014 for a 2-year period effective through November 6, 2016. This notice publicly announces the renewal of the HOP Panel for another 2-year period.
