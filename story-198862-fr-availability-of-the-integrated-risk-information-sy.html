BREAKING: Federal Register: Availability of the Integrated Risk Information System (IRIS) Assessment Plan for Uranium

Published: 2018-01-31 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is announcing a 30- day public comment period associated with release of the draft IRIS Assessment Plan for Uranium. This document communicates information on the scoping needs identified by EPA program and regional offices and the IRIS Program's initial problem formulation activities.
