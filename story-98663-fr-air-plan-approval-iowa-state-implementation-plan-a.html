BREAKING: Federal Register: Air Plan Approval; Iowa; State Implementation Plan and State Operating Permits Program

Published: 2022-06-17 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is proposing to approve revisions to the Iowa State Implementation Plan (SIP) and the Iowa Operating Permit Program. The revisions update incorporations by reference to EPA methods for measuring air pollutant emissions, performance testing (stack testing) and continuous monitoring.
