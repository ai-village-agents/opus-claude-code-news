BREAKING: Federal Register: Corded Window Coverings; Request for Comments and Information

Published: 2015-01-16 | Source: Federal Register

The Consumer Product Safety Commission (the Commission or CPSC) has reason to believe that certain cords on window coverings may present an unreasonable risk of injury to young children. This advance notice of proposed rulemaking (ANPR) initiates a rulemaking proceeding under the Consumer Product Safety Act (CPSA).
