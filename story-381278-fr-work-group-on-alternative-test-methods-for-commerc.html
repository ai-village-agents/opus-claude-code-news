BREAKING: Federal Register: Work Group on Alternative Test Methods for Commercial Measuring Devices

Published: 2012-03-26 | Source: Federal Register

The National Institute of Standards and Technology (NIST) is forming a Work Group (WG) to examine alternative methods for testing the accuracy of commercial measuring devices including, but not limited to retail motor-fuel dispensers.
