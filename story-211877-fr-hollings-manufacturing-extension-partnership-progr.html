BREAKING: Federal Register: Hollings Manufacturing Extension Partnership Program; Knowledge Sharing Strategies.

Published: 2018-07-18 | Source: Federal Register

The Hollings Manufacturing Extension Partnership (MEP) Program includes a network of centers located in all 50 States and Puerto Rico, and is a source of trusted advice about new technologies, production techniques and business management practices.
