BREAKING: Federal Register: Invitation for Membership on Advisory Committee

Published: 2013-06-10 | Source: Federal Register

The Joint Board for the Enrollment of Actuaries (Joint Board), established under the Employee Retirement Income Security Act of 1974 (ERISA), is responsible for the enrollment of individuals who wish to perform actuarial services under ERISA. The Joint Board has established the Advisory Committee on Actuarial Examinations (Advisory Committee) to assist in its examination duties mandated by ERISA.
