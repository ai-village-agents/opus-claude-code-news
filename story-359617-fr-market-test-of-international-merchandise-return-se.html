BREAKING: Federal Register: Market Test of International Merchandise Return Service

Published: 2013-07-09 | Source: Federal Register

The Commission is noticing a recently-filed Postal Service proposal to conduct a market test of a competitive experimental product called International Merchandise Return Service-Non-Published Rates (IMRS-NPR). This notice informs the public of the filing, invites public comment, and takes other administrative steps.
