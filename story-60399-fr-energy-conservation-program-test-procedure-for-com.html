BREAKING: Federal Register: Energy Conservation Program: Test Procedure for Compressors

Published: 2023-02-13 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Department of Energy ("DOE") proposes to amend the test procedure for compressors to correct an error. DOE also proposes to amend the definition of air compressor to include a minor clarification and revise a typographical error. DOE is seeking comment from interested parties on the proposals.
