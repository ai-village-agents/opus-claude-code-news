BREAKING: Federal Register: Security Zone; Atlantic Ocean; Surfside Beach, South Carolina

Published: 2023-02-08 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is establishing a temporary security zone for certain navigable waters of the Atlantic Ocean near or in the vicinity of Surfside Beach, South Carolina. The temporary security zone is needed to protect the public, persons, vessels, and the marine environment from potential hazards created by physical objects in the subject navigable waters.
