BREAKING: Federal Register: Certain Refrigerator Water Filtration Devices and Components Thereof; Notice of Institution of Investigation

Published: 2022-01-21 | Source: Federal Register

Notice is hereby given that a complaint was filed with the U.S. International Trade Commission on December 15, 2021, under section 337 of the Tariff Act of 1930, as amended, on behalf of LG Electronics Inc. of Korea, and LG Electronics Alabama, Inc. of Huntsville, Alabama. A supplement was filed on December 23, 2021.
