BREAKING: Federal Register: Certain Tunable Lenses and Products Containing the Same; Notice of the Commission's Determination Not To Review an Initial Determination Terminating the Investigation on the Basis of Settlement; Termination of the Investigation

Published: 2022-04-07 | Source: Federal Register

Notice is hereby given that the U.S. International Trade Commission has determined not to review the presiding administrative law judge's ("ALJ") initial determination ("ID") (Order No. 13) terminating the investigation on the basis of settlement. The investigation is terminated.
