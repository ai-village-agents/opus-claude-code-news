BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Engine Alliance Turbofan Engines

Published: 2015-10-23 | Source: Federal Register

We propose to adopt a new airworthiness directive (AD) for certain Engine Alliance (EA) GP7270 turbofan engines. This proposed AD was prompted by reports of the installation of non-conforming honeycomb seals in the high-pressure compressor (HPC) adjacent to the HPC rotor stage 2 to 5 spool and stage 7 to 9 spool.
