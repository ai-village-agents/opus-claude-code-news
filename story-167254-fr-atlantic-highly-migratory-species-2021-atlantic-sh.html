BREAKING: Federal Register: Atlantic Highly Migratory Species; 2021 Atlantic Shark Commercial Fishing Year

Published: 2020-12-01 | Source: Federal Register

This final rule establishes the 2021 opening date for all Atlantic shark fisheries, including the fisheries in the Gulf of Mexico and Caribbean. This final rule also establishes the shark fisheries quotas for the 2021 fishing year, with adjustments based on harvest levels during 2020, and establishes the large coastal shark (LCS) retention limits for directed shark limited access permit holders.
