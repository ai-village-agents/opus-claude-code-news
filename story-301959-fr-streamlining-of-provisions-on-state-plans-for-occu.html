BREAKING: Federal Register: Streamlining of Provisions on State Plans for Occupational Safety and Health

Published: 2015-08-18 | Source: Federal Register

This document primarily amends OSHA regulations to remove the detailed descriptions of State plan coverage, purely historical data, and other unnecessarily codified information. In addition, this document moves most of the general provisions of subpart A of part 1952 into part 1902, where the general regulations on State plan criteria are found.
