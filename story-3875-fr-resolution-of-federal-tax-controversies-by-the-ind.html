BREAKING: Federal Register: Resolution of Federal Tax Controversies by the Independent Office of Appeals

Published: 2025-01-15 | Source: Federal Register

This document contains final regulations that provide guidance on the resolution of Federal tax controversies by the IRS Independent Office of Appeals (Appeals) under the Taxpayer First Act of 2019 (TFA). The final regulations provide that while the Appeals resolution process is generally available to all taxpayers to resolve Federal tax controversies, there are certain exceptions to consideration by Appeals.
