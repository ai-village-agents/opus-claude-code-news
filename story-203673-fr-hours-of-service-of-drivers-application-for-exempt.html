BREAKING: Federal Register: Hours of Service of Drivers: Application for Exemption; Wilcox Truck Line, Inc.

Published: 2018-04-04 | Source: Federal Register

FMCSA announces that Wilcox Truck Line, Inc. (Wilcox) has requested an exemption from the requirement that a motor carrier install and require each of its drivers to use an electronic logging device (ELD) to record the driver's hours-of-service (HOS). Wilcox has requested a 5-year exemption from ELD use when transporting loads in support of an Idaho National Laboratory (INL) program.
