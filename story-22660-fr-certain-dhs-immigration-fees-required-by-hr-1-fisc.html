BREAKING: Federal Register: Certain DHS Immigration Fees Required by HR-1: Fiscal Year 2026 Adjustments for Inflation

Published: 2025-11-19 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Homeland Security (DHS) is announcing Fiscal Year (FY) 2026 inflationary adjustments to certain immigration-related fees required by the One Big Beautiful Bill Act (HR-1). HR-1 requires that DHS annually adjust certain immigration-related fees for inflation.
