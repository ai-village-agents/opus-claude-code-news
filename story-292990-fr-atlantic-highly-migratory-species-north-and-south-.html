BREAKING: Federal Register: Atlantic Highly Migratory Species; North and South Atlantic 2015 Commercial Swordfish Quotas

Published: 2015-05-05 | Source: Federal Register

This final rule adjusts the 2015 fishing season quotas for North and South Atlantic swordfish based upon 2014 quota underharvests and international quota transfers consistent with International Commission for the Conservation of Atlantic Tunas (ICCAT) Recommendations 13-02 and 13-03. This final rule applies to commercial and recreational fishing for swordfish in the Atlantic Ocean, including the Caribbean Sea and Gulf of Mexico.
