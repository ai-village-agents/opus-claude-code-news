BREAKING: Federal Register: Electronic Signatures, Forms and Storage for Drug and Alcohol Testing Records

Published: 2024-10-15 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Transportation (DOT or Department) proposes to amend its regulations for conducting workplace drug and alcohol testing for the federally regulated transportation industry to allow, but not require, electronic signatures on documents required to be created and utilized under the regulations, the use of electronic versions of forms, and the electronic storage of forms and data. The regulatory changes would apply to DOT-regulated employers and their contractors ("service agents") ...
