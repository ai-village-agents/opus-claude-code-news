BREAKING: Federal Register: Small Business Size Standards for Manufacturing; Correction

Published: 2016-12-21 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Small Business Administration (SBA) is correcting a final rule that appeared in the Federal Register on January 26, 2016 (81 FR 4469). The rule increased small business size standards for a number of industries in North American Industry Classification System (NAICS) Sector 31-33, Manufacturing.
