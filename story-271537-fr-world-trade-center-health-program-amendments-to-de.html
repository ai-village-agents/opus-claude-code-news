BREAKING: Federal Register: World Trade Center Health Program; Amendments to Definitions, Appeals, and Other Requirements

Published: 2016-08-17 | Source: Federal Register

In 2011 and 2012, the Secretary, Department of Health and Human Services (HHS), promulgated regulations designed to govern the World Trade Center (WTC) Health Program (Program), including the processes by which eligible responders and survivors may apply for enrollment in the Program, obtain health monitoring and treatment for WTC-related health conditions, and appeal enrollment and treatment decisions, as well as a process to add new conditions to the List of WTC-Related Health Conditions.
