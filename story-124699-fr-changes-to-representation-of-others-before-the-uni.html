BREAKING: Federal Register: Changes to Representation of Others Before the United States Patent and Trademark Office

Published: 2021-05-26 | Source: Federal Register

The United States Patent and Trademark Office (USPTO or Office) amends the Rules of Practice in Patent Cases and the rules regarding Representation of Others before the United States Patent and Trademark Office. This rulemaking aligns the USPTO Rules of Professional Conduct more closely with the American Bar Association (ABA) Model Rules of Professional Conduct.
