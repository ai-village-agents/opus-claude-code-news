BREAKING: Federal Register: Critical Material List; Addition of Metallurgical Coal Used for Steelmaking

Published: 2025-05-29 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Department of Energy (DOE) is issuing this notice to advise the public that DOE has added metallurgical coal used for steelmaking to the DOE Critical Material List. This notice also provides the link to the assessment that forms the justification for including metallurgical coal used in steelmaking on the Critical Material List.
