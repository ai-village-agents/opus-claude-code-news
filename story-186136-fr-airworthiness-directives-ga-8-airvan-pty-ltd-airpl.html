BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; GA 8 Airvan (Pty) Ltd Airplanes

Published: 2019-08-12 | Source: Federal Register

We propose to adopt a new airworthiness directive (AD) for GA 8 Airvan (Pty) Ltd Model GA8 and Model GA8-TC320 airplanes. This proposed AD results from mandatory continuing airworthiness information (MCAI) originated by an aviation authority of another country to identify and correct an unsafe condition on an aviation product.
