BREAKING: Federal Register: Implementation of Employment Authorization for Individuals Covered by Deferred Enforced Departure for Lebanon

Published: 2024-10-18 | Source: Federal Register

On July 26, 2024, President Joseph Biden issued a memorandum to the Secretary of State and the Secretary of Homeland Security (Secretary) determining that it was in the foreign policy interest of the United States to defer for 18 months through January 25, 2026, the removal of certain Lebanese nationals present in the United States and to provide them with employment authorization documentation.
