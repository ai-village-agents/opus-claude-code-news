BREAKING: Federal Register: Air Plan Approval; California; Antelope Valley Air Quality Management District; Optional General SIP Category

Published: 2018-12-18 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is proposing to approve a revision to the Antelope Valley Air Quality Management District (AVAQMD) portion of the California State Implementation Plan (SIP). This revision concerns emissions of volatile organic compounds (VOCs) from organic liquid loading. We are proposing to approve revisions to a local rule to regulate these emission sources under the Clean Air Act (CAA or the Act).
