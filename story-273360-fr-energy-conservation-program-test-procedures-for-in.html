BREAKING: Federal Register: Energy Conservation Program: Test Procedures for Integrated Light-Emitting Diode Lamps; Correction

Published: 2016-09-08 | Source: Federal Register

On July 1, 2016, the U.S. Department of Energy (DOE) published a final rule adopting a test procedure for integrated light-emitting diode (LED) lamps (hereafter referred to as "LED lamps") to support the implementation of labeling provisions by the Federal Trade Commission, as well as the ongoing general service lamps rulemaking, which includes LED lamps (hereafter the "July 2016 final rule"). This correction addresses an error in the July 2016 final rule to add appendix BB to 10 CFR 430.3(p)(5)...
