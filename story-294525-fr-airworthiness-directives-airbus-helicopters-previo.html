BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Airbus Helicopters (Previously Eurocopter France) Helicopters

Published: 2015-05-21 | Source: Federal Register

We are adopting a new airworthiness directive (AD) for Airbus Helicopters (previously Eurocopter France) Model AS365N3, EC155B, and EC155B1 helicopters with an external life raft in the footsteps with certain part-numbered junction units. This AD requires inspecting the junction units of the external life raft deployment system for corrosion, removing any corrosion, and performing certain measurements to determine whether the junction unit must be replaced.
