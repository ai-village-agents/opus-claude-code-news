BREAKING: Federal Register: Cooperative Research and Development Agreement (CRADA) Opportunity With the Department of Homeland Security for the Development of a Foot-and-Mouth Disease 3ABC ELISA Diagnostic Kit; Correction

Published: 2013-07-16 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Homeland Security Science and Technology Directorate (DHS S&T), through its Plum Island Animal Disease Center (PIADC), published a document in the Federal Register of May 16, 2013, seeking industry collaborators to aid DHS S&T in developing and validating an ELISA diagnostic kit for detection of Foot and Mouth Disease Virus (FMDV) non-structural proteins. The document did not specify dates for when the submission of proposals are due.
