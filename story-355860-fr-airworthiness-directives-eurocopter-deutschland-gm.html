BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Eurocopter Deutschland GmbH Helicopters

Published: 2013-05-24 | Source: Federal Register

We are adopting a new airworthiness directive (AD) for Eurocopter Deutschland GmbH (ECD) Model MBB-BK 117 C-2 helicopters. This AD requires revising the operating limitations to prohibit flights under instrument flight rules (IFR) or under night visual flight rules (VFR) when the autotrim is inoperative.
