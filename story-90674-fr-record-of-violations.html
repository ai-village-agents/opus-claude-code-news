BREAKING: Federal Register: Record of Violations

Published: 2022-03-09 | Source: Federal Register

FMCSA amends its regulations to eliminate the requirement that drivers operating commercial motor vehicles (CMVs) in interstate commerce prepare and submit a list of their convictions for traffic violations to their employers annually.
