BREAKING: Federal Register: Meeting of the National Advisory Council on the National Health Service Corps

Published: 2020-05-01 | Source: Federal Register

The National Advisory Council on the National Health Service Corps (NACNHSC) meeting scheduled for Tuesday, June 16, 2020, and Wednesday, June 17, 2020, has changed its format, date, and time. The decision to change the NACNHSC meeting has been made after carefully examining the Centers for Disease Control and Prevention's recommendations to restrict all non-essential travel, and the widespread health risks posed by COVID-19 to the American public.
