BREAKING: Federal Register: CDC Recommendations for Hepatitis C Testing Among Perinatally Exposed Infants and Children-United States, 2023; Request for Comment and Notice of Informational Webinar

Published: 2022-11-22 | Source: Federal Register

The Centers for Disease Control and Prevention (CDC), in the Department of Health and Human Services (HHS), announces the opening of a docket to obtain comment on proposed new recommendations for perinatal hepatitis C virus (HCV) infection testing to identify infants who may go on to develop chronic hepatitis C.
