BREAKING: Federal Register: Natural Grass Sod Promotion, Research, and Information Order

Published: 2024-12-10 | Source: Federal Register

This document proposes to establish an industry-funded promotion, research, and information program for natural grass sod products. The proposed program, the Natural Grass Sod Promotion, Research, and Information Order (Order), was submitted to the U.S. Department of Agriculture (USDA) by Turfgrass Producers International (TPI), a group of natural grass sod producers.
