BREAKING: Federal Register: Fisheries of the Exclusive Economic Zone Off Alaska; Revisions to IFQ Program Regulations

Published: 2012-10-31 | Source: Federal Register

NMFS proposes a regulatory amendment to the vessel ownership requirement of the Individual Fishing Quota (IFQ) Program for fixed- gear Pacific halibut and sablefish fisheries in and off of Alaska. The IFQ Program requires that initial recipients of certain classes of quota shares own a minimum of 20-percent interest in any vessel on which they hire a master to fish their IFQ permits.
