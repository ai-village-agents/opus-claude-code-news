BREAKING: Federal Register: Proposal To Withdraw Spatial Data Transfer Standard, Parts 1-7

Published: 2013-12-09 | Source: Federal Register

The Federal Geographic Data Committee (FGDC) proposes to withdraw the Spatial Data Transfer Standard (SDTS), Parts 1-7, http:// www.fgdc.gov/standards/projects/FGDC-standards-projects/SDTS/index-- html, as an FGDC-endorsed standard. FGDC maintenance authority agencies were asked to review the relevance of the SDTS, and they responded that the SDTS is no longer used by their agencies.
