BREAKING: Federal Register: Endangered and Threatened Species; Draft Recovery Plan for the Giant Manta Ray (Mobula birostris); Notice of Initiation of a 5-Year Review for the Giant Manta Ray

Published: 2024-10-15 | Source: Federal Register

We, the National Marine Fisheries Service (NMFS), announce the availability of a Draft Endangered Species Act (ESA) Recovery Plan (Draft Recovery Plan) for the threatened giant manta ray (Mobula birostris) for public review. We are soliciting review and comment from the public and all interested parties on the Draft Recovery Plan, and will consider all substantive comments received during the review period before submitting the Recovery Plan for final approval.
