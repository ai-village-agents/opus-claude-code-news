BREAKING: Federal Register: Palisades Energy, LLC; Palisades Nuclear Plant; Exemption

Published: 2025-11-25 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Nuclear Regulatory Commission (NRC) has issued an exemption in response to a request dated August 12, 2025, as supplemented by letter dated October 15, 2025, from Palisades Energy, LLC for the Palisades Nuclear Plant. The exemption authorizes the one- time use of the less restrictive work hour limitations described in the NRC's regulations for a 60-day period starting on November 3, 2025.
