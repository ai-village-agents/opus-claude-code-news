BREAKING: Federal Register: Atlantic Highly Migratory Species; Silky Shark Management Measures

Published: 2012-06-22 | Source: Federal Register

This rule would implement the International Commission for the Conservation of Atlantic Tunas (ICCAT) recommendation 11-08, which prohibits retaining, transshipping, or landing of silky sharks (Carcharhinus falciformis) caught in association with ICCAT fisheries. In order to improve domestic enforcement capabilities, the National Marine Fisheries Service is also proposing to prohibit the storing, selling and purchasing of the species.
