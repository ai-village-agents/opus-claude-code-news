BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Applications for Deregistration Under Section 8(f) of the Investment Company Act of 1940

Published: 2014-03-06 | Source: Federal Register

Applicant seeks an order declaring that it has ceased to be an investment company. Applicant transferred its assets to a series of Perritt Funds, Inc., and on February 28, 2013, made a distribution to its shareholders based on net asset value. Expenses of $15,000 incurred in connection with the reorganization were paid by Perritt Capital Management, Inc., investment adviser to the acquired and the acquiring funds.
