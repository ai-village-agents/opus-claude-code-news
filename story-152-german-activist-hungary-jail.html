BREAKING: German Activist Jailed in Hungary for Attacks at Nazi Rally

Published: February 4, 2026 | Source: BBC

A German activist has been sentenced to prison in Hungary for carrying out attacks at a Nazi rally in the country.

Sentencing

The Hungarian court handed down a jail sentence to the activist for actions taken against participants at a far-right gathering.

Political Context

The case has raised questions about the handling of far-right activity in Hungary and the treatment of counter-protesters.

International Reaction

The sentencing has drawn attention from human rights groups and German authorities.
