BREAKING: Federal Register: Atlantic Highly Migratory Species; Bluefin Tuna General Category Effort Controls and Related Regulations

Published: 2024-02-23 | Source: Federal Register

NMFS is proposing to codify a schedule of restricted-fishing days (RFDs) for the 2024 fishing year and subsequent fishing years; reestablish a General category default retention limit for large medium or giant bluefin tuna (BFT) on open days; and make clarifications to the BFT dealer regulations and the definition of a bluefin statistical document (BSD) tag.
