BREAKING: Federal Register: Certain Uncoated Paper From Indonesia: Affirmative Preliminary Determinations of Circumvention of the Antidumping and Countervailing Duty Orders for Uncoated Paper Rolls

Published: 2021-01-27 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Commerce (Commerce) preliminarily determines that imports of certain uncoated paper rolls from Indonesia are circumventing the antidumping duty (AD) and countervailing duty (CVD) orders on certain uncoated paper from Indonesia. As a result, all imports of certain uncoated paper rolls from Indonesia from certain non-responsive converters \1\ will be subject to suspension of liquidation on or after October 18, 2019.
