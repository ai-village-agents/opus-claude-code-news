BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone; Pro Hydro-X Tour, Atlantic Ocean, Islamorada, FL

Published: 2013-09-20 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is establishing a temporary safety zone on the waters of the Atlantic Ocean, Islamorada, Florida during the Pro Hydro- X Tour. The Pro Hydro-X Tour is a series of Jet Ski races. The race course is in an oval configuration. There will be 7 Jet Skis on the course for each race. The Pro Hydro-X Tour is scheduled to take place on September 20, 21, and 22, 2013.
