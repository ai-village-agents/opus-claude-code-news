BREAKING: Federal Register: 2019 Adjustment of the Penalty for Violation of Notice Posting Requirements; Correction

Published: 2020-02-27 | Source: Federal Register

The EEOC is correcting the RIN Number of its item titled "2019 Adjustment of the Penalty for Violation of Notice Posting Requirements," and adding to the authority citation to identify the statutory authority for the EEOC to make adjustments to the penalty for violating notice posting requirements.
