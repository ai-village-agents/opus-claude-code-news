BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Request for Public Comments on Section 232 National Security Investigation of Imports of Titanium Sponge

Published: 2019-03-08 | Source: Federal Register

On March 4, 2019, in response to a petition, the Secretary of Commerce (the "Secretary") initiated an investigation to determine the effects on the national security from imports of titanium sponge. This investigation has been initiated under section 232 of the Trade Expansion Act of 1962, as amended.
