BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Centennial Challenges 3D Printed Habitat Challenge-Design Competition

Published: 2015-05-15 | Source: Federal Register

This notice is issued in accordance with 51 U.S.C. 20144(c). The 3D Printed (3DP) Habitat Challenge--Design Competition is scheduled and teams that wish to compete may now register. Centennial Challenges is a program of prize competitions to stimulate innovation in technologies of interest and value to NASA and the nation.
