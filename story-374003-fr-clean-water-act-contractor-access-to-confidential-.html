BREAKING: Federal Register: Clean Water Act; Contractor Access to Confidential Business Information

Published: 2013-12-31 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA's) has authorized Abt Associates, Inc. (Abt), its subcontractors, and its consultants to access confidential business information (CBI) collected from numerous industries. Transfer of this information is necessary for Abt to assist EPA in the preparation of effluent guidelines and standards for certain industries.
