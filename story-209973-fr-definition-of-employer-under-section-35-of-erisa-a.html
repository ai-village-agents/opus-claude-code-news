BREAKING: Federal Register: Definition of “Employer” Under Section 3(5) of ERISA-Association Health Plans

Published: 2018-06-21 | Source: Federal Register

This document contains a final regulation under Title I of the Employee Retirement Income Security Act (ERISA) that establishes additional criteria under ERISA section 3(5) for determining when employers may join together in a group or association of employers that will be treated as the "employer" sponsor of a single multiple- employer "employee welfare benefit plan" and "group health plan," as those terms are defined in Title I of ERISA.
