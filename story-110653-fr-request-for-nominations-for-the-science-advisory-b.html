BREAKING: Federal Register: Request for Nominations for the Science Advisory Board Inorganic Arsenic Review Panel

Published: 2022-11-22 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) Science Advisory Board (SAB) Staff Office requests public nominations of scientific experts to form a Panel to review the draft EPA Integrated Risk Information System (IRIS) Toxicological Review of Inorganic Arsenic.
