BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Workshop and Call for Information on Integrated Science Assessment for Particulate Matter

Published: 2014-12-03 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) Office of Research and Development's National Center for Environmental Assessment (NCEA) is preparing an Integrated Science Assessment (ISA) as part of the review of the primary and secondary National Ambient Air Quality Standards (NAAQS) for particulate matter (PM).
