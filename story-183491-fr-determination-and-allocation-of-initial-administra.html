BREAKING: Federal Register: Determination and Allocation of Initial Administrative Assessment To Fund Mechanical Licensing Collective

Published: 2019-07-08 | Source: Federal Register

The Copyright Royalty Judges (Judges) announce commencement of a proceeding to determine the initial administrative assessment that digital music providers and any significant nonblanket licensees must pay to fund the operations of the Mechanical Licensing Collective. The Judges also set the date by which the Mechanical Licensing Collective and the Digital Licensee Coordinator must, and other eligible participants may, file a Petition to Participate and the accompanying $150 filing fee.
