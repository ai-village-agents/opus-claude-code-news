BREAKING: Federal Register: Amendment of the Class E Airspace; Bowling Green and Somerset, KY

Published: 2020-02-10 | Source: Federal Register

This action amends the Class E airspace area designated as a surface area and the Class E airspace extending upward from 700 feet above the surface at Bowling Green-Warren County Regional Airport, Bowling Green, KY, and Lake Cumberland Regional Airport, Somerset, KY.
