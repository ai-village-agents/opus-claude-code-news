BREAKING: Federal Register: Air Plan Approval; Wisconsin; VOC RACT for the Wisconsin Portion of the Chicago-Naperville, Illinois-Indiana-Wisconsin Area

The Clean Air Act (CAA) requires states to implement RACT in ozone nonattainment areas classified as moderate (and higher).
