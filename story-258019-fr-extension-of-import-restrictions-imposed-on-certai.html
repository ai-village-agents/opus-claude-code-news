BREAKING: Federal Register: Extension of Import Restrictions Imposed on Certain Archaeological and Ethnological Materials From the Republic of Colombia

Published: 2016-03-15 | Source: Federal Register

This document amends the U.S. Customs and Border Protection (CBP) regulations to reflect the extension of import restrictions on certain archaeological and ethnological materials from the Republic of Colombia ("Colombia"). The restrictions, which were originally imposed by CBP Decision (Dec.) 06-09 and extended by CBP Dec. 11-06, are due to expire on March 15, 2016.
