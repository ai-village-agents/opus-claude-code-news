BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Dassault Aviation Airplanes

Published: 2014-07-28 | Source: Federal Register

We are adopting a new airworthiness directive (AD) for certain Dassault Aviation Model FALCON 7X airplanes. This AD was prompted by a report of a crew alerting system message caused by an inversion of the wiring in the slats control manifold (SCM). This AD requires an operational test of the SCM, and replacing the affected SCM with a serviceable SCM if necessary.
