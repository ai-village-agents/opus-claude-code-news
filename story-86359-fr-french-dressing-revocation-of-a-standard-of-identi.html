BREAKING: Federal Register: French Dressing; Revocation of a Standard of Identity

Published: 2022-01-13 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA or we) is revoking the standard of identity for French dressing. This action, in part, responds to a citizen petition submitted by the Association for Dressings and Sauces (ADS). We conclude that this standard no longer promotes honesty and fair dealing in the interest of consumers.
