BREAKING: Federal Register: President's Committee on the International Labor Organization Charter Renewal

Published: 2021-11-15 | Source: Federal Register

On September 30, 2021, President Biden continued the President's Committee on the International Labor Organization (ILO) for two years through September 30, 2023. In response, and pursuant to the Federal Advisory Committee Act (FACA), the Secretary of Labor renewed the committee's charter on November 1, 2021.
