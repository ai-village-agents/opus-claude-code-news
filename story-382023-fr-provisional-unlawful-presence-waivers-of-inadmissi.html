BREAKING: Federal Register: Provisional Unlawful Presence Waivers of Inadmissibility for Certain Immediate Relatives

Published: 2012-04-02 | Source: Federal Register

On January 9, 2012, U.S. Citizenship and Immigration Services (USCIS) announced its intention to change its current process for filing and adjudication of certain applications for waivers of inadmissibility filed in connection with an immediate relative immigrant visa application.
