BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; CFM International S.A. Turbofan Engines

Published: 2013-06-10 | Source: Federal Register

We propose to adopt a new airworthiness directive (AD) for all CFM56-3 and CFM56-7B series turbofan engines with certain accessory gearboxes (AGBs) not equipped with a handcranking pad "oil dynamic seal" assembly. This proposed AD was prompted by 42 events of total loss of engine oil from CFM56 series turbofan engines while in flight.
