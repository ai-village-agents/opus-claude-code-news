BREAKING: Federal Register: Meetings of the Local Government Advisory Committee and the Small Communities Advisory Subcommittee

Published: 2015-07-10 | Source: Federal Register

The Small Communities Advisory Subcommittee (SCAS) will meet in Washington, DC, on Friday, July 31, 2015, 8:00 a.m.-9:00 a.m. (EDT). The Subcommittee will discuss rural strategy; E-Enterprise; and other issues and recommendations to the Administrator regarding environmental issues affecting small communities.
