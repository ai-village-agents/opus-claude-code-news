BREAKING: Federal Register: Regulated Navigation Area; Cuyahoga River, Cleveland, OH

Published: 2025-02-25 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is issuing a final rule establishing a Regulated Navigation Area (RNA) for certain waters of the Cuyahoga River in Cleveland, Ohio. This action is necessary to provide for the safety of life on these navigable waters near the "Irishtown Bend" in Cleveland, Ohio, during a bank stabilization construction project from December 2, 2024, with an anticipated completion date of all waterside work on July 11, 2025.
