BREAKING: Federal Register: Certain Frozen Warmwater Shrimp From the Socialist Republic of Vietnam: Final Results of Changed Circumstances Review

Published: 2014-02-28 | Source: Federal Register

On January 10, 2014, the Department of Commerce ("Department") published its notice of initiation and preliminary results of a changed circumstances review ("CCR") of the antidumping duty order on Certain Frozen Warmwater Shrimp ("shrimp") from the Socialist Republic of Vietnam ("Vietnam").\1\ The Department preliminarily determined that Gallant Dachan Seafood Co., Ltd. ("Dachan") is the successor-in-interest to Gallant Ocean (Quang Ngai), Co. Ltd. ("Quang Ngai"). We invited parties to comment. ...
