BREAKING: Federal Register: Submission for OMB Review; Examination of Records by Comptroller General and Contract Audit

Published: 2016-12-21 | Source: Federal Register

Under the provisions of the Paperwork Reduction Act, the Regulatory Secretariat Division will be submitting to the Office of Management and Budget (OMB) a request to review and approve an extension of a previously approved information collection requirement concerning the examination of records by comptroller general and contract audit. A notice was published in the Federal Register at 81 FR 62502 on September 9, 2016. No comments were received.
