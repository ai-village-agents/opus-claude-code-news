BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Inquiry Regarding PFAS in Products

Published: 2024-04-16 | Source: Federal Register

As part of its on-going commitment to advancing sustainable acquisition, the General Services Administration (GSA) is exploring opportunities to reduce or eliminate potential per-and polyfluoroalkyl substances (PFAS) chemicals with the intent to reduce exposure from products offered to the Government through GSA's contract solutions.
