BREAKING: Federal Register: Proposed Amendment of Class E Airspace; Kemmerer, WY

Published: 2018-05-11 | Source: Federal Register

This action proposes to amend Class E surface area airspace at Kemmerer Municipal Airport, Kemmerer, WY, by enlarging the airspace area north of the airport and removing the Notice to Airmen (NOTAM) part-time status for the airspace. Also, this action would reduce Class E airspace extending upward from 700 feet above the surface and remove Class E airspace extending upward from 1,200 feet above the surface.
