BREAKING: Federal Register: Expansion and Capacity Building Funding: Senior Medicare Patrol Program Project Grants

Published: 2014-03-20 | Source: Federal Register

The Administration for Community Living is announcing the application deadline and a single case deviation from maximum competition for the Senior Medicare Patrol (SMP) Program project grants. The SMP program has 54 SMP project grants which are currently awarded on two different three-year cycles with 26 awarded in one year and the other 28 awarded the following year.
