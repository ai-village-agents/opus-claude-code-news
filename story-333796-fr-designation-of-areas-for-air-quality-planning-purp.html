BREAKING: Federal Register: Designation of Areas for Air Quality Planning Purposes; State of Arizona; Pinal County and Gila County; Pb

Published: 2014-09-03 | Source: Federal Register

In accordance with section 107(d)of the Clean Air Act (CAA), the Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is redesignating the Hayden area, which encompasses portions of southern Gila and eastern Pinal counties, Arizona, from "unclassifiable" to "nonattainment" for the 2008 national ambient air quality standards ("NAAQS" or "standards") for lead (Pb).
