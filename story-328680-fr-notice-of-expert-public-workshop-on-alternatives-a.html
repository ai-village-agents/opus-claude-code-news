BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Expert Public Workshop on Alternatives and Risk Reduction Approaches to Trichloroethylene (TCE)

Published: 2014-07-03 | Source: Federal Register

EPA is holding a public workshop to gather information from experts on the use of trichloroethylene (TCE) as a degreaser, availability and efficacy of safer alternatives, and possible risk reduction approaches. This workshop will examine TCE use as a degreaser in various applications, including cold cleaning, vapor degreasing, or as an aerosol.
