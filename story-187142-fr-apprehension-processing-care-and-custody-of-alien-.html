BREAKING: Federal Register: Apprehension, Processing, Care, and Custody of Alien Minors and Unaccompanied Alien Children

Published: 2019-08-23 | Source: Federal Register

This final rule amends regulations relating to the apprehension, processing, care, custody, and release of alien juveniles. The rule replaces regulations that were promulgated in 1988 in response to a lawsuit filed in 1985 against the Attorney General and the Department of Justice's legacy U.S. Immigration and Naturalization Service (INS), in Flores v.
