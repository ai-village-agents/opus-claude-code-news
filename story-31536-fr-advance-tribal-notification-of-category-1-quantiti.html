BREAKING: Federal Register: Advance Tribal Notification of Category 1 Quantities of Radioactive Material Shipments

Published: 2024-03-08 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Nuclear Regulatory Commission (NRC) is proposing to amend its regulations to require NRC and Agreement State licensees to provide advance notification to participating federally recognized Tribal governments regarding shipments of category 1 quantities of radioactive material that pass within or across their reservation boundaries.
