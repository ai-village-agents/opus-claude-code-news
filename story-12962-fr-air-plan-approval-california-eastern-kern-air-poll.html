BREAKING: Federal Register: Air Plan Approval; California; Eastern Kern Air Pollution Control District; Portland Cement Kilns

Published: 2025-06-04 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is proposing to approve a revision to the Eastern Kern Air Pollution Control District (EKAPCD or "District") portion of the California State Implementation Plan (SIP). This revision concerns emissions of oxides of nitrogen (NOX) from Portland cement kilns.
