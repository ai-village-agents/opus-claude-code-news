BREAKING: Federal Register: Double Hull Tanker Escorts on the Waters of Prince William Sound, Alaska

Published: 2014-06-13 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is finalizing the escort requirements for double hull tankers over 5,000 gross tons transporting oil in bulk on the waters of Prince William Sound, Alaska (PWS). This final rule mandates two tug escorts for these tankers. The Coast Guard previously published an interim rule on August 19, 2013.
