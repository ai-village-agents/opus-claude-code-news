BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Bombardier, Inc., Airplanes

Published: 2018-03-09 | Source: Federal Register

We propose to supersede Airworthiness Directive (AD) 2013-11- 12, which applies to certain Bombardier, Inc., Model BD-100-1A10 airplanes. AD 2013-11-12 requires inspecting for the correct serial number of a certain hydraulic system accumulator, and replacing affected hydraulic system accumulators with new or serviceable accumulators.
