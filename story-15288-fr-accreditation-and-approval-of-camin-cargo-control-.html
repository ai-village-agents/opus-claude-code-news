BREAKING: Federal Register: Accreditation and Approval of Camin Cargo Control, Inc. (Tampa, FL), as a Commercial Gauger and Laboratory

Published: 2025-07-07 | Source: Federal Register

Notice is hereby given, pursuant to CBP regulations, that Camin Cargo Control, Inc. (Tampa, FL), has been approved to gauge petroleum and certain petroleum products and accredited to test petroleum and certain petroleum products for customs purposes for the next three years as of August 22, 2024.
