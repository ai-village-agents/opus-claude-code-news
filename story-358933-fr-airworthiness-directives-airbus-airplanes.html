BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Airbus Airplanes

Published: 2013-07-01 | Source: Federal Register

We propose to adopt a new airworthiness directive (AD) for all Airbus Model A318, A319, A320, and A321 series airplanes. This proposed AD was prompted by a determination that certain special washers used in the retraction jack anchorage fitting bearing installation in the main landing gear (MLG) were incorrectly manufactured.
