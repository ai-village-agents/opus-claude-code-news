BREAKING: Federal Register: Fisheries of the Northeastern United States; Small-Mesh Multispecies Specifications

Published: 2015-04-08 | Source: Federal Register

The purpose of this action is to set the small-mesh multispecies specifications for the 2015-2017 fishing years, clarify what measures can be modified in a specifications package, and to correct the northern red hake accountability measure trigger rate. This action is necessary to implement the Council's recommended measures intended to reduce the risk of continuing overfishing of northern red hake and set catch and possession limits for the 2015-2017 fishing years.
