BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone; Unexploded Ordnance Removal, Vero Beach, FL

Published: 2015-09-01 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is establishing a safety zone on the waters of the Atlantic Ocean for the removal of unexploded ordnance located east of Vero Beach. There will be a zone approximately 2.6 nautical mile wide along the beach extending due east for approximately 2.3 nautical miles, in Vero Beach, Florida. This safety zone will be in effect from August 10th through September 4, 2015. This safety zone will only be enforced while operations are being conducted.
