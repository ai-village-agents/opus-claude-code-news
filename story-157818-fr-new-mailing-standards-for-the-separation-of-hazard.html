BREAKING: Federal Register: New Mailing Standards for the Separation of Hazardous Materials

Published: 2020-08-03 | Source: Federal Register

The Postal Service proposes to amend Publication 52, Hazardous, Restricted, and Perishable Mail (Pub 52), to incorporate requirements for mailers to separate all air-eligible hazardous material (HAZMAT) from surface only transportation HAZMAT shipments and other non-HAZMAT items when tendering mail to the Postal Service in the domestic mail.
