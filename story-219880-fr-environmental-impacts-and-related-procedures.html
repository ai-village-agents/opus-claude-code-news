BREAKING: Federal Register: Environmental Impacts and Related Procedures

Published: 2018-10-29 | Source: Federal Register

This final rule amends FHWA and FTA regulations implementing the National Environmental Policy Act (NEPA) and Section 4(f) requirements. In addition, through this final rule, FRA is joining those regulations, making them FRA's NEPA and Section 4(f) implementing regulations.
