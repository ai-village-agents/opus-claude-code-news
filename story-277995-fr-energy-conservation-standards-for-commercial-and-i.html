BREAKING: Federal Register: Energy Conservation Standards for Commercial and Industrial Fans and Blowers: Availability of Provisional Analysis Tools

Published: 2016-11-01 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Department of Energy (DOE) has completed a provisional analysis that estimates the potential economic impacts and energy savings that could result from promulgating a regulatory energy conservation standard for commercial and industrial fans and blowers ("fans"). At this time, DOE is not proposing any energy conservation standard for fans.
