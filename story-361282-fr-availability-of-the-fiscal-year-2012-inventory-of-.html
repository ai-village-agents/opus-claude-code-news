BREAKING: Federal Register: Availability of the Fiscal Year 2012 Inventory of Contracts for Services

Published: 2013-07-29 | Source: Federal Register

DoD announces the availability of the Inventory of Contracts for Services for Fiscal Year 2012 pursuant to section 2330a of title 10, United States Code. Inventory is available to the public.
