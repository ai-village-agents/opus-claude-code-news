BREAKING: Federal Register: Fisheries of the Northeastern United States; Final 2012 Spiny Dogfish Fishery Specifications

Published: 2012-05-22 | Source: Federal Register

This rule implements a catch limit, commercial quota, and possession limit for the spiny dogfish fishery for the 2012 fishing year. The action was developed by the Mid-Atlantic and New England Fishery Management Councils pursuant to the fishery specification requirements of the Spiny Dogfish Fishery Management Plan.
