BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Renewal of Federal Accounting Standards Advisory Board Charter and Establishment of Appointments Panel Charter

Published: 2023-10-13 | Source: Federal Register

Notice is hereby given that the Secretary of the Treasury, the Director of the Office of Management and Budget, and the Comptroller General of the United States (the Sponsors) have agreed to continue an advisory committee to consider and recommend accounting standards and principles for the federal government.
