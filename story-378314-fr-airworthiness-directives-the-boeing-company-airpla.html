BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; The Boeing Company Airplanes

Published: 2012-02-22 | Source: Federal Register

We propose to adopt a new airworthiness directive (AD) for certain The Boeing Company Model 777-200 and -300 series airplanes. This proposed AD was prompted by reports of fatigue cracks in the lap joints, which initiated at scribe lines that were made during production when maskant was removed from the affected skin panels.
