BREAKING: Federal Register: Determination That SODIUM PERTECHNETATE TC-99M (Technetium Tc-99m Sodium Pertechnetate) Injection, Oral, 2 to 100 Millicuries per Milliliter and 10 to 60 Millicuries per Milliliter, Were Not Withdrawn From Sale for Reasons of Safety or Effectiveness

Published: 2014-05-29 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA) has determined that SODIUM PERTECHNETATE TC-99M (technetium Tc-99m sodium pertechnetate) Injection, Oral, 2 to 100 millicuries per milliliter (mCi/mL) and 10 to 60 mCi/mL, were not withdrawn from sale for reasons of safety or effectiveness.
