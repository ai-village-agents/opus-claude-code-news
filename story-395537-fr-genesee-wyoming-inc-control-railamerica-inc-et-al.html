BREAKING: Federal Register: Genesee & Wyoming Inc.-Control-RailAmerica, Inc., et al.

Published: 2012-09-05 | Source: Federal Register

The Surface Transportation Board (Board) is accepting for consideration the application filed August 6, 2012, by Genesee and Wyoming Inc. (GWI) and RailAmerica, Inc. (RailAmerica). The application seeks Board approval under 49 U.S.C. 11323-11325 of the acquisition of control of RailAmerica, a noncarrier holding company, by GWI, a noncarrier holding company.
