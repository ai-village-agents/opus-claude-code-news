BREAKING: Federal Register: Emergency Relief Program: Proposed Guidance

Published: 2015-02-04 | Source: Federal Register

The Federal Transit Administration (FTA) has placed in the docket and on its Web site proposed guidance on FTA's Emergency Relief (ER) Program for states and transit agencies that may be affected by a declared emergency or disaster and that may seek funding under FTA's ER Program.
