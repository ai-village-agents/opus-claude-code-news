BREAKING: Federal Register: Northern States Power Company: Monticello Nuclear Generating Plant

Published: 2018-05-09 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Nuclear Regulatory Commission (NRC) has issued an exemption for the Monticello Nuclear Generating Plant, Docket No. 50- 263, in response to an April 6, 2017, request from Northern States Power Company.
