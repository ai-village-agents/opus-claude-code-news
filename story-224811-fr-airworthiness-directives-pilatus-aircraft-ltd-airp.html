BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; PILATUS AIRCRAFT LTD. Airplanes

This AD results from mandatory continuing airworthiness information (MCAI) issued by an aviation authority of another country to identify and correct an unsafe condition on an aviation product.
