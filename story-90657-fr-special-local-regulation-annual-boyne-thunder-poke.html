BREAKING: Federal Register: Special Local Regulation; Annual Boyne Thunder Poker Run, Charlevoix, MI

Published: 2022-03-09 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard will enforce the Annual Boyne Thunder Poker Run special local regulation on Round Lake and Pine River Channel, Charlevoix, MI on July 9, 2022. This action is necessary and intended to protect the safety of life and property on navigable waters prior to, during, and immediately after this event.
