BREAKING: Federal Register: Critical Incident Stress Plans

Published: 2014-03-25 | Source: Federal Register

FRA issues this final rule in accordance with a statutory mandate that the Secretary of Transportation (Secretary) require certain major railroads to develop, and submit to the Secretary for approval, critical incident stress plans that provide for appropriate support services to be offered to their employees who are affected by a "critical incident" as defined by the Secretary.
