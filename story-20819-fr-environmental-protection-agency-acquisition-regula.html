BREAKING: Federal Register: Environmental Protection Agency Acquisition Regulation (EPAAR); Special Contracting Methods; Options; Contracts

Published: 2025-09-22 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is issuing a direct final rule to revise a requirement for contracting officers. The requirement updates special contracting methods, transferring the approval authority for certain contracting actions from the Service Center Manager (SCM) to the Head of the Contracting Activity (HCA).
