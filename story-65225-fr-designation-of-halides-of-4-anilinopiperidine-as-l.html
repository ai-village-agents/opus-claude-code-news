BREAKING: Federal Register: Designation of Halides of 4-Anilinopiperidine as List I Chemicals

Published: 2023-04-14 | Source: Federal Register

The Drug Enforcement Administration is proposing to modify the listing of the list I chemical, N-phenylpiperidin-4-amine (also known as 4-anilinopiperidine; N-phenyl-4-piperidinamine; 4-AP) (hereinafter referred to as 4-anilinopiperidine), to include halides of 4- anilinopiperidine. The current listing of 4-anilinopiperidine includes its amides, its carbamates, and its salts, as list I chemicals under the Controlled Substances Act.
