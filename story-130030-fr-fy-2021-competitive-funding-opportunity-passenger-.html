BREAKING: Federal Register: FY 2021 Competitive Funding Opportunity: Passenger Ferry Grant Program

Published: 2021-08-06 | Source: Federal Register

The Federal Transit Administration (FTA) announces the opportunity to apply for $38 million in competitive grants under the Fiscal Year (FY) 2021 Passenger Ferry Grant Program (Ferry Program). Of that amount, $4 million is available only for low or zero-emission ferries or ferries using electric battery or fuel cell components and the infrastructure to support such ferries.
