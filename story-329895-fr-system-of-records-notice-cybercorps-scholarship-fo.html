BREAKING: Federal Register: System of Records Notice; CyberCorps®: Scholarship For Service (SFS)

Published: 2014-07-18 | Source: Federal Register

OPM has amended an existing system of records subject to the Privacy Act of 1974 (5 U.S.C. 552a), as amended. This amendment adds several categories of records to be collected.
