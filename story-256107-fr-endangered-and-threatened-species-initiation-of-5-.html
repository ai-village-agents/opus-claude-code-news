BREAKING: Federal Register: Endangered and Threatened Species; Initiation of 5-Year Review for Indus River Dolphin

Published: 2016-02-23 | Source: Federal Register

We, NMFS, announce a 5-year review of the Indus River dolphin (Platanista gangetica minor) under the Endangered Species Act of 1973, as amended (ESA). The purpose of these reviews is to ensure that the listing classification of a species is accurate.
