BREAKING: Federal Register: Commercial Participation in Domestic and International Events on Safety, Sustainability, and Emerging Markets in Outer Space

Published: 2020-02-25 | Source: Federal Register

Following up on the success of the Space Enterprise Summit in June 2019, the U.S. Department of State seeks commercial space industry participation in a series of domestic and international events promoting space commerce as well as best practices for safety, sustainability, and emerging markets in outer space.
