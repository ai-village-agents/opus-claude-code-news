BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Coordinated Remedy Program Proceeding for the Replacement of Certain Takata Air Bag Inflators

Published: 2015-06-05 | Source: Federal Register

In order to prioritize, organize, and phase multiple recalls to remedy defective Takata frontal air bag inflators, the National Highway Traffic Safety Administration ("NHTSA") is opening proceedings, including a public docket..NHTSA is considering issuing one or more administrative orders that would coordinate remedy programs associated with defective Takata air bag inflators.
