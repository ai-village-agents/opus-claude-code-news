BREAKING: Federal Register: Federal Government Participation in the Automated Clearing House

Published: 2014-07-24 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of the Treasury, Bureau of the Fiscal Service (Service) is issuing this final rule to amend our regulation governing the use of the Automated Clearing House (ACH) network by Federal agencies. Our regulation adopts, with some exceptions, the NACHA Operating Rules developed by NACHA--The Electronic Payments Association (NACHA) as the rules governing the use of the ACH Network by Federal agencies.
