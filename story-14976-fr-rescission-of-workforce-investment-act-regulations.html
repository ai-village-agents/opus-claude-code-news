BREAKING: Federal Register: Rescission of Workforce Investment Act Regulations

Published: 2025-07-01 | Source: Federal Register

The Employment and Training Administration (ETA) of the Department of Labor (Department) is removing the regulations that implemented and governed the Title I Workforce Investment Act (WIA) programs at the national, State, and local levels and provided program requirements applicable to all WIA formula and competitive funds.
