BREAKING: Federal Register: Health and Safety Data Reporting; Addition of Certain Chemicals

Published: 2012-12-03 | Source: Federal Register

This final rule requires manufacturers (including importers) of cadmium or cadmium compounds, including as part of an article, that have been, or are reasonably likely to be, incorporated into consumer products to report certain unpublished health and safety studies to EPA.
