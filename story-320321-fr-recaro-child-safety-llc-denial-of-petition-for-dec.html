BREAKING: Federal Register: RECARO Child Safety, LLC, Denial of Petition for Decision of Inconsequential Noncompliance

Published: 2014-03-31 | Source: Federal Register

This document denies RECARO Child Safety, LLC's (RECARO) \1\ petition for an exemption from the notification and remedy requirements of 49 U.S.C. Chapter 301 on the basis that a noncompliance with Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard (FMVSS) No. 213, Child Restraint Systems, is inconsequential to motor vehicle safety.
