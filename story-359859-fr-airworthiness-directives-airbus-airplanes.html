BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Airbus Airplanes

Published: 2013-07-10 | Source: Federal Register

We are adopting a new airworthiness directive (AD) for certain Airbus Model A318, A319, A320, and A321 series airplanes. This AD was prompted by a report of an uncommanded nose landing gear (NLG) retraction. This AD requires installing a power interruption protection circuit for the landing gear control interface unit (LGCIU).
