BREAKING: Federal Register: Medical Care in Foreign Countries and Filing for Reimbursement for Community Care Not Previously Authorized by VA

Published: 2018-01-31 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Veterans Affairs (VA) proposes to amend its medical regulations related to hospital care and medical services in foreign countries. We would amend the regulations to simplify and clarify the scope of these rules. We would address medical services provided to eligible veterans in the Republic of the Philippines, and remove regulations related to grants to the Republic of the Philippines that are no longer supported by statutory authority.
