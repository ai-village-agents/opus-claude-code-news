BREAKING: Federal Register: Meeting of the National Drinking Water Advisory Council

Published: 2014-09-16 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is announcing a meeting of the National Drinking Water Advisory Council (Council), as authorized under the Safe Drinking Water Act (SDWA). The meeting is scheduled for November 6 and 7, 2014. The Council typically considers various issues associated with drinking water protection and public water systems.
