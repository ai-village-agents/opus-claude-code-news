BREAKING: Federal Register: Publication of Choice Act Section 201 Independent Assessments and Report-Change of Website Location

Published: 2018-02-05 | Source: Federal Register

This Federal Register Notice announces a change in website location of a 2015 report entitled, "Independent Assessment of the Health Care Delivery Systems and Management Processes of the Department of Veterans Affairs (Independent Assessment Report)." The Independent Assessment Report was required by Section 201 of the Veterans Access, Choice, and Accountability Act of 2014 (Choice Act).
