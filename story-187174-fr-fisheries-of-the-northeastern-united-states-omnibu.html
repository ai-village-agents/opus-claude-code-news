BREAKING: Federal Register: Fisheries of the Northeastern United States; Omnibus Deep-Sea Coral Amendment

Published: 2019-08-26 | Source: Federal Register

NMFS announces that the New England Fishery Management Council has submitted the Omnibus Deep-Sea Coral Amendment, incorporating the Environmental Assessment and the Regulatory Flexibility Analysis, for review by the Secretary of Commerce, and is requesting comments from the public. This action would protect deep-sea corals from the impacts of commercial fishing gear on Georges Bank and in the Gulf of Maine.
