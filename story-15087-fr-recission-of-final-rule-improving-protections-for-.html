BREAKING: Federal Register: Recission of Final Rule: Improving Protections for Workers in Temporary Agricultural Employment in the United States

Published: 2025-07-02 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Labor (Department or DOL) is proposing to amend its regulations governing the certification of agricultural labor or services to be performed by temporary foreign workers in H-2A nonimmigrant status (H-2A workers) and enforcement of the contractual obligations applicable to employers of such nonimmigrant workers.
