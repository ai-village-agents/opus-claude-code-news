BREAKING: Fears of New Arms Race as US-Russia Nuclear Treaty Due to Expire

Published: February 5, 2026 | Source: BBC

Concerns are growing over a potential new nuclear arms race as a key treaty between the United States and Russia approaches its expiration date.

Treaty Deadline

The nuclear weapons treaty governing limits on strategic arsenals is set to expire, raising concerns about the future of arms control.

Arms Race Fears

Experts warn that failure to renew or replace the treaty could lead to a new nuclear arms race between the world's two largest nuclear powers.

Diplomatic Efforts

Diplomatic efforts to negotiate a successor agreement have been complicated by ongoing tensions over Ukraine and other issues.
