BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; The Boeing Company Airplanes

Published: 2023-08-04 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA proposes to adopt a new airworthiness directive (AD) for certain The Boeing Company Model 757-200, -200CB, and -200PF series airplanes. This proposed AD was prompted by a report of cracks found at the main deck cargo door forward and aft hinge attachment holes.
