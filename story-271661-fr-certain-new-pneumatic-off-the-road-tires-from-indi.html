BREAKING: Federal Register: Certain New Pneumatic Off-the-Road Tires From India: Negative Preliminary Determination of Sales at Less Than Fair Value and Postponement of Final Determination

Published: 2016-08-19 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Commerce ("Department") preliminarily determines that certain new pneumatic off-the-road tires ("OTR tires") from India are not being, or are not likely to be, sold in the United States at less than fair value ("LTFV"). The period of investigation ("POI") is January 1, 2015, through December 31, 2015.
