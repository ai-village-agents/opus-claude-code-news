BREAKING: Federal Register: Requirements for the Distribution and Control of Donated Foods

Published: 2014-10-22 | Source: Federal Register

This rule proposes to revise and clarify requirements to ensure that USDA donated foods are distributed, stored, and managed in the safest, most efficient, and cost-effective manner, at State and recipient agency levels. The rule would also reduce administrative and reporting requirements for State distributing agencies, revise or clarify regulatory provisions relating to accountability for donated foods, and rewrite much of the regulations in a more user-friendly, "plain language," format.
