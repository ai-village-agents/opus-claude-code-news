BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Inventory Completion: Arkansas Archeological Survey, Fayetteville, AR; Correction

Published: 2015-04-28 | Source: Federal Register

The Arkansas Archeological Survey has corrected an inventory of human remains and associated funerary objects, published in a Notice of Inventory Completion in the Federal Register on December 22, 2014. This notice corrects the number of associated funerary objects listed in that notice.
