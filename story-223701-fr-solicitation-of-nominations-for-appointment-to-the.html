BREAKING: Federal Register: Solicitation of Nominations for Appointment to the Drone Advisory Committee (DAC)

Published: 2018-12-20 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA is issuing this notice to solicit nominations for qualified candidates to serve on the DAC. This notice seeks to fill vacancies on the DAC and does not affect the status of current DAC members. The DAC is an advisory committee established under DOT's authority, in accordance with the provisions of the Federal Advisory Committee Act (FACA) as amended.
