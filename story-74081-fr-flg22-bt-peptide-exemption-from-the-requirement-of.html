BREAKING: Federal Register: Flg22-Bt Peptide; Exemption From the Requirement of a Tolerance

Published: 2023-08-09 | Source: Federal Register

This regulation establishes an exemption from the requirement of a tolerance for residues of Flg22-Bt Peptide in or on all food commodities when used as a plant regulator and inducer of local and systemic resistance in accordance with label directions and good agricultural practices.
