BREAKING: Federal Register: Golden Parachute and Indemnification Payments

Published: 2018-08-28 | Source: Federal Register

The Federal Housing Finance Agency (FHFA) is proposing to amend its rule on golden parachute payments to better align the rule with areas of FHFA's supervisory concern and reduce administrative and compliance burdens.
