BREAKING: Federal Register: Privacy Act of 1974; System of Records

Published: 2023-08-09 | Source: Federal Register

The Federal Energy Regulatory Commission (FERC) is publishing notice of a new FERC system of records, titled "FERC-65--Agencywide Notification System." This system of records covers emergency contact information collection of current FERC employees, contractors and interns to be used in the event of an emergency.
