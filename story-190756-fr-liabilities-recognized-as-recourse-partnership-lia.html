BREAKING: Federal Register: Liabilities Recognized as Recourse Partnership Liabilities Under Section 752

Published: 2019-10-09 | Source: Federal Register

This document contains final regulations addressing when certain obligations to restore a deficit balance in a partner's capital account are disregarded under section 704 of the Internal Revenue Code (Code), when partnership liabilities are treated as recourse liabilities under section 752, and how bottom dollar payment obligations are treated under section 752.
