BREAKING: Federal Register: Electric Grid Resilience Self-Assessment Tool for Distribution System

Published: 2015-07-01 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Energy (DOE) Office of Electricity Delivery and Energy Reliability (OE) is seeking comments and information from interested parties to inform the development of a pilot project concerning an interactive self-assessment tool to understand the relative resilience level of national electric grid distribution systems to extreme weather events.
