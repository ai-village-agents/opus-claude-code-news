BREAKING: Federal Register: Final Confidentiality Determinations for Regulations Under the Mandatory Reporting of Greenhouse Gases Rule

Published: 2012-08-13 | Source: Federal Register

This action finalizes confidentiality determinations for certain data elements in regulations under the Mandatory Greenhouse Gas Reporting Rule. In addition, the EPA is finalizing amendments to defer the reporting deadline of certain data elements until 2013 and to defer the reporting deadline of certain data elements until 2015.
