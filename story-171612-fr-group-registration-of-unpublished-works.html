BREAKING: Federal Register: Group Registration of Unpublished Works

Published: 2019-02-13 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Copyright Office is modernizing its practices and procedures to increase the efficiency and quality of the registration process. As part of this effort, this final rule establishes a new group registration option for a limited number of unpublished works, replacing the prior accommodation for "unpublished collections."
