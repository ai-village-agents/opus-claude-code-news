BREAKING: Federal Register: Improvements for Heavy-Duty Engine and Vehicle Test Procedures, and Other Technical Amendments

Published: 2020-05-12 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is proposing changes to the test procedures for heavy-duty engines and vehicles to improve accuracy and reduce testing burden. EPA is also proposing other regulatory amendments concerning light-duty vehicles, heavy-duty vehicles, highway motorcycles, locomotives, marine engines, other nonroad engines and vehicles, stationary engines.
