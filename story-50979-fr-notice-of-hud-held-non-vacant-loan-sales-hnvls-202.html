BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of HUD-Held Non Vacant Loan Sales (HNVLS 2025-1)

Published: 2024-10-28 | Source: Federal Register

This notice announces HUD's intention to competitively offer approximately 1,180 home equity conversion mortgages (HECM, or reverse mortgage loans) secured by occupied properties with a loan balance of approximately $500 million. The sale will consist of due and payable Secretary-held reverse mortgage loans.
