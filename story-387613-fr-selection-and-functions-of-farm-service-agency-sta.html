BREAKING: Federal Register: Selection and Functions of Farm Service Agency State and County Committees

Published: 2012-06-05 | Source: Federal Register

This rule amends the regulations governing the selection and functions of Farm Service Agency (FSA) State and county committees. The amendments are needed to make the regulations consistent with the Farm Security and Rural Investment Act of 2002 (the 2002 Farm Bill) and the Food, Conservation, and Energy Act of 2008 (the 2008 Farm Bill).
