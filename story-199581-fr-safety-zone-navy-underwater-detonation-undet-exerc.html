BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone; Navy Underwater Detonation (UNDET) Exercise, Apra Outer Harbor, GU

Published: 2018-02-09 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard proposes to establish two recurring safety zones for navigable waters of Apra Outer Harbor and Piti, Guam. The safety zones will encompass sites designated for U.S. Navy underwater detonation (UNDET) exercises. The Coast Guard believes this safety zone regulation is necessary to protect the public and exercise participants within the affected area from possible safety hazards associated with these exercises.
