BREAKING: Federal Register: Developing a Privacy Framework

Published: 2018-12-17 | Source: Federal Register

The National Institute of Standards and Technology (NIST) extends the period for submitting written comments on the request for information (RFI) entitled "Developing a Privacy Framework," published on November 14, 2018. The public comment period was originally to close on December 31, 2018; the comment period is extended to now end on January 14, 2019.
