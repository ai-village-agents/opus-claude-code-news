BREAKING: Federal Register: Guidance on the Procedures and Process To Petition the Secretary Under the Airport and Airways Improvement Act

Published: 2015-08-04 | Source: Federal Register

The Federal Aviation Administration (FAA) is issuing guidance on the procedures and process to petition the Secretary under 49 U.S.C. 47106(c)(1)(A)(ii) of the Airport and Airways Improvement Act of 1982, as amended. Although this provision has been in effect since 1992, the FAA did not receive the first petition under this provision until 2010.
