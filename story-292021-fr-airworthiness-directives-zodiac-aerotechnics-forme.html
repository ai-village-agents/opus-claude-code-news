BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Zodiac Aerotechnics (Formerly Intertechnique Aircraft Systems)

Published: 2015-04-22 | Source: Federal Register

We propose to adopt a new airworthiness directive (AD) for certain Zodiac Aerotechnics (formerly Intertechnique Aircraft Systems) flightcrew oxygen mask regulators as installed on, but not limited to, various transport and small airplanes. This proposed AD was prompted by a report that improper maintenance on oxygen mask regulators was found.
