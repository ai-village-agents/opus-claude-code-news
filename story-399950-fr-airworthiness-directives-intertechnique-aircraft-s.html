BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Intertechnique Aircraft Systems

Published: 2012-10-25 | Source: Federal Register

We propose to adopt a new airworthiness directive (AD) that applies to certain Intertechnique Aircraft Systems oxygen mask regulators. This proposed AD was prompted by a report of a malfunctioning mask having an inflatable harness with a high premature rupture rate due to defective silicon. This proposed AD would require inspecting and replacing defective harnesses with new or modified serviceable units.
