BREAKING: Federal Register: Federally Mandated Exclusions From Income-Updated Listing

Published: 2024-01-31 | Source: Federal Register

HUD's regulations provide for HUD to periodically publish in the Federal Register a notice that lists sources of income specifically excluded by any Federal statute from consideration as income for purposes of determining eligibility or benefits in a HUD program. HUD last published a notice that listed federally mandated exclusions from consideration of income on May 20, 2014.
