BREAKING: Federal Register: Submission of Warning Plans for Cigars; Guidance for Industry; Availability

Published: 2016-12-29 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA) is announcing the availability of a guidance for industry entitled "Submission of Warning Plans for Cigars." The guidance will help those involved in the manufacture, distribution, and sale of cigars in the United States understand the new cigar warning plan requirements under FDA's final rule deeming these products to be subject to the tobacco product authorities in the Federal Food, Drug, and Cosmetic Act (the FD&C Act).
