BREAKING: Federal Register: Designation of the Financial Industry Regulatory Authority To Administer Professional Qualification Tests for Associated Persons of Registered Municipal Advisors

Published: 2015-08-21 | Source: Federal Register

The Commission is designating the Financial Industry Regulatory Authority ("FINRA") to administer professional qualification tests for associated persons of registered municipal advisors.
