BREAKING: Federal Register: Air Plan Approval; Nebraska; Lincoln-Lancaster County Health Department (LLCHD); Reopening of the Comment Period and Availability of Supplemental Information

Published: 2020-03-05 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is announcing a reopening of the comment period for the proposed rule "Air Plan Approval; Nebraska; Lincoln-Lancaster County Health Department (LLCHD)" to provide an additional 30 days for public comment. This additional opportunity to submit comments is provided due to two comments the EPA received including one noting that the State submission was not provided in the docket to allow the reviewer the ability to fully evaluate EPA's proposed action.
