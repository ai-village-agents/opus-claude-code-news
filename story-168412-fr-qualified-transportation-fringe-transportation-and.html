BREAKING: Federal Register: Qualified Transportation Fringe, Transportation and Commuting Expenses Under Section 274

Published: 2020-12-16 | Source: Federal Register

This document contains final regulations to implement legislative changes to section 274 of the Internal Revenue Code (Code) effective for taxable years beginning after December 31, 2017. Specifically, the final regulations address the elimination of the deduction under section 274 for expenses related to certain transportation and commuting benefits provided by employers to their employees.
