BREAKING: Federal Register: Public Availability of the Department of Energy's Service Contract Inventory

Published: 2021-08-23 | Source: Federal Register

In accordance with Division C of the Consolidated Appropriations Act of 2010, the Department of Energy (DOE) is publishing this notice to advise the public on the availability of the FY 2019 Service Contract Inventory and Analysis Plan and FY 2018 Service Contract Inventory Analysis. This inventory provides information on service contract actions over $150,000 that DOE completed in FY 2019.
