BREAKING: Federal Register: 504 Loan Program Rural Initiative-Extension of Pilot Program

Published: 2019-08-28 | Source: Federal Register

The 504 Loan Program Rural Initiative Pilot Program (504 Rural Pilot) authorizes Certified Development Companies (CDCs) to make 504 loans for projects in rural counties located in their Small Business Administration (SBA) Region during the two-year period beginning July 19, 2018 and ending July 20, 2020. The SBA announces the extension of the 504 Rural Pilot to September 30, 2021.
