BREAKING: Federal Register: Funding Availability Under Supportive Services for Veteran Families Program

Published: 2014-02-25 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Veterans Affairs (VA) published a Notice of Funding Availability in the Federal Register on January 14, 2014, that contained an error. Specifically, the table on page 2539 mistakenly listed one of the priority 1 Continuums of Care (CoCs) as list "KS-501 as Kansas City/Independence/Lee's Summit/Jackson County CoC."
