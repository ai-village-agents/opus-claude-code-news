BREAKING: Federal Register: Quick Path Information Disclosure Statement (QPIDS) Pilot Program

Published: 2012-05-10 | Source: Federal Register

The United States Patent and Trademark Office (USPTO) is implementing a pilot program intended to reduce pendency and applicant costs when an information disclosure statement (IDS) is filed after payment of the issue fee. This pilot program will permit an examiner to consider an IDS after payment of the issue fee without the need to reopen prosecution, effectively obviating the need to pursue a request for continued examination (RCE).
