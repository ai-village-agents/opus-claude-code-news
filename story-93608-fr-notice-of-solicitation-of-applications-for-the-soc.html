BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Solicitation of Applications for the Socially Disadvantaged Groups Grant

Published: 2022-04-14 | Source: Federal Register

This notice announces that the Rural Business-Cooperative Service (Agency) is inviting fiscal year (FY) 2022 applications for the Socially Disadvantaged Groups Grant (SDGG) program, subject to the availability of funding. This notice is being issued in order to allow applicants sufficient time to leverage financing, prepare and submit their applications, and give the Agency time to process applications within FY 2022.
