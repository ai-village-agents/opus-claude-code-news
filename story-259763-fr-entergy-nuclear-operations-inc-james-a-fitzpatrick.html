BREAKING: Federal Register: Entergy Nuclear Operations, Inc.; James A. FitzPatrick Nuclear Power Plant

Published: 2016-04-04 | Source: Federal Register

(Entergy, the licensee), to withdraw its application dated August 20, 2015, for a proposed amendment to Renewed Facility Operating License No. DPR-59, for the James A. FitzPatrick Nuclear Power Plant (JAF), located in Oswego County, New York.
