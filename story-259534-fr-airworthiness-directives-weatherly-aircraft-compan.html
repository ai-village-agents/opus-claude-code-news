BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Weatherly Aircraft Company Airplanes

Published: 2016-03-31 | Source: Federal Register

We are adopting a new airworthiness directive (AD) for all Weatherly Aircraft Company Models 201, 201A, 201B, 201C, 620, 620A, 620B, 620B-TG, and 620TP airplanes. This AD requires visually inspecting the center and outer wing front spar lower hinge fittings for cracks and corrosion and taking all necessary corrective actions.
