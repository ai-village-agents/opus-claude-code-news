BREAKING: Federal Register: Pipeline Safety: Safety of Gas Distribution Pipelines and Other Pipeline Safety Initiatives

Published: 2023-09-07 | Source: Federal Register

PHMSA proposes revisions to the pipeline safety regulations to require operators of gas distribution pipelines to update their distribution integrity management programs (DIMP), emergency response plans, operations and maintenance manuals, and other safety practices. These proposals implement provisions of the Leonel Rondon Pipeline Safety Act--part of the Protecting our Infrastructure of Pipelines and Enhancing Safety Act of 2020--and a National Transportation Safety Board (NTSB) recommendation...
