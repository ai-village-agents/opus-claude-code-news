BREAKING: Federal Register: Oil and Gas Decommissioning Activities on the Pacific Outer Continental Shelf

Published: 2023-12-13 | Source: Federal Register

The Bureau of Safety and Environmental Enforcement (BSEE) announces the availability of the Record of Decision for the Final Programmatic Environmental Impact Statement (PEIS) for Oil and Gas Decommissioning Activities on the Pacific Outer Continental Shelf. The Record of Decision identifies BSEE's selected alternative for the Final PEIS.
