BREAKING: Federal Register: Proposed Prospective Purchaser Agreement for the Manual Transmission of Muncie Site in Muncie, Indiana

Published: 2018-04-17 | Source: Federal Register

In accordance with the Prospective Purchaser Agreement, notice is hereby given of a proposed administrative settlement concerning the Manual Transmission of Muncie Site in Muncie, Indiana with the following Settling Party: Fourteen91 Loft, LLC. The settlement requires the Settling Party to, if necessary, execute and record a Declaration of Restrictive Covenant; provide access to the Site and exercise due care with respect to existing contamination.
