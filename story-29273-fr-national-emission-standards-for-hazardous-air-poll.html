BREAKING: Federal Register: National Emission Standards for Hazardous Air Pollutants: Lime Manufacturing Plants Amendments

Published: 2024-02-09 | Source: Federal Register

This action supplements our proposed amendments to the National Emission Standards for Hazardous Air Pollutants for Lime Manufacturing Plants (Lime Manufacturing NESHAP) published in the Federal Register on January 5, 2023. In that action, the Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) proposed hazardous air pollutant (HAP) emissions standards for the following pollutants: hydrogen chloride (HCl), mercury, total hydrocarbon (THC) as a surrogate for organic HAP, and dioxin/furans (D/F).
