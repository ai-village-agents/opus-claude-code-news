BREAKING: Wegovy Maker Warns Price Cuts 'Painful' as Shares Plunge

Published: February 4, 2026 | Source: BBC

Novo Nordisk, the maker of the popular weight-loss drug Wegovy, has warned that potential price cuts would be "painful" for the company, sending its shares plunging.

Share Price Drop

Investors reacted negatively to the company's warnings about pricing pressure, with shares falling sharply in trading.

Pricing Pressure

The pharmaceutical giant faces growing pressure to lower the cost of its blockbuster obesity medication amid concerns about drug affordability.

Market Impact

The development has implications for the broader weight-loss drug market as competitors also face scrutiny over pricing.
