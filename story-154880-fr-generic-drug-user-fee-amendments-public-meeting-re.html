BREAKING: Federal Register: Generic Drug User Fee Amendments; Public Meeting; Request for Comments

Published: 2020-06-26 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA, the Agency, or we) is hosting a virtual public meeting entitled "Generic Drug User Fee Amendments (GDUFA) of 2017." At the end of September 2022, new legislation will be required for FDA to continue to collect generic drug user fees for future fiscal years.
