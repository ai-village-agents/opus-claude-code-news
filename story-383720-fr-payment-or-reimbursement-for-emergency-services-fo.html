BREAKING: Federal Register: Payment or Reimbursement for Emergency Services for Nonservice-Connected Conditions in Non-VA Facilities

Published: 2012-04-20 | Source: Federal Register

This document amends the Department of Veterans Affairs (VA) "Payment or Reimbursement for Emergency Services for Nonservice- Connected Conditions in Non-VA Facilities" regulations to conform with a statutory change that expanded veterans' eligibility for reimbursement.
