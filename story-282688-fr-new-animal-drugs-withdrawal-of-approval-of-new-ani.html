BREAKING: Federal Register: New Animal Drugs; Withdrawal of Approval of New Animal Drug Applications

Published: 2016-12-27 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA) is withdrawing approval of 11 new animal drug applications (NADAs) and 4 abbreviated new animal drug applications (ANADAs). These withdrawals of approval of NADAs and ANADAs for antimicrobial drugs of importance to human medicine that are administered to food-producing animals in medicated feed are being made because the products are no longer being manufactured or marketed.
