BREAKING: Federal Register: Nomenclature Change; Technical Amendment

Published: 2023-09-13 | Source: Federal Register

This rule updates obsolete nomenclature in the regulations under the Packers and Stockyards Act, 1921 (Act). Obsolete references to the Grain Inspection, Packers and Stockyards Administration (GIPSA) are changed to the Agricultural Marketing Service (AMS) of the U.S. Department of Agriculture (USDA).
