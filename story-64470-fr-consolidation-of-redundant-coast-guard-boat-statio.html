BREAKING: Federal Register: Consolidation of Redundant Coast Guard Boat Stations

Published: 2023-04-06 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard requests public comments on the planned consolidation of three redundant, seasonally operated Coast Guard boat stations and the seasonalization of one Coast Guard boat station. As modern boat operating speeds rise and navigation technology improves, the Coast Guard can respond to incidents with multiple units significantly faster than when these boat stations were first established.
