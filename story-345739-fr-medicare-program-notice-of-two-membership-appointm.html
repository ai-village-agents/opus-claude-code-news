BREAKING: Federal Register: Medicare Program: Notice of Two Membership Appointments to the Advisory Panel on Hospital Outpatient Payment

Published: 2013-02-01 | Source: Federal Register

This notice announces two new membership appointments to the Advisory Panel on Hospital Outpatient Payment (HOP, the Panel). The two new appointments to the Panel will each serve a 4-year period. The new members will have terms that begin on February 1, 2013 and continue through January 31, 2017.
