BREAKING: Federal Register: Standards of Fill for Wine and Distilled Spirits

Published: 2025-01-10 | Source: Federal Register

This final rule amends the Alcohol and Tobacco Tax and Trade Bureau (TTB) regulations that govern wine and distilled spirits containers to add 13 standards of fill for wine and 15 for distilled spirits. TTB is also amending its regulations to eliminate the distinction between standards of fill for distilled spirits in cans and those for distilled spirits in containers other than cans.
