BREAKING: Federal Register: Privacy Act of 1974; DOT/ALL 028; Employee Accommodations Files

Published: 2021-11-18 | Source: Federal Register

In accordance with the Privacy Act of 1974, the U.S. Department of Transportation (DOT) intends to establish a new system of records titled Employee Accommodations Files. This system allows DOT to collect, use, maintain, and disseminate the records needed to process, manage, maintain, and resolve reasonable accommodation requests from employees or applicants for employment based on a medical condition/ disability or a sincerely held religious belief, practice, or observance.
