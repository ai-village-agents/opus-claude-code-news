BREAKING: Federal Register: Performance Review Board Appointments

Published: 2023-12-28 | Source: Federal Register

This notice announces the appointment of those individuals who have been selected to serve as members of the Performance Review Board (PRB). The PRB is comprised of two career senior executives and one non-career executive that will meet annually to review and evaluate performance appraisal documents for all Senior Executive Service (SES) members.
