BREAKING: Federal Register: Proposed Establishment of Class E Airspace and Amendment of Class D and E Airspace; Kaunakakai, HI

Published: 2017-07-28 | Source: Federal Register

This action proposes changes to airspace designations at Molokai Airport, Kaunakakai, HI. The FAA proposes to establish an area of Class E airspace designated as a surface area; modify Class E airspace designated as an extension to a Class D or E surface area; and modify Class E airspace extending upward from 700 feet above the surface.
