BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; BAE Systems (Operations) Limited Airplanes

Published: 2021-03-15 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA proposes to adopt a new airworthiness directive (AD) for all BAE Systems (Operations) Limited Model BAe 146 and Model Avro 146-RJ series airplanes. This proposed AD was prompted by a report indicating that during a routine battery capacity check on the emergency light power units, the printed circuit boards (PCBs) for certain power units were found to show signs of burning.
