BREAKING: Federal Register: Request for Nominations for a Voting Consumer Representative on the Pharmacy Compounding Advisory Committee and Request for Notification From Consumer Organizations Interested in Participating in the Selection Process for Nominations for a Voting Consumer Representative on the Pharmacy Compounding Advisory Committee

Published: 2014-01-13 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA) is requesting that any consumer organizations interested in participating in the selection of a voting consumer representative to serve on the Pharmacy Compounding Advisory Committee notify FDA in writing. FDA is also requesting nominations for a voting consumer representative to serve on the Pharmacy Compounding Advisory Committee for which a vacancy currently exists.
