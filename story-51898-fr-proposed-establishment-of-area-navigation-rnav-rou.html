BREAKING: Federal Register: Proposed Establishment of Area Navigation (RNAV) Routes Q-162 and Q-166; Southwest United States.

Published: 2024-11-07 | Source: Federal Register

This action proposes to establish Area Navigation (RNAV) routes Q-162 and Q-166 in the Southwest United States. The proposed new RNAV routes would provide alternative routing for air traffic travelling between southwest Arizona and western Texas in response to severe weather events during the spring and summer months.
