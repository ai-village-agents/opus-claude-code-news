BREAKING: Federal Register: Bulletin 2023-01: Unfair Billing and Collection Practices After Bankruptcy Discharges of Certain Student Loan Debts

Published: 2023-03-23 | Source: Federal Register

The Consumer Financial Protection Bureau (CFPB) is issuing this Compliance Bulletin and Policy Guidance (Bulletin) to address the treatment of certain private student loans (student loans) following bankruptcy discharge. In order to secure a discharge of "qualified education loans" in bankruptcy, borrowers must demonstrate that the loans would impose an undue hardship if not discharged.
