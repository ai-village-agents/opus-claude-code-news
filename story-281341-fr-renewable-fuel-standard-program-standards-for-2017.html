BREAKING: Federal Register: Renewable Fuel Standard Program: Standards for 2017 and Biomass-Based Diesel Volume for 2018

Published: 2016-12-12 | Source: Federal Register

Under section 211 of the Clean Air Act, the Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is required to set renewable fuel percentage standards every year. This action establishes the annual percentage standards for cellulosic biofuel, biomass-based diesel, advanced biofuel, and total renewable fuel that apply to all motor vehicle gasoline and diesel produced or imported in the year 2017.
