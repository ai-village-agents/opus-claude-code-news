BREAKING: Federal Register: Amendment of Class D and Class E Airspace; Eastover, SC and Sumter, SC

Published: 2018-08-30 | Source: Federal Register

This action amends Class D airspace and Class E airspace extending upward from 700 feet above the surface at Mc Entire Joint National Guard Base (JNGB), Eastover, SC, to accommodate airspace reconfiguration due to the decommissioning of the Mc Entire non- directional radio beacon (NDB) and cancellation of the NDB approach.
