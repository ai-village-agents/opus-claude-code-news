BREAKING: Federal Register: Yemen Sanctions Regulations

Published: 2020-10-29 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of the Treasury's Office of Foreign Assets Control (OFAC) is amending the Yemen Sanctions Regulations and reissuing them in their entirety to further implement Executive Order 13611 of May 16, 2012, "Blocking Property of Persons Threatening the Peace, Security, or Stability of Yemen."
