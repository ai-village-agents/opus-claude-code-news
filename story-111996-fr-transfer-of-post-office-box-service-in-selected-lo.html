BREAKING: Federal Register: Transfer of Post Office Box Service in Selected Locations to the Competitive Product List

Published: 2022-12-09 | Source: Federal Register

The Postal Service hereby provides notice that 291 locations providing Post Office Box service will be reassigned from their market- dominant fee groups to competitive fee groups.
