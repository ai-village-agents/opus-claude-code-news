BREAKING: Federal Register: Air Plan Approval; Minnesota; Exempt Source SIP Revision

Published: 2025-11-20 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is approving a revision to the Minnesota State Implementation Plan (SIP) with updates to Minnesota's air program rules. The Minnesota Pollution Control Agency (MPCA) submitted the request to EPA on October 1, 2021. The revision to Minnesota's air quality rules reflects changes that have occurred since July 2020.
