BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone; Lower Mississippi River, Mile Markers 230.4 to 215, Baton Rouge, LA

Published: 2018-08-24 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is establishing a temporary safety zone for all navigable waters of the Lower Mississippi River from mile marker (MM) 230.4 to MM 215, above Head of Passes. This safety zone is necessary to protect persons, vessels, and the marine environment from potential safety hazards associated with the Big River Regional 2018 Paddle Board Race.
