BREAKING: Federal Register: Privacy Act of 1974; System of Records

Published: 2019-04-01 | Source: Federal Register

Pursuant to the Privacy Act of 1974, the Federal Retirement Thrift Investment Board (FRTIB) is proposing to modify its system of records for fraud and forgery records. Records contained in this system are used to investigate potential or actual fraud against TSP participant or beneficiary accounts.
