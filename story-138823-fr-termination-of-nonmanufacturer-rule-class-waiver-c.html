BREAKING: Federal Register: Termination of Nonmanufacturer Rule Class Waiver; Correction Notice

Published: 2021-11-26 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Small Business Administration published a document in the Federal Register on November 16, 2021, concerning requests for comments on a proposed termination of a Nonmanufacturer Rule class waiver for radiology equipment. That notice did not include the closing date for submitting comments.
