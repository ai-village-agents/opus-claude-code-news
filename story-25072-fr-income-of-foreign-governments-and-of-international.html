BREAKING: Federal Register: Income of Foreign Governments and of International Organizations

Published: 2025-12-15 | Source: Federal Register

This document contains proposed regulations relating to the taxation of the income of foreign governments from investments in the United States. In particular, these proposed regulations provide guidance for determining when an acquisition of debt by a foreign government is considered to be commercial activity, and when a foreign government has effective control of an entity engaged in commercial activities.
