BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Inventory Completion: Southern Oregon Historical Society, Medford, OR; Correction

Published: 2012-12-18 | Source: Federal Register

The Southern Oregon Historical Society has corrected an inventory of human remains and associated funerary objects published in a Notice of Inventory Completion in the Federal Register on August 24, 2012. This notice corrects the list of tribes consulted and the tribes eligible to receive disposition of the human remains and associated funerary objects listed in the earlier notice.
