BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Kidde Aerospace & Defense

Published: 2020-10-15 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA is adopting a new airworthiness directive (AD) for Kidde Aerospace & Defense cargo fire extinguisher halon bottles installed on various transport category airplanes. This AD was prompted by a report indicating that certain cargo fire extinguisher halon bottles installed in the cargo compartment had low charge pressure.
