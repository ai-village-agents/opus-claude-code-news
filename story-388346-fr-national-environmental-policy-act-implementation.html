BREAKING: Federal Register: National Environmental Policy Act Implementation

Published: 2012-06-13 | Source: Federal Register

FRA is publishing this notice to request comments on FRA's proposed additions to the list of categorical exclusions (CEs) contained in FRA's Procedures for Considering Environmental Impacts (Environmental Procedures).
