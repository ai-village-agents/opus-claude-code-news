BREAKING: Federal Register: Guidelines for Home Energy Professionals: Standard Work Specifications for Single Family Energy Upgrades

Published: 2012-03-29 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Department of Energy Office of Energy Efficiency and Renewable Energy (EERE) requests comments on the revised version of the Guidelines for Home Energy Professionals: Standard Work Specifications for Single Family Energy Upgrades. This document is a set of work specifications applicable to energy efficiency retrofits of single family homes.
