BREAKING: Federal Register: AES Filing Compliance Date for Hazardous Waste Exports

Published: 2017-08-29 | Source: Federal Register

This action announces that the Automated Export System (AES) filing compliance date for export shipments of hazardous waste and certain other materials is December 31, 2017.
