BREAKING: Federal Register: 2017 Safer Choice Partner of the Year Awards Program

Published: 2016-10-11 | Source: Federal Register

EPA is seeking applications for the 2017 Safer Choice Partner of the Year Awards. In 2015, EPA developed the Partner of the Year Awards to recognize Safer Choice stakeholders who have advanced the goals of the Pollution Prevention Act and the Safer Choice program by reducing pollution at its source through safer chemistry.
