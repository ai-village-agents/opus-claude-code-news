BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Bell Helicopter Textron, Inc. (Bell) Model Helicopters

Published: 2013-04-25 | Source: Federal Register

We propose to supersede an existing revised airworthiness directive (AD) for all Bell Model 204B and certain serial-numbered Model 205A-1 helicopters with a certain tail rotor pitch control chain (chain) installed. The existing AD requires visually inspecting the chain to detect a crack in the link segments and, for affected Model 205A-1 helicopters, replacing the tail rotor chain and cable control system with a push-pull control system.
