BREAKING: Federal Register: DoD Discharge Appeal Review Board

Published: 2024-11-29 | Source: Federal Register

This interim final rule implements Section 523 of the National Defense Authorization Act (NDAA) for Fiscal Year (FY) 2020, which requires the DoD to provide Service members and their authorized representatives with one final review of requests for an upgrade in the characterization of a discharge or dismissal.
