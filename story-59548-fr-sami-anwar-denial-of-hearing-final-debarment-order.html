BREAKING: Federal Register: Sami Anwar; Denial of Hearing; Final Debarment Order

Published: 2023-02-02 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA or the Agency) is denying a request for a hearing submitted by Sami Anwar (Anwar) and is issuing an order under the Federal Food, Drug, and Cosmetic Act (the FD&C Act) permanently debarring Anwar from providing services in any capacity to a person having an approved or pending drug product application.
