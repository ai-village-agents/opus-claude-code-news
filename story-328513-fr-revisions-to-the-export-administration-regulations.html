BREAKING: Federal Register: Revisions to the Export Administration Regulations (EAR): Control of Military Electronic Equipment and Other Items the President Determines No Longer Warrant Control Under the United States Munitions List (USML)

Published: 2014-07-01 | Source: Federal Register

This final rule adds to the Commerce Control List military electronics, technology and software for certain wing folding systems, certain superconducting and cryogenic equipment, and related items the President determines no longer warrant control under the United States Munitions List (USML). This also amends ECCNs 7A006 and 7A106 to apply the "missile technology" reason for control only to items in those ECCNs on the Missile Technology Control Regime (MTCR) Annex.
