BREAKING: Federal Register: Accounting and Reporting Treatment of Certain Renewable Energy Assets

Published: 2022-10-03 | Source: Federal Register

The Federal Energy Regulatory Commission is issuing a notice of proposed rulemaking proposing reforms to the Uniform System of Accounts (USofA) for public utilities and licensees to include new accounts for wind, solar, and other non-hydro renewable assets; create a new functional class for energy storage accounts; codify the accounting treatment of renewable energy credits; and create new accounts within existing functions for hardware, software, and communication equipment.
