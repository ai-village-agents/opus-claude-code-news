BREAKING: Federal Register: Order Suspending General License Authority To Export Special Nuclear Material, Source Material, and Deuterium for Nuclear End Use to the People's Republic of China

Published: 2023-08-14 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Nuclear Regulatory Commission (NRC) is issuing an Order suspending the general license authority under NRC regulations to export special nuclear material, source material, and deuterium for nuclear end use to the People's Republic of China.
