BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Temporary Closure of the Ash Springs Recreation Site on Public Lands in Lincoln County, NV

Published: 2014-06-06 | Source: Federal Register

This is to notify the public that a temporary closure is in effect on public lands at the Ash Springs Recreation Site administered by the Bureau of Land Management (BLM) Ely District, Caliente Field Office as authorized under the provisions of the Federal Land Policy and Management Act of 1976 and pursuant to BLM regulations for the protection of persons, property, and public lands and resources.
