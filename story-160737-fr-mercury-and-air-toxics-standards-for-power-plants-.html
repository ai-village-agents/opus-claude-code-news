BREAKING: Federal Register: Mercury and Air Toxics Standards for Power Plants Electronic Reporting Revisions

Published: 2020-09-09 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is finalizing amendments to the electronic reporting requirements for the National Emission Standards for Hazardous Air Pollutants: Coal- and Oil-Fired Electric Utility Steam Generating Units (also known as the Mercury and Air Toxics Standards (MATS)).
