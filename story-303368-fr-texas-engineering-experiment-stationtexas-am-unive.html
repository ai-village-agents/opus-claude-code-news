BREAKING: Federal Register: Texas Engineering Experiment Station/Texas A&M University System Nuclear Science Center Reactor

Published: 2015-09-03 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Nuclear Regulatory Commission (NRC) is considering renewal of Facility Operating License No. R-83, held by the Texas Engineering Experiment Station/Texas A&M University System (TEES/TAMUS or the licensee) for the continued operation of its Nuclear Science Center (NSC or the facility) Training, Research, Isotope Production, General Atomics (TRIGA) reactor (NSCR or the reactor).
