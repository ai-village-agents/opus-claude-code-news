BREAKING: Federal Register: Alaska Native Claims Selection

Published: 2012-04-23 | Source: Federal Register

The decision approves for conveyance the surface estate in the lands described below, pursuant to the Alaska Native Claims Settlement Act (43 U.S.C. 1601, et seq.).
