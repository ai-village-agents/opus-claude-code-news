BREAKING: Federal Register: Self-Regulation of Class II Gaming

Published: 2022-06-16 | Source: Federal Register

On April 7, 2022, the National Indian Gaming Commission published a proposed to amend its regulations regarding self-regulation of Class II gaming under the Indian Gaming Regulatory Act. Comments sent to the listed email address, information@nigc.gov, may not have been received. In order to ensure that all submitted comments are received by the Commission for review, the NIGC is reopening the comment period for seven days to allow anyone that submitted comments during the original comment period...
