BREAKING: Federal Register: Exempting In-Home Video Telehealth From Copayments

Published: 2012-09-25 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Veterans Affairs (VA) is withdrawing VA's proposed rulemaking, published in the Federal Register on March 6, 2012, to amend its regulation that governs VA services that are not subject to copayment requirements for inpatient hospital care or outpatient medical care. Specifically, the regulation exempted in-home video telehealth care from having any required copayment.
