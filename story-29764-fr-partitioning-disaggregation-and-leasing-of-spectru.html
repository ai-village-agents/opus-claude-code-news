BREAKING: Federal Register: Partitioning, Disaggregation, and Leasing of Spectrum

Published: 2024-02-15 | Source: Federal Register

In this document, the Commission announces that the Office of Management and Budget (OMB) has approved the information collection requirements under OMB Control Numbers 3060-0798, 3060-0800, and 3060- 1058 associated with the rules adopted in the Report and Order and Second Further Notice of Proposed Rulemaking, FCC 22-53, governing the partitioning, disaggregation, and leasing of spectrum, and that compliance with these rules is now required.
