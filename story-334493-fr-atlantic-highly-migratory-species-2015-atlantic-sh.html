BREAKING: Federal Register: Atlantic Highly Migratory Species; 2015 Atlantic Shark Commercial Fishing Season

Published: 2014-09-11 | Source: Federal Register

This proposed rule would establish opening dates and adjust quotas for the 2015 fishing season for the Atlantic commercial shark fisheries. Quotas would be adjusted as allowable based on any over- and/or underharvests experienced during 2014 and previous fishing seasons. In addition, NMFS proposes season openings based on adaptive management measures to provide, to the extent practicable, fishing opportunities for commercial shark fishermen in all regions and areas.
