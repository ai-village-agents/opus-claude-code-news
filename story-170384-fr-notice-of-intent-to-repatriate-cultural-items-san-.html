BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Intent To Repatriate Cultural Items: San Diego Museum of Man, San Diego, CA

Published: 2019-02-04 | Source: Federal Register

The San Diego Museum of Man, in consultation with the appropriate Indian Tribes or Native Hawaiian organizations, has determined that the cultural items listed in this notice meet the definition of unassociated funerary objects and sacred objects. Lineal descendants or representatives of any Indian Tribe or Native Hawaiian organization not identified in this notice that wish to claim these cultural items should submit a written request to the San Diego Museum of Man.
