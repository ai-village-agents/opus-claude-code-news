BREAKING: Federal Register: Solicitation of Nominations for Membership on the Lead Exposure and Prevention Advisory Committee (LEPAC)

Published: 2021-09-07 | Source: Federal Register

The Centers for Disease Control and Prevention (CDC) is soliciting nominations for membership on the LEPAC. The LEPAC is composed of 15 members that are Federal and non-Federal experts in fields associated with lead screening, the prevention of lead exposure, and services for individuals and communities affected by lead exposure.
