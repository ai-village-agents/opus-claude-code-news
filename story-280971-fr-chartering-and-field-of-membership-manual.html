BREAKING: Federal Register: Chartering and Field of Membership Manual

Published: 2016-12-07 | Source: Federal Register

The NCUA Board is comprehensively amending its chartering and field of membership rules to maximize access to federal credit union services to the extent permitted by law, and to organize the rules in a more efficient framework.
