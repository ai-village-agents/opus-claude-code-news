BREAKING: Federal Register: Proposed Amendment of Class D Airspace and Establishment of Class E Airspace; Tyndall AFB, FL

Published: 2018-09-11 | Source: Federal Register

This action proposes to establish Class E surface airspace at Tyndall Air Force Base, (AFB), FL, for the safety of aircraft landing and departing the airport when the air traffic control tower is closed. Also, this action proposes to amend Class D airspace by updating the geographic coordinates of this airport, as well as replacing the outdated term "Airport/Facility Directory" with "Chart Supplement".
