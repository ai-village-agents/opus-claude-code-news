BREAKING: Federal Register: Ensuring Equal Treatment for Faith-Based Organizations in SBA's Loan and Disaster Assistance Programs

Published: 2021-01-19 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Small Business Administration ("SBA" or "Agency") is proposing to remove five regulatory provisions that run afoul of the Free Exercise Clause of the First Amendment. All five provisions make certain faith-based organizations ineligible to participate in certain SBA business loan and disaster assistance programs because of their religious status.
