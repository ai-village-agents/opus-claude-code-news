BREAKING: Federal Register: Video Relay Service Improvements

Published: 2024-03-21 | Source: Federal Register

In this document, the Federal Communications Commission (FCC or Commission) modifies its rules to promote improvement in the efficacy and quality of video relay service (VRS) supported by the Interstate Telecommunications Relay Services (TRS) Fund.
