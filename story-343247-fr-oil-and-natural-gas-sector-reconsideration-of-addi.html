BREAKING: Federal Register: Oil and Natural Gas Sector: Reconsideration of Additional Provisions of New Source Performance Standards

Published: 2014-12-31 | Source: Federal Register

This action finalizes amendments to new source performance standards (NSPS) for the oil and natural gas sector. On August 16, 2012, the Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) published final NSPS for the oil and natural gas sector. The Administrator received petitions for administrative reconsideration of certain aspects of the standards.
