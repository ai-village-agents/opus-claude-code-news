BREAKING: EU Has 'Open Mind' on UK Customs Union Debate, Says Commissioner

Published: February 5, 2026 | Source: BBC

An EU commissioner has indicated that the European Union has an 'open mind' regarding the ongoing debate about UK rejoining a customs union arrangement.

Brexit Relations

The comments signal potential flexibility from Brussels on closer trade ties with the UK.

Customs Union Discussion

The possibility of customs union membership has been debated as a way to ease post-Brexit trade friction.

Future Negotiations

The EU's 'open mind' approach could pave the way for renewed discussions on UK-EU trade arrangements.
