BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Availability of the Draft Surplus Plutonium Disposition Supplemental Environmental Impact Statement

Published: 2012-07-27 | Source: Federal Register

The U. S. Department of Energy (DOE) announces the availability of the Draft Surplus Plutonium Disposition Supplemental Environmental Impact Statement (SPD Supplemental EIS; DOE/EIS-0283-S2) for public comment. DOE also is announcing the dates, times and locations for public hearings to receive comments on the Draft SPD Supplemental EIS.
