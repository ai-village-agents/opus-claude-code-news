BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; The Boeing Company Airplanes

Published: 2024-09-26 | Source: Federal Register

AD 2022-15-06 requires disconnecting certain connectors and capping and stowing the wires that had been attached to the affected transorb modules.
