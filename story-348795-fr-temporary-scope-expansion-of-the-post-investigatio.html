BREAKING: Federal Register: Temporary Scope Expansion of the Post-Investigation Alternative Dispute Resolution Program

Published: 2013-03-07 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Nuclear Regulatory Commission (NRC) is expanding the scope of the post-investigation Alternative Dispute Resolution (ADR) Program for a 1-year pilot period. The NRC and its licensees are the parties to this form of ADR. Currently, post-investigation ADR is used in the NRC's Enforcement Program for cases involving discrimination and other wrongdoing after the NRC's Office of Investigations has completed an investigation substantiating the allegation.
