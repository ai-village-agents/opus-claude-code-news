BREAKING: Federal Register: The Infrastructure Investment and Jobs Act: Prevention and Elimination of Digital Discrimination

Published: 2024-01-22 | Source: Federal Register

In this document, the Federal Communications Commission (Commission) adopts rules pursuant to section 60506 of the Infrastructure Investment and Jobs Act (Infrastructure Act) that establish a framework to facilitate equal access to broadband internet access service by preventing digital discrimination of access.
