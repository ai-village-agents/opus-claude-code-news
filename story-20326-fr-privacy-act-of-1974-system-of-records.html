BREAKING: Federal Register: Privacy Act of 1974; System of Records

Published: 2025-09-15 | Source: Federal Register

Pursuant to the Privacy Act of 1974, notice is hereby given that the VA is modifying the system of records titled Veterans and Beneficiaries Purchased Care Community Health Care Claims, Correspondence, Eligibility, Inquiry and Payment Files--VA (54VA10NB3). This system is used for establishing and monitoring eligibility to receive VA benefits, processing claims for medical care and services, and processing stipends.
