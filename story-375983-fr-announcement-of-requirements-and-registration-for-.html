BREAKING: Federal Register: Announcement of Requirements and Registration for “EHR Accessibility Challenge”

Published: 2012-01-27 | Source: Federal Register

The "EHR Accessibility Challenge" challenges multidisciplinary teams to create and test a module or application that makes it easy for disabled consumers to access and interact with the health data stored in their EHRs. Accessibility and usability in health IT are high priority issues for the disability community.
