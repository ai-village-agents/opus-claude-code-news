BREAKING: Federal Register: Air Plan Approval; Kentucky; Removal of Stage II Gasoline Vapor Recovery Program

Published: 2017-07-03 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is proposing to approve changes to the Kentucky State Implementation Plan (SIP) submitted by the Commonwealth of Kentucky through its Energy and Environment Cabinet (EEC) on November 10, 2016, for the Louisville Metro Air Pollution Control District (District).
