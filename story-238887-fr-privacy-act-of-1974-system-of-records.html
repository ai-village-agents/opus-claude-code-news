BREAKING: Federal Register: Privacy Act of 1974; System of Records

Published: 2017-07-13 | Source: Federal Register

Pursuant to the provisions of the Privacy Act of 1974, the Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is giving notice that it is amending the system of record for the National Enforcement Training Institute (NETI) Online learning management system. The SORN is being amended to change the system name from NETI Online to the NETI eLearning Center and to change the system location from the Office of Criminal Enforcement to NETI in the Office of Compliance (the NETI division).
