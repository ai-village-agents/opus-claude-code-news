BREAKING: Federal Register: Transmission Planning Reliability Standards

Published: 2012-05-07 | Source: Federal Register

Under section 215 of the Federal Power Act, the Federal Energy Regulatory Commission remands proposed Transmission Planning (TPL) Reliability Standard TPL-002-0b, submitted by the North American Electric Reliability Corporation (NERC), the Commission-certified Electric Reliability Organization.
