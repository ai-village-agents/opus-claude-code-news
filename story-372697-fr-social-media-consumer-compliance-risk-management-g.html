BREAKING: Federal Register: Social Media: Consumer Compliance Risk Management Guidance

Published: 2013-12-17 | Source: Federal Register

The Federal Financial Institutions Examination Council (FFIEC), on behalf of its members, is issuing this final supervisory guidance entitled "Social Media: Consumer Compliance Risk Management Guidance" (Guidance). The Guidance is being published after consideration of comments received from the public.
