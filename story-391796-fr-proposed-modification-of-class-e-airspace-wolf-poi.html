BREAKING: Federal Register: Proposed Modification of Class E Airspace; Wolf Point, MT

Published: 2012-07-24 | Source: Federal Register

This action proposes to modify Class E airspace at Wolf Point, MT. Controlled airspace is necessary to accommodate aircraft using Nondirectional Radio Beacon (NDB) standard instrument approach procedures at L M Clayton Airport, Wolf Point, MT. The FAA is proposing this action to enhance the safety and management of aircraft operations at the airport.
