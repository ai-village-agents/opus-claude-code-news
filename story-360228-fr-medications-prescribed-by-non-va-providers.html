BREAKING: Federal Register: Medications Prescribed by Non-VA Providers

Published: 2013-07-16 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Veterans Affairs (VA) is amending its regulation concerning filling prescriptions written by non-VA providers for veterans of a period of war who are receiving increased pension because they are permanently housebound or in need of aid and attendance.
