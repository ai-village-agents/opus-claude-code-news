BREAKING: Federal Register: Proposed Information Collection Activity; Annual Report on Households Assisted by the Low Income Home Energy Assistance Program (LIHEAP) (Office of Management and Budget (OMB) #0970-0060)

Published: 2022-07-01 | Source: Federal Register

OCS, Division of Energy Assistance, is requesting a substantial change of the Household Report (OMB #0970-0060, expiration May 31, 2025). Grant recipients complete the Household Report on an annual basis, completing either the Long Form or the Short Form version of the report. Submission of the completed report is one requirement for Low Income Home Energy Assistance Program (LIHEAP) grant recipients applying for federal LIHEAP block grant funds.
