BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; The Boeing Company Airplanes

Published: 2020-12-21 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA is superseding Airworthiness Directive (AD) 2013-18- 08, which applied to certain The Boeing Company Model 737-200, -200C, - 300, -400, and -500 series airplanes. AD 2013-18-08 required repetitive inspections for cracking of certain skin panels of the fuselage, and of the fuselage skin along certain chem-milled lines, and corrective actions if necessary.
