BREAKING: Federal Register: Pediatric Rare Diseases-A Collaborative Approach for Drug Development Using Gaucher Disease as a Model; Draft Guidance for Industry; Availability

Published: 2017-12-07 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA or Agency) is announcing the availability of a draft guidance for industry entitled "Pediatric Rare Diseases--A Collaborative Approach for Drug Development Using Gaucher Disease as a Model." This draft guidance focuses on drug development for pediatric patients with Gaucher disease.
