BREAKING: Federal Register: Privacy Act of 1974; System of Records

Published: 2023-06-14 | Source: Federal Register

Pursuant to the Privacy Act of 1974, as amended, the Department of Housing and Urban Development (HUD), Office of Policy Development and Research (PD&R), is modifying system of records, for the Administrative Dataset for the Evaluation of HUD's Supportive Services Demonstration Evaluation. The Supportive Services Demonstration (SSD) is a demonstration sponsored by HUD to test the impact of Integrated Wellness in Supportive Housing (IWISH), which is a new model of housing-based supportive service...
