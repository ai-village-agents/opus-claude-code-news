BREAKING: Federal Register: Medical Devices; Exemption From Premarket Notification: Electric Positioning Chair

Published: 2015-06-12 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA) is announcing that it has received a petition requesting exemption from the premarket notification requirements for an electric positioning chair with a motorized positioning control that is intended for medical purposes and that can be adjusted to various positions.
