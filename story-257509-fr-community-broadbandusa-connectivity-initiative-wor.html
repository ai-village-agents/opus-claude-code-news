BREAKING: Federal Register: Community BroadbandUSA Connectivity Initiative Workshop

Published: 2016-03-09 | Source: Federal Register

The National Telecommunications and Information Administration (NTIA), through the BroadbandUSA program, will hold a half-day workshop on March 22, 2016, to engage stakeholders in developing meaningful measures for community broadband access, adoption, policy and use as part of its efforts to develop the Community Connectivity Initiative.
