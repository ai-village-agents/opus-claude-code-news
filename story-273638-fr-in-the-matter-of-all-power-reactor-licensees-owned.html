BREAKING: Federal Register: In the Matter of All Power Reactor Licensees Owned and Operated by First Energy Nuclear Operating Company; and First Energy Corp

Published: 2016-09-12 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Nuclear Regulatory Commission (NRC) issued a confirmatory order (Order) to First Energy Nuclear Operating Company (the licensee), confirming the agreement reached in an Alternative Dispute Resolution mediation session held on July 21, 2016. This Order will ensure the licensee restores compliance with NRC regulations.
