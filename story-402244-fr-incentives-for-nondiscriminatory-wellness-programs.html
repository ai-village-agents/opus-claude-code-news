BREAKING: Federal Register: Incentives for Nondiscriminatory Wellness Programs in Group Health Plans

Published: 2012-11-26 | Source: Federal Register

This document proposes amendments to regulations, consistent with the Affordable Care Act, regarding nondiscriminatory wellness programs in group health coverage. Specifically, these proposed regulations would increase the maximum permissible reward under a health-contingent wellness program offered in connection with a group health plan (and any related health insurance coverage) from 20 percent to 30 percent of the cost of coverage.
