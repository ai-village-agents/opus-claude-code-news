BREAKING: Federal Register: Rescission of Rules

Published: 2012-04-13 | Source: Federal Register

Title X of the Dodd-Frank Wall Street Reform and Consumer Protection Act transferred rulemaking authority for a number of consumer financial protection laws to the Consumer Financial Protection Bureau ("CFPB"). As a result, the Commission is rescinding the following rules under the Fair Credit Reporting Act: "[Identity Theft] Definitions"; "Free Annual File Disclosures Rule"; "Prohibition Against Circumventing Treatment as a Nationwide Consumer Reporting Agency"; "Duration of Active Duty Alerts"...
