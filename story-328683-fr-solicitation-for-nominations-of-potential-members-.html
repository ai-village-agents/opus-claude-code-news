BREAKING: Federal Register: Solicitation for Nominations of Potential Members of the Committee of Scientific Advisors on Marine Mammals

Published: 2014-07-03 | Source: Federal Register

The Marine Mammal Commission was created under Title II of the Marine Mammal Protection Act of 1972, as amended. The Commission is assisted in its duties by the Committee of Scientific Advisors on Marine Mammals. The Committee consists of nine members, appointed by the Chairman of the Commission. As a general rule, Committee Members are appointed for three-year terms, which may be extended as necessary, but vacancies do not occur on a regular basis.
