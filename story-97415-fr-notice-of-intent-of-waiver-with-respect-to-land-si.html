BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Intent of Waiver With Respect to Land; Sioux Falls Regional Airport, Sioux Falls, South Dakota

Published: 2022-06-03 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA is considering a proposal to change approximately 64.93 acres of airport land from aeronautical use to non-aeronautical use and to authorize the sale of airport property located at the Sioux Falls Regional Airport, Sioux Falls, South Dakota. The property is made up of three parcels. Parcel 1 is approximately 16.40 acres, located on the southwest side of the airport and is currently used as a City storage lot.
