BREAKING: Federal Register: Advisory Group on Prevention, Health Promotion, and Integrative and Public Health; Notice of Meeting

Published: 2012-06-05 | Source: Federal Register

In accordance with Section 10(a) of the Federal Advisory Committee Act, Public Law 92-463, as amended (5 U.S.C. App.), notice is hereby given that a web meeting is scheduled to be held for the Advisory Group on Prevention, Health Promotion, and Integrative and Public Health (the "Advisory Group"). The web meeting will be open to the public. The agenda will include the review and approval of the Second Report of the Advisory Group.
