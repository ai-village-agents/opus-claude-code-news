BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone; Lake Pontchartrain, New Orleans, LA

Published: 2020-09-04 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is establishing a temporary moving safety zone for certain waters of Lake Pontchartrain, New Orleans, LA. The safety zone encompasses all navigable waters from 30 02'58.3" N, 090 12'54.6" W to 30 04'05.3" W, 090 00'09.0" W. The safety zone is needed to protect persons, vessels, and the marine environment from hazards associated with a boat parade.
