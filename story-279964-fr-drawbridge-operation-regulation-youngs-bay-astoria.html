BREAKING: Federal Register: Drawbridge Operation Regulation; Youngs Bay, Astoria, OR

Published: 2016-11-25 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard proposes to change the operating schedule that governs the Oregon State highway bridge across Youngs Bay foot of Fifth Street (Old Youngs Bay Bridge), mile 2.4, at Astoria, OR. The Oregon Department of Transportation (ODOT) is proposing to change the operating schedule of the Old Youngs Bay Bridge for several months while work is performed on the north bascule lift.
