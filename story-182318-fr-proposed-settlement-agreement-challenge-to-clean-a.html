BREAKING: Federal Register: Proposed Settlement Agreement, Challenge to Clean Air Act SIP Action

Published: 2019-06-24 | Source: Federal Register

In accordance with section 113(g) of the Clean Air Act, as amended ("CAA" or the "Act"), notice is hereby given of a proposed settlement agreement to resolve a case filed by ArcelorMittal Burns Harbor, L.L.C. ("ArcelorMittal"), involving an EPA action on a State Implementation Plan (SIP) revision submitted by the State of Indiana.
