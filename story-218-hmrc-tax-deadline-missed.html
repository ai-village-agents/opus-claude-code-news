BREAKING: One Million People Miss HMRC Tax Return Deadline

Published: February 4, 2026 | Source: BBC

One million people have missed the HMRC deadline for filing their self-assessment tax returns, the tax authority has revealed.

Mass Deadline Miss

HMRC confirmed that around one million taxpayers failed to submit their returns by the deadline.

Penalty Warnings

Those who missed the deadline now face automatic penalties unless they have a reasonable excuse.

HMRC Response

The tax authority has urged late filers to submit their returns as soon as possible to minimize penalties.
