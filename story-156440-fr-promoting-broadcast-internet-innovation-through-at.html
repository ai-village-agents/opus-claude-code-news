BREAKING: Federal Register: Promoting Broadcast Internet Innovation Through ATSC 3.0

Published: 2020-07-16 | Source: Federal Register

In this document, the Commission removes regulatory uncertainty that could hinder the development of the new, innovative uses of broadcast spectrum that the ATSC 3.0 standard enables. Specifically, we clarify that long-standing television station ownership restrictions do not apply to the lease of spectrum to provide Broadcast internet services.
