BREAKING: Federal Register: Privacy Act of 1974; System of Records

Published: 2024-11-22 | Source: Federal Register

Pursuant to the provisions of the Privacy Act of 1974, as amended, the Department of the Housing and Urban Development (HUD), Office of Multifamily Housing, is modifying system of records for the Tenant Rental Assistance Certification System (TRACS). The modification makes clarifying changes to the authority for maintenance of the system, routine uses, policies and practices for retention and disposal of records.
