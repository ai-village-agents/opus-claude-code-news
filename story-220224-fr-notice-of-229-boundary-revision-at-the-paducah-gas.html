BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of 229 Boundary Revision at the Paducah Gaseous Diffusion Plant

Published: 2018-11-02 | Source: Federal Register

Notice is hereby given that the U.S. Department of Energy, pursuant to the Atomic Energy Act of 1954, as amended, prohibits the unauthorized entry and the unauthorized introduction of weapons or dangerous materials into or upon the facilities of the Paducah Gaseous Diffusion Plant, located in McCracken County, Kentucky, of the United States Department of Energy. The facilities are described in this notice.
