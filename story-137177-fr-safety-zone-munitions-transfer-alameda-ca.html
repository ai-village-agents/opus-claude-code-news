BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone; Munitions Transfer; Alameda, CA

Published: 2021-11-04 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is establishing a temporary safety zone in the navigable waters of the Oakland Estuary in the Brooklyn Basin South Channel near Coast Guard Island in Alameda, CA in support of a munitions transfer on November 14, 2021, and November 21, 2021. This safety zone is necessary to protect personnel, vessels, and the marine environment from the dangers associated with live munitions.
