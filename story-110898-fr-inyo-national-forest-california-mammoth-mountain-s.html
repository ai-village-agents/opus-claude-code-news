BREAKING: Federal Register: Inyo National Forest; California; Mammoth Mountain Ski Area Main Lodge Redevelopment

Published: 2022-11-25 | Source: Federal Register

Mammoth Mountain Ski Area (MMSA) has submitted a proposal to the Inyo National Forest (the Forest) to pursue approval of select projects from its 2022 Master Development Plan (MDP) on National Forest System (NFS) lands, in accordance with its existing Special Use Permit (SUP).
