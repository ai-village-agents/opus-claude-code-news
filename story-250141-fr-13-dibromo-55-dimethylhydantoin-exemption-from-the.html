BREAKING: Federal Register: 1,3-dibromo-5,5-dimethylhydantoin; Exemption From the Requirement of a Tolerance

Published: 2017-12-05 | Source: Federal Register

This regulation establishes an exemption from the requirement of a tolerance for residues of 1,3-dibromo-5,5-dimethylhydantoin in or on food when used in antimicrobial pesticide formulations applied to food contact surfaces in public eating places, dairy processing equipment, and/or food processing equipment and utensils.
