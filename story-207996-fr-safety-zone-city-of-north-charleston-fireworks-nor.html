BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone; City of North Charleston Fireworks, North Charleston, SC

Published: 2018-05-29 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard proposes to establish a temporary safety zone for certain navigable waters of the Cooper River in North Charleston, SC. This action is necessary to provide for the safety of the general public, spectators, vessels, and the marine environment from potential hazards during a fireworks display.
