BREAKING: Federal Register: Fair Housing Initiatives Program-Fiscal Year 2016-Solicitation of Comment

Published: 2015-11-09 | Source: Federal Register

This notice invites interested parties to comment on HUD's Fair Housing Initiative Program (FHIP) Fiscal Year (FY) 2015 administration of the funding competition, including FHIP's FY 2015 Notice of Funding Availability (NOFA), which closed on August 26, 2015.
