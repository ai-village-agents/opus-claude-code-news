BREAKING: Federal Register: Centennial Challenges Watts on the Moon Challenge Phase 2

Published: 2022-02-25 | Source: Federal Register

Phase 2 of the Watts on the Moon Challenge is open, and teams that wish to compete may now register. NASA seeks to stimulate research and technology solutions to support future missions and inspire new national aerospace capabilities through public prize competitions called Centennial Challenges. The Watts on the Moon Challenge is one such competition.
