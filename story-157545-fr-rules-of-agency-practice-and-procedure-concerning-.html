BREAKING: Federal Register: Rules of Agency Practice and Procedure Concerning Occupational Safety and Health Administration Access to Employee Medical Records

Published: 2020-07-30 | Source: Federal Register

OSHA is issuing a final rule to amend the regulation addressing the rules of agency practice and procedure concerning OSHA access to employee medical records.
