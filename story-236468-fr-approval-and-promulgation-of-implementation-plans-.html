BREAKING: Federal Register: Approval and Promulgation of Implementation Plans; Alaska: Adoption Updates and Rule Revisions

Published: 2017-06-13 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) proposes to approve state implementation plan (SIP) revisions submitted by the State of Alaska Department of Environmental Conservation (ADEC) on September 15, 2016. These revisions primarily update adoptions of Federal regulations in the Alaska SIP. The revisions also strengthen the State of Alaska's (Alaska) minor source permitting requirements and remove obsolete source-specific regulations.
