BREAKING: Federal Register: Historical Research in the Files of the Office of the Secretary of Defense (OSD)

Published: 2014-05-08 | Source: Federal Register

This proposed rule updates and clarifies procedures regarding the review and accessibility to records and information in the custody of the Secretary of Defense and the OSD Components. The purpose of this rule is to provide such guidance to former Cabinet level officials and former Presidential appointees (FPAs), including their personnel, aides, and official researchers.
