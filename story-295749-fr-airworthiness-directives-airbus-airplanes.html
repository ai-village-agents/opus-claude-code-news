BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Airbus Airplanes

Published: 2015-06-05 | Source: Federal Register

We propose to supersede Airworthiness Directive (AD) 96-18-06 for certain Airbus Model A320-211 and -231 airplanes. AD 96-18-06 currently requires visual inspections to detect cracks of the pressurized floor fittings at frame (FR) 36, and renewal of the zone protective finish or replacement of fittings with new fittings if necessary.
