BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone; Inbound Transit of M/V TEAL, Savannah River; Savannah, GA

Published: 2013-06-18 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is establishing a temporary moving safety zone around the M/V TEAL during its inbound transit on the Savannah River to the Georgia Ports Authority, Garden City Terminal Container Berth 8 (CB8). This safety zone facilitates the safe transit and offload of four oversized ship to shore (STS) cranes.
