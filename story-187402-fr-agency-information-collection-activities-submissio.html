BREAKING: Federal Register: Agency Information Collection Activities: Submission for OMB Review; Comment Request; Community Disaster Loan (CDL) Program

Published: 2019-08-28 | Source: Federal Register

The Federal Emergency Management Agency, as part of its continuing effort to reduce paperwork and respondent burden, invites the general public and other Federal agencies to take this opportunity to comment on a revision, of a currently approved information collection. In accordance with the Paperwork Reduction Act of 1995, this notice seeks comments concerning the Community Disaster Loan (CDL) Program.
