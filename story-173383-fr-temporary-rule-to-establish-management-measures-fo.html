BREAKING: Federal Register: Temporary Rule To Establish Management Measures for Red Grouper in the Gulf of Mexico

Published: 2019-03-05 | Source: Federal Register

NMFS proposes to issue an emergency rule as requested by the Gulf of Mexico Fishery Management Council (Council) to address concerns regarding the Gulf of Mexico (Gulf) red grouper stock. The Council made this request after receiving new information that indicates the stock may be in decline. This proposed emergency rule would reduce the commercial and recreational annual catch limits (ACLs) and annual catch targets (ACTs).
