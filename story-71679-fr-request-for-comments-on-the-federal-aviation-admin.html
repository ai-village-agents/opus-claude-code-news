BREAKING: Federal Register: Request for Comments on the Federal Aviation Administration's Review the Civil Aviation Noise Policy; Extension of Comment Period

Published: 2023-07-11 | Source: Federal Register

On May 1, 2023, the Federal Aviation Administration (FAA) published a Request for comments seeking input on its review of four key considerations of its civil aviation noise policy, in the context of noise metrics and noise thresholds. The civil aviation noise policy sets forth how the FAA analyzes, explains, and publicly presents changes in noise exposure from aviation activity.
