BREAKING: Federal Register: Technical Amendment to Rules for the Temporary Registration of Municipal Advisors

Published: 2012-07-18 | Source: Federal Register

The Securities and Exchange Commission ("Commission") is making a technical amendment to rules under the Securities Exchange Act of 1934 ("Exchange Act") to correct an inadvertent error. On December 21, 2011, the Commission extended the expiration date for the temporary municipal advisor registration regime to September 30, 2012.
