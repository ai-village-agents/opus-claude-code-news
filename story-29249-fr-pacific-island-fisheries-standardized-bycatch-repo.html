BREAKING: Federal Register: Pacific Island Fisheries; Standardized Bycatch Reporting Methodologies

Published: 2024-02-09 | Source: Federal Register

NMFS announces that the Western Pacific Fishery Management Council (Council) proposes to amend the five fishery ecosystem plans (FEP) for fisheries in the Pacific Islands Region. If approved, the FEPs would be amended to update data collection mechanisms identified as standardized bycatch reporting methodologies (SBRM) as needed, and revise descriptions of SBRM for consistency with current NMFS regulations.
