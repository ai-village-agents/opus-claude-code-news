BREAKING: Federal Register: Request for Input for Revisions to the “Know Your Rights” Pamphlet, Also Known as the Wilberforce Pamphlet

Published: 2015-06-02 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of State ("the Department") is planning to update the "Know Your Rights" pamphlet created by the U.S. government in 2009 pursuant to the William Wilberforce Trafficking Victims Protection Reauthorization Act (Pub. L. 110-457) and requests written recommendations on how the pamphlet could be improved.
