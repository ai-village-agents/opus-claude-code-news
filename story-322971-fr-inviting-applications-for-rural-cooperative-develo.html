BREAKING: Federal Register: Inviting Applications for Rural Cooperative Development Grants

Published: 2014-04-30 | Source: Federal Register

USDA announces the availability of grants through the Rural Cooperative Development Grant (RCDG) Program for Fiscal Year 2014. Pursuant to The Consolidated Appropriations Act of 2014 (H.R. 3547) approximately $5.8 million is available. We are requesting proposals from applicants interested in improving the economic condition of rural areas through cooperative development.
