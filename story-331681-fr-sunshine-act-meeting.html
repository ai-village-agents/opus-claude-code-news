BREAKING: Federal Register: Sunshine Act Meeting

Published: 2014-08-08 | Source: Federal Register

The members of the Broadcasting Board of Governors (Board) will meet in a closed session to consider the appointment of the Director of Global Strategy for the Agency. This meeting will be closed to public observation pursuant to 5 U.S.C. 552b(c)(6) in order to protect the privacy interests of candidates considered but not selected for the position.
