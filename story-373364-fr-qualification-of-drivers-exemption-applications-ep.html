BREAKING: Federal Register: Qualification of Drivers; Exemption Applications; Epilepsy and Seizure Disorders

Published: 2013-12-24 | Source: Federal Register

FMCSA announces its decision to grant requests from seven individuals for exemptions from the regulatory requirement that interstate commercial motor vehicle (CMV) drivers have "no established medical history or clinical diagnosis of epilepsy or any other condition which is likely to cause loss of consciousness or any loss of ability to control a CMV."
