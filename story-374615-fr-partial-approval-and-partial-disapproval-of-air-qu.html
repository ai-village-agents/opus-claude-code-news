BREAKING: Federal Register: Partial Approval and Partial Disapproval of Air Quality Implementation Plans; California; San Joaquin Valley; Reasonably Available Control Technology for Ozone

Published: 2012-01-10 | Source: Federal Register

EPA is approving in part and disapproving in part a revision to the San Joaquin Valley Unified Air Pollution Control District (SJVUAPCD or SJV) portion of the California State Implementation Plan (SIP). This action was proposed in the Federal Register on September 9, 2011 and concerns SJVUAPCD's "Reasonably Available Control Technology (RACT) Demonstration for Ozone SIP" (RACT SIP) for the 8-hour ozone National Ambient Air Quality Standard.
