BREAKING: Federal Register: Implantation or Injectable Dosage Form New Animal Drugs; Change of Sponsor

Published: 2014-03-25 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA) is amending the animal drug regulations to reflect a change of sponsor for 104 approved new animal drug applications (NADAs) and 5 approved abbreviated new animal drug applications (ANADAs) for implantation or injectable dosage form new animal drug products from Pfizer, Inc., including its several subsidiaries and divisions, to Zoetis, Inc.
