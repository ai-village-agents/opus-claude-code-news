BREAKING: Federal Register: Implementation of Additional Controls on Pakistan

Published: 2024-11-26 | Source: Federal Register

The Bureau of Industry and Security (BIS) is amending the Export Administration Regulations (EAR) by imposing new licensing requirements on exports, reexports, and transfers (in-country) to and within Pakistan of certain items identified on the Commerce Control List (CCL) that are not currently subject to a license requirement when destined for Pakistan.
