BREAKING: Federal Register: N,N-Dimethylnonanamide; Tolerance Exemption

Published: 2022-11-30 | Source: Federal Register

This regulation establishes an exemption from the requirement of a tolerance for residues of N,N-dimethylnonanamide (CAS Reg. No. 6225-08-7) when used as an inert ingredient (solvent, co-solvent, and adjuvant) not to exceed 20% by weight in pesticide formulations applied to growing crops and raw agricultural commodities pre- and post- harvest, and applied to animals.
