BREAKING: Federal Register: Voluntary Quality Management Maturity Prototype Assessment Protocol Evaluation Program

Published: 2024-01-25 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA or Agency) is announcing an opportunity for a limited number of establishments to participate in a voluntary Quality Management Maturity Prototype Assessment Protocol Evaluation Program involving the use of a prototype assessment protocol to evaluate quality management maturity (QMM).
