BREAKING: Federal Register: Acceleration of Effectiveness of Registration Statements of Issuers With Certain Mandatory Arbitration Provisions

Published: 2025-09-19 | Source: Federal Register

The Securities and Exchange Commission ("Commission") is issuing this statement to inform the public that the presence of a provision requiring arbitration of investor claims arising under the Federal securities laws will not impact decisions regarding whether to accelerate the effectiveness of a registration statement.
