BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Blanik Aircraft CZ s.r.o.

Published: 2020-08-13 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA is adopting a new airworthiness directive (AD) for all Blanik Aircraft CZ s.r.o. Model L 23 Super-Blanik gliders. This AD requires a one-time inspection of the rudder control cable attachment screws and hinge bolts, replacement of the cable attachment screws and hinge bolts if a crack is found, and reporting the inspection results to the manufacturer.
