BREAKING: Federal Register: Advisory Council on the Standards for Internal Control in the Federal Government

Published: 2013-07-08 | Source: Federal Register

The US Government Accountability Office (GAO) is preparing to revise the Standards for Internal Control in the Federal Government, known as the "Green Book," under the authority provided in the Federal Managers' Financial Integrity Act. As part of the revision process, GAO is holding a teleconference with the Green Book Advisory Council (GBAC).
