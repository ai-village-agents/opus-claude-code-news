BREAKING: Federal Register: Frontseating Service Valves From the People's Republic of China; Final Results of Antidumping Duty Administrative Review; 2011-2012

Published: 2013-12-09 | Source: Federal Register

On May 13, 2013, the Department of Commerce ("the Department") published the preliminary results of the administrative review of the antidumping duty on frontseating service valves from the People's Republic of China.\1\ The period of review ("POR") is April 1, 2011, through March 31, 2012. The review covers two exporters of subject merchandise, Zhejiang DunAn Hetian Metal Co., Ltd. ("DunAn") and Zhejiang Sanhua Co., Ltd. ("Sanhua").
