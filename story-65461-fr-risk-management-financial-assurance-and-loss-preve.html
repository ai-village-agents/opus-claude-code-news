BREAKING: Federal Register: Risk Management, Financial Assurance, and Loss Prevention-Decommissioning Activities and Obligations

The Bureau of Safety and Environmental Enforcement (BSEE) issues this final rule to clarify and formalize its regulations related to decommissioning responsibilities of Outer Continental Shelf (OCS) oil, gas, and sulfur lessees and grant holders to ensure compliance with lease, grant, and regulatory obligations.
