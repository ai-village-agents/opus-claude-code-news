BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Airbus SAS Airplanes

Published: 2019-11-15 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA proposes to adopt a new airworthiness directive (AD) for all Airbus SAS Model A350-941 and -1041 airplanes. This proposed AD was prompted by reports of passenger door girt bar fitting assembly safety hooks being stuck in the upward position.
