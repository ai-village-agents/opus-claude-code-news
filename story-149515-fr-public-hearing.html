BREAKING: Federal Register: Public Hearing

Published: 2020-04-20 | Source: Federal Register

The Susquehanna River Basin Commission will hold a public hearing on May 14, 2020. Due to the COVID-19 crises and the relevant orders in place in the Commission's member jurisdictions, the Commission will hold this meeting telephonically. At this public hearing, the Commission will hear testimony on the projects listed in the Supplementary Information section of this notice.
