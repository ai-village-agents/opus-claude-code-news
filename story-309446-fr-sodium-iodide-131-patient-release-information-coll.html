BREAKING: Federal Register: Sodium Iodide-131 Patient Release Information Collection

Published: 2015-11-16 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Nuclear Regulatory Commission (NRC) is requesting information from the general public on a number of issues associated with medical treatment of patients with sodium iodide I-131 (hereafter referred to as I-131).
