BREAKING: Federal Register: 2017 Census of Governments

Published: 2016-04-05 | Source: Federal Register

The Bureau of the Census (U.S. Census Bureau) publishes this notice to request public comment on the content of the 2017 Census of Governments. The Census of Governments is conducted at 5-year intervals (years ending in 2 and 7) and is the most comprehensive compilation of statistics about state and local governments available.
