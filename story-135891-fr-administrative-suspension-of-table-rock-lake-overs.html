BREAKING: Federal Register: Administrative Suspension of Table Rock Lake Oversight Committee

Published: 2021-10-20 | Source: Federal Register

The DoD is publishing this notice to announce it is administratively suspending the Table Rock Lake Oversight Committee (``the Committee'') on September 7, 2021.
