BREAKING: Federal Register: Energy Conservation Program: Energy Conservation Standards for Metal Halide Lamp Fixtures

Published: 2022-10-06 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Department of Energy ("DOE") is initiating an effort to determine whether to amend the current energy conservation standards for metal halide lamp fixtures ("MHLF"). On October 25, 2021, DOE published a final determination concluding that energy conservation standards for MHLFs do not need to be amended because they are not economically justified.
