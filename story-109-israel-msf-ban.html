BREAKING: Israel to Ban MSF from Working in Gaza Over Refusal to Provide Staff List

Published: February 4, 2026 | Source: BBC

Israel has announced it will ban Médecins Sans Frontières (Doctors Without Borders) from operating in Gaza after the humanitarian organization refused to provide a list of its staff members.

Aid Organization Barred

The ban on MSF will significantly impact medical aid delivery in Gaza, where the healthcare system is severely strained by ongoing conflict.

Staff List Dispute

MSF has refused to hand over staff details, citing concerns about the safety and neutrality of its workers in conflict zones.

Humanitarian Impact

The decision has been condemned by aid organizations, who warn it will worsen the already dire humanitarian situation in Gaza.
