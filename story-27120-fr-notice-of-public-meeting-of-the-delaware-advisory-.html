BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Public Meeting of the Delaware Advisory Committee to the U.S. Commission on Civil Rights

Published: 2024-01-12 | Source: Federal Register

Notice is hereby given, pursuant to the provisions of the rules and regulations of the U.S. Commission on Civil Rights (Commission) and the Federal Advisory Committee Act that the Delaware Advisory Committee (Committee) to the U.S. Commission on Civil Rights will convene a business meeting on Wednesday, January 24, 2024, at 1:00 p.m. Eastern Time. The purpose of the meeting is for the committee to discuss the progress of their report on the COVID-19 impact on people of color in Delaware.
