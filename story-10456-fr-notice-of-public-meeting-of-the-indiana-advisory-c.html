BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Public Meeting of the Indiana Advisory Committee to the U.S. Commission on Civil Rights

Published: 2025-05-02 | Source: Federal Register

Notice is hereby given, pursuant to the provisions of the rules and regulations of the U.S. Commission on Civil Rights (Commission) and the Federal Advisory Committee Act, that the Indiana Advisory Committee (Committee) to the U.S. Commission on Civil Rights will hold a public business meeting via Zoom at 12:00 p.m. Eastern Time on Tuesday, June 17, 2025. The purpose of the meeting is to review sections of the Committee's report on hate crimes in Indiana.
