BREAKING: Federal Register: Oklahoma: Approval of Coal Combustion Residuals State Permit Program; Extension of Comment Period

Published: 2018-02-20 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA or the Agency) is extending the comment period on EPA's proposal to approve Oklahoma's Coal Combustion Residuals (CCR) State Permit Program. The notice announcing this proposed approval was published on January 16, 2018, and the public comment period was scheduled to end on March 2, 2018.
