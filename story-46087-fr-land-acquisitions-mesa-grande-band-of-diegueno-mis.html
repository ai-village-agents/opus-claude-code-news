BREAKING: Federal Register: Land Acquisitions; Mesa Grande Band of Diegueno Mission Indians of the Mesa Grande Reservation, California

Published: 2024-09-05 | Source: Federal Register

The Assistant Secretary--Indian Affairs made a final agency determination to acquire 480.46 acres, more or less, of land in trust for the Mesa Grande Band of Diegueno Mission Indians of the Mesa Grande Reservation, California.
