BREAKING: Federal Register: Codex Alimentarius Commission: Meeting of the Codex Committee on Food Hygiene

Published: 2023-12-27 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Codex Office is sponsoring a public meeting on February 5, 2024. The objective of the public meeting is to provide information and receive public comments on agenda items and draft U.S. positions to be discussed at the 54th Session of the Codex Committee on Food Hygiene (CCFH) of the Codex Alimentarius Commission (CAC).
