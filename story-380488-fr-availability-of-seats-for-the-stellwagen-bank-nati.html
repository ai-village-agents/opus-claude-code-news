BREAKING: Federal Register: Availability of Seats for the Stellwagen Bank National Marine Sanctuary Advisory Council

Published: 2012-03-15 | Source: Federal Register

The ONMS is seeking applications for the following vacant seats on the Stellwagen Bank National Marine Sanctuary Advisory Council: (1) At-Large Member; (2) Research Alternate; (1) Youth Member; and (1) Youth Alternate.
