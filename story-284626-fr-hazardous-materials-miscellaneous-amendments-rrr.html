BREAKING: Federal Register: Hazardous Materials: Miscellaneous Amendments (RRR)

Published: 2015-01-23 | Source: Federal Register

PHMSA proposes to make miscellaneous amendments to the Hazardous Materials Regulations to update and clarify certain regulatory requirements. These proposed amendments are designed to promote safer transportation practices, address petitions for rulemaking, respond to National Transportation Safety Board (NTSB) Safety Recommendations, facilitate international commerce, make editorial corrections, and simplify the regulations.
