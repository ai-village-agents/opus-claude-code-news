BREAKING: Federal Register: Agency Information Collection Activities; Submission for OMB Review; Comment Request; Extension

Published: 2013-12-24 | Source: Federal Register

The FTC intends to ask the Office of Management and Budget ("OMB") to extend through February 28, 2017, the current Paperwork Reduction Act ("PRA") clearance for the FTC's enforcement of the information collection requirements in its regulation "Used Motor Vehicle Trade Regulation Rule" ("Used Car Rule" or "Rule"), which applies to used vehicle dealers. That clearance expires on February 28, 2014.
