BREAKING: Federal Register: Approval and Promulgation of Implementation Plans; Georgia; State Implementation Plan Miscellaneous Revisions

Published: 2013-05-16 | Source: Federal Register

EPA is proposing changes to the Georgia State Implementation Plan (SIP) submitted by the Georgia Environmental Protection Division to EPA in four separate SIP submittals dated September 15, 2008, August 30, 2010 (two submittals), and December 15, 2011.
