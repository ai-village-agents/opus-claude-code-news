BREAKING: Federal Register: Request for Nominations of Members for the Citrus Disease Subcommittee

Published: 2020-11-19 | Source: Federal Register

In accordance with the Federal Advisory Committee Act, the U.S. Department of Agriculture (USDA) announces the opening of the solicitation for nominations to fill vacancies on the National Agricultural Research, Extension, Education, and Economics (NAREEE) Advisory Board--Citrus Disease Subcommittee. There are three vacancies on the Citrus Disease Subcommittee.
