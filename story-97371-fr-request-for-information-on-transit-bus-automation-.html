BREAKING: Federal Register: Request for Information on Transit Bus Automation Research and Demonstrations

Published: 2022-06-02 | Source: Federal Register

The Federal Transit Administration (FTA) continues to research Advanced Driver Assistance Systems (ADAS) and Automated Driving Systems (ADS) in public transportation use cases. In 2018, FTA completed its five-year Strategic Transit Automation Research Plan (STAR Plan). In preparation for the next five-year plan, FTA is issuing this request for information (RFI).
