BREAKING: Federal Register: Security Zone: Escorted Vessels, Los Angeles-Long Beach, CA, Captain of the Port Zone

Published: 2015-10-08 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is establishing a security zone around any vessel escorted by one or more Coast Guard, State, or local law enforcement assets on the navigable waters of the Captain of the Port (COTP) Zone, Los Angeles-Long Beach, California. This action is necessary to protect personnel, vessels, and facilities from sabotage or other subversive acts, accidents, or other events of a similar nature.
