BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice on Outer Continental Shelf Oil and Gas Lease Sales; MMAA104000

Published: 2020-04-17 | Source: Federal Register

Pursuant to the Bureau of Ocean Energy Management (BOEM) regulatory restrictions on joint bidding, the Director of BOEM is publishing a List of Restricted Joint Bidders. Each entity within one of the following groups is restricted from bidding with any entity in any of the other following groups at Outer Continental Shelf oil and gas lease sales to be held during the bidding period May 1, 2020, through October 31, 2020.
