BREAKING: Federal Register: Release of Airport Property: Page Field, Fort Myers, FL

Published: 2012-01-23 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA hereby provides notice of intent to release certain airport properties 1.52 acres at Page Field, Fort Myers, FL from the conditions, release certain properties from all terms, conditions, reservations and restrictions of a Quitclaim Deed agreement, dated June 4, 1947, between the subject airport and the Federal Aviation Administration.
