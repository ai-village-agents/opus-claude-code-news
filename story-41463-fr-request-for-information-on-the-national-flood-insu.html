BREAKING: Federal Register: Request for Information on the National Flood Insurance Program's Community Rating System Redesign Effort

Published: 2024-07-11 | Source: Federal Register

The Federal Emergency Management Agency (FEMA) is issuing this Request for Information (RFI) to receive the public's input on potential future changes to the Community Rating System (CRS) under the National Flood Insurance Program (NFIP).
