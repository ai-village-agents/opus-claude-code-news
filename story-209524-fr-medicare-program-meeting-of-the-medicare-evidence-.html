BREAKING: Federal Register: Medicare Program; Meeting of the Medicare Evidence Development and Coverage Advisory Committee-August 22, 2018

Published: 2018-06-15 | Source: Federal Register

This notice announces that a public meeting of the Medicare Evidence Development & Coverage Advisory Committee (MEDCAC) ("Committee") will be held on Wednesday, August 22, 2018. This meeting will focus on the state of evidence on Chimeric Antigen Receptor (CAR) T-cell therapies that are approved by the Food and Drug Administration (FDA).
