BREAKING: Federal Register: Medical Devices; Ophthalmic Devices; Classification of the Electromechanical Tear Stimulator

Published: 2022-02-18 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA or we) is classifying the electromechanical tear stimulator into class II (special controls). The special controls that apply to the device type are identified in this order and will be part of the codified language for the electromechanical tear stimulator's classification.
