BREAKING: Federal Register: Tennessee Valley Authority; Browns Ferry Nuclear Plant, Units 1, 2, and 3

Published: 2017-05-31 | Source: Federal Register

DPR- 33, DPR-52, and DPR-68 issued to Tennessee Valley Authority (TVA, the licensee) for operation of Browns Ferry Nuclear Plant, Units 1, 2, and 3 (BFN) located in Limestone County, Alabama.
