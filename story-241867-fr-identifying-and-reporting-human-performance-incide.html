BREAKING: Federal Register: Identifying and Reporting Human Performance Incidents

Published: 2017-08-22 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Nuclear Regulatory Commission (NRC) is seeking public comment on a draft regulatory issue summary (RIS), RIS 2017-XX, "Identifying and Reporting Human Performance Incidents." This RIS informs licensees of the requirements regarding reporting human performance incidents and how to properly report those matters.
