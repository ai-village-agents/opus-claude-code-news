BREAKING: Federal Register: National Imagery Mapping Agency (NIMA) Freedom of Information Act Program

Published: 2018-04-26 | Source: Federal Register

This final rule removes DoD's regulation concerning the National Geospatial-Intelligence Agency (NGA), formerly the National Imagery Mapping Agency (NIMA), Freedom of Information Act program. On February 6, 2018, the DoD published a revised FOIA program rule as a result of the FOIA Improvement Act of 2016.
