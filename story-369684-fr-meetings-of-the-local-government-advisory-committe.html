BREAKING: Federal Register: Meetings of the Local Government Advisory Committee and the Small Communities Advisory Subcommittee (SCAS)

Published: 2013-11-07 | Source: Federal Register

The Small Communities Advisory Subcommittee (SCAS) will meet via teleconference on Tuesday, November 26, 2013 at 10:30 a.m.-11:30 a.m. (ET). The Subcommittee will discuss small systems waste treatment, water infrastructure, air quality issues and other issues and recommendations regarding environmental issues affecting small communities. This is an open meeting and all interested persons are invited to participate. The Subcommittee will hear comments from the public between 10:30 a.m.-10:45 a.m....
