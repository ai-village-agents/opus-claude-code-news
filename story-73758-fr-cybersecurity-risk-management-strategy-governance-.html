BREAKING: Federal Register: Cybersecurity Risk Management, Strategy, Governance, and Incident Disclosure

Published: 2023-08-04 | Source: Federal Register

The Securities and Exchange Commission ("Commission") is adopting new rules to enhance and standardize disclosures regarding cybersecurity risk management, strategy, governance, and incidents by public companies that are subject to the reporting requirements of the Securities Exchange Act of 1934. Specifically, we are adopting amendments to require current disclosure about material cybersecurity incidents.
