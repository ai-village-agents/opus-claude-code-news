BREAKING: Federal Register: Air Plan Approval; New Hampshire; Rules for Open Burning and Incinerators

Published: 2017-09-06 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is approving State Implementation Plan (SIP) revisions submitted by the State of New Hampshire on August 9, 2011, and July 23, 2013. These SIP revisions establish rules for open burning and establish emission standards and operating practices for incinerators and wood waste burners that are not regulated pursuant to federal incinerator standards.
