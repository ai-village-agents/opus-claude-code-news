BREAKING: Federal Register: Request for Information: NASA Public Access Plan for Increasing Access to the Results of NASA-Supported Research

Published: 2023-05-18 | Source: Federal Register

NASA seeks public input on the "NASA's Public Access Plan, Increasing Access to the Results of Scientific Research" (NASA Public Access Plan). NASA has a decades-long history of providing public access to scholarly publications and data resulting from the research it supports, including through the 2014 Open Access Plan.
