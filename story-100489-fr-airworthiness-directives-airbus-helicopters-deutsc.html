BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Airbus Helicopters Deutschland GmbH (AHD) Helicopters

Published: 2022-07-14 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA proposes to adopt a new airworthiness directive (AD) for all Airbus Helicopters Deutschland GmbH (AHD) Model MBB-BK 117 C-2 helicopters. This proposed AD was prompted by reports of excessively worn bolts that connect the cardan-pivot joint with the piston rod of the tail rotor actuator (TRA) assembly.
