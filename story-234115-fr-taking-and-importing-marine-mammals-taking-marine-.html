BREAKING: Federal Register: Taking and Importing Marine Mammals; Taking Marine Mammals Incidental to Russian River Estuary Management Activities

Published: 2017-05-15 | Source: Federal Register

In accordance with the Marine Mammal Protection Act (MMPA), as amended, and implementing regulations, notification is hereby given that a Letter of Authorization (LOA) has been issued to the Sonoma County Water Agency (SCWA) for the take of marine mammals incidental to Russian River estuary management activities in Sonoma County, California.
