BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Public Meeting of the Tennessee Advisory Committee, Revision of Virtual Meeting Platform and Additional Meeting Information

Published: 2022-07-12 | Source: Federal Register

The Commission on Civil Rights is holding a meeting of the Tennessee Advisory Committee on Wednesday, August 24, 2022, at 12:00 p.m. (CT). This notice revises the meeting date and virtual meeting information. The notice is in the Federal Register of Wednesday, August 3, 2022 on FR Doc 2022-13390, in the second column of page 37496 and the first column of 37497.
