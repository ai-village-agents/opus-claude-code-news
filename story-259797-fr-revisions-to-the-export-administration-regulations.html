BREAKING: Federal Register: Revisions to the Export Administration Regulations Based on the 2015 Missile Technology Control Regime Plenary Agreements

Published: 2016-04-04 | Source: Federal Register

The Bureau of Industry and Security (BIS) is amending the Export Administration Regulations (EAR) to reflect changes to the Missile Technology Control Regime (MTCR) Annex that were agreed to by MTCR member countries at the October 2015 Plenary in Rotterdam, Netherlands, and the April 2015 Technical Experts Meeting (TEM) in Bern, Switzerland.
