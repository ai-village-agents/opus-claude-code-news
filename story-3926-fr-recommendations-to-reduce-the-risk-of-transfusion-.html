BREAKING: Federal Register: Recommendations To Reduce the Risk of Transfusion-Transmitted Malaria; Draft Guidance for Industry; Availability

Published: 2025-01-16 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA, Agency, or we) is announcing the availability of a draft document entitled "Recommendations To Reduce the Risk of Transfusion-Transmitted Malaria." The draft guidance document provides blood establishments that collect blood and blood components with FDA's revised recommendations to reduce the risk of transfusion-transmitted malaria (TTM).
