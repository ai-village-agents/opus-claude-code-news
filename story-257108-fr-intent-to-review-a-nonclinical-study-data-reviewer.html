BREAKING: Federal Register: Intent to Review a Nonclinical Study Data Reviewer's Guide Template

Published: 2016-03-04 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA), Center for Drug Evaluation and Research (CDER), is establishing a public docket to collect comments related to a proposed Nonclinical Study Data Reviewer's Guide (SDRG) template. As part of FDA's ongoing collaboration with the Pharmaceutical Users Software Exchange (PhUSE), an independent, non-profit consortium addressing computational science issues, a PhUSE working group developed the PhUSE Nonclinical SRDG template.
