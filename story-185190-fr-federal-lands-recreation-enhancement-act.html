BREAKING: Federal Register: Federal Lands Recreation Enhancement Act

Published: 2019-07-31 | Source: Federal Register

The Chippewa National Forest is seeking public comments on a proposal that would change recreation fees to enhance campgrounds. Fee changes are being proposed at the Norway Beach Recreation Area, including an $8 amenity fee for those campsites with electricity. Fees are assessed based on the level of amenities and services provided, cost of operations and maintenance, and market assessment.
