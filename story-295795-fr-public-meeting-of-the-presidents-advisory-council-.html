BREAKING: Federal Register: Public Meeting of the President's Advisory Council on Financial Capability for Young Americans

Published: 2015-06-08 | Source: Federal Register

The President's Advisory Council on Financial Capability for Young Americans (Council) will convene for a public meeting on June 17, 2015 at 3:00 p.m. Eastern Time via teleconference. The teleconference will be open to the public. Details about how to access the teleconference are posted on the Council's Web site at http:// www.treasury.gov/pacfcya.
