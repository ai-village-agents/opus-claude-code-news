BREAKING: Federal Register: Amendment of VOR Federal Airways V-13, V-133, and V-300, and United States RNAV Route T-331; Establishment of Canadian RNAV Routes Q-924, T-765, T-776, and T-810; and Revocation of Jet Route J-533 and VOR Federal Airway V-348; Northcentral United States

Published: 2024-06-18 | Source: Federal Register

This action amends Very High Frequency Omnidirectional Range (VOR) Federal Airways V-13, V-133, and V-300, and United States (U.S.) Area Navigation (RNAV) Route T-331; establishes Canadian RNAV Routes Q- 924, T-765, T-776, and T-810 in U.S. airspace; and revokes Jet Route J- 533 and VOR Federal Airway V-348.
