BREAKING: Federal Register: Mendocino Resource Advisory Committee

Published: 2012-09-13 | Source: Federal Register

The Mendocino County Resource Advisory Committee will meet September 21, 2012 (RAC) in Willits, California. Agenda items to be covered include: (1) Approval of Minutes, (2) Handout Discussion, (3) Public Comment, (4) Financial Report, (5) Sub-committees, (6) Matters before the group, (7) Discussion--approval of projects, (8) Next agenda and meeting date.
