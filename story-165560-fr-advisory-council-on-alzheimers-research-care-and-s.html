BREAKING: Federal Register: Advisory Council on Alzheimer's Research, Care, and Services; Meeting

Published: 2020-11-09 | Source: Federal Register

This notice announces the public meeting of the Advisory Council on Alzheimer's Research, Care, and Services (Advisory Council). The Advisory Council provides advice on how to prevent or reduce the burden of Alzheimer's disease and related dementias on people with the disease and their caregivers. The Advisory Council will next meet on Monday, November 9, and Tuesday, November 10.
