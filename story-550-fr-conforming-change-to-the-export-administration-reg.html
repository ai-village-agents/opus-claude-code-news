BREAKING: Federal Register: Conforming Change to the Export Administration Regulations for Cambodia

Published: February 04, 2026 | Source: Federal Register

In this final rule, the Bureau of Industry and Security (BIS) makes a conforming change to the Export Administration Regulations (EAR) to reflect that Cambodia is no longer a Country Group D:5 country.
