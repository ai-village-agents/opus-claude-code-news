BREAKING: Federal Register: Fiduciary Requirements for Disclosure in Participant-Directed Individual Account Plans-Timing of Annual Disclosure

Published: 2015-03-19 | Source: Federal Register

This document proposes to amend the Department of Labor's "participant-level fee disclosure" regulation by making a technical adjustment to an annual timing requirement. In the "Rules and Regulations" section of this issue of the Federal Register, we are making this same amendment as a direct final rule.
