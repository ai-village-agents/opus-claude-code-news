BREAKING: Federal Register: Proposed Information Collection Activity; Child Support Portal Registration (Office of Management and Budget #: 0970-0370)

Published: 2024-07-17 | Source: Federal Register

The Office of Child Support Services (OCSS), Administration for Children and Families (ACF), is requesting the federal Office of Management and Budget (OMB) approve the "Child Support Portal Registration," with minor revisions, for an additional three years. The OCSS Child Support Portal ("Portal") contains applications to help state child support agencies administer their programs.
