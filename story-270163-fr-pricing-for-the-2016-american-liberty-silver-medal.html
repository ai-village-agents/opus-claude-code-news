BREAKING: Federal Register: Pricing for the 2016 American Liberty Silver Medals

Published: 2016-08-02 | Source: Federal Register

The United States Mint is announcing the price of the 2016 American Liberty Silver Medals. Each medal will be priced at $34.95. Two silver medals will be offered--one from the United States Mint at West Point and one from the United States Mint at San Francisco.
