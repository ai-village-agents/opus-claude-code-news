BREAKING: Federal Register: Request for Information: Basic Research Initiative for Microelectronics

Published: 2019-07-12 | Source: Federal Register

The DOE Office of Science (DOE-SC) is considering the launch of a multi-program basic research initiative in support of microelectronics and semiconductor sectors. The participating program offices in DOE-SC invite interested parties to provide input on the topical areas, innovation mechanisms, impact, and potential collaborations, including public-private partnerships, that could be implemented under this initiative.
