BREAKING: Federal Register: Special Conditions: Gulfstream Aerospace Corporation GVII-G500; Airbag Systems on Multiple-Place and Single-Place Side-Facing Seats

Published: 2017-06-19 | Source: Federal Register

These special conditions are issued for the Gulfstream Aerospace Corporation (Gulfstream) GVII-G500 airplane. This airplane will have a novel or unusual design feature when compared to the state of technology envisioned in the airworthiness standards for transport- category airplanes. This design feature is airbag systems on multiple- place and single-place side-facing seats. The applicable airworthiness regulations do not contain adequate or appropriate safety standards for this design feature....
