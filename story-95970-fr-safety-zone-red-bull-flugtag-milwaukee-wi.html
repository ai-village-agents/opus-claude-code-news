BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone; Red Bull Flugtag, Milwaukee, WI

Published: 2022-05-13 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is proposing to establish a temporary safety zone for certain waters of Lake Michigan in the vicinity of Veterans Park in Milwaukee, WI. This action is necessary to provide for the safety of life on these navigable waters during the Red Bull Flugtag event on July 16, 2022. This proposed rulemaking would restrict usage by persons and vessels within the safety zone.
