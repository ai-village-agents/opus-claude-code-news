BREAKING: Federal Register: General Services Administration Acquisition Regulation (GSAR); Transactional Data Reporting; Extension of Time for Comments

Published: 2015-05-06 | Source: Federal Register

The General Services Administration (GSA) issued a proposed rule on March 4, 2015, amending the General Services Administration Acquisition Regulation (GSAR) to include clauses that would require vendors to report transactional data from orders and prices paid by ordering activities.
