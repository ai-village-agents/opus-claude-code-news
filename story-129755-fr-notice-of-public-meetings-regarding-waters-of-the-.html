BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Public Meetings Regarding “Waters of the United States”; Establishment of a Public Docket; Request for Recommendations

Published: 2021-08-04 | Source: Federal Register

On June 9, 2021, the U.S. Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) and the Department of the Army announced their intent to revise the definition of ``waters of the United States.'' This process includes two rulemakings: A foundational rule to restore longstanding protections, and a second rulemaking process that builds on that regulatory foundation.
