BREAKING: Federal Register: Draft Guidance for Industry on Abuse-Deterrent Opioids-Evaluation and Labeling; Availability

Published: 2013-01-14 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA) is announcing the availability of a draft guidance for industry entitled "Abuse- Deterrent Opioids--Evaluation and Labeling." The draft guidance describes how abuse-deterrent properties of opioid analgesic products should be studied and evaluated, and what claims regarding such properties may be suitable for inclusion in labeling.
