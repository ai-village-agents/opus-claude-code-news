BREAKING: Federal Register: Approval of California Plan Revisions, Northern Sonoma County Air Pollution Control District; Stationary Source Permits

Published: 2018-04-04 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is proposing to approve revisions to the Northern Sonoma County Air Pollution Control District (NSCAPCD or District) portion of the California State Implementation Plan (SIP). This revision concerns the District's prevention of significant deterioration (PSD) permitting program for new and modified sources of air pollution.
