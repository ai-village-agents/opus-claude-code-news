BREAKING: Federal Register: Presumptive Service Connection for Rare Respiratory Cancers Due to Exposure to Fine Particulate Matter

Published: 2022-04-26 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Veterans Affairs (VA) is issuing this interim final rule to amend its adjudication regulations to establish presumptive service connection for nine rare respiratory cancers in association with presumed exposure to fine particulate matter.
