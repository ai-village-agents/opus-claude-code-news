BREAKING: Federal Register: Drawbridge Operation Regulation; Rice Creek, Putnam County, FL

Published: 2016-11-10 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard proposes to modify the operating schedule that governs the CSX Railroad Bridge across the Rice Creek, mile 0.8, in Palatka, Putnam County, FL. This proposed rule would change the existing open on demand during the day and 24 hour advance notice for a bridge opening during the night, to 24 hour advance notice for an opening at all times.
