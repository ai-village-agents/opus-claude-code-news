BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Final Federal Agency Actions on the Chesapeake Bay Crossing Study Tier 1 Environmental Impact Statement (EIS) in Maryland

Published: 2022-04-29 | Source: Federal Register

This notice announces actions taken by FHWA that are final. These actions relate to the Chesapeake Bay Crossing Study: Tier 1 NEPA (Bay Crossing Study) conducted to identify a corridor for a new crossing of the Chesapeake Bay in Maryland. The public is advised that FHWA issued a Record of Decision (ROD) which concludes the Tier 1 Bay Crossing Study.
