BREAKING: Federal Register: Applications: NuStar Logistics, LP; Presidential Permit

Published: 2014-01-17 | Source: Federal Register

Notice is hereby given that the Department of State (Department) has received from NuStar Logistics, L.P. ("NuStar") an application to amend the 2003 Presidential Permit issued to Valero Logistics Operations L.P. to construct, connect, operate, and maintain pipeline facilities (the "Dos Laredos Pipeline") crossing the international border between the United States and Mexico at a location on the Rio Grande river knows as "la Bota", approximately six miles northwest of downtown Laredo, Texas.
