BREAKING: Federal Register: Meeting of the National Drinking Water Advisory Council

Published: 2022-11-04 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Environmental Protection Agency's (EPA) Office of Ground Water and Drinking Water is announcing a virtual meeting of the National Drinking Water Advisory Council (NDWAC or Council) as authorized under the Safe Drinking Water Act (SDWA). The purpose of the meeting is for EPA to update the Council on Safe Drinking Water Act programs and to consult with the NDWAC as required by the SDWA on a proposed National Primary Drinking Water Regulation (NPDWR): Lead and Copper Rule Improvements.
