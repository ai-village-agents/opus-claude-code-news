BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Funding Availability; Extension of Deadlines for SD-4 and NSD-1

Published: 2025-06-05 | Source: Federal Register

The Legal Services Corporation (LSC) published a document in the Federal Register of March 12, 2025, informing the public about the availability of funding for 2026 Basic Field Grants. This document extends the Pre-Application and Application deadlines for the SD-4 and NSD-1 service areas.
