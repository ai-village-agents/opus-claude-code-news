BREAKING: Federal Register: Amendment of Class C Airspace; Lansing, MI

Published: 2020-01-22 | Source: Federal Register

This action modifies the Lansing, MI, Class C airspace area by amending the effective hours to coincide with the associated radar approach control facility hours of operation. The designated boundaries and altitudes of the Lansing, MI, Class C airspace area are not changed. Class C airspace areas are predicated on an operational air traffic control tower serviced by a radar approach control facility.
