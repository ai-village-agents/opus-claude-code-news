BREAKING: Federal Register: Request for Citizens Coinage Advisory Committee Membership Applications

Published: 2023-10-16 | Source: Federal Register

The United States Mint is accepting applications for appointment to the Citizens Coinage Advisory Committee (CCAC) as the member specially qualified to serve on the CCAC by virtue of their education, training, or experience in numismatics.
