BREAKING: Federal Register: Annual Stress Test-Schedule Shift and Adjustments to Regulatory Capital Projections

Published: 2014-07-01 | Source: Federal Register

The Office of the Comptroller of the Currency (OCC) proposes to adjust the timing of the annual stress testing cycle and to clarify the method used to calculate regulatory capital in the stress tests. The proposal would shift the dates of the annual stress testing cycle by approximately three months.
