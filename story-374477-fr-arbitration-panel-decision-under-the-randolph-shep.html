BREAKING: Federal Register: Arbitration Panel Decision Under the Randolph-Sheppard Act

Published: 2012-01-09 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Education (Department) gives notice that on August 29, 2011, an arbitration panel rendered a decision in the matter of Judy A. Davis-Perry v. Missouri Department of Social Services Rehabilitation Services for the Blind, Case no. R-S/10-1. The Department convened this panel after receiving a complaint filed by the Complainant, Judy A. Davis-Perry.
