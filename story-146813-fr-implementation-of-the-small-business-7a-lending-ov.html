BREAKING: Federal Register: Implementation of the Small Business 7(a) Lending Oversight Reform Act of 2018

Published: 2020-03-16 | Source: Federal Register

The Small Business Administration ("SBA" or "Agency") is amending its business loan program regulations to implement the Small Business 7(a) Lending Oversight Reform Act of 2018 ("Act") and make other amendments that will strengthen SBA's lender oversight and ensure the integrity of the business loan programs.
