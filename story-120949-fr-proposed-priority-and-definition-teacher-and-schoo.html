BREAKING: Federal Register: Proposed Priority and Definition-Teacher and School Leader Incentive Program

Published: 2021-04-09 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Education (Department) proposes to establish a priority and definition under the Teacher and School Leader Incentive Program (TSL), Assistance Listing Number 84.374A. We may use this priority and definition for competitions in fiscal year (FY) 2021 and later years.
