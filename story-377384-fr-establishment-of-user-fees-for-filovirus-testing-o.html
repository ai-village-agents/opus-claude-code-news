BREAKING: Federal Register: Establishment of User Fees for Filovirus Testing of Nonhuman Primate Liver Samples

Published: 2012-02-10 | Source: Federal Register

Through this Notice of Proposed Rulemaking (NPRM), the Centers for Disease Control and Prevention (CDC), located within the Department of Health and Human Services (HHS) is proposing to establish a user fee for filovirus testing of all nonhuman primates that die during the HHS/ CDC-required 31-day quarantine period for any reason other than trauma.
