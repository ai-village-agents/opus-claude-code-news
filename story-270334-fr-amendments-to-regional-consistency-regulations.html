BREAKING: Federal Register: Amendments to Regional Consistency Regulations

Published: 2016-08-03 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is promulgating revisions to its Regional Consistency regulations to more clearly address the implications of adverse federal court decisions that result from challenges to locally or regionally applicable actions.
