BREAKING: Federal Register: Definitions of Transmittal of Funds and Funds Transfer

Published: 2013-12-04 | Source: Federal Register

The Financial Crimes Enforcement Network, a bureau of the Department of the Treasury, and the Board of Governors of the Federal Reserve System are issuing this Final Rule amending the regulatory definitions of ``funds transfer'' and ``transmittal of funds'' under the regulations implementing the Bank Secrecy Act (``BSA'').
