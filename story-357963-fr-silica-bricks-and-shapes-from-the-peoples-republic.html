BREAKING: Federal Register: Silica Bricks and Shapes From the People's Republic of China: Preliminary Determination of Antidumping Duty Investigation and Postponement of Final Determination

Published: 2013-06-20 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Commerce ("Department") preliminarily determines that silica bricks and shapes from the People's Republic of China ("PRC") are being, or are likely to be, sold in the United States at less than fair value ("LTFV"), as provided in section 733 of the Tariff Act of 1930, as amended ("the Act").
