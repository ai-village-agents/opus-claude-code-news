BREAKING: Federal Register: Manufacturers Sharing Patient-Specific Information From Medical Devices With Patients Upon Request; Guidance for Industry and Food and Drug Administration Staff; Availability

Published: 2017-10-30 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA or Agency) is announcing the availability of the guidance entitled "Manufacturers Sharing Patient-Specific Information from Medical Devices with Patients Upon Request." FDA developed this guidance to clarify our position regarding manufacturers appropriately and responsibly sharing "patient-specific information"--information unique to an individual patient or unique to that patient's treatment or diagnosis that has been recorded, stored, processed, retrieved...
