BREAKING: Federal Register: Certain Steel Nails From the Republic of Korea: Final Results of Antidumping Duty Administrative Review; 2019-2020; Correction

Published: 2022-02-03 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Commerce (Commerce) published notice in the Federal Register of January 20, 2022, in which Commerce announced the final results of the 2019-2020 administrative review of the antidumping duty (AD) order on certain steel nails from the Republic of Korea (Korea). This notice was a duplicate for a notice published on January 19, 2022, in which Commerce announced the final results of the 2019- 2020 administrative review of the AD order on certain steel nails from Korea.
