BREAKING: Federal Register: Overseas Security Advisory Council (OSAC) Meeting

Published: 2020-02-06 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of State announces meetings of the U.S. State Department's Overseas Security Advisory Council on February 28, June 3, and November 17, 2020. Pursuant to Section 10(d) of the Federal Advisory Committee Act, it has been determined that the meetings will be closed to the public.
