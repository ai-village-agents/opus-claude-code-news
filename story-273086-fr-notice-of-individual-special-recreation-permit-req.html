BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Individual Special Recreation Permit Requirement in the King Range National Conservation Area, California

Published: 2016-09-06 | Source: Federal Register

The Bureau of Land Management (BLM) provides notice of a new Individual Special Recreation Permit (ISRP) requirement for overnight use in the King Range Wilderness and Backcountry Management Zone (King Range Wilderness). The ISRP will limit the number of persons entering the King Range Wilderness for overnight use to 60 persons/day during the peak season (May 15-September 15), and 30 persons/day during the non-peak season (September 16-May 14).
