BREAKING: Federal Register: Protection of Marine Archaeological Resources

Published: 2023-02-15 | Source: Federal Register

BOEM proposes to require lessees and operators to submit an archaeological report with any oil and gas exploration or development plan they submit to BOEM for approval of activities proposed on the Outer Continental Shelf (OCS). An archaeological report is currently required only if the plan covers an area that a BOEM Regional Director has reason to believe may contain an archaeological resource.
