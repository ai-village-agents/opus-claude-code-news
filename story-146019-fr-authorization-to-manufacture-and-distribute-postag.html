BREAKING: Federal Register: Authorization To Manufacture and Distribute Postage Evidencing Systems

Published: 2020-03-05 | Source: Federal Register

The Postal Service is amending its Postage Evidencing Systems regulations. These changes put the financial responsibility for returned checks and returned Automated Clearinghouse (ACH) debit payments on the applicable resetting company (RC) and PC Postage provider.
