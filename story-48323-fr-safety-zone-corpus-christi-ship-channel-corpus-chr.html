BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone; Corpus Christi Ship Channel, Corpus Christi, TX

Published: 2024-09-27 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is establishing a temporary safety zone for certain navigable waters of the Corpus Christi Ship Channel. The safety zone is needed to protect personnel, vessels, and the marine environment from potential hazards created by the removal of pipeline from the floor of the Corpus Christi Ship Channel near gated pair lights 31 and 32.
