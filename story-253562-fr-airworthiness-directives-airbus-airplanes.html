BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Airbus Airplanes

Published: 2016-01-21 | Source: Federal Register

We are superseding Airworthiness Directive (AD) 98-18-26, for certain Airbus Model A320 series airplanes. AD 98-18-26 required repetitive inspections to detect fatigue cracking of the front spar vertical stringers on the wings; and repair, if necessary.
