BREAKING: Federal Register: Final National Pollutant Discharge Elimination System (NPDES) General Permit for Stormwater Discharges From Construction Activities

Published: 2017-01-19 | Source: Federal Register

All ten EPA Regions today are issuing the 2017 National Pollutant Discharge Elimination System (NPDES) general permit for stormwater discharges from construction activities to waters of the United States, also referred to as the "2017 Construction General Permit (CGP)." The 2017 CGP replaces the existing general permit (the "2012 CGP") covering stormwater discharges from construction activities that expires on February 16, 2017.
