BREAKING: Federal Register: Modification of Class D Airspace; McChord Field (Joint Base Lewis-McChord), WA

Published: 2021-11-26 | Source: Federal Register

This action modifies the Class D airspace at McChord Field (Joint Base Lewis-McChord), Tacoma, WA. After a review of the airspace, the FAA found it necessary to amend the existing airspace for the safety and management of Instrument Flight Rules (IFR) operations at this location and Visual Flight Rules (VFR) at a satellite airport.
