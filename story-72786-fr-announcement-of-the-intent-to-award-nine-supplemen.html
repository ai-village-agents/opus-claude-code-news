BREAKING: Federal Register: Announcement of the Intent To Award Nine Supplements to ORR Grant Recipients in Seven States

Published: 2023-07-25 | Source: Federal Register

ACF, ORR, Division of Refugee Health (DRH) announces the intent to award supplements in the aggregate amount of up to $466,563 to nine grant recipients under Notice of Funding Opportunity: Direct Services for Survivors of Torture, HHS-2022-ACF-ORR-ZT-0051. The purpose of the awards is to ensure that the 222 Nicaraguan Humanitarian Parolees (NHP) who were brought to the United States will have access to holistic care and services.
