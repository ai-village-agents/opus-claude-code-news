BREAKING: Federal Register: Soybean Promotion and Research: Amend the Order To Adjust Representation on the United Soybean Board

Published: 2013-01-02 | Source: Federal Register

This final rule adjusts the number of members on the United Soybean Board (Board) to reflect changes in production levels that have occurred since the Board was reapportioned in 2009. As required by the Soybean Promotion, Research, and Consumer Information Act (Act), membership is reviewed every 3 years and adjustments are made accordingly.
