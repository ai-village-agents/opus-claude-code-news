BREAKING: Federal Register: Energy Conservation Program: Test Procedure for Direct Heating Equipment

Published: 2021-04-16 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Department of Energy (DOE) proposes to amend the test procedure for direct heating equipment (DHE) to incorporate by reference the most recent versions of the industry consensus test standards currently referenced in the Federal test procedure.
