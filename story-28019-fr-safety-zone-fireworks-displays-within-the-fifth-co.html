BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone; Fireworks Displays Within the Fifth Coast Guard District; The Wharf, Washington, DC

Published: 2024-01-25 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard will enforce a safety zone for a fireworks display at "The Wharf DC," in Washington, DC, on March 23, 2024 or, if necessary, due to inclement weather, on March 24, 2024, to provide for the safety of life on navigable waterways during this event. Our regulation, "Safety Zones; Fireworks Displays within the Fifth Coast Guard District," identifies the precise location.
