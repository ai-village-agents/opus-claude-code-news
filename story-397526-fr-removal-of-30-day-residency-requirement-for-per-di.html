BREAKING: Federal Register: Removal of 30-Day Residency Requirement for Per Diem Payments

Published: 2012-09-27 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Veterans Affairs (VA) is taking direct final action to amend its regulations concerning per diem payments to State homes for the provision of nursing home care to veterans. Specifically, this rule removes the requirement that a veteran must have resided in a State home for 30 consecutive days before VA will pay per diem for that veteran when there is no overnight stay.
