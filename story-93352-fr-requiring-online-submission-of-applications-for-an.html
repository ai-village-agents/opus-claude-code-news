BREAKING: Federal Register: Requiring Online Submission of Applications for and Renewals of DEA Registration

Published: 2022-04-11 | Source: Federal Register

This rulemaking amends the Drug Enforcement Administration's (DEA) regulations to now require all applications for DEA registrations, and renewal of those registrations, to be submitted online.
