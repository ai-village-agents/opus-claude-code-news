BREAKING: Federal Register: Grant Program To Build Tribal Energy Development Capacity

Published: 2013-12-20 | Source: Federal Register

The Secretary of the Interior (Secretary), through the Office of Indian Energy and Economic Development (IEED), is soliciting grant proposals from Indian tribes for projects to build tribal capacity for energy resource development under the Department of the Interior's (DOI) Tribal Energy Development Capacity (TEDC) grant program.
