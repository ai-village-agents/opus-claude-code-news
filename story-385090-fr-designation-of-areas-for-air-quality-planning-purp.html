BREAKING: Federal Register: Designation of Areas for Air Quality Planning Purposes; California; Western Mojave Desert Ozone Nonattainment Area; Reclassification to Severe

Published: 2012-05-08 | Source: Federal Register

Under the Clean Air Act (CAA or Act), EPA is granting a request from the State of California to reclassify the Western Mojave Desert ozone nonattainment area from "Moderate" to "Severe-15" for the 1997 8-hour ozone National Ambient Air Quality Standards (NAAQS).
