BREAKING: Federal Register: Takes of Marine Mammals Incidental to Specified Activities; Pile Placement for Fishermen's Offshore Wind Farm

Published: 2012-07-06 | Source: Federal Register

In accordance with the Marine Mammal Protection Act (MMPA) implementing regulations, notification is hereby given that NMFS has issued an incidental harassment authorization (IHA) to Fishermen's Atlantic City Windfarm, LLC (Fishermen's), allowing the take of small numbers of marine mammals, by Level B harassment only, incidental to pile driving off the New Jersey coast.
