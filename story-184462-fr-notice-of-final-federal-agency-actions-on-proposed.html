BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Final Federal Agency Actions on Proposed Highway in California

Published: 2019-07-22 | Source: Federal Register

The FHWA, on behalf of Caltrans, is issuing this notice to announce actions taken by Caltrans that are final. The actions relate to a proposed highway project, where the project limits extend approximately 2 miles north of SR-91 along I-15, approximately 2 miles west of I-15 along SR-91 and approximately 0.5 mile east of I-15 along SR-91 to Prominade Avenue in the County of Riverside, State of California.
