BREAKING: Federal Register: Government Auditing Standards-2024 Revision

Published: 2024-02-08 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Government Accountability Office (GAO) has issued its 2024 revision to Government Auditing Standards, known as the "Yellow Book." To help ensure that the standards continue to meet the needs of the government auditing community, the Comptroller General of the United States established the Yellow Book Advisory Council to provide input on revisions to the Yellow Book.
