BREAKING: Federal Register: Promoting Effective Drug Development Programs: Opportunities and Priorities for the Food and Drug Administration's Office of New Drugs; Public Meeting; Request for Comments

Published: 2019-08-12 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA, the Agency, or we) is holding a public meeting on November 7, 2019 entitled ``Promoting Effective Drug Development Programs: Opportunities and Priorities for FDA's Office of New Drugs.'' The purpose of the public meeting is to solicit specific, actionable policy suggestions that could be implemented in the near-term by the review staff of the Center for Drug Evaluation and Research's (CDER's) Office of New Drugs to promote effective drug development program...
