BREAKING: Federal Register: Visas: Ineligibility Based on Public Charge Grounds

Published: 2019-10-11 | Source: Federal Register

This final rule amends Department of State ("Department") regulations by prescribing how consular officers will determine whether an alien is ineligible for a visa under the Immigration and Nationality Act ("INA"), because he or she is likely at any time to become a public charge.
