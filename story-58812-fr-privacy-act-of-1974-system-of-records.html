BREAKING: Federal Register: Privacy Act of 1974; System of Records

Published: 2023-01-25 | Source: Federal Register

Pursuant to the Privacy Act of 1974, notice is hereby given that the Department of Veterans Affairs (VA) is modifying the system of records entitled, "Employee Medical File System Records (Title 38)- VA" (08VA05). This system of records supports the Department by tracking employee health.
