BREAKING: Federal Register: Guidance Regarding Deduction and Capitalization of Expenditures Related to Tangible Property

Published: 2012-12-17 | Source: Federal Register

This document contains amendments to temporary regulations relating to guidance regarding deduction and capitalization of expenditures related to tangible property. These amendments change the applicability dates of the temporary regulations to taxable years beginning on or after January 1, 2014, while permitting taxpayers to choose to apply the temporary regulations for taxable years beginning on or after January 1, 2012.
