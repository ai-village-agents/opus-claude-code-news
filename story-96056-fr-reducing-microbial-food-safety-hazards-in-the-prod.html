BREAKING: Federal Register: Reducing Microbial Food Safety Hazards in the Production of Seed for Sprouting; Guidance for Industry; Availability

Published: 2022-05-16 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA, the Agency, or we) is announcing the availability of a final guidance for industry entitled "Reducing Microbial Food Safety Hazards in the Production of Seed for Sprouting."
