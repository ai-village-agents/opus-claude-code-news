BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Intent To Repatriate Cultural Items: Boston Children's Museum, Boston, MA

Published: 2023-12-22 | Source: Federal Register

In accordance with the Native American Graves Protection and Repatriation Act (NAGPRA), Boston Children's Museum intends to repatriate a certain cultural item that meets the definition of an unassociated funerary object and that has a cultural affiliation with the Indian Tribes or Native Hawaiian organizations in this notice. The cultural item was removed from the Southeastern United States.
