BREAKING: Federal Register: Defense Federal Acquisition Regulation Supplement: Use of Fixed-Price Contracts for Certain Major Defense Acquisition Programs (DFARS Case 2023-D009)

Published: 2023-09-29 | Source: Federal Register

DoD is proposing to amend the Defense Federal Acquisition Regulation Supplement (DFARS) to implement a section of the National Defense Authorization Act for Fiscal Year 2023that limits the number of low-rate initial production lots associated with a major defense acquisition program under certain circumstances.
