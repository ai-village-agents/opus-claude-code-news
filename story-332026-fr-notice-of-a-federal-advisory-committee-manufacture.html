BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of a Federal Advisory Committee; Manufactured Housing Consensus Committee; Technical Systems Subcommittee Teleconference

Published: 2014-08-13 | Source: Federal Register

This notice sets forth the schedule and proposed agenda for a teleconference meeting of the Manufactured Housing Consensus Committee (MHCC), Technical Systems Subcommittee. The teleconference meeting is open to the public. The agenda provides an opportunity for citizens to comment on the business before the consensus committee.
