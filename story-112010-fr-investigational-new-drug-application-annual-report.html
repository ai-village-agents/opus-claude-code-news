BREAKING: Federal Register: Investigational New Drug Application Annual Reporting

Published: 2022-12-09 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA, the Agency, or we) is proposing to replace its current annual reporting requirement for investigational new drug applications (INDs) with a new requirement: the annual FDA development safety update report (FDA DSUR).
