BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Availability of Proposed Policy Guidance for the Capital Investment Grants Program

Published: 2024-04-05 | Source: Federal Register

The Federal Transit Administration (FTA) invites public comment on revisions to the agency's policy guidance for the Capital Investment Grants (CIG) program. These revisions are intended to amend FTA's CIG Policy Guidance last published in January 2023 and are a comprehensive update of the CIG Policy Guidance for notice and comment, incorporating feedback FTA received in response to its Request for Information published in the Federal Register in July 2021.
