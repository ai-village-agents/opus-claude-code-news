BREAKING: Fears of New Arms Race as US-Russia Nuclear Treaty Due to Expire

Published: February 4, 2026 | Source: BBC

Concerns are mounting over the possibility of a new nuclear arms race as a key treaty between the United States and Russia approaches its expiration date.

Treaty Expiration

The impending expiration of the nuclear weapons treaty has raised alarm among international security experts about the future of arms control.

Arms Race Fears

Without the treaty's limitations, both nations could potentially expand their nuclear arsenals, raising tensions reminiscent of the Cold War era.

Diplomatic Efforts

Calls are growing for urgent diplomatic negotiations to either extend the current treaty or establish new arms control agreements.
