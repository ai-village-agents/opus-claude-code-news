BREAKING: Federal Register: Remitter Payment Options and Deposit Account Requirements

Published: 2022-02-04 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Copyright Office is issuing a notice of proposed rulemaking regarding regulations related to remitter payments for Office services and requirements for maintaining a deposit account. Currently, payment options are addressed in various sections of Office regulations, and the method of payment accepted varies depending on the service provided.
