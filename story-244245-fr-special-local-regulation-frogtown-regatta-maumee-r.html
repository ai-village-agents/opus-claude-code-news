BREAKING: Federal Register: Special Local Regulation; Frogtown Regatta, Maumee River, Toledo, OH

Published: 2017-09-21 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is establishing a temporary special local regulation for all navigable waters of the Maumee River, Toledo, OH from the Martin Luther King Jr. Memorial Bridge at Maumee river mile 4.30 to the Michael DiSalle Bridge at River mile 6.73. This regulated area is necessary to protect spectators and vessels from potential hazards associated with the Frogtown Regatta.
