BREAKING: Federal Register: Non-Oriented Electrical Steel From Taiwan: Preliminary Determination of Sales at Less Than Fair Value and Postponement of Final Determination

Published: 2014-05-22 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Commerce ("Department") preliminarily determines that non-oriented electrical steel ("NOES") from Taiwan is being, or is likely to be, sold in the United States at less than fair value ("LTFV"), as provided in section 733(b) of the Tariff Act of 1930, as amended ("the Act"). The period of investigation ("POI") is July 1, 2012, through June 30, 2013. The estimated weighted-average dumping margins are listed in the "Preliminary Determination" section of this notice.
