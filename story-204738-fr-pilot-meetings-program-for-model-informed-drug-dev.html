BREAKING: Federal Register: Pilot Meetings Program for Model-Informed Drug Development Approaches

Published: 2018-04-17 | Source: Federal Register

The sixth iteration of the Prescription Drug User Fee Act (PDUFA VI), incorporated as part of the FDA Reauthorization Act of 2017 (FDARA), highlights the goal of advancing model-informed drug development (MIDD). The Food and Drug Administration (FDA or Agency) is announcing a pilot program that affords sponsors or applicants who are selected for participation the opportunity to meet with Agency staff to discuss MIDD approaches in medical product development.
