BREAKING: Federal Register: Cooperative Research and Development Agreement-Cloud Suitability of Radio Frequency (RF) Communications Capabilities

Published: 2019-11-25 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard announces its intent to enter into a cooperative research and development agreement (CRADA) with several companies to evaluate the suitability of implementing Radio Frequency (RF) communications capabilities as virtualized services in a commercial cloud/Secure Cloud Computing Architecture (SCCA) environment.
