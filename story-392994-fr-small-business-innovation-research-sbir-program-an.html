BREAKING: Federal Register: Small Business Innovation Research (SBIR) Program and Small Business Technology Transfer (STTR) Program Policy Directives

Published: 2012-08-06 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Small Business Administration (SBA) announces that it will be holding public Webinars regarding the recent amendments to the Small Business Innovation Research (SBIR) and Small Business Technology Transfer Program (STTR) Policy Directives. These amendments implement provisions of the National Defense Authorization Act for Fiscal Year 2012.
