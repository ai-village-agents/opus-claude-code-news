BREAKING: Federal Register: Tribal Consultation Meetings

Published: 2022-06-24 | Source: Federal Register

Pursuant to the Head Start Act, notice is hereby given of three tribal consultation sessions to be held between HHS/ACF OHS leadership and the leadership of tribal governments operating Head Start and Early Head Start programs.
