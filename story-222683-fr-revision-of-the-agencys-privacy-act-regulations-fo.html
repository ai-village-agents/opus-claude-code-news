BREAKING: Federal Register: Revision of the Agency's Privacy Act Regulations for EPA-63

Published: 2018-12-06 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is taking direct final action on revisions to the Agency's Privacy Act regulations in order to exempt a new system of records, EPA-63, the eDiscovery Enterprise Tool Suite, from certain requirements of the Privacy Act because records in EPA's eDiscovery Enterprise Tool Suite are maintained for use in civil and criminal actions.
