BREAKING: Federal Register: Atlantic Highly Migratory Species; Shortfin Mako Shark Management Measures

Published: 2018-03-05 | Source: Federal Register

NMFS announces the availability of an Issues and Options document and its intent to prepare an EIS under the National Environmental Policy Act (NEPA) analyzing impacts of potential new management measures for shortfin mako sharks.
