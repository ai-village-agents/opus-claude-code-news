BREAKING: Federal Register: MidAmerican Wind Energy Habitat Conservation Plan; Draft Environmental Impact Statement

Published: 2016-04-28 | Source: Federal Register

Pursuant to the National Environmental Policy Act of 1969, as amended (NEPA), we are advising the public that we intend to prepare an environmental impact statement (EIS) on a proposed Endangered Species Act (ESA) incidental take permit (ITP) application from MidAmerican Energy Company (MEC) for the federally endangered Indiana bat, the federally threatened northern long-eared bat, the little brown bat, and the bald eagle.
