BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Availability of Record of Decision for the Final Environmental Impact Statement for EA-18G “Growler” Airfield Operations at Naval Air Station Whidbey Island Complex, Island County, WA

Published: 2019-03-19 | Source: Federal Register

The United States Department of the Navy (Navy), after carefully weighing the strategic, operational, and environmental consequences of the proposed action, announces its decision to implement Alternative 2A of the Final Environmental Impact Statement (EIS) for EA-18G "Growler" Airfield Operations at Naval Air Station (NAS) Whidbey Island Complex, WA.
