BREAKING: Federal Register: Operation and Certification of Small Unmanned Aircraft Systems

Published: 2015-02-23 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA is proposing to amend its regulations to adopt specific rules to allow the operation of small unmanned aircraft systems in the National Airspace System. These changes would address the operation of unmanned aircraft systems, certification of their operators, registration, and display of registration markings.
