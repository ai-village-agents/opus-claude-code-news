BREAKING: Federal Register: Fisheries Off West Coast States; Coastal Pelagic Species Fisheries; Annual Specifications

Published: 2017-06-30 | Source: Federal Register

NMFS issues this rule to implement annual management measures and harvest specifications to establish the allowable catch levels (i.e., annual catch limit (ACL)/harvest guideline (HG)) for the northern subpopulation of Pacific sardine (hereafter, Pacific sardine), in the U.S. exclusive economic zone (EEZ) off the Pacific coast for the fishing season of July 1, 2017, through June 30, 2018.
