BREAKING: Federal Register: Great Lakes Pilotage Rates-2018 Annual Review and Revisions to Methodology

Published: 2018-01-18 | Source: Federal Register

In accordance with the Great Lakes Pilotage Act of 1960, the Coast Guard proposes new base pilotage rates and surcharges for the 2018 shipping season. Additionally, the Coast Guard is proposing several changes to the Great Lakes pilotage ratemaking methodology.
