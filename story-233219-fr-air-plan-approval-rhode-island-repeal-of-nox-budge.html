BREAKING: Federal Register: Air Plan Approval; Rhode Island; Repeal of NOX Budget Trading Program

Published: 2017-05-01 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is approving a State Implementation Plan (SIP) revision submitted by the State of Rhode Island. This revision removes Air Pollution Control (APC) Regulation 41, entitled "NOX Budget Trading Program" (Rhode Island NBP) from the Rhode Island SIP.
