BREAKING: Federal Register: Changes to Accounting Requirements for the Community Development Block Grants (CDBG) Program

Published: 2015-11-12 | Source: Federal Register

This rule makes several changes to the existing Community Development Block Grant (CDBG) program regulations in order to better track the use of grant funds and improve accounting procedures in the program. Through this rule, HUD requires grantees to commence tracking the obligations and expenditures of funds for each specific fiscal year grant, rather than track such information cumulatively.
