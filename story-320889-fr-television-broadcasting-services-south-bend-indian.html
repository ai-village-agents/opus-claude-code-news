BREAKING: Federal Register: Television Broadcasting Services; South Bend, Indiana

Published: 2014-04-07 | Source: Federal Register

Previously, the Commission substituted channel 46 for channel 48 at LeSEA's request, and LeSEA now seeks to return to its previously allotted channel 48.
