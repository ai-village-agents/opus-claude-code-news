BREAKING: Federal Register: United States Mint Kids' Baseball Coin Design Challenge

Published: 2013-04-02 | Source: Federal Register

The United States Mint announces the opening of a national kids' baseball coin design challenge on April 11, 2013, that seeks design entries from contestants age 13 years or younger on the theme, "What's Great about Baseball."
