BREAKING: Federal Register: Large Residential Washers From the Republic of Korea: Final Results of Countervailing Duty Administrative Review; 2012-2013

Published: 2015-09-15 | Source: Federal Register

On March 11, 2015, the Department published the preliminary results of the administrative review of the countervailing duty order on large residential washers from Korea.\1\ The review covers two producers/exporters of the subject merchandise, Samsung Electronics Co., Ltd. (Samsung) and Daewoo Electronics Corporation (Daewoo). The period of review (POR) is June 5, 2012, through December 31, 2013.
