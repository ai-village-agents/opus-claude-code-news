BREAKING: Federal Register: Fisheries of the Northeastern United States; Black Sea Bass Fishery; Recreational Quota Harvested

Published: 2012-10-25 | Source: Federal Register

NMFS announces that the 2012 black sea bass recreational harvest limit has been exceeded. No one may fish for or possess black sea bass in Federal waters for the remainder of calendar year 2012, unless issued a Federal moratorium permit and fishing commercially.
