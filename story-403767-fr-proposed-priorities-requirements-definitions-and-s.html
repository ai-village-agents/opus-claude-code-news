BREAKING: Federal Register: Proposed Priorities, Requirements, Definitions, and Selection Criteria-Investing in Innovation Fund

Published: 2012-12-14 | Source: Federal Register

The Assistant Deputy Secretary for Innovation and Improvement proposes priorities, requirements, definitions, and selection criteria under the Investing in Innovation Fund (i3). The Assistant Deputy Secretary may use these priorities, requirements, definitions, and selection criteria for competitions in fiscal year (FY) 2013 and later years.
