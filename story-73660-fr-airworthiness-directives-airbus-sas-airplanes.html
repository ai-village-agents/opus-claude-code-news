BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Airbus SAS Airplanes

Published: 2023-08-03 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA is superseding Airworthiness Directive (AD) 2022-17- 09, which applied to certain Airbus SAS Model A350-941 and -1041 airplanes. AD 2022-17-09 continued to require the actions of AD 2021- 16-03 and required a modification to restore two independent layers of lightning strike protection.
