BREAKING: Federal Register: Hours of Service of Drivers: American Trucking Associations (ATA); Denial of Application for Exemption

Published: 2016-03-28 | Source: Federal Register

FMCSA announces its denial of the application of ATA for an exemption from the 14-hour provision of the Agency's hours-of-service regulations to enable certain drivers to exclude waiting time from their calculations of on-duty time. Currently, only specially trained drivers of commercial motor vehicles (CMVs) that are specially constructed to service oil and natural gas extraction sites may employ this provision.
