BREAKING: Federal Register: Agency Information Collection Activities: OMB Control Number 1014-0018; Oil and Gas Drilling Operations

Published: 2017-07-07 | Source: Federal Register

To comply with the Paperwork Reduction Act of 1995 (PRA), the Bureau of Safety and Environmental Enforcement (BSEE) is inviting comments on a collection of information that we will submit to the Office of Management and Budget (OMB) for review and approval. The information collection request (ICR) concerns a renewal to the paperwork requirements in BSEE's regulations concerning Oil and Gas Drilling Operations.
