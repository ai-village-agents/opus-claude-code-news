BREAKING: Federal Register: Federal Advisory Council on Occupational Safety and Health (FACOSH); Notice of Rechartering

Published: 2023-10-13 | Source: Federal Register

The Occupational Safety and Health Administration (OSHA) hereby announces that the FACOSH charter will be renewed for a two-year period pursuant to the Federal Advisory Committee Act (FACA) and in accordance with the Committee Management Secretariat, General Services Administration.
