BREAKING: Federal Register: Air Plan Approval; Indiana; Regional Haze Five-Year Progress Report State Implementation Plan

Published: 2018-02-02 | Source: Federal Register

Indiana's SIP revision addresses requirements of the Clean Air Act (CAA) and EPA's rules that require states to submit periodic reports describing progress toward reasonable progress goals (RPGs) established for regional haze and a determination of the adequacy of the state's existing regional haze SIP.
