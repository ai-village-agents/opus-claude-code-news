BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Proposed Revision to Requirements for the Importation of Grapes From Chile Into the United States

Published: 2022-10-17 | Source: Federal Register

We are advising the public that we have prepared a pest risk assessment (PRA) and a commodity import evaluation document (CIED) relative to the importation into the United States of fresh table grapes from regions of Chile where European grapevine moth (Lobesia botrana, EGVM) is either absent or at very low prevalence.
