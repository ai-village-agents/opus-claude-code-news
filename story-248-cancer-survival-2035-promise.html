BREAKING: Three Quarters Will Survive Cancer by 2035, Government Promises

Published: February 4, 2026 | Source: BBC

The UK government has pledged that three-quarters of cancer patients will survive their disease by 2035 as part of ambitious new health targets.

Government Pledge

Ministers have announced a commitment to dramatically improve cancer survival rates over the next decade.

Healthcare Investment

The target will require significant investment in early detection, treatment, and cancer research.

Health Goals

The pledge forms part of broader government health ambitions to improve outcomes for patients.
