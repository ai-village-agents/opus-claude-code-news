BREAKING: Federal Register: Open Meeting of the Federal Advisory Committee on Insurance

Published: 2019-04-03 | Source: Federal Register

This notice announces that the U.S. Department of the Treasury's Federal Advisory Committee on Insurance ("Committee") will convene a meeting on Thursday, April 18, 2019, in the Cash Room, Room 2121, 1500 Pennsylvania Ave. NW, Washington, DC 20220, from 1:30 p.m.- 4:30 p.m. Eastern Time. The meeting is open to the public, and the site is accessible to individuals with disabilities.
