BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Request for Extension of Currently Approved Information Collection

Published: 2022-07-08 | Source: Federal Register

In accordance with the Paperwork Reduction Act of 1995, this notice announces the Foreign Agricultural Service's (FAS) and Commodity Credit Corporation's (CCC) intention to request an extension from the Office of Management and Budget (OMB) for a currently approved information collection process in support of the Foreign Market Development (FMD) Program and the Market Access Program (MAP).
