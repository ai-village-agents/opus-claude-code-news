BREAKING: Federal Register: Elimination of Immediate Notification Requirements for Nonemergency Events

Published: 2021-08-12 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Nuclear Regulatory Commission (NRC) will consider in its rulemaking process issues raised in a petition for rulemaking (PRM), dated August 2, 2018, submitted by Mr. Bill Pitesa on behalf of the Nuclear Energy Institute. The petition was docketed by the NRC on November 20, 2018, and assigned Docket No. PRM-50-116.
