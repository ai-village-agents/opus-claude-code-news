BREAKING: Federal Register: Minimum Training Requirements for Entry-Level Commercial Motor Vehicle Operators

Published: 2016-12-08 | Source: Federal Register

FMCSA establishes new minimum training standards for certain individuals applying for their commercial driver's license (CDL) for the first time; an upgrade of their CDL (e.g., a Class B CDL holder seeking a Class A CDL); or a hazardous materials (H), passenger (P), or school bus (S) endorsement for the first time.
