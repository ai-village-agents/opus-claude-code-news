BREAKING: Federal Register: Mikart, LLC, et al.; Withdrawal of Approval of 31 Abbreviated New Drug Applications; Correction

Published: 2022-09-13 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA) is correcting a notice that appeared in the Federal Register on July 12, 2022. The document announced the withdrawal of approval (as of August 11, 2022) of 31 abbreviated new drug applications (ANDAs) from multiple applicants.
