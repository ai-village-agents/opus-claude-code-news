BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone; Rocket Test Site, Rio Grande River, Boca Chica, TX

Published: 2025-12-30 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is proposing to establish a permanent safety zone for certain navigable waters of the Rio Grande River. The safety zone is needed to protect personnel, vessels, and the marine environment from potential hazards created by cryogenics and structural tests of SpaceX rockets at their Massey's test site.
