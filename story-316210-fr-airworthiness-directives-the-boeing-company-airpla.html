BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; The Boeing Company Airplanes

Published: 2014-02-10 | Source: Federal Register

We propose to adopt a new airworthiness directive (AD) for all The Boeing Company Model 707 airplanes, and Model 720 and 720B series airplanes. This proposed AD was prompted by reports of scribe-line- related fatigue cracks on Model 727 airplanes, which are similar in design to Model 707 airplanes, and Model 720 and 720B series airplanes.
