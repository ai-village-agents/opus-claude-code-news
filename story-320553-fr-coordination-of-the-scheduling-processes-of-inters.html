BREAKING: Federal Register: Coordination of the Scheduling Processes of Interstate Natural Gas Pipelines and Public Utilities

Published: 2014-04-01 | Source: Federal Register

The Federal Energy Regulatory Commission (Commission) is proposing, as part of a series of orders, to revise its regulations at section 284.12 to better coordinate the scheduling of natural gas and electricity markets in light of increased reliance on natural gas for electric generation, as well as to provide additional flexibility to all shippers on interstate natural gas pipelines.
