BREAKING: Federal Register: Animal Welfare; Importation of Live Dogs

Published: 2014-08-18 | Source: Federal Register

We are amending the regulations to implement an amendment to the Animal Welfare Act (AWA). The Food, Conservation, and Energy Act of 2008 added a new section to the AWA to restrict the importation of certain live dogs.
