BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zones; Houston Ship Channel and Morgan's Point, TX

Published: 2024-10-25 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is establishing two temporary safety zones, a 100-yard radius moving safety zone and a 25-yard radius fixed safety zone, around the M/V PIETERSGRACHT, in the navigable waters of the Houston Ship Channel and its vicinity. The temporary safety zones are necessary to protect persons, property, and the marine environment from potential hazards associated with the transfer of gantry cranes.
