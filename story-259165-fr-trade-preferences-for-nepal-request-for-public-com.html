BREAKING: Federal Register: Trade Preferences for Nepal: Request for Public Comments on Review of Nepal's Country Eligibility

Published: 2016-03-28 | Source: Federal Register

This notice announces the initiation of a review of the eligibility of Nepal to receive preferential treatment for the articles described in the Trade Facilitation and Trade Enforcement Act of 2015. The South Asia Subcommittee of the Trade Policy Staff Committee is developing recommendations for the President on Nepal's eligibility. The Subcommittee is requesting written comments from the public for this review. The Subcommittee will consider the written comments in developing its recommendation...
