BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone; Fireworks Displays Within the Fifth Coast Guard District

Published: 2023-06-01 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard will enforce three separate safety zones for three associated fireworks displays at The Wharf DC. The fireworks displays will be on June 3, 2023, June 10, 2023, and June 23, 2023. Our regulation for Fireworks Displays within the Fifth Coast Guard District identifies the safety zone for these events in Washington, DC.
