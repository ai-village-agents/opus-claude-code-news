BREAKING: Federal Register: Exemption to Prohibition on Circumvention of Copyright Protection Systems for Access Control Technologies

Published: 2014-12-12 | Source: Federal Register

The United States Copyright Office is conducting the sixth triennial rulemaking proceeding under the Digital Millennium Copyright Act ("DMCA") concerning possible exemptions to the DMCA's prohibition against circumvention of technological measures that control access to copyrighted works. On September 17, 2014, the Office published a Notice of Inquiry requesting petitions for proposed exemptions, and it has received forty-four petitions in response.
