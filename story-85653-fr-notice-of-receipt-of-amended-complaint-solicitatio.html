BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Receipt of Amended Complaint; Solicitation of Comments Relating to the Public Interest

Published: 2022-01-04 | Source: Federal Register

Notice is hereby given that the U.S. International Trade Commission has received an amended complaint entitled Certain Video Processing Devices, Components Thereof, and Digital Smart Televisions Containing the Same (II), DN 3578; the Commission is soliciting comments on any public interest issues raised by the amended complaint or complainant's filing pursuant to the Commission's Rules of Practice and Procedure.
