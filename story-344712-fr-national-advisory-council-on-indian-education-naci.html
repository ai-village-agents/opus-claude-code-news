BREAKING: Federal Register: National Advisory Council on Indian Education (NACIE)

Published: 2013-01-22 | Source: Federal Register

This notice sets forth the schedule for the upcoming public meeting of the National Advisory Council on Indian Education (the Council) and is intended to notify the general public of the meeting. This notice also describes the functions of the Council. Notice of the Council's meetings is required under Section 10(a)(2) of the Federal Advisory Committee Act.
