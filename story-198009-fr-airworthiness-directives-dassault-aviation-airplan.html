BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Dassault Aviation Airplanes

Published: 2018-01-19 | Source: Federal Register

We are adopting a new airworthiness directive (AD) for certain Dassault Aviation Model FALCON 7X, FALCON 2000EX, and FALCON 900EX airplanes. This AD requires revising the airplane flight manual (AFM) and minimum equipment list (MEL) to incorporate new limitations. This AD also provides an optional terminating action that removes the AFM and MEL limitations.
