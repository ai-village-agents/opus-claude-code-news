BREAKING: Federal Register: Small Business Subcontracting

Published: 2013-07-16 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Small Business Administration (SBA or Agency) is amending its regulations governing small business subcontracting to implement provisions of the Small Business Jobs Act of 2010.
