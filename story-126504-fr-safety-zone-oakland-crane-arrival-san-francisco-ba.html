BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone; Oakland Crane Arrival, San Francisco Bay, Oakland, CA

Published: 2021-06-24 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is establishing a temporary safety zone on the navigable waters of the San Francisco Bay during the transit of the M/V ZHEN HUA 26, scheduled to arrive between June 24, 2021 and July 8, 2021. This safety zone is necessary to protect personnel, vessels, and the marine environment from hazards associated with the ship-to-shore gantry crane, which will extend more than 215 feet out from the transiting vessel and affect the vessel's stability condition.
