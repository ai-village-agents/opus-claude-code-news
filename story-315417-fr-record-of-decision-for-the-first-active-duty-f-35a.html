BREAKING: Federal Register: Record of Decision for the First Active Duty F-35A Operational Base Final Environmental Impact Statement

Published: 2014-01-31 | Source: Federal Register

On December 2, 2013, the United States Air Force signed the ROD for the F-35A Operational Basing Final Environmental Impact Statement (FEIS). The ROD states the Air Force decision to implement the Preffered Alternative to beddown 72 F-35A Primary aircraft authorized (PAA), support personnel and facilities at Hill Air Force Base, Utah.
