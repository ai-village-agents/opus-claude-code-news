BREAKING: Federal Register: Medical Area Body Network

Published: 2012-09-11 | Source: Federal Register

This document expands the Commission's Medical Device Radiocommunications Service (MedRadio) rules to permit the development of new Medical Body Area Network (MBAN) devices in the 2360-2400 MHz band.
