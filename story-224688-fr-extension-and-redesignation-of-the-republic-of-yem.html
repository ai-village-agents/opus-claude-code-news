BREAKING: Federal Register: Extension and Redesignation of the Republic of Yemen for Temporary Protected Status

Published: 2017-01-04 | Source: Federal Register

Through this Notice, the Department of Homeland Security (DHS) announces that the Secretary of Homeland Security (Secretary) is extending the designation of the Republic of Yemen (Yemen) for Temporary Protected Status (TPS) for 18 months, from March 4, 2017, through September 3, 2018, and redesignating Yemen for TPS for 18 months, effective March 4, 2017, through September 3, 2018.
