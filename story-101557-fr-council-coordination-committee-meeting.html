BREAKING: Federal Register: Council Coordination Committee Meeting

Published: 2022-07-28 | Source: Federal Register

The National Marine Fisheries Service, Office of Sustainable Fisheries will host a closed session of the Council Coordination Committee (CCC), consisting of the Regional Fishery Management Council (Council) chairs, vice chairs, and executive directors on August 16, 2022. The intent of this virtual meeting is to discuss internal administrative matters regarding policies for preventing harassment of Council staff and all other Council process participants.
