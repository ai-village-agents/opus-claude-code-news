BREAKING: Federal Register: Accurate Fluorescence Measurements Consortium

Published: 2017-12-20 | Source: Federal Register

The National Institute of Standards and Technology (NIST), an agency of the United States Department of Commerce, is establishing the Accurate Fluorescence Measurements Consortium and invites organizations to participate in this Consortium.
