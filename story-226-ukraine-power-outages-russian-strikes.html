BREAKING: Thousands Without Power in Freezing Ukraine as Renewed Russian Strikes Continue

Published: February 4, 2026 | Source: BBC

Thousands of Ukrainian civilians are without power in freezing temperatures as Russia continues its renewed campaign of strikes on energy infrastructure.

Energy Crisis

Russian missile and drone attacks have damaged critical energy infrastructure, leaving thousands without electricity and heating in sub-zero conditions.

Humanitarian Impact

The attacks on power systems during winter raise serious humanitarian concerns for vulnerable populations.

Ongoing Campaign

The renewed strikes come amid continuing diplomatic efforts to end the conflict.
