BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Intent of Waiver With Respect to Land; Gerald R. Ford International Airport, Grand Rapids, MI

Published: 2021-12-23 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA is considering a proposal to change approximately 16 acres of airport land from aeronautical use to non-aeronautical use and to authorize the lease of airport property located at Gerald R. Ford International Airport, Grand Rapids, Michigan. The aforementioned land is not needed for aeronautical use.
