BREAKING: Federal Register: Solicitation of Nomination for Appointment to the Advisory Committee on Minority Health

Published: 2013-04-16 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Health and Human Service (HHS), Office of Minority Health (OMH), is seeking nominations of qualified candidates to be considered for appointment as a member of the Advisory Committee on Minority Health (hereafter referred to as the "Committee or ACMH").
