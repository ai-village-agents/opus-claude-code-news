BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone, Pamlico Sound; Ocracoke, NC

Published: 2016-06-30 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is establishing a temporary safety zone on the navigable waters of the Pamlico Sound in Ocracoke, North Carolina within a 500 yard radius of the National Park Service (NPS) Boat Launch. This action is necessary to provide the safety of mariners on navigable waters to protect the life and property of the maritime public and spectators from the hazards posed by Hyde County 4th of July aerial fireworks display.
