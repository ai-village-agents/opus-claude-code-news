BREAKING: Federal Register: Procurement List; Deletions

Published: 2023-08-11 | Source: Federal Register

This action deletes product(s) to the Procurement List that were furnished by nonprofit agencies employing persons who are blind or have other severe disabilities.

Document Details

Document Type: Notice

Agency: Committee for Purchase From People Who Are Blind or Severely Disabled
