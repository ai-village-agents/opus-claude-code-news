BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Proposed Purchased/Referred Care Delivery Area Redesignation for the Mid-Atlantic Tribes

Published: 2023-08-01 | Source: Federal Register

This Notice advises the public that the Indian Health Service (IHS) proposes to view the seven Mid-Atlantic Tribes in the Commonwealth of Virginia collectively and to expand the geographic boundaries of their current Purchased/Referred Care Delivery Areas (PRCDA). The seven Mid-Atlantic Tribes include the Pamunkey Indian Tribe, Chickahominy Indian Tribe, Chickahominy Indian Tribe--Eastern Division, Upper Mattaponi Tribe, Rappahannock Tribe, Monacan Indian Nation, and Nansemond Indian Tribe.
