BREAKING: Federal Register: Privacy Act of 1974; System of Records

Published: 2024-12-23 | Source: Federal Register

In accordance with the Privacy Act of 1974, the DoD is establishing a new Department-wide system of records titled, "Military Corrections and Parole Board Records" DoD-0023. This system of records describes DoD's collection, use, and maintenance of records covering military Service members confined in a correctional facility for violation of the Uniform Code of Military Justice.
