BREAKING: Federal Register: OneRD Guaranteed Loan Initiative

Published: 2022-04-27 | Source: Federal Register

On December 10, 2021, Rural Development's Rural Business- Cooperative Service, Rural Housing Service, and Rural Utilities Service, agencies of the United States Department of Agriculture (USDA), published a final rule with comment for the OneRD Guarantee Loan Program (OneRD). The final rule was effective on December 10, 2021, and applied to all applications filed by an applicant pursuant to OneRD on or after that date.
