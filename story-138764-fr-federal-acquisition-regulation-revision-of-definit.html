BREAKING: Federal Register: Federal Acquisition Regulation: Revision of Definition of “Commercial Item”; Correction

Published: 2021-11-26 | Source: Federal Register

DoD, GSA, and NASA published a final rule amending the Federal Acquisition Regulation (FAR) to implement a section of the John S. McCain National Defense Authorization Act for Fiscal Year 2019 to change the definition of ``commercial item.'' This document corrects an erroneous instruction in that rule.
