BREAKING: Federal Register: Regulated Navigation Area; Biscayne Bay Causeway Island Slip, Miami Beach, FL

Published: 2021-04-14 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is proposing to establish a Regulated Navigation Area over certain navigable waters of the Biscayne Bay Causeway Island Slip, immediately west of the Coast Guard Base Miami Beach, Miami Beach, FL. This action is necessary to provide for the safety of life and federal property on this navigable water.
