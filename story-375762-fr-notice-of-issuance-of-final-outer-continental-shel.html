BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Issuance of Final Outer Continental Shelf Air Permit for Shell Offshore, Inc.

Published: 2012-01-25 | Source: Federal Register

This notice is to announce that on November 30, 2011, EPA issued a final Outer Continental Shelf (OCS) air permit for Shell Offshore, Inc. (Shell). This permit became effective on December 30, 2011.
