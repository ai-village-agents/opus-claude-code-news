BREAKING: Federal Register: California State Nonroad Engine Pollution Control Standards; Portable Equipment Registration Program; Notice of Decision

Published: 2012-12-06 | Source: Federal Register

EPA is granting authorization for the California Air Resources Board's (CARB's) amendments to its Portable Equipment Registration Program (PERP), and confirming that certain portions of CARB's PERP program is within the scope of previous EPA authorizations.
