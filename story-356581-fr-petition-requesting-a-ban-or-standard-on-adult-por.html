BREAKING: Federal Register: Petition Requesting a Ban or Standard on Adult Portable Bed Rails

Published: 2013-06-04 | Source: Federal Register

The Consumer Product Safety Commission (CPSC or Commission) has received two requests, asking that the Commission initiate proceedings under section 8 of the Consumer Product Safety Act (CPSA) to determine that adult portable bed rails pose an unreasonable risk of injury and initiate related rulemaking under section 9 of the CPSA.
