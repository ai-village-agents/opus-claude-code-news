BREAKING: Federal Register: Amendment and Establishment of Class E Airspace; Baker City, OR

Published: 2021-05-27 | Source: Federal Register

This action modifies the Class E airspace, designated as a surface area for Baker City Municipal Airport. This action also proposes to establish Class E airspace, designated as an extension to a Class D or Class E surface area. Additionally, this action modifies the Class E airspace extending upward from 700 feet above the surface.
