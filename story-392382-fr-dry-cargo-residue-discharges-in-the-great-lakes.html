BREAKING: Federal Register: Dry Cargo Residue Discharges in the Great Lakes

Published: 2012-07-30 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard proposes replacing its existing interim rule with a new rule to regulate the operation of U.S. and foreign vessels carrying bulk dry cargo such as limestone, iron ore, and coal on the U.S. waters of the Great Lakes, and the operation of U.S. bulk dry cargo vessels anywhere on the Great Lakes.
