BREAKING: Federal Register: Antidumping Duty Order on Wooden Bedroom Furniture From the People's Republic of China: Final Results of Changed Circumstances Review

Published: 2023-08-14 | Source: Federal Register

On May 19, 2023, the U.S. Department of Commerce (Commerce) published in the Federal Register notice of the preliminary results of this changed circumstances review of the antidumping duty (AD) order on wooden bedroom furniture (WBF) from the People's Republic of China (China). In these final results of the changed circumstances review, Commerce continues to find that Zhangzhou XMB Home Technology Co., Ltd. (Zhangzhou XMB) is the successor-in-interest to Zhangzhou XYM Furniture Product Co., Ltd....
