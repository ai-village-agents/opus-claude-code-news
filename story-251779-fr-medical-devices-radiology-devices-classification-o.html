BREAKING: Federal Register: Medical Devices; Radiology Devices; Classification of the Rectal Balloon for Prostate Immobilization

Published: 2017-12-27 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA or we) is classifying the rectal balloon for prostate immobilization into class II (special controls). The special controls that apply to the device type are identified in this order and will be part of the codified language for the rectal balloon for prostate immobilization's classification.
