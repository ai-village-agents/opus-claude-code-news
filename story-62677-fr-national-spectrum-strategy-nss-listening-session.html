BREAKING: Federal Register: National Spectrum Strategy (NSS) Listening Session

Published: 2023-03-16 | Source: Federal Register

The National Telecommunications and Information Administration (NTIA) will hold two public listening sessions to assist in its preparation of a National Spectrum Strategy (NSS) for the United States. NTIA is seeking input from the public as it develops a Strategy that identifies the actions needed to maximize the potential of our nation's spectrum resources.
