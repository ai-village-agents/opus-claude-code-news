BREAKING: Federal Register: Importation, Interstate Movement, and Environmental Release of Certain Genetically Engineered Organisms

Published: 2017-01-19 | Source: Federal Register

APHIS is proposing to revise its regulations regarding the importation, interstate movement, and environmental release of certain genetically engineered organisms in order to update the regulations in response to advances in genetic engineering and understanding of the plant pest and noxious weed risk posed by genetically engineered (GE) organisms, thereby reducing burden for regulated entities whose organisms pose no plant pest or noxious weed risks.
