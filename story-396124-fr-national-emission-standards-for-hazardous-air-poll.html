BREAKING: Federal Register: National Emission Standards for Hazardous Air Pollutants From the Pulp and Paper Industry

Published: 2012-09-11 | Source: Federal Register

This action finalizes the residual risk and technology review conducted for the pulp and paper industry source category regulated under national emission standards for hazardous air pollutants. The EPA is required to conduct residual risk and technology reviews under the Clean Air Act.
