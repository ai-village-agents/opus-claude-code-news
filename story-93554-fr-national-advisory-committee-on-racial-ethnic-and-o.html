BREAKING: Federal Register: National Advisory Committee on Racial, Ethnic, and Other Populations; Charter Renewal

Published: 2022-04-13 | Source: Federal Register

The Census Bureau is publishing this notice to announce the renewal of the National Advisory Committee on Racial, Ethnic, and Other Populations (Committee or NAC). The purpose of the Committee is to provide advice to the Director of the Census Bureau on the full range of Census Bureau programs and activities, including the decennial census, demographic and economic statistical programs, field operations, and information technology.
