BREAKING: Federal Register: Air Plan Approval; North Carolina; Revision To Permit Term for Non-Title V Air Quality Permits

Published: 2019-07-03 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is taking final action to approve a State Implementation Plan (SIP) revision provided by the State of North Carolina, through the North Carolina Department of Environmental Quality, Division of Air Quality (DAQ), on January 12, 2018. The SIP revision makes changes to the State's combined construction and operating permit program for non-Title V sources.
