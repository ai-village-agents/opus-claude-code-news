BREAKING: Federal Register: Suspicious Orders of Controlled Substances

Published: 2020-11-02 | Source: Federal Register

The Drug Enforcement Administration (DEA) is proposing to revise its regulations relating to suspicious orders of controlled substances, in order to implement the Preventing Drug Diversion Act of 2018 (PDDA) and to clarify the procedures a registrant must follow for orders received under suspicious circumstances (ORUSCs).
