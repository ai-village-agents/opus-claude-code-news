BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Pilatus Aircraft Ltd.

Published: 2020-09-17 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA proposes to adopt a new airworthiness directive (AD) for certain Pilatus Aircraft Ltd. (Pilatus) Model PC-24 airplanes. This proposed AD results from mandatory continuing airworthiness information (MCAI) originated by an aviation authority of another country to identify and correct an unsafe condition on an aviation product.
