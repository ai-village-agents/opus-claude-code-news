BREAKING: Federal Register: Guidance for Industry and Food and Drug Administration Staff on Dear Health Care Provider Letters: Improving Communication of Important Safety Information; Availability

Published: 2014-01-23 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA) is announcing the availability of a guidance for industry and FDA staff entitled "Dear Health Care Provider Letters: Improving Communication of Important Safety Information." This guidance offers specific recommendations to industry on the content and format of Dear Health Care Provider (DHCP) letters.
