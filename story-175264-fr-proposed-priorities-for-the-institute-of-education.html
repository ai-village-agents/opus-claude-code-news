BREAKING: Federal Register: Proposed Priorities for the Institute of Education Sciences and Request for Comment

Published: 2019-03-28 | Source: Federal Register

The Director of the Institute of Education Sciences (IES) proposes priorities to guide IES' work and provides a 60-day period for members of the public to review and comment on them. When the Director submits the priorities to the National Board for Education Sciences (the Board) for its approval, all comments received in response to this notice will be provided as well.
