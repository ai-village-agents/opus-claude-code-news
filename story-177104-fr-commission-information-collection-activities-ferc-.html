BREAKING: Federal Register: Commission Information Collection Activities (Ferc-915); Comment Request; Withdrawal

Published: 2019-04-19 | Source: Federal Register

This document withdraws the Notice of Information Collection and request for comments published in the Federal Register of April 12, 2019, soliciting public comment on the currently approved information collection FERC-915 (Public Utility Market-Based Rate Authorization Holders--Records Retention Requirements) and submitting the information collection to the Office of Management and Budget (OMB) for review. The notice was issued in error.
