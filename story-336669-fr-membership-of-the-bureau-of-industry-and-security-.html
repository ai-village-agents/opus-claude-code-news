BREAKING: Federal Register: Membership of the Bureau of Industry and Security Performance Review Board

Published: 2014-10-07 | Source: Federal Register

In accordance with 5 U.S.C. 4314(c)(4), the Bureau of Industry and Security (BIS), Department of Commerce (DOC), announce the appointment of those individuals who have been selected to serve as members of BIS's Performance Review Board.
