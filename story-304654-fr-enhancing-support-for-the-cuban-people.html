BREAKING: Federal Register: Enhancing Support for the Cuban People

Published: 2015-09-21 | Source: Federal Register

This rule amends the Export Administration Regulations (EAR) to expand the scope of License Exception Support for the Cuban People (SCP) to facilitate engagement between the U.S. and Cuban people; the free flow of information to, from, and among the Cuban people; and independent economic activity generated by Cuba's private sector.
