BREAKING: Federal Register: Proximity Detection Systems for Continuous Mining Machines in Underground Coal Mines

Published: 2015-01-15 | Source: Federal Register

The Mine Safety and Health Administration's (MSHA) final rule requires underground coal mine operators to equip continuous mining machines, except full-face continuous mining machines, with proximity detection systems. Miners working near continuous mining machines face pinning, crushing, and striking hazards that result in accidents involving life-threatening injuries and death.
