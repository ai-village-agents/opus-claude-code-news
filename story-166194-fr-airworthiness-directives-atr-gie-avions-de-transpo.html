BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; ATR-GIE Avions de Transport Regional Airplanes

Published: 2020-11-18 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA is adopting a new airworthiness directive (AD) for all ATR--GIE Avions de Transport Regional Airplanes Model ATR42-200, -300, and -320 airplanes. This AD was prompted by false activation of the stall warning system due to wiring damage on the wire bundle between an angle of attack (AOA) probe and the crew alerting computer.
