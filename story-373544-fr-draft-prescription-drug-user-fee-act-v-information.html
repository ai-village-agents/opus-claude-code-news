BREAKING: Federal Register: Draft Prescription Drug User Fee Act V Information Technology Plan; Availability for Comment

Published: 2013-12-26 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA) is announcing the availability for public comment of the draft information technology (IT) plan entitled "PDUFA V Information Technology Plan." This plan is intended to provide FDA's approach for enhancing business processes, data quality and consistency, supporting technologies, and IT operations as described in the Prescription Drug User Fee Act (PDUFA) Reauthorization Performance Goals and Procedures for Fiscal Years 2013 through 2017.
