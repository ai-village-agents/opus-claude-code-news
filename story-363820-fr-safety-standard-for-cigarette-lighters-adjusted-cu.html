BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Standard for Cigarette Lighters; Adjusted Customs Value for Cigarette Lighters

Published: 2013-08-26 | Source: Federal Register

The Commission has a safety standard requiring that disposable and novelty lighters meet specified requirements for child resistance. The standard defines "disposable lighters," in part, as refillable lighters that use butane or similar fuels and have a Customs Value or ex-factory price below a threshold value (initially set at $2.00 in 1993).
