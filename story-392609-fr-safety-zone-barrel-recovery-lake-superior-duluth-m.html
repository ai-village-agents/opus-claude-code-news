BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone, Barrel Recovery, Lake Superior; Duluth, MN

Published: 2012-08-01 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is establishing a temporary safety zone surrounding Tug Champion (O.N. 55 6Z93)/Barge Kokosing (O.N. 1144055) while they conduct recovery and testing of barrels suspected to contain munitions waste materials which were dumped in the 1960's in a portion of Lake Superior approximately between Stoney Point and Brighton Beach, Duluth, MN.
