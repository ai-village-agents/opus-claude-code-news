BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Availability for the Tri-State Fuel Breaks Project Record of Decision, Idaho

Published: 2020-05-14 | Source: Federal Register

In accordance with the National Environmental Policy Act of 1969, as amended, and the Federal Land Policy and Management Act of 1976, as amended, the Bureau of Land Management (BLM) announces the availability of the Record of Decision (ROD) for the Idaho BLM portion of the Tri-state Fuel Breaks Project Environmental Impact Statement (EIS).
