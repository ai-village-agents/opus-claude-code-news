BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone; Pier 27 Fireworks Display, San Francisco, CA

Published: 2021-09-30 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is establishing a temporary safety zone on the navigable waters of the San Francisco Bay near Pier 27 in support of a fireworks display on October 1, 2021. The safety zone is necessary to protect personnel, vessels, and the marine environment from potential hazards created by pyrotechnics.
