BREAKING: Federal Register: Submission for OMB Review; 2024 National Survey of Early Care and Education (OMB #: 0970-0391)

Published: 2023-05-04 | Source: Federal Register

The Administration for Children and Families (ACF), U.S. Department of Health and Human Services (HHS), is proposing a data collection activity as part of the 2024 National Survey of Early Care and Education (NSECE) to be conducted October 2023 through July 2024.
