Published: 2015-03-31 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA) is announcing the availability of a guidance for industry entitled "Critical Path Innovation Meetings." This guidance describes a Critical Path Innovation Meeting (CPIM), a means by which FDA's Center for Drug Evaluation and Research (CDER) and investigators from industry, academia, government, and patient advocacy groups can communicate to improve efficiency and success in drug development.
