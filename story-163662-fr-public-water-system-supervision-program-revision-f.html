BREAKING: Federal Register: Public Water System Supervision Program Revision for the State of South Carolina

Published: 2020-10-15 | Source: Federal Register

This notice is hereby given that the State of South Carolina is revising its approved Public Water System Supervision Program. South Carolina has adopted drinking water regulations for the Ground Water Rule, Lead and Copper Rule Short-Term Regulatory Revisions and Clarifications, and Revised Total Coliform Rule.
