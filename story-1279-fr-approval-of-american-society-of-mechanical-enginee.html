BREAKING: Federal Register: Approval of American Society of Mechanical Engineers Unconditioned Code Cases

Published: 2026-01-27 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Nuclear Regulatory Commission (NRC) is confirming the effective date of January 26, 2026, for the direct final rule that was published in the Federal Register on September 26, 2025. This direct final rule amended the regulations to incorporate by reference a regulatory guide that approved unconditioned code cases published by the American Society of Mechanical Engineers.
