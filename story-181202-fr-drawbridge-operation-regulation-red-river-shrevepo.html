BREAKING: Federal Register: Drawbridge Operation Regulation; Red River, Shreveport, LA

Published: 2019-06-10 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is changing the operating schedule that governs the draws of the Union Pacific Railroad bridge, mile 227.0, and the Midsouth Railroad bridge, mile 228.2, across the Red River at Shreveport, LA. This final rule will allow for the drawbridges to remain in the closed-to-navigation position.
