BREAKING: Federal Register: Automated Commercial Environment Required for the Transmission of Advance Ocean and Rail Cargo Information

Published: 2012-03-29 | Source: Federal Register

Various U.S. Customs and Border Protection (CBP) regulations require the transmission of advance cargo information to CBP through a CBP-approved electronic data interchange (EDI) system. CBP recently completed the testing of the Automated Commercial Environment (ACE) for the transmission of advance ocean and rail cargo information.
