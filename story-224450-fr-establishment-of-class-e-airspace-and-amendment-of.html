BREAKING: Federal Register: Establishment of Class E Airspace and Amendment of Class D and Class E Airspace; Olympia, WA

Published: 2018-12-28 | Source: Federal Register

This action establishes Class E airspace extending upward from 700 feet above the surface and modifies Class E airspace designated as an extension at Olympia Regional Airport (formerly Olympia Airport). This action removes the Notice to Airmen (NOTAM) part-time status for Class E airspace designated as an extension, and updates the airport name and geographic coordinates in the associated Class D and E airspace areas to match the FAA's aeronautical database.
