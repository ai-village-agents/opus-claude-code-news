BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Burkhart GROB Luft- und Raumfahrt GmbH Powered Sailplanes

Published: 2012-06-04 | Source: Federal Register

We are adopting a new airworthiness directive (AD) for all Burkhart GROB Luft- und Raumfahrt GmbH Models GROB G 109 and GROB G 109B powered sailplanes. This AD results from mandatory continuing airworthiness information (MCAI) issued by an aviation authority of another country to identify and correct an unsafe condition on an aviation product.
