BREAKING: Federal Register: Regular Rate Under the Fair Labor Standards Act

Published: 2019-03-29 | Source: Federal Register

The Fair Labor Standards Act (FLSA or Act) generally requires that covered, nonexempt employees receive overtime pay of at least one and one-half times their regular rate of pay for time worked in excess of 40 hours per workweek. The regular rate includes all remuneration for employment, subject to the exclusions outlined in section 7(e) of the FLSA.
