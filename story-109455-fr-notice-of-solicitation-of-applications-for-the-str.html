BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Solicitation of Applications for the Strategic Economic and Community Development Program for FY 2023

Published: 2022-11-07 | Source: Federal Register

The Under Secretary for Rural Development (RD) is seeking applications for the Strategic Economic and Community Development (SECD) priority, as reauthorized by Section 6401 of the Agriculture Improvement Act of 2018 (2018 Farm Bill) for projects that support multi-jurisdictional and multi-sectoral strategic community investment plans.
