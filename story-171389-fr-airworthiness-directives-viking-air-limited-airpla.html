BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Viking Air Limited Airplanes

Published: 2019-02-11 | Source: Federal Register

We propose to adopt a new airworthiness directive (AD) for certain Viking Air Limited Models DHC-2 Mk. I and DHC-2 Mk. III airplanes. This proposed AD results from mandatory continuing airworthiness information (MCAI) originated by an aviation authority of another country to identify and correct an unsafe condition on an aviation product.
