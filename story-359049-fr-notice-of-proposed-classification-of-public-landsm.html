BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Proposed Classification of Public Lands/Minerals for State Indemnity Selection, Colorado

Published: 2013-07-02 | Source: Federal Register

The Colorado State Board of Land Commissioners (State) has filed a petition for classification and application to obtain public lands and mineral estate in lieu of lands to which the State was entitled but did not receive under its Statehood Act. The State did not receive title because the lands had previously been appropriated in an Indian Reservation or National Forests.
