BREAKING: Federal Register: National Artificial Intelligence Advisory Committee

Published: 2024-04-16 | Source: Federal Register

The National Institute of Standards and Technology (NIST) announces that the National Artificial Intelligence Advisory Committee (NAIAC or Committee) will hold an open meeting in-person and virtually via web conference on May 2, 2024, from 10:00 a.m.-4:00 p.m. eastern time. The primary purpose of this meeting is for the Committee to report working group findings, identify actionable recommendations, and receive public briefings.
