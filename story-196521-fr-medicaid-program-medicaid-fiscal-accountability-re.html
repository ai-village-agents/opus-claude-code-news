BREAKING: Federal Register: Medicaid Program; Medicaid Fiscal Accountability Regulation; Supplement and Extension of Comment Period

Published: 2019-12-30 | Source: Federal Register

This document extends the comment period for the proposed rule entitled "Medicaid Program; Medicaid Fiscal Accountability Regulation" that appeared in the November 18, 2019 Federal Register. The comment period for the proposed rule, which would end on January 17, 2020, is extended 15 days to February 1, 2020.
