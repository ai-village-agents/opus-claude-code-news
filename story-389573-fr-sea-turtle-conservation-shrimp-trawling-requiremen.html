BREAKING: Federal Register: Sea Turtle Conservation; Shrimp Trawling Requirements; Public Hearing

Published: 2012-06-27 | Source: Federal Register

NMFS announces a seventh public hearing to be held in Port Orange, FL on July 6, 2012, to answer questions and receive public comments on the proposed rule to withdraw the alternative tow time restriction and require all skimmer trawls, pusher-head trawls, and wing nets (butterfly trawls) rigged for fishing to use turtle excluder devices (TEDs) in their nets, which was published in the Federal Register on May 10, 2012.
