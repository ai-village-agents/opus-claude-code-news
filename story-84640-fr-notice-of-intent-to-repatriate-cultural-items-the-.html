BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Intent To Repatriate Cultural Items: The University of Kansas, Lawrence, KS

Published: 2023-12-19 | Source: Federal Register

In accordance with the Native American Graves Protection and Repatriation Act (NAGPRA), the University of Kansas intends to repatriate a certain cultural item that meets the definition of a sacred object and that has a cultural affiliation with the Indian Tribes or Native Hawaiian organizations in this notice. The cultural item was removed from Sonora, Mexico.
