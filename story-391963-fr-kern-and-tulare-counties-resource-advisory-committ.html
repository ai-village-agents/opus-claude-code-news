BREAKING: Federal Register: Kern and Tulare Counties Resource Advisory Committee

Published: 2012-07-25 | Source: Federal Register

The Kern and Tulare Counties Resource Advisory Committee (RAC) will be accepting applications for projects that may be recommended for funding under Title II of the Secure Rural Schools and Community Self- Determination Act (Pub. L 110-343 as amended by H.R. 4348 ENR). The purpose of the committee is to improve collaborative relationships and to provide advice and recommendations to the Forest Service concerning projects and funding consistent with Title II of the Act.
