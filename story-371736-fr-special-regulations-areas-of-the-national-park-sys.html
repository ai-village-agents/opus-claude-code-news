BREAKING: Federal Register: Special Regulations, Areas of the National Park System, Lake Meredith National Recreation Area, Bicycling

Published: 2013-12-03 | Source: Federal Register

Through the preparation of a Multi-Use Trail Environmental Assessment, the National Park Service has decided to construct an unpaved, multi-use recreational trail in Lake Meredith National Recreation Area. The multi-use trail will be approximately 22 miles in length and be open to pedestrian and bicycle use only.
