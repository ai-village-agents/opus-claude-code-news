BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone, Milwaukee Harbor, Milwaukee, WI

Published: 2015-06-12 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard will enforce the safety zone in Milwaukee Harbor, Milwaukee, WI for annual fireworks displays in the Captain of the Port Lake Michigan zone at specified times from June 6, 2015 until September 12, 2015. This action is necessary and intended to ensure safety of life on the navigable waters immediately prior to, during, and immediately after fireworks displays.
