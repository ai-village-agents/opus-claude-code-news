BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Change in Application Requirements

Published: 2012-02-08 | Source: Federal Register

The Administration for Children and Families (ACF), Administration on Developmental Disabilities (ADD) has modified the application requirements for awards made to P&As under HAVA, Public Law 107-252. Under the program, formula grants are allotted to States based on population, financial need, and need for service.
