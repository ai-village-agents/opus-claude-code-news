BREAKING: Federal Register: Pipeline Safety: Class Location Change Requirements

Published: 2018-07-31 | Source: Federal Register

PHMSA is seeking public comment on its existing class location requirements for natural gas transmission pipelines as they pertain to actions operators are required to take following class location changes due to population growth near the pipeline.
