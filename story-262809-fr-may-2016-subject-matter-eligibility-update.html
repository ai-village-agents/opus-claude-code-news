BREAKING: Federal Register: May 2016 Subject Matter Eligibility Update

Published: 2016-05-06 | Source: Federal Register

The United States Patent and Trademark Office (USPTO) issued the July 2015 Update: Subject Matter Eligibility (July 2015 Update) to provide further guidance to examiners in determining subject matter eligibility under 35 U.S.C. 101. The USPTO announced the July 2015 Update in the Federal Register, and sought public comment on the July 2015 Update.
