BREAKING: Federal Register: Denial of Motor Vehicle Defect Petition

Published: 2021-01-13 | Source: Federal Register

This notice sets forth the reasons for the denial of a petition submitted on December 19, 2019, by Mr. Brian Sparks to NHTSA's Office of Defects Investigation (ODI). The petition requests that the Agency recall Tesla vehicles for an unidentified defect that allegedly causes sudden unintended acceleration (SUA).
