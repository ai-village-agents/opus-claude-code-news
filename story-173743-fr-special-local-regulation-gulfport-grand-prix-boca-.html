BREAKING: Federal Register: Special Local Regulation; Gulfport Grand Prix, Boca Ciego Bay, Gulfport, FL

Published: 2019-03-11 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is establishing a special local regulation on the waters of the Boca Ciego Bay in the vicinity of Gulfport, Florida, during the Gulfport Grand Prix High Speed Boat Race. Approximately 75 boats, 14-30 feet in length, traveling at speeds in excess of 120 miles per hour are expected to participate.
