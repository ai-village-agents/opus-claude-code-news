BREAKING: Federal Register: Removal of Freedom of Information Act Regulations

Published: 2023-08-09 | Source: Federal Register

This rule rescinds the National Foundation on the Arts and the Humanities' (the "Foundation") regulations implementing the Freedom of Information Act ("FOIA").
