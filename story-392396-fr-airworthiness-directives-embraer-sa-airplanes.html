BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Embraer S.A. Airplanes

Published: 2012-07-30 | Source: Federal Register

We are adopting a new airworthiness directive (AD) for all Embraer S.A. Model ERJ 190 airplanes. This AD was prompted by a report of damage on the rod end of the retracting actuator rod of the main landing gear (MLG).
