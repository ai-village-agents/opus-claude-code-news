BREAKING: Federal Register: Energy Conservation Program: Test Procedure for Automatic Commercial Ice Makers

Published: 2012-01-11 | Source: Federal Register

On April 4, 2011, the U.S. Department of Energy (DOE or the Department) issued a notice of proposed rulemaking (NOPR) to amend the test procedure for automatic commercial ice makers (ACIM). That NOPR serves as the basis for today's action. This final rule amends the current test procedure for automatic commercial ice makers.
