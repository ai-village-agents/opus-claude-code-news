BREAKING: Federal Register: Methodology Committee of the Patient-Centered Outcomes Research Institute (PCORI)

Published: 2014-03-14 | Source: Federal Register

The Patient Protection and Affordable Care Act gave the Comptroller General of the United States responsibility for appointing not more than 15 members to a Methodology Committee of the Patient- Centered Outcomes Research Institute. In addition, the Directors of the Agency for Healthcare Research and Quality and the National Institutes of Health, or their designees, are members of the Methodology Committee.
