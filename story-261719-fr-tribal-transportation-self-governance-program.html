BREAKING: Federal Register: Tribal Transportation Self-Governance Program

Published: 2016-04-25 | Source: Federal Register

The FHWA is announcing its intent to establish a negotiated rulemaking committee to develop a proposed rule to carry the Tribal Transportation Self-Governance Program (TTSGP) as required by Section 1121 of the Fixing America's Surface Transportation (FAST) Act.
