BREAKING: Federal Register: Energy Conservation Program: Backstop Requirement for General Service Lamps

Published: 2021-12-13 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Department of Energy (``DOE'') proposes to codify in the Code of Federal Regulations the 45 lumens per watt (``lm/W'') backstop requirement for general service lamps (``GSLs'') that Congress prescribed in the Energy Policy and Conservation Act, as amended. DOE proposes this backstop requirement applies because DOE failed to complete a rulemaking regarding general service lamps in accordance with certain statutory criteria.
