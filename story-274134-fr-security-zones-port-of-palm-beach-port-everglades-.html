BREAKING: Federal Register: Security Zones; Port of Palm Beach, Port Everglades, Miami, and Key West, Florida

Published: 2016-09-16 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard proposes to update and modify security zones in the Port of Palm Beach, Port Everglades, Port of Miami, and the Port of Key West, Florida. The revisions create a new section for the Sector Key West security zones that previously were annotated as belonging to Sector Miami; clarify when the Port Everglades fixed security zones will be in effect; modify and lengthen a portion of the Port Everglades fixed security zone; and update language and definitions throughout the regulation. ...
