BREAKING: Federal Register: Unregulated Contaminant Monitoring Rule; Methods Request and Webinar

Published: 2024-02-08 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is requesting public input on drinking water analytical methods for emerging contaminants in drinking water, particularly those listed on the agency's Fifth Contaminant Candidate List (CCL 5), that might support monitoring under the Unregulated Contaminant Monitoring Rule.
