BREAKING: Federal Register: Finding of Failure To Attain the 1987 24-Hour PM10 Standard; Reclassification as Serious Nonattainment; Pinal County, Arizona

Published: 2020-06-24 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is taking final action to determine that the West Pinal County, Arizona nonattainment area did not attain the 1987 24-hour national ambient air quality standards (NAAQS or "standard") for particulate matter with a diameter of ten micrometers or smaller (PM10) by December 31, 2018, the statutory attainment date for the nonattainment area.
