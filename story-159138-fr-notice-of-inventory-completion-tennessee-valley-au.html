BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Inventory Completion: Tennessee Valley Authority, Knoxville, TN

Published: 2020-08-20 | Source: Federal Register

The Tennessee Valley Authority (TVA) has completed an inventory of associated funerary objects, in consultation with the appropriate Indian Tribes, and has determined that a cultural affiliation between the associated funerary objects and present-day Indian Tribes can reasonably be traced. Lineal descendants or representatives of any Indian Tribe not identified in this notice that wish to request transfer of control of the associated funerary objects should submit a written request to the TVA.
