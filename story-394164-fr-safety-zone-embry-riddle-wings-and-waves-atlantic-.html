BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone; Embry-Riddle Wings and Waves, Atlantic Ocean; Daytona Beach, FL

Published: 2012-08-20 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard proposes to establish a temporary safety zone on the waters of the Atlantic Ocean east of Daytona Beach, Florida during the Embry-Riddle Wings and Waves air show. The event is scheduled to take place from Thursday, October 11, 2012, through Sunday, October 14, 2012. This temporary safety zone is necessary for the safety of air show participants, participant vessels, spectators, and the general public during the event.
