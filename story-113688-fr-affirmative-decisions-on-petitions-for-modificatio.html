BREAKING: Federal Register: Affirmative Decisions on Petitions for Modification Granted in Whole or in Part

Published: 2021-01-05 | Source: Federal Register

The Federal Mine Safety and Health Act of 1977 and Title 30 of the Code of Federal Regulations govern the application, processing, and disposition of petitions for modification of mandatory safety standards. Any mine operator or representative of miners may petition for an alternative method of complying with an existing safety standard.
