BREAKING: Federal Register: Proposed Amendment of V-97 and V-422 in the Vicinity of Chicago, IL

Published: 2018-05-29 | Source: Federal Register

This action proposes to modify two VHF Omnidirectional Range (VOR) Federal airways (V-97 and V-422) in the vicinity of the Chicago, IL. The FAA is proposing this action due to the planned decommissioning of the Chicago O'Hare, IL, VOR/Distance Measuring Equipment (VOR/DME) navigation aid (NAVAID), which provides navigation guidance for portions of the affected Air Traffic Service (ATS) routes.
