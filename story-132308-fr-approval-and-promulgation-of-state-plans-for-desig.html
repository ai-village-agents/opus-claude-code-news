BREAKING: Federal Register: Approval and Promulgation of State Plans for Designated Facilities; New York

Published: 2021-09-03 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is approving the state plan submitted by New York State to implement and enforce Emission Guidelines (EG) for existing large municipal waste combustor (MWC) units. The state plan is consistent with the amended EG promulgated by the EPA on May 10, 2006.
