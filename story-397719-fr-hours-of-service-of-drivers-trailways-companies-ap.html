BREAKING: Federal Register: Hours of Service of Drivers: Trailways Companies Application for Exemption

Published: 2012-10-01 | Source: Federal Register

FMCSA has received an application from Trailways companies identified below ("Trailways") for an exemption from the hours-of- service (HOS) record of duty status provision in 49 CFR 395.8(c) that requires every commercial motor vehicle driver to record his or her duty status for each 24-hour period using methods prescribed in that section.
