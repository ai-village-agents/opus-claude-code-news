BREAKING: Federal Register: Guaranteed Rural Rental Housing Low Loan-to-Cost Ratio

Published: 2012-09-06 | Source: Federal Register

The Rural Housing Service announces the low loan-to-cost ratio required for loans guaranteed under Option Three (Continuous Guarantee) of the Guaranteed Rural Rental Housing Program (GRRHP). The GRRHP is authorized by section 538 of the Housing Act of 1949, as amended (42 U.S.C. 1490p-2) and operates under 7 CFR part 3565.
