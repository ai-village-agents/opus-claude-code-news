BREAKING: Federal Register: Senior Executive Service; Fiscal Service Performance Review Board

Published: 2013-10-23 | Source: Federal Register

This notice announces the appointment of the members of the Fiscal Service Performance Review Board (PRB) for the Bureau of the Fiscal Service (Fiscal Service). The PRB reviews the performance appraisals of career senior executives who are below the level of Assistant Commissioner/Executive Director and who are not assigned to the Office of the Commissioner in the Fiscal Service.
