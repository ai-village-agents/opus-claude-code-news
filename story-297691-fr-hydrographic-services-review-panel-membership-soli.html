BREAKING: Federal Register: Hydrographic Services Review Panel; Membership Solicitation

Published: 2015-06-30 | Source: Federal Register

In accordance with the Hydrographic Service Improvements Act of 1998, as amended (33 U.S.C. 892 et seq.), the National Oceanic and Atmospheric Administration (NOAA) is soliciting nominations for membership on its Hydrographic Services Review Panel (HSRP).
