BREAKING: Federal Register: Qualified Business Income Deduction

Published: 2020-06-25 | Source: Federal Register

This document contains final regulations concerning the deduction for qualified business income (QBI) under section 199A of the Internal Revenue Code (Code). The regulations will affect certain individuals, partnerships, S corporations, trusts, and estates. The regulations provide guidance on the treatment of previously suspended losses included in qualified business income.
