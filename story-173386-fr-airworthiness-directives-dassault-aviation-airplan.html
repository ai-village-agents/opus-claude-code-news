BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Dassault Aviation Airplanes

Published: 2019-03-05 | Source: Federal Register

We are adopting a new airworthiness directive (AD) for all Dassault Aviation Model Falcon 10 airplanes. This AD was prompted by a report indicating that certain wing anti-ice outboard flexible hoses were found damaged, likely resulting from the installation process.
