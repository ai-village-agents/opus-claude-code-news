BREAKING: Federal Register: American Indian Vocational Rehabilitation Services Program; Proposed Waivers and Extensions of the Project Periods

Published: 2013-06-11 | Source: Federal Register

These proposed waivers and extensions of project periods would affect two sets of grantees under the American Indian Vocational Rehabilitation Services (AIVRS) Program in the Rehabilitation Services Administration (RSA): eight grantees with 60-month projects initially funded in fiscal year (FY) 2007 (72 FR 11851) and twenty-four grantees with 60-month projects initially funded in FY 2008 (73 FR 6491).
