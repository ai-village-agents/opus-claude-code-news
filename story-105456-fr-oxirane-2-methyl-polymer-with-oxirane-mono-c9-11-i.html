BREAKING: Federal Register: Oxirane, 2-Methyl-, Polymer With Oxirane, Mono-C9-11-Isoalkyl Ethers, C10-Rich, Phosphates, Potassium Salts; Tolerance Exemption

Published: 2022-09-16 | Source: Federal Register

This regulation establishes an exemption from the requirement of a tolerance for residues of oxirane, 2-methyl-, polymer with oxirane, mono-C9-11-isoalkyl ethers, C10-rich, phosphates, potassium salts (CAS Reg. No. 2275654-37-8), when used as an inert ingredient (surfactant, related adjuvants of surfactant) in pesticide formulations used pre- and post-harvest as well as in formulations applied to livestock.
