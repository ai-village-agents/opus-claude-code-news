BREAKING: Federal Register: Review of Dust-Lead Post Abatement Clearance Levels

Published: 2021-01-07 | Source: Federal Register

Reducing childhood lead exposure is a priority for the Environmental Protection Agency (EPA). As part of EPA's efforts to reduce childhood lead exposure, and in coordination with the President's Task Force on Environmental Health Risks and Safety Risks to Children, EPA reevaluated the 2001 dust-lead clearance levels (DLCL).
