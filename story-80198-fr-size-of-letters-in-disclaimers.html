BREAKING: Federal Register: Size of Letters in Disclaimers

Published: 2023-10-20 | Source: Federal Register

The Commission announces its disposition of a Petition for Rulemaking filed on December 4, 2018, by Extreme Reach. The Petition asks the Commission to amend its regulation on the size of letters in disclaimers in certain television advertisements such that the required letter size for advertisements broadcast in high definition would be reduced.
