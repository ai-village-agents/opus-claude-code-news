BREAKING: Federal Register: Eligibility of the Socialist Republic of Vietnam To Export Siluriformes Fish and Fish Products to the United States

Published: 2019-11-05 | Source: Federal Register

The Food Safety and Inspection Service (FSIS) is amending the Siluriformes fish inspection regulations to list the Socialist Republic of Vietnam (Vietnam) as a country eligible to export Siluriformes fish and fish products to the United States.
