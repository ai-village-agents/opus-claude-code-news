BREAKING: Federal Register: Public Notice for Waiver for Aeronautical Land-Use Assurance at San Marcos Regional Airport, San Marcos, Texas

Published: 2021-10-25 | Source: Federal Register

The Federal Aviation Administration is considering a proposal to change a portion of the airport from aeronautical to nonaeronautical use and to authorize the conversion of the airport property. The proposal consists of one parcel of land containing a total of approximately 16.6 acres.The land comprising this parcel is outside the forecasted need for aviation development and, thus, is no longer needed for indirect or direct aeronautical use.
