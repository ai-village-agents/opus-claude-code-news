BREAKING: Federal Register: Physicochemical and Structural (Q3) Characterization of Topical Drug Products Submitted in Abbreviated New Drug Applications; Draft Guidance for Industry, Availability

Published: 2022-10-24 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA or Agency) is announcing the availability of a draft guidance for industry entitled "Physicochemical and Structural (Q3) Characterization of Topical Drug Products Submitted in ANDAs." This draft guidance is intended to assist applicants who submit abbreviated new drug applications (ANDAs) for liquid-based and/or other semisolid products applied to the skin, including integumentary and mucosal (e.g., vaginal) membranes (referred to as "topical products").
