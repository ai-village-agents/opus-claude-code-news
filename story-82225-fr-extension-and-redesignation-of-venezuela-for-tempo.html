BREAKING: Federal Register: Extension and Redesignation of Venezuela for Temporary Protected Status-Correction

Published: 2023-11-17 | Source: Federal Register

This document corrects a technical error that appears in the USCIS notice titled "Extension and Redesignation of Venezuela for Temporary Protected Status" that published in the Federal Register on October 3, 2023. This document aligns and makes consistent the date through which Temporary Protected Status (TPS) will be granted to certain beneficiaries and the validity period of their related employment authorization document (EAD).
