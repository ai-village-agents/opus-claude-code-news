BREAKING: Federal Register: Exploring Industry Practices on Distribution and Display of Airline Fare, Schedule, and Availability Information

Published: 2016-10-31 | Source: Federal Register

The industry group for travel sites, a number of its members which include online travel booking sites, and certain members of Congress have expressed concerns to the Department of Transportation (DOT or Department) regarding airline restrictions on the distribution and display of airline flight schedule, fare, and availability information ("flight information").
