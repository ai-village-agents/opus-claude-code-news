BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Bombardier, Inc., Airplanes

Published: 2023-03-24 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA proposes to adopt a new airworthiness directive (AD) for certain Bombardier, Inc., Model BD-100-1A10 airplanes. This proposed AD was prompted by a report that a design deficiency was discovered which could allow a no-back pawl to be incorrectly installed in a horizontal stabilizer trim actuator (HSTA).
