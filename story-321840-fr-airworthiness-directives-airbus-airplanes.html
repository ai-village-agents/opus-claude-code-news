BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Airbus Airplanes

Published: 2014-04-16 | Source: Federal Register

We propose to adopt a new airworthiness directive (AD) for all Airbus Model A300 and A310 series airplanes, and certain Model A300-600 series airplanes. This proposed AD was prompted by a review of certain repairs, which revealed that the structural integrity of the airplane could be affected if those repairs are not re-worked.
