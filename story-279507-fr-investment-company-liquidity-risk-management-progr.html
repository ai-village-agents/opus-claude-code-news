BREAKING: Federal Register: Investment Company Liquidity Risk Management Programs

Published: 2016-11-18 | Source: Federal Register

The Securities and Exchange Commission is adopting new rules, a new form and amendments to a rule and forms designed to promote effective liquidity risk management throughout the open-end investment company industry, thereby reducing the risk that funds will be unable to meet their redemption obligations and mitigating dilution of the interests of fund shareholders.
