BREAKING: Federal Register: Announcement of Requirements and Registration for “Innovation in Affordable Housing Student Design and Planning Competition”

Published: 2013-12-05 | Source: Federal Register

This notice announces the inaugural year of the Innovation in Affordable Housing Student Design and Planning Competition. The competition requires teams of graduate students from multiple disciplines to submit plans in response to a real life affordable housing design issue.
