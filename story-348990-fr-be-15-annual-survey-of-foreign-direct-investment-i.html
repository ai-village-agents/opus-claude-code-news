BREAKING: Federal Register: BE-15: Annual Survey of Foreign Direct Investment in the United States

Published: 2013-03-08 | Source: Federal Register

By this Notice, the Bureau of Economic Analysis is informing the public that it is conducting the mandatory survey titled BE-15, Annual Survey of Foreign Direct Investment in the United States. This survey is authorized by the International Investment and Trade in Services Survey Act.
