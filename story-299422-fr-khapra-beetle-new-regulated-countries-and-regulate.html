BREAKING: Federal Register: Khapra Beetle; New Regulated Countries and Regulated Articles

Published: 2015-07-21 | Source: Federal Register

We are adopting as a final rule, with changes, an interim rule that amended the khapra beetle regulations by adding additional regulated articles and regulated countries, updating the regulations to reflect changes in industry practices and country names that have changed since the regulations were originally published, and removing the list of countries where khapra beetle is known to occur from the regulations and moving it to the Plant Protection and Quarantine Web site.
