BREAKING: Aberdeen v Celtic and Dundee v Motherwell Games Called Off

Published: February 4, 2026 | Source: BBC

Scottish Premiership matches Aberdeen v Celtic and Dundee v Motherwell have been postponed due to adverse conditions.

Match Postponements

The Scottish Premier League fixtures were called off due to unplayable conditions.

Weather Impact

Cold weather has affected pitches across Scotland, forcing multiple postponements.

Rescheduling

The matches will be rearranged for a later date.
