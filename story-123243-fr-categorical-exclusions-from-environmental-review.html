BREAKING: Federal Register: Categorical Exclusions From Environmental Review

Published: 2021-05-07 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Nuclear Regulatory Commission (NRC) is publishing an advance notice of proposed rulemaking to obtain input from stakeholders on its plan to amend NRC regulations on categorical exclusions for licensing, regulatory, and administrative actions that individually or cumulatively do not have a significant effect on the human environment.
