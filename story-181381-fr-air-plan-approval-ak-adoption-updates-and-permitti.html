BREAKING: Federal Register: Air Plan Approval; AK: Adoption Updates and Permitting Rule Revisions

Published: 2019-06-11 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) proposes to approve revisions to the Alaska State Implementation Plan (SIP) submitted on October 25, 2018. The revisions adopt changes to federal emissions factors and modeling guidelines, update pre-construction permitting of stationary sources, and fix typographical and grammatical errors.
