BREAKING: Federal Register: Pears Grown in Oregon and Washington; Assessment Rate Decrease for Processed Pears

Published: 2012-12-05 | Source: Federal Register

This rule decreases the assessment rate established for the Processed Pear Committee (Committee) for the 2012-2013 and subsequent fiscal periods from $7.73 to $7.00 per ton of summer/fall processed pears. The Committee locally administers the marketing order which regulates the handling of processed pears grown in Oregon and Washington.
