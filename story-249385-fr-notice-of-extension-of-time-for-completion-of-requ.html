BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Extension of Time for Completion of Required Manufacturer Corrections

Published: 2017-11-24 | Source: Federal Register

This notice advises the public of a request for extension of completion of a plan for notification and correction of certain manufactured homes built by Clayton Home Building Group (Clayton) that were installed with a certain Tub-Shower Faucet diverter valve manufactured by StoneCrest. The affected diverter model is N8126C.
