BREAKING: Federal Register: Modification of VOR Federal Airways V-10, V-12, and V-508 in the Vicinity of Olathe, KS

Published: 2012-07-20 | Source: Federal Register

This action modifies three VHF Omnidirectional Range (VOR) Federal airways V-10, V-12, and V-508 in the vicinity of Olathe, KS. The FAA is taking this action to adjust the airway route structure due to the planned decommissioning of the Johnson County VOR navigation aid located on Johnson County Executive Airport, Olathe, KS.
