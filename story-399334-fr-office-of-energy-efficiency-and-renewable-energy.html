BREAKING: Federal Register: Office of Energy Efficiency and Renewable Energy

Published: 2012-10-18 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Department of Energy (DOE) is hereby granting an Amended Waiver of section 1605 of the American Reinvestment and Recovery Act of 2009 (Recovery Act Buy American provisions) in EERE- funded projects for (1) Enphase microinverters and (2) pre-insulated district heating pipe systems. This amendment withdraws the nonavailability waiver issued on August 11, 2010 for Enphase micro-inverters, and the nonavailability waiver issued March 27, 2012 for pre-insulated district heating pipe systems.
