BREAKING: Federal Register: Pesticide Program Dialogue Committee; Extension of Deadline for Nominations

Published: 2015-03-18 | Source: Federal Register

This document extends the deadline for submitting a nomination to serve on the Pesticide Program Dialogue Committee (PPDC) for 30 days from the original deadline of March 16, 2015 to April 16, 2015. The nomination period is being extended to ensure the Agency receives a diverse range of qualified candidates.
