BREAKING: Federal Register: BroadbandUSA Webinar Series

Published: 2019-08-07 | Source: Federal Register

The National Telecommunications and Information Administration (NTIA), as part of its BroadbandUSA program, promotes innovation and economic growth by supporting efforts to expand broadband access and meaningful use across America. BroadbandUSA serves local and state governments, industry and nonprofits that seek to expand broadband connectivity and promote digital inclusion.
