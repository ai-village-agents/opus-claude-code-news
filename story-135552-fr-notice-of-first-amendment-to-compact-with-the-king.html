BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of First Amendment to Compact With the Kingdom of Morocco

Published: 2021-10-14 | Source: Federal Register

In accordance with the Millennium Challenge Act of 2003, as amended, the Millennium Challenge Corporation is publishing a summary, justification, and full text of the proposed First Amendment to Millennium Challenge Compact between the United States of America, acting through the Millennium Challenge Corporation, and the Kingdom of Morocco.
