BREAKING: Federal Register: Announcement of eBond Test

Published: 2014-11-28 | Source: Federal Register

This notice announces U.S. Customs and Border Protection's (CBP's) plan to conduct a voluntary National Customs Automation Program test concerning automation of CBP's bond program (eBond test).
