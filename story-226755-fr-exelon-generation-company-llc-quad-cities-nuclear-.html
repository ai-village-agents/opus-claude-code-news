BREAKING: Federal Register: Exelon Generation Company, LLC; Quad Cities Nuclear Power Station, Units 1 and 2

Published: 2017-02-01 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Nuclear Regulatory Commission (NRC) has granted the request of Exelon Generation Company, LLC (the licensee), to withdraw its application dated October 20, 2016, for a proposed amendment to Renewed Facility Operating License Nos. DPR-29 and DPR-30, for Quad Cities Nuclear Power Station (QCNPS), Units 1 and 2, respectively.
