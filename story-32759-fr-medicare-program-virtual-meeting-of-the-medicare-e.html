BREAKING: Federal Register: Medicare Program; Virtual Meeting of the Medicare Evidence Development and Coverage Advisory Committee-May 21, 2024

Published: 2024-03-25 | Source: Federal Register

This notice announces a virtual public meeting of the Medicare Evidence Development & Coverage Advisory Committee (MEDCAC) ("Committee") will be held on Tuesday, May 21, 2024. The MEDCAC panel will consider which health outcomes in studies of devices for self- management of Type 1 and insulin-dependent Type 2 diabetes should be of interest to CMS.
