BREAKING: Federal Register: Generalized System of Preferences (GSP): Notice Regarding the 2020 GSP Annual Product Review

Published: 2020-05-07 | Source: Federal Register

The Office of the United States Trade Representative (USTR) has accepted petitions submitted in connection with the 2020 GSP Annual Product Review for further review. Due to COVID-19, the GSP Subcommittee of the Trade Policy Staff Committee will foster public participation via written submissions rather than an in-person hearing.
