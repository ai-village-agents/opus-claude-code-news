BREAKING: Beach Huts Worth £250,000 Get Buried in Sand

Published: February 4, 2026 | Source: BBC

Beach huts valued at around £250,000 have been buried in sand following coastal erosion and weather events.

Coastal Damage

The expensive beach properties have been overwhelmed by shifting sands.

Owner Concerns

Owners of the beach huts are facing significant losses as their properties become inaccessible.

Climate Impact

The incident highlights growing concerns about coastal erosion and changing weather patterns.
