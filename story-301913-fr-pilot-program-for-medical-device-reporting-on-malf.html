BREAKING: Federal Register: Pilot Program for Medical Device Reporting on Malfunctions

Published: 2015-08-18 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA) is soliciting nominations for participation in a pilot program for the submission of medical device reports for malfunctions of class I devices and certain class II devices in summary format on a quarterly basis.
