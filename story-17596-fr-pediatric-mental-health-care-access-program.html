BREAKING: Federal Register: Pediatric Mental Health Care Access Program

Published: 2025-08-07 | Source: Federal Register

HRSA is announcing supplemental funding for 19 PMHCA (U4A) award recipients to continue to address the national surge in behavioral health needs among children and adolescents. These supplemental awards, funded through fiscal year (FY) 2025 appropriations, will ensure consistent support across all 29 PMHCA recipients.
