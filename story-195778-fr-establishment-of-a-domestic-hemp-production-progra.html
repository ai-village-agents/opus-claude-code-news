BREAKING: Federal Register: Establishment of a Domestic Hemp Production Program

Published: 2019-12-18 | Source: Federal Register

The United States Department of Agriculture's Agricultural Marketing Service (AMS) is extending the comment period due date for an interim final rule published on October 31, 2019, by an additional thirty (30) days from December 30, 2019 to January 29, 2020. The interim final rule establishes a domestic hemp production program pursuant to the Agriculture Improvement Act of 2018.
