BREAKING: Federal Register: Margin Requirements for Uncleared Swaps for Swap Dealers and Major Swap Participants

Published: 2018-05-23 | Source: Federal Register

The Commodity Futures Trading Commission ("Commission" or "CFTC") is seeking comment on proposed amendments to the margin requirements for uncleared swaps for swap dealers ("SD") and major swap participants ("MSP") for which there is no prudential regulator ("CFTC Margin Rule").
