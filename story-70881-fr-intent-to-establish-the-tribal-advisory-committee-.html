BREAKING: Federal Register: Intent To Establish the Tribal Advisory Committee and Solicitation of Nominations for Membership to the Committee

Published: 2023-06-28 | Source: Federal Register

In accordance with section 12303 of the Agriculture Improvement Act of 2018 and the Federal Advisory Committee Act, as amended, the United States Department of Agriculture announces its intent to establish of the Tribal Advisory Committee by the Secretary of Agriculture. The Tribal Advisory Committee will advise the Secretary on matters related to Tribal and Indian affairs. The Tribal Advisory Committee will be governed by the provisions of FACA as applicable.
