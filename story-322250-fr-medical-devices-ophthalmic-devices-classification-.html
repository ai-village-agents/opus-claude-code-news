BREAKING: Federal Register: Medical Devices; Ophthalmic Devices; Classification of the Eyelid Weight

Published: 2014-04-21 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA or Agency) is classifying the eyelid weight into class II (special controls). The Agency is exempting the external eyelid weight from premarket notification, but continuing to require premarket notification for implantable eyelid weights in order to provide a reasonable assurance of safety and effectiveness of the device.
