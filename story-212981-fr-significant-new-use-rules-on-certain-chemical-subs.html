BREAKING: Federal Register: Significant New Use Rules on Certain Chemical Substances

Published: 2018-08-01 | Source: Federal Register

EPA is proposing significant new use rules (SNURs) under the Toxic Substances Control Act (TSCA) for 145 chemical substances which were the subject of premanufacture notices (PMNs). The chemical substances are subject to Orders issued by EPA pursuant to section 5(e) of TSCA.
