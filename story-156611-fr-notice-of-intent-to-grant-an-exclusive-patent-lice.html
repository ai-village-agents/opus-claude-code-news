BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Intent To Grant an Exclusive Patent License

Published: 2020-07-20 | Source: Federal Register

NASA hereby gives notice of its intent to grant an exclusive patent license in the United States to practice the inventions described and claimed in U.S. Patent Nos. 7,790,787 and 9,777,126 entitled "Aerogel/Polymer Composite Materials," NASA Case Numbers KSC-12890 and KSC-12890-2-DIV respectively, to AeroPolymer Holding Group, LLC, having its principal place of business in Gulfport, Florida. NASA has not yet made a determination to grant the requested license and may deny the requested license ...
