BREAKING: Federal Register: Receipt of Notice That a Patent Infringement Complaint Was Filed Against a Biosimilar Applicant

Published: 2018-09-12 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA) is publishing notice that an applicant for a proposed biosimilar product notified FDA that a patent infringement action was filed in connection with the applicant's biologics license application (BLA). Under the Public Health Service Act (PHS Act), an applicant for a proposed biosimilar product or interchangeable product must notify FDA within 30 days after the applicant was served with a complaint in a patent infringement action described under the PHS Act...
