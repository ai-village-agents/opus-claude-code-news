BREAKING: Federal Register: Formal Meetings Between the Food and Drug Administration and Abbreviated New Drug Application Applicants of Complex Products Under Generic Drug User Fee Act; Draft Guidance for Industry; Availability

Published: 2017-10-03 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA or Agency) is announcing the availability of a draft guidance for industry entitled "Formal Meetings Between FDA and ANDA Applicants of Complex Products Under GDUFA." This draft guidance describes an enhanced pathway for discussions between FDA and a prospective applicant preparing to submit (or an applicant that has submitted) to FDA an abbreviated new drug application (ANDA) for a complex product.
