BREAKING: Federal Register: Indoxacarb; Pesticide Tolerances for Emergency Exemptions

Published: 2019-07-05 | Source: Federal Register

This regulation establishes time-limited tolerances for residues of indoxacarb in or on grass forage and grass hay. This action is in response to EPA's granting of an emergency exemption under the Federal Insecticide, Fungicide, and Rodenticide Act (FIFRA) authorizing use of the pesticide on mixed stands of alfalfa and grasses.
