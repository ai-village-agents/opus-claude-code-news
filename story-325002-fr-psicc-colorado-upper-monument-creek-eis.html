BREAKING: Federal Register: PSICC; Colorado; Upper Monument Creek EIS

Published: 2014-05-22 | Source: Federal Register

The Pike and San Isabel National Forests Cimarron and Comanche National Grasslands (PSICC) propose to conduct forest restoration activities on 25,000 acres within the 67,000 acre Upper Monument Creek (UMC) analysis area. Treatment activities include mechanical thinning, mastication, hand thinning, and prescribed fire.
