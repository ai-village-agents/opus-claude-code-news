BREAKING: Federal Register: Secretary of Energy Advisory Board; Notice of Open Meeting

Published: 2022-07-05 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Energy hereby publishes a notice of a partially virtual, open meeting of the Secretary of Energy Advisory Board (SEAB). This meeting will be held virtually for members of the public and in-person for Board members.
