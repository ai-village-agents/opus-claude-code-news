BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Aerospace Welding Minneapolis, Inc. Mufflers

Published: 2017-04-18 | Source: Federal Register

We propose to adopt a new airworthiness directive (AD) for certain Aerospace Welding Minneapolis, Inc. mufflers, part numbers A1754001-23 and A1754001-25, installed on Textron Aviation Inc. (type certificate previously held by Cessna Aircraft Company) Models 172, 172R, 172S, and 177 airplanes.
