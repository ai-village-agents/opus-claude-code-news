BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Realty Action: Modified Competitive Sale of 35 Parcels of Public Land in Clark County, Nevada

Published: 2022-02-16 | Source: Federal Register

The Bureau of Land Management (BLM) proposes to offer 35 parcels of public land totaling 400.815 acres in the Las Vegas Valley (Valley) by modified competitive sale at no less than each parcel's Fair Market Value (FMV) pursuant to the Southern Nevada Public Land Management Act of 1998 (SNPLMA), as amended.
