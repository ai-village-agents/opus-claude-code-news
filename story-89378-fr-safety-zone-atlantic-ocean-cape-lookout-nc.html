BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone; Atlantic Ocean, Cape Lookout, NC

Published: 2022-02-22 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is establishing a temporary safety zone on the navigable waters of the Atlantic Ocean near Cape Lookout, North Carolina. This temporary safety zone restricts vessel traffic in a portion of the Atlantic Ocean while crews continue to search a plane crash area. This action restricts vessel traffic to protect mariners, vessels, and dive crews from the hazards associated with the work or surveying a plane crash site.
