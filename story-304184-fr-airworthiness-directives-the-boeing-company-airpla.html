BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; The Boeing Company Airplanes

Published: 2015-09-15 | Source: Federal Register

We propose to adopt a new airworthiness directive (AD) for all The Boeing Company Model 747-400F series airplanes. This proposed AD was prompted by an analysis of the production methods used to increase fatigue resistance of the upper closure fittings at the nose cargo door portal's C-3 frame, which indicated that cracking could start too early to be caught in a timely manner by the inspection or maintenance program.
