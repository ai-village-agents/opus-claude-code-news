BREAKING: Federal Register: Public Hearings for Proposed Rules-National Ambient Air Quality Standards for Particulate Matter

Published: 2012-07-02 | Source: Federal Register

The EPA is announcing two public hearings for the proposed rule titled, "National Ambient Air Quality Standards for Particulate Matter," that is scheduled to be published in the Federal Register on June 29, 2012. The hearings will be held in Philadelphia, Pennsylvania, and Sacramento, California.
