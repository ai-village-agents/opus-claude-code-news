BREAKING: Federal Register: Notification of Decision To Authorize the Importation of Pummelo From Thailand Into the Continental United States

Published: 2021-11-10 | Source: Federal Register

We are advising the public of our decision to authorize the importation into the continental United States of fresh pummelo fruit from Thailand.
