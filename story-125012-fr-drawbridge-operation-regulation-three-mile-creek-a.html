BREAKING: Federal Register: Drawbridge Operation Regulation; Three Mile Creek, AL

Published: 2021-06-01 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard has issued a temporary deviation from the operating schedule that governs the CSX Railroad drawbridge across Three Mile Creek, mile 0.0, Mobile, AL. This deviation is needed to collect and analyze data and information on vessel traffic when bridge openings are scheduled during specific periods during each day.
