BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; The Boeing Company Airplanes

Published: 2016-06-07 | Source: Federal Register

We are adopting a new airworthiness directive (AD) for certain The Boeing Company Model 777-200 and -300 series airplanes equipped with Pratt and Whitney engines. This AD was prompted by reports of blocked drain lines at the engine forward strut that caused flammable fluid to accumulate in a flammable leakage zone.
