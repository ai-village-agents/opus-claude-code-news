BREAKING: Federal Register: Request for Information on the Cyber Incident Reporting for Critical Infrastructure Act of 2022

Published: 2022-09-12 | Source: Federal Register

The Cybersecurity and Infrastructure Security Agency (CISA) is issuing this Request for Information (RFI) to receive input from the public as CISA develops proposed regulations required by the Cyber Incident Reporting for Critical Infrastructure Act of 2022 (CIRCIA). Among other things, CIRCIA directs CISA to develop and oversee implementation of regulations requiring covered entities to submit to CISA reports detailing covered cyber incidents and ransom payments.
