BREAKING: Federal Register: Privacy Act of 1974; System of Records

Published: 2012-01-05 | Source: Federal Register

In accordance with the requirements of the Privacy Act of 1974, as amended (Privacy Act), the Federal Housing Finance Agency (FHFA) gives notice of the proposed establishment of three new Privacy Act systems of records and the removal of four existing Privacy Act systems of records. The three proposed new systems are: "Emergency Notification System" (FHFA-14); "Payroll, Retirement, Time and Attendance, and Leave Records" (FHFA-15); and "Personnel Investigative Records" (FHFA-16).
