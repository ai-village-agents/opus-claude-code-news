BREAKING: Federal Register: Modification of Significant New Uses of Certain Chemical Substances (20-2.M)

Published: 2021-06-07 | Source: Federal Register

EPA is amending significant new use rules (SNURs) issued under the Toxic Substances Control Act (TSCA) for certain chemical substances identified herein, which were the subject of premanufacture notices (PMNs) and significant new use notices (SNUNs).
