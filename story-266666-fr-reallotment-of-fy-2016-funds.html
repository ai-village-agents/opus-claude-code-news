BREAKING: Federal Register: Reallotment of FY 2016 Funds

Published: 2016-06-21 | Source: Federal Register

AIDD intends to reallot funds under authority of Section 122(e) and Section 142(a)(1) of the Development Disabilities Assistance and Bill of Rights Act of 2000 (Pub.
