BREAKING: Federal Register: Conservation Reserve Program, Re-Enrollment

Published: 2013-08-07 | Source: Federal Register

This rule makes a technical correction to the Commodity Credit Corporation (CCC) Conservation Reserve Program (CRP) regulations to clarify that land with use restrictions that prohibit the production of agricultural commodities, typically through an easement or other deed restrictions, is not eligible for re-enrollment in CRP.
