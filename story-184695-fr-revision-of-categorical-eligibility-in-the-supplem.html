BREAKING: Federal Register: Revision of Categorical Eligibility in the Supplemental Nutrition Assistance Program (SNAP)

Published: 2019-07-24 | Source: Federal Register

Section 5(a) of the Food and Nutrition Act of 2008, as amended, provides that households in which each member receives benefits under a State program funded under part A of Title IV of the Social Security Act (SSA) (also known as Temporary Assistance for Needy Families (TANF) block grants \1\) shall be categorically eligible for the Supplemental Nutrition Assistance Program (SNAP).
