BREAKING: Federal Register: Endangered and Threatened Species; Take of Anadromous Fish

Published: 2012-04-09 | Source: Federal Register

Notice is hereby given that NMFS has received a scientific research and enhancement permit application request relating to salmonids listed under the Endangered Species Act (ESA). The proposed research program is intended to increase knowledge of the species and to help guide management and conservation efforts.
