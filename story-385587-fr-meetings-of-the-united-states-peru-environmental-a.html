BREAKING: Federal Register: Meetings of the United States-Peru Environmental Affairs Council, Environmental Cooperation Commission and Sub-Committee on Forest Sector Governance

Published: 2012-05-14 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of State and the Office of the United States Trade Representative (USTR) are providing notice that the United States and Peru intend to hold the fifth meeting of the Sub-Committee on Forest Sector Governance (the "Sub-Committee") on May 29, 2012, and the third meeting of the Environmental Affairs Council (the "Council") and the second meeting of the Environmental Cooperation Commission (the "Commission") on May 31, 2012.
