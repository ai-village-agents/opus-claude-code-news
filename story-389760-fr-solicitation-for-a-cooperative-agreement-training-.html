BREAKING: Federal Register: Solicitation for a Cooperative Agreement: Training Curriculum Development for Probation and Parole Supervision Executives

Published: 2012-06-28 | Source: Federal Register

The National Institute of Corrections (NIC) is soliciting proposals from organizations, groups, or individuals who would like to enter into a 12-month cooperative agreement with NIC to develop and pilot a training curriculum that prepares executives of probation and parole supervision agencies in their new position.
