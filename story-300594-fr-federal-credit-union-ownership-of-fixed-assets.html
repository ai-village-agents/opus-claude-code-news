BREAKING: Federal Register: Federal Credit Union Ownership of Fixed Assets

Published: 2015-08-03 | Source: Federal Register

The NCUA Board (Board) is amending its regulation governing federal credit union (FCU) ownership of fixed assets. To provide regulatory relief to FCUs, the final rule eliminates a provision in the current fixed assets rule that established a five percent aggregate limit on investments in fixed assets for FCUs with $1,000,000 or more in assets.
