BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone; Riverwalk Marketplace/Lundi Gras Fireworks Display, New Orleans, LA

Published: 2023-02-09 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard will enforce a temporary safety zone for the Riverwalk Marketplace/Lundi Gras fireworks display on February 20, 2023, to provide for the safety of life on navigable waterways during this event. Our regulation for annual fireworks displays and other events in the Eighth Coast Guard District requiring safety zones identifies the regulated area for this event on the navigable waters of the Lower Mississippi River between Mile Marker (MM) 93 and MM 96, New Orleans, LA.
