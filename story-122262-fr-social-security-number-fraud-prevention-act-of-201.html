BREAKING: Federal Register: Social Security Number Fraud Prevention Act of 2017 Implementation

Published: 2021-04-26 | Source: Federal Register

This final rule revises the Department of Commerce (Department) regulations under the Freedom of Information Act (FOIA) and the Privacy Act. The revisions clarify and update the language of procedural requirements pertaining to the inclusion of Social Security account numbers on documents that the Department sends by mail.
