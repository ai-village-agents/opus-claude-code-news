BREAKING: Federal Register: Revised Medical Criteria for Evaluating Visual Disorders

Published: 2012-02-13 | Source: Federal Register

We propose to revise and reorganize the criteria in the Listing of Impairments (listings) that we use to evaluate cases involving visual disorders in adults and children under titles II and XVI of the Social Security Act (Act). The proposed revisions reflect our program experience and address adjudicator questions we have received since we last revised these criteria in 2006.
