BREAKING: Federal Register: Introduction of Accountable Measures Regarding Access to Personal Information of .us Registrants

Published: 2023-05-01 | Source: Federal Register

The United States Department of Commerce's (Department) National Telecommunications and Information Administration (NTIA) administers the contract for the country code top-level domain (ccTLD) for the United States, ".us" (usTLD). NTIA seeks input from interested parties on the introduction of accountability measures regarding access to the personal information of usTLD registrants.
