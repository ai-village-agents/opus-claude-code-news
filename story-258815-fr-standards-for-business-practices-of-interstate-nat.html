BREAKING: Federal Register: Standards for Business Practices of Interstate Natural Gas Pipelines

Published: 2016-03-23 | Source: Federal Register

In Order No. 587-W, the Federal Energy Regulatory Commission (Commission) amended its regulations to incorporate by reference the latest version (Version 3.0) of seven business practice standards applicable to interstate natural gas pipelines adopted by the Wholesale Gas Quadrant of the North American Energy Standards Board.
