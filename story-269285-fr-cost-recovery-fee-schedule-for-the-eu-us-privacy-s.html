BREAKING: Federal Register: Cost Recovery Fee Schedule for the EU-U.S. Privacy Shield Framework

Published: 2016-07-22 | Source: Federal Register

Consistent with the guidelines in OMB Circular A-25, the U.S. Department of Commerce's International Trade Administration (ITA) is implementing a cost recovery program fee to support the operation of the EU-U.S. Privacy Shield Framework (Privacy Shield), which will require that U.S. organizations pay an annual fee to ITA in order to participate in the Privacy Shield.
