BREAKING: Federal Register: Green River National Wildlife Refuge, KY; Establishment of Hunting Permit Fees

Published: 2024-12-20 | Source: Federal Register

We, the Fish and Wildlife Service, announce our intent to establish and implement amenity fees at Green River National Wildlife Refuge (Refuge), located in Kentucky, as authorized by the Federal Lands Recreation Enhancement Act (FLREA). We intend to implement hunting permit fees. Under FLREA provisions, the refuge will identify and post the fees.
