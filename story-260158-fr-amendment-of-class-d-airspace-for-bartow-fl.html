BREAKING: Federal Register: Amendment of Class D Airspace for Bartow, FL

Published: 2016-04-06 | Source: Federal Register

This action amends Class D Airspace at Bartow Municipal Airport, Bartow, FL, by adjusting the ceiling of the Class D airspace area from 2,600 feet to 1,600 feet above the surface.
