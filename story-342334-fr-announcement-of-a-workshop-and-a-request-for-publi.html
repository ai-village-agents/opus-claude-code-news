BREAKING: Federal Register: Announcement of a Workshop and a Request for Public Comment on Questions Regarding Dietary Reference Intakes and Chronic Disease Endpoints

Published: 2014-12-19 | Source: Federal Register

The Dietary Reference Intakes (DRI) Committees of the U.S. and Canadian governments will hold a workshop entitled "Options for Consideration of Chronic Disease Endpoints for Dietary Reference Intakes."
