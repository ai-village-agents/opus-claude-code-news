BREAKING: Federal Register: Consideration of Rulemaking To Address Prompt Remediation of Residual Radioactivity During Operations

Published: 2013-06-03 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Nuclear Regulatory Commission (NRC) is seeking additional input from the public, licensees, Agreement States, non- Agreement States, and other stakeholders on a potential rulemaking to address prompt remediation of residual radioactivity during the operational phase of licensed material sites and nuclear reactors.
