BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Dassault Aviation Airplanes

Published: 2013-04-19 | Source: Federal Register

We are adopting a new airworthiness directive (AD) for all Dassault Aviation Model FALCON 7X airplanes. This AD requires revising the aircraft flight manual (AFM); performing operational tests of the oxygen mask oxygen assembly; and replacing affected stowage boxes, which terminates the AFM revision and operational tests.
