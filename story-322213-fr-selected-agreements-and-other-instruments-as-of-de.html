BREAKING: Federal Register: Selected Agreements and Other Instruments as of December 31, 2013, Between the American Institute in Taiwan and the Taipei Economic and Cultural Representative Office in the United States

Published: 2014-04-21 | Source: Federal Register

The American Institute in Taiwan has concluded a number of selected agreements and other instruments with the Taipei Economic and Cultural Representative Office in the United States (formerly the Coordination Council for North American Affairs) in order to maintain cultural, commercial and other unofficial relations between the American people and the people of Taiwan.
