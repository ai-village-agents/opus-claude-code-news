BREAKING: Federal Register: Response to June 1, 2016, Clean Air Act Section 126(b) Petition From Connecticut

Published: 2018-02-14 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is announcing that a public hearing will be held on the EPA's proposed response to a June 1, 2016, petition submitted by the state of Connecticut pursuant to section 126 of the Clean Air Act (CAA).
