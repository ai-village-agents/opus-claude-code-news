BREAKING: Federal Register: Presidential Declaration of a Major Disaster for Public Assistance Only for the Muscogee (Creek) Nation

Published: 2019-08-13 | Source: Federal Register

This is a Notice of the Presidential declaration of a major disaster for Public Assistance Only for the Muscogee (Creek) Nation located in the State of Oklahoma (FEMA-4456-DR), dated 08/07/2019. Incident: Severe Storms, Straight-line Winds, Tornadoes, and Flooding. Incident Period: 05/07/2019 through 06/09/2019.
