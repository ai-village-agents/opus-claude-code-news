BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zones; Fireworks Displays in the Fifth Coast Guard District

Published: 2019-04-23 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard will enforce the Penn's Landing, Delaware River, Philadelphia, PA, safety zone from 8:30 p.m. through 9:00 p.m. on April 26, 2019. This action is necessary to ensure safety of life on the navigable waters of the United States immediately prior to, during, and immediately after the fireworks displays.
