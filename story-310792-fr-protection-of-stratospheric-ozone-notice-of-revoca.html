BREAKING: Federal Register: Protection of Stratospheric Ozone: Notice of Revocation of Certification for Refrigerant Reclaimers

Published: 2015-12-02 | Source: Federal Register

In accordance with 40 CFR 82.164, no person may sell or offer for sale or use as a refrigerant, any class I or class II ozone- depleting substance consisting wholly or in part of used refrigerant unless the substance has been reclaimed by an Environmental Protection Agency (EPA)-certified refrigerant reclaimer.
