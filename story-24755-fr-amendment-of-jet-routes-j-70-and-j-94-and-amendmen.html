BREAKING: Federal Register: Amendment of Jet Routes J-70 and J-94 and Amendment of Very High Frequency Omnidirectional Range Federal Airways V-30, V-55, V-84, V-170, and V-274 and Revocation of Jet Routes J-547 and J-548 in the Vicinity of Pullman, MI

Published: 2025-12-10 | Source: Federal Register

This action proposes to amend Jet Routes J-70 and J-94; amend Very High Frequency Omnidirectional Range (VOR) Federal Airways V-30, V-55, V-84, V-170, and V-274; and revoke Jet Routes J-547 and J-548 in the vicinity of Pullman, MI. The FAA is proposing this action due to the planned decommissioning of the VOR portion of the Pullman, MI, VOR/ Distance Measuring Equipment (DME) navigational aid (NAVAID).
