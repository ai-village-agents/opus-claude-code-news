BREAKING: Federal Register: Promoting Innovation and Competition in the Provision of Multichannel Video Programming Distribution Services

Published: 2015-01-15 | Source: Federal Register

In this document, the Commission propose new rules designed to better reflect the fact that video services are being provided increasingly over the Internet. Specifically, we propose to modernize our interpretation of the term "multichannel video programming distributor" ("MVPD") by including within its scope services that make available for purchase, by subscribers or customers, multiple linear streams of video programming, regardless of the technology used to distribute the programming.
