BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Realty Action: Recreation and Public Purposes Act Classification; Arizona

Published: 2018-12-13 | Source: Federal Register

The Bureau of Land Management (BLM) has examined certain public lands in Pinal County, Arizona, and has found them suitable for classification for lease or conveyance to the Pinal County Board of Supervisors under the provisions of the Recreation and Public Purpose Act (R&PP), as amended, the Taylor Grazing Act, and Executive Order No. 6910.
