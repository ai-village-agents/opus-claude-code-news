BREAKING: Federal Register: National Advisory Council on Indian Education (NACIE); Meeting

Published: 2019-07-23 | Source: Federal Register

This notice sets forth the agenda for an upcoming virtual, public meeting of the National Advisory Council on Indian Education (NACIE). Notice of the meeting is required by section 10(a)(2) of the Federal Advisory Committee Act (FACA) and is intended to notify members of the public who may be interested in participating online and/or by teleconference. This notice is being posted less than 15 days due to the need to secure funding and coordinate technology services for a virtual meeting.
