BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Applications for Deregistration Under Section 8(f) of the Investment Company Act of 1940

Published: 2021-08-05 | Source: Federal Register

Applicant, a closed-end investment company, seeks an order declaring that it has ceased to be an investment company. On May 17, 2019, August 28, 2019, December 20, 2019, April 2, 2020, and July 1, 2020, applicant made liquidating distributions to its shareholders based on net asset value. Expenses of $67,000 incurred in connection with the liquidation were paid by the applicant. Applicant also has retained $67,000 for the purpose of paying outstanding accrued liabilities.
