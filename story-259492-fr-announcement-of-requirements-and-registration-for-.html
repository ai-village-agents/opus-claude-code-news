BREAKING: Federal Register: Announcement of Requirements and Registration for a Prize Competition Seeking Downstream Fish Passage at Tall Dams

Published: 2016-03-31 | Source: Federal Register

The Bureau of Reclamation, in collaboration with other Federal agencies (U.S. Geological Survey, U.S. Fish and Wildlife Service, National Oceanic and Atmospheric Administration-National Marine Fisheries Service, and U.S. Army Corps of Engineers), is seeking new ideas for gaining successful and cost-effective downstream passage of juvenile fish at tall (high-head) dams.
