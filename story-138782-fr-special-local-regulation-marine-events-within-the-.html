BREAKING: Federal Register: Special Local Regulation; Marine Events Within the Eleventh Coast Guard District-Mission Bay Parade of Lights

Published: 2021-11-26 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard will enforce the special local regulation on the waters of Mission Bay, San Diego, California, during the Mission Bay Parade of Lights on December 11, 2021. This special local regulation is necessary to provide for the safety of the participants, crew, sponsor vessels of the boat parade, and general users of the waterway.
