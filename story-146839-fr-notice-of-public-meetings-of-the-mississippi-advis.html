BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Public Meetings of the Mississippi Advisory Committee to the U.S. Commission on Civil Rights

Published: 2020-03-17 | Source: Federal Register

Notice is hereby given, pursuant to the provisions of the rules and regulations of the U.S. Commission on Civil Rights (Commission) and the Federal Advisory Committee Act that the Mississippi Advisory Committee (Committee) will hold a meeting on Wednesday April 15, 2020 at 12 p.m. Central time. The Committee will discuss next steps in their study of prosecutorial discretion in the state.
