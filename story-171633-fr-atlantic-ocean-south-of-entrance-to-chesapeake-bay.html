BREAKING: Federal Register: Atlantic Ocean South of Entrance to Chesapeake Bay; Firing Range

Published: 2019-02-13 | Source: Federal Register

The Corps of Engineers is proposing to amend an existing permanent danger zone in the waters of the Atlantic Ocean south of the entrance to the Chesapeake Bay off of the coast of Virginia. For decades, the Dam Neck Surface Danger Zone (SDZ) served as a firing range for gunnery training at what is now Naval Air Station Oceana's Dam Neck Annex.
