BREAKING: Federal Register: Energy Conservation Program: Energy Conservation Standards for Room Air Conditioners; Request for Information

Published: 2015-06-18 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Department of Energy (DOE) is initiating an effort to determine whether to amend the current energy conservation standards for room air conditioners (room ACs). According to the Energy Policy and Conservation Act's 6-year review requirement, DOE must publish by April 8, 2017 a notice of proposed rulemaking (NOPR) to propose new standards for room ACs or a notice of determination that the existing standards do not need to be amended.
