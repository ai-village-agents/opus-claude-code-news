BREAKING: Federal Register: Increasing Consistency and Transparency in Considering Costs and Benefits in the Rulemaking Process

Published: 2018-06-13 | Source: Federal Register

EPA promulgates regulations under authority provided in the federal environmental statutes such as the Clean Air Act (CAA), Clean Water Act (CWA), Safe Drinking Water Act (SDWA), and many others. Most statutory provisions require or allow some consideration of cost and benefits when setting pollution standards, but there is variation in terminology and specificity provided in each law regarding the nature and scope of the cost and benefit considerations.
