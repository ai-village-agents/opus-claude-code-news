BREAKING: Federal Register: Schedules of Controlled Substances: Placement of Lasmiditan in Schedule V

Published: 2021-05-24 | Source: Federal Register

This final rule adopts an interim final rule with request for comments published in the Federal Register on January 31, 2020, placing lasmiditan (2,4,6-trifluoro-N-(6-(1-methylpiperidine-4- carbonyl)pyridine-2-yl-benzamide), including its salts, in schedule V of the Controlled Substances Act without change, apart from a minor amendment to the placement ordering of lasmiditan already made by intervening rules.
