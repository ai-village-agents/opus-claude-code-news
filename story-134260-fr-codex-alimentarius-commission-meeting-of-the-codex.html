BREAKING: Federal Register: Codex Alimentarius Commission: Meeting of the Codex Alimentarius Commission

Published: 2021-09-29 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S Codex Office is sponsoring a public meeting on October 21, 2021. The objective of the public meeting is to provide information and receive public comments on agenda items and draft United States (U.S.) positions for the 44th Session of the of the Codex Alimentarius Commission (CAC). The CAC will not be meeting physically due to the continued COVID-19 pandemic.
