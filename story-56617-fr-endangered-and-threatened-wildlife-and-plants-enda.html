BREAKING: Federal Register: Endangered and Threatened Wildlife and Plants; Endangered Species Status for the Blue Tree Monitor

Published: 2024-12-26 | Source: Federal Register

We, the U.S. Fish and Wildlife Service (Service), propose to list the blue tree monitor (Varanus macraei), a lizard species from Indonesia, as an endangered species under the Endangered Species Act of 1973, as amended (Act). This determination also serves as our 12-month finding on a petition to list the blue tree monitor.
