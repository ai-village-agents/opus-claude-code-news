BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Availability of Errata to the Draft Tier 1 Environmental Impact Statement and Preliminary Section 4(f) Evaluation for Interstate 11 Corridor Between Nogales and Wickenburg, AZ

Published: 2019-05-01 | Source: Federal Register

The FHWA is issuing this notice of availability. This notice announces the publication of the Errata to the Draft Tier 1 Environmental Impact Statement and Preliminary Section 4(f) Evaluation for Interstate 11 Corridor between Nogales and Wickenburg, AZ for review, and that the review and comment period is extended to July 8, 2019.
