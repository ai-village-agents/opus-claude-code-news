BREAKING: Federal Register: Process for Establishing Rates for Veterinary Services User Fees

Published: 2023-08-01 | Source: Federal Register

We are revising the regulations concerning user fees that we charge for veterinary diagnostic services and for certain import- related and export-related services for live animals, animal products and byproducts, birds, germplasm, organisms, and vectors. We are removing the tables providing the individual fees from the regulations and posting them on an Animal and Plant Health Inspection Service website.
