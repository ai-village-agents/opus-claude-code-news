BREAKING: Federal Register: Requests for Applications; Practitioners Advisory Group

Published: 2017-06-21 | Source: Federal Register

In view of upcoming vacancies in the voting membership of the Practitioners Advisory Group, the United States Sentencing Commission hereby invites any individual who is eligible to be appointed to one of the vacancies to apply. The voting memberships covered by this notice are two circuit memberships (for the Sixth Circuit and the Seventh Circuit) and one at-large membership.
