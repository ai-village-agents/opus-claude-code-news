BREAKING: Federal Register: Rescission of Portions of Permanent Program Performance Standards Related to Siltation Structures

Published: 2026-01-27 | Source: Federal Register

The Office of Surface Mining Reclamation and Enforcement (OSM) is confirming the effective date of January 27, 2026, for the direct final rule "Rescission of Portions of Permanent Program Performance Standards Related to Siltation Structures," published on November 28, 2025. The direct final rule removes paragraphs that required that all surface drainage from the disturbed area pass through a siltation structure before leaving the permit area.
