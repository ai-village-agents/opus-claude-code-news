BREAKING: Federal Register: Small Business Size Standards: Revised Size Standards Methodology

Published: 2023-12-11 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Small Business Administration (SBA or Agency) advises the public that it has revised its white paper explaining how it establishes, reviews, and modifies small business size standards.
