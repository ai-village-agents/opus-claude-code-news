BREAKING: Federal Register: Hours of Service of Drivers: Reiman Corp.; Denial of Application for Exemption

Published: 2024-07-19 | Source: Federal Register

FMCSA announces its denial of Reiman Corp.'s (Reiman) request for an exemption from certain hours-of-service (HOS) regulations. Reiman's drivers transport latex embedded cement for use at highway construction sites. Reiman requests that it be allowed to operate under the same HOS exemption provided for "specially trained drivers of commercial motor vehicles that are specially constructed to service oil wells."
