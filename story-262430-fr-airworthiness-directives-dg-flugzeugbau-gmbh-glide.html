BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; DG Flugzeugbau GmbH Gliders

Published: 2016-05-02 | Source: Federal Register

We are revising Airworthiness Directive (AD) 2015-09-04 for DG Flugzeugbau GmbH Model DG-1000T gliders equipped with a Solo Kleinmotoren Model 2350 C engine. This AD results from mandatory continuing airworthiness information (MCAI) issued by an aviation authority of another country to identify and correct an unsafe condition on an aviation product.
