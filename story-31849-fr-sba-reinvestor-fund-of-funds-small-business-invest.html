BREAKING: Federal Register: SBA Reinvestor (“Fund-of-Funds”) Small Business Investment Company (SBIC) License Educational Public Webinar

Published: 2024-03-13 | Source: Federal Register

The SBA is holding a webinar to educate the public on the new Reinvestor SBIC License introduced to the market as part of the SBIC Investment Diversification and Growth Final Rule that went into effect on August 17, 2023.
