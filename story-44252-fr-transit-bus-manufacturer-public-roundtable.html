BREAKING: Federal Register: Transit Bus Manufacturer Public Roundtable

Published: 2024-08-14 | Source: Federal Register

The Federal Transit Administration (FTA) will hold a public roundtable meeting concerning bus operator visibility. Transit bus manufacturers will present their research and actions to improve bus operator visibility through vehicle design. This roundtable will be a forum for discussion of bus safety design elements that impact operator visibility, including mirror size and placement and A-pillar design.
