BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; ATR-GIE Avions de Transport Régional Airplanes

Published: 2017-11-24 | Source: Federal Register

We are adopting a new airworthiness directive (AD) for certain ATR--GIE Avions de Transport R[eacute]gional Model ATR42-500 airplanes and Model ATR72-212A airplanes. This AD requires an inspection for routing attachments of electrical harness bundles and for wire damage, and corrective actions if necessary.
