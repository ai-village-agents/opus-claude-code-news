BREAKING: Federal Register: National Organic Program; Notice of Availability of National List Petition Guidelines

Published: 2016-03-10 | Source: Federal Register

The Agricultural Marketing Service (AMS) National Organic Program (NOP) is announcing the availability of NOP 3011, National List Petition Guidelines. These guidelines apply to petitions that request an amendment to the NOP's National List of Allowed and Prohibited Substances (National List). The National List identifies the synthetic substances that may be used and the nonsynthetic substances that may not be used in organic production.
