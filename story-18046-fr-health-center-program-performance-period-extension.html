BREAKING: Federal Register: Health Center Program Performance Period Extensions; Correction

Published: 2025-08-14 | Source: Federal Register

HRSA is extending health center grantees' performance periods to a total of 4 years. The change from a 3-year performance period to a 4-year performance period will provide current health centers additional time to serve their service area before they apply for a new award and will provide a funding amount consistent with what would have been made available through the Service Area Competition (SAC).
