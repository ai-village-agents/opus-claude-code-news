BREAKING: Federal Register: Electronic Study Data Submission; Data Standards; Study Data Standardization Plan Recommendations

Published: 2015-05-18 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA) is announcing the availability of draft recommendations for preparing a Study Data Standardization Plan (Standardization Plan). The Standardization Plan is referenced in the Study Data Technical Conformance Guide (Guide). The Guide supplements the guidance for industry "Providing Regulatory Submissions in Electronic Format--Standardized Study Data" and provides specifications, recommendations, and general considerations on submitting standardized study data...
