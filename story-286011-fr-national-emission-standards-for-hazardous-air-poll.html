BREAKING: Federal Register: National Emission Standards for Hazardous Air Pollutants (Radionuclides); Availability of Updated Compliance Model

Published: 2015-02-10 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is announcing the availability of Version 4 of the CAP88-PC model. This version may be used to demonstrate compliance with the National Emission Standards for Hazardous Air Pollutants (NESHAP) applicable to radionuclides. CAP88-PC is approved for this use by EPA.
