BREAKING: Federal Register: TRISO-X, LLC; Special Nuclear Material License Application for the TRISO-X Fuel Fabrication Facility; Draft Environmental Impact Statement

Published: 2025-11-17 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Nuclear Regulatory Commission (NRC) is extending the public comment period for a draft Environmental Impact Statement (EIS) for a Special Nuclear Material License Application for the TRISO-X Fuel Fabrication Facility. TRISO-X, LLC (TRISO-X) requested authorization to possess and use special nuclear material to manufacture high-assay low enriched uranium fuel.
