BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Learjet Inc. Airplanes

Published: 2013-08-13 | Source: Federal Register

We propose to supersede an existing airworthiness directive (AD) that applies to certain Learjet Inc. Model 60 airplanes. The existing AD currently requires determining if a certain fuel crossflow tube is installed; and follow-on/corrective actions, as applicable.
