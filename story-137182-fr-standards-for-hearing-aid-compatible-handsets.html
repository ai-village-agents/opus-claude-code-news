BREAKING: Federal Register: Standards for Hearing Aid-Compatible Handsets

Published: 2021-11-04 | Source: Federal Register

The Federal Communications Commission (Commission) announces that the Office of Management and Budget (OMB) has approved, for a period of three years, the information collection requirements associated with the amendment of the Commission's rules governing standards for hearing aid-compatible handsets.
