BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Eurocopter Deutschland Helicopters

Published: 2012-11-20 | Source: Federal Register

We are adopting a new airworthiness directive (AD) for all Eurocopter Deutschland GmbH (ECD) Model MBB-BK117 C-2 helicopters with certain Generator Control Units (GCU) installed. This AD requires replacing the GCUs. This AD was prompted by reports of internal short circuits in certain GCUs.
