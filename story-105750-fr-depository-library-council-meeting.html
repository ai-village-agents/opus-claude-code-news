BREAKING: Federal Register: Depository Library Council Meeting

Published: 2022-09-21 | Source: Federal Register

The Depository Library Council (DLC) will meet in conjunction with the Federal Depository Library Conference from Monday, October 17, 2022 through Wednesday, October 19, 2022, virtually. The sessions will take place from 12 p.m.. to 5:30 p.m. (EDT). The meetings will take place online, and anyone can register to attend at https://www.fdlp.gov/2022-fdl-conference.
