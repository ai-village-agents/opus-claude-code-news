BREAKING: Federal Register: Consensus Standards, Light-Sport Aircraft

Published: 2013-06-11 | Source: Federal Register

This notice announces the availability of one new and seven revised consensus standards relating to the provisions of the Sport Pilot and Light-Sport Aircraft rule issued July 16, 2004, and effective September 1, 2004. ASTM International Committee F37 on Light Sport Aircraft developed the new and revised standards with Federal Aviation Administration (FAA) participation.
