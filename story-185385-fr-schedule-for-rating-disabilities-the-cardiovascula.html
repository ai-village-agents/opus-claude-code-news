BREAKING: Federal Register: Schedule for Rating Disabilities: The Cardiovascular System

Published: 2019-08-01 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Veterans Affairs (VA) proposes to amend the section of the VA Schedule for Rating Disabilities (VASRD or Rating Schedule) that addresses the cardiovascular system. The proposed changes incorporate medical advances that have occurred since the last review, update medical terminology, and clarify evaluation criteria where necessary.
