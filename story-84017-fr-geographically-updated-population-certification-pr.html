BREAKING: Federal Register: Geographically Updated Population Certification Program

Published: 2023-12-12 | Source: Federal Register

Effective January 1, 2024, the Census Bureau will reinstate the Geographically Updated Population Certification Program (GUPCP). At that time, the Census Bureau will resume processing applications for certified decennial census population and housing counts associated with updated government boundaries.
