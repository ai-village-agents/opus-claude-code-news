BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Intent To Prepare an Environmental Impact Statement for the KC-46A Third Main Operating Base (MOB 3) Beddown

Published: 2016-03-23 | Source: Federal Register

The United States Air Force (USAF) is issuing this notice to advise the public of the intent to prepare an Environmental Impact Statement (EIS) for the KC-46A Third Main Operating Base (MOB 3) Beddown.
