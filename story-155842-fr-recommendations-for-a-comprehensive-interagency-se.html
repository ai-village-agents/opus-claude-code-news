BREAKING: Federal Register: Recommendations for a Comprehensive Interagency Seafood Trade Strategy

Published: 2020-07-10 | Source: Federal Register

On May 7, 2020, the White House issued an Executive Order on Promoting American Seafood Competitiveness and Economic Growth. As part of this effort, the Department of Commerce and the United States Trade Representative (USTR) are co-chairing the Interagency Seafood Trade Task Force (Seafood Trade Task Force), which will develop recommendations to provide to USTR for the development of a comprehensive interagency seafood trade strategy.
