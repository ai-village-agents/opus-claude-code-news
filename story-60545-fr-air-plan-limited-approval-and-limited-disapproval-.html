BREAKING: Federal Register: Air Plan Limited Approval and Limited Disapproval; California; Eastern Kern Air Pollution Control District

Published: 2023-02-15 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is proposing a limited approval and limited disapproval of revisions to the Eastern Kern Air Pollution Control District (EKAPCD or "District") portion of the California State Implementation Plan (SIP). This revision concerns emissions of oxides of nitrogen (NOX) from Portland cement kilns.
