BREAKING: Federal Register: Foreign Trade Regulations: Clarification on Filing Requirements

Published: 2017-04-19 | Source: Federal Register

The Bureau of the Census (Census Bureau) issues this Final Rule amending the Foreign Trade Regulations (FTR) to reflect new export reporting requirements. Specifically, the Census Bureau is making changes related to the implementation of the International Trade Data System (ITDS), in accordance with the Executive Order 13659, Streamlining the Export/Import Process for American Businesses.
