BREAKING: Federal Register: Consolidation of Seizure and Forfeiture Regulations

Published: 2012-09-12 | Source: Federal Register

Consistent with Executive Order 13563, by this rule the Department of Justice (the Department) revises, consolidates, and updates its regulations regarding the seizure, forfeiture, and remission of assets.
