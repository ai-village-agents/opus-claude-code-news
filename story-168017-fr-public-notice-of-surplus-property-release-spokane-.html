BREAKING: Federal Register: Public Notice of Surplus Property Release; Spokane International Airport, Spokane, Washington

Published: 2020-12-11 | Source: Federal Register

Notice is being given that the FAA is considering a request from the City of Spokane, Washington and the County of Spokane, Washington, to waive the surplus property requirements for approximately 1.07 acres of airport property located at Spokane International Airport, in Spokane, Washington. The subject property is located away from the aeronautical area and currently vacant.
