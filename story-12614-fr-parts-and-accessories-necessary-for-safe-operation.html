BREAKING: Federal Register: Parts and Accessories Necessary for Safe Operation; Fuel Tank Overfill Restriction

Published: 2025-05-30 | Source: Federal Register

FMCSA proposes to remove the requirement in the Federal Motor Carrier Safety Regulations (FMCSRs) that a liquid fuel tank manufactured on or after January 1, 1973, be designed and constructed so that it cannot be filled, in a normal filling operation, with a quantity of fuel that exceeds 95 percent of the tank's liquid capacity.
