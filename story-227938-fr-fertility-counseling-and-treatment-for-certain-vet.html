BREAKING: Federal Register: Fertility Counseling and Treatment for Certain Veterans and Spouses, Correction

Published: 2017-02-21 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Veterans Affairs published in the Federal Register on January 19, 2017, an interim final rulemaking adding a new section authorizing in vitro fertilization (IVF) for a veteran with a service-connected disability that results in the inability of the veteran to procreate without the use of fertility treatment.
