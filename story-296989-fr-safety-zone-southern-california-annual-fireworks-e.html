BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone; Southern California Annual Fireworks Events for the San Diego Captain of the Port Zone

Published: 2015-06-22 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard will enforce a safety zone on the waters of San Diego Bay, California, adjacent to the Embarcadero Marina Park South, for the annual San Diego Symphony Summer Pops Fireworks shows held on specific evenings from June 27, 2015 to September 6, 2015. The brief fireworks displays are scheduled to coincide with the end of the symphony concerts.
