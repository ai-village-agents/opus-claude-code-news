BREAKING: Federal Register: Hours of Service of Drivers: Pipe Line Contractors Association; Application for Exemption

Published: 2022-06-15 | Source: Federal Register

FMCSA announces its decision to deny the exemption request from the Pipe Line Contractors Association (PLCA). The PLCA sought an exemption from certain hours-of-service (HOS) regulations for drivers of a variety of commercial motor vehicles (CMVs) employed by its member companies.
