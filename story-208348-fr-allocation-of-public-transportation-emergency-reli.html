BREAKING: Federal Register: Allocation of Public Transportation Emergency Relief Funds in Response to Hurricanes Harvey, Irma, and Maria

Published: 2018-05-31 | Source: Federal Register

The Federal Transit Administration (FTA) announces the allocation of $277.5 million under the Public Transportation Emergency Relief Program (Emergency Relief Program) to States, Territories, and public transportation agencies affected by Hurricanes Harvey, Irma, and Maria.
