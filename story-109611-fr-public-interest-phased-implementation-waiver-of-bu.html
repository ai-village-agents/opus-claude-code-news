BREAKING: Federal Register: Public Interest Phased Implementation Waiver of Build America, Buy America Provisions as Applied to Recipients of HUD Federal Financial Assistance

Published: 2022-11-09 | Source: Federal Register

In accordance with the Build America, Buy America Act ("BABA" or "the Act") this notice advises that HUD is proposing a public interest waiver to further HUD's phased implementation of the Buy America Domestic Content Procurement Preference ("Buy America Preference," or "BAP") for recipients of Federal Financial Assistance ("FFA") provided by HUD.
