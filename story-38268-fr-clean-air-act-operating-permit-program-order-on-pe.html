BREAKING: Federal Register: Clean Air Act Operating Permit Program; Order on Petition for Objection to State Operating Permit for DCP Operating Company L.P. (Mobile County, Alabama)

Published: 2024-05-31 | Source: Federal Register

The EPA Administrator signed an order dated May 10, 2024, granting in part and denying in part the petition dated January 8, 2024, from Mobile Environmental Justice Action Coalition and GASP. The petition requested that EPA object to Clean Air Act (CAA) title V operating permit issued by the Alabama Department of Environmental Management (ADEM) to DCP Operating Company L.P. for its natural gas treatment and processing facility located in Mobile Bay, Mobile County, Alabama.
