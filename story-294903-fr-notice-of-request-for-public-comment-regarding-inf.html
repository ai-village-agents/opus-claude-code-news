BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Request for Public Comment Regarding Information Sharing and Analysis Organizations

Published: 2015-05-27 | Source: Federal Register

This Notice announces a public comment period to allow input from the public on the formation of Information Sharing and Analysis Organizations (ISAOs) for cybersecurity information sharing, as directed by Executive Order 13691. DHS is soliciting public comments and questions from all citizens and organizations related to the provisions of E.O. 13691 "Promoting Private Sector Cybersecurity Information Sharing" of February 13, 2015.
