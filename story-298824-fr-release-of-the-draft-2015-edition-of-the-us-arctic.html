BREAKING: Federal Register: Release of the Draft 2015 Edition of the U.S. Arctic Nautical Charting Plan

Published: 2015-07-14 | Source: Federal Register

The NOAA Office of Coast Survey has released a draft of the 2015 edition of the U.S. Arctic Nautical Charting Plan. The plan provides information about three topics: NOAA electronic navigational chart (NOAA ENC[supreg]) coverage in U.S. Arctic waters, progress on publishing new Arctic charts, and specifications for eleven proposed new charts.
