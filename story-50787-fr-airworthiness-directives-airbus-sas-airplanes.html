BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Airbus S.A.S Airplanes

Published: 2024-10-24 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA is adopting a new airworthiness directive (AD) for all Airbus SAS Model A350-941 and -1041 airplanes. This AD was prompted by reports that engine nacelle anti-icing (NAI) forward bulkheads have been found with elongated locating holes.
