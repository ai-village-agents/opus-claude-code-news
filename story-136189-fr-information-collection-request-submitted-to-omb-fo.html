BREAKING: Federal Register: Information Collection Request Submitted to OMB for Review and Approval; Comment Request; TSCA Mercury Inventory Reporting (Renewal)

Published: 2021-10-22 | Source: Federal Register

This is a proposed extension of the ICR, which is currently approved through October 31, 2021.
