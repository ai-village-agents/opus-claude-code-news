BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zones; Recurring Events in Captain of the Port Duluth Zone

Published: 2016-04-19 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard proposes to amend its safety zones regulations for annual events in the Captain of the Port Duluth Zone. This proposed rule would update the locations for two safety zones, add three safety zones, and modify the format of the regulations to list the annual events and corresponding safety zones in table form.
