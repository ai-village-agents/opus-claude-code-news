BREAKING: Federal Register: Colorado Disaster Number CO-00046

Published: 2012-08-20 | Source: Federal Register

This is an amendment of the Administrative declaration of a major disaster for the State of Colorado, effective 08/08/2012. Incident: Wildfires in El Paso and Larimer Counties and Subsequent Flooding and Mudslides. Incident Period: 06/09/2012 through 07/11/2012. Effective Date: 08/08/2012. Physical Loan Application Deadline Date: 10/09/2012. EIDL Loan Application Deadline Date: 05/07/2013.
