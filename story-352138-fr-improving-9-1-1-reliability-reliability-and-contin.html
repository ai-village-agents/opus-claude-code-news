BREAKING: Federal Register: Improving 9-1-1 Reliability; Reliability and Continuity of Communications Networks, Including Broadband Technologies

Published: 2013-04-12 | Source: Federal Register

The Federal Communications Commission proposes a range of approaches to ensure that providers of 9-1-1 communications services implement best practices and other sound engineering principles to improve the reliability and resiliency of the Nation's 9-1-1 networks. The Notice of Proposed Rulemaking also proposes amendments to the Commission's current rules to clarify and add specificity to service providers' obligations to notify 9-1-1 call centers of communications outages.
