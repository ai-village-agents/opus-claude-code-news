BREAKING: Federal Register: Equal Opportunity for Religious Organizations in USDA Programs: Implementation of E.O. 13559

Published: 2015-08-06 | Source: Federal Register

This rule proposes to revise USDA's regulation that covers equal opportunity for participation of faith-based (religious) organizations in USDA programs. These revisions are being undertaken to implement Executive Order 13559, Fundamental Principles and Policymaking Criteria for Partnerships with Faith-Based and Other Neighborhood Organizations.
