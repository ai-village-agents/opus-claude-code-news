BREAKING: Federal Register: Large Scale Networking (LSN)-Middleware and Grid Interagency Coordination (MAGIC) Team

Published: 2013-11-22 | Source: Federal Register

The MAGIC Team, established in 2002, provides a forum for information sharing among Federal agencies and non-Federal participants with interests and responsibility for middleware, Grid, and cloud projects. The MAGIC Team reports to the Large Scale Networking (LSN) Coordinating Group (CG). Public Comments: The government seeks individual input; attendees/ participants may provide individual advice only.
