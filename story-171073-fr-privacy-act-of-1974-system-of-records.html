BREAKING: Federal Register: Privacy Act of 1974; System of Records

Published: 2019-02-08 | Source: Federal Register

Pursuant to the provisions of the Privacy Act of 1974, notice is given that the Board of Governors of the Federal Reserve System (Board) proposes the establishment of a new system of records, BGFRS-42 "FRB--General File of the Insurance Policy Advisory Committee." This system will store information relevant for the selection of individuals for membership on the Insurance Policy Advisory Commission (IPAC).
