BREAKING: Federal Register: Announcement of Competition under the America COMPETES, Reauthorization Act of 2010

Published: 2015-11-30 | Source: Federal Register

The goal of the prize competition (also described as "the hackathon") is to produce an open source electronic school meal application that States and school districts can adapt for their own use. FNS hopes to develop a prototype that incorporates the best ideas from the innovation community at large. The application will contain a minimum FNS-defined package of design features that target applicant error and reduce applicant burden.
