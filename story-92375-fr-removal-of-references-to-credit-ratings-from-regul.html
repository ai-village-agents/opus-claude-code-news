BREAKING: Federal Register: Removal of References to Credit Ratings From Regulation M

Published: 2022-03-30 | Source: Federal Register

The Securities and Exchange Commission ("Commission") is re- proposing amendments to remove the references to credit ratings included in certain Commission rules. The Dodd-Frank Wall Street Reform and Consumer Protection Act ("Dodd-Frank Act"), among other things, requires the Commission to remove any references to credit ratings from its regulations.
