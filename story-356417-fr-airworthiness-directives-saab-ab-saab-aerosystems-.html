BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Saab AB, Saab Aerosystems Airplanes

Published: 2013-06-03 | Source: Federal Register

We propose to adopt a new airworthiness directive (AD) for all Saab AB, Saab Aerosystems Model 340B airplanes. This proposed AD was prompted by a report that the elevator position quoted in an aircraft maintenance manual is incorrect for Saab 340B airplane. This proposed AD would require an inspection of the stick pusher rigging and an adjustment to the correct setting if necessary.
