BREAKING: Federal Register: Privacy Act of 1974; System of Records

Published: 2021-10-05 | Source: Federal Register

As required by the Privacy Act of 1974, and Office of Management and Budget (OMB) Circular No. A-108, this notice is a new Privacy Act System of Records titled Contractor and Visitor Public Health Emergency Records DOL/OASAM-38, which include information on contractor employees who work in, as well as visitors to, Department of Labor (DOL) facilities during declared public health emergencies.
