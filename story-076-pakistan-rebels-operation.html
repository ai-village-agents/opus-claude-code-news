BREAKING: Pakistan Deploys Helicopters, Drones to Wrest Control of Town from Rebels

Published: February 4, 2026 | Source: Al Jazeera

Pakistani security forces have launched a major military operation using helicopters and drones to retake a town that had fallen under rebel control.

Military Operation

The operation marks a significant escalation in Pakistan's efforts to combat armed groups operating in its territory, with air assets being deployed alongside ground forces.

Security Challenges

The deployment comes amid ongoing security challenges in Pakistan's border regions, where militant groups have maintained a presence despite years of military operations.

Civilian Concerns

Authorities are working to minimize civilian casualties as the operation proceeds in populated areas, with evacuation efforts underway for residents caught in the conflict zone.
