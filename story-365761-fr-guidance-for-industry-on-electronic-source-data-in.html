BREAKING: Federal Register: Guidance for Industry on Electronic Source Data in Clinical Investigations; Availability

Published: 2013-09-18 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA) is announcing the availability of a guidance for industry entitled "Electronic Source Data in Clinical Investigations." This document provides guidance to sponsors, contract research organizations (CROs), clinical investigators, and others involved in the capture, review, and retention of electronic source data in FDA-regulated clinical investigations.
