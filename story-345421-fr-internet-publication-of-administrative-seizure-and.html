BREAKING: Federal Register: Internet Publication of Administrative Seizure and Forfeiture Notices

Published: 2013-01-29 | Source: Federal Register

This final rule adopts, with one change, a notice of proposed rulemaking (NPRM) published in the Federal Register on February 8, 2012, that proposed to allow for publication of notices of seizure and intent to forfeit on an official U.S. Government forfeiture Web site.
