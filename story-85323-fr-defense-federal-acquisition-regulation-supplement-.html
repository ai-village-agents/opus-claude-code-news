BREAKING: Federal Register: Defense Federal Acquisition Regulation Supplement: Inapplicability of Additional Defense-Unique Laws and Certain Non-Statutory DFARS Clauses to Commercial Item Contracts (DFARS Case 2018-D074); Extension of Comment Period

Published: 2023-12-27 | Source: Federal Register
