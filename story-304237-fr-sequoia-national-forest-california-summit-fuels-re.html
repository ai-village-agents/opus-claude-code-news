BREAKING: Federal Register: Sequoia National Forest, California; Summit Fuels Reduction and Forest Health Project

Published: 2015-09-16 | Source: Federal Register

The Sequoia National Forest (SQF) is planning to prepare an environmental impact statement (EIS) to document and publicly disclose the environmental effects of implementing a fuels reduction and forest health project within a 10,600 acre project analysis area. The Summit Fuels Reduction and Forest Health Project (Summit Project) will encourage a healthy and diverse forest ecosystem that is more resilient to the effects of wildfire, drought, disease, and other disturbances.
