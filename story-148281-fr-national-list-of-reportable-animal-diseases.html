BREAKING: Federal Register: National List of Reportable Animal Diseases

Published: 2020-04-02 | Source: Federal Register

We are proposing to amend the animal disease regulations to provide for a National List of Reportable Animal Diseases, along with reporting responsibilities for animal health professionals that encounter or suspect cases of communicable animal diseases and disease agents. These proposed changes are necessary to streamline State and Federal cooperative animal disease detection, response, and control efforts.
