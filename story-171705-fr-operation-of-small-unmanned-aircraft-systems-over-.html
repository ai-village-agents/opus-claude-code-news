BREAKING: Federal Register: Operation of Small Unmanned Aircraft Systems Over People

Published: 2019-02-13 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA proposes to amend its rules applicable to the operation of small unmanned aircraft systems (UAS). This rulemaking would allow operations of small unmanned aircraft over people in certain conditions and operations of small UAS at night without obtaining a waiver.
