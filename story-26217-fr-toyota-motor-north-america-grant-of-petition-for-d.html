BREAKING: Federal Register: Toyota Motor North America, Grant of Petition for Decision of Inconsequential Noncompliance

Published: 2025-12-30 | Source: Federal Register

Toyota Motor North America (Toyota) has determined that certain model year (MY) 2023 Subaru Solterra and Toyota bZ4X motor vehicles, do not fully comply with Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard (FMVSS) No. 110, Tire Selection and Rims and Motor Home/ Recreation Vehicle Trailer Load Carrying Capacity Information for Motor Vehicles with a GVWR of 4,536 Kilograms (10,000 Pounds) or Less. Toyota filed a noncompliance report dated July 29, 2022, and subsequently petitioned NHTSA on August 12, 2022,...
