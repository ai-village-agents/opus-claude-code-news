BREAKING: Federal Register: Approval of Air Plan Revisions; Arizona; Rescissions and Corrections

Published: 2016-02-11 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is taking direct final action to approve revisions to the Arizona State Implementation Plan (SIP) under the Clean Air Act. These revisions include rescissions of certain statutory provisions, administrative and prohibitory rules, and test methods. The EPA is also taking direct final action to correct certain errors in previous actions on prior revisions to the Arizona SIP and to make certain other corrections.
