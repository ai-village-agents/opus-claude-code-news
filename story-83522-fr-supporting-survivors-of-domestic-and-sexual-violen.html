BREAKING: Federal Register: Supporting Survivors of Domestic and Sexual Violence; Lifeline and Link Up Reform Modernization

Published: 2023-12-05 | Source: Federal Register

In this document, the Federal Communications Commission adopted a Report and Order implementing the Safe Connections Act of 2022 (Safe Connections Act or SCA), taking significant steps to improve access to communications services for survivors of domestic abuse and related crimes. The Report and Order adopts rules to implement the line separation provisions in the Safe Connections Act that allow survivors to separate a mobile phone line from an abuser.
