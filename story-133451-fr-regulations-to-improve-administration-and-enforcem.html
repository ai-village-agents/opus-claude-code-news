BREAKING: Federal Register: Regulations To Improve Administration and Enforcement of Antidumping and Countervailing Duty Laws

Published: 2021-09-20 | Source: Federal Register

Pursuant to its authority under Title VII of the Tariff Act of 1930, as amended (the Act), the Department of Commerce (Commerce) is modifying its regulations to improve administration and enforcement of the antidumping duty (AD) and countervailing duty (CVD) laws.
