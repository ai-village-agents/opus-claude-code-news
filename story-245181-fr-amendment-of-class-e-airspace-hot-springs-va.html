BREAKING: Federal Register: Amendment of Class E Airspace; Hot Springs, VA

Published: 2017-10-02 | Source: Federal Register

This action amends Class E airspace extending upward from 700 feet or more above the surface at Hot Springs, VA, by adding controlled airspace for Bath Community Hospital Heliport to the Ingalls Field Airport airspace designation. Controlled airspace is necessary for the safety and management of instrument flight rules (IFR) operations at the heliport.
