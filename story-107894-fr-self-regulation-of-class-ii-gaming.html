BREAKING: Federal Register: Self-Regulation of Class II Gaming

Published: 2022-10-18 | Source: Federal Register

The National Indian Gaming Commission (NIGC) is amending its regulations regarding self-regulation of Class II gaming under the Indian Gaming Regulatory Act. The amendment revises the regulations to address an ambiguity in the petitioning process and clarifies the Office of Self-Regulation's (OSR) role once the Commission issues a certificate.
