BREAKING: Federal Register: Bulletin 2022-06: Unfair Returned Deposited Item Fee Assessment Practices

Published: 2022-11-07 | Source: Federal Register

A Returned Deposited Item is a check that a consumer deposits into their checking account that is returned to the consumer because the check could not be processed against the check originator's account. Blanket policies of charging Returned Deposited Item fees to consumers for all returned transactions irrespective of the circumstances or patterns of behavior on the account are likely unfair under the Consumer Financial Protection Act (CFPA).
