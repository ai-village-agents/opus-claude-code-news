BREAKING: Federal Register: Microbiology Devices; Reclassification of Antigen, Antibody, and Nucleic Acid-Based Hepatitis B Virus Assay Devices

Published: 2024-09-25 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA, the Agency, or we) is proposing to reclassify qualitative hepatitis B virus (HBV) antigen assays, qualitative HBV antibody assays and quantitative assays that detect anti-HBs (antibodies to HBV surface antigen (HBsAg)), and quantitative HBV nucleic acid-based assays, all of which are postamendments class III devices, into class II (general controls and special controls), subject to premarket notification.
