BREAKING: Federal Register: Special Local Regulation; San Diego Crew Classic; Mission Bay, San Diego, CA

Published: 2015-02-12 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is temporarily changing the date of the special local regulation on the navigable waters of Mission Bay, San Diego, California in support of the annual San Diego Crew Classic rowing race. This temporary interim rule adjusts the dates for the established special local regulations.
