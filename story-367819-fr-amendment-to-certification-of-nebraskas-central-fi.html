BREAKING: Federal Register: Amendment to Certification of Nebraska's Central Filing System

Published: 2013-10-23 | Source: Federal Register

In response to a request from Nebraska's Secretary of State we are approving amendments to the debtor identification and signature requirements of the certified central filing system for Nebraska to permit the conversion of all debtor social security and taxpayer identification numbers into approved unique identifiers.
