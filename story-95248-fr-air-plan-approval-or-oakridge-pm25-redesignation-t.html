BREAKING: Federal Register: Air Plan Approval; OR; Oakridge PM2.5 Redesignation to Attainment and Maintenance Plan

Published: 2022-05-05 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) proposes to redesignate the Oakridge, Oregon nonattainment area (Oakridge NAA or Oakridge area) to attainment for the 2006 24-hour fine particulate matter (PM2.5) National Ambient Air Quality Standard (NAAQS).
