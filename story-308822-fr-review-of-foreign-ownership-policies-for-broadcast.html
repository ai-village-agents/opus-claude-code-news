BREAKING: Federal Register: Review of Foreign Ownership Policies for Broadcast, Common Carrier and Aeronautical Radio Licensees

Published: 2015-11-06 | Source: Federal Register

In this document, the Federal Communications Commission (Commission) proposes to extend its foreign ownership rules and procedures that apply to common carrier licensees to broadcast licensees, with certain modifications to tailor them to the broadcast context.
