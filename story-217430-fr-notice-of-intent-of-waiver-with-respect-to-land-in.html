BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Intent of Waiver With Respect to Land; Indianapolis International Airport, Indianapolis, Indiana

Published: 2018-09-28 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA is considering a proposal to change 97.507 acres of airport land from aeronautical use to non-aeronautical use and to authorize the sale of airport property located at Indianapolis International Airport, Indianapolis, Indiana. The aforementioned land is not needed for aeronautical use. The land consists of 155 original airport acquired parcels.
