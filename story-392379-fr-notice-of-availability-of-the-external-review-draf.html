BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Availability of the External Review Draft of Framework for Human Health Risk Assessment To Inform Decision Making

Published: 2012-07-30 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) Office of the Science Advisor (OSA) announces a 60-day public comment period for the external review draft of "A Framework for Human Health Risk Assessment to Inform Decision Making." This document was developed as part of an agencywide program by the EPA Risk Assessment Forum.
