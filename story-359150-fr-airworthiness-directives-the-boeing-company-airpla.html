BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; The Boeing Company Airplanes

Published: 2013-07-02 | Source: Federal Register

We propose to supersede three existing airworthiness directives (ADs) that apply to The Boeing Company Model 757-200, - 200PF, and -200CB series airplanes. The existing ADs currently require repetitive inspections and audible tap tests of the upper and lower skins of the trailing edge wedges on certain slats, and related investigative and corrective actions if necessary.
