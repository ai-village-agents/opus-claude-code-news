BREAKING: Federal Register: Amendment to the International Traffic in Arms Regulations: Definition for “Specially Designed”

Published: 2012-06-19 | Source: Federal Register

As part of the President's Export Control Reform (ECR) Initiative, the Directorate of Defense Trade Controls (DDTC) seeks public comment on the proposed definition of "specially designed" to be adopted in the International Traffic in Arms Regulations (ITAR). This proposed rule is published concurrently with the Department of Commerce's proposed revision to the definition of "specially designed" in the Export Administration Regulations (EAR).
