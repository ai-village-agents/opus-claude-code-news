BREAKING: Federal Register: Special Local Regulation; Oak Harbor, WA

Published: 2025-06-17 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is proposing to establish a special local regulation for navigable waters within Oak Harbor Bay, Oak Harbor, WA. The regulated area shall be closed immediately prior to, during and immediately after the annual event to all persons and vessels not participating in the event and authorized by the event sponsor.
