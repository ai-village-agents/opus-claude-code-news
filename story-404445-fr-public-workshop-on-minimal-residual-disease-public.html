BREAKING: Federal Register: Public Workshop on Minimal Residual Disease; Public Workshop

Published: 2012-12-26 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA), in cosponsorship with the American Society of Clinical Oncology, is announcing a public workshop that will provide a forum for discussion of extending the qualification of minimal residual disease (MRD) detection as a prognostic biomarker to an efficacy/response biomarker in evaluating new drugs for the treatment of acute myeloid leukemia (AML).
