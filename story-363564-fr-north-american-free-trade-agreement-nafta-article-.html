BREAKING: Federal Register: North American Free-Trade Agreement (NAFTA), Article 1904 Binational Panel Reviews

Published: 2013-08-21 | Source: Federal Register

On August 6, 2013, the NAFTA Chapter 19 binational panel issued its decision in the review of the final results of the 2008-2009 antidumping administrative review made by the U.S. Department of Commerce, with respect to Light-Walled Rectangular Pipe and Tube from Mexico, NAFTA Secretariat File Number USA-MEX-2011-1904-02.
