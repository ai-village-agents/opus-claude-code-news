BREAKING: Federal Register: Proposed Amendment of the Class E Airspace; Corsicana, TX: Withdrawal

Published: 2022-06-13 | Source: Federal Register

This action withdraws the NPRM published in the Federal Register on May 27, 2022, proposing to amend the Class E airspace at Corsicana, TX. The FAA has determined that withdrawal of the NPRM is warranted as the action has already been completed by a final rule published in the Federal Register on February 25, 2022.
