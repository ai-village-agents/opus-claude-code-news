BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Honda Aircraft Company LLC

Published: 2018-06-07 | Source: Federal Register

We propose to supersede Airworthiness Directive (AD) 2018-11- 05, which applies to certain Honda Aircraft Company (Honda) LLC Model HA-420 airplanes. AD 2018-11-05 requires incorporating a temporary revision into the airplane flight manual and replacing the faulty power brake valve (PBV) upon condition.
