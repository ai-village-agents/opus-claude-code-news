BREAKING: Federal Register: National Oil and Hazardous Substances Pollution Contingency Plan; National Priorities List

Published: 2014-05-02 | Source: Federal Register

On March 12, 2014, EPA published a Notice of Intent to Delete (79 FR 13967) and a direct final Notice of Deletion (79 FR 13882) for the O'Connor Superfund Site from the National Priorities List. The EPA is withdrawing the Final Notice of Deletion due to adverse comments that were received during the public comment period.
