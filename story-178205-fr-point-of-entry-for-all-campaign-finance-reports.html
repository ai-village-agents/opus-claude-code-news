BREAKING: Federal Register: Point of Entry for All Campaign Finance Reports

Published: 2019-05-02 | Source: Federal Register

Congress amended the Federal Election Campaign Act ("FECA") to require all reports, designations, and notices mandated by FECA to be filed with the Federal Election Commission. Previously, Senate candidates and certain political committees were required to file such reports, designations, and notices with the Secretary of the Senate.
