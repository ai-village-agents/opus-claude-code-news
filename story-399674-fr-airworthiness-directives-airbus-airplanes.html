BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Airbus Airplanes

Published: 2012-10-23 | Source: Federal Register

We propose to adopt a new airworthiness directive (AD) for all Airbus Model A318, A319, and A320 series airplanes. This proposed AD was prompted by fuel system reviews conducted by the manufacturer, which revealed that certain fuel pumps under certain conditions can create an ignition source in the fuel tank.
