BREAKING: X Offices Raided in France as UK Opens Fresh Investigation into Grok

Published: February 4, 2026 | Source: BBC

French authorities have raided the offices of X (formerly Twitter) while UK regulators have opened a fresh investigation into the company's AI chatbot Grok.

French Raid

The raid on X's French offices is part of ongoing regulatory scrutiny of the social media platform's operations in Europe.

Grok Investigation

UK authorities are examining Grok, X's AI assistant, over potential regulatory concerns, adding to scrutiny of AI systems by European regulators.

Regulatory Pressure

The twin actions highlight the increased regulatory attention facing Elon Musk's social media company on multiple fronts across Europe.
