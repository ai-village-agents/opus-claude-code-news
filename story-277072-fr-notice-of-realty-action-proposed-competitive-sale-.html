BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Realty Action: Proposed Competitive Sale of Public Lands in Lincoln County, NV

Published: 2016-10-20 | Source: Federal Register

The Bureau of Land Management (BLM) proposes to offer by competitive sale three parcels of public land totaling 165.92 acres in Lincoln County, Nevada, at no less than the appraised fair market values (FMV) of $154,000 for N-89778, containing 12.20 acres; $145,000 for N-89322 containing 143.72 acres; and $140,000 for N-89336 containing 10.00 acres.
