BREAKING: Federal Register: Copyright Restoration of Works in Accordance with the Uruguay Round Agreements Act

Published: 2019-02-04 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Copyright Office is publishing a list of works for which it has received and processed a Notice of Intent to Enforce copyrights restored under the Uruguay Round Agreements Act. The Office is required by law to publish such lists to provide a public record of works in which copyrights have been restored and whose owners have notified the Office of their intent to enforce those copyrights.
