BREAKING: Federal Register: Schedules of Controlled Substances: Placement of AH-7921 Into Schedule I

Published: 2016-04-14 | Source: Federal Register

With the issuance of this final order, the Administrator of the Drug Enforcement Administration places the substance AH-7921 (Systematic IUPAC Name: 3,4-dichloro-N- [(1dimethylamino)cyclohexylmethyl]benzamide), including its isomers, esters, ethers, salts, and salts of isomers, esters and ethers, into schedule I of the Controlled Substances Act.
