BREAKING: Federal Register: Customs Broker Permit User Fee Payment for 2023

Published: 2022-12-16 | Source: Federal Register

This document provides notice to customs brokers that the annual user fee that is assessed for each permit held by a broker, whether it may be an individual, partnership, association, or corporation, is due no later than February 24, 2023.
