BREAKING: Federal Register: Improved Identification Techniques Against Alkali-Silica Reaction Concrete Degradation at Nuclear Power Plants

Published: 2015-01-12 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Nuclear Regulatory Commission (NRC) has received a petition for rulemaking (PRM) from Sandra Gavutis on behalf of C-10 Research and Education Foundation (C-10 or the petitioner), dated September 25, 2014, requesting that the NRC amend its regulations to provide improved identification techniques against Alkali-Silica Reaction (ASR) concrete degradation at nuclear power plants.
