BREAKING: Federal Register: Reopening of Comment Period for Reporting of Securities Loans

Published: 2022-03-02 | Source: Federal Register

On November 18, 2021, the Securities and Exchange Commission issued for comment a proposed rule under the Securities Exchange Act of 1934 ("Exchange Act") in Release No. 34-93613 (Nov. 18, 2021), 86 FR 69802 (Dec. 8, 2021) regarding the reporting of securities loans. The Commission is reopening the comment period for the proposed rule in light of the proposed Exchange Act rule regarding short sale disclosure.
