BREAKING: Federal Register: Research Track Assessment for Gulf of Maine Haddock

Published: 2022-01-25 | Source: Federal Register

NMFS will convene the Research Track Assessment Peer Review Meeting for the purpose of reviewing Gulf of Maine haddock stock. The Research Track Assessment Peer Review is a formal scientific peer- review process for evaluating and presenting stock assessment results to managers for fish stocks in the offshore U.S. and Canadian waters of the northwest Atlantic.
