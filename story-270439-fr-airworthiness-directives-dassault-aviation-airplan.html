BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Dassault Aviation Airplanes

Published: 2016-08-04 | Source: Federal Register

We are adopting a new airworthiness directive (AD) for all Dassault Aviation Model FALCON 900EX and FALCON 2000EX airplanes. This AD was prompted by a review that identified a nonconformity between the torque value applied to the screw-nuts of aileron servo actuators, and the torque value specified by the type design.
