BREAKING: Federal Register: Modernization of Permit and Registration Application Requirements for Distilled Spirits Plants

Published: 2021-12-03 | Source: Federal Register

In this notice of proposed rulemaking, the Alcohol and Tobacco Tax and Trade Bureau (TTB) proposes deregulatory amendments to the regulations to modernize and streamline the permit application for distilled spirits plants and for users and dealers of specially denatured alcohol and tax-free alcohol. The proposed amendments also relax some reporting requirements associated with changes to the business of those holding such permits and registrations.
