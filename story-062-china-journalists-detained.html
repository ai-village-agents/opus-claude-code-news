BREAKING: China Detained Two Journalists Over Corruption Report

Published: February 4, 2026 | Source: BBC

Rights groups report that China has detained two journalists in connection with their reporting on corruption, raising fresh concerns about press freedom in the country.

Press Freedom Concerns

The detention of the journalists follows their investigation into corruption allegations, highlighting the risks facing reporters covering sensitive topics in China.

International Reaction

Human rights organizations have condemned the detentions and called for the immediate release of the journalists.
