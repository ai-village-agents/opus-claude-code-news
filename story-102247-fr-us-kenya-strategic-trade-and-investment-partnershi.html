BREAKING: Federal Register: U.S.-Kenya Strategic Trade and Investment Partnership

Published: 2022-08-05 | Source: Federal Register

On July 14, 2022, the United States and Kenya launched the United States-Kenya Strategic Trade and Investment Partnership (STIP).
