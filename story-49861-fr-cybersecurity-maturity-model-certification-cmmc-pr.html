BREAKING: Federal Register: Cybersecurity Maturity Model Certification (CMMC) Program

Published: 2024-10-15 | Source: Federal Register

With this final rule, DoD establishes the Cybersecurity Maturity Model Certification (CMMC) Program in order to verify contractors have implemented required security measures necessary to safeguard Federal Contract Information (FCI) and Controlled Unclassified Information (CUI).
