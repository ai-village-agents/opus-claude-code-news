BREAKING: Federal Register: Amendments to Compliance Requirements for Commodity Pool Operators on Form CPO-PQR

Published: 2020-05-04 | Source: Federal Register

The Commodity Futures Trading Commission (CFTC or Commission) is proposing amendments to agency regulations on Commodity Pool Operators.
