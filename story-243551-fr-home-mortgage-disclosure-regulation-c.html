BREAKING: Federal Register: Home Mortgage Disclosure (Regulation C)

Published: 2017-09-13 | Source: Federal Register

The Bureau of Consumer Financial Protection (Bureau) is amending Regulation C to make technical corrections to and to clarify certain requirements adopted by the Bureau's Home Mortgage Disclosure (Regulation C) final rule (2015 HMDA Final Rule), which was published in the Federal Register on October 28, 2015.
