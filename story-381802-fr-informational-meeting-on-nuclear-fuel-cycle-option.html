BREAKING: Federal Register: Informational Meeting on Nuclear Fuel Cycle Options

Published: 2012-03-30 | Source: Federal Register

The Office of Fuel Cycle Technologies will be hosting a one- day informational meeting at the Argonne National Laboratory in Lemont, Illinois. At this meeting, input is being sought from participants knowledgeable in nuclear fuel cycles, including those from the public, universities, industry and national laboratories.
