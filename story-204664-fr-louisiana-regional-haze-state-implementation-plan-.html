BREAKING: Federal Register: Louisiana; Regional Haze State Implementation Plan; Petition for Reconsideration

Published: 2018-04-16 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is providing notice of its response to a petition for reconsideration of a rule published in the Federal Register on December 21, 2017 addressing Clean Air Act regional haze planning requirements for the State of Louisiana.
