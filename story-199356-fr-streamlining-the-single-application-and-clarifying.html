BREAKING: Federal Register: Streamlining the Single Application and Clarifying Eligibility Requirements

Published: 2018-02-06 | Source: Federal Register

On December 16, 2017, the U.S. Copyright Office released a new version of the Single Application, an online registration option that allows a single author to register a claim in one work that is solely owned by the same author and is not a work made for hire. The new Single Application includes enhanced features that should improve the user experience, increase the efficiency of the examination, and reduce the correspondence rate for these types of claims.
