BREAKING: Federal Register: Approval and Promulgation of State Plans for Designated Facilities; New York; Revision to Section 111(d) State Plan for MSW Landfills

Published: 2021-08-23 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is approving a revision to New York's section 111(d) state plan (the "State Plan") for Municipal Solid Waste (MSW) landfills, pursuant to the Clean Air Act ("CAA" or the "Act"). The State Plan revision consists of amendments to "Landfill Gas Collection and Control Systems for Certain Municipal Solid Waste Landfills," as well as attendant revisions to the "General Provisions."
