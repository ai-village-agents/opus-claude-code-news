BREAKING: Federal Register: Claims Procedure for Plans Providing Disability Benefits; Extension of Applicability Date

Published: 2017-10-12 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Labor proposes to delay for ninety (90) days--through April 1, 2018--the applicability of the Final Rule amending the claims procedure requirements applicable to ERISA-covered employee benefit plans that provide disability benefits. The Final Rule was published in the Federal Register on December 19, 2016, and became effective on January 18, 2017.
