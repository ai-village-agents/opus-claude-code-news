BREAKING: Federal Register: Conducting Clinical Trials With Decentralized Elements; Guidance for Industry, Investigators, and Other Interested Parties Availability

Published: 2024-09-18 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA or Agency) is announcing the availability of a final guidance for industry, investigators, and other interested parties entitled "Conducting Clinical Trials With Decentralized Elements." This guidance provides recommendations regarding the implementation of decentralized elements in clinical trials for drugs, biological products, and devices.
