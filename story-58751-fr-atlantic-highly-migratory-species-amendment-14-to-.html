BREAKING: Federal Register: Atlantic Highly Migratory Species; Amendment 14 to the 2006 Consolidated Atlantic Highly Migratory Species Fishery Management Plan

Published: 2023-01-24 | Source: Federal Register

NMFS announces the approval of Amendment 14 to the 2006 Consolidated Atlantic Highly Migratory Species (HMS) Fishery Management Plan (FMP). Amendment 14 revises the mechanism or "framework" used in establishing quotas and related management measures for Atlantic shark fisheries.
