BREAKING: Federal Register: Compounding and Repackaging of Radiopharmaceuticals by Outsourcing Facilities; Guidance for Industry; Availability

Published: 2018-09-26 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA or Agency) is announcing the availability of a final guidance for industry entitled "Compounding and Repackaging of Radiopharmaceuticals by Outsourcing Facilities." Specifically, this guidance sets forth FDA's policy regarding compounding and repackaging of radiopharmaceuticals for human use by entities that are registered with FDA as outsourcing facilities.
