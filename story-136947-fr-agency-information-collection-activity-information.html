BREAKING: Federal Register: Agency Information Collection Activity: Information Regarding Apportionment of Beneficiary's Award; Withdrawn

Published: 2021-11-02 | Source: Federal Register

On Wednesday, October 27, 2021, the Veterans Benefits Administration published a notice in the Federal Register announcing an opportunity for public comment on the proposed collection Information Regarding Apportionment of Beneficiary's Award (VA Form 21-0788). This notice was published in error; therefore, this document corrects that error by withdrawing this FR notice, document number 2021-23380.
