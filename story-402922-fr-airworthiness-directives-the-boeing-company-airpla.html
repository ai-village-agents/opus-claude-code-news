BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; The Boeing Company Airplanes

Published: 2012-12-05 | Source: Federal Register

We are adopting a new airworthiness directive (AD) for certain The Boeing Company Model 787-8 airplanes. This AD requires ensuring that lockwire is installed correctly on the engine fuel feed manifold couplings. This AD also requires inspecting the assembly of the engine fuel feed manifold rigid and full flexible couplings.
