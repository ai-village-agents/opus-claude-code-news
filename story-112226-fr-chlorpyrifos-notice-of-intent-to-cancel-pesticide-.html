BREAKING: Federal Register: Chlorpyrifos; Notice of Intent To Cancel Pesticide Registrations

Published: 2022-12-14 | Source: Federal Register

Pursuant to the Federal Insecticide, Fungicide, and Rodenticide Act (FIFRA), the Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) hereby announces its intent to cancel the registrations of three pesticide products containing the insecticide chlorpyrifos due to the Agency's revocation of all tolerances for chlorpyrifos.
