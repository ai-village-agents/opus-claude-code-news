BREAKING: Federal Register: American Indian and Alaska Native Consultation and Coordination Policy

Published: 2012-07-03 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Commerce (Department) requests public comments on a draft "Consultation and Coordination Policy of the U.S. Department of Commerce." This proposed policy establishes the manner in which the Department works with federally-recognized Indian tribes when developing Department policies that have tribal implications.
