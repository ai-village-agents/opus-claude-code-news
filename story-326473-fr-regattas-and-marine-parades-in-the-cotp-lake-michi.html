BREAKING: Federal Register: Regattas and Marine Parades in the COTP Lake Michigan Zone

Published: 2014-06-09 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard will enforce the special local regulation on the Black River for the Harborfest Dragon Boat Race in South Haven, Michigan. This regulated area will be enforced from 6 a.m. until 7 p.m. on June 21 and June 22, 2014. This action is necessary and intended to ensure safety of life and property on navigable waters immediately prior to, during, and immediately after the Dragon Boat race.
