BREAKING: Federal Register: Boundary Establishment for the Paint National Wild and Scenic River, Ottawa National Forest, Iron and Gogebic Counties, Michigan

Published: 2022-12-07 | Source: Federal Register

In accordance with Section 3(b) of the Wild and Scenic Rivers Act, the USDA Forest Service, Washington Office, is transmitting the final boundary of Paint Wild and Scenic River to Congress. The Paint Wild and Scenic River boundary description is available for review at: https://www.fs.usda.gov/activity/ottawa/recreation/wateractivities.
