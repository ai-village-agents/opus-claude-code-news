BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Filing of Self-Certification of Coal Capability Under the Powerplant and Industrial Fuel Use Act

Published: 2016-08-01 | Source: Federal Register

On July 18, 2016, Oregon Clean Energy, LLC, as owner and operator of a new combined cycle natural gas fired electric generating powerplant, submitted a coal capability self-certification to the Department of Energy (DOE) pursuant to Sec. 201(d) of the Powerplant and Industrial Fuel Use Act of 1978 (FUA), as amended, and DOE regulations in 10 CFR 501.60, 61.
