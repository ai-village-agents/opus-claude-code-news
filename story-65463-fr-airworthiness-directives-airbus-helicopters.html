BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Airbus Helicopters

Published: 2023-04-18 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA proposes to supersede Airworthiness Directive (AD) 2021-05-03, which applies to certain Airbus Helicopters Model EC225LP helicopters. AD 2021-05-03 requires various inspections of a certain part-numbered left-hand (LH) engine fuel supply (fuel supply) hose and depending on the inspection results, reinstalling or removing the fuel supply hose from service.
