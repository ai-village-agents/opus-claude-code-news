BREAKING: Federal Register: Action Subject to Intergovernmental Review Under Executive Order 12372

Published: 2015-07-01 | Source: Federal Register

The Small Business Administration (SBA) is notifying the public that it intends to fund grant applications for 22 existing Small Business Development Centers (SBDCs) beginning October 1, 2015 subject to the availability of funds. A description of the SBDC program is contained in the supplementary information below. The SBA is publishing this notice at least 90 days before the expected funding date.
