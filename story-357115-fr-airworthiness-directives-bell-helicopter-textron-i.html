BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Bell Helicopter Textron, Inc. (Bell), Model Helicopters

Published: 2013-06-11 | Source: Federal Register

This document proposes to supersede an existing airworthiness directive (AD) for the Bell Model 412, 412CF, and 412EP helicopters. The AD currently requires reidentifying each affected part-numbered main rotor yoke (yoke) on its data plate, reducing the retirement life of the reidentified yoke, and revising the Airworthiness Limitations section of the maintenance manual or the Instructions for Continued Airworthiness (ICAs) accordingly.
