BREAKING: Federal Register: Addition of Diisononyl Phthalate Category; Community Right-to-Know Toxic Chemical Release Reporting

Published: 2022-08-08 | Source: Federal Register

On September 5, 2000, in response to a petition filed under the Emergency Planning and Community Right-to-Know Act (EPCRA), EPA issued a proposed rule to add a diisononyl phthalate (DINP) category to the list of toxic chemicals subject to the reporting requirements under EPCRA and the Pollution Prevention Act (PPA).
