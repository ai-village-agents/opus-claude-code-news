BREAKING: Federal Register: Federal Property Management Regulations/Federal Management Regulation; Supply and Procurement

Published: 2015-02-24 | Source: Federal Register

GSA is proposing to amend the Federal Property Management Regulations (FPMR) and the Federal Management Regulation (FMR) by migrating regulations regarding the supply and procurement of Government personal property management from the FPMR to the FMR. The FPMR will contain a cross-reference to direct readers to the coverage in the FMR.
