BREAKING: Federal Register: Pears Grown in Oregon and Washington; Increased Assessment Rate for Processed Pears

Published: 2017-09-18 | Source: Federal Register

This proposed rule would implement a recommendation from the Processed Pear Committee (Committee) to increase the assessment rate established for the 2017-2018 and subsequent fiscal periods from $7.00 to $8.00 per ton of "summer/fall" pears for canning. The Committee locally administers the marketing order and is comprised of growers, handlers, and processors of processed pears grown in Oregon and Washington.
