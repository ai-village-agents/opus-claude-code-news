BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Airbus SAS Airplanes

Published: 2021-12-23 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA is adopting a new airworthiness directive (AD) for all Airbus SAS Model A350-941 and -1041 airplanes. This AD was prompted by reports of slat transmission jams caused by frozen slat geared rotary actuators (SGRAs) at slat 5 track 12.
