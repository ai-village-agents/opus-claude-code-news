BREAKING: Federal Register: Pilots' Medical Certificate Validity Period

Published: 2022-11-04 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is issuing this final rule to extend the maximum validity period of merchant mariner medical certificates issued to first-class pilots, and masters or mates serving as pilot, from 2 years to 5 years. We are issuing this rule in response to federal advisory committee recommendations and a petition for rulemaking.
