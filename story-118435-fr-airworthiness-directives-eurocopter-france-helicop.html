BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Eurocopter France Helicopters

Published: 2021-03-08 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA is withdrawing a notice of proposed rulemaking (NPRM) that proposed to adopt a new airworthiness directive (AD) that would have applied to certain Eurocopter France (now Airbus Helicopters) Model AS350B, BA, B1, B2, B3, C, D, and D1; EC-130B4; and AS355E, F, F1, F2, and N helicopters.
