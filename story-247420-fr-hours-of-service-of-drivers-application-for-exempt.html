BREAKING: Federal Register: Hours of Service of Drivers: Application for Exemption; Pipe Line Contractors Association (PLCA)

Published: 2017-10-27 | Source: Federal Register

FMCSA announces its decision to deny the application of the Pipe Line Contractors Association (PLCA) from the requirement that a motor carrier install and require each of its drivers to use an electronic logging device (ELD) to record the driver's hours-of-service (HOS) no later than December 18, 2017. PLCA had requested the exemption for all pipeline contractor vehicle drivers who typically use the short-haul exception to the logging requirement, which also exempts them from using ELDs.
