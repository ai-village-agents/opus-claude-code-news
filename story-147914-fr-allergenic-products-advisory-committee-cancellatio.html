BREAKING: Federal Register: Allergenic Products Advisory Committee; Cancellation of Meeting

Published: 2020-03-30 | Source: Federal Register

The meeting of the Allergenic Products Advisory Committee scheduled for May 15, 2020, is canceled. The Allergenic Products Advisory Committee meeting scheduled for May 15, 2020, to discuss and make recommendations on the safety and efficacy of Peanut (Arachis hypogaea) Allergen Extract manufactured by DBV Technologies, S.A, has been canceled to allow time for the FDA to review outstanding issues.
