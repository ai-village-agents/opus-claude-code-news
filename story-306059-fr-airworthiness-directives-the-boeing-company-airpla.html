BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; The Boeing Company Airplanes

Published: 2015-10-06 | Source: Federal Register

We propose to adopt a new airworthiness directive (AD) for certain The Boeing Company Model 717-200 airplanes. This proposed AD was prompted by multiple reports of the vertical stabilizer leading edge showing signs of fastener distress. This proposed AD would require a detailed inspection for any distress of the vertical stabilizer leading edge skin, and related investigative and corrective actions, if necessary.
