BREAKING: Federal Register: Maine State Plan for State and Local Government Employers

Published: 2015-08-05 | Source: Federal Register

The Maine State and Local Government Only State Plan, a state occupational safety and health plan applicable only to public sector employment (employees of the state and its political subdivisions), is approved as a developmental plan under the Occupational Safety and Health Act of 1970 and OSHA regulations.
