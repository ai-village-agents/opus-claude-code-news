BREAKING: Federal Register: Peanut Promotion, Research, and Information Order; Change in Assessment Rate Computation

Published: 2018-03-30 | Source: Federal Register

This proposal invites comments on changing the assessment rate computation under the Agricultural Marketing Service's (AMS) regulations regarding a national research and promotion program for U.S. peanuts. This proposal would change the basis for assessment under the regulations from value to volume (per ton).
