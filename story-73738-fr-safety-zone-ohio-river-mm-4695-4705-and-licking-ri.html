BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone; Ohio River MM 469.5-470.5 and Licking River MM 0.0 to 0.3, Cincinnati, OH

Published: 2023-08-04 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is establishing a temporary safety zone from Mile Marker 469.5--Mile Marker 470.5 of the Ohio River and from Mile Marker 0.0--Mile Marker 0.3 of the Licking River. This action is necessary to provide for the safety of life on these navigable waters near Cincinnati, OH during the Redbull Flugtag sporting event occurring on August 12, 2023.
