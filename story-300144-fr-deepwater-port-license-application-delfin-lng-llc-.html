BREAKING: Federal Register: Deepwater Port License Application: Delfin LNG LLC, Delfin LNG Deepwater Port

The Maritime Administration (MARAD), in coordination with the U.S. Coast Guard (USCG), will prepare an environmental impact statement (EIS) as part of the environmental review of the Delfin LNG LLC (Delfin LNG) deepwater port license application.
