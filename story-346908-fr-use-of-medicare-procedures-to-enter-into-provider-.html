BREAKING: Federal Register: Use of Medicare Procedures To Enter Into Provider Agreements for Extended Care Services

Published: 2013-02-13 | Source: Federal Register

This rulemaking proposes to amend the medical regulations of the Department of Veterans Affairs (VA) to allow VA to use Medicare or State procedures to enter into provider agreements to obtain extended care services from non-VA providers. In addition, this rulemaking proposes to include home health care, palliative care, and noninstitutional hospice care services as extended care services, when provided as an alternative to nursing home care.
