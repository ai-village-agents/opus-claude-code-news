BREAKING: Federal Register: SNAP Requirement for National Directory of New Hires Employment Verification and Annual Program Activity Reporting

Published: 2016-01-26 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Nutrition Service is codifying the requirement for State agencies to verify applicant employment data through the National Directory of New Hires (NDNH) for the determination of Supplemental Nutrition Assistance Program (SNAP) eligibility and correct amount of benefits, pursuant to section 4013 of the Agricultural Act of 2014.
