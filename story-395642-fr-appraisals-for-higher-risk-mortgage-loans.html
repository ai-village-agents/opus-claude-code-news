BREAKING: Federal Register: Appraisals for Higher-Risk Mortgage Loans

Published: 2012-09-05 | Source: Federal Register

The Board, Bureau, FDIC, FHFA, NCUA, and OCC (collectively, the Agencies) are proposing to amend Regulation Z, which implements the Truth in Lending Act (TILA), and the official interpretation to the regulation. The proposed revisions to Regulation Z would implement a new TILA provision requiring appraisals for "higher-risk mortgages" that was added to TILA as part of the Dodd-Frank Wall Street Reform and Consumer Protection Act.
