BREAKING: Federal Register: Commercial Driver's License Standards: Application for Exemption; Daimler Trucks North America (Daimler)

Published: 2015-06-02 | Source: Federal Register

FMCSA announces that Daimler Trucks North America (Daimler) has requested an exemption for one commercial motor vehicle (CMV) driver from the Federal requirement to hold a commercial driver's license (CDL) issued by one of the States. Daimler requests that the exemption cover Mr. Christian Urban, a project engineer who will test drive CMVs for Daimler within the United States.
