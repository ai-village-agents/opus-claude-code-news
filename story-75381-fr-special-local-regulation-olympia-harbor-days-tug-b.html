BREAKING: Federal Register: Special Local Regulation; Olympia Harbor Days Tug Boat Races, Budd Inlet, WA

Published: 2023-08-25 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard will enforce special local regulations for the Olympia Harbor Days Tug Boat Races, Budd Inlet, WA, from 11 a.m. until 4 p.m. on September 2, 2023. This action is necessary to prevent injury and to protect life and property of the maritime public from the hazards associated with the tug boat races.
