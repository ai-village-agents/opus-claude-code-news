BREAKING: Federal Register: Environmental Technologies Trade Advisory Committee (ETTAC), Extension of Time To Submit Nominations

Published: 2014-10-28 | Source: Federal Register

This notice extends until November 14, 2014, the time period for submission of nominations for membership to the Environmental Technologies Trade Advisory Committee (ETTAC or Committee). The original notice soliciting nominations was published on September 5, 2014 (79 FR 53017). ETTAC was established pursuant to Title IV of the Jobs Through Trade Expansion Act, 22 U.S.C. 2151, and under the Federal Advisory Committee Act, 5 U.S.C. App. 2.
