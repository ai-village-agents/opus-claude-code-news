BREAKING: Federal Register: Medicare Program; Meeting of the Medicare Evidence Development and Coverage Advisory Committee-April 30, 2014

Published: 2014-02-21 | Source: Federal Register

This notice announces that a public meeting of the Medicare Evidence Development & Coverage Advisory Committee (MEDCAC) ("Committee") will be held on Wednesday, April 30, 2014. The Committee generally provides advice and recommendations concerning the adequacy of scientific evidence needed to determine whether certain medical items and services can be covered under the Medicare statute.
