BREAKING: Federal Register: Extension of the Air Cargo Advance Screening (ACAS) Pilot Program and Reopening of Application Period for Participation

Published: 2013-04-23 | Source: Federal Register

On October 24, 2012, U.S. Customs and Border Protection (CBP) published a notice in the Federal Register that announced the formalization and expansion of the Air Cargo Advance Screening (ACAS) pilot program that would run for six months. This document announces that CBP is extending the pilot period for an additional six months and reopening the application period for new participants for 30 days.
