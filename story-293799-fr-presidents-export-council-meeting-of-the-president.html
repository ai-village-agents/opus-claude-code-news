BREAKING: Federal Register: President's Export Council: Meeting of the President's Export Council

Published: 2015-05-14 | Source: Federal Register

The President's Export Council (Council) will hold a meeting to deliberate on recommendations related to promoting the expansion of U.S. exports. Topics may include: North American supply chain competitiveness; innovation; Open Skies agreements; regulatory cooperation; infrastructure; and promoting sustainable building practices and products in developing countries.
