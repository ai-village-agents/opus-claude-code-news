BREAKING: Federal Register: Capital Construction Fund; Fishing Vessel Capital Construction Fund Procedures

Published: 2014-09-25 | Source: Federal Register

NMFS proposes to amend the Capital Construction Fund (CCF) regulations to eliminate provisions that no longer meet the needs of CCF participants, and to simplify and clarify the regulations to better implement the purposes of the underlying statute.
