BREAKING: Federal Register: Agency Activities in Response to a Portion of the Nuclear Energy Innovation and Modernization Act

Published: 2019-03-18 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Nuclear Regulatory Commission (NRC) is undertaking activities to implement the Nuclear Energy Innovation and Modernization Act (NEIMA) and develop a report identifying best practices for establishment and operation of local community advisory boards associated with decommissioning activities, including lessons learned from existing boards.
