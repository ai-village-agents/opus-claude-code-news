BREAKING: Federal Register: Hearing of the Attorney General's National Task Force on Children Exposed to Violence

Published: 2012-03-02 | Source: Federal Register

This is an announcement of the third hearing of the Attorney General's National Task Force on Children Exposed to Violence (the "task force"). The task force is chartered to provide OJP, a component of the Department of Justice, with valuable advice in the areas of children exposed to violence for the purpose of addressing the epidemic levels of exposure to violence faced by our nation's children.
