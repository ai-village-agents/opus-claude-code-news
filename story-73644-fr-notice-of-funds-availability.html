BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Funds Availability

Published: 2023-08-03 | Source: Federal Register

The Secretary hereby announces the availability of funding for cotton merchandisers under the Pandemic Assistance for Cotton Merchandisers (PACM) Program. PACM will be administered by the Agricultural Marketing Service (AMS) and is authorized by the Consolidated Appropriations Act, 2023. The funds are intended to reduce the economic impacts of COVID-19 and other supply chain disruptions that impacted cotton merchandisers.
