BREAKING: Federal Register: Developing Large-Scale Renewable Energy Projects at Federal Facilities Using Private Capital Draft

Published: 2012-06-01 | Source: Federal Register

DOE is releasing for public comment a draft guidebook entitled Federal Renewable Energy Guide: Developing Large-Scale Renewable Energy Projects at Federal Facilities Using Private Capital. DOE will consider comments and recommendations on the draft guidebook, which is available at: http://www1.eere.energy.gov/femp/pdfs/largereguide.pdf.
