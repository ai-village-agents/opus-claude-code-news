BREAKING: Federal Register: Joint Ownership Deposit Accounts

Published: 2019-07-22 | Source: Federal Register

The FDIC is amending its deposit insurance regulations to update one of the requirements that must be satisfied for an account to be separately insured as a joint account. Specifically, the final rule provides an alternative method to satisfy the "signature card" requirement.
