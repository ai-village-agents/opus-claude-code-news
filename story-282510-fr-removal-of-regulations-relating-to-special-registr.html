BREAKING: Federal Register: Removal of Regulations Relating to Special Registration Process for Certain Nonimmigrants

Published: 2016-12-23 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Homeland Security (DHS) is removing outdated regulations relating to an obsolete special registration program for certain nonimmigrants. DHS ceased use of the National Security Entry- Exit Registration System (NSEERS) program in 2011 after finding that the program was redundant, captured data manually that was already captured through automated systems, and no longer provided an increase in security in light of DHS's evolving assessment of the threat posed to the United States ...
