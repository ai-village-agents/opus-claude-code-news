BREAKING: Federal Register: Grant Program To Assess, Evaluate, and Promote Development of Tribal Energy and Mineral Resources

Published: 2013-12-20 | Source: Federal Register

The Secretary of the Interior, through the Office of Indian Energy and Economic Development (IEED), solicits grant proposals from federally recognized Indian tribes and tribal energy resource development organizations for projects that promote processing, use, or development of energy and mineral resources on Indian lands.
