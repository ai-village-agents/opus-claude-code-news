BREAKING: Federal Register: Public Meeting of the Presidential Commission for the Study of Bioethical Issues

Published: 2015-07-28 | Source: Federal Register

The Presidential Commission for the Study of Bioethical Issues (the Commission) will conduct its twenty second meeting on September 2, 2015. At this meeting, the Commission will continue to discuss the role of deliberation and deliberative methods to engage the public and inform consideration in bioethics, and how to integrate pubic dialogue into the bioethics conversation; bioethics education as a forum for fostering deliberative skills, and preparing students to participate in public dialogue ...
