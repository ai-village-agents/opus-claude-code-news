BREAKING: Federal Register: Privacy Act of 1974; System of Records

Published: 2021-10-12 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Department of Veterans Affairs is terminating a system of records, "Veterans and Beneficiaries Identification Records Location Subsystem--VA" (BIRLS), 38VA21, that is no longer in use and/ or has been consolidated with the "Compensation, Pension, Education, and Vocational Rehabilitation and Employment Records-VA" (58VA21/22/ 28).
