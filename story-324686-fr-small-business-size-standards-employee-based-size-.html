BREAKING: Federal Register: Small Business Size Standards: Employee Based Size Standards in Wholesale Trade and Retail Trade

Published: 2014-05-19 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Small Business Administration (SBA or Agency) proposes to increase employee based size standards for 46 industries in North American Industry Classification System (NAICS) Sector 42, Wholesale Trade, and one industry in NAICS Sector 44-45, Retail Trade and retain the current size standards in the remaining industries in those sectors.
