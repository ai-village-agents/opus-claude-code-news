BREAKING: Federal Register: Stakeholder Listening Sessions on New Rural Innovation Stronger Economy (RISE) Regulation

Published: 2020-07-22 | Source: Federal Register

The Rural Business-Cooperative Service (RBCS) is hosting three listening sessions for public input about the new Rural Innovation Stronger Economy (RISE) program and regulation. The RISE program will assist rural job accelerator partnerships in improving the ability of distressed rural communities to create high-wage jobs, accelerate the formation of new businesses, and help rural communities identify and maximize local assets.
