BREAKING: Federal Register: Importation of Peppers From the Republic of Korea; Mesh Screening Size

Published: 2018-04-20 | Source: Federal Register

We are proposing to amend the regulations that allow the importation of peppers from the Republic of Korea to increase the mesh size of the screening over vent openings from 0.6 mm to 1.6 mm, and to require the use of colored sticky traps as an additional measure for pest monitoring. We have determined that, when used alongside other mitigations in the regulations, there is no additional pest risk related to the change in mesh size.
