BREAKING: Federal Register: Titanium Dioxide; Exemption From the Requirement of a Tolerance

Published: 2018-02-28 | Source: Federal Register

This regulation establishes an exemption from the requirement of a tolerance for residues of titanium dioxide (CAS Reg. No. 13463-67- 7) in pre-harvest crops when used as an inert ingredient (colorant) at a concentration of not more than 45% in foliar applications of pesticide formulations containing anthraquinone.
