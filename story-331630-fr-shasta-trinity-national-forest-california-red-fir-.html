BREAKING: Federal Register: Shasta-Trinity National Forest, California: Red Fir Restoration Project

Published: 2014-08-07 | Source: Federal Register

The Shasta-Trinity National Forest proposes to treat diseased stands of Shasta red fir (Abies magnifica var. shastensis) and mixed conifer to reduce disease occurrence and fuels accumulations on approximately 1,400 acres. Vegetative treatments include regeneration with legacy tree retention, thin from above, sanitation/improvement cutting, plantation/pre-commercial thinning, and a roadside fuels buffer.
