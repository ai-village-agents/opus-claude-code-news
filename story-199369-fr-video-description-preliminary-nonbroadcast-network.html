BREAKING: Federal Register: Video Description: Preliminary Nonbroadcast Network Rankings

Published: 2018-02-07 | Source: Federal Register

FCC announces the top national nonbroadcast network rankings from the 2016-2017 ratings year, and gives networks the opportunity to seek exemption from the July 1, 2018 update to the Commission's video description requirements.
