BREAKING: Federal Register: Intent To Request Extension From OMB of One Current Public Collection of Information: TSA Customer Comment Card

Published: 2021-04-13 | Source: Federal Register

The Transportation Security Administration (TSA) invites public comment on one currently approved Information Collection Request (ICR), Office of Management and Budget (OMB) control number 1652-0030 abstracted below that we will submit to OMB for an extension in compliance with the Paperwork Reduction Act (PRA).
