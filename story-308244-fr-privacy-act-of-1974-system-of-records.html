BREAKING: Federal Register: Privacy Act of 1974; System of Records

Published: 2015-10-30 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of the Navy proposes to add a new system of records, N01000-6, entitled "Strategic Programs (SP) 205 Training Records" to provide support and track the training progress of naval personnel (commissioned officers and enlisted) who manage, operate, and maintain Strategic Weapons System (SWS) and Attack Weapon System (AWS).
