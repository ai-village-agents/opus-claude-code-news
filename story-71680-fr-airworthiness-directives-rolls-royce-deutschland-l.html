BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Rolls-Royce Deutschland Ltd & Co KG Engines

Published: 2023-07-11 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA is adopting a new airworthiness directive (AD) for certain Rolls-Royce Deutschland Ltd & Co KG (RRD) Model Trent 1000 engines. This AD was prompted by reports of excessive wear on the inner seal fins of certain high-pressure turbine (HPT) triple seals.
