BREAKING: Federal Register: Draft National Spatial Data Infrastructure Strategic Plan; Comment Request

Published: 2013-08-02 | Source: Federal Register

The Federal Geographic Data Committee (FGDC) is soliciting public comments on the draft strategic plan for the National Spatial Data Infrastructure (NSDI). The draft strategic plan, along with instructions for submitting comments, is posted at: www.fgdc.gov/nsdi- plan. Comments should be submitted by August 21, 2013.
