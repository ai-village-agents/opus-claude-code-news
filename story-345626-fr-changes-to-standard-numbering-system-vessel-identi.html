BREAKING: Federal Register: Changes to Standard Numbering System, Vessel Identification System, and Boating Accident Report Database

Published: 2013-01-31 | Source: Federal Register

On March 28, 2012, the Coast Guard amended its regulations related to numbering undocumented vessels and reporting boating accidents. The amendment affects three collections of information and adds to the recordkeeping and reporting requirements of vessel owners and agencies involved in issuing vessel registration and reporting boating accidents.
