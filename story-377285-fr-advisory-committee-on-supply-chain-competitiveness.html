BREAKING: Federal Register: Advisory Committee on Supply Chain Competitiveness Solicitation of Nominations for Membership

Published: 2012-02-10 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Commerce is currently seeking applications for public or semi-public organizations or entities, including ports, to serve as members of the Advisory Committee on Supply Chain Competitiveness (Committee). Representatives of ports are particularly encouraged to apply.
