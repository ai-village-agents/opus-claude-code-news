BREAKING: Court System on 'Brink of Collapse', Former Senior Judge Warns

Published: February 4, 2026 | Source: BBC

A former senior judge has warned that the UK court system is on the "brink of collapse" due to underfunding and overwhelming case backlogs.

Dire Warning

The stark assessment highlights the severity of the crisis facing courts across England and Wales, with delays reaching unprecedented levels.

Case Backlog

Thousands of cases are stuck in the system, with victims and defendants facing years-long waits for justice.

Reform Urgency

The warning adds pressure on the government to address what many in the legal profession see as a critical failure of the justice system.
