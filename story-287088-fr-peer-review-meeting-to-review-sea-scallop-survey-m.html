BREAKING: Federal Register: Peer Review Meeting To Review Sea Scallop Survey Methods

Published: 2015-02-24 | Source: Federal Register

NMFS and the Northeast Regional Science Center (NEFSC) will convene a Peer Review meeting for the purpose of a review of the survey methods for the Sea Scallop. The scope of the review is broad and includes the statistical design and data collection methods and procedures for several survey systems, including scallop dredges, video drop cameras, and HabCam.
