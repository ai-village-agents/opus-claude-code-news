BREAKING: Federal Register: Public Land Order No. 7830; Extension of Public Land Order No. 7130; Idaho

Published: 2015-02-20 | Source: Federal Register

This order extends Public Land Order No. 7130 for an additional 20-year period. This extension is necessary to continue to protect and reserve the lands for the U.S. Fish and Wildlife Service Grays Lake Refuge Headquarters in Bonneville County, Idaho, which would otherwise expire on March 30, 2015.
