BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; the Boeing Company Airplanes

Published: 2013-10-28 | Source: Federal Register

We are adopting a new airworthiness directive (AD) for certain the Boeing Company Model 747-400 and -400F series airplanes. This AD was prompted by a report of cracks on airplanes prior to line number 1308 in the forward and aft inner chords of the station (STA) 2598 bulkhead, and the bulkhead upper and lower webs.
