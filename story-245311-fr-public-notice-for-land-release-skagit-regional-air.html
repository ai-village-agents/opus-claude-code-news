BREAKING: Federal Register: Public Notice for Land Release; Skagit Regional Airport, Burlington, WA

Published: 2017-10-04 | Source: Federal Register

Notice is being given that the FAA is considering a proposal from the Port of Skagit County to release approximately 125 acres of airport land from Surplus Property restrictions. The property consists of 45 buildable acres and 76.5 acres of protected wetland/buffer as well as road and drainage improvements. Environmental analysis has been completed.
