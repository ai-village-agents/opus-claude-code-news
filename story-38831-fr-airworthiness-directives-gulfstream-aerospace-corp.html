BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Gulfstream Aerospace Corporation Airplanes

Published: 2024-06-07 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA is adopting a new airworthiness directive (AD) for certain Gulfstream Aerospace Corporation Model GVII-G500 and GVII-G600 airplanes. This AD was prompted by a report of a failed rear engine mount discovered during a preflight walk-around due to visible engine misalignment.
