BREAKING: Federal Register: Commercial Driver's License Testing and Commercial Learner's Permit Standards

Published: 2013-03-25 | Source: Federal Register

FMCSA amends its May 9, 2011, final rule in response to certain petitions for reconsideration. The 2011 final rule amended the commercial driver's license (CDL) knowledge and skills testing standards and established new minimum Federal standards for States to issue the commercial learner's permit (CLP).
