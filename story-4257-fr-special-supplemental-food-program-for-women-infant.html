BREAKING: Federal Register: Special Supplemental Food Program for Women, Infants, and Children (WIC) Overseas: Revision to Definition of Drafts

Published: 2025-01-17 | Source: Federal Register

The ASD(HA) is issuing this final rule to revise the definition of drafts, currently defined as paper instruments, within the regulation governing the WIC Overseas Program. This final rule modernizes the definition of drafts to include not only paper instruments but also other methods and processes, including electronic benefit transfers.
