BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Airbus SAS Airplanes

Published: 2024-01-03 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA is superseding Airworthiness Directive (AD) 2022-07- 15, which applied to all Airbus SAS Model A318, A319, A320, and A321 series airplanes. AD 2022-07-15 required replacing affected braking and steering control units (BSCUs) and revising the operator's existing FAA-approved minimum equipment list (MEL).
