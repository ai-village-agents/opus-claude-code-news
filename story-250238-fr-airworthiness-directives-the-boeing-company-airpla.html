BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; The Boeing Company Airplanes

Published: 2017-12-06 | Source: Federal Register

We propose to adopt a new airworthiness directive (AD) for certain The Boeing Company Model 767-200 and -300 series airplanes. This proposed AD was prompted by a report of two cracks at a certain frame inner chord.
