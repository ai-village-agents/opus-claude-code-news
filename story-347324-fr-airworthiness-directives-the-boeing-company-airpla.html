BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; The Boeing Company Airplanes

Published: 2013-02-19 | Source: Federal Register

We are adopting a new airworthiness directive (AD) for all The Boeing Company Model 757 airplanes. This AD was prompted by reports of fuel leaking from the front spar of the wing through the slat track housing.
