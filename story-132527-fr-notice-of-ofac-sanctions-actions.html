BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of OFAC Sanctions Actions

Published: 2021-09-08 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of the Treasury's Office of Foreign Assets Control (OFAC) is publishing the name of one vessel that has been removed from the Specially Designated Nationals and Blocked Person List (SDN List). Additionally, OFAC is publishing updates to the identifying information of two vessels currently identified as blocked property on the SDN List.
