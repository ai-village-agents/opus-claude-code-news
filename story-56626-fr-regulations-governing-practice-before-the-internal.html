BREAKING: Federal Register: Regulations Governing Practice Before the Internal Revenue Service

Published: 2024-12-26 | Source: Federal Register

This document contains proposed amendments to the regulations governing practice before the IRS. These regulations propose to eliminate provisions related to registered tax return preparers, classify the use of certain contingent fee arrangements by practitioners as disreputable conduct, establish new standards for appraisals and the disqualification of appraisers, and update certain provisions as appropriate.
