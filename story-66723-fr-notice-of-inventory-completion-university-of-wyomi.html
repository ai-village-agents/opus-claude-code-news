BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Inventory Completion: University of Wyoming Human Remains Repository, Laramie, WY

Published: 2023-05-04 | Source: Federal Register

In accordance with the Native American Graves Protection and Repatriation Act (NAGPRA), the University of Wyoming Human Remains Repository has completed an inventory of human remains and associated funerary objects and has determined that there is no cultural affiliation between the human remains and associated funerary objects and any Indian Tribe.
