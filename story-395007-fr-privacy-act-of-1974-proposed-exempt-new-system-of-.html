BREAKING: Federal Register: Privacy Act of 1974; Proposed Exempt New System of Records

Published: 2012-08-28 | Source: Federal Register

In accordance with the requirements of the Privacy Act of 1974, as amended (Privacy Act), the National Institutes of Health (NIH) is proposing to establish an exempt new system of records, 09-25-0223, "NIH Records Related to Research Misconduct Proceedings, HHS/NIH."
