BREAKING: Federal Register: Energy Conservation Program for High-Intensity Discharge Lamps: Public Meeting and Availability of the Interim Technical Support Document

Published: 2013-02-28 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Department of Energy (DOE) will hold a public meeting to discuss and receive comments on the interim analysis it has conducted for purposes of establishing energy conservation standards for high-intensity discharge (HID) lamps.
