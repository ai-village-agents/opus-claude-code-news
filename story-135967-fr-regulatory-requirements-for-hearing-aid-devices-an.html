BREAKING: Federal Register: Regulatory Requirements for Hearing Aid Devices and Personal Sound Amplification Products; Draft Guidance for Industry and Food and Drug Administration Staff; Availability

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA or Agency) is announcing the availability of the draft guidance entitled ``Regulatory Requirements for Hearing Aid Devices and Personal Sound Amplification Products.'' The FDA Reauthorization Act of 2017 (FDARA) directed FDA to update and finalize the draft guidance entitled ``Regulatory Requirements for Hearing Aid Devices and Personal Sound Amplification Products,'' issued on November 7, 2013.
