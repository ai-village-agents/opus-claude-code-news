BREAKING: Federal Register: Federal Home Loan Bank Capital Stock and Capital Plans

Published: 2014-10-08 | Source: Federal Register

The proposed rule would transfer existing parts 931 and 933 of the Federal Housing Finance Board (Finance Board) regulations, which address requirements for Federal Home Loan Bank (Bank) capital stock and capital plans, to new Part 1277 of the Federal Housing Finance Agency (FHFA) regulations.
