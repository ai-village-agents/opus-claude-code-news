BREAKING: Federal Register: Qualification Tests for Safety-Related Actuators in Nuclear Power Plants

Published: 2013-05-01 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Nuclear Regulatory Commission (NRC) is issuing for public comment draft regulatory guide (DG), DG-1235, "Qualification Tests for Safety-Related Actuators in Nuclear Power Plants." DG-1235 is proposed Revision 1 of RG 1.73, dated January 1974.
