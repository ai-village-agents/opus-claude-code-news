BREAKING: Federal Register: CAFTA-DR Dispute Settlement Proceeding Regarding Guatemala's Failure To Effectively Enforce Its Labor Laws

Published: 2015-01-26 | Source: Federal Register

The Office of the United States Trade Representative ("USTR") is providing notice that effective September 19, 2014, at the request of the United States, the arbitral panel reviewing the U.S. challenge to Guatemala's breach of its obligations under Article 16.2.1(a) of the Dominican Republic--Central America--United States Free Trade Agreement ("CAFTA-DR") has begun its review.
