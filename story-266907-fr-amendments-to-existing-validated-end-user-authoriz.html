BREAKING: Federal Register: Amendments to Existing Validated End-User Authorization in the People's Republic of China: Advanced Micro Devices, Inc.

Published: 2016-06-23 | Source: Federal Register

In this rule, the Bureau of Industry and Security (BIS) amends the Export Administration Regulations (EAR) to revise the existing Validated End-User (VEU) list for the People's Republic of China by updating the list of eligible items and destinations (facilities) for VEU Advanced Micro Devices, Inc. (AMD).
