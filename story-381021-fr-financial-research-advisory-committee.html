BREAKING: Federal Register: Financial Research Advisory Committee

Published: 2012-03-22 | Source: Federal Register

The Treasury Department has determined that it is in the public interest to establish the Financial Research Advisory Committee. A Charter for the Committee has been prepared and will be filed no earlier than 15 days following the date of publication of this notice. This notice establishes criteria and procedures for the selection of members.
