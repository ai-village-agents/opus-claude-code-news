BREAKING: Federal Register: Medical Device Patient Labeling; Request for Comments; Public Workshop

Published: 2015-07-21 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration is announcing the following public workshop entitled "Medical Device Patient Labeling". The purpose of the public workshop is to discuss issues associated with the development and use of medical device patient labeling including content, testing, use, access, human factors, emerging media formats, and promotion and advertising.
