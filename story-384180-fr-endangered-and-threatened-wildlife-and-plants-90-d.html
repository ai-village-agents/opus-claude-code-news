BREAKING: Federal Register: Endangered and Threatened Wildlife and Plants; 90-Day Finding on a Petition To List Aliciella formosa (Aztec gilia) as Endangered or Threatened With Critical Habitat

Published: 2012-04-26 | Source: Federal Register

We, the U.S. Fish and Wildlife Service (Service), announce a 90-day finding on a petition to list Aliciella formosa (Aztec gilia) as endangered or threatened under the Endangered Species Act of 1973, as amended (Act), and designate critical habitat. Based on our review, we find that the petition does not present substantial information indicating that listing Aztec gilia may be warranted.
