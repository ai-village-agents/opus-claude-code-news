BREAKING: Federal Register: Liquor Control Ordinance of the Kickapoo Tribe in Kansas

Published: 2021-05-03 | Source: Federal Register

This notice publishes the Liquor Control Ordinance of the Kickapoo Tribe in Kansas. The liquor control ordinance regulates and controls the possession, sale, manufacture, and distribution of alcohol in conformity with the laws of the State of Kansas for the purpose of generating new Tribal revenues.
