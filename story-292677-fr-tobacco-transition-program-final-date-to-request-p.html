BREAKING: Federal Register: Tobacco Transition Program; Final Date To Request Payments; No Change to Final Assessment Procedures

Published: 2015-04-30 | Source: Federal Register

The Farm Service Agency (FSA), on behalf of the Commodity Credit Corporation (CCC), is announcing that the final date for holders of Tobacco Transition Payment Program (TTPP) contracts to request payments on their existing contracts is July 1, 2015.
