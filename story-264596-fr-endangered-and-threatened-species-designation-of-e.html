BREAKING: Federal Register: Endangered and Threatened Species: Designation of Experimental Populations Under the Endangered Species Act

Published: 2016-05-26 | Source: Federal Register

We, the National Marine Fisheries Service (NMFS), issue final regulations to amend the Code of Federal Regulations (CFR) to implement the Endangered Species Act (ESA) regarding experimental populations.
