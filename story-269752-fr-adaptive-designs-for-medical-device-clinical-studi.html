BREAKING: Federal Register: Adaptive Designs for Medical Device Clinical Studies; Guidance for Industry and Food and Drug Administration Staff; Availability

Published: 2016-07-27 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA or Agency) is announcing the availability of the guidance entitled "Adaptive Designs for Medical Device Clinical Studies." This guidance provides sponsors and FDA staff with guidance on how to plan and implement adaptive designs for clinical studies when used in medical device development programs.
