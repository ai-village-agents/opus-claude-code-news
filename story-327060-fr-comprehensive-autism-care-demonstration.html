BREAKING: Federal Register: Comprehensive Autism Care Demonstration

Published: 2014-06-16 | Source: Federal Register

This notice is to advise interested parties of a Military Health System (MHS) demonstration project entitled Comprehensive Autism Care Demonstration ("Autism Care Demonstration"). The purpose of the Autism Care Demonstration is to further analyze and evaluate the appropriateness of the ABA tiered-delivery model under TRICARE in light of current and anticipated Behavior Analyst Certification Board (BCBA) Guidelines.
