BREAKING: Federal Register: Proposed Data Collections Submitted for Extension of Public Comment Period

Published: 2013-04-22 | Source: Federal Register

The Centers for Disease Control and Prevention (CDC), Department of Health and Human Services (HHS), is reopening the comment period, thus amending the due date for responses to its Request for Public Comments, published in Vol. 78, No. 29, of the Federal Register on February 12, 2013. The due date has been extended to May 3, 2013, to allow more time for review.
