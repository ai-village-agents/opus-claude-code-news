BREAKING: Federal Register: Enhanced Weapons, Firearms Background Checks, and Security Event Notifications

Published: 2023-03-14 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Nuclear Regulatory Commission (NRC) is amending its regulations to implement its authority under Section 161A of the Atomic Energy Act of 1954, as amended. This final rule applies to several classes of facilities as well as activities involving the transportation of radioactive material and other property designated by the NRC.
