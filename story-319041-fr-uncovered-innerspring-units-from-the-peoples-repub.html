BREAKING: Federal Register: Uncovered Innerspring Units From the People's Republic of China; Antidumping Duty Administrative Review; 2012-2013

Published: 2014-03-17 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Commerce ("the Department") is conducting an administrative review of the antidumping duty order on uncovered innerspring units from the People's Republic of China ("PRC"). The period of review is February 1, 2012, through January 31, 2013. The review covers the following exporters of subject merchandise: Goldon Bedding Manufacturing (M) Sdn Bhd ("Goldon") \1\ and Ta Cheng Coconut Knitting Company Ltd. ("Ta Cheng").
