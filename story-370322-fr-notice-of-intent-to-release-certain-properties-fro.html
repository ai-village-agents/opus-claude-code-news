BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Intent To Release Certain Properties From All Terms, Conditions, Reservations and Restrictions of a Quitclaim Deed Agreement Between the City of Marianna and the Federal Aviation Administration for the Marianna Municipal Airport, Marianna, FL

Published: 2013-11-15 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA hereby provides notice of intent to release certain airport properties 28.18 acres at the Marianna Municipal Airport, Marianna, FL from the conditions, reservations, and restrictions as contained in a Quitclaim Deed agreement between the FAA and the the City of Marianna, dated August 2, 1947. The release of property will allow the City of Marianna to dispose of the property for other than aeronautical purposes.
