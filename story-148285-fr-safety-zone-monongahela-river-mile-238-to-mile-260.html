BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone; Monongahela River Mile 23.8 to Mile 26.0, Pittsburgh, PA

Published: 2020-04-02 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is establishing a temporary safety zone for all navigable waters of the Monongahela River from mile 23.8 to mile 26.0. This action is necessary to protect persons, vessels, and the marine environment from potential hazards associated with power line work across the river near Elrama Power Plant, Pittsburgh, PA, during an electrical conductor pull from March 23, 2020 through April 6, 2020.
