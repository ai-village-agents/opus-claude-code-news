BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice To Resume the Preparation of a Final Environmental Impact Statement for the Construction and Operations Plan for Vineyard Wind LLC

Published: 2021-03-03 | Source: Federal Register

The Bureau of Ocean Energy Management (BOEM) is resuming the preparation of a final environmental impact statement (FEIS) for the Construction and Operations Plan (COP) submitted by Vineyard Wind LLC (Vineyard Wind) concerning the construction and operation of an 800- megawatt wind energy facility offshore Massachusetts (Vineyard Wind 1 Project or Project).
