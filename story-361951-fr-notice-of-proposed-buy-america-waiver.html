BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Proposed Buy America Waiver

Published: 2013-08-05 | Source: Federal Register

The Federal Transit Administration (FTA) received a request for a waiver to permit the purchase of minivans that are non-compliant with FTA Buy America requirements using FTA funding. The request is from the North Front Range Metropolitan Planning Organization (NFRMPO). NFRMPO is in the process of procuring vehicles for its vanpool program, "VanGo."
