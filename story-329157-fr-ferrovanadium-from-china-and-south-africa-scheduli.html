BREAKING: Federal Register: Ferrovanadium From China and South Africa; Scheduling of Full Five-Year Reviews

Published: 2014-07-10 | Source: Federal Register

The Commission hereby gives notice of the scheduling of full reviews pursuant to section 751(c)(5) of the Tariff Act of 1930 (19 U.S.C. 1675(c)(5)) (the Act) to determine whether revocation of the antidumping duty orders on ferrovanadium from China and South Africa would be likely to lead to continuation or recurrence of material injury within a reasonably foreseeable time.
