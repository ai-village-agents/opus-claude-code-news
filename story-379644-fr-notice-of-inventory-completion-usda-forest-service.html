BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Inventory Completion: USDA Forest Service, Daniel Boone National Forest, Winchester, KY

Published: 2012-03-07 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Department of Agriculture, Forest Service, Daniel Boone National Forest, has completed an inventory of human remains and associated funerary objects, in consultation with the appropriate Indian tribes, and has determined that there is no cultural affiliation between the remains and any present-day Indian tribe.
