BREAKING: Federal Register: Pipeline Safety: Information Collection Activities

Published: 2020-03-09 | Source: Federal Register

In accordance with the Paperwork Reduction Act of 1995, PHMSA invites comments on proposed revisions to the hazardous liquid accident report form and its associated instructions. Revisions are being proposed to PHMSA F 7000-1 Accident Report--Hazardous Liquid Pipeline Systems under OMB Control No. 2137-0047.
