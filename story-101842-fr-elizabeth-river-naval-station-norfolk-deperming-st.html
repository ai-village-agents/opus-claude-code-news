BREAKING: Federal Register: Elizabeth River, Naval Station Norfolk Deperming Station, Norfolk, VA; Restricted Area

Published: 2022-08-01 | Source: Federal Register

The Corps of Engineers is establishing a restricted area in the waters of the Elizabeth River surrounding the Naval Station Norfolk (NSN) Lambert's Point Deperming Station in Norfolk, Virginia. Naval Station Norfolk is the home port of numerous ships and its facilities provide operational readiness support to the U.S. Atlantic Fleet.
