BREAKING: Federal Register: Final Determination Against Federal Acknowledgment of the Tolowa Nation

Published: 2014-01-30 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of the Interior (Department) gives notice that the Assistant Secretary--Indian Affairs (AS-IA) declines to acknowledge the petitioner known as the Tolowa Nation (Petitioner 85) as an Indian tribe within the meaning of Federal law.
