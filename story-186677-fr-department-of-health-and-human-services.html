BREAKING: Federal Register: DEPARTMENT OF HEALTH AND HUMAN SERVICES

Published: 2019-08-19 | Source: Federal Register

As stipulated by the Federal Advisory Committee Act, the Department of Health and Human Services (HHS) is hereby giving notice that the Tick-Borne Disease Working Group (TBDWG) will hold a meeting. The meeting will be open to the public.
