BREAKING: Federal Register: Pacific Fishery Management Council; Public Meetings

Published: 2023-10-03 | Source: Federal Register

The Pacific Fishery Management Council's (Pacific Council) Groundfish Advisory Subpanel (GAP), Groundfish Management Team (GMT), Ecosystem Working Group (EWG), Salmon Technical Team (STT), Habitat Committee (HC), and Salmon Advisory Subpanel (SAS) will hold online meetings to discuss items on the Pacific Council's November Council meeting agenda as detailed in the SUPPLEMENTARY INFORMATION below. These meetings are open to the public.
