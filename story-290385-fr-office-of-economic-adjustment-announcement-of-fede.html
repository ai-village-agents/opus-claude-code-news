BREAKING: Federal Register: Office of Economic Adjustment; Announcement of Federal Funding Opportunity (FFO)

Published: 2015-04-03 | Source: Federal Register

This notice announces an opportunity to request funding from the Office of Economic Adjustment (OEA), a Department of Defense (DoD) field activity, for community planning assistance to help prevent the siting of energy projects from adversely affecting DoD's test, training, and military operations.
