BREAKING: Federal Register: Ryan White HIV/AIDS Program, Special Projects of National Significance (SPNS), the Albert Einstein College of Medicine, Enhancing Access to and Retention in Quality HIV Care for Women of Color Initiative

Published: 2014-09-04 | Source: Federal Register

HRSA will be issuing a non-competitive award under the SPNS Program to current grantee, the Albert Einstein College of Medicine (Grant #5 H97HA15152-05-00), for approximately $55,000 during the budget period September 1, 2014, to August 31, 2015.
