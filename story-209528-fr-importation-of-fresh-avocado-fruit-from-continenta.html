BREAKING: Federal Register: Importation of Fresh Avocado Fruit From Continental Ecuador Into the Continental United States

Published: 2018-06-15 | Source: Federal Register

We are proposing to amend the fruits and vegetables regulations to allow the importation into the continental United States of fresh avocado fruit from continental Ecuador.
