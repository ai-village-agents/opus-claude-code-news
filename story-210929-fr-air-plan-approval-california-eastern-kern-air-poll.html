BREAKING: Federal Register: Air Plan Approval; California; Eastern Kern Air Pollution Control District; Reclassification

Published: 2018-07-05 | Source: Federal Register

Under the Clean Air Act, the Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is granting a request by the State of California to reclassify the Eastern Kern County ("Eastern Kern") nonattainment area from "Moderate" to "Serious" for the 2008 ozone national ambient air quality standards (NAAQS).
