BREAKING: Federal Register: Parts and Accessories Necessary for Safe Operation; Virginia Tech Transportation Institute Exemption Application

Published: 2015-05-20 | Source: Federal Register

The Federal Motor Carrier Safety Administration (FMCSA) announces its decision to grant Virginia Tech Transportation Institute's (VTTI) exemption application to allow the placement of camera-based data acquisition systems (DAS) at the bottom of windshields on commercial motor vehicles (CMVs).
