BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Inventory Completion: Nebraska State Historical Society, Lincoln, NE

Published: 2017-03-13 | Source: Federal Register

The Nebraska State Historical Society (NSHS) has completed an inventory of human remains, in consultation with the appropriate Indian tribes or Native Hawaiian organizations, and has determined that there is a cultural affiliation between the human remains and present-day Indian tribes or Native Hawaiian organizations.
