Published: 2022-07-13 | Source: Federal Register

The Federal Trade Commission ("FTC" or "Commission") seeks comment on this notice of proposed rulemaking ("NPRM") related to the sale, financing, and leasing of motor vehicles by motor vehicle dealers.
