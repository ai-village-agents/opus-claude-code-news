BREAKING: Federal Register: Parts and Accessories Necessary for Safe Operation; Rear Impact Guards and Rear Impact Protection

Published: 2020-12-29 | Source: Federal Register

FMCSA proposes to amend the Federal Motor Carrier Safety Regulations (FMCSRs) to include rear impact guards on the list of items that must be examined as part of the required annual inspection for each commercial motor vehicle (CMV).
