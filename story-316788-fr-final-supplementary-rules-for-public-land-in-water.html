BREAKING: Federal Register: Final Supplementary Rules for Public Land in Water Canyon, Humboldt County, NV

Published: 2014-02-18 | Source: Federal Register

The Bureau of Land Management (BLM) Winnemucca District, Humboldt River Field Office is establishing supplementary rules relating to camping, the discharge of firearms, and the use of motor vehicles on public land within Zone 1 of the Water Canyon Recreation Area. The supplementary rules are necessary to protect natural resources and the health and safety of public land users within the Zone 1 area.
