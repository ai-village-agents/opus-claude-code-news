BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Eclipse Aerospace, Inc. Airplanes

Published: 2013-05-22 | Source: Federal Register

We propose to adopt a new airworthiness directive (AD) for all Eclipse Aerospace, Inc. Model EA500 airplanes equipped with Avio, Avio with ETT, or Avio NG 1.0 avionics suites. This proposed AD was prompted by a report of potential aircraft hardware failure in the autopilot control panel and the center switch panel.
