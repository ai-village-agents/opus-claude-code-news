BREAKING: Federal Register: Current Good Manufacturing Practices, Quality Control Procedures, Quality Factors, Notification Requirements, and Records and Reports, for Infant Formula; Correction

Published: 2014-07-15 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA or we) is correcting a final rule that appeared in the Federal Register of June 10, 2014. The final rule revised our infant formula regulations by establishing requirements for current good manufacturing practices, including audits; establishing requirements for quality factors; and amending our quality control procedures, notification, and record and reporting requirements for infant formula.
