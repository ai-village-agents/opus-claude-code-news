BREAKING: Federal Register: Hours of Service of Drivers: PJ Helicopters, Inc.; Application for Exemption

Published: 2019-03-29 | Source: Federal Register

FMCSA announces that it has received an application from PJ Helicopters, Inc. (PJH) requesting an exemption from two provisions of the hours-of-service (HOS) regulations for its ground support equipment operators. PJH requested relief from the 14-hour rule and the requirement that drivers have 10 consecutive hours off-duty at the end of the work shift.
