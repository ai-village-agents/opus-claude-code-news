BREAKING: Federal Register: Certain Digital Television Set-Top Boxes, Remote Control Devices, and Components Thereof; Commission Determination Not To Review an Initial Determination Granting Complainants' Motion for Termination of the Investigation Based on Withdrawal of the Complaint; Termination of the Investigation

Published: 2017-08-23 | Source: Federal Register

Notice is hereby given that the U.S. International Trade Commission has determined not to review an initial determination ("ID") (Corrected Order No. 16) of the presiding administrative law judge ("ALJ") granting Complainants' motion for termination of the investigation based on withdrawal of the complaint. The investigation is terminated.
