BREAKING: Federal Register: Public Land Order No. 7932; Withdrawal for Dash Lake Waterfowl Production Area; North Dakota

Published: 2023-10-20 | Source: Federal Register

This Public Land Order (PLO) withdraws 13.50 acres of public land from appropriation under the public land laws, including location and entry under the United States mining laws, but not from leasing under the mineral and geothermal leasing laws, subject to valid existing rights, for 100 years, and reserves the land for 100 years for management by the U.S. Fish and Wildlife Service as part of the Dash Lake Waterfowl Production Area (WPA) in Towner County, North Dakota.
