BREAKING: Federal Register: Completing the Transition to Electronic Filing, Licenses and Authorizations, and Correspondence in the Wireless Radio Services

Published: 2021-04-20 | Source: Federal Register

In this document, the Federal Communications Commission (Commission) adopted an Order announcing changes to the Commission's rules that will become effective. To conform changes to a rule regarding submission of environmental assessments that the Commission had made in two prior decisions, the Commission amends the non- substantive, editorial revisions by adding language pertaining to electronic filing.
