BREAKING: Federal Register: Guidance on Safe Harbor Rate Streamlining for Engineering and Design Services Consultant Contracts

Published: 2019-06-21 | Source: Federal Register

This Notice announces and outlines the final guidance for the implementation of a Safe Harbor indirect cost rate for certain engineering design service firms that find establishing such rates to be costly and a barrier to participating in engineering and design service contracts reimbursed with Federal-aid Highway Program (FAHP) funds.
