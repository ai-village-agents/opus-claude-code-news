BREAKING: Federal Register: Radiology Devices; Reclassification of Medical Image Analyzers

Published: 2018-06-04 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA or the Agency) is issuing this proposed order to reclassify medical image analyzers applied to mammography breast cancer, ultrasound breast lesions, radiograph lung nodules, and radiograph dental caries detection as postamendments class III (premarket approval) devices (regulated under product code MYN), into class II (special controls), subject to premarket notification.
