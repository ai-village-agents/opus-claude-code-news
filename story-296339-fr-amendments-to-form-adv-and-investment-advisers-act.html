BREAKING: Federal Register: Amendments to Form ADV and Investment Advisers Act Rules

Published: 2015-06-12 | Source: Federal Register

The Securities and Exchange Commission is proposing amendments to Form ADV that are designed to provide additional information regarding advisers, including information about their separately managed account business; incorporate a method for private fund adviser entities operating a single advisory business to register using a single Form ADV; and make clarifying, technical and other amendments to certain Form ADV items and instructions.
