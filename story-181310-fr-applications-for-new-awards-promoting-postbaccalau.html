BREAKING: Federal Register: Applications for New Awards; Promoting Postbaccalaureate Opportunities for Hispanic Americans Program

Published: 2019-06-11 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Education (Department) is issuing a notice inviting applications for new individual development awards for fiscal year (FY) 2019 for the Promoting Postbaccalaureate Opportunities for Hispanic Americans (PPOHA) Program, Catalog of Federal Domestic Assistance (CFDA) number 84.031M. This notice relates to the approved information collection under OMB control number 1894-0006.
