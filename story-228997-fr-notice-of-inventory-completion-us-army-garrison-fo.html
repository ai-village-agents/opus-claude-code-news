BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Inventory Completion: U.S. Army Garrison Fort Leonard Wood, Pulaski County, MO

Published: 2017-03-07 | Source: Federal Register

U.S. Army Garrison Fort Leonard Wood has completed an inventory of human remains and associated funerary objects, in consultation with the appropriate Indian tribes or Native Hawaiian organizations, and has determined that there is a cultural affiliation between the human remains and associated funerary objects and present- day Indian tribes or Native Hawaiian organizations.
