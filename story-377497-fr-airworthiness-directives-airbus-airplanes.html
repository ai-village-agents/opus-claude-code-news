BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Airbus Airplanes

Published: 2012-02-13 | Source: Federal Register

We are adopting a new airworthiness directive (AD) for all Airbus Model A340-600 series airplanes. This AD requires modifying the fire extinguishing system from a three-bottles solution with 4 flow metering compact unit into a two-bottles solution with 2 flow metering systems equipped with upgraded water absorbing filter elements.
