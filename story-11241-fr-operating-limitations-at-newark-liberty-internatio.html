BREAKING: Federal Register: Operating Limitations at Newark Liberty International Airport, Notice of Meeting and Request for Information

Published: 2025-05-14 | Source: Federal Register

FAA will conduct a meeting to discuss flight restrictions at Newark Liberty International Airport (EWR) to reduce overscheduling and flight delays during peak hours of operation at that airport. This meeting is open to all scheduled air carriers, regardless of whether they currently provide scheduled service to EWR, and to the Port Authority of New York and New Jersey, which is the airport operator of EWR.
