BREAKING: Federal Register: Pipeline Safety: Valve Installation and Minimum Rupture Detection Standards

Published: 2020-02-06 | Source: Federal Register

PHMSA is proposing to revise the Pipeline Safety Regulations applicable to newly constructed and entirely replaced onshore natural gas transmission and hazardous liquid pipelines to mitigate ruptures. Additionally, PHMSA is revising the regulations regarding rupture detection to shorten pipeline segment isolation times.
