BREAKING: Federal Register: Effective Date of Requirement for Premarket Approval for Shortwave Diathermy for All Other Uses

Published: 2012-07-06 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA) is proposing to require the filing of a premarket approval application (PMA) or a notice of completion of a product development protocol (PDP) for the class III preamendments device, shortwave diathermy (SWD) for all other uses.
