BREAKING: Federal Register: Special Local Regulation; Kailua Bay, Ironman World Championship, Kailua-Kona, Hawaii

Published: 2019-04-18 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is proposing to establish a recurring special local regulation for certain waters of Kailua Bay, Hawaii. This action is necessary to provide for the safety of life on these navigable waters located at Kailua-Kona, HI, during the swim portion of the Ironman World Championship Triathlon and practice swim held on consecutive Saturdays annually in October.
