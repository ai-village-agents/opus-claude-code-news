BREAKING: Federal Register: United States Standards for Grades of Frozen Corn on the Cob

Published: 2020-10-21 | Source: Federal Register

The Agricultural Marketing Service (AMS) of the Department of Agriculture (USDA) is soliciting comments on its proposal to revise the U.S. Standards for Grades of Frozen Corn on the Cob (July 27, 1970). AMS is proposing to replace the two-term grading system (dual nomenclature) with a single term to describe each quality level in the grade standards.
