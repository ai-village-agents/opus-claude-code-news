BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Inventory Completion: Kamehameha Schools and University of Hawai'i at Hilo, Hilo, HI

Published: 2016-03-30 | Source: Federal Register

The Kamehameha Schools and University of Hawai[revaps]i at Hilo have completed an inventory of human remains, in consultation with the appropriate Indian tribes or Native Hawaiian organizations, and have determined that there is a cultural affiliation between the human remains and present-day Indian tribes or Native Hawaiian organizations.
