BREAKING: Federal Register: Endangered Species; File No. 16230

Published: 2013-09-17 | Source: Federal Register

Notice is hereby given that NMFS has issued a permit to the North Carolina Division of Marine Fisheries (NCDMF) for the incidental take of sea turtles associated with the otherwise lawful commercial gillnet fishery in North Carolina inshore state waters.
