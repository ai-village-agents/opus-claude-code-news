BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Intent To Create a New Information Collection

Published: 2015-02-02 | Source: Federal Register

In accordance with the Paperwork Reduction Act of 1995, this notice announces the intent of the National Agricultural Statistics Service (NASS) to seek the approval to conduct a new data collection to comply with a mandate in the 2014 Farm Bill.
