BREAKING: Federal Register: Proposed Amendment of Class D and Class E Airspace; Walla Walla, WA

Published: 2015-11-27 | Source: Federal Register

This action proposes to modify Class D airspace, Class E surface area airspace, Class E surface area airspace designated as an extension, and Class E airspace extending upward from 700 feet above the surface at Walla Walla Regional Airport, Walla Walla, WA. After a review of the airspace, the FAA found it necessary to amend the airspace area for the safety and management of Instrument Flight Rules (IFR) operations for arriving and departing aircraft at the airport.
