BREAKING: Federal Register: Agency Information Collection Activities: Transfer of Cargo to a Container Station

Published: 2012-01-24 | Source: Federal Register

As part of its continuing effort to reduce paperwork and respondent burden, CBP invites the general public and other Federal agencies to comment on an information collection requirement concerning Transfer of Cargo to a Container Station. This request for comment is being made pursuant to the Paperwork Reduction Act of 1995 (Pub. L. 104-13).
