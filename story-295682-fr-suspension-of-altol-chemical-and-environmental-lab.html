BREAKING: Federal Register: Suspension of Altol Chemical and Environmental Laboratory as a Customs-Accredited Laboratory

Published: 2015-06-05 | Source: Federal Register

Altol Chemical and Environmental Laboratory has been suspended, pursuant to CBP regulations, from testing petroleum and petroleum products for customs purposes.

Agency: Homeland Security Department, U.S. Customs and Border Protection
