BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Issuance of Final Determination Concerning Various Elliptical Exercise Machines and Option Package Kits

Published: 2014-10-23 | Source: Federal Register

This document provides notice that U.S. Customs and Border Protection ("CBP") has issued a final determination concerning the country of origin of various elliptical exercise machines manufactured and distributed by Octane Fitness, and their option package kits that add from three products to the elliptical exercise machines.
