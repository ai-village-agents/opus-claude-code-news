BREAKING: Federal Register: Public Land Order No. 7869; Partial Revocation of Withdrawal Established by Secretarial Orders, Klamath River Project, California

Published: 2018-10-10 | Source: Federal Register

This PLO partially revokes 18.67 acres of public land from a withdrawal created by two Secretarial Orders dated July 19, 1904, and January 28, 1905, which withdrew land for the Bureau of Reclamation's (BOR) Klamath River Project.
