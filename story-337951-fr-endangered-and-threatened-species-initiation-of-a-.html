BREAKING: Federal Register: Endangered and Threatened Species; Initiation of a Status Review for the Guadalupe Fur Seal

Published: 2014-10-22 | Source: Federal Register

The National Marine Fisheries Service (NMFS) announces a status review of the Guadalupe fur seal (Arctocephalus townsendi) under the Endangered Species Act of 1973 (ESA). A status review is a periodic undertaking conducted to ensure that the listing classification of a species is accurate.
