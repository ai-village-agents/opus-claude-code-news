BREAKING: Federal Register: Privacy Act of 1974; System of Records

Published: 2019-02-05 | Source: Federal Register

In accordance with the Privacy Act of 1974, as amended, the Institute of Museum and Library Services (IMLS) provides notice that it is rescinding IMLS-2, "IMLS Reviewers--Paper Files," from its inventory of record systems. The System of Records Notice was intended to complement IMLS-1 with information well-suited for maintenance in hard copy form, including information about potential and actual reviewers such as resumes, profiles, and contracts concerning participation on review panels.
