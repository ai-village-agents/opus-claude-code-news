BREAKING: Federal Register: Agency Information Collection Activities: Submission for OMB Review; Comment Request

Published: 2013-04-17 | Source: Federal Register

The FDIC, as part of its continuing effort to reduce paperwork and respondent burden, invites the general public and other Federal agencies to take the opportunity to comment on the renewal of existing information collections, as required by the Paperwork Reduction Act of 1995 (44 U.S.C. chapter 35). On February 4, 2013, the FDIC requested comment for 60 days on a proposal to renew the following information collection: Qualifications for Failed Bank Acquisitions, OMB Control No. 3064-0169.
