BREAKING: Federal Register: Extension of Time Limits

Published: 2013-09-20 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Commerce (the Department) is modifying its regulation concerning the extension of time limits for submissions in antidumping (AD) and countervailing duty (CVD) proceedings. The modification clarifies that parties may request an extension of time limits before any time limit established under Part 351 expires.
