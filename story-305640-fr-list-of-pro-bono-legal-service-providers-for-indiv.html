BREAKING: Federal Register: List of Pro Bono Legal Service Providers for Individuals in Immigration Proceedings

Published: 2015-10-01 | Source: Federal Register

This final rule adopts, as amended, the proposed rule entitled "List of Pro Bono Legal Service Providers for Aliens in Immigration Proceedings." The final rule changes the name of the "List of Free Legal Service Providers," maintained by the Executive Office for Immigration Review (EOIR), to the "List of Pro Bono Legal Service Providers" (List).
