BREAKING: Federal Register: Cancellation of Emergency Order Establishing Operating Limitations on the Use of Navigable Airspace

Published: 2025-11-19 | Source: Federal Register

This Order cancels the November 12, 2025, "Emergency Order Establishing Operating Limitations on the Use of Navigable Airspace," as amended by the November 14, 2025, Addendum, (November 12 Emergency Order) reducing and temporarily prohibiting certain operations in the navigable airspace to ensure the safety of aircraft and the efficiency of the National Airspace System (NAS). This Order cancels all requirements of the November 12 Emergency Order including to reduce operations at certain airports...
