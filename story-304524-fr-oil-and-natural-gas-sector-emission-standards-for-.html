BREAKING: Federal Register: Oil and Natural Gas Sector: Emission Standards for New and Modified Sources

Published: 2015-09-18 | Source: Federal Register

This action proposes to amend the new source performance standards (NSPS) for the oil and natural gas source category by setting standards for both methane and volatile organic compounds (VOC) for certain equipment, processes and activities across this source category.
