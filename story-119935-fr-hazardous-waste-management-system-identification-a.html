BREAKING: Federal Register: Hazardous Waste Management System: Identification and Listing of Hazardous Waste; Final Rule

Published: 2021-03-26 | Source: Federal Register

The EPA is removing an exclusion granting Watervliet Arsenal to delist the electroplating wastewater treatment sludges (EPA Hazardous Waste No. F006) generated by the Watervliet, New York facility from the lists of hazardous wastes. This action revises the final rule published on January 10, 1986.
