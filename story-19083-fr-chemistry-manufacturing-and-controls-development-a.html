BREAKING: Federal Register: Chemistry, Manufacturing, and Controls Development and Readiness Pilot Program; Program Announcement

Published: 2025-08-28 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA or Agency) is announcing year four of the Chemistry, Manufacturing, and Controls (CMC) Development and Readiness Pilot (CDRP). This program facilitates the expedited CMC development of products under an investigational new drug application (IND) based on the anticipated clinical benefit of earlier patient access to the products.
