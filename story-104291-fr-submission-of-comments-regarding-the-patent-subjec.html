BREAKING: Federal Register: Submission of Comments Regarding the Patent Subject Matter Eligibility Guidance

Published: 2022-09-01 | Source: Federal Register

The United States Patent and Trademark Office (USPTO) is seeking public feedback on its existing patent subject matter eligibility guidance. The existing guidance, put in place in 2019, has contributed to more consistent examination. But there is more work to be done to impart clarity and certainty into patent eligibility.
