BREAKING: Federal Register: Filing Requirements and Processing Procedures for Changes in Control With Respect to State Nonmember Banks and State Savings Associations

Published: 2015-10-28 | Source: Federal Register

On November 25, 2014, the FDIC published a notice of proposed rulemaking (proposed rule or NPR) to amend its filing requirements and processing procedures for notices filed under the Change in Bank Control Act (Notices). The comment period closed January 26, 2015, and no comments were received. The FDIC is now adopting that proposed rule as final with one change (final rule).
