BREAKING: Federal Register: Iprodione, Pendimethalin, and Permethrin; Order To Amend Pesticide Registrations To Terminate Uses

Published: 2014-08-13 | Source: Federal Register

EPA hereby orders, pursuant to the Federal Insecticide, Fungicide, and Rodenticide Act (FIFRA), that the registrations of pesticide products containing iprodione, pendimethalin, and permethrin be amended to terminate certain uses. This order follows a May 9, 2014 Federal Register Notice of Receipt that announced and sought comment on requests from the registrants to voluntarily amend their registrations to terminate certain uses of these product registrations.
