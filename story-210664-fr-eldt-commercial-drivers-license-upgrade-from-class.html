BREAKING: Federal Register: ELDT; Commercial Driver's License Upgrade From Class B to Class A

Published: 2018-06-29 | Source: Federal Register

FMCSA proposes to amend the entry-level driver training (ELDT) regulations published on December 8, 2016, titled "Minimum Training Requirements for Entry-Level Commercial Motor Vehicle Operators" by adopting a new Class A theory instruction upgrade curriculum to reduce the training time and costs incurred by Class B commercial driver's license (CDL) holders upgrading to a Class A CDL.
