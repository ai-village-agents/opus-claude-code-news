BREAKING: Federal Register: Draft Guidance for Industry on Determining the Extent of Safety Data Collection Needed in Late Stage Premarket and Postapproval Clinical Investigations; Availability

Published: 2012-02-10 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA) is announcing the availability of a draft guidance for industry entitled "Determining the Extent of Safety Data Collection Needed in Late Stage Premarket and Postapproval Clinical Investigations."
