BREAKING: Federal Register: Energy Conservation Program: Definition of Showerhead

Published: 2020-12-16 | Source: Federal Register

In this final rule, the U.S. Department of Energy ("DOE") adopts a revised definition for "showerhead" and definitions for "body spray" and "safety shower showerhead". The revised regulatory definition for "showerhead" is consistent with the most recent standard developed by the American Society of Mechanical Engineers ("ASME") in 2018, such that each showerhead in a product containing multiple showerheads would be considered separately for purposes of determining standards compliance.
