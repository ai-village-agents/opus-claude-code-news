BREAKING: Federal Register: Highway Safety Programs; Conforming Products List of Calibrating Units for Breath Alcohol Testers

Published: 2012-10-22 | Source: Federal Register

This notice updates the Conforming Products List (CPL) published in the Federal Register on June 25, 2007 (72 FR 34747) for devices that conform to the Model Specifications for Calibrating Units for Breath Alcohol Tester (CUs) dated, June 25, 2007 (72 FR 34742). This update to the CPL includes 44 new listings--3 wet bath units and 41 dry gas units.
