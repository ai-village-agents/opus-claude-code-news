BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Airbus Helicopters Deutschland GmbH Helicopters

Published: 2025-01-02 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA is adopting a new airworthiness directive (AD) for all Airbus Helicopters Deutschland GmbH Model MBB-BK 117 C-2 helicopters. The FAA previously sent this AD as an emergency AD to all known U.S. owners and operators of these helicopters. This AD was prompted by a report of vibrations of the yaw axis during a hover taxi.
