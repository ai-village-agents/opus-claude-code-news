BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone; Oregon Inlet, Dare County, NC

Published: 2017-12-05 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard proposes to establish a temporary safety zone on the navigable waters of Oregon Inlet in Dare County, North Carolina in support of construction of the new Herbert C. Bonner Bridge.
