BREAKING: Federal Register: Approval and Promulgation of Air Quality Implementation Plans; Delaware; Low Emission Vehicle Program

Published: 2015-10-14 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is taking direct final action to approve a revision to the Delaware State Implementation Plan (SIP). The SIP revision pertains to adoption by Delaware of a Low Emission Vehicle (LEV) Program. The Clean Air Act (CAA) grants authority to EPA to adopt Federal standards for emissions from new motor vehicles, and generally preempts states from doing so.
