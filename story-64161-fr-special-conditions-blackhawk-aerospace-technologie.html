BREAKING: Federal Register: Special Conditions: Blackhawk Aerospace Technologies, Textron Model 500 Series Airplanes; Rechargeable Lithium Batteries and Battery System Installations

Published: 2023-04-03 | Source: Federal Register

These special conditions are issued for the Textron Model 500/ 550/S550/560/560XL/560XLS airplanes. These airplanes, as modified by Blackhawk Aerospace Technologies (Blackhawk), will have a novel or unusual design feature when compared to the state of technology envisioned in the airworthiness standards for transport-category airplanes.
