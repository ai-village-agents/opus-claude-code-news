BREAKING: Federal Register: Multicriteria-Based Ranking Model for Risk Management of Animal Drug Residues in Milk and Milk Products; Extension of Comment Period

Published: 2015-07-30 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA or we) is extending the comment period for the risk assessment entitled "Multicriteria-Based Ranking Model for Risk Management of Animal Drug Residues in Milk and Milk Products." A notice of the availability of the risk assessment and our request for comments appeared in the Federal Register of April 30, 2015.
