BREAKING: Federal Register: Public Comment on EPA's National Compliance Initiatives for Fiscal Years 2020-2023

Published: 2019-02-08 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is soliciting public comment and recommendations on the National Compliance Initiatives (NCIs) to be undertaken in fiscal years 2020-2023. The EPA focuses enforcement and compliance resources on the most serious environmental violations by developing and implementing national program priorities, previously called National Enforcement Initiatives.
