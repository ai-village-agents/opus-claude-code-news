BREAKING: Federal Register: Proposed Amendment of Class E Airspace; Portland, OR

Published: 2015-07-20 | Source: Federal Register

This action proposes to modify Class E surface area airspace designated as an extension to the Class C airspace, and Class E airspace extending upward from 700 feet above the surface at Portland International Airport, Portland, OR.
