BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Centennial Challenges 3D-Printed Habitat Structural Member Challenge

Published: 2016-10-07 | Source: Federal Register

This notice is issued in accordance with 51 U.S.C. 20144(c). The 3D-Printed Habitat Challenge (3DP), Structural Member Competition is open and teams that wish to compete may now register. Centennial Challenges is a program of prize competitions to stimulate innovation in technologies of interest and value to NASA and the nation.
