BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Cancellation of Accreditation of St Laboratories Group, LLC, as a Commercial Laboratory

Published: 2014-08-13 | Source: Federal Register

Notice is hereby given, pursuant to CBP regulations, that CBP has cancelled the accreditation of ST Laboratories Group, LLC, to test petroleum and certain petroleum products for customs purposes as of June 20, 2014.
