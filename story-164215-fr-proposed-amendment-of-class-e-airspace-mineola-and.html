BREAKING: Federal Register: Proposed Amendment of Class E Airspace; Mineola and Kenedy, TX

Published: 2020-10-22 | Source: Federal Register

This action proposes to amend the Class E airspace extending upward from 700 feet above the surface at Mineola Wisener Field, Mineola, TX, and Kenedy Regional Airport, Kenedy, TX. The FAA is proposing this action as the result of airspace reviews caused by the decommissioning of the Quitman and Three Rivers VHF omnidirectional range (VOR) navigation aids as part of the VOR Minimum Operational Network (MON) Program.
