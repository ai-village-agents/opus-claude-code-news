BREAKING: Federal Register: Significant New Use Rules on Certain Chemical Substances (17-3)

Published: 2019-09-18 | Source: Federal Register

EPA is issuing significant new use rules (SNURs) under the Toxic Substances Control Act (TSCA) for 19 chemical substances which are the subject of premanufacture notices (PMNs). The chemical substances are subject to Orders issued by EPA pursuant to TSCA section 5(e).
