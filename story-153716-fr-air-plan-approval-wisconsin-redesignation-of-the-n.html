BREAKING: Federal Register: Air Plan Approval; Wisconsin; Redesignation of the Newport State Park Area in Door County to Attainment of the 2015 Ozone NAAQS

Published: 2020-06-10 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) finds that the Newport State Park area in Door County Wisconsin is attaining the 2015 ozone National Ambient Air Quality Standard (NAAQS or standard). EPA is acting in accordance with a request from the Wisconsin Department of Natural Resources (WDNR) to redesignate the area to attainment for the 2015 ozone NAAQS because the request meets the statutory requirements for redesignation under the Clean Air Act (CAA), which WDNR submitted on January 27, 2020....
