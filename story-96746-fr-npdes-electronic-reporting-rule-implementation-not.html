BREAKING: Federal Register: NPDES Electronic Reporting Rule Implementation: Notice of Initial Recipient Designation for Oregon Department of Environmental Quality

Published: 2022-05-25 | Source: Federal Register

This Federal Register document provides notice of the U.S. Environmental Protection Agency's (EPA) approval of the Oregon Department of Environmental Quality as the initial recipient for electronic data reporting under the NPDES Electronic Reporting Rule ("NPDES eRule"), effective December 6, 2021, for all National Pollutant Discharge Elimination System (NPDES) data groups except for "No. 4--Sewage Sludge/Biosolids Annual Program Reports."
