BREAKING: Federal Register: Proposed Amendment of E Airspace; Flippin, AR

Published: 2018-11-27 | Source: Federal Register

This action proposes to amend Class E airspace extending upward from 700 feet above the surface at Marion County Regional Airport, Flippin, AR, and Baxter County Airport, Mountain Home, AR, which is contained within the Flippin, AR, airspace legal description.
