BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; the Boeing Company Airplanes

Published: 2015-02-23 | Source: Federal Register

We are revising an earlier proposed airworthiness directive (AD) for certain The Boeing Company Model 757 airplanes. The NPRM proposed to require modifying the fuel quantity indication system (FQIS) wiring or fuel tank systems to prevent development of an ignition source inside the center fuel tank. The NPRM was prompted by fuel system reviews conducted by the manufacturer.
