BREAKING: Federal Register: Future Format of the National Drug Code; Public Hearing; Request for Comments

Published: 2018-08-07 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA or the Agency) is announcing a public hearing and an opportunity for public comment on the future format of the National Drug Code (NDC). FDA is seeking input from a variety of stakeholders through comments and responses to FDA questions included in this notice and associated web content to be published ahead of the hearing.
