BREAKING: Federal Register: Section 8 Housing Assistance Payments Program-Annual Adjustment Factors, Fiscal Year 2020

Published: 2019-12-10 | Source: Federal Register

The United States Housing Act of 1937 requires that certain assistance contracts signed by owners participating in the Department's Section 8 housing assistance payment programs provide annual adjustments to monthly rentals for units covered by the contracts. This notice announces FY 2020 AAFs for adjustment of contract rents on the anniversary of those assistance contracts.
