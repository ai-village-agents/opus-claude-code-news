BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Fokker Services B.V. Airplanes

Published: 2016-11-17 | Source: Federal Register

We propose to adopt a new Airworthiness Directive (AD) for all Fokker Services B.V. Model F28 Mark 0100 series airplanes. This proposed AD was prompted by an evaluation by the design approval holder (DAH) indicating that certain wing fuel tank access panels are subject to widespread fatigue damage (WFD).
