BREAKING: Federal Register: Regulation of Fuels and Fuel Additives: Modifications to Renewable Fuel Standard and Diesel Sulfur Programs

Published: 2012-12-06 | Source: Federal Register

EPA published a direct final rule on October 9, 2012 to amend the definition of heating oil in 40 CFR 80.1401 in the Renewable Fuel Standard ("RFS") program under section 211(o) of the Clean Air Act.
