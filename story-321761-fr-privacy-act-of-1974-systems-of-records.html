BREAKING: Federal Register: Privacy Act of 1974; Systems of Records

Published: 2014-04-15 | Source: Federal Register

The Federal Retirement Thrift Investment Board (Agency) proposes to add email addresses and phone numbers to the categories of records maintained in the system of records called FRTIB-1, Thrift Savings Plan Records. The Agency also proposes to change the system manager for FRTIB-1.
