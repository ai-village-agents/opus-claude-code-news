BREAKING: Federal Register: Remote Document Examination for Form I-9, Employment Eligibility Verification: Request for Public Input

Published: 2021-10-26 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Homeland Security (DHS) is seeking input from the public regarding document examination practices associated with the Form I-9, Employment Eligibility Verification. DHS solicits this input to better understand employers' and employees' experiences with this process and to examine the impacts of remote document examination conducted during the Coronavirus disease (COVID-19) pandemic.
