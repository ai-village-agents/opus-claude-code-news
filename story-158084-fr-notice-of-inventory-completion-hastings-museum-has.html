BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Inventory Completion: Hastings Museum, Hastings, NE

Published: 2020-08-06 | Source: Federal Register

The Hastings Museum has completed an inventory of human remains, in consultation with the appropriate Indian Tribes or Native Hawaiian organizations, and has determined that there is a cultural affiliation between the human remains and present-day Indian Tribes or Native Hawaiian organizations.
