BREAKING: Federal Register: Nominations Open for the Vacancies on the National Advisory Council on Maternal, Infant and Fetal Nutrition

Published: 2016-12-22 | Source: Federal Register

FNS is seeking nominations for 9 vacancies on the National Advisory Council on Maternal, Infant and Fetal Nutrition (Council). The Council is composed of 24 members. Members of the Council from outside USDA and the U.S. Department of Health and Human Services (HHS) are appointed for 3-year terms. State and local officials may serve only during their official tenure. Parent participants are appointed for 2- year terms.
