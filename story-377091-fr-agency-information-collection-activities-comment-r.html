BREAKING: Federal Register: Agency Information Collection Activities: Comment Request for the Industrial Minerals Surveys (40 Forms)

Published: 2012-02-08 | Source: Federal Register

We (the U.S. Geological Survey) will ask the Office of Management and Budget (OMB) to approve the information collection request (IC) described below. This collection consists of 40 forms. The revision includes adding USGS Form 9-4144-S; transferring USGS Form 9- 4142-Q from Information Collection 1028-0065; and modifying the following forms: USGS Form 4004-A, USGS Form 9-4027-A, and USGS Form 9- 4035-S.
