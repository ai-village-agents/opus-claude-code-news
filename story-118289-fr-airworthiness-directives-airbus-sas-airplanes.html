BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Airbus SAS Airplanes

Published: 2021-03-05 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA is adopting a new airworthiness directive (AD) for certain Airbus SAS Model A350-941 and A350-1041 airplanes. This AD was prompted by reports that suitable corrosion protection treatment had not been applied to certain areas of the seat track.
