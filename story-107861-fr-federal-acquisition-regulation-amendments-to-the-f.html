BREAKING: Federal Register: Federal Acquisition Regulation: Amendments to the FAR Buy American Act Requirements; Correction

Published: 2022-10-18 | Source: Federal Register

DoD, GSA, and NASA are issuing a correction to FAC 2022-05; FAR Case 2021-008; Amendments to the FAR Buy American Act Requirements; which published in the Federal Register at 87 FR 12780, on March 7, 2022. This correction makes an editorial change to correct amendatory instruction 21.b. to section 52.212-3.
