BREAKING: Federal Register: Financial Crimes Enforcement Network; Withdrawal of Finding Regarding JSC CredexBank

Published: 2016-03-17 | Source: Federal Register

This document withdraws FinCEN's finding that JSC CredexBank ("Credex"), renamed JSC InterPayBank ("InterPay"), is a financial institution of primary money laundering concern, pursuant to Section 311 of the USA PATRIOT Act ("Section 311").
