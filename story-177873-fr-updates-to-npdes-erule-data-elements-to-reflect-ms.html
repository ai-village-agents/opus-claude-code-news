BREAKING: Federal Register: Updates to NPDES eRule Data Elements To Reflect MS4 General Permit Remand Rule

Published: 2019-04-30 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is proposing to update specific data elements within the National Pollutant Discharge Elimination System (NPDES) Electronic Reporting Rule (NPDES eRule) published on October 22, 2015 (80 FR 64064), that apply to regulated municipal separate storm sewer systems (MS4s).
