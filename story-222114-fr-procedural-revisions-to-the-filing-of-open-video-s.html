BREAKING: Federal Register: Procedural Revisions to the Filing of Open Video System Certification Applications

Published: 2018-11-28 | Source: Federal Register

In this document, the Federal Communications Commission (FCC or Commission) modernizes the Open Video System (OVS) filing procedures by specifying that OVS applications be required to send certification applications, including FCC Form 1275 and all attachments, as well as notices of intent, via electronic email (email) delivery to a designated Commission email address.
