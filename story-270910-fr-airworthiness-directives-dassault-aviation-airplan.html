BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Dassault Aviation Airplanes

Published: 2016-08-10 | Source: Federal Register

We are superseding Airworthiness Directive (AD) 2011-10-01 for all Dassault Aviation Model FALCON 7X airplanes. AD 2011-10-01 required repetitive functional tests of the ram air turbine (RAT) heater, and repair if necessary. This new AD requires revision of the maintenance or inspection program to incorporate new maintenance requirements and airworthiness limitations.
