BREAKING: Federal Register: Commercial Mail Receiving Agencies

Published: 2023-01-12 | Source: Federal Register

The Postal Service is proposing to amend Mailing Standards of the United States Postal Service, Domestic Mail Manual (DMM[supreg]) section 508.1.8, to update the regulations concerning Commercial Mail Receiving Agencies (CMRAs).
