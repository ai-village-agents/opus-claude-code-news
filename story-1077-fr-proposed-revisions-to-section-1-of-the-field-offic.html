BREAKING: Federal Register: Proposed Revisions to Section 1 of the Field Office Technical Guide for Kansas, Nebraska, and New Jersey

Published: 2026-01-28 | Source: Federal Register

NRCS is giving notice that it is proposing to revise Section 1--General Resource References of the Field Office Technical Guide for Kansas, Nebraska, and New Jersey to include State Off-Site Methods (SOSM) for Food Security Act Wetland Identification. The proposed changes will replace existing procedures in Kansas and Nebraska, and add new procedures in New Jersey.
