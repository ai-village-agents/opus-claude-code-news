BREAKING: Federal Register: Award Management Requirements Circular (C 5010.1) Proposed Updates

Published: 2024-02-14 | Source: Federal Register

The Federal Transit Administration (FTA) is seeking public comment on proposed updates to FTA's Award Management Requirements circular (C 5010.1).
