BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Intent To Repatriate Cultural Items: Milwaukee Public Museum, Milwaukee, WI

Published: 2022-11-18 | Source: Federal Register

In accordance with the Native American Graves Protection and Repatriation Act (NAGPRA), the Milwaukee Public Museum intends to repatriate a cultural item that meets the definitions of both a sacred object and an object of cultural patrimony and that has a cultural affiliation with the Indian Tribes or Native Hawaiian organizations in this notice. The cultural item was obtained in WI.
