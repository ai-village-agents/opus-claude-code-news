BREAKING: Federal Register: Submission for Review: Reinstatement of a Previously Approved Information Collection Without Change, Standard Form 2812, 2812-A, and OPM Form 1523

Published: 2018-10-10 | Source: Federal Register

The Office of Personnel Management (OPM) offers the general public and other federal agencies the opportunity to comment on a revised information collection request (ICR) for Standard Form 2812, 2812-A and OPM Form 1523. The information collection was previously published in the Federal Register on February 21, 2018 at Volume # 83 FR 7504 allowing for a 60-day public comment period.
