BREAKING: Federal Register: Exempting In-Home Video Telehealth From Copayments

Published: 2012-03-06 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Veterans Affairs (VA) is taking final action to amend its regulation that governs VA services that are not subject to copayment requirements for inpatient hospital care or outpatient medical care. Specifically, the regulation is amended to exempt in-home video telehealth care from having any required copayment.
