BREAKING: Federal Register: Transfers of Administrative Jurisdiction, Camp Frank D. Merrill and Lake Lanier, Georgia

Published: 2015-08-20 | Source: Federal Register

National Defense Authorization Act for Fiscal Year 2015, ordered transfers of two properties: Camp Frank D. Merrill from the Secretary of Agriculture to the Secretary of the Army and the Lake Lanier Property from the Secretary of the Army to the Secretary of Agriculture, as well as publication of the maps and legal description in the Federal Register. This notice provides the required maps and legal description.
