BREAKING: Federal Register: Modification of Class D and Class E Airspace and Establishment of Class E Airspace; Camarillo, CA

Published: 2022-08-24 | Source: Federal Register

This action modifies the Class E airspace, designated as an extension to a Class D or Class E surface area, at Camarillo Airport, Camarillo, CA. This action also removes the Camarillo very high frequency omnidirectional range (VOR)/distance measuring equipment (DME) from the airspace's legal description.
