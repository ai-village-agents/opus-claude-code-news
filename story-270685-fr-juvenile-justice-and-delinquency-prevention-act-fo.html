BREAKING: Federal Register: Juvenile Justice and Delinquency Prevention Act Formula Grant Program

Published: 2016-08-08 | Source: Federal Register

The Office of Justice Programs ("OJP") proposes to update the implementing regulation for the Formula Grant Program authorized by Title II, Part B, of the Juvenile Justice and Delinquency Prevention Act of 1974 ("the Act" or "JJDPA"). The purpose of the Formula Grant Program is to provide formula grant awards to states to support juvenile delinquency prevention programs and to improve their juvenile justice systems.
