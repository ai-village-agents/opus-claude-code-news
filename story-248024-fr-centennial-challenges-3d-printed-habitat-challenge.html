BREAKING: Federal Register: Centennial Challenges: 3D-Printed Habitat Challenge

Published: 2017-11-06 | Source: Federal Register

The 3D-Printed Habitat (3DPH) Challenge Phase 3 On-Site Habitat Competition is open, and teams that wish to compete may now register. Centennial Challenges is a program of prize competitions to stimulate innovation in technologies of interest and value to NASA and the nation.
