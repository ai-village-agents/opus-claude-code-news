BREAKING: Federal Register: Facilitating Shared Use in the 3.1-3.55 GHz Band

Published: 2020-01-22 | Source: Federal Register

In this document, a Notice of Proposed Rulemaking (NPRM) proposes to remove the existing non-federal secondary radiolocation and amateur allocations in the 3.3-3.55 GHz band and to relocate incumbent non-federal operations out of the band, in order to prepare the band for possible expanded commercial wireless use.
