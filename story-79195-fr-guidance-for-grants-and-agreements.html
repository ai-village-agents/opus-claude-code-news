BREAKING: Federal Register: Guidance for Grants and Agreements

Published: 2023-10-05 | Source: Federal Register

The Office of Management and Budget (OMB) is proposing to revise sections of OMB Guidance for Grants and Agreements. This proposed revision reflects comments received from Federal agencies and those received in response to the OMB Notice of Request for Information published in the Federal Register in February 2023.
