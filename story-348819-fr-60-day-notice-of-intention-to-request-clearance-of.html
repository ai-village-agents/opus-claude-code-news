BREAKING: Federal Register: 60-Day Notice of Intention To Request Clearance of Collection of Information; Opportunity for Public Comment

Published: 2013-03-07 | Source: Federal Register

We (National Park Service) will ask the Office of Management and Budget (OMB) to approve the information collection (IC) described below. To comply with the Paperwork Reduction Act of 1995 and as a part of our continuing efforts to reduce paperwork and respondent burden, we invite the general public and other Federal agencies to comment on this IC. We may not conduct or sponsor and a person is not required to respond to a collection unless it displays a currently valid OMB control number.
