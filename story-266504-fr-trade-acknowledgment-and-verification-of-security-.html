BREAKING: Federal Register: Trade Acknowledgment and Verification of Security-Based Swap Transactions

Published: 2016-06-17 | Source: Federal Register

In accordance with Section 764(a) of Title VII of the Dodd- Frank Wall Street Reform and Consumer Protection Act of 2010 ("Dodd- Frank Act"), the Securities and Exchange Commission ("Commission") is adopting Rules 15Fi-1 and 15Fi-2 under the Securities Exchange Act of 1934 ("Exchange Act") requiring security-based swap dealers and major security-based swap participants to provide trade acknowledgments and to verify those trade acknowledgments in security-based swap transactions.
