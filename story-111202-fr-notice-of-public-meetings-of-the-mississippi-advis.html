BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Public Meetings of the Mississippi Advisory Committee to the U.S. Commission on Civil Rights

Published: 2022-11-29 | Source: Federal Register

Notice is hereby given, pursuant to the provisions of the rules and regulations of the U.S. Commission on Civil Rights (Commission) and the Federal Advisory Committee Act that the Mississippi Advisory Committee (Committee) will hold a meeting on Monday, December 12, 2022 at 11 a.m.-12 p.m. Central time. The Committee will continue identifying potential civil rights topics for their first study of the 2021-2025 term.
