BREAKING: Federal Register: Promoting Spectrum Access for Wireless Microphone Operations

Published: 2017-09-01 | Source: Federal Register

In this document, the Commission proposes to permit professional theater, music, performing arts, or similar organizations that operate wireless microphones on an unlicensed basis and that meet certain criteria to obtain a license to operate in the TV bands (and the 600 MHz service band during the post-auction transition period), thereby allowing them to register in the white spaces databases for interference protection from unlicensed white space devices at venues where their events/productions...
