BREAKING: Federal Register: Disadvantaged Business Enterprise: Program Implementation Modifications

Published: 2012-09-06 | Source: Federal Register

This notice of proposed rulemaking (NPRM) proposes three categories of changes to improve implementation of the Department of Transportation's disadvantaged business enterprise (DBE) rule. First, the NPRM proposes revisions to personal net worth, application, and reporting forms. Second, the NPRM proposes modifications to certification-related provisions of the rule.
