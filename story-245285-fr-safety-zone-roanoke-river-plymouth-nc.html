BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone; Roanoke River, Plymouth, NC

Published: 2017-10-03 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is establishing a temporary safety zone for one mile of navigable waters of the Roanoke River in Plymouth, North Carolina. This temporary safety zone is intended to restrict vessel traffic from a portion of the Roanoke River during the Virginia Outlaw Drag Boat Association End of the Year Showdown high speed boat race.
