BREAKING: Federal Register: Announcement of Requirements and Registration for “Health Design Challenge”

Published: 2012-10-22 | Source: Federal Register

Blue Button for America is a collaborative Federal effort led by the Department of Health and Human Services and the Department of Veterans Affairs to ensure everyone across the country gets access to their medical records. By clicking on a Blue Button icon, patients can get their personal health information in an electronic format--a service that has not been available to most people until very recently.
