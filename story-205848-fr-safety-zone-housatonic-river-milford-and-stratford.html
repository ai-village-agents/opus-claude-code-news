BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone; Housatonic River, Milford and Stratford, CT

Published: 2018-05-01 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is establishing a temporary safety zone for certain waters of the Housatonic River. This action is necessary to provide for the safety of life on these navigable waters near Milford and Stratford, CT, during a wire replacement project on the Devon Railroad Bridge. Entry of vessels or people into the safety zone is prohibited unless authorized by the Captain of the Port Long Island Sound or a designated representative.
