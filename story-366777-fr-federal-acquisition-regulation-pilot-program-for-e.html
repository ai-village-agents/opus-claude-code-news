BREAKING: Federal Register: Federal Acquisition Regulation; Pilot Program for Enhancement of Contractor Employee Whistleblower Protections

Published: 2013-09-30 | Source: Federal Register

DoD, GSA, and NASA are issuing an interim rule amending the Federal Acquisition Regulation (FAR) to implement a statutory pilot program enhancing whistleblower protections for contractor employees.

Agency: Defense Department, General Services Administration, National Aeronautics and Space Administration
