BREAKING: Federal Register: Privacy Act of 1974; System of Records

Published: 2024-03-29 | Source: Federal Register

Pursuant to the provisions of the Privacy Act of 1974, notice is given that the General Services Administration (GSA) proposes to rescind a System of Records Notice, GSA/PPFM-10, Purchase Card Program. This system of records provides control over expenditure of funds through the use of Federal Government purchase cards.
