BREAKING: Federal Register: Accreditation and Approval of Intertek USA, Inc., as a Commercial Gauger and Laboratory

Published: 2012-03-09 | Source: Federal Register

Notice is hereby given that, pursuant to 19 CFR 151.12 and 19 CFR 151.13, Intertek USA, Inc., 5401 Evergreen Ave., Jacksonville, FL 32208, has been approved to gauge and accredited to test petroleum and petroleum products, organic chemicals and vegetable oils for customs purposes, in accordance with the provisions of 19 CFR 151.12 and 19 CFR 151.13.
