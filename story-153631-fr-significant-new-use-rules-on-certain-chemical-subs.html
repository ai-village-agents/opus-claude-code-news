BREAKING: Federal Register: Significant New Use Rules on Certain Chemical Substances (19-4.B)

Published: 2020-06-09 | Source: Federal Register

EPA is issuing significant new use rules (SNURs) under the Toxic Substances Control Act (TSCA) for chemical substances which are the subject of premanufacture notices (PMNs). This action requires persons to notify EPA at least 90 days before commencing manufacture (defined by statute to include import) or processing of any of these chemical substances for an activity that is designated as a significant new use by this rule.
