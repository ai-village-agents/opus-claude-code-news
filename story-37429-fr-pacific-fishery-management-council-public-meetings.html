BREAKING: Federal Register: Pacific Fishery Management Council; Public Meetings

Published: 2024-05-21 | Source: Federal Register

The Pacific Fishery Management Council (Council) and its advisory bodies will meet June 7-13, 2024 in San Diego, CA and via webinar. The Council meeting will be live streamed with the opportunity to provide public comment remotely.
