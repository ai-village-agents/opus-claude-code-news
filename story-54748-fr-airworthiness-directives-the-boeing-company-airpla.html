BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; The Boeing Company Airplanes

Published: 2024-12-09 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA is adopting a new airworthiness directive (AD) for certain The Boeing Company Model MD-11 and MD-11F airplanes. This AD was prompted by a report of a Model MD-11F airplane experiencing an uncommanded deployment of a thrust reverser in flight at low altitude.
