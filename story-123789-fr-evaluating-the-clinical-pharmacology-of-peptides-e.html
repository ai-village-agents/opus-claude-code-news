BREAKING: Federal Register: Evaluating the Clinical Pharmacology of Peptides; Establishment of a Public Docket; Request for Information and Comments

Published: 2021-05-14 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA or Agency) is establishing a public docket to collect comments on evaluating the clinical pharmacology of peptides. For the purpose of this request, FDA is specifically interested in comments regarding the characterization of the effects of hepatic impairment, drug-drug interactions, and immunogenicity on the pharmacokinetics of peptides, as well as the effects of peptides on cardiac electrophysiology.
