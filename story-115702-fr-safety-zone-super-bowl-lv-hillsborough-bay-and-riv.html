BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone; Super Bowl LV; Hillsborough Bay and River, Tampa, FL

Published: 2021-02-01 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is establishing a temporary safety zone on certain waters of Garrison Channel, Seddon Channel Turning Basin, and the Hillsborough River, in the vicinity of downtown Tampa, Florida during the Super Bowl LV celebrations. The safety zone is necessary to protect the public and Super Bowl LV event personnel from the hazards associated with potential vessel traffic within the area of the safety zone.
