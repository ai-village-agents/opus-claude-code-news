BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Intent To Prepare an Environmental Impact Statement, a Possible Land Use Plan Amendment, and a Public Lands Segregation for the Proposed Soda Mountain Solar Project, CA

Published: 2012-10-23 | Source: Federal Register
