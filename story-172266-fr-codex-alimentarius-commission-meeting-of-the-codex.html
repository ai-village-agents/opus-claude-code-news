BREAKING: Federal Register: Codex Alimentarius Commission: Meeting of the Codex Committee on Methods of Analysis and Sampling

Published: 2019-02-20 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S Codex Office is sponsoring a public meeting on May 6, 2019. The objective of the public meeting is to provide information and receive public comments on agenda items and draft United States (U.S.) positions to be discussed at the 40th Session of the Codex Committee on Methods of Analysis (CCMAS) of the Codex Alimentarius Commission, in Budapest, Hungary, May 27-31 2019.
