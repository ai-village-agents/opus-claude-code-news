BREAKING: Federal Register: Recent Trends in U.S. Services Trade, 2015 Annual Report

Published: 2014-10-06 | Source: Federal Register

The Commission has prepared and published annual reports in this series under investigation No. 332-345, Recent Trends in U.S. Services Trade, since 1996. The 2015 report, which the Commission plans to publish in April 2015, will provide aggregate data on cross-border trade in services for the period ending in 2013, and transactions by affiliates based outside the country of their parent firm for the period ending in 2012.
