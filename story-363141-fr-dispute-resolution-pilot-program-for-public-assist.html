BREAKING: Federal Register: Dispute Resolution Pilot Program for Public Assistance Appeals

Published: 2013-08-16 | Source: Federal Register

Section 1105 of the Sandy Recovery Improvement Act of 2013 directs FEMA to establish a nationwide Dispute Resolution Pilot Program (DRPP) in order to facilitate an efficient recovery from major disasters, including arbitration by an independent review panel, to resolve disputes relating to Public Assistance projects.
