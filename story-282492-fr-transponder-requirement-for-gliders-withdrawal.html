BREAKING: Federal Register: Transponder Requirement for Gliders; Withdrawal

Published: 2016-12-23 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA is withdrawing a previously published advance notice of proposed rulemaking that sought public comment from interested persons involving glider operations in the National Airspace System.
