BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Funds Availability (NOFA); Market Facilitation Program (MFP) Payments to Producers

Published: 2018-08-30 | Source: Federal Register

MFP provides payments to producers with commodities that have been significantly impacted by actions of foreign governments resulting in the loss of traditional exports. This NOFA announces the availability of MFP funds for eligible producers of the following commodities for 2018: Soybeans, sorghum, wheat, extra long staple (ELS) cotton, upland cotton, corn, hogs, and milk.
