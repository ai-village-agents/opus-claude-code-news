BREAKING: Federal Register: Drawbridge Operation Regulations; Bayou Lafourche, LA

Published: 2019-03-22 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is issuing a temporary deviation to the operating schedule that regulates six drawbridges across Bayou Lafourche, Lafourche Parish, Louisiana. These drawbridges are located south of the Gulf Intracoastal Waterway and cross Bayou Lafourche at miles 38.7, 36.3, 33.9, 30.6, 27.8 and 23.9.
