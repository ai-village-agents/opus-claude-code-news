BREAKING: Federal Register: Stillaguamish Tribe of Indians-Liquor Control Ordinance

Published: 2012-06-12 | Source: Federal Register

This notice publishes the Stillaguamish Tribe of Indians' Liquor Control Ordinance. The Ordinance regulates and controls the possession, sale and consumption of liquor within the Stillaguamish Tribe of Indians' Indian country.
