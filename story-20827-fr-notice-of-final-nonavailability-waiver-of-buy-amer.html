BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Final Nonavailability Waiver of Buy American Act Requirements: Port of Corpus Christi Authority To Purchase Marine Loading Arms

Published: 2025-09-22 | Source: Federal Register

MARAD is issuing a limited waiver of the Buy American Act requirements for non-domestic marine loading arms for the Port of Corpus Christi Authority's (PCCA) Avery Point Public Oil Docks Redevelopment--Phase 1: Expanding Oil Dock 3 project, which received funding under the FY 2019 Port Infrastructure Development Program (PIDP).
