BREAKING: Federal Register: Topical Dermatologic Corticosteroids: In Vivo Bioequivalence; Draft Guidance for Industry; Availability

Published: 2022-10-24 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA or Agency) is announcing the availability of a draft guidance for industry entitled "Topical Dermatologic Corticosteroids: In Vivo Bioequivalence." This draft guidance is intended to assist applicants who submit abbreviated new drug applications (ANDAs) for topical dermatologic corticosteroid products of all potency groups (referred to in this notice as topical corticosteroids).
