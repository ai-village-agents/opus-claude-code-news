BREAKING: Federal Register: Atlantic Coastal Fisheries Cooperative Management Act Provisions;

Published: 2019-04-16 | Source: Federal Register

The Assistant Regional Administrator for Sustainable Fisheries, Greater Atlantic Region, NMFS, has made a preliminary determination that an Exempted Fishing Permit application from the Commercial Fisheries Research Foundation contains all of the required information and warrants further consideration. This permit would facilitate research on the impact of increasing water temperature on female lobster size at maturity and growth.
