BREAKING: Federal Register: Proposed Waiver and Extension of the Project Period for the Native Hawaiian Career and Technical Education Program

Published: 2015-02-10 | Source: Federal Register

For the Native Hawaiian Career and Technical Education Program (NHCTEP), the Secretary proposes to waive the requirements that generally prohibit project period extensions involving the obligation of additional Federal funds and extend the project periods of the seven current NHCTEP grants for up to an additional 24 months.
