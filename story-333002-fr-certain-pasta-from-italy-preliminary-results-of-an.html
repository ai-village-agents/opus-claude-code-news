BREAKING: Federal Register: Certain Pasta From Italy: Preliminary Results of Antidumping Duty Administrative Review and Partial Rescission; 2012-2013

Published: 2014-08-25 | Source: Federal Register

In response to requests from interested parties, the Department of Commerce (the Department) is conducting an administrative review of the antidumping duty order on certain pasta (pasta) from Italy,\1\ covering the period July 1, 2012, through June 30, 2013. The review covers 10 companies, two of which are mandatory respondents: Rummo S.p.A.
