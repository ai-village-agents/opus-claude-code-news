BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Removal of Designated Chinese Military Companies

Published: 2024-10-23 | Source: Federal Register

The Deputy Secretary of Defense has decided to delist the entity listed in the SUPPLEMENTARY INFORMATION section of this notice from the list of "Chinese military companies" in accordance with the William M. (Mac) Thornberry National Defense Authorization Act for Fiscal Year 2021, without prejudice to relisting the company on additional or supplementary grounds.
