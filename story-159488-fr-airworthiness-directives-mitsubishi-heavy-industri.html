BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Mitsubishi Heavy Industries, Ltd. Airplanes

Published: 2020-08-25 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA proposes to supersede Airworthiness Directive (AD) 75- 16-20, which applies to all Mitsubishi Model MU-2B, MU-2B-10, MU-2B-15, MU-2B-20, MU-2B-25, MU-2B-26, MU-2B-30, MU-2B-35, and MU-2B-36 airplanes. AD 75-16-20 requires repetitive inspections of the propeller pitch control (PPC) lever for security and proper rigging.
