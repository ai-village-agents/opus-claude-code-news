BREAKING: Federal Register: Air Plan Approval; Missouri; Measurement of Emissions of Air Contaminants

Published: 2019-04-12 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is proposing approval of revisions to the Missouri State Implementation Plan (SIP) received by EPA on December 11, 2018. The submission revises Missouri's regulation relating to measurement of emissions of air contaminants which allows the director to obtain air contaminant emission data upon request.
