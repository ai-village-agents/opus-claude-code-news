BREAKING: Federal Register: Special Conditions: Airbus, Model A350-900 Series Airplane; Crew Rest Compartments

Published: 2014-01-14 | Source: Federal Register

These amended special conditions are issued for Airbus Model A350-900 series airplanes. Notice of proposed special conditions, request for comments, for crew rest compartments of the A350-900 were published on March 30, 2012 in the Federal Register [Docket No. FAA- 2012-0343; Notice No. 25-460-SC]. The comment period closed May 14, 2012. Comments were received. In response to an August 1, 2013 letter from Airbus, the wording of the special conditions was revised.
