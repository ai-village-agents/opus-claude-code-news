BREAKING: Federal Register: Notification of a Public Teleconference of the Science Advisory Board; Exposure and Human Health Committee

Published: 2012-08-28 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) Science Advisory Board (SAB) Staff Office announces a public teleconference of the SAB Exposure and Human Health Committee to discuss its draft report concerning EPA's application of computational toxicology (CompTox) data in risk assessment.
