BREAKING: Federal Register: Amendment to Securities Transaction Settlement Cycle

Published: 2016-10-05 | Source: Federal Register

The Securities and Exchange Commission ("Commission") proposes to amend Rule 15c6-1(a) under the Securities Exchange Act of 1934 ("Exchange Act") to shorten the standard settlement cycle for most broker-dealer transactions from three business days after the trade date ("T+3") to two business days after the trade date ("T+2").
