BREAKING: Federal Register: Designation of Areas for Air Quality Planning Purposes; State of Arizona; Pinal County and Gila County; Pb; Correction

Published: 2014-09-24 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is correcting a final rule that appeared in the Federal Register of September 3, 2014 (79 FR 52205). The rule redesignated the Hayden area, which encompasses portions of southern Gila and eastern Pinal counties, Arizona, from "unclassifiable" to "nonattainment" for the 2008 national ambient air quality standards ("NAAQS" or "standards") for lead (Pb).
