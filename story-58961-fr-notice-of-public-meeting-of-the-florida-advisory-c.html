BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Public Meeting of the Florida Advisory Committee to the U.S. Commission on Civil Rights

Published: 2023-01-26 | Source: Federal Register

Notice is hereby given, pursuant to the provisions of the rules and regulations of the U.S. Commission on Civil Rights (Commission) and the Federal Advisory Committee Act, that the Florida Advisory Committee (Committee) to the U.S. Commission on Civil Rights will hold a virtual business meeting via Zoom at 12 p.m. ET on Monday, February 6, 2023, to review its draft report on voting rights in the state.
