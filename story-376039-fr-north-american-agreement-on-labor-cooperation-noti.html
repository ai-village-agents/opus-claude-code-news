BREAKING: Federal Register: North American Agreement on Labor Cooperation Notice of Determination Regarding Review of Submission #2011-02

Published: 2012-01-27 | Source: Federal Register

The Office of Trade and Labor Affairs (OTLA) gives notice that on January 13, 2012, Submission 2011-02 was accepted for review pursuant to Article 16.3 of the North American Agreement on Labor Cooperation (NAALC). The Sindicato Mexicano de Electricistas (SME), a Mexican union, filed the submission with OTLA on November 14, 2011. The SME also filed the submission on behalf of 93 other organizations.
