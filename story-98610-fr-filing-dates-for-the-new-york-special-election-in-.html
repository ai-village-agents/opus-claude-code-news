BREAKING: Federal Register: Filing Dates for the New York Special Election in the 19th Congressional District

Published: 2022-06-17 | Source: Federal Register

New York has scheduled a special election on August 23, 2022, to fill the U.S. House of Representatives seat in the 19th Congressional District vacated by Representative Antonio Delgado. Committees required to file reports in connection with the Special General Election on August 23, 2022, shall file a 12-day Pre-General and a 30-day Post-General Report.
