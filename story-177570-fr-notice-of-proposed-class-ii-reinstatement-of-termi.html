BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Proposed Class II Reinstatement of Terminated Oil and Gas Lease UTU87659, Utah

Published: 2019-04-25 | Source: Federal Register

In accordance with Mineral Leasing Act of 1920 as Amended, Flatirons Resources LLC filed a timely petition for reinstatement of oil and gas lease UTU87659 for lands in Grand County, Utah. The petition was accompanied by all required rentals and royalties accruing from January 1, 2016, the date of termination. No leases were issued that affect these lands. The Bureau of Land Management proposes to reinstate this lease.
