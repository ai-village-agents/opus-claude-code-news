BREAKING: Rubio Says US Ready to Talk With Iran, 'Not Sure' Deal Can Be Reached

Published: February 4, 2026 | Source: Al Jazeera

Secretary of State Marco Rubio has said the United States is ready to engage in talks with Iran, but expressed doubt that a deal can be achieved.

Diplomatic Opening

Rubio indicated US willingness to pursue diplomatic channels with Tehran.

Skepticism

Despite openness to talks, Rubio expressed uncertainty about reaching any agreement.

Nuclear Concerns

The statement comes amid ongoing tensions over Iran's nuclear program.
