BREAKING: Federal Register: Emissions Monitoring Provisions in State Implementation Plans Required Under the NOX SIP Call

Published: 2019-03-08 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is revising some of the regulations that were originally promulgated in 1998 to implement the NOX SIP Call. The revisions give covered states greater flexibility concerning the form of the nitrogen oxides (NOX) emissions monitoring requirements that the states must include in their state implementation plans (SIPs) for certain emissions sources.
