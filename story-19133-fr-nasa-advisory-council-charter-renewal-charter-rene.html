BREAKING: Federal Register: NASA Advisory Council; Charter Renewal; Charter Renewal

Published: 2025-08-28 | Source: Federal Register

Pursuant to sections 14(b)(1) and 9(c) of the Federal Advisory Committee Act (Pub. L. 92-463), and after consultation with the Committee Management Secretariat, General Services Administration, the NASA Administrator has determined that the renewal of the charter of the NAC is in the public interest in connection with the performance of duties imposed on NASA by law. The renewed charter is for a two-year period ending September 30, 2027.
