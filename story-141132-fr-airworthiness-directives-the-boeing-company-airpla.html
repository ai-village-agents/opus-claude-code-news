BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; The Boeing Company Airplanes

Published: 2021-12-28 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA is adopting a new airworthiness directive (AD) for all The Boeing Company Model MD-11 and MD-11F airplanes. This AD was prompted by reports indicating incidents of wires chafing against the inboard upper corner of the observer station circuit breaker panel.
