BREAKING: Federal Register: Availability of Information Under the Freedom of Information Act

Published: 2024-02-22 | Source: Federal Register

The Office of the Comptroller of the Currency (OCC) is proposing to amend its Freedom of Information Act (FOIA) regulations. The proposal would amend the OCC's FOIA regulations to provide for expedited processing of FOIA requests and establish procedures for requestors to appeal denials of expedited processing and fee waiver requests.
