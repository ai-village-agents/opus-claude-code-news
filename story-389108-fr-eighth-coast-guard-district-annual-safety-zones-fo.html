BREAKING: Federal Register: Eighth Coast Guard District Annual Safety Zones; Fourth of July Celebration; Santa Rosa Sound; Fort Walton Beach, FL

Published: 2012-06-21 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard will enforce a Safety Zone for the Fourth of July Celebration in the Santa Rosa Sound, Fort Walton Beach, Florida from 9 p.m. until 10 p.m. on July 4, 2012. This action is necessary for the safeguard of participants and spectators, including all crews, vessels, and persons on navigable waters during the Fourth of July Celebration.
