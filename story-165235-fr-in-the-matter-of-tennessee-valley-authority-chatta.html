BREAKING: Federal Register: In the Matter of Tennessee Valley Authority, Chattanooga, TN

Published: 2020-11-04 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Nuclear Regulatory Commission (NRC) is issuing an Order Imposing Civil Penalty in the amount of $606,942 to Tennessee Valley Authority (TVA). The NRC determined that four violations of NRC's employee protection regulation occurred as identified during two investigations completed on October 3, 2019, and January 21, 2020, by the NRC's Office of Investigations (OI) relating to activities at the TVA.
