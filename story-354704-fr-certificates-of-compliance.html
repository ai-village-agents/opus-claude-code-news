BREAKING: Federal Register: Certificates of Compliance

Published: 2013-05-13 | Source: Federal Register

The United States Consumer Product Safety Commission (Commission, CPSC, or we) is issuing a proposed rule that would amend the existing regulation on certificates of compliance. The proposed amendment is intended to update the rule to clarify requirements in light of new regulations on testing and labeling pertaining to product certification, and component part testing.
