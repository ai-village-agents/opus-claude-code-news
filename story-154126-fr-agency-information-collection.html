BREAKING: Federal Register: Agency Information Collection

Published: 2020-06-17 | Source: Federal Register

EIA reinstates the Residential Energy Consumption Survey (RECS), with changes, under OMB Control Number 1905-0092, as required by the Paperwork Reduction Act of 1995. RECS collects data on energy characteristics, consumption, and expenditures for the residential sector of the United States.
