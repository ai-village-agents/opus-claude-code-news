BREAKING: Federal Register: Special Conditions: Textron Aviation Inc. Model 700 Airplane; Operation Without Normal Electrical Power

Published: 2018-12-06 | Source: Federal Register

These special conditions are issued for the Textron Aviation Inc. (Textron) Model 700 airplane. This airplane will have a novel or unusual design feature when compared to the state of technology envisioned in the airworthiness standards for transport-category airplanes. These design features are electrical and electronic systems that perform critical functions, the loss of which could be catastrophic to the airplane.
