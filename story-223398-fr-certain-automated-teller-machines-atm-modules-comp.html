BREAKING: Federal Register: Certain Automated Teller Machines, ATM Modules, Components Thereof, and Products Containing the Same; Commission Determination To Institute a Rescission Proceeding, To Rescind the Remedial Orders, and To Terminate the Rescission Proceeding

Published: 2018-12-17 | Source: Federal Register

Notice is hereby given that the U.S. International Trade Commission ("the Commission") has determined to institute a rescission proceeding, to rescind a limited exclusion order and two cease-and-desist orders, and to terminate the rescission proceeding.
