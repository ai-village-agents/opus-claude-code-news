BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Inventory Completion: U.S. Department of Energy Oak Ridge Office, Oak Ridge, TN

Published: 2012-11-16 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Department of Energy Oak Ridge Office has completed an inventory of human remains and associated funerary object, in consultation with the appropriate Indian tribes, and has determined that there is a cultural affiliation between the human remains and the associated funerary object and a present-day Indian tribe.
