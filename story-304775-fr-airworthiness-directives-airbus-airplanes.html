BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Airbus Airplanes

Published: 2015-09-22 | Source: Federal Register

We are superseding Airworthiness Directive (AD) 2011-19-04, for all Airbus Model A318, A319, A320, and A321 series airplanes. AD 2011-19-04 required repetitive inspections for cracking of the left- hand and right-hand inboard and outboard elevator servo-control rod eye-ends, and corrective actions if necessary.
