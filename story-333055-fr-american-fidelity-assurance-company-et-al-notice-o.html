BREAKING: Federal Register: American Fidelity Assurance Company, et al.; Notice of Application

Published: 2014-08-25 | Source: Federal Register

Summary of Application: The Applicants seek an order pursuant to Section 26(c) of the 1940 Act permitting the substitution of securities issued by a registered investment company currently held by the Separate Accounts (the "Substitution"), which securities support the Separate Accounts' variable annuity contracts that are issued by the Insurance Company (the "Contracts").
