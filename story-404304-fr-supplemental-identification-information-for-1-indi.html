BREAKING: Federal Register: Supplemental Identification Information for 1 Individual Designated Pursuant to Executive Order 13441

Published: 2012-12-21 | Source: Federal Register

The Treasury Department's Office of Foreign Assets Control ("OFAC") is publishing supplemental information for the name of 1 individual whose property and interests in property are blocked pursuant to Executive Order 13441 of August 1, 2007, "Blocking Property of Persons Undermining the Sovereignty of Lebanon or Its Democratic Processes and Institutions."
