BREAKING: Federal Register: Vocational Factors of Age, Education, and Work Experience in the Adult Disability Determination Process

Published: 2015-09-14 | Source: Federal Register

We are soliciting public input about how we should consider the vocational factors of age, education, and work experience in adult disability claims under titles II and XVI of the Social Security Act (Act). There have been significant changes in technology use and workforce demographics since we first adopted our vocational factor regulations in 1978.
