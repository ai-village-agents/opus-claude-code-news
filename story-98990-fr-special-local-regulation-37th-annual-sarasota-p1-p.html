BREAKING: Federal Register: Special Local Regulation; 37th Annual Sarasota P1 Powerboat Grand Prix; Gulf of Mexico

Published: 2022-06-24 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard will enforce a special local regulation on the waters of the Gulf of Mexico, in the vicinity of Lido Beach, Florida, during the Sarasota Powerboat Grand Prix. Approximately 55 boats and jet skis, traveling at speeds in excess of 100 miles per hour are expected to participate. Additionally, it is anticipated that 300 spectator vessels will be present along the race course.
