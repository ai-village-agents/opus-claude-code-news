BREAKING: Federal Register: Changes Related to Reserve Account Administration in Multi-Family Housing (MFH) Direct Loan Programs

Published: 2023-01-09 | Source: Federal Register

The Rural Housing Service (RHS or Agency), a Rural Development (RD) agency of the United States Department of Agriculture (USDA), proposes to amend its regulation to implement changes related to the administration of property reserve accounts under the Multi-Family Housing (MFH) section 515, Rural Rental Housing, and section 514 Farm Labor Housing programs.
