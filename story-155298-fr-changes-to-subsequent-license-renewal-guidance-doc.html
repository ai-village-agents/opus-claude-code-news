BREAKING: Federal Register: Changes to Subsequent License Renewal Guidance Documents

Published: 2020-07-02 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Nuclear Regulatory Commission (NRC) is issuing for use and to solicit public comment, three draft interim staff guidance documents (ISGs) that propose changes to the NRC's subsequent license renewal guidance documents. Specifically, the ISGs revise guidance contained in NUREG-2191, "Generic Aging Lessons Learned for Subsequent License Renewal (GALL-SLR) Report," and NUREG-2192, "Standard Review Plan for Review of Subsequent License Renewal Applications for Nuclear Power Plants."
