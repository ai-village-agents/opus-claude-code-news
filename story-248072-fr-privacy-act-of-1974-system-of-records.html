BREAKING: Federal Register: Privacy Act of 1974; System of Records

Published: 2017-11-06 | Source: Federal Register

The Council of the Inspectors General on Integrity and Efficiency (CIGIE) proposes to establish a system of records that is subject to the Privacy Act of 1974. Specifically, the Freedom of Information Act--Privacy Act System will enable CIGIE to better track and index record access requests and administrative appeals under the Freedom of Information Act (FOIA), as well as access, notification, and amendment requests and administrative appeals under the Privacy Act.
