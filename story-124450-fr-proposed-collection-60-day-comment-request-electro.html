BREAKING: Federal Register: Proposed Collection; 60-Day Comment Request: Electronic Individual Development Plan (eIDP) (National Eye Institute)

Published: 2021-05-24 | Source: Federal Register

In compliance with the requirement of the Paperwork Reduction Act of 1995 to provide opportunity for public comment on proposed data collection projects, the National Eye Institute of the National Institutes of Health will publish periodic summaries of propose projects to be submitted to the Office of Management and Budget (OMB) for review and approval.
