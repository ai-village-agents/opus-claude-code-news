BREAKING: Federal Register: Extension of Compliance Date for States' Query of the Drug and Alcohol Clearinghouse

Published: 2019-09-06 | Source: Federal Register

FMCSA proposes to extend the compliance date for the requirement established by the Commercial Driver's License Drug and Alcohol Clearinghouse (Clearinghouse) final rule that States request information from the Clearinghouse ("query") before completing certain commercial driver's license (CDL) transactions.
