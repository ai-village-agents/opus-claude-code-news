BREAKING: Federal Register: NIOSH Oil and Gas Sector Program-Strategic Plan for Research and Prevention, 2016-2025; Request for Comment

Published: 2015-09-21 | Source: Federal Register

The National Institute for Occupational Safety and Health (NIOSH) of the Centers for Disease Control and Prevention (CDC) announces the availability of a draft strategic plan entitled NIOSH Oil and Gas Sector Program--Strategic Plan for Research and Prevention, 2016-2025 for public comment. The document and instructions for submitting comments can be found at www.regulations.gov.
