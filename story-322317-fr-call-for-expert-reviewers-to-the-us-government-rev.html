BREAKING: Federal Register: Call for Expert Reviewers to the U.S. Government Review of the Synthesis Report (SYR) of the Fifth Assessment Report of the Intergovernmental Panel on Climate Change (IPCC)

Published: 2014-04-22 | Source: Federal Register

More information on the report can be found at http://www.ipcc-syr.nl/.
