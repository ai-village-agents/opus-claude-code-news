BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Applications for Deregistration Under Section 8(f) of the Investment Company Act of 1940

Published: 2021-12-01 | Source: Federal Register

Applicant, a closed-end investment company, seeks an order declaring that it has ceased to be an investment company. On July 16, 2021, and September 17, 2021, applicant made liquidating distributions to its shareholders based on net asset value. Expenses of approximately $872,000 incurred in connection with the liquidation were paid by the applicant.
