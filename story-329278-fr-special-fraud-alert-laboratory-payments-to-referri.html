BREAKING: Federal Register: Special Fraud Alert: Laboratory Payments to Referring Physicians

Published: 2014-07-11 | Source: Federal Register

This Special Fraud Alert addresses compensation paid by laboratories to referring physicians and physician group practices (collectively, physicians) for blood specimen collection, processing, and packaging, and for submitting patient data to a registry or database.
