BREAKING: Federal Register: Endangered and Threatened Wildlife and Plants; Endangered Species Status for the Dixie Valley Toad

Published: 2022-04-07 | Source: Federal Register

We, the U.S. Fish and Wildlife Service (Service), propose to list the Dixie Valley toad (Anaxyrus williamsi), a toad species from Nevada, as an endangered species under the Endangered Species Act of 1973, as amended (Act). This determination also serves as our 12-month finding on a petition to list the Dixie Valley toad.
