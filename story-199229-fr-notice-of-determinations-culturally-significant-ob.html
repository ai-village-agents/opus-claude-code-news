BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Determinations; Culturally Significant Objects Imported for Exhibition Determinations: “Michelangelo and the Vatican: Masterworks From the Museo e Real Bosco di Capodimonte, Naples” Exhibition

Published: 2018-02-05 | Source: Federal Register

Notice is hereby given of the following determinations: I hereby determine that certain objects to be included in the exhibition "Michelangelo and the Vatican: Masterworks from the Museo e Real Bosco di Capodimonte, Naples," imported from abroad for temporary exhibition within the United States, are of cultural significance.
