BREAKING: Federal Register: Authority of States To Enforce the Consumer Financial Protection Act of 2010

Published: 2022-05-26 | Source: Federal Register

Section 1042 of the Consumer Financial Protection Act of 2010 (CFPA) generally authorizes States to enforce the CFPA's provisions. The Consumer Financial Protection Bureau (Bureau) is issuing this interpretive rule to provide further clarity regarding the scope of State enforcement under section 1042 and related provisions of the CFPA.
