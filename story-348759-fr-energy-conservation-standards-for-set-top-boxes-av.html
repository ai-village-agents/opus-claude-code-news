BREAKING: Federal Register: Energy Conservation Standards for Set-Top Boxes: Availability of Initial Analysis

Published: 2013-03-07 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Department of Energy (DOE) has completed an initial analysis that estimates the potential economic impacts and energy savings that could result from promulgating a regulatory energy conservation standard for set-top boxes. At this time, DOE is not proposing any energy conservation standard for set-top boxes.
