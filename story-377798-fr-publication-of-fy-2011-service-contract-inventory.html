BREAKING: Federal Register: Publication of FY 2011 Service Contract Inventory

Published: 2012-02-15 | Source: Federal Register

In accordance with Section 734 of Division C of the Consolidated Appropriations Act of 2010 (Pub. L. 111-117), the Bureau of Consumer Financial Protection (Bureau) is publishing this notice to advise the public of the availability of the FY 2011 service contract inventory. This inventory provides information on service contract actions over $25,000, which the Bureau awarded during FY 2011.
