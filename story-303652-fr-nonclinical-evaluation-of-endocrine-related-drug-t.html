BREAKING: Federal Register: Nonclinical Evaluation of Endocrine-Related Drug Toxicity; Guidance for Industry; Availability

Published: 2015-09-09 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA or Agency) is announcing the availability of a guidance for industry entitled "Nonclinical Evaluation of Endocrine-Related Drug Toxicity." The purpose of this guidance is to clarify when additional studies are warranted after the standard toxicology tests have been conducted and there is a signal for potential adverse endocrine-related toxicity.
