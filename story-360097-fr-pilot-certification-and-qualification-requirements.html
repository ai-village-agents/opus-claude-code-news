BREAKING: Federal Register: Pilot Certification and Qualification Requirements for Air Carrier Operations

Published: 2013-07-15 | Source: Federal Register

This action creates new certification and qualification requirements for pilots in air carrier operations. As a result of this action, a second in command (first officer) in domestic, flag, and supplemental operations must now hold an airline transport pilot certificate and an airplane type rating for the aircraft to be flown.
