BREAKING: Federal Register: Advisory Committee on Family Residential Centers Meeting

Published: 2015-11-19 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Immigration and Customs Enforcement (ICE) Advisory Committee on Family Residential Centers (ACFRC) will hold its inaugural meeting in Washington, DC to discuss specific challenges within ICE family residential centers and areas of focus for its initial work. This meeting will be open to the public.
