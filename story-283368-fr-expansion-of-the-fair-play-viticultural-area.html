BREAKING: Federal Register: Expansion of the Fair Play Viticultural Area

Published: 2015-01-06 | Source: Federal Register

The Alcohol and Tobacco Tax and Trade Bureau (TTB) is expanding the approximately 33-square mile "Fair Play" viticultural area in El Dorado County, California, by 1,200 acres (approximately 2 square miles). The established viticultural area and the expansion area are both located entirely within the larger El Dorado and Sierra Foothills viticultural areas.
