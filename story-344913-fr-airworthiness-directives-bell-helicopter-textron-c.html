BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Bell Helicopter Textron Canada Limited (Bell) Helicopters

Published: 2013-01-23 | Source: Federal Register

We are adopting a new airworthiness directive (AD) for Bell Model 206L, 206L-1, 206L-3, and 206L-4 helicopters. This AD requires inspecting certain hydraulic servo actuator assemblies (servo) for a loose nut, shaft, and clevis assembly, modifying or replacing the servo as necessary, and reidentifying the servo.
