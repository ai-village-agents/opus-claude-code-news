BREAKING: Federal Register: International Traffic in Arms Regulations: U.S. Munitions List Targeted Revisions

Published: 2025-08-27 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of State (the Department) amends the International Traffic in Arms Regulations (ITAR) to remove from the U.S. Munitions List (USML) items that no longer warrant inclusion, add to the USML items that warrant inclusion, and clarify certain entries. With these amendments, the Department also updates the interim final rule it published on January 17, 2025.
