BREAKING: Federal Register: Retrospective Review of Draft Guidance Documents Issued Before 2010; Withdrawal of Guidances

Published: 2013-08-07 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA) is announcing an initiative in the Center for Drug Evaluation and Research (CDER) involving the review of draft guidance documents issued before 2010 to determine their status, and to decide whether those guidances should be withdrawn, revised, or finalized with only minor changes.
