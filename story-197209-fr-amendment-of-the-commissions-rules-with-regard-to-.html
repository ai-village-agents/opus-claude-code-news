BREAKING: Federal Register: Amendment of the Commission's Rules With Regard to Operation in the 3550-3650 MHz Band

Published: 2018-01-09 | Source: Federal Register

The Federal Communications Commission (Commission) is announcing that three final rules that appeared in the Federal Register as part of the Commission's rulemaking Commercial Operations in the 3550-3650 MHz Band do not need information collection approval from the Office of Management and Budget (OMB) and are effective immediately.
