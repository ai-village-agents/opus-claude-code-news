BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; The Boeing Company Airplanes

Published: 2016-02-25 | Source: Federal Register

We propose to supersede Airworthiness Directive (AD) 2013-24- 12, which applies to all The Boeing Company Model 747-8 and 747-8F airplanes. AD 2013-24-12 currently requires repetitive ultrasonic or dye penetrant inspections for cracking of the barrel nuts and bolts on each forward engine mount, and related investigative and corrective actions if necessary.
