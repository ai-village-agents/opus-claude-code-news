BREAKING: Federal Register: National Action Plan on Responsible Business Conduct: Notice of Opportunity To Submit Written Comments

Published: 2022-02-28 | Source: Federal Register

On June 16, 2021, Secretary of State Antony Blinken announced on behalf of the Biden-Harris Administration that the Department will soon begin updating and revitalizing the United States' National Action Plan on Responsible Business Conduct (NAP RBC) for U.S. businesses operating and investing abroad. This notice provides background and welcomes written comments concerning this initiative for U.S. Government agencies to take into account in developing the updated NAP RBC.
