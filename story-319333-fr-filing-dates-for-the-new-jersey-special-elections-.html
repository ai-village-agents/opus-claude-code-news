BREAKING: Federal Register: Filing Dates for the New Jersey Special Elections in the 1st Congressional District

Published: 2014-03-19 | Source: Federal Register

New Jersey has scheduled special elections on June 3, 2014, and November 4, 2014, to fill the U.S. House of Representatives seat vacated by Representative Robert E. Andrews. Committees required to file reports in connection with the Special Primary Election on June 3, 2014, shall file a 12-day Pre-Primary Report.
