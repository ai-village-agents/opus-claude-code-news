BREAKING: Federal Register: Air Plan Approval; Indiana; SO2 Emission Limitations for United States Steel-Gary Works

Published: 2019-06-27 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is approving a March 6, 2018 request by the Indiana Department of Environmental Management (IDEM) to revise its State Implementation Plan (SIP) for the United States Steel-Gary Works coke plant. The submission involves the removal of sulfur dioxide (SO2) emission limitations for the coke plant at the United States Steel-Gary Works (US Steel-Gary Works).
