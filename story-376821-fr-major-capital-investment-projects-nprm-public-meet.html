BREAKING: Federal Register: Major Capital Investment Projects (NPRM); Public Meetings

Published: 2012-02-06 | Source: Federal Register

This document announces the dates, times, and locations of three public meetings and a webinar regarding the Notice of Proposed Rulemaking (NPRM) recently issued by the Federal Transit Administration (FTA) for its discretionary Major Capital Investments program ("New Starts" and "Small Starts"). During these sessions, FTA staff will provide information on the NPRM and answer questions from interested persons.
