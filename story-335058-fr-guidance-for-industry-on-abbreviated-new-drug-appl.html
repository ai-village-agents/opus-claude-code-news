BREAKING: Federal Register: Guidance for Industry on Abbreviated New Drug Application Submissions-Refuse-to-Receive Standards; Availability

Published: 2014-09-17 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA) is announcing the availability of a guidance for industry entitled "ANDA Submissions-- Refuse-to-Receive Standards." It finalizes the draft guidance with the same name that published on October 1, 2013. This guidance is intended to assist applicants preparing to submit to FDA abbreviated new drug applications (ANDAs) and related submissions (i.e., prior approval supplements for new strengths).
