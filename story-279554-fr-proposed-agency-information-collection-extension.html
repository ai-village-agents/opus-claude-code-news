BREAKING: Federal Register: Proposed Agency Information Collection Extension

Published: 2016-11-21 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Energy has submitted to the OMB for clearance, a proposal to amend an information collection request by adding an additional collection to an ICR that already includes two previously approved collections. The two previously approved collections address DOE's Plug-in Electric Vehicle (PEV) Scorecard, and the National Clean Fleets Partnership.
