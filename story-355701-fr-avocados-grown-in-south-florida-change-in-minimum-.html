BREAKING: Federal Register: Avocados Grown in South Florida; Change in Minimum Grade Requirements

Published: 2013-05-23 | Source: Federal Register

This proposed rule invites comments on an increase to the minimum grade requirements currently prescribed under the Florida avocado marketing order (order). The order regulates the handling of avocados grown in South Florida, and is administered locally by the Avocado Administrative Committee (Committee).
