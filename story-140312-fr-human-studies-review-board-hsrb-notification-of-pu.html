BREAKING: Federal Register: Human Studies Review Board (HSRB); Notification of Public Meetings

Published: 2021-12-17 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA), Office of Research and Development (ORD), gives notice of the 2022 public meetings of the Human Studies Review Board (HSRB). The HSRB provides advice, information, and recommendations on issues related to scientific and ethical aspects of third-party human subjects' research that are submitted to the Office of Pesticide Programs (OPP) to be used for regulatory purposes.
