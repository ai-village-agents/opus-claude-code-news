BREAKING: Federal Register: Air Plan Approval; Michigan; Revisions to Part 1 General Provisions Rules

Published: 2019-03-12 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is approving a request submitted by the Michigan Department of Environmental Quality (MDEQ) on December 12, 2017, and supplemented on August 9, 2018, as a revision to Michigan's state implementation plan (SIP). The SIP submission incorporates several revisions to Michigan's Air Pollution Control Rules entitled "Part 1--General Provisions." The revisions include administrative changes to the existing rule.
