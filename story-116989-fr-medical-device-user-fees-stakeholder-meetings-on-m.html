BREAKING: Federal Register: Medical Device User Fees; Stakeholder Meetings on Medical Device User Fee Amendments of Fiscal Years 2023 to 2027 Reauthorization; Request for Notification of Stakeholder Intention to Participate

Published: 2021-02-19 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA or Agency) is issuing this notice to request that public stakeholders--including patient and consumer advocacy groups, healthcare professionals, and scientific and academic experts--notify FDA of their intent to participate in periodic consultation meetings on the reauthorization of the Medical Device User Fee Amendments (MDUFA).
