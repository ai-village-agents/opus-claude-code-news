BREAKING: Federal Register: Limited Extension of Select Compliance Dates for Occupational Exposure to Beryllium in General Industry

Published: 2018-06-01 | Source: Federal Register

OSHA is proposing a nine-month extension of the compliance date for certain ancillary requirements of the general industry beryllium standard (from March 12, 2018 to December 12, 2018). This proposal would not extend the compliance date for the permissible exposure limits (PELs), exposure assessment, respiratory protection, medical surveillance, or medical removal protection provisions, or for any provisions for which the standard already establishes compliance dates in 2019 and 2020.
