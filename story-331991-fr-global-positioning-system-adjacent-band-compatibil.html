BREAKING: Federal Register: Global Positioning System Adjacent Band Compatibility Assessment Workshop

Published: 2014-08-12 | Source: Federal Register

The purpose of this notice is to inform the public that the U.S. Department of Transportation will host a workshop to discuss implementation of a Global Positioning System (GPS) Adjacent Band Compatibility Assessment. Discussion at this workshop will focus on the various implementation steps of the GPS Adjacent Band Compatibility Assessment, including development of GPS receiver use cases, identification of representative GPS receivers, and development of a test and analysis program.
