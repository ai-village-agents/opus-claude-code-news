BREAKING: Federal Register: Privacy Act of 1974: Implementation of Exemptions; DOT/ALL 24-Departmental Office of Civil Rights System System of Records

Published: 2012-04-03 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Transportation is issuing a final rule to amend its regulations to exempt portions of a newly established or updated and reissued system of records titled, "DOT/ALL 24-- Departmental Office of Civil Rights System" from certain provisions of the Privacy Act.
