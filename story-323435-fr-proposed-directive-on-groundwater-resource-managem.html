BREAKING: Federal Register: Proposed Directive on Groundwater Resource Management, Forest Service Manual 2560

Published: 2014-05-06 | Source: Federal Register

The Forest Service proposes to amend its internal Agency directives for Watershed and Air Management to establish direction for management of groundwater resources on National Forest System (NFS) lands as an integral component of watershed management.
