BREAKING: Federal Register: Investigation Nos. 701-TA-402 and 731-TA-892 (Second Review); Honey From Argentina; Termination of Five-Year Reviews

Published: 2012-10-23 | Source: Federal Register

The subject five-year reviews were instituted in July 2012 to determine whether revocation of the countervailing duty order and antidumping duty order on imports of honey from Argentina would be likely to lead to continuation or recurrence of material injury. On September 21, 2012, the Department of Commerce published notice that it was revoking the orders effective August 2, 2012, because no domestic interested party responded to the sunset review notice of initiation by the applicable deadline...
