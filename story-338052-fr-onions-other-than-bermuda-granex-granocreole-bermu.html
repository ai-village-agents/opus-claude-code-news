BREAKING: Federal Register: Onions Other Than Bermuda-Granex-Grano/Creole; Bermuda-Granex-Grano

Published: 2014-10-23 | Source: Federal Register

This rule revises the U.S. Standards for Grades of Onions (Other Than Bermuda-Granex-Grano (BGG) and Creole Type) and the U.S. Standards for Grades of BGG Type Onions which were issued under the Agricultural Marketing Act of 1946. The Agricultural Marketing Service (AMS) is amending the "similar varietal characteristic" and "one type" requirements to allow mixed colors of onions when designated as a mixed or specialty pack.
