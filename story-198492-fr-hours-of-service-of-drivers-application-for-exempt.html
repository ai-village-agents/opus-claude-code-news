BREAKING: Federal Register: Hours of Service of Drivers: Application for Exemption; SikhsPAC and the North American Punjabiz Trucker Association

Published: 2018-01-26 | Source: Federal Register

FMCSA announces that it has received a joint application from SikhsPAC and the North American Punjabiz Trucker Association (applicants) on behalf of their members for an exemption from the electronic logging device (ELD) requirements. The exemption would allow members involved in segments of America's agricultural transportation industry to delay using ELDs.
