BREAKING: Federal Register: Hydrogen and Fuel Cell Technical Advisory Committee (HTAC): Correction

Published: 2014-03-19 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Energy (DOE) published in the Federal Register on March 5, 2014, a notice of an open meeting for the Hydrogen and Fuel Cell Technical Advisory Committee (HTAC). The notice is being corrected to change the address of the meeting.
