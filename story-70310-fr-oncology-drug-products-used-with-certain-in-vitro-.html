BREAKING: Federal Register: Oncology Drug Products Used With Certain In Vitro Diagnostic Tests: Pilot Program; Guidance for Industry, Clinical Laboratories, and Food and Drug Administration Staff; Availability

Published: 2023-06-21 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA or Agency) is announcing the availability of a final guidance for industry, clinical laboratories, and FDA staff entitled ``Oncology Drug Products Used with Certain In Vitro Diagnostic Tests: Pilot Program.'' FDA is issuing this guidance to announce and describe FDA's voluntary pilot program for certain oncology drug products regulated by FDA's Center for Drug Evaluation and Research (CDER) used with certain in vitro diagnostic tests.
