BREAKING: Federal Register: National Emissions Standards for Hazardous Air Pollutants from Secondary Lead Smelting

Published: 2014-01-03 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is taking direct final action to promulgate amendments to a final rule that revised national emission standards for hazardous air pollutants for existing and new secondary lead smelters. The final rule was published on January 5, 2012. This direct final action amends certain regulatory text to clarify compliance dates.
