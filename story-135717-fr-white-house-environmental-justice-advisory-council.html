BREAKING: Federal Register: White House Environmental Justice Advisory Council; Notification of Virtual Public Meeting

Published: 2021-10-18 | Source: Federal Register

Pursuant to the Federal Advisory Committee Act (FACA), the U.S. Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) hereby provides notice that the White House Environmental Justice Advisory Council (WHEJAC) will meet on the dates and times described below. EPA is announcing a two (2) day meeting on November 17 and 18, 2021. The meeting is open to the public.
