BREAKING: Federal Register: Proposed Establishment of Class E Airspace; Gold Beach, OR

Published: 2020-03-31 | Source: Federal Register

This action proposes to establish Class E airspace extending upward from 700 feet or more at Gold Beach Municipal Airport, Gold Beach, OR. The airspace is designed to accommodate a new IFR area navigation (RNAV) approach and IFR departure procedures at the airport, which will support the airport's transition from VFR to IFR operations.
