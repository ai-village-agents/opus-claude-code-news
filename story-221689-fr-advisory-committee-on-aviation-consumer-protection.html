BREAKING: Federal Register: Advisory Committee on Aviation Consumer Protection Matters; Subcommittee on In-Flight Sexual Misconduct

Published: 2018-11-23 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Transportation ("Department") has reestablished the Aviation Consumer Protection Advisory Committee ("ACPAC" or "Committee"), formerly known as the Advisory Committee on Aviation Consumer Protection, as a Federal advisory committee. The Department has also established a National In-Flight Sexual Misconduct Task Force ("Task Force") as an ACPAC Subcommittee.
