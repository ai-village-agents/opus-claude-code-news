BREAKING: Federal Register: Revision of the Land Management Plan for the Colville National Forest in Washington State

Published: 2018-09-07 | Source: Federal Register

The USDA Forest Service has prepared a Revised Land Management Plan (Forest Plan) for the Colville National Forest. The Forest Service has also prepared a Final Environmental Impact Statement (FEIS) and a Draft Record of Decision (ROD).
