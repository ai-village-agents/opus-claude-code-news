BREAKING: Federal Register: Board of Scientific Counselors Homeland Security Subcommittee; Notification of Public Teleconference and Public Comment

Published: 2017-03-22 | Source: Federal Register

Pursuant to the Federal Advisory Committee Act, Public Law 92- 463, the U.S. Environmental Protection Agency hereby provides notice that the Board of Scientific Counselors (BOSC) Homeland Security Subcommittee (HSS) will host a public teleconference. The meeting will be held on Tuesday, March 28, 2017 from 11:00 a.m. to 12:30 p.m. All times noted are Eastern Time and are approximate.
