BREAKING: Federal Register: Quiet Aircraft Technology Incentive for Commercial Air Tour Operators in Grand Canyon National Park

Published: 2014-02-03 | Source: Federal Register

The Moving Ahead for Progress in the 21st Century Act (MAP-21) directs the Administrator of the Federal Aviation Administration (FAA) and the Secretary of the Interior to provide incentives for commercial air tour operators in Grand Canyon National Park that convert to quiet aircraft technology. The FAA holds a limited number of flight allocations that are authorized under 14 CFR part 93, Subpart U, "Special Flight Rules in the Vicinity of Grand Canyon National Park."
