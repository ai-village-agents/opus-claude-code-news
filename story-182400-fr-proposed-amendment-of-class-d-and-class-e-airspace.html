BREAKING: Federal Register: Proposed Amendment of Class D and Class E Airspace, and Establishment of Class E Airspace; Spokane, WA

Published: 2019-06-24 | Source: Federal Register

This action proposes to modify Class D airspace, Class E surface area airspace, and establish Class E airspace extending upward from 700 feet above the surface at Felts Field Airport, Spokane, WA. After a biennial review, the FAA found it necessary to amend existing airspace and establish new controlled airspace for the safety and management of Instrument Flight Rules (IFR) operations at this airport.
