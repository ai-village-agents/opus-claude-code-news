BREAKING: Federal Register: Small Business Investment Companies-Investments in Passive Businesses

Published: 2014-10-21 | Source: Federal Register

In this final rule, the U.S. Small Business Administration (SBA) is revising the regulations for the Small Business Investment Company (SBIC) program concerning investments in passive businesses. SBICs are generally prohibited from investing in passive businesses under the Small Business Investment Act of 1958, as amended, as well as under SBIC program regulations.
