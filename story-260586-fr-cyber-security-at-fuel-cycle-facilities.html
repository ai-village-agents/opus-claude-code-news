BREAKING: Federal Register: Cyber Security at Fuel Cycle Facilities

Published: 2016-04-12 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Nuclear Regulatory Commission (NRC) is making available a final regulatory basis document to support a rulemaking that would amend its regulations by adopting new cyber security requirements for certain nuclear fuel cycle facility (FCF) licensees in order to address safety, security, and safeguards consequences of concern.
