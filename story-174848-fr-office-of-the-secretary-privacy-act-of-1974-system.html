BREAKING: Federal Register: Office of The Secretary; Privacy Act of 1974; System of Records

Published: 2019-03-22 | Source: Federal Register

This notice proposes to revise to the Privacy Act System of Records titled Farm Records File (Automated) USDA, which includes information for certain Farm Service Agency under (FSA) Farm Programs and certain Commodity Credit Corporation (CCC) programs that are administered by FSA on behalf of CCC. The records include information about the majority of agricultural producers in the United States.
