BREAKING: Federal Register: Rules of Practice for Adjudicative Proceedings

Published: 2016-04-13 | Source: Federal Register

The United States Consumer Product Safety Commission ("Commission," "CPSC," or "we") is issuing this notice of proposed rulemaking ("NPR") to update the Commission's Rules of Practice for Adjudicative Proceedings, ("Rules of Practice" or "Rules"). We are proposing to modernize the Rules of Practice to reflect changes in civil and administrative litigation since adoption of the Rules in 1980.
