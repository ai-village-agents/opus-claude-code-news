BREAKING: Federal Register: Data Specifications for Collecting Study Area Boundaries

Published: 2012-06-21 | Source: Federal Register

In this document, the Wireline Competition Bureau proposes data specifications for collecting study area boundaries for purposes of implementing various reforms adopted as part of the USF/ICC Transformation Order and seeks comment on this proposal.
