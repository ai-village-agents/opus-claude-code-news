BREAKING: Federal Register: Draft Harmonized System (HS) Code List of Critical Supply Chains

Published: 2022-10-04 | Source: Federal Register

The Executive Order directed relevant Secretaries to prepare reports on the supply chains for critical sectors and subsectors within either 100 days or 1 year of the Order.
