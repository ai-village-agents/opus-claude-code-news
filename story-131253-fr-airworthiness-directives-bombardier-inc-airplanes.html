BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Bombardier, Inc., Airplanes

Published: 2021-08-23 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA proposes to adopt a new airworthiness directive (AD) for certain Bombardier, Inc., Model BD-100-1A10 airplanes. This proposed AD was prompted by reports of erratic electrical system status on the push button annunciators (PBAs) and the engine instrument and crew alerting system (EICAS).
