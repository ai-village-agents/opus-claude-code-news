BREAKING: Federal Register: Outer Continental Shelf (OCS) Scientific Committee-Notice of Renewal

Published: 2014-03-05 | Source: Federal Register

Following consultation with the General Services Administration, notice is hereby given that the Secretary of the Interior is renewing the OCS Scientific Committee. The OCS Scientific Committee provides advice on the feasibility, appropriateness, and scientific value of the OCS Environmental Studies Program to the Secretary of the Interior through the Director of the Bureau of Ocean Energy Management.
