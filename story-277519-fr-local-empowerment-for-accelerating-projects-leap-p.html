BREAKING: Federal Register: Local Empowerment for Accelerating Projects (LEAP) Pilot Program

Published: 2016-10-26 | Source: Federal Register

The Federal Highway Administration (FHWA) is announcing a pilot program to permit, on an experimental basis, direct delivery of Federal-aid funding of up to five Local Public Agencies (LPAs). These LPAs will be subject to Federal oversight, and the State DOT will be relieved of direct oversight and accountability for projects funded under the LEAP pilot program.
