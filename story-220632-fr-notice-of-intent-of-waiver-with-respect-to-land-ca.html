BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Intent of Waiver With Respect to Land; Capital Region International Airport, Lansing, Michigan

Published: 2018-11-08 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA is considering a proposal to change approximately 1.24 acres of airport land from aeronautical use to non-aeronautical use and to authorize the lease of airport property located at Capital Region International Airport, Lansing, Michigan. The aforementioned land is not needed for aeronautical use. The subject property is located at 2824 East Circle Drive, Lansing, Michigan. This property is on the southeast side of the street, south of the Capital Region International Airport's terminal. ...
