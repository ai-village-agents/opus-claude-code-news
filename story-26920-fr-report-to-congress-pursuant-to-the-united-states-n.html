BREAKING: Federal Register: Report to Congress Pursuant to the United States-Northern Triangle Enhanced Engagement Act

Published: 2024-01-10 | Source: Federal Register

This document provides an update to the Department of State's report to Congress on July 19, 2023, regarding foreign persons who are determined to have knowingly engaged in actions that undermine democratic processes or institutions, significant corruption, or obstruction of investigation into such acts of corruption in El Salvador, Guatemala, and Honduras pursuant to the United States-- Northern Triangle Enhanced Engagement Act, as amended.
