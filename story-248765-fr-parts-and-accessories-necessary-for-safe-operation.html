BREAKING: Federal Register: Parts and Accessories Necessary for Safe Operation; Exemption Renewal for Ford Motor Company

Published: 2017-11-16 | Source: Federal Register

FMCSA announces its decision to renew for a period of 5 years Ford Motor Company's (Ford) current exemption allowing motor carriers to operate Ford's Transit-based commercial motor vehicles (CMV) that do not meet the exhaust system location requirements in the Federal Motor Carrier Safety Regulations (FMCSR).
