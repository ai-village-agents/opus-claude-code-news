BREAKING: Federal Register: Fees for the Unified Carrier Registration Plan and Agreement

Published: 2022-06-14 | Source: Federal Register

FMCSA reopens the comment period for its January 24, 2022, NPRM concerning the fees to be collected by the Unified Carrier Registration (UCR) Plan and Agreement for the 2023 year and subsequent registration years. FMCSA received a request for a reopening of the comment period from the Owner-Operator Independent Drivers Association (OOIDA).
