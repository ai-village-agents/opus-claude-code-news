BREAKING: Federal Register: Federally Mandated Exclusions From Income-Updated Listing

Published: 2014-05-20 | Source: Federal Register

HUD's regulations provide for HUD to periodically publish in the Federal Register a notice that lists amounts specifically excluded by any Federal statute from consideration as income for purposes of determining eligibility or benefits in a HUD program. HUD last published a notice that listed Federally mandated exclusions from consideration of income on December 14, 2012.
