BREAKING: Federal Register: Transferred OTS Regulations and FDIC Regulations Regarding Management Official Interlocks

Published: 2014-07-21 | Source: Federal Register

In this notice of proposed rulemaking, the Federal Deposit Insurance Corporation ("FDIC") proposes to rescind and remove parts of our regulations, entitled "Management Official Interlocks" relating to State savings associations.
