BREAKING: Federal Register: Drawbridge Operation Regulations; Industrial Seaway Canal, MS

Published: 2019-02-08 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is issuing a temporary deviation to the operating schedule that regulates the State Route 605 (SR 605), Wilkes Road bascule bridge across the Industrial Seaway Canal, mile 11.3 at Hansboro, Harrison County, Mississippi. This deviation is needed to collect and analyze information on vehicle traffic congestion on SR 605 created when the drawbridge opens to vessel traffic.
