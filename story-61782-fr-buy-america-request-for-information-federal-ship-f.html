BREAKING: Federal Register: Buy America Request for Information; Federal Ship Financing Program

Published: 2023-03-03 | Source: Federal Register

MARAD, a modal agency of DOT, administers the Federal Ship Financing Program ("Title XI"), which provides loan guarantees to finance the construction of commercial vessels in U.S. shipyards or shipyard projects. Although Title XI provides important support for U.S. shipyards, the U.S. maritime industry in general, including the shipbuilding sector, has been on the decline for decades.
