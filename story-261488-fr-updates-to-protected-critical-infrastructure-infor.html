BREAKING: Federal Register: Updates to Protected Critical Infrastructure Information Program

Published: 2016-04-21 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Homeland Security (DHS) proposes to update its procedures for accepting Critical Infrastructure Information (CII) as a step towards meeting the challenges of evolving technology and identifying ways to make the PCII Program's protective measures more effective for information-sharing partnerships between the government and the private sector.
