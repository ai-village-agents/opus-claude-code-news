BREAKING: Federal Register: Gathering of Certain Plants or Plant Parts by Federally Recognized Indian Tribes for Traditional Purposes

Published: 2016-07-12 | Source: Federal Register

The National Park Service is establishing a management framework to allow the gathering and removal of plants or plant parts by enrolled members of federally recognized Indian tribes for traditional purposes.
