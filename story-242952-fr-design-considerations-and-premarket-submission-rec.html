BREAKING: Federal Register: Design Considerations and Premarket Submission Recommendations for Interoperable Medical Devices; Guidance for Industry and Food and Drug Administration Staff; Availability

Published: 2017-09-06 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA or Agency) is announcing the availability of the guidance entitled "Design Considerations and Pre-market Submission Recommendations for Interoperable Medical Devices." FDA is issuing this guidance to assist industry and FDA staff in identifying specific considerations related to the ability of electronic medical devices to safely and effectively exchange and use exchanged information.
