BREAKING: Federal Register: Energy Conservation Program: Test Procedures for Central Air Conditioners and Heat Pumps

Published: 2016-08-24 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Department of Energy (DOE) proposes to revise its test procedures for central air conditioners and heat pumps (CAC/HP) established under the Energy Policy and Conservation Act. DOE published several proposals in a November 2015 supplemental notice of proposed rulemaking (SNOPR). DOE finalized some of the proposed test procedure amendments in a June 2016 final rule.
