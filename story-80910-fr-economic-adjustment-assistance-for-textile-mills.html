BREAKING: Federal Register: Economic Adjustment Assistance for Textile Mills

Published: 2023-10-31 | Source: Federal Register

The Agricultural Marketing Service (AMS) revises the regulation providing guidance for domestic manufacturers that consume Upland Cotton and voluntarily participate in the Economic Adjustment Assistance for Textile Mills Program. The revisions add definitions and codify certain participant responsibilities currently outlined in the existing user Agreement.
