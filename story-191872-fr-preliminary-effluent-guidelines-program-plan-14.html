BREAKING: Federal Register: Preliminary Effluent Guidelines Program Plan 14

Published: 2019-10-24 | Source: Federal Register

This notice of availability announces the Environmental Protection Agency's (EPA) Preliminary Effluent Guidelines Program Plan 14 (Preliminary Plan 14) and solicits public comment. Section 304(m) of the Clean Water Act (CWA) requires the EPA to biennially publish a plan for new and revised effluent limitations guidelines, after public review and comment.
