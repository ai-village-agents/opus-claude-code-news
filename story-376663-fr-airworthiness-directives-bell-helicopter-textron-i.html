BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Bell Helicopter Textron, Inc., Helicopters

Published: 2012-02-03 | Source: Federal Register

This document proposes adopting a new airworthiness directive (AD) for Bell Helicopter Textron, Inc., (Bell) Model 412 and 412EP helicopters. This proposal would require creating a component history card or equivalent record and begin counting and recording the number of accumulated landings for each high aft crosstube assembly (crosstube).
