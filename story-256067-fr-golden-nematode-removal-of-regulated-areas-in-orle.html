BREAKING: Federal Register: Golden Nematode; Removal of Regulated Areas in Orleans, Nassau, and Suffolk Counties, New York

Published: 2016-02-23 | Source: Federal Register

We are adopting as a final rule, without change, an interim rule that amended the golden nematode regulations by removing areas in Orleans, Nassau, and Suffolk Counties in the State of New York from the list of generally infested areas. The interim rule was necessary to relieve restrictions on the movement of regulated articles from areas no longer under quarantine for golden nematode.
