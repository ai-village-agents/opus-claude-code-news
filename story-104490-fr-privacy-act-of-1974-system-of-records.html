BREAKING: Federal Register: Privacy Act of 1974; System of Records

Published: 2022-09-06 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Environmental Protection Agency's (EPA) Office of Land and Emergency Management (OLEM) is giving notice that it proposes to create a new system of records pursuant to the provisions of the Privacy Act of 1974. The Office of Land and Emergency Management, Customer Relationship Management System (OLEM CRM) is being created to support work under the Bipartisan Infrastructure Law (BIL), Save Our Seas 2.0 (SOS 2.0), and the Resource Conservation and Recovery Act (RCRA).
