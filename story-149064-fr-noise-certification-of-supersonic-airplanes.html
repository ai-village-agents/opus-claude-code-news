BREAKING: Federal Register: Noise Certification of Supersonic Airplanes

Published: 2020-04-13 | Source: Federal Register

This action proposes to add new supersonic airplanes to the applicability of noise certification regulations, and proposes landing and takeoff noise standards for a certain class of new supersonic airplanes.
