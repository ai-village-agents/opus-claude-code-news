BREAKING: Federal Register: Official Release of EMFAC2011 Motor Vehicle Emission Factor Model for Use in the State of California

Published: 2013-03-06 | Source: Federal Register

EPA is approving and announcing the availability of the latest version of the California EMFAC model (short for EMission FACtor) for use in state implementation plan (SIP) development and transportation conformity in California. EMFAC2011 is the latest update to the EMFAC model for use by California state and local governments to meet Clean Air Act (CAA) requirements.
