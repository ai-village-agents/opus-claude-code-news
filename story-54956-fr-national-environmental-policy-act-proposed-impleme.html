BREAKING: Federal Register: National Environmental Policy Act; Proposed Implementing Procedures and Categorical Exclusions

Published: 2024-12-11 | Source: Federal Register

Federal agencies are required to develop procedures to implement the National Environmental Policy Act (NEPA) and the Council on Environmental Quality (CEQ) regulations implementing NEPA. Consistent with these requirements, the National Institute of Standards and Technology (NIST) is proposing new NEPA implementing procedures (NEPA Procedures), including the establishment of new categorical exclusions (CEs) as part of its NEPA Procedures.
