BREAKING: Federal Register: Citric Acid and Certain Citrate Salts From Canada; Termination of Five-Year Review

Published: 2020-07-23 | Source: Federal Register

The Commission instituted the subject five-year review on May 1, 2020 to determine whether revocation of the antidumping duty order on citric acid and certain citrate salts from Canada would be likely to lead to continuation or recurrence of material injury. On June 23, 2020, the Department of Commerce published notice that it was revoking the order effective June 24, 2020, because the domestic interested parties withdrew their intent to participate in this review.
