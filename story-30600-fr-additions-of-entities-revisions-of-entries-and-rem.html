BREAKING: Federal Register: Additions of Entities, Revisions of Entries, and Removal of an Entity From the Entity List

Published: 2024-02-27 | Source: Federal Register

The Bureau of Industry and Security is amending the Export Administration Regulations (EAR) by adding two entities under seven entries to the Entity List. These entities are listed under the destinations of Canada (1), the People's Republic of China (China) (1), India (1), Japan (1), Malaysia (1), Sweden (1), and the United Arab Emirates (UAE) (1).
