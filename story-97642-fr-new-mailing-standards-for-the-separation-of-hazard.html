BREAKING: Federal Register: New Mailing Standards for the Separation of Hazardous Materials

Published: 2022-06-06 | Source: Federal Register

The Postal Service is revising Publication 52, Hazardous, Restricted, and Perishable Mail (Pub 52), to incorporate new requirements for mailers to separate, into identifiable containers, all hazardous material (HAZMAT) requiring hazardous marks or labels from other mail when tendering to the Postal Service.
