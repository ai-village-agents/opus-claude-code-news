BREAKING: Federal Register: Air Plan Approval; California; Mojave Desert Air Quality Management District; Internal Combustion Engines

Published: 2025-08-28 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is taking final action to approve a revision to the Mojave Desert Air Quality Management District (MDAQMD) portion of the California State Implementation Plan (SIP). This revision concerns emissions of volatile organic compounds (VOCs) and oxides of nitrogen (NOX) from internal combustion engines.
