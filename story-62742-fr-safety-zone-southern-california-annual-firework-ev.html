BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone; Southern California Annual Firework Events for the San Diego Captain of the Port Zone

Published: 2023-03-16 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard will enforce the safety zones for the Big Bay Boom Fourth of July Fireworks on the waters of San Diego Bay, CA on Tuesday, July 4, 2023. The safety zones are necessary to provide for the safety of the participants, spectators, official vessels of the event, and general users of the waterway.
