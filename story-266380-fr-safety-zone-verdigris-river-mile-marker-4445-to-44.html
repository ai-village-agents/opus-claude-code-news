BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone; Verdigris River Mile Marker 444.5 to 443.5

Published: 2016-06-16 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard proposes to establish a permanent safety zone for an annually recurring marine event in the Verdigris River, from Mile Marker (MM) 444.5 to MM 443.5 in Catoosa, Oklahoma. This action is necessary to protect persons and vessels from the potential safety hazards associated with a fireworks display taking place between late June to early July, 2016 and recurring annually thereafter.
