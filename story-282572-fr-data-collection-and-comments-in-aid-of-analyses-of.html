BREAKING: Federal Register: Data Collection and Comments in Aid of Analyses of the Terrorism Risk Insurance Program

Published: 2016-12-27 | Source: Federal Register

The Terrorism Risk Insurance Act of 2002 (TRIA) created the Terrorism Risk Insurance Program (Program) to address disruptions in the market for terrorism risk insurance, to help ensure the continued availability and affordability of commercial property and casualty insurance for terrorism risk, and to allow for the private markets to stabilize and build insurance capacity to absorb any future losses for terrorism events.
