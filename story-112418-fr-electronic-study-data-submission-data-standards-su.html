Published: 2022-12-16 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA) is correcting a notice correction that appeared in the Federal Register of August 20, 2020. The document announced the correction dates that the support and requirement were to begin for version 1.7 of the Clinical Data Interchange Standards Consortium (CDISC) Study Data Tabulation Model (SDTM), and version 3.3 of the SDTM Implementation Guide (SDTMIG), and for version 2.1 of the Define-Extensible Markup Language (Define-XML).
