BREAKING: Federal Register: Proposed Amendment of Class E Airspace; Kotzebue, AK

Published: 2020-05-07 | Source: Federal Register

This action proposes to amend the Class E airspace, designated a surface area, by removing the extensions to the east and west of the airport. This action also proposes to properly size the Class E airspace extending upward from 700 feet above the surface. The area should be reduced east of the airport to properly contain arriving IFR aircraft descending below 1,500 feet above the surface.
