BREAKING: Federal Register: Special Local Regulation; Atlantic Intracoastal Waterway, Morehead City, NC

Published: 2020-10-27 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is establishing a temporary special local regulation (SLR) for certain navigable waters of the Atlantic Intracoastal Waterway (AICW) and Beaufort Inlet in Morehead City, North Carolina. This SLR restricts vessel traffic on the AICW and Beaufort Inlet during high-speed boat races. The restriction of vessel traffic movement in the SLR protects participants and spectators from the hazards posed by these events.
