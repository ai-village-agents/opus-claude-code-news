BREAKING: Federal Register: Parts and Accessories Necessary for Safe Operation; Application for an Exemption From Transecurity LLC (Transecurity)

Published: 2012-08-23 | Source: Federal Register

The Federal Motor Carrier Safety Administration (FMCSA) requests public comment on an application for exemption from Transecurity LLC to allow the placement of an onboard safety monitoring system (OBMS) at the bottom of windshields on commercial motor vehicles (CMVs).
