BREAKING: Federal Register: Energy Conservation Program: Test Procedures for Compressors

Published: 2016-05-05 | Source: Federal Register

In this document, the U.S. Department of Energy (DOE) proposes to prescribe new definitions, sampling provisions, and test procedures for compressors in a new subpart of DOE regulations.
