BREAKING: Federal Register: Approval and Promulgation of Implementation Plans; Florida: Removal of Sulfur Storage and Handling Rules

Published: 2014-10-16 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is taking final action to approve a revision to the Florida State Implementation Plan (SIP), submitted by the Florida Department of Environmental Protection (FDEP), on April 5, 2012. The revision modifies Florida's SIP to remove two state rules relating to new and existing sulfur storage and handling facilities because they are no longer necessary.
