BREAKING: Federal Register: Proposed Amendment of Class E Airspace; Brady, TX

Published: 2019-02-28 | Source: Federal Register

This action proposes to modify Class E airspace extending upward from 700 feet above the surface at Brady, Curtis Field, Brady, TX. This action is necessary due to the decommissioning of the Brady non-directional radio beacon (NDB), and cancellation of the NDB approach. It would enhance the safety and management of standard instrument approach procedures for instrument flight rules (IFR) operations at this airport.
