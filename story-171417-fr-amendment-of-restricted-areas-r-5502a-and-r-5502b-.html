BREAKING: Federal Register: Amendment of Restricted Areas R-5502A and R-5502B; Lacarne, OH

Published: 2019-02-12 | Source: Federal Register

This action updates the using agency information for restricted areas R-5502A and R-5502B; Lacarne, OH, and updates the controlling agency information for R-5502A. Additionally, this action adds exclusion language to the R-5502B boundaries information to overcome potential controlling agency confusion caused when both restricted areas are active in the same volume of airspace.
