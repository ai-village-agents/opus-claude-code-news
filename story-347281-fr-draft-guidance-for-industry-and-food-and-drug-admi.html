BREAKING: Federal Register: Draft Guidance for Industry and Food and Drug Administration Staff; Providing Information About Pediatric Uses of Medical Devices Under Section 515A of the Federal Food, Drug, and Cosmetic Act; Availability

Published: 2013-02-19 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA) is announcing the availability of the draft guidance entitled "Draft Guidance for Industry and Food and Drug Administration Staff: Providing Information About Pediatric Uses of Medical Devices Under Section 515A of the Federal Food, Drug, and Cosmetic Act." FDA is issuing this guidance document to describe how to compile and submit the readily available pediatric use information required under the Federal Food, Drug, and Cosmetic Act (the FD&C Act).
