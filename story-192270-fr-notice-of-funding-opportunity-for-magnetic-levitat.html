BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Funding Opportunity for Magnetic Levitation Deployment Projects

Published: 2019-10-29 | Source: Federal Register

This notice details the application requirements and procedures to obtain grant \1\ funding for eligible projects under the Magnetic Levitation Technology Deployment Program (Maglev Grants Program). Maglev Grants Program funding under this notice is provided by two sources, totaling $24,027,500.
