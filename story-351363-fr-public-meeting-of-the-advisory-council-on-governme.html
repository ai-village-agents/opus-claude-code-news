BREAKING: Federal Register: Public Meeting of the Advisory Council on Government Auditing Standards

Published: 2013-04-04 | Source: Federal Register

This notice informs the public that the Advisory Council on Government Auditing Standards will hold a public meeting by teleconference on April 12, 2013. The public is invited to listen to the Council's discussion. Members of the public will be provided an opportunity to address the Council with a brief (five-minute) presentation in the afternoon.
