BREAKING: Federal Register: Approval and Promulgation of Air Quality Implementation Plans; Maryland; Low Emission Vehicle Program

Published: 2013-06-11 | Source: Federal Register

EPA is approving State Implementation Plan (SIP) revisions submitted by the State of Maryland on December 20, 2007, November 12, 2010, and June 22, 2011, as amended March 22, 2013. These SIP revisions pertain to adoption by Maryland of a Low Emission Vehicle (LEV) program, which incorporates by reference California's second generation Low Emission Vehicle (LEVII) program regulations.
