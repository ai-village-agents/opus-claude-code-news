BREAKING: Federal Register: Endangered and Threatened Species; Take of Anadromous Fish

Published: 2022-08-29 | Source: Federal Register

Notice is hereby given that NMFS has received one permit application submitted by FISHBIO Environmental, LLC. (FISHBIO) to enhance the propagation and survival of species listed under the Endangered Species Act (ESA) of 1973, as amended, for a 5 year period.
