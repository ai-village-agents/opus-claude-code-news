BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone, Delaware River; Pipe-Removal

Published: 2017-06-26 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is proposing to establish temporary safety zones in portions of Billingsport Range, on the Delaware River, to facilitate the removal of existing pipelines along the river bed of the Federal Navigation Channel. The safety zones would be established for the waters of Billingsport Range, on the Delaware River, in the vicinity of working vessels and associated equipment.
