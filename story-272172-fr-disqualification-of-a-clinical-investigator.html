BREAKING: Federal Register: Disqualification of a Clinical Investigator

Published: 2016-08-24 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA) is proposing to amend the regulations for new animal drugs for investigational use to expand the scope of clinical investigator disqualification to include ineligibility to conduct nonclinical laboratory studies.
