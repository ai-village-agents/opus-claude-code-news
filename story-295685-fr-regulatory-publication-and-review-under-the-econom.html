BREAKING: Federal Register: Regulatory Publication and Review Under the Economic Growth and Regulatory Paperwork Reduction Act of 1996

Published: 2015-06-05 | Source: Federal Register

The OCC, Board, and FDIC (each an "Agency"; together "we" or "Agencies") are conducting a review of the regulations we have issued in order to identify outdated or otherwise unnecessary regulatory requirements imposed on insured depository institutions, as required by the Economic Growth and Regulatory Paperwork Reduction Act of 1996 (EGRPRA).
