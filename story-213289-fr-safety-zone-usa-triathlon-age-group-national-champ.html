BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone; USA Triathlon Age Group National Championships; Lake Erie, Cleveland, OH

Published: 2018-08-07 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is establishing a temporary safety zone for certain waters of Lake Erie during the USA Triathlon Age Group National Championships. This safety zone is intended to restrict vessels from a portion of Lake Erie off of Edgewater Park during the USA Triathlon Age Group National Championships.
