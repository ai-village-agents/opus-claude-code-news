BREAKING: Federal Register: Patient Protection and Affordable Care Act; Maximizing January 1, 2014 Coverage Opportunities

Published: 2013-12-17 | Source: Federal Register

This interim final rule amends the date by which a qualified individual must select a qualified health plan (QHP) through any Exchange for an effective coverage date of January 1, 2014. This rule generally allows consumers to select a QHP until December 23, 2013, which is a change from the previously stated regulatory date of December 15, 2013, but permits State Exchanges to select a different date.
