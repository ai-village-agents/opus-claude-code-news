BREAKING: Federal Register: Kaw Nation Alcohol Ordinance of 2019; Repeal and Replace

Published: 2021-12-20 | Source: Federal Register

This notice publishes the Kaw Nation Alcohol Ordinance of 2019 which repeals and replaces the Kaw Nation Alcohol Control Ordinance previously published in the Federal Register and any and all previous statutes. The Kaw Nation Alcohol Ordinance of 2019 regulates and controls the possession, sale, manufacture, and distribution of liquor on the Kaw Nation trust lands in conformity with the Federal laws and of the State of Oklahoma where applicable and necessary.
