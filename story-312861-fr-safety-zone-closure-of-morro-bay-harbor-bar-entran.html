BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone; Closure of Morro Bay Harbor Bar Entrance; Morro Bay, CA

Published: 2015-12-29 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard proposes to establish a temporary safety zone in the navigable waters of the Morro Bay Harbor Entrance. This temporary safety zone is being established to reduce significant hazards subject to the vessels, the harbor, and the public during periods of poor weather conditions.
