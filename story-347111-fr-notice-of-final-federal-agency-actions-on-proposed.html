BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Final Federal Agency Actions on Proposed Highway in California

Published: 2013-02-15 | Source: Federal Register

The FHWA, on behalf of Caltrans, is issuing this notice to announce actions taken by Caltrans, that are final within the meaning of 23 U.S.C. Sec. 139(l)(1). The actions relate to a proposed highway project on the West Mission Bay Drive crossing of the San Diego River flood control channel between West Mission Bay Drive/Sea World Drive intersection and the Sports Arena Boulevard/I-8 intersection in the County of San Diego, State of California.
