BREAKING: Federal Register: Small Business Size Standards: Waiver of the Nonmanufacturer Rule

Published: 2013-05-22 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Small Business Administration (SBA) is considering granting a class waiver of the Nonmanufacturer Rule for commercial-type ovens, gas ranges, and ranges, under Product Service Code (PSC) 7310 (Food Cooking, Baking, and Serving Equipment), under the North American Industry Classification System (NAICS) code 333318 (Other Commercial and Service Industry Machinery Manufacturing).
