BREAKING: Federal Register: Magnuson-Stevens Act Provisions; National Standard 2-Scientific Information

Published: 2013-07-19 | Source: Federal Register

This final action amends the guidelines for National Standard 2 (NS2) of the Magnuson-Stevens Fishery Conservation and Management Act (MSA) regarding scientific information.
