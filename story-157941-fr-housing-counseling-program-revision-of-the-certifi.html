BREAKING: Federal Register: Housing Counseling Program: Revision of the Certification Timeline

Published: 2020-08-05 | Source: Federal Register

HUD's current rule states that participating agencies and counselors must comply with certification requirements by 36 months after HUD commences the administration of the certification examination. That 36-month grace period ends on August 1, 2020.
