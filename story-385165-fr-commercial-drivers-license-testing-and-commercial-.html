BREAKING: Federal Register: Commercial Driver's License Testing and Commercial Learner's Permit Standards

Published: 2012-05-08 | Source: Federal Register

FMCSA published a final rule in the Federal Register on Monday, May 9, 2011, that became effective on July 8, 2011. That final rule amended the commercial driver's license (CDL) knowledge and skills testing standards and established new minimum Federal standards for States to issue the commercial learner's permit (CLP).
