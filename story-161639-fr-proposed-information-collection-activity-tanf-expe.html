BREAKING: Federal Register: Proposed Information Collection Activity; TANF Expenditure Report, ACF-196R

Published: 2020-09-22 | Source: Federal Register

The Administration for Children and Families (ACF) is requesting a 3-year extension of the Temporary Assistance for Needy Families (TANF) Expenditure Report, Form ACF-196R (OMB #0970-0446, expiration 2/28/2021). ACF is reporting a change to remove certain guidance that was associated with an earlier ACF-196 report in order to devote the instructions to the singular ACF-196R report.
