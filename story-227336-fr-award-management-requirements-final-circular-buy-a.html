BREAKING: Federal Register: Award Management Requirements-Final Circular; Buy America Handbook-Conducting Pre-Award and Post Delivery Audits for Rolling Stock Procurements; Delay of Effective Dates for Guidance Documents

Published: 2017-02-09 | Source: Federal Register

In accordance with the White House memorandum issued on January 20, 2017 regarding a "Regulatory Freeze Pending Review," this notice delays the effective dates of the Award Management Requirements Circular and the Buy America Handbook until March 21, 2017.
