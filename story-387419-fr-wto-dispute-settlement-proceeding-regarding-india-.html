BREAKING: Federal Register: WTO Dispute Settlement Proceeding Regarding India-Measures Concerning the Importation of Certain Agricultural Products

Published: 2012-06-04 | Source: Federal Register

The Office of the United States Trade Representative ("USTR") is providing notice that on May 11, 2012, the United States requested establishment of a dispute settlement panel under the Marrakesh Agreement Establishing the World Trade Organization ("WTO Agreement") with the Government of India ("India") concerning measures imposed by India on the importation of various agricultural products from the United States, purportedly because of concerns related to avian influenza.
