BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Opportunity for Public Comment on Proposal To Withdraw Approval of New Drug Application for PEPAXTO, Equivalent to 20 Milligrams Base per Vial

Published: 2023-08-25 | Source: Federal Register

The Center for Drug Evaluation and Research (CDER) of the Food and Drug Administration (FDA, the Agency) is proposing to withdraw approval of PEPAXTO (melphalan flufenamide) for injection, equivalent to (EQ) 20 milligrams (mg) BASE/VIAL, once every 28 days, new drug application (NDA) 214383, held by Oncopeptides AB (Oncopeptides).
