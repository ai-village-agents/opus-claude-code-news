BREAKING: Federal Register: Caribou-Targhee National Forest, Idaho; John Wood Forest Management Project

Published: 2017-03-30 | Source: Federal Register

The Soda Springs Ranger District proposes to conduct forest vegetation management activities and road work in a 5,590-acre project area within the Wood Canyon and Johnson Creek drainages located in the Caribou-Targhee National Forest, approximately six miles east of Soda Springs, Idaho. The project area has a forest vegetation management emphasis designated in the Caribou Revised Forest Plan (RFP) (2003).
