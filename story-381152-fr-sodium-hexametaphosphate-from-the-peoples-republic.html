BREAKING: Federal Register: Sodium Hexametaphosphate From the People's Republic of China: Preliminary Results of Second Antidumping Duty Administrative Review

Published: 2012-03-23 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Commerce (the "Department") is conducting the second administrative review of the antidumping duty order on sodium hexametaphosphate ("sodium hex") from the People's Republic of China ("PRC") for the period of review ("POR") March 1, 2010, through February 28, 2011. The Department has preliminarily determined that sales have been made below normal value ("NV") by Hubei Xingfa Chemical Group Co., Ltd. ("Hubei Xingfa").
