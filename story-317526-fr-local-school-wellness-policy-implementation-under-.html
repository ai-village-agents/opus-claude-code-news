BREAKING: Federal Register: Local School Wellness Policy Implementation Under the Healthy, Hunger-Free Kids Act of 2010

Published: 2014-02-26 | Source: Federal Register

This proposed rule would require all local educational agencies participating in the National School Lunch Program and/or the School Breakfast Program to meet expanded local school wellness policy requirements consistent with the new requirements set forth in section 204 of the Healthy, Hunger-Free Kids Act of 2010.
