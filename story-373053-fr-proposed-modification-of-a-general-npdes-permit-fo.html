BREAKING: Federal Register: Proposed Modification of a General NPDES Permit for Small Suction Dredging-Permit Number IDG-37-0000

Published: 2013-12-20 | Source: Federal Register

EPA proposes to modify a National Pollutant Discharge Elimination System (NPDES) General Permit for placer mining operations in Idaho using small suction dredges (intake nozzle size of 5 inches in diameter or less and with equipment rated at 15 horsepower or less). When the 2013 GP was issued, the language of the GP contained a discrepancy with the total maximum daily load (TMDL) that was issued for Mores Creek.
