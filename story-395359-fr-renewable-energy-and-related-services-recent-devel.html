BREAKING: Federal Register: Renewable Energy and Related Services: Recent Developments

Published: 2012-08-31 | Source: Federal Register

Following receipt of a request on July 30, 2012 from the U.S. Trade Representative (USTR) under section 332(g) of the Tariff Act of 1930 (19 U.S.C. 1332(g)), the U.S. International Trade Commission (Commission) instituted investigation No. 332-534, Renewable Energy and Related Services: Recent Developments.
