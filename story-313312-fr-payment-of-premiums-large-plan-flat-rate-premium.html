BREAKING: Federal Register: Payment of Premiums; Large-Plan Flat-Rate Premium

Published: 2014-01-03 | Source: Federal Register

Based on its regulatory review under Executive Order 13563 (Improving Regulation and Regulatory Review), the Pension Benefit Guaranty Corporation (PBGC) is moving the flat-rate premium due date for large plans to later in the premium payment year--to the same date as the variable-rate premium due date for such plans--starting with the 2014 plan year.
