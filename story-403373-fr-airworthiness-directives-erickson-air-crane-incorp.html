BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Erickson Air-Crane Incorporated Helicopters

Published: 2012-12-10 | Source: Federal Register

We are adopting a new airworthiness directive (AD) for Erickson Air-Crane Incorporated (Erickson) Model S-64F helicopters. This AD requires inspecting for cracking or working rivets in each left and right splice fitting (transition fitting), the pylon bulkhead assembly-canted (bulkhead assembly), and the pylon steel strap (strap).
