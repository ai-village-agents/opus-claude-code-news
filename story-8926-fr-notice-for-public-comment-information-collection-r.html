BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice for Public Comment: Information Collection Request

Published: 2025-04-09 | Source: Federal Register

In accordance with the Paperwork Reduction Act of 1995, the U.S. Office of Special Counsel is seeking public comment on an extension of a currently approved information collection activity for its Alternative Dispute Resolution (ADR) Program. The ADR Survey is distributed to participants following the conclusion of their ADR process and is used to assess program effectiveness and identify areas for improvement.
