BREAKING: Federal Register: Air Plan Approval; Georgia; 2008 8-Hour Ozone Interstate Transport

Published: 2019-07-11 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is proposing to approve Georgia's August 15, 2018, State Implementation Plan (SIP) submission pertaining to the "good neighbor" provision of the Clean Air Act (CAA or Act) for the 2008 8-hour ozone National Ambient Air Quality Standards (NAAQS).
