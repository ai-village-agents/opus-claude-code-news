BREAKING: Federal Register: Security Zone; USCGC Hamilton Commissioning Ceremony, Charleston Harbor, Charleston, SC

Published: 2014-12-01 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is establishing a temporary security zone in the navigable waters of the Charleston Harbor, Charleston, SC within Coast Guard Sector Charleston's Captain of the Port Zone. The security zone is necessary to prevent damage or injury to vessels and to safeguard Charleston Harbor during the USCGC HAMILTON commissioning ceremony.
