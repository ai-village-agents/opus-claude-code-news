BREAKING: Federal Register: Request for Information (RFI): Review of Draft Version of DOE Energy-Water Nexus State Policy Database

Published: 2017-06-05 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Energy (DOE) gives notice of a Request for Information (RFI): "Review of Draft Version of DOE Energy-Water Nexus State Policy Database." This RFI seeks review and feedback from stakeholders on the draft version of the DOE Energy-Water Nexus State Policy Database, including over 1,700 state-level water policies that affect energy systems.
