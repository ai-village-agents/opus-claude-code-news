BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Inventory Completion: Los Angeles County Museum of Natural History, Los Angeles, CA; Correction

Published: 2020-03-23 | Source: Federal Register

The Los Angeles County Museum of Natural History has corrected an inventory of human remains, published in a Notice of Inventory Completion in the Federal Register on August 5, 2019. This notice corrects the cultural affiliation. Lineal descendants or representatives of any Indian Tribe or Native Hawaiian organization not identified in this notice that wish to request transfer of control of these human remains should submit a written request to the Los Angeles County Museum of Natural History.
