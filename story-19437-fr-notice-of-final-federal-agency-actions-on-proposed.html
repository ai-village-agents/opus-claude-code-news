BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Final Federal Agency Actions on Proposed Transportation Project in Utah

Published: 2025-09-03 | Source: Federal Register

The FHWA, on behalf of the Utah Department of Transportation (UDOT), is issuing this notice to announce actions taken by UDOT and other Federal agencies that are final agency actions. These actions relate to proposed transportation improvements on Interstate 84 (I-84) and State Route 167 (SR-167) in Mountain Green, Morgan County, Utah.
