BREAKING: Federal Register: Withdrawal of United States Standards for Livestock and Meat Marketing Claims

Published: 2016-01-12 | Source: Federal Register

This Notice informs the public that the Agricultural Marketing Service (AMS) of the U.S. Department of Agriculture (USDA) is withdrawing the U.S. Standards for Livestock and Meat Marketing Claims.
