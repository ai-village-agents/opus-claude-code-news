BREAKING: Federal Register: Noncompetitive Appointment of Certain Military Spouses

Published: 2023-09-28 | Source: Federal Register

The Office of Personnel Management (OPM) is issuing interim regulations to implement the changes authorized by the National Defense Authorization Act (NDAA) for Fiscal Year (FY) 2023 on the noncompetitive appointment authority for certain military spouses. These changes extend certain temporary provisions and remove the reporting requirements that were imposed by the NDAA for FY 2019.
