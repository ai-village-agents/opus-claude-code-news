BREAKING: Federal Register: Media Bureau Lifts Freeze on the Filing of Television Station Minor Modification Applications and Rulemaking Petitions

Published: 2020-11-12 | Source: Federal Register

This document announces that, effective [insert date], the Media Bureau is lifting the freezes on petitions for rulemaking to change channels in the DTV Table of Allotments, petitions for rulemaking for new DTV allotments, petitions for rulemaking to change communities of license, including changes in technical parameters, and modification applications that increase a full power or Class A station's service area beyond an area that is already served..
