BREAKING: Federal Register: Fiscal Year (FY) 2013 Funding Opportunity

Published: 2013-07-19 | Source: Federal Register

This notice is to inform the public that the Substance Abuse and Mental Health Services Administration (SAMHSA) intends to award approximately $200,000 (total costs) for up to one year to Link2Health Solutions, Inc. the current grantee for the National Suicide Prevention Lifeline. This is not a formal request for applications.
