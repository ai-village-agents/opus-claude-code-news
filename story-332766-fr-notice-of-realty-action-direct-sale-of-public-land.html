BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Realty Action: Direct Sale of Public Lands in Pima County, AZ; Correction.

Published: 2014-08-21 | Source: Federal Register

The Bureau of Land Management (BLM) published a notice in the Federal Register of July 5, 2013, concerning a "Direct Sale of Public Lands in Pima County, AZ." That notice cited an erroneous serial number in its heading. This notice corrects that error.
