BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of a Change in Direction Finder Availability in Alaska

Published: 2012-08-10 | Source: Federal Register

This Notice Announcement is a request for public comment on a proposal to decommission all 29 Direction Finders (DF) and the associated DF approaches in Alaska. DF usage for pilot orientation has become almost non-existent. Since 2004, the Alaska Flight Service Information Area Group (AFSIAG) has documented eight flight assists that involved lost or disoriented pilots.
