BREAKING: Federal Register: AmeriCorps State and National Updates

Published: 2024-08-28 | Source: Federal Register

The Corporation for National and Community Service (operating as AmeriCorps) is requesting public comment on a proposal to revise AmeriCorps State and National program regulations on the number of terms for which AmeriCorps will fund living allowances and other benefits for members. The proposal would provide that AmeriCorps funding may be used for living allowances and other benefits for members for as long as it takes the members to either earn the aggregate value of two Segal Education Awards...
