BREAKING: Federal Register: Parts and Accessories Necessary for Safe Operation; Hino Motors Manufacturing U.S.A., Inc.

Published: 2017-08-03 | Source: Federal Register

The Federal Motor Carrier Safety Administration (FMCSA) announces its decision to grant Hino Motors Manufacturing U.S.A., Inc.'s (Hino) application for a limited 5-year exemption allowing motor carriers operating commercial motor vehicles (CMVs) manufactured by the company to use an Automated Emergency Braking (AEB) system and a Lane Departure Warning (LDW) system camera mounted in the windshield area at a height lower than currently allowed.
