BREAKING: Federal Register: Prompt Corrective Action: Earnings Retention Waivers and Net Worth Restoration Plans

Published: 2022-02-28 | Source: Federal Register

The NCUA Board (Board) is extending two temporary changes to its prompt corrective action (PCA) regulations to help ensure that federally insured credit unions (FICUs) remain operational and liquid during the COVID-19 crisis. The first amends these regulations to temporarily extend the Board's ability to issue an order applicable to all FICUs to waive the earnings retention requirement for any FICU that is classified as adequately capitalized.
