BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone; Monte Foundation Fireworks Display, Soquel Cove, Capitola, CA

Published: 2019-10-11 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is establishing a temporary safety zone on the navigable waters of Soquel Cove near the Capitola Wharf in support of the Monte Foundation Fireworks Display on October 13, 2019. This safety zone is necessary to protect personnel, vessels, and the marine environment from the dangers associated with pyrotechnics.
