BREAKING: Federal Register: Agency Information Collection Activities; The Alternatives Process in Hydropower Licensing

Published: 2024-06-07 | Source: Federal Register

In accordance with the Paperwork Reduction Act of 1995, the Office of the Secretary, Office of Environmental Policy and Compliance, Department of the Interior, is requesting comments on the proposed renewal of an information collection request related to the alternatives process in hydropower licensing. This notice is being issued in coordination with the Department of Commerce and the Department of Agriculture.
