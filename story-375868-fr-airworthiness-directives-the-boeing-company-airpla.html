BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; The Boeing Company Airplanes

Published: 2012-01-25 | Source: Federal Register

We are adopting a new airworthiness directive (AD) for certain The Boeing Company Model 757-200, -200CB, and -300 series airplanes with off-wing escape slide systems installed. This AD was prompted by reports of in-flight loss of the off-wing escape slide.
