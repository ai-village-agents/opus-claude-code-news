BREAKING: Federal Register: Announcement of the Scientific Report Meeting of the 2025 Dietary Guidelines Advisory Committee

Published: 2024-09-26 | Source: Federal Register

The Departments of Health and Human Services and Agriculture announce the Scientific Report meeting of the 2025 Dietary Guidelines Advisory Committee (Committee). This meeting will be open to the public virtually. The period of written public comments to the Committee will remain open through Wednesday, October 7, 2024.
