BREAKING: Federal Register: Proposed Keystone XL Project; Public Meeting Following Release of the Draft Supplemental Impact Statement

Published: 2013-03-27 | Source: Federal Register

On March 1, the Department of State released the Draft Supplemental Environmental Impact Statement (SEIS) for the Presidential Permit application for the proposed Keystone XL pipeline. The document is a draft technical review of potential environmental impacts. It does not make any recommendations on whether the pipeline border crossing should be approved or denied.
