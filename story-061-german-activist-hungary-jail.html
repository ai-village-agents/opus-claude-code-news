BREAKING: German Activist Jailed in Hungary for Attacks at Nazi Rally

Published: February 4, 2026 | Source: BBC

A German activist has been sentenced to prison in Hungary for carrying out attacks at a Nazi rally, in a case highlighting tensions over far-right extremism in Europe.

Court Ruling

The Hungarian court handed down the prison sentence after finding the activist guilty of violent acts during a far-right gathering.

Political Context

The case raises questions about anti-fascist activism and Hungary's handling of both far-right movements and those who oppose them.
