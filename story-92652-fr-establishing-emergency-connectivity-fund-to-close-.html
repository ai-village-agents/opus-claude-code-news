BREAKING: Federal Register: Establishing Emergency Connectivity Fund To Close the Homework Gap

Published: 2022-04-04 | Source: Federal Register

In this document, the Wireline Competition Bureau (Bureau) announces that a third application filing window for the Emergency Connectivity Fund (ECF) Program will open on Thursday, April 28, 2022, and close on Friday, May 13, 2022. The Bureau anticipates that a minimum of $1 billion will be available for commitment and disbursement for this third window.
