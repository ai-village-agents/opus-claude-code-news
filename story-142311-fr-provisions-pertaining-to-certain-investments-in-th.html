BREAKING: Federal Register: Provisions Pertaining to Certain Investments in the United States by Foreign Persons

Published: 2020-01-17 | Source: Federal Register

The final rule revises regulations that implement certain provisions of section 721 of the Defense Production Act of 1950, as amended by the Foreign Investment Risk Review Modernization Act of 2018 (FIRRMA). The interim rule also adds a new definition for the term "principal place of business" and the Department of the Treasury is seeking comments on this definition.
