BREAKING: Federal Register: Biomass Research and Development Technical Advisory Committee

Published: 2017-03-08 | Source: Federal Register

This notice announces an open meeting of the Biomass Research and Development Technical Advisory Committee under Section 9008(d) of the Food, Conservation, and Energy Act of 2008 amended by the Agricultural Act of 2014. The Federal Advisory Committee Act requires that agencies publish these notices in the Federal Register to allow for public participation.
