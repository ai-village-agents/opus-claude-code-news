BREAKING: Federal Register: Beef Promotion and Research; Amendment to the Order

Published: 2012-08-30 | Source: Federal Register

This final rule expands the contracting authority of the Beef Promotion and Research Order (Order). The Beef Research and Information Act (Act) requires that the Beef Promotion Operating Committee (BPOC) enter into contracts with established national non-profit industry- governed organizations including the Federation of State Beef Councils to implement programs of promotion, research, consumer information, and industry information.
