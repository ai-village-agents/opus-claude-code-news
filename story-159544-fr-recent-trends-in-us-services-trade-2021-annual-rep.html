BREAKING: Federal Register: Recent Trends in U.S. Services Trade, 2021 Annual Report

Published: 2020-08-26 | Source: Federal Register

The Commission has prepared and published annual reports in this series under Investigation No. 332-345, Recent Trends in U.S. Services Trade, since 1996. The 2021 report, which the Commission plans to publish in April 2021, will provide aggregate data on cross-border trade in services for the period ending in 2019, and transactions by affiliates based outside the country of their parent firm for the period ending in 2018.
