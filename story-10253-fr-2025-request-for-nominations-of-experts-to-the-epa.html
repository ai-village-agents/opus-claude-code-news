BREAKING: Federal Register: 2025 Request for Nominations of Experts to the EPA's Human Studies Review Board Advisory Committee

Published: 2025-04-30 | Source: Federal Register

Pursuant to the Federal Advisory Committee Act (FACA), the Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) invites nominations from a diverse range of qualified candidates with expertise in the areas of toxicology, bioethics, and statistics to be considered for appointment to its Human Studies Review Board (HSRB).
