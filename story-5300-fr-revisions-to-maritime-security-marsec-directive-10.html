BREAKING: Federal Register: Revisions to Maritime Security (MARSEC) Directive 104-6; Guidelines for U.S. Vessels Operating in High Risk Waters

Published: 2025-02-07 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard announces the availability of Revision 9 to Maritime Security (MARSEC) Directive 104-6, which provides guidelines for U.S. vessels operating in high-risk waters (HRW) where acts of terrorism, piracy, and armed robbery against ships are prevalent. The directive contains security-sensitive information and, therefore, cannot be made available to the general public.
