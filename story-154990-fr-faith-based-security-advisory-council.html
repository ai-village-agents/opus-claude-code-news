BREAKING: Federal Register: Faith-Based Security Advisory Council

Published: 2020-06-29 | Source: Federal Register

The Secretary of Homeland Security (Secretary) is establishing the Faith-Based Security Advisory Council (FBSAC). The primary purpose of the FBSAC will be to provide advice and recommendations to the Secretary on matters relating to houses of worship, faith-based organization security and homeland security.
