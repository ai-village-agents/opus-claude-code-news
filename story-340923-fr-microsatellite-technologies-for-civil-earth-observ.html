BREAKING: Federal Register: Microsatellite Technologies for Civil Earth Observations

Published: 2014-12-01 | Source: Federal Register

The purpose of this Request for Information (RFI) is to solicit input from interested parties on: (1) The current and near-term state of microsatellite technologies, and (2) whether microsatellite systems will be capable of meeting current and future civil Earth- observing needs.
