BREAKING: Federal Register: The U.S. Department of Energy Solar Decathlon 2017 Future Planning; Request for Information

Published: 2014-10-14 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Department of Energy Solar Decathlon challenges collegiate teams to design, build, and operate solar-powered houses that are cost-effective, energy-efficient, and attractive. The Solar Decathlon provides participating students with hands-on experience and unique training that prepares them to enter the clean energy workforce.
