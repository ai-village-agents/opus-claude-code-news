BREAKING: Federal Register: National Pollutant Discharge Elimination System (NPDES) Industrial Stormwater Fact Sheet Series

EPA's industrial stormwater program has 29 fact sheets currently posted online for each sector covered under the 2021 Multi-Sector General Permit (MSGP) for stormwater discharges from industrial activity.
