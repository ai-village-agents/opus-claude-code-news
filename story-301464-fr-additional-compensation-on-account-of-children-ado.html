BREAKING: Federal Register: Additional Compensation on Account of Children Adopted Out of Veteran's Family

Published: 2015-08-13 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Veterans Affairs (VA) is amending its adjudication regulations to clarify that a veteran will not receive the dependent rate of disability compensation for a child who is adopted out of the veteran's family.
