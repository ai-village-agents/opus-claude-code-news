BREAKING: Federal Register: Advisory Committee; Gastrointestinal Drugs Advisory Committee; Renewal; Correction

Published: 2022-05-23 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA) is correcting a notice entitled "Advisory Committee; Gastrointestinal Drugs Advisory Committee; Renewal" that appeared in the Federal Register of May 11, 2022. The document announced the renewal of the Gastrointestinal Drugs Advisory Committee. The document was published with the incorrect docket number. This document corrects that error.
