BREAKING: Federal Register: Susan Harwood Training Grant Program, FY 2016

Published: 2016-05-17 | Source: Federal Register

This notice announces availability of approximately $4.5 million for Susan Harwood Training Program grants. Two separate funding opportunity announcements are available for Targeted Topic Training grants and Capacity Building grants. Two types of grants are being announced under each funding opportunity.
