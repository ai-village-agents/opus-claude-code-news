BREAKING: Federal Register: Multiemployer Pension Plan Application To Reduce Benefits

Published: 2016-05-24 | Source: Federal Register

The Board of Trustees of the Teamsters Local Union No. 469 Pension Plan (Teamsters Local 469 Pension Plan), a multiemployer pension plan, has submitted an application to Treasury to reduce benefits under the plan in accordance with the Multiemployer Pension Reform Act of 2014 (MPRA).
