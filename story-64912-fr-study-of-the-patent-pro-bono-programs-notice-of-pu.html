BREAKING: Federal Register: Study of the Patent Pro Bono Programs; Notice of Public Listening Sessions and Request for Comments

Published: 2023-04-12 | Source: Federal Register

The United States Patent and Trademark Office (USPTO) seeks public comments on areas related to the study of the patent pro bono programs identified in the Unleashing American Innovators Act of 2022. This study builds upon the work the USPTO has conducted for over a decade and has scaled during the Biden Administration, to bring more people in America into the innovation ecosystem to create more jobs, foster economic prosperity, and solve world problems.
