BREAKING: Federal Register: Labeling Relief; Formaldehyde Emission Standards for Composite Wood Products

Published: 2017-07-11 | Source: Federal Register

EPA is taking direct final action to amend a final rule that published in the Federal Register on December 12, 2016 concerning formaldehyde emission standards for composite wood products. The amendment will allow compliant composite wood products and finished goods that contain compliant composite wood products that were manufactured prior to December 12, 2017 to be labeled as Toxic Substances Control Act (TSCA) Title VI compliant.
