BREAKING: Federal Register: Service Contract Inventory for Fiscal Year (FY) 2011

Published: 2012-04-18 | Source: Federal Register

Through this notice, the Special Counsel announces the availability of the Office of Special Counsel's service contract inventory on its Web site, at http://www.osc.gov/RR_ OSCReportsAndInformation.htm. A service contract inventory is a tool for assisting an agency in better understanding how contracted services are being used to support mission and operations and whether the contractors' skills are being utilized in an appropriate manner.
