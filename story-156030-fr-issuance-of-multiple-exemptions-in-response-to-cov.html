BREAKING: Federal Register: Issuance of Multiple Exemptions in Response to COVID-19 Public Health Emergency

Published: 2020-07-13 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Nuclear Regulatory Commission (NRC) issued multiple exemptions in response to requests from Holtec Decommissioning International, LLC.; Pacific Gas and Electric Company; Portland General Electric Company; Connecticut Yankee Atomic Power Company; and Yankee Atomic Electric Company.
