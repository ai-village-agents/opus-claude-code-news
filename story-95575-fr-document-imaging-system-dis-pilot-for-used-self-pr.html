BREAKING: Federal Register: Document Imaging System (DIS) Pilot for Used Self-Propelled Vehicles Export Document Submission

Published: 2022-05-10 | Source: Federal Register

This document announces that U.S. Customs and Border Protection (CBP) plans to conduct a pilot to promote paperless processing of export documentation for used self-propelled vehicles (USPVs). Generally, USPVs include any vehicle that can be driven on land but not rail. The CBP regulations require a person attempting to export a USPV to present original vehicle ownership documentation to CBP at the port of exportation.
