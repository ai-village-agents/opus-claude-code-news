BREAKING: Federal Register: Department of State Acquisition Regulation

Published: 2013-07-29 | Source: Federal Register

This proposed rule will update the Department of State Acquisition Regulation (DOSAR) to conform to recent Federal Acquisition Regulation (FAR) changes and adds a new DOSAR clause and provision regarding reporting certain categories of Government-furnished and contractor-acquired property.
