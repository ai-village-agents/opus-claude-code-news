BREAKING: Federal Register: Great Lakes Steamship Repower Incentive Program

Published: 2012-01-18 | Source: Federal Register

EPA is taking direct final action to simplify an existing provision in our marine diesel engine program that is intended to encourage owners of Great Lakes steamships to repower those steamships with cleaner marine diesel engines.
