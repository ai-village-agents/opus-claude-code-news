BREAKING: Federal Register: Export Administration Regulations: Revisions to Space-Related Export Controls

Published: 2024-10-23 | Source: Federal Register

In this interim final rule (IFR), the Bureau of Industry and Security (BIS) makes changes to controls for spacecraft and related items under the Export Administration Regulations (EAR). This IFR reduces license requirements on less sensitive items to reflect the close relations with certain countries to better facilitate space collaboration; and makes refinements and clarifications to existing controls.
