BREAKING: Federal Register: Copayments for Medications Beginning January 1, 2017

Published: 2016-01-05 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Veterans Affairs (VA) proposes to amend its regulations concerning copayments charged to certain veterans for medication required on an outpatient basis to treat non-service connected conditions.
