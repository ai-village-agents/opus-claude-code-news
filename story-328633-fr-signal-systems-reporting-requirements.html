BREAKING: Federal Register: Signal Systems Reporting Requirements

Published: 2014-07-02 | Source: Federal Register

FRA is issuing this final rule as part of a paperwork reduction initiative. The final rule eliminates the regulatory requirement that each railroad carrier file a signal system status report with FRA every five years. FRA believes the report is no longer necessary because FRA receives more updated information regarding railroad signal systems through alternative sources.
