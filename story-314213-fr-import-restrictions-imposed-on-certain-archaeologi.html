BREAKING: Federal Register: Import Restrictions Imposed on Certain Archaeological and Ecclesiastical Ethnological Material From Bulgaria

Published: 2014-01-16 | Source: Federal Register

This final rule amends the U.S. Customs and Border Protection (CBP) regulations to reflect the imposition of import restrictions on certain archaeological and ecclesiastical ethnological material from the Republic of Bulgaria.
