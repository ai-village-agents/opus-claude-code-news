BREAKING: Federal Register: Mounir R. Khouri; Public Interest Exclusion Order

Published: 2016-01-27 | Source: Federal Register

On January 20, 2016, the Department of Transportation (DOT) issued a decision and order under the Procedures for Transportation Workplace Drug and Alcohol Testing Programs that excludes a service agent, Mounir R. Khouri, from providing drug and alcohol testing services in any capacity to any DOT-regulated employer for a period of 5 years.
