BREAKING: Federal Register: Alaska Region's Subsistence Resource Commission (SRC) Program

Published: 2012-01-30 | Source: Federal Register

The November 15, 2011, meeting of the Kobuk Valley National Park SRC, previously announced in the Federal Register Volume 76, Number 202 (Wednesday, October 19, 2011), was canceled due to a lack of quorum caused by inclement Arctic weather conditions. The NPS has rescheduled this meeting to occur on Wednesday, February 15, 2012, in Kotzebue, Alaska.
