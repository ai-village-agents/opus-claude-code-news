BREAKING: Federal Register: Air Traffic Data in the Possession of Government Contractors

Published: 2012-08-28 | Source: Federal Register

The recently enacted Pilot's Bill of Rights (PBR) provides, among other things, that "air traffic data" should be made accessible to, or obtainable by, an airman in Federal Aviation Administration (FAA) investigations when such data are in the FAA's possession and the data will facilitate the individual's ability to participate in a proceeding related to an FAA investigation.
