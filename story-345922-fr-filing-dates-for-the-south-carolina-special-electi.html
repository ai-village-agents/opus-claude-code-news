BREAKING: Federal Register: Filing Dates for the South Carolina Special Elections in the 1st Congressional District

Published: 2013-02-04 | Source: Federal Register

South Carolina has scheduled special elections to fill the U.S. House seat in the 1st Congressional District vacated by Senator Tim Scott. There are three possible special elections, but only two may be necessary. Primary Election: March 19, 2013. Possible Runoff Election: April 2, 2013.
