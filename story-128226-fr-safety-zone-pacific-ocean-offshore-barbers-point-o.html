BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone; Pacific Ocean, Offshore Barbers Point, Oahu, HI-Recovery Operations

Published: 2021-07-14 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is establishing a temporary safety zone for the navigable waters of the Southwest shores of Oahu, Hawaii, near Barbers Point. The temporary safety zone encompasses all waters extending 3 nautical miles in all directions from position 21[deg]16'36" N, 158[deg]01'42" W. The safety zone is needed to protect personnel, vessels, and the marine environment from potential hazards associated with ongoing operations to salvage a downed aircraft in this area.
