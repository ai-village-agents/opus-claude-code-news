BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Availability of Funds and Solicitation for Grant Applications for Disability Employment Initiative Grants

Published: 2014-06-03 | Source: Federal Register

The Employment and Training Administration (ETA), in coordination with the Department's Office of Disability Employment Policy, announces the availability of approximately $15 million in grant funds authorized by Section171 of the Workforce Investment Act of 1998 for the Round V Disability Employment Initiative.
