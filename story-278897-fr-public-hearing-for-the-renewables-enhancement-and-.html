BREAKING: Federal Register: Public Hearing for the Renewables Enhancement and Growth Support Rule

Published: 2016-11-10 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is announcing a public hearing to be held in Chicago, Illinois on December 6, 2016, on its proposal for the "Renewables Enhancement and Growth Support (REGS) Rule." The public can view the proposal at https://www.epa.gov/ renewable-fuel-standard-program/proposed-renewables-enhancement-and- growth-support-regs-rule.
