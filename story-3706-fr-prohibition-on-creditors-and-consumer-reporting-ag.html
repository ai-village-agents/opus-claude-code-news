BREAKING: Federal Register: Prohibition on Creditors and Consumer Reporting Agencies Concerning Medical Information (Regulation V)

Published: 2025-01-14 | Source: Federal Register

The Consumer Financial Protection Bureau (CFPB) is issuing a final rule amending Regulation V, which implements the Fair Credit Reporting Act (FCRA), concerning medical information. The FCRA prohibits creditors from considering medical information in credit eligibility determinations. The CFPB is removing a regulatory exception that had permitted creditors to obtain and use information on medical debts notwithstanding this statutory limitation.
