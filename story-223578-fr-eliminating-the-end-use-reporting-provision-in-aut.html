BREAKING: Federal Register: Eliminating the End Use Reporting Provision in Authorizations for the Export of Liquefied Natural Gas

Published: 2018-12-19 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Energy's Office of Fossil Energy (DOE/FE) is discontinuing its practice, adopted in 2016, of including an "end use" reporting provision in orders authorizing the export of domestically produced natural gas, including liquefied natural gas (LNG), issued under section 3 of the Natural Gas Act (NGA).
