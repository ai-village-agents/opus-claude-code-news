BREAKING: Federal Register: Submission for Review: OPM Form 1654-B, Combined Federal Campaign Federal Retiree Pledge Form

Published: 2017-10-30 | Source: Federal Register

The Office of Combined Federal Campaign, Office of Personnel Management (OPM) offers the general public and other federal agencies the opportunity to comment on an information collection request (ICR) 3206-NEW, OPM Form 1654-B, the Combined Federal Campaign Retiree Pledge Form. As required by the Paperwork Reduction Act of 1995, as amended by the Clinger-Cohen Act, OPM is soliciting comments for this collection.
