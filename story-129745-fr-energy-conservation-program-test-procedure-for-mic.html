BREAKING: Federal Register: Energy Conservation Program: Test Procedure for Microwave Ovens

Published: 2021-08-03 | Source: Federal Register

On November 14, 2019, the U.S. Department of Energy ("DOE") published a notice of proposed rulemaking ("NOPR") for the test procedure for microwave ovens. Following receipt of comments, DOE is publishing this supplemental notice of proposed rulemaking ("SNOPR") for the limited purpose of clarifying the current procedure for testing a microwave oven that has a connected (i.e., network) function, which is generally to disable the connected function when measuring standby mode power consumption.
