BREAKING: Federal Register: Dried Prunes Produced in California; Increased Assessment Rate

Published: 2014-03-04 | Source: Federal Register

This rule increases the assessment rate established for the Prune Marketing Committee (Committee) for the 2013-14 and subsequent crop years from $0.22 to $0.28 per ton of salable dried prunes handled. The Committee locally administers the marketing order, which regulates the handling of dried prunes grown in California.
