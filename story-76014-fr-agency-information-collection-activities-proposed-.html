BREAKING: Federal Register: Agency Information Collection Activities: Proposed Collection; Comment Request; Requests for Special Priorities Assistance

Published: 2023-09-01 | Source: Federal Register

The Federal Emergency Management Agency (FEMA), as part of its continuing effort to reduce paperwork and respondent burden, invites the general public to take this opportunity to comment on an extension, without change, of a currently approved information collection. In accordance with the Paperwork Reduction Act of 1995, this notice seeks comments concerning FEMA's Requests for Special Priorities Assistance, FEMA Form FF-112-FY-23-100 (009-0-142).
