BREAKING: Federal Register: Federal Travel Regulation; Rental Car Policy Updates and Clarifications

Published: 2021-09-13 | Source: Federal Register

This proposed rule would clarify that agencies can reimburse rental car fees (up to a certain number of days) for Outside the Continental United States (OCONUS) relocations as a miscellaneous expense allowance to account for privately owned vehicle (POV) shipping delays at the employee's new official station.
