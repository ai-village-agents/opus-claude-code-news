BREAKING: Federal Register: Alternatives to the Use of Credit Ratings

Published: 2023-01-03 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Nuclear Regulatory Commission (NRC) is proposing to amend its regulations for approved financial assurance mechanisms for decommissioning, specifically for parent and self-company guarantees that require bond ratings issued by credit rating agencies.
