BREAKING: Federal Register: Importation of Fresh Unshu Oranges From Japan Into the United States

Published: 2014-10-27 | Source: Federal Register

We are amending the regulations concerning the importation of citrus fruit to remove certain restrictions on the importation of Unshu oranges from Japan. Specifically, we are removing requirements for the fruit to be grown in specified canker-free export areas with buffer zones and for joint inspection in the groves and packinghouses by the Government of Japan and the Animal and Plant Health Inspection Service.
