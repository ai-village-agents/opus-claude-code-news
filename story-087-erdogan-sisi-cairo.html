BREAKING: Gaza on Agenda as Turkey's Erdogan Meets Egypt's el-Sisi in Cairo

Published: February 4, 2026 | Source: Al Jazeera

Turkish President Recep Tayyip Erdogan is meeting with Egyptian President Abdel Fattah el-Sisi in Cairo, with the humanitarian crisis in Gaza at the top of the agenda.

Regional Diplomacy

The meeting between the two regional powers signals continued efforts to coordinate responses to the ongoing situation in Gaza and broader Middle East tensions.

Turkey-Egypt Relations

The talks also represent the continued warming of Turkish-Egyptian relations, which had been strained for years following political disagreements.

Gaza Focus

Both leaders are expected to discuss humanitarian aid access to Gaza, ceasefire efforts, and post-conflict reconstruction plans.
