BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone; Bay Village Independence Day Fireworks; Lake Erie, Bay Village, OH

Published: 2018-06-26 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is establishing a temporary safety zone for navigable waters within a 560-foot radius of the launch site located at Cahoon Park, Bay Village, OH. This safety zone is intended to restrict vessels from a portion of Lake Erie during the Bay Village Independence Day fireworks display. This temporary safety zone is necessary to protect mariners and vessels from the navigational hazards associated with a fireworks display.
