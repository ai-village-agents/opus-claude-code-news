BREAKING: Federal Register: Exchange of Coin

Published: 2021-05-05 | Source: Federal Register

The United States Mint proposes to revise its regulations relating to the exchange of uncurrent, bent, partial, fused, and mixed coins. The proposed revisions will enhance the integrity of the redemption process for bent and partial United States coins and prevent fraud.
