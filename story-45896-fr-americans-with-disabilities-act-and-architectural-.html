BREAKING: Federal Register: Americans With Disabilities Act and Architectural Barriers Act Accessibility Guidelines; EV Charging Stations

Published: 2024-09-03 | Source: Federal Register

The Architectural and Transportation Barriers Compliance Board (hereafter, "Access Board" or "Board"), is issuing this notice of proposed rulemaking to amend the accessibility guidelines for buildings and facilities covered by the Americans with Disabilities Act of 1990 (ADA) and the Architectural Barriers Act of 1968 (ABA) to specifically address the accessibility of Electric Vehicle Charging stations.
