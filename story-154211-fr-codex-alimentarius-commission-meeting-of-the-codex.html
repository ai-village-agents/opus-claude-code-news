BREAKING: Federal Register: Codex Alimentarius Commission: Meeting of the Codex Alimentarius Commission

Published: 2020-06-18 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S Codex Office is sponsoring a public meeting on June 25, 2020. The objective of the public meeting is to provide information and receive public comments on agenda items and draft United States (U.S.) positions for the 43rd Session of the of the Codex Alimentarius Commission (CAC).
