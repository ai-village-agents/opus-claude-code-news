BREAKING: Federal Register: Polyglycerol Polyricinoleate; Tolerance Exemption

Published: 2017-03-22 | Source: Federal Register

This regulation establishes an exemption from the requirement of a tolerance for residues of polyglycerol polyricinoleate when used as an inert ingredient in a pesticide chemical formulation. AgroFresh Inc., submitted a petition to EPA under the Federal Food, Drug, and Cosmetic Act (FFDCA), requesting an exemption from the requirement of a tolerance.
