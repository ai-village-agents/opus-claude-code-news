BREAKING: Federal Register: The New York North Shore Helicopter Route

Published: 2012-07-06 | Source: Federal Register

This action requires helicopter pilots to use the New York North Shore Helicopter Route when operating along the north shore of Long Island, New York. The North Shore Helicopter Route was added to the New York Helicopter Route Chart in 2008 and prior to this action, its use has been voluntary.
