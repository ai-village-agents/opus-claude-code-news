BREAKING: Federal Register: Comprehensive Review of the Military Justice System

Published: 2014-05-19 | Source: Federal Register

The Secretary of Defense directed the General Counsel of the Department of Defense (the General Counsel) to conduct a comprehensive review of the military justice system. To carry out the review, the General Counsel established the Military Justice Review Group (MJRG). The MJRG's review is focused on the structure and operation of the Uniform Code of Military Justice (UCMJ) and the Manual for Courts- Martial (MCM).
