BREAKING: Federal Register: Environmental Impact Statement for Shawnee Fossil Plant Coal Combustion Residual Management

Published: 2016-11-01 | Source: Federal Register

The Tennessee Valley Authority (TVA) intends to prepare an environmental impact statement (EIS) to address the potential environmental effects associated with ceasing operations at the special waste landfill and Ash Pond 2 and constructing, operating, and maintaining a new dry coal combustion residual (CCR) landfill at the Shawnee Fossil Plant (SHF) located near Paducah, Kentucky in McCracken County.
