BREAKING: Federal Register: Foreign Trade Regulations (FTR): Clarification on Filing Requirements; Correction

Published: 2017-09-20 | Source: Federal Register

On April 19, 2017, the Census Bureau revised the Foreign Trade Regulations (FTR). The revisions to the FTR reflected the implementation of the International Trade Data System, in accordance with the Executive Order 13659, Streamlining the Export/Import Process for American Businesses. In the Final Rule, the Census Bureau also amended the appendices section. Appendices B, C, E, and F were removed and appendix D was revised and redesignated as the new appendix B.
