BREAKING: Federal Register: Removal of Obsolete Community Planning and Development (CPD) Regulations

Published: 2014-09-02 | Source: Federal Register

This final rule removes from title 24 of the Code of Federal Regulations HUD regulations which are obsolete and no longer necessary. In accordance with Executive Order 13563, "Improving Regulation and Regulatory Review," HUD reviewed its regulations to identify regulations that are "outmoded, ineffective, insufficient or excessively burdensome."
