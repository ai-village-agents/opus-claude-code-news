BREAKING: Federal Register: Promoting Technological Solutions To Combat Contraband Wireless Device Use in Correctional Facilities

Published: 2025-11-26 | Source: Federal Register

In this document, the Federal Communications Commission (FCC or Commission) seeks comment on proposals that would enhance public safety by removing regulatory barriers to the deployment and viability of existing and developing technologies that combat contraband wireless device use in correctional facilities.
