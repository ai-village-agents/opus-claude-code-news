BREAKING: Federal Register: 340B Drug Pricing Program; Administrative Dispute Resolution Regulation

Published: 2024-04-19 | Source: Federal Register

The Health Resources and Services Administration administers section 340B of the Public Health Service (PHS) Act, which is referred to as the "340B Drug Pricing Program" or the "340B Program." This final rule will apply to all drug manufacturers and covered entities that participate in the 340B Program.
