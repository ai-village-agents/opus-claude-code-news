BREAKING: Federal Register: Clarification of Bales Made Available for Shipment by CCC-Approved Warehouses

Published: 2013-09-26 | Source: Federal Register

The Commodity Credit Corporation (CCC) proposes to amend the regulations that specify the requirements for CCC-approved warehouses storing cotton. The amendment would change the definition of Bales Made Available for Shipment (BMAS). CCC-approved cotton warehouses are currently required to report BMAS, among other data, to CCC every week.
