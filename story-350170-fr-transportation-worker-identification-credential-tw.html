BREAKING: Federal Register: Transportation Worker Identification Credential (TWIC)-Reader Requirements

Published: 2013-03-22 | Source: Federal Register

In this Notice of Proposed Rulemaking (NPRM), the Coast Guard proposes to require owners and operators of certain vessels and facilities regulated by the Coast Guard to use electronic readers designed to work with the Transportation Worker Identification Credential (TWIC) as an access control measure.
