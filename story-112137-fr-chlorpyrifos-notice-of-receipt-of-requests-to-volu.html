BREAKING: Federal Register: Chlorpyrifos; Notice of Receipt of Requests To Voluntarily Cancel Certain Pesticide Registrations and Amend Registrations To Terminate/Amend Certain Uses

Published: 2022-12-13 | Source: Federal Register

In accordance with the Federal Insecticide, Fungicide and Rodenticide Act (FIFRA), EPA is issuing a notice of receipt of requests by the registrants to voluntarily cancel certain product registrations containing the pesticide chlorpyrifos and to amend their chlorpyrifos registrations to terminate one or more uses.
