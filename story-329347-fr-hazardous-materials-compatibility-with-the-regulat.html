BREAKING: Federal Register: Hazardous Materials: Compatibility With the Regulations of the International Atomic Energy Agency (RRR)

Published: 2014-07-11 | Source: Federal Register

PHMSA, in coordination with the Nuclear Regulatory Commission (NRC), is amending requirements in the Hazardous Materials Regulations (HMR) governing the transportation of Class 7 (radioactive) materials based on recent changes contained in the International Atomic Energy Agency (IAEA) publication "Regulations for the Safe Transport of Radioactive Material, 2009 Edition, IAEA Safety Standards Series No. TS-R-1."
