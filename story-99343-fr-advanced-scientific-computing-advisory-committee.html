BREAKING: Federal Register: Advanced Scientific Computing Advisory Committee

Published: 2022-06-29 | Source: Federal Register

This notice announces an open hybrid meeting of the Advanced Scientific Computing Advisory Committee (ASCAC). Due to the COVID-19 pandemic, we are strongly encouraging virtual participation by members of the public. There will, however, be limited seating available for the public at the Westin, listed below. The Federal Advisory Committee Act requires that public notice of these meetings be announced in the Federal Register.
