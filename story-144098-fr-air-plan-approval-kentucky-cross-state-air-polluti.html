BREAKING: Federal Register: Air Plan Approval; Kentucky: Cross-State Air Pollution Rule

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is approving changes to the Kentucky State Implementation Plan (SIP) concerning the Cross- State Air Pollution Rule (CSAPR) submitted by Kentucky on September 14, 2018, as later clarified on December 18, 2018.
