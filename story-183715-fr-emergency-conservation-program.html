BREAKING: Federal Register: Emergency Conservation Program

Published: 2019-07-10 | Source: Federal Register

The Agriculture Improvement Act of 2018 (2018 Farm Bill) amended provisions of the Emergency Conservation Program (ECP). This rule implements those changes to ECP and makes additional minor technical amendments to the ECP regulations.
