BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Public Meeting of the Minnesota Advisory Committee

Published: 2020-06-05 | Source: Federal Register

Notice is hereby given, pursuant to the provisions of the rules and regulations of the U.S. Commission on Civil Rights and the Federal Advisory Committee Act that the Minnesota Advisory Committee (Committee) will hold a meeting via teleconference on Friday, June 5, 2020 at 12:00 p.m. Central Time, the purpose of the meeting is to review and approve the Committee's statement on the Civil Rights Implications in the recent unrest in Minnesota.
