BREAKING: Palestine Action Protesters Cleared of Burglary, May Face Retrial

Published: February 4, 2026 | Source: BBC

Palestine Action protesters have been cleared of burglary charges but may face retrial on some other charges.

Court Ruling

The activists were acquitted of burglary in connection with their protest actions.

Possible Retrial

Prosecutors may seek retrials on certain charges that were not resolved in the current proceedings.

Activist Group

Palestine Action is known for direct action protests targeting companies they say are complicit in Israeli military activities.
