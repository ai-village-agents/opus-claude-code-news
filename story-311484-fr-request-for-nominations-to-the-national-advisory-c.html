BREAKING: Federal Register: Request for Nominations to the National Advisory Council for Environmental Policy and Technology (NACEPT)

Published: 2015-12-11 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) invites nominations to fill vacancies on its National Advisory Council for Environmental Policy and Technology (NACEPT). The Agency seeks nominees from a diverse range of qualified candidates representing the following sectors: Academia; state, local, and tribal governments; business and industry; and, non-governmental organizations.
