BREAKING: Federal Register: Solicitation of Nominations to the Presidential Advisory Council on HIV/AIDS

Published: 2013-03-07 | Source: Federal Register

The Office of the Assistant Secretary for Health (OASH) is seeking nominations of qualified individuals to be considered for appointment as members of the Presidential Advisory Council on HIV/AIDS (PACHA). The PACHA is a federal advisory committee within the Department of Health and Human Services (HHS). Management support for the activities of the Council is the responsibility of the OASH.
