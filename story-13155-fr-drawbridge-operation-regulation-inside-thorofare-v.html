BREAKING: Federal Register: Drawbridge Operation Regulation; Inside Thorofare, Ventnor City, New Jersey

Published: 2025-06-09 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard proposes to modify the operating schedule that governs the Dorset Avenue Bridge across Inside Thorofare, mile 72.1 at Ventnor City, NJ. The proposed rule would allow the drawbridge to only open between the hours of 11 p.m. to 7 a.m. if at least four hours' notice is given. We invite your comments on this proposed rulemaking.
