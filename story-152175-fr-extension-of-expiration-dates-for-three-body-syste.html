BREAKING: Federal Register: Extension of Expiration Dates for Three Body System Listings

Published: 2020-05-21 | Source: Federal Register

We are extending the expiration dates of the following body systems in the Listing of Impairments (listings) in our regulations: Low Birth Weight and Failure to Thrive, Endocrine Disorders, and Cancer (Malignant Neoplastic Diseases). We are making no other revisions to these body systems in this final rule.
