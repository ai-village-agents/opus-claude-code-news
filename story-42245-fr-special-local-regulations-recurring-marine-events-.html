BREAKING: Federal Register: Special Local Regulations; Recurring Marine Events, Sector Key West, Update

Published: 2024-07-19 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is revising existing regulations by updating the table for existing events in the Seventh Coast Guard District Captain of the Port (COTP) Key West. This action is necessary to provide for the safety of life on these navigable waters in Key West, FL. Through this rule, the current list of recurring special local regulations is updated with the removal of an event that no longer takes place, the addition of two events, and revisions to existing events.
