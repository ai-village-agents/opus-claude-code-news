BREAKING: Federal Register: Walnuts Grown in California; Increased Assessment Rate

Published: 2015-10-28 | Source: Federal Register

This rule implements a recommendation from the California Walnut Board (Board) for an increase of the assessment rate established for the 2015-16 and subsequent marketing years from $0.0189 to $0.0379 per kernelweight pound of walnuts handled under the marketing order. The Board locally administers the marketing order and is comprised of growers and handlers of walnuts operating within the area of production.
