Published: 2020-09-29 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA) is correcting a notice that appeared in the Federal Register on July 21, 2020. The document announced the withdrawal of approval of 10 abbreviated new drug applications (ANDAs) from multiple applicants, withdrawn as of August 20, 2020.
