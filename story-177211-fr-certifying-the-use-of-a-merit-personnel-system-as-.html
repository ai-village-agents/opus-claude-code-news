BREAKING: Federal Register: Certifying the Use of a Merit Personnel System as Required by the Intergovernmental Personnel Act of 1970

Published: 2019-04-19 | Source: Federal Register

OPM wishes to publicly respond to inquiries regarding the available range of staffing options for federally-funded and state- administered low-income programs that are required to comply with the Intergovernmental Personnel Act of 1970 (IPA) and its implementing regulations.
