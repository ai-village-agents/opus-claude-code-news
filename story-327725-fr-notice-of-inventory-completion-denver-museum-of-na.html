BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Inventory Completion: Denver Museum of Nature & Science, Denver, CO

Published: 2014-06-24 | Source: Federal Register

The Denver Museum of Nature & Science has completed an inventory of human remains and associated funerary object, in consultation with Indian tribes and Native Hawaiian organizations, and has determined that there is no cultural affiliation between the human remains and associated funerary object and any present-day Indian tribes or Native Hawaiian organizations.
