BREAKING: Federal Register: Food Additives Permitted in Feed and Drinking Water of Animals; Ammonium Formate and Formic Acid

Published: 2017-11-13 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA, we, the Agency) is amending food additive regulations for food additives permitted in feed and drinking water of animals to provide for the safe use of formic acid and ammonium formate.
