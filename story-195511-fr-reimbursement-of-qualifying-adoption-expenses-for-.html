BREAKING: Federal Register: Reimbursement of Qualifying Adoption Expenses for Certain Veterans

Published: 2019-12-13 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Veterans Affairs (VA) adopts as final, with changes based on subsequent statutory authority, an interim final rule providing for reimbursement of qualifying adoption expenses incurred by a veteran with a service-connected disability that results in the inability of the veteran to procreate without the use of fertility treatment.
