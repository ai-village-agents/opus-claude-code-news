BREAKING: Federal Register: Radio Frequency Devices, FCC Form 740 Temporary Suspension

Published: 2015-11-05 | Source: Federal Register

This document temporarily waives the requirements of the Commission's rules that govern the submission of information associated with FCC Form 740 concerning imported Radio Frequency (RF) devices. U.S. Customs and Border Protection (CBP) is implementing a new electronic filing system which is scheduled to become fully operational by December 2016.
