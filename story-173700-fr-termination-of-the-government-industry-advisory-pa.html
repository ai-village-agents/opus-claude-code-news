BREAKING: Federal Register: Termination of the Government-Industry Advisory Panel

Published: 2019-03-11 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Defense is publishing this notice to announce that it is terminating the Government-Industry Advisory Panel ("the Panel"), effective March 4, 2019.
