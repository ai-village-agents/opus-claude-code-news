BREAKING: Federal Register: Proposed Revocation, Establishment, and Amendment of Class E Airspace; Multiple Texas Towns

Published: 2020-06-08 | Source: Federal Register

This action proposes to revoke the Class E airspace extending upward from 700 feet above the surface at Ambassador Field, Big Sandy, TX; and establish and amend Class E airspace extending upward from 700 feet above the surface at several Texas airports.
