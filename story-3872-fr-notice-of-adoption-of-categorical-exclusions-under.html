BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Adoption of Categorical Exclusions Under Section 109 of the National Environmental Policy Act

Published: 2025-01-15 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of the Interior is notifying the public and documenting the adoption of two U.S. Forest Service, one National Park Service, and three U.S. Fish and Wildlife Service categorical exclusions (CXs) by the Bureau of Land Management (BLM), under section 109 of the National Environmental Policy Act (NEPA).
