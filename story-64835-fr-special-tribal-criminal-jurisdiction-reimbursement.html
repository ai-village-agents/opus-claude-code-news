BREAKING: Federal Register: Special Tribal Criminal Jurisdiction Reimbursement

Published: 2023-04-11 | Source: Federal Register

The Violence Against Women Act Reauthorization Act of 2022 (VAWA 2022) authorized a new program to reimburse Tribal governments (or authorized designees of Tribal governments) for expenses incurred in exercising "special Tribal criminal jurisdiction" (STCJ) over non- Indians who commit certain covered crimes in Indian country.
