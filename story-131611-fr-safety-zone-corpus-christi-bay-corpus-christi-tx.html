BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone; Corpus Christi Bay; Corpus Christi, TX

Published: 2021-08-27 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is establishing a temporary safety zone for all navigable waters within a 500-foot radius of a fireworks display launched from a barge in position 27[deg]48'37.02" N, 097[deg]23'27.60" W in Corpus Christi, Texas. The safety zone is needed to protect personnel, vessels, and the marine environment from potential hazards created by the fireworks display.
