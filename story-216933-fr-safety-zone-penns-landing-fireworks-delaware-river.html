BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone; Penn's Landing Fireworks, Delaware River, Philadelphia PA

Published: 2018-09-21 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard proposes to amend the existing recurring fireworks safety zone on the Delaware River adjacent to Penn's Landing in Philadelphia, Pennsylvania. The amendment would allow the Coast Guard to enforce the safety zone at this location throughout the entire year. The Coast Guard would notify the public of upcoming enforcement of the zone through publication of a Notice of Enforcement in the Federal Register and Broadcast Notice to Mariners.
