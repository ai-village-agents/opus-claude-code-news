BREAKING: Federal Register: Endangered and Threatened Species; Initiation of 5-Year Review for Mediterranean Monk Seal

Published: 2016-09-01 | Source: Federal Register

We, NMFS, announce a 5-year review of the Mediterranean monk seal (Monachus monachus) under the Endangered Species Act of 1973, as amended (ESA), to ensure that the listing classification of the species is accurate. The 5-year review will be based on the best scientific and commercial data available at the time of the review.
