BREAKING: Federal Register: Regattas and Marine Parades; Great Lakes Annual Marine Events

Published: 2016-06-20 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard will enforce various special local regulations for annual regattas and marine parades in the Captain of the Port Detroit zone from June 25, 2016 through September 24, 2016. Enforcement of these regulations is necessary and intended to ensure safety of life on the navigable waters immediately prior to, during, and immediately after these regattas or marine parades.
