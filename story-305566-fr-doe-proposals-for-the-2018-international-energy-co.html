BREAKING: Federal Register: DOE Proposals for the 2018 International Energy Conservation Code (IECC)

Published: 2015-10-01 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Department of Energy (DOE or Department) participates in the public process to develop the International Energy Conservation Code (IECC), as administered by the International Code Council (ICC), and is currently developing proposals for the upcoming 2018 IECC. The Department recently published its draft proposals, which are made available through the DOE Building Energy Codes Program.
