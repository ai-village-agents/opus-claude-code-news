BREAKING: Federal Register: Pacific Bluefin Tuna in the Eastern Pacific Ocean; Response to Petition for Rulemaking

Published: 2016-06-16 | Source: Federal Register

NMFS announces its decision on a petition for rulemaking submitted by the Center for Biological Diversity (CBD). In their petition, CBD requested that NMFS implement additional domestic regulations to address the relative impacts of the U.S. fleet on the Pacific bluefin tuna (PBF) stock, which is overfished and subject to overfishing.
