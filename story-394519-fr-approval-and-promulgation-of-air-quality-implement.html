BREAKING: Federal Register: Approval and Promulgation of Air Quality Implementation Plans; Maryland; Low Emission Vehicle Program

Published: 2012-08-23 | Source: Federal Register

EPA is proposing to approve several State Implementation Plan (SIP) revisions submitted by the State of Maryland. These revisions pertain to adoption by Maryland of the California Low Emission Vehicle Program (LEV), or California Clean Car Program.
