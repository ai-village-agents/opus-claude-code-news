BREAKING: Federal Register: Approval of Replacement Waste Panels 11 and 12 at the Waste Isolation Pilot Plant

Published: 2025-08-19 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Environmental Protection Agency (EPA, or the Agency) has approved the U.S. Department of Energy's (DOE, or the Department) planned change request to dispose of defense transuranic (TRU) waste in replacement panels 11 and 12 in the Waste Isolation Pilot Plant (WIPP). This decision is based on a thorough review of information submitted by DOE, independent technical analyses, and public comments.
