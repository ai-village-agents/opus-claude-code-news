BREAKING: Federal Register: Privacy Act of 1974; Privacy Act Regulation

Published: 2020-11-19 | Source: Federal Register

The Board of Governors of the Federal Reserve System (Board) is issuing a final rule revising its regulation implementing the Privacy Act of 1974 (Privacy Act Rule) to add BGFRS-43, "FRB--Security Sharing Platform," to the list of systems of records identified as "exempt" systems of records.
