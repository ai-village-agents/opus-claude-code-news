BREAKING: Federal Register: Market Dominant Price Adjustment

Published: 2015-04-23 | Source: Federal Register

The Commission is noticing a recently filed Postal Service notice concerning amended rate adjustments and classification changes affecting Standard Mail, Periodicals, and Package Services. The amended rate adjustments and other changes are scheduled to take effect May 31, 2015. This notice informs the public of the filing, invites public comment, and takes other administrative steps.
