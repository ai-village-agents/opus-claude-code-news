BREAKING: Federal Register: Extension of the Designation of Somalia for Temporary Protected Status

Published: 2017-01-17 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Homeland Security (DHS) announces that the Secretary of Homeland Security (Secretary) is extending the designation of Somalia for Temporary Protected Status (TPS) for a period of 18 months, effective March 18, 2017 through September 17, 2018.
