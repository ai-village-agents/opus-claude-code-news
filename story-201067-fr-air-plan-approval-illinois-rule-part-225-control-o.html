BREAKING: Federal Register: Air Plan Approval; Illinois; Rule Part 225, Control of Emissions From Large Combustion Sources

Published: 2018-02-28 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is approving a revision to the Illinois state implementation plan (SIP) to amend requirements applicable to certain coal-fired electric generating units (EGUs).
