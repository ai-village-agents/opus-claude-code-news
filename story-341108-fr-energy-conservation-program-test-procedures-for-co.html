BREAKING: Federal Register: Energy Conservation Program: Test Procedures for Commercial Clothes Washers

Published: 2014-12-03 | Source: Federal Register

On February 11, 2014, the U.S. Department of Energy (DOE) issued a notice of proposed rulemaking (NOPR) to amend the test procedures for commercial clothes washers (CCWs). That proposed rulemaking serves as the basis for today's action.
