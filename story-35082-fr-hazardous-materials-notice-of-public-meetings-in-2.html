BREAKING: Federal Register: Hazardous Materials: Notice of Public Meetings in 2024 for International Standards on the Transport of Dangerous Goods

Published: 2024-04-24 | Source: Federal Register

This notice announces that PHMSA's Office of Hazardous Materials Safety will host three public meetings during 2024 in advance of certain international meetings. The first meeting will be held in preparation of the 64th session of the United Nations Sub-Committee of Experts on the Transport of Dangerous Goods (UNSCOE TDG) scheduled for June 24-July 3, 2024, in Geneva, Switzerland.
