BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; The Boeing Company Airplanes

Published: 2012-09-06 | Source: Federal Register

We propose to supersede an existing airworthiness directive (AD) that applies to certain The Boeing Company Model 747-400 and 747- 400F series airplanes. The existing AD currently requires installing drains and drain tubes to eliminate water accumulation in the dripshield above the M826 cardfile in the main equipment center.
