BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone; Swim for Alligator Lighthouse, Islamorada, FL

Published: 2021-08-16 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is establishing a temporary safety zone on certain navigable waters near Islamorada, Florida, during the Swim for Alligator Lighthouse open water swim event. A permanent safety zone exists for this event; however, for this year's event the date has changed. This temporary safety zone is a short-term modification of the existing permanent safety zone, due to a change in the date for this year's event.
