BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; AGUSTA S.p.A. Helicopters

Published: 2012-06-18 | Source: Federal Register

We are adopting a new airworthiness directive (AD) for Agusta S.p.A. (Agusta) Model AW139 helicopters to determine if the hardware that attaches the upper end of collective control rod C2 to torque tube C3 is properly installed. This AD is prompted by the discovery of an incorrectly-attached collective control rod.
