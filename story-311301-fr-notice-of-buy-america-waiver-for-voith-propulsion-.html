BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Buy America Waiver for Voith Propulsion Unit

Published: 2015-12-10 | Source: Federal Register

The Federal Transit Administration (FTA) received a request from the Virginia Department of Transportation (VDOT) for a Buy America waiver for Voith Turbo Schneider Propeller GmbH (Voith) 21/R5 propulsion units based on non-availability. Voith, located in Germany, is the sole manufacturer of the required propulsion units.
