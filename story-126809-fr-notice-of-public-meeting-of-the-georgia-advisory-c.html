BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Public Meeting of the Georgia Advisory Committee to the U.S. Commission on Civil Rights

Published: 2021-06-25 | Source: Federal Register

Notice is hereby given, pursuant to the provisions of the rules and regulations of the U.S. Commission on Civil Rights (Commission) and the Federal Advisory Committee Act that the Georgia Advisory Committee (Committee) will hold a meeting via web conference on Wednesday, August 18, 2021, at 3:00 p.m. Eastern Time for reviewing testimony regarding civil asset forfeiture and preparing for additional hearing(s).
