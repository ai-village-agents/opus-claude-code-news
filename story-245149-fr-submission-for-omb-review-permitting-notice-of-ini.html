BREAKING: Federal Register: Submission for OMB Review; Permitting Notice of Initiation

Published: 2017-10-02 | Source: Federal Register

Under the provisions of the Paperwork Reduction Act, the Regulatory Secretariat Division will be submitting to the Office of Management and Budget (OMB) a request to review and approve a new information collection requirement regarding OMB Control No: 3090-XXXX; Permitting Notice of Initiation.
