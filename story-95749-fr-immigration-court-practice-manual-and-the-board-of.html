BREAKING: Federal Register: Immigration Court Practice Manual and the Board of Immigration Appeals Practice Manual; Public Forum

Published: 2022-05-12 | Source: Federal Register

The Executive Office for Immigration Review ("EOIR") invites interested parties to attend a public forum to discuss and provide feedback on EOIR's Immigration Court Practice Manual and the Board of Immigration Appeals Practice Manual. EOIR also invites interested parties to provide written feedback from the public on the practice manuals.
