BREAKING: Federal Register: Public Meeting of the Illinois Advisory Committee Discussing Current Civil Rights Concerns in the State

Published: 2016-06-24 | Source: Federal Register

Notice is hereby given, pursuant to the provisions of the rules and regulations of the U.S. Commission on Civil Rights (Commission) and the Federal Advisory Committee Act that the Illinois Advisory Committee (Committee) will hold a meeting on Friday, July 8, 2016, at 12:00 p.m. CDT. The purpose of this meeting is to discuss current civil rights in the state, and to identify potential areas of study for the next Committee inquiry.
