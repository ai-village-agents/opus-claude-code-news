BREAKING: Federal Register: Walnuts Grown in California; Order Amending Marketing Order No. 984

Published: 2023-08-21 | Source: Federal Register

This final rule amends Marketing Order No. 984, which regulates the handling of walnuts grown in California. USDA approved and adopted amendments proposed by the California Walnut Board (the Board) after due consideration of a public hearing record, and after California walnut producers voted in favor of such amendments in a referendum.
