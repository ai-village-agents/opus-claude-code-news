BREAKING: Federal Register: Clinical Outcome Assessment Compendium

Published: 2016-01-13 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA or Agency) is announcing the establishment of a docket to receive suggestions, recommendations, and comments from interested parties (including academic institutions, regulated industry, and patient groups) on our pilot "Clinical Outcome Assessment Compendium" (COA Compendium).
