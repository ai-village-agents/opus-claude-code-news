BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Issuance of Final Determination Concerning Certain Hard Disk Drives and Self-Encrypting Drives

Published: 2013-08-21 | Source: Federal Register

This document provides notice that U.S. Customs and Border Protection ("CBP") has issued a final determination concerning the country of origin of certain digital storage devices known as hard disk drives ("HDDs") and self-encrypting drives ("SEDs"). Based upon the facts presented, CBP has concluded that the programming operations performed in the United States, using U.S.-origin firmware, substantially transform non-TAA country HDDs.
