BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Inventory Completion: U.S. Department of the Interior, Bureau of Land Management, Anchorage, AK

Published: 2022-11-23 | Source: Federal Register

In accordance with the Native American Graves Protection and Repatriation Act (NAGPRA), the U.S. Department of the Interior, Bureau of Land Management (BLM Alaska) has completed an inventory of human remains and associated funerary objects and has determined that there is a cultural affiliation between the human remains and associated funerary objects and Indian Tribes or Native Hawaiian organizations in this notice.
