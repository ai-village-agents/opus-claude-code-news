BREAKING: Federal Register: Meeting of the Communications Equity and Diversity Council

Published: 2024-03-08 | Source: Federal Register

In accordance with the Federal Advisory Committee Act, this notice announces the first meeting of the Federal Communications Commission's (Commission) re-chartered Communications Equity and Diversity Council (CEDC). The charter for the CEDC was renewed for a two-year period beginning June 22, 2023.
