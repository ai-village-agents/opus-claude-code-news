BREAKING: Tens of Thousands Evacuated as Floods Submerge Parts of Morocco

Published: February 4, 2026 | Source: Al Jazeera

Tens of thousands of people have been evacuated as severe flooding has submerged parts of Morocco, causing widespread damage.

Mass Evacuations

Authorities have conducted large-scale evacuations as floodwaters inundated homes and infrastructure across affected regions.

Damage Assessment

Roads, buildings and agricultural land have been damaged as the flooding continues to affect multiple areas.

Emergency Response

Emergency services are working to reach stranded residents and provide assistance to those displaced by the disaster.
