BREAKING: Federal Register: Nez Perce-Clearwater National Forest; Idaho; Middle Fork Vegetation Management

Published: 2014-01-03 | Source: Federal Register

The Forest Service gives notice of its intent to prepare an Environmental Impact Statement for the Middle Fork Vegetation Management Project. The Proposed action would use a combination of timber harvest, pre-commercial thinning, prescribed fire and reforestation to achieve the desired range of age classes, size classes, vegetative species distributions habitat complexity (diversity) and landscape pattern across the forested portions of the project area.
