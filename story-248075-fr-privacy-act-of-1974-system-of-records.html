BREAKING: Federal Register: Privacy Act of 1974; System of Records

Published: 2017-11-06 | Source: Federal Register

The Council of the Inspectors General on Integrity and Efficiency (CIGIE) proposes to establish a system of records that is subject to the Privacy Act of 1974. Specifically, the Employee-related Records System system of records will enable CIGIE to more efficiently track current, former, and prospective employee-related records.
