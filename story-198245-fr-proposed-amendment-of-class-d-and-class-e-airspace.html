BREAKING: Federal Register: Proposed Amendment of Class D and Class E Airspace; Atwater, CA

Published: 2018-01-23 | Source: Federal Register

This action proposes to amend Class D airspace, and Class E airspace extending upward from 700 feet above the surface at Castle Airport, Atwater, CA, to accommodate airspace redesign due to the decommissioning of the El Nido VHF Omnidirectional Range/Distance Measuring Equipment (VOR/DME) as the FAA transitions from ground-based to satellite-based navigation.
