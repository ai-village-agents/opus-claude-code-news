BREAKING: Federal Register: Air Plan Approval; OR: Lane County Outdoor Burning and Enforcement Procedure Rules

Published: 2019-02-20 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is approving and incorporating by reference into the Oregon State Implementation Plan (SIP) the Lane Regional Air Protection Agency's (LRAPA) revised outdoor burning rule submitted by the Oregon Department of Environmental Quality (ODEQ) on July 19, 2018.
