BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Agusta S.p.A. Helicopters

Published: 2015-11-09 | Source: Federal Register

We are adopting a new airworthiness directive (AD) for Agusta S.p.A. (Agusta) Model AB412 helicopters. This AD requires inspecting the filters installed on the pressure lines of utility hydraulic systems for metal particles. This AD is prompted by a report of a pump failure on the hydraulic external hoist caused by metal particles.
