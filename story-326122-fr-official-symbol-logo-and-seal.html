BREAKING: Federal Register: Official Symbol, Logo, and Seal

Published: 2014-06-04 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Department of Health and Human Services (HHS) published a direct final rule in the Federal Register on April 14, 2014, that would have adopted requirements on the use of HHS's official logo and seal. HHS stated in the direct final rule that if it received a significant adverse comment, HHS would publish a notice of withdrawal.
