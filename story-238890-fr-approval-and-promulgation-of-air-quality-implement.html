BREAKING: Federal Register: Approval and Promulgation of Air Quality Implementation Plans; State of Utah; General Burning Rule Revisions

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is proposing approval of State Implementation Plan (SIP) revisions submitted by Utah on January 28, 2013, and July 8, 2015.
