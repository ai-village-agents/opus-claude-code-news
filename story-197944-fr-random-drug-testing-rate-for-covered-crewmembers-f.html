BREAKING: Federal Register: Random Drug Testing Rate for Covered Crewmembers for 2018

Published: 2018-01-19 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard has set the calendar year 2018 minimum random drug testing rate at 25 percent of covered crewmembers.
