BREAKING: Federal Register: Special Local Regulation; Red Bull Flugtag Miami, Biscayne Bay; Miami, FL

Published: 2013-09-17 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is establishing a special local regulation on the waters of Biscayne Bay, east of Bayfront Park, in Miami, Florida, during the Red Bull Flugtag. The Red Bull Flugtag is scheduled to take place on September 21, 2013. The event consists of approximately 100 participants launching 30 self-propelled flying objects from a 30 foot ramp to the water below.
