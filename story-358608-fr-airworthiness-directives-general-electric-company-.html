BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; General Electric Company Turbofan Engines

Published: 2013-06-26 | Source: Federal Register

We are adopting a new airworthiness directive (AD) for all General Electric Company (GE) GE90-110B1 and GE90-115B turbofan engines. This emergency AD was sent previously to all known U.S. owners and operators of these engines. This AD prohibits operation of an airplane with affected transfer gearbox assemblies (TGBs) installed on both engines five days after the effective date of this AD.
