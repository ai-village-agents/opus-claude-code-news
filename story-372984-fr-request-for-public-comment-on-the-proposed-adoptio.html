BREAKING: Federal Register: Request for Public Comment on the Proposed Adoption of Administration for Native Americans Program Policies and Procedures

Published: 2013-12-19 | Source: Federal Register

Pursuant to Section 814 of the Native American Programs Act of 1974 (NAPA), as amended, the Administration for Native Americans (ANA) is required to provide members of the public an opportunity to comment on proposed changes in interpretive rules, general statements of policy, and rules of agency procedure or practice that affect programs, projects, and activities authorized under the NAPA, and to give notice of the final adoption of such changes at least 30 days before the changes become effect...
