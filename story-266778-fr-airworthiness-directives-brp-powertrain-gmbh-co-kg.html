BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; BRP-Powertrain GmbH & Co KG Reciprocating Engines

Published: 2016-06-22 | Source: Federal Register

We are adopting a new airworthiness directive (AD) for certain BRP-Powertrain GmbH & Co KG Rotax 912 F2, 912 F3, 912 F4, 912 S2, 912 S3, 912 S4, 914 F2, 914 F3, and 914 F4 reciprocating engines. This AD requires re-identification of the engine model and concurrent modification of the aircraft to indicate the maximum coolant temperature limit.
