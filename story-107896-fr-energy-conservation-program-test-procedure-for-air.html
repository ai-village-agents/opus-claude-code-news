BREAKING: Federal Register: Energy Conservation Program: Test Procedure for Air Cleaners

Published: 2022-10-18 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Department of Energy ("DOE") proposes to establish definitions, a test procedure, and sampling and representation requirements for air cleaners. Currently, air cleaners are not subject to DOE test procedures or energy conservation standards. DOE proposes a test procedure for measuring the integrated energy factor for air cleaners.
