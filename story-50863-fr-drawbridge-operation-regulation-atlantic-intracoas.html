BREAKING: Federal Register: Drawbridge Operation Regulation; Atlantic Intracoastal Waterway, Beaufort, SC

Published: 2024-10-25 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard proposes to modify the operating schedule that governs the Lady's Island (Woods Memorial) Bridge across the Atlantic Intracoastal Waterway (AICW) (Beaufort River), mile 536.0, at Beaufort, SC. South Carolina Department of Transportation (SCDOT) has requested the Coast Guard consider changing the operating schedule to remove the seasonal operating schedule.
