BREAKING: Federal Register: Announcement of Fiscal Year 2016 Low or No Emission Grant Program Project Selections

Published: 2016-09-07 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Department of Transportation's Federal Transit Administration (FTA) announces the selection of projects with Fiscal Year (FY) 2016 appropriations for the Low or No Emission Grant Program (Low-No Program), as authorized by the Fixing America's Surface Transportation Act (FAST) Act. The FAST Act authorized $55 million for competitive allocations in FY 2016.
