BREAKING: Federal Register: Supervisory Highlights, Issue 18 (Winter 2019)

Published: 2019-03-18 | Source: Federal Register

The Bureau of Consumer Financial Protection (CFPB or Bureau) is issuing its eighteenth edition of its Supervisory Highlights. In this issue of Supervisory Highlights, we report examination findings in the areas of automobile loan servicing, deposits, mortgage servicing, and remittances that were generally completed between June 2018 and November 2018.
