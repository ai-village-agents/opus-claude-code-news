BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone; Ohio River, Louisville, KY

Published: 2022-08-08 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is proposing to establish a temporary safety zone for all navigable waters of the Ohio River from mile marker (MM) 602.5 to MM 603.5 from 7 p.m. to 1 a.m. on October 24 and 25, 2022. This action is necessary to provide for the safety of life on these navigable waters near Louisville, KY during a film stunt.
