BREAKING: Federal Register: Proposed Information Collection (Principles of Excellence Complaint Intake Questionnaire) Activity; Withdrawal

Published: 2013-08-27 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Veterans Affairs (VA) published a notice in a Federal Register on April 30, 2013 (78 FR 25359), inviting the public to comment on a proposed information collection titled "Principles of Excellence Complaint Intake Questionnaire, VA Form 22-0959". This document withdraws that notice.
