BREAKING: Federal Register: Swap Clearing Requirement Exemptions

Published: 2020-05-12 | Source: Federal Register

The Commodity Futures Trading Commission (Commission or CFTC) is proposing amendments to the regulations governing which swaps are exempt from the clearing requirement set forth in the Commodity Exchange Act (CEA). The proposed amendments would address the treatment of swaps entered into by certain central banks, sovereign entities, and international financial institutions.
