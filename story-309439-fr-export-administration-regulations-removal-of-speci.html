BREAKING: Federal Register: Export Administration Regulations: Removal of Special Comprehensive License Provisions

Published: 2015-11-16 | Source: Federal Register

The Bureau of Industry and Security (BIS) publishes this final rule to amend the Export Administration Regulations (EAR) Supplement that lists "Information Collection Requirements Under the Paperwork Reduction Act: OMB Control Numbers" to remove certain citations related to Special Comprehensive Licenses listed under Collection number 0607-0152.
