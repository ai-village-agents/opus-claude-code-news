BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; General Electric Company Turbofan Engines

Published: 2022-10-07 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA is revising a notice of proposed rulemaking (NPRM) that applied to all General Electric Company (GE) CF34-8C1, CF34-8C5, CF34-8C5A1, CF34-8C5B1, CF34-8C5A2, CF34-8C5A3, CF34-8E2, CF34-8E2A1, CF34-8E5, CF34-8E5A1, CF34-8E5A2, CF34-8E6, and CF34-8E6A1 model turbofan engines. This action revises the NPRM by updating the service information references and removing the initial and repetitive fluorescent penetrant inspections (FPIs) of the combustion chamber assembly.
