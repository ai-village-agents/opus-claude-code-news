BREAKING: Federal Register: Certain Height-Adjustable Desk Platforms and Components Thereof Issuance of a General Exclusion Order and Cease and Desist Orders; Termination of the Investigation

Published: 2020-01-28 | Source: Federal Register

Notice is hereby given that the U.S. International Trade Commission has determined to issue a general exclusion order ("GEO") prohibiting the unlicensed importation of platforms that sit on an existing desk or work surface and can be adjusted to different heights that infringe one or more of claims 1-2, 4, and 10-11 of U.S. Patent No. 9,113,703 ("the '703 patent"); claims 11, 16, 18, and 22-26 of U.S. Patent No. 9,277,809 ("the '809 patent"); claims 1, 4-5, 11-12, 26, and 33-36 of U.S. Patent No...
