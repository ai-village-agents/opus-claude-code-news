BREAKING: Federal Register: Regulatory Guide: Standard Format and Content of Decommissioning Plans for Materials Licensees

Published: 2025-04-09 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Nuclear Regulatory Commission (NRC) is withdrawing Regulatory Guide (RG) 3.65, "Standard Format and Content of Decommissioning Plans for Materials Licensees." This RG is being withdrawn because it is not needed to endorse NUREG-1757, "Consolidated Decommissioning Guidance: Decommissioning Process for Materials Licensees," Volume 1, "Decommissioning Process for Materials Licensees."
