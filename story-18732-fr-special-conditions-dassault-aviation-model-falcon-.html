BREAKING: Federal Register: Special Conditions: Dassault Aviation Model Falcon 10X Airplane; Limit Pilot Forces

Published: 2025-08-22 | Source: Federal Register

These special conditions are issued for the Dassault Aviation Falcon Model 10X series airplanes. These airplanes will have a novel or unusual design feature when compared to the state of technology envisioned in the airworthiness standards for transport-category airplanes. This design feature is an electronic flight control system with a side stick controller instead of a conventional control column and wheel.
