BREAKING: Federal Register: Hazardous Materials: Transportation of Lithium Batteries

Published: 2012-04-11 | Source: Federal Register

In this document, PHMSA is seeking comment on the impact of changes to the requirements for the air transport of lithium cells and batteries that have been adopted into the 2013-2014 International Civil Aviation Organization Technical Instructions on the Transport of Dangerous Goods by Air (ICAO Technical Instructions).
