BREAKING: Federal Register: Manti-La Sal National Forest Resource Advisory Committee

Published: 2012-08-02 | Source: Federal Register

The Manti-La Sal National Forest Resource Advisory Committee will meet in Price, Utah. The committee is meeting as authorized under the Secure Rural Schools and Community Self-Determination Act (Pub.L. 110-343) and in compliance with the Federal Advisory Committee Act. The purpose of the meeting is to consider Secure Rural Schools Act Title II project proposals.
