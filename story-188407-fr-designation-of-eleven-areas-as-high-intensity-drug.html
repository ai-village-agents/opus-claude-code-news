BREAKING: Federal Register: Designation of Eleven Areas as High Intensity Drug Trafficking Areas

Published: 2019-09-11 | Source: Federal Register

The Director of the Office of National Drug Control Policy designated 13 additional areas as High Intensity Drug Trafficking Areas (HIDTA) pursuant to 21 U.S.C. 1706(b)(1).
