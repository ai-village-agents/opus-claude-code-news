BREAKING: Federal Register: Recent Trends in U.S. Services Trade, 2017 Annual Report

Published: 2016-11-16 | Source: Federal Register

The Commission has prepared and published annual reports in this series under investigation No. 332-345, Recent Trends in U.S. Services Trade, since 1996. The 2017 report, which the Commission plans to publish in May 2017, will provide aggregate data on cross-border trade in services for the period ending in 2015, and transactions by affiliates based outside the country of their parent firm for the period ending in 2014.
