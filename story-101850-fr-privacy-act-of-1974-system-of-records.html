BREAKING: Federal Register: Privacy Act of 1974; System of Records

Published: 2022-08-01 | Source: Federal Register

In accordance with the Privacy Act of 1974, as amended (Privacy Act) and Office of Management and Budget (OMB) Circular A-108, "Federal Agency Responsibilities for Review, Reporting, and Publication Under the Privacy Act," the Department of Commerce (Department) is issuing this notice of intent to modify a system of records, COMMERCE/CENSUS-7, Special Censuses of Population Conducted for State and Local Government.
