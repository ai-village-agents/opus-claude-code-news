BREAKING: Federal Register: Summer Food Service Program: 2019 Reimbursement Rates

Published: 2019-03-20 | Source: Federal Register

This notice informs the public of the annual adjustments to the reimbursement rates for meals served in the Summer Food Service Program for Children. These adjustments address changes in the Consumer Price Index, as required under the Richard B. Russell National School Lunch Act. The 2019 reimbursement rates are presented as a combined set of rates to highlight simplified cost accounting procedures.
