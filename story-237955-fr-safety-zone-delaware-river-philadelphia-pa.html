BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone; Delaware River, Philadelphia, PA

Published: 2017-06-30 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard will enforce a safety zone regulation for an annual fireworks event on the Delaware River, Philadelphia, PA from 9:30 p.m. to 11:30 p.m. on June 30, 2017 and July 1, 2017. Enforcement of this safety zone is necessary and intended to ensure safety of life on navigable waters immediately prior to, during, and immediately after these fireworks events.
