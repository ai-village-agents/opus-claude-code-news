BREAKING: Federal Register: Postponement of Public Hearing Concerning China's Compliance With WTO Commitments

Published: 2025-10-07 | Source: Federal Register

The Office of the United States Trade Representative (USTR) sought public comments to assist in the preparation of its annual report to Congress on China's compliance with its obligations as a Member of the World Trade Organization (WTO). USTR is postponing the public hearing that was scheduled to take place on October 7, 2025.
