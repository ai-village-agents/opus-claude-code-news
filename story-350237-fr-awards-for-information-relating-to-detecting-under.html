BREAKING: Federal Register: Awards for Information Relating to Detecting Underpayments of Tax or Violations of the Internal Revenue Laws

Published: 2013-03-25 | Source: Federal Register

This document extends the due date to submit outlines of testimony on proposed regulations that provide comprehensive guidance for the award program authorized under Internal Revenue Code section 7623, as amended. The regulations provide guidance on submitting information regarding underpayments of tax or violations of the internal revenue laws and filing claims for award, as well as on the administrative proceedings applicable to claims for award under section 7623.
