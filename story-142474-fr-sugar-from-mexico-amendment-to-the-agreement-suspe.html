BREAKING: Federal Register: Sugar From Mexico: Amendment to the Agreement Suspending the Countervailing Duty Investigation

Published: 2020-01-22 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Commerce (Commerce) and a representative of the Government of Mexico (GOM) have signed an amendment to the Agreement Suspending the Countervailing Duty Investigation on Sugar from Mexico (CVD Agreement).
