BREAKING: Federal Register: Acceptability of Corrective Action Programs for Fuel Cycle Facilities

Published: 2013-07-30 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Nuclear Regulatory Commission (NRC) is withdrawing draft NUREG-2154, "Acceptability of Corrective Action Programs for Fuel Cycle Facilities," based on receipt and review of public comments. The draft NUREG provided guidance to NRC staff on how to determine whether a submittal for a Corrective Action Program (CAP), voluntarily submitted by fuel cycle facility licensees, was acceptable.
