BREAKING: Federal Register: Animal Welfare; Retail Pet Stores and Licensing Exemptions

Published: 2012-05-16 | Source: Federal Register

We are proposing to revise the definition of retail pet store and related regulations to bring more pet animals sold at retail under the protection of the Animal Welfare Act (AWA).
