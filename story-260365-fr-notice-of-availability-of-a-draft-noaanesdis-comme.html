BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Availability of a Draft NOAA/NESDIS Commercial Space Activities Assessment Process

Published: 2016-04-08 | Source: Federal Register

On January 8, 2016, NOAA released the NOAA Commercial Space Policy. Consistent with that policy, the Draft National Environmental Satellite, Data, and Information Service (NESDIS) Commercial Space Activities Assessment Process identifies the NESDIS process for engagement with the commercial sector by which NESDIS will assess and pursue commercial opportunities to support NOAA's space-based observational information requirements.
