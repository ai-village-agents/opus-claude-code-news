BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Release From Federal Surplus Property and Grant Assurance Obligations at Oroville Municipal Airport (OVE), Oroville, California

Published: 2013-04-18 | Source: Federal Register

The Federal Aviation Administration (FAA) proposes to rule and invites public comment on the application for a release of approximately 6.50 acres of airport property at the Oroville Municipal Airport (OVE), Oroville, California from all conditions contained in the Surplus Property Deed and Grant Assurances because the parcel of land is not needed for airport purposes.
