BREAKING: Federal Register: Rescission of Previously Issued Cancellation Order for Methyl Parathion Product Registration

Published: 2012-03-28 | Source: Federal Register

This notice rescinds a previously issued Cancellation Order, printed in the Federal Register on December 28, 2011, to the extent it is applicable to one methyl parathion product. The product in question, EPA Registration Number 070506-00193, was previously cancelled under a separate Cancellation Order published in the Federal Register on July 27, 2010.
