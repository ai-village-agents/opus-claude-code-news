BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; BRP-Powertrain GmbH & Co KG Reciprocating Engines

Published: 2016-11-22 | Source: Federal Register

We are adopting a new airworthiness directive (AD) for certain BRP-Powertrain GmbH & Co KG Rotax 912 F2, 912 F3, 912 F4, 912 S2, 912 S3, 912 S4, 914 F2, 914 F3, and 914 F4 reciprocating engines. This AD requires replacement of any affected carburetor float with a float that is eligible for installation.
