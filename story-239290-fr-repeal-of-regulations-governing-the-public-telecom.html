BREAKING: Federal Register: Repeal of Regulations Governing the Public Telecommunications Facilities Program

Published: 2017-07-18 | Source: Federal Register

The National Telecommunications and Information Administration (NTIA) issues this NPRM to propose the repeal of its regulations governing the Public Telecommunications Facilities Program (PTFP). The PTFP is a competitive grant program that helps public broadcasting stations, state and local governments, Indian Tribes, and nonprofit organizations to construct public television and radio stations.
