BREAKING: Federal Register: Horseracing Integrity and Safety Authority Enforcement Rule Modification

Published: 2023-07-28 | Source: Federal Register

The Horseracing Integrity and Safety Act of 2020 recognizes a self-regulatory nonprofit organization, the Horseracing Integrity and Safety Authority, which is charged with developing proposed rules on a variety of subjects. Those proposed rules and proposed rule modifications take effect only if approved by the Federal Trade Commission.
