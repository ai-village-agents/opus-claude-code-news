BREAKING: Federal Register: Air Plan Approval; CT; Reasonably Available Control Technology for the 2008 Ozone Standard

Published: 2017-07-31 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is approving State Implementation Plan (SIP) revisions submitted by the State of Connecticut. These SIP revisions consist of a demonstration that Connecticut meets the requirements to implement reasonably available control technology (RACT) for the two precursors of ground-level ozone, oxides of nitrogen (NOX) and volatile organic compounds (VOCs), set forth by the Clean Air Act (CAA) with respect to the 2008 ozone standard.
