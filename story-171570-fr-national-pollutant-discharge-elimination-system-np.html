BREAKING: Federal Register: National Pollutant Discharge Elimination System (NPDES): Applications and Program Updates

Published: 2019-02-12 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is finalizing certain revisions to the National Pollutant Discharge Elimination System permitting regulations proposed on May 18, 2016. The final regulatory changes are minor and will improve and clarify the regulations in the following major categories: Regulatory definitions ("new discharger" and two definitions related to the discharge of pesticides from pesticides application); permit applications; and public notice.
