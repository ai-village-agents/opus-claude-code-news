BREAKING: Federal Register: Postapproval Pregnancy Safety Studies; Draft Guidance for Industry; Availability

Published: 2019-05-09 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA or Agency) is announcing the availability of a draft guidance for industry entitled "Postapproval Pregnancy Safety Studies." When finalized, the purpose of this guidance will be to provide sponsors and investigators with recommendations on how to design investigations to assess the outcomes of pregnancies in women exposed to drugs and biological products regulated by FDA (i.e., pregnancy safety studies).
