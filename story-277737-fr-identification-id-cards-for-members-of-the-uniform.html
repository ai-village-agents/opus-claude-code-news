BREAKING: Federal Register: Identification (ID) Cards for Members of the Uniformed Services, Their Dependents, and Other Eligible Individuals

Published: 2016-10-27 | Source: Federal Register

This rule amends an interim rule published on January 6, 2014 which provided procedures for DoD ID cards. These cards are issued to uniformed service members, their dependents, and other eligible individuals and are used as proof of identity and DoD affiliation as well as to facilitate the extension of DoD benefits.
