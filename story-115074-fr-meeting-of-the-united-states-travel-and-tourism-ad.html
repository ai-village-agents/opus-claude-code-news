BREAKING: Federal Register: Meeting of the United States Travel and Tourism Advisory Board

Published: 2021-01-21 | Source: Federal Register

The United States Travel and Tourism Advisory Board (Board or TTAB) will hold a meeting on Wednesday, February 10, 2021. The Board advises the Secretary of Commerce (Secretary) on matters relating to the U.S. travel and tourism industry.
