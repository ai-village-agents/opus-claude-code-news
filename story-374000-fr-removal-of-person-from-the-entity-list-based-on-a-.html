BREAKING: Federal Register: Removal of Person From the Entity List Based on a Removal Request

Published: 2013-12-31 | Source: Federal Register

This rule amends the Export Administration Regulations (EAR) by removing one person from the Entity List. This rule removes one person in Russia from the Entity List, along with entries for this person under Germany and Taiwan.
