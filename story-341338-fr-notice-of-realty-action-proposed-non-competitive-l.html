BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Realty Action: Proposed Non-Competitive Lease of Public Land in Sierra County, NM

Published: 2014-12-08 | Source: Federal Register

The Bureau of Land Management (BLM), Las Cruces District Office, proposes to lease two parcels of public land totaling 4.12 acres in Sierra County, New Mexico, for agricultural purposes (pecan orchard). The subject parcels were inadvertently developed by the adjacent landowner into a pecan orchard without authorization.
