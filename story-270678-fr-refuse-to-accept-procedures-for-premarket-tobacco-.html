BREAKING: Federal Register: Refuse To Accept Procedures for Premarket Tobacco Product Submissions

Published: 2016-08-08 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA) is issuing a proposed rule describing when FDA would refuse to accept a tobacco product submission (or application) because the application has not met a minimum threshold for acceptability for FDA review. Under the proposed rule, FDA would refuse to accept a tobacco product submission, for example, that is not in English, does not pertain to a tobacco product, or does not identify the type of submission.
