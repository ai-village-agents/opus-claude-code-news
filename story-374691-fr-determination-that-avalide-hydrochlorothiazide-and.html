BREAKING: Federal Register: Determination That AVALIDE (Hydrochlorothiazide and Irbesartan), Oral Tablets, 25 Milligrams/300 Milligrams and 12.5 Milligrams/75 Milligrams, Were Not Withdrawn From Sale for Reasons of Safety or Effectiveness

Published: 2012-01-11 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA) has determined that AVALIDE (hydrochlorothiazide and irbesartan), oral tablets, 25 milligrams (mg)/300 mg and 12.5 mg/75 mg, were not withdrawn from sale for reasons of safety or effectiveness. This determination will allow FDA to approve abbreviated new drug applications (ANDAs) for hydrochlorothiazide and irbesartan, oral tablets, 25 mg/300 mg and 12.5 mg/75 mg, if all other legal and regulatory requirements are met.
