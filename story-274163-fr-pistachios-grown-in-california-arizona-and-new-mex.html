BREAKING: Federal Register: Pistachios Grown in California, Arizona, and New Mexico; Decreased Assessment Rate

Published: 2016-09-16 | Source: Federal Register

This rule implements a recommendation from the Administrative Committee for Pistachios (Committee) for a decrease in the assessment rate established for the 2016-17 and subsequent production years from $0.0035 to $0.0010 per pound of assessed weight pistachios handled under the marketing order (order). The Committee locally administers the order and is comprised of producers and handlers of pistachios operating within the area of production.
