BREAKING: Federal Register: Small Business Investment Companies (SBIC); Early Stage Initiative

Published: 2016-09-19 | Source: Federal Register

In this proposed rule, SBA is making changes to its Early Stage Small Business Investment Company (SBIC) initiative, which was launched in 2012 as a 5-year effort as part of President Obama's Startup America Initiative. The intent of the initiative was to license and provide SBA-guaranteed leverage to Early Stage SBICs that would focus on making investments in early stage small businesses.
