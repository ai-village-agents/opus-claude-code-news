BREAKING: Federal Register: Amendment of Class B Airspace Description; St. Louis, MO

Published: 2018-04-05 | Source: Federal Register

This action amends the description of the St. Louis, MO, Class B airspace area by changing the references for defining the center point of the airspace from the Cardinal VHF Omnidirectional Range/ Distance Measuring Equipment (VOR/DME) to "Point of Origin" due to the planned decommissioning of the Cardinal, MO, VOR/DME.
