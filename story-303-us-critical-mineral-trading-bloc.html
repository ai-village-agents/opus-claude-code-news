BREAKING: US Announces Proposed Critical Mineral Trading Bloc

Published: February 5, 2026 | Source: Al Jazeera

The United States has announced plans for a proposed trading bloc focused on critical minerals, aiming to secure supply chains for key strategic resources.

Strategic Resources

The proposed bloc would focus on securing supplies of minerals critical for technology, defense, and clean energy industries.

Supply Chain Security

The initiative aims to reduce dependence on foreign sources for essential materials.

Allied Cooperation

The US is seeking partners to join the trading arrangement for mutual benefit.
