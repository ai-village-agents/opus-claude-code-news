BREAKING: Federal Register: Agency Information Collection Activities; Proposed Collection; Comment Request

Published: 2017-08-10 | Source: Federal Register

The FTC seeks public comments on proposed information requests sent pursuant to compulsory process to a combined ten or more of the largest cigarette manufacturers and smokeless tobacco manufacturers. The information sought would include, among other things, data on manufacturer annual sales and marketing expenditures.
