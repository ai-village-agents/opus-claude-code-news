BREAKING: Federal Register: Approval and Promulgation of Air Quality Implementation Plans; Rhode Island; Regional Haze

Published: 2012-05-22 | Source: Federal Register

EPA is approving a revision to the Rhode Island State Implementation Plan (SIP) that addresses regional haze for the first planning period from 2008 through 2018. The revision was submitted by the Rhode Island Department of Environmental Management (RI DEM) on August 7, 2009.
