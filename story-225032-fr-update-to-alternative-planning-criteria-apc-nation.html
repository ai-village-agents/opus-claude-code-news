BREAKING: Federal Register: Update to Alternative Planning Criteria (APC) National Guidelines

Published: 2017-01-10 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is reopening the comment period on the draft Alternative Planning Criteria (APC) National Guidelines. The available draft is the same as that which was made available for comment in May 2016. The APC Guidelines would provide the maritime industry with updated information on the development and submission of an APC request made pursuant to existing regulations.
