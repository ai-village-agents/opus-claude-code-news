BREAKING: Federal Register: International Space Station National Laboratory Advisory Committee; Charter Renewal

Published: 2015-10-22 | Source: Federal Register

Pursuant to sections 14(b)(1) an 9(c) of the Federal Advisory Committee Act (Pub. L. 92-463), and after consultation with the Committee Management Secretariat, General Services Administration, the NASA Administrator has determined that renewal of the charter of the International Space Station National Laboratory Advisory Committee is in the public interest in connection with the performance of duties imposed on NASA by law.
