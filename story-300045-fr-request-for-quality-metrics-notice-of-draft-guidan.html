BREAKING: Federal Register: Request for Quality Metrics; Notice of Draft Guidance Availability and Public Meeting; Request for Comments

Published: 2015-07-28 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA), Center for Drug Evaluation and Research (CDER) and Center for Biologics Evaluation and Research (CBER), is announcing the availability of a draft guidance for industry entitled "Request for Quality Metrics" and a public meeting regarding the Agency's plans associated with a quality metrics reporting program.
