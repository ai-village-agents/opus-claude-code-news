BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Embraer S.A. Airplanes

Published: 2020-07-17 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA proposes to adopt a new airworthiness directive (AD) for certain Embraer S.A. Model EMB-550 and EMB-545 airplanes. This proposed AD was prompted by reports of cracks, delamination, and failure of the flight deck side windows during certification fatigue tests.
