BREAKING: Federal Register: Intent To Prepare an Environmental Impact Statement for Increased Transit Service to King of Prussia, PA

Published: 2013-06-27 | Source: Federal Register

The FTA and the Southeastern Pennsylvania Transportation Authority (SEPTA) are planning to prepare an Environmental Impact Statement (EIS) and Section 4(f) Evaluation for increased transit service to King of Prussia, PA. The EIS will be prepared in accordance with regulations implementing the National Environmental Policy Act (NEPA), as well as FTA's regulations and guidance for implementing NEPA (40 CFR 1501.2 through 8 and 23 CFR 771.111).
