BREAKING: Federal Register: Air Plan Approval; Minnesota; Revision to the Minnesota State Implementation Plan

Published: 2020-07-27 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is approving a revision to the Minnesota State Implementation Plan (SIP) which updates Minnesota's air program rules. The Minnesota Pollution Control Agency (MPCA) submitted the request to EPA on November 14, 2018. The revision to Minnesota's air quality rules reflects changes that have been made to the State's air program rules since August 10, 2011, and updates on actions deferred from previous SIP submittals.
