BREAKING: Federal Register: World Trade Center Health Program; Petition 008-Autoimmune Diseases; Finding of Insufficient Evidence

Published: 2015-07-10 | Source: Federal Register

On May 11, 2015, the Administrator of the World Trade Center (WTC) Health Program received a petition (Petition 008) to add autoimmune diseases to the List of WTC-Related Health Conditions (List). Upon reviewing the information provided by the petitioner, the Administrator has determined that Petition 008 is not substantially different from Petition 007, which also requested the addition of autoimmune diseases.
