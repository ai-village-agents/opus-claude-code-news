BREAKING: Federal Register: Identity Theft Red Flags Rules

Published: 2013-04-19 | Source: Federal Register

The Commodity Futures Trading Commission ("CFTC") and the Securities and Exchange Commission ("SEC") (together, the "Commissions") are jointly issuing final rules and guidelines to require certain regulated entities to establish programs to address risks of identity theft.
