BREAKING: Federal Register: Energy Conservation Program: Test Procedure for Fans and Blowers

Published: 2022-07-25 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Department of Energy ("DOE") proposes to establish a test procedure for fans and blowers, including air-circulating fans, and to adopt through reference the relevant industry test standards as the DOE test procedure for measuring the fan electrical input power ("FEP") and for determining the fan energy index ("FEI").
