BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; The Boeing Company Airplanes

Published: 2020-07-28 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA proposes to supersede Airworthiness Directive (AD) 2018-14-02, which applies to certain The Boeing Company Model 777-200, -200LR, -300, and -300ER series airplanes. AD 2018-14-02 requires an inspection for foam insulation on the dripshield above the overhead panel support structure and replacement if necessary.
