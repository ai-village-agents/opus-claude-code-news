BREAKING: Federal Register: Kiwifruit Grown in California; Increased Assessment Rate

Published: 2016-02-03 | Source: Federal Register

This rule implements a recommendation from the Kiwifruit Administrative Committee (Committee) for an increase of the assessment rate established for the 2015-16 and subsequent fiscal periods from $0.025 to $0.040 per 9-kilo volume-fill container or equivalent of kiwifruit handled under the marketing order (order).
