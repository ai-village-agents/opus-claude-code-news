BREAKING: Federal Register: Physical and Digital Management Software Products Market Survey

Published: 2020-05-15 | Source: Federal Register

The National Institute of Justice (NIJ) is soliciting information for use in an upcoming Criminal Justice Testing and Evaluation Consortium (CJTEC) report tentatively titled, "A Landscape Report of Physical and Digital Management Software Products." The report will identify software and web-based technologies that are commercially available to manage physical evidence and/or digital evidence, such as photos or videos.
