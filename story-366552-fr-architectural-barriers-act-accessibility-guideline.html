BREAKING: Federal Register: Architectural Barriers Act Accessibility Guidelines; Outdoor Developed Areas

Published: 2013-09-26 | Source: Federal Register

We, the Architectural and Transportation Barriers Compliance Board (Access Board), are issuing a final rule that amends the Architectural Barriers Act Accessibility Guidelines by adding scoping and technical requirements for camping facilities, picnic facilities, viewing areas, trails, and beach access routes constructed or altered by or on behalf of federal agencies.
