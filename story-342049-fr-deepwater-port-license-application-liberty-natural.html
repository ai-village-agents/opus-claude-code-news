BREAKING: Federal Register: Deepwater Port License Application: Liberty Natural Gas LLC, Port Ambrose Deepwater Port

Published: 2014-12-16 | Source: Federal Register

The Maritime Administration (MARAD) and the U.S. Coast Guard (USCG) announce the availability of the Draft Environmental Impact Statement (DEIS) for the Liberty Natural Gas LLC, Port Ambrose Liquefied Natural Gas Deepwater Port License Application for the importation of natural gas. Please note that this application is only for the construction and operation of a deepwater port that could only be used as a natural gas import facility.
