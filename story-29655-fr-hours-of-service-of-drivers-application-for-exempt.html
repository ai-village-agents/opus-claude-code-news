BREAKING: Federal Register: Hours of Service of Drivers: Application for Exemption; Motion Picture Association

Published: 2024-02-15 | Source: Federal Register

FMCSA announces its final decision to grant the exemption from the electronic logging device (ELD) requirements requested by the Motion Picture Association (MPA) for all commercial motor vehicle (CMV) drivers providing transportation to or from a theatrical or television motion picture production site. This renewal of the exemption allows drivers to complete paper records of duty status (RODS) instead of using an ELD.
