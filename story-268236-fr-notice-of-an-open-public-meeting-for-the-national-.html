BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of an Open Public Meeting for the National Park Service Alaska Region Subsistence Resource Commission Program

Published: 2016-07-08 | Source: Federal Register

As required by the Federal Advisory Committee Act (16 U.S.C. Appendix 1-16), the National Park Service (NPS) is hereby giving notice that the Denali National Park Subsistence Resource Commission (SRC) will hold a public meeting to develop and continue work on NPS subsistence program recommendations, and other related regulatory proposals and resource management issues.
