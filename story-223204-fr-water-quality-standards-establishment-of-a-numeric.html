BREAKING: Federal Register: Water Quality Standards; Establishment of a Numeric Criterion for Selenium for the State of California

Published: 2018-12-13 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is proposing to establish a federal Clean Water Act (CWA) selenium water quality criterion applicable to California that protects aquatic life and aquatic-dependent wildlife in the fresh waters of California. In 2016, the EPA published a revised recommended aquatic life selenium criterion for freshwater based on the latest scientific knowledge.
