BREAKING: Federal Register: Atlantic Coastal Fisheries Cooperative Management Act Provisions; American Lobster Fishery

Published: 2012-06-01 | Source: Federal Register

With this final rule, NMFS implements new Federal American lobster regulations that will limit entry into the lobster trap fishery in Lobster Conservation Management Area 1 (Area 1), located in the Federal inshore waters of the Gulf of Maine. Eligibility will be based on specific eligibility criteria designed to identify active Federal Area 1 lobster trap permits.
