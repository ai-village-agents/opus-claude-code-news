BREAKING: Federal Register: Interagency Guidance on Credit Risk Review Systems

Published: 2020-06-01 | Source: Federal Register

The OCC, the Board, the FDIC, and the NCUA (collectively, the agencies) are issuing final guidance for credit risk review (final guidance). This guidance is relevant to all institutions supervised by the agencies and replaces Attachment 1 of the 2006 Interagency Policy Statement on the Allowance for Loan and Lease Losses.
