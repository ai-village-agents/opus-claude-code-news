BREAKING: Federal Register: Request for Comments on Proposed U.S.-Taiwan Initiative on 21st-Century Trade

Published: 2022-06-07 | Source: Federal Register

On June 1, 2022, the United States and Taiwan, under the auspices of the American Institute in Taiwan (AIT) and the Taipei Economic and Cultural Representative Office in the United States (TECRO), respectively, launched the U.S.-Taiwan Initiative on 21st- Century Trade.
