BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Contract Proposals (NOCP) for the Advanced Biofuels Payment Program

Published: 2014-06-16 | Source: Federal Register

This Notice of Contract Proposals announces the availability of $15 million in fiscal year 2014 to make payments to advanced biofuel producers for the production of eligible advanced biofuels. Applications for participating in the Advanced Biofuel Payment Program for fiscal year 2014 were accepted from October 1 through October 31, 2013.
