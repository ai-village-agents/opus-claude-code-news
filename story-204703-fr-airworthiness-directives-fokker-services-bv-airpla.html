BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Fokker Services B.V. Airplanes

Published: 2018-04-16 | Source: Federal Register

We are superseding Airworthiness Directive (AD) 2005-12-16, which applied to all Fokker Services B.V. Model F28 Mark 0100 airplanes. AD 2005-12-16 required an inspection to determine the part number of the passenger service unit (PSU) panels for the PSU modification status, and corrective actions if applicable.
