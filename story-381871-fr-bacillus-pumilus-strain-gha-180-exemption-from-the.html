BREAKING: Federal Register: Bacillus Pumilus Strain GHA 180; Exemption From the Requirement of a Tolerance

Published: 2012-03-30 | Source: Federal Register

This regulation establishes an exemption from the requirement of a tolerance for residues of Bacillus pumilus strain GHA 180 in or on all food commodities when used in accordance with good agricultural practices. Premier Horticulture submitted a petition to EPA under the Federal Food, Drug, and Cosmetic Act (FFDCA), requesting an exemption from the requirement of a tolerance.
