BREAKING: Federal Register: Clinical Pharmacology Data To Support a Demonstration of Biosimilarity to a Reference Product; Guidance for Industry; Availability

Published: 2016-12-29 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA or Agency) is announcing the availability of a guidance for industry entitled "Clinical Pharmacology Data to Support a Demonstration of Biosimilarity to a Reference Product." This guidance is to assist the pharmaceutical industry and other investigators engaged in biosimilar product development in determining the clinical pharmacology data necessary for evaluation of a proposed biosimilar product.
