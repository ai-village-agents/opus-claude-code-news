BREAKING: Federal Register: Publication of the List of Services, Software, and Hardware Incident to Communications

Published: 2024-05-17 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of the Treasury's Office of Foreign Assets Control (OFAC) is publishing a list of items that have been determined to be incident to communications and therefore authorized for export or reexport to Iran under a general license issued pursuant to the Iranian Transactions and Sanctions Regulations (ITSR).
