BREAKING: Federal Register: Solicitation for Annual Combating Human Trafficking in Transportation Impact Award

Published: 2021-01-28 | Source: Federal Register

The annual Combating Human Trafficking in Transportation Impact Award (the award) incentivizes an increase in human trafficking awareness, training, and prevention among transportation stakeholders. The award is a component of the Department's Transportation Leaders Against Human Trafficking initiative.
