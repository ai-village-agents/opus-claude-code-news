BREAKING: Federal Register: CHIPS for America Incentives Program Webinar on Notice of Proposed Rulemaking for National Security Guardrails

Published: 2023-03-27 | Source: Federal Register

The CHIPS Program Office will host a public webinar in connection with the Notice of Proposed Rulemaking for national security guardrails included in the CHIPS for America Incentives Program. In this webinar, the CHIPS Program Office will review the national security measures included in the CHIPS and Science Act and the additional details and definitions outlined in the Notice of Proposed Rulemaking.
