BREAKING: Federal Register: Changes To Implement Transitional Program for Covered Business Method Patents

Published: 2012-02-10 | Source: Federal Register

The United States Patent and Trademark Office (Office or USPTO) proposes new rules to implement the provisions of the Leahy- Smith America Invents Act that create a new transitional post-grant review proceeding for covered business method patents to be conducted before the Patent Trial and Appeal Board (Board).
