BREAKING: Federal Register: Arbitrage Restrictions on Tax-Exempt Bonds

Published: 2013-09-16 | Source: Federal Register

This document contains proposed regulations on the arbitrage restrictions under section 148 of the Internal Revenue Code applicable to tax-exempt bonds and other tax-advantaged bonds. These proposed regulations amend existing regulations to address certain current market developments, simplify certain provisions, address certain technical issues, and make the regulations more administrable.
