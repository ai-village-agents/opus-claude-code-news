BREAKING: Federal Register: Draft Environmental Assessment and Draft Habitat Conservation Plan; Davis Ranch, Bexar County, Texas

Published: 2019-03-18 | Source: Federal Register

We, the U.S. Fish and Wildlife Service (Service), make available the draft environmental assessment and habitat conservation plan for development of a 724-acre property in Bexar County, Texas. The Davis McCrary Property Trust has applied to the Service for an incidental take permit (ITP) under the Endangered Species Act.
