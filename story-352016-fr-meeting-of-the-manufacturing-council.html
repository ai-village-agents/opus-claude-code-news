BREAKING: Federal Register: Meeting of the Manufacturing Council

Published: 2013-04-12 | Source: Federal Register

The Manufacturing Council will hold its first meeting of the newly appointed members to discuss and identify the priority issues affecting the U.S. manufacturing industry. The meeting will be an organizational meeting to discuss priorities that will be addressed in future meetings.
