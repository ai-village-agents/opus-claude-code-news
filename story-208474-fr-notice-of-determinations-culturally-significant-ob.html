BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Determinations: Culturally Significant Objects Imported for Exhibition Determinations: “Spain: 500 Years of Spanish Painting From the Museums of Madrid” Exhibition

Published: 2018-06-04 | Source: Federal Register

Notice is hereby given of the following determinations: I hereby determine that certain objects to be included in the exhibition "Spain: 500 Years of Spanish Painting from the Museums of Madrid," imported from abroad for temporary exhibition within the United States, are of cultural significance. The objects are imported pursuant to loan agreements with the foreign owners or custodians.
