BREAKING: Federal Register: Labeling of Certain Beers Subject to the Labeling Jurisdiction of the Food and Drug Administration: Guidance for Industry; Availability

Published: 2014-12-23 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA) is announcing the availability of a guidance entitled "Labeling of Certain Beers Subject to the Labeling Jurisdiction of the Food and Drug Administration." The document provides guidance to industry on how to label bottled or otherwise packaged beers that are subject to FDA's labeling laws and regulations.
