BREAKING: Federal Register: Review of Floating Houses

Published: 2014-04-30 | Source: Federal Register

The Tennessee Valley Authority (TVA) is conducting a review of) the recent growth in the Tennessee River Watershed of floating houses and nonnavigable houseboats designed and used primarily for human habitation and potential management actions TVA may take in response to the proliferation of these structures.
