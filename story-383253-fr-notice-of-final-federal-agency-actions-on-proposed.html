BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Final Federal Agency Actions on Proposed Highway in California

Published: 2012-04-17 | Source: Federal Register

The FHWA, on behalf of Caltrans, is issuing this notice to announce actions taken by Caltrans, U.S. Army Corps of Engineers, U.S. Fish and Wildlife Service, and the National Oceanic and Atmospheric Administration--National Marine Fisheries Service that are final within the meaning of 23 U.S.C. 139(l)(1). The actions relate to a proposed highway project, State Route 76 (SR-76) from South Mission Road in Bonsall to just east of Interstate 15 (I-15), including interchange improvements, in Fallbrook...
