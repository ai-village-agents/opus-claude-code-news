BREAKING: Federal Register: Significant New Use Rules on Certain Chemical Substances (22-1.5e); Correction

Published: 2022-12-15 | Source: Federal Register

EPA is issuing a correction to a proposed rule that published in the Federal Register of Friday, December 2, 2022, in which EPA proposed significant new use rules (SNURs) under the Toxic Substances Control Act (TSCA) for chemical substances that were the subject of premanufacture notices (PMNs) and are also subject to Orders issued by EPA pursuant to TSCA.
