BREAKING: Federal Register: Pacific Island Fisheries; 2016 Annual Catch Limits and Accountability Measures

Published: 2017-04-21 | Source: Federal Register

In this final rule, NMFS specifies the 2016 annual catch limits (ACLs) for Pacific Island bottomfish, crustacean, precious coral, and coral reef ecosystem fisheries, and accountability measures (AMs) to correct or mitigate any overages of catch limits. The final ACLs and AMs are effective for fishing year 2016.
