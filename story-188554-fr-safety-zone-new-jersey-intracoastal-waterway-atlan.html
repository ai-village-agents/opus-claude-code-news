BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone; New Jersey Intracoastal Waterway, Atlantic City, NJ

Published: 2019-09-12 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is establishing a temporary safety zone for certain navigable waters of the New Jersey Intracoastal Waterway. The safety zone is needed to protect participants of the 4th Annual Ironman 70.3 Atlantic City Triathlon on these navigable waters near Atlantic City, NJ, during a swim event on September 15, 2019.
