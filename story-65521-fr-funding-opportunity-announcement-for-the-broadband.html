BREAKING: Federal Register: Funding Opportunity Announcement for the Broadband Technical Assistance for Fiscal Year 2023

Published: 2023-04-19 | Source: Federal Register

The Rural Utilities Service (RUS or the Agency), a Rural Development (RD) agency of the United States Department of Agriculture (USDA), announces the acceptance of applications--for Broadband Technical Assistance (BTA) for Fiscal Year (FY) 2023. Broadband Technical Assistance provides competitive cooperative agreement funding to eligible entities to receive or deliver broadband technical assistance and training that promotes the expansion of broadband into rural areas.
