BREAKING: Federal Register: Competitive Generic Therapies; Guidance for Industry; Availability

Published: 2020-03-16 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA or Agency) is announcing the availability of a final guidance for industry entitled "Competitive Generic Therapies." This guidance provides a description of the process that applicants should follow to request designation of a drug as a competitive generic therapy (CGT) and the criteria for designating a drug as a CGT.
