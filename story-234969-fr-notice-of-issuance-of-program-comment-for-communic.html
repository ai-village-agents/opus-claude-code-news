BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Issuance of Program Comment for Communications Projects on Federal Lands and Property

Published: 2017-05-24 | Source: Federal Register

The Advisory Council on Historic Preservation (ACHP) issued a Program Comment for Communications Projects on Federal Lands and Property at the request of the U.S. Department of Homeland Security (DHS) to accelerate the review of these projects, particularly broadband deployment, under Section 106 of the National Historic Preservation Act.
