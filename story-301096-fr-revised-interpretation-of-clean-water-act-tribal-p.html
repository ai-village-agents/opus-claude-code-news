BREAKING: Federal Register: Revised Interpretation of Clean Water Act Tribal Provision

Published: 2015-08-07 | Source: Federal Register

Waters on the majority of Indian reservations do not have water quality standards under the Clean Water Act to protect human health and the environment. Only 40 of over 300 federally recognized tribes with reservations have completed the process of obtaining EPA's approval to be treated in a manner similar to a state (TAS), and adopting standards for their waters that EPA has approved.
