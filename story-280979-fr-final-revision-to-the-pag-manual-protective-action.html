BREAKING: Federal Register: Final Revision to the PAG Manual: Protective Action Guides and Planning Guidance for Radiological Incidents

Published: 2016-12-08 | Source: Federal Register

As part of its mission to protect human health and the environment, the U.S. Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) publishes protective action guides to help federal, state, local and tribal emergency response officials make radiation protection decisions during emergencies.
