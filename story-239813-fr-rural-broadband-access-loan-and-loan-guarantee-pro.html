BREAKING: Federal Register: Rural Broadband Access Loan and Loan Guarantee Program

Published: 2017-07-25 | Source: Federal Register

The Rural Utilities Service (RUS), an agency of the United States Department of Agriculture (USDA), announces the second application window for fiscal year (FY) 2017 for the Rural Broadband Access Loan and Loan Guarantee Program (the Broadband Program). Announcing a second application window within the current FY is a statutory requirement of the 2014 Farm Bill.
