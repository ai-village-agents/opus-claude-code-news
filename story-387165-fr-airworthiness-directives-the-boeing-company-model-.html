BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; The Boeing Company Model 747 Airplanes

Published: 2012-05-31 | Source: Federal Register

We propose to supersede an existing airworthiness directive (AD) that applies to certain The Boeing Company Model 747 airplanes. The existing AD currently requires repetitive visual inspections around the bushings of the wing landing gear (WLG) beam outboard end fittings for corrosion, and rework if necessary; and ultrasonic inspections for cracks of the outboard end fittings of the WLG support beams, and rework if necessary.
