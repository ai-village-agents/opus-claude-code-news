BREAKING: Federal Register: Approval and Promulgation of Air Quality Implementation Plans; Virginia; Revisions to the Regulatory Definition of Volatile Organic Compound

Published: 2018-03-13 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is approving two state implementation plan (SIP) revisions (Revision C16 and Revision I16) formally submitted by the Commonwealth of Virginia (Virginia). The revisions pertain to amendments made to the definition of "volatile organic compound" (VOC) in the Virginia Administrative Code to conform with EPA's regulatory definition of VOC.
