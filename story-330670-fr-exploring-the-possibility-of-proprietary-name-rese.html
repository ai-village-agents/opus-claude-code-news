BREAKING: Federal Register: Exploring the Possibility of Proprietary Name Reservation for Drug Products; Establishment of a Public Docket

Published: 2014-07-28 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA or Agency) is establishing a public docket to discuss issues related to reserving proprietary names for drug products. During the negotiations for the 2007 reauthorization of the Prescription Drug User Fee Amendments Act (PDUFA IV), FDA agreed to several performance goals related to the review of drug and biological product proprietary names to reduce medication error.
