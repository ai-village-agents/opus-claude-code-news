BREAKING: Federal Register: Registration Review Final and Interim Decisions; Notice of Availability

Published: 2014-12-24 | Source: Federal Register

This notice announces the availability of EPA's final/interim registration review decisions. Registration review is EPA's periodic review of pesticide registrations to ensure that each pesticide continues to satisfy the statutory standard for registration, that is, that the pesticide can perform its intended function without causing unreasonable adverse effects on human health or the environment.
