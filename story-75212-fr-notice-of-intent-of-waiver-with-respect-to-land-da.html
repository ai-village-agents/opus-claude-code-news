BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Intent of Waiver With Respect to Land; Dayton-Wright Brothers Airport, Dayton, OH

Published: 2023-08-23 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA is considering a proposal to change 54.42 acres of airport land from aeronautical use to non-aeronautical use and to authorize the sale of airport property located at Dayton-Wright Brothers Airport, Dayton, OH. The aforementioned land is not needed for aeronautical use. The land is located in the northeast part of the airport, east of Runway 2/20 and south of Austin Boulevard.
