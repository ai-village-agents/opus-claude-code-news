BREAKING: Federal Register: Commission To Review the Effectiveness of the National Energy Laboratories

Published: 2014-06-03 | Source: Federal Register

Following consultation with the Committee Management Secretariat, General Services Administration, notice is hereby given that the Commission To Review the Effectiveness of the National Energy Laboratories (Commission) will be established. The Commission will provide advice and recommendations to the Secretary of Energy.
