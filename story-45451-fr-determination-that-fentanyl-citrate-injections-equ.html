BREAKING: Federal Register: Determination That FENTANYL CITRATE Injections, Equivalent to 2.5 Milligram Base/50 Milliliter and Equivalent to 5 Milligram Base/100 Milliliter, Were Not Withdrawn From Sale for Reasons of Safety or Effectiveness

Published: 2024-08-28 | Source: Federal Register
