BREAKING: Federal Register: Solicitation for Members of the NOAA Science Advisory Board

Published: 2016-09-02 | Source: Federal Register

NOAA is soliciting nominations for members of the NOAA Science Advisory Board (SAB). The SAB is the only Federal Advisory Committee with the responsibility to advise the Under Secretary of Commerce for Oceans and Atmosphere and NOAA Administrator on long- and short-range strategies for research, education, and application of science to resource management and environmental assessment and prediction.
