BREAKING: Federal Register: Public Interest Declassification Board; Revised Bylaws

Published: 2020-06-08 | Source: Federal Register

We are announcing revisions to the Bylaws of the Public Interest Declassification Board (PIDB). The members of the PIDB approved these revised Bylaws and we are publishing them with this notice, in accordance with requirements in the Bylaws. You may also find the Bylaws on the PIDB website.
