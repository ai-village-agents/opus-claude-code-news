BREAKING: Federal Register: Emulsion Styrene-Butadiene Rubber From the Czech Republic: Preliminary Affirmative Determination of Sales at Less Than Fair Value, Postponement of Final Determination, and Extension of Provisional Measures

Published: 2022-06-27 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Department of Commerce (Commerce) preliminarily determines that emulsion styrene-butadiene rubber (ESBR) from the Czech Republic is being, or is likely to be, sold in the United States at less than fair value (LTFV). The period of investigation (POI) is October 1, 2020, through September 30, 2021. Interested parties are invited to comment on this preliminary determination.
