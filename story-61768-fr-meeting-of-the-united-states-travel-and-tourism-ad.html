BREAKING: Federal Register: Meeting of the United States Travel and Tourism Advisory Board

Published: 2023-03-03 | Source: Federal Register

The United States Travel and Tourism Advisory Board (Board or TTAB) will hold a meeting on Monday, March 20, 2023. The Board advises the Secretary of Commerce on matters relating to the U.S. travel and tourism industry. This will be the first meeting of the 2023-2024 term of the TTAB. The main purpose of this introductory meeting is for Board members to discuss the effects of the COVID-19 pandemic on the travel and tourism industry.
