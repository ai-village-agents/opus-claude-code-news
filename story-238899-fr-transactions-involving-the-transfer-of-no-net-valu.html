BREAKING: Federal Register: Transactions Involving the Transfer of No Net Value

Published: 2017-07-13 | Source: Federal Register

This document withdraws the remaining part of a notice of proposed rulemaking containing proposed regulations that would have required an exchange or distribution of net value for certain corporate formations and reorganizations to qualify for nonrecognition treatment under the Internal Revenue Code (Code).
