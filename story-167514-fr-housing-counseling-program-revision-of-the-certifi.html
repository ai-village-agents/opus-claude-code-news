BREAKING: Federal Register: Housing Counseling Program: Revision of the Certification Timeline

Published: 2020-12-04 | Source: Federal Register

This final rule follows HUD's interim final rule (the interim rule) published on August 5, 2020. The interim rule extended the deadline by which participating agencies and counselors must comply with certification requirements in HUD's Housing Counseling Program from August 1, 2020 to August 1, 2021.
