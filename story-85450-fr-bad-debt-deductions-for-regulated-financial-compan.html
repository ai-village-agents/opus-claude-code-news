BREAKING: Federal Register: Bad Debt Deductions for Regulated Financial Companies and Members of Regulated Financial Groups

Published: 2023-12-28 | Source: Federal Register

This document contains proposed regulations that would provide guidance regarding whether a debt instrument is worthless for Federal income tax purposes. The proposed regulations are necessary to update the standard for determining when a debt instrument held by a regulated financial company or a member of a regulated financial group will be conclusively presumed to be worthless.
