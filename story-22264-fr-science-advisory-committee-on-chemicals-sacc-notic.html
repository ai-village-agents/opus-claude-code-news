BREAKING: Federal Register: Science Advisory Committee on Chemicals (SACC); Notice of Postponement of Public Meeting

Published: 2025-11-13 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency has postponed the virtual preparatory meeting for the Science Advisory Committee on Chemicals (SACC) to consider the scope and clarity of the draft charge questions for the peer review of the draft risk evaluation of octamethylcyclotetrasiloxane (D4). The meeting was scheduled for November 18, 2025, and will now be held December 1, 2025. The meeting was announced in the Federal Register on September 19, 2025.
