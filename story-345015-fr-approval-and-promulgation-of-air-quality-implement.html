BREAKING: Federal Register: Approval and Promulgation of Air Quality Implementation Plans; Colorado; Revisions to New Source Review Rules

Published: 2013-01-24 | Source: Federal Register

EPA is taking final action to approve revisions adopted by the State of Colorado on December 15, 2005, to Regulation No. 3 (Air Pollutant Emission Notice and Permitting Requirements). Colorado submitted the request for approval of these rule revisions into the State Implementation Plan (SIP) on August 21, 2006. The revisions remove repealed provisions in Regulation No. 3 that pertain to the issuance of Colorado air quality permits; the revisions also implement other minor administrative changes ...
