BREAKING: Federal Register: Importation of Fresh Persimmons From New Zealand Into the United States

Published: 2017-10-03 | Source: Federal Register

We are amending the regulations concerning the importation of fruits and vegetables to allow the importation of fresh persimmons from New Zealand into the United States. As a condition of entry, the persimmons must be produced in accordance with a systems approach that includes requirements for orchard certification, orchard pest control, post-harvest safeguards, fruit culling, traceback, sampling, and treatment with either hot water or modified atmosphere treatment.
