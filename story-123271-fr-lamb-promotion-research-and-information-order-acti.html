BREAKING: Federal Register: Lamb Promotion, Research, and Information Order; Activity Changes; Comment Period Reopened

Published: 2021-05-07 | Source: Federal Register

The Agricultural Marketing Service (AMS) is providing an additional 60 days for public comments on the proposed rule that would amend the Lamb Promotion, Research, and Information Order (Order). During the February 22, 2021, through March 24, 2021, comment period, AMS received two comments requesting additional time to analyze a potential volume threshold that would allow low-volume market agencies to be eligible for flexibilities to the proposed assessment remittance process.
