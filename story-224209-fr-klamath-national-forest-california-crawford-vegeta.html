BREAKING: Federal Register: Klamath National Forest; California; Crawford Vegetation Management Project

Published: 2018-12-27 | Source: Federal Register

The Klamath National Forest is withdrawing the Notice of Intent (NOI) to prepare an Environmental Impact Statement (EIS) for the Crawford Vegetation Management Project. The original NOI was published in the Federal Register on January 27, 2014 (79 FR 4323). Upon further evaluation, there are no expected significant impacts to the human environment associated with the project.
