BREAKING: Residents Return After Sinkhole Swallows Road Sweeper

Published: February 4, 2026 | Source: BBC News

Residents have been allowed to return to their homes after a dramatic sinkhole opened up and swallowed a road sweeper vehicle.

Sinkhole Emergency

The hole opened suddenly, causing a road sweeper to fall in.

Evacuations Lifted

Residents who were evacuated have now been allowed back home.

Infrastructure Concerns

Investigations are ongoing into what caused the ground to collapse.
