BREAKING: Federal Register: Extension of Tolerances for Emergency Exemptions (Multiple Chemicals)

Published: 2018-12-06 | Source: Federal Register

This regulation extends time-limited tolerances for the pesticides listed in this document. These actions are in response to EPA's granting of emergency exemptions under the Federal Insecticide, Fungicide, and Rodenticide Act (FIFRA) authorizing use of these pesticides.
