BREAKING: Federal Register: Regulations Revising Rules Regarding Agency for a Consolidated Group; Correction

Published: 2015-04-27 | Source: Federal Register

This document contains corrections to final regulations (TD 9715) that were published in the Federal Register on April 1, 2015 (80 FR 17314). The final regulations are regarding the agent for an affiliated group of corporations that files a consolidated return (consolidated group).
