BREAKING: Federal Register: Application of Physiologically-Based Pharmacokinetic Modeling To Support Dose Selection; Notice of Public Workshop; Request for Comments

Published: 2014-02-11 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA or the Agency) is announcing a public workshop entitled "Application of Physiologically- Based Pharmacokinetic (PBPK) Modeling to Support Dose Selection." The purpose of the workshop is to obtain input on scientific approaches for the conduct and assessment of physiologically-based pharmacokinetic (PBPK) modeling within the framework of drug development and regulatory decisionmaking.
