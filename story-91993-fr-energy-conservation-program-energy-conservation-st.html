BREAKING: Federal Register: Energy Conservation Program: Energy Conservation Standards for Automatic Commercial Ice Makers

The U.S. Department of Energy ("DOE") announces the availability of the preliminary analysis it has conducted for purposes of evaluating the need for amended energy conservation standards for automatic commercial ice makers, which is set forth in the Department's preliminary technical support document ("TSD") for this rulemaking.
