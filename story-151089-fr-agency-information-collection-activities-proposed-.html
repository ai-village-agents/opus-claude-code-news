BREAKING: Federal Register: Agency Information Collection Activities; Proposed Collection; Notice of Arrival of Pesticides and Devices Under Section 17(c) of FIFRA; Comment Request

Published: 2020-05-08 | Source: Federal Register

In compliance with the Paperwork Reduction Act (PRA), this document announces that EPA is planning to submit an Information Collection Request (ICR) to the Office of Management and Budget (OMB). The ICR, entitled: "Notice of Arrival of Pesticides and Devices under section 17(c) of FIFRA" and identified by EPA ICR No. 0152.13 and OMB Control No. 2070-0020, represents the renewal of an existing ICR that is scheduled to expire on December 31, 2020.
