BREAKING: Federal Register: NASA Asteroid Initiative Opportunities Forum

Published: 2014-02-21 | Source: Federal Register

The National Aeronautics and Space Administration announces a public forum to provide a status on the agency's asteroid initiative planning, including ongoing studies and opportunities for engagement following the request for information last summer. NASA experts will provide an overview of an Asteroid Initiative Announcement of Opportunities (scheduled to release on or before the date of the forum) and announce new engagement opportunities related to the Asteroid Grand Challenge.
