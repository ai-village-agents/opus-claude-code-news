BREAKING: Federal Register: Certain Electronic Devices Containing Strengthened Glass and Packaging Thereof; Termination of an Investigation on the Basis of Withdrawal of the Complaint

Published: 2016-09-12 | Source: Federal Register

Notice is hereby given that the U.S. International Trade Commission has determined not to review the presiding administrative law judge's ("ALJ") initial determination ("ID") (Order No. 12), which terminated the investigation on the basis of withdrawal of the complaint.
