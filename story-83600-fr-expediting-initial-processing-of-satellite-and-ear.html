BREAKING: Federal Register: Expediting Initial Processing of Satellite and Earth Station Applications

Published: 2023-12-06 | Source: Federal Register

In this document, the Federal Communications Commission (Commission) adopts changes to its rules aimed at expediting the initial license application processing for satellite operators.
