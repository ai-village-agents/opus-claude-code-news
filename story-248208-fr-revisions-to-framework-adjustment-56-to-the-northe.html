BREAKING: Federal Register: Revisions to Framework Adjustment 56 to the Northeast Multispecies Fishery Management Plan

Published: 2017-11-08 | Source: Federal Register

We are adjusting the 2017 fishing year Georges Bank cod allocation for the common pool and making minor corrections to the 2017 sector carryover and annual catch entitlement. This action is necessary to respond to a 2016 overage of the Georges Bank cod allocation for the common pool fishery and correct an error in the carryover and annual catch entitlement available to sectors in 2017.
