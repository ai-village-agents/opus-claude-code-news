BREAKING: Federal Register: Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standards; Child Restraint Systems, Incorporation by Reference

Published: 2020-11-02 | Source: Federal Register

In accordance with the Moving Ahead for Progress in the 21st Century Act (MAP-21), this document proposes to amend Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard (FMVSS) No. 213, "Child restraint systems," by updating the standard seat assembly on which child restraint systems (CRSs) are tested to determine their compliance with the standard's dynamic performance requirements.
