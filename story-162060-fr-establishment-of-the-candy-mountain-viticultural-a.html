BREAKING: Federal Register: Establishment of the Candy Mountain Viticultural Area and Modification of the Yakima Valley Viticultural Area

Published: 2020-09-25 | Source: Federal Register

The Alcohol and Tobacco Tax and Trade Bureau (TTB) establishes the approximately 815-acre "Candy Mountain" viticultural area in Benton County, Washington. TTB is also expanding the boundary of the existing 1,093-square mile Yakima Valley viticultural area by approximately 72 acres in order to avoid a partial overlap with the newly established Candy Mountain viticultural area.
