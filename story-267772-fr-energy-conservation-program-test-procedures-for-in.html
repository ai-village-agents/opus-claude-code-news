BREAKING: Federal Register: Energy Conservation Program: Test Procedures for Integrated Light-Emitting Diode Lamps

Published: 2016-07-01 | Source: Federal Register

This final rule adopts a test procedure for integrated light- emitting diode (LED) lamps (hereafter referred to as LED lamps) to support the implementation of labeling provisions by the Federal Trade Commission (FTC), as well as the ongoing general service lamps rulemaking, which includes LED lamps.
