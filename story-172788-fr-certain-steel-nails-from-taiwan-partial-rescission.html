BREAKING: Federal Register: Certain Steel Nails From Taiwan: Partial Rescission of Antidumping Duty Administrative Review; 2017-2018

Published: 2019-02-27 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Commerce (Commerce) is rescinding the administrative review, in part, of the antidumping duty order on certain steel nails from Taiwan for the period July 1, 2017, through June 30, 2018.
