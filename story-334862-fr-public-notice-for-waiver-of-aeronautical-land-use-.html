BREAKING: Federal Register: Public Notice for Waiver of Aeronautical Land-Use Assurance

Published: 2014-09-16 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA is considering a proposal to change 76.592 acres of airport land from the federal obligation dedicating it to aeronautical use and to authorize this land to be used for revenue producing, non- aeronautical purposes at Indianapolis International Airport, Indianapolis, Indiana. The aforementioned land is not needed for current or future aeronautical use. The land is located along the southern boundary of the airport, bordered by Col. H.
