BREAKING: Federal Register: Public Land Order No. 7794; Extension of Public Land Order No. 6941; Utah

Published: 2012-08-09 | Source: Federal Register

This order extends the duration of the withdrawal created by Public Land Order No. 6941 for an additional 20-year period. The extension is necessary to continue the protection of the unique geologic, recreational, and visual resources of the Bonneville Salt Flats, which would otherwise expire on August 5, 2012.
