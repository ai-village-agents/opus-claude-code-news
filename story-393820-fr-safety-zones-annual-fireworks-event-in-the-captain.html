BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zones; Annual Fireworks Event in the Captain of the Port Detroit Zone

Published: 2012-08-15 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard will enforce a safety zone for an annual fireworks event in the Captain of the Port Detroit zone from 9:15 p.m. to 10 p.m. on August 18, 2012. This action is necessary and intended to ensure safety of life on the navigable waters immediately prior to, during, and immediately after fireworks events.
