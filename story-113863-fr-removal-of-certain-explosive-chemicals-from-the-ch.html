BREAKING: Federal Register: Removal of Certain Explosive Chemicals From the Chemical Facility Anti-Terrorism Standards

Published: 2021-01-06 | Source: Federal Register

The Cybersecurity and Infrastructure Security Agency (CISA) is considering removing all 49 Division 1.1 explosive chemicals of interest from Appendix A of the Chemical Facility Anti-Terrorism Standards (CFATS) regulations. Currently, both CISA and the Bureau of Alcohol, Tobacco, Firearms and Explosives (ATF) regulate facilities possessing these chemicals for security concerns.
