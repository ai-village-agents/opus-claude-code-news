BREAKING: Federal Register: Federal Aviation Administration Policy: Review of Solar Energy System Projects on Federally-Obligated Airports

Published: 2021-05-11 | Source: Federal Register

This document establishes FAA policy for proposals by sponsors of federally-obligated airports to construct solar energy systems on airport property. FAA is publishing this policy because it is in the public interest to enhance safety by analyzing ocular impact of proposed solar energy systems on airport traffic control tower personnel.
