BREAKING: German Activist Jailed in Hungary for Attacks at Nazi Rally

Published: February 5, 2026 | Source: BBC

A German activist has been sentenced to prison in Hungary following attacks at a far-right rally in the country.

Court Ruling

Hungarian courts handed down the prison sentence to the German national convicted of violent acts at the gathering.

Far-Right Rally

The incident occurred at a rally attended by far-right and neo-Nazi groups in Hungary.

International Reaction

The case has drawn attention to both far-right activities in Hungary and the treatment of counter-protesters.
