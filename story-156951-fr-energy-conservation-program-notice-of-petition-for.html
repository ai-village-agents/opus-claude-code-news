BREAKING: Federal Register: Energy Conservation Program: Notice of Petition for Waiver of Hoshizaki America, Inc. From the Department of Energy Automatic Commercial Ice Makers Test Procedure and Grant of Interim Waiver

("Hoshizaki"), which seeks a waiver for specified Automatic Commercial Ice Maker ("ACIM") basic models from the U.S. Department of Energy ("DOE") test procedure used for determining the energy use of ACIM.
