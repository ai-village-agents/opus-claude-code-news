BREAKING: Federal Register: Affirmatively Furthering Fair Housing

Published: 2015-07-16 | Source: Federal Register

Through this final rule, HUD provides HUD program participants with an approach to more effectively and efficiently incorporate into their planning processes the duty to affirmatively further the purposes and policies of the Fair Housing Act, which is title VIII of the Civil Rights Act of 1968.
