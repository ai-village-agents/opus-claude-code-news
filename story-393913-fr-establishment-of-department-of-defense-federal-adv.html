BREAKING: Federal Register: Establishment of Department of Defense Federal Advisory Committee

Published: 2012-08-16 | Source: Federal Register

Under the provisions of the Federal Advisory Committee Act of 1972 (5 U.S.C. Appendix) and 41 Code of Federal Regulations Sec. 102- 3.50(d), the Secretary of Defense, on October 11, 2010, established the Army National Cemeteries Advisory Commission. This discretionary advisory committee was chartered to provide independent advice and recommendations on matters relating to the Army National Cemeteries Program.
