BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Submission of Proposed Information Collection to OMB HUD Lead Hazard Control Grantees Regarding Their Use of Healthy Homes Supplemental Funding

Published: 2013-02-06 | Source: Federal Register

The proposed information collection requirement described below has been submitted to the Office of Management and Budget (OMB) for review, as required by the Paperwork Reduction Act. The Department is soliciting public comments on the subject proposal. Requirements for notification of lead based paint hazard in federally-owned residential properties and housing receiving Federal assistance, as codified in 24 CFR part 35.
