BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Piper Aircraft, Inc. Airplanes

Published: 2013-07-10 | Source: Federal Register

We are adopting a new airworthiness directive (AD) for certain Piper Aircraft, Inc. Models PA-46-310P, PA-46-350P, PA-46R-350T, and PA-46-500TP airplanes. This AD requires inspecting the fuel vent valves to identify if the nitrile parts are installed and modifying and eventually replacing the fuel vent valves if the nitrile parts are installed.
