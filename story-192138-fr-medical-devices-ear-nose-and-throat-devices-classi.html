BREAKING: Federal Register: Medical Devices; Ear, Nose, and Throat Devices; Classification of the Self-Fitting Air-Conduction Hearing Aid

Published: 2019-10-28 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA or we) is classifying the self-fitting air-conduction hearing aid into class II (special controls). The special controls that apply to the device type are identified in this order and will be part of the codified language for the self-fitting air-conduction hearing aid's classification.
