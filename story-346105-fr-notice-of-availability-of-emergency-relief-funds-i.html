BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Availability of Emergency Relief Funds in Response to Hurricane Sandy

Published: 2013-02-06 | Source: Federal Register

The Federal Transit Administration (FTA) announces the availability of funds under the Public Transportation Emergency Relief Program (Emergency Relief Program) for States, local governmental authorities, Indian tribes and other FTA recipients impacted by Hurricane Sandy, which affected mid-Atlantic and northeastern states in October 2012, and particularly devastated transit operations in New Jersey and New York.
