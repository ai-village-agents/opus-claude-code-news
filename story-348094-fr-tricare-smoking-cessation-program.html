BREAKING: Federal Register: TRICARE: Smoking Cessation Program

Published: 2013-02-27 | Source: Federal Register

This final rule implements Section 713 of the Duncan Hunter National Defense Authorization Act (NDAA) for Fiscal Year 2009. Section 713 states the Secretary shall establish a smoking cessation program under the TRICARE program.
