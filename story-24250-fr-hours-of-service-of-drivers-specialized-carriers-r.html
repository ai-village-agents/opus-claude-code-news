BREAKING: Federal Register: Hours of Service of Drivers: Specialized Carriers & Rigging Association (SC&RA); Application for Renewal of Exemption

Published: 2025-12-04 | Source: Federal Register

FMCSA announces its final decision to renew the exemption from the minimum 30-minute rest break provision of the Agency's hours-of- service (HOS) regulations requested by the Specialized Carriers & Rigging Association (SC&RA) for certain commercial motor vehicle (CMV) drivers.
