BREAKING: Federal Register: Withdrawal of the Commission Policy Statement on Monetary Equitable Remedies in Competition Cases

Published: 2012-08-07 | Source: Federal Register

In 2003 the Federal Trade Commission issued a Policy Statement on Monetary Remedies in Competition Cases. The Commission has now withdrawn the Policy Statement.
