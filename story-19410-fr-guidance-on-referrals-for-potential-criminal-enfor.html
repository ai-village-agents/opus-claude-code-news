BREAKING: Federal Register: Guidance on Referrals for Potential Criminal Enforcement

Published: 2025-09-03 | Source: Federal Register

On May 9, 2025, the President signed Executive Order (E.O.) 14294, Fighting Overcriminalization in Federal Regulations. Section 7 of the Executive Order requires each agency to publish guidance in the Federal Register that describes the agency's plan to address criminally liable regulatory offenses. None of the statutes or regulations that the Inter-American Foundation (IAF) administers carry criminal penalties for violations.
