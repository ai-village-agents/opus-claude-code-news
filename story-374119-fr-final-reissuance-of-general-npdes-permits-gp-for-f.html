BREAKING: Federal Register: Final Reissuance of General NPDES Permits (GP) for Facilities Related to Oil and Gas Extraction

Published: 2012-01-03 | Source: Federal Register

A GP regulating the activities of facilities related to oil and gas extraction on the North Slope of the Brooks Range, Alaska expired on January 2, 2009. On July 2, 2009, EPA proposed to reissue the GP expanding the coverage area to the TransAlaska Pipeline Corridor along with other potential corridors. There was a 45 day comment period.
