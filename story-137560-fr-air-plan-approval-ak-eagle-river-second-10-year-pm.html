BREAKING: Federal Register: Air Plan Approval; AK; Eagle River Second 10-Year PM10 Limited Maintenance Plan

Published: 2021-11-09 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is approving the Eagle River, Alaska (AK) limited maintenance plan (LMP) submitted on November 10, 2020, by the Alaska Department of Environmental Conservation (ADEC or "the State"). This plan addresses the second 10-year maintenance period after redesignation for particulate matter with an aerodynamic diameter less than or equal to a nominal 10 micrometers (PM10).
