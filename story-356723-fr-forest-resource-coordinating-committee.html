BREAKING: Federal Register: Forest Resource Coordinating Committee

Published: 2013-06-06 | Source: Federal Register

The Forest Resource Coordinating Committee will meet via teleconference every month on the following dates within 2013: June 20, July 18, August 15, and September 19 from 12:00 p.m. to 1:00 p.m. (eastern time). The committee is authorized under the Food, Conservation, and Energy Act of 2008 (P.L. 110-246).
