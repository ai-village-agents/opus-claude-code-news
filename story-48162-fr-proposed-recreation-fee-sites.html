BREAKING: Federal Register: Proposed Recreation Fee Sites

Published: 2024-09-26 | Source: Federal Register

The National Forests in Mississippi are proposing to establish a recreation fee site and two special recreation permits. Proposed recreation fees collected at the proposed recreation fee site and for the proposed special recreation permits would be used for operation, maintenance, and improvement of the site and the specialized recreation use covered by the proposed special recreation permit.
