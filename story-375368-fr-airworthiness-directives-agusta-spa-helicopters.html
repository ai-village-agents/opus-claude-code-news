BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Agusta S.p.A. Helicopters

Published: 2012-01-20 | Source: Federal Register

We propose to adopt a new airworthiness directive (AD) for Agusta S.p.A. (Agusta) Model AB139 and AW139 helicopters with a certain generator control unit (GCU). This proposed AD was prompted by laboratory tests which revealed a potential fault in the overvoltage protection on a certain part-numbered GCU.
