BREAKING: Federal Register: Bacillus Calmette-Guérin-Unresponsive Nonmuscle Invasive Bladder Cancer: Developing Drugs and Biologics for Treatment; Guidance for Industry; Availability

Published: 2018-02-13 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA or Agency) is announcing the availability of a guidance for industry entitled "Bacillus Calmette-Gu[eacute]rin (BCG)-Unresponsive Nonmuscle Invasive Bladder Cancer: Developing Drugs and Biologics for Treatment." This guidance was developed to assist in the development of drugs and biologics for patients with a form of bladder cancer that is not amenable to currently available medical therapy and remains an unmet medical need.
