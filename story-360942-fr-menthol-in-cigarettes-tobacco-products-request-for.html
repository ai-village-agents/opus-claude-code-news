BREAKING: Federal Register: Menthol in Cigarettes, Tobacco Products; Request for Comments

Published: 2013-07-24 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA) is issuing this advance notice of proposed rulemaking (ANPRM) to obtain information related to the potential regulation of menthol in cigarettes. FDA is also making available its preliminary scientific evaluation of public health issues related to the use of menthol in cigarettes.
