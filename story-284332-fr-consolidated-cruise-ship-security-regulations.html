BREAKING: Federal Register: Consolidated Cruise Ship Security Regulations

Published: 2015-01-21 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard announces a public meeting will take place on February 9, 2015, in Fort Lauderdale, Florida to receive comments on a notice of proposed rulemaking (NPRM) published in the Federal Register on December 10, 2014, under the title "Consolidated Cruise Ship Security Regulations."
