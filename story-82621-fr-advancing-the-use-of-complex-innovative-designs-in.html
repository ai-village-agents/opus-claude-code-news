BREAKING: Federal Register: Advancing the Use of Complex Innovative Designs in Clinical Trials: From Pilot to Practice; Public Workshop; Request for Comments

Published: 2023-11-22 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA or the Agency) is announcing the following public workshop entitled "Advancing the Use of Complex Innovative Designs in Clinical Trials: From Pilot to Practice." The purpose of the public workshop is to discuss aspects of complex adaptive, Bayesian, and other novel clinical trial designs.
