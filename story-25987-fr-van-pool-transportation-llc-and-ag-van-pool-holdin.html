BREAKING: Federal Register: Van Pool Transportation LLC and Ag Van Pool Holdings, LP-Continuance in Control-Rolling V Bus Corp. and STS of New Mexico, LLC

Published: 2025-12-23 | Source: Federal Register

On November 26, 2025, Van Pool Transportation LLC (Van Pool) and AG Van Pool Holdings, LP (AG Holdings) (collectively, Applicants), both noncarriers, seek Board approval to continue in control of two of Applicants' subsidiaries--Rolling V Bus Corp. (Rolling V) and STS of New Mexico, LLC (STS)--upon Rolling V and STS becoming federally regulated passenger motor carriers.
