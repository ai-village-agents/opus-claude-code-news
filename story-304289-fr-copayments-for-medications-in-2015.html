BREAKING: Federal Register: Copayments for Medications in 2015

Published: 2015-09-16 | Source: Federal Register

This document adopts as a final rule, with changes, an interim final rule amending the Department of Veterans Affairs (VA) medical regulations to freeze the copayments required for certain medications provided by VA until December 31, 2015.
