BREAKING: Federal Register: Internal Revenue Service Advisory Council (IRSAC); Nominations

Published: 2012-04-23 | Source: Federal Register

The Internal Revenue Service (IRS) requests applications of individuals to be considered for selection as members of the Internal Revenue Service Advisory Council (IRSAC).
