BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Availability of the Draft Environmental Impact Statement for Department of Energy Activities in Support of Commercial Production of High-Assay Low-Enriched Uranium (HALEU)

Published: 2024-03-07 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Department of Energy (DOE) announces the availability of the Draft Environmental Impact Statement for Department of Energy Activities in Support of Commercial Production of High-Assay Low- Enriched Uranium (HALEU) (Draft HALEU EIS) (DOE/EIS-0559). DOE is also announcing a public comment period and public hearings to receive comments on the Draft HALEU EIS.
