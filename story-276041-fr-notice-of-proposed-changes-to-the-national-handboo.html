BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Proposed Changes to the National Handbook of Conservation Practices for the Natural Resources Conservation Service

Published: 2016-10-07 | Source: Federal Register

Notice is hereby given of the intention of NRCS to issue a series of revised conservation practice standards in NHCP. These standards include Brush Management (Code 314), Herbaceous Weed Treatment (Code 315), Lined Waterway or Outlet (Code 468), Prescribed Grazing (Code 528), and Restoration of Rare or Declining Natural Communities (Code 643).
