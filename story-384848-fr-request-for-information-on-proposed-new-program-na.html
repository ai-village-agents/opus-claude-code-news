BREAKING: Federal Register: Request for Information on Proposed New Program: National Network for Manufacturing Innovation (NNMI)

Published: 2012-05-04 | Source: Federal Register

The NIST-hosted Advanced Manufacturing National Program Office (AMNPO) invites interested parties to provide input on a new public- private partnership program, the National Network for Manufacturing Innovation (NNMI or Network).
