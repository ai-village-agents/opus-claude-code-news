BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Availability of the Winnemucca District Resource Management Plan and Record of Decision, Nevada

Published: 2015-05-22 | Source: Federal Register

The Bureau of Land Management (BLM) announces the availability of the Record of Decision (ROD) for the Approved Resource Management Plan (RMP) for the Winnemucca District located in northern Nevada. The Nevada State Director signed the ROD on May 21, 2015, which constitutes the final decision of the BLM and makes the Approved RMP effective immediately.
