BREAKING: Federal Register: Draft-American Indian and Alaska Native Worker Safety and Health Strategic Plan

Published: 2022-06-28 | Source: Federal Register

The National Institute for Occupational Safety and Health (NIOSH) in the Centers for Disease Control and Prevention (CDC), an Operating Division of the Department of Health and Human Services (HHS), announces the availability of a draft strategic plan for public comment entitled Revised Draft of American Indian and Alaska Native Worker Safety and Health Strategic Plan (Revised Draft Strategic Plan).
