BREAKING: Federal Register: Proposed Amendment of Class E Airspace, Bloomsburg, PA

Published: 2018-06-22 | Source: Federal Register

This action proposes to amend Class E airspace extending upward from 700 feet or more above the surface at Bloomsburg, PA, due to the decommissioning of the Milton very high frequency omni- directional range collocated tactical air navigation aid (VORTAC) which requires airspace reconfiguration at Bloomsburg Municipal Airport.
