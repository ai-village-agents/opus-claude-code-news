BREAKING: Federal Register: Privacy Act of 1974; System of Records

Published: 2023-06-08 | Source: Federal Register

Pursuant to the Privacy Act of 1974, the National Credit Union Administration (NCUA) gives notice of a new proposed Privacy Act system of records. The new system is NCUA-28, Anti-Harassment Case Tracking and Records. This system will maintain information collected for the purpose of conducting internal investigations into allegations of harassment brought by NCUA employees and NCUA contractors and taking appropriate action(s).
