BREAKING: Federal Register: Small Business Size Standards: Real Estate and Rental and Leasing

Published: 2012-09-24 | Source: Federal Register

The United States Small Business Administration (SBA) is increasing the small business size standards for 21 industries and one sub-industry in North American Industry Classification System (NAICS) Sector 53, Real Estate and Rental and Leasing, and retaining the current standards for the remaining four industries in that Sector.
