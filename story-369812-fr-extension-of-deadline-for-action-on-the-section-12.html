BREAKING: Federal Register: Extension of Deadline for Action on the Section 126 Petition From Eliot, Maine

Published: 2013-11-08 | Source: Federal Register

In this action, the EPA is determining that 60 days is insufficient time to complete the technical and other analyses and public notice-and-comment process required for our review of a petition submitted by the Town of Eliot, Maine pursuant to section 126 of the Clean Air Act (CAA).
