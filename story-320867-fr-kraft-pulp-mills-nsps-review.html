BREAKING: Federal Register: Kraft Pulp Mills NSPS Review

Published: 2014-04-04 | Source: Federal Register

This action finalizes revisions to the new source performance standards for kraft pulp mills. These revised standards include particulate matter emission limits for recovery furnaces, smelt dissolving tanks and lime kilns, and opacity limits for recovery furnaces and lime kilns equipped with electrostatic precipitators.
