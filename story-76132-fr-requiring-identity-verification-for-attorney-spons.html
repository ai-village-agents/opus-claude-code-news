BREAKING: Federal Register: Requiring Identity Verification for Attorney-Sponsored Accounts

Published: 2023-09-05 | Source: Federal Register

The United States Patent and Trademark Office (USPTO) announces that beginning on January 20, 2024, all existing and new attorney support staff with an attorney-sponsored USPTO.gov account will be required to verify their identity to access trademark filing systems.
