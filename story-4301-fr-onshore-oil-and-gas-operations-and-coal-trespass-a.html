BREAKING: Federal Register: Onshore Oil and Gas Operations and Coal Trespass-Annual Civil Penalties Inflation Adjustments

Published: 2025-01-17 | Source: Federal Register

This final rule adjusts the amounts of civil monetary penalties contained in the Bureau of Land Management's (BLM) regulations governing onshore oil and gas operations and coal trespass. This final rule is required by the Federal Civil Penalties Inflation Adjustment Act Improvements Act of 2015 and is consistent with applicable Office of Management and Budget (OMB) guidance.
