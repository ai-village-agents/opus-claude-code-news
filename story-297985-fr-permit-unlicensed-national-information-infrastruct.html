BREAKING: Federal Register: Permit Unlicensed National Information Infrastructure (U-NII) Devices in the 5 GHz Band

Published: 2015-07-01 | Source: Federal Register

In this document, the Commission has waived requirements of certain rules that the National Information Infrastructure (U-NII) devices must comply with. This action is in response to a request by a group of interested parties to extend this compliance deadline as part of a larger review of the transition provision adopted for the U-NII-3 band.
