BREAKING: Federal Register: Inflation Catch-Up Adjustment of Civil Monetary Penalty Amounts

Published: 2016-06-15 | Source: Federal Register

This interim final rule amends HUD's civil monetary penalty regulations by making inflation adjustments as mandated by the Federal Civil Penalties Inflation Adjustment Act Improvements Act of 2015. HUD also removes three obsolete civil monetary penalty regulations previously authorized under statutes for which either HUD no longer has enforcement authority or the program is no longer active.
