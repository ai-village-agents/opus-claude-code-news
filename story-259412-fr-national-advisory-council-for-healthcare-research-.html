BREAKING: Federal Register: National Advisory Council for Healthcare Research and Quality: Request for Nominations for Public Members

Published: 2016-03-30 | Source: Federal Register

42 U.S.C. 299c establishes a National Advisory Council for Healthcare Research and Quality (the Council). The Council is to advise the Secretary of HHS (Secretary) and the Director of the Agency for Healthcare Research and Quality (AHRQ) on matters related to activities of the Agency to carry out its mission.
