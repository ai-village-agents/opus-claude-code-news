BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Intent To Prepare an Environmental Impact Statement for Energy Conservation Standards for Manufactured Housing

Published: 2021-07-07 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Department of Energy (DOE) is required, as set forth in the Energy Independence and Security Act of 2007 (EISA), to establish energy conservation standards for manufactured housing. EISA further directs DOE to base its energy conservation standards on the most recent version of the International Energy Conservation Code (IECC), and any supplements to that document, except where DOE finds that the IECC is not cost effective or where a more stringent standard would be more cost effective....
