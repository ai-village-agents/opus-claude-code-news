BREAKING: Federal Register: Wind and Water Power Technologies Office; Notice of a Meeting

Published: 2015-03-02 | Source: Federal Register

The Atmosphere to Electrons (A2e) Initiative within the U.S. Department of Energy's (DOE) Office of Energy Efficiency and Renewable Energy intends to hold an External Merit Review in Washington, DC, on March 31 & April 1, 2015. The External Review Panel will review the current program planning and provide suggestion on the formulation of A2e strategy, goals and implementation approaches.
