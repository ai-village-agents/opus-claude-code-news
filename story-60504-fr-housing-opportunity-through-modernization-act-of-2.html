BREAKING: Federal Register: Housing Opportunity Through Modernization Act of 2016: Implementation of Sections 102, 103, and 104

Published: 2023-02-14 | Source: Federal Register

This final rule revises HUD regulations to implement parts of the Housing Opportunity Through Modernization Act of 2016 (HOTMA). In addition to amending regulations for HUD's public housing and Section 8 programs, this final rule revises the program regulations for several other HUD programs. HUD did this in the interest of aligning its requirements across its programs or because the underlying program statute required HUD to make the revisions.
