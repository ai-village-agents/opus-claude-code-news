BREAKING: Federal Register: Change in Control: Cove Point LNG, LP

Published: 2023-08-01 | Source: Federal Register

The Office of Fossil Energy and Carbon Management (FECM) of the Department of Energy (DOE) gives notice of receipt of a Notification in Accordance with Procedures for Changes in Control (Notification) filed by Cove Point LNG, LP (CPLNG) on July 11, 2023. The Notification describes an expected change in CPLNG's upstream ownership. The Notification was filed under the Natural Gas Act (NGA).
