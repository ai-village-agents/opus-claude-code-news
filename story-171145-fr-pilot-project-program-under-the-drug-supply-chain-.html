BREAKING: Federal Register: Pilot Project Program Under the Drug Supply Chain Security Act; Program Announcement

Published: 2019-02-08 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA or Agency) is announcing the start of the Pilot Project Program Under the Drug Supply Chain Security Act (DSCSA Pilot Project Program). The DSCSA Pilot Project Program is intended to assist FDA and members of the pharmaceutical distribution supply chain in the development of the electronic, interoperable system that will identify and trace certain prescription drugs as they are distributed within the United States.
