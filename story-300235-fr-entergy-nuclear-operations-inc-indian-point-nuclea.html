BREAKING: Federal Register: Entergy Nuclear Operations, Inc.; Indian Point Nuclear Generating, Unit No. 3

Published: 2015-07-30 | Source: Federal Register

The Nuclear Regulatory Commission (NRC) has granted the request of Entergy Nuclear Operations, Inc., to withdraw its application dated August 14, 2012, for a proposed amendment to Facility Operating License No. DPR-64 for Indian Point Nuclear Generating, Unit No. 3.
