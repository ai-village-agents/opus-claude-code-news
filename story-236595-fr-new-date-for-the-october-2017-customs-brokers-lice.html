BREAKING: Federal Register: New Date for the October 2017 Customs Broker's License Examination

Published: 2017-06-14 | Source: Federal Register

This document announces that U.S. Customs and Border Protection has changed the date on which the semi-annual examination for an individual broker's license will be held in October 2017.

Document Type: Notice

Agency: Homeland Security Department, U.S. Customs and Border Protection
