BREAKING: Federal Register: Notification of Regional Roundtable Discussions Regarding “Waters of the United States”

Published: 2021-10-25 | Source: Federal Register

On June 9, 2021, the U.S. Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) and the U.S. Department of the Army (hereafter, "the agencies") announced their intent to revise the definition of "waters of the United States" under the Clean Water Act through two rulemakings-- first, a foundational rule that will propose to restore longstanding protections, and a second rulemaking process that builds on that regulatory foundation.
