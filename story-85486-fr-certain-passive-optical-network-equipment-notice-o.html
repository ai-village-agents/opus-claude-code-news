BREAKING: Federal Register: Certain Passive Optical Network Equipment; Notice of Institution of Investigation

Published: 2023-12-29 | Source: Federal Register

Notice is hereby given that a complaint was filed with the U.S. International Trade Commission on November 14, 2023, under section 337 of the Tariff Act of 1930, as amended, on behalf of Optimum Communications Services, Inc. of Jersey City, New Jersey. An amended complaint was filed on November 22, 2023.
