BREAKING: Federal Register: Importation of Beef From a Region in Argentina

Published: 2015-07-02 | Source: Federal Register

We are amending the regulations governing the importation of certain animals, meat, and other animal products to allow, under certain conditions, the importation of fresh (chilled or frozen) beef from a region in Argentina located north of Patagonia South and Patagonia North B, referred to as Northern Argentina.
