BREAKING: Federal Register: National Service Life Insurance Premium Payment and Loan Amendment

Published: 2022-06-10 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Veterans Affairs (VA) is amending its National Service Life Insurance (NSLI) regulations to offer Service- Disabled Veterans' Insurance (S-DVI) policyholders the option of remitting premiums for government life insurance coverage only on a monthly or annual basis.
