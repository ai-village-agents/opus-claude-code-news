BREAKING: Federal Register: Solicitation of Nominations for Members of the USDA Grain Inspection Advisory Committee

Published: 2013-12-10 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Agriculture's (USDA) Grain Inspection, Packers and Stockyards Administration (GIPSA) is seeking nominations for individuals to serve on the USDA Grain Inspection Advisory Committee (Advisory Committee). The Advisory Committee meets annually to advise GIPSA on the programs and services it delivers under the U.S. Grain Standards Act (USGSA).
