BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zones in Reentry Sites; Tampa, and Tallahassee, Florida

Published: 2023-04-20 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is activating two safety zones for the National Aeronautics and Space Administration (NASA) Commercial Resupply Service 27 (CRS 27) mission, reentry vehicle splashdown, and recovery operations. These operations will occur in the U.S. Exclusive Economic Zone (EEZ). Our regulation for safety zones in reentry sites within the Seventh Coast Guard District identifies the regulated areas for this event.
