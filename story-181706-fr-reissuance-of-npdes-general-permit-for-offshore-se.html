BREAKING: Federal Register: Reissuance of NPDES General Permit for Offshore Seafood Processors in Alaska (AKG524000)

Published: 2019-06-17 | Source: Federal Register

The Director of the Water Division, EPA Region 10, is reissuing a National Pollutant Discharge Elimination System (NPDES) General Permit to Offshore Seafood Processors in Alaska.
