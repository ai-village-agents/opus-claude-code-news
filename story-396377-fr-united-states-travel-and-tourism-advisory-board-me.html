BREAKING: Federal Register: United States Travel and Tourism Advisory Board; Meeting

Published: 2012-09-14 | Source: Federal Register

This notice sets forth the schedule and agenda for an open meeting of the United States Travel and Tourism Advisory Board (Board). The Board will meet to present updates on the work of its subcommittees and hear briefings from representatives of the U.S. government on the implementation of the National Travel and Tourism Strategy and the progress on implementing the President's Executive Order 13597 on travel and tourism.
