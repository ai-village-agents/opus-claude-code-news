BREAKING: Federal Register: Security Zones; Port of Palm Beach, Port Everglades, Port of Miami, and Port of Key West, Florida

Published: 2019-07-12 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is modifying the existing security zone regulations for the Port of Palm Beach, Port Everglades, Port of Miami, and Port of Key West Florida. The amendments will create a new section for Sector Key West security zones; clarify when the Port Everglades fixed security zones will be in effect; modify and lengthen a portion of one Port Everglades fixed security zone; and update language and definitions throughout the regulation.
