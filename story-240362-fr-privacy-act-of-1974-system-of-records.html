BREAKING: Federal Register: Privacy Act of 1974; System of Records

Published: 2017-08-01 | Source: Federal Register

Pursuant to the provisions of the Privacy Act, notice is hereby given that the Department of Veterans Affairs (VA) is amending the system of records currently entitled, "Department of Veterans Affairs Federal Docket Management System (VAFDMS--Commenter Info)-- (140VA02REG)" as set forth in the Federal Register on March 3, 2015.
