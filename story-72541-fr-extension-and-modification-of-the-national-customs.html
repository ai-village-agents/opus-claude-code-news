BREAKING: Federal Register: Extension and Modification of the National Customs Automation Program Test Concerning the Submission Through the Automated Commercial Environment of Certain Unique Entity Identifiers for the Global Business Identifier Evaluative Proof of Concept

Published: 2023-07-21 | Source: Federal Register

On December 2, 2022, U.S. Customs and Border Protection (CBP) published a notice in the Federal Register announcing a National Customs Automation Program Test concerning the submission of unique entity identifiers for the Global Business Identifier (GBI) Evaluative Proof of Concept (EPoC).
