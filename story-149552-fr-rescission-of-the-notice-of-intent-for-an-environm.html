BREAKING: Federal Register: Rescission of the Notice of Intent for an Environmental Impact Statement: Hartford County, Connecticut

Published: 2020-04-20 | Source: Federal Register

The FHWA is issuing this notice to advise the public that we are rescinding the Notice of Intent (NOI) to prepare an Environmental Impact Statement (EIS) for the I-84 Hartford Project in Hartford County, Connecticut.
