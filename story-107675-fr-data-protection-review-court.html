BREAKING: Federal Register: Data Protection Review Court

Published: 2022-10-14 | Source: Federal Register

As authorized and directed by the Executive order of October 7, 2022, "Enhancing Safeguards for United States Signals Intelligence Activities," this rule amends Department of Justice regulations to establish within the Department a Data Protection Review Court ("DPRC").
