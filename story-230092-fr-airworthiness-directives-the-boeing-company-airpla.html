BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; The Boeing Company Airplanes

Published: 2017-03-21 | Source: Federal Register

We are superseding Airworthiness Directive (AD) 2017-03-04, which applied to all The Boeing Company Model 737-500 series airplanes. AD 2017-03-04 required inspections to detect cracks in the fuselage skin panels, permanent repairs of time-limited repairs, skin panel replacement, and related investigative and corrective actions if necessary.
