BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Dassault Aviation Airplanes

Published: 2020-12-21 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA is superseding Airworthiness Directive (AD) 2019-23- 05, which applied to all Dassault Aviation Model MYSTERE-FALCON 900 airplanes. AD 2019-23-05 required revising the existing maintenance or inspection program, as applicable, to incorporate new or more restrictive airworthiness limitations.
