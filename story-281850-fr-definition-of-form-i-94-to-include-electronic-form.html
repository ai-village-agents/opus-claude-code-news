BREAKING: Federal Register: Definition of Form I-94 To Include Electronic Format

Published: 2016-12-19 | Source: Federal Register

This final rule adopts, without change, interim amendments to the Department of Homeland Security (DHS) regulations which were published in the Federal Register on March 27, 2013, as CBP Dec. No. 13-06. These amendments enabled DHS to transition the issuance of the Form I-94 (Arrival/Departure Record) to an automated process.
