BREAKING: Federal Register: Advisory Committee on Immunization Practices (ACIP); Notice of Meeting and Request for Comment

Published: 2019-08-30 | Source: Federal Register

In accordance with the Federal Advisory Committee Act, the Centers for Disease Control and Prevention (CDC), announces the following meeting of the Advisory Committee on Immunization Practices (ACIP). This meeting is open to the public, limited only by room seating. The meeting room accommodates 216 for public seating.
