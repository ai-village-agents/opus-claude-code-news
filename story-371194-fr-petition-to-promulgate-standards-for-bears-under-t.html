BREAKING: Federal Register: Petition To Promulgate Standards for Bears Under the Animal Welfare Act Regulations

Published: 2013-11-26 | Source: Federal Register

We are notifying the public that the Animal and Plant Health Inspection Service has received a petition requesting that we amend the Animal Welfare Act regulations to add specific standards for the humane handling, care, treatment, and transportation of all species of bears held in captivity except polar bears, for which there are already standards.
