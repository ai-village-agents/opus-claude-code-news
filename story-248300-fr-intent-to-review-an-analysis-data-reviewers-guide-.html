BREAKING: Federal Register: Intent To Review an Analysis Data Reviewer's Guide; Notice of Availability, Request for Comments

Published: 2017-11-08 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA), Center for Drug Evaluation and Research (CDER), is establishing a public docket to collect comments related to a proposed Analysis Data Reviewer's Guide (ADRG) template. As part of FDA's ongoing collaboration with the Pharmaceutical Users Software Exchange (PhUSE), an independent, non-profit consortium addressing computational science issues, a PhUSE working group developed the PhUSE ADRG template.
