BREAKING: Federal Register: Procedures Further Implementing the Annual Limitation on Suspension of Deportation and Cancellation of Removal

Published: 2016-11-30 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Justice proposes to amend the regulations of the Executive Office for Immigration Review (EOIR) governing the annual statutory limitation on cancellation of removal and suspension of deportation decisions. First, the rule proposes to eliminate certain procedures created in 1998 that were used to convert 8,000 conditional grants of suspension of deportation and cancellation of removal to outright grants before the end of fiscal year 1998.
