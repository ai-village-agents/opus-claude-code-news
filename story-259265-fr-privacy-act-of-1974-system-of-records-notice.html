BREAKING: Federal Register: Privacy Act of 1974; System of Records Notice

Published: 2016-03-29 | Source: Federal Register

In accordance with the requirements of the Privacy Act of 1974, as amended (5 U.S.C. 552a), HHS is updating a department-wide system of records, System No. 09-90-0058, currently titled "Freedom of Information Case Files and Correspondence Control Log, HHS/OS/ASPA/ FOIA." This system of records was established prior to 1979 (see 44 FR 58144) and was previously revised in 1989 and 1994 (see 54 FR 41684 and 59 FR 55845).
