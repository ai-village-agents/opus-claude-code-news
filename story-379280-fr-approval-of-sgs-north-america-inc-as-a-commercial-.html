BREAKING: Federal Register: Approval of SGS North America, Inc., as a Commercial Gauger

Published: 2012-03-02 | Source: Federal Register

Notice is hereby given that, pursuant to 19 CFR 151.13, SGS North America, Inc., 6624 Langley Dr., Baton Rouge, LA 70809, has been approved to gauge petroleum, petroleum products, organic chemicals and vegetable oils for customs purposes, in accordance with the provisions of 19 CFR 151.13.
