BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Final Adoption and Effective Date

Published: 2020-11-12 | Source: Federal Register

The National Capital Planning Commission (NCPC) has adopted revised Submission Guidelines related to tree preservation and replacement on federal development sites. Federal and non-federal agency applicants whose development proposals and plans are subject to statutory mandated Commission plan and project review must submit their proposals to the Commission following a process laid out in the Submission Guidelines.
