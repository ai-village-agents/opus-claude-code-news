BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Cancellation of Customs Broker Licenses Due to Death of the License Holder

Published: 2014-07-11 | Source: Federal Register

Notice is hereby given that the customs broker license of certain brokers have been canceled without prejudice due to the death of the license holders.
