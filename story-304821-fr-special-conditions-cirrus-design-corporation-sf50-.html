BREAKING: Federal Register: Special Conditions: Cirrus Design Corporation, SF50; Full Authority Digital Engine Control (FADEC) System

Published: 2015-09-23 | Source: Federal Register

These special conditions are issued for the Cirrus Design Corporation SF50 airplane. This airplane will have a novel or unusual design feature(s) associated with the use of an electronic engine control system instead of a traditional mechanical control system. The applicable airworthiness regulations do not contain adequate or appropriate safety standards for this design feature.
