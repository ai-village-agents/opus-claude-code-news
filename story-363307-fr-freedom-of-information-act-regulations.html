BREAKING: Federal Register: Freedom of Information Act Regulations

Published: 2013-08-19 | Source: Federal Register

The National Capital Planning Commission ("NCPC" or "Commission") proposes to revise the current regulations the NCPC follows for processing Requests for Information under the Freedom of Information Act (FOIA). The revisions reorganize the regulations to focus each section on a discrete topic. The revisions also incorporate new information in response to changes to the FOIA.
