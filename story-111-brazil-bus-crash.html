BREAKING: Bus Carrying Pilgrims Overturns in Brazil Killing 15

Published: February 4, 2026 | Source: BBC

A bus carrying religious pilgrims has overturned in Brazil, killing at least 15 people on board in a tragic accident.

Fatal Crash

The accident occurred when the bus carrying pilgrims overturned, with emergency services responding to the scene of the devastating crash.

Casualties

At least 15 people were killed in the incident, with additional passengers injured and receiving medical treatment.

Investigation

Authorities are investigating the cause of the crash as the nation mourns the victims of the pilgrimage tragedy.
