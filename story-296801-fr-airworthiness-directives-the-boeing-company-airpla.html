BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; The Boeing Company Airplanes

Published: 2015-06-18 | Source: Federal Register

We are adopting a new airworthiness directive (AD) for certain The Boeing Company Model and 777 airplanes. This AD was prompted by reports of uncommanded door closure of a large lower lobe cargo door. This AD requires inspecting for part numbers and serial numbers of the rotary actuators of the forward and aft large lower lobe cargo doors, as applicable, and corrective action if necessary.
