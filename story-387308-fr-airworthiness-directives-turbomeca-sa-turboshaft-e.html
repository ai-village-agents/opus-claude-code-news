BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Turbomeca S.A. Turboshaft Engines

Published: 2012-06-01 | Source: Federal Register

We propose to supersede an existing airworthiness directive (AD) that applies to certain Turbomeca S.A. Arriel 2B and 2B1 turboshaft engines. The existing AD currently requires accomplishment of the TU166 modification. Since we issued that AD, we became aware of an accident involving an engine in-flight shutdown on a twin-engine helicopter powered by two Arriel 2S2 engines.
