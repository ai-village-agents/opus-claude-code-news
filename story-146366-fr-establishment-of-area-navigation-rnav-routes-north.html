BREAKING: Federal Register: Establishment of Area Navigation (RNAV) Routes; Northeastern United States

Published: 2020-03-10 | Source: Federal Register

This action establishes two new low altitude RNAV routes, designated T-356, and T-358, in the northeastern United States. The new routes enhance the efficiency of the National Airspace System (NAS) by expanding the availability of RNAV routing and supporting the transition of the NAS from ground-based to satellite-based navigation.
