BREAKING: Federal Register: Changes To Eliminate Unnecessary Regulations

Published: 2018-01-19 | Source: Federal Register

The United States Patent and Trademark Office (USPTO or Office) proposes to remove its regulations governing reservation clauses, petitions from the refusal of a primary examiner to admit an amendment, the publication of amendments to the regulations, and limits that the Director can impose on the number of inter partes reviews and post-grant reviews heard by the Patent Trial and Appeal Board.
