BREAKING: Federal Register: Carbon Dioxide Capture, Utilization and Sequestration (CCUS) Federal Lands and Outer Continental Shelf Permitting Task Force

Published: 2022-07-28 | Source: Federal Register

As required by the Utilizing Significant Emissions with Innovative Technologies (USE IT) Act, the Council on Environmental Quality (CEQ) is seeking member nominations from a diverse range of qualified candidates to serve on the "Carbon Dioxide Capture, Utilization and Sequestration (CCUS) Federal Lands and Outer Continental Shelf Permitting Task Force" (Federal and OCS Task Force). Vacancies are anticipated to be filled by December 31, 2022.
