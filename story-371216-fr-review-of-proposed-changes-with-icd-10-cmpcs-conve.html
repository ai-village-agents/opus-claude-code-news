BREAKING: Federal Register: Review of Proposed Changes with ICD-10-CM/PCS; Conversion of Quality IndicatorsTM (QIs)

Published: 2013-11-26 | Source: Federal Register

The Agency for Healthcare Research and Quality (AHRQ) seeks comments on the proposed conversion of the AHRQ Quality IndicatorsTM to "International Classification of Diseases, 10th Edition, Clinical Modification/Procedure Coding System" (ICD-10- CM and ICD-10-PCS) codes. These changes would be applicable to hospital discharges occurring on or after October 1, 2014. The proposed ICD-10- CM/PCS codes are posted on the AHRQ QI Web site for review at: http:// www.qualityindicators.ahrq.gov...
