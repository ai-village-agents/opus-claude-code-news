BREAKING: Federal Register: Partitioning, Disaggregation, and Leasing of Spectrum

Published: 2021-12-29 | Source: Federal Register

In this document, the Federal Communications Commission (Commission or FCC) proposed an Enhanced Competition Incentive Program to encourage licensees to offer opportunities for small carriers, Tribal Nations, and entities committing to serve rural areas to obtain spectrum via lease, partition, or disaggregation.
