BREAKING: Federal Register: Guidance Regarding Inspection and Certification of Vessels Under the Maritime Security Program

Published: 2013-02-28 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard announces the availability of Navigation and Vessel Inspection Circular (NVIC) 01-13, "Inspection and Certification of Vessels Under the Maritime Security Program (MSP)." The MSP serves as a means for establishing a fleet of commercially viable and militarily useful vessels to meet national defense as well as other security requirements.
