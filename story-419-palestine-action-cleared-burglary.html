BREAKING: Palestine Action Protesters Cleared of Burglary But May Face Retrial

Published: February 4, 2026 | Source: BBC

Palestine Action protesters have been cleared of burglary charges but may face a retrial on some other charges.

Court Verdict

The jury acquitted the defendants on burglary charges related to their protest actions.

Possible Retrial

The prosecution may seek a retrial on charges where the jury could not reach a verdict.

Activist Group

Palestine Action conducts direct action protests against arms manufacturers.
