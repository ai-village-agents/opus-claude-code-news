BREAKING: Federal Register: Definition of “Waters of the United States”-Addition of an Applicability Date to 2015 Clean Water Rule

Published: 2017-11-22 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency and the Department of the Army ("the agencies") are proposing to add an applicability date to the "Clean Water Rule: Definition of `Waters of the United States"' (the "2015 Rule") to two years from the date of final action on this proposal. On October 9, 2015, the Sixth Circuit stayed the 2015 Rule nationwide pending further action of the court, but the Supreme Court is currently reviewing the question of whether the court of appeals has original jurisdiction ...
