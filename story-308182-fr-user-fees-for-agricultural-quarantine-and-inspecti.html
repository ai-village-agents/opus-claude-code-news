BREAKING: Federal Register: User Fees for Agricultural Quarantine and Inspection Services

Published: 2015-10-29 | Source: Federal Register

We are amending the user fee regulations by adding new fee categories and adjusting current fees charged for certain agricultural quarantine and inspection services that are provided in connection with certain commercial vessels, commercial trucks, commercial railroad cars, commercial aircraft, and international passengers arriving at ports in the customs territory of the United States.
