BREAKING: Federal Register: Walnuts Grown in California; Hearing on Proposed Amendment of Marketing Order No.984

Published: 2020-02-11 | Source: Federal Register

Notice is hereby given of a public hearing to receive evidence on proposed amendments to Federal Marketing Order No. 984 (Order) regulating the handling of walnuts grown in California.
