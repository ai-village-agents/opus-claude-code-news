BREAKING: Federal Register: Special Local Regulation; St. Thomas Lighted Boat Parade, St. Thomas, U.S. Virgin Island

Published: 2019-12-20 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is establishing a special local regulation for the St. Thomas Lighted Boat Parade marine event. The special local regulation is for certain navigable waters of Crown Bay, Haulover Cay, and St. Thomas Harbor, St. Thomas, U.S. Virgin Islands. The special local regulation is necessary to ensure the safety of vessels, spectators, and public during the event.
