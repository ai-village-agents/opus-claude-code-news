BREAKING: Federal Register: Programmatic Environmental Assessment and Draft Finding of No Significant Impact Regarding Iron Dome Defense System-Army

The Department of the Army (Army) announces the availability of a Programmatic Environmental Assessment (PEA) and a Draft Finding of No Significant Impact (FONSI) regarding the proposed fielding of two Iron Dome Defense System--Army (IDDS-A) batteries.
