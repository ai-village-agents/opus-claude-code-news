BREAKING: Federal Register: Hours of Service of Drivers: Farruggio's Express, Application for Exemption

Published: 2016-02-12 | Source: Federal Register

FMCSA announces that it has received an application from Farruggio's Express (Farruggio) for an exemption from timecard requirements for its drivers who may not meet all of the conditions for utilization of the 100 air-mile radius log book exemption in that section. The request would exempt Farruggio's drivers who stay within the 100 air-mile radius, but may occasionally exceed the 12 hour limitation, from having to complete a daily record of duty status (RODS).
