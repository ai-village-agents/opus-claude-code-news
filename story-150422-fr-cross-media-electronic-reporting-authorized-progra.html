BREAKING: Federal Register: Cross-Media Electronic Reporting: Authorized Program Revision Approval, Commonwealth of Virginia

Published: 2020-04-30 | Source: Federal Register

This notice announces the Environmental Protection Agency's (EPA) approval of the Commonwealth of Virginia's Department of Environmental Quality (DEQ) request to revise/modify certain its EPA- authorized programs to allow electronic reporting.
