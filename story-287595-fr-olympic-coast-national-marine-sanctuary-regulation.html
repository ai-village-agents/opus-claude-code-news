BREAKING: Federal Register: Olympic Coast National Marine Sanctuary Regulations; Correction

Published: 2015-03-02 | Source: Federal Register

The National Oceanic and Atmospheric Administration (NOAA) is reinstating missing paragraphs of the Olympic Coast National Marine Sanctuary (OCNMS) regulations that pertain to the issuance of permits. NOAA inadvertently excluded the paragraphs in the publication of a November 2011 final rule revising OCNMS permitting regulations.
