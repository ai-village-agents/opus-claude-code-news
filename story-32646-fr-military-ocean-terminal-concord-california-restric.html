BREAKING: Federal Register: Military Ocean Terminal Concord, California; Restricted Area

Published: 2024-03-22 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Army Corps of Engineers (Corps) is amending its regulations to modify an existing permanent restricted area within waters along the shoreline of the Military Ocean Terminal Concord (MOTCO), on the south shore of Suisun Bay, north of the City of Concord, Contra Costa County, California.
