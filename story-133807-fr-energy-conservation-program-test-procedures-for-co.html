BREAKING: Federal Register: Energy Conservation Program: Test Procedures for Consumer Products; Early Assessment Review; Faucets and Showerheads

Published: 2021-09-24 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Department of Energy ("DOE") is extending the public comment period for the early assessment request for information ("RFI") regarding proposals to amend the test procedures for faucets and showerheads. DOE published the RFI in the Federal Register on September 2, 2021, establishing a 32-day public comment period ending October 4, 2021.
