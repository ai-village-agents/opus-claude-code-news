BREAKING: Federal Register: Importation of Jackfruit, Pineapple, and Starfruit From Malaysia Into the Continental United States

Published: 2013-05-07 | Source: Federal Register

We are proposing to amend the fruits and vegetables regulations to allow the importation of fresh jackfruit, pineapple, and starfruit from Malaysia into the continental United States. As a condition of entry, all three commodities would have to be irradiated for insect pests, inspected, and imported in commercial consignments.
