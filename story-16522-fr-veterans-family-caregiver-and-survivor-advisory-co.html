BREAKING: Federal Register: Veterans' Family, Caregiver, and Survivor Advisory Committee

Published: 2025-07-23 | Source: Federal Register

We are giving notice that the Secretary of Veterans Affairs intends to reestablish the Veterans' Family, Caregiver, and Survivor Advisory Committee for a 2-year period. The Secretary has determined that the Committee is necessary and in the public interest.
