BREAKING: Federal Register: Submission for OMB Review; Prohibition on Contracting With Corporations With Delinquent Taxes or a Felony Conviction

Published: 2016-06-28 | Source: Federal Register

Under the provisions of the Paperwork Reduction Act, the Regulatory Secretariat Division has submitted to the Office of Management and Budget (OMB) a request to review and approve a new information collection requirement regarding Prohibition on Contracting with Corporations with Delinquent Taxes or a Felony Conviction. A notice and request for comments was published in the Federal Register at 80 FR 75903 on December 4, 2015, as part of an interim rule under FAR case 2015-011. No public comments...
