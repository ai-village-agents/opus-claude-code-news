BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of a Change in Status of the Extended Benefit (EB) Program for the Virgin Islands

Published: 2018-01-31 | Source: Federal Register

This notice announces a change in benefit payment status under the EB program for the Virgin Islands. The following change has occurred since the publication of the last notice regarding the Virgin Islands' EB status: The Virgin Islands' 13-week insured unemployment rate (IUR) for the week ending December 02, 2017 was 5.68 percent which exceeds 120 percent of the corresponding rate in the prior year.
