BREAKING: Federal Register: Proposed Amendment of Class D Airspace and Establishment of Class E Airspace; Butts Army Airfield (AAF) (Fort Carson) Airport, CO

Published: 2022-08-02 | Source: Federal Register

An airspace review was conducted at Butts AAF (Fort Carson) Airport, CO to support the airport's transition from visual flight rules (VFR) to instrument flight rules (IFR), in addition to fulfilling a biennial review requirement. This action proposes to modify the Class D surface area, and establish Class E airspace extending upward from 700 feet above the surface at Butts AAF (Fort Carson) Airport, CO.
