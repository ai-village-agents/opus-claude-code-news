BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Intent of Waiver With Respect to Land; Indianapolis International Airport, Indianapolis, Indiana

Published: 2020-07-30 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA is considering a proposal to change 24.813 acres of airport land from aeronautical use to non-aeronautical use and to authorize the sale of airport property located at Indianapolis International Airport, Indianapolis, Indiana. The aforementioned land is not needed for aeronautical use. The land is located east of Ronald Regan Parkway, on the northwest side of the airport.
