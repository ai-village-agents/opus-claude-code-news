BREAKING: Federal Register: Florida Disaster Number FL-00119

Published: 2016-11-01 | Source: Federal Register

This is an amendment of the Presidential declaration of a major disaster for Public Assistance Only for the State of Florida (FEMA-4280-DR), dated 09/28/2016. Incident: Hurricane Hermine. Incident Period: 08/31/2016 through 09/11/2016. Effective Date: 10/21/2016. Physical Loan Application Deadline Date: 11/28/2016. Economic Injury (EIDL) Loan Application Deadline Date: 06/28/2017.
