BREAKING: Federal Register: Seizure and Forfeiture Procedures

Published: 2023-07-25 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Fish and Wildlife Service (Service, FWS, or we) is revising its seizure and forfeiture regulations. These regulations establish procedures relating to property seized or subject to administrative forfeiture under various laws enforced by the Service.
