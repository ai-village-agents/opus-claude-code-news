BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Inventory Completion Amendment: U.S. Department of the Interior, National Park Service, Yellowstone National Park, Mammoth Hot Springs, WY

Published: 2024-08-13 | Source: Federal Register

In accordance with the Native American Graves Protection and Repatriation Act (NAGPRA), the U.S. Department of the Interior, National Park Service, Yellowstone National Park (YELL) has amended a notice of inventory completion published in the Federal Register on November 22, 2006. This notice amends the Indian Tribes or Native Hawaiian organizations with cultural affiliation.
