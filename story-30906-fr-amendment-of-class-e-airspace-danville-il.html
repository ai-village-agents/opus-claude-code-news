BREAKING: Federal Register: Amendment of Class E Airspace; Danville, IL

Published: 2024-03-01 | Source: Federal Register

This action amends the Class E airspace at Danville, IL. This action is the result of an airspace review conducted due to the decommissioning of the Danville very high frequency omnidirectional range (VOR) as part of the VOR Minimum Operating Network (MON) Program. The name and geographic coordinates of the airport are also being updated to coincide with the FAA's aeronautical database.
