BREAKING: Federal Register: Property Carrier Broker Listening Session; Public Meeting

Published: 2020-10-13 | Source: Federal Register

FMCSA announces that it will host a listening session pertaining to property carrier brokers. FMCSA would like to hear from members of the public on their views on the regulation of property carrier brokers in general, and on three pending petitions concerning specific property carrier broker regulation issues.
