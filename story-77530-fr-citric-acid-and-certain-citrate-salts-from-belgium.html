BREAKING: Federal Register: Citric Acid and Certain Citrate Salts From Belgium: Preliminary Results of the Sunset Review of the Antidumping Duty Order

Published: 2023-09-22 | Source: Federal Register

On June 1, 2023, the U.S. Department of Commerce (Commerce) initiated a sunset review of the antidumping duty (AD) order on citric acid and certain citrate salts (citric acid) from Belgium pursuant to section 751(c) of the Tariff Act of 1930, as amended (the Act). Commerce determined that it was appropriate to conduct a full review.
