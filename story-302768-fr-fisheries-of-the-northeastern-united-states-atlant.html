BREAKING: Federal Register: Fisheries of the Northeastern United States; Atlantic Herring Fishery; Framework Adjustment 4

Published: 2015-08-27 | Source: Federal Register

NMFS proposes management measures recommended by the New England Fishery Management Council in Framework Adjustment 4 to the Atlantic Herring Fishery Management Plan to further enhance catch monitoring and address discarding in the herring fishery.
