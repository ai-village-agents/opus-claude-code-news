BREAKING: Federal Register: Stainless Steel Butt-Weld Pipe Fittings From the Philippines: Final Results of Antidumping Duty Administrative Review and Final Determination of No Shipments; 2021-2022

Published: 2023-02-24 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Department of Commerce (Commerce) determines that there were no shipments of merchandise subject to the antidumping duty (AD) order on stainless steel butt-weld pipe fittings (SSBWF) from the Philippines during the period of review (POR) February 1, 2021, through January 31, 2022, from any of the companies under review.
