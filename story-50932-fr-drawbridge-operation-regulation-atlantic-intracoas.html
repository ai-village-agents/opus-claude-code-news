BREAKING: Federal Register: Drawbridge Operation Regulation; Atlantic Intracoastal Waterway, Jupiter, FL

Published: 2024-10-25 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is temporarily modifying the operating schedule that governs the Indiantown Road Bridge across the Atlantic Intracoastal Waterway (AICW), mile 1006.2, at Jupiter, FL. The bridge is currently operating under a temporary final rule (TFR) allowing the drawbridge to remain closed to navigation during designated times daily including weekends, to alleviate vehicle traffic congestion caused by the replacement of an adjacent bridge.
