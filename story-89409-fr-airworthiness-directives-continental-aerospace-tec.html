BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Continental Aerospace Technologies, Inc. and Continental Motors Reciprocating Engines

Published: 2022-02-22 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA is adopting a new airworthiness directive (AD) for certain Continental Aerospace Technologies, Inc. C-125, C145, IO-360, IO-470, IO-550, O-300, O-470, TSIO-360, TSIO-520 series model reciprocating engines and certain Continental Motors IO-520 series model reciprocating engines with a certain oil filter adapter installed.
