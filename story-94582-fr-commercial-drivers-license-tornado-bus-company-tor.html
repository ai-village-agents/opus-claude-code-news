BREAKING: Federal Register: Commercial Driver's License: Tornado Bus Company (Tornado); Application for Exemption

Published: 2022-04-27 | Source: Federal Register

FMCSA announces its decision to deny the exemption request from the Tornado Bus Company (Tornado). Tornado requests an exemption from certain provisions of the Federal Motor Carrier Safety Regulations (FMCSRs) for its drivers who currently hold a Mexican Licencia Federal de Conductor (LFC), and are seeking permanent resident status in the United States through the Department of Homeland Security and have over two years' experience driving in the United States (U.S.) and Mexico.
