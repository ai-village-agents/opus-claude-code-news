BREAKING: Federal Register: Representation Procedures and Rulemaking Authority

Published: 2012-12-21 | Source: Federal Register

In response to amendments to the Railway Labor Act in the Federal Aviation Administration Modernization Reform Act of 2012, the National Mediation Board amends its existing regulations pertaining to representation elections, run-off elections, and rulemaking to reflect changes in statutory language.
