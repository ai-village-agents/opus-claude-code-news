BREAKING: Federal Register: Opportunity To Co-Sponsor Office for Human Research Protections Research Community Forums

Published: 2016-07-06 | Source: Federal Register

The Office for Human Research Protections (OHRP) announces the opportunity for non-federal public and private sector entities to co-sponsor OHRP Research Community Forums (RCFs). Potential co-sponsors must have an approved Federal-wide Assurance with OHRP, be recipients of HHS grants for human subject research, and have a demonstrated interest and experience in the protection of human subjects in research.
