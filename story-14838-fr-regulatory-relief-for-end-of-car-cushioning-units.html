BREAKING: Federal Register: Regulatory Relief for End of Car Cushioning Units

Published: 2025-07-01 | Source: Federal Register

FRA proposes to amend regulations concerning freight car draft arrangement and end-of-car cushioning units (EOCCs) to make regulatory relief now provided by waiver permanent. The amendments would allow a freight car to remain in service if the EOCC is operative and equipped with a unit condition indicator (UCI) that indicates a non-discharged EOCC.
