BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Funding Availability (NOFA) for Delta Health Care Services Grants

Published: 2013-03-26 | Source: Federal Register

Rural Business--Cooperative Service (RBS), an agency of the United States Department of Agriculture, announces the availability of grant funds through the Delta Health Care Services Grant Program. Pursuant to the 2012 Appropriations Act, $3,000,000 is available to be competitively awarded for the Delta Health Care Services Grant Program. The minimum grant amount is $50,000.
