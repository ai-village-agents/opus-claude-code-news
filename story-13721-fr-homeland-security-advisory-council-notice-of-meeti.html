BREAKING: Federal Register: Homeland Security Advisory Council; Notice of Meeting

Published: 2025-06-16 | Source: Federal Register

The Office of Partnership and Engagement is publishing this notice to announce that the Homeland Security Advisory Council will meet in person on Monday, June 30, 2025. This meeting will be open to the public via livestream. This meeting will be led by senior leadership of Homeland Security. Senior Leadership will introduce the new Chair and Vice Chair of the Council, and the Council will discuss the Council's purpose, focus, and potential taskings.
