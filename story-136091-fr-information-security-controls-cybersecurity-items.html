BREAKING: Federal Register: Information Security Controls: Cybersecurity Items

Published: 2021-10-21 | Source: Federal Register

This interim final rule outlines the progress the United States has made in export controls pertaining to cybersecurity items, revised Commerce Control List (CCL) implementation, and requests from the public information about the impact of these revised controls on U.S. industry and the cybersecurity community.
