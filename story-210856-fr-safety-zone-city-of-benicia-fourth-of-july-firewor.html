BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone: City of Benicia Fourth of July Fireworks Display, Carquinez Straight, Benicia, CA

Published: 2018-07-03 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is establishing a temporary safety zone in the navigable waters of San Francisco Bay near Carquinez Strait in support of the City of Benicia Fourth of July Fireworks Display on July 4, 2018. This safety zone is established to ensure the safety of participants and spectators from the dangers associated with pyrotechnics.
