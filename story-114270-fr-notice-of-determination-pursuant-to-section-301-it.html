BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Determination Pursuant to Section 301: Italy's Digital Services Tax

Published: 2021-01-12 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Trade Representative has determined that Italy's Digital Services Tax (DST) is unreasonable or discriminatory and burdens or restricts U.S. commerce and thus is actionable under Section 301.

Document Type: Notice

Agency: Trade Representative, Office of United States
