BREAKING: Federal Register: Trand Doan Nguyen; Denial of Hearing; Final Debarment Order

Published: 2017-10-24 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA) is denying Trang Doan Nguyen's (Nguyen's) request for a hearing and is issuing an order under the Federal Food, Drug, and Cosmetic Act (the FD&C Act) debarring Nguyen for 5 years from providing services in any capacity to a person that has an approved or pending drug product application.
