BREAKING: Federal Register: Extensions of Credit by Federal Reserve Banks

Published: 2014-01-06 | Source: Federal Register

The Board invites public comment on proposed amendments to Regulation A (Extensions of Credit by Federal Reserve Banks) that would implement sections 1101 and 1103 of the Dodd-Frank Wall Street Reform and Consumer Protection Act of 2010 (the "Dodd-Frank Act").
