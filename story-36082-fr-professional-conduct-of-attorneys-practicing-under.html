BREAKING: Federal Register: Professional Conduct of Attorneys Practicing Under the Cognizance and Supervision of the Judge Advocate General

Published: 2024-05-03 | Source: Federal Register

This rule proposes to remove existing general information about the professional responsibility requirements of attorneys practicing under the cognizance and supervision of the Judge Advocate General (JAG) and includes a new requirement for all non-U.S. Government attorneys to file a notice of appearance before appearing in any matter for which the JAG is charged with supervising the provision of legal services.
