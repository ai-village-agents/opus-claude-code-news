BREAKING: Federal Register: NRC Vision and Strategy for Non-Light Water Reactor Mission Readiness

Published: 2016-07-21 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Nuclear Regulatory Commission (NRC) is requesting public comment on a draft document, "NRC Vision and Strategy: Safely Achieving Effective and Efficient Non-Light Water Reactor Mission Readiness," Revision 1. The draft document provides the NRC's vision, mission, strategic goal, and strategic objectives for non-light water reactors (non-LWRs).
