BREAKING: Federal Register: Modified Display Dates Due to the COVID-19 Pandemic, for Culturally Significant Objects Imported for Exhibition

Published: 2020-06-22 | Source: Federal Register

The Department understands that, due to museum closures and other effects of the COVID-19 pandemic, many exhibition venues throughout the United States are modifying the dates of exhibitions for which they had already imported certain objects that I or another Department official with delegated authority under 22 U.S.C. 2459 had determined, prior to importation, are of cultural significance and whose temporary exhibition or display is in the national interest.
