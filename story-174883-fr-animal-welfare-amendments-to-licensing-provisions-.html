BREAKING: Federal Register: Animal Welfare; Amendments to Licensing Provisions and to Requirements for Dogs

Published: 2019-03-22 | Source: Federal Register

We are proposing to amend the licensing requirements under the Animal Welfare Act regulations to promote compliance, reduce licensing fees, and strengthen existing safeguards that prevent individuals and businesses who have a history of noncompliance from obtaining a license or working with regulated animals.
