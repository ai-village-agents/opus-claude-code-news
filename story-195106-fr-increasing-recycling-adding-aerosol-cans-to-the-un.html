BREAKING: Federal Register: Increasing Recycling: Adding Aerosol Cans to the Universal Waste Regulations

Published: 2019-12-09 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA or the Agency) is adding hazardous waste aerosol cans to the universal waste program under the Federal Resource Conservation and Recovery Act (RCRA) regulations. This change will benefit the wide variety of establishments generating and managing hazardous waste aerosol cans, including the retail sector, by providing a clear, protective system for managing discarded aerosol cans.
