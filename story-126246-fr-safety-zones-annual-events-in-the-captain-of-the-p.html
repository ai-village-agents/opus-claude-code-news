BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zones; Annual Events in the Captain of the Port Detroit Zone

Published: 2021-06-17 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard will enforce various safety zones for annual marine events in the Captain of the Port Detroit zone. Enforcement of these zones is necessary and intended to protect safety of life and property on the navigable waters immediately prior to, during, and immediately after these fireworks events.
