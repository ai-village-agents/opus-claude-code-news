BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Bell Helicopter Textron Canada Helicopters

Published: 2021-03-09 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA is withdrawing a notice of proposed rulemaking (NPRM) that proposed to adopt a new airworthiness directive (AD) that would have applied to certain Bell Helicopter Textron Canada Model 206A, 206B, and 206B3 helicopters. The NPRM was prompted by the need for corresponding operating limitations prohibiting flight, including hover, with the litter doorpost removed when certain litter kits are installed.
