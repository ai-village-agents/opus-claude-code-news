BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Final Federal Agency Actions on Proposed Highway in California

Published: 2014-06-20 | Source: Federal Register

The FHWA, on behalf of Caltrans, is issuing this notice to announce actions taken by Caltrans, that are final within the meaning of 23 U.S.C. 139(l)(1).
