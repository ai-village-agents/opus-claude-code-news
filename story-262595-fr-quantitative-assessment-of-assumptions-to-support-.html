BREAKING: Federal Register: Quantitative Assessment of Assumptions To Support Extrapolation of Efficacy in Pediatrics; Public Workshop

Published: 2016-05-04 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA), in collaboration with the University of Maryland Center of Excellence in Regulatory Science and Innovation (CERSI), is announcing a public workshop entitled "Quantitative Assessment of Assumptions to Support Extrapolation of Efficacy in Pediatrics." The objective of the workshop is to discuss quantitative and qualitative approaches for verifying assumptions pertaining to disease and therapeutic response similarity between adults and children.
