BREAKING: Federal Register: Availability of Data on Allocations of Cross-State Air Pollution Rule Allowances to Existing Electricity Generating Units

Published: 2021-05-17 | Source: Federal Register

Under the Cross-State Air Pollution Rule (CSAPR) trading program regulations, EPA allocates emission allowances to existing electricity generating units as provided in a notice of data availability (NODA).
