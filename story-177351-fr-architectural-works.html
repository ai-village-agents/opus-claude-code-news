BREAKING: Federal Register: Architectural Works

Published: 2019-04-23 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Copyright Office is amending its regulations pertaining to the registration of architectural works. To improve the efficiency of the registration process, and encourage broader participation in the registration system, the final rule will require applicants to submit their claims using an online application, rather than a paper application.
