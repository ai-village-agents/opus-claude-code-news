BREAKING: Federal Register: Fisheries of the Caribbean, Gulf, and South Atlantic; Aquaculture

Published: 2014-08-28 | Source: Federal Register

NMFS proposes regulations to implement the Fishery Management Plan for Regulating Offshore Aquaculture in the Gulf of Mexico (FMP), as prepared by the Gulf of Mexico Fishery Management Council (Council). The FMP entered into effect by operation of law on September 3, 2009.
