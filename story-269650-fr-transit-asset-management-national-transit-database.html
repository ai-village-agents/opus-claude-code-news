BREAKING: Federal Register: Transit Asset Management; National Transit Database

Published: 2016-07-26 | Source: Federal Register

The Federal Transit Administration is publishing a final rule to define the term state of good repair and to establish minimum Federal requirements for transit asset management that will apply to all recipients and subrecipients of chapter 53 funds that own, operate, or manage public transportation capital assets.
