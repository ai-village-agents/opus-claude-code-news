BREAKING: Federal Register: Reissuance of NPDES General Permit for Concentrated Animal Feeding Operations (CAFOs) Located in Idaho

Published: 2012-04-09 | Source: Federal Register

The Director, Office of Water and Watersheds, EPA Region 10, is publishing notice of availability of the final National Pollutant Discharge Elimination System (NPDES) general permit (IDG010000), authorizing discharges from CAFOs in Idaho including CAFOs in Indian Country.
