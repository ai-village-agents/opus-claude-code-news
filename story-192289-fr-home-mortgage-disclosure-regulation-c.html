BREAKING: Federal Register: Home Mortgage Disclosure (Regulation C)

Published: 2019-10-29 | Source: Federal Register

The Bureau of Consumer Financial Protection (Bureau) is amending Regulation C to adjust the threshold for reporting data about open-end lines of credit by extending to January 1, 2022, the current temporary threshold of 500 open-end lines of credit.
