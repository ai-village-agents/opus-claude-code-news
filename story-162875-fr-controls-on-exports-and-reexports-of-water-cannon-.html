BREAKING: Federal Register: Controls on Exports and Reexports of Water Cannon Systems

Published: 2020-10-06 | Source: Federal Register

In this rule, the Bureau of Industry and Security (BIS) amends the Export Administration Regulations to impose a license requirement on exports and reexports of water cannon systems for riot or crowd control and parts and components specially designed therefor.
