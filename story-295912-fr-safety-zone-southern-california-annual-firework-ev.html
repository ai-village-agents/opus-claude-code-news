BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone; Southern California Annual Firework Events for the San Diego Captain of the Port Zone

Published: 2015-06-09 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard will enforce the Coronado Glorietta Bay Fourth of July Fireworks safety zone on July 4, 2015. This reoccurring annual marine event occurs on the navigable waters of Glorietta Bay, a subsection of San Diego Bay in San Diego, California. This action is necessary to provide for the safety of the participants, crew, spectators, safety vessels, and general users of the waterway.
