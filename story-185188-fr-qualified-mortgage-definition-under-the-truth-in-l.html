BREAKING: Federal Register: Qualified Mortgage Definition Under the Truth in Lending Act (Regulation Z)

Published: 2019-07-31 | Source: Federal Register

With certain exceptions, Regulation Z requires creditors to make a reasonable, good faith determination of a consumer's ability to repay any residential mortgage loan, and loans that meet Regulation Z's requirements for "qualified mortgages" obtain certain protections from liability.
