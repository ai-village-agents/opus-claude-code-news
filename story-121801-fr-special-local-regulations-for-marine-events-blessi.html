BREAKING: Federal Register: Special Local Regulations for Marine Events; Blessing of the Fleet, Tiburon, CA

Published: 2021-04-21 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard will enforce the special local regulations for the annual Blessing of the Fleet Boat Parade on April 25, 2021 to provide for the safety of life on the navigable waters of Raccoon Strait during this event. Our regulation for marine events in Northern California identifies the regulated area for this event in Raccoon Strait near Tiburon, CA.
