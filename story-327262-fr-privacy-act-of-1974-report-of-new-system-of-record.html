BREAKING: Federal Register: Privacy Act of 1974; Report of New System of Records

Published: 2014-06-17 | Source: Federal Register

In accordance with the requirements of the Privacy Act of 1974, we are proposing to establish a new SOR, titled "CMS Encounter Data System (EDS)", System No. 09-70-0506. CMS intends to collect encounter data, or data on each item or service delivered to enrollees of Medicare Advantage (MA) plans offered by MA organizations as defined at Title 42, Code of Federal Regulation (CFR), Sec. 422.4.
