BREAKING: Federal Register: Endangered and Threatened Wildlife and Plants; Endangered Species Status for Ghost Orchid

Published: 2025-06-05 | Source: Federal Register

We, the U.S. Fish and Wildlife Service (Service), propose to list the ghost orchid (Dendrophylax lindenii), a flowering plant species found in the United States in southwestern Florida and in Cuba as an endangered species under the Endangered Species Act of 1973, as amended (Act). This determination also serves as our 12-month finding on a petition to list the ghost orchid.
