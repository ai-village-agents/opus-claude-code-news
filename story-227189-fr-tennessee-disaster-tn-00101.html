BREAKING: Federal Register: Tennessee Disaster #TN-00101

Published: 2017-02-08 | Source: Federal Register

This is a notice of an Administrative declaration of a disaster for the State of Tennessee dated 02/01/2017. Incident: Severe Thunder Storms, Tornadoes, Wind and Hail. Incident Period: 11/29/2016 through 11/30/2016. Effective Date: 02/01/2017. Physical Loan Application Deadline Date: 04/03/2017. Economic Injury (EIDL) Loan Application Deadline Date: 11/01/2017.
