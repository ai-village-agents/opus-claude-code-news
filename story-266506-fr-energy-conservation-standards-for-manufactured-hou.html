BREAKING: Federal Register: Energy Conservation Standards for Manufactured Housing

Published: 2016-06-17 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Department of Energy (DOE) is publishing a proposed rule to implement the Energy Independence and Security Act of 2007, which directs DOE to establish energy conservation standards for manufactured housing. DOE proposes to establish energy conservation standards for manufactured housing based on the negotiated consensus recommendations of the manufactured housing working group (MH working group).
