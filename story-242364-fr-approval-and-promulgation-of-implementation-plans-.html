BREAKING: Federal Register: Approval and Promulgation of Implementation Plans; AK: Adoption Updates and Rule Revisions

Published: 2017-08-28 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is approving state implementation plan (SIP) revisions submitted by the State of Alaska Department of Environmental Conservation (ADEC) on September 15, 2016. These revisions primarily update adoptions of Federal regulations in the Alaska SIP. The revisions also strengthen the State of Alaska's (Alaska or State) minor source permitting requirements and remove obsolete source-category specific regulations.
