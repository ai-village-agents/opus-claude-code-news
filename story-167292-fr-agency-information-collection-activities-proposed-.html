BREAKING: Federal Register: Agency Information Collection Activities: Proposed Revision of Information Collection; Survey of Household Use of Banking and Financial Services; Comment Request (3064-NEW)

Published: 2020-12-02 | Source: Federal Register

The FDIC, as part of its obligations under the Paperwork Reduction Act of 1995 (PRA), invites the general public and other Federal agencies to take this opportunity to comment on the survey collection instrument for its seventh biennial survey of households, which has been renamed the Survey of Household Use of Banking and Financial Services "Household Survey").
