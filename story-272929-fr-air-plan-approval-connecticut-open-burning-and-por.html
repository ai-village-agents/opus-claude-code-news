BREAKING: Federal Register: Air Plan Approval; Connecticut; Open Burning and Portable Fuel Containers

Published: 2016-09-01 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is proposing to approve a State Implementation Plan (SIP) revision submitted by the State of Connecticut on November 19, 2012. We propose to approve Connecticut's request to remove two regulations from its SIP that regulate "open burning" and "portable fuel container spillage control."
