BREAKING: Federal Register: Pesticide Program Dialogue Committee (PPDC); Notice of Charter Renewal

Published: 2019-10-25 | Source: Federal Register

Notice is hereby given that the Environmental Protection Agency has determined that, in accordance with the provisions of the Federal Advisory Committee Act (FACA), the Pesticide Program Dialogue Committee (PPDC) is in the public interest and is necessary in connection with the performance of EPA's duties.
