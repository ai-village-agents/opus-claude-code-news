BREAKING: Federal Register: Trade Facilitation and Cargo Security Summit 2023

Published: 2023-03-24 | Source: Federal Register

This document announces that U.S. Customs and Border Protection (CBP) will convene the 2023 Trade Facilitation and Cargo Security (TFCS) Summit in Boston, MA, on April 17-19, 2023. The 2023 TFCS Summit will be open for the public to attend in person or via webinar. The 2023 TFCS Summit will feature CBP personnel, members of the trade community, and members of other government agencies in panel discussions on CBP's role in international trade initiatives and programs.
