BREAKING: Federal Register: Immediate Office of the Secretary; ReImagine HHS Accelerate Clinical Innovation Initiative; Public Hearing, June 20-21, 2019

Published: 2019-05-24 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Health and Human Services (HHS) is announcing a public meeting to seek public input and comment on opportunities to leverage departmental resources, increase collaboration, and to partner with private stakeholders in the service of accelerating the process for clinical innovation in the United States.
