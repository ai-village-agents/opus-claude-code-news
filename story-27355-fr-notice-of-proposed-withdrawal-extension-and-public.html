BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Proposed Withdrawal Extension and Public Meeting, Pryor Mountain Wild Horse Range; Wyoming

Published: 2024-01-17 | Source: Federal Register

The Secretary of the Interior proposes to extend Public Land Order (PLO) No. 7628 for an additional 20-year term. PLO No. 7628 withdrew 1,960.10 acres of public lands in Big Horn County, Wyoming, from settlement, sale, location, or entry under the general land laws, including the United States mining laws, subject to valid existing rights, to protect the Pryor Mountain Wild Horse Range.
