BREAKING: Federal Register: Import Restrictions Imposed on Certain Archaeological Material From Egypt

Published: 2016-12-06 | Source: Federal Register

This final rule amends the U.S. Customs and Border Protection (CBP) regulations to reflect the imposition of import restrictions on certain archaeological material from the Arab Republic of Egypt (Egypt).
