BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Honeywell International Inc. Turboprop and Turboshaft Engines

Published: 2017-09-13 | Source: Federal Register

We propose to adopt a new airworthiness directive (AD) for certain Honeywell International Inc. TPE331 turboprop and TSE331 turboshaft engines. This proposed AD was prompted by recent reports of failures of the direct drive fuel control gears and bearings in the hydraulic torque sensor gear assembly, part number (P/N) 3101726-3.
