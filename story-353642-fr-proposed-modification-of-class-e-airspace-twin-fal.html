BREAKING: Federal Register: Proposed Modification of Class E Airspace; Twin Falls, ID

Published: 2013-05-01 | Source: Federal Register

This action proposes to modify Class E airspace at Twin Falls Joslin Field-Magic Valley Regional Airport, Twin Falls, ID. Controlled airspace is necessary to accommodate aircraft using the Area Navigation (RNAV) Global Positioning System (GPS) and the Instrument Landing System (ILS) or Localizer (LOC) standard instrument approach procedures at the airport.
