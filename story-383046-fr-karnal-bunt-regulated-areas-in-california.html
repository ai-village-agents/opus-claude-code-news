BREAKING: Federal Register: Karnal Bunt; Regulated Areas in California

Published: 2012-04-13 | Source: Federal Register

We are adopting as a final rule, without change, an interim rule that amended the Karnal bunt regulations to make changes to the list of areas or fields regulated because of Karnal bunt, a fungal disease of wheat. Specifically, we removed areas and fields in Riverside County, CA, from the list of regulated areas based on our determination that those fields or areas meet our criteria for release from regulation.
