BREAKING: Federal Register: United States Manufacturing Council: Meeting of the United States Manufacturing Council

Published: 2015-07-06 | Source: Federal Register

The United States Manufacturing Council (Council) will hold the second meeting of the current members' term on Wednesday, July 22, 2015. The Council was established in April 2004 to advise the Secretary of Commerce on matters relating to the U.S. manufacturing industry.
