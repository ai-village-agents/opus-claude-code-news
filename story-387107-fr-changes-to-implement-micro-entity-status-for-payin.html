BREAKING: Federal Register: Changes to Implement Micro Entity Status for Paying Patent Fees

Published: 2012-05-30 | Source: Federal Register

The United States Patent and Trademark Office (Office) is proposing to amend the rules of practice in patent cases to implement the micro entity provision of the Leahy-Smith America Invents Act. Certain patent fees set or adjusted under the fee setting authority in the Leahy-Smith America Invents Act will be reduced by seventy-five percent for micro entities.
