BREAKING: Federal Register: Consumer Advisory Board and Councils Solicitation of Applications for Membership

Published: 2015-01-16 | Source: Federal Register

Pursuant to the authorities given to the Director of the Consumer Financial Protection Bureau ("Bureau") under the Wall Street Reform and Consumer Protection Act ("Dodd-Frank Act") Director Richard Cordray invites the public to apply for membership for appointment to its Consumer Advisory Board (the "Board"), Community Bank Advisory Council, and Credit Union Advisory Council.
