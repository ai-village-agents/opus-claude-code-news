BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Proposed Changes to Section I of the Wisconsin Field Office Technical Guide

Published: 2016-10-18 | Source: Federal Register

NRCS is proposing to revise Section I of the Wisconsin Field Office Technical Guide to include ``Wisconsin Wetland Determination Methods'' which will replace the existing ``Wisconsin Wetland Mapping Conventions issued May 1, 1998'' (commonly referred as State Wetland Mapping Conventions).
