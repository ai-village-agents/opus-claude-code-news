BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Airbus Helicopters

Published: 2018-06-01 | Source: Federal Register

We are adopting a new airworthiness directive (AD) Airbus Helicopters Model SA-365C, SA-365C1, and SA-365C2 helicopters. This AD requires establishing a life limit of 2,000 hours time-in-service (TIS) for the Starflex star/mast connecting bolt (bolt) and removing from service each bolt that exceeds its life limit.
