BREAKING: Federal Register: Differential Pricing Analysis; Request for Comments

Published: 2014-05-09 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Commerce (the Department) seeks public comment on its "differential pricing" analysis. This analysis is currently being applied in less-than-fair-value investigations and certain reviews, including administrative reviews to determine when it may be appropriate to use an alternative comparison method based on the average-to-transaction comparison method in making comparisons of export price or constructed export price and normal value.
