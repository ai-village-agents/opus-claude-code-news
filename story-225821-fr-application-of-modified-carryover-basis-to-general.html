BREAKING: Federal Register: Application of Modified Carryover Basis to General Basis Rules

Published: 2017-01-19 | Source: Federal Register

This document contains final regulations regarding the application of the modified carryover basis rules of section 1022 of the Internal Revenue Code (Code). Specifically, the final regulations modify provisions of the Treasury Regulations involving basis rules by including a reference to section 1022 where appropriate.
