BREAKING: Federal Register: Exemptions for Security-Based Swaps Issued by Certain Clearing Agencies

Published: 2012-04-05 | Source: Federal Register

We are adopting exemptions under the Securities Act of 1933, the Securities Exchange Act of 1934, and the Trust Indenture Act of 1939 for security-based swaps issued by certain clearing agencies satisfying certain conditions.
