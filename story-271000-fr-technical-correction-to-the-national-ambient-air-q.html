BREAKING: Federal Register: Technical Correction to the National Ambient Air Quality Standards for Particulate Matter

Published: 2016-08-11 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is taking direct final action to make a technical correction to an equation in an appendix in the National Ambient Air Quality Standards (NAAQS) for Particle Pollution. Equation 2 describes an intermediate step in the calculation of the design value for the annual PM2.5 (particles with an aerodynamic diameter less than or equal to a nominal 2.5 micrometers) NAAQS.
