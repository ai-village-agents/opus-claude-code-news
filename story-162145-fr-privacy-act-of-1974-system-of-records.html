BREAKING: Federal Register: Privacy Act of 1974; System of Records

Published: 2020-09-28 | Source: Federal Register

In accordance with the Privacy Act of 1974, as amended ("Privacy Act"), the U.S. Department of Labor ("Department") publishes this notice of a new system of records titled "Office of Inspector General Warehouse and Learning System (OWLS)," DOL/OIG-12.
