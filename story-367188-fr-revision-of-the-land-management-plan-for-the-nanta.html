BREAKING: Federal Register: Revision of the Land Management Plan for the Nantahala and Pisgah National Forests

Published: 2013-10-03 | Source: Federal Register

The Nantahala and Pisgah National Forests, located in North Carolina, are initiating the development of a land management plan revision (forest plan) for the Nantahala and Pisgah National Forests (NFs). A Draft Assessment has been posted to our Web site. We are inviting the public to help us develop a preliminary "need for change" and a proposed action for the land management plan revision.
