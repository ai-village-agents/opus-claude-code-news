BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Issuance of a Presidential Permit to TransCanada Keystone Pipeline, L.P.

Published: 2017-04-04 | Source: Federal Register

The Under Secretary of State for Political Affairs issued a Presidential permit to TransCanada Keystone Pipeline, L.P. ("Keystone") on March 23, 2017, authorizing Keystone to construct, connect, operate, and maintain pipeline facilities at the U.S.-Canada border in Phillips County, Montana for the importation of crude oil.
