BREAKING: Federal Register: Privacy Act of 1974; Implementation

Published: 2021-07-23 | Source: Federal Register

On July 14, 2021, the Office of Legal Policy (OLP), a component within the United States Department of Justice (DOJ or Department), published in the Federal Register a notice of a modified system of records for the OLP system of records, Judicial Nominations Files, JUSTICE/OLP-002. In this notice of proposed rulemaking, OLP proposes to modify the exemptions from certain provisions of the Privacy Act claimed for this system of records, as well as other administrative modifications.
