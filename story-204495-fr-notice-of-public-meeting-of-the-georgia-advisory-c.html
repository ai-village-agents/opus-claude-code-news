BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Public Meeting of the Georgia Advisory Committee

Published: 2018-04-13 | Source: Federal Register

Notice is hereby given, pursuant to the provisions of the rules and regulations of the U.S. Commission on Civil Rights (Commission) and the Federal Advisory Committee Act that the Georgia Advisory Committee will hold a meeting on Tuesday, May 1, 2018, for the purpose of reviewing and accepting the public hearing transcript.
