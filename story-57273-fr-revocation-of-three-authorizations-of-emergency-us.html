BREAKING: Federal Register: Revocation of Three Authorizations of Emergency Use of In Vitro Diagnostic Devices for Detection and/or Diagnosis of COVID-19; Availability

Published: 2023-01-03 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA) is announcing the revocation of the Emergency Use Authorizations (EUAs) (the Authorizations) issued to the University of Texas MD Anderson Cancer Center, Molecular Diagnostics Laboratory (MD Anderson) for the MD Anderson High-throughput SARS-CoV-2 RT-PCR Assay, and Visby Medical, Inc. for the Visby Medical COVID-19 and Visby Medical COVID-19 Point of Care Test.
