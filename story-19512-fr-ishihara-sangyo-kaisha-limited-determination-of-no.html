BREAKING: Federal Register: Ishihara Sangyo Kaisha, Limited: Determination of Nonregulated Status of ISK-311NR-4 Phalaenopsis (Moth Orchid) Genetically Engineered To Produce a Blue-Purple Flower Color

Published: 2025-09-04 | Source: Federal Register

We are advising the public of our determination that ISK- 311NR-4 phalaenopsis (moth orchid), which was developed using genetic engineering to produce a blue-purple flower color, is no longer considered regulated.
