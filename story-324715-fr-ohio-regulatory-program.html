BREAKING: Federal Register: Ohio Regulatory Program

Published: 2014-05-20 | Source: Federal Register

OSM announces receipt of a proposed amendment to the Ohio regulatory program under the Surface Mining Control and Reclamation Act of 1977 (SMCRA or the Act). Ohio's proposed amendment is prompted by requirements within the Ohio statute that all agencies must review their administrative rules every five years.
