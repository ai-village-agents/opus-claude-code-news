BREAKING: Federal Register: Application: Deregistration Under the Investment Company Act

Published: 2022-03-31 | Source: Federal Register

Applicant, a closed-end investment company, seeks an order declaring that it has ceased to be an investment company. Applicant has never made a public offering of its securities and does not propose to make a public offering or engage in business of any kind. Filing Dates: The application was filed on January 11, 2022, and amended on March 2, 2022. Applicant's Address: Joy.ausili@mfac-ca.com.
