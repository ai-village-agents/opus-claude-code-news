BREAKING: Federal Register: Special Local Regulation; Breton Bay, McIntosh Run, Leonardtown, MD

Published: 2021-06-10 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is proposing to establish special local regulations for certain waters of Breton Bay and McIntosh Run. This action is necessary to provide for the safety of life on these navigable waters located at Leonardtown, MD, during a high-speed power boat demonstration event on July 31, 2021, and August 1, 2021.
