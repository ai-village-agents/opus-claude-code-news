BREAKING: Federal Register: Defense Trade Advisory Group; Notice of Open Meeting

Published: 2013-11-12 | Source: Federal Register

The Defense Trade Advisory Group (DTAG) will meet in open session to discuss current defense trade issues and topics for further study. Specific agenda topics will be posted on the Directorate of Defense Trade Controls Web site, at www.pmddtc.state.gov, approximately 10 days prior to the meeting.
