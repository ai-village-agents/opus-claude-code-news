BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; The Boeing Company Airplanes

Published: 2022-06-29 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA proposes to supersede Airworthiness Directive (AD) 2020-24-04, which applies to all The Boeing Company Model 787-8, 787-9, and 787-10 airplanes. AD 2020-24-04 requires revising the existing airplane flight manual (AFM) to incorporate procedures for an approach with a localizer-based navigation aid, monitoring localizer raw data, calling out any significant deviations, and performing an immediate go around under certain conditions.
