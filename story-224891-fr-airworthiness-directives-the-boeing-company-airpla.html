BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; The Boeing Company Airplanes

Published: 2017-01-06 | Source: Federal Register

We propose to adopt a new airworthiness directive (AD) for all The Boeing Company Model 707 airplanes and Model 720 and 720B series airplanes. This proposed AD was prompted by a determination that undetected web fatigue cracking caused by oil canning may exist in the station 1440 aft pressure bulkhead web.
