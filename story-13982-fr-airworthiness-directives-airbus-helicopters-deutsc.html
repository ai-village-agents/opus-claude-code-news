BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Airbus Helicopters Deutschland GmbH

Published: 2025-06-18 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA proposes to adopt a new airworthiness directive (AD) for all Airbus Helicopters Deutschland GmbH Model MBB-BK 117 D-2 and MBB-BK 117 D-3 helicopters. This proposed AD was prompted by reports of airspeed and altitude indication errors.
