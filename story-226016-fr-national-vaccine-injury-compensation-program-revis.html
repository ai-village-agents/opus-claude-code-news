BREAKING: Federal Register: National Vaccine Injury Compensation Program: Revisions to the Vaccine Injury Table

Published: 2017-01-19 | Source: Federal Register

On July 29, 2015, the Secretary of Health and Human Services (the Secretary) published in the Federal Register a Notice of Proposed Rulemaking (NPRM) to amend the regulations governing the National Vaccine Injury Compensation Program (VICP or program) by proposing revisions to the Vaccine Injury Table (Table).
