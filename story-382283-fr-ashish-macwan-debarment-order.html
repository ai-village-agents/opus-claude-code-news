BREAKING: Federal Register: Ashish Macwan: Debarment Order

Published: 2012-04-05 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA) is issuing an order under the Federal Food, Drug, and Cosmetic Act (the FD&C Act) debarring Ashish Macwan for 5 years from providing services in any capacity to a person that has an approved or pending drug product application.
