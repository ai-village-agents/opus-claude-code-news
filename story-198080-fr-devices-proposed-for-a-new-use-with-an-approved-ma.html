BREAKING: Federal Register: Devices Proposed for a New Use With an Approved, Marketed Drug; Public Hearing; Reopening of the Comment Period

Published: 2018-01-22 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA, the Agency, or we) is reopening the comment period for the document published in the Federal Register on September 26, 2017, announcing a public hearing on a potential approach for device sponsors who seek to obtain marketing authorization for their products that are intended for a new use with an approved, marketed drug when the sponsor for the approved, marketed drug does not wish to pursue or collaborate on the new use.
