BREAKING: Federal Register: Independent Contractor Status Under the Fair Labor Standards Act (FLSA): Delay of Effective Date

Published: 2021-03-04 | Source: Federal Register

Consistent with the Presidential directive as expressed in the memorandum of January 20, 2021, from the Assistant to the President and Chief of Staff, titled "Regulatory Freeze Pending Review," this action finalizes the Department of Labor's proposal to delay until May 7, 2021, the effective date of the rule titled Independent Contractor Status Under the Fair Labor Standards Act ("Independent Contractor Rule" or "January 2021 Rule"), which was published in the Federal Register on January 7, 2021...
