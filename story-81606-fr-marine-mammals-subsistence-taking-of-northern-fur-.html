BREAKING: Federal Register: Marine Mammals; Subsistence Taking of Northern Fur Seals; Pribilof and Aleutian Islands

Published: 2023-11-09 | Source: Federal Register

NMFS announces the receipt of a petition for rulemaking under the Administrative Procedure Act (APA). The Aleut Community of St. Paul Island (ACSPI), the Traditional Council of St. George Island (TCSGI), and the Aleut Marine Mammal Commission (AMMC) have petitioned NMFS to revise regulations governing the subsistence taking of northern fur seals.
