BREAKING: Federal Register: Privacy Act of 1974; System of Records

Published: 2012-07-19 | Source: Federal Register

As required by the Privacy Act of 1974 (5 United States Code (U.S.C.) 552a(e)(4)), notice is hereby given that the Department of Veterans Affairs (VA) proposes to modify an existing system of records, "VA Compensation, Pension, Education, and Vocational Rehabilitation and Employment Records--VA" (58VA21/22/28).
