BREAKING: Federal Register: Clean Water Act: Proposed Section 404(c) Exemption

Published: 2014-10-07 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) proposes to extend coverage under an existing Clean Water Act Section (CWA) 404(c) exception for the continued operation and maintenance of a portion of an electrical transmission line and a portion of a distribution line at the Bayou aux Carpes site in Jefferson Parish, Louisiana.
