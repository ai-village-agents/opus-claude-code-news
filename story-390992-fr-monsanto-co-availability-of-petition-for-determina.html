BREAKING: Federal Register: Monsanto Co.; Availability of Petition for Determination of Nonregulated Status of Soybean Genetically Engineered for Herbicide Tolerance

Published: 2012-07-13 | Source: Federal Register

We are advising the public that the Animal and Plant Health Inspection Service (APHIS) has received a petition from the Monsanto Company (Monsanto) seeking a determination of nonregulated status of soybean designated as MON 87708, which has been genetically engineered for tolerance to the herbicide dicamba.
