BREAKING: Federal Register: Unified Agenda of Federal Regulatory and Deregulatory Actions

Published: 2022-08-08 | Source: Federal Register

This agenda announces the proposed regulatory actions that the Committee for Purchase From People Who Are Blind or Severely Disabled (Committee) plans for the next 12 months. It is issued in accordance with Executive Order 12866, "Regulatory Planning and Review", and E.O. 13563, "Improving Regulation and Regulatory Review".
