BREAKING: Federal Register: Meeting To Implement Pandemic Response Voluntary Agreement Under Section 708 of the Defense Production Act

Published: 2021-11-15 | Source: Federal Register

The Federal Emergency Management Agency (FEMA) will hold a meeting remotely via web conference to implement the Voluntary Agreement for the Manufacture and Distribution of Critical Healthcare Resources Necessary to Respond to a Pandemic. A portion of the meeting will be open to the public.
