BREAKING: Federal Register: Certain Carbon and Alloy Steel Products; Commission Determination To Reset the Time for the Beginning of the April 20, 2017, Oral Argument

Published: 2017-04-19 | Source: Federal Register

Notice is hereby given that the U.S. International Trade Commission has determined to reset the time for the beginning of the oral argument, see 82 FR 16417-8 (Apr. 4, 2017), to 10 a.m. on April 20, 2017.
