BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Technify Motors GmbH Reciprocating Engines

Published: 2015-07-08 | Source: Federal Register

We propose to adopt a new airworthiness directive (AD) for all Technify Motors GmbH TAE 125-02 reciprocating engines with a dual mass flywheel installed. This proposed AD was prompted by reports of a gearbox drive shaft breaking during starting or restarting of the engine. This proposed AD would require installation of a start phase monitoring system and associated specified software.
