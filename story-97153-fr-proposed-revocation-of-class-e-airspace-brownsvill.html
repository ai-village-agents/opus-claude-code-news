BREAKING: Federal Register: Proposed Revocation of Class E Airspace; Brownsville, PA

Published: 2022-05-31 | Source: Federal Register

This action proposes to remove Class E airspace in Brownsville, PA, as Brownsville General Hospital Heliport has been abandoned, and controlled airspace is no longer required. This action would enhance the safety and management of controlled airspace within the national airspace system.
