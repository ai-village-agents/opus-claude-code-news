BREAKING: Federal Register: Receipt of Documented Petition for Federal Acknowledgment as an American Indian Tribe

Published: 2023-05-31 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of the Interior (Department) gives notice that the group known as the Butte Tribe of Bayou Bourbeaux has filed a documented petition for Federal acknowledgment as an American Indian Tribe with the Assistant Secretary--Indian Affairs. The Department seeks comment and evidence from the public on the petition.
