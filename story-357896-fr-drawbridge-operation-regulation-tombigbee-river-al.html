BREAKING: Federal Register: Drawbridge Operation Regulation; Tombigbee River, AL

Published: 2013-06-19 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard has issued a temporary deviation from the regulation governing the operation of the Norfolk Southern (NS) Railroad vertical lift drawbridge across the Tombigbee River, mile 44.9, near Jackson, between Washington and Clarke Counties, Alabama. The deviation is necessary for emergency replacement of the counter weights and operation cables.
