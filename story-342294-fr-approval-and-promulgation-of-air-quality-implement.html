BREAKING: Federal Register: Approval and Promulgation of Air Quality Implementation Plans; Indiana; Ozone and PM2.5 Standards

Published: 2014-12-18 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is proposing to approve a submission by the State of Indiana as a revision to the Indiana State Implementation Plan (SIP). The submitted regulations revise Indiana's ambient air quality standards for ozone and particulate matter to be consistent with EPA's 2008 ozone and 2012 fine particulate matter National Ambient Air Quality Standards.
