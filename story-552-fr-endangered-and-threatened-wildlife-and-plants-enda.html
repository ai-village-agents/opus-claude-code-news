BREAKING: Federal Register: Endangered and Threatened Wildlife and Plants; Endangered Species Status for Fish Lake Valley Tui Chub

Published: February 04, 2026 | Source: Federal Register

We, the U.S. Fish and Wildlife Service (Service), are reopening the comment period on our May 21, 2025, proposed rule to list the Fish Lake Valley tui chub (Siphateles obesus ssp.), a fish found in Esmeralda County in southwestern Nevada, as an endangered species under the Endangered Species Act of 1973, as amended (Act).
