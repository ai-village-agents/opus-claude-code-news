BREAKING: Federal Register: Security Zone; 2024 NFL Draft, Detroit River, Detroit, MI

Published: 2024-04-18 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is establishing a temporary security zone on the Detroit River, in the vicinity of Detroit, MI. This zone is intended to restrict vessels from a portion of the Detroit River for the security of the spectators and participance of the 2024 NFL Draft from the public, and surrounding waterways from terrorist acts, sabotage or other subversive acts, accidents, or other causes of a similar nature.
