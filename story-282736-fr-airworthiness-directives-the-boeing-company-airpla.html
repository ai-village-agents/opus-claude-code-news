BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; The Boeing Company Airplanes

Published: 2016-12-27 | Source: Federal Register

We are adopting a new airworthiness directive (AD) for certain The Boeing Company Model 767-200 and -300 series airplanes. This AD was prompted by a report of a fire in the bilge area of the cargo compartment that burned through the insulation blankets that were intended to prevent smoke from migrating behind the cargo compartment sidewall liners and upward into the main cabin.
