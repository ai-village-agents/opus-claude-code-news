BREAKING: Federal Register: Payment for Home Health Services and Hospice Care to Non-VA Providers

Published: 2013-05-06 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Veterans Affairs (VA) amends its regulations concerning the billing methodology for non-VA providers of home health services and hospice care. Because the newly applicable methodology cannot supersede rates for which VA has specifically contracted, this rulemaking will only affect home health and hospice care providers who do not have existing negotiated contracts with VA.
