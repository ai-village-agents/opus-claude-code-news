BREAKING: Federal Register: Request for Written Comments Concerning China's Compliance With WTO Commitments

Published: 2022-08-29 | Source: Federal Register

The interagency Trade Policy Staff Committee (TPSC) invites interested persons to submit written comments to assist the Office of the United States Trade Representative (USTR) in the preparation of its annual report to the Congress on China's compliance with the commitments made in connection with its accession to the World Trade Organization (WTO).
