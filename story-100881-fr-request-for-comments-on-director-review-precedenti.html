BREAKING: Federal Register: Request for Comments on Director Review, Precedential Opinion Panel Review, and Internal Circulation and Review of Patent Trial and Appeal Board Decisions

Published: 2022-07-20 | Source: Federal Register

The United States Patent and Trademark Office (USPTO or Office) seeks public comments on practices and policies for the review of Patent Trial and Appeal Board (PTAB or Board) decisions. The USPTO has implemented a number of processes that promote the accuracy, consistency, and integrity of PTAB decision-making in Leahy-Smith America Invents Act of 2011 (AIA) proceedings.
