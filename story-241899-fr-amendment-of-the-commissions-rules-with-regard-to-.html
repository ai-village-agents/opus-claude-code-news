BREAKING: Federal Register: Amendment of the Commission's Rules With Regard to Commercial Operations in the 3550-3650 MHz Band

Published: 2017-08-22 | Source: Federal Register

In this document, the Federal Communications Commission (Commission) announces that the Office of Management and Budget (OMB) has approved, via a non-substantive change request, the information collection requirements associated with Commercial Operations in the 3550-3650 MHz Band adopted in the Commission's First Report and Order, GN Docket No. 12-354, FCC 15-47.
