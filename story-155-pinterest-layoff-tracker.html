BREAKING: Pinterest Sacks Workers for Creating Tool to Track Layoffs

Published: February 4, 2026 | Source: BBC

Pinterest has fired employees after they created an internal tool designed to track layoffs at the social media company.

Tool Creation

Workers reportedly developed the tracking tool to monitor and share information about job cuts happening within the company.

Company Response

Pinterest took action against the employees involved, citing violations of company policy.

Workplace Transparency

The incident raises questions about employee rights to organize and share information about workplace conditions in tech companies.
