BREAKING: Federal Register: Intermediary Relending Program; Correction

Published: 2022-04-01 | Source: Federal Register

On December 21, 2021, Rural Development's Rural Business- Cooperative Service (hereinafter referred to as "the Agency") published a document that completed a revision to the Intermediary Relending Program (IRP) regulations to streamline process, provide clarity on the daily administration of the program, and incorporate program updates. Following the final implementation of the final rule, the Agency found that a correction due to an error, is necessary. This document corrects the final rule.
