BREAKING: Federal Register: Walk-Behind Snow Throwers From China; Scheduling of the Final Phase of Countervailing Duty and Anti-Dumping Duty Investigations

Published: 2021-12-07 | Source: Federal Register

The Commission hereby gives notice of the scheduling of the final phase of antidumping and countervailing duty investigations Nos. 701-TA-666 and 731-TA-1558 (Final) pursuant to the Tariff Act of 1930 ("the Act") to determine whether an industry in the United States is materially injured or threatened with material injury, or the establishment of an industry in the United States is materially retarded, by reason of imports of walk-behind snow throwers from China, provided for in subheading 8430....
