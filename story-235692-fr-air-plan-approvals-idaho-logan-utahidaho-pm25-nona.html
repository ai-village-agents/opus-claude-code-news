BREAKING: Federal Register: Air Plan Approvals, Idaho: Logan Utah/Idaho PM2.5 Nonattainment Area

Published: 2017-06-01 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is proposing to approve revisions to Idaho's State Implementation Plan (SIP) submitted in 2012 and 2014 to address Clean Air Act (CAA) requirements for the Idaho portion of the Logan, Utah-Idaho fine particulate matter (PM2.5) nonattainment area (Logan UT-ID area).
