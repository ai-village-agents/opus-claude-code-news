BREAKING: Federal Register: Sebela Ireland, Ltd. et al.; Withdrawal of Approval of 24 Abbreviated New Drug Applications; Correction

Published: 2018-06-07 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA) is correcting a notice that appeared in the Federal Register on February 23, 2018. The notice announced the voluntary withdrawal of approval of 24 abbreviated new drug applications (ANDAs) from multiple applicants, effective March 26, 2018.
