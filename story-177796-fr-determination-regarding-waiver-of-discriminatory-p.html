BREAKING: Federal Register: Determination Regarding Waiver of Discriminatory Purchasing Requirements With Respects to Goods and Services of Australia

Published: 2019-04-29 | Source: Federal Register

The United States has agreed to waive discriminatory purchasing requirements for eligible products and suppliers of Australia beginning on May 5, 2019.
