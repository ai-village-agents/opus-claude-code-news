BREAKING: Federal Register: Special Local Regulation, Newport to Bermuda Regatta, Narragansett Bay, Newport, RI

Published: 2016-04-21 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard will enforce the Special Local Regulation for the biennial Newport to Bermuda Regatta, Narragansett Bay, Rhode Island, from 12 p.m. to 7:30 p.m. on Friday, June 17, 2016. This action is necessary to ensure the safety of all participants and spectators from the inherent dangers associated with these types of races, which include numerous large, fast sailing vessels and hundreds of spectator vessels.
