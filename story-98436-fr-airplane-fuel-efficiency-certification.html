BREAKING: Federal Register: Airplane Fuel Efficiency Certification

Published: 2022-06-15 | Source: Federal Register

This action proposes fuel efficiency requirements for certification of certain airplanes. These certification requirements would implement the emissions standards adopted by the Environmental Protection Agency, allowing manufacturers to certificate their aircraft for fuel efficiency in the United States, and fulfilling the statutory obligations of the FAA under the Clean Air Act.
