BREAKING: Federal Register: Exemptions From the Proxy Rules for Proxy Voting Advice

Published: 2020-09-03 | Source: Federal Register

The Securities and Exchange Commission ("Commission") is adopting amendments to its rules governing proxy solicitations so that investors who use proxy voting advice receive more transparent, accurate, and complete information on which to make their voting decisions, without imposing undue costs or delays that could adversely affect the timely provision of proxy voting advice.
