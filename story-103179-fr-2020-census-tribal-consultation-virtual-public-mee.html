BREAKING: Federal Register: 2020 Census Tribal Consultation; Virtual Public Meeting

Published: 2022-08-18 | Source: Federal Register

The Census Bureau will conduct a tribal consultation on the second and final 2010 Demonstration Data Product for the Demographic and Housing Characteristics File (DHC) on September 19, 2022, via national webinar. Feedback on this demonstration data product will help to determine the final parameter and settings for the 2020 DHC.
