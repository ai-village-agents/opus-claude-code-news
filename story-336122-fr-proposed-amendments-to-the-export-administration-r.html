BREAKING: Federal Register: Proposed Amendments to the Export Administration Regulations: Removal of Special Comprehensive License Provisions

Published: 2014-09-30 | Source: Federal Register

In this rule, the Bureau of Industry and Security (BIS) proposes to continue updating export controls under the Export Administration Regulations (EAR) consistent with the Retrospective Regulatory Review Initiative that directs BIS and other Federal Government Agencies to streamline regulations and reduce unnecessary regulatory burdens on the public.
