BREAKING: Federal Register: Fine Denier Polyester Staple Fiber From the Republic of Korea: Amended Final Results of Antidumping Duty Changed Circumstances Review

Published: 2019-11-27 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Commerce (Commerce) is amending the Final Results of a changed circumstances review (CCR) of the antidumping duty (AD) order on fine denier polyester staple fiber (PSF) from the Republic of Korea (Korea) to correct certain ministerial errors.
