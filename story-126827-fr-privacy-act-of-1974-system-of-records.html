BREAKING: Federal Register: Privacy Act of 1974; System of Records

Published: 2021-06-25 | Source: Federal Register

ExecVA was a repository for Veterans' calls. The details of the calls are recorded and assigned to field personnel within the system. Users throughout VA can collaborate on responses to Veterans and other stakeholders, which results in improved accuracy, consistency, and timeliness of responses through shared data and knowledge management. ExecVA was developed on the COTS product Footprints.
