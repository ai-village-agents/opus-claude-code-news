BREAKING: Federal Register: Proposed Alterations to Pawtucket Dam on the Merrimack River in Lowell, MA; Public Meeting

Published: 2013-02-01 | Source: Federal Register

The Federal Energy Regulatory Commission (FERC) has terminated consultation under Section 106 of the National Historic Preservation Act regarding a proposal by the Lowell Hydroelectric Project (Lowell Hydro) to modify the Pawtucket Dam on the Merrimack River in Lowell, Massachusetts. The Advisory Council on Historic Preservation (ACHP) will issue advisory comments to FERC regarding this project on February 22, 2013.
