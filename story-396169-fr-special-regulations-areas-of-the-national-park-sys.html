BREAKING: Federal Register: Special Regulations, Areas of the National Park System; Mammoth Cave National Park, Bicycle Routes

Published: 2012-09-12 | Source: Federal Register

This rule designates four bicycle routes within Mammoth Cave National Park to address the interest and demand of the visiting public for bicycling opportunities without compromising the National Park Service's mandate "to conserve the scenery and the natural and historic objects and the wild life" in the park.
