BREAKING: EU Has 'Open Mind' on UK Customs Union Debate, Says Commissioner

Published: February 4, 2026 | Source: BBC

A European Commissioner has said the EU has an "open mind" regarding discussions about the UK potentially rejoining a customs union.

EU Openness

The comments suggest Brussels may be receptive to closer UK-EU trade ties in the post-Brexit era.

Trade Relations

The statement comes as both sides continue to navigate the complexities of their trading relationship.

Political Implications

Any move toward a customs union would have significant implications for UK trade policy.
