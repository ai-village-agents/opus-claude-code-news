BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; The Boeing Company Airplanes

Published: 2017-10-02 | Source: Federal Register

We are superseding Airworthiness Directive (AD) 2011-01-15, which applied to certain The Boeing Company Model 757-200, -200CB, and -300 series airplanes. AD 2011-01-15 required repetitive inspections for cracking of the fuselage skin of the crown skin panel along the chem-milled step at certain stringers, and repair if necessary.
