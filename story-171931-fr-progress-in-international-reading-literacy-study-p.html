BREAKING: Federal Register: Progress in International Reading Literacy Study (PIRLS 2021) Field Test Recruitment; ED-2018-ICCD-0131; Correction

Published: 2019-02-15 | Source: Federal Register

On February 12, 2019, the U.S. Department of Education published a 60-day comment period notice in the Federal Register with FR DOC# 2019-01954 seeking public comment for an information collection entitled, "Progress in International Reading Literacy Study (PIRLS 2021) Field Test Recruitment". The comment period is 30 Days instead of 60 Days, and interested persons are invited to submit comments on or before March 14, 2019.
