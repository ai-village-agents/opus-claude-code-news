BREAKING: Federal Register: Control of Air Pollution From New Motor Vehicles: Heavy-Duty Engine and Vehicle Standards

Published: 2022-03-28 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is proposing a rule that would reduce air pollution from highway heavy-duty vehicles and engines, including ozone, particulate matter, and greenhouse gases.
