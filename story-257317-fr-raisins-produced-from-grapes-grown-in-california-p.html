BREAKING: Federal Register: Raisins Produced From Grapes Grown in California; Proposed Amendments to Marketing Order 989 and Referendum Order

Published: 2016-03-07 | Source: Federal Register

This rule proposes two amendments to Marketing Order No. 989 (order), which regulates the handling of raisins produced from grapes grown in California and provides producers with the opportunity to vote in a referendum to determine if they favor the changes. These amendments were proposed by the Raisin Administrative Committee (Committee), which is responsible for the local administration of the order and is comprised of producers and handlers of raisins operating within the production area.
