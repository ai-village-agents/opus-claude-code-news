BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Bombardier, Inc., Airplanes

Published: 2018-05-08 | Source: Federal Register

We propose to adopt a new airworthiness directive (AD) for certain Bombardier, Inc., Model BD-700-1A10 and BD-700-1A11 airplanes. This proposed AD was prompted by a report of cracking at the fastener holes of the left-hand-side support bracket of the elevator bell crank for the control linkage in the vertical stabilizer.
