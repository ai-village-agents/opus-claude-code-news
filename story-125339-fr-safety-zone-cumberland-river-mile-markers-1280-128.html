BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone; Cumberland River, Mile Markers 128.0-128.3, Clarksville, TN

Published: 2021-06-07 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is proposing to establish a temporary safety zone for navigable waters on the Cumberland River from mile 128.0 to mile 128.3. The safety zone is needed to protect life and the marine environment from potential hazards created by the Clarksville Independence Day fireworks display. Entry of vessels or persons into this zone is prohibited unless specifically authorized by the Captain of the Port Sector Ohio Valley or a designated representative.
