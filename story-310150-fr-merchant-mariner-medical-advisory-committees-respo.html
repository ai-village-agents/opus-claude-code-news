BREAKING: Federal Register: Merchant Mariner Medical Advisory Committee's Response to Task Statement 1, Navigation and Vessel Inspection Circular 04-08 Revision

Published: 2015-11-24 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard announces the availability of the Merchant Mariner Medical Advisory Committee's response to Task Statement 1, "Navigation and Vessel Inspection Circular 04-08 Revision Working Group." This document recommends various changes to NVIC 04-08, "Medical and Physical Evaluation Guidelines for Merchant Mariner Credentials," which the Coast Guard uses when making decisions on mariner credentialing.
