BREAKING: Federal Register: Repeal of Federal Housing Finance Board Regulations; Technical Amendments to FHFA Regulations

Published: 2018-08-09 | Source: Federal Register

The Federal Housing Finance Agency (FHFA) is repealing two parts of the Federal Housing Finance Board (Finance Board) regulations, which define terms used in Finance Board regulations, and which describe the process by which the Finance Board conducted its monthly interest rate survey (MIRS). The repealed definitions are either obsolete or duplicate definitions that FHFA has previously adopted.
