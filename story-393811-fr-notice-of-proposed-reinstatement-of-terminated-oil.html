BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Proposed Reinstatement of Terminated Oil and Gas Lease NMNM 108040, New Mexico

Published: 2012-08-15 | Source: Federal Register

Under the Class II provisions of Title IV of the Federal Oil and Gas Royalty Management Act of 1982, the Bureau of Land Management received a petition for reinstatement of oil and gas lease NMNM 108040 from the lessees ABO Petro Corp., MYCO Industries, Inc., and OXY Y-1 Co., for lands in Chaves County, New Mexico. The petition was filed on time and was accompanied by all the rentals due since the date the lease terminated under the law.
