BREAKING: Federal Register: Noninsured Crop Disaster Assistance Program

Published: 2020-03-02 | Source: Federal Register

This rule implements changes to the Noninsured Crop Disaster Assistance Program (NAP) as required by the Agriculture Improvement Act of 2018 (the 2018 Farm Bill). The rule makes buy-up coverage levels available for 2019 and future years, increases service fees, and extends the service fee waiver and premium reduction to eligible veterans.
