BREAKING: Federal Register: Exemption for Certain Exchange Members

Published: 2023-09-07 | Source: Federal Register

The Securities and Exchange Commission ("Commission") is adopting amendments to a rule under the Securities Exchange Act of 1934 ("Act" or "Exchange Act") that exempts certain Commission- registered brokers or dealers from membership in a registered national securities association ("Association").
