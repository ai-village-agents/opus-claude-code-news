BREAKING: Federal Register: National Organic Program; Amendments to the National List of Allowed and Prohibited Substances (Crops, Livestock and Processing)

Published: 2012-08-02 | Source: Federal Register

This final rule amends the U.S. Department of Agriculture's (USDA's) National List of Allowed and Prohibited Substances (National List) to enact recommendations submitted to the Secretary of Agriculture (Secretary) by the National Organic Standards Board (NOSB) on October 28, 2010, and April 29, 2011.
