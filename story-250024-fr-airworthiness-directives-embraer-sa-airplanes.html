BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Embraer S.A. Airplanes

Published: 2017-12-04 | Source: Federal Register

We propose to adopt a new airworthiness directive (AD) for Embraer S.A. Models EMB-500 and EMB-505 airplanes. This proposed AD results from mandatory continuing airworthiness information (MCAI) originated by an aviation authority of another country to identify and correct an unsafe condition on an aviation product.
