BREAKING: Federal Register: Request for Information on Black Veterans and Good Jobs

Published: 2024-06-21 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Labor (DOL or the Department) is seeking information from the public regarding evidence on Black veterans' experience transitioning from military to civilian employment and veterans' access to good jobs and meaningful careers.
