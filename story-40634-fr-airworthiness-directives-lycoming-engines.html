BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Lycoming Engines

Published: 2024-06-28 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA proposes to adopt a new airworthiness directive (AD) for Lycoming Engines (Lycoming) model engines that have a certain connecting rod assemblies installed. This proposed AD was prompted by several reports of connecting rod failures, which resulted in uncontained engine failure and in-flight shutdowns (IFSDs).
