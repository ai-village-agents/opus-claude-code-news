BREAKING: Federal Register: Receipt of Incidental Take Permit Application and Proposed Habitat Conservation Plan for the Audubon's Crested Caracara; Glades County, FL; Categorical Exclusion

Published: 2022-07-05 | Source: Federal Register

We, the Fish and Wildlife Service (Service), announce receipt of an application from Stewart Materials, LLC (applicant) for an incidental take permit (ITP) under the Endangered Species Act. The applicant requests the ITP to take the federally listed Audubon's crested caracara incidental to the construction and operation of a sand mine in Glades County, Florida.
