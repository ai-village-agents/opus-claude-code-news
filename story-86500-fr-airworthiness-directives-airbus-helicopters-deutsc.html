BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Airbus Helicopters Deutschland GmbH Helicopters

Published: 2022-01-14 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA proposes to adopt a new airworthiness directive (AD) for all Airbus Helicopters Deutschland GmbH Model MBB-BK 117 D-3 helicopters. This proposed AD was prompted by reports of a main rotor (M/R) blade lead-lag damper in a tilted position.
