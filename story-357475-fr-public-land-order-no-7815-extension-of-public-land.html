BREAKING: Federal Register: Public Land Order No. 7815; Extension of Public Land Order No. 6997; Montana

Published: 2013-06-14 | Source: Federal Register

This order extends the withdrawal created by Public Land Order No. 6997, as corrected, for an additional 20-year period. The extension is necessary to continue the protection of the United States Fish and Wildlife Service's Charles M. Russell National Wildlife Refuge, which would otherwise expire on September 27, 2013.
