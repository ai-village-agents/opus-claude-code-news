BREAKING: Federal Register: Minor Revisions to AP-42 Section 13.5: Industrial Flares

Published: 2017-01-23 | Source: Federal Register

On December 14, 2016, the Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) issued minor revisions to AP-42 Section 13.5: Industrial Flares. AP-42 is the primary compilation of the EPA's emissions factor information. The EPA finalized these revisions in compliance with a settlement agreement entered into with Air Alliance Houston, Community In-Power and Development Association, Inc., Louisiana Bucket Brigade, and Texas Environmental Justice Advocacy Services ("Plaintiffs").
