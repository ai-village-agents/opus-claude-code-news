BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Intent To Repatriate Cultural Items: St. Joseph Museums, Inc., St. Joseph, MO

Published: 2023-08-31 | Source: Federal Register

In accordance with the Native American Graves Protection and Repatriation Act (NAGPRA), the St. Joseph Museums, Inc., intends to repatriate a certain cultural item that meets the definition of an unassociated funerary object and certain cultural items that meet the definition objects of cultural patrimony, and that have a cultural affiliation with the Indian Tribes or Native Hawaiian organizations in this notice.
