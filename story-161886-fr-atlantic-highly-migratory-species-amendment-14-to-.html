BREAKING: Federal Register: Atlantic Highly Migratory Species; Amendment 14 to the 2006 Consolidated Atlantic Highly Migratory Species Fishery Management Plan

Published: 2020-09-24 | Source: Federal Register

NMFS announces the availability of Draft Amendment 14 to the 2006 Consolidated Atlantic Highly Migratory Species (HMS) Fishery Management Plan (FMP). Draft Amendment 14 is being undertaken to revise the mechanism or "framework" used in establishing quotas and related management measures for Atlantic shark fisheries.
