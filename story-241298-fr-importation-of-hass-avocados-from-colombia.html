BREAKING: Federal Register: Importation of Hass Avocados From Colombia

Published: 2017-08-15 | Source: Federal Register

We are amending the regulations to allow the importation of fresh Hass avocado fruit from Colombia into the continental United States. As a condition of entry, fresh Hass avocado fruit from Colombia will have to be produced in accordance with a systems approach that includes orchard and packinghouse requirements and port of entry inspection.
