BREAKING: Federal Register: Public Comment on the Cumulative Risk Assessment Guidelines for Planning and Problem Formulation

Published: 2023-06-16 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is announcing a 60-day public comment period on the draft Cumulative Risk Assessment (CRA) Guidelines for Planning and Problem Formulation. The CRA Guidelines for Planning and Problem Formulation describe steps for the planning and problem formulation of CRAs and offer guidelines for when cumulative risk assessments could be appropriate.
