BREAKING: Federal Register: Zhang Xiao Dong: Final Debarment Order

Published: 2020-03-17 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA) is issuing an order under the Federal Food, Drug, and Cosmetic Act (FD&C Act) debarring Zhang Xiao Dong for a period of 5 years from importing articles of food (including dietary supplements) or offering such articles for importation into the United States.
