BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Aviat Aircraft Inc.

Published: 2020-08-17 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA is adopting a new airworthiness directive (AD) for all Aviat Aircraft Inc. Models A-1, A-1A, A-1B, A-1C-180, and A-1C-200 airplanes. This AD requires repetitive inspections of the forward horizontal stabilizer support assembly and the rear horizontal stabilizer support tube and reporting information to the FAA.
