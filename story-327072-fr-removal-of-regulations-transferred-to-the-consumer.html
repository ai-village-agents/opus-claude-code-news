BREAKING: Federal Register: Removal of Regulations Transferred to the Consumer Financial Protection Bureau

Published: 2014-06-16 | Source: Federal Register

Through this rule, HUD removes its regulations previously authorized under the Real Estate Settlement Procedures Act of 1974 (RESPA), the Secure and Fair Enforcement for Mortgage Licensing Act of 2008 (SAFE Act), and the Interstate Land Sales Full Disclosure Act (ILSFDA).
