BREAKING: Federal Register: Proposed Modification of Class C Airspace at Luis Munoz Marin International Airport, San Juan, PR; Informal Airspace Meeting

Published: 2022-12-12 | Source: Federal Register

This notice announces a fact-finding informal airspace meeting regarding a plan to modify the Class C Airspace at Luis Munoz Marin International Airport (TJSJ), San Juan, PR. The meeting will be a virtual format via the Zoom platform. The purpose of the meeting is to solicit aeronautical comments on the proposal's effects on local aviation operations.
