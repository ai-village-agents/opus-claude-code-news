BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Gulfstream Aerospace LP Airplanes

Published: 2021-09-09 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA is adopting a new airworthiness directive (AD) for certain Gulfstream Aerospace LP Model Gulfstream G280 airplanes. This AD was prompted by a report that during full-scale fatigue testing, a crack was found in the area of the attachment of the wing rib 0 to the front spar.
