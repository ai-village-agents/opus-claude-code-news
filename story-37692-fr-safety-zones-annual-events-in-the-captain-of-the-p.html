BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zones; Annual Events in the Captain of the Port Eastern Great Lakes Zone Marine Events Within the Ninth District Coast Guard District

Published: 2024-05-23 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard will enforce a Safety Zone for the Clayton Chamber of Commerce 4th of July Fireworks on July 3, 2024, to provide for the safety of life on navigable waterways during this event. Fireworks will be fired from a barge at 44[deg]14'54" N by 76[deg]05'26" W the Saint Lawernce River. Our regulation for marine events within the Ninth Coast Guard District identifies the regulated area for this event in Clayton, NY.
