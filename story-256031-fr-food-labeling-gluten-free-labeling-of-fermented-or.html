BREAKING: Federal Register: Food Labeling; Gluten-Free Labeling of Fermented or Hydrolyzed Foods; Reopening of the Comment Period

Published: 2016-02-23 | Source: Federal Register

In the Federal Register of November 18, 2015, the Food and Drug Administration (FDA) published a proposed rule entitled "Food Labeling; Gluten-Free Labeling of Fermented or Hydrolyzed Foods." The proposed rule would establish requirements concerning "gluten-free" labeling for foods that are fermented or hydrolyzed or that contain fermented or hydrolyzed ingredients.
