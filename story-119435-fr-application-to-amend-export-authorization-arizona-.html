BREAKING: Federal Register: Application To Amend Export Authorization; Arizona Public Service Company

Published: 2021-03-22 | Source: Federal Register

Arizona Public Service Company (APS or Applicant) filed a letter informing the Department of Energy (DOE or Department) of a change in the entity identified as the specific recipient of emergency power exports in Export Authorization Order No. EA-108. As a result of the change in counterparty, EA-108 must be amended to reflect that change.
