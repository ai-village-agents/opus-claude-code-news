BREAKING: Federal Register: Recordkeeping Requirements for Qualified Financial Contracts

Published: 2016-12-28 | Source: Federal Register

The FDIC proposes to amend its regulations regarding Recordkeeping Requirements for Qualified Financial Contracts ("Part 371"), which require insured depository institutions ("IDIs") in a troubled condition to keep records relating to qualified financial contracts ("QFCs") to which they are party.
