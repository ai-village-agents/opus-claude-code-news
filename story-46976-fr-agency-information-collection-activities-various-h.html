BREAKING: Federal Register: Agency Information Collection Activities: Various Homeland Security Acquisitions Regulations Forms OMB Control No. 1600-0002

Published: 2024-09-13 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Homeland Security, DHS will submit the following information collection request (ICR) to the Office of Management and Budget (OMB) for review and clearance in accordance with the Paperwork Reduction Act of 1995. DHS previously published this information collection request (ICR) in the Federal Register on June 13, 2024, for a 60-day public comment period. One comment was received by DHS. The purpose of this notice is to allow additional 30-days for public comments.
