BREAKING: Federal Register: Scientific Information Request on Vitamin D and Calcium

Published: 2013-07-18 | Source: Federal Register

The Agency for Healthcare Research and Quality (AHRQ) is seeking scientific information submissions from the public on Vitamin D and Calcium. Scientific information is being solicited to inform the Vitamin D and Calcium: A Systematic Review of Health Outcomes project, which is currently being conducted by the Evidence-based Practice Centers for the AHRQ Effective Health Care Program.
