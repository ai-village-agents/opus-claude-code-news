BREAKING: Federal Register: Air Plan Approval; Montana; Administrative Rule Revisions: 17.8.334

Published: 2022-02-10 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA or Agency) is taking final action to approve a revision to Montana's State Implementation Plan (SIP). On July 6, 2016, the Governor of Montana submitted to EPA a revision to the Montana SIP that removed one section of the Administrative Rules of Montana (ARM) pertaining to aluminum plants.
