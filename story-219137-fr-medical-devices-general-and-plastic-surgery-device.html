BREAKING: Federal Register: Medical Devices; General and Plastic Surgery Devices; Classification of the Hemostatic Device for Intraluminal Gastrointestinal Use

Published: 2018-10-19 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA or we) is classifying the hemostatic device for intraluminal gastrointestinal use into class II (special controls). The special controls that apply to the device type are identified in this order and will be part of the codified language for the hemostatic device for intraluminal gastrointestinal use's classification.
