BREAKING: Federal Register: Parts and Accessories Necessary for Safe Operation; Retroreflective Sheeting on Semitrailers and Trailers

Published: 2025-05-30 | Source: Federal Register

FMCSA proposes to rescind the requirements for retroreflective sheeting on semitrailers and trailers manufactured prior to December 1, 1993, which is the compliance date for the National Highway Traffic Safety Administration's (NHTSA) conspicuity rules applicable to trailer manufacturers.
