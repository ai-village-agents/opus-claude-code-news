BREAKING: Federal Register: Rural Opportunities To Use Transportation for Economic Success: Request for Information

Published: 2019-11-27 | Source: Federal Register

On October 28, 2019, the Secretary of Transportation issued an order launching the Rural Opportunities to Use Transportation for Economic Success (ROUTES) initiative. ROUTES will help the Department of Transportation (DOT) address disparities in rural transportation infrastructure to tangibly improve safety and economic competitiveness in all parts of the country.
