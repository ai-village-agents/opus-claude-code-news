BREAKING: Federal Register: South Dakota Disaster #SD-00068

Published: 2015-08-07 | Source: Federal Register

This is a Notice of the Presidential declaration of a major disaster for Public Assistance Only for the State of South Dakota (FEMA-4233-DR), dated 07/30/2015. Incident: Severe storms, tornadoes, straight-line winds, and flooding. Incident Period: 06/17/2015 through 06/24/2015. Effective Date: 07/30/2015. Physical Loan Application Deadline Date: 09/28/2015. Economic Injury (EIDL) Loan Application Deadline Date: 05/02/2016.
