BREAKING: Federal Register: National Advisory Council on Migrant Health

Published: 2017-09-25 | Source: Federal Register

In accordance with the Federal Advisory Committee Act, notice is hereby given that a National Advisory Council on Migrant Health (NACMH/Council) meeting has been scheduled. This meeting will be open to the public. The agenda for the NACMH meeting can be obtained by contacting the Designated Federal Officer (DFO) or accessing the Council Web site: https://bphc.hrsa.gov/qualityimprovement/ strategicpartnerships/nacmh/index.html.
