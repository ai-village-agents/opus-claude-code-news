BREAKING: Federal Register: Privacy Act of 1974; System of Records

Published: 2015-04-24 | Source: Federal Register

The Privacy Act of 1974 (5 U.S.C. 552(e) (4)) requires that all agencies publish in the Federal Register a notice of the existence and character of their systems of records. Notice is hereby given that the Department of Veterans Affairs (VA) is amending the system of records currently entitled "Suicide Prevention Database-VA" (158VA11).
