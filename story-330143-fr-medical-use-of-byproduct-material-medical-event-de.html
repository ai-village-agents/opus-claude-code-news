BREAKING: Federal Register: Medical Use of Byproduct Material-Medical Event Definitions, Training and Experience, and Clarifying Amendments

Published: 2014-07-21 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Nuclear Regulatory Commission (NRC) is proposing to amend its regulations related to the medical use of byproduct material. In this action the NRC addresses three ongoing rulemaking projects and several other related topics. First, this rule proposes amendments to the reporting and notification requirements for a medical event for permanent implant brachytherapy.
