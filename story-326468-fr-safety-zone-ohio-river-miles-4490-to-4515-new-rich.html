BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone, Ohio River, Miles 449.0 to 451.5; New Richmond, OH

Published: 2014-06-09 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard proposes to establish a temporary safety zone for all waters of the Ohio River, surface to bottom, extending from Ohio River mile 449.0 to mile 451.5 at New Richmond, Ohio. This temporary safety zone is necessary to protect persons and property from potential damage and safety hazards during the New Richmond Bicentennial Big Bash, which involves tossing pumpkins into the river with trebuchets, which are catapults operating under a raised counterweight system.
