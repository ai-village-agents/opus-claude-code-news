BREAKING: Federal Register: Special Local Regulations; Recurring Marine Events in the Seventh Coast Guard District

Published: 2013-11-08 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard will enforce the special local regulations pertaining to the Key West World Championship in the Atlantic Ocean, off Key West, FL from 9:30 a.m. until 4:30 p.m. on November 10, 2013. This action is necessary to protect race participants, participant vessels, spectators, and the general public from the hazards associated with high-speed boat races.
