BREAKING: Federal Register: New Animal Drug Applications; Beta-Aminopropionitrile Fumarate; n-Butyl Chloride; Cupric Glycinate Injection; Dichlorophene and Toluene; Orgotein for Injection; Tetracycline Tablets

Published: 2021-02-23 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA) is amending the animal drug regulations to reflect the withdrawal of approval of seven new animal drug applications (NADAs) for lack of compliance with the reporting requirements in an FDA regulation.
