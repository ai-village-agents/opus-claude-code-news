BREAKING: Federal Register: OMB Sequestration Update Report to the President and Congress for Fiscal Year 2013

Published: 2012-08-27 | Source: Federal Register

Public Law 112-25, the Budget Control Act of 2011 (BCA, "the Act") amended the Balanced Budget and Emergency Deficit Control Act of 1985 (BBEDCA) by reinstating the discretionary spending limits that had expired after 2002. Section 254 of the BBEDCA requires the Office of Management and Budget (OMB) to issue a Sequestration Update Report on August 20th of each year on the overall status of discretionary legislation.
