BREAKING: Federal Register: Collaborative Workshop on Aquatic Models and 21st Century Toxicology; Notice of Public Meeting and Registration Information

Published: 2014-04-11 | Source: Federal Register

The National Toxicology Program (NTP) Interagency Center for the Evaluation of Alternative Toxicological Methods (NICEATM) announces a "Collaborative Workshop on Aquatic Models and 21st Century Toxicology." The workshop proposes to explore and discuss how small aquarium fish species may be used as model organisms to screen and prioritize compounds for further in vivo testing and assess mechanisms of chemical toxicity.
