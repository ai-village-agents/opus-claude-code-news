BREAKING: Federal Register: Address and Agency Name Changes for Region 4 State and Local Agencies; Technical Correction

Published: 2017-07-17 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is correcting the addresses and agencies names for EPA Region 4 State and local agencies in EPA regulations. The jurisdiction of EPA Region 4 includes the States of Alabama, Florida, Georgia, Kentucky, Mississippi, North Carolina, South Carolina and Tennessee. Certain EPA air pollution control regulations require submittal of notifications, reports and other documents to the appropriate authorized State or local agency.
