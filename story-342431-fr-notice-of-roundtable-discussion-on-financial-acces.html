BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Roundtable Discussion on Financial Access for Money Services Businesses (MSBs)

Published: 2014-12-22 | Source: Federal Register

Treasury is announcing a January 13, 2015 roundtable discussion on financial access for money services businesses (MSBs). Treasury is hosting the roundtable to share the U.S. Government perspective on issues pertaining to financial access for MSBs and to hear from industry.
