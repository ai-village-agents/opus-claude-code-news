BREAKING: Federal Register: Passenger Vessels Accessibility Guidelines

Published: 2013-06-25 | Source: Federal Register

We, the Architectural and Transportation Barriers Compliance Board (Access Board), are proposing accessibility guidelines for the construction and alteration of passenger vessels covered by the Americans with Disabilities Act (ADA) to ensure that the vessels are readily accessible to and usable by passengers with disabilities.
