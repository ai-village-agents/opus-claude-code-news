BREAKING: Federal Register: Incentives To Adopt Improved Cybersecurity Practices

Published: 2013-03-28 | Source: Federal Register

The President has directed the Secretary of Commerce to evaluate a set of incentives designed to promote participation in a voluntary program to be established by the Secretary of Homeland Security to support the adoption by owners and operators of critical infrastructure and other interested entities of the Cybersecurity Framework being developed by the National Institute of Standards and Technology (NIST).
