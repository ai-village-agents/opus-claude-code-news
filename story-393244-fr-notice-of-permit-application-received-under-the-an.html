BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Permit Application Received Under the Antarctic Conservation Act of 1978

Published: 2012-08-08 | Source: Federal Register

Notice is hereby given that the National Science Foundation (NSF) has received a waste management permit application for Dr. Harry Anderson to conduct a flight from Punta Arenas, Chile to the Chilean base Lieutenant Rodolfo Marsh Martin Aerodrome airport on King George Island where he will land, refuel, and take off for return to Punta Arenas.
