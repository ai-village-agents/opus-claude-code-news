BREAKING: Federal Register: Certain Partnership Related-Party Basis Adjustment Transactions as Transactions of Interest

Published: 2024-06-18 | Source: Federal Register

This document contains proposed regulations that would identify certain partnership related-party basis adjustment transactions and substantially similar transactions as transactions of interest, a type of reportable transaction. Material advisors and certain participants in these transactions would be required to file disclosures with the IRS and would be subject to penalties for failure to disclose.
