BREAKING: Federal Register: Virginia Electric and Power Company; North Anna Power Station; Independent Spent Fuel Storage Installation

Published: 2014-07-22 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Nuclear Regulatory Commission (NRC) has received, by letter dated June 9, 2014, an application from Virginia Electric and Power Company (Dominion) requesting an amendment, in the form of changes to the Technical Specifications (TS) to Materials License Number SNM-2507 for the North Anna Power Station (NAPS) Independent Spent Fuel Storage Installation (ISFSI).
