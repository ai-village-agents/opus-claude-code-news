BREAKING: Federal Register: Data Practices, Computer III Further Remand: BOC Provision of Enhanced Services

Published: 2013-07-02 | Source: Federal Register

The Report and Order eliminates comparably efficient interconnection (CEI) and open network architecture (ONA) narrowband reporting requirements applicable to the Bell Operating Companies (BOCs). These requirements have been in place to monitor the BOCs' compliance with access and interconnection services that they must offer to competitive enhanced service providers (ESPs).
