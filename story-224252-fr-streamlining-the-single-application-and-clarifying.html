BREAKING: Federal Register: Streamlining the Single Application and Clarifying Eligibility Requirements

Published: 2018-12-27 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Copyright Office is amending its regulations to update the eligibility requirements for its application forms to reflect recent technical updates. The final rule clarifies that the Single Application may be used to register one work that is created and solely owned by one author and is not a work made for hire.
