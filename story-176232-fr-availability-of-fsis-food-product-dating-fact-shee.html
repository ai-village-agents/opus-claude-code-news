BREAKING: Federal Register: Availability of FSIS Food Product Dating Fact Sheet

Published: 2019-04-09 | Source: Federal Register

FSIS is announcing the availability of an updated fact sheet on food product dating. The fact sheet is aimed at reducing food waste through encouraging food manufacturers and retailers that apply product dating to use a "Best if Used By" date label. FSIS recommends the use of a "Best if Used By" date label because research shows that this phrase is easily understood by consumers as an indicator of quality rather than safety.
