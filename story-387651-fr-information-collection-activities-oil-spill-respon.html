BREAKING: Federal Register: Information Collection Activities: Oil-Spill Response Requirements for Facilities Located Seaward of the Coast Line; Proposed Collection; Comment Request

Published: 2012-06-06 | Source: Federal Register

To comply with the Paperwork Reduction Act of 1995 (PRA), BSEE is inviting comments on a collection of information that we will submit to the Office of Management and Budget (OMB) for review and approval. The information collection request (ICR) concerns a renewal to the paperwork requirements in the regulations under Part 254, "Oil-Spill Response Requirements for Facilities Located Seaward of the Coast Line."
