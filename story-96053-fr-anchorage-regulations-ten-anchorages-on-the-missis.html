BREAKING: Federal Register: Anchorage Regulations; Ten Anchorages on the Mississippi River Mile Markers 12-85 AHP

Published: 2022-05-16 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is amending anchorage regulations for the Lower Mississippi River (LMR) between mile marker (MM) 12 above head of passes (AHP), to MM 85 AHP. This amendment modifies nine anchorage grounds and establishes one new anchorage ground.
