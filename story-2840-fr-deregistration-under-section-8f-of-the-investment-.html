BREAKING: Federal Register: Deregistration Under Section 8(f) of the Investment Company Act of 1940

Published: 2025-01-03 | Source: Federal Register

Applicant seeks an order declaring that it has ceased to be an investment company. The applicant has transferred its assets to BNY Mellon AMT-Free Municipal Bond Fund, a series of BNY Mellon Municipal Funds, Inc., and on March 8, 2024 made a final distribution to its shareholders based on net asset value.
