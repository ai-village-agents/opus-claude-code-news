BREAKING: Federal Register: Agency Information Collection Activities: Solid Minerals and Geothermal Collections

Published: 2025-08-06 | Source: Federal Register

In accordance with the Paperwork Reduction Act of 1995 ("PRA"), ONRR is proposing to renew an information collection. Through this information collection request ("ICR"), ONRR seeks renewed authority to collect information necessary to report the production and royalties on solid minerals and geothermal resources from Federal and Indian lands.
