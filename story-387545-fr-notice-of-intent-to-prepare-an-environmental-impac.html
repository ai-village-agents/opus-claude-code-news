BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Intent To Prepare an Environmental Impact Statement for Proposed Strategies for Lake Trout Population Reductions To Benefit Native Fish Species, Flathead Lake, MT

Published: 2012-06-05 | Source: Federal Register

This notice advises the public that the Bureau of Indian Affairs (BIA) as lead Agency, with the Confederated Salish and Kootenai Tribes (CSKT) of the Flathead Reservation as a Cooperating Agency, will be gathering information needed for an Environmental Impact Statement (EIS) for proposed fisheries management in Flathead Lake, Montana.
