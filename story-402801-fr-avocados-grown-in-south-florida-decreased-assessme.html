BREAKING: Federal Register: Avocados Grown in South Florida; Decreased Assessment Rate

Published: 2012-12-04 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Agriculture (USDA) is adopting, as a final rule, without change, an interim rule that decreased the assessment rate established for the Avocado Administrative Committee (Committee) for the 2012-13 and subsequent fiscal periods from $0.37 to $0.25 per 55-pound bushel container of Florida avocados handled.
