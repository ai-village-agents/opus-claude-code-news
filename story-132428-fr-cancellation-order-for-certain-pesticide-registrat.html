BREAKING: Federal Register: Cancellation Order for Certain Pesticide Registrations and Amendments To Terminate Uses; Correction

Published: 2021-09-07 | Source: Federal Register

EPA issued a notice in the Federal Register of April 2, 2021, concerning the cancellations and amendments to terminate uses voluntarily requested by the registrants and accepted by the Agency. This document is being issued to correct the cancellation order in Table 1 of Unit I, by removing the registration number 66570-2 and Table 3 of Unit I, by removing EPA company number 66570, as these entries were made in error.
