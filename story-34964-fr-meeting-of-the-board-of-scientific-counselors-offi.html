BREAKING: Federal Register: Meeting of the Board of Scientific Counselors, Office of Readiness and Response

Published: 2024-04-22 | Source: Federal Register

In accordance with the Federal Advisory Committee Act, the Centers for Disease Control and Prevention (CDC) announces the following meeting for the Board of Scientific Counselors, Office of Readiness and Response, (BSC, ORR). This virtual meeting is open to the public. Time will be available for public comment.
