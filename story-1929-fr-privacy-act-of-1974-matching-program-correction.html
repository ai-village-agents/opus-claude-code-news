BREAKING: Federal Register: Privacy Act of 1974; Matching Program; Correction

Published: 2026-01-16 | Source: Federal Register

We published a new matching program in the Federal Register on October 15, 2024 concerning a matching program with the States, including tribal agencies and United States territories. That document contained an incorrect date on which the matching program would be applicable. This notice corrects that error.
