BREAKING: Federal Register: General Services Administration Property Management Regulations, (GSPMR), Enterprise Data & Privacy Management Office (IDE); Social Security Number Fraud Prevention

Published: 2022-10-07 | Source: Federal Register

The General Service Administration (GSA) is proposing to amend GSA's regulations under the Privacy Act. The revisions would clarify and update the language of procedural requirements pertaining to the inclusion of Social Security account numbers (SSNs) on documents that GSA sends by mail.
