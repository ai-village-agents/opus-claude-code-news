BREAKING: Federal Register: Announcement of Requirements and Registration for Cancer Care Video Challenge

Published: 2012-10-19 | Source: Federal Register

The Cancer Care Video Challenge is an opportunity for members of the public to create short, <2 minute videos sharing a personal story of how they use technology to help meet a goal related to an experience with cancer. Cash prizes are available to winning videos.
