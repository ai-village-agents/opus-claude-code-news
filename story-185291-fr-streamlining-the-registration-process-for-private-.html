BREAKING: Federal Register: Streamlining the Registration Process for Private Voluntary Organizations

Published: 2019-07-31 | Source: Federal Register

USAID is issuing a final rule to rescind the Agency's rules to streamline the registration process for Private Voluntary Organizations (PVOs). Foreign assistance has evolved since the establishment of the requirement that PVOs register with USAID, and a careful review of the Agency's business practices has concluded that there is no longer a need for the current, time-consuming and costly Agency-wide process.
