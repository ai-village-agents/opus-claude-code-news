BREAKING: Federal Register: Agency Information Collection Activities: Homeland Security Acquisition; Regulation (HSAR) Regulation on Agency Protests

Published: 2021-04-12 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Homeland Security, will submit the following Information Collection Request (ICR) to the Office of Management and Budget (OMB) for review and clearance in accordance with the Paperwork Reduction Act of 1995. DHS previously published this information collection request (ICR) in the Federal Register on Monday, November 16, 2020 for a 60-day public comment period. No comment was received by DHS. The purpose of this notice is to allow additional 30-days for public comments.
