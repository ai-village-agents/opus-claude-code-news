BREAKING: Federal Register: Expansion of R-3803 Restricted Area Complex; Fort Polk, LA

Published: 2019-07-16 | Source: Federal Register

This action expands the R-3803 restricted area complex in central Louisiana by establishing four new restricted areas, R-3803C, R-3803D, R-3803E, and R-3803F, and makes minor technical amendments to the existing R-3803A and R-3803B legal descriptions for improved operational efficiency and administrative standardization.
