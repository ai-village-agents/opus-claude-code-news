BREAKING: Federal Register: Request for Nominations on Public Advisory Panels of the Medical Devices Advisory Committee

Published: 2019-12-05 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA) is requesting that any industry organizations interested in participating in the selection of nonvoting industry representatives to serve on certain panels of the Medical Devices Advisory Committee (MDAC or Committee) in the Center for Devices and Radiological Health (CDRH) notify FDA in writing.
