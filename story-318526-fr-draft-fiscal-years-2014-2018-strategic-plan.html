BREAKING: Federal Register: Draft Fiscal Years 2014-2018 Strategic Plan

Published: 2014-03-11 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Nuclear Regulatory Commission (NRC) is correcting a notice that appeared in the Federal Register on March 5, 2014. The notice notified the public of the availability of draft NUREG-1614, Volume 6, "U.S. Nuclear Regulatory Commission Strategic Plan, Fiscal Years 2014-2018."
