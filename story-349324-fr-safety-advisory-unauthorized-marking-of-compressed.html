BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Advisory: Unauthorized Marking of Compressed Gas Cylinders

Published: 2013-03-13 | Source: Federal Register

This notice advises the public that PHMSA has confirmed the marking, service and/or sale of certain high pressure DOT specification cylinders marked with a requalification identification number (RIN) without performing a valid hydrostatic requalification test. Kraus Fire Equipment Co. marked DOT cylinders as tested without approval from the Associate Administrator to requalify DOT cylinders and without calibrating its required systems.
