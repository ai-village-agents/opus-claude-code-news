BREAKING: Federal Register: Prior Notice of Imported Food Questions and Answers (Edition 4); Draft Guidance for Industry; Availability

Published: 2022-09-13 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA or we) is announcing the availability of a draft guidance for industry entitled "Prior Notice of Imported Food Questions and Answers; Draft Guidance for Industry (Edition 4)." The draft guidance adds three additional questions. One question relates to any effect systems recognition or equivalency determinations have on prior notice requirements.
