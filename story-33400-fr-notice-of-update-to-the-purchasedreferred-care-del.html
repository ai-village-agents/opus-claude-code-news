BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Update to the Purchased/Referred Care Delivery Area for the Mississippi Band of Choctaw Indians

Published: 2024-04-02 | Source: Federal Register

Notice is hereby given that the Indian Health Service (IHS) has updated the geographic boundaries of the purchased/referred care delivery area (PRCDA) for the Mississippi Band of Choctaw Indians to include the counties of Carroll and Jackson in the State of Mississippi and the county of Lauderdale in the State of Tennessee.
