BREAKING: Federal Register: Amendment of Class D and Class E Airspace and Establishment of Class E Airspace; Marquette, MI

Published: 2020-12-23 | Source: Federal Register

This action amends the Class D and Class E airspace and establishes a Class E airspace area designated as an extension to Class D and Class E surface areas at Sawyer International Airport, Marquette, MI. This action is the result of an airspace review caused by the decommissioning of the Iron Mountain VHF omnidirectional range (VOR) navigation aid as part of the VOR Minimum Operational Network (MON) Program.
