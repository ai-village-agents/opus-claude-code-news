BREAKING: Federal Register: Expedited Vocational Assessment Under the Sequential Evaluation Process

Published: 2012-07-25 | Source: Federal Register

We are revising our rules to give adjudicators the discretion to proceed to the fifth step of the sequential evaluation process for assessing disability when we have insufficient information about a claimant's past relevant work history to make the findings required for step 4.
