BREAKING: Federal Register: QTc Information in Human Prescription Drug and Biological Product Labeling; Draft Guidance for Industry; Availability

Published: 2023-08-08 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA or Agency) is announcing the availability of a draft guidance for industry entitled "QTc Information in Human Prescription Drug and Biological Product Labeling." This guidance is intended to assist applicants with incorporating corrected QT (QTc) interval prolongation-related information into the labeling of non-antiarrhythmic human prescription drug and biological products.
