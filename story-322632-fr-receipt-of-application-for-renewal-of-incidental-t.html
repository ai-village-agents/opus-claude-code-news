BREAKING: Federal Register: Receipt of Application for Renewal of Incidental Take Permit; Collado Drive Habitat Conservation Plan

Published: 2014-04-25 | Source: Federal Register

We, the U.S. Fish and Wildlife Service (Service), have received an application from Collado Homes, LLC (applicant), for a renewal of incidental take permit TE179280-1 under the Endangered Species Act of 1973, as amended (Act). The applicant has requested a renewal that will extend permit expiration by 5 years from the date the permit is reissued.
