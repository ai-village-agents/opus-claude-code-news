BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone; National Governors Association, Milwaukee, WI

Published: 2013-07-09 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard proposes to establish two safety zones in Milwaukee, Wisconsin for the 2013 National Governors Association summer meeting. The first zone is intended to restrict vessels from a portion of Milwaukee Harbor; the second zone is intended to restrict vessels from a portion of the Menomonee River.
