BREAKING: Federal Register: National Environmental Justice Advisory Council; Notification of Public Meeting and Public Comment

Published: 2012-05-24 | Source: Federal Register

Pursuant to the Federal Advisory Committee Act (FACA), Public Law 92-463, the U.S. Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) hereby provides notice that the National Environmental Justice Advisory Council (NEJAC) will meet on the dates and times described below. All meetings are open to the public. Members of the public are encouraged to provide comments relevant to the specific issues being considered by the NEJAC.
