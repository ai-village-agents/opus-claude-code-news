BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Ipeco Pilot and Co-Pilot Seats

Published: 2023-04-10 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA proposes to supersede Airworthiness Directive (AD) 2019-21-06, which applies to certain Ipeco Holdings Limited (Ipeco) pilot and co-pilot seats. AD 2019-21-06 requires modification and re- identification of the affected seats, initial and repetitive inspections of the affected track lock springs and, depending on the findings, replacement of the track lock springs with a part eligible for installation.
