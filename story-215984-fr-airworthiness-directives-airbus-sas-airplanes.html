BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Airbus SAS Airplanes

Published: 2018-09-10 | Source: Federal Register

We are adopting a new airworthiness directive (AD) for all Airbus SAS Model A330-200 Freighter series airplanes, Model A330-200 and -300 series airplanes, and Model A340-200 and -300 series airplanes. This AD was prompted by reports of cracked slat tracks at the location of the front stop attachment to the track.
