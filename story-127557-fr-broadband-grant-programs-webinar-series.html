BREAKING: Federal Register: Broadband Grant Programs Webinar Series

Published: 2021-07-06 | Source: Federal Register

The National Telecommunications and Information Administration (NTIA) will host webinars in connection with the three new broadband grant programs authorized and funded by the Consolidated Appropriations Act, 2021: The Broadband Infrastructure Program, the Tribal Broadband Connectivity Program, and the Connecting Minority Communities Pilot Program.
