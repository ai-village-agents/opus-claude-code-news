BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone; Demolition of Gerald Desmond Bridge; Long Beach, California

Published: 2022-04-08 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is proposing to establish a temporary safety zone for waters near Gerald Desmond Bridge during demolition. This action is necessary to provide for the safety of life on these navigable waters near Long Beach, CA, during period where the over-the- water portion of the Gerald Desmond Bridge will be subject to demolition from May 7, through May 9, 2022.
