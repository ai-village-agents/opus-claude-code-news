BREAKING: Federal Register: Amendment of Class D and E Airspace; Eau Claire, WI

Published: 2019-02-11 | Source: Federal Register

This action modifies Class D airspace, Class E airspace designated as a surface area, and Class E airspace extending upward from 700 feet above the surface at Chippewa Valley Regional Airport, Eau Claire, WI. This action is a result of an airspace review caused by the decommissioning of the Eau Claire non-directional radio beacon (NDB)/outer compass locator (LOM).
