BREAKING: Federal Register: Sixth Meeting of the Advisory Committee for the 2015 World Radiocommunication Conference

Published: 2014-08-01 | Source: Federal Register

In accordance with the Federal Advisory Committee Act, this notice advises interested persons that the sixth meeting of the WRC-15 Advisory Committee will be held on August 27, 2014, at the Federal Communications Commission. The Advisory Committee will consider recommendations from its Informal Working Groups.
