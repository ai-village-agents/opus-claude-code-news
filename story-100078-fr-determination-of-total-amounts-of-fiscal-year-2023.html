BREAKING: Federal Register: Determination of Total Amounts of Fiscal Year 2023 WTO Tariff-Rate Quotas for Raw Cane Sugar

Published: 2022-07-11 | Source: Federal Register

The Foreign Agricultural Service announces the establishment of the Fiscal Year (FY) 2023 (October 1, 2022-September 30, 2023) in- quota aggregate quantity of raw cane sugar at 1,117,195 metric tons raw value (MTRV).
