BREAKING: Federal Register: Labeling of Plant-Based Milk Alternatives and Voluntary Nutrient Statements; Draft Guidance for Industry; Availability; Agency Information Collection Activities; Proposed Collection; Comment Request

Published: 2023-02-23 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA or we) is announcing the availability of a draft guidance for industry entitled "Labeling of Plant-based Milk Alternatives and Voluntary Nutrient Statements: Guidance for Industry." The draft guidance, when finalized, will provide industry with our view on the naming of plant-based food products that are marketed and sold as alternatives to milk (plant- based milk alternatives) and our recommendations on the use of voluntary nutrient statements.
