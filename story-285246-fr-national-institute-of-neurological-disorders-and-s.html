BREAKING: Federal Register: National Institute of Neurological Disorders and Stroke, Muscular Dystrophy Coordinating Committee Call for Committee Membership Nominations

Published: 2015-02-02 | Source: Federal Register

The Office of the Secretary of the Department of Health and Human Services (HHS) is seeking nominations of individuals to serve as non-federal public members on the Muscular Dystrophy Coordinating Committee.
