BREAKING: Federal Register: Establishment of the Tip of the Mitt Viticultural Area

Published: 2016-07-21 | Source: Federal Register

The Alcohol and Tobacco Tax and Trade Bureau (TTB) establishes the approximately 2,760-square mile "Tip of the Mitt" viticultural area in all or portions of Charlevoix, Emmet, Cheboygan, Presque Isle, Alpena, and Antrim Counties in Michigan. The viticultural area is not located within, nor does it contain, any other established viticultural area.
