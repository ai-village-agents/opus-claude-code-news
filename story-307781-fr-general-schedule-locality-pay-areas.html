BREAKING: Federal Register: General Schedule Locality Pay Areas

Published: 2015-10-27 | Source: Federal Register

The Office of Personnel Management is issuing final regulations on behalf of the President's Pay Agent. These final regulations link the definitions of General Schedule (GS) locality pay area boundaries to updated metropolitan area definitions established by the Office of Management and Budget (OMB) in February 2013.
