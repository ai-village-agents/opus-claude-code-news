BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Honda Aircraft Company LLC

Published: 2020-03-20 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA is adopting a new airworthiness directive (AD) for certain Honda Aircraft Company LLC (Honda) Model HA-420 airplanes. This AD requires inspecting the wheel speed transducer (WST) wiring harness, replacing the wiring harness if necessary, installing wiring hardware, and rerouting the WST wiring harness on both the left and right brake assemblies.
