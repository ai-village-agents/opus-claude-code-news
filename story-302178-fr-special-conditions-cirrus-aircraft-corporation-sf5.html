BREAKING: Federal Register: Special Conditions: Cirrus Aircraft Corporation, SF50; Auto Throttle.

Published: 2015-08-21 | Source: Federal Register

This action proposes special conditions for the Cirrus Aircraft Corporation Model SF50 airplane. This airplane will have a novel or unusual design feature(s) associated with installation of an Auto Throttle System. The applicable airworthiness regulations do not contain adequate or appropriate safety standards for this design feature.
