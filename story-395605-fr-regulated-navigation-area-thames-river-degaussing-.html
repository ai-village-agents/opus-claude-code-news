BREAKING: Federal Register: Regulated Navigation Area; Thames River Degaussing Range Replacement Operations; New London, CT

Published: 2012-09-05 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard proposes to establish a regulated navigation area (RNA) on the navigable waters of the Thames River in New London Harbor, New London, CT. The proposed RNA would establish speed and wake restrictions as well as allow the Coast Guard to prohibit all vessel traffic through the RNA during degaussing range replacement operations, both planned and unforeseen, that could pose an imminent hazard to persons and vessels operating in the area.
