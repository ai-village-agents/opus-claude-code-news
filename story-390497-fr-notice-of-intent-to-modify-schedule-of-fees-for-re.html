BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Intent To Modify Schedule of Fees for Reviewing Historic Preservation Certification Applications; Correction

Published: 2012-07-06 | Source: Federal Register

This notice corrects the address and fax number that the public should use in making comments on the changes to the fees the National Park Service (NPS) charges for reviewing Historic Preservation Certification Applications, and extends the period for making comments.
