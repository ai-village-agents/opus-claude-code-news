BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Public Open House Meetings for the Tongass Land and Resource Management Plan Amendment

Published: 2015-01-23 | Source: Federal Register

The Tongass National Forest is working to complete an amendment to the Tongass Land and Resource Management Plan (forest plan) by August 2016. The scope of the plan amendment is narrowly focused to accomplish a transition to young growth management as provided in the Secretary's Memorandum (1044-009), and to make renewable energy development more permissive on the Tongass.
