BREAKING: Federal Register: Certain Marine Air Conditioning Systems, Components Thereof, and Products Containing the Same; Notice of a Commission Determination To Review in Part and, on Review, To Affirm With Modification a Final Initial Determination Finding No Violation; Termination of Investigation

Published: 2024-03-13 | Source: Federal Register

Notice is hereby given that the U.S. International Trade Commission has determined to review in part and, on review, to affirm with modification a final initial determination ("FID") of the presiding administrative law judge ("ALJ"). The investigation is terminated.
