BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Empresa Brasileira de Aeronautica S.A. (Embraer) Airplanes

Published: 2015-08-21 | Source: Federal Register

We propose to adopt a new airworthiness directive (AD) for certain Empresa Brasileira de Aeronautica S.A. (Embraer) Model EMB-135 airplanes and Model EMB-145, -145ER, -145MR, -145LR, -145MP, and -145EP airplanes. This proposed AD was prompted by a report of chafing between the fuel pump electrical harness and the fuel pump tubing during scheduled maintenance.
