BREAKING: Federal Register: Joint Failure on Continuous Welded Rail Track

Published: 2013-08-05 | Source: Federal Register

FRA is issuing Safety Advisory 2013-05 to remind railroad track owners about the importance of reviewing current, internal continuous welded rail (CWR) plans and properly inspecting CWR joints to identify and correct locations that indicate potential joint failure that may cause a derailment. FRA is issuing this notice in response to two recent train derailments.
