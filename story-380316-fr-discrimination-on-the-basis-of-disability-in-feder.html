BREAKING: Federal Register: Discrimination on the Basis of Disability in Federally Assisted Programs and Activities

Published: 2012-03-14 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Education (Department or Education) provides notice of its interpretation of Section 504 of the Rehabilitation Act of 1973 and the Department's implementing regulations, which prohibit discrimination on the basis of disability in federally assisted programs and activities (Education's Section 504 regulations).
