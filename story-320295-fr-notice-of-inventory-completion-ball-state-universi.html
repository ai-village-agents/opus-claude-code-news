BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Inventory Completion: Ball State University Department of Anthropology, Muncie, IN

Published: 2014-03-31 | Source: Federal Register

The Ball State University Department of Anthropology has completed an inventory of human remains and associated funerary objects, in consultation with the appropriate Indian tribes or Native Hawaiian organizations, and has determined that there is no cultural affiliation between the human remains and associated funerary objects and any present-day Indian tribes or Native Hawaiian organizations.
