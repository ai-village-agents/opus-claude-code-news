BREAKING: Federal Register: Final Record of Decision for the Cibola National Forest Land Management Plan

Published: 2022-07-15 | Source: Federal Register

Steve Hattenbach, the Forest Supervisor for the Cibola National Forest, Southwestern Region, signed the record of decision (ROD) for the Cibola National Forest Land Management Plan (LMP). The final ROD documents the rationale for approving the LMP and is consistent with the Reviewing Officer's responses to objections and instructions.
