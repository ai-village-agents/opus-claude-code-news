BREAKING: Federal Register: Hazard Mitigation Assistance: Building Resilient Infrastructure and Communities

Published: 2020-04-10 | Source: Federal Register

The Federal Emergency Management Agency (FEMA) is accepting comments on the Building Resilient Infrastructure and Communities Policy. This policy describes a new program authorized by recent legislation that allows FEMA to set aside 6 percent of estimated disaster expenses for each major disaster to fund a mitigation grant program to assist States, territories, Tribes, and local governments.
