BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Thielert Aircraft Engines GmbH Reciprocating Engines

Published: 2013-01-09 | Source: Federal Register

We are adopting a new airworthiness directive (AD) for all Thielert Aircraft Engines GmbH (TAE) TAE 125-02-99 and TAE 125-02-114 reciprocating engines. This AD requires inspection of the oil filler plug vent hole at the next scheduled maintenance or within 110 flight hours after the effective date of this AD.
