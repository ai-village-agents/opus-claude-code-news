BREAKING: Federal Register: Certain Steel Nails From the People's Republic of China: Preliminary Results of the Fourth Antidumping Duty Administrative Review

Published: 2013-09-16 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Commerce ("Department") is conducting the fourth administrative review of the antidumping duty order on certain steel nails ("nails") from the People's Republic of China ("PRC").\1\ The Department has preliminarily determined that sales have been made below normal value ("NV") by the respondents examined during the period of review ("POR"), August 1, 2011, through July 31, 2012.
