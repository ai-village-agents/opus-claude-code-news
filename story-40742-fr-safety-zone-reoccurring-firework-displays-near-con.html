BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone; Reoccurring Firework Displays Near Convention Center on the Mobile River; Mobile, AL

Published: 2024-07-01 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is establishing a safety zone for reoccurring firework displays on the Mobile River in the vicinity of the Arthur R. Outlaw Convention Center in Mobile, AL. The safety zone will be enforced only during the firework displays. Establishment of this safety zone is necessary to protect waterway users and vessels from potential hazards associated with these firework displays.
