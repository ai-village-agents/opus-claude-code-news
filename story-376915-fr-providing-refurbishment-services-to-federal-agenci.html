BREAKING: Federal Register: Providing Refurbishment Services to Federal Agencies

Published: 2012-02-07 | Source: Federal Register

In accordance with the National Strategy for Electronics Stewardship recommendations (http://www.gsa.gov/estewardship), GSA is exploring whether and how best to make cost-effective refurbishment services available to Federal agencies to extend the useful life of non-functional electronic equipment.
