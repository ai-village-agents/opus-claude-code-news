BREAKING: Federal Register: Importation of Live Birds and Poultry, Poultry Meat, and Poultry Products From a Region in the European Union

Published: 2013-03-29 | Source: Federal Register

We are amending the regulations governing the importation of animals and animal products by recognizing 25 Member States of the European Union (EU) as the Animal and Plant Health Inspection Service (APHIS)-defined EU poultry trade region and adding it to the list of regions we consider to be free of Newcastle disease. We are taking this action based on a risk evaluation that we prepared in which we determined that the region meets our requirements for being considered free of Newcastle disease. ...
