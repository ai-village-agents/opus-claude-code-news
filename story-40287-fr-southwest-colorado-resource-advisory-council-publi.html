BREAKING: Federal Register: Southwest Colorado Resource Advisory Council Public Meetings

Published: 2024-06-26 | Source: Federal Register

In accordance with the Federal Land Policy and Management Act of 1976, as amended, and the Federal Advisory Committee Act of 1972, the U.S. Department of the Interior, Bureau of Land Management's (BLM) Colorado Southwest Resource Advisory Council (RAC) is announcing two public meetings in August and November 2024.
