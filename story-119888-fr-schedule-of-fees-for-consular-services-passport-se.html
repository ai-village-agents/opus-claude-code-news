BREAKING: Federal Register: Schedule of Fees for Consular Services-Passport Security Surcharge

Published: 2021-03-26 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of State proposes an adjustment to the Schedule of Fees for Consular Services (Schedule of Fees) of the Bureau of Consular Affairs (CA) based on the findings of the most recently approved update to the Cost of Service Model (CoSM). The CoSM is updated annually using a combination of costs, predicted workload, and level of effort data.
