BREAKING: Federal Register: Land Acquisitions; Three Affiliated Tribes of the Fort Berthold Reservation, North Dakota

Published: 2020-10-06 | Source: Federal Register

The Assistant Secretary--Indian Affairs has made a final determination to acquire 9,303.79 acres, more or less, into trust for the Three Affiliated Tribes of the Fort Berthold Reservation, North Dakota.
