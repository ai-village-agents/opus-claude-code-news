BREAKING: Federal Register: DTE Electric Company; Fermi 2

Published: 2014-06-18 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Nuclear Regulatory Commission (NRC) is considering an application for the renewal of operating license number NPF-43, which authorizes DTE Electric Company, to operate Fermi 2. The renewed license would authorize the applicant to operate Fermi 2 for an additional 20 years beyond the period specified in the current license.
