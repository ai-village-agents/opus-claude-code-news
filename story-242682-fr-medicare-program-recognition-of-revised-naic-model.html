BREAKING: Federal Register: Medicare Program; Recognition of Revised NAIC Model Standards for Regulation of Medicare Supplemental Insurance

Published: 2017-09-01 | Source: Federal Register

This notice announces the changes made by the Medicare Access and CHIP Reauthorization of 2015 (MACRA) to section 1882 of the Social Security Act (the Act), which governs Medicare supplemental insurance.
