BREAKING: Federal Register: Pesticide Reregistration Performance Measures and Goals; Annual Progress Report for 2019; Notice of Availability

Published: 2022-07-01 | Source: Federal Register

This notice announces the availability of EPA's progress report in meeting its performance measures and goals for pesticide reregistration during fiscal year 2019. This progress report also presents the total number of products registered under the "fast- track" provisions of the Federal Insecticide Fungicide and Rodenticide Act (FIFRA).
