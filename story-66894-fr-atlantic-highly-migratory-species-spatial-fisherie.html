BREAKING: Federal Register: Atlantic Highly Migratory Species; Spatial Fisheries Management

Published: 2023-05-05 | Source: Federal Register

This proposed rule would implement Draft Amendment 15 to the 2006 Consolidated Atlantic Highly Migratory Species (HMS) Fishery Management Plan (FMP) (Amendment 15). NMFS is proposing changes to Atlantic HMS fishery management measures regarding four commercial longline spatial management areas and the administration and funding of the HMS pelagic longline electronic monitoring (EM) program.
