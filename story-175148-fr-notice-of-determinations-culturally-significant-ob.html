BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Determinations; Culturally Significant Objects Imported for Exhibition-Determinations: “Camp: Notes on Fashion” Exhibition

Published: 2019-03-27 | Source: Federal Register

Notice is hereby given of the following determinations: I hereby determine that certain objects to be included in the exhibition "Camp: Notes on Fashion," imported from abroad for temporary exhibition within the United States, are of cultural significance. The objects are imported pursuant to loan agreements with the foreign owners or custodians.
