BREAKING: Federal Register: Implementation of the Privacy Act of 1974, as Amended; New System of Records Notice, Digital Identity Access Management System

Published: 2014-09-29 | Source: Federal Register

Pursuant to the Privacy Act of 1974 (U.S.C. 552a (e)(4)), as amended, and Office of Management and Budget (OMB), Circular No. A-130, notice is hereby given that the Department of Housing and Urban Development (HUD), Office of the Chief Information Officer (OCIO) proposes to establish a new system of records, the Digital Identity Access Management System (DIAMS).
