BREAKING: Federal Register: Regulatory Hearing Before the Food and Drug Administration; General Provisions; Technical Amendment

Published: 2016-08-11 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA) is amending its regulations to correct an error in the lists of statutory and regulatory provisions that provide an opportunity for an informal hearing so that the lists correctly reference the statutory and regulatory provisions that provide such an opportunity in connection with a ban of a device.
