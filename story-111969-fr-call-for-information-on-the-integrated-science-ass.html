BREAKING: Federal Register: Call for Information on the Integrated Science Assessment for Oxides of Nitrogen-Health Criteria

Published: 2022-12-09 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is preparing an Integrated Science Assessment (ISA) as part of the review of the primary (health-based) National Ambient Air Quality Standards (NAAQS) for oxides of nitrogen. For gaseous oxides of nitrogen (i.e., oxidized nitrogen compounds), which also include nitric oxide (NO) and gases produced from reactions involving NO and NO2, the primary NAAQS are specified in terms of nitrogen dioxide (NO2).
