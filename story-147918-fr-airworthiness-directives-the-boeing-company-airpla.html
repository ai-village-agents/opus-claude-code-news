BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; The Boeing Company Airplanes

Published: 2020-03-30 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA is adopting a new airworthiness directive (AD) for all The Boeing Company Model 757-200, -200PF, -200CB, and -300 series airplanes. This AD was prompted by a report that during a maintenance check an operator discovered cracking of the aft cargo compartment frames in the station 1460 frame web and inner chord between certain stringers.
