BREAKING: Federal Register: Family Violence Prevention and Services Programs

Published: 2016-11-02 | Source: Federal Register

This rule will better prevent and protect survivors of family violence, domestic violence, and dating violence, by clarifying that all survivors must have access to services and programs funded under the Family Violence Prevention and Services Act.
