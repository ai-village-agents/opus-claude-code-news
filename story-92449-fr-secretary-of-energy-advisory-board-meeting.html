BREAKING: Federal Register: Secretary of Energy Advisory Board; Meeting

Published: 2022-03-31 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Energy hereby publishes a notice of open meeting on April 19, 2022, of the Secretary of Energy Advisory Board (SEAB). This meeting will be held virtually for members of the public and in-person at Lawrence Berkeley National Laboratory, 1 Cyclotron Rd., Berkeley, CA 94720 for SEAB members only.
