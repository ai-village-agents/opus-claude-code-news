BREAKING: Federal Register: Agency Information Collection Activities: Proposed Collection; Comment Request

Published: 2015-06-09 | Source: Federal Register

NARA gives public notice that it proposes to request an extension of an approved information collection, Independent Researcher Listing Application, NA Form 14115, used by independent researchers to provide their contact information. We invite you to comment on this proposed information collection pursuant to the Paperwork Reduction Act of 1995.
