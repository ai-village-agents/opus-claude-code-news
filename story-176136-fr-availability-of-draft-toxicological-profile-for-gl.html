BREAKING: Federal Register: Availability of Draft Toxicological Profile for Glyphosate

Published: 2019-04-08 | Source: Federal Register

The Agency for Toxic Substances and Disease Registry (ATSDR), within the Department of Health and Human Services (HHS), announces the opening of a docket to obtain comments on the Draft Toxicological Profile for Glyphosate. On February 12, 2015 ATSDR announced that it was preparing to develop their Set 28 Draft Toxicological Profiles, including Glyphosate, for public comment release (80 FR 7870).
