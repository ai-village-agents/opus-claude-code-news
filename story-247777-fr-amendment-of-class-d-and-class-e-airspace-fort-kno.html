BREAKING: Federal Register: Amendment of Class D and Class E Airspace; Fort Knox, KY, and Louisville, KY

Published: 2017-11-01 | Source: Federal Register

This action amends Class E airspace designated as an extension to Class D airspace by removing the Notice to Airmen (NOTAM) part-time status at Godman Army Airfield (AAF) Fort Knox, KY; and Bowman Field Airport, Louisville, KY. This action also updates and corrects the geographic coordinates of these airports, and Louisville International Airport-Standiford Field (formerly Louisville Standiford Field) in the associated Class D and E airspace descriptions.
