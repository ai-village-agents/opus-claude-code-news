BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Continuation and Request for Nominations for the Industry Trade Advisory Committees

Published: 2022-04-12 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Trade Representative and the Secretary of Commerce (Secretary) have established a new four-year charter term ending in February 2026 for the Industry Trade Advisory Committees (ITACs) and are accepting applications from qualified individuals interested in serving as members.
