BREAKING: Federal Register: Review of Nomination for Mariana Trench National Marine Sanctuary

Published: 2022-03-10 | Source: Federal Register

On January 21, 2022, NOAA published a notice in the Federal Register requesting written and oral comments to facilitate ONMS' five- year review of the nomination for the Mariana Trench National Marine Sanctuary (NMS) at the five-year interval. In that notice, NOAA requested relevant information as it pertains to its 11 evaluation criteria for inclusion in the inventory.
