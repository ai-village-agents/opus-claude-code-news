BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Inventory Completion Amendment: University of Arkansas Museum Collections, Fayetteville, AR

Published: 2022-12-20 | Source: Federal Register

In accordance with the Native American Graves Protection and Repatriation Act (NAGPRA), the University of Arkansas Museum Collections has amended a Notice of Inventory Completion published in the Federal Register on April 29, 2022. This notice amends the number of associated funerary objects in collections removed from Cross, Mississippi, and Poinsett Counties, AR.
