BREAKING: Federal Register: Marketing Order Regulations for Almonds Grown in California

Published: 2023-04-27 | Source: Federal Register

This proposed rule would implement a recommendation from the Almond Board of California (Board) to make changes to multiple provisions in the administrative requirements prescribed under the Federal marketing order regulating the handling of almonds grown in California (Order). This action would amend administrative requirements regulating quality control, exempt dispositions, and interest and late charges provisions.
