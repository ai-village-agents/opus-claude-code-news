BREAKING: Federal Register: Application for Amended Authorization To Export Electric Energy; NRG Business Marketing LLC

Published: 2024-02-09 | Source: Federal Register

NRG Business Marketing LLC (NRGBM or the Applicant), formerly known as Direct Energy Business Marketing, LLC (DEBM), has applied for amended authorization to transmit electric energy from the United States to Mexico pursuant to the Federal Power Act.
