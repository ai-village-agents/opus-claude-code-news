BREAKING: Federal Register: Government Securities: Call for Large Position Reports

Published: 2018-04-13 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Department of the Treasury ("Department" or "Treasury") called for the submission of Large Position Reports by those entities whose positions in the 2\1/4\% Treasury Notes of November 2027 equaled or exceeded $4.58 billion as of January 12, 2018.
