BREAKING: Federal Register: Constellation Energy Generation, LLC; Calvert Cliffs Nuclear Power Plant, Unit 2; License Amendment Request

Published: 2025-11-24 | Source: Federal Register

The proposed license amendment would extend the completion time (CT) for Technical Specification 3.8.1, "AC [alternating current] Sources- Operating," Action D.3.
