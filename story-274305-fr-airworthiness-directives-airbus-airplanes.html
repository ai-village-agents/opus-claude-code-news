BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Airbus Airplanes

Published: 2016-09-19 | Source: Federal Register

We are superseding Airworthiness Directive (AD) 2005-15-07 for certain Airbus Model A320-111 airplanes and Model A320-200 series airplanes. AD 2005-15-07 required installing insulator and cable ties to the electrical cables of the S routes at the gaps in the raceway in the wing trailing edge and the wing tip and wing root areas.
