BREAKING: Federal Register: Mandatory Inspection of Egg Substitutes and Freeze-Dried Egg Products Imported Into the United States

Published: 2021-09-07 | Source: Federal Register

On October 29, 2020, the Food Safety and Inspection Service (FSIS) published the final rule, "Egg Products Inspection Regulations," which, among other things, announced that FSIS would begin exercising jurisdiction over plants that produce egg substitutes and freeze-dried egg products on October 30, 2023.
