BREAKING: Federal Register: Resumption of the Population Estimates Challenge Program

Published: 2022-11-22 | Source: Federal Register

The Bureau of the Census (Census Bureau) is resuming the Population Estimates Challenge Program to provide eligible governmental units the opportunity to file requests for the review of population estimates for 2021 and subsequent years in forthcoming estimates series, beginning with the Vintage 2022 series that is scheduled to be published in 2023.
