BREAKING: Federal Register: Revisions to Safety Standard for Infant Bath Seats

Published: 2019-09-20 | Source: Federal Register

In December 2013, the U.S. Consumer Product Safety Commission (CPSC) published an update to the consumer product safety standard for infant bath seats. The standard incorporated by reference the applicable ASTM voluntary standard. ASTM has since published two revised versions of the voluntary standard for infant bath seats.
