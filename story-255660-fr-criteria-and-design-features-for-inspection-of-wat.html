BREAKING: Federal Register: Criteria and Design Features for Inspection of Water-Control Structures Associated With Nuclear Power Plants

Published: 2016-02-18 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Nuclear Regulatory Commission (NRC) is issuing Revision 2 of Regulatory Guide (RG) 1.127, "Criteria and Design Features for Inspection of Water-Control Structures Associated with Nuclear Power Plants." This RG describes a method that the staff of the NRC considers acceptable for designing water-control structures (e.g., dams, slopes, canals, reservoirs, and associated conveyance facilities) such that periodic inspections may be performed.
