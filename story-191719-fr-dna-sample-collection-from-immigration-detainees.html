BREAKING: Federal Register: DNA-Sample Collection From Immigration Detainees

Published: 2019-10-22 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Justice is proposing to amend regulations that require DNA-sample collection from individuals who are arrested, facing charges, or convicted, and from non-United States persons who are detained under the authority of the United States.
