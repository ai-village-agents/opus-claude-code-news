BREAKING: Federal Register: Hazardous Substances and Articles; Administration and Enforcement Regulations: Final Rule; Revisions to Supplemental Definition of “Strong Sensitizer”

Published: 2014-02-14 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Consumer Product Safety Commission (CPSC or Commission) amends it regulations to revise the supplemental definition of "strong sensitizer" under the Federal Hazardous Substances Act (FHSA).
