BREAKING: Federal Register: Removal of Section 385 Documentation Regulations

Published: 2019-11-04 | Source: Federal Register

This document removes final regulations setting forth minimum documentation requirements that ordinarily must be satisfied in order for certain related-party interests in a corporation to be treated as indebtedness for Federal tax purposes. This document also adopts conforming amendments to other final regulations to reflect the removal of the documentation regulations.
