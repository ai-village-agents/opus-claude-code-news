BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone, Indian River Bay; Millsboro, DE

Published: 2015-07-16 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard proposes to establish a temporary safety zone on the waters of Indian River Bay adjacent to Millsboro, Delaware. The safety zone will restrict vessel traffic on Indian River Bay in the vicinity of a fireworks barge on August 22 and September 26, 2015, from 8:45 p.m. until 10:15 p.m. on each day.
