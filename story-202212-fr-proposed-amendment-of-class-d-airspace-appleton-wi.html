BREAKING: Federal Register: Proposed Amendment of Class D Airspace; Appleton, WI

Published: 2018-03-15 | Source: Federal Register

This action proposes to modify Class D airspace at Appleton International Airport (formerly Outagamie County Airport), Appleton, WI. The FAA is proposing this action due to the decommissioning of the GAMIE locator outer marker (LOM) and collocated outer marker (OM) which provided navigation guidance to the airport.
