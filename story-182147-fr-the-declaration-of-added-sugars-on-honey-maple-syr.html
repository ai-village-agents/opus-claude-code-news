BREAKING: Federal Register: The Declaration of Added Sugars on Honey, Maple Syrup, Other Single-Ingredient Sugars and Syrups, and Certain Cranberry Products; Guidance for Industry; Availability

Published: 2019-06-20 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA or we) is announcing the availability of a final guidance for industry entitled "The Declaration of Added Sugars on Honey, Maple Syrup, Other Single- Ingredient Sugars and Syrups, and Certain Cranberry Products."
