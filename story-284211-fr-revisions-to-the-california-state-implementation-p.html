BREAKING: Federal Register: Revisions to the California State Implementation Plan, South Coast Air Quality Management District and Ventura County Air Pollution Control District

Published: 2015-01-20 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is proposing to approve revisions to the South Coast Air Quality Management District (SCAQMD) and the Ventura County Air Pollution Control District (VCAPCD) portions of the California State Implementation Plan (SIP). These revisions concern volatile organic compound (VOC) emissions from delayed coking units used in petroleum refining, and sulfur dioxide primary emissions from fossil fuel combustion.
