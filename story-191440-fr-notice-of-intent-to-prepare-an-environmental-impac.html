BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Intent To Prepare an Environmental Impact Statement for a Multimodal Project in Allston, Massachusetts

Published: 2019-10-18 | Source: Federal Register

The FHWA is issuing this notice to advise the public that it is preparing an Environmental Impact Statement (EIS) in accordance with the National Environmental Policy Act (NEPA) for the Allston Multimodal Project sponsored by the Massachusetts Department of Transportation (MassDOT). MassDOT proposes to address roadway deficiencies of Interstate 90 in Allston, Massachusetts.
