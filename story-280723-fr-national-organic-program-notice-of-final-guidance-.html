BREAKING: Federal Register: National Organic Program: Notice of Final Guidance on Classification of Materials and Materials for Organic Crop Production

Published: 2016-12-05 | Source: Federal Register

This notice announces availability of final guidance intended for use by accredited certifying agents, certified operations, material evaluation programs, and other organic industry stakeholders. The first set of guidance documents, NOP 5033, follows recommendations from the National Organic Standards Board (NOSB) concerning the classification of materials under the USDA organic regulations (7 CFR part 205).
