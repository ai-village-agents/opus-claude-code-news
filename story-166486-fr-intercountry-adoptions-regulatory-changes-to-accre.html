BREAKING: Federal Register: Intercountry Adoptions: Regulatory Changes to Accreditation and Approval Regulations in Intercountry Adoption

Published: 2020-11-20 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of State (the Department) is proposing revisions to the Code of Federal Regulations to amend requirements for accreditation and authorization by the United States to provide adoption services in intercountry adoption cases. This proposed rule amends regulations to provide clarification, updating, or other adaptation of familiar accreditation and approval standards for intercountry adoption.
