BREAKING: Federal Register: Southern Arizona Resource Advisory Committee

Published: 2021-09-24 | Source: Federal Register

The Southern Arizona Resource Advisory Committee (RAC) will hold a virtual meeting by phone and/or video conference. The committee is authorized under the Secure Rural Schools and Community Self- Determination Act (the Act) and operates in compliance with the Federal Advisory Committee Act (FACA).
