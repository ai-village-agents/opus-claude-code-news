Published: 2012-03-09 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA) is announcing the availability of a draft guidance titled "Drug Safety Information-- FDA's Communication to the Public." This draft guidance updates and revises the March 2007 guidance entitled "Drug Safety Information-- FDA's Communication to the Public."
