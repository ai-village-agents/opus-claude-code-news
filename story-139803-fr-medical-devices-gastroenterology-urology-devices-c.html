BREAKING: Federal Register: Medical Devices; Gastroenterology-Urology Devices; Classification of the Ingested, Transient, Space Occupying Device for Weight Management and/or Weight Loss

Published: 2021-12-10 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA or we) is classifying the ingested, transient, space occupying device for weight management and/or weight loss into class II (special controls). The special controls that apply to the device type are identified in this order and will be part of the codified language for the ingested, transient, space occupying device for weight management and/or weight loss's classification.
