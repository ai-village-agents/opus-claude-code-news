BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Airbus Airplanes

Published: 2014-04-08 | Source: Federal Register

We propose to supersede airworthiness directive (AD) 2011-17- 08, which applies to all Airbus Model A330-200 series airplanes, Model A330-200 Freighter series airplanes, and Model A330-300 series airplanes. AD 2011-17-08 currently requires revising the maintenance program by incorporating certain Airworthiness Limitation Items (ALIs).
