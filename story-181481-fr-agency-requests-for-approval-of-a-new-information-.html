BREAKING: Federal Register: Agency Requests for Approval of a New Information Collection(s): Report of Ocean Shipments Moving Under the Cargo Preference Act of 1954-Bill of Lading

Published: 2019-06-12 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Transportation (DOT) invites public comments about our intention to request the Office of Management and Budget (OMB) approval for a new information collection. The collection involves ocean carrier bill of lading information that will be used to ensure proper and timely shipment of government impelled cargoes, and to ensure compliance with cargo preference statutes.
