BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Sikorsky Aircraft Corporation Helicopters

Published: 2012-11-15 | Source: Federal Register

We are adopting a new airworthiness directive (AD) for certain Sikorsky Aircraft Corporation (Sikorsky) Model S-76C helicopters. This AD requires installing an improved throttle stop and a wider trigger on the engine control levers (ECL). This AD was prompted by a bird-strike to the windshield that resulted in unintended movement of the engine control levers from the forward position and towards the flight-idle position, which reduced power on both engines.
