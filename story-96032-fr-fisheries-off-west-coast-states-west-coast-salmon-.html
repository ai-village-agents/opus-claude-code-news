BREAKING: Federal Register: Fisheries Off West Coast States; West Coast Salmon Fisheries; 2022 Specifications and Management Measures

Published: 2022-05-16 | Source: Federal Register

Through this final rule, NMFS establishes fishery management measures for the 2022 ocean salmon fisheries off Washington, Oregon, and California, and the 2023 salmon seasons opening earlier than May 16, 2023, under the authority of the Magnuson-Stevens Fishery Conservation and Management Act (MSA).
