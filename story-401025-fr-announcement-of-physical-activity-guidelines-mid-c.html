BREAKING: Federal Register: Announcement of Physical Activity Guidelines Mid-Course Report Availability and Public Comment Period

Published: 2012-11-09 | Source: Federal Register

A subcommittee of the President's Council on Fitness, Sports and Nutrition (PCFSN) was convened to complete the PAG Mid- course Report.
