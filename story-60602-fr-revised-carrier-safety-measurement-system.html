BREAKING: Federal Register: Revised Carrier Safety Measurement System

Published: 2023-02-15 | Source: Federal Register

Since 2010, FMCSA has used its Safety Measurement System (SMS) to identify motor carriers for safety interventions. The National Research Council of the National Academy of Sciences (NAS) recommended on June 27, 2017, that FMCSA develop and test a new statistical model.
