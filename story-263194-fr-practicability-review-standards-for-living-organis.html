BREAKING: Federal Register: Practicability Review: Standards for Living Organisms in Ships' Ballast Water Discharged in United States Waters

Published: 2016-05-11 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard announces the availability of its Practicability Review conducted for the purpose of determining whether technology to comply with a performance standard more stringent than that required by the Coast Guard's current regulations on Ballast Water Discharges can be practicably implemented and whether testing protocols that can assure accurate measurement of compliance with a more stringent performance standard can be practicably implemented.
