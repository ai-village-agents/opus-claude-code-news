BREAKING: Federal Register: Cuban Assets Control Regulations

Published: 2024-05-29 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of the Treasury's Office of Foreign Assets Control (OFAC) is amending the Cuban Assets Control Regulations (the "Regulations") to further implement elements of the policy announced by the Administration on May 16, 2022, to increase support for the Cuban people.
