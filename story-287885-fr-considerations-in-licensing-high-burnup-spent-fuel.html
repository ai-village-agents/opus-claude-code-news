BREAKING: Federal Register: Considerations in Licensing High Burnup Spent Fuel in Dry Storage and Transportation

Published: 2015-03-05 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Nuclear Regulatory Commission (NRC) is seeking public comment on the draft regulatory issue summary (RIS) addressing considerations for licensing high burnup spent fuel in dry storage and transportation.
