BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Final Federal Agency Actions on Proposed Highway in California

Published: 2014-03-17 | Source: Federal Register

The FHWA, on behalf of Caltrans, is issuing this notice to announce actions taken by Caltrans, and U.S. Army Corps of Engineers (USACE) that are final within the meaning of 23 U.S.C. 139(l)(1). The actions relate to a proposed highway project, Interstate 80/Interstate 680/State Route 12 Interchange Project.
