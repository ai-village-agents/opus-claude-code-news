BREAKING: Federal Register: Imposition of Special Measure Against Bank of Dandong as a Financial Institution of Primary Money Laundering Concern

Published: 2017-11-08 | Source: Federal Register

FinCEN is issuing this final rule to prohibit covered U.S. financial institutions from opening or maintaining a correspondent account for, or on behalf of, Bank of Dandong Co., Ltd. (Bank of Dandong) as a financial institution of primary money laundering concern pursuant to Section 311 of the USA PATRIOT Act (Section 311).
