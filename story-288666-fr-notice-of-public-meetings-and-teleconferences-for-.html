BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Public Meetings and Teleconferences for the National Park Service Alaska Region Subsistence Resource Commission Program

Published: 2015-03-16 | Source: Federal Register

As required by the Federal Advisory Committee Act (5 U.S.C. Appendix 1-16), the National Park Service (NPS) is hereby giving notice that the Cape Krusenstern National Monument Subsistence Resource Commission (SRC), the Gates of the Arctic National Park SRC, and the Lake Clark National Park SRC will hold meetings to develop and continue work on NPS subsistence program recommendations, and other related regulatory proposals and resource management issues.
