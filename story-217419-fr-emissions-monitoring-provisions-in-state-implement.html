BREAKING: Federal Register: Emissions Monitoring Provisions in State Implementation Plans Required Under the NOX SIP Call

Published: 2018-09-27 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is proposing to update the regulations that were originally promulgated in 1998 to implement the NOX SIP Call.
