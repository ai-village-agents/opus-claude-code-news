On February 13, 2015, the Department of State published a list of goods and services produced by independent Cuban entrepreneurs that are eligible for importation into the United States, pursuant to Sec. 515.582 of the Department of the Treasury's Cuban Assets Control Regulations, 31 CFR part 515 ("CACR"). The State Department is issuing a Federal Register notice to this effect.
