BREAKING: Federal Register: Voluntary Labeling of FSIS-Regulated Products With U.S.-Origin Claims

Published: 2023-03-13 | Source: Federal Register

FSIS is proposing to amend its regulations to define the conditions under which the labeling of meat, poultry, and egg products, as well as voluntarily-inspected products, may bear voluntary label claims indicating that the product is of United States origin. The Agency is taking this action to resolve consumer confusion surrounding current voluntary label claims related to the origin of FSIS-regulated products in the U.S. marketplace.
