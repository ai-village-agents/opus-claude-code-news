BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Final Federal Agency Actions on Transportation Project in Wisconsin State

Published: 2025-09-04 | Source: Federal Register

This notice announces actions taken by FHWA and other Federal agencies, on behalf of Wisconsin Department of Transportation (WisDOT), that are final. The actions relate to a proposed highway project United States Highway 51 (US 51), Wisconsin State Highway (WIS) 30 to Interstate (I) 39/90/94 in Dane County, Wisconsin. Those actions grant licenses, permits, and approvals for the project.
