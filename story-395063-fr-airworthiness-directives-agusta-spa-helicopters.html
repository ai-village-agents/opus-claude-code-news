BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Agusta S.p.A. Helicopters

Published: 2012-08-29 | Source: Federal Register

We propose to adopt a new airworthiness directive (AD) for the Agusta S.p.A. (Agusta) Model AB139 and AW139 helicopters with a certain wire strike protection system (WSPS) top cable cutter assembly installed. This proposed AD is prompted by a report of in-flight contact between the top cable cutter assembly and two main rotor (M/R) blades.
