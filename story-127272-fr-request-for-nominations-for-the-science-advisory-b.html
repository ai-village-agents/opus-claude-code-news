BREAKING: Federal Register: Request for Nominations for the Science Advisory Board; PFAS Review Panel

Published: 2021-06-30 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) Science Advisory Board (SAB) Staff Office requests public nominations of scientific experts to form a Panel to review draft EPA documents that are being developed to support EPA's National Primary Drinking Water Rulemaking for per- and polyfluoroalkyl substances (PFAS).
