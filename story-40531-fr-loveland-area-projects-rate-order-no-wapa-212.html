Published: 2024-06-28 | Source: Federal Register

LAP consists of the Fryingpan-Arkansas Project (Fry-Ark) and the Pick-Sloan Missouri Basin Program (P-SMBP)--Western Division (WD), which were integrated for marketing and rate-making purposes in 1989.
