BREAKING: Federal Register: Certain Softwood Lumber Products From Canada: Final Results of Antidumping Duty Administrative Review; 2019; Correction

Published: 2025-12-22 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Department of Commerce (Commerce) published notice in the Federal Register on December 2, 2021, in which Commerce announced the final results of the 2019 administrative review of the antidumping duty (AD) order on certain softwood lumber products from Canada. In preparing the liquidation instructions, Commerce discovered that this notice inadvertently listed incorrect names for certain companies under review and incorrectly excluded certain company names.
