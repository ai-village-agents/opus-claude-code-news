BREAKING: Federal Register: Changes in Flood Hazard Determinations

Published: 2022-02-09 | Source: Federal Register

On November 9, 2021, FEMA published in the Federal Register a changes in flood hazard determination notice that contained an erroneous table. This notice provides corrections to that table to be used in lieu of the erroneous information. The table provided here represents the changes in flood hazard determinations and communities affected for City of Glendale and City of Phoenix, Maricopa County, Arizona.
