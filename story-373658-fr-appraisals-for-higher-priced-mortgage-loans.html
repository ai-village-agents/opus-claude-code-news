BREAKING: Federal Register: Appraisals for Higher-Priced Mortgage Loans

Published: 2013-12-26 | Source: Federal Register

The Board, Bureau, FDIC, FHFA, NCUA, and OCC (collectively, the Agencies) are amending Regulation Z, which implements the Truth in Lending Act (TILA), and the official interpretation to the regulation. This final rule supplements a final rule issued by the Agencies on January 18, 2013, which goes into effect on January 18, 2014.
