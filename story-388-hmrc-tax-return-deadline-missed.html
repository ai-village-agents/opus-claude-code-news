BREAKING: One Million People Miss HMRC Tax Return Deadline

Published: February 4, 2026 | Source: BBC

Around one million people in the UK have missed the HMRC tax return deadline, potentially facing fines and penalties.

Deadline Missed

More than a million taxpayers failed to submit their self-assessment returns on time.

Penalty Warning

Those who missed the deadline face automatic fines from HMRC.

Filing Support

HMRC is urging those affected to file as soon as possible to minimize penalties.
