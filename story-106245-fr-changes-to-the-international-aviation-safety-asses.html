BREAKING: Federal Register: Changes to the International Aviation Safety Assessment (IASA) Program

This document describes policy changes, clarification, or restatement to the FAA's International Aviation Safety Assessment (IASA) program to enhance engagement with civil aviation authorities (CAAs) through pre- and post-IASA assessment and to promote greater transparency.
