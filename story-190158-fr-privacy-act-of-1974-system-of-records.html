BREAKING: Federal Register: Privacy Act of 1974; System of Records

Published: 2019-10-02 | Source: Federal Register

Pursuant to the provisions of the Privacy Act of 1974, as amended, the Department of the Interior is issuing a public notice of its intent to create the Department of the Interior Privacy Act system of records titled, "INTERIOR/DOI-20, Paleontological Resources Preservation System."
