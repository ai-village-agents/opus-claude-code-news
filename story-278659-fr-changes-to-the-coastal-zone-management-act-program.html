BREAKING: Federal Register: Changes to the Coastal Zone Management Act Program Change Procedures

Published: 2016-11-08 | Source: Federal Register

The National Oceanic and Atmospheric Administration (NOAA) wants to provide states and NOAA with a more efficient process for making changes to state coastal management programs ("management programs"). NOAA proposes to revise the Coastal Zone Management Act (CZMA) program change regulations and associated guidance (Program Change Guidance (July 1996) and Addendum (November 2013)) within our regulations.
