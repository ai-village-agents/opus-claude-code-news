BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Trig Avionics Limited Transponders

Published: 2019-03-22 | Source: Federal Register

We propose to adopt a new airworthiness directive (AD) for certain Trig Avionics Limited TT31, Avidyne Corporation AXP340, and BendixKing/Honeywell International KT74 Mode S transponders. This proposed AD was prompted by the discovery that the retaining cam that engages in the mounting tray may not withstand g-forces experienced during an emergency landing.
