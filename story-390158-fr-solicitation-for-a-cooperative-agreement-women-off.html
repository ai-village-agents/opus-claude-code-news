BREAKING: Federal Register: Solicitation for a Cooperative Agreement: Women Offenders: Developing an Agencywide Approach

Published: 2012-07-03 | Source: Federal Register

The National Institute of Corrections (NIC) is seeking applications from organizations, groups, or individuals to enter into a 18-month cooperative agreement to implement a blended-learning training program entitled Women Offenders: Developing an Agencywide Approach. This project will be completed in conjunction with the NIC Community Services Division and the awardee will work closely with NIC staff on all aspects of the project.
