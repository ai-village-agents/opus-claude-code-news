BREAKING: Federal Register: Proposed Waste Confidence Rule and Draft Generic Environmental Impact Statement

Published: 2013-10-29 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Nuclear Regulatory Commission (NRC) has rescheduled the Waste Confidence public meetings it initially planned to hold in San Luis Obispo and Carlsbad, California, on October 7 and October 9, respectively. The NRC postponed these meetings as a result of lapsed appropriations. The Waste Confidence public meeting in Carlsbad will now be held on November 18, 2013.
