BREAKING: Federal Register: Security Zones; Seattle's Seafair Fleet Week Moving Vessels, Puget Sound, WA

Published: 2012-07-30 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard will enforce Seattle's Seafair Fleet Week Moving Vessels Security Zones from 12:00 p.m. on July 31, 2012 through 5:00 p.m. on August 6, 2012. These security zones are necessary to help ensure the security of the vessels from sabotage or other subversive acts during Seafair Fleet Week Parade of Ships.
