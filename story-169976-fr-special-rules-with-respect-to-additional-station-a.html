BREAKING: Federal Register: Special Rules With Respect to Additional Station and Signal Lights

Published: 2019-01-31 | Source: Federal Register

This final rule removes Department of the Navy regulations concerning Special Rules with Respect to Additional Station and Signal Lights. Public notification of U.S. Navy vessels which display additional station and signal lights is governed by statute and executive order which direct that such notice be published in the Federal Register.
