BREAKING: Federal Register: Final Environmental Impact Statement and Habitat Conservation Plan for Thurston County, Washington

Published: 2022-05-13 | Source: Federal Register

We, the U.S. Fish and Wildlife Service (Service), announce the availability of a final environmental impact statement (FEIS), which analyzes a proposed habitat conservation plan (HCP) developed by Thurston County, Washington (applicant, or the County). This FEIS was prepared jointly by the Service and Thurston County to satisfy both the National Environmental Policy Act and the Washington State Environmental Policy Act.
