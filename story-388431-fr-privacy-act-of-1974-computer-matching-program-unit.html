BREAKING: Federal Register: Privacy Act of 1974, Computer Matching Program: United States Postal Service and the Defense Manpower Data Center, Department of Defense

Published: 2012-06-13 | Source: Federal Register

The United States Postal Service[supreg] (Postal Service[supreg]) plans to participate as the recipient agency in a computer matching program with the Defense Manpower Data Center (DMDC), Department of Defense (DoD), as the source agency.
