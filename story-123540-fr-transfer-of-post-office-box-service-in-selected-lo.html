BREAKING: Federal Register: Transfer of Post Office Box Service in Selected Locations to the Competitive Product List

Published: 2021-05-11 | Source: Federal Register

The Postal Service hereby provides notice that Post Office BoxTM service for approximately 237 locations will be reassigned from their market-dominant fee groups to competitive fee groups.
