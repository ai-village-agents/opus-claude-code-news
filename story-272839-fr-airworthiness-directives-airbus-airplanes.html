BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Airbus Airplanes

Published: 2016-08-31 | Source: Federal Register

We propose to adopt a new airworthiness directive (AD) for all Airbus Model A330-200 Freighter, -200, and -300 series airplanes; and Airbus Model A340-500, and -600 series airplanes. This proposed AD was prompted by reports that non-conforming aluminum alloy was used to manufacture several structural parts on the inboard flap.
