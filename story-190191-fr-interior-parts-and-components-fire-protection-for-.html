BREAKING: Federal Register: Interior Parts and Components Fire Protection for Transport Category Airplanes

Published: 2019-10-02 | Source: Federal Register

This action extends the comment period for an NPRM that was published in the Federal Register on July 3, 2019. In the NPRM, the FAA proposed to amend certain airworthiness regulations for fire protection of interior compartments on transport category airplanes. The proposal would convert those flammability regulations from detailed, prescriptive requirements into simpler, performance-based standards.
