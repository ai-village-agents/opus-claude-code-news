BREAKING: Federal Register: United States Enrichment Corporation, Paducah Gaseous Diffusion Plant

Published: 2013-10-31 | Source: Federal Register

On April 2, 2013, the United States Enrichment Corporation (USEC) filed an application with the U.S. Nuclear Regulatory Commission (NRC) for the renewal of its Certificate of Compliance (CoC) for the Paducah Gaseous Diffusion Plant (PGDP). The current CoC for PGDP is set to expire on December 31, 2013.
