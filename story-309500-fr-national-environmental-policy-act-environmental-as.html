BREAKING: Federal Register: National Environmental Policy Act; Environmental Assessments for Tobacco Products; Categorical Exclusions; Correction

Published: 2015-11-16 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA or we) is correcting the preamble to a final rule that appeared in the Federal Register of September 24, 2015. This final rule provided FDA with categorical exclusions from the requirement to prepare environmental assessments for certain actions regarding the marketing of tobacco products under the Family Smoking Prevention and Tobacco Control Act (Tobacco Control Act).
