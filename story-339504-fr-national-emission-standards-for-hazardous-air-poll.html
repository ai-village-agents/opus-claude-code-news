BREAKING: Federal Register: National Emission Standards for Hazardous Air Pollutants; Delegation of Authority to Arkansas

Published: 2014-11-12 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is approving, through a "direct final" procedure, the straight delegation of authority and approval of the mechanism used for the implementation and enforcement of certain unchanged Federal section 112 rules to the Arkansas Department of Environmental Quality (ADEQ), pursuant to section 112(l) of the Clean Air Act (Act or CAA).
