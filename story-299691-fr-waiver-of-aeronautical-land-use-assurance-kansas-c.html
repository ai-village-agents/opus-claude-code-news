BREAKING: Federal Register: Waiver of Aeronautical Land-Use Assurance: Kansas City International Airport (MCI), Kansas City, MO

Published: 2015-07-23 | Source: Federal Register

The Federal Aviation Administration (FAA) is considering a proposal from the Kansas City Aviation Department (sponsor), Kansas City, MO, to release a 14.94 acre parcel of land from the federal obligation dedicating it to aeronautical use and to authorize this parcel to be used for revenue-producing, non- aeronautical purposes.
