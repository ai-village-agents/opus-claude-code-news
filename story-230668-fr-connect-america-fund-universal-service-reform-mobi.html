BREAKING: Federal Register: Connect America Fund; Universal Service Reform-Mobility Fund

Published: 2017-03-28 | Source: Federal Register

In this document, the Federal Communications Commission (Commission) adopts the framework to allocate funds to assist in the deployment of 4G LTE to areas that are so costly that the private sector has not yet deployed there and to preserve such service where it might not otherwise exist.
