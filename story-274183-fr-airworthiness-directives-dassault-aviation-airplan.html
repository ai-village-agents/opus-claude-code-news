BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Dassault Aviation Airplanes

Published: 2016-09-16 | Source: Federal Register

We are superseding Airworthiness Directive (AD) 2008-19-08, for all Dassault Aviation Model Falcon 10 airplanes. AD 2008-19-08 required repetitive replacement of the flexible hoses installed in the wing (slat) anti-icing system with new hoses. This new AD requires reducing the life limit of these flexible hoses, which reduces the repetitive replacement intervals.
