BREAKING: Federal Register: Alternatives to the Use of Credit Ratings

Published: 2020-12-21 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Nuclear Regulatory Commission (NRC) is considering an amendment to its regulations that would alter financial assurance mechanisms approved by the NRC for the decommissioning of nuclear power plants and other nuclear facilities. Specifically, this action would amend provisions for parent company and self company guarantees that require bond ratings issued by credit rating agencies.
