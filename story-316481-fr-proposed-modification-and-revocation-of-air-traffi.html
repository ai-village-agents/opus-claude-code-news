BREAKING: Federal Register: Proposed Modification and Revocation of Air Traffic Service (ATS) Routes; Northcentral United States

Published: 2014-02-13 | Source: Federal Register

This action proposes to modify three jet routes and four VOR Federal airways and to remove three jet routes in the northcentral United States. The FAA is proposing this action due to the scheduled decommissioning of the Peck, MI, VHF Omnidirectional Range (VOR), which provides navigation guidance for portions of the affected routes.
