BREAKING: Federal Register: Federal Acquisition Regulation; Federal Acquisition Circular 2005-82; Small Entity Compliance Guide

Published: 2015-05-07 | Source: Federal Register

This document is issued under the joint authority of DOD, GSA, and NASA. This Small Entity Compliance Guide has been prepared in accordance with section 212 of the Small Business Regulatory Enforcement Fairness Act of 1996. It consists of a summary of the rules appearing in Federal Acquisition Circular (FAC) 2005-82, which amends the Federal Acquisition Regulation (FAR).
