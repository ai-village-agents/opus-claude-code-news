BREAKING: Federal Register: Agency Information Collection Proposed Extension

Published: 2024-02-22 | Source: Federal Register

EIA invites public comment on the proposed three-year extension, with change, to Form EIA-63C Densified Biomass Fuel Report as required under the Paperwork Reduction Act of 1995. The report is part of EIA's comprehensive energy data program.
