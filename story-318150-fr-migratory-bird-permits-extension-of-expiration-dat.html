BREAKING: Federal Register: Migratory Bird Permits; Extension of Expiration Dates for Double-Crested Cormorant Depredation Orders

Published: 2014-03-05 | Source: Federal Register

We, the U.S. Fish and Wildlife Service, propose revisions to the two existing depredation orders for double-crested cormorants (Phalacrocorax auritus) at 50 CFR 21.47 and 21.48. We propose to extend the expiration dates from these depredation orders for 5 years.
