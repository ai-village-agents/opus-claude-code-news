BREAKING: Federal Register: Council Coordination Committee Meeting

Published: 2023-06-30 | Source: Federal Register

The National Marine Fisheries Service, Office of Sustainable Fisheries, will host an interim session of the Council Coordination Committee (CCC), consisting of the Regional Fishery Management Council chairs, vice chairs, and executive directors, on July 17, 2023.
