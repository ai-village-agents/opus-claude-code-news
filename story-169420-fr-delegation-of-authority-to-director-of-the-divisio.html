BREAKING: Federal Register: Delegation of Authority to Director of the Division of Enforcement

Published: 2020-12-29 | Source: Federal Register

The Securities and Exchange Commission ("Commission") is revising its regulations with respect to the delegations of authority to the Director of the Division of Enforcement. These revisions are the result of the Commission's experience with its nonpublic investigations, litigation in Federal court, and disgorgement and Fair Fund plans in administrative and cease-and-desist proceedings instituted by the Commission.
