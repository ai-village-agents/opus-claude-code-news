BREAKING: Federal Register: Port Access Route Study: Approaches to the Ports of Puerto Rico and U.S. Virgin Islands

Published: 2023-04-07 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is conducting a Port Access Route Study to evaluate safe access routes for the movement of vessel traffic proceeding to or from the ports or places in the Commonwealth of Puerto Rico and the U.S. Virgin Islands and to determine whether shipping safety fairways and/or routing measures should be established, adjusted, or modified.
