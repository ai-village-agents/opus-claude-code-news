BREAKING: Federal Register: Establishment of Homeland Security Academic Partnership Council Subcommittees

Published: 2023-11-22 | Source: Federal Register

On November 14, 2023, the Secretary of DHS, Alejandro N. Mayorkas, issued three memoranda tasking the Homeland Security Academic Partnership Council (HSAPC) to establish three subcommittees further outlined below. This notice is not a solicitation for membership.
