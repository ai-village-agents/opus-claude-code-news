BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone; San Juan Harbor, San Juan, PR

Published: 2019-12-17 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is proposing to revise an existing moving safety zone for San Juan Harbor, San Juan, Puerto Rico. The proposed revisions would expand the existing moving safety zone to include an adjacent berthing; add a 50-yard radius around moored liquefied natural gas and liquefied petroleum gas carriers; and update terminal names.
