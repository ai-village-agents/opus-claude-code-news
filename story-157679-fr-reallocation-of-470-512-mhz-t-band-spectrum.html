BREAKING: Federal Register: Reallocation of 470-512 MHz (T-Band) Spectrum

Published: 2020-07-31 | Source: Federal Register

In this document, the Commission seeks comment on reallocating spectrum associated with broadcast television channels 14-20 (470-512 MHz or T-Band), assigning new licenses by auction for the 6 megahertz to 18 megahertz of spectrum that is potentially available in each of the eleven urbanized areas, and relocating "public safety eligibles" from the T-Band.
