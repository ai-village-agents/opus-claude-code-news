BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Pratt & Whitney Turbofan Engines

Published: 2021-08-27 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA proposes to supersede Airworthiness Directive (AD) 2011-07-02, which applies to all Pratt & Whitney (P&W) JT8D-209, JT8D- 217, JT8D-217A, JT8D-217C, and JT8D-219 model turbofan engines. AD 2011-07-02 requires initial and repetitive torque inspections of the 3rd-stage and 4th-stage low-pressure turbine (LPT) blades.
