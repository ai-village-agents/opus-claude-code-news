BREAKING: Federal Register: Yavapai County Resource Advisory Committee

Published: 2012-08-08 | Source: Federal Register

The Yavapai County Resource Advisory Committee (RAC) will meet in Prescott, Arizona. The committee is meeting as authorized under the Secure Rural Schools and Community Self-Determination Act (Pub. L 110- 343) and in compliance with the Federal Advisory Committee Act. The purpose of the meeting is to discuss the 1-year extension and select projects for the Yavapai County RAC.
