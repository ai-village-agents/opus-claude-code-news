BREAKING: Federal Register: Charter Renewal for the Advisory Council on Blood Stem Cell Transplantation

Published: 2021-02-26 | Source: Federal Register

In accordance with the Federal Advisory Committee Act, HHS is hereby giving notice that the Advisory Council on Blood Stem Cell Transplantation (ACBSCT or Council) has been rechartered. The effective date of the recharter is February 19, 2021.
