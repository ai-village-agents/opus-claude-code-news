BREAKING: Federal Register: Special Local Regulations and Safety Zones; Marine Events in Captain of the Port Long Island Sound Zone

Published: 2014-08-12 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is establishing two special local regulations for two separate marine events and establishing three safety zones for two fireworks displays and one swim event within the Captain of the Port (COTP) Long Island Sound (LIS) Zone. This temporary final rule is necessary to provide for the safety of life on navigable waters during these events.
