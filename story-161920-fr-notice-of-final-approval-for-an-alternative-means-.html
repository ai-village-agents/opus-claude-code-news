BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Final Approval for an Alternative Means of Emission Limitation

Published: 2020-09-24 | Source: Federal Register

This document announces our approval of the alternative means of emission limitation (AMEL) request under the Clean Air Act (CAA) submitted by Lyondell Chemical Company (Lyondell) to operate multi- point ground flares (MPGFs) at its Channelview chemical plant in Houston, Texas. The U.S. Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) received no adverse comments on the request.
