BREAKING: Federal Register: Supervisory Highlights: Fall 2016

Published: 2016-11-22 | Source: Federal Register

In this issue of Supervisory Highlights, we report examination findings in the areas of auto originations, automobile loan servicing, debt collection, mortgage origination, student loan servicing, and fair lending.
