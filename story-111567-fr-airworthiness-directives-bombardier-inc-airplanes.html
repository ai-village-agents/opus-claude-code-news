BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Bombardier, Inc., Airplanes

Published: 2022-12-05 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA is adopting a new airworthiness directive (AD) for certain Bombardier, Inc., Model BD-700-2A12 airplanes. This AD was prompted by the investigation of erroneous radio altimeter data that was displayed on an in-service airplane. It was revealed that certain radio altimeter coaxial cables used by the radio altimeter systems, in the aft fuselage equipment bay, were damaged.
