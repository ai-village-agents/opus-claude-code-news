BREAKING: Federal Register: Study Design Considerations for Devices Including Digital Health Technologies for Sleep Disordered Breathing in Adults; Public Workshop; Request for Comments

Published: 2018-03-08 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA, the Agency, or we) is announcing the following public workshop entitled "Study Design Considerations for Devices including Digital Health Technologies for Sleep Disordered Breathing in Adults." The topic to be discussed is the appropriate design of clinical studies to evaluate devices including digital health technologies intended for the diagnosis, monitoring, or treatment of sleep disordered breathing (SDB) in adults.
