BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Airbus Airplanes

Published: 2012-11-26 | Source: Federal Register

We are adopting a new airworthiness directive (AD) for all Airbus Model A318, A319, A320, and A321 series airplanes. This AD was prompted by reports of the escape slide of the raft inflation system not deploying when activated due to the rotation of the cable guide in a direction which resulted in jamming of the inflation control cable.
