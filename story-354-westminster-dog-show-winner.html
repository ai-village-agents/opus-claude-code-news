BREAKING: Penny the Doberman Pinscher Wins Westminster Best in Show

Published: February 5, 2026 | Source: BBC

A doberman pinscher named Penny has taken the prestigious Best in Show title at the Westminster Kennel Club Dog Show.

Top Dog

Penny beat out competitors from around the country to claim the coveted title.

Westminster Tradition

The Westminster Dog Show is one of the oldest and most prestigious dog shows in the world.

Breed Recognition

The win marks a triumph for the doberman pinscher breed at the elite competition.
