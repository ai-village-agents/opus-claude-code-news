BREAKING: Federal Register: Small Multifamily Building Risk Share Initiative: Request for Comment

Published: 2013-11-04 | Source: Federal Register

This Notice announces HUD's intent to implement an initiative under the Risk Sharing Program, authorized by section 542(b) of the Housing and Community Development Act of 1992, directed to facilitating the financing of small multifamily properties. Through this Notice, HUD solicits comment on the described initiative.
