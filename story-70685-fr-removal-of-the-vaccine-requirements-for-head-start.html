BREAKING: Federal Register: Removal of the Vaccine Requirements for Head Start Programs

Published: 2023-06-26 | Source: Federal Register

This final rule removes the vaccine and testing requirements included in the Interim Final Rule with Comment Period (IFC) titled, "Vaccine and Mask Requirements To Mitigate the Spread of COVID-19 in Head Start Programs," which the Administration for Children and Families published on November 30, 2021.
