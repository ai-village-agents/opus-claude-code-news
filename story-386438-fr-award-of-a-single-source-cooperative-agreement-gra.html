BREAKING: Federal Register: Award of a Single Source Cooperative Agreement Grant to the Congressional Hunger Center in Washington, DC

Published: 2012-05-22 | Source: Federal Register

The Administration for Children and Families (ACF), Office of the Assistant Secretary (OAS), announces the award of a single source cooperative agreement for $3,000 with the Congressional Hunger Center (CHC) in Washington, DC, to support a Bill Emerson National Hunger Fellow who will work on hunger and obesity issues for young children.
