BREAKING: Federal Register: Olives Grown in California; Decreased Assessment Rate

Published: 2013-07-30 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Agriculture (USDA) is adopting, as a final rule, without change, an interim rule that decreased the assessment rate established for the California Olive Committee (Committee) for the 2013 and subsequent fiscal years from $31.32 to $21.16 per ton of assessable olives handled. The Committee locally administers the marketing order for olives grown in California.
