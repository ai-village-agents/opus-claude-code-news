BREAKING: Federal Register: Federal Management Regulation; Art in Architecture

Published: 2022-02-02 | Source: Federal Register

GSA is issuing a final rule amending the Federal Management Regulation (FMR) to update certain provisions of the Art in Architecture program. These revisions clarify the policies to collect, manage, fund, and commission visual art in Federal buildings. The rule updates policies consistent with the requirements of the Executive Order (E.O.) issued May 14, 2021, titled "Revocation of Certain Presidential Actions and Technical Amendment."
