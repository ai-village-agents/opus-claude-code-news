BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Solicitation of Applications for the Repowering Assistance Program

Published: 2018-04-20 | Source: Federal Register

This Notice announces the solicitation of applications for up to $5 Million of available funding under the Repowering Assistance Program to encourage the use of renewable biomass as a replacement fuel source for fossil fuels used to provide process heat or power in the operation of eligible biorefineries. To be eligible for payments, biorefineries must have been in existence on or before June 18, 2008.
