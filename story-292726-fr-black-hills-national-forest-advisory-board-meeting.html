BREAKING: Federal Register: Black Hills National Forest Advisory Board Meeting

Published: 2015-05-01 | Source: Federal Register

The Black Hills National Forest Advisory Board (Board) will meet in Rapid City, South Dakota. The Board is established consistent with the Federal Advisory Committee Act of 1972, the Forest and Rangeland Renewable Resources Planning Act of 1974, the National Forest Management Act of 1976, and the Federal Public Lands Recreation Enhancement Act.
