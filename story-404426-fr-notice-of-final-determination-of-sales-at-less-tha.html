BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Final Determination of Sales at Less Than Fair Value: Large Residential Washers From the Republic of Korea

Published: 2012-12-26 | Source: Federal Register

We determine that imports of large residential washers (washers) from the Republic of Korea (Korea) are being, or are likely to be, sold in the United States at less than fair value (LTFV), as provided in section 735 of the Tariff Act of 1930, as amended (the Act). Based on our analysis of the comments received, we have made changes in the margin calculations. Therefore, the final determination differs from the preliminary determination.
