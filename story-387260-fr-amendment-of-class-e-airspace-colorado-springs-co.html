BREAKING: Federal Register: Amendment of Class E Airspace; Colorado Springs, CO

Published: 2012-06-01 | Source: Federal Register

This action delays the effective date for the amendment of Class E airspace at City of Colorado Springs Municipal Airport, Colorado Springs, CO, until September 20, 2012. The FAA is taking this action to allow additional time for processing and charting.
