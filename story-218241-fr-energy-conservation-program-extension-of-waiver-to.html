BREAKING: Federal Register: Energy Conservation Program: Extension of Waiver to Apple Inc. From the Department of Energy External Power Supply Test Procedure

Published: 2018-10-10 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Department of Energy ("DOE") is granting a waiver extension (Case No. 2018-005) to Apple Inc. ("Apple") to waive certain requirements of the DOE external power supply test procedure for determining the energy efficiency of the Apple brand external power supply basic model A1882.
