BREAKING: Federal Register: National Organic Program (NOP); Amendment to the National List of Allowed and Prohibited Substances (Livestock)

Published: 2012-09-19 | Source: Federal Register

This final rule amends the U.S. Department of Agriculture's (USDA) National List of Allowed and Prohibited Substances (National List) to enact one recommendation submitted to the Secretary of Agriculture (Secretary) by the National Organic Standards Board (NOSB) on April 29, 2010. This final rule revises the annotation for one substance on the National List, methionine, to reduce the maximum levels of synthetic methionine allowed in organic poultry production after October 1, 2012.
