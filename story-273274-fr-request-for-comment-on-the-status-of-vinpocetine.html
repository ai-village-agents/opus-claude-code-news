BREAKING: Federal Register: Request for Comment on the Status of Vinpocetine

Published: 2016-09-07 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA or we) is requesting comments related to the regulatory status of vinpocetine. Specifically, we request comments on our tentative conclusion that vinpocetine is not a dietary ingredient and is excluded from the definition of dietary supplement in the Federal Food, Drug, and Cosmetic Act (FD&C Act).
