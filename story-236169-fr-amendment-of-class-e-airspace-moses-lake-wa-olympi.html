BREAKING: Federal Register: Amendment of Class E Airspace; Moses Lake, WA; Olympia, WA

Published: 2017-06-07 | Source: Federal Register

This action withdraws the final rule, technical amendment published in the Federal Register on April 25, 2017. In that action, the FAA amended Class E Airspace at Grant County International Airport, Moses Lake, WA, and Olympia Regional Airport, Olympia, WA.
