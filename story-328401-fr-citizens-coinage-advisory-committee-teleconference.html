BREAKING: Federal Register: Citizens Coinage Advisory Committee; Teleconference

Published: 2014-07-01 | Source: Federal Register

Pursuant to United States Code, Title 31, section 5135(b)(8)(C), the United States Mint announces the Citizens Coinage Advisory Committee (CCAC) public meeting scheduled for July 8, 2014. Date: July 8, 2014. Time: 1:00 p.m. to 3:30 p.m. EDT. Location: This meeting will occur via teleconference. Interested members of the public may attend the meeting at the United States Mint; 801 9th Street NW., Washington, DC; Conference Room A.
