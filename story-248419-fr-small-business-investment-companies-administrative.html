BREAKING: Federal Register: Small Business Investment Companies-Administrative Fees

Published: 2017-11-13 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Small Business Administration (SBA) is revising its regulations to increase the Small Business Investment Company (SBIC) licensing and examination fees. The Small Business Investment Act of 1958, as amended, allows SBA to collect licensing and examination fees to offset SBA's costs associated with the administration of these two activities.
