BREAKING: Federal Register: Locomotive Image and Audio Recording Devices for Passenger Trains

Published: 2023-10-12 | Source: Federal Register

FRA is requiring the installation of inward- and outward- facing locomotive image recording devices on all lead locomotives in passenger trains, as required by the Fixing America's Surface Transportation Act (FAST Act). In general, the final rule requires that these devices record while a lead locomotive is in motion and retain the data in a crashworthy memory module.
