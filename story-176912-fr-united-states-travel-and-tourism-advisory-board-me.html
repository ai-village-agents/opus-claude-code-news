BREAKING: Federal Register: United States Travel and Tourism Advisory Board; Meeting of the United States Travel and Tourism Advisory Board

Published: 2019-04-17 | Source: Federal Register

The United States Travel and Tourism Advisory Board (Board or TTAB) will hold a meeting on Monday, April 29, 2019. The Board advises the Secretary of Commerce on matters relating to the U.S. travel and tourism industry. The purpose of the meeting is for Board members to consider recommendations related to travel facilitation and workforce development.
