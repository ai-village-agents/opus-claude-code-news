BREAKING: Federal Register: Emergency Import Restrictions Imposed on Categories of Archaeological and Ethnological Material of Ukraine

Published: 2024-09-10 | Source: Federal Register

This document amends the U.S. Customs and Border Protection (CBP) regulations to reflect the imposition of emergency import restrictions on categories of archaeological and ethnological material of Ukraine, pursuant to a determination made by the United States Department of State under the terms of the Convention on Cultural Property Implementation Act.
