BREAKING: Federal Register: United States Investment Advisory Council; Meeting

Published: 2022-09-07 | Source: Federal Register

In accordance with the Federal Advisory Committee Act (FACA), this notice announces, the United States Investment Advisory Council (IAC) will hold a public meeting on September 29, 2022. In August 2022, U.S. Secretary of Commerce Gina M. Raimondo appointed a new cohort of members who will serve two-year terms.
