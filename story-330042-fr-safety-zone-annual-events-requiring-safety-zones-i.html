BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone; Annual Events Requiring Safety Zones in the Captain of the Port Detroit Zone

Published: 2014-07-21 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard will enforce the safety zone on Lake Erie in Cedar Point, Ohio, for the Revolution 3 Triathlon in Cedar Point, Ohio. This zone will be enforced from 6 a.m. until 10 a.m. on each day of September 6 and 7, 2014. This action is necessary and intended to ensure safety of life on navigable waters during the Revolution 3 Triathlon.
