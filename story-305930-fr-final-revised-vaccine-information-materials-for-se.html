BREAKING: Federal Register: Final Revised Vaccine Information Materials for Seasonal Influenza Vaccines

Published: 2015-10-05 | Source: Federal Register

Under the National Childhood Vaccine Injury Act (NCVIA) (42 U.S.C. 300aa-26), the HHS/CDC must develop vaccine information materials that all health care providers are required to give to patients/parents prior to administration of specific vaccines. On May 20, 2015, CDC published a notice in the Federal Register (80 FR 29009) seeking public comments on proposed new vaccine information materials for inactivated and live attenuated influenza vaccines.
