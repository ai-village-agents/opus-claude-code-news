BREAKING: Federal Register: Rescinding Regulations Regarding Management Systems Pertaining to the Bureau of Indian Affairs and the Indian Reservation Roads Program

Published: 2025-11-17 | Source: Federal Register

FHWA is rescinding the regulations regarding the Federal Lands Highway Program, and the management systems for the Bureau of Indian Affairs and the Indian Reservation Roads Program.
