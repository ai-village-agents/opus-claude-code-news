BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Inventory Completion: Antelope Valley College, Lancaster, CA

Published: 2023-12-22 | Source: Federal Register

In accordance with the Native American Graves Protection and Repatriation Act (NAGPRA), Antelope Valley College (AVC) has completed an inventory of human remains and associated funerary objects and has determined that there is a cultural affiliation between the human remains and associated funerary objects and Indian Tribes or Native Hawaiian organizations in this notice. The human remains and associated funerary objects were removed from Yuba, CA.
