BREAKING: Federal Register: Kiwifruit Grown in California; Decreased Assessment Rate

Published: 2013-10-23 | Source: Federal Register

This rule decreases the assessment rate established for the Kiwifruit Administrative Committee (Committee) for the 2013-14 and subsequent fiscal periods from $0.035 to $0.025 per 9-kilo volume-fill container or equivalent of kiwifruit. The Committee locally administers the marketing order, which regulates the handling of kiwifruit grown in California.
