BREAKING: Federal Register: Privacy Act of 1974; System of Records

Published: 2022-12-16 | Source: Federal Register

In accordance with the Privacy Act of 1974, the DoD is establishing a new Department-wide system of records titled, "Enterprise Identity, Credential, and Access Management (ICAM) Records, DoD-0015."
