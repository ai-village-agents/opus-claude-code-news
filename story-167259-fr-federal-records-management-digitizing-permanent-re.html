BREAKING: Federal Register: Federal Records Management: Digitizing Permanent Records and Reviewing Records Schedules

Published: 2020-12-01 | Source: Federal Register

We are proposing to amend our electronic records management regulations to add a subpart containing standards for digitizing permanent Federal records so that agencies may dispose of the original source records, where appropriate and in accordance with the Federal Records Act amendments of 2014.
