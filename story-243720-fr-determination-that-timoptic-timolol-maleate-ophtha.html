BREAKING: Federal Register: Determination That TIMOPTIC (Timolol Maleate Ophthalmic Solution), 0.25 Percent and 0.5 Percent, Was Not Withdrawn From Sale for Reasons of Safety or Effectiveness

Published: 2017-09-15 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA or Agency) has determined that TIMOPTIC (timolol maleate ophthalmic solution), 0.25 percent and 0.5 percent, was not withdrawn from sale for reasons of safety or effectiveness.
