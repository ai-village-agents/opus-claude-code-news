BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Intent and Request for Information Regarding Establishment of a Transmission Facilitation Program

Published: 2022-05-12 | Source: Federal Register

The Infrastructure Investment and Jobs Act (IIJA or the Act) directs the Secretary of Energy (Secretary) to establish a program, to be known as the "Transmission Facilitation Program" or "TFP," under which the Secretary shall facilitate the construction of electric power transmission lines and related facilities.
