BREAKING: Federal Register: Proposed Amendment of Class C Airspace; El Paso International Airport, TX

Published: 2016-08-17 | Source: Federal Register

This action proposes to modify Class C airspace at El Paso International Airport, El Paso, TX, by removing a cutout from the Class C airspace area that excludes the airspace within a 2-mile radius of West Texas Airport and the airspace beyond an 8-mile arc from the El Paso International Airport beginning at the 115[deg] bearing from the airport clockwise to the Rio Grande River.
