BREAKING: Federal Register: State Highway-Rail Grade Crossing Action Plans

Published: 2019-11-07 | Source: Federal Register

FRA is issuing this proposed rule in response to a mandate from the Fixing America's Surface Transportation Act to issue a rule requiring 40 States and the District of Columbia to develop and implement highway-rail grade crossing action plans.
