BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Public Meeting of the Indiana Advisory Committee to the U.S. Commission on Civil Rights

Published: 2019-11-08 | Source: Federal Register

Notice is hereby given, pursuant to the provisions of the rules and regulations of the U.S. Commission on Civil Rights (Commission) and the Federal Advisory Committee Act that the Indiana Advisory Committee (Committee) will hold community forum on Saturday, November 16, 2019, from 2:30-5:30 p.m. Eastern Time for the purpose of discussing the civil rights implications of indoor and outdoor lead exposure in the state.
