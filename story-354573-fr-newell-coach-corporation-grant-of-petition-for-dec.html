BREAKING: Federal Register: Newell Coach Corporation, Grant of Petition for Decision of Inconsequential Noncompliance

Published: 2013-05-10 | Source: Federal Register

Newell Coach Corporation (Newell) has determined that certain motor homes that it manufactured between June 17, 1996 and August 26, 2008, do not fully comply with paragraph S5.3 of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard (FMVSS) No. 120 Tire Selection and Rims for Motor Vehicles with a GVWR of More than 4,536 Kilograms (10,000 pounds).
