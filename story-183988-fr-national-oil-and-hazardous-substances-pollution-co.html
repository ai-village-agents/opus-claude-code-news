BREAKING: Federal Register: National Oil and Hazardous Substances Pollution Contingency Plan; National Priorities List

Published: 2019-07-15 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is reopening the comment period for a proposed rule published on July 31, 2018. In the July 31, 2018 notice of proposed rulemaking, EPA proposed to delete portions of the South Valley Superfund Site from the National Priorities List. EPA is reopening the comment period subsequent to a meeting requested by commenters to discuss the partial deletion activities.
