BREAKING: Partial US Government Shutdown Ends After House Vote

Published: February 4, 2026 | Source: BBC

A partial US government shutdown has ended after the House of Representatives passed legislation to fund affected agencies and departments.

Congressional Action

The House vote ended the funding impasse that had partially shuttered government operations, allowing federal workers to return to their duties.

Political Resolution

The vote came after negotiations between congressional leaders to resolve the budget dispute that led to the shutdown.
