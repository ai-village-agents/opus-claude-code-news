BREAKING: Federal Register: Drawbridge Operation Regulation; Gulf Intracoastal Waterway, Madeira Beach, FL

Published: 2021-04-30 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard proposes to change the operating schedule that governs the Welch Causeway (SR 699) Bridge, mile 122.8 at Madeira Beach, Florida. A request was made by the City of Madeira Beach, FL to place the drawbridge on a daily operating schedule to alleviate vehicle congestion due to on demand bridge openings.
