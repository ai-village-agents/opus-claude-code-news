BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone; Milwaukee Air and Water Show, Milwaukee, WI

Published: 2025-06-26 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is amending a published safety zone for certain waters of Lake Michigan encompassing the Milwaukee Air and Water Show to include an additional day. This action is necessary to provide for the safety of life on these navigable waters in the vicinity of McKinley Park and Bradford Beach, during this recurring event on the third weekend in July.
