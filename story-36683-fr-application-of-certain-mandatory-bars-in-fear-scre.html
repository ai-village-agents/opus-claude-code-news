BREAKING: Federal Register: Application of Certain Mandatory Bars in Fear Screenings

Published: 2024-05-13 | Source: Federal Register

DHS proposes to allow asylum officers ("AOs") to consider the potential applicability of certain bars to asylum and statutory withholding of removal during certain fear screenings. Specifically, under this proposed rule, AOs would be authorized to consider certain bars during credible and reasonable fear screenings, including credible fear screenings where the Circumvention of Lawful Pathways ("CLP") rule applies.
