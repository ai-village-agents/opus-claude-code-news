BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Availability of Draft NPDES General Permits for Dewatering and Remediation Activity Discharges in Massachusetts and New Hampshire, Federal Facilities in Vermont, and Indian Country in Connecticut and Rhode Island: The Dewatering and Remediation General Permit

Published: 2022-04-26 | Source: Federal Register

The Director of the Water Division, U.S. Environmental Protection Agency--Region 1 (EPA), is providing a notice of availability of draft National Pollutant Discharge Elimination System (NPDES) general permits for discharges from sites engaged in certain dewatering and remediation activities to certain waters in the Commonwealth of Massachusetts and the State of New Hampshire, sites in Connecticut and Rhode Island located on Indian Country lands, and federal facilities in Vermont.
