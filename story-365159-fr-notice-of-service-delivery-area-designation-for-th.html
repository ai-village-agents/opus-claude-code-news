BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Service Delivery Area Designation for the Shinnecock Indian Nation

Published: 2013-09-11 | Source: Federal Register

This Notice advises the public that the Indian Health Service (IHS) proposes the geographic boundaries of the Service Delivery Area (SDA) for the newly recognized Shinnecock Indian Nation. The Shinnecock Indian Nation SDA is to be comprised of Nassau and Suffolk counties in the State of New York.
