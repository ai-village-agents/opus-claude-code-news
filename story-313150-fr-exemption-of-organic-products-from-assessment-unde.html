BREAKING: Federal Register: Exemption of Organic Products From Assessment Under a Commodity Promotion Law

Published: 2015-12-31 | Source: Federal Register

This rule implements the provisions of section 10004 of the Agricultural Act of 2014 and modifies the organic assessment exemption regulations under 23 Federal marketing orders and 22 research and promotion programs (commodity promotion programs).
