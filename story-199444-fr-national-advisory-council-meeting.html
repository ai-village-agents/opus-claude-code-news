BREAKING: Federal Register: National Advisory Council; Meeting

Published: 2018-02-07 | Source: Federal Register

The Federal Emergency Management Agency (FEMA) National Advisory Council (NAC) will meet in person and remotely via teleconference on Friday, February 23, 2018, in Washington, DC. The meeting will be open to the public.
