BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Ameri-King Corporation Emergency Locator Transmitters

Published: 2016-06-03 | Source: Federal Register

We propose to adopt a new airworthiness directive (AD) for certain Ameri-King Corporation emergency locator transmitters (ELTs) as installed on various aircraft. This proposed AD was prompted by multiple reports of ELT failure. This proposed AD was also prompted by a report of noncompliance to quality standards and manufacturer processes related to Ameri-King Corporation ELTs.
