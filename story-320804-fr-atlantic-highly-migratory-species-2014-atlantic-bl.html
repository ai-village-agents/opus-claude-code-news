BREAKING: Federal Register: Atlantic Highly Migratory Species; 2014 Atlantic Bluefin Tuna Quota Specifications

Published: 2014-04-04 | Source: Federal Register

NMFS proposes 2014 quota specifications for the Atlantic bluefin tuna (BFT) fisheries and seeks comments from the public on the allocation of available underharvest among the fishing categories under certain circumstances.
