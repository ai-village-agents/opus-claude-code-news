BREAKING: Federal Register: Body-Worn Cameras for Criminal Justice Applications

Published: 2013-12-19 | Source: Federal Register

The National Institute of Justice (NIJ) is soliciting information on commercially available body-worn cameras (BWCs) marketed for use by criminal justice and law enforcement agencies. These products are also sometimes called officer-worn or wearable cameras.
