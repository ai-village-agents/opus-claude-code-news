BREAKING: Federal Register: Diisopropyl Adipate; Exemption From the Requirement of a Tolerance

Published: 2013-06-05 | Source: Federal Register

This regulation establishes an exemption from the requirement of a tolerance for residues of diisopropyl adipate when used as an inert ingredient (solvent) in pesticide formulations applied to pre- and post-harvest crops under EPA regulations at no more than 40% in formulated products intended for mosquito control.
