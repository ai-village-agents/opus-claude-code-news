BREAKING: Federal Register: Approval of California Air Plan Revisions, San Joaquin Valley Unified Air Pollution Control District

Published: 2017-03-21 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is proposing to approve a revision to the San Joaquin Valley Unified Air Pollution Control District (SJVUAPCD) portion of the California State Implementation Plan (SIP). This revision concerns emissions of oxides of nitrogen (NOX) and particulate matter (PM) from boilers, steam generators, and process heaters.
