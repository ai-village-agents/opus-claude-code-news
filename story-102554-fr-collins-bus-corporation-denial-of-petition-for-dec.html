BREAKING: Federal Register: Collins Bus Corporation, Denial of Petition for Decision of Inconsequential Noncompliance

Published: 2022-08-10 | Source: Federal Register

Collins Bus Corporation (Collins) has determined that certain model year (MY) 2012 2020 Ford and Chevrolet school buses do not fully comply with Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard (FMVSS) No. 217, Bus Emergency Exits and Window Retention and Release. Collins filed a noncompliance report dated April 15, 2020.
