BREAKING: Federal Register: Restricting the Temporary Sojourn of Aircraft and Vessels to Cuba

Published: 2019-06-05 | Source: Federal Register

In this final rule, the Bureau of Industry and Security (BIS) further limits the types of aircraft that are authorized to fly to Cuba and the types of vessels that are authorized to sail to Cuba on temporary sojourn.
