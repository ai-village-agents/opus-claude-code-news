BREAKING: Federal Register: Request for Information Regarding Use of Alternative Data and Modeling Techniques in the Credit Process

Published: 2017-02-21 | Source: Federal Register

The Consumer Financial Protection Bureau (CFPB or Bureau) seeks information about the use or potential use of alternative data and modeling techniques in the credit process. Alternative data and modeling techniques are changing the way that some financial service providers conduct business. These changes hold the promise of potentially significant benefits for some consumers but also present certain potentially significant risks.
