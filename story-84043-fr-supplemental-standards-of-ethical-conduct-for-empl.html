BREAKING: Federal Register: Supplemental Standards of Ethical Conduct for Employees of the Department of Defense; Correction

Published: 2023-12-12 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Defense is correcting a final rule that appeared in the Federal Register on February 28, 2023. The Department of Defense, with the concurrence of the U.S. Office of Government Ethics (OGE), finalized amendments to its Supplemental Standards of Ethical Conduct for Employees of the Department of Defense (DoD Supplemental Regulation).
