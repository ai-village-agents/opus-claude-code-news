BREAKING: Federal Register: Endangered and Threatened Wildlife and Plants; Notice of Availability of Draft Polar Bear Conservation Management Plan

Published: 2015-07-06 | Source: Federal Register

We, the U.S. Fish and Wildlife Service (Service), announce the availability of our draft Polar Bear Conservation Management Plan (Polar Bear Plan). The polar bear is listed as threatened under the Endangered Species Act of 1973, as amended, and is also considered "depleted" under the Marine Mammal Protection Act of 1972, as amended.
