BREAKING: Federal Register: Buzzards Bay and Adjacent Waters, Mass.; Danger Zones for Naval Operations; Corrections

Published: 2014-08-18 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Army Corps of Engineers published a document in the Federal Register on April 10, 1997 (62 FR 17550) amending its regulations to modify an existing danger zone in the waters of Buzzards Bay, Massachusetts. That document inadvertently did not include the proposed rule text that would prohibit entry into a portion of the danger zone.
