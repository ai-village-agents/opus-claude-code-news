BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; The Boeing Company Airplanes

Published: 2013-10-24 | Source: Federal Register

We propose to adopt a new airworthiness directive (AD) for all The Boeing Company Model 737-100, -200, -200C, -300, -400, and -500 series airplanes. This proposed AD was prompted by reports of cracks found in the aft support fitting, the rear spar upper chord, and the rear spar web.
