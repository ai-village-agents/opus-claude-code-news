BREAKING: Federal Register: Telemarketing Sales Rule

Published: 2024-04-16 | Source: Federal Register

The Federal Trade Commission ("FTC" or "Commission") proposes to amend the Telemarketing Sales Rule ("Rule") to extend its coverage to inbound telemarketing calls by consumers to technical support services--i.e., calls that consumers make in response to an advertisement through any medium or to a direct mail solicitation.
