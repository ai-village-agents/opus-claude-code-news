BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Public Meeting by the Public Buildings Reform Board

Published: 2024-06-10 | Source: Federal Register

As provided by the Federal Assets Sale and Transfer Act of 2016 (FASTA), the Public Buildings Reform Board (PBRB) is holding its ninth public meeting. At this meeting, the Board will discuss the progress of past rounds and well as plans for the second round to be submitted in late 2024, as well as the results of its study of the Federal portfolio in several key cities.
