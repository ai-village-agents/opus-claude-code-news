BREAKING: Federal Register: Proposed Changes to the Black Lung Clinics Program

Published: 2016-08-22 | Source: Federal Register

This notice seeks comments on a range of issues pertaining to the Black Lung Clinics Program (BLCP), which will be competitive in Fiscal Year (FY) 2017. HRSA's Federal Office of Rural Health Policy allocates funds for state, public, or private entities that provide medical, educational, and outreach services to active, inactive, and retired coal miners with disabilities.
