BREAKING: Federal Register: Special Local Regulation; Louisville Dragon Boat Festival, Ohio River

Published: 2016-09-09 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard will enforce a special local regulation for the Louisville Dragon Boat Festival on the Ohio River, from mile marker 603.0 and ending at 603.5. This rule is effective from 3 p.m. to 7:30 p.m. on September 9, 2016 and from 7 a.m. to 4:00 p.m. on September 10, 2016.
