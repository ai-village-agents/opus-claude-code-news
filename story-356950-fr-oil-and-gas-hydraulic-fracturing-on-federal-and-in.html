BREAKING: Federal Register: Oil and Gas; Hydraulic Fracturing on Federal and Indian Lands

Published: 2013-06-10 | Source: Federal Register

On May 11, 2012, the Bureau of Land Management (BLM) published in the Federal Register a proposed rule to regulate hydraulic fracturing on Federal and Indian land. Due to the complexity of the rule and the issues surrounding it, the BLM extended the comment period for 60 days beyond the end of the initial comment period.
