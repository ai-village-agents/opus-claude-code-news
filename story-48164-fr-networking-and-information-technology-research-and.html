BREAKING: Federal Register: Networking and Information Technology Research and Development Request for Information on a National Plan for Cyber-Physical Systems Resilience

Published: 2024-09-26 | Source: Federal Register

On behalf of Office of Science and Technology Policy (OSTP), the NITRD National Coordination Office seeks public input for the creation of a National Plan for Cyber-Physical Systems Resilience Research (the Plan). The goal of the plan is to shape a whole-of- government research and development (R&D) plan related to cyber- physical resilience across systems that may be local, regional, or national in scope.
