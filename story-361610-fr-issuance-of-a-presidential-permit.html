BREAKING: Federal Register: Issuance of a Presidential Permit

Published: 2013-07-31 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of State issued a Presidential Permit to Vantage Pipeline US LP ("Vantage") on July 16, 2013, authorizing Vantage to construct, connect, operate, and maintain pipeline facilities at the border of the United States and Canada in Divide County, North Dakota, for the export of liquefied ethane from the United States to Canada.
