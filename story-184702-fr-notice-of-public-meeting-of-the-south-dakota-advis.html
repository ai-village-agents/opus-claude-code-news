BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Public Meeting of the South Dakota Advisory Committee; Cancellation

Published: 2019-07-24 | Source: Federal Register

The Commission on Civil Rights published a notice in the Federal Register concerning a meeting of the South Dakota Advisory Committee. The meeting scheduled for Monday, July 22, 2019 at 12:00 p.m. (MDT) is cancelled. The notice is in the Federal Register of Friday, July 5, 2019, in FR Doc. 2019-14341, on page 32120-32121.
