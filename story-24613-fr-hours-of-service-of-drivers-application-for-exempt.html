BREAKING: Federal Register: Hours of Service of Drivers: Application for Exemption; Northern Clearing, Inc.

Published: 2025-12-09 | Source: Federal Register

FMCSA announces its decision to deny the application from Northern Clearing, Inc., requesting an exemption from the hours-of- service (HOS) maximum driving time limits for drivers of property- carrying commercial motor vehicles (CMV). The company requested this exemption to support its work providing restoration, clean up, and re- construction services in areas effected by Hurricane Helene.
