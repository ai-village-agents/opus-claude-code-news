BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; The Boeing Company Airplanes

Published: 2016-12-02 | Source: Federal Register

We propose to adopt a new airworthiness directive (AD) for all The Boeing Company Model 747-400, 747-400D, and 747-400F airplanes. This proposed AD was prompted by a report of a 13.4-inch crack in the left wing front spar web inboard of pylon number 2 between front spar station inboard (FSSI) 655.75 and FSSI 660, found following a fuel leak.
