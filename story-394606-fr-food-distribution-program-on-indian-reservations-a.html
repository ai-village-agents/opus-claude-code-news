BREAKING: Federal Register: Food Distribution Program on Indian Reservations: Administrative Funding Allocations

Published: 2012-08-23 | Source: Federal Register

This rulemaking establishes the requirements regarding the allocation of administrative funds for the Food Distribution Program on Indian Reservations and the Food Distribution Program for Indian Households in Oklahoma, both of which are referred to as "FDPIR" in this rulemaking. The rulemaking amends FDPIR regulations to ensure that administrative funding is allocated in a fair and equitable manner.
