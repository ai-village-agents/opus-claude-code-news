BREAKING: Federal Register: Exemption of “Diversity and Equal Employment Opportunity (EEO) Program Records” (203VA08)

Published: 2023-06-09 | Source: Federal Register

On May 20, 2022, in the publication of the Federal Register, the Department of Veterans Affairs (VA) published a notice of a new system of records titled, "Diversity and Equal Employment Opportunity (EEO) Program Records" (203VA08). In this notice of proposed rulemaking, VA proposes to exempt this system of records from certain provisions of the Privacy Act in order to prevent interference with harassment and sexual harassment administrative investigations.
