BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Bombardier, Inc., Airplanes

Published: 2017-08-21 | Source: Federal Register

We are superseding Airworthiness Directive (AD) 2014-20-09, which applied to certain Bombardier, Inc., Model DHC-8-400 series airplanes. AD 2014-20-09 required an inspection for missing clamps that are required to provide positive separation between the alternating current (AC) feeder cables and the hydraulic line of the landing gear alternate extension, and related investigative and corrective actions if necessary.
