BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Airbus Airplanes

Published: 2016-08-19 | Source: Federal Register

We are superseding Airworthiness Directive (AD) 2013-20-11, for all Airbus Model A318, A319, A320, and A321 series airplanes. AD 2013-20-11 required modifying the passenger emergency oxygen container assembly. This new AD expands the affected group of oxygen containers to include those labeled "DAe Systems."
