BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Zodiac Aero Evacuation Systems (also known as Air Cruisers Company)

Published: 2018-11-23 | Source: Federal Register

We are adopting a new airworthiness directive (AD) for Zodiac Aero Evacuation Systems (also known as Air Cruisers Company) fusible plugs installed on emergency evacuation equipment for various transport category airplanes. This AD was prompted by reports indicating that affected fusible plugs activated (vented gas) below the rated temperature.
