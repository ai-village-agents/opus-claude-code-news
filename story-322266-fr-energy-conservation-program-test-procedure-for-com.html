BREAKING: Federal Register: Energy Conservation Program: Test Procedure for Commercial Refrigeration Equipment

Published: 2014-04-21 | Source: Federal Register

In this final rule, the U.S. Department of Energy (DOE) revises and reorganizes its test procedure for commercial refrigeration equipment (CRE) to clarify certain terms, procedures, and compliance dates to improve the repeatability and remove ambiguity from the CRE test procedure.
