BREAKING: Federal Register: Amendment of Class E Airspace; Lansing, MI

Published: 2018-08-10 | Source: Federal Register

This action modifies Class E airspace extending upward from 700 feet above the surface at Capital Region International Airport, Lansing, MI. This action is the result of an airspace review due to the decommissioning of the Lansing VHF omnidirectional range (VOR) navigation aid as part of the VOR Minimum Operational Network (MON) Program.
