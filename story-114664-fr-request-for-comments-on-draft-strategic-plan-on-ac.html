BREAKING: Federal Register: Request for Comments on Draft Strategic Plan on Accessible Transportation

Published: 2021-01-15 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Department of Transportation (DOT) seeks public comment on a draft Strategic Plan on Accessible Transportation. The proposed document guides the DOT's efforts to create a transportation system that is inclusive of people with disabilities. The document may be found on the Federal regulations website at http:// www.regulations.gov as an attachment to Docket No. DOT-OST-2021-0004.
