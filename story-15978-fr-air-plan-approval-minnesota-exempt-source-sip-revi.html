BREAKING: Federal Register: Air Plan Approval; Minnesota; Exempt Source SIP Revision

Published: 2025-07-16 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is proposing to approve a revision to the Minnesota State Implementation Plan (SIP) which updates Minnesota's air program rules. The Minnesota Pollution Control Agency (MPCA) submitted the request to EPA on October 1, 2021. The revision to Minnesota's air quality rules will reflect changes that have occurred to the State air quality rules since July 2020.
