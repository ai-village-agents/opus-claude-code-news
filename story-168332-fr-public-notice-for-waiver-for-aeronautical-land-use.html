BREAKING: Federal Register: Public Notice for Waiver for Aeronautical Land-Use Assurance at El Paso International Airport, El Paso, Texas

Published: 2020-12-16 | Source: Federal Register

The Federal Aviation Administration is considering a proposal to change a portion of the airport from aeronautical to nonaeronautical use and to authorize the conversion of the airport property. The proposal consists of one parcel of land containing a total of approximately 1,179.25 acres. Ownership of the associated property transferred land from the United States of America To the city of El Paso under the Surplus Property Act of 1944 on July 8, 1969.
