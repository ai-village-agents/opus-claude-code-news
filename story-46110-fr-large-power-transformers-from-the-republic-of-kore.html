BREAKING: Federal Register: Large Power Transformers From the Republic of Korea: Continuation of Antidumping Duty Order

Published: 2024-09-05 | Source: Federal Register

As a result of the determinations by the U.S. Department of Commerce (Commerce) and the U.S. International Trade Commission (ITC) that revocation of the antidumping duty (AD) order on large power transformers (LPTs) from the Republic of Korea (Korea) would likely lead to the continuation or recurrence of dumping, and material injury to an industry in the United States, Commerce is publishing a notice of continuation of this AD order.
