BREAKING: Federal Register: Manufactured Housing Program: Minimum Payments to the States

Published: 2020-11-12 | Source: Federal Register

This rule revises the minimum payments that HUD distributes to states that participate in the Manufactured Housing Program as State Administrative Agencies (SAAs) in order to provide for a more equitable guarantee of minimum funding and to reduce administrative burden.
