BREAKING: Federal Register: Strategic Plan for Recordation of Documents

Published: 2014-01-15 | Source: Federal Register

The United States Copyright Office is requesting public comment on proposed key elements relevant to reengineering the function of recording documents pertaining to copyright pursuant to 17 U.S.C. 205. In a separate notice that will be published soon, the Office will also announce a series of public hearings on these elements, scheduled shortly after the end of the comment period on this Notice of Inquiry.
