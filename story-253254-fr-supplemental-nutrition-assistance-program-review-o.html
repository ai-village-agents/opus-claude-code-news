BREAKING: Federal Register: Supplemental Nutrition Assistance Program: Review of Major Changes in Program Design and Management Evaluation Systems

Published: 2016-01-19 | Source: Federal Register

This rule finalizes provisions of the proposed rule entitled Review of Major Changes in Program Design and Management Evaluation Systems, which was published May 3, 2011. This final rule amends the Supplemental Nutrition Assistance Program (SNAP) (formerly the Food Stamp Program) regulations to implement section 4116 of the Food, Conservation and Energy Act of 2008 (FCEA).
