BREAKING: Federal Register: Reactor Oversight Process Enhancement Initiative

Published: 2019-08-07 | Source: Federal Register

In an effort to revise and improve the Reactor Oversight Process (ROP), the U.S. Nuclear Regulatory Commission (NRC) staff recently proposed targeted, near-term ROP enhancements to the Commission, described in SECY-19-0067, "Recommendations for Enhancing the Reactor Oversight Process," dated June 28, 2019.
