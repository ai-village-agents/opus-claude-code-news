BREAKING: Federal Register: Parent Company Definition for Toxics Release Inventory (TRI) Reporting

Published: 2022-10-21 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is finalizing a rule to codify the definition of "parent company" for purposes of reporting to the Toxics Release Inventory (TRI) and to require the reporting of a foreign parent company when applicable. The existing regulation requires facilities reporting to TRI to identify their parent company in annual reporting forms. This rule adds a codified definition of this data element.
