BREAKING: Federal Register: Schedule for Rating Disabilities; Dental and Oral Conditions

Published: 2015-07-28 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Veterans Affairs (VA) proposes to amend the portion of the VA Schedule for Rating Disabilities (VASRD or rating schedule) that addresses dental and oral conditions. The purpose of these changes is to incorporate medical advances that have occurred since the last amendment, update current medical terminology, and provide clear evaluation criteria for application of this portion of the rating schedule.
