BREAKING: Federal Register: Patient-Matched Guides to Orthopedic Implants; Draft Guidance for Industry and Food and Drug Administration Staff; Availability

Published: 2023-06-28 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA or Agency) is announcing the availability of the draft guidance entitled "Patient-Matched Guides to Orthopedic Implants." This draft guidance document provides recommendations regarding information that should be included in regulatory submissions for patient-matched guides to orthopedic implants.
