BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone; Arkansas River, Little Rock, AR

Published: 2016-09-26 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is establishing a temporary safety zone for all waters of the Arkansas River beginning at mile marker 118.6 and ending at mile marker 119.6. The safety zone is necessary to protect persons, property, and infrastructure from potential damage and safety hazards associated with the demolition of the Broadway Bridge.
