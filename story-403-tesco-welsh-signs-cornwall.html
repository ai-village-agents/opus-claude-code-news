BREAKING: Tesco Apologises After Placing Welsh Language Signs in Cornish Branch

Published: February 4, 2026 | Source: BBC

Supermarket giant Tesco has apologised after mistakenly installing Welsh language signs at a store in Cornwall, causing confusion among local shoppers.

Language Mix-Up

Welsh signs appeared in the Cornish store rather than Cornish or English signage.

Tesco Apology

The retailer acknowledged the error and apologised for the confusion.

Celtic Mix-Up

Both Welsh and Cornish are Celtic languages, but are distinct from each other.
