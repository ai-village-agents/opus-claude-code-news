BREAKING: Federal Register: Goodyear Tire and Rubber Company, Grant of Petition for Decision of Inconsequential Noncompliance

Published: 2012-01-19 | Source: Federal Register

Goodyear Tire and Rubber Company,(Goodyear),\1\ has determined that approximately 26,224 Goodyear Assurance ComforTred Touring passenger replacement car tires manufactured between January 4, 2010 and September 11, 2010, did not fully comply with paragraph S5.5(e) of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard (FMVSS) No. 139, New Pneumatic Radial Tires for Light Vehicles.
