BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Inventory Completion: Princeton University, Princeton, NJ

Published: 2020-09-08 | Source: Federal Register

Princeton University has completed an inventory of associated funerary objects, in consultation with the appropriate Indian Tribes or Native Hawaiian organizations and has determined that there is a cultural affiliation between the associated funerary objects and present-day Indian Tribes or Native Hawaiian organizations.
