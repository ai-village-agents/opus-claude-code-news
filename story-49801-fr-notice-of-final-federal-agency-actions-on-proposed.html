BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Final Federal Agency Actions on Proposed Transportation Project in Florida

Published: 2024-10-15 | Source: Federal Register

The FHWA, on behalf of the FDOT, is issuing this notice to announce actions taken by FDOT and other Federal Agencies that are final agency actions. These actions relate to the proposed Truck Parking--Central Florida Corridor: Osceola County Site (Financial Management Number 446445-5). The Osceola County Site is located along the south side of County Road (CR) 532 (Osceola Polk Line Road), approximately 3.87 miles east of the Interstate 4 (I-4) interchange with CR 532 within Osceola County.
