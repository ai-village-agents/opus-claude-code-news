BREAKING: Federal Register: New Analytic Methods and Sampling Procedures for the United States National Residue Program for Meat, Poultry, and Egg Products

Published: 2012-07-06 | Source: Federal Register

The Food Safety and Inspection Service (FSIS) is announcing that it is restructuring the United States National Residue Program (NRP) with respect to how sampling of chemical compounds and animal production and egg product classes is scheduled.
