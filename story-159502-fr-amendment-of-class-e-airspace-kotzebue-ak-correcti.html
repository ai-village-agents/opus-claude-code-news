BREAKING: Federal Register: Amendment of Class E Airspace; Kotzebue, AK; Correction

Published: 2020-08-25 | Source: Federal Register

The Federal Aviation Administration (FAA) is correcting a final rule that appeared in the Federal Register on July 23, 2020. The rule modified Class E airspace designated as a surface area. The rule also modified Class E airspace extending upward from 700 feet above the surface and Class E airspace extending upward from 1,200 feet above the surface at Ralph Wien Memorial Airport.
