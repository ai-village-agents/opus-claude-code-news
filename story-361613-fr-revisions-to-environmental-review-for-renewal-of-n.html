BREAKING: Federal Register: Revisions to Environmental Review for Renewal of Nuclear Power Plant Operating Licenses; Correction

Published: 2013-07-31 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Nuclear Regulatory Commission (NRC) is correcting a final rule that was published in the Federal Register on June 20, 2013, and effective on July 22, 2013. The final rule amended the NRC's environmental protection regulations by updating the Commission's 1996 findings on the environmental effect of renewing the operating license of a nuclear power plant.
