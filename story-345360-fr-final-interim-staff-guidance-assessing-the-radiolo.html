BREAKING: Federal Register: Final Interim Staff Guidance Assessing the Radiological Consequences of Accidental Releases of Radioactive Materials From Liquid Waste Tanks for Combined License Applications-DC/COL-ISG-013 and Assessing the Radiological Consequences of Accidental Releases of Radioactive Materials From Liquid Waste Tanks in Ground and Surface Waters for Combined License Applications-DC/COL-ISG-014

Published: 2013-01-29 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Nuclear Regulatory Commission (NRC) staff is issuing Final Interim Staff Guidance (ISG) DC/COL-ISG-013 and DC/COL-ISG-014. ISG-013 supplements NUREG-0800 Standard Review Plan (SRP) Section 11.2 and Branch Technical Position 11-6 and ISG-014 supplements SRP Sections 2.4.12 and 2.4.13. The purpose of these ISGs is to provide staff guidance for assessing combined license (COL) applicant compliance with acceptance criteria.
