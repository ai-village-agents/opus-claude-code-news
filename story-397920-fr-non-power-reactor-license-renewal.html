BREAKING: Federal Register: Non-Power Reactor License Renewal

Published: 2012-10-02 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Nuclear Regulatory Commission (NRC or the Commission) is publishing the final regulatory basis for rulemaking to streamline non-power reactor license renewal. This final regulatory basis incorporates input from the public, licensees, certificate holders, and other stakeholders provided during the public comment period that ended July 31, 2012.
