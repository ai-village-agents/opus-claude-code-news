BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone; I-5 Bridge Construction Project, Columbia River, Vancouver, WA

Published: 2020-06-22 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is proposing to establish a temporary safety zone for certain waters of the Columbia River. This action is necessary to provide for the safety of life on these navigable waters around the Northbound Interstate Bridge at Columbia River Mile 106.5. This proposed rulemaking would prohibit persons and vessels from being in the safety zone unless authorized by the Captain of the Port Sector Columbia River or a designated representative.
