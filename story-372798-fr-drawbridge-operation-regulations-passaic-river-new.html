BREAKING: Federal Register: Drawbridge Operation Regulations; Passaic River, Newark and Harrison, NJ

Published: 2013-12-17 | Source: Federal Register

The Commander, First Coast Guard District, has issued a temporary deviation from the regulation governing the operation of the National Railroad Passenger Corporation (AMTRAK) Dock Railroad Bridge across Passaic River, mile 5.0, between Newark and Harrison, New Jersey.
