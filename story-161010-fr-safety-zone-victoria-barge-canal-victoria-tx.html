BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone; Victoria Barge Canal, Victoria, TX

Published: 2020-09-14 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is establishing a temporary safety zone for parts of the navigable waters of the Victoria Barge Canal from approximate position 28[deg]30'49" N, 096[deg]48'08" W to approximate position 28[deg]31'48" N, 096[deg]48'172" W. The safety zone is needed to protect personnel, vessels, and the marine environment from potential hazards associated with SH-35 bridge fender repair operations.
