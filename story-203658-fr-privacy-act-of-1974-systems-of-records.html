BREAKING: Federal Register: Privacy Act of 1974: Systems of Records

Published: 2018-04-04 | Source: Federal Register

Pursuant to the Privacy Act of 1974, the National Credit Union Administration (NCUA) proposes the following changes to: Rescind a duplicative system of records; reflect changes in information access and retrieval; and change the name of the office system owner for an existing system of records.
