BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Request for Information (RFI) on Fostering Energy Innovation Ecosystems

Published: 2017-01-25 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Department of Energy (DOE) invites public comment on this Request for Information (RFI) regarding regional innovation ecosystems and regional cooperation. The purpose of this RFI is to support a public discussion about how to create and foster regional and local "innovation ecosystems," specifically for energy technologies and energy use.
