BREAKING: Federal Register: Applications for New Awards; Opportunity Scholarship Program; Corrections

Published: 2025-05-02 | Source: Federal Register

On April 23, 2025, the Department of Education (Department) published in the Federal Register a notice inviting applications (NIA) for the fiscal year (FY) 2025 District of Columbia Opportunity Scholarship Program competition. The Department is correcting the NIA by revising the deadline date for transmittal of applications, together with the intergovernmental review deadline. All other information in the NIA remains the same.
