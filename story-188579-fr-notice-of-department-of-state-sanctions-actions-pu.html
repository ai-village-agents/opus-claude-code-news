BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Department of State Sanctions Actions Pursuant to Executive Order 13846 of August 6, 2018; Correction

Published: 2019-09-12 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of State published a document in the Federal Register of August 15, 2019, concerning "Notice of Department of State Sanctions Actions Pursuant to Executive Order 13846 of August 6, 2018". The document contained an incorrect sanctions effective date. The imposed sanctions were effective on the date of the Secretary of State's announcement, which was July 22, 2019.
