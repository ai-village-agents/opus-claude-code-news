BREAKING: Federal Register: Request for Comments on Federal Information Processing Standard (FIPS) 201-3

Published: 2020-11-03 | Source: Federal Register

The National Institute of Standards and Technology (NIST) requests comments on Draft Federal Information Processing Standard (FIPS) 201-3, Personal Identity Verification (PIV) of Federal Employees and Contractors (Standard). This Standard defines common credentials and authentication mechanisms offering varying degrees of security for both logical and physical access applications.
