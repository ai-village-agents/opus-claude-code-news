BREAKING: Federal Register: OSC Annual Survey

Published: 2020-08-21 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Office of Special Counsel (OSC), seeks approval from the Office of Management and Budget (OMB) for use of a survey that differs only slightly from previously approved information collections. OSC is requesting emergency approval for the annual survey, which by statute must be completed by the end of FY2020.
