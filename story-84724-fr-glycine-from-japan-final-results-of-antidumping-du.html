BREAKING: Federal Register: Glycine From Japan: Final Results of Antidumping Duty Administrative Review; 2021-2022

Published: 2023-12-20 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Department of Commerce (Commerce) determines that Yuki Gosei Kogyo Co., Ltd. (YGK) and Nagase & Co., Ltd. (Nagase) (collectively, YGK/Nagase) made sales of glycine from Japan at less than normal value during the period of review (POR) June 1, 2021, through May 31, 2022.
