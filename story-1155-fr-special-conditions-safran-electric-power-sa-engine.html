BREAKING: Federal Register: Special Conditions: Safran Electric & Power S.A. ENGINeTM US100 Series Electric Engines

Published: 2026-01-27 | Source: Federal Register

This action amends the applicability of special conditions that were issued for the Safran Electric & Power S.A. Model ENGINe US100A1 electric engines. These engines have a novel or unusual design feature when compared to the state of technology envisioned in the airworthiness standards applicable to aircraft engines.
