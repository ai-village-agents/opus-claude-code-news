BREAKING: Federal Register: Requirement for Certificates of Export for Importations of Steel Products of the Republic of Korea

Published: 2019-09-18 | Source: Federal Register

This document provides notice that importations into the United States of steel products of the Republic of Korea subject to absolute quota limits must be presented with valid and properly executed certificates of exportation.
