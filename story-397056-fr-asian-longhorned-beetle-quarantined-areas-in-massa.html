BREAKING: Federal Register: Asian Longhorned Beetle; Quarantined Areas in Massachusetts, Ohio, and New York

Published: 2012-09-21 | Source: Federal Register

We are adopting as a final rule, without change, an interim rule that amended the Asian longhorned beetle regulations by adding portions of Worcester County, MA, and Clermont County, OH, to the list of quarantined areas and by removing a portion of Suffolk County, NY, from the list of quarantined areas.
