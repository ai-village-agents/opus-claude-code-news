BREAKING: Federal Register: Opportunity for Stakeholder Engagement in the ENERGY STAR Products Program Plans

Published: 2024-04-24 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is announcing an opportunity for public input on ENERGY STAR product specification development activities. Since its creation in 1992, the ENERGY STAR program has grown to designate highly efficient products in more than 75 categories, all of which are independently certified.
