BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zones; Sector Upper Mississippi River Annual and Recurring Safety Zones Update

Published: 2017-05-08 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard proposes to amend and update its annual and recurring safety zones that take place in the Sector Upper Mississippi River Captain of the Port Zone (COTP Zone). This proposed rulemaking informs the public of regularly scheduled events that require additional safety measures through establishing of a safety zone.
