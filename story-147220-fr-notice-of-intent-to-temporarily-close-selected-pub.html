BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Intent To Temporarily Close Selected Public Lands in Maricopa and Pinal Counties, AZ

Published: 2020-03-20 | Source: Federal Register

The Bureau of Land Management (BLM) proposes to temporarily close public lands to public entry for all uses for up to 180 days on certain public lands administered by the Hassayampa and Lower Sonoran Field Offices, during the construction of four recreational shooting sports sites.
