BREAKING: Federal Register: Income Contingent Repayment Plan Options

Published: 2024-11-15 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Education (Department) issues this interim final rule (IFR) to amend the regulations governing income contingent repayment (ICR) plans available to Federal student loan borrowers to make certain that the Department meets its statutory obligation under the Higher Education Act of 1965, as amended, (HEA) to offer borrowers access to an income contingent repayment plan.
