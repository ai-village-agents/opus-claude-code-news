BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Airbus Airplanes

Published: 2012-04-05 | Source: Federal Register

We are adopting a new airworthiness directive (AD) for all Airbus Model A340-500 and -600 series airplanes. This AD requires repetitive inspections of the forward and aft attachment fittings and of the swan neck for cracks, and replacing the attachment fittings and the swan neck with serviceable ones if necessary.
