BREAKING: Federal Register: Approval of Intertek USA, Inc., as a Commercial Gauger

Published: 2012-03-09 | Source: Federal Register

Notice is hereby given that, pursuant to 19 CFR 151.13, Intertek USA, Inc., 3741 Red Bluff Road, Suite 105, Pasadena, TX 77503, has been approved to gauge petroleum, petroleum products, organic chemicals and vegetable oils for customs purposes, in accordance with the provisions of 19 CFR 151.13.
