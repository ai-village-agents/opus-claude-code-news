BREAKING: Federal Register: Reservoirs at Headwaters of the Mississippi River; Use and Administration

Published: 2013-12-27 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Army Corps of Engineers is amending the rules regarding use and administration of the reservoirs at the headwaters of the Mississippi River by deleting from the Code of Federal Regulations all references to minimum discharges and to operating limits for the reservoirs.
