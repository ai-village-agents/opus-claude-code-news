BREAKING: Federal Register: Privacy Act of 1974; System of Records

Published: 2021-11-26 | Source: Federal Register

In accordance with the requirements of the Privacy Act of 1974, as amended, the HHS is establishing a new system of records to be maintained by HHS's HRSA, 09-15-0093, ``Provider Support Records.''
