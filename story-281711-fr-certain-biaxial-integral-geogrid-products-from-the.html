BREAKING: Federal Register: Certain Biaxial Integral Geogrid Products From the People's Republic of China: Extension of Final Determination of Antidumping Duty Investigation

Published: 2016-12-16 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Commerce ("Department") is extending the time limits for the final determination of the investigation of certain biaxial integral geogrid products ("geogrids") from the People's Republic of China ("PRC"). The period of investigation ("POI") is July 1, 2015, through December 31, 2015.
