BREAKING: Federal Register: National Emission Standards for Aerospace Manufacturing and Rework Facilities Risk and Technology Review

Published: 2015-02-17 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is proposing amendments to the national emissions standards for hazardous air pollutants (NESHAP) for Aerospace Manufacturing and Rework Facilities to address the results of the residual risk and technology review (RTR) conducted as required under the Clean Air Act (CAA), and to correct errors and deficiencies identified during the review of these standards.
