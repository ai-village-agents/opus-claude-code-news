BREAKING: Federal Register: Regional Conservation Partnership Program

Published: 2021-01-15 | Source: Federal Register

This final rule adopts, with minor changes, an interim rule published in the Federal Register on February 13, 2020. The interim rule implemented changes to RCPP that were either necessitated by the Agriculture Improvement Act of 2018 (the 2018 Farm Bill) and changes for administrative streamlining improvements and clarifications.
