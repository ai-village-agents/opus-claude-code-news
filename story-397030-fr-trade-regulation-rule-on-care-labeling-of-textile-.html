BREAKING: Federal Register: Trade Regulation Rule on Care Labeling of Textile Wearing Apparel and Certain Piece Goods

Published: 2012-09-20 | Source: Federal Register

Based on comments received in response to its Advance Notice of Proposed Rulemaking ("ANPR"), the Federal Trade Commission proposes to amend its trade regulation rule on Care Labeling of Textile Wearing Apparel and Certain Piece Goods as Amended ("Rule") to: Allow garment manufacturers and marketers to include instructions for professional wetcleaning on labels; permit the use of ASTM Standard D5489-07, "Standard Guide for Care Symbols for Care Instructions on Textile Products," or ISO 3758:2005...
