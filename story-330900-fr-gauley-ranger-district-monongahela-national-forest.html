BREAKING: Federal Register: Gauley Ranger District, Monongahela National Forest; West Virginia; Big Rock Project

Published: 2014-07-30 | Source: Federal Register

The USDA Forest Service, Monongahela National Forest, Gauley Ranger District intends to prepare an environmental impact statement (EIS) to disclose the environmental consequences of a vegetation management project. In the EIS the USDA Forest Service will address the potential environmental impacts due to creating early successional forest; enhancing the growth and mast production of forest stands; and increasing wildlife habitat diversity.
