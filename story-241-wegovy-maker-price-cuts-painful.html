BREAKING: Wegovy Maker Warns Price Cuts 'Painful' for Firm as Shares Plunge

Published: February 4, 2026 | Source: BBC

Novo Nordisk, the maker of weight-loss drug Wegovy, has warned that price cuts will be 'painful' for the company as its shares plunge in market trading.

Stock Decline

Shares in the Danish pharmaceutical giant have fallen sharply following the pricing announcement.

Price Pressure

The company is facing pressure to reduce prices on its popular weight-loss medications.

Market Impact

The share price drop reflects investor concerns about the impact on the company's profit margins.
