BREAKING: Federal Register: National Environmental Policy Act; Mars Sample Return Campaign; Correction

Published: 2022-04-27 | Source: Federal Register

The National Aeronautics and Space Administration (NASA) published a document in the Federal Register of April 15, 2022, concerning a notice of intent; notice of meetings; and request for comments. The document inadvertently omits the meeting number (access code) for the virtual public scoping meetings which is required for audio-only users to gain access to the meeting.
