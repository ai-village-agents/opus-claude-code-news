BREAKING: Federal Register: Distribution of 2004, 2005, 2006, 2007, 2008, and 2009 Cable Royalty Funds

Published: 2013-08-16 | Source: Federal Register

The Copyright Royalty Judges (Judges) announce the commencement of a proceeding to determine the Phase II distribution of royalties deposited with the Register of Copyrights for the statutory license allowing distant retransmission of over-the-air television and radio broadcast signals by cable system operators.
