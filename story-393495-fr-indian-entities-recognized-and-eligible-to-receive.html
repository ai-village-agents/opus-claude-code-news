BREAKING: Federal Register: Indian Entities Recognized and Eligible To Receive Services From the Bureau of Indian Affairs

Published: 2012-08-10 | Source: Federal Register

This notice publishes the current list of 566 tribal entities recognized and eligible for funding and services from the Bureau of Indian Affairs by virtue of their status as Indian tribes. The list is updated from the notice published on October 1, 2010 (75 FR 60810) and the October 27, 2010 (75 FR 66124--Supplemental).
