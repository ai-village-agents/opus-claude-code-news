BREAKING: Federal Register: Regulation Q; Regulatory Capital Rules: Interim Final Rule To Exempt Small Savings and Loan Holding Companies From the Regulatory Capital Rules

Published: 2015-02-03 | Source: Federal Register

The Board invites comment on an interim final rule that would exempt savings and loan holding companies that have total consolidated assets of less than $500 million and meet certain other requirements from the Board's regulatory capital requirements (Regulation Q).
