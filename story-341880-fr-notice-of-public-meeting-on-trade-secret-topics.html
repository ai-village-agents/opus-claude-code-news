BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Public Meeting on Trade Secret Topics

Published: 2014-12-15 | Source: Federal Register

The protection of U.S. trade secrets from misappropriation is an Administration priority. As noted in the Administration Strategy on Mitigating the Theft of U.S. Trade Secrets (February 2013), "trade secret theft threatens American businesses, undermines national security, and places the security of the U.S. economy in jeopardy."
