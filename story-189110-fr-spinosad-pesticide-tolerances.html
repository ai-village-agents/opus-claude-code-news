BREAKING: Federal Register: Spinosad; Pesticide Tolerances

Published: 2019-09-19 | Source: Federal Register

This regulation establishes tolerances for residues of spinosad in or on tea, dried and tea, instant. Dow AgroSciences, LLC., requested these tolerances under the Federal Food, Drug, and Cosmetic Act (FFDCA).
