BREAKING: Federal Register: Partial Replacement of an Earlier National Registration or Registrations by an International Registration

Published: 2025-06-02 | Source: Federal Register

The United States Patent and Trademark Office (USPTO) issues this final rule to implement an amendment to the Regulations under the Protocol Relating to the Madrid Agreement Concerning the International Registration of Marks (Madrid Protocol) adopted by the Madrid Union Assembly that went into effect on February 1, 2021, with a delayed implementation date of February 1, 2025.
