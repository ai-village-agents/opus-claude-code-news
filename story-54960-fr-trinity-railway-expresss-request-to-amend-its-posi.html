BREAKING: Federal Register: Trinity Railway Express's Request To Amend Its Positive Train Control Safety Plan and Positive Train Control System

Published: 2024-12-11 | Source: Federal Register

This document provides the public with notice that, on October 30, 2024, Trinity Railway Express (TRE) submitted its Positive Train Control Safety Plan (PTCSP), Version 2.0, dated October 30, 2024, to FRA's Secure Information Repository. TRE asks FRA to approve its updated PTCSP and certify TRE's Interoperable Electronic Train Management System (I-ETMS) as a "mixed" positive train control (PTC) system.
