BREAKING: Federal Register: Federal Housing Administration (FHA): Single Family Sale Program

Published: 2024-12-11 | Source: Federal Register

This rule amends the requirements for the sale of eligible single family mortgage loans insured by the Federal Housing Administration (FHA) that have been assigned to the Secretary of the Department of Housing and Urban Development (HUD) in exchange for claim payments.
