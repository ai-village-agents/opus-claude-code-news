BREAKING: Federal Register: Women-Owned Small Business Federal Contract Program NAICS Code Updates

Published: 2017-10-11 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Small Business Administration (SBA) is updating the North American Industry Classification System (NAICS) codes authorized for use in the Women-Owned Small Business (WOSB) Federal Contract Program (WOSB Program). The update is being made to reflect the U.S. Office of Management and Budget's (OMB) NAICS revision for 2017, identified as NAICS 2017.
