BREAKING: Federal Register: Privacy Act; Implementation

Published: 2024-06-07 | Source: Federal Register

In accordance with subsections (j)(2) and (k)(2) of the Privacy Act of 1974, as amended (the Privacy Act or the Act), the Department of Health and Human Services (HHS or Department) is proposing to exempt a new system of records maintained by the National Institutes of Health (NIH), System No. 09-25-0224, "NIH Police Records," from certain requirements of the Act.
