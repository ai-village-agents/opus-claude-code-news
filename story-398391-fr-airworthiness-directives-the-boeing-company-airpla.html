BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; The Boeing Company Airplanes

Published: 2012-10-05 | Source: Federal Register

We are superseding an existing airworthiness directive (AD) for certain The Boeing Company Model 747 airplanes. That AD currently requires repetitive visual inspections around the bushings of the wing landing gear (WLG) beam outboard end fittings for corrosion, and rework if necessary; and ultrasonic inspections for cracks of the outboard end fittings of the WLG support beams, and rework if necessary.
