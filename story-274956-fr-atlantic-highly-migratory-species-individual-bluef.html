BREAKING: Federal Register: Atlantic Highly Migratory Species; Individual Bluefin Quota Program; Inseason Transfers

Published: 2016-09-26 | Source: Federal Register

NMFS proposes to modify the Atlantic highly migratory species (HMS) regulations to provide additional flexibility regarding the distribution of inseason Atlantic bluefin tuna (BFT) quota transfers to the Longline category.
