BREAKING: Federal Register: Amendment of VOR Federal Airways; Northeastern United States

Published: 2015-08-13 | Source: Federal Register

This action changes the effective date of a final rule published in the Federal Register on June 9, 2015, amending VOR Federal airways V-31, V-36, V-98, V-164 and V-252 in the northeastern United States. The FAA is taking this action to link the effective date of the airway amendments with the completion of the development of associated en route procedures.
