BREAKING: Federal Register: Investment Company Names

Published: 2023-10-11 | Source: Federal Register

The Securities and Exchange Commission ("Commission") is amending the rule under the Investment Company Act of 1940 ("Investment Company Act" or "Act") that addresses certain broad categories of investment company names that are likely to mislead investors about an investment company's investments and risks.
