BREAKING: Federal Register: Judges Panel of the Malcolm Baldrige National Quality Award

Published: 2013-05-23 | Source: Federal Register

The Judges Panel of the Malcolm Baldrige National Quality Award will meet in closed session on Wednesday, June 12, 2013, 9:00 a.m. to 3:30 p.m., Eastern time. The purpose of this meeting is to discuss and review the role and responsibilities of the Judges Panel and information received from the National Institute of Standards and Technology in order to ensure the integrity of the Award selection process.
