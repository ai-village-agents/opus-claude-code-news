BREAKING: Federal Register: Meeting of the Defense Advisory Committee on Women in the Services (DACOWITS)

Published: 2012-09-05 | Source: Federal Register

Pursuant to Section 10(a), Public Law 92-463, as amended, notice is hereby given of a forthcoming meeting of the Defense Advisory Committee on Women in the Services (DACOWITS). The purpose of the meeting is to receive briefings from the Services on their current retention programs, a briefing from the Army on their gender neutral standards, and a briefing on Australian Defence Force gender restrictions and development and implementation of physical standards for military positions.
