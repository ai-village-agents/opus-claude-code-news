BREAKING: Federal Register: Governance Requirements for Derivatives Clearing Organizations

Published: 2023-07-13 | Source: Federal Register

The Commodity Futures Trading Commission (CFTC or Commission) is adopting amendments to its rules to require derivatives clearing organizations (DCOs) to establish and consult with one or more risk management committees (RMCs) comprised of clearing members and customers of clearing members on matters that could materially affect the risk profile of the DCO.
