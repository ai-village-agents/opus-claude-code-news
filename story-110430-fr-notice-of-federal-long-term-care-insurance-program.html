BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Federal Long Term Care Insurance Program (FLTCIP)-Suspension of Applications for FLTCIP Coverage

Published: 2022-11-18 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Office of Personnel Management (OPM) is announcing a suspension of applications for Federal Long Term Care Insurance Program (FLTCIP) coverage. During the suspension period, no applications for FLTCIP coverage will be accepted, and current enrollees may not apply to increase their coverage.
