BREAKING: Federal Register: Tobacco Transition Program Assessments; Final Appeals and Revisions Procedures

Published: 2014-04-09 | Source: Federal Register

The Commodity Credit Corporation (CCC) is amending the regulations for the Tobacco Transition Program (TTP) to clarify the final administrative actions required for the orderly close-out of the program.
