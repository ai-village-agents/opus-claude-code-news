BREAKING: Federal Register: Air Plan Approval: South Carolina; Standards for Volatile Organic Compounds and Oxides of Nitrogen

Published: 2017-10-13 | Source: Federal Register

Due to the receipt of an adverse comment, the Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is withdrawing a portion of the August 16, 2017, direct final rule that approves changes to South Carolina's state implementation plan (SIP) related to the regulation of volatile organic compounds (VOC) and oxides of nitrogen (NOX).
