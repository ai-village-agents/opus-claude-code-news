BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone; Big Bay Boom Fourth of July Fireworks, San Diego, CA

Published: 2014-05-14 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard will enforce the Big Bay Boom Fourth of July Fireworks safety zones on July 4, 2014. This recurring marine event occurs on the navigable waters of San Diego Bay in San Diego, California. This action is necessary to provide for the safety of the participants, crew, spectators, safety vessels, and general users of the waterway.
