BREAKING: Federal Register: Public Availability of Social Security Administration Fiscal Year (FY) 2017 Service Contract Inventory

Published: 2019-04-02 | Source: Federal Register

In accordance with Section 743 of Division C of the Consolidated Appropriations Act of 2010, we are publishing this notice to advise the public of the availability of the FY 2017 Service Contract inventory. This inventory provides information on FY 2017 service contract actions over $25,000. We organized the information by function to show how we distribute contracted resources throughout the agency.
