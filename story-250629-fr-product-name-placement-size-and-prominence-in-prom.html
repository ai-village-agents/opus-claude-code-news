BREAKING: Federal Register: Product Name Placement, Size, and Prominence in Promotional Labeling and Advertisements; Guidance for Industry; Availability

Published: 2017-12-12 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA or Agency) is announcing the availability of a guidance for industry entitled "Product Name Placement, Size, and Prominence in Promotional Labeling and Advertisements." The guidance clarifies the requirements for product name placement, size, prominence, and frequency in promotional labeling and advertisements for human prescription drugs, including prescription biological products, and for animal prescription drugs.
