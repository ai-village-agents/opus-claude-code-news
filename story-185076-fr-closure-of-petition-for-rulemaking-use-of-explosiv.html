BREAKING: Federal Register: Closure of Petition for Rulemaking; Use of Explosives on Surface Coal Mining Operations

Published: 2019-07-30 | Source: Federal Register

We, the Office of Surface Mining Reclamation and Enforcement (OSMRE), withdraw our decision to initiate rulemaking related to the release of emissions generated by blasting on surface coal mining operations.
