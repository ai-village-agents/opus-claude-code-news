BREAKING: Federal Register: Special Conditions: Airbus, Model A318-112 Airplane (S/N 3238); Certification of Cooktops

Published: 2013-01-30 | Source: Federal Register

These special conditions are issued for the Airbus Model A318- 112 airplane, serial number (S/N) 3238. This airplane, as modified by Fokker Services B.V., will have a novel or unusual design feature associated with a cooktop installation. The applicable airworthiness regulations do not contain adequate or appropriate safety standards for this design feature.
