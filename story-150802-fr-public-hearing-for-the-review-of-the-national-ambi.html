BREAKING: Federal Register: Public Hearing for the Review of the National Ambient Air Quality Standards for Particulate Matter

Published: 2020-05-05 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is announcing that a virtual public hearing will be held for the proposed action titled, "Review of the National Ambient Air Quality Standards for Particulate Matter," which was signed on April 14, 2020. The hearing will be held May 20 and 21, 2020.
