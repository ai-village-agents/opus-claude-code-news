BREAKING: Federal Register: AAPCO/SFIREG Full Committee; Notice of Public Meeting

Published: 2015-11-23 | Source: Federal Register

The Association of American Pesticide Control Officials (AAPCO)/State FIFRA Issues Research and Evaluation Group (SFIREG), Full Committee will hold a 2-day meeting, beginning on December 7, 2015, and ending December 8, 2015. This notice announces the location and times for the meeting and sets forth topics that may be included in the agenda.
