BREAKING: Federal Register: Margin Requirements for Uncleared Swaps for Swap Dealers and Major Swap Participants

Published: 2021-01-25 | Source: Federal Register

The Commodity Futures Trading Commission ("Commission" or "CFTC") is amending the margin requirements for uncleared swaps ("Final Rule") for swap dealers ("SD") and major swap participants ("MSP") for which there is not a prudential regulator ("CFTC Margin Rule").
