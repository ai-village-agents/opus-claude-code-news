BREAKING: Federal Register: Sulfoxaflor; Receipt of Application for Emergency Exemption, Solicitation of Public Comment

Published: 2016-01-27 | Source: Federal Register

EPA has received a specific exemption request from the Texas Department of Agriculture to use the insecticide sulfoxaflor (CAS No. 946578-00-3) to treat up to 3,000,000 acres of sorghum to control sugarcane aphid.
