BREAKING: Federal Register: Volkswagen Group of America, Inc., Receipt of Petition for Decision of Inconsequential Noncompliance

Published: 2025-07-28 | Source: Federal Register

Volkswagen Group of America, Inc. (Volkswagen) has determined that certain model year (MY) 2018-2024 Audi SQ5, MY 2021-2024 Audi SQ5 Sportback, MY 2018-2024 Audi Q5, and MY2021-2024 Audi Q5 Sportback do not fully comply with Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard (FMVSS) No. 118, Power-Operated Window, Partition, and Roof Panel Systems.
