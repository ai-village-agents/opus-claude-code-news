BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; The Boeing Company Airplanes

Published: 2016-03-01 | Source: Federal Register

We propose to adopt a new airworthiness directive (AD) for all The Boeing Company Model 757-200 and -200CB series airplanes. This proposed AD was prompted by an evaluation by the design approval holder (DAH) indicating that the lap splices at stringer (S)-14R, lower fastener row, are subject to widespread fatigue damage (WFD).
