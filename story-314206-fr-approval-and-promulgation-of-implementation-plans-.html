BREAKING: Federal Register: Approval and Promulgation of Implementation Plans and Title V Operating Permit Program; State of Iowa

Published: 2014-01-16 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is proposing to approve revisions to the State Implementation Plan (SIP) for the state of Iowa.
