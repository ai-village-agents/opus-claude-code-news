BREAKING: Federal Register: Export Sales Reporting Requirements

Published: 2012-03-22 | Source: Federal Register

A proposed rule published in the Federal Register on March 8, 2012, proposing requirements to add reporting for pork (fresh, chilled, and frozen box/primal cuts) and distillers dried grain (DDG) to the Export Sales Reporting Requirements, is being withdrawn. The Foreign Agricultural Service (FAS) is currently awaiting approval for publication from the Office of Management and Budget.
