BREAKING: Spain Announces Plans to Ban Social Media for Under-16s

Published: February 4, 2026 | Source: BBC Europe

Spain has announced plans to ban social media access for children under 16 years old.

Age Restriction

The proposed ban would restrict all social media use for those under 16.

Child Protection

The measure aims to protect young people from the harms of social media.

European Policy

Spain joins other countries considering stricter social media regulations for minors.
