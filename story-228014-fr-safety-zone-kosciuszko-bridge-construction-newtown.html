BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone; Kosciuszko Bridge Construction, Newtown Creek, Brooklyn and Queens, NY

Published: 2017-02-22 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard proposes to establish two safety zones on the navigable waters of Newtown Creek, NY. The first safety zone is within 500 feet of the two barges and assist vessels to be used for the removal and loading of the existing center span from the Kosciuszko Bridge at mile 2.1.
