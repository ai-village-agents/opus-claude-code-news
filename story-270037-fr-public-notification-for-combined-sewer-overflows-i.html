BREAKING: Federal Register: Public Notification for Combined Sewer Overflows in the Great Lakes; Public Listening Session; Request for Stakeholder Input

Published: 2016-08-01 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is announcing plans to hold a public "listening session" on September 14, 2016 in Chicago, Illinois to obtain information from the public to help inform development of a new regulation establishing public notification requirements for combined sewer overflow discharges in the Great Lakes.
