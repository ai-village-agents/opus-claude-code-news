BREAKING: Spain Announces Plans to Ban Social Media for Under-16s

Published: February 4, 2026 | Source: BBC

Spain has announced plans to ban children under 16 from using social media platforms in a major policy move to protect young people.

Age Restriction

The proposed legislation would prohibit social media use for anyone under 16 years old, joining similar moves in other countries.

Child Protection

Officials cite concerns about mental health, online bullying, and inappropriate content as reasons for the ban.

Implementation

Details of how the ban would be enforced are still being developed by Spanish authorities.
