BREAKING: Federal Register: Meeting of the Judicial Conference Committee on Rules of Practice and Procedure

Published: 2014-01-02 | Source: Federal Register

On February 7, 2014, the Advisory Committee on Rules of Civil Procedure will hold a one-day public hearing on the proposed amendments to Civil Rules 1, 4, 6, 16, 26, 30, 31, 33, 34, 36, 37, 55, 84, and Appendix of Forms.
