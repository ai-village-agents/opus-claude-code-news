BREAKING: Federal Register: Northeast Corridor Safety Committee; Notice of Meeting

Published: 2012-04-05 | Source: Federal Register

FRA announces the second meeting of the NECSC, a Federal Advisory Committee mandated by Section 212 of the Passenger Rail Investment and Improvement Act of 2008 (PRIIA). The NECSC is made up of stakeholders operating on the Northeast Corridor (NEC), and the purpose of the Committee is to provide annual recommendations to the DOT Secretary.
