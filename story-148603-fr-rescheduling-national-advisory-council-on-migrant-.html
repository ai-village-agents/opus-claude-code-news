BREAKING: Federal Register: Rescheduling National Advisory Council on Migrant Health Meeting

Published: 2020-04-07 | Source: Federal Register

This is to notify the public that the National Advisory Council on Migrant Health (NACMH) meeting, originally scheduled for May 5-6, 2020, is re-scheduled to July 29-30, 2020. The May 5-6, 2020, NACMH meeting was announced in the Federal Register, Vol. 85, No. 41, on Monday, March 2, 2020 (FR Doc. 2020-04169 Filed 2-28-20).
