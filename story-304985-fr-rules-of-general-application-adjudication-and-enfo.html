BREAKING: Federal Register: Rules of General Application, Adjudication and Enforcement

Published: 2015-09-24 | Source: Federal Register

The United States International Trade Commission ("Commission") proposes to amend its Rules of Practice and Procedure concerning rules of general application, adjudication, and enforcement. The amendments are necessary to make certain technical corrections, to clarify certain provisions, to harmonize different parts of the Commission's rules, and to address concerns that have arisen in Commission practice.
