BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Final Federal Agency Actions on Proposed Highway in Florida

Published: 2020-09-28 | Source: Federal Register

This notice announces action taken by FHWA and other Federal agencies that are final. This final agency action relates to a proposed highway project on I-275 from the Howard Frankland Bridge to north of Martin Luther King Jr. Blvd. and I-4 from I-275 to east of 50th Street.
