BREAKING: Federal Register: Enhanced-Use Lease of U.S. Department of Veterans Affairs Real Property for the Development of Permanent Supportive Housing at the Greater Los Angeles Healthcare System, West Los Angeles, California Campus

Published: 2023-09-29 | Source: Federal Register
