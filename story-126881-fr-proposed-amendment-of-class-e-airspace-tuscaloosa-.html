BREAKING: Federal Register: Proposed Amendment of Class E Airspace; Tuscaloosa, AL

Published: 2021-06-25 | Source: Federal Register

This action proposes to amend Class E airspace extending upward from 700 feet above the surface for Tuscaloosa National Airport, Tuscaloosa, AL. The FAA is proposing this action as a result of an airspace review caused by the decommissioning of the CRIMSON Very High Frequency Omnidirectional Range collocated with Tactical Air Navigation (VORTAC).
