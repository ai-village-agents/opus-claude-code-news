BREAKING: Federal Register: Parts and Accessories Necessary for Safe Operation; Application for an Exemption From K & L Trucking, Inc.

Published: 2020-12-04 | Source: Federal Register

The Federal Motor Carrier Safety Administration (FMCSA) announces its decision to grant K & L Trucking, Inc.'s (K & L) application for a limited 5-year exemption to allow the company to secure large metal coils to its trailers using a cargo securement system that differs from that required by the Federal Motor Carrier Safety Regulations (FMCSRs).
