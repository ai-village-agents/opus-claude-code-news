BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone; Ohio River, Mile Markers 317 to 317.5, Catlettsburg, KY

Published: 2023-08-17 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is establishing a temporary safety zone for the City of Catlettsburg Labor Day Fireworks Display occurring on September 1, 2023, on the Ohio River, Catlettsburg, KY. The safety zone will cover all navigable waters between mile marker 317 and 317.5 to protect personnel, vessels, and the marine environment from potential hazards associated with a fireworks event.
