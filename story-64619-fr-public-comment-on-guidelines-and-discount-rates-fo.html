BREAKING: Federal Register: Public Comment on Guidelines and Discount Rates for Benefit-Cost Analysis of Federal Programs

Published: 2023-04-07 | Source: Federal Register

OMB is proposing revisions to Circular A-94, which provides guidance on benefit-cost analysis and cost-effectiveness analysis of Federal spending. Circular A-94 was last revised in 1992. These proposed revisions reflect scientific and economic advances since that time. Included in the proposed revisions are changes in the discount rate for benefit-cost analysis.
