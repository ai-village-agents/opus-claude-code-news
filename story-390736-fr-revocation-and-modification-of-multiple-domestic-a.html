BREAKING: Federal Register: Revocation and Modification of Multiple Domestic, Alaskan, and Hawaiian Compulsory Reporting Points

Published: 2012-07-10 | Source: Federal Register

This action removes eighteen Domestic and Alaskan compulsory reporting points defined by navigation aids previously decommissioned, removed, or shutdown and taken out of the FAA aeronautical database as compulsory reporting points. Additionally, this action also requires changes to the name of four navigation aids that define associated compulsory reporting points.
