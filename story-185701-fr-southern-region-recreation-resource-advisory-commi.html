BREAKING: Federal Register: Southern Region Recreation Resource Advisory Committee

Published: 2019-08-06 | Source: Federal Register

The Southern Region Recreation Resource Advisory Committee (Recreation RAC) will meet in Decatur, Georgia. The committee is authorized under the Federal Lands Recreation Enhancement Act (the Act) and operates in compliance with the Federal Advisory Committee Act.
