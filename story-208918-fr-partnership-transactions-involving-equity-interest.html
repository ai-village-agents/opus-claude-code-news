BREAKING: Federal Register: Partnership Transactions Involving Equity Interests of a Partner

Published: 2018-06-08 | Source: Federal Register

This document contains final regulations that prevent a corporate partner from avoiding corporate-level gain through transactions with a partnership involving equity interests of the partner or certain related entities.
