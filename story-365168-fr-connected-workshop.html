BREAKING: Federal Register: ConnectED Workshop

Published: 2013-09-11 | Source: Federal Register

The National Telecommunications and Information Administration (NTIA) will convene a meeting of stakeholders from the education, technology, and government sectors to share ideas, discuss policies, and consider promising strategies for achieving the President's goal of connecting virtually all K-12 students in the United States to next- generation broadband. This Notice announces that the ConnectED Workshop will be held on October 7, 2013.
