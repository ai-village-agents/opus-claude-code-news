BREAKING: Federal Register: Approved Drug Products With Therapeutic Equivalence Evaluations (the “Orange Book”); Establishment of a Public Docket; Request for Comments

Published: 2020-06-01 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA or Agency) is announcing the establishment of a public docket to solicit comments on FDA's publication entitled "Approved Drug Products With Therapeutic Equivalence Evaluations" (commonly known as the "Orange Book"). The Orange Book identifies drug products approved by FDA under the Federal Food, Drug, and Cosmetic Act (FD&C Act) and includes related information.
