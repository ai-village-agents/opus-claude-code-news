BREAKING: Federal Register: Privacy Act of 1974; System of Records

Published: 2023-10-23 | Source: Federal Register

The National Science Foundation (NSF) is creating a new system of records: NSF-80, Education and Training Application Data System (ETAP). This new system of records will contain records about individuals interested in participating in NSF education and training activities, and individuals engaged in the planning, management, and implementation of those activities.
