BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Public Meeting of the Guam Advisory Committee; Cancellation

Published: 2023-06-12 | Source: Federal Register

The Commission on Civil Rights published a notice in the Federal Register concerning an in-person meeting of the Guam Advisory Committee. This meeting, scheduled for Friday, June 2, 2023, at 9:00 a.m. ChST, has been cancelled due to Typhoon Mawar. The notice is in the Federal Register of Thursday, May 11, 2023, in FR Document Number 2023-10088, in the first and second columns of page 30276.
