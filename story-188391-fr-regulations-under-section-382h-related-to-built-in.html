BREAKING: Federal Register: Regulations Under Section 382(h) Related to Built-In Gain and Loss

Published: 2019-09-10 | Source: Federal Register

This document contains proposed regulations regarding the items of income and deduction which are included in the calculation of built-in gains and losses under section 382 of the Internal Revenue Code (Code), and reflecting numerous changes made to the Code by the enactment of recent tax legislation. These proposed regulations would affect corporations that experience an ownership change for purposes of section 382.
