BREAKING: Federal Register: Program-Specific Guidance About Possession Licenses for Manufacturing and Distribution

Published: 2016-07-13 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Nuclear Regulatory Commission (NRC) is revising its licensing guidance for possession licenses for manufacturing and distribution. The NRC is requesting public comment on draft NUREG-1556, Volume 12, Revision 1, "Consolidated Guidance about Materials Licenses: Program-Specific Guidance about Possession Licenses for Manufacturing and Distribution."
