BREAKING: Federal Register: Disclosure of Information to State Officials Regarding Tax-Exempt Organizations

Published: 2022-08-16 | Source: Federal Register

These final regulations provide guidance to states regarding the process by which they may obtain or inspect certain returns and return information (including information about final and proposed denials and revocations of tax-exempt status) for the purpose of administering State laws governing certain tax-exempt organizations and their activities.
