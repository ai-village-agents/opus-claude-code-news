BREAKING: Federal Register: Drawbridge Operation Regulation; Gulf Intracoastal Waterway, Osprey, FL

Published: 2024-08-06 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard has issued a temporary deviation from the operating schedule that governs the Blackburn Point Bridge across the Gulf Intracoastal Waterway (GICW), mile 63.1, at Osprey, FL. The Casey Key Association has requested the Coast Guard changing the operating schedule allowing the drawbridge scheduled openings.
