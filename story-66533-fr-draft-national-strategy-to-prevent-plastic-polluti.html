BREAKING: Federal Register: Draft National Strategy to Prevent Plastic Pollution: Request for Public Comment

Published: 2023-05-02 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) has developed the Draft National Strategy to Prevent Plastic Pollution to reduce plastic waste and other post-consumer materials in waterways and oceans, as directed by Congress in section 301 of the Save Our Seas 2.0 Act.
