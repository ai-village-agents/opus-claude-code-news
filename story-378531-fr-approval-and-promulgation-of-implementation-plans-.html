BREAKING: Federal Register: Approval and Promulgation of Implementation Plans; State of Alaska; Regional Haze State Implementation Plan

Published: 2012-02-24 | Source: Federal Register

EPA is proposing to approve a State Implementation Plan (SIP) revision, submitted by the State of Alaska on April 4, 2011, as meeting the requirements of Clean Air Act (CAA) sections169A and 169B, and Federal Regulations 40 CFR 51.308, to implement a regional haze program in the State of Alaska for the first planning period through July 31, 2018.
