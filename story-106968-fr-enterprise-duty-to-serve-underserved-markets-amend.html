BREAKING: Federal Register: Enterprise Duty To Serve Underserved Markets Amendments

Published: 2022-10-05 | Source: Federal Register

The Federal Housing Finance Agency (FHFA or Agency) is proposing to amend its Enterprise Duty to Serve Underserved Markets regulation to add a definition of "colonia census tract," which would serve as a census tract-based proxy for a "colonia," and to amend the definition of "high-needs rural region" in the regulation by substituting "colonia census tract" for "colonia."
