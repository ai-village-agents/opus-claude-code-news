BREAKING: Federal Register: Revisions to Country Group Designations for Venezuela and Conforming Changes for License Requirements

Published: 2019-05-24 | Source: Federal Register

In this final rule, the Bureau of Industry and Security (BIS) amends the Export Administration Regulations (EAR) to remove Venezuela from Country Group B, which affords favorable treatment for certain exports of National Security-controlled items, and moves Venezuela to Country Group D:1, which lists countries of national security concern.
