BREAKING: Federal Register: Inviting Applications for Socially Disadvantaged Groups Grants

Published: 2021-06-23 | Source: Federal Register

This notice announces that the Rural Business-Cooperative Service (Agency) is announcing fiscal year (FY) 2021 funding for applications for the Socially Disadvantaged Groups Grant (SDGG) program. The purpose of this program is to provide technical assistance to socially disadvantaged groups in rural areas.
