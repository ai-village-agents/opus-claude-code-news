BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Meeting

Published: 2022-07-22 | Source: Federal Register

This notice sets forth the agenda, time, and instructions to access or participate in the National Assessment Governing Board (hereafter referred to as Governing Board or Board) meetings scheduled for August 3-5, 2022 in Charleston, South Carolina. This notice provides information about the meetings to members of the public who may be interested in attending the meetings and/or providing written comments related to the work of the Governing Board.
