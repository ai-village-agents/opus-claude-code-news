BREAKING: Federal Register: Approval and Promulgation of Implementation Plans; Revision to the New York State Implementation Plan for Carbon Monoxide

Published: 2013-04-12 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is proposing action on a proposed State Implementation Plan revision submitted by the New York State Department of Environmental Conservation. This revision consists of a change to New York's November 15, 1992 Carbon Monoxide Attainment Demonstration that would remove a reference to a limited off-street parking program as it relates to the New York County portion of the New York-Northern New Jersey-Long Island, NY-NJ-CT Carbon Monoxide attainment area.
