BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; The Boeing Company Airplanes

Published: 2014-05-27 | Source: Federal Register

We are adopting a new airworthiness directive (AD) for certain The Boeing Company Model 757-200, -200PF, -200CB, and -300 series airplanes. This AD was prompted by reports of cracking of the forward bulkhead web, web stiffeners, attachment angles, and thermal anti-ice (TAI) spray ring assemblies of the engine air intake cowl.
