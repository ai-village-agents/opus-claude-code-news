BREAKING: Federal Register: Repealing Certain Bridge Load Capacity Evaluation Requirements

Published: 2025-07-01 | Source: Federal Register

FRA proposes to eliminate the Federal requirement that defines the process a track owner must follow when scheduling the evaluation of bridges with no load capacity determination. The requirement was intended as a transitional measure to phase in compliance after the bridge safety regulations became effective.
