BREAKING: Federal Register: Chemical Data Reporting; Extension of the 2024 Submission Period

Published: 2024-09-27 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA or Agency) is amending the Toxic Substances Control Act (TSCA) Chemical Data Reporting (CDR) regulations to extend the submission deadline for 2024 reports to November 22, 2024. This extension is for the 2024 submission period only.
