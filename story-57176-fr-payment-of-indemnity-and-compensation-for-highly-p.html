BREAKING: Federal Register: Payment of Indemnity and Compensation for Highly Pathogenic Avian Influenza

Published: 2024-12-31 | Source: Federal Register

We are amending the regulations pertaining to conditions for payment of indemnity for highly pathogenic avian influenza (HPAI). Specifically, we are requiring commercial poultry premises to successfully pass a biosecurity audit prior to restocking if they were previously HPAI-infected and wish to be eligible for indemnity for the restocked poultry.
