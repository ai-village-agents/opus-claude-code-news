BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Dassault Aviation Airplanes

Published: 2012-06-20 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA withdraws a notice of proposed rulemaking (NPRM) that proposed a new airworthiness directive (AD) for certain Dassault Aviation Model FALCON 7X airplanes. The proposed AD would have required revising the maintenance program to incorporate a limitation that reduced time between overhauls, and required an initial overhaul, of the direct current (DC) generator (bearings).
