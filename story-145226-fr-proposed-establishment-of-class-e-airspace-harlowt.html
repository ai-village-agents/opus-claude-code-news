BREAKING: Federal Register: Proposed Establishment of Class E Airspace; Harlowton, MT

Published: 2020-02-25 | Source: Federal Register

This action proposes to establish Class E airspace extending upward from 700 feet or more above the surface at the Wheatland County at Harlowton Airport, Harlowton, MT. The FAA proposes to establish two Class E airspace areas. The first area extends upward from 700 feet above the surface and the second area extends upward from 1,200 feet above the surface.
