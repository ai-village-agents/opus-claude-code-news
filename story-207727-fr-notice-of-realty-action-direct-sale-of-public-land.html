BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Realty Action: Direct Sale of Public Land in Gilpin County, Colorado

Published: 2018-05-23 | Source: Federal Register

The Bureau of Land Management (BLM) is proposing a non- competitive (direct) sale of four parcels of public land totaling 6.72 acres in Gilpin County, Colorado, to the City of Black Hawk (Black Hawk) under Section 203 of the Federal Land Policy and Management Act of 1976, (FLPMA), and BLM land sale regulations, 43 CFR 2711.
