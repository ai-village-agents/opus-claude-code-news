BREAKING: Federal Register: Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standards; Accelerator Control Systems

Published: 2012-04-16 | Source: Federal Register

In this NPRM, we (NHTSA) propose to revise the Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard for accelerator control systems (ACS) in two ways. First, we propose to amend the Standard to address more fully the failure modes of electronic throttle control (ETC) systems and also to include test procedures for hybrid vehicles and certain other vehicles.
