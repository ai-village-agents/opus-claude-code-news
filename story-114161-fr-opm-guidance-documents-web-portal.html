BREAKING: Federal Register: OPM Guidance Documents Web Portal

Published: 2021-01-11 | Source: Federal Register

The Office of Personnel Management (OPM) is announcing an online centralized portal that includes information about our guidance and a searchable, indexed listing of, and links to, our guidance documents. The portal, located on our website, does not displace other listings of or links to our guidance documents in topic-specific sections of our website.
