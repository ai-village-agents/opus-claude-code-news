BREAKING: Federal Register: Limitation on Claims Against Proposed Public Transportation Project-Point of the Mountain Transit Project, Salt Lake County and Utah County, Utah

Published: 2025-10-02 | Source: Federal Register

This notice announces final environmental actions taken by the Federal Transit Administration (FTA) regarding the Point of the Mountain Transit Project, Salt Lake County and Utah County, Utah. The purpose of this notice is to publicly announce FTA's environmental decisions on the subject project, and to activate the limitation on any claims that may challenge these final environmental actions.
