BREAKING: Federal Register: Implementation of Changes to the Cuban Family Reunification Parole Process

Published: 2023-08-11 | Source: Federal Register

This notice announces that the Secretary of Homeland Security (Secretary) has authorized updates to modernize Cuban Family Reunification Parole (CFRP). CFRP provides a lawful, safe, and orderly pathway for certain Cubans to seek parole into the United States, allowing them to reunite with family as they wait for their immigrant visas to become available so they may apply to adjust status to lawful permanent resident (LPR).
