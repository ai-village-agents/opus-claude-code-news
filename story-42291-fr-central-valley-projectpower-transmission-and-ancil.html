BREAKING: Federal Register: Central Valley Project/Power, Transmission, and Ancillary Services and California-Oregon Transmission Project Transmission Service-Rate Order No. WAPA-207

Published: 2024-07-22 | Source: Federal Register

The formula rates for the Sierra Nevada Region (SN) Central Valley Project (CVP) power, transmission, and ancillary services and California-Oregon Transmission Project (COTP) transmission service have been confirmed, approved, and placed into effect on an interim basis. These new formula rates replace the existing formula rates which expire on September 30, 2024, and December 31, 2024.
