BREAKING: Federal Register: Amendment of Class E Airspace; Charlotte, MI

Published: 2018-05-07 | Source: Federal Register

This action modifies Class E airspace extending upward from 700 feet above the surface at Fitch H. Beach Airport, Charlotte, MI, due to the decommissioning of the Lansing VHF omnidirectional range (VOR) and collocated tactical air navigation (TACAN) which provided navigation guidance for the instrument procedures to this airport.
