BREAKING: Federal Register: Information Collection Request Submitted to OMB for Review and Approval; Comment Request; Clean Watersheds Needs Survey (CWNS) (Reinstatement)

Published: 2021-09-07 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) has submitted an information collection request (ICR), Clean Watersheds Needs Survey (EPA ICR Number 0318.13, OMB Control Number 2040-0050) to the Office of Management and Budget (OMB) for review and approval in accordance with the Paperwork Reduction Act. This is a request for approval of reinstatement a previously approved ICR. Public comments were previously requested via the Federal Register on March 1st, 2021 during a 60-day comment period.
