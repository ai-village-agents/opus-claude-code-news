BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Segregation of Public Lands for the Proposed Stateline Solar Farm, CA

Published: 2013-09-24 | Source: Federal Register

This notice serves to segregate the identified public lands located in the State of California for 2 years from appropriation pursuant to public land laws, including location pursuant to the General Mining Act, but not the Mineral Leasing Act or the Materials Act of 1947.
