BREAKING: Federal Register: Veterans Community Integration Resources Military Life Cycle Module

Published: 2019-02-07 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Veterans Affairs (VA) is announcing to the public its intent to develop a "Community Integration Resources" (CIR) Military Life Cycle (MLC) module. MLC modules are learning opportunities that are available at designated touchpoints throughout servicemembers' careers and that help servicemembers plan for transition well before separation.
