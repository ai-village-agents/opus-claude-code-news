BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone; Coronado Glorietta Bay Fourth of July Fireworks, San Diego, CA

Published: 2014-05-21 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard will enforce the Coronado Glorietta Bay Fourth of July Fireworks safety zone on July 4, 2014. This recurring annual marine event occurs on the navigable waters of San Diego Bay in San Diego, California. This action is necessary to provide for the safety of the participants, crew, spectators, safety vessels, and general users of the waterway.
