BREAKING: Federal Register: Privacy Act of 1974: New System of Records

Published: 2012-02-10 | Source: Federal Register

Ex-Im Bank proposes to add a new application form subject to the Privacy Act of 1974 (5 U.S.C. 522a), as amended. This action is necessary to meet the requirements of the Privacy Act which is to publish in the Federal Register a notice of the existence and character of records maintained by the agency (5 U.S.C. 522s(e)(4)).
