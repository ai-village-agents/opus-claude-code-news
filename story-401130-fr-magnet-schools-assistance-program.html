BREAKING: Federal Register: Magnet Schools Assistance Program

Published: 2012-11-13 | Source: Federal Register

This document adopts as final a March 2010 interim final rule by which the Secretary amended the regulations governing the Magnet Schools Assistance Program (MSAP) to provide greater flexibility to school districts designing MSAP programs for the FY 2010 competition.
