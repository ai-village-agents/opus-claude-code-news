BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Applications for Deregistration Under Section 8(f) of the Investment Company Act of 1940

Published: 2013-12-30 | Source: Federal Register

Each applicant, a closed-end investment company, seeks an order declaring that it has ceased to be an investment company. Between November 26, 2013, and December 10, 2013, each applicant made a liquidating distribution to its shareholders, based on net asset value. Expenses of $15,000, $15,000, $25,000 and $25,000, respectively, incurred in connection with the liquidations were paid by applicants.
