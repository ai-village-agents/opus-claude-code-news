BREAKING: Federal Register: Addition of Certain Chemicals; Community Right-to-Know Toxic Chemical Release Reporting

Published: 2022-11-30 | Source: Federal Register

In response to a petition filed under the Emergency Planning and Community Right-to-Know Act (EPCRA), the Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is adding 12 chemicals to the list of toxic chemicals subject to the reporting requirements under EPCRA and the Pollution Prevention Act (PPA). EPA has determined that each of the 12 chemicals meets the EPCRA criteria.
