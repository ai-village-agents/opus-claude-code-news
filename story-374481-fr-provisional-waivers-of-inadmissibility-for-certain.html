BREAKING: Federal Register: Provisional Waivers of Inadmissibility for Certain Immediate Relatives of U.S. Citizens

Published: 2012-01-09 | Source: Federal Register

U.S. Citizenship and Immigration Services (USCIS) intends to change its current process for filing and adjudication of certain applications for waivers of inadmissibility filed in connection with an immediate relative immigrant visa application.
