BREAKING: Federal Register: HHS-Operated Risk Adjustment Methodology Meeting; March 25, 2016

Published: 2016-01-11 | Source: Federal Register

This notice announces a meeting on the HHS-operated risk adjustment program, which is open to the public. The purpose of this stakeholder meeting is to solicit feedback on the HHS-operated risk adjustment methodology and to discuss potential improvements to the HHS risk adjustment methodology for the 2018 benefit year and beyond.
