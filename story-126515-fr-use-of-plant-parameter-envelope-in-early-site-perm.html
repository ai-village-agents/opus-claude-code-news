BREAKING: Federal Register: Use of Plant Parameter Envelope in Early Site Permit Applications

Published: 2021-06-24 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Nuclear Regulatory Commission (NRC) is issuing for public comment a draft regulatory guide (DG), DG-4029, "Use of Plant Parameter Envelope in Early Site Permit Applications." DG-4029 is a new regulatory guide that proposes guidance for nuclear power plant applicants that elect to use the plant parameter envelope (PPE) concept to assume certain design parameters for an early site permit (ESP) application when a specific reactor technology has not been selected for a proposed site.
