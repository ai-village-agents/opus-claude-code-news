BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone; Vessel Launch, San Diego Bay, San Diego, CA

Published: 2023-10-27 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is establishing a temporary safety zone for navigable waters in the vicinity of General Dynamics NASSCO shipyard in San Diego Bay, San Diego, CA, during the launch of the USNS Robert Kennedy. The safety zone is needed to protect personnel, vessels, and the marine environment from potential hazards associated with the launching and subsequent berthing of the USNS Robert Kennedy.
