BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Pratt & Whitney Division Turbofan Engines

Published: 2014-12-24 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA is correcting an airworthiness directive (AD) that published in the Federal Register. That AD applies to all Pratt & Whitney Division (PW) PW4074, PW4074D, PW4077, PW4077D, PW4084D, PW4090, and PW4090-3 turbofan engine models with certain second-stage high-pressure turbine (HPT) air seals installed.
