BREAKING: Federal Register: Reporting on Border Gateway Protocol Risk Mitigation Progress; Secure Internet Routing

Published: 2024-06-17 | Source: Federal Register

In this document, the Federal Communications Commission (Commission) seeks to increase the security of the information routed across the internet by proposing certain reporting obligations on providers of broadband internet access service (BIAS providers) and their use of the Border Gateway Protocol (BGP).
