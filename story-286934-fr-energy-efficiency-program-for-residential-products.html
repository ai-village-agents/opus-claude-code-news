BREAKING: Federal Register: Energy Efficiency Program for Residential Products: Energy Conservation Standards for Residential Dishwashers

Published: 2015-02-23 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Department of Energy (DOE) has published a notice of proposed rulemaking (NOPR) which is proposing energy conservation standards for residential dishwashers. The comment period for the NOPR pertaining to the energy conservation standards for residential dishwasher products ended February 17, 2015. DOE is reopening the comment period for comments related to the analysis that estimates the potential economic impacts and energy savings that could result from an energy conservation standard...
