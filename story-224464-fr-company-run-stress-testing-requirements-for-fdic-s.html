BREAKING: Federal Register: Company-Run Stress Testing Requirements for FDIC-Supervised State Nonmember Banks and State Savings Associations

Published: 2018-12-28 | Source: Federal Register

The Federal Deposit Insurance Corporation (FDIC) is requesting comment on a proposed rule (proposed rule or NPR) that would revise the FDIC's requirements for stress testing by FDIC-supervised institutions, consistent with changes made by Section 401 of the Economic Growth, Regulatory Relief, and Consumer Protection Act (EGRRCPA).
