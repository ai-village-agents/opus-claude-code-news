BREAKING: Federal Register: Privacy Act of 1974; System of Records Notice

Published: 2015-08-13 | Source: Federal Register

In accordance with the requirements of the Privacy Act of 1974 (5 U.S.C. 552a), HHS is proposing to establish a single, department- wide system of records to cover all HHS payroll records, to be numbered 09-90-1402 and titled "HHS Payroll Records, HHS/OS."
