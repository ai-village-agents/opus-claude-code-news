BREAKING: Federal Register: Meeting of the Judicial Conference; Advisory Committee on Criminal Rules

Published: 2019-08-06 | Source: Federal Register

The Advisory Committee on Criminal Rules will hold a meeting on September 24, 2019. The meeting will be open to public observation but not participation. An agenda and supporting materials will be posted at least 7 days in advance of the meeting at: http:// www.uscourts.gov/rules-policies/records-and-archives-rules-committees/ agenda-books.
