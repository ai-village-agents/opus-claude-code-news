BREAKING: Federal Register: Pilot Records Database

Published: 2020-03-30 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA is proposing to require the use of an electronic Pilot Records Database (PRD) and implement statutory requirements. The PRD would be used to facilitate the sharing of pilot records among air carriers and other operators in an electronic data system managed by the FAA.
