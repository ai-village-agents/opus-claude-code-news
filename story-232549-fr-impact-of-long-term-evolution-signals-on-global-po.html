BREAKING: Federal Register: Impact of Long Term Evolution Signals on Global Positioning System Receivers

Published: 2017-04-21 | Source: Federal Register

The National Institute of Standards and Technology (NIST) announces that National Advanced Spectrum and Communications Test Network (NASCTN) will hold a public meeting on May 4, 2017 to inform the public about the NASCTN project "Impact of Long Term Evolution (LTE) signals on Global Positioning System (GPS) Devices". At this meeting, the public will learn about this project, as described in the report released to the public on February 15, 2017, available at: http://nvlpubs.nist.gov/nistpubs/Tec...
