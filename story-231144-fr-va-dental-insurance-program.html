BREAKING: Federal Register: VA Dental Insurance Program

Published: 2017-04-04 | Source: Federal Register

This document revises Department of Veterans Affairs (VA) medical regulations to reflect the codification of the authority for the VA Dental Insurance Program (VADIP), a program through which VA contracts with private dental insurers to offer premium-based dental insurance to enrolled veterans and certain survivors and dependents of veterans.
