BREAKING: Federal Register: Air Plan Approval; Minnesota; 2008 Ozone Transport

Published: 2017-12-11 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is approving a May 26, 2016, State Implementation Plan (SIP) submission from Minnesota that is intended to demonstrate that the Minnesota SIP meets certain interstate transport requirements of the Clean Air Act (CAA) for the 2008 ozone National Ambient Air Quality Standards (NAAQS).
