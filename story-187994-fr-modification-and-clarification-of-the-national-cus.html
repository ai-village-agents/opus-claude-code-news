BREAKING: Federal Register: Modification and Clarification of the National Customs Automation Program Test Regarding Periodic Monthly Statements

Published: 2019-09-05 | Source: Federal Register

This document announces changes to the U.S. Customs and Border Protection's (CBP) National Customs Automation Program (NCAP) test concerning periodic monthly statements (PMS) to reflect that CBP will identify PMS as paid upon the completion of the funds transfer when payment is made via Automated Clearinghouse (ACH).
