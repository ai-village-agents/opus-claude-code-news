BREAKING: Federal Register: Oil and Natural Gas Sector: Reconsideration of Additional Provisions of New Source Performance Standards

Published: 2014-07-17 | Source: Federal Register

On August 16, 2012, the Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) published final new source performance standards for the oil and natural gas sector. The Administrator received petitions for administrative reconsideration of certain aspects of the standards. Among issues raised in the petitions were time-critical issues related to certain storage vessel provisions and well completion provisions.
