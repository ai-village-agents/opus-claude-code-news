BREAKING: Federal Register: Centennial Challenges Space Robotics Challenge Phase 2

Published: 2019-08-09 | Source: Federal Register

Phase 2 of the Space Robotics Challenge is open, and teams that wish to compete may now register. Centennial Challenges is a program of prize competitions to stimulate innovation in technologies of interest and value to NASA and the nation.
