BREAKING: Federal Register: Material Threat Medical Countermeasure Priority Review Vouchers; Draft Guidance for Industry; Availability

Published: 2018-01-19 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA or Agency) is announcing the availability of a draft guidance for industry entitled "Material Threat Medical Countermeasure Priority Review Vouchers." There is stakeholder interest in FDA's implementation of the provision of the 21st Century Cures Act (Cures Act) that adds a new section to the Federal Food, Drug, and Cosmetic Act (FD&C Act) on priority review vouchers for material threat medical countermeasure applications.
