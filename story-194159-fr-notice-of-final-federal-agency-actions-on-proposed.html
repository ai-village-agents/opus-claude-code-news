BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Final Federal Agency Actions on Proposed Highway in Utah

Published: 2019-11-25 | Source: Federal Register

The FHWA, on behalf of UDOT, is issuing this notice to announce actions taken by UDOT and NPS that are final Federal agency actions. The final agency actions relate to a proposed highway project, improvements to State Route 12 (SR-12), from milepost (MP) 14.42 to MP 18.16 in the Bryce Canyon National Park (BCRA), Garfield County, State of Utah.
