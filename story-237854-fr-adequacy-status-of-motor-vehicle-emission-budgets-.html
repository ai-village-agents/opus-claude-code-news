BREAKING: Federal Register: Adequacy Status of Motor Vehicle Emission Budgets in Submitted Ozone Attainment Plan for San Joaquin Valley, California

Published: 2017-06-29 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is notifying the public that the Agency has found that the motor vehicle emission budgets (MVEBs or "budgets") for ozone for the years 2018, 2021, 2024, 2027, 2030, and 2031 in the San Joaquin Valley 2016 Plan for the 2008 8-Hour Ozone Standard ("2016 Ozone Plan") are adequate for transportation conformity purposes for the 2008 8-hour ozone national ambient air quality standards (NAAQS).
