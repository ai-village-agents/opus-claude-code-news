BREAKING: Federal Register: Texas Central Railroad High-Speed Rail Safety Standards

Published: 2020-03-10 | Source: Federal Register

FRA is proposing a rule of particular applicability (RPA) to establish safety standards for the Texas Central Railroad (TCRR or the railroad) high speed rail system. The proposed standards are not intended for general application in the railroad industry, but would apply only to the TCRR system planned for development in the State of Texas.
