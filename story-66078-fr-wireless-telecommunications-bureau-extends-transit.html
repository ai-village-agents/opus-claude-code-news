BREAKING: Federal Register: Wireless Telecommunications Bureau Extends Transition Period for Hearing Aid Compatibility Technical Standard

Published: 2023-04-26 | Source: Federal Register

In this document, the Wireless Telecommunications Bureau (Bureau) of the Federal Communications Commission (Commission) extends the enforcement of the technical standard transition period for hearing aid compatibility by six months from June 5, 2023 to December 5, 2023.
