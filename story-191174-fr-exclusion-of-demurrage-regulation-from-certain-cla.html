BREAKING: Federal Register: Exclusion of Demurrage Regulation From Certain Class Exemptions

Published: 2019-10-15 | Source: Federal Register

The Surface Transportation Board (STB or Board) proposes to clarify its regulations governing exemptions for certain miscellaneous commodities and boxcar transportation so that those regulations unambiguously state that demurrage continues to be subject to Board regulation.
