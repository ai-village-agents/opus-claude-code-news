BREAKING: Federal Register: Rescission of Portions of Permanent Program Performance Standards Related to Siltation Structures

Published: 2025-11-28 | Source: Federal Register

This direct final rule revises the Federal regulations to remove paragraphs that required that all surface drainage from the disturbed area pass through a siltation structure before leaving the permit area.
