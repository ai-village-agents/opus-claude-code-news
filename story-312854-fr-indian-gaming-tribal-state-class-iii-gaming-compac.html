BREAKING: Federal Register: Indian Gaming; Tribal-State Class III Gaming Compacts Taking Effect in the State of New Mexico

Published: 2015-12-29 | Source: Federal Register

The Pueblo of Nambe and State of New Mexico entered into a Tribal-State compact governing Class III gaming; this notice announces that the compact is taking effect.
