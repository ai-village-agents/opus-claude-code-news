BREAKING: Federal Register: Assistance to States for the Education of Children With Disabilities

Published: 2019-10-21 | Source: Federal Register

The Individuals with Disabilities Education Act (IDEA) established the National Instructional Materials Access Center (NIMAC) in 2004 to assist State educational agencies (SEAs) and local educational agencies (LEAs) to produce accessible instructional materials for students with print disabilities.
