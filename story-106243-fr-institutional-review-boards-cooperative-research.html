BREAKING: Federal Register: Institutional Review Boards; Cooperative Research

Published: 2022-09-28 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA or we) is proposing to replace current requirements for FDA-regulated cooperative research with new requirements that would require any institution located in the United States participating in FDA-regulated cooperative research to rely on review and approval by a single institutional review board (IRB) for that portion of the research that is conducted in the United States, with some exceptions.
