BREAKING: Federal Register: Eligibility Criteria for Expanded Conditional Approval of New Animal Drugs; Draft Guidance for Industry; Availability

Published: 2019-09-30 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA or Agency) is announcing the availability of a draft guidance for industry (GFI) #261 entitled "Eligibility Criteria for Expanded Conditional Approval of New Animal Drugs." This draft guidance is intended for persons interested in pursuing conditional approval of new animal drugs for certain major uses in major species.
