BREAKING: Federal Register: Addition of the United States Space Force as a Registration Waiver and Registration Fee Exempt Military Entity

Published: 2021-09-17 | Source: Federal Register

This final rule amends existing regulations to include the United States Space Force as a registration waiver and registration fee exempt military entity.
