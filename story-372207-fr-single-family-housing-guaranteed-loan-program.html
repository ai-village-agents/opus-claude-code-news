BREAKING: Federal Register: Single Family Housing Guaranteed Loan Program

Published: 2013-12-09 | Source: Federal Register

The Rural Housing Service (RHS) is streamlining and reengineering its Single Family Housing Guaranteed Loan Program (SFHGLP) regulation. This action is taken to reduce regulations, improve customer service, achieve greater efficiency, flexibility, and effectiveness in managing the program.
