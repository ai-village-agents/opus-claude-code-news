BREAKING: Federal Register: Energy Conservation Program: Test Procedures for Cooking Products

Published: 2021-11-04 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Department of Energy ("DOE") proposes to establish a test procedure for a category of cooking products, i.e., conventional cooking tops, under a proposed new appendix.
