BREAKING: Federal Register: Processes and Procedures for Issuance and Use of Guidance Documents

Published: 2021-07-16 | Source: Federal Register

This interim final rule ("rule") implements Executive Order 13992, which, among other things, revoked Executive Order 13891 and directed the heads of all agencies to promptly take steps to rescind any orders, rules, regulations, guidelines, or policies, or portions thereof, implementing or enforcing the revoked Executive Order.
