BREAKING: Federal Register: Energy Conservation Program: Energy Conservation Standards for Fluorescent Lamp Ballasts

Published: 2023-03-27 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Department of Energy ("DOE") is initiating an effort to determine whether to amend the current energy conservation standards for fluorescent lamp ballasts ("FLB").
