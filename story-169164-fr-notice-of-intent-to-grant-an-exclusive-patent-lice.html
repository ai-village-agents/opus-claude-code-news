BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Intent To Grant an Exclusive Patent License

Published: 2020-12-28 | Source: Federal Register

Pursuant to the Bayh-Dole Act, and implementing regulations, the Department of the Air Force hereby gives notice of its intent to grant an exclusive patent license to Inquis Medical Inc., a small business having a place of business at 1735 East Bayshore Rd, Suite 4, Redwood City, CA 94063.
