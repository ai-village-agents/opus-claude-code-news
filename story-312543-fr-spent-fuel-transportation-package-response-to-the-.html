BREAKING: Federal Register: Spent Fuel Transportation Package Response to the Newhall Pass Fire Scenario

Published: 2015-12-24 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Nuclear Regulatory Commission (NRC) is issuing for public comment a draft NUREG/CR, NUREG/CR-7207, "Spent Fuel Transportation Package Response to the Newhall Pass Fire Scenario." This report presents analyses that were performed to examine the hypothetical effects on a spent fuel transportation package from conditions during the Newhall Pass accident in 2007.
