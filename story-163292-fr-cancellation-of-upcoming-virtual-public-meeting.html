BREAKING: Federal Register: Cancellation of Upcoming Virtual Public Meeting

Published: 2020-10-13 | Source: Federal Register

The Federal Prevailing Rate Advisory Committee is issuing this notice to cancel the October 15, 2020, virtual public meeting scheduled to be held via teleconference, beginning at 10:00 a.m. (EDT). The original Federal Register notice announcing this virtual public meeting was published Monday, September 28, 2020, at 85 FR 60844.
