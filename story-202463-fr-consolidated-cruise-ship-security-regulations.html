BREAKING: Federal Register: Consolidated Cruise Ship Security Regulations

Published: 2018-03-19 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is issuing a final rule to eliminate outdated regulations that imposed unnecessary screening requirements on cruise ships and cruise ship terminals. This final rule replaces these outdated regulations with simpler, consolidated regulations that provide efficient and clear requirements for the screening of baggage, personal items, and persons on a cruise ship.
