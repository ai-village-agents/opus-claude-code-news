BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Cessna Aircraft Company Airplanes

Published: 2013-02-12 | Source: Federal Register

We are adopting a new airworthiness directive (AD) for certain Cessna Aircraft Company Models 172R and 172S airplanes. This AD was prompted by reports of chafing of a new configuration of the fuel return line assembly, which was caused by the fuel return line assembly rubbing against the right steering tube assembly during rudder pedal actuation.
