BREAKING: Federal Register: Termination of the In-Bond Export Consolidator Program and Associated Bond

Published: 2022-02-11 | Source: Federal Register

This document announces the termination of the In-Bond Export Consolidator program (IBEC program) and the associated bond, known as the In-Bond Export Consolidation bond (IBEC bond), implemented at Customs District 52 (Miami).
