BREAKING: Federal Register: Maritime Environmental and Technical Assistance (META) Program Forum

Published: 2015-07-01 | Source: Federal Register

The Maritime Administration (MARAD), in cooperation with the Maryland Environmental Resource Center, will hold an open forum to solicit individual input to MARAD on the Agency's Maritime Environmental and Technical Assistance (META) Program and key environmental issues facing maritime transportation.
