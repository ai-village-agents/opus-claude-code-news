BREAKING: Federal Register: Effluent Limitations Guidelines and Standards for the Steam Electric Power Generating Point Source Category

Published: 2013-06-07 | Source: Federal Register

EPA is proposing a regulation that would strengthen the controls on discharges from certain steam electric power plants by revising technology-based effluent limitations guidelines and standards for the steam electric power generating point source category. Steam electric power plants alone contribute 50-60 percent of all toxic pollutants discharged to surface waters by all industrial categories currently regulated in the United States under the Clean Water Act.
