BREAKING: Federal Register: Proposed Amendment of Class E Airspace; Forest City, IA

Published: 2019-05-16 | Source: Federal Register

This action proposes to amend the Class E airspace extending upward from 700 feet above the surface at Forest City Municipal Airport, Forest City, IA. The FAA is proposing this action as the result of an airspace review caused by the decommissioning of the Forest City non-directional beacon (NDB), which provided navigation information for the instrument procedures at this airport.
