BREAKING: Federal Register: Funding Opportunity Title; Risk Management Education and Outreach Partnerships Program

Published: 2012-04-19 | Source: Federal Register

The following paragraph has been added to the beginning of the Summary portion of Federal Register Notice 77 FR 21067, April 9, 2012: The Risk Management Agency (RMA) is changing the Catalog of Federal Domestic Assistance (CFDA) Number from 10.460 to 10.459. The CFDA number is needed in order to process an application through Grants.gov. The original CFDA number 10.460 published in the Federal Register on April 9, 2012, is not valid.
