BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Availability of Record of Decision for the Pocatello Field Office Resource Management Plan/Environmental Impact Statement

Published: 2012-08-15 | Source: Federal Register

The Bureau of Land Management (BLM) announces the availability of the Record of Decision (ROD)/Approved Resource Management Plan (RMP) for the Pocatello Field Office located in southeastern Idaho. The Idaho State Director signed the ROD on July 10, 2012, which constitutes the final decision of the BLM and makes the Approved RMP effective immediately.
