BREAKING: Federal Register: Raw Honey From Ukraine: Termination of Less-Than-Fair-Value Investigation

Published: 2022-04-06 | Source: Federal Register

Based on a withdrawal of the antidumping duty (AD) petition on raw honey from Ukraine by the American Honey Producers Association and the Sioux Honey Association (collectively, the petitioners), we are terminating this less-than-fair-value (LTFV) investigation.
