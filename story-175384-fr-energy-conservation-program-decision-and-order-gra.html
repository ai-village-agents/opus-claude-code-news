BREAKING: Federal Register: Energy Conservation Program: Decision and Order Granting a Waiver to TCL Air Conditioner (Zhongshan) Co., Ltd. From the Department of Energy Central Air Conditioners and Heat Pumps Test Procedure

("TCL AC"), a waiver from specified portions of the DOE test procedure for determining the energy efficiency of specified central air conditioners and heat pumps.
