BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Realty Action: Proposed Non-Competitive (Direct) Sale of Public Land in Slana, Alaska

Published: 2015-09-09 | Source: Federal Register

The Bureau of Land Management (BLM) is proposing a non- competitive (direct) sale of 5 acres of public land in Slana, Alaska, to the adjacent private landowner, Mr. Frederick Voight. The sale would take place under the provisions of the Federal Land Policy and Management Act of 1976 (FLPMA), at no less than the appraised fair market of value (FMV) of $5,500, to resolve an unauthorized use of public lands as a result of a failed homestead headquarters site claim.
