BREAKING: Federal Register: Facilitating Shared Use in the 3100-3550 MHz Band

Published: 2022-12-22 | Source: Federal Register

In this document, the Wireless Telecommunication Bureau (Bureau) selects the four entities that will represent the interest of the new entrants to the 3.45-3.55 GHz band in selecting the Reimbursement Clearinghouse. The four entities selected are NBCUniversal, Nexstar Broadcasting (Nexstar), CTIA-The Wireless Association (CTIA), and the Competitive Carriers Association (CCA).
