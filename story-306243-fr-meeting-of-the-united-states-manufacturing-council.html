BREAKING: Federal Register: Meeting of the United States Manufacturing Council

Published: 2015-10-08 | Source: Federal Register

The United States Manufacturing Council (Council) will hold an open meeting on Friday, October 23, 2015. The Council was established in April 2004 to advise the Secretary of Commerce on matters relating to the U.S. manufacturing industry.
