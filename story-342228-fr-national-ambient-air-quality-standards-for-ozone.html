BREAKING: Federal Register: National Ambient Air Quality Standards for Ozone

Published: 2014-12-17 | Source: Federal Register

Based on its review of the air quality criteria for ozone (O3) and related photochemical oxidants and national ambient air quality standards (NAAQS) for O3, the Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) proposes to make revisions to the primary and secondary NAAQS for O3 to provide requisite protection of public health and welfare, respectively.
