BREAKING: Federal Register: Rural Community Development Initiative (RCDI) for Fiscal Year 2023

Published: 2023-05-04 | Source: Federal Register

The Rural Housing Service (RHS or the Agency), a Rural Development (RD) agency of the United States Department of Agriculture (USDA), announces the acceptance of applications under the Rural Community Development Initiative (RCDI) program for fiscal year (FY) 2023.
