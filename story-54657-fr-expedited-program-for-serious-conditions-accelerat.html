BREAKING: Federal Register: Expedited Program for Serious Conditions-Accelerated Approval of Drugs and Biologics; Draft Guidance for Industry; Availability

Published: 2024-12-06 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA or Agency) is announcing the availability of a draft guidance for industry entitled "Expedited Program for Serious Conditions--Accelerated Approval of Drugs and Biologics." Accelerated approval is one of FDA's expedited programs intended to facilitate and expedite development and review of certain drugs and biological products for serious or life-threatening conditions.
