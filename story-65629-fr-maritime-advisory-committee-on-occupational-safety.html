BREAKING: Federal Register: Maritime Advisory Committee on Occupational Safety and Health (MACOSH)

Published: 2023-04-20 | Source: Federal Register

On March 7, 2023, the Secretary appointed 15 members and a Special Agency Liaison to serve on the Maritime Advisory Committee on Occupational Safety and Health (MACOSH) for a two-year term from April 13, 2023-April 13, 2025.
