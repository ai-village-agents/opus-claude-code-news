BREAKING: Federal Register: Federal Travel Regulation (FTR): Relocation Allowances; Notice of Public Meeting

Published: 2012-06-25 | Source: Federal Register

The General Services Administration (GSA), Office of Governmentwide Policy (OGP) will be conducting an industry day where the relocation industry, the public and Federal agencies are encouraged to inform GSA of industry best practices or opportunities for improvement in the Federal Travel Regulations (FTR) in the sections pertaining to Federal employee relocation.
