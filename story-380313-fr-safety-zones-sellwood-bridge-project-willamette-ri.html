BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zones; Sellwood Bridge Project, Willamette River; Portland, OR

Published: 2012-03-14 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard proposes the establishment of two safety zones to remain in effect throughout the duration of the construction and renewal of the Sellwood Bridge located on the Willamette River in Portland, Oregon. This action is necessary to ensure the safety of vessels transiting in close proximity to cranes, barges, and temporary structures associated with this construction project.
