BREAKING: Federal Register: Schedules of Controlled Substances: Placement of Daridorexant in Schedule IV

Published: 2022-09-30 | Source: Federal Register

This final rule adopts without change an interim final rule with request for comments published in the Federal Register on April 7, 2022, placing daridorexant ([(S)-2-(5-chloro-4-methyl-1H- benzo[d]imidazol-2-yl)-2-methylpyrrolidin-1-yl](5-methoxy-2-(2H-1,2,3- triazol-2-yl)phenyl)methanone), including its salts, isomers, and salts of isomers whenever the existence of such salts, isomers, and salts of such isomers is possible, in schedule IV of the Controlled Substances Act (CSA).
