BREAKING: Federal Register: Changes to the Representation of Others in Design Patent Matters Before the United States Patent and Trademark Office

Published: 2023-05-16 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Patent and Trademark Office (USPTO or Office) proposes to amend the rules of practice in patent cases and the rules regarding the representation of others before the USPTO to create a separate design patent practitioner bar whereby admitted design patent practitioners would practice in design patent proceedings only.
