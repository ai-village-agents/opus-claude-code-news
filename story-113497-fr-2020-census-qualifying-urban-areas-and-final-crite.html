BREAKING: Federal Register: 2020 Census Qualifying Urban Areas and Final Criteria Clarifications

Published: 2022-12-29 | Source: Federal Register

The Bureau of the Census (Census Bureau) delineates urban areas after each decennial census for the purpose of tabulating and presenting data for the urban and rural population and housing within the United States, Puerto Rico, and the Island Areas. The Census Bureau delineated the 2020 urban areas based on 2020 Census of Population and Housing counts and density calculations.
