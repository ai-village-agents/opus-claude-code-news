BREAKING: Federal Register: Aquatic Life Ambient Water Quality Criteria for Aluminum in Freshwater

Published: 2018-12-21 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is announcing the availability of Aquatic Life Ambient Water Quality Criteria for Aluminum in Freshwater. The EPA first released freshwater criteria for aluminum in 1988 to protect aquatic life from harmful effects of aluminum toxicity. The EPA updated its recommended aluminum criteria to reflect the latest science and to provide users the flexibility to develop criteria based on site-specific water chemistry.
