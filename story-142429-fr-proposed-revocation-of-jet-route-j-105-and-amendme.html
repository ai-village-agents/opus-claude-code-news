BREAKING: Federal Register: Proposed Revocation of Jet Route J-105 and Amendment of VOR Federal Airways V-15, V-63, V-272, and V-583 in the Vicinity of McAlester, OK

Published: 2020-01-21 | Source: Federal Register

This action proposes to remove one Jet Route, J-105, and amend four VHF Omnidirectional Range (VOR) Federal airways, V-15, V-63, V- 272, and V-583, in the vicinity of McAlester, OK. The modifications are necessary due to the planned decommissioning of the VOR portion of the McAlester, OK, VOR/Tactical Air Navigation (VORTAC) navigation aid (NAVAID), which provides navigation guidance for portions of the affected air traffic service (ATS) routes.
