BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Inventory Completion: American University, Department of Anthropology, Washington, DC

Published: 2021-10-19 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Anthropology, American University has completed an inventory of human remains, in consultation with the appropriate Indian Tribes or Native Hawaiian organizations, and determined that there is a cultural affiliation between the human remains and present-day Indian Tribes or Native Hawaiian organizations.
