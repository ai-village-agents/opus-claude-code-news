BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Bell Helicopter Textron Canada Limited Helicopters

Published: 2018-08-08 | Source: Federal Register

We propose to supersede airworthiness directive (AD) 2015-22- 02 for Bell Helicopter Textron Canada Limited (Bell) Model 429 helicopters. AD 2015-22-02 requires inspecting the tail rotor (TR) pitch link assemblies. This proposed AD would retain the inspections of AD 2015-22-02 and would require replacing certain pitch link bearings.
