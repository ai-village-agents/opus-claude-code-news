BREAKING: Federal Register: Prohibitions and Restrictions on Proprietary Trading and Certain Interests in, and Relationships With, Hedge Funds and Private Equity Funds

Published: 2014-01-31 | Source: Federal Register

The OCC, Board, FDIC, and SEC (individually, an "Agency," and collectively, "the Agencies") are adopting a rule that would implement section 13 of the BHC Act, which was added by section 619 of the Dodd-Frank Wall Street Reform and Consumer Protection Act ("Dodd- Frank Act").
