BREAKING: Federal Register: New Mexico Regulatory Program

Published: 2012-01-30 | Source: Federal Register

We are approving an amendment to the New Mexico regulatory program (the "New Mexico program") under the Surface Mining Control and Reclamation Act of 1977 ("SMCRA" or "the Act"). New Mexico proposed non-substantive editorial revisions to its rules; substantive revisions and additions to rules concerning ownership and control; and substantive revisions to one rule about retention of sedimentation ponds.
