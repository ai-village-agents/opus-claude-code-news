BREAKING: Federal Register: Group Registration of Unpublished Works

Published: 2017-10-12 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Copyright Office is proposing to create a new group registration option for a limited number of unpublished works. To qualify for this group option, all the works must be created by the same author or the same joint authors, and the author or joint authors must be named as the copyright claimant for each work.
