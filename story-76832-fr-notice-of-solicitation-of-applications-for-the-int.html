BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Solicitation of Applications for the Intermediary Relending Program for Fiscal Year 2024

Published: 2023-09-14 | Source: Federal Register

The Rural Business-Cooperative Service (RBCS or Agency), an agency of the United States Department of Agriculture (USDA), invites applications under the Intermediary Relending Program (IRP) for fiscal year (FY) 2024, subject to availability of funding.
