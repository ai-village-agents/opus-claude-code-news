BREAKING: Federal Register: Hawaii State Plan for Occupational Safety and Health; Proposed Modification of 18(e) Plan Approval

Published: 2012-07-19 | Source: Federal Register

Hawaii administers an occupational safety and health state plan approved by federal OSHA. During the past three years, the state plan has faced significant budgetary constraints and staffing challenges, and has requested federal OSHA assistance to ensure that workers are afforded adequate worker protection during this period.
