BREAKING: Federal Register: Environmental Impact Statement; Queens County, NY

Published: 2017-06-01 | Source: Federal Register

The FHWA is issuing this notice to advise the public that an Environmental Impact Statement (EIS) will be prepared for a proposed construction project known as the Van Wyck Expressway Capacity & Access Improvements to John F. Kennedy (JFK) Airport, in Queens County, New York.
