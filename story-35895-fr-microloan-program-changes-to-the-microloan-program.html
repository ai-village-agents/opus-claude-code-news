BREAKING: Federal Register: Microloan Program; Changes to the Microloan Program Under the Economic Aid To Hard-Hit Small Businesses, Nonprofits, and Venues Act

Published: 2024-05-02 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Small Business Administration (SBA) is amending its Microloan Program regulations to reflect statutory changes to the Microloan Program contained in the Economic Aid to Hard-Hit Small Businesses, Nonprofits, and Venues Act.
