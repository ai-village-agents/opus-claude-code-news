BREAKING: Federal Register: Readjustment Counseling Service Scholarship Program

Published: 2021-11-05 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Veterans Affairs (VA) proposes to amend its regulations by adding new regulations that would govern scholarship programs to certain health care professionals. This rulemaking implements the mandates of the Commander John Scott Hannon Veterans Mental Health Care Improvement Act of 2019 by establishing the Readjustment Counseling Service Scholarship Program (RCSSP).
