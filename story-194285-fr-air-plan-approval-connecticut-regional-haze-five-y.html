BREAKING: Federal Register: Air Plan Approval; Connecticut; Regional Haze Five Year Progress Report

Published: 2019-11-26 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is approving the Connecticut Regional Haze 5-Year Progress Report submitted as a State Implementation Plan (SIP) revision on June 30, 2015. This revision addresses the requirements of the Clean Air Act and its implementing regulations that States submit periodic reports describing progress toward reasonable progress goals established for regional haze and a determination of adequacy of the State's existing regional haze SIP.
