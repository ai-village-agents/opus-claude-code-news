BREAKING: Federal Register: Request for Information on the Use of APIs in Higher Education Data and Student Aid Processes

To assist in its efforts to explore potential uses of Application Program Interfaces (APIs), the U.S. Department of Education (Department) requests information regarding the use of APIs in the context of higher education data and student aid programs and processes at the Department.
