BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Sikorsky Aircraft Corporation (Sikorsky) Model Helicopters

Published: 2015-06-09 | Source: Federal Register

We are adopting a new airworthiness directive (AD) for Sikorsky Model S-92A helicopters. This AD requires installing a main gearbox (MGB) failed pump sensor and vacuum switch wiring, installing an MGB oil auto bypass system, activating Aircraft Management System (AMS) 7.1 software to show a new visual warning, and installing updated enhanced ground proximity warning system (EGPWS) software that includes an aural annunciation of a complete oil pressure loss condition.
