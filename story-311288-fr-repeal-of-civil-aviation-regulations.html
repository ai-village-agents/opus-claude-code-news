BREAKING: Federal Register: Repeal of Civil Aviation Regulations

Published: 2015-12-10 | Source: Federal Register

In accordance with Executive Order 13563 of January 18, 2011, which addresses agency review of existing regulations, including those that may be outmoded or ineffective, the Department of State is repealing our regulations on civil aviation.
