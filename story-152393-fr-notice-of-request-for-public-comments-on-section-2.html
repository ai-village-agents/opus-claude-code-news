BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Request for Public Comments on Section 232 National Security Investigation of Imports of Mobile Cranes

Published: 2020-05-26 | Source: Federal Register

On May 19, 2020, in response to a petition, the Secretary of Commerce (the "Secretary") initiated an investigation to determine the effects on the national security from imports of mobile cranes. This investigation has been initiated under section 232 of the Trade Expansion Act of 1962, as amended.
