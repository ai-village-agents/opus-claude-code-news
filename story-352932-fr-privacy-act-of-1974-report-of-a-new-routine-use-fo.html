BREAKING: Federal Register: Privacy Act of 1974; Report of a New Routine Use for Selected CMS Systems of Records

Published: 2013-04-23 | Source: Federal Register

In accordance with the requirements of the Privacy Act of 1974 (5 U.S.C. 552a), CMS is adding a new routine use for emergency preparedness and response to eight CMS systems of records.
