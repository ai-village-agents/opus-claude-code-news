BREAKING: Federal Register: Security Zone; San Diego Bay, San Diego, CA

Published: 2021-02-24 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is establishing a temporary security zone for navigable waters in the vicinity of U.S. Coast Guard Sector San Diego, CA. The security zone is necessary to protect the official party and the surrounding waterway and structures from terrorist acts, sabotage or other subversive acts, accidents or other causes of a similar nature.
