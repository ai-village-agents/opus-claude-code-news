BREAKING: Federal Register: Draft Regulatory Guide: Preparing Probabilistic Fracture Mechanics Submittals

Published: 2025-08-08 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Nuclear Regulatory Commission (NRC) is issuing for public comment Revision 1 to draft Regulatory Guide (DG), DG-1422, "Preparing Probabilistic Fracture Mechanics Submittals." This DG is proposed Revision 1 to Regulatory Guide (RG) 1.245, "Preparing Probabilistic Fracture Mechanics Submittals."
