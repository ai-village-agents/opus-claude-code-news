BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Availability of the California Offshore Wind Draft Programmatic Environmental Impact Statement

Published: 2024-11-14 | Source: Federal Register

The Bureau of Ocean Energy Management (BOEM) announces the availability of the draft programmatic environmental impact statement (PEIS) for California Offshore Wind. The Proposed Action of the draft PEIS is the identification of programmatic mitigation measures to lessen the environmental impacts of commercial-scale offshore wind energy development in the five lease areas offshore Northern and Central California on the Outer Continental Shelf (OCS).
