BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Proposed Changes to the National Handbook of Conservation Practices for the Natural Resources Conservation Service

Published: 2015-02-03 | Source: Federal Register

Notice is hereby given of the intention of NRCS to issue a new conservation practice standard in the National Handbook of Conservation Practices. This standard is High Tunnel System (Code 325). NRCS State Conservationists who choose to adopt this practice for use within their States will incorporate it into section IV of their respective electronic Field Office Technical Guide.
