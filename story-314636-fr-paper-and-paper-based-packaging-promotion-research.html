BREAKING: Federal Register: Paper and Paper-Based Packaging Promotion, Research and Information Order

Published: 2014-01-22 | Source: Federal Register

This rule establishes a Paper and Paper-Based Packaging Promotion, Research and Information Order (Order). The purpose of the program is to maintain and expand markets for paper and paper-based packaging. The program will be financed by an assessment on paper and paper-based packaging manufacturers (domestic producers) and importers and administered by a board of industry members appointed by the Secretary of Agriculture (Secretary).
