BREAKING: Federal Register: Certain Hot-Rolled Steel Flat Products From the Republic of Korea: Final Results of Antidumping Duty Administrative Review and Rescission of Administrative Review, in Part; 2017-2018

Published: 2020-10-06 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Commerce (Commerce) determines that the producer/exporter individually examined in this administrative review made sales of certain hot-rolled steel flat products (hot-rolled steel) from the Republic of Korea (Korea) at less than normal value during the period of review October 1, 2017 through September 30, 2018.
