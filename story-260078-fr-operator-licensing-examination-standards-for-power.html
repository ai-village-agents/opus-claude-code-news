BREAKING: Federal Register: Operator Licensing Examination Standards for Power Reactors

Published: 2016-04-06 | Source: Federal Register

The public comment period was originally scheduled to close on March 21, 2016. On March 3, 2016, the comment period was extended to April 5, 2016.
