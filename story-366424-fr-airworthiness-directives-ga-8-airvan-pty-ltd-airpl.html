BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; GA 8 Airvan (Pty) Ltd Airplanes

Published: 2013-09-25 | Source: Federal Register

We are adopting a new airworthiness directive (AD) for GA 8 Airvan (Pty) Ltd Models GA8 and GA8-TC320 airplanes. This AD results from mandatory continuing airworthiness information (MCAI) issued by the aviation authority of another country to identify and correct an unsafe condition on an aviation product.
