BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Expansion and Proposed Restructuring of the Digital Opportunity Demonstration Program

Published: 2024-02-27 | Source: Federal Register

On October 17, 2023, HUD issued a Notice of Expansion and Proposed Restructuring of the Digital Demonstration Program (ConnectHomeUSA). ConnectHomeUSA is designed to further the collaborative efforts by HUD-assisted housing organizations, government, industry, and nonprofit organizations to accelerate broadband internet adoption and use in HUD-assisted homes.
