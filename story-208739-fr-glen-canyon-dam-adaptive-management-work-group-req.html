BREAKING: Federal Register: Glen Canyon Dam Adaptive Management Work Group; Request for Nominations

Published: 2018-06-06 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Department of the Interior proposes to appoint members to the Glen Canyon Dam Adaptive Management Work Group (AMWG). The Secretary of the Interior, acting as administrative lead, is requesting nominations for qualified persons to serve as members of the AMWG.
