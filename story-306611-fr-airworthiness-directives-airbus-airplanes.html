BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Airbus Airplanes

Published: 2015-10-13 | Source: Federal Register

We propose to supersede Airworthiness Directive (AD) 2010-04- 03, for all Airbus Model A310 series airplanes. AD 2010-04-03 currently requires accomplishing repetitive detailed visual inspections for cracking around the fastener holes in certain wing top skin panels between the right side and left side of the front and rear spars, and repair if needed.
