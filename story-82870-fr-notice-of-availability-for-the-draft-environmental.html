BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Availability for the Draft Environmental Assessment for the Mexico Pacific Limited Facility

Published: 2023-11-27 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Department of Energy (DOE) has prepared a Draft Environmental Assessment (EA) to determine how to review the potential environmental impacts associated with authorizing Mexico Pacific Limited LLC (MPL) to export natural gas to Mexico and, after liquefaction in Mexico, to other countries from the proposed MPL Facility.
