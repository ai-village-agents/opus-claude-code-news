BREAKING: Federal Register: Information Collection; Request for Comment; National Visitor Use Monitoring; Correction

Published: 2020-09-09 | Source: Federal Register

The Forest Service published a document in the Federal Register on August 20, 2020, in accordance with the Paperwork Reduction Act of 1995, to seek comments from all interested individuals and organizations on the extension of a currently approved information collection, National Visitor Use Monitoring (0596-0110). The document contained the incorrect Expiration Date of Approval.
