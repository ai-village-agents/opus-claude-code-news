BREAKING: Federal Register: Establishment of a New Recreation Fee Area at Minuteman Missile National Historic Site

Published: 2015-06-16 | Source: Federal Register

The National Park Service plans to establish fees for the tour of the Launch Control Facility Delta-01. The proposed amenity fee is intended to provide enhanced tour reservation services through the Recreation.gov system. This reservation service would replace the existing first-come first-serve system for providing tour tickets.
