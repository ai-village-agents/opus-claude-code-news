BREAKING: Federal Register: Payments of Amounts Due Mentally Incompetent Members of the Naval Service

Published: 2019-04-24 | Source: Federal Register

This final rule removes the Department of the Navy (DON) regulation concerning Payments of Amounts Due Mentally Incompetent Members of the Naval Service. Removal is appropriate because the regulation does not affect how the public engages the DON regarding these payments and does not place obligations on the public.
