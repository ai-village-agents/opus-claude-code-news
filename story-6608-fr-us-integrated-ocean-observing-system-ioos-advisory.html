BREAKING: Federal Register: U.S. Integrated Ocean Observing System (IOOS®) Advisory Committee Public Meeting Cancelled

Published: 2025-03-07 | Source: Federal Register

On February 21, 2025, the National Oceanic and Atmospheric Administration published a notice in the Federal Register announcing a U.S. Integrated Ocean Observing System (IOOS[supreg]) Advisory Committee public meeting to be held from March 12th to March 14th, 2025. This notice announces the cancellation of that public meeting.
