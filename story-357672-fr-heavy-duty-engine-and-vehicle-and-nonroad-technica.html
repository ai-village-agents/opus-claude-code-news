BREAKING: Federal Register: Heavy-Duty Engine and Vehicle, and Nonroad Technical Amendments

Published: 2013-06-17 | Source: Federal Register

EPA is proposing to amend provisions in the Medium- and Heavy- Duty Greenhouse Gas Emissions and Fuel Efficiency final rule issued on September 15, 2011. These proposed amendments would eliminate duplicative reporting requirements, reduce inadvertent minor differences between the EPA and NHTSA programs regarding such matters as voluntary early model year compliance, better align testing procedures to market realities, and reduce unnecessary testing burdens.
