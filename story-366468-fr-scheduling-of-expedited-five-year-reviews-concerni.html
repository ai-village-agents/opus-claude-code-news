BREAKING: Federal Register: Scheduling of Expedited Five-Year Reviews Concerning the Countervailing Duty Order and the Antidumping Order on Circular Welded Carbon-Quality Steel Pipe From China

Published: 2013-09-26 | Source: Federal Register

The Commission hereby gives notice of the scheduling of expedited reviews pursuant to section 751(c)(3) of the Tariff Act of 1930 (19 U.S.C. 1675(c)(3)) (the Act) to determine whether revocation of the countervailing duty order and the antidumping duty order on circular welded carbon-quality steel pipe from China would be likely to lead to continuation or recurrence of material injury within a reasonably foreseeable time.
