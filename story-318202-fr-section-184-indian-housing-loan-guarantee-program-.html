BREAKING: Federal Register: Section 184 Indian Housing Loan Guarantee Program Increase in the Loan Guarantee Fee

Published: 2014-03-05 | Source: Federal Register

The Section 184 Indian Housing Loan Guarantee program (Section 184 program) provides access to sources of private financing for Indian families, Indian housing authorities, and Indian tribes that otherwise could not acquire housing financing because of the unique legal status of Indian land, by guaranteeing loans to eligible persons and entities.
