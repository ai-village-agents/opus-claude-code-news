BREAKING: Federal Register: Request for Information Regarding the Small Business Lending Market; Extension

Published: 2017-07-12 | Source: Federal Register

On May 15, 2017, the Consumer Financial Protection Bureau (CFPB or Bureau) published a Request for Information Regarding the Small Business Lending Market (RFI), which provides that comments must be received on or before July 14, 2017. On May 23, 2017, the Bureau received a letter from thirteen industry trade associations requesting a 60-day comment period extension for this RFI.
