BREAKING: Federal Register: Women-Owned Small Business and Economically Disadvantaged Women-Owned Small Business-Certification

Published: 2015-12-18 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Small Business Administration (SBA) is seeking input and comments on certification of Women-Owned Small Businesses (WOSB) and Economically Disadvantaged Women-Owned Small Businesses (EDWOSB) in connection with the Women-Owned Small Business Federal Contract Program (WOSB Program). SBA is planning to amend its regulations to implement section 825 of the National Defense Authorization Act for Fiscal Year 2015 (2015 NDAA).
