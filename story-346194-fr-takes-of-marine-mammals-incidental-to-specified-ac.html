BREAKING: Federal Register: Takes of Marine Mammals Incidental to Specified Activities; Marine Geophysical Survey Off the Central Coast of California, November to December, 2012

Published: 2013-02-06 | Source: Federal Register

Notice is hereby given that Lamont-Doherty Earth Observatory of Columbia University (L-DEO), in cooperation with the Pacific Gas and Electric Company (PG&E) has withdrawn its application for an Incidental Harassment Authorization (IHA).
