BREAKING: Federal Register: Drawbridge Operation Regulation; Snake Creek; Islamorada, FL

Published: 2017-10-26 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard has issued a temporary deviation from the operating schedule that governs the Snake Creek Bridge across Snake Creek, at Islamorada, FL. The deviation is necessary to alleviate the increased traffic congestion on US 1 Highway resulting from relief efforts after the passing of Hurricane Irma.
