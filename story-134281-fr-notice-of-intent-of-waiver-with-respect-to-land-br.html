BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Intent of Waiver With Respect to Land; Brookings Regional Airport

Published: 2021-09-29 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA is considering a proposal to change 1.44 acres of airport land from aeronautical use to non-aeronautical use and to authorize the sale of airport property located at Brookings Regional Airport, Brookings, South Dakota. The aforementioned land is not needed for aeronautical use. The property is located approximately 6 miles south east of the airport, on the north side of 217th Street between 475th Ave. and 476th Ave., just east of the grove of trees.
