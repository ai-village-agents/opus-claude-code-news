BREAKING: Federal Register: Hours of Service of Drivers: National Ready Mixed Concrete Association; Application for Exemption; Final Disposition

Published: 2015-04-02 | Source: Federal Register

FMCSA announces its decision to grant the National Ready Mixed Concrete Association (NRMCA) a limited exemption from the minimum 30- minute rest break provision of the Agency's hours-of-service (HOS) regulations for commercial motor vehicle (CMV) drivers.
