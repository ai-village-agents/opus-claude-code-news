BREAKING: Federal Register: Medication Assisted Treatment for Opioid Use Disorders

Published: 2016-07-08 | Source: Federal Register

This final rule increases access to medication-assisted treatment (MAT) with buprenorphine and the combination buprenorphine/ naloxone (hereinafter referred to as buprenorphine) in the office-based setting as authorized under the United States Code. Section 303(g)(2) of the Controlled Substances Act (CSA) allows individual practitioners to dispense or prescribe Schedule III, IV, or V controlled substances that have been approved by the Food and Drug Administration (FDA).
