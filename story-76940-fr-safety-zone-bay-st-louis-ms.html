BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone; Bay St. Louis, MS

Published: 2023-09-15 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is establishing a temporary safety zone for navigable waters at the opening of Bay St. Louis, extending the entire width of the channel, approximately \1/2\ mile south of the Hwy 90 Bridge. This safety zone is needed to protect personnel, vessels, and the marine environment from potential hazards created by the 2023 Swim Across the Bay.
