BREAKING: Federal Register: Ship Disposal: Ex-USNS COMET

Published: 2014-04-23 | Source: Federal Register

The Maritime Administration (MARAD) plans to dispose of an obsolete vessel the ex-USNS COMET, which is currently located at its Suisun Bay Reserve Fleet in Benicia, California. This action constitutes an undertaking as defined under Section 106 of the National Historic Preservation Act of 1966 (16 U.S.C. 470 et seq.).
