BREAKING: Federal Register: North Dakota: Proposed Authorization of State Hazardous Waste Management Program Revisions and Incorporation by Reference

Published: 2018-06-05 | Source: Federal Register

The state of North Dakota has applied to the EPA for final authorization of the changes to its hazardous waste program under the Resource Conservation and Recovery Act (RCRA). The EPA has reviewed North Dakota's application and has determined that these changes satisfy all requirements needed to qualify for final authorization and is proposing to authorize the state's changes.
