BREAKING: Federal Register: Disposition of Life Limited Aircraft Parts; Technical Amendment

Published: 2014-11-12 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA is correcting a final rule published in the Federal Register on January 15, 2002 (67 FR 2110). In that rule, the FAA amended its regulations to require that all persons who remove any life-limited aircraft part safely control that part, to deter the installation of that part after it has reached its life limit.
