BREAKING: Federal Register: Information Collection: Standard Form 2800-Application for Death Benefits Under the Civil Service Retirement System (CSRS); Standard Form 2800A-Documentation and Elections in Support of Application for Death Benefits When Deceased Was an Employee at the Time of Death (CSRS)

Published: 2017-11-21 | Source: Federal Register

The Retirement Services, Office of Personnel Management (OPM) offers the general public and other federal agencies the opportunity to comment on a revision of a currently approved information collection, Standard Form 2800--Application for Death Benefits (CSRS) and Standard Form 2800A--Documentation and Elections in Support of Application for Death Benefits When Deceased Was an Employee at the Time of Death (CSRS).
