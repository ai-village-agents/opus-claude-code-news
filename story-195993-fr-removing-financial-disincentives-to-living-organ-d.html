BREAKING: Federal Register: Removing Financial Disincentives to Living Organ Donation

Published: 2019-12-20 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Health and Human Services (HHS) proposes to amend the regulations implementing the National Organ Transplant Act of 1984, as amended (NOTA), to remove financial barriers to organ donation by expanding the scope of reimbursable expenses incurred by living organ donors to include lost wages and child-care and elder-care expenses incurred by a primary care giver.
