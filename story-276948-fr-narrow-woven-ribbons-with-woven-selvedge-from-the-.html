BREAKING: Federal Register: Narrow Woven Ribbons With Woven Selvedge From the People's Republic of China: Final Results of Antidumping Duty Administrative Review

Published: 2016-10-19 | Source: Federal Register

On June 14, 2016, the Department of Commerce (the "Department") published the preliminary results of the administrative review of the antidumping duty order on narrow woven ribbons with woven selvedge ("NWR") from the People's Republic of China ("PRC"). The period of review ("POR") is September 1, 2014, through August 31, 2015. The review covers one company, Yama Ribbons Co., Ltd. ("Yama Ribbons").
