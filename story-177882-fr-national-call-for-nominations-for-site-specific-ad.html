BREAKING: Federal Register: National Call for Nominations for Site-specific Advisory Councils

Published: 2019-04-30 | Source: Federal Register

The purpose of this notice is to request public nominations for five Bureau of Land Management (BLM) citizens' advisory councils affiliated with specific sites on the BLM's National Conservation Lands and for the North Slope Science Initiative (NSSI) Science Technical Advisory Panel (STAP).
