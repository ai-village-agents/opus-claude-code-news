BREAKING: Federal Register: Privacy Act; Notice of Amended System of Records-Single Family Housing Enterprise Data Warehouse

Published: 2015-03-09 | Source: Federal Register

HUD is proposing to revise information published in the Federal Register about one of its system of records, the Single Family Housing Enterprise Data Warehouse (SFHEDW). The revision implemented under this republication, reflects current corrective and administrative changes to the system of records purpose, location, authority, and records retention captions.
