BREAKING: Federal Register: Amendment to the International Traffic in Arms Regulations: Third Rule Implementing Export Control Reform

Published: 2014-01-02 | Source: Federal Register

As part of the President's Export Control Reform (ECR) effort, the Department of State is amending the International Traffic in Arms Regulations (ITAR) to revise five more U.S. Munitions List (USML) categories and provide other changes. The revisions contained in this rule are part of the Department of State's retrospective plan under E.O. 13563.
