BREAKING: Federal Register: Certain Sleep-Disordered Breathing Treatment Systems and Components Thereof; Commission Determination Not To Review an Initial Determination Granting a Joint Motion To Terminate the Investigation in Its Entirety Based Upon Settlement

Published: 2017-02-15 | Source: Federal Register

Notice is hereby given that the U.S. International Trade Commission has determined not to review the presiding administrative law judge's ("ALJ") initial determination ("ID") (Order No. 44) granting a joint motion to terminate the investigation in its entirety based upon settlement.
