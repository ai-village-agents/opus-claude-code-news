BREAKING: Federal Register: Revisions to the Freedom of Information Act Regulation

Published: 2017-06-30 | Source: Federal Register

The NCUA Board (Board) is finalizing its interim final rule amending its Freedom of Information Act (FOIA) regulation. The FOIA Improvement Act of 2016 amended the FOIA and required agencies to review their FOIA regulations and issue certain amendments by December 27, 2016.
