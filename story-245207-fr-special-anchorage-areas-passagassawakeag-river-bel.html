BREAKING: Federal Register: Special Anchorage Areas; Passagassawakeag River, Belfast Bay, Belfast, Maine

Published: 2017-10-03 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard proposes to establish two special anchorage areas in the Passagassawakeag River in the vicinity of Belfast, Maine. This proposed action is necessary to facilitate safe navigation in that area and provide safe and secure anchorages for vessels less than 20 meters in length.
