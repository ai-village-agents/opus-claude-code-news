BREAKING: Federal Register: Special Local Regulations; Crystal Pier Outrigger Race, San Diego, CA

Published: 2019-05-09 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard will enforce special local regulations for the Crystal Pier Outrigger Race on May 11, 2019. These special local regulations are necessary to provide for the safety of the participants, crews, spectators, sponsor vessels of the event and general users of the waterway.
