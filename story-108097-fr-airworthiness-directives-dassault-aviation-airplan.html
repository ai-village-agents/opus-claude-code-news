BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Dassault Aviation Airplanes

Published: 2022-10-20 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA proposes to supersede Airworthiness Directive (AD) 2022-09-15, which applies to all Dassault Aviation Model FALCON 2000 and FALCON 2000EX airplanes. AD 2022-09-15 requires relocating affected servo-valves and revising the existing airplane flight manual (AFM) to provide temporary information necessary to operate airplanes fitted with at least one affected brake servo-valve.
