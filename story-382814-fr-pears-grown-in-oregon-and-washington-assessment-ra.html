BREAKING: Federal Register: Pears Grown in Oregon and Washington; Assessment Rate Decrease for Fresh Pears

Published: 2012-04-11 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Agriculture is adopting, as a final rule, without change, an interim rule that decreased the assessment rate established for the Fresh Pear Committee (Committee) for the 2011-2012 and subsequent fiscal periods from $0.501 to $0.471 per standard box or equivalent of fresh winter pears handled.
