BREAKING: Federal Register: Drawbridge Operation Regulation; River Rouge, Detroit, MI; Correction

Published: 2024-02-29 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is correcting regulations that published in the Federal Register on December 28, 2023. The final rule announced changes to the operations of all movable bridges over the River Rouge, Detroit, MI, to improve communications and establish winter hours. This correction fixes incorrect language in the regulations.
