BREAKING: Federal Register: National Highway Traffic Safety Administration Electric Vehicle Safety Technical Symposium

Published: 2012-04-24 | Source: Federal Register

The National Highway Traffic Safety Administration (NHTSA) is announcing a technical symposium that will be held in Washington, DC on May 18, 2012 to discuss safety considerations for electric vehicles powered by lithium-ion (Li-ion) batteries.
