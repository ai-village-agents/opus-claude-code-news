BREAKING: Federal Register: Extension of the Application Deadline Date for the Supporting Effective Educator Development Program Grant Application

Published: 2017-05-08 | Source: Federal Register

On April 20, 2017, we published in the Federal Register a notice inviting applications for new awards for fiscal year (FY) 2017 for the Supporting Effective Educator Development (SEED) program. This notice extends the deadlines for transmittal of applications and intergovernmental review. All other requirements and conditions stated in the notice inviting applications remain the same.
