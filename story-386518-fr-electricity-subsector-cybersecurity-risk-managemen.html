BREAKING: Federal Register: Electricity Subsector Cybersecurity Risk Management Process

Published: 2012-05-23 | Source: Federal Register

This serves as public notification of the publication, by the Department of Energy (DOE) of the Electricity Subsector Cybersecurity Risk Management Process guideline. The guideline describes a risk management process that is targeted to the specific needs of electricity sector organizations.
