BREAKING: Federal Register: Electronic Submission of Certain Materials Under the Securities Exchange Act of 1934; Amendments Regarding the FOCUS Report

Published: 2023-04-18 | Source: Federal Register

The Securities and Exchange Commission ("Commission" or "SEC") is proposing to require electronic filing or submission of certain forms and other filings or submissions that are required to be filed with or submitted to the Commission under the Securities Exchange Act of 1934 ("Exchange Act") and the rules and regulations under the Exchange Act.
