BREAKING: Federal Register: Proposed Amendment of Class D and E Airspace; Pendleton, OR

Published: 2020-07-06 | Source: Federal Register

This action proposes to amend Class D airspace at Eastern Oregon Regional at Pendleton Airport. This action also proposes to amend Class E airspace, designated as a surface area. Additionally, this action proposes to establish Class E airspace, designated as an extension to a Class D or Class E surface area. Further, this action proposes to amend Class E airspace, extending upward from 700 feet above the surface, and remove the Class E airspace extending upward from 1,200 feet above the surface....
