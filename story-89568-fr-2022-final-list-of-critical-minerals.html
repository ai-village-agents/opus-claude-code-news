BREAKING: Federal Register: 2022 Final List of Critical Minerals

Published: 2022-02-24 | Source: Federal Register

By this notice, the Secretary of the Interior, acting through the Director of the U.S. Geological Survey (USGS), presents the 2022 final list of critical minerals and the methodology used to develop the list.
