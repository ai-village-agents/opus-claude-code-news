BREAKING: Federal Register: Proposed Agency Information Collection

Published: 2012-03-15 | Source: Federal Register

The EIA invites public comment on a proposed collection of information that EIA is developing for submission to the Office of Management and Budget (OMB) pursuant to the Paperwork Reduction Act of 1995. The EIA is soliciting comments on two proposed actions (1) revisions to the Form EIA-923, "Power Plant Operations Report" and Form EIA-861, "Annual Electric Power Industry Report," and (2) creation of the Form EIA-861S, Annual Electric Power Industry Report (Short Form).
