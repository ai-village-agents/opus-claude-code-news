BREAKING: Federal Register: Angeles and San Bernardino National Forests; California; San Gabriel Mountains National Monument Management Plan

Published: 2015-06-12 | Source: Federal Register

The Forest Service intends to prepare an environmental assessment to establish management direction for the land and resources within San Gabriel Mountains National Monument, designated by Presidential Proclamation on October 10, 2014. The Forest Service, as the responsible agency, proposes to amend the 2006 Angeles National Forest Land Management Plan with a management plan to provide for the protection of the objects of interest identified in the Proclamation.
