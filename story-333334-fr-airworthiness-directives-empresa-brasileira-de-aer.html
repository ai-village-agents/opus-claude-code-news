BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Empresa Brasileira de Aeronautica S.A. (Embraer)

Published: 2014-08-27 | Source: Federal Register

We are adopting a new airworthiness directive (AD) for certain Empresa Brasileira de Aeronautica S.A. (Embraer) Model EMB-135BJ airplanes. This AD was prompted by reports of failure of the bolts that connect the cockpit windshield center-post to the forward fuselage.
