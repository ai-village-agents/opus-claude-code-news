BREAKING: Federal Register: Updating Abbreviated New Drug Application Labeling After the Marketing Application for the Reference Listed Drug Has Been Withdrawn; Draft Guidance for Industry; Availability

Published: 2016-07-11 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA or Agency) is announcing the availability of a draft guidance for industry entitled "Updating ANDA Labeling After the Marketing Application for the Reference Listed Drug Has Been Withdrawn."
