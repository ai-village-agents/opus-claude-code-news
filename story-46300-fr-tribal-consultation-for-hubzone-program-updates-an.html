BREAKING: Federal Register: Tribal Consultation for HUBZone Program Updates and Clarifications and Potential Reforms

Published: 2024-09-06 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Small Business Administration (SBA or Agency) announces that it is holding a Tribal consultation meeting in Washington, DC, concerning forthcoming proposed revisions to the Historically Underutilized Business Zone (HUBZone) program regulations. The proposed rule would amend the 8(a) Business Development (BD) and size regulations to clarify certain policies.
