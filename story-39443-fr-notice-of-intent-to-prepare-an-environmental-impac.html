BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Intent To Prepare an Environmental Impact Statement for the Elm and Turkey Creeks Watershed Project in Buffalo and Dawson Counties, Nebraska

Published: 2024-06-14 | Source: Federal Register

The Natural Resources Conservation Service (NRCS) Nebraska State Office announces its intent to prepare an EIS that will examine alternative solutions to flood risk reduction for the Elm and Turkey Creeks Project watershed plan for these watersheds near Kearney, Nebraska. The Elm Creek and Turkey Creek Watersheds face recurring flooding issues due to limited channel capacity and frequent overland flooding.
