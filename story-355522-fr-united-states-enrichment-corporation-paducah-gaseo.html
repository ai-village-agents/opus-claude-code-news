BREAKING: Federal Register: United States Enrichment Corporation, Paducah Gaseous Diffusion Plant

Published: 2013-05-22 | Source: Federal Register

By letter dated April 2, 2013, the United States Enrichment Corporation (USEC) submitted its Application for Renewal of its Certificate of Compliance (CoC) for the Paducah Gaseous Diffusion Plant (PGDP). The existing CoC (No. GDP-1) authorizes operation of a uranium enrichment facility in Paducah, Kentucky. The Certificate currently has an expiration date of December 31, 2013.
