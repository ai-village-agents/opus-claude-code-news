BREAKING: Federal Register: Supplemental Award; Infant-Toddler Court Program-National Resource Center

Published: 2023-08-22 | Source: Federal Register

HRSA is providing approximately $1.91 million in supplemental award funds under HRSA-22-074 in fiscal year 2023 to the recipient of the Infant-Toddler Court Program (ITCP)--National Resource Center (NRC) award, to support state and local capacity to implement the ITC approach at sites that previously received funding under HRSA-18-123 but that do not currently receive HRSA funding under HRSA-22-073 or HRSA-22-074.
