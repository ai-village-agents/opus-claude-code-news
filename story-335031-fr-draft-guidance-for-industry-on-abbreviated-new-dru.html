BREAKING: Federal Register: Draft Guidance for Industry on Abbreviated New Drug Application Submissions-Refuse To Receive for Lack of Proper Justification of Impurity Limits; Availability

Published: 2014-09-17 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA) is announcing the availability of a draft guidance for industry entitled "ANDA Submissions--Refuse to Receive for Lack of Proper Justification of Impurity Limits." This draft guidance is intended to assist applicants preparing to submit to FDA abbreviated new drug applications (ANDAs) and related submissions (i.e., prior approval supplements) for which the applicant is seeking approval of a new strength of the drug product.
