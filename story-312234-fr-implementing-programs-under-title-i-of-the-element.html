BREAKING: Federal Register: Implementing Programs under Title I of the Elementary and Secondary Education Act

Published: 2015-12-22 | Source: Federal Register

The Secretary of Education (Secretary) is soliciting advice and recommendations from interested parties prior to publishing proposed regulations to implement programs under title I of the Elementary and Secondary Education Act of 1965, as amended (title I). Programs under title I are designed to help disadvantaged children meet high academic standards.
