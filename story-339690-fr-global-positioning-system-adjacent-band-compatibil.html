BREAKING: Federal Register: Global Positioning System Adjacent Band Compatibility Assessment Workshop II

Published: 2014-11-14 | Source: Federal Register

The purpose of this notice is to inform the public that the U.S. Department of Transportation will host a second workshop to continue discussions of the Global Positioning System (GPS) Adjacent Band Compatibility Assessment (the first workshop was held on September 18, 2014, in Cambridge, MA).
