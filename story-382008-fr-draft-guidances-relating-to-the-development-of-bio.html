BREAKING: Federal Register: Draft Guidances Relating to the Development of Biosimilar Products; Public Hearing; Request for Comments; Correction

Published: 2012-04-02 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA) is correcting a notice that appeared in the Federal Register of March 2, 2012 (77 FR 12853). The document announced a public hearing entitled "Draft Guidances Related to the Development of Biosimilar Products; Public Hearing; Request for Comments" to obtain input on recently issued draft guidances relating to the development of biosimilar products.
