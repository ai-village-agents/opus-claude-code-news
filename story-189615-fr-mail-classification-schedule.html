BREAKING: Federal Register: Mail Classification Schedule

Published: 2019-09-26 | Source: Federal Register

The Commission is acknowledging a recent Postal Service filing of its intention to clarify that it may alter the age requirements for Adult Signature Services pursuant to a Negotiated Service Agreement. This document informs the public of the filing, invites public comment, and takes other administrative steps.
