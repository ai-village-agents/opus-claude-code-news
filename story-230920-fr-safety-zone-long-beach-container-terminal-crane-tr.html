BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone; Long Beach Container Terminal Crane Transit; Long Beach, California

Published: 2017-03-31 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Coast Guard is establishing a temporary moving safety zone in the Port of Long Beach around the M/V ZHEN HUA 28. This temporary safety zone is necessary to provide for the safety of the waterway users and the M/V ZHEN HUA 28 during the vessel's transit into the Port Long Beach, its stay at Long Beach Container Terminal (LBCT), and its outbound transit departing LBCT.
