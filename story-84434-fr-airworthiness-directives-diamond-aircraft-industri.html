BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Diamond Aircraft Industries Inc. Airplanes

Published: 2023-12-15 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA is adopting a new airworthiness directive (AD) for all Diamond Aircraft Industries Inc. Model DA 62 airplanes. This AD was prompted by reports of baggage nets installed with defective buckles, which may result in failure of the baggage net to restrain the baggage or cargo, which could lead to injury to the occupants in the case of an emergency landing.
