BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Airbus Helicopters

Published: 2020-05-21 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA is revising an earlier proposed airworthiness directive (AD) for Airbus Helicopters Model SA330J helicopters that proposed to require replacing certain left-hand (LH) and right-hand (RH) hydraulic pumps. The NPRM was prompted by reports that bolts that attach the cover of the hydraulic pump were broken.
