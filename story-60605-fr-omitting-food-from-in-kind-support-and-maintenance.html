BREAKING: Federal Register: Omitting Food From In-Kind Support and Maintenance Calculations

Published: 2023-02-15 | Source: Federal Register

We propose to update our regulations to remove food from the calculation of In-Kind Support and Maintenance (ISM). We also propose to add conforming language to our definition of income, excluding food from the ISM calculation. Accordingly, Supplemental Security Income (SSI) applicants and recipients would no longer need to provide information about their food expenses for us to consider in our ISM calculations.
