BREAKING: Federal Register: Special Local Regulation; Crown Bay, Charlotte Amalie, U.S. Virgin Islands

Published: 2021-10-28 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is proposing to establish a temporary special local regulation for the St. Thomas Lighted Boat Parade marine event for certain navigable waters of Crown Bay, Haulover Cut, and Charlotte Amalie Harbor, St. Thomas, U.S. Virgin Islands. This action is necessary to protect personnel, vessels, and the marine environment from potential hazards created by the lighted boat parade.
