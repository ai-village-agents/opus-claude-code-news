BREAKING: Federal Register: General Provisions; Operating and Strategic Business Planning

Published: 2012-05-01 | Source: Federal Register

The Farm Credit Administration (FCA, we, or our) amends its regulation requiring the board of directors of each Farm Credit System (FCS or System) institution to adopt an operational and strategic business plan (business plan or plan) to include, among other things, outreach toward diversity and inclusion.
