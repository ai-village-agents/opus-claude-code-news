BREAKING: Federal Register: Board of Scientific Counselors Executive Committee; Notification of Public Teleconference and Public Comment

Published: 2017-04-10 | Source: Federal Register

Pursuant to the Federal Advisory Committee Act, Public Law 92- 463, the U.S. Environmental Protection Agency hereby provides notice that the Board of Scientific Counselors (BOSC) Executive Committee (EC) will host a public teleconference. The meeting will be held on Tuesday, April 11, 2017 from 1:00 p.m. to 5:00 p.m. All times noted are Eastern Time and are approximate.
