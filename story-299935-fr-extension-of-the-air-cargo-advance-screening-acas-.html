BREAKING: Federal Register: Extension of the Air Cargo Advance Screening (ACAS) Pilot Program and Reopening of Application Period for Participation

Published: 2015-07-27 | Source: Federal Register

On October 24, 2012, U.S. Customs and Border Protection (CBP) published a notice in the Federal Register that announced the formalization and expansion of the Air Cargo Advance Screening (ACAS) pilot program that would run for six months. CBP subsequently published several notices extending the pilot period and/or reopening the application period to new participants for limited periods.
