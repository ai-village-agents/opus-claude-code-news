BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; M7 Aerospace LLC Airplanes

Published: 2020-10-14 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA proposes to adopt a new airworthiness directive (AD) for M7 Aerospace LLC Model SA26-AT and SA26-T airplanes. This proposed AD was prompted by reports of the airplane power lever linkage detaching from the TPE331 engine propeller pitch control (PPC) shaft.
