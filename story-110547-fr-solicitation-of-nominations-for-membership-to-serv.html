BREAKING: Federal Register: Solicitation of Nominations for Membership To Serve on the Advisory Committee on Infant and Maternal Mortality

Published: 2022-11-22 | Source: Federal Register

HRSA is seeking nominations of qualified candidates for consideration for appointment as members of the Advisory Committee on Infant and Maternal Mortality (ACIMM or Committee). ACIMM advises the Secretary of HHS (Secretary) on department activities, partnerships, policies, and programs directed at reducing infant mortality, maternal mortality and severe maternal morbidity, and improving the health status of infants and women before, during, and after pregnancy.
