BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Publication of the Revised Guidebook for the National Practitioner Data Bank (NPDB)

Published: 2015-06-29 | Source: Federal Register

The National Practitioner Data Bank (NPDB) announces the release of the revised user Guidebook. The NPDB is a confidential information clearinghouse created by Congress and intended to facilitate a comprehensive review of the professional credentials of health care practitioners, entities, and suppliers.
