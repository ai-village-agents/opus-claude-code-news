BREAKING: Federal Register: Final 2014 Effluent Guidelines Program Plan and 2014 Annual Effluent Guidelines Review Report

Published: 2015-08-04 | Source: Federal Register

This notice announces the availability of the Environmental Protection Agency's (EPA) Final 2014 Effluent Guidelines Program Plan and EPA's 2014 Annual Effluent Guidelines Review Report. Section 304(m) of the Clean Water Act requires EPA to biennially publish a plan for new and revised effluent guidelines, after public notice and comment.
