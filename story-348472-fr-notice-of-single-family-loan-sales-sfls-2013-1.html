BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Single Family Loan Sales (SFLS 2013-1)

Published: 2013-03-05 | Source: Federal Register

This notice announces HUD's intention to competitively sell certain unsubsidized single family mortgage loans, in a sealed bid sale offering called SFLS 2013-1, without Federal Housing Administration (FHA) mortgage insurance. This notice also generally describes the bidding process for the sale and describes certain persons who are ineligible to bid. This first sale of Fiscal Year (FY) 2013 is scheduled for March 2013.
