BREAKING: Federal Register: Prescription Drug User Fee Act VII; Independent Assessment of Communication Through Product Quality Information Requests During Application Review; Statement of Work; Request for Comments

Published: 2022-10-14 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA or Agency) is announcing an opportunity for public comment on the Statement of Work to assess communication between FDA and sponsors through product quality information requests during application review and to identify best practices and areas of improvement. The independent assessment is part of FDA performance commitments under the recent reauthorization of the Prescription Drug User Fee Act (PDUFA).
