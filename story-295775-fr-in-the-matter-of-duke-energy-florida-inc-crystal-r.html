BREAKING: Federal Register: In the Matter of Duke Energy Florida, Inc.; Crystal River Unit 3 Nuclear Generating Plant

(DEF), approving the direct transfer of Facility Operating License No. DPR-72 for Crystal River Unit 3 Nuclear Generating Plant (CR-3), to the extent held by eight minority co-owners to DEF.
