BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of a Change in Status of an Extended Benefit (EB) Period for Alaska

Published: 2012-02-13 | Source: Federal Register

This notice announces a change in benefit period eligibility under the EB program for Alaska. The following changes have occurred since the publication of the last notice regarding the State's EB status: Alaska's 13-week insured unemployment rate (IUR) for the week ending January 7, 2012 rose to meet the 6% threshold to trigger ``on'' to the EB program.
