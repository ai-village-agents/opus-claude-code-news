BREAKING: Federal Register: Civilian Health and Medical Program of the Uniformed Services (CHAMPUS)/TRICARE: TRICARE Retail Pharmacy Program

Published: 2012-06-26 | Source: Federal Register

This proposed rule would make several administrative changes to the TRICARE Pharmacy Benefits Program regulations in order to conform them more closely to the statute and to clarify some procedures regarding the operation of the uniform formulary.
