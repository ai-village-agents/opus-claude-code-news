BREAKING: Federal Register: Virtual Hybrid Public Meeting

Published: 2022-09-16 | Source: Federal Register

The Federal Salary Council will hold a virtual hybrid meeting on Friday, October 28, 2022, at the time shown below. There will be no in-person public gathering for this meeting. The Council is an advisory body composed of representatives of Federal employee organizations and experts in the fields of labor relations and pay policy.
