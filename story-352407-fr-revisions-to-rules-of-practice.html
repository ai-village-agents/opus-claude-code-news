BREAKING: Federal Register: Revisions to Rules of Practice

Published: 2013-04-17 | Source: Federal Register

This notice addresses proposed revisions to the Commission's rules of practice. The revisions (as clarified by an errata issued March 19, 2013) are intended to update existing rules due to recent statutory and regulatory changes; make technical editorial changes; and foster clarity and simplicity to reduce the potential for confusion.
