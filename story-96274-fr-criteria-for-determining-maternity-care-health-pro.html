BREAKING: Federal Register: Criteria for Determining Maternity Care Health Professional Target Areas

Published: 2022-05-19 | Source: Federal Register

Section 332 of the Public Health Service Act (PHSA) directs the Department of Health and Human Services (HHS), through the Health Resources and Services Administration (HRSA), to identify Maternity Care Target Areas (MCTA), or geographic areas within health professional shortage areas that have a shortage of maternity care health professionals, for the purpose of providing maternity health care assistance to such health professional shortage areas.
