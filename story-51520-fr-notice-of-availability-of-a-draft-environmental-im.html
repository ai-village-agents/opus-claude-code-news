BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Availability of a Draft Environmental Impact Statement for a New Federal Courthouse in Hartford, Connecticut

Published: 2024-11-01 | Source: Federal Register

Pursuant to the requirements of the National Environmental Policy Act of 1969 (NEPA), GSA has prepared a Draft Environmental Impact Statement (EIS) to analyze the potential impacts from the proposed acquisition of a site in Hartford, CT, and the subsequent design, construction, and operation of a new Federal courthouse.
