BREAKING: Federal Register: Disaster Assistance Loan Program Changes to Maximum Loan Amounts and Miscellaneous Updates

Published: 2023-06-16 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Small Business Administration (SBA or Agency) is amending various regulations governing SBA's Disaster Loan Program in order to expand options for disaster loan recipients as well as reflect the impact of inflationary increases over time that result in higher costs.
