BREAKING: Federal Register: Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standards; Automatic Emergency Braking

Published: 2017-01-25 | Source: Federal Register

This document denies a January 13, 2016 rulemaking petition jointly submitted by Consumer Watchdog, Center for Auto Safety, and Public Citizen. The petition requested NHTSA to begin a rulemaking proceeding to mandate that all light vehicles be equipped with three types of automatic emergency braking (AEB) technologies: Forward crash warning, crash imminent braking, and dynamic brake support.
