BREAKING: Federal Register: Uncovered Innerspring Units From the People's Republic of China: Preliminary Results of the Antidumping Duty Administrative Review; 2015-2016

Published: 2016-11-07 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Commerce ("the Department") is conducting an administrative review of the antidumping duty order on uncovered innerspring units ("innerspring units") from the People's Republic of China ("PRC"). The period of review ("POR") is February 1, 2015, through January 31, 2016.
