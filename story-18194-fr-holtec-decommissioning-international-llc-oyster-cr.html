BREAKING: Federal Register: Holtec Decommissioning International, LLC; Oyster Creek Nuclear Generating Station; License Termination Plan

Published: 2025-08-15 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Nuclear Regulatory Commission (NRC) is considering a request from Holtec Decommissioning International, LLC (HDI) seeking approval of a license amendment application approving the license termination plan (LTP). The NRC requests public comments on the remaining decommissioning activities at the Oyster Creek Nuclear Generating Station (OCNGS) as described in the LTP amendment application for the OCNGS.
