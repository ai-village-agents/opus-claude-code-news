BREAKING: Federal Register: Plant-Specific Applicability of Transition Break Size Specified in 10 CFR 50.46a

Published: 2016-12-08 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Nuclear Regulatory Commission (NRC) is announcing the discontinuation of further regulatory action on Draft Regulatory Guide (DG) DG-1216, "Plant-Specific Applicability of Transition Break Size Specified in 10 CFR 50.46a," and its withdrawal.
