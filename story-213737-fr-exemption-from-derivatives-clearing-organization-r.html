BREAKING: Federal Register: Exemption From Derivatives Clearing Organization Registration

Published: 2018-08-13 | Source: Federal Register

The Commodity Futures Trading Commission (Commission) is proposing amendments to its regulations to establish a regulatory framework within which the Commission may exempt a clearing organization that is organized outside of the United States (hereinafter referred to as "non-U.S. clearing organization") from registration as a derivatives clearing organization (DCO) in connection with the clearing organization's clearing of swaps.
