BREAKING: Federal Register: Public Availability of Railroad Retirement Board FY 2012 Service Contract Inventory

Published: 2013-02-04 | Source: Federal Register

In accordance with Section 743 of Division C of the Consolidated Appropriations Act of 2010 (Pub. L. 111-117), Railroad Retirement Board is publishing this notice to advise the public of the availability of the FY 2012 Service Contract inventories. This inventory provides information on service contract actions, over $25,000, which the RRB awarded during FY 2012.
