BREAKING: Federal Register: Administrative Law Judges

Published: 2020-09-21 | Source: Federal Register

The Office of Personnel Management (OPM) is issuing proposed regulations governing the appointment and employment of administrative law judges (ALJs). The proposed rule will implement Executive Order (E.O.) 13843 titled "Excepting Administrative Law Judges from the Competitive Service."
