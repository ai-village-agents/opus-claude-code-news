BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Inventory Completion: Center for Archaeological Studies, Texas State University, San Marcos, TX

Published: 2015-08-31 | Source: Federal Register

The Center for Archaeological Studies, Texas State University (Texas State), has completed an inventory of human remains, in consultation with the appropriate Indian tribes or Native Hawaiian organizations, and has determined that there is no cultural affiliation between the human remains and any present-day Indian tribes or Native Hawaiian organizations.
