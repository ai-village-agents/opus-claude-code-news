BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Availability of Funds and Solicitation for Grant Applications for National Farmworker Jobs Program Grants

Published: 2013-02-12 | Source: Federal Register

The Employment and Training Administration (ETA), U.S. Department of Labor (DOL or Department) announces a grant competition for the National Farmworker Jobs Program (NFJP), authorized under section 167 of the Workforce Investment Act (WIA). NFJP provides training, employment services, and related assistance in order to increase economic opportunities for migrant and seasonal farmworkers (MSFW) and their dependents.
