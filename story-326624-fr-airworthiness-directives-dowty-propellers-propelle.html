BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Dowty Propellers Propellers

Published: 2014-06-10 | Source: Federal Register

We are revising airworthiness directive (AD) 2008-21-07 for certain Dowty Propellers model R408/6-123-F/17 propellers. AD 2008-21- 07 required initial and repetitive inspections of the blade bonded metallic leading edge (L/E) guards for correct bonding until they accumulate more than 1,200 flight hours (FH) time-in-service.
