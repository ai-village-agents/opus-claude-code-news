BREAKING: Federal Register: Proposed Extension of Approval of Information Collection; Comment Request-Infant Bath Seats

Published: 2013-08-30 | Source: Federal Register

As required by the Paperwork Reduction Act of 1995 (44 U.S.C. chapter 35), the Consumer Product Safety Commission (CPSC or Commission) requests comments on a proposed request for extension of approval of a collection of information for the safety standard for infant bath seats. The Office of Management and Budget (OMB) previously approved the collection of information under control number 3041-0145. OMB's approval will expire on October 30, 2013.
