BREAKING: Federal Register: Defense Business Board; Notice of Federal Advisory Committee Meeting; Cancellation

Published: 2013-10-23 | Source: Federal Register

On Monday, September 23, 2013 (78 FR 58290-58291), the Department of Defense published a notice announcing a meeting of the Defense Business Board (DBB), which was scheduled for Thursday, October 17, 2013. This notice announces the cancellation of the October 17, 2013 meeting. Due to the lapse of appropriations, the scheduled DBB meeting on October 17, 2013 is cancelled.
