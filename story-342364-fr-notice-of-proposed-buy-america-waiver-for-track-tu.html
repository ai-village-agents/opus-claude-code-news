BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Proposed Buy America Waiver for Track Turnout Components

Published: 2014-12-19 | Source: Federal Register

The Federal Transit Administration (FTA) is providing notice of a request from the New York Metropolitan Transportation Authority's Long Island Rail Road (LIRR) to waive Buy America requirements for the purchase of components of high-speed track turnouts that do not meet FTA's Buy America requirements.
