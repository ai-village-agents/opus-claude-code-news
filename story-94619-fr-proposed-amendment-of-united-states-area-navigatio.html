BREAKING: Federal Register: Proposed Amendment of United States Area Navigation (RNAV) Route T-260; Nome, AK

Published: 2022-04-27 | Source: Federal Register

This action proposes to amend United States Area Navigation (RNAV) T-route, T-260 in the vicinity of Nome, AK. The RNAV amendments are necessary due to the planned decommissioning of the Tin City, AK, (TNC) Non-Directional Beacons (NDB)/Distance Measuring Equipment (DME) and the Point Hope, AK, (PHO) NDB.
