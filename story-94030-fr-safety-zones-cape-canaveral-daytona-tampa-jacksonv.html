BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zones; Cape Canaveral, Daytona, Tampa, Jacksonville, and Tallahassee, Florida

Published: 2022-04-20 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is establishing five temporary safety zones for the reentry of capsules launched by Space Exploration Technologies Corporation (Space X) in support of the Axiom and the National Aeronautics and Space Administration (NASA) Crew-3 capsule recovery missions. These five temporary safety zones are located within the Coast Guard District Seven area of responsibility offshore of Cape Canaveral, Daytona, Jacksonville, Tampa, and Tallahassee, Florida.
