BREAKING: Federal Register: Certain Computer Network Security Equipment and Systems, Related Software, Components Thereof, and Products Containing Same; Notice of Commission Determination Not To Review an Initial Determination Granting a Motion for Leave To Amend the Complaint and Notice of Investigation

Published: 2023-03-09 | Source: Federal Register

Notice is hereby given that the U.S. International Trade Commission ("Commission") has determined not to review an initial determination ("ID") (Order No. 32) granting complainants' motion for leave to amend the complaint and notice of investigation to reflect a corporate name change.
