BREAKING: Federal Register: Definition of Troubled Condition

Published: 2013-01-18 | Source: Federal Register

The NCUA Board (Board) is issuing a final rule amending the definition of "troubled condition" as that term is used to trigger the statutory requirement to give the Board notice and an opportunity to disapprove a change of credit union officials, and as that term appears elsewhere in NCUA's regulations.
