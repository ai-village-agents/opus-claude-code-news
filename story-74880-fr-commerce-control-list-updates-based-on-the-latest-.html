BREAKING: Federal Register: Commerce Control List: Updates Based on the Latest Nuclear Suppliers Group (NSG) Plenary Meetings

Published: 2023-08-18 | Source: Federal Register

The Bureau of Industry and Security (BIS) publishes this final rule to amend the Export Administration Regulations (EAR) to reflect changes reached by the Nuclear Suppliers Group (NSG) in its June 2019 plenary meeting in Nur-Sultan (now Astana), Kazakhstan and its plenary meeting of June 2022 in Warsaw, Poland.
