BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Airbus SAS Airplanes

Published: 2023-04-11 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA proposes to supersede Airworthiness Directive (AD) 2006-10-13, which applies to all Airbus SAS Model A330-223, -321, -322, and -323 airplanes. AD 2006-10-13 requires repetitive inspections of the firewall of the lower aft pylon fairing (LAPF), and corrective actions if necessary. AD 2006-10-13 also provides an optional terminating action for the repetitive inspections.
