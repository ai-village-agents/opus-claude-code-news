BREAKING: Federal Register: Longshore and Harbor Workers' Compensation Act: Maximum and Minimum Compensation Rates

Published: 2016-08-26 | Source: Federal Register

The Office of Workers' Compensation Programs is proposing rules to implement the Longshore and Harbor Workers' Compensation Act's maximum and minimum compensation provisions. Some of these provisions, which cap the amounts of compensation and death benefits payable to entitled claimants and provide a floor below which compensation may not fall, have become the topic of litigation.
