BREAKING: Federal Register: Energy Conservation Program: Test Procedures for Commercial Equipment; Early Assessment Review: Commercial Refrigerators, Refrigerator-Freezers, and Freezers

Published: 2021-06-11 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Department of Energy (``DOE'') is undertaking an early assessment review to determine whether amendments are warranted for the test procedure for commercial refrigerators, refrigerator- freezers, and freezers (``CRE''). DOE has identified certain issues associated with the currently applicable test procedure on which DOE is interested in receiving comment.
