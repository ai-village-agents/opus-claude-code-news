BREAKING: Federal Register: Public Meeting on Patient-Focused Drug Development for Patients Who Have Received an Organ Transplant

Published: 2016-04-27 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA or Agency) is announcing a public meeting and an opportunity for public comment on Patient- Focused Drug Development for patients who have received an organ transplant. Patient-Focused Drug Development is part of FDA's performance commitments made as part of the fifth authorization of the Prescription Drug User Fee Act (PDUFA V).
