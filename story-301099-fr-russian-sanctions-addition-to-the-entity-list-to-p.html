BREAKING: Federal Register: Russian Sanctions: Addition to the Entity List To Prevent Violations of Russian Industry Sector Sanctions

Published: 2015-08-07 | Source: Federal Register

This final rule amends the Export Administration Regulations (EAR) to further implement U.S. sanctions on certain Russian energy projects. Specifically, in this rule, the Bureau of Industry and Security (BIS) amends the EAR by adding a Russian oil and gas field, the Yuzhno-Kirinskoye Field located in the Sea of Okhotsk, to the Entity List.
