BREAKING: Federal Register: 2025 Rate Changes for the Basetime, Overtime, Holiday, Laboratory Services, and Export Application Fees

Published: 2024-12-30 | Source: Federal Register

FSIS is announcing the 2025 rates it will charge meat, poultry, and egg products establishments, and importers and exporters for providing voluntary, overtime, and holiday inspection and identification, certification, and laboratory services. Additionally, FSIS is announcing a change to the fee FSIS assesses to exporters that choose to apply for export certificates electronically through the export component of the Agency's Public Health Information System.
