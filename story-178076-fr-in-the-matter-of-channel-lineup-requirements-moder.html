BREAKING: Federal Register: In the Matter of Channel Lineup Requirements; Modernization of Media Regulation Initiative

Published: 2019-05-01 | Source: Federal Register

In this final rule document, we eliminate two unnecessary rules pertaining to cable operators' channel lineups. First, we eliminate the requirement that cable operators maintain at their local office a current listing of the cable television channels that each cable system delivers to its subscribers. Second, we eliminate the requirement that certain cable operators make their channel lineup available through their Commission-hosted online public inspection file.
