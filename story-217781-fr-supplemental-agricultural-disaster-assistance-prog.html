BREAKING: Federal Register: Supplemental Agricultural Disaster Assistance Programs, Payment Limitation and Payment Eligibility

Published: 2018-10-02 | Source: Federal Register

This rule implements changes to the Emergency Assistance for Livestock, Honeybees, and Farm-Raised Fish Program (ELAP); Livestock Indemnity Program (LIP); and Tree Assistance Program (TAP) as required by the Bipartisan Budget Act of 2018 (BBA), including changes to the payment limitations, the funding limitation for ELAP, and losses for injured livestock sold at a reduced price under LIP.
