BREAKING: Federal Register: Criminal Justice Restraints Standard, NIJ Standard-1001.00

Published: 2014-11-19 | Source: Federal Register

The document can be found here: https://ncjrs.gov/pdffiles1/nij/247988.pdf. Conformity assessment organizations are invited to review the document and provide any feedback to the point of contact listed below.
