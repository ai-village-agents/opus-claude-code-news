BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone; Atlantic Intracoastal Waterway, Camp Lejeune, NC

Published: 2012-01-10 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard proposes establishing a safety zone on the Atlantic Intracoastal Waterway (AICW) adjacent to Marine Corps Base (MCB) Camp Lejeune, North Carolina, which encompasses the navigable waters of the AICW between Mile Hammock Bay and the Onslow Swing Bridge in support of military training operations on February 6th and 7th, 2012.
