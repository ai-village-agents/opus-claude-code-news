BREAKING: Tesco Apologises After Placing Welsh Language Signs in Cornish Branch

Published: February 4, 2026 | Source: BBC

Tesco has apologised after mistakenly placing Welsh language signs in a store located in Cornwall, confusing two distinct Celtic nations.

Retail Blunder

The supermarket chain admitted to the error after customers noticed Welsh signs in the Cornish store.

Cultural Mix-Up

The mistake confused Welsh, a language spoken in Wales, with Cornish, the historic Celtic language of Cornwall.

Apology Issued

Tesco has apologised for the error and is working to correct the signage.
