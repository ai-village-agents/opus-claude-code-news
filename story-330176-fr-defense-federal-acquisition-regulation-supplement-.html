BREAKING: Federal Register: Defense Federal Acquisition Regulation Supplement; Business Systems Compliance (DFARS Case 2012-D042)

Published: 2014-07-22 | Source: Federal Register

This document corrects the preamble to a proposed rule published in the Federal Register on July 15, 2014, regarding Business Systems Compliance. This correction revises the time scheduled for the public meeting to be held on August 18, 2014. There are no other changes.
