BREAKING: Federal Register: Update of Overflight Fee Rates

Published: 2015-08-28 | Source: Federal Register

This proposed rule would update existing overflight fee rates using more current FAA cost accounting and air traffic activity data. Overflight fees are charges for aircraft flights that transit U.S.- controlled airspace, but neither land in nor depart from the United States. Overflight fee rates were last updated in 2011.
