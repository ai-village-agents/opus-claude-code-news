BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; McCauley Propeller Systems Governors

Published: 2020-04-07 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA proposes to adopt a new airworthiness directive (AD) for certain model McCauley Propeller Systems (McCauley) governors installed on airplanes. This proposed AD was prompted by reports of an unapproved variant McCauley idler gear bearing, part number (P/N) A- 20028, that could be installed in the affected governors.
