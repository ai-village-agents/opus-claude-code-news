BREAKING: Federal Register: Environmental Impact Statement: Madison County, IL

Published: 2012-05-01 | Source: Federal Register

The FHWA is issuing this notice of intent to advise the public that an Environmental Impact Statement will be prepared for a proposed transportation project in Alton and Godfrey, Illinois in an area bounded roughly by IL Route 3 on the south; Seminary Road on the east; Seiler Road on the north and US 67 on the west.
