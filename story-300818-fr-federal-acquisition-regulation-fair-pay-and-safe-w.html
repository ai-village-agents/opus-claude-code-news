BREAKING: Federal Register: Federal Acquisition Regulation; Fair Pay and Safe Workplaces; Second Extension of Time for Comments

Published: 2015-08-05 | Source: Federal Register

DoD, GSA, and NASA issued a proposed rule (FAR Case 2014-025) on May 28, 2015, amending the Federal Acquisition Regulation (FAR) to implement Executive Order (E.O.) 13673, "Fair Pay and Safe Workplaces," which is designed to improve contractor compliance with labor laws and increase efficiency and cost savings in Federal contracting.
