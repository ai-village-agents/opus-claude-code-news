BREAKING: Federal Register: Cooper Tire & Rubber Company, Receipt of Petition for Decision of Inconsequential Noncompliance

Published: 2022-05-16 | Source: Federal Register

Cooper Tire & Rubber Company (Cooper Tire), has determined that certain Cooper CS5 Grant Touring and Cooper Evolution Tour replacement passenger car tires do not fully comply with Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard (FMVSS) No. 139, New Pneumatic Radial Tires for Light Vehicles.
