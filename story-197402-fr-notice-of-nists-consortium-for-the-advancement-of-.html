BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of NIST's Consortium for the Advancement of Genome Editing

Published: 2018-01-11 | Source: Federal Register

The National Institute of Standards and Technology (NIST), an agency of the United States Department of Commerce is establishing the Genome Editing Consortium with the goal of bringing together stakeholders across the genome editing community to identify and address measurement and standards needs to support this technical area.
