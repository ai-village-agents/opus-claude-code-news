BREAKING: Federal Register: Agency Notice of Record of Decision for the Arecibo Observatory in Puerto Rico

Published: 2017-12-26 | Source: Federal Register

On November 15, 2017, the National Science Foundation (NSF) signed a Record of Decision (ROD) for the Arecibo Observatory in Puerto Rico. This important step concludes the agency's decision-making process with respect to the general path forward for facility operations in a budget-constrained environment, and provides the basis for a future decision regarding a new collaborator.
