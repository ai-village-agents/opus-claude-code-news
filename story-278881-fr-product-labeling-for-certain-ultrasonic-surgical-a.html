BREAKING: Federal Register: Product Labeling for Certain Ultrasonic Surgical Aspirator Devices; Draft Guidance for Industry and Food and Drug Administration Staff; Availability

Published: 2016-11-10 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA or Agency) is announcing the availability of the draft guidance entitled "Product Labeling for Certain Ultrasonic Surgical Aspirator Devices." FDA is providing a specific labeling recommendation in this guidance to promote the safe and effective use of ultrasonic surgical aspirator devices.
