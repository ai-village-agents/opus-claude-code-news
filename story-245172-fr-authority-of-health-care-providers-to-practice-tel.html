BREAKING: Federal Register: Authority of Health Care Providers To Practice Telehealth

Published: 2017-10-02 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Veterans Affairs (VA) proposes to amend its medical regulations by standardizing the delivery of care by VA health care providers through telehealth. This rule would ensure that VA health care providers provide the same level of care to all beneficiaries, irrespective of the State or location in a State of the VA health care provider or the beneficiary.
