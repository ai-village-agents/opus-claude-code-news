BREAKING: Federal Register: Medicare Program: Notice of Six Membership Appointments to the Advisory Panel on Hospital Outpatient Payment

Published: 2012-02-16 | Source: Federal Register

This notice announces six new membership appointments to the Advisory Panel on Hospital Outpatient Payment (HOP, the Panel). The six appointments to the 19 member Panel will each serve a 4-year period. Five of the new members will have terms that begin on February 1, 2012 and continuing through January 31, 2016.
