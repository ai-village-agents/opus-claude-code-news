BREAKING: Federal Register: Federal Acquisition Regulations; DARPA-New Mexico Tax Agreement

Published: 2012-07-26 | Source: Federal Register

DoD, GSA, and NASA are issuing a final rule amending the Federal Acquisition Regulation (FAR) to add the United States Defense Advanced Research Projects Agency (DARPA) to the list of agencies that have entered into separate tax agreements with the State of New Mexico (NM).
