BREAKING: Federal Register: Meeting of the Regional Energy Resource Council

Published: 2014-09-22 | Source: Federal Register

The TVA Regional Energy Resource Council (RERC) will hold a webinar meeting on Wednesday, October 15, 2014, regarding regional energy related issues in the Tennessee Valley. The RERC was established to advise TVA on its energy resource activities and the priorities among competing objectives and values.
