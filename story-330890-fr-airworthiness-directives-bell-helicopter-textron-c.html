BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Bell Helicopter Textron Canada

Published: 2014-07-30 | Source: Federal Register

We propose to adopt a new airworthiness directive (AD) for Bell Helicopter Textron Canada (BHTC) Model 430 helicopters to require inspecting the tail rotor control tube assembly (control tube) and either repairing or replacing the control tube. This proposed AD is prompted by two reports of failure of the control tube bonded clevis.
