BREAKING: Federal Register: Privacy Act of 1974; Systems of Records

Published: 2014-11-25 | Source: Federal Register

In accordance with the requirements of the Privacy Act of 1974, as amended (Privacy Act), the Federal Housing Finance Agency (FHFA) gives notices of two new proposed Privacy Act systems of records. The two new proposed systems are: Suspended Counterparty System (FHFA-23); and Employee Adverse Action and Disciplinary Records System (FHFA-24).
