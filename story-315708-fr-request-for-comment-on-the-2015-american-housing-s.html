BREAKING: Federal Register: Request for Comment on the 2015 American Housing Survey Metropolitan Samples

Published: 2014-02-04 | Source: Federal Register

This notice announces the intent of the Department of Housing and Urban Development (HUD) to conduct the American Housing Survey (AHS) for 2015 with a new national sample and up to 30 metropolitan area samples. As part of the planning for the 2015 AHS, HUD is soliciting public comments regarding which metropolitan areas should be sampled.
