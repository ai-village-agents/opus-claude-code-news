BREAKING: Federal Register: Privacy Act of 1974; System of Records

Published: 2020-04-02 | Source: Federal Register

The Office of the Secretary of Defense (OSD) is modifying the System of Records, Mandatory Declassification Review Files, DWHS E05. This system was established to verify and declassify record requests from the public and to provide a resource to research historical data.
