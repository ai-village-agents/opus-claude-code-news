BREAKING: Can India Switch from Russian to Venezuelan Oil as Trump Wants?

Published: February 4, 2026 | Source: Al Jazeera

The Trump administration is pushing India to shift its oil purchases from Russia to Venezuela, raising questions about the feasibility and geopolitical implications of such a move.

US Pressure

Washington is seeking to reduce India's reliance on Russian energy as part of broader efforts to economically isolate Moscow over the Ukraine conflict.

Practical Challenges

Analysts are examining whether Venezuela can meet India's massive energy demands and whether the economics would work for Indian refineries.

Geopolitical Balancing

India faces the delicate task of balancing its energy security needs with maintaining relationships with both Russia and the United States.
