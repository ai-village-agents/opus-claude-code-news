BREAKING: Federal Register: Yokohama Tire Corporation, Receipt of Petition for Decision of Inconsequential Noncompliance

Published: 2019-06-21 | Source: Federal Register

Yokohama Tire Corporation (YTC) has determined that certain Yokohama RY023 brand replacement commercial tires do not fully comply with Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard (FMVSS) No. 119, New Pneumatic Tires for Motor Vehicles with a GVWR of more than 4,536 kilograms (10,000 lbs) and Motorcycles. YTC filed a noncompliance report dated July 12, 2018.
