BREAKING: Federal Register: National Toxicology Program Board of Scientific Counselors; Announcement of Meeting; Request for Comments; Amended Notice

Published: 2015-11-23 | Source: Federal Register

This notice amends Federal Register notice 80 FR 61831, published October 14, 2015, announcing the National Toxicology Program (NTP) Board of Scientific Counselors (BSC) meeting and requesting comments. The deadline for written public comment submission has been changed to January 8, 2016. Persons submitting comments for the BSC meeting are encouraged to send them by November 30, 2015, to facilitate review by the BSC and NTP staff prior to the meeting.
