BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Intent To Establish a Negotiated Rulemaking Committee

Published: 2015-11-09 | Source: Federal Register

The Bureau of Indian Education (BIE) is announcing its intent to establish an Accountability Negotiated Rulemaking Committee (Committee). The Committee will recommend revisions to the existing regulations for BIE's accountability system.
