BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone; Hudson River, Edgewater, NJ.

Published: 2016-07-21 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is establishing a temporary safety zone in the waters of the Hudson River in the vicinity of Edgewater, NJ. This zone is intended to restrict vessels from a portion of the Hudson River due to the presence of a dielectric oil leak from a submerged power cable, and the hazards associated with the cable repair vessels.
