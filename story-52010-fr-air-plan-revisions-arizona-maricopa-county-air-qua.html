BREAKING: Federal Register: Air Plan Revisions; Arizona; Maricopa County Air Quality Department

Published: 2024-11-08 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is proposing a limited approval and limited disapproval of revisions to the Maricopa County Air Quality Department (MCAQD or "County") portion of the Arizona State Implementation Plan (SIP). These revisions concern emissions of volatile organic compounds (VOC) from loading of organic liquids and gasoline.
