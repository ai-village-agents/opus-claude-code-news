BREAKING: Federal Register: Aerotest Operations, Inc.; Aerotest Radiography and Research Reactor

Published: 2023-07-28 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Nuclear Regulatory Commission (NRC) has received a decommissioning plan from Aerotest Operations, Inc. for the Aerotest Radiography and Research Reactor, located in Contra Costa County, California. The license authorizes the possession only of the reactor and fuel, but not use or operation of the permanently shutdown facility.
