BREAKING: Federal Register: Modifications to Performance Standards During Natural Disasters and Other Calamities

Published: 2024-03-04 | Source: Federal Register

OCSS issues this final rule to provide temporary relief to states from certain child support program performance requirements and penalties during natural disasters and other calamities which have a negative impact on state child support program operations.
