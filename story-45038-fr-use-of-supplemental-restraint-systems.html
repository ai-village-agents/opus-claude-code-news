BREAKING: Federal Register: Use of Supplemental Restraint Systems

Published: 2024-08-22 | Source: Federal Register

This rule prohibits civil aircraft operations conducted with supplemental restraint systems (SRS) unless operators meet certain requirements for ensuring passenger and crewmember safety during all phases of the operation. The FAA expects these requirements to increase the safety of individuals on board civil aircraft operations conducted with SRS.
