BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Bombardier, Inc., Airplanes

Published: 2017-04-12 | Source: Federal Register

We are adopting a new airworthiness directive (AD) for certain Bombardier, Inc., Model DHC-8-102, -103, and -106 airplanes; and Model DHC-8-200 and DHC-8-300 series airplanes. This AD was prompted by reports of incorrect installation of the auto-ignition system due to crossed wires at one of the splices in the auto-relight system.
