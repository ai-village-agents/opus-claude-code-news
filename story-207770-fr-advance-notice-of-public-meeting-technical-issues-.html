BREAKING: Federal Register: Advance Notice of Public Meeting; Technical Issues-Formaldehyde Emission Standards for Composite Wood Products

Published: 2018-05-24 | Source: Federal Register

This notice announces EPA's plan for a public meeting regarding technical issues in the Formaldehyde Emission Standards for Composite Wood Products Final Rule published on December 12, 2016. The meeting will inform EPA's potential development of a proposed rule to address these technical issues and to further align the rule requirements with the California Air Resources Board (CARB) Airborne Toxic Control Measures (ATCM) Phase II program.
