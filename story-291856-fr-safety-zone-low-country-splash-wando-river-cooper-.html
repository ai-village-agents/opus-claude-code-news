BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone, Low Country Splash, Wando River, Cooper River, and Charleston Harbor, Charleston, SC

Published: 2015-04-21 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard proposes to establish temporary moving safety zone during the Low Country Splash, a swimming race occurring on the Wando River, the Cooper River, and Charleston Harbor, in Charleston, South Carolina. The Low Country Splash is scheduled on May 30, 2015, from 7:30 a.m. to 9:45 a.m. The temporary moving safety zone is necessary to protect swimmers, participant vessels, spectators, and the general public during the event.
