BREAKING: Federal Register: Ongoing Equivalence Verifications of Foreign Food Regulatory Systems

Published: 2013-01-25 | Source: Federal Register

The Food Safety and Inspection Service (FSIS) is describing the new methodology it is employing to conduct ongoing equivalence verifications of the regulatory systems of countries that export meat, poultry, or processed egg products to the United States. FSIS uses a three-part approach that includes: (1) Document reviews, (2) on-site system audits, and (3) port-of-entry (POE) reinspections. FSIS conducts document reviews at least yearly.
