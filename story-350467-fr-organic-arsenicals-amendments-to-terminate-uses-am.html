BREAKING: Federal Register: Organic Arsenicals; Amendments to Terminate Uses; Amendment to Existing Stocks Provisions

Published: 2013-03-27 | Source: Federal Register

EPA issued a document in the Federal Register of September 30, 2009 (74 FR 50187) (FRL-8437-7) concerning the cancellations and amendments of pesticide products containing organic arsenicals. A portion of the voluntary cancellation request giving rise to the 2009 document was conditioned upon the convening of a public scientific peer review process that would address the mode of action for carcinogenic effects of inorganic arsenic.
