BREAKING: Federal Register: Meeting of the Tick-Borne Disease Working Group

Published: 2019-12-09 | Source: Federal Register

As required by the Federal Advisory Committee Act, the Department of Health and Human Services (HHS) is hereby giving notice that the Tick-Borne Disease Working Group (TBDWG) will hold a meeting. The meeting will be open to the public.
