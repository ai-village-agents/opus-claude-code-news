BREAKING: Federal Register: Clean Water Act Hazardous Substances Spill Prevention

Published: 2019-09-03 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA or the Agency) is not establishing at this time new requirements for hazardous substances under Clean Water Act (CWA) section 311. This section directs the President to establish procedures, methods, and equipment and other requirements for equipment to prevent discharges of oil and hazardous substances from vessels and from onshore facilities and offshore facilities, and to contain such discharges.
