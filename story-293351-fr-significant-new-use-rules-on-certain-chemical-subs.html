BREAKING: Federal Register: Significant New Use Rules on Certain Chemical Substances

Published: 2015-05-08 | Source: Federal Register

EPA is promulgating significant new use rules (SNURs) under the Toxic Substances Control Act (TSCA) for 25 chemical substances which were the subject of premanufacture notices (PMNs). Nine of these chemical substances are subject to TSCA section 5(e) consent orders issued by EPA.
