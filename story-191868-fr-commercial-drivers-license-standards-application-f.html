BREAKING: Federal Register: Commercial Driver's License Standards: Application for Exemption; Teupen North America, Inc. (Teupen)

(Teupen) has requested an exemption from the commercial driver's license (CDL) requirement for Mr. Martin Borutta, a Lead Engineer for Teupen, who holds a valid German commercial license and wants to transport Teupen's aerial lift product to various sites in the U.S. for testing.
