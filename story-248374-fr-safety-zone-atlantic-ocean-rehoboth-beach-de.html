BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone; Atlantic Ocean, Rehoboth Beach, DE

Published: 2017-11-09 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is establishing temporary safety zones in the Atlantic Ocean, off the coast of Rehoboth Beach, DE and in Breakwater Harbor near Cape Henlopen. Safety zone one is being established for navigable waters in the vicinity of the derrick barge HAAKON during dredging operations, pipe laying operations, diving operations and underwater construction operations.
