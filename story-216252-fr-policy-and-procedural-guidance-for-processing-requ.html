BREAKING: Federal Register: Policy and Procedural Guidance for Processing Requests To Alter US Army Corps of Engineers Civil Works Projects

Published: 2018-09-13 | Source: Federal Register

The US Army Corps of Engineers (USACE) has issued Engineer Circular (EC) 1165-2-220, Policy and Procedural Guidance for Processing Requests to Alter US Army Corps of Engineers Civil Works Projects Pursuant to Section 408. This agency policy document provides the policies and procedures related to how USACE will process certain requests by others to alter a USACE Civil Works project under Section 14 of the Rivers and Harbors Act of 1899 (more commonly referred to as Section 408).
