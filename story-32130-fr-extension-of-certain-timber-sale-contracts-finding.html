BREAKING: Federal Register: Extension of Certain Timber Sale Contracts; Finding of Substantial Overriding Public Interest

Published: 2024-03-15 | Source: Federal Register

The Chief of the Forest Service, Department of Agriculture, has determined that it is in the substantial overriding public interest (SOPI) to redetermine pulpwood rates and extend up to two years certain National Forest System timber sales and sale of property stewardship contracts.
