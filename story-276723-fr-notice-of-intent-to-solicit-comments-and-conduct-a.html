BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Intent To Solicit Comments and Conduct a Public Scoping Meeting on a Global Water Strategy

Published: 2016-10-17 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Department of State (Department) will host a listening session to solicit public comments on the development and content of a strategy to address global water challenges including, but not necessarily limited to: (1) Increasing access to safe drinking water, sanitation, and hygiene; (2) improving water resource management; and (3) promoting cooperation on shared waters.
