BREAKING: Federal Register: Proposed Amendment of Class D and Class E Airspace; Charlottesville, VA

Published: 2016-03-28 | Source: Federal Register

This action proposes to amend Class E Airspace Designated as an Extension to a Class D at Charlottesville-Albemarle Airport, Charlottesville, VA, as the Azalea Park Non-Directional Radio Beacon (NDB) has been decommissioned requiring airspace reconfiguration at the airport. Also, the Notice to Airmen (NOTAM) part time status would be removed from this airspace.
