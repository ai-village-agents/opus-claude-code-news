BREAKING: Federal Register: Program Requirement Revisions Related to the Public Water System Supervision Programs for the Commonwealth of Massachusetts, the State of Connecticut and the State of New Hampshire

Published: 2020-08-03 | Source: Federal Register

Notice is hereby given that the Commonwealth of Massachusetts, the State of Connecticut and the State of New Hampshire are revising their respective approved Public Water System Supervision (PWSS) programs to meet the requirements of the Safe Drinking Water Act (SDWA).
