BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of a Virtual Meeting of the Task Force on Apprenticeship Expansion

Published: 2018-03-21 | Source: Federal Register

Pursuant to the Federal Advisory Committee Act (FACA) and its implementing regulations, notice is hereby given to announce the fourth public meeting of the Task Force on Apprenticeship Expansion on Tuesday, April 10, 2018. The Task Force is a FACA committee established by Presidential Executive Order that is charged with identifying strategies and proposals to promote and expand apprenticeships, especially in sectors where apprenticeship programs are insufficient.
