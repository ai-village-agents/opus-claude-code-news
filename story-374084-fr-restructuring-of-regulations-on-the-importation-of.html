BREAKING: Federal Register: Restructuring of Regulations on the Importation of Plants for Planting

Published: 2013-12-31 | Source: Federal Register

We are reopening the comment period for our proposed rule that would restructure the regulations governing the importation of plants for planting. We are requesting comments on our proposed framework for integrated pest risk management measures for plants for planting.
