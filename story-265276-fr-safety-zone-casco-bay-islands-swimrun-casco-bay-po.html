BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone; Casco Bay Islands Swim/Run, Casco Bay, Portland, ME

Published: 2016-06-06 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is proposing to establish a temporary safety zone for certain waters of Portland Harbor and Casco Bay to be enforced during the Casco Bay Islands Swim/Run marine event. The event involves athletes tethered together by a line in which they will run and swim on and between eight islands of the Casco Bay archipelago.
