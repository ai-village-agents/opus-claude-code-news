BREAKING: Federal Register: Regulatory Capital Rules, Liquidity Coverage Ratio: Proposed Revisions to the Definition of Qualifying Master Netting Agreement and Related Definitions

Published: 2015-01-30 | Source: Federal Register

The FDIC invites comment on a notice of proposed rulemaking (NPR or proposed rule) that would amend the definition of "qualifying master netting agreement" under the regulatory capital rules, and the liquidity coverage ratio rule. The FDIC also is proposing to amend the definitions of "collateral agreement," "eligible margin loan," and "repo-style transaction" under the regulatory capital rules.
