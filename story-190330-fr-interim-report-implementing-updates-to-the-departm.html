BREAKING: Federal Register: Interim Report Implementing Updates to the Department of Energy's Information Quality Act Guidelines

Published: 2019-10-04 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Department of Energy (DOE) gives notice of an interim report to the Office of Management and Budget (OMB) on the DOE Information Quality Act (IQA) Guidelines setting forth updates to DOE's policy and procedures to ensure and maximize the quality, utility, objectivity, and integrity of the information that DOE disseminates to members of the public.
