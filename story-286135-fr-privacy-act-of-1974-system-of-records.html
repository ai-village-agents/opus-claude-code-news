BREAKING: Federal Register: Privacy Act of 1974; System of Records

Published: 2015-02-11 | Source: Federal Register

The Office of the Secretary of Defense proposes to alter a system of records, DMDC 15 DoD, entitled "Armed Services Military Accession Testing" in its inventory of record systems subject to the Privacy Act of 1974, as amended. The system is used to establish eligibility for enlistment; verify enlistment and placement scores; verify retest eligibility; and provide aptitude test scores as an element of career guidance to participants in the DoD Student Testing Program.
