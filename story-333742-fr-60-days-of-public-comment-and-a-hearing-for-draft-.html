BREAKING: Federal Register: 60 Days of Public Comment, and a Hearing, for Draft Amendments to Rules for Judicial-Conduct and Judicial-Disability Proceedings

Published: 2014-09-02 | Source: Federal Register

The Judicial Conference of the United States Committee on Judicial Conduct and Disability has released a draft of proposed amendments to the Judicial Conference's Rules for Judicial-Conduct and Judicial-Disability Proceedings ("JCD Rules") for public comment and has scheduled a public hearing.
