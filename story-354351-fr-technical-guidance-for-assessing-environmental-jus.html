BREAKING: Federal Register: Technical Guidance for Assessing Environmental Justice in Regulatory Analysis

Published: 2013-05-09 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) has issued for public comment a document entitled, "Technical Guidance for Assessing Environmental Justice in Regulatory Analysis." The purpose of this guidance is to provide EPA analysts with technical information on how to consider environmental justice in regulatory analyses.
