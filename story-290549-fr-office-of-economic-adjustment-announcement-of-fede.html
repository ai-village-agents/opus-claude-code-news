BREAKING: Federal Register: Office of Economic Adjustment; Announcement of Federal Funding Opportunity

Published: 2015-04-06 | Source: Federal Register

This notice announces the opportunity to enter into a cooperative agreement with the Office of Economic Adjustment (OEA), a Department of Defense (DoD) Field Activity, to undertake a research project to assess and evaluate the design and effect of OEA's Defense Industry Adjustment (DIA) program and the assistance that its Grantees provide to affected communities, workers, and businesses.
