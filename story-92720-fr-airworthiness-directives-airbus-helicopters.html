BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Airbus Helicopters

Published: 2022-04-04 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA is adopting a new airworthiness directive (AD) for certain Airbus Helicopters Model SA330J helicopters. This AD was prompted by a review of Model EC225LP helicopter data that revealed potential tightening torque loss of the attachment screws of the upper deck fittings of the three main gearbox (MGB) suspension bars.
