BREAKING: Federal Register: Employee Retirement Benefit Plan Returns Required on Magnetic Media

Published: 2013-08-30 | Source: Federal Register

This document contains proposed regulations relating to the requirements for filing certain employee retirement benefit plan statements, returns, and reports on magnetic media. The term magnetic media includes electronic filing, as well as other magnetic media specifically permitted under applicable regulations, revenue procedures, publications, forms, instructions, or other guidance on the IRS.gov Internet Web site.
