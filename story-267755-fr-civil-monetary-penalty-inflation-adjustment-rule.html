BREAKING: Federal Register: Civil Monetary Penalty Inflation Adjustment Rule

Published: 2016-07-01 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is promulgating this interim final rule to adjust the level of statutory civil monetary penalty amounts for the statutes that the agency administers.
