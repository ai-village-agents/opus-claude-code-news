BREAKING: Federal Register: Record of Decision for the Uranium Leasing Program Programmatic Environmental Impact Statement

Published: 2014-05-12 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Department of Energy (DOE) announces its decision to continue management of the Uranium Leasing Program (ULP) for 31 lease tracts for the next 10 years, consistent with DOE's preferred alternative identified in the Final Uranium Leasing Program Programmatic Environmental Impact Statement (Final ULP PEIS) (DOE/EIS- 0472).
