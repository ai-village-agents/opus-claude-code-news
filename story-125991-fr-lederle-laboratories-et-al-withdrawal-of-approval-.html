BREAKING: Federal Register: Lederle Laboratories et al.; Withdrawal of Approval of 12 Abbreviated New Drug Applications; Correction

Published: 2021-06-15 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA) is correcting a notice that appeared in the Federal Register on May 12, 2021. The document announced the withdrawal of approval of 12 abbreviated new drug applications (ANDAs) from multiple applicants, withdrawn as of June 11, 2021. The document indicated that FDA was withdrawing the approval of ANDA 060164, Nystatin Ointment, held by Lederle Laboratories.
