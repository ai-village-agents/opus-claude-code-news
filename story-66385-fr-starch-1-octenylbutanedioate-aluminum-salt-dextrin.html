BREAKING: Federal Register: Starch, 1-Octenylbutanedioate, Aluminum Salt; Dextrin, Hydrogen 1-Octenylbutanedioate; and Amylopectin, 2-Hydroxypropyl Ether, Acid-; Exemption From the Requirement of a Tolerance

Published: 2023-05-01 | Source: Federal Register

This regulation establishes exemptions from the requirement of a tolerance for residues of starch, 1-octenylbutanedioate, aluminum salt; dextrin, hydrogen 1-octenylbutanedioate; and amylopectin, 2- hydroxypropyl ether, acid- when used as inert ingredients (for seed treatment only) in pesticide formulations applied pre-harvest.
