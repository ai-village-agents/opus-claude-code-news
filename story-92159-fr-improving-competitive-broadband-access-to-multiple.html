BREAKING: Federal Register: Improving Competitive Broadband Access to Multiple Tenant Environments

Published: 2022-03-28 | Source: Federal Register

In this document, the Federal Communications Commission (Commission or FCC) adopts final rules to improve competition for communications services in multi-tenant environments. The rules prohibit telecommunications carriers and covered multichannel video programming distributors (MVPDs) from entering into certain revenue sharing agreements with a building owner that keep competitive providers out of buildings.
