BREAKING: Federal Register: Air Plan Approval; MO; Restriction of Visible Air Contaminant Emissions

Published: 2023-03-08 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is taking final action to approve revisions to the Missouri State Implementation Plan (SIP) received on November 29, 2016, and March 7, 2019. The revisions were submitted by Missouri in response to a finding of substantial inadequacy and SIP call published on June 12, 2015, for a provision in the Missouri SIP related to excess emissions during startup, shutdown, and malfunction (SSM) events.
