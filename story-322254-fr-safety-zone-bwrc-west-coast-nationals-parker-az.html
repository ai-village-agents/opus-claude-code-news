BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone; BWRC West Coast Nationals; Parker, AZ

Published: 2014-04-21 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is establishing a temporary safety zone within the Lake Moolvalya region of the navigable waters of the Colorado River in Parker, Arizona in support of the Blue Water Resort and Casino (BWRC) West Coast Nationals high speed boat race. This safety zone is necessary to provide for the safety of the participants, crew, spectators, participating vessels, and other vessels and users of the waterway.
