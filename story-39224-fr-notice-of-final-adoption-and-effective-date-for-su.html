BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Final Adoption and Effective Date for Submission Guidelines Equity Updates

Published: 2024-06-12 | Source: Federal Register

The National Capital Planning Commission (NCPC) adopted the Submission Guidelines Equity Updates on June 6, 2024. Federal and non- Federal agency applicants whose development proposals and plans are subject to statutory mandated Commission plan and project review must submit their proposals to the Commission following a process laid out in the Submission Guidelines.
