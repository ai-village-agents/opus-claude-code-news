BREAKING: Federal Register: Arbitration Agreements

Published: 2017-11-22 | Source: Federal Register

Under the Congressional Review Act, Congress has passed and the president has signed a joint resolution disapproving a final rule published by the Bureau of Consumer Financial Protection (Bureau) on July 19, 2017, to regulate arbitration agreements in contracts for specified consumer financial products and services.
