BREAKING: Federal Register: Amendment of the Federal Docket Management System (EPA/GOV-2)

Published: 2013-10-02 | Source: Federal Register

Pursuant to the provisions of the Privacy Act of 1974 (5 USC 552a), the Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is giving notice that it is amending the Federal Docket Management System (FDMS) system of records to add information collected in a Freedom of Information Act (FOIA) system. The FOIA system, "FOIAonline", is a software application on the FDMS system infrastructure.
