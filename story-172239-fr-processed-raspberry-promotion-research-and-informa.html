BREAKING: Federal Register: Processed Raspberry Promotion, Research, and Information Order; Termination of Assessments

Published: 2019-02-20 | Source: Federal Register

This rule removes the requirement in the Processed Raspberry Promotion, Research, and Information Order (Order) that each eligible producer of raspberries for processing and eligible importer of processed raspberries pay to the National Processed Raspberry Council (Council) an assessment in the amount of one cent per pound as specified in the Order.
