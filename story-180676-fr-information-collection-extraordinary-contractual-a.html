BREAKING: Federal Register: Information Collection; Extraordinary Contractual Action Requests

Published: 2019-06-04 | Source: Federal Register

In accordance with section 3506(c)(2)(A) of the Paperwork Reduction Act of 1995, and the Office of Management and Budget (OMB) regulations, DoD, GSA, and NASA invite the public to comment on a revision concerning extraordinary contractual action requests.
