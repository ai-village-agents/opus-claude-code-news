BREAKING: Federal Register: Management of Federal Agency Disbursements; Correction

Published: 2024-03-14 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of the Treasury ("Treasury"), Bureau of the Fiscal Service ("Fiscal Service") is correcting a final rule that was published in the Federal Register on February 21, 2024, to amend Fiscal Service's Management of Federal Agency Disbursements regulation. The regulation implements a statutory mandate requiring the Federal Government to deliver non-tax payments by electronic funds transfer (EFT) unless Treasury determines that a waiver of the requirement is appropriate.
