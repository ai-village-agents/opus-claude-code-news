BREAKING: Federal Register: Improving Access to Affordable, High Quality Child Care: Request for Information

Published: 2019-10-02 | Source: Federal Register

As part of the Administration for Children and Families (ACF) commitment to supporting working families and promoting the healthy development and well-being of children, ACF is seeking input from the public and interested stakeholders on strategies to improve access to high quality, affordable child care in the U.S. Child care is one of the biggest expenses a family faces and can be a barrier to work.
