BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone; Beaufort River, Beaufort, SC

Published: 2024-12-27 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is establishing a temporary safety zone for certain navigable waters of the Beaufort River in Beaufort, SC, for a barge-based fireworks display. The safety zone will encompass all waters within a 150-yard radius of the fireworks barge. The safety zone is needed to protect personnel, vessels, and the marine environment from potential hazards created by a fireworks display.
