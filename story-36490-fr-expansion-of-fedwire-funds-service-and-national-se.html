BREAKING: Federal Register: Expansion of Fedwire® Funds Service and National Settlement Service Operating Hours

Published: 2024-05-09 | Source: Federal Register

The Board of Governors of the Federal Reserve System (Board) is seeking input on a proposal to expand the operating hours of the Fedwire[supreg] Funds Service and the National Settlement Service (NSS). The Board proposes to expand the operating hours of the Fedwire Funds Service to 22 hours per day, 7 days per week, every day of the year (22x7x365) and to correspondingly expand the operating hours of NSS, with NSS closing 30 minutes earlier than the Fedwire Funds Service.
