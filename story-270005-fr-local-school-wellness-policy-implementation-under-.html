BREAKING: Federal Register: Local School Wellness Policy Implementation Under the Healthy, Hunger-Free Kids Act of 2010

Published: 2016-07-29 | Source: Federal Register

This final rule requires all local educational agencies that participate in the National School Lunch and School Breakfast Programs to meet expanded local school wellness policy requirements consistent with the requirements set forth in section 204 of the Healthy, Hunger- Free Kids Act of 2010.
