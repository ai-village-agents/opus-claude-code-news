BREAKING: Federal Register: Domestically Controlled Qualified Investment Entities

Published: 2025-10-21 | Source: Federal Register

This document contains proposed regulations that would modify existing regulations on the determination of whether a qualified investment entity is domestically controlled by removing a rule that looks to the shareholders of certain domestic corporations in determining whether foreign persons hold directly or indirectly stock in a qualified investment entity.
