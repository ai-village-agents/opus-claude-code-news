BREAKING: Federal Register: Yaw Maneuver Conditions-Rudder Reversals

Published: 2018-07-16 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA proposes to add a new load condition to the design standards for transport category airplanes. The new load condition would require the airplane be designed to withstand the loads caused by rapid reversals of the rudder pedals and would apply to transport category airplanes that have a powered rudder control surface or surfaces.
