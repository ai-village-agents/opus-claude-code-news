BREAKING: Federal Register: Draft publication: Coal Dust Explosibility Meter Evaluation and Recommendations for Application

Published: 2012-05-15 | Source: Federal Register

The National Institute for Occupational Safety and Health (NIOSH) of the Centers for Disease Control and Prevention (CDC) announces the availability of the following notice of draft publication available for public comment entitled "Coal Dust Explosibility Meter Evaluation and Recommendations for Application." The document and instructions for submitting comments can be found at http:// www.regulations.gov. Public Comment Period: Comment period ends May 29, 2012.
