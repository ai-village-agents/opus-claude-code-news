BREAKING: Federal Register: National Artificial Intelligence Advisory Committee

Published: 2024-06-24 | Source: Federal Register

The National Institute of Standards and Technology (NIST) announces that the National Artificial Intelligence Advisory Committee (NAIAC or Committee) will hold a series of virtual briefing sessions. These sessions will be held via web conference on Tuesday, July 9, 2024, Wednesday, July 10, 2024, and July 11, 2024, from 2:00 p.m.-3:30 p.m. Eastern Time each day.
