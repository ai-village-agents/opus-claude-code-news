BREAKING: Federal Register: Large Residential Washers From the Republic of Korea: Final Results of Antidumping Duty Administrative Review; 2017-2018

Published: 2019-02-26 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Commerce (Commerce) determines that LG Electronics, Inc. (LGE), a producer/exporter of large residential washers from the Republic of Korea (Korea), did not make sales at prices below normal value during the February 1, 2017, through January 31, 2018, period of review (POR).
