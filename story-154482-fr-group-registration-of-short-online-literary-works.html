BREAKING: Federal Register: Group Registration of Short Online Literary Works

Published: 2020-06-22 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Copyright Office is amending its regulations to establish a new group registration option for short online literary works. This final rule largely adopts the eligibility requirements set forth in the Office's December 2018 notice of proposed rulemaking, with certain updates. To qualify for this option, each work must contain at least 50 but no more than 17,500 words.
