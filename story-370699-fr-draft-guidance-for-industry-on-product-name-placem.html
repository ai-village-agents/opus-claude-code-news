BREAKING: Federal Register: Draft Guidance for Industry on Product Name Placement, Size, and Prominence in Advertising and Promotional Labeling; Availability

Published: 2013-11-20 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA) is announcing the availability of a draft guidance for industry entitled "Product Name Placement, Size, and Prominence in Advertising and Promotional Labeling." When finalized, the draft guidance will replace the guidance of the same title issued January 25, 2012.
