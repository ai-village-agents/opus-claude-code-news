BREAKING: Federal Register: Agency Information Collection Activities; Submission for OMB Review; Comment Request; Unemployment Compensation for Federal Employees

Published: 2015-10-22 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Labor (DOL) is submitting the Employment and Training Administration (ETA) sponsored information collection request (ICR) titled, "Unemployment Compensation for Federal Employees," to the Office of Management and Budget (OMB) for review and approval for continued use, without change, in accordance with the Paperwork Reduction Act of 1995 (PRA), 44 U.S.C. 3501 et seq. Public comments on the ICR are invited.
