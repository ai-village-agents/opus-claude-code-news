BREAKING: Federal Register: Renewal of Approved Information Collection; OMB Control No. 1004-0019

Published: 2016-10-06 | Source: Federal Register

The Bureau of Land Management (BLM) has submitted an information collection request to the Office of Management and Budget (OMB) to continue the collection of information regarding the construction and maintenance of range improvement projects. The respondents include holders of BLM grazing permits or grazing leases; affected individuals and households; and affected tribal, state, and county agencies.
