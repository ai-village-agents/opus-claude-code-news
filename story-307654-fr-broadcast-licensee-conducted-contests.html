BREAKING: Federal Register: Broadcast Licensee-Conducted Contests

Published: 2015-10-23 | Source: Federal Register

In this document, the Federal Communications Commission ("Commission") amends the portion of its rules known as the "Contest Rule" to permit broadcast licensees to comply with their obligation to disclose material contest terms either by broadcasting those terms or by making them available in writing on a publicly accessible Internet Web site.
