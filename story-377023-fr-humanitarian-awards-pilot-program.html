BREAKING: Federal Register: Humanitarian Awards Pilot Program

Published: 2012-02-08 | Source: Federal Register

Following on last year's Request for Comments, the United States Patent and Trademark Office (USPTO) is launching a twelve-month pilot program to incentivize the distribution of patented technologies to address humanitarian needs. The pilot program will be run as an awards competition.
