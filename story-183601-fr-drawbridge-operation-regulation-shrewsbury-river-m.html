BREAKING: Federal Register: Drawbridge Operation Regulation; Shrewsbury River, Monmouth County Highway Bridge, Sea Bright, New Jersey

Published: 2019-07-09 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is modifying the operating schedule that governs the Monmouth County Highway Bridge (alternatively referred to as the "Sea Bright Bridge" or the "S-32 Bridge") across the Shrewsbury River, mile 4.0 at Sea Bright, New Jersey.
