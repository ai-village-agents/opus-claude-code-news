BREAKING: Federal Register: Pipeline Safety: Gas and Liquid Advisory Committee Member Nominations

Published: 2016-05-24 | Source: Federal Register

PHMSA is requesting nominations for individuals to serve on the Gas Pipeline Advisory Committee (GPAC), also known as the Technical Pipeline Safety Standards Committee, and the Liquid Pipeline Advisory Committee (LPAC), also known as the Technical Hazardous Liquid Pipeline Safety Standards Committee.
