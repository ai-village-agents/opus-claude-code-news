BREAKING: Federal Register: Wolf Creek Generating Station: Consideration of Approval of Transfer of License

Published: 2017-11-15 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Nuclear Regulatory Commission (NRC) received and is considering approval of an indirect license transfer application filed by Wolf Creek Nuclear Operating Corporation (WCNOC) on September 5, 2017. The WCNOC is the licensed operator of Wolf Creek Generating Station (WCGS).
