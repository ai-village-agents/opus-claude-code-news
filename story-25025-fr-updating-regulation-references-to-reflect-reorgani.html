BREAKING: Federal Register: Updating Regulation References To Reflect Reorganizations at the Department of Justice and the Internal Revenue Service

Published: 2025-12-15 | Source: Federal Register

This document contains proposed regulations that would update points of contact within the Department of Justice and the IRS. The proposed regulations are necessary to reflect a reorganization within the Department of Justice to identify new points of contact for matters involving the internal revenue laws.
