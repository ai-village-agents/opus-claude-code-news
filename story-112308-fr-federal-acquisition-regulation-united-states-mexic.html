BREAKING: Federal Register: Federal Acquisition Regulation: United States-Mexico-Canada Agreement; Correction

Published: 2022-12-14 | Source: Federal Register

DoD, GSA, and NASA published a final rule amending the Federal Acquisition Regulation (FAR) to implement the United States-Mexico- Canada Agreement Implementation Act in the Federal Register of December 1, 2022. This document corrects an erroneous instruction in that rule.
