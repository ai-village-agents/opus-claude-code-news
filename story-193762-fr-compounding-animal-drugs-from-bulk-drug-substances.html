BREAKING: Federal Register: Compounding Animal Drugs From Bulk Drug Substances; Draft Guidance for Industry; Availability

Published: 2019-11-20 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA or Agency) is announcing the availability of draft guidance for industry (GFI) #256 entitled "Compounding Animal Drugs from Bulk Drug Substances." The draft guidance, if finalized, will describe FDA's current thinking about compounding animal drugs from bulk drug substances.
