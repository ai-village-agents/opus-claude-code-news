BREAKING: Federal Register: Vendor Outreach Workshop for Small Information Technology (IT) Businesses in the National Capitol Region of the United States

Published: 2012-02-08 | Source: Federal Register

The Office of Small and Disadvantaged Business Utilization of the Department of the Interior is hosting a Vendor Outreach Workshop for small IT businesses in the National Capitol region of the United States that are interested in doing business with the Department. This outreach workshop will review market contracting opportunities for the attendees.
