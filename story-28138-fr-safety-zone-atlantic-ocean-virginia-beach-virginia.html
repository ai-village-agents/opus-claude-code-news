BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone; Atlantic Ocean, Virginia Beach, Virginia

Published: 2024-01-26 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is establishing a temporary safety zone for navigable waters within a 1,000-yard radius of the M/V HOS WARLAND, HOS INNOVATOR, and, or HOS MYSTIQUE. Operations are planned to relocate unexploded ordinance (UXO) in the Atlantic Ocean, within 12 miles of the shores of the State Military Reservation, in Virginia Beach, Virginia.
