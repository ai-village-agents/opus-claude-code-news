BREAKING: Federal Register: Open Meeting of the Financial Research Advisory Committee

Published: 2023-05-05 | Source: Federal Register

The Financial Research Advisory Committee for the Treasury's Office of Financial Research (OFR) is convening for its twenty-first meeting on Thursday, May 18, 2023, via webcast and in person at the OFR's New York office (290 Broadway, New York, New York 10007), beginning at 10:00 a.m. Eastern Time. The meeting will be open to the public and advance registration is required.
