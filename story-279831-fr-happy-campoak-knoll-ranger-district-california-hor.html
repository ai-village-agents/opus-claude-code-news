BREAKING: Federal Register: Happy Camp/Oak Knoll Ranger District; California; Horse Creek Community Protection and Forest Restoration Project

Published: 2016-11-23 | Source: Federal Register

The purpose of the Horse Creek Community Protection and Forest Restoration Project (Horse Creek Project) is to reduce fuels along egress and ingress roads, on strategic ridges, and adjacent to private property; to reduce safety hazards along roads and in concentrated stands in and around the community of Horse Creek, California; to restore previously stocked units; and treat the riparian areas within the Horse Creek Botanical Special Interest Area.
