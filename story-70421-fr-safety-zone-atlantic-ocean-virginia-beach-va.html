BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone; Atlantic Ocean, Virginia Beach, VA

Published: 2023-06-22 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is establishing a temporary safety zone for all navigable waters within 200-yards of the Virginia Beach oceanfront. The safety zone is needed to protect mariners from the potential hazards which would result if a large congregation of spectator vessels anchors in close proximity to a shoreside concert on the Virginia Beach oceanfront.
