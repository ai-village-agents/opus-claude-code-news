BREAKING: Federal Register: Exposure Draft-Standards for Internal Control in the Federal Government

Published: 2013-11-14 | Source: Federal Register

The US Government Accountability Office (GAO) is extending the comment period for the proposed revisions to the Standards for Internal Control in the Federal Government, known as the "Green Book," to January 15, 2014. We are requesting public comments on the proposed revisions in the exposure draft.
