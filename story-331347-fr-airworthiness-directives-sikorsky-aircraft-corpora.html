BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Sikorsky Aircraft Corporation Helicopters

Published: 2014-08-04 | Source: Federal Register

We are adopting a new airworthiness directive (AD) for the Sikorsky Aircraft Corporation (Sikorsky) Model S-92A helicopter. This AD requires revising the Rotorcraft Flight Manual (RFM) to include the appropriate operating limitations for performing Class D external load- combination operations.
