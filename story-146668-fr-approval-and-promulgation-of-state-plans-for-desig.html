BREAKING: Federal Register: Approval and Promulgation of State Plans for Designated Facilities and Pollutants; Oregon Department of Environmental Quality; Control of Emissions From Existing Municipal Solid Waste Landfills

Published: 2020-03-13 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is proposing to approve a Clean Air Act (CAA) section 111(d) plan submitted by the Oregon Department of Environmental Quality (ODEQ). This state plan establishes emission limits for existing municipal solid waste (MSW) landfills and provides for the implementation and enforcement of these limits.
