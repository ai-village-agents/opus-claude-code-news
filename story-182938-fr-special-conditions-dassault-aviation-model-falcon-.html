BREAKING: Federal Register: Special Conditions: Dassault Aviation Model Falcon 7X Airplanes; Large Non-Structural Glass in the Passenger Compartment

Published: 2019-07-01 | Source: Federal Register

These special conditions are issued for the Dassault Aviation (Dassault) Model Falcon 7X airplane. This airplane will have a novel or unusual design feature when compared to the state of technology envisioned in the airworthiness standards for transport-category airplanes. This design feature is the installation of large, non- structural glass panels in the passenger compartment.
