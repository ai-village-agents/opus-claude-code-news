BREAKING: Federal Register: Wait-Time Goals of the Department for the Veterans Choice Program

Published: 2014-10-17 | Source: Federal Register

The Veterans Access, Choice, and Accountability Act of 2014 directs the Department of Veterans Affairs (VA) to establish a program (the "Veterans Choice Program") to furnish hospital care and medical services through non-VA health care entities and providers to Veterans who either cannot be seen within the "wait-time goals of the Veterans Health Administration" or who qualify based on their place of residence.
