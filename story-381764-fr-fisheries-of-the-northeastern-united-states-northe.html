BREAKING: Federal Register: Fisheries of the Northeastern United States; Northeast Multispecies Fishery Management Plan; Secretarial Amendment

Published: 2012-03-30 | Source: Federal Register

This final rule implements a Secretarial Amendment to the Northeast Multispecies Fishery Management Plan to establish a mechanism for specifying annual catch limits and accountability measures for the small-mesh multispecies fishery beginning in fishing year 2012.
