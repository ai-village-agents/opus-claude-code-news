BREAKING: Federal Register: Amendment of Class E Airspace; Cadiz, Caldwell, and Cambridge, OH

Published: 2020-05-05 | Source: Federal Register

This action amends the Class E airspace extending upward from 700 feet above the surface at Harrison County Airport, Cadiz, OH; Noble County Airport, Caldwell, OH; and Cambridge Municipal Airport, Cambridge, OH. These actions are the result of airspace reviews caused by the decommissioning of the Newcomerstown VHF omnidirectional range (VOR) navigation aid, which provided navigation information for the instrument procedures at these airports, as part of the VOR Minimum Operational Network (MON) ...
