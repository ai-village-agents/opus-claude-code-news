BREAKING: Federal Register: Regulation of NMS Stock Alternative Trading Systems

Published: 2018-11-13 | Source: Federal Register

This document makes technical corrections to a rule that was published in the Federal Register on August 7, 2018. The Commission adopted amendments to the regulatory requirements in Regulation ATS under the Securities Exchange Act of 1934 applicable to alternative trading systems ("ATSs") that trade National Market System ("NMS") stocks (hereinafter referred to as "NMS Stock ATSs"), which included, among other items, Form ATS-N.
