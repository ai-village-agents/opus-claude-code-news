BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Funding Availability (NOFA) for Loan Guarantees Under the Guaranteed Rural Rental Housing Program (GRRHP) for Fiscal Year 2012

Published: 2012-02-06 | Source: Federal Register

This is a request for proposals for guaranteed loans under the section 538 Guaranteed Rural Rental Housing Program (GRRHP) pursuant to 7 CFR 3565.4 for Fiscal Year (FY) 2012. The Consolidated and Further Continuing Appropriations Act, 2012 (Pub. L. 112-55) (November 18, 2011) (2012 Appropriations Act) provides FY 2012 funding for the section 538 program at $130,000,000.
