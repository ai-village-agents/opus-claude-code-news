BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Availability of Funds and Solicitation for Grant Applications for Training To Work 2-Adult Reentry

The Employment and Training Administration (ETA), U.S. Department of Labor, announces the availability of approximately $30 million for grants to serve male and female ex-offenders enrolled in work release programs (WRPs) as authorized by the Workforce Investment Act and the Second Chance Act of 2007.
