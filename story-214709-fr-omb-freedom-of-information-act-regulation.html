BREAKING: Federal Register: OMB Freedom of Information Act Regulation

Published: 2018-08-23 | Source: Federal Register

The Office of Management and Budget (OMB) seeks public comment on a proposed rule that would revise OMB's regulations found in our regulations implementing the Freedom of Information Act (FOIA).
