BREAKING: Federal Register: Proposed Modification of VOR Federal Airway V-345 in the Vicinity of Ashland, WI

Published: 2013-03-26 | Source: Federal Register

This action proposes to modify the legal description of VHF Omnidirectional Range (VOR) Federal airway V-345 in the vicinity of Ashland, WI. The Ashland (ASX) VOR Distance Measuring Equipment (VOR/ DME) navigation aid, which forms the northern most point of the airway, has been out of service for over ten months, with a Notice to Airmen (NOTAM) published, and is scheduled to be decommissioned in 2013.
