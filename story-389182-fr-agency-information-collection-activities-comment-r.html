BREAKING: Federal Register: Agency Information Collection Activities: Comment Request for the Industrial Minerals Surveys (40 Forms)

Published: 2012-06-22 | Source: Federal Register

To comply with the Paperwork Reduction Act of 1995 (PRA), we are notifying the public that we have submitted to the Office of Management and Budget (OMB) an Information Collection Request (ICR) for the revision of the currently approved paperwork requirements for the Industrial Minerals Surveys. This collection consists of 40 forms.
