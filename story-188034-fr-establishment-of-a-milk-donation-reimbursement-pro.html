BREAKING: Federal Register: Establishment of a Milk Donation Reimbursement Program

Published: 2019-09-05 | Source: Federal Register

This rule establishes the Milk Donation Reimbursement Program. Under the program, eligible dairy organizations that account to a Federal milk marketing order marketwide pool and incur qualified expenses related to certain fluid milk product donations may apply for and receive limited reimbursements to cover those expenses.
