BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Airbus SAS Airplanes

Published: 2018-07-23 | Source: Federal Register

We are adopting a new airworthiness directive (AD) for certain Airbus SAS Model A319-115, -132, and -133 airplanes; and Model A320- 214, -216, -232, -233, -251N, and -271N airplanes. This AD was prompted by reports of safety pins that had been installed on the inflation reservoirs of escape slides/slide rafts during production, but had not been removed.
