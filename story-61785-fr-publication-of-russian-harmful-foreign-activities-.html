BREAKING: Federal Register: Publication of Russian Harmful Foreign Activities Sanctions Regulations Web General Licenses 56A and 57A

Published: 2023-03-03 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of the Treasury's Office of Foreign Assets Control (OFAC) is publishing two general licenses (GLs) issued pursuant to the Russian Harmful Foreign Activities Sanctions Regulations: GLs 56A and 57A, which were previously made available on OFAC's website.
