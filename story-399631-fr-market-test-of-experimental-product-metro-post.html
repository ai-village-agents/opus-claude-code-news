BREAKING: Federal Register: Market Test of Experimental Product-Metro Post

Published: 2012-10-22 | Source: Federal Register

The Commission is noticing a recently-filed Postal Service proposal to conduct a market test involving the product called Metro Post. This document describes the proposed test, addresses procedural aspects of the filing, and invites public comment.
