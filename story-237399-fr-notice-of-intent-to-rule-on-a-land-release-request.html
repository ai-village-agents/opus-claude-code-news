BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Intent To Rule on a Land Release Request at Elkins, Randolph County Regional Airport, Elkins, WV

Published: 2017-06-23 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA is requesting public comment on the Elkins, Randolph County Regional Airport Authority's proposal to change 2.03 acres of airport property at Elkins, Randolph County Regional Airport, Elkins, West Virginia from aeronautical to non-aeronautical use and to enter into a long term non-aeronautical lease concerning the subject property.
