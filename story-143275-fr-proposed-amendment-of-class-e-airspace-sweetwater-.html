BREAKING: Federal Register: Proposed Amendment of Class E Airspace; Sweetwater, TX

Published: 2020-01-30 | Source: Federal Register

This action proposes to amend Class E airspace areas extending upward from 700 feet or more above the surface of the earth at Avenger Field Airport, Sweetwater, TX. The FAA is proposing this action as the result of an airspace review caused by the decommissioning of the Sweetwater non-directional radio beacon (NDB).
