BREAKING: Federal Register: Appointing Authority for Military Commissions

Published: 2013-01-16 | Source: Federal Register

This final rule removes 32 CFR part 18 concerning the Appointing Authority for Military Commissions. This rule pertains to a military function of the United States and is exempt from rulemaking requirements. Previously, this rule was published for informational purposes only.
