BREAKING: Federal Register: Certain X-Ray Breast Imaging Devices and Components Thereof; Institution of Investigation

Published: 2017-08-01 | Source: Federal Register

Notice is hereby given that a complaint was filed with the U.S. International Trade Commission on June 28, 2017, under section 337 of the Tariff Act of 1930, as amended, on behalf of Hologic, Inc. of Marlborough, Massachusetts. A supplement was filed on July 10, 2017.
