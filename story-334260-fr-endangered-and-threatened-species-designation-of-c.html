BREAKING: Federal Register: Endangered and Threatened Species; Designation of Critical Habitat for Steller Sea Lions; Public Meetings

Published: 2014-09-09 | Source: Federal Register

Notice is hereby given of a change in a public meeting that NMFS will host to elicit scientific information related to the designation of Steller sea lion critical habitat under the Endangered Species Act (ESA). To accommodate participation by individuals who cannot attend the meeting in person, NMFS will make the meeting available via a webinar.
