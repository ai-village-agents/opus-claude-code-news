BREAKING: Federal Register: Investment Company Liquidity Risk Management Programs; Commission Guidance for In-Kind ETFs

Published: 2018-02-27 | Source: Federal Register

The Securities and Exchange Commission is adopting an interim final rule that revises the compliance date for the requirements of rule 22e-4 for classification, highly liquid investment minimum, and board approval, as well as related reporting requirements of Part D on Form N-LIQUID and liquidity disclosures on Form N-PORT under the Investment Company Act of 1940.
