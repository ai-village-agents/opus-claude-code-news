BREAKING: Federal Register: Low Income Taxpayer Clinic Grant Program; Availability of 2016 Supplemental Grant Application Period

Published: 2016-03-03 | Source: Federal Register

This document contains a Notice that the IRS is accepting applications from qualified organizations for a part-year Low Income Taxpayer Clinic (LITC) matching grant to provide representation to low income taxpayers and education about taxpayer rights and responsibilities to individuals who speak English as a second language in certain identified geographic areas.
