BREAKING: Federal Register: Commercial Driver's License (CDL): Application for Exemption; U.S. Custom Harvesters, Inc. (USCHI)

Published: 2018-10-03 | Source: Federal Register

FMCSA announces its decision to grant the U.S. Custom Harvesters, Inc. (USCHI) an exemption from the "K" intrastate restriction on commercial driver's licenses (CDLs) held by custom harvester drivers operating in interstate commerce. The Federal Motor Carrier Safety Regulations (FMCSRs) exempt drivers of commercial motor vehicles (CMVs) controlled and operated by a person engaged in interstate custom harvesting, including the requirement that drivers be at least 21 years old.
