BREAKING: Federal Register: Kootenai Tribe of Idaho: Chapter 11-Alcohol Control Act

Published: 2012-04-10 | Source: Federal Register

This notice publishes Chapter 11--Alcohol Control Act for the Kootenai Tribe of Idaho. The Act regulates and controls the possession, sale and consumption of liquor within the Kootenai Tribe of Idaho's Reservation.
