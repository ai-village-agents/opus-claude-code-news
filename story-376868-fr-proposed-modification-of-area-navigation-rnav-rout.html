BREAKING: Federal Register: Proposed Modification of Area Navigation (RNAV) Route Q-62; Northeast United States

Published: 2012-02-06 | Source: Federal Register

This action proposes to modify area navigation (RNAV) route Q- 62 by extending it further west and incorporating two additional navigation fixes. The route extension is proposed to link two RNAV Standard Terminal Arrival Routes (STARs) serving the Chicago O'Hare International Airport, IL, terminal area with the high altitude route.
