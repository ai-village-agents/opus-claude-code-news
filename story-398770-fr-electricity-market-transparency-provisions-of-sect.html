BREAKING: Federal Register: Electricity Market Transparency Provisions of Section 220 of the Federal Power Act

The Commission is revising its regulations pursuant to section 220 of the Federal Power Act (FPA), as enacted by section 1281 of the Energy Policy Act of 2005 (EPAct 2005), to facilitate price transparency in markets for the sale and transmission of electric energy in interstate commerce.
