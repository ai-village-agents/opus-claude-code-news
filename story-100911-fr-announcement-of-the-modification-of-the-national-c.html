BREAKING: Federal Register: Announcement of the Modification of the National Customs Automation Program (NCAP) Test Concerning the Automated Commercial Environment (ACE) Portal Accounts To Establish the Vessel Agency Portal Account and To Decommission the Cartman and Lighterman Portal Accounts

Published: 2022-07-20 | Source: Federal Register

This document announces U.S. Customs and Border Protection's (CBP) modification of the National Customs Automation Program (NCAP) test concerning Automated Commercial Environment (ACE) Portal Accounts to establish the ACE Vessel Agency Portal Account, and to decommission the Cartman and Lighterman Portal Accounts due to a lack of usage by the public.
