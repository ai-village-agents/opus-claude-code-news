BREAKING: Federal Register: Draft Habitat Conservation Plan and Draft Environmental Assessment, Meier Group LLC, Thurston County, Washington

Published: 2015-10-26 | Source: Federal Register

We, the U.S. Fish and Wildlife Service (Service), have received an application from The Meier Group, LLC (applicant) for an incidental take permit (permit) under the Endangered Species Act of 1973, as amended (ESA). The applicant requests a permit with a 5-year term that would authorize "take" of the threatened Olympia pocket gopher incidental to otherwise lawful land development in Thurston County, Washington.
