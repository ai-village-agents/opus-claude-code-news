BREAKING: Federal Register: Buy America Handbook; Conducting Pre-Award and Post-Delivery Audits for Rolling Stock Procurements

Published: 2015-06-16 | Source: Federal Register

The Federal Transit Administration (FTA) has placed in the docket and on its Web site proposed guidance, in the form of a handbook, on complying with FTA's Buy America pre-award and post- delivery audit requirements for rolling stock procurements, from the solicitation phase through final acceptance of the rolling stock.
