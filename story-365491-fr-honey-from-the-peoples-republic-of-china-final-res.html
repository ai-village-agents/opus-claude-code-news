BREAKING: Federal Register: Honey From the People's Republic of China: Final Results of Antidumping Duty Administrative Review; 2011-2012

Published: 2013-09-16 | Source: Federal Register

On June 28, 2013, the Department of Commerce (the "Department") published the Preliminary Results \1\ of the 2011-2012 administrative review of the antidumping duty order on honey from the People's Republic of China ("PRC"). The period of review ("POR") is December 1, 2011, through November 30, 2012. We gave interested parties an opportunity to comment on the Preliminary Results, but we received none.
