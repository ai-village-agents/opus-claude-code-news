BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice and Request for Information-State Small Business Credit Initiative (SSBCI)

Published: 2021-05-21 | Source: Federal Register

The State Small Business Credit Initiative (SSBCI) provides funds to States, Territories, and Tribal governments to enable these jurisdictions to support programs for small businesses. Specifically, beginning in FY 2021, the Department of the Treasury (Treasury) is authorized to provide up to $10 billion in support for small business capital and technical assistance programs as a response to the economic effects of the COVID-19 pandemic.
