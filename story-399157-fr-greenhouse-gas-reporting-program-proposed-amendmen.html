BREAKING: Federal Register: Greenhouse Gas Reporting Program: Proposed Amendments and Confidentiality Determinations for Subpart I

Published: 2012-10-16 | Source: Federal Register

This action proposes amending the calculation and monitoring methodologies for the Electronics Manufacturing, of the Greenhouse Gas Reporting Rule. Proposed changes include revising certain calculation methods and adding a new method, amending data reporting requirements, and clarifying terms and definitions.
