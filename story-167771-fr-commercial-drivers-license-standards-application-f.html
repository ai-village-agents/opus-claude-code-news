BREAKING: Federal Register: Commercial Driver's License Standards: Application for Exemption; Daimler Trucks North America, LLC (Daimler)

Published: 2020-12-09 | Source: Federal Register

FMCSA corrects the September 8, 2020 notice seeking public comment on Daimler Trucks North America, LLC's (Daimler) request for an exemption from the commercial driver's license (CDL) requirements for nine of its commercial motor vehicle (CMV) drivers. Daimler also requested an exemption for the same drivers from the requirement to register CDL holders in the Drug and Alcohol Clearinghouse (DAC).
