BREAKING: Federal Register: Compliance With Court Orders by Personnel and Command Sponsored Family Members

Published: 2020-10-14 | Source: Federal Register

This final rule removes DoD's regulation concerning policies on compliance with court orders by DoD employees and DoD Members. The purpose of the DoD Instruction on which this rule is based is to provide internal guidance to DoD Components on cooperation with law enforcement agencies.
