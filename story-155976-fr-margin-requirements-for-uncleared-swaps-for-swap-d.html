BREAKING: Federal Register: Margin Requirements for Uncleared Swaps for Swap Dealers and Major Swap Participants

Published: 2020-07-10 | Source: Federal Register

The Commodity Futures Trading Commission ("Commission" or "CFTC") is seeking comment on a proposed amendment to the margin requirements for uncleared swaps for swap dealers ("SD") and major swap participants ("MSP") for which there is no prudential regulator (the "CFTC Margin Rule").
