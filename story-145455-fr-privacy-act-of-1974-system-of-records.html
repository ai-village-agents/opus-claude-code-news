BREAKING: Federal Register: Privacy Act of 1974; System of Records

Published: 2020-02-27 | Source: Federal Register

The NGA is rescinding a System of Records, Alcoholism and Drug Abuse Files, B0901-07. This System of Records maintained documents relating to alcohol and narcotic use, treatment, assistance, and advice provided to NGA personnel. This system is no longer in use at NGA and all records previously contained within the Alcoholism and Drug Abuse Files were destroyed in accordance with the retention and disposition schedule as stated in the SORN.
