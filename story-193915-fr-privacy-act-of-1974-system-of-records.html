BREAKING: Federal Register: Privacy Act of 1974; System of Records

Published: 2019-11-21 | Source: Federal Register

The Office of the Secretary of Defense (OSD) is modifying a System of Records Notice (SORN), titled "Defense Institute of Security Assistance Management (DISAM) Information System Mission (DISM)," DSCA 05. This modified system will provide efficient administration of U.S. and international students, Defense Institute of Security Cooperation Studies (DISCS) personnel and guest lecturers.
