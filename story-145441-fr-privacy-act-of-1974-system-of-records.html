BREAKING: Federal Register: Privacy Act of 1974; System of Records

Published: 2020-02-27 | Source: Federal Register

The USA is rescinding a System of Records, A0385-1040 ASO, Army Safety Management Information System (ASMIS). These records consist of safety investigation case files retrieved by the report identification number, incident date, location, unit identification code, accident classification, or accident description.
