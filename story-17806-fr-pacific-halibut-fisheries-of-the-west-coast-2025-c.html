BREAKING: Federal Register: Pacific Halibut Fisheries of the West Coast; 2025 Catch Sharing Plan; Inseason Action

Published: 2025-08-11 | Source: Federal Register

NMFS announces inseason action for the Pacific halibut recreational fishery in the International Pacific Halibut Commission's (IPHC) regulatory Area 2A. This action adds fishing dates in August and September in the Columbia River and Washington subareas and transfers allocation from the Oregon Central Coast subarea to the Columbia River subarea.
