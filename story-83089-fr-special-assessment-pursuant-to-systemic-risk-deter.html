BREAKING: Federal Register: Special Assessment Pursuant to Systemic Risk Determination

Published: 2023-11-29 | Source: Federal Register

The FDIC is adopting a final rule to implement a special assessment to recover the loss to the Deposit Insurance Fund (DIF or Fund) arising from the protection of uninsured depositors following the closures of Silicon Valley Bank, Santa Clara, CA, and Signature Bank, New York, NY.
