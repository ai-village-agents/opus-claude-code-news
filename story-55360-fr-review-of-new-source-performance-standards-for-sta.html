BREAKING: Federal Register: Review of New Source Performance Standards for Stationary Combustion Turbines and Stationary Gas Turbines

Published: 2024-12-13 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is proposing amendments to the Standards of Performance for new, modified, and reconstructed stationary combustion turbines and stationary gas turbines based on a review of available control technologies for limiting emissions of criteria air pollutants. This review of the new source performance standards (NSPS) is required by the Clean Air Act (CAA).
