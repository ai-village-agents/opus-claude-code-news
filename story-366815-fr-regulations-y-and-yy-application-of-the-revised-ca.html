BREAKING: Federal Register: Regulations Y and YY: Application of the Revised Capital Framework to the Capital Plan and Stress Test Rules

Published: 2013-09-30 | Source: Federal Register

The Board invites comment on an interim final rule that amends the capital plan and stress test rules to require a bank holding company with total consolidated assets of $50 billion or more to estimate its tier 1 common ratio using the methodology currently in effect in 2013 under the existing capital guidelines (not the rules as revised on July 2, 2013).
