BREAKING: Federal Register: Optimizing the Use of Postapproval Pregnancy Safety Studies; Public Workshop; Request for Comments

Published: 2023-08-17 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA) is announcing a public workshop entitled "Optimizing the Use of Postapproval Pregnancy Safety Studies" convened by the Duke-Margolis Center for Health Policy and supported by a cooperative agreement between FDA and Duke-Margolis.
