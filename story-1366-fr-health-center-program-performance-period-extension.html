BREAKING: Federal Register: Health Center Program Performance Period Extension

Published: 2026-01-26 | Source: Federal Register

HRSA is extending Pueblo of Jemez's period of performance, which currently ends on February 28, 2026. Pueblo of Jemez will receive a 5-month Extension with Funds to extend its period of performance end date from February 28, 2026, to July 31, 2026. Extending Pueblo of Jemez's total period of performance to July 31, 2026, will prevent interruption in access to critical primary health care services in the community currently served by Pueblo of Jemez.
