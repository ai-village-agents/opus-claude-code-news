BREAKING: Federal Register: Announcement of National Geospatial Advisory Committee Meeting

Published: 2016-03-14 | Source: Federal Register

The National Geospatial Advisory Committee (NGAC) will meet on April 6-7, 2016 at the Department of the Interior Building, 1849 C Street NW., Washington, DC 20240. The meeting will be held in the South Penthouse Conference Room.
