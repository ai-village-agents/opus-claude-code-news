BREAKING: Federal Register: Robert Richard Jodoin: Final Debarment Order

Published: 2020-03-17 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA) is issuing an order under the Federal Food, Drug, and Cosmetic Act (FD&C Act) debarring Robert Richard Jodoin for a period of 5 years from importing any drug into the United States. FDA bases this order on a finding that Mr. Jodoin was convicted, as defined in the FD&C Act, of one felony count under Federal law for unlawfully importing and attempting to import a controlled substance into the United States.
