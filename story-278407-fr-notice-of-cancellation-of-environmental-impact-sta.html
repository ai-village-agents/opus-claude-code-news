BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Cancellation of Environmental Impact Statement for the Norfolk International Airport, Norfolk, Virginia

Published: 2016-11-04 | Source: Federal Register

The Federal Aviation Administration (FAA) announces that it has discontinued preparation of an Environmental Impact Statement (EIS) for the proposed construction of new Runway 5R/23L and associated development at Norfolk International Airport, Norfolk, Virginia. The FAA's discontinued preparation of the EIS is based upon the completion of the first phase of the EIS.
