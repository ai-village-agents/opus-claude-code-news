BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Funding Opportunity for America's Marine Highway Projects

Published: 2018-01-05 | Source: Federal Register

The Consolidated Appropriations Act of 2017, signed by the President on May 5, 2017, appropriated $5,000,000 to the Short Sea Transportation Program, commonly referred to as the America's Marine Highway Program (AMHP). The purpose of the appropriation is to make grants to previously designated Marine Highway Projects that support the development and expansion of documented vessels, and port and landside infrastructure.
