BREAKING: Federal Register: Ocean Exploration Advisory Board (OEAB)

Published: 2016-11-29 | Source: Federal Register

OAR publishes this notice to solicit applications to fill a single membership vacancy on the Ocean Exploration Advisory Board (OEAB) with an individual demonstrating expertise in data science and management and one other area of expertise relevant to ocean exploration, such as seafloor mapping. The new OEAB member will serve an initial three-year term, renewable once.
