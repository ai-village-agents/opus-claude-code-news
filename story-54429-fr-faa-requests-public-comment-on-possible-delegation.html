BREAKING: Federal Register: FAA Requests Public Comment on Possible Delegation of Certain Air Operator Certification Tasks

Published: 2024-12-05 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA issues this document to assess interest in and invite public comment on the possible expansion of FAA's delegation program to include new certification tasks that might be accomplished by individual designees or Organization Designation Authorization (ODA) holders. The expanded tasks being considered are certification of all commuter and on-demand operator types, excluding 10 or more passenger operations.
