BREAKING: Federal Register: Amendment of Class D and Class E Airspace, and Revocation of Class E Airspace; Quantico, VA

Published: 2023-05-11 | Source: Federal Register

This action amends Class D airspace, removes Class E airspace designated as an extension to a Class D surface area, establishes a Class D airspace extension, and amends Class E airspace extending upward from 700 feet above the surface at Quantico MCAF (Turner Field), Quantico, VA, as an airspace evaluation determined an update is necessary.
