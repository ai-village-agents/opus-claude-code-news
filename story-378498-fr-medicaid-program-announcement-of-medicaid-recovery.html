BREAKING: Federal Register: Medicaid Program; Announcement of Medicaid Recovery Audit Contractors (RACs) Contingency Fee Update

Published: 2012-02-24 | Source: Federal Register

This notice announces an increase to the maximum contingency fee, for which Federal financial participation (FFP) will be available, that may be paid to Medicaid Recovery Audit Contractors (RAC) by State Medicaid programs as authorized by section 1902(a)(42)(B) of the Social Security Act (the Act), as amended by the Affordable Care Act, requiring States to establish Medicaid RAC programs.
