BREAKING: Federal Register: Tongass National Forest; Alaska; Shoreline II Outfitter/Guide Environmental Impact Statement

Published: 2014-06-16 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Agriculture, Forest Service, will prepare an Environmental Impact Statement (EIS) to allocate a portion of the overall visitor capacity to outfitter and guide (O/G) use within four ranger districts of the Tongass National Forest. The project will consider marine shoreline-based commercial non-motorized recreation use on Admiralty Island National Monument and Juneau, Hoonah and Sitka Ranger Districts.
