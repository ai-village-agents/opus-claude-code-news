BREAKING: Federal Register: Pilot Program for Bulk Submission of Claims to Copyright

Published: 2017-05-09 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Copyright Office is announcing a pilot program that will allow for the bulk submission of claims to copyright in certain limited types of literary works. Specifically, at this time, the pilot program is limited to claims to single literary works that have a single author, where all content that appears in the work was created and is owned solely by that single author.
