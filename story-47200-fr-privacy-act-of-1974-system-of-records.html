BREAKING: Federal Register: Privacy Act of 1974; System of Records

Published: 2024-09-17 | Source: Federal Register

As required by the Privacy Act of 1974 and the Office of Management and Budget (OMB) Circulars A-108 and A-130, the Peace Corps is issuing public notice of its intent to modify a System of Records that it maintains subject to the Privacy Act of 1974, PC-33, entitled "Security Incident Management System (SIMS)".
