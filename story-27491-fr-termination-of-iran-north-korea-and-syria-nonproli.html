BREAKING: Federal Register: Termination of Iran, North Korea, and Syria Nonproliferation Act Measures Against an Indian Entity

Published: 2024-01-18 | Source: Federal Register

A determination has been made, pursuant to the Iran, North Korea, and Syria Nonproliferation Act, to terminate nonproliferation measures pursuant to this Act on an Indian entity.
