BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Bell Helicopter Textron, Inc. Helicopters

Published: 2012-06-13 | Source: Federal Register

We propose to adopt a new airworthiness directive (AD) for the Bell Helicopter Textron, Inc. (BHTI) Model 205A, 205A-1, and 205B helicopters with certain starter/generator power cable assemblies (power cable assemblies). This proposed AD is prompted by the determination that the power cable assembly connector (connector) can deteriorate, causing a short in the connector that may lead to a fire.
