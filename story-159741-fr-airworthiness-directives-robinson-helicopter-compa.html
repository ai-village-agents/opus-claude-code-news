BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Robinson Helicopter Company

Published: 2020-08-27 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA is superseding Airworthiness Directive (AD) 2019-12-18 for Robinson Helicopter Company (Robinson) Model R44 II helicopters. AD 2019-12-18 required inspecting certain engine air induction hoses (hoses) and replacing any hose that was not airworthy. AD 2019-12-18 also prohibited the installation of certain hoses.
