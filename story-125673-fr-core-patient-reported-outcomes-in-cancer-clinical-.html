BREAKING: Federal Register: Core Patient-Reported Outcomes in Cancer Clinical Trials; Draft Guidance for Industry; Availability

Published: 2021-06-10 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA or Agency) is announcing the availability of a draft guidance for industry entitled "Core Patient-Reported Outcomes in Cancer Clinical Trials." This draft guidance provides recommendations to sponsors regarding the collection of a core set of patient-reported clinical outcomes in cancer clinical trials and related considerations for instrument selection and trial design.
