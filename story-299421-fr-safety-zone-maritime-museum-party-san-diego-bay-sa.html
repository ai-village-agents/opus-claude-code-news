BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone; Maritime Museum Party, San Diego Bay; San Diego, CA

Published: 2015-07-21 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is establishing a temporary safety zone on the navigable waters of the San Diego Bay for a fireworks display on the evening of July 23, 2015. This action is necessary to provide for the safety of the participants, crew, spectators, participating vessels, and other vessels and users of the waterway.
