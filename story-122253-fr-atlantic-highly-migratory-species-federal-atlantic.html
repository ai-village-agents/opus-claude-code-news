BREAKING: Federal Register: Atlantic Highly Migratory Species; Federal Atlantic Tunas Regulations in Maine State Waters

Published: 2021-04-26 | Source: Federal Register

NMFS proposes adding Maine to the list of states for which NMFS has determined that Federal Atlantic tunas regulations are applicable within state waters. NMFS is proposing the addition after considering a request from the Maine Department of Marine Resources (MEDMR) and reviewing the state's relevant laws and regulations.
