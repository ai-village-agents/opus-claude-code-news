BREAKING: Federal Register: Certain Integrated Circuit Products and Devices Containing the Same; Notice of a Commission Determination Not To Review an Order Denying Respondent Realtek Semiconductor Corporation's Motion for Sanctions; Termination of the Sanctions Proceeding

Published: 2022-09-22 | Source: Federal Register

Notice is hereby given that the U.S. International Trade Commission has determined not to review an order (Order No. 11) issued by the presiding administrative law judge ("ALJ") denying Respondent Realtek Semiconductor Corp.'s ("Realtek") motion for sanctions. This sanctions proceeding is hereby terminated.
