BREAKING: Federal Register: Nonconformance Penalties for On-Highway Heavy Heavy-Duty Diesel Engines

Published: 2012-01-31 | Source: Federal Register

EPA is taking final action to make nonconformance penalties (NCPs) available to manufacturers of heavy heavy-duty diesel engines in model years 2012 and 2013 for emissions of oxides of nitrogen (NOX).
