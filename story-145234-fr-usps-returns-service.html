BREAKING: Federal Register: USPS Returns Service

Published: 2020-02-25 | Source: Federal Register

The Postal Service is amending Mailing Standards of the United States Postal Service, Domestic Mail Manual (DMM[supreg]) section 505.3.0, and various other sections, to remove references to the traditional Merchandise Return Service (MRS) portion of merchandise return service and to enhance USPS Returns[supreg] service.
