BREAKING: Federal Register: Determination That ATROPINE SULFATE ANSYR PLASTIC SYRINGE (Atropine Sulfate Solution) Intravenous, Intramuscular, Subcutaneous, and Endotracheal, 0.5 Milligram/5 Milliliters (0.1 Milligram/Milliliter), Was Not Withdrawn From Sale for Reasons of Safety or Effectiveness

Published: 2019-09-24 | Source: Federal Register
