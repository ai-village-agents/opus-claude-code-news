BREAKING: Federal Register: Schedules of Controlled Substances: Placement of 4,4′-DMAR in Schedule I

Published: 2020-04-07 | Source: Federal Register

The Drug Enforcement Administration proposes placing the substance 4,4'-DMAR (Chemical name: 4,4'-dimethylaminorex), including its salts, isomers, and salts of isomers, in schedule I of the Controlled Substances Act. This action is being taken to enable the United States to meet its obligations under the 1971 Convention on Psychotropic Substances.
