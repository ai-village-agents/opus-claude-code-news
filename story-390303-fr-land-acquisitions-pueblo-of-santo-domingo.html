BREAKING: Federal Register: Land Acquisitions: Pueblo of Santo Domingo

Published: 2012-07-05 | Source: Federal Register

The Assistant Secretary--Indian Affairs, made a final agency determination to acquire approximately 7,393.75 acres of land into trust for the Pueblo of Santo Domingo on January 27, 2012.
