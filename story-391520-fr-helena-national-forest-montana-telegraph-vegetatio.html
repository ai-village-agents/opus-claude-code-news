BREAKING: Federal Register: Helena National Forest, Montana, Telegraph Vegetation Project

New to the proposed action is the addition of approximately 449 acres of slashing generally small diameter trees followed by prescribed burning within the Jericho Mountain Inventoried Roadless Area.
