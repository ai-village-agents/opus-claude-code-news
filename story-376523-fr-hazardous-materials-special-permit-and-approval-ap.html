BREAKING: Federal Register: Hazardous Materials: Special Permit and Approval Applicant Fitness Determinations; Public Meeting

Published: 2012-02-02 | Source: Federal Register

This notice is to advise interested persons that PHMSA will conduct a public meeting to discuss Special Permit and Approval applicant fitness determinations. The public meeting, to be held on February 29, 2012, in Washington, DC, is intended to provide interested persons with an opportunity to submit oral comments and participate in discussions concerning the criteria used when determining an applicant's minimum level of fitness.
