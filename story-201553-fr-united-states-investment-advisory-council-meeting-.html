BREAKING: Federal Register: United States Investment Advisory Council: Meeting of the United States Investment Advisory Council

Published: 2018-03-07 | Source: Federal Register

The United States Investment Advisory Council (Council) will hold a meeting on Tuesday, March 20, 2018. The Council was chartered on April 6, 2016, to advise the Secretary of Commerce on matters relating to the promotion and retention of foreign direct investment in the United States.
