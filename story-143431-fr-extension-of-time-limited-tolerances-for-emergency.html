BREAKING: Federal Register: Extension of Time-Limited Tolerances for Emergency Exemptions (Multiple Chemicals, Various Commodities)

Published: 2020-01-31 | Source: Federal Register

This regulation extends time-limited tolerances for residues of five pesticides on various commodities, as identified in this document. These actions are in response to EPA's granting of emergency exemptions under the Federal Insecticide, Fungicide, and Rodenticide Act (FIFRA) authorizing use of these pesticides.
