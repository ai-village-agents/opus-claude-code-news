BREAKING: Federal Register: Environmental Quality Incentives Program

Published: 2019-12-17 | Source: Federal Register

The Agriculture Improvement Act of 2018 (the 2018 Farm Bill) made changes to the Environmental Quality Incentives Program (EQIP). This interim rule makes conforming changes to EQIP policies and procedures in the regulations. NRCS has responsibility for administering EQIP using funding, facilities, and authorities of the CCC.
