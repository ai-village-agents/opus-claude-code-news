BREAKING: Federal Register: Making Permanent the Attorney Advisor Program

Published: 2018-08-15 | Source: Federal Register

We are making permanent the attorney advisor program, which has proved to be an integral tool in providing timely decisions to the public while maximizing the use of our administrative law judges (ALJs).
