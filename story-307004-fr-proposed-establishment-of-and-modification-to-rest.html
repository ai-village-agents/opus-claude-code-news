BREAKING: Federal Register: Proposed Establishment of and Modification to Restricted Areas; Fort Sill, OK

Published: 2015-10-19 | Source: Federal Register

This action proposes to establish 2 new restricted areas (R- 5601G and R-5601H) to expand the special use airspace (SUA) complex located at Fort Sill, OK, to provide additional maneuvering airspace for current and planned training activities determined to be hazardous to non-participating aircraft.
