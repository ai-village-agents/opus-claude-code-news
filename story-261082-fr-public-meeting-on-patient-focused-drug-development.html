BREAKING: Federal Register: Public Meeting on Patient-Focused Drug Development for Neuropathic Pain Associated With Peripheral Neuropathy

Published: 2016-04-18 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA or Agency) is announcing a public meeting and an opportunity for public comment on Patient- Focused Drug Development for neuropathic pain associated with peripheral neuropathies. Patient-Focused Drug Development is part of FDA's performance commitments made as part of the fifth authorization of the Prescription Drug User Fee Act (PDUFA V).
