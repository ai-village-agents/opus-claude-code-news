BREAKING: Federal Register: Privacy Act of 1974; System of Records

Published: 2023-09-15 | Source: Federal Register

Pursuant to the Privacy Act of 1974, notice is hereby given that the VA is modifying the system of records entitled, "Corporate Travel and Charge Cards--VA" (131VA047). This system is used for operating, auditing and managing the charge card program involving commercial purchases authorized by VA employees.
