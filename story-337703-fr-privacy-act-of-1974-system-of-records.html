BREAKING: Federal Register: Privacy Act of 1974; System of Records

Published: 2014-10-20 | Source: Federal Register

The Office of the Secretary of Defense proposes to alter a system of records, DHA 14, entitled "Computer/Electronic Accommodations Program" in its inventory of record systems subject to the Privacy Act of 1974, as amended. This system is used to administer a centrally funded program that provides assistive (computer/ electronic) technology solutions to individuals with hearing, visual, dexterity, cognitive, and/or communications impairments in the form of an accessible work environment.
