BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Pratt & Whitney Canada Corp. Turboprop Engines

Published: 2015-05-01 | Source: Federal Register

We are revising airworthiness directive (AD) 2014-17-08 for all Pratt & Whitney Canada Corp. (P&WC) PT6A-114 and PT6A-114A turboprop engines. AD 2014-17-08 required initial and repetitive borescope inspections (BSIs) of compressor turbine (CT) blades, and the removal from service of blades that fail inspection.
