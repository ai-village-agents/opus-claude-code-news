BREAKING: Federal Register: Credit Card Penalty Fees (Regulation Z)

Published: 2023-03-29 | Source: Federal Register

The Consumer Financial Protection Bureau (Bureau) proposes to amend Regulation Z, which implements the Truth in Lending Act (TILA), to better ensure that the late fees charged on credit card accounts are "reasonable and proportional" to the late payment as required under TILA.
