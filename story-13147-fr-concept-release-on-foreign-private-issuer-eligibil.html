BREAKING: Federal Register: Concept Release on Foreign Private Issuer Eligibility

Published: 2025-06-09 | Source: Federal Register

The Securities and Exchange Commission ("Commission") is publishing this concept release to solicit comments on the definition of a foreign private issuer ("FPI"). There have been several developments within the FPI population since the Commission last conducted a broad review of reporting FPIs and the eligibility criteria for FPI status.
