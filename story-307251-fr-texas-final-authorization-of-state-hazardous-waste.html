BREAKING: Federal Register: Texas: Final Authorization of State Hazardous Waste Management Program Revisions

Published: 2015-10-21 | Source: Federal Register

The State of Texas has applied to the Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) for Final authorization of the changes to its hazardous waste program under the Resource Conservation and Recovery Act (RCRA). EPA proposes to grant Final authorization to the State of Texas. In the "Rules and Regulations" section of this Federal Register, EPA is authorizing the changes by direct final rule.
