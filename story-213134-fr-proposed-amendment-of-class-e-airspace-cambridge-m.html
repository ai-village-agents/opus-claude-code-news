BREAKING: Federal Register: Proposed Amendment of Class E Airspace, Cambridge, MD

Published: 2018-08-03 | Source: Federal Register

This action proposes to amend Class E airspace extending upward from 700 feet above the surface at Cambridge-Dorchester Regional Airport, Cambridge, MD, to accommodate airspace reconfiguration due to the decommissioning of the Cambridge non-directional radio beacon and cancellation of the NDB approach. Controlled airspace is necessary for the safety and management of instrument flight rules (IFR) operations at this airport.
