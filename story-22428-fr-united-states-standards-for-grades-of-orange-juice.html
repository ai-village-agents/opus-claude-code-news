BREAKING: Federal Register: United States Standards for Grades of Orange Juice

Published: 2025-11-18 | Source: Federal Register

The Agricultural Marketing Service (AMS) is revising the U.S. Standards for Grades of Orange Juice (U.S. OJ Standards). This Interim Final Notice (IFN) revises the limits for Grade B Brix allowances in Pasteurized Orange Juice (POJ) under the U.S. OJ Standards to reference the Food and Drug Administration's (FDA) Standard of Identity (SOI) for POJ.
