BREAKING: Federal Register: Schedules of Controlled Substances: Temporary Placement of N-Pyrrolidino Metonitazene and N-Pyrrolidino Protonitazene in Schedule I

Published: 2024-09-17 | Source: Federal Register

The Administrator of the Drug Enforcement Administration is issuing this notice of intent to publish a temporary order to schedule two synthetic benzimidazole-opioid substances, N-pyrrolidino metonitazene and N-pyrrolidino protonitazene, including their isomers, esters, ethers, salts, and salts of isomers, esters, and ethers whenever the existence of such isomers, esters, ethers, and salts is possible, in schedule I of the Controlled Substances Act.
