BREAKING: Federal Register: Section 306D Water Systems for Rural and Native Villages in Alaska

Published: 2013-12-20 | Source: Federal Register

The Rural Utilities Service (RUS), an Agency of the United States Department of Agriculture (USDA), proposes to modify its existing regulations and establish a separate regulation for making grants to rural or Native Alaskan Villages under the Rural Alaska Village Grant (RAVG) Program.
