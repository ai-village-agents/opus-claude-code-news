BREAKING: Federal Register: Streamlining Mortgage Servicing for Borrowers Experiencing Payment Difficulties; Regulation X

Published: 2024-07-24 | Source: Federal Register

The Consumer Financial Protection Bureau (Bureau or CFPB) is proposing a rule that would amend regulations originally issued in 2013 regarding the responsibilities of mortgage servicers. The proposed amendments would streamline existing requirements when borrowers seek payment assistance in times of distress, add safeguards when borrowers seek help, and revise existing requirements with respect to borrower assistance.
