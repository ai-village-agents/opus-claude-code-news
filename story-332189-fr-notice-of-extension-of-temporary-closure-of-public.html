BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Extension of Temporary Closure of Public Land in Doña Ana County, NM

Published: 2014-08-14 | Source: Federal Register

The Bureau of Land Management (BLM), Las Cruces District Office is extending a temporary closure of public land in the vicinity of the Community Pit No. 1 in Do[ntilde]a Ana County, New Mexico, in order to protect persons, property, and public lands and resources.
