BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; The Boeing Company Airplanes

Published: 2019-06-10 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA is adopting an airworthiness directive (AD) for certain The Boeing Company Model 737-700C, -800, and -900ER series airplanes. This AD requires a maintenance records check to determine if any main slat track assembly has been removed, an inspection of the main slat track assemblies for a suspect lot number or a lot number that cannot be determined, and applicable on-condition actions.
