BREAKING: Federal Register: Financial Responsibility Requirements Under CERCLA Section 108(b) for Facilities in the Chemical Manufacturing Industry; Extension of Comment Period

Published: 2020-04-17 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is extending the comment period for the proposed rule entitled "Financial Responsibility Requirements under CERCLA Section 108(b) for Classes of Facilities in the Chemical Manufacturing Industry." This proposal was published on February 21, 2020, and the public comment period was scheduled to end on April 21, 2020.
