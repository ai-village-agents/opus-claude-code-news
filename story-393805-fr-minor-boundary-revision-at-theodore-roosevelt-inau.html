BREAKING: Federal Register: Minor Boundary Revision at Theodore Roosevelt Inaugural National Historic Site

Published: 2012-08-15 | Source: Federal Register

Notice is hereby given that, pursuant to 16 U.S.C. 460l- 9(c)(1), the boundary of Theodore Roosevelt Inaugural National Historic Site is modified to include Tract 01-102 containing 0.15 of an acre. The tract is located in Erie County, New York, immediately adjacent to the boundary of the Theodore Roosevelt Inaugural National Historic Site.
