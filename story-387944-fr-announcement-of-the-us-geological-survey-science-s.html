BREAKING: Federal Register: Announcement of the U.S. Geological Survey Science Strategy Planning Feedback Process

Published: 2012-06-08 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Geological Survey is creating 10-year strategies for each of its Mission Areas: Climate and Land Use Change, Core Science Systems, Ecosystems, Energy and Minerals, Environmental Health, Natural Hazards, and Water. This process involves gathering input from the public on draft strategy documents. Feedback can be offered at http:// www.usgs.gov/start--with--science.
