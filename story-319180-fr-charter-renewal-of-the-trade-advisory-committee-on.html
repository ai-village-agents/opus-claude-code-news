BREAKING: Federal Register: Charter Renewal of the Trade Advisory Committee on Africa (TACA); Request for Nominations

Published: 2014-03-18 | Source: Federal Register

The Office of the United States Trade Representative ("USTR"), pursuant to Section 135 of the Trade Act of 1974 (19 U.S.C. 2155(c)(1)) as amended, the Federal Advisory Committee Act (5 U.S.C. App. II), announces the renewal of the charter of the Trade Advisory Committee on Africa (TACA), a federal advisory committee established to provide trade and development policy advice regarding the countries of sub- Saharan Africa.
