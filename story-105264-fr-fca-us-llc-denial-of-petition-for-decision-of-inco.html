BREAKING: Federal Register: FCA US, LLC, Denial of Petition for Decision of Inconsequential Noncompliance

Published: 2022-09-15 | Source: Federal Register

FCA US, LLC, (f/k/a Chrysler Group, LLC) "FCA," has determined that certain Mopar branded headlamp assemblies sold as aftermarket equipment and installed as original equipment in certain model year (MY) 2017-2018 Dodge Journey motor vehicles do not fully comply with Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard (FMVSS) No. 108, Lamps, Reflective Devices, and Associated Equipment.
