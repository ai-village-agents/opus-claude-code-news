BREAKING: Federal Register: Control Date for Qualifying Landings History in the Central Gulf of Alaska Trawl Groundfish Fisheries

Published: 2012-12-26 | Source: Federal Register

At the request of the North Pacific Fishery Management Council (Council), this notice announces a control date of December 31, 2012, that may be used as a reference for future management actions applicable to, but not limited to, qualifying landings and permit history for an allocation-based management or catch share program in the Central Gulf of Alaska (GOA) trawl groundfish fisheries.
