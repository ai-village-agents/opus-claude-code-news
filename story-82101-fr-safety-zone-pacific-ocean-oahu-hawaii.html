BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone; Pacific Ocean, Oahu, Hawaii

Published: 2023-11-16 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is establishing a temporary safety zone for the re-entry of a space vehicle from the Starship Super Heavy launch by Space Exploration Technologies Corporation (Space X). The temporary safety zone is located within the Coast Guard District Fourteen area of responsibility, approximately 129 nautical miles offshore of Oahu, Hawaii.
