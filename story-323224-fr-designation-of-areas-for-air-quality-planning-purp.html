BREAKING: Federal Register: Designation of Areas for Air Quality Planning Purposes; State of Arizona; Pinal County and Gila County; Pb

Published: 2014-05-02 | Source: Federal Register

Pursuant to section 107(d)(3) of the Clean Air Act, the Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is proposing to redesignate the Hayden area in Arizona, which encompasses portions of southern Gila and eastern Pinal counties, from "unclassifiable" to "nonattainment" for the 2008 national ambient air quality standards for lead (Pb).
