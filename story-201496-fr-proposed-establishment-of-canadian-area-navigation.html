BREAKING: Federal Register: Proposed Establishment of Canadian Area Navigation (RNAV) Route T-705; Northeastern United States

Published: 2018-03-06 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA is proposing to establish Canadian area navigation (RNAV) route T-705 in the Northeastern United States (U.S.). This proposal would extend the Canadian Route into U.S. airspace.
