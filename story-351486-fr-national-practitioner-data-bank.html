BREAKING: Federal Register: National Practitioner Data Bank

Published: 2013-04-05 | Source: Federal Register

This final rule revises existing regulations under sections 401-432 of the Health Care Quality Improvement Act of 1986 and section 1921 of the Social Security Act, governing the National Practitioner Data Bank, to incorporate statutory requirements under the Patient Protection and Affordable Care Act of 2010 (Affordable Care Act).
