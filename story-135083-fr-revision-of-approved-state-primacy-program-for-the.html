BREAKING: Federal Register: Revision of Approved State Primacy Program for the State of Nevada

Published: 2021-10-07 | Source: Federal Register

Notice is hereby given that the State of Nevada (State) revised its approved State primacy program under the federal Safe Drinking Water Act (SDWA) by adopting the federal Filter Backwash Recycling Rule. The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) has determined that the State authorities implementing the program revision are no less stringent than the corresponding Federal regulations and that the State's request for a program revision meets applicable SDWA primacy requirements.
