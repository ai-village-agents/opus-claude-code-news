BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Diamond Aircraft Industries GmbH Airplanes

Published: 2021-08-03 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA proposes to adopt a new airworthiness directive (AD) for all Diamond Aircraft Industries GmbH Models DA 42, DA 42 NG, and DA 42 M-NG airplanes. This proposed AD was prompted by mandatory continuing airworthiness information (MCAI) originated by an aviation authority of another country to identify and correct an unsafe condition on an aviation product.
