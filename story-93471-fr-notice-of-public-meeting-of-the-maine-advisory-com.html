BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Public Meeting of the Maine Advisory Committee; Cancellation

Published: 2022-04-13 | Source: Federal Register

The Commission on Civil Rights published a notice in the Federal Register concerning a meeting of the Maine Advisory Committee. The meeting scheduled for Wednesday, May 4, 2022, at 12:00 p.m. (ET) is cancelled. The notice is in the Federal Register of Thursday, March 3, 2022, in FR Doc. 2022-04438, in the first of page 12078 and the first and second columns of page 12079.
