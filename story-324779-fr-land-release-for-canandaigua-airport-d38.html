BREAKING: Federal Register: Land Release for Canandaigua Airport (D38)

Published: 2014-05-20 | Source: Federal Register

The Federal Aviation Administration is requesting public comment on the Canandaigua Airport (D38), Canandaigua, New York, Notice of Proposed Release from Aeronautical Use of approximately 8.315 +/- acres of airport property, to allow for non-aeronautical development. The parcel is located approximately west of the midpoint of Runway 13-31 at the Canandaigua Airport. The tract currently consists of 8.315 +/- acres and it is currently vacant.
