BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Bombardier Inc. Airplanes

Published: 2014-03-05 | Source: Federal Register

We propose to supersede Airworthiness Directive (AD) 2011-15- 09, which applies to certain Bombardier, Inc. Model DHC-8-400, -401, and -402 airplanes. AD 2011-15-09 currently requires repetitive inspections for proper operation of the main landing gear (MLG) alternate extension system (AES), and corrective actions if necessary.
