BREAKING: Federal Register: Submission for OMB Review; OCSE-75 Tribal Child Support Enforcement Program Annual Data Report (OMB #0970-0320)

Published: 2019-12-19 | Source: Federal Register

The Office of Child Support Enforcement (OCSE), Administration for Children and Families (ACF), U.S. Department of Health and Human Services (HHS), is requesting a three-year extension of the form OCSE- 75--Tribal Child Support Enforcement Annual Data Report (OMB # 0970- 0320, expiration 03/31/2020). There are no changes requested to the form.
