BREAKING: Federal Register: Title I-Improving the Academic Achievement of the Disadvantaged (Migrant Education Program)

Published: 2016-05-10 | Source: Federal Register

The Secretary issues regulations to implement the Migrant Student Information Exchange (MSIX), a nationwide, electronic records exchange mechanism mandated under title I, part C, of the Elementary and Secondary Education Act of 1965, as amended (ESEA). As a condition of receiving a grant of funds under the Migrant Education Program (MEP), each State educational agency (SEA) must collect, maintain, and submit minimum educational and health information to MSIX within established time frames.
