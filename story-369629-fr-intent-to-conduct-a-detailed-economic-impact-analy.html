BREAKING: Federal Register: Intent To Conduct a Detailed Economic Impact Analysis

Published: 2013-11-07 | Source: Federal Register

This notice is to inform the public that the Export-Import Bank of the United States is withdrawing a previous Federal Register notice informing the public of its intent to conduct a detailed economic impact analysis regarding a loan guarantee to support the export of U.S.-manufactured Boeing 787 wide-body passenger aircraft to an airline in China.
