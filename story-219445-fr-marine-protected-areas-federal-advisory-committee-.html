BREAKING: Federal Register: Marine Protected Areas Federal Advisory Committee: Call for Nominations and Public Meeting

Published: 2018-10-24 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Commerce (Department) is seeking nominations for membership on the Marine Protected Areas Federal Advisory Committee (Committee). The Committee advises the Secretaries of Commerce and Interior on implementing Section 4 of Executive Order 13158, focusing mainly on strategies and priorities for the design, monitoring and adaptive management of effective MPAs in U.S. waters.
