BREAKING: Federal Register: Whistleblower Protection Advisory Committee (WPAC)

Published: 2012-05-17 | Source: Federal Register

In accordance with the provisions of the Federal Advisory Committee Act, as amended (5. U.S.C., App. 2), the Secretary of Labor intends to establish the Whistleblower Protection Advisory Committee (WPAC).
