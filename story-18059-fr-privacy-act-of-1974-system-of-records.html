BREAKING: Federal Register: Privacy Act of 1974; System of Records

Published: 2025-08-14 | Source: Federal Register

Pursuant to the Privacy Act of 1974, U.S.C. 552a, and Office of Management and Budget (OMB) Circular No. A-108, the Consumer Financial Protection Bureau (CFPB) proposes to rescind existing system of record "CFPB.023 Prize Competitions Program Records."
