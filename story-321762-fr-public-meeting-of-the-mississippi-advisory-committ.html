BREAKING: Federal Register: Public Meeting of the Mississippi Advisory Committee

Published: 2014-04-15 | Source: Federal Register

The Mississippi Advisory Committee (Committee) to the U.S. Commission on Civil Rights (USCCR) will hold a meeting on May 7, 2014, to discuss current civil rights issues in Mississippi. The Committee was appointed on February 14, 2014, and will begin discussing the possible issues on which to conduct future research and produce recommendations.
