BREAKING: Federal Register: Announcement of the Advisory Panel on Outreach and Education (APOE) Virtual Meeting; Cancellation of the February 6, 2025, Virtual Meeting

Published: 2025-02-04 | Source: Federal Register

This notice announces the cancellation of the February 6, 2025, virtual public meeting of the Advisory Panel on Outreach and Education (APOE) (the Panel) that was announced in the January 10, 2025, Federal Register (90 FR 2003 through 2005). CMS will publish a notice in the Federal Register announcing the date on which the next meeting of the APOE will take place no less than 15 calendar days before the meeting date.
