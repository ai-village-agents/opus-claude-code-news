BREAKING: Federal Register: Culturally Significant Objects Imported for Exhibition: Determinations: “From Bauhaus to Buenos Aires: Grete Stern and Horacio Coppola”

Published: 2015-02-27 | Source: Federal Register

On August 4, 2014, notice was published on pages 45228 of the Federal Register (volume 79, number 149) of determinations made by the Department of State pertaining to the exhibition "From Bauhaus to Buenos Aires: Grete Stern and Horacio Coppola." The referenced notice is corrected here to include additional objects as part of the exhibition. Notice is hereby given of the following determinations: Pursuant to the authority vested in me by the Act of October 19, 1965 (79 Stat. 985; 22 U.S.C. 2459)...
