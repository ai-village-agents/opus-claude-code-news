BREAKING: Federal Register: South Dakota: Final Authorization of State Hazardous Waste Management Program Revisions and Incorporation by Reference

Published: 2016-06-24 | Source: Federal Register

The State of South Dakota has applied to the Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) for Final authorization of the changes to its hazardous waste program under the Resource Conservation and Recovery Act (RCRA). The EPA has determined that these changes satisfy all requirements needed to qualify for final authorization, and is authorizing the State's changes through this direct final action.
