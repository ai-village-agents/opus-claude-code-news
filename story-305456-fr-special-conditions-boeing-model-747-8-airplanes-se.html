BREAKING: Federal Register: Special Conditions: Boeing Model 747-8 Airplanes; Seats With Inflatable Lap Belts

Published: 2015-09-30 | Source: Federal Register

These special conditions are issued for Boeing Model 747-8 airplanes. These airplanes will have a novel or unusual design feature associated with seats with inflatable lap belts. The applicable airworthiness regulations do not contain adequate or appropriate safety standards for this design feature.
