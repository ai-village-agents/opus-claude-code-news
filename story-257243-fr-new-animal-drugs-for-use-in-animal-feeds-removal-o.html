BREAKING: Federal Register: New Animal Drugs for Use in Animal Feeds; Removal of Obsolete and Redundant Regulations

Published: 2016-03-07 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA) is removing regulations that required sponsors to submit data regarding the subtherapeutic use of certain antibiotic, nitrofuran, and sulfonamide drugs administered in animal feed as these regulations have been determined to be obsolete.
