BREAKING: Federal Register: Advisory Commission on Childhood Vaccines

Published: 2020-09-01 | Source: Federal Register

The original Federal Register Notice announcing the September 2020 Advisory Commission on Childhood Vaccines (ACCV) meeting indicated that this meeting would be held on September 3-4, 2020. This meeting is not being conducted over 2 days, and instead will only take place only on September 4, 2020. The ACCV will hold a public meeting on September 4, 2020, at 10:00 a.m. ET.
