BREAKING: Federal Register: 1,3-Benzenedicarboxylic Acid, 5-Sulfo-, Sodium Salt (1:1), Polymer With 1,3-Benzenedicarboxylic Acid, 1,4-Cyclohexanedimethanol and 2,2′-Oxybis[ethanol]; Tolerance Exemption

Published: 2022-11-04 | Source: Federal Register

This regulation establishes an exemption from the requirement of a tolerance for residues of 1,3-benzenedicarboxylic acid, 5-sulfo-, sodium salt (1:1), polymer with 1,3-benzenedicarboxylic acid, 1,4- cyclohexanedimethanol and 2,2'-oxybis[ethanol] (CAS Reg. No. 54590-72- 6), when used as an inert ingredient in a pesticide chemical formulation.
