BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Public Meeting of the Tennessee Advisory Committee; Cancellation

Published: 2023-03-13 | Source: Federal Register

The Commission on Civil Rights published a notice in the Federal Register concerning a meeting of the Tennessee Advisory Committee. The meeting scheduled for Thursday, March 16, 2023, at 12:00 p.m. (CT) is cancelled. The notice is in the Federal Register of Tuesday, February 28, 2023, in FR Doc. 2023-04004, on page 12653-12654 (2 pages).
