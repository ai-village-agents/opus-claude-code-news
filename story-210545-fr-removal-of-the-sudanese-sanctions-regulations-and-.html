BREAKING: Federal Register: Removal of the Sudanese Sanctions Regulations and Amendment of the Terrorism List Government Sanctions Regulations

Published: 2018-06-29 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of the Treasury's Office of Foreign Assets Control (OFAC) is removing from the Code of Federal Regulations the Sudanese Sanctions Regulations as a result of the revocation of certain provisions of one Executive Order and the entirety of another Executive Order on which the regulations were based.
