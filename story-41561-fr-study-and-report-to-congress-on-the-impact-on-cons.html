BREAKING: Federal Register: Study and Report to Congress on the Impact on Consumers and Markets in the United States of a Final International Insurance Capital Standard

Published: 2024-07-12 | Source: Federal Register

The Board of Governors of the Federal Reserve System (Board) and the Federal Insurance Office (FIO) of the Department of the Treasury (together, the agencies) are providing notice that the agencies intend to commence drafting a report to Congress on the impact on consumers and markets in the United States before supporting or consenting to the adoption of any final international insurance capital standard.
