BREAKING: Federal Register: Somalia Sanctions Regulations

Published: 2021-04-28 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of the Treasury's Office of Foreign Assets Control (OFAC) is amending the Somalia Sanctions Regulations and reissuing them in their entirety to further implement an April 12, 2010 Somalia-related Executive order, and to implement a July 20, 2012 Somalia-related Executive order.
