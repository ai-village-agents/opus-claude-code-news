BREAKING: Federal Register: Special Conditions: CFM International, LEAP-1B Engine Models; Incorporation of Woven Composite Fan Blades

Published: 2015-11-20 | Source: Federal Register

These special conditions are issued for the CFM International (CFM), LEAP-1B engine models. This engine model will have a novel or unusual design feature associated with the engine: woven composite fan blades. The applicable airworthiness regulations do not contain adequate or appropriate safety standards for this design feature.
