BREAKING: Federal Register: Bureau of International Labor Affairs; Labor Advisory Committee for Trade Negotiations and Trade Policy

Published: 2014-07-16 | Source: Federal Register

Notice is hereby given that a meeting of the Labor Advisory Committee for Trade Negotiation and Trade Policy has been postponed until further notice. This meeting, which was closed to the public, was to be held on July 7, 2014, from 10:00 a.m. to 11:30 a.m. at the U.S. Department of Labor, Secretary's Conference Room, 200 Constitution Ave. NW., Washington, DC. The original Federal Register notice announcing this meeting was published on June 18, 2014, at 79 FR 34786.
