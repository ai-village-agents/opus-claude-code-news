BREAKING: Federal Register: Privacy Act of 1974; Publication in Full of All Notices of Systems of Records, Including Several New Systems, Substantive Amendments to Existing Systems, Decommissioning of Obsolete Legacy Systems, and Publication of Proposed Routines Uses

Published: 2016-04-29 | Source: Federal Register

This notice provides a complete publication of all Department of Labor Systems of Records. This notice proposes two new universal routine uses to the systems. This notice will update our last complete publication in full which appeared in the Federal Register on April 8, 2002. In addition to the 21 new systems which are being published for the first time, the notice proposes substantive and nonsubstantive amendments to 108 other systems that have been previously published.
