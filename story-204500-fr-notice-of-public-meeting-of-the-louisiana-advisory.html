BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Public Meeting of the Louisiana Advisory Committee To Discuss the Barriers to Voting Report

Published: 2018-04-13 | Source: Federal Register

Notice is hereby given, pursuant to the provisions of the rules and regulations of the U.S. Commission on Civil Rights (Commission) and the Federal Advisory Committee Act that the Louisiana Advisory Committee (Committee) will hold a meeting on Monday, April 23, 2018, at 1:00:00 p.m. Central for a discussion on the Barriers to Voting in Louisiana report.
