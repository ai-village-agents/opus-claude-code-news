BREAKING: Federal Register: Special Local Regulation; Clinch River, Oak Ridge, TN

Published: 2021-05-07 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard establishes a temporary special local regulation for all navigable waters on the Clinch River from mile marker (MM) 48.5 to MM 52.0 during the U.S. Rowing Southeast Youth Championship.
