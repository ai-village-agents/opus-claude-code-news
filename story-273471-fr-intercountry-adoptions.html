BREAKING: Federal Register: Intercountry Adoptions

Published: 2016-09-08 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of State (the Department) proposes to amend requirements for accreditation of agencies and approval of persons to provide adoption services in intercountry adoption cases. The proposed rule includes a new subpart establishing parameters for U.S. accrediting entities to authorize adoption service providers who have received accreditation or approval to provide adoption services in countries designated by the Secretary, which will be known as "country-specific authorization" (CSA). ...
