BREAKING: Federal Register: Agency Information Collection Activities; Customs-Trade Partnership Against Terrorism (C-TPAT)

Published: 2013-07-25 | Source: Federal Register

U.S. Customs and Border Protection (CBP) will be submitting the following information collection request to the Office of Management and Budget (OMB) for review and approval in accordance with the Paperwork Reduction Act: Customs-Trade Partnership Against Terrorism (C-TPAT). This is a proposed extension of an information collection that was previously approved. CBP is proposing that this information collection be extended with a change to the burden hours.
