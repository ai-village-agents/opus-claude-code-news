BREAKING: Federal Register: Regulated Navigation Area, Chicago Sanitary and Ship Canal, Romeoville, IL

Published: 2018-07-19 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard Ninth District Commander is amending the navigational and operational restrictions of the Regulated Navigation Area (RNA) on the Chicago Sanitary and Ship Canal (CSSC) near Romeoville, Illinois, and removing the redundant Safety Zone currently in place.
