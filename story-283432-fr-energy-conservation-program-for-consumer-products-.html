BREAKING: Federal Register: Energy Conservation Program for Consumer Products: Test Procedures for Direct Heating Equipment and Pool Heaters

Published: 2015-01-06 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Department of Energy (DOE) is amending its test procedures for vented home heating equipment and pool heaters established under the Energy Policy and Conservation Act. This rulemaking fulfills DOE's statutory obligation to review its test procedures for covered products at least once every seven years.
