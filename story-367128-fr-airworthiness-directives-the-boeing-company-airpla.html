BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; The Boeing Company Airplanes

Published: 2013-10-02 | Source: Federal Register

We are adopting a new airworthiness directive (AD) for all The Boeing Company Model 717-200 airplanes. This AD was prompted by multiple reports of cracks of overwing frames. This AD requires repetitive inspections for cracking of the overwing frames, and corrective actions if necessary.
