BREAKING: Federal Register: Supplemental Nutrition Assistance Program: Suspension of SNAP Benefit Payments to Retailers

Published: 2013-02-22 | Source: Federal Register

Integrity in the Supplemental Nutrition Assistance Program (SNAP) is a primary Program concern. This proposed rule codifies a provision of the Food, Conservation, and Energy Act of 2008 (FCEA) which authorizes the Department to suspend the payment of redeemed SNAP benefits to certain retail food stores or wholesale food concerns pending administrative action to disqualify the firms for fraudulent activity.
