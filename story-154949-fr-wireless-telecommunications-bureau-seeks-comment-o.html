BREAKING: Federal Register: Wireless Telecommunications Bureau Seeks Comment on PSSI Global Services, L.L.C. Request for Stay of 3.7-4.2 GHz Band Report and Order and Order of Proposed Modification

Published: 2020-06-29 | Source: Federal Register

In this document, the Wireless Telecommunications Bureau (Bureau) seeks comment on a request that the Commission stay, pending judicial review, the rules adopted in the Report and Order and Order of Proposed Modification, filed by PSSI Global Services, L.L.C. (PSSI).
