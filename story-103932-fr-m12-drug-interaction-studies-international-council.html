BREAKING: Federal Register: M12 Drug Interaction Studies; International Council for Harmonisation; Draft Guidance for Industry; Availability

Published: 2022-08-29 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA or Agency) is announcing the availability of a draft guidance for industry entitled "M12 Drug Interaction Studies." The draft guidance was prepared under the auspices of the International Council for Harmonisation (ICH), formerly the International Conference on Harmonisation.
