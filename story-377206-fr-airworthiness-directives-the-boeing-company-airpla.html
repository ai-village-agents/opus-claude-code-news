BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; The Boeing Company Airplanes

Published: 2012-02-09 | Source: Federal Register

We propose to supersede an existing airworthiness directive (AD) that applies to certain The Boeing Company Model 767 airplanes. The existing AD currently requires an inspection to detect cracks and fractures of the outboard hinge fitting assemblies on the trailing edge of the inboard main flap, and follow-on and corrective actions if necessary.
