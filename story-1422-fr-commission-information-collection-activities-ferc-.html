BREAKING: Federal Register: Commission Information Collection Activities (Ferc-725s); Comment Request; Revision

Published: 2026-01-26 | Source: Federal Register

In compliance with the requirements of the Paperwork Reduction Act of 1995, the Federal Energy Regulatory Commission (Commission or FERC) is soliciting public comment on proposed revisions of the currently approved information collection, FERC-725S, Mandatory Reliability Standards for the Bulk-Power System; EOP Reliability Standards. The 60-day notice published on September 23, 2025; no comments were received.
