BREAKING: Federal Register: Livestock Mandatory Reporting Program; Establishment of the Reporting Regulation for Wholesale Pork

Published: 2012-08-22 | Source: Federal Register

On April 2, 2001, the U.S. Department of Agriculture (USDA), Agricultural Marketing Service (AMS) implemented the Livestock Mandatory Reporting (LMR) program as required by the Livestock Mandatory Reporting Act of 1999 (1999 Act). In October 2006, the LMR program was reauthorized by Congress through September 2010.
