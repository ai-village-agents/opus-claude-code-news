BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of the Next CAFTA-DR Environmental Affairs Council Meeting

Published: 2013-04-11 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of State and the Office of the United States Trade Representative are providing notice that the government parties to the Dominican Republic-Central America-United States Free Trade Agreement (CAFTA-DR) intend to hold the seventh meeting of the Environmental Affairs Council (Council) established under Chapter 17 of that agreement in Santo Domingo, Dominican Republic on May 9, 2013 at a venue to be announced.
