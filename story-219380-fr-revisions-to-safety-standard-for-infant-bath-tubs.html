BREAKING: Federal Register: Revisions to Safety Standard for Infant Bath Tubs

Published: 2018-10-23 | Source: Federal Register

In accordance with section 104(b) of the Consumer Product Safety Improvement Act of 2008 (CPSIA), the U.S. Consumer Product Safety Commission (CPSC), in March 2017, published a consumer product safety standard for infant bath tubs. The standard incorporated by reference the applicable ASTM voluntary standard.
