BREAKING: Federal Register: Amendments to Financial Disclosures About Acquired and Disposed Businesses

Published: 2020-08-31 | Source: Federal Register

We are adopting amendments to our rules and forms to improve their application, assist registrants in making more meaningful determinations of whether a subsidiary or an acquired or disposed business is significant, and to improve the disclosure requirements for financial statements relating to acquisitions and dispositions of businesses, including real estate operations and investment companies.
