BREAKING: Federal Register: Final Supplementary Rules for Guffey Gorge in Park County, Colorado

Published: 2017-12-20 | Source: Federal Register

The Bureau of Land Management (BLM) Royal Gorge Field Office is implementing supplementary rules to regulate certain activities on public lands within Guffey Gorge in Park County, Colorado. These supplementary rules are necessary to implement decisions found in the Guffey Gorge Management Plan approved on June 29, 2015, to provide for the protection of persons, property, and public lands and resources located within the 80-acre site.
