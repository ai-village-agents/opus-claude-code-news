BREAKING: Federal Register: Center for Devices and Radiological Health Premarket Approval Application Critical to Quality Pilot Program

Published: 2017-09-12 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration's (FDA or Agency or we) Center for Devices and Radiological Health (CDRH or Center), Office of Compliance (OC) and Office of In Vitro Diagnostics and Radiological Health (OIR) is announcing its Premarket Approval Application Critical to Quality (PMA CtQ) pilot program.
