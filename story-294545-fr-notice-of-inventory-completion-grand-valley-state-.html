BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Inventory Completion: Grand Valley State University, Allendale, MI; Correction

Published: 2015-05-22 | Source: Federal Register

Grand Valley State University has corrected an inventory of human remains published in a Notice of Inventory Completion in the Federal Register on February 2, 2015. This notice corrects the minimum number of individuals listed in that notice. Lineal descendants or representatives of any Indian tribe or Native Hawaiian organization not identified in this notice that wish to request transfer of control of these human remains should submit a written request to Grand Valley State University.
