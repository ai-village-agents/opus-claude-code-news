BREAKING: Federal Register: Special Conditions: Airbus A350-900 Airplane; Crashworthiness, Emergency Landing Conditions

Published: 2014-07-25 | Source: Federal Register

These special conditions are issued for Airbus Model A350-900 airplanes. These airplanes have a novel or unusual design feature associated with crashworthiness of carbon-fiber-reinforced plastic used in the construction of the fuselage. The applicable airworthiness regulations do not contain adequate or appropriate safety standards for this design feature.
