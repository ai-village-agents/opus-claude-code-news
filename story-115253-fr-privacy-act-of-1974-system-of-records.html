BREAKING: Federal Register: Privacy Act of 1974; System of Records

Published: 2021-01-25 | Source: Federal Register

As required by the Privacy Act of 1974, notice is hereby given that the Department of Veterans Affairs (VA) is amending the system of records currently entitled, "Patient Advocate Tracking System (PATS)[dash]VA" (100VA10NS10) as set forth in the Federal Register. VA is amending the system of records by revising the System Name, System Number; System Location; System Manager; Record Source Categories; Routine Uses of Records Maintained in the System, Including Categories of Users and the Purposes...
