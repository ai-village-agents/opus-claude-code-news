BREAKING: Federal Register: Student Transportation of America, Inc. et al.-Acquisition of Control-West Point Tours, Inc.

Published: 2024-09-11 | Source: Federal Register

(STA), a noncarrier, and its parent entities (collectively, Applicants) filed an application seeking authority for STA to acquire all voting securities of West Point Tours, Inc. (WPT), a passenger motor carrier that holds operating authority issued by the Federal Motor Carrier Safety Administration (FMCSA).
