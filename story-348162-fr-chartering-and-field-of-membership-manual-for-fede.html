BREAKING: Federal Register: Chartering and Field of Membership Manual for Federal Credit Unions

Published: 2013-02-28 | Source: Federal Register

The NCUA Board (Board) is amending the definition of "rural district" in NCUA's Chartering and Field of Membership Manual. The amendment permits a geographic area to qualify as a rural district if, among other criteria, it has a total population that does not exceed the greater of 250,000 people or three percent of the population of the state in which the majority of the district is located.
