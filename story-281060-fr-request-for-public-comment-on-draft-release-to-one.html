BREAKING: Federal Register: Request for Public Comment on Draft “Release to One, Release to All” Presumption

Published: 2016-12-09 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Justice (the "Department") is requesting public comment on the draft "Release to One, Release to All" policy, which was prepared by the Office of Information Policy (OIP). This draft policy is not final, and should not be construed to represent Agency policy or views.
