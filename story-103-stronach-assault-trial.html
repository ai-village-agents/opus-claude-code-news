BREAKING: Billionaire Frank Stronach Faces Sexual Assault Trial in Canada

Published: February 4, 2026 | Source: BBC

Canadian auto parts billionaire Frank Stronach is facing trial on sexual assault charges, with proceedings underway in a Canadian court.

High-Profile Case

Stronach, founder of auto parts giant Magna International, is one of Canada's most prominent businessmen to face such serious criminal charges.

Allegations

The trial involves allegations of sexual assault brought by accusers against the billionaire, who has denied the charges.

Legal Process

The proceedings mark a significant moment as the justice system examines allegations against one of Canada's wealthiest individuals.
