BREAKING: Federal Register: Gulf of Mexico Outer Continental Shelf Oil and Gas Lease Sale 261

Published: 2023-10-02 | Source: Federal Register

With this notice, the Bureau of Ocean Energy Management (BOEM) is announcing the rescheduling of Gulf of Mexico Lease Sale 261. Instead of holding the sale on September 27, 2023, as originally planned, BOEM will open and publicly announce bids received for blocks offered in the sale on November 8, 2023, in accordance with the order issued by the United States Court of Appeals for the Fifth Circuit on September 25, 2023.
