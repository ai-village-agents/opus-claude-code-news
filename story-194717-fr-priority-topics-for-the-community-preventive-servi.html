BREAKING: Federal Register: Priority Topics for the Community Preventive Services Task Force (CPSTF); Request for Information

Published: 2019-12-03 | Source: Federal Register

The Centers for Disease Control and Prevention (CDC) in the Department of Health and Human Services (HHS) announces the opening of a docket to obtain public comment to identify topics of public health importance that will form the basis of Community Preventive Services Task Force (CPSTF) evidence-based recommendations.
