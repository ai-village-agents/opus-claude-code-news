BREAKING: Federal Register: Renaming of Restricted Areas R-5311A, R-5311B, and R-5311C; Fort Bragg, NC

Published: 2023-11-28 | Source: Federal Register

This action is an administrative change to rename restricted areas R-5311A, R-5311B, and R-5311C, Fort Bragg, NC, and to update the using agency description to reflect the change. Additionally, geographic coordinate technical amendments for two boundary points listed in the restricted areas are made to accurately align the existing boundary with the Little River referenced in the descriptions.
