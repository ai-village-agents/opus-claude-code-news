BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Glasflugel Gliders

Published: 2012-08-07 | Source: Federal Register

We are adopting a new airworthiness directive (AD) for Glasflugel Models Standard Libelle-201B, Club Libelle 205, Mosquito, and Kestrel gliders. This AD results from mandatory continuing airworthiness information (MCAI) issued by an aviation authority of another country to identify and correct an unsafe condition on an aviation product.
