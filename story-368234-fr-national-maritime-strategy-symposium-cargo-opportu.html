BREAKING: Federal Register: National Maritime Strategy Symposium: Cargo Opportunities and Sealift Capacity

Published: 2013-10-28 | Source: Federal Register

The Maritime Administration (MARAD) invites the public and other Marine Transportation System stakeholders to participate in a discussion intended to develop a robust national maritime strategy. The purpose of this public meeting is to gather ideas for improving the Nation's cargo opportunities and sealift capacity while ensuring future sustainability.
