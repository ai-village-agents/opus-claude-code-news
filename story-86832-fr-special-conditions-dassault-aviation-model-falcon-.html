BREAKING: Federal Register: Special Conditions: Dassault Aviation Model Falcon 6X Airplane; Design Roll Maneuver

Published: 2022-01-20 | Source: Federal Register

These amended special conditions are issued for the Dassault Aviation (Dassault) Model Falcon 6X airplane. This airplane will have a novel or unusual design feature when compared to the state of technology envisioned in the airworthiness standards for transport- category airplanes. This design feature is electronic flight controls that affect maneuvering. The applicable airworthiness regulations do not contain adequate or appropriate safety standards for this design feature.
