BREAKING: Federal Register: Procedures for Disclosure or Production of Information Under the Freedom of Information Act; Amendments

Published: 2017-01-03 | Source: Federal Register

The Consumer Product Safety Commission (Commission, CPSC, or we) is issuing this notice of proposed rulemaking (NPR) to update its Freedom of Information Act (FOIA) rule. We are proposing to revise the rule to conform to the amendments of the FOIA Improvement Act of 2016 (the 2016 FOIA) to the FOIA.
