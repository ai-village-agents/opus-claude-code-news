BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Competitive Offer and Notice of Segregation for Solar Energy Development on Public Land, Clark County, Nevada

Published: 2022-04-05 | Source: Federal Register

The Bureau of Land Management (BLM), Las Vegas Field Office is announcing that it will accept competitive bids to determine a preferred right-of-way applicant for solar energy development on a 1,635-acre parcel of public land, referred to as the Dry Lake East Designated Leasing Area, located in Clark County, Nevada.
