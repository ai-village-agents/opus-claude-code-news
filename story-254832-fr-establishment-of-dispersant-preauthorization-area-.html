BREAKING: Federal Register: Establishment of Dispersant Preauthorization Area in Alaska

Published: 2016-02-09 | Source: Federal Register

On behalf of the Alaska Regional Response Team (ARRT), the U.S. Coast Guard (USCG) announces establishment of a more inclusive, comprehensive, and conservative dispersant use policy that includes a preauthorization area and an enhanced protocol for use of chemical dispersant during responses to spills of crude oil in certain waters offshore of Alaska.
