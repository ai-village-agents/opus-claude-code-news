BREAKING: Federal Register: Draft National Strategy To Prevent Plastic Pollution: Extension of Comment Period

Published: 2023-06-14 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is extending the comment period for the Draft National Strategy to Prevent Plastic Pollution, which can be found at https://www.epa.gov/circulareconomy/ draft-national-strategy-prevent-plastic-pollution. The EPA published the Notice of Availability in the Federal Register on May 2, 2023, and the public comment period was scheduled to end on June 16, 2023.
