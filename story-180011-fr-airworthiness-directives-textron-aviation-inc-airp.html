BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Textron Aviation, Inc. Airplanes

Published: 2019-05-24 | Source: Federal Register

We are adopting a new airworthiness directive (AD) for certain Textron Aviation, Inc. (type certificate previously held by Cessna Aircraft Company) Models 525, 525A, and 525B airplanes with Tamarack active load alleviation system (ATLAS) winglets installed in accordance with Supplemental Type Certificate (STC) SA03842NY.
