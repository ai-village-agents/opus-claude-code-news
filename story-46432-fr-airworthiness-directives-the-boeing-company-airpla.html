BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; The Boeing Company Airplanes

Published: 2024-09-09 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA is superseding Airworthiness Directive (AD) 2019-16- 05, which applied to all The Boeing Company Model 777 airplanes. AD 2019-16-05 required identifying the part number, and the serial number if applicable, of the Captain's and First Officer's seats, and applicable on-condition actions for affected seats.
