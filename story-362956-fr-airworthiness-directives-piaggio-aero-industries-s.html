BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; PIAGGIO AERO INDUSTRIES S.p.A Airplanes

Published: 2013-08-15 | Source: Federal Register

We are rescinding an airworthiness directive (AD) for PIAGGIO AERO INDUSTRIES S.p.A. Model P-180 airplanes. The rescinded AD resulted from mandatory continuing airworthiness information (MCAI) originated by an aviation authority of another country to identify and correct an unsafe condition on an aviation product.
