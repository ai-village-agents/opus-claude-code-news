BREAKING: Federal Register: Administration of Multiemployer Plan Participant Vote on an Approved Suspension of Benefits Under MPRA

Published: 2015-09-02 | Source: Federal Register

The Multiemployer Pension Reform Act of 2014 (MPRA) pertains to multiemployer plans that are projected to have insufficient funds, at some point in the future, to pay the full plan benefits to which individuals will be entitled (referred to as plans in "critical and declining status"). The sponsor of such a plan is permitted to reduce the pension benefits payable to plan participants and beneficiaries if certain conditions are satisfied (referred to as a "suspension of benefits").
