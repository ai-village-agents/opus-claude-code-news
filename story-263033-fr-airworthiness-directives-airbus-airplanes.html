BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Airbus Airplanes

Published: 2016-05-09 | Source: Federal Register

We are adopting a new airworthiness directive (AD) for certain Airbus Model A330-200, -200 Freighter, and -300 series airplanes; and Model A340-200 and -300 series airplanes. This AD was prompted by reports that cracks were found on an adjacent hole of certain frames of the center wing box (CWB).
