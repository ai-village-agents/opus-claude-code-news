BREAKING: Federal Register: Rescheduling of Meeting of the United States Travel and Tourism Advisory Board

Published: 2021-02-01 | Source: Federal Register

The International Trade Administration is announcing a new date/time for the United States Travel and Tourism Advisory Board (Board or TTAB) meeting; the meeting is now scheduled for February 26, 2021. Please note that this meeting was originally scheduled for Wednesday, February 10, 2021, notice of which was published in the Federal Register on January 21, 2021.
