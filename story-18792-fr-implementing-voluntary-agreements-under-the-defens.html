BREAKING: Federal Register: Implementing Voluntary Agreements Under the Defense Production Act

Published: 2025-08-25 | Source: Federal Register

This interim final rule codifies standards and procedures the Department of Energy will follow when developing and carrying out voluntary agreements and plans of action under the Defense Production Act. The Defense Production Act provides a defense from antitrust laws with respect to any action taken to develop or carry out any voluntary agreement or plan of action when certain criteria are met.
