BREAKING: Federal Register: Certain Microperforated Packaging Containing Fresh Produce; Notice of Correction Concerning Notice of Institution of Investigation

Published: 2018-01-30 | Source: Federal Register

Correction is made to notice 83 FR 3020, which was published on January 22, 2018, to clarify that the patent claims identified in paragraph 1 of the notice (claims 1-6, 11, and 13), refer to claims 1- 6, 11, and 13 of U.S. Patent No. 7,083,837.
