BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Funding Opportunity for the Empowering Rural America (New ERA) Program

Published: 2023-05-16 | Source: Federal Register

The Rural Utilities Service (RUS or the Agency), a Rural Development (RD) agency of the United States Department of Agriculture (USDA), is soliciting Letters of Interest (LOI) for applications under the Empowering Rural America (New ERA) Program. In addition, the Agency is announcing the eligibility requirements, application process and deadlines, and the criteria that will be used by RUS to assess New ERA Applications.
