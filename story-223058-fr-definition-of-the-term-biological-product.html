BREAKING: Federal Register: Definition of the Term “Biological Product”

Published: 2018-12-12 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA or the Agency) is proposing to amend its regulation that defines "biological product" to incorporate changes made by the Biologics Price Competition and Innovation Act of 2009 (BPCI Act), and to provide its interpretation of the statutory terms "protein" and "chemically synthesized polypeptide."
