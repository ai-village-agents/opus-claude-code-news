BREAKING: Federal Register: Request for Statements of Interest Regarding the WAPA Transmission System in the Area of Boulder City, Nevada

Published: 2022-08-23 | Source: Federal Register

Western Area Power Administration (WAPA), a Federal power marketing administration of the Department of Energy (DOE), is evaluating options that could reduce congestion on its transmission system and facilitate the interconnection and/or transmittal of energy, including renewable generation, in southern Nevada.
