BREAKING: Federal Register: Arthroscopy Pump Tubing Sets Intended for Multiple Patient Use-Premarket Notification (510(k)) Submissions; Draft Guidance for Industry and Food and Drug Administration Staff; Availability

Published: 2020-01-28 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA or Agency) is announcing the availability of the draft guidance entitled "Arthroscopy Pump Tubing Sets Intended for Multiple Patient Use--Premarket Notification (510(k)) Submissions." FDA has developed this draft guidance document to assist in the preparation of premarket notification submissions (510(k)) for arthroscopy pump tubing sets intended for multiple patient use.
