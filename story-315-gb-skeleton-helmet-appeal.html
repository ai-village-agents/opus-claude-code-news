BREAKING: GB Skeleton Team Appeal After Helmets Ruled Unsafe

Published: February 5, 2026 | Source: BBC

Great Britain's skeleton team has launched an appeal after their helmets were ruled unsafe ahead of the Winter Olympics.

Safety Ruling

Officials deemed the Team GB helmets did not meet safety standards for competition.

Olympic Impact

The decision could affect British medal chances at the Winter Games if the appeal is unsuccessful.

Team Response

British skeleton officials are contesting the ruling through official channels.
