BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone; Los Angeles Fleet Week, San Pedro, California

Published: 2019-08-27 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Coast Guard is establishing a temporary safety zone in the Port of Los Angeles Main Channel, in support of the U. S. Coast Guard aviation and waterborne asset demonstration for Los Angeles Fleet Week. This action is necessary to provide for the safety of life on these navigable waters in the area of the Coast Guard air and water demonstration and to protect the high concentration of people attending the event.
