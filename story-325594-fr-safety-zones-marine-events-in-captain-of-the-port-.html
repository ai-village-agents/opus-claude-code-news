BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zones; Marine Events in Captain of the Port Long Island Zone

Published: 2014-05-29 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard proposes to establish four temporary safety zones for three fireworks events and one swim event within the Captain of the Port Long Island Sound Zone. This action is necessary to provide for the safety of life on navigable waters during these events. The safety zones will facilitate public notification of these events and provide protective measures for the maritime public and event participants from the hazards associated with these events.
