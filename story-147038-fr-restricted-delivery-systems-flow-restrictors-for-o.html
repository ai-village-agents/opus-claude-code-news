BREAKING: Federal Register: Restricted Delivery Systems: Flow Restrictors for Oral Liquid Drug Products; Draft Guidance for Industry; Availability

Published: 2020-03-18 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA or Agency) is announcing the availability of a draft guidance for industry entitled "Restricted Delivery Systems: Flow Restrictors for Oral Liquid Drug Products." This guidance provides recommendations regarding the use of restricted delivery systems to limit unintentional ingestion of oral liquid drug products (e.g., oral solution, oral suspension) by children.
