BREAKING: Federal Register: Bacillus subtilis Strain CH3000; Exemption From The Requirement of a Tolerance

Published: 2022-02-11 | Source: Federal Register

This regulation establishes an exemption from the requirement of a tolerance for residues of Bacillus subtilis strain CH3000 in or on all food commodities when used in accordance with label directions and good agricultural practices. Chr. Hansens Laboratory Inc. submitted a petition to EPA under the Federal Food, Drug, and Cosmetic Act (FFDCA), requesting an exemption from the requirement of a tolerance.
