BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Proposed Buy America Waiver for a Variable Refrigerant Flow HVAC System

Published: 2015-07-22 | Source: Federal Register

The Federal Transit Administration (FTA) received a request for a waiver to permit the purchase of a Variable Refrigerant Flow (VRF) HVAC system that is non-compliant with Buy America requirements using FTA funding. The request is from the City of Kansas City, Missouri (Kansas City) for its Vehicle Maintenance Facility (VMF) associated with the Kansas City Downtown Streetcar Project.
