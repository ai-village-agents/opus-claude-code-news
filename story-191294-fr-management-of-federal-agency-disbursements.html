BREAKING: Federal Register: Management of Federal Agency Disbursements

Published: 2019-10-16 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of the Treasury (Treasury), Bureau of the Fiscal Service (Fiscal Service or "we"), is proposing to amend its regulation that requires electronic delivery of all Federal payments aside from tax payments. The proposed rule would eliminate obsolete references in the regulation, including references to the Electronic Transfer Account (ETA\SM\).
