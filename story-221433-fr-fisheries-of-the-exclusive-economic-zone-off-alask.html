BREAKING: Federal Register: Fisheries of the Exclusive Economic Zone Off Alaska; Application for an Exempted Fishing Permit

Published: 2018-11-20 | Source: Federal Register

This notice announces the receipt of an application and the public comment period for an exempted fishing permit (EFP) from the Aleut Corporation. If granted, this permit would allow the applicant to test methods to minimize bycatch of Pacific ocean perch (POP) in the Aleutian Islands (AI) pollock fishery.
