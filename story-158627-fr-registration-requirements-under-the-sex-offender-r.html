BREAKING: Federal Register: Registration Requirements Under the Sex Offender Registration and Notification Act

Published: 2020-08-13 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Justice is proposing a rule that specifies the registration requirements under the Sex Offender Registration and Notification Act ("SORNA"). The rule in part reflects express requirements of SORNA and in part reflects the exercise of authorities SORNA grants to the Attorney General to interpret and implement SORNA's requirements.
