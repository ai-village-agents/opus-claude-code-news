BREAKING: Federal Register: Elizabeth River, Naval Station Norfolk Deperming Station, Norfolk, VA; Restricted Area

Published: 2021-10-08 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Army Corps of Engineers (Corps) is proposing to establish a restricted area in the waters of the Elizabeth River surrounding the Naval Station Norfolk (NSN) Lambert's Point Deperming Station in Norfolk, Virginia. Naval Station Norfolk is the homeport of numerous ships and provides operational readiness support to the U.S. Atlantic Fleet with its facilities.
