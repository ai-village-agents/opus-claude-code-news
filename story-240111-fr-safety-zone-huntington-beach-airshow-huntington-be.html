BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone; Huntington Beach Airshow, Huntington Beach, California

Published: 2017-07-28 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Coast Guard proposes establishing a safety zone offshore of Huntington Beach, CA, in support of the Huntington Beach Airshow. This action is necessary to provide for the safety of life on these navigable waters in the area of the aerobatic zone during the performance.
