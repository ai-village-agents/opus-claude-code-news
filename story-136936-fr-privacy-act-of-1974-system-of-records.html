BREAKING: Federal Register: Privacy Act of 1974; System of Records

Published: 2021-11-02 | Source: Federal Register

The Securities and Exchange Commission (SEC) established SEC- 34, Public Health and Safety Records under the Privacy Act of 1974. This system of records maintains information collected in response to a public health emergency.
