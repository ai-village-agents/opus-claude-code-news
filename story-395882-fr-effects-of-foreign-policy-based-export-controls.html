BREAKING: Federal Register: Effects of Foreign Policy-Based Export Controls

Published: 2012-09-07 | Source: Federal Register

In developing its report to Congress, BIS is seeking public comments on the effect of existing foreign policy-based export controls in the Export Administration Regulations. BIS is requesting public comments to conduct consultations with U.S. industries. Section 6 of the Export Administration Act (EAA) requires BIS to consult with industry on the effect of such controls and to report the results of the consultations to Congress.
