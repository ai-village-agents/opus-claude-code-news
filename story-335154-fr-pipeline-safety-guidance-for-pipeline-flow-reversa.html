BREAKING: Federal Register: Pipeline Safety: Guidance for Pipeline Flow Reversals, Product Changes and Conversion to Service

Published: 2014-09-18 | Source: Federal Register

PHMSA is issuing this advisory bulletin to alert operators of hazardous liquid and gas transmission pipelines of the potential significant impact flow reversals, product changes and conversion to service may have on the integrity of a pipeline. Failures on natural gas transmission and hazardous liquid pipelines have occurred after these operational changes.
