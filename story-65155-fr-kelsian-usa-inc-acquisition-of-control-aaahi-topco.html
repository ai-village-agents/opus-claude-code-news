BREAKING: Federal Register: Kelsian USA Inc.-Acquisition of Control-AAAHI Topco Corporation

Published: 2023-04-14 | Source: Federal Register

On March 15, 2023, Kelsian USA Inc., (Kelsian USA), a noncarrier, filed an application to acquire from AAAHI Holdings LLC (Seller), a noncarrier, the motor carrier assets and direct control of AAAHI Topco Corporation (Topco).
