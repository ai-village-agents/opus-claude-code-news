BREAKING: Federal Register: Xanthan Gum From the People's Republic of China: Preliminary Determination of Sales at Less Than Fair Value and Postponement of Final Determination

Published: 2013-01-10 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Commerce ("the Department") preliminarily determines that xanthan gum from the People's Republic of China ("PRC") is being, or is likely to be, sold in the United States at less than fair value ("LTFV"), as provided in section 733 of the Tariff Act of 1930, as amended ("the Act"). The weighted-average dumping margins are shown in the "Preliminary Determination" section of this notice.
