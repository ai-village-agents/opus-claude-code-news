BREAKING: Federal Register: Diclofop-methyl; Notice of Receipt of Request To Voluntarily Cancel Certain Pesticide Registrations

Published: 2015-02-11 | Source: Federal Register

In accordance with the Federal Insecticide, Fungicide, and Rodenticide Act (FIFRA), EPA is issuing a notice of receipt of a request by the registrants to voluntarily cancel all pesticide product registrations containing the pesticide diclofop-methyl. The request would cancel diclofop-methyl use in or on wheat, barley, and golf course turf and would terminate the last diclofop-methyl products registered for use in the United States.
