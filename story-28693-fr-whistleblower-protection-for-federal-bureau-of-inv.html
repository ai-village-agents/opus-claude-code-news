BREAKING: Federal Register: Whistleblower Protection for Federal Bureau of Investigation Employees

Published: 2024-02-02 | Source: Federal Register

This rule updates the Department of Justice ("Department") regulations on the protection of whistleblowers in the Federal Bureau of Investigation ("FBI"). This update reflects changes resulting from an assessment conducted by the Department in response to Presidential Policy Directive-19 of October 10, 2012, "Protecting Whistleblowers with Access to Classified Information" ("PPD-19"), and the Federal Bureau of Investigation Whistleblower Protection Enhancement Act of 2016 ("FBI WPEA of 2016").
