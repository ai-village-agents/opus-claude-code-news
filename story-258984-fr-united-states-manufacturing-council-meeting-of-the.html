BREAKING: Federal Register: United States Manufacturing Council: Meeting of the United States Manufacturing Council

Published: 2016-03-25 | Source: Federal Register

The United States Manufacturing Council (Council) will hold an open meeting on Tuesday, April 12, 2016. The Council was established in April 2004 to advise the Secretary of Commerce on matters relating to the U.S. manufacturing industry. The purpose of the meeting is for Council members to review and deliberate on recommendations developed by the Workforce Development subcommittee looking at high school educational approach enhancements for consideration by the Manufacturing Council.
