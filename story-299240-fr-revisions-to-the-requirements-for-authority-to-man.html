BREAKING: Federal Register: Revisions to the Requirements for Authority To Manufacture and Distribute Postage Evidencing Systems

Published: 2015-07-17 | Source: Federal Register

The Postal Service is revising the rules concerning authorization to manufacture and distribute postage evidencing systems to reflect new revenue assurance practices.
