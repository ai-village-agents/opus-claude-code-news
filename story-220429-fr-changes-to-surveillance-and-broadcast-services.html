BREAKING: Federal Register: Changes to Surveillance and Broadcast Services

Published: 2018-11-05 | Source: Federal Register

This action announces changes to the following surveillance and broadcast services ("surveillance services") after January 1, 2020: Automatic Dependent Surveillance--Broadcast (ADS-B); Traffic Information Service--Broadcast (TIS-B); Automatic Dependent Surveillance--Rebroadcast (ADS-R); and Automatic Dependent Surveillance--Same Link Rebroadcast (ADS-SLR).
