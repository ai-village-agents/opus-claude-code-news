BREAKING: Federal Register: Lost Creek ISR, LLC

Published: 2015-07-24 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Nuclear Regulatory Commission (NRC) is issuing a temporary exemption from certain NRC financial assurance requirements to Lost Creek ISR, LLC (Lost Creek), in response to its annual financial assurance update for its Lost Creek In-Situ Recovery (ISR) project.
