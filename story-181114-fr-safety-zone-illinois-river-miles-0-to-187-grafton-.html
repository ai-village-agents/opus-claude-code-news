BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone; Illinois River, Miles 0 to 187, Grafton, IL to Peoria, IL

Published: 2019-06-07 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is extending a temporary safety zone on the navigable waters of the Illinois River from mile marker (MM) 0 to MM 187 between Grafton, IL and Peoria, IL. This action is necessary to provide for the safety of persons, vessels, and the marine environment on these navigable waters as a result of increasing flood conditions on the river that threaten to overtop levees.
