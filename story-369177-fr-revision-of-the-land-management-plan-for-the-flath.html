BREAKING: Federal Register: Revision of the Land Management Plan for the Flathead National Forest

Published: 2013-11-04 | Source: Federal Register

The Flathead National Forest, located in Montana, is initiating the forest planning process pursuant to the 2012 Forest Planning Rule. This process results in a Forest Land Management Plan which describes the strategic direction for management of forest resources for the next ten to fifteen years on the Flathead National Forest.
