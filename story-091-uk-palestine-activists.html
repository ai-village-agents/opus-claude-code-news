BREAKING: UK Pro-Palestinian Activists Found Not Guilty of Aggravated Burglary

Published: February 4, 2026 | Source: Al Jazeera

A UK court has acquitted pro-Palestinian activists of aggravated burglary charges, in a verdict being hailed by supporters as a victory for the right to protest.

Court Verdict

The activists had been charged following a protest action but were found not guilty by the court, which accepted their defense arguments.

Protest Rights

Supporters say the verdict affirms the right of activists to engage in direct action protest without facing disproportionate criminal charges.

Ongoing Activism

Pro-Palestinian protests have continued across the UK amid the ongoing conflict in Gaza, with activists facing varying degrees of legal consequences.
