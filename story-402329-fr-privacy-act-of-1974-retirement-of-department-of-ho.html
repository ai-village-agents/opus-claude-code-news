BREAKING: Federal Register: Privacy Act of 1974; Retirement of Department of Homeland Security Transportation Security Administration System of Records

Published: 2012-11-27 | Source: Federal Register

In accordance with the Privacy Act of 1974, the Department of Homeland Security is giving notice that it will retire the following Privacy Act system of records notice, Department of Homeland Security/ Transportation Security Administration-009 General Legal Records (August 18, 2003, 68 FR 49496), which was written to assist attorneys in the Office of Chief Counsel in providing legal advice to management and to cover general legal records and programs.
