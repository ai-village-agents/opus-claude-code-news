BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Safran Helicopter Engines, S.A., Turboshaft Engines

Published: 2017-07-24 | Source: Federal Register

We are superseding airworthiness directive (AD) 2017-04-51 for all Safran Helicopter Engines, S.A., Arriel 1A1, 1A2, 1B, 1C, 1C1, 1C2, 1D, 1D1, 1E2, 1K1, 1S, and 1S1 turboshaft engines. AD 2017-04-51 required inspecting, wrapping, and replacing the affected drain valve assembly (DV) installed on these Arriel 1 engines.
