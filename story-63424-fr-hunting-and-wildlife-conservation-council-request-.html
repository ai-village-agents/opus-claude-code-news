BREAKING: Federal Register: Hunting and Wildlife Conservation Council; Request for Nominations

Published: 2023-03-24 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Department of the Interior and Department of Agriculture are seeking member nominations to the Hunting and Wildlife Conservation Council (Council) to fill one vacancy for a representative from a State fish and wildlife management agency.
