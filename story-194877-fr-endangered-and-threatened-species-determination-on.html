BREAKING: Federal Register: Endangered and Threatened Species; Determination on the Designation of Critical Habitat for Giant Manta Ray

Published: 2019-12-05 | Source: Federal Register

We, NMFS, have determined that a designation of critical habitat is not prudent at this time. Based on a comprehensive review of the best scientific data available, we find that there are no identifiable physical or biological features that are essential to the conservation of the giant manta ray within areas under U.S. jurisdiction.
