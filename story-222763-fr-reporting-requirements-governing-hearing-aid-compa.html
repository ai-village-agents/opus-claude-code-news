BREAKING: Federal Register: Reporting Requirements Governing Hearing Aid-Compatible Mobile Handsets

Published: 2018-12-07 | Source: Federal Register

In this document, the Federal Communications Commission ("Commission" or "FCC") revises its rules to require service providers to post on their publicly accessible websites information regarding the hearing aid compatibility of their offered handsets. Service providers are also required to retain information regarding the hearing aid compatibility of handsets previously offered.
