BREAKING: Federal Register: Phasedown of Hydrofluorocarbons: Review and Renewal of Eligibility for Application-Specific Allowances

Published: 2025-08-26 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is finalizing, pursuant to the statutory framework established in the American Innovation and Manufacturing Act of 2020 (AIM Act), the eligibility of six applications to continue to receive priority access to allowances to produce or import hydrofluorocarbons.
