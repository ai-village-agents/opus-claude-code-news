BREAKING: Federal Register: Method for the Determination of Lead in Total Suspended Particulate Matter

Published: 2013-07-03 | Source: Federal Register

The EPA is establishing a new Federal Reference Method (FRM) for measuring Lead (Pb) in total suspended particulate matter (TSP) collected from ambient air. This method is intended for use by analytical laboratories performing the analysis of Pb in TSP to support data collection for the Pb National Ambient Air Quality Standard (NAAQS).
