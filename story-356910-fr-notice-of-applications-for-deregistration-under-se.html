BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Applications for Deregistration Under Section 8(f) of the Investment Company Act of 1940

Published: 2013-06-07 | Source: Federal Register

Each applicant seeks an order declaring that it has ceased to be an investment company. The applicants transferred their assets to Oppenheimer Global Strategic Income Fund and, on September 21, 2012, and October 5, 2012, respectively, made final distributions to their shareholders based on net asset value.
