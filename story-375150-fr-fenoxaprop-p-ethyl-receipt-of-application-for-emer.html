BREAKING: Federal Register: Fenoxaprop-p-ethyl; Receipt of Application for Emergency Exemption, Solicitation of Public Comment

Published: 2012-01-18 | Source: Federal Register

EPA has received a specific exemption request from the Oregon Department of Agriculture to use the pesticide fenoxaprop-p-ethyl (CAS No. 148-79-8) to treat up to 40,000 acres of grasses grown for seed to control weeds. The applicant proposes a use which is supported by the Interregional (IR)-4 program and has been requested in 5 or more previous years, and a petition for tolerance has not yet been submitted to the Agency.
