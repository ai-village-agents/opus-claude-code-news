BREAKING: Federal Register: Establishment of the Electronic Visa Update System (EVUS)

Published: 2016-10-20 | Source: Federal Register

This rule amends the Department of Homeland Security's regulations to establish the Electronic Visa Update System ("EVUS"). This system will allow for the collection of biographic and other information from nonimmigrant aliens who hold a passport issued by an identified country containing a U.S. nonimmigrant visa of a designated category.
