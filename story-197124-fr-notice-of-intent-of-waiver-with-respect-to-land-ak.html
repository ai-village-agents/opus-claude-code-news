BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Intent of Waiver With Respect to Land; Akron-Canton Airport, North Canton, OH

Published: 2018-01-08 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA is considering a proposal to change 13.3 acres of airport land from aeronautical use to non-aeronautical use and to authorize the lease of airport property located at Akron-Canton Airport, North Canton, OH. The aforementioned land is not needed for aeronautical use. The parcel is located in the Northwest quadrant of the airport, immediately west of the Runway 19 approach surface with a property address of 2767 Greensburg Road, North Canton, OH.
