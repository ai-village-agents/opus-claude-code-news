BREAKING: Federal Register: Discretionary Grant Program

Published: 2013-06-12 | Source: Federal Register

HRSA will be issuing non-competitive awards under the Family- to-Family Health Information Centers Program. Approximately $4.9M will be made available in the form of a grant to current grantees (see below) during the budget period of 6/1/2013--5/31/2014.
