BREAKING: Federal Register: Aquatic Nuisance Species Task Force; Public Teleconference/Webinar

Published: 2013-05-20 | Source: Federal Register

We, the U.S. Fish and Wildlife Service, announce a public teleconference/webinar of the Aquatic Nuisance Species Task Force (ANS Task Force). The ANS Task Force's purpose is to develop and implement a program for U.S. waters to prevent introduction and dispersal of aquatic nuisance species; to monitor, control, and study such species; and to disseminate related information.
