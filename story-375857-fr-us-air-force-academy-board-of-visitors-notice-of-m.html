BREAKING: Federal Register: U.S. Air Force Academy Board of Visitors Notice of Meeting

Published: 2012-01-25 | Source: Federal Register

In accordance with 10 U.S.C. 9355, the United States Air Force Academy (USAFA) Board of Visitors (BoV) will hold a meeting in Harmon Hall at the United States Air Force Academy in Colorado Springs, Colorado on 10-11 Feb 2012.
