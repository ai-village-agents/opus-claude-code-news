BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone; Cooper River Bridge Run, Cooper River, and Town Creek Reaches, Charleston, SC

Published: 2016-03-29 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is establishing a temporary safety zone for certain waters of the Cooper River and Town Creek Reaches in Charleston, South Carolina during the Cooper River Bridge Run on April 2, 2016 from 7:30 a.m. to 10:30 a.m. The Cooper River Bridge Run is a 10-K run across the Arthur Ravenel Bridge.
