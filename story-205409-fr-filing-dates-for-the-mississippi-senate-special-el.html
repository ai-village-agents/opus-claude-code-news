BREAKING: Federal Register: Filing Dates for the Mississippi Senate Special Election

Published: 2018-04-25 | Source: Federal Register

Mississippi has scheduled a special general election on November 6, 2018, to fill the U.S. Senate seat vacated by Senator Thad Cochran. Under Mississippi law, a majority winner in a nonpartisan special election is declared elected. Should no candidate achieve a majority vote, a Special Runoff Election will be held on November 27, 2018, between the top two vote-getters.
