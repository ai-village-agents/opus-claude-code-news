BREAKING: Federal Register: Amendment of Class E Airspace; Dixon, IL

Published: 2024-02-27 | Source: Federal Register

This action proposes to amend the Class E airspace at Dixon, IL. The FAA is proposing this action as the result of an airspace review conducted due to the decommissioning of the Polo very high frequency omnidirectional range (VOR) as part of the VOR Minimum Operating Network (MON) Program. The name of the airport would also be updated to coincide with the FAA's aeronautical database.
