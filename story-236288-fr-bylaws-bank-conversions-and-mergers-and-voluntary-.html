BREAKING: Federal Register: Bylaws; Bank Conversions and Mergers; and Voluntary Mergers of Federally Insured Credit Unions

Published: 2017-06-08 | Source: Federal Register

The NCUA Board (Board) proposes to revise the procedures a federal credit union (FCU) must follow to merge voluntarily with another credit union. The proposed changes: Revise and clarify the contents and format of the member notice; require merging FCUs to disclose all merger-related financial arrangements for covered persons; increase the minimum member notice period; and provide procedures to allow reasonable member-to-member communications regarding the proposed merger.
