BREAKING: Federal Register: Energy Conservation Program: Test Procedures for Consumer Products; Early Assessment Review: Dehumidifiers

Published: 2021-06-30 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Department of Energy ("DOE") is undertaking an early assessment review to determine whether to proceed with a rulemaking to amend the test procedure for dehumidifiers. Through this request for information ("RFI"), DOE seeks data and information regarding issues pertinent to whether an amended test procedure would more accurately or fully comply with the requirement that the test procedure produces results that measure energy use during a representative average use cycle for the product ...
