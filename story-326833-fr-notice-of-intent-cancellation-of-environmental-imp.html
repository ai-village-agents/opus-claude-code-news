BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Intent Cancellation of Environmental Impact Statement on the Proposal To Relocate the 18th Aggressor Squadron From Eielson Air Force Base, Alaska to Joint Base Elmendorf-Richardson, Alaska

Published: 2014-06-12 | Source: Federal Register

The Air Force is issuing this notice to advise the public that per direction of the Secretary of the Air Force, the Air Force is cancelling the preparation of an Environmental Impact Statement under the National Environmental Policy Act on its proposal to relocate the 18th Aggressor Squadron from Eielson AFB, Alaska to Joint Base Elmendorf-Richardson (JBER), Alaska, and for the Air Force to adjust the size of the remaining base operating support functions at Eielson.
