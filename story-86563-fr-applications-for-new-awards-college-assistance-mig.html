BREAKING: Federal Register: Applications for New Awards; College Assistance Migrant Program

Published: 2022-01-18 | Source: Federal Register

On December 3, 2021, the Department of Education (Department) published in the Federal Register a notice inviting applications (NIA) for the fiscal year (FY) 2022 College Assistance Migrant Program (CAMP), Assistance Listing Number 84.149A. This notice clarifies that the deadline to submit applications for the FY 2022 competition is February 1, 2022.
