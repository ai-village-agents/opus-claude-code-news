BREAKING: Federal Register: Removal of Personally Identifiable Information From Registration Records

Published: 2017-02-02 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Copyright Office is issuing a final rule to allow authors and claimants to replace or remove personally identifiable information ("PII") from the Office's online registration catalog.
