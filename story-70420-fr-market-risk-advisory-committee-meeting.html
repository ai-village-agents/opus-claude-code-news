BREAKING: Federal Register: Market Risk Advisory Committee; Meeting

Published: 2023-06-22 | Source: Federal Register

The Commodity Futures Trading Commission (CFTC) announces that on July 10, 2023, from 10 a.m. to 1 p.m. (Eastern Daylight Time), the Market Risk Advisory Committee (MRAC or Committee) will hold a public meeting via teleconference.
