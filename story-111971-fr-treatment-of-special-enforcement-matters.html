BREAKING: Federal Register: Treatment of Special Enforcement Matters

Published: 2022-12-09 | Source: Federal Register

This document contains final regulations that except certain partnership-related items from the centralized partnership audit regime created by the Bipartisan Budget Act of 2015, and sets forth alternative rules that will apply to the examination of excepted items by the IRS. The centralized partnership audit regime does not apply to a partnership-related item if the item involves a special enforcement matter described in these regulations.
