BREAKING: Federal Register: Special Local Regulations; Marine Events in the Coast Guard Sector Detroit Captain of the Port Zone

Published: 2018-10-17 | Source: Federal Register

This proposed rule is intended to amend the rules that regulate vessel traffic and control navigation of portions of waterways during events that pose a hazard to public safety. This rule, if adopted, would add six new reoccurring special local regulations, remove six special local regulations, and amend the event, dates, and/ or regulated areas for the 15 recurring special local regulations that will be listed in a table.
