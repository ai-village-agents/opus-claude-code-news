BREAKING: Federal Register: Certain Memory Modules and Components Thereof; Notice of Request for Submissions on the Public Interest

Published: 2019-10-29 | Source: Federal Register

Notice is hereby given that the presiding administrative law judge ("ALJ") has issued a recommended determination on remedy and bonding should a violation be found in the above-captioned investigation. The Commission is soliciting submissions on public interest issues raised by the recommended limited exclusion order against certain memory modules and components thereof, manufactured and imported by respondents SK hynix, Inc.; SK hynix America, Inc.; and SK hynix memory solutions, Inc.
