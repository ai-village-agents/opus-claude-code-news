BREAKING: Federal Register: Change in Postal Prices

Published: 2014-01-06 | Source: Federal Register

The Commission is noticing a recent Postal Service filing concerning changes in prices for Global Expedited Package Service--Non- Published Rates. This notice informs the public of the filing, invites public comment, and takes other administrative steps.
