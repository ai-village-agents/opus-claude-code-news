BREAKING: Federal Register: Actuarial Assumptions for Determining an Employer's Withdrawal Liability

Published: 2022-11-10 | Source: Federal Register

The Pension Benefit Guaranty Corporation (PBGC) is extending the comment period for a proposed rule that would provide interest rate assumptions that may be used by a plan actuary in determining a withdrawing employer's liability under a multiemployer plan. PBGC published the proposed rule in the Federal Register on October 14, 2022, with a comment period that was scheduled to end on November 14, 2022.
