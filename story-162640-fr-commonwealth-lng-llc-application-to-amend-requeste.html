BREAKING: Federal Register: Commonwealth LNG, LLC; Application To Amend Requested Export Term in Pending Long-Term Application Through December 31, 2050

Published: 2020-10-02 | Source: Federal Register

The Office of Fossil Energy (FE) of the Department of Energy (DOE) gives notice (Notice) of receipt of an application (Application), filed on September 11, 2020, by Commonwealth LNG, LLC (Commonwealth). Commonwealth seeks to amend the export term set forth in its pending application requesting authorization to export liquefied natural gas (LNG) to non-free trade agreement countries to a term ending on December 31, 2050.
