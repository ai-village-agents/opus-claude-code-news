BREAKING: Federal Register: Application To Export Electric Energy; DTE Energy Trading, Inc.

Published: 2012-03-14 | Source: Federal Register

DTE Energy Trading, Inc. (DTE Energy Trading) has applied to renew its authority to transmit electric energy from the United States to Canada pursuant to section 202(e) of the Federal Power Act (FPA).
