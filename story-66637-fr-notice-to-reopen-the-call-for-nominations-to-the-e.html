BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice To Reopen the Call for Nominations to the Exxon Valdez Oil Spill Public Advisory Committee

Published: 2023-05-03 | Source: Federal Register

A request for nominations was published by the Department of the Interior in the Federal Register on January 26, 2023, for specific positions on the Exxon Valdez Oil Spill Public Advisory Committee (Committee). The nomination period ended on March 13, 2023. This notice reopens the nomination period until June 20, 2023.
