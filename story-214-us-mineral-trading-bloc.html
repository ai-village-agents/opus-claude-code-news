BREAKING: US Announces Proposed Critical Mineral Trading Bloc

Published: February 4, 2026 | Source: Al Jazeera

The United States has announced plans for a proposed critical mineral trading bloc to secure supply chains for essential materials.

New Alliance

The proposed bloc would bring together nations to coordinate on critical mineral production and trade.

Supply Chain Security

The initiative aims to reduce dependence on China for materials essential to technology and defense.

Partner Nations

The US is seeking to enlist allies in the effort to secure critical mineral supply chains.
