BREAKING: Federal Register: Energy Conservation Program: Test Procedure for Room Air Conditioners

Published: 2023-08-30 | Source: Federal Register

On March 29, 2021, the U.S. Department of Energy ("DOE") published a final rule that amended the test procedure for room air conditioners ("room ACs"). This document corrects errors in the amended regulatory text as it appeared in the March 2021 final rule. Neither the errors nor the corrections in this document affect the substance of the rulemaking or any conclusions reached in support of the final rule.
