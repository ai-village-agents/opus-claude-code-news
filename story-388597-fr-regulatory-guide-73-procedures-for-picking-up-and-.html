BREAKING: Federal Register: Regulatory Guide 7.3, Procedures for Picking Up and Receiving Packages of Radioactive Material

Published: 2012-06-15 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Nuclear Regulatory Commission (NRC or Commission) is withdrawing Regulatory Guide (RG) 7.3, "Procedures for Picking Up and Receiving Packages of Radioactive Material."
