BREAKING: Federal Register: Acquisition Process: Task and Delivery Order Contracts, Bundling, Consolidation

Published: 2013-10-02 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Small Business Administration (SBA) is amending its regulations governing small business contracting procedures. Specifically, this rule amends SBA's regulations to establish policies and procedures for setting aside, partially setting aside and reserving Multiple Award Contracts for small business concerns.
