BREAKING: Federal Register: 21st Century U.S. Port Competitiveness Initiative: Request for Public Comment

Published: 2016-05-27 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Department of Commerce (Department) is seeking public input on U.S. seaport efficiency and competitiveness issues for its 21st Century U.S. Port Competitiveness Initiative.
