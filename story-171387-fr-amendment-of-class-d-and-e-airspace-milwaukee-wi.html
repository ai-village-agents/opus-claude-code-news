BREAKING: Federal Register: Amendment of Class D and E Airspace; Milwaukee, WI

Published: 2019-02-11 | Source: Federal Register

This action modifies Class D airspace and Class E airspace extending upward from 700 feet above the surface at Lawrence J. Timmerman Airport, Milwaukee, WI, due to the decommissioning of the Timmerman VHF omnidirectional range (VOR) navigation aid, which provided navigation information for the instrument procedures at this airport, as part of the VOR Minimum Operational Network (MON) Program.
