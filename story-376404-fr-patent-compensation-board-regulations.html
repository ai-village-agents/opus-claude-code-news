BREAKING: Federal Register: Patent Compensation Board Regulations

Published: 2012-02-01 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Energy (DOE) today is amending its Patent Compensation Board regulations to provide that the Secretary of Energy, or a person acting in that position, shall appoint, as needed, a three member panel to serve as the Patent Compensation Board to hear and decide cases falling under the subject matter jurisdiction of the Board.
