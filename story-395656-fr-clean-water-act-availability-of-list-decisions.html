BREAKING: Federal Register: Clean Water Act: Availability of List Decisions

Published: 2012-09-06 | Source: Federal Register

This notice announces EPA's decision to identify certain water quality limited waters and the associated pollutant to be listed pursuant to the Clean Water Act Section 303(d)(2) on New York's list of impaired waters, and requests public comment.
