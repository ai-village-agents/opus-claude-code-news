BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Adjustment of Minimum Project Worksheet Amount

Published: 2023-10-20 | Source: Federal Register

FEMA gives notice that the minimum Project Worksheet Amount under the Public Assistance program for disasters and emergencies declared on or after October 1, 2023, will be increased.
