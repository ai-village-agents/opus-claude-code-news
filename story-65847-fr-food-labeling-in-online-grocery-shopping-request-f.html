BREAKING: Federal Register: Food Labeling in Online Grocery Shopping; Request for Information

Published: 2023-04-24 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA or we) is requesting information to help empower consumers with accurate, informative, and accessible food labeling. The purpose of this request is to obtain current information on the content, format, and accuracy of food label information that is presented to consumers through online grocery shopping platforms.
