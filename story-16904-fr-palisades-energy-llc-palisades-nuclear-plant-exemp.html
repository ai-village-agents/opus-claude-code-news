BREAKING: Federal Register: Palisades Energy, LLC; Palisades Nuclear Plant; Exemption

Published: 2025-07-29 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Nuclear Regulatory Commission (NRC) is issuing an exemption in response to a September 28, 2023, request from Holtec Decommissioning International, LLC (HDI), on behalf of Holtec Palisades, LLC.
