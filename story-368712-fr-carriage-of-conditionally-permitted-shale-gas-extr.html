BREAKING: Federal Register: Carriage of Conditionally Permitted Shale Gas Extraction Waste Water in Bulk

Published: 2013-10-30 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard announces the availability of a proposed policy letter concerning the carriage of shale gas extraction waste water in bulk via barge, and invites public comment. The policy letter specifies the conditions under which a barge owner may request and be granted a Certificate of Inspection endorsement or letter allowing the barge to transport shale gas extraction waste water in bulk.
