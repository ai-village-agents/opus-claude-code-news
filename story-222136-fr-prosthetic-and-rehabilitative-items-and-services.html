BREAKING: Federal Register: Prosthetic and Rehabilitative Items and Services

Published: 2018-11-28 | Source: Federal Register

On October 16, 2017, the Department of Veterans Affairs published a proposed rulemaking to amend its regulations on the provision of prosthetic and rehabilitative items and services. This supplemental notice of proposed rulemaking (SNPRM) provides clarification about provisions of that proposed rulemaking and seeks additional public comments on them.
