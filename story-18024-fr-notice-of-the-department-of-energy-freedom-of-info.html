BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of the Department of Energy Freedom of Information Act (FOIA) “Still Interested” Inquiry

Published: 2025-08-14 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Energy Headquarters (DOE HQ) is providing this notice regarding its efforts to increase efficiency, reduce the backlog of Freedom of Information Act (FOIA) requests, and assist those requesters still interested in with processing FOIA requests. This notice applies to requesters that are still interested in having DOE HQ process FOIA request(s) filed prior to Fiscal Year (FY) 2025 (i.e., any FOIA request submitted to DOE HQ prior to October 1, 2024).
