BREAKING: Federal Register: Special Local Regulation; Marine Events Within the Eleventh Coast Guard District-San Diego Bayfair

Published: 2025-09-10 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard will enforce the San Diego Bayfair special local regulation on the waters of Mission Bay, California, from September 12, 2025, through September 14, 2025. This special local regulation is necessary to provide for the safety of the participants, crew, spectators, sponsor vessels, and general users of the waterway.
