BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Embraer S.A. Airplanes

Published: 2023-08-14 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA proposes to adopt a new airworthiness directive (AD) for certain Embraer S.A. (Embraer) Model EMB-505 airplanes. This proposed AD was prompted by occurrences of an uncommanded change in the setting of the barometric pressure in both primary flight displays (PFDs).
