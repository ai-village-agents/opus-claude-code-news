BREAKING: Federal Register: Proposed Expansion of R-3803 Restricted Area Complex; Fort Polk, LA

Published: 2018-11-26 | Source: Federal Register

This action proposes to expand the R-3803 restricted area complex in central Louisiana by establishing four new restricted areas, R-3803C, R-3803D, R-3803E, and R-3803F, and make minor technical amendments to the existing R-3803A and R-3803B legal descriptions for improved operational efficiency and administrative standardization.
