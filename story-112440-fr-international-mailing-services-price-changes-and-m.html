BREAKING: Federal Register: International Mailing Services: Price Changes and Minor Classification Changes

Published: 2022-12-16 | Source: Federal Register

On October 7, 2022, the Postal Service published notice of price adjustments and minor classification changes with the Postal Regulatory Commission (PRC). The Postal Regulatory Commission (PRC) concluded that price adjustments and classification changes contained in the Postal Service's notification may go into effect on January 22, 2023.
