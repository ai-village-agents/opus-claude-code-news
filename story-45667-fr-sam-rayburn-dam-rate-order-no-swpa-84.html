BREAKING: Federal Register: Sam Rayburn Dam-Rate Order No. SWPA-84

Published: 2024-08-30 | Source: Federal Register

The Administrator, Southwestern Power Administration (Southwestern), has confirmed, approved and placed into effect on an interim basis Rate Order No. SWPA-84 (Rate Order), which provides the rate schedule Wholesale Rates for Hydro Power and Energy at Sam Rayburn Dam and Reservoir (SRD-23). This new rate schedule for the Sam Rayburn Dam and Reservoir (Rayburn) replaces the existing power rate under Rate Schedule SRD-15 which expires on September 30, 2024.
