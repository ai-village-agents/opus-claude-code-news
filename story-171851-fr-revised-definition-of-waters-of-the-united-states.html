BREAKING: Federal Register: Revised Definition of “Waters of the United States”

Published: 2019-02-14 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency and the Department of the Army ("the agencies") are publishing for public comment a proposed rule defining the scope of waters federally regulated under the Clean Water Act (CWA).
