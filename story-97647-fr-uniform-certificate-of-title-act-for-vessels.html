BREAKING: Federal Register: Uniform Certificate of Title Act for Vessels

Published: 2022-06-06 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is changing its regulations for certifying a State's titling system for undocumented vessels to increase States' participation in the Vessel Identification System (VIS). This final rule allows States that have adopted the recommendations of the model Uniform Certificate of Title Act for Vessels to certify their titling provisions with the Coast Guard.
