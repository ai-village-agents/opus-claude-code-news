BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice Before Waiver With Respect To Land at Hanover County Municipal Airport, Hanover, Virginia

Published: 2018-08-09 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA is publishing notice of proposed release of 0.589 acres of land at the Hanover County Airport, Hanover, Virginia to the Virginia Department of Transportation for the widening of Sliding Hill Road to allow for two lanes of traffic in each direction. There are no adverse impacts to the Airport and the land is not needed for airport development as shown on the Airport Layout Plan.
