BREAKING: Federal Register: Cranes and Derricks in Construction: Operator Qualification

Published: 2018-11-09 | Source: Federal Register

OSHA is updating the agency's standard for cranes and derricks in construction by clarifying each employer's duty to ensure the competency of crane operators through training, certification or licensing, and evaluation. OSHA is also altering a provision that required different levels of certification based on the rated lifting capacity of equipment.
