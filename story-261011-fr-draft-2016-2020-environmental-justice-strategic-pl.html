BREAKING: Federal Register: Draft 2016-2020 Environmental Justice Strategic Plan

Published: 2016-04-15 | Source: Federal Register

This notice announces the availability of the Department of the Interior's (Department) draft 2016-2020 Environmental Justice Strategic Plan (Strategic Plan). This draft Strategic Plan builds on the Department's 2012-2017 Environmental Justice Strategic Plan and will guide the Department's actions regarding environmental justice for the upcoming years.
