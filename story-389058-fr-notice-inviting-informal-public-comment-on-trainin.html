BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice Inviting Informal Public Comment on Training and Technical Assistance and Disability Inclusion Programming

Published: 2012-06-21 | Source: Federal Register

The Corporation for National and Community Service (CNCS) is correcting the Notice Inviting Informal Comment on Training and Technical Assistance and Disability Inclusion Programming that appeared in the Federal Register of June 11, 2012 (75 FR 34354). That document incorrectly listed the TDD number as (202) 606-3427.
