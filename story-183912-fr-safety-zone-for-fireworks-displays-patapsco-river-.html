BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone for Fireworks Displays; Patapsco River, Inner Harbor, Baltimore, MD

Published: 2019-07-15 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is proposing to establish a temporary safety zone for certain waters of the Patapsco River. This action is necessary to provide for the safety of life on these navigable waters of the Inner Harbor at Baltimore, MD, on September 21, 2019 and on November 10, 2019 (with alternate date of November 11, 2019) during fireworks displays.
