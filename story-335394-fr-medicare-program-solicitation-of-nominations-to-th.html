BREAKING: Federal Register: Medicare Program; Solicitation of Nominations to the Advisory Panel on Hospital Outpatient Payment

Published: 2014-09-23 | Source: Federal Register

This notice solicits nominations for up to four new members to the Advisory Panel on Hospital Outpatient Payment (HOP, the Panel). There are vacancies on the Panel effective September 30, 2014.
