BREAKING: Federal Register: Comprehensive Child Welfare Information System

Published: 2015-11-04 | Source: Federal Register

The Administration for Children and Families reopens the comment period for the notice of proposed rulemaking entitled, "Comprehensive Child Welfare Information System." We take this action to respond to requests from the public for more time to submit comments. The notice of proposed rulemaking and our request for comments appeared in the Federal Register on August 11, 2015. We initially set October 13, 2015 as the deadline for the comment period.
