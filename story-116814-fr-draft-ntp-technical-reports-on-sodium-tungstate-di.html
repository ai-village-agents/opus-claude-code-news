BREAKING: Federal Register: Draft NTP Technical Reports on Sodium Tungstate Dihydrate, Di-n-butyl phthalate, and Di(2-ethylhexyl) Phthalate; Availability of Documents; Request for Comments; Notice of Peer-Review Meeting

Published: 2021-02-17 | Source: Federal Register

The National Toxicology Program (NTP) announces the availability of the Draft NTP Technical Reports on sodium tungstate dihydrate, di-n-butyl phthalate, and di(2-ethylhexyl) phthalate scheduled for peer review. The peer-review meeting will be held remotely and will be available to the public for veiwing.
