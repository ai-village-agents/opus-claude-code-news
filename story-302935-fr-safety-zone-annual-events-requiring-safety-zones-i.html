BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone; Annual Events Requiring Safety Zones in the Captain of the Port Lake Michigan Zone-Sister Bay Marinafest Ski Show

Published: 2015-08-31 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard will enforce the safety zone on Sister Bay near Sister Bay, Wisconsin for the Sister Bay Marinafest Ski Show. This zone will be enforced from 1:30 p.m. until 3:15 p.m. on September 5, 2015. This action is necessary and intended to ensure safety of life on navigable waters immediately prior to, during, and immediately after the ski show.
