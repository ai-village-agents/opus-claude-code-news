BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Pratt & Whitney Division Turbofan

Published: 2018-08-24 | Source: Federal Register

We are superseding Airworthiness Directive (AD) 2017-12-03 for certain Pratt & Whitney Division (PW) PW2037, PW2037M, and PW2040 turbofan engines. AD 2017-12-03 required installing a software standard eligible for installation and precludes the use of electronic engine control (EEC) software standards earlier than SCN 5B/I.
