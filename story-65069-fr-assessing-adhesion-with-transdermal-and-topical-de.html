BREAKING: Federal Register: Assessing Adhesion With Transdermal and Topical Delivery Systems for Abbreviated New Drug Applications; Revised Draft Guidance for Industry; Availability

This draft guidance (Revision 2) revises the Revision 1 draft guidance of the same name, which was announced in the Federal Register on October 10, 2018.
