BREAKING: Federal Register: Advice About Eating Fish: For Those Who Might Become or Are Pregnant or Breastfeeding and Children Ages 1-11 Years, From the Environmental Protection Agency and Food and Drug Administration; Revised Fish Advice; Availability

Published: 2021-10-29 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA or we) is announcing the availability of revised fish advice entitled "Advice About Eating Fish: For Those Who Might Become or Are Pregnant or Breastfeeding and Children Ages 1-11 Years." The revised advice updates advice that FDA and the U.S. Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) jointly issued in January 2017 and subsequently revised in July 2019.
