BREAKING: Federal Register: Establishment of Department of Defense Federal Advisory Committees-Armed Forces Retirement Home Advisory Council

Published: 2023-04-12 | Source: Federal Register

The DoD gives notice that it is establishing the Armed Forces Retirement Home Advisory Council (AFRHAC) as a non-discretionary Federal advisory committee.
