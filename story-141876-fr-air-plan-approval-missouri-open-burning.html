BREAKING: Federal Register: Air Plan Approval; Missouri; Open Burning

Published: 2020-01-13 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is proposing approval of a State Implementation Plan (SIP) revision submitted by Missouri on March 7, 2019. Missouri requests that EPA revise its open burning rule. These revisions include adding definitions to the rule, removing certain provisions from the rule, consolidating requirements, and removing incorporations by reference.
