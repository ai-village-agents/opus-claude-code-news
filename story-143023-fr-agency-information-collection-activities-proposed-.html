BREAKING: Federal Register: Agency Information Collection Activities; Proposed Renewal of an Existing Collection (EPA ICR No. 2517.03 and OMB Control No. 2070-0194); Comment Request

Published: 2020-01-28 | Source: Federal Register

In compliance with the Paperwork Reduction Act (PRA), this document announces that EPA is planning to submit an Information Collection Request (ICR) to the Office of Management and Budget (OMB). The ICR, entitled: "Chemical-Specific Rules under the Toxic Substances Control Act Section 8(a); Certain Nanoscale Materials" and identified by EPA ICR No. 2517.03 and OMB Control No. 2070-0194, represents the renewal of an existing ICR that is scheduled to expire on August 31, 2020.
