BREAKING: Federal Register: Petition for Rulemaking Submitted by C-10 Research and Education Foundation, Inc.

Published: 2012-10-16 | Source: Federal Register

(the petitioner). The petition was docketed by the NRC and assigned Docket No. PRM-72-6.
