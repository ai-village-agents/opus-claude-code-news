BREAKING: Federal Register: Food and Drug Administration/Xavier University Global Medical Device Conference

Published: 2014-02-26 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA) Cincinnati District, in cosponsorship with Xavier University, is announcing a public conference entitled "FDA/Xavier University Global Medical Device Conference (MedCon)." This 3-day public conference includes presentations from key FDA officials and industry experts with small group breakout sessions.
