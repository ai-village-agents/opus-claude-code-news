BREAKING: Federal Register: United Nuclear Corporation Church Rock Project

Published: 2021-06-17 | Source: Federal Register

The public comment period closed on May 27, 2021. The NRC has decided to re-open the public comment period to allow more time for members of the public to develop and submit their comments.
