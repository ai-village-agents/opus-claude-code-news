BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone; Intracoastal Waterway, Biscayne Bay, Miami, FL

Published: 2018-09-24 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is establishing a temporary safety zone for certain navigable waters of Biscayne Bay east of Bayfront Park in connection with aerobatic helicopter demonstrations sponsored by Red Bull in Miami, Florida. The safety zone is needed to protect persons, vessels, and the marine environment from potential hazards associated with the aerial demonstrations over Biscayne Bay.
