BREAKING: Federal Register: Gulf of the Farallones and Monterey Bay National Marine Sanctuaries Regulations on Introduced Species

Published: 2013-03-18 | Source: Federal Register

This action withdraws a notice of proposed rulemaking (NPRM) published in the Federal Register on October 1, 2009 (74 FR 50740) concerning regulations on the introduction of introduced species into Gulf of the Farallones and Monterey Bay national marine sanctuaries.
