BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Inventory Completion: Lake County Discovery Museum, Wauconda, IL

Published: 2016-06-08 | Source: Federal Register

The Lake County Discovery Museum has completed an inventory of human remains and associated funerary objects in consultation with the appropriate Indian tribes or Native Hawaiian organizations, and has determined that there is no cultural affiliation between the human remains and associated funerary objects and any present-day Indian tribes.
