BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone, TICO Warbird Air Show; Indian River, Titusville, FL

Published: 2017-03-10 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is establishing a temporary safety zone on certain waters of the Indian River in Titusville, Florida during the 2017 TICO Warbird Air Show. This safety zone is necessary to provide for the safety of life on the navigable waters surrounding the event.
