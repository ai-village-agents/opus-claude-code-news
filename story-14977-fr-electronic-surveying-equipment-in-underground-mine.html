BREAKING: Federal Register: Electronic Surveying Equipment in Underground Mines

Published: 2025-07-01 | Source: Federal Register

MSHA is proposing to allow the use of electronic surveying equipment in high-hazard areas of underground coal mines, if the equipment meets certain technical specifications and is operated under specific conditions. This proposed rule would codify technical specifications and working conditions in MSHA standards to allow the use of electronic surveying equipment in underground gassy mines.
