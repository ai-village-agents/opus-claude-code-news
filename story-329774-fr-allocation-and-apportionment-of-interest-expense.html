BREAKING: Federal Register: Allocation and Apportionment of Interest Expense

Published: 2014-07-16 | Source: Federal Register

This document contains final regulations that provide guidance concerning the allocation and apportionment of interest expense by corporations owning a 10 percent or greater interest in a partnership, as well as the allocation and apportionment of interest expense using the fair market value method.
