BREAKING: Federal Register: Revision to Power Marketing Policy Kerr-Philpott System of Projects

Published: 2020-10-19 | Source: Federal Register

Southeastern Power Administration (Southeastern or SEPA) announces revision to the power marketing policy for the Kerr-Philpott System of Projects (Kerr-Philpott System). The Kerr-Philpott System power marketing policy was published on July 29, 1985, and is reflected in contracts for the sale of system power, which are maintained in Southeastern's headquarters office.
