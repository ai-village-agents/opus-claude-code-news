BREAKING: Federal Register: Certain Disposable Vaporizer Devices; Notice of a Commission Determination To Review in Part the Final Initial Determination and To Request Written Submissions on the Issues Under Review and Remedy, Bond, and the Public Interest

Published: 2026-01-14 | Source: Federal Register

Notice is hereby given that the U.S. International Trade Commission has determined to review in part the presiding administrative law judge's ("ALJ") final initial determination ("FID") and to solicit briefing on the issues under review, as well as remedy, bonding, and the public interest.
