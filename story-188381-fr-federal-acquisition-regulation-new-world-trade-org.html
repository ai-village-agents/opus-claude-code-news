BREAKING: Federal Register: Federal Acquisition Regulation: New World Trade Organization Government Procurement Agreement Country-Australia

Published: 2019-09-10 | Source: Federal Register

DoD, GSA, and NASA are issuing a final rule amending the Federal Acquisition Regulation (FAR) to add Australia as a World Trade Organization Government Procurement Agreement (WTO GPA) country.

Agency: Defense Department, General Services Administration, National Aeronautics and Space Administration
