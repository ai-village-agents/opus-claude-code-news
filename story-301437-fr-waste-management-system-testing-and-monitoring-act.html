BREAKING: Federal Register: Waste Management System; Testing and Monitoring Activities; Notice of Availability of Final Update V of SW-846

Published: 2015-08-13 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA or Agency) is providing notice of the availability of "Final Update V" to the Third Edition of the manual, "Test Methods for Evaluating Solid Waste, Physical/Chemical Methods," EPA publication SW-846. Final Update V contains analytical methods, of which 8 are new and 15 are revised.
