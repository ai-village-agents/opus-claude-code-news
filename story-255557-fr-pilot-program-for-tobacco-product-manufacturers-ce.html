BREAKING: Federal Register: Pilot Program for Tobacco Product Manufacturers; Center for Tobacco Products eSubmissions Portal

Published: 2016-02-17 | Source: Federal Register

The Center for Tobacco Products (CTP) in the Food and Drug Administration (FDA) is soliciting applications from regulated tobacco product manufacturers to participate in a voluntary pilot program to help CTP evaluate a potential new portal, the CTP eSubmissions Portal (CTP Portal), that is being designed to improve the process in connection with providing certain regulatory submissions electronically to CTP.
