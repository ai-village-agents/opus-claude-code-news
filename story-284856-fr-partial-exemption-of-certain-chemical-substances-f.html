BREAKING: Federal Register: Partial Exemption of Certain Chemical Substances From Reporting Additional Chemical Data

Published: 2015-01-27 | Source: Federal Register

EPA is amending the list of chemical substances that are partially exempt from reporting additional information under the Chemical Data Reporting (CDR) rule. EPA has determined that, based on the totality of information available on the chemical substances listed in this direct final rule, the Agency has low current interest in their CDR processing and use information.
