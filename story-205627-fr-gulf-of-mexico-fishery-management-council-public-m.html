BREAKING: Federal Register: Gulf of Mexico Fishery Management Council; Public Meetings

Published: 2018-04-27 | Source: Federal Register

The Gulf of Mexico Fishery Management Council (Council) will hold eight public hearings and webinar to solicit public comments on Draft Amendment 9--Coral Habitat Areas Considered for Management in the Gulf of Mexico.
