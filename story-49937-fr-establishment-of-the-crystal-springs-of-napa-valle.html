BREAKING: Federal Register: Establishment of the Crystal Springs of Napa Valley Viticultural Area; Modification of the Calistoga Viticultural Area

Published: 2024-10-16 | Source: Federal Register

The Alcohol and Tobacco Tax and Trade Bureau (TTB) establishes the approximately 4,000-acre "Crystal Springs of Napa Valley" American viticultural area (AVA) in Napa County, California. The newly- established AVA is located entirely within the existing North Coast and Napa Valley viticultural areas. TTB also is modifying the existing Calistoga AVA in response to comments received during the comment period.
