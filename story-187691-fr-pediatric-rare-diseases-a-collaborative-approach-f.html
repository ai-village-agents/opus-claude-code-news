BREAKING: Federal Register: Pediatric Rare Diseases-A Collaborative Approach for Drug Development Using Gaucher Disease as a Model; Draft Guidance for Industry; Availability; Correction

Published: 2019-08-30 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration is correcting a notice entitled "Pediatric Rare Diseases--A Collaborative Approach for Drug Development Using Gaucher Disease as a Model; Draft Guidance for Industry; Availability" that appeared in the Federal Register of December 7, 2017. The document announced the availability of a draft guidance focusing on drug development for pediatric patients with Gaucher disease. The document was published with the incorrect docket number.
