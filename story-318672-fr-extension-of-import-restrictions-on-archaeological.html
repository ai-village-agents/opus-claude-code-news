BREAKING: Federal Register: Extension of Import Restrictions on Archaeological and Ecclesiastical Ethnological Materials from Honduras

Published: 2014-03-12 | Source: Federal Register

This final rule amends U.S. Customs and Border Protection (CBP) regulations to reflect the extension of import restrictions on certain archaeological materials from Honduras. These restrictions, which were last extended by CBP Decision (Dec.) 09-05, are due to expire on March 12, 2014, unless extended.
