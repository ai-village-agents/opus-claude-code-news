BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone; Upper Mississippi River, Cape Girardeau, MO

Published: 2022-05-17 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is establishing a temporary safety zone for all navigable waters of the Upper Mississippi River extending from mile marker (MM) 49 to MM 50 on the Upper Mississippi River near Cape Girardeau, MO. The safety zone is needed to protect personnel and vessels from potential hazards due to a partially sunken cement barge in the navigational channel.
