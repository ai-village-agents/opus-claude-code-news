BREAKING: Federal Register: Granular Polytetrafluoroethylene Resin From Italy Termination of Five-Year Review

Published: 2016-08-22 | Source: Federal Register

The Commission instituted the subject five-year review in June 2016 to determine whether revocation of the antidumping duty order on granular polytetrafluoroethylene resin from Italy would be likely to lead to continuation or recurrence of material injury. On August 11, 2016, the Department of Commerce published notice that it was revoking the order effective July 18, 2016, because "the domestic interested parties did not participate in this sunset review."
