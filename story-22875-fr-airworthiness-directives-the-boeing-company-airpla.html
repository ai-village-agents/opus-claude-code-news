BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; The Boeing Company Airplanes

Published: 2025-11-21 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA proposes to adopt a new airworthiness directive (AD) for certain The Boeing Company Model 787-8, 787-9, and 787-10 airplanes. This proposed AD was prompted by reports of door assist handles pulled loose from their lower attach point in the doorway support bracket during pre-flight checks.
