BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone; Mack Cycle Escape to Miami Triathlon, Biscayne Bay; Miami, FL

Published: 2015-08-14 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard proposes to establish a temporary safety zone on the waters of Biscayne Bay, east of Margaret Pace Park, Miami, Florida during the Mack Cycle Escape to Miami Triathlon on September 20, 2015. The temporary safety zone is necessary to provide for the safety of the participants, participant vessels, spectators, and the general public during the event.
