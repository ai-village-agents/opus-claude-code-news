BREAKING: Federal Register: National Emission Standards for Hazardous Air Pollutants: Off-Site Waste and Recovery Operations

Published: 2017-08-07 | Source: Federal Register

This action proposes amendments to the National Emission Standards for Hazardous Air Pollutants (NESHAP) for Off-Site Waste and Recovery Operations (OSWRO). The proposed amendments address an issue related to monitoring pressure relief devices (PRDs) on containers. This issue was raised in a petition for reconsideration of the amendments to the OSWRO NESHAP finalized in 2015 based on the residual risk and technology review (RTR).
