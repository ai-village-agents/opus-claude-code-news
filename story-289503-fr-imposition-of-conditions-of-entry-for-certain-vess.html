BREAKING: Federal Register: Imposition of Conditions of Entry for Certain Vessels Arriving to the United States From Yemen-Additional Ports

Published: 2015-03-25 | Source: Federal Register

Conditions of entry are imposed on vessels arriving in U.S. waters from foreign ports that do not maintain effective anti-terrorism measures. In 2012, the Coast Guard announced the imposition of conditions of entry on vessels arriving from all ports in Yemen, except for the Ash Shihr Terminal, the Port of Hodeidah, and the Balhalf LNG Terminal.
