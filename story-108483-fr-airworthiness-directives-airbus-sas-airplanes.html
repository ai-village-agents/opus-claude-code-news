BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Airbus SAS Airplanes

Published: 2022-10-25 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA is superseding Airworthiness Directive (AD) 2021-16- 03, which applied to certain Airbus SAS Model A350-941 and -1041 airplanes. AD 2021-16-03 required an inspection for missing or incorrect application of the lightning strike edge glow sealant protection at certain locations in the wing tanks, and corrective action.
