BREAKING: Federal Register: FCA US, LLC, Receipt of Petition for Decision of Inconsequential Noncompliance

Published: 2020-06-12 | Source: Federal Register

FCA US LLC (f/k/a Chrysler Group LLC) "FCA US" has determined that certain model year (MY) 2017-2018 Alfa Romeo Giulia motor vehicles do not fully comply with Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard (FMVSS) No. 110, Tire Selection and Rims and Motor Home/ Recreation Vehicle Trailer Load Carrying Capacity Information for Motor Vehicles with a GVWR of 4,536 Kilograms (10,000 Pounds) or Less. FCA US filed a noncompliance report dated June 6, 2019. FCA US subsequently petitioned NHTSA on June 28, 2019...
