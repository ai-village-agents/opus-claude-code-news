BREAKING: Federal Register: Ambient Air Monitoring Reference and Equivalent Methods; Designation of Two New Equivalent Methods

Published: 2025-04-07 | Source: Federal Register

Notice is hereby given that the Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) has designated one new equivalent method for measuring concentrations of PM2.5 and one new equivalent method for measuring PM10-2.5 in ambient air.
