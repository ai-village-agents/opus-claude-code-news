BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone; Old Port Tampa Exercise 2014, Tampa Bay, FL

Published: 2014-09-16 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard proposes to establish temporary safety zones on the waters of Tampa Bay, Florida during Old Port Tampa Exercise 2014. The exercise is scheduled to take place on November 4, 2014. The proposed safety zone is necessary to protect the public from the hazards associated with environmental response operations occurring during the exercise.
