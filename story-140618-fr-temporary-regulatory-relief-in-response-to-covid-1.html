BREAKING: Federal Register: Temporary Regulatory Relief in Response to COVID-19-Extension

Published: 2021-12-22 | Source: Federal Register

The NCUA Board (Board) is further extending its temporary final rule, which modified certain regulatory requirements to help ensure that federally insured credit unions (FICUs) remain operational and can address economic conditions caused by the COVID-19 pandemic.
