BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Proposed Buy America Waiver and Request for Comment

Published: 2022-07-19 | Source: Federal Register

The Federal Transit Administration (FTA) has received multiple individual requests for a Buy America waiver for non-ADA accessible passenger vans and minivans that can be used in vanpool programs, based on the nonavailability of Buy America-compliant vehicles. FTA is proposing a partial general nonavailability waiver of limited duration for mass-produced, unmodified non-ADA accessible vans and minivans.
