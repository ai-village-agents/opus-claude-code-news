BREAKING: Federal Register: Kansas State University; Exemption

Published: 2024-12-17 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Nuclear Regulatory Commission (NRC) is issuing an exemption in response to a request dated March 7, 2024, as supplemented by letters dated June 11, October 4, and November 20, 2024, from Kansas State University (KSU).
