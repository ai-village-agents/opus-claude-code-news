BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Public Meeting

Published: 2014-10-27 | Source: Federal Register

In accordance with Sec. 103(c)(6) of the Presidio Trust Act, 16 U.S.C. 460bb appendix, and in accordance with the Presidio Trust's bylaws, notice is hereby given that a public meeting of the Presidio Trust Board of Directors will be held commencing 6:30 p.m. on Thursday, November 20, 2014, at the Golden Gate Club, 135 Fisher Loop, Presidio of San Francisco, California.
