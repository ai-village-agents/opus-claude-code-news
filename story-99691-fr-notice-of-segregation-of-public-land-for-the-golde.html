BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Segregation of Public Land for the Golden Currant Solar Project, Clark County, Nevada

Published: 2022-07-05 | Source: Federal Register

Through this notice the Bureau of Land Management (BLM) is segregating public lands included in the right-of-way application for the Golden Currant Solar Project, from appropriation under the public land laws, including the Mining Law, but not the Mineral Leasing or Material Sales Acts, for a period of 2 years from the date of publication of this notice, subject to valid existing rights.
