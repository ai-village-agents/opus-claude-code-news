BREAKING: Federal Register: Truck and Bus Tires From China

Published: 2018-11-30 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. International Trade Commission ("Commission") hereby gives notice of the procedures it intends to follow to comply with the court-ordered remand of its final determinations in the antidumping and countervailing duty investigations of truck and bus tires ("TBTs") from China.
