BREAKING: Federal Register: Oral Drug Products Administered via Enteral Feeding Tube: In Vitro Testing and Labeling Recommendations; Draft Guidance for Industry; Availability

Published: 2021-06-03 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA or Agency) is announcing the availability of a draft guidance for industry entitled "Oral Drug Products Administered Via Enteral Feeding Tube: In Vitro Testing and Labeling Recommendations." This draft guidance provides recommendations for consistent in vitro testing of oral drug products to demonstrate their suitability to be administered via enteral tube.
