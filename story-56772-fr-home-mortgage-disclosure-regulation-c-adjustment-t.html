BREAKING: Federal Register: Home Mortgage Disclosure (Regulation C) Adjustment to Asset-Size Exemption Threshold

Published: 2024-12-27 | Source: Federal Register

The Consumer Financial Protection Bureau (CFPB) is amending official commentary interpreting requirements of the CFPB's Regulation C to reflect the asset-size exemption threshold for banks, savings associations, and credit unions based on the annual percentage change in the average of the Consumer Price Index for Urban Wage Earners and Clerical Workers (CPI-W).
