BREAKING: Federal Register: Approval and Promulgation of Air Quality Implementation Plans; Delaware; Attainment Plan for the Philadelphia-Wilmington, Pennsylvania-New Jersey-Delaware Nonattainment Area for the 1997 Annual Fine Particulate Matter Standard

EPA is approving a State Implementation Plan (SIP) revision submitted by the State of Delaware.
