BREAKING: Federal Register: Meetings of the Local Government Advisory Committee and the Small Communities Advisory Subcommittee (SCAS)

Published: 2012-07-13 | Source: Federal Register

The Small Communities Advisory Subcommittee (SCAS) will meet via teleconference on Tuesday, July 24, 2012, 2:30 p.m.-4 p.m. (ET). The Subcommittee will discuss sustainable communities, decentralized wastewater treatment, and other issues and recommendations regarding environmental issues affecting small communities.
