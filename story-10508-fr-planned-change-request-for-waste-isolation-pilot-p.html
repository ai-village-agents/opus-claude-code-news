BREAKING: Federal Register: Planned Change Request for Waste Isolation Pilot Plant Replacement Panels 11 and 12; Closing of Public Comment Period

Published: 2025-05-02 | Source: Federal Register

On July 16, 2024, the Environmental Protection Agency (EPA or the Agency) announced for public comment the availability of a Planned Change Request (PCR) submitted by the U.S. Department of Energy (DOE) to modify the Waste Isolation Pilot Plant (WIPP). The proposed change will involve adding two additional waste panels west of the current repository to replace lost disposal capacity resulting from the 2014 radiological incident and ground control issues throughout WIPP's operational history.
