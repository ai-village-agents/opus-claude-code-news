BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; The Boeing Company Airplanes

Published: 2014-12-10 | Source: Federal Register

We propose to adopt a new airworthiness directive (AD) for certain The Boeing Company Model 777-200, -200LR, -300ER, and 777F series airplanes. This proposed AD was prompted by a report of a jettison fuel pump that was shut off by the automatic shutoff system during the center tank fuel scavenge process on a short-range flight.
