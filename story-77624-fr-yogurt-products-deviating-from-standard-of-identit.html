BREAKING: Federal Register: Yogurt Products Deviating From Standard of Identity; Amendment of Temporary Marketing Permit

Published: 2023-09-25 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA or we) are amending Chobani, LLC's temporary permit to market test lower-fat yogurt products. The temporary permit is amended to allow the use of the test product as an ingredient in other nonstandardized food applications including drinkable beverages, dips, and sauces.
