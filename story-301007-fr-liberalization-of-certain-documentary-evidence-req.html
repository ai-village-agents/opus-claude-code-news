BREAKING: Federal Register: Liberalization of Certain Documentary Evidence Required as Proof of Exportation on Drawback Claims

Published: 2015-08-07 | Source: Federal Register

This document amends U.S. Customs and Border Protection (CBP) regulations by removing some of the requirements for documentation used to establish proof of exportation for drawback claims. Currently, claimants must provide originally signed documentary evidence or a certified copy of such documentary evidence to establish the date and fact of exportation of articles for drawback purposes.
