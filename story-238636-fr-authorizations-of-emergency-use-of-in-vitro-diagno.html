BREAKING: Federal Register: Authorizations of Emergency Use of In Vitro Diagnostic Devices for Detection of Zika Virus; Availability; Correction

Published: 2017-07-10 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration is correcting a notice entitled "Authorizations of Emergency Use of In Vitro Diagnostic Devices for Detection of Zika Virus; Availability" that appeared in the Federal Register of June 30, 2017 (82 FR 29886). The document announced the issuance of two Emergency Use Authorizations for in vitro diagnostic devices for detection of the Zika virus in response to the Zika virus outbreak in the Americas.
