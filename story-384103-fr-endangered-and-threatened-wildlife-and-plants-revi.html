BREAKING: Federal Register: Endangered and Threatened Wildlife and Plants; Revising the Proposed Special Rule for the Utah Prairie Dog

Published: 2012-04-26 | Source: Federal Register

We, the U.S. Fish and Wildlife Service (Service) notify the public that we are making changes to our proposed rule of June 2, 2011, to revise the special rule for the Utah prairie dog (Cynomys parvidens). We are reopening the comment period because we are making substantive changes and one addition to our proposed rule based on public and peer review comments received.
