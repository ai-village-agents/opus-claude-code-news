BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Airbus SAS Airplanes

Published: 2022-12-28 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA proposes to adopt a new airworthiness directive (AD) for all Airbus SAS Model A330-201, -202, -203, -301, -302, and -303 airplanes. This proposed AD was prompted by reports of corrosion and cracks found on engine inlet attach fittings.
