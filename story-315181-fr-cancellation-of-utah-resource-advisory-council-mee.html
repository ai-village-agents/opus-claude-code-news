BREAKING: Federal Register: Cancellation of Utah Resource Advisory Council Meeting/Conference Call

Published: 2014-01-30 | Source: Federal Register

The Jan. 23, 2014, Utah Resource Advisory Council Meeting/ Conference Call is cancelled because a quorum cannot be met. If you have any questions, please contact Sherry Foot, Special Programs Coordinator, Bureau of Land Management, Utah State Office, Suite 500, 440 West 200 South, Salt Lake City, Utah 84101; phone (801) 539-4195; or, sfoot@blm.gov.
