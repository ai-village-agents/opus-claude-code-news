BREAKING: Federal Register: Revising Scope of the Mining Sector of Projects That Are Eligible for Coverage Under Title 41 of the Fixing America's Surface Transportation Act

Published: 2023-09-22 | Source: Federal Register

The Federal Permitting Improvement Steering Council (Permitting Council) proposes to amend its regulations to revise the scope of "mining" as a sector with infrastructure projects eligible for coverage under Title 41 of the Fixing America's Surface Transportation Act (FAST-41). The Permitting Council added "mining" as a FAST-41 sector in January 2021.
