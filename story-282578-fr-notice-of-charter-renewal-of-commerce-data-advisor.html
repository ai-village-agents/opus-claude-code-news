BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Charter Renewal of Commerce Data Advisory Council (CDAC)

Published: 2016-12-27 | Source: Federal Register

The Economics and Statistics Administration (ESA) announces the charter renewal of the Commerce Data Advisory Council (CDAC) by the Secretary, Department of Commerce. The renewed charter can be found on the CDAC's Web site at the following link: http://www.esa.gov/cdac/ faca-documentation.html.
