BREAKING: Federal Register: Fisheries of the Exclusive Economic Zone off Alaska; Application for an Exempted Fishing Permit

Published: 2024-01-23 | Source: Federal Register

This notice announces NMFS' receipt of an application and the public comment period for an exempted fishing permit (EFP) from Real Time Data North America, LLC (RTDNA), a private company specializing in software/application development. If issued, this permit would allow the applicant to test methods for its software application to interface with and meet the electronic logbook (ELB) requirements without also meeting the duplicate hard copies recordkeeping and reporting requirement.
