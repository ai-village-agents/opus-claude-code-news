BREAKING: Federal Register: Drawbridge Operation Regulation; Norwalk River, Norwalk CT

Published: 2015-08-31 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard proposes to modify the operating schedule that governs the Metro-North WALK Bridge across the Norwalk River, mile 0.1, at Norwalk, Connecticut. The bridge owner submitted a request to require a greater advance notice for bridge openings and to increase the time periods the bridge remains in the closed position during the weekday morning and evening commuter rush hours.
