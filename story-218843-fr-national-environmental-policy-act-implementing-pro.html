BREAKING: Federal Register: National Environmental Policy Act Implementing Procedures for the Bureau of Reclamation (516 DM 14)

Published: 2018-10-17 | Source: Federal Register

This notice announces the intent to revise the Bureau of Reclamation (Reclamation) procedures for compliance with the National Environmental Policy Act of 1969 (NEPA) in the Departmental Manual (DM) at 516 DM 14. The proposed revisions are to establish a new Categorical Exclusion (CE) for the transfer of title of certain projects and facilities from Reclamation to a qualifying non-Federal project entity.
