BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone for Fireworks Displays; Upper Potomac River, Washington Channel, DC

Published: 2019-07-26 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard will enforce a safety zone for three fireworks displays taking place over the Washington Channel, adjacent to The Wharf DC, Washington, DC; the first on August 4, 2019, the second on September 21, 2019, and the third on December 7, 2019. This action is necessary to ensure the safety of life on navigable waterways during these fireworks displays.
