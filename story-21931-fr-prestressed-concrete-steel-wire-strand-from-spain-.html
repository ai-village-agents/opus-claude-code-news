BREAKING: Federal Register: Prestressed Concrete Steel Wire Strand From Spain: Preliminary Results of Antidumping Duty Administrative Review; 2023-2024

Published: 2025-10-03 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Department of Commerce (Commerce) preliminarily determines that the producer/exporter subject to this administrative review made sales of subject merchandise at less than normal value (NV) during the period of review (POR), June 1, 2023, through May 31, 2024. We invite interested parties to comment on these preliminary results.
