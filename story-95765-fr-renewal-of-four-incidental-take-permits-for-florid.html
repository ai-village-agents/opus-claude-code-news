BREAKING: Federal Register: Renewal of Four Incidental Take Permits for Florida Scrub-Jay, Charlotte County, FL

Published: 2022-05-12 | Source: Federal Register

We, the Fish and Wildlife Service, announce receipt of an application from Peter Famulari to renew four incidental take permits (ITPs) under the Endangered Species Act. If granted, the renewed ITPs would authorize take of the Florida scrub-jay via destruction of the species' feeding, breeding, and sheltering habitat.
