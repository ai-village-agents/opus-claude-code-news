BREAKING: Federal Register: Petition To Manufacture Foot-and-Mouth Disease Vaccine in the United States

Published: 2020-12-02 | Source: Federal Register

We are reopening the comment period for our notice of receipt of a petition from Zoetis, Inc. requesting approval for the manufacture within the continental United States of a vaccine derived from a leaderless strain of the foot-and-mouth disease (FMD) virus.
