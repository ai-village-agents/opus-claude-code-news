BREAKING: Federal Register: Fire Protection for Recreational Vessels

Published: 2019-05-13 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard proposes to amend the rules for recreational vessels by moving fire extinguishing equipment standards for recreational vessels from the uninspected vessel subchapter, which includes requirements for both recreational and commercial vessels, to the subchapter applicable only to recreational vessels.
