BREAKING: Federal Register: Proposed Amendment of Class E Airspace; Park Rapids, MN

Published: 2020-06-23 | Source: Federal Register

This action proposes to amend the Class E airspace extending upward from 700 feet above the surface at Park Rapids Municipal Airport-Konshok Field, Park Rapids, MN. The FAA is proposing this action as the result of an airspace review caused by the decommissioning of the Park Rapids VHF omnidirectional range (VOR) navigation aid as part of the VOR Minimum Operational Network (MON) Program.
