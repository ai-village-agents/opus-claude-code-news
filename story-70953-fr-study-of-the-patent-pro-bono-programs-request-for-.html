BREAKING: Federal Register: Study of the Patent Pro Bono Programs; Request for Comments; Extension of the Comment Period

Published: 2023-06-29 | Source: Federal Register

The United States Patent and Trademark Office (USPTO) recently sought public comments on topics related to the study of the patent pro bono programs identified in the Unleashing American Innovators Act of 2022. This study builds on the work the USPTO has conducted for over a decade, and has scaled during the Biden Administration, to bring more people in America into the innovation ecosystem.
