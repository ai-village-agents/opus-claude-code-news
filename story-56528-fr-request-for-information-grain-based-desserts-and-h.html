BREAKING: Federal Register: Request for Information: Grain-Based Desserts and High-Protein Yogurt Crediting in Child Nutrition Programs

Published: 2024-12-26 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Department of Agriculture's (USDA) Food and Nutrition Service (FNS) requests comments from the public to help inform future policymaking, guidance, and technical assistance related to grain-based desserts and high-protein yogurt (which may include Greek and Greek- style yogurt) crediting in the Child Nutrition Programs.
