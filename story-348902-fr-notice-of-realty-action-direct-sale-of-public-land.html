BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Realty Action: Direct Sale of Public Land in Pinal County, AZ

Published: 2013-03-08 | Source: Federal Register

The Bureau of Land Management (BLM), Lower Sonoran Field Office (LSFO), proposes a noncompetitive direct sale of one land parcel totaling approximately 160 acres in Pinal County, Arizona. The public land would be sold to the Ak-Chin Indian Community (Community) at not less than the appraised fair market value (FMV).
