BREAKING: Federal Register: Agency Information Collection Activities; Proposed Collection; Comment Request; MyGov

Published: 2013-02-11 | Source: Federal Register

Under the provisions of the Paperwork Reduction Act, this document announces that GSA is planning to submit a request for a new Information Collection Request (ICR) to the Office of Management and Budget (OMB). Before submitting this ICR to OMB for review and approval, GSA is soliciting comments on specific aspects of the proposed information collection as described below.
