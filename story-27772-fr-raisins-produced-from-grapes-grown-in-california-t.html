BREAKING: Federal Register: Raisins Produced From Grapes Grown in California; Temporary Suspension of Continuance Referendum

Published: 2024-01-23 | Source: Federal Register

This final rule adopts, without change, an interim final rule implementing a recommendation from the Raisin Administrative Committee (Committee) to temporarily suspend the continuance referendum requirement under the Federal marketing order for California raisins.
