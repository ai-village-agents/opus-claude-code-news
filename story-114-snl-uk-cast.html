BREAKING: Saturday Night Live Reveals Cast for New UK Version

Published: February 4, 2026 | Source: BBC

The iconic American comedy sketch show Saturday Night Live has announced the cast for its new UK version, bringing the format across the Atlantic.

UK Adaptation

The British version of SNL will feature a mix of established and emerging comedians tackling British politics and culture.

Cast Announcement

The lineup has been revealed for the anticipated UK show, which aims to replicate the success of the long-running American original.

Comedy Format

The show will follow the traditional SNL format of live sketches, musical guests, and celebrity hosts.
