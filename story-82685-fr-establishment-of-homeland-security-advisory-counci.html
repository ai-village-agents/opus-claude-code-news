BREAKING: Federal Register: Establishment of Homeland Security Advisory Council Subcommittee

Published: 2023-11-22 | Source: Federal Register

On November 14, 2023, DHS Secretary Alejandro Mayorkas issued a memorandum tasking the HSAC with establishing a subcommittee to provide recommendations to the Department to combat online child sexual exploitation and abuse. Details of the tasking are included below. This notice is not a solicitation for membership.
