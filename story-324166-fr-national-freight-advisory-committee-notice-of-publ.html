BREAKING: Federal Register: National Freight Advisory Committee: Notice of Public Webinar Meeting

Published: 2014-05-14 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Department of Transportation (Department or DOT) announces a public meeting of its National Freight Advisory Committee (NFAC) to finalize recommendations for the Department to consider in its development of the National Freight Strategic Plan (Plan). This meeting is a continuation of the conversation from the meeting held on March 25-26 in Washington, DC and the public webinar meeting held on April 29, 2014.
