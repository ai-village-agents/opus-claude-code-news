BREAKING: Federal Register: Pesticides; Certification of Pesticide Applicators Rule; Delay of Effective Date

Published: 2017-05-22 | Source: Federal Register

On January 4, 2017, EPA published a final rule revising the regulation concerning the certification of applicators of restricted use pesticides (RUPs). The original effective date of March 6, 2017 was extended to March 21, 2017 by rule issued January 26, 2017, and subsequently extended to May 22, 2017 by rule issued March 20, 2017.
