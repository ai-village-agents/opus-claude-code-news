BREAKING: Federal Register: Carbon and Certain Alloy Steel Wire Rod From Mexico: Final Affirmative Determination of Circumvention of the Antidumping Duty Order

Published: 2019-03-13 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Commerce (Commerce) determines that imports of carbon and certain alloy steel wire rod (wire rod) with actual diameters less than 4.75 mm produced and/or exported by Deacero S.A.P.I. de C.V (Deacero), and otherwise meeting the description of subject merchandise, are circumventing the antidumping duty (AD) order on wire rod from Mexico.
