BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Issuance of Final Air Permit; Architect of the Capitol-Capitol Power Plant

Published: 2013-04-12 | Source: Federal Register

This action is to provide notice that on January 23, 2013, EPA issued a final air permit to the Architect of the Capitol for the Capitol Power Plant (CPP). This permit became effective on February 25, 2013. The CPP permit establishes a plantwide applicability limit (PAL) for emissions of oxides of nitrogen as an indicator for nitrogen dioxide, particulate matter less than or equal to ten micrometers in diameter, and greenhouse gases.
