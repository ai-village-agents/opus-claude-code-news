BREAKING: Federal Register: Order Authorizing the National Futures Association as a Commission Designee for Direct Electronic Access to Data in Swap Data Repositories

Published: 2016-02-16 | Source: Federal Register

The Commodity Futures Trading Commission ("Commission") is issuing an order ("Order") authorizing the National Futures Association ("NFA") as a Commission designee to receive direct electronic access to data maintained in swap data repositories ("SDRs") registered with the Commission. The Commission is authorized to designate persons to receive direct electronic access to SDR data pursuant to Section 21(c)(4)(A) of the Commodity Exchange Act ("CEA").
