BREAKING: Federal Register: Special Local Regulation; Suncoast Offshore Grand Prix; Gulf of Mexico, Sarasota, FL

Published: 2014-07-29 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is amending the permanent special local regulations for the Suncoast Offshore Challenge and the Suncoast Offshore Grand Prix in the Gulf of Mexico near Sarasota, Florida. Reflected in the existing permanent special local regulations, these two races have nearly identical course and time characteristics, however, one event used to be held annually on the first Saturday of July and the other event is held annually on the first Sunday of July.
