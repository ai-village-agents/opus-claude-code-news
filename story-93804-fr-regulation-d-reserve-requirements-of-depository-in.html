BREAKING: Federal Register: Regulation D: Reserve Requirements of Depository Institutions

Published: 2022-04-18 | Source: Federal Register

The Board of Governors of the Federal Reserve System ("Board") has adopted final amendments to its regulations to revise the rate of interest paid on balances ("IORB") maintained at Federal Reserve Banks by or on behalf of eligible institutions. The final amendments specify that IORB is 0.40 percent, a 0.25 percentage point increase from its prior level.
