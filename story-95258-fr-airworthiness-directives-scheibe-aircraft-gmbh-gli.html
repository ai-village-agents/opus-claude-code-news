BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Scheibe-Aircraft-GmbH Gliders

Published: 2022-05-06 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA is adopting a new airworthiness directive (AD) for certain Scheibe-Aircraft-GmbH Model SF 25 C gliders. This AD was prompted by mandatory continuing airworthiness information (MCAI) issued by the aviation authority of another country to identify and correct an unsafe condition on an aviation product.
