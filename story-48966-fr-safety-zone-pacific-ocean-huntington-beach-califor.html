BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone; Pacific Ocean, Huntington Beach, California

Published: 2024-10-03 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Coast Guard is establishing a safety zone offshore of Huntington Beach, CA in support of the Pacific Airshow. This action is necessary to provide for the safety of life on these navigable waters in the area of air and water demonstrations and to protect the high concentration of people attending the event.
