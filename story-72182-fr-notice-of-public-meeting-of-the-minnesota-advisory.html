BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Public Meeting of the Minnesota Advisory Committee; Cancellation

Published: 2023-07-18 | Source: Federal Register

The Commission on Civil Rights published a notice in the Federal Register concerning a virtual business meeting of the Minnesota Advisory Committee. The meeting scheduled for Thursday, July 27, 2023, at 12:30 p.m. (CT) is cancelled. The notice is in the Federal Register of Wednesday, June 21, 2023, in FR Doc. 2023-13124 in the third column of page 40199 and first column of page 40200.
