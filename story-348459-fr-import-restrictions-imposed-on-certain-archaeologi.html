BREAKING: Federal Register: Import Restrictions Imposed on Certain Archaeological Material From Belize

Published: 2013-03-05 | Source: Federal Register

This final rule amends the U.S. Customs and Border Protection (CBP) regulations to reflect the imposition of import restrictions on certain archaeological material from Belize.
