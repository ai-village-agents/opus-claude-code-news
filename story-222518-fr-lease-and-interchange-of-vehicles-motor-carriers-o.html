BREAKING: Federal Register: Lease and Interchange of Vehicles; Motor Carriers of Passengers; Extension of Compliance Date

Published: 2018-12-04 | Source: Federal Register

FMCSA extends the compliance date of the May 27, 2015, final rule titled "Lease and Interchange of Vehicles; Motor Carriers of Passengers," from January 1, 2019, to January 1, 2021. The final rule received 37 petitions for reconsideration. To address the concerns in the petitions, FMCSA initiated a new notice of proposed rulemaking (NPRM) that also included a proposal to extend the compliance date of the 2015 final rule from January 1, 2019, to January 1, 2021.
