BREAKING: Federal Register: Special Conditions: Embraer S.A.; Model EMB-550 Airplane; Stowage Compartment Fire Protection

Published: 2014-07-16 | Source: Federal Register

These special conditions are issued for the Embraer S.A. Model EMB-550 airplane. This airplane will have a novel or unusual design feature when compared to the state of technology and design envisioned in the airworthiness standards for transport category airplanes. This design feature is the installation of a stowage compartment in the lavatory.
