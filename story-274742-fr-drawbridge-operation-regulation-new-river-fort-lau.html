BREAKING: Federal Register: Drawbridge Operation Regulation; New River, Fort Lauderdale, FL

Published: 2016-09-23 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is changing the operating schedule that governs the Florida East Coast Railway (FEC) Railroad Bridge across the New River, mile 2.5, at Fort Lauderdale, FL. This rule implements requirements for the operator to ensure that adequate notice of bridge closure times are available to the waterway traffic.
