BREAKING: Federal Register: Single Source Cooperative Agreement to the National Emerging Special Pathogens Training and Education Center

Published: 2025-09-29 | Source: Federal Register

ASPR intends to provide a five-year single-source Cooperative Agreement (CoAg) to the National Emerging Special Pathogens Training and Education Center (NETEC). The CoAg will enable NETEC to continue to lead special pathogen preparedness and response for currently active or emergent High Consequence Infectious Disease (HCID) threats.
