BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Award of a Single-Source Cooperative Agreement To Fund the Ho Chi Minh City Department of Health (HCMC DOH), Vietnam

Published: 2022-02-22 | Source: Federal Register

The Centers for Disease Control and Prevention (CDC), located within the Department of Health and Human Services (HHS), announces the award of approximately $2,000,000 for Year 1 of funding to the Ho Chi Minh City Department of Health (HCMC DOH). The award will strengthen the capacity of the HCMC DOH to provide sustainable evidence-based effective HIV prevention, care and treatment services.
