BREAKING: Federal Register: Disaster Assistance Loan Program; Disaster Loan Credit and Collateral Requirements.

Published: 2014-04-25 | Source: Federal Register

SBA is amending its disaster loan program regulations in response to Hurricane Sandy Rebuilding Task Force recommendations. One change allows SBA to rely on the disaster loan applicant's credit, including credit score, rather than personal or business cash flow in order to assess repayment ability for those applicants with strong credit.
