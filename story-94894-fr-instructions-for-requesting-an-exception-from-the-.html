BREAKING: Federal Register: Instructions for Requesting an Exception From the Secretary of Energy Under Presidential Proclamation Relating to the Regulation of the Anchorage and Movement of Russian-Affiliated Vessels to United States Ports

Published: 2022-05-02 | Source: Federal Register

Per Proclamation 10371, "Declaration of National Emergency and Invocation of Emergency Authority Relating to the Regulation of the Anchorage and Movement of Russian-Affiliated Vessels to United States Ports" ("the Proclamation"), DOE is providing instructions for requesting an exception from the Secretary of Energy to the prohibition set forth in the Proclamation.
