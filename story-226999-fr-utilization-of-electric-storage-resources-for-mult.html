BREAKING: Federal Register: Utilization of Electric Storage Resources for Multiple Services When Receiving Cost-Based Rate Recovery

Published: 2017-02-06 | Source: Federal Register

The Commission issues this policy statement to clarify its precedent and provide guidance on the ability of electric storage resources to provide services at and seek to recover their costs through both cost-based and market-based rates concurrently.
