BREAKING: Federal Register: Disturbance Control Standard-Contingency Reserve for Recovery From a Balancing Contingency Event Reliability Standard

Published: 2017-02-02 | Source: Federal Register

The Commission approves Reliability Standard BAL-002-2 (Disturbance Control Standard--Contingency Reserve for Recovery from a Balancing Contingency Event) submitted by the North American Electric Reliability Corporation (NERC). Reliability Standard BAL-002-2 is designed to ensure that balancing authorities and reserve sharing groups balance resources and demand and return their Area Control Error to defined values following a Reportable Balancing Contingency Event.
