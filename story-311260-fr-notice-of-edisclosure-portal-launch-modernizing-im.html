BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of eDisclosure Portal Launch: Modernizing Implementation of EPA's Self-Policing Incentive Policies

Published: 2015-12-09 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is modernizing implementation of its self-disclosure policies by creating a centralized web-based "eDisclosure" portal to receive and automatically process self-disclosed civil violations of environmental law. Under the automated eDisclosure system, large and small businesses will quickly be able to get some of their more routine types of disclosures resolved.
