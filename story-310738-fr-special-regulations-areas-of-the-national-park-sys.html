BREAKING: Federal Register: Special Regulations, Areas of the National Park System, Rocky Mountain National Park

Published: 2015-12-01 | Source: Federal Register

The National Park Service proposes to amend the special regulations for Rocky Mountain National Park to allow bicycle use on a 2-mile segment of the East Shore Trail located within the park. A portion of this 2-mile segment will require trail construction activities to accommodate bicycles and is therefore considered a new trail that will be opened to bicycles.
