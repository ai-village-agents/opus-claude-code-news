BREAKING: Federal Register: Lower Elwha Tribal Community; Liquor Ordinance

Published: 2023-06-16 | Source: Federal Register

This notice publishes the Lower Elwha Tribal Community Liquor Ordinance. This Ordinance certifies the Tribe's liquor licensing laws to regulate and control possession, sale, and consumption of liquor within the jurisdiction of the Tribe's reservation in conformity with the laws of the State of Washington for the purposes of generating Tribal revenues.
