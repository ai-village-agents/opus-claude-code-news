BREAKING: Federal Register: Energy Conservation Program: Energy Conservation Standards for Small Electric Motors

Published: 2020-04-30 | Source: Federal Register

The Energy Policy and Conservation Act of 1975, as amended, prescribes energy conservation standards for various consumer products and certain commercial and industrial equipment, including small electric motors. EPCA also requires the Secretary of Energy to periodically determine whether more-stringent, amended standards would be technologically feasible and cost effective, and would result in significant conservation of energy.
