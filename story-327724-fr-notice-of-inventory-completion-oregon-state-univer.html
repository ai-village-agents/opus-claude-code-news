BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Inventory Completion: Oregon State University, Department of Anthropology, Corvallis, OR

Published: 2014-06-24 | Source: Federal Register

The Oregon State University Department of Anthropology has completed an inventory of human remains, in consultation with the appropriate Indian tribes or Native Hawaiian organizations, and has determined that there is a cultural affiliation between the human remains and present-day Indian tribes or Native Hawaiian organizations.
