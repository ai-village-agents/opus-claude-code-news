BREAKING: Federal Register: Pilot Program for Collaborative Research on Motor Vehicles With High or Full Driving Automation

NHTSA is seeking public comment on matters related to the near-term and long-term challenges of Automated Driving Systems (ADS) testing, development and eventual deployment. ADS testing and development are already underway in several areas of the United States.
