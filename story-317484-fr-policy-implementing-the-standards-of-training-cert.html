BREAKING: Federal Register: Policy Implementing the Standards of Training, Certification and Watchkeeping Final Rule; Notice of Availability

Published: 2014-02-26 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard announces the availability of nine Navigation and Vessel Inspection Circulars (NVICs), which are the first set of a series of NVICs to implement the final rule that aligned Coast Guard regulations with amendments to the International Convention on Standards of Training, Certification and Watchkeeping for Seafarers, and made changes to national endorsements.
