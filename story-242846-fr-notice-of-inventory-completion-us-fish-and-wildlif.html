BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Inventory Completion: U.S. Fish and Wildlife Service, Alaska Region, Anchorage, AK

Published: 2017-09-05 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Fish and Wildlife Service, Alaska Region, (Alaska Region USFWS), has completed an inventory of human remains and associated funerary objects, in consultation with the appropriate Indian Tribes or Native Hawaiian organizations, and has determined that there is a cultural affiliation between the human remains and present- day Indian Tribes or Native Hawaiian organizations.
