BREAKING: Federal Register: Public Land Order No. 7834; Withdrawal of Public Lands, North and Middle Fork of the American River, California

Published: 2015-04-16 | Source: Federal Register

This order withdraws 6,737.42 acres of public lands from location and entry under the United States mining laws for 20 years on behalf of the Bureau of Land Management to protect and preserve the riparian areas, wildlife habitat, scenic quality, and high recreational values of lands within the North and Middle Fork of the American River and to provide protection of lands associated with the congressionally designated Auburn Dam Reclamation Project Area pending a decision on future development of...
