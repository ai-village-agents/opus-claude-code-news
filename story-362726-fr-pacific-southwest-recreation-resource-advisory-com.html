BREAKING: Federal Register: Pacific Southwest Recreation Resource Advisory Committee

Published: 2013-08-13 | Source: Federal Register

The Pacific Southwest Recreation Resource Advisory Committe will meet in Sacramento, California. The Committee is authorized under the Federal Lands Recreation Enhancement Act (REA) (Pub. L. 108-447) and operates in compliance with the Federal Advisory Committee Act (FACA) (5 U.S.C. App. II).
