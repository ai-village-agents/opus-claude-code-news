BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Segregation of Public Land for the Rough Hat Clark County Solar Project, Clark County, Nevada

Published: 2021-10-20 | Source: Federal Register

Through this notice the Bureau of Land Management (BLM) is segregating public lands included in the right-of-way application for the Rough Hat Clark County Solar Project from appropriation under the public land laws, including the Mining Law, but not the Mineral Leasing or Material Sales Acts, for a period of 2 years from the date of publication of this notice, subject to valid existing rights.
