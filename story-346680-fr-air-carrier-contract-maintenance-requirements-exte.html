BREAKING: Federal Register: Air Carrier Contract Maintenance Requirements; Extension of Comment Period

Published: 2013-02-12 | Source: Federal Register

This action extends the comment period for an NPRM that was published November 13, 2012. In that rulemaking, the FAA proposed to amend the maintenance regulations for domestic, flag, and supplemental operations, and commuter and on-demand operations for aircraft type certificated with a passenger seating configuration of 10 seats or more (excluding any pilot seat).
