BREAKING: Federal Register: Hooper Springs Transmission Project

Published: 2015-03-30 | Source: Federal Register

This notice announces the availability of the ROD to implement the Hooper Springs Transmission Project in Caribou County, Idaho. BPA has decided to implement the South Alternative's Option 3A (Option 3A) identified in the Hooper Springs Transmission Project Final Environmental Impact Statement (DOE/EIS-0451, January 2015).
