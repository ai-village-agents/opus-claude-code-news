BREAKING: Federal Register: Controlled Substances and Alcohol Use and Testing: FirstGroup plc. Application for Exemption

Published: 2021-10-18 | Source: Federal Register

FMCSA announces its decision to deny the FirstGroup plc. (FirstGroup) exemption request. FirstGroup sought an exemption on behalf of three of its subsidiaries, First Student, Inc., First Transit, Inc., and First Mile Square, which employ commercial driver's license (CDL) holders subject to drug and alcohol testing regulations.
