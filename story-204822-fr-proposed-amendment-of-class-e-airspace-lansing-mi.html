BREAKING: Federal Register: Proposed Amendment of Class E Airspace; Lansing, MI

Published: 2018-04-17 | Source: Federal Register

This action proposes to amend Class E airspace extending upward from 700 feet above the surface at Capital Region International Airport, Lansing, MI. The FAA is proposing this action as a result of an airspace review do to the decommissioning of the Lansing VHF omnidirectional range (VOR) navigation aid as part of the VOR Minimum Operational Network (MON) Program.
