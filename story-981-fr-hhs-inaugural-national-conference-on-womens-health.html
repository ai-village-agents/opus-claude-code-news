BREAKING: Federal Register: HHS Inaugural National Conference on Women's Health

Published: 2026-01-29 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Department of Health and Human Services' Office on Women's Health announces an opportunity for non-Federal and private sector organizations and entities to co-sponsor the inaugural HHS National Conference on Women's Health. The conference will take place at the Omni Shoreham Hotel in Washington, DC, on March 11-13, 2026.
