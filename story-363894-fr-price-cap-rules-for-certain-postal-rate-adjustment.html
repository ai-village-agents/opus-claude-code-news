BREAKING: Federal Register: Price Cap Rules for Certain Postal Rate Adjustments

Published: 2013-08-26 | Source: Federal Register

The Commission is issuing a set of final rules addressing the price cap for market dominant price adjustments. Adoption of the rules follows a review of comments on proposed rules. In brief, proposed rules that generated no opposition have been adopted. Proposed rules that raised easily-resolved questions have been modified, as appropriate, and adopted.
