BREAKING: Federal Register: Determination That TENEX (Guanfacine Hydrochloride) Tablets, 1 Milligram, 2 Milligrams, and 3 Milligrams, Was Not Withdrawn From Sale for Reasons of Safety or Effectiveness

Published: 2020-06-24 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA or Agency) has determined that TENEX (guanfacine hydrochloride) tablets, 1 milligram (mg), 2 mg, and 3 mg, was not withdrawn from sale for reasons of safety or effectiveness.
