BREAKING: Federal Register: Privacy Act of 1974; System of Records

Published: 2015-02-13 | Source: Federal Register

The Office of the Secretary of Defense proposes to alter a system of records, DMDC 10 DoD, entitled "Defense Biometric Identification Data System (DBIDS)" in its inventory of record systems subject to the Privacy Act of 1974, as amended. The records in the system are used to support DoD physical security programs, to issue individual facility/installation access credentials, and for identity verification purposes.
