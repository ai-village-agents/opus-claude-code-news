BREAKING: Federal Register: Announcement of the Fall 2013 Annual Grant Competition for Immediate Release

Published: 2013-05-14 | Source: Federal Register

The Agency announces its Annual Grant Competition, which offers support for research, education and training, and the dissemination of information on international peace and conflict resolution. The Annual Grant Competition is open to any project that falls within the Institute's broad mandate of international conflict resolution. Deadline: October 1, 2013; Online application available: http:// www.usip.org/grants-fellowships/annual-grantcompetition.
