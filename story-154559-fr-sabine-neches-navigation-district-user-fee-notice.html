BREAKING: Federal Register: Sabine Neches Navigation District User Fee Notice

Published: 2020-06-23 | Source: Federal Register

The Water Resources Reform and Development Act (WRRDA) of 2014 authorizes a non-federal interest to levy port or harbor dues in the form of tonnage duties or fees in conjunction with a harbor navigation project whose usable increment of the project is complete to finance the cost, construction or maintenance of the project.
