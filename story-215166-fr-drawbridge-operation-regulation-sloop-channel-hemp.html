BREAKING: Federal Register: Drawbridge Operation Regulation; Sloop Channel, Hempstead, NY

Published: 2018-08-29 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard has issued a temporary deviation from the operating schedule that governs the Meadowbrook State Parkway Bridge across Sloop Channel, mile 12.8, at Hempstead, NY. The deviation is necessary to complete structural, mechanical, and electrical rehabilitations on the bridge.
