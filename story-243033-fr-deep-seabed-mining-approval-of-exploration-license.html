BREAKING: Federal Register: Deep Seabed Mining: Approval of Exploration License Extensions

Published: 2017-09-07 | Source: Federal Register

NOAA is announcing the approval of two, five-year extensions of deep seabed hard mineral exploration licenses issued under the Deep Seabed Hard Mineral Resource Act (DSHMRA). The decision to approve the extensions follows a determination that the Licensee has substantially complied with the licenses, their terms, conditions and restrictions, and the associated exploration plan, and a review of comments on the requested extensions.
