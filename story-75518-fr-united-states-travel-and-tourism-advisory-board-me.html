BREAKING: Federal Register: United States Travel and Tourism Advisory Board: Meeting of the United States Travel and Tourism Advisory Board

Published: 2023-08-28 | Source: Federal Register

The United States Travel and Tourism Advisory Board (Board or TTAB) will hold a meeting on Wednesday, September 13, 2023. The Board advises the Secretary of Commerce on matters relating to the U.S. travel and tourism industry. The main purpose of this meeting is for Board members to deliberate and potentially adopt five letters of recommendation for the Secretary of Commerce.
