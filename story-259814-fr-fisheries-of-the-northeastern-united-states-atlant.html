BREAKING: Federal Register: Fisheries of the Northeastern United States; Atlantic Herring Fishery; Framework Adjustment 4

Published: 2016-04-04 | Source: Federal Register

NMFS implements approved measures in Framework 4 to the Atlantic Herring Fishery Management Plan. The New England Fishery Management Council developed Framework 4 to further enhance catch monitoring and address discarding in the herring fishery.
