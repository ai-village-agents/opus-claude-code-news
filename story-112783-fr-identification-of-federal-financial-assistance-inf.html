BREAKING: Federal Register: Identification of Federal Financial Assistance Infrastructure Programs Subject to the Build America, Buy America Provisions of the Infrastructure Investment and Jobs Act

Published: 2022-12-20 | Source: Federal Register

Pursuant to Section 70913(a) of the Infrastructure Investment and Jobs Act and consistent with OMB Memorandum 22-08, Identification of Federal Financial Assistance Infrastructure Programs Subject to the Build America, Buy America Provisions of the Infrastructure Investment and Jobs Act, Federal entities are required to provide the Office of Management and Budget (OMB) and Congress a report listing all Federal financial assistance programs for infrastructure administered by the agency.
