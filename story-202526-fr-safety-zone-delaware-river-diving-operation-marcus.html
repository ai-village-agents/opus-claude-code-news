BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone; Delaware River, Diving Operation, Marcus Hook, PA

Published: 2018-03-20 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is establishing a safety zone encompassing all navigable waters within a 250-yard radius of Commerce Construction vessels and machinery conducting survey and diving operations in the Delaware River, in the vicinity of Anchorage 7, near Marcus Hook, PA. The safety zone is needed to protect personnel, vessels, and the marine environment from potential hazards created by survey and diving operations.
