BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Agusta S.p.A. Helicopters

Published: 2014-06-19 | Source: Federal Register

We are adopting a new airworthiness directive (AD) for Agusta S.p.A. (Agusta) Model AB412 and AB412EP helicopters with a certain rotor brake pinion installed. This AD requires inspecting the rotor brake pinion for a crack, and replacing it if there is a crack. This AD is prompted by a report of a rotor brake pinion failure.
