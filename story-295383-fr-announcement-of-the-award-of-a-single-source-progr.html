BREAKING: Federal Register: Announcement of the Award of a Single-Source Program Expansion Supplement Grant to the U.S. Committee for Refugees and Immigrants in Arlington, VA

Published: 2015-06-02 | Source: Federal Register

The Administration for Children and Families (ACF), Office of Refugee Resettlement (ORR) announces the award of a single-source program expansion supplement grant to U.S. Committee for Refugees and Immigrants in Arlington, Virginia for a total of $1,060,805. The supplemental funding will ensure that clients' essential needs, such as housing, transportation, communication, food, and medical care, will be met.
