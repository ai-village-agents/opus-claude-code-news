BREAKING: Federal Register: Medicare Program; Solicitation of Two Nominations to the Advisory Panel on Hospital Outpatient Payment

Published: 2012-08-24 | Source: Federal Register

This notice solicits nominations for two new members to the Advisory Panel on Hospital Outpatient Payment (HOP, the Panel). There will be two vacancies on the Panel beginning September 30, 2012.
