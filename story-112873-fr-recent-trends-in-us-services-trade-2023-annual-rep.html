BREAKING: Federal Register: Recent Trends in U.S. Services Trade, 2023 Annual Report

Published: 2022-12-21 | Source: Federal Register

The Commission has prepared and published annual reports in this series, Recent Trends in U.S. Services Trade, since 1996.\1\ The 2023 report, which the Commission plans to publish in May 2023, will provide aggregate data on cross-border trade in services for the period ending in 2021, and transactions by affiliates based outside the country of their parent firm for the period ending in 2020.
