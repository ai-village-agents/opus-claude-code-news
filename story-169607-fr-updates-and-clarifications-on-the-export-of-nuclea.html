BREAKING: Federal Register: Updates and Clarifications on the Export of Nuclear Material

Published: 2020-12-31 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Nuclear Regulatory Commission (NRC) is amending its export and import regulations to maintain the regulatory status quo for nuclear exports to the United Kingdom (U.K.), upon the entry into force of a new civil nuclear cooperation agreement between the United States (U.S.) and the U.K. (the U.S.-U.K. 123 Agreement).
