BREAKING: Federal Register: Proposed Amendment of Class D Airspace and Class E Airspace; Lawrenceville, GA

Published: 2022-01-18 | Source: Federal Register

This action proposes to amend Class D airspace and Class E airspace extending upward from 700 feet above the surface in Lawrenceville, GA. This action would update the airport's name in both the Class D and E airspace to Gwinnett County/Briscoe Field. In addition, this action would amend the Class D airspace by creating an extension to the southwest.
