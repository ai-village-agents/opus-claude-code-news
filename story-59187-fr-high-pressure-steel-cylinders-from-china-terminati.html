BREAKING: Federal Register: High Pressure Steel Cylinders From China; Termination of Five-Year Reviews

Published: 2023-01-30 | Source: Federal Register

The Commission instituted the subject five-year reviews on November 1, 2022 to determine whether revocation of the countervailing duty order and antidumping duty order on high pressure steel cylinders from China would be likely to lead to continuation or recurrence of material injury.
