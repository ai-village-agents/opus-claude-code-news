BREAKING: Federal Register: Petition for Exemption From the Federal Motor Vehicle Theft Prevention Standard; BMW of North America, LLC

Published: 2018-05-16 | Source: Federal Register

This document grants in full the BMW of North America, LLC's (BMW) petition for exemption of the 8 series vehicle line in accordance with Exemption from the Theft Prevention Standard.
