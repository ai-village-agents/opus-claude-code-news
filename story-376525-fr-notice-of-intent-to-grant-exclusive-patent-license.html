BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Intent To Grant Exclusive Patent License; Daniel Defense, Inc.

Published: 2012-02-02 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of the Navy hereby gives notice of its intent to grant to Daniel Defense, Inc. a revocable, nonassignable, exclusive license to practice in the United States, the Government-owned invention described below: Patent application 13/168,459 (Navy Case 100,359): filed June 24, 2011, entitled "Accessory Interface System".
