BREAKING: Federal Register: Verification of Statements of Account Submitted by Cable Operators and Satellite Carriers

Published: 2012-06-14 | Source: Federal Register

The Copyright Office is proposing a new regulation to implement provisions in the Satellite Television Extension and Localism Act of 2010 ("STELA") that will allow copyright owners to audit certain Statements of Account filed with the Copyright Office.
