BREAKING: Federal Register: Special Conditions: Pratt and Whitney Canada, PW210A; Flat 30-Second and 2-Minute One Engine Inoperative Rating

Published: 2015-07-08 | Source: Federal Register

These special conditions are issued for the Pratt and Whitney Canada PW210A engine model. This engine will have a novel or unusual design feature--an additional one engine inoperative (OEI) rating that combines the 30-second and 2-minute OEI ratings into a single rating. The applicable airworthiness regulations do not contain adequate or appropriate safety standards for this design feature.
