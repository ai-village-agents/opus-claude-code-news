BREAKING: Federal Register: Reciprocal Waivers of Claims for Licensed or Permitted Launch and Reentry Activities

Published: 2015-01-13 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA proposes to amend the reciprocal waivers of claims regulations so that customers would waive claims against all the customers involved in a launch or reentry, including those signing a different set of reciprocal waivers. Also, customers of any customer contracting directly with a licensee or permittee would not have to sign a waiver directly with the licensee or permittee, other customers, or the FAA.
