BREAKING: Federal Register: Secretarial Determination To Lower Head Start Center-Based Service Duration Requirements

Published: 2020-01-30 | Source: Federal Register

The Secretary of Health and Human Services has the authority, on or before February 1, 2020, to lower the percentage of center-based funded enrollment slots for which Head Start programs must provide 1,020 annual hours of planned class operations, based on an assessment of the availability of sufficient funding to mitigate a substantial reduction in funded enrollment.
