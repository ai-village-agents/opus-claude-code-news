BREAKING: Federal Register: Prohibition of Children's Toys and Child Care Articles Containing Specified Phthalates

Published: 2022-12-05 | Source: Federal Register

The Consumer Product Safety Commission (Commission or CPSC) is publishing this document in response to a federal court decision remanding the Commission's final phthalates rule, without vacatur, to allow the Commission to address two procedural deficiencies the court found.
