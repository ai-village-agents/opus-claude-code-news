BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Inventory Completion: California Department of Parks and Recreation, Sacramento, CA

Published: 2012-09-28 | Source: Federal Register

The California Department of Parks and Recreation has completed an inventory of human remains and associated funerary objects, in consultation with the appropriate Indian tribes, and has determined that there is a cultural affiliation between the human remains and associated funerary objects and present-day Indian tribes.
