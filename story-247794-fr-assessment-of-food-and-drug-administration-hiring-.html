BREAKING: Federal Register: Assessment of Food and Drug Administration Hiring and Retention; Public Meeting; Request for Comments

Published: 2017-11-02 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA, the Agency, or we) is announcing the following public meeting entitled "Assessment of FDA Hiring and Retention". The purpose of the public meeting is to share high-level findings from a recently completed diagnostic assessment of FDA's hiring process conducted by a qualified, independent contractor with expertise in assessing human resources operations and transformation.
