BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone; Upper Mississippi River, Miles 179 to 184, St. Louis, MO

Published: 2019-05-10 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is establishing a temporary safety zone on the navigable waters of the Upper Mississippi River from mile marker (MM) 179 to MM 184 in St. Louis, MO. This action is necessary to provide for the safety of persons, vessels, and the marine environment on these navigable waters as a result of increasing flow and high water conditions on the river.
