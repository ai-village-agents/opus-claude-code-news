BREAKING: Federal Register: Annual Marine Events in the Eighth Coast Guard District, Sabine River; Orange, TX

Published: 2013-09-10 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard will enforce Special Local Regulations for the Southern Professional Outboard Racing Tour (S.P.O.R.T.) boat races to be held on the Neches River in Orange, TX from 3 p.m. on September 20, 2013, through 6 p.m. on September 22, 2013. This action is necessary to provide for the safety of the participants, crew, spectators, participating vessels, non-participating vessels and other users of the waterway.
