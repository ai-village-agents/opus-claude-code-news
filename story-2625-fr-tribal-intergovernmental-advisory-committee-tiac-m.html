BREAKING: Federal Register: Tribal Intergovernmental Advisory Committee (TIAC) Membership Announcement

Published: 2026-01-07 | Source: Federal Register

This notice announces the list of Committee Members of HUD's Tribal Intergovernmental Advisory Committee (TIAC) starting in January 2026. The committee will perform several advisory functions while it considers HUD policies that affect Indian Country. HUD will publish a subsequent notice with details of any scheduled TIAC meeting.
