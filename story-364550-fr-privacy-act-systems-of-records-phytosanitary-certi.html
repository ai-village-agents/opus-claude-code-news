BREAKING: Federal Register: Privacy Act Systems of Records; Phytosanitary Certificate Issuance and Tracking System

Published: 2013-09-04 | Source: Federal Register

We are withdrawing a Privacy Act System of Records notice published on August 9, 2013, (78 FR 48642-48644). The system of records was the Phytosanitary Certificate Issuance and Tracking System, USDA- APHIS-13. The August 9, 2013, notice was a duplicate notice published in error; in this document, we are withdrawing the August 9, 2013, notice from publication.
