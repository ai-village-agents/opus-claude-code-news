BREAKING: Federal Register: Transition of NOAA's Shoreline Compilation Scale

Published: 2014-02-14 | Source: Federal Register

The Office of National Geodetic Survey has adjusted the Nation's Shoreline Compilation Scale for more efficient application to the primary product that it supports, NOAA Nautical Charts. The current requirement is "For any portion of a project area, the Compilation Scale within that portion is equal to two times the scale of the largest scale chart or chart inset that includes that portion.
