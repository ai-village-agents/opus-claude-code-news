BREAKING: Federal Register: Request for Information Regarding Home Mortgage Disclosure Act Resubmission Guidelines

Published: 2016-01-12 | Source: Federal Register

The Consumer Financial Protection Bureau (Bureau) supervises and enforces compliance with the Home Mortgage Disclosure Act (HMDA) for certain financial institutions and maintains a resubmission schedule and guidelines (Resubmission Guidelines) describing when supervised institutions should correct and resubmit HMDA data.
