BREAKING: Federal Register: Qualification of Drivers; Exemption Applications; Vision

Published: 2012-06-27 | Source: Federal Register

FMCSA announces its decision to exempt eight individuals from the vision requirement in the Federal Motor Carrier Safety Regulations (FMCSRs). The exemptions will enable these individuals to operate commercial motor vehicles (CMVs) in interstate commerce without meeting the prescribed vision requirement in one eye.
