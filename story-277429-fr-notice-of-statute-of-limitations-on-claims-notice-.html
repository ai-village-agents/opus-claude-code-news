BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Statute of Limitations on Claims; Notice of Final Federal Agency Actions on Proposed Highway in California

Published: 2016-10-25 | Source: Federal Register

The FHWA, on behalf of Caltrans, is issuing this notice to announce actions taken by Caltrans, that are final within the meaning of 23 U.S.C. 139(l)(1). The actions relate to a proposed highway project, located at the Galleria Boulevard/Stanford Ranch Road/State Route 65 interchange between post miles R5.4 to R6.4 in the County of Placer, State of California. Those actions grant licenses, permits, and approvals for the project.
