BREAKING: Federal Register: Energy Conservation Program: Test Procedures for Conventional Cooking Products With Induction Heating Technology

Published: 2013-01-30 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Department of Energy (DOE) proposes to revise its test procedures for cooking products established under the Energy Policy and Conservation Act. Test procedures for cooking products can be found at DOE's regulations for Energy Conservation Program for Consumer Products, subpart B, appendix I (Appendix I).
