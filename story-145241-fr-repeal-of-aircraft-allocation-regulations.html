BREAKING: Federal Register: Repeal of Aircraft Allocation Regulations

Published: 2020-02-25 | Source: Federal Register

This final rule rescinds DOT regulations regarding aircraft allocation from the Code of Federal Regulations. The regulations prescribe procedures for the allocation of aircraft to the Civil Reserve Air Fleet (CRAF) program.
