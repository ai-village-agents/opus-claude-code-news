BREAKING: Federal Register: Overdraft Lending: Very Large Financial Institutions

Published: 2024-02-23 | Source: Federal Register

The Consumer Financial Protection Bureau (CFPB) proposes to amend Regulations E and Z to update regulatory exceptions for overdraft credit provided by very large financial institutions, thereby ensuring that extensions of overdraft credit adhere to consumer protections required of similarly situated products, unless the overdraft fee is a small amount that only recovers applicable costs and losses.
