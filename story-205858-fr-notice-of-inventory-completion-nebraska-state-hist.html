BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Inventory Completion: Nebraska State Historical Society, Lincoln, NE; Correction

Published: 2018-05-01 | Source: Federal Register

The Nebraska State Historical Society (NSHS) has corrected an inventory of human remains and associated funerary objects published in a Notice of Inventory Completion in the Federal Register on May 3, 2017. This notice corrects the cultural affiliation determined in that notice.
