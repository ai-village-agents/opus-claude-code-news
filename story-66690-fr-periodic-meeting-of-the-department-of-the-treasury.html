BREAKING: Federal Register: Periodic Meeting of the Department of the Treasury Tribal Advisory Committee

Published: 2023-05-04 | Source: Federal Register

This notice announces that the Department of the Treasury Tribal Advisory Committee (TTAC) will convene a public meeting from 1:00 p.m.-4:00 p.m. Eastern Time on Wednesday, June 14, 2023. The meeting will be held in person at the Treasury Building in Washington, DC. The meeting is open to the public.
