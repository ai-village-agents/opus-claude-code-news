BREAKING: Federal Register: FDIC Advisory Committee of State Regulators; Notice of Establishment

Published: 2019-12-02 | Source: Federal Register

The Chairman of the FDIC is establishing the FDIC Advisory Committee of State Regulators (the ACSR). The ASCR will provide advice and recommendations to the FDIC on a broad range of policy issues regarding the regulation of state-chartered financial institutions throughout the United States, including its territories.
