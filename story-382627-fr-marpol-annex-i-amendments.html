BREAKING: Federal Register: MARPOL Annex I Amendments

Published: 2012-04-09 | Source: Federal Register

In this notice of proposed rulemaking (NPRM), we are proposing to update our regulations to harmonize U.S. regulations with international conventions regarding oil pollution and safety of life at sea.
