BREAKING: Federal Register: Changes to Production Certificates and Approvals; Notice of Availability of Proposed Advisory Circulars

Published: 2014-12-22 | Source: Federal Register

This notice announces the availability of and request for comments on proposed revisions to three FAA Advisory Circulars (ACs). The proposed revisions correspond to proposed regulatory changes outlined in the FAA's February 27, 2014, notice of proposed rulemaking (NPRM), Changes to Production Certificates and Approvals.
