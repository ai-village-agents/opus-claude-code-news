BREAKING: Federal Register: USDA Departmental Freedom of Information Act Regulations

Published: 2018-06-11 | Source: Federal Register

The United States Department of Agriculture (USDA) is proposing revisions to its current regulations implementing the Freedom of Information Act (FOIA).
