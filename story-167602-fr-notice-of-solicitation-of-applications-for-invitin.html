BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Solicitation of Applications for Inviting Applications for the Intermediary Relending Program for Fiscal Year 2021

Published: 2020-12-07 | Source: Federal Register

This Notice is to invite applications under the Intermediary Relending Program (IRP) for fiscal year (FY) 2021, subject to availability of funding. This Notice is being issued in order to allow applicants enough time to leverage financing, prepare and submit their applications, and give the Agency time to process applications within FY 2021.
