BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Public Release of Stewardship of the National Training and Education Resource (NTER)®

Published: 2015-12-09 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Energy (DOE) today gives notice to inform any interested parties that: DOE will no longer provide financial support for development and web hosting of the National Training and Education Resource (NTER)[supreg]. DOE is releasing stewardship of the learning management system (LMS) and content management system (CMS) of NTER[supreg] to the open source community, consistent with the original development strategy.
