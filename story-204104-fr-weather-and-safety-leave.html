BREAKING: Federal Register: Weather and Safety Leave

Published: 2018-04-10 | Source: Federal Register

The Office of Personnel Management is issuing new regulations on the granting and recording of weather and safety leave for Federal employees. The Administrative Leave Act of 2016 created four new categories of statutorily authorized paid leave--administrative leave, investigative leave, notice leave, and weather and safety leave--and established parameters for their use by Federal agencies.
