BREAKING: Federal Register: Endangered and Threatened Wildlife and Plants; Endangered Status of the Gulf of Mexico Bryde's Whale

Published: 2019-04-15 | Source: Federal Register

We (NMFS) issue a final rule to list the Gulf of Mexico Bryde's whale (Balaenoptera edeni) (hereafter GOMx Bryde's whale) as endangered under the Endangered Species Act (ESA). We have completed a status review of the GOMx Bryde's whale in response to a petition submitted by the Natural Resource Defense Council.
