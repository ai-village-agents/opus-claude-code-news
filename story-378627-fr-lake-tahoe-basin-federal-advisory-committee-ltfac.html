BREAKING: Federal Register: Lake Tahoe Basin Federal Advisory Committee (LTFAC)

Published: 2012-02-27 | Source: Federal Register

The Lake Tahoe Federal Advisory Committee will hold a meeting on March 21, 2012 at the Sierra Nevada College, 999 Tahoe Boulevard, Incline Village, Nevada 89451-9500. This Committee, established by the Secretary of Agriculture on December 15, 1998 (64 FR 2876), is chartered to provide advice to the Secretary on implementing the terms of the Federal Interagency Partnership on the Lake Tahoe Region and other matters raised by the Secretary.
