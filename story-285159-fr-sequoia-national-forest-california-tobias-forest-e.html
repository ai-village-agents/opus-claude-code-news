BREAKING: Federal Register: Sequoia National Forest, California; Tobias Forest Ecosystem Restoration Project

Published: 2015-01-30 | Source: Federal Register

The Forest Service proposes ecological restoration in the Tobias Forest Ecosystem Restoration Project (Tobias Project) by encouraging a healthy and diverse forest ecosystem that is resilient to the effects of wildfire, drought, disease, and other disturbances. The Tobias Project is located in the Greenhorn Mountains on the Western Divide Ranger District, between Alta Sierra and Johnsondale in Tulare County, California.
