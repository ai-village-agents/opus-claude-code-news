BREAKING: Federal Register: National Wildlife Refuge System; Biological Integrity, Diversity, and Environmental Health

Published: 2024-12-19 | Source: Federal Register

We, the U.S. Fish and Wildlife Service (Service), withdraw the proposed rule (proposal) published on February 2, 2024, that proposed new regulations addressing the biological integrity, diversity, and environmental health (BIDEH) of the National Wildlife Refuge System (Refuge System) and updates to the existing BIDEH policy.
