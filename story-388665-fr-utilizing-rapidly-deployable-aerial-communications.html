BREAKING: Federal Register: Utilizing Rapidly Deployable Aerial Communications Architecture in Response to an Emergency

Published: 2012-06-15 | Source: Federal Register

In this document, the Commission seeks comment on the role of deployable aerial communications architecture (DACA) in facilitating emergency response by rapidly restoring communications capabilities in the immediate aftermath of a catastrophic event. The Notice of Inquiry explores the technologies that are or will be available, including innovative DACA technologies that are still in development.
