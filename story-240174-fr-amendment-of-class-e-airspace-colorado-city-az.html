BREAKING: Federal Register: Amendment of Class E Airspace, Colorado City, AZ

Published: 2017-07-28 | Source: Federal Register

This action modifies Class E airspace extending upward from 700 feet above the surface at Colorado City Municipal Airport, Colorado City, AZ, to support the implementation of new Area Navigation (RNAV) Global Positioning System (GPS) standard instrument approach procedures for instrument flight rules (IFR) operations at the airport.
