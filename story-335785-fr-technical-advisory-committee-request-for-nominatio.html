BREAKING: Federal Register: Technical Advisory Committee; Request for Nominations

Published: 2014-09-26 | Source: Federal Register

The BLS is soliciting new members for the BLS Technical Advisory Committee (BLSTAC). The Committee presents advice and makes recommendations to the BLS on technical aspects of the collection and formulation of economic measures. The Committee functions solely as an advisory body to the BLS, on technical topics selected by the BLS.
