BREAKING: Federal Register: Boston Harbor Islands National Recreation Area Advisory Council

Published: 2015-02-17 | Source: Federal Register

This notice announces the annual meeting of the Boston Harbor Islands National Recreation Area Advisory Council. The agenda includes updates from the Massachusetts Department of Conservation and Recreation, the Boston Harbor Island Alliance, and the National Park Service about project, program, marketing, and water transportation plans for the 2015 Season.
