BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Availability of Treatment Evaluation Document for Aircraft Treatments for Certain Hitchhiking Pests

Published: 2017-08-08 | Source: Federal Register

We are advising the public that we have determined that it is necessary to immediately add to the Plant Protection and Quarantine Treatment Manual two new chemical treatments for targeting regulated pests in the cargo holds of aircraft. We have prepared a treatment evaluation document that describes the new treatment schedules and explains why we have determined that they are effective at neutralizing certain target pests.
