BREAKING: Federal Register: Meeting of the President's Advisory Commission on Educational Excellence for African Americans

Published: 2016-04-15 | Source: Federal Register

This notice sets forth the schedule and agenda of the meeting of the President's Advisory Commission on Educational Excellence for African Americans (PACEEAA). The notice also describes the functions of the PACEEAA. Notice of the meeting is required by Sec. 10(a)(2) of the Federal Advisory Committee Act and intended to notify the public of its opportunity to attend.
