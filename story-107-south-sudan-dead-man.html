BREAKING: South Sudan's Leader Sacks Aides After Dead Man Appointed to Position

Published: February 4, 2026 | Source: BBC

South Sudan's leader has fired aides after the embarrassing revelation that a deceased person was appointed to an official position.

Administrative Failure

The incident exposed serious failures in the government's vetting process and record-keeping systems.

Staff Dismissed

Several aides involved in the appointment process have been removed from their positions following the scandal.

The incident highlights the ongoing administrative challenges facing South Sudan's government as it tries to build state institutions.
