BREAKING: Federal Register: Special Local Regulations; Marine Events Within the Fifth Coast Guard District

Published: 2024-10-17 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard will enforce a special local regulation for the Manasquan Inlet Intracoastal Tug on October 13, 2024, with a rain date of October 19, 2024, to provide for the safety of life on navigable waterways during this event. Our regulation for marine events within the Fifth Coast Guard District identifies the regulated area for this event in Manasquan, NJ and Point Pleasant Beach, NJ.
