BREAKING: Federal Register: Record of Decision for the Final Supplemental Environmental Impact Statement for the Roybal Campus 2025 Master Plan; Correction

Published: 2022-12-20 | Source: Federal Register

On November 17, 2022, the Centers for Disease Control and Prevention (CDC), within the Department of Health and Human Services (HHS), published a notice in the Federal Register announcing the Record of Decision (ROD) for the Final Supplemental Environmental Impact Statement (SEIS) for CDC's Roybal Campus in Atlanta, Georgia. In the Decision section of the notice, the description of the incinerator was incorrect.
