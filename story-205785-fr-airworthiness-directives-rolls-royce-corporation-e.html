BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Rolls-Royce Corporation Engines

Published: 2018-04-30 | Source: Federal Register

We propose to adopt a new airworthiness directive (AD) for Rolls-Royce Corporation (RRC) AE 2100D2A and AE 2100D3 model turboprop engines and AE 3007A2 model turbofan engines. This proposed AD was prompted by the possibility of a low-cycle fatigue failure on certain turbine wheels.
