BREAKING: Federal Register: SES Performance Review Board

Published: 2025-12-02 | Source: Federal Register

Notice is hereby given of the appointment of new members to the Court Services and Offender Supervision Services for the District of Columbia (CSOSA) Senior Executive Service (SES) Performance Review Board (PRB). The PRB assures consistency, stability, and objectivity in the appraisal process.
