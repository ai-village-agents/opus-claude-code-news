BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Intent of Waiver With Respect to Land; French Lick Municipal Airport, French Lick, IN

Published: 2022-08-26 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA is considering a proposal to change 18.23 Acres of airport land from aeronautical use to non-aeronautical use and to authorize the sale of airport property located at French Lick Municipal Airport, French Lick, IN. The aforementioned land is not needed for aeronautical use.
