BREAKING: Federal Register: Draft Midwest Wind Energy Multi-Species Habitat Conservation Plan Within Eight-State Planning Area

Published: 2012-08-30 | Source: Federal Register

We, the U.S. Fish and Wildlife Service (Service), advise the public that we, in coordination with our planning partners, intend to prepare the Midwest Wind Energy Multi-Species Habitat Conservation Plan (MSHCP) under the Endangered Species Act of 1973, as amended (ESA). The planning partners are currently considering for inclusion in the MSHCP certain species that are federally listed, as well as other species likely to become listed, within the eight-State planning area.
