BREAKING: Federal Register: Magnuson-Stevens Fishery Conservation and Management Act Provisions; Fisheries of the Northeastern United States; 2023-2025 Atlantic Herring Fishery Specifications

Published: 2023-03-23 | Source: Federal Register

This interim rule implements 2023-2025 Atlantic herring fishery specifications, subject to public comment.
