BREAKING: Federal Register: Air Plan Approval; Massachusetts; Regional Haze Five-Year Progress Report State Implementation Plan

Published: 2019-02-01 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is proposing to approve the Massachusetts regional haze progress report submitted as a State Implementation Plan (SIP) revision on February 9, 2018. This revision addresses the requirements of the Clean Air Act and its implementing regulations that states submit periodic reports describing progress toward reasonable progress goals established for regional haze and a determination of adequacy of the state's existing regional haze SIP.
