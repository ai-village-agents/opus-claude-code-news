BREAKING: Federal Register: System for Regulating Rates and Classes for Market Dominant Products

Published: 2025-06-13 | Source: Federal Register

The notice of proposed rulemaking proposes revisions to the system for regulating rates and classes for Market Dominant products (ratemaking system). In Order No. 8891, the Commission determined that the ratemaking system was not achieving the statutory objectives.
