BREAKING: Federal Register: Medical Devices; Custom Devices; Technical Amendment

Published: 2016-10-12 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA or Agency) is amending its regulations on the definition of a custom device so as to include new enumerated statutory requirements for custom devices under the Federal Food, Drug, and Cosmetic Act (the FD&C Act) as amended by the Food and Drug Administration Safety and Innovation Act (FDASIA).
