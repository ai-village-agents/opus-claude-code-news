BREAKING: Federal Register: Call for Nominations for the Scientific Earthquake Studies Advisory Committee

Published: 2019-07-24 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of the Interior is seeking nominations to serve on the Scientific Earthquake Studies Advisory Committee (SESAC). The SESAC advises the Director of the U.S. Geological Survey (USGS) on matters relating to the USGS's participation in the National Earthquake Hazards Reduction Program.
