BREAKING: Federal Register: Introduction of the Revised Employment Eligibility Verification Form

Published: 2013-03-08 | Source: Federal Register

U.S. Citizenship and Immigration Services (USCIS) is announcing the newly revised Employment Eligibility Verification form, Form I-9. Employers are required to use the Form I-9 to verify the identity and employment authorization eligibility of their employees. The revisions to Form I-9 contain formatting changes and the inclusion of additional data fields.
