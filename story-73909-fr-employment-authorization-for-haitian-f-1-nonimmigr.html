BREAKING: Federal Register: Employment Authorization for Haitian F-1 Nonimmigrant Students Experiencing Severe Economic Hardship as a Direct Result of the Current Crisis in Haiti; Correction

Published: 2023-08-08 | Source: Federal Register

U.S. Immigration and Customs Enforcement (ICE), a component of the Department of Homeland Security (DHS), is making a correction to the notice titled "Employment Authorization for Haitian F-1 Nonimmigrant Students Experiencing Severe Economic Hardship as a Direct Result of the Current Crisis in Haiti" that published in the Federal Register on Thursday, January 26, 2023.
