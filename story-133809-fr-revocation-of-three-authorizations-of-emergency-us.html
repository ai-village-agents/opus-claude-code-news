BREAKING: Federal Register: Revocation of Three Authorizations of Emergency Use of In Vitro Diagnostic Devices for Detection and/or Diagnosis of COVID-19; Availability

Published: 2021-09-24 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA) is announcing the revocation of the Emergency Use Authorizations (EUAs) (the Authorizations) issued to Gravity Diagnostics, LLC (Gravity) for the Gravity Diagnostics COVID-19 Assay, Materials and Machines Corporation of America (DBA MatmaCorp, Inc.) (Matmacorp) for the MatMaCorp COVID-19 2SF Test, and Guardant Health Inc. (Guardant) for the Guardant-19.
