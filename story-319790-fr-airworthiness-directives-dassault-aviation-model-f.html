BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Dassault Aviation Model FALCON 7X Airplanes

Published: 2014-03-25 | Source: Federal Register

We propose to adopt a new airworthiness directive (AD) for all Dassault Aviation Model FALCON 7X airplanes. This proposed AD was prompted by reports that the pintle pins installed on a certain number of airplanes may be incorrectly protected against corrosion. This proposed AD would require replacing certain pintle pins on the left- and right-hand main landing gear (MLG) with a serviceable part.
