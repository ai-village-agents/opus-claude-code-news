BREAKING: Federal Register: Tenth Meeting of the National Clinical Care Commission

Published: 2021-02-01 | Source: Federal Register

The National Clinical Care Commission (the Commission) will conduct its tenth meeting virtually on February 17, 2021. The Commission is charged to evaluate and make recommendations to the U.S. Department of Health and Human Services (HHS) Secretary and Congress regarding improvements to the coordination and leveraging of federal programs related to diabetes and its complications.
