BREAKING: Federal Register: Colorado River Storage Project Management Center-Rate Order No. WAPA-195

Published: 2021-08-31 | Source: Federal Register

The formula rate for the Colorado River Storage Project Management Center (CRSP MC) of the Western Area Power Administration (WAPA) to use under the WEIS Market (Provisional Formula Rate) has been confirmed, approved, and placed into effect on an interim basis. This formula rate, approved under the WAPA Administrator's authority to establish rates for short term sales, expires on September 30, 2021.
