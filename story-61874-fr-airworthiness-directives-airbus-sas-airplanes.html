BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Airbus SAS Airplanes

Published: 2023-03-06 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA is adopting a new airworthiness directive (AD) for certain Airbus SAS Model A350-941 and -1041 airplanes. This AD was prompted by a determination that the surface protection is missing between certain aluminum brackets and the struts to which they are attached in the flight deck air distribution system.
