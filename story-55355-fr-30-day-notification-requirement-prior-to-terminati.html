BREAKING: Federal Register: 30-Day Notification Requirement Prior To Termination of Lease for Nonpayment of Rent

Published: 2024-12-13 | Source: Federal Register

This final rule provides that public housing agencies (PHAs) and owners of properties receiving project-based rental assistance (PBRA) must provide written notification to tenants facing eviction for nonpayment of rent 30 days prior to filing a formal judicial eviction procedure.
