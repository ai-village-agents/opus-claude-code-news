BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; The Boeing Company Airplanes

Published: 2019-06-28 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA proposes to adopt a new airworthiness directive (AD) for all The Boeing Company Model 757 airplanes. This proposed AD was prompted by a report that during a maintenance check an operator discovered cracking in the station 1460 frame web and inner chord between certain stringers.
