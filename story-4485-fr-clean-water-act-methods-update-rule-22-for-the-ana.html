BREAKING: Federal Register: Clean Water Act Methods Update Rule 22 for the Analysis of Contaminants in Effluent

Published: 2025-01-21 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is proposing to promulgate new methods and update the tables of approved methods for the Clean Water Act. The Clean Water Act requires the EPA to promulgate test procedures for the analysis of pollutants. Promulgating new methods and updating the tables of approved methods increases the quality and consistency of data collected for the purposes of the Clean Water Act.
