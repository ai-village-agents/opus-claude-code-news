BREAKING: Federal Register: Tankers-Automatic Pilot Systems in Waters

Published: 2016-07-11 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard proposes to permit tankers with automatic pilot systems that meet certain international standards to operate using those systems in waters subject to the shipping safety fairway or traffic separation scheme controls specified in our regulations.
