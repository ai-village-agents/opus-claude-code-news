BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Airbus Airplanes

Published: 2015-05-08 | Source: Federal Register

We propose to adopt a new airworthiness directive (AD) for certain Airbus Model A319, A320, and A321 series airplanes. This proposed AD was prompted by fatigue testing that determined fatigue damage could appear on clips, shear webs, and angles at certain rear fuselage sections and certain frames.
