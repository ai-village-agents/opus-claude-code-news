BREAKING: Federal Register: Special Local Regulation; Sail Grand Prix, Upper Bay, New York City, NY

Published: 2025-06-06 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is establishing a special local regulation in the Upper Bay of New York Harbor in support of a racing event, Sail Grand Prix 2025. This special local regulation is necessary to provide for the safety of life from the dangers associated with high-speed sailing during the event.
