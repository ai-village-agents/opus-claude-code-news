BREAKING: Federal Register: Program for Parallel Review of Medical Devices

Published: 2016-10-24 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA) and the Centers for Medicare & Medicaid Services (CMS) (the Agencies) are informing the public that the Parallel Review of medical devices pilot program will be fully implemented and extended indefinitely. The Agencies are soliciting nominations from manufacturers of innovative medical devices to participate in the "Program for Parallel Review of Medical Devices."
