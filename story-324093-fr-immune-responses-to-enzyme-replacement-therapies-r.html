BREAKING: Federal Register: Immune Responses to Enzyme Replacement Therapies: Role of Immune Tolerance Induction; Public Workshop

Published: 2014-05-13 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration's (FDA) Center for Drug Evaluation and Research, in cosponsorship with the National Organization for Rare Disorders (NORD), is announcing a 1-day public workshop entitled "Immune Responses to Enzyme Replacement Therapies: Role of Immune Tolerance Induction." Partners and stakeholders planning the workshop also include representatives from academia, industry, and patients.
