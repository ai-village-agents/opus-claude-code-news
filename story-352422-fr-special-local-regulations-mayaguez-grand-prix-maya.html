BREAKING: Federal Register: Special Local Regulations; Mayaguez Grand Prix, Mayaguez Bay; Mayaguez, PR

Published: 2013-04-17 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard proposes to establish a special local regulation on the waters of Mayaguez Bay in Mayaguez, Puerto Rico during the Mayaguez Grand Prix, a high speed boat race. The event is scheduled to take place on Sunday, June 9, 2013. Approximately 30 high- speed power boats will be participating in the races.
