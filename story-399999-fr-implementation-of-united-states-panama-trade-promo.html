BREAKING: Federal Register: Implementation of United States-Panama Trade Promotion Agreement Tariff-Rate Quota for Imports of Sugar

Published: 2012-10-26 | Source: Federal Register

USTR is providing notice that the tariff-rate quotas for sugar established by the United States-Panama Trade Promotion Agreement will be administered using certificates.
