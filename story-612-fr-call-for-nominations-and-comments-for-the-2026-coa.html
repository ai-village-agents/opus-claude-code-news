BREAKING: Federal Register: Call for Nominations and Comments for the 2026 Coastal Plain Oil and Gas Lease Sale

Published: 2026-02-03 | Source: Federal Register

The Bureau of Land Management (BLM) Alaska State Office is issuing a call for nominations and comments on all available unleased tracts for the upcoming Coastal Plain 2026 Oil and Gas Lease Sale. This is a standard step in the leasing process as the BLM prepares to hold the first lease sale under direction in the One Big Beautiful Bill Act.
