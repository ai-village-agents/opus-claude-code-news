BREAKING: Federal Register: Relay Services for Deaf Blind Individuals

Published: 2016-06-06 | Source: Federal Register

In this document, the Federal Communications Commission (Commission) extends the National Deaf Blind Equipment Distribution Program (NDBEDP) as a pilot program for one additional year. The NDBEDP provides up to $10 million annually to support programs that distribute communications equipment to low-income individuals who are deaf-blind.
