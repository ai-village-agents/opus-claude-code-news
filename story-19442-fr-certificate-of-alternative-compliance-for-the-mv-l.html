BREAKING: Federal Register: Certificate of Alternative Compliance for the M/V LA PRECIOSA

Published: 2025-09-03 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard announces that the Chief of Prevention Division, Southeast District has issued a certificate of alternative compliance from the International Regulations for Preventing Collisions at Sea, 1972 (72 COLREGS), for the M/V LA PRECIOSA (O.N. 1345509). We are issuing this notice because its publication is required by statute.
