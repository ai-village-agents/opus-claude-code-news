BREAKING: Federal Register: Fisheries of the Northeastern United States; Atlantic Sea Scallop Fishery; Amendment 19

Published: 2016-11-03 | Source: Federal Register

This final rule implements Amendment 19 to the Atlantic Sea Scallop Fishery Management Plan, which the New England Fishery Management Council adopted and submitted to NMFS for approval. Amendment 19 establishes a specifications process outside of the current framework adjustment process and adjusts the start of the scallop fishing year from March 1 to April 1.
