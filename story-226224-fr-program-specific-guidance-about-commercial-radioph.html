BREAKING: Federal Register: Program-Specific Guidance About Commercial Radiopharmacy Licenses

Published: 2017-01-24 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Nuclear Regulatory Commission (NRC) is revising its licensing guidance for licenses authorizing commercial nuclear pharmacy use of byproduct material. The NRC is requesting public comment on draft NUREG-1556, Volume 13, Revision 2, "Consolidated Guidance About Materials Licenses: Program-Specific Guidance About Commercial Radiopharmacy Licenses."
