BREAKING: Federal Register: Certain Electronic Anti-Theft Shopping Cart Wheels, Components Thereof and Systems Containing the Same; Notice of Commission Decision Not To Review an Initial Determination Terminating the Investigation Based on Settlement; Termination of the Investigation

Published: 2023-10-17 | Source: Federal Register

Notice is hereby given that the U.S. International Trade Commission has determined not to review an initial determination ("ID") (Order No. 13) of the presiding Administrative Law Judge ("ALJ") terminating the investigation in its entirety based on settlement. The investigation is terminated.
