BREAKING: Federal Register: Agency Information Collection Activities: Proposed Collection; Comment Request

Published: 2014-03-11 | Source: Federal Register

NARA is giving public notice that the agency proposes to request extension of two currently approved information collections. The first information collection is used to advise requesters of (1) the correct procedures to follow when requesting certified copies of records for use in civil litigation or criminal actions in courts of law, and (2) the information to be provided so that records may be identified.
