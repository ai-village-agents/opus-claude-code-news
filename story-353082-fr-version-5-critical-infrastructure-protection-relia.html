BREAKING: Federal Register: Version 5 Critical Infrastructure Protection Reliability Standards

Published: 2013-04-24 | Source: Federal Register

Pursuant to section 215 of the Federal Power Act, the Commission proposes to approve the Version 5 Critical Infrastructure Protection Reliability Standards, CIP-002-5 through CIP-011-1, submitted by the North American Electric Reliability Corporation, the Commission-certified Electric Reliability Organization.
