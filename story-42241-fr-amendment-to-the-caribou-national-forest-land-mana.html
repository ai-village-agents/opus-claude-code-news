BREAKING: Federal Register: Amendment to the Caribou National Forest Land Management Plan

Published: 2024-07-19 | Source: Federal Register

The Forest Service, U.S. Department of Agriculture, is amending the Caribou National Forest Land Management Plan (forest plan). The Forest Service has prepared a Final Supplemental Environmental Impact Statement for the Crow Creek Pipeline Project Lower Valley Energy and draft record of decision that includes a programmatic plan amendment to the forest plan.
