BREAKING: Federal Register: Palmetto Railways Request for Approval of a Railroad Safety Program Plan and Product Safety Plan

Published: 2022-06-13 | Source: Federal Register

This document provides the public with notice that, on August 18, 2021, Palmetto Railways submitted a request for FRA approval of its Railroad Safety Program Plan (RSPP) and Product Safety Plan (PSP) for use of the RailSoft TrackAccess system in autonomous mode on its Charity Church Subdivision. As this request for FRA's approval involves a railroad's PSP, FRA is publishing this notice and inviting public comment on the document.
