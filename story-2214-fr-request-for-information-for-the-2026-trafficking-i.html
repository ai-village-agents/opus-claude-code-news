BREAKING: Federal Register: Request for Information for the 2026 Trafficking in Persons Report

Published: 2026-01-14 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of State ("the Department") submits this annual request to the public, through the Federal Register, seeking written information to assist in reporting on the degree to which the United States and foreign governments meet the minimum standards for the elimination of trafficking in persons ("minimum standards") that are prescribed by the Trafficking Victims Protection Act of 2000.
