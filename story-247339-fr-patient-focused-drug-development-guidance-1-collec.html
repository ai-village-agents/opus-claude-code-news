BREAKING: Federal Register: Patient-Focused Drug Development: Guidance 1-Collecting Comprehensive and Representative Input; Public Workshop; Request for Comments

Published: 2017-10-27 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA) is announcing a public workshop to convene a discussion on methodological approaches that a person seeking to collect patient experience data for submission to FDA to inform regulatory decision making may use.
