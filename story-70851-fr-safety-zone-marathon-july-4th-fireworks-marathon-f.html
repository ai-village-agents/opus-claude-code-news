BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone; Marathon July 4th Fireworks, Marathon, FL

Published: 2023-06-28 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is establishing a temporary safety zone on the waters surrounding Marathon, Florida, during the Marathon July 4th Fireworks event. The safety zone will encompass a 200-yard radius on Sombrero Beach. The safety zone is necessary to ensure the safety of participant vessels, spectators, and the general public during the event.
