BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Changes to Application Procedures for the Cuban Family Reunification Parole Program

Published: 2014-12-18 | Source: Federal Register

U.S. Citizenship and Immigration Services (USCIS) announces that as of February 17, 2015, USCIS will begin requiring the filing of an Application for Travel Document (Form I-131) and payment of its associated fee or approval of a fee waiver request from individuals who are applying for the Cuban Family Reunification Parole (CFRP) Program on behalf of a beneficiary in Cuba.
