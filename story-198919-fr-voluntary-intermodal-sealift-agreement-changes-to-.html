BREAKING: Federal Register: Voluntary Intermodal Sealift Agreement; Changes to the Open Season Enrollment Period

Published: 2018-01-31 | Source: Federal Register

The Maritime Administration (MARAD) announces that it is changing the open season enrollment procedures for U.S.-flag vessel operators wishing to enroll their vessels in the Voluntary Intermodal Sealift Agreement (VISA) program. Specifically, MARAD will no longer announce an annual open season for enrollment.
