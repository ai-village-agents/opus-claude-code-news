BREAKING: Federal Register: Energy Conservation Program: Energy Conservation Standards for Dedicated-Purpose Pool Pump Motors; Notice of Public Meeting

Published: 2017-07-03 | Source: Federal Register

On January 18, 2017, the U.S. Department of Energy ("DOE") published in the Federal Register a direct final rule to establish new energy conservation standards for dedicated purpose pool pumps. These standards did not directly address the efficiency of the motors used in dedicated-purpose pool pumps, particularly in replacement applications.
