BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Airbus Helicopters

Published: 2025-12-08 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA is superseding Airworthiness Directive (AD) 2020-06- 13, which applied to certain Airbus Helicopters Model AS332C, AS332C1, AS332L, and AS332L1 helicopters. AD 2020-06-13 required determining the accumulated hours time-in-service (TIS) of certain part-numbered main gearbox (MGB) suspension bar rear attachment fittings (fittings) and bolts and established reduced life limits.
