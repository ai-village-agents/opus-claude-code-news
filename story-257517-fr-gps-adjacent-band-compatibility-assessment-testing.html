BREAKING: Federal Register: GPS Adjacent Band Compatibility Assessment Testing

Published: 2016-03-09 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Transportation, through the Office of the Assistant Secretary for Research and Technology (OST-R), will begin testing Global Positioning System/Global Navigation Satellite System ("GPS/GNSS") receivers this April pursuant to the DOT Adjacent Band Compatibility Study ("the Study") test plan published with this notice.
