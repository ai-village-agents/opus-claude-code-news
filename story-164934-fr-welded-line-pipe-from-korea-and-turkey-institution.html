BREAKING: Federal Register: Welded Line Pipe From Korea and Turkey; Institution of Five-Year Reviews

Published: 2020-11-02 | Source: Federal Register

The Commission hereby gives notice that it has instituted reviews pursuant to the Tariff Act of 1930 ("the Act"), as amended, to determine whether revocation of the countervailing duty order on welded line pipe from Turkey and the antidumping duty orders on welded line pipe from Korea and Turkey would be likely to lead to continuation or recurrence of material injury.
