BREAKING: Federal Register: Submission for OMB Review; Identifying and Addressing Human Trafficking in Child Welfare Agencies (New Collection)

Published: 2020-10-20 | Source: Federal Register

The Administration for Children and Families (ACF) within the U.S. Department of Health and Human Services (HHS) is proposing to collect data on child welfare agencies' efforts to identify human trafficking and subsequent service delivery.
