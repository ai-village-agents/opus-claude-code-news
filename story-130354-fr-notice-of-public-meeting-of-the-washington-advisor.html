BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Public Meeting of the Washington Advisory Committee

Published: 2021-08-12 | Source: Federal Register

Notice is hereby given, pursuant to the provisions of the rules and regulations of the U.S. Commission on Civil Rights (Commission) and the Federal Advisory Committee Act that the Washington Advisory Committee (Committee) will hold a series of meetings via web teleconference on the dates and times listed below for the purpose of reviewing the latest draft of their report on police use of force and barriers to accountability.
