BREAKING: Federal Register: Regional AIDS Education and Training Centers Program Supplemental Award

Published: 2023-05-15 | Source: Federal Register

HRSA will provide supplemental funding to UMass, a current Regional AETC Program award recipient, for a 1-year period of performance and 1-year budget period to support a program designed to train internal and family medicine residents to specialize in HIV treatment and care management.
