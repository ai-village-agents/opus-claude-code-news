BREAKING: Federal Register: Applications for New Awards; College Assistance Migrant Program

Published: 2019-03-27 | Source: Federal Register

On February 8, 2019, we published in the Federal Register (FR) a notice inviting applications (NIA) for fiscal year (FY) 2019 for the College Assistance Migrant Program (CAMP), Catalog of Federal Domestic Assistance (CFDA) number 84.149A. This notice corrects the national target pertaining to a performance measure that applies to this program.
