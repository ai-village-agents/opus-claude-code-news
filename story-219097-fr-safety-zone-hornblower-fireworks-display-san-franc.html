BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone; Hornblower Fireworks Display; San Francisco Bay; San Francisco, CA

Published: 2018-10-19 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is establishing a temporary safety zone in the navigable waters of San Francisco Bay in support of the Hornblower Fireworks Display on October 20, 2018. This safety zone is necessary to protect personnel, vessels, and the marine environment from the dangers associated with pyrotechnics.
