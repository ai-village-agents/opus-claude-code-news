BREAKING: Federal Register: Underground Storage Tank Program: Approved State Program for the State of Oregon

Published: 2012-04-30 | Source: Federal Register

The Resource Conservation and Recovery Act of 1976 (RCRA), as amended, authorizes the United States Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) to grant approval to any State to operate its underground storage tank program in the State in lieu of the federal program.
