BREAKING: Federal Register: Improving Mail Management Policies, Procedures, and Activities

Published: 2012-05-29 | Source: Federal Register

The General Services Administration (GSA) has issued Federal Management Regulation (FMR) Bulletin G-03 which provides guidance to Executive Branch agencies for improving mail management policies, procedures, and activities. FMR Bulletin G-03 and all other FMR Bulletins may be found at http://www.gsa.gov/portal/content/ 102955#MailManagement.
