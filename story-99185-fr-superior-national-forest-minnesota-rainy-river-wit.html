BREAKING: Federal Register: Superior National Forest; Minnesota; Rainy River Withdrawal Environmental Assessment

Published: 2022-06-28 | Source: Federal Register

The Forest Service, U.S. Department of Agriculture, has prepared an environmental assessment to support its application for a 20-year withdrawal of the Rainy River watershed from disposition of Federally owned minerals under United States mineral and geothermal leasing laws.
