BREAKING: Federal Register: Privacy Act of 1974: Systems of Records.

Published: 2012-10-31 | Source: Federal Register

In accordance with the requirements of the Privacy Act of 1974, as amended, 5 U.S.C. 552a, the Securities and Exchange Commission ("Commission" or "SEC") proposes to establish a new system of records, "Backup Care Employee and Family Records (SEC-66)." Additionally, two existing systems of records are being revised: "Freedom of Information Act Requests (SEC-24)" last published in the Federal Register Volume 62, Number 176 on Thursday, September 11, 1997 and "Child Care Subsidy Program (SEC-41)",...
