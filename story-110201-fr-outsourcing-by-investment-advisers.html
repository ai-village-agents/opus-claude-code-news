BREAKING: Federal Register: Outsourcing by Investment Advisers

Published: 2022-11-16 | Source: Federal Register

The Securities and Exchange Commission ("Commission" or "SEC") is proposing a new rule under the Investment Advisers Act of 1940 ("Advisers Act") to prohibit registered investment advisers ("advisers") from outsourcing certain services or functions without first meeting minimum requirements. The proposed rule would require advisers to conduct due diligence prior to engaging a service provider to perform certain services or functions.
