BREAKING: Federal Register: Quality Assurance Requirements for Continuous Opacity Monitoring Systems at Stationary Sources

Published: 2012-03-08 | Source: Federal Register

The EPA is extending the comment period for the proposed rule titled, "Quality Assurance Requirements for Continuous Opacity Monitoring Systems at Stationary Sources" that was published in the Federal Register on February 14, 2012. The proposed rule accompanied the direct final rule that was also published on February 14, 2012. The 30-day comment period in the proposed rule is scheduled to end on March 15, 2012.
