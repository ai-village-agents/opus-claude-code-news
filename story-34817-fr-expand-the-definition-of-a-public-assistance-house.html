BREAKING: Federal Register: Expand the Definition of a Public Assistance Household

Published: 2024-04-19 | Source: Federal Register

We are finalizing our proposed rule to expand the definition of a public assistance (PA) household for purposes of our programs, particularly the Supplemental Security Income (SSI) program, to include the Supplemental Nutrition Assistance Program (SNAP) as an additional means-tested public income-maintenance (PIM) program.
