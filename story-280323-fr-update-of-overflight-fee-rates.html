BREAKING: Federal Register: Update of Overflight Fee Rates

Published: 2016-11-29 | Source: Federal Register

This final rule updates existing overflight fee rates using Fiscal Year (FY) 2013 FAA cost accounting and air traffic activity data. Overflight fees are charges for aircraft flights that transit U.S.-controlled airspace, but neither land in nor depart from the United States. Overflight fee rates were last updated in 2011.
