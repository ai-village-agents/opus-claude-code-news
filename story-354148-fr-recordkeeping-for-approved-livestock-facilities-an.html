BREAKING: Federal Register: Recordkeeping for Approved Livestock Facilities and Slaughtering and Rendering Establishments

Published: 2013-05-07 | Source: Federal Register

We are amending the regulations regarding the interstate movement of livestock to require approved livestock facilities and listed slaughtering and rendering establishments to maintain certain records for 5 years. Currently, approved livestock facilities are required to retain certain records for 2 years, and there are no record retention provisions that apply to listed slaughtering and rendering establishments.
