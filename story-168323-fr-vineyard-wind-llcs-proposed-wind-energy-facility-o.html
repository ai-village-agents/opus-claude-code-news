BREAKING: Federal Register: Vineyard Wind LLC's Proposed Wind Energy Facility Offshore Massachusetts

Published: 2020-12-16 | Source: Federal Register

The preparation of an Environment Impact Statement (EIS) for the Construction and Operations Plan (COP) submitted by Vineyard Wind LLC (Vineyard Wind) concerning the construction and operation of an 800 megawatt wind energy facility offshore Massachusetts (Vineyard Wind 1 Project) is no longer necessary and the process is hereby terminated.
