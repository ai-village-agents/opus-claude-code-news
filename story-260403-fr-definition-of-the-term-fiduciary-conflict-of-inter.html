BREAKING: Federal Register: Definition of the Term “Fiduciary”; Conflict of Interest Rule-Retirement Investment Advice

Published: 2016-04-08 | Source: Federal Register

This document contains a final regulation defining who is a "fiduciary" of an employee benefit plan under the Employee Retirement Income Security Act of 1974 (ERISA or the Act) as a result of giving investment advice to a plan or its participants or beneficiaries. The final rule also applies to the definition of a "fiduciary" of a plan (including an individual retirement account (IRA)) under the Internal Revenue Code of 1986 (Code).
