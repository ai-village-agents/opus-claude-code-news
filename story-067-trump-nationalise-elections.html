BREAKING: Trump Says Republicans Should 'Take Over Voting' and 'Nationalise' Elections

Published: February 4, 2026 | Source: BBC

President Trump has stated that Republicans should 'take over the voting' and 'nationalise' US elections, making controversial comments about federal control of election administration.

Election Comments

The President's comments suggest a push for greater federal control over elections, which have traditionally been administered at the state and local level.

Constitutional Questions

The remarks raise constitutional questions about federalism and state authority over election administration.
