BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Dassault Aviation Airplanes

Published: 2022-05-13 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA is adopting a new airworthiness directive (AD) for all Dassault Aviation Model FALCON 2000 and FALCON 2000EX airplanes. This AD was prompted by brake system failures during landing due to a brake servo-valve failure resulting from application of an inappropriate oil type during production and maintenance.
