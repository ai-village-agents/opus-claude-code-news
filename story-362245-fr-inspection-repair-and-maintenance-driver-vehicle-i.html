BREAKING: Federal Register: Inspection, Repair, and Maintenance; Driver-Vehicle Inspection Report

Published: 2013-08-07 | Source: Federal Register

FMCSA proposes to rescind the requirement that commercial motor vehicle (CMV) drivers operating in interstate commerce, except drivers of passenger-carrying CMVs, submit, and motor carriers retain, driver-vehicle inspection reports when the driver has neither found nor been made aware of any vehicle defects or deficiencies.
