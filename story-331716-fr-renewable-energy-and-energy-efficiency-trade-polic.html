BREAKING: Federal Register: Renewable Energy and Energy Efficiency Trade Policy Mission to Peru

Published: 2014-08-08 | Source: Federal Register

The United States Department of Commerce, International Trade Administration, U.S. and Foreign Commercial Service is amending the Notice published at 79 FR 28683, May 19, 2014, for the Renewable Energy and Energy Efficiency Trade Policy Mission to Peru originally scheduled for November 12-13, 2014, in Lima, Peru, to revise the mission description to notify potential applicants that the mission will be led by an Executive official, rather than a senior official, of the Department of Commerce.
