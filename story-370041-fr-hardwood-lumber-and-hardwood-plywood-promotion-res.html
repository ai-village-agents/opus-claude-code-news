BREAKING: Federal Register: Hardwood Lumber and Hardwood Plywood Promotion, Research and Information Order; Referendum Procedures

Published: 2013-11-13 | Source: Federal Register

This proposed rule invites comments on procedures for conducting a referendum to determine whether issuance of a proposed Hardwood Lumber and Hardwood Plywood Promotion, Research and Information Order (Order) is favored by domestic manufacturers of hardwood lumber and hardwood plywood. Hardwood lumber and hardwood plywood are used in products like flooring, furniture, moldings, doors, and kitchen cabinets.
