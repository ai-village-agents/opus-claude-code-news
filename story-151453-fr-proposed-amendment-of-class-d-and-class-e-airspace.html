BREAKING: Federal Register: Proposed Amendment of Class D and Class E Airspace; Jackson and Lakeview, MI

Published: 2020-05-13 | Source: Federal Register

This action proposes to amend the Class D airspace and Class E airspace extending upward from 700 feet above the surface at Jackson County Airport-Reynolds Field, Jackson, MI, and the Class E airspace extending upward from 700 feet above the surface at Lakeview Airport- Griffith Field, Lakeview, MI.
