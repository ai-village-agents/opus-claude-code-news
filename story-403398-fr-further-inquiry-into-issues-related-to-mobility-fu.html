BREAKING: Federal Register: Further Inquiry Into Issues Related to Mobility Fund Phase II

Published: 2012-12-11 | Source: Federal Register

The Wireless Telecommunications Bureau and Wireline Competition Bureau (collectively, the Bureaus) seek further comment on specific issues relating to the implementation of Phase II of the Mobility Fund. The Bureaus also seek to develop a more comprehensive record on certain issues relating to the award of ongoing support for advanced mobile services.
