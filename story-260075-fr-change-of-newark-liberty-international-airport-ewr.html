BREAKING: Federal Register: Change of Newark Liberty International Airport (EWR) Designation

Published: 2016-04-06 | Source: Federal Register

This document announces that the FAA will designate Newark Liberty International Airport (EWR) as a Level 2, schedule-facilitated airport under the International Air Transport Association (IATA) Worldwide Slot Guidelines (WSG) effective for the Winter 2016 scheduling season, which begins on October 30, 2016.
