BREAKING: Federal Register: Special Local Regulations; Lake Havasu Triathlon, Lake Havasu, AZ

Published: 2024-03-08 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard will enforce the Lake Havasu Triathlon special local regulations on the waters of the Colorado River on March 17, 2024. These special local regulations are necessary to provide for the safety of the participants, crew, spectators, sponsor vessels, and general users of the waterway.
