BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Intent To Grant an Exclusive Patent License

Published: 2023-06-12 | Source: Federal Register

Pursuant to the Bayh-Dole Act and implementing regulations, the Department of the Air Force hereby gives notice of its intent to grant an exclusive patent license to Advanced Cooling Technologies, Inc., having a place of business at 1046 New Holland Avenue, Lancaster, PA 17601.
