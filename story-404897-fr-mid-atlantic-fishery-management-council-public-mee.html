BREAKING: Federal Register: Mid-Atlantic Fishery Management Council; Public Meeting

Published: 2012-12-31 | Source: Federal Register

The Mid-Atlantic Fishery Management Council (Council) announces its intent to hold a workshop. The purpose of the workshop is to consider options for improving the management of the longfin and Illex squid fisheries, with a focus on responsive harvest strategies that account for changing stock conditions over the course of the year.
