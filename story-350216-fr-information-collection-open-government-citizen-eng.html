BREAKING: Federal Register: Information Collection; Open Government Citizen Engagement Ratings, Rankings, and Flagging

Published: 2013-03-25 | Source: Federal Register

Under the provisions of the Paperwork Reduction Act, the Regulatory Secretariat will be submitting to the Office of Management and Budget (OMB) a request to review and approve a previously approved information collection requirement regarding open government citizen engagement ratings, rankings, and flagging.
