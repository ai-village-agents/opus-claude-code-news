BREAKING: Federal Register: Request for Nominations for Board of Governors of the Patient-Centered Outcomes Research Institute (PCORI)

Published: 2016-06-17 | Source: Federal Register

The Patient Protection and Affordable Care Act gave the Comptroller General of the United States responsibility for appointing 19 members to the Board of Governors of the Patient-Centered Outcomes Research Institute. In addition, the Directors of the Agency for Healthcare Research and Quality and the National Institutes of Health, or their designees, are members of the Board.
