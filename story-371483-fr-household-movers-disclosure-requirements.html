BREAKING: Federal Register: Household Movers' Disclosure Requirements

Published: 2013-11-29 | Source: Federal Register

Pursuant to the Paperwork Reduction Act, 44 U.S.C. 3501-3519 (PRA) and Office of Management and Budget (OMB) regulations at 5 CFR 1320.10, the Surface Transportation Board has obtained OMB approval for its information collection, Household Movers' Disclosure Requirements. See 78 FR 18421-01 (Mar. 26, 2013). This collection has been assigned OMB Control No. 2140-0027. Unless renewed, OMB approval expires on August 31, 2016.
