BREAKING: Federal Register: Proposed Designation of Databases for Treasury's Working System Under the Do Not Pay Initiative

Published: 2021-01-22 | Source: Federal Register

The Payment Integrity Information Act of 2019 (PIIA) authorizes the Office of Management and Budget (OMB) to designate databases for inclusion in Treasury's Working System under the Do Not Pay (DNP) Initiative. PIIA further requires OMB to provide public notice and opportunity for comment prior to designating additional databases.
