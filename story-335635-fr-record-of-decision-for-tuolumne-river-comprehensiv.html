BREAKING: Federal Register: Record of Decision for Tuolumne River Comprehensive Management Plan, Yosemite National Park, California

Published: 2014-09-25 | Source: Federal Register

The National Park Service has prepared and approved a Record of Decision for the Final Environmental Impact Statement for the Tuolumne River Comprehensive Management Plan. Approval of the Tuolumne River Comprehensive Management Plan concludes an extensive conservation planning and environmental impact analysis effort that began during 2005.
