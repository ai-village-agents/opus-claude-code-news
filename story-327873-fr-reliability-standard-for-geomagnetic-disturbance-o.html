BREAKING: Federal Register: Reliability Standard for Geomagnetic Disturbance Operations

Published: 2014-06-25 | Source: Federal Register

Pursuant to section 215 of the Federal Power Act, the Federal Energy Regulatory Commission (Commission) approves Reliability Standard EOP-010-1 (Geomagnetic Disturbance Operations). The North American Electric Reliability Corporation, the Commission-certified Electric Reliability Organization, submitted the Reliability Standard for Commission approval in response to a Commission directive in Order No. 779.
