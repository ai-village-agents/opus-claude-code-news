BREAKING: Federal Register: Small Business Investment Company Investment Diversification and Growth; Public Webinar

Published: 2023-08-17 | Source: Federal Register

The SBA is holding a webinar to update the public on new regulations governing the Small Business Investment Company (SBIC) program contained in the final rule titled Small Business Investment Company Investment Diversification and Growth that goes into effect on August 17, 2023.
