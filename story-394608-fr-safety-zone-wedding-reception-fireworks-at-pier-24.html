BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone: Wedding Reception Fireworks at Pier 24, San Francisco, CA

Published: 2012-08-23 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is establishing a temporary safety zone in the navigable waters of the San Francisco Bay near Pier 14 in San Francisco, CA in support of the Wedding Reception Fireworks at Pier 24 on August 24, 2012. This safety zone is established to ensure the safety of mariners and spectators from the dangers associated with the pyrotechnics.
