BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Bombardier, Inc. Airplanes

Published: 2014-02-07 | Source: Federal Register

We are superseding Airworthiness Directive (AD) 2011-03- 13 for certain Bombardier, Inc. Model CL-600-2C10 (Regional Jet Series 700, 701, & 702) airplanes, Model CL-600-2D15 (Regional Jet Series 705) airplanes, and Model CL-600-2D24 (Regional Jet Series 900) airplanes. AD 2011-03-13 required repetitive inspections of the rudder travel limiter (RTL) return springs and primary actuator, and corrective actions if necessary.
