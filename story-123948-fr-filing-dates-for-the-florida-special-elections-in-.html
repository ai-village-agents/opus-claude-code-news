BREAKING: Federal Register: Filing Dates for the Florida Special Elections in the 20th Congressional District

Published: 2021-05-18 | Source: Federal Register

Florida has scheduled special elections on November 2, 2021, and January 11, 2022, to fill the U.S. House of Representatives seat in the 20th Congressional District held by the late Representative Alcee Hastings. Committees required to file reports in connection with the Special Primary Election on November 2, 2021, shall file a 12-day Pre- Primary Report.
