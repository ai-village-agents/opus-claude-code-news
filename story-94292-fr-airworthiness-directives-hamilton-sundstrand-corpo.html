BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Hamilton Sundstrand Corporation Propellers

Published: 2022-04-22 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA is superseding Airworthiness Directive (AD) 2020-12-07 for certain Hamilton Sundstrand Corporation (Hamilton Sundstrand) 54H model propellers. AD 2020-12-07 required initial and repetitive eddy current inspections (ECI) of certain propeller blades and replacement of the propeller blades that fail the inspection.
