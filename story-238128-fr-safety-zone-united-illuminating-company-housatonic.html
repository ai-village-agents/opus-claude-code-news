BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone; United Illuminating Company, Housatonic River Crossing Project; Milford and Stratford, CT

Published: 2017-06-30 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is amending a temporary safety zone on the Housatonic River near Milford and Stratford, CT. Amending the safety zone is necessary to protect personnel, vessels, and the marine environment from potential hazards created by the United Illuminating Company Housatonic River Crossing Project.
