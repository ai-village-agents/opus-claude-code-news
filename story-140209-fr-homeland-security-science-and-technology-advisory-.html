BREAKING: Federal Register: Homeland Security Science and Technology Advisory Committee; Correction

Published: 2021-12-16 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Homeland Security (DHS) is correcting a notice published in the Federal Register on November 1, 2021 regarding then re-establishment of the Homeland Security Science and Technology Advisory Committee (HSSTAC). The notice listed incorrect effective and expiration dates for the HSSTAC charter. The correct effective date for the charter should be November 23, 2021 and the correct expiration date should be November 23, 2023.
