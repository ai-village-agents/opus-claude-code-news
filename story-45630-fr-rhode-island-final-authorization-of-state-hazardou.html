BREAKING: Federal Register: Rhode Island: Final Authorization of State Hazardous Waste Management Program; Revisions and Corrections

Published: 2024-08-29 | Source: Federal Register

The State of Rhode Island Department of Environmental Management (RIDEM) has applied to the Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) for final authorization of revisions to its hazardous waste program under the Resource Conservation and Recovery Act (RCRA), as amended. The EPA has reviewed Rhode Island's application and has determined that Rhode Island's hazardous waste program revision satisfies all of the requirements necessary to qualify for final authorization.
