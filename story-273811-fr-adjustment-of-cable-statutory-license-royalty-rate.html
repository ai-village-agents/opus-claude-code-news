BREAKING: Federal Register: Adjustment of Cable Statutory License Royalty Rates

Published: 2016-09-13 | Source: Federal Register

On April 26, 2016, the Copyright Royalty Judges (Judges) published for comment proposed regulations governing royalty rates and terms for the distant retransmission of over-the-air television and radio broadcast stations by cable television systems to their subscribers. The participants in the proceeding concluded their negotiations and asked for readoption of the cable rate regulations without change.
