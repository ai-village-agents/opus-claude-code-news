BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Airbus SAS Airplanes

Published: 2024-12-09 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA is superseding Airworthiness Directive (AD) 2022-24- 05, which applied to all Airbus SAS Model A318, A319, A320, and A321 series airplanes. AD 2022-24-05 required repetitive inspections of certain galleys for corrosion of trolley retainer aluminum blocks and delamination of the upper panel of the trolley compartment, and applicable corrective action.
