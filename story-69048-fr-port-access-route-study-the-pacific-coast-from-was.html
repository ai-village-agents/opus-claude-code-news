BREAKING: Federal Register: Port Access Route Study: The Pacific Coast From Washington to California

Published: 2023-06-05 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard announces the availability of the study results of the Pacific Coast Port Access Route Study. This study evaluated safe access routes for the movement of vessel traffic proceeding to or from ports or places along the western seaboard of the United States.
