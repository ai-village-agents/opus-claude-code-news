BREAKING: Federal Register: Privacy Act of 1974; System of Records

Published: 2020-08-04 | Source: Federal Register

The United States Postal Service\TM\ (USPS\TM\) is proposing to revise one General Privacy Act Systems of Records and one Customer Privacy Act Systems of Records. These updates are being made to facilitate the implementation of web-based collaboration and communication applications.
