BREAKING: Federal Register: Noise Exposure Map Notice; Tampa International Airport (TPA) Airport, Tampa, Florida

Published: 2022-04-22 | Source: Federal Register

The Federal Aviation Administration (FAA) announces its determination that the Noise Exposure Maps (NEMs) submitted by the Hillsborough County Aviation Authority for Tampa International Airport under the provisions of the Aviation Safety and Noise Abatement Act and are in compliance with applicable requirements.
