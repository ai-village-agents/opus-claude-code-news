BREAKING: Federal Register: Change of Publication Manner for Invention Licenses

Published: 2019-09-16 | Source: Federal Register

Currently, the Department of Veterans Affairs (VA) publishes notices of prospective exclusive, co-exclusive, or partially-exclusive domestic or foreign licenses of Government-owned inventions in the Federal Register. VA is announcing that it will begin publishing such notices at the Federal Laboratory Consortium for Technology Transfer (FLC) Business website (http://www.federallabs.org/licenses-list), providing opportunity for filing written objections within at least a 15-day period.
