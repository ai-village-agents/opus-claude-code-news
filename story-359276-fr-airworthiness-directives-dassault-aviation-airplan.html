BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Dassault Aviation Airplanes

Published: 2013-07-03 | Source: Federal Register

We propose to supersede an existing airworthiness directive (AD) that applies to all Dassault Aviation Model FALCON 7X airplanes.
