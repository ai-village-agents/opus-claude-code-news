BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Lycoming Engines Fuel Injected Reciprocating Engines

Published: 2015-09-29 | Source: Federal Register

We are superseding airworthiness directive (AD) 2011-26-04 for certain fuel injected reciprocating engines manufactured by Lycoming Engines. AD 2011-26-04 required inspection, replacement if necessary, and proper clamping of externally mounted fuel injector fuel lines. This new AD retains the requirements of AD 2011-26-04, and expands the list of affected engine models.
