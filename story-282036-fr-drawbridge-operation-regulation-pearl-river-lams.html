BREAKING: Federal Register: Drawbridge Operation Regulation; Pearl River, LA/MS

Published: 2016-12-20 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard has issued a temporary deviation from the operating schedule that governs the operation of the US 90 highway bridge (East Pearl River Bridge), a swing span bridge across the Pearl River, mile 8.8, between Slidell, St. Tammany Parish, Louisiana and Pearlington, Hancock County, Mississippi.
