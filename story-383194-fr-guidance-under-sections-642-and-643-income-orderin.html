BREAKING: Federal Register: Guidance Under Sections 642 and 643 (Income Ordering Rules)

Published: 2012-04-16 | Source: Federal Register

This document contains final regulations under Internal Revenue Code (Code) section 642(c) with regard to the Federal tax consequences of an ordering provision in a trust, a will, or a provision of local law that attempts to determine the tax character of the amounts paid to a charitable beneficiary of the trust or estate.
