BREAKING: Federal Register: Energy Conservation Program: Compliance Date for the Dehumidifier Test Procedure

Published: 2014-02-07 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Energy (DOE) is issuing a final rule that will require manufacturers to test dehumidifiers using the active mode provisions in the test procedure for dehumidifiers currently found in DOE regulations to determine compliance with the existing energy conservation standards.
