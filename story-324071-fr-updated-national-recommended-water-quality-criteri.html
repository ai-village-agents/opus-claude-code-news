BREAKING: Federal Register: Updated National Recommended Water Quality Criteria for the Protection of Human Health

Published: 2014-05-13 | Source: Federal Register

EPA is announcing the availability of draft updated national recommended water quality criteria for the protection of human health for the purpose of obtaining public comments. EPA has updated its national recommended water quality criteria for human health for ninety-four chemical pollutants to reflect the latest scientific information and current EPA policies.
