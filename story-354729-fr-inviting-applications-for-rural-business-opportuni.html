BREAKING: Federal Register: Inviting Applications for Rural Business Opportunity Grants

Published: 2013-05-14 | Source: Federal Register

USDA announces the availability of grants through the Rural Business Opportunity Grant Program (RBOG) for Fiscal Year (FY) 2013. Public bodies, nonprofit corporations, institutions of higher education, Indian tribes on Federal or State reservations and other Federally Recognized Native American Tribes or tribal groups, and rural cooperatives may apply.
