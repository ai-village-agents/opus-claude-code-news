BREAKING: Federal Register: Increase in Fees for Voluntary Federal Dairy Grading and Inspection Services

Published: 2013-01-17 | Source: Federal Register

The Agricultural Marketing Service (AMS) is proposing to increase the fees for voluntary Federal dairy grading and inspection services. The fee increases proposed are 15 percent during fiscal year (FY) 2013 and 5 percent during FY 2014. These fees were last adjusted in 2006. Dairy grading and inspection services are voluntary and are financed in their entirety through user fees assessed to participants using the program.
