BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; The Boeing Company Airplanes

Published: 2013-05-01 | Source: Federal Register

We are adopting a new airworthiness directive (AD) for certain The Boeing Company Model 767-300 series airplanes. This AD was prompted by a report that certain airplanes might not have reinforcement straps installed on the center overhead stowage bins in the passenger compartment, and some installed reinforcement straps might not have been bonded.
