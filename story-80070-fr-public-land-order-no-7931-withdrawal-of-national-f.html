BREAKING: Federal Register: Public Land Order No. 7931; Withdrawal of National Forest System Land in the San Bernardino National Forest; CA

Published: 2023-10-20 | Source: Federal Register

This Public Land Order (PLO) withdraws 2,841 acres of National Forest System lands from location and entry under the United States mining laws, but not from leasing under the mineral or geothermal leasing laws, or disposal under the Mineral Materials Act of 1947, for a period of 50 years, subject to valid existing rights, to maintain, protect, and conserve critical habitat for listed threatened and endangered plant species in the San Bernardino National Forest, California.
