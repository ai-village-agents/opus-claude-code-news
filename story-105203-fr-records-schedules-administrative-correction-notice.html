BREAKING: Federal Register: Records Schedules; Administrative Correction Notice

Published: 2022-09-14 | Source: Federal Register

We are making the following administrative corrections to schedule DAA-0566-2018-0006, which provides disposition authority for United States Citizenship and Immigration Services Form 1-824, Application for Action on an Approved Application or Petition. The schedule covers forms and supporting documentation used to request duplicates of immigration forms and notices, and to request notifications of immigration status be sent to various U.S. government entities, such as U.S. consulates.
