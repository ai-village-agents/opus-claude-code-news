BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Final Revision to Guidance for the Use of Binding Arbitration Under the Administrative Dispute Resolution Act of 1996

Published: 2012-06-11 | Source: Federal Register

Under existing guidance, FMCSA must use a form of arbitration known as "Night Baseball" for its civil penalty forfeiture proceedings in which the only issues remaining to be resolved are the amount of the civil penalty owed and/or the length of time in which to pay it.
