BREAKING: Federal Register: Methane Sulfonic Acid; Exemption from the Requirement of a Tolerance

Published: 2015-08-19 | Source: Federal Register

This regulation establishes an exemption from the requirement of a tolerance for residues of methane sulfonic acid (CAS Reg.
