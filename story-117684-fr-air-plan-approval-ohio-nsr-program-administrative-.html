BREAKING: Federal Register: Air Plan Approval; Ohio; NSR Program Administrative Rules

Published: 2021-02-26 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is proposing to approve, under the Clean Air Act (CAA), new and updated administrative rules for the Ohio State Implementation Plan (SIP) for the New Source Review (NSR) permitting program. The new and amended administrative rules in the Ohio Administrative Code (OAC) would replace the currently effective procedural rules in the NSR SIP in their entirety.
