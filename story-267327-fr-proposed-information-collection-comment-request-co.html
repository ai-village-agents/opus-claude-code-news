BREAKING: Federal Register: Proposed Information Collection; Comment Request; Community Connectivity Initiative Self-Assessment Tool

Published: 2016-06-28 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Commerce, as part of its continuing effort to reduce paperwork and respondent burden as required by the Paperwork Reduction Act of 1995, invites the general public and other federal agencies to take this opportunity to comment on the proposed framework for the community connectivity self-assessment tool.
