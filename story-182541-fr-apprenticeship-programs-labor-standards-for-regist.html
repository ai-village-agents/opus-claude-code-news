BREAKING: Federal Register: Apprenticeship Programs, Labor Standards for Registration, Amendment of Regulations

Published: 2019-06-25 | Source: Federal Register

To address America's skills gap and expand the apprenticeship model to new industries, the U.S. Department of Labor proposes a rule under the National Apprenticeship Act (NAA) to establish a process for recognizing Standards Recognition Entities (SREs), which will in turn recognize Industry-Recognized Apprenticeship Programs (Industry Programs).
