BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Funding Availability for the Section 533 Housing Preservation Grant (HPG) for Fiscal Year 2023

Published: 2023-04-19 | Source: Federal Register

The Rural Housing Service (RHS or the Agency), a Rural Development (RD) mission area agency of the United States Department of Agriculture (USDA), announces the availability of funding under the Housing Preservation Grant (HPG) program for fiscal year (FY) 2023, making available grant funds to sponsoring organizations for the repair or rehabilitation of housing owned or occupied by low- and very-low- income rural citizens under the HPG Program.
