BREAKING: Federal Register: Transport Airplane Fuel Tank and System Lightning Protection

Published: 2014-12-18 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA proposes to amend certain airworthiness regulations for transport category airplanes regarding lightning protection of fuel tanks and systems.
