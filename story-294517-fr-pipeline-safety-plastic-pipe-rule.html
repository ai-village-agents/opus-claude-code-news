BREAKING: Federal Register: Pipeline Safety: Plastic Pipe Rule

Published: 2015-05-21 | Source: Federal Register

PHMSA is proposing to amend the natural and other gas pipeline safety regulations (49 CFR part 192) to address regulatory requirements involving plastic piping systems used in gas services. These proposed amendments are intended to correct errors, address inconsistencies, and respond to petitions for rulemaking.
