BREAKING: Federal Register: Draft Guidance for Institutional Review Boards, Clinical Investigators, and Sponsors: Considerations When Transferring Clinical Investigation Oversight to Another Institutional Review Board; Availability

Published: 2012-06-12 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA) is announcing the availability of a draft guidance entitled "Considerations When Transferring Clinical Investigation Oversight to Another IRB." The draft guidance discusses regulatory responsibilities of institutional review boards (IRBs), clinical investigators, and sponsors when oversight of a previously approved clinical investigation under FDA's jurisdiction is transferred from one IRB to another IRB.
