BREAKING: Federal Register: Control of Immediate Precursor Used in the Illicit Manufacture of Fentanyl as Schedule II Controlled Substances; Correction

Published: 2019-02-07 | Source: Federal Register

On June 29, 2010, the Drug Enforcement Administration (DEA) placed the fentanyl immediate precursor chemical "4-anilino-N- phenethyl-4-piperidine," (CASRN 21409-26-7) into Schedule II of the Controlled Substances Act. It has come to DEA's attention that the drug name listed in the final rule contained a minor error and the drug name should have been "4-anilino-N-phenethylpiperidine (ANPP)."
