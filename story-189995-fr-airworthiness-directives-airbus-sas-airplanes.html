BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Airbus SAS Airplanes

Published: 2019-10-01 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA is superseding Airworthiness Directive (AD) 2018-22- 13, which applied to certain Airbus SAS Model A350-941 and -1041 airplanes. AD 2018-22-13 required revising the existing airplane flight manual (AFM) to provide the flightcrew with updated procedures related to inboard aileron fault operations.
