BREAKING: Federal Register: Certain Lined Paper Products From India: Preliminary Results of Antidumping Duty Administrative Review; Rescission of Administrative Review, in Part; and Preliminary Determination of No Shipments; 2020-2021

Published: 2022-10-06 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Department of Commerce (Commerce) is conducting an administrative review of the antidumping duty order on certain lined paper products from India, covering the period of review (POR), September 1, 2020, through August 31, 2021. We preliminarily find that Cellpage Ventures Private Limited (Cellpage) made sales of subject merchandise at less than normal value during the POR and Navneet Education Ltd. (Navneet) did not.
