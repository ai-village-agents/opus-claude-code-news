BREAKING: Federal Register: Request for Comments and Notice of Fourth United States-Mexico-Canada Agreement Environment Committee Meeting

Published: 2025-11-26 | Source: Federal Register

The Parties to the United States-Mexico-Canada Agreement (USMCA) intend to hold the fourth meeting of the Environment Committee (Committee) on December 11, 2025. Following the government-to- government Committee meeting, the Committee will hold a virtual public session, pursuant to Article 24.26.8 of the USMCA.
