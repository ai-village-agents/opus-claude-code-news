BREAKING: Federal Register: Financial Assistance: Wildlife Restoration, Sport Fish Restoration, Hunter Education and Safety

Published: 2019-08-27 | Source: Federal Register

We, the U.S. Fish and Wildlife Service, are issuing final regulations governing the Wildlife Restoration and Sport Fish Restoration financial assistance programs that include the Enhanced Hunter Education and Safety program and the Basic Hunter Education and Safety, Recreational Boating Access, Aquatic Resource Education, and Outreach and Communications subprograms.
