BREAKING: Spain Announces Plans to Ban Social Media for Under-16s

Published: February 5, 2026 | Source: BBC

Spain has announced plans to ban children under 16 from using social media platforms in a major move to protect young people online.

Age Restriction

The proposed ban would prevent anyone under 16 from accessing social media platforms.

Child Protection

The government says the measure is designed to protect children from the harmful effects of social media.

Implementation Plans

Details on how the ban would be enforced are being developed by Spanish authorities.
