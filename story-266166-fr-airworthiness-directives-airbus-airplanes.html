BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Airbus Airplanes

Published: 2016-06-14 | Source: Federal Register

We are superseding Airworthiness Directive (AD) 2016-09-07 for all Airbus Model A318, A319, A320, and A321 series airplanes. AD 2016- 09-07 required replacing certain pitot probes on the captain, first officer, and standby sides. This new AD retains those requirements, but with a revised compliance time.
