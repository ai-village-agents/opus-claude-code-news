BREAKING: Federal Register: Draft Guidance for Reporting of Chemical Substances When Manufactured or Processed as Nanoscale Materials; Notice of Availability and Request for Comment

Published: 2017-05-16 | Source: Federal Register

With this document, EPA is announcing the availability of and requesting public comment on the draft guidance document, entitled: "Guidance on EPA's Section 8(a) Information Gathering Rule on Nanomaterials in Commerce".
