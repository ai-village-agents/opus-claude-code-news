BREAKING: Federal Register: VA Vocational Rehabilitation and Employment Nomenclature Change for Position Title-Revision

Published: 2023-07-18 | Source: Federal Register

This final rule adopts, without change, an interim final rule published in the Federal Register on November 17, 2017, which affirmed a May 2, 2016, final rule amending Department of Veterans Affairs (VA) regulations to reflect a nomenclature change in the title of certain personnel hired by VA's Veteran Readiness and Employment (VR&E) Service, previously known as Vocational Rehabilitation and Employment Service.
