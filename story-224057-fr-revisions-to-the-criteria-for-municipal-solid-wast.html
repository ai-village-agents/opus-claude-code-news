BREAKING: Federal Register: Revisions to the Criteria for Municipal Solid Waste Landfills To Address Advances in Liquids Management

Published: 2018-12-26 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is considering whether to propose revisions to the criteria for Municipal Solid Waste Landfills (MSWLFs) to support advances in effective liquids management.
