BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone; Lower Mississippi River, New Orleans, LA

Published: 2018-01-29 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is establishing a temporary safety zone for the navigable waters on the Lower Mississippi River between mile marker (MM) 95.6 and MM 96.6 Above Head of Passes (AHP). This safety zone is necessary to protect persons and vessels from potential safety hazards associated with a fireworks display on February 3, 2018.
