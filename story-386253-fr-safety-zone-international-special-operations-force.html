BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone; International Special Operations Forces Week Capability Exercise, Seddon Channel, Tampa, FL

Published: 2012-05-21 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is establishing a temporary safety zone on the waters of Seddon Channel in the vicinity of the Tampa Convention Center in Tampa, Florida during the International Special Operations Forces Week Capability Exercise. The exercise is scheduled to take place on Tuesday, May 22, 2012 and Wednesday, May 23, 2012.
