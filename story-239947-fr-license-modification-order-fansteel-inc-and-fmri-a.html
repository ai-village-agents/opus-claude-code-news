BREAKING: Federal Register: License Modification Order: Fansteel, Inc. and FMRI (a Subsidiary of Reorganized Fansteel)

Published: 2017-07-26 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Nuclear Regulatory Commission (NRC) is issuing an Immediately Effective Order to Fansteel, Inc. and FMRI (a subsidiary of Reorganized Fansteel). The Order modifies License No. SMB-911 to include "Fansteel, Inc." as a co-Licensee with "FMRI (a subsidiary of Fansteel)" for the complex decommissioning site in Muskogee, Oklahoma.
