BREAKING: Federal Register: Port Access Route Study: The Pacific Coast From Washington to California

Published: 2021-07-29 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is conducting a Port Access Route Study (PARS) to evaluate safe access routes for the movement of vessel traffic proceeding to or from ports or places along the western seaboard of the United States and to determine whether a Shipping Safety Fairway ("Fairway") and/or routing measures should be established, adjusted or modified.
