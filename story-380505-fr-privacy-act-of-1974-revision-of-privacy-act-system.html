BREAKING: Federal Register: Privacy Act of 1974: Revision of Privacy Act System of Records

Published: 2012-03-16 | Source: Federal Register

SBA is revising the Privacy Act Systems of Records for the Loan System, SBA 21 ("SOR 21") and the Suspension and Debarment Files, SBA 36 ("SOR 36"), to add new and revised routine uses, to expand the categories of covered individuals and categories of records, and to update the systems' managers and the systems' locations.
