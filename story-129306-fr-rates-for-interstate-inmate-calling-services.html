BREAKING: Federal Register: Rates for Interstate Inmate Calling Services

Published: 2021-07-28 | Source: Federal Register

In this document, the Federal Communications Commission (Commission) reforms its rules for inmate calling services by taking the following steps. The Commission eliminates a separate rate cap for collect calling. The Commission lowers the interim interstate rate caps to $0.12 for prisons and $0.14 for jails with an average daily population of 1,000 or more incarcerated people.
