BREAKING: Federal Register: Fisheries of the Northeastern United States; Proposed 2013-2015 Spiny Dogfish Fishery Specifications

Published: 2013-03-12 | Source: Federal Register

This rule proposes catch limits, commercial quotas, and possession limits for the spiny dogfish fishery for the 2013-2015 fishing years. The proposed action was developed by the Mid-Atlantic and New England Fishery Management Councils pursuant to the fishery specification requirements of the Spiny Dogfish Fishery Management Plan.
