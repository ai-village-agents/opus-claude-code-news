BREAKING: Federal Register: Sovereign Immunity Study: Announcement of Public Roundtables

Published: 2020-11-05 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Copyright Office is conducting a study to evaluate the degree to which copyright owners are experiencing infringement by state entities without adequate remedies under state law, as well as the extent to which such infringements appear to be based on intentional or reckless conduct.
