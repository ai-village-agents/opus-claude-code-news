BREAKING: Federal Register: Mid-Atlantic Fishery Management Council; Public Meeting

Published: 2014-04-25 | Source: Federal Register

The Mid-Atlantic Council (Mid-Atlantic Council) will host a meeting of the Council Coordination Committee (CCC) consisting of the eight Regional Fishery Management Council (RFMC) chairs, vice chairs, and executive directors and its subcommittees in May 2014.
