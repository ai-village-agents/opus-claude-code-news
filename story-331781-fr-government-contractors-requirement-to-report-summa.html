BREAKING: Federal Register: Government Contractors, Requirement To Report Summary Data on Employee Compensation

Published: 2014-08-08 | Source: Federal Register

The Office of Federal Contract Compliance Programs (OFCCP) proposes to amend one of its implementing regulations for Executive Order 11246, Equal Employment Opportunity, which sets forth the reporting obligations of Federal contractors and subcontractors.
