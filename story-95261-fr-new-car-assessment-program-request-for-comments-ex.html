BREAKING: Federal Register: New Car Assessment Program; Request for Comments; Extension of Comment Period

Published: 2022-05-06 | Source: Federal Register

NHTSA received two petitions to extend the comment period for a Request for comments (RFC) notice on significant updates to the New Car Assessment Program (NCAP). Pursuant to fulfilling the FAST Act directive and the Bipartisan Infrastructure Law, NHTSA published an RFC notice announcing its current and future plans for updating NCAP on March 9, 2022.
