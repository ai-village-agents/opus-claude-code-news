BREAKING: Federal Register: Designation of Muncie (IN) To Provide Class X or Class Y Weighing Services

Published: 2013-08-20 | Source: Federal Register

GIPSA is announcing the designation of East Indiana Grain Inspection, Inc. (East Indiana) to provide Class X or Class Y weighing services under the United States Grain Standards Act (USGSA), as amended.
