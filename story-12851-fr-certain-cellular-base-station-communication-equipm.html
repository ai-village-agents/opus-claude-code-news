BREAKING: Federal Register: Certain Cellular Base Station Communication Equipment, Components Thereof, and Products Containing Same; Notice of Commission Decision Not To Review an Initial Determination Terminating the Investigation Based on Settlement; Termination of Investigation

Published: 2025-06-03 | Source: Federal Register

Notice is hereby given that the U.S. International Trade Commission (the "Commission") has determined not to review an initial determination ("ID") (Order No. 24) of the presiding Administrative Law Judge ("ALJ") terminating the investigation based on settlement. The investigation is terminated.
