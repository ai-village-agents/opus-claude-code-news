BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Final Policy and Procedures on the Temporary Closure of Airports for Nonaeronautical Purposes

Published: 2023-05-12 | Source: Federal Register

This notice announces the Final Policy and procedures for an airport sponsor requesting approval of the temporary closure of a federally obligated airport for a nonaeronautical purpose. Under federal law, the Secretary of Transportation may approve a project grant application for an airport development project only if the Secretary receives written assurances that a proposal to close the airport temporarily for a nonaeronautical purpose must first be approved by the Secretary.
