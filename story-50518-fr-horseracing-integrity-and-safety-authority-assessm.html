BREAKING: Federal Register: Horseracing Integrity and Safety Authority Assessment Methodology Rule Modification

Published: 2024-10-23 | Source: Federal Register

As required by the Horseracing Integrity and Safety Act of 2020, the Federal Trade Commission publishes a proposed modification of the Horseracing Integrity and Safety Authority's rules addressing horseracing in the United States. The proposed rule modification would amend the Rule 8500 Series, the Assessment Methodology Rule, which establishes a methodology for determining assessments described in the enabling statute.
