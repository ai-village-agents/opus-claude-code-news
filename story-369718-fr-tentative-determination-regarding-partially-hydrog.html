BREAKING: Federal Register: Tentative Determination Regarding Partially Hydrogenated Oils; Request for Comments and for Scientific Data and Information

Published: 2013-11-08 | Source: Federal Register

Based on new scientific evidence and the findings of expert scientific panels, the Food and Drug Administration (FDA) has tentatively determined that partially hydrogenated oils (PHOs), which are the primary dietary source of industrially-produced trans fatty acids, or trans fat, are not generally recognized as safe (GRAS) for any use in food based on current scientific evidence establishing the health risks associated with the consumption of trans fat, and therefore that PHOs are food additives...
