BREAKING: Federal Register: Certain Electronic Devices and Semiconductor Devices With Timing-Aware Dummy Fill and Components Thereof; Notice of the Commission's Determination Not To Review an Initial Determination Setting a 20-Month Target Date and an Initial Determination Granting a Motion To Intervene

Published: 2022-07-28 | Source: Federal Register

Notice is hereby given that the U.S. International Trade Commission has determined not to review the presiding administrative law judge's ("ALJ") initial determination ("ID") (Order No. 3) setting a 20-month target date and an ID (Order No. 5) granting Cadence Design Systems, Inc.'s ("Cadence") motion to intervene.
