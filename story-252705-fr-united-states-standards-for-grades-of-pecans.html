BREAKING: Federal Register: United States Standards for Grades of Pecans

Published: 2016-01-12 | Source: Federal Register

The Agricultural Marketing Service (AMS) of the Department of Agriculture (USDA) proposes to revise the United States Standards for Grades of Shelled Pecans and the United States Standards for Grades of Pecans in the Shell. AMS is proposing to replace the term "midget" with "extra small" in the Shelled Pecan standards.
