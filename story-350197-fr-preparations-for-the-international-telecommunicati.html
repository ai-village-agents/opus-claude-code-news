BREAKING: Federal Register: Preparations for the International Telecommunication Union World Telecommunication Development Conference (ITU WTDC 2014)

Published: 2013-03-25 | Source: Federal Register

This notice announces meetings of the Department of State's International Telecommunication Advisory Committee (ITAC) to review the activities of its ad hoc group for preparations for the ITU World Telecommunication Development Conference (WTDC 2014), as well as preparations for other, related meetings at the ITU.
