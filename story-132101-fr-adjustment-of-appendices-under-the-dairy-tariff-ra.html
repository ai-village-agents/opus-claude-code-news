BREAKING: Federal Register: Adjustment of Appendices Under the Dairy Tariff-Rate Quota Import Licensing Regulation

Published: 2021-09-02 | Source: Federal Register

This notice announces the transfer of amounts for certain dairy articles from the historical license category (Appendix 1) to the lottery (nonhistorical) license category (Appendix 2) pursuant to the Dairy Tariff-Rate Quota Import Licensing regulations for the 2021 quota year.
