BREAKING: Federal Register: Privacy Act of 1974: System of Records

Published: 2022-10-21 | Source: Federal Register

Pursuant to the Privacy Act of 1974, notice is hereby given that the Department of Veterans Affairs (VA) is amending the system of records entitled "Police and Security Records--VA" (103VA07B).
