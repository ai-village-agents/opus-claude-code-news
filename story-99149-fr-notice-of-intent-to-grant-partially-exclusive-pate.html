BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Intent To Grant Partially Exclusive Patent License

Published: 2022-06-27 | Source: Federal Register

Pursuant to the Bayh-Dole Act and implementing regulations, the Department of the Air Force hereby gives notice of its intent to grant a partially exclusive patent license to Smart Response Technologies, Inc., a C Corporation, having a place of business at 726 East Main Street, Suite F117, Lebanon, Ohio 45036.
