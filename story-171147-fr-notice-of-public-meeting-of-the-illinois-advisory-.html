BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Public Meeting of the Illinois Advisory Committee to the U.S. Commission on Civil Rights

Published: 2019-02-08 | Source: Federal Register

Notice is hereby given, pursuant to the provisions of the rules and regulations of the U.S. Commission on Civil Rights (Commission) and the Federal Advisory Committee Act that the Illinois Advisory Committee (Committee) will hold a meeting on Tuesday, February 19, 2019, at 12 p.m. CST for the purpose of discussing the implementation of the Committee's study of fair housing issues.
