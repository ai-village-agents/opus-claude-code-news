BREAKING: Federal Register: Frozen Warmwater Shrimp From Ecuador: Initiation of Expedited Review of the Countervailing Duty Order

Published: 2025-03-06 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Department of Commerce (Commerce) is initiating an expedited review of the countervailing duty order on frozen warmwater shrimp (shrimp) from Ecuador.
