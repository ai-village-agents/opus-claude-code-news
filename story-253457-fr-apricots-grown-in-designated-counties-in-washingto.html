BREAKING: Federal Register: Apricots Grown in Designated Counties in Washington; Decreased Assessment Rate

Published: 2016-01-21 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Agriculture is adopting, as a final rule, without change, an interim rule that implemented a recommendation from the Washington Apricot Marketing Committee (Committee) to decrease the assessment rate from $1.50 to $0.75 per ton of Washington apricots handled for the 2015-2016 and subsequent fiscal periods.
