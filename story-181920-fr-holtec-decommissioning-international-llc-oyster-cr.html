BREAKING: Federal Register: Holtec Decommissioning International, LLC; Oyster Creek Nuclear Generating Station

Published: 2019-06-18 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Nuclear Regulatory Commission (NRC) is considering issuance of exemptions in response to a November 30, 2018, request from Holtec Decommissioning International, LLC (HDI), for the Oyster Creek Nuclear Generating Station (Oyster Creek). The exemptions would permit HDI to use funds from the Oyster Creek decommissioning trust fund (DTF or the Trust) for irradiated fuel management activities and site restoration.
