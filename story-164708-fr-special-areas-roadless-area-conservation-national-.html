BREAKING: Federal Register: Special Areas; Roadless Area Conservation; National Forest System Lands in Alaska

Published: 2020-10-29 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Department of Agriculture (USDA or Department), is adopting a final rule to exempt the Tongass National Forest from the 2001 Roadless Area Conservation Rule (2001 Roadless Rule), which prohibits timber harvest and road construction/reconstruction with limited exceptions within designated inventoried roadless areas.
