BREAKING: Federal Register: Approval and Promulgation of Implementation Plans; Mississippi: New Source Review-Prevention of Significance Deterioration

Published: 2015-03-05 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is taking final action to approve revisions to the Mississippi State Implementation Plan (SIP) submitted by the State of Mississippi, through the Mississippi Department of Environmental Quality (MDEQ), on February 10, 2012. The SIP revision modifies Mississippi's New Source Review (NSR) Prevention of Significant Deterioration (PSD) program to incorporate by reference (IBR) certain Federal PSD regulations.
