BREAKING: Federal Register: Funding Opportunity Title: Risk Management Education in Targeted States (Targeted States Program)

Published: 2013-06-07 | Source: Federal Register

The Federal Crop Insurance Corporation (FCIC), operating through the Risk Management Agency (RMA), announces its intent to award approximately $5,000,000 to fund cooperative agreements under the Risk Management Education in Targeted States Program.
