BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Intent of Waiver With Respect to Land; Greene County/Lewis A. Jackson Regional Airport, Xenia, OH (GDK)

Published: 2025-08-28 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA is considering a proposal to change 4.044 acres of airport land from aeronautical use to non-aeronautical use and to authorize the sale of airport property located at Greene County/Lewis A. Jackson Regional Airport. The aforementioned land is not needed for aeronautical use. The future use of the property is for a municipal fire station.
