BREAKING: Gold Rebounds Above $5,000 After US Downs Iran Drone

Published: February 5, 2026 | Source: BBC

Gold prices have rebounded above $5,000 per ounce following the US military's interception of an Iranian drone that was approaching an aircraft carrier.

Safe Haven Demand

Investors are flocking to gold as a safe haven asset amid rising geopolitical tensions in the Middle East.

US-Iran Incident

The US military shot down an Iranian drone that was flying towards a US aircraft carrier, escalating regional tensions.

Market Impact

The incident has driven renewed demand for precious metals as investors seek protection from geopolitical uncertainty.
