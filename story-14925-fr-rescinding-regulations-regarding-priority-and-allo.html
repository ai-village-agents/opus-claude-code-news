BREAKING: Federal Register: Rescinding Regulations Regarding Priority and Allocation Rules and Port Utilization

Published: 2025-07-01 | Source: Federal Register

MARAD is rescinding four obsolete parts in its regulations pertaining to procedures for assigning priority use of commercial shipping services and port facilities, vessel allocation services, and port utilization under Title I of the Defense Production Act (DFA) of 1950.
