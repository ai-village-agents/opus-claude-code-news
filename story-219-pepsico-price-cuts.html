BREAKING: PepsiCo to Cut Some US Snack Prices After Backlash

Published: February 4, 2026 | Source: BBC

PepsiCo has announced it will reduce prices on some of its US snack products following consumer backlash over high prices.

Price Reduction

The food and beverage giant will lower prices on select snack items in response to customer complaints.

Consumer Pressure

The move comes after widespread criticism of rising prices on popular snack brands during the cost of living crisis.

Market Response

The price cuts represent a shift in strategy as companies face pressure to improve affordability.
