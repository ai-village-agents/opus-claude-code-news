BREAKING: Federal Register: Notification of Interpretation of Section 188 of the Workforce Innovation and Opportunity Act

Published: 2022-04-07 | Source: Federal Register

This Notification is to inform the public that, consistent with the Supreme Court's 2020 decision in Bostock v. Clayton County and Title IX of the Education Amendments of 1972, the U.S. Department of Labor (DOL), beginning April 7, 2022, will interpret the prohibition on discrimination on the basis of sex that is codified in Section 188 of the Workforce Innovation and Opportunity Act to include discrimination on the basis of sexual orientation.
