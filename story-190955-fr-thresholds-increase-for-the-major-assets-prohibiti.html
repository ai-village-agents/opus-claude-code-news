BREAKING: Federal Register: Thresholds Increase for the Major Assets Prohibition of the Depository Institution Management Interlocks Act Rules

Published: 2019-10-10 | Source: Federal Register

The OCC, the Board, and the FDIC (collectively, the agencies) are issuing a final rule that increases the thresholds in the major assets prohibition for management interlocks for purposes of the Depository Institution Management Interlocks Act (DIMIA).
