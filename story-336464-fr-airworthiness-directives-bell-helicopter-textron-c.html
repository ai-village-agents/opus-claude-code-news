BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Bell Helicopter Textron Canada Limited

Published: 2014-10-03 | Source: Federal Register

We are revising and reopening the comment period for an earlier notice of proposed rulemaking (NPRM) for certain Bell Helicopter Textron Canada Limited (Bell) Model 206L-3 and 206L-4 helicopters. The NPRM proposes to require installing a placard and revising the limitations section of the rotorcraft flight manual (RFM).
