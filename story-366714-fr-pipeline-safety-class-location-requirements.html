BREAKING: Federal Register: Pipeline Safety: Class Location Requirements

Published: 2013-09-30 | Source: Federal Register

On August 1, 2013, PHMSA published a notice of inquiry in the Federal Register on "Class Location Requirements," seeking comments on whether integrity management program (IMP) requirements, or elements of IMP, should be expanded beyond high consequence areas (HCA) and, with respect to gas transmission pipeline facilities, whether applying IMP requirements to additional areas would mitigate the need for class location requirements.
