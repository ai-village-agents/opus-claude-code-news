BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Inventory Completion: Peabody Museum of Archaeology and Ethnology, Harvard University, Cambridge, MA; Correction

Published: 2021-08-11 | Source: Federal Register

The Peabody Museum of Archaeology and Ethnology, Harvard University has corrected an inventory of human remains and associated funerary objects published in a Notice of Inventory Completion in the Federal Register on July 30, 2007. This notice corrects the minimum number of individuals and number of associated funerary objects.
