BREAKING: Federal Register: Filing of Plat of Survey, New Mexico

Published: 2025-05-23 | Source: Federal Register

The plat of survey of the following described lands is scheduled to be officially filed 30 days after the date of this notice in the Bureau of Land Management (BLM) New Mexico State Office, Santa Fe, New Mexico. The survey announced in this notice is necessary for the management of lands administered by the U.S. Army Corps of Engineers.
