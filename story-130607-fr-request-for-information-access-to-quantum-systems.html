BREAKING: Federal Register: Request for Information: Access to Quantum Systems

Published: 2021-08-16 | Source: Federal Register

Congress has requested DOE to develop a roadmap to provide researchers access to quantum systems so as to enhance the U.S. quantum research enterprise, stimulate the fledgling U.S. quantum computing industry, educate the future quantum computing workforce, and accelerate advancement of quantum computer capabilities.
