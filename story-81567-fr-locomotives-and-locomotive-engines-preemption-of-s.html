BREAKING: Federal Register: Locomotives and Locomotive Engines; Preemption of State and Local Regulations

Published: 2023-11-08 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is finalizing revisions to its regulations addressing preemption of State and local regulation of locomotives and engines used in locomotives.
