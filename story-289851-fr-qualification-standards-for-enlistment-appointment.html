BREAKING: Federal Register: Qualification Standards for Enlistment, Appointment, and Induction

Published: 2015-03-27 | Source: Federal Register

This rule updates policies and responsibilities for basic entrance qualification standards for enlistment, appointment, and induction into the Armed Forces and delegates the authority to specify certain standards to the Secretaries of the Military Departments. It establishes the age, aptitude, character/conduct, citizenship, dependents, education, medical, physical fitness, and other disqualifying conditions that are causes for rejection from military service.
