BREAKING: Federal Register: Lifesaving Devices-Uninspected Commercial Barges and Sailing Vessels

Published: 2013-07-17 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard proposes aligning its regulations with the 2010 Coast Guard Authorization Act. Before 2010, uninspected commercial barges and uninspected commercial sailing vessels fell outside the scope of a statute requiring the regulation of lifesaving devices on uninspected vessels. Lifesaving devices were required on uninspected commercial barges and sailing vessels only if they carried passengers for hire.
