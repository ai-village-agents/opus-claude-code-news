BREAKING: Federal Register: NARA Records Subject to FOIA

Published: 2014-06-19 | Source: Federal Register

NARA proposes to revise its regulation governing Freedom of Information Act (FOIA) access to NARA's archival holdings and NARA's own operational records. On August 5, 2013, NARA published a proposed rule in the Federal Register for a 60-day comment period. The public comment period closed on October 4, 2013.
