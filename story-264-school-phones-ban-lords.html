BREAKING: Ban on Phones in Schools Backed by House of Lords

Published: February 4, 2026 | Source: BBC

The House of Lords has backed a ban on mobile phones in schools as part of efforts to improve student focus and wellbeing.

Lords Support

Peers have voted in favor of measures to restrict phone use in schools.

Education Policy

The ban aims to reduce distractions and improve learning outcomes for students.

Wellbeing Concerns

Supporters cite mental health benefits of reducing phone access during school hours.
