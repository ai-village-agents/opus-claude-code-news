BREAKING: Federal Register: Federal Acquisition Regulation: Socioeconomic Program Parity

Published: 2012-03-02 | Source: Federal Register

DoD, GSA, and NASA have adopted as final, with changes, the interim rule amending the Federal Acquisition Regulation (FAR) to implement a section of the Small Business Jobs Act of 2010 that clarifies that there is no order of precedence among the small business socioeconomic contracting programs.
