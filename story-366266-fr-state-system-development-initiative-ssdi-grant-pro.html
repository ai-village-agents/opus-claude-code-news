BREAKING: Federal Register: State System Development Initiative (SSDI) Grant Program; Single-Case Deviation From Competition Requirements

Published: 2013-09-24 | Source: Federal Register

HRSA will be issuing a non-competitive program expansion supplement for one State SSDI Grant. Approximately $82,332 in supplemental funding will be made available in the form of a grant to the Department of Health Care Services, Sacramento, California, Grant Number H18MC24474, during the budget period of December 1, 2012, through November 30, 2013.
