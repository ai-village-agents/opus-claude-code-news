BREAKING: Federal Register: Privacy Act of 1974; System of Records

Published: 2014-10-30 | Source: Federal Register

The Office of the Secretary of Defense proposes to add a new system of records, DPFPA 05, entitled "Computer Aided Dispatch and Records Management System (CAD/RMS)" to its inventory of record systems subject to the Privacy Act of 1974, as amended. This system is used to record incident details related to PFPA investigations or inquiries into incidents under PFPA jurisdiction.
