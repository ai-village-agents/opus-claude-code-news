BREAKING: Federal Register: Six Mile Munitions Storage Area on Joint Base Elmendorf-Richardson, Alaska; Knik Arm; Restricted Area

Published: 2022-07-13 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Army Corps of Engineers (Corps) is proposing to revise its regulations to establish a restricted area within the explosive arc of Six Mile Munitions Storage Area (MSA) on Joint Base Elmendorf-Richardson (JBER). The United States Air Force--Pacific Air Command (USAF PACAF) requested establishment of a restricted area which would be located within the navigable waters of Knik Arm which are a part of the Six Mile MSA explosive arc.
