BREAKING: Federal Register: Disclosure of Records

Published: 2023-10-31 | Source: Federal Register

The Bureau of the Fiscal Service within the Department of the Treasury (Fiscal Service or Treasury) proposes to adopt regulations to implement statutory requirements under the SECURE 2.0 Act of 2022 requiring Treasury to provide information on applicable savings bonds to states.
