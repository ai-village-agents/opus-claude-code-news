BREAKING: Federal Register: Special Local Regulation; Back River, Poquoson, VA

Published: 2016-10-24 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard will enforce a special local regulation to keep vessels from entering the Poquoson Seafood Festival Workboat Races route near the vicinity of Messick Point, in Back River, Poquoson, VA on October 23, 2016. This action is necessary to ensure safety of life on navigable waters during this event.
