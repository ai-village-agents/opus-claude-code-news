BREAKING: Federal Register: Privacy Act of 1974; System of Records

Published: 2020-04-09 | Source: Federal Register

The Air Force Deputy Chief Information Officer is adding a new System of Records titled, Bring Your Own Approved Device (BYOAD), F017 SAF CN A. The BYOAD program provides authorized AF users the ability to voluntarily use their authorized personal mobile devices to conduct government business.
