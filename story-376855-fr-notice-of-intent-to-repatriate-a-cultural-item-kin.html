BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Intent To Repatriate a Cultural Item: Kingman Museum, Inc., Battle Creek, MI

Published: 2012-02-06 | Source: Federal Register

The Kingman Museum, Inc., in consultation with the appropriate Indian tribe, has determined that the cultural item meets the definition of unassociated funerary object and repatriation to the Indian tribe stated below may occur if no additional claimants come forward. Representatives of any Indian tribe that believes itself to be culturally affiliated with the cultural item may contact the Kingman Museum, Inc.
