BREAKING: Federal Register: Special Areas; Roadless Area Conservation; Applicability to the National Forests in Colorado

Published: 2012-07-03 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Department of Agriculture (USDA or Department), is adopting a State-specific final rule to provide management direction for conserving and managing approximately 4.2 million acres of Colorado Roadless Areas (CRAs) on National Forest System (NFS) lands. The final Colorado Roadless Rule is a rule that addresses current issues and concerns specific to Colorado.
