BREAKING: Federal Register: Small Business Size Standards; Waiver of the Nonmanufacturer Rule

Published: 2013-04-04 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Small Business Administration is proposing to rescind a class waiver from the Nonmanufacturer Rule for NAICS code 332991, Aerospace Ball and Roller Bearings, PSC 3110.
