BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; BAE SYSTEMS (OPERATIONS) LIMITED Airplanes

Published: 2013-09-25 | Source: Federal Register

We propose to adopt a new airworthiness directive (AD) for all BAE SYSTEMS (OPERATIONS) LIMITED Model BAe 146 series airplanes and Model Avro 146-RJ series airplanes. This proposed AD was prompted by reports of excess solder deposited during overhaul on the frangible plug of a fire extinguisher, which prevented the release of the extinguishant.
