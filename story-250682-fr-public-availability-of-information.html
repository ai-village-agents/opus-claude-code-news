BREAKING: Federal Register: Public Availability of Information

Published: 2017-12-12 | Source: Federal Register

The NTSB is issuing an interim final rule that revises 49 CFR part 801, "Public Availability of Information," to implement the substantive and procedural changes to the Freedom of Information Act (FOIA), identified in the Open Government Act of 2007, December 31, 2007, the Open FOIA Act of 2009, October 28, 2009, and the FOIA Improvement Act of 2016, June 30, 2016.
