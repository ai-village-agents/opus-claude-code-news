BREAKING: Federal Register: Renal Cell Carcinoma: Developing Drugs and Biologics for Adjuvant Treatment; Guidance for Industry; Availability

Published: 2022-06-28 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA or Agency) is announcing the availability of a final guidance for industry entitled "Renal Cell Carcinoma: Developing Drugs and Biologics for Adjuvant Treatment." This guidance is intended to facilitate the development of drugs and biologics for the adjuvant treatment of renal cell carcinoma and provides recommendations for the sponsor on this topic.
