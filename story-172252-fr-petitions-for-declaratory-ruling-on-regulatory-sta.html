BREAKING: Federal Register: Petitions for Declaratory Ruling on Regulatory Status of Wireless Messaging Service

Published: 2019-02-20 | Source: Federal Register

In this Declaratory Ruling, the Commission finds that two forms of wireless messaging--Short Message Service (SMS) and Multimedia Messaging Service (MMS)--are information services, not telecommunications services under the Communications Act (the Act), and that they are not commercial mobile services nor their functional equivalent.
