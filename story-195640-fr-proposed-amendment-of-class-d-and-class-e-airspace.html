BREAKING: Federal Register: Proposed Amendment of Class D and Class E Airspace, Nashua, NH

Published: 2019-12-16 | Source: Federal Register

This action proposes to amend Class D airspace, Class E airspace designated as an extension to a Class D surface area, and Class E airspace extending upward from 700 feet above the surface at Boire Field, Nashua, NH, to accommodate airspace reconfiguration due to the decommissioning of CHERN non-directional beacon, and cancellation of the associated approaches.
