BREAKING: Federal Register: Assessment Regulations

Published: 2018-04-05 | Source: Federal Register

The FDIC is making technical amendments to its rules governing deposit insurance assessments. The FDIC believes that the amendments will have little or no effect on the deposit insurance assessments for insured depository institutions (IDIs), and any potential effect would result in lower assessments.
