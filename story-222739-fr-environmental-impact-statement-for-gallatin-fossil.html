BREAKING: Federal Register: Environmental Impact Statement for Gallatin Fossil Plant Surface Impoundment Closure and Restoration Project

Published: 2018-12-07 | Source: Federal Register

The Tennessee Valley Authority (TVA) intends to prepare an Environmental Impact Statement (EIS) to address the potential environmental effects associated with management of coal combustion residual (CCR) material at the Gallatin Fossil Plant (GAF) located near Gallatin in Sumner County, Tennessee.
