BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Honda Aircraft Company LLC Airplanes

Published: 2023-06-01 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA is superseding Airworthiness Directive (AD) 2022-18- 03, which applied to certain Honda Aircraft Company LLC (Honda) Model HA-420 airplanes. AD 2022-18-03 required incorporating temporary revisions into the airplane flight manual (AFM) and the quick reference handbook (QRH) that modify procedures for windshield heat operation until the affected windshield assemblies are replaced.
