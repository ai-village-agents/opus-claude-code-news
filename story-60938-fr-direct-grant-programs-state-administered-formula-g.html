BREAKING: Federal Register: Direct Grant Programs, State-Administered Formula Grant Programs

Published: 2023-02-22 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Department of Education (we or the Department) proposes to rescind regulations related to religious student organizations at certain public institutions of higher education (IHEs) that prescribe a novel role for the Department in enforcing grant conditions related to religious student organizations.
