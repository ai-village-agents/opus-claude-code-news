BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Availability of Funds and Solicitation for Grant Applications for YouthBuild

Published: 2014-02-24 | Source: Federal Register

The Employment and Training Administration (ETA), U.S. Department of Labor (DOL), announces the availability of approximately $73 million for YouthBuild grants. YouthBuild grants will be awarded through a competitive process. Under this SGA, DOL will award grants to organizations to oversee the provision of education, occupational skills training, and employment services to disadvantaged youth in their communities while performing meaningful work and service to their communities.
