BREAKING: Russia Jails Stand-Up Comic Artemy Ostani Over War Joke

Published: February 4, 2026 | Source: Al Jazeera

Russian authorities have jailed stand-up comedian Artemy Ostani for making jokes about the war in Ukraine, in another crackdown on dissent.

Comedy Criminalized

The comedian was sentenced for making jokes critical of Russia's military operations in Ukraine.

Free Speech Crackdown

The case highlights Russia's ongoing suppression of any criticism of the war.

International Concern

Rights groups have condemned the imprisonment as a violation of free expression.
