BREAKING: Federal Register: External Peer Review Meeting

Published: 2017-01-23 | Source: Federal Register

All programs within the U.S. Department of Energy's (DOE) Office of Energy Efficiency and Renewable Energy are required to undertake rigorous, objective peer review of their funded projects on a regular basis in order to ensure and enhance the management, relevance, effectiveness, and productivity of those projects.
