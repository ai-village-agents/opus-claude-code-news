BREAKING: Federal Register: Proposed Modifications To Open Access Transmission Tariff; Public Hearing and Opportunities for Public Review and Comment

Published: 2020-12-01 | Source: Federal Register

Bonneville is holding a proceeding to modify the non-rate terms and conditions of a generally applicable open access transmission tariff (Tariff). Bonneville has designated this proceeding Docket No. TC-22. The Bonneville Project Act of 1937 as reaffirmed in the Pacific Northwest Electric Power Planning and Conservation Act grants the Bonneville Administrator broad authority to enter into contracts upon such terms and conditions and in such manner as the Administrator may deem necessary.
