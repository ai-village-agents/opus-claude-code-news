BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; The Boeing Company Airplanes

Published: 2013-01-18 | Source: Federal Register

We are adopting a new airworthiness directive (AD) for certain The Boeing Company Model 747-100, 747-200B, 747-200C, 747-200F, 747- 300, 747-400, 747-400F, and 747SR series airplanes. This AD was prompted by reports of broken and damaged latch pin retention bolts and subsequent migration of the latch pins of the main deck side cargo door (MDSCD).
