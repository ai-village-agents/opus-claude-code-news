BREAKING: Federal Register: Wooden Cabinet and Vanities and Components Thereof From the People's Republic of China: Final Results and Partial Rescission of the Antidumping Duty Administrative Review; 2022-2023

Published: 2024-11-12 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Department of Commerce (Commerce) determines that and The Ancientree Cabinet Co., Ltd. (Ancientree) and Jiangsu Weisen Houseware Co., Ltd. (Weisen) did not make sales of wooden cabinets and vanities and components thereof (cabinets) at prices below normal value during the period of review (POR) April 1, 2022, through March 31, 2023.
