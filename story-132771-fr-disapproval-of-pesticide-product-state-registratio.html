BREAKING: Federal Register: Disapproval of Pesticide Product State Registrations for Special Local Needs

Published: 2021-09-13 | Source: Federal Register

As provided under the Federal Insecticide, Fungicide, and Rodenticide Act (FIFRA), State-designated lead agencies may register pesticides, within their respective States, to meet special local needs. EPA's regulations require the State lead agencies to notify EPA of such special local need registrations. EPA may disapprove any such State registration.
