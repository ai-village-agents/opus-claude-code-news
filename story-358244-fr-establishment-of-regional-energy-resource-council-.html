BREAKING: Federal Register: Establishment of Regional Energy Resource Council and Solicitation of Nominations for Membership

Published: 2013-06-24 | Source: Federal Register

Pursuant to the Tennessee Valley Authority Act of 1933, as amended, and the Federal Advisory Committee Act (5 U.S.C. Appendix 2), TVA announces the establishment of the Regional Energy Resource Council. The Council will advise TVA on its energy resource activities and the priorities among competing objectives and values.
