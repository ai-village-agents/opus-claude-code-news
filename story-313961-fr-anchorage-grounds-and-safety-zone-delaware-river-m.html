BREAKING: Federal Register: Anchorage Grounds and Safety Zone, Delaware River; Marcus Hook, PA

Published: 2014-01-14 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is establishing temporary regulations in support of the annual U.S. Army Corps of Engineers (USACE) dredging of Marcus Hook Range in the Delaware River. Anchorage restrictions and a safety zone affecting the Marcus Hook area of the Delaware River are in place every year from September 1 through December 31.
