BREAKING: Federal Register: Proposed Collections; Comment Requests

Published: 2014-05-27 | Source: Federal Register

The Financial Stability Oversight Council (the "Council") invites members of the public and affected agencies to comment on proposed and/or continuing information collections, as required by the Paperwork Reduction Act of 1995, Public Law 104-13 (44 U.S.C. 3506(c)(2)(A)). The Council is soliciting comments concerning its collection of information related to its authority to designate financial market utilities as systemically important.
