BREAKING: Federal Register: Chemical Data Reporting; Final Extension of the 2020 Submission Period

Published: 2020-11-25 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is amending the Toxic Substances Control Act (TSCA) Chemical Data Reporting (CDR) regulations by extending the submission deadline for 2020 reports to January 29, 2021. This is the final extension for the 2020 submission period only.
