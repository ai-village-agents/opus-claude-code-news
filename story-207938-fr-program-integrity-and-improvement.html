BREAKING: Federal Register: Program Integrity and Improvement

Published: 2018-05-25 | Source: Federal Register

The Secretary proposes to delay, until July 1, 2020, the effective date of the final regulations entitled Program Integrity and Improvement published in the Federal Register on December 19, 2016 (the final regulations). The current effective date of the final regulations is July 1, 2018.
