BREAKING: Federal Register: Certain Transfers of Property to Real Estate Investment Trusts [REITs]

Published: 2019-03-26 | Source: Federal Register

This document withdraws a portion of a notice of proposed rulemaking published in the Proposed Rules section of the Federal Register on June 8, 2016. If adopted, the proposed rules would have provided guidance for transactions in which property of a C corporation becomes the property of a REIT following certain corporate distributions of controlled corporation stock.
