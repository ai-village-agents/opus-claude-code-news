BREAKING: Federal Register: Special Local Regulations; North Charleston Fireworks Display

Published: 2016-06-24 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard will enforce the North Charleston Fireworks Special Local Regulation from 8:45 p.m. through 10:15 p.m. on July 4, 2016. This action is necessary to ensure safety of life on navigable waters of the United States during the Fourth of July Fireworks Displays.
