BREAKING: Federal Register: Hours of Service (HOS) of Drivers; Revision of Exemption; American Pyrotechnics Association (APA)

Published: 2012-06-27 | Source: Federal Register

The FMCSA announces a revision to the list of member motor carriers of the American Pyrotechnics Association (APA) that were granted an exemption from FMCSA's prohibition on driving commercial motor vehicles (CMVs) after the 14th hour after the driver comes on duty, during the periods of June 28-July 8, inclusive, in 2011 and 2012.
