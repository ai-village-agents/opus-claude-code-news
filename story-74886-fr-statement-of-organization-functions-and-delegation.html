BREAKING: Federal Register: Statement of Organization, Functions, and Delegations of Authority

Published: 2023-08-21 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA) is correcting a notice entitled "Statement of Organization, Functions, and Delegations of Authority" that appeared in the Federal Register of August 14, 2023. The document announced amendments to the organization structure for FDA, Center for Devices and Radiological Health (CDRH), Office of Strategic Partnerships and Technology Innovation (OST).
