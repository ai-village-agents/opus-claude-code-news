BREAKING: Federal Register: Freedom of Information Act Regulations

Published: 2022-07-01 | Source: Federal Register

The Central Intelligence Agency (CIA or the Agency) has undertaken and completed a review of its public regulations governing its implementation of the Freedom of Information Act (FOIA), as amended by the FOIA Improvement Act of 2016. As a result of this review, the Agency proposes to revise its FOIA regulations concerning the requirements for filing FOIA requests and CIA's procedures for processing and reviewing such requests.
