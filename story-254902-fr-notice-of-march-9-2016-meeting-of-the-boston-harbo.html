BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of March 9, 2016, Meeting of the Boston Harbor Islands National Recreation Area Advisory Council

Published: 2016-02-09 | Source: Federal Register

This notice announces the annual meeting of the Boston Harbor Islands National Recreation Area Advisory Council (Council). The agenda includes a talk about the history and contemporary nature of the Boston Harbor Islands as "islands on the edge." Since their ancient formation by rising sea level, the Boston Harbor Islands have literally been on the edge of the continent, places where land meets sea, and now at the edge of a major metropolitan area.
