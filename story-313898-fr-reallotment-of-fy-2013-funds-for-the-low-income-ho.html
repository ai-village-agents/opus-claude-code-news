BREAKING: Federal Register: Reallotment of FY 2013 Funds for the Low Income Home Energy Assistance Program (LIHEAP)

Published: 2014-01-14 | Source: Federal Register

Notice is hereby given of a preliminary determination that funds from the fiscal year (FY) 2013 Low Income Home Energy Assistance Program (LIHEAP) are available for reallotment to States, Territories, Tribes, and Tribal Organizations that receive FY 2014 direct LIHEAP grants. No subgrantees or other entities may apply for these funds.
