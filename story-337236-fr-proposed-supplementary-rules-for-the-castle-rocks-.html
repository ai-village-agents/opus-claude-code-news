BREAKING: Federal Register: Proposed Supplementary Rules for the Castle Rocks Land Use Plan Amendment Area, Idaho

Published: 2014-10-15 | Source: Federal Register

The Bureau of Land Management (BLM) is proposing supplementary rules for all BLM-administered public lands within an approximately 400-acre area in Idaho known as Castle Rocks. The BLM addressed this area in the November 2013 Cassia Resource Management Plan (CRMP) Amendment and Record of Decision (ROD).
