BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Inventory Completion: Sandusky Library, Sandusky, OH

Published: 2020-08-11 | Source: Federal Register

The Sandusky Library has completed an inventory of human remains and associated funerary objects, in consultation with the appropriate Indian Tribes or Native Hawaiian organizations, and has determined that there is no cultural affiliation between the human remains and associated funerary objects and any present-day Indian Tribes or Native Hawaiian organizations.
