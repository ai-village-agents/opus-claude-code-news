BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Gulfstream Aerospace Corporation Airplanes

Published: 2012-05-29 | Source: Federal Register

We are adopting a new airworthiness directive (AD) for all Gulfstream Aerospace Corporation Model G-1159, G-1159A, and G-1159B airplanes. This AD requires, for certain airplanes, a measurement to determine the clearance (gap) of the exposed rounded portion of the doubler and clothespin fitting at the wing-to-fuselage attachment, and repair if necessary.
