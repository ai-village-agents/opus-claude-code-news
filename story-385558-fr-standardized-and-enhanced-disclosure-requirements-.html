BREAKING: Federal Register: Standardized and Enhanced Disclosure Requirements for Television Broadcast Licensee Public Interest Obligations; Extension of the Filing Requirement for Children's Television Programming Report (FCC Form 398)

Published: 2012-05-11 | Source: Federal Register

In this document, the Commission revises its public file regulations to require that television station public inspection files be made available in an online public file to be hosted on the Commission's Web site.
