BREAKING: Federal Register: Fisheries of the Northeastern United States; Special Management Zones for 13 New Jersey Artificial Reefs

Published: 2018-02-13 | Source: Federal Register

NMFS proposes management measures to implement special management zones for 13 New Jersey artificial reefs under the black sea bass provisions of the Summer Flounder, Scup, and Black Sea Bass Fishery Management Plan. The implementing regulations for the special management zones require NMFS to publish proposed measures to provide an opportunity for public comment.
