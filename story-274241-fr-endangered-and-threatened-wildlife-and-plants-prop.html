BREAKING: Federal Register: Endangered and Threatened Wildlife and Plants; Proposed Rule To List Two Guitarfishes as Threatened

Published: 2016-09-19 | Source: Federal Register

We, NMFS, have completed a comprehensive status review under the Endangered Species Act (ESA) for the common guitarfish (Rhinobatos rhinobatos) and the blackchin guitarfish (Rhinobatos cemiculus). We have determined that, based on the best scientific and commercial data available, and after taking into account efforts being made to protect these species, both species meet the definition of a threatened species under the ESA.
