BREAKING: Federal Register: Soybean Promotion and Research: Adjusting Representation on the United Soybean Board

Published: 2021-04-15 | Source: Federal Register

This proposed rule would adjust the number of members on the United Soybean Board (Board) to reflect changes in production levels that have occurred since the Board was last reapportioned in 2018. As required by the Soybean Promotion, Research, and Consumer Information Act (Act), membership on the Board is reviewed every 3 years and adjustments are made accordingly.
