BREAKING: Federal Register: Agency Information Collection Extension

Published: 2018-04-24 | Source: Federal Register

EIA is requesting a three-year extension, without changes, of Form NWPA-830G Appendix G--Standard Remittance Advice for Payment of Fees, including Annex A to Appendix G, as required by the Paperwork Reduction Act of 1995. Form NWPA-830G is part of the Standard Contract for Disposal of Spent Nuclear Fuel and/or High-Level Radioactive Waste.
