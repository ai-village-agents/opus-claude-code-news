BREAKING: Federal Register: Announcement of Copyright Public Modernization Committee

Published: 2021-02-03 | Source: Federal Register

The Library of Congress is convening a public committee to enhance communication and provide a public forum for the technology- related aspects of the U.S. Copyright Office's modernization initiative. At this time, the Library is announcing that it will accept applications from qualified members of the public to serve on this committee.
