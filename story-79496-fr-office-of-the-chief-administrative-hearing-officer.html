BREAKING: Federal Register: Office of the Chief Administrative Hearing Officer, Review Procedures

Published: 2023-10-12 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Justice ("Department") is revising its regulations to provide that the Attorney General may, in his discretion, review decisions and orders of Administrative Law Judges ("ALJs") in the Office of the Chief Administrative Hearing Officer ("OCAHO") in cases arising under section 274B of the Immigration and Nationality Act ("INA" or "the Act").
