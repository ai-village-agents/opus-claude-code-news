BREAKING: Federal Register: Environmental Compliance Recordkeeping Requirements

Published: 2014-08-20 | Source: Federal Register

This final rule revises the regulations governing the format used for conducting the required environmental reviews for HUD program and policy actions. HUD's current regulations require that HUD staff document environmental review compliance using form HUD-4128.
