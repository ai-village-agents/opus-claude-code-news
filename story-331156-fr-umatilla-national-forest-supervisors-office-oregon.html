BREAKING: Federal Register: Umatilla National Forest, Supervisor's Office; Oregon; Kahler Dry Forest Restoration Project

Published: 2014-08-01 | Source: Federal Register

The USDA, Forest Service will prepare an Environmental Impact Statement (EIS) to analyze impacts for vegetative treatment in the Kahler Creek area of the Heppner Ranger District of the Umatilla National Forest. The district has started an Environmental Assessment (EA) for the project, but has determined that an Environmental Impact Statement would be more appropriate for this project.
