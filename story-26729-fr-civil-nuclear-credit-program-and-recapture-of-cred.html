BREAKING: Federal Register: Civil Nuclear Credit Program and Recapture of Credits

Published: 2024-01-08 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Energy (DOE or the Department) publishes this interim final rule to establish the procedure for the recapture of credits awarded under the Civil Nuclear Credit Program in accordance with the Infrastructure Investment and Jobs Act.
