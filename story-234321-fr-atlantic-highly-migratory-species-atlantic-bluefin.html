BREAKING: Federal Register: Atlantic Highly Migratory Species; Atlantic Bluefin Tuna Fisheries

Published: 2017-05-17 | Source: Federal Register

NMFS is adjusting the Atlantic bluefin tuna (BFT) General category daily retention limit from the default limit of one large medium or giant BFT to four large medium or giant BFT for June 1 through August 31, 2017.
