BREAKING: Federal Register: Implementing the Pallone-Thune Telephone Robocall Abuse Criminal Enforcement and Deterrence Act

Published: 2020-02-14 | Source: Federal Register

In this document, the Commission proposes rules to implement the Pallone-Thune Telephone Robocall Abuse Criminal Enforcement and Deterrence Act (TRACED Act) to establish a registration process for the registration of a single consortium that conducts private-led efforts to trace back the origin of suspected unlawful robocalls.
