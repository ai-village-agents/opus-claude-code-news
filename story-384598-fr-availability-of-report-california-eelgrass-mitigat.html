BREAKING: Federal Register: Availability of Report: California Eelgrass Mitigation Policy; Extension of Comment Period

NMFS is announcing an extension in the public comment period for the notice to allow other agencies and the public an opportunity to review and provide comments on the proposed adoption of the California Eelgrass Mitigation Policy (CEMP) by NMFS Southwest Region (SWR) Habitat Conservation Division (HCD).
