BREAKING: Federal Register: Hours of Service; United Parcel Service Inc. Application for an Exemption From Certain Electronic Logging Device Requirements

Published: 2017-10-20 | Source: Federal Register

The Federal Motor Carrier Safety Administration (FMCSA) announces its decision to grant part of United Parcel Service Inc.'s (UPS) application for a limited 5-year exemption from various provisions of the mandate to use electronic logging devices (ELD).
