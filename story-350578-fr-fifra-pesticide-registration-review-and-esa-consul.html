BREAKING: Federal Register: FIFRA Pesticide Registration Review and ESA Consultation Processes; Stakeholder Input; Notice of Availability

Published: 2013-03-27 | Source: Federal Register

EPA is announcing the availability of the final paper describing enhanced opportunities for stakeholder input during its review of pesticide registrations under the Federal Insecticide, Fungicide, and Rodenticide Act (FIFRA) and associated consultations under the Endangered Species Act (ESA).
