BREAKING: Federal Register: Determination That OPANA ER (Oxymorphone Hydrochloride) Drug Products Covered by New Drug Application 21-610 Were Not Withdrawn From Sale for Reasons of Safety or Effectiveness

Published: 2013-06-25 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA) has determined that OPANA ER (oxymorphone hydrochloride (HCl)) Extended-Release Tablet products approved under new drug application (NDA) 21-610 were not withdrawn from sale for reasons of safety or effectiveness.
