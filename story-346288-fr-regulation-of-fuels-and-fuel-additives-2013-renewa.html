BREAKING: Federal Register: Regulation of Fuels and Fuel Additives: 2013 Renewable Fuel Standards

Published: 2013-02-07 | Source: Federal Register

Under section 211(o) of the Clean Air Act, the Environmental Protection Agency is required to set the renewable fuel standards each November for the following year. In general the standards are designed to ensure that the applicable volumes of renewable fuel specified in the statute are used.
