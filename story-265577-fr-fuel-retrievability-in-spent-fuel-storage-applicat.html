BREAKING: Federal Register: Fuel Retrievability in Spent Fuel Storage Applications

Published: 2016-06-08 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Nuclear Regulatory Commission (NRC) is issuing Interim Staff Guidance (ISG)-2, Revision 2, "Fuel Retrievability in Spent Fuel Storage Applications." This revision to the guidance was developed to improve regulatory clarity due to uncertain duration of spent fuel storage in an independent spent fuel storage installation (ISFSI).
