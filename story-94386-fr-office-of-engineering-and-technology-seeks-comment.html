BREAKING: Federal Register: Office of Engineering and Technology Seeks Comment Following Court Remand of 6 GHz Band Order

Published: 2022-04-25 | Source: Federal Register

In this document, the Commission invites comments in connection with the remand by the United States Court of Appeals for the District of Columbia Circuit of the Commission's 6 GHz Report and Order. The 6 GHz Report and Order opened the 6 GHz band to the operation of unlicensed low power access points. The D.C.
