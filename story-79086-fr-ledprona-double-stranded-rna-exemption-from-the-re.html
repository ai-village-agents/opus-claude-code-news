BREAKING: Federal Register: Ledprona Double-Stranded RNA; Exemption From the Requirement of a Tolerance

Published: 2023-10-05 | Source: Federal Register

This regulation establishes an exemption from the requirement of a tolerance for residues of Ledprona double-stranded (ds) RNA in or on potato when used as a foliar-applied insecticide for the selective control of Colorado potato beetle and in accordance with label directions and good agricultural practices.
