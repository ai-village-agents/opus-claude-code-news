BREAKING: Federal Register: Proposed Amendment of Air Traffic Service (ATS) Route T-333; Western United States

Published: 2018-11-27 | Source: Federal Register

This action proposes to amend Area Navigation (RNAV) Route T- 333 in the western United States. The modification is necessary due to the planned decommissioning of Priest, CA, VOR navigation aid (NAVAID), which provides navigation guidance for portions of affected ATS route V-485. The decommissioning has rendered portions of V-485 unusable and amending T-333 will overcome affected portions of V-485.
