BREAKING: Federal Register: Endangered Species; File No. 16253

Published: 2012-02-23 | Source: Federal Register

Notice is hereby given that the NMFS Southeast Fisheries Science Center (SEFSC; Responsible Party: Bonnie Ponwith, Ph.D.), has been issued a permit to take green (Chelonia mydas), Kemp's ridley (Lepidochelys kempii), hawksbill (Eretmochelys imbricata), leatherback (Dermochelys coriacea), olive ridley (L. olivacea), and loggerhead (Caretta caretta) sea turtles for scientific research.
