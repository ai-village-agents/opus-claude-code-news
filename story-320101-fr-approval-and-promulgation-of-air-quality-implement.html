BREAKING: Federal Register: Approval and Promulgation of Air Quality Implementation Plans; Pennsylvania; Carbon Monoxide Second Limited Maintenance Plan for the Pittsburgh Area

Published: 2014-03-27 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is taking direct final action to approve a State Implementation Plan (SIP) revision submitted by the Commonwealth of Pennsylvania. The revision consists of a second limited maintenance plan for the carbon monoxide (CO) Pittsburgh Area ("the Pittsburgh Area" or "the Area") in Allegheny County, formerly designated as a CO nonattainment area.
