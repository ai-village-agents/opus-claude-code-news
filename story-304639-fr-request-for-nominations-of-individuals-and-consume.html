BREAKING: Federal Register: Request for Nominations of Individuals and Consumer Organizations for the Patient Engagement Advisory Committee

Published: 2015-09-21 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA) is requesting that any consumer organizations interested in participating in the selection of a voting consumer representative to serve on the Patient Engagement Advisory Committee (the Committee) notify FDA in writing. FDA is also requesting nominations for a voting consumer representative to serve on the Committee. Nominees recommended to serve as a voting consumer representative may either be self-nominated or may be nominated by a consumer organization....
