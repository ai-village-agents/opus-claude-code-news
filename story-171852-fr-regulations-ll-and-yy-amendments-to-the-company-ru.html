BREAKING: Federal Register: Regulations LL and YY; Amendments to the Company-Run and Supervisory Stress Test Rules

Published: 2019-02-14 | Source: Federal Register

The Board is requesting comment on a proposed rule that would amend the Board's company-run stress test and supervisory stress test rules, consistent with section 401 of the Economic Growth, Regulatory Relief, and Consumer Protection Act (EGRRCPA).
