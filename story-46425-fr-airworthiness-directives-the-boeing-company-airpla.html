BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; The Boeing Company Airplanes

Published: 2024-09-09 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA proposes to supersede Airworthiness Directive (AD) 2019-14-13, which applies to all The Boeing Company Model 767-200, - 300, -300F, and -400ER series airplanes. AD 2019-14-13 was prompted by reports of uncommanded fore and aft movement of the Captain's and First Officer's seats. AD 2019-14-13 requires identifying the part number, and the serial number if applicable, of the Captain's and First Officer's seats, and applicable on-condition actions for affected seats.
