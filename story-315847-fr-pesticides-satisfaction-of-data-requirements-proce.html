BREAKING: Federal Register: Pesticides; Satisfaction of Data Requirements; Procedures To Ensure Protection of Data Submitters' Rights

Published: 2014-02-05 | Source: Federal Register

EPA is revising and updating its regulations governing the procedures for the satisfaction of data requirements under the Federal Insecticide, Fungicide, and Rodenticide Act (FIFRA). Specifically, this regulation addresses procedures for the protection of exclusive use and data compensation rights of data submitters, which have not been revised since issuance in 1984.
