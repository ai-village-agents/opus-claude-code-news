BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; BAE SYSTEMS (Operations) Limited Airplanes

Published: 2012-05-21 | Source: Federal Register

We propose to adopt a new airworthiness directive (AD) for all BAE SYSTEMS (Operations) Limited Model 4101 airplanes. This proposed AD was prompted by reports that the fire extinguisher in the toilet vanity unit needs to be mounted vertically rather than horizontally. This proposed AD would require inspecting to determine if a certain fire extinguisher bottle is installed, and repositioning the affected fire extinguisher bottle to the vertical position.
