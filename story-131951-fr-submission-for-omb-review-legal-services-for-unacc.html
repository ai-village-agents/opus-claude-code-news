BREAKING: Federal Register: Submission for OMB Review; Legal Services for Unaccompanied Children (0970-0565)

Published: 2021-08-31 | Source: Federal Register

The Office of Refugee Resettlement (ORR), Administration for Children and Families (ACF), U.S. Department of Health and Human Services (HHS), is proposing to continue to collect information that will allow the Unaccompanied Children (UC) Program to provide legal services to UC. These information collections were originally approved under emergency approval for 6 months. This request is to continue data collection.
