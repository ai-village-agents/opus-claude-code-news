BREAKING: Federal Register: State Rail Plan Guidance

Published: 2012-08-14 | Source: Federal Register

FRA is publishing this notice to request comments on FRA's technical guidance for the development, submission, and acceptance of State rail plans. State rail plans are documents that are required under Section 303 of the Passenger Rail Investment and Improvement Act of 2008 (Pub. L. 110-432).
