BREAKING: Federal Register: Lake Clark National Park Subsistence Resource Commission; Meetings

Published: 2013-09-03 | Source: Federal Register

As required by the Federal Advisory Committee Act (Pub. L. 92- 463, 86 Stat. 770), the National Park Service (NPS) is hereby giving notice that the Lake Clark National Park Subsistence Resource Commission (SRC) will hold meetings to develop and continue work on NPS subsistence program recommendations and other related subsistence management issues.
