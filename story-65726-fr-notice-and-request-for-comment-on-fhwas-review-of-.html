BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice and Request for Comment on FHWA's Review of Its General Applicability Waiver of Buy America Requirements for Manufactured Products

Published: 2023-04-21 | Source: Federal Register

The FHWA is extending the comment period for a request for comments on its existing general applicability waiver for manufactured products under FHWA's Buy America waiver authorities, which was published on March 17, 2023, in the Federal Register. The comment period is currently set to close on April 17, 2023.
