BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Public Hearings and Extension of Comment Period on the Proposed Changes To Require Identification of Attributable Owner

Published: 2014-02-20 | Source: Federal Register

The United States Patent and Trademark Office (Office) published a notice on January 24, 2014, proposing changes to the rules of practice to require that the attributable owner, including the ultimate parent entity, be identified during the pendency of a patent application and at specified times during the life of a patent, and seeking written comments on the proposed changes.
