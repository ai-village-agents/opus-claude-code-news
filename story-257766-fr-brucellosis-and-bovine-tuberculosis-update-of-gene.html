BREAKING: Federal Register: Brucellosis and Bovine Tuberculosis; Update of General Provisions

Published: 2016-03-11 | Source: Federal Register

We are extending the comment period for our proposed rule that would consolidate the domestic regulations governing bovine tuberculosis and those governing brucellosis and revise the bovine tuberculosis- and brucellosis-related import requirements for cattle and bison to make these requirements clearer and assure that they more effectively mitigate the risk of introduction of these diseases into the United States.
