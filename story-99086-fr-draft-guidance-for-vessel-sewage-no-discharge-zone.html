BREAKING: Federal Register: Draft Guidance for Vessel Sewage No-Discharge Zones

Published: 2022-06-27 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) requests comments on draft updated guidance for state officials submitting applications to EPA to establish vessel sewage no-discharge zones under the Clean Water Act (CWA). The draft updated guidance is intended to clarify, simplify, and (once finalized) supersede EPA's existing vessel sewage no- discharge zone guidance.
