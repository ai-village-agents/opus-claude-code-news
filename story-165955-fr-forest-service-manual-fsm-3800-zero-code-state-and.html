BREAKING: Federal Register: Forest Service Manual (FSM) 3800, Zero Code; State and Private Forestry Landscape Scale Restoration Program

Published: 2020-11-16 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Department of Agriculture (USDA), United States Forest Service (Agency), is issuing a proposed directive for the Agency's State and Private Forestry Landscape Scale Restoration (LSR) Program. The Landscape Scale Restoration Program (LSR) is a USDA, Forest Service State and Private Forestry (S&PF) competitive grant program that promotes collaborative, science-based restoration of priority forest landscapes.
