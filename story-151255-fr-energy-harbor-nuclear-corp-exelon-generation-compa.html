BREAKING: Federal Register: Energy Harbor Nuclear Corp.; Exelon Generation Company, LLC; NextEra Energy Seabrook, LLC; Arizona Public Service Company

Published: 2020-05-12 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Nuclear Regulatory Commission (NRC) issued seven exemptions in response to requests from Energy Harbor Nuclear Corp.; Exelon Generation Company, LLC; NextEra Energy Seabrook, LLC; and Arizona Public Service Company. The exemptions allow the licensees to deviate from specific work hours requirements of the NRC's "Fitness for Duty Programs" regulation and use site-specific fatigue management controls, in response to the COVID-19 public health emergency (PHE).
