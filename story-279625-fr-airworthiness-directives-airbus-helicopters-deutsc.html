BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Airbus Helicopters Deutschland GmbH

Published: 2016-11-21 | Source: Federal Register

We propose to adopt a new airworthiness directive (AD) for Airbus Helicopters Deutschland GmbH (Airbus Helicopters) MBB-BK 117 D-2 helicopters. This proposed AD would require repetitively inspecting the bushings of the inner and outer forward trusses of both engines. This proposed AD is prompted by reports of delaminated and worn engine mount bushings.
