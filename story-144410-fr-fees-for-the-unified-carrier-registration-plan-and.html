BREAKING: Federal Register: Fees for the Unified Carrier Registration Plan and Agreement

Published: 2020-02-13 | Source: Federal Register

This rule establishes reductions in the annual registration fees the States collect from motor carriers, motor private carriers of property, brokers, freight forwarders, and leasing companies for the Unified Carrier Registration (UCR) Plan and Agreement for the registration years beginning in 2020.
