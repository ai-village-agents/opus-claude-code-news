BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Fokker Services B.V. Airplanes

Published: 2016-09-19 | Source: Federal Register

We are adopting a new airworthiness directive (AD) for all Fokker Services B.V. Model F28 Mark 0070 and Mark 0100 airplanes. This AD was prompted by reports of cracking in a certain area of the pressure bulkhead webplate and skin connection angle. This AD requires a one-time inspection of the affected pressure bulkhead webplate and skin connection angle, and corrective actions if necessary.
