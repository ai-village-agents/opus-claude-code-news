BREAKING: Federal Register: Privacy Act of 1974; as Amended; Notice of a New System of Records

Published: 2014-05-28 | Source: Federal Register

Pursuant to the provisions of the Privacy Act of 1974, as amended, the Department of the Interior is issuing a public notice of its intent to create the National Park Service "Planning, Environment and Public Comment (PEPC) System--NPS-23" system of records. The system is an online collaborative tool designed to facilitate the project management process in conservation planning and environmental impact analysis.
