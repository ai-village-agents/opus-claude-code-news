BREAKING: Federal Register: Ground Water Protection at Uranium In Situ Recovery Facilities

Published: 2019-01-31 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Nuclear Regulatory Commission (NRC) is requesting views from interested stakeholders on whether the NRC should resume rulemaking to amend its regulations governing the domestic licensing of source material by codifying general requirements to address ground water protection at uranium in situ recovery (ISR) facilities.
