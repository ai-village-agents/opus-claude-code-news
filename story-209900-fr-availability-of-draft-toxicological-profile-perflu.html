BREAKING: Federal Register: Availability of Draft Toxicological Profile: Perfluoroalkyls

Published: 2018-06-21 | Source: Federal Register

The Agency for Toxic Substances and Disease Registry (ATSDR), within the Department of Health and Human Services (HHS) announces the availability of the Draft Toxicological Profile for Perfluoroalkyls for review and comment. All toxicological profiles issued as "Drafts for Public Comment" represent ATSDR's best efforts to provide important toxicological information on priority hazardous substances.
