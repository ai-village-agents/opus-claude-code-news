BREAKING: Federal Register: Rural Development Voucher Program

Published: 2013-08-14 | Source: Federal Register

The Rural Housing Service, an agency within the Rural Development mission area, is adding new regulations to implement its Rural Development Voucher Program (RDVP). Section 542 of the Housing Act of 1949, as amended, authorizes RDVP.
