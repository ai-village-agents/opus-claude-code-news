BREAKING: Federal Register: FCC Extends Reply Comment Dates for Indecency Cases Policy

Published: 2013-07-23 | Source: Federal Register

In this document, the Federal Communications Commission Enforcement Bureau and Office of General Counsel extend the deadlines for filing reply comments in GN Docket No. 13-86 by 15 days. On July 1, 2013, the College Broadcasters, Inc. (CBI) requested an extension for filing reply comments. We recognize the importance of affording all interested parties sufficient time to review the comments in the Docket and to prepare their reply comments as warranted.
