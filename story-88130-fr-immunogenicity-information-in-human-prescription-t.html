BREAKING: Federal Register: Immunogenicity Information in Human Prescription Therapeutic Protein and Select Drug Product Labeling-Content and Format; Draft Guidance for Industry; Availability

Published: 2022-02-04 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA or the Agency) is announcing the availability of a draft guidance for industry entitled "Immunogenicity Information in Human Prescription Therapeutic Protein and Select Drug Product Labeling--Content and Format." This draft guidance provides recommendations for incorporating clinically relevant immunogenicity information into the labeling of products having immunogenicity assessments.
