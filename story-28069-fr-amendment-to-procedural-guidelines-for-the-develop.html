The U.S. Department of Labor's Bureau of International Labor Affairs ("ILAB") amends its procedural guidelines ("Guidelines") for the development and maintenance of a list of goods from countries that ILAB has reason to believe are produced by child labor or forced labor in violation of international standards ("List").
