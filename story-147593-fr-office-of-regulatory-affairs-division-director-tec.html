BREAKING: Federal Register: Office of Regulatory Affairs Division Director; Technical Amendments

Published: 2020-03-24 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA, the Agency, or we) is revising chapter I of its regulations. These revisions are necessary to reflect changes to the Agency's organizational structure, including the reorganization of the Office of Regulatory Affairs. The revisions replace references to the District Director with references to the Division Director and make other related changes. The rule does not impose any new regulatory requirements on affected parties. This action is editorial in nature...
