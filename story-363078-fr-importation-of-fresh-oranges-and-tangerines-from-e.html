BREAKING: Federal Register: Importation of Fresh Oranges and Tangerines From Egypt Into the United States

Published: 2013-08-16 | Source: Federal Register

We are advising the public of our decision to allow the importation of oranges and tangerines from Egypt. Based on the findings of a pest list and commodity import evaluation document, which we made available to the public for review and comment through a previous notice, we have concluded that the application of one or more designated phytosanitary measures will be sufficient to mitigate the pest risk associated with the importation of oranges and tangerines from Egypt.
