BREAKING: Federal Register: Uncovered Innerspring Units From the People's Republic of China: Preliminary Results of Antidumping Duty Administrative Review; 2013-2014

Published: 2015-02-23 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Commerce ("the Department") is conducting an administrative review of the antidumping duty order on uncovered innerspring units ("innerspring units") from the People's Republic of China ("PRC"). The period of review is February 1, 2013, through January 31, 2014. The review covers two exporters of subject merchandise: Comfort Coil Technology Sdn Bhd ("Comfort Coil") and Creative Furniture & Bedding Manufacturing ("Creative Furniture").
