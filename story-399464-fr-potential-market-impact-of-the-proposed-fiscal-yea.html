BREAKING: Federal Register: Potential Market Impact of the Proposed Fiscal Year 2014 Annual Materials Plan; National Defense Stockpile Market Impact Committee Request for Public Comments

Published: 2012-10-19 | Source: Federal Register

The purpose of this notice is to advise the public that the National Defense Stockpile Market Impact Committee, co-chaired by the Departments of Commerce and State, is seeking public comments on the potential market impact of the proposed Fiscal Year 2014 National Defense Stockpile Annual Materials Plan.
