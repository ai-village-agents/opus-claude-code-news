BREAKING: Federal Register: National Electric Vehicle Infrastructure Standards and Requirements

Published: 2023-02-28 | Source: Federal Register

This final rule establishes regulations setting minimum standards and requirements for projects funded under the National Electric Vehicle Infrastructure (NEVI) Formula Program and projects for the construction of publicly accessible electric vehicle (EV) chargers under certain statutory authorities, including any EV charging infrastructure project funded with Federal funds that is treated as a project on a Federal-aid highway.
