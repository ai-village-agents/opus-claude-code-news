BREAKING: Federal Register: Hours of Service of Drivers: U.S. Department of Energy (DOE); FAST Act Extension of Expiration Date

Published: 2016-07-25 | Source: Federal Register

FMCSA announces the extension of the hours-of-service (HOS) exemption granted to the U.S. Department of Energy (DOE) on June 30, 2015, for certain commercial motor vehicle (CMV) drivers. The Agency extends the expiration date of the exemption to June 29, 2020 in response to section 5206(b)(2)(A) of the "Fixing America's Surface Transportation Act" (FAST Act).
