BREAKING: Federal Register: Meeting of the United States-Colombia Environmental Affairs Council and Environmental Cooperation Commission and Request for Comments on the Meeting Agendas

Published: 2013-12-04 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of State and the Office of the United States Trade Representative (USTR) are providing notice that the United States and Colombia intend to hold the first meeting of the Environmental Affairs Council (the ``Council'') and the first meeting of the Environmental Cooperation Commission (the ``Commission'') on December 18 and 19, 2013.
