BREAKING: Federal Register: Endangered and Threatened Species; Take of Anadromous Fish

Published: 2017-01-11 | Source: Federal Register

Notice is hereby given that the Northwest Indian Fisheries Commission (NWIFC) has submitted a Tribal Resource Management Plan (Tribal Plan) for NMFS to evaluate. It was presented by the Bureau of Indian Affairs (BIA) on behalf of the Northwest Indian Tribes.
