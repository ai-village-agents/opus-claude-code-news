BREAKING: Federal Register: Daimler Trucks North America, LLC, Denial of Petition for Decision of Inconsequential Noncompliance

Published: 2024-07-19 | Source: Federal Register

Daimler Trucks North America LLC (DTNA) has determined that certain model year (MY) 2019-2022 Thomas Built Bus school buses do not fully comply with Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard (FMVSS) No. 217, Bus Emergency Exits and Window Retention and Release. DTNA filed an original noncompliance report dated February 9, 2022, and amended the report on April 13, 2022.
