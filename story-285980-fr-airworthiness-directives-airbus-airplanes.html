BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Airbus Airplanes

Published: 2015-02-09 | Source: Federal Register

We are adopting a new airworthiness directive (AD) for certain Airbus Model A319-115, A319-133, A320-214, A320-232, and A320-233 airplanes. This AD requires repetitive detailed inspections of the outboard main landing gear (MLG) support rib lower flange fasteners for discrepancies, and corrective actions if necessary.
