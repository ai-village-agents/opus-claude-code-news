BREAKING: Federal Register: Freedom of Information Act

Published: 2025-11-24 | Source: Federal Register

This proposed rule amends the Defense Nuclear Facilities Board's (the Board or DNFSB) Freedom of Information Act (FOIA) regulations to incorporate certain changes made by the OPEN Government Act of 2007 and the FOIA Improvement Act of 2016.
