BREAKING: Federal Register: Security Zone; Surfside Building Collapse, Surfside, FL

Published: 2021-07-08 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is establishing a temporary security zone for certain navigable waters of the Atlantic Ocean near the town of Surfside, Florida. The security zone will cover all navigable waters from approximately one-half nautical mile north and south of the Champlain Towers South (accident site) and 500 yards offshore.
