BREAKING: Federal Register: Amendment of Class E Airspace; Dubois, PA

Published: 2020-12-11 | Source: Federal Register

This action amends Class E surface airspace and Class E airspace extending upward from 700 feet above the surface in Dubois, PA, due to the decommissioning of the Clarion Very High Frequency Omnidirectional Range Tactical Air Navigational System (VORTAC) and cancellation of the associated instrument approach procedure at Dubois Regional Airport.
