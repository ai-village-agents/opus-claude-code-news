BREAKING: Federal Register: Schedule for Rating Disabilities: Musculoskeletal System and Muscle Injuries; Correction

Published: 2021-02-04 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Veterans Affairs (VA) is making correcting amendments to the final rule published on November 30, 2020. The final rule amends the Department of Veterans Affairs (VA) Schedule for Rating Disabilities ("VASRD" or "rating schedule") by revising the portion of the rating schedule that addresses the musculoskeletal system.
