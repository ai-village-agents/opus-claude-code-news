BREAKING: Federal Register: Proposed Revocation of Class E Airspace; Rocksprings Four Square Ranch Airport and Sonora Canyon Ranch, TX

Published: 2022-05-09 | Source: Federal Register

This action proposes to remove the Class E airspace at Rocksprings Four Square Ranch Airport, TX, and Sonora Canyon Ranch, TX. The FAA is proposing this action due to the cancellation of the instrument procedures at the associated airports, and the airspace no longer being required.
