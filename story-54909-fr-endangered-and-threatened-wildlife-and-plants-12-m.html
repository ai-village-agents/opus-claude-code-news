BREAKING: Federal Register: Endangered and Threatened Wildlife and Plants; 12-Month Not-Warranted Finding for the Rio Grande Cutthroat Trout

Published: 2024-12-10 | Source: Federal Register

We, the U.S. Fish and Wildlife Service (Service), announce a 12-month finding on a petition to list the Rio Grande cutthroat trout (Oncorhynchus clarkii virginalis) as an endangered or threatened species under the Endangered Species Act of 1973, as amended (Act). Rio Grande cutthroat trout, a subspecies of cutthroat trout (Oncorhynchus clarkii), inhabit high-elevation streams in New Mexico and southern Colorado.
