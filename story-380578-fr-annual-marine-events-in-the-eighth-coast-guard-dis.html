BREAKING: Federal Register: Annual Marine Events in the Eighth Coast Guard District

Published: 2012-03-16 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard will enforce Special Local Regulations for the Neches River Festival boat races in Port Neches, TX from 3 p.m. on May 4, 2012 through 6 p.m. on May 6, 2012. This action is necessary to provide for the safety of the race participants, crew, spectators, participating vessels, non-participating vessels and other users of the waterway.
