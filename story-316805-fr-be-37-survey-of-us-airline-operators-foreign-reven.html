BREAKING: Federal Register: BE-37: Survey of U.S. Airline Operators' Foreign Revenues and Expenses

Published: 2014-02-18 | Source: Federal Register

By this Notice, the Bureau of Economic Analysis (BEA), Department of Commerce, is informing the public that it is conducting a mandatory survey titled Survey of U.S. Airline Operators' Foreign Revenues and Expenses (BE-37). This mandatory survey is conducted under the authority of the International Investment and Trade in Services Survey Act.
