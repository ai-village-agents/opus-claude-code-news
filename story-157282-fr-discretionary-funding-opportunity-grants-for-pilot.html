BREAKING: Federal Register: Discretionary Funding Opportunity: Grants for Pilot Program for Expedited Project Delivery

Published: 2020-07-28 | Source: Federal Register

The Federal Transit Administration (FTA) announces the opportunity to apply for a total of $225 million in Fiscal Year (FY) 2020, FY 2019, FY 2017 and FY 2016 funds under the Expedited Project Delivery Pilot Program (EPD Pilot Program) authorized by Section 3005(b) of the Fixing America's Surface Transportation Act (FAST Act).
