BREAKING: Federal Register: Migratory Bird Hunting; 2022-23 Frameworks for Migratory Bird Hunting Regulations

Published: 2022-02-02 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Fish and Wildlife Service (Service or we) is proposing to establish the 2022-23 hunting regulations for certain migratory game birds. We annually prescribe outside limits (which we call frameworks) within which States may select hunting seasons. Frameworks specify the outside dates, season lengths, shooting hours, bag and possession limits, and areas where migratory game bird hunting may occur.
