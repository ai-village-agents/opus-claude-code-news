BREAKING: Federal Register: Specialty Crops; Import Regulations; New Pistachio Import Requirements

Published: 2012-08-27 | Source: Federal Register

This rule establishes a minimum quality regulation for lots of pistachios imported into the United States. The regulation specifies maximum aflatoxin tolerance levels as well as mandatory aflatoxin testing and certification requirements. These import quality requirements are the same as or comparable to those in effect for the domestically produced commodity.
