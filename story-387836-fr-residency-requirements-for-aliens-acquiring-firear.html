BREAKING: Federal Register: Residency Requirements for Aliens Acquiring Firearms (2011R-23P)

Published: 2012-06-07 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Justice is amending the regulations of the Bureau of Alcohol, Tobacco, Firearms, and Explosives (ATF) by removing the 90-day State residency requirement for aliens lawfully present in the United States to purchase or acquire a firearm.
