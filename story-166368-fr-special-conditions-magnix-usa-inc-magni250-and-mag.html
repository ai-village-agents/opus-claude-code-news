BREAKING: Federal Register: Special Conditions: magniX USA, Inc., magni250 and magni500 Model Engines

Published: 2020-11-19 | Source: Federal Register

This action proposes special conditions for magniX USA, Inc. (magniX), magni250 and magni500 model engines that operate using electrical technology installed on the aircraft for use as an aircraft engine. These engines have a novel or unusual design feature when compared to the state of technology envisioned in the airworthiness standards applicable to aircraft engines.
