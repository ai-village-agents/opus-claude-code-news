BREAKING: Federal Register: Work Opportunity Tax Credit (WOTC) Program

Published: 2024-06-11 | Source: Federal Register

This Notice announces the revised administrative formula methodology that ETA uses to distribute annual allotment funding to State grantees (53 State Workforce Agencies (SWAs)) for the purpose of administering the Work Opportunity Tax Credit (WOTC).
