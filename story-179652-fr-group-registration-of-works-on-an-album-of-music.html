BREAKING: Federal Register: Group Registration of Works on an Album of Music

Published: 2019-05-20 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Copyright Office is proposing to create a new group registration option for musical works, sound recordings, and certain other works contained on an album. The proposed rule will expand the registration options currently available to register multiple musical works or sound recordings under one application.
