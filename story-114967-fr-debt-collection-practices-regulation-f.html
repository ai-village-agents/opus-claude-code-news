BREAKING: Federal Register: Debt Collection Practices (Regulation F)

Published: 2021-01-19 | Source: Federal Register

The Bureau of Consumer Financial Protection (Bureau) is issuing this final rule to revise Regulation F, which implements the Fair Debt Collection Practices Act (FDCPA). The final rule governs certain activities by debt collectors, as that term is defined in the FDCPA.
