BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Finding of Failure To Submit State Plans for the Municipal Solid Waste Landfills Emission Guidelines

Published: 2020-03-12 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) finds that 42 states and territories have failed to submit state plans for the 2016 Emission Guidelines and Compliance Times for Municipal Solid Waste Landfills (MSW Landfills EG). According to the MSW Landfills EG, states were required to submit state plans to the EPA for review and approval by August 29, 2019.
