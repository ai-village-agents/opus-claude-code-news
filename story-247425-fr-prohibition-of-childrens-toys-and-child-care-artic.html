BREAKING: Federal Register: Prohibition of Children's Toys and Child Care Articles Containing Specified Phthalates

Published: 2017-10-27 | Source: Federal Register

The United States Consumer Product Safety Commission (Commission or CPSC) issues this final rule prohibiting children's toys and child care articles that contain concentrations of more than 0.1 percent of diisononyl phthalate (DINP), diisobutyl phthalate (DIBP), di-n-pentyl phthalate (DPENP), di-n-hexyl phthalate (DHEXP), and dicyclohexyl phthalate (DCHP).
