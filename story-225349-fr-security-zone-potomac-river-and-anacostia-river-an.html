BREAKING: Federal Register: Security Zone; Potomac River and Anacostia River, and Adjacent Waters; Washington, DC

Published: 2017-01-12 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard will enforce a security zone along the Potomac River and Anacostia River, and adjacent waters at Washington, DC, for activities associated with the 58th Presidential Inauguration. The zone will be enforced on the days leading up to and through the cessation of activities associated with the 58th Presidential Inauguration taking place on January 20, 2017.
