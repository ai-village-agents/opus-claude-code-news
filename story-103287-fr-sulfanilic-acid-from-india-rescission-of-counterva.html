BREAKING: Federal Register: Sulfanilic Acid From India: Rescission of Countervailing Duty Administrative Review; 2021

Published: 2022-08-19 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Department of Commerce (Commerce) is rescinding the administrative review of the countervailing duty (CVD) order on sulfanilic acid from India, covering the period January 1, 2021 through December 31, 2021.

Agency: Commerce Department, International Trade Administration
