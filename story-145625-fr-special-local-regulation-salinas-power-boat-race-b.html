BREAKING: Federal Register: Special Local Regulation, Salinas Power Boat Race; Bahia De Rincon, PR

Published: 2020-02-28 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is establishing a special local regulation on the waters of Bahia De Rincon, Puerto Rico during the Salinas Power Boat Race. Approximatly 50 high speed boats and personal water crafts are expected to participate in the race. The special local regulation is necessary to ensure the safety to race participants, participant vessels, and the general pubic during the event.
