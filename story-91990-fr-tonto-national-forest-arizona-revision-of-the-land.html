BREAKING: Federal Register: Tonto National Forest; Arizona; Revision of the Land Management Plan for the Tonto National Forest

The Forest Service is revising the Tonto National Forest's Land Management Plan (Forest Plan). The Forest Service has prepared a Final Environmental Impact Statement (FEIS) for its revised Forest Plan and a draft Record of Decision (ROD).
