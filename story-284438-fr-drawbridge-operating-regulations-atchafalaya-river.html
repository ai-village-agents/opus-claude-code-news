BREAKING: Federal Register: Drawbridge Operating Regulations; Atchafalaya River, Morgan City, Louisiana

Published: 2015-01-22 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is modifying the effective date of a published temporary deviation from the operating schedule that governs the Morgan City (Berwick Bay) Railroad Bridge across the Atchafalaya River, mile 17.5 (Gulf Intracoastal Waterway (Morgan City-Port Allen Alternate Route) mile 0.3) in Morgan City, St. Mary Parish, Louisiana.
