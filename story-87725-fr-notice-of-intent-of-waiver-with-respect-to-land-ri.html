BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Intent of Waiver With Respect to Land; Rickenbacker International Airport, Columbus, Ohio

Published: 2022-02-01 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA is considering a proposal to change approximately 328 acres of airport land from aeronautical use to non-aeronautical use and to authorize the lease and ultimate sale of airport property located at Rickenbacker International Airport, Columbus, Ohio. The aforementioned land is not needed for aeronautical use. The property is located southeast of the airfield and currently consists of vacant land, paved roadways, fencing and utilities.
