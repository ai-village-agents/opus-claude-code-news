BREAKING: Federal Register: State of Tennessee Underground Injection Control (UIC) Program; Primacy Approval

Published: 2015-04-06 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) proposes to approve the State of Tennessee Underground Injection Control (UIC) Program for primacy to implement and enforce state regulations for all UIC injection wells located within the state, except for Class VI wells and all wells on Indian lands, as required by rule under the Safe Drinking Water Act (SDWA).
