BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; The Boeing Company Airplanes

Published: 2020-05-01 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA is revising an earlier proposal for certain The Boeing Company Model 737-600, -700, -700C, -800, and -900 series airplanes. This action revises the notice of proposed rulemaking (NPRM) by revising certain inspections to provide the correct thickness callouts for the fuselage skin and bear strap.
