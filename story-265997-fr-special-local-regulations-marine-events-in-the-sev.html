BREAKING: Federal Register: Special Local Regulations; Marine Events in the Seventh Coast Guard District

Published: 2016-06-13 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is updating the regulation that governs recurring special local regulations in the Seventh Coast Guard District. These regulations will apply to all recurring events held on navigable waters of the Seventh District, such as regattas and parades.
