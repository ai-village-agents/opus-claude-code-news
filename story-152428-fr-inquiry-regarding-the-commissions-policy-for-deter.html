BREAKING: Federal Register: Inquiry Regarding the Commission's Policy for Determining Return on Equity

Published: 2020-05-27 | Source: Federal Register

On March 21, 2019, the Federal Energy Regulatory Commission issued a notice of inquiry seeking information and stakeholder views regarding whether, and if so how, it should modify its policies concerning the determination of the return on equity (ROE) to be used in designing jurisdictional public utility rates and whether any changes to the Commission's policies concerning public utility ROEs should be applied to interstate natural gas and oil pipelines.
