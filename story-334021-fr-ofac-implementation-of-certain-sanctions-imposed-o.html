BREAKING: Federal Register: OFAC Implementation of Certain Sanctions Imposed on JAM PETROCHEMICAL COMPANY and NIKSIMA FOOD AND BEVERAGE JLT Pursuant to Executive Order 13622 of July 30, 2012

Published: 2014-09-05 | Source: Federal Register

The Treasury Department's Office of Foreign Assets Control ("OFAC") is providing notice of actions taken by OFAC to implement certain of the sanctions imposed on two entities by the Secretary of State pursuant to Executive Order 13622 of July 30, 2012, "Authorizing Additional Sanctions With Respect to Iran."
