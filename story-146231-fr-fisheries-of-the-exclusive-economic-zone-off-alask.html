BREAKING: Federal Register: Fisheries of the Exclusive Economic Zone Off Alaska; Adjust the North Pacific Observer Program Fee

Published: 2020-03-09 | Source: Federal Register

NMFS proposes regulations to adjust the North Pacific Observer Program fee. This proposed rule is intended to increase funds available to support observer and EM deployment in the partial coverage category of the Observer Program and increase the likelihood of meeting desired monitoring objectives.
