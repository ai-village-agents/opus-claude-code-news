BREAKING: Federal Register: Seventh Annual Drug Information Association/Food and Drug Administration Statistics Forum-2013; Public Conference

Published: 2013-02-26 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA), in cosponsorship with the Drug Information Association (DIA), is announcing a public conference entitled "Seventh Annual DIA/FDA Statistics Forum--2013." The purpose of the conference is to discuss relevant statistical issues associated with the development and review of therapeutic drugs and biologics.
