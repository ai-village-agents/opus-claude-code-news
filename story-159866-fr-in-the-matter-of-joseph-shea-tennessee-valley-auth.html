BREAKING: Federal Register: In the Matter of Joseph Shea, Tennessee Valley Authority, Chattanooga, TN

The U.S. Nuclear Regulatory Commission (NRC) is issuing an Order prohibiting involvement in NRC-licensed activities to Mr. Joseph Shea, Vice President Nuclear Technology Innovation at Tennessee Valley Authority (TVA), for 5 years.
