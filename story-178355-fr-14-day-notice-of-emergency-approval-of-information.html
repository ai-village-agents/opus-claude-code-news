BREAKING: Federal Register: 14-Day Notice of Emergency Approval of Information Collection: The Housing Counseling Program; Correction

Published: 2019-05-06 | Source: Federal Register

On April 25, 2019 at 84 FR 17419, HUD published a 14-day emergency notice of proposed information collection entitled, "The Housing Counseling Program" (FR-7011-N-12) (FR Doc. 2019-08371). The notice contained the incorrect burden hours. The correct burden hours should be 20,224. Today's notice corrects the 14-day notice published on April 25, 2019.
