BREAKING: Federal Register: Removal of Certain Entities From the Entity List; and Revisions of Entries on the Entity List

Published: 2017-09-25 | Source: Federal Register

This rule amends the Export Administration Regulations (EAR) by removing three entities under four entries from the Entity List. This rule removes one entity listed under the destination of Australia, one entity listed under the destination of China, and one entity listed under the destinations of Iran and the United Arab Emirates from the Entity List.
