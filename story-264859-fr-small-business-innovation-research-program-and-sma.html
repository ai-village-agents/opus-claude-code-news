BREAKING: Federal Register: Small Business Innovation Research Program and Small Business Technology Transfer Program Policy Directive

Published: 2016-05-31 | Source: Federal Register

On April 7, 2016, the U.S. Small Business Administration (SBA) published a notice in the Federal Register to solicit public comments on a proposed SBIR/STTR Policy Directive, which among other things seeks to clarify the data rights and Phase III preference afforded to SBIR and STTR small business awardees, add definitions relating to data rights, and clarify the benchmarks for progress towards commercialization.
