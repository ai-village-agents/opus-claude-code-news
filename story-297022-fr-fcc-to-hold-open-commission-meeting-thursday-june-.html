BREAKING: Federal Register: FCC To Hold Open Commission Meeting Thursday, June 18, 2015

Published: 2015-06-22 | Source: Federal Register

The Commission will consider a Second Further Notice of Proposed Rulemaking, Order on Reconsideration, Second Report and Order, and Memorandum Opinion and Order in order to comprehensively restructure and modernize the Lifeline program to efficiently and effectively connect low-income Americans to broadband, strengthen program oversight and administration, and take additional measures to eliminate waste, fraud, and abuse.
