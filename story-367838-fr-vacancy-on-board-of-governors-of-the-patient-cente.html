BREAKING: Federal Register: Vacancy on Board of Governors of the Patient-Centered Outcomes Research Institute (PCORI)

Published: 2013-10-23 | Source: Federal Register

The Patient Protection and Affordable Care Act gave the Comptroller General of the United States responsibility for appointing 19 members to the Board of Governors of the Patient-Centered Outcomes Research Institute and for filling vacancies that may occur. Board members must meet the qualifications listed in Section 6301 of the Act.
