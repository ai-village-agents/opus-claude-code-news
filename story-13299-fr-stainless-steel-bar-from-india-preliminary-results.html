BREAKING: Federal Register: Stainless Steel Bar From India: Preliminary Results and Intent To Rescind, in Part, of Antidumping Duty Administrative Review; 2023-2024

Published: 2025-06-11 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Department of Commerce (Commerce) is conducting an administrative review of the antidumping duty (AD) order on stainless steel bar from India. The period of review (POR) is February 1, 2023, through January 31, 2024. This review covers eight producers/exporters of the subject merchandise.
