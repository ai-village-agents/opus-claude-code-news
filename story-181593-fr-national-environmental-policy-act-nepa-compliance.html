BREAKING: Federal Register: National Environmental Policy Act (NEPA) Compliance

Published: 2019-06-13 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Department of Agriculture, Forest Service (Agency) is proposing revisions to its National Environmental Policy Act (NEPA) regulations. The Agency proposes these revisions to increase efficiency in its environmental analysis while meeting NEPA's requirements and fully honoring its environmental stewardship responsibilities.
