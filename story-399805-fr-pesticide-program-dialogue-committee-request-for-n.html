BREAKING: Federal Register: Pesticide Program Dialogue Committee; Request for Nominations to the Pesticide Program Dialogue Committee

Published: 2012-10-24 | Source: Federal Register

Pursuant to the Federal Advisory Committee Act, the Environmental Protection Agency's (EPA's) Office of Pesticide Programs is inviting nominations from a diverse range of qualified candidates to be considered for appointment to the Pesticide Program Dialogue Committee (PPDC). EPA values and welcomes diversity. In an effort to obtain nominations of diverse candidates, EPA encourages nominations of women and men of all racial and ethnic groups.
