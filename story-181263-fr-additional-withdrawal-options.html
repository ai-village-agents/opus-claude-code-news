BREAKING: Federal Register: Additional Withdrawal Options

Published: 2019-06-10 | Source: Federal Register

The Federal Retirement Thrift Investment Board ("FRTIB") is proposing to amend its regulations to provide TSP participants with additional withdrawal options and flexibility, effective September 15, 2019.
