BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Airbus SAS Airplanes

Published: 2021-10-22 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA proposes to supersede Airworthiness Directive (AD) 2020-16-01, which applies to all Airbus SAS Model A318, A319, A320, and A321 series airplanes. AD 2020-16-01 requires repetitive cleaning and greasing of affected cargo door seals (both original equipment manufacturer (OEM) and parts manufacturer approval (PMA) parts).
