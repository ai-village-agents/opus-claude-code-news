BREAKING: Federal Register: Rescission of Certain Emergency Exemptions

Published: 2014-04-10 | Source: Federal Register

The Federal Motor Carrier Safety Administration (FMCSA), after consultation with representatives of the governors of the affected States, rescinds certain covered emergency exemptions. The exemptions originally took effect automatically upon declaration of an emergency by various governors or FMCSA, and were extended by FMCSA after consultation with representatives of the governors.
