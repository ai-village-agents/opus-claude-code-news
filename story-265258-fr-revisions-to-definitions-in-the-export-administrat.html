BREAKING: Federal Register: Revisions to Definitions in the Export Administration Regulations

Published: 2016-06-03 | Source: Federal Register

This final rule is part of the Administration's Export Control Reform (ECR) Initiative. The Initiative will enhance U.S. national and economic security, facilitate compliance with export controls, update the controls, and further the goal of reducing unnecessary regulatory burdens on U.S. exporters.
