BREAKING: Federal Register: Decision To Revise the Requirements for the Importation of Fresh Citrus Fruit From Australia Into the Continental United States

Published: 2021-08-18 | Source: Federal Register

We are advising the public of our decision to revise the requirements for the importation of citrus from Australia in order to authorize the importation into the continental United States of citrus from additional areas of production. Based on the findings of a pest risk analysis, which we made available to the public for review and comment through a previous notice, we have determined that the application of one or more designated phytosanitary measures will be sufficient to mitigate the risks ...
