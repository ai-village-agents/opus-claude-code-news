BREAKING: Federal Register: Financial Crimes Enforcement Network; Withdrawal of Notice of Proposed Rulemaking Regarding JSC CredexBank

Published: 2016-03-17 | Source: Federal Register

This document withdraws FinCEN's proposed rulemaking to impose the first and fifth special measure regarding JSC CredexBank ("Credex"), renamed JSC InterPayBank ("InterPay"), as a financial institution of primary money laundering concern, pursuant to Section 311 of the USA PATRIOT Act ("Section 311"). Because of material subsequent developments that have mitigated the money laundering risks associated with Credex, FinCEN has determined that Credex is no longer a primary money laundering concern ...
