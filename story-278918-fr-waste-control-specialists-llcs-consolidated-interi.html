BREAKING: Federal Register: Waste Control Specialists LLC's Consolidated Interim Spent Fuel Storage Facility Project

Published: 2016-11-14 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Nuclear Regulatory Commission (NRC) received a license application by letter dated April 28, 2016, from Waste Control Specialists LLC (WCS). By this application, WCS is requesting authorization to construct and operate a Consolidated Interim Storage Facility (CISF) for spent nuclear fuel at WCS's facility in Andrews County, Texas (the proposed action).
