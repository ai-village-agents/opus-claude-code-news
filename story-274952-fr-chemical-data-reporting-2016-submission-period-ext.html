BREAKING: Federal Register: Chemical Data Reporting; 2016 Submission Period Extension

Published: 2016-09-26 | Source: Federal Register

EPA is amending the Toxic Substances Control Act (TSCA) Chemical Data Reporting (CDR) regulations by extending the submission deadline for 2016 reports from September 30, 2016 to October 31, 2016. This is a one-time extension for the 2016 submission period only.
