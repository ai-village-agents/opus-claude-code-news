BREAKING: Federal Register: Denial of Motor Vehicle Defect Petition, DP22-003

Published: 2024-09-16 | Source: Federal Register

This notice sets forth the reasons for the denial of a defect petition (DP22-003) jointly submitted by a consulting firm and four automotive associations requesting that the Agency initiate a safety defect investigation into the loss of motive power incidents attributed to oil drain pan assembly failures on certain Hyundai Motor America, Inc. and Kia America, Inc. vehicles spanning Model Years (MY) 2005- 2021.
