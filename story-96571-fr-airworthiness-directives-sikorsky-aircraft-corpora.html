BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Sikorsky Aircraft Corporation Helicopters

Published: 2022-05-23 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA is superseding Airworthiness Directive (AD) 2020-26- 13, which applied to certain Sikorsky Aircraft Corporation (Sikorsky) Model S-92A helicopters. AD 2020-26-13 required establishing the life limit for certain part-numbered horizontal stabilizer root fittings FWD (forward root fittings) and certain part-numbered stabilizer strut fittings.
