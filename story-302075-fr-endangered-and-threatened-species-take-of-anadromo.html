BREAKING: Federal Register: Endangered and Threatened Species; Take of Anadromous Fish

Published: 2015-08-20 | Source: Federal Register

Notice is hereby given that NMFS has issued Endangered Species Act (ESA) scientific research Permit 18251 to the Marine Science Institute. Additionally, NMFS has received four scientific research and enhancement permit modification requests relating to anadromous species listed under the ESA. The proposed research activities are intended to increase knowledge of the species and to help guide management and conservation efforts.
