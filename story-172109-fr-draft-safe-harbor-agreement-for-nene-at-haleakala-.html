BREAKING: Federal Register: Draft Safe Harbor Agreement for Nene at Haleakala Ranch, Maui

Published: 2019-02-19 | Source: Federal Register

We, the U.S. Fish and Wildlife Service (Service), have received an application from the Haleakala Ranch Company (the Ranch) for an enhancement of survival permit (permit) pursuant to the Endangered Species Act of 1973, as amended (ESA). The permit application includes a draft Safe Harbor Agreement (SHA) between the Ranch, the State of Hawaii Department of Land and Natural Resources (DLNR), and the Service.
