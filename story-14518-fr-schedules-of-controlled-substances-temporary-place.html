BREAKING: Federal Register: Schedules of Controlled Substances: Temporary Placement of Seven Benzimidazole-Opioids in Schedule I

Published: 2025-06-26 | Source: Federal Register

The Acting Administrator of the Drug Enforcement Administration is issuing this notice of intent to publish a temporary order to schedule seven benzimidazole-opioid substances in schedule I of the Controlled Substances Act.
