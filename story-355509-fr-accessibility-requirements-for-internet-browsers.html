BREAKING: Federal Register: Accessibility Requirements for Internet Browsers

Published: 2013-05-22 | Source: Federal Register

In this document, the Commission adopts rules to implement section 718 of the Communications Act of 1934 (the Act), as amended, which was added to the Act by the Twenty-First Century Communications and Video Accessibility Act of 2010 (CVAA). Section 718 of the Act requires Internet browsers built into mobile phones to be accessible to individuals who are blind or visually impaired.
