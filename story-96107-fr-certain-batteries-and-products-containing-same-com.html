BREAKING: Federal Register: Certain Batteries and Products Containing Same; Commission Decision To Review in Part an Initial Determination Granting in Part Complainants' Motion for Summary Determination of a Violation of Section 337; Request for Written Submissions

Published: 2022-05-17 | Source: Federal Register

Notice is hereby given that the U.S. International Trade Commission has determined to review in part an initial determination ("ID") (Order No. 15) of the presiding Administrative Law Judge ("ALJ") granting-in-part the complainants' motion for summary determination of a violation of section 337.
