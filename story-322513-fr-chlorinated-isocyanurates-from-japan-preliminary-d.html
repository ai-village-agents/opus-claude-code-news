BREAKING: Federal Register: Chlorinated Isocyanurates From Japan: Preliminary Determination of Sales at Less Than Fair Value and Postponement of Final Determination

Published: 2014-04-24 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Commerce ("the Department") preliminarily determines that chlorinated isocyanurates ("isos") from Japan is being, or is likely to be, sold in the United States at less than fair value ("LTFV"), as provided in section 733(b) of the Tariff Act of 1930, as amended ("the Act"). The period of investigation is July 1, 2012, through June 30, 2013.
