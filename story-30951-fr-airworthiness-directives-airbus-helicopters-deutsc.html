BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Airbus Helicopters Deutschland GmbH (AHD) Helicopters

Published: 2024-03-04 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA is adopting a new airworthiness directive (AD) for all Airbus Helicopters Deutschland GmbH (AHD) Model EC135P1, EC135P2, EC135P2+, EC135P3, EC135T1, EC135T2, EC135T2+, EC135T2, EC135T3, and EC635T2+ helicopters. This AD was prompted by a report of a separated tail rotor (T/R) blade due to a crack which was caused by intergranular corrosion.
