BREAKING: Federal Register: 2019 Data Call Under the Terrorism Risk Insurance Program

Published: 2019-04-04 | Source: Federal Register

Pursuant to the Terrorism Risk Insurance Act of 2002 (TRIA), insurers that participate in the Terrorism Risk Insurance Program (TRIP or Program) are directed to submit information for the 2019 TRIP Data Call for the reporting period from January 1, 2018 to December 31, 2018. Participating insurers are required to register and report information in a series of forms approved by the Office of Management and Budget (OMB).
