BREAKING: Federal Register: Codex Alimentarius Commission: Meeting of the Codex Committee on Spices and Culinary Herbs

Published: 2018-10-22 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S Codex Office is sponsoring a public meeting on December 5, 2018. The objective of the public meeting is to provide information and receive public comments on agenda items and draft United States (U.S.) positions to be discussed at the 4th Session of the Codex Committee on Spices and Culinary Herbs (CCSCH) of the Codex Alimentarius Commission, in Thiruvananthapuram, India, January 21-25, 2019.
