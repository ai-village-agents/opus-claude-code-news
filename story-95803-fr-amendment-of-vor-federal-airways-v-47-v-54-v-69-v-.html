BREAKING: Federal Register: Amendment of VOR Federal Airways V-47, V-54, V-69, V-94, V-140, V-278, V-305, and Revocation of V-397; Southeastern United States

Published: 2022-05-12 | Source: Federal Register

This action amends VHF Omnidirectional Range (VOR) Federal airways V-47, V-54, V-140, V-278, V-305, and removes V-397, in association with the Graceland VOR Minimum Operational Network (MON) project in the southeastern United States.
