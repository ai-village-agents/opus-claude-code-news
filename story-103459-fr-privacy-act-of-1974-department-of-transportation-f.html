BREAKING: Federal Register: Privacy Act of 1974; Department of Transportation, Federal Aviation Administration; DOT/FAA-815; Investigative Record System; System of Record Notice

Published: 2022-08-22 | Source: Federal Register

In accordance with the Privacy Act of 1974, the United States Department of Transportation (DOT), Federal Aviation Administration (FAA) proposes to update and reissue a current DOT system of records titled, "DOT/FAA-815, Investigative Record System." The system collects information on FAA employees, contractors, and members of the public in support of the Personnel Security Background and Internal investigations programs.
