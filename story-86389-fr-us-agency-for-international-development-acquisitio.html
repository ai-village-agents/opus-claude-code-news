BREAKING: Federal Register: U.S. Agency for International Development Acquisition Regulation (AIDAR): Revised and Expanded Fringe Benefits for U.S. Personal Services Contracts With Individuals

Published: 2022-01-13 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Agency for International Development (USAID) seeks public comment on a proposed rule to revise AIDAR in order to expand fringe benefits for personal services contracts with individuals who are U.S. nationals (USPSCs).
