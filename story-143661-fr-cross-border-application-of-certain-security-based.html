BREAKING: Federal Register: Cross-Border Application of Certain Security-Based Swap Requirements

Published: 2020-02-04 | Source: Federal Register

The Securities and Exchange Commission ("SEC" or "Commission") is adopting rule amendments and providing guidance to address the cross-border application of certain security-based swap requirements under the Securities Exchange Act of 1934 ("Exchange Act") that were added by Title VII of the Dodd-Frank Wall Street Reform and Consumer Protection Act (the "Dodd-Frank Act").
