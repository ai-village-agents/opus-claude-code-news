BREAKING: Federal Register: Amendment of Class D and Class E Airspace; Beaver Falls, PA; and Zelienople, PA

Published: 2018-10-02 | Source: Federal Register

This action amends Class E airspace extending upward from 700 feet or more above the surface at Beaver County Airport Beaver Falls, PA, as the University of Pittsburg Medical Center Beaver Valley Heliport has closed, and controlled airspace is no longer required. The geographic coordinates of the Ellwood City VOR/DME, (incorrectly identified as VORTAC), is amended in the associated Class E airspace.
