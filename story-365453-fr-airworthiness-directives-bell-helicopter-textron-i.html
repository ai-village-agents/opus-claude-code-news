BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Bell Helicopter Textron, Inc. (Bell) Helicopters

Published: 2013-09-13 | Source: Federal Register

We are superseding revised Airworthiness Directive (AD) 76-12- 07 for all Bell Model 204B and certain serial-numbered Model 205A-1 helicopters with a certain tail rotor pitch control chain (chain) installed. AD 76-12-07 required visually inspecting the chain to detect a crack in the link segments and, for affected Model 205A-1 helicopters, replacing the chain and cable control system with a push- pull control system.
