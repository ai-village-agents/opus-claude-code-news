BREAKING: Federal Register: Horseracing Integrity and Safety Authority Enforcement Rule Proposed Modification

Published: 2025-09-09 | Source: Federal Register

As required by the Horseracing Integrity and Safety Act of 2020, the Federal Trade Commission publishes a proposed modification of the Horseracing Integrity and Safety Authority's rules addressing horseracing in the United States.
