BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone; America's Cup Aerobatic Box, San Francisco Bay, San Francisco, CA

Published: 2013-09-19 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is establishing safety zones in the navigable waters of the San Francisco Bay near Pier 27 in San Francisco, CA in support of 2013 America's Cup air shows. These safety zones are established to provide a clear area on the water for pilots to initiate maneuvers and also provide for the safety of pilots, spectators, and other vessels transiting the area in the unlikely event that an aircraft crashes during the air show.
