BREAKING: Federal Register: Privacy Act of 1974; System of Records

Published: 2012-12-05 | Source: Federal Register

Pursuant to the provisions of the Privacy Act of 1974 (5 U.S.C. 552a), the United States Agency for International Development (USAID) is giving notice that it proposes to alter a system of records, the Partner Vetting System (PVS).
