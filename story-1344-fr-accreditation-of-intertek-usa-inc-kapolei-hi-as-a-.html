BREAKING: Federal Register: Accreditation of Intertek USA, Inc. (Kapolei, HI) as a Commercial Laboratory

Published: 2026-01-26 | Source: Federal Register

Notice is hereby given, pursuant to CBP regulations, that Intertek, USA, Inc., (Kapolei, HI), has been accredited to test petroleum and certain petroleum products for customs purposes for the next three years as of August 26, 2025.
