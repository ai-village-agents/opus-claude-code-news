BREAKING: Federal Register: Approval and Promulgation of Implementation Plans; American Samoa

Published: 2015-02-12 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is completing the process begun in 2005 to revise the format of the "identification of plan" section for the American Samoa State Implementation Plan (SIP). Specifically, the EPA is adding the nonregulatory provisions and quasi- regulatory measures to the revised "identification of plan" section.
