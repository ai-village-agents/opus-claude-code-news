BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone; Little Potato Slough, Stockton, CA

Published: 2024-01-16 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is establishing a temporary safety zone for navigable waters within a 100-yard radius of the vessels and machinery conducting operations at the site of the tug MAZAPETA in Little Potato Slough near Stockton, CA. The safety zone is needed to protect personnel, vessels, and the marine environment from potential hazards created by salvage and pollution removal operations.
