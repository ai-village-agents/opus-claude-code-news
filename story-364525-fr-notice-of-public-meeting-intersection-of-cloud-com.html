BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Public Meeting-Intersection of Cloud Computing and Mobility Forum and Workshop

Published: 2013-09-04 | Source: Federal Register

The National Institute of Standards and Technology (NIST) announces the Intersection of Cloud and Mobility Forum and Workshop to be held on Tuesday, October 1, Wednesday, October 2, and Thursday, October 3, 2013. The format is a three-day forum with breakout sessions held each day.
