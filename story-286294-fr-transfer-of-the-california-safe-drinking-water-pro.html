BREAKING: Federal Register: Transfer of the California Safe Drinking Water Program From the California Department of Public Health to the California State Water Resources Control Board

Published: 2015-02-12 | Source: Federal Register

This document informs the public that the California Drinking Water Program has been transferred from the California Department of Public Health to the California State Water Resources Control Board.
