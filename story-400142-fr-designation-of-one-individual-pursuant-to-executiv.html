BREAKING: Federal Register: Designation of One Individual Pursuant to Executive Order 13566 of February 25, 2011

Published: 2012-10-29 | Source: Federal Register

The Treasury Department's Office of Foreign Assets Control ("OFAC") is publishing the name of an individual designated on October 18, 2012 as a person whose property and interests in property is blocked pursuant to Executive Order 13566 of February 25, 2011 "Blocking Property and Prohibiting Certain Transactions Related to Libya."
