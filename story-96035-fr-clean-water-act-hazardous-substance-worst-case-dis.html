BREAKING: Federal Register: Clean Water Act Hazardous Substance Worst Case Discharge Planning Regulations; Extension of Comment Period

Published: 2022-05-16 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA or the Agency) is announcing an extension to the comment period for the proposed rule requiring an owner or operator of a facility to prepare and submit a plan for responding, to the maximum extent practicable, to a worst case discharge, and to a substantial threat of such a discharge, of a hazardous substance published in the Federal Register on March 28, 2022.
