BREAKING: Federal Register: Homeland Security Advisory Council

Published: 2022-08-02 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Homeland Security announces the rescheduling of the public meeting of the Homeland Security Advisory Council scheduled for August 3, 2022. This meeting will be held August 24, 2022 from 3 p.m. to 4 p.m. ET. The meeting is open to the public.
