BREAKING: Federal Register: Stainless Steel Bar From Spain: Preliminary Results of Antidumping Duty Administrative Review; 2012-2013

Published: 2014-04-23 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Commerce (the Department) is conducting an administrative review of the antidumping duty order on stainless steel bar (SSB) from Spain. The period of review (POR) is March 1, 2012, through February 28, 2013. The review covers one producer/exporter of the subject merchandise, Gerdau Aceros Especiales Europa, S.L.
