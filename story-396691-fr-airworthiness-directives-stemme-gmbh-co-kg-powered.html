BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Stemme GmbH & Co. KG Powered Sailplanes

Published: 2012-09-18 | Source: Federal Register

We propose to adopt a new airworthiness directive (AD) for all Stemme GmbH & Co. KG Models S10, S10-V, and S10-VT powered sailplanes. This proposed AD results from mandatory continuing airworthiness information (MCAI) originated by an aviation authority of another country to identify and correct an unsafe condition on an aviation product.
