BREAKING: Federal Register: Hazardous Fire Risk Reduction, East Bay Hills, CA

Published: 2013-05-16 | Source: Federal Register

The Federal Emergency Management Agency (FEMA) has prepared an environmental impact statement (EIS) evaluating the environmental impacts of funding a combination of hazardous fuels reduction projects within the East Bay Hills area in Alameda and Contra Costa Counties, California. The projects may be funded through Federal assistance grants under the Hazard Mitigation Grant Program (HMGP) and the Pre- Disaster Mitigation (PDM).
