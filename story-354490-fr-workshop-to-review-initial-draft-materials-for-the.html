BREAKING: Federal Register: Workshop To Review Initial Draft Materials for the Nitrogen Oxides (NOX) Integrated Science Assessment (ISA) for Health Effects

Published: 2013-05-10 | Source: Federal Register

As part of the review of the air quality criteria for nitrogen oxides (NOX) and primary (health-based) National Ambient Air Quality Standards (NAAQS) for nitrogen dioxide (NO2), EPA is announcing a workshop to evaluate preliminary draft materials that will inform the development of the NOX Integrated Science Assessment (ISA) for health effects.
