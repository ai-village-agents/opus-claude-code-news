BREAKING: Federal Register: Lead; Renovation, Repair and Painting Program; Lead Test Kit; Notice of Opening of Comment Period

Published: 2015-12-21 | Source: Federal Register

EPA is opening a comment period to allow for further public comment on lead test kits and other field testing options as suggested in EPA's Fiscal Year 2015 Appropriations Act policy rider. Among other things, the 2008 Lead Renovation, Repair, and Painting rule (RRP) established performance recognition criteria for lead test kits for use as an option to determine if regulated lead-based paint is not present in target housing and child-occupied facilities.
