BREAKING: Federal Register: Fees for Rice Inspection Services and Removal of Specific Fee References

Published: 2019-08-29 | Source: Federal Register

The Agricultural Marketing Service (AMS) invites comments on a proposal to reduce the fees for the sampling, inspection, weighing, and certification of rice performed under authority of the Agricultural Marketing Act of 1946 (AMA), as amended. Under the proposal, fees would decrease by 20 percent for fiscal year (FY) 2020 and by another 20 percent for FY 2021.
