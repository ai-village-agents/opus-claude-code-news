BREAKING: Federal Register: Schedule for Rating Disabilities: Neurological Conditions and Convulsive Disorders

Published: 2024-11-12 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Veterans Affairs (VA) proposes to amend the portion of the VA Schedule for Rating Disabilities (VASRD or Rating Schedule) that addresses neurological conditions and convulsive disorders. The purpose of these changes is to incorporate medical advancements that have occurred since the last revision, update current medical terminology, and provide clear evaluation criteria.
