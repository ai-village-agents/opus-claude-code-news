BREAKING: Federal Register: Southern California Regional Rail Authority's Request to Amend Its Positive Train Control Safety Plan and Positive Train Control System

Published: 2025-07-16 | Source: Federal Register

This document provides the public with notice that, on July 7, 2025, Southern California Regional Rail Authority (SCAX) submitted a request for amendment (RFA) to its FRA-approved Positive Train Control Safety Plan (PTCSP) and positive train control (PTC) system, seeking FRA's approval to, as a result of updates made to its onboard PTC software, modify its PTC braking algorithm.
