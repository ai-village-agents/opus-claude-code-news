BREAKING: Federal Register: Information Reporting of Catastrophic Health Coverage and Other Issues Under Section 6055

Published: 2016-08-02 | Source: Federal Register

This document contains proposed regulations relating to information reporting of minimum essential coverage under section 6055 of the Internal Revenue Code (Code). Health insurance issuers, certain employers, and others that provide minimum essential coverage to individuals must report to the IRS information about the type and period of coverage and furnish related statements to covered individuals.
