BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Public Meetings of the Missouri Advisory Committee to the U.S. Commission on Civil Rights

Published: 2023-01-26 | Source: Federal Register

Notice is hereby given, pursuant to the provisions of the rules and regulations of the U.S. Commission on Civil Rights (Commission) and the Federal Advisory Committee Act that the Missouri Advisory Committee (Committee) will hold a meeting on Thursday, February 9, 2023 at 11:30 a.m.-12:30 p.m. Central time. The Committee will continue orientation and begin identifying potential civil rights topics for their first study of the 2022-2026 term.
