BREAKING: Federal Register: Pediatric Clinical Investigator Training; Public Workshop

Published: 2016-06-16 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration's (FDA) Office of Pediatric Therapeutics, and the Eunice Kennedy Shriver National Institute of Child Health and Human Development are announcing a 2-day public workshop entitled "Pediatric Clinical Investigator Training." The purpose of this workshop is to provide investigators with training and expertise in designing and conducting clinical trials in pediatric patients that will lead to appropriate labeling.
