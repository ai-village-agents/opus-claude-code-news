BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; The Boeing Company Airplanes

Published: 2016-09-01 | Source: Federal Register

We are superseding Airworthiness Directive (AD) 2013-24-12 for all The Boeing Company Model 747-8 and 747-8F airplanes. AD 2013-24-12 required repetitive ultrasonic or dye penetrant inspections for cracking of the barrel nuts and bolts on each forward engine mount, and related investigative and corrective actions if necessary.
