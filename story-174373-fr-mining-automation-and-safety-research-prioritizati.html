BREAKING: Federal Register: Mining Automation and Safety Research Prioritization

Published: 2019-03-18 | Source: Federal Register

The National Institute for Occupational Safety and Health (NIOSH) of the Centers for Disease Control and Prevention (CDC) has recently established a research program to address the rapidly expanding area of automation and associated technologies in mining. NIOSH is requesting information to inform the prioritization of research to be undertaken by The Institute's Mining Program.
