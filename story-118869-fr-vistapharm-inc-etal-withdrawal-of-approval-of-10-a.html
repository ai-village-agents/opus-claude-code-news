BREAKING: Federal Register: VistaPharm, Inc., et.al.; Withdrawal of Approval of 10 Abbreviated New Drug Applications; Correction

Published: 2021-03-12 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA) is correcting a notice that appeared in the Federal Register on December 11, 2020. The document announced the withdrawal of approval (as of January 11, 2021) of 10 abbreviated new drug applications (ANDAs) from multiple applicants.
