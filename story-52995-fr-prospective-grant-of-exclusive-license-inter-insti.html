BREAKING: Federal Register: Prospective Grant of Exclusive License, Inter-Institutional Agreement-Institution Lead: Peptides and Peptide Microarrays for Detection and Differentiation of Antibody Responses to Ebola Virus and Other Pathogens.

Published: 2024-11-20 | Source: Federal Register
