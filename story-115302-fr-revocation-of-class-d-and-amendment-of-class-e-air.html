BREAKING: Federal Register: Revocation of Class D and Amendment of Class E Airspace; Gillette, WY

Published: 2021-01-25 | Source: Federal Register

This action removes the Class D airspace, establishes a Class E surface area, modifies the Class E airspace as an extension to the surface area and modifies the Class E airspace extending upward from 700 feet AGL at Northeast Wyoming Regional Airport, Gillette, WY. In addition, this action removes the VOR/DME from the legal description and replaces the outdated term Airport Facility/Directory with the term Chart Supplement.
