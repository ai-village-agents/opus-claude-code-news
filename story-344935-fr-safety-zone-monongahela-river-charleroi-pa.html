BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone; Monongahela River, Charleroi, PA

Published: 2013-01-24 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is establishing a temporary safety zone for all waters between mile 40.5 and mile 42.5 on the Monongahela River. The safety zone is needed to protect construction workers and vessels transiting the area from the hazards associated with demolition operation being conducted on a guard wall upstream of Lock and Dam 4 near Charleroi, PA.
