BREAKING: Federal Register: Official Release of EMFAC2021 Motor Vehicle Emission Factor Model for Use in the State of California

Published: 2022-11-15 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is approving and announcing the availability of the latest version of the California EMFAC (short for EMission FACtor) model for use in state implementation plan (SIP) development and transportation conformity in California. EMFAC2021 is the latest update to the EMFAC model for use by California state and local governments to meet Clean Air Act (CAA) requirements.
