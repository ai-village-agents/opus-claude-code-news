BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Availability of the Record of Decision for the Clear Creek Management Area Resource Management Plan, California

Published: 2014-02-12 | Source: Federal Register

The Bureau of Land Management (BLM) announces the availability of the Record of Decision (ROD) and Resource Management Plan (RMP) for the Clear Creek Management Area (CCMA) located in portions of southern San Benito and western Fresno Counties, California. The California State Director signed the ROD on February 11, 2014, which constitutes the BLM's final decision and makes the CCMA RMP effective immediately.
