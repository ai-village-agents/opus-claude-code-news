BREAKING: Federal Register: United Nuclear Corporation Church Rock Project

Published: 2019-03-13 | Source: Federal Register

On September 24, 2018, United Nuclear Corporation (UNC) requested an amendment to its reclamation plan for its Uranium Mill site near Gallup, New Mexico. UNC is a wholly owned indirect subsidiary corporation of the General Electric Company (GE). This amendment, if granted, would allow construction of a repository for mine-impacted soil.
