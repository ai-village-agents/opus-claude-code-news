BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Alpha Aviation Concept Limited Airplanes

Published: 2012-10-05 | Source: Federal Register

We are adopting a new airworthiness directive (AD) for Alpha Aviation Concept Limited Model R2160 Airplanes. This AD results from mandatory continuing airworthiness information (MCAI) issued by an aviation authority of another country to identify and correct an unsafe condition on an aviation product.
