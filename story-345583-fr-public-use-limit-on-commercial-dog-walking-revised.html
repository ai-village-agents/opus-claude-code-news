BREAKING: Federal Register: Public Use Limit on Commercial Dog Walking; Revised Disposal Conditions

Published: 2013-01-30 | Source: Federal Register

The Presidio Trust (Trust) is requesting public comment on a proposed public use limit on persons who are walking four or more dogs at one time in Area B of the Presidio of San Francisco for consideration (Commercial Dog Walkers). The limit will require any person walking four or more dogs at one time for consideration in Area B to possess a valid Commercial Dog Walking permit obtained from the City and County of San Francisco (City).
