BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Robinson Helicopter Company Model R22 Series Helicopters

Published: 2021-02-25 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA is withdrawing a notice of proposed rulemaking (NPRM) that proposed to adopt a new airworthiness directive (AD) that would have applied to Robinson Helicopter Company (RHC) Model R22 series helicopters. The NPRM was prompted by an in-flight break up of a helicopter on which both teeter stop brackets (brackets) failed.
