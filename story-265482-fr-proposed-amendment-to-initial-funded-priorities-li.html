BREAKING: Federal Register: Proposed Amendment to Initial Funded Priorities List

Published: 2016-06-07 | Source: Federal Register

The Gulf Coast Ecosystem Restoration Council (Council) seeks public and Tribal comment on a proposal to amend its Initial Funded Priorities List (FPL) to approve implementation funding for the Apalachicola Bay Oyster Restoration project in Florida (Project). The Council is proposing to approve $3,978,000 in implementation funding for this Project.
