BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Fokker Services B.V. Airplanes

Published: 2016-05-26 | Source: Federal Register

We are adopting a new airworthiness directive (AD) for all Fokker Services B.V. Model F.28 Mark 0070 and 0100 airplanes. This AD was prompted by accomplishment of a taxi-out checklist which revealed that the elevator movement was partially obstructed due to rotation of the flight control lock adjuster bracket.
