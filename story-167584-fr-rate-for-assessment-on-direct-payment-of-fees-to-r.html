BREAKING: Federal Register: Rate for Assessment on Direct Payment of Fees to Representatives in 2021

Published: 2020-12-07 | Source: Federal Register

We are announcing that the assessment percentage rate under the Social Security Act (Act) is 6.3 percent for 2021.
