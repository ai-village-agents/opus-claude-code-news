BREAKING: Federal Register: Proposed Revision to Information Collection Activity; Next Generation of Enhanced Employment Strategies Project

Published: 2022-03-28 | Source: Federal Register

The Office of Planning, Research, and Evaluation (OPRE) within the Administration for Children and Families is proposing revisions to data collection activities conducted for the Next Generation of Enhanced Employment Strategies (NextGen) Project (OMB #0970-0545). The project is rigorously evaluating innovative interventions to promote employment and economic security among low-income individuals with complex challenges.
