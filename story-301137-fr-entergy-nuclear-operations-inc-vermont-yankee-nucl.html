BREAKING: Federal Register: Entergy Nuclear Operations, Inc.; Vermont Yankee Nuclear Power Station

Published: 2015-08-10 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Nuclear Regulatory Commission (NRC) is considering issuance of exemptions in response to a request from Entergy Nuclear Operations, Inc. (Entergy or the licensee) that would permit the licensee to reduce its emergency planning (EP) activities at the Vermont Yankee Nuclear Power Station (Vermont Yankee or VY).
