BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Airbus Airplanes

Published: 2012-12-10 | Source: Federal Register

We are revising an earlier proposed airworthiness directive (AD) for all Airbus Model A318, A319, A320, and A321 series airplanes. That supplemental notice of proposed rulemaking (SNPRM) proposed an inspection to determine if certain angle of attack (AOA) probes are installed, and replacement of any affected AOA probe.
