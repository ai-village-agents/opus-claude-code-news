BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of March 23, 2020 FRTIB Board Member Meeting: Correction

Published: 2020-03-20 | Source: Federal Register

Due to the COVID-19 pandemic, the March 23, 2020 FRTIB Board Member meeting will be conducted telephonically and not at the Agency's 77 K St., Washington, DC location. Members of the public who are interested in the meeting can listen to the meeting by calling 1-877-446-3914 and using passcode 8249934. Issues caused by national higher-than-normal usage of phone lines may limit the number of individuals able to listen in on the meeting.
