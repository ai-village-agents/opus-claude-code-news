BREAKING: Federal Register: Medicare Program; Revised Process for Making National Coverage Determinations

Published: 2013-08-07 | Source: Federal Register

This notice updates the process we use for opening, deciding or reconsidering national coverage determinations (NCDs) under the Social Security Act (the Act). It addresses external requests and internal reviews for new NCDs or for reconsideration of existing NCDs. The notice further outlines an expedited administrative process to remove certain NCDs, thereby enabling local Medicare contractors to determine coverage under the Act.
