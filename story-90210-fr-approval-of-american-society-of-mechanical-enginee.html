BREAKING: Federal Register: Approval of American Society of Mechanical Engineers' Code Cases

Published: 2022-03-03 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Nuclear Regulatory Commission (NRC) is amending its regulations to incorporate by reference revisions of three regulatory guides to approve new, revised, and reaffirmed code cases published by the American Society of Mechanical Engineers. The NRC is also incorporating by reference one NRC NUREG associated with a condition on one of the regulatory guides.
