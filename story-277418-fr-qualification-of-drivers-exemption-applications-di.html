BREAKING: Federal Register: Qualification of Drivers; Exemption Applications; Diabetes

Published: 2016-10-25 | Source: Federal Register

FMCSA announces its denial of 74 applications from individuals who requested an exemption from the Federal diabetes standard applicable to interstate truck and bus drivers and the reasons for the denials. FMCSA has statutory authority to exempt individuals from the diabetes requirement if the exemptions granted will not compromise safety.
