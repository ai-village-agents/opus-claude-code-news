BREAKING: Federal Register: Regulation of Human Cells, Tissues, and Cellular and Tissue-Based Products: Small Entity Compliance Guide; Availability

Published: 2022-11-01 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA, Agency, or we) is announcing the availability of a final guidance entitled "Regulation of Human Cells, Tissues, and Cellular and Tissue-Based Products (HCT/ Ps): Small Entity Compliance Guide." The small entity compliance guide (SECG) is intended to help small entity establishments that manufacture HCT/Ps better understand the comprehensive regulatory framework for HCT/Ps set forth in the regulations and comply with certain HCT/P- related final rules.
