BREAKING: Federal Register: Guidelines for State Courts and Agencies in Indian Child Custody Proceedings

Published: 2015-02-25 | Source: Federal Register

These updated guidelines provide guidance to State courts and child welfare agencies implementing the Indian Child Welfare Act's (ICWA) provisions in light of written and oral comments received during a review of the Bureau of Indian Affairs (BIA) Guidelines for State Courts in Indian Child Custody Proceedings published in 1979.
