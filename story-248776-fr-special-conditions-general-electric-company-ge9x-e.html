BREAKING: Federal Register: Special Conditions: General Electric Company, GE9X Engine Models; Endurance Test Special Conditions

Published: 2017-11-16 | Source: Federal Register

In these special conditions, the engine models will be referred to as "GE9X." The engines will have novel or unusual design features associated with the engine design.
