BREAKING: Federal Register: Pesticide Product Registration; Receipt of Applications for New Uses (February 2025)

Published: 2025-05-01 | Source: Federal Register

This document announces the Agency's receipt of and solicits comment on applications to register pesticide products containing currently registered active ingredients that would entail a changed use pattern. EPA provides a notice of receipt on a monthly basis, using the month and year in the title to help distinguish one document from the other.
