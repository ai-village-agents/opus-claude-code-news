BREAKING: Federal Register: Conformance of Cost Accounting Standards to Generally Accepted Accounting Principles for Operating Revenue and Lease Accounting

Published: 2025-09-11 | Source: Federal Register

The Office of Federal Procurement Policy (OFPP), Cost Accounting Standards Board (the Board), is publishing, with additional clarification based on public comments from the notice of proposed rulemaking (NPRM), a final rule revising the Cost Accounting Standards (CAS) to conform them with changes in Generally Accepted Accounting Principles (GAAP) related to operating revenue and lease accounting.
