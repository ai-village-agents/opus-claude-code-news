BREAKING: Federal Register: Regulatory Improvements for Power Reactors Transitioning to Decommissioning

Published: 2017-03-15 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Nuclear Regulatory Commission (NRC) is requesting comments on a draft regulatory basis to support a rulemaking that would amend NRC's regulations for the decommissioning of nuclear power reactors.
