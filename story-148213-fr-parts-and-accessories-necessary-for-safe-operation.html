BREAKING: Federal Register: Parts and Accessories Necessary for Safe Operation; Application for an Exemption From Nauto, Inc.

Published: 2020-04-02 | Source: Federal Register

The Federal Motor Carrier Safety Administration (FMCSA) requests public comment on an application for exemption from Nauto, Inc. (Nauto) to allow its multi-sensor device to be mounted lower in the windshield on commercial motor vehicles than is currently permitted.
