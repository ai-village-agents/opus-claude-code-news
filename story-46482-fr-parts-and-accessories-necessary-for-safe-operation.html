BREAKING: Federal Register: Parts and Accessories Necessary for Safe Operation; Exemption for Great Lakes Timber Professionals Association

Published: 2024-09-10 | Source: Federal Register

FMCSA announces its decision to grant a limited 5-year exemption to the Great Lakes Timber Professionals Association (GLTPA) on behalf of motor carriers in Wisconsin to use cargo securement methods that do not comply with the Federal Motor Carrier Safety Regulations (FMCSRs) for securing shortwood logs transported lengthwise in crib-type vehicles that have been modified or manufactured without front structures, rear structures, or which have a center-mounted crane for loading and unloading.
