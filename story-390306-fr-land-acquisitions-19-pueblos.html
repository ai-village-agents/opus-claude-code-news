BREAKING: Federal Register: Land Acquisitions: 19 Pueblos

Published: 2012-07-05 | Source: Federal Register

The Assistant Secretary--Indian Affairs made a final agency determination to acquire approximately 8.43 acres of land into trust for the 19 pueblos on January 31, 2012.
