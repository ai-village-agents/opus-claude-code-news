BREAKING: Federal Register: Application To Export Electric Energy; Rainbow Energy Marketing Corporation

Published: 2015-04-27 | Source: Federal Register

Rainbow Energy Marketing Corporation (Applicant or Rainbow) has applied to renew its authority to transmit electric energy from the United States to Mexico pursuant to section 202(e) of the Federal Power Act.
