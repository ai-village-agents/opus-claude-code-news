BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone; Great Egg Harbor Bay, Marmora, NJ

Published: 2016-08-31 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is establishing two temporary safety zones on the waters of Great Egg Harbor Bay in Marmora, NJ. The first safety zone includes all waters within 250 feet of vessel and machinery conducting demolition operations on the remaining portions of the Route 9, Beesley Point Bridge bascule span.
