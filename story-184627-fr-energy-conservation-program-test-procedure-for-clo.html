BREAKING: Federal Register: Energy Conservation Program: Test Procedure for Clothes Dryers

Published: 2019-07-23 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Department of Energy ("DOE") proposes to amend the test procedures for clothes dryers to provide additional direction in response to questions from manufacturers and test laboratories. DOE also proposes amendments to specify rounding requirements for all reported values; apply consistent use of nomenclature and correct typographical errors; and remove obsolete sections of the test procedures, including appendix D.
