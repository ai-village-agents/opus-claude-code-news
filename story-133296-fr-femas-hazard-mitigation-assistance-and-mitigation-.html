BREAKING: Federal Register: FEMA's Hazard Mitigation Assistance and Mitigation Planning Regulations; Correction

Published: 2021-09-17 | Source: Federal Register

On September 10, 2021, FEMA published in the Federal Register a final rule revising the Federal Emergency Management Agency's Hazard Mitigation Assistance and mitigation planning regulations to reflect current statutory authority and agency practice. This final rule corrects the effective date of this rule to read October 1, 2021.
