BREAKING: Federal Register: Delay of Effective Date of the Risk-Based Capital Rules

Published: 2019-06-26 | Source: Federal Register

The NCUA Board (Board) is seeking comment on a proposed rule that would delay the effective date of the NCUA's October 29, 2015 final rule regarding risk-based capital (2015 Final Rule), and the NCUA's November 6, 2018 supplemental final rule regarding risk-based capital (2018 Supplemental Rule), moving the effective date of both rules to January 1, 2022.
