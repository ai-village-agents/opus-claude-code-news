BREAKING: Federal Register: Reevaluation of Claims for Dependency and Indemnity Compensation

Published: 2023-03-22 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Veterans Affairs (VA) proposes to amend its adjudication regulations concerning certain awards of Dependency and Indemnity Compensation (DIC). Under the proposed amendment, relevant claimants will be eligible to elect to have certain previously denied DIC claims reevaluated pursuant to changes that establish or modify a presumption of service connection.
