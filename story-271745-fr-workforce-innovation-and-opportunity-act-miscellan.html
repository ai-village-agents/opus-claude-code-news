BREAKING: Federal Register: Workforce Innovation and Opportunity Act, Miscellaneous Program Changes

Published: 2016-08-19 | Source: Federal Register

The Secretary amends the regulations governing a number of programs administered by the Rehabilitation Services Administration (RSA) to implement changes to the Rehabilitation Act of 1973 (Act) made by the Workforce Innovation and Opportunity Act, signed on July 22, 2014.
