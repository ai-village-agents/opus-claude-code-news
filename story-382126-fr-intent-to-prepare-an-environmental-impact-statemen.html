BREAKING: Federal Register: Intent To Prepare an Environmental Impact Statement for the Georgia Multi-Modal Passenger Terminal

Published: 2012-04-03 | Source: Federal Register

The FTA and the Georgia Department of Transportation (GDOT) intend to prepare an Environmental Impact Statement (EIS) for a proposed transit terminal project in Atlanta, Fulton County, Georgia.
