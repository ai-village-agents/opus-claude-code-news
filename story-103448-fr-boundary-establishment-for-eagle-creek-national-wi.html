BREAKING: Federal Register: Boundary Establishment for Eagle Creek National Wild and Scenic River, Mt. Hood National Forest, Clackamas County, Oregon

Published: 2022-08-22 | Source: Federal Register

In accordance with section 3(b) of the Wild and Scenic Rivers Act, the USDA Forest Service, Washington Office, is transmitting the final boundary of the Eagle Creek National Wild and Scenic River to Congress.
