BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; The Boeing Company Airplanes

Published: 2015-06-24 | Source: Federal Register

We propose to adopt a new airworthiness directive (AD) for all The Boeing Company Model 737-100, -200, -200C, -300, -400, and -500 series airplanes. This proposed AD was prompted by a report of a crack of the forward leg of the left front spar lower chord and cracks on the lower wing skin at three fastener holes common to the nacelle outboard side load fitting.
