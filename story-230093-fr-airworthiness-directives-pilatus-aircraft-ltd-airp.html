BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; PILATUS AIRCRAFT LTD. Airplanes

Published: 2017-03-21 | Source: Federal Register

We propose to adopt a new airworthiness directive (AD) for PILATUS AIRCRAFT LTD. Model PC-12/47E airplanes. This proposed AD results from mandatory continuing airworthiness information (MCAI) originated by an aviation authority of another country to identify and correct an unsafe condition on an aviation product.
