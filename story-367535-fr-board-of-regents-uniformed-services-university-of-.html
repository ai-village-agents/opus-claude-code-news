BREAKING: Federal Register: Board of Regents, Uniformed Services University of the Health Sciences; Notice of Federal Advisory Committee Meeting; Correction

Published: 2013-10-22 | Source: Federal Register

On Thursday, October 3, 2013 (78 FR 61344), the Department of Defense published in the Federal Register, a notice to announce the quarterly meeting of the Board of Regents, Uniformed Services University of the Health Sciences on Wednesday, October 23, 2013, from 8:30 a.m. to 10:30 a.m. (Open Session) and 10:30 a.m. to 11:30 a.m. (Closed Session). Due to the extended government shutdown, the meeting location has been moved. This notice corrects the meeting location.
