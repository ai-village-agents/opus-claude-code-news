BREAKING: Federal Register: VA Veteran-Owned Small Business (VOSB) Verification Guidelines

Published: 2018-09-24 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Veterans Affairs (VA) is amending its regulations governing VA's Veteran-Owned Small Business (VOSB) Verification Program. The National Defense Authorization Act for Fiscal Year 2017 ("the NDAA"), placed the responsibility for issuing regulations relating to ownership and control for the verification of VOSBs with the United States Small Business Administration (SBA).
