BREAKING: Federal Register: Energy Conservation Program: Test Procedures for Certain Commercial and Industrial Equipment; Early Assessment Review: Refrigerated Bottled or Canned Beverage Vending Machines

Published: 2021-05-19 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Department of Energy ("DOE" or "the Department") is undertaking an early assessment review to determine whether amendments are warranted for the test procedure for refrigerated bottled or canned beverage vending machines ("BVMs"). DOE has identified certain issues associated with the currently applicable test procedure on which DOE is interested in receiving comment.
