BREAKING: Federal Register: Federal Travel Regulation; Enhancement of Privately Owned Vehicle and Rental Vehicle Policy

Published: 2014-10-20 | Source: Federal Register

GSA is proposing to amend the Federal Travel Regulation (FTR) by requiring agencies to have an internal policy for determining whether to authorize a privately owned vehicle (POV), as opposed to a rental car, in conjunction with temporary duty travel (TDY). Further, GSA proposes to specify that travelers, who have been authorized to travel via common carrier or rental car, and choose to use a POV instead, will be reimbursed at the applicable POV mileage rate.
