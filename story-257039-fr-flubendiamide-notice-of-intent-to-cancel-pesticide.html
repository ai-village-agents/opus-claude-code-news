BREAKING: Federal Register: Flubendiamide; Notice of Intent To Cancel Pesticide Registrations

Published: 2016-03-04 | Source: Federal Register

Pursuant to section 6(e) of the Federal Insecticide, Fungicide and Rodenticide Act (FIFRA), EPA hereby announces its intent to cancel the registration of four (4) pesticide products containing the insecticide flubendiamide owing to the registrants' failure to comply with a required condition of their registrations.
