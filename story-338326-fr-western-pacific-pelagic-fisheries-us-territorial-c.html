BREAKING: Federal Register: Western Pacific Pelagic Fisheries; U.S. Territorial Catch and Fishing Effort Limits

Published: 2014-10-28 | Source: Federal Register

This final rule implements a management framework for specifying catch and effort limits and accountability measures for pelagic fisheries in the U.S. Pacific territories of American Samoa, Guam, and the Commonwealth of the Northern Mariana Islands (CNMI). Using the established framework, NMFS is also specifying a catch limit of 2,000 metric tons (mt) of longline-caught bigeye tuna for each territory for 2014.
