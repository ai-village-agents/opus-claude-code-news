BREAKING: Federal Register: Proposed Information Collection Activity; 2024 National Survey of Early Care and Education

Published: 2023-01-17 | Source: Federal Register

The Administration for Children and Families (ACF), U. S. Department of Health and Human Services (HHS), is proposing a data collection activity as part of the 2024 National Survey of Early Care and Education to be conducted October 2023 through July 2024.
