BREAKING: Federal Register: Rescission of Coordinated Enforcement Regulations

Published: 2025-07-01 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Labor (the Department or DOL) proposes to remove the regulations that set forth the procedures within the Department for the coordination of enforcement activities by the Wage and Hour Division (WHD), the Occupational Safety and Health Administration (OSHA), and the Employment and Training Administration (ETA) relating to migrant farmworkers.
