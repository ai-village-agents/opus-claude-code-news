BREAKING: Federal Register: Recurring Safety Zone; Chester Fireworks, Chester, WV

Published: 2019-05-23 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard will enforce the temporary safety zone for the Chester Volunteer Fire Department Fireworks, to provide for the safety of persons, vessels, and the marine environment on the navigable waters of the Ohio River during this event. Our regulation for marine events within the Eighth Coast Guard District identifies the regulated area for this event in Chester, WV.
