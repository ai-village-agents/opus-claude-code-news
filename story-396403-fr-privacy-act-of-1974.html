BREAKING: Federal Register: Privacy Act of 1974

Published: 2012-09-14 | Source: Federal Register

The Privacy Act of 1974 (5 U.S.C. 552(e) (4)) requires that all agencies publish in the Federal Register a notice of the existence and character of their systems of records. Notice is hereby given that the Department of Veterans Affairs (VA) is establishing a new system of records entitled "Veteran Child Care Programs--VA" (169VA10NC).
