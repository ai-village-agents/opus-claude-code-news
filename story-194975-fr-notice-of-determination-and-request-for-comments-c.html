BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Determination and Request for Comments Concerning Action Pursuant to Section 301: France's Digital Services Tax

Published: 2019-12-06 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Trade Representative has determined that France's Digital Services Tax is unreasonable or discriminatory and burdens or restricts U.S. commerce. The U.S. Trade Representative proposes action in the form of additional duties of up to 100 percent on products of France to be drawn from the preliminary list in the Annex to this notice.
