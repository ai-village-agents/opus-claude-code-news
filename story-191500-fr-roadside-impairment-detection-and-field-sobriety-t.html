BREAKING: Federal Register: Roadside Impairment Detection and Field Sobriety Testing Technologies Market Survey

Published: 2019-10-18 | Source: Federal Register

The National Institute of Justice (NIJ) is soliciting information for use in an upcoming Criminal Justice Testing and Evaluation Consortium (CJTEC) report tentatively titled, "A Landscape Report of Roadside Impairment Detection and Field Sobriety Testing Technologies." The report will identify technologies that are commercially available or near-market technologies to determine impairment of an individual in a field setting.
