BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone; Intracoastal Waterway; Lake Charles, LA

Published: 2016-01-04 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is establishing a temporary safety zone for all waters of the Intracoastal Waterway (ICW) extending 100-yards east and west of ICW Mile Marker 244.5 located at position 30[deg]03'38" N. 093[deg]22'19" W. (NAD 83) in Lake Charles, Louisiana. This safety zone is necessary to protect personnel, vessels, and the marine environment from hazards created by high power electrical line installation operations via helicopter over the Intracoastal Waterway.
