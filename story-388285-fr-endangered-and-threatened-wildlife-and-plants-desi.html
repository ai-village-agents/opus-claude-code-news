BREAKING: Federal Register: Endangered and Threatened Wildlife and Plants; Designation of Critical Habitat for Dusky Gopher Frog (Previously Mississippi Gopher Frog)

Published: 2012-06-12 | Source: Federal Register

We, the U.S. Fish and Wildlife Service, designate critical habitat for the dusky gopher frog under the Endangered Species Act. In previous publications, we used the common name "Mississippi gopher frog" for this species. We are taking this action to fulfill our obligations under the Act.
