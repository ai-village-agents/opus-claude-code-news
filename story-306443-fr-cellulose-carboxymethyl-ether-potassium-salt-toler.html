BREAKING: Federal Register: Cellulose Carboxymethyl Ether, Potassium Salt; Tolerance Exemption

Published: 2015-10-09 | Source: Federal Register

This regulation establishes an exemption from the requirement of a tolerance for residues of cellulose carboxymethyl ether, potassium salt; when used as an inert ingredient in a pesticide chemical formulation. Spring Trading Company on behalf of Lamberti USA, Incorporated submitted a petition to EPA under the Federal Food, Drug, and Cosmetic Act (FFDCA), requesting an exemption from the requirement of a tolerance.
