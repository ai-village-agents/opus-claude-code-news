BREAKING: Federal Register: Special Local Regulations; Crystal Pier Outrigger Race, San Diego, CA

Published: 2021-04-27 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard will enforce the special local regulation for the Crystal Pier Outrigger Race on May 8, 2021. These special local regulations are necessary to provide for the safety of the participants, crew, spectators, sponsor vessels, and general users of the waterway. Our regulation for marine events within the Eleventh Coast Guard District identifies the regulated area for this event in San Diego, CA.
