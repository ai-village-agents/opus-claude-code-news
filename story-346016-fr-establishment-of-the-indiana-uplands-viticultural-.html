BREAKING: Federal Register: Establishment of the Indiana Uplands Viticultural Area and Modification of the Ohio River Valley Viticultural Area

Published: 2013-02-05 | Source: Federal Register

The Alcohol and Tobacco Tax and Trade Bureau (TTB) establishes the approximately 4,800-square mile "Indiana Uplands" viticultural area in south-central Indiana. TTB also modifies the boundary of the established 26,000-square mile Ohio River Valley viticultural area to eliminate a potential overlap with the Indiana Uplands viticultural area.
