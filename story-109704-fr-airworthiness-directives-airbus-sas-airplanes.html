BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Airbus SAS Airplanes

Published: 2022-11-09 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA proposes to supersede Airworthiness Directive (AD) 2022-09-03, which applies to certain Airbus SAS Model A350-941 and - 1041 airplanes. AD 2022-09-03 requires revising the existing maintenance or inspection program, as applicable, to incorporate new or more restrictive airworthiness limitations.
