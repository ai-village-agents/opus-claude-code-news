BREAKING: Federal Register: Partitioning, Disaggregation, and Leasing of Spectrum

Published: 2019-04-02 | Source: Federal Register

In this document, the Federal Communications Commission explores how potential changes to partitioning, disaggregation, and leasing rules might close the digital divide and to increase spectrum access by small and rural carriers.
