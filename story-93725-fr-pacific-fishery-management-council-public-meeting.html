BREAKING: Federal Register: Pacific Fishery Management Council; Public Meeting

Published: 2022-04-15 | Source: Federal Register

The Coastal Pelagic Species (CPS) Subcommittee of the Pacific Fishery Management Council's (Pacific Council's) Scientific and Statistical Committee (SSC) will hold a meeting to review proposed revisions to the Terms of Reference for the CPS Stock Assessment Review Process for 2023 and 2024. The meeting is open to the public.
