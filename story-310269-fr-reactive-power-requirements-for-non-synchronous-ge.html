BREAKING: Federal Register: Reactive Power Requirements for Non-Synchronous Generation

Published: 2015-11-25 | Source: Federal Register

The Federal Energy Regulatory Commission (Commission) is proposing to eliminate the exemptions for wind generators from the requirement to provide reactive power. As a result, all newly interconnecting generators, including both synchronous and non- synchronous generators, would be required to provide reactive power.
