BREAKING: Federal Register: Approval and Promulgation of Implementation Plans; New York; Motor Vehicle Enhanced Inspection and Maintenance Program

Published: 2012-02-28 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is approving a proposed State Implementation Plan revision submitted by the New York State Department of Environmental Conservation. This revision consists of changes to New York's motor vehicle enhanced inspection and maintenance program that would eliminate the transient emission short test program as it relates to the New York portion of the New York- Northern New Jersey-Long Island, NY-NJ-CT 8-hour ozone moderate nonattainment area.
