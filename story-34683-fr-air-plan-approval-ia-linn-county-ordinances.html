BREAKING: Federal Register: Air Plan Approval; IA; Linn County Ordinances

Published: 2024-04-18 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is proposing to approve revisions to the Iowa State Implementation Plan (SIP) to include recent changes to the Linn County Code of Ordinances. The revisions to this rule include updating definitions and references to federal rules, revising methods and procedures for performance test/ stack test and continuous monitoring systems, and making minor clarifications and grammatical changes.
