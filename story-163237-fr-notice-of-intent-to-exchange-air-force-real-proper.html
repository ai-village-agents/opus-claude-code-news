BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Intent To Exchange Air Force Real Property for Non-Air Force Real Property

Published: 2020-10-09 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of the Air Force is publishing this notice to identify federal real property that it intends to exchange for property that is needed by the Air Force to limit encroachment and other constraints on military operations at Buckley AFB, CO.
