BREAKING: Federal Register: Emergency Preparedness and Response

Published: 2014-09-23 | Source: Federal Register

Pursuant to 42 U.S.C. 2286a(b)(5), the Defense Nuclear Facilities Safety Board has made a recommendation to the Secretary of Energy concerning the need to take actions to improve the emergency preparedness and response capability at the Department of Energy's (DOE) defense nuclear facilities.
