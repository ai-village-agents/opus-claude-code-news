BREAKING: Federal Register: Banda de Lupinus albus doce (BLAD); Exemption From the Requirement of a Tolerance

Published: 2013-03-22 | Source: Federal Register

This regulation establishes an exemption from the requirement of a tolerance for residues of banda de Lupinus albus doce (BLAD), a naturally occurring polypeptide from the catabolism of a seed storage protein ([beta]-conglutin) of sweet lupines (Lupinus albus), in or on all food commodities when applied as a fungicide and used in accordance with label directions and good agricultural practices.
