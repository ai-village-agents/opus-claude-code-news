BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone; Lower Mississippi River, Mile Markers 595-597, Waxhaw, MS

Published: 2021-11-26 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is establishing a temporary safety zone for all navigable waters of the Lower Mississippi River (LMR), between Mile Marker 595 and 597. The safety zone is needed to protect persons, property, and the marine environment from the potential safety hazards associated with rock placement operations in the vicinity of Waxhaw, MS.
