BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; The Boeing Company Airplanes

Published: 2013-08-28 | Source: Federal Register

We propose to adopt a new airworthiness directive (AD) for certain The Boeing Company Model 747-200B, -200C, -200F, -300, and 747SR series airplanes. This proposed AD was prompted by reports of cracks of both lower chords and web on certain outboard struts.
