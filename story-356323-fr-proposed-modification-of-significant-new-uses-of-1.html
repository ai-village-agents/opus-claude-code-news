BREAKING: Federal Register: Proposed Modification of Significant New Uses of 1-Propene, 2,3,3,3-tetrafluoro-

Published: 2013-05-31 | Source: Federal Register

Under section 5(a)(2) of the Toxic Substances Control Act (TSCA), EPA is proposing to amend the significant new use rule (SNUR) for the chemical substance identified as 1-Propene, 2,3,3,3- tetrafluoro-, which was the subject of premanufacture notice (PMN) P- 07-601. This action would amend the SNUR to allow the manufacture and processing for certain uses without requiring a significant new use notice (SNUN).
