BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Request for Comments on Barriers Facing Small Businesses in Contracting With the Department of Defense

Published: 2021-09-08 | Source: Federal Register

The participation of dynamic, resilient, and innovative small businesses in the defense industrial base is critical to the United States' efforts to maintain its technological superiority, military readiness, and warfighting advantage. In furtherance of its efforts to maximize opportunities for small businesses to contribute to national security, the Department seeks public input on the barriers that small businesses face in working with the Department.
