BREAKING: Federal Register: Qal-Tek Associates LLC; Mayfield, Idaho Waste Handling and Temporary Storage Facility

Published: 2023-02-06 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Nuclear Regulatory Commission (NRC) is issuing a finding of no significant impact (FONSI) and accompanying environmental assessment (EA) for an application from Qal-Tek Associates LLC (QTA) to operate a waste handling and temporary storage facility near Mayfield, Idaho.
