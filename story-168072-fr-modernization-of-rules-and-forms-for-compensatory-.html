BREAKING: Federal Register: Modernization of Rules and Forms for Compensatory Securities Offerings and Sales

Published: 2020-12-11 | Source: Federal Register

The Securities and Exchange Commission ("Commission") is proposing for public comment amendments to Rule 701 under the Securities Act of 1933 (the "Securities Act"), which provides an exemption from registration for securities issued by non-reporting issuers pursuant to compensatory arrangements, and Form S-8, the Securities Act registration statement for compensatory offerings by reporting issuers.
