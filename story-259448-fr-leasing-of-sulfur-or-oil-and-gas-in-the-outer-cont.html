BREAKING: Federal Register: Leasing of Sulfur or Oil and Gas in the Outer Continental Shelf

Published: 2016-03-30 | Source: Federal Register

This final rule updates and streamlines the existing Outer Continental Shelf (OCS) leasing regulations and clarifies implementation of the Federal Oil and Gas Royalty Simplification and Fairness Act of 1996, which amended the Federal Oil and Gas Royalty Management Act of 1982 (FOGRMA). The final rule reorganizes leasing requirements to more effectively communicate the leasing process as it has evolved over the years.
