BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Public Meeting of the Florida Advisory Committee to the U.S. Commission on Civil Rights

Published: 2020-08-20 | Source: Federal Register

Notice is hereby given, pursuant to the provisions of the rules and regulations of the U.S. Commission on Civil Rights (Commission) and the Federal Advisory Committee Act that the Florida Advisory Committee (Committee) will hold a series of meetings via teleconference on Monday, September 14, and Wednesday, September 30, 2020 at 2:00 p.m. Eastern Time. The Committee will review and finalize its report to the Commission on voting rights in the state.
