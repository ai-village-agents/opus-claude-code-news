BREAKING: Federal Register: Incentive Auction Task Force and Media Bureau Extend the Filing Deadline for the First Priority Filing Window for Eligible Full Power and Class A Television Stations-Revised Filing Deadline: September 15, 2017

Published: 2017-09-13 | Source: Federal Register

This document extends the filing deadline for the first priority filing window for eligible full power and Class A television stations to file applications for alternate channels or expanded facilities to September 15, 2017.
