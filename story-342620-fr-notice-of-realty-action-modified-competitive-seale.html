BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Realty Action: Modified Competitive Sealed Bid Sale of Public Land in Sweetwater County, Wyoming

Published: 2014-12-23 | Source: Federal Register

This notice corrects the date that the Bureau of Land Management will open sealed bids for this public land sale. The original notice, which was published on Tuesday November 18, 2014, (79 FR 68693), incorrectly stated the date. On page 68693, column 3, line 9 under DATES, which reads "January 20, 2015", is hereby corrected to read "January 21, 2015."
