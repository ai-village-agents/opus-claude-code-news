BREAKING: Federal Register: Informed Consent and Advance Directives

Published: 2022-02-04 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Veterans Affairs (VA) published an interim final rule amending its regulation regarding informed consent and advance directives. In that rulemaking, we amended the regulation by reorganizing it and amending language where necessary to enhance clarity.
