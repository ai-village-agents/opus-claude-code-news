BREAKING: Federal Register: Special Local Regulation; Marine Events Within the Eleventh Coast Guard District-San Diego Fall Classic

Published: 2021-11-09 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard will enforce the special local regulation on the waters of Mission Bay, San Diego, California, during the San Diego Fall Classic on November 14, 2021. This special local regulation is necessary to provide for the safety of the participants, crew, sponsor vessels of the rowing event, and general users of the waterway.
