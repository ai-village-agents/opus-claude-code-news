BREAKING: Federal Register: Air Plan Approval; Illinois; Volatile Organic Compounds Definition

Published: 2015-10-05 | Source: Federal Register

Under the Clean Air Act, the Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is proposing to approve a revision to the Illinois State Implementation Plan. The revision amends the Illinois Administrative Code by updating the definition of volatile organic material or volatile organic compounds to exclude 2,3,3,3-tetrafluoropropene.
