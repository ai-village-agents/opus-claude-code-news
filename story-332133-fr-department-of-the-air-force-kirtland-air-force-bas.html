BREAKING: Federal Register: Department of the Air Force, Kirtland Air Force Base, New Mexico

Published: 2014-08-14 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Nuclear Regulatory Commission (NRC) is considering an amendment to License No. 42-23539-01AF (Docket No. 030-28641) which authorizes the unrestricted release of Radioactive Waste Site RW-06 at Kirtland Air Force Base. The NRC has reviewed the Department of the Air Force's final status survey report and conducted an independent confirmatory survey of the site.
