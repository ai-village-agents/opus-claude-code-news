BREAKING: Federal Register: Narrow Woven Ribbons With Woven Selvedge From Taiwan: Final Results of Antidumping Duty Administrative Review; 2011-2012

Published: 2013-08-19 | Source: Federal Register

On May 21, 2013, the Department of Commerce (the Department) published the preliminary results of the second administrative review of the antidumping duty order on narrow woven ribbons with woven selvedge (narrow woven ribbons) from Taiwan. The period of review (POR) is September 1, 2011, through August 31, 2012. We received no comments from interested parties.
