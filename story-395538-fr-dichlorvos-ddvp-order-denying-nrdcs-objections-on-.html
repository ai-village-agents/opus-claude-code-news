BREAKING: Federal Register: Dichlorvos (DDVP); Order Denying NRDC's Objections on Remand

Published: 2012-09-05 | Source: Federal Register

In this order, EPA denies an objection to a prior order denying a petition requesting that EPA revoke all pesticide tolerances for dichlorvos under section 408(d) of the Federal Food, Drug, and Cosmetic Act. The objection was filed on February 1, 2008, by the Natural Resources Defense Council (NRDC). The original petition was also filed by NRDC.
