BREAKING: Federal Register: Atlantic Highly Migratory Species; Exempted Fishing Permits

Published: 2017-03-02 | Source: Federal Register

On November 16, 2016, NMFS published a Notice of Intent (NOI) announcing our intent to issue exempted fishing permits (EFPs), scientific research permits (SRPs), letters of acknowledgement (LOAs), and display permits for research regarding highly migratory species (HMS) in 2017. In the NOI, NMFS requested comments regarding the issuance of EFPs and LOAs for HMS research.
