BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone; Bayou Lafourche, Galliano, LA

Published: 2023-11-13 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is establishing a temporary safety zone for Bayou Lafourche. This temporary safety zone encompasses an area 440 yards north and south of position 29[deg]25'28.6" N 90[deg]17'31.5" W. The safety zone is needed to protect personnel, vessels, and the marine environment from potential hazards created by the construction of the Airport Road Bridge.
