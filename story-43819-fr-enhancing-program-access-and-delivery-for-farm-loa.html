BREAKING: Federal Register: Enhancing Program Access and Delivery for Farm Loans

Published: 2024-08-08 | Source: Federal Register

The Farm Service Agency (FSA) is amending the Farm Loan Programs (FLP) regulations to implement the Distressed Borrower Set- Aside (DBSA) Program and other changes. DBSA will provide a new loan servicing program for financially distressed borrowers that will allow for the deferral of one annual loan installment at a reduced interest rate.
