BREAKING: Federal Register: Annual Fireworks Displays and Other Events in the Eighth Coast Guard District Requiring Safety Zones

Published: 2022-03-15 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard will enforce a safety zone for the Riverfest fireworks display on the Neches River in Port Neches, TX, from 8:30 p.m. through 10 p.m. on April 30, 2022 to provide for the safety of life on navigable waterways during this event. Our regulation for fireworks displays and other events within the Eighth Coast Guard District identifies the regulated area for this event in Port Neches, TX.
