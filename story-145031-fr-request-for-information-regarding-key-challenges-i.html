BREAKING: Federal Register: Request for Information Regarding Key Challenges in Reconstituting Uranium Mining and Conversion Capabilities in the United States

Published: 2020-02-24 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S Department of Energy (DOE) is issuing this RFI to invite public input on key challenges in reconstituting uranium mining and conversion capabilities in the United States. This invitation is in recognition of the importance of nuclear fuel supply chain capabilities to the United States.
