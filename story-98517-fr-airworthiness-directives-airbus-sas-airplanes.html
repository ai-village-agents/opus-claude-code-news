BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Airbus SAS Airplanes

Published: 2022-06-16 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA proposes to remove Airworthiness Directive (AD) 2020- 25-03, which applies to all Airbus SAS Model A318, A319, A320, and A321 series airplanes. AD 2020-25-03 requires repetitive checks of the pressure gauges on the inflation reservoir of each emergency escape slide/raft to determine the amount of pressure, and applicable corrective actions.
