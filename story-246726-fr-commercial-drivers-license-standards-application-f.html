BREAKING: Federal Register: Commercial Driver's License Standards: Application for Exemption; C.R. England

Published: 2017-10-20 | Source: Federal Register

FMCSA announces its decision to re-affirm renewal of an exemption granted to C.R. England from the regulation that requires a commercial learner's permit (CLP) holder to be accompanied by a commercial driver's license (CDL) holder with the proper CDL class and endorsements, seated in the front seat of the vehicle while the CLP holder performs behind-the-wheel training on public roads or highways.
