BREAKING: Federal Register: Energy Conservation Program: Test Procedure for Circulator Pumps

Published: 2022-09-19 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Department of Energy ("DOE") is establishing definitions, a test procedure, sampling and rating requirements, and enforcement provisions for circulator pumps. Currently, circulator pumps are not subject to DOE test procedures or energy conservation standards. DOE is adopting a test procedure for measuring the circulator energy index for circulator pumps.
