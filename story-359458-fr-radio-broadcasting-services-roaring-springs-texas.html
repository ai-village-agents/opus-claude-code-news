BREAKING: Federal Register: Radio Broadcasting Services; Roaring Springs, Texas

Published: 2013-07-05 | Source: Federal Register

The Audio Division, at the request of Jesus B. Salazar, allots FM Channel 227A and deletes FM Channel 249A at Roaring Springs, Texas, and allots FM Channel 249C3 and deletes FM Channel 276C3 at Roaring Springs. These allotment changes are part of a rule making and hybrid application proposal.
