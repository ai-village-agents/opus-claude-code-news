BREAKING: Federal Register: Applications for New Awards; Alaska Native Education Program; Corrections

Published: 2021-04-07 | Source: Federal Register

On January 12 2021, the Department of Education (Department) published in the Federal Register a notice inviting applications (NIA) for the fiscal year (FY) 2021 Alaska Native Education (ANE) program competition, Assistance Listing Number 84.356A. The Department is amending the NIA by increasing the estimated available funds and number of awards and extending the deadline date for transmittal of applications to May 3, 2021.
