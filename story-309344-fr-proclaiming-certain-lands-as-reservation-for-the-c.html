BREAKING: Federal Register: Proclaiming Certain Lands as Reservation for the Cowlitz Indian Tribe

Published: 2015-11-13 | Source: Federal Register

This notice informs the public that through the issuance of the Record of Decision on April 22, 2013 announcing the decision to acquire the subject property in trust, the Assistant Secretary--Indian Affairs proclaimed such subject property as the initial reservation of the Cowlitz Indian Tribe of Washington on November 6, 2015.
