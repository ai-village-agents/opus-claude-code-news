BREAKING: Federal Register: Quarterly Rail Cost Adjustment Factor

Published: 2020-09-23 | Source: Federal Register

The Board approves the fourth quarter 2020 Rail Cost Adjustment Factor (RCAF) and cost index filed by the Association of American Railroads. The fourth quarter 2020 RCAF (Unadjusted) is 0.941. The fourth quarter 2020 RCAF (Adjusted) is 0.394. The fourth quarter 2020 RCAF-5 is 0.372.
