BREAKING: Federal Register: Atlantic Highly Migratory Species; Technical Amendment to Regulations

Published: 2017-01-11 | Source: Federal Register

NMFS is hereby making technical amendments to the regulations for Atlantic highly migratory species. Currently, certain cross- references meant to be in the regulations are either missing or incorrect. This final action will make the cross-references in the regulations accurate. The action also simplifies regulatory text by removing unnecessary language.
