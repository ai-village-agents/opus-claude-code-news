BREAKING: Federal Register: Terrorism Risk Insurance Program 2019 Data Call

Published: 2018-11-09 | Source: Federal Register

Pursuant to the Terrorism Risk Insurance Act of 2002 (TRIA),\1\ the Federal Insurance Office (FIO) requests public feedback on the proposed data collection forms for use in the 2019 Terrorism Risk Insurance Program Data Call (2019 TRIP Data Call). As was the case in connection with the 2018 TRIP Data Call, these forms will be utilized in connection with both the federal and state data calls regarding terrorism risk insurance.
