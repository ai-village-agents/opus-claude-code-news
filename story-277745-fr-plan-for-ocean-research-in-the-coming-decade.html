BREAKING: Federal Register: Plan for Ocean Research in the Coming Decade

Published: 2016-10-28 | Source: Federal Register

The Subcommittee on Ocean Science and Technology (SOST) is requesting input on the overall framing and content of a plan for Ocean Research in the Coming Decade ("the Plan"). The SOST is chartered under the National Science and Technology Council to advise and assist on national issues related to ocean science and technology.
