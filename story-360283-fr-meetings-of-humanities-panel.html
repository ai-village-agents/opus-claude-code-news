BREAKING: Federal Register: Meetings of Humanities Panel

Published: 2013-07-16 | Source: Federal Register

Pursuant to section 10(a)(2) of the Federal Advisory Committee Act (5 U.S.C. App.), notice is hereby given that 18 meetings of the Humanities Panel will be held during August 2013 as follows. The purpose of the meetings is for panel review, discussion, evaluation, and recommendation of applications for financial assistance under the National Foundation on the Arts and Humanities Act of 1965 (20 U.S.C. 951-960, as amended).
