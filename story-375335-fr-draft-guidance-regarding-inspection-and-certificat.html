BREAKING: Federal Register: Draft Guidance Regarding Inspection and Certification of Vessels Under the Maritime Security Program

Published: 2012-01-19 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard announces the availability of a draft Navigation and Inspection Circular (NVIC) that sets forth the Coast Guard's policies and procedures regarding the inspection and certification of vessels under the Maritime Security Program (MSP). The draft NVIC provides a comprehensive approach to the MSP inspection process through the establishment of two levels of MSP inspection and oversight.
