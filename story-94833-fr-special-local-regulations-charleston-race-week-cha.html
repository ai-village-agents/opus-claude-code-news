BREAKING: Federal Register: Special Local Regulations; Charleston Race Week, Charleston, SC

Published: 2022-04-29 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard will enforce special local regulations for the Charleston Race Week from April 28 through May 1, 2022. This action is necessary to ensure the safety of life on navigable waters of the United States during the Charleston Race Week event. Our regulation for marine events within the Seventh Coast Guard District identifies the regulated area for this event in Charleston, SC.
