BREAKING: Federal Register: Amendment of Class E Airspace; Rifle, CO

Published: 2020-03-03 | Source: Federal Register

This action amends Class E airspace at Rifle Garfield County Airport, Rifle, CO. The action increases the circular radius of the Class E airspace, designated as a surface area, to the west and northwest of the airport. Also, this action amends the Class E airspace by adding an airspace area, designated as an extension to a Class D or Class E surface area, to the east of the airport.
