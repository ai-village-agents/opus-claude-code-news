BREAKING: Federal Register: Passports: Form DS-3053 Statement of Consent; Consular Reports of Birth Abroad (CRBA)

Published: 2022-10-20 | Source: Federal Register

The Department proposes to amend its rule regarding minor passport applications to allow a non-applying parent to sign the statement of consent before a notary public or a passport specialist at one of the public passport agency/center counters located within the United States.
