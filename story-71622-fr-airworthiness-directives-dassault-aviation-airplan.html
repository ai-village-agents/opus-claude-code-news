BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Dassault Aviation Airplanes

Published: 2023-07-10 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA proposes to adopt a new airworthiness directive (AD) for certain Dassault Aviation Model MYSTERE-FALCON 900, FALCON 900EX, FALCON 2000, and FALCON 2000EX airplanes. This proposed AD was prompted by reports of the wing anti-icing (WAI) system leaking in the wing leading edge.
