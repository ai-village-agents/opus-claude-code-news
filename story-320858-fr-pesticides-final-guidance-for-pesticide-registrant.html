BREAKING: Federal Register: Pesticides; Final Guidance for Pesticide Registrants on Web-Distributed Labeling

Published: 2014-04-04 | Source: Federal Register

The Agency is announcing the availability of a Pesticide Registration Notice (PR Notice) titled "Web-Distributed Labeling for Pesticide Products." This PR Notice was issued by the Agency on March 24, 2014, and is identified as PR Notice 2014-1.
