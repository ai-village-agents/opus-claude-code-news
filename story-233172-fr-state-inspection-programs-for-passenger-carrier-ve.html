BREAKING: Federal Register: State Inspection Programs for Passenger-Carrier Vehicles; Withdrawal

Published: 2017-05-01 | Source: Federal Register

FMCSA withdraws its April 27, 2016, advance notice of proposed rulemaking (ANPRM) concerning the establishment of requirements for States to implement annual inspection programs for commercial motor vehicles (CMVs) designed or used to transport passengers (passenger- carrying CMVs). FMCSA sought information from all interested parties that would enable the Agency to assess the risks associated with improperly maintained or inspected passenger-carrying CMVs.
