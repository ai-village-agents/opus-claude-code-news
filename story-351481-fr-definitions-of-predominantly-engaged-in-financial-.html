BREAKING: Federal Register: Definitions of “Predominantly Engaged In Financial Activities” and “Significant” Nonbank Financial Company and Bank Holding Company

Published: 2013-04-05 | Source: Federal Register

The Board is adopting this final rule to establish, for purposes of Title I of the Dodd-Frank Wall Street Reform and Consumer Protection Act (the "Dodd-Frank Act" or "Act"), the requirements for determining if a company is "predominantly engaged in financial activities"; and definitions of the terms "significant nonbank financial company" and "significant bank holding company."
