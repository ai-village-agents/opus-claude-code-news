BREAKING: Federal Register: Submission for OMB Review; Comment Request Agency Proposal for the Collection of Information Submitted to the Office of Management and Budget for Review; Renewal of Generic Clearances; Comment Request

Published: 2020-11-24 | Source: Federal Register

Consistent with the Paperwork Reduction Act of 1995, the U.S. International Trade Commission (Commission) has submitted a proposal for the collection of information to the Office of Management and Budget (OMB) for approval. The proposed information collection is a three-year extension of the current generic clearance (approved by OMB under Control No. 3117-0222) under which the Commission can issue information collections for the collection of qualitative feedback on agency service delivery.
