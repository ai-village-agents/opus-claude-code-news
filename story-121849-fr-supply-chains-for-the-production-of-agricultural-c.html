BREAKING: Federal Register: Supply Chains for the Production of Agricultural Commodities and Food Products

Published: 2021-04-21 | Source: Federal Register

On February 24, 2021, President Biden issued an Executive Order on "America's Supply Chains," which directs several Federal agency actions to secure and strengthen America's supply chains. One of these directions is for the Secretary of Agriculture (the Secretary) to submit, within one year, a report to the President that assesses the supply chains for the production of agricultural commodities and food products.
