BREAKING: Federal Register: Air Plan Approval; OR; Lane County Permitting Rule Revisions

Published: 2025-09-05 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) proposes to approve the June 26, 2024, submitted revisions to the Oregon State Implementation Plan (SIP) applicable in Lane County, Oregon. The changes update the local stationary source permitting regulations to align with existing State regulations.
