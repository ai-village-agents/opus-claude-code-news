BREAKING: Federal Register: Schedules of Controlled Substances: Placement of 2-Methyl AP-237 in Schedule I

Published: 2024-03-15 | Source: Federal Register

With the issuance of this final order, the Administrator of the Drug Enforcement Administration is permanently placing 1-(2-methyl- 4-(3-phenylprop-2-en-1-yl)piperazin-1-yl)butan-1-one (commonly known as 2-methyl AP-237), including its optical and geometric isomers, esters, ethers, salts, and salts of isomers, esters, and ethers whenever the existence of such isomers, esters, ethers, and salts is possible within the specific chemical designation, in schedule I of the Controlled Substances Act.
