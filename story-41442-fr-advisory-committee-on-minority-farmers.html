BREAKING: Federal Register: Advisory Committee on Minority Farmers

Published: 2024-07-10 | Source: Federal Register

Pursuant to the provisions of the rules and regulations of the United States Department of Agriculture (USDA) and the Federal Advisory Committee Act (FACA), notice is hereby given that a public meeting of the Advisory Committee on Minority Farmers (ACMF) will be convened. During this public meeting, the ACMF will consider USDA programs and policies and their impacts on minority farmers.
