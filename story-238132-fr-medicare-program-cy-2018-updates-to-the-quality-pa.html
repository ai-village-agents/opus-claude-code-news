BREAKING: Federal Register: Medicare Program; CY 2018 Updates to the Quality Payment Program

Published: 2017-06-30 | Source: Federal Register

The Medicare Access and CHIP Reauthorization Act of 2015 (MACRA) established the Quality Payment Program for eligible clinicians. Under the Quality Payment Program, eligible clinicians can participate via one of two tracks: Advanced Alternative Payment Models (APMs); or the Merit-based Incentive Payment System (MIPS).
