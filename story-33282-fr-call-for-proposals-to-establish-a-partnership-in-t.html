BREAKING: Federal Register: Call for Proposals To Establish a Partnership in the State of Iowa

Published: 2024-04-01 | Source: Federal Register

The National Endowment for the Humanities (NEH) requests proposals from interested nonprofit organizations and institutions of higher education to partner with NEH as the designated state humanities council in Iowa.
