BREAKING: Federal Register: Scheduling of Annual Leave by Employees Determined Necessary To Respond to Certain National Emergencies

Published: 2020-08-10 | Source: Federal Register

The Office of Personnel Management is issuing interim regulations to assist agencies and employees responding to the National Emergency Concerning the Novel Coronavirus Disease (COVID-19) Outbreak and for future national emergencies.
