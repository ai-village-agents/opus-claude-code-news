BREAKING: Federal Register: Termination of the Vietnam War Commemoration Advisory Committee

Published: 2019-05-20 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Defense is publishing this notice to announce that it is terminating the Vietnam War Commemoration Advisory Committee ("the Committee"), effective June 30, 2019.
