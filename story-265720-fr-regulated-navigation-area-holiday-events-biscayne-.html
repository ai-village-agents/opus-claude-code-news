BREAKING: Federal Register: Regulated Navigation Area; Holiday Events; Biscayne Bay, Miami, FL

Published: 2016-06-09 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is amending the Columbus Day Weekend regulated navigation area on Biscayne Bay in Miami, Florida. The proposed amended regulation extends the Biscayne Bay regulated navigation enforcement period to New Year's Eve and Fourth of July events. It also expands the boundaries of the regulated navigation area south to Turkey Point, east to Elliott Key, west to the shoreline, and north to the Julia Tuttle Causeway.
