BREAKING: Federal Register: Importer of Controlled Substances Registration: Alkermes Gainesville LLC

Published: 2014-10-08 | Source: Federal Register

Alkermes Gainesville LLC applied to be registered as an importer of a basic class of controlled substance. The DEA grants Alkermes Gainesville LLC registration as an importer of this controlled substance.
