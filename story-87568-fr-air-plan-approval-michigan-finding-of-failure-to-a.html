BREAKING: Federal Register: Air Plan Approval; Michigan; Finding of Failure To Attain the 2010 Sulfur Dioxide Primary National Ambient Air Quality Standard for the Detroit Nonattainment Area

Published: 2022-01-28 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is determining that the Detroit sulfur dioxide (SO2) nonattainment area failed to attain the 2010 primary 1-hour SO2 national ambient air quality standard (NAAQS or "standard") by the applicable attainment date of October 4, 2018. This determination is based upon air quality modeling using actual and allowable emissions.
