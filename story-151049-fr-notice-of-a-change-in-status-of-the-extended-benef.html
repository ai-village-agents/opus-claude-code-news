BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of a Change in Status of the Extended Benefit (EB) Program for Rhode Island

Published: 2020-05-08 | Source: Federal Register

This notice announces a change in benefit payment status under the EB program for Rhode Island. The following change has occurred since the publication of the last notice regarding Rhode Island's EB status: Rhode Island's 13-week insured unemployment rate (IUR) for the week ending April 11, 2020 was 5.49 percent, which exceeds 120 percent of the corresponding rate in the prior two years.
