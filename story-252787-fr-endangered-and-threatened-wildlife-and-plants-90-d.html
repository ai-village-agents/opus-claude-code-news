BREAKING: Federal Register: Endangered and Threatened Wildlife and Plants; 90-Day Findings on 17 Petitions

Published: 2016-01-12 | Source: Federal Register

We, the U.S. Fish and Wildlife Service (Service), announce 90- day findings on various petitions to list, reclassify, or delist fish, wildlife, or plants under the Endangered Species Act of 1973, as amended (Act). Based on our review, we find that six petitions do not present substantial scientific or commercial information indicating that the petitioned actions may be warranted, and we are not initiating status reviews in response to these petitions.
