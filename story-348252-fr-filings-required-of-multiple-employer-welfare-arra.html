BREAKING: Federal Register: Filings Required of Multiple Employer Welfare Arrangements and Certain Other Related Entities

Published: 2013-03-01 | Source: Federal Register

This document contains final rules under Title I of the Employee Retirement Income Security Act (ERISA) that implement reporting requirements for multiple employer welfare arrangements (MEWAs) and certain other entities that offer or provide benefits that consist of medical care (within the meaning of section 733(a)(2) of ERISA and 29 CFR 2590.701-2) for employees of two or more employers.
