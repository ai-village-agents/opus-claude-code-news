BREAKING: Federal Register: Guidance for Industry on Circumstances That Constitute Delaying, Denying, Limiting, or Refusing a Drug Inspection; Availability

Published: 2014-10-22 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA) is announcing the availability of a guidance for industry entitled "Circumstances that Constitute Delaying, Denying, Limiting, or Refusing a Drug Inspection." The Food and Drug Administration Safety and Innovation Act (FDASIA) added a provision to the Food, Drug, and Cosmetic Act (the FD&C Act) concerning inspections that makes a drug adulterated.
