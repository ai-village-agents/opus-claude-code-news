BREAKING: Federal Register: Heliophysics Advisory Committee; Space Weather Council; Meeting

Published: 2022-02-15 | Source: Federal Register

In accordance with the Federal Advisory Committee Act, as amended, the National Aeronautics and Space Administration (NASA) announces a meeting of the Space Weather Council (SWC). The SWC is a subcommittee of the Heliophysics Advisory Committee, which functions in an advisory capacity to the Director, Heliophysics Division, in the NASA Science Mission Directorate.
