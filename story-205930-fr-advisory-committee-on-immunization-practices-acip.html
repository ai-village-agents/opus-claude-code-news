BREAKING: Federal Register: Advisory Committee on Immunization Practices (ACIP)

Published: 2018-05-01 | Source: Federal Register

In accordance with the Federal Advisory Committee Act, the Centers for Disease Control and Prevention (CDC), announces the following meeting of the Advisory Committee on Immunization Practices (ACIP). This meeting is open to the public, limited only by room seating. The meeting room accommodates approximately 400 people. Time will be available for public comment.
