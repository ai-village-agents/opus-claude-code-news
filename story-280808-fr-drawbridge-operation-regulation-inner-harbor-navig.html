BREAKING: Federal Register: Drawbridge Operation Regulation; Inner Harbor Navigation Canal, New Orleans, LA

Published: 2016-12-06 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard has issued a temporary deviation from the operating schedule that governs the L & N Railroad/Almonaster Road drawbridge across the Inner Harbor Navigation Canal, mile 2.9 at New Orleans, Orleans Parish, Louisiana. The deviation is necessary to conduct repair and replacement of the lift rail assembly on the south end of the bridge. These repairs are essential for the continued safe operation of the bridge.
