BREAKING: Federal Register: Aviation Services

Published: 2013-10-03 | Source: Federal Register

In this document, the Federal Communications Commission (Commission) amends its rules to authorize the use of frequency 1090 MHz by aeronautical utility mobile stations for airport surface detection equipment, commonly referred to as vehicle "squitters," to help reduce collisions between aircraft and airport ground vehicles.
