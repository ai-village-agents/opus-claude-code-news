BREAKING: Federal Register: Labeling for Combined Hormonal Contraceptives; Draft Guidance for Industry; Availability

Published: 2018-01-02 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA or Agency) is announcing the availability of a draft guidance for industry entitled "Labeling for Combined Hormonal Contraceptives." This draft guidance provides recommendations on information that should be included in the prescribing information for combined hormonal contraceptives (CHCs), which contain estrogen and progestin.
