BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Formal Determination on Records Release

Published: 2024-10-24 | Source: Federal Register

The Civil Rights Cold Case Records Review Board received 404 pages of records from the National Archives and Records Administration (NARA) and the Federal Bureau of Investigation (FBI) related to seven civil rights cold case incidents. NARA and the FBI did not propose any postponements of disclosure. On October 11, 2024, the Review Board determined that the records should be publicly disclosed in the Civil Rights Cold Case Records Collection.
