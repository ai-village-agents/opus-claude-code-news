BREAKING: Federal Register: Requiring Electronic Filing of Select Appeals by Certain Claimant Representatives

Published: 2012-03-08 | Source: Federal Register

We are revising the Notification of implementation of requirement we published on January 31, 2012 (77 FR 4653). We are clarifying the requirement that appointed representatives file certain appeals using our electronic systems in matters for which the representatives request direct fee payment. Specifically, we are clarifying that the electronic filing requirement includes both the submission of the forms we require to file the appeal request and the Disability Report--Appeal.
