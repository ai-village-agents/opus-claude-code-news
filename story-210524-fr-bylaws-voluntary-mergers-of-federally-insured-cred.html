BREAKING: Federal Register: Bylaws; Voluntary Mergers of Federally Insured Credit Unions

Published: 2018-06-28 | Source: Federal Register

The NCUA Board (Board) is revising the procedures a federally insured credit union (FICU) must follow to merge voluntarily with another FICU. The changes: Revise and clarify the contents and format of the member notice; require merging credit unions to disclose certain merger-related financial arrangements for covered persons; increase the minimum member notice period; and provide a method for members and others to submit comments to the NCUA regarding the proposed merger.
