BREAKING: Federal Register: Eighth Coast Guard District Annual Special Local Regulation; REV3 Triathlon; Tennessee River 646.0-649.0; Knoxville, TN

Published: 2015-05-18 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard will enforce a Special Local Regulation for the "REV3 Triathlon" on the Tennessee River mile 646.0 to 649.0 from 7:00 a.m. until 9:30 a.m. on May 17, 2015. This action is necessary for the safeguard of participants and spectators, including all crews, vessels, and persons on navigable waters during the "REV3 Triathlon."
