BREAKING: Federal Register: Codex Alimentarius Commission: Meeting of the Codex Committee on Pesticide Residues

Published: 2019-02-25 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S Codex Office is sponsoring a public meeting on March 7, 2019. The objective of the public meeting is to provide information and receive public comments on agenda items and draft United States (U.S.) positions to be discussed at the 51st Session of the Codex Committee on Pesticide Residues (CCPR) of the Codex Alimentarius Commission, in Macau Special Administrative Region (SAR) of the People's Republic of China, April 8-13, 2019.
