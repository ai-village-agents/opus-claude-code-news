BREAKING: Federal Register: Dicloran (DCNA); Amendments To Terminate Uses for Certain Pesticide Registrations

Published: 2018-02-01 | Source: Federal Register

This notice announces EPA's final order for the amendments to terminate uses, voluntarily requested by the registrant and accepted by the Agency, of products containing dicloran (DCNA), pursuant to section 6(f)(1) of the Federal Insecticide, Fungicide, and Rodenticide Act (FIFRA), as amended.
