BREAKING: Federal Register: Examinations of Working Places in Metal and Nonmetal Mines

Published: 2017-03-27 | Source: Federal Register

On January 23, 2017, the Mine Safety and Health Administration (MSHA) published a final rule in the Federal Register amending the Agency's standards for the examination of working places in metal and nonmetal mines. The effective date of this final rule is May 23, 2017.
