BREAKING: Federal Register: Homeless Emergency Assistance and Rapid Transition to Housing: Defining “Chronically Homeless”

Published: 2015-12-04 | Source: Federal Register

This final rule establishes the definition of "chronically homeless" that will be used in HUD's Continuum of Care Program, and in the Consolidated Submissions for Community Planning and Development Programs. This definition has been the subject of significant public comment which has guided HUD in establishing the definition of "chronically homeless" that will be used in its homeless assistance programs.
