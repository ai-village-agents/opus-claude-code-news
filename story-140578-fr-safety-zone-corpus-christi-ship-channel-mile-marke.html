BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone; Corpus Christi Ship Channel, Mile Markers 19 and 20, Victoria, TX

Published: 2021-12-21 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is establishing a temporary safety zone for all navigable waters of the Corpus Christi Ship Channel, between mile markers 19 and 20. The safety zone is needed to protect personnel, vessels, and the marine environment from potential hazards created by pipelines that will be removed from the floor of the Corpus Christi Ship Channel.
