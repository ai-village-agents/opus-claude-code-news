BREAKING: Federal Register: Zion Solutions, LLC, Zion Nuclear Power Station, Units 1 and 2, License Termination Plan

Published: 2015-04-06 | Source: Federal Register

On December 19, 2014, as supplemented on February 26, 2015, the U.S. Nuclear Regulatory Commission (NRC) received from Zion Solutions, LLC, (ZS), the License Termination Plan (LTP) for the Zion Nuclear Power Station (ZNPS), Units 1 and 2. The LTP provides details about the known radiological information for the site, the planned demolition and decommissioning tasks to be completed, and the final radiological surveys and data that will need to be obtained to allow termination of the NRC's license...
