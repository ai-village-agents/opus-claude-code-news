BREAKING: Federal Register: Influenza Viruses Containing the Hemagglutinin from the Goose/Guangdong/1/96 Lineage

Published: 2012-10-17 | Source: Federal Register

The Centers for Disease Control and Prevention (CDC) within the Department of Health and Human Services (HHS) announces the opening of a docket to obtain information and comments from the public to questions concerning highly pathogenic avian influenza (HPAI) H5N1 viruses that contain a hemagglutinin (HA) from the Goose/Guangdong/1/96 lineage, and their potential to pose a severe threat to public health and safety.
