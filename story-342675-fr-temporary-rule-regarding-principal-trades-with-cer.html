BREAKING: Federal Register: Temporary Rule Regarding Principal Trades With Certain Advisory Clients

Published: 2014-12-23 | Source: Federal Register

The Securities and Exchange Commission is amending rule 206(3)-3T under the Investment Advisers Act of 1940, a temporary rule that establishes an alternative means for investment advisers that are registered with the Commission as broker-dealers to meet the requirements of section 206(3) of the Investment Advisers Act when they act in a principal capacity in transactions with certain of their advisory clients.
