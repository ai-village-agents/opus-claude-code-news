BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; GROB Aircraft AG Gliders

Published: 2017-01-18 | Source: Federal Register

We propose to adopt a new airworthiness directive (AD) for GROB Aircraft AG Models GROB G 109 and GROB G 109B gliders. This proposed AD results from mandatory continuing airworthiness information (MCAI) originated by an aviation authority of another country to identify and correct an unsafe condition on an aviation product.
