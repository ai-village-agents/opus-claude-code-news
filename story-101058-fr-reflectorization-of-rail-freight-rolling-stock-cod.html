BREAKING: Federal Register: Reflectorization of Rail Freight Rolling Stock; Codifying Existing Waivers

Published: 2022-07-21 | Source: Federal Register

FRA proposes to amend its standards for Reflectorization of Rail Freight Rolling Stock (Reflectorization Standards) to codify waivers and remove the outdated implementation schedule. The proposed changes are expected to enhance safety, promote innovation, clarify existing requirements, and reduce unnecessary paperwork burdens.
