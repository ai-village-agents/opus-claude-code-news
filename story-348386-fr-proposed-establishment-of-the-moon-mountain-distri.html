BREAKING: Federal Register: Proposed Establishment of the Moon Mountain District Sonoma County Viticultural Area

Published: 2013-03-04 | Source: Federal Register

The Alcohol and Tobacco Tax and Trade Bureau (TTB) proposes to establish the approximately 17,663-acre "Moon Mountain District Sonoma County" viticultural area in Sonoma County, California. The proposed viticultural area lies completely within the established Sonoma Valley viticultural area which, in turn, is within the multi-county North Coast viticultural area.
