BREAKING: Federal Register: Announcement of Foreign-Trade Zones Test

Published: 2013-06-07 | Source: Federal Register

This notice announces U.S. Customs and Border Protection's ("CBP's") plan to conduct a voluntary general test regarding certain foreign-trade zone ("FTZ" or "zone") activities. Pursuant to the FTZ test, under prescribed conditions, zone operators will be permitted liberalized procedures for certain zone activities.
