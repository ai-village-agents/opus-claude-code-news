BREAKING: Federal Register: Air Plan Approval; California; Mojave Desert Air Quality Management District; Oxides of Nitrogen

Published: 2024-12-26 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is proposing to approve a revision to the Mojave Desert Air Quality Management District (MDAQMD) portion of the California State Implementation Plan (SIP). This revision concerns emissions of oxides of nitrogen (NOX) from industrial, institutional, and commercial boilers, steam generators, and process heaters.
