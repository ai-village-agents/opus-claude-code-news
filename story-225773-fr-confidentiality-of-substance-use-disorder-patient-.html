BREAKING: Federal Register: Confidentiality of Substance Use Disorder Patient Records

Published: 2017-01-18 | Source: Federal Register

On Feb. 9, 2016, the Substance Abuse and Mental Health Services Administration (SAMHSA) published a Notice of Proposed Rulemaking (NPRM) that proposed policy changes to update and modernize the Confidentiality of Alcohol and Drug Abuse Patient Records (42 CFR part 2).
