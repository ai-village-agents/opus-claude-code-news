BREAKING: Federal Register: Public Hearing; Susquehanna River Basin Commission

Published: 2014-07-11 | Source: Federal Register

The Susquehanna River Basin Commission will hold a public hearing on August 7, 2014, in Harrisburg, Pennsylvania. At this public hearing, the Commission will hear testimony on the projects listed in the Supplementary Information section of this notice. Such projects are intended to be scheduled for Commission action at its next business meeting, tentatively scheduled for September 4, 2014, which will be noticed separately.
