BREAKING: Federal Register: Dental Devices; Reclassification of Temporary Mandibular Condyle Prosthesis

Published: 2013-12-30 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA) is issuing a final order to reclassify temporary mandibular condyle prosthesis, a preamendments class III device, into class II (special controls), and rename the device "temporary mandibular condyle reconstruction plate."
