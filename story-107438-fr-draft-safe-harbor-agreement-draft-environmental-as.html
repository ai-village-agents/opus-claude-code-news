BREAKING: Federal Register: Draft Safe Harbor Agreement; Draft Environmental Assessment for the Marbled Murrelet in Washington

Published: 2022-10-12 | Source: Federal Register

We, the U.S. Fish and Wildlife Service, have received an application from Weyerhaeuser Timber Holdings, Inc., for an enhancement of survival permit (permit) pursuant to the Endangered Species Act. If granted, the permit would authorize incidental take of the marbled murrelet, associated with forest management actions on private lands.
