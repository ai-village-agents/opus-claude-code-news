BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Pilatus Aircraft Ltd. Airplanes

Published: 2023-05-31 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA is adopting a new airworthiness directive (AD) for certain Pilatus Aircraft Ltd. (Pilatus) Model PC-12, PC-12/45, PC-12/ 47, and PC-12/47E airplanes. This AD is prompted by mandatory continuing airworthiness information (MCAI) originated by an aviation authority of another country to identify and correct an unsafe condition on an aviation product.
