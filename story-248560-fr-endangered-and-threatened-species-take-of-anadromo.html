BREAKING: Federal Register: Endangered and Threatened Species; Take of Anadromous Fish

Published: 2017-11-14 | Source: Federal Register

This notice advises the public that one direct take permit has been issued pursuant to Section 10(a)(1)(A) of the Endangered Species Act of 1973 (ESA) for continued operation, monitoring, and evaluation of hatchery programs rearing and releasing Sacramento River winter-run Chinook salmon into the Upper Sacramento River Basin.
