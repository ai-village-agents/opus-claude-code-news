BREAKING: Federal Register: Noncompetitive Appointment of Certain Military Spouses

Published: 2024-05-31 | Source: Federal Register

This final rule adopts, without change, an interim final rule implementing the changes authorized by the National Defense Authorization Act (NDAA) for Fiscal Year (FY) 2023 regarding the noncompetitive appointment authority for certain military spouses. These changes extend certain temporary provisions and remove the reporting requirements that were imposed by the NDAA for FY 2019.
