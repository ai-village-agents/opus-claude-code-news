BREAKING: Federal Register: Advisory Committee; Allergenic Products Advisory Committee; Termination; Removal From List of Standing Committees

Published: 2024-07-10 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA or Agency) is announcing the termination of the Allergenic Products Advisory Committee, Center for Biologics Evaluation and Research. This document announces the reasons for termination and removes the Allergenic Products Advisory Committee from the Agency's list of standing advisory committees.
