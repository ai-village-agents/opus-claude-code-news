BREAKING: Federal Register: Offshore Supply Vessels of at Least 6,000 GT ITC

Published: 2014-08-18 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard Authorization Act of 2010 (the Act) removed the statutory size limit previously placed on offshore supply vessels (OSVs), and required the Coast Guard to issue regulations to mitigate the risk created as a result, noting the need ``to ensure the safe carriage of oil, hazardous substances, and individuals in addition to the crew'' on OSVs exceeding the previous size limit.
