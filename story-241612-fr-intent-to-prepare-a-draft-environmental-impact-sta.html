BREAKING: Federal Register: Intent To Prepare a Draft Environmental Impact Statement for the Coos Bay Channel Modification Project

Published: 2017-08-18 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Army Corps of Engineers, Portland District intends to prepare an Environmental Impact Statement (EIS) to analyze the potential environmental effects of approving the Oregon International Port of Coos Bay's (OIPCB) proposed Coos Bay Channel Modification Project.
