BREAKING: Federal Register: Air Plan Approval; Ohio; Withdrawal of Technical Amendment

Published: 2024-02-22 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is proposing to correct the November 19, 2020, removal of the Air Nuisance Rule (ANR) from the Ohio State Implementation Plan (SIP). This action is in response to a February 10, 2023, decision by the United States Court of Appeals for the Sixth Circuit to remand without vacatur EPA's removal of the ANR from the Ohio SIP.
