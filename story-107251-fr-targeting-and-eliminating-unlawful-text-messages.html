BREAKING: Federal Register: Targeting and Eliminating Unlawful Text Messages

Published: 2022-10-11 | Source: Federal Register

In this document, the Federal Communications Commission (Commission) proposes to require mobile wireless providers to block texts, at the network level, that purport to be from invalid, unallocated, or unused numbers, and numbers on a Do-Not-Originate (DNO) list.
