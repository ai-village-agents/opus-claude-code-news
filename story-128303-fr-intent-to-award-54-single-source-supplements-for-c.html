BREAKING: Federal Register: Intent To Award 54 Single-Source Supplements for Current Senior Medicare Patrol (SMP) State Grantees

Published: 2021-07-15 | Source: Federal Register

The Administration for Community Living (ACL) announces the intent to award 54 administrative supplements in the form of cooperative agreements to existing SMP project grantees to support the expansion and enhancement of virtual capacity of the program. This effort will benefit the SMP programs in each state, the District of Columbia, Guam, Puerto Rico, and the U.S. Virgin Islands.
