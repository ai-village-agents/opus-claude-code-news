BREAKING: Federal Register: Department of Transportation Final Environmental Justice Strategy

Published: 2012-03-28 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Transportation is issuing a revised environmental justice strategy, which sets forth DOT's commitment to identifying and addressing disproportionately high and adverse human health and environmental effects of agency policies and activities on minority and low-income populations.
