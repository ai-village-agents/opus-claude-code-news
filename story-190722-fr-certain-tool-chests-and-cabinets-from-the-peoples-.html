BREAKING: Federal Register: Certain Tool Chests and Cabinets From the People's Republic of China: Preliminary Results of Countervailing Duty Administrative Review; 2017-2018

Published: 2019-10-09 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Commerce (Commerce) preliminarily determines that Zhongshan Geelong Manufacturing Co. Ltd. (Geelong), the sole producer subject to this administrative review of certain tool chests and cabinets (tool chests) from the People's Republic of China (China) received countervailable subsidies during the period of review (POR), September 15, 2017 through December 31, 2018. Interested parties are invited to comment on these preliminary results of review.
