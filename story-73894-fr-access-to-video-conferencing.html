BREAKING: Federal Register: Access to Video Conferencing

Published: 2023-08-07 | Source: Federal Register

In this document, the Federal Communications Commission (FCC or Commission) proposes to amend its rules to ensure that interoperable video conferencing services (IVCS) are accessible to people with disabilities and to facilitate the integration and appropriate use of telecommunications relay services (TRS) with video conferencing.
