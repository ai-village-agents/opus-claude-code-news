BREAKING: Federal Register: Federal Acquisition Regulation; Submission for OMB Review; Accident Prevention Plans and Recordkeeping

Under the provisions of the Paperwork Reduction Act, Regulatory Secretariat will be submitting to the Office of Management and Budget (OMB) a request to review and approve an extension of a previously approved information collection requirement concerning Accident Prevention Plans and Recordkeeping.
