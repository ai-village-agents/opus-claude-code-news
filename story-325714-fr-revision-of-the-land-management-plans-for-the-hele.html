BREAKING: Federal Register: Revision of the Land Management Plans for the Helena and Lewis and Clark National Forests

Published: 2014-05-30 | Source: Federal Register

The Helena and Lewis and Clark National Forests, located in Montana, are initiating the forest planning process pursuant to the 2012 Forest Planning Rule. One assessment and one plan will be developed for both Forests. This process results in a Forest Land Management Plan which describes the strategic direction for management of forest resources for the next ten to fifteen years on the Helena and Lewis and Clark National Forests.
