BREAKING: Federal Register: Privacy Act of 1974; System of Records

Published: 2014-06-11 | Source: Federal Register

The Office of the Secretary of Defense is adding a system of records, DHRA 13 DoD, entitled "Defense Travel Management Office (DTMO) Workforce Assessment," to its inventory of record systems subject to the Privacy Act of 1974, as amended. This system will be used to administer an assessment that will enable the analysis of workforce capabilities and competency gaps within the DTMO organization.
