BREAKING: Federal Register: National Wildlife Refuge System; 2024-2025 Station-Specific Hunting and Sport Fishing Regulations

Published: 2024-11-07 | Source: Federal Register

We, the U.S. Fish and Wildlife Service (Service), open or expand hunting opportunities on 12 National Wildlife Refuges (NWRs). We also close hunting on 111 acres at Crab Orchard NWR so that the area can be repurposed for other recreational uses, including camping.
