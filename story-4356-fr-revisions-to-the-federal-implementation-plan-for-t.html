BREAKING: Federal Register: Revisions to the Federal Implementation Plan for the Billings/Laurel, Montana, Sulfur Dioxide Area

Published: 2025-01-17 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is revising a Federal Implementation Plan (FIP) applicable to sulfur dioxide (SO2) emissions from four sources located in Billings and Laurel, Montana. Specifically, the EPA is revising a portion of the FIP promulgated by the EPA in 2008 (2008 Billings/Laurel SO2 FIP) by removing a provision which contained an affirmative defense for exceedances of flare emission limits during malfunctions, startups, and shutdowns.
