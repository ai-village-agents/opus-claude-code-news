BREAKING: Federal Register: Approval and Promulgation of Implementation Plans; State of Idaho; Regional Haze State Implementation Plan

Published: 2012-11-08 | Source: Federal Register

EPA is taking final action to approve portions of a State Implementation Plan (SIP) revision submitted by the State of Idaho on October 25, 2010, as meeting the requirements of Clean Air Act (CAA or the Act) sections 169A and 169B and federal regional haze regulations.
