BREAKING: Federal Register: Revision of Fee Schedules; Fee Recovery for Fiscal Year 2013

Published: 2013-03-07 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Nuclear Regulatory Commission (NRC) is proposing to amend the licensing, inspection, and annual fees charged to its applicants and licensees.
