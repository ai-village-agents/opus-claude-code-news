BREAKING: Federal Register: The Declaration of Allulose and Calories From Allulose on Nutrition and Supplement Facts Labels; Availability

Published: 2020-10-19 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA or we) is announcing the availability of a final guidance for industry entitled "The Declaration of Allulose and Calories from Allulose on Nutrition and Supplement Facts Labels." The guidance describes FDA's views on the declaration of allulose on Nutrition Facts and Supplement Facts labels and the caloric content of allulose.
