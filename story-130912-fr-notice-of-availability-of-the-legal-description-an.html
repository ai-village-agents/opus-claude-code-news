BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Availability of the Legal Description and Maps Depicting Oregon and California Railroad Revested Lands, Oregon

Published: 2021-08-18 | Source: Federal Register

In accordance with the Western Oregon Tribal Fairness Act, the Bureau of Land Management (BLM) is providing notice of the availability of the legal description and maps depicting Oregon and California Railroad Revested (O&C) lands in western Oregon that were conveyed to two tribes in 2018, and other public domain lands in western Oregon that were reclassified as O&C lands in 2020.
