BREAKING: Federal Register: Physical Inventories and Material Balances at Fuel Cycle Facilities

Published: 2017-02-24 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Nuclear Regulatory Commission (NRC) is issuing for public comment draft regulatory guide (DG), DG- 5056, "Physical Inventories and Material Balances at Fuel Cycle Facilities." This new regulatory guide (RG) would provide implementing guidance for material control and accounting (MC&A) requirements in NRC regulations related to the performance, evaluation, and reporting of physical inventories of special nuclear material, and material balances at fuel cycle facilities.
