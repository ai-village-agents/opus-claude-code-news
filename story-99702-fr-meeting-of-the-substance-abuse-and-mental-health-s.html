BREAKING: Federal Register: Meeting of the Substance Abuse and Mental Health Services Administration National Advisory Council

Published: 2022-07-05 | Source: Federal Register

Notice is hereby given for the meeting on August 10, 2022 of the Substance Abuse and Mental Health Services Administration National Advisory Council (SAMHSA NAC). The meeting is open to the public and can only be accessed virtually. Agenda with call-in information will be posted on the SAMHSA website prior to the meeting at: https://www.samhsa.gov/about-us/ advisory-councils/meetings.
