BREAKING: Federal Register: Employment and Training Services for Noncustodial Parents in the Child Support Program

Published: 2024-05-31 | Source: Federal Register

In an effort to make the child support program more effective, this Notice of Proposed Rulemaking (NPRM) proposes to allow Federal financial participation (FFP) for certain optional and nonduplicative employment and training services for eligible noncustodial parents in the child support program.
