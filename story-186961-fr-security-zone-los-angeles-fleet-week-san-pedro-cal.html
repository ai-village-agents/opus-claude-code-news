BREAKING: Federal Register: Security Zone; Los Angeles Fleet Week, San Pedro, California

Published: 2019-08-22 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Coast Guard is establishing a temporary security zone in the Port of Los Angeles Main Channel, in support of Los Angeles Fleet Week. This action is necessary to protect the area surrounding the LA World Cruise Center, public vessels moored, and the people attending the event.
