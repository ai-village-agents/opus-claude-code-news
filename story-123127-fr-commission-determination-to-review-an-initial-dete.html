BREAKING: Federal Register: Commission Determination To Review an Initial Determination Granting Summary Determination and on Review To Vacate as Moot; Notice of Commission Determination Not To Review an Initial Determination Terminating the Investigation in Its Entirety Based on a Withdrawal of the Complaint; Termination of the Investigation; Certain Pre-Filled Syringes for Intravitreal Injection and Components Thereof

Published: 2021-05-06 | Source: Federal Register

Notice is hereby given that the U.S. International Trade Commission ("Commission") has determined to review an initial determination ("ID") (Order No. 31) granting summary determination of infringement and of domestic industry, and on review, to vacate that ID as moot, and not to review a second ID (Order No. 33) terminating the investigation based on a withdrawal of the complaint. The investigation is terminated.
