BREAKING: Federal Register: Large Diameter Welded Pipe From Greece: Initiation of Antidumping Duty Changed Circumstances Review

Published: 2020-02-21 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Commerce (Commerce) is initiating a changed circumstances review (CCR) of the antidumping duty (AD) order on large diameter welded pipe from Greece.
