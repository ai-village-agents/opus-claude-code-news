BREAKING: Federal Register: Certification of Tribal Housing Counselors

Published: 2024-06-12 | Source: Federal Register

HUD's Housing Counseling Program provides counseling to individuals seeking information about financing, maintaining, renting, or owning a home. Established by the Dodd-Frank Wall Street Reform and Consumer Protection Act, entities and individual counselors that participate in the Housing Counseling Program are required to be certified by HUD as competent to provide counseling services.
