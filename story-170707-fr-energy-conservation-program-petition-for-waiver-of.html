BREAKING: Federal Register: Energy Conservation Program: Petition for Waiver of Beghelli North America From the Department of Energy Illuminated Exit Signs Test Procedure

Published: 2019-02-06 | Source: Federal Register

This document announces receipt of and publishes a petition for waiver from Beghelli North America ("Beghelli"), which seeks a waiver from the U.S. Department of Energy ("DOE") test procedure used for determining the energy consumption of specified illuminated exit sign basic models. Beghelli seeks to use an alternate test procedure to address issues involved in testing the basic models identified in its petition.
