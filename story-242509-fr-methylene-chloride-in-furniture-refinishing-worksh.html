BREAKING: Federal Register: Methylene Chloride in Furniture Refinishing; Workshop

Published: 2017-08-30 | Source: Federal Register

On September 12, 2017, EPA is holding a workshop on the use of methylene chloride in furniture refinishing. In a proposed rule published on January 19, 2017, EPA proposed to prohibit manufacture (including import), processing, and distribution in commerce of methylene chloride in consumer paint and coating removal and most types of commercial paint and coating removal, except for commercial furniture refinishing.
