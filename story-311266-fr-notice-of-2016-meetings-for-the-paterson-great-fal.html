BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of 2016 Meetings for the Paterson Great Falls National Historical Park Advisory Commission

Published: 2015-12-09 | Source: Federal Register

As required by the Federal Advisory Committee Act (5 U.S.C. Appendix 1-16), the National Park Service is hereby giving notice for the 2016 schedule of meetings for the Paterson Great Falls National Historical Park Advisory Commission. The Commission is authorized by the Omnibus Public Land Management Act, (16 U.S.C. 410lll), "to advise the Secretary in the development and implementation of the management plan."
