BREAKING: Federal Register: Federal Implementation Plans Under the Clean Air Act for Indian Reservations in Idaho, Oregon, and Washington

Published: 2022-10-12 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) proposes to revise the Federal Air Rules for Reservations (FARR), which is a collection of Federal Implementation Plans (FIPs) under the Clean Air Act for Indian reservations in Idaho, Oregon, and Washington.
