BREAKING: Federal Register: Radio Broadcasting Services; Dove Creek, CO

Published: 2012-12-26 | Source: Federal Register

This document sets forth a proposal to amend the FM Table of Allotments, Section 73.202(b) of the Commission's rules. The Commission requests comment on a petition filed by Cochise Media Licenses, LLC, proposing to amend the Table of Allotments by allotting FM Channel 229C3 as a first local service at Dove Creek, Colorado.
