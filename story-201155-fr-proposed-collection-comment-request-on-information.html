BREAKING: Federal Register: Proposed Collection; Comment Request on Information Collection Tools Relating to IRS Customer Satisfaction Surveys

Published: 2018-03-01 | Source: Federal Register

The Internal Revenue Service (IRS), as part of its continuing effort to reduce paperwork and respondent burden, invites the general public and other Federal agencies to take this opportunity to comment on information collections, as required by the Paperwork Reduction Act of 1995. The IRS is soliciting comments concerning IRS Customer Satisfaction Surveys.
