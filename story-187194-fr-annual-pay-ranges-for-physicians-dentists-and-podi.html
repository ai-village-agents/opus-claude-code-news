BREAKING: Federal Register: Annual Pay Ranges for Physicians, Dentists, and Podiatrists of the Veterans Health Administration (VHA)

Published: 2019-08-26 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Veterans Affairs (VA) is correcting a Notice that published in the Federal Register on August 20, 2019 which provides information on the annual pay range, which is the sum of the base pay rate and market pay for Veterans Health Administration (VHA) physicians, dentists, and podiatrists as prescribed by the Secretary for Department-wide applicability.
