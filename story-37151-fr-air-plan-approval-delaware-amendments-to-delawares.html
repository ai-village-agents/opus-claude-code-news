BREAKING: Federal Register: Air Plan Approval; Delaware; Amendments to Delaware's Requirements for Public Notice of Certain Permits

Published: 2024-05-16 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is approving a state implementation plan (SIP) revision submitted by the State of Delaware (Delaware SIP). This revision approves amendments Delaware made to its underlying regulation to standardize the public notices requirements across various permits covered by Delaware regulation 1102.
