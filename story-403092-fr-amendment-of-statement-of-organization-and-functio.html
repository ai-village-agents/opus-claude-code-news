BREAKING: Federal Register: Amendment of Statement of Organization and Functions; Restructuring of National Labor Relations Board's Field Organization

Published: 2012-12-06 | Source: Federal Register

The National Labor Relations Board is restructuring its Regional Offices in Kansas City, Winston-Salem, Hartford and Memphis to designate them as Subregional Offices assigned to the supervision of the St. Louis, Atlanta, Boston and New Orleans Regional Offices, respectively.
