BREAKING: Federal Register: Finding of Failure To Attain the 2006 24-Hour Fine Particulate Matter Standards; California; Los Angeles-South Coast Air Basin

Published: 2020-09-16 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) has determined that the Los Angeles-South Coast Air Basin nonattainment area failed to attain the 2006 24-hour fine particulate matter ("PM2.5") national ambient air quality standards by the December 31, 2019 "Serious" area attainment date. This determination is based on ambient air quality monitoring data from 2017 through 2019.
