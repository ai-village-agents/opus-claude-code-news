BREAKING: Federal Register: Possible Models for the Administration and Support of Discipline-Specific Guidance Groups for Forensic Science

Published: 2013-11-13 | Source: Federal Register

NIST is extending the deadline for submitting comments relating to Possible Models for the Administration and Support of Discipline-Specific Guidance Groups for Forensic Science. Due to the lack of availability of information posted on the NIST Web site, and the lack of NIST staff to respond to questions during the recent government shutdown due to a lapse in appropriations, the public may not have been able to formulate or submit their input.
