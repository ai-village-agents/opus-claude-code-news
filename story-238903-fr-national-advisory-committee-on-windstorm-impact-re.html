BREAKING: Federal Register: National Advisory Committee on Windstorm Impact Reduction Meetings

Published: 2017-07-13 | Source: Federal Register

The National Advisory Committee on Windstorm Impact Reduction (NACWIR or Committee), will hold three upcoming meetings continuing the work of the Committee. Interested members of the public will be able to view the video conferences and participate from remote locations by calling in to a central phone number.
