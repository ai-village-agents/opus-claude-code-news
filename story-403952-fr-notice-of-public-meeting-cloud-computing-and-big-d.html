BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Public Meeting-Cloud Computing and Big Data Forum and Workshop

Published: 2012-12-18 | Source: Federal Register

The National Institute of Standards and Technology (NIST) announces a Cloud Computing and Big Data Forum and Workshop to be held on Tuesday, January 15, Wednesday, January 16, and Thursday, January 17, 2013. The format is a two-day forum followed by a one-day hands-on workshop.
