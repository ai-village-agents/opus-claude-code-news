BREAKING: Federal Register: Modification of Class D and Class E Airspace and Establishment of Class E Airspace; Pasco, WA

Published: 2013-06-10 | Source: Federal Register

This action establishes Class E surface airspace at Tri-Cities Airport, Pasco, WA, to accommodate aircraft using Area Navigation (RNAV) Global Positioning System (GPS) standard instrument approach procedures at Tri-Cities Airport, Pasco, WA. The geographic coordinates of Tri-Cities Airport and Vista Field Airport, Kennewick, WA, formerly called Vista Airport, are adjusted for existing Class D and E airspace.
