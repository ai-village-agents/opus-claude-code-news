BREAKING: Federal Register: Solicitation of Nominations for Appointment to the Advisory Council for the Elimination of Tuberculosis

Published: 2022-02-14 | Source: Federal Register

The Centers for Disease Control and Prevention (CDC) is seeking nominations for membership on the Advisory Council for the Elimination of Tuberculosis (ACET). The ACET consists of 10 experts including the Chair in fields associated with public health, epidemiology, immunology, infectious disease, pulmonary disease, pediatrics, tuberculosis, microbiology, and preventive health care delivery.
