BREAKING: Federal Register: Aircraft Certification Service (AIR) Reorganization/Merger Announcement

Published: 2014-01-29 | Source: Federal Register

This action gives notice to the American public and aviation industry of the FAA's Aviation Safety Office's (AVS), Aircraft Certification Service (AIR) reorganization/merger. The Aircraft Engineering Division (AIR-100) and Production and Airworthiness Division (AIR-200) are merging to become the Design, Manufacturing, and Airworthiness Division, AIR-100.
