BREAKING: Federal Register: Expiration of the Extended Missing Parts Pilot Program

Published: 2019-01-28 | Source: Federal Register

The United States Patent and Trademark Office (USPTO) implemented a pilot program (Extended Missing Parts Pilot Program) in which an applicant, under certain conditions, can request a 12-month time period to pay the search fee, the examination fee, any excess claim fees, and the surcharge (for the late submission of the search fee and the examination fee) in a nonprovisional application.
