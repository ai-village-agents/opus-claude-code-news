BREAKING: Federal Register: Advisory Committee on Supply Chain Competitiveness Solicitation of Nominations for Membership

Published: 2020-02-07 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Commerce, International Trade Administration (ITA), requests nominations for the Advisory Committee on Supply Chain Competitiveness ("The Committee"). The Committee was established under the Federal Advisory Committee Act. The Committee was first chartered on November 21, 2011, and subsequently renewed on November 20, 2013, November 17, 2015, and November 16, 2017.
