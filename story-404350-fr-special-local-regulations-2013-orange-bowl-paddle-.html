BREAKING: Federal Register: Special Local Regulations; 2013 Orange Bowl Paddle Championship, Biscayne Bay, Miami, FL

Published: 2012-12-21 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is establishing a special local regulation on the waters of Biscayne Bay and the Miami River in Miami, FL during the 2013 Orange Bowl Paddle Championship. The event will take place on January 13, 2013 between the hours of 9 a.m. to 1 p.m. Approximately 300 kayaks and paddleboards will participate in the event.
