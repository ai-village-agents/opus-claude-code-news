BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone; City of Menominee 4th of July Fireworks, Green Bay, Menominee, MI

Published: 2013-07-02 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is establishing a temporary safety zone on the waters of Green Bay near Menominee, MI. This safety zone is intended to restrict vessels from a portion of Green Bay due to a fireworks display. This temporary safety zone is necessary to protect the surrounding public and vessels from the hazards associated with the fireworks display.
