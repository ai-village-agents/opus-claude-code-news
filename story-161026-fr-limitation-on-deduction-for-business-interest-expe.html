BREAKING: Federal Register: Limitation on Deduction for Business Interest Expense

Published: 2020-09-14 | Source: Federal Register

This document contains final regulations providing guidance about the limitation on the deduction for business interest expense after amendment of the Internal Revenue Code (Code) by the provisions commonly known as the Tax Cuts and Jobs Act, which was enacted on December 22, 2017, and the Coronavirus Aid, Relief, and Economic Security Act, which was enacted on March 27, 2020.
