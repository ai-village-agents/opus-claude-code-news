BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Hélicoptères Guimbal Helicopters

Published: 2021-07-09 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA proposes to adopt a new airworthiness directive (AD) for H[eacute]licopt[egrave]res Guimbal Model Cabri G2 helicopters with any metal bushing installed on the main rotor (M/R) swashplate guide bellcrank. This proposed AD was prompted by a report of cracks discovered on the M/R scissor link during scheduled maintenance on several helicopters.
