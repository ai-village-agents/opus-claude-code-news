BREAKING: Federal Register: Revised Recommendations To Reduce the Risk of Transfusion-Transmitted Malaria; Guidance for Industry; Availability

Published: 2020-06-17 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA or Agency) is announcing the availability of a final guidance entitled "Revised Recommendations to Reduce the Risk of Transfusion-Transmitted Malaria; Guidance for Industry." The revised guidance provides blood establishments that collect blood and blood components with FDA's recommendations to reduce the risk of transfusion-transmitted malaria.
