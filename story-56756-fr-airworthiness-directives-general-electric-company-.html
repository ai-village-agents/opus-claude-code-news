BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; General Electric Company Engines

Published: 2024-12-27 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA proposes to adopt a new airworthiness directive (AD) for certain General Electric Company (GE) Model CF34-8C1, CF34-8C5, CF34-8C5A1, CF34-8C5A2, CF34-8C5A3, CF34-8C5B1, CF34-8E2, CF34-8E2A1, CF34-8E5, CF34-8E5A1, CF34-8E5A2, CF34-8E6, and CF34-8E6A1 engines. This proposed AD was prompted by a predicted reduction in the cyclic life of the combustion chamber assembly (CCA) forward flange.
