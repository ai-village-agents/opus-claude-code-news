BREAKING: Federal Register: Sexual Assault Prevention and Response Program Procedures

Published: 2020-09-17 | Source: Federal Register

This final rule removes the Department of Defense's (DoD's) duplicative regulation concerning the Sexual Assault Prevention and Response Program (SAPR) Procedures. On July 15, 2020, DoD published a single revised DoD-level SAPR Program rule, which finalized two previously published interim final rules.
