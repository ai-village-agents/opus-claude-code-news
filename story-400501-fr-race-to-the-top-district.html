BREAKING: Federal Register: Race to the Top-District

Published: 2012-11-02 | Source: Federal Register

The Secretary reopens the Race to the Top--District competition to extend the deadline for submitting applications. Hurricane Sandy prevented many applicants from submitting their applications by the October 30, 2012, deadline. The hurricane also closed Federal Government offices in Washington, DC, on October 29 and 30, 2012. The Department, therefore, could not receive applications on those days.
