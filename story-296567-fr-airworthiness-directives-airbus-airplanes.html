BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Airbus Airplanes

Published: 2015-06-16 | Source: Federal Register

We are adopting a new airworthiness directive (AD) for all Airbus Model A318, A319, A320, and A321 series airplanes. This AD was prompted by a report of corrosion found during the manufacturing process for some oxygen pipe assemblies that are used to supply oxygen to the flightcrew.
