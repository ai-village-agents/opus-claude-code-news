BREAKING: Federal Register: Drawbridge Operation Regulations; Belle River, LA

Published: 2019-04-16 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is issuing a temporary deviation to the operating schedule that regulates the State Route 70 (SR 70), pontoon bridge across the Belle River mile 23.8, near Pierre Part, Assumption Parish, Louisiana.
