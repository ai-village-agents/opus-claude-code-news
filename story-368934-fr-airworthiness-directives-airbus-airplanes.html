BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Airbus Airplanes

Published: 2013-10-31 | Source: Federal Register

We are adopting a new airworthiness directive (AD) for all Airbus Model A330-300 series airplanes and Model A340-200 and -300 series airplanes. This AD was prompted by reports of corrosion found on certain trimmable horizontal stabilizer actuators (THSA), affecting the ballscrew lower splines between the tie bar and the screw-jack.
