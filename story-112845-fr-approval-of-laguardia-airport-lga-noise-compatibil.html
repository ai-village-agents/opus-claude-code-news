BREAKING: Federal Register: Approval of LaGuardia Airport (LGA) Noise Compatibility Program

Published: 2022-12-21 | Source: Federal Register

On July 6, 2022 the FAA determined that the revised noise exposure maps submitted by LGA were in compliance with applicable requirements and that the noise compatibility program would be initiating final review for approval or disapproval.
