BREAKING: Federal Register: In the Matter of Southern Nuclear Operating Company

Published: 2019-11-27 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Nuclear Regulatory Commission (NRC) is issuing a Confirmatory Order to Southern Nuclear Operating Company (SNC). This action is based on two investigations conducted by the NRC Office of Investigations (OI), that apparent willful violations of NRC's regulations regarding "Employee Protection," occurred.
