BREAKING: Federal Register: Vithal K. Patel; Denial of Hearing; Final Debarment Order

Published: 2021-08-02 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA) is denying a request for a hearing submitted by Vithal K. Patel (Mr. Patel) and issuing an order under the Federal Food, Drug, and Cosmetic Act (FD&C Act) debarring Mr. Patel for 1 year from providing services in any capacity to a person that has an approved or pending drug product application.
