BREAKING: Federal Register: Deep Seabed Mining: Revisions to Regulations for Exploration License and Commercial Recovery Permit Applications

Published: 2025-08-04 | Source: Federal Register

NOAA will hold two virtual public hearings, on September 3, 2025, and on September 4, 2025, to receive oral comments on the July 7, 2025, proposed rule for revisions to the Deep Seabed Hard Mineral Resources Act (DSHMRA or the Act) regulations.
