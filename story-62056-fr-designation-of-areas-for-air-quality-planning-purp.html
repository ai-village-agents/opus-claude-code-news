BREAKING: Federal Register: Designation of Areas for Air Quality Planning Purposes; California; Coachella Valley Ozone Nonattainment Area; Reclassification to Extreme

Published: 2023-03-08 | Source: Federal Register

Under the Clean Air Act (CAA), the Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is granting requests by the California Air Resources Board (CARB) to reclassify the Coachella Valley ozone nonattainment area in California from "Severe-15" to "Extreme" for the 2008 ozone national ambient air quality standards (NAAQS).
