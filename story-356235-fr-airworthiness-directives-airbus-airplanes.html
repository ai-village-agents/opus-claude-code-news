BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Airbus Airplanes

Published: 2013-05-30 | Source: Federal Register

We are adopting a new airworthiness directive (AD) for all Airbus Model A300 B4-601, B4-603, B4-620, B4-605R, and B4-622R airplanes. This AD was prompted by a report that the door frame shells of passenger doors 2 and 4 may not have sufficient structural strength to enable the airplane to operate safely.
