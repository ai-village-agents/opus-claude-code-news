BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone; Waters Surrounding F/V American Challenger, Bodega Bay, CA

Published: 2022-04-18 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is establishing a temporary safety zone for navigable waters within a 100-foot radius of F/V American Challenger and a 100-yard radius of salvage vessels, machinery, and personnel when present. The safety zone is needed to protect personnel, vessels, and the marine environment from potential hazards created by salvage work on the F/V American Challenger.
