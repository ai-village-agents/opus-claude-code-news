BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety and Security Zones, Jacksonville Captain of the Port Zone

Published: 2014-06-18 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard proposes to modify several aspects of the safety and security zones within the Sector Jacksonville Captain of the Port Zone. This action is necessary to consolidate, clarify, and otherwise modify safety and security zone regulations to eliminate unnecessary regulations and better meet the safety and security needs of the Ports of Jacksonville, Fernandina, and Canaveral.
