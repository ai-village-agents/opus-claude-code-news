BREAKING: Federal Register: Fuel Tank Vent Fire Protection

Published: 2014-08-15 | Source: Federal Register

This action would amend certain airworthiness regulations for transport category airplanes to require fuel tank designs that prevent a fuel tank explosion caused by flame propagation through the fuel tank vents from external fires. This action would add a new requirement for fuel tank vent fire protection and would increase the time available for passenger evacuation and emergency response.
