BREAKING: Federal Register: Clean Vessel Act Grant Program

Published: 2015-09-14 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Fish and Wildlife Service (Service) is seeking comments to assist us in developing a proposed rule for the Clean Vessel Act Grant Program (CVA). The proposed rule will use plain language to clarify topics that have led to varying interpretations and will incorporate changes in legislation and technology.
