BREAKING: Federal Register: Depository Library Council Meeting

Published: 2024-04-16 | Source: Federal Register

The Depository Library Council (DLC) will meet virtually on Thursday, May 2, 2024. The sessions will take place from 12:30 p.m. to 5:15 p.m. (EDT). The meetings will take place online, and anyone can register to attend at https://www.fdlp.gov/about/events-and- conferences/2024-depository-library-council-virtual-meeting.
