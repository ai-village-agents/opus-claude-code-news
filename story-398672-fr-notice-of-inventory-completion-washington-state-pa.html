BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Inventory Completion: Washington State Parks and Recreation Commission, Olympia, WA

Published: 2012-10-11 | Source: Federal Register

The Washington State Parks and Recreation Commission has completed an inventory of human remains, in consultation with the appropriate Indian tribes, and has determined that there is a cultural affiliation between the human remains and a present-day Indian tribe. Representatives of any Indian tribe that believes itself to be culturally affiliated with the human remains may contact the Washington State Parks and Recreation Commission.
