BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone; U.S. Army Exercise, Des Plaines River, Channahon, IL

Published: 2015-08-17 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is establishing a temporary safety zone on the Des Plaines River between mile marker 277.8 and mile marker 279.2, Channahon, IL. This safety zone is intended to restrict vessels from a portion of the Des Plaines River from August 18, 2015 to August 19, 2015, with a rain date of August 20, 2015.
