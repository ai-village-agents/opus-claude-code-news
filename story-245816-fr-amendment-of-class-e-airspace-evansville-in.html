BREAKING: Federal Register: Amendment of Class E Airspace; Evansville, IN

Published: 2017-10-10 | Source: Federal Register

This action amends Class E airspace extending upward from 700 feet above the surface at Evansville Regional Airport, Evansville, Indiana. This action is necessary due to the decommissioning of the Evansville non-directional radio beacon (NDB) and cancellation of the NDB approach, and it enhances the safety and management of instrument flight rules (IFR) operations at the airport.
