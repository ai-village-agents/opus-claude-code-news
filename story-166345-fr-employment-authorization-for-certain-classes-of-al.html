BREAKING: Federal Register: Employment Authorization for Certain Classes of Aliens With Final Orders of Removal

Published: 2020-11-19 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Department of Homeland Security (DHS) is proposing to eliminate employment authorization eligibility for aliens who have final orders of removal but are temporarily released from custody on an order of supervision with one narrow exception.
