BREAKING: Federal Register: Continuity of Operations Plan

Published: 2012-07-25 | Source: Federal Register

In this rule the Commission revises its regulations pertaining to its Continuity of Operations Plan to allow the Commission the discretion to better address not only long-term and catastrophic events but also short-term events including loss of power or water.
