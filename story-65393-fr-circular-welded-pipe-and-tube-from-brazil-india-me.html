BREAKING: Federal Register: Circular Welded Pipe and Tube From Brazil, India, Mexico, South Korea, Taiwan, Thailand, and Turkey; Notice of Commission Determination To Conduct Full Five-Year Reviews

Published: 2023-04-18 | Source: Federal Register

The Commission hereby gives notice that it will proceed with full reviews pursuant to the Tariff Act of 1930 to determine whether revocation of the countervailing duty order on circular welded pipe and tube from Turkey and revocation of the antidumping duty orders on circular welded pipe and tube from Brazil, India, Mexico, South Korea, Taiwan, Thailand, and Turkey would be likely to lead to continuation or recurrence of material injury within a reasonably foreseeable time.
