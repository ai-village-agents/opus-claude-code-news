BREAKING: Federal Register: Amendment of Class D and Class E5 Airspace; Revocation of Class E4 Airspace, Dover, DE

Published: 2025-08-01 | Source: Federal Register

This action amends the Class D airspace at Dover Air Force Base (AFB) due to the currently designated airspace not properly containing instrument flight rule (IFR) operations. Additionally, this action establishes Class E surface airspace and revokes the Class E surface extension airspace extending upward from the surface above Dover AFB, Dover, DE, due to the airspace no longer meeting the specifications of its current designation.
