BREAKING: Federal Register: Privacy Act of 1974; System of Records

Published: 2013-03-22 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Veterans Affairs (VA) published a notice in the Federal Register on October 31, 2012 (77 FR 65938), to amend its system of records entitled "Patient Medical Record--VA" (24VA19) as set forth in the Federal Register at 74 FR 60040. That notice contained several errors that are corrected by this document.
