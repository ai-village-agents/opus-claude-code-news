BREAKING: Federal Register: Performance Specification 18-Specifications and Test Procedures for Gaseous HCl Continuous Emission Monitoring Systems at Stationary Sources

Published: 2014-06-03 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is announcing that the period for providing public comments on the May 14, 2014, proposed "Performance Specification 18--Specifications and Test Procedures for Gaseous HCl Continuous Emission Monitoring Systems at Stationary Sources" is being extended by 30 days.
