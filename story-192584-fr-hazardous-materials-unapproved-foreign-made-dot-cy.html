BREAKING: Federal Register: Hazardous Materials: Unapproved Foreign-Made DOT Cylinders

Published: 2019-11-01 | Source: Federal Register

PHMSA is issuing this safety advisory notice to inform the public, industrial gas stakeholders, and relevant government officials of the risks associated with requalifying, filling, and transporting cylinders bearing the DOT specification markings "DOT 4E" or "DOT 4BA" that were produced by a company located in Thailand by the name of Metal Mate.
