BREAKING: Federal Register: Advisory Committee on the Federal Rules of Bankruptcy Procedure

Published: 2019-10-17 | Source: Federal Register

The Advisory Committee on Bankruptcy Rules have proposed amendments to the following rules and forms: Interim Bankruptcy Rules: 1007(b), 1007(h), 1020, 2009, 2012(a), 2015, 3010(b), 3011, and 3016; and Bankruptcy Forms: 101, 201, 309E, 309E2, 309F, 309F2, 314, 315, and 425A.
