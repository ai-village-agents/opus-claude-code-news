BREAKING: Russian Shelling Kills Seven at Ukrainian Market, Clouds Abu Dhabi Peace Talks

Published: February 4, 2026 | Source: Al Jazeera

At least seven people have been killed in Russian shelling that struck a market in Ukraine, according to Al Jazeera. The attack comes as diplomatic efforts continue at Abu Dhabi talks aimed at ending the conflict.

The Attack

Russian forces struck a civilian market in Ukraine, killing at least seven people. The attack targeted a populated civilian area, continuing a pattern of strikes on Ukrainian infrastructure and civilian locations.

Diplomatic Impact

The deadly shelling casts a shadow over ongoing peace negotiations in Abu Dhabi, where international mediators have been working to find a resolution to the conflict that began with Russia's full-scale invasion in February 2022.

The attack underscores the challenges facing diplomatic efforts as military operations continue despite negotiation attempts.

Ongoing Conflict

This attack is part of continued Russian military operations targeting Ukrainian cities and infrastructure. The war has resulted in thousands of civilian casualties and displaced millions of Ukrainians.
