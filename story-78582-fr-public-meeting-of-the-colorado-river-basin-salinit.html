BREAKING: Federal Register: Public Meeting of the Colorado River Basin Salinity Control Advisory Council

Published: 2023-10-02 | Source: Federal Register

The Bureau of Reclamation is publishing this notice to announce that a Federal Advisory Committee meeting of the Colorado River Basin Salinity Control Advisory Council (Council) will take place. This meeting is open to the public.
