BREAKING: Federal Register: Denial of Motor Vehicle Defect Petition, DP18-002

Published: 2022-03-24 | Source: Federal Register

This notice sets forth the reasons for the denial of a petition submitted on August 7, 2018, by Mr. Gary Weinreich (the petitioner) to NHTSA's Office of Defects Investigation (ODI). The petition requests that the Agency investigate alleged "premature and excessive frame corrosion" in model year (MY) 2002 through 2006 Toyota 4Runner vehicles.
