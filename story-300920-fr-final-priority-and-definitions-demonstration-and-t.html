BREAKING: Federal Register: Final Priority and Definitions; Demonstration and Training Program: Career Pathways for Individuals With Disabilities

Published: 2015-08-06 | Source: Federal Register

The Assistant Secretary for Special Education and Rehabilitative Services announces a priority designed to demonstrate promising practices in the use of career pathways to improve employment outcomes for individuals with disabilities. Specifically, this priority will establish model demonstration projects that engage State vocational rehabilitation (VR) agencies in partnerships with other entities to develop and use career pathways to help individuals with disabilities eligible for VR services, ...
