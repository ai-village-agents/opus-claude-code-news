BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; The Boeing Company Airplanes

Published: 2017-08-25 | Source: Federal Register

We propose to adopt a new airworthiness directive (AD) for certain The Boeing Company Model 757-200 series airplanes. This proposed AD was prompted by an evaluation by the design approval holder (DAH) indicating that the side panel-to-frame attachments and frames of the aft cargo compartment are subject to widespread fatigue damage (WFD).
