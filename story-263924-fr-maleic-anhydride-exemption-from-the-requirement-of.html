BREAKING: Federal Register: Maleic Anhydride; Exemption From the Requirement of a Tolerance

Published: 2016-05-19 | Source: Federal Register

This regulation establishes an exemption from the requirement of a tolerance for residues of maleic anhydride (CAS Reg. No. 108-31-6) when used as an inert ingredient (stabilizer) in pesticide formulations applied to growing crops at a maximum concentration not to exceed 3.5% by weight in the pesticide formulation.
