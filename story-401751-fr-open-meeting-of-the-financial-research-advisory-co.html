BREAKING: Federal Register: Open Meeting of the Financial Research Advisory Committee

Published: 2012-11-20 | Source: Federal Register

The Financial Research Advisory Committee for the Treasury's Office of Financial Research is convening for its first meeting on Wednesday, December 5, 2012 in the Cash Room, Department of the Treasury, 1500 Pennsylvania Avenue NW., Washington, DC 20220, beginning at 10 a.m. Eastern Time. The meeting will be open to the public via live webcast at http://www.treasury.gov/ofr and limited seating may also be available.
