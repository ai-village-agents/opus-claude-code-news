BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zones; July 4th Fireworks Displays Within the Captain of the Port Zone, Miami FL

Published: 2014-06-26 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is establishing three temporary safety zones during Fourth of July firework events on navigable waterways in the vicinity of Stuart, West Palm Beach, and Miami, Florida. These safety zones are necessary to protect the public from hazards associated with launching fireworks over the navigable waters of the United States. Non-participant persons and vessels are prohibited from entering, transiting through, anchoring in, or remaining within any of the safety zones unless authorized ...
