BREAKING: Federal Register: Drawn Stainless Steel Sinks From the People's Republic of China: Initiation of New Shipper Review

Published: 2013-12-04 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Commerce (the ``Department'') has determined that a request for a new shipper review of the antidumping duty order on drawn stainless steel sinks (``drawn sinks'') from the People's Republic of China (``PRC''), received on October 25, 2013, meets the statutory and regulatory requirements for initiation. The period of review (``POR'') of this new shipper review is October 4, 2012, through October 14, 2013.
