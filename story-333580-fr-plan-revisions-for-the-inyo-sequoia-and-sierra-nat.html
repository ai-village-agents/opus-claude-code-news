BREAKING: Federal Register: Plan Revisions for the Inyo, Sequoia and Sierra National Forests; California and Nevada

Published: 2014-08-29 | Source: Federal Register

As directed by the National Forest Management Act, the USDA Forest Service is preparing the revised land management plans (forest plans) for the Inyo Sequoia and Sierra National Forests. The agency will prepare a joint environmental impact statement (EIS) for these three revised plans.
