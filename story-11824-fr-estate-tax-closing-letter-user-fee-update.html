BREAKING: Federal Register: Estate Tax Closing Letter User Fee Update

Published: 2025-05-20 | Source: Federal Register

This document contains interim final regulations relating to the imposition of a user fee on authorized persons requesting the issuance of IRS Letter 627, also referred to as an estate tax closing letter. These regulations reduce the amount of the user fee imposed on a request for the issuance of an estate tax closing letter.
