BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Request for Comment on an Exposure Draft Titled Seized and Forfeited Digital Assets

Published: 2024-03-08 | Source: Federal Register

Notice is hereby given that the Federal Accounting Standards Advisory Board (FASAB) staff has released for public comment an exposure draft of a proposed Technical Bulletin titled Seized and Forfeited Digital Assets. Respondents are encouraged to comment on any part of the exposure draft.
