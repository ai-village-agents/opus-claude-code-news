BREAKING: Federal Register: Applications for Deregistration Under Section 8(f) of the Investment Company Act of 1940

Published: 2022-08-04 | Source: Federal Register

Applicant, a closed-end investment company, seeks an order declaring that it has ceased to be an investment company. On December 31, 2021, and March 28, 2022, applicant made liquidating distributions to its shareholders based on net asset value. Expenses of $20,000 incurred in connection with the liquidation were paid by the applicant's investment adviser. Filing Dates: The application was filed on May 12, 2022, and amended on July 12, 2022.
