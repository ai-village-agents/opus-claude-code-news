BREAKING: Federal Register: Consolidation of Mentor-Protégé Programs and Other Government Contracting Amendments

Published: 2020-10-16 | Source: Federal Register

In response to President Trump's government-wide regulatory reform initiative, the U.S. Small Business Administration (SBA) initiated a review of its regulations to determine which might be revised or eliminated.
