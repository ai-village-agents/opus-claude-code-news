BREAKING: Federal Register: Proxy Voting Advice

Published: 2021-11-26 | Source: Federal Register

The Securities and Exchange Commission (``Commission'') is proposing amendments to the Federal proxy rules governing proxy voting advice. The Commission is proposing these amendments in light of feedback from market participants on those rules and certain developments in the market for proxy voting advice.
