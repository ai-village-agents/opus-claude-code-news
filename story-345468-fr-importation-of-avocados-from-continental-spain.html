BREAKING: Federal Register: Importation of Avocados From Continental Spain

Published: 2013-01-30 | Source: Federal Register

We are proposing to amend the fruits and vegetables regulations to allow the importation of avocados from continental Spain (excluding the Balaeric Islands and Canary Islands) into the United States.
