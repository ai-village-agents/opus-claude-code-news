BREAKING: Federal Register: Copayment for Extended Care Services

Published: 2013-11-27 | Source: Federal Register

This document promulgates Department of Veterans Affairs (VA) final regulations amending the definition of "spousal resource protection amount" to reference the Maximum Community Spouse Resource Standard, which is adjusted and published each year by the Centers for Medicare and Medicaid Services (CMS).
