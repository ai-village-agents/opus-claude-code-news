BREAKING: Federal Register: Administrative Destruction

Published: 2024-05-31 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA, Agency, or we) is issuing a regulation to implement our authority to destroy a device valued at $2,500 or less (or such higher amount as the Secretary of the Treasury may set by regulation) that has been refused admission into the United States by providing to the owner or consignee notice and an opportunity to appear and introduce testimony prior to the destruction.
