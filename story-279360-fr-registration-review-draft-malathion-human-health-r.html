BREAKING: Federal Register: Registration Review; Draft Malathion Human Health Risk Assessment; Extension of Comment Period

Published: 2016-11-18 | Source: Federal Register

EPA issued a notice in the Federal Register of September 22, 2016 (81 FR 65355) (FRL-9952-53), opening a 60-day comment period for the draft malathion human health risk assessment. This document extends that comment period for 30 days. The new closing date will be December 21, 2016 rather than November 21, 2016.
