BREAKING: Federal Register: Announcing the Twenty-Second Public Meeting of the Crash Injury Research and Engineering Network (CIREN)

Published: 2014-08-21 | Source: Federal Register

This notice announces the Twenty-second Public Meeting of members of the Crash Injury Research and Engineering Network. CIREN is a collaborative effort to conduct research on crashes injury causation at six Level I Trauma Centers across the United States. The CIREN process combines prospective data collection with professional multidisciplinary analysis of medical and engineering evidence to determine injury causation in every crash investigation conducted.
