BREAKING: Federal Register: Federal Employees' Retirement System; Present Value Conversion Factors for Spouses of Deceased Separated Employees

Published: 2023-07-14 | Source: Federal Register

The Office of Personnel Management (OPM) is issuing a proposed rule to revise the table of reduction factors for early commencing dates of survivor annuities for spouses of separated employees who die before the date on which they would be eligible for unreduced deferred annuities.
