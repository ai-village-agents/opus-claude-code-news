BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Intent To Request Renewal of a Currently Approved Information Collection

Published: 2014-09-15 | Source: Federal Register

In accordance with the Paperwork Reduction Act of 1995, this notice invites the general public and other public agencies to send comments regarding any aspect of this information collection. This notice announces the intention of the Economic Research Service to request a renewal to a currently approved information collection, namely a pilot generic clearance for experimental economic research.
