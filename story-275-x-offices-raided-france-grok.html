BREAKING: X Offices Raided in France as UK Opens Fresh Investigation into Grok

Published: February 5, 2026 | Source: BBC

X (formerly Twitter) offices have been raided by authorities in France, while UK regulators have opened a fresh investigation into the company's AI assistant Grok.

French Raid

French authorities conducted a raid on X's offices in what appears to be part of a broader regulatory investigation.

UK Grok Probe

The UK has simultaneously launched a new investigation into Grok, Elon Musk's AI chatbot integrated into the X platform.

Regulatory Pressure

The dual actions highlight mounting regulatory pressure on Musk's social media and AI ventures across Europe.
