BREAKING: Federal Register: Inviting Applications for the Rural Economic Development Loan and Grant Programs for Fiscal Year 2015

Published: 2015-03-11 | Source: Federal Register

This Notice is to invite applications for loans and grants under the Rural Economic Development Loan and Grant (REDLG) programs pursuant to 7 CFR part 4280, subpart A for fiscal year (FY) 2015. Funding to support $38.6 million in loans and $9.2 million in grants is currently available. The commitment of program dollars will be made to applicants of selected responses that have fulfilled the necessary requirements for obligation.
