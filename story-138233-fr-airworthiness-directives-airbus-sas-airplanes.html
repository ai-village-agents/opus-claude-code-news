BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Airbus SAS Airplanes

Published: 2021-11-18 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA proposes to supersede Airworthiness Directive (AD) 2019-26-01, which applies to certain Airbus SAS Model A350-941 and - 1041 airplanes. AD 2019-26-01 requires repetitive detailed inspections, and applicable corrective actions, and provides an optional modification that would terminate the inspections.
