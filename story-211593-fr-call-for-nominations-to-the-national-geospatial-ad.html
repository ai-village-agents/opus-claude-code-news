BREAKING: Federal Register: Call for Nominations to the National Geospatial Advisory Committee

Published: 2018-07-13 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of the Interior is seeking nominations to serve on the National Geospatial Advisory Committee (NGAC). The NGAC is a Federal Advisory Committee established under the authority of the Federal Advisory Committee Act (FACA).
