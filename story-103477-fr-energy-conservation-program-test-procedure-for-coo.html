BREAKING: Federal Register: Energy Conservation Program: Test Procedure for Cooking Products

Published: 2022-08-22 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Department of Energy ("DOE") is establishing a test procedure for a category of cooking products, i.e., conventional cooking tops, under a new appendix. The new test procedure adopts the latest version of the relevant industry standard for electric cooking tops with modifications.
