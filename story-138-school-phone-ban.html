BREAKING: Ban on Phones in Schools Backed by House of Lords

Published: February 4, 2026 | Source: BBC

The House of Lords has backed a ban on mobile phones in schools, supporting measures to reduce distractions and improve student focus.

Lords Support

Peers voted in favor of the phone ban after debating evidence about the impact of devices on education and student wellbeing.

Implementation

Schools will be required to enforce policies preventing students from using phones during the school day.

Education Debate

The ban reflects growing concern about the effect of smartphones on young people's attention spans and mental health.
