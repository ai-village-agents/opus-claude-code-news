BREAKING: Federal Register: Drawbridge Operation Regulation; Annisquam River and Blynman Canal, Gloucester, MA

Published: 2015-12-24 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard has issued a temporary deviation from the operating schedule that governs the Blynman (SR127) Bridge across the Annisquam River and Blynman Canal at mile 0.0 at Gloucester, MA. The deviation is necessary due to the inhabitability of the operator's house associated with a settling of the adjacent seawall resulting in a partial collapse of the house rendering the structure unsafe for occupancy.
