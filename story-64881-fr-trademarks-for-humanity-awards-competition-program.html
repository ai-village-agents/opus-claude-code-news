BREAKING: Federal Register: Trademarks for Humanity Awards Competition Program

Published: 2023-04-11 | Source: Federal Register

Pursuant to its statutory authority to conduct intellectual property programs, the United States Patent and Trademark Office (USPTO or Office) is launching a pilot program to promote and incentivize brand owners who offer products and services that help address humanitarian issues utilizing a federally registered trademark.
