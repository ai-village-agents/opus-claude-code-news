BREAKING: Federal Register: COVID-19 Mitigation Policy Requirement in Head Start Programs; Recission

Published: 2025-12-19 | Source: Federal Register

This direct final rule (DFR) removes the requirement that Head Start programs have a COVID-19 mitigation policy. This requirement was included in the final rule titled "Mitigating the Spread of COVID-19 in Head Start Programs," which ACF published on January 6, 2023.
