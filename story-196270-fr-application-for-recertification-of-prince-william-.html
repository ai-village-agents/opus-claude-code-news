BREAKING: Federal Register: Application for Recertification of Prince William Sound Regional Citizens' Advisory Council

Published: 2019-12-26 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard announces the availability of, and seeks comments on, the recertification of the Prince William Sound Regional Citizen's Advisory Council (PWSRCAC) for March 2, 2020 through February 28, 2021. Under the Oil Pollution Act of 1990 (OPA 90), the Coast Guard may certify the PWSRCAC on an annual basis.
