BREAKING: Federal Register: Atlantic Highly Migratory Species; 2013 Atlantic Bluefin Tuna Quota Specifications

Published: 2013-04-11 | Source: Federal Register

NMFS proposes 2013 quota specifications for the Atlantic bluefin tuna (BFT) fishery, and seeks comments from the public on the allocation of available underharvest among the fishery categories under certain circumstances.
