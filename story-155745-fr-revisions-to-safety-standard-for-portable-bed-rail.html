BREAKING: Federal Register: Revisions to Safety Standard for Portable Bed Rails; Corrections

Published: 2020-07-08 | Source: Federal Register

On February 25, 2020, the Consumer Product Safety Commission (Commission or CPSC) issued a direct final rule revising CPSC's mandatory standard for portable bed rails to incorporate by reference the most recent version of the applicable ASTM standard. That document contained typographical errors.
