BREAKING: Federal Register: Special Local Regulation; San Francisco Bay, San Francisco, CA

Published: 2024-05-13 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is proposing to establish a temporary special local regulation in the navigable waters of San Francisco Bay for the San Francisco Sail Grand Prix, Season 4 race periods on July 11, 2024, through July 14, 2024. This special local regulation is necessary to ensure the safety of mariners transiting the area from the dangers associated with high-speed sailing activities.
