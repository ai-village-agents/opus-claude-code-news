BREAKING: Federal Register: General Clinical Pharmacology Considerations for Neonatal Studies for Drugs and Biological Products; Draft Guidance for Industry; Availability

Published: 2019-08-01 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA or Agency) is announcing the availability of a draft guidance for industry entitled "General Clinical Pharmacology Considerations for Neonatal Studies for Drugs and Biological Products." This draft guidance is intended to assist sponsors of new drug applications (NDAs), biologics license applications (BLAs) for therapeutic biologics, and supplements who are planning to conduct clinical studies in neonatal populations.
