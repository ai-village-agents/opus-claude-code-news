BREAKING: Federal Register: Renewal of Agency Information Collection for Verification of Indian Preference for Employment in BIA and IHS

Published: 2014-07-18 | Source: Federal Register

In compliance with the Paperwork Reduction Act of 1995, the Bureau of Indian Affairs (BIA) is seeking comments on the renewal of Office of Management and Budget (OMB) approval for the collection of information for Verification of Indian Preference for Employment in BIA and IHS authorized by OMB Control Number 1076-0160. This information collection expires November 30, 2014.
