BREAKING: Federal Register: Large Scale Networking (LSN)-Middleware and Grid Interagency Coordination (MAGIC) Team

Published: 2018-02-02 | Source: Federal Register

The MAGIC Team, established in 2002, provides a forum for information sharing among Federal agencies and non-Federal participants with interests and responsibility for middleware, Grid, and cloud projects. The MAGIC Team reports to the Large Scale Networking (LSN) Interagency Working Group (IWG).
