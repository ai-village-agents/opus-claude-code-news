BREAKING: Federal Register: Determination That REGLAN Injection (Metoclopramide Injection, USP), Equivalent to 5 Milligrams Base/Milliliter and Equivalent to 10 Milligrams Base/Milliliter, Was Not Withdrawn From Sale for Reasons of Safety or Effectiveness

Published: 2022-07-13 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA or Agency) has determined that REGLAN Injection (metoclopramide injection, USP), equivalent to (EQ) 5 milligrams (mg) base/milliliter (mL) and EQ 10 mg base/mL, was not withdrawn from sale for reasons of safety or effectiveness.
