BREAKING: Federal Register: Osteoarthritis: Structural Endpoints for the Development of Drugs, Devices, and Biological Products for Treatment; Draft Guidance for Industry; Availability

Published: 2018-08-23 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA or Agency) is announcing the availability of a draft guidance for industry entitled "Osteoarthritis: Structural Endpoints for the Development of Drugs, Devices, and Biological Products for Treatment." The purpose of this draft guidance is to assist sponsors who are developing drugs, devices, or biological products to treat the underlying pathophysiology and structural progression of osteoarthritis (OA).
