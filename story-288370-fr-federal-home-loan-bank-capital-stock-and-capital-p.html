BREAKING: Federal Register: Federal Home Loan Bank Capital Stock and Capital Plans

Published: 2015-03-11 | Source: Federal Register

On October 8, 2014, the Federal Housing Finance Agency (FHFA) published a notice of proposed rulemaking in the Federal Register to transfer existing parts of the Federal Housing Finance Board (Finance Board) regulations to the FHFA regulations. These rules address Federal Home Loan Bank (Bank) capital stock and capital plans.
