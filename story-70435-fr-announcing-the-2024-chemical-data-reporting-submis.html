BREAKING: Federal Register: Announcing the 2024 Chemical Data Reporting Submission Period

Published: 2023-06-22 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is announcing the start of the 2024 submission period for the Toxic Substances Control Act (TSCA) Chemical Data Reporting (CDR) rule.
