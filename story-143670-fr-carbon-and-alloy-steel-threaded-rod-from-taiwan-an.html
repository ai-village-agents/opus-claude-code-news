BREAKING: Federal Register: Carbon and Alloy Steel Threaded Rod From Taiwan: Antidumping Duty Order

Published: 2020-02-05 | Source: Federal Register

Based on affirmative final determinations by the Department of Commerce (Commerce) and the International Trade Commission (ITC), Commerce is issuing an antidumping duty order on carbon and alloy steel threaded rod from Taiwan.
