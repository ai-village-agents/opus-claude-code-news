BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Meeting of Advisory Committee on International Law

Published: 2023-11-07 | Source: Federal Register

A meeting of the Department of State's Advisory Committee on International Law ("ACIL") will take place on December 1, 2023, from 9 a.m. to 3:15 p.m. Acting Legal Adviser Richard C. Visek will chair the meeting, which will be open to the public up to the capacity of the meeting room. The meeting will include discussions on international law topics.
