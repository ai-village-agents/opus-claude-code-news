BREAKING: Federal Register: Public Availability of the U.S. International Trade Commission's FY 2015 Service Contract Inventory

Published: 2016-04-20 | Source: Federal Register

In accordance with Section 743 of Division C of the Consolidated Appropriations Act of 2010 (Pub. L. 111-117), the U.S. International Trade Commission is publishing this notice to advise the public of the availability of the FY 2015 Service Contract Inventory. The USITC has posted its inventory and a summary of the inventory on USITC's Web site at the following link: http://www.usitc.gov/ procurement.htm.
