BREAKING: Federal Register: Pilot Program for the Temporary Exchange of Information Technology Personnel

Published: 2014-05-14 | Source: Federal Register

This part assigns responsibilities and provides procedures for implementing a Pilot Program for the Temporary Exchange of Information Technology Personnel, known as the Information Technology Exchange Program pilot. This Pilot is envisioned to promote the interchange of DoD and private sector IT professionals to enhance skills and competencies.
