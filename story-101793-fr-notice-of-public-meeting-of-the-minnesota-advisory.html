BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Public Meeting of the Minnesota Advisory Committee; Cancellation

Published: 2022-08-01 | Source: Federal Register

The Commission on Civil Rights published a notice in the Federal Register concerning a meeting of the Minnesota Advisory Committee. The meeting scheduled for Thursday, July 28, 2022, at 11 a.m. (CT) is cancelled. The notice is in the Federal Register of Monday, June 13, 2022, in FR Doc. 2022-12597, in the first and second columns of page 35723.
