BREAKING: Federal Register: Air Plan Approval; Arizona; Regional Haze Progress Report

Published: 2019-03-27 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is proposing to approve Arizona's Regional Haze Progress Report ("Progress Report" or "Report"), submitted by the State of Arizona on November 12, 2015, as a revision to its state implementation plan (SIP). Arizona submitted its Progress Report and a negative declaration stating that further revision of the existing regional haze implementation plan is not needed at this time.
