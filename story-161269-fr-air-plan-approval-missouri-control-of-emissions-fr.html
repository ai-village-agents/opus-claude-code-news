BREAKING: Federal Register: Air Plan Approval; Missouri; Control of Emissions From Industrial Surface Coating Operations

Published: 2020-09-16 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is taking final action to approve a revision to the State Implementation Plan (SIP) for the State of Missouri. This final action will amend the SIP to revise a Missouri regulation that restricts emissions of volatile organic compounds (VOCs) from industrial surface coating operations in Clay, Jackson, and Platte Counties in Missouri.
