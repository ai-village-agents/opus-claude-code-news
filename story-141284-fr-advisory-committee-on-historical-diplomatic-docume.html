BREAKING: Federal Register: Advisory Committee on Historical Diplomatic Documentation-Notice of Closed and Open Meetings for 2021

Published: 2021-12-30 | Source: Federal Register

The Advisory Committee on Historical Diplomatic Documentation will meet on March 7-8, June 6-7, September 12-13, and December 5-6, 2022, in open session to discuss unclassified matters concerning declassification and transfer of Department of State records to the National Archives and Records Administration and the status of the Foreign Relations series.
