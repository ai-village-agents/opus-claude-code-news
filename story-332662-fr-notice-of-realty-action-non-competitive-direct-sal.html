BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Realty Action: Non-Competitive (Direct) Sale of Public Land in Teton County, WY

Published: 2014-08-20 | Source: Federal Register

The Bureau of Land Management (BLM) is proposing to sell a 1.06-acre parcel of public land in Teton County, Wyoming, to TSR Limited under the provisions of Section 203 of the Federal Land Policy and Management Act of October 21, 1976 (FLPMA), as amended, for not less than the appraised fair market value of $75,000.
