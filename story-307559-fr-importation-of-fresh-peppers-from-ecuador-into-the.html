BREAKING: Federal Register: Importation of Fresh Peppers From Ecuador Into the United States

Published: 2015-10-23 | Source: Federal Register

We are amending the fruits and vegetables regulations to allow the importation of fresh peppers into the United States from Ecuador. As a condition of entry, the fruit will have to be produced in accordance with a systems approach that includes requirements for fruit fly trapping, pre-harvest inspections, production sites, and packinghouse procedures designed to exclude quarantine pests.
