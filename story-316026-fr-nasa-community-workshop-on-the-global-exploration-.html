BREAKING: Federal Register: NASA Community Workshop on the Global Exploration Roadmap

Published: 2014-02-07 | Source: Federal Register

The National Aeronautics and Space Administration announces that the agency will conduct a public workshop to involve the broader community in work related to the second iteration of the Global Exploration Roadmap, released in August 2013.
