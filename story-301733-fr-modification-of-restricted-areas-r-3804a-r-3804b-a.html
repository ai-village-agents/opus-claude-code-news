BREAKING: Federal Register: Modification of Restricted Areas R-3804A, R-3804B, and R-3804C; Fort Polk, LA

Published: 2015-08-17 | Source: Federal Register

This action expands the lateral boundary of restricted area R- 3804B, Fort Polk, LA, and raises the restricted area ceiling to, but not including 10,000 feet mean sea level (MSL). The expanded restricted area airspace will contain new live fire ranges to support mission requirements of the U.S. Army in order to fully exploit the capabilities of modern weapons systems and complex training scenarios that replicate conditions encountered during military deployments today.
