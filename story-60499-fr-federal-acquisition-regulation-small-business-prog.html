BREAKING: Federal Register: Federal Acquisition Regulation: Small Business Program Amendments

Published: 2023-02-14 | Source: Federal Register

DoD, GSA, and NASA are issuing a final rule amending the Federal Acquisition Regulation (FAR) to implement several changes made to the Small Business Administration (SBA) regulations.

Agency: Defense Department, General Services Administration, National Aeronautics and Space Administration
