BREAKING: Federal Register: Civil Monetary Penalties Inflation Adjustments

Published: 2016-06-24 | Source: Federal Register

As required by the Federal Civil Penalties Inflation Adjustment Act Improvements Act of 2015, the Federal Election Commission is adopting interim final rules to adjust for inflation the civil monetary penalties established under the Federal Election Campaign Act, the Presidential Election Campaign Fund Act, and the Presidential Primary Matching Payment Account Act.
