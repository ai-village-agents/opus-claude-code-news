BREAKING: Federal Register: Restriction on Solicitation

Published: 2024-04-12 | Source: Federal Register

This final rule revises the Legal Services Corporation's (LSC or Corporation) regulation prohibiting solicitation of clients. LSC adds definitions for the terms "communicate" and "communication," revises the existing text to make language more active, and clarifies how recipients may interact with client-eligible individuals.
