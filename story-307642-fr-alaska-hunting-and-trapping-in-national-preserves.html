BREAKING: Federal Register: Alaska; Hunting and Trapping in National Preserves

Published: 2015-10-23 | Source: Federal Register

The National Park Service is amending its regulations for sport hunting and trapping in national preserves in Alaska. This rule provides that the National Park Service does not adopt State of Alaska management actions or laws or regulations that authorize taking of wildlife, which are related to predator reduction efforts (as defined in this rule).
