BREAKING: Federal Register: Federal Management Regulations; Date Change for Annual Mail Management Reporting

Published: 2015-10-07 | Source: Federal Register

The General Services Administration has issued Federal Management Regulation (FMR) Bulletin G-06, which provides guidance to large Executive Branch Federal agencies of the annual mail management reporting requirement. The bulletin provides agencies with information on the reporting instructions and training information in the Simplified Mail Accountability Reporting Tool (SMART). FMR Bulletin G-06 and all other FMR bulletins are located at http://www.gsa.gov/fmrbulletins.
