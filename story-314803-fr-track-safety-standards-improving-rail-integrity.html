BREAKING: Federal Register: Track Safety Standards; Improving Rail Integrity

Published: 2014-01-24 | Source: Federal Register

FRA is amending the Federal Track Safety Standards to promote the safety of railroad operations by enhancing rail flaw detection processes. In particular, FRA is establishing minimum qualification requirements for rail flaw detection equipment operators, as well as revising requirements for effective rail inspection frequencies, rail flaw remedial actions, and rail inspection records.
