BREAKING: Federal Register: Addition of Certain Entities; and Modification of Entry on the Entity List

Published: 2018-08-01 | Source: Federal Register

This rule amends the Export Administration Regulations (EAR) by adding forty-four entities (eight entities and thirty-six subordinate institutions) to the Entity List. The entities that are being added to the Entity List have been determined by the U.S. Government to be acting contrary to the national security or foreign policy interests of the United States.
