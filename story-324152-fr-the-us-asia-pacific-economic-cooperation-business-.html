BREAKING: Federal Register: The U.S. Asia-Pacific Economic Cooperation Business Travel Card Program

Published: 2014-05-13 | Source: Federal Register

This interim final rule establishes the U.S. Asia-Pacific Economic Cooperation (APEC) Business Travel Card Program. APEC is an economic forum comprised of twenty-one members, including the United States, whose primary goal is to support sustainable economic growth and prosperity in the Asia-Pacific region.
