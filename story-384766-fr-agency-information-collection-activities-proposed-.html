BREAKING: Federal Register: Agency Information Collection Activities; Proposed Collection; Comment Request; Notification Requirements for Coal and Woodburning Appliances

Published: 2012-05-03 | Source: Federal Register

The information collection requirements in a Consumer Product Safety Commission ("CPSC" or "Commission") coal and woodburning appliance rule have been approved by the Office of Management and Budget ("OMB") under OMB control number 3041-0040. As required by the Paperwork Reduction Act of 1995 (44 U.S.C. Chapter 35), the Commission now requests comments on a proposed extension of approval of those information collection requirements for a period of three years from the date of approval by the OMB...
