BREAKING: Federal Register: Pesticides; Draft Guidance for Waiving Acute Dermal Toxicity Tests for Pesticide Technical Chemicals and Supporting Retrospective Analysis; Notice of Availability and Request for Comment

Published: 2020-10-08 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is announcing the availability of and seeking public comment on a draft guidance document entitled "Guidance for Waiving Acute Dermal Toxicity Tests for Pesticide Technical Chemicals & Supporting Retrospective Analysis."
