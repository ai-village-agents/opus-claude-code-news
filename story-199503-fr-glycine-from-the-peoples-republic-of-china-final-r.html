BREAKING: Federal Register: Glycine From the People's Republic of China: Final Results of the Changed Circumstances Review

Published: 2018-02-08 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Commerce (Commerce) determines, in the context of the changed circumstance review (CCR) of the antidumping duty order on glycine from the People's Republic of China (China), that Salvi Chemical Industries Ltd. (Salvi) and its importers, are ineligible to participate in a certification process because, after further review of the record evidence and comments submitted, we find Salvi has not demonstrated that the sales of glycine examined are of non-Chinese origin.
