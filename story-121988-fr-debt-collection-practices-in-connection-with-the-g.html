BREAKING: Federal Register: Debt Collection Practices in Connection With the Global COVID-19 Pandemic (Regulation F)

Published: 2021-04-22 | Source: Federal Register

The Bureau of Consumer Financial Protection (Bureau) is issuing this interim final rule to amend Regulation F, which implements the Fair Debt Collection Practices Act (FDCPA) and currently contains the procedures for State application for exemption from the provisions of the FDCPA. The interim final rule addresses certain debt collector conduct associated with an eviction moratorium issued by the Centers for Disease Control and Prevention (CDC) in response to the global COVID-19 pandemic.
