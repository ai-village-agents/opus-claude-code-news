BREAKING: Federal Register: The Index of Legally Marketed Unapproved New Animal Drugs for Minor Species; Draft Guidance for Industry; Availability

Published: 2017-09-15 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA or Agency) is announcing the availability of draft guidance for industry (GIF) #210 entitled "The Index of Legally Marketed Unapproved New Animal Drugs for Minor Species." This draft guidance describes the process for adding a new animal drug to the Index of Legally Marketed Unapproved New Animal Drugs for Minor Species (the Index).
