BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Intent To Repatriate Cultural Items: Denver Museum of Nature & Science, Denver, CO

Published: 2021-11-04 | Source: Federal Register

The Denver Museum of Nature & Science, in consultation with the appropriate Indian Tribes or Native Hawaiian organizations, has determined that the cultural item listed in this notice meets the definition of a sacred object and object of cultural patrimony. Lineal descendants or representatives of any Indian Tribe or Native Hawaiian organization not identified in this notice that wish to claim this cultural item should submit a written request to the Denver Museum of Nature & Science.
