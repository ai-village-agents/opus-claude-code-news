BREAKING: Federal Register: Call for Nominations for the Rio Grande Natural Area Commission, Colorado

Published: 2013-07-16 | Source: Federal Register

The Secretary of the Interior (Secretary) was directed by the Rio Grande Natural Area Act of 2006 to establish the Rio Grande Natural Area Commission (Commission). The nine-member Commission was formed in February 2011 to advise the Secretary with respect to the Rio Grande Natural Area (Natural Area) and to prepare a management plan relating to non-Federal land in the Natural Area.
