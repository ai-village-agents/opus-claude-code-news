BREAKING: Federal Register: Announcement of Grant Application Deadlines and Funding Levels

Published: 2016-12-07 | Source: Federal Register

The Rural Utilities Service (RUS) announces its Household Water Well System (HWWS) Grant Program application window for fiscal year (FY) 2017. RUS will make grants to qualified private non-profit organizations to establish lending programs for homeowners to borrow up to $11,000 to construct or repair household water wells for an existing home.
