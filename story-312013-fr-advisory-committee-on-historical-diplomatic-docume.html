BREAKING: Federal Register: Advisory Committee on Historical Diplomatic Documentation-Notice of Closed and Open Meetings for 2016

Published: 2015-12-18 | Source: Federal Register

The Advisory Committee on Historical Diplomatic Documentation will meet on March 7, June 6, August 29, and December 12, 2016, in open session to discuss unclassified matters concerning declassification and transfer of Department of State records to the National Archives and Records Administration and the status of the Foreign Relations series.
