BREAKING: Federal Register: Energy Conservation Program: Test Procedures and Energy Conservation Standards for Consumer Products; Consumer Air Cleaners; Reopening of Comment Period

Published: 2022-03-01 | Source: Federal Register

On January 25, 2022, the U.S. Department of Energy (DOE or the Department) published in the Federal Register a request for information (RFI) regarding Test Procedures and Energy Conservation Standards for Consumer Air Cleaners.
