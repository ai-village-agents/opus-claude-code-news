BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Intent To Suspend Certain Pesticide Registrations

Published: 2014-08-20 | Source: Federal Register

This notice, pursuant the Federal Insecticide, Fungicide and Rodenticide Act (FIFRA), publishes a Notice of Intent to Suspend (NOITS) certain pesticide registrations issued by EPA.
