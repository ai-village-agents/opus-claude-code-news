BREAKING: Federal Register: Advisory Committee on the Medical Uses of Isotopes: Meeting Notice

Published: 2018-06-07 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Nuclear Regulatory Commission (NRC) will convene a teleconference meeting of the Advisory Committee on the Medical Uses of Isotopes (ACMUI) on July 16, 2018, to discuss the draft report of the ACMUI Subcommittee on Training and Experience Requirements for All Modalities.
