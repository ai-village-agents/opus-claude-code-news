BREAKING: Federal Register: United States Standards for Beans

Published: 2017-05-15 | Source: Federal Register

The United States Department of Agriculture's (USDA) Grain Inspection, Packers and Stockyards Administration (GIPSA) is proposing a revision to the United States Standards for Beans to (1) establish a class and grade requirement chart for "chickpeas," also known as "garbanzo beans," and (2) establish a new grade determining factor, definition, factor limits, and visual reference image for "contrasting chickpeas."
