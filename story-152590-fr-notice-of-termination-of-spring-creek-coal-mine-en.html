BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Termination of Spring Creek Coal Mine Environmental Impact Statement, Big Horn County, MT

Published: 2020-05-28 | Source: Federal Register

The preparation of an Environmental Impact Statement (EIS) for Spring Creek Coal Mine is no longer necessary and the process is hereby terminated.

Agency: Interior Department, Land Management Bureau
