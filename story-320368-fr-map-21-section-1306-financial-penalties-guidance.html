BREAKING: Federal Register: MAP-21 Section 1306 Financial Penalties Guidance

Published: 2014-03-31 | Source: Federal Register

The Federal Highway Administration (FHWA) and the Federal Transit Administration (FTA) are issuing joint guidance on the implementation of the financial penalty provisions contained in Section 1306 of the Moving Ahead for Progress in the 21st Century Act (MAP-21).
