BREAKING: Federal Register: A Risk-Based Approach To Monitoring of Clinical Investigations: Questions and Answers; Draft Guidance for Industry; Availability

Published: 2019-03-15 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA or Agency) is announcing the availability of a draft guidance for industry entitled "A Risk- Based Approach to Monitoring of Clinical Investigations: Questions and Answers." The draft guidance provides information to sponsors on risk- based approaches to monitoring of investigational studies of human drug and biological products, medical devices, and combinations thereof.
