BREAKING: Federal Register: Reopening of Comment Period for Universal Proxy

Published: 2021-05-06 | Source: Federal Register

The Securities and Exchange Commission ("Commission") is reopening the comment period for its proposal to require the use of universal proxy cards in all non-exempt solicitations in connection with contested elections of directors ("Proposed Rules"). The Proposed Rules were set forth in a release published in the Federal Register on November 10, 2016 (Release No. 34-79164) ("2016 Release"), and the related comment period ended on January 9, 2017.
