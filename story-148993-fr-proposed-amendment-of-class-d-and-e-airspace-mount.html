BREAKING: Federal Register: Proposed Amendment of Class D and E Airspace; Mountain Home, ID

Published: 2020-04-13 | Source: Federal Register

This action proposes to amend the Class D airspace at the Mountain Home Air Force Base Airport by removing the extensions to the northwest and southeast of the airport. This action also proposes to amend the Class E surface area to match the new dimensions of the Class D.
