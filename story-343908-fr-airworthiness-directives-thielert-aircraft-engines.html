BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Thielert Aircraft Engines GmbH Reciprocating Engines

Published: 2013-01-09 | Source: Federal Register

We are superseding an existing airworthiness directive (AD) for all Thielert Aircraft Engines GmbH models TAE 125-01, TAE 125-02- 99, and TAE 125-02-114 reciprocating engines. That AD currently requires installation of full-authority digital electronic control (FADEC) software version 2.91. This new AD requires removing all software mapping versions prior to 292, 301, or 302, applicable to the TAE engine model.
