BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Virtual Public and Tribal Meetings Regarding the U.S. Army Corps of Engineers Civil Works Environmental Justice Strategic Plan and Vision, Establishment of a Public Docket, Request for Input

Published: 2024-09-12 | Source: Federal Register

Pursuant to the Executive order, "Revitalizing our Nation's Commitment to Environmental Justice for All", the U.S. Army Corps of Engineers (Corps) is preparing its Environmental Justice Strategic Plan for the Civil Works programs. As part of that effort, the Corps is soliciting feedback on its Draft Environmental Justice Strategic Plan vision, goals, and objectives.
