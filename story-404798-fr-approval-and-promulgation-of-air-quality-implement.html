BREAKING: Federal Register: Approval and Promulgation of Air Quality Implementation Plans; Idaho; Update to Materials Incorporated by Reference

Published: 2012-12-28 | Source: Federal Register

EPA is updating the materials submitted by Idaho that are incorporated by reference (IBR) into the Idaho State Implementation Plan (SIP). The regulations affected by this update have been previously submitted by the Idaho Department of Environmental Quality (IDEQ) and approved by EPA.
