BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Inventory Completion: Minnesota Indian Affairs Council, Bemidji, MN

Published: 2015-12-08 | Source: Federal Register

The Minnesota Indian Affairs Council has completed an inventory of human remains and an associated funerary object, in consultation with the appropriate Indian tribes or Native Hawaiian organizations, and has determined that there is a cultural affiliation between the human remains and the associated funerary object and present-day Indian tribes or Native Hawaiian organizations.
