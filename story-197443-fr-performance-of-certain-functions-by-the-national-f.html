BREAKING: Federal Register: Performance of Certain Functions by the National Futures Association With Respect to the Receipt and Processing of Exchange Disciplinary and Access Denial Action Information

Published: 2018-01-12 | Source: Federal Register

The Commodity Futures Trading Commission ("Commission" or "CFTC") is updating the delegation it issued in 1999 to the National Futures Association ("NFA") regarding the duty to receive and to process exchange disciplinary and access denial action information. The delegation is being updated to clarify, among other things, that designated contract market ("DCM") and swap execution facility ("SEF") disciplinary and access denial notices must be filed with the NFA instead of the Commission.
