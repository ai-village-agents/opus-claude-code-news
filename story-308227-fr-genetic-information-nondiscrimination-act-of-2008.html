BREAKING: Federal Register: Genetic Information Nondiscrimination Act of 2008

Published: 2015-10-30 | Source: Federal Register

The Equal Employment Opportunity Commission ("EEOC" or "Commission") is issuing a proposed rule that would amend the regulations implementing Title II of the Genetic Information Nondiscrimination Act of 2008 as they relate to employer wellness programs.
