BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Updated Population Estimates for Basic Field-Agricultural Worker Grants

Published: 2022-05-03 | Source: Federal Register

The Legal Services Corporation (LSC) announces revised, updated population estimates of agricultural workers that LSC uses to distribute funds among grants for providing civil legal services to those workers and their dependents.
