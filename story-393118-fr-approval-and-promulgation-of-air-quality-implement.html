BREAKING: Federal Register: Approval and Promulgation of Air Quality Implementation Plans; Wisconsin; Regional Haze

Published: 2012-08-07 | Source: Federal Register

EPA is approving revisions to the Wisconsin State Implementation Plan (SIP) addressing regional haze for the first implementation period, which extends through July 31, 2018. Wisconsin submitted its regional haze plan on January 18, 2012, with a supplemental submittal on June 7, 2012.
