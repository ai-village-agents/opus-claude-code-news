BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Eurocopter France Helicopters

Published: 2013-03-26 | Source: Federal Register

We are adopting a new airworthiness directive (AD) for Eurocopter France (Eurocopter) Model EC130 B4 helicopters with a cabin vibration damper installed. This AD requires installing a vibration damper casing assembly on both sides of the helicopter. This AD was prompted by a crack and failure of a cabin vibration damper blade.
