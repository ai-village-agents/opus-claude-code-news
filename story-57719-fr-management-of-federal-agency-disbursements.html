BREAKING: Federal Register: Management of Federal Agency Disbursements

Published: 2023-01-10 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of the Treasury's (Treasury) Bureau of the Fiscal Service ("Fiscal Service" or "we"), is proposing to amend its regulation that implements a statutory mandate requiring the Federal Government to deliver non-tax payments by electronic funds transfer (EFT) unless a waiver is available.
