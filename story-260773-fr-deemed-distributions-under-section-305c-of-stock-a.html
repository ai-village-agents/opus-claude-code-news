BREAKING: Federal Register: Deemed Distributions Under Section 305(c) of Stock and Rights to Acquire Stock

Published: 2016-04-13 | Source: Federal Register

This document contains proposed regulations regarding deemed distributions of stock and rights to acquire stock. The proposed regulations would resolve ambiguities concerning the amount and timing of deemed distributions that are or result from adjustments to rights to acquire stock.
