BREAKING: Federal Register: Pearson Field Airport Special Flight Rules Area

Published: 2015-10-06 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA is proposing to establish a Special Flight Rules Area in the vicinity of Pearson Field Airport, Vancouver, Washington. Pearson Field Airport is located approximately three nautical miles northwest of Portland International Airport, Portland, Oregon.
