BREAKING: Federal Register: Debt Collection Practices (Regulation F); Pay-to-Pay Fees

Published: 2022-07-05 | Source: Federal Register

Section 808(1) of the Fair Debt Collection Practices Act (FDCPA or Act) prohibits debt collectors from collecting any amount (including any interest, fee, charge, or expense incidental to the principal obligation) unless that amount is expressly authorized by the agreement creating the debt or permitted by law.
