BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zones; San Francisco, CA

Published: 2017-01-18 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard proposes to amend, add, and delete several permanent safety zones located in the Captain of the Port San Francisco zone that are established to protect public safety during annual firework displays. These amendments will update listed events to accurately reflect the firework display locations.
