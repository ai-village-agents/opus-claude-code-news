BREAKING: Federal Register: 4.9 GHz Band

Published: 2018-05-07 | Source: Federal Register

In 2002, the Commission allocated the 4940-4990 MHz (4.9 GHz) band for fixed and mobile use and designated the band for public safety broadband communications. Since then, the band has experienced relatively light usage compared to the heavy use of other public safety bands.
