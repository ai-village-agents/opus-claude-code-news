BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Availability of Draft FAA Order 8000.95D, Change 1, Regarding Individual Designee Management Policy

Published: 2025-11-24 | Source: Federal Register

Draft FAA Order 8000.95D, Change 1, Designee Management Policy (Draft Change 1) would transition from the Designee Registration System to the Designee Management System (DMS) and would align the Order 8000.95 with the DMS tool workflows. The draft would revise several procedural descriptions to better match DMS workflows, including updates to digital signatures and automation for registering, enrolling, tracking, and recording designee training completions.
