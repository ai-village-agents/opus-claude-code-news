BREAKING: Federal Register: Special Conditions: Dassault Aviation Model Falcon 6X Airplane; Electronic Flight-Control System and Control Surface Position Awareness

Published: 2023-05-19 | Source: Federal Register

These special conditions are issued for the Dassault Aviation (Dassault) Model Falcon 6X airplane. This airplane will have a novel or unusual design feature when compared to the state of technology envisioned in the airworthiness standards for transport category airplanes. This design feature is an electronic flight-control system requiring flight-control surface position awareness.
