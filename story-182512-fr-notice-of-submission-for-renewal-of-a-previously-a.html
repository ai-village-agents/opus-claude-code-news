BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Submission for Renewal of a Previously Approved Information Collection: Interview Survey Form, INV 10

Published: 2019-06-25 | Source: Federal Register

The National Background Investigation Bureau (NBIB), Office of Personnel Management (OPM) is notifying the general public and other federal agencies that OPM is seeking Office of Management and Budget (OMB) approval of a revised information collection control number 3206- 0106, Interview Survey Form, INV 10.
