BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Proposed New Fee Site; Federal Lands Recreation Enhancement Act (Title VIII. Pub. L. 108-447)

Published: 2016-12-13 | Source: Federal Register

The Manti-La Sal National Forest is proposing to charge a fee at the Mammoth Administrative Site. Mammoth and Lake Cabins would be available June 5 to September 30 at $50.00 per night each. Either one or both cabins could be rented, but if both are rented, they must be to the same customer. Fees are assessed based on the level of amenities and services provided, cost of operations and maintenance, and market assessment.
