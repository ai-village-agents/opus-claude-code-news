BREAKING: Federal Register: Air Plan Approval; Illinois; Volatile Organic Compounds Definition

Published: 2016-12-28 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is proposing to approve a revision to the Illinois State Implementation Plan (SIP). The revision amends the Illinois Administrative Code by updating the definition of volatile organic material or volatile organic compounds to exclude 2-amino-2-methyl-1-propanol.
