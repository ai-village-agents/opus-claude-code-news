BREAKING: Federal Register: Endangered and Threatened Wildlife and Plants; 12-Month Finding for the Sonoran Desert Tortoise

Published: 2022-02-08 | Source: Federal Register

We, the U.S. Fish and Wildlife Service (Service), announce a 12-month finding on a petition to list the Sonoran desert tortoise (Gopherus morafkai) as an endangered or threatened species under the Endangered Species Act of 1973, as amended (Act). After a thorough review of the best available scientific and commercial information, we find that it is not warranted at this time to list the Sonoran desert tortoise.
