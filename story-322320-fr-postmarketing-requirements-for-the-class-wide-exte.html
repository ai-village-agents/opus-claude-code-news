BREAKING: Federal Register: Postmarketing Requirements for the Class-Wide Extended-Release/Long-Acting Opioid Analgesics; Public Meeting; Request for Comments

Published: 2014-04-22 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA) is announcing a public meeting to obtain stakeholder input on the design and conduct of the postmarketing requirements (PMRs) for the class-wide extended-release/ long-acting (ER/LA) opioid analgesic drug products to further assess the serious risks of misuse, abuse, hyperalgesia, addiction, overdose, and death associated with their long-term use.
