BREAKING: Federal Register: Domestic Requests for Broadcasting Board of Governors Program Materials

Published: 2013-11-08 | Source: Federal Register

The Broadcasting Board of Governors ("BBG") finalizes an interim final rule, published in the Federal Register on July 2, 2013. The interim final rule established procedures for the BBG to respond to domestic requests from members of the public, organizations, and media, for program materials disseminated by BBG abroad.
