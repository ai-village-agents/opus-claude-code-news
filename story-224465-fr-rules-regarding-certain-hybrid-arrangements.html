BREAKING: Federal Register: Rules Regarding Certain Hybrid Arrangements

Published: 2018-12-28 | Source: Federal Register

This document contains proposed regulations implementing sections 245A(e) and 267A of the Internal Revenue Code ("Code") regarding hybrid dividends and certain amounts paid or accrued in hybrid transactions or with hybrid entities. Sections 245A(e) and 267A were added to the Code by the Tax Cuts and Jobs Act, Public Law 115-97 (2017) (the "Act"), which was enacted on December 22, 2017.
