BREAKING: Federal Register: Roadless Area Conservation; National Forest System Lands in Colorado

Published: 2016-12-19 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Department of Agriculture (USDA) is reinstating the North Fork Coal Mining Area exception to the Colorado Roadless Rule. The Colorado Roadless Rule is a State-specific rule that establishes management direction for the conservation of roadless area values and characteristics across approximately 4.2 million acres of land located within the State of Colorado in Roadless Areas on National Forest System (NFS) lands.
