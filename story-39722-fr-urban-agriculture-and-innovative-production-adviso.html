BREAKING: Federal Register: Urban Agriculture and Innovative Production Advisory Committee Meeting

Published: 2024-06-18 | Source: Federal Register

The Natural Resources Conservation Service (NRCS) will hold a public meeting of the Urban Agriculture and Innovative Production Advisory Committee (UAIPAC). UAIPAC will convene to discuss proposed recommendations for the Secretary of Agriculture on the development of policies and outreach relating to urban, indoor, and other emerging agriculture production practices.
