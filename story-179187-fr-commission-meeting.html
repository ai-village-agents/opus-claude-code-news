BREAKING: Federal Register: Commission Meeting

Published: 2019-05-15 | Source: Federal Register

The Susquehanna River Basin Commission will hold its regular business meeting on June 14, 2019, in Harrisburg, Pennsylvania. Details concerning the matters to be addressed at the business meeting are contained in the Supplementary Information section of this notice. Also the Commission published a document in the Federal Register on April 11, 2019, concerning its public hearing on May 9, 2019, in Harrisburg, Pennsylvania.
