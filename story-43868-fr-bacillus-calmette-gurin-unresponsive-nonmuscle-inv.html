BREAKING: Federal Register: Bacillus Calmette-Guérin-Unresponsive Nonmuscle Invasive Bladder Cancer: Developing Drug and Biological Products for Treatment; Revised Draft Guidance for Industry; Availability

Published: 2024-08-09 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA or Agency) is announcing the availability of a draft guidance for industry entitled "Bacillus Calmette-Gu[eacute]rin-Unresponsive Nonmuscle Invasive Bladder Cancer: Developing Drug and Biological Products for Treatment." The purpose of this guidance is to assist sponsors in the development of drug and biological products for the treatment of patients with bacillus Calmette-Gu[eacute]rin (BCG)-unresponsive nonmuscle invasive bladder cancer (NMIBC).
