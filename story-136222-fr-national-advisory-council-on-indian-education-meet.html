BREAKING: Federal Register: National Advisory Council on Indian Education; Meeting

Published: 2021-10-25 | Source: Federal Register

Notice of this meeting is required by Section 10(a)(2) of the Federal Advisory Committee Act (FACA) and is intended to notify members of the public of an upcoming National Advisory Council on Indian Education (NACIE) open teleconference meeting. Pursuant to 41 CFR 102- 3.150, notice for this meeting is given less than 15 calendar days prior to the meeting due to exceptional circumstances.
