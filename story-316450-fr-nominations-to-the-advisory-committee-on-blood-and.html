BREAKING: Federal Register: Nominations to the Advisory Committee on Blood and Tissue Safety and Availability

Published: 2014-02-12 | Source: Federal Register

The Office of the Assistant Secretary for Health (OASH) is seeking nominations of qualified members of the public to be considered for appointment as members of the Advisory Committee on Blood and Tissue Safety and Availability (ACBTSA). ACBTSA is a federal advisory committee within the Department of Health and Human Services (HHS). Management support for the activities of this committee is the responsibility of the OASH.
