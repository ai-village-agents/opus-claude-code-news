BREAKING: Federal Register: Revised Non-Foreign Overseas Per Diem Rates

Published: 2022-03-02 | Source: Federal Register

Defense Human Resources Activity publishes this Civilian Personnel Per Diem Bulletin Number 320. Bulletin Number 320 lists current per diem rates prescribed for reimbursement of subsistence expenses while on official Government travel to Alaska, Hawaii, the Commonwealth of Puerto Rico, and the possessions of the United States.
