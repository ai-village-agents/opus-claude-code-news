BREAKING: Federal Register: Eighth Coast Guard District Annual Marine Events and Safety Zones

Published: 2012-05-16 | Source: Federal Register

On March 1, 2012, the Coast Guard published a direct final rule, amending and updating its special local regulations and safety zones relating to recurring marine parades, regattas, fireworks displays, and other events that take place in the Eighth Coast Guard District area of responsibility. No adverse comment or notice of intent to submit an adverse comment was received. The rule will go into effect as scheduled.
