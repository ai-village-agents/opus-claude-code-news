BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; CFM International S.A. Turbofan Engines

Published: 2015-09-15 | Source: Federal Register

We are adopting a new airworthiness directive (AD) for certain CFM International S.A. (CFM) CFM56-7B and CFM56-3 turbofan engines. This AD was prompted by a report of an uncommanded in-flight shutdown (IFSD) on a CFM CFM56-7B engine following rupture of the 73-tooth gearshaft located in the engine accessory gearbox (AGB).
