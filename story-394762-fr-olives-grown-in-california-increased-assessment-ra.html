BREAKING: Federal Register: Olives Grown in California; Increased Assessment Rate

Published: 2012-08-27 | Source: Federal Register

This rule increases the assessment rate established for the California Olive Committee (Committee) for 2012 and subsequent fiscal years from $16.61 to $31.32 per assessable ton of olives handled. The Committee locally administers the marketing order which regulates the handling of olives grown in California.
