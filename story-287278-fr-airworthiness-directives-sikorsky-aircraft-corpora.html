BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Sikorsky Aircraft Corporation (Sikorsky) Helicopters

Published: 2015-02-25 | Source: Federal Register

We are adopting a new airworthiness directive (AD) for certain Sikorsky Model S-76A, B, C, and D helicopters. This AD requires inspecting the tail rotor drive shaft (TDS) flange-to-shaft attachment hardware for correct assembly and correct torque of the fasteners.
