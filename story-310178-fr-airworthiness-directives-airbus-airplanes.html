BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Airbus Airplanes

Published: 2015-11-24 | Source: Federal Register

We are superseding Airworthiness Directive (AD) 2008-22-20 for certain Airbus Model A330-200, A330-300, and A340-300 series airplanes. AD 2008-22-20 required repetitive high frequency eddy current (HFEC) inspections for cracking, repair if necessary, and modification of the upper shell structure of the fuselage.
