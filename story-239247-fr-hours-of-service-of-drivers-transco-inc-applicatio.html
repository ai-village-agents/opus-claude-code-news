BREAKING: Federal Register: Hours of Service of Drivers: Transco, Inc.; Application for Exemption

Published: 2017-07-18 | Source: Federal Register

FMCSA announces its decision to deny the application of Transco, Inc. (Transco) (USDOT # 1062707) for an exemption from the 30- minute rest break provision of the Agency's hours-of-service (HOS) regulations for commercial motor vehicle (CMV) drivers. Transco asked that its drivers be permitted to comply with the 30-minute rest break requirement while performing "on-duty, not-driving" tasks.
