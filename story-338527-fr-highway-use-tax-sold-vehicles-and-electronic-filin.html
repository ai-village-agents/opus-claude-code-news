BREAKING: Federal Register: Highway Use Tax; Sold Vehicles and Electronic Filing; Taxable Period Beginning July 1, 2011

Published: 2014-10-29 | Source: Federal Register

This document contains final regulations that provide guidance on the mandatory electronic filing of Form 2290, "Heavy Highway Vehicle Use Tax Return," for 25 or more vehicles; credits or refunds for sold, destroyed, or stolen vehicles; and the tax liability and computation of tax on the use of certain second-hand vehicles.
