BREAKING: Federal Register: Special Local Regulation; Marine Events Within the Eleventh Coast Guard District-Another Dam Race

Published: 2021-11-12 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard will enforce the special local regulation on the waters between river miles 179 and 185 (between the Roadrunner Resort and Headgate Dam), Parker, Arizona during Another Dam Race on November 13, 2021. This special local regulation is necessary to provide for the safety of the participants, crew, sponsor vessels of the rowing event, and general users of the waterway.
