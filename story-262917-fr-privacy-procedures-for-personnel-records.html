BREAKING: Federal Register: Privacy Procedures for Personnel Records

Published: 2016-05-06 | Source: Federal Register

The Office of Personnel Management (OPM) proposes to amend part 297 of title 5, Code of Federal Regulations, to implement a 60-day timeframe for individuals to appeal or submit requests for administrative review of initial decisions regarding access and amendment requests involving records maintained in OPM systems of records.
