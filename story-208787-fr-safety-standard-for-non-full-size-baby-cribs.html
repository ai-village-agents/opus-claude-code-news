BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Standard for Non-Full-Size Baby Cribs

Published: 2018-06-06 | Source: Federal Register

In accordance with section 104(b) of the Consumer Product Safety Improvement Act of 2008 (CPSIA), also known as the Danny Keysar Child Product Safety Notification Act, the U.S. Consumer Product Safety Commission (CPSC), in December 2010, published a consumer product safety standard for non-full-size baby cribs (NFS cribs).
