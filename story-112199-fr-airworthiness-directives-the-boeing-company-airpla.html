BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; The Boeing Company Airplanes

Published: 2022-12-13 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA proposes to adopt a new airworthiness directive (AD) for all The Boeing Company Model 787-8, 787-9, and 787-10 airplanes. This proposed AD was prompted by reports of a loss of water pressure during flight and water leaks that affected multiple pieces of electronic equipment.
