BREAKING: Federal Register: Connecting Waters From Lake Huron to Lake Erie; Traffic Rules

Published: 2020-09-29 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is proposing to amend the navigation regulations between the Great Lakes. Specifically, this proposed amendment would allow a vessel to overtake another vessel that has slowed its speed to await berth availability or to make the turn for Rouge River and the overtaking vessel has so advised the Canadian Coast Guard Marine communications and Traffic Services Centre located in Sarnia, Ontario.
