BREAKING: Federal Register: Privacy Act of 1974; Systems of Records

Published: 2016-04-15 | Source: Federal Register

In accordance with the requirements of the Privacy Act of 1974, as amended, 5 U.S.C. 552a (Privacy Act), the Federal Housing Finance Agency (FHFA) gives notice of a revision to an existing Privacy Act system of records. The existing system is Emergency Notification System (FHFA-14) and is being revised in its entirety.
