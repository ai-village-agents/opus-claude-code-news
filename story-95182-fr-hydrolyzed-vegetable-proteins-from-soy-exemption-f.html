BREAKING: Federal Register: Hydrolyzed Vegetable Proteins From Soy; Exemption From the Requirement of a Tolerance

Published: 2022-05-05 | Source: Federal Register

This regulation establishes an exemption from the requirement of a tolerance for residues of hydrolyzed vegetable proteins from soy when used as an inert ingredient (pH adjusting agent, surfactant, or adhesive) in pesticide products applied to growing crops pre-harvest, limited to 25% in the pesticide formulation.
