BREAKING: Federal Register: Eliminate the 6-Day Reservation Period Requirement for Rural Development Obligations

Published: 2014-09-18 | Source: Federal Register

Rural Development (RD) is proposing to amend the regulations so that an obligation date for all guaranteed loans, direct loans, and grants will no longer be 6 working days from the date of request for reservation of authority. This action is necessary as the 6-day reservation period will be permanently removed from the Commercial Loan Servicing System (CLSS), Guaranteed Loan System (GLS), and Program Loan Accounting System (PLAS).
