BREAKING: Federal Register: Hours of Service of Drivers: Pipe Line Contractors Association (PLCA); Application for Exemption

Published: 2020-07-22 | Source: Federal Register

FMCSA announces that it has received an application from the Pipe Line Contractors Association (PLCA) for an exemption from certain hours-of-service (HOS) regulations for drivers of a variety of commercial motor vehicles (CMVs) employed by its member pipeline contractors.
