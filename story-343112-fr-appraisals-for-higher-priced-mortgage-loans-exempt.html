BREAKING: Federal Register: Appraisals for Higher-Priced Mortgage Loans Exemption Threshold Adjustment-Final Rule

Published: 2014-12-30 | Source: Federal Register

The OCC, the Board and the Bureau are publishing final rules amending the official staff interpretations for their regulations that implement section 129H of the Truth in Lending Act (TILA). Section 129H of TILA establishes special appraisal requirements for "higher-risk mortgages," termed "higher-priced mortgages" or "HPMLs" in the agencies' regulations.
