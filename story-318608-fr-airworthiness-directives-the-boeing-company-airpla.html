BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; The Boeing Company Airplanes

Published: 2014-03-11 | Source: Federal Register

We are adopting a new airworthiness directive (AD) for all The Boeing Company Model 717-200 airplanes. This AD was prompted by multiple reports of cracking in the overwing frames. This AD requires repetitive inspections for cracking in the overwing frames, and corrective actions if necessary.
