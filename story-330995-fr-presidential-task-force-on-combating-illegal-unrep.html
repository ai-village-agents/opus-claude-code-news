BREAKING: Federal Register: Presidential Task Force on Combating Illegal, Unreported and Unregulated Fishing and Seafood Fraud

Published: 2014-07-31 | Source: Federal Register

On June 17, 2014, the White House released a Presidential Memorandum entitled "Establishing a Comprehensive Framework to Combat Illegal, Unreported, and Unregulated Fishing and Seafood Fraud." Among other actions, the Memorandum established a Presidential Task Force on Combating Illegal, Unreported, and Unregulated Fishing and Seafood Fraud (Task Force), co-chaired by the Departments of State and Commerce and made up of a broad range of other federal agencies.
