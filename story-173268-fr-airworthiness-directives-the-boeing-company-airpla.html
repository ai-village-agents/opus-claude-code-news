BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; The Boeing Company Airplanes

Published: 2019-03-04 | Source: Federal Register

We are adopting a new airworthiness directive (AD) for certain The Boeing Company Model 737-600, -700, -700C, -800, -900, and -900ER series airplanes. This AD was prompted by reports of passenger service units (PSUs) becoming detached from the supporting airplane structure in several Model 737 series airplanes.
