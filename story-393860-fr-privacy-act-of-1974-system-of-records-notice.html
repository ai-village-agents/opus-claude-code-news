BREAKING: Federal Register: Privacy Act of 1974; System of Records Notice

Published: 2012-08-15 | Source: Federal Register

In accordance with the requirements of the Privacy Act of 1974, HHS gives notice of a proposed alteration to two existing systems of records covering payroll records: 09-40-0006 entitled "Public Health Service (PHS) Commissioned Corps Payroll Records, HHS/PSC/HRS," and 09-40-0010 entitled "Pay, Leave and Attendance Records, HHS/PSC/ HRS." The systems are being amended to revise an existing routine use covering disclosures to contractors and to add a new routine use pertaining to system security....
