BREAKING: Federal Register: Proposed Collection; Comment Request; Renewal Without Change of the FinCEN Suspicious Activity Reports Electronic Data Fields

Published: 2014-08-11 | Source: Federal Register

FinCEN invites all interested parties to comment on its proposed renewal without change to the collection of information filings by financial institutions required to file such reports under the Bank Secrecy Act ("BSA"). This notice does not propose any new regulatory requirements or changes to the requirements related to suspicious activity reporting.
