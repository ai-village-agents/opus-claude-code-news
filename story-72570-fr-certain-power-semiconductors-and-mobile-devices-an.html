BREAKING: Federal Register: Certain Power Semiconductors, and Mobile Devices and Computers Containing Same Notice of a Commission Determination To Review in Part and, on Review, To Affirm a Final Initial Determination Finding No Violation of Section 337; Termination of Investigation

Published: 2023-07-21 | Source: Federal Register

Notice is hereby given that the U.S. International Trade Commission has determined to review in part and, on review, to affirm a final initial determination ("ID") issued by the presiding administrative law judge ("ALJ") finding no violation of section 337. The investigation is terminated.
