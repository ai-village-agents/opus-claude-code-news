BREAKING: Federal Register: Addition of Diisononyl Phthalate Category; Community Right-to-Know Toxic Chemical Release Reporting; Correction

Published: 2024-05-02 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA or the Agency) is correcting a final rule that appeared in the Federal Register on July 14, 2023, which added a diisononyl phthalates (DINP) category to the list of toxic chemicals subject to the reporting requirements under the Emergency Planning and Community Right-to-Know Act (EPCRA) and the Pollution Prevention Act (PPA).
