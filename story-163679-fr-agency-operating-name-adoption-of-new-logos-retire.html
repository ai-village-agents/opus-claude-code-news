BREAKING: Federal Register: Agency Operating Name, Adoption of New Logos, & Retirement of Logos

Published: 2020-10-15 | Source: Federal Register

This rule makes non-substantive amendments to the Corporation for National and Community Service's (CNCS) regulations to change the operating name of the agency to "AmeriCorps," to adopt two new logos, and to retire all existing logos, except Days of Service, from daily use. This final rule adds a new part--2500--to Title 45 of the Code of Federal Regulations to reflect the operational name change, new logos, and retirement of logos.
