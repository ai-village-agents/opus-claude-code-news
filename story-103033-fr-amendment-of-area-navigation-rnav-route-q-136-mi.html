BREAKING: Federal Register: Amendment of Area Navigation (RNAV) Route Q-136; MI

Published: 2022-08-16 | Source: Federal Register

This action amends the description of Area Navigation (RNAV) route Q-136 by changing the name of the "BAACN", IA, waypoint (WP) to "DIYAP". The FAA is taking this action due to a similarly pronounced and sounding Fix (BACNN, MO) located approximately 55 nautical miles (NM) southwest of the BAACN WP which contributes to communications errors resulting from the similar-sounding Fix and WP names in radio communications.
