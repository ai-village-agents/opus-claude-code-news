BREAKING: Federal Register: Updated Legal Framework for Patent Electronic System

Published: 2025-09-11 | Source: Federal Register

The updated legal framework defines the USPTO patent electronic filing system requirements, and provides guidance on the relevant statutes, regulations, and policies that support the USPTO's Patent Electronic System.
