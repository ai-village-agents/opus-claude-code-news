BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Alexander Schleicher GmbH & Co. Segelflugzeugbau Gliders

Published: 2022-10-26 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA proposes to supersede Airworthiness Directive (AD) 2022-14-14, which applies to all Alexander Schleicher GmbH & Co. Segelflugzeugbau Model ASW-15 gliders. AD 2022-14-14 requires repetitively inspecting the wing root ribs for cracks, looseness, and damage and replacing any root rib with a crack, a loose rib or lift pin bushing, or any damage.
