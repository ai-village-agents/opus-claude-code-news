BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Bombardier, Inc., Airplanes

Published: 2019-06-07 | Source: Federal Register

We are revising an earlier proposal for certain Bombardier, Inc., Model DHC-8-400 series airplanes. This action revises the notice of proposed rulemaking (NPRM) by adding a requirement to replace the lower lock link of the nose landing gear (NLG), which would terminate the repetitive inspections proposed in the NPRM.
