BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Reallotment of Federal Fiscal Year (FFY) 2011 Funds for the Low Income Home Energy Assistance Program (LIHEAP)

Published: 2012-08-14 | Source: Federal Register

This notice provides information on a preliminary determination that funds from the Federal Fiscal Year (FFY) 2011 Low Income Home Energy Assistance Program (LIHEAP) are available for reallotment to States, Territories, Tribes, and Tribal Organizations that receive FFY 2012 direct LIHEAP grants. No subgrantees or other entities may apply for these funds.
