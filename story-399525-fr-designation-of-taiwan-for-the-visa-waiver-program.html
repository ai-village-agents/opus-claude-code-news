BREAKING: Federal Register: Designation of Taiwan for the Visa Waiver Program

Published: 2012-10-22 | Source: Federal Register

Eligible citizens, nationals and passport holders from designated Visa Waiver Program countries \1\ may apply for admission to the United States at U.S. ports of entry as nonimmigrant aliens for a period of ninety days or less for business or pleasure without first obtaining a nonimmigrant visa, provided that they are otherwise eligible for admission under applicable statutory and regulatory requirements.
