BREAKING: Federal Register: Public Notice of Airport Improvement Program Property Release Portland-Hillsboro Airport, Hillsboro, Oregon

Published: 2022-05-26 | Source: Federal Register

Notice is being given that the FAA is considering a request from the Port of Portland, Oregon to waive the property requirements for approximately .78 acres of airport property and the release from aeronautical to non-aeronautical for approximately .30 acres of airport property located at Hillsboro Airport, in Hillsboro, Oregon.
