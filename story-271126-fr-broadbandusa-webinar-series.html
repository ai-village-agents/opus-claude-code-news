BREAKING: Federal Register: BroadbandUSA Webinar Series

Published: 2016-08-12 | Source: Federal Register

The National Telecommunications and Information Administration (NTIA), as part of its BroadbandUSA program, will host a series of webinars on a monthly basis to engage the public and stakeholders with information to accelerate broadband access, improve digital inclusion, strengthen broadband policies, and support local community priorities.
