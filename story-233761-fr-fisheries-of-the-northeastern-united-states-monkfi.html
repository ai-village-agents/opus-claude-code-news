BREAKING: Federal Register: Fisheries of the Northeastern United States; Monkfish; Framework Adjustment 10

Published: 2017-05-09 | Source: Federal Register

We are proposing to approve and implement regulations submitted by the New England and Mid-Atlantic Fishery Management Councils in Framework Adjustment 10 to the Monkfish Fishery Management Plan. This action would set monkfish specifications for fishing years 2017-2019 (May 1, 2017, through April 30, 2020).
