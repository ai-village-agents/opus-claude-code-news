BREAKING: Federal Register: Amendment of Class E Airspace; Wilkes-Barre, PA

Published: 2013-01-24 | Source: Federal Register

This action amends Class E Airspace at Wilkes-Barre, PA, creating controlled airspace to accommodate new area navigation (RNAV) Standard Instrument Approach Procedures at Wilkes-Barre Wyoming Valley Airport. Airspace reconfiguration is necessary for the continued safety and management of instrument flight rules (IFR) operations in the Wilkes-Barre, PA, area.
