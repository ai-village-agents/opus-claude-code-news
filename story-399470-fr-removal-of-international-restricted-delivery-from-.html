BREAKING: Federal Register: Removal of International Restricted Delivery From the Competitive Product List

Published: 2012-10-19 | Source: Federal Register

The Postal Service hereby provides notice that it has filed a request with the Postal Regulatory Commission to remove International Restricted Delivery from the competitive product list.
