BREAKING: Federal Register: Agency Request for Approval of a New Information Collection: Mariner Survey Pre-Test

Published: 2021-09-14 | Source: Federal Register

The Maritime Administration (MARAD) invites public comments about our intention to request the Office of Management and Budget (OMB) approval for a new information collection. This Mariner Survey Pre-Test collection provides for cognitive interviews and a pilot survey of a sample of appropriately credentialed U.S. merchant mariners to validate and improve the study design for a subsequent Mariner Survey.
