BREAKING: Federal Register: Winter Use Plan, Supplemental Environmental Impact Statement, Yellowstone National Park, Idaho, Montana, and Wyoming

Published: 2012-02-08 | Source: Federal Register

Pursuant to the National Environmental Policy Act of 1969, the National Park Service (NPS) is preparing a supplemental Environmental Impact Statement (SEIS) for a Winter Use Plan for Yellowstone National Park, located in Idaho, Montana and Wyoming. The preparation of a SEIS is deemed necessary to further the purposes of the National Environmental Policy Act (NEPA).
