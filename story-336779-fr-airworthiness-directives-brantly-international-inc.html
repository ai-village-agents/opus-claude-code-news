BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Brantly International, Inc. Helicopters

Published: 2014-10-07 | Source: Federal Register

We are adopting a new airworthiness directive (AD) for Brantly International, Inc. (Brantly) Model B-2, Model B-2A, and Model B-2B helicopters with certain main rotor blades. This AD requires inspecting each main rotor (M/R) blade for a crack or delamination and removing the blade if a crack exists or if the delamination exceeds certain thresholds.
