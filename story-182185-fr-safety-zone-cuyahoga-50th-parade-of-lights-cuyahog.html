BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone; Cuyahoga 50th Parade of Lights; Cuyahoga River, Cleveland, OH

Published: 2019-06-21 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is establishing a moving safety zone to provide for the safety of life on these navigable waters in the Cuyahoga River, Cleveland, OH, during the Cuyahoga 50th Parade of Lights on June 22, 2019. This temporary safety zone is necessary to protect personnel, vessels, and the marine environment from the potential hazards created by 125 vessels transiting in the river with decorative lights not normally used as navigation lights.
