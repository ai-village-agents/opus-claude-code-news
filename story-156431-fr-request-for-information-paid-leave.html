BREAKING: Federal Register: Request for Information; Paid Leave

Published: 2020-07-16 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Labor (Department) seeks information from the public regarding paid leave. For purposes of this Request, paid leave refers to paid family and medical leave to care for a family members, or for one's own health.
