BREAKING: Federal Register: Air Plan Revisions; California; Eastern Kern Air Pollution Control District; Oxides of Nitrogen

Published: 2023-06-15 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is finalizing a limited approval and limited disapproval of a revision to the Eastern Kern Air Pollution Control District (EKAPCD) portion of the California State Implementation Plan (SIP). This revision concerns emissions of oxides of nitrogen (NOX) from stationary gas turbines.
