BREAKING: Federal Register: Visual-Manual NHTSA Driver Distraction Guidelines for In-Vehicle Electronic Devices

Published: 2014-09-16 | Source: Federal Register

On April 26, 2013, NHTSA released the Visual-Manual NHTSA Driver Distraction Guidelines for In-Vehicle Electronic Devices (Phase 1 Guidelines) in an effort to promote safety by discouraging the introduction of excessively distracting devices in vehicles.
