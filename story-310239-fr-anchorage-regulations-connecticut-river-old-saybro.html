BREAKING: Federal Register: Anchorage Regulations; Connecticut River, Old Saybrook, CT

Published: 2015-11-25 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard proposes to establish three special anchorage areas in the Connecticut River in the vicinity Old Saybrook, CT. This proposed action is necessary to facilitate safe navigation in that area and provide safe and secure anchorages for vessels less than 20 meters in length.
