BREAKING: Federal Register: Modification of Class E Airspace; Gulkana, AK

Published: 2021-08-11 | Source: Federal Register

This action modifies the Class E airspace, designated as a surface area at Gulkana Airport, Gulkana, AK. This action also modifies the Class E airspace extending upward from 700 feet above the surface. The NPRM for this action proposed a modification to the Class E airspace extending upward from 1,200 feet above the surface, however, the proposed change has been removed from the Final Rule.
