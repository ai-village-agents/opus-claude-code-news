BREAKING: Federal Register: Announcement of the Availability of Disaster Relief Act 2019 Grant Funds for Community Facilities

Published: 2019-09-10 | Source: Federal Register

As part of the Additional Supplemental Appropriations for Disaster Relief Act, 2019, the Rural Housing Service's Community Facilities Program (CF) received supplemental grant funding available for necessary expenses related to the consequences of Hurricanes Michael and Florence and wildfires occurring in calendar year 2018, tornadoes and floods occurring in calendar year 2019, and other natural disasters, to remain available until expended.
