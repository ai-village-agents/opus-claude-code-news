BREAKING: Federal Register: Enhanced-Use Lease of U.S. Department of Veterans Affairs (VA) Real Property for the Development of Permanent Supportive Housing Facility at the VA Greater Los Angeles Healthcare System-West Los Angeles, Los Angeles, California

Published: 2017-03-20 | Source: Federal Register

The Secretary of VA intends to enter into an EUL for the purpose of utilizing Building 209, located on the grounds of the VA Greater Los Angeles Healthcare System--West Los Angeles Campus, Los Angeles, California, and consisting of 55 housing units of permanent supportive housing for Veterans.
