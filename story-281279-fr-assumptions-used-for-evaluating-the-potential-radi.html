BREAKING: Federal Register: Assumptions Used for Evaluating the Potential Radiological Consequences of a Fuel Handling Accident in the Fuel Handling and Storage Facility for Boiling and Pressurized Water Reactors

Published: 2016-12-12 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Nuclear Regulatory Commission (NRC) is withdrawing regulatory guide (RG) 1.25, "Assumptions used for Evaluating the Potential Radiological Consequences of a Fuel Handling Accident in the Fuel Handling and Storage Facility for Boiling and Pressurized Water Reactors."
