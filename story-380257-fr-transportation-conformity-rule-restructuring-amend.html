BREAKING: Federal Register: Transportation Conformity Rule Restructuring Amendments

Published: 2012-03-14 | Source: Federal Register

EPA is amending the transportation conformity rule to finalize provisions that were proposed on August 13, 2010. These amendments restructure several sections of the transportation conformity rule so that they apply to any new or revised National Ambient Air Quality Standards. EPA is also finalizing several clarifications to improve implementation of the rule.
