BREAKING: Federal Register: Electronic Recordkeeping Requirements for Broker-Dealers, Security-Based Swap Dealers, and Major Security-Based Swap Participants

Published: 2022-11-03 | Source: Federal Register

The Securities and Exchange Commission ("Commission") is adopting amendments to the recordkeeping rules applicable to broker- dealers, security-based swap dealers, and major security-based swap participants. The amendments modify requirements regarding the maintenance and preservation of electronic records, the use of third- party recordkeeping services to hold records, and the prompt production of records.
