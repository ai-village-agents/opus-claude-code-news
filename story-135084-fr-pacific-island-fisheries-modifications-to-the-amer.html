BREAKING: Federal Register: Pacific Island Fisheries; Modifications to the American Samoa Longline Fishery Limited Entry Program

Published: 2021-10-07 | Source: Federal Register

This final rule implements Amendment 9 to the Fishery Ecosystem Plan for Pelagic Fisheries of the Western Pacific (FEP). It modifies the American Samoa longline fishery limited entry program to consolidate vessel class sizes, modify permit eligibility requirements, and reduce the minimum harvest requirements for small vessels.
