BREAKING: Federal Register: Amendment of Class D and Class E Airspace; Aspen, CO

Published: 2018-12-14 | Source: Federal Register

This action modifies Class E airspace designated as an extension at Aspen-Pitkin County Airport/Sardy Field, Aspen, CO, and removes the part-time NOTAM language from the legal description. This action also amends Class E airspace extending upward from 700 feet above the surface and removes the Class E 1,200 foot airspace.
