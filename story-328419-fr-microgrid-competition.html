BREAKING: Federal Register: Microgrid Competition

Published: 2014-07-01 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Department of Energy (DOE) announced the administration of an incentive prize competition titled "Microgrid Competition." The goal of this Department of Energy Incentive Prize Competition is to support implementation of microgrids to enhance resiliency of the U.S. grid and to provide a cleaner and more efficient and cost-effective power system.
