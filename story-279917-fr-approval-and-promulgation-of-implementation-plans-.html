BREAKING: Federal Register: Approval and Promulgation of Implementation Plans; State of Arizona; Revised Format for Materials Incorporated by Reference

Published: 2016-11-23 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is revising the format for materials submitted by the State of Arizona that are incorporated by reference (IBR) into the Arizona State Implementation Plan (SIP). The regulations affected by this format change have all been previously submitted by the State of Arizona and approved by the EPA.
