BREAKING: Federal Register: Guidance for Industry, Third Parties and Food and Drug Administration Staff; Medical Device ISO 13485:2003 Voluntary Audit Report Submission Pilot Program; Availability

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA) is announcing the availability of the guidance entitled "Medical Device ISO 13485:2003 Voluntary Audit Report Submission Pilot Program." This guidance document is intended to provide information on the implementation of the Food and Drug Administration Amendments Act of 2007 (FDAAA), which amends the Federal Food, Drug, and Cosmetic Act (the FD&C Act).
