BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Meeting for Star-Spangled Banner National Historic Trail Advisory Council

Published: 2014-06-18 | Source: Federal Register

As required by the Federal Advisory Committee Act (5 U.S.C. Appendix 1-16), the National Park Service (NPS) is hereby giving notice that the Advisory Council for the Star-Spangled Banner National Historic Trail will hold a meeting. The trail commemorates the Chesapeake Campaign of the War of 1812, including the British invasion of Washington, District of Columbia, and its associated feints, and the Battle of Baltimore in summer 1814.
