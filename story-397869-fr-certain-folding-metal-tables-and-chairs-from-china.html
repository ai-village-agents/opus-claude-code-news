BREAKING: Federal Register: Certain Folding Metal Tables and Chairs From China; Institution of a Five-Year Review Concerning the Antidumping Duty Order on Certain Folding Metal Tables and Chairs From China

Published: 2012-10-01 | Source: Federal Register

The Commission hereby gives notice that it has instituted a review pursuant to section 751(c) of the Tariff Act of 1930 (19 U.S.C. 1675(c)) (the Act) to determine whether revocation of the antidumping duty order on certain folding metal tables and chairs from China would be likely to lead to continuation or recurrence of material injury.
