BREAKING: Federal Register: Freedom of Information Act Regulations

Published: 2014-09-12 | Source: Federal Register

The Military Compensation and Retirement Modernization Commission (MCRMC) is issuing an interim final rule, establishing procedures for the public to obtain information from MCRMC under the Freedom of Information Act (FOIA).
