BREAKING: Federal Register: Revisions to Defense Priorities and Allocations System Regulations

Published: 2014-01-31 | Source: Federal Register

This proposed rule would clarify existing standards and procedures by which the Bureau of Industry and Security (BIS) may require that certain contracts or orders that promote the national defense be given priority over other contracts or orders. It also proposes new standards and procedures for such prioritization with respect to contracts or orders for emergency preparedness activities.
