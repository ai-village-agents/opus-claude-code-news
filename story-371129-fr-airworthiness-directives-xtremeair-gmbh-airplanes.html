BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; XtremeAir GmbH Airplanes

Published: 2013-11-25 | Source: Federal Register

We are adopting a new airworthiness directive (AD) for XtremeAir GmbH Model XA42 airplanes. This AD results from mandatory continuing airworthiness information (MCAI) issued by the aviation authority of another country to identify and correct an unsafe condition on an aviation product.
