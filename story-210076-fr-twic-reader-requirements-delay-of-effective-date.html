BREAKING: Federal Register: TWIC-Reader Requirements; Delay of Effective Date

Published: 2018-06-22 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard proposes delaying the effective date for certain facilities affected by the final rule entitled "Transportation Worker Identification Credential (TWIC)--Reader Requirements," published in the Federal Register on August 23, 2016. The current effective date for the final rule is August 23, 2018.
