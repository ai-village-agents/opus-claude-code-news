BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Eurocopter Deutschland GmbH Helicopters

Published: 2013-07-23 | Source: Federal Register

We propose to adopt a new airworthiness directive (AD) for certain Eurocopter Deutschland GmbH (Eurocopter) Model EC135P2+ and EC135T2+ helicopters. This proposed AD would require inspecting the mechanical air conditioning system compressor bearing block upper bearing (upper bearing) for corrosion, leaking grease, condensation, or water.
