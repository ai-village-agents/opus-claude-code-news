BREAKING: Federal Register: Special Local Regulations; Marine Events, Spa Creek and Annapolis Harbor; Annapolis, MD

Published: 2013-06-25 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is withdrawing its proposed rule concerning amendments to the regattas and marine parades regulations. The rulemaking was initiated to establish special local regulations during the swim segment of the "TriRock Triathlon Series," a marine event to be held on the waters of Spa Creek and Annapolis Harbor on July 20, 2013. The Coast Guard was notified on May 21, 2013, that the event had been cancelled.
