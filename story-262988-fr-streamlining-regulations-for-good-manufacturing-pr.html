BREAKING: Federal Register: Streamlining Regulations for Good Manufacturing Practices for Hearing Aids; Public Workshop; Extension of Comment Period

Published: 2016-05-09 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA or we) is extending the comment period for the document entitled "Streamlining Regulations for Good Manufacturing Practices for Hearing Aids; Public Workshop" that appeared in the Federal Register of January 7, 2016. In the document, FDA requested comments on the appropriate level of good manufacturing practices (GMPs) regulation to ensure the safety and effectiveness of air-conduction hearing aid devices.
