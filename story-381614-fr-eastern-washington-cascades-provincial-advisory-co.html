BREAKING: Federal Register: Eastern Washington Cascades Provincial Advisory Committee and the Yakima Provincial Advisory Committee

Published: 2012-03-28 | Source: Federal Register

The Eastern Washington Cascades Provincial Advisory Committee and the Yakima Provincial Advisory Committee will meet on April 12, 2012 from 9 a.m. to 3 p.m. at the Washington State Parks office, 270 9th Street NE., East Wenatchee, WA. During this meeting information will be shared about the Forest Service Chief's 10-Year Stewardship Challenge, Upper Yakima Basin Water Enhancement Project, Holden Mine Remediation progress, and an update on the Forest Plan Revision.
