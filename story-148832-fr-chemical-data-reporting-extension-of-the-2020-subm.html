BREAKING: Federal Register: Chemical Data Reporting; Extension of the 2020 Submission Period

Published: 2020-04-09 | Source: Federal Register

EPA is amending the Toxic Substances Control Act (TSCA) Chemical Data Reporting (CDR) regulations by extending the submission deadline for 2020 reports from September 30, 2020, to November 30, 2020. This is a one-time extension for the 2020 submission period only.
