BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Airbus SAS Airplanes

Published: 2023-05-15 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA proposes to supersede Airworthiness Directive (AD) 2022-17-09, which applies to certain Airbus SAS Model A350-941 and - 1041 airplanes. AD 2022-17-09 continues to require the actions of AD 2021-16-03 and requires a modification to restore two independent layers of lightning strike protection.
