BREAKING: Federal Register: Tank Level Probing Radars

Published: 2012-04-30 | Source: Federal Register

This document proposes to expand the scope of this proceeding to propose a set of technical rules for the operation of unlicensed level probing radars (LPR) in several frequency bands. LPR devices are low-power radars that measure the level (relative height) of various substances in man-made or natural containments.
