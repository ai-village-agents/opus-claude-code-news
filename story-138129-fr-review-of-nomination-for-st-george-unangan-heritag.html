BREAKING: Federal Register: Review of Nomination for St. George Unangan Heritage National Marine Sanctuary

Published: 2021-11-17 | Source: Federal Register

The Office of National Marine Sanctuaries (ONMS) of the National Oceanic and Atmospheric Administration (NOAA) is requesting written comments to facilitate ONMS' review of the nomination for St. George Unangan Heritage National Marine Sanctuary (NMS) at the five- year interval. In particular, NOAA is requesting relevant information as it pertains to its 11 evaluation criteria for inclusion in the inventory.
