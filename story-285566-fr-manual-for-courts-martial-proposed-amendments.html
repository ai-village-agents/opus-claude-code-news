BREAKING: Federal Register: Manual for Courts-Martial; Proposed Amendments

Published: 2015-02-04 | Source: Federal Register

The Joint Service Committee on Military Justice (JSC) is publishing final proposed amendments to the Manual for Courts-Martial, United States (MCM). The proposed changes concern the rules of evidence and the punitive articles applicable in trials by courts-martial.
