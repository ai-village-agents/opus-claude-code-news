BREAKING: Federal Register: Submission of Credit Card Agreements Under the Truth In Lending Act (Regulation Z)

Published: 2015-02-26 | Source: Federal Register

The Bureau of Consumer Financial Protection (Bureau) is proposing to amend Regulation Z, which implements the Truth in Lending Act, and the official interpretation to that regulation. The proposal would temporarily suspend card issuers' obligations to submit credit card agreements to the Bureau for a period of one year (i.e., four quarterly submissions), in order to reduce burden while the Bureau works to develop a more streamlined and automated electronic submission system.
