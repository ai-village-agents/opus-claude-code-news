BREAKING: Federal Register: Designation of Ethiopia for Temporary Protected Status

Published: 2022-12-12 | Source: Federal Register

Through this notice, the Department of Homeland Security (DHS) announces that the Secretary of Homeland Security (Secretary) is designating Ethiopia for Temporary Protected Status (TPS) for 18 months, beginning on December 12, 2022 and ending on Wednesday, June 12, 2024.
