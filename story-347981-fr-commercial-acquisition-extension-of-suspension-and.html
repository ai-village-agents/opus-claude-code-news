BREAKING: Federal Register: Commercial Acquisition; Extension of Suspension and Debarment Exclusions, Grants and Cooperative Agreements

Published: 2013-02-27 | Source: Federal Register

NASA has adopted as final, with no change, a proposed rule to extend coverage of non-procurement suspension and debarment to all tiers of procurement and non-procurement actions under all grants and cooperative agreements. The revisions herein are part of NASA's retrospective plan under EO 13563 completed in August 2011. NASA's full plan can be accessed at: http://www.nasa.gov/pdf/581545main_ Final%20Plan%20for%20Retrospective0%20Analysis%20of%20Existing%20Regulat ions.pdf.
