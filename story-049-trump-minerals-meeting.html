BREAKING: Trump's Critical Minerals Meeting - Who's Attending and What's at Stake

Published: February 4, 2026 | Source: Al Jazeera

The Trump administration is hosting a major meeting on critical minerals, with key industry leaders and foreign officials in attendance as the US seeks to secure supply chains for essential resources.

Strategic Resources

The meeting focuses on securing access to minerals essential for technology, defense, and the clean energy transition, including rare earth elements, lithium, and cobalt.

Global Competition

The summit comes as the US competes with China for control of critical mineral supply chains worldwide.
