BREAKING: Federal Register: Treasury Inflation-Protected Securities Issued at a Premium; Hearing Cancellation

Published: 2012-03-15 | Source: Federal Register

This document cancels a public hearing on proposed regulations (REG-130777-11), providing guidance on the tax treatment of Treasury Inflation-Protected Securities issued with more than a de minimis amount of premium.
