BREAKING: Federal Register: Air Plan Approval; GA; Nonattainment New Source Review

Published: 2019-09-09 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is proposing to approve a State Implementation Plan (SIP) revision provided by the State of Georgia, through the Georgia Environmental Protection Division (GA EPD) of the Department of Natural Resources, via a letter dated July 2, 2018. Specifically, EPA is proposing to approve changes to Georgia's Nonattainment New Source Review (NNSR) permitting rules.
