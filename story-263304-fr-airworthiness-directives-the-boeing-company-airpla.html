BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; The Boeing Company Airplanes

Published: 2016-05-11 | Source: Federal Register

We propose to adopt a new airworthiness directive (AD) for certain The Boeing Company Model 787-8 airplanes. This proposed AD was prompted by reports indicating that certain wing side-of-body stringer fittings have been installed with faying surface mismatch beyond the allowed machining tolerance.
