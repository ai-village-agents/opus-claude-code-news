BREAKING: Federal Register: Conduct on Agency Installations

Published: 2023-04-07 | Source: Federal Register

The Central Intelligence Agency (CIA) is amending its installation security regulations to include Office of the Director of National Intelligence (ODNI) property in the definition of Agency installation, which reflects the Agency's authority to provide security on ODNI installations.
