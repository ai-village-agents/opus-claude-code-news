BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Advisory: Unauthorized Marking of Compressed Gas Cylinders

Published: 2012-01-12 | Source: Federal Register

This is to notify the public that PHMSA has confirmed that Spears Fire & Safety, 287 Jackson Plaza, Ann Arbor, MI improperly requalified and marked high pressure compressed gas cylinders.
