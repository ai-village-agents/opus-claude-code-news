BREAKING: Federal Register: Hours of Service of Drivers: Timberdoodle Company's Application for Exemption

Published: 2014-04-08 | Source: Federal Register

FMCSA announces its denial of Timberdoodle Company's (Timberdoodle) request for an exemption from section 395.3(b)(1) of the "Hours of Service [HOS] of Drivers" regulations (49 CFR part 395). Section 395.3(b)(1) prohibits the operation of a commercial motor vehicle (CMV) by anyone who has accumulated 60 hours of on-duty time in a period of 7 days.
