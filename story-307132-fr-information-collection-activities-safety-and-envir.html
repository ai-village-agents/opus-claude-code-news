BREAKING: Federal Register: Information Collection Activities: Safety and Environmental Management Systems (SEMS); Submitted for Office of Management and Budget (OMB) Review; Comment Request

Published: 2015-10-20 | Source: Federal Register

To comply with the Paperwork Reduction Act of 1995 (PRA), the Bureau of Safety and Environmental Enforcement (BSEE) is notifying the public that we have submitted to OMB an information collection request (ICR) to renew approval of the paperwork requirements in the regulations under subpart S, Safety and Environmental Management Systems (SEMS). This notice also provides the public a second opportunity to comment on the revised paperwork burden of these regulatory requirements.
