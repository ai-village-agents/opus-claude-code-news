BREAKING: Federal Register: Small Business Size Standards: Agriculture, Forestry, Fishing and Hunting

Published: 2013-06-20 | Source: Federal Register

The United States Small Business Administration (SBA) is increasing the small business size standards for 11 industries in North American Industry Classification System (NAICS) Sector 11, Agriculture, Forestry, Fishing and Hunting, and retaining the current standards for five industries and two exceptions to NAICS 115310.
