BREAKING: Federal Register: Privacy Act of 1974; System of Records

Published: 2022-02-02 | Source: Federal Register

In accordance with the Privacy Act of 1974, the National Labor Relations Board ("NLRB" or "Agency") proposes to issue a National Labor Relations Board system of records notice titled, "Backpay Management System" (NLRB-36). The Agency is authorized by the National Labor Relations Act to remedy unfair labor practices by issuing a backpay remedy to an individual, called a "discriminatee," for a monetary loss that results from an unfair labor practice.
