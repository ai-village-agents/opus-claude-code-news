BREAKING: Federal Register: Proposed Information Collection: Beachgoer and Vessel Surveys

Published: 2016-12-19 | Source: Federal Register

In compliance with the Paperwork Reduction Act, the Bureau of Ocean Energy Management (BOEM) will ask the Office of Management and Budget (OMB) to approve a collection of information to support ocean observation planning and management on public lands and waters. The respondents will be recreationists visiting public and private Gulf Coast beaches, and coastal and offshore vessel (boat) operators in the Gulf of Mexico region.
