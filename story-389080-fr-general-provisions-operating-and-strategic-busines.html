BREAKING: Federal Register: General Provisions; Operating and Strategic Business Planning; Effective Date

Published: 2012-06-21 | Source: Federal Register

The Farm Credit Administration (FCA or Agency), through the FCA Board (Board), issued a final rule under part 618 on May 1, 2012 (77 FR 25577) amending our regulations to require the board of directors of each Farm Credit System institution to adopt an operational and strategic business plan to include, among other things, outreach toward diversity and inclusion.
