BREAKING: Federal Register: Food Labeling: Health Claims; Soy Protein and Coronary Heart Disease

Published: 2017-10-31 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA, the Agency, or we) is proposing to revoke its regulation authorizing the use of health claims on the relationship between soy protein and coronary heart disease on the label or in the labeling of foods.
