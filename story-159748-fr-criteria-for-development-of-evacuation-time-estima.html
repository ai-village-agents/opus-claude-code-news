BREAKING: Federal Register: Criteria for Development of Evacuation Time Estimate Studies (NUREG/CR-7002, Revision 1)

Published: 2020-08-27 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Nuclear Regulatory Commission (NRC) is issuing for public comment a draft NUREG, NUREG/CR-7002, "Criteria for Development of Evacuation Time Estimate Studies," Revision 1. This draft NUREG provides guidance to meet NRC requirements for development of evacuation time estimates (ETEs) to support emergency planning.
