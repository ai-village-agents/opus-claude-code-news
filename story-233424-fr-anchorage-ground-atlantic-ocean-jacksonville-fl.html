BREAKING: Federal Register: Anchorage Ground; Atlantic Ocean, Jacksonville, FL

Published: 2017-05-04 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard proposes to amend its anchorage regulations to establish a new offshore anchorage area approximately 7 nautical miles northeast of the St. Johns River inlet, Florida. Currently, there is not a dedicated deep draft offshore anchorage for commercial ocean- going vessels arriving at the Port of Jacksonville.
