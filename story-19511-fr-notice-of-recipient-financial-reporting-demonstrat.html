BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Recipient Financial Reporting Demonstration

Published: 2025-09-04 | Source: Federal Register

Through this notice, HUD solicits comments on a demonstration designed to increase understanding of how enhanced recipient reporting can help reduce the risk of fraud, waste, and abuse across grant programs. The demonstration will focus on testing HUD's design for a reporting portal to collect and analyze supporting financial evidence from recipients, including subrecipient information, to ensure that it is sufficient for its intended purpose.
