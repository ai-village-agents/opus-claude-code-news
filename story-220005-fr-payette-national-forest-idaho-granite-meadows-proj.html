BREAKING: Federal Register: Payette National Forest; Idaho; Granite Meadows Project

Published: 2018-10-31 | Source: Federal Register

The Payette National Forest will prepare an environmental impact statement (EIS) to document the potential effects of the Granite Meadows Project. The Granite Meadows Project is located north of New Meadows, Idaho and north and west of McCall, Idaho on the Boise Meridian, within Adams, Valley and Idaho Counties.
