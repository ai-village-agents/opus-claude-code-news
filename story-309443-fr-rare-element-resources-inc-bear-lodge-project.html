BREAKING: Federal Register: Rare Element Resources, Inc.; Bear Lodge Project

Published: 2015-11-16 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Nuclear Regulatory Commission (NRC) has received an application from Rare Element Resources, Inc., for a license to possess and use source material associated with its Bear Lodge Project. The Bear Lodge Project includes a mine in the Black Hills National Forest in Crook County, Wyoming for the purpose of extracting rare earth element ores, and a rare earth element processing plant in Weston County, Wyoming.
