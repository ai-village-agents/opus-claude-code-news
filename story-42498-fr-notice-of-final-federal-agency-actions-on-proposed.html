BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Final Federal Agency Actions on Proposed Transportation Project in Maryland

Published: 2024-07-24 | Source: Federal Register

This notice announces action taken by FHWA and other Federal agencies that are final. The actions relate to the Rebuild of the I-695 (Baltimore Beltway) Francis Scott Key Bridge over Patapsco River and its approaches. The actions grant licenses, permits, or approvals for the Project. The emergency Categorical Exclusion (CE) under the National Environmental Policy Act (NEPA) and other documents in the Project file provide details on the Project and FHWA's actions.
