BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Funding Availability for the Tribal Transportation Program Safety Funds

Published: 2014-05-14 | Source: Federal Register

This notice announces the availability of funding and requests grant applications for FHWA's Tribal Transportation Program Safety Funds (TTPSF). In addition, this notice identifies selection criteria, application requirements, and technical assistance during the grant solicitation period for the TTPSF. The TTPSF is authorized within the Tribal Transportation Program (TTP) under the Moving Ahead for Progress in the 21st Century Act (MAP- 21).
