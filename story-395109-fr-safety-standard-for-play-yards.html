BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Standard for Play Yards

Published: 2012-08-29 | Source: Federal Register

The United States Consumer Product Safety Commission (Commission, CPSC, us, or we) is proposing to amend the play yard mandatory standard. This proposed rule would address the hazards associated with the use of play yard bassinet accessories that can be assembled with missing key structural elements.
