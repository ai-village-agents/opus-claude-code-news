BREAKING: Federal Register: Security Zone; Watson Bayou, Panama City, FL

Published: 2023-10-23 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is establishing a temporary security zone for navigable waters of Watson Bayou, Panama City FL, within an area bound by the following points: 30[deg]08'17.8" N, 85[deg]38'6.6" W (Diamond Point), thence northeast toward 30[deg]08'34.6" N, 85[deg]37'55.7" W (Eastern Shipbuilding), thence east to the South East Avenue Bridge.
