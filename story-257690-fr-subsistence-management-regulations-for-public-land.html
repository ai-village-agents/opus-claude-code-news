BREAKING: Federal Register: Subsistence Management Regulations for Public Lands in Alaska; Rural Determinations, Nonrural List

Published: 2016-03-10 | Source: Federal Register

The Federal Subsistence Board is adopting, without change, a direct final rule that revised the list of areas in Alaska determined to be nonrural for purposes of the Federal Subsistence Program to the list that existed prior to 2007. Accordingly, the community of Saxman and the area of Prudhoe Bay were removed from the nonrural list.
