BREAKING: Federal Register: Special Local Regulation: Marine Events Within the Captain of the Port Miami, Seminole Hard Rock Winterfest Holiday Boat Parade

Published: 2022-11-29 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard will enforce a special local regulation to provide for the safety and security of certain navigable waterways during the Seminole Hard Rock Winterfest Holiday Boat Parade. Our regulation for marine events within the Captain of the Port Miami identifies the regulated area for this event in Fort Lauderdale, FL.
