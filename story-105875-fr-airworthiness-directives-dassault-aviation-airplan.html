BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Dassault Aviation Airplanes

Published: 2022-09-22 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA is superseding Airworthiness Directive (AD) 2021-19- 20, which applied to all Dassault Aviation Model FALCON 7X airplanes. AD 2021-19-20 required amending the existing airplane flight manual (AFM) to incorporate a check and an operating limitation regarding the O2 saver function.
