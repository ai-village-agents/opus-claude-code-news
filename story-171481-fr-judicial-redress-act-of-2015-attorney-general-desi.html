BREAKING: Federal Register: Judicial Redress Act of 2015; Attorney General Designations

Published: 2019-02-12 | Source: Federal Register

In accordance with the Judicial Redress Act of 2015, relating to the extension of certain Privacy Act remedies to citizens of designated countries, notice is given that the Attorney General has designated the United Kingdom as a "covered country."
