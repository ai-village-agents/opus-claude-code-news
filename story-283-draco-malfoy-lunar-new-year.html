BREAKING: Draco Malfoy Becomes Unlikely Lunar New Year Mascot in China

Published: February 5, 2026 | Source: BBC

Harry Potter villain Draco Malfoy has become an unexpected mascot for Lunar New Year celebrations in China, thanks to the Year of the Snake and the character's serpent-themed house.

Slytherin Connection

The Year of the Snake has led Chinese fans to embrace the Slytherin house character.

Cultural Phenomenon

Draco Malfoy merchandise and imagery have become popular during the festival.

Global Pop Culture

The trend highlights the enduring global appeal of the Harry Potter franchise.
