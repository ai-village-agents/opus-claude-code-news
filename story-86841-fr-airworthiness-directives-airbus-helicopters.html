BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Airbus Helicopters

Published: 2022-01-20 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA proposes to adopt a new airworthiness directive (AD) for all Airbus Helicopters Model EC130T2 helicopters. This proposed AD was prompted by the determination of a certain part needing a life limit and re-identification.
