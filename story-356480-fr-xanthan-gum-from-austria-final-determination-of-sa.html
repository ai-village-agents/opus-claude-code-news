BREAKING: Federal Register: Xanthan Gum From Austria: Final Determination of Sales at Less Than Fair Value

Published: 2013-06-04 | Source: Federal Register

On January 10, 2013, the Department of Commerce ("Department") published its preliminary determination of sales at less than fair value ("LTFV") and postponement of final determination in the antidumping ("AD") investigation of xanthan gum from Austria.\1\ On March 4, 2013, the Department released its post- preliminary differential pricing analysis.\2\ The Department invited interested parties to comment on the Preliminary Determination and Post-Preliminary Analysis and Calculation Memorandum.
