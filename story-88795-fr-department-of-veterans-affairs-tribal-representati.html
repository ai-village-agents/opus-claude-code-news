BREAKING: Federal Register: Department of Veterans Affairs' Tribal Representation Expansion Project

Published: 2022-02-14 | Source: Federal Register

REP)", a project through which VA strives to ensure that Native American Veterans have access to responsible, qualified representation in the preparation, presentation, and prosecution of their benefit claims before VA.
