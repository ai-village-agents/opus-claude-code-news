BREAKING: Federal Register: Genome in a Bottle Consortium-Progress and Planning Workshop

Published: 2015-01-22 | Source: Federal Register

NIST announces the Genome in a Bottle Consortium meeting to be held on Thursday and Friday, January 29 and 30, 2015. The Genome in a Bottle Consortium is developing the reference materials, reference methods, and reference data needed to assess confidence in human whole genome variant calls. A principal motivation for this consortium is to enable performance assessment of sequencing and science-based regulatory oversight of clinical sequencing.
