BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Solicitation of Applications for Inviting Applications for the Intermediary Relending Program for Fiscal Year 2023

Published: 2022-08-31 | Source: Federal Register

The Rural Business-Cooperative Service (Agency), an agency of the United States Department of Agriculture (USDA) invites applications under the Intermediary Relending Program (IRP) for fiscal year (FY) 2023, subject to availability of funding. This Notice is being issued prior to passage of a FY 2023 Appropriations Act in order to allow applicants enough time to leverage financing, prepare and submit their applications, and give the Agency time to process program applications within FY 2023.
