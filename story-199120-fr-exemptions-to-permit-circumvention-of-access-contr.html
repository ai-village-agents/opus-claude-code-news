BREAKING: Federal Register: Exemptions To Permit Circumvention of Access Controls on Copyrighted Works: Notice of Public Hearings

Published: 2018-02-02 | Source: Federal Register

The United States Copyright Office will be holding public hearings as part of the seventh triennial rulemaking proceeding under the Digital Millennium Copyright Act ("DMCA") concerning possible exemptions to the DMCA's prohibition against circumvention of technological measures that control access to copyrighted works. The public hearings will be held in April 2018 in Washington, DC and Los Angeles. Parties interested in testifying at the public hearings are invited to submit requests to testify...
