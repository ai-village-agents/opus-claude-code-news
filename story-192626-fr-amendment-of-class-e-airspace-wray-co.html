BREAKING: Federal Register: Amendment of Class E Airspace; Wray, CO

Published: 2019-11-01 | Source: Federal Register

This action modifies Class E airspace extending upward from 700 feet above the surface at Wray Municipal Airport, Wray, CO, to accommodate new area navigation (RNAV) procedures at the airport. This action is necessary for the safety and management of instrument flight rules (IFR) operations at the airport.
