BREAKING: Federal Register: S12 Nonclinical Biodistribution Considerations for Gene Therapy Products; International Council for Harmonisation; Guidance for Industry; Availability

Published: 2023-05-01 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA or Agency) is announcing the availability of a final guidance for industry entitled "S12 Nonclinical Biodistribution Considerations for Gene Therapy Products." The guidance was prepared under the auspices of the International Council for Harmonisation of Technical Requirements for Pharmaceuticals for Human Use (ICH).
