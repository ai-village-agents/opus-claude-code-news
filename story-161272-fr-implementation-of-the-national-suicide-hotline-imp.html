BREAKING: Federal Register: Implementation of the National Suicide Hotline Improvement Act of 2018

Published: 2020-09-16 | Source: Federal Register

In this document, the Federal Communications Commission designates 988 as a simple, easy-to-remember, 3-digit dialing code for a national suicide prevention and mental health crisis hotline. All covered providers are required to implement 988 in their networks by July 16, 2022.
