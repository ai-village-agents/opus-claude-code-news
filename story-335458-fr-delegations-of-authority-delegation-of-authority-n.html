BREAKING: Federal Register: Delegations of Authority: Delegation of Authority No. 12-G, Revision 1

Published: 2014-09-23 | Source: Federal Register

This document provides the public notice of a revision to Delegations of Authority No. 12-G (70 FR 21262, April 25, 2005), which delegated authority for lender oversight and enforcement activities by the Administrator of the Small Business Administration (SBA) to the Associate Administrator for the Office of Lender Oversight (AA/OLO), the Lender Oversight Committee, and the Associate Deputy Administrator for Capital Access (ADA/CA).
