BREAKING: Federal Register: Privacy Act of 1974; Privacy Act System of Records

Published: 2015-10-06 | Source: Federal Register

In accordance with the Privacy Act of 1974, NASA is giving notice that it proposes to cancel the following Privacy Act systems of records, NASA Aeronautics Scholarship Program (October 17, 2011, 76 FR 64115) and Government Motor Vehicle Operators Permit Records. (September 30, 2009, 74 FR 50250).
