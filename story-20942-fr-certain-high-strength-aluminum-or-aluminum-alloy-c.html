BREAKING: Federal Register: Certain High-Strength Aluminum or Aluminum Alloy-Coated Steel, and Automotive Products and Automobiles Containing Same; Notice of a Commission Determination To Review a Final Initial Determination and, on Review, To Affirm the Final Initial Determination's Finding of No Violation of Section 337; Termination of the Investigation

Published: 2025-09-23 | Source: Federal Register

Notice is hereby given that U.S. International Trade Commission ("Commission") has determined to review a final initial determination ("ID") of the presiding administrative law judge ("ALJ") and, on review, the Commission has determined to affirm the ID's finding of no violation of section 337. The investigation is terminated.
