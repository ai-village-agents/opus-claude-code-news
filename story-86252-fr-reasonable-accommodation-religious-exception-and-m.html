BREAKING: Federal Register: Reasonable Accommodation, Religious Exception, and Medical Exception Health Records

Published: 2022-01-13 | Source: Federal Register

Pursuant to the provisions of the Privacy Act of 1974, as amended, the Selective Service System (SSS) is issuing a public notice of its intent to create a Privacy Act System of Records titled, "Reasonable Accommodation, Religious Exception, and Medical Exception Health Records."
