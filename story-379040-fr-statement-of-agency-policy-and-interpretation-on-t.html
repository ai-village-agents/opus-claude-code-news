BREAKING: Federal Register: Statement of Agency Policy and Interpretation on the Hours of Service Laws as Amended; Response to Public Comment

Published: 2012-02-29 | Source: Federal Register

In this document FRA states the agency's position on certain interpretive questions arising out of some of the complex and important amendments enacted in 2008 to the Federal railroad safety laws that govern such matters as how long a railroad may require or allow an employee in a certain category to remain on duty and how long the railroad must give the employee off duty before the employee may go on duty again.
