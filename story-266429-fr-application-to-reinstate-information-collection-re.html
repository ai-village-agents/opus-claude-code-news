BREAKING: Federal Register: Application To Reinstate Information Collection Request OMB No. 2105-0566

Published: 2016-06-17 | Source: Federal Register

In compliance with the Paperwork Reduction Act of 1995 (44 U.S.C. 3501 et seq.), this notice announces that the request for reinstatement of an OMB Control Number for the Information Collection Request (ICR) abstracted below is being forwarded to the Office of Management and Budget (OMB) for review and comments. A Federal Register Notice with a 60-day comment period soliciting comments on the following information collection was published on June 2, 2015 (80 FR 31455).
