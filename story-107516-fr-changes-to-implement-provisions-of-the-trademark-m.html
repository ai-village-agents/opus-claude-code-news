BREAKING: Federal Register: Changes To Implement Provisions of the Trademark Modernization Act of 2020; Delay of Effective Date and Correction

Published: 2022-10-13 | Source: Federal Register

On November 17, 2021, the United States Patent and Trademark Office (USPTO or Office) published in the Federal Register a final rule amending its regulations to implement provisions of the Trademark Modernization Act of 2020 (TMA). This action changes the effective date for the regulations published in the November 17, 2021, final rule that established new Office action response periods and set fees for requests to extend Office action response deadlines.
