BREAKING: Federal Register: Fisheries off West Coast States; Pacific Coast Groundfish Fishery; Electronic Monitoring Program

Published: 2019-06-28 | Source: Federal Register

NMFS issues this final rule to implement an electronic monitoring (EM) program for two sectors of the limited entry trawl fishery, consistent with the Magnuson-Stevens Fishery Conservation and Management Act (MSA) and the Pacific Coast Groundfish Fishery Management Plan (FMP).
