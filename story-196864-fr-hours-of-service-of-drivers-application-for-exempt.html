BREAKING: Federal Register: Hours of Service of Drivers: Application for Exemption; Owner Operator Independent Drivers Association, Inc.

Published: 2018-01-02 | Source: Federal Register

FMCSA announces that the Owner Operator Independent Drivers Association, Inc. (OOIDA) has requested an exemption from the electronic logging device (ELD) requirements for motor carriers considered to be a small transportation trucking business.
