BREAKING: Federal Register: Standard Review Plan for Spent Fuel Transportation

Published: 2019-08-16 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Nuclear Regulatory Commission (NRC) requests public comment on draft NUREG-2216, Revision 0, "Standard Review Plan for Spent Fuel Transportation". This Standard Review Plan (SRP) provides guidance to NRC staff for reviewing an application for package approval. NRC approval of a package design typically results in issuance of a certificate of compliance (CoC) or a letter amendment for a transportation package.
