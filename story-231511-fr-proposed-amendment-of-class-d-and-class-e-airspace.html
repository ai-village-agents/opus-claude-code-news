BREAKING: Federal Register: Proposed Amendment of Class D and Class E Airspace; Morgantown, WV

Published: 2017-04-07 | Source: Federal Register

This action proposes to amend Class E airspace designated as an extension to Class D airspace at Morgantown, WV, by removing Notice to Airmen (NOTAM) part-time status at Morgantown Municipal Airport-- Walter L. Bill Hart Field, and would amend the airport's geographic coordinates. The geographic coordinates of the airport in Class D airspace, Class E surface area airspace and Class E 700 foot airspace also would be adjusted.
