BREAKING: Federal Register: Approval and Promulgation of Implementation Plans; Tennessee; Transportation Conformity Revisions

Published: 2013-05-17 | Source: Federal Register

EPA is proposing to approve a State Implementation Plan (SIP) revision submitted by the State of Tennessee, through the Tennessee Department of Environment and Conservation, Bureau of Environment, Air Pollution Control Division, on July 12, 2012. This revision consists of updates to transportation conformity criteria and procedures related to interagency consultation and enforceability of certain transportation-related control and mitigation measures.
