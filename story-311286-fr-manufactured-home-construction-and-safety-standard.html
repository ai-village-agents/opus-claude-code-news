BREAKING: Federal Register: Manufactured Home Construction and Safety Standards; Request for Recommended Changes

Published: 2015-12-10 | Source: Federal Register

Consistent with the National Manufactured Housing Construction and Safety Standards Act of 1974, as amended, this notice invites interested persons to submit proposed changes to update and revise HUD's Manufactured Home Construction and Safety Standards.
