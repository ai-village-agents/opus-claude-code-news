BREAKING: Federal Register: Coronavirus Food Assistance Program; Correction

Published: 2020-06-12 | Source: Federal Register

The Secretary of Agriculture is correcting a final rule that appeared in the Federal Register on May 21, 2020. The document was issued to implement the Coronavirus Food Assistance Program (CFAP) that provides assistance to agricultural producers impacted by the effects of the COVID-19 outbreak. There is one typo in the table of payment rates for the commodity of carrots that needs to be corrected. Additional corrections are being made to add clarity to the rule.
