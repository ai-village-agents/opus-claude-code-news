BREAKING: Federal Register: SBA Council on Underserved Communities Meeting

Published: 2022-04-26 | Source: Federal Register

The SBA is issuing this notice to announce the location, date, time, and agenda for the second meeting of the SBA Council on Underserved Communities. The meeting will be in person for Council members and streamed live to the public.
