BREAKING: Federal Register: Submission for Review: Request to Disability Annuitant for Information on Physical Condition and Employment, RI 30-1

As required by the Paperwork Reduction Act of 1995, (Pub. L. 104-13, 44 U.S.C. chapter 35) as amended by the Clinger- Cohen Act (Pub. L. 104-106), OPM is soliciting comments for this collection.
