BREAKING: Federal Register: Special Local Regulations; Safety Zones; Recurring Events in Captain of the Port Long Island Sound Zone

Published: 2013-06-10 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard will enforce seventeen annual fireworks display safety zones and one regatta event special local regulation in the Sector Long Island Sound area of responsibility on various dates and times listed in the tables below. This action is necessary to provide for the safety of life on navigable waterways during these fireworks displays and regatta event.
