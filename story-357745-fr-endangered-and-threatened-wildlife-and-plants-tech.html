BREAKING: Federal Register: Endangered and Threatened Wildlife and Plants; Technical Agency Draft Recovery Plan for Golden Sedge

Published: 2013-06-18 | Source: Federal Register

We, the Fish and Wildlife Service, announce the availability of the technical agency draft recovery plan for the endangered golden sedge, a species endemic to the coastal plain in North Carolina. The draft recovery plan includes specific recovery objectives and criteria to be met in order to downlist this species to threatened or delist it under the Endangered Species Act of 1973, as amended (Act).
