BREAKING: Federal Register: Drawbridge Operation Regulation; Rancocas Creek, Centerton, NJ

Published: 2015-07-06 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard proposes to change the regulation that governs the operation of the SR#38 Bridge in Centerton (Burlington County Route 635) over Rancocas Creek, mile 7.8, at Mt. Laurel, Westampton and Willingboro Townships in Burlington County, NJ. The proposed rule intends to change the current operating regulation and allow the bridge to remain in the closed position for the passage of vessels.
