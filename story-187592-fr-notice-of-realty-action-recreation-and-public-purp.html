BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Realty Action: Recreation and Public Purposes Act Classification: Nevada

Published: 2019-08-30 | Source: Federal Register

The Bureau of Land Management (BLM) has examined certain public lands in Clark County, Nevada, and has found them suitable for classification for lease or conveyance to Clark County School District under the provisions of the Recreation and Public Purposes (R&PP) Act, as amended, Sec. 7 of the Taylor Grazing Act, and Executive Order No. 6910.
