BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone; Homewood Wedding Fireworks Display, Homewood, CA

Published: 2022-07-18 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is establishing a temporary safety zone on the navigable waters of Lake Tahoe, near Homewood, CA, in support of the Homewood Wedding Fireworks display on July 23, 2022. This safety zone is necessary to protect personnel, vessels, and the marine environment from the dangers associated with pyrotechnics.
