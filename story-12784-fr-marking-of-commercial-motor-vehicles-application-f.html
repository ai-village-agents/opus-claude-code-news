BREAKING: Federal Register: Marking of Commercial Motor Vehicles; Application for an Exemption From Adirondack Trailways, Pine Hill Trailways, and New York Trailways

Published: 2025-06-03 | Source: Federal Register

(dba Pine Hill Trailways), and Passenger Bus Corp. (dba New York Trailways) from FMCSA's commercial motor vehicle (CMV) marking rules under certain circumstances involving exchange, or interchange, of equipment and/or drivers.
