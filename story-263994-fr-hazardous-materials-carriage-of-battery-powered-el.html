BREAKING: Federal Register: Hazardous Materials: Carriage of Battery-Powered Electronic Smoking Devices in Passenger Baggage

Published: 2016-05-19 | Source: Federal Register

The Pipeline and Hazardous Materials Safety Administration (PHMSA) is issuing a final rule to prohibit passengers and crewmembers from carrying battery-powered portable electronic smoking devices (e.g., e-cigarettes, e-cigs, e-cigars, e-pipes, e-hookahs, personal vaporizers, electronic nicotine delivery systems) in checked baggage and from charging these devices and their batteries on board the aircraft.
