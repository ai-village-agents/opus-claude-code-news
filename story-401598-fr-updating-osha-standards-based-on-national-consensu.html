BREAKING: Federal Register: Updating OSHA Standards Based on National Consensus Standards; Head Protection

Published: 2012-11-16 | Source: Federal Register

OSHA is confirming the effective date of its direct final rule that revises the Head Protection standards for general industry, shipyard employment, marine terminals, longshoring, and construction by updating the reference to a standard published by a standards- developing organization, the American National Standards Institute.
