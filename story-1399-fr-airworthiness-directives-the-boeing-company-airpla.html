BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; The Boeing Company Airplanes

Published: 2026-01-26 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA proposes to supersede Airworthiness Directive (AD) 2018-11-14, which applies to certain The Boeing Company Model 767-300 and -300F series airplanes. AD 2018-11-14 requires high frequency eddy current (HFEC) inspections for cracking of the lower outboard wing skin, and repair or modification if necessary.
