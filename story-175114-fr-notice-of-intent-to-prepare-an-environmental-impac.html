BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Intent To Prepare an Environmental Impact Statement for Basing of the Permanent F-22 Formal Training Unit

Published: 2019-03-26 | Source: Federal Register

The Air Force is issuing this notice to advise the public of its intent to prepare an Environmental Impact Statement (EIS) for the proposed action to permanently beddown its F-22 Formal Training Unit (FTU).
