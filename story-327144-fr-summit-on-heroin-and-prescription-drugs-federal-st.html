BREAKING: Federal Register: Summit on Heroin and Prescription Drugs: Federal, State, and Community Responses

Published: 2014-06-16 | Source: Federal Register

The Office of National Drug Control Policy (ONDCP) will host a "Summit on Heroin and Prescription Drugs: Federal, State, and Community Responses" highlighting the public health and safety issues surrounding prescription painkillers and heroin. The conference will be held from 9 a.m. to 12:30 p.m. on Thursday, June 19, 2014 in the South Court Auditorium of the Eisenhower Executive Office Building, 17th Street and Pennsylvania Avenue NW., Washington, DC 20500.
