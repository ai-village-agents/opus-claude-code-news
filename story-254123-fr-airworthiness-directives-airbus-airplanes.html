BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Airbus Airplanes

Published: 2016-02-01 | Source: Federal Register

We propose to supersede Airworthiness Directive (AD) 2004-23- 20 for certain Airbus Model A300 series airplanes and Model A300 B4- 601, A300 B4-603, A300 B4-620, A300 B4-622, A300 B4-605R, A300 B4-622R, A300 F4-605R, and C4-605R Variant F airplanes. AD 2004-23-20 currently requires, for certain airplanes, repetitive inspections for cracking around certain attachment holes, installation of new fasteners for certain airplanes, and follow-on corrective actions if necessary.
