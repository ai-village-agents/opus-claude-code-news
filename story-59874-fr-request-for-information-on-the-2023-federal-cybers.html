BREAKING: Federal Register: Request for Information on the 2023 Federal Cybersecurity Research and Development Strategic Plan

Published: 2023-02-07 | Source: Federal Register

Pursuant to the Cybersecurity Enhancement Act of 2014, Federal agencies must update the Federal cybersecurity research and development (R&D) strategic plan every four years. The NITRD NCO seeks public input for the 2023 update of the Federal cybersecurity R&D strategic plan.
