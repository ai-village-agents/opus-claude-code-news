BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Thielert Aircraft Engines GmbH (TAE) Reciprocating Engines

Published: 2012-03-07 | Source: Federal Register

We are revising an existing airworthiness directive (AD) for TAE models TAE 125-01 and TAE 125-02-99 reciprocating engines installed on, but not limited to, Diamond Aircraft Industries Model DA 42 airplanes. That AD currently requires initial and repetitive replacements of proportional pressure reducing valves (PPRVs) (also known as propeller control valves).
