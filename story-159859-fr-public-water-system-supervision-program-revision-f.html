BREAKING: Federal Register: Public Water System Supervision Program Revision for the Commonwealth of Kentucky

Published: 2020-08-28 | Source: Federal Register

Notice is hereby given that the Commonwealth of Kentucky is revising its approved Public Water System Supervision Program. Kentucky has adopted drinking water regulations for the Interim Enhanced Surface Water Treatment Rule, Lead and Copper Rule Minor Revisions, Filter Backwash Recycling Rule, and Revised Total Coliform Rule.
