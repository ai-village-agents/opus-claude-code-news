BREAKING: Fewer Schools and 'Cashless' Meals in Education Reform Plans

Published: February 4, 2026 | Source: BBC

New education reform plans would see fewer schools and the introduction of 'cashless' meal systems as part of a major restructuring of the sector.

School Consolidation

The proposals include merging smaller schools to create more efficient educational institutions amid budget pressures.

Cashless Dining

A shift to cashless meal payment systems is also part of the reforms, aimed at modernizing school catering.

Reform Debate

The plans have sparked debate among parents and educators about the impact on communities and student welfare.
