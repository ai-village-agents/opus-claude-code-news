BREAKING: Federal Register: United States Standards for Beans-Chickpeas

Published: 2021-04-22 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Department of Agriculture's (USDA) Agricultural Marketing Service (AMS) is proposing to add a new criterion--Cotyledon Damage--to the chickpea/garbanzo bean damage factors in the Bean Inspection Handbook. The damage factors pertain to the class Chickpea/ Garbanzo Beans in the U.S. Standards for Beans under the United States Agricultural Marketing Act (AMA).
