BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Availability of Guidance on Accelerated Decisionmaking in Environmental Reviews

Published: 2014-11-13 | Source: Federal Register

DOT is announcing the availability of guidance implementing Section 1319 of MAP-21, on Accelerated Decisionmaking in Environmental Reviews. This section of MAP-21 provides for the use of errata sheets on the Draft Environmental Impact Statement, in lieu of a Final EIS (FEIS), when the EIS has limited or factual changes.
