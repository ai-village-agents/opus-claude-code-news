BREAKING: Federal Register: Annual Marine Events in the Eighth Coast Guard District, Sabine River; Orange, TX

Published: 2012-08-09 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard will enforce Special Local Regulations for the S.P.O.R.T. Power Boat Neches River in Orange, TX from 3 p.m. on September 21, 2012, through 6 p.m. on September 23, 2012. This action is necessary to provide for the safety of the participants, crew, spectators, participating vessels, non-participating vessels and other users of the waterway.
