BREAKING: Federal Register: Amendments to the Pale Cyst Nematode Regulations

Published: 2020-12-29 | Source: Federal Register

We are amending the domestic quarantine regulations for pale cyst nematode by adding procedures that allow persons to review and comment on the protocols for regulating and deregulating quarantined and associated areas. As part of this action, we have made the protocols available online.
