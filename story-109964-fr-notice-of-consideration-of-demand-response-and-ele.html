BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Consideration of Demand Response and Electric Vehicle Standards

Published: 2022-11-15 | Source: Federal Register

The Tennessee Valley Authority (TVA) is considering adopting for itself and the distributors of TVA power certain demand response and electric vehicle standards. The standards being considered are the Demand Response Practices (hereinafter "Demand Response") and Electric Vehicle Charging Programs standards (hereinafter "Electric Vehicles") listed in the Public Utility Regulatory Act of 1978, as amended by the Infrastructure Investment and Jobs Act.
