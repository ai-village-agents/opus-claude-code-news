BREAKING: Federal Register: Remittance Transfers Under the Electronic Fund Transfer Act (Regulation E)

Published: 2020-06-05 | Source: Federal Register

The Electronic Fund Transfer Act, as amended by the Dodd-Frank Wall Street Reform and Consumer Protection Act, establishes certain protections for consumers sending international money transfers, or remittance transfers. The Bureau of Consumer Financial Protection's (Bureau) remittance rule in Regulation E (Remittance Rule or Rule) implements these protections.
