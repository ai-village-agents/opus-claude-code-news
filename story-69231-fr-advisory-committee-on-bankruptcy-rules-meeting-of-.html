BREAKING: Federal Register: Advisory Committee on Bankruptcy Rules; Meeting of the Judicial Conference

Published: 2023-06-06 | Source: Federal Register

The Advisory Committee on Bankruptcy Rules will hold a meeting in a hybrid format with remote attendance options on September 14, 2023 in Washington, DC. The meeting is open to the public for observation but not participation. An agenda and supporting materials will be posted at least 7 days in advance of the meeting at: https:// www.uscourts.gov/rules-policies/records-and-archives-rules-committees/ agenda-books.
