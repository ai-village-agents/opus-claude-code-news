BREAKING: Federal Register: Direct Single Family Housing Loans and Grants

Published: 2014-05-20 | Source: Federal Register

The Rural Housing Service is amending its regulations for the section 502 direct single family housing loans program by reinstating language pertaining to payment assistance method 1 that was inadvertently changed or omitted when the payment subsidy regulation was revised on December 27, 2007.
