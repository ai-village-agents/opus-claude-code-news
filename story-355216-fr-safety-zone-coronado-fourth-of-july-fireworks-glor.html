BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone; Coronado Fourth of July Fireworks, Glorietta Bay; Coronado, CA

Published: 2013-05-17 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard proposes a temporary safety zone for the Coronado Fourth of July Fireworks from 8:45 p.m. to 10 p.m. on July 4, 2013. This regulated area encompasses the navigable waters of Glorietta Bay in Coronado, CA in support of Coronado Fourth of July Fireworks display. This action is necessary to provide for the safety of the participants, crew, spectators, participating vessels, and other vessels and users of the waterway.
