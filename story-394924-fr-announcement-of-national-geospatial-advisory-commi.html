BREAKING: Federal Register: Announcement of National Geospatial Advisory Committee Meeting

Published: 2012-08-28 | Source: Federal Register

The National Geospatial Advisory Committee (NGAC) will meet on September 18-19, 2012 at the American Institute of Architects Building, 1735 New York Avenue NW., Washington, DC 20006. The meeting will be held in the Gallery Room.
