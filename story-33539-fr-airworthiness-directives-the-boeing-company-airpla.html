BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; The Boeing Company Airplanes

Published: 2024-04-03 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA is adopting a new airworthiness directive (AD) for certain The Boeing Company Model 757-200, -200CB, and -300 series airplanes. This AD was prompted by a report of a crack at fuselage station (STA) 1640 frame web common to the lower hinge intercostal tee clip center hole of the upper fastener row.
