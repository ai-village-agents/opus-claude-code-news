BREAKING: Federal Register: Hazardous Materials: Harmonization With International Standards (RRR)

Published: 2016-09-07 | Source: Federal Register

The Pipeline and Hazardous Materials Safety Administration (PHMSA) proposes to amend the Hazardous Materials Regulations (HMR) to maintain consistency with international regulations and standards by incorporating various amendments, including changes to proper shipping names, hazard classes, packing groups, special provisions, packaging authorizations, air transport quantity limitations, and vessel stowage requirements.
