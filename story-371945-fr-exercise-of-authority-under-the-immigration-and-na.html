BREAKING: Federal Register: Exercise of Authority Under the Immigration and Nationality Act; Correction

Published: 2013-12-05 | Source: Federal Register

On November 4, 2013, DHS published a notice in the Federal Register, announcing a Secretarial determination under section 212(d)(3)(B)(i) of the Immigration and Nationality Act, 8 U.S.C. 1182(d)(3)(B)(i), as amended. DHS inadvertently omitted three words from that notice. With this document, DHS is making three typographical corrections to that notice: inserting the words "alien," "in," and "or."
