BREAKING: Federal Register: Special Local Regulation; Great Western Tube Float; Colorado River, Parker, AZ

Published: 2020-06-22 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is changing the location of the special local regulation for the annual Great Western Tube Float, which is held on the navigable waters of the Colorado River in Parker, AZ. The change of the location for the special local regulation is necessary to provide for the safety of life on the navigable waters during the event.
