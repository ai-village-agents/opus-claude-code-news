BREAKING: Federal Register: Implementation of the Administrative False Claims Act

Published: 2025-11-03 | Source: Federal Register

This final rule updates the Department of Homeland Security (DHS) administrative procedures for assessing penalties and recovering funds procured by fraud under departmental programs. This rule implements the Administrative False Claims Act of 1986, as amended by the National Defense Authorization Act (NDAA) for Fiscal Year 2025.
