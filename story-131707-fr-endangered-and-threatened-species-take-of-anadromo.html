BREAKING: Federal Register: Endangered and Threatened Species; Take of Anadromous Fish

Published: 2021-08-27 | Source: Federal Register

Notice is hereby given that NMFS has received five plans for hatchery programs rearing and releasing Chinook salmon, coho salmon, and sockeye salmon in the Lake Washington basin. The plans describe hatchery programs operated by the Muckleshoot Indian Tribe and Washington Department of Fish and Wildlife (WDFW).
