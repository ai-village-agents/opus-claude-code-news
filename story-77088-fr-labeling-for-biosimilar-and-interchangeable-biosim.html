BREAKING: Federal Register: Labeling for Biosimilar and Interchangeable Biosimilar Products; Draft Guidance for Industry; Availability

Published: 2023-09-18 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA or Agency) is announcing the availability of a draft guidance for industry entitled "Labeling for Biosimilar and Interchangeable Biosimilar Products." This draft guidance is intended to help applicants develop draft labeling for proposed biosimilar and interchangeable biosimilar products.
