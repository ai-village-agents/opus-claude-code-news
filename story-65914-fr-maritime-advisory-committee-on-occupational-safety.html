BREAKING: Federal Register: Maritime Advisory Committee on Occupational Safety and Health (MACOSH); Notice of Meeting

Published: 2023-04-25 | Source: Federal Register

The Maritime Advisory Committee on Occupational Safety and Health (MACOSH) will meet on May 23 and 24, 2023, in a hybrid format. Committee members will meet in person, while the public is invited to participate either in person or virtually via WebEx.
