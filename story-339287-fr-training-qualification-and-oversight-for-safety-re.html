BREAKING: Federal Register: Training, Qualification, and Oversight for Safety-Related Railroad Employees

Published: 2014-11-07 | Source: Federal Register

FRA is establishing minimum training standards for all safety- related railroad employees, as required by the Rail Safety Improvement Act of 2008 (RSIA). The final rule requires each railroad or contractor that employs one or more safety-related railroad employee to develop and submit a training program to FRA for approval and to designate the minimum training qualifications for each occupational category of employee.
