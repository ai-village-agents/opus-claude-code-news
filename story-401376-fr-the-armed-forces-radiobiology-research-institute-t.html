BREAKING: Federal Register: The Armed Forces Radiobiology Research Institute TRIGA Reactor: Facility Operating License No. R-84

Published: 2012-11-15 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Nuclear Regulatory Commission (NRC) is considering an application for the renewal of Facility Operating License No. R-84 (Application), which currently authorizes the Armed Forces Radiobiology Research Institute (the licensee) to operate the Armed Forces Radiobiology Research Institute (AFFRI) TRIGA Reactor at a maximum steady-state thermal power of 1.1 megawatts (MW).
