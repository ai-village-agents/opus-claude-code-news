BREAKING: Federal Register: Universal Identifier and System of Award Management; Corrections

Published: 2015-09-10 | Source: Federal Register

The Office of Management and Budget (OMB) is correcting the final guidance that appeared in the Federal Register on September 14, 2010 (75 FR 55673) and December 26, 2013 (78 FR 78589). OMB is amending the guidance to make technical corrections where needed. The final guidance is revised to remove references to the "System of Award Management" and replace them with the correct term "System for Award Management".
