BREAKING: Federal Register: Determination That NASONEX (Mometasone Furoate) Nasal Spray, 0.05 Milligram/Spray (50 Microgram), Was Not Withdrawn From Sale for Reasons of Safety or Effectiveness

Published: 2022-04-08 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA, Agency, or we) has determined that NASONEX (mometasone furoate) nasal spray, 0.05 milligram (mg)/spray (50 microgram (mcg)), was not withdrawn from sale for reasons of safety or effectiveness.
