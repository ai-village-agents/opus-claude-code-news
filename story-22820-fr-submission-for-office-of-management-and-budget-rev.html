BREAKING: Federal Register: Submission for Office of Management and Budget Review; Caseload Reduction Documentation Process

Published: 2025-11-20 | Source: Federal Register

The Administration for Children and Families (ACF) is requesting a three-year extension of the form ACF-202: Caseload Reduction Report (Office of Management and Budget (OMB) #0970-0338, expiration October 31, 2026). There are substantive changes requested to the instructions and form.
