BREAKING: Federal Register: Special Local Regulation; Grand Haven Coast Guard Festival Waterski Show, Grand Haven, MI

Published: 2018-07-17 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is amending a special local regulation for certain waters of the Grand River at Waterfront Stadium in Grand Haven, MI. This action is necessary and is intended to ensure safety of life on navigable waters to be used for a waterski show. This action will prohibit persons or vessels from entering certain waters immediately prior to, during, and immediately after the marine event.
