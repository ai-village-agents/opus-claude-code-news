BREAKING: Federal Register: Environmental Impact Statement-Transmission System Vegetation Management Program

Published: 2017-01-23 | Source: Federal Register

The Tennessee Valley Authority ("TVA") intends to prepare an Environmental Impact Statement ("EIS") to address the management of vegetation on its transmission system. In order to ensure that electric service to the public is not disrupted by outages on its transmission lines, TVA must control the vegetation on about 260,000 acres of the rights of way ("ROW") for those lines.
