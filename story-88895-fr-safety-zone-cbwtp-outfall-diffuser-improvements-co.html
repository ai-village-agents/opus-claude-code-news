BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone; CBWTP Outfall Diffuser Improvements, Columbia River, Portland, OR

Published: 2022-02-15 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is amending a temporary final rule currently establishing a temporary safety zone for certain waters of the Columbia River. This temporary rule extends the duration of the temporary safety zone an additional 15 days. This action is necessary to provide for the safety of life on these navigable waters near Portland, OR, at Columbia River Mile 105.6 during construction through the newly anticipated end date of March 15, 2022.
