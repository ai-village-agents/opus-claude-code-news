BREAKING: Federal Register: Security Zones; Corpus Christi Ship Channel, Corpus Christi, TX

Published: 2021-09-30 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is establishing two 500-yard radius temporary moving security zones around Liquefied Natural Gas Carriers M/V GASLOG WARSAW and M/V CELSIUS CANBERRA within the Corpus Christi Ship Channel and La Quinta Channel. The security zones are needed to protect personnel, vessels, and facilities from sabotage or other subversive acts, accidents, or other events of a similar nature.
