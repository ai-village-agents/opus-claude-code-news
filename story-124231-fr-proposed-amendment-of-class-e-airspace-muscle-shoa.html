BREAKING: Federal Register: Proposed Amendment of Class E Airspace; Muscle Shoals, AL

Published: 2021-05-20 | Source: Federal Register

This action proposes to amend Class E airspace extending upward from 700 feet above the surface in Muscle Shoals, AL, due to the decommissioning of the Muscle Shoals Very High Frequency Omni- Directional Radio Range Tactical Air Navigation Aid (VORTAC), and cancellation of the associated approach at Northwest Alabama Regional Airport.
