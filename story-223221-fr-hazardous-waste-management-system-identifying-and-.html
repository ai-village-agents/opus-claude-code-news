BREAKING: Federal Register: Hazardous Waste Management System; Identifying and Listing Hazardous Waste Exclusion

Published: 2018-12-14 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) (also, "the Agency" in this preamble) is granting a petition submitted by Sandvik Special Metals (Sandvik), in Kennewick, Washington to exclude (or "delist") up to 1,500 cubic yards of F006 wastewater treatment sludge per year from the list of Federal hazardous wastes.
