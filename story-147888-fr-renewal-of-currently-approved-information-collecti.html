BREAKING: Federal Register: Renewal of Currently Approved Information Collection; Comment Request; The Environmental Questionnaire and Checklist (EQC)

Published: 2020-03-30 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Commerce, as part of its continuing effort to reduce paperwork and respondent burden, invites the general public and other Federal agencies to comment on continuing the Environmental Questionnaire and Checklist, as required by the Paperwork Reduction Act of 1995. The purpose of this notice is to allow for 60 days of public comment.
