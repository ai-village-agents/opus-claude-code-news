BREAKING: Federal Register: Renewal of the MCC Economic Advisory Council and Call for Nominations

Published: 2022-10-11 | Source: Federal Register

In accordance with the requirements of the Federal Advisory Committee Act, the Millennium Challenge Corporation (MCC) has renewed the charter for the MCC Economic Advisory Council ("EAC") and is hereby soliciting representative nominations for the 2022-2024 term. The EAC serves MCC in an advisory capacity only and provides insight to sharpen MCC's analytical capacity and ensure continued expertise on relevant issues related to economic development.
