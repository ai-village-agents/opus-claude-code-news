BREAKING: Federal Register: Value Engineering

Published: 2013-08-29 | Source: Federal Register

The FHWA proposes to update the existing value engineering (VE) regulations to make the regulations consistent with the statutory changes in the Moving Ahead for Progress in the 21st Century Act (MAP- 21) and to make other non-substantive changes for clarity.
