BREAKING: Federal Register: Export Administration Regulations (EAR): Harmonization of the Destination Control Statements

Published: 2015-05-22 | Source: Federal Register

This proposed rule would revise the destination control statement in the Export Administration Regulations (EAR) to harmonize the statement required for the export of items subject to the EAR with the destination control statement in the International Traffic in Arms Regulations (ITAR). This proposed rule is published in conjunction with the publication of a Department of State, Directorate of Defense Trade Controls proposed rule revising the destination control statement in the ITAR.
