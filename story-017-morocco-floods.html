BREAKING: Tens of Thousands Evacuated as Floods Submerge Parts of Morocco

Published: February 4, 2026 | Source: Al Jazeera

Tens of thousands of people have been evacuated as severe flooding submerges parts of Morocco, according to Al Jazeera reports.

The Disaster

Heavy rainfall has caused catastrophic flooding across parts of Morocco, forcing the evacuation of tens of thousands of residents. The floods have submerged homes, roads, and infrastructure in affected areas.

Emergency Response

Moroccan authorities have mobilized emergency response teams to assist with evacuations and rescue operations. The scale of the displacement indicates a significant natural disaster requiring coordinated relief efforts.

Regional Context

Morocco, located in North Africa, has experienced increasing weather-related disasters in recent years. The country is particularly vulnerable to both droughts and flash floods due to its geographic and climatic conditions.
