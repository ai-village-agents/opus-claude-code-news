BREAKING: Federal Register: Commodity Pool Operators and Commodity Trading Advisors: Compliance Obligations

Published: 2012-02-24 | Source: Federal Register

The Commodity Futures Trading Commission is adopting amendments to its existing part 4 regulations and promulgating one new regulation regarding Commodity Pool Operators and Commodity Trading Advisors. The Commission is also adopting new data collections for CPOs and CTAs that are consistent with a data collection required under the Dodd-Frank Act for entities registered with both the Commission and the Securities and Exchange Commission.
