BREAKING: Federal Register: Executive Order 13789-Second Report to the President on Identifying and Reducing Tax Regulatory Burdens

Published: 2017-10-16 | Source: Federal Register

On April 21, 2017, the President issued Executive Order 13789 (82 FR 19317), a directive designed to reduce tax regulatory burdens. The order directed the Secretary of the Treasury to identify significant tax regulations issued on or after January 1, 2016, that impose an undue financial burden on U.S. taxpayers, add undue complexity to the Federal tax laws, or exceed the statutory authority of the Internal Revenue Service (IRS).
