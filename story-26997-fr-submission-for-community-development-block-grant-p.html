BREAKING: Federal Register: Submission for Community Development Block Grant Program, Consolidated Plans, and Indian Community Development Block Grant Program Changes

Published: 2024-01-10 | Source: Federal Register

HUD is proposing to revise the Community Development Block Grant (CDBG) and related Section 108 loan guarantee program regulations to make it easier for recipients to promote economic development and recovery in low- and moderate-income communities and support investments in underserved areas.
