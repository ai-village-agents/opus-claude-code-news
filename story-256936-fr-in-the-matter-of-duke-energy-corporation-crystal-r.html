BREAKING: Federal Register: In The Matter of Duke Energy Corporation, Crystal River Nuclear Generating Station, Independent Spent Fuel Storage Installation: Order Modifying License (Effective Immediately)

Published: 2016-03-03 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Nuclear Regulatory Commission (NRC) has issued a general license to the Duke Energy Corporation (Duke), authorizing the operation of the Crystal River Nuclear Generating Plant Independent Spent Fuel Storage Installation (ISFSI), in accordance with its regulations. The Order is being issued to Duke to impose additional security requirements because Duke has identified near term plans to store spent fuel in an ISFSI under the general license provisions of the NRC's regulations.
