BREAKING: Federal Register: Privacy Act of 1974; New System of Records, Office of General Counsel E-Discovery Management System: Republication of System Description and Solicitation of Comment

This notice solicits additional comment on a new system of records for the Office of General Counsel (OGC) E- Discovery Management System (EDMS). EDMS was first announced and described in a July 17, 2012, Federal Register notice, in which HUD solicited comment.
