BREAKING: Federal Register: National Television Multiple Ownership Rule

Published: 2018-01-26 | Source: Federal Register

This Notice of Proposed Rulemaking (NPRM) initiates a comprehensive review of the national television audience reach cap, including the UHF discount used by broadcasters to determine compliance with the cap. The national cap limits entities from owning or controlling television stations that, together, reach more than 39 percent of the television households in the country.
