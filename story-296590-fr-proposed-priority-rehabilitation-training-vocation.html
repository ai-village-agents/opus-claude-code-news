BREAKING: Federal Register: Proposed Priority-Rehabilitation Training: Vocational Rehabilitation Workforce Innovation Technical Assistance Center

Published: 2015-06-17 | Source: Federal Register

The Assistant Secretary for Special Education and Rehabilitative Services proposes a priority to establish the Workforce Innovation Technical Assistance Center. The Assistant Secretary may use this priority for competitions in fiscal year (FY) 2015 and later years.
