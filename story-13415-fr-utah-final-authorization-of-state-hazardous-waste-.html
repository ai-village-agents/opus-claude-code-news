BREAKING: Federal Register: Utah: Final Authorization of State Hazardous Waste Management Program Revisions and Incorporation by Reference

Published: 2025-06-11 | Source: Federal Register

The Solid Waste Disposal Act, as amended, commonly referred to as the Resource Conservation and Recovery Act (RCRA), allows the Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) to authorize States to operate their hazardous waste management programs in lieu of the Federal program. Utah has applied to EPA for final authorization of the changes to its hazardous waste program under RCRA.
