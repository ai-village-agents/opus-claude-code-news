BREAKING: Federal Register: Defense Federal Acquisition Regulation Supplement: Cost and Software Data Reporting for Major Weapons Systems (2021-D028)

Published: 2024-10-10 | Source: Federal Register

DoD is proposing to amend the Defense Federal Acquisition Regulation Supplement (DFARS) to implement statutory and other policy updates made to the cost and software data reporting requirements for major systems.
