BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Applications for Deregistration Under Section 8(f) of the Investment Company Act of 1940

Published: 2017-08-02 | Source: Federal Register

Applicant, a closed-end investment company, seeks an order declaring that it has ceased to be an investment company. On June 19, 2017 and July 21, 2017, applicant made liquidating distributions to its shareholders, based on net asset value. Expenses of approximately $27,000 incurred in connection with the liquidation were paid by the applicant. Filing Dates: The application was filed on June 27, 2017 and amended on July 24, 2017.
