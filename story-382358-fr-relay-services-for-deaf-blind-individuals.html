BREAKING: Federal Register: Relay Services for Deaf-Blind Individuals

Published: 2012-04-05 | Source: Federal Register

In this document, the Commission conditionally waives the requirement for National Deaf Blind Equipment Distribution Program (NDBEDP) certified programs to submit reimbursement claims only once every six months, to permit certified programs to submit reimbursement claims as frequently as monthly.
