BREAKING: Federal Register: Third Party Billing for Medical Care Provided Under Special Treatment Authorities

Published: 2018-07-06 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Veterans Affairs (VA) is amending its medical regulations to clarify that VA will not bill third party payers for care and services provided by VA under certain statutory provisions, which we refer to as "special treatment authorities." These special treatment authorities direct VA to provide care and services to veterans based upon discrete exposures or experiences that occurred during active military, naval, or air service.
