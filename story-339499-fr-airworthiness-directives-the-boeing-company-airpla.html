BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; the Boeing Company Airplanes

Published: 2014-11-12 | Source: Federal Register

We are superseding Airworthiness Directive (AD) 2012-13-08 for certain the Boeing Company Model 747-100, 747-100B, 747-200B, 747-200C, 747-200F, 747-400F, 747SR, and 747SP series airplanes, without a stretched upper deck or stretched upper deck modification.
