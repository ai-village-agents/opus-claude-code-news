BREAKING: Federal Register: Patient-Focused Drug Development on Chronic Pain; Public Meeting; Request for Comments

Published: 2018-05-15 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA, the Agency, or we) is announcing a public meeting and an opportunity for public comment on "Patient-Focused Drug Development for Chronic Pain." The public meeting will provide patients (including adult and pediatric patients) with an opportunity to present to FDA their perspectives on the impacts of chronic pain, views on treatment approaches for chronic pain, and challenges or barriers to accessing treatments.
