BREAKING: Federal Register: Importation of Horses From Contagious Equine Metritis-Affected Countries

Published: 2013-02-11 | Source: Federal Register

We are adopting as a final rule, with changes, an interim rule that amended the regulations regarding the importation of horses from countries affected with contagious equine metritis (CEM) by incorporating an additional certification requirement for imported horses 731 days of age or less and adding new testing protocols for test mares and imported stallions and mares more than 731 days of age.
