BREAKING: Federal Register: Advanced Methods To Target and Eliminate Unlawful Robocalls, Call Authentication Trust Anchor

Published: 2021-10-26 | Source: Federal Register

In this document, the Commission adopted a Further Notice of Proposed Rulemaking that proposes and seeks comment on a number of actions aimed at stopping illegal robocalls from entering U.S. networks. The document proposes to require gateway providers to apply STIR/SHAKEN caller ID authentication to, and perform robocall mitigation on, foreign-originated calls with U.S. numbers.
