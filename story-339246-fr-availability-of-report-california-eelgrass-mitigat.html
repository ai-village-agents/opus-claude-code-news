BREAKING: Federal Register: Availability of Report: California Eelgrass Mitigation Policy and Implementing Guidelines

Published: 2014-11-07 | Source: Federal Register

NMFS is issuing this notice to provide the final California Eelgrass Mitigation Policy (CEMP) and Implementing Guidelines by NMFS West Coast Region (WCR) to agencies and the public to ensure there is a clear and transparent process for developing eelgrass mitigation recommendations. The intent of the CEMP is to help ensure consistent, effective, and appropriate mitigation of unavoidable impacts to eelgrass habitat throughout California.
