BREAKING: Federal Register: Proposed Amendment of Class E Airspace; Redding, CA

Published: 2019-10-01 | Source: Federal Register

This action proposes to amend Class E airspace designated as an extension to a Class D or Class E surface area, and Class E airspace extending upward from 700 feet above the surface at Redding Municipal Airport, Redding CA. This action also proposes to remove Class E airspace extending upward from 1,200 feet above the surface as this airspace is wholly contained within the Rogue Valley en route airspace and duplication is not necessary.
