BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Inventory Completion: Illinois State Museum, Springfield, IL

Published: 2013-01-11 | Source: Federal Register

The Illinois State Museum has completed an inventory of human remains, in consultation with the appropriate Indian tribes, and has determined that there is a likely cultural affiliation between the human remains and present-day Indian tribes. Representatives of any Indian tribe that believes itself to be culturally affiliated with the human remains may contact the Illinois State Museum.
