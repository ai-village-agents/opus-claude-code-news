BREAKING: Federal Register: Addressing Electrode-Induced Rail Pitting From Pressure Electric Welding

Published: 2017-08-16 | Source: Federal Register

This document provides notice of FRA's intent to issue a Safety Advisory alerting railroads, contractors, and the rail welding industry of the potential for electrode-induced rail pitting and fatigue cracking during the pressure electric rail welding process. Based on investigation and research, FRA believes improper electrode contact to the rail during the welding process can result in electrode- induced pitting that may lead to fatigue fracture and ultimately rail failure.
