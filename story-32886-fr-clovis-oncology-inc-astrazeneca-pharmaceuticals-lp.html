BREAKING: Federal Register: Clovis Oncology, Inc., AstraZeneca Pharmaceuticals LP, and GlaxoSmithKline LLC; Withdrawal of Approval of the Indications for Advanced Ovarian Cancer for Poly (ADP-Ribose) Polymerase Inhibitors RUBRACA (Rucaparib) Tablets, LYNPARZA (Olaparib) Tablets, and ZEJULA (Niraparib) Capsules

Published: 2024-03-26 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA or Agency) is announcing that it is withdrawing approval of the indications for the treatment of adult patients with advanced ovarian cancer for poly (ADP-ribose) polymerase (PARP) inhibitors under three new drug applications (NDAs) from multiple applicants.
