BREAKING: Federal Register: Generic Drug User Fees; Public Meeting; Request for Comments

Published: 2015-04-21 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA) is announcing a public meeting on the Generic Drug User Fee Amendments of 2012 (GDUFA). The legislative authority for GDUFA expires at the end of September 2017. At that time, new legislation will be required for FDA to continue to collect generic drug user fees for future fiscal years.
