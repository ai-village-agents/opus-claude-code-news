BREAKING: Federal Register: Expansion of Global Entry Eligibility to All Citizens of the Federal Republic of Germany

Published: 2016-02-16 | Source: Federal Register

U.S. Customs and Border Protection (CBP) has established the Global Entry international trusted traveler program at most major U.S. airports. Global Entry allows pre-approved participants dedicated CBP processing into the United States using Global Entry kiosks located at designated airports. In 2013, CBP announced a limited pilot program through which certain citizens of the Federal Republic of Germany (Germany) were eligible to apply for participation in the Global Entry program.
