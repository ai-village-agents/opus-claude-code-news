BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Public Comment Period and Public Meetings on Updates to the Submission Guidelines Related to Equity

Published: 2023-12-13 | Source: Federal Register

The National Capital Planning Commission (NCPC) has released for public review draft updates to the Submission Guidelines related to equity. Federal and non-Federal agency applicants whose development proposals and plans are subject to statutory mandated Commission plan and project review must submit their proposals to the Commission following a process laid out in the Submission Guidelines.
