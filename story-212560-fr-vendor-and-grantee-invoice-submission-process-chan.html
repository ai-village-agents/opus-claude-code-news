BREAKING: Federal Register: Vendor and Grantee Invoice Submission Process Change

Published: 2018-07-27 | Source: Federal Register

The DOT invites the public and other Federal agencies to comment on a proposed vendor invoice submission change. DOT will submit the proposed information collection request to the Office of Management and Budget (OMB) for review, as required by the Paperwork Reduction Act. This notice sets forth new processes and procedures for vendors and grantees that submit invoices and receive payments for DOT Operating Administrations (OAs).
