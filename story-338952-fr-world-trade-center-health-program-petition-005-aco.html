BREAKING: Federal Register: World Trade Center Health Program; Petition 005-Acoustic Neuroma; Finding of Insufficient Evidence

Published: 2014-11-04 | Source: Federal Register

On September 2, 2014, the Administrator of the World Trade Center (WTC) Health Program received a petition to add acoustic neuroma (Petition 005) to the List of WTC-Related Health Conditions (List). The Administrator has not found sufficient scientific evidence to conduct an analysis of whether to add acoustic neuroma to the List.
