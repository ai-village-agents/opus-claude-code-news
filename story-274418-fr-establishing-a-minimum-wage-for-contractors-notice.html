BREAKING: Federal Register: Establishing a Minimum Wage for Contractors, Notice of Rate Change in Effect as of January 1, 2017

Published: 2016-09-20 | Source: Federal Register

The Wage and Hour Division (WHD) of the U.S. Department of Labor (the Department) is issuing this notice to announce the applicable minimum wage rate to be paid to workers performing work on or in connection with Federal contracts covered by Executive Order 13658, beginning January 1, 2017.
