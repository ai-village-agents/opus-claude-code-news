BREAKING: Federal Register: Requests for Approving Certain Alternative Methods for Computing Withdrawal Liability; Settlement of Withdrawal and Mass Withdrawal Liability

Published: 2017-01-05 | Source: Federal Register

This is a request for information (RFI) to inform PBGC on issues arising from arrangements between employers and multiemployer plans involving an alternative "two-pool" withdrawal liability method.
