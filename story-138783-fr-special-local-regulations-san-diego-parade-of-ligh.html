BREAKING: Federal Register: Special Local Regulations; San Diego Parade of Lights, San Diego, CA

Published: 2021-11-26 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard will enforce the San Diego Parade of Lights special local regulations on the waters of San Diego Bay, California, on December 12, 2021, and December 19, 2021. These special local regulations are necessary to provide for the safety of the participants, crew, spectators, sponsor vessels, and general users of the waterway.
