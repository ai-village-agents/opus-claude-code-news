BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; BAE Systems (Operations) Limited Airplanes

Published: 2022-08-22 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA proposes to adopt a new airworthiness directive (AD) for all BAE Systems (Operations) Limited Model BAe 146 series airplanes. This proposed AD was prompted by a finding that when the autopilot is engaged, the architecture of the autopilot system does not automatically disconnect the autopilot in response to pilot application of a pitch input or when the electric pitch trim switch on either pilot control wheel is operated.
