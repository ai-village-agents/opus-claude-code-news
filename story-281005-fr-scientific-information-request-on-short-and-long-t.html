BREAKING: Federal Register: Scientific Information Request on Short and Long Term Outcomes After Bariatric Therapies in the Medicare Population

The Agency for Healthcare Research and Quality (AHRQ) is seeking scientific information submissions to inform our review of Short and Long Term Outcomes after Bariatric Therapies in the Medicare Population, which is currently being conducted by the AHRQ's Evidence- based Practice Centers (EPC) Programs.
