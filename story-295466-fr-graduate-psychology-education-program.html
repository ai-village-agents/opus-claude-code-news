BREAKING: Federal Register: Graduate Psychology Education Program

Published: 2015-06-03 | Source: Federal Register

The Health Resources and Services Administration (HRSA) is issuing a limited competition for awards among the 40 current Graduate Psychology Education (GPE) Program grantees whose project periods end June 30, 2016. No more than $1,000,000 will be made available in federal fiscal year (FY) 2015 in the form of 1-year project period grants.
