BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Fokker Services B.V. Airplanes

Published: 2012-06-21 | Source: Federal Register

We propose to supersede an existing airworthiness directive (AD) that applies to certain Fokker Services B.V. Model F.28 Mark 0070 and 0100 airplanes. The existing AD currently requires performing a detailed visual inspection for cracks of the pistons on the main landing gear (MLG), and replacing the affected pistons if necessary.
