BREAKING: Federal Register: Estimates of the Voting Age Population for 2014

Published: 2015-02-06 | Source: Federal Register

This notice announces the voting age population estimates as of July 1, 2014, for each state and the District of Columbia. We are providing this notice in accordance with the 1976 amendment to the Federal Election Campaign Act, Title 2, United States Code, Section 441a(e).
