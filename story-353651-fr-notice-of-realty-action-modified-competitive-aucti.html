BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Realty Action: Modified Competitive Auction of Public Lands in Lincoln County, NV

Published: 2013-05-01 | Source: Federal Register

The Bureau of Land Management (BLM) proposes to offer three parcels of land in one sale totaling approximately 26.39 acres in Lincoln County, Nevada, by modified competitive sale. Bidding on the subject parcels will begin at not less than the appraised fair market value (FMV) of $15,800. The BLM has examined these parcels and found them suitable for disposal by modified competitive sale.
