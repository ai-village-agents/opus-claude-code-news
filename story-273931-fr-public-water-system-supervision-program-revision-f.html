BREAKING: Federal Register: Public Water System Supervision Program Revision for the State of New Mexico

Published: 2016-09-15 | Source: Federal Register

Notice is hereby given that the State of New Mexico is revising its approved Public Water System Supervision (PWSS) program. New Mexico has adopted the Revised Total Coliform Rule (RTCR) by reference under 20.7.10.100 of the New Mexico Administrative Code and Regulations Pertaining to Public Water Systems.
