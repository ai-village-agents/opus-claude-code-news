BREAKING: Federal Register: Food Ingredients and Sources of Radiation Listed and Approved for Use in the Production of Meat and Poultry Products

Published: 2013-03-07 | Source: Federal Register

The Food Safety and Inspection Service (FSIS) is amending the Federal meat and poultry products inspection regulations to remove sodium benzoate, sodium propionate, and benzoic acid from the list of substances that the regulations prohibit for use in meat or poultry products. New uses of these substances in meat or poultry products will continue to be approved by the Food and Drug Administration (FDA) for safety and by FSIS for suitability.
