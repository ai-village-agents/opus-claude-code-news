BREAKING: Federal Register: Imposition of Nonproliferation Measures Against Foreign Persons, Including a Ban on U.S. Government Procurement

Published: 2021-01-22 | Source: Federal Register

A determination has been made that a number of foreign persons have engaged in activities that warrant the imposition of measures pursuant to the Iran, North Korea, and Syria Nonproliferation Act.
