BREAKING: Federal Register: Commission Information Collection Activities (FERC-542); Comment Request; Extension

Published: 2022-09-01 | Source: Federal Register

In compliance with the requirements of the Paperwork Reduction Act of 1995, the Federal Energy Regulatory Commission (Commission or FERC) is soliciting public comment on the currently approved information collections, FERC-542 (Gas Pipeline Rates: Rate Tracking). The Commission published a 60-day notice in the Federal Register on June 15, 2022, and received no comments.
