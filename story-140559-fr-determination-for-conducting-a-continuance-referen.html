BREAKING: Federal Register: Determination for Conducting a Continuance Referendum Regarding Amendments to the Cotton Research and Promotion Act

Published: 2021-12-21 | Source: Federal Register

This notice announces the U.S. Department of Agriculture's (USDA) determination not to conduct a continuance referendum regarding the 1991 amendments to the Cotton Research and Promotion Order provided for in the Cotton Research and Promotion Act amendments of 1990.
