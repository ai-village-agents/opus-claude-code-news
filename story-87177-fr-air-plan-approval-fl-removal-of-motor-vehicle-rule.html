BREAKING: Federal Register: Air Plan Approval; FL; Removal of Motor Vehicle Rules

Published: 2022-01-25 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is approving changes to a State Implementation Plan (SIP) revision submitted by the State of Florida, through the Florida Department of Environmental Protection (FDEP), in a letter dated July 2, 2020. Specifically, EPA is approving the removal of rules prohibiting tampering with motor vehicle air pollution control equipment and rules concerning visible emissions from motor vehicles.
