BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Intent To Conduct Scoping Process and Prepare Environmental Impact Statement; Northern States Power Company-Minnesota; Monticello Nuclear Generating Plant, Unit 1

Published: 2023-03-10 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Nuclear Regulatory Commission (NRC) will conduct a scoping process to gather information necessary to prepare an environmental impact statement (EIS) to evaluate the environmental impacts for the subsequent license renewal (SLR) of the Renewed Facility Operating License No. DPR-22 for Monticello Nuclear Generating Plant, Unit 1 (Monticello). The NRC is seeking public comment on this action and has scheduled a public scoping meeting that will take place in person in Monticello, Minnesota...
