BREAKING: Federal Register: Approval of Source Specific Air Quality Implementation Plans; New Jersey

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is approving a revision to the New Jersey State Implementation Plan (SIP) for the 2008 8-hour ozone National Ambient Air Quality Standard in relation to a Source Specific SIP for Gerdau Ameristeel in Sayreville, New Jersey.
