BREAKING: Federal Register: Taking of Marine Mammals Incidental to Specified Activities; Vashon Seismic Retrofit Project

Published: 2014-12-31 | Source: Federal Register

NMFS has received a request from the Washington State Department of Transportation (WSDOT) Ferries Division (WSF) for an authorization to take small numbers of nine species of marine mammals, by Level B harassment, incidental to proposed construction activities for Vashon Seismic Retrofit Project in Vashon Island, Washington State.
