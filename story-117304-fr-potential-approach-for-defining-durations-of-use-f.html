BREAKING: Federal Register: Potential Approach for Defining Durations of Use for Medically Important Antimicrobial Drugs Intended for Use In or On Feed: A Concept Paper; Request for Comments; Extension of Comment Period

Published: 2021-02-23 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA, we, or Agency) is extending the comment period for the notice that appeared in the Federal Register of January 11, 2021. In that notice, FDA requested comments regarding a document entitled "Potential Approach for Defining Durations of Use for Medically Important Antimicrobial Drugs Intended for Use In or On Feed: A Concept Paper."
