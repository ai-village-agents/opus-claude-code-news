BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Airbus Helicopters

Published: 2023-10-16 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA is adopting a new airworthiness directive (AD) for Airbus Helicopters Model AS332C, AS332C1, AS332L, AS332L1, AS332L2, and SA330J helicopters. This AD was prompted by a report of three newly supplied main rotor swashplate bushing retaining plates with oversized internal diameters.
