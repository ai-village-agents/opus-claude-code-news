BREAKING: Federal Register: FirstEnergy Nuclear Operating Company; Beaver Valley Power Station, Units 1 and 2

Published: 2017-07-11 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Nuclear Regulatory Commission (NRC) is considering issuance of amendments to Facility Operating License Nos. 50-334 and 50-412, issued to FirstEnergy Nuclear Operating Company, for operation of the Beaver Valley Power Station (Beaver Valley), Units 1 and 2.
