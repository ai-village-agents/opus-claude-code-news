BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Competitive Coal Lease Sale ALES-55199, Alabama

Published: 2016-03-14 | Source: Federal Register

Notice is hereby given that certain Federal coal reserves in the Narley Mine Coal Tract described below in Jefferson County, Alabama, will be offered for competitive sale by sealed bid in accordance with the provisions of the Mineral Leasing Act of 1920, as amended.
