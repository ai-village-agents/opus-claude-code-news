BREAKING: Federal Register: Tribal Listening Sessions on Climate Change and Discretionary Grants

Published: 2021-10-06 | Source: Federal Register

The Department invites representatives of federally recognized Tribes to participate in three upcoming virtual listening sessions focused on climate change and Tribal Nations and two upcoming virtual listening sessions focused on Bureau of Indian Affairs (BIA) discretionary grants for Tribes. The Department also invites Tribal youth to the first scheduled climate listening session, which is focused on Tribal youth and climate.
