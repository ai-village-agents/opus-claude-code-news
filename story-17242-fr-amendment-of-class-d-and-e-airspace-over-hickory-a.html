BREAKING: Federal Register: Amendment of Class D and E Airspace Over Hickory and Morganton, NC

Published: 2025-08-01 | Source: Federal Register

This rule amends the Class D and E surface airspaces at Hickory Regional Airport, Hickory, NC. Additionally, this rule amends Class E5 airspace extending upward from 700 feet above the surface for Hickory Regional Airport, Hickory, NC, by increasing the radius to 7.1 miles and adding an extension.
