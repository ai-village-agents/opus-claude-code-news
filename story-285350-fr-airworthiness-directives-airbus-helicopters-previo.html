BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Airbus Helicopters (Previously Eurocopter France) Helicopters

Published: 2015-02-02 | Source: Federal Register

We are superseding Airworthiness Directive (AD) 2013-19-19 for certain Eurocopter France Model AS332C, AS332L, AS332L1, AS332L2, and EC225LP helicopters. AD 2013-19-19 required replacing certain serial- numbered main gearbox (MGB) bevel gear vertical shafts and inspecting and replacing, if necessary, each MGB bevel gear vertical shaft (shaft).
