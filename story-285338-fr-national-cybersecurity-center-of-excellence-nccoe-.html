BREAKING: Federal Register: National Cybersecurity Center of Excellence (NCCoE) Situational Awareness Use Case for the Energy Sector

Published: 2015-02-02 | Source: Federal Register

The National Institute of Standards and Technology (NIST) invites organizations to provide products and technical expertise to support and demonstrate security platforms for situational awareness for the energy sector. This notice is the initial step for the National Cybersecurity Center of Excellence (NCCoE) in collaborating with technology companies to address cybersecurity challenges identified under the Energy sector program.
