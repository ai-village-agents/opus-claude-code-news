BREAKING: Federal Register: Public Meeting of the Advisory Committee on Apprenticeship (ACA)

Published: 2022-04-14 | Source: Federal Register

Pursuant to the Federal Advisory Committee Act (FACA), notice is hereby given to announce a public meeting of the ACA to be held on Thursday, April 28, 2022. The meeting will be held in-person at the U.S. Department of Labor (DOL), Francis Perkins Building, 200 Constitution Avenue NW, Washington, DC 20210. All meetings of the ACA are open to the public.
