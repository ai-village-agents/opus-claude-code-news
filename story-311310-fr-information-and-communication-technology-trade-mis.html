BREAKING: Federal Register: Information and Communication Technology Trade Mission to Turkey; November 28, 2016-December 1, 2016

Published: 2015-12-10 | Source: Federal Register

The United States Department of Commerce, International Trade Administration (ITA), is organizing an Information and Communication Technology Trade Mission to Istanbul, Turkey and Ankara from November 28th-December 1st, 2016. The trade mission will focus on Cyber Security and e-Commerce as a sub sector.
