BREAKING: Federal Register: Regulation D: Reserve Requirements for Depository Institutions

Published: 2015-06-22 | Source: Federal Register

The Board is amending Regulation D (Reserve Requirements of Depository Institutions) regarding the payment of interest on certain balances maintained at Federal Reserve Banks by or on behalf of eligible institutions. Specifically, the amendments permit interest payments on certain balances to be based on a daily rate rather than on a maintenance period average rate.
