BREAKING: Federal Register: Certain Frozen Fish Fillets from the Socialist Republic of Vietnam: Rescission of Antidumping Duty New Shipper Review; 2012-2013

Published: 2013-07-03 | Source: Federal Register

In response to a request from Ngoc Ha Co. Ltd. Food Processing and Trading ("Ngoc Ha"), the Department of Commerce ("the Department") initiated a new shipper review of the antidumping duty order on certain frozen fish fillets from the Socialist Republic of Vietnam ("Vietnam") covering the period August 1, 2012, through January 31, 2013.\1\ On May 23, 2013, Ngoc Ha withdrew its request for a new shipper review.
