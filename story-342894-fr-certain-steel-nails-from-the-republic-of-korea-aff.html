BREAKING: Federal Register: Certain Steel Nails From the Republic of Korea: Affirmative Preliminary Determination of Sales at Less Than Fair Value and Postponement of Final Determination

Published: 2014-12-29 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Commerce ("Department") preliminarily determines that certain steel nails ("nails") from the Republic of Korea ("Korea") are being, or are likely to be, sold in the United States at less than fair value ("LTFV"), as provided in section 733(b) of the Tariff Act of 1930, as amended (the "Act").
