BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Standard for Architectural Glazing Materials

Published: 2015-05-22 | Source: Federal Register

The Consumer Product Safety Commission ("CPSC" or "Commission") is proposing an amendment to the Safety Standard for Architectural Glazing Materials (16 CFR part 1201) to clarify certain test procedures specified in the standard.
