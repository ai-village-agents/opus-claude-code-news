BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Gulfstream Aerospace Corporation Airplanes

Published: 2017-10-02 | Source: Federal Register

We are superseding Airworthiness Directive (AD) 2009-17-01, which applied to certain Gulfstream Model G-IV, GIV-X, GV-SP airplanes and Model GV airplanes. AD 2009-17-01 required an inspection for sealant applied to the exterior of the auxiliary power unit (APU) enclosure (firewall), and a revision of the airplane flight manual (AFM), as applicable.
