BREAKING: Federal Register: Right-of-Way and Real Estate

Published: 2016-08-23 | Source: Federal Register

The FHWA is revising its regulations governing the acquisition, management, and disposal of real property for transportation programs and projects receiving funds under title 23, United States Code. The revisions are prompted by enactment of the Moving Ahead for Progress in the 21st Century Act (MAP-21).
