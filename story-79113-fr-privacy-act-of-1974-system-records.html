BREAKING: Federal Register: Privacy Act of 1974; System Records

Published: 2023-10-05 | Source: Federal Register

The Office of Government Ethics (OGE) proposes to revise one of its existing internal systems of records pursuant to the provisions of the Privacy Act of 1974. This system of records currently contains personally identifiable information (PII) collected from Federal employees and/or members of the public who register to attend or otherwise participate in OGE meetings and events.
