BREAKING: Federal Register: Endangered and Threatened Wildlife and Plants; Endangered Species Status for the Bleached Sandhill Skipper

Published: 2025-01-08 | Source: Federal Register

We, the U.S. Fish and Wildlife Service (Service), propose to list the bleached sandhill skipper (Polites sabuleti sinemaculata), an insect subspecies from Humboldt County, Nevada, as an endangered species under the Endangered Species Act of 1973, as amended (Act). This determination also serves as our 12-month finding on a petition to list the bleached sandhill skipper.
