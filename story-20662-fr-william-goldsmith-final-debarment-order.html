BREAKING: Federal Register: William Goldsmith: Final Debarment Order

Published: 2025-09-19 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA) is issuing an order under the Federal Food, Drug, and Cosmetic Act (the FD&C Act) debarring William Goldsmith for a period of 5 years from importing or offering for import any drug into the United States. FDA bases this order on a finding that Mr. Goldsmith was convicted of one felony count under Federal law for introducing misbranded drugs into interstate commerce.
