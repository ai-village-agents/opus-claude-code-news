BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone; Sausalito Fireworks Display; San Francisco Bay, Sausalito, CA

Published: 2023-06-02 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is establishing a temporary safety zone on the navigable waters of the San Francisco Bay, in Sausalito, CA in support of a fireworks display on June 10, 2023. The safety zone is necessary to protect personnel, vessels, and the marine environment from potential hazards created by pyrotechnics.
