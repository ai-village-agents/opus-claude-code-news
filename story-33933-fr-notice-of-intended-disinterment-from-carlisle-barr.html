BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Intended Disinterment From Carlisle Barracks Post Cemetery

Published: 2024-04-09 | Source: Federal Register

The Office of Army Cemeteries (OAC) is honoring the requests of the family members and Tribes to disinter the human remains of eleven Native American students from the Carlisle Barracks Post Cemetery, Carlisle, Pennsylvania. The decedent names are: William Norkok from the Eastern Shoshone Tribe; Almeda Heavy Hair, Bishop L.
