BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone; Cable Laying Corridor, Atlantic Ocean, Virginia Beach, Virginia

Published: 2025-01-15 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is proposing to establish a temporary moving safety zone to surround nearshore operations conducted by a cable laying barge. Cable lay and burial operations will create navigational hazards moving along a corridor from shore extending seaward 12 NM. This action is necessary to provide for the safety of life on these navigable waters near Virginia Beach, Virginia.
