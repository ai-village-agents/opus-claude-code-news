BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Ameri-King Corporation Emergency Locator Transmitters

Published: 2017-09-19 | Source: Federal Register

We are adopting a new airworthiness directive (AD) for certain Ameri-King Corporation emergency locator transmitters (ELTs) as installed on various aircraft. This AD was prompted by multiple reports of ELT failure and a report of noncompliance to quality standards and manufacturer processes related to Ameri-King Corporation ELTs.
