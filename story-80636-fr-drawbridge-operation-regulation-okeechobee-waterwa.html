BREAKING: Federal Register: Drawbridge Operation Regulation; Okeechobee Waterway, Stuart, FL

Published: 2023-10-27 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard proposes to modify the operating schedule that governs the Roosevelt (US1) Bridge, across the Okeechobee Waterway, mile 7.5, at Stuart, FL. This action is necessary to allow the drawbridge to operate on demand as outlined in the Record of Decision for the high-level fixed US1 Roosevelt Bridge which was constructed in 1997.
