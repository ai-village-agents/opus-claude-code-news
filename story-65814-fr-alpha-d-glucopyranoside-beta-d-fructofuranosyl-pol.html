BREAKING: Federal Register: [alpha]-D-Glucopyranoside, [beta]-D-Fructofuranosyl, Polymer With Methyloxirane and Oxirane; Tolerance Exemption

Published: 2023-04-24 | Source: Federal Register

This regulation establishes an exemption from the requirement of a tolerance for residues of [alpha]-D-glucopyranoside, [beta]-D- fructofuranosyl, polymer with methyloxirane and oxirane with a minimum number average molecular weight (in amu) of 9,800 (CAS Reg. No. 26301- 10-0) when used as an inert ingredient in a pesticide chemical formulation.
