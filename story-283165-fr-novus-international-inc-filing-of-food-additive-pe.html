BREAKING: Federal Register: Novus International, Inc.; Filing of Food Additive Petition (Animal Use); Reopening of the Comment Period

Published: 2016-12-30 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA) is reopening the comment period for the notice of petition, published in the Federal Register of November 8, 2016 (81 FR 78528), proposing that the food additive regulations be amended to provide for the safe use of poly (2- vinylpyridine-co-styrene) as a nutrient protectant for methionine hydroxy analog in animal food for beef cattle, dairy cattle, and replacement dairy heifers.
