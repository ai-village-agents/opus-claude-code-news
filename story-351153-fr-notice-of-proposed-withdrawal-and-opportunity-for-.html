BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Proposed Withdrawal and Opportunity for a Public Meeting; Colorado

Published: 2013-04-03 | Source: Federal Register

The Assistant Secretary of the Interior for Policy, Management and Budget proposes to withdraw, on behalf of the Bureau of Land Management (BLM), 2,214.31 acres of public lands in Chaffee County, Colorado, to protect the scenic, recreational, and other natural resource values along with the capital investments of developed recreational facilities found within the scenic Browns Canyon corridor along the Arkansas River.
