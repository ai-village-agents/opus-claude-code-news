BREAKING: Federal Register: Manual for Courts-Martial; Proposed Amendments

Published: 2016-03-22 | Source: Federal Register

The JSC is publishing final proposed amendments to the MCM. The proposed changes concern the Rules for Courts-Martial, the Military Rules of Evidence, and the punitive articles applicable in trials by courts-martial.
