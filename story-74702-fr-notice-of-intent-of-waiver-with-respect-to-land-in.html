BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Intent of Waiver With Respect to Land; Indianapolis International Airport, Indianapolis, Indiana

Published: 2023-08-17 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA is considering a proposal to change 1.423 acres of airport land from aeronautical use to non-aeronautical use of airport property located at Indianapolis International Airport, Indianapolis, Indiana. The aforementioned land is not needed for aeronautical use. The land is made up of one parcel and is located along the White River, just west of downtown Indianapolis, Indiana.
