BREAKING: Federal Register: New Animal Drugs; Updating Tolerances for Residues of New Animal Drugs in Food

Published: 2016-10-28 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA or we) is proposing to amend our 2012 document entitled "New Animal Drugs; Updating Tolerances for Residues of New Animal Drugs in Food." The document proposed to revise the animal drug regulations regarding tolerances for residues of approved and conditionally approved new animal drugs in food by standardizing, simplifying, and clarifying the determination standards and codification style.
