BREAKING: Federal Register: Privacy Act of 1974; System of Records

Published: 2022-07-20 | Source: Federal Register

In accordance with the Privacy Act of 1974, the DoD is establishing a new system of records titled, "OIG Data Analytics Platform, CIG-30." This system of records covers the DoD OIG collection and maintenance of records necessary in order to fulfill the responsibilities of the Inspector General Act of 1978, as amended.
