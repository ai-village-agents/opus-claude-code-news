BREAKING: Federal Register: Update to Air Carrier Definitions

Published: 2022-12-07 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA proposes to amend the regulatory definitions of certain air carrier and commercial operations. This proposed rule would add powered-lift to these definitions to ensure the appropriate sets of rules apply to air carriers' and certain commercial operators' operations of aircraft that FAA regulations define as powered-lift.
