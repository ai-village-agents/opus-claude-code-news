BREAKING: Federal Register: Stakeholder Meeting on Using Leading Indicators To Improve Safety and Health Outcomes

Published: 2019-09-30 | Source: Federal Register

OSHA invites interested parties to participate in a stakeholder meeting to share information on their use of leading indicators to improve safety and health outcomes in the workplace. OSHA plans to use the information to create additional tools that may help employers with developing and using leading indicators.
