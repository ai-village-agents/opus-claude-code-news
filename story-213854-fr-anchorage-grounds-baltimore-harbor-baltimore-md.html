BREAKING: Federal Register: Anchorage Grounds; Baltimore Harbor, Baltimore, MD

Published: 2018-08-14 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard proposes to amend its Baltimore Harbor anchorage grounds regulation. The proposed changes would reduce the size of three general anchorages, establish one new general anchorage, rename two existing general anchorages, and change the duration a vessel may remain within an anchorage for two existing general anchorages.
