BREAKING: Federal Register: Russian Sanctions: Revisions and Clarifications for Licensing Policy for the Crimea Region of Ukraine

Published: 2015-05-22 | Source: Federal Register

The Bureau of Industry and Security (BIS) issues this final rule to amend the Export Administration Regulations (EAR) to facilitate Internet-based communications with persons in the Crimea region of Ukraine.
