BREAKING: Federal Register: FCC Seeks To Enable State-of-the-Art Radar Sensors in 60 GHz Band

Published: 2021-08-19 | Source: Federal Register

In this document, the Commission proposes to revise the Commission's rules to provide expanded operational flexibility to unlicensed field disturbance sensor (FDS) devices (e.g., radars) that operate in the 57-64 GHz band (60 GHz band).
