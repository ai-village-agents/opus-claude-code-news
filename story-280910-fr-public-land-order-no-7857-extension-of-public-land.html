BREAKING: Federal Register: Public Land Order No. 7857; Extension of Public Land Order No. 7254; Montana

Published: 2016-12-07 | Source: Federal Register

This order extends the duration of the withdrawal created by Public Land Order No. 7254, as corrected and amended, for an additional 20-year period, which would otherwise expire on April 9, 2017. Public Land Order No. 7254 withdrew 19,686.09 acres of public mineral estate in Toole and Liberty Counties, Montana, from location and entry under the United States mining laws, but not from leasing under the mineral leasing laws.
