BREAKING: Federal Register: Group Registration of Unpublished Works: Extension of Comment Period

Published: 2017-11-13 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Copyright Office is extending the deadlines for the submission of written comments in response to its October 12, 2017 notice of proposed rulemaking, regarding the creation of a new group registration option for unpublished works to replace the existing "unpublished collections" registration option.
