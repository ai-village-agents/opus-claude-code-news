BREAKING: Federal Register: Pulmonary-Allergy Drugs Advisory Committee; Postponed

Published: 2020-04-07 | Source: Federal Register

The meeting of the Pulmonary-Allergy Drugs Advisory Committee (PADAC) scheduled for April 21, 2020, is postponed. The Food and Drug Administration (FDA), like other government agencies, is taking the necessary steps to ensure the Agency is prepared to continue our vital public health mission in the event that our day-to-day operations are impacted by the COVID-19 public health emergency.
