BREAKING: Federal Register: Requirements for the Disposition of Non-Ambulatory Disabled Veal Calves

Published: 2015-05-13 | Source: Federal Register

The Food Safety and Inspection Service (FSIS) is proposing to amend its regulations on ante-mortem inspection to remove a provision that permits establishments to set apart and hold for treatment veal calves that are unable to rise from a recumbent position and walk because they are tired or cold. Under the proposed rule, non-ambulatory disabled veal calves that are offered for slaughter will be condemned and promptly euthanized.
