BREAKING: Federal Register: Decommissioning Financial Assurance for Sealed and Unsealed Radioactive Materials

Published: 2022-07-19 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Nuclear Regulatory Commission (NRC) is correcting the notification published in the Federal Register on April 28, 2022, regarding a regulatory basis to support a rulemaking that would amend its regulations for decommissioning financial assurance for sealed and unsealed radioactive materials. This action is necessary to correct a publication issue with the regulatory basis.
