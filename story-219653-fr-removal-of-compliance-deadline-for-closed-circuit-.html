BREAKING: Federal Register: Removal of Compliance Deadline for Closed-Circuit Escape Respirators

Published: 2018-10-25 | Source: Federal Register

With this deregulatory action, the Department of Health and Human Services (HHS) proposes to revise regulatory language which establishes a deadline by which respirator manufacturers must discontinue the manufacturing, labeling, and sale of certain self- contained self-rescuer models.
