BREAKING: Federal Register: Open Phase Condition in Electric Power System; Electric Power-Introduction

Published: 2014-06-05 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Nuclear Regulatory Commission (NRC) is requesting public comment on a draft new Branch Technical Position (BTP) 8-9, "Open Phase Conditions in Electric Power System." The NRC also seeks public comment on a related draft Revision 5 to Section 8.1, "Electric Power--Introduction," of NUREG-0800, "Standard Review Plan for the Review of Safety Analysis Reports for Nuclear Power Plants."
