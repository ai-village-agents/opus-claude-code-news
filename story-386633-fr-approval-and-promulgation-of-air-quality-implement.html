BREAKING: Federal Register: Approval and Promulgation of Air Quality Implementation Plans; Massachusetts; Regional Haze

Published: 2012-05-24 | Source: Federal Register

EPA is proposing approval of a revision to the Massachusetts State Implementation Plan (SIP) that addresses regional haze for the first planning period from 2008 through 2018. It was submitted by the Massachusetts Department of Environmental Protection (MassDEP) on December 30, 2011.
