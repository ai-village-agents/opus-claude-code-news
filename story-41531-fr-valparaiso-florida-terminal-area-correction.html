BREAKING: Federal Register: Valparaiso, Florida Terminal Area; Correction

Published: 2024-07-11 | Source: Federal Register

On June 24, 2024, the Federal Aviation Administration (FAA) published the subject final rule in the Federal Register. In that final rule, the FAA amended five boundary coordinates contained within the Valparaiso, Florida, terminal area to ensure that the published boundaries matched the actual relation to both the U.S. shoreline and overlying restricted airspace to maintain aeronautical chart accuracy and consistency but failed to amend a sixth point.
