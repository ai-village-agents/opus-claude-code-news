BREAKING: Federal Register: Proximity Detection Systems for Mobile Machines in Underground Mines

Published: 2017-01-09 | Source: Federal Register

The Mine Safety and Health Administration (MSHA) is reopening the rulemaking record and requesting additional comments on the Agency's proposed rule on Proximity Detection Systems for Mobile Machines in Underground Mines which was published in the Federal Register on September 2, 2015. The proposed rule would require underground coal mine operators to equip coal hauling machines and scoops with proximity detection systems.
