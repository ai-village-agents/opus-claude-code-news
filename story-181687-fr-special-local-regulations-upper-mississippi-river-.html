BREAKING: Federal Register: Special Local Regulations, Upper Mississippi River, Mile Markers 839 to 840 St. Paul, MN

Published: 2019-06-14 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard proposes to establish a temporary Special Local Regulation for the navigable waters of the Upper Mississippi River between mile marker (MM) 839 and MM 840 in St. Paul, MN on September 7, 2019 in order to provide for the safety of life on these waters during a Red Bull Flugtag event.
