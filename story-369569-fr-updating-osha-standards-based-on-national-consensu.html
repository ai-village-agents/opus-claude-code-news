BREAKING: Federal Register: Updating OSHA Standards Based on National Consensus Standards; Signage

Published: 2013-11-06 | Source: Federal Register

On June 13, 2013, OSHA published in the Federal Register (78 FR 35559) a direct final rule that revised its signage standards for general industry and construction by updating the references to national consensus standards approved by the American National Standards Institute, a clearinghouse that verifies that the criteria for approval of consensus standards have been met.
