BREAKING: Federal Register: Forest Service Manual 2470, Silvicultural Practices

Published: 2024-08-16 | Source: Federal Register

The United States Department of Agriculture, Forest Service (Agency), is revising its directive related to silvicultural practices on National Forest System lands. The proposed directive updates Forest Service Manual 2470, "Silvicultural Practices." This directive sets forth policy, responsibilities, and direction for several aspects of management and moves the agency closer to its goal of providing more current direction.
