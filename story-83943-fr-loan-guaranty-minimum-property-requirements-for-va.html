BREAKING: Federal Register: Loan Guaranty: Minimum Property Requirements for VA-Guaranteed and Direct Loans

Published: 2023-12-11 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Veterans Affairs (VA) is requesting public comment on the minimum property requirements (MPRs) for VA-guaranteed and direct loans. VA will consider information received in response to this advance notice of proposed rulemaking (ANPRM) in implementing the Improving Access to the VA Home Loan Benefit Act of 2022 (the Act).
