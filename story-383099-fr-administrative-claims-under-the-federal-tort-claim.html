BREAKING: Federal Register: Administrative Claims Under the Federal Tort Claims Act and Related Statutes

Published: 2012-04-13 | Source: Federal Register

This amendment revises the Department of Labor's (DOL) regulations governing administrative claims submitted to DOL pursuant to the Federal Tort Claims Act (FTCA), the Military Personnel and Civilian Employees' Claims Act (MPCECA), and for payment of claims arising out of the operation of the Job Corps.
