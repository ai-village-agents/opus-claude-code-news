BREAKING: Federal Register: Chlorpyrifos; Tolerance Revocations; Notice of Data Availability and Request for Comment

Published: 2016-11-17 | Source: Federal Register

EPA is announcing and inviting comment on additional information obtained and developed by EPA in conjunction with the proposed tolerance revocation for chlorpyrifos. This information includes the revised human health risk assessment and the drinking water assessment.
