BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Intent To Prepare an Environmental Impact Statement for the Surplus Plutonium Disposition Program

Published: 2020-12-16 | Source: Federal Register

The National Nuclear Security Administration (NNSA), a semi- autonomous agency within the United States (U.S.) Department of Energy (DOE), announces its intent, consistent with the National Environmental Policy Act of 1969 (NEPA), to prepare a Surplus Plutonium Disposition Program (SPDP) Environmental Impact Statement (EIS) to evaluate alternatives for the safe and timely disposition of plutonium surplus to the defense needs of the United States.
