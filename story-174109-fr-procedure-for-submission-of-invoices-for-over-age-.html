BREAKING: Federal Register: Procedure for Submission of Invoices for Over-Age Contracts

Published: 2019-03-14 | Source: Federal Register

The Overseas Private Investment Corporation (OPIC) currently has contracts that are considered over-age and developed procedures that would enable the Agency to closeout these elapsed actions in an efficient and cost-effective manner. OPIC seeks to facilitate the closeout of these actions by requesting all OPIC contractors with contracts identified on this list submit any outstanding invoices.
