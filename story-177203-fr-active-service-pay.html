BREAKING: Federal Register: Active Service Pay

Published: 2019-04-19 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Veterans Affairs (VA) proposes to amend its adjudication regulations. One amendment would permit VA to suspend disability compensation payments upon receipt of notice from the Department of Defense (DoD) that the veteran has received, is receiving, or will begin to receive active service pay.
