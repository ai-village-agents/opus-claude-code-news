BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; The Boeing Company Airplanes

Published: 2016-06-07 | Source: Federal Register

We are adopting a new airworthiness directive (AD) for certain The Boeing Company Model 787-8 airplanes. This AD was prompted by a report of wire chafing caused by a left wing spoiler actuator wire not having enough separation from a certain bracket when the spoiler is in the deployed position.
