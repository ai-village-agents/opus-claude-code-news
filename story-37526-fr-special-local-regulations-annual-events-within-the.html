BREAKING: Federal Register: Special Local Regulations; Annual Events Within the Captain of the Port Eastern Great Lakes Zone

Published: 2024-05-21 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard will enforce certain safety zones located in the federal regulations for Annual Events in the Captain of the Port Eastern Great Lakes Zone. This action is necessary and intended to protect the safety of life and property on navigable waters prior to, during, and immediately after these events.
