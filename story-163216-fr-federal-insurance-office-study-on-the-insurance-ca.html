BREAKING: Federal Register: Federal Insurance Office Study on the Insurance Capital Standard

Published: 2020-10-09 | Source: Federal Register

The Federal Insurance Office (FIO) of the U.S. Department of the Treasury (Treasury) is issuing this notice (Notice) to solicit input on a future study by FIO (FIO Study) to evaluate the potential effects of the insurance capital standard (ICS) on U.S. insurance markets, U.S. consumers, and U.S. insurers.
