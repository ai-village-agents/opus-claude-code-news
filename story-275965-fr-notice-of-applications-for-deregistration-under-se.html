BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Applications for Deregistration Under Section 8(f) of the Investment Company Act of 1940

Published: 2016-10-06 | Source: Federal Register

Applicant, a closed-end investment company and a feeder fund in a master/feeder structure, seeks an order declaring that it has ceased to be an investment company. On May 31, 2016, applicant made a liquidating distribution to its shareholders, based on net asset value. Expenses of approximately $37,218 incurred in connection with the liquidation were paid by applicant. Filing Date: The application was filed on August 17, 2016.
