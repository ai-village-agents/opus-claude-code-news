BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice for Termination of a Disease Management Demonstration Project for TRICARE Standard Beneficiaries

Published: 2013-03-15 | Source: Federal Register

This notice is to advise interested parties of the termination of a Military Health System (MHS) demonstration project entitled "Disease Management Demonstration Project for TRICARE Standard Beneficiaries." The demonstration provided disease management (DM) services to TRICARE Standard beneficiaries who are not eligible to receive some DM-like services under the basic benefit regulations.
