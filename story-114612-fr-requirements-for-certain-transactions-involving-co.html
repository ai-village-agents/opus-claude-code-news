BREAKING: Federal Register: Requirements for Certain Transactions Involving Convertible Virtual Currency or Digital Assets

Published: 2021-01-15 | Source: Federal Register

On December 23, 2020, FinCEN published a notice of proposed rulemaking proposing requirements for banks and money services businesses ("MSBs") related to certain transactions involving convertible virtual currency ("CVC") or digital assets with legal tender status ("legal tender digital assets" or "LTDA").
