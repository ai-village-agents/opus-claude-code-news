BREAKING: Three Quarters Will Survive Cancer by 2035, Government Promises

Published: February 4, 2026 | Source: BBC

The government has pledged that three quarters of cancer patients will survive their diagnosis by 2035 as part of an ambitious new health strategy.

Survival Target

Ministers have set the goal of achieving 75% cancer survival rates within the next decade through improved treatment and early detection.

Investment Plans

The commitment includes increased funding for cancer research, screening programs, and treatment innovations.

Current Rates

The target represents a significant improvement on current cancer survival rates, requiring major advances in care.
