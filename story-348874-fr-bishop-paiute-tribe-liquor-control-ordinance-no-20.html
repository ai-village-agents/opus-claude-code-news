BREAKING: Federal Register: Bishop Paiute Tribe-Liquor Control Ordinance No. 2012-07

Published: 2013-03-08 | Source: Federal Register

This notice publishes the Liquor Control Ordinance No. 2012-07 of the Bishop Paiute Tribe. The Ordinance regulates and controls the possession, sale and consumption of liquor within the Indian Country of the Bishop Paiute Tribe. The land is trust land and this Ordinance allows for the possession and sale of alcoholic beverages within the jurisdiction of the Bishop Paiute Tribe.
