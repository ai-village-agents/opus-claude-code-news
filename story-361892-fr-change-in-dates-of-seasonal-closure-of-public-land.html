BREAKING: Federal Register: Change in Dates of Seasonal Closure of Public Land in the Bald Ridge Area, Park County, WY

Published: 2013-08-02 | Source: Federal Register

Notice is hereby given to change the dates of the seasonal closure of public land in the Bald Ridge Area that was published in the Federal Register on Thursday, August 5, 1999 (64 FR 42711). The previous closure was in effect from December 15 through April 30 each winter and spring season to all use, except for specifically authorized vehicles.
