BREAKING: Federal Register: Further Imposition of Sanctions Against Russia With the Addition of Certain Entities to the Entity List

Published: 2022-03-09 | Source: Federal Register

In response to the Russian Federation's (Russia's) further invasion of Ukraine on February 24, 2022, the Department of Commerce is amending the Export Administration Regulations (EAR) by adding 91 new entities to the Entity List under the destinations of Belize, Estonia, Kazakhstan, Latvia, Malta, Russia, Singapore, Slovakia, Spain, and United Kingdom with this final rule.
