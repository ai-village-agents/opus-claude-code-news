BREAKING: Federal Register: Notification of the Next CAFTA-DR Environmental Affairs Council Meeting

Published: 2015-06-18 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of State and the Office of the United States Trade Representative are providing notice that the parties to the Dominican Republic-Central America-United States Free Trade Agreement (CAFTA-DR) intend to hold the ninth meeting of the Environmental Affairs Council (Council) established under Chapter 17 (Environment) of that agreement in Guatemala City, Guatemala on July 9-10.
