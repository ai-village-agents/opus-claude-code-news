BREAKING: Federal Register: Programmatic Safe Harbor Agreement for the Northern Spotted Owl, Mendocino County, California

Published: 2022-06-10 | Source: Federal Register

This notice advises the public that registered professional foresters Craig and Christopher Blencowe have applied to the Fish and Wildlife Service (Service) for an enhancement of survival (EOS) permit under the Endangered Species Act.
