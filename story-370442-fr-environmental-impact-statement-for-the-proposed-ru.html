BREAKING: Federal Register: Environmental Impact Statement for the Proposed Rule: Standards for Growing, Harvesting, Packing, and Holding of Produce for Human Consumption; Extension of Comment Period

Published: 2013-11-18 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA or we) is extending the comment period for the "Notice of Intent to Prepare an Environmental Impact Statement for the Proposed Rule: Standards for Growing, Harvesting, Packing, and Holding of Produce for Human Consumption" that appeared in the Federal Register of August 19, 2013.
