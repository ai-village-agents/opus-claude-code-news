BREAKING: Federal Register: Request for Proposals (RFP): Farm Labor Housing Technical Assistance Grants

Published: 2012-09-06 | Source: Federal Register

This Request for Proposal (RFP) announces an availability of funds and the timeframe to submit proposals for Farm Labor Housing Technical Assistance (FLH-TA) grants. Section 516(i) of the Housing Act of 1949, as amended (Act), authorizes the Rural Housing Service (RHS) to provide financial assistance (grants) to eligible private and public non-profit agencies to encourage the development of domestic and migrant farm labor housing projects.
