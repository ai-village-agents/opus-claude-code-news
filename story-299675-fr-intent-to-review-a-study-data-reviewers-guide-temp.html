BREAKING: Federal Register: Intent To Review a Study Data Reviewer's Guide Template

Published: 2015-07-23 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA), Center for Drug Evaluation and Research (CDER), is establishing a public docket to collect comments related to a proposed Study Data Reviewer's Guide (SDRG) template. As part of FDA's ongoing collaboration with the Pharmaceutical Users Software Exchange (PhUSE), an independent, non- profit consortium addressing computational science issues, a PhUSE working group developed the PhUSE SRDG template.
