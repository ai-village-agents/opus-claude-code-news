BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Expansion and Proposed Restructuring of the Digital Opportunity Demonstration Program

Published: 2023-10-17 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Department of Housing and Urban Development (HUD) is committed to advancing digital opportunities in HUD-assisted communities by expanding its ConnectHomeUSA initiative to between 50 and 100 new communities. HUD's ConnectHome pilot program was launched in 2015 to address the "homework gap" for students in grades K-12 living in public and Indian housing.
