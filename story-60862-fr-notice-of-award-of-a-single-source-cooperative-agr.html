BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Award of a Single-Source Cooperative Agreement To Fund Africa Centres for Disease Control and Prevention

Published: 2023-02-21 | Source: Federal Register

The Centers for Disease Control and Prevention (CDC), located within the Department of Health and Human Services (HHS), announces the award of approximately $3,000,000 with an expected total funding of approximately $15,000,000 over a 5-year period, to Africa Centres for Disease Control and Prevention.
