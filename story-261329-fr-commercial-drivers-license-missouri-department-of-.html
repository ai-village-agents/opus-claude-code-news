BREAKING: Federal Register: Commercial Driver's License: Missouri Department of Revenue (DOR); Application for Exemption

Published: 2016-04-20 | Source: Federal Register

FMCSA announces that it has received an application from the Missouri Department of Revenue (DOR), Driver's License Bureau, for a limited exemption from the Agency's commercial driver's license (CDL) regulations. These regulations require a driver to pass the general knowledge test before being issued a Commercial Learner's Permit (CLP).
