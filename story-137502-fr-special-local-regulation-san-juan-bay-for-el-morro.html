BREAKING: Federal Register: Special Local Regulation; San Juan Bay for El Morro Downwind Challenge, San Juan, PR

Published: 2021-11-09 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is proposing to establish a temporary special local regulation for certain waters of Bah[iacute]a de San Juan. This action is necessary to provide for the safety of life on these navigable waters east of Anegado Channel and San Antonio Channel, San Juan, PR, during a paddle board race on January 8, 2022. This proposed rulemaking would prohibit persons and vessels from being in the regulated area unless authorized by the Captain of the Port San Juan or a designated representative....
