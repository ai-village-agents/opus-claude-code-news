BREAKING: Federal Register: Commercial Driver's License (CDL): Application for Exemption Renewal; U.S. Custom Harvesters, Inc.

(USCHI) exemption from the "K" intrastate restriction on commercial driver's licenses (CDLs) for custom harvester drivers operating in interstate commerce for a two-year period, with additional terms and conditions.
