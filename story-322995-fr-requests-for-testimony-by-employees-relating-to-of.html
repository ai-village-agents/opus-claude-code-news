BREAKING: Federal Register: Requests for Testimony by Employees Relating to Official Information and Production of Official Records in Litigation

Published: 2014-04-30 | Source: Federal Register

This regulation amends the Commission's provisions for release of public information and related delegation of authority by issuing procedures for requests for testimony by Federal Maritime Commission employees and production of official Commission records in litigation, and delegating responsibility for determinations relating to such procedures to the General Counsel.
