BREAKING: Federal Register: FDA Science Forum 2023; Public Workshop

Published: 2023-05-01 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA, the Agency, or we) is announcing the following public workshop entitled "FDA Science Forum 2023." The purpose of the public workshop is to inform the public about the breadth of research underway at the Agency, and to show how cutting-edge science informs FDA's regulatory decision-making to protect and promote public health.
