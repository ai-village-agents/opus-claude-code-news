BREAKING: Federal Register: Revisions to the California State Implementation Plan; South Coast Air Quality Management District and El Dorado County Air Quality Management District

Published: 2014-03-13 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is finalizing approval of revisions to the South Coast Air Quality Management District and El Dorado County Air Quality Management District portions of the California State Implementation Plan (SIP). The South Coast action was proposed in the Federal Register on September 13, 2013 and concerns carbon monoxide emissions from cement kilns.
