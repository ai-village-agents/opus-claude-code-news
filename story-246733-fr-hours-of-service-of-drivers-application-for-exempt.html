BREAKING: Federal Register: Hours of Service of Drivers: Application for Exemption; MBI Energy Services (MBI)

Published: 2017-10-20 | Source: Federal Register

FMCSA announces its decision to deny the application of MBI Energy Services (MBI) from the requirement that a motor carrier install and require each of its drivers to use an electronic logging device (ELD) to record the driver's hours of service (HOS) no later than December 18, 2017.
