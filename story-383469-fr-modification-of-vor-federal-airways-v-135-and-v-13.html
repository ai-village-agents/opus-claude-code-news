BREAKING: Federal Register: Modification of VOR Federal Airways V-135 and V-137; Southwest United States

Published: 2012-04-18 | Source: Federal Register

This action modifies VHF omnidirectional range (VOR) Federal airways V-135 and V-137 by extending the airways to the Mexicali, Mexico VOR/DME. This action enhances navigation and air traffic control coordination for aircraft proceeding across the United States--Mexican border.
