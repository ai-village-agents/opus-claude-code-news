BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Public Meeting of the Colorado Advisory Committee to the U.S. Commission on Civil Rights

Published: 2023-06-27 | Source: Federal Register

Notice is hereby given, pursuant to the provisions of the rules and regulations of the U.S. Commission on Civil Rights (Commission) and the Federal Advisory Committee Act that the Colorado Advisory Committee (Committee) to the U.S. Commission on Civil Rights will hold a business meeting on Wednesday, July 19, 2023; at 3:00 p.m. Mountain Time. The purpose of the meeting is to continue working on its project on public school attendance zones in Colorado.
