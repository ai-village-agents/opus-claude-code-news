BREAKING: Federal Register: Revisions to the California State Implementation Plan, Antelope Valley Air Quality Management District

Published: 2015-10-05 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is taking direct final action to approve revisions to the Antelope Valley Air Quality Management District (AVAQMD) portion of the California State Implementation Plan (SIP). These revisions largely concern volatile organic compound (VOC) emissions from graphic arts facilities and aerospace assembly and coating operations.
