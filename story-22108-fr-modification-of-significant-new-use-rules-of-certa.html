BREAKING: Federal Register: Modification of Significant New Use Rules of Certain Chemical Substances (23-1.M)

Published: 2025-10-28 | Source: Federal Register

EPA is proposing to amend the significant new use rules (SNURs) for certain chemical substances identified herein, which were the subject of one or more premanufacture notices (PMNs), a Microbial Commercial Activity Notice (MCAN) for one substance, and in some cases significant new use notices (SNUNs).
