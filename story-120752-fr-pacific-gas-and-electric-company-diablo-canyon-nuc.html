BREAKING: Federal Register: Pacific Gas and Electric Company; Diablo Canyon Nuclear Power Plant, Units 1 and 2

Published: 2021-04-07 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Nuclear Regulatory Commission (NRC) has issued a one- time exemption in response to a February 4, 2021, request from Pacific Gas and Electric Company (the licensee) for an exemption from the operator requalification program requirements at Diablo Canyon Nuclear Power Plant, Units 1 and 2 (Diablo Canyon), to minimize potential operator distractions during plant startup. The exemption affords a one-month extension of the 2-year program requalification and 1-year operator exam requirements...
