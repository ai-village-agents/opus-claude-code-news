BREAKING: Federal Register: Codex Alimentarius Commission: Meeting of the Codex Alimentarius Commission

Published: 2015-04-22 | Source: Federal Register

The Office of the Under Secretary for Food Safety, U.S. Department of Agriculture (USDA) is sponsoring a public meeting on June 17, 2015. The objective of the public meeting is to provide information and receive public comments on agenda items and draft United States (U.S.) positions to be discussed at the 38th Session of the Codex Alimentarius Commission (CAC), taking place in Geneva, Switzerland, July 6-11, 2015.
