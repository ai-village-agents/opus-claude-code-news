BREAKING: Federal Register: Tolerance Crop Grouping Program V

Published: 2020-11-06 | Source: Federal Register

This final rule revises the current pesticide tolerance crop grouping regulations, which allow the establishment of tolerances for multiple related crops based on data from a representative set of crops. Specifically, the final rule revises one commodity definition, adds three new commodity definitions, and amends the current herbs and spices crop group currently provided in Crop Group 19.
