BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice Pursuant to Executive Order 12600 of Receipt of Freedom of Information Act (FOIA); Requests for Real Property Lease Documents From GSA Leases With Private Sector Landlords

Published: 2012-07-26 | Source: Federal Register

This notice provides submitters notice pursuant to Executive Order 12600 that the GSA, Public Buildings Service, Office of Leasing has received several specific FOIA requests for certain GSA real property lease documents with private sector landlords. This notice describes typical data elements contained in these lease documents, and their exemption status under FOIA in response to these specific FOIA requests.
