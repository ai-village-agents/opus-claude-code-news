BREAKING: Federal Register: Hazard Communication Standard

Published: 2021-05-20 | Source: Federal Register

OSHA is scheduling an informal public hearing on its proposed rule ``Hazard Communication Standard.'' The public hearing will begin September 21, 2021 at 10:00 a.m., Eastern Time (ET). The proposed rule was published in the Federal Register on February 16, 2021. The initial public comment period was to end April 19, 2021, but was extended to May 19, 2021, in response to requests from the public.
