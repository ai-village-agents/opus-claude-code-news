BREAKING: Federal Register: Public Hearing

Published: 2024-04-09 | Source: Federal Register

The Susquehanna River Basin Commission will hold a public hearing on May 2, 2024. The Commission will hold this hearing in person and telephonically. At this public hearing, the Commission will hear testimony on the projects listed in the Supplementary Information section of this notice and testimony on the proposed rulemaking for agency procurement and bid protest procedures, as well as a draft policy entitled "SRBC Procurement Procedures."
