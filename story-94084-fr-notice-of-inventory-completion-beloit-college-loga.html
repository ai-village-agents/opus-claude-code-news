BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Inventory Completion: Beloit College, Logan Museum of Anthropology, Beloit, WI

Published: 2022-04-20 | Source: Federal Register

Beloit College, Logan Museum of Anthropology has completed an inventory of human remains, in consultation with the appropriate Indian Tribes or Native Hawaiian organizations and has determined that there is no cultural affiliation between the human remains and any present- day Indian Tribes or Native Hawaiian organizations.
