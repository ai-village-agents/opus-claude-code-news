BREAKING: Federal Register: Federal Acquisition Regulation; Service Contracts Reporting Requirements

Published: 2013-12-31 | Source: Federal Register

DoD, GSA, and NASA are issuing a final rule amending the Federal Acquisition Regulation (FAR) to implement a section of the Consolidated Appropriations Act, 2010. This final rule amends the FAR to require service contractors for executive agencies, except where DoD has fully funded the contract or order, to submit information annually in support of agency-level inventories for service contracts.
