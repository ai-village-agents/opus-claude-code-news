BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Inventory Completion: Logan Museum of Anthropology, Beloit College, Beloit, WI

Published: 2012-08-14 | Source: Federal Register

The Logan Museum of Anthropology, Beloit College, has completed an inventory of human remains and associated funerary objects, in consultation with the appropriate Indian tribes, and has determined that there is a cultural affiliation between the human remains and associated funerary objects and a present-day Indian tribe.
