BREAKING: Federal Register: Fifth Generation Wireless Network and Device Security

Published: 2017-01-23 | Source: Federal Register

In this document, the Commission seeks comment on new security issues that implementation of the fifth generation (5G) wireless network and device security presents to the general public, and on the current state of planning to address these issues. The inquiry, focusing on cybersecurity for 5G, raises fundamental questions about scope and responsibilities for such security.
