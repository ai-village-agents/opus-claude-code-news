BREAKING: Federal Register: Prospective Grant of an Exclusive Patent License: The Development of an Anti-BCMA Immunotoxin for the Treatment of Human Cancer

Published: 2018-06-07 | Source: Federal Register

The National Cancer Institute, an institute of the National Institutes of Health, Department of Health and Human Services, is contemplating the grant of an Exclusive Patent License to practice the inventions embodied in the Patents and Patent Applications listed in the Supplementary Information section of this notice to BEORO Therapeutics, GmbH. ("Beoro") located in Seefeld, Germany.
