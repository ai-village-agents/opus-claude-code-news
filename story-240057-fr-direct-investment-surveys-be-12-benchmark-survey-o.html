BREAKING: Federal Register: Direct Investment Surveys: BE-12, Benchmark Survey of Foreign Direct Investment in the United States

Published: 2017-07-27 | Source: Federal Register

This proposed rule would amend regulations of the Department of Commerce's Bureau of Economic Analysis (BEA) to set forth the reporting requirements for the 2017 BE-12, Benchmark Survey of Foreign Direct Investment in the United States. The BE-12 survey is conducted every five years; the prior survey covered 2012.
