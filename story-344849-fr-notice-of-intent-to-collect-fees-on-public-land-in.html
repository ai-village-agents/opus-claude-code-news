BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Intent To Collect Fees on Public Land in Clark County, ID

Published: 2013-01-23 | Source: Federal Register

Pursuant to applicable provisions of the Federal Lands Recreation Enhancement Act (REA), the Bureau of Land Management (BLM) Upper Snake Field Office is proposing to collect a reservation fee for large group sites at the Birch Creek Campground in Clark County, ID. Under Section 2(2) of the REA, Birch Creek Campground qualifies as a site wherein visitors can be charged an "Expanded Amenity Recreation Fee" authorized under section 3(g).
