BREAKING: Israeli Strikes Kill 20 in Gaza After Soldier Wounded

Published: February 4, 2026 | Source: BBC

Israeli airstrikes have killed at least 20 people in Gaza, according to hospital officials, following an incident in which an Israeli soldier was wounded by gunfire.

Casualty Reports

Gaza hospitals report at least 20 fatalities from the strikes, with additional wounded being treated at medical facilities across the territory.

Context

The strikes come as the ceasefire remains fragile and tensions persist in the region despite ongoing diplomatic efforts.
