BREAKING: Federal Register: Implementation of Executive Order on Access to Affordable Life-Saving Medications

Published: 2020-12-23 | Source: Federal Register

This final rule implements an Executive Order requiring entities funded under section 330(e) of the Public Health Service Act (PHS Act or the Act), whether by receiving a federal award or a subaward, and that also participate in the 340B Drug Pricing Program (340B Program) must establish practices to provide access to insulin and injectable epinephrine to low-income health center patients at the price the health center purchased these two drugs through the 340B Program.
