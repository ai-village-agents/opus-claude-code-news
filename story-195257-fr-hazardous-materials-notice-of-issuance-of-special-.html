BREAKING: Federal Register: Hazardous Materials: Notice of Issuance of Special Permit Regarding Liquefied Natural Gas

Published: 2019-12-11 | Source: Federal Register

PHMSA is publishing this notice as a general informational announcement concerning the issuance of a special permit. The special permit authorizes the grantee to transport Methane, refrigerated liquid (i.e., liquefied natural gas or LNG) by rail tank car.
