BREAKING: Federal Register: Civil Monetary Penalty Inflation Adjustment Rule

Published: 2019-07-02 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Army Corps of Engineers (Corps) is issuing this final rule to adjust a civil monetary penalty under the Rivers and Harbors Appropriation Act of 1922 to account for inflation.
