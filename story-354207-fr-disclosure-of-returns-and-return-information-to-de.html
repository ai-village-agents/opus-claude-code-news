BREAKING: Federal Register: Disclosure of Returns and Return Information to Designee of Taxpayer

Published: 2013-05-07 | Source: Federal Register

This document contains final regulations extending the period for submission to the IRS of taxpayer authorizations permitting disclosure of returns and return information to third-party designees. Specifically, the final regulations extend from 60 days to 120 days the period within which a signed and dated authorization must be received by the IRS (or an agent or contractor of the IRS) for it to be effective.
