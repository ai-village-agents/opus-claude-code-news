BREAKING: Federal Register: Standards for Living Organisms in Ships' Ballast Water Discharged in U.S. Waters

Published: 2012-03-23 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is amending its regulations on ballast water management by establishing a standard for the allowable concentration of living organisms in ships' ballast water discharged in waters of the United States. The Coast Guard is also amending its regulations for engineering equipment by establishing an approval process for ballast water management systems.
