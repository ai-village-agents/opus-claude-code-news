BREAKING: Federal Register: National Poultry Improvement Plan and Auxiliary Provisions

Published: 2020-10-05 | Source: Federal Register

We are amending the regulations governing the National Poultry Improvement Plan (NPIP). These amendments establish a U.S. Newcastle Disease Clean program within the NPIP, create an NPIP subpart specific to game birds, revise testing requirements, and clarify existing provisions of the regulations.
