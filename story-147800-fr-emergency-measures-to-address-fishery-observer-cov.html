BREAKING: Federal Register: Emergency Measures To Address Fishery Observer Coverage During the COVID-19 Coronavirus Pandemic

Published: 2020-03-27 | Source: Federal Register

NMFS issues this temporary rule (also referred to herein as "emergency action") to provide it with authority to waive observer coverage requirements established in regulations promulgated under the Magnuson-Stevens Fishery Conservation and Management Act (MSA) and other statutes, consistent with applicable law and international obligations.
