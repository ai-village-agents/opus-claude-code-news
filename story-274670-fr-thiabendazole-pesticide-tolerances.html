BREAKING: Federal Register: Thiabendazole; Pesticide Tolerances

Published: 2016-09-22 | Source: Federal Register

This regulation establishes tolerances for residues of thiabendazole in or on the legume vegetable group 6 and foliage of legume vegetable group 7. Syngenta Crop Protection requested these tolerances under the Federal Food, Drug, and Cosmetic Act (FFDCA).
