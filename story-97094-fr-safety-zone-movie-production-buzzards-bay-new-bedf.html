BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone; Movie Production; Buzzards Bay, New Bedford, MA

Published: 2022-05-31 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is establishing a temporary safety zone for navigable waters within a 150-yard radius of movie production vessels filming near the hurricane barrier in New Bedford, Massachusetts. The safety zone is needed to protect cast members in the water and vessels operating in the area during movie production operations.
