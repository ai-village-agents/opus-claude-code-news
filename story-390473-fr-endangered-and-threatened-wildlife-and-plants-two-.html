BREAKING: Federal Register: Endangered and Threatened Wildlife and Plants; Two Foreign Macaw Species

Published: 2012-07-06 | Source: Federal Register

We, the U.S. Fish and Wildlife Service (Service), propose to list as endangered the military macaw (Ara militaris) and the great green macaw (Ara ambiguus) under the Endangered Species Act of 1973, as amended (ESA). We are taking this action in response to a petition to list these parrot species as endangered or threatened under the ESA.
