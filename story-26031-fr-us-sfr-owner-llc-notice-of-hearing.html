BREAKING: Federal Register: US SFR Owner, LLC; Notice of Hearing

Published: 2025-12-23 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Nuclear Regulatory Commission (NRC or the Commission) will commence an uncontested hearing, in which the Commission will receive written evidence in the form of testimony and exhibits regarding the application submitted by TerraPower, LLC (TerraPower), on behalf of US SFR Owner, LLC (USO), for a construction permit to construct a nuclear-powered generating plant termed Kemmerer Power Station Unit 1 in Lincoln County, Wyoming.
