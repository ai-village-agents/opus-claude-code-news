BREAKING: Federal Register: Amateur Radio Service Rules To Permit Greater Flexibility in Data Communications

Published: 2023-12-07 | Source: Federal Register

In this document, the Federal Communications Commission (Commission) adopted a Further Notice of Proposed Rulemaking (FNPRM) that proposes to remove the baud rate limitation in the 135.7-137.8 kHz (2200 meter band), 472-479 kHz (630 meter band), the very high frequency (VHF) bands, and the ultra-high frequency (UHF) band in the amateur radio service.
