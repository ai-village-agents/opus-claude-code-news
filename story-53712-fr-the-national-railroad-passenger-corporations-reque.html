BREAKING: Federal Register: The National Railroad Passenger Corporation's Request To Amend Its Positive Train Control Safety Plan and Positive Train Control System

Published: 2024-11-27 | Source: Federal Register

This document provides the public with notice that, on November 14, 2024, the National Railroad Passenger Corporation (Amtrak) submitted a request for amendment (RFA) to its FRA-approved Positive Train Control Safety Plan (PTCSP) to provide supplemental information to its initial RFA, submitted November 7, 2024, to support updates to Amtrak's Onboard Computer (OBC) Serial platform and Fusion software release.
