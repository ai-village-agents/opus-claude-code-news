BREAKING: Federal Register: Record of Decision for the Coral Reef Restoration Plan, Biscayne National Park, FL

Published: 2013-03-14 | Source: Federal Register

Pursuant to the National Environmental Policy Act (NEPA) of 1969, 42 U.S.C. 4332(2)(C), the National Park Service announces the availability of the Record of Decision (ROD) for the Coral Reef Restoration Plan (Plan) for Biscayne National Park, Florida. On May 31, 2012, the Regional Director, Southeast Region, approved the ROD for the project.
