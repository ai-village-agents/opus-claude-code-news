BREAKING: Federal Register: Certain Electronic Devices, Including Streaming Players, Televisions, Set Top Boxes, Remote Controllers, and Components Thereof; Commission Determination To Review the Final Initial Determination in Part and To Request Written Submissions on the Issues Under Review, Remedy, Bonding, and the Public Interest

Published: 2021-09-15 | Source: Federal Register

Notice is hereby given that the U.S. International Trade Commission (``Commission'') has determined to review in part certain findings in a final initial determination (``ID'') issued by the presiding administrative law judge (``ALJ'') and to solicit briefing on the issues under review, as well as remedy, bonding, and the public interest.
