BREAKING: Federal Register: Branch Technical Position on Concentration Averaging and Encapsulation

Published: 2012-06-11 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Nuclear Regulatory Commission (NRC or the Commission) is soliciting public comments on a revised draft Revision 1 of its Branch Technical Position on Concentration Averaging and Encapsulation (CA BTP). An earlier draft was completed in August 2011 and made available to the public in September 2011 (ADAMS Accession No. ML112061191).
