BREAKING: Federal Register: "Specially Designed" Definition

Published: 2012-06-19 | Source: Federal Register

As part of the President's Export Control Reform (ECR) Initiative, this proposed rule, and a separate proposed rule from the Department of State, Directorate of Defense Trade Controls, being published in conjunction with this document, sets forth, as much as possible, a common definition of the term "specially designed" for use in the Export Administration Regulations (EAR) and the International Traffic in Arms Regulations (ITAR).
