BREAKING: Federal Register: FAA Approval of Noise Compatibility Program for Hawthorne Municipal Airport, Hawthorne, California

Published: 2018-02-20 | Source: Federal Register

The Federal Aviation Administration (FAA) announces its findings on the noise compatibility program submitted by the City of Hawthorne, California. On April 11, 2014, the FAA determined that the noise exposure maps submitted by the City of Hawthorne were in compliance with applicable requirements. On December 18, 2017, the FAA approved the Hawthorne Municipal Airport Noise Compatibility Program. All 11 of the recommendations of the program were approved.
