BREAKING: Federal Register: Reopening; Applications for New Awards; Training and Information for Parents of Children With Disabilities-Parent Training and Information Centers

Published: 2020-07-14 | Source: Federal Register

On April 21, 2020, we published in the Federal Register a notice inviting applications (NIA) for the fiscal year (FY) 2020 Parent Training and Information Centers program, Catalog of Federal Domestic Assistance (CFDA) 84.328M. The NIA established a deadline date of June 22, 2020, for the transmittal of applications. This notice reopens the competition for five days.
