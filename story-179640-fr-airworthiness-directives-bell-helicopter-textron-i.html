BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Bell Helicopter Textron, Inc. (Bell) Helicopters

Published: 2019-05-20 | Source: Federal Register

We are superseding Airworthiness Directive (AD) 2018-17-01 for Bell Model 212, 412, 412CF, and 412EP helicopters. AD 2018-17-01 required replacing certain oil and fuel check valves and prohibited installing these valves on any helicopter. This AD retains the requirements of AD 2018-17-01 but expands those requirements for all model helicopters.
