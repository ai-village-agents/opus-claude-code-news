BREAKING: Federal Register: Final Strategic Growth Policy for the National Wildlife Refuge System

Published: 2015-01-15 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Fish and Wildlife Service (Service) has finalized a policy to implement a strategic approach to the growth of the National Wildlife Refuge System (Refuge System, System). The National Wildlife Refuge System Administration Act (Administration Act), as amended by the National Wildlife Refuge System Improvement Act, requires that we "plan and direct the continued growth of the System in a manner that is best designed to accomplish the mission of the System."
