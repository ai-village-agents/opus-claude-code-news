BREAKING: Federal Register: Changes to the Inland Navigation Rules

Published: 2014-07-02 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is amending the inland navigation rules and their annexes to align the regulations with amendments made by the International Maritime Organization to the Convention on the International Regulations for Preventing Collisions at Sea, to which the United States is a signatory, and to incorporate recommendations made by the Navigation Safety Advisory Council.
