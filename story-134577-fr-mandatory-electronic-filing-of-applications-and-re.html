BREAKING: Federal Register: Mandatory Electronic Filing of Applications and Reports Administered by the International Bureau

Published: 2021-10-01 | Source: Federal Register

In this document, the Commission requires that any remaining applications and reports administered by the International Bureau and filed on paper or through an alternative filing process be filed only electronically through the Commission's International Bureau Filing System.
