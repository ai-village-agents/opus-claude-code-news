BREAKING: Federal Register: Proposed Guidance on Safe Harbor Rate Streamlining for Engineering and Design Services Consultant Contracts

Published: 2018-07-17 | Source: Federal Register

The FHWA is soliciting comments regarding proposed guidance on implementation of a Safe Harbor indirect cost rate for certain engineering design service firms that find establishing such rates to be costly and a barrier to participating in engineering and design service contracts reimbursed with Federal-aid Highway Program (FAHP) Funds.
