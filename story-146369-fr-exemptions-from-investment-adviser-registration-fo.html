BREAKING: Federal Register: Exemptions From Investment Adviser Registration for Advisers to Certain Rural Business Investment Companies

Published: 2020-03-10 | Source: Federal Register

We are amending the definition of the term "venture capital fund" and the private fund adviser exemption under the Investment Advisers Act of 1940 (the "Advisers Act") to reflect in our rules exemptions from registration for investment advisers who advise rural business investment companies ("RBICs"). These exemptions were enacted as part of the RBIC Advisers Relief Act of 2018 (the "RBIC Advisers Relief Act"), which amended Advisers Act sections 203(l) and 203(m), among other provisions.
