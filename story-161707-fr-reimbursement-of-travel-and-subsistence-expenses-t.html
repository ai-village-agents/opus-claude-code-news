BREAKING: Federal Register: Reimbursement of Travel and Subsistence Expenses Toward Living Organ Donation Program Eligibility Guidelines

A notice was published in the Federal Register on March 31, 2020, to solicit comments on the eligibility criteria that were proposed by HRSA concerning the Living Organ Donation Reimbursement Program (formerly Reimbursement of Travel and Subsistence Expenses toward Living Organ Donation Program).
