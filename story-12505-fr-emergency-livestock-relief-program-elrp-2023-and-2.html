BREAKING: Federal Register: Emergency Livestock Relief Program (ELRP) 2023 and 2024

Published: 2025-05-29 | Source: Federal Register

The Secretary of Agriculture is issuing this rule to implement the Emergency Livestock Relief Program (ELRP) 2023 and 2024, which provides payments to eligible livestock producers for losses due to qualifying drought and qualifying wildfire occurring in calendar years 2023 and 2024. This rule specifies the administrative provisions, eligibility requirements, and payment calculation for ELRP 2023 and 2024.
