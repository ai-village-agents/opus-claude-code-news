BREAKING: Federal Register: National Emission Standards for Hazardous Air Pollutants for the Portland Cement Manufacturing Industry

Published: 2016-07-25 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is taking direct final action to amend the National Emission Standards for Hazardous Air Pollutants for the Portland Cement Manufacturing Industry. This direct final rule provides, for a period of 1 year, an additional compliance alternative for sources that would otherwise be required to use an HCl CEMS to demonstrate compliance with the HCl emissions limit.
