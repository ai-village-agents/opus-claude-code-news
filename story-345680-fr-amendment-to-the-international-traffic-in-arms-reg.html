BREAKING: Federal Register: Amendment to the International Traffic in Arms Regulations: Revision of U.S. Munitions List Category IV

Published: 2013-01-31 | Source: Federal Register

As part of the President's Export Control Reform effort, the Department of State proposes to amend the International Traffic in Arms Regulations (ITAR) to revise Category IV (launch vehicles, guided missiles, ballistic missiles, rockets, torpedoes, bombs, and mines) of the U.S. Munitions List (USML) to describe more precisely the articles warranting control on the USML.
