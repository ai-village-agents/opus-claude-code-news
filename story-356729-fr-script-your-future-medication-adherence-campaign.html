BREAKING: Federal Register: "Script Your Future" Medication Adherence Campaign

Published: 2013-06-06 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA) is announcing the availability of grant funds for the continuing support of a national effort to promote the importance of medication adherence to enhance the health of Americans. Medication adherence is taking medicine as directed to treat an illness or disease in order to get the best health outcome possible for each patient.
