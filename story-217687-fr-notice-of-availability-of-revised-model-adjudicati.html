BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Availability of Revised Model Adjudication Rules

Published: 2018-10-02 | Source: Federal Register

The Office of the Chairman of the Administrative Conference of the United States, through its Model Adjudication Rules Working Group, has completed a revision of the Conference's 1993 Model Adjudication Rules.
