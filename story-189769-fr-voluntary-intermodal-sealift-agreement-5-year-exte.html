BREAKING: Federal Register: Voluntary Intermodal Sealift Agreement-5 Year Extension

Published: 2019-09-30 | Source: Federal Register

The Maritime Administration (MARAD) announces the extension of the Voluntary Intermodal Sealift Agreement (VISA) until October 1, 2024, pursuant to section 708 of the Defense Production Act of 1950, as amended.
