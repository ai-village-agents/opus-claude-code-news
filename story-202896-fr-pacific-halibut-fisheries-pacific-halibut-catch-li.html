BREAKING: Federal Register: Pacific Halibut Fisheries; Pacific Halibut Catch Limits for Area 2A Fisheries in 2018

Published: 2018-03-26 | Source: Federal Register

This interim final rule sets the 2018 Pacific halibut catch limit in the International Pacific Halibut Commission's Regulatory Area 2A off Washington, Oregon, and California. The International Pacific Halibut Commission, at its annual meeting, did not recommend 2018 catch limits for any of its regulatory areas, including Area 2A.
