BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Effective Date of Modifications to a Rule of Origin of the United States-Australia Free Trade Agreement

Published: 2012-05-29 | Source: Federal Register

In Proclamation 8334 of December 31, 2008, the President modified the rules of origin for certain goods of Australia under the USAFTA. While these modifications were incorporated in the Harmonized Tariff Schedule of the United States (the "HTS") at that time, the proclamation stated that the modifications would be effective on a date that the United States Trade Representative (USTR) announced in the Federal Register.
