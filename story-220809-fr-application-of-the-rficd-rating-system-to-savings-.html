BREAKING: Federal Register: Application of the RFI/C(D) Rating System to Savings and Loan Holding Companies

Published: 2018-11-09 | Source: Federal Register

The Board has determined that it will apply the RFI/C(D) rating system to certain savings and loan holding companies (SLHCs). This is the same supervisory rating system that the Board currently applies to bank holding companies (BHCs). SLHCs that are engaged in significant commercial or insurance activities will continue to receive indicative supervisory ratings.
