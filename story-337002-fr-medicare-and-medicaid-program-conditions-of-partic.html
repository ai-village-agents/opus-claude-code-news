BREAKING: Federal Register: Medicare and Medicaid Program: Conditions of Participation for Home Health Agencies

Published: 2014-10-09 | Source: Federal Register

This proposed rule would revise the current conditions of participation (CoPs) that home health agencies (HHAs) must meet in order to participate in the Medicare and Medicaid programs. The proposed requirements would focus on the care delivered to patients by home health agencies, reflect an interdisciplinary view of patient care, allow home health agencies greater flexibility in meeting quality care standards, and eliminate unnecessary procedural requirements.
