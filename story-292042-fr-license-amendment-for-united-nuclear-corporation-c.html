BREAKING: Federal Register: License Amendment for United Nuclear Corporation, Church Rock Facility, McKinley County, New Mexico

Published: 2015-04-23 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Nuclear Regulatory Commission (NRC) is considering amending Source Materials License SUA-1475 issued to the United Nuclear Corporation (UNC), a subsidiary of General Electric (GE), to revise current ground water protection standards in License Condition 30.B of SUA-1475. The NRC has prepared an environmental assessment (EA) for this proposed action in accordance with its regulations.
