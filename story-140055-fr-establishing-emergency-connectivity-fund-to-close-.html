BREAKING: Federal Register: Establishing Emergency Connectivity Fund To Close the Homework Gap

Published: 2021-12-14 | Source: Federal Register

In this document, the Wireline Competition Bureau (the Bureau) grants a petition for an expedited waiver of the Emergency Connectivity Fund (ECF) Program's invoice filing deadline submitted by the State E- rate Coordinators' Alliance (SECA) and clarifies the service delivery date for certain funding requests.
