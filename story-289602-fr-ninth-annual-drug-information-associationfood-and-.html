BREAKING: Federal Register: Ninth Annual Drug Information Association/Food and Drug Administration Statistics Forum-2015; Public Conference

Published: 2015-03-25 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA), in cosponsorship with the Drug Information Association (DIA), is announcing a public conference entitled "Ninth Annual DIA/FDA Statistics Forum--2015". The purpose of the conference is to discuss relevant statistical issues associated with the development and review of therapeutic drugs and biologics.
