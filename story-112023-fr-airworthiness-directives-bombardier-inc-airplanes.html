BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Bombardier, Inc., Airplanes

Published: 2022-12-09 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA is adopting a new airworthiness directive (AD) for certain Bombardier, Inc., Model BD-700-1A10 and BD-700-1A11 airplanes. This AD was prompted by reports of the passenger door failing to dampen during opening at regularly scheduled maintenance checks, causing the door to open more rapidly than normal.
