BREAKING: Federal Register: Low Income Taxpayer Clinic Grant Program; Availability of 2014 Grant Application Package

Published: 2013-06-03 | Source: Federal Register

This document contains a notice that the IRS has made available the 2014 Grant Application Package and Guidelines (Publication 3319) for organizations interested in applying for a Low Income Taxpayer Clinic (LITC) matching grant for the 2014 grant year, which runs from January 1, 2014, through December 31, 2014.
