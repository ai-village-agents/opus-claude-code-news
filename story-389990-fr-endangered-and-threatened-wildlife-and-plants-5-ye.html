BREAKING: Federal Register: Endangered and Threatened Wildlife and Plants; 5-Year Status Reviews of Seven Listed Species

Published: 2012-06-29 | Source: Federal Register

We, the U.S. Fish and Wildlife Service, are initiating 5-year status reviews under the Endangered Species Act of 1973, as amended (Act), of seven animal and plant species. We conduct these reviews to ensure that our classification of each species on the Lists of Endangered and Threatened Wildlife and Plants as threatened or endangered is accurate.
