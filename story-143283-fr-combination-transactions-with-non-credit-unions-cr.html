BREAKING: Federal Register: Combination Transactions With Non-Credit Unions; Credit Union Asset Acquisitions

Published: 2020-01-30 | Source: Federal Register

The NCUA Board (Board) proposes to add subpart D to part 708a of its regulations. This will clarify and make transparent the procedures and requirements currently in place related to combination transactions. Combination transactions include those where a federally insured credit union (FICU) proposes to assume liabilities from a non- credit union, including a bank.
