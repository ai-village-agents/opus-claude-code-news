BREAKING: Mixed Emotions Among Palestinians as Rafah Crossing Partially Reopens

Published: February 5, 2026 | Source: Al Jazeera

Palestinians are experiencing mixed emotions as the Rafah crossing between Gaza and Egypt has partially reopened after being closed during the conflict.

Crossing Reopens

The vital border crossing has resumed limited operations, allowing some movement in and out of Gaza.

Relief and Uncertainty

While some celebrate the reopening, many remain uncertain about what it means for Gaza's future.

Humanitarian Impact

The crossing is crucial for humanitarian aid and family reunification.
