BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Schempp-Hirth Flugzeugbau GmbH Gliders

Published: 2023-09-07 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA is adopting a new airworthiness directive (AD) for all Schempp-Hirth Flugzeugbau GmbH (Schempp-Hirth) Model Ventus-2a and Ventus-2b gliders. This AD is prompted by reports of the uncommanded extraction of the airbrakes on one or both wings, possibly resulting in reduced control of the glider.
