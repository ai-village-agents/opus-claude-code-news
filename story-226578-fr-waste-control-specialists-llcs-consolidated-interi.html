BREAKING: Federal Register: Waste Control Specialists LLC's Consolidated Interim Spent Fuel Storage Facility Project

Published: 2017-01-30 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Nuclear Regulatory Commission (NRC) received a license application from Waste Control Specialists LLC (WCS) by letter dated April 28, 2016, as supplemented on July 20, August 19, August 31, September 27, October 7, November 16, December 16, and December 22, 2016, respectively. By this application, WCS is requesting authorization to construct and operate a Consolidated Interim Storage Facility (CISF) for spent nuclear fuel at WCS's facility in Andrews County, Texas.
