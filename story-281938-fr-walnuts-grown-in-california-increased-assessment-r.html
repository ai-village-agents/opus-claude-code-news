BREAKING: Federal Register: Walnuts Grown in California; Increased Assessment Rate

Published: 2016-12-19 | Source: Federal Register

This rule implements a recommendation from the California Walnut Board (Board) to increase the assessment rate established for the 2016-17 and subsequent marketing years from $0.0379 to $0.0465 per kernelweight pound of assessable walnuts. The Board locally administers the marketing order and is comprised of growers and handlers of walnuts operating within the area of production.
