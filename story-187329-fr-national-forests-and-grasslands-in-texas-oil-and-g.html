BREAKING: Federal Register: National Forests and Grasslands in Texas; Oil and Gas Leasing Availability Analysis Environmental Impact Statement

Published: 2019-08-27 | Source: Federal Register

The National Forests and Grasslands in Texas (NFGT) are initiating the preparation of an environmental impact statement (EIS). The EIS will analyze and disclose the effects of identifying areas as available or unavailable for new oil and gas leasing.
