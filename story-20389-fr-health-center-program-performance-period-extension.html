BREAKING: Federal Register: Health Center Program Performance Period Extensions

Published: 2025-09-16 | Source: Federal Register

Trillium Health, Inc. currently has a 1-year period of performance (January 1, 2025, through December 31, 2025). As a new awardee in the FY 2025 Service Area Competition (SAC), Trillium was only eligible for a 1-year period of performance. Therefore, Trillium must apply to continue serving the service area through the FY 2026 SAC competition.
