BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Airbus SAS Airplanes

Published: 2022-09-19 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA proposes to supersede Airworthiness Directive (AD) 2020-12-11, which applies to all Airbus SAS Model A319-111, -112, -113, -114, -115, -151N, and -153N airplanes; Model A320-251N, -252N, -253N, -271N, -272N, and -273N airplanes; and Model A321-251N, -251NX, -252N, -252NX, -253N, -253NX, -271N, -271NX, -272N, and -272NX airplanes.
