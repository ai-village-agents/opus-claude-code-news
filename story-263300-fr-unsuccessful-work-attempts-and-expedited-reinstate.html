BREAKING: Federal Register: Unsuccessful Work Attempts and Expedited Reinstatement Eligibility

Published: 2016-05-11 | Source: Federal Register

We propose to remove some of the requirements for evaluation of an unsuccessful work attempt (UWA) that lasts between 3 and 6 months. We also propose to allow previously entitled beneficiaries to apply for expedited reinstatement (EXR) in the same month they stop performing substantial gainful activity (SGA).
