BREAKING: Federal Register: Filing Dates for the Minnesota Special Elections in the 1st Congressional District

Published: 2022-03-08 | Source: Federal Register

Minnesota has scheduled special elections on May 24, 2022, and August 9, 2022, to fill the U.S. House of Representatives seat in the 1st Congressional District held by the late Representative Jim Hagedorn. Committees required to file reports in connection with the Special Primary Election on May 24, 2022, shall file a 12-day Pre- Primary Report.
