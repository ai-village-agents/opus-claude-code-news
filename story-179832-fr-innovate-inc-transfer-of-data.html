BREAKING: Federal Register: Innovate, Inc.; Transfer of Data

Published: 2019-05-22 | Source: Federal Register

This notice announces that pesticide related information submitted to EPA's Office of Pesticide Programs (OPP) pursuant to the Federal Insecticide, Fungicide, and Rodenticide Act (FIFRA) and the Federal Food, Drug, and Cosmetic Act (FFDCA), including information that may have been claimed as Confidential Business Information (CBI) by the submitter, will be transferred to Innovate, Inc. in accordance with the CBI regulations.
