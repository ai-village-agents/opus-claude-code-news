BREAKING: Federal Register: Commercial Driver's License Standards: Application for Exemption; Wilson Logistics

Published: 2019-11-06 | Source: Federal Register

FMCSA announces that Wilson Logistics has applied for an exemption from the requirement that the holder of a Commercial Learner's Permit (CLP) be accompanied by the holder of a Commercial Driver's License (CDL), seated in the front seat, while the commercial motor vehicle (CMV) is being driven by the CLP holder.
