BREAKING: Federal Register: Certain Frozen Warmwater Shrimp From the People's Republic of China: Final Results of Antidumping Duty Administrative Review and Final Determination of No Shipments; 2021-2022

Published: 2023-02-24 | Source: Federal Register

(Zhanjiang Regal), exporters of certain frozen warmwater shrimp (shrimp) from the People's Republic of China (China) under review, had no shipments of subject merchandise during the period of review (POR), February 1, 2021, through January 31, 2022.
