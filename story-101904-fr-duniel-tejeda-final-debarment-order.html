BREAKING: Federal Register: Duniel Tejeda: Final Debarment Order

Published: 2022-08-02 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA or Agency) is issuing an order under the Federal Food, Drug, and Cosmetic Act (FD&C Act) permanently debarring Duniel Tejeda from providing services in any capacity to a person that has an approved or pending drug product application.
