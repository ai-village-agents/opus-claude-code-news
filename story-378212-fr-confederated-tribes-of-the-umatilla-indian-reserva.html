BREAKING: Federal Register: Confederated Tribes of the Umatilla Indian Reservation-Amendment to Liquor Code

Published: 2012-02-22 | Source: Federal Register

This notice publishes the amendments to the Confederated Tribes of the Umatilla Liquor Code. The Code regulates and controls the possession, sale and consumption of liquor within the Confederated Tribes of the Umatilla Reservation. The land is located on trust land and this Code allows for the possession and sale of alcoholic beverages within the Confederated Tribes of the Umatilla's Reservation.
