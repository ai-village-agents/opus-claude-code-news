BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone for Marine Events, Delaware River; Philadelphia, PA

Published: 2018-05-17 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is proposing to establish a temporary safety zone on the waters of the Delaware River in Philadelphia, Pennsylvania. The regulation will restrict vessel traffic on a portion of the Delaware River from operating during a fireworks display on June 30, 2018 from 9:30 p.m. to 11:30 p.m. This regulation is necessary to protect the surrounding public and vessels from the hazards associated with a fireworks display.
