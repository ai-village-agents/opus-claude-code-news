BREAKING: Federal Register: Manufacturing USA Semiconductor Institutes

Published: 2022-10-13 | Source: Federal Register

The National Institute of Standards and Technology (NIST) is seeking public input to inform the design of, and requirements for, potential Manufacturing USA institutes to strengthen the semiconductor and microelectronics innovation ecosystem, which could include design, fabrication, advanced test, assembly, and packaging capability.
