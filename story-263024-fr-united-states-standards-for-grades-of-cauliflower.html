BREAKING: Federal Register: United States Standards for Grades of Cauliflower

Published: 2016-05-09 | Source: Federal Register

The Agricultural Marketing Service (AMS) is proposing to revise the United States Standards for Grades of Cauliflower. The current U.S. grade standards do not have provisions for grading purple, orange, or green cauliflower. The proposed revision would amend the color requirement to allow all colors of cauliflower to be certified to a U.S. grade.
