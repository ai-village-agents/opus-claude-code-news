BREAKING: Federal Register: Rescinding New Construction Requirements Related to Nondiscrimination in Federally Assisted Programs or Activities

Published: 2025-05-16 | Source: Federal Register

This DFR rescinds certain new construction requirements related to disability nondiscrimination in federally assisted programs or activities.
