BREAKING: Federal Register: Supplemental Guidance for Radiological Consequence Analyses Using Alternative Source Terms

Published: 2021-05-21 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Nuclear Regulatory Commission (NRC) is soliciting public comment on its draft Interim Staff Guidance (ISG) Supplemental Guidance for Radiological Consequence Analyses Using Alternative Source Terms. This draft ISG proposes changes to the NRC's license amendment request (LAR) review guidance document.
