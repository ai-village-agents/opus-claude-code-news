BREAKING: Federal Register: Collecting and Reporting of Foreign Indebtedness Within the Department of Defense

Published: 2019-08-19 | Source: Federal Register

This final rule removes the Department of Defense (DoD) regulation that relates to the collecting and reporting of foreign indebtedness because it contains DoD's internal guidelines on identifying, billing, collecting, and managing foreign arrearages and indebtedness. These guidelines are internal and provide performance and reporting requirements to the Defense Finance and Accounting Service, the Defense Security Cooperation Agency, and the DoD Components.
