BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Inventory Completion: The American Museum of Natural History, New York, NY; Correction

Published: 2015-05-22 | Source: Federal Register

The American Museum of Natural History has revised a Notice of Inventory Completion that was published in the Federal Register on February 4, 2015. This notice revises the listing of Indian tribes who are recognized as aboriginal to the area from which Native American human remains were removed.
