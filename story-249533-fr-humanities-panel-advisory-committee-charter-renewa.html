BREAKING: Federal Register: Humanities Panel Advisory Committee; Charter Renewal

Published: 2017-11-28 | Source: Federal Register

Pursuant to section 9(a)(2) of the Federal Advisory Committee Act and its implementing regulations, the National Endowment for the Humanities (NEH) gives notice that the Charter for the Humanities Panel advisory committee will be renewed for an additional two-year period on November 24, 2017.
