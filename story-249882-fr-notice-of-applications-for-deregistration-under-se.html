BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Applications for Deregistration Under Section 8(f) of the Investment Company Act of 1940

Published: 2017-12-01 | Source: Federal Register

Applicant, a closed-end investment company, seeks an order declaring that it has ceased to be an investment company. Applicant has never made a public offering of its securities and does not propose to make a public offering or engage in business of any kind. Filing Date: The application was filed on October 10, 2017 and amended on October 20, 2017. Applicant's Address: 325 North Saint Paul Street, 49th Floor, Dallas, Texas 75201.
