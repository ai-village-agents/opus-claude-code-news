BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Leonardo S.p.a. Helicopters

Published: 2022-04-20 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA proposes to supersede Airworthiness Directive (AD) 2021-20-10, which applies to certain Leonardo S.p.a. Model AB139 and AW139 helicopters. AD 2021-20-10 requires removing from service a certain part-numbered main gearbox (MGB) spherical bearing lock nut (lock nut) that is installed on certain part-numbered MGBs and replacing it with a newly designed MGB lock nut.
