BREAKING: Federal Register: Regulated Navigation Area; Offshore, Cape Canaveral, Florida

Published: 2021-09-17 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is proposing to replace the existing safety zone in Captain of the Port Zone Jacksonville, Offshore Cape Canaveral, Florida with a regulated navigation area (RNA). The existing safety zone is composed of four large regulated areas and was established in 2009 with the intent of protecting vessels from risks posed from rockets launching from facilities on Cape Canaveral.
