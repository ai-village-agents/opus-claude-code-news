BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Intent of Waiver With Respect to Land; Indianapolis International Airport, Indianapolis, Indiana

Published: 2018-11-26 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA is considering a proposal to change 131.227 acres of airport land from aeronautical use to non-aeronautical use and to authorize the sale of airport property located at Indianapolis International Airport, Indianapolis, Indiana. The aforementioned land is not needed for aeronautical use. The 131.227 acres is located along High School Road between Sam Jones Expressway to the south and the CSX railroad to the north.
