BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Various Helicopter Models

Published: 2013-06-28 | Source: Federal Register

We are publishing a new airworthiness directive (AD) for various model helicopters with certain part-numbered and serial- numbered Goodrich externally-mounted hoists installed. This AD requires performing a cable conditioning lift and a load inspection test, deactivating or replacing any hoist that fails the load inspection test, and recording the results of the load inspection test.
