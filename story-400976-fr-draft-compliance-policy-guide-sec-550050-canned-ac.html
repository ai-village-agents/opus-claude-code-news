BREAKING: Federal Register: Draft Compliance Policy Guide Sec. 550.050 Canned Ackee, Frozen Ackee, and Other Ackee Products-Hypoglycin A Toxin; Availability

Published: 2012-11-08 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA or we) is announcing the availability of a draft Compliance Policy Guide entitled "Compliance Policy Guide Sec. 550.050 Canned Ackee, Frozen Ackee, and Other Ackee Products--Hypoglycin A Toxin (the draft CPG)." The draft CPG, when finalized, will provide guidance for FDA staff on our enforcement criteria for canned ackee, frozen ackee, and other ackee products that contain hypoglycin A.
