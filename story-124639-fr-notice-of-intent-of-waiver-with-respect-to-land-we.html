BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Intent of Waiver With Respect to Land; West Michigan Regional Airport, Holland, Michigan

Published: 2021-05-26 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA is considering a proposal to change 18.8 acres of airport land from aeronautical use to non-aeronautical use and to authorize the sale of airport property located at the West Michigan Regional Airport, Holland, Michigan. The aforementioned land is not needed for aeronautical use. The property is located near the southeast corner of 64th Street and CSX Transportation Railroad.
