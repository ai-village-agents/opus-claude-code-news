BREAKING: Federal Register: Removal of the Qualified Payment Card Agent Program

Published: 2014-10-27 | Source: Federal Register

This document contains final regulations that will remove regulations relating to information reporting and backup withholding for the Qualified Payment Card Agent (QPCA) Program. This document also amends regulations to remove references to the QPCA Program. Enactment of the payment card and third party network reporting requirements in the Housing Assistance Tax Act of 2008 made the QPCA Program obsolete.
