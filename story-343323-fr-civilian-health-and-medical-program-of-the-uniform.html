BREAKING: Federal Register: Civilian Health and Medical Program of the Uniformed Services (CHAMPUS); TRICARE Retired Reserve

Published: 2014-12-31 | Source: Federal Register

TRICARE Retired Reserve (TRR) is a premium-based TRICARE health plan available for purchase worldwide by qualified members of the Retired Reserve and by qualified survivors of TRR members. This final rule responds to public comments received to an interim final rule that was published in the Federal Register on August 6, 2010 (75 FR 47452-47457).
