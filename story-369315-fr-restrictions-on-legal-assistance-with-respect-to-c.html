BREAKING: Federal Register: Restrictions on Legal Assistance With Respect to Criminal Proceedings

Published: 2013-11-04 | Source: Federal Register

This proposed rule updates the Legal Services Corporation (LSC or Corporation) regulation on legal assistance with respect to criminal proceedings. The Tribal Law and Order Act of 2010 (TLOA) amended the LSC Act to authorize LSC funds to be used for representation of persons charged with criminal offenses in tribal courts.
