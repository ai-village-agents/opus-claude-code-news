BREAKING: Federal Register: Covered Asset Acquisitions

Published: 2016-12-07 | Source: Federal Register

This document contains temporary Income Tax Regulations under section 901(m) of the Internal Revenue Code (Code) with respect to transactions that generally are treated as asset acquisitions for U.S. income tax purposes and either are treated as stock acquisitions or are disregarded for foreign income tax purposes.
