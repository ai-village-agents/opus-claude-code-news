BREAKING: Federal Register: Registration Requirements for Pooled Plan Providers

Published: 2020-09-01 | Source: Federal Register

This proposed rule would establish the requirements for registering with the Department of Labor as a "pooled plan provider" for "pooled employer plans" under sections 3(43) and 3(44) of the Employee Retirement Income Security Act of 1974, as amended (ERISA).
