BREAKING: Federal Register: Trade Regulation Rule on the Use of Consumer Reviews and Testimonials

Published: 2024-08-22 | Source: Federal Register

The Federal Trade Commission ("FTC" or "Commission") is issuing this final rule and Statement of Basis and Purpose ("SBP") relating to certain specified unfair or deceptive acts or practices involving consumer reviews or testimonials.
