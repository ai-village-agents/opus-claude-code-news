BREAKING: Federal Register: NASA FAR Supplement Regulatory Review No. 3

Published: 2015-04-07 | Source: Federal Register

NASA is updating the NASA FAR Supplement (NFS) with the goal of eliminating unnecessary regulation, streamlining overly-burdensome regulation, clarifying language, and simplifying processes where possible. This proposed rule is the third and final in a series and includes updates and revisions to 10 parts of the NFS.
