BREAKING: Federal Register: Cooperative Research and Development Agreement: Broadband Cellular and Satellite Communications Exploratory Development

Published: 2016-01-12 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard announces its intent to enter into a Cooperative Research and Development Agreement (CRADA) with General Dynamics Mission Systems, Inc., to investigate the operational use of broadband cellular technology, including equipment that may be integrated into the National Public Safety Broadband Communication Band 14 LTE Network (commonly called FirstNet).
