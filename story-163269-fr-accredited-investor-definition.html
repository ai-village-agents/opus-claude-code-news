BREAKING: Federal Register: Accredited Investor Definition

Published: 2020-10-09 | Source: Federal Register

We are adopting amendments to the definition of "accredited investor" in our rules to add new categories of qualifying natural persons and entities and to make certain other modifications to the existing definition.
