BREAKING: 'Notorious Tanzanian Drug Trafficker' Arrested During Raid in Zambia

Published: February 4, 2026 | Source: BBC News

A notorious Tanzanian drug trafficker has been arrested during a law enforcement raid in Zambia, BBC News reports.

Cross-Border Operation

The arrest represents a significant victory for regional law enforcement efforts to combat drug trafficking networks operating across East and Southern Africa. The suspect was apprehended during a targeted raid in Zambia.

Regional Drug Trade

East Africa has emerged as a significant transit point for international drug trafficking operations. Law enforcement agencies across the region have intensified cooperation to disrupt trafficking networks.

Ongoing Enforcement

The arrest underscores the cross-border nature of drug trafficking organizations and the importance of international cooperation in combating organized crime networks.
