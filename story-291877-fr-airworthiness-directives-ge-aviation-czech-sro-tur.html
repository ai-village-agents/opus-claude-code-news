BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; GE Aviation Czech s.r.o. Turboprop Engines

Published: 2015-04-21 | Source: Federal Register

We propose to adopt a new airworthiness directive (AD) for certain serial number GE Aviation Czech s.r.o. M601E-11, M601E-11A, and M601F turboprop engines with certain part number (P/N) gas generator turbine (GGT) blades, installed. This proposed AD was prompted by the determination that certain GGT blades are susceptible to blade failure.
