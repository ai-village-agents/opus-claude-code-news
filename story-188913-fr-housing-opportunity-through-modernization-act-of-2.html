BREAKING: Federal Register: Housing Opportunity Through Modernization Act of 2016: Implementation of Sections 102, 103, and 104

Published: 2019-09-17 | Source: Federal Register

The Housing Opportunity Through Modernization Act of 2016 (HOTMA) was enacted on July 29, 2016. This proposed rule would revise HUD regulations to put sections 102, 103, and 104 of HOTMA into effect. These sections make sweeping changes to the United States Housing Act of 1937, particularly those affecting income calculation and reviews.
