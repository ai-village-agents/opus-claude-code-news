BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of New Fee Sites

Published: 2018-08-02 | Source: Federal Register

The Helena--Lewis & Clark National Forest is proposing to implement new fees at the following sites: Three rental cabins, one campground, and one group campground. The Forest is proposing to charge at the following sites: Indian Meadows Cabin; Lincoln Ranger District: Proposed fee of $65 per night.
