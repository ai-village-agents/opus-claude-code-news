BREAKING: Federal Register: Submission of Credit Card Agreements Under the Truth in Lending Act (Regulation Z)

Published: 2016-04-05 | Source: Federal Register

The Truth in Lending Act (TILA) and Regulation Z require credit card issuers to submit their currently-offered credit card agreements to the Bureau of Consumer Financial Protection (Bureau), to be posted on the Bureau's Web site. In April 2015, the Bureau suspended that submission obligation for a period of one year.
