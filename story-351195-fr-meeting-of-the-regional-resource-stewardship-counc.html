BREAKING: Federal Register: Meeting of the Regional Resource Stewardship Council

Published: 2013-04-03 | Source: Federal Register

The TVA Regional Resource Stewardship Council (RRSC) will hold a meeting on Wednesday, April 24, and Thursday, April 25, 2013, to obtain views and advice on the topic of Trout Fish Hatchery projects in Tennessee and Georgia. The RRSC was established to advise TVA on its natural resource stewardship activities. Notice of this meeting is given under the Federal Advisory Committee Act (FACA), 5 U.S.C. App. 2.
