BREAKING: Federal Register: State Guidelines for Greenhouse Gas Emissions from Existing Electric Utility Generating Units

Published: 2017-12-28 | Source: Federal Register

An advance notice of proposed rulemaking (ANPRM) is a notice intended to solicit information from the public as the Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) considers proposing a future rule.
