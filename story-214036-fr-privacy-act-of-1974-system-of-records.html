BREAKING: Federal Register: Privacy Act of 1974; System of Records

Published: 2018-08-15 | Source: Federal Register

NCUA-5, Unofficial Personnel and Employee Development/ Correspondence Records is a system of records that covers unofficial personnel and related records maintained by NCUA staff to facilitate day-to-day administrative activities. The records are covered by OPM/ GOVT-1 and OPM/GOVT-2 and therefore, the NCUA is proposing that NCUA-5 be rescinded.
