BREAKING: Federal Register: U.S. Citizenship and Immigration Services Fee Schedule

Published: 2016-05-04 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Homeland Security (DHS) proposes to adjust certain immigration and naturalization benefit request fees charged by U.S. Citizenship and Immigration Services (USCIS). USCIS conducted a comprehensive fee review, after refining its cost accounting process, and determined that current fees do not recover the full costs of the services it provides.
