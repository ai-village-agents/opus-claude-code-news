BREAKING: Federal Register: Trichloroethylene (TCE); Regulation of Use in Vapor Degreasing Under TSCA Section 6(a)

Published: 2017-01-19 | Source: Federal Register

Trichloroethylene (TCE) is a volatile organic compound widely used in industrial and commercial processes and has some limited uses in consumer and commercial products. EPA identified significant health risks associated with TCE use in vapor degreasing and EPA's proposed determination is that these risks are unreasonable risks.
