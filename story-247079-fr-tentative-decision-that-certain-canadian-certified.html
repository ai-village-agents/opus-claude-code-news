BREAKING: Federal Register: Tentative Decision That Certain Canadian-Certified Vehicles Are Eligible for Importation

Published: 2017-10-24 | Source: Federal Register

This notice requests comments on a tentative decision by NHTSA that certain vehicles that do not comply with all applicable Federal motor vehicle safety standards (FMVSS), but that are certified by their original manufacturer as complying with all applicable Canadian motor vehicle safety standards (CMVSS), are nevertheless eligible for importation into the United States.
