BREAKING: Federal Register: Possible Revisions To Audit Committee Disclosures

Published: 2015-07-08 | Source: Federal Register

The Commission is publishing this concept release to seek public comment regarding audit committee reporting requirements, with a focus on the audit committee's reporting of its responsibilities with respect to its oversight of the independent auditor.
