BREAKING: Federal Register: Rescission of the Notice of July 23, 2019, Designating Aliens for Expedited Removal

Published: 2022-03-21 | Source: Federal Register

This Notice rescinds the July 23, 2019 Notice, Designating Aliens for Expedited Removal, which expanded to the maximum extent permitted by the Immigration and Nationality Act (INA) the application of expedited removal procedures to noncitizens not already covered by previous designations.
