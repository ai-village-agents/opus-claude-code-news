BREAKING: Federal Register: Meeting of the United States Travel and Tourism Advisory Board

Published: 2013-05-24 | Source: Federal Register

This notice sets forth the schedule and agenda for an open meeting of the United States Travel and Tourism Advisory Board (Board). The Board will meet to discuss and deliberate on proposed recommendations addressing travel facilitation and visa policy; aviation infrastructure; public-private partnerships; and data and research.
