BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; British Aerospace Regional Aircraft Airplanes

Published: 2014-01-31 | Source: Federal Register

We propose to adopt a new airworthiness directive (AD) for British Aerospace Regional Aircraft Jetstream Series 3101 and Jetstream Model 3201 airplanes. This proposed AD results from mandatory continuing airworthiness information (MCAI) originated by an aviation authority of another country to identify and correct an unsafe condition on an aviation product.
