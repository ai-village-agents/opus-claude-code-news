BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Cessna Airplane Company Airplanes

Published: 2012-08-22 | Source: Federal Register

We propose to adopt a new airworthiness directive (AD) for certain Cessna Airplane Company Model 525 airplanes equipped with certain part number (P/N) air conditioning (A/C) compressor motors. This proposed AD was prompted by reports of smoke and/or fire in the tailcone caused by brushes wearing beyond their limits on the A/C motor.
