BREAKING: Federal Register: Environmental Impact Statement for the Milwaukee, WI to Minneapolis, MN Rail Corridor

Published: 2013-05-24 | Source: Federal Register

On December 9, 2010, FRA published a notice of intent to advise the public that a Tier I environmental impact statement (EIS) would be prepared for the Milwaukee, WI to Minneapolis-St. Paul, MN (Milwaukee-Twin Cities) High-Speed Rail Corridor Program. The original project included passenger stations, maintenance facilities, and the construction of a high-speed rail line between Milwaukee and the Twin Cities.
