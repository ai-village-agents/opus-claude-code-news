BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Airbus Airplanes

Published: 2012-10-16 | Source: Federal Register

We propose to adopt a new airworthiness directive (AD) for all Airbus Model A310-203 airplanes. This proposed AD was prompted by a report of an analysis that demonstrated a reduced fatigue life for the side link bolts, center sway link bolts, and thrust link bolts on the forward engine mounts. This proposed AD would require repetitive replacement of those bolts.
