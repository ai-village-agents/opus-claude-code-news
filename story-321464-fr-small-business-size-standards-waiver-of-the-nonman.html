BREAKING: Federal Register: Small Business Size Standards: Waiver of the Nonmanufacturer Rule

Published: 2014-04-11 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Small Business Administration (SBA) is rescinding four class waivers of the Nonmanufacturer Rule which were classified under NAICS code 335999 (All Other Miscellaneous Electrical Equipment and Component Manufacturing) and which were further classified under Product Service Codes (PSCs) 5999, 6210, 6240, and 6250.
