BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone; Riverside Music Festival, Missouri River, Mile 372.0; Riverside, MO

Published: 2014-09-22 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is establishing a temporary safety zone for all waters of the Missouri River, covering all waters within a 700 foot radius of the barge located at mile 372.0. This temporary safety zone is necessary to protect persons and property from potential damage and safety hazards during the Riverside Music Festival.
