BREAKING: Federal Register: Volkswagen Group of America, Inc., Denial of Petitions for Decision of Inconsequential Noncompliance

Published: 2023-08-22 | Source: Federal Register

Volkswagen Group of America, Inc. (Volkswagen or the "Petitioner") has determined that certain model year (MY) 2019 and 2021 Audi motor vehicles do not fully comply with Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard (FMVSS) No. 135, Light Vehicle Brake Systems. Volkswagen filed noncompliance reports dated March 27, 2019, and July 26, 2021.
