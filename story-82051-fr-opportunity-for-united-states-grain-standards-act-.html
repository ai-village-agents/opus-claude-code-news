BREAKING: Federal Register: Opportunity for United States Grain Standards Act Designation in Decatur, IN and Marshall, MI

Published: 2023-11-15 | Source: Federal Register

The Agricultural Marketing Service (AMS) is seeking persons or governmental agencies interested in providing official services in the areas noted below to submit an application for designation. Designation provides for private entities or State governmental agencies to be an integral part of the official grain inspection system (https:// www.ams.usda.gov/services/fgis/official-grain-inspection-weighing- system).
