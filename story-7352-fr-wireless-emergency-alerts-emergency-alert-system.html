BREAKING: Federal Register: Wireless Emergency Alerts; Emergency Alert System

Published: 2025-03-18 | Source: Federal Register

In this document, the Federal Communications Commission (Commission) revises the Wireless Emergency Alerts (WEA) rules to allow alert originators the option to send "silent alerts" that do not trigger WEA's common audio attention signal or vibration cadence. This action grants alert originators greater flexibility in tailoring how WEA messages are presented.
