BREAKING: Federal Register: Certain Frozen Warmwater Shrimp From the Socialist Republic of Vietnam: Final Results and Final Partial Rescission of Antidumping Duty Administrative Review

Published: 2012-09-11 | Source: Federal Register

On March 7, 2012, the Department of Commerce ("Department") published in the Federal Register the Preliminary Results of the 6th administrative review of the antidumping duty order on certain frozen warmwater shrimp ("shrimp") from the Socialist Republic of Vietnam ("Vietnam").\1\ Based upon our analysis of the comments, we made changes to the margin calculations for the final results. ---------------------------------------------------------------------------
