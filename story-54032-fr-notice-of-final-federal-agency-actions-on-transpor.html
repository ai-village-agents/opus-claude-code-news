BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Final Federal Agency Actions on Transportation Project in Washington State

Published: 2024-12-02 | Source: Federal Register

This notice announces actions taken by the FHWA that are final. The action relates to the SR 3 Freight Corridor--New Alignment project, located in western Washington within the City of Bremerton, Kitsap County, and Mason County. The Project will construct the SR 3 Freight Corridor--New Alignment to the east of the existing SR3 with a design and posted speed of 50 miles per hour.
