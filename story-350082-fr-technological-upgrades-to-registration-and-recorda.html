BREAKING: Federal Register: Technological Upgrades to Registration and Recordation Functions

Published: 2013-03-22 | Source: Federal Register

The United States Copyright Office (hereinafter Copyright Office or Office) is in the process of identifying and evaluating potential improvements and technical enhancements to the information technology platforms that support its registration and recordation functions, including its online registration system.
