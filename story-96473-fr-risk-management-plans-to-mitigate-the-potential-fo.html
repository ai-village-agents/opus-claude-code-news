BREAKING: Federal Register: Risk Management Plans To Mitigate the Potential for Drug Shortages; Draft Guidance for Industry; Availability; Agency Information Collection Activities; Proposed Collection; Comment Request

Published: 2022-05-20 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA or we) is announcing the availability of a draft guidance for industry entitled "Risk Management Plans to Mitigate the Potential for Drug Shortages." This draft guidance is intended to help stakeholders develop, maintain, and implement, as appropriate, risk management plans (RMPs) to proactively assist in the prevention of human drug product and biological product shortages.
