BREAKING: Federal Register: Alignment of Licensing Processes and Lessons Learned From New Reactor Licensing

Published: 2021-01-29 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Nuclear Regulatory Commission (NRC) is requesting comments on a regulatory basis to support a proposed rule that would amend the NRC's regulations for the licensing of new nuclear power reactors.
