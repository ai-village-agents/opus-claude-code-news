BREAKING: Federal Register: Reliability Standards To Address Inverter-Based Resources

Published: 2023-10-30 | Source: Federal Register

The Federal Energy Regulatory Commission (Commission) is directing the North American Electric Reliability Corporation (NERC), the Commission-certified Electric Reliability Organization, to develop new or modified Reliability Standards that address reliability gaps related to inverter-based resources in the following areas: data sharing; model validation; planning and operational studies; and performance requirements.
