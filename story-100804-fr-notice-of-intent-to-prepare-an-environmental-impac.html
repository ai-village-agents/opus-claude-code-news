BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Intent To Prepare an Environmental Impact Statement for Air National Guard F-15EX Eagle II and F-35A Lightning II Beddowns

Published: 2022-07-19 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of the Air Force (DAF) is issuing this Notice of Intent (NOI) to prepare an Environmental Impact Statement (EIS) to assess the potential social, economic, and environmental impacts associated with beddown of F-15EX and F-35A aircraft that would replace the legacy F-15C/D aircraft. The DAF is the lead agency on the preparation of the EIS and the Department of the Navy and the Federal Aviation Administration are participating as cooperating agencies.
