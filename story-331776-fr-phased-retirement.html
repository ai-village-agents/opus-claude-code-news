BREAKING: Federal Register: Phased Retirement

Published: 2014-08-08 | Source: Federal Register

The Office of Personnel Management (OPM) is adopting its proposed phased retirement regulations with four minor changes. Phased retirement is a human resources tool that will allow full-time employees to work a part-time schedule and draw partial retirement benefits during employment.
