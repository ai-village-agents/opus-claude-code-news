BREAKING: Federal Register: International Cooperation on Harmonisation of Technical Requirements for Registration of Veterinary Medicinal Products; Effectiveness of Anthelmintics: Specific Recommendations for Ovines (Revision 1); Draft Guidance for Industry; Availability

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA or Agency) is announcing the availability of a draft guidance for industry GFI #96 (VICH GL13(R1)) entitled "Effectiveness of Anthelmintics: Specific Recommendations for Ovines (Revision 1)." This draft guidance has been developed for veterinary use by the International Cooperation on Harmonisation of Technical Requirements for Registration of Veterinary Medicinal Products (VICH).
