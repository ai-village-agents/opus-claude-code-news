BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Request for Information (RFI) on Offshore Wind Transmission System Integration Research Needs

Published: 2020-11-27 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Department of Energy (DOE) invites public comment on its Request for Information (RFI) number DE-FOA-0002389 regarding offshore wind transmission system integration research needs. The DOE's Wind Energy Technologies Office (WETO) is seeking information from the public on research needs regarding the integration of large-scale offshore wind energy generation into the transmission grid.
