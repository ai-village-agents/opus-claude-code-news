BREAKING: Federal Register: Certain Pumping Bras: Issuance of a General Exclusion Order; Termination of the Investigation

Published: 2017-04-13 | Source: Federal Register

Notice is hereby given that the U.S. International Trade Commission has issued a general exclusion order (GEO) denying entry of certain pumping bras. The investigation is terminated.
