BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; The Boeing Company Airplanes

Published: 2016-02-18 | Source: Federal Register

We are superseding Airworthiness Directive (AD) 2010-26-10, which applied to certain The Boeing Company Model 747-200C, -200F, - 400, -400D, and -400F series airplanes. AD 2010-26-10 required repetitive inspections for cracking of the lap joints, modification of certain lap joints, and certain post-repair inspections of the lap joints.
