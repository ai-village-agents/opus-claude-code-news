BREAKING: Federal Register: Codex Alimentarius Commission: Meeting of the Codex Committee on Food Hygiene

Published: 2019-09-03 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S Codex Office is sponsoring a public meeting on September 26, 2019. The objective of the public meeting is to provide information and receive public comments on agenda items and draft United States (U.S.) positions to be discussed at the 51st Session of the Codex Committee on Food Hygiene (CCFH) of the Codex Alimentarius Commission, in Cleveland, Ohio, November 4-8, 2019.
