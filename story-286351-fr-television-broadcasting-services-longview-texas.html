BREAKING: Federal Register: Television Broadcasting Services; Longview, Texas

Published: 2015-02-13 | Source: Federal Register

A petition for rulemaking was filed by KCEB License Company, LLC ("KCEB License"), the licensee of KCEB(TV), channel 51, Longview, Texas, requesting the substitution of channel 26 for channel 51 at Longview. KCEB License filed comments reaffirming its interest in the proposed channel substitution and explained that the channel substitution will replicate KCEB(TV)'s current coverage area on channel 51 and serve 34,104 more persons than the current channel 51 facility.
