BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Airbus SAS Airplanes

Published: 2020-09-22 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA proposes to adopt a new airworthiness directive (AD) for certain Airbus SAS Model A350-941 and -1041 airplanes. This proposed AD was prompted by reports of migration of the bushings of the horizontal tail plane (HTP) lateral load fittings (LLFs) on the left- and right-hand sides during flight test.
