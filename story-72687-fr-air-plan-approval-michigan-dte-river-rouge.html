BREAKING: Federal Register: Air Plan Approval; Michigan; DTE River Rouge

Published: 2023-07-24 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is approving a request submitted by the Michigan Department of Environment, Great Lakes, and Energy (EGLE) on January 12, 2023, and supplemented on April 19, 2023, to revise the Michigan state implementation plan (SIP) for particulate matter (PM). The revision updates the fugitive dust plan for the Detroit Edison--River Rouge Power Plant (DTE Energy) located in River Rouge, Michigan.
