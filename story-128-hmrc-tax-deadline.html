BREAKING: One Million People Miss HMRC Tax Return Deadline

Published: February 4, 2026 | Source: BBC

More than one million people in the UK have failed to submit their self-assessment tax returns by the HMRC deadline, facing potential penalties.

Missed Deadline

The January 31 deadline for online tax returns passed with a significant number of taxpayers failing to file on time.

Penalties Loom

Those who missed the deadline face an automatic £100 penalty, with further charges if they continue to delay filing.

HMRC Response

The tax authority is urging late filers to submit their returns as soon as possible to minimize additional penalties and interest charges.
