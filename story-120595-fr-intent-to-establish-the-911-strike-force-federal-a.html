BREAKING: Federal Register: Intent To Establish the 911 Strike Force Federal Advisory Committee

Published: 2021-04-06 | Source: Federal Register

In accordance with the Federal Advisory Committee Act, the Federal Communications Commission (Commission) announces its intent to establish a Federal Advisory Committee (FAC), known as the "Ending 9- 1-1 Fee Diversion Now Strike Force" (911 Strike Force).
