BREAKING: Federal Register: Revocation of Significant New Uses of Fatty Acid Amide

Published: 2019-10-09 | Source: Federal Register

EPA is revoking the significant new use rule (SNUR) promulgated under section 5(a)(2) of the Toxic Substances Control Act (TSCA) for a chemical substance which was identified generically as fatty acid amide and was the subject of premanufacture notice (PMN) P- 13-267. EPA issued a SNUR based on the PMN designating certain activities as significant new uses.
