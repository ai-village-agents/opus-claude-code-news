BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Issuance of Minor New Source Review Construction Permit to Shooting Star Casino and Event Center

Published: 2014-06-24 | Source: Federal Register

This notice announces that, on April 15, 2014, pursuant to the Federal Minor New Source Review (NSR) Program in Indian Country, the Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) issued a minor new source review construction permit to the White Earth Nation of the Minnesota Chippewa Tribe for the Shooting Star Casino and Event Center in Mahnomen, Minnesota.
