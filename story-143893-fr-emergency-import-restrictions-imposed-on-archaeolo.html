BREAKING: Federal Register: Emergency Import Restrictions Imposed on Archaeological and Ethnological Material From Yemen

Published: 2020-02-07 | Source: Federal Register

This final rule amends the U.S. Customs and Border Protection (CBP) regulations to reflect the imposition of emergency import restrictions on certain archaeological and ethnological material from the Republic of Yemen (Yemen).
