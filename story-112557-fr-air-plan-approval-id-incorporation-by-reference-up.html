BREAKING: Federal Register: Air Plan Approval; ID; Incorporation by Reference Updates

Published: 2022-12-19 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) proposes to approve a revision to the Idaho State Implementation Plan (SIP) submitted on May 4, 2022. The submission updates the incorporation by reference of the national ambient air quality standards and related planning and monitoring requirements into the Idaho air quality rules as of July 1, 2021.
