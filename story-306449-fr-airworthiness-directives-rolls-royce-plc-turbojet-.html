BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Rolls-Royce plc Turbojet Engines

Published: 2015-10-09 | Source: Federal Register

We propose to supersede airworthiness directive (AD) 2013-11- 13 that applies to all Rolls-Royce plc (RR) Viper Mk. 601-22 turbojet engines. Since we issued AD 2013-11-13, RR determined that additional parts for the RR Viper Mk. 601-22 as well as additional engine models are affected. This proposed AD would add two new engine models and additional engine parts to the applicability.
