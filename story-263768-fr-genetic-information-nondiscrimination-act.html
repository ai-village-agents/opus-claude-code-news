BREAKING: Federal Register: Genetic Information Nondiscrimination Act

Published: 2016-05-17 | Source: Federal Register

The Equal Employment Opportunity Commission (EEOC or Commission) is issuing a final rule to amend the regulations implementing Title II of the Genetic Information Nondiscrimination Act of 2008 as they relate to employer-sponsored wellness programs.
