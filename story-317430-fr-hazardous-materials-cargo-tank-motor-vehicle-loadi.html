BREAKING: Federal Register: Hazardous Materials: Cargo Tank Motor Vehicle Loading and Unloading Operations

Published: 2014-02-25 | Source: Federal Register

PHMSA is closing this rulemaking proceeding under this docket having reconsidered our proposal for additional regulations associated with cargo tank motor vehicle (CTMV) loading or unloading operations. This action is based on the findings of the regulatory assessment, comments to docket of this rulemaking, and completion of a supplementary policy analysis on how best to address the safety risks of bulk loading and unloading operations.
