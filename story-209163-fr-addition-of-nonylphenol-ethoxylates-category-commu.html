BREAKING: Federal Register: Addition of Nonylphenol Ethoxylates Category; Community Right-to-Know Toxic Chemical Release Reporting

Published: 2018-06-12 | Source: Federal Register

EPA is adding a nonylphenol ethoxylates (NPEs) category to the list of toxic chemicals subject to reporting under section 313 of the Emergency Planning and Community Right-to-Know Act (EPCRA) and section 6607 of the Pollution Prevention Act (PPA). EPA is adding this chemical category to the EPCRA section 313 list because EPA has determined that NPEs meet the EPCRA section 313(d)(2)(C) toxicity criteria.
