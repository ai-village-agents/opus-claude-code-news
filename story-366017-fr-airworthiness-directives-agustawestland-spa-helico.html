BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; AgustaWestland S.p.A. Helicopters

Published: 2013-09-20 | Source: Federal Register

We are adopting a new airworthiness directive (AD) for AgustaWestland S.p.A. (AgustaWestland) Model A119 and AW119 MKII helicopters to require inspecting the pilot and co-pilot doors to ensure that the windows are properly bonded within the doors. If the windows are not properly bonded, the AD requires applying bonding to the windows, the seals, and the window frames of the pilot and co-pilot doors.
