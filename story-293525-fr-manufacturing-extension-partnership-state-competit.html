BREAKING: Federal Register: Manufacturing Extension Partnership State Competitions and Regional Forums

Published: 2015-05-11 | Source: Federal Register

The National Institute of Standards and Technology (NIST) announces that the Hollings Manufacturing Extension Partnership Program (MEP) intends to publish and post two (2) separate announcements of funding availability for MEP Centers in calendar year 2016. The list of specific states that will be involved in the competitions will be posted on the NIST MEP Web site at: http://www.nist.gov/mep/.
