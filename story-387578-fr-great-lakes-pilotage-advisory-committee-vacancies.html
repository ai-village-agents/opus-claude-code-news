BREAKING: Federal Register: Great Lakes Pilotage Advisory Committee; Vacancies

Published: 2012-06-05 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard seeks applications for membership on the Great Lakes Pilotage Advisory Committee (GLPAC). The GLPAC provides advice and makes recommendations to the Secretary of Homeland Security and the Coast Guard on matters relating to Great Lakes pilotage, including review of proposed Great Lakes pilotage regulations and policies.
