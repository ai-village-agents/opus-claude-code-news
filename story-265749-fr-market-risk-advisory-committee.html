BREAKING: Federal Register: Market Risk Advisory Committee

Published: 2016-06-09 | Source: Federal Register

The Commodity Futures Trading Commission (CFTC) announces that on June 27, 2016, from 10:00 a.m. to 1:30 p.m., the Market Risk Advisory Committee (MRAC) will hold a public meeting at the CFTC's Washington, DC, headquarters.
