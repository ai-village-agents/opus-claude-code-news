BREAKING: Federal Register: Tribal Consultation for 8(a) Business Development Program and Mentor-Protégé Program Issues and Best Practices

Published: 2025-05-12 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Small Business Administration (SBA) announces that it is holding a tribal consultation meeting in Anchorage, Alaska requesting comments and input on a variety of topics relating to the 8(a) program and the mentor-prot[eacute]g[eacute] program. SBA is requesting general comments and input on how the 8(a) program is working and is inviting suggestions on potential avenues for making the program more efficient or reducing the regulatory burden on participants in the program.
