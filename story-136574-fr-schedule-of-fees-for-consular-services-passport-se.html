BREAKING: Federal Register: Schedule of Fees for Consular Services-Passport Security Surcharge

Published: 2021-10-28 | Source: Federal Register

This rule adopts as final the notice of proposed rulemaking (NPRM) published in the Federal Register on March 26, 2021. This final rule adjusts the Schedule of Fees for Consular Services (Schedule of Fees) by increasing the passport book security surcharge (PSS) from $60 to $80. This increase reflects increases in security-related costs for processing passports attributed to the PSS.
