BREAKING: Federal Register: Proposed Revision 0 to Fitness-for-Duty Standard Review Plan

Published: 2013-07-16 | Source: Federal Register

The NRC seeks comments on the proposed new sections of the Standard Review Plan (SRP), concerning implementation of a Fitness-for- Duty (FFD) program.
