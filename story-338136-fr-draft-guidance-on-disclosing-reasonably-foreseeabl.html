BREAKING: Federal Register: Draft Guidance on Disclosing Reasonably Foreseeable Risks in Research Evaluating Standards of Care

Published: 2014-10-24 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Health and Human Services (HHS), through the Office for Human Research Protections (OHRP) is announcing the availability of a draft guidance for the research community entitled "Guidance on Disclosing Reasonably Foreseeable Risks in Research Evaluating Standards of Care."
