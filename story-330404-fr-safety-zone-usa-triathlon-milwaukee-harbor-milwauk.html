BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone; USA Triathlon; Milwaukee Harbor; Milwaukee, WI

Published: 2014-07-24 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard will enforce the safety zone on Lake Michigan within Milwaukee Harbor, Wisconsin for the 2014 Olympic and Sprint Distance National Championships at specified times from August 8 to August 10, 2014. This action is necessary and intended to ensure safety of life on the navigable waters of the United States during the Olympic and Sprint Distance National Championships.
