BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Airbus Helicopters

Published: 2020-11-24 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA proposes to supersede Airworthiness Directive (AD) 2017-17-01, which applies to certain Airbus Helicopters Model AS332L2 and EC225LP helicopters. AD 2017-17-01 requires repetitive inspections of the main rotor blade (MRB) attachment pins.
