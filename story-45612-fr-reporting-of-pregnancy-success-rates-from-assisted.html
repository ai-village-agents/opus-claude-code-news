BREAKING: Federal Register: Reporting of Pregnancy Success Rates From Assisted Reproductive Technology (ART) Programs; Proposed Modifications to Data Collection Fields and Data Validation Procedures; Final Notice

Published: 2024-08-29 | Source: Federal Register

The Centers for Disease Control and Prevention (CDC) within the Department of Health and Human Services (HHS) announces revised plans for data collection fields for reporting of pregnancy success rates from assisted reproductive technology (ART) programs and for data validation procedures. This reporting is required by the Fertility Clinic Success Rate and Certification Act of 1992 (FCSRCA).
