BREAKING: Federal Register: Approval of Barrios Measurement Services LLC (Cut Off, LA), as a Commercial Gauger

Published: 2020-02-13 | Source: Federal Register

Notice is hereby given, pursuant to CBP regulations, that Barrios Measurement Services LLC (Cut Off, LA), has been approved to gauge petroleum and certain petroleum products for customs purposes for the next three years as of September 24, 2019.
