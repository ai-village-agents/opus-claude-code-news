BREAKING: Federal Register: Exempting Whole Health Well-Being Services From Copayment

Published: 2025-01-03 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Veterans Affairs (VA) proposes to revise its medical regulations to exempt Whole Health well-being services from the copayment requirements for inpatient hospital care and outpatient medical care.
