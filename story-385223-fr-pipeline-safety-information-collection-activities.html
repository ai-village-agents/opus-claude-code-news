BREAKING: Federal Register: Pipeline Safety: Information Collection Activities

Published: 2012-05-09 | Source: Federal Register

In accordance with the Paperwork Reduction Act of 1995, PHMSA invites comments on two information collections that we will be submitting to the Office of Management and Budget (OMB) for renewal. The information collections relate to the pipeline integrity management requirements for gas transmission pipeline operators and the response plan requirements for operators of onshore oil pipelines.
