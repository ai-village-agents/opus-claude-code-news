BREAKING: Federal Register: Occupational Exposure to Beryllium and Beryllium Compounds in Construction and Shipyard Sectors

Published: 2017-06-27 | Source: Federal Register

The Occupational Safety and Health Administration (OSHA) proposes to revoke the ancillary provisions for the construction and the shipyard sectors that OSHA adopted on January 9, 2017 but retain the new lower permissible exposure limit (PEL) of 0.2 [mu]g/m\3\ and the short term exposure limit (STEL) of 2.0 [mu]g/m\3\ for each sector.
