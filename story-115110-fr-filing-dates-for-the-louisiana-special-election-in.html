BREAKING: Federal Register: Filing Dates for the Louisiana Special Election in the 5th Congressional District Special Election

Published: 2021-01-22 | Source: Federal Register

Louisiana has scheduled a Special General Election on March 20, 2021, to fill its U.S. House of Representatives seat in the 5th Congressional District following the passing of Representative-elect Luke J. Letlow. Under Louisiana law, a majority winner in a Special General Election is declared elected. Should no candidate achieve a majority vote, a Special Runoff Election will be held on April 24, 2021, between the top two vote-getters.
