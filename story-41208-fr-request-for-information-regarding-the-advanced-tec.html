BREAKING: Federal Register: Request for Information Regarding the Advanced Technology Vehicles Manufacturing Loan Program; Reopening of Comment Period

Published: 2024-07-08 | Source: Federal Register

The Loan Programs Office ("LPO") of the Department of Energy ("DOE") is seeking public input to inform its implementation of the Inflation Reduction Act of 2022 provisions relating to the Advanced Technology Vehicles Manufacturing Loan Program (the "ATVM Program"). On May 29, 2024, DOE published an RFI on these subjects. The RFI provided an opportunity for submitting written comments, data, and information no later than June 28, 2024.
