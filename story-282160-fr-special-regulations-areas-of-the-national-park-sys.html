BREAKING: Federal Register: Special Regulations; Areas of the National Park System, Cape Hatteras National Seashore-Off-Road Vehicle Management

Published: 2016-12-21 | Source: Federal Register

The National Park Service (NPS) amends its special regulation for off-road vehicle (ORV) use at Cape Hatteras National Seashore, North Carolina, to revise the times that certain beaches open to ORV use in the morning, to extend the dates that certain seasonal ORV routes are open in the fall and spring, and to modify the size and location of certain vehicle-free areas.
