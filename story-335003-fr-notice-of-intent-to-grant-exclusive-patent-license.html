BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Intent to Grant Exclusive Patent License; Grey Matter, LLC

Published: 2014-09-17 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of the Navy hereby gives notice of its intent to grant to Grey Matter, LLC a revocable, nonassignable, exclusive license to practice in the field of use of Periodic Mesoporous Organosilicate (PMO) material for use as wearable Personal Protective Equipment (PPE). Wearable PPE will be designed for use in job-related occupational safety, health purposes, and other recreational activities.
