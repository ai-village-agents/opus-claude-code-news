BREAKING: Federal Register: Entergy Nuclear Operations, Inc., Indian Point Nuclear Generating, Unit 2

Published: 2015-03-02 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Nuclear Regulatory Commission (NRC) is considering issuance of an amendment to Facility Operating License No. DPR-26, issued to Entergy Nuclear Operations, Inc., for operation of the Indian Point Nuclear Generating, Unit 2. The proposed amendment would allow a revision to the acceptance criteria for the Surveillance Requirement 3.1.4.2 for Control Rod G-3.
