BREAKING: Federal Register: Onshore Oil and Gas Operations; Federal and Indian Oil and Gas Leases; Onshore Oil and Gas Order Number 1, Approval of Operations

Published: 2017-01-10 | Source: Federal Register

The Bureau of Land Management (BLM) hereby amends its existing Onshore Oil and Gas Order Number 1 (Onshore Order 1) to require the electronic filing (or e-filing) of all Applications for Permit to Drill (APD) and Notices of Staking (NOS). Previously, Onshore Order 1 stated that an "operator must file an APD or any other required documents in the BLM Field Office having jurisdiction over the lands described in the application," but allowed for e-filing of such documents as an alternative.
