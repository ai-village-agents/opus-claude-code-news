BREAKING: Federal Register: The National Institutes of Health (NIH) Sexual & Gender Minority Research Listening Session

Published: 2020-10-26 | Source: Federal Register

The National Institutes of Health (NIH) Sexual & Gender Minority Research Office (SGMRO) will be holding its second annual listening session with Sexual & Gender Minority (SGM)-focused organizations. The primary objective of the NIH's listening session is to hear from community stakeholders about what issues are on their minds regarding SGM-related research and related activities at NIH.
