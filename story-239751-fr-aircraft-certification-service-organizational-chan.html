BREAKING: Federal Register: Aircraft Certification Service Organizational Changes

Published: 2017-07-25 | Source: Federal Register

This notice informs the American public and aviation industry of organizational changes in the Aircraft Certification Service (AIR) of the FAA Aviation Safety Office (AVS).
