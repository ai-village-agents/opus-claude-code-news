BREAKING: Federal Register: Proposed Amendment of V-221 and V-305 in the Vicinity of Bloomington, IN

Published: 2020-11-04 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA is withdrawing the NPRM published in the Federal Register on June 26, 2020, proposing to amend VHF Omnidirectional Range (VOR) Federal airways V-221 and V-305 due to the planned decommissioning of the VOR portion of the Hoosier, IN, VOR/Tactical Air Navigation (VORTAC) in support of the FAA's VOR Minimum Operational Network (MON) program.
