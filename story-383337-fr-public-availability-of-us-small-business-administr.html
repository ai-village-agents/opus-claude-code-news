BREAKING: Federal Register: Public Availability of U.S. Small Business Administration FY 2010 Service Contract Inventory

Published: 2012-04-17 | Source: Federal Register

In accordance with Section 743 of Division C of the Consolidated Appropriations Act of 2010 (Pub. L. 111-117), the Small Business Administration is publishing this notice to advise the public of the availability of the FY 2011 Service Contract inventory. This inventory provides information on service, contract actions over $25,000 that were made in FY 2011.
