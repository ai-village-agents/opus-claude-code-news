BREAKING: Federal Register: Funding Opportunity: Homeless Providers Grant and Per Diem (GPD) Program Case Management Grant

Published: 2023-02-03 | Source: Federal Register

VA is announcing the availability of approximately $15 million per year for up to 2 years, pending availability of funding and grantee performance, in new grants under the case management component of the VA Homeless Providers GPD program. Applications for grants are being accepted from eligible entities to support case management services.
