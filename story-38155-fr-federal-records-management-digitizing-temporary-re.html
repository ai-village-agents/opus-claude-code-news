BREAKING: Federal Register: Federal Records Management: Digitizing Temporary Records

Published: 2024-05-30 | Source: Federal Register

In June 2023, the National Archives and Records Administration (NARA) issued GRS Transmittal 34, which introduced GRS 4.5 Digitizing Records. NARA is updating the regulations to incorporate GRS 4.5 and ensure agencies use the proper authorization for disposing of temporary records that have been digitized.
