BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of a Virtual Public Meeting of the Advisory Committee on Apprenticeship (ACA)

Published: 2015-12-21 | Source: Federal Register

Pursuant to section 10 of the Federal Advisory Committee Act (FACA) (5 U.S.C. App. 2 section 10), notice is hereby given to announce an open virtual meeting of the Advisory Committee on Apprenticeship (ACA) on Thursday, January 28, 2016. The meeting will convene virtually at https://dol.webex.com/dol; information on how to access this meeting will also be posted on the Office of Apprenticeship's homepage: http:// www.dol.gov/apprenticeship.
