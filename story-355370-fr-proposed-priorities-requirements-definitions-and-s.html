BREAKING: Federal Register: Proposed Priorities, Requirements, Definitions, and Selection Criteria-Race to the Top-Early Learning Challenge

Published: 2013-05-20 | Source: Federal Register

The Secretary of Education and Secretary of Health and Human Services ("the Secretaries") propose priorities, requirements, definitions, and selection criteria under the Race to the Top--Early Learning Challenge (RTT-ELC) Grant program. The Secretaries may use one or more of these priorities, requirements, definitions, and selection criteria for competitions in fiscal year (FY) 2013 and later years.
