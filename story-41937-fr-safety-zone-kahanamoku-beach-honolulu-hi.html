BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone, Kahanamoku Beach, Honolulu, HI

Published: 2024-07-17 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is proposing to establish a temporary safety zone for certain waters of the Kahanamoku Beach. This action is necessary to provide for the safety of life on these navigable waters near Honolulu, HI, during a drone show display at various times on August 13 through August 18, 2024.
