BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; The Boeing Company Airplanes

Published: 2025-10-02 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA is adopting a new airworthiness directive (AD) for all The Boeing Company Model 737-600, -700, -700C, -800, -900, and -900ER series airplanes. This AD was prompted by a report of improper grinding of the inner diameter of the main landing gear (MLG) outer cylinders, resulting in possible heat damage to the outer cylinders.
