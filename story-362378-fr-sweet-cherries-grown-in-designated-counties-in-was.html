BREAKING: Federal Register: Sweet Cherries Grown in Designated Counties in Washington; Decreased Assessment Rate

Published: 2013-08-08 | Source: Federal Register

This rule decreases the assessment rate established for the Washington Cherry Marketing Committee (Committee) for the 2013-2014 and subsequent fiscal periods from $0.18 to $0.15 per ton of sweet cherries handled. The Committee locally administers the marketing order, which regulates the handling of sweet cherries grown in designated counties in Washington.
