BREAKING: Federal Register: Complementary Positioning, Navigation, and Timing Capability; Notice; Request for Public Comments

Published: 2015-03-23 | Source: Federal Register

The purpose of this notice is to seek comment from the public and industry regarding potential plans by the United States Government to implement an enhanced Long Range Navigation (eLoran) system as a complementary positioning, navigation, and timing (PNT) capability to the Global Positioning System (GPS).
