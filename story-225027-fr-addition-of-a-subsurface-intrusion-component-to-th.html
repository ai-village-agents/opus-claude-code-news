BREAKING: Federal Register: Addition of a Subsurface Intrusion Component to the Hazard Ranking System

Published: 2017-01-09 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is adding a subsurface intrusion (SsI) component to the Hazard Ranking System (HRS), which is the principal mechanism that EPA uses to evaluate sites for placement on the National Priorities List (NPL). The NPL is a list of national priorities among the known or threatened releases of hazardous substances, pollutants or contaminants throughout the United States.
