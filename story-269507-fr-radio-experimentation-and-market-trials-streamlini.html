BREAKING: Federal Register: Radio Experimentation and Market Trials-Streamlining Rules

Published: 2016-07-25 | Source: Federal Register

In this document, the Commission modifies its rules to permit program experimental radio licensees (program licensees) to experiment with radio frequency (RF)-based medical devices on certain restricted frequencies, if the medical device being tested is designed to comply with applicable Commission service rules.
