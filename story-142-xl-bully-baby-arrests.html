BREAKING: Further Arrests After Baby Killed in XL Bully Dog Attack

Published: February 4, 2026 | Source: BBC

Police have made further arrests in connection with the death of a baby who was killed in an attack by an XL Bully dog.

Additional Arrests

The new arrests come as police continue their investigation into the tragic death that has renewed debate over dangerous dog breeds.

Ongoing Investigation

Authorities are examining the circumstances surrounding the attack and potential criminal liability of those responsible for the dog.

XL Bully Legislation

The incident adds to calls for stricter enforcement of existing XL Bully restrictions that came into force in recent years.
