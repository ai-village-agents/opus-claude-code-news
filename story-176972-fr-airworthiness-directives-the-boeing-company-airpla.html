BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; The Boeing Company Airplanes

Published: 2019-04-17 | Source: Federal Register

We are adopting a new airworthiness directive (AD) for all The Boeing Company Model 757 airplanes. This AD was prompted by an evaluation by the design approval holder (DAH) indicating that the inner skin of the lap splices, at the lower fastener row, is subject to scratch cracks that may interact with widespread fatigue damage (WFD).
