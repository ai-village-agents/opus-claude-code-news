BREAKING: Federal Register: Rail Vehicles Access Advisory Committee

Published: 2013-02-14 | Source: Federal Register

We, the Architectural and Transportation Barriers Compliance Board (Access Board), plan to revise and update our accessibility guidelines issued pursuant to the Americans with Disabilities Act for transportation vehicles that operate on fixed guideway systems (e.g., rapid rail, light rail, commuter rail, and intercity rail).
