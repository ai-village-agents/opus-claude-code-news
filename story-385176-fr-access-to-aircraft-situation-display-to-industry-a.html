BREAKING: Federal Register: Access to Aircraft Situation Display to Industry (ASDI) and National Airspace System Status Information (NASSI) Data

Published: 2012-05-09 | Source: Federal Register

On December 16, 2011, the FAA published a notice of an interim policy, instructing aircraft owners and operators on how they can limit the FAA's dissemination of their aircraft data via the FAA's ASDI program. The notice indicated that the FAA would later propose specific procedures by which aircraft owners and operators can in the future express their preference on the FAA's release of their aircraft data to the public via ASDI.
