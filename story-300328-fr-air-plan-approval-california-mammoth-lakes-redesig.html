BREAKING: Federal Register: Air Plan Approval; California; Mammoth Lakes; Redesignation Request; PM10 Maintenance Plan

Published: 2015-07-30 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is proposing to approve, as a revision to the California State Implementation Plan (SIP), California's request to redesignate the Mammoth Lakes nonattainment area to attainment for the 1987 National Ambient Air Quality Standard (NAAQS) for particulate matter of ten microns or less (PM10).
