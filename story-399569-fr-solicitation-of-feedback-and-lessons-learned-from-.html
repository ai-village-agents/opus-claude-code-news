BREAKING: Federal Register: Solicitation of Feedback and Lessons-Learned from the Pilot of the Revised Construction Reactor Oversight Process

Published: 2012-10-22 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Nuclear Regulatory Commission (NRC or the Commission) is soliciting feedback and lessons-learned from members of the public, licensees, and interest groups regarding the effectiveness of the revised construction reactor oversight process (cROP) as demonstrated during the pilot program currently in progress.
