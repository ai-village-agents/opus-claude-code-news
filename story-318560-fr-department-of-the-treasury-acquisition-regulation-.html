BREAKING: Federal Register: Department of the Treasury Acquisition Regulation; Internet Payment Platform; Technical Amendment

Published: 2014-03-11 | Source: Federal Register

On July 9, 2012, the Department of the Treasury amended the Department of the Treasury Acquisition Regulation (DTAR) to implement use of the Internet Payment Platform, a centralized electronic invoicing and payment information system, and to change the definition of bureau to reflect the consolidation on July 21, 2011 of the Office of Thrift Supervision with the Office of the Comptroller of the Currency. This document makes one technical amendment to a clause heading.
