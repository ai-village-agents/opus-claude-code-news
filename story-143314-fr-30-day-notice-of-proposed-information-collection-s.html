BREAKING: Federal Register: 30-Day Notice of Proposed Information Collection: Section 202 Supportive Housing for the Elderly Application Submission Requirements, OMB Control No. 2502-0267

Published: 2020-01-31 | Source: Federal Register

HUD is seeking approval from the Office of Management and Budget (OMB) for the information collection described below. The purpose of this notice is to allow for an additional 30 days of public comment.
