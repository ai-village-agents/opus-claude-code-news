BREAKING: Federal Register: Entry-Level Driver Training: Application for Exemption; Alaska's Ice Road Driving School

Published: 2023-09-12 | Source: Federal Register

FMCSA announces its decision to deny the application from Alaska's Ice Road Driving School requesting an exemption from certain portions of the behind-the-wheel (BTW) entry-level driver training (ELDT) requirements for driver trainees.
