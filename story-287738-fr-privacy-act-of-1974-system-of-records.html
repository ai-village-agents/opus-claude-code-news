BREAKING: Federal Register: Privacy Act of 1974; System of Records

Published: 2015-03-03 | Source: Federal Register

As required by the Privacy Act of 1974, 5 U.S.C. 552a(e), notice is hereby given that the Department of Veterans Affairs (VA) is amending the system of records currently entitled "Customer Relationship Management System (CRMS)-VA" (155VA16) as set forth in the Federal Register 77 FR 72123. VA is amending the system by revising the System Number, System Location, Categories of Individuals Covered by the System, Categories of Records in the System, Safeguards, and System Managers and Address.
