BREAKING: Federal Register: Spiny Lobster Fishery of the Gulf of Mexico and South Atlantic; Amendment 11

Published: 2012-07-27 | Source: Federal Register

NMFS issues this final rule to implement Amendment 11 to the Fishery Management Plan for the Spiny Lobster Fishery in the Gulf of Mexico and South Atlantic (FMP), as prepared and submitted by the Gulf of Mexico and South Atlantic Fishery Management Councils (Councils).
