BREAKING: Federal Register: Fisheries of the Caribbean, Gulf of Mexico, and South Atlantic; Reef Fish Fishery of the Gulf of Mexico; Revisions to Red Snapper and Hogfish Management Measures

Published: 2019-03-05 | Source: Federal Register

NMFS issues regulations to implement management measures described in two framework actions to the Fishery Management Plan (FMP) for the Reef Fish Resources of the Gulf of Mexico (Gulf), as prepared by the Gulf of Mexico Fishery Management Council (Council). The framework actions are titled "Modify the Annual Catch Limit (ACL) for the Gulf Red Snapper and Hogfish Stocks" (ACL Framework Action) and "Modify the Red Snapper Recreational Annual Catch Targets (ACT)" (ACT Framework Action).
