BREAKING: Federal Register: Determination That TRISENOX (Arsenic Trioxide) Injection, 1 Milligram/Milliliter, Was Not Withdrawn From Sale for Reasons of Safety or Effectiveness

Published: 2019-04-18 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA or Agency) has determined that TRISENOX (arsenic trioxide) injection, 1 milligram (mg)/milliliter (mL), was not withdrawn from sale for reasons of safety or effectiveness.
