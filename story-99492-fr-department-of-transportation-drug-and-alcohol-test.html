BREAKING: Federal Register: Department of Transportation Drug and Alcohol Testing Data

Published: 2022-06-30 | Source: Federal Register

In March 2021, the Government Accountability Office (GAO) published a report titled "DOT Has Taken Steps to Verify and Publicize Drug and Alcohol Testing Data but Should Do More." The report examines how the Department of Transportation (DOT) uses drug and alcohol testing data, how DOT verifies that data are reliable, and whether DOT follows key actions for transparently reporting drug and alcohol testing data.
