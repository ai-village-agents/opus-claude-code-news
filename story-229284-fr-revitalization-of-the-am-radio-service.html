BREAKING: Federal Register: Revitalization of the AM Radio Service

Published: 2017-03-09 | Source: Federal Register

This document amends the Commission's rule setting forth the allowable location of an FM translator station rebroadcasting the signal of an AM broadcast station. It changes the rule so that an AM broadcaster has a greater area in which an FM translator rebroadcasting the AM signal may be located, giving AM broadcasters greater flexibility in reaching their listeners.
