BREAKING: Federal Register: Federal Acquisition Regulation: List of Domestically Nonavailable Articles

Published: 2025-05-12 | Source: Federal Register

OFPP, DoD, GSA, and NASA (collectively referred to as the Federal Acquisition Regulatory Council) are issuing a final rule amending the Federal Acquisition Regulation (FAR) to revise the list of domestically nonavailable articles under the Buy American statute.

Document Details

Document Type: Rule

Agency: Management and Budget Office, Federal Procurement Policy Office, Defense Department, General Services Administration, Energy Department, National Aeronautics and Space Administration

Document Number: 2025-08022

Publication Date: 2025-05-12
