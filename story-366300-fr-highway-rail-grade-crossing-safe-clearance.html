BREAKING: Federal Register: Highway-Rail Grade Crossing; Safe Clearance

Published: 2013-09-25 | Source: Federal Register

FMCSA and PHMSA amend the Federal Motor Carrier Safety Regulations (FMCSRs) and Hazardous Materials Regulations (HMRs), respectively, to prohibit a driver of a commercial motor vehicle or of a motor vehicle transporting certain hazardous materials or certain agents or toxins (hereafter collectively referenced as "regulated motor vehicle") from entering onto a highway-rail grade crossing unless there is sufficient space to drive completely through the grade crossing without stopping.
