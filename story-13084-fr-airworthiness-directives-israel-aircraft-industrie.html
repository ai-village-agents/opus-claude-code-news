BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Israel Aircraft Industries Ltd. Airplanes

Published: 2025-06-06 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA is adopting a new airworthiness directive (AD) for all Israel Aircraft Industries Ltd. Model 1124 and 1124A airplanes. This AD was prompted by the need to ensure proper thrust reverser system status and function and to minimize the possibility of thrust reverser operation in flight or before landing.
