BREAKING: Federal Register: Fisheries of the Northeastern United States; 2018-2020 Small-Mesh Multispecies Specifications

Published: 2018-06-14 | Source: Federal Register

NMFS issues final 2018 and projected 2019-2020 specifications for the small-mesh multispecies fishery, and corrects an error from a previous action. The specifications are necessary to establish allowable catch limits for each stock within the fishery to control overfishing while allowing optimum yield, consistent with the Magnuson- Stevens Fishery Conservation and Management Act.
