BREAKING: Federal Register: Mortgage Servicing Rights

Published: 2020-12-31 | Source: Federal Register

The NCUA Board (Board) proposes to amend its investment regulation to permit federal credit unions (FCUs) to purchase mortgage servicing rights from other federally insured credit unions under certain conditions.
