BREAKING: Federal Register: Table of Frequency Allocations and Radio Regulations; Correction

Published: 2023-06-23 | Source: Federal Register

On June 7, 2023, the Federal Communications Commission's Office of Engineering and Technology issued a final rule making non- substantive editorial revisions to conform certain of the Commission's rules to the formatting requirements of the Code of Federal Regulations. This document corrects two typographical errors in the rule.
