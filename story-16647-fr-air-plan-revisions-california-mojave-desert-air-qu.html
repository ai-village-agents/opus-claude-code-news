BREAKING: Federal Register: Air Plan Revisions; California; Mojave Desert Air Quality Management District; New Source Review; Stationary Source Permits

Published: 2025-07-24 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is proposing conditional approval of five permitting rules as a revision to the Mojave Desert Air Quality Management District (MDAQMD) portion of the California State Implementation Plan (SIP). These are revisions to the District's New Source Review (NSR) air permitting program rules for new and modified sources of air pollution under the Clean Air Act (CAA or "Act").
