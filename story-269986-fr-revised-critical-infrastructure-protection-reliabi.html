BREAKING: Federal Register: Revised Critical Infrastructure Protection Reliability Standards

Published: 2016-07-29 | Source: Federal Register

The Federal Energy Regulatory Commission (Commission) directs the North American Electric Reliability Corporation to develop a new or modified Reliability Standard that addresses supply chain risk management for industrial control system hardware, software, and computing and networking services associated with bulk electric system operations.
