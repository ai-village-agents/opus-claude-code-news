BREAKING: Federal Register: Innovative Public Transportation Workforce Development Program (Ladders of Opportunity Initiative)

Published: 2014-09-05 | Source: Federal Register

The Federal Transit Administration (FTA) announces a Notice of Funding Availability (NOFA) for the Innovative Public Transportation Workforce Development Program Ladders of Opportunity Initiative. FTA will make available approximately $7.85 million in funds appropriated in Fiscal Year 2013 and Prior Years, in support of this effort.
