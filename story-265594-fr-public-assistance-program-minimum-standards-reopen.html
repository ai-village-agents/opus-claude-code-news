BREAKING: Federal Register: Public Assistance Program Minimum Standards; Reopening of Comment Period

Published: 2016-06-08 | Source: Federal Register

The Federal Emergency Management Agency (FEMA) is issuing this notice to advise the public that FEMA is reopening the comment period for Docket ID FEMA-2016-0007. The initial notice of availability of FEMA's proposed policy regarding minimum standards for Public Assistance restoration of damaged facility projects published in the April 21, 2016 Federal Register, and requested public comments no later than May 23, 2016.
