BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; The Boeing Company Airplanes

Published: 2020-03-30 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA is withdrawing a notice of proposed rulemaking (NPRM) that proposed to adopt a new airworthiness directive (AD) that would have applied to certain The Boeing Company Model 787-8 and 787-9 airplanes. The NPRM was prompted by a report that a passenger entry door assist handle became detached during use.
