BREAKING: Federal Register: Railroad Cost Recovery Procedures-Productivity Adjustment

Published: 2024-01-24 | Source: Federal Register

In a decision served on January 19, 2024, the Board proposed to adopt 1.011 (1.1% per year) as the measure of average (geometric mean) change in railroad productivity for the 2018-2022 (five-year) period. The Board's January 19, 2024 decision stated that comments may be filed addressing any perceived data and computational errors in the Board's calculation.
