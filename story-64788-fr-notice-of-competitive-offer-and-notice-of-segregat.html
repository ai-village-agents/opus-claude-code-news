BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Competitive Offer and Notice of Segregation for Solar Energy Development on Public Land, Nye County, Nevada

Published: 2023-04-11 | Source: Federal Register

The Bureau of Land Management (BLM), Pahrump Field Office will accept competitive bids on four parcels of public lands in Nye County, Nevada, for photovoltaic solar energy development: two parcels located within the Amargosa Valley Solar Energy Zone offered for lease, and two parcels to determine preferred right-of-way applicants.
