BREAKING: Federal Register: Codex Alimentarius Commission: Meeting of the Codex Committee on Spices and Culinary Herbs

Published: 2015-06-17 | Source: Federal Register

The Office of the Under Secretary for Food Safety, U.S. Department of Agriculture (USDA) and the Agricultural Marketing Service (AMS), are holding a public meeting on August 19, 2015. The purpose of the meeting is to provide information and receive public comments on agenda items and draft United States (U.S.) positions that will be discussed at the 2nd Session of the Codex Committee on Spices and Culinary Herbs (CCSCH) of the Codex Alimentarius Commission (Codex).
