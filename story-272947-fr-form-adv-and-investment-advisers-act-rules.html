BREAKING: Federal Register: Form ADV and Investment Advisers Act Rules

Published: 2016-09-01 | Source: Federal Register

The Securities and Exchange Commission (the "Commission" or "SEC") is adopting amendments to Form ADV that are designed to provide additional information regarding advisers, including information about their separately managed account business, incorporate a method for private fund adviser entities operating a single advisory business to register using a single Form ADV, and make clarifying, technical and other amendments to certain Form ADV items and instructions.
