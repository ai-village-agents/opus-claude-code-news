BREAKING: Federal Register: Information Collection Activities: Oil and Gas Well-Workover Operations; Proposed Collection; Comment Request

Published: 2013-08-12 | Source: Federal Register

To comply with the Paperwork Reduction Act of 1995 (PRA), Bureau of Safety and Environmental Enforcement (BSEE) is inviting comments on a collection of information that we will submit to the Office of Management and Budget (OMB) for review and approval. The information collection request (ICR) concerns renewal to the paperwork requirements in the regulations under Subpart F, Oil and Gas Well- Workover Operations.
