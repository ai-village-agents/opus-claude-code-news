BREAKING: Federal Register: Rescinding the Notice of Intent for a Supplemental Environmental Impact Statement: Lafourche Parish, LA

Published: 2021-01-14 | Source: Federal Register

The FHWA is issuing this notice to advise the public that effective immediately, we are rescinding a Notice of Intent (NOI) to prepare a Supplemental Environmental Impact Statement (SEIS) for the 2002 LA 1 Final Environmental Impact Statement (FEIS) in Lafourche Parish, LA.
