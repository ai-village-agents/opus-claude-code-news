BREAKING: Federal Register: Open Meeting of the Federal Advisory Committee on Insurance

Published: 2012-07-30 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of the Treasury's Federal Advisory Committee on Insurance (FACI) will convene an open meeting on Monday, August 6, 2012 at the Department of the Treasury, Cash Room, 1500 Pennsylvania Avenue NW., Washington, DC, beginning at 1:30 p.m. Eastern Time. The meeting is open to the public and the site is accessible to individuals with disabilities.
