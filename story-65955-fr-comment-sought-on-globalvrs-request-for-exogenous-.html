BREAKING: Federal Register: Comment Sought on GlobalVRS Request for Exogenous VRS DeafBlind Costs

Published: 2023-04-25 | Source: Federal Register

In this document, the Federal Communications Commission (Commission) seeks comment on the petition of ASL Services Holdings, LLC dba GlobalVRS (GlobalVRS) for exogenous cost reimbursement for the provision of Video Relay Service (VRS) to people who are deafblind.
