BREAKING: Federal Register: Approval and Promulgation of Air Quality Implementation Plans; Ohio; Ohio PM2.5 NSR

Published: 2014-10-28 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is proposing to approve, under the Clean Air Act (CAA), revisions to Ohio's state implementation plan (SIP) as requested by the Ohio Environmental Protection Agency (OEPA) to EPA on June 19, 2014.
