BREAKING: Federal Register: Rules of Adjudication and Enforcement

Published: 2013-05-21 | Source: Federal Register

The United States International Trade Commission ("Commission") amends its Rules of Practice and Procedure concerning adjudication and enforcement. The amendments address concerns that have arisen about the scope of discovery in Commission proceedings under section 337 of the Tariff Act of 1930.
