BREAKING: Federal Register: Restrictions on Qualified Financial Contracts of Certain FDIC-Supervised Institutions; Revisions to the Definition of Qualifying Master Netting Agreement and Related Definitions

Published: 2017-10-30 | Source: Federal Register

The FDIC is adding regulations to improve the resolvability of systemically important U.S. banking organizations and systemically important foreign banking organizations and enhance the resilience and the safety and soundness of certain State savings associations and State-chartered banks that are not members of the Federal Reserve System ("State non-member banks" or "SNMBs") for which the FDIC is the primary Federal regulator (together, "FSIs" or "FDIC-supervised institutions").
