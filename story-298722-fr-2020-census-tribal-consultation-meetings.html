BREAKING: Federal Register: 2020 Census Tribal Consultation Meetings

Published: 2015-07-13 | Source: Federal Register

Pursuant to Executive Order 13175, the Bureau of the Census (Census Bureau) plans to conduct eight tribal consultation meetings and one national webinar with federally recognized tribes across the country between October 2015 and April 2016. These meetings will provide a forum for tribes to share insights, make recommendations and discuss concerns related to the 2020 Census.
