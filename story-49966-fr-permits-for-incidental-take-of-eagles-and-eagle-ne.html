BREAKING: Federal Register: Permits for Incidental Take of Eagles and Eagle Nests; Correction and Technical Amendment

Published: 2024-10-17 | Source: Federal Register

We, the U.S. Fish and Wildlife Service, correct and amend a final rule that revised the regulations for the issuance of permits for eagle incidental take and eagle nest take. A provision of the regulations in the final rule specifies eligibility criteria for general permits for wind energy projects based on eagle relative abundance and proximity to eagle nests.
