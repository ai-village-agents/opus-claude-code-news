BREAKING: Federal Register: Finding Regarding Foreign Social Insurance or Pension System of Mongolia

Published: 2022-07-01 | Source: Federal Register

We find that, under the Alien Nonpayment Provision of the Social Security Act (Act), as amended, citizens of Mongolia may not continue to receive Social Security benefits under title II after 6 consecutive calendar months of absence from the United States, unless they meet an exception not related to citizenship of Mongolia.
