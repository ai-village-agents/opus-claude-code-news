BREAKING: Federal Register: Pipeline Safety: Inspection and Protection of Pipeline Facilities After Railway Accidents

Published: 2012-07-31 | Source: Federal Register

PHMSA is issuing an advisory bulletin to alert all pipeline owners and operators of the circumstances of the Cherry Valley, Illinois derailment and remind them of the importance of assuring that pipeline facilities have not been damaged either during a railroad accident or other event occurring in the right-of-way.
