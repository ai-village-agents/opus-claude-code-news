BREAKING: Federal Register: Hours of Service of Drivers: WestRock, Application for Renewal of Exemption

Published: 2019-02-21 | Source: Federal Register

WestRock, formerly known as RockTenn has requested a renewal of its exemption from certain provisions of the hours-of-service (HOS) requirements for drivers of property-carrying vehicles.
