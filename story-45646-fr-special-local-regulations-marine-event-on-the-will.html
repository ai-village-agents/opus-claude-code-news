BREAKING: Federal Register: Special Local Regulations; Marine Event on the Willamette River, Portland, OR

Published: 2024-08-30 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard will enforce special local regulations for the Portland Dragon Boat Races from September 7 through 8, 2024 to provide for the safety of life on navigable waterways during this event. Our regulation for marine events within the Thirteenth Coast Guard District identifies the regulated area for this event in Portland, OR.
