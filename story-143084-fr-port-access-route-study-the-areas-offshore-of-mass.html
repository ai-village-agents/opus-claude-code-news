BREAKING: Federal Register: Port Access Route Study: The Areas Offshore of Massachusetts and Rhode Island

Published: 2020-01-29 | Source: Federal Register

From March 26, 2019 through December 15, 2019, the Coast Guard conducted The Areas Offshore of Massachusetts and Rhode Island Port Access Route Study and is now requesting your comments on a draft version of the study report. The goal of the study is to enhance navigational safety in the study area by examining existing shipping routes and waterway uses.
