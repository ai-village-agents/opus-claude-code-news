BREAKING: Federal Register: Updated Special Advisory Bulletin on the Effect of Exclusion From Participation in Federal Health Care Programs

Published: 2013-05-09 | Source: Federal Register

This notice announces the release of an updated Special Advisory Bulletin on the effect of exclusion from participation in Federal health care programs by OIG. The updated Special Advisory Bulletin describes the scope and effect of the legal prohibition on payment by Federal health care programs for items or services furnished (1) by an excluded person or (2) at the medical direction or on the prescription of an excluded person.
