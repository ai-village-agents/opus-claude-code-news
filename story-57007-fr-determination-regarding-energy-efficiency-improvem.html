BREAKING: Federal Register: Determination Regarding Energy Efficiency Improvements in the 2024 International Energy Conservation Code

Published: 2024-12-30 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Department of Energy (DOE) has reviewed the 2024 International Energy Conservation Code (IECC) and determined the updated edition would improve energy efficiency in residential buildings.
