BREAKING: Federal Register: Rail-Term Corp.-Petition for Declaratory Order

Published: 2014-02-18 | Source: Federal Register

On December 13, 2013, Rail-Term Corp. (Rail-Term) filed a petition for reconsideration of the Board's November 19, 2013 decision which found Rail-Term to be a "rail carrier," as defined in the Interstate Commerce Act at 49 U.S.C. 10102(5), and, therefore, subject to the Board's jurisdiction.
