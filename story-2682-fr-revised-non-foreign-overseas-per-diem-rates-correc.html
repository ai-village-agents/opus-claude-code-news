BREAKING: Federal Register: Revised Non-Foreign Overseas Per Diem Rates; Correction

Published: 2026-01-06 | Source: Federal Register

On December 31, 2025, the DoD published a notice titled Revised Non-Foreign Overseas Per Diem Rates. Subsequent to publication of the notice, the DoD realized that some of the numbers in the table published incorrectly. This correction reprints the table in its entirety to include the updated numbers. All other information in the original notice remains the same.
