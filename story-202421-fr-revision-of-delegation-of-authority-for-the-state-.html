BREAKING: Federal Register: Revision of Delegation of Authority for the State Executive Director (SED) for the Farm Loan Programs

Published: 2018-03-19 | Source: Federal Register

This document amends the delegations of authority from the Farm Service Agency (FSA) Deputy Administrator of Farm Loan Programs (FLP). The change will specify that the Deputy Administrator redelegates certain authority to the State Executive Directors (SED).
