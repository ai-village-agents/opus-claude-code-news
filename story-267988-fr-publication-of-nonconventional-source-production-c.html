BREAKING: Federal Register: Publication of Nonconventional Source Production Credit Reference Price for Calendar Year 2015

Published: 2016-07-06 | Source: Federal Register

Publication of the reference price for the nonconventional source production credit for calendar year 2015. The credit period for nonconventional source production credit ended on December 31, 2013 for facilities producing coke or coke gas (other than from petroleum based products).
