BREAKING: Federal Register: Postmarketing Safety Reports for Approved New Animal Drugs; Electronic Submission Requirements; Correction

Published: 2020-09-22 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA, the Agency, or we) is correcting a final rule that published in the Federal Register of July 29, 2020. That final rule requires electronic submission of certain postmarketing safety reports for approved new animal drugs and provides a procedure for requesting a temporary waiver of the electronic submission requirement.
