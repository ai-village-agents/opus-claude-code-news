BREAKING: Federal Register: Fees for the Unified Carrier Registration Plan and Agreement

Published: 2018-12-28 | Source: Federal Register

This rule establishes reductions in the annual registration fees collected from motor carriers, motor private carriers of property, brokers, freight forwarders, and leasing companies for the Unified Carrier Registration (UCR) Plan and Agreement for the registration years 2019, 2020 and thereafter.
