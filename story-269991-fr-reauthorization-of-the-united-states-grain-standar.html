BREAKING: Federal Register: Reauthorization of the United States Grain Standards Act

Published: 2016-07-29 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Agriculture (USDA) Grain Inspection, Packers and Stockyards Administration (GIPSA) is revising existing regulations and adding new regulations under the United States Grain Standards Act (USGSA), as amended, in order to comply with amendments to the USGSA made by the Agriculture Reauthorizations Act of 2015.
