BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Sikorsky Aircraft Corporation (Sikorsky) Helicopters

Published: 2016-03-31 | Source: Federal Register

We are superseding Airworthiness Directive (AD) 2014-07-04R1 for certain Model S-92A helicopters. AD 2014-07-04R1 required repetitive inspections in the upper deck area for incorrectly installed clamps and chafing between the electrical wires and the hydraulic lines and replacing any unairworthy wires or hydraulic lines.
