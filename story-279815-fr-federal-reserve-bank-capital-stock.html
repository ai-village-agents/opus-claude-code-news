BREAKING: Federal Register: Federal Reserve Bank Capital Stock

Published: 2016-11-23 | Source: Federal Register

The Board of Governors (Board) is adopting, in final form and without change, an interim final rule amending Regulation I. The final rule establishes procedures for payment of dividends by the Federal Reserve Banks (Reserve Banks) to implement the provisions of section 32203 of the "Fixing America's Surface Transportation Act."
