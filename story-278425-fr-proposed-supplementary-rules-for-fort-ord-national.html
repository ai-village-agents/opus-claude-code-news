BREAKING: Federal Register: Proposed Supplementary Rules for Fort Ord National Monument, California

Published: 2016-11-04 | Source: Federal Register

The California State Director of the Bureau of Land Management (BLM) is proposing to establish new supplementary rules related to dog management and public safety on public lands at Fort Ord National Monument (FONM), California. Furthermore, these proposed rules would clarify some of the existing restrictions that have been in place on the FONM since 1996.
