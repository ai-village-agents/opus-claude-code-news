BREAKING: Federal Register: Denial of Motor Vehicle Defect Petition

Published: 2020-03-04 | Source: Federal Register

This notice sets forth the reasons for the denial of a petition submitted on May 9, 2018, by Mr. J. Kevin Byrne (the petitioner) to NHTSA's Office of Defects Investigation (ODI). The petition requests that the Agency "undertake a defects investigation" into "rust-related fuel tank detachment of Isuzu Rodeo fuel tanks."
