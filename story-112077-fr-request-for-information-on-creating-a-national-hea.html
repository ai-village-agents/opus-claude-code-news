BREAKING: Federal Register: Request for Information on Creating a National Healthcare System Action Alliance To Advance Patient Safety

Published: 2022-12-12 | Source: Federal Register

The Agency for Healthcare Research and Quality (AHRQ), on behalf of the Department of Health and Human Services (HHS), seeks public comment about advancing patient and healthcare workforce safety through the development of a National Healthcare System Action Alliance to Advance Patient Safety (Action Alliance) in partnership with healthcare systems, patients, families and caregivers, HHS and other Federal agencies, and other stakeholders to support sustained improvements in patient safety.
