BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone; Navy UNDET, Apra Outer Harbor and Piti, GU

Published: 2016-05-13 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is establishing a temporary safety zone for underwater detonation operations in the waters of Apra Outer Harbor and Piti, Guam. The Coast Guard believes this safety zone regulation is necessary to protect all persons and vessels that would otherwise transit or be within the affected areas from possible safety hazards associated with underwater detonation operations.
