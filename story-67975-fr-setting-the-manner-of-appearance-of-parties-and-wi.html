BREAKING: Federal Register: Setting the Manner of Appearance of Parties and Witnesses at Hearings

Published: 2023-05-19 | Source: Federal Register

We propose to update our hearing regulations by changing the terms "video teleconference" to "video"; changing "telephone" to "audio"; and permitting "video" and "audio" to be used as standard manners of appearance. These changes would clarify that claimants may appear for hearings remotely, using private electronic devices that we do not own, operate, or approve.
