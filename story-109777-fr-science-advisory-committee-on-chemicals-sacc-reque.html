BREAKING: Federal Register: Science Advisory Committee on Chemicals (SACC); Request for Nominations

Published: 2022-11-10 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) invites the public to nominate scientific experts from a diverse range of disciplines to be considered for appointment to the Science Advisory Committee on Chemicals (SACC), established pursuant to the Toxic Substances Control Act (TSCA). EPA anticipates appointing new SACC members by mid-2023 due to expiring membership terms.
