BREAKING: Federal Register: Small Business Investment Company (SBIC) Program: Update to TechNote 9: Guidance for Obtaining a Regulatory Exemption for Overline Investments

Published: 2014-07-10 | Source: Federal Register

The Small Business Administration (SBA) intends to update TechNote 9: Guidance for Obtaining a Regulatory Exemption for Overline Investments (TechNote 9) to conform to changes to the overline regulation that have occurred and explain the process SBICs have to follow to obtain SBA's prior written exemption for an overline investment. The SBA welcomes comments from the public on how to achieve these objectives.
