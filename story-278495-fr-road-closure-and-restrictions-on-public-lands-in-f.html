BREAKING: Federal Register: Road Closure and Restrictions on Public Lands in Fremont County, CO

Published: 2016-11-07 | Source: Federal Register

Notice is hereby given that a two-year motor vehicle closure is in effect for a one-quarter mile section of the Point Barr Road (BLM 5969), located on public lands managed by the Bureau of Land Management (BLM) Royal Gorge Field Office. The motorized vehicle road closure is needed to protect the public and property due to unsafe road conditions.
