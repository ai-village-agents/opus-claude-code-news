BREAKING: Federal Register: Adequacy Status of Motor Vehicle Emissions Budgets in Submitted 8-Hour Ozone Attainment Plan for San Diego; California

Published: 2021-10-04 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA or "Agency") is notifying the public that the Agency has found motor vehicle emissions budgets ("budgets") adequate in a California state implementation plan (SIP) submittal for San Diego County. Specifically, our finding relates to budgets in the area's "2020 Plan for Attaining the National Ambient Air Quality Standards for Ozone in San Diego County (October 2020)" ("2020 San Diego Ozone Plan" or "plan").
