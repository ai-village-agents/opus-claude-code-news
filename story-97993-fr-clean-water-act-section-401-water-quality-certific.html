BREAKING: Federal Register: Clean Water Act Section 401 Water Quality Certification Improvement Rule

Published: 2022-06-09 | Source: Federal Register

Following a careful reconsideration of the water quality certification rule promulgated in 2020, the Environmental Protection Agency (EPA or the Agency) is publishing for public comment a proposed rule revising and replacing the Agency's 2020 regulatory requirements for water quality certification under Clean Water Act (CWA) section 401.
