BREAKING: Federal Register: Household Goods Consumer Protection Working Group: Membership Solicitation

Published: 2016-04-20 | Source: Federal Register

FMCSA announces its intent to establish the Household Goods (HHG) Consumer Protection Working Group (Working Group). The Fixing America's Surface Transportation (FAST) Act requires FMCSA to establish this Working Group to provide recommendations on how to better educate and protect HHG moving customers (consumers) during an interstate HHG move.
