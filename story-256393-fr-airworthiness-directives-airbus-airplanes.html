BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Airbus Airplanes

Published: 2016-02-25 | Source: Federal Register

We propose to supersede Airworthiness Directive (AD) 2013-25- 08, for all Airbus Model A330-200, -200 Freighter, and -300 series airplanes; and Model A340-200 and -300 series airplanes.
