BREAKING: Federal Register: American Society of Mechanical Engineers Code Cases and Update Frequency; Correction

Published: 2024-07-29 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Nuclear Regulatory Commission (NRC) is correcting a final rule that was published in the Federal Register on July 17, 2024, regarding changes to its regulations to incorporate by reference revisions of three regulatory guides, which would approve new, revised, and reaffirmed code cases published by the American Society of Mechanical Engineers. This action is necessary to correct a misspelled word, a grammatical error, an amendatory instruction, and footnote references.
