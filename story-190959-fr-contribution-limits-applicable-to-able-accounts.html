BREAKING: Federal Register: Contribution Limits Applicable to ABLE Accounts

Published: 2019-10-10 | Source: Federal Register

This document contains proposed regulations related to the Internal Revenue Code (Code), which allows a State (or its agency or instrumentality) to establish and maintain a tax-advantaged savings program under which contributions may be made to an ABLE account for the purpose of paying for the qualified disability expenses of the designated beneficiary of the account.
