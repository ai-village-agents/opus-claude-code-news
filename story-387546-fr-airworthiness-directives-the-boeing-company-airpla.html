BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; The Boeing Company Airplanes

Published: 2012-06-05 | Source: Federal Register

This document announces a reopening of the comment period for the above-referenced NPRM, which proposed the adoption of a new airworthiness directive (AD) for certain The Boeing Company Model 757 airplanes. That NPRM invites comments concerning the proposed requirement to modify the fuel quantity indication system (FQIS) wiring or fuel tank systems to prevent development of an ignition source inside the center fuel tank.
