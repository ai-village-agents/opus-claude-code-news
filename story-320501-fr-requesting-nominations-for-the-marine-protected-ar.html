BREAKING: Federal Register: Requesting Nominations for the Marine Protected Areas Federal Advisory Committee; Notice of Public Meeting

Published: 2014-04-01 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Commerce (Department) is seeking nominations for membership on the Marine Protected Areas Federal Advisory Committee (Committee). The Committee advises the Secretary of Commerce and the Secretary of the Interior in implementing Section 4 of Executive Order 13158, specifically on strategies and priorities for developing the national system of marine protected areas (MPAs) and on practical approaches to further enhance and expand protection of new and existing MPAs.
