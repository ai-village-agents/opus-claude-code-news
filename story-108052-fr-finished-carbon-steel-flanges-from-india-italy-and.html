BREAKING: Federal Register: Finished Carbon Steel Flanges From India, Italy, and Spain; Scheduling of Expedited Five-Year Reviews

Published: 2022-10-20 | Source: Federal Register

The Commission hereby gives notice of the scheduling of expedited reviews pursuant to the Tariff Act of 1930 ("the Act") to determine whether revocation of the countervailing duty order on finished carbon steel flanges from India and the antidumping duty orders on finished carbon steel flanges from India, Italy, and Spain would be likely to lead to continuation or recurrence of material injury within a reasonably foreseeable time.
