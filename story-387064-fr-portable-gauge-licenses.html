BREAKING: Federal Register: Portable Gauge Licenses

Published: 2012-05-30 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Nuclear Regulatory Commission (NRC or the Commission) is revising its licensing guidance for portable gauge licensees. The NRC is requesting public comment on NUREG-1556, Volume 1, Revision 2, "Consolidated Guidance About Materials Licenses: Program-Specific Guidance About Portable Gauge Licenses."
