BREAKING: Federal Register: FD&C Yellow No. 5; Exemption From the Requirement of a Tolerance

Published: 2013-09-26 | Source: Federal Register

This regulation establishes an exemption from the requirement of a tolerance for residues of FD&C Yellow No. 5 (CAS Reg. No. 1934-21- 0) when used as an inert ingredient (dye) in antimicrobial pesticide formulations applied to food-contact surfaces in public eating places, dairy-processing equipment, and food-processing equipment and utensils.
