BREAKING: Federal Register: Hispanic-Serving Agricultural Colleges and Universities (HSACU) Certification Process

Published: 2012-04-27 | Source: Federal Register

This final rule establishes the process and procedures to certify a qualifying college or university as a Hispanic-Serving Agricultural Colleges and Universities (HSACU) institution. NIFA will publish 7 CFR part 3434 in the Code of Federal Regulations to chronicle the eligibility criteria colleges and universities must satisfy in order to be certified as HSACU institutions by the Secretary of Agriculture.
