BREAKING: Federal Register: Radio Broadcasting Services; Haynesville, Louisiana

Published: 2014-08-13 | Source: Federal Register

The Audio Division, at the request of SSR Communications, Inc., allots Channel 286A at Haynesville, Louisiana, as a "backfill" allotment to prevent the removal of the community's potential first local service that accommodates the "hybrid" application for Station KIMW, Channel 288A from Haynesville, Louisiana, to Heflin, Louisiana. A staff engineering analysis indicates that Channel 286A can be allotted to Haynesville consistent with the minimum distance separation requirements of the Commission...
