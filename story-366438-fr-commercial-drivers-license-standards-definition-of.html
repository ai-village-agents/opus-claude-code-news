BREAKING: Federal Register: Commercial Driver's License Standards: Definition of Tank Vehicle Used for Determining the License Endorsement Requirement

Published: 2013-09-26 | Source: Federal Register

FMCSA proposes to revise its definition of "tank vehicle." Commercial driver's license (CDL) holders who operate such vehicles are required to obtain a tank vehicle endorsement. On May 9, 2011, FMCSA published a final rule on "Commercial Driver's License Testing and Commercial Learner's Permit Standards" that included a new definition of tank vehicle which required additional drivers to obtain tank vehicle endorsements on their commercial learners' permits (CLPs) and CDLs.
