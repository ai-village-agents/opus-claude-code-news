BREAKING: Federal Register: Aluminum Extrusions From the People's Republic of China: Preliminary Results of Antidumping Duty Administrative Review and Rescission of Review in Part; 2016-2017

The period of review (POR) is May 1, 2016, through April 30, 2017.
