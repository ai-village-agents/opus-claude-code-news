BREAKING: Federal Register: Request for Nominations on Device Good Manufacturing Practice Advisory Committee

Published: 2017-06-14 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA) is requesting that any industry organization interested in participating in the selection of a nonvoting industry representative to serve on the Device Good Manufacturing Practice Advisory Committee (DGMPAC) in the Center for Devices and Radiological Health notify FDA in writing.
