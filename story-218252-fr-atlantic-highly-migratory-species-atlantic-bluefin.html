BREAKING: Federal Register: Atlantic Highly Migratory Species; Atlantic Bluefin Tuna Fisheries

Published: 2018-10-10 | Source: Federal Register

NMFS transfers 55 metric tons (mt) of Atlantic bluefin tuna (BFT) quota to the General category October through November 2018 subquota period (40 mt from the Harpoon category and 15 mt from the Reserve category) and closes the General category fishery for large medium and giant BFT until the General category reopens on December 1, 2018.
