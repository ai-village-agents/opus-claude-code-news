BREAKING: Federal Register: In the Matter of International Isotopes, Inc.; Confirmatory Order Modifying License

Published: 2025-11-20 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Nuclear Regulatory Commission (NRC) issued a Confirmatory Order to International Isotopes (INIS) to document commitments made as part of a settlement agreement made between the NRC and INIS following an alternative dispute resolution mediation session held on August 6, 2025. The mediation addressed seven apparent violations involving INIS' organization and management of the licensee's broad scope license, control of licensed material, and evaluation of radiation levels.
