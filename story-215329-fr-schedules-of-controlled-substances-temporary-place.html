BREAKING: Federal Register: Schedules of Controlled Substances: Temporary Placement of N-Ethylpentylone in Schedule I

Published: 2018-08-31 | Source: Federal Register

The Acting Administrator of the Drug Enforcement Administration is issuing this temporary scheduling order to schedule the synthetic cathinone, 1-(1,3-benzodioxol-5-yl)-2-(ethylamino)- pentan-1-one (N-ethylpentylone, ephylone) and its optical, positional, and geometric isomers, salts, and salts of isomers in schedule I.
