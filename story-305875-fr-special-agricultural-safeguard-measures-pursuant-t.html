BREAKING: Federal Register: Special Agricultural Safeguard Measures Pursuant to the Uruguay Round Agreements Act

Published: 2015-10-05 | Source: Federal Register

After reviewing the volume of butter imports (including fresh or sour cream containing over 45 percent by weight of butterfat), the Administrator of the Foreign Agricultural Service has determined that the yearly special safeguard trigger level has been met and a special safeguard duty on certain imports of butter and fresh or sour cream will be imposed effective from the date of this notification through December 31, 2015.
