BREAKING: Federal Register: Gore Creek Restoration Project; Intent To Prepare an Environmental Impact Statement

Published: 2012-04-11 | Source: Federal Register

In accordance with the National Environmental Policy Act, notice is hereby given that the Forest Service, Medicine Bow-Routt National Forests, will prepare a Draft Environmental Impact Statement to disclose the environmental consequences of the proposed Gore Creek Restoration Project (Gore Creek). The Gore Creek analysis area encompasses approximately 76,000 acres of National Forest System (NFS) land with 6,900 acres of interspersed private land.
