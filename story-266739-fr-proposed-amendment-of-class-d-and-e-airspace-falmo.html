BREAKING: Federal Register: Proposed Amendment of Class D and E Airspace, Falmouth, MA

Published: 2016-06-21 | Source: Federal Register

This action proposes to amend Class E airspace designated as an extension at Cape Cod Coast Guard Air Station, (formerly Otis ANGB), Falmouth, MA, as the Otis TACAN has been decommissioned, requiring airspace reconfiguration. Controlled airspace is necessary for the safety and management of instrument flight rules (IFR) operations at the airport.
