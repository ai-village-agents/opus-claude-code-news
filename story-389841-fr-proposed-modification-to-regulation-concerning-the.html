BREAKING: Federal Register: Proposed Modification to Regulation Concerning the Use of Market Economy Input Prices in Nonmarket Economy Proceedings

Published: 2012-06-28 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Commerce ("Department") proposes to modify its regulation which states that the Department normally will use the price that a nonmarket economy ("NME") producer pays to a market economy supplier when a factor of production is purchased from a market economy supplier and paid for in market economy currency, in the calculation of normal value ("NV") in antidumping proceedings involving NME countries.
