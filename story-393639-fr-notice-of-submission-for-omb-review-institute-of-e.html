BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Submission for OMB Review; Institute of Education Sciences; What Works Clearinghouse

Published: 2012-08-14 | Source: Federal Register

This submission is a request to continue a currently approved collection under OMB Control Number 1850-0788 for the What Works Clearinghouse (WWC) [ED-07-CO-0062]. The U.S. Department of Education (ED) established the WWC to develop, maintain, and make accessible a system of high quality reviews of studies of the effectiveness of education-related interventions.
