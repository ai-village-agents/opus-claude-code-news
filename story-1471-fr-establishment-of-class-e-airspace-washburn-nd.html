BREAKING: Federal Register: Establishment of Class E Airspace; Washburn, ND

Published: 2026-01-23 | Source: Federal Register

This action establishes Class E airspace at Washburn, ND. This action is due to the development of new public instrument procedures at Washburn Municipal Airport, Washburn, ND, and to support instrument flight rule (IFR) operations.
