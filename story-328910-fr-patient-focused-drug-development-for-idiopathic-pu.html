BREAKING: Federal Register: Patient-Focused Drug Development for Idiopathic Pulmonary Fibrosis; Public Meeting; Request for Comments

Published: 2014-07-08 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA or Agency) is announcing a public meeting and an opportunity for public comment on Patient- Focused Drug Development for idiopathic pulmonary fibrosis. Patient- Focused Drug Development is part of FDA's performance commitments made as part of the fifth authorization of the Prescription Drug User Fee Act (PDUFA V).
