BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Fokker Services B.V. Airplanes

Published: 2013-08-12 | Source: Federal Register

We propose to adopt a new airworthiness directive (AD) for all Fokker Services B.V. Model F.28 Mark 0070 and 0100 airplanes. This proposed AD was prompted by a design review, which revealed that no controlled bonding provisions are present on a number of critical locations inside the fuel tank or connected to the fuel tank wall.
