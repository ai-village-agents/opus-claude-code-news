BREAKING: Federal Register: Proximity Detection Systems for Mobile Machines in Underground Mines

Published: 2015-09-02 | Source: Federal Register

The Mine Safety and Health Administration (MSHA) is proposing to require underground coal mine operators to equip coal hauling machines and scoops with proximity detection systems. Miners working near these machines face pinning, crushing, and striking hazards that result in accidents involving life threatening injuries and death.
