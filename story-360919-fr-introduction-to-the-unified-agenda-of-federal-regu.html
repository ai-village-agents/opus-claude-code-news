BREAKING: Federal Register: Introduction to the Unified Agenda of Federal Regulatory and Deregulatory Actions

Published: 2013-07-23 | Source: Federal Register

The Regulatory Flexibility Act requires that agencies publish semiannual regulatory agendas in the Federal Register describing regulatory actions they are developing that may have a significant economic impact on a substantial number of small entities (5 U.S.C. 602).
