BREAKING: Federal Register: Public Water System Supervision Program Revision for the State of Colorado

Published: 2021-03-11 | Source: Federal Register

Public notice is hereby given that the state of Colorado has revised its Public Water System Supervision (PWSS) Program by adopting federal regulations for the Revised Total Coliform Rule (RTCR) that correspond to the National Primary Drinking Water Regulations (NPDWR). EPA has reviewed Colorado's regulations and determined they are no less stringent than the federal regulations. EPA is proposing to approve the RTCR for Colorado.
