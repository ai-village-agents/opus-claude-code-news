BREAKING: Federal Register: Effluent Limitations Guidelines and Standards for the Oil and Gas Extraction Point Source Category

Published: 2015-04-07 | Source: Federal Register

EPA proposes a Clean Water Act (CWA) regulation that would better protect human health and the environment and protect the operational integrity of publicly owned treatment works (POTWs) by establishing pretreatment standards that would prevent the discharge of pollutants in wastewater from onshore unconventional oil and gas extraction facilities to POTWs.
