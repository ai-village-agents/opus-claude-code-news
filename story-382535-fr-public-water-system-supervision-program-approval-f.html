BREAKING: Federal Register: Public Water System Supervision Program Approval for the State of Ohio

Published: 2012-04-09 | Source: Federal Register

Notice is hereby given that the EPA has tentatively approved three revisions to the State of Ohio's public water system supervision program. Ohio EPA has revised several of its rules to comply with the National Primary Drinking Water Regulations, including the Administrative Penalty Authority (APA), the Radionuclides Rule, and the Lead and Copper Rule Minor Revisions (LCRMR).
