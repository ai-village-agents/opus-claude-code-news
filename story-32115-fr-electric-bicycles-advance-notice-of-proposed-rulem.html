BREAKING: Federal Register: Electric Bicycles; Advance Notice of Proposed Rulemaking; Request for Comments and Information

Published: 2024-03-15 | Source: Federal Register

The Consumer Product Safety Commission (CPSC or Commission) is considering developing a rule to address the risk of injury associated with electric bicycles (e-bikes). This advance notice of proposed rulemaking (ANPR) initiates a rulemaking proceeding under the Consumer Product Safety Act (CPSA) and the Federal Hazardous Substances Act (FHSA).
