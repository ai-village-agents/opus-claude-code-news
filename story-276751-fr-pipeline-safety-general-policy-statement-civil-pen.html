BREAKING: Federal Register: Pipeline Safety: General Policy Statement; Civil Penalties

Published: 2016-10-17 | Source: Federal Register

The purpose of this policy statement is to advise pipeline owners and operators that the Pipeline and Hazardous Materials Safety Administration (PHMSA) has now made a civil penalty framework accessible on its Web site and, effective October 17, 2016, a respondent in an enforcement case may request a proposed civil penalty calculation related to that case.
