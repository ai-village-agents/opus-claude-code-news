BREAKING: Federal Register: Economic Impact of Section 232 and 301 Tariffs on U.S. Industries

Published: 2022-07-19 | Source: Federal Register

Due to the large number of requests to appear at the Commission's public hearing in this investigation, the U.S. International Trade Commission (Commission) has added two additional days to the public hearing, July 20, 2022, and July 22, 2022. The public hearing originally was scheduled for one day, July 21, 2022. As rescheduled, it will be held on July 20-22, 2022.
