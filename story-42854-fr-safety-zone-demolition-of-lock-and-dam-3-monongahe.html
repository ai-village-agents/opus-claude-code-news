BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone; Demolition of Lock and Dam 3, Monongahela River Mile Marker 23.5 to 24.5, Elizabeth, PA

Published: 2024-07-29 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is establishing a temporary safety zone on the waters of the Monongahela River from mile marker 23.5 to mile marker 24.5 in Elizabeth, PA. This rule is substantially similar to a temporary safety zone published on June 27, 2024. We must establish this temporary safety zone because of the continuation of lock and dam demolition.
