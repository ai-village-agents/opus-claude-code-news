BREAKING: Federal Register: Energy Conservation Program: Notice of Petition for Waiver of LG Electronics U.S.A., Inc. From the Department of Energy Central Air Conditioners and Heat Pumps Test Procedure and Notice of Grant of Interim Waiver

Published: 2020-07-06 | Source: Federal Register

This notice announces receipt of and publishes a petition for waiver and interim waiver from LG Electronics U.S.A., Inc. ("LGE"), which seeks a waiver from the U.S. Department of Energy ("DOE") test procedure used for determining the efficiency of specified central air conditioner ("CAC") and heat pump ("HP") basic models.
