BREAKING: Federal Register: Availability of Seat for Olympic Coast National Marine Sanctuary Advisory Council

Published: 2012-03-22 | Source: Federal Register

The ONMS is seeking applications for the following vacant seat on the Olympic Coast National Marine Sanctuary Advisory Council: Coastal Marine Resources Committees. Applicants must be members of either the Grays Harbor or North Pacific Coast marine resources committees.
