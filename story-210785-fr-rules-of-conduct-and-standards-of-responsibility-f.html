BREAKING: Federal Register: Rules of Conduct and Standards of Responsibility for Appointed Representatives

Published: 2018-07-02 | Source: Federal Register

We are revising our rules of conduct and standards of responsibility for representatives. We are also updating and clarifying the procedures we use when we bring charges against a representative for violating these rules and standards. These changes are necessary to better protect the integrity of our administrative process and to further clarify representatives' existing responsibilities in their conduct with us.
