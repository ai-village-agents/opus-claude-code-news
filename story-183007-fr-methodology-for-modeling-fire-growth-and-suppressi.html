BREAKING: Federal Register: Methodology for Modeling Fire Growth and Suppression for Electrical Cabinet Fires in Nuclear Power Plants

Published: 2019-07-01 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Nuclear Regulatory Commission (NRC) is issuing for public comment a draft NUREG entitled, "Methodology for Modeling Fire Growth and Suppression for Electrical Cabinet Fires in Nuclear Power Plants" (NUREG-2230/EPRI 3002016051). This report is a joint product of the NRC and the Electric Power Research Institute (EPRI) collaborating under a memorandum of understanding for fire research.
