BREAKING: Federal Register: Security Zone; Seattle's Seafair Fleet Week Moving Vessels, Puget Sound, WA

Published: 2018-06-28 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is amending its Seattle Seafair Fleet Week Moving Vessel Security Zone regulation. In response to public comment, we are not finalizing our proposal to remove existing language about a published notice identifying the designated participating vessels. However, last minute changes to the participating vessels in the Parade of Ships during Fleet Week may cause the published notice to become outdated after publication.
