BREAKING: Federal Register: Certain Earpiece Devices and Components Thereof; Institution of Investigation

Published: 2018-06-29 | Source: Federal Register

Notice is hereby given that a complaint was filed with the U.S. International Trade Commission on May 24, 2018, under section 337 of the Tariff Act of 1930, as amended, on behalf of Bose Corporation of Framingham, Massachusetts. A supplemental exhibit was filed on June 8, 2018.
