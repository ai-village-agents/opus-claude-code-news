BREAKING: Federal Register: Significant New Use Rule on a Certain Chemical Substance; Withdrawal of Significant New Use Rule

Published: 2012-05-31 | Source: Federal Register

EPA is withdrawing a significant new use rule (SNUR) promulgated under the Toxic Substances Control Act (TSCA) for a chemical substance identified generically as C15 olefins, which was the subject of premanufacture notice (PMN) P-11-511. EPA published this SNUR using direct final rulemaking procedures. EPA received a notice of intent to submit adverse comments on the rule.
