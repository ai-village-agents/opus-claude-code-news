BREAKING: Federal Register: Endangered and Threatened Species; Initiation of 5-Year Review for North Atlantic Right Whale

Published: 2016-07-29 | Source: Federal Register

NMFS announces a 5-year review of the North Atlantic right whale (Eubalaena glacialis) under the Endangered Species Act of 1973, as amended (ESA). The purpose of these reviews is to ensure that the listing classification of a species is accurate.
