BREAKING: Federal Register: Not Applying the Mark of Inspection Pending Certain Test Results

Published: 2012-12-10 | Source: Federal Register

The Food Safety and Inspection Service (FSIS) is announcing that it is changing its procedures and will withhold its determination as to whether meat and poultry products are not adulterated, and thus eligible to enter commerce, until all test results that bear on the determination have been received.
