BREAKING: Federal Register: Agency Information Collection Activities; Proposed Collection; Comment Request

Published: 2016-03-31 | Source: Federal Register

The FTC intends to conduct a survey of consumers to advance its understanding of the prevalence of consumer fraud and to allow the FTC to better serve people who experience fraud. The survey will be a follow-up to three previous surveys, the most recent of which was conducted between November 2011 and February 2012.
