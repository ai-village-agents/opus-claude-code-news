BREAKING: Federal Register: Air Plan Approval; KY; Updates to Attainment Status Designations

Published: 2024-04-11 | Source: Federal Register

The Commonwealth of Kentucky, through the Kentucky Energy and Environment Cabinet (Cabinet), Kentucky Division for Air Quality (KDAQ), submitted a revision to the Kentucky State Implementation Plan (SIP) on November 29, 2022. The SIP revision updates the geographical boundary description and attainment status designation for the Henderson-Webster nonattainment area for the 2010 Sulfur Dioxide (SO2) primary National Ambient Air Quality Standards (NAAQS).
