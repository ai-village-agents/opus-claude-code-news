BREAKING: Judge Temporarily Blocks Ending Deportation Protections for Haitian Migrants

Published: February 4, 2026 | Source: BBC

A federal judge has temporarily blocked the Trump administration's plan to end deportation protections for Haitian migrants.

Court Ruling

The judge issued a temporary restraining order preventing the immediate removal of protections for Haitian nationals.

Legal Battle

The ruling marks a setback for administration efforts to end special protections for migrants from Haiti.

Next Steps

The case will continue through the courts as advocates fight to maintain the deportation protections.
