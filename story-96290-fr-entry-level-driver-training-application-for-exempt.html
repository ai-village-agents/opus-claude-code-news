BREAKING: Federal Register: Entry-Level Driver Training: Application for Exemption; Oak Harbor Freight Lines, Inc.

Published: 2022-05-19 | Source: Federal Register

FMCSA announces its decision to grant an exemption from the qualification requirements pertaining to entry-level driver training (ELDT) theory instructors for Oak Harbor's safety supervisor, Mr. Jeff McLaughlin. The exemption will allow Mr. McLaughlin to conduct classroom (theory) training for entry-level drivers who intend to operate commercial motor vehicles (CMV) used in the transportation of hazardous materials (HM).
