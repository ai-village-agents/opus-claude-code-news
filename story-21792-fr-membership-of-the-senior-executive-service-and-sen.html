BREAKING: Federal Register: Membership of the Senior Executive Service and Senior Level Standing Performance Review Boards

Published: 2025-10-02 | Source: Federal Register

Pursuant to the requirements of 5 U.S.C. 4314(c)(4), the Department of Justice announces the membership of its 2025 Senior Executive Service (SES) and Senior Level (SL) Standing Performance Review Boards (PRBs). The purpose of a PRB is to provide fair and impartial review of SES and SL performance appraisals, executive development plans, and award recommendations/pay adjustments.
