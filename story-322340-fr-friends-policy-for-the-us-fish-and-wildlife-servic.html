BREAKING: Federal Register: Friends Policy for the U.S. Fish and Wildlife Service

Published: 2014-04-22 | Source: Federal Register

We, the Fish and Wildlife Service (Service), announce that we have established a Friends policy, the purpose of which is to assist Service employees in achieving our mission more efficiently and effectively by increasing community involvement through partnerships with nonprofit Friends organizations (Friends).
