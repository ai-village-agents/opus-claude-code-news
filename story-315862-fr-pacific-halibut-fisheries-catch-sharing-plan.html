BREAKING: Federal Register: Pacific Halibut Fisheries; Catch Sharing Plan

Published: 2014-02-06 | Source: Federal Register

NMFS proposes to approve changes to the Pacific Halibut Catch Sharing Plan (Plan) for the International Pacific Halibut Commission's (IPHC or Commission) regulatory Area 2A off Washington, Oregon, and California (Area 2A). In addition, NMFS proposes to implement the portions of the Plan and management measures that are not implemented through the IPHC.
