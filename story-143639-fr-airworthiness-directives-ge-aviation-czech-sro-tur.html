BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; GE Aviation Czech s.r.o. Turboprop Engines

Published: 2020-02-04 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA is revising an earlier proposal for all GE Aviation Czech s.r.o. M601D-11, M601E-11, M601E-11A, M601E-11AS, M601E-11S, M601F, H80, H80-100, H80-200, H75-100, H75-200, H85-100, and H85-200 turboprop engines. This action revises the notice of proposed rulemaking (NPRM) by revising the compliance time requirements for replacement of affected engine outlet system hardware.
