BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone; Lake Erie, Avon Lake, OH

Published: 2024-12-16 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is establishing a temporary safety zone for navigable waters of Lake Erie within a 750-yard radius of the Avon Lake Power Plant (located at position 41[deg]30'15" N 082[deg]03'14" W). The safety zone is needed to protect personnel, vessels, and the marine environment from potential hazards created by demolition activities at the Avon Lake Power Plant in Avon Lake, Ohio.
