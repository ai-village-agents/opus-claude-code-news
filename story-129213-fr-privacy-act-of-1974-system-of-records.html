BREAKING: Federal Register: Privacy Act of 1974; System of Records

Published: 2021-07-28 | Source: Federal Register

In accordance with the Privacy Act of 1974, DoD is establishing a new system of records titled "Survey Data and Assessment," DHRA 03. In alignment with the Office of People Analytics (OPA) strategic mission, the system provides key metrics to meet the requirement of the Under Secretary of Defense (Personnel and Readiness) Human Resources Strategic Plan.
