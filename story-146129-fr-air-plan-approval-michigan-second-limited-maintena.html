BREAKING: Federal Register: Air Plan Approval; Michigan; Second Limited Maintenance Plans for 1997 Ozone NAAQS

Published: 2020-03-06 | Source: Federal Register

Pursuant to the Clean Air Act (CAA), the Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is approving a state implementation plan (SIP) revision submitted by the State of Michigan.
