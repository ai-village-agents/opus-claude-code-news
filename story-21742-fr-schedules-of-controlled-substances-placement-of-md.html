BREAKING: Federal Register: Schedules of Controlled Substances: Placement of MDMB-4en-PINACA in Schedule I

Published: 2025-10-02 | Source: Federal Register

The Drug Enforcement Administration proposes placing methyl 3,3-dimethyl-2-(1-(pent-4-en-1-yl)-1H-indazole-3-carboxamido)butanoate (other name: MDMB-4en-PINACA), including its salts, isomers, and salts of isomers whenever the existence of such salts, isomers, and salts of isomers is possible, in schedule I of the Controlled Substances Act.
