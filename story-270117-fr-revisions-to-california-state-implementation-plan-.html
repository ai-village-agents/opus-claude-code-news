BREAKING: Federal Register: Revisions to California State Implementation Plan; Bay Area Air Quality Management District; Stationary Source Permits

Published: 2016-08-01 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is finalizing a limited approval and limited disapproval of revisions to Regulation 2, Rules 1 and 2 for the Bay Area Air Quality Management District (BAAQMD or District) portion of the California State Implementation Plan (SIP) submitted on April 22, 2013.
