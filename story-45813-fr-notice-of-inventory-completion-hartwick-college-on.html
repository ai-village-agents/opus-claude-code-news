BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Inventory Completion: Hartwick College, Oneonta, NY

Published: 2024-09-03 | Source: Federal Register

In accordance with the Native American Graves Protection and Repatriation Act (NAGPRA), Hartwick College has completed an inventory of human remains and associated funerary objects and has determined that there is a cultural affiliation between the human remains and associated funerary objects and Indian Tribes or Native Hawaiian organizations in this notice. The remains were recovered from Oneonta, NY, and Hyndesville, NY, respectively.
