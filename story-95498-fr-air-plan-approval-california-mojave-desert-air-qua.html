BREAKING: Federal Register: Air Plan Approval; California; Mojave Desert Air Quality Management District, Placer County Air Pollution Control District

Published: 2022-05-09 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is taking final action to approve revisions to the Mojave Desert Air Quality Management District (MDAQMD) and the Placer County Air Pollution Control District (PCAPCD) portions of the California State Implementation Plan (SIP). These revisions concern emissions of volatile organic compounds (VOCs) from metal coating operations and general regulatory definitions.
