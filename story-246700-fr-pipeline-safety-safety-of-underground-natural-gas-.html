BREAKING: Federal Register: Pipeline Safety: Safety of Underground Natural Gas Storage Facilities

Published: 2017-10-19 | Source: Federal Register

The Pipeline and Hazardous Materials Safety Administration (PHMSA) is announcing an additional opportunity for the public to comment on an interim final rule (IFR) titled: "Pipeline Safety: Underground Natural Gas Storage Facilities." PHMSA is reopening the comment period in response to a petition for reconsideration filed jointly by the American Gas Association, American Petroleum Institute, and the American Public Gas Association.
