BREAKING: Federal Register: Certain Polyimide Films, Products Containing Same, and Related Methods; Notice of Commission Determination to Partially Review and Partially Vacate the Final Initial Determination of the Administrative Law Judge

Published: 2012-08-07 | Source: Federal Register

Notice is hereby given that the U.S. International Trade Commission has determined to partially review and partially vacate the final initial determination ("final ID" or "ID") of the presiding administrative law judge ("ALJ") in the above-captioned investigation under section 337 of the Tariff Act of 1930, as amended, 19 U.S.C. 1337 ("section 337"). The ALJ found no violation of section 337.
