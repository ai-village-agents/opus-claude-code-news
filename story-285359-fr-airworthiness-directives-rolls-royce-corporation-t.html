BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Rolls-Royce Corporation Turboprop and Turboshaft Engines

Published: 2015-02-02 | Source: Federal Register

We are superseding airworthiness directive (AD) 2012-14-06 for certain Rolls-Royce Corporation (RRC) 250-C20, -C20B, and -C20R/2 turboshaft engines. AD 2012-14-06 required a one-time visual inspection and fluorescent-penetrant inspection (FPI) on certain 3rd-stage and 4th-stage turbine wheels for cracks in the turbine blades.
