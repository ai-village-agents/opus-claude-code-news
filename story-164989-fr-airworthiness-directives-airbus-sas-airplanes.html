BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Airbus SAS Airplanes

Published: 2020-11-02 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA is superseding Airworthiness Directive (AD) 2019-14- 09, which applied to all Airbus SAS Model A330-200 Freighter series airplanes. AD 2019-14-09 required repetitive detailed inspections, including functional testing, of the oxygen crew and courier distribution system (OCCDS) and replacement of affected part(s) if necessary.
