BREAKING: Federal Register: Provisions Pertaining to Certain Transactions by Foreign Persons Involving Real Estate in the United States

Published: 2019-09-24 | Source: Federal Register

This proposed rule would establish new regulations to implement the provisions relating to real estate transactions in section 721 of the Defense Production Act of 1950, as amended by the Foreign Investment Risk Review Modernization Act of 2018.
