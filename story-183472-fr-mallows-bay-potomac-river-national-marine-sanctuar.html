BREAKING: Federal Register: Mallows Bay-Potomac River National Marine Sanctuary Designation

Published: 2019-07-08 | Source: Federal Register

The National Oceanic and Atmospheric Administration (NOAA) issues final regulations to implement the designation of the Mallows Bay-Potomac River National Marine Sanctuary (MPNMS or sanctuary). The area is 18 square miles of waters and submerged lands encompassing and surrounding the Mallows Bay area of the tidal Potomac River.
