BREAKING: Federal Register: Special Local Regulation; San Diego Bay, San Diego, CA

Published: 2024-10-07 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is establishing a special local regulation for the San Diego Sharkfest Swim marine event that will be held on the navigable waters of San Diego Bay, San Diego, CA. This action is necessary to provide for the safety of life on these navigable waters of San Diego Bay during a swim event on October 20, 2024.
