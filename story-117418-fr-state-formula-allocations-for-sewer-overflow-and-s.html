BREAKING: Federal Register: State Formula Allocations for Sewer Overflow and Stormwater Reuse Grants

Published: 2021-02-24 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is announcing the allocation formula for the Sewer Overflow and Stormwater Reuse Municipal Grants Program as required by the Clean Water Act (CWA). EPA is required to establish a formula to allocate proportional shares of the amount appropriated to state entities to fund actions that will help manage combined sewer overflows, sanitary sewer overflows, and stormwater.
