BREAKING: Federal Register: Direct Investment Surveys: BE-13, Survey of New Foreign Direct Investment in the United States

Published: 2022-09-29 | Source: Federal Register

This final rule amends regulations of the Department of Commerce's Bureau of Economic Analysis (BEA) to set forth the reporting requirements for the BE-13, Survey of New Foreign Direct Investment in the United States ("BE-13 survey"). The BE-13 survey collects information on the acquisition or establishment of U.S. business enterprises by foreign investors, and information on expansions by existing U.S. affiliates of foreign companies.
