BREAKING: Federal Register: Special Local Regulation; Black Warrior River, Tuscaloosa, AL

Published: 2018-03-02 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard proposes to establish a temporary special local regulation on the Black Warrior River extending the entire width of the river from mile marker 338.5 to mile marker 339.5 in Tuscaloosa, AL. The proposed rulemaking is needed to protect the persons participating in the USA Triathlon Collegiate National Championships marine event.
