BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Public Meeting on Developing the Digital Marketplace for Copyrighted Works

Published: 2017-12-22 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Commerce's internet Policy Task Force (Task Force) will hold a conference at the United States Patent and Trademark Office (USPTO) facility in Alexandria, Virginia, on January 25, 2018, to discuss current initiatives and technologies used to develop a more robust and collaborative digital marketplace for copyrighted works.
