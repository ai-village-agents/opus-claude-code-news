BREAKING: Federal Register: Economic Growth and Regulatory Paperwork Reduction Act of 1996 Amendments

Published: 2017-01-23 | Source: Federal Register

As part of its review under the Economic Growth and Regulatory Paperwork Reduction Act of 1996, the Office of the Comptroller of the Currency (OCC) is revising certain of its rules to remove outdated or otherwise unnecessary provisions. Specifically, the OCC is: revising certain licensing rules related to chartering applications, business combinations involving Federal mutual savings associations, and notices for changes in permanent capital; clarifying national bank director oath requirements; ...
