BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Bombardier, Inc. Airplanes

Published: 2014-07-23 | Source: Federal Register

We are revising an earlier proposed airworthiness directive (AD) for certain Bombardier, Inc. Model BD-700-1A11 airplanes. The NPRM proposed to require replacement of certain oxygen mask lanyards. The NPRM was prompted by a report that certain lanyards for the passenger oxygen masks are longer than the specified length, possibly leading to inactive oxygen masks in an emergency.
