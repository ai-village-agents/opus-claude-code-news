BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Airbus Airplanes

Published: 2015-01-30 | Source: Federal Register

We are adopting a new airworthiness directive (AD) for all Airbus Model A330-201, -202, -203, -301, -302, and -303 airplanes. This AD requires a one-time ultrasonic inspection for fractures of all aft mount-pylon bolts of each engine. This AD was prompted by a report of one bolt on the aft engine mount upper beam found totally broken.
