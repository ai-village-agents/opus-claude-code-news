BREAKING: Federal Register: Large Residential Washers; Institution and Scheduling of Safeguard Investigation and Determination That the Investigation Is Extraordinarily Complicated, Amendment

Published: 2017-07-21 | Source: Federal Register

The Commission published a notice in the Federal Register of June 13, 2017, concerning the institution and scheduling of investigation No. TA-201-76 pursuant to section 202 of the Trade Act of 1974 ("the Act") to determine whether large residential washers are being imported into the United States in such increased quantities as to be a substantial cause of serious injury, or the threat thereof, to the domestic industry producing an article like or directly competitive with the imported articles...
