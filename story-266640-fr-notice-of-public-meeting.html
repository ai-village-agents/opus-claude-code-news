BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Public Meeting

Published: 2016-06-21 | Source: Federal Register

This Committee will meet on Wednesday July 20, 2016, from 1:00 p.m. to 5:00 p.m. Eastern Time at the American Institute of Architects, Board Room, 1735 New York Avenue NW., Washington, DC 20006.
