BREAKING: Federal Register: Approval of International Marine Consultants, as a Commercial Gauger

Published: 2012-03-02 | Source: Federal Register

Notice is hereby given that, pursuant to 19 CFR 151.13, International Marine Consultants, 429 Padre Rufo St. Floral Park, Hato Rey, PR 00917, has been approved to gauge petroleum, petroleum products, organic chemicals and vegetable oils for customs purposes, in accordance with the provisions of 19 CFR 151.13.
