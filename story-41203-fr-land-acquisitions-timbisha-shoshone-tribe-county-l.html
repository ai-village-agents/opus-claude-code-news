BREAKING: Federal Register: Land Acquisitions; Timbisha Shoshone Tribe, County Land Parcel, Kern County, California

Published: 2024-07-08 | Source: Federal Register

The Assistant Secretary--Indian Affairs made a final agency determination to acquire in trust 20.03 acres, more or less, of land known as the County Land Parcel in Kern County, California, for the Timbisha Shoshone Tribe for gaming and other purposes.
