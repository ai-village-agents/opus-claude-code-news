BREAKING: Federal Register: Pipeline Safety: Public Workshop on Hazardous Liquid Integrity Verification Process

Published: 2015-08-14 | Source: Federal Register

This notice is announcing a public workshop to be held on the concept of "Hazard Liquid Integrity Verification Process (HL IVP)." The HL IVP is to confirm the Maximum Operating Pressure when pipeline records are not traceable, verifiable, or complete.
