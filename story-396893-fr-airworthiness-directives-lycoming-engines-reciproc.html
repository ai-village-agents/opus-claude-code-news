BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Lycoming Engines Reciprocating Engines

Published: 2012-09-19 | Source: Federal Register

We are superseding an existing airworthiness directive (AD) for certain Lycoming Engines (L)O-360, (L)IO-360, AEIO-360, O-540, IO- 540, AEIO-540, (L)TIO-540, IO-580, and IO-720 series reciprocating engines. That AD currently requires replacing certain crankshafts in the affected engines.
