BREAKING: Federal Register: Parts and Accessories Necessary for Safe Operation; Exemption Application From JM Bozeman Enterprises, Inc.

Published: 2023-02-01 | Source: Federal Register

The Federal Motor Carrier Safety Administration (FMCSA) requests public comment on JM Bozeman Enterprises, Inc.'s (Bozeman) application for an exemption from the requirement that lighting devices be steady burning. The exemption would allow the company to operate commercial motor vehicles (CMVs) equipped with a module manufactured by Intellistop, Inc. (Intellistop) which pulses the rear clearance, identification, and brake lamps from low-level lighting intensity to high-level lighting intensity ...
