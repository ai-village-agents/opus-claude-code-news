BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Solicitation of Applications for the Rural Energy for America Program for Fiscal Years 2023 and 2024

Published: 2023-03-31 | Source: Federal Register

The Rural Business-Cooperative Service (the Agency) is issuing a second Notice of Solicitation of Applications (Notice) under the Rural Energy for America Program (REAP) as referenced in the notice that was published in the Federal Register on December 16, 2022. This second notice announces the availability of $1.055 billion in Inflation Reduction Act funds across six quarterly cycles to be obligated by September 30, 2024.
