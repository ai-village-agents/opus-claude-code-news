BREAKING: Federal Register: Closed Captioning Display Settings Proposal

Published: 2023-02-01 | Source: Federal Register

In this document, the Media Bureau of the Federal Communications Commission (Commission) seeks comment on a proposal in the record of this proceeding that when the Commission is determining whether specific closed captioning display settings are readily accessible, it should consider the following factors: proximity, discoverability, previewability, and consistency and persistence.
