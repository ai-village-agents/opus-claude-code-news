BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Airbus Helicopters

Published: 2021-09-07 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA is revising a supplemental notice of proposed rulemaking SNPRM that applied to certain Airbus Helicopters Model EC225LP helicopters. This action revises the SNPRM by proposing to require the installation of an improved part, which would also provide a terminating action for the proposed requirements.
