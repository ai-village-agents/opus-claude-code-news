BREAKING: Federal Register: Filing Dates for the Texas Special Election in the 6th Congressional District Special Election

Published: 2021-03-04 | Source: Federal Register

Texas has scheduled a Special General Election on May 1, 2021, to fill its U.S. House of Representatives seat in the 6th Congressional District of the late Representative Ron Wright. There are two possible elections, but only one may be necessary. Under Texas law, all qualified candidates, regardless of party affiliation, will appear on the ballot.
