BREAKING: Federal Register: President's Advisory Council on African Diaspora Engagement

Published: 2023-10-13 | Source: Federal Register

Pursuant to the Federal Advisory Committee Act, 5 U.S.C. 1001 et seq., and 41 CFR 102-3.65, the Department of State hereby provides notice of the inaugural plenary session meeting and swearing-in ceremony of the President's Advisory Council on African Diaspora Engagement ("the Advisory Council").
