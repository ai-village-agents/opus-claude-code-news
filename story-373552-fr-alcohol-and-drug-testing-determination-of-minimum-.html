BREAKING: Federal Register: Alcohol and Drug Testing: Determination of Minimum Random Testing Rates for 2014

Published: 2013-12-26 | Source: Federal Register

According to data from FRA's Management Information System, the rail industry's random drug testing positive rate has remained below 1.0 percent for the last two years. FRA's Administrator has therefore determined that the minimum annual random drug testing rate for the period January 1, 2014, through December 31, 2014, will remain at 25 percent of covered railroad employees.
