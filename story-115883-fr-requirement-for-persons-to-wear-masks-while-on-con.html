BREAKING: Federal Register: Requirement for Persons To Wear Masks While on Conveyances and at Transportation Hubs

Published: 2021-02-03 | Source: Federal Register

The Centers for Disease Control and Prevention (CDC), a component of the U.S. Department of Health and Human Services (HHS), announces an Agency Order requiring persons to wear masks over the mouth and nose when traveling on any conveyance (e.g., airplanes, trains, subways, buses, taxis, ride-shares, ferries, ships, trolleys, and cable cars) into or within the United States.
