BREAKING: Federal Register: Forged Steel Fittings From India: Amended Preliminary Affirmative Countervailing Duty Determination

Published: 2020-06-18 | Source: Federal Register

On March 30, 2020, the Department of Commerce (Commerce) published in the Federal Register the preliminary determination of the countervailing duty (CVD) investigation on forged steel fittings from India. Commerce is amending the scope of the preliminary determination.
