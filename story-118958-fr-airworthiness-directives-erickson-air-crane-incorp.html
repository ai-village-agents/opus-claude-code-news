BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Erickson Air-Crane Incorporated Model S-64E and S-64F Helicopters

Published: 2021-03-15 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA is withdrawing a notice of proposed rulemaking (NPRM) that proposed to adopt a new airworthiness directive (AD) that would have applied to Erickson Air-Crane Incorporated (now Erickson Incorporated) (Erickson) Model S-64E and S-64F helicopters. The NPRM was prompted by several reports of cracking in the strap and pocket areas of the tail rotor blade.
