BREAKING: Federal Register: Amended Columbia River Basin Fish and Wildlife Program

Published: 2015-03-19 | Source: Federal Register

Pursuant to Section 4(h) of the Northwest Power Act, the Council has amended its Columbia River Basin Fish and Wildlife Program (program). The final amended program may be found on the Council's Web site at http://www.nwcouncil.org/fw/2014F&WProgram/.
