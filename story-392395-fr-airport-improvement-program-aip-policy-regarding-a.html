BREAKING: Federal Register: Airport Improvement Program (AIP): Policy Regarding Access to Airports From Residential Property

Published: 2012-07-30 | Source: Federal Register

This action proposes a policy, based on Federal law, concerning through-the-fence access to a federally obligated airport from an adjacent or nearby property, when that property is used as a residence. This proposed policy limits application of the FAA's previously published interim policy (76 FR 15028; March 18, 2011) to commercial service airports that certified existing residential through-the-fence access agreements.
