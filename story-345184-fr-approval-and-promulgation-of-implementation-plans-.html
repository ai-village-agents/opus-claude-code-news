BREAKING: Federal Register: Approval and Promulgation of Implementation Plans and Designation of Areas for Air Quality Planning Purposes; Alabama; Redesignation of the Birmingham 2006 24-Hour Fine Particulate Matter Nonattainment Area to Attainment

Published: 2013-01-25 | Source: Federal Register

EPA is taking final action to approve a request submitted on June 17, 2010, from the State of Alabama, through the Alabama Department of Environmental Management (ADEM), Air Division, to redesignate the Birmingham fine particulate matter (PM2.5) nonattainment area (hereafter referred to as the "Birmingham Area" or "Area") to attainment for the 2006 24-hour PM2.5 national ambient air quality standards (NAAQS).
