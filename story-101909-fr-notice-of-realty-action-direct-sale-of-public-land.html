BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Realty Action: Direct Sale of Public Lands in Shasta County, CA

Published: 2022-08-02 | Source: Federal Register

The Bureau of Land Management (BLM) is proposing a noncompetitive (direct) sale of 4.12 acres of public lands in Shasta County, California, to John Friesen. There is no legal public access to these parcels, which are surrounded by private land on three sides. The sale would be subject to applicable provisions of the Federal Land Policy and Management Act of 1976, as amended (FLPMA), and BLM land- sale regulations.
