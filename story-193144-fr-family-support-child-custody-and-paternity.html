BREAKING: Federal Register: Family Support, Child Custody, and Paternity

Published: 2019-11-12 | Source: Federal Register

This final rule removes the Army's regulation governing support and nonsupport of family members; child custody involving a soldier; paternity complaints against soldiers; and adoption proceedings involving children of soldiers. This part does not impose obligations on members of the public that are not already imposed by statute. This part is obsolete and unnecessary. Therefore, this part can be removed from the CFR.
