BREAKING: Federal Register: Industry Day-Expanding Clean Energy Generation on DOE Lands

Published: 2023-06-30 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Department of Energy (DOE) is launching an initiative to increase clean energy production by making DOE land available for the potential development of new clean electricity generation through leases. To support this effort, DOE is hosting an Industry Day. At the Industry Day, Secretary Granholm will present DOE's vision for utilizing DOE land to achieve clean electricity goals and senior leaders will share information on sites potentially available for development.
