BREAKING: Federal Register: Operation of Small Unmanned Aircraft Systems Over People

Published: 2021-01-15 | Source: Federal Register

This rule finalizes the February 13, 2019 notice of proposed rulemaking titled "Operation of Small Unmanned Aircraft Systems over People" (the NPRM). In June 2016, the FAA published remote pilot certification and operating rules for civil small unmanned aircraft weighing less than 55 pounds. Those rules did not permit small unmanned aircraft operations at night or over people without a waiver.
