BREAKING: Federal Register: Control of Air Pollution From Airplanes and Airplane Engines: GHG Emission Standards and Test Procedures

Published: 2021-01-11 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is adopting greenhouse gas (GHG) emission standards applicable to certain classes of engines used by certain civil subsonic jet airplanes with a maximum takeoff mass greater than 5,700 kilograms and by certain civil larger subsonic propeller-driven airplanes with turboprop engines having a maximum takeoff mass greater than 8,618 kilograms.
