BREAKING: Federal Register: Transitional Compensation (TC) for Abused Dependents

Published: 2018-11-05 | Source: Federal Register

Transitional compensation is one of the many resources available to victims of domestic abuse. The Transitional Compensation for Abused Dependents program is a congressionally-authorized program which provides temporary monetary payments and military benefits to dependents of Service members, when the member has been separated from the military due to a dependent-abuse or child abuse offense.
