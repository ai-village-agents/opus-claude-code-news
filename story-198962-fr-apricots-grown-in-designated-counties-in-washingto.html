BREAKING: Federal Register: Apricots Grown in Designated Counties in Washington; Decreased Assessment Rate

Published: 2018-01-31 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Agriculture (USDA) is adopting, as a final rule an interim rule that implemented a recommendation from the Washington Apricot Marketing Committee (Committee) to decrease the assessment rate established for the 2017-2018 and subsequent fiscal periods. The interim rule was necessary to allow the Committee to reduce its financial reserve while still providing adequate funding to meet program expenses.
