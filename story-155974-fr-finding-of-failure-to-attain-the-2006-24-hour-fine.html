BREAKING: Federal Register: Finding of Failure To Attain the 2006 24-Hour Fine Particulate Matter Standards; California; Los Angeles-South Coast Air Basin

Published: 2020-07-10 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is proposing to determine that the Los Angeles-South Coast Air Basin nonattainment area failed to attain the 2006 24-hour fine particulate matter ("PM2.5") national ambient air quality standards by the December 31, 2019 "Serious" area attainment date.
