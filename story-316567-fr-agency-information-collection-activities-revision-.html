BREAKING: Federal Register: Agency Information Collection Activities; Revision of a Currently Approved Collection: Practices of Household Goods Brokers

Published: 2014-02-14 | Source: Federal Register

In accordance with the Paperwork Reduction Act of 1995, FMCSA announces its plan to submit the Information Collection Request (ICR) described below to the Office of Management and Budget (OMB) for its review and approval and invites public comment.
