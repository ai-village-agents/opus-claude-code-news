BREAKING: Federal Register: Irish Potatoes Grown in Washington; Decreased Assessment Rate

Published: 2013-08-08 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Agriculture is adopting, as a final rule, without change, an interim rule that decreased the assessment rate established for the State of Washington Potato Committee (Committee) for the 2013-2014 fiscal year and all subsequent fiscal periods from $0.003 to $0.0025 per hundredweight of potatoes handled.
