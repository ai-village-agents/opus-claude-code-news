BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone; AASCIF Fireworks Display, Lake Erie, Cleveland, OH

Published: 2019-06-12 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is proposing to establish a temporary safety zone for certain waters of Lake Erie during the AASCIF Fireworks display. This action is necessary to provide for the safety of life on these navigable waters near the Great Lakes Science Center, Cleveland, OH, during a fireworks display on July 21, 2019.
