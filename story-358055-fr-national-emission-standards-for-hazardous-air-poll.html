BREAKING: Federal Register: National Emission Standards for Hazardous Air Pollutants From Petroleum Refineries

Published: 2013-06-20 | Source: Federal Register

This action amends the national emission standards for hazardous air pollutants for heat exchange systems at petroleum refineries. The amendments address issues raised in a petition for reconsideration of the EPA's final rule setting maximum achievable control technology rules for these systems and also provides additional clarity and regulatory flexibility with regard to that rule.
