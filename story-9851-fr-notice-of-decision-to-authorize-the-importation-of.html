BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Decision To Authorize the Importation of Fresh Rhizomes of Wasabi From Indonesia Into the United States

Published: 2025-04-23 | Source: Federal Register

We are advising the public of our decision to authorize the importation of fresh rhizomes of wasabi, Eutrema japonicum (Miq.) Koidz. (Brassicaceae), into the United States for consumption from Indonesia.
