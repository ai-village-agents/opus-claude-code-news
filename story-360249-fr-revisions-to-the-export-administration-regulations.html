BREAKING: Federal Register: Revisions to the Export Administration Regulations Based on the 2012 Missile Technology Control Regime Plenary Agreements

Published: 2013-07-16 | Source: Federal Register

The Bureau of Industry and Security (BIS) is amending the Export Administration Regulations (EAR) to reflect changes to the Missile Technology Control Regime (MTCR) Annex that were agreed to by MTCR member countries at the October 2012 Plenary in Berlin, Germany, and at the MTCR Reinforced Point of Contact (RPOC) meeting in Paris, France, in December 2011.
