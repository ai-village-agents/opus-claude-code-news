BREAKING: Federal Register: Special Local Regulation; Poquoson Seafood Festival Workboat Races; Back River, Poquoson, VA

Published: 2022-07-12 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard will enforce a special local regulation for the Poquoson Seafood Festival Workboat Races on the Back River, VA, on September 18, 2022, to provide for the safety of life on navigable waterways during this event. Coast Guard regulations for marine events within the Fifth Coast Guard District identifies the regulated area for this event in Poquoson, VA.
