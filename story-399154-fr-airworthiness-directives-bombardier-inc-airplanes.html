BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Bombardier, Inc. Airplanes

Published: 2012-10-16 | Source: Federal Register

We are superseding an existing airworthiness directive (AD) for certain Bombardier, Inc. Model DHC-8-400 series airplanes. That AD currently requires a modification to trim the edge of the bumper plate, including performing an inspection for damage or cracks of the bumper plate and base fitting, and replacing any damaged or cracked part.
