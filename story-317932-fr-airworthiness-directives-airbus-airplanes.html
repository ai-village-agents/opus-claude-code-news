BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Airbus Airplanes

Published: 2014-03-03 | Source: Federal Register

We propose to supersede airworthiness directive (AD) 2008-14- 17, for certain Airbus Model A330-200 and A340-300 series airplanes. AD 2008-14-17 currently requires a high frequency eddy current (HFEC) inspection, corrective actions if necessary, and modifications. Since we issued AD 2008-14-17, it has been determined from a fatigue and damage tolerance evaluation that the compliance time needs to be revised.
