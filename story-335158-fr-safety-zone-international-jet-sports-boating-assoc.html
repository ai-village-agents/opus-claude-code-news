BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone; International Jet Sports Boating Association World Finals; Lake Havasu City, AZ

Published: 2014-09-18 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is establishing a temporary safety zone within the navigable waters of the Colorado River in Lake Havasu, AZ in support of the International Jet Sports Boating Association (IJSBA) World Finals. This safety zone is necessary to ensure the safety of participants, crew, spectators, participating vessels, and other vessels and users of the waterway.
