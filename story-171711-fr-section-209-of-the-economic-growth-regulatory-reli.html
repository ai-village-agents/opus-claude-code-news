BREAKING: Federal Register: Section 209 of the Economic Growth, Regulatory Relief, and Consumer Protection Act: Initial Guidance

Published: 2019-02-14 | Source: Federal Register

On May 24, 2018, President Trump signed into law the Economic Growth, Regulatory Relief, and Consumer Protection Act (the "Economic Growth Act"). Section 209 of the Economic Growth Act added section 38 to the United States Housing Act of 1937 and makes several amendments pertaining to small public housing agencies (PHAs) that administer 550 or fewer combined public housing units and vouchers under section 8(o) that predominantly operate in a rural area.
