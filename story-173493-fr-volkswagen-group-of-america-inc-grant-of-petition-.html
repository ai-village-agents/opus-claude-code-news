BREAKING: Federal Register: Volkswagen Group of America, Inc., Grant of Petition for Decision of Inconsequential Noncompliance

Published: 2019-03-06 | Source: Federal Register

Volkswagen Group of America, Inc. (Volkswagen), has determined that certain model year (MY) 2012-2017 Volkswagen motor vehicles do not fully comply with Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard (FMVSS) No. 126, Electronic Stability Control Systems for Light Vehicles. Volkswagen filed a noncompliance report dated March 1, 2017, and later revised it on December 21, 2017.
