BREAKING: Federal Register: Agency Information Collection Extension

Published: 2014-05-06 | Source: Federal Register

EIA intends to revise and extend for three years, Form EIA-914 "Monthly Natural Gas Production Report," with the Office of Management and Budget (OMB) pursuant to the Paperwork Reduction Act of 1995. The revisions include increasing the number of states for which natural gas production will be collected.
