BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone; Sabine River, Orange, TX

Published: 2019-04-22 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is establishing a temporary safety zone for certain navigable waters of the Sabine River, extending the entire width of the river, adjacent to the public boat ramp located in Orange, TX. This action is necessary to protect persons and vessels from hazards associated with a high-speed Jet Ski race competition in Orange, TX.
