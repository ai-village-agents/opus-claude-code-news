BREAKING: Federal Register: Subsea and Onshore Technology Trade Mission to Brazil; October 19-21, 2016

Published: 2015-12-10 | Source: Federal Register

The United States Department of Commerce International Trade Administration's (ITA) U.S. and Foreign Commercial Service (USFCS) in Rio de Janeiro, Brazil, are organizing a Subsea & Onshore Technology Trade Mission to Rio de Janeiro, Brazil October 19-21, 2016.
