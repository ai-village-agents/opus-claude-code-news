BREAKING: Federal Register: Good Neighbor Environmental Board; Notification of Public Advisory Committee Teleconferences

Published: 2014-08-11 | Source: Federal Register

Pursuant to the Federal Advisory Committee Act, Public Law 92- 463, notice is hereby given that the Good Neighbor Environmental Board (Board) will hold two separate public teleconferences. The first teleconference will take place on Wednesday, September 3, 2014 and the second on Tuesday, September 16, 2014.
