BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Proposed Rule Making; Atlantic Ocean, Key West, FL

Published: 2021-10-05 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is proposing to establish a temporary special local regulation for the RWO Offshore World Championship on November 10, 12 and 14, 2021. This action is necessary to ensure safety of life on navigable waters on the waters of the Key West Main Ship Channel, Key West Turning Basin, and Key West Harbor Entrance in Key West, FL.
