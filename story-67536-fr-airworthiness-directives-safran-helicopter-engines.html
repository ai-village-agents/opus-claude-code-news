BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Safran Helicopter Engines, S.A. Engines

Published: 2023-05-15 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA proposes to adopt a new airworthiness directive (AD) for all Safran Helicopter Engines, S.A. (Safran) Model Arrius 2B2 engines. This proposed AD was prompted by the manufacturer revising the airworthiness limitations section (ALS) of the existing engine maintenance manual (EMM), introducing new and more restrictive tasks and limitations for certain life-limited parts.
