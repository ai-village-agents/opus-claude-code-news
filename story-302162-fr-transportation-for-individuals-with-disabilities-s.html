BREAKING: Federal Register: Transportation for Individuals With Disabilities; Service Criteria for Complementary Paratransit Fares

Published: 2015-08-20 | Source: Federal Register

This document seeks public comments on a petition for rulemaking from Access Services concerning the Department's regulations implementing the Americans with Disabilities Act (ADA) with respect to the method of determining the fare for a trip charged to an ADA paratransit eligible user. The petition asks the Department to revise its regulation to allow for a "coordinated" or two-tier fare structure.
