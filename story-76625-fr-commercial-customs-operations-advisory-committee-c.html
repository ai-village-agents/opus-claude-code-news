BREAKING: Federal Register: Commercial Customs Operations Advisory Committee (COAC)

Published: 2023-09-12 | Source: Federal Register

U.S. Customs and Border Protection (CBP) is revising the notice published in the Federal Register on August 30, 2023, which announced that the next meeting of the Commercial Customs Operations Advisory Committee (COAC) will be held on Wednesday, September 20, 2023.
