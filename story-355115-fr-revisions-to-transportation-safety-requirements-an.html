BREAKING: Federal Register: Revisions to Transportation Safety Requirements and Harmonization With International Atomic Energy Agency Transportation Requirements

Published: 2013-05-16 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Nuclear Regulatory Commission (NRC), in consultation with the U.S. Department of Transportation (DOT), is proposing to amend its regulations for the packaging and transportation of radioactive material. These amendments would make NRC regulations conform to revisions to the International Atomic Energy Agency (IAEA) regulations for the international transportation of radioactive material and maintain consistency with DOT regulations.
