BREAKING: Federal Register: Creation of Freedom of Information Act Advisory Committee

Published: 2014-05-05 | Source: Federal Register

This notice is published in accordance with the provisions of section 9(a)(2) of the Federal Advisory Committee Act (Pub. L. 92-463, 5 U.S.C., App.) and advises of the creation of the National Archives and Records Administration's (NARA) Freedom of Information Act (FOIA) Advisory Committee.
