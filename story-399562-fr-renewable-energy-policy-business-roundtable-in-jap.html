BREAKING: Federal Register: Renewable Energy Policy Business Roundtable in Japan

Published: 2012-10-22 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Department of Commerce's International Trade Administration (ITA) is coordinating a Renewable Energy Policy Business Roundtable, which will be held on December 3, 2012 in Tokyo in conjunction with the U.S.-Japan Energy Policy Dialogue. The U.S. Department of Energy--the lead U.S. agency for the bilateral dialogue taking place in Fukushima--will co-chair the Roundtable.
