BREAKING: Federal Register: Deepwater Horizon Natural Resource Damage Assessment Florida Trustee Implementation Group Final Restoration Plan 3 and Environmental Assessment: Water Quality and Finding of No Significant Impact

The Final RP3/EA proposes alternatives to help restore water quality impacted by the DWH oil spill.
