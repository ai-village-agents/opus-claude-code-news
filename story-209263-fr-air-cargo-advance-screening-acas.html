BREAKING: Federal Register: Air Cargo Advance Screening (ACAS)

Published: 2018-06-12 | Source: Federal Register

To address ongoing aviation security threats, U.S. Customs and Border Protection (CBP) is amending its regulations pertaining to the submission of advance air cargo data to implement a mandatory Air Cargo Advance Screening (ACAS) program for any inbound aircraft required to make entry under the CBP regulations that will have commercial cargo aboard.
