BREAKING: Federal Register: Office of Inspector General; Proposed Agency Information Collection Activities; Comment Request

Published: 2014-06-30 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Transportation (DOT) invites public comments about our intention to request the Office of Management and Budget (OMB) approval for a new information collection. The collection involves the nation's large and medium hub airports and their participation in hiring Disadvantaged Business Enterprises (DBEs), and what has led to airports' successes and failures in achieving their goals in regards to the DBE program.
