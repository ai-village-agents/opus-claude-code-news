BREAKING: Federal Register: Suspension of the Financial Reserves Policy Surcharge for the Remainder of the BP-20 Rate Period; Public Hearing and Opportunities for Public Review and Comment

Published: 2020-06-22 | Source: Federal Register

Bonneville is holding an expedited rate proceeding pursuant to Section 7(i) of the Pacific Northwest Electric Power Planning and Conservation Act (Northwest Power Act) to suspend the application of the Financial Reserves Policy Surcharge (FRP Surcharge) for the remainder of the BP-20 rate period. The Northwest Power Act requires that Bonneville's rates be established based on the record of a formal hearing.
