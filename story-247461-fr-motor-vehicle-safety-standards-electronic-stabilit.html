BREAKING: Federal Register: Motor Vehicle Safety Standards; Electronic Stability Control Systems for Heavy Vehicles

Published: 2017-10-30 | Source: Federal Register

This final rule addresses a petition for reconsideration of the final rule for FMVSS No. 136, Electronic stability control systems for heavy vehicles. The petitioner, Truck and Engine Manufacturers Association (EMA), requested that NHTSA amend the test conditions for the agency's performance test by allowing a larger lane width for long wheelbase truck tractors.
