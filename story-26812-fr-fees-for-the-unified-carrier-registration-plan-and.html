BREAKING: Federal Register: Fees for the Unified Carrier Registration Plan and Agreement

Published: 2024-01-09 | Source: Federal Register

FMCSA proposes amendments to its regulations governing the annual registration fees that participating States collect from motor carriers, motor private carriers of property, brokers, freight forwarders, and leasing companies for the Unified Carrier Registration (UCR) Plan and Agreement for the 2025 registration year and subsequent registration years.
