BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Airbus Airplanes

Published: 2018-02-15 | Source: Federal Register

We are adopting a new airworthiness directive (AD) for all Airbus Model A320-271N, A321-271N, and A321-272N airplanes. This AD requires de-pairing certain International Aero Engines (IAE) engines in order to continue to operate affected airplanes and discontinuing extended operations (ETOPS) for airplanes with at least one affected engine.
