BREAKING: Federal Register: Lake Tahoe Basin Federal Advisory Committee (LTFAC)

Published: 2012-05-17 | Source: Federal Register

The Lake Tahoe Federal Advisory Committee will hold a meeting on June 14, 2012 at the Tahoe Regional Planning Agency, 128 Market Street, Stateline, Nevada 89449-5310. This Committee, established by the Secretary of Agriculture on December 15, 1998 (64 FR 2876), is chartered to provide advice to the Secretary on implementing the terms of the Federal Interagency Partnership on the Lake Tahoe Region and other matters raised by the Secretary.
