BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; General Electric Company Turbofan Engines

Published: 2019-02-08 | Source: Federal Register

We are superseding Airworthiness Directive (AD) 2018-16-07, which applied to certain General Electric Company (GE) GEnx turbofan engines. AD 2018-16-07 required removal and replacement of affected high-pressure turbine (HPT) stator cases (HPT cases). This AD retains those requirements, but reduces certain compliance times.
