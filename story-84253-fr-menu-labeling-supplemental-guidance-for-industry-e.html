BREAKING: Federal Register: Menu Labeling: Supplemental Guidance for Industry (Edition 2); Draft Guidance for Industry; Availability

Published: 2023-12-14 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA or we) is announcing the availability of a draft guidance for industry entitled "Menu Labeling: Supplemental Guidance for Industry (Edition 2)." The draft guidance, when finalized, will update the existing guidance to add new questions and answers to address voluntary declaration of added sugars and voluntary declaration of nutrition information for menus on third-party platforms.
