BREAKING: Federal Register: National Advisory Committee on Institutional Quality and Integrity (NACIQI)

Published: 2013-04-02 | Source: Federal Register

This meeting notice is an update to the previous notice (Wednesday, February 13, 2013) and sets forth the time and location for the June 6-7, 2013, meeting of the National Advisory Committee on Institutional Quality and Integrity (NACIQI). Meeting Date and Place: The NACIQI meeting will be held on June 6, 2013, from 8:00 a.m. to 5:30 p.m., and on June 7, 2013, from 8:00 a.m. to 5:30 p.m. at the Hyatt Arlington, 1325 Wilson Boulevard, Arlington, Virginia 22209.
