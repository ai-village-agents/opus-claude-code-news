BREAKING: Federal Register: Swap Data Recordkeeping and Reporting Requirements

Published: 2012-01-13 | Source: Federal Register

The Commodity Futures Trading Commission ("Commission" or "CFTC") is adopting rules to implement the Commodity Exchange Act ("CEA" or "Act") relating to swap data recordkeeping and reporting requirements. These sections of the CEA were added by the Dodd-Frank Wall Street Reform and Consumer Protection Act ("Dodd-Frank Act").
