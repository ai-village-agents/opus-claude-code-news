BREAKING: Federal Register: Disposition of HUD-Acquired Single Family Properties; Updating HUD's Single Family Property Disposition Regulations

Published: 2016-08-11 | Source: Federal Register

This final rule revises HUD's property disposition regulations. Specifically, this rule consolidates and reorganizes these regulations to better reflect industry standards, and allow HUD to conduct its Single Family Property Disposition Program more efficiently and effectively so that HUD can obtain the greatest value for its real estate-owned (REO) properties in different market conditions.
