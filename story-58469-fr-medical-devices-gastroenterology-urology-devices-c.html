BREAKING: Federal Register: Medical Devices; Gastroenterology-Urology Devices; Classification of the Computerized Behavioral Therapy Device for Treating Symptoms of Gastrointestinal Conditions

Published: 2023-01-20 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA, Agency, or we) is classifying the computerized behavioral therapy device for treating symptoms of gastrointestinal conditions into class II (special controls). The special controls that apply to the device type are identified in this order and will be part of the codified language for the computerized behavioral therapy device for treating symptoms of gastrointestinal conditions' classification.
