BREAKING: Federal Register: Debt Collection Practices (Regulation F); Extension of Comment Period

Published: 2020-05-21 | Source: Federal Register

On March 3, 2020, the Bureau of Consumer Financial Protection (Bureau) published in the Federal Register a Supplemental Notice of Proposed Rulemaking (SNPRM) requesting comment on the Bureau's proposal to amend Regulation F, which implements the Fair Debt Collection Practices Act (FDCPA), to require debt collectors to make certain disclosures when collecting time-barred debts.
