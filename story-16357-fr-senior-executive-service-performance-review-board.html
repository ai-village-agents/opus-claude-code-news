BREAKING: Federal Register: Senior Executive Service Performance Review Board

Published: 2025-07-21 | Source: Federal Register

In accordance with 5 U.S.C. 4314(c)(4), the U.S. Office of Special Counsel (OSC) announces the appointment of members to the agency's Senior Executive Service (SES) Performance Review Board (PRB). The PRB is responsible for reviewing and evaluating the initial appraisal of a senior executive's performance by the supervisor, along with any response by the senior executive, and making recommendations to the appointing authority regarding the performance of the senior executive.
