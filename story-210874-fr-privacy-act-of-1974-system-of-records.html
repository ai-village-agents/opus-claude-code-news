BREAKING: Federal Register: Privacy Act of 1974; System of Records

Published: 2018-07-03 | Source: Federal Register

In accordance with the Privacy Act and our disclosure regulations, we are issuing public notice of our intent to publish a new routine use applicable to four of our system of records. The routine use will permit disclosures we intend to make to new entities to support the administration of our representative payee program.
