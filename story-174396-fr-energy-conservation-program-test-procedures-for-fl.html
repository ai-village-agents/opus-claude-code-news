BREAKING: Federal Register: Energy Conservation Program: Test Procedures for Fluorescent Lamp Ballasts

Published: 2019-03-18 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Department of Energy (DOE) proposes to revise its test procedures for fluorescent lamp ballasts. DOE proposes to update references to industry standards; clarify the selection of reference lamps; provide a second stabilization option for measuring ballast luminous efficiency; provide a test procedure for measuring the performance of ballasts at light outputs less than full light output; and revise the test procedure for measuring standby mode energy consumption.
