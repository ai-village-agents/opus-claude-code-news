BREAKING: Federal Register: NASA Heliophysics Advisory Committee; Space Weather Council; Meeting

Published: 2022-08-05 | Source: Federal Register

In accordance with the Federal Advisory Committee Act, the National Aeronautics and Space Administration (NASA) announces a meeting of the Space Weather Council. The Space Weather Council is a subcommittee of the NASA Heliophysics Advisory Committee. The Committee functions in an advisory capacity to the Director, Heliophysics Division, in the NASA Science Mission Directorate.
