BREAKING: Federal Register: Right-of-Way Grant of Submerged Lands on the Outer Continental Shelf to Support Renewable Energy Development

Published: 2012-08-29 | Source: Federal Register

BOEM Form 0009 would be used to issue Outer Continental Shelf (OCS) renewable energy right-of-way (ROW) grants in order to streamline this process and increase efficiency and consistency for applicants.
