BREAKING: Federal Register: Privacy Act of 1974; System of Records

Published: 2023-05-02 | Source: Federal Register

In accordance with the Privacy Act of 1974, as amended, the FDIC proposes to establish a new FDIC system of records titled FDIC- 041, "Personal Information Allowing Network Operations (PIANO)."
