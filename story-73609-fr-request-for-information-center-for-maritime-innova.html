BREAKING: Federal Register: Request for Information: Center for Maritime Innovation

Published: 2023-08-03 | Source: Federal Register

This notice requests information from the public to assist MARAD in determining how best to organize and structure a Center for Maritime Innovation (the "Center"). MARAD authority in this area provides the Center to support the study, research, development, assessment, and deployment of emerging marine technologies and practices related to the maritime transportation system.
