BREAKING: Federal Register: Establishment of Class E airspace; Limon Municipal Airport, CO

Published: 2022-06-01 | Source: Federal Register

This action establishes Class E airspace at Limon Municipal Airport, Limon, CO. The establishment of airspace supports the airport's transition from visual flight rules (VFR) to instrument flight rule (IFR) operations.
