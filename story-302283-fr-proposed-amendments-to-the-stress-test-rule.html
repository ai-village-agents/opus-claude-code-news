BREAKING: Federal Register: Proposed Amendments to the Stress Test Rule

Published: 2015-08-21 | Source: Federal Register

The Federal Housing Finance Agency (FHFA) is proposing amendments to its stress testing rule adopted in 2013 to implement section 165(i) of the Dodd-Frank Wall Street Reform and Consumer Protection Act. The amendments would modify the start date of the stress test cycles from October 1 of a calendar year to January 1 of the following calendar year.
