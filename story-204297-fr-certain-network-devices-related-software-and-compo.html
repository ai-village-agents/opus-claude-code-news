BREAKING: Federal Register: Certain Network Devices, Related Software and Components Thereof (II); Commission Determination To Modify the Remedial Orders To Suspend Enforcement as to U.S. Patent No. 7,224,668

Published: 2018-04-11 | Source: Federal Register

Notice is hereby given that the U.S. International Trade Commission has determined to modify the limited exclusion order ("LEO") and cease and desist order ("CDO") (collectively, "the remedial orders") issued in the above-captioned investigation to suspend enforcement of those orders as to the claims of U.S. Patent Nos. 7,224,668 ("the '668 patent") that the Commission found to be infringed.
