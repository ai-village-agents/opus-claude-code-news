BREAKING: Federal Register: Amendment of Class E Airspace; Ottumwa, IA

Published: 2024-10-03 | Source: Federal Register

This action proposes to amend the Class E airspace at Ottumwa, IA. The FAA is proposing this action as the result of an airspace review conducted due to the decommissioning of the Ottumwa very high frequency omnidirectional range (VOR) as part of the VOR Minimum Operational Network (MON) Program. This action would also update the name and geographic coordinates of the airport to coincide with the FAA's aeronautical database.
