BREAKING: Federal Register: Federal Perkins Loan, Federal Work Study, and Federal Supplemental Educational Opportunity Grant programs; 2014-2015 Award Year Deadline Dates

Published: 2014-01-15 | Source: Federal Register

The Secretary announces the 2014-2015 award year deadline dates for the submission of requests and documents from postsecondary institutions for the Federal Perkins Loan, Federal Work Study (FWS), and Federal Supplemental Educational Opportunity Grant (FSEOG) programs (collectively, the "campus-based programs").
