BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; American Champion Aircraft Corp.

Published: 2018-07-13 | Source: Federal Register

We are superseding Airworthiness Directive (AD) (AD) 2017-07- 10 for certain American Champion Aircraft Corp. (ACAC) Model 8KCAB airplanes. AD 2017-07-10 required fabrication and installation of a placard to prohibit aerobatic flight, inspection of the aileron hinge rib and support, and a reporting requirement of the inspection results to the FAA.
