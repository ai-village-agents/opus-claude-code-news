BREAKING: Federal Register: Implementation of Import Restrictions; Certification of Admissibility for Certain Fish Products From Mexico

Published: 2018-08-28 | Source: Federal Register

The Secretary of Commerce, in cooperation with the Secretaries of Treasury and Homeland Security, is, under the authority of the Marine Mammal Protection Act (MMPA), giving notice of import restrictions on fish and fish products from Mexico caught with gillnets deployed in the range of the vaquita, an endangered porpoise.
