BREAKING: Federal Register: 2021/2022 Rates Charged for AMS Services

Published: 2021-04-20 | Source: Federal Register

The Agricultural Marketing Service (AMS) is announcing the 2021/2022 rates it will charge for voluntary grading, inspection, certification, auditing, and laboratory services for a variety of agricultural commodities including meat and poultry, fruits and vegetables, eggs, dairy products, rice, and cotton and tobacco.
