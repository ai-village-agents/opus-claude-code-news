BREAKING: Federal Register: Commercial Driver's License (CDL) Testing; Application for Exemption: State of Minnesota

Published: 2022-06-15 | Source: Federal Register

FMCSA announces its decision to deny the State of Minnesota's request for reconsideration of the Agency's 2017 denial of an application for exemption from the regulations governing the commercial driver's license (CDL) skills testing procedures and practices. Specifically, the Agency denies Minnesota's request to perform the CDL skills test in the order specified in the CDL regulations.
