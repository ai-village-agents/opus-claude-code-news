BREAKING: Federal Register: Policy on Donations, Fundraising, and Solicitation

Published: 2014-06-17 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Fish and Wildlife Service (Service) announces that it has established a policy that covers Service procedures for accepting, using, and recognizing donations. This donations policy is an extension of the Department of the Interior's guidance on donations, found in the Departmental Manual (DM) at 374 DM 6.
