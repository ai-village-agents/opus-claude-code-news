BREAKING: Federal Register: Privacy Act of 1974; System of Records

Published: 2024-02-22 | Source: Federal Register

Pursuant to the Privacy Act of 1974 and Office of Management and Budget (OMB) Circular No. A-108, the Federal Communications Commission (FCC) proposes to rescind the FCC/OMD-7, FCC Transit Benefit and Parking Permit Programs, system of records. The FCC previously used information in this system to administer the transit benefit and parking permit programs for FCC employees.
