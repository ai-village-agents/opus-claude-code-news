BREAKING: Federal Register: Certain Argon Plasma Coagulation System Probes, Their Components, and Other Argon Plasma Coagulation System Components for Use Therewith Commission Determination Not To Review an Initial Determination Terminating the Investigation as to Certain Respondents and Granting Leave To Amend the Complaint and Notice of Investigation

Published: 2020-02-28 | Source: Federal Register

Notice is hereby given that the U.S. International Trade Commission ("Commission") has determined not to review an initial determination ("ID") of the presiding administrative law judge ("ALJ") terminating this investigation as to certain respondents and granting leave to amend the complaint and notice of investigation to add a respondent.
