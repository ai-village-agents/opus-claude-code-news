BREAKING: Federal Register: Agency Information Collection Activities: Announcement of Board Approval Under Delegated Authority and Submission to OMB

Published: 2025-11-17 | Source: Federal Register

The Board of Governors of the Federal Reserve System (Board) is adopting a proposal to extend for three years, without revision, the Consumer Complaint Form (FR 1379c), and Interagency Appraisal Complaint Form (FR 1379d) (collectively FR 1379; OMB No. 7100-0135).
