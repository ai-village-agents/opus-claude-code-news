BREAKING: Federal Register: Privacy Act of 1974: Systems of Records

Published: 2023-07-06 | Source: Federal Register

Pursuant to the Privacy Act of 1974, the National Credit Union Administration (NCUA) gives notice of a proposed modified Privacy Act system of records titled NCUA-11, ``Office of Inspector General (OIG) Investigative Records.''
