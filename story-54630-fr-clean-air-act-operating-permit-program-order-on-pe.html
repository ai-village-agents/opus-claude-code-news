BREAKING: Federal Register: Clean Air Act Operating Permit Program; Order on Petition for Objection to State Operating Permit for Sundance Power Plant

Published: 2024-12-06 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) Administrator signed an order dated November 6, 2024, denying a petition dated June 27, 2024, from Sierra Club. The Petition requested that the EPA object to a Clean Air Act (CAA) title V operating permit issued by the Pinal County Air Quality Control District (PCAQCD) to the Arizona Public Service Company Sundance Power Plant ("APS Sundance"), an electricity generating station located in Casa Grande, Arizona.
