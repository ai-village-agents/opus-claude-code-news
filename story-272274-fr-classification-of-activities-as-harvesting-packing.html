BREAKING: Federal Register: Classification of Activities as Harvesting, Packing, Holding, or Manufacturing/Processing for Farms and Facilities; Draft Guidance for Industry; Availability

Published: 2016-08-25 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA or we) is announcing the availability of a draft guidance for industry entitled "Classification of Activities as Harvesting, Packing, Holding, or Manufacturing/ Processing for Farms and Facilities; Draft Guidance for Industry." The draft guidance, when finalized, will help food establishments determine whether the activities that they perform are within the "farm" definition established in our regulation for Registration of Food Facilities.
