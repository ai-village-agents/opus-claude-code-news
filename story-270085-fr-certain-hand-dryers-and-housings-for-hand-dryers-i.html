BREAKING: Federal Register: Certain Hand Dryers and Housings for Hand Dryers: Institution of Investigation

Published: 2016-08-01 | Source: Federal Register

Notice is hereby given that a complaint was filed with the U.S. International Trade Commission on June 24, 2016, under section 337 of the Tariff Act of 1930, as amended, 19 U.S.C. 1337, on behalf of Excel Dryer, Inc. of East Longmeadow, Massachusetts. A supplement to the complaint was filed on July 14, 2016.
