BREAKING: Federal Register: Revision of Fee Schedules; Fee Recovery for Fiscal Year 2021

Published: 2021-08-13 | Source: Federal Register

On June 16, 2021, the U.S. Nuclear Regulatory Commission (NRC) published a final rule that amends its regulations pertaining to user fees and annual fees charged to its applicants and licensees.
