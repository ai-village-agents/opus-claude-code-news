BREAKING: Federal Register: Extension of Expiration Dates for Five Body System Listings

Published: 2019-11-26 | Source: Federal Register

We are extending the expiration dates of the following body systems in the Listing of Impairments (listings) in our regulations: Musculoskeletal System, Cardiovascular System, Digestive System, Skin Disorders, and Immune System Disorders. We are making no other revisions to these body systems in this final rule.
