BREAKING: Federal Register: Interlake Stamping Corp. (Also Doing Business as Interlake Industries, Inc.); Revocation of an Experimental Variance and Interim Order

Published: 2014-03-07 | Source: Federal Register

In this notice, the Occupational Safety and Health Administration ("OSHA" or the "Agency") revokes an experimental variance and interim order granted by OSHA in 1976 and 1978, respectively, to Interlake Stamping Corp., ("Interlake" or the "applicant") from several provisions of the OSHA standard that regulates mechanical power presses at 29 CFR 1910.217.
