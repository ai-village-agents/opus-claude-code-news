BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of a Commission Determination Not to Review an Initial Determination Terminating the Investigation in Its Entirety Based on Withdrawal of the Complaint; Termination of the Investigation; Certain Active Matrix OLED Display Devices and Components Thereof

Published: 2020-11-30 | Source: Federal Register

Notice is hereby given that the International Trade Commission has determined not to review an initial determination ("ID") (Order No. 7) of the presiding administrative law judge ("ALJ") terminating the investigation based on withdrawal of the complaint. The investigation is terminated.
