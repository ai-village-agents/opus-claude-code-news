BREAKING: Federal Register: Endangered and Threatened Species; Take of Anadromous Fish

Published: 2013-05-03 | Source: Federal Register

Notice is hereby given that NMFS has received an application for a direct take permit, in the form of a Hatchery and Genetic Management Plan (HGMP), pursuant to the Endangered Species Act of 1973, as amended (ESA). The application is for a hatchery program in Idaho, for the propagation of sockeye salmon. The proposed permit would be issued for a period of 10 years.
