BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Public Meeting of the Ohio Advisory Committee to the U.S. Commission on Civil Rights

Published: 2019-07-02 | Source: Federal Register

Notice is hereby given, pursuant to the provisions of the rules and regulations of the U.S. Commission on Civil Rights (Commission) and the Federal Advisory Committee Act that the Ohio Advisory Committee (Committee) will hold a meeting via teleconference on Wednesday, July 17, 2019, from 10:30-11:30 a.m. EDT for the purpose of reviewing received testimony and discussing next steps in the Committee's final report and recommendations to the Commission on education funding in the state.
