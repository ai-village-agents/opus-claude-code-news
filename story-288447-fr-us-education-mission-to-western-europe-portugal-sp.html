BREAKING: Federal Register: U.S. Education Mission to Western Europe; Portugal, Spain, United Kingdom, France (Optional)

Published: 2015-03-12 | Source: Federal Register

The United States (U.S.) Department of Commerce, International Trade Administration, is organizing an education mission to Portugal, Spain, United Kingdom (UK) with an optional stop to France. The Department of Commerce is partnering with the Department of State's EducationUSA Advising Centers in Portugal, Spain, and France and the Fulbright Commission in the UK to connect schools directly with potential students at fairs and provide market insight.
