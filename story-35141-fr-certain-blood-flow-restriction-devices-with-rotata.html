BREAKING: Federal Register: Certain Blood Flow Restriction Devices With Rotatable Windlasses and Components Thereof; Notice of a Commission Determination That the March 19, 2024, Initial Determination (Order No. 23) is an Order and Recommended Determination Rather Than an Initial Determination; Request for Written Submissions on Remedy, the Public Interest, and Bonding

Published: 2024-04-24 | Source: Federal Register
