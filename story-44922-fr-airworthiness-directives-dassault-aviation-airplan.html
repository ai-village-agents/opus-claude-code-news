BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Dassault Aviation Airplanes

Published: 2024-08-21 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA proposes to adopt a new airworthiness directive (AD) for certain Dassault Aviation Model FALCON 7X, FALCON 900EX, and FALCON 2000EX airplanes. This proposed AD was prompted by reported occurrences of swelling of the lithium-polymer internal and external batteries of certain electronic display units (EDUs).
