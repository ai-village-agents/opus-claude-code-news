BREAKING: Federal Register: Submission for Review: Designation of Beneficiary, Federal Employees Retirement System, (FERS), SF 3102

Published: 2017-04-13 | Source: Federal Register

The Retirement Services, Office of Personnel Management (OPM) offers the general public and other Federal agencies the opportunity to comment on an extension, without change, of a currently approved information collection request (ICR), Designation of Beneficiary (FERS), SF 3102.
