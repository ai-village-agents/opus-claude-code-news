BREAKING: Federal Register: State Enforcement of Inland Navigation Rules

Published: 2023-06-12 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is issuing this final rule to adopt the 2022 interim rule removal of an incorrect statement in the Code of Federal Regulations about field preemption of State or local regulations regarding inland navigation. The incorrect language was added by a 2014 final rule, and the error was subsequently discovered.
