BREAKING: Federal Register: Special Local Regulation; Upper Mississippi River, Mile 842.0 to 840.0

Published: 2012-07-03 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is establishing a temporary special local regulation for all waters of the Upper Mississippi River, Mile 842.0 to 840.0, extending the entire width of the river. This special local regulation is needed to protect participants and event personnel during the swim leg of the Optum Health Twin Cities Triathlon occurring on the Upper Mississippi River.
