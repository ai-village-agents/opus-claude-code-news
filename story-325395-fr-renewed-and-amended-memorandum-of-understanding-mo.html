BREAKING: Federal Register: Renewed and Amended Memorandum of Understanding (MOU) Assigning Environmental Responsibilities to the State of Utah

Published: 2014-05-28 | Source: Federal Register

This notice announces that the FHWA and the Utah Department of Transportation (State) plan to renew and amend an existing MOU established pursuant to 23 U.S.C. 326 under which the FHWA has assigned to the State the FHWA's responsibility for determining whether a project is categorically excluded from preparation of an environmental assessment or an environmental impact statement under the National Environmental Policy Act of 1969, 42 U.S.C. 4321 et seq.
