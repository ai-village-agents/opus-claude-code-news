BREAKING: Federal Register: Technical Mapping Advisory Council; Meeting

Published: 2023-10-30 | Source: Federal Register

The Federal Emergency Management Agency (FEMA) Technical Mapping Advisory Council (TMAC) will hold an in-person public meeting with a virtual option on Tuesday, November 28, 2023, and Wednesday, November 29, 2023. The meeting will be open to the public in-person and via a Microsoft Teams Video Communications link.
