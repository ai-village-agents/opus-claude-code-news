BREAKING: Federal Register: Intent To Make Preemption Determination Under the Truth in Lending Act (Regulation Z)

Published: 2022-12-15 | Source: Federal Register

The Consumer Financial Protection Bureau (CFPB) has received a written request to make a determination that the Truth in Lending Act (TILA) preempts a New York State commercial financing law with respect to certain provisions. The CFPB is publishing this notification of intent to make a preemption determination about that law and has made a preliminary conclusion that this law is not preempted by TILA.
