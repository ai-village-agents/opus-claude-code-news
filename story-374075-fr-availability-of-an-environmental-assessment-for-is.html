BREAKING: Federal Register: Availability of an Environmental Assessment for Issuance of a Permit for Distribution and Sale of an Infectious Hematopoietic Necrosis Virus Vaccine, DNA

Published: 2013-12-31 | Source: Federal Register

We are advising the public that the Animal and Plant Health Inspection Service has prepared an environmental assessment concerning authorization to import under permit, for distribution and sale, an Infectious Hematopoietic Necrosis Virus Vaccine, DNA. The environmental assessment, which is based on a risk analysis prepared to assess the risks associated with the use of this vaccine, examines the potential effects that this veterinary vaccine could have on the quality of the human environment.
