BREAKING: Federal Register: Air Plan Approval; Florida: Public Notice Procedures for Minor Operating Permits

Published: 2020-05-05 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is proposing to approve portions of a State Implementation Plan (SIP) revision submitted by the State of Florida, through the Florida Department of Environmental Protection (FDEP), on February 27, 2013, that change the State's public notice and comment rule for air permitting by modifying the public comment period for minor source operating permitting and making administrative edits.
