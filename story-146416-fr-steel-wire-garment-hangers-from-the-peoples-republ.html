BREAKING: Federal Register: Steel Wire Garment Hangers From the People's Republic of China: Final Results of Antidumping Duty Administrative Review; 2017-2018

Published: 2020-03-11 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Commerce (Commerce) continues to find that Shanghai Wells Hanger Co., Ltd., and Hong Kong Wells Ltd. (collectively, Shanghai Wells) failed to demonstrate eligibility for separate rate status during the period of review (POR), and these companies, therefore, are a part of the China-wide entity. The POR is October 1, 2017 through September 30, 2018.
