BREAKING: Federal Register: Filing Requirements and Processing Procedures for Changes in Control With respect to State Nonmember Banks and State Savings Associations

Published: 2014-11-25 | Source: Federal Register

The FDIC is proposing to amend its filing requirements and processing procedures for notices filed under the Change in Bank Control Act (Notices). The proposed amendments are intended to accomplish several objectives.
