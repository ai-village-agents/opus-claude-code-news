BREAKING: Federal Register: Bonneville Power Administration Klickitat Hatchery Upgrades Environmental Impact Statement (EIS) Termination

Published: 2023-02-24 | Source: Federal Register

This notice advises the public that BPA is terminating the preparation of the Klickitat Hatchery Upgrades EIS and instead will prepare an EA. This EIS would have considered BPA's decision whether to fund the Confederated Tribes and Bands of the Yakama Nation's (Yakama Nation) proposal to upgrade facilities at the Klickitat Hatchery to facilitate a possible increase in production of spring Chinook salmon in the Klickitat River Basin.
