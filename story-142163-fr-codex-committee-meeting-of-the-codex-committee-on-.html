BREAKING: Federal Register: Codex Committee Meeting of the Codex Committee on General Principles

Published: 2020-01-16 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S Codex Office is sponsoring a public meeting on February 21, 2020. The objective of the public meeting is to provide information and receive public comments on agenda items and draft United States (U.S.) positions to be discussed at the 32nd Session of the Codex Committee on General Principles (CCGP) of the Codex Alimentarius Commission, in Bordeaux, France, March 23-27, 2020.
