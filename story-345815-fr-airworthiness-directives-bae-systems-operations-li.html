BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; BAE SYSTEMS (OPERATIONS) LIMITED Airplanes

Published: 2013-02-01 | Source: Federal Register

We are adopting a new airworthiness directive (AD) for all BAE SYSTEMS (OPERATIONS) LIMITED Model BAe 146, and Avro 146-RJ series airplanes. This AD was prompted by a report of loss of the end caps on the anti-icing piccolo tube of the wing leading edge. This AD requires a detailed inspection of the end caps on the anti-icing piccolo tube for lost or loose end caps, and replacing or repairing the end caps if necessary.
