BREAKING: Federal Register: Revocation of Two Authorizations of Emergency Use of In Vitro Diagnostic Devices for Detection and/or Diagnosis of COVID-19; Availability

Published: 2022-03-14 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA) is announcing the revocation of the Emergency Use Authorizations (EUAs) (the Authorizations) issued to LifeHope Labs for the LifeHope 2019-nCoV Real-Time RT-PCR Diagnostic Panel, and Omnipathology Solutions Medical Corporation for the Omni COVID-19 Assay by RT-PCR.
