BREAKING: Federal Register: Food and Drug Administration Prescription Drug User Fee Act VI Benefit-Risk Implementation Plan; Request for Comments

Published: 2018-04-02 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA or Agency) is announcing the availability of a draft 5-year plan describing the Agency's approach to further the implementation of structured benefit-risk assessment, including the incorporation of the patient's voice in drug development and decision-making, in the human drug review program and the opportunity for public comment on the draft plan.
