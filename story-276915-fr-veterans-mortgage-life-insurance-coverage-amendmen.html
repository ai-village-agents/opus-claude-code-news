BREAKING: Federal Register: Veterans' Mortgage Life Insurance-Coverage Amendment

Published: 2016-10-18 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Veterans Affairs (VA) proposes to amend its regulations governing the Veterans' Mortgage Life Insurance (VMLI) program in order to provide VMLI-eligible individuals the option to lower their premiums by purchasing less than the minimum coverage amount required under current VA regulations.
