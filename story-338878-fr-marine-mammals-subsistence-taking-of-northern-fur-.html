BREAKING: Federal Register: Marine Mammals; Subsistence Taking of Northern Fur Seals; St. George Island, Alaska

Published: 2014-11-04 | Source: Federal Register

This final rule modifies the subsistence harvest regulations for the Eastern Pacific stock of northern fur seals (Callorhinus ursinus) based on a petition from the Pribilof Island Aleut Community of St. George Island, Traditional Council (Council).
