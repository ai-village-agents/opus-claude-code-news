BREAKING: Federal Register: Clinical Investigator Training Course

Published: 2016-09-16 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA), in collaboration with the University of Maryland Center of Excellence in Regulatory Science and Innovation (M-CERSI), is announcing a 3-day training course for clinical investigators on the scientific, ethical, and regulatory aspects of clinical trials for medical products.
