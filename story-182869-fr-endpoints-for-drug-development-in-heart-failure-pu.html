BREAKING: Federal Register: Endpoints for Drug Development in Heart Failure; Public Workshop

Published: 2019-06-28 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA, the Agency, or we) is announcing a public workshop entitled "Endpoints for Drug Development in Heart Failure." The purpose of this public meeting is to bring the stakeholder community together to discuss clinical endpoints for trials in heart failure that could be used to support FDA approval of drugs.
