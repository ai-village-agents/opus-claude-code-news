BREAKING: Federal Register: Modification of Significant New Uses of Tris Carbamoyl Triazine; Technical Amendment

Published: 2012-04-24 | Source: Federal Register

This technical amendment implements a technical correction that published in the Federal Register of March 7, 2012. Specifically, the correction involves the removal of a cross-reference that was erroneously included in a final rule that published in the Federal Register of February 8, 2012.
