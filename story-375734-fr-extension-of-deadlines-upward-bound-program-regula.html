BREAKING: Federal Register: Extension of Deadlines; Upward Bound Program (Regular Upward Bound (UB))

Published: 2012-01-25 | Source: Federal Register

The Secretary extends to February 1, 2012, and April 2, 2012, respectively, the Deadline for Transmittal of Applications and the Deadline for Intergovernmental Review dates that were published in the Applications for New Awards; Upward Bound Program (Regular Upward Bound (UB)) notice on December 19, 2011 (76 FR 78621).
