BREAKING: Federal Register: Expansion of the Microprocessor Military End-Use and End-User Control

Published: 2014-12-17 | Source: Federal Register

The Bureau of Industry and Security (BIS) is amending the microprocessor military end-use and end-user control in the Export Administration Regulations by expanding the scope of microprocessors subject to the restriction to harmonize with technological advances to microprocessor chips and expand the scope to include related software and technology for the development and production of these chips.
