BREAKING: Federal Register: Information Collection: NRC Form 483, “Registration Certificate-In Vitro Testing With Byproduct Material Under General License”

Published: 2020-10-08 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Nuclear Regulatory Commission (NRC) invites public comment on the renewal of Office of Management and Budget (OMB) approval for an existing collection of information. The information collection is entitled, NRC Form 483, "Registration Certificate--In Vitro Testing with Byproduct Material Under General License."
