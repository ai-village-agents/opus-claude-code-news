BREAKING: Federal Register: Entergy Nuclear Operations, Inc., Indian Point Nuclear Generating, Units No. 2 and 3

Published: 2017-04-20 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Nuclear Regulatory Commission (NRC) has issued a director's decision with regard to a petition dated June 30, 2016, filed by Mr. David A. Lochbaum of the Union of Concerned Scientists (the petitioner), requesting that the NRC take enforcement action against Entergy Nuclear Operations, Inc., the licensee for Indian Point Nuclear Generating, Units No. 2 and 3 (Indian Point 2 and 3).
