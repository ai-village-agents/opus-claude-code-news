BREAKING: Federal Register: Public Notice for Waiver of Aeronautical Land-Use Assurance

Published: 2013-12-09 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA is considering a proposal to change 24.19 acres of airport land from aeronautical use to non-aeronautical use of airport property located at Burnett County Airport, Siren WI. The aforementioned land is not needed for aeronautical use. The Gandy Dancer Trail is a large recreational trail system in Western Wisconsin.
