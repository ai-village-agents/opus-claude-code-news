BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Opportunity for Public Comment To Dispose of 1.93 Acres of Land at Eastern Slopes Regional Airport, Fryeburg, ME

Published: 2021-04-21 | Source: Federal Register

Notice is being given that the FAA is considering a request from the Town of Fryeburg, ME to dispose of 2.0 Acres of land at Eastern Slopes Regional Airport, Fryeburg ME. The land was the site of the non-directional beacon located 10.2 nautical miles from the airport and was decommissioned and removed from the site.
