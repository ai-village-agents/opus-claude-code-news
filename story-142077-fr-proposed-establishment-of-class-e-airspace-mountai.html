BREAKING: Federal Register: Proposed Establishment of Class E Airspace; Mountain Home, ID

Published: 2020-01-15 | Source: Federal Register

This action proposes to establish Class E airspace extending upward from 700 feet or more above the surface at Mountain Home Municipal Airport, Mountain Home, ID. The first area is proposed to extend upward from 700 feet above the surface. The second area is proposed to extend upward from 1,200 feet above the surface.
