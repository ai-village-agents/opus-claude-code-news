BREAKING: Federal Register: VizRisk Prize Competition Challenge

Published: 2014-07-21 | Source: Federal Register

VizRisk is the first-ever behavioral health data visualization challenge hosted by the US Department of Health and Human Services (HHS). Its goal is to foster increased utilization, innovation, and critical analyses of publically available but underutilized government health data to better inform personal and health policy decisions.
