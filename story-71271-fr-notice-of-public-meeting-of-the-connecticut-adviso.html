BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Public Meeting of the Connecticut Advisory Committee to the U.S. Commission on Civil Rights; Revision to Date

Published: 2023-07-05 | Source: Federal Register

The Commission on Civil Rights published a notice in the Federal Register of Friday, June 16, 2023, concerning a meeting of the Connecticut Advisory Committee. The notice is in FR Doc. 2023-12881, in the first and second columns of page 39398. The document contains a meeting date that is now revised from Thursday, June 29, 2023, at 11:00 a.m. ET to Thursday, July 6, 2023, at 12:00 p.m. ET.
