BREAKING: Federal Register: Nevada and Placer Counties Resource Advisory Committee

Published: 2017-09-07 | Source: Federal Register

The Nevada and Placer Counties Resource Advisory Committee (RAC) will meet in Truckee, California. The committee is authorized under the Secure Rural Schools and Community Self-Determination Act of 2000 (the Act) as reauthorized by Public Law 114-10 and operates in compliance with the Federal Advisory Committee Act.
