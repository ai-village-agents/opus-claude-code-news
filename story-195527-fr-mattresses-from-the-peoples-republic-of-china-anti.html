BREAKING: Federal Register: Mattresses From the People's Republic of China: Antidumping Duty Order

Published: 2019-12-16 | Source: Federal Register

Based on the affirmative final determination by the Department of Commerce (Commerce) and the International Trade Commission (ITC), Commerce is issuing the antidumping duty (AD) order on mattresses from the People's Republic of China (China).
