BREAKING: Federal Register: 504 Loans and Debentures With 25 Year Maturity

Published: 2018-04-04 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Small Business Administration is making available a 504 Loan, and the Debenture that funds it, with a 25 year maturity in addition to the 10 and 20 year 504 Loan and Debenture that are currently available in the 504 Loan Program.
