BREAKING: Federal Register: Approval and Promulgation of Air Quality Implementation Plans; South Dakota; Revisions to the Permitting Rules

Published: 2018-06-26 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is taking final action to approve State Implementation Plan (SIP) revisions submitted by the State of South Dakota on October 4, 2017 related to South Dakota's Air Pollution Control Program. The October 4, 2017 submittal revises certain definitions and dates of incorporation by reference and contains new, amended and renumbered rules.
