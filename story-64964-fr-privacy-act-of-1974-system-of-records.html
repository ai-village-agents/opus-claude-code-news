BREAKING: Federal Register: Privacy Act of 1974; System of Records

Published: 2023-04-12 | Source: Federal Register

Pursuant to the Privacy Act of 1974, notice is hereby given that the VA is modifying the system of records entitled, "National Patient Databases-VA" (121VA10A7). VA provides health care services to many of America's Veterans through the VHA. However, the care of America's Veterans extends beyond VHA and into community providers.
