BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zones; Annual Safety Zones in the Captain of the Port Detroit Zone

Published: 2019-03-25 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard proposes to amend its recurring safety zones regulations in the Captain of the Port Detroit zone. This proposed rule would update 51 safety zone locations, dates, and sizes, add 3 safety zones, remove 6 established safety zones and reformat the regulations into an easier to read table format. These proposed amendments will protect spectators, participants, and vessels from the hazards associated with annual marine events and firework shows, and improve the clarity and readability...
