BREAKING: Federal Register: Request for Information on the Future Direction of the Rehabilitation Training Program

Published: 2012-11-08 | Source: Federal Register

The Department is requesting input to revitalize its training and technical assistance (TA) activities funded under the Rehabilitation Training Program.
