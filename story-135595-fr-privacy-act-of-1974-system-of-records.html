BREAKING: Federal Register: Privacy Act of 1974; System of Records

Published: 2021-10-15 | Source: Federal Register

In accordance with the Privacy Act of 1974, the DoD is establishing a new system of records titled ``Office of Military Commissions (OMC), Office of Court Administration's Nominees and Panel Member Records,'' DGC 23. This system will be used to verify panel members' eligibility to serve on the panel, confirm panel members' clearances, and coordinate their travel to the location of the court.
