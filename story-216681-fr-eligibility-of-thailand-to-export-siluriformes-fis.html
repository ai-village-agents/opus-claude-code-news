BREAKING: Federal Register: Eligibility of Thailand To Export Siluriformes Fish and Fish Products to the United States

Published: 2018-09-19 | Source: Federal Register

The Food Safety and Inspection Service (FSIS) is proposing to amend the Siluriformes fish inspection regulations to list Thailand as a country eligible to export Siluriformes fish and fish products to the United States.
