BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Lycoming Engines Reciprocating Engines

Published: 2015-04-27 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA is withdrawing a notice of proposed rulemaking (NPRM). The NPRM proposed a new airworthiness directive (AD) that had applied to certain Lycoming Engines (type certificate formerly held by Textron Lycoming) with Superior Air Parts, Inc. (SAP), piston pins installed. The NPRM had applied to those engines using SAP piston pins, part number (P/N) 13444-1.
