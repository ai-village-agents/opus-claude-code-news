BREAKING: Federal Register: Air Plan Approval; Kentucky; 2008 Ozone NAAQS Interstate Transport SIP Requirements

Published: 2018-07-17 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is approving a revision to Kentucky's State Implementation Plan (SIP) pertaining to the "good neighbor" provision of the Clean Air Act (CAA or Act) for the 2008 8-hour ozone National Ambient Air Quality Standard (NAAQS). Kentucky submitted a draft version of this SIP revision for parallel processing by EPA on February 28, 2018, and submitted a final version that contained no substantive changes on May 10, 2018.
