BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zones; Annual Fireworks Displays Within the Puget Sound

Published: 2024-08-28 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard will enforce the safety zone regulation for the Mukilteo Lighthouse Festival to provide for the safety of life on navigable waters on the Possession Sound, Seattle, Washington during an annual fireworks display. This safety zone will consist of all navigable waters within a 450-yard radius surrounding the event's launch site.
