BREAKING: Federal Register: Announcement of Requirements and Registration for “I VetoViolence Because . . .”: Teen Dating Violence Prevention Public Service Announcement Contest

Published: 2013-07-11 | Source: Federal Register

The Centers for Disease Control and Prevention (CDC) located within the Department of Health and Human Services (HHS) launches the "I VetoViolence Because . . .": Teen Dating Violence Prevention Public Service Announcement Contest. The purpose of the contest is to encourage the development of video public service announcements (PSA) that increase the understanding (1) that teen dating violence is a public health problem and (2) that prevention efforts can stop it before it starts.
