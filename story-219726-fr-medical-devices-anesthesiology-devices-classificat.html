BREAKING: Federal Register: Medical Devices; Anesthesiology Devices; Classification of the High Flow Humidified Oxygen Delivery Device

Published: 2018-10-26 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA or we) is classifying the high flow humidified oxygen delivery device into class II (special controls). The special controls that apply to the device type are identified in this order and will be part of the codified language for the high flow humidified oxygen delivery device's classification.
