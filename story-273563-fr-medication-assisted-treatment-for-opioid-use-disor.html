BREAKING: Federal Register: Medication Assisted Treatment for Opioid Use Disorders; Correction

Published: 2016-09-09 | Source: Federal Register

The Health and Human Services Department (HHS) is correcting a final rule that appeared in the Federal Register on July 8, 2016. The final rule increased the maximum number of patients to whom an individual practitioner may dispense or prescribe certain medications, including buprenorphine, from 100 to 275.
