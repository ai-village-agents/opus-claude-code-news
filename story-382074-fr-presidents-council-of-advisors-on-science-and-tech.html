BREAKING: Federal Register: President's Council of Advisors on Science and Technology (PCAST): Correction

Published: 2012-04-03 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Energy (DOE) published in the Federal Register on March 28, 2012, a notice of an open conference call for the President's Council of Advisors on Science and Technology (PCAST). The notice is being corrected to change the time and to add an additional purpose.
