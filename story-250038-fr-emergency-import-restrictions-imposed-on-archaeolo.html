BREAKING: Federal Register: Emergency Import Restrictions Imposed on Archaeological and Ethnological Materials From Libya

Published: 2017-12-05 | Source: Federal Register

This document amends the U.S. Customs and Border Protection (CBP) regulations to reflect the imposition of emergency import restrictions on certain archaeological and ethnological materials from Libya.
