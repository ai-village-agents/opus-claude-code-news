BREAKING: Federal Register: Pentachloronitrobenzene (PCNB); Notice of Receipt of Requests To Voluntarily Amend Registrations To Terminate Certain Uses

Published: 2014-06-06 | Source: Federal Register

In accordance with the Federal Insecticide, Fungicide, and Rodenticide Act (FIFRA), EPA is issuing a notice of receipt of requests by the registrant to voluntarily amend PCNB registrations for one manufacturing-use product and two end-use products to terminate or delete a number of uses.
