BREAKING: Federal Register: International Services Surveys and Direct Investment Surveys Reporting

Published: 2012-04-24 | Source: Federal Register

The Bureau of Economic Analysis (BEA) revises its rules to establish general guidelines for how BEA will collect data on international trade in services and direct investment surveys, which are provided for by the International Investment and Trade in Services Survey Act (the Act). In addition to the Act, the Omnibus Trade and Competitiveness Act of 1988 authorizes BEA to conduct international trade in services surveys.
