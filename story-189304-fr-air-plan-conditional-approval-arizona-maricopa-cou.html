BREAKING: Federal Register: Air Plan Conditional Approval; Arizona; Maricopa County

Published: 2019-09-23 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is proposing to conditionally approve revisions to the Maricopa County Air Quality Department (MCAQD or the County) portion of the Arizona State Implementation Plan (SIP). These revisions concern emissions of volatile organic compounds (VOCs) from organic liquid and gasoline storage and transfer operations.
