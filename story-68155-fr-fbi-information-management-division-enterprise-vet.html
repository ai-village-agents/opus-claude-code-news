BREAKING: Federal Register: FBI Information Management Division, Enterprise Vetting Center User Fee Schedule

Published: 2023-05-23 | Source: Federal Register

This notice establishes a revised user fee for federal agencies requesting name-based, non-criminal justice background checks of the FBI's Central Records System through the Enterprise Vetting Center (EVC), formally known as the National Name Check Program (NNCP). The total resource costs associated with providing name check services have been calculated to ensure full reimbursement to the FBI ("cost reimbursement portion" of the fee).
