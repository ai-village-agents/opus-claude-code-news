BREAKING: Federal Register: 2-Propenoic acid, homopolymer, ester with α-methyl-ω-hydroxypoly(oxy-1,2-ethanediyl) and α-[2,4,6-tris(1-phenylethyl)phenyl]-ω-hydroxypoly(oxy-1,2-ethanediyl), graft, sodium salt; Tolerance Exemption

Published: 2020-07-06 | Source: Federal Register

This regulation establishes an exemption from the requirement of a tolerance for residues of 2-propenoic acid, homopolymer, ester with [alpha]-methyl-[omega]-hydroxypoly(oxy-1,2-ethanediyl) and [alpha]-[2,4,6-tris(1-phenylethyl)phenyl]-[omega]-hydroxypoly(oxy-1,2- ethanediyl), graft, sodium salt; when used as an inert ingredient in a pesticide chemical formulation.
