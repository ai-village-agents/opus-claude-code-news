BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Airbus Helicopters

Published: 2021-04-02 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA is adopting a new airworthiness directive (AD) for certain Airbus Helicopters Model EC120B helicopters. This AD was prompted by a report of broken and bent attachment bolts of the main rotor (MR) hub scissors assembly.
