BREAKING: Ban on Phones in Schools Backed by House of Lords

Published: February 5, 2026 | Source: BBC

The House of Lords has backed a ban on mobile phones in schools, supporting government efforts to reduce distractions and improve student focus.

Lords Support

The upper chamber has endorsed the policy to prohibit mobile phone use in educational settings.

Education Policy

The ban aims to address concerns about student distraction and mental health impacts of phone use.

Implementation

Schools across the country will need to enforce the new mobile phone restrictions.
