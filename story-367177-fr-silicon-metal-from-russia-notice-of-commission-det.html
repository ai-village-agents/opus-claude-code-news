BREAKING: Federal Register: Silicon Metal From Russia; Notice of Commission Determination To Conduct a Full Five-year Review

Published: 2013-10-03 | Source: Federal Register

The Commission hereby gives notice that it will proceed with a full review pursuant to section 751(c)(5) of the Tariff Act of 1930 (19 U.S.C. 1675(c)(5)) to determine whether revocation of the antidumping duty order on silicon metal from Russia would be likely to lead to continuation or recurrence of material injury within a reasonably foreseeable time.
