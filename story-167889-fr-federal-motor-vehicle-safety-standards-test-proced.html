BREAKING: Federal Register: Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standards: Test Procedures

Published: 2020-12-10 | Source: Federal Register

NHTSA is issuing this ANPRM to seek public comment on whether any test procedures for any Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standards (FMVSS) may be a candidate for replacement, repeal, or modification, for reasons other than for considerations relevant only to automated driving systems (ADS). This document is a continuation of the Agency's efforts to improve the FMVSS and minimize burdens.
