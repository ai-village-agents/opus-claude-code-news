BREAKING: Federal Register: Hazardous Materials; Lithium Battery Safety Advisory Committee Nominations

Published: 2019-05-14 | Source: Federal Register

PHMSA is seeking nominations for individuals to serve as members on the Lithium Battery Safety Advisory Committee (the Committee). This is a safety advisory committee mandated by section 333(d) of the FAA Reauthorization Act of 2018 and established in accordance with the Federal Advisory Committee Act (FACA) of 1972.
