BREAKING: Federal Register: Child Nutrition Programs: Flexibilities for Milk, Whole Grains, and Sodium Requirements

Published: 2017-11-30 | Source: Federal Register

This interim final rule extends through school year 2018-2019 three menu planning flexibilities currently available to many Child Nutrition Program operators, giving them near-term certainty about Program requirements and more local control to serve nutritious and appealing meals to millions of children nationwide.
