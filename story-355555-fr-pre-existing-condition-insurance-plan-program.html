BREAKING: Federal Register: Pre-Existing Condition Insurance Plan Program

Published: 2013-05-22 | Source: Federal Register

This interim final rule with comment period sets the payment rates for covered services furnished to individuals enrolled in the Pre-Existing Condition Insurance Plan (PCIP) program administered directly by HHS beginning with covered services furnished on June 15, 2013.
