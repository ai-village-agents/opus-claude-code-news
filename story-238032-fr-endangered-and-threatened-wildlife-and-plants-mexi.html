BREAKING: Federal Register: Endangered and Threatened Wildlife and Plants; Mexican Wolf Draft Recovery Plan, First Revision

Published: 2017-06-30 | Source: Federal Register

We, the U.S. Fish and Wildlife Service (Service), announce the availability of our Mexican Wolf (Canis lupus baileyi) Draft Recovery Plan, First Revision (draft recovery plan). The Mexican wolf is listed as endangered under the Endangered Species Act of 1973, as amended (Act), and is currently found in Arizona and New Mexico, in the United States, and in Chihuahua, Mexico.
