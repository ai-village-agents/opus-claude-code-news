BREAKING: Federal Register: Derivatives Clearing Organization General Provisions and Core Principles

Published: 2020-01-27 | Source: Federal Register

The Commodity Futures Trading Commission (Commission) is amending certain regulations applicable to registered derivatives clearing organizations (DCOs). The amendments address certain risk management and reporting obligations, clarify the meaning of certain provisions, simplify processes for registration and reporting, and codify existing staff relief and guidance, among other things.
