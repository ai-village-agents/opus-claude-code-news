BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Realty Action: Recreation and Public Purposes Act Classification of Public Lands in Humboldt County, Nevada

Published: 2023-02-14 | Source: Federal Register

The Bureau of Land Management (BLM), Humboldt River Field Office, has examined certain public lands in Humboldt County, Nevada, and has found them suitable for classification for conveyance to Humboldt County under the provisions of the Recreation and Public Purposes (R&PP) Act, as amended.
