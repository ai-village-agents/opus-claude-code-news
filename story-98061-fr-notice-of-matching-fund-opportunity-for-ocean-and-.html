BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Matching Fund Opportunity for Ocean and Coastal Mapping and Request for Partnership Proposals

Published: 2022-06-10 | Source: Federal Register

By establishing selection criteria and requirements for the NOAA Rear Admiral Richard T. Brennan Ocean Mapping Matching Fund program, to be known as the Brennan Matching Fund, this notice invites non-Federal entities to partner with NOAA National Ocean Service's ocean and coastal mapping programs on jointly funded projects of mutual interest.
