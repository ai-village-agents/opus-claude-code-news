BREAKING: Federal Register: Special Local Regulation; Lake Havasu, Lake Havasu City, AZ

Published: 2024-11-20 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is establishing a special local regulation for the 2024 Lake Havasu City Christmas Parade of Lights that will be held on the navigable waters of Lake Havasu, AZ. This action is necessary to provide for the safety of life on these navigable waters of Lake Havasu during a vessel parade.
