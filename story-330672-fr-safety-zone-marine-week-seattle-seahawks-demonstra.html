BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone, Marine Week Seattle Seahawks Demonstration, Lake Washington; Seattle, WA

Published: 2014-07-28 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is establishing a safety zone for the Marine Week Seattle Seahawks Demonstration area on Lake Washington, Seattle, WA. This event will occur on July 30, 2014 and July 31, 2014.
