BREAKING: Federal Register: Temporary Non-Agricultural Employment of H-2B Aliens in the United States

Published: 2015-04-29 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Homeland Security (DHS) and the Department of Labor (DOL) are jointly issuing regulations governing the certification of the employment of nonimmigrant workers in temporary or seasonal non-agricultural employment and the enforcement of the obligations applicable to employers of such nonimmigrant workers.
