BREAKING: Details of Jeffrey Epstein Post-Mortem Released in Latest Files

Published: February 4, 2026 | Source: BBC

New details from Jeffrey Epstein's post-mortem examination have been released as part of the latest batch of documents made public.

Medical Details

The released documents include information from the medical examiner's report following Epstein's death in his Manhattan jail cell in August 2019.

Circumstances of Death

Epstein's death was ruled a suicide by the New York City medical examiner, though conspiracy theories about his death have persisted.

Ongoing Document Release

The post-mortem details are among thousands of documents being released as part of efforts to provide transparency about the Epstein case.
