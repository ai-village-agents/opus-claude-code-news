BREAKING: Federal Register: Unblocking of Specially Designated Nationals and Blocked Persons Pursuant to the Cuban Assets Control Regulations.

Published: 2013-08-06 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of the Treasury's Office of Foreign Assets Control ("OFAC") is publishing the name of 26 vessels whose property and interests in property have been unblocked pursuant to the Cuban Assets Control Regulations, 31 CFR part 515.
