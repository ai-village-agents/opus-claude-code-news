BREAKING: Federal Register: Bulletin 2022-03: Servicer Responsibilities in Public Service Loan Forgiveness Communications

Published: 2022-03-01 | Source: Federal Register

The Consumer Financial Protection Bureau (CFPB) is issuing this Compliance Bulletin and Policy Guidance (Bulletin) regarding the servicing of Federal student loans, including Federal Family Education Loan Program and Perkins loans, for borrowers who may be eligible for Public Service Loan Forgiveness (PSLF).
