BREAKING: Federal Register: Special Local Regulation; New River Raft Race, New River; Fort Lauderdale, FL

Published: 2013-03-20 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is establishing a special local regulation on the New River in Fort Lauderdale, Florida during the Rotary Club of Fort Lauderdale New River Raft Race, on Saturday, March 23, 2013. The special local regulation is necessary to ensure the safety of the participants, participant vessels, and the general public during the event.
