BREAKING: Federal Register: Addition of Entities, Revision and Correction of Entries, and Removal of Entities From the Entity List

Published: 2022-06-30 | Source: Federal Register

In this rule, the Bureau of Industry and Security (BIS) amends the Export Administration Regulations (EAR) by adding 36 entities under 41 entries to the Entity List. These 36 entities have been determined by the U.S. Government to be acting contrary to the national security or foreign policy interests of the United States.
