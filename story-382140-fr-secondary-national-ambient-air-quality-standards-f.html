BREAKING: Federal Register: Secondary National Ambient Air Quality Standards for Oxides of Nitrogen and Sulfur

Published: 2012-04-03 | Source: Federal Register

This final rule is being issued as required by a consent decree governing the schedule for completion of this review of the air quality criteria and the secondary national ambient air quality standards (NAAQS) for oxides of nitrogen and oxides of sulfur.
