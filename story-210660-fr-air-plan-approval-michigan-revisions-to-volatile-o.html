BREAKING: Federal Register: Air Plan Approval; Michigan; Revisions to Volatile Organic Compound Rules

Published: 2018-06-29 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is approving revised rules submitted by the State of Michigan as State Implementation Plan (SIP) revisions. The main revision specifies volatile organic compound (VOC) limits for cutback and emulsified asphalts as well as the test methods for determining the VOC content of these products.
