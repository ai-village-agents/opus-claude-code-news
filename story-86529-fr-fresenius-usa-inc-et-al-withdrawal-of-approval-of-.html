BREAKING: Federal Register: Fresenius USA, Inc., et al.; Withdrawal of Approval of 216 Abbreviated New Drug Applications; Correction

Published: 2022-01-18 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA) is correcting a notice that appeared in the Federal Register of October 22, 2021. The document announced the withdrawal of approval of 216 abbreviated new drug applications (ANDAs) from multiple applicants, as of November 22, 2021. The document was published with an incorrect date.
