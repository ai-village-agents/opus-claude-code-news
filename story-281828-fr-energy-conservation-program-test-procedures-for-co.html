BREAKING: Federal Register: Energy Conservation Program: Test Procedures for Cooking Products

Published: 2016-12-16 | Source: Federal Register

On August 22, 2016, the U.S. Department of Energy (DOE) issued a supplemental notice of proposed rulemaking to amend the test procedure for conventional cooking products. That proposed rulemaking serves as the basis for this final rule.
