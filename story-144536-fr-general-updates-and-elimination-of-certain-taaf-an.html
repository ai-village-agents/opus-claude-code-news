BREAKING: Federal Register: General Updates and Elimination of Certain TAAF and PWEDA Regulations

Published: 2020-02-14 | Source: Federal Register

The Economic Development Administration ("EDA"), U.S. Department of Commerce ("DOC"), is issuing a final rule to update the agency's regulations implementing the Trade Adjustment Assistance for Firms ("TAAF") provisions of the Trade Act of 1974, as amended ("Trade Act"), and the Public Works and Economic Development Act of 1965, as amended ("PWEDA").
