BREAKING: Federal Register: Air Plan Approval; SC; VOC Definition

Published: 2018-06-26 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is taking final action to approve a revision to the South Carolina State Implementation Plan (SIP). The revision makes a modification to the definition of "volatile organic compounds" (VOC).
