BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Applications for Deregistration Under Section 8(f) of the Investment Company Act of 1940

Published: 2012-01-06 | Source: Federal Register

Applicant seeks an order declaring that it has ceased to be an investment company. On April 30, 2008, applicant transferred its assets to Tax-Free Cash Reserve Portfolio, a series of Short-Term Investments Trust, based on net asset value. Expenses of $29,100 incurred in connection with the reorganization were paid by Invesco Advisers, Inc., applicant's investment adviser.
