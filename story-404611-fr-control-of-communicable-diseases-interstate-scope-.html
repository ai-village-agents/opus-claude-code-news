BREAKING: Federal Register: Control of Communicable Diseases: Interstate; Scope and Definitions

Published: 2012-12-26 | Source: Federal Register

In this Direct Final Rule, the Centers for Disease Control and Prevention (CDC), located within the Department of Health and Human Services (HHS) is proposing to update the definitions for interstate quarantine regulations to reflect modern terminology and plain language used by private industry and public health partners.
