BREAKING: Federal Register: Federal Participation in the Development and Use of Voluntary Consensus Standards and in Conformity Assessment Activities

Published: 2012-03-30 | Source: Federal Register

The Office of Management and Budget (OMB) invites interested parties to provide input on current issues regarding Federal agencies' standards and conformity assessment related activities. Input is being sought to inform OMB's consideration of whether and how to supplement Circular A-119 (Federal Participation in the Development and Use of Voluntary Consensus Standards and in Conformity Assessment Activities).
