BREAKING: Federal Register: Medical Benefits Package; Chiropractic Services

Published: 2023-04-21 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Veterans Affairs (VA) is amending its medical regulations to add chiropractic services to the definitions of medical services and preventive care. VA will further revise the definition of medical services to include rehabilitative services consistent with its statutory definition and to reflect changes made in other VA medical regulations and in prior legislation not previously codified.
