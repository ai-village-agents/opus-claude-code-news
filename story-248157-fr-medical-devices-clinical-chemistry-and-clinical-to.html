BREAKING: Federal Register: Medical Devices; Clinical Chemistry and Clinical Toxicology Devices; Classification of the Total 25-Hydroxyvitamin D Mass Spectrometry Test System

Published: 2017-11-07 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA, the Agency, or we) is classifying the total 25-hydroxyvitamin D mass spectrometry test system into class II (special controls). The special controls that apply to the device type are identified in this order and will be part of the codified language for the total 25-hydroxyvitamin D mass spectrometry test system's classification.
