BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Public Meeting of the Assembly of the Administrative Conference of the United States

Published: 2012-05-25 | Source: Federal Register

Pursuant to the Federal Advisory Committee Act (Pub. L. 92- 463), notice is hereby given of a meeting of the Assembly of the Administrative Conference of the United States to consider proposed recommendations which deal with: (1) Regulatory analysis requirements, (2) midnight rules, (3) immigration removal adjudication, (4) the Paperwork Reduction Act, and (5) improving coordination of related agency responsibilities.
