BREAKING: Federal Register: Kansas Disaster #KS-00063

Published: 2012-05-01 | Source: Federal Register

This is a notice of an Administrative declaration of a disaster for the State of Kansas dated 04/23/2012. Incident: Severe storms, hail and tornadoes. Incident Period: 04/14/2012 through 04/15/2012.
