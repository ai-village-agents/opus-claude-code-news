BREAKING: Federal Register: Disqualification of Felons and Other “Bad Actors” From Rule 506 Offerings

Published: 2013-07-24 | Source: Federal Register

We are adopting amendments to our rules to implement Section 926 of the Dodd-Frank Wall Street Reform and Consumer Protection Act. Section 926 requires us to adopt rules that disqualify securities offerings involving certain "felons and other `bad actors"' from reliance on Rule 506 of Regulation D.
