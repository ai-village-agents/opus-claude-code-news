BREAKING: Federal Register: National Cybersecurity Center of Excellence (NCCoE) and Electric Power Sector Identity and Access Management Use Case

Published: 2014-03-18 | Source: Federal Register

The National Institute of Standards and Technology (NIST) invites organizations to provide products and technical expertise to support and demonstrate security platforms for identity and access management for the electric power sector. This notice is the initial step for the National Cybersecurity Center of Excellence (NCCoE) in collaborating with technology companies to address cybersecurity challenges identified under the Energy Sector program.
