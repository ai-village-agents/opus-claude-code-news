BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Pratt & Whitney Canada Corp. Turboshaft Engines

Published: 2012-11-07 | Source: Federal Register

We propose to adopt a new airworthiness directive (AD) for certain serial number Pratt & Whitney Canada Corp. (P&WC) PW206B, PW206B2, PW206C, PW207C, PW207D, PW207D1, PW207D2, and PW207E turboshaft engines. This proposed AD was prompted by the discovery that certain power turbine (PT) disks were made to specific heat codes that may not achieve the maximum in-service life.
