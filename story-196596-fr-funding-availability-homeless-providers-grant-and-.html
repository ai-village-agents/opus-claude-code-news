BREAKING: Federal Register: Funding Availability: Homeless Providers Grant and Per Diem Program

Published: 2019-12-30 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Veterans Affairs (VA) is announcing the availability of per diem funds to eligible entities to provide transitional housing beds or service centers for Veterans who are homeless or at risk for becoming homeless under VA's Homeless Providers GPD Program models.
