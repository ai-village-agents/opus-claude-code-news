BREAKING: Federal Register: Walnuts Grown in California; Increased Assessment Rate

Published: 2013-09-17 | Source: Federal Register

This proposed rule would increase the assessment rate established for the California Walnut Board (Board) for the 2013-14 and subsequent marketing years from $0.0175 to $0.0189 per kernelweight pound of merchantable walnuts. The Board locally administers the marketing order which regulates the handling of walnuts grown in California.
