BREAKING: Federal Register: Submission for Office of Management and Budget (OMB) Review; National Medical Support Notice Part A (OMB No.: 0970-0222)

Published: 2022-09-21 | Source: Federal Register

The Office of Child Support Enforcement (OCSE), Administration for Children and Families (ACF), is requesting a three-year extension of the National Medical Support Notice (NMSN) Part A. This request includes minor revisions to the NMSN Part A form, revisions to and separation of the instructions into a stand-alone attachment, a Part A sample, and the addition of the State Medical Support Contacts and Program Requirements matrix.
