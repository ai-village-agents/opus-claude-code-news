BREAKING: Federal Register: Administrative Requirements for Grants and Cooperative Agreements

Published: 2015-09-03 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Energy (DOE) is adopting, a rule amending the administrative requirements for grants and cooperative agreements with for-profit organizations. The regulations modify title provisions, and requirements related to the handling of real property and equipment acquired with federal funds.
