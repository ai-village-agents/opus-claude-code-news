BREAKING: Federal Register: Sunshine Act Meeting: Board of Directors and Operations & Regulations Committee Telephonic Meetings

Published: 2016-11-18 | Source: Federal Register

On November 6, 2016, the Legal Services Corporation (LSC) published a notice in the Federal Register (81 FR 80686) titled "Board of Directors will meet telephonically on November 22, 2016. Immediately following the Board of Directors telephonic meeting, the Operations and Regulations Committee will hold a telephonic meeting."
