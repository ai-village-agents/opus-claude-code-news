BREAKING: Federal Register: IoT Home Inspector Challenge

Published: 2017-01-04 | Source: Federal Register

The Federal Trade Commission ("FTC") announces a prize competition that challenges the public to create a technical solution ("tool") that consumers can deploy to guard against security vulnerabilities in software on the Internet of Things ("IoT") devices in their homes. The tool would, at a minimum, help protect consumers from security vulnerabilities caused by out-of-date software.
