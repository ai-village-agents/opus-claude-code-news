BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone, Great Egg Harbor Bay; Somers Point, NJ

Published: 2015-12-15 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is extending the dates for a temporary safety zone on the waters of Great Egg Harbor Bay in the vicinity of the Garden State Parkway Bridge in Somers Point, NJ. Due to the severe weather felt in the Mid-Atlantic region in the past month the project has been delayed by a number of weeks and more time is needed to complete the critical repairs for the Garden State Parkway Bridge.
