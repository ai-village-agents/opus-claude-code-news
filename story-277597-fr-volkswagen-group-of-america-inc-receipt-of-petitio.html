BREAKING: Federal Register: Volkswagen Group of America, Inc., Receipt of Petition for Decision of Inconsequential Noncompliance

Published: 2016-10-26 | Source: Federal Register

Volkswagen Group of America , Inc. (Volkswagen), has determined that certain model year (MY) 2016 Volkswagen eGolf motor vehicles do not fully comply with paragraph S6.5.3.2 of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard (FMVSS) No. 108, Lamps, reflective devices and associated equipment. Volkswagen filed a report dated September 16, 2016, pursuant to 49 CFR part 573, Defect and Noncompliance Responsibility and Reports.
