BREAKING: Federal Register: Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standards; Child Restraint Systems, Child Restraint Anchorage Systems; Incorporation by Reference

Published: 2015-01-23 | Source: Federal Register

In accordance with NHTSA's 2011-2013 Priority Plan and the Moving Ahead for Progress in the 21st Century Act (MAP-21), this document proposes to amend Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard (FMVSS) No. 225, "Child restraint anchorage systems," to improve the ease of use of the lower anchorages of child restraint anchorage systems and the ease of use of tether anchorages.
