BREAKING: Federal Register: Mitigation of Beyond-Design-Basis Events

Published: 2019-08-09 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Nuclear Regulatory Commission (NRC) is amending its regulations that establish regulatory requirements for nuclear power reactor applicants and licensees to mitigate beyond-design-basis events. The NRC is making generically applicable the requirements in NRC orders for mitigation of beyond-design-basis events and for reliable spent fuel pool instrumentation (SFPI).
