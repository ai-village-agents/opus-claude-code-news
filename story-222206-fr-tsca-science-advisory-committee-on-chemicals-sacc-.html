BREAKING: Federal Register: TSCA Science Advisory Committee on Chemicals (SACC); Notice of Public Meetings

Published: 2018-11-30 | Source: Federal Register

This notice announces the peer review of the draft Risk Evaluation for Colour Index (C.I.) Pigment Violet 29 (PV29) and associated documents developed under EPA's existing chemical substance process under the Toxic Substances Control Act (TSCA). The peer review panel deliberations will be conducted during a 4-day, in-person, public meeting of the TSCA Science Advisory Committee on Chemicals (SACC).
