BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Airbus Airplanes

Published: 2016-10-20 | Source: Federal Register

We are adopting a new airworthiness directive (AD) for certain Airbus Model A330-200 and -300 series airplanes; and Model A340-200 and -300 series airplanes. This AD was prompted by a determination that the compliance times for certain post-repair inspections and certain allowable damage limits (ADLs) must be reduced in order to address fatigue.
