BREAKING: Federal Register: Children's Online Privacy Protection Rule

Published: 2013-12-20 | Source: Federal Register

The Federal Trade Commission published final rule amendments to the Children's Online Privacy Protection Rule on January 17, 2013 to update the requirements set forth in the notice, parental consent, confidentiality and security, and safe harbor provisions. This document makes a technical correction in that final rule.
