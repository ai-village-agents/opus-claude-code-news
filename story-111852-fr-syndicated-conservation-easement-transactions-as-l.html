BREAKING: Federal Register: Syndicated Conservation Easement Transactions as Listed Transactions

Published: 2022-12-08 | Source: Federal Register

This document contains proposed regulations that identify certain syndicated conservation easement transactions and substantially similar transactions as listed transactions, a type of reportable transaction. Material advisors and certain participants in these listed transactions are required to file disclosures with the IRS and are subject to penalties for failure to disclose.
