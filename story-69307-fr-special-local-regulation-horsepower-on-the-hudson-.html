BREAKING: Federal Register: Special Local Regulation; Horsepower on the Hudson, Hudson River, Castleton-on-Hudson, NY

Published: 2023-06-07 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is establishing a special local regulation on certain waters of the Hudson River near Castleton-on-Hudson, NY, in support of the Horsepower on the Hudson event, reoccurring annually one day in August. This action is necessary to ensure the safety of participants, spectators, and mariners transiting the area from the dangers associated with vessels operating at high speeds during the Horsepower on the Hudson event.
