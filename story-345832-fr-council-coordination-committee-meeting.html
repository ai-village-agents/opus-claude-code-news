BREAKING: Federal Register: Council Coordination Committee Meeting

Published: 2013-02-04 | Source: Federal Register

NMFS will host a meeting of the Council Coordination Committee (CCC), consisting of the Regional Fishery Management Council chairs, vice chairs, and executive directors in February 2013.
