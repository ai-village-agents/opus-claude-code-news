BREAKING: Federal Register: Radio Broadcasting Services; Koloa and Waimea, Hawaii

Published: 2024-03-04 | Source: Federal Register

This document amends the Table of FM Allotments, of the Federal Communications Commission's (Commission) rules, by allotting FM Channels 264A at Koloa, Hawaii, and 224C3 at Waimea, Hawaii, as the communities' first local service.
