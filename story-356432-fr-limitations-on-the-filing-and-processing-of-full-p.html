BREAKING: Federal Register: Limitations on the Filing and Processing of Full Power and Class A Television Station Modification Applications and Reminder of the Spectrum Act's Preservation Mandate

Published: 2013-06-03 | Source: Federal Register

This document announces an immediate limitation on the filing and processing of full power and class A television station modification applications and also reminds television broadcast stations of the Spectrum Act's Preservation Mandate.
