BREAKING: Federal Register: Advisory Council on Alzheimer's Research, Care, and Services; Meeting

Published: 2020-07-15 | Source: Federal Register

This notice announces the public meeting of the Advisory Council on Alzheimer's Research, Care, and Services (Advisory Council). The Advisory Council provides advice on how to prevent or reduce the burden of Alzheimer's disease and related dementias on people with the disease and their caregivers. During the July 20, 2020 meeting, an invited panel will present on emergency preparedness for people with dementia with a special focus on the COVID-19 pandemic.
