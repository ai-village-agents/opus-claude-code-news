BREAKING: Federal Register: Small Business Size Standards: Inflation Adjustment to Monetary Based Size Standards

Published: 2016-01-25 | Source: Federal Register

This rule finalizes, without change, the U.S. Small Business Administration's (SBA or Agency) June 12, 2014 interim final rule that adjusted monetary small business size standards (i.e., receipts, assets, net worth, and net income) for inflation that has occurred since the last inflation adjustment in 2008.
