BREAKING: Federal Register: Finding of Failure To Submit State Implementation Plan Submissions for the 2012 Fine Particulate Matter National Ambient Air Quality Standards; California; Los Angeles-South Coast Air Basin

Published: 2024-01-30 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is taking final action to find that California has failed to submit state implementation plan (SIP) elements required under the Clean Air Act (CAA or "Act") to implement the 2012 national ambient air quality standards (NAAQS) for fine particulate matter (PM2.5) ("2012 PM2.5 NAAQS") in the Los Angeles-South Coast Air Basin ("South Coast").
