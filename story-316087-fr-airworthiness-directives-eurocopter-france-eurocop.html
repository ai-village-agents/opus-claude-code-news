BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Eurocopter France (Eurocopter) Helicopters

Published: 2014-02-07 | Source: Federal Register

We are adopting a new airworthiness directive (AD) for Eurocopter Model EC225LP and AS332L1 helicopters with emergency floatation gear. The AD requires, before the next overwater flight, inspecting the strap installation on the hinged rods of the emergency flotation gear on both rear cradles for correct installation.
