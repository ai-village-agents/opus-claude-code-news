BREAKING: Federal Register: Medical, Physical Readiness, Training, and Access Authorization Standards for Protective Force Personnel

Published: 2013-09-10 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Energy (DOE or Department) is amending its regulations governing the standards for medical, physical performance, training, and access authorizations for protective force (PF) personnel employed by contractors providing security services to the Department. Since the publication of the existing regulations in 1984, and particularly since 9/11, the DOE has totally transformed its approach to dealing with a much-evolved terrorist threat.
