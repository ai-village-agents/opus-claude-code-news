BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Inventory Completion: Seminole Tribe of Florida, Clewiston, FL

Published: 2016-12-16 | Source: Federal Register

The Seminole Tribe of Florida has completed an inventory of human remains, in consultation with the appropriate Indian tribes or Native Hawaiian organizations, and has determined that there is a cultural affiliation between the human remains and present-day Indian tribes or Native Hawaiian organizations.
