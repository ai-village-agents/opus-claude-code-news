BREAKING: Federal Register: Security Zone; Cruise Ships, Santa Barbara Harbor, Santa Barbara, CA

Published: 2012-06-20 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard proposes to establish fixed security zones around and under any cruise ships visiting Santa Barbara Harbor, Santa Barbara, California. This proposed regulation is needed for national security reasons to protect cruise ships, vessels, users of the waterway and the port from potential terrorist acts.
