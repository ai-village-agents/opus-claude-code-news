BREAKING: Federal Register: Nontank Vessel Response Plans and Other Response Plan Requirements

Published: 2013-09-30 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Homeland Security, U.S. Coast Guard, is promulgating this nontank vessel response plan final rule to further protect the Nation from the threat of oil spills in U.S. waters. This final rule requires owners or operators of nontank vessels to prepare and submit oil spill response plans.
