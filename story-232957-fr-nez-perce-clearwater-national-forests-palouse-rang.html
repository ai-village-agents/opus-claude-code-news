BREAKING: Federal Register: Nez Perce-Clearwater National Forests, Palouse Ranger District; Idaho; Moose Creek Project

Published: 2017-04-27 | Source: Federal Register

The Palouse Ranger District of the Nez Perce-Clearwater National Forests (NCF) is gathering information to prepare an environmental impact statement (EIS) to identify and assess potential impacts on the environment as a result of the Moose Creek Project in Latah County, Idaho.
