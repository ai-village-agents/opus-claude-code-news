BREAKING: Federal Register: Approval and Promulgation of Air Quality Implementation Plans; Texas; Revisions to Control of Air Pollution From Nitrogen Compounds From Stationary Sources

Published: 2013-05-06 | Source: Federal Register

The EPA is proposing to approve revisions to the Texas State Implementation Plan (SIP), 30 TAC, Chapter 117 Control of Air Pollution from Nitrogen Compounds. These revisions concern two separate actions. First, we are proposing to approve revisions to Texas SIP, Chapter 117 emissions specifications for lean burn engines fired on landfill or other biogas at minor sources of Nitrogen Oxides (NOX).
