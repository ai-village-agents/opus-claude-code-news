BREAKING: Federal Register: Determination That TOTECT (Dexrazoxane Hydrochloride) for Injection, 500 Milligrams, Was Not Withdrawn From Sale for Reasons of Safety or Effectiveness

Published: 2022-04-13 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA, Agency, or we) has determined that TOTECT (dexrazoxane hydrochloride) for injection, 500 milligrams (mg), was not withdrawn from sale for reasons of safety or effectiveness.
