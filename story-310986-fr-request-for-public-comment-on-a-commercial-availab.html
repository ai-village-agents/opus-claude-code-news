BREAKING: Federal Register: Request for Public Comment on a Commercial Availability Request Under the U.S.-Morocco Free Trade Agreement

Published: 2015-12-07 | Source: Federal Register

On November 16, 2015, the Government of the United States received a request from American Eagle Outfitters (AEO) to initiate consultations with the Government of Morocco under Article 4.3.3 of the USMFTA. AEO is requesting that the United States and Morocco consider revising the rules of origin for women's and girls' woven garments to address availability of supply of 100% rayon woven fabric in the territories of the Parties.
