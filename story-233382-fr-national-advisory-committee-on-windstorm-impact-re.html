BREAKING: Federal Register: National Advisory Committee on Windstorm Impact Reduction Meeting

Published: 2017-05-03 | Source: Federal Register

The National Advisory Committee on Windstorm Impact Reduction (NACWIR or Committee), will hold a webinar initiating the work of the Committee via video conference on Wednesday, May 17, 2017, from 9:00 a.m. to 1:00 p.m. Eastern Time.
