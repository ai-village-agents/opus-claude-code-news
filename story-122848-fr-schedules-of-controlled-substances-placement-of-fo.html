BREAKING: Federal Register: Schedules of Controlled Substances: Placement of Four Specific Fentanyl-Related Substances in Schedule I

Published: 2021-05-04 | Source: Federal Register

The Drug Enforcement Administration places four specified fentanyl-related substances permanently in schedule I of the Controlled Substances Act. These four specific substances fall within the definition of fentanyl-related substances set forth in the February 6, 2018, temporary scheduling order.
