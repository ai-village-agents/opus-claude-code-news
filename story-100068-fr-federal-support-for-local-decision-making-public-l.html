BREAKING: Federal Register: Federal Support for Local Decision-Making Public Listening Session

Published: 2022-07-08 | Source: Federal Register

This notice announces a public meeting hosted by the Bureau of Transportation Statistics (BTS) in support of the Infrastructure Investment and Jobs Act (IIJA). The meeting will be a facilitated listening session that will document data and analytical challenges local decision-makers face making informed infrastructure investments.
