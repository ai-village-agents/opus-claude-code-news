BREAKING: Federal Register: Air Plan Approval; Arizona; Maricopa County Air Quality Department

Published: 2021-06-23 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is proposing to approve revisions to the Maricopa County Air Quality Department's (MCAQD) Rule 510 as part of the Arizona State Implementation Plan (SIP). These rule revisions concern revisions to the maximum levels of ambient air pollution for the protection of public health and welfare.
