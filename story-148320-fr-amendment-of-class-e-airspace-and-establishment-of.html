BREAKING: Federal Register: Amendment of Class E Airspace and Establishment of Class E Airspace Extension; Battle Mountain, NV

Published: 2020-04-03 | Source: Federal Register

This action amends the Class E surface area, Class E airspace extending upward from 700 feet above the surface and creates Class E airspace as an extension to the Class E surface area at Battle Mountain Airport, Battle Mountain, NV.
