BREAKING: Federal Register: Small Business Investment Companies: Passive Business Expansion and Technical Clarifications

Published: 2016-12-28 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Small Business Administration (SBA) is revising the regulations for the Small Business Investment Company (SBIC) program to expand permitted investments in passive businesses and provide further clarification with regard to investments in such businesses. SBICs are generally prohibited from investing in passive businesses under the Small Business Investment Act of 1958, as amended (Act).
