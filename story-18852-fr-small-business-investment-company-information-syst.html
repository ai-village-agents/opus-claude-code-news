BREAKING: Federal Register: Small Business Investment Company Information System Modification

Published: 2025-08-25 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Small Business Administration (SBA) proposes to modify its system of records, titled Small Business Investment Company Information System (SBICIS) (SBA SBICS 40), to update its inventory of records systems subject to the Privacy Act of 1974, as amended. Publication of this notice complies with the Privacy Act and the Office of Management and Budget (OMB) Circular A-108 and Circular A-130.
