BREAKING: Federal Register: Reactive Power Requirements for Non-Synchronous Generation

Published: 2016-06-23 | Source: Federal Register

The Federal Energy Regulatory Commission (Commission) is eliminating the exemptions for wind generators from the requirement to provide reactive power by revising the pro forma Large Generator Interconnection Agreement (LGIA), Appendix G to the pro forma LGIA, and the pro forma Small Generator Interconnection Agreement (SGIA).
