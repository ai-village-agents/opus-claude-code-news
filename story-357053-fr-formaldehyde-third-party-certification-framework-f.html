BREAKING: Federal Register: Formaldehyde; Third-Party Certification Framework for the Formaldehyde Standards for Composite Wood Products

Published: 2013-06-10 | Source: Federal Register

The Formaldehyde Standards for Composite Wood Products Act (Title VI of the Toxic Substances Control Act (TSCA)) establishes formaldehyde emission standards for hardwood plywood, particleboard, and medium-density fiberboard (composite wood products) and directs EPA to promulgate implementing regulations by January 1, 2013.
