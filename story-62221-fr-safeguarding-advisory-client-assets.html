BREAKING: Federal Register: Safeguarding Advisory Client Assets

Published: 2023-03-09 | Source: Federal Register

The Securities and Exchange Commission ("Commission" or "SEC") is proposing a new rule under the Investment Advisers Act of 1940 ("Advisers Act" or "Act") to address how investment advisers safeguard client assets. To effect our redesignation of the current custody rule for the proposed new safeguarding rule, we are proposing to renumber the current rule.
