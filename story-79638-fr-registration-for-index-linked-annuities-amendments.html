BREAKING: Federal Register: Registration for Index-Linked Annuities; Amendments to Form N-4 for Index-Linked and Variable Annuities

Published: 2023-10-13 | Source: Federal Register

The Securities and Exchange Commission ("Commission") is proposing rule and form amendments to provide a tailored form to register the offerings of registered index-linked annuities ("RILAs"). Specifically, the Commission is proposing to amend the form currently used by most variable annuity separate accounts, Form N- 4, to require issuers of RILAs to register offerings on that form as well.
