BREAKING: Federal Register: Risk Weighting of High Volatility Commercial Real Estate (HVCRE) Exposures

Published: 2021-08-26 | Source: Federal Register

The Farm Credit Administration (FCA, we, or our) is seeking comments on this proposed rule that would revise our regulatory capital requirements for Farm Credit System (FCS or System) institutions to define and establish risk-weightings for High Volatility Commercial Real Estate (HVCRE) exposures.
