BREAKING: Federal Register: Truck and Bus Tires From the People's Republic of China: Antidumping Duty Order

Published: 2019-02-15 | Source: Federal Register

On November 1, 2018 the U.S. Court of International Trade (CIT) remanded the International Trade Commission's (ITC) final negative injury determination on truck and bus tires from the People's Republic of China (China). On January 30, 2019, the ITC filed its final remand determination, finding material injury to an industry in the United States by reason of imports of truck and bus tires from China.
