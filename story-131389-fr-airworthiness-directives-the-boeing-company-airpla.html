BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; The Boeing Company Airplanes

Published: 2021-08-24 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA proposes to adopt a new airworthiness directive (AD) for certain The Boeing Company Model 737-300, -400, and -500 series airplanes. This proposed AD was prompted by an evaluation by the design approval holder (DAH) indicating that the frame splice between certain stringers is subject to widespread fatigue damage (WFD).
