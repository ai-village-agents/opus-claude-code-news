BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone; Execpro Services Fireworks Display, Lake Tahoe, Incline Village, NV

Published: 2013-06-28 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is establishing a temporary safety zone in the navigable waters of Lake Tahoe near Incline Village, NV in support of the Execpro Services Fireworks Display on July 5, 2013. This safety zone is established to ensure the safety of participants and spectators from the dangers associated with the pyrotechnics.
