BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Inventory Completion: University of Denver Museum of Anthropology, Denver, CO

Published: 2021-02-24 | Source: Federal Register

The University of Denver Museum of Anthropology has completed an inventory of human remains, in consultation with the appropriate Indian Tribes or Native Hawaiian organizations, and has determined that there is a cultural affiliation between the human remains and present- day Indian Tribes or Native Hawaiian organizations.
