BREAKING: Federal Register: Evaluation of State Coastal Management Programs

Published: 2015-04-16 | Source: Federal Register

The NOAA Office for Coastal Management announces its intent to evaluate the performance of the Puerto Rico Coastal Zone Management Program. Coastal Zone Management Program evaluations are conducted pursuant to section 312 of the Coastal Zone Management Act of 1972, as amended (CZMA) and regulations at 15 CFR part 923, subpart L.
