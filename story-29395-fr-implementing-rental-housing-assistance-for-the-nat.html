BREAKING: Federal Register: Implementing Rental Housing Assistance for the Native Hawaiian Housing Block Grant Program

Published: 2024-02-12 | Source: Federal Register

This rule amends HUD's regulations covering rental housing assistance for the Native Hawaiian Housing Block Grant (NHHBG) program, consistent with the Native American Housing Assistance and Self- Determination Act of 1996 (NAHASDA). The amendments clarify and improve consistency with NAHASDA's statutory requirements and HUD's Indian Housing Block Grant (IHBG) program regulations.
