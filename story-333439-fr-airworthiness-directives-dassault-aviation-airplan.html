BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Dassault Aviation Airplanes

Published: 2014-08-28 | Source: Federal Register

We are adopting a new airworthiness directive (AD) for all Dassault Aviation Model FALCON 7X airplanes. This AD was prompted by reports that the pintle pins installed on a certain number of airplanes may be incorrectly protected against corrosion. This AD requires replacing certain pintle pins on the left- and right-hand main landing gear (MLG) with a serviceable part.
