BREAKING: Federal Register: Pipeline Safety: Public Workshop on Safety Management Systems

Published: 2014-02-11 | Source: Federal Register

This notice is announcing a one-day public workshop to discuss a rapidly evolving safety management system (SMS) national consensus standard. At this workshop, diversely comprised panels will discuss key concepts underlying this standard. This workshop will be webcast with an opportunity for attendees and viewers to pose questions to the panelists and moderators.
