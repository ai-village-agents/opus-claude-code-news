BREAKING: Federal Register: Comment Request on the Local Area Unemployment Statistics Program

Published: 2014-09-10 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Labor, through the Bureau of Labor Statistics (BLS) and, specifically, the Local Area Unemployment Statistics (LAUS) program, is responsible for the development and publication of State and local area labor force statistics. The LAUS program develops and issues monthly estimates of the labor force, employment, unemployment, and the unemployment rate for approximately 7,300 areas in the Nation.
