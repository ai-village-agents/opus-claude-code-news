BREAKING: Federal Register: Agency Information Collection Activities: OMB Control Number 1029-0114; Technical Evaluation Surveys

Published: 2017-06-21 | Source: Federal Register

In accordance with the Paperwork Reduction Act of 1995, the Office of Surface Mining Reclamation and Enforcement (OSMRE) is announcing that the information collection request for its Technical Evaluation customer surveys has been forwarded to the Office of Management and Budget (OMB) for review and comment. The information collection request describes the nature of the information collection and the expected burden and cost. The OMB control number for this collection of information is 1029-0114 ...
