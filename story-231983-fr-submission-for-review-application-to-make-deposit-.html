BREAKING: Federal Register: Submission for Review: Application To Make Deposit or Redeposit (CSRS), Standard Form 2803, and Application To Make Service Credit Payment for Civilian Service (FERS), Standard Form 3108

Published: 2017-04-13 | Source: Federal Register

The Retirement Services, Office of Personnel Management (OPM) offers the general public and other Federal agencies the opportunity to comment on an extension without change of a currently approved information collection (ICR), Application to Make Deposit or Redeposit (CSRS) Standard Form (SF) 2808 and Application to Make Service Credit Payment for Civilian Service (FERS), SF 3108.
