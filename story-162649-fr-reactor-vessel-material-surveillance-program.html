BREAKING: Federal Register: Reactor Vessel Material Surveillance Program

Published: 2020-10-02 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Nuclear Regulatory Commission (NRC) is proposing to amend the reactor vessel material surveillance program requirements for commercial light-water power reactors. This action would amend the requirements associated with the testing of specimens contained within surveillance capsules and reporting the surveillance test results.
