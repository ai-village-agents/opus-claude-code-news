BREAKING: Federal Register: Cyber Security Programs for Nuclear Power Reactors

Published: 2023-02-13 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Nuclear Regulatory Commission (NRC) is issuing Revision 1 to Regulatory Guide (RG) 5.71, "Cyber Security Programs for Nuclear Power Reactors." Revision 1 incorporates references to industry guidance on identifying and protecting critical digital assets for safety-related, important to safety, balance of plant, and emergency preparedness equipment.
