BREAKING: Federal Register: Commercial Fishing Safety Advisory Committee

Published: 2018-02-23 | Source: Federal Register

The Commercial Fishing Safety Advisory Committee (CFSAC) will meet via teleconference to discuss the Regulatory Reform tasking efforts (CFSAC task statement #01-17) and may take action to submit their report to the United States Coast Guard. The teleconference will be open to the public.
