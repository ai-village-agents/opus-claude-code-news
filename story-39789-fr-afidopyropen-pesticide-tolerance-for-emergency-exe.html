BREAKING: Federal Register: Afidopyropen; Pesticide Tolerance for Emergency Exemption

Published: 2024-06-20 | Source: Federal Register

This regulation establishes a time-limited tolerance for residues of afidopyropen, including its metabolites and degradates, in or on strawberry. This action is in response to EPA's granting of an emergency exemption under the Federal Insecticide, Fungicide, and Rodenticide Act (FIFRA) authorizing use of the pesticide on field-grown strawberry.
