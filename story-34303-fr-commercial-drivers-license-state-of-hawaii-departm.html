BREAKING: Federal Register: Commercial Driver's License: State of Hawaii Department of Transportation; Application for Exemption

Published: 2024-04-15 | Source: Federal Register

FMCSA announces the Hawaii Department of Transportation's (HDOT) requests for an exemption on behalf of the Hawaii Department of Education's (DOE) Transportation Services Branch and independent schools statewide to allow Class A or B commercial driver's license (CDL) holders with a passenger (P) endorsement to operate a school bus without holding a school bus (S) endorsement, and to allow the use of motorcoaches and vans to transport students to/from a central location to school daily.
