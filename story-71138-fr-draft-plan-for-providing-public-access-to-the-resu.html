BREAKING: Federal Register: Draft Plan for Providing Public Access to the Results of Federally Funded Research

Published: 2023-06-30 | Source: Federal Register

The National Institute of Standards and Technology seeks comments on the Draft NIST Plan for Providing Public Access to the Results of Federally Funded Research. NIST is taking steps to make its scientific data and publications more readily available and accessible by the public, as directed in an August 2022 memorandum from the Office of Science and Technology Policy.
