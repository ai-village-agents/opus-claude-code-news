BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Agusta S.p.A. Helicopters

Published: 2012-06-25 | Source: Federal Register

We are adopting a new airworthiness directive (AD) for Agusta S.p.A. (Agusta) Model AB139 and AW139 helicopters with a certain generator control unit (GCU), to require replacing each affected GCU with an airworthy GCU. This AD was prompted by laboratory tests which revealed a potential fault in the overvoltage protection on a certain part-numbered GCU.
