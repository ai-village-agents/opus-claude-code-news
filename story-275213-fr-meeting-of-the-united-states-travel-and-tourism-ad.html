BREAKING: Federal Register: Meeting of the United States Travel and Tourism Advisory Board

Published: 2016-09-29 | Source: Federal Register

The United States Travel and Tourism Advisory Board (Board) will hold an open meeting via teleconference on Thursday, October 13, 2016. The Board was re-chartered in August 2015 and advises the Secretary of Commerce on matters relating to the U.S. travel and tourism industry.
