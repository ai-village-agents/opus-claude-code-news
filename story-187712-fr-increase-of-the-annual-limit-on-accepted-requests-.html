BREAKING: Federal Register: Increase of the Annual Limit on Accepted Requests for Track I Prioritized Examination

Published: 2019-09-03 | Source: Federal Register

The Leahy-Smith America Invents Act (America Invents Act) includes provisions for prioritized examination of patent applications, which have been implemented by the United States Patent and Trademark Office (USPTO or Office) in previous rulemakings. The America Invents Act provides that the Office may not accept more than 10,000 requests for prioritization in any fiscal year (October 1 to September 30) until regulations are prescribed setting another limit.
