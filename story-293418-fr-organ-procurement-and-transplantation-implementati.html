BREAKING: Federal Register: Organ Procurement and Transplantation: Implementation of the HIV Organ Policy Equity Act

Published: 2015-05-08 | Source: Federal Register

This final rule amends the regulations implementing the National Organ Transplant Act of 1984, as amended, (NOTA) pursuant to statutory requirements of the HIV Organ Policy Equity Act (HOPE Act), enacted in 2013.
