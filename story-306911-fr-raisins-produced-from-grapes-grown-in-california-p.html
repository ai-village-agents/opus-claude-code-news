BREAKING: Federal Register: Raisins Produced From Grapes Grown in California; Proposed Amendments to Marketing Order

Published: 2015-10-16 | Source: Federal Register

This proposed rule invites public comments on proposed amendments to Marketing Order No. 989, which regulates the handling of raisins produced from grapes grown in California.
