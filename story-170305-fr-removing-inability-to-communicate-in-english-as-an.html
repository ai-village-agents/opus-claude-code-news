BREAKING: Federal Register: Removing Inability To Communicate in English as an Education Category

Published: 2019-02-01 | Source: Federal Register

We propose to eliminate the education category "inability to communicate in English" when we evaluate disability claims for adults under titles II and XVI of the Social Security Act (Act). Changes in the national workforce since we added this category to our rules in 1978 demonstrate that this education category is no longer a reliable indicator of an individual's educational attainment or the vocational impact of an individual's education.
