BREAKING: Federal Register: Expanding the Fair Housing Testing Pool for FHIP and FHAP Funded Entities

Published: 2024-04-03 | Source: Federal Register

Through this final rule, HUD eliminates the restrictions for Fair Housing Initiatives Program (FHIP) grantees and for Fair Housing Assistance Program (FHAP) agencies that currently bar FHIP and FHAP funded entities from using HUD funds to deploy fair housing testers with prior felony convictions or convictions of crimes involving fraud or perjury.
