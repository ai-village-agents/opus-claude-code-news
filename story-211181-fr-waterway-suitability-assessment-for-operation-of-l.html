BREAKING: Federal Register: Waterway Suitability Assessment for Operation of Liquefied Natural Gas Terminal; Cameron, LA

Published: 2018-07-09 | Source: Federal Register

We are requesting your comments on a Letter of Intent and Preliminary Waterway Suitability Assessment we received from Sabine Pass LNG, L.P. (SPLNG) regarding SPLNG's plans to construct a new berth at its Cameron Parish, LA facility and to increase the number of liquefied natural gas vessels calling at the facility from approximately 400 to 580 annually.
