BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Filing of Plats of Surveys; Eastern States

Published: 2019-12-18 | Source: Federal Register

The plats of survey of the following described lands are scheduled to be officially filed in the Bureau of land Management (BLM), Eastern States Office, Washington, DC, 30 days from the date of this publication. The surveys, executed at the request of the identified agencies, are required for the management of these lands.
