BREAKING: Federal Register: Chemical Management and Permissible Exposure Limits (PELs)

Published: 2014-10-10 | Source: Federal Register

OSHA is reviewing its overall approach to managing chemical exposures in the workplace and seeks stakeholder input about more effective and efficient approaches that addresses challenges found with the current regulatory approach. This review involves considering issues related to updating permissible exposure limits (PELs), as well as examining other strategies that could be implemented to address workplace conditions where workers are exposed to chemicals.
