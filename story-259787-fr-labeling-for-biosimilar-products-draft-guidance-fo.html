BREAKING: Federal Register: Labeling for Biosimilar Products; Draft Guidance for Industry; Availability

Published: 2016-04-04 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA or Agency) is announcing the availability of a draft guidance for industry entitled "Labeling for Biosimilar Products." This draft guidance is intended to assist applicants in developing draft prescription drug labeling for proposed biosimilar products. The recommendations for prescription drug labeling in this guidance pertain only to the prescribing information (commonly referred to as the package insert).
