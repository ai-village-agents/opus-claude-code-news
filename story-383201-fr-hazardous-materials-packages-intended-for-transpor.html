BREAKING: Federal Register: Hazardous Materials; Packages Intended for Transport by Aircraft

Published: 2012-04-16 | Source: Federal Register

PHMSA is amending the Hazardous Materials Regulations to require closures of inner packagings containing liquids within a combination packaging intended for transportation by aircraft to be secured by a secondary means or, where a secondary closure cannot be applied or it is impracticable to apply, permit the use of a leakproof liner.
