BREAKING: Federal Register: Special Local Regulation; San Jacinto River, Houston, TX

Published: 2024-09-26 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is amending its regulations for annual marine events in the Sector Houston-Galveston area of responsibility. This rulemaking establishes a special local regulation to provide for the safety of life on certain waters of the San Jacinto River, in Houston, TX and will be enforced annually during a high-speed boat race on the first or second Saturday in March.
