BREAKING: Federal Register: DOL Acquisition Regulation: Department of Labor Acquisition Regulation System

Published: 2024-08-16 | Source: Federal Register

With this final rule, the Department of Labor (DOL) is revising the Department of Labor Acquisition Regulation (DOLAR) to remove provisions from the regulation that were redundant or obsolete. The final rule also codifies the use of certain contractual provisions that DOL has developed and deployed in recent years.
