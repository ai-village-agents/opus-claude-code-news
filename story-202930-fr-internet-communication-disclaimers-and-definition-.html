BREAKING: Federal Register: Internet Communication Disclaimers and Definition of “Public Communication”

Published: 2018-03-26 | Source: Federal Register

The Federal Election Commission requests comment on two alternative proposals to amend its regulations concerning disclaimers on public communications on the internet that contain express advocacy, solicit contributions, or are made by political committees.
