BREAKING: Federal Register: Hazardous Materials: Revision of Maximum and Minimum Civil Penalties

Published: 2016-06-29 | Source: Federal Register

PHMSA is revising the maximum and minimum civil penalties for a knowing violation of the Federal hazardous material transportation law or a regulation, order, special permit, or approval issued under that law.
