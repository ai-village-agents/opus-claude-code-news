BREAKING: Federal Register: Contracts and Provider Agreements for State Home Nursing Home Care

Published: 2012-12-06 | Source: Federal Register

This interim final rule amends Department of Veterans Affairs (VA) regulations to allow VA to enter into contracts or provider agreements with State homes for the nursing home care of certain disabled veterans. This rulemaking is required to implement a change in law that revises how VA will pay for care provided to these veterans and authorizes VA to use provider agreements to pay for such care.
