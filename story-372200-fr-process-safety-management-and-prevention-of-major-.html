BREAKING: Federal Register: Process Safety Management and Prevention of Major Chemical Accidents

Published: 2013-12-09 | Source: Federal Register

In response to Executive Order 13650, OSHA requests comment on potential revisions to its Process Safety Management (PSM) standard and its Explosives and Blasting Agents standard, potential updates to its Flammable Liquids standard and Spray Finishing standard, and potential changes to PSM enforcement policies.
