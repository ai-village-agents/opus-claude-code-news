BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Eurocopter France Helicopters

Published: 2013-04-15 | Source: Federal Register

We propose to adopt a new airworthiness directive (AD) for Eurocopter France (Eurocopter) Model EC120B and EC130B4 helicopters with certain emergency flotation gear (float) installed. This proposed AD would require inspecting the float for chafing of the fabric covering and adding protectors to the float installation to prevent contact between the float and the protruding sections of the installation.
