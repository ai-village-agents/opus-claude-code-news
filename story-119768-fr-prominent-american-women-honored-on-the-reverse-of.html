BREAKING: Federal Register: Prominent American Women Honored on the Reverse of Quarter-Dollar Coins-Request for Recommendations

Published: 2021-03-25 | Source: Federal Register

The Circulating Collectible Coin Redesign Act of 2020 directs the Secretary of the Treasury (Secretary) to redesign and issue quarter-dollar coins that feature designs on the reverse emblematic of the accomplishments of a prominent American woman (Program).
