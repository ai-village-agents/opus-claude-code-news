BREAKING: Federal Register: Louisiana: Final Approval of State Underground Storage Tank Program Revisions and Incorporation by Reference

Published: 2020-06-04 | Source: Federal Register

Pursuant to the Resource Conservation and Recovery Act (RCRA or Act), the Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is proposing to approve revisions to the State of Louisiana Underground Storage Tank (UST) program submitted by the State. This action is based on EPA's determination that these revisions satisfy all requirements needed for program approval.
