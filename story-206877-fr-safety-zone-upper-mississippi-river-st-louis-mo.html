BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone; Upper Mississippi River, St. Louis, MO

Published: 2018-05-14 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is establishing a temporary safety zone for all navigable waters on the Upper Mississippi River from mile marker (MM) 179 to MM 179.5, extending the entire width of the river, near St. Louis, MO. This safety zone is necessary to protect persons, vessels, and the marine environment from potential hazards that could occur while emergency work is completed on new power lines extending across the river.
