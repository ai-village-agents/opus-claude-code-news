BREAKING: Federal Register: Removal of Regulated Navigation Areas Within District 5

Published: 2023-02-03 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is updating District 5 regulations to remove two regulated navigation areas in Captain of the Port Zone (COTP) North Carolina within District 5 that are no longer needed. These areas were created to address the impacts of extreme shoaling in the Oregon Inlet, but subsequent Army Corps of Engineers dredging activities have alleviated the issue.
