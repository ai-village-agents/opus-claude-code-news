BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Diamond Aircraft Industries GmbH Airplanes

Published: 2021-12-23 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA proposes to supersede Airworthiness Directive (AD) 2017-18-10, which applies to certain Diamond Aircraft Industries GmbH (DAI) Model DA 42, DA 42 M-NG, and DA 42 NG airplanes. AD 2017-18-10 requires modifying the flap control system, repetitively inspecting the flap bell crank, and replacing the flap bell crank as necessary.
