BREAKING: Federal Register: Amendment of Class D and Class E Airspace; Elkhart and Goshen, IN

Published: 2025-06-27 | Source: Federal Register

This action proposes to amend the Class D and Class E airspace at Elkhart, IN, and the Class E airspace at Goshen, IN. The geographic coordinates of the Elkhart Municipal Airport, Elkhart, IN, and the Goshen Municipal Airport, Goshen, IN, would also be updated to coincide with the FAA's aeronautical database.
