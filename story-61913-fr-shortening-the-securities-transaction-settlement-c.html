BREAKING: Federal Register: Shortening the Securities Transaction Settlement Cycle

Published: 2023-03-06 | Source: Federal Register

The Securities and Exchange Commission ("Commission") is adopting rule amendments to shorten the standard settlement cycle for most broker-dealer transactions from two business days after the trade date ("T+2") to one business day after the trade date ("T+1"). In addition, the Commission is adopting new rules related to the processing of institutional trades by broker-dealers and certain clearing agencies.
