BREAKING: Federal Register: Aviation Rulemaking Advisory Committee-New Task

Published: 2012-02-23 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA has withdrawn a task assigned to the Aviation Rulemaking Advisory Committee (ARAC) concerning commercial air tours. This notice is to inform the public of the FAA's decision to withdraw this task.
