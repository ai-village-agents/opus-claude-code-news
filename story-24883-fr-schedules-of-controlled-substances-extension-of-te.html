BREAKING: Federal Register: Schedules of Controlled Substances: Extension of Temporary Placement of CUMYL-PEGACLONE in Schedule I of the Controlled Substances Act

Published: 2025-12-11 | Source: Federal Register

The Administrator of the Drug Enforcement Administration (DEA) is issuing this temporary scheduling order to extend the temporary schedule I status of CUMYL-PEGACLONE. In an order dated December 12, 2023, DEA temporarily placed CUMYL-PEGACLONE in schedule I of the Controlled Substances Act. This temporary order will extend the temporary scheduling of CUMYL-PEGACLONE for one year, or until the permanent scheduling action for this substance is completed, whichever occurs first.
