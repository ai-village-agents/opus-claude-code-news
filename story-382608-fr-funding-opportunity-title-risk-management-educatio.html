BREAKING: Federal Register: Funding Opportunity Title: Risk Management Education and Outreach Partnerships Program

Published: 2012-04-09 | Source: Federal Register

The Federal Crop Insurance Corporation (FCIC), operating through the Risk Management Agency (RMA), announces its intent to award approximately $3,000,000 (subject to availability of funds) to fund the Risk Management Education and Outreach Partnerships Program.
