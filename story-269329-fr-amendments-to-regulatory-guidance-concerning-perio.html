BREAKING: Federal Register: Amendments to Regulatory Guidance Concerning Periodic Inspection of Commercial Motor Vehicles

Published: 2016-07-22 | Source: Federal Register

FMCSA amends regulatory guidance, previously published in the Federal Register, regarding the periodic inspection of commercial motor vehicles (CMVs). Elsewhere in today's issue of the Federal Register, FMCSA amends the Federal Motor Carrier Safety Regulations (FMCSRs) to, among other things, eliminate the option for a motor carrier to satisfy the periodic (annual) inspection requirement through a violation-free roadside inspection.
