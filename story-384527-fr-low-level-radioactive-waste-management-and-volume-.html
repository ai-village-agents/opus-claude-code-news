BREAKING: Federal Register: Low-Level Radioactive Waste Management and Volume Reduction

Published: 2012-05-01 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Nuclear Regulatory Commission (NRC or the Commission) is revising its 1981 Policy Statement on Low-Level Radioactive Waste (LLRW) Volume Reduction (Policy Statement). This statement encouraged licensees to take steps to reduce the amount of waste generated and to reduce the volume of waste once generated.
