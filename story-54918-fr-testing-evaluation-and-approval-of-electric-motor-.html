BREAKING: Federal Register: Testing, Evaluation, and Approval of Electric Motor-Driven Mine Equipment and Accessories

Published: 2024-12-10 | Source: Federal Register

The Mine Safety and Health Administration (MSHA) is revising its regulations that set out the testing, evaluation, and approval requirements for electric motor-driven mine equipment and accessories intended for use in gassy mines.
