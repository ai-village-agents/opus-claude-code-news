BREAKING: Federal Register: Human Gene Therapy for Retinal Disorders; Draft Guidance for Industry; Availability

Published: 2018-07-12 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA or Agency) is announcing the availability of a draft document entitled "Human Gene Therapy for Retinal Disorders; Draft Guidance for Industry." The draft guidance provides recommendations to stakeholders developing human gene therapy (GT) products for retinal disorders affecting adult and pediatric patients.
