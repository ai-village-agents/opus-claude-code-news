BREAKING: Federal Register: U.S. Standards for Grades of Shelled Walnuts and Walnuts in the Shell

Published: 2017-08-22 | Source: Federal Register

This rule revises the U.S. Standards for Grades of Shelled Walnuts and the U.S. Standards for Grades of Walnuts in the Shell issued under the Agricultural Marketing Agreement Act of 1946. The Agricultural Marketing Service (AMS) of the Department of Agriculture (USDA) is amending the color requirements to include red-colored walnuts.
