BREAKING: Federal Register: Perchloroethylene (PCE); Regulation Under the Toxic Substances Control Act (TSCA)

Published: 2023-06-16 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is proposing to address the unreasonable risk of injury to human health presented by perchloroethylene (PCE) under its conditions of use as documented in EPA's December 2020 Risk Evaluation for PCE and December 2022 revised risk determination for PCE prepared under the Toxic Substances Control Act (TSCA).
