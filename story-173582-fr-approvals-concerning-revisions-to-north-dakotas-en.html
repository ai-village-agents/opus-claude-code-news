BREAKING: Federal Register: Approvals Concerning Revisions to North Dakota's Environmental Protection Programs; Delay of Effective Dates

Published: 2019-03-07 | Source: Federal Register

Due to unforeseen delays resulting from the lapse in appropriations for the Environmental Protection Agency (EPA), the EPA's final approvals concerning revisions to North Dakota's environmental protection programs that have the effect of transferring authority from the North Dakota Department of Health (NDDH) to the newly-created North Dakota Department of Environmental Quality (NDDEQ) are delayed until April 30, 2019.
