BREAKING: Federal Register: Draft Core Toll Concessions Public Private Partnership Model Contract Guide

Published: 2014-02-06 | Source: Federal Register

The Moving Ahead for Progress in the 21st Century Act (MAP-21) requires DOT and FHWA to develop public-private partnership (P3) transaction model contracts for the most popular types of P3s for transportation projects. Based on public input on the most prevalent P3 transaction type and the need for an educational, rather than prescriptive contract model, the FHWA has developed a draft Core Toll Concession Model Contract Guide.
