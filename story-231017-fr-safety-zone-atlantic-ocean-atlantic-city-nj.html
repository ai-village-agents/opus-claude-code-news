BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone; Atlantic Ocean, Atlantic City, NJ

Published: 2017-04-03 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard will enforce the Thunder Over the Boardwalk Air show special local regulation from 11 a.m. through 3:30 p.m. on August 22-23, 2017. This action is necessary to ensure safety of life on the navigable waters of the United States immediately prior to, during, and immediately after this air show.
