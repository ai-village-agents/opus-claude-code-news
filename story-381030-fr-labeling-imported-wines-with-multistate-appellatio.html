BREAKING: Federal Register: Labeling Imported Wines With Multistate Appellations

Published: 2012-03-22 | Source: Federal Register

The Alcohol and Tobacco Tax and Trade Bureau is amending the wine labeling regulations to allow the labeling of imported wines with multistate appellations of origin. This amendment provides treatment for imported wines similar to that currently available to domestic wines bearing multistate appellations. It also provides consumers with additional information regarding the origin of these wines.
