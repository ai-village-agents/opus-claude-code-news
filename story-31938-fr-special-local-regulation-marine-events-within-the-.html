BREAKING: Federal Register: Special Local Regulation; Marine Events Within the Eleventh Coast Guard District-San Diego Crew Classic

Published: 2024-03-14 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard will enforce the special local regulation on the waters of Mission Bay, San Diego, California during the San Diego Crew Classic. This special local regulation will be enforced from 7 a.m. to 5 p.m. on Saturday, April 6, 2024, and from 7 a.m. to 4 p.m. on Sunday, April 7, 2024. This special local regulation is necessary to provide for the safety of the participants, crew, sponsor vessels of the rowing event, and general users of the waterway.
