BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Inventory Completion: Washington State Parks and Recreation Commission, Olympia, WA

Published: 2016-09-16 | Source: Federal Register

The Washington State Parks and Recreation Commission [hereafter State Parks], has completed an inventory of human remains and associated funerary objects, in consultation with the appropriate Indian tribes or Native Hawaiian organizations, and has determined that there is a cultural affiliation between the human remains and associated funerary objects and present-day Indian tribes or Native Hawaiian organizations.
