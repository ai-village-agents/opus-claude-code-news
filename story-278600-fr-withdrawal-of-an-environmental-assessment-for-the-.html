BREAKING: Federal Register: Withdrawal of an Environmental Assessment for the Field Release of Genetically Engineered Diamondback Moths

Published: 2016-11-08 | Source: Federal Register

We are advising the public that we are withdrawing an environmental assessment that was prepared by the Animal and Plant Health Inspection Service relative to a permitted environmental release of diamondback moths which have been genetically engineered for repressible female lethality and to express red fluorescence as a marker.
