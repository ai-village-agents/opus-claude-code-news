BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice To Establish the Transforming Transportation Advisory Committee (TTAC)

Published: 2022-07-20 | Source: Federal Register

The Office of the Secretary of Transportation (OST) announces the establishment of the Transforming Transportation Advisory Committee (TTAC). The Secretary has determined that establishing TTAC is necessary and is in the public interest.
