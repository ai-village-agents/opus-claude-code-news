BREAKING: Federal Register: Pacific Whiting; Advisory Panel

Published: 2017-07-12 | Source: Federal Register

NMFS is soliciting nominations for appointments to the United States Advisory Panel (AP) established in the Agreement between the Government of the United States of America and the Government of Canada on Pacific Hake/Whiting (Pacific Whiting Treaty). Nominations are being sought to fill one position on the AP for a four year term beginning in 2018.
