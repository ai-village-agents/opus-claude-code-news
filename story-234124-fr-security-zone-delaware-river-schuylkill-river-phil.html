BREAKING: Federal Register: Security Zone; Delaware River, Schuylkill River; Philadelphia, PA

Published: 2017-05-15 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard proposes to establish a permanent security zone within portions of the Delaware River, and Schuylkill River, for the protection of Very Important Persons (VIP) who arrive and depart from Philadelphia International Airport, Philadelphia, PA. This rule is necessary to expedite the establishment and enforcement of the security zones when short notice is received by the Coast Guard for VIP(s) traveling to the Philadelphia area.
