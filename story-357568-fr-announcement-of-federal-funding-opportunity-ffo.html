BREAKING: Federal Register: Announcement of Federal Funding Opportunity (FFO)

Published: 2013-06-17 | Source: Federal Register

This notice announces a federal funding opportunity (FFO) to obtain funding from the DoD OEA for community planning assistance and economic diversification in response to reductions or cancellations in Department of Defense (DoD) spending. Assistance may be granted if the reduction has a direct and significant adverse impact on a community or its residents.
