BREAKING: Federal Register: Capital and Financial Reporting Requirements for Swap Dealers and Major Swap Participants

Published: 2024-01-16 | Source: Federal Register

The Commodity Futures Trading Commission ("Commission" or "CFTC") proposes to amend certain of the Commission's regulations that impose minimum capital requirements and financial reporting obligations on swap dealers ("SDs") and major swap participants ("MSPs").
