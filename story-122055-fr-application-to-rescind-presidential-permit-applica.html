BREAKING: Federal Register: Application To Rescind Presidential Permit; Application for Presidential Permit; Versant Power

Published: 2021-04-23 | Source: Federal Register

Versant Power (formerly Emera Maine, legacy of Maine Public Service Company and Bangor Hydro-Electric Company) has filed an application to effect a voluntary transfer of Presidential Permit No. PP-89-2 to its name, via the Department of Energy's (DOE) rescission and simultaneous reissuance of the permit.
