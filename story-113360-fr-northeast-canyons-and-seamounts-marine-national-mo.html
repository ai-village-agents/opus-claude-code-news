BREAKING: Federal Register: Northeast Canyons and Seamounts Marine National Monument; Proposed Joint Monument Management Plan

Published: 2022-12-28 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Fish and Wildlife Service and the National Oceanic and Atmospheric Administration intend to prepare a draft monument management plan for the Northeast Canyons and Seamounts Marine National Monument, which was established by Presidential Proclamation 9496 and updated by Presidential Proclamation 10287.
