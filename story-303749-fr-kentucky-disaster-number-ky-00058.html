BREAKING: Federal Register: Kentucky Disaster Number KY-00058

Published: 2015-09-10 | Source: Federal Register

This is an amendment of the Presidential declaration of a major disaster for the State of Kentucky (FEMA--4239--DR), dated 08/12/ 2015. Incident: Severe Storms, Tornadoes, Straight-line Winds, Flooding, Landslides, and Mudslides. Incident Period: 07/11/2015 through 07/20/2015. Effective Date: 08/28/2015. Physical Loan Application Deadline Date: 10/12/2015. EIDL Loan Application Deadline Date: 05/12/2016.
