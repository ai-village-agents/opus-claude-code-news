BREAKING: Federal Register: American Indian Probate Regulations

Published: 2021-01-07 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of the Interior (Department) is updating regulations governing probate of property that the United States holds in trust or restricted status for American Indians. Since the regulations were last revised in 2008, the Department identified opportunities for improving the probate process.
