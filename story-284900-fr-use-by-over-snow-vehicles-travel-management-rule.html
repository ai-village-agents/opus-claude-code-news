BREAKING: Federal Register: Use By Over-Snow Vehicles (Travel Management Rule)

Published: 2015-01-28 | Source: Federal Register

The Forest Service manages winter uses to protect National Forest System (NFS) resources and to provide a range of opportunities for motorized and non-motorized recreation. In 2005, the agency regulated winter motorized use as a discretionary activity under its regulations for Use by Over-Snow Vehicles.
