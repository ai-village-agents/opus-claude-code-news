BREAKING: Federal Register: North American Free Trade Agreement (NAFTA), Article 1904; Binational Panel Review: Notice of NAFTA Panel Decision

Published: 2020-12-16 | Source: Federal Register

On December 7, 2020, a NAFTA Binational Panel issued its Final Decision in the matter of Ammonium Sulphate from the United States of America (Determination on Remand). The Binational Panel remanded the Secretaria de Economia's (Economia) Determination on Remand and ordered Economia to issue a redetermination within 90 days.
