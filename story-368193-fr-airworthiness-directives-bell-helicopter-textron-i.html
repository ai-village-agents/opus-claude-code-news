BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Bell Helicopter Textron, Inc. (Bell) Helicopters

Published: 2013-10-25 | Source: Federal Register

We are superseding Airworthiness Directive (AD) 2009-05-09 for Bell Model 412, 412CF, and 412EP helicopters. AD 2009-05-09 required reidentifying each affected part-numbered main rotor yoke (yoke) on its data plate, reducing the retirement life of the reidentified yoke, and revising the Airworthiness Limitations section of the maintenance manual or the Instructions for Continued Airworthiness (ICAs) accordingly.
