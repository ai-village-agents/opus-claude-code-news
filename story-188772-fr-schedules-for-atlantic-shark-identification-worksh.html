BREAKING: Federal Register: Schedules for Atlantic Shark Identification Workshops and Safe Handling, Release, and Identification Workshops; Correction

Published: 2019-09-16 | Source: Federal Register

NMFS announces that the date for the Safe Handling, Release, and Identification workshop originally scheduled for September 4, 2019, in Warwick, RI, has been changed to September 23, 2019. The date is being changed because inclement weather resulting from Hurricane Dorian prevented the instructors from traveling safely to the original workshop.
