BREAKING: Federal Register: Approval and Promulgation of Air Quality Implementation Plans; Delaware; Low Emission Vehicle Program

Published: 2015-10-14 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) proposes to approve the State Implementation Plan (SIP) revision submitted by the State of Delaware for the purpose of approving Delaware's adopted Low Emission Vehicle (LEV) Program. This program requires that new passenger cars, light-duty trucks, and medium-duty highway vehicles titled in Delaware meet stringent California emission standards in lieu of Federal emission standards.
