BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Renewal of the Civil Nuclear Trade Advisory Committee

Published: 2012-11-06 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Commerce (the Department) International Trade Administration is giving notice that the charter for the Civil Nuclear Trade Advisory Committee (CINTAC) has been renewed as of September 16, 2012.
