BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Weatherly Aircraft Company

Published: 2020-05-11 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA is adopting a new airworthiness directive (AD) for all Weatherly Aircraft Company (Weatherly) Models 201, 201A, 201B, 201C, 620, 620A, 620B, 620B-TG, and 620TP airplanes. This AD was prompted by reports of fatigue cracking of the center wing and outer wing spar hinge brackets due to corrosion pitting.
