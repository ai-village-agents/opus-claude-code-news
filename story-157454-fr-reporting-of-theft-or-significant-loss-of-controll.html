BREAKING: Federal Register: Reporting of Theft or Significant Loss of Controlled Substances

Published: 2020-07-29 | Source: Federal Register

This proposed rule would amend Drug Enforcement Administration (DEA) regulations regarding DEA Form 106, used by DEA registrants to report thefts or significant losses of controlled substances, to clarify that all such forms must be submitted electronically. In addition, the proposed rule would add new requirements for the form to be submitted accurately and within a 15-day time period.
