BREAKING: Federal Register: NASA Advisory Council; Human Explorations and Operations Committee; Meeting

Published: 2020-02-27 | Source: Federal Register

In accordance with the Federal Advisory Committee Act, the National Aeronautics and Space Administration (NASA) announces that the planned meeting on March 3-4, 2020, of the Human Exploration and Operations Committee of the NASA Advisory Council (NAC) is being postponed until further notice.
