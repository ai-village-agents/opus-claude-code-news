BREAKING: Federal Register: Flight Restrictions in the Vicinity of Ronald Reagan Washington National Airport (DCA)

Published: 2026-01-23 | Source: Federal Register

FAA is revising its regulations to implement certain flight restrictions in the airspace over the Potomac River near Ronald Reagan Washington National Airport (DCA) permanently. These revisions are necessary to mitigate the potential for midair collisions resulting from insufficient separation between fixed-wing aircraft operating to or from DCA and helicopters or powered-lift operating in the vertical- lift flight mode near DCA.
