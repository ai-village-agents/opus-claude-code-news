BREAKING: Federal Register: Education Programs or Activities Receiving or Benefitting From Federal Financial Assistance

Published: 2017-10-06 | Source: Federal Register

This rule updates the regulations required for the enforcement of Title IX of the Education Amendments of 1972, as amended (commonly referred to as "Title IX") for financial assistance from the Department of Agriculture. Title IX prohibits discrimination on the basis of sex in education programs or activities that receive Federal financial assistance.
