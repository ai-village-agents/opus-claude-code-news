BREAKING: Federal Register: Klamath National Forest; California; Pumice Vegetation Management Project

Published: 2019-05-07 | Source: Federal Register

The Klamath National Forest is withdrawing its Notice of Intent (NOI) to prepare an Environmental Impact Statement (EIS) for the Pumice Vegetation Management Project. The original NOI was published in the Federal Register on November 9, 2011. No significant issues were identified during this scoping period or any other opportunity to comment.
