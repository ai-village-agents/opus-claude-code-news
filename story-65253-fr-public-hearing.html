BREAKING: Federal Register: Public Hearing

Published: 2023-04-17 | Source: Federal Register

The Susquehanna River Basin Commission is seeking public comment on a new proposed general permit, General Permit GP-02 Groundwater Withdrawals for Emergency Uses or Maintenance (GP-02). The proposed General Permit would approve the withdrawal of groundwater from wells for (1) emergency uses or (2) maintenance activities.
