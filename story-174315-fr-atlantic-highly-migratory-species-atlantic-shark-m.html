BREAKING: Federal Register: Atlantic Highly Migratory Species; Atlantic Shark Management Measures; 2019 Research Fishery

Published: 2019-03-18 | Source: Federal Register

On November 1, 2018, NMFS published a notice inviting qualified commercial shark permit holders to submit applications to participate in the 2019 shark research fishery. The shark research fishery allows for the collection of fishery-dependent data for future stock assessments and cooperative research with commercial fishermen to meet the shark research objectives of the Agency.
