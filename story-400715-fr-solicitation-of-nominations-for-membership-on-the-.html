BREAKING: Federal Register: Solicitation of Nominations for Membership on the Civil Nuclear Trade Advisory Committee

Published: 2012-11-06 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Commerce (the Department), as of September 2012, has rechartered the Civil Nuclear Trade Advisory Committee (CINTAC) and is seeking nominations for 40 members to be appointed for the new two-year charter term.
