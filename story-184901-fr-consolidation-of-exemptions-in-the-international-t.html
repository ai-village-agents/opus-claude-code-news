BREAKING: Federal Register: Consolidation of Exemptions in the International Traffic in Arms Regulations

Published: 2019-07-26 | Source: Federal Register

As part of an ongoing effort to better organize the International Traffic in Arms Regulations (ITAR), the Directorate of Defense Trade Controls (DDTC) seeks public comment on consolidating and clarifying the various exemptions located throughout the regulations.
