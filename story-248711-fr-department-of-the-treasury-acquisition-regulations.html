BREAKING: Federal Register: Department of the Treasury Acquisition Regulations; Tax Check Requirements

Published: 2017-11-16 | Source: Federal Register

Pursuant to Section 6103 of the Internal Revenue Code, taxpayer return information, with few exceptions, is confidential. Under this authority, officers and employees of the Department of the Treasury may have access to taxpayer return information as necessary for purposes of tax administration.
