BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; MD Helicopters, LLC Helicopters

Published: 2025-11-18 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA proposes to supersede Airworthiness Directive (AD) 2024-23-06, which applies to certain MD Helicopters, LLC (MDHI) Model 369, 369A, 369D, 369E, 369F, 369FF, 369H, 369HE, 369HM, 369HS, 500N, and 600N helicopters. AD 2024-23-06 requires repetitively inspecting the torque tube assembly and roller bearings, and depending on the results, replacing parts or accomplishing additional inspections.
