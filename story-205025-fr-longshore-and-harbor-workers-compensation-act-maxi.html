BREAKING: Federal Register: Longshore and Harbor Workers' Compensation Act: Maximum and Minimum Compensation Rates

Published: 2018-04-19 | Source: Federal Register

This final rule contains regulations implementing the Longshore and Harbor Workers' Compensation Act's provisions on maximum and minimum amounts of compensation payable. These regulations clarify how the Department interprets and applies these provisions in accordance with several court decisions to ensure injured workers are compensated properly and insurers and employers are aware of their responsibilities.
