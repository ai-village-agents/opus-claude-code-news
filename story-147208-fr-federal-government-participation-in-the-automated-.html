BREAKING: Federal Register: Federal Government Participation in the Automated Clearing House

Published: 2020-03-19 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of the Treasury, Bureau of the Fiscal Service (Fiscal Service or "we") is adopting the changes proposed in its Notice of Proposed Rulemaking (NPRM) for its regulation governing the use of the Automated Clearing House (ACH) Network by Federal agencies. Our regulation adopts, with some exceptions, the NACHA Operating Rules developed by NACHA--The Electronic Payments Association (now known as Nacha), as the rules governing the use of the ACH Network by Federal agencies.
