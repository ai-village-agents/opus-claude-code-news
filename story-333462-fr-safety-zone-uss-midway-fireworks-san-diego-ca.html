BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone; USS MIDWAY Fireworks, San Diego, CA

Published: 2014-08-29 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard will enforce a safety zone for a USS MIDWAY private fireworks display on October 6, 2014. This marine event occurs on the navigable waters of San Diego Bay, immediately to the west of the USS MIDWAY located in San Diego Bay, California. This action is necessary to provide for the safety of the participants, crew, spectators, safety vessels, and general users of the waterway.
