BREAKING: Federal Register: Amendment of Asian Longhorned Beetle Quarantine Areas in Massachusetts and New York

Published: 2015-11-19 | Source: Federal Register

We are adopting as a final rule, without change, an interim rule that amended the Asian longhorned beetle (ALB) regulations by removing the boroughs of Manhattan and Staten Island in New York City, as well as the counties of Suffolk and Norfolk in the State of Massachusetts, from the list of quarantined areas for ALB.
