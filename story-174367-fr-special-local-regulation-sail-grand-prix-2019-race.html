BREAKING: Federal Register: Special Local Regulation: Sail Grand Prix 2019 Race Event, San Francisco, CA

Published: 2019-03-18 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard proposes to establish a temporary special local regulation in the navigable waters of San Francisco Bay in San Francisco, CA in support of the San Francisco Sail Grand Prix 2019 race periods on May 4, 2019 and May 5, 2019. This special local regulation is necessary to ensure the safety of mariners transiting the area from the dangers associated with high-speed sailing activities associated with the Sail Grand Prix 2019 race event.
