BREAKING: Federal Register: Office of Shared Solutions and Performance Improvement (OSSPI); Chief Data Officers Council (CDO); Notification of Upcoming Public Meeting

Published: 2023-01-06 | Source: Federal Register

The Federal Chief Data Officers Council (CDO Council) is having a public meeting during which the CDO Council will provide updates about its activities and the implementation of the Chief Data Officer role in the Federal Government. The meeting will include panel discussions on how CDOs are impacting their agency missions and collaborating across the Government to address data challenges.
