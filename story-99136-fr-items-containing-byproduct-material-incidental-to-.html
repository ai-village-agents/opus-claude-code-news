BREAKING: Federal Register: Items Containing Byproduct Material Incidental to Production

Published: 2022-06-27 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Nuclear Regulatory Commission (NRC) is proposing to amend its regulations by adding a new class exemption from licensing and associated distribution requirements. This new class exemption would create a path for licensing current and future products that contain byproduct material incidental to their production.
