BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Hamilton Sundstrand Corporation Propellers

Published: 2013-02-07 | Source: Federal Register

We propose to adopt a new airworthiness directive (AD) for Hamilton Sundstrand Corporation 14SF-7, 14SF-15, and 14SF-23 series propellers using certain Hamilton Sundstrand Corporation auxiliary pumps and motors (auxiliary feathering pumps). This proposed AD was prompted by a report of a propeller not moving into the feathering position after an engine in-flight shutdown.
