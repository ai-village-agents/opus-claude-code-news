BREAKING: Federal Register: Expanded Delegation Authority for Procedures Related to Representation of Claimants

Published: 2017-06-09 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Veterans Affairs (VA) is amending its regulations on representation of claimants for VA benefits to specifically permit additional delegations of authority within the Office of the General Counsel (OGC), update the titles of certain individuals and offices in OGC, and make a minor procedural clarification.
