BREAKING: Federal Register: Wagner-Peyser Act Staffing

Published: 2023-11-24 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Department of Labor (Department or DOL) is issuing a final rule that requires States to use State merit staff to provide Wagner-Peyser Act Employment Service (ES) services. In the notice of proposed rulemaking (NPRM), the Department proposed that this requirement would apply to all States.
