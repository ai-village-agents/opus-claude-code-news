BREAKING: Federal Register: Registration Review Proposed Interim Decisions for Several Pesticides; Notice of Availability

Published: 2019-03-18 | Source: Federal Register

This notice announces the availability of EPA's proposed interim registration review decisions and opens a 60-day public comment period on the proposed interim decisions for the following pesticides: Aviglycine hydrochloride, buprofezin, diflubenzuron, lufenuron, oxytetracycline, prohexadione calcium, pymetrozine, streptomycin, tebuthiuron, and thiobencarb.
