BREAKING: Federal Register: Low Enriched Uranium From France: Preliminary Results of Antidumping Duty Changed Circumstances Review

Published: 2012-02-10 | Source: Federal Register

In response to a request from an interested party, Eurodif S.A. and AREVA NP Inc. (collectively, AREVA), the Department of Commerce (Department) initiated a changed circumstances review (CCR) of the antidumping duty order of low enriched uranium (LEU) from France.\1\ We preliminarily determine that it is appropriate to issue a one-time amendment to the scope of the order to extend by 18 months the deadline otherwise applicable to AREVA for the re-exportation of one entry of LEU.
