BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Final Federal Agency Actions on Proposed Highway in Utah

Published: 2012-03-26 | Source: Federal Register

This notice announces actions taken by the FHWA and other Federal agencies that are final within the meaning of 23 U.S.C. 139(l)(1). The actions relate to a proposed transportation corridor project (Cottonwood Street; 4500 South to Vine Street in Murray City, Salt Lake County in the State of Utah). These actions grant licenses, permits, and approvals for the project.
