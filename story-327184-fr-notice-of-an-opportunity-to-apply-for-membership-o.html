BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of an Opportunity To Apply for Membership on the National Advisory Council on Innovation and Entrepreneurship

Published: 2014-06-17 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Commerce is currently seeking applications for membership on the National Advisory Council on Innovation and Entrepreneurship (Council). The purpose of the Council is to advise the Secretary of Commerce on matters related to accelerating innovation and entrepreneurship.
