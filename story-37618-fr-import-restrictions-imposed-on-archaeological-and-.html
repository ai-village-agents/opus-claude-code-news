BREAKING: Federal Register: Import Restrictions Imposed on Archaeological and Ethnological Material From Ecuador; Correction

Published: 2024-05-22 | Source: Federal Register

On February 14, 2020, U.S. Customs and Border Protection (CBP) published a final rule in the Federal Register (CBP Dec. 20-03) imposing import restrictions on certain archaeological and ethnological material from Ecuador, pursuant to a memorandum of understanding between the United States and Ecuador. This document corrects the expiration date of the import restrictions to February 11, 2025, to correspond with the date the import restrictions entered into force.
