BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; The Boeing Company Airplanes

Published: 2013-04-10 | Source: Federal Register

We propose to adopt a new airworthiness directive (AD) for certain The Boeing Company Model 767-200, -300, -300F, and -400ER series airplanes. This proposed AD was prompted by reports of cracked and corroded nuts on an outboard flap support rib.
