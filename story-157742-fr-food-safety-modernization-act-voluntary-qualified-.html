BREAKING: Federal Register: Food Safety Modernization Act Voluntary Qualified Importer Program User Fee Rate for Fiscal Year 2021

Published: 2020-08-03 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA) is announcing the fiscal year (FY) 2021 annual fee rate for importers approved to participate in the Voluntary Qualified Importer Program (VQIP) that is authorized by the Federal Food, Drug, and Cosmetic Act (FD&C Act), as amended by the FDA Food Safety Modernization Act (FSMA). This fee is effective August 1, 2020, and will remain in effect through September 30, 2021.
