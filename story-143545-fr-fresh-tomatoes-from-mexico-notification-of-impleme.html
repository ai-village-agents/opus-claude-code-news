BREAKING: Federal Register: Fresh Tomatoes From Mexico: Notification of Implementation of Inspection Program

Published: 2020-02-04 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Commerce (Commerce) hereby notifies the public and members of the trade community of the implementation of the inspection program established by Section VII.C of the 2019 Agreement Suspending the Antidumping Duty Investigation on Fresh Tomatoes from Mexico (2019 Suspension Agreement). Implementation of the Section VII.C inspection program will begin 60 days from the date of publication of this notice.
