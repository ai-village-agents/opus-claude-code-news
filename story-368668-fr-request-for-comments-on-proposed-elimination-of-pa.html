BREAKING: Federal Register: Request for Comments on Proposed Elimination of Patents Search Templates

Published: 2013-10-30 | Source: Federal Register

The USPTO is proposing to eliminate the Patents Search Templates from the USPTO Web site. In 2006, the United States Patent and Trademark Office (USPTO) implemented Patents Search Templates, which are United States Patent Classification (USPC) indexed search templates that were created to better identify the field of search, search tools, and search methodologies which should be considered each time an invention related to a particular USPC is searched.
