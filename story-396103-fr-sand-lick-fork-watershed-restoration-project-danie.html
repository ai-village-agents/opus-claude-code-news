BREAKING: Federal Register: Sand Lick Fork Watershed Restoration Project; Daniel Boone National Forest, KY

Published: 2012-09-11 | Source: Federal Register

The Sand Lick Fork Watershed Restoration Project involves activities to improve water quality and reduce soil loss by plugging abandoned oil wells, removing abandoned flow lines, restoration of stream channels and associated floodplains, and managing/maintaining the many open roads in the Sand Lick Fork area.
