BREAKING: Federal Register: Partner Vetting in USAID Assistance

Published: 2013-08-29 | Source: Federal Register

The United States Agency for International Development (USAID) proposes to amend its regulation governing the administration of USAID- funded assistance awards to implement a Partner Vetting System (PVS).
