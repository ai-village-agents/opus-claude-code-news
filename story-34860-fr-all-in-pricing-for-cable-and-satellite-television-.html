BREAKING: Federal Register: All-In Pricing for Cable and Satellite Television Service

Published: 2024-04-19 | Source: Federal Register

In this document, the Federal Communications Commission (Commission) implements the "all-in" rule, requiring cable operators and direct broadcast satellite (DBS) providers to state an aggregate price for the video programming that they provide as a clear, easy-to- understand, and accurate single line item on subscribers' bills, including on bills for legacy or grandfathered video programming service plans.
