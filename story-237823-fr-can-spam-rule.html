BREAKING: Federal Register: CAN-SPAM Rule

Published: 2017-06-28 | Source: Federal Register

The Commission is requesting public comments on its rule implementing the Controlling the Assault of Non-Solicited Pornography and Marketing Act (the "CAN-SPAM Rule" or "Rule"). The Commission is soliciting comments about the efficiency, costs, benefits, and regulatory impact of the Rule as part of its systematic review of all current Commission regulations and guides.
