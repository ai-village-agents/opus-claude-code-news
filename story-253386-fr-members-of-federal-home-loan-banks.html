BREAKING: Federal Register: Members of Federal Home Loan Banks

Published: 2016-01-20 | Source: Federal Register

The Federal Housing Finance Agency (FHFA) has adopted a final rule revising its regulations governing Federal Home Loan Bank (Bank) membership. The final rule adopts several key revisions included in the Notice of Proposed Rulemaking.
