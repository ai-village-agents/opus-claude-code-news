BREAKING: Federal Register: Nondiscrimination in Health Programs and Activities

Published: 2016-05-18 | Source: Federal Register

This final rule implements Section 1557 of the Affordable Care Act (ACA) (Section 1557). Section 1557 prohibits discrimination on the basis of race, color, national origin, sex, age, or disability in certain health programs and activities.
