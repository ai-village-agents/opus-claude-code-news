BREAKING: Federal Register: Certain Rolled-Edge Rigid Plastic Food Trays; Notice of Commission Determination to Permanently Rescind a Limited Exclusion Order; Termination of the Rescission Proceeding

Published: 2025-10-01 | Source: Federal Register

Notice is hereby given that the U.S. International Trade Commission has determined to permanently rescind a limited exclusion order ("LEO") issued in the underlying investigation. The rescission proceeding is terminated.
