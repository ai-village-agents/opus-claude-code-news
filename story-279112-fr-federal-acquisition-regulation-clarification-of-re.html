BREAKING: Federal Register: Federal Acquisition Regulation: Clarification of Requirement for Justifications for 8(a) Sole Source Contracts

Published: 2016-11-15 | Source: Federal Register

DoD, GSA, and NASA are proposing to amend the Federal Acquisition Regulation (FAR) to clarify the guidance for sole-source 8(a) contract awards exceeding $22 million.
