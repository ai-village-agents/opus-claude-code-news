BREAKING: Federal Register: Special Regulations, Areas of the National Park System, Pea Ridge National Military Park; Bicycles

Published: 2018-09-25 | Source: Federal Register

The National Park Service promulgates special regulations for Pea Ridge National Military Park to allow bicycle use on two multi-use trails located within the park. One trail will be approximately 0.55 miles in length and the other will be approximately 1.17 miles in length. Both trails will require trail construction activities to accommodate bicycles and are therefore considered new trails that will be opened to bicycles.
