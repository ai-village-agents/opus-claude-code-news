BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Funding Opportunity for Magnetic Levitation Deployment Projects

Published: 2020-07-01 | Source: Federal Register

This notice details the application requirements and procedures to obtain grant funding for eligible projects under the Magnetic Levitation Technology Deployment Program (Maglev Grants Program). This notice solicits applications for $2,000,000 in Maglev Grants Program funds. The opportunity described in this notice is made available under Catalog of Federal Domestic Assistance (CFDA) number 20.318, "Maglev Project Selection Program--SAFETEA-LU."
