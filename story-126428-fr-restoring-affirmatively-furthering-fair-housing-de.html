BREAKING: Federal Register: Restoring Affirmatively Furthering Fair Housing Definitions and Certifications

Published: 2021-06-23 | Source: Federal Register

On July 10, 2021, HUD published its Restoring Affirmatively Furthering Fair Housing Definitions and Certifications interim final rule. Shortly thereafter, the Office of the Federal Register alerted HUD to a scrivener's error in the amendatory instructions of the interim final rule. In this document, HUD corrects this error.
