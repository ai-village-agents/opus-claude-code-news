BREAKING: Federal Register: Agency Information Collection Activities: OMB Control Number 1029-0055; Rights of Entry

Published: 2017-06-21 | Source: Federal Register

In accordance with the Paperwork Reduction Act of 1995, the Office of Surface Mining Reclamation and Enforcement (OSMRE) is announcing its intention to request renewed approval for the collection of information for states or Indian tribes, pursuant to an approved reclamation program, to use police powers, if necessary, to effect entry upon private lands to conduct reclamation activities or exploratory studies if the landowner's consent is refused or the landowner is not available.
