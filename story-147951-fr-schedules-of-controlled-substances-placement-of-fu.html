BREAKING: Federal Register: Schedules of Controlled Substances: Placement of FUB-AMB in Schedule I

Published: 2020-03-30 | Source: Federal Register

The Drug Enforcement Administration (DEA) places methyl 2-(1- (4-fluorobenzyl)-1H-indazole-3-carboxamido)-3-methylbutanoate (other names: FUB-AMB, MMB-FUBINACA, AMB-FUBINACA), including its salts, isomers, and salts of isomers whenever the existence of such salts, isomers, and salts of isomers is possible, in schedule I of the Controlled Substances Act.
