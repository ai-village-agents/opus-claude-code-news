BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone; Cape Fear River, Wilmington, NC

Published: 2018-02-16 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard proposes to establish a temporary safety zone on the navigable waters of the Cape Fear River in New Hanover County, North Carolina. This temporary safety zone is intended to restrict vessel traffic on the Cape Fear River while a vessel prepares for and actively off-loads two new Post-Panamax gantry cranes to the North Carolina State Port Authority in Wilmington, North Carolina.
