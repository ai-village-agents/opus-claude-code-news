BREAKING: Federal Register: Reactor Vessel Material Surveillance Program

Published: 2020-12-29 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Nuclear Regulatory Commission (NRC) is confirming the effective date of February 1, 2021, for the direct final rule that was published in the Federal Register on October 2, 2020. The direct final rule amends the NRC's reactor vessel material surveillance program requirements for commercial light-water reactors.
