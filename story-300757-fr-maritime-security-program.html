BREAKING: Federal Register: Maritime Security Program

Published: 2015-08-05 | Source: Federal Register

The Maritime Administration ("MARAD") is soliciting public comments on amendments to its regulations that implement amendments to the Maritime Security Act of 2003 by the National Defense Authorization Act for Fiscal Year 2013 ("NDAA 2013").
