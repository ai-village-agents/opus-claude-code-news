BREAKING: Federal Register: Medicare Program; Solicitation of Nominations to the Advisory Panel on Hospital Outpatient Payment

Published: 2015-08-28 | Source: Federal Register

This notice solicits nominations for up to seven new members to the Advisory Panel on Hospital Outpatient Payment (HOP, the Panel). There will be vacancies on the Panel for four-year terms that begin during Calendar Year 2016.
