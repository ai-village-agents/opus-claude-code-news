BREAKING: Federal Register: Agency Information Collection Activities; Comment Request; Renewal Without Change of Bank Secrecy Act Regulations Requiring Depository Institutions and Insurance Companies To Report Suspicious Activity

Published: 2018-02-09 | Source: Federal Register

FinCEN, a bureau of the U.S. Department of the Treasury ("Treasury"), invites all interested parties to comment on its proposed renewal without change of the Bank Secrecy Act ("BSA") Suspicious Activity Reporting regulations for certain financial institutions, i.e., depository institutions and insurance companies.
