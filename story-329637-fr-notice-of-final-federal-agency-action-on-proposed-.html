BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Final Federal Agency Action on Proposed Highway in North Carolina

Published: 2014-07-15 | Source: Federal Register

This final agency action relates to a proposed highway project, Bonner Bridge Replacement Project along NC 12, from Rodanthe to Bodie Island in Dare County, North Carolina.
