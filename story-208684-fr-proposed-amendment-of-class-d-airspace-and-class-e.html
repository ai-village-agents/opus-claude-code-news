BREAKING: Federal Register: Proposed Amendment of Class D Airspace and Class E Airspace; Williamsport, PA

Published: 2018-06-05 | Source: Federal Register

This action proposes to amend Class D airspace, Class E surface airspace, Class E airspace designated as an extension to a Class D surface area, and Class E airspace area extending upward from 700 feet or more above the surface at Williamsport Regional Airport (formerly Williamsport-Lycoming County Airport), Williamsport, PA.
