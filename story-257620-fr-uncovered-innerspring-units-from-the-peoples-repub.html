BREAKING: Federal Register: Uncovered Innerspring Units From the People's Republic of China: Preliminary Results of the Antidumping Duty Administrative Review; 2014-2015

Published: 2016-03-10 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Commerce (the "Department") is conducting an administrative review of the antidumping duty order on uncovered innerspring units from the People's Republic of China ("PRC"), for the period of review ("POR"), February 1, 2014, to January 31, 2015. The Department preliminarily determines that Macao Commercial and Industrial Spring Mattress Manufacturer ("Macao Commercial") had no reviewable shipments of subject merchandise during the POR.
