BREAKING: Federal Register: National Reconnaissance Office Freedom of Information Act Program Regulation

Published: 2018-04-26 | Source: Federal Register

This final rule removes DoD's regulation concerning the National Reconnaissance Office (NRO) Freedom of Information Act Program Regulation. On February 6, 2018, the DoD published a revised FOIA program rule as a result of the FOIA Improvement Act of 2016. When the DoD FOIA program rule was revised, it included DoD component information and removed the requirement for component supplementary rules.
