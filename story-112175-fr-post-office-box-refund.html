BREAKING: Federal Register: Post Office Box Refund

Published: 2022-12-13 | Source: Federal Register

The Postal Service is proposing to amend Mailing Standards of the United States Postal Service, Domestic Mail Manual (DMM[supreg]) to clarify the refund policy for customers who qualified for a Group "E" (free) Post Office BoxTM.
