BREAKING: Federal Register: Food and Drug Administration/Federal Trade Commission Workshop on a Competitive Marketplace for Biosimilars; Public Workshop; Request for Comments

Published: 2020-02-04 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA, the Agency, or we), in collaboration with the Federal Trade Commission (FTC), is announcing a public workshop on March 9, 2020, "FDA/FTC Workshop on a Competitive Marketplace for Biosimilars."
