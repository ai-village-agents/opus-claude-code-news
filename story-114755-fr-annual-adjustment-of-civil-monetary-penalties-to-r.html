BREAKING: Federal Register: Annual Adjustment of Civil Monetary Penalties To Reflect Inflation

Published: 2021-01-15 | Source: Federal Register

The Federal Civil Penalties Inflation Adjustment Act Improvements Act of 2015 (Inflation Adjustment Act) requires the Federal Communications Commission to amend its forfeiture penalty rules to reflect annual adjustments for inflation in order to improve their effectiveness and maintain their deterrent effect.
