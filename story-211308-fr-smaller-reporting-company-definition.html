BREAKING: Federal Register: Smaller Reporting Company Definition

Published: 2018-07-10 | Source: Federal Register

We are adopting amendments to the definition of "smaller reporting company" as used in our rules and regulations. The amendments expand the number of registrants that qualify as smaller reporting companies and are intended to reduce compliance costs for these registrants and promote capital formation, while maintaining appropriate investor protections.
