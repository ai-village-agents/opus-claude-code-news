BREAKING: Federal Register: United States-Mexico-Canada Agreement (USMCA), Article 10.12; Binational Panel Review: Notice of Panel Decision

Published: 2022-06-24 | Source: Federal Register

On June 14, 2022, the Binational Panel issued its Decision in the matter of Certain Gypsum Board, Sheet, or Panel originating in or exported from the United States of America. The Binational Panel affirmed the Canadian Intentional Trade Tribunal's Final Determination.
