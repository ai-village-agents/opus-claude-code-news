BREAKING: Federal Register: Pipeline Safety: Gaseous Carbon Dioxide Pipelines

Published: 2016-06-27 | Source: Federal Register

PHMSA is seeking public comment on a PHMSA-authored report titled: "Background for Regulating the Transportation of Carbon Dioxide in a Gaseous State," which is available in the docket at PHMSA-2016-0049.
