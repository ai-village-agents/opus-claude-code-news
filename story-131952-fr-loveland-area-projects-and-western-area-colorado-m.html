BREAKING: Federal Register: Loveland Area Projects and Western Area Colorado Missouri Balancing Authority-Rate Order No. WAPA-197

Published: 2021-08-31 | Source: Federal Register

The formula rates for the Rocky Mountain Region (RMR) of the Western Area Power Administration (WAPA) to use under the WEIS Market (Provisional Formula Rates) have been confirmed, approved, and placed into effect on an interim basis. RMR has converted the existing rates for short-term sales for RMR to use under the WEIS Market to long-term formula rates.
