BREAKING: Federal Register: BNSF Railway Company-Temporary Trackage Rights Exemption-Union Pacific Railroad Company

Published: 2012-12-03 | Source: Federal Register

Under 49 U.S.C. 10502, the Board revokes the class exemption as it pertains to the trackage rights described in Docket No. FD 35676 \1\ to permit the trackage rights to expire at midnight on December 31, 2012, in accordance with the agreement of the parties, subject to the employee protective conditions set forth in Oregon Short Line Railroad--Abandonment Portion Goshen Branch Between Firth & Ammon, in Bingham & Bonneville Counties, Idaho, 360 I.C.C. 91 (1979). ----------------------------------...
