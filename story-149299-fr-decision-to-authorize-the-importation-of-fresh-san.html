BREAKING: Federal Register: Decision To Authorize the Importation of Fresh Sand Pears From Japan Into the United States

Published: 2020-04-16 | Source: Federal Register

We are advising the public of our decision to authorize importation of sand pears from all production areas of Japan into the United States and to revise the conditions under which they may be imported.
