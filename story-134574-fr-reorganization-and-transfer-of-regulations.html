BREAKING: Federal Register: Reorganization and Transfer of Regulations

Published: 2021-10-01 | Source: Federal Register

This rule transfers regulations pertaining to the Economic Adjustment Assistance for Textile Manufacturers program from the Commodity Credit Corporation (CCC) to the Agricultural Marketing Service (AMS) to reflect changes in the organizational structure and delegated authorities within the United States Department of Agriculture (USDA).
