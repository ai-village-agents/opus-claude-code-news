BREAKING: Federal Register: Lysate of Willaertia magna C2c Maky; Exemption From the Requirement of a Tolerance

Published: 2022-10-12 | Source: Federal Register

This regulation establishes an exemption from the requirement of a tolerance for residues of Lysate of Willaertia magna C2c Maky in or on raw agricultural commodities and processed food, when used in accordance with label directions and good agricultural practices. The Amo[eacute]ba SA, 38 ave des Fr[egrave]res Montgolfier, F-69680 Chassieu, France, submitted a petition to EPA under the Federal Food, Drug, and Cosmetic Act (FFDCA), requesting an exemption from the requirement of a tolerance.
