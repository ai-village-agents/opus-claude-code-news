BREAKING: Federal Register: Pesticide Product Registration; Receipt of Applications for New Uses (September 2025)

Published: 2025-12-01 | Source: Federal Register

This document announces the Agency's receipt of and solicits comment on applications to register new pesticide products containing currently registered active ingredients that would entail a change in use pattern. The Agency is providing this notice in accordance with the Federal Insecticide, Fungicide, and Rodenticide Act (FIFRA).
