BREAKING: Federal Register: Unblocking of One Entity and One Individual Pursuant to Executive Order 13219, as Amended

Published: 2015-06-11 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Department of the Treasury's Office of Foreign Assets Control (OFAC) is publishing the name of one entity and one individual whose property and interests in property are being unblocked pursuant to Executive Order (E.O.) 13219 of June 26, 2001, as amended by E.O. 13304 of May 28, 2003.
