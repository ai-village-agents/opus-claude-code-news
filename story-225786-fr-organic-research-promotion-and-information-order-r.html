BREAKING: Federal Register: Organic Research, Promotion, and Information Order; Referendum Procedures

Published: 2017-01-18 | Source: Federal Register

This proposed rule invites comments on procedures for conducting a referendum to determine whether the issuance of a proposed Organic Research, Promotion, and Information Order (proposed Order) is favored by certified organic producers, certified organic handlers, and importers of certified organic products.
