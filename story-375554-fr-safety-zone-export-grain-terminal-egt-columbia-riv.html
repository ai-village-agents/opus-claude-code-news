BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone; Export Grain Terminal (EGT), Columbia River, Longview, WA

Published: 2012-01-23 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is establishing a temporary safety zone around the Export Grain Terminal located on the Columbia River in Longview, WA. This safety zone extends to waters of the Columbia River approximately between the navigable channel and the Export Grain Terminal in Longview, WA.
