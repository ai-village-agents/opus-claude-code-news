BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Award of a Single-Source Cooperative Agreement To Fund the United Nations Children's Fund

Published: 2023-01-11 | Source: Federal Register

The Centers for Disease Control and Prevention (CDC), located within the Department of Health and Human Services (HHS), announces the award of approximately $36,000,000 for Year 1 funding to the United Nations Children's Fund (UNICEF).
