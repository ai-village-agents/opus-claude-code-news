BREAKING: Federal Register: Meeting of the National Drinking Water Advisory Council

Published: 2021-09-22 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Environmental Protection Agency's (EPA) Office of Ground Water and Drinking Water is announcing a virtual meeting of the National Drinking Water Advisory Council (NDWAC or Council) as authorized under the Safe Drinking Water Act (SDWA).
