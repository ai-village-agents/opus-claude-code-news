BREAKING: Federal Register: Drawbridge Operation Regulation; The Straights, Harkers Island, NC

Published: 2012-06-01 | Source: Federal Register

The Commander, Fifth Coast Guard District, has issued a temporary deviation from the regulations governing the operation of the Route 70/Harkers Island Bridge across The Straights, at Harkers Island, NC. The deviation is necessary to facilitate maintenance to the bridge motor. This deviation allows the bridge to remain closed-to-navigation while the maintenance is conducted.
