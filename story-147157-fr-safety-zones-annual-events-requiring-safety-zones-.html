BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zones; Annual Events Requiring Safety Zones in the Captain of the Port Lake Michigan Zone

Published: 2020-03-19 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is proposing to amend the rules that regulate vessel traffic and control navigation on portions of waterways in the Lake Michigan Captain of the Port Zone during events that will introduce safety concerns to life and property on the navigable waters of the United States.
