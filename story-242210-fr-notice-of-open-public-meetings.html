BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Open Public Meetings

Published: 2017-08-25 | Source: Federal Register

Notice is hereby given of meetings of the U.S.-China Economic and Security Review Commission to review and edit drafts of the 2017 Annual Report to Congress. The Commission is mandated by Congress to investigate, assess, and report to Congress annually on the "the national security implications of the economic relationship between the United States and the People's Republic of China."
