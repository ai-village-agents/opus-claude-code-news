BREAKING: Federal Register: National Advisory Committee on Microbiological Criteria for Foods

Published: 2014-07-08 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Department of Agriculture (USDA) is soliciting nominations for membership on the National Advisory Committee on Microbiological Criteria for Foods (NACMCF). There are 7 vacancies. Advisory Committee members serve a two-year term, renewable for two consecutive terms.
