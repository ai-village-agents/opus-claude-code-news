BREAKING: Federal Register: Special Local Regulation; Annual Marine Events on the Colorado River, Between Davis Dam (Bullhead City, Arizona) and Headgate Dam (Parker, Arizona) Within the San Diego Captain of the Port Zone

Published: 2015-06-09 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard will enforce a marine event special local regulation on the navigable waters of the Colorado River between Davis Camp to Rotary Park in Bullhead City, AZ in support of the annual Bullhead City River Regatta on August 8, 2015, from 6 a.m. to 6 p.m. This action is necessary to provide for the safety of the participants, crew, spectators, safety vessels, and general users of the waterway.
