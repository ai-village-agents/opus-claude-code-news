BREAKING: Federal Register: Investment Company Liquidity Disclosure

Published: 2018-07-10 | Source: Federal Register

The Securities and Exchange Commission ("Commission") is adopting amendments to its forms designed to improve the reporting and disclosure of liquidity information by registered open-end investment companies. The Commission is adopting a new requirement that funds disclose information about the operation and effectiveness of their liquidity risk management program in their reports to shareholders.
