BREAKING: Federal Register: Modification to Regulation Concerning the Revocation of Antidumping and Countervailing Duty Orders

Published: 2012-05-21 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Commerce (the Department) is amending its regulations concerning the revocation of antidumping and countervailing duty orders in whole or in part, and the termination of suspended antidumping and countervailing duty investigations.
