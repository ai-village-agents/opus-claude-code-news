BREAKING: Federal Register: Simplified Cost Accounting and Other Actions To Reduce Paperwork in the Summer Food Service Program

Published: 2018-06-01 | Source: Federal Register

This final rule amends the Summer Food Service Program (SFSP) regulations to incorporate statutory changes mandated by Section 738 of the Consolidated Appropriations Act, 2008, which extends simplified cost accounting and reporting procedures to SFSP sponsors in all States, and eliminates the cost comparison requirements for determining payments to sponsors.
