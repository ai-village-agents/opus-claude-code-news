BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Proposed Action: Crude Helium Sale and Auction for Fiscal Year 2016 Delivery

Published: 2015-06-12 | Source: Federal Register

The purpose of this action is to inform the public that the Department of the Interior, Bureau of Land Management (BLM), will conduct a Phase B sale and auction of crude helium for Fiscal Year (FY) 2016 delivery.
