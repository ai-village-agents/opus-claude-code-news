BREAKING: Federal Register: Motorola Mobility LLC and Google Inc.; Analysis of Proposed Consent Order To Aid Public Comment

Published: 2013-01-16 | Source: Federal Register

The document inadvertently did not include the Statement of the Commission. This document contains the Statement of the Commission.
