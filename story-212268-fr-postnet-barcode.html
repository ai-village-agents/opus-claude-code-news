BREAKING: Federal Register: POSTNET Barcode

Published: 2018-07-23 | Source: Federal Register

The Postal Service is proposing an amendment of the Mailing Standards of the United States Postal Service, Domestic Mail Manual, (DMM[reg]) to remove all references to the POSTNETTM barcode.
