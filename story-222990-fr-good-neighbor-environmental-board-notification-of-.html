BREAKING: Federal Register: Good Neighbor Environmental Board; Notification of Public Advisory Committee Teleconference

Published: 2018-12-12 | Source: Federal Register

Pursuant to the Federal Advisory Committee Act, notice is hereby given that the Good Neighbor Environmental Board (Board) will hold a public teleconference on December 19, 2018 from 12:00 p.m.-4:00 p.m. Eastern Daylight Time. Due to unforeseen administrative circumstances, EPA is announcing this teleconference with less than 15 calendar days' notice.
