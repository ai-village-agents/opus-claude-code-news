BREAKING: Federal Register: Laboratory Accreditation for Analyses of Foods

Published: 2021-12-03 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA, the Agency, or we) is amending its regulations to establish a program for the testing of food in certain circumstances by accredited laboratories, as required under the Federal Food, Drug, and Cosmetic Act (FD&C Act).
