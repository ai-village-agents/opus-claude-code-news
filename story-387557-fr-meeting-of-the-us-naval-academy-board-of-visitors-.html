BREAKING: Federal Register: Meeting of the U.S. Naval Academy Board of Visitors; Correction

Published: 2012-06-05 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of the Navy published a document in the Federal Register (77 FR 103) on May 29, 2012, concerning the partially closed meeting of the U.S. Naval Academy Board of Visitors. The document failed to publish before the 15-day statutory requirement.
