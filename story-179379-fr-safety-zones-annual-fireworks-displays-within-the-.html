BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zones; Annual Fireworks Displays Within the Sector Columbia River Captain of the Port Zone

Published: 2019-05-17 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard will enforce safety zone regulations at 29 locations in the Sector Columbia River Captain of the Port zone during the dates and times noted under SUPPLEMENTARY INFORMATION. This action is necessary to prevent injury and to protect life and property of the maritime public from the hazards associated with annual fireworks displays.
