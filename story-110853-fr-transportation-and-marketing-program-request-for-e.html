BREAKING: Federal Register: Transportation and Marketing Program; Request for Extension of a Currently Approved Information Collection

Published: 2022-11-25 | Source: Federal Register

In accordance with the Paperwork Reduction Act of 1995, this notice announces the Agricultural Marketing Service's (AMS) intention to request approval from the Office of Management and Budget, for extension of a currently approved collection titled "Local Food Directories and Survey" (OMB 0581-0169). Under the Agricultural Marketing Act of 1946, as amended, AMS is responsible for conducting research to enhance market access for small and medium sized farmers.
