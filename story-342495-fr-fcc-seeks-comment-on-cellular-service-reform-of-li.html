BREAKING: Federal Register: FCC Seeks Comment on Cellular Service Reform of Licensing and Technical Rules, Including Power Limits

Published: 2014-12-22 | Source: Federal Register

In this document, the Commission proposes and seeks comment on reforms of its rules governing the 800 MHz Cellular ("Cellular") Service. The proposals include a geographic-based discontinuance of operations rule to replace the current site-based approach, and the establishment of frequency coordinators to review certain applications prior to their submission to the Commission.
