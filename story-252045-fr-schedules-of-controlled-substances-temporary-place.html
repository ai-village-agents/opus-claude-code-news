BREAKING: Federal Register: Schedules of Controlled Substances: Temporary Placement of Fentanyl-Related Substances in Schedule I

Published: 2017-12-29 | Source: Federal Register

The Administrator of the Drug Enforcement Administration is publishing this notice of intent to issue an order temporarily scheduling fentanyl-related substances that are not currently listed in any schedule of the Controlled Substances Act (CSA). The temporary order will place these substances in schedule I.
