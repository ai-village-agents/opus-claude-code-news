BREAKING: Federal Register: Privacy Act of 1974; System of Records

Published: 2019-07-09 | Source: Federal Register

The Pension Benefit Guaranty Corporation (PBGC) is proposing the following changes to its system of records notices to establish a new system of records PBGC-26: PBGC Insider Threat and Data Loss Prevention. The new system of records will cover records about individuals, retrieved by personal identifier, which are compiled and used by PBGC's Insider Threat and Data Loss Prevention teams, to administer PBGC's insider threat and data loss prevention programs.
