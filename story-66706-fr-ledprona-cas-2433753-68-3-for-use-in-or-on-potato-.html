BREAKING: Federal Register: Ledprona (CAS# 2433753-68-3) for Use in or on Potato; Temporary Exemption From the Requirement of a Tolerance

Published: 2023-05-04 | Source: Federal Register

This regulation establishes a temporary exemption from the requirement of a tolerance for residues of Ledprona (CAS# 2433753-68-3) in or on potatoes when used in accordance with the terms of Experimental Use Permit (EUP) No. 94614-EUP-1. GreenLight Bioscience, Inc. submitted a petition to EPA under the Federal Food, Drug, and Cosmetic Act (FFDCA) for a temporary exemption from the requirement of a tolerance for residues of Ledprona on all raw agricultural products and food products.
