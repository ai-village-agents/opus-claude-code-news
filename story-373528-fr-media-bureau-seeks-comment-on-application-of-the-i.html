BREAKING: Federal Register: Media Bureau Seeks Comment on Application of the IP Closed Captioning Rules to Video Clips

Published: 2013-12-26 | Source: Federal Register

This document seeks updated information on the closed captioning of video clips delivered by Internet protocol ("IP"), including the extent to which industry has voluntarily captioned IP- delivered video clips. The Commission directed the Media Bureau to issue this document to seek comment on the industry's progress in captioning IP-delivered video clips.
