BREAKING: Federal Register: Designation of 10 Areas as High Intensity Drug Trafficking Areas

Published: 2018-10-16 | Source: Federal Register

The Director of the Office of National Drug Control Policy designated 10 additional areas as High Intensity Drug Trafficking Areas (HIDTA) pursuant to 21 U.S.C. 1706.
