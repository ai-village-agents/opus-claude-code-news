BREAKING: Federal Register: Privacy Act of 1974: New System of Records

Published: 2021-11-19 | Source: Federal Register

Peace Corps proposes to add a new system of records to its inventory of records systems subject to the Privacy Act of 1974. The purpose of this system is to allow the Peace Corps to collect and maintain records related to an individual's request and receipt of reasonable accommodations through the Office of Civil Rights and Diversity.
