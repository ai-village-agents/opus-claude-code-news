BREAKING: Federal Register: Tennessee Valley Authority; Watts Bar Nuclear Plant, Units 1 and 2

Published: 2018-06-08 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Nuclear Regulatory Commission (NRC) received, and is considering approval of, an application from Tennessee Valley Authority (TVA or the licensee) for amendments to Facility Operating License Nos. NPF-90 and NPF-96, which authorizes the operation of Watts Bar Nuclear Plant (WBN), Unit Nos. 1 and 2, respectively.
