BREAKING: Federal Register: Announcement of Requirements and Registration for Offender Reintegration Toolkit Challenge

Published: 2015-05-08 | Source: Federal Register

(1) What action is being taken? The Substance Abuse and Mental Health Services Administration (SAMHSA) has issued an Offender Reintegration Toolkit Challenge to help reduce recidivism and promote the public health and safety of communities. (2) Why is this action necessary? Studies show that people leaving the criminal justice system have a higher proportion of substance use and mental disorders than the general population.
