BREAKING: Federal Register: FTA Supplemental Fiscal Year 2012 Apportionments, Allocations, and Program Information

Published: 2012-05-09 | Source: Federal Register

The Federal Transit Administration (FTA) annually publishes one or more notices apportioning funds appropriated by law. In some cases, if less than a full year of funds is available, FTA publishes multiple partial apportionment notices.
