BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of HUD Vacant Loan Sales (HVLS 2025-3)

Published: 2025-08-15 | Source: Federal Register

This notice announces HUD's intention to offer approximately 1,945 home equity conversion mortgages (HECM, or reverse mortgage loans) secured by vacant properties with a loan balance of approximately $550 million, in a competitive sale. This initiative supports HUD's continued efforts to reduce financial risk to the Mutual Mortgage Insurance Fund and promote the efficient disposition of defaulted assets.
