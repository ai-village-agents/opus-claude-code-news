BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Fokker Services B.V. Airplanes

Published: 2019-11-25 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA is adopting a new airworthiness directive (AD) for all Fokker Services B.V. Model F28 Mark 0070 and 0100 airplanes. This AD was prompted by reports of lavatory waste bin fire extinguishers found depleted.
