BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Availability of Roundtable on Military Spouse Hiring Meeting

Published: 2023-11-16 | Source: Federal Register

The DoD is publishing this notice to announce the Roundtable on Military Spouse Hiring will take place pursuant to the 2023 National Defense Authorization Act (NDAA). This Roundtable will bring together stakeholders from the government, private sector, and military families, to discuss the landscape of opportunities and challenges surrounding military spouse hiring.
