BREAKING: Federal Register: Migratory Birds; Take of Peregrine Falcons for Use in Falconry

Published: 2017-09-11 | Source: Federal Register

In December 2008, the U.S. Fish and Wildlife Service completed an environmental assessment (EA) on the take of peregrine falcons for use in falconry. In 2009 and 2010, we published notices in the Federal Register describing the take limits and geographic allocation of take for first-year fall-migrant (passage) peregrine falcons consistent with the selected alternative in that EA.
