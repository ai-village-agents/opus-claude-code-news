BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone; Lower Mississippi River, Mile Marker 368 Through 370, Natchez, MS

Published: 2021-03-04 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is establishing a temporary safety zone for all navigable waters of the Lower Mississippi River (LMR), Mile Marker 368 through 370. The safety zone is needed to protect persons, property, and the marine environment from the potential safety hazards associated with line pulling operations in the vicinity of the Natchez, MS.
