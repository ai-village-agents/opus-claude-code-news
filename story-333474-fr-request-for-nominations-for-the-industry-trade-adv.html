BREAKING: Federal Register: Request for Nominations for the Industry Trade Advisory Committees (ITACs); Amendment

Published: 2014-08-29 | Source: Federal Register

This notice amends the Department of Commerce, International Trade Administration Request for Nominations for the Industry Trade Advisory Committees (ITACs) of February 24, 2014 (79 FR 10099) to revise the eligibility criteria to permit federally-registered lobbyists to apply for membership on the ITACs.
