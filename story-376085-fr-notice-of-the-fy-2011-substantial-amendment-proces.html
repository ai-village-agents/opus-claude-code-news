BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of the FY 2011 Substantial Amendment Process and Other Related Information for Recipients of Emergency Solutions Grants Program Funds

Published: 2012-01-27 | Source: Federal Register

This Notice advises the public of the amounts, and spending restrictions on the use, of the second allocation of Fiscal Year (FY) 2011 Emergency Shelter Grants/Emergency Solutions Grants funding (including requirements for establishing each recipient's expenditure limit for emergency shelter and street outreach activities), requirements for receiving the second allocation, and requirements that apply to FY 2012 and future consolidated planning submissions.
