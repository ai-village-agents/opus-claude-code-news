BREAKING: Federal Register: Interagency Guidance on Reconsiderations of Value of Residential Real Estate Valuations

Published: 2023-07-21 | Source: Federal Register

The Board, CFPB, FDIC, NCUA, and OCC (together, the agencies) are issuing proposed guidance that would highlight risks associated with deficient residential real estate valuations and describe how financial institutions may incorporate reconsiderations of value (ROV) processes and controls into established risk management functions.
