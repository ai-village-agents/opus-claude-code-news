BREAKING: Federal Register: Certain Integrated Circuits, Mobile Devices Containing the Same, and Components Thereof; Notice of Commission Decision Not To Review an Initial Determination Terminating the Investigation in its Entirety Based on Settlement; Termination of the Investigation

Published: 2024-04-16 | Source: Federal Register

Notice is hereby given that the U.S. International Trade Commission has determined not to review an initial determination ("ID") (Order No. 84) of the presiding Administrative Law Judge ("ALJ") terminating the above-captioned investigation in its entirety based on settlement. The investigation is terminated.
