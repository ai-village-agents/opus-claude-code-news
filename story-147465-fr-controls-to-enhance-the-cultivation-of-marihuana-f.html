BREAKING: Federal Register: Controls To Enhance the Cultivation of Marihuana for Research in the United States

Published: 2020-03-23 | Source: Federal Register

The Drug Enforcement Administration is proposing to amend its regulations to comply with the requirements of the Controlled Substances Act, including consistency with treaty obligations, in order to facilitate the cultivation of marihuana for research purposes and other licit purposes.
