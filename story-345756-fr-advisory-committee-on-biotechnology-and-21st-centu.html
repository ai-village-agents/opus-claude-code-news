BREAKING: Federal Register: Advisory Committee on Biotechnology and 21st Century Agriculture; Renewal

Published: 2013-02-01 | Source: Federal Register

Notice is hereby given that the Secretary of Agriculture has renewed the Advisory Committee on Biotechnology and 21st Century Agriculture (AC21). The Secretary of Agriculture has determined that the Committee is necessary and in the public interest.
