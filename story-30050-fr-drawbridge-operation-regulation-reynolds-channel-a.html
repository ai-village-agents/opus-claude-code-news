BREAKING: Federal Register: Drawbridge Operation Regulation; Reynolds Channel, Atlantic Beach, NY

Published: 2024-02-21 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard proposes to modify the operating schedule that governs the Atlantic Beach Bridge across the Reynolds Channel, mile 0.4, at Atlantic Beach, NY. The bridge owner, Nassau County Bridge Authority, submitted a request on September 22, 2022 to modify the regulation to decrease the number of openings on signal from October through May.
