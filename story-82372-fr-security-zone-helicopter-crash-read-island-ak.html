BREAKING: Federal Register: Security Zone; Helicopter Crash, Read Island, AK

Published: 2023-11-20 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is establishing a temporary security zone for all navigable waters within a 2 nautical miles radius of Read Island, AK, centered at Latitude: 57[deg]06'48.3" N, Longitude: 133[deg]11'43.4" W, where a MH-60 Jayhawk Helicopter crashed (CG- 6016), early on Tuesday, November 14, 2023. This security zone is needed to protect and preserve the crash site of the MH-60 Helicopter wreck.
