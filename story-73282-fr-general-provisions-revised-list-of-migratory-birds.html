BREAKING: Federal Register: General Provisions; Revised List of Migratory Birds

Published: 2023-07-31 | Source: Federal Register

We, the U.S. Fish and Wildlife Service (Service), revise the List of Birds protected by the Migratory Bird Treaty Act (MBTA) by both adding and removing species and changing names to conform to accepted use by the scientific community.
