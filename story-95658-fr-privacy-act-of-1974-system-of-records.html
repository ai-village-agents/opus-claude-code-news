BREAKING: Federal Register: Privacy Act of 1974; System of Records

Published: 2022-05-11 | Source: Federal Register

The United States Agency for International Development (USAID) proposes to modify an existing Agency-wide system of records entitled, Litigation Records. This system of records contains records used to assist attorneys and legal staff in providing legal advice to the agency on a wide variety of legal issues.
