BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Intent of Waiver With Respect to Land; Fort Wayne International Airport, Fort Wayne, IN

Published: 2018-03-12 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA is considering a proposal to change approximately 78.902 acres of airport land from aeronautical use to non-aeronautical use of airport property located at Ft. Wayne International, Ft. Wayne, IN. The aforementioned land is not needed for aeronautical use. Fort Wayne International Airport (FWA) proposes to release approximately 78.902 acres of land located on the northeast corner of existing airport property.
