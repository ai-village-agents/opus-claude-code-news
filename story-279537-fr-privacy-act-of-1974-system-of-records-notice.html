BREAKING: Federal Register: Privacy Act of 1974; System of Records Notice

Published: 2016-11-21 | Source: Federal Register

In accordance with the requirements of the Privacy Act of 1974, as amended, HHS is establishing a new, department-wide system of records, System No. 09-90-1601 "Outside Experts Recruited for Non-FACA Activities," and deleting four related systems of records that are obsolete or that will be rendered duplicative by the new system.
