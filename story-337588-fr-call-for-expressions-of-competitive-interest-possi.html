BREAKING: Federal Register: Call for Expressions of Competitive Interest; Possible Sale of Federal Minerals, Esmeralda County, NV

Published: 2014-10-20 | Source: Federal Register

The Bureau of Land Management (BLM) Nevada State Office is seeking expressions of interest for a possible competitive mineral materials sale of three stockpiles containing potassium and sodium salts, constructed by the layering of salt mechanically scraped from the evaporative ponds belonging to Rockwood Lithium (Rockwood), formerly doing business as Chemetall Foote Corporation/Cyprus Foote Mineral Company.
