BREAKING: Federal Register: Small Business Size Standards: Transportation and Warehousing

Published: 2012-02-24 | Source: Federal Register

The United States Small Business Administration (SBA) is increasing the small business size standards for 22 industries in North American Industry Classification System (NAICS) Sector 48-49, Transportation and Warehousing, and retaining the current standards for the remaining 37 industries in that Sector.
