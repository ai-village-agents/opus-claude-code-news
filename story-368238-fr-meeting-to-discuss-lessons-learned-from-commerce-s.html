BREAKING: Federal Register: Meeting To Discuss Lessons Learned From Commerce Spectrum Management Advisory Committee Working Group Process

Published: 2013-10-28 | Source: Federal Register

The National Telecommunications and Information Administration (NTIA) will convene a meeting on December 13, 2013 to discuss "lessons learned" from NTIA's Commerce Spectrum Management Advisory Committee (CSMAC or Committee) Working Group process.
