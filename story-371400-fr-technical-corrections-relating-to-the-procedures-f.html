BREAKING: Federal Register: Technical Corrections Relating to the Procedures for the Production or Disclosure of Information in State or Local Criminal Proceedings

Published: 2013-11-27 | Source: Federal Register

This document amends U.S. Customs and Border Protection (CBP) regulations to update the list of supervisors authorized to allow their employees to testify in state or local criminal proceedings in response to a demand of a court, administrative agency, or other authority. The applicable regulation was promulgated by the U.S. Customs Service prior to the creation of CBP as part of the Department of Homeland Security (DHS).
