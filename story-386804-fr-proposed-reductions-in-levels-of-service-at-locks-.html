BREAKING: Federal Register: Proposed Reductions in Levels of Service at Locks and Dams on the Ouachita and Black Rivers

Published: 2012-05-25 | Source: Federal Register

It is proposed that the hours of availability at Jonesville and Columbia Locks on the Ouachita and Black Rivers will be reduced from the current schedule of 24 hours per day, 7 days per week, 365 days per year to 18 hours per day, separated into two 9 hour periods, 5 a.m. to 2 p.m., and 5 p.m. to 2 a.m., 7 days per week, 365 days per year.
