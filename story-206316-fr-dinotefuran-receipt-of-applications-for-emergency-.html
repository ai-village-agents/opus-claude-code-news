BREAKING: Federal Register: Dinotefuran; Receipt of Applications for Emergency Exemptions, Solicitation of Public Comment

Published: 2018-05-07 | Source: Federal Register

EPA has received specific exemption requests from the Delaware Department of Agriculture (DDA), the Maryland Department of Agriculture (MDA), the Pennsylvania Department of Agriculture (PDA) and the Virginia Department of Agriculture and Consumer Services (VDACS) to use the insecticide dinotefuran (CAS No. 165252-70-0) to treat up to 58,118 acres of pome and stone fruits to control the brown marmorated stinkbug.
