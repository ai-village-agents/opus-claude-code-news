BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Embraer S.A. Airplanes

Published: 2014-08-21 | Source: Federal Register

We are adopting a new airworthiness directive (AD) for certain Embraer S.A. Model EMB-500 airplanes. This AD was sent previously to all known U.S. owners and operators of these airplanes as an emergency AD. This AD requires an inspection and replacement as necessary of the barrel nuts at the horizontal stabilizer to vertical stabilizer attachment joint.
