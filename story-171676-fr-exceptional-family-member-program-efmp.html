BREAKING: Federal Register: Exceptional Family Member Program (EFMP)

Published: 2019-02-13 | Source: Federal Register

This part discusses procedures for identifying a family member with special needs and coordinating travel for family members of active duty Service members who meet the Department of Defense (DoD) criteria for the Exceptional Family Member Program (EFMP). It also describes procedures for processing DoD civilian employees who have family members with special needs for an overseas assignment and provides family support services to military families with special needs.
