BREAKING: Federal Register: Receipt of Domestic Interested Party Petition Concerning the Tariff Classification of Cane Sugar Molasses and Liquid Sugar

Published: 2025-04-23 | Source: Federal Register

U.S. Customs and Border Protection (CBP) has received a petition submitted on behalf of a domestic interested party requesting the reclassification, under the Harmonized Tariff Schedule of the United States (HTSUS), of certain cane sugar molasses and liquid sugar. CBP currently classifies the subject cane sugar molasses under subheading 1703.10.30, HTSUS, as molasses, and the liquid sugar under subheading 1702.90.40, HTSUS.
