BREAKING: Federal Register: Securities Investor Protection Corporation

Published: 2013-11-05 | Source: Federal Register

The Securities Investor Protection Corporation ("SIPC") filed a proposed rule change with the Securities and Exchange Commission ("Commission"). The proposed rule change amends SIPC Rule 400, entitled "Rules Relating to Satisfaction of Customer Claims for Standardized Options," which relates to the satisfaction of customer claims for standardized options under the Securities Investor Protection Act of 1970 ("SIPA").
