BREAKING: Federal Register: Revision to Ambient Nitrogen Dioxide Monitoring Requirements

Published: 2013-03-14 | Source: Federal Register

The EPA is finalizing revisions to the deadlines established in the national ambient air quality standard (NAAQS) for nitrogen dioxide (NO2) for the near-road component of the NO2 monitoring network in order to implement a phased deployment approach. This approach will create a series of deadlines that will make the near-road NO2 network operational between January 1, 2014, and January 1, 2017.
