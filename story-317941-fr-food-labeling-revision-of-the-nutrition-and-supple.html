BREAKING: Federal Register: Food Labeling: Revision of the Nutrition and Supplement Facts Labels

Published: 2014-03-03 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA, the Agency, or we) is proposing to amend its labeling regulations for conventional foods and dietary supplements to provide updated nutrition information on the label to assist consumers in maintaining healthy dietary practices.
