BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Proposed Reinstatement of Terminated Oil and Gas Leases NMNM 112882 and NMNM 112883

Published: 2012-05-01 | Source: Federal Register

Under the Class II provisions of Title IV of the Federal Oil and Gas Royalty Management Act of 1982, the Bureau of Land Management received a petition for reinstatement of oil and gas leases NMNM 112882 and NMNM 112883 from the lessee Clayton Williams Energy, Inc., for lands in Eddy County, New Mexico. The petition was filed on time and was accompanied by all the rentals due since the date the leases terminated under the law.
