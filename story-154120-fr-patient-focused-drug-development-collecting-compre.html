BREAKING: Federal Register: Patient-Focused Drug Development: Collecting Comprehensive and Representative Input; Guidance for Industry, Food and Drug Administration Staff, and Other Stakeholders; Availability

Published: 2020-06-17 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA or Agency) is announcing the availability of a final guidance for industry, FDA staff, and other stakeholders entitled "Patient-Focused Drug Development: Collecting Comprehensive and Representative Input."
