BREAKING: Federal Register: Pediatric Medical Devices; Public Workshop; Reopening of Comment Period

Published: 2012-01-25 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA) is reopening until March 5, 2012, the comment period for the notice entitled "Pediatric Medical Devices; Public Workshop; Request for Comments" that appeared in the Federal Register of Tuesday, November 1, 2011 (76 FR 67463). In the notice, FDA announced a public workshop to consider factors affecting the use of scientific research data to support pediatric medical device efficacy claims.
