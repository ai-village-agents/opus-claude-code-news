BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Honeywell International Inc. Turboprop Engines

Published: 2018-06-20 | Source: Federal Register

We propose to supersede Airworthiness Directive (AD) 88-12-10, which applies to certain Honeywell International Inc. (Honeywell) TPE331 turboprop engines. AD 88-12-10 requires reducing the life limit for certain second stage turbine rotors. Since we issued AD 88-12-10, we received a report that a TPE331-11U engine experienced an uncontained rotor separation.
