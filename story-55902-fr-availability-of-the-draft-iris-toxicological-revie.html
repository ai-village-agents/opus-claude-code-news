BREAKING: Federal Register: Availability of the Draft IRIS Toxicological Review of Chloroform (Inhalation)

Published: 2024-12-18 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is announcing a 60- day public comment period associated with release of the draft Integrated Risk Information System (IRIS) Toxicological Review of Chloroform (Inhalation). The draft document was prepared by the Center for Public Health and Environmental Assessment (CPHEA) within EPA's Office of Research and Development (ORD). EPA is releasing this draft IRIS assessment for public comment in advance of a Science Advisory Board (SAB) managed peer review....
