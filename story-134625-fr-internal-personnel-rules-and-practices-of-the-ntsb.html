BREAKING: Federal Register: Internal Personnel Rules and Practices of the NTSB

Published: 2021-10-04 | Source: Federal Register

The National Transportation Safety Board (NTSB) is amending its Internal Personnel Rules and Practices to reflect that the agency is closing its "public reference room" and will now make qualifying records electronically available.
