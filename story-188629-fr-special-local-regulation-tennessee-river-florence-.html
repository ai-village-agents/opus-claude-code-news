BREAKING: Federal Register: Special Local Regulation; Tennessee River, Florence, AL

Published: 2019-09-13 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is establishing a temporary Special Local Regulation for all navigable waters of the Tennessee River, extending the entire width of the river, from mile marker (MM) 254.0 to 258.0. This action is necessary to provide for the safety of life on these navigable waters near Florence, AL, during a Triathlon on September 22, 2019.
