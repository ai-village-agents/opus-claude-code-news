BREAKING: Federal Register: Request for Nominations for the Federal System Funding Alternative Advisory Board to the Federal Highway Administration

Published: 2023-10-03 | Source: Federal Register

The FHWA announces a solicitation of Membership to the Federal System Funding Alternative Advisory Board (Advisory Board). Advisory Board members will serve for 2 years after the date on which the Advisory Board is established, with the potential for reappointment.
