BREAKING: Federal Register: Most Favored Nation (MFN) Model

Published: 2020-11-27 | Source: Federal Register

This interim final rule with comment period (IFC) implements the Most Favored Nation (MFN) Model, a new Medicare payment model under section 1115A of the Social Security Act (the Act).
