BREAKING: Federal Register: Privacy Act of 1974; System Records

Published: 2019-12-20 | Source: Federal Register

The Office of Government Ethics (OGE) proposes to create a new system of records pursuant to the provisions of the Privacy Act of 1974. This system of records contains personally identifiable information (PII) collected from Federal employees and/or members of the public who register to attend or otherwise participate in OGE- hosted meetings and events.
