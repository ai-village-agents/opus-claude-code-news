BREAKING: Federal Register: Updates to List of National System of Marine Protected Areas (MPAs)

Published: 2012-04-19 | Source: Federal Register

The National System of Marine Protected Areas (MPAs) provides a mechanism for MPAs managed by diverse government agencies to work together on common conservation priorities. In July 2011, NOAA and the Department of the Interior (DOI) invited federal, state, commonwealth, territorial and tribal MPA programs with potentially eligible existing MPAs to nominate their sites to the National System of MPAs (national system).
