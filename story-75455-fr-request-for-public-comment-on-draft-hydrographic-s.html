BREAKING: Federal Register: Request for Public Comment on Draft Hydrographic Survey Specifications and Deliverables

Published: 2023-08-28 | Source: Federal Register

National Oceanic and Atmospheric Administration's (NOAA's) Office of Coast Survey (OCS) requests public comment from all interested parties on a draft of the newly-revised Hydrographic Survey Specifications and Deliverables document (HSSD).
