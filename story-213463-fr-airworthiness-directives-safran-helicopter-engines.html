BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Safran Helicopter Engines, S.A., Turboshaft Engines

Published: 2018-08-08 | Source: Federal Register

We are superseding Airworthiness Directive (AD) 2012-03-11 for all Safran Helicopter Engines, S.A., Arriel 2B and 2B1 turboshaft engines. AD 2012-03-11 required checking the transmissible torque between the low-pressure (LP) pump impeller and the high-pressure (HP) pump shaft on the HP/LP pump and metering valve assembly, hereafter referred to as the hydro-mechanical metering unit (HMU).
