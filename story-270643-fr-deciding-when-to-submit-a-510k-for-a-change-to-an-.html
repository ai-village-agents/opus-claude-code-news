BREAKING: Federal Register: Deciding When To Submit a 510(k) for a Change to an Existing Device; Draft Guidance for Industry and Food and Drug Administration Staff; Availability

Published: 2016-08-08 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA or Agency) is announcing the availability of the draft guidance entitled "Deciding When to Submit a 510(k) for a Change to an Existing Device." FDA is issuing this draft guidance document to clarify when a change in a legally marketed medical device would require that a manufacturer submit a premarket notification (510(k)) to FDA.
