BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Request for Information (RFI) on Lighting R&D Opportunities

Published: 2019-11-08 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Department of Energy (DOE) invites public comment on its Request for Information (RFI) number DE-FOA-0002160 regarding the DOE Building Technologies Office (BTO) Lighting Research and Development (R&D) Program. The purpose of this RFI is to seek broad stakeholder input to inform the strategic direction of the DOE Lighting Research & Development (R&D) Program and resulting portfolio.
