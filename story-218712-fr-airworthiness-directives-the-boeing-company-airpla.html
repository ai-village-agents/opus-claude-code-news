BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; The Boeing Company Airplanes

Published: 2018-10-15 | Source: Federal Register

We propose to adopt a new airworthiness directive (AD) for certain The Boeing Company Model 757 airplanes. This proposed AD was prompted by an evaluation by the design approval holder (DAH) indicating that the inner skin at the lower fastener row is subject to widespread fatigue damage (WFD).
