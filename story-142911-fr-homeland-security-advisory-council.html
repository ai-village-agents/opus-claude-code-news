BREAKING: Federal Register: Homeland Security Advisory Council

Published: 2020-01-28 | Source: Federal Register

The Homeland Security Advisory Council ("HSAC" or "Council") will meet via teleconference on Monday, February 24, 2020 to announce new taskings, appoint new members, and review, deliberate, and vote on the final draft reports of the Emerging Technology Subcommittee focused on Unmanned Aircraft Systems and 3D printing. The meeting will be open to the public.
