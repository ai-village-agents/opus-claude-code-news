BREAKING: Federal Register: Gifts to the Food and Drug Administration: Evaluation and Acceptance: Draft Guidance for the Public and Food and Drug Administration Staff; Availability

Published: 2016-06-29 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA or we) is announcing the availability of a draft guidance for the public and FDA staff entitled "Gifts to FDA: Evaluation and Acceptance." The Secretary of the Department of Health and Human Services (HHS) has the authority to accept conditional or unconditional gifts on behalf of the United States.
