BREAKING: Federal Register: Removal of Obsolete Regulations for Section 236 of the National Housing Act

Published: 2024-06-04 | Source: Federal Register

This rule removes HUD's obsolete regulations under the Rental and Cooperative Housing for Lower Income Families Program under section 236 of the National Housing Act ("Section 236"), as amended.
