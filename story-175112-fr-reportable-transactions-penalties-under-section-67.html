BREAKING: Federal Register: Reportable Transactions Penalties Under Section 6707A

Published: 2019-03-26 | Source: Federal Register

This document contains final regulations that provide guidance regarding the amount of the penalty under section 6707A of the Internal Revenue Code (Code) for failure to include on any return or statement any information required to be disclosed under section 6011 with respect to a reportable transaction.
