The Food and Drug Administration (FDA or the Agency) is announcing a regional public meeting entitled "U.S. Food and Drug Administration and Health Canada Joint Regional Consultation on International Council for Harmonisation of Technical Requirements for Pharmaceuticals for Human Use (ICH)."
