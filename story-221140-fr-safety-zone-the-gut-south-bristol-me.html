BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone; The Gut, South Bristol, ME

Published: 2018-11-15 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is establishing a temporary safety zone for the navigable waters within a 50 yard radius from the center point of The Gut Bridge in South Bristol, ME between Rutherford Island and Bristol Neck. The safety zone is necessary to protect personnel, vessels, and the marine environment from potential hazards created during bedrock removal operations.
