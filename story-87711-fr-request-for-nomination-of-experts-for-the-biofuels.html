BREAKING: Federal Register: Request for Nomination of Experts for the Biofuels and the Environment: Third Triennial Report to Congress Peer Review Panel

Published: 2022-02-01 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is requesting nominations for an external expert panel to peer review EPA's Biofuels and the Environment: Third Triennial Report to Congress (RtC3). The peer review will be conducted under the framework of EPA's Scientific Integrity Policy (https://www.epa.gov/sites/default/files/2014-02/ documents/scientific_integrity_policy_2012.pdf) and follow procedures established in EPA's Peer Review Handbook 4th Edition, 2015 (EPA/100/B- 15/001).
