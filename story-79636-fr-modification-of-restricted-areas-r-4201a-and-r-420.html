BREAKING: Federal Register: Modification of Restricted Areas R-4201A and R-4201B; Camp Grayling, MI

Published: 2023-10-13 | Source: Federal Register

This action proposes to modify the designated altitudes of restricted area R-4201B, Camp Grayling, MI, by raising the restricted area ceiling from 9,000 feet mean sea level (MSL) to 23,000 feet MSL to match the ceiling of the adjacent restricted area R-4201A, Camp Grayling, MI.
