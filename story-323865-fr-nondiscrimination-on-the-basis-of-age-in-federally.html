BREAKING: Federal Register: Nondiscrimination on the Basis of Age in Federally Assisted Programs or Activities

Published: 2014-05-09 | Source: Federal Register

In this final rule, the National Endowment for the Humanities (NEH) is issuing Age Discrimination Act of 1975 (the Act or the Age Act) regulations. These regulations implement provisions of the Act and the general, government-wide age discrimination regulations promulgated by the United States Department of Health and Human Services (HHS).
