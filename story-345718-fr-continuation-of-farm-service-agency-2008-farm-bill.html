BREAKING: Federal Register: Continuation of Farm Service Agency 2008 Farm Bill Programs

Published: 2013-02-01 | Source: Federal Register

The American Taxpayer Relief Act of 2012 (ATRA) extended the authorization of the Food, Conservation, and Energy Act of 2008 (the 2008 Farm Bill) through the 2013 crop year, fiscal year (FY), or calendar year, as applicable, for certain Commodity Credit Corporation (CCC) commodity and conservation programs administered by the Farm Service Agency (FSA).
