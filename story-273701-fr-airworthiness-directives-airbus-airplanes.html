BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Airbus Airplanes

Published: 2016-09-12 | Source: Federal Register

We propose to supersede Airworthiness Directive (AD) 2015-16- 02, for all Airbus Model A330 series airplanes. AD 2015-16-02 currently requires revising the maintenance program or inspection program to incorporate certain maintenance requirements and airworthiness limitations.
