BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Intent to Prepare an Environmental Impact Statement and Notice of Scoping Meeting

Published: 2012-06-18 | Source: Federal Register

In accordance with the National Environmental Policy Act (NEPA), 42 U.S.C. 4321-4347, the Department of State (DOS) announces its intent to prepare an Environmental Impact Statement (EIS) and initiate the scoping process for the proposed agency action as specified below.
