BREAKING: Federal Register: Extension of Emergency Measures To Address Overfishing of Atlantic Shortfin Mako Shark

Published: 2018-08-22 | Source: Federal Register

This rule extends the measures in an emergency interim final rule for the North Atlantic shortfin mako shark fishery published on March 2, 2018. This extension is necessary to maintain measures that address overfishing of shortfin mako sharks while long-term measures are developed and to meet requirements of a recommendation of the International Commission for the Conservation of Atlantic Tunas (ICCAT).
