BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone; Redwood City Fourth of July Fireworks; Redwood Creek, Redwood City, CA

Published: 2022-06-29 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is establishing a temporary safety zone on the navigable waters of the Redwood Creek in Redwood City, CA in support of a fireworks display on July 4, 2022. The safety zone is necessary to protect personnel, vessels, and the marine environment from potential hazards created by pyrotechnics.
