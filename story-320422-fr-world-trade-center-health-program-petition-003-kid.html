BREAKING: Federal Register: World Trade Center Health Program; Petition 003-Kidney Damage; Finding of Insufficient Evidence

Published: 2014-03-31 | Source: Federal Register

On January 22, 2014, the Administrator of the World Trade Center (WTC) Health Program received a petition to add "kidney damage" (Petition 003) to the List of WTC-Related Health Conditions (List). The Administrator has not found sufficient scientific evidence to conduct an analysis of whether to add kidney damage and/or disease to the List.
