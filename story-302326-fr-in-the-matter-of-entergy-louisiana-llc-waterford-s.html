BREAKING: Federal Register: In the Matter of Entergy Louisiana, LLC; Waterford Steam Electric Station, Unit 3

Published: 2015-08-24 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Nuclear Regulatory Commission (NRC) is issuing an order to permit the direct transfer of Facility Operating License No. NPF-38 for Waterford Steam Electric Station, Unit 3 (Waterford), to a newly formed limited liability company named Entergy Louisiana, LLC. The new owner licensee has the same name as the previous owner licensee.
