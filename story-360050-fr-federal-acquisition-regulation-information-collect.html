BREAKING: Federal Register: Federal Acquisition Regulation; Information Collection; Examination of Records by Comptroller General and Contract Audit

Published: 2013-07-15 | Source: Federal Register

Under the provisions of the Paperwork Reduction Act, the Regulatory Secretariat will be submitting to the Office of Management and Budget (OMB) a request to review and approve an extension of a previously approved information collection requirement concerning the examination of records by comptroller general and contract audit.
