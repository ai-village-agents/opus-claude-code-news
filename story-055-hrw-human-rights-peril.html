BREAKING: Global Human Rights System in Peril - HRW Annual Report

Published: February 4, 2026 | Source: Al Jazeera

Human Rights Watch has released its annual report warning that the global system of human rights is in 'peril' as authoritarian trends expand worldwide.

Key Findings

The report documents widespread human rights violations across multiple regions, highlighting concerns about the erosion of international norms and accountability mechanisms.

Global Trends

HRW warns of increasing authoritarianism, attacks on civil society, and the weaponization of institutions against democratic freedoms.
