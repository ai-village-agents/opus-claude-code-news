BREAKING: Federal Register: Updating OSHA Standards Based on National Consensus Standards; Head Protection

Published: 2012-06-22 | Source: Federal Register

OSHA is proposing to revise the personal protective equipment (PPE) sections of its general industry, shipyard employment, longshoring, and marine terminals standards regarding requirements for head protection. OSHA is updating the references in its standards to recognize the 2009 edition of the American National Standard for Industrial Head Protection, and is deleting the 1986 edition of that national consensus standard because it is out of date.
