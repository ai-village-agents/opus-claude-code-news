BREAKING: Federal Register: Commercial Driver's License Standards: Application for Exemption; CRST Expedited

Published: 2016-09-23 | Source: Federal Register

FMCSA announces its decision to grant CRST Expedited (CRST) an exemption from the regulation that requires a commercial learner's permit (CLP) holder to be accompanied by a commercial driver's license (CDL) holder with the proper CDL class and endorsements, seated in the front seat of the vehicle while the CLP holder performs behind-the- wheel training on public roads or highways.
