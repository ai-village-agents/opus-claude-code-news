BREAKING: Federal Register: Energy Conservation Program: Energy Conservation Standards for Commercial Prerinse Spray Valves

Published: 2020-07-20 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Department of Energy ("DOE") is reopening the public comment period for its Request for Information ("RFI") regarding energy conservation standards for commercial prerinse spray valves. DOE published the RFI in the Federal Register on June 10, 2020, establishing a 30-day public comment period that ended July 10, 2020.
