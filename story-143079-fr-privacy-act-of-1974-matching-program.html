BREAKING: Federal Register: Privacy Act of 1974; Matching Program

Published: 2020-01-29 | Source: Federal Register

In accordance with the provisions of the Privacy Act, as amended, this notice announces a new matching program with the Department of the Treasury--Internal Revenue Service (IRS).
