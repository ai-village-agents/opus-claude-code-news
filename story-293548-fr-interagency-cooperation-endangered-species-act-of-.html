BREAKING: Federal Register: Interagency Cooperation-Endangered Species Act of 1973, as Amended; Incidental Take Statements

Published: 2015-05-11 | Source: Federal Register

We, the U.S. Fish and Wildlife Service and the National Marine Fisheries Service (collectively, the Services), are amending the incidental take statement provisions of the implementing regulations for section 7 of the Endangered Species Act of 1973, as amended (ESA).
