BREAKING: Federal Register: Sec. 221 Public Private Partnerships Public Meeting

Published: 2012-05-22 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA is conducting a public meeting on May 30 to seek initial input from interested stakeholders about program design and implementation of an equipage incentives program for commercial aircraft and general aviation to equip their aircraft with Next Generation Air Transportation (NextGen) capabilities, pursuant to the FAA's authority in the FAA Modernization and Reform Act of 2012 (sec. 221).
