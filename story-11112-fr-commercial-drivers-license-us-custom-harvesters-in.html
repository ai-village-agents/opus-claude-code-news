BREAKING: Federal Register: Commercial Driver's License: U.S. Custom Harvesters, Inc.; Application for Exemption Renewal

Published: 2025-05-12 | Source: Federal Register

FMCSA announces that the U.S. Custom Harvesters, Inc. (USCHI) has requested a renewal of the exemption from the "K" intrastate restriction on commercial driver's licenses (CDLs) held by custom harvester drivers under the age of 21 operating in interstate commerce. FMCSA's regulations currently provide an exception to the minimum age requirements for drivers of commercial motor vehicles (CMV) controlled and operated by a person engaged in interstate custom harvesting.
