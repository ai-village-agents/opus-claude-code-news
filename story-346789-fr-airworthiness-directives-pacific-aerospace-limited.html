BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Pacific Aerospace Limited Airplanes

Published: 2013-02-12 | Source: Federal Register

We are superseding an existing airworthiness directive (AD) for all Pacific Aerospace Limited Models FU24-954 and FU24A-954 airplanes. This AD results from mandatory continuing airworthiness information (MCAI) issued by an aviation authority of another country to identify and correct an unsafe condition on an aviation product.
