BREAKING: Federal Register: Food and Drug Administration Tribal Consultation Policy; Availability

Published: 2017-01-05 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA or we) is announcing the availability of the FDA Tribal Consultation Policy. The purpose of the FDA Tribal Consultation Policy is to further the government-to- government relationship between FDA and American Indian and Alaskan Native Tribes (Indian Tribes) and facilitate tribal consultation with FDA.
