BREAKING: Federal Register: The NCUA Staff Draft 2025-2026 Budget Justification

Published: 2024-11-04 | Source: Federal Register

The NCUA's staff draft "detailed business-type budget" is being made available for public review as required by Federal statute. The proposed resources will finance the agency's annual operations and capital projects, both of which are necessary for the agency to accomplish its mission of protecting the system of cooperative credit and its member-owners through effective chartering, supervision, regulation, and insurance.
