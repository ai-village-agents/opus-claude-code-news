BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Rolls-Royce plc Turbofan Engines

Published: 2014-09-11 | Source: Federal Register

We propose to supersede airworthiness directive (AD) 2013-15- 10 that applies to certain Rolls-Royce (RR) RB211 turbofan engines. AD 2013-15-10 requires inspecting the intermediate-pressure compressor (IPC) rotor shaft rear balance land for cracks. This AD eliminates a terminating action, expands one inspection and eliminates others.
