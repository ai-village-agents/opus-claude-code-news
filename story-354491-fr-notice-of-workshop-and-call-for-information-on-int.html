BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Workshop and Call for Information on Integrated Science Assessment for Oxides of Sulfur

Published: 2013-05-10 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. EPA Office of Research and Development's National Center for Environmental Assessment (NCEA) is preparing an Integrated Science Assessment (ISA) as part of the review of the primary National Ambient Air Quality Standards (NAAQS) for oxides of sulfur (SOX) (for which the indicator is sulfur dioxide [SO2]).
