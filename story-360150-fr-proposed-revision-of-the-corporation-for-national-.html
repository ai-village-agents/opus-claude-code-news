BREAKING: Federal Register: Proposed Revision of the Corporation for National and Community Service Strategic Plan; Request for Input

Published: 2013-07-15 | Source: Federal Register

The Corporation for National and Community Service (CNCS) is revising its Strategic Plan. The current CNCS Strategic Plan was approved in 2011. All Federal Agencies are required to publish an updated Strategic Plan, concurrent with the publication of the FY 2015 President's Budget in February 2014. After the February 2014 publication of a strategic plan, agencies will next issue a new Strategic Plan in February 2018.
