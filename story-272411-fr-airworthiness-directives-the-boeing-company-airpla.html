BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; The Boeing Company Airplanes

Published: 2016-08-26 | Source: Federal Register

We are adopting a new airworthiness directive (AD) for all The Boeing Company Model 777 airplanes. This AD was prompted by a report of an incident involving a landing in which the pilots needed to input corrections due to airplane yaw and roll to the right; the main landing gear (MLG) aft trunnion pin was later found to be fractured.
