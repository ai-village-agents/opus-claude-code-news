BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Airbus Airplanes

Published: 2014-09-26 | Source: Federal Register

We are superseding Airworthiness Directive (AD) 2004-03-19, which applies to certain Airbus Model A320-111, -211 and -231 series airplanes. AD 2004-03-19 required repetitive inspections for cracking in the transition and pick-up angles in the lower part of the center fuselage area, and corrective action if necessary.
