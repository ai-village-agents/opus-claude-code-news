BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Availability and Request for Comment: Revision to the Voluntary Standard for Bassinets and Cradles

Published: 2022-07-28 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Consumer Product Safety Commission's (Commission or CPSC) mandatory rule, Safety Standard for Bassinets and Cradles, incorporates by reference ASTM F2194-13, Standard Consumer Safety Specification for Bassinets and Cradles, with modifications. ASTM revised F2194-13 several times between 2013 and 2022, but did not notify the Commission of those revisions.
