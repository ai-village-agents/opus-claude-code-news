BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Proposed Airport Access Restriction and Opportunity for Public Comment

Published: 2014-06-27 | Source: Federal Register

The Airport Noise and Capacity Act of 1990 (hereinafter referred to as "the Act" or "ANCA") provides notice, review, and approval requirements for airports seeking to impose noise or access restrictions on Stage 3 aircraft operations that become effective after October 1, 1990. 49 U.S.C. 47521 et seq. This notice is issued pursuant to ANCA and 14 CFR 161.315(b).
