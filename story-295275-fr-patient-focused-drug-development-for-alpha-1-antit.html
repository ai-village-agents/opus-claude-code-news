BREAKING: Federal Register: Patient-Focused Drug Development for Alpha-1 Antitrypsin Deficiency; Public Meeting

Published: 2015-06-01 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA or Agency) is announcing a public meeting and an opportunity for public comment on Patient- Focused Drug Development for Alpha-1 Antitrypsin Deficiency (AATD). Patient-Focused Drug Development is an FDA performance commitment under the fifth authorization of the Prescription Drug User Fee Act (PDUFA V).
