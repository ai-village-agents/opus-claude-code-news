BREAKING: Federal Register: Michelin North America, Inc., Receipt of Petition for Decision of Inconsequential Noncompliance

Published: 2024-03-26 | Source: Federal Register

Michelin North America, Inc. (MNA) has determined that certain Michelin LTX AT2 tires do not fully comply with Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard (FMVSS) No. 139, New Pneumatic Radial Tires For Light Vehicles. MNA filed an original noncompliance report dated April 14, 2023, and later amended the report on July 3, 2023.
