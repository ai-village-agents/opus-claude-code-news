BREAKING: Federal Register: Pacific Fishery Management Council; Public Meetings

Published: 2024-03-20 | Source: Federal Register

The Pacific Fishery Management Council (Council) and its advisory bodies will meet April 5-11, 2024 in Seattle, WA and via webinar. The Council meeting will be live streamed with the opportunity to provide public comment remotely.
