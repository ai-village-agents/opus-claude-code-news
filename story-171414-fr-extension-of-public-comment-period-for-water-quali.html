BREAKING: Federal Register: Extension of Public Comment Period for Water Quality Standards; Establishment of a Numeric Criterion for Selenium for the State of California

Published: 2019-02-12 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is extending the comment period for the proposed rule "Water Quality Standards; Establishment of a Numeric Criterion for Selenium for the State of California" for an additional 45 days, from February 11, 2019, to March 28, 2019. The EPA will offer virtual public hearings on the proposed rule via the internet on March 19, 2019, and March 20, 2019.
