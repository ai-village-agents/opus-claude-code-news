BREAKING: Federal Register: Significant New Use Rules on Certain Chemical Substances; Withdrawal

Published: 2014-12-23 | Source: Federal Register

EPA is withdrawing significant new use rules (SNURs) promulgated under the Toxic Substances Control Act (TSCA) for thirty chemical substances, which were the subject of premanufacture notices (PMNs). EPA published these SNURs using direct final rulemaking procedures, which requires EPA to take certain actions if an adverse comment is received.
