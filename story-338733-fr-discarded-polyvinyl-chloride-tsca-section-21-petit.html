BREAKING: Federal Register: Discarded Polyvinyl Chloride; TSCA Section 21 Petition; Reasons for Agency Response

Published: 2014-10-31 | Source: Federal Register

This document announces the availability of EPA's response to a petition it received under the Toxic Substances Control Act (TSCA). The TSCA section 21 petition was received from the Center for Biological Diversity (CBD) on July 29, 2014. The petitioner requested that EPA initiate rulemaking under TSCA to address risks related to polyvinyl chloride (PVC), vinyl chloride, and phthalates used as plasticizers.
