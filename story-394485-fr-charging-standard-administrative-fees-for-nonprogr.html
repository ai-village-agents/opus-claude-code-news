BREAKING: Federal Register: Charging Standard Administrative Fees for Nonprogram-Related Information

Published: 2012-08-22 | Source: Federal Register

We are announcing the standardized administrative fees we will charge to recover our full cost of providing information and related services for nonprogram purposes we provide to the public. Previously, the fees we charged for nonprogram information requests were not standardized.
