BREAKING: Federal Register: Reporting and Distribution of Royalties to Copyright Owners by the Mechanical Licensing Collective

Published: 2020-09-17 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Copyright Office is issuing an interim rule regarding the obligations of the mechanical licensing collective to report and distribute royalties paid by digital music providers under the blanket license to musical work copyright owners under title I of the Orrin G. Hatch-Bob Goodlatte Music Modernization Act.
