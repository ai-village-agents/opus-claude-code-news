BREAKING: Federal Register: Hollings Manufacturing Extension Partnership-Amendment to Venue for Publishing Notices of Funding Opportunities for Financial Assistance

Published: 2021-10-08 | Source: Federal Register

NIST is issuing a final rule to amend the regulations governing the Hollings Manufacturing Extension Partnership (MEP) program to reflect the current requirements for publishing Notices of Funding Opportunities (NOFOs) for the establishment and operation of MEP Centers, consistent with the current MEP authorizing statute and Department of Commerce (Department or DOC) policy.
