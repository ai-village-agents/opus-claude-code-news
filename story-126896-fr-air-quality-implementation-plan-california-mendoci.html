BREAKING: Federal Register: Air Quality Implementation Plan; California; Mendocino County Air Quality Management District; Stationary Source Permits

Published: 2021-06-25 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is taking final action to approve revisions to the Mendocino County Air Quality Managment District (MCAQMD or "District") portion of the California State Implementation Plan (SIP). These revisions concern the District's prevention of significant deterioration (PSD) permitting program for new and modified stationary sources of air pollution. We are approving these local rules pursuant to requirements under part C of title I of the Clean Air Act as amended...
