BREAKING: Federal Register: Southeastern Pennsylvania Transportation Authority's Request To Amend Its Positive Train Control System

Published: 2023-06-27 | Source: Federal Register

This document provides the public with notice that, on June 2 and 7, 2023, the Southeastern Pennsylvania Transportation Authority (SEPTA) submitted a request for amendment (RFA) to its FRA-certified positive train control (PTC) system. FRA is publishing this notice and inviting public comment on the railroad's RFA to its PTC system.
