BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Sikorsky Aircraft Corporation Helicopters

Published: 2013-10-02 | Source: Federal Register

We are adopting a new airworthiness directive (AD) for Sikorsky Aircraft Corporation (Sikorsky) Model S-92A helicopters to require modifying the No. 1 engine forward firewall center fire extinguisher discharge tube (No. 1 engine tube) and inspecting the outboard discharge tube to determine if it is correctly positioned.
