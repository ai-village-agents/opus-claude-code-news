BREAKING: Federal Register: Petition on Clean Air Act Title V Permit; Gateway Generating Station; Antioch, CA

Published: 2014-11-21 | Source: Federal Register

Pursuant to Clean Air Act (CAA) section 505(b) and 40 CFR 70.8(d), the United States Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) Administrator signed an Order, dated October 15, 2014, denying a petition to object to a CAA title V operating permit proposed by the Bay Area Air Quality Management District (BAAQMD) for the Gateway Generating Station, LLC facility in Antioch, California (Gateway).
