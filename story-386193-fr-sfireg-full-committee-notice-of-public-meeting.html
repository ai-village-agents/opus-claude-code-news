BREAKING: Federal Register: SFIREG Full Committee; Notice of Public Meeting

Published: 2012-05-18 | Source: Federal Register

The Association of American Pesticide Control Officials (AAPCO)/State FIFRA Issues Research and Evaluation Group (SFIREG), Full Committee will hold a 2-day meeting, beginning on June 18, 2012 and ending June 19, 2012. This notice announces the location and times for the meeting and sets forth the tentative agenda topics.
