BREAKING: Federal Register: Fair Hiring in Banking Act

Published: 2024-08-07 | Source: Federal Register

The Federal Deposit Insurance Corporation (FDIC) is revising its regulations to conform with the Fair Hiring in Banking Act (FHBA)-- which was enacted on and immediately effective as of December 23, 2022. Among other provisions, the FHBA excluded or exempted categories of otherwise-covered offenses from the scope of statutory prohibitions on participation in banking. These categories pertain to certain older offenses, offenses committed by individuals 21 or younger, and relatively minor offenses...
