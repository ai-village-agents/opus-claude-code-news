BREAKING: Federal Register: Assessment Report of Ecological/Social/Economic Sustainability, Conditions, and Trends for the Tonto National Forest

Published: 2014-02-26 | Source: Federal Register

The Tonto National Forest, located in Arizona, is initiating the forest planning process pursuant to the 2012 Forest Planning Rule. This process results in a Forest Land Management Plan which describes the strategic direction for management of forest resources for the next ten to fifteen years on the Tonto National Forest.
