BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; The Boeing Company Airplanes

Published: 2012-02-17 | Source: Federal Register

We are adopting a new airworthiness directive (AD) for certain The Boeing Company Model 767-200, -300, and -300F series airplanes. This AD was prompted by reports of loss of avionics cooling due to an unserviceable relay installed on a panel as part of the cabin air conditioning and temperature control system (CACTCS).
