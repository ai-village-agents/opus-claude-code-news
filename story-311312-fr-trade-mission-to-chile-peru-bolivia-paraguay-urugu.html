BREAKING: Federal Register: Trade Mission to Chile, Peru, Bolivia, Paraguay, Uruguay, Mexico and Argentina in Conjunction With Trade Winds-Latin America Business Forum

Published: 2015-12-10 | Source: Federal Register

The United States Department of Commerce, International Trade Administration is organizing a trade mission to Chile, Peru, Bolivia, Paraguay, Uruguay, Mexico and Argentina that will include the Trade Winds--Latin America business forum in Santiago, Chile on September 7- 9, 2016. U.S. trade mission members will participate in the Trade Winds--Latin America business forum in Santiago, Chile, which is also open to U.S. companies not participating in the trade mission.
