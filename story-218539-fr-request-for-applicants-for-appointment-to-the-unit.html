BREAKING: Federal Register: Request for Applicants for Appointment to the United States-Brazil CEO Forum

Published: 2018-10-12 | Source: Federal Register

In March 2007, the Governments of the United States and Brazil established the U.S.-Brazil CEO Forum. This notice announces the opportunity for up to twelve individuals for appointment to the U.S. Section of the Forum. The three-year term of the incoming members of the U.S. Section starts on December 1, 2018, and will expire November 30, 2021.
