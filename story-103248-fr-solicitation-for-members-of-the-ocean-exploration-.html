BREAKING: Federal Register: Solicitation for Members of the Ocean Exploration Advisory Board

Published: 2022-08-19 | Source: Federal Register

NOAA is soliciting nominations for Indigenous, Tribal, Native American, Alaska Native, or Native Hawaiian stakeholders from the Alaska or Pacific Ocean basin regions to join the Ocean Exploration Advisory Board (OEAB).
