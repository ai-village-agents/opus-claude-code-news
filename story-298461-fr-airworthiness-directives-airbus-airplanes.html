BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Airbus Airplanes

Published: 2015-07-08 | Source: Federal Register

We propose to adopt a new airworthiness directive (AD) for all Airbus Model A318, A319, and A321 series airplanes. This proposed AD was prompted by reports of in-flight loss of fixed and hinged main landing gear (MLG) fairings, and reports of post-modification MLG fixed fairing assemblies that have wear and corrosion.
