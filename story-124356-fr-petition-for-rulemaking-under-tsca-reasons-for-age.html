BREAKING: Federal Register: Petition for Rulemaking Under TSCA; Reasons for Agency Response; Denial of Requested Rulemaking

Published: 2021-05-21 | Source: Federal Register

This document announces the availability of EPA's response to a portion of the petition it received February 8, 2021, from People for Protecting Peace River, Center for Biological Diversity, and 16 other organizations. While the petition requested three actions related to TSCA, EPA has determined that only one of those actions is an appropriate request: A request to issue a test rule under TSCA requiring testing of phosphogypsum and process wastewater from phosphoric acid production.
