BREAKING: Federal Register: Clearing Agency Governance and Conflicts of Interest

Published: 2022-08-23 | Source: Federal Register

The Securities and Exchange Commission ("Commission") is proposing rules under the Securities Exchange Act of 1934 ("Exchange Act") to help improve the governance of clearing agencies registered with the Commission ("registered clearing agencies") by reducing the likelihood that conflicts of interest may influence the board of directors or equivalent governing body ("board") of a registered clearing agency.
