BREAKING: Federal Register: Mercedes-Benz USA, LLC, Denial of Petition for Decision of Inconsequential Noncompliance

Published: 2022-12-13 | Source: Federal Register

Mercedes-Benz AG (MB AG) and Mercedes-Benz USA, LLC (MBUSA) (collectively, "Mercedes-Benz"), formerly known as Daimler AG has determined that certain model year (MY) 2019 Mercedes-Benz AMG GT motor vehicles do not fully comply with Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard (FMVSS) No. 201, Occupant Protection in Interior Impact.
