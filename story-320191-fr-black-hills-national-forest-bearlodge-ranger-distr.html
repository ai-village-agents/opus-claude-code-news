BREAKING: Federal Register: Black Hills National Forest, Bearlodge Ranger District; Wyoming; Bear Lodge Project

Published: 2014-03-28 | Source: Federal Register

A Plan of Operation has been submitted by Rare Element Resources, Inc., for the purpose of constructing and operating a rare earth elements mine in the Black Hills National Forest, Bearlodge District in Crook County, Wyoming. The Bear Lodge Project proposed action consists of the Bull Hill Mine, the Miller Creek access road, the power line, and ongoing mineral exploration.
