BREAKING: Federal Register: Supplemental Standards of Ethical Conduct for Employees of the Department of the Treasury; Correction

Published: 2015-02-12 | Source: Federal Register

This document contains corrections to a final rule that was published in the Federal Register on Thursday, November 6, 2014. The final rule amended the Department of the Treasury's (the Department or Treasury) Supplemental Standards of Ethical Conduct for Employees of the Department of the Treasury (Supplemental Standards) that was issued by the Department with the concurrence of the Office of Government Ethics (OGE).
