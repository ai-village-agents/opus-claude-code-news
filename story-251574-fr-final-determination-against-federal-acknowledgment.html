BREAKING: Federal Register: Final Determination Against Federal Acknowledgment of the Georgia Tribe of Eastern Cherokee

Published: 2017-12-26 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of the Interior (Department) gives notice that the Principal Deputy Assistant Secretary--Indian Affairs, exercising the authority of the Assistant Secretary--Indian Affairs has determined that the Georgia Tribe of Eastern Cherokee (GTEC) is not an Indian Tribe within the meaning of Federal law. This notice is based on a determination that affirms the reasoning, analysis, and conclusions in the Proposed Finding (PF) that the petitioner does not satisfy the seven mandatory criteria ...
