BREAKING: Federal Register: National Standards for the Physical Inspection of Real Estate: Inspection Standards

Published: 2023-06-22 | Source: Federal Register

This notice of NSPIRE Inspection Standards serves as a complementary document to the Economic Growth Regulatory Relief and Consumer Protection Act: Implementation of National Standards for the Physical Inspection of Real Estate (NSPIRE) rule. The rule provides that HUD publish in the Federal Register a set of NSPIRE inspection Standards to consolidate and align housing quality requirements and associated inspection Standards across programs.
