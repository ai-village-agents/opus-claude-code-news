BREAKING: Federal Register: Manual of Regulations and Procedures for Federal Radio Frequency Management

Published: 2023-11-21 | Source: Federal Register

The National Telecommunications and Information Administration (NTIA) is making certain changes to its regulations relating to the public availability of the Manual of Regulations and Procedures for Federal Radio Frequency Management (NTIA Manual). NTIA has the authority, delegated by the president, to assign frequencies to radio stations or classes of radio stations belonging to and operated by the United States.
