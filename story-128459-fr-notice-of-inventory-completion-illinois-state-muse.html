BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Inventory Completion: Illinois State Museum, Springfield, IL

Published: 2021-07-19 | Source: Federal Register

The Illinois State Museum has completed an inventory of associated funerary objects, in consultation with the appropriate Indian Tribes or Native Hawaiian organizations, and has determined that there is a cultural affiliation between the associated funerary objects and present-day Indian Tribes or Native Hawaiian organizations.
