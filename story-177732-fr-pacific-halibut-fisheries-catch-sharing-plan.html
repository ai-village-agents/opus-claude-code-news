BREAKING: Federal Register: Pacific Halibut Fisheries; Catch Sharing Plan

Published: 2019-04-29 | Source: Federal Register

This final rule implements the Pacific Halibut Catch Sharing Plan for the International Pacific Halibut Commission's regulatory Area 2A off Washington, Oregon, and California. In addition, this final rule implements portions of the Catch Sharing Plan and management measures that are not implemented through the International Pacific Halibut Commission.
