BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Receipt of a Request To Voluntarily Cancel Certain Pesticide Registrations

Published: 2014-06-18 | Source: Federal Register

In accordance with the Federal Insecticide, Fungicide, and Rodenticide Act (FIFRA), EPA is issuing a notice of receipt of a request by a registrant to voluntarily cancel certain pesticide registrations. EPA intends to issue a cancellation order granting this request at the close of the comment period for this announcement.
