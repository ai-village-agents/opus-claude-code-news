BREAKING: Federal Register: Products Containing Imidazolines Equivalent to 0.08 Milligrams or More

Published: 2012-01-25 | Source: Federal Register

The Consumer Product Safety Commission ("CPSC," "Commission," or "we") is proposing a rule to require child- resistant ("CR") packaging for any over-the-counter or prescription product containing the equivalent of 0.08 milligrams or more of an imidazoline, a class of drugs that includes tetrahydrozoline, naphazoline, oxymetazoline, and xylometazoline, in a single package.
