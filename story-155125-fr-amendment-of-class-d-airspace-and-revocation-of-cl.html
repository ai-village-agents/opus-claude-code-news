BREAKING: Federal Register: Amendment of Class D Airspace and Revocation of Class E Airspace; Bogue, NC

Published: 2020-06-30 | Source: Federal Register

This action amends Class D airspace by updating the geographic coordinates, and removes Class E airspace extending upward from 700 feet above the surface at Bogue Field Marine Corps Auxiliary Field, Bogue, NC, at the request of the US Marine Corps. Class E airspace is no longer required, as there are no instrument approaches into Bogue Field MCALF.
