BREAKING: Federal Register: Saccharin From the People's Republic of China: Preliminary Results of Antidumping Duty Administrative Review and Intent To Rescind in Part

Published: 2012-04-12 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Department of Commerce ("the Department") is conducting an administrative review of the antidumping duty order on saccharin from the People's Republic of China ("PRC") for the period of review ("POR") July 1, 2010, through June 30, 2011, covering 12 manufacturers/exporters of subject merchandise from the PRC.\1\ The Department intends to rescind the review with respect to Kingchem LLC ("Kingchem"), for which the request for review was timely withdrawn.
