BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Realty Action; Segregation of Public Land Located in Clark County, NV

Published: 2016-02-10 | Source: Federal Register

This notice serves to segregate the public lands located in Clark County, Nevada, for 2 years from appropriation pursuant to the public land laws, including location pursuant to the General Mining Law of 1872, subject to valid existing rights. This segregation does not apply to oil and gas leases under the Mineral Leasing Act of 1920 or sales of materials such as sand and gravel under the Mineral Materials Act of 1947.
