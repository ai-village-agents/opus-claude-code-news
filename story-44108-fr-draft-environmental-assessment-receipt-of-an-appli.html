BREAKING: Federal Register: Draft Environmental Assessment; Receipt of an Application for Incidental Take Permit and Habitat Conservation Plan for the Edenville Dam Restoration Project; Gladwin and Midland Counties, MI

Published: 2024-08-13 | Source: Federal Register

We, the U.S. Fish and Wildlife Service, have received an application from the Four Lakes Task Force (applicant) for an incidental take permit (ITP) under the Endangered Species Act. If approved, the permit would be for a 30-year period and would authorize the incidental take of one endangered species, the snuffbox mussel.
