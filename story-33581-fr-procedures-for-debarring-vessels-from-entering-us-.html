BREAKING: Federal Register: Procedures for Debarring Vessels From Entering U.S. Ports

Published: 2024-04-04 | Source: Federal Register

This final rule amends Department of Homeland Security (DHS) regulations by adding procedures regarding DHS's authority to debar from entering U.S. ports vessels owned or chartered by an entity found to be in violation of certain laws and regulations relating to the performance of longshore work by nonimmigrant crew members.
