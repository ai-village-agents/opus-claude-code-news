BREAKING: Federal Register: Applications for New Awards; National Comprehensive Center on Improving Literacy for Students With Disabilities

Published: 2021-07-13 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Education (Department) is issuing a notice inviting applications for a new award for fiscal year (FY) 2021 for a National Comprehensive Center on Improving Literacy for Students with Disabilities (Comprehensive Centers program), Assistance Listing Number 84.283D. This notice relates to the approved information collection under OMB control number 1894-0006.
