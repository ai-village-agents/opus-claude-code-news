BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Submission for OMB Review; Application for Grants Under the Veterans Upward Bound Program

Published: 2012-04-09 | Source: Federal Register

The Veterans Upward Bound (VUB) program provides grants to institutions of higher education, public and private agencies and organizations, community-based organization with experience in serving disadvantaged youth, combinations of such institutions, agencies and organizations, and secondary schools.
