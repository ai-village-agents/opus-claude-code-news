BREAKING: Federal Register: Vaccine and Mask Requirements To Mitigate the Spread of COVID-19 in Head Start Programs

Published: 2021-11-30 | Source: Federal Register

This interim final rule with comment (IFC) adds new provisions to the Head Start Program Performance Standards to mitigate the spread of the coronavirus disease 2019 (COVID-19) in Head Start programs.
