BREAKING: Federal Register: Effluent Limitations Guidelines and New Source Performance Standards for the Airport Deicing Category

Published: 2012-05-16 | Source: Federal Register

EPA is promulgating technology-based effluent limitations guidelines (ELGs) and new source performance standards (NSPS) under the Clean Water Act (CWA) for discharges from airport deicing operations. The requirements generally apply to wastewater associated with the deicing of airfield pavement at primary airports.
