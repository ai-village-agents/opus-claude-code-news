BREAKING: Federal Register: Inland Waterways Users Board; Request for Nominations

Published: 2016-07-15 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of the Army is publishing this notice to request nominations to serve as representatives on the Inland Waterways Users Board, sponsored by the U.S. Army Corps of Engineers. Section 302 of Public Law 99-662 established the Inland Waterways Users Board. The Board is an independent Federal advisory committee.
