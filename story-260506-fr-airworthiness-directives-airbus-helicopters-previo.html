BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Airbus Helicopters (Previously Eurocopter France) Helicopters

Published: 2016-04-11 | Source: Federal Register

We are adopting a new airworthiness directive (AD) for Airbus Helicopters Model EC225LP, AS332C, AS332L, AS332L1, and AS332L2 helicopters. This AD requires inspecting each TECALEMIT flexible hydraulic hose (hose) installed in the main gearbox (MGB) compartment and replacing the hose if a crack, cut, or other damage exists.
