BREAKING: Federal Register: Records Management; General Records Schedule (GRS); GRS Transmittal 25; Email Managed Under a Capstone Approach

Published: 2015-09-16 | Source: Federal Register

NARA is issuing a new General Records Schedule (GRS) via GRS Transmittal 25. The GRS provides agencies with mandatory disposition instructions for administrative records common to several or all Federal agencies. Transmittal 25 announces changes we have made to the GRS since we published Transmittal 24 in August 2015.
