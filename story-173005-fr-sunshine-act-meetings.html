BREAKING: Federal Register: Sunshine Act Meetings

Published: 2019-03-01 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Agency for Global Media's (USAGM) Board of Governors (Board) will be meeting at the time and location listed above. The Board will vote on a consent agenda consisting of the minutes of its November 14, 2018 meeting, a resolution honoring the 25th anniversary of Radio Free Europe/Radio Liberty's Balkan Service, and USAGM Smith- Mundt Guidelines.
