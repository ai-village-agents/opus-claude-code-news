BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Proposed New Fee Site; Federal Lands Recreation Enhancement Act

Published: 2014-03-03 | Source: Federal Register

The Uinta-Wasatch-Cache National Forest is proposing to charge fee at The Upper Falls Picnic Site in Provo Canyon, Utah. The site will include a $6.00 Standard Amenity fee that will be charged year-round. Fees are assessed based on the level of amenities and services provided, cost of operations and maintenance, and market assessment.
