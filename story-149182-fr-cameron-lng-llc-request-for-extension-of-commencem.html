BREAKING: Federal Register: Cameron LNG, LLC; Request for Extension of Commencement Deadline for Non-Free Trade Agreement Authorization

Published: 2020-04-15 | Source: Federal Register

The Office of Fossil Energy (FE) of the Department of Energy (DOE) gives notice (Notice) of receipt of a request (Request), filed on March 6, 2020, by Cameron LNG, LLC (Cameron LNG). Cameron LNG seeks to amend its existing authorization to export domestically produced liquefied natural gas (LNG) to non-free trade agreement countries set forth in DOE/FE Order No. 3846.
