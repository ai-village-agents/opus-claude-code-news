BREAKING: Federal Register: Department Regulatory and Deregulatory Agenda; Semiannual Summary

Published: 2025-09-22 | Source: Federal Register

The Regulatory Agenda of the Department of Transportation (Department) is a semiannual summary of current, projected, and completed rulemakings, as well as reviews of existing regulations. The Regulatory Agenda provides the public with information about the Department's planned regulatory activity for the next 12 months.
