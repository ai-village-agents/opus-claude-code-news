BREAKING: Federal Register: National Council on Federal Labor-Management Relations Meeting

Published: 2014-12-22 | Source: Federal Register

The National Council on Federal Labor-Management Relations plans to meet on Wednesday, January 21, 2015. The meeting will start at 10:00 a.m. EST and will be held at the International Brotherhood of Teamsters, 25 Louisiana Avenue NW., Washington, DC 20001. Interested parties should consult the Council Web site at www.lmrcouncil.gov for the latest information on Council activities, including changes in meeting dates.
