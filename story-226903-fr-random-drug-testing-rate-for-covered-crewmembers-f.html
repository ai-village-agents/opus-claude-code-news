BREAKING: Federal Register: Random Drug Testing Rate for Covered Crewmembers for 2017

Published: 2017-02-03 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard has set the calendar year 2017 minimum random drug testing rate at 25 percent of covered crewmembers.
