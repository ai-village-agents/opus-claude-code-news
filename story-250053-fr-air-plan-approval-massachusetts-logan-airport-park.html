BREAKING: Federal Register: Air Plan Approval; Massachusetts; Logan Airport Parking Freeze

Published: 2017-12-05 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is proposing to approve a State Implementation Plan (SIP) revision submitted by the Commonwealth of Massachusetts. This SIP revision increases the total number of commercial parking spaces allowed in the Logan Airport Parking Freeze Area by 5,000 parking spaces.
