BREAKING: Federal Register: Army Privacy Act Program

Published: 2013-03-27 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of the Army is amending its rule on notification of the Army Litigation Division when complaints citing the Privacy Act are filed in order to correct the mailing address in Sec. 505.12. The address for notifying the Army Litigation Division of cases citing the Privacy Act and filed in a U.S. District Court has changed.
