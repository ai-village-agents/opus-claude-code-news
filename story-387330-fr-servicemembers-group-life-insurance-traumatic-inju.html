BREAKING: Federal Register: Servicemembers' Group Life Insurance Traumatic Injury Protection Program-Genitourinary Losses

Published: 2012-06-01 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Veterans Affairs (VA) is issuing this final rule that amends the regulations governing the Servicemembers' Group Life Insurance Traumatic Injury Protection (TSGLI) program by adding certain genitourinary (GU) system losses to the TSGLI Schedule of Losses and defining terms relevant to these new losses.
