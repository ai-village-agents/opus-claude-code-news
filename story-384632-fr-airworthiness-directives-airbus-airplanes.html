BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Airbus Airplanes

Published: 2012-05-02 | Source: Federal Register

We propose to adopt a new airworthiness directive (AD) for certain Airbus Model A310-203, -221, and -222 airplanes. This proposed AD was prompted by the manufacturer re-classifying slat extension eccentric bolts as principal structural elements (PSE) with replacement due at or before their calculated fatigue lives.
