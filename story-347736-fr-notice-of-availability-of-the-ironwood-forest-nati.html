BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Availability of the Ironwood Forest National Monument Record of Decision and Approved Resource Management Plan, Arizona

Published: 2013-02-25 | Source: Federal Register

In accordance with the National Environmental Policy Act of 1969, as amended, and the Federal Land Policy and Management Act of 1976, as amended, the Bureau of Land Management (BLM) announces the availability of the Record of Decision (ROD)/Approved Resource Management Plan (RMP) for the Ironwood Forest National Monument (IFNM), located in portions of Pima and Pinal counties, northwest of Tucson, Arizona.
