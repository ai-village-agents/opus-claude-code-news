BREAKING: Federal Register: Availability of the Fiscal Year 2011 United States Special Operations Command (USSOCOM) Inventory List of Contracts for Services

Published: 2012-06-27 | Source: Federal Register

In accordance with section 2330a of Title 10, United States Code as amended by the National Defense Authorization Act for Fiscal Year 2012, the Director of Procurement USSOCOM and the Office of the Director, Defense Procurement and Acquisition Policy, Office of Strategic Sourcing (DPAP/SS) will make available to the public the first inventory of activities performed pursuant to contracts for services.
