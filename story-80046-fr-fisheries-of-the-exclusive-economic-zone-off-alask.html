BREAKING: Federal Register: Fisheries of the Exclusive Economic Zone Off Alaska; Cook Inlet Salmon; Amendment 16

Published: 2023-10-19 | Source: Federal Register

NMFS proposes Amendment 16 to the Fishery Management Plan for the Salmon Fisheries in the Exclusive Economic Zone (EEZ) Off Alaska (Salmon FMP) and associated implementing regulations. If approved, Amendment 16 and this proposed rule would establish Federal fishery management for all salmon fishing that occurs in the Cook Inlet EEZ, which includes commercial drift gillnet and recreational salmon fishery sectors.
