BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; The Boeing Company Airplanes

Published: 2014-12-01 | Source: Federal Register

We propose to adopt a new airworthiness directive (AD) for The Boeing Company Model 747 airplanes equipped with a main deck side cargo door (MDSCD). This proposed AD was prompted by recent testing that indicates that intermodal containers, when loaded as cargo, under certain flight-load conditions, can shift and impact the adjacent fuselage frames.
