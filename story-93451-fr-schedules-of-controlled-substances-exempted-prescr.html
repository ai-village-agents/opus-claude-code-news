BREAKING: Federal Register: Schedules of Controlled Substances; Exempted Prescription Products

Published: 2022-04-12 | Source: Federal Register

The Drug Enforcement Administration proposes to revoke the exempted prescription product status for all butalbital products previously granted exemptions. Upon publication of a final rule, these products shall become subject to all schedule III controls under the Controlled Substances Act.
