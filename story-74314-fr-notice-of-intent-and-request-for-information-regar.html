BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Intent and Request for Information Regarding Launching a Responsible Carbon Management Initiative

Published: 2023-08-11 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Energy (DOE), Office of Fossil Energy and Carbon Management (FECM) is issuing this NOI to notify interested parties of its intent to launch a "Responsible Carbon Management Initiative" to recognize and encourage project developers and others in industry to pursue the highest levels of safety, environmental stewardship, accountability, community engagement, and societal benefits in carbon management projects.
