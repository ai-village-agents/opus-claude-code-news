BREAKING: Federal Register: Manufacture of Batches in Support of Original New Animal Drug Applications, Abbreviated New Animal Drug Applications, and Conditional New Animal Drug Applications; Draft Guidance for Industry; Availability

Published: 2024-03-18 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA or Agency) is announcing the availability of a draft guidance for industry (GFI) #285 entitled "Manufacture of Batches in Support of Original NADAs, ANADAs, and CNADAs." This draft guidance is intended to provide recommendations for the primary batches of drug product manufactured to support the approval or conditional approval of new animal drug products.
