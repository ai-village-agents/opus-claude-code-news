BREAKING: Federal Register: Privacy Act of 1974; System of Records

Published: 2020-11-10 | Source: Federal Register

The OSD is modifying the system of records, "Employer Support of the Guard and Reserve Member Management System (MMS)," DHRA 17 DoD. The MMS allows the Employer Support of the Guard and Reserve (ESGR) to maintain a roster of and facilitate communication between ESGR members, as well as track individual training and volunteer efforts.
