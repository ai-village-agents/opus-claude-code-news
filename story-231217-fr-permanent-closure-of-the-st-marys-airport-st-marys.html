BREAKING: Federal Register: Permanent Closure of the St. Marys Airport, St. Marys, Georgia

Published: 2017-04-05 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA is publishing this notice of a pending action required by statute. The National Defense Authorization Act (NDAA) for Fiscal Year 2017 requires the FAA to release the City of St. Marys, Georgia, from all restrictions, conditions, and limitations on the use, encumbrance, conveyance, and closure of the St. Marys Airport.
