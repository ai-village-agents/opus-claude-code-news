BREAKING: Federal Register: Marine Fisheries Advisory Committee

Published: 2016-07-22 | Source: Federal Register

Nominations are being sought for appointment to a new task force of the Marine Fisheries Advisory Committee (MAFAC) to support its advisory work for the Secretary of Commerce on living marine resource matters. The task force will focus on providing information and advice on the establishment of long-term goals for salmon and steelhead in the Columbia River Basin.
