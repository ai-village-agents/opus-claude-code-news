BREAKING: Federal Register: Calendar Year (CY) 2023 Privately Owned Vehicle (POV) Mileage Reimbursement Rates; CY 2023 Standard Mileage Rate for Moving Purposes

Published: 2023-01-05 | Source: Federal Register

GSA is updating the mileage reimbursement rate for privately owned automobiles (POA), airplanes, and motorcycles as required by statute. This information will be available in FTR Bulletin 23-05, which can be found on GSA's website at https://gsa.gov/ftrbulletins.
