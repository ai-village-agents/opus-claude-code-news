BREAKING: Federal Register: Public Information Collections Approved by the Office of Management and Budget

Published: 2012-09-17 | Source: Federal Register

The Federal Communications Commission (FCC) has received Office of Management and Budget (OMB) approval for the following information collection pursuant to the Paperwork Reduction Act of 1995, Public Law 104-13. An agency may not conduct or sponsor and a person is not required to respond to a collection of information unless it displays a currently valid control number.
