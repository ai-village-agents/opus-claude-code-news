BREAKING: Federal Register: Glyphosate Registration Review; Draft Endangered Species Act Biological Evaluations; Extension of Comment Period

Published: 2021-01-15 | Source: Federal Register

EPA issued a notice in the Federal Register of November 27, 2020, opening a 60-day comment period on the draft nationwide biological evaluations for the registration review of the pesticide glyphosate relative to the potential effects on threatened and endangered species and their designated critical habitats. This document extends the comment period for 45 days, from January 26, 2021 to March 12, 2021.
