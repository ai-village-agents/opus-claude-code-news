BREAKING: Federal Register: Revised Schedule of Remuneration for the Unemployment Compensation for Ex-Servicemembers

Published: 2022-01-03 | Source: Federal Register

Each year, the Department of Defense issues a Schedule of Remuneration used by states for UCX purposes.
