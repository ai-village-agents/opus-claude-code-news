BREAKING: Federal Register: Notification of Single Source Cooperative Agreement Award for Project Hope

Published: 2012-07-31 | Source: Federal Register

In FY2012, HHS/ASPR/OPP plans to provide a single source cooperative agreement award to Project Hope to strengthen emergency care delivery in the United States healthcare system through health information and promotion in support of the Homeland Security Presidential Directive-21 (2007) and the National Health Security Strategy (2009) and Implementation Plan (2012).
