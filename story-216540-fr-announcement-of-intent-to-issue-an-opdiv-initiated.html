BREAKING: Federal Register: Announcement of Intent To Issue an OPDIV-Initiated Supplement Under the Standing Announcement for Residential (Shelter) Services for Unaccompanied Children, HHS-2017-ACF-ORR-ZU-1132

The aggregate total of the multiple installments will not exceed $367,860,381. The first two installments will be issued prior to September 30, 2018.
