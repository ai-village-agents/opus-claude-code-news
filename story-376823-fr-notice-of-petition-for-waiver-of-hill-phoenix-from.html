BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Petition for Waiver of Hill PHOENIX From the Department of Energy Commercial Refrigerator, Freezer and Refrigerator-Freezer Test Procedure

Published: 2012-02-06 | Source: Federal Register

This notice announces receipt of and publishes the Hill PHOENIX Inc. (Hill PHOENIX) petition for waiver (hereafter, "petition") from the U.S. Department of Energy (DOE) test procedure for determining the energy consumption of commercial refrigerators, freezers and refrigerator-freezers. Through this notice, DOE also solicits comments with respect to the Hill PHOENIX petition.
