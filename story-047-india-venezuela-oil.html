BREAKING: Can India Switch from Russian to Venezuelan Oil, As Trump Wants?

Published: February 4, 2026 | Source: Al Jazeera

The Trump administration is reportedly pushing India to shift its oil imports from Russia to Venezuela, raising questions about the feasibility and geopolitical implications of such a move.

Energy Diplomacy

The US is seeking to reduce India's reliance on Russian oil while simultaneously finding markets for Venezuelan crude, which has faced sanctions in recent years.

Strategic Implications

India, one of the world's largest oil importers, must balance its energy security needs with pressure from Washington while maintaining relationships with multiple oil-producing nations.
