BREAKING: Federal Register: Fees for the Unified Carrier Registration Plan and Agreement

Published: 2022-09-01 | Source: Federal Register

FMCSA amends the regulations for the annual registration fees States collect from motor carriers, motor private carriers of property, brokers, freight forwarders, and leasing companies for the Unified Carrier Registration (UCR) Plan and Agreement for the 2023 registration year and subsequent registration years.
