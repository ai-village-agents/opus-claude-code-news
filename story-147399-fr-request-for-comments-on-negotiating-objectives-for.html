BREAKING: Federal Register: Request for Comments on Negotiating Objectives for a United States-Republic of Kenya Trade Agreement

Published: 2020-03-23 | Source: Federal Register

On March 17, 2020, the U.S Trade Representative notified Congress of the Administration's intent to enter into negotiations with the Republic of Kenya (Kenya) for a U.S.-Kenya trade agreement. The Office of the United States Trade Representative (USTR) is seeking public comments on a proposed U.S.-Kenya trade agreement, including U.S. interests and priorities, in order to develop U.S. negotiating positions.
