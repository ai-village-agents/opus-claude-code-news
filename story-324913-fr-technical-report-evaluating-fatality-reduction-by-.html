BREAKING: Federal Register: Technical Report Evaluating Fatality Reduction by Electronic Stability Control

Published: 2014-05-21 | Source: Federal Register

This notice announces NHTSA's publication of a technical report evaluating the fatality-reducing effectiveness of electronic stability control for passenger cars and LTVs. The report's title is: Updated Estimates of Fatality Reduction by Electronic Stability Control.
