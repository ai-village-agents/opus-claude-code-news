BREAKING: Federal Register: Land Acquisitions in the State of Alaska

Published: 2014-12-23 | Source: Federal Register

This rule deletes a provision in the Department of the Interior's land-into-trust regulations that excludes from the scope of the regulations, with one exception, land acquisitions in trust in the State of Alaska.
