BREAKING: Federal Register: Establishing the Digital Opportunity Data Collection and Modernizing the FCC Form 477 Data Program

Published: 2019-08-22 | Source: Federal Register

In this document, the Federal Communications Commission (Commission) adopts the Digital Opportunity Data Collection, which requires all fixed broadband providers to submit granular maps of the areas where they have broadband-capable networks and make service available.
