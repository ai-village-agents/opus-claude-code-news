BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Inventory Completion: History Colorado, Denver, CO

Published: 2012-02-27 | Source: Federal Register

History Colorado (formerly the Colorado Historical Society) has completed an inventory of human remains, in consultation with the appropriate Indian tribes, and has determined that there is insufficient evidence to reasonably establish cultural affiliation between the human remains and present-day Indian tribes.
