BREAKING: Federal Register: Preoperational Testing of Instrument and Control Air Systems

Published: 2012-03-16 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Nuclear Regulatory Commission (NRC or the Commission) is issuing for public comment draft regulatory guide (DG), DG-1268, "Preoperational Testing of Instrument and Control Air Systems." This regulatory guide is being revised to address new issues that have been raised since RG 1.68.3 was first issued.
