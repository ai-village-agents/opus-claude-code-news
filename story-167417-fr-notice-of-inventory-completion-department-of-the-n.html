BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Inventory Completion: Department of the Navy, Navy Region Southeast, Jacksonville, FL

Published: 2020-12-04 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of the Navy, Navy Region Southeast, has completed an inventory of human remains, in consultation with the appropriate Indian Tribes or Native Hawaiian organizations, and has determined that there is no cultural affiliation between the human remains and any present-day Indian Tribes or Native Hawaiian organizations.
