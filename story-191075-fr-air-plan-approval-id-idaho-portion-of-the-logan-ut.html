BREAKING: Federal Register: Air Plan Approval; ID: Idaho Portion of the Logan UT-ID 2006 24-Hour PM2.5 Nonattainment Area; Moderate Plan Elements

Published: 2019-10-15 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is proposing to approve revisions to the Idaho State Implementation Plan (SIP) submitted on July 31, 2018. Idaho's submission addresses specific Clean Air Act (CAA) requirements for the Idaho portion of the Logan, Utah-Idaho fine particulate matter (PM2.5) nonattainment area (Logan UT-ID area).
