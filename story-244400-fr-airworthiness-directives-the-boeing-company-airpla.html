BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; The Boeing Company Airplanes

Published: 2017-09-22 | Source: Federal Register

We are adopting a new airworthiness directive (AD) for certain The Boeing Company Model 757-200, -200PF, and -200CB series airplanes. This AD was prompted by an analysis of the cam support assemblies of the main cargo door (MCD) that indicated that the existing maintenance program for the cam support assemblies is not adequate to reliably detect cracks before two adjacent cam support assemblies could fail.
