BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Eurocopter Deutschland Helicopters

Published: 2012-05-22 | Source: Federal Register

We propose to adopt a new airworthiness directive (AD) for all Eurocopter Deutschland GmbH Model MBB-BK117 C-2 helicopters with certain Generator Control Units (GCU) installed. This proposed AD is prompted by reports of internal short circuits in certain GCUs.
