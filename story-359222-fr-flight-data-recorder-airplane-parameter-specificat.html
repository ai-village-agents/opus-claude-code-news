BREAKING: Federal Register: Flight Data Recorder Airplane Parameter Specification Omissions and Corrections

Published: 2013-07-03 | Source: Federal Register

This action amends the operating regulations for flight data recorders by correcting errors in recording rates in three different appendices. These errors create requirements that could not be met by certain airplanes without extensive modification, which was not intended when the requirements were adopted.
