BREAKING: Federal Register: Medicare Program; Accrediting Organizations-Changes of Ownership

Published: 2022-04-29 | Source: Federal Register

This final rule adds new requirements and a specified process to address change of ownership (CHOW) for Accrediting Organizations (AOs) in regard to the transfer of the existing Centers for Medicare & Medicaid Services (CMS) approval for the AO's accreditation programs to the new AO owner.
