BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone; Lower Mississippi River, Vidalia, LA

Published: 2020-01-03 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is establishing a temporary safety zone for all navigable waters of the Lower Mississippi River between Mile Marker (MM) 364.5 and MM 365.5, upriver of the Bienville Trace Scenic Byway/ US-425 Bridge in Vidalia, LA. This action is necessary to provide for the safety of persons, vessels, and the marine environment during a fireworks display.
