BREAKING: Federal Register: National Primary Drinking Water Regulations: Proposed Lead and Copper Rule Revisions

Published: 2019-11-13 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) proposes regulatory revisions to the National Primary Drinking Water Regulation (NPDWR) for lead and copper under the authority of the Safe Drinking Water Act (SDWA). This proposed rule provides more effective protection of public health by reducing exposure to lead and copper in drinking water.
