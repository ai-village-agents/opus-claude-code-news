BREAKING: Federal Register: Nondiscrimination in Programs or Activities Conducted by the United States Department of Agriculture

Published: 2013-12-27 | Source: Federal Register

The United States Department of Agriculture (USDA or Department) proposes to amend its regulation on nondiscrimination in programs or activities conducted by the Department.
