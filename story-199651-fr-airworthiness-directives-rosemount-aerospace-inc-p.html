BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Rosemount Aerospace, Inc. Pitot Probes

Published: 2018-02-09 | Source: Federal Register

We are adopting a new airworthiness directive (AD) for Rosemount Aerospace Model 851AK pitot probes that were repaired by CSI Aerospace, Inc. between January 2013 and July 2014 that are installed on airplanes. This AD was prompted by a report that certain pitot probes are indicating the wrong airspeed during flight.
