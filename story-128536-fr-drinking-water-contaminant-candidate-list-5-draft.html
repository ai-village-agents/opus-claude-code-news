BREAKING: Federal Register: Drinking Water Contaminant Candidate List 5-Draft

Published: 2021-07-19 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is publishing a draft list of contaminants that are currently not subject to any proposed or promulgated national primary drinking water regulations for public review and comment. These contaminants are known or anticipated to occur in public water systems and may require regulation under the Safe Drinking Water Act (SDWA).
