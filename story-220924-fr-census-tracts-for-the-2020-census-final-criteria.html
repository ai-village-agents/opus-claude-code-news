BREAKING: Federal Register: Census Tracts for the 2020 Census-Final Criteria

Published: 2018-11-13 | Source: Federal Register

Census tracts are relatively permanent small-area geographic divisions of a county or statistically equivalent entity defined for the tabulation and presentation of data from the decennial census and selected other statistical programs. The Census Bureau is publishing this notice in the Federal Register to announce final criteria for defining census tracts for the 2020 Census.
