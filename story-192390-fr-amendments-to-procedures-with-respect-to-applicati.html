BREAKING: Federal Register: Amendments to Procedures With Respect to Applications Under the Investment Company Act of 1940

Published: 2019-10-30 | Source: Federal Register

The Securities and Exchange Commission (the "Commission") is proposing amending rule 0-5 under the Investment Company Act of 1940 ("Act") to establish an expedited review procedure for applications that are substantially identical to recent precedent as well as a new rule to establish an internal timeframe for review of applications outside of such expedited procedure.
