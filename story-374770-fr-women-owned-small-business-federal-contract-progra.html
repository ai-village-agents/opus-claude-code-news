BREAKING: Federal Register: Women-Owned Small Business Federal Contract Program

Published: 2012-01-12 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Small Business Administration (SBA) is amending its regulations to make them consistent with the inflationary adjustments that are already codified in the Federal Acquisition Regulation (FAR) as they relate to the Women-Owned Small Business (WOSB) Program and the Simplified Acquisition Threshold.
