BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Intent To Prepare an Environmental Impact Statement for the Willamette Valley System Operations and Maintenance

Published: 2019-04-01 | Source: Federal Register

The Portland District, U.S. Army Corps of Engineers (Corps) intends to prepare an Environmental Impact Statement (EIS) to address the continued operations and maintenance of the Willamette Valley System (WVS) in accordance with authorized project purposes; while meeting Endangered Species Act (ESA) obligations to avoid jeopardizing the continued existence of listed species. The Corps will serve as the lead federal agency for purposes of the National Environmental Policy Act (NEPA).
