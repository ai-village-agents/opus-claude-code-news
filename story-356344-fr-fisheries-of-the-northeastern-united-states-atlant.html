BREAKING: Federal Register: Fisheries of the Northeastern United States; Atlantic Herring Fishery; Amendment 5

Published: 2013-06-03 | Source: Federal Register

NMFS proposes regulations to implement measures in Amendment 5 to the Atlantic Herring Fishery Management Plan (FMP). Amendment 5 was developed by the New England Fishery Management Council (Council) to: Improve the collection of real-time, accurate catch information; enhance the monitoring and sampling of catch at-sea; and address bycatch issues through responsible management.
