BREAKING: Federal Register: Energy Conservation Program: Test Procedure for Clothes Dryers

Published: 2021-10-08 | Source: Federal Register

On July 23, 2019, the U.S. Department of Energy ("DOE") issued a notice of proposed rulemaking ("NOPR") to amend the test procedure for clothes dryers. That proposed rulemaking serves as the basis for the final rule.
