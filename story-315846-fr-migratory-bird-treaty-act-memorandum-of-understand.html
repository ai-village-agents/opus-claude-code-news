BREAKING: Federal Register: Migratory Bird Treaty Act; Memorandum of Understanding

Published: 2014-02-05 | Source: Federal Register

EPA is announcing the availability of the draft Memorandum of Understanding (MOU) between the Environmental Protection Agency, Office of Pesticide Programs (OPP) and the Department of the Interior, U.S. Fish and Wildlife Service (FWS) regarding implementation of Executive Order 13186, "Responsibilities of Federal Agencies to Protect Migratory Birds" for public comment.
