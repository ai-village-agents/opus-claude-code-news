BREAKING: Federal Register: Indefinite Delivery and Indefinite Quantity Contracts for Federal-Aid Construction

Published: 2022-11-09 | Source: Federal Register

On November 16, 2020, FHWA published an interim final rule (IFR) amending FHWA's regulations to allow States the ability to use the Indefinite Delivery and Indefinite Quantity (ID/IQ) method of contracting, including job order contracting (JOC), on Federal-aid highway projects, under certain circumstances, on a permanent basis.
