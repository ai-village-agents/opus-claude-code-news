BREAKING: Federal Register: Fatigue Risk Management Programs for Certain Passenger and Freight Railroads

Published: 2022-06-13 | Source: Federal Register

Pursuant to the Rail Safety Improvement Act of 2008, FRA is issuing regulations requiring certain railroads to develop and implement a Fatigue Risk Management Program, as one component of the railroads' larger railroad safety risk reduction programs.
