BREAKING: Federal Register: Special Local Regulation; Great Western Tube Float; Parker, AZ

Published: 2018-06-04 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is temporarily changing the location of the special local regulation for the annual Great Western Tube Float event held on the navigable waters of the Colorado River, Parker, AZ. The change of the location for the special local regulation is necessary to provide for the safety of life on navigable waters during the event.
