BREAKING: Federal Register: Southern Nuclear Operating Company, Inc.; Vogtle Electric Generating Plant, Units 3 and 4

Published: 2018-03-20 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Nuclear Regulatory Commission (NRC) has granted the request of Southern Nuclear Operating Company to withdraw its application dated July 29, 2016, for proposed amendments to Combined License (COL) Nos. NPF-91 and NPF-92 for the Vogtle Electric Generating Plant (VEGP), Units 3 and 4, respectively.
