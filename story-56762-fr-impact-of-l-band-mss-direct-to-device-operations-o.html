BREAKING: Federal Register: Impact of L-Band MSS ‘Direct-to-Device’ Operations on GPS

Published: 2024-12-27 | Source: Federal Register

The National Telecommunications and Information Administration (NTIA) is seeking information and public comment on the potential impact on the GPS L1 signal at 1575.42 MHz of the increasing deployment of mobile devices capable of operating on satellite systems in the L- band at 1610-1660.5 MHz.
