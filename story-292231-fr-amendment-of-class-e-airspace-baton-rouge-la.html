BREAKING: Federal Register: Amendment of Class E Airspace; Baton Rouge, LA

Published: 2015-04-24 | Source: Federal Register

This action amends the legal description of the Baton Rouge, LA, VHF Omnidirectional Range/Tactical Air Navigation aid (VORTAC) located in Class E airspace at Baton Rouge, LA. The Baton Rouge VORTAC and Baton Rouge Metropolitan Airport, Ryan Field, have similar names and both share the same facility identifier but are not co-located.
