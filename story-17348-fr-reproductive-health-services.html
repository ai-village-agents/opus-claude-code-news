BREAKING: Federal Register: Reproductive Health Services

Published: 2025-08-04 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Veterans Affairs (VA) is proposing to reinstate the full exclusion on abortions and abortion counseling from the medical benefits package, which was removed in 2022. Before that time, this exclusion had been firmly in place since the medical benefits package was first established in 1999.
