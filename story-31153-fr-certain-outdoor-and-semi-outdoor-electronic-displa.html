BREAKING: Federal Register: Certain Outdoor and Semi-Outdoor Electronic Displays, Products Containing Same, and Components Thereof; Notice of a Commission Determination Not To Review a Final Initial Determination Finding No Violation of Section 337; Termination of the Investigation

Published: 2024-03-05 | Source: Federal Register

Notice is hereby given that the U.S. International Trade Commission has determined not to review the presiding administrative law judge's ("ALJ") final initial determination ("FID") finding no violation of section 337 of the Tariff Act of 1930, as amended. The investigation is terminated with a finding of no violation.
