BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Airbus SAS Airplanes

Published: 2019-07-01 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA proposes to supersede Airworthiness Directive (AD) 2019-05-09, which applies to certain Airbus SAS Model A320-251N and - 271N airplanes, and Model A321-253N airplanes. AD 2019-05-09 requires repetitive detailed inspections of certain electrical harnesses for discrepancies and corrective actions, if necessary.
