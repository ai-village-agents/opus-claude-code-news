BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; The Boeing Company Airplanes

Published: 2016-06-15 | Source: Federal Register

This document announces an extension of the comment period for the above-referenced NPRM, which proposed the adoption of a new airworthiness directive (AD) for certain The Boeing Company Model 747- 400, 747-400D, and 747-400F series airplanes. That NPRM invited comments concerning the proposed requirement to modify the fuel quantity indicating system (FQIS) to prevent development of an ignition source inside the center fuel tank due to electrical fault conditions.
