BREAKING: Federal Register: NASA FAR Supplement Regulatory Review No. 1

Published: 2013-04-18 | Source: Federal Register

NASA is updating the NASA FAR Supplement (NFS) with the goal of eliminating unnecessary regulation, streamlining overly burdensome regulation, clarifying language, and simplifying processes where possible. This proposed rule is the first in a series and includes updates and revisions to five NFS parts.
