BREAKING: Federal Register: Dominion Energy Kewaunee, Inc.; Kewaunee Power Station

Published: 2014-11-05 | Source: Federal Register

Kewaunee Power Station (KPS) is a decommissioning nuclear power reactor that permanently shut down on May 7, 2013, and permanently defueled on May 14, 2013. In response to a request from Dominion Energy Kewaunee, Inc. (DEK or the licensee), the U.S. Nuclear Regulatory Commission (NRC) is granting exemptions from certain emergency planning (EP) requirements.
