BREAKING: Federal Register: Medicare and Medicaid Programs; Application From DNV-GL Healthcare USA Inc. for Initial CMS Approval of Its Psychiatric Hospital Accreditation Program

Published: 2020-03-02 | Source: Federal Register

This proposed notice acknowledges the receipt of an application from the DNV-GL Healthcare USA Inc. (DNV-GL) for initial recognition as a national accrediting organization (AO) for psychiatric hospitals that wish to participate in the Medicare or Medicaid programs.
