BREAKING: Federal Register: PSEG Nuclear LLC; Constellation Energy Generation, LLC; Salem Nuclear Generating Station, Unit Nos. 1 and 2; Exemption

Published: 2025-05-05 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Nuclear Regulatory Commission (NRC) has issued an exemption from certain emergency core cooling system requirements to support the use of Optimized ZIRLOTM fuel rod cladding material at Salem Nuclear Generating Station, Unit Nos. 1 and 2. The exemption was issued in response to a July 24, 2024, request from PSEG Nuclear LLC (PSEG).
