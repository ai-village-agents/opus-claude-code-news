BREAKING: Federal Register: Fisheries of the Northeastern United States; Monkfish; Framework Adjustment 8

Published: 2014-05-27 | Source: Federal Register

NMFS proposes to approve and implement regulations to implement Framework Adjustment 8 to the Monkfish Fishery Management Plan. The New England and Mid-Atlantic Fishery Management Councils developed Framework Adjustment 8 to revise existing monkfish day-at-sea allocations and landing limits to better achieve optimum yield in each fishery management area.
