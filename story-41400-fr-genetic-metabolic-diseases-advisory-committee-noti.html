BREAKING: Federal Register: Genetic Metabolic Diseases Advisory Committee; Notice of Meeting; Establishment of a Public Docket; Request for Comments-New Drug Application 214927, for Arimoclomol

Published: 2024-07-10 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA) announces a forthcoming public advisory committee meeting of the Genetic Metabolic Diseases Advisory Committee (the Committee). The general function of the Committee is to provide advice and recommendations to FDA on regulatory issues. The meeting will be open to the public. FDA is establishing a docket for public comment on this document.
