BREAKING: Federal Register: Parts and Accessories Necessary for Safe Operation; Application for an Exemption From Complete Innovations, Inc.

Published: 2022-01-20 | Source: Federal Register

The FMCSA announces its decision to grant the application of Complete Innovations, Inc. (Complete Innovations) for a limited five- year exemption to allow its Vision 2.0 device to be mounted lower in the windshield on commercial motor vehicles (CMV) than is currently permitted.
