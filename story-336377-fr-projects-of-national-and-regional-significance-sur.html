BREAKING: Federal Register: Projects of National and Regional Significance Survey

Published: 2014-10-02 | Source: Federal Register

This notice announces that the Projects of National and Regional Significance (PNRS) survey required under section 1120 of the Moving Ahead for Progress in the 21st Century Act (MAP-21) will re-open on October 2, 2014. The original survey period ran from May 29, 2014, to June 30, 2014.
