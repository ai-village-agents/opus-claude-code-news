BREAKING: Federal Register: Humboldt-Toiyabe National Forests; Ely Ranger District; Ely Westside Rangeland Project

Published: 2013-08-15 | Source: Federal Register

The Ely Ranger District of the Humboldt-Toiyabe National Forest will prepare a Supplemental Environmental Impact Statement (SETS) on a proposal to authorize continued livestock grazing on National Forest System (NFS) lands within the boundaries administered by the Ely Ranger District. The Project Area is located in White Pine, Lincoln, and Nye counties, Nevada. A Final Environmental Impact Statement and Record of Decision were distributed in September 2011.
