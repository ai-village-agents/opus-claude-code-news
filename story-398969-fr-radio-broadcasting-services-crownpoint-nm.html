BREAKING: Federal Register: Radio Broadcasting Services; Crownpoint, NM

Published: 2012-10-15 | Source: Federal Register

This document sets forth a proposal to amend the FM Table of Allotments. The Commission requests comment on a petition filed by Navajo Technical College, proposing to amend the Table of Allotments by allotting Channel 297A at Crownpoint, New Mexico, as a Tribal Allotment. Channel 297A would constitute a first local service at Crownpoint.
