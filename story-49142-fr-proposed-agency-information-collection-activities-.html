BREAKING: Federal Register: Proposed Agency Information Collection Activities; Comment Request

Published: 2024-10-07 | Source: Federal Register

In accordance with the requirements of the Paperwork Reduction Act of 1995 (PRA), the OCC, the Board, and the FDIC (collectively, the "agencies") may not conduct or sponsor, and the respondent is not required to respond to, an information collection unless it displays a currently valid Office of Management and Budget (OMB) control number.
