BREAKING: Federal Register: Proposed 2025 Power Marketing Plan

Published: 2016-05-06 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Energy (DOE), Western Area Power Administration (Western), Sierra Nevada Region (SNR) has developed a Proposed 2025 Power Marketing Plan (Proposed Plan). The Proposed Plan provides for marketing power from the Central Valley Project (CVP) and the Washoe Project from January 1, 2025, through December 31, 2054.
