BREAKING: Federal Register: U.S. Strategic Command Strategic Advisory Group; Notice of Advisory Committee Closed Meeting; Cancellation

Published: 2019-11-14 | Source: Federal Register

On Monday, October 28, 2019 the DoD published a notice announcing a meeting of the U.S. Strategic Command Strategic Advisory Group that was to take place on Tuesday, November 19, 2019 to Wednesday, November 20, 2019. Due to schedule conflicts, the DoD is cancelling the November 19-20 meeting.
