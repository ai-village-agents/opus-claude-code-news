BREAKING: Federal Register: Proposed Revision 0 to Fitness-for-Duty-Construction

Published: 2014-05-05 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Nuclear Regulatory Commission (NRC) is soliciting public comment on draft NUREG-0800, "Standard Review Plan for the Review of Safety Analysis Reports for Nuclear Power Plants: LWR Edition," Section 13.7.2, "Fitness for Duty--Construction." The NRC seeks comments on the proposed new section of the Standard Review Plan (SRP), concerning implementation of a Fitness-for-Duty (FFD) program during construction.
