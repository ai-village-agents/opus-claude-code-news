BREAKING: Federal Register: Proposed Voluntary Product Standard 1-09, Structural Plywood

Published: 2019-09-06 | Source: Federal Register

The National Institute of Standards and Technology (NIST) is soliciting public comment on a proposed revision to Voluntary Product Standard (PS) 1-09, Structural Plywood. The standard, prepared by the Committee for PS 1, establishes requirements (for those who choose to adhere to the standard) for the principal types and grades of structural plywood and provides a basis for common understanding among producers, distributors, and users of the product.
