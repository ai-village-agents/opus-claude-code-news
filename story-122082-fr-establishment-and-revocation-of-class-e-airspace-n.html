BREAKING: Federal Register: Establishment and Revocation of Class E Airspace; North Dakota, ND

Published: 2021-04-23 | Source: Federal Register

This action establishes a Class E airspace area extending upward from 1,200 feet above the surface over the State of North Dakota and removes the enroute domestic airspace areas at Harvey and Linton, ND.
