BREAKING: Federal Register: Helicopter Air Ambulance, Commercial Helicopter, and Part 91 Helicopter Operations

Published: 2014-02-21 | Source: Federal Register

This final rule addresses helicopter air ambulance, commercial helicopter, and general aviation helicopter operations. To address an increase in fatal helicopter air ambulance accidents, the FAA is implementing new operational procedures and additional equipment requirements for helicopter air ambulance operations.
