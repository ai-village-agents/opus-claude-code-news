BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Engine Alliance Turbofan Engines

Published: 2019-02-08 | Source: Federal Register

We are superseding Airworthiness Directive (AD) 2018-22-05, which applied to Engine Alliance (EA) GP7270, GP7272, and GP7277 turbofan engines with a certain high-pressure turbine (HPT) case installed. AD 2018-22-05 required removal of affected HPT stator cases (HPT cases) from service and their replacement with a part eligible for installation.
