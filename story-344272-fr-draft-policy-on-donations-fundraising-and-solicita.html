BREAKING: Federal Register: Draft Policy on Donations, Fundraising, and Solicitation

Published: 2013-01-15 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Fish and Wildlife Service (Service) proposes to establish a policy that covers Service procedures for accepting, using, and recognizing donations. This draft donations policy is an extension of the Department of the Interior's guidance on donations, found in the Departmental Manual (DM) at 374 DM 6.
