BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Funds Availability: Inviting Applications for the Market Access Program

Published: 2013-04-23 | Source: Federal Register

The Commodity Credit Corporation (CCC) announces that it is inviting proposals for the 2014 Market Access Program (MAP). The intended effect of this notice is to solicit applications from eligible applicants for fiscal year 2014 and to set out criteria for the award of funds under the program in October 2013.
