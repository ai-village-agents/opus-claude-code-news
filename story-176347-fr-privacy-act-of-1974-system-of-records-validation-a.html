BREAKING: Federal Register: Privacy Act of 1974; System of Records, Validation and Disposition Services (VDS-Best Ex)

Published: 2019-04-10 | Source: Federal Register

In accordance with the Privacy Act of 1974, as amended, the Office of Single Family Asset Management, Department of Housing and Urban Development (HUD), proposes to create a new system of records (SOR) titled Validation and Disposition Services (VDS-Best Ex).
