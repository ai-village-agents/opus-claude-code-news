BREAKING: Federal Register: Request for Information (RFI): Information Literacy Programs, Resources, and Promising Practices

Published: 2023-07-25 | Source: Federal Register

The Institute of Museum and Library Services requests information on programs, resources, and activities designed to strengthen information literacy skills. The agency is particularly interested in programs and activities that can be implemented at the local level, in community organizations such as museums and libraries, to help individuals develop the skills necessary to find, evaluate, use, and create information in meaningful ways.
