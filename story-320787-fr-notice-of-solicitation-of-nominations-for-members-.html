BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Solicitation of Nominations for Members of the Foundation for Food and Agriculture Research

Published: 2014-04-04 | Source: Federal Register

In accordance with Sec. 7601 of Pub. L. 113-79, the United States Department of Agriculture announces solicitation for nominations to fill 15 vacancies on the Foundation for Food and Agricultural Research. Seven (7) representatives are to be selected from lists of candidates provided by industry, and eight (8) representatives are to be selected from a list of candidates provided by the National Academy of Sciences.
