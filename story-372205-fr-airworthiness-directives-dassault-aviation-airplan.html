BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; DASSAULT AVIATION Airplanes

Published: 2013-12-09 | Source: Federal Register

We are adopting a new airworthiness directive (AD) for all DASSAULT AVIATION Model Falcon 10 airplanes. This AD requires a one- time inspection for cracking of the right-hand (RH) and left-hand (LH) inboard flaps, and replacement if necessary. This AD was prompted by reports of cracking found on the inboard end plate (rib) of the RH inboard flap.
