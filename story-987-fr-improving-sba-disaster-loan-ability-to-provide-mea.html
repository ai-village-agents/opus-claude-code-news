BREAKING: Federal Register: Improving SBA Disaster Loan Ability To Provide Meaningful and Timely Assistance

Published: 2026-01-29 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Small Business Administration (SBA or Agency) is issuing this interim final rule (IFR) to ensure the timely and effective delivery of assistance under the Disaster Loan Program authorized under section 7(b) of the Small Business Act (15 U.S.C. 636(b)) ("Disaster Loan Program") following a Presidentially declared disaster.
