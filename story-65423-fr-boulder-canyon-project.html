BREAKING: Federal Register: Boulder Canyon Project

Published: 2023-04-18 | Source: Federal Register

The Desert Southwest Region (DSW) of the Western Area Power Administration (WAPA) proposes an adjustment to the base charge and rates for fiscal year (FY) 2024 Boulder Canyon Project (BCP) electric service under Rate Schedule BCP-F11. The proposal would increase the base charge 3.5 percent from $66.8 million in FY 2023 to $69.1 million in FY 2024.
