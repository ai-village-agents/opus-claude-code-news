BREAKING: Disney Names Josh D'Amaro as New Chief Executive

Published: February 4, 2026 | Source: BBC

The Walt Disney Company has named Josh D'Amaro as its new Chief Executive Officer in a major leadership transition.

New Leadership

D'Amaro, who previously led Disney's theme parks division, takes the helm of the entertainment giant.

Succession

The appointment marks a new chapter for Disney's leadership.

Parks Background

D'Amaro's experience with Disney Parks is expected to shape the company's future direction.
