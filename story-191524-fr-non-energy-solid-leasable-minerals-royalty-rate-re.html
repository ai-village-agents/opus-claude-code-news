BREAKING: Federal Register: Non-Energy Solid Leasable Minerals Royalty Rate Reduction Process

Published: 2019-10-18 | Source: Federal Register

The Bureau of Land Management (BLM) proposes to amend its regulations to revise the process for lessees to seek and for the BLM to grant reductions of rental fees, royalty rates, and/or minimum production requirements associated with non-energy solid leasable minerals.
