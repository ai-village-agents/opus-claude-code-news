BREAKING: Federal Register: Availability of Draft Vessel Sanitation Program (VSP) Operations Manual and VSP Construction Guidelines

The Centers for Disease Control and Prevention (CDC), within the Department of Health and Human Services (HHS), announces the opening of a public docket to obtain comment on the draft Vessel Sanitation Program (VSP) Operations Manual and the VSP Construction Guidelines. Information about locating these documents can be found in the supporting materials section and on the VSP Web site.
