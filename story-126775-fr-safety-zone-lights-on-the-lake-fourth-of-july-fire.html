BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone; Lights on the Lake Fourth of July Fireworks; South Lake Tahoe, CA

Published: 2021-06-25 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard will enforce the safety zone for South Lake Tahoe's Light on the Lake Fourth of July Fireworks in the Captain of the Port, San Francisco area of responsibility during the dates and times noted below. This action is necessary to protect personnel, vessels, and the marine environment from the dangers associated with pyrotechnics.
