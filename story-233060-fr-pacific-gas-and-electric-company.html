BREAKING: Federal Register: Pacific Gas and Electric Company

Published: 2017-04-28 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Nuclear Regulatory Commission (NRC) has issued a director's decision with regard to a Petition to Intervene and Request for Hearing concerning Diablo Canyon Power Plant (DCPP) dated August 26, 2014, filed by Friends of the Earth (FOE or petitioner), asserting, in part, its concerns about DCPP's operational safety and ability to safely shut down in the event of a nearby earthquake.
