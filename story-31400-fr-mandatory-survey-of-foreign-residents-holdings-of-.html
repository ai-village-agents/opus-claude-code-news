BREAKING: Federal Register: Mandatory Survey of Foreign-Residents' Holdings of U.S. Securities

Published: 2024-03-07 | Source: Federal Register

By this Notice, the Department of the Treasury is informing the public that it is conducting a mandatory Survey of Foreign- Residents' Holdings of U.S. Securities as of June 30, 2024. This Notice constitutes legal notification to all United States persons (defined below) who meet the reporting requirements set forth in this Notice that they must respond to, and comply with, this survey.
