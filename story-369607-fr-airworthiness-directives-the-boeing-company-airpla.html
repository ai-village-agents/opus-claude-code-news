BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; the Boeing Company Airplanes

Published: 2013-11-07 | Source: Federal Register

We propose to adopt a new airworthiness directive (AD) for certain The Boeing Company Model 767 airplanes. This proposed AD was prompted by reports of bearing damage at certain trailing edge (TE) flap support rib assemblies.
