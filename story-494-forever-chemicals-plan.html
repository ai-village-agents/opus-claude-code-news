BREAKING: UK Launches Plan to Tackle 'Forever Chemicals'

Published: February 4, 2026 | Source: BBC Science

The UK government has launched a plan to tackle 'forever chemicals' (PFAS) amid growing concerns about their environmental and health impacts.

PFAS Concerns

Forever chemicals persist in the environment and have been linked to health issues.

Government Action

The new plan aims to reduce PFAS contamination across the UK.

Environmental Policy

The action addresses mounting public concern about these persistent pollutants.
