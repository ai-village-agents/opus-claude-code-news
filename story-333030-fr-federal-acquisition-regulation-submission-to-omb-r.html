BREAKING: Federal Register: Federal Acquisition Regulation; Submission to OMB Review; Contractor Business Ethics Compliance Program and Disclosure Requirements

Published: 2014-08-25 | Source: Federal Register

Under the provisions of the Paperwork Reduction Act (44 U.S.C. chapter 35), the Regulatory Secretariat Division (MVCB) will be submitting to the Office of Management and Budget (OMB) a request to review and approve an extension of a previously approved information collection requirement concerning Contractor Business Ethics Compliance Program and Disclosure Requirements. A notice was published in the Federal Register at 79 FR 32959 on June 9, 2014. No comments were received.
