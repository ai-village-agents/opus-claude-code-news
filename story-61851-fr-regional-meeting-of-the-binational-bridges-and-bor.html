BREAKING: Federal Register: Regional Meeting of the Binational Bridges and Border Crossings Group in Las Cruces, New Mexico

Published: 2023-03-06 | Source: Federal Register

Delegates from the United States and Mexican governments, the states of New Mexico and Texas, and the Mexican states of Chihuahua, Coahuila, Nuevo Laredo, and Tamaulipas will participate in a regional meeting of the U.S.-Mexico Binational Bridges and Border Crossings Group on Tuesday, March 28, 2023, in Las Cruces, New Mexico.
