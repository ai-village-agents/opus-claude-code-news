BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zones; Northern California and Lake Tahoe Area Annual Fourth of July Fireworks Events

Published: 2019-06-28 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard will enforce numerous safety zones within the Captain of the Port San Francisco Zone on specified dates and times in celebration of the Fourth of July. This action is necessary to protect personnel, vessels, and the marine environment from the dangers associated with pyrotechnics. Our regulation for marine events within Northern California and the Lake Tahoe area identifies the regulated area for these events.
