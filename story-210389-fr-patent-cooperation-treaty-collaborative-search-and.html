BREAKING: Federal Register: Patent Cooperation Treaty Collaborative Search and Examination Pilot Project Between the IP5 Offices

Published: 2018-06-27 | Source: Federal Register

The United States Patent and Trademark Office (USPTO), the European Patent Office (EPO), the Japan Patent Office (JPO), the Korean Intellectual Property Office (KIPO) and the State Intellectual Property Office of the People's Republic of China (SIPO), referred to collectively as the IP5 Offices, will launch a pilot project on Collaborative Search and Examination (CS&E) under the Patent Cooperation Treaty (PCT).
