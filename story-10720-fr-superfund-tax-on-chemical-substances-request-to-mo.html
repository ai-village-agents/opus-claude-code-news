BREAKING: Federal Register: Superfund Tax on Chemical Substances; Request To Modify List of Taxable Substances; Notice of Filing for Hydrogenated Acrylonitrile-Butadiene Rubber (x = 2,783.50, y = 1,907.27; a = 5.74); Correction

Published: 2025-05-06 | Source: Federal Register

The IRS and Treasury published a document in the Federal Register of April 3, 2025, concerning the notice of filing for hydrogenated acrylonitrile-butadiene rubber ((C4H8)x- (C3H3N)y- (C15H24O)a; x = 2,783.05, y = 1,907.27, a = 5.74), public docket IRS-2025-0033. This document contained an incorrect date for the petition filing date for purposes of refund claims.
