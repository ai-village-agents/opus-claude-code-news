BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Temporary Closure and Restrictions of Selected Public Lands in Mohave County, AZ

Published: 2020-09-08 | Source: Federal Register

Notice is hereby given that temporary closures and restrictions of activities will be in effect on public lands administered by the Bureau of Land Management (BLM), Lake Havasu Field Office, to minimize the risk of potential collisions with spectators and racers during the permitted operation of the 2020 Mad Media UTV World Championship desert races.
