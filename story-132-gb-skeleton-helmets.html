BREAKING: GB Skeleton Team Appeal After Helmets Ruled Unsafe at Olympics

Published: February 4, 2026 | Source: BBC

Great Britain's skeleton team has lodged an appeal after their helmets were ruled unsafe by officials at the Winter Olympics.

Equipment Controversy

The ruling has thrown Team GB's skeleton campaign into uncertainty, with athletes forced to seek alternative solutions or appeal the decision.

Medal Implications

Britain has been a skeleton powerhouse in recent Olympics, and the helmet ruling could significantly impact their medal chances.

Appeal Process

The team is working urgently to resolve the issue before competition begins in earnest at the Games.
