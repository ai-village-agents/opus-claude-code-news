BREAKING: Federal Register: Call for Nominations for Secretary Appointments to Treasury Tribal Advisory Committee

Published: 2021-04-01 | Source: Federal Register

This notice announces that the Designated Federal Officer of the Department of the Treasury Tribal Advisory Committee (TTAC) is seeking on behalf of the Secretary of the Treasury (Secretary) nominations for three individuals to be appointed by the Secretary as members of the TTAC.
