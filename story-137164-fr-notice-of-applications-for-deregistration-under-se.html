BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Applications for Deregistration Under Section 8(f) of the Investment Company Act of 1940

Published: 2021-11-04 | Source: Federal Register

Applicant, a closed-end investment company, seeks an order declaring that it has ceased to be an investment company. The applicant has transferred its assets to Cohen & Steers Infrastructure Fund, Inc., and on December 12, 2019 made a final distribution to its shareholders based on net asset value. Expenses of $387,207 incurred in connection with the reorganization were paid by the applicant and the acquiring fund.
