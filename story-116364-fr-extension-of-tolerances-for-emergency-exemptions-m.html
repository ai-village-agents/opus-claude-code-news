BREAKING: Federal Register: Extension of Tolerances for Emergency Exemptions; Multiple Chemicals

Published: 2021-02-09 | Source: Federal Register

This regulation extends time-limited tolerances for residues of the pesticides clothianidin, methyl bromide, and triclopyr in or on various commodities, as identified in this document. These actions are in response to EPA's granting of emergency exemptions under the Federal Insecticide, Fungicide, and Rodenticide Act (FIFRA) authorizing use of these pesticides.
