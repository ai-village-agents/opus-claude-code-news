BREAKING: Federal Register: Work Zone Safety and Mobility and Temporary Traffic Control Devices

Published: 2024-11-01 | Source: Federal Register

The FHWA amends its regulations that govern traffic safety and mobility in highway and street work zones. The FHWA recognizes that increasing road construction activity on our highways can lead to travel disruptions which could potentially result in congestion and crashes, as well as loss in productivity and public frustration with work zones.
