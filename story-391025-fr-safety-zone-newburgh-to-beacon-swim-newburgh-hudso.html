BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone; Newburgh to Beacon Swim, Newburgh, Hudson River, NY

Published: 2012-07-13 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is establishing a temporary safety zone on the navigable waters of the Hudson River, NY in the vicinity of Newburgh, NY for the annual Newburgh Beacon Swim event. This temporary safety zone is necessary to protect swimmers, spectators and vessels from the hazards associated with swimmers competing in a swim across the Hudson River.
