BREAKING: Federal Register: Use of Supplemental Restraint Systems

Published: 2023-11-21 | Source: Federal Register

This proposed rule would prohibit civil aircraft operations conducted with supplemental restraint systems (SRS) unless operators meet certain requirements for ensuring passenger safety during all phases of the operation. The FAA expects these proposed requirements would increase the safety of passengers during civil aircraft operations conducted with SRS.
