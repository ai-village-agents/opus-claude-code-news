BREAKING: Federal Register: Roadless Area Conservation; National Forest System Lands in Colorado

Published: 2015-04-07 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Department of Agriculture is initiating a supplemental environmental impact statement (SEIS) to propose reinstatement of the North Fork Coal Mining Area exception of the Colorado Roadless Rule. The exception would allow for temporary road construction for coal exploration and/or coal-related surface activities in a 19,100-acre area defined as the North Fork Coal Mining Area.
