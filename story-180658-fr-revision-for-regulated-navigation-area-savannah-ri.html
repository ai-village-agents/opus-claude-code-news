BREAKING: Federal Register: Revision for Regulated Navigation Area; Savannah River, GA

Published: 2019-06-03 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is proposing to amend the regulated navigation area (RNA) on the Savannah River located between Fort Jackson, GA (32[deg]04.93 N, 081[deg]02.19 W) and the Savannah River Channel Entrance Sea Buoy. This document proposes to remove inapplicable and/or outdated definitions, processes and requirements in the RNA following a change in capability, infrastructure and layout of the Southern Liquefied Natural Gas (LNG) facility on the Savannah River.
