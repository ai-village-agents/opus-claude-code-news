BREAKING: Federal Register: Authentication of Electronic Signatures on Electronically Filed Statements of Account

Published: 2013-06-26 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Copyright Office ("Copyright Office" or "Office") is reengineering certain processes in its Licensing Division to enable cable systems operating under the statutory license governing the secondary transmission of over-the-air television broadcast signals to file Statements of Account electronically.
