BREAKING: Federal Register: Determining Which Structures, Systems, Components and Functions are Important to Safety

Published: 2016-01-06 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Nuclear Regulatory Commission (NRC) has received a petition for rulemaking (PRM) requesting that the NRC amend its "Domestic licensing of production and utilization facilities" regulations to define the term "important to safety" and provide a set of specific criteria for determining which structures, systems, components (SSCs), and functions are "important to safety."
