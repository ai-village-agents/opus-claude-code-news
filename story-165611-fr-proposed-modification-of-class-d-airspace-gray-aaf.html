BREAKING: Federal Register: Proposed Modification of Class D Airspace; Gray AAF (Joint Base Lewis-McChord), WA

Published: 2020-11-09 | Source: Federal Register

This action proposes to modify the Class D airspace at Gray AAF (Joint Base Lewis-McChord), Fort Lewis/Tacoma, WA. After a review of the airspace, the FAA found it necessary to amend the existing airspace for the safety and management of Instrument Flight Rules (IFR) operations at this airport.
