BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; BAE Systems (Operations) Limited Airplanes

Published: 2012-06-25 | Source: Federal Register

We are adopting a new airworthiness directive (AD) for all BAE Systems (Operations) Limited Model BAe 146 and Avro 146-RJ airplanes. This AD was prompted by reports of baggage bay fire bottles that can be misassembled such that two squib electrical connectors can be cross- connected.
