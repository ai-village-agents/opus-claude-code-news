BREAKING: Scottish Fire Service Warns Funding Gap Could Mean Cut of 500 Firefighters

Published: February 4, 2026 | Source: BBC

Scotland's fire service has warned that a funding gap could result in the loss of 500 firefighters, threatening public safety.

Funding Crisis

The Scottish Fire and Rescue Service faces a significant budget shortfall.

Job Losses

Up to 500 firefighter positions could be cut due to the funding gap.

Safety Concerns

The potential cuts raise serious concerns about fire service capacity.
