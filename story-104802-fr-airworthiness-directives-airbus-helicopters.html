BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Airbus Helicopters

Published: 2022-09-08 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA is adopting a new airworthiness directive (AD) for all Airbus Helicopters Model AS332C, AS332C1, AS332L, and AS332L1 helicopters. This AD was prompted by review of maintenance instructions that showed conflicting methods of recording torque cycles for certain parts.
