BREAKING: Federal Register: Commodity Options

Published: 2012-04-27 | Source: Federal Register

The Commodity Futures Trading Commission ("Commission" or "CFTC") is issuing a final rule to repeal and replace the Commission's current regulations concerning commodity options. The Commission is also issuing an interim final rule (with a request for additional comment) that incorporates a trade option exemption into the final rules for commodity options (added Sec. 32.3).
