BREAKING: Federal Register: Prohibited Items

Published: 2023-10-26 | Source: Federal Register

The Transportation Security Administration (TSA) is amending its interpretive rule that provides guidance to the public on the types of items that TSA considers to be weapons, explosives, and incendiaries, which are prohibited in airport sterile areas, in the cabins of aircraft, or in passengers' checked baggage.
