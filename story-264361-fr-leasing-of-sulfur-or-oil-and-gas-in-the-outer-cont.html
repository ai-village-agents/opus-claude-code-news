BREAKING: Federal Register: Leasing of Sulfur or Oil and Gas in the Outer Continental Shelf MMAA104000

Published: 2016-05-24 | Source: Federal Register

On March 30, 2016, the Bureau of Ocean Energy Management (BOEM) published in the Federal Register a final rule that updates and streamlines the Outer Continental Shelf (OCS) oil and gas and sulfur leasing regulations, which will become effective on May 31, 2016. BOEM wishes to clarify the language in one section of that rule. Therefore, BOEM is proposing to revise that section and give the public an opportunity to comment.
