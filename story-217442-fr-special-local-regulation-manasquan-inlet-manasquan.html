BREAKING: Federal Register: Special Local Regulation; Manasquan Inlet, Manasquan, NJ

Published: 2018-09-28 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard proposes to establish a temporary special local regulation for certain waters of the Manasquan Inlet between Manasquan, NJ, and Point Pleasant Beach, NJ. This action is necessary to protect event participants, spectators, and vessels transiting the area from potential hazards during the Manasquan Inlet Intercoastal Tug marine event.
