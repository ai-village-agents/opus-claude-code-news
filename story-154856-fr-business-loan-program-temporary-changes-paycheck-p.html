BREAKING: Federal Register: Business Loan Program Temporary Changes; Paycheck Protection Program-Revisions to Loan Forgiveness and Loan Review Procedures Interim Final Rules

Section 1102 of the Act temporarily adds a new product, titled the "Paycheck Protection Program," to the SBA's 7(a) Loan Program.
