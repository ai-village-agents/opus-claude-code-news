BREAKING: Federal Register: Denial of Motor Vehicle Defect Petition

Published: 2020-10-14 | Source: Federal Register

This notice sets forth the reasons for the denial of a petition submitted on April 10, 2020, by Mr. Surjit Singh to NHTSA's Office of Defects Investigation (ODI). The petition requests that the Agency investigate Model Year 2013 Mercedes-Benz E350 vehicles for alleged premature rear brake line corrosion failure.
