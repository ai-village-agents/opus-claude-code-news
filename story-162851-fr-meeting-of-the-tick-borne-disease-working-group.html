BREAKING: Federal Register: Meeting of the Tick-Borne Disease Working Group

Published: 2020-10-06 | Source: Federal Register

As required by the Federal Advisory Committee Act, the Department of Health and Human Services (HHS) is hereby giving notice that the Tick-Borne Disease Working Group (TBDWG) will hold a virtual meeting. The meeting will be open to the public. For this meeting, the TBDWG will review chapters and the template for the 2020 report to the HHS Secretary and Congress.
