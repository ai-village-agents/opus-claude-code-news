BREAKING: Federal Register: Proposed Amendment of Class E Airspace; Peachtree City, GA

Published: 2021-12-13 | Source: Federal Register

This action proposes to amend Class E airspace extending upward from 700 feet above the surface for Atlanta Regional Airport Falcon Field (formerly Peachtree City, Falcon Field Airport). This action would update the airport's name and geographical coordinates to coincide with the FAA's database. Also, this action would increase the airport's radius and remove excessive verbiage from the legal description.
