BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Public Meeting of Presidio Institute Advisory Council

Published: 2016-08-12 | Source: Federal Register

Pursuant to the Federal Advisory Committee Act, as amended (5 U.S.C. Appendix 2), notice is hereby given that a public meeting of the Presidio Institute Advisory Council (Council) will be held from 10:00 a.m. to 11:30 a.m. on Monday, September 12, 2016. The meeting is open to the public, and oral public comment will be received at the meeting.
