BREAKING: Federal Register: Federal Prevailing Rate Advisory Committee; Cancellation of Upcoming Meeting

Published: 2015-07-17 | Source: Federal Register

The Federal Prevailing Rate Advisory Committee is issuing this notice to cancel the August 20, 2015, public meeting scheduled to be held in Room 5A06A, U.S. Office of Personnel Management Building, 1900 E Street NW., Washington, DC. The original Federal Register notice announcing this meeting was published Monday, December 8, 2014, at 79 FR 72714, with a correction published Wednesday, December 17, 2014, at 79 FR 75189.
