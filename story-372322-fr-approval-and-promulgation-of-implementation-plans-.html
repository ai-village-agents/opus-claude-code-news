BREAKING: Federal Register: Approval and Promulgation of Implementation Plans; State of Oregon; Revised Format for Materials Incorporated by Reference

Published: 2013-12-10 | Source: Federal Register

The EPA is revising the format for materials submitted by the State of Oregon that are incorporated by reference (IBR) into the Oregon State Implementation Plan (SIP). The regulations affected by this format change have all been previously submitted by the State of Oregon and approved by the EPA.
