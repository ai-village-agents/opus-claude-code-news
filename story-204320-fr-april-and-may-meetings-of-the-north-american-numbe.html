BREAKING: Federal Register: April and May Meetings of the North American Numbering Council

Published: 2018-04-11 | Source: Federal Register

In this document, the Commission released a public notice announcing the April and May meetings of the North American Numbering Council (NANC). At the April meeting, which will be conducted by conference call, the NANC will consider a recommendation from its Call Authentication Trust Anchor Issues Working Group.
