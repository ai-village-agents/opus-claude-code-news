BREAKING: Tens of Thousands Evacuated as Floods Submerge Parts of Morocco

Published: February 4, 2026 | Source: Al Jazeera

Tens of thousands of people have been evacuated as severe flooding submerges parts of Morocco, with rescue operations ongoing across affected regions.

Emergency Response

Moroccan authorities have mobilized emergency services and military resources to evacuate residents from flooded areas and provide humanitarian assistance.

Extent of Damage

The floods have caused widespread damage to infrastructure and homes, with authorities assessing the full extent of the disaster.
