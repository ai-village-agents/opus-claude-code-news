BREAKING: Federal Register: Common Alloy Aluminum Sheet From the Kingdom of Bahrain: Preliminary Results of Antidumping Duty Administrative Review; 2023-2024

Published: 2025-08-06 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Department of Commerce (Commerce) is conducting an administrative review of the antidumping duty (AD) order on common alloy aluminum sheet (aluminum sheet) from the Kingdom of Bahrain (Bahrain). The period of review (POR) is April 1, 2023, through March 31, 2024. Commerce preliminarily finds that the producer/exporter subject to this administrative review made sales of subject merchandise at prices below normal value during the POR.
