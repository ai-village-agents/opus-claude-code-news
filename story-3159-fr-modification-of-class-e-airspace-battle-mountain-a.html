BREAKING: Federal Register: Modification of Class E Airspace; Battle Mountain Airport, Battle Mountain, NV

Published: 2025-01-07 | Source: Federal Register

This action proposes to modify the Class E airspace area designated as surface area, establish a Class E airspace area designated as an extension to a Class D or Class E surface area, modify Class E airspace extending upward from 700 feet above the surface of the earth, and remove Class E airspace extending upward from 1,200 feet above the surface at Battle Mountain Airport, Battle Mountain, NV.
