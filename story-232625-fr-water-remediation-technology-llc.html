BREAKING: Federal Register: Water Remediation Technology LLC

Published: 2017-04-24 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Nuclear Regulatory Commission (NRC) has received an application from Water Remediation Technology, LLC for renewal of Materials License No. SUC-1591, which authorizes the licensee to offer a water treatment program to remove uranium from drinking water at community water systems.
