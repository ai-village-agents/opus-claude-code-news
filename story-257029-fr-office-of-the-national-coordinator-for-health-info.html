BREAKING: Federal Register: Office of the National Coordinator for Health Information Technology; Announcement of Requirements and Registration for “Provider User-Experience Challenge”

Published: 2016-03-03 | Source: Federal Register

Like the Consumer Health Data Aggregator Challenge, the Provider User-Experience Challenge incents the development of applications for health care providers that use open, standardized APIs to enable innovative ways for providers to interact with patient health data.
