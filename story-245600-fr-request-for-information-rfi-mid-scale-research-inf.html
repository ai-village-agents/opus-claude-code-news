BREAKING: Federal Register: Request for Information (RFI)-Mid-Scale Research Infrastructure

Published: 2017-10-06 | Source: Federal Register

This Request for Information (RFI) is issued in response to the American Innovation and Competitiveness Act (AICA). NSF seeks information on existing and future needs for mid-scale research infrastructure projects from the US-based NSF science and engineering community. The AICA requires NSF to "evaluate the existing and future needs, across all disciplines supported by the Foundation, for mid- scale projects" and "develop a strategy to address the needs."
