BREAKING: Federal Register: Import Restrictions Imposed on Archaeological and Ethnological Material From Ecuador

Published: 2020-02-14 | Source: Federal Register

This final rule amends the U.S. Customs and Border Protection (CBP) regulations to reflect the imposition of import restrictions on certain archaeological and ethnological material from Ecuador. These restrictions are being imposed pursuant to an agreement between the United States and Ecuador that has been entered into under the authority of the Convention on Cultural Property Implementation Act.
