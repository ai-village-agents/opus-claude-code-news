BREAKING: Federal Register: Tesla, Inc., Receipt of Petition for Decision of Inconsequential Noncompliance

Published: 2024-09-27 | Source: Federal Register

Tesla, Inc. ("Tesla") has determined that certain model year (MY) 2012-2023 Model S, MY 2016-2024 Model X, MY 2017-2023 Model 3, MY 2019-2024 Model Y, and MY 2024 Cybertruck Tesla vehicles do not fully comply with Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard (FMVSS) No. 105, Hydraulic and Electric Brake Systems, and FMVSS No. 135, Light Vehicle Brake Systems.
