BREAKING: Federal Register: Technical Assistance on State Data Collection-IDEA Data Management Center

Published: 2024-11-07 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Education (Department) proposes a priority for an IDEA Data Management Center, under the Technical Assistance on State Data Collection program. The Department may use this priority for competitions in fiscal year (FY) 2025 and later years.
