BREAKING: Federal Register: Federal Management Regulation; Annual Real Property Inventories

Published: 2018-08-22 | Source: Federal Register

The General Services Administration (GSA) is issuing a direct final rule to remove and reserve regulations regarding the Federal Management Regulation (FMR). The regulations contain ambiguous and outdated provisions regarding reporting requirements, as well as references to systems and forms that are no longer used by agencies or GSA.
