BREAKING: Federal Register: Walla Walla Basin Spring Chinook Hatchery Program

Published: 2013-03-28 | Source: Federal Register

In accordance with the National Environmental Policy Act (NEPA), BPA intends to prepare an EIS on its decision whether to fund the Confederated Tribes of the Umatilla Indian Reservation's (CTUIR) proposal to construct and operate a hatchery for spring Chinook salmon in the Walla Walla River basin.
