BREAKING: Federal Register: Chlorpyrifos Registration Review; Revised Human Health Risk Assessment; Extension of Comment Period

Published: 2015-03-13 | Source: Federal Register

EPA issued a notice in the Federal Register of January 14, 2015, concerning the availability of the chlorpyrifos registration review; revised human health risk assessment. This document extends the comment period for 45 days, from March 16, 2015 to April 30, 2015.
