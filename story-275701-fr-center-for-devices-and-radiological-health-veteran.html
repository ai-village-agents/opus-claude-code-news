BREAKING: Federal Register: Center for Devices and Radiological Health Veteran Amputee Devices; Public Workshop; Request for Comments

Published: 2016-10-04 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA) is announcing the following public workshop entitled "Center for Devices and Radiological Health Veteran Amputee Devices." The purpose of this workshop is to engage all stakeholders involved in the research, development, and marketing of prosthetic limb medical devices used by veteran amputees.
