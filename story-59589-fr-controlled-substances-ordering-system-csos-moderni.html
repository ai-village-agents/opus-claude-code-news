BREAKING: Federal Register: Controlled Substances Ordering System (CSOS) Modernization

Published: 2023-02-02 | Source: Federal Register

This rule proposes to amend the Drug Enforcement Administration's (DEA) regulations to conform to the Controlled Substances Ordering System (CSOS) modernization effort by requiring all CSOS enrollment applications and supporting materials to be submitted through the Diversion Control Division secure online portal.
