BREAKING: Federal Register: Approval of California Air Plan Revisions, Sacramento Metropolitan Air Quality Management District

Published: 2017-12-04 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is taking final action to approve a revision to the Sacramento Metropolitan Air Quality Management District (SMAQMD) portion of the California State Implementation Plan (SIP). This revision concerns emissions of volatile organic compounds (VOCs) from landfill gas flaring at the Kiefer Landfill in Sacramento, California.
