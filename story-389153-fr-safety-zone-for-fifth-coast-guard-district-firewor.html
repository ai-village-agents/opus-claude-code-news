BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone for Fifth Coast Guard District Fireworks Display Pasquotank River; Elizabeth City, NC

Published: 2012-06-21 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is temporarily changing the enforcement location of a safety zone for one specific recurring fireworks display in the Fifth Coast Guard District. This regulation applies to only one recurring fireworks event, held adjacent to the Pasquotank River, Elizabeth City, North Carolina.
