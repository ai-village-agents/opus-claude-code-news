BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Final Federal Agency Action on Proposed Transportation Project in Illinois

Published: 2017-01-13 | Source: Federal Register

This notice announces actions taken by the FHWA that are final within the meaning of 23 U.S.C. 139(l)(1). The action relates to a the proposed construction of a new highway between Huntley Road and Illinois Route 62 and a new bridge crossing over the Fox River in Kane County.
