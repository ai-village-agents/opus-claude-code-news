BREAKING: Federal Register: Milk in California; Federal Milk Marketing Order Promulgation

Published: 2018-06-08 | Source: Federal Register

This final rule establishes a Federal Milk Marketing Order (FMMO) regulating the handling of milk in California. This final rule issues a marketing order incorporating the entire state of California and adopts the same dairy product classification and pricing provisions used throughout the current FMMO system.
