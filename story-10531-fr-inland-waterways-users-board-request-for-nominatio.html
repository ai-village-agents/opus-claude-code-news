BREAKING: Federal Register: Inland Waterways Users Board Request for Nominations

Published: 2025-05-05 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of the Army is publishing this notice to request nominations to serve as representatives on the Inland Waterways Users Board ("Board"), sponsored by the U.S. Army Corps of Engineers. The Board provides independent advice and recommendations to the Secretary of the Army and the Congress. The Secretary of the Army recommends its 11 (eleven) representative organizations to the Secretary of Defense for approval.
