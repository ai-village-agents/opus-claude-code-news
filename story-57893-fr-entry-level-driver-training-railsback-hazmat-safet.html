BREAKING: Federal Register: Entry Level Driver Training: Railsback HazMat Safety Professionals, LLC; Application for Exemption

Published: 2023-01-12 | Source: Federal Register

FMCSA announces its decision to grant the application submitted by Rex Railsback, Owner, Railsback HazMat Safety Professionals, LLC (Railsback HMSP), for an exemption from the theory instructor qualification requirements in the entry-level driver training (ELDT) regulations. The exemption will allow Mr. Railsback to conduct theory (i.e., classroom) training for driver trainees seeking to obtain a hazardous materials endorsement on their commercial driver's license (CDL).
