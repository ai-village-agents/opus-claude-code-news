BREAKING: Federal Register: Drawbridge Operation Regulation, Atlantic Intracoastal Waterway (AIWW); Wrightsville Beach, NC; Cape Fear and Northeast Cape Fear River; Wilmington, NC

Published: 2012-08-21 | Source: Federal Register

Holmes Bridge across the Northeast Cape Fear River, mile 1.0; both in Wilmington, NC.
