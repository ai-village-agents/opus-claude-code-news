BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Eurocopter France Helicopters

Published: 2014-01-14 | Source: Federal Register

We are adopting a new airworthiness directive (AD) for Eurocopter France (Eurocopter) Model EC225LP helicopters. This AD requires inspecting the swashplates for corrosion or a crack, and making the appropriate repairs or replacement of parts. This AD was prompted by the discovery of corrosion on the swashplates when the main rotor hub (MRH) assemblies were reconditioned.
