BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Safran Helicopter Engines, S.A. (Type Certificate Previously Held by Turbomeca, S.A.) Engines

Published: 2023-07-10 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA is superseding Airworthiness Directive (AD) 2023-01- 12, which applied to all Safran Helicopter Engines, S.A. (Safran) Model Arriel 1C, Arriel 1C1, and Arriel 1C2 engines. AD 2023-01-12 required replacing affected fire detectors and prohibited installation of affected fire detectors. Since the FAA issued AD 2023-01-12, the FAA has determined that Model Arriel 1K1 engines are also affected by the unsafe condition. This AD is prompted by reports of false engine fire warnings.
