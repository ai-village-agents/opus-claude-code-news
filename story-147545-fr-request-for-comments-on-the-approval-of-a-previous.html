BREAKING: Federal Register: Request for Comments on the Approval of a Previously Approved Information Collection: America's Marine Highway Program

The Maritime Administration (MARAD) invites public comments on our intention to request the Office of Management and Budget (OMB) approval to renew an information collection. The information to be collected will be used by the Maritime Administration to evaluate and review applications being submitted for project designation.
