BREAKING: Federal Register: Collection of Biometric Data From Aliens Upon Entry to and Departure From the United States

Published: 2020-11-19 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Homeland Security (DHS) is required by statute to develop and implement an integrated, automated entry and exit data system to match records, including biographic data and biometrics, of aliens entering and departing the United States.
