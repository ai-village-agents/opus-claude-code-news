BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Intent To Rule on Request To Dispose 10.3 Acres of Land at Dillant-Hopkins Airport, Swanzey, NH

Published: 2021-05-12 | Source: Federal Register

Notice is being given that the FAA is considering a request from the City of Keene to dispose of 10.3 acres of land at Dillant- Hopkins Airport, Swanzey, NH, under the provisions of 49 U.S.C. 47107(h)(2). The land is no longer needed for aviation purposes and may be disposed of by the airport. Ninety percent of the proceeds will be returned to the Federal Aviation Administration and used for a future airport grant.
