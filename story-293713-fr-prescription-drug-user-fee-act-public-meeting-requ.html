BREAKING: Federal Register: Prescription Drug User Fee Act; Public Meeting; Request for Comments

Published: 2015-05-13 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA or Agency) is announcing a public meeting on the reauthorization of the Prescription Drug User Fee Act (PDUFA) for fiscal years (FYs) 2018 through 2022. PDUFA authorizes FDA to collect user fees for the process for the review of human drugs. The current legislative authority for PDUFA expires in September 2017.
