BREAKING: Federal Register: Advisory Committee on Historical Diplomatic Documentation; Notice of Meeting

Published: 2012-11-21 | Source: Federal Register

The Advisory Committee on Historical Diplomatic Documentation's meeting location on December 10-11, 2012 has changed from that provided in the previous Federal Register Notice at 77 FR 5292, February 2, 2012. The new location will be at the National Archives in College Park, MD on December 10, 2012; and in room 1205 of the Harry S. Truman building of Main State on December 11, 2012.
