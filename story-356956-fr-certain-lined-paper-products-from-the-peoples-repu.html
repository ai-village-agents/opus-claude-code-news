BREAKING: Federal Register: Certain Lined Paper Products From the People's Republic of China: Preliminary Results and Rescission in Part of Antidumping Duty Administrative Review; 2011-2012

Published: 2013-06-10 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Commerce ("the Department") is conducting an administrative review of the antidumping duty order on certain lined paper products ("CLPP") from the People's Republic of China ("PRC"). The period of review ("POR") is September 1, 2011, through August 31, 2012.
