BREAKING: Federal Register: Gulf of the Farallones and Monterey Bay National Marine Sanctuaries Regulations on Introduced Species

Published: 2014-03-27 | Source: Federal Register

On March 18, 2013 (78 FR 16622), NOAA proposed to prohibit the introduction of introduced species into the state waters of Gulf of the Farallones and Monterey Bay national marine sanctuaries (GFNMS and MBNMS, respectively). The proposed prohibition included exceptions for the catch and release of striped bass and for introduced species as part of mariculture activities in the Tomales Bay region of GFNMS (the only geographic area within sanctuaries offshore of California where mariculture occurs)...
