BREAKING: Federal Register: Export Administration Regulations: Crime Controls and Expansion/Update of U.S. Persons Controls; Extension of Comment Period

Published: 2024-09-26 | Source: Federal Register

On July 29, 2024, the Bureau of Industry and Security (BIS) published in the Federal Register a proposed rule, "Export Administration Regulations: Crime Controls and Expansion/Update of U.S. Persons Controls" with comments originally due September 27, 2024. This notification extends the deadline for written comments to October 15, 2024.
