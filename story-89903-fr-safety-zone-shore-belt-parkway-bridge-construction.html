BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone; Shore (Belt) Parkway Bridge Construction, Mill Basin; Brooklyn, NY

Published: 2022-03-01 | Source: Federal Register

The safety zone was established to protect persons and vessels from potential hazards associated with bridge demolition and construction operations.
