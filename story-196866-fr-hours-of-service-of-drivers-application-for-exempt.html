BREAKING: Federal Register: Hours of Service of Drivers: Application for Exemption; American Disposal Services, Inc. (ADS)

Published: 2018-01-02 | Source: Federal Register

FMCSA announces that American Disposal Services, Inc. (ADS) has requested an exemption from the requirement that a motor carrier install and require each of its drivers to use an electronic logging device (ELD) to record the driver's hours-of-service (HOS) no later than December 18, 2017. ADS further requested an exemption from the required use of paper records of duty status (RODS).
