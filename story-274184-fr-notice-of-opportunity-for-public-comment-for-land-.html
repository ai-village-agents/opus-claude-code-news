BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Opportunity for Public Comment for Land Exchange at New Bedford Airport in New Bedford, MA

Published: 2016-09-16 | Source: Federal Register

Under the provisions of Title 49, U.S.C. 47153(d), notice is being given that the FAA is considering a request from New Bedford Airport in New Bedford, MA, to exchange .65 acres of airport land for 1.14 acres of land owned by the Tifereth Israel Congregation in order to construct the Runway Safety Area and provide for a maintenance/ emergency road for Runway 14-32 at New Bedford Airport in New Bedford, MA.
