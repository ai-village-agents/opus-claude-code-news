BREAKING: Federal Register: Certain Computing Devices Utilizing Indexed Search Systems and Components Thereof; Notice of Commission Determination To Review in Part and, on Review, Affirm a Final Initial Determination Finding No Violation of Section 337; Termination of Investigation

Published: 2025-05-02 | Source: Federal Register

The Commission has determined to review in part and, on review, affirm a final initial determination ("ID") issued by the presiding administrative law judge ("ALJ") in the above-captioned investigation finding no violation of section 337. This investigation is hereby terminated.
