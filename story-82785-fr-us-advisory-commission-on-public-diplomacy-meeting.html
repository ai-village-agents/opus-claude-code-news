BREAKING: Federal Register: U.S. Advisory Commission on Public Diplomacy; Meeting

Published: 2023-11-24 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Advisory Commission on Public Diplomacy (ACPD) will hold an in-person public meeting with online (Zoom) access. The meeting will focus on the integration of diversity, equity, inclusion, and accessibility (DEIA) principles into U.S. government public diplomacy programming abroad, based on a forthcoming ACPD special report titled "Public Diplomacy and DEIA Promotion: Telling America's Story to the World."
