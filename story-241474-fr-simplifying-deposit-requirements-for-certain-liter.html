BREAKING: Federal Register: Simplifying Deposit Requirements for Certain Literary Works and Musical Compositions

Published: 2017-08-16 | Source: Federal Register

The United States Copyright Office is proposing to amend the regulations governing the deposit requirements for certain types of literary works and musical compositions. Specifically, the proposed rule will apply to certain types of "literary monographs," which are defined, in part, as literary works published in one volume or a finite number of separate volumes.
