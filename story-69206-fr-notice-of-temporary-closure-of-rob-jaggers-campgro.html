BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Temporary Closure of Rob Jaggers Campground

Published: 2023-06-06 | Source: Federal Register

Notice is hereby given that a temporary closure will be in effect on public lands administered by the Bureau of Land Management (BLM) Roswell Field Office during the American Endurance Ride Conference's official permitted endurance horseback riding events.
