BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone, Coast Guard Exercise Area, Hood Canal, WA

Published: 2012-10-05 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Coast Guard is proposing to establish a safety zone around vessels involved in Coast Guard training exercises in Hood Canal, WA. A safety zone is necessary to ensure the safety of the maritime public during these exercises, which involve fast moving surface vessels, smoke machines, pyrotechnics, and other elements which could create safety concerns for waterway users.
