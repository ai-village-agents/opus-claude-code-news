BREAKING: Federal Register: Legal Assistance

Published: 2019-08-02 | Source: Federal Register

This final rule removes Department of the Navy (DON) regulations concerning Legal Assistance. Removal is appropriate because the regulation does not affect how the public engages the DON regarding legal assistance and does not place obligations on the public.
