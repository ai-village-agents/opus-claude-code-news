BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Final Federal Agency Action on Proposed Transportation Project in Georgia

Published: 2025-10-02 | Source: Federal Register

The FHWA, on behalf of the Georgia Department of Transportation (GDOT), is issuing this notice to announce actions taken by GDOT and other Federal agencies that are final agency actions. These actions relate to the proposed construction of a new interchange along Interstate 75 (I-75) at County Road (CR) 312/Bethlehem Road.
