BREAKING: Federal Register: Proposed Information Collection Request Submitted for Public Comment; Retirement Savings Lost and Found

Published: 2024-04-16 | Source: Federal Register

The Department is proposing to collect information voluntarily in order to establish the Retirement Savings Lost and Found online searchable database described in section 523 of the Employee Retirement Income Security Act of 1974 (ERISA) and to connect missing participants and other individuals who have lost track of their retirement benefits with such benefits.
