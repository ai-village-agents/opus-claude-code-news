BREAKING: Federal Register: Publication or Submission of Quotations Without Specified Information

Published: 2020-10-27 | Source: Federal Register

The Securities and Exchange Commission (the "SEC" or the "Commission") is adopting amendments to Rule 15c2-11 (the "Rule") under the Securities Exchange Act of 1934 (the "Exchange Act"), which governs the publication of quotations for securities in a quotation medium other than a national securities exchange, i.e., over-the- counter ("OTC") securities.
