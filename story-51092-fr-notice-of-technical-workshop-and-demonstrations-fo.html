BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Technical Workshop and Demonstrations for Vehicle Classification Test Procedure

Published: 2024-10-29 | Source: Federal Register

NHTSA seeks public comment on draft test procedure (TP) number TP-523-00, which is intended to assess vehicles for compliance with certain off-road capabilities requirements for vehicle classification within the Corporate Average Fuel Economy (CAFE) program. This TP is prepared for the limited purpose of use by contracted independent laboratories conducting tests for NHTSA. The TP presents guidelines for a uniform testing and data recording format.
