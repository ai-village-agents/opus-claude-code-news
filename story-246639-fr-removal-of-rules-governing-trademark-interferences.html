BREAKING: Federal Register: Removal of Rules Governing Trademark Interferences

Published: 2017-10-18 | Source: Federal Register

Consistent with Executive Order 13777, "Enforcing the Regulatory Reform Agenda," and Executive Order 13771, "Reducing Regulation and Controlling Regulatory Costs," the United States Patent and Trademark Office (USPTO or Office) proposes to amend the Rules of Practice in Trademark Cases to remove the rules governing trademark interferences.
