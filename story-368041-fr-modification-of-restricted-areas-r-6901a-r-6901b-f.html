BREAKING: Federal Register: Modification of Restricted Areas R-6901A & R-6901B; Fort McCoy, WI

Published: 2013-10-25 | Source: Federal Register

This action amends Restricted Areas R-6901A and R-6901B, Fort McCoy, WI, to accurately identify the segment of Wisconsin State Highway 21 used to identify the shared boundary between the restricted areas. The geographic coordinates contained in the existing legal descriptions for defining the highway actually plot south of it.
