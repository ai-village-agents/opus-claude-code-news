BREAKING: Federal Register: Wayne E. Spencer: Debarment Order

Published: 2012-09-13 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA) is issuing an order under the Federal Food, Drug, and Cosmetic Act (the FD&C Act) permanently debarring Wayne E. Spencer from providing services in any capacity to a person that has an approved or pending drug product application.
