BREAKING: Federal Register: Atlantic Highly Migratory Species; 2012 Atlantic Shark Commercial Fishing Season

Published: 2012-01-24 | Source: Federal Register

This final rule establishes the opening dates and adjusts quotas for the 2012 fishing season for the Atlantic commercial shark fisheries. Quotas were adjusted based on over- and/or underharvests experienced during the 2010 and 2011 Atlantic commercial shark fishing seasons.
