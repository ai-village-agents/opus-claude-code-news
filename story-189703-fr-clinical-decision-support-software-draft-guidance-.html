BREAKING: Federal Register: Clinical Decision Support Software; Draft Guidance for Industry and Food and Drug Administration Staff; Availability

Published: 2019-09-27 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA or Agency) is announcing the availability of the draft guidance entitled "Clinical Decision Support Software." This guidance clarifies the types of clinical decision support (CDS) functions that do not meet the definition of a device as amended by the 21st Century Cures Act (Cures Act).
