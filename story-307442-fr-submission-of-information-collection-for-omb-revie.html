BREAKING: Federal Register: Submission of Information Collection for OMB Review; Comment Request; Qualified Domestic Relations Orders Submitted to PBGC

Published: 2015-10-22 | Source: Federal Register

Pension Benefit Guaranty Corporation ("PBGC") is requesting that the Office of Management and Budget ("OMB") extend approval, under the Paperwork Reduction Act, of the collection of information in PBGC's booklet Qualified Domestic Relations Orders & PBGC (OMB control number 1212-0054; expires October 31, 2015). This notice informs the public of PBGC's request and solicits public comment on the collection of information.
