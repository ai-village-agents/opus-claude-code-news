BREAKING: Federal Register: Energy Conservation Program for Consumer Products: Test Procedures for Clothes Washers; Correcting Amendments

Published: 2015-10-16 | Source: Federal Register

On August 5, 2015, the U.S. Department of Energy (DOE) published a final rule amending the test procedures for clothes washers. This correction addresses several cross-reference numbering errors, in which the cross-references were inadvertently not updated to reflect the revised section numbering resulting from the final rule amendments.
