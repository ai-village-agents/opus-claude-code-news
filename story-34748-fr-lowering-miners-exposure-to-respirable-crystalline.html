BREAKING: Federal Register: Lowering Miners' Exposure to Respirable Crystalline Silica and Improving Respiratory Protection

Published: 2024-04-18 | Source: Federal Register

The Mine Safety and Health Administration (MSHA) is amending its existing standards to better protect miners against occupational exposure to respirable crystalline silica, a significant health hazard, and to improve respiratory protection for miners from exposure to airborne contaminants.
