BREAKING: Federal Register: Extension of Comment Period for Asset-Backed Securities Release

Published: 2014-04-02 | Source: Federal Register

On February 25, 2014, the Securities and Exchange Commission re-opened the comment period on two releases related to asset-backed securities. The Commission re-opened the comment period to permit interested persons to comment on an approach for the dissemination of potentially sensitive asset-level data.
