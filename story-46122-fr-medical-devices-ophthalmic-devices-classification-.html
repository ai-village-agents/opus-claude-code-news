BREAKING: Federal Register: Medical Devices; Ophthalmic Devices; Classification of the Hydrophilic Re-Coating Solution

Published: 2024-09-05 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA, Agency, or we) is classifying the hydrophilic re-coating solution into class II (special controls). The special controls that apply to the device type are identified in this order and will be part of the codified language for the hydrophilic re-coating solution's classification.
