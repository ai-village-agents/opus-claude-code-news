BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Airbus Airplanes

Published: 2015-10-19 | Source: Federal Register

We are adopting a new airworthiness directive (AD) for certain Airbus Model A330-200, A330-200 Freighter, A330-300, A340-200, A340- 300, A340-500, and A340-600 series airplanes. This AD requires a detailed inspection of the girt installation of each escape slide and slide raft, and corrective action if necessary.
