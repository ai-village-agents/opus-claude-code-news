BREAKING: Federal Register: Special Local Regulations; Savannah Harbor Boat Parade of Lights and Fireworks, Savannah River

Published: 2016-10-24 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard will enforce the Savannah Harbor Boat Parade of Lights and Fireworks Special Local Regulation from 5 p.m. through 10 p.m. on November 26, 2016. This action is necessary to ensure safety of life on navigable waters of the United States during the Savannah Harbor Boat Parade of Lights and Fireworks displays.
