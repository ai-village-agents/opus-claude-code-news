BREAKING: Federal Register: Meeting of the United States Travel and Tourism Advisory Board

Published: 2018-09-25 | Source: Federal Register

The United States Travel and Tourism Advisory Board (Board or TTAB) will hold a meeting on Thursday, October 11, 2018. The Board advises the Secretary of Commerce on matters relating to the U.S. travel and tourism industry. This will be the first meeting of the newly appointed 2018-2020 Board.
