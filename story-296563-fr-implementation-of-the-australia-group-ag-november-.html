BREAKING: Federal Register: Implementation of the Australia Group (AG) November 2013 Intersessional Decisions

Published: 2015-06-16 | Source: Federal Register

The Bureau of Industry and Security (BIS) publishes this final rule to amend the Export Administration Regulations (EAR) to implement the recommendations presented at the November 2013 Australia Group (AG) intersessional implementation meeting and later adopted pursuant to the AG silent approval procedure.
