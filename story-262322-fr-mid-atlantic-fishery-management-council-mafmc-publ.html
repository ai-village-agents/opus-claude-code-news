BREAKING: Federal Register: Mid-Atlantic Fishery Management Council (MAFMC); Public Hearings

Published: 2016-05-02 | Source: Federal Register

The Mid-Atlantic Fishery Management Council (Council) will hold seven public hearings in May and June 2016 to solicit public input on the Unmanaged Forage Omnibus Amendment. The Council is also soliciting written comments on the amendment through 11:59 p.m. on Friday, June 17, 2016.
