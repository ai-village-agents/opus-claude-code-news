BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone, Chesapeake Bay; Cape Charles, VA

Published: 2014-10-03 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard proposes to establish a safety zone on the navigable waters of the Chesapeake Bay in the vicinity of Bayshore Road in the Cape Charles Harbor, Cape Charles, VA. This proposed safety zone would restrict vessel movement in the specified area during the Town of Cape Charles New Years Eve fireworks display between 10 p.m. and 10:30 p.m. on December 31, 2014.
