BREAKING: Federal Register: n-Methylpyrrolidone (NMP); Regulation Under the Toxic Substances Control Act (TSCA)

Published: 2024-06-14 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA or the "Agency") is proposing to address the unreasonable risk of injury to human health presented by n-methylpyrrolidone (NMP) under its conditions of use as documented in EPA's risk evaluation and risk determination for NMP pursuant to the Toxic Substances Control Act (TSCA).
