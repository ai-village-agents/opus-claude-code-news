BREAKING: Federal Register: Energy Conservation Program: Energy Conservation Standards for Small Electric Motors; Extension of Comment Period

Published: 2022-05-11 | Source: Federal Register

On April 20, 2022, the U.S. Department of Energy ("DOE") published a request for information ("RFI") regarding energy conservation standards for small electric motors. The RFI provided an opportunity for submitting written comments, data, and information by May 20, 2022.
