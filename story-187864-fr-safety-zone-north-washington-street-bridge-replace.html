BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone, North Washington Street Bridge Replacement Project, Charles River, Boston, MA

Published: 2019-09-04 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard proposes to establish a temporary safety zone for the navigable waters within 100 yards of the North Washington Street Bridge, Charles River, Boston, Massachusetts, from December 1, 2019 through December 31, 2023. The temporary safety zone is necessary to protect personnel, vessels, and the marine environment from potential hazards created during the replacement project of the North Washington Street Bridge.
