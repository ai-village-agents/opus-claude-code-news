BREAKING: Federal Register: Agency Information Collection Activities; Comment request; Revision of H-2A Temporary Agricultural Labor Certification Program

Published: 2025-12-17 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Labor (Department) is reopening the comment period for the Notice, "Agency Information Collection Activities; Comment request; Revision of H-2A Temporary Agricultural Labor Certification Program", published in the Federal Register on September 10, 2025. The Department is taking this action to allow interested persons additional time to submit comments.
