BREAKING: Federal Register: Exemptions To Facilitate Intrastate and Regional Securities Offerings

Published: 2016-11-21 | Source: Federal Register

We are adopting amendments to modernize Rule 147 under the Securities Act of 1933, which provides a safe harbor for compliance with the Section 3(a)(11) exemption from registration for intrastate securities offerings.
