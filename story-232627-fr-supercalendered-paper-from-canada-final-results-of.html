BREAKING: Federal Register: Supercalendered Paper From Canada: Final Results of Countervailing Duty Expedited Review

Published: 2017-04-24 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Commerce (the Department) has conducted an expedited review of the countervailing duty (CVD) order on supercalendered paper (SC paper) from Canada. The period of review (POR) for which we are measuring subsidies is January 1, 2014, through December 31, 2014. We determine that Irving Paper Limited received countervailable subsidies during the POR and that Catalyst received de minimis countervailable subsidies.
