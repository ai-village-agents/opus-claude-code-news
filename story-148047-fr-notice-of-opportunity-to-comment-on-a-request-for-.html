BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Opportunity To Comment on a Request for a General Statement of Policy or Guidance on Whether “Zipper Clauses” Are Mandatory Subjects of Bargaining

Published: 2020-03-31 | Source: Federal Register

The Federal Labor Relations Authority (Authority) solicits written comments on a request from the U.S. Office of Personnel Management (OPM) for a general statement of policy or guidance (general statement) holding that "zipper clauses"--which are provisions that would foreclose or limit mid-term bargaining during the term of a collective-bargaining agreement (CBA)--are a mandatory subject of bargaining.
