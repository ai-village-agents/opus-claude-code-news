BREAKING: Federal Register: Civilian Employment and Reemployment Rights for Service Members, Former Service Members and Applicants of the Uniformed Services

Published: 2014-07-28 | Source: Federal Register

This rule updates established policy, assigned responsibilities, and procedures for informing Service members and individuals who apply for uniformed service of their civilian employment and reemployment rights, benefits, and obligations.
