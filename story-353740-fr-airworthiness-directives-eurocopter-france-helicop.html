BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Eurocopter France Helicopters

Published: 2013-05-01 | Source: Federal Register

We are adopting a new airworthiness directive (AD) for Eurocopter France (Eurocopter) Model SA-365N, SA-365N1, AS-365N2, AS 365 N3, and SA-366G1 helicopters. This AD requires an initial and recurring inspection of the 9-degree frame for a crack, and repair of the frame if there is a crack.
