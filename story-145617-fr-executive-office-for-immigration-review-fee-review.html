BREAKING: Federal Register: Executive Office for Immigration Review; Fee Review

Published: 2020-02-28 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Justice's Executive Office for Immigration Review ("EOIR") imposes fees, also known as user charges, for the filing of certain EOIR forms for applications for relief, appeals filed with the Board of Immigration Appeals ("BIA"), and motions to reopen or reconsider. When applicable, the current fee for EOIR applications for relief is $100, and the fee for motions or appeals is $110. EOIR last reviewed and updated these fees 33 years ago, in 1986.
