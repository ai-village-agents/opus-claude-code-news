BREAKING: Federal Register: Amendment of Class D and Class E Airspace and Revocation of Class E Airspace; Youngstown/Warren, OH

Published: 2024-12-10 | Source: Federal Register

This action amends the Class D and Class E airspace and revokes Class E airspace at Youngstown/Warren, OH. This action is the result of an airspace review conducted due to the decommissioning of the Youngstown very high frequency omnidirectional range (VOR) as part of the VOR Minimum Operational Network (MON) Program.
