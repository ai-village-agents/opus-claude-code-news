BREAKING: Federal Register: Updating OSHA Standards Based on National Consensus Standards; Signage

Published: 2013-06-13 | Source: Federal Register

The Occupational Safety and Health Administration ("OSHA" or "the Agency") proposes to update its general industry and construction signage standards by adding references to the latest versions of the American National Standards Institute ("ANSI") standards on specifications for accident prevention signs and tags, ANSI Z535.1-2006(R2011), Z535.2-2011, and Z535.5-2011.
