BREAKING: Federal Register: Amendment of Class D Airspace; St. Paul, MN

Published: 2014-03-04 | Source: Federal Register

This action amends Class D airspace within the St. Paul, MN, area by updating the geographic coordinates for St. Paul Downtown Airport/Holman Field, and South St. Paul Municipal Airport-Richard E. Fleming Field. This action does not change the boundaries or operating requirements of the airspace.
