BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Airbus SAS Airplanes

Published: 2025-06-17 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA proposes to supersede Airworthiness Directive (AD) 2024-22-02, which applies to certain Airbus SAS Model A330-200, -200 Freighter, -300, -800, and -900 series airplanes. AD 2024-22-02 requires revising the existing maintenance or inspection program, as applicable, to incorporate new or more restrictive airworthiness limitations.
