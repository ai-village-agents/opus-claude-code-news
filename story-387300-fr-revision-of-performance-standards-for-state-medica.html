BREAKING: Federal Register: Revision of Performance Standards for State Medicaid Fraud Control Units

Published: 2012-06-01 | Source: Federal Register

This notice sets forth OIG guidance regarding standards OIG will apply in assessing the performance of State Medicaid Fraud Control Units (MFCU or Unit). These standards replace and supersede standards published on September 26, 1994 (59 FR 49080). OIG will apply these standards in certifying and recertifying each Unit and to determine if a Unit is effectively and efficiently carrying out its duties and responsibilities.
