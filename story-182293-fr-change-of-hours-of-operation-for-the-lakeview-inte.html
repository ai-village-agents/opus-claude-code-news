BREAKING: Federal Register: Change of Hours of Operation for the Lakeview Interagency Office, Oregon

Published: 2019-06-21 | Source: Federal Register

The Lakeview Interagency Office, which includes the Bureau of Land Management Lakeview District Office and the Fremont-Winema National Forest Supervisor's Office, will implement new hours of operation, weekdays, excluding Federal holidays, from 8:00 a.m. to 4:30 p.m. The hours of operation for all other Lakeview BLM Offices will not change.
