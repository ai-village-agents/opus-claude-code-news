BREAKING: Federal Register: 180-Day Exclusivity: Questions and Answers; Draft Guidance for Industry; Availability

Published: 2017-01-13 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA or Agency) is announcing the availability of a draft guidance for industry entitled "180-Day Exclusivity: Questions and Answers." This draft guidance is intended to address questions that have been raised about the provisions of the Federal Food, Drug, and Cosmetic Act (FD&C Act) that relate to generic drug exclusivity, which commonly is known as "180-day exclusivity" for generic drug products.
