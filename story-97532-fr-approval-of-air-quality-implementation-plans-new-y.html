BREAKING: Federal Register: Approval of Air Quality Implementation Plans; New York; Revision to 6 NYCRR Part 235 Consumer Products

Published: 2022-06-03 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is proposing to approve a revision to the New York State Implementation Plan (SIP) for the purposes of implementing control of air pollution for volatile organic compounds (VOC). The proposed SIP revision consists of amendments to New York's Codes, Rules, and Regulations (NYCRR) that implement control measures for Consumer Products.
