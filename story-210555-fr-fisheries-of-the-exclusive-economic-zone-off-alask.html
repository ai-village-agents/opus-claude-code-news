BREAKING: Federal Register: Fisheries of the Exclusive Economic Zone Off Alaska; Nontrawl Lead Level 2 Observers

Published: 2018-06-29 | Source: Federal Register

NMFS issues regulations to modify specific provisions of the North Pacific Observer Program. The first two elements of this final rule implement requirements for an observer to obtain a nontrawl lead level 2 (LL2) deployment endorsement and implement a pre-cruise meeting requirement for vessels required to carry an observer with a nontrawl LL2 deployment endorsement.
