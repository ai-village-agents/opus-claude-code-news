BREAKING: Federal Register: Ecosystem Restoration Policy

Published: 2016-04-27 | Source: Federal Register

The Forest Service is issuing a permanent Ecosystem Restoration policy that replaces the Interim Directive, "Ecological Restoration and Resilience Policy," in Forest Service Manual (FSM) 2020. The policy provides broad guidance for restoring ecosystems on National Forest System lands so that they are self-sustaining and, if subject to disturbances or environmental change, have the ability to reorganize and renew themselves.
