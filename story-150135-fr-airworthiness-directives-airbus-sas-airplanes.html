BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Airbus SAS Airplanes

Published: 2020-04-27 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA proposes to adopt a new airworthiness directive (AD) for all Airbus SAS Model A330-941 airplanes. This proposed AD was prompted by a report that seven spoiler servo-controls (SSCs) lost hydraulic locking function due to a sheared seal on the blocking valve.
