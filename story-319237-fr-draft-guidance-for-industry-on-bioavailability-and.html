BREAKING: Federal Register: Draft Guidance for Industry on Bioavailability and Bioequivalence Studies Submitted in New Drug Applications or Investigational New Drug Applications-General Considerations; Availability

Published: 2014-03-18 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA) is announcing the availability of a draft guidance for industry entitled "Bioavailability and Bioequivalence Studies Submitted in NDAs or INDs--General Considerations" (draft BA and BE guidance for NDAs).
