BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Meeting of the National Parks Overflights Advisory Group

Published: 2018-08-14 | Source: Federal Register

The Federal Aviation Administration (FAA) and the National Park Service (NPS), in accordance with the National Parks Air Tour Management Act of 2000, announce the next meeting of the National Parks Overflights Advisory Group (NPOAG). This notification provides the date, location, and agenda for the meeting. Date and Location: The NPOAG will meet on September 18, 2018.
