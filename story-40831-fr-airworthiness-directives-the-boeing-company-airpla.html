BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; The Boeing Company Airplanes

Published: 2024-07-02 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA proposes to adopt a new airworthiness directive (AD) for all The Boeing Company Model 737-600, -700, -700C, -800, -900, and -900ER series airplanes. This proposed AD was prompted by a report of a frame web crack at fuselage station (STA) 328 between stringers S-20R and S-21R common to the frame web notch.
