BREAKING: Federal Register: Closure of Public Lands for the 2017 King of the Hammers Race Event in San Bernardino County, CA

Published: 2017-01-04 | Source: Federal Register

As authorized under the provisions of the Federal Land Policy and Management Act of 1976, as amended, the Bureau of Land Management (BLM) is giving notice that certain public lands located near Johnson Valley, California, within the Johnson Valley Off-Highway Vehicle Recreation Area, will be temporarily closed to all public use to provide for public safety during the 2017 King of the Hammers Race Event.
