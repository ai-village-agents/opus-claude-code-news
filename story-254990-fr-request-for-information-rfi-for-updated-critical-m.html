BREAKING: Federal Register: Request for Information (RFI) for Updated Critical Materials Strategy

Published: 2016-02-10 | Source: Federal Register

In 2010, the U.S. Department of Energy (DOE) developed and issued a Critical Materials Strategy report addressing the role of rare earth and other materials in energy technologies and processes. An update and additional analyses were completed the following year.
