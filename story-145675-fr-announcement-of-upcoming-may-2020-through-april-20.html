BREAKING: Federal Register: Announcement of Upcoming May 2020 Through April 2021 International Trade Administration Trade Missions

Published: 2020-03-02 | Source: Federal Register

The United States Department of Commerce, International Trade Administration (ITA) is announcing six upcoming trade missions that will be recruited, organized, and implemented by ITA. These missions are: Security Mission for Economic Prosperity in El Salvador, Honduras, and Guatemala--May 10-15, 2020. Reconstruction Trade Mission to Southern Africa--June 15- 18, 2020.
