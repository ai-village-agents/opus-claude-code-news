BREAKING: Federal Register: Radio Experimentation and Market Trials-Streamlining Rules

Published: 2013-06-19 | Source: Federal Register

In this document the Commission modifies on its own motion the rules adopted in this proceeding regarding transfer and assignment of experimental licenses of its rules. Upon reflection, the Commission found it in the public interest to specifically prohibit the transfer of program, medical testing, and compliance testing experimental radio licenses, while continuing to permit conventional experimental authorizations to be transferred with the written approval of the Commission.
