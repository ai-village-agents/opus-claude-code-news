BREAKING: Federal Register: Energy Conservation Program: Test Procedure for Walk-In Coolers and Walk-In Freezers

Published: 2020-09-28 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Department of Energy ("DOE") proposes to amend the test procedures for walk-in freezers to address an issue affecting hot gas defrost-equipped unit coolers ("hot gas defrost unit coolers"). DOE is proposing to amend the current test procedure regarding hot gas unit coolers consistent with an update to the industry standard that is incorporated by reference in the DOE test procedure for walk-in freezer refrigeration systems.
