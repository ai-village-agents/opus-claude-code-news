BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone; Charleston Harbor, Charleston County, SC

Published: 2024-12-04 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is establishing a temporary safety zone for navigable waters of the Atlantic Ocean at the Charleston Harbor Entrance Channel, Charleston Harbor, and Cooper River, within a 100- yard radius of the M/V CAPE RACE and all towing vessels supporting its operations.
