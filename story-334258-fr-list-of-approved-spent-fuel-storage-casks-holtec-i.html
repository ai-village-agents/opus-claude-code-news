BREAKING: Federal Register: List of Approved Spent Fuel Storage Casks: Holtec International HI-STORM Underground Maximum Capacity Canister Storage System, Certificate of Compliance No. 1040

Published: 2014-09-09 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Nuclear Regulatory Commission (NRC) is amending its spent fuel storage regulations by adding the Holtec International HI- STORM Underground Maximum Capacity (UMAX) Canister Storage System, Certificate of Compliance (CoC) No. 1040, to the "List of approved spent fuel storage casks." Holtec International intends to provide an underground storage option compatible with the Holtec International HI- STORM FLOOD/WIND (FW) System (CoC No. 1032).
