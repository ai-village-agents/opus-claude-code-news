BREAKING: Federal Register: Proposed Amendment of Class D and Class E Airspace; Elmira, NY

Published: 2016-12-13 | Source: Federal Register

This action proposes to amend Class E Airspace designated as an extension to a Class D surface area at Elmira/Corning Regional Airport, Elmira, NY, as the ERINN Outer Marker has been decommissioned, requiring airspace reconfiguration at the airport. This action also would eliminate the Notice to Airmen (NOTAM) part time status of this Class E Airspace area.
