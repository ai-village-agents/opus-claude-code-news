BREAKING: Federal Register: Hungary Electronic System for Travel Authorization Validity and Eligibility

Published: 2025-09-18 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Department of Homeland Security (DHS), in consultation with the U.S. Department of State, is normalizing Hungary's Electronic System for Travel Authorization (ESTA) travel authorization validity period, and changing the eligibility for citizens or nationals of Hungary traveling under the Visa Waiver Program (VWP) to the United States.
