BREAKING: Federal Register: Equal Participation of Faith-Based Organizations in USAID's Programs and Activities: Implementation of Executive Order 13831

Published: 2020-01-17 | Source: Federal Register

This proposed rule would amend USAID's regulations to implement Executive Order 13831, "Establishment of a White House Faith and Opportunity Initiative."
