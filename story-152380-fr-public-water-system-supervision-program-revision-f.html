BREAKING: Federal Register: Public Water System Supervision Program Revision for the State of Montana

Published: 2020-05-26 | Source: Federal Register

Public notice is hereby given that the state of Montana has revised its Public Water System Supervision (PWSS) Program by establishing Administrative Penalty Authority that applies to its drinking water program. The EPA has reviewed Montana's submittal, and determined that the Administrative Penalty Authority is no less stringent than the federal regulations.
