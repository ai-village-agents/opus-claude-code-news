BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Airbus Airplanes

Published: 2015-01-22 | Source: Federal Register

We are adopting a new airworthiness directive (AD) for certain Airbus Model A319-115, A319-133, A320-214, A320-232, and A320-233 airplanes. This emergency AD was sent previously to all known U.S. owners and operators of these airplanes.
