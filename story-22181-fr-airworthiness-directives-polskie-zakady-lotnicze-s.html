BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Polskie Zakłady Lotnicze Sp. z o.o. Airplanes

Published: 2025-11-04 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA is adopting a new airworthiness directive (AD) for all Polskie Zak[lstrok]ady Lotnicze Sp. z o.o. Model PZL M28 05 airplanes. The FAA previously sent this AD as an emergency AD to all known U.S. owners and operators of these airplanes. This AD was prompted by damage found in Frame No. 29 and rudder control unit mounting components on Frame No. 29.
