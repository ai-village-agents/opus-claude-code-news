BREAKING: Federal Register: Applications for New Awards; Developing Hispanic-Serving Institutions Program

Published: 2020-01-28 | Source: Federal Register

On December 27, 2019, we published in the Federal Register a notice inviting applications for the Developing Hispanic Serving Institutions (DHSI) Program for fiscal year (FY) 2020. This notice revises the deadline for transmittal of applications and the deadline for intergovernmental review. All other requirements and conditions in the notice remain the same.
