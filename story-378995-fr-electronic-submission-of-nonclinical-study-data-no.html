BREAKING: Federal Register: Electronic Submission of Nonclinical Study Data; Notice of Pilot Project

Published: 2012-02-29 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA) Center for Biologics Evaluation and Research (CBER) is announcing an invitation to participate in a pilot evaluation program to test the electronic submission of nonclinical study data using the Standard for Exchange of Nonclinical Data (SEND), a new electronic data standard format, which can be used to support review activity.
