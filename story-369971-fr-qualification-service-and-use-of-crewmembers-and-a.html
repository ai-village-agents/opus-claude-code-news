BREAKING: Federal Register: Qualification, Service, and Use of Crewmembers and Aircraft Dispatchers

Published: 2013-11-12 | Source: Federal Register

This final rule revises the training requirements for pilots in air carrier operations. The regulations enhance air carrier pilot training programs by emphasizing the development of pilots' manual handling skills and adding safety-critical tasks such as recovery from stall and upset.
