BREAKING: Federal Register: Melamine From Qatar; Termination of Investigation

Published: 2024-12-20 | Source: Federal Register

On December 9, 2024, the Department of Commerce published notice in the Federal Register of a negative final antidumping duty determination in connection with the subject investigation concerning Qatar. Accordingly, the antidumping duty investigation concerning melamine from Qatar (Investigation No. 731-TA-1671 (Final)) is terminated.
