BREAKING: Federal Register: Certain Computer and Computer Peripheral Devices, and Components Thereof, and Products Containing Same; Commission Decision to Review an Initial Determination; Schedule for Filing Written Submissions Including Remedy, the Public Interest, and Bonding

Published: 2013-10-30 | Source: Federal Register

Notice is hereby given that the U.S. International Trade Commission has determined to review in the entirety the final initial determination ("ID") issued by the presiding administrative law judge ("ALJ") on August 2, 2013, finding a violation of section 337 of the Tariff Act of 1930, 19 U.S.C. 1337, in this investigation.
