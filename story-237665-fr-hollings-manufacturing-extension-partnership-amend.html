BREAKING: Federal Register: Hollings Manufacturing Extension Partnership-Amendments to the Terms and Schedule of Financial Assistance

Published: 2017-06-27 | Source: Federal Register

NIST is issuing a final rule to amend the regulations governing the Hollings Manufacturing Extension Partnership (MEP) program to reflect the current cost sharing requirements for cooperative agreements for the establishment and operation of MEP Centers, consistent with recent amendments to the MEP authorizing statute.
