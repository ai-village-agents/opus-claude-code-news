BREAKING: Federal Register: Information Collection; Improving Customer Experience (OMB Circular A-11, Section 280 Implementation)

Published: 2020-07-13 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Transportation (DOT) as part of its continuing effort to reduce paperwork and respondent burden, is announcing an opportunity for public comment on a new proposed collection of information by the Agency. Under the Paperwork Reduction Act of 1995 (PRA), Federal Agencies are required to publish notice in the Federal Register concerning each proposed collection of information, and to allow 60 days for public comment in response to the notice.
