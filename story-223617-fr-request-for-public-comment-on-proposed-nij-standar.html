BREAKING: Federal Register: Request for Public Comment on Proposed NIJ Standard Entitled Patrol Rifles for Police Officers

Published: 2018-12-19 | Source: Federal Register

The National Institute of Justice (NIJ) seeks feedback from the public on a proposed NIJ Standard entitled Patrol Rifles for Law Enforcement that specifies minimum performance requirements and test methods for patrol rifles used by U.S. law enforcement.
