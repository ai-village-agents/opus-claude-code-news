BREAKING: Federal Register: Regulations Implementing the FOIA Improvement Act of 2016 and Clarifying the FOIA Regulations

Published: 2016-12-01 | Source: Federal Register

On June 30, 2016, President Obama signed the Freedom of Information Act Improvement Act of 2016. The Act requires agencies to revise their regulations within 180 days to account for the new statutory mandates. After undertaking a review of Commission regulations in accordance with Section 3 of the Act, the Commission is revising its FOIA regulations to incorporate the statutory mandates.
