BREAKING: Federal Register: Amendments to Quality Systems Verification Programs and Conforming Changes

Published: 2016-11-07 | Source: Federal Register

The Agricultural Marketing Service (AMS) proposes to amend its regulations to better reflect the current needs of Quality Systems Verification Program (QSVP) activities and to implement changes created by the merger of the AMS Livestock and Seed Program and the AMS Poultry Programs.
