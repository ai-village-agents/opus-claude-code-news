BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Pratt & Whitney (PW) Turbofan Engines

Published: 2012-03-20 | Source: Federal Register

We are adopting a new airworthiness directive (AD) for Pratt & Whitney (PW) PW2037, PW2037(M), and PW2040 turbofan engines with certain fan blades with a cutback leading edge, installed. This AD was prompted by reports from PW that fan blade leading edge erosion can result in a fan thrust deterioration mode (FTDM) condition, a condition that cannot be detected by the crew, and that reduces the engine's capability of producing required thrust.
