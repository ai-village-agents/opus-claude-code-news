BREAKING: Federal Register: Dixie National Forest; Utah; Brian Head Resort Expansion Environmental Impact Statement

Published: 2025-07-15 | Source: Federal Register

Brian Head Resort Recreation 1, LLC (BHR) has submitted a proposal to the Dixie National Forest (DNF) to pursue approval of projects identified within BHR's 2023 Master Development Plan (MDP).
