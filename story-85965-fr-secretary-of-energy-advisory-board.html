BREAKING: Federal Register: Secretary of Energy Advisory Board

Published: 2022-01-10 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Energy hereby publishes a notice of open meeting on January 25, 2022, of the Secretary of Energy Advisory Board (SEAB). Due to the COVID-19 pandemic, this meeting will be held virtually for members of the public and in-person at DOE Headquarters, James V. Forrestal Building, 1000 Independence Ave. SW, Washington, DC 20585 for SEAB members only.
