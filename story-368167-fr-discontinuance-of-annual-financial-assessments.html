BREAKING: Federal Register: Discontinuance of Annual Financial Assessments

Published: 2013-10-25 | Source: Federal Register

This Notice informs the public that the Department of Veterans Affairs (VA) will no longer request that certain veterans enrolled in the VA health care system annually submit income and asset information. VA uses such information to verify a veteran's continuing eligibility for certain health care benefits; however, VA is now able to get similar information through a means less burdensome on veterans.
