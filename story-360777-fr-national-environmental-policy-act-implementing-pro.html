BREAKING: Federal Register: National Environmental Policy Act; Implementing Procedures; Addition of Categorical Exclusion for Real Property Disposal

Published: 2013-07-23 | Source: Federal Register

This notice announces the addition of a new Categorical Exclusion (CATEX) for Real Property Disposal under the National Environmental Policy Act (NEPA). This CATEX amends the United States Coast Guard and Department of Homeland Security NEPA implementing procedures by establishing a new CATEX for real property disposal undertaken by the United States Coast Guard.
