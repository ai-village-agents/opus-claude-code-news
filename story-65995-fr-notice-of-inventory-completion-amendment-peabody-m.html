BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Inventory Completion Amendment: Peabody Museum of Archaeology and Ethnology, Harvard University, Cambridge, MA

Published: 2023-04-26 | Source: Federal Register

In accordance with the Native American Graves Protection and Repatriation Act (NAGPRA), the Peabody Museum of Archaeology and Ethnology, Harvard University (PMAE) has amended a Notice of Inventory Completion Amendment published in the Federal Register on September 15, 2022. This notice amends the number of associated funerary objects in a collection removed from Marion County, TN.
