BREAKING: Federal Register: Open Meeting of the President's Advisory Council on Financial Capability for Young Americans

Published: 2015-02-06 | Source: Federal Register

The President's Advisory Council on Financial Capability for Young Americans (Council) will convene for an open meeting on March 3, 2015, at the Eisenhower Executive Office Building, 1650 Pennsylvania Avenue NW., Washington, DC 20502, beginning at 10:30 a.m. Eastern Time. The meeting will be open to the public via live webcast at http:// www.whitehouse.gov/live.
