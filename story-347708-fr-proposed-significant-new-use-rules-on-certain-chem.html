BREAKING: Federal Register: Proposed Significant New Use Rules on Certain Chemical Substances

Published: 2013-02-25 | Source: Federal Register

EPA is proposing significant new use rules (SNURs) under the Toxic Substances Control Act (TSCA) for 37 chemical substances which were the subject of premanufacture notices (PMNs). Seventeen of these chemical substances are subject to TSCA section 5(e) consent orders issued by EPA.
