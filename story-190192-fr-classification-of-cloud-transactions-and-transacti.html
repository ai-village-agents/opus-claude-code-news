BREAKING: Federal Register: Classification of Cloud Transactions and Transactions Involving Digital Content; Correction

Published: 2019-10-02 | Source: Federal Register

This document contains a correction to a notice of proposed rulemaking (REG-130700-14) that was published in the Federal Register on August 14, 2019. The proposed regulations relate to classification of cloud transactions for purposes of the international provisions of the Internal Revenue Code.
