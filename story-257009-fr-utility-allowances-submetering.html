BREAKING: Federal Register: Utility Allowances Submetering

Published: 2016-03-03 | Source: Federal Register

This document contains proposed regulations that amend the utility allowance regulations concerning the low-income housing credit. The proposed regulations relate to the circumstances in which utility costs paid by a tenant based on actual consumption in a submetered rent-restricted unit are treated as paid by the tenant directly to the utility company.
