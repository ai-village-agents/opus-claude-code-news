BREAKING: Federal Register: Exemption From Operating Authority Regulations for Providers of Recreational Activities

Published: 2023-06-21 | Source: Federal Register

FMCSA proposes the implementation of the statutory exemption from its operating authority registration rules for providers of recreational activities.
