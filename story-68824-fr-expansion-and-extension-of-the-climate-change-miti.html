BREAKING: Federal Register: Expansion and Extension of the Climate Change Mitigation Pilot Program

Published: 2023-06-01 | Source: Federal Register

On June 3, 2022, the United States Patent and Trademark Office (USPTO) implemented the Climate Change Mitigation Pilot Program as a component of its ongoing efforts to encourage and incentivize innovation in the climate space and as an example of its commitment to policies tackling climate change.
