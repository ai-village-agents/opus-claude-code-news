BREAKING: Federal Register: 2016 National Preparedness for Response Exercise Program (PREP) Guidelines

Published: 2017-12-22 | Source: Federal Register

This notice announces proposed changes to the 2016 PREP Guidelines and solicits public comment to the proposed changes. The U.S. Coast Guard (USCG) is publishing this notice on behalf of the Preparedness for Response Exercise Program Compliance, Coordination, and Consistency Committee (PREP 4C).
