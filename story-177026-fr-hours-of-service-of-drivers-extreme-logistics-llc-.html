BREAKING: Federal Register: Hours of Service of Drivers: Extreme Logistics, LLC, Application for Exemption

Published: 2019-04-18 | Source: Federal Register

FMCSA announces that it has received an application from Extreme Logistics, LLC (applicant) for an exemption from the requirement that drivers of commercial motor vehicles (CMVs) must not drive following the 14th hour after coming on duty. The exemption would apply solely to the drivers of 12 CMVs employed by the applicant in conjunction with staging fireworks shows celebrating Independence Day during the period of June 26-July 8, for the next five years (2019- 2024) inclusive.
