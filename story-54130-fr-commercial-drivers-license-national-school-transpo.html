BREAKING: Federal Register: Commercial Driver's License: National School Transportation Association Application for Renewal of Exemption

Published: 2024-12-02 | Source: Federal Register

FMCSA announces its final decision to renew the exemption granted to the National School Transportation Association (NSTA) for a period of two years to permit all commercial driver's license (CDL) applicants seeking a school bus endorsement to forgo the engine compartment portion of the pre-trip vehicle inspection skills testing requirement, known informally as the "under-the-hood" testing requirement.
