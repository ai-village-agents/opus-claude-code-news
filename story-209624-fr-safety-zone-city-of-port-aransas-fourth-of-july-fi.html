BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone; City of Port Aransas Fourth of July Fireworks, Port Aransas, TX

Published: 2018-06-18 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard will enforce the safety zone for the City of Port Aransas' Fourth of July Fireworks Display on July 4, 2018, to provide for the safety of life on these navigable waterways during this event. Our regulation for marine events within the Eighth Coast Guard District identifies the regulated area for this event in Port Aransas, TX.
