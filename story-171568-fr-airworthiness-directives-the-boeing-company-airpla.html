BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; The Boeing Company Airplanes

Published: 2019-02-12 | Source: Federal Register

We are adopting a new airworthiness directive (AD) for all The Boeing Company Model 737-100, -200, -200C, -300, -400, and -500 series airplanes. This AD was prompted by a report of cracks in a certain body station (STA) frame web and doubler at fastener holes common to the stop fitting at a certain stringer.
