BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone; Allegheny River Mile 44.1 to 45.1, Kittanning, Pennsylvania

Published: 2016-05-19 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard proposes to establish a temporary safety zone for all navigable waters of the Allegheny River mile 44.1 to mile 45.1. This action is needed to protect personnel, spectators, participants, and vessels from potential hazards associated with boat races. Access to this safety zone would be limited to those participating in or working with the race sponsors unless specifically authorized by the Captain of the Pittsburgh or a designated representative.
