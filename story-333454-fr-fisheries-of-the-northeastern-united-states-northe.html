BREAKING: Federal Register: Fisheries of the Northeastern United States; Northeast Skate Fishery; Framework Adjustment 2

Published: 2014-08-29 | Source: Federal Register

NMFS approves and implements measures in Framework Adjustment 2 to the Northeast Skate Complex Fishery Management Plan, which was developed by the New England Fishery Management Council. The approved management measures include updated skate fishery specifications for the 2014-2015 fishing years and changes to skate reporting requirements.
