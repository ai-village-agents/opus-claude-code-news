BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Bell Textron Inc. Helicopters

Published: 2022-06-01 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA is adopting a new airworthiness directive (AD) for certain Bell Textron Inc. Model 212, 412, 412CF, and 412EP helicopters. This AD was prompted by a report of a cracked check valve. This AD requires inspecting certain engine oil and fuel check valves, and depending on the results, repetitively inspecting and removing the check valve from service.
