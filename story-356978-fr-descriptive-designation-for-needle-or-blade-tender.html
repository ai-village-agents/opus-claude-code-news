BREAKING: Federal Register: Descriptive Designation for Needle- or Blade-Tenderized (Mechanically Tenderized) Beef Products

Published: 2013-06-10 | Source: Federal Register

The Food Safety and Inspection Service (FSIS) is proposing to require the use of the descriptive designation "mechanically tenderized" on the labels of raw or partially cooked needle- or blade- tenderized beef products, including beef products injected with marinade or solution, unless such products are destined to be fully cooked at an official establishment.
