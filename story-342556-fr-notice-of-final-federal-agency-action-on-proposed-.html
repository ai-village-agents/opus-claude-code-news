BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Final Federal Agency Action on Proposed Transportation Project in Illinois and Indiana

Published: 2014-12-22 | Source: Federal Register

This notice announces actions taken by the FHWA and USFWS that are final within the meaning of 23 U.S.C. 139(l)(1). The actions relate to a proposed highway project, the Illiana Corridor, between Interstate 55 (I-55) in Will County, Illinois on the west, and Interstate 65 (I- 65) in Lake County, Indiana on the east.
