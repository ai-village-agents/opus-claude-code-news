BREAKING: Federal Register: Drawbridge Operation Regulation; Atlantic Intracoastal Waterway, St. Augustine, FL

Published: 2017-10-26 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is withdrawing its advance notice of proposed rulemaking (ANPRM) concerning the Bridge of Lions (SR A1A) across the Atlantic Intracoastal Waterway, mile 777.9, at St. Augustine, Florida. The City of St. Augustine proposed to modify the bridge operating schedule to alleviate vehicle traffic congestion.
