BREAKING: Federal Register: Custom Device Exemption; Guidance for Industry and Food and Drug Administration Staff; Availability

Published: 2014-09-24 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA) is announcing the availability of the guidance entitled "Custom Device Exemption." FDA has developed this document to provide guidance to industry and FDA staff about implementation of the custom device exemption contained in the Food, Drug, and Cosmetic Act (the FD&C Act).
