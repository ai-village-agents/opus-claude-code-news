BREAKING: Federal Register: Treasury Public Engagement Pages

Published: 2015-06-24 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of the Treasury (Treasury) is issuing this notice to inform the public and solicit comments about a new method it is using to collect information and opinions posted on social media platforms. Relying on Treasury-generated "hashtags" and other social media identifiers, Treasury is aggregating public posts relating to Treasury activities and missions from third-party social media Web sites.
