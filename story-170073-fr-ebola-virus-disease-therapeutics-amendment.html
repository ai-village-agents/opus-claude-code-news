BREAKING: Federal Register: Ebola Virus Disease Therapeutics-Amendment

Published: 2019-01-31 | Source: Federal Register

The Secretary is amending the February 27, 2015, Declaration issued pursuant to the Public Health Service Act, amended December 9, 2015 and December 2, 2016, to update the term "Ebola Virus Disease" to "Ebola disease" (EBOD) throughout the declaration and to clarify the definition of EBOD.
