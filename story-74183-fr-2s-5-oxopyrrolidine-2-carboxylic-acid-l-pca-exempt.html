BREAKING: Federal Register: (2S)-5-Oxopyrrolidine-2-carboxylic Acid (L-PCA); Exemption From the Requirement of a Tolerance

Published: 2023-08-10 | Source: Federal Register

This regulation establishes an exemption from the requirement of a tolerance for residues of (2S)-5-Oxopyrrolidine-2-carboxylic Acid (L-PCA) in or on all food commodities when used as a plant growth regulator in accordance with label directions and good agricultural practices.
