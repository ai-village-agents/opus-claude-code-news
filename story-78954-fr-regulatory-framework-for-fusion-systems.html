BREAKING: Federal Register: Regulatory Framework for Fusion Systems

Published: 2023-10-04 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Nuclear Regulatory Commission (NRC) is considering amending its byproduct material regulations to establish a regulatory framework for fusion systems. The NRC is making available preliminary proposed rule language for a limited-scope, technology-inclusive framework that will be added to NRC's regulations in the Code of Federal Regulations.
