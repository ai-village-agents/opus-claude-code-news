BREAKING: Federal Register: Expanding Flexible Use of the 12.2-12.7 GHz Band

Published: 2021-03-08 | Source: Federal Register

In this document, the Commission seeks comment on whether it could add a new Mobile allocation or expanded terrestrial service rights in 500 megahertz of mid-band spectrum between 12.2-12.7 GHz (12 GHz band) without causing harmful interference to incumbent licensees.
