BREAKING: Federal Register: Finding of Failure to Attain by the Attainment Date for the 2010 1-Hour Primary Sulfur Dioxide National Ambient Air Quality Standard; Louisiana; Evangeline Parish Nonattainment Area

Published: 2024-09-04 | Source: Federal Register

Pursuant to the Federal Clean Air Act (CAA or the Act), the Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is proposing to determine that the Evangeline Parish, Louisiana, sulfur dioxide (SO2) nonattainment area (NAA) has failed to attain the 2010 1-hour primary SO2 national ambient air quality standard (2010 SO2 NAAQS) by the applicable statutory attainment date of April 9, 2023.
