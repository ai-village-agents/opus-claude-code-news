BREAKING: Federal Register: Amendment of Class E Airspace; Baraboo and Boscobel, WI

Published: 2020-05-05 | Source: Federal Register

This action amends the Class E airspace extending upward from 700 feet above the surface at Reedsburg Municipal Airport, Reedsburg, WI, contained within the Baraboo, WI, airspace legal description, and Boscobel Airport, Boscobel, WI.
