BREAKING: Federal Register: Television Broadcasting Services; Seaford and Dover, Delaware

Published: 2014-05-14 | Source: Federal Register

The Commission, at the request of Western Pacific Broadcast, LLC ("Western Pacific"), amends the Post-Transition Table of DTV Allotments to delete channel 5 at Seaford and substitute channel 5 at Dover, Delaware and to modify WMDE(TV)'s construction permit to specify Dover as the station's community of license.
