BREAKING: Federal Register: Request for Information Regarding State Relief and Empowerment Waivers

Published: 2019-05-03 | Source: Federal Register

This request for information (RFI) solicits public comment on ideas for innovative programs and waiver concepts that states could consider in developing a 1332 waiver plan. The Department of the Treasury and the Centers for Medicare & Medicaid Services in the Department of Health and Human Services (collectively, the Departments) are seeking feedback and ideas on how states might take advantage of new flexibilities provided in recently published October 2018 guidance.
