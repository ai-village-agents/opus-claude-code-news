BREAKING: Federal Register: Solicitation of Nominations for Appointment to the Board of Scientific Counselors, Center for Preparedness and Response

Published: 2023-08-28 | Source: Federal Register

The Centers for Disease Control and Prevention (CDC), within the Department of Health and Human Services (HHS), is seeking nominations for membership on the Board of Scientific Counselors, Center for Preparedness and Response, (BSC, CPR).
