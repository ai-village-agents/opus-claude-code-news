BREAKING: Federal Register: Protection of Federal Property

Published: 2025-06-09 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Homeland Security (DHS) adopts this regulation to govern the protection of Federal property. DHS developed this regulation in consultation with the U.S. General Services Administration (GSA).
