BREAKING: Federal Register: Intent to Prepare an Environmental Impact Statement (EIS) for the Donlin Gold Project

Published: 2012-12-14 | Source: Federal Register

The Alaska District, U.S. Army Corps of Engineers (Corps) intends to prepare an Environmental Impact Statement (EIS) to identify and analyze the potential impacts associated with the proposed Donlin Gold Project, which would be an open pit, hardrock gold mine located 10 miles north of the village of Crooked Creek, Alaska.
