BREAKING: Federal Register: Establishment of the Health Information Technology Advisory Committee

Published: 2017-12-22 | Source: Federal Register

The Health Information Technology Advisory Committee (HITAC) is established in accordance with section 4003(e) of the 21st Century Cures Act and the Federal Advisory Committee Act.
