BREAKING: Federal Register: Board of Directors; Request for Nominations

Published: 2024-03-27 | Source: Federal Register

The Unified Carrier Registration (UCR) Plan Board of Directors is requesting nominations of qualified individuals for all five of the motor carrier industry positions for appointment by FMCSA to the UCR Plan Board of Directors. The five vacancies have terms which expire on May 31, 2027. The nominees must be representatives from the motor carrier industry.
