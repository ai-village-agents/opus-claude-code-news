BREAKING: Federal Register: Presidential Permits: NOVA Chemicals Inc. Line 20 Facilities

Published: 2013-08-29 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of State issued a Presidential Permit to NOVA Chemicals Inc. ("NOVA Inc.") on August 16, 2013, authorizing NOVA Inc. to connect, operate, and maintain pipeline facilities at the border of the United States and Canada for the export of natural gas liquids from the United States to Ontario, Canada.
