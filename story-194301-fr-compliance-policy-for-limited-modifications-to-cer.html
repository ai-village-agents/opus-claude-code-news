BREAKING: Federal Register: Compliance Policy for Limited Modifications to Certain Marketed Tobacco Products; Guidance for Industry; Availability

Published: 2019-11-26 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA, the Agency, or we) is announcing the availability of a final guidance for industry entitled "Compliance Policy for Limited Modifications to Certain Marketed Tobacco Products."
