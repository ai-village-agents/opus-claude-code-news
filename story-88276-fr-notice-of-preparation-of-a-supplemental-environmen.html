BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Preparation of a Supplemental Environmental Impact Statement for the Willow Master Development Plan

Published: 2022-02-07 | Source: Federal Register

The Bureau of Land Management (BLM) is preparing a Supplemental Environmental Impact Statement (SEIS) to address deficiencies identified by the U.S. District Court for Alaska in the 2020 Willow Master Development Plan (MDP)/Final Environmental Impact Statement (EIS) and Record of Decision (ROD) issued in October 2020, and to ensure compliance with applicable law.
