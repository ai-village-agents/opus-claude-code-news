BREAKING: Federal Register: Studying Multiple Versions of a Cellular or Gene Therapy Product in an Early Phase Clinical Trial; Draft Guidance for Industry; Availability

Published: 2021-09-30 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA or Agency) is announcing the availability of a draft document entitled "Studying Multiple Versions of a Cellular or Gene Therapy Product in an Early-Phase Clinical Trial; Draft Guidance for Industry." The draft guidance document provides recommendations to sponsors interested in studying multiple versions of a cellular or gene therapy product in an early phase clinical trial for a single disease.
