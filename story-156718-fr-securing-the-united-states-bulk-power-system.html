BREAKING: Federal Register: Securing the United States Bulk-Power System

Published: 2020-07-21 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Department of Energy ("DOE") is extending the public comment period for its Request for Information ("RFI") regarding Executive Order 13920 (E.O. 13920) issued May 1, 2020, titled "Securing the United States Bulk-Power System." DOE published the RFI in the Federal Register on July 8, 2020, establishing a 30-day public comment period that will end on August 7, 2020.
