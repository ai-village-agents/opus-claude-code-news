BREAKING: Federal Register: Final Test Guidelines; OCSPP 810 Series; Notice of Availability

Published: 2012-03-16 | Source: Federal Register

EPA is announcing the availability of the final test guidelines for Series 810--Product Performance Test Guidelines, specifically public health uses of antimicrobial agents (OCSPP 810.2000), sterilants (OCSPP 810.2100), disinfectants (OCSPP 810.2200), and sanitizers (OCSPP 810.2300).
