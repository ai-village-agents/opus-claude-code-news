BREAKING: Federal Register: Special Conditions: H4 Aerospace (UK) Ltd, The Boeing Company Model 757-200 Series Airplane; Seats With Non-Traditional, Large, Non-Metallic Panels

Published: 2025-09-23 | Source: Federal Register

These special conditions are issued for The Boeing Company (Boeing) Model 757-200 series airplane. This airplane, as modified by H4 Aerospace (UK) Ltd (H4 Aerospace), will have a novel or unusual design feature when compared to the state of technology envisioned in the airworthiness standards. This design feature is associated with seats that include non-traditional, large, non-metallic panels.
