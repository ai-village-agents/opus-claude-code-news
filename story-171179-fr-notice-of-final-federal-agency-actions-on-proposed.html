BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Final Federal Agency Actions on Proposed Highway in Utah

Published: 2019-02-08 | Source: Federal Register

This notice announces certain actions taken by UDOT that are final Federal agency actions within the meaning of 23 U.S.C. 139(l)(1). These actions relate to a proposed highway project on Interstate 15 (I- 15) Northbound, from Bangerter Highway to Interstate 215 (I-215), in the County of Salt Lake, State of Utah. Those actions grant licenses, permits and/or approvals for the project.
