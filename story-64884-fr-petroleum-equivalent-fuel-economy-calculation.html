BREAKING: Federal Register: Petroleum-Equivalent Fuel Economy Calculation

Published: 2023-04-11 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Department of Energy ("DOE") proposes to revise its regulations regarding procedures for calculating a value for the petroleum-equivalent fuel economy of electric vehicles (or "EVs") for use in the Corporate Average Fuel Economy (CAFE) program administered by the Department of Transportation (DOT).
