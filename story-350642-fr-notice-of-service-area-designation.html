BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Service Area Designation

Published: 2013-03-28 | Source: Federal Register

This notice is published to exercise the authority delegated by the Secretary of the Interior to the Assistant Secretary--Indian Affairs to provide notice, under the regulations, of the service area designation for the Pit River Tribe that is recognized and eligible to receive services from the Bureau of Indian Affairs (BIA).
