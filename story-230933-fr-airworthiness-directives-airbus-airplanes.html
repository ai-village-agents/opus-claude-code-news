BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Airbus Airplanes

Published: 2017-03-31 | Source: Federal Register

We are adopting a new airworthiness directive (AD) for all Airbus Model A330-243, -243F, -341, -342, and -343 airplanes. This AD requires an inspection to determine if affected hydraulic pressure tube assemblies are installed, and replacement with serviceable hydraulic pressure tube assemblies if necessary.
