BREAKING: Federal Register: Importation of Fresh Citrus From China Into the Continental United States

Published: 2014-08-28 | Source: Federal Register

We are proposing to amend the fruits and vegetables regulations to allow the importation into the continental United States of commercial consignments of five species of fresh citrus fruit from China.
