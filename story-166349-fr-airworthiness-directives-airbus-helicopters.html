BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Airbus Helicopters

Published: 2020-11-19 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA is adopting a new airworthiness directive (AD) for all Airbus Helicopters Model EC130B4 helicopters. This AD was prompted by reports of inflight detachment of the left-hand (LH) side cabin sliding doors and cases of impact damage on the main rotor blades, which were caused by degradation of the sliding door locking mechanism.
