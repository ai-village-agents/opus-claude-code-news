BREAKING: Federal Register: Privacy Act of 1974; System of Records

Published: 2019-07-18 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of the Army proposes to modify a System of Records Notice (SORN), Army Personnel Systems (APS), A0600-8-104 AHRC. This system of records is comprised of Human Resources (HR) information required to manage a Soldier's career from initial accession through separation, and for life for retirees.
