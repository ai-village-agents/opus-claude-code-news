BREAKING: Federal Register: Approval of Air Quality Implementation Plans; Missouri State Implementation Plan for the 2008 Lead Standard

Published: 2016-09-26 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is taking final action to approve a revision to the State Implementation Plan (SIP) for the State of Missouri. This final action will approve Missouri's SIP for the lead National Ambient Air Quality Standard (NAAQS) received by EPA on October 20, 2014. EPA proposed approval of this plan on February 29, 2016.
