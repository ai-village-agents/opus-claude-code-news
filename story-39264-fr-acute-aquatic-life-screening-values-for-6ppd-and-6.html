BREAKING: Federal Register: Acute Aquatic Life Screening Values for 6PPD and 6PPD-Quinone in Freshwater

Published: 2024-06-13 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is announcing the availability of Acute Freshwater Aquatic Life Screening Values for 6PPD (N-(1,3-Dimethylbutyl)-N'-phenyl-p-phenylenediamine) and its transformation product 6PPD-quinone (6PPD-q; N-(1,3-Dimethylbutyl)-N'- phenyl-p-phenylenediamine quinone).
