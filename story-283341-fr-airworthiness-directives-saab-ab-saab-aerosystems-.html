BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Saab AB, Saab Aerosystems Airplanes

Published: 2015-01-05 | Source: Federal Register

We are adopting a new airworthiness directive (AD) for all Saab AB, Saab Aerosystems Model 340B airplanes. This AD was prompted by a report that the elevator position quoted in an aircraft maintenance manual is incorrect and a report that the trunnion at the lower part of the control column was installed incorrectly.
