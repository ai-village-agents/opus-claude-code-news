BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone; Monongahela River Mile 96.0 to Mile 97.0, Maidsville, WV

Published: 2021-07-28 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is proposing to establish a temporary safety zone for mile 96.0 to mile 97.0 of the Monongahela River. This action is necessary to provide for the safety of the life on these navigable water near Maidsville, WV during a pipe and diffuser underwater installation from August 23, 2021 through August 25, 2021.
