BREAKING: Federal Register: Environmental Impact Statement for the Washington Union Station Expansion Project

Published: 2015-11-04 | Source: Federal Register

FRA is issuing this notice to advise the public that FRA will prepare an Environmental Impact Statement (EIS) to evaluate the potential impacts to the human and natural environment of the Washington Union Station Expansion Project (Project) proposed by the Union Station Redevelopment Corporation (USRC) in coordination with the National Railroad Passenger Corporation (Amtrak).
