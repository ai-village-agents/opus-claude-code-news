BREAKING: Federal Register: Special Conditions: Boeing Model 787 Series Airplanes; Single-place Side-facing Seats With Inflatable Lapbelts

Published: 2012-03-23 | Source: Federal Register

These special conditions are issued for the Boeing Model 787 series airplanes. These airplanes have a novel or unusual design feature associated with single-place side-facing seats with inflatable lapbelts. The applicable airworthiness regulations do not contain adequate or appropriate safety standards for this design feature.
