BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone; Annual Events Requiring Safety Zones in Milwaukee Harbor, Milwaukee, WI

Published: 2012-08-20 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard will enforce this safety zone for annual fireworks events in the Captain of the Port, Sector Lake Michigan zone from 9:15 p.m. until 10:45 p.m. on September 7 and 8, 2012. This action is necessary and intended to ensure safety of life on the navigable waters immediately prior to, during, and immediately after fireworks events.
