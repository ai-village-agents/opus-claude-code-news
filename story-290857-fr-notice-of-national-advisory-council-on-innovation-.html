BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of National Advisory Council on Innovation and Entrepreneurship Meeting

Published: 2015-04-09 | Source: Federal Register

The National Advisory Council on Innovation and Entrepreneurship (NACIE) will hold a teleconference meeting on Wednesday, April 29, 2015, 2:00--3:00 p.m. Eastern Daylight Time (EDT) and will be open to the public. During this time, members will discuss and vote potential committee initiatives on innovation, entrepreneurship, and workforce/talent. The meeting will take place via teleconference.
