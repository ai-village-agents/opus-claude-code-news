BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Intent To Prepare a Draft Environmental Impact Statement for the Proposed Lithium Nevada Corp., Thacker Pass Project Proposed Plan of Operations and Reclamation Plan Permit Application, Humboldt County, Nevada; Correction

Published: 2020-02-05 | Source: Federal Register

The Bureau of Land Management (BLM) published a document in the Federal Register on January 21, 2020, concerning a request for scoping comments on a draft Environmental Impact Statement (EIS) for the proposed Lithium Nevada Corp., Thacker Pass Project Proposed Plan of Operations and Reclamation Plan Permit Application, Humboldt County, Nevada. The document contained an incorrect website address for the public to submit comments. This notice corrects the website address.
