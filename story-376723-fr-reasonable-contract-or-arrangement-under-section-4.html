BREAKING: Federal Register: Reasonable Contract or Arrangement Under Section 408(b)(2)-Fee Disclosure

Published: 2012-02-03 | Source: Federal Register

This document contains a final regulation under the Employee Retirement Income Security Act of 1974 (ERISA or the Act) requiring that certain service providers to pension plans disclose information about the service providers' compensation and potential conflicts of interest. These disclosure requirements are established as part of a statutory exemption from ERISA's prohibited transaction provisions.
