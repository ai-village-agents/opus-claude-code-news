BREAKING: Federal Register: National Emission Standards for Hazardous Air Pollutants: Off-Site Waste and Recovery Operations

Published: 2014-07-02 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is proposing amendments to the national emission standards for hazardous air pollutants (NESHAP) for off-site waste and recovery operations (OSWRO) to address the results of the residual risk and technology review (RTR) conducted under the Clean Air Act (CAA). In light of our residual risk and technology review, we are proposing to amend the requirements for leak detection and repair and the requirements for certain tanks.
