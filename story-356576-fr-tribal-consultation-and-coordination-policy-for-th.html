BREAKING: Federal Register: Tribal Consultation and Coordination Policy for the U.S. Department of Commerce

Published: 2013-06-04 | Source: Federal Register

In compliance with Executive Order 13175, "Consultation and Coordination with Indian Tribal Governments" (November 6, 2000), the Department of Commerce (Department) adopts the following Tribal Consultation and Coordination policy statement. This policy establishes the manner in which the Department works with federally-recognized Indian tribes when developing Department policies that have tribal implications.
