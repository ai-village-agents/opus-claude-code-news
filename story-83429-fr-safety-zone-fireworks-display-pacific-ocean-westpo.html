BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone; Fireworks Display, Pacific Ocean, Westport, WA

Published: 2023-12-05 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is proposing to establish a temporary safety zone for certain waters of the Pacific Ocean. This action is necessary to provide for the safety of life on these navigable waters near Westport, WA, during a fireworks display December 31, 2023, to January 1, 2024. This proposed rulemaking would prohibit persons and vessels from entering the safety zone unless authorized by the Captain of the Port Sector Columbia River or a designated representative.
