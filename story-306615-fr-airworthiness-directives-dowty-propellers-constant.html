BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Dowty Propellers Constant Speed Propellers

Published: 2015-10-13 | Source: Federal Register

We propose to adopt a new airworthiness directive (AD) for certain Dowty Propellers R352/6-123-F/1, R352/6-123-F/2, and R410/6- 123-F/35 model propellers. This proposed AD was prompted by reports of dowel hole cracks in the face of the rear hub half. This proposed AD would require a records review to determine repair status and marking the affected propeller hubs as required.
