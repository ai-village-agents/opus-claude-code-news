BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zones, Hurricane Lane Port Closures for Hawaiian Islands

Published: 2018-08-28 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is establishing 9 temporary safety zones encompassing Hawaii's commercial harbors to include Nawiliwili and Port Allen, Kauai; Barber's Point and Honolulu Harbor, Oahu; Kaunakakai, Molokai; Kaumalapau, Lanai; Kahului, Maui; and Kawaihae and Hilo on the Island of Hawaii.
