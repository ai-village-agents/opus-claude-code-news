BREAKING: Federal Register: Policy Letters: Guidance for the Use of Liquefied Natural Gas as a Marine Fuel

Published: 2015-02-25 | Source: Federal Register

This notice announces the availability of the finalized Coast Guard policy letters, including explanations of changes made to the policy letters and enclosures based on the public comments received.
