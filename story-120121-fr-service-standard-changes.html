BREAKING: Federal Register: Service Standard Changes

Published: 2021-03-30 | Source: Federal Register

The Commission notices a filing by the Postal Service of its intent to conduct a pre-filing conference regarding its proposed changes to the service standards for First-Class Mail and end-to-end Periodicals. This document informs the public of this proceeding and the pre-filing conference, and takes other administrative steps.
