BREAKING: Federal Register: DISH Network Corporation, Petition for Waiver and Request for Extension of Time

Published: 2014-02-24 | Source: Federal Register

In this document, the Federal Communications Commission (Commission), Wireless Telecommunications Bureau provides notice of a Memorandum Opinion and Order in which it granted waivers to DISH Network Corporation, subject to certain conditions, in response to a petition filed by DISH to provide DISH with flexibility to elect whether to use 20 megahertz of Advanced Wireless Services-4 (AWS-4) spectrum at 2000-2020 MHz (the Lower AWS-4 Band) for uplink or downlink operations.
