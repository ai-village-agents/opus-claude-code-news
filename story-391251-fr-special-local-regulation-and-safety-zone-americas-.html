BREAKING: Federal Register: Special Local Regulation and Safety Zone; America's Cup Sailing Events, San Francisco, CA

Published: 2012-07-17 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is establishing a special local regulation and a safety zone for sailing regattas that may be conducted on the waters of San Francisco Bay adjacent to the City of San Francisco waterfront in the vicinity of the Golden Gate Bridge and Alcatraz Island.
