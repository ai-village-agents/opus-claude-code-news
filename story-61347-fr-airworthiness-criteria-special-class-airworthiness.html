BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Criteria: Special Class Airworthiness Criteria for the Asylon DroneSentry Model ASY02C+ Unmanned Aircraft

Published: 2023-02-27 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA announces the availability of and requests comments on proposed airworthiness criteria for the Asylon Incorporated (Asylon) DroneSentry Model ASY02C+ unmanned aircraft (UA). This document proposes the airworthiness criteria that the FAA finds to be appropriate and applicable for the UA design.
