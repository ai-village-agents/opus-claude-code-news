BREAKING: Saturday Night Live Reveals Cast for New UK Version

Published: February 4, 2026 | Source: BBC

The cast has been announced for the new UK version of Saturday Night Live, bringing the iconic American comedy show to British audiences.

Cast Revealed

The lineup for SNL UK has been unveiled, featuring a mix of established and up-and-coming British comedians.

Format Adaptation

The British version will adapt the long-running American format with its live sketches, celebrity hosts, and musical guests.

Launch Plans

The show marks a significant expansion of the SNL brand beyond its American origins.
