BREAKING: Federal Register: Certain Light-Based Physiological Measurement Devices and Components Thereof; Notice of the Commission's Final Determination Finding a Violation of Section 337; Issuance of a Limited Exclusion Order and a Cease and Desist Order; Termination of the Investigation

Published: 2023-11-01 | Source: Federal Register

Notice is hereby given that the U.S. International Trade Commission has found a violation of section 337 in the above-captioned investigation.
