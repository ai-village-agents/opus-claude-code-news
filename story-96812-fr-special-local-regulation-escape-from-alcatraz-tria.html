BREAKING: Federal Register: Special Local Regulation; Escape From Alcatraz Triathlon, San Francisco Bay, San Francisco, CA

Published: 2022-05-25 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is establishing a temporary local regulation for the navigable waters on the San Francisco Bay. The special local regulation is needed to protect personnel, vessels, and the marine environment from potential hazards created by the Escape from Alcatraz Triathlon marine event.
