BREAKING: Federal Register: U.S. Coral Reef Task Force Public Meeting and Public Comment

Published: 2012-02-09 | Source: Federal Register

Notice is hereby given of a public meeting of the U.S. Coral Reef Task Force. The meeting will be held in Washington, DC This meeting, the 27th bi-annual meeting of the U.S. Coral Reef Task Force, provides a forum for coordinated planning and action among federal agencies, state and territorial governments, and nongovernmental partners.
