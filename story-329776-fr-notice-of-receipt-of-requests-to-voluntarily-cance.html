BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Receipt of Requests to Voluntarily Cancel Certain Pesticide Registrations; Correction

Published: 2014-07-16 | Source: Federal Register

EPA issued a notice in the Federal Register of June 11, 2014, concerning receipt of requests to voluntarily cancel certain pesticide registrations. In this notice, EPA inadvertently listed the pesticide products AC 801, 757 Miticide-Insecticide (EPA Reg. No. 071711-00022) and AC 801, 757 3EC Miticide-Insecticide (EPA Reg. No. 071711-00023).
