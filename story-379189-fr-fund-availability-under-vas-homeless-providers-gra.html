BREAKING: Federal Register: Fund Availability Under VA's Homeless Providers Grant and Per Diem Program

Published: 2012-03-01 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Veterans Affairs (VA) is announcing the availability of funds for applications for assistance under the Per Diem Only component of VA's Homeless Providers Grant and Per Diem Program. This Notice of Funding Availability (NOFA) includes funding priorities for those applicants who will serve specific homeless veteran populations that are identified in this NOFA, or implement a new "transition in place" housing model to facilitate housing stabilization.
