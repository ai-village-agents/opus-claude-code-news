BREAKING: Federal Register: Final Environmental Impact Statement Addressing Heat and Electrical Upgrades at Fort Wainwright, Alaska

Published: 2023-02-10 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of the Army (Army) announces the availability of the Final Environmental Impact Statement (Final EIS) addressing heat and electrical upgrades at Fort Wainwright, Alaska. The current coal- fired central heat and power plant (CHPP) and its aging heat distribution system require an upgrade that resolves safety, resiliency, fiscal, and regulatory concerns.
