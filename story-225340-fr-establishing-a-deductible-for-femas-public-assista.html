BREAKING: Federal Register: Establishing a Deductible for FEMA's Public Assistance Program

Published: 2017-01-12 | Source: Federal Register

The Federal Emergency Management Agency (FEMA) is considering implementing a Public Assistance deductible that would condition States' receipt of FEMA reimbursement for the repair and replacement of public infrastructure damaged by a disaster event. The primary intent of the deductible concept is to incentivize greater State resilience to future disasters, thereby reducing future disaster costs nationally.
