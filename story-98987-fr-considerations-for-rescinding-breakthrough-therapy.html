BREAKING: Federal Register: Considerations for Rescinding Breakthrough Therapy Designation; Draft Guidance for Industry; Availability

Published: 2022-06-24 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA or Agency) is announcing the availability of a draft guidance for industry entitled "Considerations for Rescinding Breakthrough Therapy Designation." This guidance explains how, during its evaluation of a drug development program, FDA may consider whether to rescind a breakthrough therapy designation (BTD) that has been granted.
