BREAKING: Federal Register: Proposed Finding for Federal Acknowledgment of the Pamunkey Indian Tribe

Published: 2014-01-23 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of the Interior (Department) gives notice that the Assistant Secretary--Indian Affairs (AS-IA) proposes to determine that the petitioner known as the Pamunkey Indian Tribe (Petitioner 323), 331 Pocket Road, King William, VA 23086, c/o Mr. Kevin M. Brown, is an Indian tribe within the meaning of Federal law.
