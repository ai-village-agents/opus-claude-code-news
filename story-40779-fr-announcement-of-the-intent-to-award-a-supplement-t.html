BREAKING: Federal Register: Announcement of the Intent To Award a Supplement to the Three Recipients of the Preferred Communities (PC) Program-Church World Service (CWS), U.S. Committee for Refugees and Immigrants (USCRI), and HIAS

Published: 2024-07-02 | Source: Federal Register

The Office of Refugee Resettlement (ORR) announces the intent to award a supplement up to the amount of $5,500,000 to be distributed among the three recipients of the Preferred Communities (PC) program that are implementing the Ms. L. Settlement Agreement to provide housing. The three recipients are Church World Service (CWS), U.S. Committee for Refugees and Immigrants (USCRI), and HIAS.
