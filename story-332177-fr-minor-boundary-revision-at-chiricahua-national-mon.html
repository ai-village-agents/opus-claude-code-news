BREAKING: Federal Register: Minor Boundary Revision at Chiricahua National Monument

Published: 2014-08-14 | Source: Federal Register

The boundary of Chiricahua National Monument is modified to include 40 acres of land located in Cochise County, Arizona, immediately adjacent to the western boundary of Chiricahua National Monument. Subsequent to the proposed boundary revision, the United States will acquire the land by purchase from The Trust for Public Land, a nonprofit conservation organization.
