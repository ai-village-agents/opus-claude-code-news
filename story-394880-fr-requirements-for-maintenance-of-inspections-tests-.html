BREAKING: Federal Register: Requirements for Maintenance of Inspections, Tests, Analyses, and Acceptance Criteria

Published: 2012-08-28 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Nuclear Regulatory Commission (NRC or the Commission) is amending its regulations related to verification of nuclear power plant construction activities through inspections, tests, analyses, and acceptance criteria (ITAAC) under a combined license, and issuing a revision to Regulatory Guide (RG) 1.215, "Guidance for ITAAC Closure Under 10 CFR [Title 10 of the Code of Federal Regulations] Part 52."
