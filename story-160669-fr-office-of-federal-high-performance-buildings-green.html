BREAKING: Federal Register: Office of Federal High-Performance Buildings; Green Building Advisory Committee; Request for Membership Nominations

Published: 2020-09-09 | Source: Federal Register

The Green Building Advisory Committee provides advice to GSA as a mandatory federal advisory committee, as specified in the Energy Independence and Security Act of 2007 (EISA) and in accordance with the provisions of the Federal Advisory Committee Act (FACA). This notice invites qualified candidates to apply for appointment to a voluntary position on the Committee. This is a competitive process for a limited number of positions.
