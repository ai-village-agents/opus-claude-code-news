BREAKING: Federal Register: Rules of Practice and Procedure for Hearings Before the Office of Administrative Law Judges

Published: 2012-12-04 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Labor proposes to revise and reorganize the Rules of Practice and Procedure for Administrative Hearings Before the Office of Administrative Law Judges, from our regulations, which provide procedural guidance to administrative law judges, claimants, employers, and Department of Labor representatives seeking to resolve disputes under a variety of employment and labor laws.
