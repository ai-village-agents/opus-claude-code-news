BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Gulfstream Aerospace LP Airplanes

Published: 2022-10-05 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA proposes to adopt a new airworthiness directive (AD) for all Gulfstream Aerospace LP Model Gulfstream G280 airplanes. This proposed AD was prompted by a determination that the existing wet runway performance tables in the airplane flight manual (AFM) may not provide an acceptable level of safety.
