BREAKING: Federal Register: Proposed Flood Elevation Determinations for the Unincorporated Areas of Highlands County, FL

Published: 2012-09-21 | Source: Federal Register

The Federal Emergency Management Agency (FEMA) is withdrawing its proposed rule concerning proposed flood elevation determinations for the Unincorporated Areas of Highlands County, Florida.
