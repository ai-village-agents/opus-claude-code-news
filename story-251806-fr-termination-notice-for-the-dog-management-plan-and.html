BREAKING: Federal Register: Termination Notice for the Dog Management Plan and Environmental Impact Statement, Golden Gate National Recreation Area, California

Published: 2017-12-27 | Source: Federal Register

The National Park Service (NPS) has cancelled its planning process for the Golden Gate National Recreation Area dog management plan, and no longer intends to issue a Record of Decision.
