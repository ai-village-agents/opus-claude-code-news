BREAKING: Federal Register: Position Limits for Derivatives

Published: 2021-01-14 | Source: Federal Register

The Commodity Futures Trading Commission ("Commission" or "CFTC") is adopting amendments in this final rule ("Final Rule") to conform regulations concerning speculative position limits to the relevant Wall Street Transparency and Accountability Act of 2010 ("Dodd-Frank Act") amendments to the Commodity Exchange Act ("CEA").
