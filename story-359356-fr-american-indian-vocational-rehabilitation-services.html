BREAKING: Federal Register: American Indian Vocational Rehabilitation Services Program; Notice of Tribal Consultation and Request for Comments

Published: 2013-07-05 | Source: Federal Register

The Department seeks input from tribal officials, tribal governments, tribal organizations, and affected tribal members on the Department's interpretation of the term "reservation" in section 121(c) of the Rehabilitation Act of 1973, as amended (Act). The Department is also exploring additional measures of consultation, including regional face-to-face meetings to be held in August and September of 2013.
