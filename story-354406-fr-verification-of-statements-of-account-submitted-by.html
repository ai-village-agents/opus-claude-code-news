BREAKING: Federal Register: Verification of Statements of Account Submitted by Cable Operators and Satellite Carriers

Published: 2013-05-09 | Source: Federal Register

On June 14, 2012, the United States Copyright Office published a notice of proposed rulemaking and request for comments concerning a new regulation that will allow copyright owners to audit the Statements of Account and royalty fees that cable operators and satellite carriers deposit with the Copyright Office for secondary transmissions of broadcast programming made pursuant to statutory licenses.
