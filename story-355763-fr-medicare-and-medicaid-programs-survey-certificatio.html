BREAKING: Federal Register: Medicare and Medicaid Programs; Survey, Certification and Enforcement Procedures; Extension of Comment Period

Published: 2013-05-24 | Source: Federal Register

This notice extends the comment period for the Survey, Certification and Enforcement Procedures proposed rule, which was published in the April 5, 2013 Federal Register (78 FR 20564 through 20581). In the proposed rule, we proposed to revise the survey, certification, and enforcement procedures related to CMS oversight of national accreditation organizations (AOs).
