BREAKING: Federal Register: Energy Conservation Program: Decision and Order Granting a Waiver to LG Electronics USA, Inc. From the Department of Energy Room Air Conditioner Test Procedure

Published: 2019-05-08 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Department of Energy ("DOE") gives notice of a Decision and Order (Case Number 2018-003) that grants LG Electronics USA, Inc. ("LG") a waiver from specified portions of the DOE test procedure for determining the energy efficiency of specified room air conditioners. Under the Decision and Order, LG is required to test and rate the specified basic models of its room air conditioners in accordance with the alternate test procedure specified in the Decision and Order.
