BREAKING: Federal Register: Request for Nominations for Voting Members on a Public Advisory Committee; Blood Products Advisory Committee

Published: 2021-03-26 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA) is requesting nominations for voting members to serve on the Blood Products Advisory Committee (the Committee) in the Center for Biologics Evaluation and Research. Nominations will be accepted for upcoming vacancies effective with this notice.
