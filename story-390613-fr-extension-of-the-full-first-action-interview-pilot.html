BREAKING: Federal Register: Extension of the Full First Action Interview Pilot Program and Request for Comments

Published: 2012-07-09 | Source: Federal Register

The United States Patent and Trademark Office (Office) is extending the First Action Interview (FAI) Pilot Program while completing a comprehensive review of the program, deciding what, if any, adjustments should be made to the program, and whether the program should be extended further or made permanent.
