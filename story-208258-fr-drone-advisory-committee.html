BREAKING: Federal Register: Drone Advisory Committee

Published: 2018-05-31 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA is issuing this notice to advise the public of the establishment of the DAC for 2 years. The Federal Aviation Administration (FAA) is establishing the DAC under agency authority in accordance with the provisions of the Federal Advisory Committee Act (FACA). The DAC is necessary and in the public interest.
