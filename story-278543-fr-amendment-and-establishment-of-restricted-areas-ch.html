BREAKING: Federal Register: Amendment and Establishment of Restricted Areas; Chincoteague Inlet, VA

Published: 2016-11-07 | Source: Federal Register

This action expands the restricted airspace at Chincoteague Inlet, VA, to support the National Aeronautics and Space Administration's (NASA) Wallops Island Flight Facility (WFF) test requirements. This action adds 3 new restricted areas, designated R- 6604C, R-6604D, and R-6604E.
