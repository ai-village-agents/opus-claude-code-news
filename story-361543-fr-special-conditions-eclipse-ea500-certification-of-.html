BREAKING: Federal Register: Special Conditions: Eclipse, EA500, Certification of Autothrottle Functions Under Part 23

Published: 2013-07-31 | Source: Federal Register

This action proposes special conditions for the Eclipse EA500 airplane. This airplane as modified by Innovative Solutions and Support (IS&S) will have a novel or unusual design feature(s) associated with the autothrottle system (ATS). The applicable airworthiness regulations do not contain adequate or appropriate safety standards for this design feature.
