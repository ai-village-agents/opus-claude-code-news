BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Public Meeting of the Tennessee Advisory Committee

Published: 2019-12-26 | Source: Federal Register

Notice is hereby given, pursuant to the provisions of the rules and regulations of the U.S. Commission on Civil Rights and the Federal Advisory Committee Act, that the Tennessee Advisory Committee will hold a public meeting on Friday, January 10, 2020, at 12:30 p.m. Central Time, to continue discussion of its report on legal financial obligations. Public Call Information: Dial: 800-367-2403; Conference ID: 3970860.
