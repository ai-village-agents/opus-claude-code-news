BREAKING: Federal Register: Special Local Regulation; Gulf of Mexico, Fort Myers Beach, FL

Published: 2019-10-07 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is establishing a temporary special local regulation (SLR) for certain waters of the Gulf of Mexico adjacent to Fort Myers Beach, FL. This action is necessary to provide for the safety of race participants, participant vessels, spectators, and the general public on these navigable waters near Fort Myers Beach during the Roar Offshore boat race.
