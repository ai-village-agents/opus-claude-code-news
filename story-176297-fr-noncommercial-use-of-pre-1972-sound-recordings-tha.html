BREAKING: Federal Register: Noncommercial Use of Pre-1972 Sound Recordings That Are Not Being Commercially Exploited

Published: 2019-04-09 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Copyright Office is issuing a final rule regarding the Classics Protection and Access Act, title II of the Orrin G. Hatch- Bob Goodlatte Music Modernization Act. In connection with the establishment of federal remedies for unauthorized uses of sound recordings fixed before February 15, 1972 ("Pre-1972 Sound Recordings"), Congress established an exception for certain noncommercial uses of Pre-1972 Sound Recordings that are not being commercially exploited.
