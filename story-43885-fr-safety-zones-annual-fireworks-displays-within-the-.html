BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zones; Annual Fireworks Displays Within the Puget Sound

Published: 2024-08-09 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard will enforce regulations for the Medina Days safety zone in Medina Beach Park on Lake Washington, Seattle, Washington to provide for the safety of life on navigable waters during an annual fireworks display. This safety zone will consist of all navigable waters within a 450-yard radius surrounding the event's launch site.
