BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Zodiac Seats California LLC Seating Systems

Published: 2016-06-27 | Source: Federal Register

This document announces the reopening of the comment period for the above-referenced NPRM, which proposed the adoption of a new airworthiness directive (AD) that would apply to certain Zodiac Seats California LLC seating systems. The NPRM proposed to require removing affected seating systems.
