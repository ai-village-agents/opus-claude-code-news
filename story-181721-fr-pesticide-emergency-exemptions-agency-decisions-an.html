BREAKING: Federal Register: Pesticide Emergency Exemptions; Agency Decisions and State and Federal Agency Crisis Declarations

Published: 2019-06-17 | Source: Federal Register

EPA has granted emergency exemptions under the Federal Insecticide, Fungicide, and Rodenticide Act (FIFRA) for use of pesticides as listed in this notice. The exemptions were granted during the period October 1, 2018 to March 30, 2019, except for one granted in September 2018, to control unforeseen pest outbreaks.
