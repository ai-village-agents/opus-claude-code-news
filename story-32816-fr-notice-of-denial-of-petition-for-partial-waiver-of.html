BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Denial of Petition for Partial Waiver of 2023 Cellulosic Biofuel Standard Under the Renewable Fuel Standard Program

Published: 2024-03-26 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is providing notice of its final action entitled Denial of AFPM Petition for Partial Waiver of 2023 Cellulosic Biofuel Standard ("AFPM Petition Denial Action"), in which EPA denied a petition from the American Fuel & Petrochemical Manufacturers (AFPM) for a partial waiver of the 2023 cellulosic biofuel standard under the Renewable Fuel Standard (RFS) program.
