BREAKING: Federal Register: Approval and Promulgation of Air Quality Implementation Plans; Michigan; PSD Regulations

Published: 2012-06-19 | Source: Federal Register

EPA is proposing to approve revisions to the Prevention of Significant Deterioration (PSD) construction permit program of State of Michigan's State Implementation Plan (SIP) to meet the Clean Air Act (CAA) requirements for PSD in Class I Areas attaining the National Ambient Air Quality Standards.
