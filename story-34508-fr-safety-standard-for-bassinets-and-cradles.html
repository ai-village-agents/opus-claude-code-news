BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Standard for Bassinets and Cradles

Published: 2024-04-16 | Source: Federal Register

In 2013, the United States Consumer Product Safety Commission (Commission or CPSC) published a safety standard for bassinets and cradles (bassinets/cradles). By statute, after promulgating a mandatory rule, the Commission must periodically review and revise rules for durable infant or toddler products to ensure that they provide the highest level of safety for such products that is feasible.
