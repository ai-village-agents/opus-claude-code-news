BREAKING: Federal Register: Class Waiver of the Nonmanufacturer Rule

Published: 2020-01-23 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Small Business Administration (SBA) is considering granting a request for a class waiver of the Nonmanufacturer Rule (NMR) for leather holsters (M18 System) and accessories. According to the request, no small business manufacturers can manufacture and supply a specific proprietary holster system to the Federal government.
