BREAKING: Federal Register: Greater-Than-Class-C and Transuranic Waste

Published: 2019-09-13 | Source: Federal Register

On July 22, 2019, the U.S. Nuclear Regulatory Commission (NRC) solicited comments on a draft regulatory basis to support the development of a rulemaking for the disposal of certain types of greater-than-Class-C waste in a low-level radioactive waste land disposal facility. The public comment period was originally scheduled to close on September 20, 2019.
