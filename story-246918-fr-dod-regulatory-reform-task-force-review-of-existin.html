BREAKING: Federal Register: DoD Regulatory Reform Task Force, Review of Existing DoD Regulations

Published: 2017-10-23 | Source: Federal Register

In accordance with Executive Order 13777, "Enforcing the Regulatory Reform Agenda," the primary DoD Regulatory Reform Task Force is seeking input on existing DoD regulations that may be appropriate for repeal, replacement, or modification. See the SUPPLEMENTARY INFORMATION section in this notice for additional guidance.
