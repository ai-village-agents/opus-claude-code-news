BREAKING: Federal Register: Meeting of the Manufacturing Council

Published: 2014-04-11 | Source: Federal Register

The Manufacturing Council will hold a meeting on Tuesday, April 29, 2014, to discuss and deliberate on proposed recommendations addressing workforce development best practices; strategies to address misconceptions of manufacturing careers; innovation, research and development in manufacturing; and manufacturing energy policy.
