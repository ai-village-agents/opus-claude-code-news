BREAKING: Federal Register: Group Registration of Two-Dimensional Artwork

Published: 2025-12-19 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Copyright Office is creating a new group registration option for two-dimensional artwork. This option will allow applicants to register up to twenty works published within one calendar year by submitting a single online application with a digital deposit copy of each work. The Office will examine each work to determine if it contains a sufficient amount of creative pictorial or graphic authorship.
