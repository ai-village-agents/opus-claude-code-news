BREAKING: Federal Register: Pacific Halibut Fisheries; Catch Sharing Plan

Published: 2022-07-26 | Source: Federal Register

Under the authority of the Northern Pacific Halibut Act of 1982, this action would create a permitting system for the Pacific halibut commercial and recreational charter halibut fisheries in International Pacific Halibut Commission (IPHC) Regulatory Area 2A off of Washington, Oregon, and California.
