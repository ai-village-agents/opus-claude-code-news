BREAKING: Federal Register: Iron Oxide (Fe₃O₄) in Pesticide Formulations Applied to Animals; Tolerance Exemption

Published: 2022-12-22 | Source: Federal Register

This regulation establishes an exemption from the requirement of a tolerance for residues of iron oxide (Fe3O4) (CAS Reg. No. 1317-61-9) when used as an inert ingredient (colorant) in pesticide formulations applied to animals.
