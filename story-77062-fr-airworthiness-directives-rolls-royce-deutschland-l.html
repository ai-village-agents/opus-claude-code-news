BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Rolls-Royce Deutschland Ltd & Co KG Engines

Published: 2023-09-15 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA proposes to adopt a new airworthiness directive (AD) for all Rolls-Royce Deutschland Ltd & Co KG (RRD) Model RB211-Trent 800 engines. This proposed AD was prompted by reports of cracks on certain intermediate-pressure compressor (IPC) rotor shaft balance lands.
