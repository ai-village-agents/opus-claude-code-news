BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Sikorsky Aircraft Corporation

Published: 2016-03-11 | Source: Federal Register

We are adopting a new airworthiness directive (AD) for Sikorsky Aircraft Corporation (Sikorsky) Model S-92A helicopters. This AD requires certain inspections of the main rotor and tail rotor control pushrods (pushrods). This AD is prompted by a Sikorsky investigation that indicated that some pushrods may have incorrectly installed locking mechanisms.
