BREAKING: Federal Register: Hours of Service of Drivers: R.J. Corman Railroad Services, Cranemasters, Inc., and National Railroad Construction and Maintenance Association, Inc.

Published: 2019-03-08 | Source: Federal Register

Corman Railroad Services, Cranemasters, Inc., ("Companies") and the National Railroad Construction and Maintenance Association, Inc. requesting a limited exemption from the regulatory hours-of-service (HOS) maximum driving time requirements for drivers of property- carrying vehicles.
