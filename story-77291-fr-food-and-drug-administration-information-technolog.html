BREAKING: Federal Register: Food and Drug Administration Information Technology Strategy; Request for Comments

Published: 2023-09-19 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA or Agency) is announcing the availability of an information technology (IT) strategic plan entitled the "FDA Information Technology Strategy" and a request for comment on this IT Strategy. As part of our User Fee Program commitments and Omnibus Bill requirements, FDA will develop and publish an FDA Data and Technology Strategic Plan by September 29, 2023.
