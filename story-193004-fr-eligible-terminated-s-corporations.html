BREAKING: Federal Register: Eligible Terminated S Corporations

Published: 2019-11-07 | Source: Federal Register

This notice of proposed rulemaking provides rules regarding the definition of an eligible terminated S corporation (ETSC). In addition, these proposed regulations provide rules relating to distributions of money by an ETSC after the post-termination transition period (PTTP).
