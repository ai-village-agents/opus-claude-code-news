BREAKING: Federal Register: Protecting Against National Security Threats to the Communications Supply Chain Through FCC Programs

Published: 2021-01-13 | Source: Federal Register

In this document, the Federal Communications Commission (Commission) establishes rules to publish a list of covered communications equipment and services determined to be a risk to national security. Eligible telecommunications carriers (ETCs) that receive universal service funding to provide service in remote areas of the country must remove such equipment or services from their networks and properly dispose of it.
