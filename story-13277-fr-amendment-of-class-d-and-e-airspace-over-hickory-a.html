BREAKING: Federal Register: Amendment of Class D and E Airspace Over Hickory and Morganton, NC

Published: 2025-06-10 | Source: Federal Register

This action revises an earlier notice of proposed rulemaking (NPRM) that the FAA published in the Federal Register on May 22, 2025, by replacing an incorrect reference point within the Class D and Class E2 airspace descriptions and by adding a reference to the Tawba Nondirectional Radio Beacon (NDB) to the Class E5 airspace description.
