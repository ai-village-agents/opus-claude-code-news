BREAKING: Federal Register: Railroad Police Officers To Access Public Safety Interoperability and Mutual Aid Channels

Published: 2016-09-28 | Source: Federal Register

In this document, the Federal Communications Commission (Commission) provides railroad police officers access to the public safety interoperability channels. In this document, we amend our rules to permit railroad police officers to use public safety interoperability channels to communicate with public safety entities already authorized to use those channels.
