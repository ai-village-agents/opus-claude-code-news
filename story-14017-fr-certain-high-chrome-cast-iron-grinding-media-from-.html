BREAKING: Federal Register: Certain High Chrome Cast Iron Grinding Media From India: Amended Final Affirmative Antidumping Duty Determination and Antidumping Duty Order; Countervailing Duty Order

Published: 2025-06-18 | Source: Federal Register

Based on affirmative final determinations by the U.S. Department of Commerce (Commerce) and the U.S. International Trade Commission (ITC), Commerce is issuing antidumping duty (AD) and countervailing duty (CVD) orders on certain high chrome cast iron grinding media (grinding media) from India. In addition, Commerce is amending its final determination in the less-than-fair-value (LTFV) investigation of certain high chrome cast iron grinding media from India to correct a ministerial error.
