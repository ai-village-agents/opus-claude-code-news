BREAKING: Federal Register: Privacy Act of 1974; Establishment of a New System of Records

Published: 2015-12-07 | Source: Federal Register

In accordance with the Privacy Act of 1974 (5 U.S.C. 552a), the USIBWC is issuing public notice of its intent to modify an existing Privacy Act system of records notice, DOI-85, "Payroll, Attendance, Retirement, and Leave Records."
