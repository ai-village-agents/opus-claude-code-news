BREAKING: Federal Register: Continuing Education for Licensed Customs Brokers

Published: 2021-09-10 | Source: Federal Register

This document proposes to amend the U.S. Customs and Border Protection (CBP) regulations to require continuing education for individual customs broker license holders (individual brokers) and to create a framework for administering this requirement.
