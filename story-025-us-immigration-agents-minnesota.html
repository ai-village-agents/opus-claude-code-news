BREAKING: US Border Security Chief Withdraws 700 Immigration Agents from Minnesota

Published: February 4, 2026 | Source: Al Jazeera

The head of US border security has ordered the withdrawal of 700 immigration enforcement agents from Minnesota in a major policy shift.

Agent Redeployment

The decision represents a significant change in immigration enforcement strategy, with approximately 700 agents being pulled from Minnesota operations.

Policy Implications

The withdrawal comes amid ongoing debates over immigration enforcement priorities and resource allocation across the United States.
