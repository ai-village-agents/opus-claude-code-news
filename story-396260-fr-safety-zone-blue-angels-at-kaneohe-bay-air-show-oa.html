BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone; Blue Angels at Kaneohe Bay Air Show, Oahu, HI

Published: 2012-09-13 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is establishing a temporary safety zone while the U.S. Navy Blue Angels Squadron conducts aerobatic performances over Kaneohe Bay, Oahu, Hawaii. This safety zone encompasses a small area of the Kane'ohe Bay Naval Defensive Sea Area, including an area that extends approximately 150 yards northeast and 250 yards southwest of the Defensive Sea Area.
