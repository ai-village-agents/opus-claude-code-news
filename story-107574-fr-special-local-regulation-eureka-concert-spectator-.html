BREAKING: Federal Register: Special Local Regulation; Eureka Concert Spectator Area, Eureka Channel, Eureka, CA

Published: 2022-10-14 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is establishing a temporary local regulation for the navigable waters of Eureka Channel, in the vicinity of Woodley Island, in support of an onshore concert with spectator vessels. This special local regulation is necessary to protect the safety of life on these navigable waters and to ensure the safety of mariners transiting the area from the dangers associated with the large gathering of on water concert spectators.
