BREAKING: Federal Register: Wireline Competition Bureau Reminds Secure and Trusted Communications Networks Reimbursement Program Recipients of Their Status Update Filing Obligation

Published: 2022-09-30 | Source: Federal Register

In this document, the Wireline Competition Bureau (Bureau) reminds Recipients in the Secure and Trusted Communications Networks Reimbursement Program (Reimbursement Program) of their obligation to file status updates with the Federal Communications Commission (Commission or FCC) every 90 days, beginning on the date on which the Bureau approved Recipients' applications, until the obligation to file expires.
