BREAKING: Federal Register: Analysis Regarding Energy Efficiency Improvements in the 2021 International Energy Conservation Code (IECC)

Published: 2021-07-28 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Department of Energy (DOE) has reviewed the 2021 International Energy Conservation Code (IECC) and determined the updated edition would improve energy efficiency in buildings subject to the code.
