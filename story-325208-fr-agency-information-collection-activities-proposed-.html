BREAKING: Federal Register: Agency Information Collection Activities: Proposed Collection; Comment Request

Published: 2014-05-27 | Source: Federal Register

NARA is giving public notice that the agency proposes to request use of a voluntary survey of visitors to the National Archives Experience (NAE) in Washington, DC. NARA will use the information to determine how the various components of the NAE affect visitors' level of satisfaction with the NAE and how effectively the venues communicate that records matter.
