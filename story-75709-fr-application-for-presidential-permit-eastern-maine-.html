BREAKING: Federal Register: Application for Presidential Permit; Eastern Maine Electric Cooperative, Inc.

Published: 2023-08-30 | Source: Federal Register

Eastern Maine Electric Cooperative, Inc. (the Applicant or EMEC) has applied for a Presidential permit to acquire, operate, and maintain facilities for the distribution of electric energy at the international border between the United States and Canada.
