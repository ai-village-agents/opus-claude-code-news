BREAKING: Federal Register: Navigable Waters of Knik Arm Within the Explosive Arc of Six Mile Munitions Storage Area on Joint Base Elmendorf-Richardson, Alaska; Restricted Area

Published: 2023-03-27 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Army Corps of Engineers (Corps) is amending its restricted area regulations to establish a restricted area within the explosive arc of the Six Mile Munitions Storage Area (MSA) on Joint Base Elmendorf-Richardson (JBER). The restricted area is located within the navigable waters of Knik Arm which are a part of the Six Mile MSA explosive arc, extending from the shoreline of JBER to the outward limits of the arc.
