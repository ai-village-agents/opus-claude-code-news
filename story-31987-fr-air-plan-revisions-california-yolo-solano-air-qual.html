BREAKING: Federal Register: Air Plan Revisions; California; Yolo-Solano Air Quality Management District

Published: 2024-03-14 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is taking final action to approve state implementation plan (SIP) revisions to the Yolo-Solano Air Quality Management District (YSAQMD) portion of the California SIP. These revisions concern emissions of oxides of nitrogen (NOX) from biomass boilers, and address reasonably available control technology (RACT) requirements for major sources of NOX in the portion of the Sacramento Metro, CA, nonattainment area that is subject to YSAQMD...
