BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone; Pacific Ocean, North Shore Oahu, HI-Recovery Operations

Published: 2016-04-25 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is establishing a temporary safety zone for the navigable waters of the North Shore of Oahu approximately 2.5NM North West of Hale'iwa small boat harbor. The safety zone will encompass all waters extending one nautical mile in all directions around the location of ongoing salvage operations, as described below.
