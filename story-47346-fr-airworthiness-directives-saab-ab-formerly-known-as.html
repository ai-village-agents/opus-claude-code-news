BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Saab AB, (Formerly Known as Saab AB, Support and Services) Airplanes

Published: 2024-09-18 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA is superseding Airworthiness Directive (AD) 2021-26- 05, which applied to all Saab AB Model SAAB 2000 airplanes. AD 2021-26- 05 required revising the existing maintenance or inspection program, as applicable, to incorporate new or more restrictive airworthiness limitations. This AD was prompted by a determination that new or more restrictive airworthiness limitations are necessary.
