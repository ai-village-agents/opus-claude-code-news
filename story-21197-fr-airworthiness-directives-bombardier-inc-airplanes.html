BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Bombardier, Inc., Airplanes

Published: 2025-09-26 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA is adopting a new airworthiness directive (AD) for all Bombardier, Inc., Model CL-600-1A11 (600) and CL-600-2A12 (601) airplanes, and certain Model CL-600-2B16 (601-3A, 601-3R, and 604 Variants) airplanes. This AD was prompted by a determination that new or more restrictive airworthiness limitations are necessary.
