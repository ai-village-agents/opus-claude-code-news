BREAKING: Federal Register: Carriage of Conditionally Permitted Shale Gas Extraction Waste Water in Bulk

Published: 2016-02-23 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard announces that it has withdrawn the October 30, 2013, proposed policy letter concerning the carriage of shale gas extraction waste water (SGEWW) in bulk via barge. The policy letter proposed a new standardized process and specified conditions under which a barge owner could request and be granted a Certificate of Inspection endorsement or letter allowing the barge to transport SGEWW in bulk.
