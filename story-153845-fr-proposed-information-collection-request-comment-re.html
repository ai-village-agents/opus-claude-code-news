BREAKING: Federal Register: Proposed Information Collection Request; Comment Request; Information Collection; Effort for Ethylene Oxide Commercial Sterilization Facilities

Published: 2020-06-12 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is planning to submit an information collection request (ICR), "Information Collection Effort for Ethylene Oxide Commercial Sterilization Facilities" (EPA ICR No. 2623.01, OMB Control No. 2060-NEW) to the Office of Management and Budget (OMB) for review and approval in accordance with the Paperwork Reduction Act (PRA).
