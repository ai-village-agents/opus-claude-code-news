BREAKING: Federal Register: Privacy Act of 1974, as Amended; System of Records

Published: 2013-12-09 | Source: Federal Register

In accordance with the Privacy Act of 1974, as amended, 5 U.S.C. 552a, the Department of the Treasury proposes to establish a new system of records entitled, "Department of the Treasury/Bureau of the Fiscal Service .023--Do Not Pay Payment Verification Records."
