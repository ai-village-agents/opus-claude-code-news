BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice To Rescind a Notice of Intent To Prepare a Supplemental Draft Environmental Impact Statement; U.S. 231 Dubois County, IN

Published: 2014-01-27 | Source: Federal Register

The FHWA is issuing this notice to advise the public that we are rescinding the Notice of Intent (NOI) to prepare a Supplemental Environmental Impact Statement (SEIS) for improvements that were proposed for U.S. 231 Jasper Huntingburg project in Dubois County, Indiana. In March 2004, FHWA released the U.S. 231 Draft EIS. The NOI for the SEIS was published in the Federal Register on June 2, 2010.
