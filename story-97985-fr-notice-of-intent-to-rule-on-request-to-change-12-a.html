BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Intent To Rule on Request To Change 1.2 Acres of Airport Land in the Existing Business Park From Aeronautical to Non-Aeronautical Use at Martha's Vineyard Airport, West Tisbury, MA

Published: 2022-06-09 | Source: Federal Register

Notice is being given that the FAA is considering a request from the County of Dukes to change 1.2 acres of land from Aeronautical Use to Non-Aeronautical Use in the existing Airport Business Park at Martha's Vineyard Airport, West Tisbury, MA. The two parcels are being used for non-aeronautical development in the Airport Business Park.
