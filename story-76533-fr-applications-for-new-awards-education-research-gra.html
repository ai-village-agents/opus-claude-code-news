BREAKING: Federal Register: Applications for New Awards; Education Research Grant Programs

Published: 2023-09-11 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Education (Department) is issuing a notice inviting applications for new awards for fiscal year (FY) 2024 for the Research Training Programs in the Education Sciences, Research Networks Focused on Critical Problems of Education Policy and Practice, and Statistical and Research Methodology in Education Grant Programs, Assistance Listing Numbers (ALNs) 84.305B, 84.305D, and 84.305N. This notice relates to the approved information collection under OMB control number 4040-0001.
