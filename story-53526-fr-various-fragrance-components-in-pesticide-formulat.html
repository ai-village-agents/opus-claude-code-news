BREAKING: Federal Register: Various Fragrance Components in Pesticide Formulations; Tolerance Exemption

Published: 2024-11-25 | Source: Federal Register

This regulation establishes an exemption from the requirement of a tolerance for residues of various fragrance components listed in Unit II of this document when they used as inert ingredients in antimicrobial formulations applied to food-contact surfaces in public eating places, dairy-processing equipment, and food-processing equipment and utensils when the end-use concentration does not exceed 33 parts per million (ppm).
