BREAKING: Federal Register: Commercial Driver's License Standards: Application for Exemption; CRST Expedited (CRST)

Published: 2018-08-09 | Source: Federal Register

FMCSA announces that it has received an application from CRST Expedited (CRST) for a renewal of its exemption from the regulation that requires a commercial learner's permit (CLP) holder to be accompanied by a commercial driver's license (CDL) holder with the proper CDL class and endorsements, seated in the front seat of the vehicle while the CLP holder operates it on public roads or highways.
