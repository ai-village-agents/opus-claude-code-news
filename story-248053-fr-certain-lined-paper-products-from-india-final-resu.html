BREAKING: Federal Register: Certain Lined Paper Products From India: Final Results of Expedited Second Sunset Review of Countervailing Duty Order

Published: 2017-11-06 | Source: Federal Register

On July 3, 2017, the Department of Commerce (the Department) initiated a sunset review of the countervailing duty (CVD) order on certain lined paper products from India pursuant to section 751(c) of the Tariff Act of 1930, as amended (the Act). The Department has conducted an expedited sunset review of this order pursuant to the Act and 19 CFR 351.
