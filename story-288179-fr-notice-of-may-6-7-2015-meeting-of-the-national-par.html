BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of May 6-7, 2015, Meeting of the National Park System Advisory Board

Published: 2015-03-09 | Source: Federal Register

Notice is hereby given in accordance with the Federal Advisory Committee Act, 5 U.S.C. Appendix 1-16, and Part 65 of title 36 of the Code of Federal Regulations that the National Park System Advisory Board will meet May 6-7, 2015, in Pensacola Beach, Florida. The agenda will include the review of proposed actions regarding the National Historic Landmarks (NHL) Program.
