BREAKING: Federal Register: Request for Information: Sustainable Chemistry

Published: 2022-04-04 | Source: Federal Register

The Office of Science and Technology Policy (OSTP) requests input from interested parties on sustainable chemistry to guide future Federal efforts. The term "sustainable chemistry" does not have a consensus definition and most uses of the term indicate that it is synonymous with "green chemistry." Therefore, information is requested on the preferred definition for sustainable chemistry.
