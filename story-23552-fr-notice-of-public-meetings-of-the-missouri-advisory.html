BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Public Meetings of the Missouri Advisory Committee to the U.S. Commission on Civil Rights

Published: 2025-11-26 | Source: Federal Register

Notice is hereby given, pursuant to the provisions of the rules and regulations of the U.S. Commission on Civil Rights (Commission) and the Federal Advisory Committee Act that the Missouri Advisory Committee (Committee) will hold a meeting on Tuesday, December 9, 2025, at 12:00 p.m. Central time. The purpose of the meeting is for the Committee to review the report on Curriculum Censorship.
