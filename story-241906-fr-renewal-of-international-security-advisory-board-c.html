BREAKING: Federal Register: Renewal of International Security Advisory Board Charter

Published: 2017-08-22 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of State announces the renewal of the Charter of the International Security Advisory Board (ISAB). The purpose of the ISAB is to provide the Department with a continuing source of independent insight, advice, and innovation on all aspects of arms control, disarmament, nonproliferation, and international security, and related aspects of public diplomacy.
