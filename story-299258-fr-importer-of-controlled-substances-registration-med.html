BREAKING: Federal Register: Importer of Controlled Substances Registration: Meda Pharmaceuticals, Inc.

Published: 2015-07-17 | Source: Federal Register

Meda Pharmaceuticals, Inc. applied to be registered as an importer of a certain basic class of controlled substance. The Drug Enforcement Administration (DEA) grants Meda Pharmaceuticals, Inc. registration as an importer of this controlled substance.
