BREAKING: Federal Register: Clean Water Act Methods Update Rule for the Analysis of Effluent

Published: 2019-10-22 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is proposing changes to its test procedures required to be used by industries and municipalities when analyzing the chemical, physical, and biological properties of wastewater and other environmental samples for reporting under the EPA's National Pollutant Discharge Elimination System (NPDES) permit program.
