BREAKING: Federal Register: National Maritime Week Tugboat Races, Seattle, WA

Published: 2013-05-09 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard will enforce the Special Local Regulation for the annual National Maritime Week Tugboat Races in Elliott Bay, WA from 12 p.m. until 4:30 p.m. on May 11, 2013. This action is necessary to ensure the safety of all participants and spectators from the inherent dangers associated with these types of races which includes large wakes.
