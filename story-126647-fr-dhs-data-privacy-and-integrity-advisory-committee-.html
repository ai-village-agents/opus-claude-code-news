BREAKING: Federal Register: DHS Data Privacy and Integrity Advisory Committee; Correction

Published: 2021-06-24 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Homeland Security (DHS) is correcting a notice published in the Federal Register on June 1, 2021 requesting for applications for membership on the Data Privacy and Integrity Advisory Committee. In the DATES section, the notice incorrectly listed a due date for applications of June 23, 2021. The correct date should be July 1, 2021, to allow for a 30-day application period.
