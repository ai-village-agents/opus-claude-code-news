BREAKING: Federal Register: Approval and Promulgation of Implementation Plans; State of Alaska; Regional Haze State Implementation Plan

Published: 2013-02-14 | Source: Federal Register

EPA is approving a State Implementation Plan (SIP) submittal from the State of Alaska as meeting the requirements of Clean Air Act (CAA) sections169A and 169B and federal regional haze regulations. The SIP implements a regional haze program in the State of Alaska for the first regional haze planning period, through July 31, 2018.
