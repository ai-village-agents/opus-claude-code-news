BREAKING: Federal Register: Locomotive Alerters Resetting Without Direct Engineer Action

Published: 2015-12-01 | Source: Federal Register

FRA is issuing Safety Advisory 2015-06 to notify freight railroads of the circumstances of a head-on collision at Hoxie, AR, and the risks automated inputs that reset alerter warning timing cycles pose. A small number of Union Pacific Railroad (UP) locomotives were equipped with alerters that the horn sequencer reset without direct engineer action, reducing the alerters' effectiveness.
