BREAKING: Federal Register: Privacy Act of 1974; System of Records

Published: 2012-07-02 | Source: Federal Register

As required by the Privacy Act of 1974, 5 U.S.C. 552a(e)(4) notice is hereby given that the Department of Veterans Affairs (VA) proposes to amend a system of records entitled "Personnel and Accounting Integrated Data System"--VA (27VA047). This amendment will update our Routine Uses of Records Maintained in the System.
