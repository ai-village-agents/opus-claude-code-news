BREAKING: Federal Register: Federal Advisory Committee Act (FACA); Database Cost Reporting

Published: 2022-09-28 | Source: Federal Register

FMR Bulletin 2022-F1. This bulletin clarifies, highlights, and reminds agencies of the importance of accurately reporting the cost of federal advisory committees to Congress and the public. It also adds the requirement to include financial reporting instructions in agency administrative procedures.
