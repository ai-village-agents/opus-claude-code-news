BREAKING: Federal Register: Atlantic Highly Migratory Species; 2013 Atlantic Bluefin Tuna Quota Specifications

Published: 2013-06-19 | Source: Federal Register

NMFS establishes 2013 quota specifications for the Atlantic bluefin tuna (BFT) fishery and closes the incidental Longline category northern and southern area fisheries for large medium and giant BFT for the remainder of 2013.
