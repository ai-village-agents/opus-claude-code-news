BREAKING: Federal Register: Maryland: Final Authorization of State Hazardous Waste Management Program Revisions

Published: 2016-08-30 | Source: Federal Register

Maryland has applied to the United States Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) for final authorization of revisions to its hazardous waste program under the Resource Conservation and Recovery Act (RCRA). EPA proposes to grant final authorization to Maryland. In the Rules and Regulations section of this Federal Register, EPA is authorizing the revisions by a direct final rule.
