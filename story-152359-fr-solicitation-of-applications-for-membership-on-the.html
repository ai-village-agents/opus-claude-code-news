BREAKING: Federal Register: Solicitation of Applications for Membership on the Community Advisory Council Extension of Application Period

Published: 2020-05-26 | Source: Federal Register

On March 27, 2020 the Board published in the Federal Register a notice seeking applications for membership on the Community Advisory Council (CAC). The application period for this notice has been extended in light of ongoing challenges for households and businesses caused by the COVID-19 emergency in order to provide additional opportunity for interested persons to submit their application.
