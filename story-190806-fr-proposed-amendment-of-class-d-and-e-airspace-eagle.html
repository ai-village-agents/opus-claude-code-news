BREAKING: Federal Register: Proposed Amendment of Class D and E Airspace; Eagle County, CO

Published: 2019-10-09 | Source: Federal Register

This action proposes to amend Class D airspace and Class E surface airspace; establish Class E surface airspace designated as an extension to the Class D and Class E2 surface areas; amend the Class E5 airspace extending from 700 feet above the surface of the earth; and update the geographic coordinates of the airport to match the FAA's database at the Eagle County Regional Airport in Eagle, CO.
