BREAKING: Federal Register: President's Board of Advisors on Historically Black Colleges and Universities

Published: 2016-10-27 | Source: Federal Register

This notice sets forth the agenda for the October 26, 2016 meeting of the President's Board of Advisors on Historically Black Colleges and Universities (Board) and provides information regarding an opportunity to attend. Notice of the meeting is required by the Federal Advisory Committee Act (FACA) and is intended to notify the public of its opportunity to attend.
