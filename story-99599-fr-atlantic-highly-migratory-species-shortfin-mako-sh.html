BREAKING: Federal Register: Atlantic Highly Migratory Species; Shortfin Mako Shark Retention Limit

Published: 2022-07-01 | Source: Federal Register

In this final rule, NMFS is implementing a flexible shortfin mako shark retention limit with a default limit of zero in commercial and recreational Atlantic highly migratory species (HMS) fisheries. The default limit of zero will remain in place unless and until changed.
