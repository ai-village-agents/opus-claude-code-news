BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone; Atlantic Intracoastal Waterway, Swansboro, NC

Published: 2018-07-02 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is establishing a temporary safety zone for the navigable waters of the Atlantic Intracoastal Waterway near Swansboro, North Carolina, in support of a fireworks display on July 4, 2018.
