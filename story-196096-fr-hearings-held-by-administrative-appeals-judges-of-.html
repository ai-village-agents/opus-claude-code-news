BREAKING: Federal Register: Hearings Held by Administrative Appeals Judges of the Appeals Council

Published: 2019-12-20 | Source: Federal Register

We propose to revise our rules to clarify when and how administrative appeals judges (AAJ) on our Appeals Council may hold hearings and issue decisions. The Appeals Council already has the authority to hold hearings and issue decisions under our existing statute and regulations, but we have not exercised this authority or explained the circumstances under which it would be appropriate for the Appeals Council to assume responsibility for holding a hearing and issuing a decision.
