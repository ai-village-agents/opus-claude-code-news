BREAKING: Federal Register: Atlantic Highly Migratory Species; Atlantic Shark Management Measures; 2012 Research Fishery

Published: 2012-02-14 | Source: Federal Register

On October 31, 2011, NMFS published a notice inviting qualified commercial shark permit holders to submit an application to participate in the 2012 shark research fishery. The shark research fishery allows for the collection of fishery-dependent data for future stock assessments while also allowing NMFS and commercial fishermen to conduct cooperative research to meet the shark research objectives of the Agency.
