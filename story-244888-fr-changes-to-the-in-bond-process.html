BREAKING: Federal Register: Changes to the In-Bond Process

Published: 2017-09-28 | Source: Federal Register

This final rule adopts, with several changes, proposed amendments to U.S. Customs and Border Protection (CBP) regulations regarding changes to the in-bond process published in the Federal Register on February 22, 2012.
