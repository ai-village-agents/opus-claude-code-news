BREAKING: Federal Register: National Television Multiple Ownership Rule

Published: 2013-11-14 | Source: Federal Register

This Notice commences a proceeding to consider elimination of the so-called UHF discount in the Commission's national television multiple ownership rule. Currently, the national television ownership rule prohibits a single entity from owning television stations that, in the aggregate, reach more than 39 percent of the total television households in the nation.
