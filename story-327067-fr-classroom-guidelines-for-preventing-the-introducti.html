BREAKING: Federal Register: Classroom Guidelines for Preventing the Introduction and Spread of Aquatic Invasive Species (AIS)

Published: 2014-06-16 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Fish and Wildlife Service (Service), announces the availability of the Classroom Guidelines for Preventing the Introduction and Spread of Aquatic Invasive Species (AIS).
