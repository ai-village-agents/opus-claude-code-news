BREAKING: Federal Register: Air Plan Approval; Arizona; Maricopa County Air Quality Department

Published: 2019-06-10 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is proposing to approve revisions to the Maricopa County Air Quality Department (MCAQD) portion of the Arizona State Implementation Plan (SIP). These revisions concern emissions of volatile organic compounds (VOCs) from graphic arts and from coating of wood furniture and fixtures.
