BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Extension To Comment Period on the Request for Information on Improving the Military Selective Service Process and Increasing Participation in Military, National, and Public Service

Published: 2018-04-20 | Source: Federal Register

The National Commission on Military, National, and Public Service (the "Commission") is extending the comment period for the notice, "Request for Information on Improving the Military Selective Service Process and Increasing Participation in Military, National, and Public Service." In response to stakeholder requests, the Commission is extending the comment period until September 2018. Additionally, the contact information for the Commission has been updated.
