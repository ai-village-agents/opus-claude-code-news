BREAKING: Federal Register: Meeting of the CDC/HRSA Advisory Committee on HIV, Viral Hepatitis and STD Prevention and Treatment

Published: 2019-09-12 | Source: Federal Register

In accordance with the Federal Advisory Committee Act, the Centers for Disease Control and Prevention (CDC)/HRSA Advisory Committee on HIV, Viral Hepatitis and STD Prevention and Treatment (CHAC) has scheduled a public meeting. Information about CHAC can be found on the CHAC website at https://www.cdc.gov/maso/facm/ facmCHACHSPT.html. Meeting information and the agenda can be found at http://www.chachspt.com.
