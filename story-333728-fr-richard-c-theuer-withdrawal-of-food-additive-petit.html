BREAKING: Federal Register: Richard C. Theuer; Withdrawal of Food Additive Petition and Citizen Petition

Published: 2014-09-02 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA or we) is announcing the withdrawal, without prejudice to a future filing, of a food additive petition (FAP 3A4798) proposing that the food additive regulations be amended to prohibit the use of carrageenan and salts of carrageenan in infant formula.
