BREAKING: Federal Register: Pick-Sloan Missouri Basin Program-Eastern Division-Rate Order No. WAPA-180

Published: 2017-07-03 | Source: Federal Register

Western Area Power Administration (WAPA) is proposing revised rates for Pick-Sloan Missouri Basin Program--Eastern Division (P-SMBP-- ED) firm power and firm peaking power service, and a new formula rate for sales of surplus products. Current firm power and firm peaking power service rates, under Rate Schedules P-SED-F12 and P-SED-FP12, are in effect through December 31, 2019.
