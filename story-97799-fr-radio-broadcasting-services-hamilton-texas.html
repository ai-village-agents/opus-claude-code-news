BREAKING: Federal Register: Radio Broadcasting Services; Hamilton, Texas

Published: 2022-06-08 | Source: Federal Register

This document amends the FM Table of Allotments, of the Federal Communications Commission's (Commission or FCC) rules, by adding Channel 263A at Hamilton, Texas. Channel 263A would provide a second local service at Hamilton, Texas. A staff engineering analysis reveals that Channel 263A can be allotted to Hamilton in conformity with the FCC's rules at reference coordinates 31-39-48.1 NL and 98-21- 29.4 WL.
