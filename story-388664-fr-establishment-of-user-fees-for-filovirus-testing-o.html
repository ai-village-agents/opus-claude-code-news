BREAKING: Federal Register: Establishment of User Fees for Filovirus Testing of Nonhuman Primate Liver Samples

Published: 2012-06-15 | Source: Federal Register

On February 10, 2012, the Centers for Disease Control and Prevention (CDC), within the Department of Health and Human Services (HHS) published a Direct Final Rule (DFR) that solicited public comment on the establishment of user fees for filovirus testing of all nonhuman primates that die during the HHS/CDC-required 31-day quarantine period for any reason other than trauma.
