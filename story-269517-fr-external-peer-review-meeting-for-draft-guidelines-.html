BREAKING: Federal Register: External Peer Review Meeting for Draft Guidelines for Human Exposure Assessment

Published: 2016-07-26 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Environmental Protection Agency (EPA or Agency) is announcing that Versar, a contractor to the EPA, will convene an independent panel of experts to review the draft document, Guidelines for Human Exposure Assessment (draft guidelines). EPA previously announced release of the draft guidance for comment. The public comment period ended on March 22, 2016.
