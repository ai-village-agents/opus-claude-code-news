BREAKING: Federal Register: Sole-Source Concession Contract for Lake Mead National Recreation Area

Published: 2016-11-08 | Source: Federal Register

Public notice is hereby given that the National Park Service proposes to award a sole-source concession contract for the conduct of certain visitor services within Lake Mead National Recreation Area (Lake Mead NRA) for a term not to exceed five (5) years. The visitor services include the provision of wireless Internet access within remote but developed areas of Lake Mead NRA.
