BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone; Apple Vacations Fireworks; Lake Michigan, Chicago IL

Published: 2019-08-29 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is establishing a temporary safety zone on Lake Michigan in Chicago, Illinois, in order to protect vessels and persons from the potential hazards associated with a barge based fireworks display. The temporary safety zone will encompass all waters within a 560 foot radius from the designated barge responsible for the display.
