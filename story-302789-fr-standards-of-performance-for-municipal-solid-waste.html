BREAKING: Federal Register: Standards of Performance for Municipal Solid Waste Landfills

Published: 2015-08-27 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is issuing this supplemental proposal for the Standards of Performance for Municipal Solid Waste (MSW) Landfills to address the nonmethane organic compound (NMOC) emission rate threshold at which an affected MSW landfill must install controls.
