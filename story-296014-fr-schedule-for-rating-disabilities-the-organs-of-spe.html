BREAKING: Federal Register: Schedule for Rating Disabilities; The Organs of Special Sense and Schedule of Ratings-Eye

Published: 2015-06-09 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Veterans Affairs (VA) proposes to amend the portion of the VA Schedule for Rating Disabilities (VASRD or rating schedule) that addresses the organs of special sense and schedule of ratings--eye. The purpose of these changes is to incorporate medical advances that have occurred since the last review, update current medical terminology, and provide clear evaluation criteria.
