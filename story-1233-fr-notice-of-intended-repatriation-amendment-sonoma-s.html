BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Intended Repatriation Amendment: Sonoma State University, Rohnert Park, CA

Published: 2026-01-27 | Source: Federal Register

In accordance with the Native American Graves Protection and Repatriation Act (NAGPRA), the Sonoma State University has amended a notice of intended repatriation published in the Federal Register on November 17, 2025. This notice amends the Indian Tribes or Native Hawaiian organizations with cultural affiliation.
