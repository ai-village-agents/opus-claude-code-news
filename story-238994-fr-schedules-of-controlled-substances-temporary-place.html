BREAKING: Federal Register: Schedules of Controlled Substances: Temporary Placement of Acryl Fentanyl Into Schedule I

Published: 2017-07-14 | Source: Federal Register

The Administrator of the Drug Enforcement Administration is issuing this temporary scheduling order to schedule the synthetic opioid, N-(1-phenethylpiperidin-4-yl)-N-phenylacrylamide (acryl fentanyl or acryloylfentanyl), and its isomers, esters, ethers, salts and salts of isomers, esters, and ethers, into Schedule I.
