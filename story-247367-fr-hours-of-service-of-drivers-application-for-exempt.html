BREAKING: Federal Register: Hours of Service of Drivers: Application for Exemption; Motion Picture Association of America

Published: 2017-10-27 | Source: Federal Register

FMCSA announces that the Motion Picture Association of America (MPAA) has requested an exemption from the electronic logging device (ELD) requirements for all commercial motor vehicle (CMV) drivers providing transportation to or from a theatrical or television motion picture production site. MPAA request this exemption to allow these drivers to complete paper records of duty status (RODS) instead of using an ELD device.
