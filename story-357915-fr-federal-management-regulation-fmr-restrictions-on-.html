BREAKING: Federal Register: Federal Management Regulation (FMR); Restrictions on International Transportation of Freight and Household Goods

Published: 2013-06-19 | Source: Federal Register

GSA is proposing to amend the Federal Management Regulation (FMR) provisions pertaining to the use of United States air carriers for cargo under the provisions of the "Fly America Act." This proposed rule would additionally update the current provisions in the FMR regarding the Cargo Preference Act of 1954, as amended.
