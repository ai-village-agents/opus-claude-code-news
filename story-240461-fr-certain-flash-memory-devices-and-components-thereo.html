BREAKING: Federal Register: Certain Flash Memory Devices and Components Thereof; Notice of Commission Determination Not To Review and Initial Determination Granting a Joint Motion To Terminate the Investigation in Its Entirety Based Upon Settlement; Termination of the Investigation

Published: 2017-08-02 | Source: Federal Register

Notice is hereby given that the U.S. International Trade Commission has determined not to review the presiding administrative law judge's ("ALJ") initial determination ("ID") (Order No. 22) granting a joint motion to terminate the investigation in its entirety based upon settlement.
