BREAKING: Federal Register: Official Release of the MOVES2014 Motor Vehicle Emissions Model for SIPs and Transportation Conformity

Published: 2014-10-07 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is approving and announcing the availability of the Motor Vehicle Emissions Simulator model (MOVES2014) for official use outside of California. MOVES2014 is the latest state-of-the art upgrade to EPA's modeling tools for estimating emissions from cars, trucks, buses, and motorcycles, based on the latest data and regulations.
