BREAKING: Federal Register: Request for Renewal of the Incidental Take Permit and Short-Term Habitat Conservation Plan for Operation and Maintenance of Existing and Limited Future Facilities Associated With the Kauai Island Utility Cooperative on Kauai, Hawaii

Published: 2018-06-15 | Source: Federal Register

The Kauai Island Utility Cooperative (KIUC, or applicant) has submitted an application to the U.S. Fish and Wildlife Service (Service) for renewal of their incidental take permit (permit) under the Endangered Species Act of 1973, as amended.
