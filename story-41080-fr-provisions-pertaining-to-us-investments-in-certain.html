BREAKING: Federal Register: Provisions Pertaining to U.S. Investments in Certain National Security Technologies and Products in Countries of Concern

Published: 2024-07-05 | Source: Federal Register

This proposed rule sets forth regulations that would implement Executive Order 14105 of August 9, 2023, "Addressing United States Investments in Certain National Security Technologies and Products in Countries of Concern," which declares a national emergency to address the threat to the United States posed by countries of concern, which seek to develop and exploit sensitive technologies or products critical for military, intelligence, surveillance, or cyber-enabled capabilities.
