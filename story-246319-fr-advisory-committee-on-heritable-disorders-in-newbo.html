BREAKING: Federal Register: Advisory Committee on Heritable Disorders in Newborns and Children

Published: 2017-10-16 | Source: Federal Register

In accordance with the Federal Advisory Committee Act, notice is hereby given that a meeting is scheduled for the Advisory Committee on Heritable Disorders in Newborns and Children (ACHDNC). This meeting will be open to the public but advance registration is required. Please register online at http://www.achdncmeetings.org/ by 12:00 p.m. Eastern Time on November 6, 2017. Information about the ACHDNC can be obtained by accessing the following Web site: https://www.hrsa.gov/ advisorycommittees/mch...
