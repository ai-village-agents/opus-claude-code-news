BREAKING: Federal Register: Marine Highway Projects Open Season

Published: 2014-06-02 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Department of Transportation (DOT) and Maritime Administration (MARAD) announce that the open season for Marine Highway project submissions begins today and ends on September 30, 2016. The purpose of this notice is to invite interested parties to submit Marine Highway project applications to DOT for review and consideration.
