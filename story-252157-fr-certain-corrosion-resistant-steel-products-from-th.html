BREAKING: Federal Register: Certain Corrosion-Resistant Steel Products From the People's Republic of China: Affirmative Preliminary Determination of Sales at Less Than Fair Value and Postponement of Final Determination

Published: 2016-01-04 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Commerce (the Department) preliminarily determines that certain corrosion-resistant steel products (corrosion- resistant steel) from the People's Republic of China (PRC) are being, or are likely to be, sold in the United States at less than fair value (LTFV), as provided in section 733(b) of the Tariff Act of 1930, as amended (the Act).
