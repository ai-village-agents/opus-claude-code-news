BREAKING: Federal Register: Outsourcing Facility Fee Rates for Fiscal Year 2026

Published: 2025-07-30 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA or we) is announcing the fiscal year (FY) 2026 rates for the establishment and reinspection fees related to entities that compound human drugs and elect to register as outsourcing facilities under the Federal Food, Drug, and Cosmetic Act (FD&C Act). The FD&C Act authorizes FDA to assess and collect an annual establishment fee from outsourcing facilities, as well as a reinspection fee for each reinspection of an outsourcing facility.
