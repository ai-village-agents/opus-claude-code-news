BREAKING: Federal Register: Forged Steel Fittings From the People's Republic of China: Preliminary Results and Partial Rescission of Countervailing Duty Administrative Review; 2020

Published: 2022-12-07 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Department of Commerce (Commerce) is conducting an administrative review of the countervailing duty (CVD) order on forged steel fittings from the People's Republic of China (China) for the period of review January 1, 2020, through December 31, 2020. Commerce preliminarily determines that countervailable subsidies are being provided to producers/exporters of forged steel fittings from China subject to this review.
