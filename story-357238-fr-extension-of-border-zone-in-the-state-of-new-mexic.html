BREAKING: Federal Register: Extension of Border Zone in the State of New Mexico

Published: 2013-06-12 | Source: Federal Register

This final rule amends Department of Homeland Security (DHS) regulations to extend the distance that certain nonimmigrant Mexican nationals presenting a Border Crossing Card, or other proper immigration documentation, may travel in New Mexico without obtaining a U.S. Customs and Border Protection (CBP) Form I-94 (Form I-94), Arrival/Departure Record.
