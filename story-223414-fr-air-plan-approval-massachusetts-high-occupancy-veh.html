BREAKING: Federal Register: Air Plan Approval; Massachusetts; High Occupancy Vehicle Lanes

Published: 2018-12-17 | Source: Federal Register

This revision provides for the Massachusetts Department of Transportation (MassDOT) to construct and operate specified transit facilities and high occupancy vehicle (HOV) lanes established therein.
