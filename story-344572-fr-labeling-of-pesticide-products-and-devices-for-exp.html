BREAKING: Federal Register: Labeling of Pesticide Products and Devices for Export; Clarification of Requirements

Published: 2013-01-18 | Source: Federal Register

EPA is revising the regulations on the labeling of pesticide products and devices intended solely for export. Specifically, EPA is restructuring the current regulations to clarify which provisions apply under various circumstances.
