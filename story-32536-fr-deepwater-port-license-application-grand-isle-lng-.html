BREAKING: Federal Register: Deepwater Port License Application: Grand Isle LNG Operating Company, LLC; Application Withdrawal

Published: 2024-03-21 | Source: Federal Register

The Maritime Administration (MARAD) and the U.S. Coast Guard (USCG) announce the cancellation of all actions related to the processing of a license application for the proposed Grand Isle LNG Export Deepwater Port Development Project deepwater port. The action announced here also includes cancellation of all activities related to the deepwater port application review and preparation of an Environmental Impact Statement that was previously published in the Federal Register on Monday, July 3, 2023...
