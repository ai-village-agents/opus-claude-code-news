BREAKING: Federal Register: Proposed Addition of Singani to the Standards of Identity for Distilled Spirits

Published: 2021-08-25 | Source: Federal Register

The Alcohol and Tobacco Tax and Trade Bureau (TTB) proposes to amend the regulations that set forth the standards of identity for distilled spirits to include Singani as a type of brandy that is a distinctive product of Bolivia. This proposal follows a joint petition submitted by the Plurinational State of Bolivia and Singani 63, Inc., and subsequent discussions with the Office of the United States Trade Representative.
