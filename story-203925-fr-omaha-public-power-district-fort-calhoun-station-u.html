BREAKING: Federal Register: Omaha Public Power District; Fort Calhoun Station, Unit No. 1

Published: 2018-04-06 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Nuclear Regulatory Commission (NRC) has issued exemption from certain requirements to maintain financial protection from offsite liability for the Fort Calhoun Station (FCS) in response to a request from Omaha Public Power District (OPPD or the licensee) dated April 28, 2017.
