BREAKING: Federal Register: Air Carrier Contract Maintenance Requirements; Correction

Published: 2015-05-04 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA is correcting a final rule published on March 4, 2015 (80 FR 11537). In that rule, the FAA amended its maintenance regulations for domestic, flag, and supplemental operations, and for commuter and on-demand operations for aircraft type certificated with a passenger seating configuration of 10 seats or more (excluding any pilot seat).
