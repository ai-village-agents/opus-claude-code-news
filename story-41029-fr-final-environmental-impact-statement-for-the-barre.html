BREAKING: Federal Register: Final Environmental Impact Statement for the Barred Owl Management Strategy; Washington, Oregon, and California

Published: 2024-07-05 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Fish and Wildlife Service (Service) developed a proposed barred owl management strategy (strategy) to address the threat that the nonnative and invasive barred owl (Strix varia) poses to two native western owl subspecies--the northern spotted owl (Strix occidentalis caurina) and the California spotted owl (Strix occidentalis occidentalis).
