BREAKING: Federal Register: Draft Revised Management Plan for the Kachemak Bay National Estuarine Research Reserve

Published: 2021-05-27 | Source: Federal Register

The National Oceanic and Atmospheric Administration (NOAA) is soliciting comments from the public regarding a proposed revision of the management plan for the Kachemak Bay National Estuarine Research Reserve. A management plan: Provides a framework for the direction and timing of a reserve's programs; allows reserve managers to assess a reserve's success in meeting its goals and to identify any necessary changes in direction; and is used to guide programmatic evaluations of the reserve.
