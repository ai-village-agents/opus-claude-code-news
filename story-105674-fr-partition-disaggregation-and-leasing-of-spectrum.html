BREAKING: Federal Register: Partition, Disaggregation, and Leasing of Spectrum

Published: 2022-09-20 | Source: Federal Register

In this document, the Commission modifies partitioning, disaggregation, and leasing rules to provide specific incentives for small carriers and Tribal Nations, and entities in rural areas, to voluntarily participate in the Enhanced Competition Incentive Program (ECIP).
