BREAKING: Federal Register: Railroad Cost Recovery Procedures-Productivity Adjustment

Published: 2012-02-10 | Source: Federal Register

In a decision served on February 6, 2012, we proposed to adopt 1.008 (0.8% per year) as the measure of average change in railroad productivity for the 2006-2010 (5-year) averaging period. This represents a 0.6% decrease over the average for the 2005-2009 period. The Board's February 6, 2012 decision in this proceeding stated that comments may be filed addressing any perceived data and computational errors in our calculation.
