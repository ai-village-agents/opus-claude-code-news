BREAKING: Federal Register: Privacy Act of 1974; System of Records

Published: 2019-05-01 | Source: Federal Register

The Federal Communications Commission (FCC, Commission, or Agency) has modified an existing system of records, FCC/OMD-12, Integrated Library System (ILS) Records, subject to the Privacy Act of 1974, as amended. This action is necessary to meet the requirements of the Privacy Act to publish in the Federal Register notice of the existence and character of records maintained by the Agency.
