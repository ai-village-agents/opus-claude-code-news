BREAKING: Federal Register: Adding International Energy Efficiency (IEE) Certificate to List of Certificates a Recognized Classification Society May Issue

Published: 2012-10-02 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard proposes to amend its Vessel Inspection Alternatives regulations to add the International Energy Efficiency (IEE) Certificate to the list of certificates that a recognized classification society may issue on behalf of the Coast Guard.
