BREAKING: Federal Register: Federal Acquisition Regulation; EPEAT Items

Published: 2015-09-03 | Source: Federal Register

DoD, GSA, and NASA are adopting as final, with changes, an interim rule amending the Federal Acquisition Regulation (FAR) to implement changes in the Electronic Product Environmental Assessment Tool (EPEAT[supreg]) registry.
