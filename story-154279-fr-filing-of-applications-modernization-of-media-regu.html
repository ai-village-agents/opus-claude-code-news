BREAKING: Federal Register: Filing of Applications; Modernization of Media Regulation Initiative; Revision of the Public Notice Requirements

Published: 2020-06-18 | Source: Federal Register

In this document the Commission adopts changes to its rules and procedures for broadcast station applicants to provide public notice of application filings to the principal area that is served or to be served by the station. The changes are designed to improve, streamline, and standardize the notices, including replacing notice by newspaper with online notice.
