BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone; USA Triathlon; Milwaukee Harbor, Milwaukee, Wisconsin.

Published: 2013-07-17 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard will enforce the safety zone on Lake Michigan within Milwaukee Harbor, Wisconsin for the 2013 Olympic and Sprint Distance National Championships. This action is necessary and intended to ensure safety of life on the navigable waters during the 2013 Olympic and Sprint Distance National Championships.
