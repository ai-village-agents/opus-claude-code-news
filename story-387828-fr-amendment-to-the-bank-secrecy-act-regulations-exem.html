BREAKING: Federal Register: Amendment to the Bank Secrecy Act Regulations-Exemption From the Requirement To Report Transactions in Currency

Published: 2012-06-07 | Source: Federal Register

FinCEN is issuing this final rule to amend the regulations that allow depository institutions to exempt transactions of certain payroll customers \1\ from the requirement to report transactions in currency in excess of $10,000. The rule substitutes the term "frequently" for "regularly" in the provision of the exemption rules dealing with payroll customers.
