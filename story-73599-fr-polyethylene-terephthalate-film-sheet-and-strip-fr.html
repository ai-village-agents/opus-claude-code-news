BREAKING: Federal Register: Polyethylene Terephthalate Film, Sheet, and Strip From India: Preliminary Results and Partial Rescission of Antidumping Duty Administrative Review; 2021-2022

Published: 2023-08-03 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Department of Commerce (Commerce) is conducting an administrative review of the antidumping duty (AD) order on polyethylene terephthalate film, sheet, and strip (PET film) from India. The period of review (POR) is July 1, 2021, through June 30, 2022. This review covers the following producers and exporters from India: Jindal Poly Films Ltd. (Jindal) and SRF Limited (SRF).
