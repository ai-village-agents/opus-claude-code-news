BREAKING: Federal Register: Biennial Review Under the United States-Singapore Memorandum of Intent on Environmental Cooperation

Published: 2020-08-28 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Department of State is providing notice that the United States and Singapore intend to hold a biennial review under the Memorandum of Intent between the Government of the United States of America and the Government of the Republic of Singapore on Cooperation in Environmental Matters (MOI) on September 15, 2020.
