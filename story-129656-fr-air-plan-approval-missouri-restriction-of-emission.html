BREAKING: Federal Register: Air Plan Approval; Missouri; Restriction of Emissions From Lithographic and Letterpress Printing Operations

Published: 2021-08-02 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is taking final action to approve a State Implementation Plan (SIP) revision submitted by the State of Missouri on November 10, 2020. This final action will amend the SIP to revise a Missouri regulation which restricts volatile organic compound emissions from lithographic and letterpress printing operations in the St. Louis Metropolitan Area.
