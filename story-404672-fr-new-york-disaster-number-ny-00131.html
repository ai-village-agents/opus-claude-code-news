BREAKING: Federal Register: New York Disaster Number NY-00131

Published: 2012-12-28 | Source: Federal Register

This is an amendment of the Presidential declaration of a major disaster for Public Assistance Only for the State of New York (FEMA--4085--DR), dated 11/03/2012. Incident: Hurricane Sandy. Incident Period: 10/27/2012 through 11/08/2012, Effective Date: 12/18/2012. Physical Loan Application Deadline Date: 01/02/2013. Economic Injury (EIDL) Loan Application Deadline Date: 08/05/2013.
