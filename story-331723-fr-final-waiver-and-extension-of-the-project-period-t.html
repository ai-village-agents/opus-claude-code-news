BREAKING: Federal Register: Final Waiver and Extension of the Project Period; Training and Advisory Services-Equity Assistance Centers Program

Published: 2014-08-08 | Source: Federal Register

For the 36-month projects funded in fiscal year (FY) 2011 under the Training and Advisory Services--Equity Assistance Centers (EACs) program, the Secretary waives the requirements that generally prohibit project period extensions involving the obligation of additional Federal funds. The Secretary also extends the project period of these grants for up to an additional 24 months.
