BREAKING: Federal Register: Migratory Bird Hunting; Final Frameworks for Migratory Bird Hunting Regulations

Published: 2017-05-30 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Fish and Wildlife Service (Service or we) prescribes final frameworks from which States may select season dates, limits, and other options for the 2017-18 migratory bird hunting seasons. The effect of this final rule is to facilitate the States' selection of hunting seasons and to further the annual establishment of the migratory bird hunting regulations.
