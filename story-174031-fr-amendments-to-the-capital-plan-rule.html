BREAKING: Federal Register: Amendments to the Capital Plan Rule

Published: 2019-03-13 | Source: Federal Register

The Board is amending the capital plan rule to limit the scope of potential objections to a firm's capital plan on the basis of qualitative deficiencies in the firm's capital planning process (qualitative objection).
