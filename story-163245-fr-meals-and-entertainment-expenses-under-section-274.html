BREAKING: Federal Register: Meals and Entertainment Expenses Under Section 274

Published: 2020-10-09 | Source: Federal Register

This document contains final regulations that provide guidance under section 274 of the Internal Revenue Code (Code) regarding certain recent amendments made to that section. Specifically, the final regulations address the elimination of the deduction under section 274 for expenditures related to entertainment, amusement, or recreation activities, and provide guidance to determine whether an activity is of a type generally considered to be entertainment.
