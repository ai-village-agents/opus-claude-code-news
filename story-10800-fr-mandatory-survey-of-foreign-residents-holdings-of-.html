BREAKING: Federal Register: Mandatory Survey of Foreign-Residents' Holdings of U.S. Securities

Published: 2025-05-07 | Source: Federal Register

By this Notice the Department of the Treasury is informing the public that it is conducting a mandatory survey of foreign-residents' holdings of U.S. securities, including selected money market instruments, as of June 30, 2025. This mandatory survey is conducted under the authority of the International Investment and Trade in Services Survey Act.
