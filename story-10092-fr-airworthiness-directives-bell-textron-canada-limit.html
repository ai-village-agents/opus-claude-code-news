BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Bell Textron Canada Limited Helicopters

Published: 2025-04-28 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA is adopting a new airworthiness directive (AD) for certain Bell Textron Canada Limited (BTCL) Model 505 helicopters. This AD was prompted by reports of possible chafing in certain locations between the basic and supplemental helicopter wiring and the structure.
