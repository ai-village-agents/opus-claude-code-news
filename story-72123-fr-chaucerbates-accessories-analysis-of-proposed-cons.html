BREAKING: Federal Register: Chaucer/Bates Accessories; Analysis of Proposed Consent Order To Aid Public Comment

Published: 2023-07-17 | Source: Federal Register

The consent agreement in this matter settles alleged violations of Federal law prohibiting unfair or deceptive acts or practices.
