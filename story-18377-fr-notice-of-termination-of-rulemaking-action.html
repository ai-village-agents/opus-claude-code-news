BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Termination of Rulemaking Action

Published: 2025-08-19 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Transportation (DOT) is announcing its decision to terminate its rulemaking titled "Notifying Air Travelers of DOT's Aviation Consumer Hotline" (RIN 2105-AE83), which was previously listed in DOT's semiannual Regulatory Agenda.
