BREAKING: Federal Register: Special Local Regulation; Bush River and Otter Point Creek; Between Perryman, MD and Edgewood, MD

Published: 2025-04-15 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is establishing a temporary special local regulation for certain waters of the Bush River and Otter Point Creek, in Maryland. This action is necessary to provide for the safety of life on these navigable waters, located at Edgewood, MD, during a high-speed power boat race on May 3, 2025, and May 4, 2025.
