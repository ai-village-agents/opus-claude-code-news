BREAKING: Federal Register: Air Plan Approval; Hawaii; Regional Haze Progress Report

Published: 2019-04-11 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is proposing to approve Hawaii's Regional Haze Progress Report ("Progress Report" or "Report") submitted by the State of Hawaii on October 20, 2017, as a revision to its state implementation plan (SIP). Hawaii submitted its Progress Report and a negative declaration stating that further revision of the existing regional haze plan is not needed at this time.
