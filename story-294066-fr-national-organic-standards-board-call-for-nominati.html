BREAKING: Federal Register: National Organic Standards Board: Call for Nominations; Extension of Nomination Period

Published: 2015-05-18 | Source: Federal Register

The Agricultural Marketing Service (AMS) published a notice soliciting nominations from qualified individuals to serve on the National Organic Standards Board (NOSB) in the Federal Register on April 9, 2015. Nominees will be considered for a 5-year NOSB term that will commence on January 24, 2016, and end January 23, 2021. The nomination period, as set in the notice, was to end on May 15, 2015.
