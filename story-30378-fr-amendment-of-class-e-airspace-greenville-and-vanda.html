BREAKING: Federal Register: Amendment of Class E Airspace; Greenville and Vandalia, IL

Published: 2024-02-26 | Source: Federal Register

This action proposes to amend the Class E airspace at Greenville, IL, and Vandalia, IL. The FAA is proposing this action as the result of airspace reviews conducted due to the decommissioning of the Vandalia very high frequency omnidirectional range (VOR) as part of the VOR Minimum Operating Network (MON) Program.
