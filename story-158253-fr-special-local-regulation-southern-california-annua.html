BREAKING: Federal Register: Special Local Regulation; Southern California Annual Marine Events for San Diego-San Diego Bayfair

Published: 2020-08-07 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard will enforce the San Diego Bayfair special local regulations on the waters of Mission Bay, California from September 18 through September 20, 2020. These special local regulations are necessary to provide for the safety of the participants, crew, spectators, sponsor vessels, and general users of the waterway.
