BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Request for Information; Technology Roadmap To Increase Wildfire Firefighting Capabilities

The Office of Science and Technology Policy (OSTP) requests input from all interested parties on the development of a comprehensive, technology roadmap to increase wildfire firefighting capabilities at the Federal, State, and local levels, pursuant to Executive Order 14308.
