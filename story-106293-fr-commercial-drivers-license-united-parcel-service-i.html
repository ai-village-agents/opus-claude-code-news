BREAKING: Federal Register: Commercial Driver's License: United Parcel Service, Inc. (UPS); Application for Exemptions

Published: 2022-09-28 | Source: Federal Register

FMCSA announces its decision to grant United Parcel Service, Inc.'s (UPS) application for an exemption from certain provisions of the Agency's commercial driver's license (CDL) regulations.
