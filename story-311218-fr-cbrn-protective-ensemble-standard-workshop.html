BREAKING: Federal Register: CBRN Protective Ensemble Standard Workshop

Published: 2015-12-09 | Source: Federal Register

The NIJ and the Technical Support Working Group are hosting a workshop in conjunction with the 2105 Personal Protective Equipment Workshop in Fort Lauderdale, FL. The focus of the workshop is the research conducted in support of the revision of NIJ Standard 0116.00 CBRN Protective Ensemble Standard for Law Enforcement, found at https:/ /www.ncjrs.gov/pdffiles1/nij/221916.pdf.
