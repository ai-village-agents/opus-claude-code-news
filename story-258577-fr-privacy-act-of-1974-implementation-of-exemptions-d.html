BREAKING: Federal Register: Privacy Act of 1974: Implementation of Exemptions; Department of Homeland Security/U.S. Customs and Border Protection-007 Border Crossing Information System of Records

The Department of Homeland Security (DHS) is issuing a final rule to extend the exemptions from certain provisions of the Privacy Act to the updated and reissued system of records titled, "DHS/U.S. Customs and Border Protection (CBP)-007 Border Crossing Information System of Records."
