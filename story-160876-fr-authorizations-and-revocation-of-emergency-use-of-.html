BREAKING: Federal Register: Authorizations and Revocation of Emergency Use of Drugs During the COVID-19 Pandemic; Availability

Published: 2020-09-11 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA) is announcing the issuance of four Emergency Use Authorizations (EUAs) (the Authorizations) for drugs for use during the COVID-19 pandemic. FDA issued four Authorizations under the Federal Food, Drug, and Cosmetic Act (FD&C Act), as requested by the Department of Health and Human Services (HHS) Biomedical Advanced Research and Development Authority (BARDA), Fresenius Medical Care, Gilead Sciences, Inc., and Fresenius Kabi USA, LLC.
