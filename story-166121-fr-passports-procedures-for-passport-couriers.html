BREAKING: Federal Register: Passports; Procedures for Passport Couriers

Published: 2020-11-17 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of State (the Department) proposes regulations to continue a registration program and hand delivery procedures for courier companies used by applicants to transport their passport applications, and U.S. passports issued to them, to and from participating passport agencies.
