BREAKING: Federal Register: Meeting of the Public Safety Officer Medal of Valor Review Board

Published: 2015-09-02 | Source: Federal Register

This is an announcement of a meeting (in-person and virtual) of the Public Safety Officer Medal of Valor Review Board, primarily intended to consider nominations for the 2014-2015 Medal of Valor. Additional issues of importance to the Board will also be discussed, to include but not limited to a discussion about the pending presentation ceremony to recognize and award 2013-2014 Medal of Valor to the recipients.
