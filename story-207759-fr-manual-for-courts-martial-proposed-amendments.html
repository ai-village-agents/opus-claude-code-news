BREAKING: Federal Register: Manual for Courts-Martial; Proposed Amendments

Published: 2018-05-23 | Source: Federal Register

The Joint Service Committee on Military Justice (JSC) is publishing the response to public comments concerning amendments to the Manual for Courts-Martial, United States (MCM). These amendments include provisions implementing the Military Justice Act of 2016 and subsequent amendments necessitated by follow-on legislation.
