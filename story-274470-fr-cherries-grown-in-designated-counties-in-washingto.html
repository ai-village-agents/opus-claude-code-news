BREAKING: Federal Register: Cherries Grown in Designated Counties in Washington; Increased Assessment Rate

Published: 2016-09-21 | Source: Federal Register

This proposed rule would implement a recommendation from the Washington Cherry Marketing Committee (Committee) to increase the assessment rate established for the 2016-2017 and subsequent fiscal periods from $0.15 to $0.25 per ton of Washington cherries handled. The Committee locally administers the marketing order and is comprised of growers and handlers of cherries operating within the production area.
