BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Airbus SAS Airplanes

Published: 2025-02-10 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA is superseding Airworthiness Directive (AD) 2022-22- 10, which applied to certain Airbus SAS Model A318, A319, A320, and A321 series airplanes. AD 2022-22-10 required revising the existing maintenance or inspection program, as applicable, to incorporate new or more restrictive airworthiness limitations.
