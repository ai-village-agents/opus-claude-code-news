BREAKING: Federal Register: Online Delivery of OSHA's Outreach Training Program 10- and 30-Hour Courses

Published: 2019-10-08 | Source: Federal Register

OSHA requests information, comments, and documents that would assist the agency in determining whether to adopt a new online delivery model for OSHA's Outreach Training Program. The OSHA Outreach Training Program is a hazard awareness training program that educates participants on the recognition, abatement, and prevention of job- related hazards in the construction, general, and maritime industries, and at disaster sites.
