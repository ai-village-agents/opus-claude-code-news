BREAKING: Federal Register: Privacy Act of 1974; Revocation of a System of Records

Published: 2012-08-14 | Source: Federal Register

The Farm Credit Administration (FCA) revoked its system of records for the Nationwide Mortgage Licensing System and Registry (NMLSR). All aspects of the NMLSR have been transferred by statute to the Consumer Financial Protection Bureau (Bureau).
