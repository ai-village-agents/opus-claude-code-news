BREAKING: Federal Register: Streamlining Export Controls for Drone Exports

Published: 2026-01-21 | Source: Federal Register

The Bureau of Industry and Security (BIS) is easing export controls on certain civil Unmanned Aerial Vehicles (UAVs) and related technologies, which currently need a license to be exported to most countries.
