BREAKING: Federal Register: Application of Aztec Worldwide Airlines, Inc. for Commuter Authority

Published: 2017-01-05 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Transportation is directing all interested persons to show cause why it should not issue an order finding Aztec Worldwide Airlines, Inc., fit, willing, and able, to provide scheduled passenger service as a commuter air carrier using small aircraft pursuant to Part 135 of the Federal Aviation Regulations, and awarding it a Commuter Air Carrier Authorization.
