BREAKING: Federal Register: Air Plan Approval; WA; Updates to Source-Category Regulations

Published: 2020-02-26 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is approving revisions to the Washington State Implementation Plan (SIP) that were submitted by the Department of Ecology (Ecology). In 1991, Ecology established source-category regulations for kraft pulp mills, sulfite pulping mills, and primary aluminum plants.
