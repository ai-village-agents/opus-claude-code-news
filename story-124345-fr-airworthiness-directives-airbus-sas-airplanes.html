BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Airbus SAS Airplanes

Published: 2021-05-21 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA proposes to supersede Airworthiness Directive (AD) 2020-21-05, which applies to all Airbus SAS Model A330-200 Freighter, A330-200, A330-300, A330-900, A340-200, A340-300, A340-500, and A340- 600 series airplanes.
