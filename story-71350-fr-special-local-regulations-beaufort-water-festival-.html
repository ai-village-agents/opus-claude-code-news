BREAKING: Federal Register: Special Local Regulations; Beaufort Water Festival and Air Show, Beaufort, SC

Published: 2023-07-06 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard will enforce special local regulations at various locations in Captain of the Port Charleston from July 14, 2023, to July 22, 2023. Our regulation for marine events within the Captain of the Port (COTP) Charleston identifies the regulated areas for these annual events in South Carolina. This action is necessary to provide for the safety of life on these navigable waters during marine events.
