BREAKING: Federal Register: Inline XBRL Filing of Tagged Data

Published: 2018-08-16 | Source: Federal Register

We are adopting amendments to require the use of the Inline eXtensible Business Reporting Language ("XBRL") format for the submission of operating company financial statement information and fund risk/return summary information. We also are adopting the elimination of the 15 business day XBRL filing period for fund risk/ return summaries.
