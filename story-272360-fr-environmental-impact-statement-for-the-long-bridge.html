BREAKING: Federal Register: Environmental Impact Statement for the Long Bridge Project in Washington, DC

Published: 2016-08-26 | Source: Federal Register

FRA announces its intent to prepare an EIS for the Long Bridge Project jointly with the District Department of Transportation (DDOT). The Long Bridge Project (Proposed Action) consists of potential improvements to bridge and related railroad infrastructure located between the Virginia Railway Express (VRE) Crystal City Station in Arlington, Virginia and Control Point (CP) Virginia in Washington, DC.
