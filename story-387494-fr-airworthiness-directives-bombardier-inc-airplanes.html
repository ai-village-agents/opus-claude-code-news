BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Bombardier Inc. Airplanes

Published: 2012-06-04 | Source: Federal Register

We are superseding an existing airworthiness directive (AD) for certain Bombardier Inc. Model CL-215-1A10 and CL-215-6B11 (CL-215T Variant) airplanes. That AD currently requires repetitive inspections to detect cracking of the lower cap of the wing front and rear spars at wing station (WS) 51.00, and the wing lower skin.
