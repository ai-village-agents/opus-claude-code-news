BREAKING: Federal Register: Compliance Policy Guide Regarding Food Facility Registration-Human and Animal Food; Availability

Published: 2014-06-03 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA or we) is announcing the availability of Compliance Policy Guide Sec. 100.250 Food Facility Registration--Human and Animal Food (the CPG). The CPG provides guidance for our staff on enforcement of food facility registration requirements.
