BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone; Recurring Marine Events, Sector Key West, Florida

Published: 2018-04-17 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is establishing moving safety zones for certain waters within the Sector Key West Captain of the Port (COTP) Zone for five annually recurring marine events. This action is necessary to provide for the safety of the participants, participant vessels, and the general public on the navigable waters of the United States during these events.
