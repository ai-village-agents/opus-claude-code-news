BREAKING: Federal Register: Establishment of Class E Airspace, and Amendment of Class D and Class E Airspace; Prescott, AZ

Published: 2015-03-13 | Source: Federal Register

This action establishes Class E airspace and modifies Class D and Class E surface area airspace at Prescott, AZ, to accommodate aircraft departing and arriving under Instrument Flight Rules (IFR) at Ernest A. Love Field. New Area Navigation (RNAV) Global Positioning System (GPS) standard instrument approach procedures have made this action necessary for the safety and management of IFR operations at the airport.
