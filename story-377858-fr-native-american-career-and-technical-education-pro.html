BREAKING: Federal Register: Native American Career and Technical Education Program; Proposed Waivers and Extension of the Project Period; CFDA Number 84.101A

Published: 2012-02-16 | Source: Federal Register

For 60-month projects funded in fiscal year (FY) 2007 under the Native American Career and Technical Education Program (NACTEP), the Secretary proposes to waive the regulations that generally restrict project periods to 60 months and that restrict project period extensions involving the obligation of additional Federal funds.
