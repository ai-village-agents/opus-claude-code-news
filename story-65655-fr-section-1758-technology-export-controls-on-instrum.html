BREAKING: Federal Register: Section 1758 Technology Export Controls on Instruments for the Automated Chemical Synthesis of Peptides

Published: 2023-04-20 | Source: Federal Register

The Bureau of Industry and Security (BIS), maintains controls on the export, reexport and transfer (in-country) of dual-use items and less sensitive military items pursuant to the Export Administration Regulations (EAR). Certain instruments for the automated synthesis of peptides (automated peptide synthesizers) have been identified by BIS as a Section 1758 emerging and foundational technology.
