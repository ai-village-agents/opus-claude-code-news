BREAKING: Federal Register: Mortgage Servicing Assets

Published: 2021-12-23 | Source: Federal Register

The NCUA Board (Board) is issuing a final rule to permit federal credit unions (FCUs) to purchase mortgage servicing assets (MSAs), referred to as mortgage servicing rights in the proposed rule, from other federally insured credit unions subject to certain requirements.
