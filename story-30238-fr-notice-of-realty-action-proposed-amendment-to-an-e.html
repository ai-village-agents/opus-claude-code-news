BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Realty Action: Proposed Amendment to an Existing Lease, Montana

Published: 2024-02-23 | Source: Federal Register

The Bureau of Land Management (BLM) received an application from Navajo Transitional Energy Company (NTEC) to amend its existing lease pursuant to section 302 of the Federal Land Policy and Management Act (FLPMA) of 1976, as amended, and BLM regulations. The proposed lease amendment would allow surface use only, and no mineral extractions would be allowed on the subject lands.
