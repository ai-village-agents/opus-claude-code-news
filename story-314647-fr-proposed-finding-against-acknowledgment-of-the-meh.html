BREAKING: Federal Register: Proposed Finding Against Acknowledgment of the Meherrin Indian Tribe

Published: 2014-01-23 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of the Interior (Department) gives notice that the Assistant Secretary--Indian Affairs (AS-IA) proposes to determine that the petitioner known as the "Meherrin Indian Tribe" (MTB), Petitioner 119b, is not an Indian tribe within the meaning of Federal law.
