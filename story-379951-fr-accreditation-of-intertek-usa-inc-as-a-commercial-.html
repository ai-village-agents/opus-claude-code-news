BREAKING: Federal Register: Accreditation of Intertek USA, Inc., as a Commercial Laboratory

Published: 2012-03-09 | Source: Federal Register

Notice is hereby given that, pursuant to 19 CFR 151.12, Intertek USA, Inc., 15602 Jacintoport Blvd. Stolthaven Terminal, Houston, TX 77015, has been accredited to test petroleum, petroleum products, organic chemicals and vegetable oils for customs purposes, in accordance with the provisions of 19 CFR 151.12.
