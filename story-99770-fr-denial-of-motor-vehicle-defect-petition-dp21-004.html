BREAKING: Federal Register: Denial of Motor Vehicle Defect Petition, DP21-004

Published: 2022-07-05 | Source: Federal Register

This notice sets forth the reasons for the denial of a petition submitted to NHTSA on September 14, 2021, requesting that the agency investigate whether a defect related to motor vehicle safety exists in van-type or box semi-trailers due to a lack of side underride guards. On November 17, 2021, NHTSA opened Defect Petition DP21-004 to evaluate petitioners' request.
