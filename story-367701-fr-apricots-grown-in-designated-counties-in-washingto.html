BREAKING: Federal Register: Apricots Grown in Designated Counties in Washington; Suspension of Handling Regulations

Published: 2013-10-23 | Source: Federal Register

This rule suspends the minimum grade, size, quality, maturity, and inspection requirements prescribed under the Washington apricot marketing order (order) for the remainder of the 2013-2014 fiscal period and subsequent fiscal periods. The order regulates the handling of apricots grown in designated counties in Washington and is administered locally by the Washington Apricot Marketing Committee (Committee).
