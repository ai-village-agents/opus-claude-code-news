BREAKING: Federal Register: Rural Call Completion

Published: 2018-05-10 | Source: Federal Register

In this document, the Commission reorients its existing rural call completion rules to better reflect strategies that have worked to reduce rural call completion problems while at the same time reducing the overall burden of its rules on providers.
