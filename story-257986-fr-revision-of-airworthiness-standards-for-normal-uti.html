BREAKING: Federal Register: Revision of Airworthiness Standards for Normal, Utility, Acrobatic, and Commuter Category Airplanes

Published: 2016-03-14 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA proposes to amend its airworthiness standards for normal, utility, acrobatic, and commuter category airplanes by removing current prescriptive design requirements and replacing them with performance-based airworthiness standards.
