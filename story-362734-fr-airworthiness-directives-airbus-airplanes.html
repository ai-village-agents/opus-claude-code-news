BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Airbus Airplanes

Published: 2013-08-13 | Source: Federal Register

We propose to supersede airworthiness directive (AD) 2011-14- 06 that applies to all Airbus Model A318, A319, A320, and A321 series airplanes. That AD currently requires revising the maintenance program. Since we issued that AD, we have determined that more restrictive limitations are necessary. This proposed AD would require revising the maintenance program to incorporate new limitations.
