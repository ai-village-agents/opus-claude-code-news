BREAKING: Federal Register: Cattle Fever Tick; Importation Requirements for Ruminants From Mexico

Published: 2015-02-26 | Source: Federal Register

We are amending the regulations to recognize the State of Sonora as a region in Mexico that is free of fever ticks. We are also establishing an exemption from acaricide dipping treatment requirements, and the documentation requirements associated with such dipping, that were formerly applicable to cattle and other ruminants originating from Sonora as a condition of eligibility for entry to the United States, provided that certain conditions are met.
