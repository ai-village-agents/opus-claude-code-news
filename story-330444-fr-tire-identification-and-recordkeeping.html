BREAKING: Federal Register: Tire Identification and Recordkeeping

Published: 2014-07-24 | Source: Federal Register

The tire identification number (TIN), which must appear on virtually all new and retreaded motor vehicle tires sold in the United States, plays an important role in identifying which tires are subject to recall and remedy campaigns for safety defects and noncompliances. This document proposes two amendments to the TIN.
