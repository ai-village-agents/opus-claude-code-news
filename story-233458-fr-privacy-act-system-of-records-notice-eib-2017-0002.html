BREAKING: Federal Register: Privacy Act System of Records Notice; EIB 2017-0002-Federal Personnel and Payroll System (FPPS)

Published: 2017-05-04 | Source: Federal Register

The Export-Import Bank of the United States (EXIM Bank) proposes to add a new electronic system of records to coincide with migrating its personnel and payroll administration to the Department of Interior (DOI) Interior Business Center's (IBC) Federal Personnel and Payroll Systems (FPPS) and Time and Attendance system known as Quicktime, which are subject to the Privacy Act of 1974 (5 U.S.C. 522a), as amended.
