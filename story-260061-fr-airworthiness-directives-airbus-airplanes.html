BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Airbus Airplanes

Published: 2016-04-05 | Source: Federal Register

We are adopting a new airworthiness directive (AD) for certain Airbus Model A318, A319, A320, and A321 series airplanes. This AD was prompted by a report that, during the assembly process, several gaps between the two parts of the girt bar fittings for the aft passenger doors were found to exceed tolerances.
