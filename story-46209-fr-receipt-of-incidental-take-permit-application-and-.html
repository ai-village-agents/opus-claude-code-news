BREAKING: Federal Register: Receipt of Incidental Take Permit Application and Proposed Habitat Conservation Plan for the Eastern Indigo Snake; Turner County, GA; Categorical Exclusion

Published: 2024-09-06 | Source: Federal Register

We, the Fish and Wildlife Service (Service), announce receipt of an application from Double Run Solar, LLC (applicant) for an incidental take permit (ITP) under the Endangered Species Act. The applicant requests the ITP to take the federally listed threatened Eastern indigo snake incidental to the construction and operation of a solar facility in Turner County, Georgia.
