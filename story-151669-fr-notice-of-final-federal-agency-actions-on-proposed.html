BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Final Federal Agency Actions on Proposed Highway Project in Rhode Island

Published: 2020-05-15 | Source: Federal Register

This notice announces actions taken by the FHWA that are final pursuant to the statute. The actions relate to a proposed highway project, Reconstruction of the Pell Bridge Approaches in the City of Newport in the State of Rhode Island.
