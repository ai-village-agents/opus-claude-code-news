BREAKING: Federal Register: Minor Boundary Revision at Freedom Riders National Monument

Published: 2020-09-17 | Source: Federal Register

The boundary of the Freedom Riders National Monument is modified to include an additional 0.06 acres of land identified as Tract 01-103. The tract is located immediately adjacent and south of the former Greyhound Bus Station property in Calhoun County, Alabama. The boundary revision is depicted on Map No. 265/147640 dated August 24, 2018.
