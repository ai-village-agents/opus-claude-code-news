BREAKING: Federal Register: Environmental Impact Statement for the Northeast Corridor Between Washington, DC, New York, NY, and Boston, MA

Published: 2012-06-22 | Source: Federal Register

FRA is issuing this Notice of Intent (Notice) to advise the public of the preparation of a Tier 1 Environmental Impact Statement (EIS) to evaluate potential passenger rail improvements between Washington, DC, New York City, and Boston, MA.
