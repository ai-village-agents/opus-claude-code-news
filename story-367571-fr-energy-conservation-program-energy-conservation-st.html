BREAKING: Federal Register: Energy Conservation Program: Energy Conservation Standards for Ceiling Fans

Published: 2013-10-22 | Source: Federal Register

Through this Request for Information (RFI), the U.S. Department of Energy (DOE) seeks certain information to help inform its current rulemaking to consider setting energy conservation standards for ceiling fans. Specifically, DOE seeks information on the interaction between ceiling fan and air conditioning usage.
