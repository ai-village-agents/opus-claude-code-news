BREAKING: Federal Register: Agency Information Collection Activities: Submission for OMB review; Comment Request; The Declaration Process: Requests for Damage Assessment, Federal Disaster Assistance, Appeals, Cost Share Adjustment

Published: 2012-05-15 | Source: Federal Register

The Federal Emergency Management Agency (FEMA) is submitting a request for review and approval of a collection of information under the emergency processing procedures in Office of Management and Budget (OMB) regulation 5 CFR 1320.13. FEMA is requesting that this information collection be approved by June 1, 2012. The approval will authorize FEMA to use the collection through December 1, 2012.
