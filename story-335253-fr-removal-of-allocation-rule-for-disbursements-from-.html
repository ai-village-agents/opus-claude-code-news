BREAKING: Federal Register: Removal of Allocation Rule for Disbursements From Designated Roth Accounts to Multiple Destinations

Published: 2014-09-19 | Source: Federal Register

This document contains proposed amendments to the regulations that address the tax treatment of distributions from designated Roth accounts under tax-favored retirement plans.
