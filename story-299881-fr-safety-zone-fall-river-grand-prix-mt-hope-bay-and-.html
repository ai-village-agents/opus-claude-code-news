BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone, Fall River Grand Prix, Mt. Hope Bay and Taunton River, Fall River, MA

Published: 2015-07-27 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is establishing a safety zone in the navigable waters of Mt. Hope Bay and the Taunton River in the vicinity of Fall River, MA, during the Fall River Grand Prix marine event from August 14-16, 2015. This safety zone is intended to safeguard mariners from the hazards associated with high-speed, high-performance motorboats competing in the event.
