BREAKING: Federal Register: Land Acquisitions; Snoqualmie Indian Tribe

Published: 2020-06-18 | Source: Federal Register

The Assistant Secretary--Indian Affairs has made a final determination to acquire 16.63 acres, more or less, into trust for the Snoqualmie Indian Tribe on March 18, 2020.
