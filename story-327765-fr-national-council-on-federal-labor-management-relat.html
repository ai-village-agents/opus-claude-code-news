BREAKING: Federal Register: National Council on Federal Labor-Management Relations Meeting

Published: 2014-06-24 | Source: Federal Register

The National Council on Federal Labor-Management Relations meeting previously scheduled for Wednesday, July 16, 2014, and announced in 78 FR 77172 (December 20, 2013), has been rescheduled for Wednesday, July 23, 2014. The meeting will start at 10:00 a.m. EDT and will be held in Room 1350, U.S. Office of Personnel Management, 1900 E Street NW., Washington, DC 20415.
