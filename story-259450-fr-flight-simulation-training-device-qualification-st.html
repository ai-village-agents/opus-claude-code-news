BREAKING: Federal Register: Flight Simulation Training Device Qualification Standards for Extended Envelope and Adverse Weather Event Training Tasks

Published: 2016-03-30 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA has determined this rule is necessary to amend the Qualification Performance Standards for flight simulation training devices (FSTDs) for the primary purpose of improving existing technical standards and introducing new technical standards for full stall and stick pusher maneuvers, upset recognition and recovery maneuvers, maneuvers conducted in airborne icing conditions, takeoff and landing maneuvers in gusting crosswinds, and bounced landing recovery maneuvers.
