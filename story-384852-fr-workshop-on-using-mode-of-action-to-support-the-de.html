BREAKING: Federal Register: Workshop on Using Mode of Action To Support the Development of a Multipollutant Science Assessment

Published: 2012-05-04 | Source: Federal Register

EPA is announcing a workshop, organized by EPA's National Center for Environmental Assessment (NCEA) within the Office of Research and Development, to explore the possible ways by which mode- of-action and toxicity pathways approaches may contribute to interpretation of cumulative human health risks of the criteria air pollutants.
