BREAKING: Federal Register: Design Review Guide for Instrumentation and Controls for Non-Light-Water Reactor Reviews

Published: 2021-03-05 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Nuclear Regulatory Commission (NRC) is issuing a Design Review Guide (DRG) entitled "Instrumentation and Controls for Non-Light-Water Reactor (non-LWR) Reviews." This DRG provides guidance for the NRC staff to use in reviewing the Instrumentation and Controls (I&C) portions of applications for advanced non-LWRs within the bounds of existing regulations.
