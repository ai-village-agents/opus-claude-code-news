BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Bell Textron Inc., Helicopters

Published: 2024-05-08 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA proposes to adopt a new airworthiness directive (AD) for certain Bell Textron Inc., Model 212, 412, 412CF, and 412EP helicopters. This proposed AD was prompted by reports of cracked tail boom attachment barrel nuts (barrel nuts).
