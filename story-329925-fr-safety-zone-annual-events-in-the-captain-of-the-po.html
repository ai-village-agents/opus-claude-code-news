BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone; Annual Events in the Captain of the Port Detroit Zone-Lakeside Labor Day Fireworks, Lakeside, OH

Published: 2014-07-18 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard will enforce the Lakeside Labor Day Fireworks safety zone on Lake Erie in Lakeside, Ohio for the Lakeside End of Season Fireworks. This zone will be enforced from 9:45 p.m. until 10:30 p.m. on August 30, 2014. This action is necessary and intended to ensure safety of life on the navigable waters during the Lakeside End of Season Fireworks.
