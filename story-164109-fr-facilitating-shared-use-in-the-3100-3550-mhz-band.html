BREAKING: Federal Register: Facilitating Shared Use in the 3100-3550 MHz Band

Published: 2020-10-21 | Source: Federal Register

In this document, the Commission proposes rules to govern commercial wireless operations in the 3.45-3.55 GHz band. It proposes to add a new primary allocation for fixed and mobile (except aeronautical mobile) services and to adopt technical, licensing, and competitive bidding rules governing licenses in this band.
