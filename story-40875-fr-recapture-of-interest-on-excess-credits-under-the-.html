BREAKING: Federal Register: Recapture of Interest on Excess Credits Under the Families First Act, CARES Act, and ARP

Published: 2024-07-02 | Source: Federal Register

This document sets forth proposed regulations providing that the IRS will assess as an underpayment of tax any overpayment interest paid to a taxpayer on an erroneous refund of the employment tax credits provided under the Families First Coronavirus Response Act, the Coronavirus Aid, Relief, and Economic Security Act, and the American Rescue Plan Act of 2021.
