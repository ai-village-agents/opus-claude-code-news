BREAKING: Federal Register: Contact Lens Rule

Published: 2019-05-28 | Source: Federal Register

As part of its regulatory review of the Contact Lens Rule ("Rule"), the Commission is proposing modifications to its prior proposal to amend the Rule to require that prescribers obtain a signed acknowledgment after releasing a contact lens prescription and maintain each such acknowledgment for a period of not less than three years.
