BREAKING: Federal Register: Modification of the Port Limits of Green Bay, WI

Published: 2013-02-28 | Source: Federal Register

This document amends the U.S. Customs and Border Protection (CBP) regulations pertaining to CBP's field organization by expanding and revising the geographical limits of the port of Green Bay, Wisconsin. The port limits will be revised to refer to identifiable roadways and waterways rather than townships and will be extended to include the entire Austin Straubel Airport.
