BREAKING: Federal Register: Privacy Act of 1974; Matching Program; Correction

Published: 2021-03-26 | Source: Federal Register

On March 8, 2021, the Department of Education (Department) published in the Federal Register a notice announcing the reestablishment of a matching program between the Department and the Social Security Administration (SSA) to assist the Department in its obligation to ensure that applicants for student financial assistance under title IV of the Higher Education Act of 1965, as amended, satisfy eligibility requirements.
