BREAKING: Federal Register: Commerce in Firearms and Ammunition-Reporting Theft or Loss of Firearms in Transit (2007R-9P)

Published: 2016-01-12 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Justice is amending the regulations of the Bureau of Alcohol, Tobacco, Firearms, and Explosives (ATF) concerning the statutory reporting requirement for firearms that have been stolen or lost. The final rule specifies that when a Federal firearms licensee (FFL) discovers a firearm it shipped was stolen or lost in transit, the transferor/sender FFL must report the theft or loss to ATF and to the appropriate local authorities within 48 hours of discovery.
