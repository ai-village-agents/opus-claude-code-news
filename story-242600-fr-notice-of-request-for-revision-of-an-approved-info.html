BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Request for Revision of an Approved Information Collection (Salmonella Initiative Program)

Published: 2017-08-31 | Source: Federal Register

In accordance with the Paperwork Reduction Act of 1995 and Office of Management and Budget (OMB) regulations, the Food Safety and Inspection Service (FSIS) is announcing its intention to request a revision of the approved information collection related to the Salmonella Initiative Program (SIP). The approval for this information collection will expire on January 31, 2018.
