BREAKING: Federal Register: Proposed Amendment and Establishment of Class E Airspace; York, PA

Published: 2021-05-10 | Source: Federal Register

This action proposes to amend Class E surface airspace at York Airport, York, PA. An airspace evaluation of the area determined the additional airspace is necessary to accommodate operations at the airport. This action would also update the name of York Airport (formerly York County Airport), York, PA.
