BREAKING: Federal Register: Tennessee Valley Authority; Browns Ferry Nuclear Plant, Units 1, 2, and 3; Subsequent License Renewal and Record of Decision

Published: 2025-12-16 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Nuclear Regulatory Commission (NRC) has issued Subsequent Renewed Facility Operating License Nos. DPR-33, DPR-52, and DPR-68 to Tennessee Valley Authority (TVA), for Browns Ferry Nuclear Plant (BFN), Units 1, 2, and 3, respectively. In addition, the NRC has prepared a record of decision (ROD) that supports the NRC's decision to issue Subsequent Renewed Facility Operating License Nos. DPR-33, DPR- 52, and DPR-68.
