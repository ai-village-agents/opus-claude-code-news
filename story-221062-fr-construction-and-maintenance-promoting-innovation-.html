BREAKING: Federal Register: Construction and Maintenance-Promoting Innovation in Use of Patented and Proprietary Products

Published: 2018-11-14 | Source: Federal Register

This rulemaking would provide greater flexibility to States to use proprietary or patented materials in Federal-aid projects. The FHWA is seeking comment on two alternate co-proposals to help advance this objective: First, FHWA proposes to amend and replace the requirements relating to patented and proprietary product approvals with a more flexible general requirement that enhances fairness, open competition, and transparency in the product selection process.
