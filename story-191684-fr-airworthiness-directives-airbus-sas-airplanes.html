BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Airbus SAS Airplanes

Published: 2019-10-22 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA is adopting a new airworthiness directive (AD) for all Airbus SAS Model A330-243, -243F, -341, -342, and -343 airplanes. This AD was prompted by a determination that cracks can develop on the ripple damper weld of the hydraulic pressure tube assembly and reports of failure of the ripple damper of the hydraulic pressure tube assembly.
