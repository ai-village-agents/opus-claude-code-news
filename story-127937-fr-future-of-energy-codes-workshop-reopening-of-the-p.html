BREAKING: Federal Register: Future of Energy Codes Workshop; Reopening of the Public Comment Period

Published: 2021-07-09 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Department of Energy (DOE) is reopening the public comment period for the request for public comments on its public stakeholder workshop on the Future of Energy Codes held June 22 and 24, 2021. DOE published notice of the workshop on June 14, 2021 and requested comments by July 8, 2021.
