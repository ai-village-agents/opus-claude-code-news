BREAKING: Federal Register: Second Meeting of the Advisory Committee for the 2015 World Radiocommunication Conference

Published: 2012-09-12 | Source: Federal Register

In accordance with the Federal Advisory Committee Act, this notice advises interested persons that the second meeting of the WRC-15 Advisory Committee will be held on October 1, 2012, at the Federal Communications Commission. The Advisory Committee will consider any preliminary views introduced by the Advisory Committee's Informal Working Groups.
