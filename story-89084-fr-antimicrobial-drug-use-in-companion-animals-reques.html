BREAKING: Federal Register: Antimicrobial Drug Use in Companion Animals; Request for Comments

Published: 2022-02-16 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA, Agency, or we) is soliciting comments from the public on antimicrobial drug use practices in companion animals and the potential impacts of such uses on antimicrobial resistance in both humans and animals. We are issuing this notice as part of our objective to engage with our stakeholders to develop and implement a strategy for promoting antimicrobial stewardship in companion animals.
