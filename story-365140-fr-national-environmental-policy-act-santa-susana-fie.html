BREAKING: Federal Register: National Environmental Policy Act; Santa Susana Field Laboratory

Published: 2013-09-11 | Source: Federal Register

A Notice of Availability (NOA) for the DEIS for Demolition and Cleanup Activities for the NASA-administered portion of the Santa Susana Field Laboratory was published in the Federal Register by the U.S. Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) on August 2, 2013, Vol. 78, No. 149, page 46940. NASA also published an NOA of the DEIS in the Federal Register on the same day (August 2, 2013, Vol. 78, No. 149, pages 47007-47009). The comment period for the DEIS was to end on September 16, 2013.
