BREAKING: Federal Register: Certain Outbound Property Transfers by Domestic Corporations; Certain Stock Distributions by Domestic Corporations

Published: 2013-03-19 | Source: Federal Register

This document contains final and temporary regulations that apply to transfers of certain property by a domestic corporation to a foreign corporation in certain nonrecognition exchanges, or to distributions of stock of certain foreign corporations by a domestic corporation in certain nonrecognition distributions.
