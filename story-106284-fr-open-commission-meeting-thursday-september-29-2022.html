BREAKING: Federal Register: Open Commission Meeting Thursday, September 29, 2022

Published: 2022-09-28 | Source: Federal Register

The Commission will consider a Second Report and Order that would adopt rules requiring low-Earth orbit space station operators planning disposal through uncontrolled atmospheric re-entry to complete disposal as soon as practicable, and no more than five years following the end of their mission. The Report and Order would also adopt a grandfathering period of two years and address the potential for waivers for certain types of research and scientific missions. 2..................
