BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone; South Timbalier Block 22, Gulf of Mexico, Port Fourchon, LA

Published: 2021-08-03 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is establishing a temporary safety zone for all navigable waters within a one nautical mile radius around a capsized vessel in the Gulf of Mexico, South Timbalier block 22, near Port Fourchon, LA. The temporary safety zone is needed to protect life and property during emergency salvage operations surrounding the capsized vessel.
