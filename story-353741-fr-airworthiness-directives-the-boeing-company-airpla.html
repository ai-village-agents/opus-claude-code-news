BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; The Boeing Company Airplanes

Published: 2013-05-01 | Source: Federal Register

We are adopting a new airworthiness directive (AD) for certain The Boeing Company Model 737-600, -700, -700C, -800, -900 and -900ER series airplanes. This AD was prompted by a report of leaking fuel from the wing leading edge area at the inboard end of the number 5 leading edge slat.
