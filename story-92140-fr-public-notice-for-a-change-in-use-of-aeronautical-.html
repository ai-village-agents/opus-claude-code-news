BREAKING: Federal Register: Public Notice for a Change in Use of Aeronautical Property at Lancaster Airport in Lititz, Pennsylvania

Published: 2022-03-28 | Source: Federal Register

The Federal Aviation Administration is requesting public comment on Lancaster Airport Authority's (LAA) request to change 5 acres of airport property from aeronautical use to non-aeronautical use. The parcel was purchased as part of a larger parcel with AIP grants 3-42-0049-022-2002 and 3-42-0049-023-2002.
