BREAKING: Federal Register: Approval of Intertek USA, Inc. as a Commercial Gauger; Wilmington, NC

Published: 2020-04-28 | Source: Federal Register

Notice is hereby given, pursuant to CBP regulations, that Intertek USA, Inc. (Wilmington, NC), has been approved to gauge petroleum and certain petroleum products for customs purposes for the next three years as of August 21, 2019.
