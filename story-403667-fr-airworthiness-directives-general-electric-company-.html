BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; General Electric Company Turbofan Engines

Published: 2012-12-13 | Source: Federal Register

We propose to adopt a new airworthiness directive (AD) for all General Electric Company (GE) CF34-8C and CF34-8E turbofan engines with certain part numbers (P/N) of operability bleed valves (OBV) installed. This proposed AD was prompted by three failure events of ring lock fuel fittings on the OBV. Two of those events led to an engine fire.
