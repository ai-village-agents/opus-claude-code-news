BREAKING: Federal Register: Home Mortgage Disclosure (Regulation C) Adjustment to Asset-Size Exemption Threshold

Published: 2019-01-31 | Source: Federal Register

The Bureau of Consumer Financial Protection (Bureau) is amending the official commentary that interprets the requirements of the Bureau's Regulation C (Home Mortgage Disclosure) to reflect the asset-size exemption threshold for banks, savings associations, and credit unions based on the annual percentage change in the average of the Consumer Price Index for Urban Wage Earners and Clerical Workers (CPI-W).
