BREAKING: Federal Register: International Affairs; Antarctic Marine Living Resources Convention Act

Published: 2024-03-21 | Source: Federal Register

This final rule sets forth changes to the Antarctic Marine Living Resources Convention Act of 1984 (AMLRCA) regulations, including those that implement the trade-monitoring program for frozen and fresh Dissostichus species, commonly marketed or referred to as Chilean seabass or Patagonian toothfish.
