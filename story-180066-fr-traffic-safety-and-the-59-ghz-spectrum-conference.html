BREAKING: Federal Register: Traffic Safety and the 5.9 GHz Spectrum Conference

Published: 2019-05-24 | Source: Federal Register

The purpose of this notice is to inform the public that the U.S. Department of Transportation (DOT) is planning to hold a conference entitled Traffic Safety and the 5.9 GHz Spectrum on June 3, 2019 to seek input regarding Vehicle-to-Everything (V2X) Communications.
