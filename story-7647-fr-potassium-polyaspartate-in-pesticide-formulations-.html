BREAKING: Federal Register: Potassium Polyaspartate in Pesticide Formulations; Exemption From the Requirement of a Tolerance

Published: 2025-03-20 | Source: Federal Register

This regulation establishes an exemption from the requirement of a tolerance for residues of potassium polyaspartate (CASRN 64723-18- 8) when used as an inert ingredient (complexing agent), at a maximum of 10% in formulation, pre-harvest. Rosen's Inc., submitted a petition to EPA under the Federal Food, Drug, and Cosmetic Act (FFDCA), requesting an exemption from the requirement of a tolerance.
