BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Standard for Stationary Activity Centers

Published: 2019-06-18 | Source: Federal Register

The Consumer Product Safety Improvement Act of 2008 (CPSIA) requires the United States Consumer Product Safety Commission (CPSC) to adopt safety standards for durable infant or toddler products. To comply with the CPSIA, the Commission is issuing a safety standard for stationary activity centers (SACs).
