BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Issuance of Final Determination Concerning a Certain Visitor Management System

Published: 2017-05-19 | Source: Federal Register

This document provides notice that U.S. Customs and Border Protection ("CBP") has issued a final determination concerning the country of origin of a certain visitor management system known as the Raptor Basic System.
