BREAKING: Federal Register: Proposed Amendment of Class D and Class E Airspace; Cheyenne, WY

Published: 2017-06-02 | Source: Federal Register

This action proposes to modify Class D airspace, Class E surface area airspace, Class E airspace extending upward from 700 feet above the surface, and Class E airspace extending upward from 1,200 feet above the surface at Cheyenne Regional/Jerry Olson Field Airport (formerly, Cheyenne Airport), Cheyenne, WY.
