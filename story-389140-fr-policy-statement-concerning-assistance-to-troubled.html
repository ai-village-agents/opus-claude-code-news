BREAKING: Federal Register: Policy Statement Concerning Assistance to Troubled Farm Credit System Institutions

Published: 2012-06-21 | Source: Federal Register

The Farm Credit System Insurance Corporation (Corporation or FCSIC) is publishing for comment a draft Policy Statement Concerning Assistance to Troubled Farm Credit System (System) Institutions to replace the Corporation's present Policy Statement Concerning Stand- Alone Assistance. The draft revised policy statement provides additional transparency concerning the Corporation's authority to provide assistance and how the least-cost test might be performed.
