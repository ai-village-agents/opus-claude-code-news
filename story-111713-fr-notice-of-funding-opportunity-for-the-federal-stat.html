BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Funding Opportunity for the Federal-State Partnership for Intercity Passenger Rail Program

Published: 2022-12-07 | Source: Federal Register

This notice (FSP-National) details the application requirements and procedures to obtain grant funding for projects not located on the Northeast Corridor under the Federal-State Partnership for Intercity Passenger Rail Program (FSP Program) for Fiscal Year 2022. The FSP-National notice solicits applications for FSP funds made available by the Consolidated Appropriations Act, 2022, and the Infrastructure Investment and Jobs Act.
