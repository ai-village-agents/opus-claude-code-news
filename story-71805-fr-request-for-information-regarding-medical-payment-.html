BREAKING: Federal Register: Request for Information Regarding Medical Payment Products

Published: 2023-07-12 | Source: Federal Register

The CFPB, an independent agency, HHS, and the Treasury (collectively, the agencies), are soliciting comments from the public and interested parties on medical credit cards, loans, and other financial products used to pay for health care. The agencies seek to understand the prevalence, nature, and impact of these products, including disparities across different demographic groups.
