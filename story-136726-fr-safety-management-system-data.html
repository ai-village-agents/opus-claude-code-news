BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Management System Data

Published: 2021-10-29 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA is proposing to designate certain reports, data, and information created as part of the development and implementation of safety management systems (SMS) as protected information when the information is voluntarily provided to the agency. Protected information generally is not subject to public disclosure.
