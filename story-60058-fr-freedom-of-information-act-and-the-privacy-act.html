BREAKING: Federal Register: Freedom of Information Act and the Privacy Act

Published: 2023-02-08 | Source: Federal Register

The Office of the Intellectual Property Enforcement Coordinator (IPEC) is issuing its implementing regulations for the Freedom of Information Act (FOIA) and the Privacy Act. The final rule describes how to make a FOIA request with IPEC and how IPEC processes requests for records. The final rule also states IPEC's Privacy Act Policies and Procedures.
