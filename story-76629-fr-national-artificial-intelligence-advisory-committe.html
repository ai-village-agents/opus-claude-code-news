BREAKING: Federal Register: National Artificial Intelligence Advisory Committee

Published: 2023-09-12 | Source: Federal Register

The National Institute of Standards and Technology (NIST) announces that the National Artificial Intelligence Advisory Committee (NAIAC or Committee) will hold a virtual briefing session. This session will be held on Friday, September 29, 2023 from 12:00 p.m. to 1:00 p.m. Eastern Time. The purpose of this session is for invited experts to brief the Committee on topics of interest related to the Committee's year two efforts.
