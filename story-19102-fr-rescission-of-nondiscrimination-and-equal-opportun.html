BREAKING: Federal Register: Rescission of Nondiscrimination and Equal-Opportunity Provisions of the Workforce Investment Act

Published: 2025-08-28 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Department of Labor (Department) is confirming the effective date of September 2, 2025, for the direct final rule (DFR) that was published in the Federal Register on July 1, 2025. This DFR rescinds the regulations that implemented the nondiscrimination and equal opportunity provisions of the Workforce Investment Act (WIA).
