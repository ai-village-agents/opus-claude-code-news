BREAKING: Federal Register: Consolidated Service Rules for the 758-769 and 788-799 MHz Bands

Published: 2014-01-06 | Source: Federal Register

The Federal Communications Commission (Commission) adopts a Second Report and Order that establishes consolidated service rules for the 758-769 and 788-799 MHz bands, the 700 MHz spectrum licensed to the First Responder Network Authority (FirstNet) for purposes of establishing a nationwide public safety broadband network.
