BREAKING: Federal Register: National Oceanic and Atmospheric Administration Hydrographic Services Review Panel Meeting

Published: 2020-02-28 | Source: Federal Register

The Hydrographic Services Review Panel (HSRP) will hold a meeting that will be open to the public, April 28-30, 2020, in Oahu, Hawaii. Public comments are requested in advance and/or during the meeting. Information about the HSRP meeting, agenda, presentations, webinar registration, and background documents will be posted and updated online.
