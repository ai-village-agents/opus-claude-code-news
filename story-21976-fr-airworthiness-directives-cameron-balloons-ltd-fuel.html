BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Cameron Balloons Ltd. Fuel Cylinders

Published: 2025-10-10 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA is superseding Airworthiness Directive (AD) 2025-11- 10, which applied to Cameron Balloons Ltd. (Cameron) fuel cylinders fitted with certain pressure relief valve (PRV) adaptors on hot air balloons. AD 2025-11-10 required repetitively visually checking the PRV adaptor for cracks and removing any fuel cylinder with a cracked PRV adaptor from service.
