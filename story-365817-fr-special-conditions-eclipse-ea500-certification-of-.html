BREAKING: Federal Register: Special Conditions: Eclipse, EA500, Certification of Autothrottle Functions

Published: 2013-09-19 | Source: Federal Register

These special conditions are issued for the Eclipse EA500 airplane. This airplane as modified by Innovative Solutions and Support (IS&S) will have a novel or unusual design feature(s) associated with the autothrottle system (ATS). The applicable airworthiness regulations do not contain adequate or appropriate safety standards for this design feature.
