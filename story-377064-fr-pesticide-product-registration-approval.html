BREAKING: Federal Register: Pesticide Product Registration Approval

Published: 2012-02-08 | Source: Federal Register

This notice announces Agency approval of an application to register the pesticide product Contram ST-1 containing an active ingredient not included in any previously registered products pursuant to the provisions of section 3(c)(5) of the Federal Insecticide, Fungicide, and Rodenticide Act (FIFRA), as amended.
