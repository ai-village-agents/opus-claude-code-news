BREAKING: Federal Register: Federal Acquisition Regulation: Use of Project Labor Agreements for Federal Construction Projects

Published: 2022-08-19 | Source: Federal Register

DoD, GSA, and NASA are proposing to amend the Federal Acquisition Regulation (FAR) to implement an Executive Order pertaining to project labor agreements in Federal construction projects.

Agency: Defense Department, General Services Administration, National Aeronautics and Space Administration
