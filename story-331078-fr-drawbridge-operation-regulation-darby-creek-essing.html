BREAKING: Federal Register: Drawbridge Operation Regulation; Darby Creek, Essington, PA

Published: 2014-08-01 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard proposes to change the operating regulation that governs the Conrail railroad bridge over Darby Creek in Essington, PA. The bridge owner, Conrail, is modifying the existing remote operating system which controls the bridge operations. Cameras will be installed and the remote operating site will move from its current location in Delair, NJ to Mt. Laurel, NJ.
