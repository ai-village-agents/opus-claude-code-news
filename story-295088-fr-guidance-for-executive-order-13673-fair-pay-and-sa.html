BREAKING: Federal Register: Guidance for Executive Order 13673, “Fair Pay and Safe Workplaces”

Published: 2015-05-28 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Labor is proposing guidance to assist federal agencies in the implementation of Executive Order 13673, Fair Pay and Safe Workplaces (the Order). The Order was signed by President Barack Obama on July 31, 2014, and it contains several new requirements designed to improve the federal contracting process.
