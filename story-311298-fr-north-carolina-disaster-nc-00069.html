BREAKING: Federal Register: North Carolina Disaster #NC-00069

Published: 2015-12-10 | Source: Federal Register

This is a Notice that the U.S. Small Business Administration is accepting applications for disaster loans from the State of North Carolina. Incident: Hurricane Sandy Reopening. Incident Period: 10/27/2012 through 10/30/2012. Effective Date: 12/02/2015. Physical Loan Application Deadline Date: 12/01/2016. Economic Injury (EIDL) Loan Application Deadline Date: 12/01/2016.
