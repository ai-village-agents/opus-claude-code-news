BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Embraer S.A. Airplanes

Published: 2019-07-01 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA proposes to supersede Airworthiness Directive (AD) 2017-06-08, which applies to certain Embraer S.A. Model ERJ 170-100 LR, -100 STD, -100 SE, and -100 SU airplanes; and Model ERJ 170-200 LR, - 200 SU, and -200 STD airplanes. AD 2017-06-08 requires revising the existing maintenance or inspection program, as applicable, to incorporate more restrictive airworthiness limitations.
