BREAKING: Federal Register: NPDES Electronic Reporting Rule Implementation Guidance

Published: 2016-09-09 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) recently promulgated the NPDES Electronic Reporting Rule ("final rule") to modernize Clean Water Act reporting for municipalities, industries, and other facilities by converting to an electronic data reporting system.
