BREAKING: Federal Register: Endangered and Threatened Wildlife and Plants; Listing the Hyacinth Macaw

Published: 2016-11-28 | Source: Federal Register

We, the U.S. Fish and Wildlife Service, notify the public that we are making changes to our July 6, 2012, proposed rule to list the hyacinth macaw (Anodorhynchus hyacinthinus) as an endangered species under the Endangered Species Act of 1973, as amended (Act). Based on new information, we now propose to list the hyacinth macaw as a threatened species under the Act. We also propose a concurrent rule under section 4(d) of the Act for this species.
