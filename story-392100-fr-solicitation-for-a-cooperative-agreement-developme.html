BREAKING: Federal Register: Solicitation for a Cooperative Agreement-Development of a 21st Century Corrections Learning Professional Competency Model

Published: 2012-07-26 | Source: Federal Register

The National Institute of Corrections (NIC) is soliciting proposals from organizations, groups, or individuals to enter into a cooperative agreement in a twelve (12) month project period for the development of a Corrections Learning Professional Competency Model. This project will identify the workplace learning competencies needed by correctional learning/training professionals at different organizational levels.
