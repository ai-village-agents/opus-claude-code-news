BREAKING: Federal Register: Limited Extension of Select Compliance Dates for Occupational Exposure to Beryllium in General Industry

Published: 2018-08-09 | Source: Federal Register

With this final rule, OSHA is extending the compliance date for certain ancillary requirements of the general industry beryllium standard to December 12, 2018. This standard protects workers from the hazards of beryllium exposure.
