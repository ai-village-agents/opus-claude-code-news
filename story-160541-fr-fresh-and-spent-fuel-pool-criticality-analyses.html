BREAKING: Federal Register: Fresh and Spent Fuel Pool Criticality Analyses

Published: 2020-09-08 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Nuclear Regulatory Commission (NRC) is issuing for public comment draft regulatory guide (DG), DG-1373, "Fresh and Spent Fuel Pool Criticality Analyses." This draft guide, if finalized as a new regulatory guide (RG), would update and supersede Interim Staff Guidance DSS-ISG-2010-01, "Staff Guidance Regarding the Nuclear Criticality Safety Analysis for Spent Fuel Pools."
