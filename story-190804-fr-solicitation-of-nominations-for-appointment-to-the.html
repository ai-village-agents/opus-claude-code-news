BREAKING: Federal Register: Solicitation of Nominations for Appointment to the Youth Access to American Jobs in Aviation Task Force

Published: 2019-10-09 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA is issuing this notice to solicit nominations for qualified candidates to serve on the Youth Access to American Jobs in Aviation Task Force (Task Force). Section 602, Youth Access to American Jobs in Aviation Task Force, of FAA Reauthorization Act of 2018, requires the FAA Administrator to establish a Task Force.
