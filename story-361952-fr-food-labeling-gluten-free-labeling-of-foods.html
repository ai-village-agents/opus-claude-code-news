BREAKING: Federal Register: Food Labeling; Gluten-Free Labeling of Foods

Published: 2013-08-05 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA or we) is issuing a final rule to define the term "gluten-free" for voluntary use in the labeling of foods.
