BREAKING: Federal Register: Agreement for a Social Impact Partnership Project

Published: 2021-04-20 | Source: Federal Register

In accordance with the Social Impact Partnerships to Pay for Results Act ("SIPPRA"), the U.S. Department of the Treasury ("Treasury"), the U.S. Department of Labor ("DOL"), and the New York State Energy and Research Development Authority ("NYSERDA") have entered into an agreement for a social impact partnership project (the "Project Grant Agreement").
