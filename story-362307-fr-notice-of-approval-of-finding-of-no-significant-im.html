BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Approval of Finding of No Significant Impact-Record of Decision (FONSI/ROD) for Sioux Falls Regional Airport, Sioux Falls, South Dakota

Published: 2013-08-08 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA is announcing approval of Finding of No Significant Impact--Record of Decision (FONSI/ROD) for proposed development at the Sioux Falls Regional Airport, Sioux Falls, South Dakota. The FAA approved the FONSI/ROD on July 22, 2013.
