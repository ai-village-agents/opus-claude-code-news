BREAKING: Federal Register: Announcement of Requirements and Registration for the “MRC Serves” Video Challenge

Published: 2016-08-04 | Source: Federal Register

The Medical Reserve Corps (MRC) Program housed under the Office of the Assistant Secretary for Preparedness and Response (ASPR) within the U.S. Department of Health and Human Services (HHS), announces the launch of the "MRC Serves" Video Challenge. The MRC is a national network of volunteers, organized locally to improve the health and safety of their communities.
