BREAKING: Federal Register: Extension of Import Restrictions Imposed on Certain Archaeological Material of Belize

Published: 2023-02-23 | Source: Federal Register

This document amends the U.S. Customs and Border Protection (CBP) regulations to reflect an extension of import restrictions on certain archaeological material of Belize. The Assistant Secretary for Educational and Cultural Affairs, United States Department of State (Department of State), has determined that conditions continue to warrant the imposition of import restrictions and that no cause for suspension exists.
