BREAKING: Federal Register: Proposed Agency Information Collection

Published: 2013-03-06 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Energy has submitted to the OMB for clearance, a proposal for collection of information pursuant to the Paperwork Reduction Act of 1995. The collection would be used to develop information that will enable DOE to measure the impact and progress of DOE's National Clean Fleets Partnership (Partnership).
