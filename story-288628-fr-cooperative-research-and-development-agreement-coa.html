BREAKING: Federal Register: Cooperative Research and Development Agreement-Coast Guard Response Boat-Medium Data Recorder

Published: 2015-03-13 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard announces its intent to enter into a Cooperative Research and Development Agreement (CRADA) with Vector Controls, Inc. (Vector) to develop changes to the response boat-medium (RB-M) onboard engine data bus that will convert Society of Automotive Engineers (SAE) J1939 format to the National Marine Electronics Association (NMEA) 0183/2000 data format.
