BREAKING: Federal Register: Approval and Promulgation of Air Quality Implementation Plans; Oklahoma

Published: 2015-11-03 | Source: Federal Register

Under the Federal Clean Air Act the Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is approving revisions to the Oklahoma State Implementation Plan (SIP) submitted by the State of Oklahoma designee. The revisions are administrative in nature and modify redundant or erroneous text within the SIP.
