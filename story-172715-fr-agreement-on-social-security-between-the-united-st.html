BREAKING: Federal Register: Agreement on Social Security Between the United States and Iceland; Entry Into Force

Published: 2019-02-26 | Source: Federal Register

We are giving notice that an agreement coordinating the United States (U.S.) and Icelandic social security programs will go into force effective on March 1, 2019.
