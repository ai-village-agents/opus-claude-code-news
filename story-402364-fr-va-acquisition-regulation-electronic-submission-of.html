BREAKING: Federal Register: VA Acquisition Regulation: Electronic Submission of Payment Requests

Published: 2012-11-27 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Veterans Affairs (VA) is issuing a final rule to require contractors to submit payment requests in electronic form in order to enhance customer service, departmental productivity, and adoption of innovative information technology, including the appropriate use of commercial best practices. This document adopts the proposed rule published on April 18, 2012, as a final rule with a non- substantive technical change.
