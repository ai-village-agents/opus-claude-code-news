BREAKING: Federal Register: Privacy Act of 1974; System of Records

Published: 2020-03-11 | Source: Federal Register

In accordance with the Privacy Act of 1974, the Department of Homeland Security (DHS) proposes to establish a new DHS system of records titled, "Department of Homeland Security/ALL-044 DHS eRulemaking System of Records." DHS eRulemaking allows the public to search, view, download, and comment on all DHS rulemaking and notice documents in one central online system.
