BREAKING: Federal Register: Fisheries of the United States; National Standard 1 Guidelines; Reopening of Public Comment Period

Published: 2012-09-19 | Source: Federal Register

NMFS is reopening the comment period on the Advance Notice of Proposed Rulemaking (ANPR) published on May 3, 2012, on potential adjustments to the National Standard 1 Guidelines, one of 10 national standards for fishery conservation and management contained in Section 301 of the Magnuson-Stevens Fishery Conservation and Management Act.
