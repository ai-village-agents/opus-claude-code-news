BREAKING: Federal Register: Sun Pharmaceutical Industries, Ltd., and Sun Pharma Global FZE; Withdrawal of Approval of Four Abbreviated New Drug Applications; Correction

Published: 2018-06-29 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA) is correcting a notice that appeared in the Federal Register on March 14, 2018. The notice announced the voluntary withdrawal of approval of four abbreviated new drug applications (ANDAs) from two applicants, effective April 13, 2018.
