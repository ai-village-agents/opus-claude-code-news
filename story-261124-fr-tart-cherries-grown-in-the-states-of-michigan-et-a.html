BREAKING: Federal Register: Tart Cherries Grown in the States of Michigan, et al.; Revision of Exemption Requirements

Published: 2016-04-18 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Agriculture (USDA) is adopting, as a final rule, without change, an interim rule implementing a recommendation from the Cherry Industry Administrative Board (Board) that revised the exemption provisions under the marketing order for tart cherries grown in the States of Michigan, New York, Pennsylvania, Oregon, Utah, Washington, and Wisconsin (order).
