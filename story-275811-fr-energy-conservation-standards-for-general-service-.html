BREAKING: Federal Register: Energy Conservation Standards for General Service Lamps: Public Meeting

Published: 2016-10-05 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Department of Energy (DOE) will hold a public meeting to discuss and receive comments on a proposed definition for general service lamps (GSLs) to be published in a forthcoming notice of proposed definition and data availability (NOPDDA).
