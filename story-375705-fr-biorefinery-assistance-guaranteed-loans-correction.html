BREAKING: Federal Register: Biorefinery Assistance Guaranteed Loans; Correction

Published: 2012-01-24 | Source: Federal Register

The Agency published a rule in the Federal Register on February 14, 2011, establishing a guaranteed loan program for the development and construction of commercial-scale biorefineries and for the retrofitting of existing facilities using eligible technology for the development of advanced biofuels. The document inadvertently omitted provisions as to what an applicant is to do in the event either an appraisal is not completed or a credit rating cannot be obtained at the time of application.
