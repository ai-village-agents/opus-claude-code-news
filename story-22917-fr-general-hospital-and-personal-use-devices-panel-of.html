BREAKING: Federal Register: General Hospital and Personal Use Devices Panel of the Medical Devices Advisory Committee; Amendment of Notice-Establishment of Public Docket; Request for Comments

Published: 2025-11-21 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA) is announcing an amendment to the notice of the meeting of the General Hospital and Personal Use Devices Panel of the Medical Devices Advisory Committee (the Committee). This meeting was previously announced in the Federal Register of September 3, 2025. The amendment is being made to reflect changes in the DATES, ADDRESSES and SUPPLEMENTARY INFORMATION portions of the document. There are no other changes.
