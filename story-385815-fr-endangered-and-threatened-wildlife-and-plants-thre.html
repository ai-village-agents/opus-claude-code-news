BREAKING: Federal Register: Endangered and Threatened Wildlife and Plants; Threatened Status for Eriogonum codium (Umtanum Desert Buckwheat) and Physaria douglasii subsp. tuplashensis (White Bluffs Bladderpod) and Designation of Critical Habitat

Published: 2012-05-15 | Source: Federal Register

We, the U.S. Fish and Wildlife Service, propose to list Umtanum desert buckwheat (Eriogonum codium) and White Bluffs bladderpod (Physaria douglasii subsp. tuplashensis) as threatened, under the Endangered Species Act of 1973, as amended (Act). We are also proposing to designate critical habitat for both species under the Act.
