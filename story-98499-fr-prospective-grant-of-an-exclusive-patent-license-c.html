BREAKING: Federal Register: Prospective Grant of an Exclusive Patent License: Chimeric Adaptor Proteins (CAPs) for Use in Cancer Immunotherapy Against Solid Tumors

Published: 2022-06-16 | Source: Federal Register

The National Cancer Institute, an institute of the National Institutes of Health, Department of Health and Human Services, is contemplating the grant of an Exclusive Patent License to practice the inventions embodied in the U.S. Patents and Patent Applications listed in the Supplementary Information section of this notice to Preverna, Inc. located in California, USA.
