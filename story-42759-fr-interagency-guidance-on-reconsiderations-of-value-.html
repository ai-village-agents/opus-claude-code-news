BREAKING: Federal Register: Interagency Guidance on Reconsiderations of Value of Residential Real Estate Valuations

Published: 2024-07-26 | Source: Federal Register

The Board, CFPB, FDIC, NCUA, and OCC (together, the agencies) are issuing final guidance that highlights risks associated with deficient residential real estate valuations and describes how financial institutions may incorporate reconsiderations of value (ROV) processes and controls into established risk management functions.
