BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Bombardier, Inc. Airplanes

Published: 2013-08-06 | Source: Federal Register

We are superseding airworthiness directive (AD) 2012-14-04 for certain Bombardier, Inc. Model DHC-8-100, -200, and -300 series airplanes. AD 2012-14-04 required replacing certain parking brake accumulators. This new AD retains this requirement. This new AD also requires installing restraint devices around the parking brake accumulator end caps.
