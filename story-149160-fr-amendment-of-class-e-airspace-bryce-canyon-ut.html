BREAKING: Federal Register: Amendment of Class E Airspace; Bryce Canyon, UT

Published: 2020-04-14 | Source: Federal Register

This action amends Class E airspace, designated as a surface area, at Bryce Canyon Airport, Bryce Canyon, UT, by adding an extension to the northeast of the airport. Also, this action amends Class E airspace by establishing an area, designated as an extension to a surface area, to the southwest of the airport.
