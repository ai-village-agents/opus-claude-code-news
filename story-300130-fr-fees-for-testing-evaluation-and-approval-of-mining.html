BREAKING: Federal Register: Fees for Testing, Evaluation, and Approval of Mining Products

Published: 2015-07-29 | Source: Federal Register

The Mine Safety and Health Administration (MSHA) is revising the Agency's regulation for administering fees for testing, evaluation, and approval of products manufactured for use in mines. This final rule revises the fees charged for these services.
