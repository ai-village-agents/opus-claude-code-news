BREAKING: Federal Register: Air Plan Approval; OR; Emission Standard Definition Rule Revision

Published: 2020-02-07 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) proposes to approve a revision to the Oregon State Implementation Plan (SIP) submitted on September 5, 2019. The submission revises the SIP to incorporate by reference a more recent update to the emission standards for specific industries by defining the specific Code of Federal Regulations (CFR) edition referenced throughout the revised rule.
