BREAKING: Federal Register: Pipeline Safety: Liquefied Natural Gas Facility User Fee Rate Increase

Published: 2015-05-07 | Source: Federal Register

On July 3, 2014, (79 FR 38124) the Pipeline and Hazardous Materials Safety Administration (PHMSA) published a notice in this docket to advise all liquefied natural gas facility (LNG) operators subject to PHMSA user fee billing of a change in the LNG user fee rates to align these rates with the actual allocation of PHMSA resources to LNG program costs.
