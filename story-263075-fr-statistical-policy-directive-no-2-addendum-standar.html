BREAKING: Federal Register: Statistical Policy Directive No. 2 Addendum: Standards and Guidelines for Cognitive Interviews

Published: 2016-05-10 | Source: Federal Register

Under the Budget and Accounting Procedures Act of 1950 (31 U.S.C. 1104(d)) and the Paperwork Reduction Act of 1995 (44 U.S.C. 3504(e)), the Office of Management and Budget (OMB) issues for comment a proposed addendum to Statistical Policy Directive No. 2: Standards and Guidelines for Statistical Surveys (71 FR 55522, September 22, 2006).
