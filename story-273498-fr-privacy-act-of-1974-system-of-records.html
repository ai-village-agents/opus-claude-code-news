BREAKING: Federal Register: Privacy Act of 1974; System of Records

Published: 2016-09-09 | Source: Federal Register

Pursuant to the Privacy Act of 1974, 5 U.S.C. 552a, and Office of Management and Budget (OMB) Circular No. A-130, notice is hereby given that the Office of the Secretary of Defense proposes to add a new system of records, DSCA 06, entitled "Defense Security Assistance Management System (DSAMS)." The system will facilitate case development, implementation, and management of the Foreign Military Sales and International Military Education and Training (IMET) Programs.
