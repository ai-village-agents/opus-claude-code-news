BREAKING: Federal Register: Findings of Research Misconduct

Published: 2019-04-01 | Source: Federal Register

Findings of research misconduct have been made against Edward J. Fox, Ph.D. (Respondent), Acting Assistant Professor in the Department of Pathology, University of Washington (UW). Dr. Fox engaged in research misconduct in research supported by National Cancer Institute (NCI), National Institutes of Health (NIH), grants R01 CA193649, R01 CA160674, P01 CA77852, and R01 CA102029.
