BREAKING: Federal Register: Apothecon, et al.; Withdrawal of Approval of 103 New Drug Applications and 35 Abbreviated New Drug Applications; Correction

Published: 2023-12-19 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA) is correcting a notice that appeared in the Federal Register on February 11, 2009. The document announced the withdrawal of approval of 103 new drug applications and 35 abbreviated new drug applications (ANDAs) from multiple applicants, withdrawn as of March 13, 2009.
