BREAKING: Federal Register: Amendments to Municipal Securities Disclosure

Published: 2018-08-31 | Source: Federal Register

The Securities and Exchange Commission ("Commission" or "SEC") is adopting amendments to the Municipal Securities Disclosure Rule under the Securities Exchange Act of 1934 ("Exchange Act"). The amendments add transparency to the municipal securities market by increasing the amount of information that is publicly disclosed about material financial obligations incurred by issuers and obligated persons.
