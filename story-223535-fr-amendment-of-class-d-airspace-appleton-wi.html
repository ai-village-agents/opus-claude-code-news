BREAKING: Federal Register: Amendment of Class D Airspace; Appleton, WI

Published: 2018-12-18 | Source: Federal Register

This action modifies Class D airspace at Appleton International Airport (formerly Outagamie County Airport), Appleton, WI. This action is required due to the decommissioning of the GAMIE locator outer marker (LOM) and collocated outer marker (OM) which provided navigation guidance to the airport. This action enhances the safety and management of instrument flight rules (IFR) operations at the airport.
