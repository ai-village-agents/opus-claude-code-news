BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Request To Release Property at Owensboro-Daviess County Regional Airport, Owensboro, KY (OWB)

Published: 2022-05-27 | Source: Federal Register

The Federal Aviation Administration is requesting public comment on a request by the Owensboro-Daviess County Airport Board, to release land (10.76 acres) at Owensboro--Daviess County Regional Airport (OWB) from federal obligations.
