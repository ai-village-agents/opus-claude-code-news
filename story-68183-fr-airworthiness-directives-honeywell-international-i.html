BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Honeywell International Inc. Engines

Published: 2023-05-23 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA proposes to adopt a new airworthiness directive (AD) for all Honeywell International Inc. Model AS907-1-1A and AS907-2-1G engines. This proposed AD was prompted by reports of compressor surge, including a dual engine compressor surge, during takeoff climb out through a steep temperature inversion, causing a loss of engine thrust control.
