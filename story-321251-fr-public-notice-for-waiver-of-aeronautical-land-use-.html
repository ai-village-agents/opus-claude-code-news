BREAKING: Federal Register: Public Notice for Waiver of Aeronautical Land-Use Assurance

Published: 2014-04-10 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA is considering a proposal to change approximately 2.54 acres of airport land from aeronautical use to non-aeronautical use and to authorize the lease of airport property located at Cleveland Hopkins International, Cleveland, Ohio. The aforementioned land is not needed for aeronautical use. The property is located near the northwest corner of Brook Park Road and Rocky River Drive, north of the airport and outside the airport fence line.
