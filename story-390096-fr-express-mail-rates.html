BREAKING: Federal Register: Express Mail Rates

Published: 2012-07-02 | Source: Federal Register

The Commission is noticing a recently-filed Postal Service request to implement prices for a new Express Mail Padded Flat Rate Envelope. This notice addresses procedural steps associated with these filings.
