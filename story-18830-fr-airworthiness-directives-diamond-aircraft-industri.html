BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Diamond Aircraft Industries GmbH Airplanes

Published: 2025-08-25 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA proposes to adopt a new airworthiness directive (AD) for all Diamond Aircraft Industries GmbH (DAI) Model DA 42, DA 42 NG, and DA 42 M-NG airplanes. This proposed AD was prompted by several reports of passenger door separation from the fuselage.
