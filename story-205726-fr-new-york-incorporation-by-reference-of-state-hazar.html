BREAKING: Federal Register: New York: Incorporation by Reference of State Hazardous Waste Management Program

Published: 2018-04-27 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) proposes to revise the codification of New York's authorized hazardous waste program which is set forth in the regulations entitled "Approved State Hazardous Waste Management Programs".
