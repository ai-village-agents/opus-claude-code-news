BREAKING: Federal Register: Meeting of the Advisory Committee on Training in Primary Care Medicine and Dentistry

Published: 2017-02-13 | Source: Federal Register

In accordance with the Federal Advisory Committee Act, notice is hereby given that a meeting is scheduled for the Advisory Committee on Training in Primary Care Medicine and Dentistry (ACTPCMD). This meeting will be open to the public. Information about ACTPCMD and the agenda for this meeting can be obtained by accessing the ACTPCMD Web site at http://www.hrsa.gov/advisorycommittees/bhpradvisory/ACTPCMD.
