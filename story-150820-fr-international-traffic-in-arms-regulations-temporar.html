BREAKING: Federal Register: International Traffic in Arms Regulations: Temporary Reduction in Certain Registration Fees

Published: 2020-05-06 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of State is making a temporary change in the Tier I and Tier II and new registrant payment guidelines on the Directorate of Defense Trade Controls (DDTC) website at www.pmddtc.state.gov.
