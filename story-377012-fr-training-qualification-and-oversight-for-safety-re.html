BREAKING: Federal Register: Training, Qualification, and Oversight for Safety-Related Railroad Employees

Published: 2012-02-07 | Source: Federal Register

FRA proposes regulations establishing minimum training standards for each category and subcategory of safety-related railroad employee, as required by the Rail Safety Improvement Act of 2008. The proposed rule would require each railroad or contractor that employs one or more safety-related railroad employee to develop and submit a training program to FRA for approval and to designate the qualification of each such employee.
