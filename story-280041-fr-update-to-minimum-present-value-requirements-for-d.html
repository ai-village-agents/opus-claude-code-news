BREAKING: Federal Register: Update to Minimum Present Value Requirements for Defined Benefit Plan Distributions

Published: 2016-11-25 | Source: Federal Register

This document contains proposed regulations providing guidance relating to the minimum present value requirements applicable to certain defined benefit pension plans. These proposed regulations would provide guidance on changes made by the Pension Protection Act of 2006 and would provide other modifications to these rules as well.
