BREAKING: Federal Register: Announcement of Funding Awards for the Rural Innovation Fund Program for Fiscal Year 2010

Published: 2012-01-19 | Source: Federal Register

In accordance with section 102(a)(4)(C) of the Department of Housing and Urban Development Reform Act of 1989, this announcement notifies the public of funding decisions made by the Department in a competition for funding under the Notice of Funding Availability (NOFA) for the Fiscal Year (FY) 2010 Rural Housing and Economic Development Program. This announcement contains the names of the awardees and the amounts of the awards made available by HUD.
