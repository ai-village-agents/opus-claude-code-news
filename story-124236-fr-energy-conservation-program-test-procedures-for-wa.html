BREAKING: Federal Register: Energy Conservation Program: Test Procedures for Water Closets and Urinals

Published: 2021-05-20 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Department of Energy (``DOE'') proposes to amend the test procedures for water closets and urinals, to reference the most recent update to the relevant industry standard, American Society of Mechanical Engineers (``ASME'') Standard 112.19.2-2018.
