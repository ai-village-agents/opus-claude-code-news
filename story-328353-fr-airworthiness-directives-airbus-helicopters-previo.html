BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Airbus Helicopters (Previously Eurocopter France) Helicopters

Published: 2014-06-30 | Source: Federal Register

We are adopting a new airworthiness directive (AD) for certain Airbus Helicopters Model EC120B and EC130B4 helicopters. This AD requires replacing parts of the sliding door star support attachment assembly, depending on the outcome of required inspections. This AD is prompted by a report that passengers in a helicopter were forced to exit through the pilot door after landing because they could not open the sliding door from the inside.
