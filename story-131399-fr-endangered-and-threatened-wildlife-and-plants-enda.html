BREAKING: Federal Register: Endangered and Threatened Wildlife and Plants; Endangered Species Status for Franklin's Bumble Bee

Published: 2021-08-24 | Source: Federal Register

We, the U.S. Fish and Wildlife Service (Service), are listing the Franklin's bumble bee (Bombus franklini), an invertebrate species from Douglas, Jackson, and Josephine Counties in Oregon, and Siskiyou and Trinity Counties in California, as an endangered species under the Endangered Species Act of 1973, as amended (Act).
