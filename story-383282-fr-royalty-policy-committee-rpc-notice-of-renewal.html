BREAKING: Federal Register: Royalty Policy Committee (RPC) Notice of Renewal

Published: 2012-04-17 | Source: Federal Register

Following consultation with the General Services Administration, notice is hereby given that the Secretary of the Interior is renewing the Royalty Policy Committee. The Royalty Policy Committee provides advice to the Secretary of the Interior on the management of Federal and Indian mineral leases and revenues under the laws governing the Department of the Interior.
