BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Realty Action; Non-Competitive Land Use Authorization of Public Lands in the Fairbanks North Star Borough, Alaska

Published: 2014-07-11 | Source: Federal Register

The Bureau of Land Management (BLM) is considering the issuance of a permit for mineral exploration on lands withdrawn for use by the National Oceanic and Atmospheric Administration (NOAA). The proposal to conduct sampling associated with mineral assessment on the subject lands was provided by Fairbanks Gold Mining, Inc. (FGMI).
