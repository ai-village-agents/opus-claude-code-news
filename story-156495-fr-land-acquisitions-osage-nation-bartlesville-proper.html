BREAKING: Federal Register: Land Acquisitions; Osage Nation, Bartlesville Property, Oklahoma

Published: 2020-07-17 | Source: Federal Register

The Assistant Secretary--Indian Affairs made a final agency determination to acquire 125 acres, more or less, of land in trust for the Osage Nation for gaming and other purposes on June 26, 2020.
