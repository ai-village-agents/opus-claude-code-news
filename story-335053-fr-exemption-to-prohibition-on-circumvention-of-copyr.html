BREAKING: Federal Register: Exemption to Prohibition on Circumvention of Copyright Protection Systems for Access Control Technologies

Published: 2014-09-17 | Source: Federal Register

The United States Copyright Office is initiating the sixth triennial rulemaking proceeding under the Digital Millennium Copyright Act, concerning possible exemptions to the Act's prohibition against circumvention of technological measures that control access to copyrighted works. The Copyright Office invites written petitions for proposed exemptions from interested parties.
