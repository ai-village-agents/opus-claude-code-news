BREAKING: Federal Register: Air Plan Approval; Wisconsin; Definition of Chemical Process Plants Under State PSD Regulations and Operating Permit Program

Published: 2023-02-21 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA)is approving revisions to the State Implementation Plan (SIP) for Wisconsin and revisions to the title V Operating Permit Program for Wisconsin. The revisions incorporate changes to the definition of "chemical process plants" under Wisconsin's Prevention of Significant Deterioration (PSD) and title V Operating Permit Programs.
