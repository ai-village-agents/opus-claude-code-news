BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Decision To Allow Interstate Movement of Sapote Fruit From Puerto Rico Into the Continental United States

Published: 2013-10-24 | Source: Federal Register

We are advising the public of our decision to begin allowing the interstate movement into the continental United States of fresh sapote fruit from Puerto Rico.
