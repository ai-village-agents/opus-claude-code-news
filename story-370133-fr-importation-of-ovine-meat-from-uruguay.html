BREAKING: Federal Register: Importation of Ovine Meat From Uruguay

Published: 2013-11-14 | Source: Federal Register

We are amending the regulations governing the importation of certain animals, meat, and other animal products to allow, under certain conditions, the importation of fresh (chilled or frozen) ovine meat from Uruguay. A risk assessment that we have prepared indicates that fresh (chilled or frozen) ovine meat can safely be imported from Uruguay under these conditions.
