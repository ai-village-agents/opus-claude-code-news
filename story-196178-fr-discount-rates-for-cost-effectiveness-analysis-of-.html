BREAKING: Federal Register: Discount Rates for Cost-Effectiveness Analysis of Federal Programs

Published: 2019-12-23 | Source: Federal Register

The Office of Management and Budget (OMB) revised Circular A- 94 in 1992. With this action, OMB has revised the Circular and specified certain discount rates to be updated annually when the interest rate and inflation assumptions used to prepare the Budget of the United States Government were changed.
