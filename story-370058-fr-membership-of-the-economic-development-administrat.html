BREAKING: Federal Register: Membership of the Economic Development Administration Performance Review Board

Published: 2013-11-13 | Source: Federal Register

In accordance with 5 U.S.C. 4314(c)(4), the Economic Development Administration (EDA), Department of Commerce (DOC), announce the appointment of those individuals who have been selected to serve as members of EDA's Performance Review Board.
