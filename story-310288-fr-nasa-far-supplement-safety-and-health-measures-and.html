BREAKING: Federal Register: NASA FAR Supplement: Safety and Health Measures and Mishap Reporting

Published: 2015-11-25 | Source: Federal Register

NASA is issuing a final rule to amend the NASA FAR Supplement (NFS) to revise a clause related to safety and health measures and mishaps reporting, reduce burden on contractors, and to provide guidance on specific safety and health measures that the contractor must take when working on a Federal facility, and the remedies the Government may take for failure to maintain an effective safety and health program.
