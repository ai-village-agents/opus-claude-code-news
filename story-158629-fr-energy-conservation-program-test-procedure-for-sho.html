BREAKING: Federal Register: Energy Conservation Program: Test Procedure for Showerheads

Published: 2020-08-13 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Department of Energy ("DOE") proposes to amend the existing test procedure for showerheads to revise the definition of a showerhead consistent with the most recent standard developed by the American Society of Mechanical Engineers ("ASME") in 2018.
