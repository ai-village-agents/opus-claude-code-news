BREAKING: Federal Register: Energy Conservation Program: Test Procedure for Walk-In Coolers and Walk-In Freezers

Published: 2021-03-26 | Source: Federal Register

This final rule amends the current test procedure for hot gas defrost unit coolers by making it consistent with a recent update to the industry testing standard that is incorporated by reference in the relevant Federal test procedure for walk-in freezer refrigeration systems.
