BREAKING: Federal Register: Marine Diesel Engine Emission Standards

Published: 2019-09-06 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is proposing to amend the national marine diesel engine program to provide relief provisions to address concerns associated with finding and installing certified Tier 4 marine diesel engines in certain high-speed commercial vessels. The proposed relief is in the form of additional lead time for qualifying engines and vessels.
