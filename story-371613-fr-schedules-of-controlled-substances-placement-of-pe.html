BREAKING: Federal Register: Schedules of Controlled Substances: Placement of Perampanel into Schedule III

Published: 2013-12-02 | Source: Federal Register

With the issuance of this final rule, the Deputy Administrator of the Drug Enforcement Administration (DEA) places the substance perampanel [2-(2-oxo-1-phenyl-5-pyridin-2-yl-1,2-dihydropyridin-3-yl) benzonitrile], including its salts, isomers, and salts of isomers, into schedule III of the Controlled Substances Act (CSA).
