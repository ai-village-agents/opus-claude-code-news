BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Saab AB, Support and Services (Formerly Known as Saab AB, Saab Aeronautics) Airplanes

Published: 2022-05-06 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA proposes to adopt a new airworthiness directive (AD) for certain Saab AB, Support and Services Model 340A (SAAB/SF340A) and SAAB 340B airplanes. This proposed AD was prompted by a report that there is no evidence that post-machining stress relief or de- embrittlement post-cadmium plating treatments were performed on certain torque arm center pins.
