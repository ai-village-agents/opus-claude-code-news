BREAKING: Federal Register: Changes to Aging Management Guidance for Various Steam Generator Components

Published: 2016-06-07 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Nuclear Regulatory Commission (NRC) is seeking public comment on the draft License Renewal Interim Staff Guidance (LR-ISG), LR-ISG-2016-01, "Changes to Aging Management Guidance for Various Steam Generator Components."
