BREAKING: Federal Register: Canned Tuna Deviating From Identity Standard; Temporary Permit for Market Testing

Published: 2013-01-10 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA or we) received an application for a temporary permit from Chicken of the Sea International (the applicant). We are announcing that we have issued a temporary permit to the applicant to market test products (designated as "no drain canned tuna" products) that deviate from the U.S. standard of identity for canned tuna.
