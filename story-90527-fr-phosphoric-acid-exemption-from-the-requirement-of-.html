BREAKING: Federal Register: Phosphoric Acid; Exemption From the Requirement of a Tolerance

Published: 2022-03-08 | Source: Federal Register

This regulation establishes an exemption from the requirement of a tolerance for residues of phosphoric acid (CAS Reg. No. 7664-38-2) when used as an inert ingredient (pH adjuster) in antimicrobial formulations applied to food-contact surfaces in public eating places, dairy-processing equipment, food-processing equipment and utensils.
