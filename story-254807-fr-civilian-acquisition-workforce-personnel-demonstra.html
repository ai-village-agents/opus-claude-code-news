BREAKING: Federal Register: Civilian Acquisition Workforce Personnel Demonstration Project in the Department of Defense: Correction

Published: 2016-02-09 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Office of Personnel Management published a document in the Federal Register on March 31, 2015, announcing amendments to a demonstration project plan for the civilian acquisition workforce of the Department of Defense. The document requires three technical corrections that will ensure access for the entirety of an organization to participate.
