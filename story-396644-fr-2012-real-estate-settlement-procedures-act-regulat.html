BREAKING: Federal Register: 2012 Real Estate Settlement Procedures Act (Regulation X) Mortgage Servicing Proposal

Published: 2012-09-17 | Source: Federal Register

The Bureau of Consumer Financial Protection (the Bureau) is proposing to amend Regulation X, which implements the Real Estate Settlement Procedures Act of 1974 (RESPA) and the official interpretation of the regulation. The proposed amendments implement the Dodd-Frank Wall Street Reform and Consumer Protection Act (Dodd-Frank Act) provisions regarding mortgage loan servicing.
