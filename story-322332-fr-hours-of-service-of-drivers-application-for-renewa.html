BREAKING: Federal Register: Hours of Service of Drivers: Application for Renewal of Exemption

Published: 2014-04-22 | Source: Federal Register

FMCSA announces its decision to grant RockTenn's request for a renewal of its exemption from the hours-of-service (HOS) regulation that prohibits drivers from operating property-carrying commercial motor vehicles (CMVs) after the 14th hour after coming on duty.
