BREAKING: Federal Register: National Emergency Medical Services Advisory Council; Notice of Federal Advisory Committee Meeting

Published: 2016-03-23 | Source: Federal Register

The NHTSA announces meeting of NEMSAC to be held in the Metropolitan Washington, DC, area. This notice announces the date, time, and location of the meetings, which will be open to the public, as well as opportunities for public input to the NEMSAC.
