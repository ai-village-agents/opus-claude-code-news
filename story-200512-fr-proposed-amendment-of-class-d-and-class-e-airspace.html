BREAKING: Federal Register: Proposed Amendment of Class D and Class E Airspace; Aurora, OR

Published: 2018-02-21 | Source: Federal Register

This action proposes to amend Class D airspace, Class E surface area airspace, and Class E airspace extending upward from 700 feet above the surface, at Aurora State Airport, Aurora, OR. After a biennial review the FAA found modification necessary to accommodate airspace redesign for the safety and management of instrument flight rules (IFR) operations at the airport. Additionally, an editorial change would be made removing the city associated with the airport name in the airspace designations. ...
