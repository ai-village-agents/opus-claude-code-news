BREAKING: Federal Register: Inventions and Contributions

Published: 2012-05-10 | Source: Federal Register

NASA is amending its regulations to clarify and update the procedures for board recommended awards, and the procedures and requirements for recommended special initial awards, including patent application awards, software release awards, and Tech Brief awards, and to update citations and the information on the systems used for reporting inventions and issuing award payments.
