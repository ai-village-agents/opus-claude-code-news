BREAKING: Federal Register: Privacy Act of 1974; System of Records

Published: 2019-04-17 | Source: Federal Register

As required by the Privacy Act of 1974, notice is hereby given that the Department of Veterans Affairs (VA) is amending the system of records known as "Criminal Investigations" (11VA51) by amending the Routine Uses and the Policies and Practices for Storing, Retrieving, Accessing, Retaining, and Disposing of Records within the System. VA is republishing the system notice in its entirety.
