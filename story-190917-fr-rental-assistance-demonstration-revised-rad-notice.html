BREAKING: Federal Register: Rental Assistance Demonstration: Revised RAD Notice

Published: 2019-10-10 | Source: Federal Register

The Rental Assistance Demonstration (RAD) provides the opportunity to facilitate the conversion of assistance of public housing properties and other HUD-assisted properties to long-term, project-based Section 8 rental assistance. This Federal Register notice announces the posting of the fourth revision to the RAD notice (Revised RAD Notice Revision 4, PIH-2019-23 (HA)/H-2019-09, REV-4) and solicits public comment on changed eligibility and selection criteria.
