BREAKING: Federal Register: State Inspection Programs for Passenger-Carrier Vehicles

Published: 2022-11-15 | Source: Federal Register

FMCSA confirms its May 1, 2017, decision to withdraw its April 27, 2016, advance notice of proposed rulemaking (ANPRM) concerning the establishment of requirements for States to implement annual inspection programs for commercial motor vehicles (CMVs) designed or used to transport passengers (passenger-carrying CMVs).
