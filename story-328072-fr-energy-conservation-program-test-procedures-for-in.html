BREAKING: Federal Register: Energy Conservation Program: Test Procedures for Integrated Light-Emitting Diode Lamps

Published: 2014-06-26 | Source: Federal Register

On June 3, 2014, the U.S. Department of Energy (DOE) published a supplemental notice of proposed rulemaking (SNOPR) (hereafter the June 2014 SNOPR) in which DOE proposed test procedures for light- emitting diode (LED) lamps. The June 2014 SNOPR defined methods for measuring the lumen output, input power, and relative spectral distribution (to determine correlated color temperature, or CCT).
