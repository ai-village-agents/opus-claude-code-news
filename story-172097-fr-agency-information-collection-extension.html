BREAKING: Federal Register: Agency Information Collection Extension

Published: 2019-02-19 | Source: Federal Register

EIA submitted an information collection request for extension as required by the Paperwork Reduction Act of 1995. EIA requests a three-year extension with changes to the information collection EIA- 882T, "Generic Clearance for Questionnaire Testing and Research."
