BREAKING: Federal Register: 2-Propenoic Acid, Homopolymer, Ester With α-[2,4,6-Tris(1-Phenylethyl)Phenyl]-ω-Hydroxypoly(Oxy-1,2-Ethanediyl), Compd. With 2,2′,2″-Nitrilotris[Ethanol]; Tolerance Exemption

Published: 2015-12-23 | Source: Federal Register

This regulation establishes an exemption from the requirement of a tolerance for residues of 2-propenoic acid, homopolymer, ester with [alpha]-[2,4,6-tris(1-phenylethyl)phenyl]-[omega]-hydroxypoly(oxy- 1,2-ethanediyl), compd. with 2,2',2"-nitrilotris[ethanol] (CAS Reg. No. 1477613-46-9) when used as an inert ingredient in a pesticide chemical formulation.
