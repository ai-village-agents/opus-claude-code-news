BREAKING: Federal Register: 30-Day Notice of Proposed Information Collection: Public Housing Agencies Service Areas Solicitation of Comments: Withdrawal

Published: 2018-02-22 | Source: Federal Register

On February 13, 2018, HUD inadvertently published a 30-day notice of proposed information collection entitled Public Housing Agencies Service Areas Solicitation of Comments. HUD will republish the notice in the Federal Register at a later date. This notice withdraws the notice published on February 13, 2018.
