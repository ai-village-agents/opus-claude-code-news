BREAKING: Federal Register: Russian Sanctions: Addition of Certain Persons to the Entity List

Published: 2015-12-28 | Source: Federal Register

The Bureau of Industry and Security (BIS) amends the Export Administration Regulations (EAR) by adding sixteen persons under seventeen entries to the Entity List. The sixteen persons who are added to the Entity List have been determined by the U.S. Government to be acting contrary to the national security or foreign policy interests of the United States.
