BREAKING: Federal Register: Drawbridge Operation Regulation; Okeechobee Waterway, Indiantown, FL

Published: 2021-03-05 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard has issued a temporary deviation from the operating schedule that governs the Seaboard System Railroad Bridge, across the Okeechobee Waterway, mile 28.2, at Indiantown, Florida. The bridge owner requested to start the three hour advance notice for an opening earlier each evening and end it one hour later each morning.
