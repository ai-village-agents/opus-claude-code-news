BREAKING: Federal Register: Approval and Promulgation of Implementation Plans; Designation of Areas for Air Quality Planning Purposes; State of California; PM2.5; Redesignation of Yuba City-Marysville to Attainment; Approval of PM2.5 Redesignation Request and Maintenance Plan for Yuba City-Marysville

Published: 2014-10-15 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is proposing to approve, as a revision of the California state implementation plan (SIP), the State's request to redesignate the Yuba City-Marysville nonattainment area to attainment for the 2006 24-hour fine particulate matter (PM2.5) National Ambient Air Quality Standard.
