BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Airbus SAS Airplanes

Published: 2018-10-03 | Source: Federal Register

We are adopting a new airworthiness directive (AD) for certain Airbus SAS Model A300 B4-603, B4-620, and B4-622 airplanes; Model A300 B4-600R series airplanes; Model A300 C4-605R Variant F airplanes; and Model A300 F4-605R airplanes. This AD was prompted by reports of cracking on a certain frame (FR) angle fitting.
