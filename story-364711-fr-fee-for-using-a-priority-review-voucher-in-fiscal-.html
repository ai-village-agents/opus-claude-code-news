BREAKING: Federal Register: Fee for Using a Priority Review Voucher in Fiscal Year 2014

Published: 2013-09-05 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA or the Agency) is announcing the fee rates for using a tropical disease priority review voucher for fiscal year (FY) 2014.
