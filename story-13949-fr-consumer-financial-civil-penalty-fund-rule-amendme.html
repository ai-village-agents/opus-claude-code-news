BREAKING: Federal Register: Consumer Financial Civil Penalty Fund Rule Amendment

Published: 2025-06-18 | Source: Federal Register

The Consumer Financial Protection Bureau (Bureau or CFPB) is proposing to amend its 2013 rule implementing the provision of the Consumer Financial Protection Act of 2010 (CFPA or Act) that establishes a Consumer Financial Civil Penalty Fund (Civil Penalty Fund).
