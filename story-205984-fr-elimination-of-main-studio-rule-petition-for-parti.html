BREAKING: Federal Register: Elimination of Main Studio Rule; Petition for Partial Reconsideration

Published: 2018-05-02 | Source: Federal Register

This document dismisses and otherwise denies the Petition for Reconsideration filed by De La Hunt Broadcasting Corp. The Commission's rules provide that a petition for reconsideration which relies on facts or arguments not previously presented to the Commission will only be granted if one of three circumstances is present, and the Media Bureau concludes that none of the specified circumstances is present here.
