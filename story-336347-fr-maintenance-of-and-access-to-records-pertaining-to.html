BREAKING: Federal Register: Maintenance of and Access to Records Pertaining to Individuals

Published: 2014-10-02 | Source: Federal Register

This rule conforms DOT's regulations on Maintenance of and Access to Records Pertaining to Individuals to the applicable System of Records Notices (SORNs) and current DOT practice. This rule adds the General Investigation Records System to the list of DOT Privacy Act Systems of Records that are exempt from one or more provisions of the Privacy Act.
