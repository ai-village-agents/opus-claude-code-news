BREAKING: Federal Register: Exelon Generation Corporation, LLC; Calvert Cliffs Independent Spent Fuel Storage Installation

Published: 2014-12-12 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Nuclear Regulatory Commission (NRC) has docketed a license amendment application from Exelon Generation Corporation, LLC (Exelon Generation). Exelon Generation is requesting revisions to its renewed license to increase maximum enrichment levels; to increase the amount of fuel allowed to be stored; and to change various Technical Specifications of the 32PHB DSC casks utilized at the Calvert Cliffs Independent Storage Installation located in Calvert County, Maryland.
