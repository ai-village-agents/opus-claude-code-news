BREAKING: Federal Register: United States Standards for Grades of Pork Carcasses

Published: 2017-10-23 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Department of Agriculture's (USDA) Agricultural Marketing Service (AMS) is seeking public comment on revisions to the United States Standards for Grades of Pork Carcasses (pork standards). The last revision to the pork standards occurred in 1985 and the standards no longer accurately reflect value differences in today's pork products.
