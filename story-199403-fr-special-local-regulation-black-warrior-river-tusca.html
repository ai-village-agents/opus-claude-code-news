BREAKING: Federal Register: Special Local Regulation; Black Warrior River, Tuscaloosa, AL

Published: 2018-02-07 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard will enforce a special local regulation on the Black Warrior River extending the entire width of the channel from mile marker 339.0 to mile marker 341.5 in Tuscaloosa, AL on March 24, 2018, to provide for the safety of life on navigable waterways during the Rowing Competition marine event.
