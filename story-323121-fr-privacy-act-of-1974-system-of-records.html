BREAKING: Federal Register: Privacy Act of 1974; System of Records

Published: 2014-05-01 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of the Army proposes to add a new system of records, A0600-63 DAPE G-1, entitled "Commander's Risk Reduction Dashboard Interim" to its inventory of record systems subject to the Privacy Act of 1974, as amended. This system will provide Commanders with information they need to recognize early warning signs and proactively engage in intervention activities to help reduce Soldier suicides, risk-related deaths, and other negative outcomes of high risk behavior.
