BREAKING: Federal Register: Refuge Alternatives for Underground Coal Mines

Published: 2013-11-15 | Source: Federal Register

The Mine Safety and Health Administration (MSHA) is reopening the rulemaking record for MSHA's existing rule on Refuge Alternatives for the limited purpose of obtaining comments on the frequency for motor task (also known as "hands-on" training), decision-making, and expectations training for miners to deploy and use refuge alternatives in underground coal mines.
