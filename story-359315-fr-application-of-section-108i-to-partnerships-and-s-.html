BREAKING: Federal Register: Application of Section 108(i) to Partnerships and S Corporations

Published: 2013-07-03 | Source: Federal Register

This document contains final regulations relating to the application of section 108(i) of the Internal Revenue Code (Code) to partnerships and S corporations and provides rules regarding the deferral of discharge of indebtedness income and original issue discount deductions by a partnership or an S corporation with respect to reacquisitions of applicable debt instruments after December 31, 2008, and before January 1, 2011.
