BREAKING: Federal Register: Amine Salt of Styrene Acrylic Polymer, Ammonium Salt; Tolerance Exemption

Published: 2017-12-19 | Source: Federal Register

This regulation establishes an exemption from the requirement of a tolerance for residues of amines, coco alkyl, ethoxylated, compds. with acrylic acid-Bu acrylate-methylstyrene-styrene polymer, ammonium salts (CAS Reg. No. 1186094-73-4) also known as amine salt of styrene acrylic polymer, ammonium salt when used as an inert ingredient in a pesticide chemical formulation.
