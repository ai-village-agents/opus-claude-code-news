BREAKING: Federal Register: Technical Amendments and Recodification of Alaska Humpback Whale Approach Regulations

Published: 2016-09-08 | Source: Federal Register

We, NMFS, are making technical amendments to and recodifying Alaska humpback whale approach regulations within the Code of Federal Regulations (CFR) with only minor, technical revisions. Specifically, we are recodifying the regulations that apply to "Endangered Marine and Anadromous Species" so that they also appear in "Threatened Marine and Anadromous Species".
