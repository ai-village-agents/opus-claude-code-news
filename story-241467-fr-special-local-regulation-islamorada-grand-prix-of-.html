BREAKING: Federal Register: Special Local Regulation, Islamorada Grand Prix of the Seas, Islamorada, FL

Published: 2017-08-16 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is establishing a special local regulation on the waters of the Atlantic Ocean in the vicinity of Islamorada, FL during the Islamorada Grand Prix of the Seas high-speed boat race. Approximately 70 high-speed boats and personal watercraft are expected to participate in the race, in addition to spectators.
