BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Funding Opportunity for Consolidated Rail Infrastructure and Safety Improvements

Published: 2018-07-19 | Source: Federal Register

This notice details the application requirements and procedures to obtain grant \1\ funding for eligible projects under the Consolidated Rail Infrastructure and Safety Improvements (CRISI) Program. CRISI Program funding under this notice is provided by the Consolidated Appropriations Act, 2018, (2018 Appropriation).
