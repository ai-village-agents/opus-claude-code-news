BREAKING: Federal Register: Antimony Trioxide TSCA Chemical Risk Assessment; Notice of Public Meetings and Opportunity To Comment

Published: 2013-09-27 | Source: Federal Register

EPA's contractor, The Scientific Consulting Group (SCG), Inc., has identified a panel of scientific experts to conduct a peer review of EPA's draft Toxic Substances Control Act (TSCA) chemical risk assessment, "TSCA Workplan Chemical Risk Assessment for Antimony Trioxide." EPA will hold three peer review meetings by web connect and teleconference.
