BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Establishment of a Federal Advisory Committee

Published: 2024-06-28 | Source: Federal Register

The General Services Administration (GSA) announces the establishment of the Open Government Federal Advisory Committee (hereinafter "the Committee" or "the OG FAC") in accordance with the Federal Advisory Committee Act, as amended.
