BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone; Ohio River, Cincinnati, OH

Published: 2018-10-11 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is establishing a temporary safety zone for all navigable waters of the Ohio River, extending the entire width of the river, from mile marker (MM) 469 to MM 470.5 in Cincinnati, OH. This safety zone is necessary to provide for the safety of persons, vessels, and the marine environment during the Yeatman's Cove fireworks display.
