BREAKING: Federal Register: President's Council of Advisors on Science and Technology (PCAST)

Published: 2022-07-15 | Source: Federal Register

This notice announces a partially-closed meeting of the President's Council of Advisors on Science and Technology (PCAST). Due to the COVID-19 pandemic, this meeting will be held virtually for members of the public and in-person for PCAST members. The Federal Advisory Committee Act (FACA) requires that public notice of these meetings be announced in the Federal Register.
