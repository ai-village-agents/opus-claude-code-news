BREAKING: Federal Register: System Energy Resources Inc.; Grand Gulf Early Site Permit; Early Site Permit Renewal Application

Published: 2026-01-27 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Nuclear Regulatory Commission (NRC) is considering an application for the renewal of the Grand Gulf Site Early Site Permit (ESP) No. ESP-002. The renewed permit would allow a construction permit or combined license application to reference the permit for an additional 20 years specified in the current permit. The current permit for the Grand Gulf Site expires on April 5, 2027.
