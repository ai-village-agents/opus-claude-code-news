BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Airbus Airplanes

Published: 2016-01-28 | Source: Federal Register

We are superseding Airworthiness Directive (AD) 96-18-06 for certain Airbus Model A320-211 and -231 airplanes. AD 96-18-06 required visual inspections to detect cracks of the pressurized floor fittings at frame 36, and renewal of the zone protective finish or replacement of fittings with new fittings if necessary.
