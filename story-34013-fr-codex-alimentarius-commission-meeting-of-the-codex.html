BREAKING: Federal Register: Codex Alimentarius Commission: Meeting of the Codex Committee on Pesticide Residues

Published: 2024-04-10 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Codex Office is sponsoring a public meeting on May 7, 2024. The objective of the public meeting is to provide information and receive public comments on agenda items and draft U.S. positions to be discussed at the 55th Session of the Codex Committee on Pesticide Residues (CCPR) of the Codex Alimentarius Commission (CAC), which will convene in Chengdu, China from June 3-8, 2024.
