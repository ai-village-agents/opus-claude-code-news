BREAKING: Federal Register: International Trade Data System Test-Voluntary Export Pilot Project

Published: 2016-10-12 | Source: Federal Register

Along with other agencies, the Bureau of Alcohol, Tobacco, Firearms and Explosives (ATF) will participate in a U.S. Customs and Border Protection (CBP) pilot test of the International Trade Data System (ITDS), using electronic processing of export data through the Automated Export System (AES) which is accessed within CBP's Automated Commercial Environment (ACE).
