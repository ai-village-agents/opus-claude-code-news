BREAKING: Federal Register: Dried Prunes Produced in California; Suspension of the Marketing Order

Published: 2023-10-12 | Source: Federal Register

This proposed rule would suspend the Federal marketing order regulating dried prunes produced in California (Order) effective at the beginning of the 2023-2024 crop year. After operating for 18 years without handling regulations, the Prune Administrative Committee (Committee) recommended the Agricultural Marketing Service (AMS) indefinitely suspend the Order.
