BREAKING: Federal Register: FAA Approval of Noise Compatibility Program; Westover Metropolitan Airport, Chicopee, Massachusetts

Published: 2015-02-24 | Source: Federal Register

The Federal Aviation Administration (FAA) announces its findings on the Noise Compatibility Program submitted by the Westover Metropolitan Development Authority under the provisions of Title I of the Aviation Safety and Noise Abatement Act of 1979. On September 25, 2014, the FAA determined the noise exposure maps submitted by the Westover Metropolitan Development Corporation under Part 150 were in compliance with applicable requirements.
