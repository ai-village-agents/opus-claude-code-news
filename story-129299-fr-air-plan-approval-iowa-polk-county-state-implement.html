BREAKING: Federal Register: Air Plan Approval; Iowa; Polk County; State Implementation Plan

Published: 2021-07-28 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is proposing to approve revisions to the Iowa State Implementation Plan (SIP) to include recent changes to the Polk County Board of Health Rules and Regulations in addition to revisions from past submittals.
