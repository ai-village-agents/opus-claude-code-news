BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety and Security Zone; Lake Michigan at Chicago Harbor & Burnham Park Harbor

Published: 2024-08-23 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard will enforce the Lake Michigan at Chicago Harbor and Burnham Park Harbor safety and security zone on a portion of Lake Michigan in Chicago, IL. This action is intended to protect spectators and dignitaries associated with the Democratic National Convention.
