BREAKING: Federal Register: Privacy Act of 1974; System of Records

Published: 2021-04-30 | Source: Federal Register

As required by the Privacy Act of 1974, notice is hereby given that the Department of Veterans Affairs (VA) is publishing SORN "Veterans Tracking Application (VTA)--VA" (163VA005Q3). The VTA is a joint Veterans Affairs (VA)/Department of Defense (DoD) application that supports the effective management and tracking of Veteran and Service member beneficiaries at all levels of the continuum of care.
