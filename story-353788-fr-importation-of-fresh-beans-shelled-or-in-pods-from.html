BREAKING: Federal Register: Importation of Fresh Beans, Shelled or in Pods, From Jordan Into the Continental United States

Published: 2013-05-02 | Source: Federal Register

We are proposing to amend the fruits and vegetables regulations to allow the importation of commercial shipments of fresh beans, shelled or in pods (French, green, snap, and string), from Jordan into the continental United States. As a condition of entry, the beans would have to be produced in accordance with a systems approach that would include requirements for packing, washing, and processing.
