BREAKING: Federal Register: Proposed New Restricted Category Aircraft-Special Purpose Operations

Published: 2019-03-25 | Source: Federal Register

This notice announces the availability of and requests comment on the proposed addition of several new restricted category special purpose operations pursuant to Title 14 of the Code of Federal Regulations. These include Humanitarian Assistance/Disaster Relief, and An expansion of Patrolling to include patrolling of railroads, waterways and harbors.
