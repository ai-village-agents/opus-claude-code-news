BREAKING: Federal Register: Request for Comments To Compile the National Trade Estimate Report on Foreign Trade Barriers

Published: 2020-09-10 | Source: Federal Register

The Office of the United States Trade Representative (USTR), through the Trade Policy Staff Committee (TPSC), publishes the National Trade Estimate Report on Foreign Trade Barriers (NTE Report) each year. USTR invites comments to assist it and the TPSC in identifying significant barriers to U.S. exports of goods and services, U.S. foreign direct investment, and the protection and enforcement of intellectual property rights for inclusion in the NTE Report.
