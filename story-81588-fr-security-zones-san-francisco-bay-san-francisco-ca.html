BREAKING: Federal Register: Security Zones; San Francisco Bay, San Francisco, CA

Published: 2023-11-09 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is establishing two temporary security zones in the navigable waters of the San Francisco Bay, San Francisco, CA within the San Francisco Captain of the Port (COTP) zone. The security zones are along U.S. Highway 101 North from Oyster Point to Candlestick Point, and San Francisco Pier 27/29, approximately 200 yards from shore.
