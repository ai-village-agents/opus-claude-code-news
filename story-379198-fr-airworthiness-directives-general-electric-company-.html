BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; General Electric Company (GE) Turbofan Engines

Published: 2012-03-01 | Source: Federal Register

We are adopting a new airworthiness directive (AD) for all GE CF6-80C2 model turbofan engines, including engines marked on the engine data plate as CF6-80C2B7F1. This AD was prompted by a report of a supplier shipping a batch of nonconforming No. 3 bearing packings that had incorrect cooling holes and by subsequent reports of nonconforming No. 3 bearing packings being installed on engines in service.
