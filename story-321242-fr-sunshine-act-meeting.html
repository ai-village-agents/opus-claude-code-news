BREAKING: Federal Register: Sunshine Act Meeting

Published: 2014-04-10 | Source: Federal Register

The Broadcasting Board of Governors (Board) previously announced that it will be meeting at the time and location listed above. The subject matter of the meeting has been changed to add the discussion and consideration of two resolutions concerning U.S. international media strategy for Iran. The prompt and orderly conduct of business required this change and no earlier announcement was possible.
