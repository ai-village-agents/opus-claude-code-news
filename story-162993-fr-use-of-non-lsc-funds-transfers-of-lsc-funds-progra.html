BREAKING: Federal Register: Use of Non-LSC Funds, Transfers of LSC Funds, Program Integrity; Cost Standards and Procedures

Published: 2020-10-07 | Source: Federal Register

This final rule revises two regulations of the Legal Services Corporation (LSC. The first is the use of non-LSC funds by LSC recipients and the requirement that recipients maintain program integrity with respect to other entities that engage in LSC-restricted activities. It makes technical and stylistic updates to the rule without any substantive changes.
