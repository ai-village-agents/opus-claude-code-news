BREAKING: Federal Register: Authorization To Manufacture and Distribute Postage Evidencing Systems

Published: 2019-10-07 | Source: Federal Register

The Postal Service proposes to amend its Postage Evidencing Systems regulations. These changes would put the financial responsibility for returned checks and returned Automatic Clearinghouse (ACH) debit payments on the applicable resetting company (RC) and PC Postage provider.
