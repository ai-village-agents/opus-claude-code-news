BREAKING: Federal Register: New Source Performance Standards Review for Secondary Lead Smelters

Published: 2023-11-20 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is finalizing amendments to the new source performance standards (NSPS) for secondary lead smelters pursuant to the periodic review required by the Clean Air Act (CAA). Specifically, the EPA is finalizing revisions to the NSPS that applies to affected secondary lead smelters constructed, reconstructed, or modified after December 1, 2022 (NSPS subpart La).
