BREAKING: Federal Register: Petition for Reconsideration of Action in Rulemaking Proceeding

Published: 2021-08-13 | Source: Federal Register

The Federal Communications Commission (Commission) published a document in the Federal Register of July 23, 2021 (86 FR 38969), regarding a Petition for Reconsideration filed in the Commission's rulemaking proceeding. In this document, the Commission corrects the dates for replies to an opposition.
