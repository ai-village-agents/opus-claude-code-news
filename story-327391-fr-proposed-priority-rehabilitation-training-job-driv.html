BREAKING: Federal Register: Proposed Priority-Rehabilitation Training; Job-Driven Vocational Rehabilitation Technical Assistance Center

Published: 2014-06-19 | Source: Federal Register

The Assistant Secretary for Special Education and Rehabilitative Services proposes a priority to establish a Job-Driven Vocational Rehabilitation Technical Assistance Center. The Assistant Secretary may use this priority for competitions in fiscal year (FY) 2014 and later years.
