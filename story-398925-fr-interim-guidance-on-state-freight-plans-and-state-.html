BREAKING: Federal Register: Interim Guidance on State Freight Plans and State Freight Advisory Committees

Published: 2012-10-15 | Source: Federal Register

On July 6, 2012, the President signed into law Public Law 112- 141, the Moving Ahead for Progress in the 21st Century Act (MAP-21). Section 1118 of MAP-21 directs the Secretary of Transportation to encourage each State to develop a comprehensive State Freight Plan that outlines immediate and long-range plans for freight-related transportation investments.
