BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Federal Advisory Committee Meeting; Manufactured Housing Consensus Committee (MHCC)

Published: 2025-06-09 | Source: Federal Register

This notice sets forth the schedule and proposed agenda for a meeting of the Manufactured Housing Consensus Committee (MHCC) to be held on June 24, 2025, from 11:00 a.m. to 5:00 p.m. Eastern Daylight Time (EDT) via teleconference and webinar. The meeting is open to the public. The agenda provides an opportunity for interested parties to comment on the business before the MHCC.
