BREAKING: Federal Register: Definition of “Waters of the United States”-Schedule of Public Meetings

Published: 2017-08-28 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) and the U.S. Department of the Army (the agencies) will hold ten teleconferences to hear from stakeholders their recommendations to revise the definition of "Waters of the United States" under the Clean Water Act (CWA).
