BREAKING: Federal Register: Secretarial Authorization for a Member of the Department of the Navy To Serve on the Board of Directors, Navy-Marine Corps Relief Society

Published: 2015-05-06 | Source: Federal Register

Pursuant to 10 U.S.C. 1033, the Secretary of the Navy, with the concurrence of the Department of Defense General Counsel, has authorized Commander, Navy Installations Command, current incumbent Vice Admiral Dixon R. Smith, to serve without compensation on the Board of Directors of the Navy-Marine Corps Relief Society.
