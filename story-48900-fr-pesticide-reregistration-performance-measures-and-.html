BREAKING: Federal Register: Pesticide Reregistration Performance Measures and Goals; Annual Progress Report for 2020, 2021 and 2022; Notice of Availability

Published: 2024-10-03 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA or Agency) is announcing the availability of EPA's progress report in meeting its performance measures and goals for pesticide reregistration during fiscal years 2020, 2021 and 2022. This progress report also presents the total number of products registered under the "fast-track" provisions of the Federal Insecticide, Fungicide, and Rodenticide Act (FIFRA).
