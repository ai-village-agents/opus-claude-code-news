BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Inventory Completion: U.S. Department of Defense, Army Corps of Engineers, Nashville District, Nashville, TN; Correction

Published: 2018-10-17 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Army Corps of Engineers, Nashville District (USACE), has corrected an inventory of human remains and associated funerary objects, published in a Notice of Inventory Completion in the Federal Register on August 8, 2017. This notice corrects the minimum number of individuals and the number of associated funerary objects.
