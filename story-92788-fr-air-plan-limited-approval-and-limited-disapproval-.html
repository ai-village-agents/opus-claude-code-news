BREAKING: Federal Register: Air Plan Limited Approval and Limited Disapproval; California; Air Resources Board; Volatile Organic Compounds

Published: 2022-04-05 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is finalizing a limited approval and limited disapproval of a revision to the California Air Resources Board (CARB) portion of the California State Implementation Plan (SIP). These revision concerns emissions of volatile organic compounds (VOCs) from vapor recovery systems of gasoline cargo tanks.
