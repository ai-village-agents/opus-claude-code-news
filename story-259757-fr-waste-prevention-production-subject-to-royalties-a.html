BREAKING: Federal Register: Waste Prevention, Production Subject to Royalties, and Resource Conservation

Published: 2016-04-04 | Source: Federal Register

On February 8, 2016, the Bureau of Land Management (BLM) published in the Federal Register a proposed rule that would reduce waste of natural gas from venting, flaring, and leaks during oil and natural gas production activities on onshore Federal and Indian leases. The proposed rule would also clarify when produced gas lost through venting, flaring, or leaks is subject to royalties, and when oil and gas production used on site would be royalty-free.
