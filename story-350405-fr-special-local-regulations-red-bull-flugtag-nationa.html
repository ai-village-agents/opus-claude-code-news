BREAKING: Federal Register: Special Local Regulations; Red Bull Flugtag National Harbor Event, Potomac River; National Harbor Access Channel, MD

Published: 2013-03-26 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard proposes to establish special local regulations during the "Red Bull Flugtag National Harbor event," to be held on the waters of the Potomac River on September 21, 2013. These special local regulations are necessary to provide for the safety of life on navigable waters during the event.
