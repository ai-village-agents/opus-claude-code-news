BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone; Upper Mississippi River, Miles 109.9 to 647.8, Chester, IL to Guttenberg, IA

Published: 2019-06-20 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is extending a temporary safety zone on the navigable waters of the Upper Mississippi River from mile marker (MM) 109.9 to MM 647.8 between Chester, IL and Guttenberg, IA. This action is necessary to provide for the safety of persons, vessels, and the marine environment on these navigable waters as a result of increasing flood conditions on the river that threaten to overtop levees.
