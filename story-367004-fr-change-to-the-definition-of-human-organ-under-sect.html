BREAKING: Federal Register: Change to the Definition of “Human Organ” Under Section 301 of the National Organ Transplant Act of 1984

Published: 2013-10-02 | Source: Federal Register

This notice seeks public comment on the proposed change in the definition of "human organ" in section 301 of the National Organ and Transplant Act of 1984, as amended, (NOTA) to explicitly incorporate hematopoietic stem cells (HSCs) within peripheral blood in the definition of "bone marrow."
