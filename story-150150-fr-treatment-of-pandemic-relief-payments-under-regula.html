BREAKING: Federal Register: Treatment of Pandemic Relief Payments Under Regulation E and Application of the Compulsory Use Prohibition

Published: 2020-04-27 | Source: Federal Register

The Bureau of Consumer Financial Protection (Bureau) is issuing this interpretive rule to provide guidance to government agencies distributing aid to consumers in response to the COVID-19 pandemic. The Bureau concludes in this interpretive rule that certain pandemic-relief payments are not "government benefits" for purposes of Regulation E and the Electronic Fund Transfer Act (EFTA) and are therefore not subject to the compulsory use prohibition in EFTA, if certain conditions are met.
