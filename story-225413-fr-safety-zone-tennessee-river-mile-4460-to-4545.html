BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety zone; Tennessee River, Mile 446.0 to 454.5

Published: 2017-01-13 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard proposes to establish a safety zone for all waters of the Tennessee River, beginning at mile marker 446.0 and ending at mile marker 454.5 during periods of high water flow. High water flow is determined by flow rates that have reached or exceeded 100,000 cubic feet per second at Chickamauga lock and dam on the Tennessee River at mile marker 471.0.
