BREAKING: Federal Register: Wyoming Regulatory Program

Published: 2013-02-26 | Source: Federal Register

We are announcing receipt of a proposed amendment to the Wyoming regulatory program (hereinafter, the "Wyoming program") under the Surface Mining Control and Reclamation Act of 1977 ("SMCRA" or "the Act").
