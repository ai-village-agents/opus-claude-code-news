BREAKING: Federal Register: Implementation of the Administrative False Claims Act

Published: 2025-12-30 | Source: Federal Register

The National Aeronautics and Space Administration is proposing to amend its regulations in order to fully implement the Administrative False Claims Act (AFCA), as amended by the Fiscal Year 2025 National Defense Authorization Act.
