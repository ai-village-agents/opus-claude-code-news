BREAKING: Federal Register: Professional Conduct of Attorneys Practicing Under the Cognizance and Supervision of the Judge Advocate General

Published: 2013-05-01 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of the Navy (DoN) is revising its Rules of Professional Conduct and procedures for receiving, processing, and taking action on complaints of professional misconduct made against attorneys practicing under the supervision of the Judge Advocate General of the Navy (JAG). The revision to this part generally aligns with recent changes to the American Bar Association Model Rules of Professional Conduct.
