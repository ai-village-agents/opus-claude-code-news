BREAKING: Federal Register: Hours of Service of Drivers: Application for Exemption; National Pork Producers Council (NPPC)

Published: 2017-10-31 | Source: Federal Register

FMCSA announces that the National Pork Producers Council (NPPC) has requested an exemption from the requirement that a motor carrier require each of its drivers to use an electronic logging device (ELD) no later than December 18, 2017, to record the driver's hours-of- service (HOS).
