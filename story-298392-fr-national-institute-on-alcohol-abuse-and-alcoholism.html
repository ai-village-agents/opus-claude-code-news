BREAKING: Federal Register: National Institute on Alcohol Abuse and Alcoholism; Notice of Presentation

Published: 2015-07-08 | Source: Federal Register

The National Institute on Alcohol Abuse and Alcoholism (NIAAA) will host an online presentation to enable public discussion of the Institute's proposal to create a new division; Division of Medications Development. The proposal seeks to better reflect the NIAAA priorities by increasing the emphasis on medications development efforts on treating alcohol use disorders (AUD).
