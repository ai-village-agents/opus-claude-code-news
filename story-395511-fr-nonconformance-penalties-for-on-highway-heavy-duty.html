BREAKING: Federal Register: Nonconformance Penalties for On-Highway Heavy-Duty Diesel Engines

Published: 2012-09-05 | Source: Federal Register

EPA is taking final action to establish nonconformance penalties (NCPs) for manufacturers of heavy heavy-duty diesel engines (HHDDE) in model years 2012 and later for emissions of oxides of nitrogen (NOX) because we have found the criteria for NCPs and the Clean Air Act have been met.
