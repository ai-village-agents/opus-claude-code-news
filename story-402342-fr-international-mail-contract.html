BREAKING: Federal Register: International Mail Contract

Published: 2012-11-27 | Source: Federal Register

The Commission is noticing a recent Postal Service filing concerning a Type 2 rate adjustment in conjunction with a mail contract with China Post. This notice informs the public of the filing, invites public comment, and takes other administrative steps.
