BREAKING: Federal Register: Intent To Prepare an Environmental Impact Statement on the Durham-Orange Light Rail (LRT) Project, Durham and Orange Counties, NC

Published: 2012-04-03 | Source: Federal Register

The Federal Transit Administration (FTA) and the Research Triangle Regional Public Transportation Authority, dba "Triangle Transit," intend to prepare an Environmental Impact Statement (EIS) to study a proposed premium transit service corridor in Durham and Orange Counties, North Carolina.
