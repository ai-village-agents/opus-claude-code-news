BREAKING: Federal Register: Certain New Chemicals; Receipt and Status Information for January 2019; Correction

Published: 2019-05-30 | Source: Federal Register

EPA is hereby correcting information presented in a notice that published in the Federal Register of April 10, 2019. That notice provided information concerning submissions to EPA under TSCA Section 5, but inadvertently duplicated test submission information from the previous month. EPA is providing the test information received in January 2019, and providing an opportunity for public comment on this information.
