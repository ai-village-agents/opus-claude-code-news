BREAKING: Federal Register: Proposed Collection; Comment Request; Correction

Published: 2013-08-27 | Source: Federal Register

On Tuesday, August 20, 2013 (78 FR 51172-51173), the Department of Defense published a notice titled Proposed Collection; Comment Request. Subsequent to the publication of this notice in the Federal Register, DoD discovered that the Docket ID was printed incorrectly to read DoD-2008-HA-0180. The Docket ID is corrected to read as set forth in this notice. The corrected Docket ID should read DoD-2013-HA-0180.
