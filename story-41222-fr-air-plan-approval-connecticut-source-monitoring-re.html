BREAKING: Federal Register: Air Plan Approval; Connecticut; Source Monitoring, Record Keeping and Reporting

Published: 2024-07-08 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is approving a State Implementation Plan (SIP) revision submitted by the State of Connecticut. This revision addresses source monitoring in Connecticut. The principal revision is replacement of Regulations of Connecticut State Agencies (RCSA) section 22a-174-4 (source monitoring, record keeping and reporting) with a new regulation section 22a-174-4a, also called "source monitoring, record keeping and reporting," in the Connecticut SIP.
