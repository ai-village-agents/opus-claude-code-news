BREAKING: Federal Register: Special Conditions: Aerospace Quality Research and Development, Textron Aviation Inc. Model 680A Latitude Airplane; Rechargeable Lithium Batteries and Battery Systems Installations

Published: 2022-10-06 | Source: Federal Register

These special conditions are issued for the Textron Aviation Inc. (Textron) Model 680A Latitude airplane, as modified by Aerospace Quality Research and Development (AQRD). These airplanes will have a novel or unusual design feature when compared to the state of technology envisioned in the airworthiness standards for transport- category airplanes.
