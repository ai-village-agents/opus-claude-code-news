BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Crude Helium Auction and Sale for Fiscal Year 2017 Delivery

Published: 2016-07-05 | Source: Federal Register

The Secretary of the Interior (Secretary), through the Bureau of Land Management (BLM) New Mexico State Office, is issuing this Notice to conduct an auction and sale from the Federal Helium Program, administered by the BLM New Mexico Amarillo Field Office. The Helium Stewardship Act of 2013 (HSA) (Pub. L. 113-40) requires the BLM to conduct an annual auction and sale of crude helium.
