BREAKING: Federal Register: Requiring Electronic Access to the Electronic Folder by Certain Claimant Representatives

Published: 2016-04-18 | Source: Federal Register

This notice provides advance notification of the requirement that, for claims with certified electronic folders pending at the hearing or Appeals Council levels, an appointed representative must access and obtain a claimant's folder through Appointed Representative Services (ARS) in matters for which the representative requests direct fee payment.
