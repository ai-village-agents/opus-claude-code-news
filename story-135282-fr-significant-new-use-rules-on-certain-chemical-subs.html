BREAKING: Federal Register: Significant New Use Rules on Certain Chemical Substances (21-1.F)

Published: 2021-10-12 | Source: Federal Register

EPA is proposing significant new use rules (SNURs) under the Toxic Substances Control Act (TSCA) for chemical substances that were the subject of premanufacture notices (PMNs). The chemical substances received "not likely to present an unreasonable risk" determinations under TSCA.
