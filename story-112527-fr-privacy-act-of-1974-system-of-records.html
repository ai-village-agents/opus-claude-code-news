BREAKING: Federal Register: Privacy Act of 1974; System of Records

Published: 2022-12-16 | Source: Federal Register

In accordance with the Privacy Act of 1974, the DoD is modifying, renumbering, and renaming a Department-wide system of records titled DoD DPR-39, "DoD Personnel Accountability and Assessment System." This system of records is being modified to support additional information systems being established within the DoD using the same categories of data for the same purposes.
