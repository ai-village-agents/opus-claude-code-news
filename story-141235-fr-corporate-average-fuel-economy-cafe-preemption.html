BREAKING: Federal Register: Corporate Average Fuel Economy (CAFE) Preemption

Published: 2021-12-29 | Source: Federal Register

This document finalizes NHTSA's proposal to repeal in full "The Safer Affordable Fuel-Efficient (SAFE) Vehicles Rule Part One: One National Program," published September 27, 2019 (SAFE I Rule), in which NHTSA codified regulatory text and made additional pronouncements regarding the preemption of state and local laws related to fuel economy standards.
