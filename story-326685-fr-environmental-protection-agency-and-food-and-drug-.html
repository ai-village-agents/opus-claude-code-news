BREAKING: Federal Register: Environmental Protection Agency and Food and Drug Administration Advice About Eating Fish: Availability of Draft Update

Published: 2014-06-11 | Source: Federal Register

In March 2004, the Food and Drug Administration (FDA) and the U.S. Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) (the Agencies) jointly released a document entitled "What You Need to Know About Mercury in Fish and Shellfish" (the 2004 advice). FDA and EPA are now announcing a draft update that contains both advice and supplemental questions and answers for those who want to understand the advice in greater detail.
