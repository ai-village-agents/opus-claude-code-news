BREAKING: Federal Register: Sulfoxaflor; Pesticide Tolerances; Technical Correction

Published: 2013-06-26 | Source: Federal Register

EPA issued a final rule in the Federal Register of May 17, 2013, concerning the establishment of tolerances for the insecticide sulfoxaflor on multiple commodities. This document corrects an inadvertent error by moving the tolerances established under 40 CFR 180.670 to 40 CFR 180.668 and removing Sec. 180.670.
