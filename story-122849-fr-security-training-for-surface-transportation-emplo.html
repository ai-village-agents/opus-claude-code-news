BREAKING: Federal Register: Security Training for Surface Transportation Employees; Extension of Compliance Dates; Correcting Amendments

Published: 2021-05-04 | Source: Federal Register

This action amends the "Security Training for Surface Transportation Employees" (Security Training) final rule (published March 23, 2020, and amended May 1, 2020, and October 26, 2020) to extend the compliance date by which a security training program must be submitted to TSA, and make minor technical corrections.
