BREAKING: Federal Register: Non-Federal Oil and Gas Development Within the National Wildlife Refuge System

Published: 2014-02-24 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Fish and Wildlife Service (Service) is seeking comments to assist us in developing a proposed rule on managing activities associated with non-Federal oil and gas development on lands and waters of the National Wildlife Refuge System (Refuge System). Non-Federal oil and gas development refers to oil and gas activities associated with any private, state, or tribally owned mineral interest where the surface estate is administered by the Service as part of the Refuge System.
