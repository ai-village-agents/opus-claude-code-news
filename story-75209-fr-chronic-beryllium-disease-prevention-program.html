BREAKING: Federal Register: Chronic Beryllium Disease Prevention Program

Published: 2023-08-23 | Source: Federal Register

On June 7, 2016, the U.S. Department of Energy (DOE or the Department) published a Notice of Proposed Rulemaking (NOPR) in the Federal Register proposing to amend its current Chronic Beryllium Disease Prevention Program (CBDPP) regulations. In the NOPR, DOE proposed an action level of 0.05 micrograms of beryllium per cubic meter of air ([mu]g/m\3\), calculated as an 8-hour time-weighted average (TWA), but declined to propose a short-term exposure limit (STEL).
