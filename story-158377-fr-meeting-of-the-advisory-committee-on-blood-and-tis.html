BREAKING: Federal Register: Meeting of the Advisory Committee on Blood and Tissue Safety and Availability

Published: 2020-08-11 | Source: Federal Register

As required by the Federal Advisory Committee Act, the U.S. Department of Health and Human Services is hereby giving notice that the Advisory Committee on Blood and Tissue Safety and Availability (ACBTSA) will hold a meeting. The meeting will be open to the public. The committee will discuss recommendations to improve the blood community's response to future public health emergencies.
