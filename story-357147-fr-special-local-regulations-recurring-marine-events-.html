BREAKING: Federal Register: Special Local Regulations; Recurring Marine Events in the Seventh Coast Guard District

Published: 2013-06-11 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard will enforce the regulation pertaining to the Beaufort Water Festival from 1 p.m. through 4 p.m. on July 27, 2013. This action is necessary to ensure safety of life on navigable waters of the United States during the Beaufort Water Festival Air Show. During the enforcement period, the special local regulation establishes a regulated area which will all people and vessels will be prohibited from entering.
