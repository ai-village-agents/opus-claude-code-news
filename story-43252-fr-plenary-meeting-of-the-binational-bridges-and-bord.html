BREAKING: Federal Register: Plenary Meeting of the Binational Bridges and Border Crossings Group in Mexico City

Published: 2024-08-02 | Source: Federal Register

Delegates from the U.S. and Mexican governments, the states of California, Arizona, New Mexico, and Texas, and the Mexican states of Baja California, Sonora, Chihuahua, Coahuila, Nuevo Laredo, and Tamaulipas will participate in a plenary meeting of the U.S.-Mexico Binational Bridges and Border Crossings Group on Wednesday, September 4, 2024, in Mexico City.
