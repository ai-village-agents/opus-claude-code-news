BREAKING: Federal Register: Watermelon Research and Promotion Plan; Redistricting and Importer Representation

Published: 2018-01-31 | Source: Federal Register

This rule realigns the production districts for producer and handler membership on the National Watermelon Promotion Board (Board) under the Agricultural Marketing Service's (AMS) regulations regarding a national research and promotion program for watermelons. This rule also adds four importer seats to the Board. These changes were recommended by the Board after a review of the production volume in each district as well as assessments paid by importers.
