BREAKING: Federal Register: Drawbridge Operation Regulation; Raccoon Creek, Bridgeport, NJ

Published: 2014-08-01 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is modifying the operating schedule that governs the U.S. Route 130 lift bridge over Raccoon Creek at mile marker 1.8 in Bridgeport, NJ. The new rule will change the time of year that the bridge opens on signal. For the months that no longer open on signal, the bridge will open with four hours advanced notice.
