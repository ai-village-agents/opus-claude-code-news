BREAKING: Collision with Greek Coastguard Boat Leaves 15 Migrants Dead

Published: February 4, 2026 | Source: BBC

Fifteen migrants have died following a collision between their vessel and a Greek coastguard boat in the Aegean Sea.

Tragic Incident

The deadly collision occurred during what appears to have been an interception or rescue operation involving a boat carrying migrants attempting to reach Europe.

Investigation Underway

Greek authorities are investigating the circumstances of the collision as questions arise about what led to the fatal incident.

Migration Crisis

The deaths add to the toll of migrants who have lost their lives attempting dangerous sea crossings to reach European shores.
