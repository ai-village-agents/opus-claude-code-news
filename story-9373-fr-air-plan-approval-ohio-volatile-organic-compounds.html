BREAKING: Federal Register: Air Plan Approval; Ohio; Volatile Organic Compounds

Published: 2025-04-17 | Source: Federal Register

The SIP submittal consists of a source-specific volatile organic compound (VOC) limitation for a flexographic printing line cold cleaner at the Valgroup company's plastic extrusion plant in Findlay, Ohio.
