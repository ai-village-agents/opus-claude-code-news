BREAKING: Federal Register: Hydroelectric Licensing Regulations Under the America's Water Infrastructure Act of 2018

Published: 2019-04-24 | Source: Federal Register

In this final rule, the Federal Energy Regulatory Commission (Commission) is establishing an expedited process for issuing original licenses for qualifying facilities at existing nonpowered dams and for closed-loop pumped storage projects, pursuant to sections 3003 and 3004 of the America's Water Infrastructure Act of 2018.
