BREAKING: Federal Register: Modification of Class B Airspace; Minneapolis, MN

Published: 2013-10-25 | Source: Federal Register

This action amends the Minneapolis, MN, Class B airspace area to contain large turbine-powered aircraft conducting published instrument procedures at the Minneapolis-St. Paul International Airport (MSP), MN, within Class B airspace.
