BREAKING: Federal Register: Tribal Notice To Consult on Implementation of the Inflation Reduction Act

Published: 2022-12-28 | Source: Federal Register

The Bureau of Reclamation (Reclamation) is publishing this notice to announce that Reclamation plans to consult with federally recognized Indian Tribes in Reclamation's 17 western states on implementation of the Inflation Reduction Act as it applies to Tribes.
