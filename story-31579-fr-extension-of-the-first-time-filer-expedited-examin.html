BREAKING: Federal Register: Extension of the First-Time Filer Expedited Examination Pilot Program

Published: 2024-03-11 | Source: Federal Register

On March 9, 2023, the United States Patent and Trademark Office (USPTO) implemented the First-Time Filer Expedited Examination Pilot Program, which permits patent applications from certain micro entity first-time filers to be advanced out of turn for examination and reviewed earlier (accorded special status).
