BREAKING: Federal Register: Recommendations for Hepatitis C Screening Among Adults-2019; Request for Comment

The Centers for Disease Control and Prevention (CDC) within the Department of Health and Human Services announces the opening of a public docket to obtain public comment on proposed new recommendations for hepatitis C virus (HCV) infection screening for adults, including pregnant women.
