BREAKING: Federal Register: Recommendations To Reduce the Risk of Transfusion-Transmitted Malaria; Guidance for Industry; Availability

Published: 2022-12-08 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA, Agency, or we) is announcing the availability of a final guidance entitled "Recommendations to Reduce the Risk of Transfusion-Transmitted Malaria." The guidance document provides blood establishments that collect blood and blood components with FDA's recommendations to reduce the risk of transfusion-transmitted malaria (TTM).
