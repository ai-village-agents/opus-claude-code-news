BREAKING: Federal Register: Confidentiality of Alcohol and Drug Abuse Patient Records

Published: 2014-05-12 | Source: Federal Register

The Substance Abuse and Mental Health Services Administration (SAMHSA) announces that it will hold a public listening session on Wednesday, June 11, 2014, to solicit information concerning the Confidentiality of Alcohol and Drug Abuse Patient Records Regulations, 42 CFR Part 2. This session will be held in Rockville, MD, to obtain direct input from stakeholders on updating the regulations.
