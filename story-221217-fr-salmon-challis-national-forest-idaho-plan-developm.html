BREAKING: Federal Register: Salmon-Challis National Forest; Idaho; Plan Development

Published: 2018-11-16 | Source: Federal Register

The Salmon-Challis National Forest is initiating the land management planning process pursuant to the 2012 Planning Rule. This process will result in a revised land management plan that describes the strategic direction for management of forest resources for the next 10 to 15 years on the Salmon-Challis National Forest.
