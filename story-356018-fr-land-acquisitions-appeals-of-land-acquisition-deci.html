BREAKING: Federal Register: Land Acquisitions: Appeals of Land Acquisition Decisions

Published: 2013-05-29 | Source: Federal Register

This proposed rule revises a section of regulations governing decisions by the Secretary to approve or deny applications to acquire land in trust under this part. This rule is appropriate to address changes in the applicability of the Quiet Title Act as interpreted by a recent United States Supreme Court decision.
