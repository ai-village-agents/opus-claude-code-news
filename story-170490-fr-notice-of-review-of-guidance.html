BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Review of Guidance

Published: 2019-02-05 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Department of Transportation (Department or DOT) is reviewing its existing guidance documents to evaluate their continued necessity and determine whether they need to be updated or revised. As part of this review, the Department invites the public to identify and provide input on existing guidance documents that are good candidates for repeal, replacement, or modification.
