BREAKING: Federal Register: Certain Uncoated Paper From Brazil: Affirmative Preliminary Determination of Circumvention of the Antidumping Duty Order for Uncoated Paper Rolls

Published: 2021-01-27 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Commerce (Commerce) preliminarily determines that imports of certain uncoated paper rolls from Brazil are circumventing the antidumping duty (AD) order on certain uncoated paper from Brazil. As a result, all imports of certain uncoated paper rolls from Brazil from Ahlstrom Brasil Ltd. (Ahlstrom) will be subject to suspension of liquidation on or after October 18, 2019.
