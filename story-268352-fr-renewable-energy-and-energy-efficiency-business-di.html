BREAKING: Federal Register: Renewable Energy and Energy Efficiency Business Directory Survey

Published: 2016-07-11 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Departments of State, Commerce, and Energy (the "Interagency Team") announce an opportunity for U.S.-based suppliers and providers of clean energy, smart grid, and energy efficiency solutions to participate in an interactive directory of renewable energy and energy efficiency solutions. The Interagency Team has developed the beta version of an interactive app to serve as a mobile business directory for U.S. clean energy exporters, which can be accessed at http://www.re3app.com/.
