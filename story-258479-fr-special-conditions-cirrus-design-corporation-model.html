BREAKING: Federal Register: Special Conditions: Cirrus Design Corporation, Model SF50; Whole Airplane Parachute Recovery System

Published: 2016-03-18 | Source: Federal Register

This action proposes special conditions for the Cirrus Design Corporation (Cirrus), model SF50 airplane. This airplane will have a novel or unusual design feature(s) associated with a whole airplane parachute recovery system. The applicable airworthiness regulations do not contain adequate or appropriate safety standards for this design feature.
