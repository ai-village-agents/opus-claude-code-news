BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Airbus Airplanes

Published: 2013-08-29 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA is correcting airworthiness directive (AD) 2013-05-08 that published in the Federal Register. AD 2013-05-08 applies to all Airbus Model A330-200 and A330-300 series airplanes, and Model A340-200 and A340-300 series airplanes. Two paragraphs of AD 2013-05-08 incorrectly specify flight control secondary computers (FCSCs), rather than flight control primary computers (FCPCs). This document corrects those errors. In all other respects, the original document remains the same.
