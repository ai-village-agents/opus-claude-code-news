BREAKING: Federal Register: EPA Responses to Certain State Designation Recommendations for the 2010 Sulfur Dioxide Primary National Ambient Air Quality Standard: Notice of Availability and Public Comment Period

Published: 2020-08-21 | Source: Federal Register

Notice is hereby given that the Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) has posted our responses to certain state designation recommendations for the 2010 Sulfur Dioxide (SO2) Primary National Ambient Air Quality Standard (NAAQS) on the Agency's website and electronic docket. These responses include our intended designations for the affected areas, specifically all remaining undesignated areas for the 2010 SO2 NAAQS in the United States.
