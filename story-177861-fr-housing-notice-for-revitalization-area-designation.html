BREAKING: Federal Register: Housing Notice for Revitalization Area Designation Criteria; Solicitation of Comment

Published: 2019-04-30 | Source: Federal Register

Section 204(h) of the National Housing Act authorizes HUD to make HUD-held single family homes and formerly insured mortgages on Single Family properties, referred to as "eligible assets," available for sale in a manner that promotes revitalization, through expanded homeownership opportunities, in "revitalization areas."
