BREAKING: Federal Register: Air Plan Approval; California; South Coast Air Quality Management District

Published: 2021-08-24 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is proposing to approve revisions to the South Coast Air Quality Management District (SCAQMD) portion of the California State Implementation Plan (SIP). These revisions concern emissions of volatile organic compounds (VOCs) from marine and pleasure craft coating operations.
