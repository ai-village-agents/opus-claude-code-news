BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Request to Rescind Buy America Waiver for Minivans and Minivan Chassis

Published: 2012-08-03 | Source: Federal Register

The Vehicle Production Group LLC (VPG) has asked the Federal Transit Administration (FTA) to rescind the Buy America non- availability waiver it issued on June 21, 2010, for minivans and minivan chassis. According to VPG, it manufactures minivans and minivan chassis that comply with all Buy America requirements under 49 CFR 661.11, including the final assembly requirement that was the subject of the June 2010 waiver.
