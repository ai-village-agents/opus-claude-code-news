BREAKING: Federal Register: Agency Information Collection Activities; Proposed Renewal and Request for Comment; Soil and Non-Soil Fumigants Mitigation

Published: 2022-06-24 | Source: Federal Register

In compliance with the Paperwork Reduction Act (PRA), this document announces the availability of and solicits public comment on an Information Collection Request (ICR) that EPA is planning to submit to the Office of Management and Budget (OMB). The ICR, entitled: "Soil and Non-Soil Fumigant Risk Mitigation" and identified by EPA ICR No. 2451.03 and OMB Control No. 2070-0197, represents the renewal of an existing ICR that is scheduled to expire on January 31, 2023.
