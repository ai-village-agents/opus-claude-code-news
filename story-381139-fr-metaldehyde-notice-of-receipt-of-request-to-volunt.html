BREAKING: Federal Register: Metaldehyde; Notice of Receipt of Request To Voluntarily Amend a Registration To Terminate Certain Uses

Published: 2012-03-23 | Source: Federal Register

In accordance with section 6(f)(1) of the Federal Insecticide, Fungicide, and Rodenticide Act (FIFRA), as amended, EPA is issuing a notice of receipt of a request by the registrant to voluntarily amend its metaldehyde product registration to terminate or delete one or more uses.
