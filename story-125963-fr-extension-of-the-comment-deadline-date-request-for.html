BREAKING: Federal Register: Extension of the Comment Deadline Date; Request for Information on Transportation Equity Data

Published: 2021-06-14 | Source: Federal Register

On May 25, 2021, DOT published in the Federal Register a request for information (RFI) regarding an potential data and assessment tools that could assist in the ongoing and continuous evaluation of Federal policies and programs concerning equitable services and safety in the transportation sector. The RFI is assisting the DOT in responding to the Executive Order 13985, ``Advancing Racial Equity and Support for Underserved Communities Through the Federal Government'' (Equity E.O.).
