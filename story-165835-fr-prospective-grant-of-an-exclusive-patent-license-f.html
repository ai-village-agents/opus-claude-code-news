BREAKING: Federal Register: Prospective Grant of an Exclusive Patent License: Field-Deployable Mass Spectrometer Diagnostic for SARS, SARS-CoV-2 and Other Viruses, Bacteria and Bacterial Serovar, and Drug Impurities
