BREAKING: Federal Register: Extension and Redesignation of South Sudan for Temporary Protected Status

Published: 2022-03-03 | Source: Federal Register

Through this notice, the Department of Homeland Security (DHS) announces that the Secretary of Homeland Security (Secretary) is extending the designation of South Sudan for Temporary Protected Status (TPS) for 18 months, from May 3, 2022, through November 3, 2023, and redesignating South Sudan for 18 months, effective May 3, 2022, through November 3, 2023.
