BREAKING: Federal Register: National Forests in North Carolina; Revision of the Land Management Plan for the Nantahala and Pisgah National Forests

Published: 2022-01-21 | Source: Federal Register

The Forest Service, U.S. Department of Agriculture, is revising the Nantahala and Pisgah National Forests' Land Management Plan (Forest Plan). The Forest Service has prepared a Final Environmental Impact Statement (FEIS) for its revised Forest Plan, and a draft Record of Decision (ROD).
