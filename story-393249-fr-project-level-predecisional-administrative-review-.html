BREAKING: Federal Register: Project-Level Predecisional Administrative Review Process

Published: 2012-08-08 | Source: Federal Register

This proposed rule establishes the sole process by which the public may file objections seeking predecisional administrative review for proposed projects and activities implementing land management plans, including projects authorized pursuant to the Healthy Forests Restoration Act of 2003 (HFRA).
