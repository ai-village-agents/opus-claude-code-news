BREAKING: Federal Register: Privacy Act of 1974; System of Records

Published: 2021-11-05 | Source: Federal Register

Pursuant to the provisions of the Privacy Act of 1974, notice is given that the Board of Governors of the Federal Reserve System (Board) proposes to establish a new system of records, entitled BGFRS- 44, "FRB--Public Health and Safety System."
