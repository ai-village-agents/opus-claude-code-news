BREAKING: Federal Register: National Chemical Transportation Safety Advisory Committee; June 2022 Meetings

Published: 2022-05-18 | Source: Federal Register

The National Chemical Transportation Safety Advisory Committee (Committee) will meet to discuss matters relating to the safe and secure marine transportation of hazardous materials. The meetings will be open to the public via both a virtual platform and limited in-person seating on a first come first served basis.
