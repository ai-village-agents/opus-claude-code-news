BREAKING: Federal Register: 2016 Parenteral Drug Association/Food and Drug Administration Joint Conference: Aligning Manufacturing Goals With Patient Needs Through Successful Innovation and Compliance

Published: 2016-03-22 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA) is announcing a public conference, to be held in cosponsorship with the Parenteral Drug Association (PDA), entitled "Aligning Manufacturing Goals with Patient Needs through Successful Innovation and Compliance." The conference will cover current issues affecting the industry as well as explore strategies to facilitate the development and continuous improvement of safe and effective medical products.
