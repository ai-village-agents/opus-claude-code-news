BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Intent of Waiver With Respect to Land; Bolton Field Airport, Columbus, OH

Published: 2018-01-24 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA is considering a proposal to change 2.128 acres of airport land from aeronautical use to non-aeronautical use and to authorize the lease of airport property located at Bolton Field Airport, Columbus, OH. The aforementioned land is not needed for aeronautical use. The property is located in the northeast corner of the airport adjacent to the existing Columbus State Community College Building.
