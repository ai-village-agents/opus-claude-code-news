BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Airbus SAS Airplanes

Published: 2019-11-26 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA is adopting a new airworthiness directive (AD) for certain Airbus SAS Model A350-941 airplanes. This AD was prompted by the results of a structural analysis, which identified that the upper frame fittings (UFFs) of the forward cargo door surrounding structure have a low fatigue life.
