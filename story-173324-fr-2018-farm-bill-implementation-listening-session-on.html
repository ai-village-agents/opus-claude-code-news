BREAKING: Federal Register: 2018 Farm Bill Implementation Listening Session on Hemp

Published: 2019-03-05 | Source: Federal Register

In preparing to implement the Agriculture Improvement Act of 2018 (commonly referred to as the 2018 Farm Bill), the Agricultural Marketing Service (AMS) will host a listening session for initial public input about a new program to regulate hemp production.
