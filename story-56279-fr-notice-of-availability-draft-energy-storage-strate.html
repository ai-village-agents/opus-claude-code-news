BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Availability: Draft Energy Storage Strategy and Roadmap

Published: 2024-12-20 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Department of Energy (DOE or the Department) seeks public comment to inform development of its Energy Storage Strategy and Roadmap (SRM). DOE is seeking input from the public, especially from interested individuals and entities, such as industry, academia, research laboratories, government agencies, and other stakeholders in the energy storage sector, on its draft Energy Storage SRM.
