BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Cameron Balloons Ltd. Burner Assemblies

Published: 2022-05-05 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA proposes to adopt a new airworthiness directive (AD) for certain Cameron Balloons Ltd. (Cameron) Stratus double burner assemblies installed on hot air balloons. This proposed AD was prompted by reports from mandatory continuing airworthiness information (MCAI) originated by an aviation authority of another country to identify and correct an unsafe condition on an aviation product.
