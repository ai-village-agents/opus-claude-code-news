BREAKING: Federal Register: Determination That GLUCOTROL (Glipizide) Tablets, 5 Milligrams and 10 Milligrams, Were Not Withdrawn From Sale for Reasons of Safety or Effectiveness

Published: 2024-04-05 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA or Agency) has determined that GLUCOTROL (glipizide) tablets, 5 milligrams (mg) and 10 mg, were not withdrawn from sale for reasons of safety or effectiveness.
