BREAKING: Federal Register: Knowledge Testing of New Entrant Motor Carriers, Freight Forwarders and Brokers

Published: 2014-03-10 | Source: Federal Register

FMCSA announces that it will hold two public listening sessions on March 28 and April 7, 2014, to solicit ideas and information concerning sections 32101 and 32916 of the Moving Ahead for Progress in the 21st Century Act. These provisions require the assessment of applicants' knowledge of regulations and industry practices for persons seeking registration authority as motor carriers [property, passenger, and household goods (HHG)], freight forwarders, and brokers.
