BREAKING: Federal Register: Office of Engineering and Technology Seeks Comment on Measurements of LTE Into DTV Interference

Published: 2014-06-30 | Source: Federal Register

In this document, the Federal Communications Commission's (Commission) Office of Engineering and Technology (OET) seeks to supplement the record in the incentive auction proceeding by inviting comment on measurements of wireless Long-Term Evolution (LTE) interference into digital television (DTV) receivers conducted by OET engineers
