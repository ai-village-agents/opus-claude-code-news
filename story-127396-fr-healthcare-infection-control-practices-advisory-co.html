BREAKING: Federal Register: Healthcare Infection Control Practices Advisory Committee (HICPAC) Meeting

Published: 2021-07-01 | Source: Federal Register

In accordance with the Federal Advisory Committee Act, the CDC announces the following meeting for the Healthcare Infection Control Practices Advisory Committee (HICPAC). This virtual meeting is open to the public, limited only by audio and web conference lines (300 audio and web conference lines are available). Registration is required.
