BREAKING: Federal Register: Clarification of VA's Processing of Survivors Benefits Claims

Published: 2026-01-22 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Veterans Affairs (VA) amends its adjudication regulations concerning survivors benefits claims to ensure that VA provides the most beneficial outcome for surviving spouses and children. This final rule clarifies that a surviving spouse or child claimant for either Survivors Pension or dependency and indemnity compensation (DIC) is entitled to the greater benefit.
