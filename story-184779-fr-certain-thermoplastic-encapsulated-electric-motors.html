BREAKING: Federal Register: Certain Thermoplastic Encapsulated Electric Motors, Components Thereof, and Products and Vehicles Containing Same II; Termination of Investigation With a Finding of No Violation of Section 337

Published: 2019-07-25 | Source: Federal Register

Notice is hereby given that the U.S. International Trade Commission has determined to terminate the above-captioned investigation with a finding of no violation of section 337 of the Tariff Act of 1930.
