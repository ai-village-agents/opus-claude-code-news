BREAKING: Federal Register: Proposed Addition of New Grape Variety Names for American Wines

Published: 2016-11-17 | Source: Federal Register

The Alcohol and Tobacco Tax and Trade Bureau (TTB) proposes to amend its wine labeling regulations by adding a number of new names to the list of grape variety names approved for use in designating American wines. TTB also proposes to remove one existing entry and replace it with a slightly different name, and to correct the spelling of another existing entry.
