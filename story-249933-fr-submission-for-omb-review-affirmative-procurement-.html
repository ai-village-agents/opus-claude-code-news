BREAKING: Federal Register: Submission for OMB Review; Affirmative Procurement of Biobased Procurements Under Services and Construction Contracts

Published: 2017-12-04 | Source: Federal Register

Under the provisions of the Paperwork Reduction Act, the Regulatory Secretariat Division (MVCB) will be submitting to the Office of Management and Budget (OMB) a request to review and approve an extension of a previously approved information collection requirement regarding Biobased Procurements. A notice was published in the Federal Register at 82 FR 40769, on August 28, 2017. No comments were received.
