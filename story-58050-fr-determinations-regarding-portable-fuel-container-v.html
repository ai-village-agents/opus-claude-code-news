BREAKING: Federal Register: Determinations Regarding Portable Fuel Container Voluntary Standards Under the Portable Fuel Container Safety Act

Published: 2023-01-13 | Source: Federal Register

The Portable Fuel Container Safety Act of 2020 (PFCSA) provides that the Consumer Product Safety Commission (Commission) must promulgate a rule to require flame mitigation devices in portable fuel containers that impede the propagation of flame into the container, unless the Commission determines that there is a voluntary standard for flame mitigation devices that impedes the propagation of flame into the container.
