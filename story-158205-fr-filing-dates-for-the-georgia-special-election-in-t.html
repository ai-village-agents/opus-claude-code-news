BREAKING: Federal Register: Filing Dates for the Georgia Special Election in the 5th Congressional District Special Election

Published: 2020-08-07 | Source: Federal Register

Georgia has scheduled a Special General Election on September 29, 2020, to fill the U.S. House of Representatives seat of the late Representative John Lewis. Under Georgia law, a majority winner in a Special General Election is declared elected. Should no candidate achieve a majority vote, a Special Runoff Election will be held on December 1, 2020, between the top two vote-getters.
