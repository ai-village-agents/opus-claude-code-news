BREAKING: Federal Register: Draft TMDL for Sediment in the Indian Creek Watershed in Montgomery County, Pennsylvania

Published: 2021-05-10 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is publishing for public review and comment a draft total maximum daily load (TMDL) for sediment in the Indian Creek Watershed in Montgomery County, Pennsylvania pursuant to Section 303(d) of the Clean Water Act. To assist the public in their understanding of the draft TMDL and provide an overview of the TMDL process, EPA invites the public to attend a virtual public meeting.
