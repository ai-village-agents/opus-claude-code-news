BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Pratt & Whitney (PW) Division Turbofan Engines

Published: 2012-03-02 | Source: Federal Register

We propose to adopt a new airworthiness directive (AD) for certain PW4000 series turbofan engines. This proposed AD was prompted by reports of 3rd and 4th stage vane fractures in the low pressure turbine (LPT) of certain PW4000-94" and PW4000-100" turbofan engines. These fractures caused an uncontained engine failure, an LPT case puncture, and multiple in flight shutdowns.
