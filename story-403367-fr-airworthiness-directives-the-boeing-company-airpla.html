BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; The Boeing Company Airplanes

Published: 2012-12-10 | Source: Federal Register

We are adopting a new airworthiness directive (AD) for certain The Boeing Company Model 737-600, -700, -700C, -800, -900, and -900ER series airplanes. This AD was prompted by reports of flight crew failure to activate air data probe heat. This AD requires modifying the anti-icing system for the angle of attack sensor, the total air temperature, and the pitot probes.
