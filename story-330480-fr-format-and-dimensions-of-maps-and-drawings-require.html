BREAKING: Federal Register: Format and Dimensions of Maps and Drawings Required by the Commission's Hydropower Program

Published: 2014-07-24 | Source: Federal Register

The Commission is issuing this Final Rule to make minor changes to its regulations. This Final Rule amends requirements pertaining to the format and dimensions of maps and drawings submitted to the Commission by applicants and licensees in the Commission's hydropower program.
