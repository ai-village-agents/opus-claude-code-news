BREAKING: Federal Register: Collaborative Forest Restoration Program Technical Advisory Panel

Published: 2014-05-05 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Agriculture is seeking nominations for the Collaborative Forest Restoration Program (CFRP) Technical Advisory Panel (Panel) pursuant to Section 606 of the Community Forest Restoration Act (Act) (Pub. L. 106-393), and the Federal Advisory Committee Act (FACA), (5 U.S.C. App. 2). Additional information on the Panel can be found by visiting the CFRP Web site at: http:// www.fs.usda.gov/goto/r3/cfrp.
