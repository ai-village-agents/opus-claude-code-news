BREAKING: Federal Register: First Responder Network Authority Board Meetings

Published: 2014-09-04 | Source: Federal Register

The Board of the First Responder Network Authority (FirstNet) will convene an open public meeting of the Board on September 17, 2014, preceded by meetings of the Board Committees on September 16, 2014.
