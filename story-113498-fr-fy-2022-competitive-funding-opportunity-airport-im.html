BREAKING: Federal Register: FY 2022 Competitive Funding Opportunity: Airport Improvement Program Supplemental Discretionary Grants

Published: 2022-12-29 | Source: Federal Register

The Federal Aviation Administration (FAA) announces the opportunity to apply for approximately $268,728,965 in fiscal year (FY) 2022 competitive supplemental discretionary grants. The purpose of the supplemental discretionary grant program is to make grants to eligible airports for airport construction projects, associated airport capital planning, noise planning and noise mitigation projects, and energy and environmental sustainability projects.
