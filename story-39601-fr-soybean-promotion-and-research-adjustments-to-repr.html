BREAKING: Federal Register: Soybean Promotion and Research: Adjustments to Representation on the United Soybean Board

Published: 2024-06-17 | Source: Federal Register

The Agricultural Marketing Service (AMS) proposes to adjust the number of members on the United Soybean Board (Board) to reflect changes in production levels that have occurred since the Board was last reapportioned in 2021. As required by the Soybean Promotion, Research, and Consumer Information Act (Act), membership on the Board is reviewed every 3 years and adjustments are made accordingly.
