BREAKING: Federal Register: Revocation of Statement of Policy on Public Participation in Rulemaking

Published: 2013-10-28 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Department of Agriculture (USDA) is revoking the Statement of Policy titled "Public Participation in Rulemaking," published in the Federal Register on July 24, 1971 (36 FR 13804), which required agencies in USDA to follow the Administrative Procedure Act's (APA) notice-and-comment rulemaking procedures in situations where the APA does not require it.
