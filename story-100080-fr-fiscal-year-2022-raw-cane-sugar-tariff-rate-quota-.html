BREAKING: Federal Register: Fiscal Year 2022 Raw Cane Sugar Tariff-Rate Quota Increase and Extension of the Entry Period

Published: 2022-07-11 | Source: Federal Register

The Foreign Agricultural Service is providing notice of an increase in the fiscal year (FY) 2022 raw cane sugar tariff-rate quota (TRQ) of 90,718 metric tons raw value (MTRV) and an extension of the TRQ entry period.
