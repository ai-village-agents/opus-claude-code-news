BREAKING: Federal Register: Deadline for Notification of Intent To Use the Airport Improvement Program (AIP) Primary, Cargo, Nonprimary Entitlement Funds, and Discretionary Funds Available to Date for Fiscal Year 2025

Published: 2024-12-11 | Source: Federal Register

This action announces April 1, 2025, as the deadline for each airport sponsor to notify the FAA if it will use its Fiscal Year (FY) 2025 entitlement funds (referred to as apportionments) to accomplish Airport Improvement Program (AIP) eligible projects. Each sponsor has previously identified to the FAA such projects through the Airports Capital Improvement Plan (ACIP) process.
