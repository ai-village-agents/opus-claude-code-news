BREAKING: Federal Register: Freedom of Information Act; Privacy Act; Federal Tort Claims Act; Debt Collection Regulations

Published: 2018-10-10 | Source: Federal Register

This final rule revises Presidio Trust (Trust) regulations addressing requests under the Freedom of Information Act (FOIA), requests under the Privacy Act, administrative claims under the Federal Tort Claims Act (FTCA), and Debt Collection.
