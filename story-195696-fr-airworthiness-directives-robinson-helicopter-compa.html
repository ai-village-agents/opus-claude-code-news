BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Robinson Helicopter Company Helicopters

Published: 2019-12-17 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA proposes to adopt a new airworthiness directive (AD) for Robinson Helicopter Company Model R44 and R44 II helicopters with an agricultural spray system installed by Supplemental Type Certificate (STC) SR00286BO (spray system). This spray system is also known as a Simplex Manufacturing Company (Simplex) Model 244 spray system.
