BREAKING: Federal Register: Oil and Natural Gas Sector: Emission Standards for New, Reconstructed, and Modified Sources Reconsideration

Published: 2018-10-29 | Source: Federal Register

On October 15, 2018, the Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) published in the Federal Register a proposed rule titled "Oil and Natural Gas Sector: Emission Standards for New, Reconstructed, and Modified Sources Reconsideration." The comment period on the proposed action will end on December 17, 2018. The EPA is announcing that it will hold a public hearing on the proposed action.
