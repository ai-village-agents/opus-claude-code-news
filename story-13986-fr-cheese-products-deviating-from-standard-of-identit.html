BREAKING: Federal Register: Cheese Products Deviating From Standard of Identity; Temporary Permit for Market Testing

Published: 2025-06-18 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA or we) is announcing an amendment to the temporary permit issued to Bongards' Creameries to market test pasteurized process cheese deviating from the standard of identity for these cheeses by using extra virgin olive oil as the slice anti-sticking agent.
