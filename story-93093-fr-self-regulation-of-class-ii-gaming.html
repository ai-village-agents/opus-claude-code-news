BREAKING: Federal Register: Self-Regulation of Class II Gaming

Published: 2022-04-07 | Source: Federal Register

The National Indian Gaming Commission (NIGC) proposes to amend its regulations regarding self-regulation of Class II gaming under the Indian Gaming Regulatory Act. The proposed rule will amend the regulations to address an ambiguity in the petitioning process and clarify and expand the Office of Self-Regulation's (OSR) role once the Commission issues a certificate.
