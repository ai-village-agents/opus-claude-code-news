BREAKING: Federal Register: Onshore Oil and Gas Operations; Federal and Indian Oil and Gas Leases; Site Security

Published: 2016-11-17 | Source: Federal Register

This final rule replaces Onshore Oil and Gas Order No. 3, Site Security (Order 3), with new regulations codified in the Code of Federal Regulations (CFR).
