BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Empresa Brasileira de Aeronautica S.A. (Embraer) Airplanes

Published: 2014-08-26 | Source: Federal Register

We propose to adopt a new airworthiness directive (AD) for all Empresa Brasileira de Aeronautica S.A. (Embraer) Model EMB-135BJ airplanes. This proposed AD was prompted by a determination that more restrictive fuel limitations are needed. This proposed AD would require revising the maintenance or inspection program to incorporate new compliance times and fuel limitations.
