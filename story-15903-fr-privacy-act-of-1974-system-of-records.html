BREAKING: Federal Register: Privacy Act of 1974; System of Records

Published: 2025-07-15 | Source: Federal Register

The purpose of the Supervision and Examination Records system of record is to enable the Consumer Financial Protection Bureau (CFPB) to carry out its responsibilities with respect to supervised entities to ensure compliance with Federal consumer protection laws and identify unfair, deceptive, or abusive acts and practices in connection with consumer financial products and services.
