BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Mooney Aviation Company, Inc. (Mooney) Airplanes

Published: 2012-02-29 | Source: Federal Register

We are adopting a new airworthiness directive (AD) for certain Mooney Aviation Company, Inc. (Mooney) Models M20R and M20TN airplanes. This emergency AD was sent previously to all known U.S. owners and operators of these airplanes. This AD requires inspecting the tail pitch trim assembly for correct positioning and proper attachment and inspecting the Huck Bolt fasteners for proper security with repair as necessary.
