BREAKING: Federal Register: Environmental Technologies Trade Advisory Committee, Request for Nominations

Published: 2012-08-23 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Commerce, International Trade Administration (ITA) is requesting nominations for memberships on the Environmental Technologies Trade Advisory Committee (ETTAC). The ETTAC was established under the Federal Advisory Committee Act, 5 U.S.C. App., and pursuant to Section 2313(c) of the Export Enhancement Act of 1988, as amended, 15 U.S.C. 4728(c).
