BREAKING: Federal Register: Updates to the Uniform Standard for Waiver of the Ryan White HIV/AIDS Program Core Medical Services Expenditure

Published: 2024-08-20 | Source: Federal Register

The Ryan White HIV/AIDS Program (RWHAP) statute of the Public Health Service Act requires that RWHAP Parts A, B, and C recipients expend 75 percent of Parts A, B, and C grant funds on core medical services for individuals who are identified with HIV/AIDS and eligible for RWHAP services under the statute, after reserving statutorily permissible amounts for administrative and clinical quality management costs.
