BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Teleconference of the Chronic Hazard Advisory Panel on Phthalates and Phthalate Substitutes

Published: 2012-06-08 | Source: Federal Register

The Consumer Product Safety Commission ("CPSC" or "Commission") is announcing a teleconference and the seventh meeting of the Chronic Hazard Advisory Panel ("CHAP") on phthalates and phthalate substitutes.
