BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Inventory Completion Amendment: San Diego State University, San Diego, CA

Published: 2025-09-11 | Source: Federal Register

In accordance with the Native American Graves Protection and Repatriation Act (NAGPRA), San Diego State University (SDSU) has amended a notice of inventory completion published in the Federal Register on December 18, 2000. This notice amends the Indian Tribes or Native Hawaiian organizations with cultural affiliation.
