BREAKING: Federal Register: Media Bureau Freezes the Filing of Minor Change Applications for LPTV/Translator Stations

Published: 2018-01-10 | Source: Federal Register

This document announces a freeze on the filing of minor change applications by low power television and TV translator stations.
