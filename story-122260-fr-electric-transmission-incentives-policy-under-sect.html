BREAKING: Federal Register: Electric Transmission Incentives Policy Under Section 219 of the Federal Power Act

Published: 2021-04-26 | Source: Federal Register

The Federal Energy Regulatory Commission has proposed in this proceeding to revise its existing regulations that implemented section 219 of the Federal Power Act (FPA) in light of the changes in transmission development and planning over the last few years. This supplemental notice of proposed rulemaking proposes to modify the incentive proposed for transmitting and electric utilities that join Transmission Organizations in the March 20, 2020 notice of proposed rulemaking in this proceeding.
