BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; The Boeing Company Airplanes

Published: 2012-08-01 | Source: Federal Register

We propose to adopt a new airworthiness directive (AD) for certain The Boeing Company Model 737-200 and -200C series airplanes. This proposed AD was prompted by a report of elevator vibration and bearing swage failures.
