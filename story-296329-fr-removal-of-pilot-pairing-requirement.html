BREAKING: Federal Register: Removal of Pilot Pairing Requirement

Published: 2015-06-12 | Source: Federal Register

This final rule conforms Federal Aviation Administration regulations to International Civil Aviation Organization standards and the Fair Treatment for Experienced Pilots Act, both of which no longer contain a pilot pairing requirement.
