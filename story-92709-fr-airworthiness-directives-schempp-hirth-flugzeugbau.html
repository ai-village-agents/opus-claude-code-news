BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Schempp-Hirth Flugzeugbau GmbH Gliders

Published: 2022-04-04 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA is adopting a new airworthiness directive (AD) for certain Schempp-Hirth Flugzeugbau GmbH Model Janus, Mini-Nimbus HS-7, Nimbus-2, and Standard Cirrus gliders. This AD was prompted by mandatory continuing airworthiness information (MCAI) originated by an aviation authority of another country to identify and correct an unsafe condition on an aviation product.
