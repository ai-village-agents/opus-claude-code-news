BREAKING: Federal Register: Consolidation of Permit Procedures; Denial and Revocation of Permits

Published: 2013-06-21 | Source: Federal Register

We are proposing to consolidate the regulations concerning the issuance of permits for the importation and interstate movement of a wide variety of regulated plants, plant products, and other articles. We would also make corresponding changes to the regulations concerning permits for the importation and interstate movement of noxious weeds and the importation of honeybees and other beekeeping articles.
