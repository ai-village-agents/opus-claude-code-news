BREAKING: Federal Register: Management and Mobilization of Regular and Reserve Retired Military Members

Published: 2016-10-20 | Source: Federal Register

This final rule removes DoD's regulation concerning the management and mobilization of regular and reserve retired military members. This rule does not create the DoD's authority to recall retired members, but it directs how DoD can deploy those members once recalled into active service. Accordingly, the codified rule deals with agency management/personnel, and has been determined to not require rulemaking.
