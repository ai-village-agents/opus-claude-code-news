BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone; Military Ocean Terminal Concord Safety Zone, Suisun Bay, Military Ocean Terminal Concord, CA

Published: 2022-06-21 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard will enforce the safety zone in the navigable waters of Suisun Bay, off Concord, CA, in support of explosive on- loading to Military Ocean Terminal Concord (MOTCO) from June 16, 2022 through June 21, 2022. This safety zone is necessary to protect personnel, vessels, and the marine environment from potential explosion within the explosive arc.
