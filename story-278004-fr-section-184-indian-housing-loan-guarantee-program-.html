BREAKING: Federal Register: Section 184 Indian Housing Loan Guarantee Program Increase to Annual Premium

Published: 2016-11-01 | Source: Federal Register

The Section 184 Indian Housing Loan Guarantee program (Section 184 program) is a home mortgage program specifically designed for American Indian and Alaska Native families, Alaska villages, tribes, or tribally designated housing entities. Over the last five years, the Section 184 program has doubled the number of loans and eligible families being assisted by the program.
