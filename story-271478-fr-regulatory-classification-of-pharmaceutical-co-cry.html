BREAKING: Federal Register: Regulatory Classification of Pharmaceutical Co-Crystals; Draft Guidance for Industry; Availability

Published: 2016-08-17 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA or Agency) is announcing the availability of a draft guidance for industry entitled "Regulatory Classification of Pharmaceutical Co-Crystals." This guidance provides applicants planning to submit new drug applications (NDAs) and abbreviated new drug applications (ANDAs) with information on the appropriate regulatory classification of pharmaceutical co-crystal solid-state forms.
