BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of New System of Records; Response to Comments

Published: 2022-12-23 | Source: Federal Register

The United States Postal Service[supreg] (USPS) is responding to public comments regarding the creation of a new Customer Privacy Act System of Records (SOR) 845.000, Commercial Mail Receiving Agency (CMRA) Records. This new SOR was created to consolidate all CMRA paper and electronic records under one new and dedicated SOR in support of a future planned initiative to centralize CMRA records into an electronic database and improve the security of the In-Person enrollment process.
