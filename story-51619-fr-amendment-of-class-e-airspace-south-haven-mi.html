BREAKING: Federal Register: Amendment of Class E Airspace; South Haven, MI

Published: 2024-11-04 | Source: Federal Register

This action amends the Class E airspace at South Haven, MI. This action is the result of an airspace review conducted due to the decommissioning of the Pullman very high frequency omnidirectional range (VOR) as part of the VOR Minimum Operational Network (MON) Program.
