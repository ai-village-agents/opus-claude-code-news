BREAKING: Federal Register: Inyo National Forest; California; Revision of the Land Management Plan for the Inyo National Forest

Published: 2018-08-03 | Source: Federal Register

The Forest Service is revising the Inyo National Forest's Land and Resource Management Plan (forest plan). The Forest Service has prepared a Final Environmental Impact Statement (FEIS) for its revised forest plan and a draft Record of Decision (ROD).
