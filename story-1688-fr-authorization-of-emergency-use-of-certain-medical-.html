BREAKING: Federal Register: Authorization of Emergency Use of Certain Medical Devices During COVID-19; Availability

Published: 2026-01-21 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA or the Agency) is announcing the issuance of Emergency Use Authorizations (EUA) (the Authorizations) for certain medical devices related to Coronavirus Disease 2019 (COVID-19). FDA has issued the Authorizations listed in this document under the Federal Food, Drug, and Cosmetic Act (FD&C Act).
