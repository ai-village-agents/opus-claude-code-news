BREAKING: Federal Register: Clean Air Act Advisory Committee (CAAAC): Request for Nominations

Published: 2018-12-21 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) invites nominations from a diverse range of qualified candidates to be considered for appointment to its Clean Air Act Advisory Committee (CAAAC). Vacancies are anticipated to be filled by May 2019. Sources in addition to this Federal Register Notice may also be utilized in the solicitation of nominees.
