BREAKING: Federal Register: Removing Regulatory Barriers for Vehicles With Automated Driving Systems

Published: 2018-02-13 | Source: Federal Register

NHTSA is announcing a public meeting as part of the Agency's effort to seek public comments to identify any regulatory barriers in the existing Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standards (FMVSS) to the testing, compliance certification, and compliance verification of vehicles with Automated Driving Systems (ADSs) and certain unconventional interior designs.
