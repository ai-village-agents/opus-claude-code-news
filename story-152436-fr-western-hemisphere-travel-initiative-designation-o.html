BREAKING: Federal Register: Western Hemisphere Travel Initiative: Designation of an Approved Native American Tribal Card Issued by the Confederated Tribes of the Colville Reservation as an Acceptable Document To Denote Identity and Citizenship for Entry in the United States at Land and Sea Ports of Entry

Published: 2020-05-27 | Source: Federal Register

This notice announces that the Commissioner of U.S. Customs and Border Protection is designating an approved Native American tribal card issued by the Confederated Tribes of the Colville Reservation ("Colville Tribes") to U.S. and Canadian citizens as an acceptable travel document for purposes of the Western Hemisphere Travel Initiative.
