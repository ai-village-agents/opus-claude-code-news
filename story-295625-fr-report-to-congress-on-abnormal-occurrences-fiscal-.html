BREAKING: Federal Register: Report to Congress on Abnormal Occurrences; Fiscal Year 2014; Dissemination of Information

Published: 2015-06-05 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Nuclear Regulatory Commission (NRC) is making available NUREG-0090, Volume 37, "Report to Congress on Abnormal Occurrences: Fiscal Year 2014." The report describes those events that the NRC or an Agreement State identified as abnormal occurrences (AOs) during fiscal year (FY) 2014, based on the criteria defined in this report's Appendix A, "Abnormal Occurrence Criteria and Guidelines for Other Events of Interest."
