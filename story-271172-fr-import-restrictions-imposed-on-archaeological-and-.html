BREAKING: Federal Register: Import Restrictions Imposed on Archaeological and Ethnological Material of Syria

Published: 2016-08-15 | Source: Federal Register

This document amends the U.S. Customs and Border Protection (CBP) regulations to reflect the imposition of import restrictions on archaeological and ethnological material of Syria pursuant to the Protect and Preserve International Cultural Property Act. This document also contains the Designated List of Archaeological and Ethnological Material of Syria that describes the types of objects or categories of archaeological or ethnological material that are subject to import restrictions, if unlawful...
