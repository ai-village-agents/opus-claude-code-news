BREAKING: Federal Register: An Assessment of Potential Mining Impacts on Salmon Ecosystems of Bristol Bay, AK

Published: 2012-05-25 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is announcing a public comment period for the draft document titled, "An Assessment of Potential Mining Impacts on Salmon Ecosystems of Bristol Bay, Alaska" (EPA-910-R-12-004a-d). The document was prepared by the EPA's Region 10 (Pacific Northwest and Alaska), EPA's Office of Water, and EPA's Office of Research and Development.
