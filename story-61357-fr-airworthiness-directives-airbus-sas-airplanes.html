BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Airbus SAS Airplanes

Published: 2023-02-27 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA is adopting a new airworthiness directive (AD) for certain Airbus SAS Model A350-941 airplanes. This AD was prompted by a report that an interference was detected between the installed nut and the foot radius of a section of a certain frame (FR) on the right-hand side.
