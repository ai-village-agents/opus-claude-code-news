BREAKING: Federal Register: Open Internet Advisory Committee

Published: 2012-12-17 | Source: Federal Register

The Commission announces the next meeting date, time, and agenda of the Open Internet Advisory Committee (Committee). The Committee was established to track and evaluate the effects of the Commission's Open Internet rules, and to provide any recommendations it deems appropriate to the Commission regarding policies and practices related to preserving the open Internet.
