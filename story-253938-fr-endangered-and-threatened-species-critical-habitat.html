BREAKING: Federal Register: Endangered and Threatened Species; Critical Habitat for Endangered North Atlantic Right Whale

Published: 2016-01-27 | Source: Federal Register

We (NMFS) are issuing this final rule to replace the critical habitat for right whales in the North Atlantic with two new areas. The areas being designated as critical habitat contain approximately 29,763 nm \2\ of marine habitat in the Gulf of Maine and Georges Bank region (Unit 1) and off the Southeast U.S. coast (Unit 2).
