BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Review of Guidance

Published: 2019-03-08 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Department of Transportation (the Department or DOT) is extending the comment period for its Notice of Review of Guidance by 30 days. The comment period was originally scheduled to end April 8, 2019. It will now end May 8, 2019.
