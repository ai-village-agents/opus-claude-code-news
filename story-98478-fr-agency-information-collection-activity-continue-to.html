BREAKING: Federal Register: Agency Information Collection Activity; Continue To Collect Information: Oil and Gas Industry Safety Data Program

Published: 2022-06-16 | Source: Federal Register

On October 21, 2021, the Bureau of Transportation Statistics (BTS) announced its intention in a Federal Register Notice to request that the Office of Management and Budget (OMB) approve the following information collection: Voluntary Oil and Gas Industry Safety Data Program.
