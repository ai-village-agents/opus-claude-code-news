BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone; Mississippi River, Baton Rouge, LA

Published: 2017-12-29 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is establishing a temporary safety zone for all navigable waters from mile marker (MM) 229.5 to MM 230.5 Above Head of Passes on the Lower Mississippi River. This temporary safety zone is necessary to provide for the safety of life on these navigable waters near downtown, Baton Rouge, LA, during a fireworks display on December 31, 2017.
