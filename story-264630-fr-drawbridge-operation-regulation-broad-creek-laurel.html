BREAKING: Federal Register: Drawbridge Operation Regulation; Broad Creek, Laurel, DE

Published: 2016-05-27 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is changing the regulation that governs the operation of the Norfolk Southern Railroad Bridge over Broad Creek, mile 8.0, at Laurel, DE. This final rule changes the current regulation requiring a four-hour advance notice and allows the bridge to remain in the closed to navigation position.
