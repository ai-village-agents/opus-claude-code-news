BREAKING: Federal Register: Approval and Promulgation of State Plans for Designated Facilities and Pollutants; West Virginia; Control of Emissions From Existing Municipal Solid Waste Landfills

Published: 2019-07-01 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is proposing to approve a Clean Air Act (CAA) section 111(d) plan submitted by the West Virginia Department of Environmental Protection (WVDEP). This plan was submitted to fulfill the requirements of the CAA and in response to the EPA's promulgation of Emissions Guidelines and Compliance Times for municipal solid waste (MSW) landfills.
