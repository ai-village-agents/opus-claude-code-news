BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Intended Repatriation: University of Georgia, Laboratory of Archaeology, Athens, GA

Published: 2024-09-10 | Source: Federal Register

In accordance with the Native American Graves Protection and Repatriation Act (NAGPRA), the University of Georgia, Laboratory of Archaeology intends to repatriate a certain cultural item that meets the definition of an object of cultural patrimony and that has a cultural affiliation with the Indian Tribes or Native Hawaiian organizations in this notice.
