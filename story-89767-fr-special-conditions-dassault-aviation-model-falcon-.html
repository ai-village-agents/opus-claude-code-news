BREAKING: Federal Register: Special Conditions: Dassault Aviation Model Falcon 6X Airplane; Side Stick Controllers-Controllability and Maneuverability.

Published: 2022-02-25 | Source: Federal Register

These special conditions are issued for the Dassault Aviation (Dassault) Model Falcon 6X airplane. This airplane will have a novel or unusual design feature when compared to the state of technology envisioned in the airworthiness standards for transport category airplanes. This design feature is side-stick controllers for pitch and roll control. The applicable airworthiness regulations do not contain adequate or appropriate safety standards for this design feature.
