BREAKING: Federal Register: Privacy Act of 1974; System of Records

Published: 2022-12-07 | Source: Federal Register

ODNI provides notice that it is establishing a new Privacy Act system of records identified as ODNI-23, "Workforce Health and Safety Records." ODNI proposed to add a new system of records to its inventory of records systems subject to the Privacy Act of 1974, as amended. This action is necessary to meet the requirements of the Privacy Act to publish in the Federal Register the existence and character of records maintained by the agency.
