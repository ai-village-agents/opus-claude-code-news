BREAKING: Federal Register: NASA Advisory Council; Science Committee; Public Nominations for Subcommittees

Published: 2015-11-03 | Source: Federal Register

NASA announces its annual invitation for public nominations for service on the NASA Advisory Council's Science Committee subcommittees. Five science subcommittees report to the Science Committee of the NASA Advisory Council (NAC), a Federal advisory committee under the Federal Advisory Committee Act (FACA).
