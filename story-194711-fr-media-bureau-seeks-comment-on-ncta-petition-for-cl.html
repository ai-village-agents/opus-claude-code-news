BREAKING: Federal Register: Media Bureau Seeks Comment on NCTA Petition for Clarification of Order Denying Motion for Stay of Section 621 Third Report and Order

Published: 2019-12-03 | Source: Federal Register

NCTA--The Internet & Television Association (NCTA) has filed with the Commission a Petition for Clarification of the Media Bureau's Order Denying Motion for Stay of the Commission's Third Report and Order in the proceeding referenced above. In this document, the Media Bureau gives public notice of that Petition and seeks comment on it.
