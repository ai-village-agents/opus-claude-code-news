BREAKING: Federal Register: Approval and Promulgation of Air Quality Implementation Plans; West Virginia; Prevention of Significant Deterioration

Published: 2012-10-17 | Source: Federal Register

EPA is granting full approval of revisions to the West Virginia State Implementation Plan (SIP), submitted by the State of West Virginia through the West Virginia Department of Environmental Protection (WVDEP) on August 31, 2011, with the exception of the narrow issue of the requirement to include condensable emissions of particulate matter (condensables) in the definition of "regulated NSR pollutant" in the State's Prevention of Significant Deterioration (PSD) program.
