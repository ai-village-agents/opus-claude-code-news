BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Standard for Stationary Activity Centers

Published: 2025-03-27 | Source: Federal Register

In June 2019, the U.S. Consumer Product Safety Commission (CPSC or Commission) published a consumer product safety standard for stationary activity centers pursuant to section 104 of the Consumer Product Safety Improvement Act of 2008 (CPSIA).
