BREAKING: Federal Register: Filing Dates for The New Jersey Special Election in The 10th Congressional District

Published: 2012-04-11 | Source: Federal Register

New Jersey has scheduled elections on June 5, 2012, and November 6, 2012, to fill the U.S. House seat in the 10th Congressional District held by the late Representative Donald M. Payne. Committees required to file reports in connection with the Special Primary Election on June 5, 2012, shall file a 12-day Pre-Primary Report.
