BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Availability of Calendar Year 2023 Competitive Grant Funds for the Veterans Pro Bono Program

Published: 2022-03-10 | Source: Federal Register

The Legal Services Corporation (LSC) provides grants of federally-appropriated funds for civil legal services to low-income individuals and families. LSC administers the process of awarding grant funds for the Veterans Pro Bono Program to furnish effective, efficient, and high-quality pro bono legal services to eligible veterans appearing before the United States Court of Appeals for Veterans Claims (Court).
