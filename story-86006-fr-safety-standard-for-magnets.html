BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Standard for Magnets

Published: 2022-01-10 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Consumer Product Safety Commission (Commission or CPSC) has determined preliminarily that there is an unreasonable risk of injury and death, particularly to children and teens, associated with ingestion of one or more high-powered magnets.
