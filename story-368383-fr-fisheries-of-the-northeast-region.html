BREAKING: Federal Register: Fisheries of the Northeast Region

Published: 2013-10-29 | Source: Federal Register

This action serves as a notice that NMFS, on behalf of the Secretary of Commerce (Secretary), has determined that Georges Bank (GB) cod and Gulf of Maine (GOM) cod are subject to overfishing and continue to be in an overfished condition.
