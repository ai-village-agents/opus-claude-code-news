BREAKING: Federal Register: Announcing the Intent To Award a Single-Source Supplement for the National Paralysis Resource Center (PRC)

Published: 2024-07-16 | Source: Federal Register

The Administration for Community Living (ACL) announces the intent to award a single-source supplement to the current cooperative agreement held by the Christopher and Dana Reeve Foundation. The National Paralysis Resource Center (NPRC) is operated by the Christopher and Dana Reeve Foundation and offers important programmatic opportunities for persons with disabilities and older adults.
