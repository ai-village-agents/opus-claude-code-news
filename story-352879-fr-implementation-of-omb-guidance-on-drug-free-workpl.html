BREAKING: Federal Register: Implementation of OMB Guidance on Drug-Free Workplace Requirements

Published: 2013-04-22 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Department of Commerce is proposing to remove its regulation implementing the Governmentwide common rule on drug-free workplace requirements for financial assistance, and issuing a new regulation to adopt the Office of Management and Budget (OMB) guidance. This regulatory action implements the OMB's initiative to streamline and consolidate into one title of the CFR all Federal regulations on drug-free workplace requirements for financial assistance.
