BREAKING: Federal Register: Atlantic Highly Migratory Species; Atlantic Bluefin Tuna Fisheries

Published: 2015-11-04 | Source: Federal Register

NMFS is transferring 35 metric tons (mt) of Atlantic bluefin tuna (BFT) quota from the Harpoon category and 65 mt from the Reserve category to the General category for the remainder of the 2015 fishing year. This transfer results in adjusted quotas of 566.7 mt, 43.6 mt and 82.1 mt for the General, Harpoon, and Reserve categories, respectively.
