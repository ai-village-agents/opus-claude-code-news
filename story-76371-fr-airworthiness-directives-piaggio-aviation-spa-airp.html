BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Piaggio Aviation S.p.A. Airplanes

Published: 2023-09-07 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA proposes to adopt a new airworthiness directive (AD) for certain Piaggio Aviation S.p.A. (Piaggio) Model P-180 airplanes. This proposed AD was prompted by a report of corrosion on the various aluminum alloy reinforcements in the horizontal stabilizer (HS) central box caused by a humid environment inside the box from water ingress and/or condensation.
