BREAKING: Federal Register: Announcement of Renewal of Charter of the FCC Consumer Advisory Committee

Published: 2020-10-15 | Source: Federal Register

In accordance with the Federal Advisory Committee Act, the Federal Communications Commission announces that the renewal of the charter of the FCC Consumer Advisory Committee (CAC) has been approved by the General Services Administration (GSA) for a two-year period. The CAC is a federal advisory committee under the Federal Advisory Committee Act.
