BREAKING: Federal Register: Issuance of Biological Opinion

Published: 2026-01-29 | Source: Federal Register

In accordance with the statute of limitations provision in title 41 of the Fixing America's Surface Transportation Act (FAST-41) NMFS is providing this notification, upon request by the project sponsor (8 Star Alaska, LLC), of our issuance of a biological opinion for the Alaska Liquefied Natural Gas Project. We issued this biological opinion under the Endangered Species Act (ESA).
