BREAKING: Federal Register: Announcement of Test Providing Centralized Decision-Making Authority for Four CBP Centers of Excellence and Expertise

Published: 2012-08-28 | Source: Federal Register

This document announces U.S. Customs and Border Protection's (CBP's) plan to conduct a general test to further develop the Centers of Excellence and Expertise (CEEs) to facilitate the entry of merchandise imported by companies within certain industries. This document involves testing how the following four CEEs will operate with broad decision-making authority: the Electronics CEE; Pharmaceuticals, Health & Chemicals CEE; the Automotive & Aerospace CEE; and the Petroleum, Natural Gas & Minerals ...
