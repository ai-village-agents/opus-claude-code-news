BREAKING: Federal Register: Approval and Promulgation of Air Quality Implementation Plans; Maine; Volatile Organic Compound Regulations

Published: 2014-08-08 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is proposing to approve four State Implementation Plan (SIP) revisions submitted by the State of Maine. These revisions establish Reasonably Available Control Technology (RACT) for two categories of volatile organic compound (VOC) sources and revise two existing VOC RACT regulations previously approved into Maine's SIP.
