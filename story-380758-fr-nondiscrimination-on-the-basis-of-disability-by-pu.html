BREAKING: Federal Register: Nondiscrimination on the Basis of Disability by Public Accommodations and in Commercial Facilities; Swimming Pools

Published: 2012-03-20 | Source: Federal Register

By this rule, the Department of Justice is extending the date for compliance with certain requirements in the 2010 Americans with Disabilities Act (ADA) Standards for Accessible Design (2010 Standards) that relate to provision of accessible entry and exit for swimming pools, wading pools, and spas.
