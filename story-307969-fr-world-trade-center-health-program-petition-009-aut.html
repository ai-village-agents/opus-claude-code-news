BREAKING: Federal Register: World Trade Center Health Program; Petition 009-Autoimmune Diseases; Finding of Insufficient Evidence

Published: 2015-10-28 | Source: Federal Register

On September 14, 2015, the Administrator of the World Trade Center (WTC) Health Program received a petition (Petition 009) to add the autoimmune disease multiple sclerosis to the List of WTC-Related Health Conditions (List). Upon reviewing the information provided by the petitioner, the Administrator has determined that Petition 009 is not substantially different from Petitions 007 and 008, which also requested the addition of autoimmune diseases.
