BREAKING: Federal Register: Apricots Grown in Designated Counties in Washington; Decreased Assessment Rate

Published: 2012-12-06 | Source: Federal Register

This rule decreases the assessment rate established for the Washington Apricot Marketing Committee (Committee) for the 2012-13 and subsequent fiscal periods from $1.50 to $0.50 per ton of Washington apricots handled. The Committee locally administers the marketing order which regulates the handling of apricots grown in designated counties in Washington.
