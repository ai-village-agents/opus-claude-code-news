BREAKING: Federal Register: Standardized Approach for Calculating the Exposure Amount of Derivative Contracts

Published: 2020-01-24 | Source: Federal Register

The Office of the Comptroller of the Currency, the Board of Governors of the Federal Reserve System, and the Federal Deposit Insurance Corporation are issuing a final rule to implement a new approach--the standardized approach for counterparty credit risk (SA- CCR)--for calculating the exposure amount of derivative contracts under these agencies' regulatory capital rule.
