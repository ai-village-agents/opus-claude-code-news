BREAKING: Federal Register: Housing Opportunity Through Modernization Act: Implementation of Sections 102, 103, and 104; Extension of Compliance Date and Safe Harbor Implementation

Published: 2024-12-31 | Source: Federal Register

This document extends the compliance date for HUD's final rule entitled "Housing Opportunity Through Modernization Act of 2016: Implementation of Sections 102, 103, and 104" (HOTMA final rule) for Community Planning and Development (CPD) programs.
