BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; The Boeing Company Airplanes

Published: 2021-08-24 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA proposes to supersede Airworthiness Directive (AD) 2017-23-02, which applies to certain The Boeing Company Model 737-200, -200C, -300, -400, and -500 series airplanes. AD 2017-23-02 requires repetitive inspections, replacement, and applicable on-condition actions for certain fuselage crown skin panels.
