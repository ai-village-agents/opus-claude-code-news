BREAKING: Federal Register: Unlicensed Devices and Equipment Approval

Published: 2014-07-14 | Source: Federal Register

This document terminates the above captioned proceeding on unlicensed transmitter operations. Based on the record and considering that the Commission has not received any additional requests in recent years advocating the need for a spectrum etiquette requirement for unlicensed operations in the requested bands, the Commission concludes that adoption of such a requirement does not merit further evaluation at this time.
