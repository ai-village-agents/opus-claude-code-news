BREAKING: Federal Register: Civilian Board of Contract Appeals; Rules of Procedure of the Civilian Board of Contract Appeals

Published: 2019-03-05 | Source: Federal Register

The Civilian Board of Contract Appeals (Board) proposes to issue rules of procedure for arbitration of disputes between applicants for public assistance grants and the Federal Emergency Management Agency (FEMA) regarding disasters after January 1, 2016.
