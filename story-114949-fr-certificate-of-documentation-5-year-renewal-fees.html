BREAKING: Federal Register: Certificate of Documentation-5 Year Renewal Fees

Published: 2021-01-19 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is issuing a final rule extending the validity of a recreational vessel endorsement on a Certificate of Documentation (COD) from 1 to 5 years. Congress passed and the President signed the Frank LoBiondo Coast Guard Authorization Act of 2018, which requires the Coast Guard to issue recreational vessel CODs for 5 years.
