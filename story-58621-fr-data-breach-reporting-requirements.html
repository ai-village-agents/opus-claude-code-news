BREAKING: Federal Register: Data Breach Reporting Requirements

Published: 2023-01-23 | Source: Federal Register

In this document, the Federal Communications Commission (Commission) begins the process to update and strengthen its data breach rule to provide greater protections to the public. We propose to expand the Commission's definition of "breach" to include inadvertent disclosures of customer information and seek comment on adopting a harm-based trigger for breach notifications.
