BREAKING: Federal Register: Availability of Funds and Collection of Checks (Regulation CC)

Published: 2019-07-03 | Source: Federal Register

The Board and the Bureau (Agencies) are amending Regulation CC, which implements the Expedited Funds Availability Act (EFA Act), to implement a statutory requirement in the EFA Act to adjust the dollar amounts under the EFA Act for inflation.
