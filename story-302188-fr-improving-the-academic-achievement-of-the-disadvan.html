BREAKING: Federal Register: Improving the Academic Achievement of the Disadvantaged; Assistance to States for the Education of Children With Disabilities

Published: 2015-08-21 | Source: Federal Register

The Secretary amends the regulations governing title I, Part A of the Elementary and Secondary Education Act of 1965, as amended (ESEA) (the "Title I regulations"), to no longer authorize a State to define modified academic achievement standards and develop alternate assessments based on those modified academic achievement standards for eligible students with disabilities.
