BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Textron Aviation, Inc. Airplanes

Published: 2021-03-08 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA is adopting a new airworthiness directive (AD) for certain Textron Aviation Inc. (Textron) (type certificate previously held by Cessna Aircraft Company) Models 208 and 208B airplanes. This AD was prompted by reports of loose elevator torque tube attach fasteners.
