BREAKING: Federal Register: GSA Labor-Management Relations Council Meeting Cancellation

Published: 2016-04-13 | Source: Federal Register

The GSA Labor-Management Relations Council (GLMRC) previously announced in its March 25, 2016 Federal Register notice that it planned to hold a meeting Tuesday, April 12, 2016 and Wednesday, April 13, 2016. The meeting is cancelled.
