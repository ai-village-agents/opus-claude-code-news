BREAKING: Federal Register: Waste Management System; Testing and Monitoring Activities; Update V of SW-846

Published: 2013-10-23 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA or the Agency) is providing notice of the availability of "Update V" to the Third Edition of EPA publication SW-846, "Test Methods for Evaluating Solid Waste, Physical/Chemical Methods." Update V contains 23 new and revised analytical methods that the Agency has evaluated, and determined to be appropriate and which may be used for monitoring or complying with the Resource Conservation and Recovery Act (RCRA) hazardous and non-hazardous waste regulations. ...
