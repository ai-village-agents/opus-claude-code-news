BREAKING: Federal Register: Medical Devices; Gastroenterology-Urology Devices; Classification of the Hemodialyzer With Expanded Solute Removal Profile

Published: 2024-09-06 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA or we) is classifying the hemodialyzer with expanded solute removal profile into class II (special controls). The special controls that apply to the device type are identified in this order and will be part of the codified language for the hemodialyzer with expanded solute removal profile's classification.
