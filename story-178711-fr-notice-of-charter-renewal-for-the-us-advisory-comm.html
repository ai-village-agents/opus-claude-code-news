BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Charter Renewal for the U.S. Advisory Commission on Public Diplomacy

Published: 2019-05-09 | Source: Federal Register

The official designation of this advisory committee is the United States Advisory Commission on Public Diplomacy. The Commission was originally established under Section 604 of the United States Information and Educational Exchange Act of 1948, as amended, and under Section 8 of Reorganization Plan Number 2 of 1977.
