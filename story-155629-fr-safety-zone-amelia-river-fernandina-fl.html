BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone; Amelia River, Fernandina, FL

Published: 2020-07-08 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is establishing a temporary moving safety zone for navigable waters within a 500-yard radius of the VB-10,000 work barge while transiting the Sector Jacksonville Captain of the Port Zone. Once the VB-10,000 work barge is moored at the Nassau Terminal in Fernandina Beach, FL, the safety zone will be reduced to a 100-yard radius.
