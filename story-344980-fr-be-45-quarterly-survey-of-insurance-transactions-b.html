BREAKING: Federal Register: BE-45: Quarterly Survey of Insurance Transactions by U.S. Insurance Companies With Foreign Persons

Published: 2013-01-24 | Source: Federal Register

By this Notice, the Bureau of Economic Analysis (BEA), Department of Commerce, is informing the public that it is conducting the mandatory survey titled Quarterly Survey of Insurance Transactions by U.S. Insurance Companies with Foreign Persons (BE-45). This mandatory survey is conducted under the authority of the International Investment and Trade in Services Survey Act (22 U.S.C. 3101-3108, as amended).
