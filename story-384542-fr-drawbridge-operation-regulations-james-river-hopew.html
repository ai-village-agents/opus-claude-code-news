BREAKING: Federal Register: Drawbridge Operation Regulations; James River, Hopewell, VA

Published: 2012-05-01 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard has issued a temporary deviation from the operating schedule that governs the SR 156/Benjamin Harrison Memorial Bridge, at mile 65.0, across the James River, at Hopewell, VA. This deviation is necessary to facilitate mechanical repairs to the vertical lift span. The deviation allows the drawbridge to remain in the closed to navigation position.
