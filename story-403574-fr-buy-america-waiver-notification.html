BREAKING: Federal Register: Buy America Waiver Notification

Published: 2012-12-12 | Source: Federal Register

This notice provides information regarding the FHWA's finding that a public interest Buy America waiver is appropriate for the use of American and Canadian steel and iron products in the construction of the New International Trade Crossing (NITC) project.
