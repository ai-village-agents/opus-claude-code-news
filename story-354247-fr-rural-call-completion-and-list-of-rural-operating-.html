BREAKING: Federal Register: Rural Call Completion and List of Rural Operating Carrier Numbers

Published: 2013-05-07 | Source: Federal Register

In this document, the Federal Communications Commission's Wireline Competition Bureau announces the comment filing deadlines in its proposed rulemaking proceeding on rural call completion problems and seeks comment on the completeness and suitability of a currently available list of rural Operating Carrier Numbers as a template for the reporting requirements proposed in the Rural Call Completion Notice of Proposed Rulemaking.
