BREAKING: Federal Register: WTO Dispute Settlement Proceeding Regarding Argentina-Measures Affecting the Importation of Goods

Published: 2012-09-04 | Source: Federal Register

The Office of the United States Trade Representative ("USTR") is providing notice that on August 21, 2012, the United States requested consultations with the Government of Argentina ("Argentina") under the Marrakesh Agreement Establishing the World Trade Organization ("WTO Agreement") concerning certain measures imposed by Argentina on the importation of goods.
