BREAKING: Federal Register: 2020 Tax Information for Use in the Revenue Shortfall Allocation Method

Published: 2021-06-14 | Source: Federal Register

The Board is publishing, and providing the public an opportunity to comment on, the 2020 weighted average state tax rates for each Class I railroad, as calculated by the Association of American Railroads (AAR), for use in the Revenue Shortfall Allocation Method (RSAM).
