BREAKING: Federal Register: Department of Defense Science and Technology Reinvention Laboratory (STRL) Personnel Management Demonstration Project Program

Published: 2014-07-28 | Source: Federal Register

STRLs will implement Senior Scientific Technical Manager (SSTM) positions, which are defined as senior professional scientific and technical positions classified above the GS-15 level of the General Schedule (GS). The primary functions of these positions shall be (1) to engage in research and development in the physical, biological, medical, or engineering sciences, or another field closely related to the mission of such STRL; and (2) to carry out technical supervisory responsibilities.
