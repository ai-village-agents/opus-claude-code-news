BREAKING: Federal Register: Drawbridge Operation Regulations; Reynolds Channel, Nassau, NY

Published: 2013-06-21 | Source: Federal Register

The Commander, First Coast Guard District, has issued a temporary deviation from the regulation governing the operation of the Long Beach Bridge, mile 4.7, across Reynolds Channel at Nassau, New York. Under this temporary deviation, the bridge may remain in the closed position for two and a half hours to facilitate a public event, the Town of Hempstead Annual Fireworks Display.
