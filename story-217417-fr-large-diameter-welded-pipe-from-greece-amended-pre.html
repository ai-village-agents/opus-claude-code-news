BREAKING: Federal Register: Large Diameter Welded Pipe From Greece: Amended Preliminary Determination of Sales at Less Than Fair Value

Published: 2018-09-27 | Source: Federal Register

On August 27, 2018, the Department of Commerce (Commerce) published its preliminary determination in the less-than-fair-value investigation of large diameter welded pipe (welded pipe) from Greece in the Federal Register. Commerce is amending this preliminary determination to correct a significant ministerial error.
