BREAKING: Federal Register: Amendment to Class B Airspace; Detroit, MI

Published: 2014-01-21 | Source: Federal Register

This action modifies the Detroit, MI, Class B airspace area to contain aircraft conducting published instrument procedures at Detroit Metropolitan Wayne County Airport (DTW), Detroit, MI, within Class B airspace.
