BREAKING: Federal Register: Certain Interactive Fitness Products Including Stationary Exercise Bikes, Treadmills, Elliptical Machines, and Rowing Machines and Components Thereof; Notice of Institution of Investigation

Published: 2022-04-07 | Source: Federal Register

Notice is hereby given that a complaint was filed with the U.S. International Trade Commission on March 3, 2022, under section 337 of the Tariff Act of 1930, as amended, on behalf of Peloton Interactive, Inc. of New York, New York. A supplement to the complaint was filed on March 21, 2022.
