BREAKING: Federal Register: Watermelon Research and Promotion Plan; Redistricting and Importer Representation

Published: 2017-09-27 | Source: Federal Register

This proposal invites comments on realigning the production districts under the Watermelon Research and Promotion Plan (Plan) for producer and handler membership on the National Watermelon Promotion Board (Board), and adding four importer seats to the Board. The Board administers the Plan with oversight by the U.S. Department of Agriculture (USDA).
