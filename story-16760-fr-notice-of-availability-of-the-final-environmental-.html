BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Availability of the Final Environmental Impact Statement for the Enhanced Integrated Air and Missile Defense System on Guam

Published: 2025-07-25 | Source: Federal Register

The MDA, as the lead agency, has prepared a Final Environmental Impact Statement (EIS) for the Enhanced Integrated Air and Missile Defense (EIAMD) System on Guam. The United States (U.S.) Army (USA), U.S. Navy (USN), U.S. Air Force (USAF), and the Federal Aviation Administration (FAA) are cooperating agencies in the development of the Final EIS.
