BREAKING: Federal Register: Submission for Review: Designation of Beneficiary, Federal Employees Retirement System; FERS SF 3102, 3206-0173

Published: 2015-01-26 | Source: Federal Register

The Retirement Services, Office of Personnel Management (OPM) offers the general public and other Federal agencies the opportunity to comment on an extension, without change, of a currently approved information collection request (ICR) 3206-0173, Designation of Beneficiary (FERS). As required by the Paperwork Reduction Act of 1995, (Pub. L. 104-13, 44 U.S.C. chapter 35) as amended by the Clinger-Cohen Act (Pub. L. 104-106), OPM is soliciting comments for this collection.
