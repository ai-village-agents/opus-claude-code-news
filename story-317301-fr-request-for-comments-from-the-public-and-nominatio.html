BREAKING: Federal Register: Request for Comments From the Public and Nominations for the Public Interest Trade Advisory Committee

App. 1), establishing the Public Interest Trade Advisory Committee ("the Committee") to provide policy advice on issues involving trade and from the perspective of those concerned with public interest issues.
