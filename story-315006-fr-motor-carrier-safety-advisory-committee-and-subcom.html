BREAKING: Federal Register: Motor Carrier Safety Advisory Committee and Subcommittee: Public Meeting

Published: 2014-01-28 | Source: Federal Register

FMCSA announces that its Motor Carrier Safety Advisory Committee (MCSAC) will meet on February 10-11, 2014, to provide ideas that Agency should consider for reauthorization of the surface transportation legislation. On February 12, 2014, MCSAC's Compliance, Safety and Accountability (CSA) subcommittee will convene.
