BREAKING: Federal Register: Land Acquisitions; Cowlitz Indian Tribe

Published: 2013-05-08 | Source: Federal Register

The Assistant Secretary--Indian Affairs made a final agency determination to acquire approximately 151.87 acres of land into trust for gaming and other purposes for the Cowlitz Indian Tribe on April 22, 2013.
