BREAKING: Federal Register: Update to Minimum Present Value Requirements for Defined Benefit Plan Distributions

Published: 2024-01-19 | Source: Federal Register

This document sets forth final regulations providing guidance relating to the minimum present value requirements applicable to certain defined benefit pension plans. These regulations provide guidance on changes made by the Pension Protection Act of 2006 to the prescribed interest rate and mortality table and other guidance, including rules regarding the treatment of preretirement mortality discounts and Social Security level income options.
