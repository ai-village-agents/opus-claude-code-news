BREAKING: Federal Register: Additions to List of Section 241.4 Categorical Non-Waste Fuels: Other Treated Railroad Ties

Published: 2016-11-01 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA or the Agency) is proposing to issue amendments to the Non-Hazardous Secondary Materials rule, initially promulgated on March 21, 2011, and amended on February 7, 2013 and February 8, 2016, under the Resource Conservation and Recovery Act.
