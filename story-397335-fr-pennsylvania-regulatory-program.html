BREAKING: Federal Register: Pennsylvania Regulatory Program

Published: 2012-09-25 | Source: Federal Register

We are reopening and extending the public comment period and will be holding two public hearings on the proposed amendment to the Commonwealth of Pennsylvania's approved regulatory program (the "Pennsylvania program") published on July 11, 2012. The comment period is being reopened and extended in order to afford the public more time to comment and to allow ample time to conduct two public hearings. This extension was requested by the Citizens Coal Council and the Environmental Integrity Project...
