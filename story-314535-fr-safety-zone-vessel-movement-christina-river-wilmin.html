BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone, Vessel Movement, Christina River; Wilmington, DE

Published: 2014-01-22 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is establishing a temporary safety zone on the Christina River in Wilmington, DE, from January 18, 2014 to January 31, 2014, to be enforced for a period of 48 hours within this time frame. The safety zone will restrict vessel traffic on the Christina River in the immediate area of the M/V OCEAN FORCE, which will be moored inside a boundary described as originating from 39[deg]43'14" N, 075[deg]31'41" W; northeasterly to 39[deg]43'17" N, 75[deg]31'40" W; then east to 39[deg]43'15"...
