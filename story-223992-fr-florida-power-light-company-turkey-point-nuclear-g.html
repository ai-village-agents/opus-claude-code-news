BREAKING: Federal Register: Florida Power & Light Company; Turkey Point Nuclear Generating Unit Nos. 3 and 4

Published: 2018-12-26 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Nuclear Regulatory Commission (NRC) is considering issuance of amendments to Renewed Facility Operating License Nos. DPR- 31 and DPR-41, issued to Florida Power & Light Company, for operation of Turkey Point Nuclear Generating Unit Nos. 3 and 4, respectively. The proposed amendments would modify Paragraph 3.D, "Transition License Conditions," of the Renewed Facility Operating Licenses to delete Implementation Item 22.
