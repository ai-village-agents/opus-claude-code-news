BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Intent To Repatriate Cultural Items: Berkshire Museum, Pittsfield, MA

Published: 2022-01-10 | Source: Federal Register

The Berkshire Museum, in consultation with the appropriate Indian Tribes or Native Hawaiian organizations, has determined that the cultural items listed in this notice meet the definition of objects of cultural patrimony. Lineal descendants or representatives of any Indian Tribe or Native Hawaiian organization not identified in this notice that wish to claim these cultural items should submit a written request to the Berkshire Museum.
