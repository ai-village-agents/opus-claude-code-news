BREAKING: Federal Register: Advisory Committee on Heritable Disorders in Newborns and Children Request for Nominations

Published: 2015-06-26 | Source: Federal Register

The Health Resources and Services Administration (HRSA) is seeking nominations of qualified candidates to be considered for appointment as members of the Advisory Committee on Heritable Disorders in Newborns and Children (Committee). The Committee provides advice, recommendations, and technical information about aspects of heritable disorders and newborn and childhood screening to the Secretary of Health and Human Services.
