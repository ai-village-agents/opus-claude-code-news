BREAKING: Federal Register: Endangered and Threatened Wildlife and Plants; Endangered Species Status for South Llano Springs Moss

Published: 2023-04-27 | Source: Federal Register

We, the U.S. Fish and Wildlife Service (Service), determine endangered species status under the Endangered Species Act of 1973 (Act), as amended, for the South Llano springs moss (Donrichardsia macroneuron), an aquatic moss species from Edwards County, Texas. We are excluding the single unit of proposed critical habitat, and, therefore, no critical habitat is being designated for the South Llano springs moss.
