BREAKING: Federal Register: Special Local Regulation; Marine Events Within the Eleventh Coast Guard District-San Diego Bayfair

Published: 2022-09-13 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard will enforce the special local regulation on the waters of Mission Bay, CA, during the San Diego Bayfair on September 16, 2022, through September 18, 2022. This special local regulation is necessary to provide for the safety of the participants, crew, sponsor vessels of the event, and general users of the waterway.
