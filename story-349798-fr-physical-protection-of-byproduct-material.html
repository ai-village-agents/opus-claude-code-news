BREAKING: Federal Register: Physical Protection of Byproduct Material

Published: 2013-03-19 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Nuclear Regulatory Commission (NRC) is amending its regulations to establish security requirements for the use and transport of category 1 and category 2 quantities of radioactive material. The NRC considers these quantities to be risk significant and, therefore, to warrant additional protection.
