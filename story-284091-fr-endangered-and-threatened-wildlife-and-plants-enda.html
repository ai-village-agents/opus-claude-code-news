BREAKING: Federal Register: Endangered and Threatened Wildlife and Plants; Endangered Status for the Mexican Wolf

Published: 2015-01-16 | Source: Federal Register

We, the U.S. Fish and Wildlife Service (Service), determine endangered status under the Endangered Species Act of 1973, as amended, for the Mexican wolf (Canis lupus baileyi). The effect of this regulation will be to revise the List of Endangered and Threatened Wildlife by making a separate entry for the Mexican wolf.
