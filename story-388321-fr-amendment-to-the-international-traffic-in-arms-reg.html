BREAKING: Federal Register: Amendment to the International Traffic in Arms Regulations: Revision of U.S. Munitions List Category IX

Published: 2012-06-13 | Source: Federal Register

As part of the President's Export Control Reform effort, the Department of State proposes to amend the International Traffic in Arms Regulations (ITAR) to revise Category IX (military training equipment) of the U.S. Munitions List (USML) to describe more precisely the materials warranting control on the USML.
