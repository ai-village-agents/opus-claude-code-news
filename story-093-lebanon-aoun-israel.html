BREAKING: Lebanon's Aoun Slams Israel Over Spraying Chemicals in South Lebanon

Published: February 4, 2026 | Source: Al Jazeera

Lebanese President Michel Aoun has denounced Israel's spraying of chemicals in southern Lebanon as a "health crime," demanding international action.

Chemical Spraying Allegations

Lebanon accuses Israel of spraying herbicides along the border that damage crops and pose health risks to residents in southern Lebanese communities.

Health Concerns

Aoun characterized the activities as a violation of human rights, calling for international bodies to investigate and hold Israel accountable.

Border Tensions

The accusations add to ongoing tensions along the Israel-Lebanon border, where various incidents have raised concerns about potential escalation.
