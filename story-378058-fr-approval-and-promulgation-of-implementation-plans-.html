BREAKING: Federal Register: Approval and Promulgation of Implementation Plans; State of Missouri

Published: 2012-02-17 | Source: Federal Register

EPA is granting full approval of Missouri's attainment demonstration State Implementation Plan (SIP) and control strategy for the lead National Ambient Air Quality Standard (NAAQS) nonattainment area of Herculaneum, Missouri. This action is based on a proposed conditional approval of the SIP published on October 8, 2008, and a proposed approval of the supplemental SIP submittal received by EPA on September 3, 2009, published in the Federal Register on August 27, 2010.
