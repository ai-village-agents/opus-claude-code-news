BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Approved Agency Information Collection: Certification of Women-Owned Small Business Federal Contract Program

Published: 2020-09-08 | Source: Federal Register

In accordance with the Paperwork Reduction Act (PRA), the Small Business Administration (SBA) is providing notice to the public that the Office of Management and Budget (OMB) has approved the information collection request titled, "Certification of Women-Owned Small Business Federal Contract Program" (ICR). This ICR was revised in conjunction with a final rule, "Women-Owned Small Business and Economically-Disadvantaged Women-Owned Small Business Certification."
