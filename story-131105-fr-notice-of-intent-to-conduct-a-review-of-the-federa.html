BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Intent To Conduct a Review of the Federal Coal Leasing Program and To Seek Public Comment

Published: 2021-08-20 | Source: Federal Register

The Bureau of Land Management (BLM), Headquarters Office seeks public comment on the Federal coal program in advance of the BLM's intended review of that program. The Department of the Interior (DOI) also intends to conduct government-to-government consultation with affected Indian tribes about the Federal coal leasing program and to consider the potential environmental, social, and cultural impacts of the coal program on indigenous communities and their lands during this review.
