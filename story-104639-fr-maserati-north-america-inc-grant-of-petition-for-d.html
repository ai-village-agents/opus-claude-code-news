BREAKING: Federal Register: Maserati North America, Inc., Grant of Petition for Decision of Inconsequential Noncompliance

Published: 2022-09-07 | Source: Federal Register

Maserati North America, Inc., (MNA), has determined that certain model year (MY) 2014-2021 Maserati Ghibli, Quattroporte, and Levante motor vehicles do not fully comply with Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard (FMVSS) No. 208, Occupant Crash Protection. MNA filed a noncompliance report dated August 5, 2021.
