BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; The Boeing Company Airplanes

Published: 2012-09-19 | Source: Federal Register

We are superseding an existing airworthiness directive (AD) for all The Boeing Company Model 737-100, -200, -200C, -300, -400, and -500 series airplanes. That AD currently requires repetitive inspections to detect cracking in the web of the aft pressure bulkhead at body station 1016 at the aft fastener row attachment to the "Y" chord, and corrective actions if necessary.
