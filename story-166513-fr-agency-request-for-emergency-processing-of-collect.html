BREAKING: Federal Register: Agency Request for Emergency Processing of Collection of Information by the Office of Management and Budget

Published: 2020-11-23 | Source: Federal Register

Consistent with the Paperwork Reduction Act of 1995 and its implementing regulations, this document provides notice that FRA is submitting the following Information Collection Request to the Office of Management and Budget (OMB) to collect information on ridership data and certified schedule metrics as required by the recently issued final rule on Metrics and Minimum Standards for Intercity Passenger Rail Service.
