BREAKING: Federal Register: Proposed Amendment of Class E Airspace and Establishment of Class E Airspace Extension; Battle Mountain, NV

Published: 2019-11-29 | Source: Federal Register

This action proposes to modify the Class E surface area, Class E airspace extending upward from 700 feet above the surface and create Class E airspace as an extension to the Class E surface area at Battle Mountain Airport, Battle Mountain, NV.
