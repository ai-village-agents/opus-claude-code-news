BREAKING: Federal Register: Proposed Modification of Class D and Establishment of Class E Airspace; Hayward, CA

Published: 2020-09-15 | Source: Federal Register

This action proposes to modify the Class D airspace, establish Class E airspace extending upward from the surface, and establish Class E airspace as an extension to the Class D and Class E surface areas at Hayward Executive Airport, Hayward, CA. After a biennial review of the airspace, the FAA found it necessary to amend the existing airspace for the safety and management of Instrument Flight Rules (IFR) operations at this airport.
