BREAKING: Federal Register: Correction to Establishment and Solicitation of Nominations for Tribal Advisory Council

Published: 2020-04-30 | Source: Federal Register

HRSA is soliciting comments and recommendations regarding HRSA's intent to establish the HRSA Tribal Advisory Council (TAC) and is seeking nominations of qualified tribal officials as candidates for consideration for appointment as voluntary delegate members of the HRSA TAC.
