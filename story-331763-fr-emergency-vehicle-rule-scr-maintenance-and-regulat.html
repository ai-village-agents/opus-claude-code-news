BREAKING: Federal Register: Emergency Vehicle Rule-SCR Maintenance and Regulatory Flexibility for Nonroad Equipment

Published: 2014-08-08 | Source: Federal Register

This rule consists of three parts. First, the Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is adopting minimum maintenance intervals for replenishment of consumable chemical reductant (commonly known as diesel exhaust fluid, or DEF) in connection with the use of selective catalytic reduction (SCR) technologies.
