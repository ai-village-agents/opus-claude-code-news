BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Embraer S.A. Airplanes

Published: 2015-11-30 | Source: Federal Register

We propose to adopt a new airworthiness directive (AD) for all Embraer S.A. Model ERJ 170 airplanes; and all Embraer S.A. Model ERJ 190-100 STD, -100 LR, -100 IGW, -200 STD, -200 LR, and -200 IGW airplanes. This proposed AD was prompted by reports of cracks in certain engine low-stage bleed check valves.
