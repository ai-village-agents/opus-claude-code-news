BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Inventory Completion: Indiana University, Bloomington, IN; Correction

Published: 2021-01-28 | Source: Federal Register

Indiana University has corrected an inventory of human remains and associated funerary objects, published in a Notice of Inventory Completion in the Federal Register on December 2, 2016. This notice corrects the minimum number of individuals and the number of associated funerary objects.
