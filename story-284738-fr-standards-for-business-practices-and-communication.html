BREAKING: Federal Register: Standards for Business Practices and Communication Protocols for Public Utilities

Published: 2015-01-26 | Source: Federal Register

Order No. 676-H, published on September 14, 2014, requires public utilities to comply with the incorporated Version 003 business practice standards by February 2, 2015 (former), with the exception of those standards related to the Network Integration Transmission Service (NITS) Open Access Same-Time Information System (OASIS) template, for which compliance is required by April 25, 2016 (latter).
