BREAKING: Federal Register: Financial Crimes Enforcement Network; Amendment to the Bank Secrecy Act Regulations-Reports of Foreign Financial Accounts

Published: 2016-03-10 | Source: Federal Register

FinCEN, a bureau of the Department of the Treasury ("Treasury"), is proposing to revise the regulations implementing the Bank Secrecy Act ("BSA") regarding Reports of Foreign Bank and Financial Accounts ("FBAR"). The proposed rule would expand and clarify the exemptions for certain U.S. persons with signature or other authority over foreign financial accounts.
