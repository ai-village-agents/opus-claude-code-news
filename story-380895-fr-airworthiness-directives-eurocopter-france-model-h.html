BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Eurocopter France Model Helicopters

Published: 2012-03-20 | Source: Federal Register

We are adopting a new airworthiness directive (AD) for the Eurocopter France (ECF) Model SA-365C, SA-365C1, SA-365C2, SA-365N, SA- 365N1, AS-365N2, AS 365 N3, and SA-366G1 helicopters. This AD was prompted by some reports of deterioration and two reports of failure of Starflex star arm ends.
