BREAKING: Federal Register: Agency Information Collection Activities: Comment Request

Published: 2019-05-17 | Source: Federal Register

The Export-Import Banks of the United States (EXIM), as part of its continuing effort to reduce paperwork and respondent burden, invites the general public and other Federal Agencies to comment on the proposed information collection, as required by the Paperwork Reduction Act of 1995. This collection of information is necessary to determine eligibility of the applicant for EXIM assistance.
