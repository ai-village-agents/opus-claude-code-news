BREAKING: Federal Register: Fiscal Year (FY) 2014 Funding Opportunity

Published: 2014-06-27 | Source: Federal Register

This notice is to inform the public that the Substance Abuse and Mental Health Services Administration (SAMHSA) intends to award up to $1,448,432 (total costs) for one year to Link2Health Solutions, Inc., as a program supplement to the National Suicide Prevention Lifeline.
