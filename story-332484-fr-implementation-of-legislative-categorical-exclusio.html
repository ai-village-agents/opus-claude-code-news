BREAKING: Federal Register: Implementation of Legislative Categorical Exclusion for Environmental Review of Performance Based Navigation Procedures

Published: 2014-08-19 | Source: Federal Register

The Federal Aviation Administration (FAA) is considering how to implement Section 213(c)(2) of the FAA Modernization and Reform Act of 2012 which directs the FAA to issue and file a categorical exclusion for any navigation performance or other performance based navigation (PBN) procedure that would result in measureable reductions in fuel consumption, carbon dioxide emissions, and noise on a per flight basis as compared to aircraft operations that follow existing instrument flight rule procedure...
