BREAKING: Federal Register: Exemptions To Facilitate Intrastate and Regional Securities Offerings

Published: 2015-11-10 | Source: Federal Register

We are proposing amendments to Rule 147 under the Securities Act of 1933, which currently provides a safe harbor for compliance with the Section 3(a)(11) exemption from registration for intrastate securities offerings. Our proposal would modernize the rule and establish a new exemption to facilitate capital formation, including through offerings relying upon recently adopted intrastate crowdfunding provisions under state securities laws.
