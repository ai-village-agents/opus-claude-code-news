BREAKING: Federal Register: Various Fragrance Components in Pesticide Formulations; Tolerance Exemption

Published: 2023-05-19 | Source: Federal Register

This regulation establishes exemptions from the requirement of a tolerance for residues of various fragrance components listed in Unit II of this document when they are used as inert ingredients in antimicrobial pesticide formulations applied to food-contact surfaces in public eating places, dairy-processing equipment, and food- processing equipment and utensils when the end-use concentration does not exceed 100 parts per million (ppm).
