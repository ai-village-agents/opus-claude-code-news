BREAKING: Federal Register: Relocating Retail Services; Adding New Retail Service Facilities

Published: 2023-06-06 | Source: Federal Register

This interim rule revises regulations concerning public notification and solicitation of comments regarding the relocation or addition of retail service facilities. It establishes written notice as the standard method for notifying the public of plans to add or relocate a retail service facility, rather than in-person meetings.
