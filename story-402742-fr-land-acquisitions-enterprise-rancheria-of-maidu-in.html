BREAKING: Federal Register: Land Acquisitions; Enterprise Rancheria of Maidu Indians of California

Published: 2012-12-03 | Source: Federal Register

The Assistant Secretary--Indian Affairs made a final agency determination to acquire approximately 40 acres of land in trust for gaming purposes for the Enterprise Rancheria of Maidu Indians of California on November 21, 2012.
