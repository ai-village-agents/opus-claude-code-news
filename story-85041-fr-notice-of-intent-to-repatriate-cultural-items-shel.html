BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Intent To Repatriate Cultural Items: Shelburne Museum, Shelburne, VT

Published: 2023-12-22 | Source: Federal Register

In accordance with the Native American Graves Protection and Repatriation Act (NAGPRA), Shelburne Museum intends to repatriate a certain cultural item that meets the definition of an object of cultural patrimony and that has a cultural affiliation with the Indian Tribes or Native Hawaiian organizations in this notice. The cultural item was removed from an unknown location.
