BREAKING: Federal Register: MHK Distributed and Alternate Applications Forum

Published: 2017-11-15 | Source: Federal Register

This notice serves to announce that the Water Power Technologies Office (WPTO) within the Department of Energy (DOE) intends to hold a marine and hydrokinetic (MHK) Distributed and Alternate Applications Forum ("Forum") in Washington, DC from December 5-7, 2017. The purpose of the Forum is to gather the latest information on potential high-priority alternative markets for MHK technologies.
