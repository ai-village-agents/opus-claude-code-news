BREAKING: Federal Register: Receipt of Application for the Amendment of the Incidental Take Permit for the Multiple Species Conservation Program County of San Diego Subarea Plan, County of San Diego, California

Published: 2012-06-18 | Source: Federal Register

We, the U.S. Fish and Wildlife Service (Service) intend to amend the incidental take permit (PRT-840414) issued for the Multiple Species Conservation Program County of San Diego Subarea Plan (MSCP Subarea Plan). The County of San Diego (Applicant) has requested an amendment to the incidental take permit.
