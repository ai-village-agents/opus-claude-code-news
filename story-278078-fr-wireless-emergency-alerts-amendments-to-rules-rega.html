BREAKING: Federal Register: Wireless Emergency Alerts; Amendments to Rules Regarding the Emergency Alert System

Published: 2016-11-01 | Source: Federal Register

In this document, the Federal Communications Commission (Commission) adopts revisions to Wireless Emergency Alert (WEA) rules to take advantage of the significant technological changes and improvements experienced by the mobile wireless industry since the passage of the Warning, Alert and Response Network (WARN) Act, and deployment of Wireless Emergency Alerts (WEA) to improve utility of WEA as a life-saving tool.
