BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Issuance of Final Determination Concerning Certain Cordless Headsets

Published: 2014-02-12 | Source: Federal Register

This document provides notice that U.S. Customs and Border Protection ("CBP") has issued a final determination concerning the country of origin of certain cordless headsets with included dongles. Based upon the facts presented, CBP has concluded that the non-TAA country where the headsets and dongles are assembled is the country where the last substantial transformation occurs.
