BREAKING: Federal Register: Standard Review Plan for Spent Fuel Dry Storage Systems and Facilities

Published: 2017-11-15 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Nuclear Regulatory Commission (NRC) is issuing for public comment a draft NUREG, NUREG-2215, "Standard Review Plan for Spent Fuel Dry Storage Systems and Facilities."
