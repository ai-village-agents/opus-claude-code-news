BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Airbus SAS Airplanes

Published: 2025-12-09 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA is superseding Airworthiness Directive (AD) 2017-23- 04, which applied to all Airbus SAS Model A300 B4-600R series airplanes; all Model A300-B4 603, B4-620, and B4-622 airplanes; all Model A300 C4-605R Variant F airplanes; and certain Model A300 F4-605R airplanes. AD 2017-23-04 required an inspection of the upper wing skin and top stringer joints, and modification of the stringer joint couplings if necessary.
