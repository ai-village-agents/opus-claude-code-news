BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone; Oregon Inlet, Dare County, NC

Published: 2018-05-10 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is re-establishing and extending a temporary final rule that creates a temporary safety zone on the waters of Oregon in Dare County, NC. The duration of this safety zone is extended through June 15, 2018.
