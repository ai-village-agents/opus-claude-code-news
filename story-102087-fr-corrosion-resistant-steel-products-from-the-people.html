BREAKING: Federal Register: Corrosion-Resistant Steel Products From the People's Republic of China: Preliminary Results of Antidumping Duty Administrative Review; 2020-2021

(Metalco), the sole company subject to this administrative review of the antidumping duty (AD) order on corrosion-resistant steel products (CORE) from the People's Republic of China (China), is part of the China-wide entity because it did not file a separate rate application (SRA).
