BREAKING: Federal Register: Proposed Cancelation

Published: 2012-04-02 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Transportation is directing all interested persons to show cause why it should not issue an order finding that VIH Cougar Helicopters, Inc. is not a U.S. citizen as defined in 49 U.S.C. 40102(a)(15) and canceling its Part 298 exemption authority.
