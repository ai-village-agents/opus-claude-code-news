BREAKING: Federal Register: Saguache Ranger District, Rio Grande National Forest; Colorado; La Garita Hills Restoration Project

Published: 2014-10-17 | Source: Federal Register

The Saguache Ranger District, Rio Grande National Forest (USFS), and San Luis Valley Field Office, Bureau of Land Management (BLM), propose to conduct restoration activities on up to 90,000 acres within the 187,778 acre La Gartia Hills (LGH) analysis area; approximately 145,709 acres are managed by the USFS and 33,294 acres are managed by the BLM.
