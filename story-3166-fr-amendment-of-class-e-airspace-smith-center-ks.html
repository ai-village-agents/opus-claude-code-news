BREAKING: Federal Register: Amendment of Class E Airspace; Smith Center, KS

Published: 2025-01-07 | Source: Federal Register

This action amends the Class E airspace at Smith Center, KS. This action is the result of an airspace review conducted due to the decommissioning of the Mankato very high frequency omnidirectional range (VOR) as part of the VOR Minimum Operational Network (MON) Program. This action also updates the geographic coordinates of the airport to coincide with the FAA's aeronautical database.
