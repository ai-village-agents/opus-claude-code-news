BREAKING: Federal Register: Transferred OTS Regulations Regarding Possession by Conservators and Receivers for Federal and State Savings Associations

Published: 2015-01-30 | Source: Federal Register

The Federal Deposit Insurance Corporation (FDIC) is rescinding and removing the former OTS regulation entitled "Possession by Conservators and Receivers for Federal and State Savings Associations" from the Code of Federal Regulations because it is not necessary.
