BREAKING: Federal Register: Radio Experimentation and Market Trials-Streamlining Rules

Published: 2013-04-29 | Source: Federal Register

This document revises and streamlines the Commission rules to modernize the Experimental Radio Service (ERS). The rules adopted in the Report and Order updates the ERS to a more flexible framework to keep pace with the speed of modern technological change while continuing to provide an environment where creativity can thrive.
