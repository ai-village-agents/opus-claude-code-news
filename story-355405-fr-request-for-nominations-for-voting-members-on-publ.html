BREAKING: Federal Register: Request for Nominations for Voting Members on Public Advisory Panels or Committees

Published: 2013-05-21 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA) is requesting nominations for voting members on the Food Advisory Committee with expertise in microbiology, nutrition, food science, food technology, pediatric development, or nanotechnology in food. Nominations will be accepted for current vacancies and those that will or may occur through June 30, 2013.
