BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Issuance of Final Determination Concerning Digital Projectors

Published: 2012-06-12 | Source: Federal Register

This document provides notice that U.S. Customs and Border Protection ("CBP") has issued a final determination concerning the country of origin of certain digital projectors. Based upon the facts presented, CBP has concluded in the final determination that the assembly and programming operations performed in the Taiwan substantially transform the non-TAA country components of the digital projectors.
