BREAKING: Federal Register: Black Lung Benefits Act: Disclosure of Medical Information and Payment of Benefits

Published: 2016-04-26 | Source: Federal Register

This final rule revises the regulations implementing the Black Lung Benefits Act to address certain procedural issues that have arisen in claim adjudications and other technical issues.
