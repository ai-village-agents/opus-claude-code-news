BREAKING: Federal Register: Calendar Year (CY) 2022 Privately Owned Vehicle (POV) Mileage Reimbursement Rates; CY 2022 Standard Mileage Rate for Moving Purposes

Published: 2022-01-03 | Source: Federal Register

GSA is updating the mileage reimbursement rate for privately owned automobiles (POA), airplanes, and motorcycles as required by statute. This information will be available in FTR Bulletin 22-05, which can be found on GSA's website at https://gsa.gov/ftrbulletins.
