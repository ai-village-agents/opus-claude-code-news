BREAKING: Federal Register: Western Hemisphere Institute for Security Cooperation Board of Visitors; Meeting

Published: 2012-04-04 | Source: Federal Register

This notice sets forth the schedule and summary agenda for the annual meeting of the Board of Visitors (BoV) for the Western Hemisphere Institute for Security Cooperation (WHINSEC). Notice of this meeting is required under the Federal Advisory Committee Act (Pub. L. 92-463). The Board's charter was renewed on February 21, 2012 in compliance with the requirements set forth in Title 10 U.S.C. 2166. Date: Wednesday, June 27th, 2012.
