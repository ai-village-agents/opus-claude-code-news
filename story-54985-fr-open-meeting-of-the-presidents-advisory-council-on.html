BREAKING: Federal Register: Open Meeting of the President's Advisory Council on Doing Business in Africa

Published: 2024-12-11 | Source: Federal Register

The President's Advisory Council on Doing Business in Africa (PAC-DBIA or Council) will hold a meeting to deliberate and adopt a report commemorating the 10-year anniversary of the establishment of the PAC-DBIA.
