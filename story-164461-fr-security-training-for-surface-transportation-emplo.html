BREAKING: Federal Register: Security Training for Surface Transportation Employees; Compliance Dates; Amendment

Published: 2020-10-26 | Source: Federal Register

This final rule amends the "Security Training for Surface Transportation Employees" (Security Training) final rule (published March 23, 2020, and amended May 1, 2020) to extend the compliance dates by which certain requirements must be completed.
