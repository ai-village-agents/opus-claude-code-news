BREAKING: Federal Register: Final Revision and Publication of the 2012 Form M-1, Notice

Published: 2013-03-01 | Source: Federal Register

This document announces revisions to the Form M-1, Report for Multiple Employer Welfare Arrangements (MEWAs) and Certain Entities Claiming Exception (ECEs), and its availability. The revisions can be viewed on the Employee Benefits Security Administration's (EBSA) Web site at www.dol.gov/ebsa. The revised form is substantively different from previous versions of the Form M-1.
