BREAKING: Federal Register: Privacy Act of 1974; System of Records

Published: 2015-05-14 | Source: Federal Register

The Office of the Secretary of Defense proposes to add a new system of records, DMDC 19 DoD, entitled "Secure Web Fingerprint Transmission (SWFT)" to its inventory of record systems subject to the Privacy Act of 1974, as amended.
