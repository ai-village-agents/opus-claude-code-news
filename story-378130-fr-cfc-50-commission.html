BREAKING: Federal Register: CFC-50 Commission

Published: 2012-02-21 | Source: Federal Register

The CFC-50 Advisory Commission will hold its third and final meeting on March 2, 2012, at the time and location shown below. The Commission shall advise the Director of the U.S. Office of Personnel Management (OPM) on strengthening the integrity, the operation and effectiveness of the Combined Federal Campaign (CFC) to ensure its continued growth and success.
