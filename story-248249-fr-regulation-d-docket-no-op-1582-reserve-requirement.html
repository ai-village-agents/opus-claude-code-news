BREAKING: Federal Register: Regulation D; Docket No. OP-1582; Reserve Requirements of Depository Institutions

Published: 2017-11-08 | Source: Federal Register

The Board is amending Regulation D, Reserve Requirements of Depository Institutions, to reflect the annual indexing of the reserve requirement exemption amount and the low reserve tranche for 2018. The Regulation D amendments set the amount of total reservable liabilities of each depository institution that is subject to a zero percent reserve requirement in 2018 at $16.0 million (up from $15.5 million in 2017).
