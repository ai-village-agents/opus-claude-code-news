BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone for Fireworks Display; Upper Potomac River, Washington, DC

Published: 2020-06-08 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is proposing to establish a temporary safety zone for certain waters of the Upper Potomac River. This action is necessary to provide for the safety of life on these navigable waters near the National Mall and Memorial Parks at Washington, DC, on July 4, 2020, (with alternate date of July 5, 2020) during a fireworks display to commemorate the July 4th holiday.
