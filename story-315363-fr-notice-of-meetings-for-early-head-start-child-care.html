BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Meetings for Early Head Start-Child Care Partnerships

Published: 2014-01-31 | Source: Federal Register

This public meeting is to obtain input on the creation of Early Head Start (EHS)-Child Care Partnerships that were authorized by the 2014 Omnibus Act, which was released on January 17, 2014. The purpose of this meeting is to provide an open door forum for public input in order to help the Department of Health and Human Services as we develop future planning activities.
