BREAKING: Federal Register: Methyl Bromide; Pesticide Tolerances for Emergency Exemptions

Published: 2018-03-01 | Source: Federal Register

This regulation establishes time-limited tolerances for residues of the fumigant methyl bromide, including its metabolites and degradates in or on post-harvest imported/domestic agricultural commodities. This action is in response to EPA's granting quarantine exemptions under the Federal Insecticide, Fungicide, and Rodenticide Act (FIFRA) authorizing use of the pesticide on specified agricultural commodities.
