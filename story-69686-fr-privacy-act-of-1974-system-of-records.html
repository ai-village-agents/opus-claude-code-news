BREAKING: Federal Register: Privacy Act of 1974; System of Records

Published: 2023-06-12 | Source: Federal Register

Pursuant to the Privacy Act of 1974, notice is hereby given that the VA is modifying the system of records titled "Veterans Crisis Line Database--VA" (158VA10NC5). This system of records is used to document contact interactions with the Veterans Crisis Line (VCL), and to assist with follow-up care based on those interactions.
