BREAKING: Federal Register: Regulated Navigation Area; Port of Miami, Miami, FL

Published: 2024-08-28 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is proposing to establish a regulated navigation area for certain waters surrounding the Port of Miami. This action is necessary to provide for the safety of life and promote national security by enhancing the protection of increased high-risk vessel traffic and reducing the navigational hazards of the mariners who operate throughout the port.
