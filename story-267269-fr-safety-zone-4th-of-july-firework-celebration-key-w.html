BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zone; 4th of July Firework Celebration; Key West, FL

Published: 2016-06-28 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is establishing a temporary safety zone for the 4th of July Firework Celebration on Saturday, July 4, 2016. The safety zone will encompass a 300 yard radius around the White Street Pier in Key West Harbor. The safety zone is necessary to ensure the safety of spectators and the general public during the event.
