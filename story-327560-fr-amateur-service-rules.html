BREAKING: Federal Register: Amateur Service Rules

Published: 2014-06-20 | Source: Federal Register

This document amends the rules for the Amateur Radio Service by modifying the qualifying examination system to grant partial examination credit for certain expired amateur operator licenses, permits examinations to be administered remotely, and allows amateur stations to transmit certain additional emission types.
