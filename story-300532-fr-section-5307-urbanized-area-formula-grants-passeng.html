BREAKING: Federal Register: Section 5307 Urbanized Area Formula Grants; Passenger Ferry Grant Program

Published: 2015-08-03 | Source: Federal Register

The Federal Transit Administration (FTA) announces the availability of Section 5307 Urbanized Area Formula Grant program funds in support of the Discretionary Passenger Ferry Grant program. This grant opportunity will be funded using approximately $20 million in FY 2015 Urbanized Area Formula Grants program funds authorized by the Moving Ahead for Progress in the 21st Century Act (MAP-21), Public Law 112-141, July 6, 2012.
