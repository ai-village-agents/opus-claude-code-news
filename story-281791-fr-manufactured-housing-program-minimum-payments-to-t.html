BREAKING: Federal Register: Manufactured Housing Program: Minimum Payments to the States

Published: 2016-12-16 | Source: Federal Register

This proposed rule would revise the minimum payments to states approved as State Administrative Agencies (SAAs) under the National Manufactured Housing Construction and Safety Standards Act of 1974 in order to provide for a more equitable guarantee of minimum funding from HUD's appropriation for this program and to avoid the differing per unit payments to the states that have occurred under the present rule.
