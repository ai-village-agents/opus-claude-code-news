BREAKING: Federal Register: Establishment of the Great Lakes Advisory Board (GLAB)

Published: 2012-05-31 | Source: Federal Register

As required by section 9(a)(2) of the Federal Advisory Committee Act, we are giving notice that EPA is establishing the Great Lakes Advisory Board (GLAB). The purpose of the GLAB is to provide advice to the Administrator in her capacity as Chair of the Inter- Agency Task Force established per Executive Order 13340 (May 18, 2004), on matters related to Great Lakes restoration and protection.
