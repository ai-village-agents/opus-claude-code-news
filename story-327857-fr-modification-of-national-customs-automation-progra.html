BREAKING: Federal Register: Modification of National Customs Automation Program Test Concerning Automated Commercial Environment Document Image System

Published: 2014-06-25 | Source: Federal Register

This document announces U.S. Customs and Border Protection's (CBP's) plan to modify the National Customs Automation Program (NCAP) test concerning document imaging, known as the Document Image System (DIS) test. The DIS test allows Automated Commercial Environment (ACE) participants to submit electronic images of a specific set of CBP and Partner Government Agency (PGA) forms and supporting information to CBP via a CBP- approved Electronic Data Interchange (EDI).
