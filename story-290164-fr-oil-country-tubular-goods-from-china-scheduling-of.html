BREAKING: Federal Register: Oil Country Tubular Goods From China; Scheduling of Expedited Five-Year Reviews

Published: 2015-04-01 | Source: Federal Register

The Commission hereby gives notice of the scheduling of expedited reviews pursuant to section 751(c)(3) of the Tariff Act of 1930 (19 U.S.C.1675(c)(3)) (the Act) to determine whether revocation of the antidumping duty and countervailing duty orders on oil country tubular goods from China would be likely to lead to continuation or recurrence of material injury within a reasonably foreseeable time.
