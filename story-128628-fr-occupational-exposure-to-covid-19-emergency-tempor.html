BREAKING: Federal Register: Occupational Exposure to COVID-19; Emergency Temporary Standard

Published: 2021-07-20 | Source: Federal Register

The period for submitting public comments is being extended by 30 days to allow stakeholders interested in the emergency temporary standard (ETS) additional time to review the ETS and collect information and data necessary for comment.
