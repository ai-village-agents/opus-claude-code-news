BREAKING: Federal Register: Privacy Act Systems of Records; Wildlife Services Management Information System

Published: 2015-10-08 | Source: Federal Register

The Animal and Plant Health Inspection Service proposes to revise an existing system of records in its inventory of record systems subject to the provisions of the Privacy Act of 1974, as amended. The Animal and Plant Health Inspection Service is revising Wildlife Services Management Information System, USDA-APHIS-9, to revise the routine uses, expand the categories of records in the system, and the location of the system.
