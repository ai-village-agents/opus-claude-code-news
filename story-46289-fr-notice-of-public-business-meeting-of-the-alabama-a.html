BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Public Business Meeting of the Alabama Advisory Committee to the U.S. Commission on Civil Rights

Published: 2024-09-06 | Source: Federal Register

Notice is hereby given, pursuant to the provisions of the rules and regulations of the U.S. Commission on Civil Rights (Commission) and the Federal Advisory Committee Act that the Alabama Advisory Committee (Committee) will hold a business meeting on Thursday, September 26, 2024 at 10:00 a.m. Central time. The Committee will review Committee processes to discuss civil rights topics.
