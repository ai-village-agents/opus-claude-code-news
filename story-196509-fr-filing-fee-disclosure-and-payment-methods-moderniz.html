BREAKING: Federal Register: Filing Fee Disclosure and Payment Methods Modernization

Published: 2019-12-27 | Source: Federal Register

We are proposing amendments that would modernize filing fee disclosure and payment methods. We are proposing to amend most fee- bearing forms, schedules, statements, and related rules to require each fee table and accompanying disclosure to include all required information for fee calculation in a structured format.
