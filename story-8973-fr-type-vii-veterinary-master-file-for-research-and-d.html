BREAKING: Federal Register: Type VII Veterinary Master File for Research and Development and Risk Reviews; Draft Guidance for Industry; Reopening of the Comment Period

Published: 2025-04-09 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA or the Agency) is reopening the comment period for the notice announcing the availability of a draft guidance for industry (GFI) that appeared in the Federal Register of January 7, 2025. In that notice, FDA requested comments on draft GFI #260 entitled "Type VII Veterinary Master File for Research and Development and Risk Reviews."
