BREAKING: Federal Register: Change to County-Equivalents in the State of Connecticut

Published: 2022-06-06 | Source: Federal Register

This notice provides information about the State of Connecticut's (hereafter Connecticut or the State) formal request to the Census Bureau to adopt the State's nine planning regions as county- equivalent geographic units for purposes of collecting, tabulating, and disseminating statistical data, replacing the eight counties which ceased to function as governmental and administrative entities in 1960.
