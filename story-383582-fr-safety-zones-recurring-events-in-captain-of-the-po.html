BREAKING: Federal Register: Safety Zones; Recurring Events in Captain of the Port New York Zone

Published: 2012-04-19 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard will enforce various safety zones in the Captain of the Port New York Zone on specified dates and times. This action is necessary to ensure the safety of vessels and spectators from hazards associated with fireworks displays. During the enforcement period, no person or vessel may enter the safety zone without permission of the Captain of the Port (COTP).
