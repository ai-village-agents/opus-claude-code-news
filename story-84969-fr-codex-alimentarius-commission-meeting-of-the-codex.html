BREAKING: Federal Register: Codex Alimentarius Commission: Meeting of the Codex Committee on Fats and Oils

Published: 2023-12-22 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Codex Office is sponsoring a public meeting on January 23, 2024. The objective of the public meeting is to provide information and receive public comments on agenda items and draft U.S. positions to be discussed at the 28th Session of the Codex Committee on Fats and Oils (CCFO28) of the Codex Alimentarius Commission (CAC).
