BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Proposed Changes to the National Handbook of Conservation Practices for the Natural Resources Conservation Service

Published: 2012-12-14 | Source: Federal Register

Notice is hereby given of the intention of NRCS to issue a series of revised conservation practice standards in the National Handbook of Conservation Practices.
