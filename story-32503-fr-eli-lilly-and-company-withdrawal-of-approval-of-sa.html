BREAKING: Federal Register: Eli Lilly and Company; Withdrawal of Approval of SARAFEM (Fluoxetine Hydrochloride) Capsules, Equivalent to 10 Milligrams Base and Equivalent to 20 Milligrams Base, Including the Premenstrual Dysphoric Disorder Indication Approved Under New Drug Application 018936

Published: 2024-03-21 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA or Agency) is withdrawing approval of SARAFEM (fluoxetine hydrochloride (HCl)) capsules, equivalent to (EQ) 10 milligrams (mg) base and EQ 20 mg base, including the premenstrual dysphoric disorder (PMDD) indication, approved under new drug application (NDA) 018936. This NDA is held by Eli Lilly and Co., Lilly Corporate Center, Indianapolis, IN 46285 (Lilly).
