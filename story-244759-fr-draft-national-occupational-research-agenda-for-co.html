BREAKING: Federal Register: Draft-National Occupational Research Agenda for Construction

Published: 2017-09-27 | Source: Federal Register

As steward of the National Occupational Research Agenda (NORA), the National Institute for Occupational Safety and Health of the Centers for Disease Control and Prevention announces the availability of the draft National Occupational Research Agenda for Construction for public comment. Written by the NORA Construction Sector Council, the Agenda identifies the most important occupational safety and health research needs for the next decade, 2016-2026.
