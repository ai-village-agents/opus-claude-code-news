BREAKING: Federal Register: Request for Letters of Intent To Apply for 2015 Technology Initiative Grant Funding

Published: 2016-01-20 | Source: Federal Register

The Legal Services Corporation (LSC) issues this Notice describing the conditions under which Letters of Intent To Apply for Technology Initiative Grants (TIG) will be received. LSC's TIG program was established in 2000. Since that time, LSC has made 647 grants totaling more than $53 million. This grant program provides an important tool to help achieve LSC's goal of increasing the quantity and quality of legal services available to eligible persons.
