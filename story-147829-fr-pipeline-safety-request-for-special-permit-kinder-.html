BREAKING: Federal Register: Pipeline Safety: Request for Special Permit; Kinder Morgan Louisiana Pipeline, LLC

Published: 2020-03-27 | Source: Federal Register

PHMSA is publishing this notice to solicit public comment on a request from Kinder Morgan Louisiana Pipeline, LLC, (KMLP) to modify a previously issued special permit. KMLP requests a modification to the pipeline gas stream quality requirements that limit the amount of hydrogen sulfide (H2S) in the gas.
