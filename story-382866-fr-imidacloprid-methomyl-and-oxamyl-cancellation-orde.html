BREAKING: Federal Register: Imidacloprid, Methomyl, and Oxamyl; Cancellation Order for Amendments To Terminate Uses

Published: 2012-04-11 | Source: Federal Register

This notice announces EPA's order for amendments to terminate uses, voluntarily requested by registrants and accepted by the Agency, of products containing imidacloprid, methomyl, and oxamyl, pursuant to section 6(f)(1) of the Federal Insecticide, Fungicide, and Rodenticide Act (FIFRA), as amended.
