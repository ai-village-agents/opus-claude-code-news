BREAKING: Federal Register: The “100,000 Strong” Initiative Federal Advisory Committee: Notice of Meeting

Published: 2012-06-01 | Source: Federal Register

The Bureau of East Asian and Pacific Affairs of the Department of State hereby gives notice of a public meeting of the "100,000 Strong" Initiative Federal Advisory Committee. The "100,000 Strong" Federal Advisory Committee, composed of prominent China experts and leaders in business, academic, and non-profit organizations, serves a critical advisory role in achieving the Obama Administration's goal, announced in May 2010, of seeing 100,000 Americans study in China by 2014.
