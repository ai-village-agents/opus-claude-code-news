BREAKING: Federal Register: Significant New Use Rules on Certain Chemical Substances

Published: 2016-11-17 | Source: Federal Register

EPA is promulgating significant new use rules (SNURs) under the Toxic Substances Control Act (TSCA) for 57 chemical substances which were the subject of premanufacture notices (PMNs).
