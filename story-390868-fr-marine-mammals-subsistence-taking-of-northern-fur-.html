BREAKING: Federal Register: Marine Mammals; Subsistence Taking of Northern Fur Seals; St. Paul Island

Published: 2012-07-12 | Source: Federal Register

NMFS announces the receipt of a petition for rulemaking under the Administrative Procedure Act (APA). The Pribilof Island Community of St. Paul Island, Aleut Community of St. Paul Island-Tribal Government (St. Paul) petitioned NMFS to revise regulations governing the subsistence taking of northern fur seals on St. Paul Island.
