BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Proposed Revisions for the LSC Grant Assurances for Calendar Year 2014 Funding

Published: 2013-05-01 | Source: Federal Register

The Legal Services Corporation ("LSC") intends to revise the LSC Grant Assurances for calendar year 2014 funding since the last publication of the LSC Grant Assurances for 2013 funding and is soliciting public comment on the proposed changes. The proposed revisions affect Grant Assurances 13 and 17.
