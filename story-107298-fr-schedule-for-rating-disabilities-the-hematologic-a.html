BREAKING: Federal Register: Schedule for Rating Disabilities: The Hematologic and Lymphatic Systems; Correction

Published: 2022-10-11 | Source: Federal Register

On October 29, 2018, the Department of Veterans Affairs (VA) published in the Federal Register a final rule that amended the portion of the VA Schedule for Rating Disabilities ("VASRD" or "rating schedule") that addresses the hematologic and lymphatic systems.
