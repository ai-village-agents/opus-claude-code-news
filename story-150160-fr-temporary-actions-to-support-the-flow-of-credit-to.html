BREAKING: Federal Register: Temporary Actions To Support the Flow of Credit to Households and Businesses by Encouraging Use of Intraday Credit

Published: 2020-04-28 | Source: Federal Register

Due to the extraordinary disruptions from the coronavirus disease 2019 (COVID-19), the Board of Governors of the Federal Reserve System (Board) is announcing temporary actions aimed at encouraging healthy depository institutions to utilize intraday credit extended by Federal Reserve Banks (Reserve Banks).
