BREAKING: Federal Register: Public Input on the Development and Potential Issuance of Treasury Floating Rate Notes

Published: 2012-03-19 | Source: Federal Register

The Secretary of the Treasury ("Secretary") is authorized under Chapter 31 of Title 31, United States Code, to issue United States obligations and to offer them for sale under such terms and conditions as the Secretary may prescribe. The Department of the Treasury ("Treasury") is requesting comments on the potential issuance of a floating rate note.
