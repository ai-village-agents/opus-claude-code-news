BREAKING: Federal Register: Supplemental Nutrition Assistance Program: Trafficking Controls and Fraud Investigations

Published: 2013-08-21 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Nutrition Service (FNS) is issuing a final rule to amend Supplemental Nutrition Assistance Program (SNAP or Program) regulations to allow State agencies to deny a request for a replacement card until contact is made by the household with the State agency, if the requests for replacement cards are determined to be excessive.
