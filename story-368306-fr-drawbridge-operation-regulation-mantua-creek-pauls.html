BREAKING: Federal Register: Drawbridge Operation Regulation; Mantua Creek, Paulsboro, NJ

Published: 2013-10-28 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard proposes to modify the operating schedule that governs the New Jersey State Route 44 lift bridge over the Mantua Creek, mile marker 1.7, at Paulsboro, NJ. Bridge tender logs from 2007- 2013 indicates that the majority of the marine traffic transits Mantua Creek during the summer months.
