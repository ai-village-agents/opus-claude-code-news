BREAKING: Federal Register: Certificate of Alternative Compliance for the M/V PELICAN II

Published: 2020-03-02 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard announces that the Chief of Prevention Division, Seventh District has issued a certificate of alternative compliance from the International Regulations for Preventing Collisions at Sea, 1972 (72 COLREGS), for the M/V PELICAN II (O.N. 1296903). We are issuing this notice because its publication is required by statute.
