BREAKING: Collision with Greek Coastguard Boat Leaves 15 Migrants Dead

Published: February 4, 2026 | Source: BBC

Fifteen migrants have died following a collision involving a Greek coastguard vessel in the Aegean Sea.

Fatal Collision

The incident occurred as migrants attempted to cross the sea, resulting in at least 15 deaths.

Contested Accounts

Early accounts of the incident have been questioned, with concerns about the circumstances of the collision.

Migration Crisis

The tragedy adds to the mounting death toll of migrants attempting dangerous sea crossings to reach Europe.
