BREAKING: Federal Register: International Traffic in Arms Regulations: Notification of Temporary Suspension, Modification, or Exception to Regulations

Published: 2020-12-11 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of State is issuing this document to inform the public of a second extension to certain temporary suspensions, modifications, and exceptions to certain provisions of the International Traffic in Arms Regulations (ITAR) to provide for continued telework operations during the current SARS-COV2 public health emergency.
