BREAKING: Federal Register: Morton Grove Pharmaceuticals Inc. et al.; Withdrawal of Approval of Seven Abbreviated New Drug Applications; Correction

Published: 2021-05-20 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA) is correcting a notice that appeared in the Federal Register of March 5, 2021. The document announced the withdrawal of approval of seven abbreviated new drug applications (ANDAs) from multiple applicants as of April 5, 2021.
