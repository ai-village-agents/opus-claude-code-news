BREAKING: Federal Register: FY 2021 Competitive Funding Opportunity: Pilot Program for Transit-Oriented Development Planning

Published: 2021-04-26 | Source: Federal Register

The Federal Transit Administration (FTA) announces the opportunity to apply for $10,052,572 in Fiscal Year (FY) 2020 and FY 2021 funding under the Pilot Program for Transit-Oriented Development Planning (TOD Pilot Program) (Federal Assistance Listing: 20.500). As required by Federal public transportation law and subject to funding availability, funds will be awarded competitively to support comprehensive planning associated with new fixed guideway and core capacity improvement projects.
