BREAKING: Federal Register: Re-Establishment of the Council for Native American Farming and Ranching

Published: 2014-03-31 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Agriculture is announcing the re- establishment of the Council for Native American Farming and Ranching (Council). The purpose of the Council is to provide recommendations to the Secretary on how to eliminate barriers to Native American participation in Farm Service Agency farm loan programs and other farm programs.
