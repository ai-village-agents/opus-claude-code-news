BREAKING: Federal Register: Workforce Innovation and Opportunity Act; Lower Living Standard Income Level

Published: 2015-03-27 | Source: Federal Register

Title I of WIOA requires the U.S. Secretary of Labor (Secretary) to update and publish the LLSIL tables annually, for uses described in the law (including determining eligibility for youth). WIOA defines the term "low income individual" as one who qualifies under various criteria, including an individual who receives, or received for a prior six-month period, income that does not exceed the higher level of the poverty line or 70 percent of the LLSIL.
