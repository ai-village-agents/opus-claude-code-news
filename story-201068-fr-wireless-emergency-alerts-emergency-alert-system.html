BREAKING: Federal Register: Wireless Emergency Alerts; Emergency Alert System

Published: 2018-02-28 | Source: Federal Register

This document adopts revisions to Wireless Emergency Alert (WEA) rules to improve utility of WEA as a life-saving tool. By this action, the Commission adopts rules that will improve the accuracy with which Participating CMS Providers transmit Alert Messages to the specified target area.
