BREAKING: Federal Register: Temporary Rule to Increase the Commercial Annual Catch Limit for South Atlantic Yellowtail Snapper

Published: 2012-11-07 | Source: Federal Register

NMFS issues this temporary rule to increase the commercial annual catch limit (ACL) for yellowtail snapper, as requested by the South Atlantic Fishery Management Council (Council). NMFS has determined that the commercial ACL may be increased from 1,142,589 lb (518,270 kg) to 1,596,510 lb (724,165 kg).
