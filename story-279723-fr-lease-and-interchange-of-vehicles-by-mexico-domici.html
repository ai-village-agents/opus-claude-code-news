BREAKING: Federal Register: Lease and Interchange of Vehicles by Mexico-Domiciled Motor Carriers

Published: 2016-11-22 | Source: Federal Register

Section 219(d) of the Motor Carrier Safety Improvement Act of 1999 (MCSIA) restricted Mexico-domiciled motor carriers from leasing commercial motor vehicles (CMVs) to U.S. carriers to transport property into the United States until the international obligations under the North American Free Trade Agreement (NAFTA) chapter on cross-border trade in services were met.
