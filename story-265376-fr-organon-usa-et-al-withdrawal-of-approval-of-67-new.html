BREAKING: Federal Register: Organon USA et al.; Withdrawal of Approval of 67 New Drug Applications and 128 Abbreviated New Drug Applications; Correction

Published: 2016-06-06 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA) is correcting a notice that appeared in the Federal Register of October 13, 2015 (80 FR 61426). The document announced the withdrawal of approval of 67 new drug applications (NDAs) and 128 abbreviated new drug applications from multiple applicants, effective November 12, 2015.
