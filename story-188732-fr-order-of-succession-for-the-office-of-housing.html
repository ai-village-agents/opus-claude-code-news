BREAKING: Federal Register: Order of Succession for the Office of Housing

Published: 2019-09-16 | Source: Federal Register

In this notice, the Assistant Secretary for Housing--FH Commissioner designates the Order of Succession for the Office of Housing. This Order of Succession supersedes all prior orders of succession for the Assistant Secretary for Housing--FH Commissioner, including the Order of Succession published on April 20, 2015.
