BREAKING: Federal Register: Environmental Impact and Related Procedures

Published: 2013-02-19 | Source: Federal Register

This final rule amends the Federal Highway Administration (FHWA) and Federal Transit Administration (FTA) joint procedures that implement the National Environmental Policy Act (NEPA) by enacting a new categorical exclusion (CE) for emergency actions as required by the Moving Ahead for Progress in the 21st Century Act (MAP-21).
