BREAKING: Federal Register: Authority of DOE Protective Force Officers That Are Federal Employees To Make Arrests Without a Warrant for Certain Crimes

Published: 2015-04-29 | Source: Federal Register

Section 161 k. of the Atomic Energy Act, as amended, empowers the Secretary of Energy ("the Secretary") to authorize designated U.S. Department of Energy (DOE) employees and contractors to make an arrest without a warrant for certain crimes. Specifically, the Secretary may authorize the arrest of any individual who has committed a federal crime in the presence of a DOE protective force officer regarding the property of the United States in the custody of DOE or DOE contractors.
