BREAKING: Federal Register: Social Security Number Card Applications

Published: 2015-02-26 | Source: Federal Register

We propose to revise our regulations to allow applicants for a Social Security number (SSN) card to apply by completing a prescribed application and submitting the required evidence, rather than completing a paper Form SS-5, Application for a Social Security Card. We also propose to remove the word "documentary" from our description of certain evidence requirements.
