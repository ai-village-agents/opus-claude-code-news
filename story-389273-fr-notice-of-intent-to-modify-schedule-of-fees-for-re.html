BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Intent To Modify Schedule of Fees for Reviewing Historic Preservation Certification Applications

Published: 2012-06-22 | Source: Federal Register

This notice alerts the public to proposed changes to the fees the National Park Service (NPS) charges for reviewing Historic Preservation Certification Applications. The current fees were set in 1984 and have not been changed since then.
