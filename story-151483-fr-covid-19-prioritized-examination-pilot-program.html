BREAKING: Federal Register: COVID-19 Prioritized Examination Pilot Program

Published: 2020-05-14 | Source: Federal Register

The United States Patent and Trademark Office (USPTO or Office) is implementing a pilot program to provide prioritized examination of certain patent applications. To qualify, the claim(s) of an application must cover a product or process related to COVID-19, and such product or process must be subject to an applicable FDA approval for COVID-19 use.
