BREAKING: Federal Register: Temporary Rule To Establish Management Measures for Red Grouper in the Gulf of Mexico

Published: 2019-05-17 | Source: Federal Register

NMFS issues an emergency rule as requested by the Gulf of Mexico Fishery Management Council (Council) to address concerns that the Gulf of Mexico (Gulf) red grouper stock may be in decline. This emergency rule reduces the commercial and recreational annual catch limits (ACLs) and annual catch targets (ACTs).
