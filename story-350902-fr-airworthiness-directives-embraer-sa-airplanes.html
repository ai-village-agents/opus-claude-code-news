BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Embraer S.A. Airplanes

Published: 2013-03-29 | Source: Federal Register

We are adopting a new airworthiness directive (AD) for certain Embraer S.A. Model ERJ 170 and ERJ 190 airplanes. This AD was prompted by a report that high rate discharge (HRD) bottle explosive cartridges of a cargo compartment fire extinguisher system were swapped between the forward and aft cargo compartments.
