BREAKING: Federal Register: Small Business Size Standards: Adoption of 2022 North American Industry Classification System for Size Standards

Published: 2022-09-29 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Small Business Administration ("SBA" or "Agency") amends its small business size regulations to incorporate the U.S. Office of Management and Budget's (OMB) North American Industry Classification System (NAICS) revision for 2022, identified as NAICS 2022, into its table of small business size standards.
