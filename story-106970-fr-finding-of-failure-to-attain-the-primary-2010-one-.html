BREAKING: Federal Register: Finding of Failure To Attain the Primary 2010 One-Hour Sulfur Dioxide Standard for the St. Bernard Parish, Louisiana Nonattainment Area

Published: 2022-10-05 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is making a determination that the St. Bernard Parish sulfur dioxide (SO2) nonattainment area ("St. Bernard area" or "area") failed to attain the primary 2010 one-hour SO2 national ambient air quality standard (NAAQS) under the Clean Air Act (CAA or the Act) by the applicable attainment date of October 4, 2018.
