BREAKING: Federal Register: Protection of Stratospheric Ozone: Request for Methyl Bromide Critical Use Exemption Applications

Published: 2015-07-01 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is providing notice of the process for submitting applications for critical use exemptions for 2018 and subsequent years. Critical use exemptions are exceptions to the phaseout of production and import of methyl bromide, a controlled class I ozone-depleting substance.
