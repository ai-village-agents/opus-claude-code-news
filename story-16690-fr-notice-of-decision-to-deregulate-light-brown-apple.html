BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Decision To Deregulate Light Brown Apple Moth for Fruit Imported From New Zealand Into the United States

Published: 2025-07-25 | Source: Federal Register

We are advising the public of our decision to deregulate light brown apple moth (LBAM) for fruit imported from New Zealand into the United States. Currently, fruit imported from New Zealand into the United States must be accompanied by a phytosanitary certificate with an additional declaration stating that the fruit is free of LBAM.
