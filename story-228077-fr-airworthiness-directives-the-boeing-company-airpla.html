BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; The Boeing Company Airplanes

Published: 2017-02-22 | Source: Federal Register

We are adopting a new airworthiness directive (AD) all The Boeing Company Model 737-600, -700, -700C, -800, and -900 series airplanes. This AD was prompted by reports of paint deterioration on the surface of the main landing gear (MLG) and the early onset of corrosion in the trunnion bore of the MLG outer cylinder.
