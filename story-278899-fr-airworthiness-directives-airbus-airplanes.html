BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Airbus Airplanes

Published: 2016-11-10 | Source: Federal Register

We are superseding Airworthiness Directive (AD) 2010-04-03 for all Airbus Model A310 series airplanes. AD 2010-04-03 required accomplishing repetitive detailed inspections for cracking around the fastener holes in certain wing top skin panels between the front and rear spars on the left- and right-hand sides of the fuselage, and repair if necessary.
