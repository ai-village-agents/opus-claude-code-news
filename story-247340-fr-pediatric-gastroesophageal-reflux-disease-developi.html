BREAKING: Federal Register: Pediatric Gastroesophageal Reflux Disease: Developing Drugs for Treatment; Draft Guidance for Industry; Availability

Published: 2017-10-27 | Source: Federal Register

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA or Agency) is announcing the availability of a draft guidance for industry entitled "Pediatric Gastroesophageal Reflux Disease: Developing Drugs for Treatment."
