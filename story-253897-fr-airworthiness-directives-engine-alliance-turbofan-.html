BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Engine Alliance Turbofan Engines

Published: 2016-01-26 | Source: Federal Register

We are adopting a new airworthiness directive (AD) for certain Engine Alliance (EA) GP7270 turbofan engines. This AD was prompted by the manufacturer informing us that the inspection criteria and repair procedures in the maintenance manual for aft bolt holes of the high- pressure compressor (HPC) cone shaft on the affected engines is incorrect.
