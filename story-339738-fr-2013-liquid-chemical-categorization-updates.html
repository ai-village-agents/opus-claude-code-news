BREAKING: Federal Register: 2013 Liquid Chemical Categorization Updates

Published: 2014-11-14 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard announces an additional two-year delay of the effective date of its 2013 interim rule, which updates and revises tables that list liquid hazardous materials, liquefied gases, and compressed gases that have been approved by the Coast Guard and the International Maritime Organization for maritime transportation in bulk.
