BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Intent To Make Temporary Changes in the State Title V Maternal and Child Health Block Grant Allocations

Published: 2022-09-09 | Source: Federal Register

HRSA plans to move forward in implementing temporary changes to the method of calculating poverty-based allocations under Title V of the Social Security Act for HRSA's State Title V Maternal and Child Health (MCH) Services Block Grant, beginning in Fiscal Year (FY) 2023. Since FY 2017, the poverty-based allocation has been based on the U.S. Census Bureau's 3-year American Community Survey (ACS) estimates using three pooled 1-year estimates.
