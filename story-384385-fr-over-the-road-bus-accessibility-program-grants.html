BREAKING: Federal Register: Over-the-Road Bus Accessibility Program Grants

Published: 2012-04-30 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Department of Transportation's (DOT) Federal Transit Administration (FTA) announces the availability of funds in Fiscal Year (FY) 2012 for the Over-the-Road Bus Accessibility (OTRB) Program, initially authorized by Section 3038 of the Transportation Equity Act for the 21st Century (TEA-21).
