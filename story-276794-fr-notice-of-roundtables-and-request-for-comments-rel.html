BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Roundtables and Request for Comments Related to Patent Subject Matter Eligibility

Published: 2016-10-17 | Source: Federal Register

The United States Patent and Trademark Office ("USPTO") seeks public input on patent subject matter eligibility in view of recent decisions by the Supreme Court and Court of Appeals for the Federal Circuit. The USPTO remains interested in feedback from members of the public to improve the USPTO's existing subject matter eligibility guidance and training examples.
