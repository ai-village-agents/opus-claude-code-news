BREAKING: Federal Register: University of Missouri-Columbia Facility Operating License No. R-103

Published: 2013-05-20 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Nuclear Regulatory Commission (NRC) is considering an application for the renewal of Facility Operating License No. R-103 ("Application"), which currently authorizes the Curators of the University of Missouri--Columbia (the licensee) to operate the Missouri University Research Reactor (MURR) at a maximum steady-state thermal power of 10 megawatts (MW).
