BREAKING: Federal Register: Portable Fuel Container Safety Act Regulation

Published: 2023-10-31 | Source: Federal Register

The Portable Fuel Container Safety Act of 2020 (PFCSA) provides that the Consumer Product Safety Commission (Commission) must promulgate a rule to require flame mitigation devices in portable fuel containers that impede the propagation of flame into the container, unless the Commission determines that there is a voluntary standard for flame mitigation devices that achieves the same result.
