BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Request for Extension of a Currently Approved Information Collection

Published: 2012-01-04 | Source: Federal Register

In accordance with the Paperwork Reduction Act of 1995, this notice announces the Rural Business-Cooperative Service's intention to request an extension for a currently approved information collection in support of the Energy Audit and Renewable Energy Development Assistance (EA/REDA) program administered under the Rural Energy for America Program (REAP).
