BREAKING: Federal Register: Microloan Program Expanded Eligibility and Other Program Changes

Published: 2014-03-17 | Source: Federal Register

This proposed rule would amend certain existing regulations for the Microloan Program. The Microloan Program assists women, low income, veteran, and minority entrepreneurs, and others capable of operating a small business that are in need of small amounts of financial assistance.
