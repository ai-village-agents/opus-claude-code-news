BREAKING: Federal Register: Delegation of Authority for the Office of Departmental Equal Employment Opportunity

Published: 2024-03-27 | Source: Federal Register

In this notice, the Secretary of HUD delegates concurrent authority to the Director and Deputy Director of the Office of Departmental Equal Employment Opportunity (ODEEO) with respect to all matters pertaining to the work of ODEEO and supersedes any prior delegation of authority for ODEEO including the delegation of authority published in the Federal Register on June 22, 2011.
