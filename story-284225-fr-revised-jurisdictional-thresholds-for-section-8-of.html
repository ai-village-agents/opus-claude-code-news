BREAKING: Federal Register: Revised Jurisdictional Thresholds for Section 8 of the Clayton Act

Published: 2015-01-21 | Source: Federal Register

The Federal Trade Commission announces the revised thresholds for interlocking directorates required by the 1990 amendment of Section 8 of the Clayton Act. Section 8 prohibits, with certain exceptions, one person from serving as a director or officer of two competing corporations if two thresholds are met.
