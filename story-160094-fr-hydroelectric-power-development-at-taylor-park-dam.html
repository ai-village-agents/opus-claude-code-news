BREAKING: Federal Register: Hydroelectric Power Development at Taylor Park Dam, Uncompahgre Project, Colorado

Published: 2020-09-01 | Source: Federal Register

Current Federal policy allows non-Federal development of electrical power resource potential on Federal water resource projects. The Bureau of Reclamation (Reclamation) will consider proposals for non-Federal development of hydroelectric power at Taylor Park Dam, a feature of the Uncompahgre Project, located in Colorado.
