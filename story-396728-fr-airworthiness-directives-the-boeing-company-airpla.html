BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; The Boeing Company Airplanes

Published: 2012-09-18 | Source: Federal Register

We propose to adopt a new airworthiness directive (AD) for certain The Boeing Company Model 737-700 and -700C series airplanes. This proposed AD was prompted by reports of early fatigue cracks at chem-mill areas on the crown skin panels. This proposed AD would require repetitive inspections for cracking of the fuselage skin at certain locations at chem-mill areas, and repair if necessary.
