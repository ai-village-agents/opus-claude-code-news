BREAKING: Federal Register: Request for Comments on the Renewal of a Previously Approved Information Collection-Quarterly Readiness of Strategic Seaport Facilities Reporting

Published: 2021-05-21 | Source: Federal Register

The Maritime Administration (MARAD) invites public comments on our intention to renew an information collection. The information to be collected will be used by MARAD and Department of Defense (DoD) personnel to evaluate strategic commercial seaport readiness to prepare for contingency military deployment needs and make plans for the use of this capability to meet national emergency requirements.
