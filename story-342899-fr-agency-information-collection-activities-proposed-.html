BREAKING: Federal Register: Agency Information Collection Activities; Proposed Renewal and Consolidation of Several Currently Approved Collections; Comment Request

Published: 2014-12-29 | Source: Federal Register

In compliance with the Paperwork Reduction Act (PRA), this document announces that EPA is planning to submit a request to renew and consolidate three currently approved Information Collection Requests (ICRs) to the Office of Management and Budget (OMB). The consolidated ICR is entitled: "Lead Training, Certification, Accreditation and Authorization Activities" and identified by (EPA ICR No. 2507.01 and OMB Control No. 2070-(NEW)).
