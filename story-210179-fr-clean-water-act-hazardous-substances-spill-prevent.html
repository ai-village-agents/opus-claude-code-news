BREAKING: Federal Register: Clean Water Act Hazardous Substances Spill Prevention

Published: 2018-06-25 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA or the Agency) is proposing to establish no new requirements under Clean Water Act (CWA), section 311. This section directs the President to issue regulations to prevent discharges of oil and hazardous substances from onshore and offshore facilities, and to contain such discharges.
