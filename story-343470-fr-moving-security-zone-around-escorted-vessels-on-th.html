BREAKING: Federal Register: Moving Security Zone Around Escorted Vessels on the Lower Mississippi River

Published: 2013-01-03 | Source: Federal Register

The Captain of the Port of New Orleans (COTP New Orleans) is establishing a Moving Security Zone on the Mississippi river, mile marker 88.0 through mile marker 106.0, extending 300 yards on all sides of vessels being escorted by one or more Coast Guard assets or other federal, state, or local law enforcement agency assets.
