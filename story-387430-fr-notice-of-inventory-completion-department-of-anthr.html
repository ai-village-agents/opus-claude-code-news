BREAKING: Federal Register: Notice of Inventory Completion: Department of Anthropology Museum at the University of California, Davis, Davis, CA

Published: 2012-06-04 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of Anthropology Museum at the University of California, Davis, has completed an inventory of human remains, in consultation with the appropriate Indian tribes, and has determined that there is no cultural affiliation between the remains and any present-day tribe. Representatives of any Indian tribe that believes itself to be culturally affiliated with the human remains may contact the Department of Anthropology Museum at the University of California, Davis.
