BREAKING: Federal Register: Interstate Compact on Educational Opportunity for Military Children

Published: 2016-03-07 | Source: Federal Register

DOD is establishing policies based on section 539 of Public Law 111-84 to implement the Interstate Compact on Educational Opportunity for Military Children (referred to as the "Compact") within the DoD. The proposed rule provides components with policies to support the intent of the Compact, which is to aid the transition of school-age children in military families between school districts (to include between Department of Defense Educational Activity schools and state school districts).
