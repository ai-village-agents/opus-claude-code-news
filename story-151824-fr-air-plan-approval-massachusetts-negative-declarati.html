BREAKING: Federal Register: Air Plan Approval; Massachusetts; Negative Declaration for the Oil and Gas Industry

Published: 2020-05-18 | Source: Federal Register

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is proposing to approve a State Implementation Plan (SIP) revision submitted by the Commonwealth of Massachusetts. The revision provides Massachusetts' determination, via a negative declaration, that there are no facilities within its borders subject to EPA's 2016 Control Technique Guideline (CTG) for the oil and gas industry.
