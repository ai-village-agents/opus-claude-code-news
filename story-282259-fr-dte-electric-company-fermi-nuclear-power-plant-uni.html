BREAKING: Federal Register: DTE Electric Company; Fermi Nuclear Power Plant, Unit 2

Published: 2016-12-22 | Source: Federal Register

The U.S. Nuclear Regulatory Commission (NRC) issued a renewal of Facility Operating License No. NPF-43, held by DTE Electric Company (DTE or the licensee), for the continued operation of Fermi Nuclear Power Plant, Unit 2 (Fermi 2).
