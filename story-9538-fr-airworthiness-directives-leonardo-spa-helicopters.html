BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; Leonardo S.p.a. Helicopters

Published: 2025-04-18 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA is adopting a new airworthiness directive (AD) for all Leonardo S.p.a. Model A109C, A109E, A109S, and AW109SP helicopters. This AD was prompted by a report of a quality escape on a batch of main rotor blades (MRBs). This AD requires repetitively tap inspecting certain MRBs and replacing those MRBs.
