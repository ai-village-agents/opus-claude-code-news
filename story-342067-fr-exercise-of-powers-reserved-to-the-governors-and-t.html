BREAKING: Federal Register: Exercise of Powers Reserved to the Governors and the Board of Governors

Published: 2014-12-16 | Source: Federal Register

The Board of Governors of the Postal Service has adopted a resolution regarding the exercise of those powers reserved to the Board upon the loss of a quorum of the Board. During the time in which the Board is unable to form a quorum, those powers needed to provide for continuity of operations would be delegated to a Temporary Emergency Committee composed of the remaining members of the Board.
