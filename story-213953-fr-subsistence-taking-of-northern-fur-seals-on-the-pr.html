BREAKING: Federal Register: Subsistence Taking of Northern Fur Seals on the Pribilof Islands

Published: 2018-08-14 | Source: Federal Register

NMFS proposes to modify the subsistence use regulations for the Eastern Pacific stock of northern fur seals (Callorhinus ursinus) in response to a petition from the Aleut Community of St. Paul Island, Tribal Government (ACSPI).
