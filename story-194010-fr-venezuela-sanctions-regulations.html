BREAKING: Federal Register: Venezuela Sanctions Regulations

Published: 2019-11-22 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of the Treasury's Office of Foreign Assets Control (OFAC) is amending the Venezuela Sanctions Regulations to incorporate additional Executive orders, add a general license authorizing U.S. Government activities, and add an interpretive provision.
