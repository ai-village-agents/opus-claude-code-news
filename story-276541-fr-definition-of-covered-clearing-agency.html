BREAKING: Federal Register: Definition of Covered Clearing Agency

Published: 2016-10-13 | Source: Federal Register

The Securities and Exchange Commission ("SEC" or "Commission") proposes to amend the definition of "covered clearing agency" under Rule 17Ad-22 to mean a registered clearing agency that provides the services of a central counterparty ("CCP"), central securities depository ("CSD"), or a securities settlement system ("SSS").
