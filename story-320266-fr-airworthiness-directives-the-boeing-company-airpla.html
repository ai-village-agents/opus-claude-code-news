BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; The Boeing Company Airplanes

Published: 2014-03-28 | Source: Federal Register

We are superseding Airworthiness Directive (AD) 2008-08-25 that applied to certain The Boeing Company Model 747-400 and 747-400F series airplanes. AD 2008-08-25 required installing drains and drain tubes to eliminate water accumulation in the drip shield above the M826 cardfile in the main equipment center.
