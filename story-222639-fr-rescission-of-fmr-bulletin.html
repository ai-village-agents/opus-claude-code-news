BREAKING: Federal Register: Rescission of FMR Bulletin

Published: 2018-12-06 | Source: Federal Register

GSA has determined the guidance for requesting a delegation of authority for the procurement of transportation (freight and cargo, including household goods) and traffic management services from the Administrator of General Services to be administratively burdensome and ineffective. Therefore, GSA is officially rescinding GSA Bulletin FMR D-1, Transportation Management.
