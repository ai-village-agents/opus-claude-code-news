BREAKING: Federal Register: National Environmental Policy Act Implementing Procedures for the Bureau of Reclamation

Published: 2024-06-07 | Source: Federal Register

The Department of the Interior (Department), Bureau of Reclamation (Reclamation) proposes to revise seven categorical exclusions (CE) under the National Environmental Policy Act of 1969 (NEPA) in Reclamation's NEPA implementing procedures, Departmental Manual (DM) at part 516, chapter 14.
