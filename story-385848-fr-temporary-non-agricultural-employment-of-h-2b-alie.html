BREAKING: Federal Register: Temporary Non-agricultural Employment of H-2B Aliens in the United States

The Department of Labor (the Department) is providing notice of the judicial order enjoining the Department from implementing and enforcing the Temporary Non-agricultural Employment of H-2B Aliens in the United States, published February 21, 2012 (the 2012 H-2B Final Rule).
