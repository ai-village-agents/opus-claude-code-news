BREAKING: Federal Register: Proposed Technical Standard Order (TSO)-C151c, Terrain Awareness and Warning System (TAWS)

Published: 2012-02-03 | Source: Federal Register

This notice announces the availability of, and request for public comments on a second draft of Technical Standard Order (TSO)- C151c, Terrain Awareness and Warning System. Comments received from the initial June 2011 release, resulted in changes to the proposed document significant enough to require this public comment offering.
