BREAKING: Federal Register: Airworthiness Directives; The Boeing Company Airplanes

Published: 2023-04-06 | Source: Federal Register

The FAA proposes to adopt a new airworthiness directive (AD) for all The Boeing Company Model 747-8 and 747-8F series airplanes. This proposed AD was prompted by reports of cracks in stringers, common to the end fittings, forward and aft of the pressure bulkhead at station (STA) 2360 at multiple stringer locations.
