BREAKING: Federal Register: Approval and Promulgation of Implementation Plans; Oregon: Interstate Transport of Fine Particulate Matter

Published: 2015-01-16 | Source: Federal Register

The EPA is approving a portion of the State Implementation Plan submission from the State of Oregon to address Clean Air Act interstate transport requirements for the 2006 24-hour fine particulate matter (PM2.5) National Ambient Air Quality Standards. The Clean Air Act requires that each State Implementation Plan contain adequate provisions prohibiting air emissions that will have certain adverse air quality effects in other states.
