BREAKING: Federal Register: Investment Company Reporting Modernization

Published: 2016-11-18 | Source: Federal Register

The Securities and Exchange Commission is adopting new rules and forms as well as amendments to its rules and forms to modernize the reporting and disclosure of information by registered investment companies. The Commission is adopting new Form N-PORT, which will require certain registered investment companies to report information about their monthly portfolio holdings to the Commission in a structured data format.
