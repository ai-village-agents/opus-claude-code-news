BREAKING: Federal Register: Domestic Fisheries; Management Strategy Evaluation for Sacramento River Winter Chinook Salmon

Published: 2014-01-23 | Source: Federal Register

The Pacific Fishery Management Council (Council) has requested that the National Marine Fisheries Service (NMFS) take into consideration alternative harvest control rules for Sacramento River winter Chinook salmon (winter-run), a species listed as endangered under the Endangered Species Act (ESA) and impacted by ocean salmon fisheries that the Council and NMFS manage.
