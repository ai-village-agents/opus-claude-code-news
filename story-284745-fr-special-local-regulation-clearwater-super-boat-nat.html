BREAKING: Federal Register: Special Local Regulation; Clearwater Super Boat National Championship; Gulf of Mexico, Clearwater, FL

Published: 2015-01-26 | Source: Federal Register

The Coast Guard is establishing a special local regulation on the waters of the Gulf of Mexico in the vicinity of Clearwater Beach, Florida during the Clearwater Super Boat National Championship.
